Slight train-wreck that is mostly fun!
Overall I would recommend this game. However don't buy it full price, wait for a sale. (unlike me)

I have loved the Just Cause franchise and when it was released this game was a huge disappointment to the series, it felt rushed, buggy and the graphics were closer to Just Cause 2 than 3 let alone what it should've been for a game released in 2018, however over the next year or so, the Devs smoothed out the bugs and made the water look better (thank god). So as of 2021 it is a playable game when exploring the open world. If it is being used as a sandbox and you love blowing stuff up in creative ways, then this game is for you. A lot of content is very similar to Just Cause 3 and in some ways that is a good thing but it others it felt used and disappointing. I like the addition of a radio in the car, it makes long journeys more fun.

Getting into the campaign was a huge chore which felt buggy and would consistently annoy me. The cut scenes look dreadful like an un-rendered preview of an unreleased episode of a 3D animated show. I endured the entire campaign of this game, there was no real character development and it felt improvised. If you compare the final cut-scenes throughout the game with the E3 ones then this is really disappointing, (I know those were big renders and these are game engine scenes but compared to JC3 it's horrific).

With all this being said there are still some aspects which I really enjoyed from this game, hence i'm still giving it a recommended rating. There are some really cool mechanics with the storms, I also really enjoyed the variety in the different biomes across the map. The combustibility of the grappling hook is really precious in this game and I like the many different ways of moving stuff around and leaving it up to the player on how they decide they want to blow tf out of a base. I also like Rico's occasional witty remarks when something interesting happens.

Don't mess up JC5, tysm ;)

TL;DR - With most of the bugs and early release snags ironed out the campaign is still ♥♥♥♥ but it is a really cool enjoyable sandbox.\t\t\t\t\t
