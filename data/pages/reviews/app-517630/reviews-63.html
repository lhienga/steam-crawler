\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis could be a very good game, except for the following.

The programmers behind the game really suck at making things proper difficult, and seem to be too stupid to take anything but the easy way.
What do I mean?
- Enemy helicopters ALWAYS magically appear behind you. You will never see them coming until you're currently getting shot.
- Whenever you are flying, which should be one of the many joys of this game, there will ALWAYS be a super accurate enemy plane shooting you from behind. They have perfect evasive maneuvers and perfect aim. Again, they, too, are magic and will only appear directly behind you. There is no way to stop them, and no way to fight back. This and the helicopters problem makes me think that the developers hate their customers in that they don't want them to fly around the game, DESPITE giving the player a fun jet pack and access to all flying vehicles. "You can have these things, but you just can't use them for fun." -devs
- Ground bases magically have enemy soldiers appear in infinite amounts and they, too, are perfectly accurate when shooting at you.
- Many times something will just blow you out of the sky and you will have no idea where it came from and how it hit you, even though you may have been flying randomly. It will still hit you and you will just fall from the sky unable to do anything because the recovery times takes about three days.


Despite the possibility of this game being a very fun very open-ended open world game full of exploration and multiple side quests to feel like you're making a difference in the game world, and making good progress, the developers have seen fit to completely ruin that with poor enemy programming and garbage enemy cheating. It's so very frustrating that I cannot play this trash fire for more than a couple hours before I end up hating it because of the problems listed above.
