\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuch a disappointment. Good thing I got it on Spring sale.
I'll probably rack up quite some game time anyway because I'm a bit of a completionist and have to take everything over, but compared to 3, everything is worse.

Most fun part of JC3? Blowing up and taking over settlements and bases like that. Now? Yeah they completely removed it. Sure, you can blow some stuff up, but settlements and bases are now taken over (or at least, get a tick on the map, no taking over) by doing "side objectives" for them. Those aren't quests, mind you. Those are mindless "drive through this ring at this speed", "fly through these 3 rings in a wingsuit", "drive this car model through this ring", stuff like that. Busy work everyone hates.

Second most fun part in JC3? Thetering stuff together. That you can still do but they ruined it in a way that could even have worked if they did it better. your tethers now have 3 extra slots, balloons (think the fultons from metal gear), retractor and booster. Boosters are like the small mine things you had in JC3, you can now shoot them, which would be cool. but because all is tied to 3 different loadouts of the tethers, it doesn't really work. The three things don't really synergize. You can set to activate each of them automatically, on key press or on key hold, but they're deployed anyway, they're just activated by that key. So say you have tethers with strong retractors (to pull them together) and balloons with explosive gas. Even if you just want tethering, even if you have the balloons on a keypress, the minimally inflated balloon is still there, can still explode and snap your tether, will explode if you set the retraction speed too high and so on. So most people probably just set one of each on one of the three loadouts.
So wasted. Why have the balloon there if you specifically requested it to only be there on keypress?

Enemies? yeah they respawn constantly, behind you, in vehicles. Remember when you could pretty much clear a base before doing an objective in JC3? Forget that. Kill everything in front of you, turn around for a second and what is that, laserpointers from the friggin helicopter with snipers that spawned where you just looked a moment ago?
That also means, whenever they're onto you, pretty much the only way to lose them is to go out in the wilderness and wait. They seem to only spawn in populated areas. Well I guess better than nothing.
Speaking of helicopters, those have to be some of the best pilots, Tether a helicopter so it does a barrel roll and spins wildly, your tethher snaps, and in a second they stabilized again and are shooting at you.

The graphics are also a big step back. Everything is yellow tinted, the colors don't pop. It looks ugly. Also, some designer thought "hey, let's give /everyone/ cellulite. In the face! That looks realistic, when you see huge pores on everyone's face, right?"

And because this time they decided " mods" and didn't include it, you have to launch the game through a mod loader instead of steam if you want mods.

It's also an awful port. you know how to get to the grapple loadout menu? You open your map with the 1 number key, then use 2 and 3 number keys to find the correct page. Then you use the 1 key again to close it because ESC, as you'd expect, does nothing. That's the kind of port you'd expet from a japanese game, a JRPG, they're famous for that.

I better stop now or I rant too much, but.. Summa sumarum, I didn't notice a single thing it did better than JC3. Do not get unless you can get complete/gold edition for10 bucks - or if you never played JC3. But then, rather go get JC3.\t\t\t\t\t
