\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a total spit-in-the-face to my favourite game series of all time.

Firstly, the (very few) pros.
The Audio is god-tier, the guns sound great and the controller controls are absolutely great.

Now the cons.
The story is terrible. The Just Cause games' stories have always been like a cheesy hollywood film, knowing that, they don't take themselves very seriously mostly. Just Cause 4 however, keeps all the cheesiness but takes itself as seriously as a late 2000s Call of Duty title. IT's basically just "something something oscar espinosa, something something Rico's dad, bla bla project iiapa, something something Agency from jc2 are the BAD GUYS?!?!?!?! " The story is just a half-assed attempt at a serious story.

The graphics are... atrocious. They look worse than Just Cause 2 (2010) at times.

The missions are So. ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. samey. It's quite literally just "Protect these NPCs" or "Protect this satellite as 10♥♥♥♥ingthousand helicopters toll up on it". If you're lucky, you might get a "Spend 30 minutes finding some tiny indoor entrance to flip 2 switches". Just Cause 2 had very repetitive missions, but they were FUN. Just Cause 3 had some incredible missions, like "Son of Medici" and "Taking Control". Plus, they had some dope scenes and set-pieces (That one mission where you're in the underground base, and Di Ravello starts monologing as you are crowded by a bunch of soldiers, The last story mission where you have to shoot down Di Ravello's golden helicopter inside a volcano , etc etc)

The grappling hook upgrades are needlessly complicated and just a burden.

The missions with that Garland lady are ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ abysmal. Rico isn't even an "Agent" anymore. He's like some celebrity that everyone suddenly now knows.

They removed the Bavarium wingsuit, making travelling 100x times worse.
End of cons.

This game is absolutely terrible, and Avalanche should be ASHAMED for even thinking that this is worthy of being the next game in this series. I would only ever recommend getting it when the reloaded edition it is on sale for extremely cheap or if you get it in the Just Cause Complete bundle. If you haven't played a JC game before, play 3 or 2; those are infinitely better then this dumpster fire disguised as a "game".

tl;dr game ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ sucks\t\t\t\t\t
