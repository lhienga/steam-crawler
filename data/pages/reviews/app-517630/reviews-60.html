\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game I could only really describe as 'only exists because it's a cash-cow sequel.' It's also a bug-riddled bad-physics hell which deserves refunding.

If this had been released as Just Cause 1, it would've been slammed so hard into the ground by reviews that the only thing 'hot' about it would be its average temperature upon reaching the Earth's mantle. Just about everything in this iteration is exactly that: an iteration, and sadly not very many are good ones. The wingsuit controls are SO bad. The game is full of sloppy ideas that sound way cooler than they perform, physics that just feels more off than the last 3 games, controls and AI logic that are hard to wrap your head around, it's honestly a shame this game has as many cool concepts as it does, because that's probably the only other reason it moved as many numbers as it did, apart from die-hard fanboys desperately clinging to a sinking ship.

It's also impossible to escape the black hand forces once they start sending air units after you, as they just keep spawning helicopters and drones right behind you indefinitely, or they'll send a jet which is impossible to shoot down without homing missiles. It's actually easier and more playable to just die and restart at that point.

More Playtime Edit

God, it's so bad. Omniscient AI. DLC equipment that doesn't interact with other DLC equipment. Instant death because one random sniper shot you in the face. Every vehicle but the mech is made of Papier-mch and tacky glue, causing you to die if you so much as think about brushing against any terrain for any reason. Extremely un-fun and spammy DLC enemies. An entire car-driving DLC in a game where making a left turn over 20 mph is a herculean task. They even took out all the fun of the jetpack from the 3rd game and replaced it with a total nothing 'upgrade' system that just makes it useless faster. This game is such wasted potential that it makes me angry.

Don't bother picking this one up. Just go back and play 3, or even 2.\t\t\t\t\t
