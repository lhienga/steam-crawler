\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCrazy physics chaos simulator. Improves on 3 in many ways.
Best thing compared to 3 in my opinion is that this always has a war going on in the background and you are very much fighting in it as well. With front lines that have some pretty fierce fighting going on.
Its not about the story even though I think its not too bad but you probably wont get too attached to many characters and will forget who some are by the time you get back to them depending on how your playing the game. But when you forgive its shortcomings what you have here is a really fun chaos first person shooter that could be best described as bionic commando on steroids. The overall variety of units rolling around on both sides seems better as well. Fighter jets will target you and you will run into groups of tanks and helicopters and of course troops etc all mixing and getting crazy in the field. Its a lot of fun to just watch the crazy @#$% that happens sometimes as it can get comical in a what the hell just happened way. I think this game actually deserves a better rap because it is just a heck of a lot of fun.\t\t\t\t\t
