\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI tried to like this series, but it didn't click with me at all. The first game has a really outdated gameplay, it was so bad I quit. JC2 gets a 100% better gameplay, but it had a problem that persisted in the other entries: you MUST grind to unlock the main missions.
I have no problem to grind when I love a game, but when it's just another generic third person shooter with very underwhelming plot, I just want to play the main missions and be done with it. This is no Final Fantasy, the grinding gets stale and boring fast but you're obligated to do it to unlock main missions. To make things worse for JC4, the gameplay became confusing and it was a downgrade from JC3 (JC3 is the best in the series, for better or worse). The graphics are horrible for an AAA game released on 2018. If I had played it on PS Plus or Gamepass I would never had bough it. Give me one Final Fantasy or let me play GTA5 for the fourth time, even though I spent lots of hours on JC2 and JC3, I don't think I'll finish those games.. \t\t\t\t\t
