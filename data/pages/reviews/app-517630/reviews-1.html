The story isn't super engaging and has a lot of story elements that happen within the cut scenes: i.e. it doesn't have one singular story mission to do at a time, but multiple that unlock after each cut scene. Additionally, the movement between the parachute, wing suit, and grappling hook are pretty janky with delay times between opening the parachute and grappling. Although this may not be a problem for others, to me it just feels different and slower than past Just Cause games. Overall, the graphics look good, and the game play (despite some of my nitpicks) is fun enough to warrant a buy if the game goes on sale (which is does frequently and for a small amount of money).\t\t\t\t\t
