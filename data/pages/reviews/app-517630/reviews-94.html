\r\n\t\t\t\t\tArguably the weakest of the modern Just Cause Games, but its honestly still a good game, liberation is less impactful than it was in 3, and the game has a lot of bugs. But I didnt come to Just Cause for the liberation, the story, etc. I came here to blow everything up, and that is done spectacularly in this game.

I will admit that I believe this game should've been left in the cooker a bit longer, as everything is perfect apart from the liberation-gameplay & story, something 3 excelled in. Its not everyday that you get to destroy an enormous fuel tank attached to a skycrane above a city, and no matter what i'm doing I will always destroy those things because I like explosions. Its also not often that you also have actually decent weather disasters in a game, the tornado is always fun to throw the cargo jet into :).

Its not as impactful as the story in 3 was, but this is still a very good casual game for when you just want to relax & have some dumb fun.

I hope that if there is a Just Cause 5, they bring back the liberation from 3, but still keep the absolute dumb fun & chaos this game can be.\t\t\t\t\t
