\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like the huge explosions, the destructible stuff and the ease of use of Just Cause 3? then replay it, because this game is in no way good, let alone a good Just Cause (and the bar isn't really that high).
This game has so few destructible objects that you'd be forgiven if you thought this is an HD mod of Just Cause 1. The objects you destoy don't matter anyway since once you get out of the loading range and go back they pop back in.
Rico feels somehow worse, as if he's half asleep. sometimes he grapples two objects, sometimes he just holds the other tether wondering what to do, and sometimes doesn't do anything. The movement has this delay that makes him keep walking a few feet after you stop the input too.
Cars are terrible, huge oversteer always except when they just won't turn. Planes turn likes wasps and bikes are even worse. The only good vehicle are helicopters but they get gunned down by everything.
The "heat" or "pursuit" system is so bad that if you have a bit of wanted level when you get on a plane you'll be shot until you die, it doesn't matter how many you down, they keep respawning behind you.
The map is soooo boring, towns don't have purpose. Bases are all very bland and forgettable, and the missions suck, besides there's like 4 variations for what? 30 bases? Just terrible, would rather keep liberating the same town in JC3.
I was going to play through this because it's still just cause but of course I had to get constant low LOD buildings, objectives not loading, the AI staying still until they are killed, etc.
Not worth a single coin, terrible game when compared with the predecessors.
If you still play it enjoy the hacking "mechanic"! you can't move nor do anything while waiting for a timer, and if a single shotgun pellet hits you from 50 meters away you gotta start over \t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuch a terrible step-down when comparing the game to Just Cause 3. I come very biased.

The Gameplay:
The controls FEEL terrible. They could've literally copypaste 3's control's one to one and have the game handle itself much better. Something about this game feels very, very off. I can't really put this into words, but i'll describe at like this: In Just Cause 3 when you parachute, wingsuit and grapple the game's world works with you and lets you glide across it smoothly. Here - to me, it feels like you have to fight it to move across.
The game's world feels very bland to me and doesn't speak much volume as 3 did. feels like a bad redo of Just Cause 2's world and nothing is very unique.
Explosions feel somehow worse than Just Cause 3 and 2 and the combat is about the same as 3's - though, enemies sometimes have insane aim hitting you with surgery precision in literal mud water or can't do ♥♥♥♥ and just stand there while you're in front of them.

Graphics:
I don't know how they messed this up, really. The game again compared to 3's graphics looks TERRIBLE. As typing this will be very painful, I just recommend you see comparisons between 3 and 4, absolutely awful.

Performance:
I have an RTX 3060Ti paired with an i7 12700f with 16Gb of 3200Mhz RAM.
This is by no means a top-notch setup, but it is well beyond to at least allow the game to run at 1440p, 120-100 FPS. But I can't, not at least at a steady 100. There's way too much stuttering and random FPS drops. The game slows to a crawl of 70-50 FPS at big fights, when shooting an RPG (I honestly don't know why) and some more random ♥♥♥♥. Just Cause 3 never really had an issue for me, but maybe my PC is weird.

And finally, the story: It's terrible.
The games takes itself way too seriously and most characters are completely forgettable and unfunny. step down from 3's action-comedy style story, It doesn't let you get as attached to characters here, the likes of which, for example, Mario (my mannn) from 3.
There aren't any funny tropes to characters that captivate you from the start or anything interesting really. Honestly, skip the story when playing.

Overall, though - the game is fun at it's core. It is just a huge step-down from 3 and If you don't have the money, don't buy this game and get 3 now instead, as when it is on sale, it's like 5-11 dollars.\t\t\t\t\t
