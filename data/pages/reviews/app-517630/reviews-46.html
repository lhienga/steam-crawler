\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game but just barely. It can be good fun especially if your a fan of the series but it does not live up to just cause 2 or 3. I have 100% completed this game and then uninstalled directly after. It is not a game i ever intend on playing again or replaying. I am a fan of the series but seeing reviews being mixed i held off and bought on sale and got all of the dlc's i recommend you do the same.

Pros-
*Fun game mechanics.
*Large map
*Beautiful areas to explore.
*Lots of content
*numerous vehicles to play with.

Cons-
*Sometimes there is too much content. repetition is enough to make you drink with all the stunts and everything this game has you doing over and over.
*Why can i grapple parachute hoverboard fly launch missiles off my back but only carry 2 weapons at a time.In my opinion this is a great disservice and there should be a weapon wheel with more weapon unlocks being added to it and a primary focus on ammo like in Grand theft auto. (Wow i can't believe i said that either) but that system would work so well in this game.
*Cut scenes are horrendous. In most games they look better than the game. In this it feels like someone swapped my graphics card for the time being to something from the 90's then right back after it's over.
*Honestly the story is probably 6-8 hours of this game and the rest is collecting this or that and finding locations.
*The missions could be longer they don't take very much time at all to finish.
*the dlc's are ok but i didn't want more stuff to hunt down or a damned hoverboard i was forced to use.
*I really missed the mechanic like JC3 had where there were locations and you had to destroy things to clear the location. I feel as if that should have been present not solely but mixed in as well as having stunts. Some locations are stunts others are destruction.
*Grappling hook while parachuting is awful it seems there isn't as much reach as what you had in JC2 or JC3 which would often leave me stranded in free air in scenarios where i did not or could not use the glider suit.

I could probably go on forever but in summary i give it a 2/5. Its fun but after buying it i put it down immediately probably for the better part of 2 years. I finally made it my job to finish this game so it wasn't looking at me all the time and i had another 100% on my steam account. I say job because most of the time in this game was not fun but more like something i wanted to get out of the way. I hope this helps. It's an easy 100% it just takes time. i say play it but don't expect a whole lot.\t\t\t\t\t
