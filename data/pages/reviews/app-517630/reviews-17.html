\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly don't understand the hate, this is legit my favorite Just Cause game and I played all of them.

First of all, the game plays and looks pretty much exactly like Just Cause 3. You will get into the gameplay pretty easy if you played that and this one probably has some of the best weapons and vehicles in the entire series.

I like the entire building up your army and conquering territory on the map. It's better than the tired destroying the same looking bases a million times. Don't get me wrong, you still do a lot of repetitive stuff but it's still a little more varied than the other games. + the storm mechanics from Mad Max definetely added to the enjoyment.

The story is pretty basic, but the build up is pretty good and how you slowly build up counter messures to Illapa is enjoyable to watch and play. I also like the tired old Rico who just wants to settle down, but has to keep going.

What I didn't like:
There's definetely a lot of woke in it. Every scientist on the "good" side is a woman and so is about 90% of the military forces in the entire game. It always took me out to shoot soldiers to then hear a woman moan when they died.

I like the idea that the plans to take out a base go wrong, but I wish that didn't happen 100% of the time. Sometimes a plan should just go right, but I guess they had to stretch the game length a little so the player wouldn't beat it in less than 10 hours.

[For Steam Deck users: The game runs mostly pretty good, but I had a few crashes throughout my playthrough.]

Finishing the story and conquering the entire map takes: ~20 hours
(The story missions alone probably take less than 10 hours.)\t\t\t\t\t
