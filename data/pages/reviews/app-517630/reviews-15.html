\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWorse than Just Cause 3

I have played Just Cause 3 and 4 and have to say that JC4 is a serious downgrade to the previous game:

-Cutscene Graphics straight out of the Play Station 2 era (even on max settings)
-No handheld explosives and no C4 anymore (the unlimited C4 was fun as hell in JC3)
-Weird progression system: The main story isn't linear anymore but hidden behind having to unlock areas by doing the same dumb missions over and over again
-Progression system doesn't work by blowing up enemy bases or liberating a city anymore but by doing repetitive "mini missions" e.g destroying the same stuff over and over again in THE SAME BASE and doing multiple missions like escort missions in the same base
-Enemies spawn fast as hell behind you and roll up in tanks and helis, kinda weird that there is not at least some reaction time needed for them
-Vehicles DON'T have unlimited ammo anymore
-Overall I have the feeling that the graphics were downgraded because even the water is flat and looks fake and boring compared to JC3

Pros of this title:

+ New funny mechanics like baloons and boosters
+ Weather system allows to create tornados (unfortunately wayyy later in the game)
+ New enemies like agency agents, who also have a hook and can jump around


All in all I don't understand why they downgraded this game this much. I want my progress be tied to me blowing up the bases and not doing the same tedious and repetitive missions over and over again until I unlock the main story and get some upgrades like the rocket on my back.

In short: LET ME BLOW ♥♥♥♥ UP AND BE COOL. If they manage to fix this in a patch it would be a serious improvement. But I doubt that they will remaster this game just yet lol.
I hope Square Enix learns from this mistake and makes the next game better in the points mentioned.

Thankfully I did not pay the full price (above 80 bucks for all) but managed to get the complete edition for 10 bucks otherwise I'd be mad a hell lol
