\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust Cause 4 was not what I had expected. I originally thought that JC4 would just be more JC3, but I was wrong. It sucks that I have to give this game a bad review. I kinda wish there was an in between for the yes and no.

One thing that I did not enjoy was the liberation system. In JC3, there were multiple towns and bases that you could liberate. Those were fun and made you feel like a badass. JC4 on the other hand, has you do strikes on bigger bases instead. I expected the strikes to be fun, but most of them contain the same objectives. I understand that in JC3, it was the same objectives as well: destroy, but those objectives were fun. This game's objectives is just flipping breakers and a few other things that are repeated. After that, you just advance your frontlines into the next area you liberated (if its adjacent).

The game is definitely worth the price on sale because you can get the game and all the DLC for under $20, and that's a steal. Overall, JC4 is a decent game but just isn't the same as three. Also, Danger Rising is an actually good content DLC. The other two, I couldn't finish because they just weren't that fun.

TLDR: just get the game and all the DLC when it's on sale. (also, Danger Rising is cool)\t\t\t\t\t
