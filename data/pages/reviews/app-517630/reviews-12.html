\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust Cause 4. Frankly i cannot say game is perfect, it is not, it is not bad if you like doing crazy stuff in it (and you can). But i feel that each new game of JC franchize is going downhill.

When JC originally was released in 2006, it was ok, i cannot say it was bad because i had quite fun playing it, but again it was blank, empty. Big map, really, really big map but empty. I had feeling i am alone on map with exception of few NPC's most of map was empty.
JC2 was not better, Panau was not bad setting, but again you have that empty feeling, there was more NPCs on certain areas but not all around, driving around was dissaster.
JC3 was fun, i really like it, although in beginning had problems with AMD but i feel that game in same time is improvement and downgraded from the JC2, there were problems with graphic settings and everything looked like game was older then it was. Vehicles were not as great, it seems Avalanche studios cant make cars to work as they should, camera is strange, movement of land vehicles is very strange, jets are terrible and the only thing that actually worked was boats and helicopters. Story was well generic, take another dictator out. Standard for JC series.

And then we get to the JC4. This game is downgrade from JC3, sure some graphic aspects look better (i said some) but most is bad. Like in previous games, you need to remove dictator from power and stopping him from doing bad thngs to world with his special super weapon. With exception of few characters, all others were not really important and not worth to remember at all. Combat remain the same as with previous game, modified and slightly upgrade which ok. Grappler was modified so much that added many new abilities which is great and some of those make me laugh when i used them but ok. Weapon selection os ok. DLC brings even more (as usual, they always release bunch of crappy DLCs, some actually good but most bad, rather to focus on bugs and problems, they give us DLC we don't want), driving a car or bike in this game is terrible, TERRIBLE with capital letters. I mean driving camera can give you headeche, even seizure from sudden turns. Using vehicles in this game is catastrophy, big downgrade from last game, this is why i use grapling hook and wingsuit for moving across map, its slower but more fun. Planes the same, maybe helicopters are okay but fixed planes are terrible. Boats, ok, not perfect but they work.
THere is no story, story is that you must defeat the dictator like in other games, so basically copy paste with different characters and premise all over again. Unfortunatley this game is worse then last one, and while there was word that JC5 is in making i hope they upgrade the whole system. Otherwise JC franchise most definitly going down. Progression in JC4 is something that bother me a lot, they should leave it been as in JC3, not invent the wheel again, however they did go invent wheel and they make it worse. If you love JC games, buy it on sale. It doesnt worth full price. I mean its bad version of JC3. I rarely say that i do not recommend games because i respect people who create games (even if game sucks) but this game in this state as its made is not recommend at full price, only on sale. Sry Avalanche studios, i do respect your work but you need to improve your games.\t\t\t\t\t
