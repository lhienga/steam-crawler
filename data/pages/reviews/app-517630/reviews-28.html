Just Cause 3 was an excellent game, it was great both in terms of user friendliness, visuals, UI and controls. My question is, why do you change from a user-friendly control and interface structure which works perfectly smooth, to one that is literally "♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥"? So I really wanted to ask this...
The game has enough bugs already. Starting right from the first mission, Yes! I'm talking about "Behind the Lines". The truck catches fire out of nowhere and explodes for no apparent reason! So unless you download a stupid save game from somewhere you can't overcome the problem! ITS A GLITCH!
Let's put that aside, let' say you wanted to drive with the open world feature; there is no way! As soon as you crash the car, you switch to a ridiculous blur and slow motion mode, the funny thing is, the game gets stuck there and you have to exit to main menu!
Or you get another error message stating the game stopped responding when there is no problem. (No it's not Graphics Driver)
So, while Just Cause 3 works flawlessly and perfectly, its newer and more advanced version is helpless, bad and inadequate, as if you were discovering Rome all over again. Plus it's full of bugs!
I fell in love with Just Cause 3. Now I will play it again.
But Just Cause 4 is a complete disappointment and JUNK! Unfortunately there are no other words to describe it. I wish it was...\t\t\t\t\t
