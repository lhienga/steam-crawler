\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like Batman Arkham Knight? (gliding and grappling hook)
Do you like GTA V? (vehicle hi-jacking)
Do you like Metal Gear Solid 5? (Guns, guns and more guns)
Do you like Mad Max? (Get the DLC for car battles)
Do you like Tony Hawks Pro Skater? (DLC with hover board grinding)
Do you like Far Cry 6? (they made their game the same as Just Cause)

If you said yes to any of the above then you should buy buy buy this game. So much fun in this game you can pretend to be any super hero you want really. Now when I play other games I wish that I had Rico to get me around their map instead looking at it from the other way round.

Map traversing in most games is the boring part, like if you lose a car in GTA V and have to jog for 3 minutes to get a new one, try 3 seconds in JUST CAUSE 5. And you may not ever choose to drive outside of the missions because you get a jet pack and wing suit, this means getting from point A to B is Epic, jump out of a moving car into a parachute, then into the wing suit and then jet-pack off into the skies to glide to the next location, it never gets old!
I made a mistake in my first play through to turn off the jet pack in the load out menu, because it said you could not complete some of the stunts with it enabled, but it is not any stunts that matter, it is for the stunts that don't contribute to 100% completion, and they are way too hard anyway so thank devs for that! So I actually played the story and quit for months maybe a year and came back to it, completed all the DLC and then took over the whole map and 100% everything (except for the hover board race stunts DLC).

The game excels at character mobility, its never slow to get back into what you were doing, if you fall into water it is not a problem with the hoverboard it boosts you straight to the surface again then you can jump into the wing suit and jetpack back into a vehicle if you desire.

Vehicles, there are many, all with different control schemes, but still familiar, it takes a second to remind your self of helicopter controls compared to jet airplane or even the jet airplane that hovers too. True to life in a way as they all control differently, some cars swap gun and nos controls for some reason not sure why but it only takes a second to realize.

Gun play, it is the only game I have played where you can float above the enemy with a parachute and hail down bullets as go to tactic. You don't really need to get into gun fights at all as you can get around so quickly with the grappling hook you'll just need to clear the area before hacking a console or open a door etc, sometimes easy and sometimes hard to clear the area if there is a giant missile launcher or 2 homed in on your location. There are only 2 gun slots to carry any combination of guns and you can carry 2 snipers or 2 rocket launchers etc any of the strange and powerful guns they have. It's hard to run out of ammo in the action but at the same time you will run out for that gun you carried but always pick up an enemies gun straight away, it's another none pace slowing perk. I have wanted a back up side pistol only in a few situations, I ran out of ammo hanging from a helicopter and I could not hijack the helicoptor with out killing the 2 snipers sitting in the back first, a back up pistol just for that situation would be great.

Some hijacking seems a bit harsh for the Hero to perform, like throwing out an innocent or chaos army member from a helicopter seems a bit harsh when they would probably happily switch seats with Rico and let him drive, they could get out when it is safe like when some car passengers do, they would probably stay in the Heli and get blown up when I jump out of the vehicle anyway so maybe Ricos method of a fall to the ground has a higher survivability rate.

Water, you can swim and boat, the grappling hook means you won't be swimming for long periods either, you can also shoot from in the water with your torso above the water, most games disable abilities like that in the water but not Just Cause 4. The hover board makes getting out of the water so speedy it is a must have in Just Cause 5, along with the wing suit and boosters.

I got the game in a sale for a great price and it is worth full price.\t\t\t\t\t
