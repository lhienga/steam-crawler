\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe good:
Graphics, sounds, voices, cutscenes, landscapes, a diverse world... A lot of work went into that. That's what makes it at least fun for a few hours.

The bad:
It's just... Empty. You keep blasting around, having fun with the parachute and the wingsuit, and hunt for story pieces... But everything is the same - just shoot and kill everything, until the thing says you're done. And then (other than in previous games) if you revisit the location, everything is built back up again! So it doesn't even matter. And as soon as you set foot in enemy territory, the sky is suddenly buzzing with ridiculous drones, planes and enemy helicopters, that came out of nowhere.

It's also buggy. I've experienced multiple CTD's, have had to reinstall because fullscreen didn't work any more, have had movement keys stuck, have glitched through vehicles, suddenly died out of nowhere, fell down from cliffs without taking a single hit, been unable to open or close the parachute... And then there's the servers being offline so I can't play, and then constantly mismatched cloud saves. It's just a mess. And that's all within 5 hours of playtime.

So yeah, it's a nope for me. I got it on sale for less than 10 bucks and what's 10 buck for a few hours of relative fun, so hey you can give it a try on a good sale, but the frustration about how badly the thing is put together just wins out.\t\t\t\t\t
