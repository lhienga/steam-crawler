Overall it was fun but I had a really tough time with some of the controls (using keyboard/mouse) and had some troubles with the driving and hoverboard. "Danger Rising" in the Expansion Pass has a hoverboard I only use for surfacing when underwater and a "SMG" with a homing alt-fire that became my primary weapon in the game. The "Adversary Vehicle Pack" has the X700 Flycatcher VTOL which is amazing, and the hover mode moves over 60kph for clearing early speed challenges. I didn't find any other DLC item particularly useful in practice, but could've missed some.\t\t\t\t\t
