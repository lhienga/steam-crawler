There's no different heat levels, beyond if they'll send a helicopter or tank or something, but it's not like it means anything when most of the game is the same loop of go to base, kill bad dudes, complete one of three objectives.

Honestly it's more of a JC1 remake with what seems futuristic in current year then it is a standalone game. I think it would have been better if that's actually what it was instead of what it ended up as. Compared to JC2 and JC3 it's just lacking over all.

It's not a functionally bad game nor an ugly one. The setting is nice to look at, the guns are pretty neat. The bases are more complex then any of the other games. Just a little disappointing that beyond the one mission at a base you need to do in order to get the region they aren't used for much else.

Over all Just Cause 4 is just bland, and doesn't hold up to the older entries in the series.\t\t\t\t\t
