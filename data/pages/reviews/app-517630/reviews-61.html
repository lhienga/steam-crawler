\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFranchise core elements were all gone other than the wing suit, parachute, and grapple. New element implemented as gracefully as attaching someone's broken legs to two separate dogs and training them to help a person walk.

Key binds are broken. I can not exit vehicles despite tapping or holding the buttons down if I swap keys around. This is a deal breaker. Especially since they never made plane or jets easier to fly on keyboard and mouse. You are strictly limited to pitch, yaw, and roll keys. Did I mention yawing is z and c rather than q and e? You can't change because the exit vehicle key will not work unless it's e.

Game forces you to play story for progression and don't explain what you can do in the open word when they hand you the ability to free roam.

AI path finding is completely degraded to it's basic function. It will not check if it can fly from point A to point B without accidentally committing a terrorist act.

The game changed too much from Just Cause 3 to Just Cause 4 without properly adjusting us to the game play. They removed the heat system's escalations and made it uniform, so difficulty no longer scales when you have the misfortune of requiring to bail out of your vehicle and get in trouble for it. It only gets worse when you realize enemies spawn around you Cyberpunk style. I wish I was joking. They did this to artificially make the game challenging in the least fair way possible. Minor touches to game immersion such as the explosive chisel have been removed. Everyone just leaves their doors and tank hatches unlocked because it would be unfair to the one man army with a grappling hook.

I found a runway with a plane and a bunch of explosive barrels on the runway. I got tipped over when trying to fly the plane out of the shack and spun backwards hitting obstacles.

Conclusion: I wish I could refund this legal scam of a game. I'd get intimate with my ex a second time to make this game not be an absolute disgrace to the Just Cause franchise.\t\t\t\t\t
