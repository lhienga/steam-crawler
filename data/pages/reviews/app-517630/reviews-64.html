There is literally nothing better in this then in JC3, even graphics are similar to 3, it's just more jungle and more UI-crap on the screen that means nothing. "Just Cause" and "buttons-galore" are not two things that belong in the same sentence. It's like the devs noticed there is thing called keyboard and they could cram more things into it then previously!

I can imagine the discussion:
Director: I want buttons for everything.
Coders: Everything..?
Director: EVERYTHING!!!
Coders: How about two functions for every button!?
Director: *heavy breathing*

The functions are scattered all around the keyboard with little logic. You need several hours to just learn and memorize the buttons and their functions. They changed it from "mouse right" because heck, why not! It's only the most used thing in the game! "Right mouse" has now zoom aim. And that "mouse right" has to have two modes, press and hold, just so they could get more things in! And if you happen to have machine gun with a shield, it's a third button for one gun plus the mandatory reload button! And that previously working grab hook, the biggest and funniest thing in Just Cause-games..? There is now half a dozen buttons for it! You apparently can't tighten the tether yourself anymore, it does it automatically. But there is now two buttons for "quick-reel" because that was apparently a priority. Previously used "F" for throwing grenades was changed to the industry standard "Middle Mouse". Why? So they could cram more buttons for that "F"!!!

And all in all, everything that previously nice and neat, is now menus, CONSTANT mayhem (whether you wanted or not) and idiotic controls because .. i don't know. They like it confusing. It's not like nobody wanted to play a fun and casual game like Just Cause. More convenient to take button controls, mix them up in a bag and throw them to table and see how confusing they could make everything. I'm not going to spend hours to just get this game playable in menus and with mods, this game is dead to me. Make the next Just Cause like JC2 or JC3 and i'll consider buying. With big reservations.\t\t\t\t\t
