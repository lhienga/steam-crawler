\r\n\t\t\t\t\twhen this game was announced, i was super hyped. by that stage every instalment from the just cause series got better and better, and i was hoping that 4 would follow that trend but it seemed that for every minor improvement they added, they would remove a feature from 3 that made it awesome. on its own merit the game itself can be fun, but compared to 3 its not that great. i do want to give it some due respect. the graphics are good, the word and weather are interesting to look at, the enemy ai is smarter and there's a lot more variety to them, the physics are visually and technically impressive, and the guns feel a lot better to use.
now let me give you 20 reasons why this game is a pile of peepee.
1. the world may be more varied with a lot of attention to detail then 3's but it feels very bland and empty, 3 did have this issue but not as much as 4 and navigating through it can be a slug
2. the new weather systems add nothing to gameplay other then "oh cool a tornado"
3. the story has a much more serious tone then 3 but in turn it makes it come off as corny and slow
4. just cause 3 starts with a sick montage, just cause 4 starts with a mindless exposition dump
5. they removed c4
6. the bad guy is a cliche rich man, just cause 3's villain was a cold, ruthless and yet lazy dictator
7. the secondary fire makes each gun feel uniqe but it kinda feels like an after thought as i would always forget about it.
8. you cant blow up bridges
9. they removed c4
10. the plot structure is go here, meet new person, do things for them. build a tool to stop weather thing, done. and that happens 4 times
11. you cant liberate towns
12. the atheistic went into a more si-fi rout which is more of a personal preference thing but i like the idea of a one man army going up against an actual army rather then teched up soldiers
13. the characters are far less likeable then in three
14. the plot starts with a strong sense of finale and personal connections but ends up devolving into 'this happened once, remember just cause 1? and this is the next game"
15. the army of chaos feels more like a sales point. its there to give chaos points an actually reason for existing but it removes the liberation of bases and instead gives incentives taking over land. this adds nothing to gameplay or mission structure. what if they added big battles that you can fight, win or loose in, what if you could train your army to be better and have them help liberate towns. in the end it feels more in the way then anything as you have to use them to move forward in the story
16. they removed c4
17. the ui is a mess
18. the different grapple options are kinda cool but mostly forgettable as its easiest to probably just stick with the retracting one. its mainly there to send goats and cows flying through the air
19. probably the main problem, they changed the way you liberate bases, instead of blowing everything up, you insted have to do a mission that range from escorting someone, breaking prisoners out, hacking something, ect, kinda like in just cause 2 this is so incredibly stupid. in 3 it gave you so much freedom in the ways you can blow things up and that was essentially the main gameplay. in 4 they hold you arm back and forces you to do stuff you don't want to do, when what you actually want to do (explosions) just sits there teasing you.
20. why oh god did they remove the c4
in the end i wonder why i got this rather then just playing just cause 3\t\t\t\t\t
