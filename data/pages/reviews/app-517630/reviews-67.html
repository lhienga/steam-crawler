\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI hate leaving bad reviews... (warning: possible spoilers)

Just Cause 4 is the weakest game in the series on many levels. Here's some of the worst issues:

1. Story: ...or the lack of one. You get a quick glimpse of the main bad guy at the start of the game, a quick exchange with his second-in-command a short while later, then nothing. No interaction with the enemy until the very end of the game. There is some interactions with friendly NPCs, but 90% of the game is non-interactive.

2. Missions/Goals: There is a main quest line but it is incredibly brief. However, to perform some of these quests, the game forces you to go out and clear some regions. Clearing regions is the only real game play here.

3. Weapons/Vehicles/Tools: To be honest, I played the entire way through with the automatic rifle I started with and a grenade launcher. Every other weapon had limitations that made them nonviable. As always, the grappling hook and call for gun/vehicle drops and location drops makes up the bulk of world traversal.

4. Graphics/Characters/Environments/Etc.: Very, very dated. Feels and looks like a lot of recycled assets from previous versions.

This game ruined my faith in the Just Cause series. I'm going to be very cautious when/if I see JC5 drop here on Steam. I'll watch game play videos and read a few reviews, but I won't buy it just on brand recognition.\t\t\t\t\t
