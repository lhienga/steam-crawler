\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love 3rd person action games especially the ones where you just blow stuff up. Mercenaries 1 and 2 were some of my favorites. I really liked Just Cause 3. It was a great game to turn your brain off and just blow stuff up. Taking out each base at a time as I improved my movement and weapons was great. So it was really a surprise to find out how much I didn't like this one. They ruined.. well.. everything. The things that pop out in my head in no particular order.
- infinite enemies harass you at all times no matter what. Not in a fun way, more like being attacked by really weak bees but instead of stingers they just wear on your interest by buzzing around you.
- Its no longer about destroying bases and big explosions... which i thought was the whole pitch of the JC games. Seems like stunts are the focus now. I mean destroying bases isnt even a thing any more.
- The whole movement thing doesnt feel good any more. I found it impossible to stay in the air with the wing suit unlike the 3rd one.
- 3rd game was easy to grapple what you want like enemy planes but I found it more difficult in this game.
- Driving isnt fun, cant put my finger on why.
- The map is huge but completely empty of anything interesting.
- Objectives are not clear as to what they are or why you should do them or what reward you will get.
- You start with all abilities. There is no unlocking, no leveling.
- The weaponry is not interesting.
- The grappling hook was turned into a gimmick and made incredibly complicated.
- The wing suit is a nightmare. I'm unsure why but it just doesnt feel smooth and its hard to even go in the direction you want. Grapple hooking for speed just seems to send you into the ground.

I would just recommend playing Just Cause 3.
