\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter finally rebeating each game once more I still somehow found myself having a lot of fun with these games.
BN4 while rough still has some qualities I like about it such as a very visually appealing net, double souls being op, a great soundtrack, and couple of laughs from its old goofy translation errors.
BN5 has liberation missions which really makes it unique for having a strategy puzzle mode that only appears in this game, it also introduces a very fun risky mechanic (Chaos Unison) which adds high risk but high rewards if executed correctly. I just wish Double Team DS was brought into this collection.
BN6 is easily the strongest for having a lot of QOL changes from the other games as well as having a really fun/balanced PVP (if you exclude dbl beast lmao), the Cybeast mechanics are still fun as hell, This game really feels like it innovated the most in comparison to every other games (including the Volume 1 games).

Of course while I enjoyed playing these games, I cant go without bringing some criticism I have for this collection:

-It feels like they only gave BN6 some special treatment as it feels like its the only game that got the most changes. They removed the incident occurence theme from playing in BN6 after beating the game but they never give this QOL change to 4 or 5.

-Getting to connect with someone online wether its for PVP, trades, or comparisons is uncommon for any other game that isnt BN6. I've only been able to compare libraries in BN4. I feel like matchmaking for PVP should also have a feature where certain filters are disabled after a certain time of being unable to connect to someone (ex; if you cant find a ranked triple real deal battle with patch cards on, then there should be an option where you can search for literally any other game that doesnt meet the criteria youre looking for to increase the odds of finding someone.

-Adding a fast forward feature would be really cool as there are some parts of the games that take too long to get through (ex; when boarding into a levBus in BN6, you're forced to watch the whole animation where you see it come in and leave and there is no way of skipping it)

-as of writing this review, there is a bug where the screen slowly dims while playing BN5 and it carries over to any other game until you reboot the collection (may no longer be valid in the future)

Aside from these complaints I still highly recommend this legacy collection as these games have a battle system I wish I would see more in games nowadays (yes I know one step from eden exists but I want to see one that doesnt feel so fast paced). I still remember the days of playing BN4 red sun and completing this game to oblivion where I have beaten this game about 40+ times as it was the only GBA game that caught my interest the most. The Battle Network is the Series that got me into the whole Mega Man franchise and I truly hold these games dearly to my heart. Yes there may be lots of flaws especially for games like BN4 or BN5 which have many flaws but at the end of the day these games still have some great moments. Thank you capcom for bringing this back and I hope to see a legends/starforce collection in the future.

(oh yeah now I gotta beat the other versions of these games for 100% achievements woooo yeah idk who decided this was a good idea but if it means I get to beat BN4 again 3 times then Im in for another ride. leg's go lan!)\t\t\t\t\t
