\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, please hear me out, My Girlfriend and I have played every single Observation Duty games, and also all fan made games that include the same spot the difference concept. But I will be focusing on the Observation Duty Series ONLY. 1-5 were incredible, they were very fun and difficult, we lost many, many times, and that's what made the game so fun. Every hour in game felt like a goal, and we both had to be on our toes the whole time, trying to memorize and spot the smallest of changes, from shadows, to lights, to an extremely small silhouette of a man waving at you from the backyard. Every time we loaded into a map, anomalies would be different, very rarely we would get the same ones again, which made it more challenging to beat. Now, let's talk about 6... This game is a downgrade, this game has some overall improvements in color, graphics and even sound effects, but gameplay wise, it was boring, predictable, and has absolutely 0 replay ability. Also, this to a lot of people might not be a big deal since its still not that bad but, this game is $12, you could literally buy 1-5 and still have change, and I can assure you that by getting 1-5 you will have at least 10 hours of fun. Or, you can get this one, and beat every level on the first try and then uninstall it after 2 hours of boring gameplay. Please Developers if you read this, please listen to your fans, this series started off on the right track, this series literally was the start of the spot the difference horror genre in gaming, but after this game, the other games are better. If you want to make the game this easy and boring the least you can do is 2 things, 1. Add difficulties, so that your hardcore fans can have fun and experience the real Observation Duty. And 2. If its gonna be so easy, why not punish players for wrong reporting? I can basically spam report as soon as I see the warning and proceed to completing the level. Some Anomaly interactions were very cool in this game, but lets be honest.. What happened to all the naked creepy people doing weird inappropriate things? We loved that, it was random, and creepy and made us feel uneasy, now all we get is silhouettes with glowing eyes. Also, the amount of anomalies in the game is impossible for me to know having beat each level on the first try, But I did watch some streamers play the game and they got the same anomalies I got, seems like there aren't as many as in other games. This might all seem a little harsh but its coming from a couple of long time fans of the series, we look forward to every new installment, and this one was a let down. We really would love to see these games continue and be successful and to see that there are improvements.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has been a staple and ongoing thing in mine and my boyfriend's relationship and we enjoyed each part of the series thoroughly. We've been here since the first game, and have constantly been waiting for more! We finished the 5th game the week it came out, and have been waiting patiently for a year for the 6th game. That being said, here's a short review:

Liked:
- Much more refined than other parts of the series and I would suggest that any new players start here.
- Better reporting system than the previous games!
- Addition of some lore! Hopefully expanded in the next game? ;)
- Great new maps that all made sense with their layout and selection of furniture/objects. Some of the older games had random objects just thrown around the room to confuse players.

Disliked:
- Overall difficulty is lower than other parts, as only one of the maps gave us any difficulties. It felt like the older games have trained us to look for the smallest changes, like a cup switching the way it was facing. In this game, the cup would just disappear with everything else on the table.
- The new reporting system seems to lower the difficulty of the one-shot intruders, since you no longer have the stress of opening the report menu and correctly selecting the anomaly as well as the location, instead, you can click on it.

Would love to see a new difficulty added to the game with more subtle changes; something to master. Maybe in the form of a DLC with already existing maps. All in all, great job, and we hope to see more!!\t\t\t\t\t
