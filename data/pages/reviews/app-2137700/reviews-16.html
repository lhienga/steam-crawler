\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished the first level. This was a nice surprise on a holiday weekend.

If you're a fan of the series you already know you're going to like this game, so just get it. If you're new here, this is a spot the difference game where you are a paranormal camera operator. When something changes to the scenery you have to report them for correction. If too many things have changed without being reported the game ends. The difficulty ramps up throughout each level as the timer gets closer to 6am. Often times the changes will be creepy/scary....or dangerous

1. Loved the addition of music during the final hour, that really increases the tension
2. Thanks for adding backstory to the levels for some world building and creepiness factor
3. Not the biggest fan of not being able to blanket report missing/moved objects. Those were good saving throws whenever you know something is wrong but couldn't quite figure it out. But, it could be argued that it adds to the difficulty level.\t\t\t\t\t
