\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst game of this series I've ever played and I had a fun time.
This game is basically Spot the differences with a sacry twist. Not all horror elements land and a few are honestly pathetic - especially in the first level - but when the clock is ticking and you're frenetically switching between camera views and trying to dissect every details as fast as possible you become way more susceptible to the ... Unexpected. The game pushes you to open yourself to uneasiness and exploits that opening unpredictably.
Play in the dark, don't expect to be so scared you'll scream and you'll have around 6 solid hours of fun. Double or triple that playtime if you want to 100% the game on hard mode (the time multiplier depends on how close you are to our monkey ancestors on the human evolutionary tree). There are a few events I find annoyingly punishing but the game as a whole is fair.
I'm genuinely curious to see what the devs are going to do from this point on. Will they improve this formula by expanding on the lore and the mechanics ? Will they stagnate until the series just fade into obscurity ? Or will they just move on and end the series on this (high) note ? Only time will tell.\t\t\t\t\t
