\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor the comments complaining about the difficulty, and if that fact scared away anyone: don't fret. Hard mode has been added, like it had been announced on release.
On my part, I have no complaint on the game now that Hard mode has been added. The game became more available for people who wanted to give it a try but thought that it would be too difficult for them, but for old players like myself, hard mode is just as hard as it was before. At least I think so.
The graphics are better, the little music playing at 5am ads to the stress that we all feel when we know that there's only one hour left and we're not sure that we might make it.
The new report system is also a plus for me. Easier to report simple anomalies, still keeping you on your toes for certain anomalies with 2 ways to report them.
I still haven't finished the game with the hard mode added and am still raging on the hospital level on hard mode! Lots of fun.
In general the game is even better, do not hesitate, especially if the difficulty was the thing that made you hesitate!\t\t\t\t\t
