\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSTUB review::: I will update this review and write a proper one when I have the time, but for the time being I at least want to give a heads up on whether it's good or not, so...

It's great! I recommend it. Had a lot of fun playing it with friends. Netcode is okay, but it should be MUCH better.

It's a lot like Command & Conquer Generals; every 3rd match desyncs if there is at least ONE AI player involved.

HOWEVER, unlike Generals, this game at least saves the game and allows you to reload it with the same players in a new lobby.

It also crashes randomly, especially when not using standard AI (which sucks because mod AIs are MUCH better and MUCH more interesting), but if it was a multiplayer match and not everyone crashes, ''at least'' that person can make a save and rehost the game, but...

STILL: this should NOT be a thing. Those are WAY too frequent for a REMASTER. These issues were in the ORIGINAL game and plagued it a TON and there is NO way they couldn't have fixed this.

Other than that it's decent. Big amount of mods and custom missions and even campaigns.

Like I said, a stub :D I will write something better at a later point.
