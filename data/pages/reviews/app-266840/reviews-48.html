\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAge of Mythology alongside with the expansion pack The Titans is an absolute classic sitting right next to the iconic Age of Empires series. That alone would make me suggest that you give it a go and play it, if you're a fan of the genre. (I will not talk about the later Tale of the Dragon expansion pack, for that you can see my (not so stoked) review on its own page.) Now, I can't speak objectively as I am full of nostalgia but I'll try.
The campaign has a nicely written and executed storyline, the graphics are about what you'd expect from a game that old, even if reworked a bit but the building and unit designs are really pretty. Gameplay-wise it is probably not the best real-time strategy but I don't know much about the genre and what is considered a plus and a minus there. I also feel the unit pathing is a wee wonkier here than in the original but that might be just me. Overall, given I've bought the game twice - once years ago on a CD & once now - I would say it is pretty worth it.
There are no achievements in the game itself and the ones here on Steam could use a little bit more creativity as they are very (VERY) grindy.\t\t\t\t\t
