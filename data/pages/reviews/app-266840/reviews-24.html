\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAge of Mythology is a great RTS game from the early 2000s. Unlike Age of Empires/Kings the units for each faction are completely unique and have their own mechanics for gathering resources (and/or favour). It's best feature is the option to choose (in game) from a pantheon of demi-gods, each of which gives a different "god power" as well as specific "upgrades/technology" to research, which in turn gives players maximum freedom and allows for multiple adaptive and unexpected strategic options. Overall I highly recommend this for anyone with an interest in mythology or mythical creatures.\t\t\t\t\t
