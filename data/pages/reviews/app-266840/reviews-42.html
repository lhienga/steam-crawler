\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI LOVE Age of Mythology, but this version is pure garbage.

Firstly, there's a very common issue where the game crashes on startup. For some reason this is widely known but was never fixed: https://steamcommunity.com/app/266840/discussions/0/558755529996982698

Secondly, the multiplayer is horribly laggy and prone to crashes. Seriously, just check the Steam forums. This is a straight up regression from the original game which worked like a charm.

Thirdly, despite the "improved visuals", many of the menus have not been properly updated. For example, some of the loading screens do not fill the screen, and the god portraits in the multiplayer lobby are incredibly blurry and pixellated. While many may be content to overlook these smaller details, it just screams "lazy" to me.

Overall, this version adds nothing of value to the original game, and actually makes it significantly worse in many ways.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcellent, somewhat underrated RTS game with a fun campaign. I like this game a lot more than Age of Empires 2 which some might consider blasphemy.

+ four races with completely different playstyles
+ tons of deities to choose to worship, all of them add great depth to the game
+ awesome myth units and powers
+ awesome campaigns that are around 30 hours long total
+ streamlined, QoL features over Age of Empires 2 which is a big plus in my book
+ hero units are interesting and well balanced
+ units visibly change with upgrades
+ awesome myth units with unique powers
+ mythology is just very interesting and i think it was implemented very well, in a somewhat balanced maner
+ relic system is interesting enough and a definite upgrade over Age of Empires 2
- god powers can only be cast once unless you play as Atlantis
- pretty expensive for an old game, buy during sale
- pathing is pretty bad, sometimes your units get stuck, units dont move nowhere near as smoothly as in Age of Empires 2.
+- game runs very well but i got my save file corrupted during the last mission in Dwarf campaign. Mind you i used the same save file for all the campaigns, everything worked well after restart.\t\t\t\t\t
