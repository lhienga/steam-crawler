\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a classic of the early 2000s, a staple of the genre, and for some, the pinnacle of early RTS. This re-release contains both the original game as well as its expansion. It complements these two with an additional campaign, that's honestly best ignored and forgotten.

The positives are mostly all intact: fun voice acting (with much of the cast admitting they found random words in libraries and speaking them phonetically as-is), the story is engaging (even if at times very early 00s) and the variety and options in the various people you can play as is salivating. The game creators really put a lot of effort into a cohesive, interesting game world with an abundance of options and incredible opportunities for smart play.

Unfortunately, that was the original creators. The new studio that picked this up hacked up the AI, making some of the most interesting and well-designed RTS AI, years ahead of its time, a limp, weak foe that is trampled easily. Their own campaign, Gift of the Dragon, is a glitchy, unbalanced mess, managing to squander the incredible opportunity of a far east campaign setting and different mythology to explore.

The original's flaws are also still intact: the story is engaging but sometimes nonsensical, the characters are flat and given little to work with. The graphics are dated, and though charming might still put some people off.

Still, if you get this on sale, then you're in for a treat! Relive your early RTS gaming days, or try out a stone-cold classic for yourself.\t\t\t\t\t
