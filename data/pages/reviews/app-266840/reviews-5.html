\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're tired of the same old historical strategy games where you just send troops marching across the map, then Age of Mythology is your divine intervention! This game isn't just about conquering lands; it's about conquering the hearts of gods and mortals alike with its quirky charm and epic battles.

First off, let's talk about the graphics. Sure, they might not be as cutting-edge as the latest triple-A titles, but who needs hyper-realistic landscapes when you have mythical creatures duking it out onscreen? Watching a giant Kraken rising from the depths to wreak havoc on your enemies never gets old.

And speaking of mythical creatures, Age of Mythology has them in spades. From fire-breathing dragons to lumbering giants, every unit feels like it was plucked straight from the pages of ancient folklore. Forget about boring old tanks and infantry – here, your army consists of minotaurs, centaurs, and even the occasional flying pig (yes, really).

But it's not all about brute force – strategy plays a huge role in Age of Mythology. You'll need to balance your resources, manage your economy, and outwit your opponents if you want to come out on top. Of course, it helps if you have the favour of the gods on your side – just remember to sacrifice a goat or two for good measure.

And let's not forget about the campaign mode, where you'll embark on epic quests across the ancient world. From battling the forces of darkness in the depths of Hades to facing off against the mighty Titans themselves, there's never a dull moment in Age of Mythology. Plus, with the Extended Edition, you get all the expansion packs thrown in!

In conclusion, if you're looking for a game that combines strategy and mythology, then Age of Mythology is the game for you. Just be prepared to lose track of time as you build your empire, summon your gods, and unleash chaos upon your enemies. Trust me, Zeus himself would give this game two thunderbolts up!\t\t\t\t\t
