Though the campaign's difficulty might be underwhelming for more mature audiences (or people accustomed to these kinds of games), I would still really recommend it, as the story is fairly entertaining. Also! The god powers? Mythical units and heroes? Titans?! The Easter eggs?! Oh Canada?! Yes please.

Bonus: Units have a button that explains who or what are they in their respective mythology!

Very recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
