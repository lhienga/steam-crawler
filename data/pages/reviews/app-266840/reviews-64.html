\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProstagma. Etimos. Skipan. Proseche. Vulome - the words that I still remember from this fantastic game, the game that marked my childhood. I had only great times playing Age of Mythology.

I don't remember exactly when I started play it, but I think it was when I was 10 years old. When I saw the trailer I was impressed, when I saw the cyclops, the centaurs, the Minotaur, I couldn't wait to play the game.

Did I have a favorite civilization? Yes. All of them. I will start with Greeks - my favorite God is Hades. When is about Egyptians, of course, I pick Set. Norse? Hard choice, but I'm with Loki. And finally, Atlanteans. Is simple, Kronos all the way. Oh, I forgot the Chinese? I'm not impressed about it. Skip(an).

The campaign is great, with many obstacles and objectives that sometimes are difficult to overcome. Arkantos is a heroic Atlantean admiral in Age of Mythology and the main protagonist in Fall of the Trident.

Even if I do not like so much the Extended Edition, because sometimes is buggy and laggy, I still enjoy Age of Mythology. It will never be boring playing this game.
