Where history and mythology collide in a battle of epic proportions
Age of Mythology: Extended Edition is a remastered version of the classic 2002 real-time strategy game that continues to be a beloved title for many players. The game offers a unique blend of traditional RTS gameplay with elements of ancient mythology, creating a truly immersive experience.

Campaign
Each campaign offers a unique experience as players take control of one of these civilisations and interact with the characters and gods from the mythology. Players must complete a series of missions with their own objectives and challenges to progress through the story. The campaigns provide a good introduction to the game's mechanics and allow players to familiarise themselves with the different units and abilities of the civilisations.

Gameplay
The gameplay is divided into several segments: the campaign, resource gathering, building and technology, summoning gods, and Multiplayer mode. These segments offer players a unique and engaging experience to legally commit war crimes.

Resource gathering, building, and technology are the backbone of the game, as players need to gather resources, construct buildings and research technologies to improve their civilisation and gain access to new units and abilities. Summoning gods is also an important aspect of the game, as players can worship one of the gods to gain access to powerful abilities and move into the next age.

Art Style
One of the most notable changes in the extended edition is the improved graphics. The game features detailed and realistic depictions of the four civilisations. Their architecture, units, and gods are all designed to be true to the historical and mythological sources they are based on.

The environments are detailed, with lush forests, deserts, mountains, and other terrains that are true to the regions on which the civilisations are based. The game's art style is rich in colours and textures, giving the game a vibrant and immersive feel especially given the game was made in 2002.

The units are designed to be historically accurate while maintaining a fantastical element. The gods are also designed to be true to their mythological counterparts, with detailed and impressive designs that reflect their powerful and godly nature.

Conclusion
Overall, Age of Mythology: Extended Edition is a great remastered version of a classic game that offers a unique and engaging experience. It's a must-have for fans of the original game or anyone looking for a fun and challenging RTS experience with an ancient mythology twist. The game's art style and the attention to detail in the units and gods designs make it an even more enjoyable and immersive experience.\t\t\t\t\t
