\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere's something you gotta understand. I absolutely LOVE this game to death. It is easily one of my top favorite games of all time and would recommend it to anyone who's ever tried or even looked at an RTS. The game has an amazing campaign that I constantly go back to after a few years. The gameplay is still very fun and holds up to this day.

HOWEVER

I say here that I would recommend NOT picking up the game as it is now. For 2 reasons. For one, a Remake for this game was announced a while ago. While no date was given yet, I would recommend waiting for that. But depending on how you want to experience it, you might want to play this first and see how the Remake holds, that's up to you.
The sond thing is the current price. This game originally came out in 2002, this game is now over 20 years old. For that time, I'd say the game has aged very well in my opinion. The graphics still look fine and the gameplay and story is still very good to this day. But for over 30$ when Age of Empires 3 is only 20$? That makes me wince. It makes very little sense... Personally I would say it's worth it for the campaigns and stuff, but that;s just me saying that as a fan who loved this game as a teen and still do as an adult. But for a game that's now over 20 years old and its most recent DLC being very bad... I would highly recommend you instead get this game during a sale. I would still think this game be wroth it if it was at only half the price it currently is.
tl:dr Game is great but game is old so its price is too much. Wait for sale or Remake.\t\t\t\t\t
