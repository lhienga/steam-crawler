Myths coming alive

In the masses of the real time strategies this one is my favourite. A simple idea of having major cultures showcase their legends makes all the difference in the world. Moving through the ages you uncover the creatures of Greek, Egyptian, Norse, Atlantean or Chinese mythology. Along with them you have a vast variety of infantry, cavalry and artillery, so you will definitely will not get bored.

The original campaign is massive and extremely well done. The titans expansion is a little bit weaker in terms of story, however, it adds an amazing Atlantean nation. On the other hand, the last expansion seems like a lazy attempt to skate on the glory of its predecessors - poor execution that made me wish I didn't see it.

But the main dish is random maps that you can play against your friends or random people on the net. Challenge yourself with unpredictable moves and strategies that shock the fiercest warlords of all time.

Memorable soundtrack along with decent graphics makes this game an obvious choice if you like strategies and want to have a good time.\t\t\t\t\t
