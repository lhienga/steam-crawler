\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBarely recommended. Yet another game, which is fine but not anything more.
I liked Age of Empires a lot more.
This game has most of the same mechanics, but it's very glitchy, troop pathfinding is abysmal, the performance is some of the worst I've ever experienced. No matter on highest or lowest graphics, the game sometimes drops from smooth performance to like 10 FPS, and such awful performance drops happen pretty often.
The gameplay itself is solid, nothing amazing, but it is pretty fun, and its huge content amount makes this game not bad. Still, it just narrowly passes for being thumbs up for me, like most of the never before beaten games I've been playing recently...


7/10\t\t\t\t\t
