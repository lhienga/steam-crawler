\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh man, was I glad to see this one ported. No need for command line arguments to run in 1080p, The Titans + Golden Gift already bundled, and a new set of Gods? What's not to like? (The $9.99 DLC price tag I suppose, but it's $2.49 at the time of this review!)

While it's going to generally be the same game as the 02/03 versions, there are minor QOL differences such as the amount of villagers per resource being displayed in the HUD.

One big feature worth mentioning is the workshop which
A) allows people to submit their own content (Scenarios, UI, civilizations, etc.)
B) creates less hassle and removes the need for the classic modded and unmodded game folders

Furthermore, some mods such as UI mods work online allowing you to play in real time without worrying about desyncs. Personally, I have the original The Titans UI, logo etc. and I love it!

With all that being said this is being written post AOM Retold announcement. I snagged the game for less than $10 over the winter sale so it wasn't a hard purchase to justify. If you have the original, unless you really want to play online and can justify the usual $30-40 I would just wait until Retold.

Overall, AOM EX is a great game with admittedly questionable DLC. It revives the classic with modern graphics, compatibility, online play, QOL enhancements, and so forth. Given that AOM Retold now "exists" at the time of writing, it does make even purchasing the game a questionable choice. Let it be known this is not due to the overall quality but the fact it is a 2014 release. Time goes forth. With that being said, which God will you go forth with?\t\t\t\t\t
