\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is not worth 30 bucks. its actually absurd they charge so much for it.
especially given that the "retold" edition (which is a remaster of the game) comes out in the near future, and that likely means a $60+ pricetag.

when i bought this extended edition i expected at least SOME graphical updates and perhaps some QoL improvements, but nope, its like literally the same damn game that they made back in 2002.

things get even worse when you encounter numerous bugs or saved game corruptions.
the chinese campaign in particular is beyond atrocious, like its obvious this one did not get past quality assurance department... the very last cinematic in the campaign (which is supposed to be the most epic) is riddled with bugs.... scenes not finishing, the music blasting so loud you can't hear the character dialogue and the cutscenes actually interrupt and overlap each other as well... just an overall complete mess.

the game itself is fun when it DOESN'T riddle your ass with bugs on a nigh on constant basis, but i highly recommend not getting this game. especially at this ridiculous pricetag.\t\t\t\t\t
