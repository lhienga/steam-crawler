\r\n\t\t\t\t\t+ Cheap(ish)
+ Gliding has been replaced with normal flying, which is very welcome
+ Improves on most of the powers from Saints Row 4
+ Just as fun as Saints Row 4
+ Pretty much an updated Saints Row 4

- Pretty much an updated Saints Row 4
- Small selection of weapons, and cosmetic customization is gone
- Rather short (Have about 5hr logged with 87% completion ratio, could probably 100% in 6hr)

+/- You play as Kinzie/Gat instead of a custom character
+/- 2 SR4 powers are missing, although what you get works excellently anyway for the ~5hrs it'll take
+/+ There's a musical number

The only real gripes I have about the game are the same I had about Saints Row 4; "If there were no powers, this would be a glorified expansion". Only problem is, SR4 actually did have powers, so this is essentially a glorified expansion. But then again, it knows that, and it's priced in light of that, so no harm done. That and if you don't like the SR4 activities, you won't like the activites in this, because they're all exactly the same minus the flying time trials and a new one invloving catching falling people. If you liked SR4, this is an excellent way to waste an afternoon in anticipation of whatever is to come in the future. That said, it's up to you as to weather or not it's worth $15 for an afternoon.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRemember how Saints Row 4 was kind of a shadow of Saints Row 3? Basicly the same thing but just kinda 'less"? Gat out of Hell isn't even that. Most of the elements of SR3 have been stripped out. No customizing cars, no clothing options, no gang. No DLC. No, I tell a lie. There is DLC. One new weapon. One recolor of your wings. You don't even get to play the boss. So that charater you spent time crafting the image of in 3 and 4? You see him/her for a couple minutes at each end of the game. You play Johnny Gat or Kinsey. There is a co-op mode in case you want to bore a friend. I honestly think the game was never finished. The game play consists almost entirely of shooting demons, using powers to kill demons and flying around collecting orbs(souls) to upgrade you powers. There really isn't much story line. The wackyness is largely gone. Don't be expecting gimp-drawn cart gun battles. Best you get are demon fratboys and a Satan/Satan's daughter musical number. Seriously, save your money(I paid $5) and go replay SR3.\t\t\t\t\t
