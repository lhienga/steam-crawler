\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game because I was excited to kick some ass and fly around with cool wings in hell. I loved Saints Row 4 and how absurd it was, loved the neon look of the city, the powers, the running on the side of buildings, etc. I was horribly disappointed to see that in this game, hell is partially a really bland, retextured city version. The rest of the environment all looks the same. Rock, rock, lava, more rock. There was a lot of design potential there.

And it seems that the main mission is mostly just a bunch of annoying secondary stuff. Activities like insurance fraud and whatnot, which really suck because they're horribly repetitive. With a hellish spin. But still the same stuff. I thought hell would be more entertaining! Boring.

Also, this should have been a Saints Row 4 DLC, and it not being labeled as such really rubs me the wrong way. Might seem like a minor detail, but it's pretty bad considering 1/ the lack of original content and 2/ the lack of exposure and context at the beginning, for new players.

Save your bucks, this is not worth buying.
Unless you're a hardcore fan and Gat out of Hell is on sale.

TL;DR. Badly retextured Saints Row 4, less content, less fun. Pass.\t\t\t\t\t
