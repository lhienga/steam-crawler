DISCLAIMER: This is a full review, and NOT a first impressions review

Saints Row: Gat out of Hell is a game developed by Volition and published by Deep Silver and is the fifth (?) game in the Saints Row franchise and branches out of the Saints Row franchise and delves into the depths of Hell

The game has some really nice environments and the graphics are really well designed and laid out

The game does suffer with some repetitiveness at certain areas of the game but a fun game nontheless, however, Saints Row: Gat out of Hell just feels like a re-skin of Saints Row IV which was hugely disappointing

Saints Row: Gat out of Hell overall, however, is a fun game to play in short bursts and a good open world action game to play!
