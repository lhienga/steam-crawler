\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWill Earth be restored, after what happened in Saints Row 4? You have to find out on your own :)

Pros:
* New city with a lot of funny accents.
* New skills. Flying feels awesome!
* New activities. Each with its own background story.
* New friends, with good dose of grotesque.
* Same crappy, Goat Simulator like physics.
* Extremely well optimized, runs 4K solid 60 FPS on medium details on ancient GTX 770.

Cons:
* Balance. I seriously recommend starting on hard difficulty. You quickly become so overpowered that game stops being challenging on medium. You will be spraying infinite bullets from zero reload time gun with crazy range and damage while taking almost no damage back. Game does not force you to get good synergy between Arcane Powers and weapons in combat. And achieving something too effortlessly is not rewarding.
* New allies does not play any role in final battle.

IMO Worth playing. Nicely wraps-up whole series.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, my main criticism with this game is how short it is. I've played through this game twice, once blazing through and one 100% run and I have 40 hours in it. It's pretty short for a saints row game, but the price reflects that rather well.

As for story missions, there isn't much. It's mostly recruiting the homies and doing quests for them. Once you've wrought enough destruction in Hell, you can go challenge big daddy satan and have your choice of ending. Though there are multiple cutscenes sprinkled throughout the process. There's also little collectibles you can get that reveal the unneccesarily in-depth lore of Hell.

But the main attraction here is the movement. MY GOD. The flight is so freaking smooth. It's easy to control and stupidly satisfying to use. I DESPERATELY want this flight in a future full-length SR game because it's just so utterly fantastic.

And of course there's a bunch of new weapons. They decided it's hell so ♥♥♥♥ it let's just go crazy with it. There's a gun that shoots homing locusts, one that fires exploding frogs, a flaming sword that shoots projectiles, a chair that doubles as WMD, and more that I can't remember off the top of my head. ♥♥♥♥'s nuts.

All in all, this is the most fun Saints Row has ever been. It's a crying shame it isn't longer.\t\t\t\t\t
