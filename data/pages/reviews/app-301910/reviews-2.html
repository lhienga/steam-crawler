\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI’m going to start off by saying I wouldn’t consider this a ‘game’ rather an expansion to saints row IV. I feel like this should have been a dlc for SR4 given the fact it possesses a lot of similarities. If given the option I would have opted to give it a ‘mixed’ recommendation, however I have opted to recommend it as I found it fun and enjoyable.

As others have pointed out it can be completed in around 10 hours and the story has very few missions (majority of the missions are side quests). There is no soundtrack which I’m guessing is by design since it is based in hell after all, so I’d recommend putting your favourite music on when you decide to play. I think the reason it was so short and lacking so many features was down to the concept of being set in hell. So where it would have been nice to be able to have character customisation I feel like it would have lost the charm it was going for.

Overall I think the story is okay and the 7 Sin weapons are actually quite fun to use, especially the ‘sloth’ armchair mini gun/rocket chair. If you’re not a fan of musicals then this could very well be hell for you!

My final take on this is if you do want to play it, I would recommend to buy it on sale as it is something quick to play if you have a library full of games and ‘nothing to play’,\t\t\t\t\t

The powers are nothing special almost straight copies from SR4 with now the addition of flying which is actually super fun but would be alot better back in the map of steel port instead of hell. It's sad to see what this series has become especially with Agents of Mayhem and the Saints row reboot they should have stopped here or at the very least stopped after AoM.\t\t\t\t\t
