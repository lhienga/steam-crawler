\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe developers were truly desperate with this one. The story is practically non-existent and too short. The gameplay itself is more entertaining than SR4 but falls flat with similar issues such as repetitive missions. Then there is the crown jewel of artificially stretching the game by adding a ton of pointless challenges which you have to complete to get all achievements. I was done with all the main activities within 4 hours and was left with nonsensical challenges such as "Get X amount of kills with weapon Y". And the required amount is always in the 3 digital area just to make it more tedious.

Despite completing all challenges I was still not done. You see, there is an achievement in this game that requires you to play for 20 hours. The problem being that I was done with everything in less than 15 hours, meaning I could do nothing but idle and wait to be granted this mindless achievement. Another problem was the game crashing on me if I left it on for too long, so I couldn't just leave the game running while doing something else and instead had to monitor it frequently and save every time 5 minutes passed. The developers were even too incompetent to give you an option to replay certain activites which is essential for another achievement.\t\t\t\t\t
