\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSaints Row: Gat out of Hell is a standalone expansion to the excellent Saints Row IV, that although very similar, is a bit disappointing in what truly matters here. The gameplay mechanics are very similar, however the game lacks customization and innovation. You still use a mixture of crazy weapons and superpowers, however most of them are repeated, lack creativity or aren't fun to use. The story is good, but very small. So, most of the game is a grind, between collectables, grinding kills and repetitive side activities. Perhaps the worst thing was the lack of music. Maybe it was expensive to license a full soundtrack, but even only 10 hell themed songs were better than none at all. Still, i had some fun with this game, and for cheap, i can recommend it if you enjoyed Saints Row IV and wanted more of the same mechanics, with some twists.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore you buy; no, this game is not worth €14.99. But on sale, yes it's worth it - still, it should have been a DLC for Saints Row IV.

This game is short, and there is not much story to it. However, the story feels well-made and has some unique moments and weapons with a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ of customization to it. While there are a lot to do in the city (activities), the activities are repetitive (and just a reskin of the activities in SR IV) and nothing new.

The map. I believe it's the same map as in SR IV, but you won't really notice it. It's so different so it bareley looks similar to the SR IV map at all.

Now, here is the big dealbreaker. If you've played Saints Row IV; this game WILL NOT WORK and WILL NOT be able to start a new game file. Apparently, they updated the save file format for SR IV, which this game has not been updated to work with. Which means, you will HAVE TO DELETE ALL SR IV DATA to be able to play this game! (how to delete SR IV game files: http://steamcommunity.com/app/301910/discussions/0/135507548123928865/ )

So to summarize: buy it if it's on sale and if you're a SR fan, and are willing to delete all your SR IV data. If you haven't played SR IV nor SR3, go play them first.

I'll have to leave a negative review until they fix this issue.\t\t\t\t\t
