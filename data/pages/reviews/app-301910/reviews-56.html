\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo now we have this standalone expansion for SR IV... I'd rate it as "mixed" but unfortunately Steam doesn't have this kind of rating so I must give it thumbs down. It's fun to play, I must admit, but it takes only 10 hours to complete and there's absolutely NOTHING to do later. It lacks a lot of basic SR features and for me it's really big dissapointent.

PROS:
+ Crazy idea and concept.
+ Musical.
+ Well done new city.
+ The weather is different depending on the district.
+ Activities (most of them are copied from SR IV but still, they're fun).
+ Upgrade system.
+ Really good optimization.
+ Awesome weapons and superpowers.
+ Audio logs and commentary.
+ Historical characters.
+ Lots of references to previous SR games.
+ Very few bugs.
+ Modding.

CONS:
- Really primitive storyline.
- 5 storyline missions. (!)
- Lack of Boss appearance.
- Very few characters overall.
- No radio stations.
- Small amount of weapons.
- No vehicle storage.
- No customization of ANY kind.
- No minigames nor text adventures (it wouldn't be too hard to put something like that, come on).
- Lack of easter eggs.
- Only ONE type of pedestrian. (!)
- No homies.
- They finally put Dex but it's done really sh*tty.
- There are five endings but only two of them actually don't suck.
- No weapon skins.
- Outro includes more storyline than the whole game.
- Kinzie and Gat don't have any differences except model and voice lines (also, you can't complete the final mission as Kinzie for some reason),
- Again, no mission nor cutscene replay.
- Again, no cribs (except Ultor Building).
- Again, pretty weak car damage system.
- Again, there's no way to toggle upgrades.
- The whole game looks like a poor overlay for SR IV.
- Literally nothing to do in the freeroam.

I rate it 5,5 out of 10. I find this game fun but way too short and unfinished. I know it's just a standalone expansion but for real, just take a look on Episodes from Liberty City. It shows that you can do a standalone expansion without cutting out literally everything. I just hope they'll put some effort in Saints Row V.\t\t\t\t\t
