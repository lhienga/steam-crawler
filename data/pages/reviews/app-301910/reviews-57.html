\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot a bad game, and if you just have money to burn, and want to waste a weekend... sure, go for it. But be aware it is basically a straight up copy and paste of Saints Row 4, but with far less content. They keep the super powers, and the flight and speed works almost the same way (flight is slightly improved). I saw no means of character customization, and completed the game in 26 hours. Most of that time was just screwing around and grinding for levels. You go on a bunch of "loyalty" quests as you did in SR IV, but they dont actually mean anything.

If you want to do anything more than casually stroll through and dominate everything in your path, play on hard, and make no special efforts to increase your power before a big fight. You'll be fine with the powerups you earn along the way.

Bottom line - Dont waste your time with this game when you can just get Saints Row IV instead. The only reason to get this is for what basically amounts to different skins and dialog. Should have been a DLC for IV.
