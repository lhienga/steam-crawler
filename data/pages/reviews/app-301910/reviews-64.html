\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSR:GOoH is an expansion of SRIV. You can play as Johnny Gat or Kinzie Kensington to delve into Space Hell and rescue those soul survivors who has been fell by the Satan. It's based on Solo or Online Co-op game and you can work together to do fun stuff.

The storyline is a bit of lacking that it doesn't give enough information to tell more about what's happening in the game, but I won't spoil it for you further more. Although, the cutscenes were pretty hardcore comedy, I must say.

In the gameplay, you are able to fly around with the hell wings across hell city for Free Roaming with Super Power Abilities and Boosts to do destruction. In order to upgrade it, you need to collect many soul orbs across the hell city and spend it. Even the weapon upgrades, you need to collect devil's money and spend it on vending machine for Stat Boost. This can be boring for collecting many soul orbs as you can because you're just flying around the hell city to do things. Especially the hidden collectibles.

The missions aren't really a lot to do things because there are only 8 Main Quests with less cutscenes and all Side Quests are mostly be Activities which it can be really boring and repetitive! This has got to be the shortest and dumbest ideas that the devs really got run out of ideas for mission objectives with storyline and put all Side Quests into more crappy activities like only 5 types. Rockstar and 2K can do better for developing Storyline with missions than Deep Silver Volition. Can't complain about it.

Everything else all I can say is almost exact same as SRIV gameplay mechanics.

Pros:
  • Hardcore Comedy, including cutscenes
  • Free Roaming with Super Power Abilities and Boosts
Cons:
  • Only 8 Main Quests with a bit lack of storyline
  • All Side Quests are really boring and repetitive for doing activities
Overall:
5.5/10
It's a mediocre game that felt like there aren't a lot of interactions to play with. Saints Row IV was probably be the good one, but bit of boring to say as it felt almost the same as this one. Saints Row 1 through 3 was the best as it features a lot of quests to interact much like GTA Series. For this game, I would stay away from it unless if you're biggest fan of Saints Row Series, then wait for the sale to come.\t\t\t\t\t
