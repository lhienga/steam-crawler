\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally short game play, like really really short if you ignore the side quests (that also you can complete in like 20 hours or so). I cringed at the game initially, hated the flying mechanics, but as the game progressed a little bit with the upgrades and the powers, it got a little bit fun, but that was very short lived. What I love about the Saint Row series is its customisation which was not there, which really sucked. It was almost identical to Saints Row IV, with very small changes. Open world is very boring, and playing as Kinzie was very annoying with her cringe dialogues every now and then. Also wtf was that musical thing going on?!?!?!?!?!
Although the game sucked a lot but there was some things that I loved, like playing as Gat, he's totally amazing and cool, love the special weapons which were based on 7 deadly sins (You can only get them after completing all the side quests and challenges).
Game is overall very Meh and unsatisfying, the only thing I really enjoyed was the different endings, that's about it.

I'd rate it 5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
