\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs Gat out of Hell some quick dumb fun to kill some boredom? Sure. Can I recommend it? Uhhhh

Definitely not to people new to the Saints Row games as it's too self-referential imo.

And I'm not so sure I can recommend this to anyone who considers themselves a hardcore Saints Row fan either, and as for why, that depends on which camp you fall into. If you're of the belief that the series peaked with Saints Row 2 then the level of goof and gaff alone is already not gonna be your cup of tea anyway and don't even get me started on what they did to Dex. But that's... not the issue I personally take with it, having started the series with SR3, then SR4 and then SR2 (never got to play OG Saints Row as I don't own an Xblob 420). I love all three of these games tremendously, and yet I still feel like, fun though Gat out of Hell can be, it largely falls flat compared to the others, and imo lacks their replayability.

Some of the hellish spins on familiar activities and diversions I kinda enjoyed, but it could just be that I like those activities in the mainline Saints Row games anyway and, other than giving these activities and diversions hell-themed skins, they haven't done enough to distinguish it or make it stand out in any way? I dunno. What irks me is that, because the main questline is made up exclusively out of activities and diversions - no unique missions or setpieces - and the open world present here is, to be honest, kinda bland and samey... I'm actually disincentivised to continue playing post-endgame. That's not something you want to say, or hear, about a game bearing the Saints Row title.

I wish Steam had a "get it on sale, and ONLY on sale" button, because that's how I really feel about this game to be completely honest. If you just wanna fly around and play as Johnny Gat for a couple hours (and, I mean, come on, who doesn't wanna be Johnny Gat?) this is fine for that (I know Kinzie is here too but please, the game is called Gat out of Hell. I like Kinzie but in this game she's relegated to an afterthought 😐). I wouldn't get it full-price (especially not in Australia 💀), and I wouldn't go in expecting a long game, or one with much substance.\t\t\t\t\t
