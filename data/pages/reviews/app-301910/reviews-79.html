\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished it!

As a an avid Saints Row fan from the get-go, this game capitalises on its humour and mayhem mechanics well. It's, as other reviewers say, not as comprehensive as the rest and should be seen as a tag-along game rather than a stand-alone, but as one that succeeds in pushing forward the story (and should you choose, a happy ending for Gat :3) and adding more Saints into your life.

Pros:
Crazy weapons
MUSICAL SCENE ?????? YES PLEASE
Punch Satan
Punch Satan in the face
Johnny Gat and Kinzie save Boss!
Nice addition to Saints lore for a fan
FLIGHT

Cons:
Mission structure is random
Force teleports to do all missions save the last
Some games are irritatingly time-constraining
Flying enemies and shielded flying enemies are your BANE

I played this game to prepare for the new saints I've heard is coming, and it didn't disappoint overall :) Just not for the completionist, I feel, as it doesn't have enough story written into it to not bore you for longer than 30h? Love it still!

Saints for life <3\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSaints Row: Gat out of Hell is a sequelish/Add on game for Saints Row IV. As in Saints Row fashion it has good gameplay and humor.

The boss gets kidnaped by satan to mary his daughter during kinseys' birthday.Johny Gat and Kinsey must go to hell and help rescue the boss. In hell you must get satans' attention by causing chaos etc. While meeting old friends and enemies. The game also takes people from history like Black Beard, Wiliam Shakesspeare and Vlad Tepes a.k.a Vlad The Impailer.
The gameplay is the same as Saints Row IV with super powers (super speed, Projectiles etc.). But the game also brings new stuff like flying and creating minions that will fight for you. The flying alone is super fun and reminds me about Batman Arkham City not just with the flying but the game in general with upgrades and other stuff.
One thing to remember is that this game is a standalone expansion* so that means it's quite short. You can easily play this game in a coupple of hours. The humor in this game is the best I mean for gods' sake it has a musical number

Good
-Gameplay
-Humor
-Music
-Weapons
-Upgrades

Bad
-Flight controlls take some time to used to
-Too short

Overall this is my favorite part of the Saints Row franchise but it's too short that's the only major problem.
9/10\t\t\t\t\t
