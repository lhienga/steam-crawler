I finished the story and did all activities in the game. Overall I am very happy with the experience. The story is short, around 4-5 hours but the side activities are fun and add another couple of hours on to the game. Flying around the city with wings is also very enjoyable.

Overall I'd give this game as solid 7/10. I enjoyed the setting and it was nice to have an open world game that didn't require tens of hours to finish. I'd likely not buy the game at full price but on sale it's definitely worth a purchase.

My only complaint is that there are too many collectables. No game needs over 1,000 collectables and it was the only part of this game that I didn't play to completion.\t\t\t\t\t
