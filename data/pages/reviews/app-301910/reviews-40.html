\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow! It's the same damn game as SRIV, but this time you either play as Jesus Christ SuperGat or the one very kinky woman whose name I always forget. It's amazing how much more boring and ugly the setting is--all the people look the same (damned souls be damned, amiright?), the big guys you fight to advance the story (basically replacing the gangs from SR1-SR3) are all exactly alike, and the whole thing exists only to lampshade a franchise that already had a dozen lampshades stacked on it like caution cones.
The sole reason I went ahead and bought this expansion pack (it's not a game. you have to have IV to run it, it's an expansion pack) was because I heard that a character I loved who died in SR2 was referenced. I got all the way to the end of the storyline, 100% completion, and no sign of him. Off I traipse to the wiki... he's shown briefly in a single stillshot of a punch of characters playing volleyball or something, at the very end of the game.
On a related note, the "cutscenes" are all Jane Austen narrating over motionless drawings. Hooray.\t\t\t\t\t
