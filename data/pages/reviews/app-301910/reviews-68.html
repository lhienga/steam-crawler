\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI initially left a negative review for this game, but after a second playthrough I'm changing it to a positive review.

It's very short, the cutscenes are excellent, and the gameplay feels less like missions and more like the side quest acitivities from Saints Row 4. It looks great and is fun to play.

The storyline and humor are enjoyable. 5 different endings to choose from. They're all worth seeing, and they're on YouTube if you don't want to take the time to replay enough of the game to view them yourself.

Grab it on a sale - it's a great deal at 75% off but not worth full price due to such little content.

I'm a Saints Row fan and I liked this game enough to recommend it, although there are a few frustrating parts. When you're saving the twins - use your stomp power to disable their shields and stun those enormous demons that will have you furious if you don't know a strategy lol.

Unfortunately once you beat it there's not much to do, regardless of the ending you choose.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOn the outside Saints Row: Gat out of Hell looks like a fun and interesting expansion so a strangely evolving universe. But on the inside it is a bland and lifeless game that is tedious and annoying.

PRO
- Gat is back
- Interesting powers
- Hell reskin of city is pretty nice to look at

CON
- It's mostly just side mission seen in other games
- Direction can sometimes be a little uninformative
- Enemies can glitch out and not appear at all
- About 4 out of every 10 side missions would glitch out and not able to complete
- Kinzie is not a fun character to play as because she is boring as
- Achievements are bugged and some don't unlock
- It's very short (Story missions alone would probably take about 30 minutes)
- Feels like they attempted a musical then abandoned it after two songs
- Apart from enemies, nothing seems to be new in this game

In the beginning it was fun, playing with a friend and enjoying both the subtle and outlandish humour that the franchise has come to nurture. But after about an hour it felt long, boring and that we were playing Saints Row 3 and 4 all over again but with a dull, uninteresting story.
Even with the small price we paid, I think it was around 3, it wasn't worth it. Finishing it felt like a chore. You're better off playing 2, 3 and at a stretch 4.
3/10\t\t\t\t\t
