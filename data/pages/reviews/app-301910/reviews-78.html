\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't want to like this game or play it at first due you not being able to load your character from SR4 for some reason. I decided to play it for a bit anyway just to see how it was. However, "a bit" soon turned into a lot, and I ended up finishing the game pretty much on 100%. Besides not being able to load your character, here's what this game got right that the previous two games [and a lot of other open world games] didn't: 1) A "small" number of side missions. 2) The game in its entirety is just the right amount of gameplay. It isn't too short and it isn't too long. It's enough gameplay where I can finish it and just move on to another game. 3) Upgrading was far cheaper to afford which made for great progression. and 4) Obtaining the "special weapons" (the 7 deadly sins) was an absolute joy. It would be nice to see special weapons obtained in this way in the future.

The content cut from this game compaired to the previous titles are as follows: 1) Can't load your chraracter (the devs know about it and have yet to care to address it). 2) Limited number of vehicles (you fly everywhere so this is understandable). 2) No stores (except weapon vending machines). 3) No radio stations (as far as I could tell). and 4) Limited side quests (which is a good thing in this case).

Gat out of hell is a departure from the previous two games, but that doesn't mean that it's bad...it's just diffrent...and how much you enjoy this game initially all depends on how well you can deal with change. However, it is a good game in my opinion, and it deserves an opportunity to be played.\t\t\t\t\t
