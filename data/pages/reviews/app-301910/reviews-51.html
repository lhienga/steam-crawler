\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello Friends. I want to write about Gat out of hell. Alot of people consider this a stand-alone expansion for saints row 4. I feel it's a game in itself. The game seems very stable from my viewpoint, although the co-op partner i had in my game got disconnected a couple times. Regardless, i have done nothing special to run this game, unlike saints row 4. AS i said, seems stable to me.

As for the game itself, It features two heroes from Saints row 4, Gat and Kinze. I love playing as Gat. The gameplay is very similiar to saints row 4...you do missions for different people and collect "souls" to get super powers.

Recommonded as a great game. Ignore my hours, by the time im done playing the game, it will be much more hours. Note that i actually played this before on xbox360...but lost that copy in a flood. Thank god i got it on steam.

UPDATED: Sadly, i experienced the same bug i did in saints row 4. I am going to attempt to play with my fix for saints row 4 on this game and see if it works.\t\t\t\t\t
