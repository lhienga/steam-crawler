Gat Out of Hell is the last Saints Row Title currently. Needless to say, it has a LOT of issues, but is pretty fun for a 2 Player game. The story follows Gat and Kinzie after the Boss gets abducted to Hell to marry Satan's daughter on his Birthday. After meeting a old enemy who came to terms, they give them a cracked Halo that gives them powers (Because SR4's powers need to be put somewhere.) and then the both of them are on the chase.

Story : The story is fine. the only issue is that it lasts *2 Hours*. Yes, you hear me. 2 HOURS. There are a variety of issues too, how the story does not take itself seriously at all, and breaks into song sometime (I'm not joking) and full of dated jokes. The story gets a pass, but it's not that bad despite the flaws. - 6/10

Gameplay : The game is flawed, it's another SR3 Recycle and is missing a ton of things that Saints Row is know for, like a Radio Station (even though you can hear songs in passing cars which go unused), Car customization or even clothing that you can buy, but hey, there is a mod for that which ports over the Cheatmenu so you can press ctrl+ardown and open up a clothing shop or arup car menu for saved cars. With mods, it is fun, but the base game is missing so much. Of course you got new collectables and the gliding is replaced by wings you can fly around in, but the game is still feeling meh to be frank. But dispite all that, it still is fun to play, even though it feels cheap, and the new overworld looks neat, even though most of it's assests were taken from Saint's Row 4's pre-alpha days (before the DLC got cancelled and turned into the whole game), if you want proof, look at the tech-demo footage on the Saints Row Anniversary Stream - Day 4 on Volition's Youtube, there is a section that looks EXACTLY like the slums, but was untextured. Weapons look nice, even though some are retextures from SR4, they look nice and still got somewhat that Unf to them. And that Kill Dex feature? That's dumb. It's just wait for their icon to appear in the Overworld, they shoot at you, then you blow their head off with whatever gun you got, about 5 times. It's dumb and it's just a collectable. - 4/10

Visuals : The Visuals look great honestly. The engine got a update and the game looks nice and sleek. Enemy variety sucks though, as the same model is used every enemy and ped. Like, I wish there was variety. Throw some Humans in the downtown, like they are higherup so they can keep their look or add some different color demons. Just something. Other then that, Hell looks great. - 8/10.

Verdict : It's ok. It's not the best in the serious (that's reserved for 2 or 3) but Gat Out Hell tries. It fails on it's legs but gets back up on a cane. It sucks, but it's not a bad entry. Try it out yourself if you want, the game is pretty low spec, and the game has a lot to offer, but not much compared to the others. It gets a 7/10.\t\t\t\t\t
