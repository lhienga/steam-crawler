If you have played Saint's Row 4, then... this is the same thing but more condensed. you can finish the game in less than 3 hours. I just took 2 long break while leaving the game open and flew everywhere but the story area... The story forces itself on you even if you don't give a rat's arse about it. Sometime even teleports you directly to the story even if you're just flying around randomly on the opposite side of the map.
Speaking of the map... it's about the size of 1/5th of saint's row 3 or 4. And with the flying ability it seems even smaller. You only need 1 or 2 upgraded guns and 1 upgraded spell. run and shoot willy nilly silly for like 15 minutes and you're almost max level (which is 20) with 200,000 money.
Graphically it's kinda boring. Red lightning, red lava, brown and black rocky stuff, holes, craters, floating island, huge useless castle... but it's not interesting unless you like looking at bargain Doom levels design... which you can't even utilize in any interesting way.
But is it fun? It is, actually. If you want a good bout of mindless fun "a la saint's row" then yeah this game is super fun. I had a blast playing it.
I got it on sale at 5$ and it's.... a fine price for such a short game.\t\t\t\t\t
