\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: Love the Game. Worth it if you like survival games.

Pros:
-Hunt, gather, loot, and raid Hunters
-Single player or co-op modes.
-You can feel the difference between early, mid, and late game environment
-Can disable Hunters or toggle how they interact with you if you want a more peaceful environment
-Updates are consistent (map expansion, new items, etc.)
-Beautiful map/landscape

Cons:
-Combat/Hunting interactions are not smooth. You have to wait until the hunters stay still or an animal is running at you in a straight line to get a good shot.
-Looting random boxes is a weird concept. Would like to see a system for looting that makes more sense, but have no current suggestion on how it can change

Things I would like to see in the future:
- Rainwater collection
- Water purifier machine (so that you don't always have to boil water)
- More animals (wild/domesticated)
- Animal breeding
- Pets (dog, cat, etc.) that can help you hunt and can interact with
- Vehicles (ATV, Boats, Etc.)
- Sights/Attachments for Weapons
- Rogues should be enemies with you AND Hunters (it would be fun to see them fighting in the distance and then you can join in the battle and collect the spoils of war!)

Important Note: It is my understanding that this game was literally made and is maintained by one person, so ignore a lot of the negative reviews expecting it to be the exact same as a game with a ton of employees.
