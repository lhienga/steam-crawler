To list all the negatives of this game; Low spawn rates for the majority of items making it difficult to craft anything, difficult to kill enemies and janky combat.
This game needs a lot of work. Main reason being, you have to spend hours on end just to initially build a base, hours and hours crafting equipment for your character. Yeah I understand that this game was intended to be difficult but even on lower difficulty settings it's still such a grind, which for me just makes it a whole lot boring, just doing the exact same thing over and over again. Not to mention this game tries to be realistic but it fails, some of the crafting recipes don't even make sense and are just unnecessarily hard just for the sake of being hard, with little reward too. This game is fun don't get me wrong but I just wish you could muck around a bit more.
If you still want to pick it up, go ahead it's a good game but if you don't want to go through all the pain maybe just wait some more for a full release but on sale it's a fair price.\t\t\t\t\t
