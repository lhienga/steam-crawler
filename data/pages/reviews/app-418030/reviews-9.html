\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely challenging game, a lot of grinding to get anything done. Once you realize that you must run all over the map to collect resources (especially food plants/chickens at first) to survive, then it becomes a little easier. Also elevation is key for temperature factors, so start in the low lands. Finding the key trees that give you grubs also makes a big difference so you can go fishing. The game is seasonal as well, so each season, you want to focus on certain items. Personally in the hot months of summer is when I spend most of my time in the water fishing to stay cool and collect meat (make sure to have a fridge up and running). Have fun and enjoy the challenge.\t\t\t\t\t
