\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would recommend this game not for the faint of hearted survival fans, but rather those who want an actual challenge and are okay with exploring a vast world they do not understand. Why I state this is simply because of the mechanics of the game being unforgiving even in the lowest possible difficulty (which I might add as being easy). What is meant by that is the starting health is only 90, you spawn with one clip in solo and no pistol in co-op, the damage that is taken from an angered animal will kill someone in 2 to 3 hits and after the first one from bears you will start bleeding. The challenges met through weather are also a thing of considerable amount as unless you are wearing "decent" clothing you will not be able to work in the night hours or away from a fire. The hunters are also grueling making those who decide to challenge them their enemies and do revenge attacks (this option however can be turned off). Through the time this game has been out it has seen an incredible growth in what can be made, what can be done, and extending the range of the map this is a survival to keep an eye on if you like being a masochist towards your own pleasure and relaxation. I would like to point out that it can be very fun with friends from the amount of random occurrences that can happen.\t\t\t\t\t
