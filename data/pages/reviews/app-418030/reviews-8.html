\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't usually bother with Steam reviews (I think this is my first one ever) but I was annoyed with the number of complaints about this game I'd read. I honestly think they either didn't pay attention to what the game was before they bought it, or got frustrated and quit the moment things became in any way challenging.

The game is challenging and grindy, there's no two ways about it. But it's not grinding for the sake of grinding. Everything you collect has or will eventually have a purpose as you make slow but steady forward progress. One example, I was initially sad that in pursuit of cloth and feathers I had over-hunted and some of my meat had rotted, but found out later the rotten meat (and really any material you have too much of) can be recycled and used to help further develop my base. Loot crates that spawn around the world do make up a decent chunk of your resource gathering, and I can understand how that might put some people off. I personally don't mind it and found it constantly gave me a reason to explore.

The game is punishing, but not impossible. It does explain things at certain points to help nudge you in the right direction. Expect to die a few times as you get used to the game's mechanics and get a feel for the pace and what tasks you need to accomplish in the first few days of a run. Predators are plentiful and aggressive, but give you plenty of warning before attacking and you can easily outrun them before they eventually lose interest. You can even eventually fight off the smaller predators if you manage to loot enough ammo for your starting pistol. In an ideal world I would probably prefer them to be fewer and more dangerous, but I think it's fine as is.

Don't buy this game expecting the next Valheim or The Long Dark. I'll probably only give it a single playthrough for now, then maybe come back to it later after some more updates. But if you're looking for a decent survival experience that you can sink your teeth into for a bit, this game is a very good value at $14.\t\t\t\t\t
