Fire is life. Don't let your fire go out. Only grubs are for fishing, and only certain trees have them. Cotton does not allow you to harvest seed, but you can kill bears for cloth. Make a bed and bow as soon as you can. The bed helps you if you need to respawn, so you do not freeze and die again trying to find your base or body. The bow gets you chickens which give you decent meat, more feathers, and can also kill the wolves if you can evade and shoot several times. Good game, difficult with two players, extremely difficult on your own.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to love this game. I still play it to try and change my mind, but it's not great in it's current form.

The Good:
The map is plenty big and really quite beautiful. The seasons change and that's really amazing, I don't know any other game that does it. I like that there's separate protein/vegetable health bars to give a little more nutrition than you get in other games.

Neutral: A lot of the crafting seems silly to me. It doesn't seem realistic to craft glass, much less guns, wood burning stoves. refridgerators, etc. It seems easier to craft a pistol than craft a shirt. I'm not really interested in hunters (enemy NPCs) so I just have them turned off, no opinion there.

Bad: Wolves and bears seem to be more numerous than prey animals, they are violently aggressive, and on patrol. The care packages randomly spawning (that you use to craft most stuff) is a bit immersion breaking as well. Food spoils in a single day and it's not filling.

Now you may say, "Hey go easy, this is on early access!" I'd agree with that, except it doesn't seem to be making a ton of movement. In my mind I'd like to see sliders for how many predators, fruits/veg, packages, nutrition from food, or something of the like. The only update I've seen is a sneak peek at getting an ATV in the game. I don't know, I'm just not crazy about the direction it's going in.

Hopefully it does improve, because there's a lot of potential, but I don't think it's worth buying at this moment.\t\t\t\t\t
