\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSpawn in, hit a tree for a few wood. Bear sees you. Die. Spawn in. Wolf sees you. Die. Spawn in. Its night. Freeze to death because making a fire in this game takes finding VERY rare plants, and something called biofuel, that I have never seen. Also NO tutorial of any sort, so you end up mashing keys trying to figure out what does what.

Now that I have played it for a while, it is more fun than I originally thought. Still tough, but enjoyable. But there are a lot of idiotic things in it. For example... You can make steak and eggs, but the only way you can make JUST eggs is boiled.... I have a frying pan, why not fried or scrambled? And then there is the whole crafting thing where items take rare material to make just for the sake of using rare material. Doesnt many ANY sense to use that, but it does. Like to upgrade your shotgun you need 3 rope. And rope takes sinew, which means you have to kill several mountain lions or moose.

Now for the positive.. The game is really beautiful. Kudos to the artists! Underwater caves, waterfalls, snowy mountains. And there is quite a bit of crafting you can do along with the building aspects.

Currently the game needs more content, imo, and they really need to make some of the crafting a lot more logical. But so far its looking like a decent game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI did enjoy the feel of this game. Graphics are good, while i like a challenge i do not like how limited finding fiber was. It is required for everything and there is not enough of it. Also the fishing lure breaks way to easy. These issues made the game less enjoyable for me. I tried many different strategies, all ended the same way not enough fiber to make what i needed to survive. One more, 7 rounds from a 9mm pistol should kill a Pig. I thought at first this would be one of my go to craft games but after 9 hours of play i don't think so. Like a lot of people in my age bracket looking for a non zombie survival game allowing us to build our environment and sustain things, it started out in the right direction and then went off track. These games are fun when the resources match the challenge. There were times that a 5 minute walk around the map only gave me 2 fiber plants. it became annoying after awhile\t\t\t\t\t
