The magic in the game is that time doesn't exist IRL, you get lost, time zones out and is not relevant. It's a chill game, but at first play it can be brutal but this is easily overcome once you put in the hard yards, then it turns to mega chill.

There are constants, like the random locations of spawned loot boxes, the locked boxes are a treat if the games algorithm decides you're worthy! Graphics and sounds are great, super chilled during storms and wonderfully peaceful underwater. The caves are creepy sounding with the bats but oh so rewarding with the crystals which are so important for the upgrading of weapons.

We're still waiting on the next update which will include the long awaited ATV!

Highly recommended with multiplayer, building a community as we have under the Wussy Gaming / Just Trev banner with like minded folk on YT (yes, the bottom feeders).\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am about 29 hours into this game. I come from Ark and Conan and I have loved those games dearly but with a combined 4200 hours in them I needed new content. This game is wonderful as it is maddening. Starting with nothing, the game does NOT hold your hand in the beginning. You literally have no idea what your doing or what anything is. Upon entering the game is seems pretty basic, boring ect. I love the menu layout, things are pretty simple there and clean ( not like Greenhell and Forest insert massive eye roll) I love, and I mean really love the fact that resources are not just handed to you. It is NOT easy starting out or even nearly 30 hours in. The grind is real with this one. But also more rewarding. For an example, at 30 hours in I FINALLY finished putting a roof on my small 6x6 base. I just made a gun and killed a bigger animal. The fact that its not all handed to you really keeps my interest in wanting to play! I love the AI people and the fact they actually build their own bases. You never know when what of those little freakers is gunna run up on you! The game itself is beautiful. Hunting is fun, fishing is fun, building is hard but fun. Staying alive is still difficult.

So the not so good. I really dislike that things like cooked meat, liver, grubs, ect do not have spoiler timers when things like berries, carrots, and sea weed do. I really have no idea how long these items last and thats bothersome. Should be an easy patch for devs.

Sometimes I feel like my cotton is vanishing. Does this have a timer too? Who knows!

One other major things I find annoying is when I am trying to look online for help, there really is not much. I apparently had some kind of virus from not washing my hands after killing an animal ( awesome concept) and my health and stam were stuck at half full. I seen a weird logo but could not for the life of me find what it was and how to cure it. After hours of searching I found a reddit that was able to explain!

All in all this game has kept my attention, and makes me want to come home to play which is way more then what most other games have gotten out of me.

I hope they do more updates, maybe add more things to hunt, make animals tamable? ridable? Maybe add in water piping in singular pieces to be able to run water to your base similar to Ark water system. I would really like to see more building pieces! and a better way to get sinew!!! Should be craftable!!

Moral of the story- If you want to bang your head against your moniter for hours collecting trees, and other resources to only just survive, then this game is for you! Like I said- its fun and worth the money, but its also purely maddening! HAVE FUN!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite having fun with it I'd never recommend it. You really just need to accept the game for what it is, flaws and all. Its easier to just give it bullet points.

* The game is VERY grindy an tedious. Materials are randomized and crafting is expensive, so that upgrade you want could take 15 minutes it could take 4 hours because the contents are RNG based. The most commonly used materials are always the rarest.

* You better love mind-numbing repetitive and boring gameplay. The game uses bags and boxes as a visual for each node you need to farm. They spawn in completely random locations throughout the map. The contents of the bags/boxes are also completely randomized. You quite literally cannot go out to farm something specific. You just loot everything and cross your fingers you get what you need.

* The AI is crazy stupid and easy, so to make this more difficult the developer increases the amount of animals/people to the point where it is just a chore killing them.

* The developer has zero interest in what you think would improve the game. This game is for them and we're just along for the ride. There are the same multitude of justifiable suggestions in the forums over several years to the point bringing them up is met with condescension because they are actively being ignored and they know it.

* Quality of life mechanics are nonexistent.

* Even when you ignore the painful material farming, you are met with time gates at every corner. I mean that literally too, the game uses something called "Mass" to bottleneck your progress. Essentially its something required to craft anything, and until you are later in the game can only be acquired by waiting. Even in endgame, to create Mass quickly requires Energy, which can only be gained over time by building generators/solar panels/wind turbines. You'll need a lot of them before they are helpful. See the bullet point for farming crafting materials..

* Resource management is also a chore, and this never changes. Most survival games reward your progress by eventually letting you craft something that relieves some of this tediousness. This game does not.

I could go on but at this point if you still want to buy the game then its on you.\t\t\t\t\t
