\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game, but the balance is pretty far off. I've only played it for a little while, but there's a level of frustration creeping in. First off, the scenery and ambiance is really good this early in a game's development. The realisticness (?) of the crafting you have to do is good, as well. But here's where I start getting frustrated. Here you are, in and open wilderness, scrounging for resources...and there aren't any. I mean, they are very scarce. I'm running around, nothing but pants on, hoping to craft some cordage, or ignition sources, and the one scrub bush I need is so scarce, I can't get my fire lit. Look, I know how to light a fire without the specific items needed in this game, so we should probably dumb this requirement down a bit. Considering this bush is a primary ingredient for most things, yeah, make it more plentiful or re-tool the recipes. The other thing that really doesn't seem to jibe right now is that finding a source of nutrition is difficult, yet I'm surrounded by apex predators! Bears and wolves everywhere! You can't swing a dead cat by its tail without hitting one. What the heck are they feeding on? The number of each of these apex predators should be about 25% of what it is right now. Or lower. The prey items are more scarce than the predators; I've seen one deer, one moose and a few rabbits and chickens - and that's just not a balanced ecosystem. Granted, this is early in the development, but if you're gonna make it more realistic in one area, you gotta balance it out everywhere. I like the game and I look forward to the updates. Hopefully these issues will be addressed. For the money, I'd buy it again.\t\t\t\t\t
