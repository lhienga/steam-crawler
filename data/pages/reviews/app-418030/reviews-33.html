\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a few gold nuggets in a sea of .
I wish i could give it a good review, because i do love certain aspects of this game. But it completely fails as a whole.
Game is full of problems and poor design decisions, that developer simply does not address. At all. Instead he marches to the sound of his own drum and just adds stuff that nobody asked for. All while various suggestions and fixes proposed in community section went completely ignored for years.
"Feature Suggestion" part of Community Hub, second biggest section after "General Discussion", is completely pointless. Nothing anyone writes there will ever be addressed. It is just for show.
Game has ton of issues and QOL problems, with a lot of suggestions being made. None of them have been ever addressed. None of building players asked repeatedly have been added, instead we got an elevator. Something that absolutely nobody asked for. Over past 4 years there has been almost no QOL additions made. Just look up how many times scopes have been suggested for example.
That would not be too bad if modding was an option. But dev intentionally encrypts all files. So he neither fixes/adds things community wants, nor he lets others to do so.
So overall, good idea, pisspoor execution.

Take for example just released ATV update. It takes ~4 months for an update and we got something that adds no real benefit to the game.
There is literally no point to have an ATV in this game, as game is structured in a way that neither solves any of the existing problems, not it adds any QoL improvement. It is purely for "I have a wrum-wrum brrr-brrr in my garage" checkbox.
Does it help with scavenging?! Not really, due to how lootbox spawning is organized. Driving around you will miss most of them and even when you do spot one, then you have to dismount, collect and then mount again. There are no large cashes spawning in different parts of the map, that would justify the need for long trips.
Does it help with hunting?! Again, no. Bears and wolves are everywhere, there is no centralized spawning for deer, moose, wild boar. You just come across them from time to time. Maybe cougars, as they only spawn in one region, alas big one. So if there is benefit for hunting, it is a very marginal one.
Mining!? Nope. There is no point in driving half-way across map to mine couple of small deposits that may not even spawn.
Fishing!? Really!?! Who builds base so far from nearest water source that you would need an ATV to get there!?!?
Maybe cave entrances, as those are rather far between and can take awhile to get to. But again benefit is fairly marginal.
And you cant even drive this thing fast as terrain is mostly too rough for high speeds.
So we have new addition that solves nothing, adds nothing and in general is there purely for checkbox. Four months of development for nothing.
Id rather take gun customization, or reworked woodcutting system, or about dozen of other improvements, that would be miles more beneficial to the game, than this pointless crap.\t\t\t\t\t
