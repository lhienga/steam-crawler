\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSurprising amount of detail to this game. Between single player and multiplayer mode you really see the difference, in a video game setting, a single person survival versus multiperson survival situation can be. Single player the progress is slow. Having to balance your time between surviving and improving your base is a real challenge. In multiplayer you have a somewhat easier time improving your tech at a faster pace, but you still face the challenges of generating enough food diversity to thrive at times. This is one of the few survival game that has noticeable and impactful seasons. You find different plants in different seasons. Animals behave differently depending on time of year. Temperatures change. There are rain storms, thunderstorms, snow storms, blizzards... Leaves change colors and fall during fall and come back in the spring. Snow collects on surfaces during winter. Lakes and rivers freeze over in the cold. Eagles steal your hunts, Moose strike back, and bears are bears. If there's one bright sport it's that you don't have to contend with snakes and spiders. Overall Subsistence is a fun, but challenging, survival game.\t\t\t\t\t
