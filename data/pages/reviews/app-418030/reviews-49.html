\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is discouragingly difficult. It takes way too many mats and to make even the basic of basic things in order to survive (curing poison? good luck with that). The game is 90% find crates and supplies out in the wild. Hardly any foraging or creative crafting. You pretty much only survive if you find boxes. Wildlife will insultingly level if you die by their hands, but do you level for your efforts? Nope. You can survive considerably longer without water than you can without food - when in reality it's the opposite. You need a fire starter every single time a fire gets extinguished and that leaves you with either being graced enough to find matches on a random box, or four (seriously? four?) medicinal herbs to make bio fuel. I've read countless articles and watched several Youtube videos on this game. Fibers are really hard to find at times, the amount of lumber gathered on a single tree is laughable, and you waste more energy and food trying to catch your food after it takes an arrow hit and runs off than you actually get from eating it. Food drops from prey are laughable as well. Elitists will defend this game left and right. But it seriously needs some work.

I really want to like this game, as I enjoy survival games. But I feel like this is a game that doesn't want you to have fun.\t\t\t\t\t
