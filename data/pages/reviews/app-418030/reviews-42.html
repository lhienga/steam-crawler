\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing this game for almost 100 hours i finally have what i thing is a sum up of this game.
Off the start i found this game a real challenge, from animals to food and water. I love this game its great and has so much potential but i want to list some things that need some work.
*First-the amount of bear and wolf spawns is ridiculous even when the game is on easy they are everywhere. I feel if the player is on easy the spawn of said animals should be toned down greatly.
*Second-the grind isn't really all that bad late game but early game its exhausting and in my opinion it kind of steers new players away from the game with the lack of nails,and steel in the world and only being able to find nails it in loot containers really sucks and it really gets boring running around looking for said loot containers. And yes i know you can craft nails but it takes more resources to craft just one which sucks.
*Third-the amount of emptiness in the map is very lackluster, there being no POIs really makes the game dull and boring, and the closest thing we have to POIs is hunter bases. I would play this game so much more if there was abandoned cottages and cabins to loot and we all know for sure that a player is gonna try and take his BCU and put it in the cabin but you can put a barrier around said cabin to prevent this.
*Forth-i really don't have much to say about the lakes i think they are fine the way they are maybe adding barracudas or some sort of aggressive fish species to attack and to fish for is a great idea. Also i would love to see a boat to fish from added in the future.
*Fifth-when first playing to now being pretty good at the game i found myself dying to how cold it is during the night, even during the summer, nights get below 0 and it sucks. It really makes nighttime useless unless you got a sweater or heavy jacket to witch we know new players do not have. So i feel during the months March through September nights should not go below 0 but say warm enough to make night time resource runs. Through the months of October though February is when nights start to get cold, unless you put your base on or very close to the mountains is when its should be cold 24-7.
My final thoughts on this game are very positive i feel if your a new player to give this game a try it will have it negatives but i know this game has so much potential to be a great game. I will continue to play this game because its fun and i hope to see it grow into a amazing game.\t\t\t\t\t
