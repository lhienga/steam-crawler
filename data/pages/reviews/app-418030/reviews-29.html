\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played Subsistence for a while now and on most days I like it, although there are times when I rage-quit and uninstall the game but later install it again. This cycle repeats itself until the end of time (most likely). To say it frustrates me would be the understatement of the century. At the same time I find the gameplay interesting and challenging.
Buy this game if you like (sort of) peaceful, grindy survival games but do not if you want it to be easy. I have always played it alone and enabled hunters with revenge and periodic attacks so it does not get lonely because it is mostly you against a bunch of animals and the forces of nature.
I really like how the developers think of new items and aspects for the game but sometimes I do wish for some parts to be easier or items to require less resources. The latter is my only bigger issue with the game overall.\t\t\t\t\t

Seriously what is the developer thinking? Loot needs serious adjustment.

1. Kill a bear and only eat for half a day
2. Eat two whole chickens and still not be full
3. Kill a hunter army and all their guns, and armor disappear (Apparently they were shooting you with nails and an apple)
4. Build a floating empire
5. Cut down and entire tree and only get 5 logs
6. Die come back to life and still have the infection from your previous life.

Only to name a few.. Come on be a bit more imaginative with the loot system balance maybe even consider getting rid of the loot boxes and make crafting a more serious part of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
