\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start by saying I LOVE this game overall. I have a good amount of hours in this game and is honestly my favorite survival games. Coldgames has made a great game mostly by himself or actually by himself. I enjoy it so much that I did buy the pack for the visual changes to the guns or "gun skins" so that I could show my appreciation to his hard work.

That being said the last update was terrible when it comes to vegetables going bad. Fruits having a type of life expectancy bar is poorly done especially since we have been asking for years to have a bar on food and meats to tell us how long we have until it goes bad and we have yet to get it. I'm annoyed that fruits and vegetables go bad so quickly. Poorly executed solution to something that wasn't a problem. "Farming" if that's what you want to call it is one of my favorite parts of the game so I have many plant beds and I produce vegetables and fruits in abundance but that has been nerfed when there was no real problem with it. I do feel bad for the very early game people that don't have a fridge they can use to preserve their fruits and vegetables.

With that I am going to put the game down for awhile now. I have turned a blind eye and have made excuses for a couple past terrible changes but this one really took a lot of the fun out of the game for me. Hopefully this change either gets taken out or is massively changed.\t\t\t\t\t

Subsistence is a survival game with emphasis on survival, unlike others where survival is guaranteed unless you are inept. It promotes preparedness, planning and being indomitable. A difficult yet rewarding game that is a fresh change of pace in the Open-World survival genre.

That being said it is not everyone's cup-of-tea, which is understandable, but if you enjoy a set challenge with ever changing seasons, threats and an unforgivable landscape. This is the game for you.

If by chance you manage to survive to winter, only then can you gain prospects of forming a survivor's life. Good Hunting

I give it 10/10. Perfect in what is strives to accomplish with its own charm that sets it apart from other games.\t\t\t\t\t
