\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this early access in 2016, I have 2hr's total playtime over a 7 year period. I checked it out again today, and It occurred to me the Devs are actually still trying to develop this, so here's the issues as I see them:

1. The grind. It's too much. It's as though they're trying to copy Rust with a more realistic feel to it, but the 7 bullet limit for the 9mm pistol is ridiculous. Fix #1 give me my 15 shots or make fewer animal spawns in the world.

Problem 2. Animals are all but deaf, dumb, and blind. They're more like mines you stumble across by accident, then they kill you. Early game, they're a bane to your existence, and can cause rage inducing fits. They agro for no real reason when you chop trees, and won't agro when you loot a crate 15 feet away from them. Fix 2: Make the animals smarter, make them more scarce. You wouldn't see a wolf every 50 feet IRL, why do it in a game?

3. Hunters. Just why? I get wanting to make another enemy, but why hunters? And, why do they KOS when you haven't attacked them? Are they supposed to be poachers or something? Makes no sense. Fix 3: Give the hunters/poachers a backstory or lore. I don't know why I'm being attacked, and it's confusing.

4. Give better tools for harvesting materials. Chopping endless trees for wood is mind numbing. Fix 4: allow the player to craft a chainsaw/sawmill you place near trees and it does it for you.

5. Things disappear in the world too quickly. When I set a fireplace it degraded over the course of the 1.5 hrs I played between last night and this morning. I get wanting a degrading feature for server performance, but why when I have ONE firepit would the server see fit to take it from me? Isn't an IRL firepit a stack of wood, just like ingame, to which you continually add material? Fix 5: Put a limit on the amount of items a player can build before the oldest items have a degrade timer kick in, OR have the firepit degrade timer reset when you add more material to the fire. Same for buildings etc.

The Devs have put some work in this game, the sunrise and sunsets are gorgeous, it's a shame the game mechanics can't be worthy of their effort.
