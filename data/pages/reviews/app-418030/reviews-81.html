\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcited to see what this develops into as it moves further into the stages of early-access publishing. Right now, it's unbalanced and a little frustrating. Could be improved by some spawn-point management options (bedrolls in 7 Days to Die) and less carnivores. Really, honestly, this map is like the most carnivore dense forest I've ever seen and if you even sneeze in the wrong direction you get chased down by god knows what, bears, wolves, etc. If they wind up camping your house, you can wind up having to walk like, over a mile just to get back to where you spawn, as it is within a radius of where you die. This does mean, however, if you die while trying to return to your bag/base area, it will likely spawn you EVEN FURTHER away.

This is a simple fix though and the game has potential, would like to have some explanation of why there's random bars of "scrap" in the forest area, or what the crates with raw materials are being dropped for. Suffers from a current lack of story, but it IS early access, so that is to be expected.

Overall, has potential to be a neat title. Worth the money for me to support the creators and hopefully help the game get further into development where some of these things can be fixed.\t\t\t\t\t
