\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasic survival genre -- have to keep scrounging for food and water to not die. That's fine, it's been done over and over and over in other games, this one's no different. But shooting an animal at point blank range with 9mm pistol and doing little to no damage, while it kills me every time -- b*llsh*t. I empty the entire clip into an animal's face at less than 10-feet, and it lives and kills me.

And there are so many animals (wolves, bears, etc.) waiting to kill you -- the map is saturated to the point that you will have a hard time finding any place without these animals waiting every few steps...there's no reason a normal person would think the volume of mobs, even on 'easy' mode, is even remotely OK.

Trying to fish -- should be basic, beginner level activity -- if you haven't got the means to manufacture pistols, rifles, etc., nor ammunition, then fishing should be beginner level thing to get you by...but not in this game!! You can only fish if you harvest "tree-grubs" -- and on the entire map out of hundreds, if not thousands, of trees available to be chopped down for firewood and logs there are approx. 10 "special" trees that will each give you ONE GRUB. Approx. 10 magic "grub trees" ON THE ENTIRE MAP!!!

To fish one time you need to create fishing "tackle" along with a fishing pole/rod -- the "tackle" is the ammo -- one-time-use. So for each grub you find you can hope to catch one fish. Grubs, worms, or other types of fishing "bait" should be easiest things to find as beginner just scrounging to get by. Plant fiber, couple sticks and a bug should enable a person to keep from starving...but not in this game -- for some reason developer decided only ten grubs on the entire map -- then you wait for the trees to respawn.

Harvesting logs from trees is something we've all had to do in games of this genre -- start cutting down trees, get logs, make planks, start building some shelter...but in this developer's mind one plank requires THREE LOGS -- and nearly every tree gives only 3-4 logs. Wait, so you're saying one entire tree is needed to make ONE PLANK?!? Yes. Does not even make sense -- I can drop each of the three logs on the ground INDIVIDUALLY -- but to make ONE PLANK I somehow 'magically' glue three logs together into one plank. And this 'issue' has been pointed out to developer over and over, and ignored.

Basic, common-sense 'balancing' for this game is being pushed aside and ignored, while the developer caters to a hand-full of fan-bois -- creating 'diving suits' and hang-gliders and glass-walls for houses so one can have a nice 'view'. Really, are you serious?!? Glass-walls for your mansion IN A SURVIVAL GAME?!? But you can't be bothered to balance the damage output of weapons, nor look at availability of basic items like fishing-bait.

Do yourself a favor and spend your money elsewhere -- there are many other survival-genre games available that are way better.
Oh, and before the fan-bois show up to claim "early-access", this has all been going on for 6-YEARS!\t\t\t\t\t
