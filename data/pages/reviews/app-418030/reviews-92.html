\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSubsistence is an amazing game, especially with only having one developer. The amount of content already in the game is phenomenal. I don't really have any complaints about the game and haven't encountered any bugs. Updates are fairly frequent, and bug fixes are as well, which is amazing.

I do have some suggestions I'd like to make such as, more animals, that give different resources. or tweaking current animals to drop different resources. Such as bears dropping hide to create crude clothing that offers some warmth bonus in colder weather. Different clothing options with different bonuses. Some more types of weapons would be nice, maybe crude spears like sharpened sticks crafted out of logs and eventually being able to make a spear with a steel end that causes more damage. Basically would like some melee weapon options. Would also like to see maybe a crossbow as an option alongside the bow.

Another suggestion I'd like to make is an alternative fuel source other than just rendering animal fat, maybe add in an oil source in the caves and elsewhere that you then have to refine into fuel.

Also I'd like to mention that the lever action rifle would make more sense if it used 44 rounds and not 7.62 rounds.

Another thing I'd like and I'm sure many others would as well, would be some kind of story mode. Like how you ended up there, and your quest to survive while trying to either find your way out or signal some kind of rescue.
Something maybe similar to The Long Dark?

All in all this is an amazing game and I'm excited to see what other updates there will be.\t\t\t\t\t

- Woke up next to a bear, it leapt off a rock into my face and tore it off.... 10 out of 10
- Woke up next time to attempt to make progress only to get chased by a wolf into another wolf! We can be pretty sure
they shared a lovely meal.... 10 out of 10

After some major screw ups on my part and a bit of practice I started to realise this game is a slow burner, you need to
put time and effort in over a large period of time to really enjoy it and i love that about it, I'm getting my monies worth!
300 hours on and i adore this game, its very rewarding and once you get the swing of it you realise the stuff you worked
so hard to find you end up having boat loads and end up giving it away.

First of all you need to understand that this is a hardcore survival, things wont come for free or easily and you will die, you have to adapt and learn how to deal with threats, there is no shortcut to end game you have to grind! to many people are giving this game bad comments because its not an easy game (that's the point though) and i think that is unfair. There is a huge gap in the market for a great hardcore survival game and this fills this gap brilliantly! I also just want to point out that a fantastic solo developer puts incredible amounts of time and effort into this game, I didn't know this till i was told last week but hes working on it alone and constantly updating, making things better and adding content, just scroll through the recent events and announcements tab and see for your self. All in all amazing game well worth the money! Don't listen to the haters.\t\t\t\t\t
