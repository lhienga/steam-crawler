\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSummary:
Spend dozens of hours grinding for resources.
Admire the new content that you will never be able to play because you dont have enough time to grind resources.
Resources are mainly gathered by finding crate drops. Crates include a miniscule amount of resources.
So you have to wander the map every game day.
Wandering the map is dangerous and stressful all the time unless you play on easy mode.
Note easy mode does not reduce grind. You still have to search for loot crates.
Trees yield 3-5 wood. Wood is important for everything but also is a limited resource.
Ores are gathered by spotting "warts" on rocks. If you have a pickaxe you can collect a miniscule amount of ore per day.
Special resources like pearls are found by diving. Again an entire lake may only have 5 or so pearls. But it takes a dozen pearls to be useful.
You can spend a pile of ammo and difficult to find resources and attack a bandit camp and not get back enough resources to pay for the raid. oh. and next day they raid you and you have to spend more ammo to defend yourself.

This game is a frustrating chore of spending each day gathering very limited resources. Fighting cold. Fighting hunger. Defending from animals. Defending from bandits if enabled.

When you do finally get some gain or progress in the game its so lacklustre you regret all the work it took to gain that item. For example you grind forever to get a simple bow to defend yourself but then the bow sucks pretty bad and the ammo for it requires, you guessed it, more grinding of precious nails.

There are no creative or freeplay or experimental mode to test things out. Do you really want to spend two real life days gathering resources to test out the atv only to find out its not so good?

Anyway this could be a good game but Its way too time consuming. If you read the discussions and discord you will find that this is intentional. This game is not intended for the casual gamer or anyone who has kids or a family. Its a massive time sink especially if you want to progress.

Not recommended for casual gamers or people who like typical survival games. Its misleading to lump this with the survival game genre. Because its not well suited for typical casual players. Its more suited to people who have a lot of time and dedication. Easy mode, (which i found to be too easy), only makes enemies and survival easier but there is still the daily grind to get 10 nails or spend all day diving to get 5 pearls. Turning off bandits is ok for learning the ropes but it gets boring and you still have to wait for resources to respawn.

I would change my recommendation if the game added a "weekend player" mode where resources are not so hard to come by. At least make more settings configurable and disable achievements like most recent games do.

TLDR;

Anyway this is not recommended for the person who is the casual gamer at this time. Its recommended for people who have a lot of time and like a challenge and for the people who already have the game and love it.
