\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy this game! I don't play with hunters enabled because I like to gather up materials and build whatever I can dream of. Also I don't enjoy having to kill people to survive, so I'm glad there's a hunter free game play option. It truly is a game of subsistence because your survival depends on how much you do and strategy as well. The scenery is beautiful and relaxing (until a bear or wolf spots me), it adds to the gaming experience. Sometimes it does get a little mundane, until I think of a new building project.
I'd enjoy this game even more if there were more building options and items available, such as a fireplace with chimney, gate doors, curtains, antler decoration, and pot for plants. Being able to craft metal pipes and use to connect to an in home sink would be neat. Also more realistic snow cover, such as more snow on the ground after a storm and snow on roofs. Perhaps there could be clay available after a rain storm used for new building objects. Having the option to build with stone would be cool. It would be exciting finding rare items found in the lock boxes such as a clock, temperature gauge, or a calendar. Also having the option to make gloves, hat, etc to help deal with the weather and provide extra protection would be nice. Perhaps new environmental challenges could be introduced, such as a small forest fire occurs and the player has to clean up and replant the area.
Getting an update geared towards non hunter players would be nice to see. I have lots more building projects planned and therefore will be playing this game for a while. I'm excited to see how this game grows.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMore time played, more detailed review!

If you like survival games, this is worth the money. Truly. It's in the small details that it already contains. Thievey birds might interrupt your fishing. Animal behaviours changing based upon the season. And, btw, SEASONS. Temperature changes, visual changes, different plants growing, SNOW, you name it. You don't have biomes, you have a world, and that world changes based upon the time of the year. Love it.

I wont say it's perfect, it's definitely got it's quirks and frustrations, one static map, resource gathering can be an issue with the sheer amount of hostiles out there, sometimes you can struggle with getting out of the water.

But it's build system is pretty nice and relatively simple once you get used to it, you certainly don't (so far at least) feel like you're OP compared to the NPCs so it remains challenging to achieve your goals and certainly takes effort to advance. Give it a shot. It's worth it.\t\t\t\t\t

THIS game on the other hand has plenty of content to keep you busy for quite some time. It's VERY challenging, which not for everyone, is great for me as I love a good survival challenge. I recommend this game if you like a strong challenge and enjoy survival games...\t\t\t\t\t
