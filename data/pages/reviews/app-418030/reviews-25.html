\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I've played this game for a little over 26 hours now. And... I find it hilariously difficult and repetitive. There is NO end-game. There is NO surviving. You ARE gonna die. Multiple times. This game is VERY unbalanced. Even if you play on easy with no hunters, there are predators everywhere. Surviving your 1st day is very rare and has caused me to rage quit several times. Item crafting is ridiculously expensive and resources are very scarce. Hunters/rogues carry very little of value. And, when you kill them you can't even grab their weapons or clothing/armor. The dev does NOT listen to the players and wants to add extras instead of QoL fixes. Don't get me wrong, I like the game. But, it needs a lot of fixing. Also, game settings could be more in-depth. For example, you can turn off hunters but you can't turn down predators. Adding difficulty sliders that ACTUALLY adjust the difficulty would be great!!! The map is spectacular. But, getting around it is atrocious. Respawn points/beds should be easily craftable. And, who puts cordage in their foundations? Come on man!!! Also, risk vs reward is just lame. Cut down on the "extras" and work on improvements to QoL features. Increase resource spawning and gathering and reduce/add a slider for predators. Then the game won't just have promise. It will be great!!! I recommend this game. But, be prepared to die and grind... A LOT.\t\t\t\t\t
