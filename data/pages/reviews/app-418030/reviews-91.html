\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Hardcore mostly PVP game, which does indeed feel like a loot box simulator at times. This is the best description I can give you if you want to know in one sentence what this game is about.

I don't normally give bad game reviews which is to say I really appreciate how much time, effort, sweat, and tears it takes to make something this creative and complicated but there were some things about this that really bugged me and made it hard to not write a review for this.

For a survival game, I expected to have to craft and make more items rather than running around the map trying to find these crates or loot boxes, but as I said for PVP I can see this making more sense, so if you are a PVEer you might find this part rather tedious and sometimes quite annoying.

The other thing I found inexcusable is setting your npc's to **revenge attacks only** is a terrible description for what occurs once in game. Territorial is a better description and if you get to close they will start shooting/killing you. What's worse is they make their bases pretty dang close to yours and once things go down with them or someone misinterprets revenge attacks they will not stop messing with your base. We re-rolled a server twice due to this. Npc's also grow too fast compared to the player. They will have a full base set up before you have yours with everything they need to molotov and destroy you in just a few game days, feels a little unbalanced that way.

Eating is a full time job in this game, and its worse if you like to sprint. If you think you are the sort of player that has little patience and wants to run a lot this is not the game for you. You will need to eat all the time if you play this way, and In my opinion, not running around a lot to save on your food makes the game feel somewhat slow and grindy.

All these things being said I don't hate this game, I got 24 hours out of it, I just don't know that it has any re-play-ability for me in its current state and as a PVE'er myself I don't see that this game really caters too much to that play style.
