\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is way too tedious starting off. The progression is wonky. You spawn in with a pistol and enough ammo to kill one animal. On normal, there is a wolf or bear approximately every 20-25ft. They one shot you, which is not an issue. Its that the first craftable weapon is a bow(no spear??), which you have to make by wandering around for an hour trying to find fibrous plants and avoiding the bears and wolves walking around right next to each other. Mob placement is like an MMO, not a survival game. After you have wandered around for an hour to make your bow, now you have to go kill chickens to get feathers for arrows. You have to CHASE them through the bears and wolves that are as populous as people in a food court at the mall. You CANNOT shoot them with your pistol, as chickens are apparently combustible and become an unusable "ragged carcass" or something, that rewards no feathers. It takes 9 or so shots from the pistol to kill a wolf. A 9mm round can kill a 200lb man, or at least incapacitate him. A 9mm round should kill a wolf, or at least injure it and cause it to flee for its life. If you were to stand in one place on the map, and take note of every animal in your viewing distance, it would represent a state militias worth of ammo.

Unless you are planning to play with a bunch of friends, its not worth it. Also, if a game requires friends to make it good, is it really a good game? I think not.\t\t\t\t\t
