\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe whole mechanic of AI hunters slowly moving into the area and setting up their bases that can eventually turn into massive compounds if left unchecked is an AMAZING mechanic for a single-player base building game and by far the best part of this game.

My main issue with this game is progression. It's either blindingly slow, or nonexistent in some cases. For instance, gathering wood never gets easier. There isn't a better tool or a perk or anything that helps make the more tedious tasks easier the further you progress, so a massive majority of the game is spent hitting trees.

Loot gathering is wondering around looking for boxes and getting 1 - 2 items per box. This also never gets easier or better (Other than making lockpicks and unlocking big boxes which are extremely difficult to find)

The game is repetitive with no real feel of progression for a survival game. The entire game is:

1) wonder around gathering boxes
2) Kill wolf or bear for fat
3) hit trees endlessly
4) you have now waited long enough for power/mass for your next base upgrade
5) repeat

Would be much better if I crafted all of the junk I get from boxes myself by harvesting raw resources with tools I can later upgrade for more/better resources.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright,

I never write reviews. I just need to get this off my back on how ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ frustrating this game is.

I only have 9 hours of game play, in that 9 hours. With a teammate, I was able to get a tiny base. Far smaller than the bases that the NPCs make the second you create a wall. Trying to get cordage is like finding a needle in a hay stack, but it is the most important resource. You need cordage for literally everything yet it is so hard to get.

I understand this game is suppose to be brutal but damn, if I die from NPCs, it spawns me all the way across the map and if you do not know the map or have a beacon set up, you might as well build a new base, you'll never find the old one. Once it hits winter, don't even get me started. if you end up dying you spawn all the way at the end of the map, furthest away from your base/body. You get one little pack of "health/heat" which if the wolves don't get you first, the cold will. Can't even get the resources need in time before you die of coldness. It makes the game almost unplayable.

I really cannot recommend this game. Do I have high hopes for it. Yes, for sure. It can be so great and I would play it all the time. But how hard it is to get resources has to be fixed. Also, this is being played on a normal difficulty. I cannot imagine how unplayable a harder difficulty would be.

Maybe if you have like 4 or 5 other people playing with you, it could be easier. But if you're solo/duo. kiss your ass goodbye because you'll rage quit.

My rant is over, take from it what you will.\t\t\t\t\t
