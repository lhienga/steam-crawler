The Good:
Hunting is fun.
The building mechanics are pretty cool.
AI element is a good addition.

The Bad:
The AI seems overpowered. They don't run out of ammunition (which is hard as hell to come by), and when you kill them they drop precious little loot.
Hit detection is wonky. I can't tell you many times I shot at someone who wasn't moving, while I wasn't moving, and the hit just didn't register.

All in all, this isn't worth the 13.99 I paid for it. Buy it on sale or wait till it's further along in the unending alpha and beta phases that every game is stuck in.
