\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to like this game. I enjoy survival games. 7dtd, the infected, and the forest are some of my favorites.

I bought the game, tried it, and refunded it because I thought it was terrible. Then I watched a couple let's plays, and figured I'd give it another chance. So I bought it again. Nope, still sucks.

1. The whole wandering around and hoping you run into these loot satchels which are the ONLY way to get most of your needed crafting materials is lame. Let's not even get into where the hell these things are coming from, like what possible reason could these things have to exist? And why do they just magically appear out of thin air in a place I just was? But whatever, all games are unrealistic and I can (mostly) overlook this mechanic. Except it just sucks. Gives me eyestrain to try and find these things and puts me on edge. It's like the opposite of fun.

2. Predators everywhere. Again, totally unrealistic but who cares as long as it's fun, right? It's not fun. Oops a bear, I should turn around. Nope, wolf that way, let's go left. Nope, another bear that way. I'm not even joking. I know you can put the game on "easy" and the predators are a little less abundant, but they are still annoying. Aaannd they can kill you in 2 seconds. Aaaannd their attack timing and animations are janky as hell and hard to defend against.

3. It takes an S ton of resources to make anything in this game. I mean like, go farm all day long and you can make one or 2 walls for your base.

4. There's more, but I'm running out of steam. Look, I know the game gets easier. I know some people love it. I'm not saying you shouldn't. But it's not for me. It comes down to some questionable design decisions that make things very tedious, combined with a gameplay loop that is just not satisfying or fun. I can't recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
