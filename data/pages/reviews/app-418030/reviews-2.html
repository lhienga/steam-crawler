\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I played this game off and on, casually it was sort of fun. But the loot boxes/crates are just ridiculous...
You can not do a single thing, unless you find crates.
You can not build structures without nails... you can only acquire nails from boxes until you get a work bench. You can not even make arrows without nails. I feel like the crates/boxes should be a COOL BONUS I FOUND ONE! But they are such a detrimental/required element that kills the game for me. Wildlife is sooo heavily populated... Wolves and bears are basically playing patty cake with each other every 100 feet. Every time I try to chase and catch a chicken for some feathers, they ALWAYS! ALWAYS! ALWAYS! run right for the ♥♥♥♥♥ BEARS and tap dance under them.
You just can not do the most basic of basic things without having to find loot crates/boxes. You can spend hours running around looking for a box just to get one more electronic piece or... A NAIL

Like why is a arrow... feather, stick, nail.... Why can it not be... Stick/stone/thread or rope? Like how does the nail aspect come into the picture for a basic wooden arrow?

I do like the updates that the developer has done, I can just never get to that point without mindlessly running around for a lootbox or crate and that just ruins the game for me.\t\t\t\t\t
