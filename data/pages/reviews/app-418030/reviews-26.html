\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: sadly I will change this review to not recommending the game at this point. For some inexplicable reason the developer, who is just one person, decided to make the game even more grindy. More difficult. It's not even realistic it's beyond realistic. You can shoot a bear in the face with a shotgun two times upgraded and the thing will still kill you. And then it's just miserable because all that time energy and effort that you spent getting your health up point by point very tediously all gets reset the moment you die. Why the hell do fruits and vegetables now decay, was that really necessary? Good luck finding any lock boxes or loot boxes they are so far and few between it's ridiculous. The hunters are not even enjoyable. You spend more ammo killing them than you get from their loots. You can wipe out a whole base and 12 hours later they're right back where they were and this time with rifles and flaming arrows, like if you wipe out a whole squad should there be a reprieve for some time period? Ohh and they can spot you from so far away I was on my map about I don't know 100 meters away and I saw the rifle shot go off and I got hit next thing you know I'm running for my life. Yeah they added an airplane that crashes, that's probably cool. Except I couldn't never find it and it wasn't on the map so I never got to loot it or do anything with that. I just don't understand why the game has to be so *** **** grindy. We only have one life to live and I mean our actual lives I don't wanna spend 12 hours running around looking on the ground for lock boxes just so I can then finally enjoy the game for 15 minutes it's just a bad setup uninstalling it at this point.

I recommend this game... but very reluctantly. The reason being is the sheer tedious grind nature of it sucks the fun right out the window for me. It is a very fun game, progressing, looking back and seeing how far you've come. That is satisfying. However, the way you have to go about everything is just so painstakingly slow and cumbersome. For instance, in Terraria there is a button you can click which stacks everything in your inventory to all nearby chests which have that same item already. No such thing in this game. You have to manually go from chest to chest to chest and deposit everything properly which takes time. God forbid you can set the lights on your plant box to shut off during the daytime automatically - that would be convenient. When you take a drink of water, god forbid the canteen stay in the hotkey slot. That too would make it very convenient in order to fill back up. Nope, you must drag all 4 canteens back to the hotkey bar and repeat. What else is there.... oh yes, somethings are found so seldomly it begins to feel hopeless. Cotton, for instance. Also, you can play with or without hunters. I chose without. Why? Because they are nothing but a burden. Whatever you use to kill them with (ammo mainly) will be more costly then whatever piss poor loot you get from them. It is not much. Everything in the game that you build is in need of constant attention. Must manually feed the chickens, water said chickens. Fertilize the plants. Water the plants. Refuel the generators. Provide more fuel for the stove. Then for yourself you must eat a varied diet. Simple protein bars will not suffice. You must also find fruits and vegetables in addition to the protein source. Also, water is a must. But not just any water, it must be boiled if coming from the lake or streams which takes more time and manual attention. By the time you finish all your chores (and that is what they are, chores), you will have spent so much time that the very first thing you did is once again in need of more attention. That just SUCKS the fun from the game very very quickly. I don't know anybody who feels differently. If there was some automated processes that you could implement, it would make the game just so much more fun. The fun part is going out, killing some deadly beasts or hunters and farming precious materials all whilst staying alive and healthy enough to make it back to your hideout in one piece with said loot. Then spend a little while crafting materials into increasingly complex and fun gear. If you could, at this point in your game loop, not spend a huge amount of time taking care of all of the chores and just go back out to the world again, the game would be a slam dunk. Instead, you are constantly in a state of needing to do some chore or else all will go to - quickly. I understand the need to manually do somethings in order to balance the game in a sense... if the game consisted of an endless loop of.... 1) Go kill beasts & hunters 2) Loot resources simultaneously 3) Make it back alive with loot 4) Craft increasingly complex and fun gear.... and repeat the process after step 4, the game would burn itself out quickly. As in the fun part would no longer be fun after a short time. The manual processes are the crucial balance to the fun portion, they allow you to have that fun in other words. So I am not advocating for the complete removal of them, but they need to be tuned down big time and streamlined where possible. It quickly ends up taking more time to do the chores than the exploring portion. Inevitably, that ends with me not spending much time on the game anymore because of that.

I hope the Developer or whomever takes my review into consideration. Just make things a bit easier in some ways my friend. Start with the deposit all to nearby chests button. Then you can add right click options such as "Fill all canteens with just a click, and you can fill them from your inventory instead of the pointless moving of them to your hotkey bar... twice.... just so you can drink. That is just an example and for those who have ever played the game it is very basic stuff and understood instantly. Other folks, the gist is simply I recommend a way which the chores can be made easier and streamlined.

~~ Cheers ~~ I will update this happily (or not) as more updates come out. I love how things have been expanded all the way to ATVs being craftable. But really the quality of life updates I recommend take precedence WAY more than say a boat being added at this particular juncture in the history of Subsistence.\t\t\t\t\t
