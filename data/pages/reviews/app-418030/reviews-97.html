\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is way too hard and unbalanced. Now i know there is a lot of bad reviews stating that the game is too hard and that is what initially sounded appealing to me. Once i spent an hour or so... I realised the game was hard for all the wrong reasons... Hear me out.

- There are WAY too many aggressive animals and you can't go for a 100m stroll without aggroing 3 wolves and 2 bears.

- Basic resources are too sparse. See after 30min or so i found myself with a bunch of electrical components, multiple ores, even a bunch of ammunition... yet i can barely farm enough fiber in a day to build a single foundation... Why would it be so hard to find plant fiber in the middle of a forest?! And don't get me started on food... sure you can live on kelp... but that is no fun. There seems to be no mushroom, no apple, etc...

- No game loop. You simply find yourself grinding... There is absolutely no loop... you do not get attacked at night, there is pretty much little to no progression and the mechanics are very simple. I do not see any loop in the gameplay.

- Mobs are bullet sponges... it takes about 6 9mm bullets to kill a wold even while shooting the head... now that is simply ridiculous. Having a good AI that dodges bullet can make it difficult, having an AI that tries to gather other AIs to fight as a group can be difficult yet hard, but having an AI that charges at you and tank your bullets makes it hard sure... but yet again for the wrong reason.

I would NOT recommend this game as it is VERY poorly designed. Devs took a few assets and a few game mechanics they could get their hands on and simply threw them together. I highly doubt this is going anywhere and would not say it is worth the 15$ as there is no apparent depth into the design or the development. Cash grab if anything.\t\t\t\t\t
