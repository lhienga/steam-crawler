\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game! No bugs encountered so far.

It has really good graphics and very good building mechanics. I would like to see more stuff on the map, maybe an abandoned town with more resources (and more hunters). Also, I feel like that the hunger/thirst system is not well balanced. To be more specific, I feel like the character needs to eat/drink more than he can gather/hunt(especially in the beginning). When he hunts a bear and gets one or two pieces of meat that will last him for about a half of day, its really really weird. Maybe make big wild animals harder to find and harder to kill but with more resources gathered from them. The same happens with small animals that he can raise. They eat and drink more than they should and also die too fast. If the character is out for two days in a row, the animals are dead.

The hunters move way too fast and their aim is crazy good.

I think that the character should be able to sleep or rest to be able to pass the time during some nights. Also, the day time should be a bit longer. Between gathering/hunting and building or defending the house there is not much time for anything else.
