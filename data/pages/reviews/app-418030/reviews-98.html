\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSooo this review is a little mixed, there are features about this game i absolutely enjoy, and then there are some parts of the game which i am not even sure if they are supposed to be that way.

This game is very challenging which is a great thing. it is pretty difficult to get a starter base up and running, Predators are OP and most the time will kill you unless you can out maneuver them or avoid them all together(until you get a good set up. My only issue with the strength of the predators is they frequently wander into my "base" as i am building it and quickly destroy it. Normally i would not care as that adds to the challenge. but the hunt for materials to build the base is extremely grindy and if you run into a predator while grinding materials your basically screwed early game.

The Co-Op feature which i was really looking forward to does not seem to work for a lot of people, ive read talks about opening ports in the router and messing with the firewall, and even adding coding to some files. Personally that is to much work for simply playing co-op.

Overall: I recommend this game to players who like survival games, are up for a challenge, and dont mind the realism of grinding a lot for survival.


Pros:
Great challenge can easily sink hours into the game.
More realistic than most survival based games.
Graphics are actually pretty good.

Cons:
Extremely grindy.
Difficult starting off (Even on easy mode)
Co-Op does not seem to work 100%\t\t\t\t\t
