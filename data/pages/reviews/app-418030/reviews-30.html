\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of those games that looks real good while watching it on YouTube but is not very good at being a game while "playing" it.

The combat is mediocre at best with AI that has no good interactions besides killing you. Wolves, bears, boars, rabbits and chicken live in harmony side-by-side but I'm not allowed to swim away from a wolf.

The building goes from build a campfire with logs which is normal to straight up requiring nails and computer parts to build a base... there is no lean-to or low level bases to start with and everything drops from crates or bags that you have to find.

You need food to survive, that's fine. You need water to survive, also fine. You need a campfire to cook and sterilize these things, good so far. Hunting however is a joke. You have a pistol to start but the bullets destroy the small game. Yeah okay I guess, I can deal with that. But if you use a bow and arrow..... hah! Every arrow breaks after a single shot, takes way too much to craft a single arrow and if you're hunting chickens to get feathers to make more arrows you'll probably use more arrows than you make if you miss even once.

This really seems like a solid game that I want to like but has more to be desired than it delivers. Even Dark and Light can be better than this.\t\t\t\t\t
