\r\n\t\t\t\t\t6 years is far too long to be early access. The game doesnt remember screen resolutions chosen after selecting and saving several different resolutions. The game doesn't recognize hunters allowed but no attacks or revenge attacks only, they still attack. On the gameplay options, turning off (unticking) "Show Graphic Violence" does nothing, the blood and gore all still work with this option off. Respawns are random and too far from base and retieving the backpack always ends up with missing items (normal mode or higher), so saves are pointless. Getting to crafting a bed is far to far away from start of game and players end up in death loops. This game is definitely focused on grinding and do not have a balanced harvesting/crafting ratio. There is no sense of progression because of this.

The game is very low impact on GPU even on ultra settings and is very smooth. There is no improvement to the player for achieving stages of progression, but is well suited for people that like to chop trees for a living. The environment is very well made and realistic and very appealing to the eye. I would recommend this game for those players expecting improvements to the game, but I would not recommend it for people expecting a fully functional game after 6 years of development.\t\t\t\t\t
