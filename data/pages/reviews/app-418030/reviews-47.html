14 minute timer on a body may still be a bit low as you navigate around animals going from one side of the map to the other in the hopes of making it in time. Perhaps a solid 20 minute timer would give a little more flexibility?

Based on the limited time put into this game thus far my experiences and feelings are mixed as I prepare for the next big update while continuing to feel out the early meta on default settings. Neutral rating leaning towards a hopeful more positive experience.
