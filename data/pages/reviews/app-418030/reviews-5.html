\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm in and out of this game since 2018, trying to make it work for me and waiting that the next update will make the game more realistic.

This game has a lot of potential but it's unbalanced.

Grind, grind, grind all the time.

You need a "aircraft carrier" full of wood and fiber to build a small shelter.

In normal mode, there are too many wolves and bears everywhere. It is not realistic.
In easy mode that part is balanced, but you still spend most of the time collecting wood, fiber and other materials. Huge amounts to craft small things.

Then you need to hunt animals. If you kill a bear you should get food for a couple of days, but that's not the case. You get two or three chunks of meat that will not last for long. It doesn't make sense.

Other annoying thing is that until you craft the BCU you are limited in what you can do. After that you will be able to craft useful things, but the hunters will move soon. I know you can disable hunters, but in that case it's just you, the animals and the seasons. It's a kind of pointless, play that way, in my opinion.

When the hunters start establishing their bases in the game, you will discover that you don't have enough gear to match them.

If you are playing solo, you will be outnumbered, with less weapons, less ammo and less protective gear, than them.

They get their gear from nowwhere. I don't know, it's not realistic. Is there a hidden store, for the NPCs to get their stuff ? :)

They will not miss a shot also, so you have little chances with them.

This game has a lot of punishing and you get very little change to succeed, at least if you are playing solo. Maybe with co-op this would be better, but it should be more balanced if you are playing solo.

I need to feel that the game rewards my effort and I'm not feeling that with this game . It doesn't matter how hard I grind, I'm always loosing for the weather, for the animals or the hunters.

It's enough for me. Game uninstalled and I will not return until the game is more realistic.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted a challenging, grindy early game survival experience, and i found it here. I don't normally write reviews, but in this case i wanted to say something about this game, because there are so many negative reviews that kept me from taking the leap for so long. The game unforgiving and can be a frustrating learn, i understand how many were turned away. For its price on sale or not its absolutely amazing. The environmental details are really immersive, especially as the seasons change. Holy balls that first winter was rough. Loving it, even tho its not perfect. i hope this game gets the support it deserves to get completely polished out. Based on the last update, shes not forgotten and has a ton of growth potential.

Biggest complaints are that melee combat is clunky, almost non existent outside of a desperate last hit after running out of bullets, perhaps i will figure that out and get good. Other games have set a bar there. And losing that hard earned additional point of health and stamina to cold exposure in the winter that was unavoidable? not cool man! I mean, don't take my level up away hard video game. But i will come crawling back for more, and will be trying to lure some friends in.

Overall, game is tough, grindy, but really good if that's what your into. Its like Day Z without zombies and The Long Dark mixed into a Skyrim setting. Oh and i should say i disabled the npc hunters. not ready for that, wildlife / basic survival needs tough enough.\t\t\t\t\t
