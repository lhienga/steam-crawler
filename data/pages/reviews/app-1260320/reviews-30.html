\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlaying this since release, I have now surpassed 500 hours in this creative masterpiece, and am desperately attempting to take a break from it, I feel like it's a good milestone to take some time and talk about it.

I have no doubt I will spend thousands of hours here as the time goes. This game has been no contest the alltime best investment of my money on Steam, and that won't change simply because of how much fun I've had with it already. I laughed in the ways I didn't know I could laugh, and honestly - I really needed that. I never expected it to tick all my boxes, but it sure did.

This game is goofy, silly, random and super stylish. Everything, EVERYTHING is superb- gameplay mechanics, visual presentation and the kickass funk fusion of a soundtrack. Even the tiny references here and there to various creative works around the world.

Even if it doesn't seem to be your cup of tea, even if you are turned away by XYZ things about it - just play 10 or so games, and I guarantee you will have a good chuckle at some point.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3061672996

Even though the game is very random at times (and that's its design intention, even though I may not agree with some parts of it), there's plenty room for being competitive and showing "skill", which I personally consider important. It's this mix of pure random and skill based factors that make it more fun for me, there's a certain beauty in trying to tame the chaotic nature of the game.

Oh, and it's a great place to make friends, actually really great.

Now the devs themselves have admitted this is their first serious project and they are new to game development, it shows partially, but I haven't really seen anything critically bad. So far they have responded rather quickly to critical game issues, bugs, and at the same time add more and more content to the game.

A lot of the criticism regarding the game I don't get, like the micro transaction stuff, there's nothing wrong with it. It's not a pay to win game or heavily micro transaction based. If you don't gamble all your "Nemo Bucks" away on eggs to get the skins that will always be available, especially now with the redesigned reward system, the developers give you around 10k of those for free as long as you reach Level 100 (a bit of a grind, but not tedious in the slightest). You can also get that currency from mystery boxes that drop daily.

There are a bunch of bugs that haven't been adressed for a long time (like the bugged steam friendlist), but I can forgive that. The rest is just miscellaneous game random or ping related things, or servers lagging because more and more people flock to the game.

All in all, I find it simply great and it has quickly become one of my all-time favorite games. I hope it thrives and flourishes for a long time, and more people get to experience this amazing unhinged chaos. 🙂
