\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGoofy lil game about fluffy animals beating each other in the head with baseball bats.

It's expensive normally, I don't think I'd ever pay $20, but $14? Yeah, sure.

The in-game currency is surprisingly very friendly. There are two currency types, and for the most part you can buy a lot of skins with the main currency you can earn just by playing the game, even the Holiday skins and IP Crossover skins. You can also earn the premium currency from playing the game, not as much as the main currency but still enough to buy a special skin if you save up.

Graphically I think the game looks very good, they get the fluffiness of the animals down pretty well.

The UI is clicky, responsive, and unique. It's not trying to fall into the modern interfaces a lot of other new games have done which I really appreciate.

I like it, if you think you might, give it a try. You can play quite a few quick matches before the 2 hour mark is up, so if you don't like it you have more than enough time for a refund.\t\t\t\t\t
