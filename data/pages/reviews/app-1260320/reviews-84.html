\r\n\t\t\t\t\t12 hours in, and I don't think I've ever laughed as much in a game as I have with this one. It's ridiculous, challenging, hilarious, and engaging.

The biggest challenge is learning the controls, and how the game responds. It's not as "snappy" as modern FPS's are, and the controls are on the "soft" side, in that you have to anticipate what you want to do and adjust for the wobbly physics. That said, once you are a couple hours in, it all "clicks" and you will be hard pressed to find a more hilarious and literal laugh out loud game.

Pros:
- Fun and funny almost every round
- Easy to pick up, hard to master
- Lots of fun skins to unlock/earn
- Simple premise, slick interface
- No real thought required, great for blowing off steam
- Ridiculous in how the action plays out
- Good variety in the different levels/mini games

Cons:
- Slow loading times (for how graphically non-intense it is)
- Tutorial leaves out quite a lot such as running rolls, how to swim, etc...
- Replays in sports minigames are time wasters, as well as the winner ceremony (would rather it just jumps into the next game)
- Sometimes inconsistent "grab"/"jump" mechanics where very simple tasks are much harder than they feel like

Overall, truly a wonderful game, easily worth the money, hours of fun here, and with a few tweaks could stand out even more!\t\t\t\t\t
