\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game could be good, but suffers from the typical problems of online-only multiplayer games. Every unlockable cosmetic either costs too much of one of the forms of in-game currency, or requires ridiculous amounts of time spent playing to unlock. The rewards of the leveling system are pathetic for the amount of grinding you have to do to level, and the experience needed to level up increases each time, resulting in diminishing returns. And if you're not winning every single match, you're barely gaining any experience. The game feels like a grind. Not to mention, many of the rewards for leveling up are essentially worthless avatars, avatar frames, or emojis rather than characters or character costumes. No one cares about unlocking emojis.

The core gameplay is fun but clumsy, and too many characters and costumes are locked behind leveling and the in-game shop. How is that a good model for a casual family game? Start being more generous with character and costume unlocks, and come up with some more character designs (try some new species besides cats and dogs; 9 variations of cats/dogs are more than enough). This game could be brilliant, but it falls short because of the problems above.\t\t\t\t\t

Here is a few that come to mind every single time I play:
1. Weapons, while the weapons are fun to use when you obtain them and can be useful, they are far too powerful, and I believe they should be weakened in some way since obtaining one on a map that has a small boundary, or ice on the floor is pretty much a guaranteed victory. I also thing the weapons could be more balanced by making them break, or make the player holding the weapon drop it after so much use.
2. When players die, they get the opportunity to throw items at the players still playing, I feel like this mechanic is a bit to overpowered and unfair. It essentially rewards the worse players, and punishes the more experienced players, I absolutley despise the fact that I could be dominating the match, but them all the dead players target me because, they know I am much more of a threat. WHile in the mean time, another player is picking up a weapon getting ready to launch all the surrounding group off the map, I love this game so much, but these things always manage to get on my nerves, even though these things still bother me I would never not recommend it to people looking to buy it
