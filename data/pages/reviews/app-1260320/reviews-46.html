\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is tricky. On one hand, it’s a fun lighthearted beat em’ up, and on the other it’s misleading in what it truly is.

This game is not a couch party game. You can play with max 4 players locally and they’ve chosen to make it split screen for some reason. You can play a similar game in Gang Beasts with 7 other players locally all on one fixed screen. if I were to add 2 things to this game it would be those two. More local player support, and optional central camera. It also is WAY less fun without playing with friends, so take advantage of the friend pass. I would trade less maps and modes for a better coach play experience.

This game has so much to love and offer. The cosmetics are adorable and easy enough to grind for in their pass. It strikes a good balance between grinding and paywall.
The maps are abundant. There are so many maps and modes that really work well. Most have a method of thinning the population and shrinking the board size as it goes. The submarine map slowly submerges making it difficult to stay on the map. The iceberg map slowly breaks apart making the safe areas smaller and smaller. There are times of game modes and maps to have a great time with.
Lastly, the weapons. They are such a fun addition to the game that really makes people take notice as you rampage across with a huge lollipop or a frying pan.

Overall this game is good. It’s basically gang beasts with cute animals. It’s definitely worth if you are planning to play online with friends, but I would not recommend if you plan on this being a coach game for you and your friends. Play gang beasts instead or any party game that doesn’t require you to split screen (except screen cheat, that game is goated). It could be a much better coach party game in my opinion, but it’s a fantastic game for online play with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
