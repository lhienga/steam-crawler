\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWatching videos of other people playing this game was much more fun than playing it by myself. For me controlling the character seems too clunky and random. I don't feel that i have control of anything at all in this game. I understand that maybe this clumsiness is intended, but discovered that such play style is definitely not for me. I need the feeling of more responsiveness between me pressing the keys and what is happening on the screen. Also the drunk like moving of character gets tiring very fast. I even got almost sea sick from this constant staggering from side to side.

For anyone who is looking really fun and cute game with animals i recommend Super Animal Royale. Good community, fast paced and fun gameplay, basically no FOMO - season passes do not expire and events are easy and fast to complete. Also the game gets constant updates and support from team for already many years.\t\t\t\t\t
