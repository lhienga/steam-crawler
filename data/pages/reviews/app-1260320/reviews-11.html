\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a married woman in my 30's and I thoroughly enjoy this game! I don't particularly love shooter games, like my husband does, I prefer melee combat games. This game is ridiculous and fun, and it was easy for me to learn the game mechanics after investing some time into it. I keep coming back to this game for an easy and relaxing way to blow off steam after a work night.

There are some frustrating aspects of the game - for example, when my husband plays with me, he gets annoyed that the mechanics are not as "precise" as his other games are. Sometimes you don't hit another player as expected, or you can get killed by a single hit if you're really unfortunate... However, I've learned that the game has unique features that often rationalize the crap that seems unfair. For instance, if someone has FULL stamina and hits you just right, you may die instantly. I didn't know that early on, and I got frustrated along the way until I learned some of my own tricks. Now I'm pretty darn good at the game!!! I also just laugh off the ridiculous things, the game is meant to be funny!!

Note: I grew up playing on a PlayStation, and over the past few years I've been learning to game on a PC. I've played Skyrim, Call of Duty, Fornite, and a LOT of different games over the years. As I've gotten older, I've been playing cozy story-based games like Baldur's Gate, Stardew Valley, Coral Island, Wylde Flowers, and so many others. But when I saw Party Animals, I just had to try it, it looked so funny!!

I learned to play this game on my PC, and I've since connected an Xbox controller to my PC which has made the game even smoother. The joystick controller makes throwing other players much easier. I switch between the keyboard and Xbox controller when my hands start to hurt. Lol!

Hope that someone finds this review helpful. If you're on the fence about buying the game, I recommend you just get it and give it a try! I love playing with my hubby (when he's free), but I'm usually playing solo over here and giggling my butt off. And winning!!!! That makes it so much more fun.\t\t\t\t\t
