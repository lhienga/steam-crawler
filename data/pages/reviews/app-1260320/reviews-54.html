\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like parties, and if you like animals, you'll love Party Animals. This game take the ragdoll death battle concept from Gang Beasts and Human Fall Flat, and inserts it into a fun title with beautiful graphics. This game, Party Animals, is about beating up your friends and foes and tossing about so your the last animal standing.

I've dabbled in playing Gang Beasts before, but I will say that Party Animals really utilizes the ridiculously high quality graphics and the adorable cuteness of the characters to make it a genuinely hilarious experience whether playing with friends locally or online.

This game also has a bunch of different modes in which to engage in, each with in depth mechanics that make each one unique from the other. One stage has two trains running side by side, here you have to lug coal into your train while making sure the enemy team falls behind. Another stage is set in a winter cabin where you're supposed to toss your opponents outside so they freeze in the cold. There are a bunch of other stages that utilize janky platforming and physics to rid your enemies from this mortal coil.

I've played this games a fair amount of times with friends, online and offline, and it's always a great time. The only thing I'd have to ding the game on is for not being a game I'll play absolutely all the time, but this game achieves what it wants and quite competently if I do say so. 8 out of 10, excellent party game.\t\t\t\t\t
