\r\n\t\t\t\t\tParty Animals is the perfect party game for a large group. It does feel and play very, very similar to Gang Beasts. Some of its maps, graphics, game play, concepts and physics are all clearly inspired and taken directly from Gang Beasts. However, Party Animals does the unthinkable and transforms these ideas into a better game with enough new mechanics to come with. The inclusion of weapons is a great addition. It works very well with the goofy combat. Each weapon is hilarious to use on opponents, though some of them may appear similar, they all have different perks and traits. Some have better range, better damage or better knock back. The twenty one different maps are all unique, with great mechanics and events that occur. Though, what brings some maps down are some of the team score maps, they might drag on for a tad too long, especially soccer, hockey, football and fluffy redemption. Perhaps the best part of Party Animals are the actual animal characters themselves. They all look hilarious and perfectly set the tone for the hilariousness of this game. What is really fantastic though, is the endless amount of customisation administered to the players. There is a large, large, LARGE array of animals to select, each with over five skins each! They all look visually amazing and work really well with the graphics design. Some costumes or animals can be purchased from the item shop, which doesn't even require money to spend. Both currencies (Nemo bucks and cookies) are simply earned from playing the game and obtaining them through the free battle pass which also gives out awesome rewards. Another great thing about Party Animals is the constant, frequent and thoughtful updates that are being put into the game. There's almost a new update every few weeks. Whether its a new event, new costume, collaboration or map, its amazing to see the developers put in so much effort. Though very similar to Gang Beasts, Party Animals is a very worthy successor and improves upon its inspiration in so many ways. 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
