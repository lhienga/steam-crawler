\r\n\t\t\t\t\tApparently there is intentional input lag... in a game that already suffers from laggy servers that many people complain about (if you're one of the few that doesn't have lag, great for you, but that doesn't change this being the case for most). This makes the game essentially a random button-mash fest. Maybe it would still be decent for children who just mash a bunch of buttons and don't use any skill, but for parent-gamers playing along with their children, it is an exercise in frustration, which makes a game with low appeal even less appealing.

However, what really sours this game even further is the recent inclusion of a character from Cult of the Lamb, probably one of the most inappropriate games for children to be playing. The game developers and/or publishers either don't seem to understand who their target demographic is, or are intentionally trying to shove demonic content in front of children's faces. Unacceptable. There are also other characters who can be unlocked after playing the game for some time which have similar demonic appearances. Yet another increasingly common game that starts off all "cute and kid-friendly" but eventually descends into demonic garbage.

Too bad. This title had the potential to be a fun little game to play with your kids, but it is ruined by unresponsive controls and the abhorrent inclusion of non-kid-friendly demonic characters. Money wasted.

Oh, and how could I forget the cash shop... in a paid-for game.\t\t\t\t\t
