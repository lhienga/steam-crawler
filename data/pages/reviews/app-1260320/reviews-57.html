\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game, is just a joy overall.

It's like Gang Beasts but generally better in almost every way. It's not just a free for all/team brawl, but it's also got different party modes where you have to work together as a team to do different objectives like collecting different candies, moving coal into your own train while trying to stop the other team from putting coal into theirs so your train goes faster, football/soccer, all the goodness. There's a decent amount of maps all with different gimmicks which keep the game fun to play.

A big upcoming upside is that specifically on Steam, you can give a "Friend Pass" to someone, so they can play the game for free with the caveat that they play at the same time as you. Seeing how this is a party game, that's not really much of an issue, and is a feature that more games like this should have as a default. The monetisation of the game is fine since there is nothing that would give you an advantage, just cosmetic stuff. Anything that can be bought in the store, even for the "premium" currency can be earned by exchanging the free earned currency, and at a rate that I've not had any real trouble with.

The game is fun and 100% worth the price. This is a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ gem of a game, and pretty much any time it's brought up as something to play, it's almost always a yes.

Also! Additional maps are being added and I'm just generally really pleased with the game. I wanna see where it goes. I'll gladly put it up there alongside Ultimate Chicken Horse for a go-to party game! :)\t\t\t\t\t
