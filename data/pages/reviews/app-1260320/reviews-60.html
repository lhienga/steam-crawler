\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a ton of fun! It's all the best parts of Gang Beasts but in a much cuter and prettier package. I really like all the customization options and look forward to more to come. (fingers crossed for a panda or dragon skin) The maps are diverse and have different gimmicks to them. I like that some maps are a simple last man standing but others are a game mode like football or "capture the flag". The game is best played with a group of friends, but even if you have just one friend or play alone, it's still a lot of fun playing in the public lobbies. Overall, an amazing game that I plan to continue to come back to for months to come.\t\t\t\t\t
