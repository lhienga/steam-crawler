\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolid 9/10 with friends
7/10 solo

Have u ever wanted to release all ur pent up anger and frustrations? Well guess what, now u be an animal of ur choice and scrap with others for a living.
Whether you go full Bruce Lee with nunchuks on others, yeet them into poisonous gas, or drown them, this game has it all!

***Pros***
-The rag doll physics are funny
-Fun game with friends
-Skins aren't entirely locked behind a paywall
-Quick match variety of maps and game modes helps with repeatedly playing without getting boring fast
-Oh, you got insta KO'd after starting a match? Well, u can spectate and throw crap at others for revenge or even out of rage

***Cons***
-Maybe it's just a keyboard/mouse thing, but it sometimes feels like I am inaccurate with landing punches, headbutts, and precision grabs

-Variety of game modes that exist for Quick match do not exist for custom matches, unless I am blind and cannot find the right options (can't tell if this is the case, or if it's due to the 8 player capacity condition)

-Spectating after death is hit or miss with some maps. Some maps give u camera angles that do not view the maps entirety and these angles make it hard for u to mess with others when tossing throwables

-The reticle for tossing things as spectator is inaccurate bc it doesn't account for throwable physics and environmental interaction. Hopefully this gets changed in the future, maybe an aiming arc is better rather than a reticle?

-Just a nitpick but, it feels like some animals have faster knockdown recovery than others.\t\t\t\t\t
