\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has filled the Banished sized/shaped hole in my life.

Pros:
Extreme player freedom
Fun System Interactions
Engaged Developers
Encourages Creativity

Cons:
Everything you expect from an in-development, early access game

As my playtime shows, I love this game. It can be a gritty survivalist challenge. It can be a pure city builder. For me, it's mostly an RPG with this little village as the character.

There are lots of nuanced, interconnecting systems. Some are very intuitive, others maybe a little less. But it's a game where I feel satisfied digging a root cellar to keep food cool in the summer so it lasts longer AND when I build an elevated bridge to my castle so raiders bunch together and are easier to kill when they attack. Mastering the systems is fun and building castles is cool.

You have all the fun planning of a city builder like Banished plus a good dash of The Sims: Black Plague Survivors. It's great!

The game has grown by leaps and bounds since it first popped up and it's only ever gotten more enjoyable. Yes, occasionally a patch breaks something. But the devs are very quick with fixes. I adore this game!\t\t\t\t\t
