\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to like this game, interesting premise, 3D Rimworld (and I've put about 1100 hours in to Rimworld) but I cant recommend it. I went back to checking out this game again after having purchased it on its initial EA launch - but now when I play, my mouse/keyboard randomly get stuck almost as if the polling rate of my keyboard is somehow interacting with the game. This might seem trivial at first, but basically imagine panning through your settlement and for no real reason your mouse stops working for 3-5 seconds, and the edge scroll keeps happening in some random direction. Wait a moment, spam a few keys, hey maybe it was on me!

Wrong, it wasnt on you, it happens again. You can try googling this problem, you wont find an answer. Best suggestion: restart your computer, maybe dont play in fullscreen mode. I tried both of these things multiple times. This is also the only game that this issue happens on for me, and I play a wide variety of games from everything simulation to FPS.

The worst part is that its completely unpredictable. It happens at least once a minute, but no idea whats causing it. Usually if I'm spinning my camera around or trying to orient myself in space, all of a sudden the keyboard edge scrolling feature will just send my camera in to oblivion.

I could maaaybe look past the problems of the mouse getting stuck for no reason, if I didn't also hear audio skipping constantly. Almost like little ticks in the background, or the type of stuttering that you'd get if your graphics/soundcard is problematic. Remember soundcard problems? Playing this game make it feel like you have a broken soundcard because you're constantly hearing pops/skips.

The audio skips are almost happening at the same frequency as the controls being locked out. Its definitely still in early access and I feel like my particular situation will be very difficult to fix. I just wish there was more information I could provide. I'm pretty sure an AMD 5950 and an RTX 4070ti are overkill for this game, and as I mentioned earlier I play a variety of games, so I dont think its my machine.

Id love to refund this purchase if this is how its going to be for me, but at this point I purchased the product over 2 years ago and it just feels like I cant play it. Sorry.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI almost never leave reviews but I am absolutely obsessed with this game! It's an incredible mix of resource management, farming, and colony sim/strategy - think minecraft x dwarf fortress x the sims. It took some trial and error to find the mix of settings that work for me, but the attention to detail and nuance within the game made me WANT to open a new save, versus other games where I felt I NEEDED to open a new save.

If I could add things to the game here's what I would put forth:
- the production items that use heat (smokestack, smelter, etc) it would be nice if they heated up the room they're set up in
- When ordering my settlers to haul I would love to be able to prioritize categories of items vs the current forbid tool - ie I want to be able to choose "haul all wood" instead of individually selecting the wood piles or forbidding the items around it. Maybe even just a prioritize tool in addition to the forbid tool.
- Item rotation by PC - I feel like as long as no outside construction/deconstruction has to happen the PC should be able to rotate the item without needing a settler to come and completely deconstruct and move the item.

That all being said, 10/10 highly recommend can't wait to see what comes next :)\t\t\t\t\t
