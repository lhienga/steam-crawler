\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great castle and community builder - the dev team is ACTIVE and LISTENING - they deserve praise for the project so far!

It's a 3D Voxel game with the ability to have a city span decades. Right now my settlement has been around for 19 years in game so far, and is still going strong. Lots of replayability with the variation of seeded environments. I do wish for MORE environments, to include water/islands, and volcanoes/lava. Yet to be said.

For the price as is, definitely worth the money - it's a great game to "press play" and step away for a minute/while. It's cool to see the planning of your castle come to fruition, it's deep in the ways you can organize the community. It's lacking some elements which are still being developed, it's already been fun to be apart of the ride, planning on continuing the same community for as long as the current version permits!\t\t\t\t\t
