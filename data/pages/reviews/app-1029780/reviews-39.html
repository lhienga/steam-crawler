\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCore:
It´s an interesting take on Rimworld in a medieval Time with some flaws being polished out thtat Rimworld obviously has. Its fun about 2 Hours. The game has some flaws of its own, for example it´s a bit annoying that you´re almost forced to take the first few Research Options, otherwise the happiness of your settlers goes down too much. You could´ve saved that bit.

I was playing 95% of the game at speed of 3, because its mostly waiting. There are no inbetween events or anything that would need your attention. I like that, but i think a lot of players would be missing something.

Graphics:
The biggest flaw of the game is the graphics. They´re just hideous. Indiepocalypse is real. But i´m so baffled that today noone seems to get partnered with a 3D Artist. The game looks like a puddle of oversaturated colors. And the extreme sunrays for no reason wont help that.

You need to get used to the camera, wich has about 70% useless functions. There is literally no need to have about 2-4 layers of wall height. None.

Ambience:
The Music is quite nice and its good and simple held. The smileys above your settlers give some impression how they´re doing for the moment, wich is quite nice.

UI:
The UI is pretty simple and good. But the Storage at the top right is really useless unless you get to know what pixel is wich item. There is no intuition in what could be what.

Conclusion:
Right now the game needs a LOT of work. The concept is nice, but i would not recommend it at the given state.
