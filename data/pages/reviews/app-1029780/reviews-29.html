\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought this game full prize in February and can honestly say it fulfilled every promise. I love survival vs building base games and this is one of my two current favourite games. Developers are still active and listen to committed players resulting them to keep adapting and further developping the game.
Players are fanatically testing and retesting their guides and 'how to'. Community is very open and helpful.

For mechanics there are a lot of variations possible. There is a good system of management you can tweak and use. Characters at random or build by you who will keep developping traits if you set their age young. Start alone and gather other villagers or start with a group up to 10 villagers. Start from scratch or with all benefits and resources, knowledge you want. Set it to survival or just fun to build the perfect castle. You grow your own crops, train your animals and develop your knowledge so you can make your own armour and clothing and such. With the season changes, wild animals and raids on your castle you keep getting entertained. Want to build a trading settlement, go ahead and build up your prestige on the map. I have played many, many hours and still not utilized all developments and possibilities. In part because my ADHD loves starting anew in yet another variation. Want a game that is casual but still throwes a curveball, this will fulfill your every need.\t\t\t\t\t
