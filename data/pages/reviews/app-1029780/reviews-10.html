\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKudos to the developers. For an early access game it runs well, and is quite fun.
One aspect of the game that's enjoyable is all buildings are your own. No prefab designs. Another component that is well done is the terraforming and the animal training/ domestication. These aren't the only enjoyable aspects, just don't want to rant on. With that said, one aspect of the game that should be overhauled or omitted is hauling. It just shouldn't be a thing unless a settler dies or a raider is killed. When the settlers are gathering they should automatically gather and store the resource the settler is working on. It shouldn't be left on the ground to decay, or need to alter the importance of the job. But that is my chief complaint about the game.
Besides that, if the developers could add priests for the religions and could add some more furniture (such as pews and dressers) and more aesthetic options it would make a good game that much better.\t\t\t\t\t
