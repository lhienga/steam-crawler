\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can't believe I waited this long to purchase this game. I love the colony survival sim games, previously having loved Banished (another medieval colony sim). The problem I had before was that I know this genre of games has a relatively small cult following and it seemed that the support and development on these games was lacklustre.

With Going Medieval, the exact opposite is true. The support and development on this game is amazing. There's so much replay-ability as it is, but with constant updates, it just continues to increase. This game is super fun, chill, and addicting (even got my friend into it and immediately within 5 minutes of playing it, he said he's addicted). You can play this game no matter the mood you are in, as the different scenarios allow for more strategy and stress, or more calm and chilled out. Regardless, it still always manages to be fun.

A massive thank you to the developers of this game for producing something I've been wanting for ages. 10/10 would recommend this game, especially if you like colony simulators.\t\t\t\t\t
