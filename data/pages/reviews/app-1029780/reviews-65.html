\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt this state I can't recommend tis game: it feels like a clone of Rimworld with small additions sprinkled on top, like ability to dig down or build tall buildings. Also seasons... and thats all. Everything else you saw in Rimworld with the same or practically the same features.
The same problem that you can't train your people how to fight or archery (it seems that shooting range is a thing that unknown to medieval people :))
The same mechanism of assigning and managing jobs, fields and other resources.

The digging underground tunnels and chambers is cumbersome due to every build level is separated into several smaller ones: e.g. ground level, when you do not see stuff laying on the floor, middle level, where you can't build shelfs on the wall under under-the-roof view point - I believe this one should be reviceds, as it caused quite an annoyance for me.

If you want to play such type of game - go for Rimworld, it has workshop it is polished.
If you want something even harder or with ability to build complex things - Dwarf Fortress would be far better choice, especially if you are looking for a challenge. Loosing is fun.
If you want to play something relaxed and in 3d with mechanics of two above, but not as deep and challenging as DF - this might be your choice. But be prepared that there is nothing new and you will get the feeling that you done all of it before in Rimworld be with you very soon.\t\t\t\t\t
