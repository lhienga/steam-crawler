\r\n\t\t\t\t\tComplicated to start, to advance, to manage, almost no suggestions on how to progress.
With default settings you get 3 villagers, few goats and a bunch of resources.
You build a research bench and work at it one after another regardless of their skill level, you must open special menu to set job priority for your workers.
You build a fence for your goats by drawing a square but it looks terrible - you must flip half of fence tiles to make it looks like a fence, do it one tile at a time or try searching for special command if it exists; you must change appearance of corner tiles so they look like corners and then rotate each one of them.
The fence is made out of sticks and you waste few minute to guess that you can get them not only from stick piles lying around but also from chopping a tree (good thing that when you select a tree you can see the resources it will yield). You assign a guy to chop a tree and after that he stands idle - you must open special menu to assign chopping area to chop several trees.
You equip a guy with a bow, assign him o hunt a specific deer and you get no idea whether he will hunt next one automatically, furthermore after killing a deer he has to bring carcass to stockpile, so when that was hunting suddenly is hauling to stockpile it is not clear whether he brings the carcass or just decided to haul books from research desk and whether he is going o keep hunting. Previously you assigned him to hunting deer but then see a rabbit that he will not hunt automatically. How to assign him him to hunting just everything?..
But the strangest thing is there is no goal and not much suggestions. The game tells you to build walls, a door and roof, some sleeping bags. It informs that resources laying not under roof deteriorate quicker, you see that production benches are less efficient out doors. But what's next? And is there any sense in building those if your guys can sleep on the ground, eat berries from bushes and meat meals from hunting, they seem already happy with that. And where is my milk from goat that was milked twice somehow automatically? And what should I do with that when I get milk?..
Alright, researched agriculture, now I can plant cabbage crops but I have no cabbage seeds. Should i build merchant stand or just go google how to actually start this game? Rather quit and refund\t\t\t\t\t
