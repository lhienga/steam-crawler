\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a pretty good rendition of the colony sim in 3D. The mechanics follow the form of Rim World rather than Dwarf Fortress in that many things are simplified. The colonists have fewer interests and have a simplified trait system rather than the fully fledged lore system of Dwarf Fortress. This along with the simpler sandbox make it far more accessible to players than some of the other colony sims out there.

Among 3D colony sims this one is rather promising. The only things important missing are more significance of the "Region" map mode, diplomacy, polish on the production and research lines, and "filler" content to add more diversity to bases.

Update 7 added some fixes by adding more mood modifiers, aesthetic opinions, and fleshing out the construction and tech trees more.

Overall I would recommend it to fans of the more accessible colony sims who are willing to sacrifice depth for a shallower learning curve.\t\t\t\t\t
