\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI just finished playing this game for over twenty hours and I have really enjoyed it so far! This game is what would happen if Rimworld and Timber and Stone had a baby. The day/night and season cycle is incredibly detailed and life-like! The sun changes angles during different seasons, and the nights get longer in the winter. The temperature mechanic is something to get used to, and you will quickly learn how to make a cold storage so your food doesn't spoil. Combat is familiar and feels good and the UI is fantastic and one of the better UIs I have seen in a colony sim. My only complaint would be the settler AI as they seem to do some really dumb stuff, but it is easily remedied by micro-managing them for a bit. I am just about half way thru my first winter in the my first settlement and I am more than glad that I stored enough food away to survive. If you liked RImworld and/or Timber and Stone, you will love this game as well!\t\t\t\t\t
