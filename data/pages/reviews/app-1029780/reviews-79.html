\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first I was skeptical, but now I just can't put it down. Please please devs don't stop developing this game!

Cons: So far the only thing that's bothering me a bit are the Valheim style graphics. The game does have an overall un-finished feel, but it just got into EA, so that's to be expected. Haven't found anything else so far, will update if I do.

Pros:
* Proper settlement building. Harvesting resources, crafting from said resources, researching, upgrading, the works.
* The interesting thing is the individuality of your people. They are customisable, with positive and negative traits, you can make the settlement group you want when you first start, and they seem to grow as well.
* The interaction with animals is exceptional, you can have pets that help you haul, fetch resources, chase down vermin and even help you in combat. You tame and train various animals.
* The game is detailed and nuanced in many aspects, starting from your stockpiles, to where and when and how much of what you can put where, to the levels and type of injuries your settlers are inflicted with, to how much resistance a specific wild animal has to your taming or hunting it, to the building aspect that I suspect later on is astonishing.

Overall it's absolutely worth it, saying this after 4 hours of gameplay and I've yet to delve deeper.\t\t\t\t\t
