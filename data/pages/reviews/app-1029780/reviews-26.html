\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far loving the game, its fun and I have about 30 hours in. But the game needs to be harder, at this point in time the game is pretty much a fast forward simulator

1. Food isn't a problem- cellar makes food spoilage a non-issue, cooking just enough meals to prolong the food means I can skip farming for a year. Also I have way too many animals, so if crops do fail I have milk and dry meat to last until the next growing seasons.
> Make preserving food more important IE salt, drying, pickling, fermenting, making cheese.
> Make seeds harder to come by, less seeds when you harvest crops, seeds more expensive from traders, etc.
> Make animals more scarce during winter months, harder to tame, or maybe have random events where to animals catch a disease so we have to cull the herd and start over.
2. Raids are essentially linen generators
> build walls and funnel into a choke point, raids need to be harder and maybe attack in waves that take a couple days to finish.
3. Moods really don't matter
4. type of clothing doesn't matter
5. weather events doesn't really matter
If the game is going for a chill experience, it for sure nailed it because its chiller than the Sims games. I still rate it a 5.5/10 its still a decent game, I would just like an option for it to be actually harder. (I even tried the lone winter start as my first game, it was a joke) if you're looking for a chill time buy it, if you're looking for something like rimworld, you're not going to find it here\t\t\t\t\t
