I was soooo excited to have this dog, because I had been taming a wolf for (in game) years to no avail. Imagine my disappointment when I discovered there's no way to actually feed this dog before it's 100% trained and becomes a pet other than leaving food out to rot or get eaten by every wolf and fox in the neighborhood. So much wasted food and effort. I just let the dog go and now I don't even want to play this game anymore for a while. I'm so frustrated as a player. It is poor game design to add in an animal without a way to feed it at all points of its life. A simple dog bowl or making the dog able to be penned before it's trained would be simple fixes to this issue. I understand this is early access and bugs I can handle, but the devs simply did not even take the time to fully flesh out this animal they specifically added into the game. I'm done at least for now..... I really hope this gets fixed.

Edited to add: I've played some more.... I'm still mad about the dog..... but it's still a good game, just don't get the dogs or tame the wolves until they fix it...\t\t\t\t\t
