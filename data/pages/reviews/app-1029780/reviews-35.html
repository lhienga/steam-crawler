I do highly reccomend checking it out, it's been entertaining to say the least ^.^ (maybe play other music in the background tho, the same one track will get stuck in your mind right quick otherwise)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoy these sorts of games, the corner stone of these microscale civ builders being Rimworld obviously. However i believe that going medieval should be the new bar for this genre. I love the vertical nature of the game which really opens up the 2000 blocks of space allowing you to take full advantage of the space you're given. the flat and restrictive nature of rim world which forced you to build outwards into complex inefficient designs. A side note is that the verticallity that the game offers allows for an added element of designing aesthetic bases and creating castles limited only by your imagination and the in game stability feature. Whilst the games has room for improvement this improvement simply highlights the sheer scale in potential the game holds both with the art, and the mechanics.
If you're looking for something light harted and you enjoy building whimsical little fortresses then i recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
