\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe z layer system is pretty close to dwarf fortress with walls and floors, while the UI is mostly from rimworld.
The speed of the game itself is very slow, thankfully this can be changed in a config file but it'll still start to chug quite rapidly with pathfinding bottlenecks, animals, lots of tasks, etc

I found that the view system had more or less all the features in place that it needed to, it just didn't use them automatically or in a context sensitive way, they all have to be largely set manually with ctrl+click being one of the few saving graces.

There isn't a vast amount of content or emergent storytelling, danger doesn't really scale that well and the consequences of combat failure aren't much

I tried a winter start with one settler, on the smallest map and it still didn't pose much danger

Later on the enemy will bring siege weapons that damage your constructions, but the damage is very minimal. I have also had early raids defeated by a single wooden door because the enemy could not bash it down in time.

Building is somewhat interesting and has some interesting thermodynamics built in as well, stability as well.

I am not sure why the game is limited to three starting locations that determine map generation settings, they should be an analogue of whatever tile is selected on the world map. It would be much more interesting to have more extreme heightmaps to build around and farm on.

The AI is generally pretty braindead, but that tends to be the norm in this type of game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've never played a colony sim game and found them rather boring. This game looked cool and fun for cheap, considering it's still in development. The devs in their discord really listen to the players and have a suggestion channel and implement those suggestions based on how many positive reactions it has. The game has a long way to go but it's extremely good and fun. My only "current" complaint is that the maps are not endless, once you run out of resources, that's it. There's also no automatic defenes/turrets your settlers can use. When you get attacked you HAVE to risk them dying and fight with the settlers, seems kinda silly that they're incapable of making some type of re-settable trap systems or archer tower. However it's likely these will get changed in the future so I don't really mind, the game is still really enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
