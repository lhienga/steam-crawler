You learn things along the journey, like foxes will steal from your meat pile and even from your food storage if they have a path to it. This will make you want to build out your house better and fence in areas. It is very satisfying watching your goats have little ones, especially when you didn't know that would happen. The only bad thing I have found so far is 1 hour of play, becomes 2 hours, becomes 5 hours, becomes 7 hours, ughh... I have played all day! If you like building and watching people and animals do crazy things this game is for you. I have only played peaceful so far to get the hang of all the things you need to manage. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has come a long way since my review a year ago. I've sunk some more time into the game, and it has certainly improved. The AI Pathing is better, but not perfect. The combat is less tedious, but only a little bit. There are some quality of life improvements, and the new temperature and weather systems make things more interesting. The addition of ladders is a life saver, especially since it makes it easier to mine without having to strip mine an entire area. I also really enjoy how your people will take certain paths in the environment, and the terrain changes to a path. It's fascinating to see exactly how my people navigate around the map.

It's been a year of solid, consistent updates that add so much positive content to the game which really elevates it from its other colony sim contemporaries. It's still not as feature-filled as Rimworld, but it truly is coming into its own. I'm quite happy with the progress so far!

Old Review: 06/22

The best way that I can sum up this game is by saying "Almost."
It's Almost a good 3D rimworld type game.
It's Almost a good simulator game.
It's Almost a good strategy game.
It's Almost a fun game.

It reminds me of a prettier Towns more than anything, but with less jank. It has a pretty solid buildup, but as other reviewers mentioned, once you get the high ground/towers, it makes combat encounters fairly mundane. The only threat that is remotely dangerous are the catapults. Even then, you can build adequate defenses against them IE WWI Trench Warfare style lol.
I only recommend the game because they've already come a long way and I think the game has potential. In a year I'll return and give an update.\t\t\t\t\t
