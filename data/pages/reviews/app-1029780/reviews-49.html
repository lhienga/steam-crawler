\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy reviews are based on my experience and not pro vs con elements of the game. That being said if you are a gamer of similar likes and dislikes you will enjoy this game. I like the chill this game posses and find it extremely engaging. It has all the elements of games in this genre need. Building, crafting, animal husbandry, farming. Its simple design is where it shines, it doesn't have to be complicated to be good, or have high end graphics. Some recent titles have proven that all that glitters is not always gold. There are a few areas that I think need improvement. The settlers AI is abstract, for example if you place wall pieces the settlers should build those pieces in the order that you put them down. This is not the case however, it seems that the build order is completely random which causes some significant delays in construction. This by itself is not game breaking but if you have an imminent attack coming in you would like the settlers to complete your defense before building a animal trough.

This title is not high action, or demanding of keyboard/controller dexterity it's just plain good entertainment. I hope you will give it a chance.\t\t\t\t\t
