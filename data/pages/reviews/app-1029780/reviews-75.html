\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWaited a long time before buying this game because it looked like complex in the trailers and I expected much micromanagement. Boy I was wrong. This is a modern sim colony management. It delivery what it promisses, but with all the facilities from modern games.

The game is set in medieval England time and has many challanges and things to discover and learn. Unlike other games like Banished and Rise of Man, it is possible to build customized structures and the workshops are made by specific crafiting stations. This gives lots of flexibility and greatly increases replayability. It stills lack some features that would increase playtime, like allowing to expand by creating new settlements and to raid enemy villages (but the game is still an in-progress work with frequent updates).\t\t\t\t\t
