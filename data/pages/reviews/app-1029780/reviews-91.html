\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is still in Early Access and it shows. This is not a negative, as its more about how much potential and room to grow the game has.

Having the game be fully free form is fantastic. Being able to design and build your own buildings is great, and gives the game a great amount of replayabilty. The systems are complex but once you figure out how they work it can be very easy to get people doing exactly what they need to do. Though there can still be issues, as I once lost all my goats and cows because all my villagers just decided not to fill their troughs, even though they had no issues filling the sheep and bunnies.

It looks great, the controls are easy to use except in a few cases. Mostly is dealing with the layers, especially when it comes to underground. It can be hard to figure out exactly where you're telling your villagers to dig, not to mention unless you're micromanging their digging they can dig in a way that makes the selected blocks inaccesable or abandons them on a spire of rock.

There's a few quality of life improvements i'd like to see. The major one is making it easier to replace existing construction with other materials. If you have some nice wooden buildings but decide you want to make a limestone manorhouse, you have to dissamble the wooden house piece by piece, and that can be time consuming. being able to select a space and tell the villagers to replace it with a another building material would save a lot of wasted time and endless clicking. I'd also like to see a larger population growth. I know you can start your scenario with up to 10 villagers, but new villagers are still few and far between. Its not terribly difficult to have more than enough food for your villagers to live off easily, so having more mouths to feed would offer a new challange.

As was said, its still early access so these can be added in later. Most of the rest of the things I'd like are cosmetic, i'd like to see more variation in clothing and furniture.

But as it sits right now, its a tight little colony sim that has a lot of potential to expand into something really great.
