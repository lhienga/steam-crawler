\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI always found colony sim games "enticing", but never found myself actually gripped or enjoying them.

This Early Access Game? Hooked. I never want to put it down. Coming up on my 4th year of my first settlement and I simply love this game.

There are a fair few intricacies to learn - reasonably intuitive to do, but once you get there it shines all the brighter.

It is definitely in its earlier stages, but even that offers quite a substantial amount of options and development within its framework. I honestly cannot wait to see what it brings next, and what it looks like at final release!

I have a general rule that for every £1 I spend on a game, I want at least 1 hour of entertainment. For example, I finished a CoD story in a handful of hours, for a £40 game, didnt want to MP, not high value (though the hours I played were fun). This game cost me what.. £15? Already up to 32 hours and I WILL be creating other playthroughs too. Fantastic value for money. I got it on sale, but full price is definitely worth it for those that know they like colony sim games.

For those on the fence - if you like medieval, base building, some combat (automated dice-rolls behind the scenes, but tactically and equipment-wise entirely up to you), advancement with some reasonable realism in there, skill levels for characters, etc, then nab it on sale or buy it full price - this honestly is a blast.

Hope this helps - support this game!\t\t\t\t\t
