\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game does not deserve the amount of positive reviews it has. It is actually extremely frustrating to play. It's a boring rimworld clone with 1/10th of the content and 10x the bugs. Settlers are completely brain dead and will ignore priorities until they finish whatever task they were originally going to do. Clear action doesn't do anything. You have to draft and undraft them to clear their actions and then prioritize something. The UI is missing an insane amount of features and information. There's no handy dandy sound when you draft like rimworld, and if you select multiple people and draft the UI does not change or inform you. So it's pretty hard to tell.

Items on the map spawn in randomly and aren't forbidden, so when I built a stockpile my settler ran all over collecting dead peasants. Wanted to start over and try lone wolf, and this time my settler ran all over trying to pick up random bones across the map. The settler antics continued when trying to build walls near edges. Despite having a pile of clay literally 2 tiles from the wall I wanted to build, the settler spent an extra half of a day in game to run across the map and find a ramp to go down a level, then run all the way back and build it from below.

Potential customers - If you want a Z axis that bad there's a mod for it on Rimworld. Almost 2 years in EA at the time of writing this. If devs can't figure out how to smooth out frustrating AI shenanigans and get the fundamentals going after all this time, I doubt there's much hope for this game.

Edit: So here we are, 6 months later and I notice the game has had "Major updates" and numerous fixes. And by that, they meant they added a polecat. Possibly bears and mirrors too, but to be honest it didn't feel like anything changed. No wait, that's not true, actually it felt even WORSE. Animations worked before, but were broken now. Settlers "running" on top of their beds all night (they actually slept but the animation stuck) for example. Enemy AI still absolutely brain dead. breaking random doors that lead no where while getting shot full of arrows. And then after breaking the pointless side doors which lead to the single entrance, idling about getting shot, or run backwards, through the killbox and traps to the front of the other side doors to attack that.

It took me about 6-8 hours to set up an almost completely autonomous base. The rest of my hours were afk'ing until the next notice popped to get rest of the achievements.

Except dismantle 50 items which is broken. Because I made and dismantled 50 of almost every item trying to proc it.\t\t\t\t\t
