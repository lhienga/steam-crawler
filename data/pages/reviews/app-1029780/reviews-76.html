The point where Going Medieval is different to Rimworld, is that it gives you much larger construction projects and really an inch to flex your architecture and creative muscles.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI gotta say, I kinda no lifed the beginning. The 11 hours I have on record is one session. The game is mesmerizing and can get you lost in trying to complete that build or the research your doing, time just kinda vanishes. All in all this is a great game if you like the tiny details of colony sims, it actually reminds me a lot of rimworld but entirely medieval. I'd say this is an 8/10 and its still only is Early Access, I can't wait until the game is fully released, and while there are some bugs, they are easily dealt with and theres even a bug report button in game. Totally a great grab if your into small details, fully customized buildings, resource and temp management and if you like slow and controlled combat.

Just to say as well, I did not do your typical castle, I made a fully underground base with farming and animals on top and my colony has managed to function greatly, I was very surprised that building all the rooms, food storage and jobs all underground didn't cause any issues. If you get this game, start a small world in peaceful first to learn the controls and how to manage everything, I didn't and have made some very big mistakes but I also enjoy going about fixing things lol, Hope you all enjoy the game to.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would say buy it on sale, at least while it is on early access.

So first of all, this is a pretty good game very VERY similar to Rimworld in terms of mechanics. The main differences are that it is set in medieval times and it is 3D. The 3D thing is cool and adds some interesting depth (see what I did there) but otherwise it feels very much like Rimworld with a less developed "storyteller" system. If you like Rimworld, you would probably like this game but I am not sure it is going to give you very much that Rimworld doesn't give you already. That could change later in development but right now there isn't a whole lot there.

Good game but definitely early access. I have not encountered any bugs that are remotely game breaking but there are definitely small bugs and some systems still need tuning. Main issue with the game right now is that there isn't that much content. I have played for 10 hours and feel like I have done all of the interesting things there are to do. That said, the game has a lot of promise and I could imagine some really cool stuff as the systems are more advanced and there is more to research.\t\t\t\t\t
