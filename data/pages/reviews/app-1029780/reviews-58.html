\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has great potential, but it's hampered by seemingly random and near-constant crashes. I don't even know what's causing it, either. I've tried messing around with the graphics, but it still crashes. I've tried closing as many background tasks as possible without bricking my system, and it still crashes. And what makes it even worse is that is crashes during what are seemingly routine things like mining, building, chopping down trees, etc. Hopefully these issues get fixed, because I really want to play this game.

Update:

After looking at how early my first review was, I wanted to make a small update. The game is great, and I'm not being sarcastic. It's a really fun mix of combat strategy and city-builder that scratches the itch for a medieval history based game of this style. However, the biggest problem this game suffers from is routine and seemingly unexplained crashes, and so I've decided to keep my review negative until these crashes are fixed. I hope the developers work on performance and stability in the future, because as fun as this game is, it's tainted by this constant crashing issue.

Edit: Changing my review to positive because I figured out the reason my game was crashing. It was memory instability. Originally, I was running 32GB of DDR5 at 5600mhz. After resetting the memory to it's base speed of 4800mhz, the constant crashing issues completely disappeared. If you have constant crashing issues like I had, then memory instability may be the culprit.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGoing Medieval is a solid game that's worth playing if you enjoy colony-building and survival games. The game's medieval setting and sandbox gameplay provide endless possibilities for creativity and experimentation, and the graphics and sound design are both top-notch.

The game's combat system, however, leaves something to be desired. While it's not terrible, it lacks the polish and depth that some other games in the genre offer. Additionally, the game can be a bit overwhelming at first, as there's a lot to manage and learn.

That being said, Going Medieval is still a fun and engaging game that's worth checking out. Just be prepared to invest some time and effort to truly master its mechanics. With that in mind, I give Going Medieval a score of 7/10.

If you're a fan of medieval history or just enjoy building and managing your own settlement, this game is definitely worth a look. Plus, who doesn't want to live out their Game of Thrones fantasies and build their own castle? Just be sure to watch out for those pesky invaders who might try to crash your party.\t\t\t\t\t

Despite that, what it already offers sums up to a great experience and has already claimed my brain hostage for multiple hours and many more to come lol.
