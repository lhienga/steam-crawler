8/10

Summary: Owned the game one week and have played 75 hours!

Early Access has been hit-and-miss for me. I've had games get abandoned. I've had games I feel the designers "changed" to please the most vocal players and morph into something other than originally planned... And then I've had some that are pure gold. That being said, I now do much more research before buying an Early Access game.

Intentionally bought this game when NOT on sale to support it because:
-It's NOT RimWorld. Never played it ... can't get past the trailer. Like the concept, but don't care for the paper doll graphics or tower-defense aspect.
-It's NOT Banished. Played Banished and loved it, but the settlers/meeples are just numbers and you don't get "invested" in them.
-It's NOT Stranded: Alien Dawn. I would really have loved to play that, but will not buy it because of their use of the controversial Denuvo DRM. Plus, the discussion threads are full of bugs and complaints about it being stupid-hard.
-It's NOT punishing/hard. Watched a few videos, set up some good defenses, and raids have all been manageable.
-The dev is very engaged with weekly updates and a solid RoadMap.
-So far I find the game very engaging without being stressful. Yesterday they added the ability to customize the difficulty level rather than make the game easier/harder overall (bravo!). Zero crashes and almost zero bugs so far.

I've already got my money's worth, but excited to see what they have planned!\t\t\t\t\t
