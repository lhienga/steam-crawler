\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is obviously still in Early access so this review may be subject to change.

Overall, the game is pretty good! Its a relatively chill medieval rimworld-ish game. Food isn't really a matter and pretty easy to come by and manage. The real grind is making enough bricks to create your medieval hold of your fantasy.

o Graphics: Cartoon-ish, however it does have that uniqueness that can only be attributed to this game.

o Story: Its up to you to create your own.

o Game play: Little finicky at first. Coming from rimworld, the z-axis is one of the unique feature of the game. I really like the cellar mechanic and would be very keen to see a cave mechanic, mining for rare metals (idk how rich is irl Britain in minerals) and era progressions. (I am very keen to see late medieval stuff and even early renaissance)

My main gripe would be the lack of advance technologies/late game content. Sure there is 'advance research' but the advance research that I want to see is in structure. Like you need this structure to make this type of room that is needed for this bonus or there is some main objective that you have to commit to.

Lastly, mods. Mods mods mods mods!!1! This game has the mod potential of skyrim and rimworld. I am keen to buy DLCs to support this game (like rimworld's Royalty, Ideology and Bionic dlcs). However, mods will make the game live essentially forever.

Overall great game with limitless potential!\t\t\t\t\t
