\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy this game. I'm a huge fan of base building and kingdom building games. This game has a lot of potential and the developers are very responsive, consistently improving it and listen to their player base...which is awesome. You'll find it as a fun distraction but it's not quite there yet.

They just added water effects which has expanded gameplay with fishing and creating water obstacles/features. But that has led to a number of bugs where characters will get stuck and the only way to resolve the issue is to reload from the last save. I had a character go unconcsious because they were stuck in water, I sent another npc in to get them and they got stuck, then the third npd got stuck as well. It was weird but a reload resolved it. This game also is lacking when it comes to relationship gameplay, food for animals and the tech tree. Your characters don't interact except when eating or praying. That's it. If you raise animals, you have to dedicate one of your toons to take care of them 24/7 which would be fine unless you like playing a low population settlement. The tech tree is tedious. You basically have to assign one toon to study/write nonstop in order for you to advance to basic things...like a stove or better defensive tools, or medicine.

It has a way to set task by priority scale but there's no way to queue up the task you want your npc to focus on automatically. The priority coding doesn't work properly all the time. For example, I had cooking as priority 1 and research as priority 2 for a character...and they spent their time chopping trees down when I had the ingredients for cooking stocked, a cook limit of 50 with only 2 meals in inventory. I left the game running and my character was basically starving instead of cooking fish/meat in stock. This is a relatively new issue I've been having but just a heads up.

This game is really great but I will be taking a break from it as they work on it or smooth out some game play issues.

It's definitely worth playing and I've poured a substantial amount of hours into genuinely enjoying the game.\t\t\t\t\t
