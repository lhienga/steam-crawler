\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm giving a thumbs-up not because of what it is, but based on the potential it has to be. A lot of comparisons are made to Rimworld, and a lot of the interface and gameplay is like that but in 3D. However, the charm of Rimworld was the personality the pawns had. Chatting to each other, getting into fights, marrying each other, etc. The guys in this game may as well be worlds apart as they never interact. I appreciate this is still early-access (hence the thumbs-up), but without that this game will remain superficial. I like base/castle building, although it is a bit finicky at times, I got through the entire tech-tree in about 3 hours of playing.\t\t\t\t\t
