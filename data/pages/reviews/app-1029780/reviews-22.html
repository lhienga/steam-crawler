\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love colony sim games, this one stands apart particularly with the building and terrain deforming, and now with the addition of water there are even more possibilities - I've been having a blast getting water to flow into my underground castle city. This is still an early access game and some features aren't complete, or certain systems aren't completely fleshed out (settler AI, religion, faction raids ,combat ect.) but the framework for each of these systems is there and it is very promising, example: religion is present, but only in the sense that certain villagers prefer one or another of the two choices, as far as I can tell this only effects which shrine they use and doesn't have any other effect (yet)

that being said, the development road map is highly visible and can even be viewed from the game. Very excited for the potential here to be the absolute best colony sim game out there. Very frequently updated and great engagement with the community from the dev team, awesome game changing features keep getting dropped in causing me to come back to it multiple times over the past year or so.

highly recommend, if you like rimworld, combined with "minecraft-esque" building, in a medieval setting - you will dig this game.\t\t\t\t\t
