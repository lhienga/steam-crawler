\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery solid colony sim game. If you've played any colony sim games, especially RImworld which it borrows a lot of UI and gameplay aspects from, you will find it very easy to get used too. Probably a decent choice for getting into the genre as well. My only real complaints are that it feels a little bit slow and a little bit basic. Events are less often and less varied than other colony sims, with only the necessities (Enemy attack, new villager, trader) which makes gameplay feel a little bland. I have yet to reach the endgame and I am only playing on the default normal difficulty so perhaps that can change. However, the fact that my only complaint for a game this early in development is that it is "a little bland" shows its great quality and potential. Going Medieval has a very strong foundation and in a couple years it may easily be one of the best games in the genre. If you are thinking of getting into the colony sim genre or simply want to try something new it is a great buy.\t\t\t\t\t
