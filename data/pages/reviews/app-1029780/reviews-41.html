\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am a massive fan of Banished and I wanted something similar. Going Medieval was an excellent comparative game to Banished with an element of being raided and trading with outside parties. I really enjoyed the game play and love the variety of elements that are in the game. There are a few bugs here an there but that's to be expected of an early access game. I look forward to seeing what the polished game looks like. One current issue is that there is not a single hare in the game. I think that the wolves and foxes eat them early game so there are none for the villagers to hunt. This is a bummer to an achievement hunter such as myself as hunting 5x and 20x hares are the only two trophies I have left. Since in like 15 games I have only seen one hare that was promptly killed by a wolf, I doubt I can finish that trophy. Hope the game devs can balance out the hare reproduction and animal hunting aspect. I bet that can also help low level marksmen villagers up their shooting game. Overall, excellent game with a few areas that need polishing. Highly recommend if you are a fan of village builder games.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game a lot and wish every fortune of good luck for the developers to complete their roadmap. I think this game is very in-depth and time has been taken to make sure it plays very well - there is so much going on in the game. I am 24 hours in when writing and only barely scratched the surface.

Pro's
- The weather effects are amazing, the rain is great and feels like everywhere is sodden and muddy.
- There is always something to do so the time just flies past when you are playing - and it's enjoyable.
- The building is made to be very easy to do.
- The music just sets the scene perfectly as your serfs just get to work.
- It is reminiscent of the RimWorld formula but in 3D and different Medieval theme. I prefer this personally.
- Feels very Medieval when you are playing it.

Not so good
- It is not intuitive on how to build underground but after a few searches and examples on websites it is clearer.
- There are no toilets or latrines in the game, I think we eat, work, sleep and produce waste and this last bit is missing as if that's not managed, life becomes very short.
