\r\n\t\t\t\t\tToo early in development and light on content to call this a great game but everything so far is very promising. The game as is is stable and runs well, there are regular updates, and the 3D voxel-world colony sim idea is novel and works well. The scale of what is possible to create here vs. other similar games is limited, and I foresee will probably always be due to the complexity of the map per area and corresponding pathfinding logic, but the ability to incorporate verticality into designs makes designing builds very engaging despite the relatively limited available area. Overall the game has a long way to go but seems to be on track for very good things.\t\t\t\t\t
