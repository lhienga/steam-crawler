\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello everyone !

Here is my review for 05 / 09 / 2022.

Pros:
- Good gameplay mechanisms:
- Using highness as a majour gameplay part, like conserving food, or aiming farther with bowmen;
- Material used is not only adding HPs to the building, it also have other properties, like temperature isolation.
- Ennemies are difficult, but not too difficult. It's cool because we're not frustrated.
- Beautiful game. The game is pleasant to watch, in all seasons, even in underground, there are some cool details, like if you started to mine a tile, it will be a little fractured.
- The lore is cool, after the Great Plague is a surprisingly fitting period to put the game into. Also I like the description loading page with custom names, it reminds me of Stonehearth.
- As far as I saw the tutoral, it's very well build, especially the building support part, where it explains all the possibilities of building supports with videos and all. The video is also not no slow, and we are not frustrated when we see it.

- Cons:
- Gameplay problems:
- When we build some items to store, we can't multiselect. So if you have 100 storages to change, well you have to do the same task 100 times. I have 400-500 shelfs on my game, and I felt that.
- When we tame an animal, it will follow you. It also applies to the other than dog animals. So if you tame too much your cow, it will follow you, and not be in it's pen.
- Some items are blocking the production of others. Example, when you produce honey, you also produce beeswax. However, when you want to produce a limited amount of honey, and you use all of your beeswax, then the beekeper will not produce more honey (and thus beeswax). Maybe there is a trick, but I didn't found.

Of course, it's a new game, so these incoherences will be erased sooner or later.

Suggestions:
- Adding a mod manager. The management games are perfect to add some mods on !
- Soil tiling to increase feritility of the soil (to lessen the growth time for example);
- Complexification of food chains. We have 4-5 alcool types, but only 2 food types.
- Adding hay uses. I have like 3000 grain and 15000 hay at my base, it's too much, and I can't sell it :D;
- Adding other storage points or increase storage place for items. 25 x 11 x 11 (11 side square) carrots are not suffisent. Even with only shelves.

Of course, a lot of us will compare this game to Rimworld, but I think that it's biased for many points. Give it a try, you'll not be deceived :) .\t\t\t\t\t
