\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun and entertaining but still needs some work. This is a game I'll play for like a week and then not play for a few months. For sure worth buying if you enjoy Sims.

Pros:
-Tons of fun if you get some buddies to play with you
-Building mechanics are easy to use and fairly easy if you've played games like Rust.
-The Ranching is easy to pick up and get busy fast if you don't pay attention to your animals.
-Since I bought the game devs have made updates regularly and listen to feedback.

Cons:
-Vehicle physics make driving a pain at first.
-Not very many side quests so it gets repetitive once you have an established ranch.
-Chopping trees doesn't give enough wood IMO.
-Shooting mechanics are a little weird but you don't need them all to often anyway.

Overall if you're looking for a game to play with your friends while relaxing and drinking some beers, this is a 12/10 game for about a couple weeks or so at a time (like that minecraft urge you get once a year)

If you're looking for something super realistic, this game has the potential to get there and has come some way. Even after i bought it 7 months ago.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who's not typically a fan of the farming sim outside of the occasional exception (i.e. Stardew Valley and a brief stint with Rune Factory) I went into this game thinking I wouldn't really enjoy it. I had tried my hand at Farming Sim 22 after watching a LGIO video about it, but I found it a bit too farmy for my liking. That said, my wife thought this game might be a fun experience for the both of us, so I decided to give it a whirl. After a few hours I can honestly say I really enjoy it! My wife and I have been having a lot of fun with the game thus far, and as the game is in Early Access I can only assume it will (hopefully) get better as updates roll out.

PROS:

- Large Open Sandbox-style farmland to build on to your liking.
- Fun base/farm building mechanics akin to ARK?Valhiem?similar
- Decent prefab building options for players not interested in custom base building
- BEEKEEPING!

CONS:
- Very little direction after the tutorial
- Very basic quest system that can be exploited for easy money
- Janky physics at times resulting in frustrating, allbeit funny item placements
- far too easy to pick up placed tools, leading to the player replacing and restarting production.

I'm sure I'll have more to love/hate as we play more of the game, but most of my gripes could be fixed with a patch in the future. All in all really loving this experience and enjoying the casual gaming time with my wife.

Tentative 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
