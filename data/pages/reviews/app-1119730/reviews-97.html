\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a good game so far, play time is about 30hrs at time of review. From an overview the game is decent from taking care of live stock to hunting and of course building your ranch. As with all games there are a few bugs and the development team does update an fix some every so often which is a plus. This game also supports controllers (Xbox - PC types) although since the normal buttons can not be changed adding the controller can add aggravation because of the overlapping of buttons. (as a side note if you do use a controller you have to assign each button since its not auto assigned with a template in game) I don't know if a wheel works or not might be something to try to make the driving a bit easier.

I like the building aspect of the game from logging to building. The hunting aspect needs a little work and a good hunting rifle would help a great deal and adding of an icon "scope" at the end of the mouse point to see what you are aiming at would be helpful as well. a down side to hunting wolves and bears is that you can be mauled and killed if you shoot an miss... Then the game says "you've died" reminds me of the 1980's games "Game Over" lol. Then you have to exit the game and reload it because at the time of this review the "go to main menu" button doesn't work. Taking care of animals is a bit of work since the food an upkeep goes fast the more animals you have. The animals can die if you don't keep up with them and if they do die you can then process them for meats an cook with other foods grown in your greenhouse or gardens to increase or decrease the value of profit when you go to sale the final product.
I would rate this game 5 stars and say Thanks to the Creators for a cool Ranch Simulator game!\t\t\t\t\t
