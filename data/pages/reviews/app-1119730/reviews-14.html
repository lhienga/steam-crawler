\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun and fairly addictive game... preferred it in some ways over the other Farming Simulator franchise, especially the animal husbandry and making money.
If you're into arable farming, it's kinda lame however. Growing wheat in order to feed animals was hardly economically more beneficial than just paying to fill the grain silos themselves, after you've forked out for fertiliser and spent time cultivating, sowing and harvesting the land. I literally grew wheat once and then abandoned that part of the game. Horses, although cool and fun to ride and bid for at auction, have limited interaction capability apart from riding. You can't breed them either. There are also a couple of weird bugs, like fertilised chicken eggs not hatching after a while. Vehicle handling is also a bit on the wild side.
Having said that, I did have fun getting rich selling mouldy cow cheese and micromanaging my bred farm animals! And building in the game is good. You can choose prefab farm houses that you supply the material for and then, once complete, spawn furniture and have a nice lived-in feel. Or you can build your own structures, although the lack of ability to repaint or texture the brown wood is a bit disappointing. Definitely recommend though for what the game CAN do.\t\t\t\t\t
