First, thank you and congrats on the game, I love it!

Great depth ranching life, lovely landscapes.

I would like to leave my feedback on some things that I think would make the game even greater if you are interested.

Lovely in-game houses, but this game makes you want to build your own, that's why I've customised a whole house, but there is not enough furniture to make it feel like a home. Could it be possible to add some more furniture, for example, kitchen, chairs, double bed and a bathroom?

Also, thinking about who plays solo, would it be possible to give us the option to hire employees or have children, to help with the work around the farm? There's just so much to do (not complaining).

Another thing that playing for so many hours I noticed would increase the quality of the game play, an inventory, or be possible to carry anything inside the coolers lol. That would come so handy for building, carrying the planks, paint etc...

One more suggestion. I miss physiological needs, such as hunger, thirst or dirtiness.

Talking about dirt, would be lovely if where the pigs are there will be mud (or is it ♥♥♥♥?!) :D Imagine.... <3

Another thing that would be so helpful, animal enclosure (fence) that is ready in square and round shape, that could be increased as we wish. That would save so much time, especially because I find it difficult to work with the fence we have right now.

I have many more suggestions, like wardrobe change in-game. But I know it might be too much xD

Anyways, finally, what about an auto save?

I recommend this game, it's so good. Definitely has so much more potential.
