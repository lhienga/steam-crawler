\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's alright but severely lacking. For a game that supposedly came out of EA you'd expect something better.

The following are some of my observations & problems with the game:

- The town, buildings etc DO NOT have any lighting at all. When it's dark out, everywhere is completely dark. No street lights, no interior lights. The workers and townfolk are all sitting in complete darkness...

-Driving is horrible, it is no different than driving on legitimate ice all around. The NPC cars are super weird as well, as they will spawn nearby and jump on the road, drive very slowly.

-Character animations are incredibly bad and robotic. Your movements are stiff, the animations are janky. There is no fall damage and horrible physics. Cars don't get damaged or give damage when you hit something.

-Furnitures in built homes don't work or don't work as they should. The double bed doesn't allow another player to sleep together with you. You can't break these furnitures as well as it will also destroy the house...

-The map and town feel desolate. There is no thought given to their design and purpose, everything is just made with premade assets and slapped together. UI is horrible and far too simple, most often also broken.

-Worst of all, it doesn't let you play without making or linking Epic account.

Game is alright enough to play despite these flaws but they severely drop the enjoyment you could get. I hope and pray developers will take heed and continue to improve the game but as it is with most games, likely will never happen.\t\t\t\t\t
