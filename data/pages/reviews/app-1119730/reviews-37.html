\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game WAS a fun, if janky to play, ranch / building sim. The update to the new engine removed all the goodwill I had by making it pretty but nauseating to play.

The awkward first person view that had some issues when picking up / placing items when carrying something has become a serious chore because the new pretty boxes make it harder to see around. Looking around is slow and clunky, and for some reason you can't aim down the sites of the rifle in first person, so you have to switch to third for an aim indicator. This was funny before, now it's just annoying.

Driving in 3rd person feels like you are not in control of the camera. When (when, not if) you flip your UTV, the camera goes into some weird direction. All the NCP cars look like they have hydraulics (bouncing up and down the roads), and your own car feels that way over just about any terrain.

The new town looks cool and has a lot of potential, but right now is empty. They also removed the ability to take multiple quests from one NPC, which is annoying.

I like the combinations and food crafting; it's easy to get into a nice daily rhythm (wake up, milk goats, milk cows, make cheese, etc.) with enough styles / ease of making money that you can probably find something you enjoy doing. I like the variety of vehicles; there is a surprising amount of non-farming vehicles for a farm / ranch game.

The music was pretty great, if strange. Of course, the update broke the radio, so there's that.

Hints for new players: Buy a lot of coolers (you can put almost anything in them) and several pans. Trailers are great for logging (if you put the tailgate down, the UTV works in the early game). Honey / bees are good for early game cash. Animals will try to escape pens and barns so you might want an "airlock" to keep them from getting out. Pigs multiply like rabbits. Go to the settings and change the day length to longer, otherwise you end up spending early game sleeping more than doing anything else.

I really hope to get back to it and give it a recommend, but the new update makes me really dislike it right now.\t\t\t\t\t
