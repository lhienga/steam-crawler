\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game but still feels incomplete, after a bit caring it can shine like a star on the Steam main page. I listed some of the problems/suggestions of/for the game down below:
1-FPS problem makes the game insufferable, especially when you build a large farm with a lot of animals.
2-They should add more npc for more different quests and interaction.
3-On the multiplayer side pings on the map should be seen by everyone to communicate easier.
4-After you reach some point you feel pointless and bored of the game, at least thats how i felt like. Maybe new quests, animals, crops can fix this issue. Also planting crops don't pay the effort you give they sell to market too cheap.
5-Trees needs to regrow after you chop them, or when you left the log at least.
6-The river at the map is unusable, you can't even get close to it. Maybe they can bring fishing system, boats and a fisherman as a new npc to sell the fishes.
7-Map feels a bit small after a while maybe they can add another town. When you reach the end of the map a tunnel leads you to a new town with new farm, new npc and new biome. It can have different weather conditions, crops, animals also.
8-It definitely needs a better ui asap, this ui feels so primitive. For example to check your budget you need to go the escape menu. It should be seen on the main game screen. Also they should add health bar.
9-Guns' mechanic is horrific, They need a better ADS and hipfire system.
10-The game needs either autosave system or a save system when you buy/sell something. It feels too bad when you see all the things you made for hours didn't saved.
All in all i still have good time while playing it with my friends, thumbs up.\t\t\t\t\t
