\r\n\t\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t
\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game I really wanted to like because it really does have a lot of potential to be a really fun game. But, in it's current state I just can't in good conscience recommend it at this point in time - even at its sale price, nor is it in a complete state and chose to ultimately refund it. The controls are probably the jankiest I've seen in a game, there's some very simple quality of life features that aren't currently in game - such as regrowing trees...once they're gone, they're gone? Really? I know it sounds a little nitpicky, but when there's a limited amount around your ranch, not being able to regrow trees or anything is a little absurd to me. Sure you can purchase what you need from the store, but for me at least that takes a little bit of the fun out. My biggest concern is that at the time of this review I have found no real road map, and the developers haven't seemed to be very active with the community. And after being burnt by Valheim, that's a huge red flag for me. Maybe given a little bit more time the developers end up addressing what the community is suggesting and the game becomes more complete, which if that happens I'll be happy to give it another shot. But, for now this is a big nope for me.\t\t\t\t\t
