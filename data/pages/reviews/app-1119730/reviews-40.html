\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game isn't great. I bought this during early access and kept hopping back in when they'd put out an update. I kept waiting to see it evolve, but I never felt like it did. The upgrade from UE4 to UE5 DID make it look prettier and run better (great decision).

There's maybe 15-20 hours of content if you take your time. And that's playing it solo. I actually expect you would spend much less time if you played with friends because it's honestly so limited in what you CAN do. There are basically 2 types of farming: container and tractor. Nothing in between.

I spent most of my time raising pigs and goats. Once I built the premium chicken coop the chickens were just... there. Eggs and chicken sausage were a nice little supplement I checked every few days.

I just described about 70% of the gameplay. In two paragraphs. You can hunt deer, bears, and wolves. You can do a few quests for a handful of the folks around town. MOST of your "interactions" with the world is through computer terminals with an NPC standing nearby staring past you. It ends up feeling pretty flat and dead as a world.

And for me, the fact that single player doesn't allow me to pause... it grates on me because it's such a basic feature that is just ignored. When I went looking I found where it was said "well, the game was meant as co-op and you can't pause in co-op." Like that is the only way people SHOULD play their game.

I wanted to like this game so much. But honestly, there is neither lore to keep me interested or enough variety in game play to keep me coming back.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t One of the aspects that needs to be improved is the game’s economy system. The prices of animals, crops, and items seem to be arbitrary and unrealistic. There is no clear logic behind these numbers, and they make the game feel unbalanced and unfair. It would be better if the game had a more realistic and dynamic economy system.
Another issue that I encountered is the vehicle handling. They feel too heavy and sluggish after mounting something in the front or back.
Also building system is not very user-friendly or intuitive. It is hard to build a more regular shape when building a fence, it would be much better to snap the fence posts to a grid or align them to a straight line. Manually adjust the angle and the position of each post, which is tedious and frustrating. I wish the game had a smarter and easier building system.
Despite these flaws, this game still has a lot of potential and charm. However, it is important to recognize that the game is still in development and the features may change in the future. There may not be a lot of playable content at the moment, so it is best to wait for an update before getting into the game. I hope the developers will continue to work on the game and make it better and more enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
