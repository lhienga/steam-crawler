\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA lot of potential here. It's fun and I can see this getting to be a really great game once it gets some issues cleaned up.

There's a lot of House Flipper vibes, a lot of Stardew Valley vibes.

Graphics are great in some places, bad in others.

Not sure I'd recommend it in its current state, but I think it's got a lot of room to grow as long as the devs aren't cashing out on the current version and fleeing. If you're willing to let the game grow, then get it on sale.

For devs:
*The game world doesn't feel 'lived in'. Stores are very weird feeling and bare. Store "owners" sit behind the desk in the middle of the night when the store is closed...
*Player animations are just...odd. Staring at my player it's like a caveman lumbering around. People don't move the way the player does.
*Demolition was just clunky. Not at all as fun as it could have been.
*As many have said, driving is just chaos. Thank you for not allowing us to damage stuff when we crash---because it happens constantly.\t\t\t\t\t
