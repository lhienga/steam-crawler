\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShıttiest game I've ever played with a high potential to become something better, if devs work hard and turn this into something better I will change this review. Honestly it doesn't feel like they've put effort into it. Probably another forever early access that will be abandoned in few years.

Pros:
Fun to mess around
Cool bullet physics, they sometimes bounce off which is something I truly love no matter how unrealistic it is time to time.
Worth the 1.25 USD I've spent on it thanks to being a Turkish citizen. They've also increased the game 2.5x in my region for some reason.

Cons:
Absolutely zero QoL in every aspect.
Everything is bound to E key so... bad.
If you accidentally try to demolish something, no way to cancel.
Bad gun mechanics in every way. Can skip reload by changing weapons.
Somewhat bad animations
Awful purchase UI but compared to other games with similar layout, not too much different (House Flipper is a bit better in comparison)
Empty world with empty houses, like there's no furniture in it.
Game loop is literally; gain money, buy things that make money, sell the product and buy again.
I remember playing this game when it first released back in 2021 and I can certainly say that nothing feels like changed. I bet someone with adequate skill can do everything they've done in 2 years in just a week.
