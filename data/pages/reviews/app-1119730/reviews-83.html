\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dont know about single player, but this game is a blast with my friend. Perfectly scratches that farming/ranching itch that we havent been able to get from other like games. The act of raising cows and goats has been the best for me.

Milking actually takes effort, its not just click and done.
Making cheeses and meat products equally takes effort (and for some ALOT)
Animals gradually grow, they dont just hop from baby to adult. I love this so much as i love watching my livestock grow up.
Has a few bugs with desync in multiplayer but thus far it has all been hilarious, and thus far only occurs when someone who isnt the host drives into the ranch with a car (and even then its not all the time, but having trailers hitched to vehicles makes it much more frequent, so un-hitch trailers that arent in use!)

A few things i would love to see in the furture is the ability to breed and raise your own horses, play with a genetics system to breed the best. You can buy horses and train them as it is right now, but ive found more often than not you actually lose profit when training them up to full and selling them. So having the ability to breed our own horses would be so much more rewarding and worth the effort that goes into training.

Another smaller thing, the ability to make butter. I think playing with a mechanic to churn butter could be fun, at least until working up to something more automatic once you tire of it.

Elsewise, for early access this game has been everything my friend and i have been looking for in a game. 10/10 recommend and cant wait to see what future updates bring!\t\t\t\t\t

Livestock can get demanding fast, even with the timescale set to its slowest setting.
Performance is OK on my RX 6750XT, but the framerate is choppy and gives my headaches if I play for a long time in one sitting (and I'm an Arma player, so I'm not usually picky about framerate!).
The driving is wacky but can be really fun for all of its quirks - I strongly recommend building a ramp off the cliff between your ranch and the restaurant.
