\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuper fun game to play with friends - just make sure to buy lots of bullets because they will kill each other a lot. Lots of features have been added over the years since original release which is great. It's a sandbox type of game so you can really do whatever you want. Hunting is a fun thing to do on the side of farming. Car physics are comical but sometimes leads to unfortunate bugs of vehicles getting stuck in the air (and never coming down). Quests from NPCs offer helpful monetary starts at the beginning of the game when you're clawing for a dollar.

However, as a crazy horse girl, I was hoping the horses system would be a bit better. There's no breeding and the horse is constantly trying to run away. The auction system is a bit funky in that horses always get bid up by the NPCs to be overpriced. By the time it takes to fully train the horses, you will still sell them for either a loss, or maybe a max profit of $200. Given how expensive the horses are and how time consuming and repetitive the training is, it's not worth messing with the horses unless you're me and want them for the aesthetic.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI play a lot of different games and been on a few survey groups (Final Fantasy online, WoW, Secondlife, Roblox, Hayday, Farm Simulator, Dual Universe, Guild Wars, Valheim, Minecraft, Creativerse, Star Stables, etc.) and always look for games to do fishing or building with co-op to enjoy the company of my friends or play offline alone.

I Love the way this game is going and how the mechanics to start working and figure out.

Fence sizes would nice to complete a padlock, some more options for house building plans (also furniture if building on your own) as well as maybe premade Animal padlocks plans to build also. Terra-forming the land options? Could add in breeding options of animals to throw off different colors....I think I'm getting way ahead of myself, but I listen to what others look for and communicate what we would enjoy better in the gameplay.

The Burger joint has maybe little more you can do, specially if any meters are added to keep yourself alive is in the future. Options to build in town with other town buildings, or plots that one of your friends can buy and build their own farm that can add in or out of game to connect with possibly to help or hang out while online.

***With that said should have options for anyone playing the game to save and also enter with a server that the other player doesn't have to be online to continue building, feeding, etc with password to get in as I was always getting kicked out and everything I did was gone that I worked on several times which became frustrating.***

Adding more towns to visit maybe different themed areas to venture to as doing the same quests over and over gets repetitive. (More expansions to do this) More recipes for cooking. Could also maybe add in mining caves to mine to build? Butcher shop? Craftsmen shop to build things like buildings, furniture, etc. More equipment choices. Options with planting that give you a feeling of achievement to what your working on, maybe level your planting up with doing this.

I play with a farm on something called a G&S system on another game that I love that is like this in a way, as other try to create more that is like it but this old way of working your way up gives you more pride in what you do.

I see great potential for this game to expand and build out into something amazing.

Fishing with leveling would be great to be added into the game with a fishing boat and recipes to cook it. Give little more options to character clothes or options to make them in game, sheep for wool, spinning equipment to make yarn and dyes.

People love to connect together and build, play, farm together and its hard to find good co-op games to enjoy along with the realistic feel of a game to decompose, or strive to work together to build a great place.

All in all I would pay a subscription to this game if a lot of these added features would be added in to create more content for others to enjoy as well and bring in endless options.\t\t\t\t\t
