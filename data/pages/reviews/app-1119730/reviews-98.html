\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI picked this up on a whim in my quest for the ideal farming game for myself, and honestly this is the game that’s scratched that itch the best so far. Its early access is simple so far but incredibly promising.

I assume I’m in the minority, but one of the major criticisms I’ve had personally with Stardew Valley and other similar games is that in addition to farming, it expects a bunch of other unrelated tasks and interacting with NPCs ad nauseum, and god forbid locking progress and features behind these tasks. Those games are still good and have their purpose! But I have been searching long and hard for my “weird little freak hermit that occasionally descends from their mountain hideaway with a chest freezer full of sausages to pawn before scuttling back into solitude and nobody questions this serial killer behavior” simulator, and I’ve finally found that.

That being said, I fully support the devs in whatever vision and ambition they have for their game, even if it includes quests and NPCs, but I would be extremely charmed if they kept it possible or at least left in a mode to simply free-farm as the game is basically presented now.

The game is not without its flaws, the driving in particular being the most egregious. Inputs are delayed and the vehicles are incredibly floaty at any sort of reasonable speed, but this is somewhat reasonably compensated for with a quick way to unstick yourself from a predicament as well as having no damage system to the vehicles. Still, in a game where there is a lot of space to roam around and a lot of stuff to haul, it sticks out.

My other gripe is that opening gates is like opening the Walmart doors on Black Friday. It seems like every animal in a ten mile radius makes a beeline to go through it, blocking you and whatever it is you might be trying to move through it and escape. I’ve had to basically fashion airlocks for my larger pens and it kind of breaks up some things I’d like to build and operate.

But with a grain of salt, it's early access, and overall I love the game so far. I look forward to seeing more potential crops, animals, and products! I have only played solo as of now, but I can only see the experience being enriched by like-minded friends and there’s plenty of space to spread out. I’m reviewing this as someone who was looking desperately for a no-frills farming game, so if you’re looking for a story or grand experience or lots of world interaction, this is probably not the game for you (yet!!). But if you just want to shut your brain off and hit the ground running (or more likely, hit a bump, backflip, and slide 200 feet on your front bumper before smashing directly into a deer), then I can definitely recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
