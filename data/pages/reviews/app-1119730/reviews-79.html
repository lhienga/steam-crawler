\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am obsessed with this game. It’s exactly the sort of game I enjoy, I’ve inherited a farm from a deceased family member and have to build from almost nothing to get it running strong. The graphics of this are fantastic and a lot of the mechanics feel very true-to-real-life. Like how costly it is to raise animals vs the return on investment you get. And ESPECIALLY the cost of farming holy cow. It’s one steep bill to get all the equipment needed to get going.

A few things I feel can be improved on is the controls. Right now it’s pretty darn impossible to drive around with a controller joystick, which is what the official steam deck layout has you use. It’s just telling the game you’re using wasd and it makes turning and such an all or nothing endeavor. The game needs native controller support IMO

It also needs a PAUSE BUTTON. What if the baby starts crying? Can’t just ignore the baby, that’s frowned upon by the government. (And ya know bad for the baby) but if you don’t go back to your tent to save and then exit the game (which can take like 5 minutes depending on where you are), time will March forward and oooooh boy if you need to pause in the middle of hunting have fun being murdered by a bear and losing the entire days progress anyway. So please, developers, a pause button.\t\t\t\t\t
