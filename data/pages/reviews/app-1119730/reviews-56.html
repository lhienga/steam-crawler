\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have about 45 hours into this game. I bought it during early access. This game has a lot of potential but after playing the new beta for an hour or I am disappointed with their decision to leave early access. This is not a refined game but a very complete demo showing some great ideas and showing what "can" be done and a vision for a good game.

I assume that by leaving early access the developers have decided to release this version and move on. Regardless of what the developer might say about their intentions I have witnessed too many times game that leave early access and are abandoned shortly thereafter.

This is why I can't recommend it at this time.
1. The vehicles and driving mechanics are quite unstable.
When the game first came out the developer added a "flip" option in case your vehicle turned over. I assumed this would be a temporary fix until something more permanent was developed. With the beta the vehicles are even more unstable than they were before the beta. In fact the ATV is constantly flipping and is very difficult to control. In the previous version the ATV would not flip unless you did something stupid. Now it flips all the time.
2. The map you see in the beta and will see in the full release has been improved to give the appearance of a town. It adds some character but this seems like a "lipstick on a pig" effort to make the game appear like it has more content or "world" than it really does. Certainly without this, the game is a very good demo in my harsh opinion.
3. Lack of "things to do". There are some production chains that seem quite well thought out like cheese production or sausages. But there are no production chains for the immense farming and gardening.
4. Lack of quests. there are only half a dozen quests to do. There is no variation. There is no purpose to create a complex production chain for the purpose of fulfilling quests. The basics are there but there is simply no "Content". For example a good quest is granny likes bacon and eggs in the morning. great. But the restaurant has no quests? How about something like deliver 1000 sausages. Give me a REASON to keep playing.
5. Oil wells? What random thing does an oil pump have to do with ranching? This was a random and incomplete addition to the game. I feel like there could have been something you could do with the oil like maybe an interactive POI that lets you refine the oil or something. Again doesn't really fit with the theme of the game but at least make it more than just a money making feature.
6. MOTION SICKNESS. If you suffer from motion sickness avoid this game. There is no way to turn off head bobbing. I would call this "drunken rancher simulator" even though there is no alcohol involved. The camera dips left and right. Don't drive vehicles in first person or you will have a headache even without motion sickness. I have been drunk and this is the best drunk simulator there is. This is my beef with all gaming developers. They try to make things feel natural but real life is not a gopro attached to your head. in real life our brains smooth things out. I have driven ATVs in very rough situations and never does my vision flop around like it does in this game.

Summary

Is the game fun? Yes it is a very fun "EARLY ACCESS" game but it is not ready to leave early access and NO I don't recommend it. (I would recommend it if it stays in early access). If the game continues to get updates and new content after a year I may change my recommendation.
