\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWasn't sure how I was going to initially like this game. It was on my wishlist for awhile and I finally went through with the purchase after snagging a deal on it.

Pros-
1. I like the variety of livestock types the game provides. I
2. The ability to make the ranch exactly how you want layout wise is really nice. I like being able to customize the setup with fences and such.

Neutral Thoughts
1. The weather system is fine, but it would be nice to have some sort of seasons and/or weather information panel displayed somewhere. Sometimes it is snowing and my character is out there running around in a tank top.
2. The graphics are fine, but not outstanding. Lower range when compared to other similar games. I don't think this impacts my game as much, but just something to note.

Cons-
1. My character seems very lonely. There are no NPC's to interact with, besides the limited interactions with some store clerks/quest tasks. I know this isn't what the game it about, but may make it more interesting.
2. The breeding system is not great. For instance, every one of my chicks born is a hen. Almost impossible to get a rooster. I understand they did this because roosters and valued so much higher in the shop than hens, but not really how nature works.
3. Limited number of crops available to grow. You also have to use planter boxes for the garden plants. Wish there was a way to till them in the ground.
4. Limited crop plots. If you have ever played another farming simulator, this will be very evident.
5. A single log only makes 2 wood planks. So tedious.
6. The wildlife could act a bit more wild-lifey. The deer just hop around aimlessly waiting for you to shoot them.

Wishlist Items -
1. Seasonal impacts on crop and garden growth.
2. When you scroll over a livestock animal, more information such as sex and age would be helpful.
3. More ranch buildings and items available to build.
4. More livestock breeds.
5. The ability to chick the chicken that keeps attacking me every time I collect eggs. :)
6. I wish the breeding mechanic was a little more realistic. At this time, a male will only breed with one female a day. If you've worked on a farm, you know this to be quite unrealistic during breeding season.

Some other wishlist items they already seem to be tackling in upcoming updates, so I wouldn't list them here.

Overall opinion - The game is worth a buy. If you are in to very relaxed independent (maybe even soothing) gameplay, this is going to be it for you. If you are really into farming, like you have a much higher interest in farming equipment and a bit more realistic crops and farming mechanisms in your game, you will be disappointed here. If you are just in it to make a bunch of money and breed a bunch of animals, and are okay with the relatively lower graphics and realistic features, I think you will enjoy it enough to get your monies worth.\t\t\t\t\t
