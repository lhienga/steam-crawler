\r\n\t\t\t\t\tso initially it's a decent game at first you can get into it you can build your own ranch raise and care for pigs chickens goat horses and beef and dairy cows each animal providing a specific resource

the vehicles are not bad but theres not to many to choose from and in truth there is likely only one you will go for aside from the mandatory vehicles if your concidering agriculture alongside animal husbandry

the build size is quite big concidering the size of the map which is extremely small.

there are a few methods for making money raise animals milk cows for milk or beef and chickens for eggs or pigs for pork and either sell as is or turn them into cheese curd meatballs or sausages or you can grow crops and sell them or find bees in the world and sell the honey they produce or find 2 oil slicks and builld oil drills or a combination there off

but thats really about it. the problem is the early onset of gameplay requires you to clear the ranch of most if not all of the trees that have over run the ranch and it can take ages just to chop them all down and turn them into logs and thus wooden planks.

animal movemenet animations gives off playstation 2 era vibes which is not what you would expect from a modern game on a modern game engine

some vehicle physics can get pretty tempremental

tasks do become very repetetive after awhile

hunting isn't really that well thought out

maps way to small

not many places to sell stuff to

the game needs alot of work and i mean alot of work before it can be concidered great right now i'd rate the game 4/10\t\t\t\t\t
