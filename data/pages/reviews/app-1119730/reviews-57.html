\r\n\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t
\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlthough the concept for the game is an amazing one, the implementation of basic systems just isn't there. I have tried and tried to give this game the benefit of the doubt and tried to be understanding given that the development team is small. However, the core systems of the game are just as broken today, roughly two weeks away from FULL RELEASE, as they were when I first got the game. The vehicle system still feels like you are skating around on skis, even though they have "improved" it multiple times. The physics engine is absolutely atrocious along with most hitbox locations. When you look around in first person view it still feels like you are drunk, the camera whips back and forth just like the vehicles do. The farming system isn't even working as of the last time I checked and the gardening is pointless. All you can do is sell your produce for a small profit. The animal husbandry is bare bones at best with very little to do with the "products" you gain from the animals. You can make beef or pork meat products and cheese. The goat meat is still not able to be used and the wild meat from deer is just sold as is. Why not have summer sausage? I have enjoyed playing this game with my wife in co-op for many hours, but the idea that the developers are going to release a game that is so still early in it's development cycle is absurd. They just switched to UE5, that engine alone has many of the systems they want to use built into in when you use it. If it took them a year to switch over to UE5, why not use the tools the engine gives you to help improve your game so that you can make good on your promises. Now, I am an indie developer myself, and I know how hard it is to make a good cohesive game so I feel for the developers. However, leaving early access is just irresponsible when you don't have the basics down let alone a cohesive experience. I'm sorry, I do like the game but I cannot recommend anyone pay full price for it in its current state.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a wonderful game! The Devs are great! Im currently helping test the unstable branch and the game continues to be a fun one. I prefer to solo play it and I have a ton of hours. I have seen so many bugs get fixed between versions, and the u5 update is GREAT!

The guide can be a little simple and some players get confused how to make items with the milk/meat - I recommend checking a guide for help.

Dont do what I did when I first played and cut down the entire forest basically to make money. Theres plenty of good ways to get started - either by chickens, piggies (I prefer), or fruits... def dont need to cut down trees for lumber to sell for crap.

My only CONs with release of unstable...
- They limited the amount of trees you can cut down, you cant go anywhere to cut down trees. Its now only around the farm which I think is dumb. I dont want to buy lumber later in the game to expand my... "Processing" ... plant.
- Rain/storm water does not water plants when exposed to the outdoors. Falling rain should water the plants.

Other than that - Wonderful!\t\t\t\t\t
