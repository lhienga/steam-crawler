\r\n\t\t\t\t\t2.5 out of 5 stars
Look, I REALLY wanna love this game! I really do! It has so much potential to be better than farm simulator, but I can't. There's too many things that need fixed, or changed. I've only got 15 hours on record, but, I think that's enough time to make a semi accurate review. For starters, the driving needs a complete overhaul, especially for keyboard users. The fact the vehicles are almost "brake locked" until you build up a high rpm, makes its almost impossible to accurately hook up to a trailer, or similar equipment. I just spent almost 10 minutes trying to hook onto a trailer I just bought! I wasn't able to do it, which is when i decided to save, quit, and come here to review. Secondly, the trailer handling UI is terrible! If you have to use brakes on your vehicle while moving, even in my full sized pickup and the small trailer, the trailer shoots to the side and almost always spins me out. Not to mention, when driving through the woods, your vehicle is like a heat seeking missile to any rock, tree, or deer in the area, always stopping you dead in your tracks. Your vehicle flips way too easy. If you get it pinned between 2 things or you land on a guardrail? Have fun getting it off!!! You can flip your vehicle, but, it just sends your vehicle straight up then back down in the same spot! That needs to be fixed. When it rains, it should fill any water buckets, troughs, and gardens/fields but it doesn't. Make the targeting with guns more fluid. It's too jumpy and hard to aim accurately. Even when changing view, your character doesn't look down the sights, they look to the side of the gun. You have no way of knowing where you're shooting. One of the most important things in my opinion, is resource re-spawn. The trees should grow back after a certain period of time. I'm on day 18 in game and the trees I cut down on day one, still haven't come back. I believe they should if you haven't built anything there. That's all i can think of for now. If any, or all, of those things happen, my rating will go up accordingly.\t\t\t\t\t

There is still so much potential to this game that so much more can be done with it.

My cons;
Driving is difficult especially with a trailer attached.

TREES... i HATE the fact that we cant go anywhere to chop them down anymore.
Cant kill animals by hitting them.

IDEAS THAT COULD BE ADDED TO THE GAME;
i would suggest allowing us to get licenses so we can chop trees anywhere, allow trees to re spawn/regrow back, allow us to plant normal trees & start and orchard! All those would surely make a lot of people happy.

As for adding licenses, this can be applied for killing deer/bears/wolves or even the idea of fishing in the game.

Gravel to make paths or drive ways.

WAY MORE FURNITURE. we have the ability to build a home or shed but not a lot of furniture to put in our buildings making them less homey & sad.

Character customization... Maybe more hair options and longer hair for females? More clothes as well.

Maybe you have to repair your car slowly over time?

I dont mind the idea of eating & drinking, going to the bathroom & sleeping to maintain your character. It would give the houses more of a reason to be used and make the game more real.

i have so many more ideas but idk if these comments even get read by the creators. I like this game so much and im girly af but the idea of marriage & having a partner who helps you farm would be interesting.
