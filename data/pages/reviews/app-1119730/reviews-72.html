\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, I have sunk more time into this dude. And...this is what I think.
It is a good game. It is also an addicting one. Waking up every day to tend your fields and livestock has a good "wholesome" feel to it. I know. I put air quotes because...sarcasm. You absolutely slaughter animals in this game. You have to, because they will die otherwise. I suppose you could play this without delving into animal husbandry but you'd be cutting yourself short. It's more than half of the entire concept of farming.

Now to the things I dislike/need to change/be added: (<---I would gladly pay for these things)

- Fencing. Oh my gawd. FIX. IT. Getting stuck inside, on top of and a few feet from fences is gut wrenching when you haven't saved since you last slept. The work around is to save frequently. That's...just not good enough guys. Fix it. Also...the placing of fences should work like...how places walls/fencing work in games like The Sims 4. Simplified toggles, drags and clicks.

-Destroying things. There should be a prompt: Are you sure you want to destroy this? This would work better if you could click and drag to select multiple objects, because that would be intentional but when it comes to switching tools and accidentally deleting a whole building...man. Or a more simplified idea: have the option to redo/backspace/undo your last action when building.

-Pigs....should not ever be able to climb on things like they can in this game. How in the world would a 650lb swine be able to walk up the side of a table and then over a fence? They wouldn't.

-Castration should be an option: because pigs. I'd pay for this service in the general store or even the tools to do it myself. Only drawback? It's not reversible.

-Horses should be able to breed and they need their own buildings. The barn is suitable sure, but we need one or two building options specifically for them. If you choose to forgo a horse barn, you could build a horse shed in the pasture for them. Would be nice to have tack options to buy, even premade designs. Then just a little page for selecting your available tack in their profile page. Or having an object tied to your horses that names them and tacks them up all in one go.

-Chickens: The mechanics of chickens needs a revamp. They are able to sometimes walk through walls. Which, is not good man, not good. The egg basket should have the option to deposit the eggs into the ovoscope to fill slots and then to deposit them all back into the basket with a button/command. Remove their ability to stand on top of the chicken feeder. They poop in one spot and you have to physically move each of them out of the way to get to the poo. There should be more breeds/colors. We get three options. I know the work around is to place the feeders outside of the fence/area but how realistic is that?

-Poop: There's a lot of sh** in this game. A service that would clean this up would be easy but awesome to have. Or, the composter should work like the silo/windmill and auto suck up the poop in a certain area, which you could toggle on just like the silos/windwill. Then pick it up and rotate it around your animals or...put on in each area. Boom.

-Driving mechanics. This is wonky. Ya'll know it. Work on that.

-Weapons. We don't have enough options.
-Quests: We don't have enough quests.
-Maps. This map is okay. But having two more options from the start would be fantastic.

-Lumber - The option to sell logs like planks at a discounted rate. Obviously unprocessed lumber would be worth less. The wood cutters need something to auto feed them, either another buyable option or a setting on the more expensive one. Also, automatically shutting off after a few moments when not loaded would be nice. I know that last one might be asking a little much but...I'd like that a lot.

-Build/Map/Terrain: We don't have the able to change the terrain on our property. Again, like in the Sims 4 you could add the option to paint the terrain, even with just three or four options: gravel, grass, dirt, concrete. Having pathways around your property would be super amazing. Grass should not be able to grow through concrete/gravel/dirt options.

-Buyable Objects: There aren't enough decorative options, nor are there enough shelving, meat drying options, etc. I would like to be able to purchase a home furnished or unfurnished with the option to buy and place things in the rooms. Big ask, but seriously. Would be AMAZING.

I would pay for any or all of things in a purchasable DLC as I know several of my friends and family would as well. Heck, I'd gift it to some of them myself. This game has a ton of potential just laying there waiting for the devs to make bank off of.

Thank you for your time.
And thank you...for this game. Even in the state it's presently in. ;)\t\t\t\t\t
