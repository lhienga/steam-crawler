\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is too clunky for me. Got to the point where i could finally build my granary so i could plant and harvest wheat to feed my animals.
After the massive cost of all the equipment ( around 6k ) and growing the wheat and then harvesting it with a lot of clunkyness ( harvester getting stuck and tractor reverses at 0,01mph with the gran trailer attached), and the finally getting my full trailer to the granary which awaited to be filled, i couldn't find a away of unloading my trailer and then noticed on the granary i could fill it with a cost of only $200.

To add insult to injury after i returned with the wheat that i could not unload ( but i could sell at the shop ) i found all my animals bar 1 cow dead , all starved to death even though they had full water and full troughs before i set off to collect my harvest.

The game could be a lot more fun if it wasn't so clunky, I already mention about the harvesting and harvester getting stuck on the smallest bump but the driving aspect is a lot to be desired too.
Reversing of trailers is a nightmare as they soon jack knife without giving you time to recover, The steering is way too sensitive and you can soon find yourself on your side or doing a full 360 with all the bumps in the road.

I know i have only payed for 40 hours but that 40 hours was enough for me to quit the game and wait again for more updates with the hop a lot of the clunkiness is taken away, I play mostly Farming Sim and wanted a break from it which is why i got this game but as it stands at the moment finding another game is my only option.

I do hope that the devs do take on board the players concerns and fixes them because this game could be a ton of fun to play without the clunkiness.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played this game with my husband. It is fun, up to a point. When we play these types of games, our play styles are very different. I like the building aspect of it, while he likes dealing with the animals (hunting, etc). Because of this, when I play alone, I tend to use a "creative" mode. That mode doesn't exist in this game. It's a simulator only. The animals are always on. There is no peaceful mode. Most survival games with animals, or mobs include one, these days. It just extends the audience for the builder types. If you like a 'peaceful' mode, don't buy this game. It's not there. If that's not your style. I would say, definitely buy this game, but not at $25. I grew up on a farm. Animals could be a problem. But there were never bears at the hardware store. I also like games where modders can be active. These days the modders add all the things, we wished the original developers would have included. That also doesn't seem to exist for this game.

I think this is a very "closed" game. Niche ONLY. The developers had an idea of how game play would proceed, all other ideas, dead in the water. So, no modding community. No peaceful mods. No settings to tweak. So, what do you get? You get a game without a hot bar. So, when the bear comes to kill you at the hardware store you have to hit the tab key and find a weapon on a wheel. Wait, what?\t\t\t\t\t
