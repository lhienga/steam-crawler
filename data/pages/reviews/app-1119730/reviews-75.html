\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo much to do, and I haven't even scratched the surface yet! I really love this game and wish I didn't have to go to work or sleep so I could just keep playing. For actual game-play, it is fun and enjoyable. Chopping down a tree (which there is a lot of) is not annoying, because it only takes 1 swing to chop it down, and another to make logs. This makes a tedious task less repetitive like what other games have done. Again, although I have played for over 20 hours at this point, I only have chickens and honey production and I just get excited knowing there is so much more to expand on!
All that being said, it is pretty clear it is still in early access, but I am so happy I saw the details for the upcoming update they are planning on to enhance and add even more content! If the developers are reading this, I would like to say my 2 biggest updates ideas are 1: The lighting at night. It is PITCH BLACK without your head lamp on. Maybe have the other cars have headlights/brake lights? But again, an update will be dropping within a month or two, so maybe this has already been addressed. And 2: more options for building our own freestyle buildings. I LOVE the barn and how there is the front, end, and you can pick how long you want it by adding middle parts. I Am trying to build a large garage that I can park all my vehicles and trailers in, but I cannot make something where the door is wide enough for the trailers to fit! More options and variety would be amazing. (P.S. I also really like the greenhouse parts... nice open windows to let in natural light!)\t\t\t\t\t
