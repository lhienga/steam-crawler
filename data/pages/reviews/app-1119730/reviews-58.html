\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've really tried to like this game. I WANT to like this game. But I don't like this game. It's like some executive who never plays video games heard that Stardew Valley was a hit but that it was in low-tech pixel graphics and hired some programmers with the directive to make Stardew Valley, but 3D and realistic. They even directly ripped off the basic premise of Stardew, with you inheriting your grandfather's old, rundown ranch because you decided to get away from the grind. But they never bothered to check what it was that actually made Stardew Valley endearing, and just decided to nix any story or NPCs. The only people you run into are lifeless mannequins who all work at places like the real estate office or the store, and have names like Real Estate Agent or Store Employee. No dialog options, no story. You guys all your merchandise from a computer terminal. The streets of town are devoid of life except for dreary cars that drive slowly down the road with darkened windows, presumably to hide the frozen zombies inside. Honestly, driving through town feels like the setting for a horror movie. I've rarely felt more lonely.

Soulless lack of story/setting aside, the game mechanics are rather clunky. You're stuck in 3rd person view, running is kinda drifty and awkward, for some reason I carry my chickens by the throat like a demented 4 year old, the hunting is nothing more than point and click, the animal animations are...well, let's just say the animals in the old claymation Rudolf the Rednose Raindeer move more realistically. Oh, and the driving is so floaty and drifty that you might well be on the moon. Just moving around and doing stuff is a chore, even when you're not actually doing chores. And there's no real progression to the gear or technology, just a price tag, so as long as you're willing to grind you can get anything you want. That can be nice, I suppose, but it removes any incentive to progress or feeling of accomplishment.

I dunno; it's a pretty game, but everything just feels so sterile and soulless. I like the premise, and I have played all sorts of games in this genre, so it isn't like this just isn't my kind of game. Maybe if they update it enough to make the controls feel good and bring in some characters with backstories we can get to know or something I'll give it another go, but as of right now it just feels like lifeless and clunky.\t\t\t\t\t
