PAINT! being able to paint would be a great idea
Back to fences on my propert alone I have wooden fencing, metal fencing, barb wire, and electric fencing just some ideas.
PLOW!?!?!?!?!? you can get everything but a plow...... strange.

Some one had mentioned on you-tube that its like owning farm animals your check on them a lot. As someone who does own a ranch I check my animals at least twice a day and not every 5 minutes to ensure they eat and drink. I also would like to see some more added features in the future as mentioned this game to me was a 50/50. Still worth a shot if it gets updated.
