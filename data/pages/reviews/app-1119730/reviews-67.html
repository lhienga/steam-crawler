- A better system for placing items down so that items can align better (if you have OCD or just like things aligned and straight... be prepared to spend hours on placing objects down)
- A count for the materials we need- for example if we place foundation for a fence it would be cool if we could see what we need and how many of that item in the top corner or something

- A little description when buying things that says what the item can be used for
- The ability to pet the animals lol
- The ability to lead multiple animals at a time
- The ability to carry more than one log at a time
- Recipe book of some kind
- Increase in speed of harvester
- The ability to move objects once placed
- The ability to move outline of object once placed
- A option to "quick place" logs and planks when loading them in vehicle
- More people for multi player

It may seem like a lot but the reality is the game is already great. Its not like the game is unplayable- these are just things we feel would enhance the game quality! We cant wait to see what else is added!!
