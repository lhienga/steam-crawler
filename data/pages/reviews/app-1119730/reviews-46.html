\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello,

I've had my eye on this game for some time and have managed to just miss the sale prices... until this week. After playing for a couple days I can see why there are such mixed reviews of this game. It has a truly casual feel but lacks a lot of refinement.

PROS:
- Casual play style
- Diverse Livestock & Farming
- Building: preset structures or customize
- Learning curve isn't too steep

CONS:
- Vehicle physics is comical at best
- Market: prices aren't well thought out.
- Economy: prices are fixed and don't change
- Quests: there are some but barely a days play worth.

GAME NEEDS:
- Vehicle: Physics & Pricing reworked
- Objectives: Time based (daily, weekly, monthly...) orders to fill for rewards.
- Quality: poor to excellent crops & livestock with scaling values.
- Depth: There is nothing driving this game other than one's desire to play.
- Property: Defined lots & expansion options.
*I could go on and on with ideas on ways to make the game better but... in the end it's their vision.

I miss the days of a game being released to the public when it's finished and when Beta's were play tested for free. This game is clearly in beta and not worthy of retail sales. With that being said, I could justify maybe paying up to $5.00 (USD) or less. I can not recommend buying this beyond a cheap curiosity.
