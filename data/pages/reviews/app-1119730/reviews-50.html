\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game crashes and crashes OFTEN. With no auto-save feature, this is a MAJOR issue with the game. I've tried various fixes and none have seemed to work.

Additionally, the game lacks quite a few major features that you would think would just be an easy add to the game. For example, you rely heavily on wood (obviously), yet it limits you to only be allowed to cut the trees down on your property. To make matters worse, trees can't be planted and they don't auto-regenerate. This means once you finish off the trees on your property, your only option is to pay a crazy rate to get lumber from the store.

Other key features that it's lacking- There's no way to see your property boundaries. It's all a guessing game. The best way I've found around this is just to see which trees you can cut down and which trees aren't allowed. There's also no way to expand your ranch, no new plots of land to purchase. I'm hopeful though that this is something that will be added in later releases.

Hunting options are limited. You have only 3 animals to hunt- Deer, Wolf, and Bear. For a part of the game they hugely advertise, it's crazy how lacking the experience feels. You also have only two options for weapons- a revolver and a rifle. There's no difference in ammunition types, no difference in types of weapons... Just a revolver and a rifle.

The building isn't terrible. The worst part is that you can't move or edit items once they've been placed, so if, for example, you build a trough in a place or position that you don't want or like, your only option is to destroy the trough, lose half of your materials that you put into it, and move rebuild it where you would like. For a game that feels limited already on the available materials, this is a huge issue.

Honestly, I feel like the game is passable for the price and probably would be a recommended from me were it not for the crashing and lack of auto-save. At this point I've put in 20 hours total, but after the total amount of work I've lost, I'm probably more around the 12 hour mark, which is VERY frustrating.

Developers: Add auto-save and I'll gladly change to recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
