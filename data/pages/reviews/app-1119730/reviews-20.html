\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has some interesting concepts but the execution is lacking. I feel like this is a first game for someone that is trying to learn and get more into game development while still holding a full time job.

The graphics are basic, the interface and mechanisms are a bit clunky. The driving physics are awful. Animal behavior is comical and unrealistic. The town is devoid of life. And everything you do consists of hitting the E key. Want to chop down a tree. Hit the E key. Want to saw a log into boards. Hit the E key. Want to build a house? Carry your boards to the house and hit the E key. It was fun for a few hours and then became rather mundane after that and I doubt it will get much better in the next year.\t\t\t\t\t
