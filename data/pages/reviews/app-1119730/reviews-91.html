\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's really great and if it wasn't for this glaring issue I'd recommend it, but there's a game breaking bug where vehicles are launched into the upper atmosphere, along with yourself, seemingly at random with no way at all to retrieve them. Vehicles in this game take a good bit of effort to unlock and are absolutely essential to the progression and navigation of the game as a whole. This has been an issue for over a year and many (even temporary) solutions by the community have been suggested (such as a simple vehicle reset with prerequisites to avoid exploits).

The developers have been responsive about awareness of the issue, with in depth methodology about their testing, but the lack of any attempt to implement a way to actually play the video game has yet to happen. They're working on an engine update (from Unreal 4 to 5) and some mechanic rewrites to fix it, but until it's fixed I can't recommend an effectively unplayable game.
