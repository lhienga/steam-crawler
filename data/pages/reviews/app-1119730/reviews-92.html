\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great! A lot of potential for where this game can go. You can tell it’s still an early access game based on the way the terrain can go from super detail grass to chunky-graphic rock terrain.

Some things I would suggest:
-fruit trees and maybe flowers
-more wildlife and biome diversity to create a more immersive ranching experience.
-a town: right now the area is spread out with very little interaction with any NPCs outside of just shopping which is done on ATM style machines.
-ability for dogs and or cats on your farm.
-making the UTV less reactive and touchy when going over hills so you don’t fly all over
-ability to pet your animals or interact with them more then just milking and watching them get pregnant.
-more decorations for your home to make it more customizable instead of cookie cutter repetition.
-NPC traveling and maybe visiting your ranch with some conversation dialogue

I appreciate the game and have a lot of fun as is but would definitely love to see some more features in game to make the experience more immersive and encapturing. Keep up the great work!\t\t\t\t\t
