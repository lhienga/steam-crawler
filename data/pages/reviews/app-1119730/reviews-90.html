Controls okay except vehicle physics and control are insanely horrible, graphics okay-ish for low end game, sound is okay at best, though often there is none at all and it makes me wonder if it is broken. Building mode is sorely deficient in content and use, especially for a game based around it. Chickens and cows have some wild quirks to deal with. MP is limited 4 person P2P using Steam, but honestly maybe the most fun part of the game is laughing at the bugs and janky physics with friends. Game crash after a couple hours? Sucks to be you! And it WILL happen... Considering what we get from some other indie sims, Ranch Sim is lacking. I was very much looking forward to it and REALLY wanted to like this game and play with friends. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing this game for a few hours (like 81) I have to say for a game that seems like not much to do in it, The game is very well made and keeps you busy. Time just fly's by while your cutting down trees, building animal pens and house, out hunting or catching rabbits smoking bees from hives or at the auction buying horses.
A few things I would really like to see would be more Furniture to making a custom house, flowers for the garden beds and garden fencing, food you can actually eat. Kinda feels weird having to feed my animals but not myself. Possibly fishing in the lake???? Being able to paint the garage (if you keep it) and the well. More crops to grow, and cooking recipe's to use them in so instead of planting and harvesting lets say tomatoes you can now make sauce and use your wheat to make spaghetti or pizza. Also allow the pig meat to be made into small sausages or pepperoni that would be cool ahhahahahah.

One big issue I have been finding while playing multiplayer is sometimes when i go to get out of my truck i will get superman'ed across the map and end up on the edge while the truck is where i flew from and have to run all the way back to it, As well as the truck slightly moving on it's own making it hard to put things in, and if you leave the trailer attached I hear them banging together and sometimes see it.

All in all, This is an amazing homestead game for anyone looking for a getaway to your own ranch. I would highly recommend this game to everyone, and look forward to seeing where this game goes. Hopefully with some of my suggestions.\t\t\t\t\t
