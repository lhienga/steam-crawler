\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay, where do I begin?

I absolutely love this game. True open world, a bit of a grind, but relaxing and the graphics are gorgeous!
I have played about 6 hours so far, and have been progressing along slowly, but mostly due to playing solo. I have since invited a friend to play (I bought them a copy of the game) and it's a lot more fun playing with a friend.

Things I enjoy:

-The building system. It's actually fun to bring wood to different places to actually build the structure, and see it go up.
-UTV. The all terrain vehicle you get when you start is SUPER handy! It's actually decent on fuel as well and can carry quite a bit of cargo
-Music. The music is very nice. I don't mind it at all!
-Overall gameplay. Honestly I like everything about the game, my only complaints are just small things to make the game a bit more polished
-Growing system is pretty simple to understand

Things I would love to see improved upon:

-At night when you go to town, the other cars have no head or tail lights. I would love to see this added to the NPC vehicles so it's easier to see them and not crash head on at night
-PC vehicle lights. I find the light are VERY bright on the PC vehicles, which can also be an issue. I'd love to see the intensity of the lights turned down a bit to chill on how bright it makes things.


Overall rating in my personal opinion is 8/10 as I eagerly await more content for the game, I swear I am not addicted\t\t\t\t\t
