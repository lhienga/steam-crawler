\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this at about 60% off, and this game is uhhhh... interesting?

The things I like. I like the gameplay, what you can do for your little farm, and the various ways to get money and just have fun. You can raise livesstock, grow crops, chop trees, hunt wild animals, keeping bees, make animal-based products, even drilling for oil! This is definitely the number one reason to get this game, and I've noticed that it hasn't changed much for a year from earlier Steam guides.
It all feels extremely chill, with lots of save points across the maps, and you can easily change the daylight cycle to make days longer or shorter. Some of the controls and "feel" might be off, but I was used to them after about 5 hours into the game. A lot more explanation to the players would be nice, especially the prices of produce and what to expect. Hemorrhaging money for lack of knowledge is not fun.

The things I don't like: plenty. The Unreal Engine the game relies on does three things, one is overheat my laptop extremely easily, and two is that it's very poorly optimized. I have a Geforce GTX 1650 Ti, and I have to turn it down to the lowest settings just to play the game. I know it's below the recommended specs, but not everybody has time and money for a high end graphics card, especially when my CPU and RAM goes above and beyond that.
The third is that the actual engine itself doesn't even run well, because I just spent about 10 minutes dealing with my buggy UTV that flies, glitches out, and does all sorts of things a motor engine should not do. Even the "roll over" feature just doesn't fix it. It's an example of just a lot of bugs with this game that can get really annoying at times, and I hope the devs fix them in time.

Yet, I recommend it because it's just fun. I sank 15 hours into the game, and want to play yet more. I can't wait to revisit my money-making bees and oil drills, because the core gameplay is that good! So, get this game if your hardware supports it, and I think it'll be fun with friends because that's what I nominated it for in the Steam Awards.\t\t\t\t\t
