The king penguin is stellar! We absolutely needed an iconic penguin species for our zoos, and the king penguin is perfect. They are unlike anything else we have in game, fun to build for, a joy to watch and so adorable! This pack is worth buying just for them.

The grey seal is the next great inclusion. Our first seal, they are brilliant to watch and build for. Their models and animations are beautiful and lifelike.

The giant river otters are my personal favourite in this pack! For our first group of swimming animals, the giant otter’s size makes them a great choice - as well as being another representative of South America, a region which needs as many species added to Planet Zoo as possible. They’re very cute and playful, a tropical mammal species for some diversity in this pack.

The Cuvier’s dwarf caiman is a brilliant model and a great small crocodilian, also helping to flesh out the South American sections of our zoos. Fun to build for as we can create smaller habitats to fill our zoos!

The terrapin was unexpected and is an animated little guy that is much more lively than most exhibit animals!

The build pieces are absolutely brilliant! Versatile, mostly flexicolour and not heavily themed, they are among the most useful DLC items added to Planet Zoo. You’ll be using them everywhere! Even the more specific items like the animal statues are graceful and will fit into any existing zoo style. The best build set of all DLC packs to date.\t\t\t\t\t
