I have to agree with others that these packs are not worth the price for what you get. 90% scenery objects 10% new animals. By now there are enough scenery pieces to make pretty much anything you could dream of. In any style, any theme, any colour.

I really think they should be separated into animal packs and scenery packs, available to buy individually. Because we don't need any more scenery items, we want more animals. That said, in this pack the penguins are really well done, as are the other animals. and while there are some new rocks, waterfall pieces and other bits, it just doesn't feel worth the price.
