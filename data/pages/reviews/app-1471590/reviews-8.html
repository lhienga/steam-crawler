BUT. I recommend this pack over all the others simply because the five new animals in this are SO GOOD. If I had to pick a favorite, I couldn't choose between the giant otters and the gray seals. The baby gray seals are so unbelievably adorable. All habitat animals in this pack also have unique diving and underwater feeding animations... there's a new deep-water fish feeder that basically splurges fish into the pool for the animals to catch and eat.
