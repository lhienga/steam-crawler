Compared to Madden 20 which again is the last Madden I have played, Franchise does have new and interesting features that have been added to it.(Also I don't know if these features I am going to talk about have been in Madden already or not but they are new to me that's why I am calling them new) Like editing your OC and DC and giving them traits and having to strategically assign points to them is interesting. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tAh, Madden 23... where do I even start? I remember the days when Madden games were simple, enjoyable and actually had soul. But this latest installment? It's like they took all the fun, threw it in a blender and hit "puree."

First off, the graphics are supposed to be next-level, but they just look like someone smeared Vaseline all over my screen. And don't even get me started on the AI - it's like the computer players are controlled by chimpanzees. I swear, I've seen better decision-making from my 3-year-old nephew playing with his toy cars.

But the real kicker? They got rid of all the classic features that made Madden games so great. No more create-a-player, no more Superstar mode, no more... well, anything that made it worth playing for more than 5 minutes. It's like they took a time machine back to the 1990s and brought back all the worst parts of video game design.

Honestly, I'd rather play Madden NFL '06 on my dusty old Xbox 360. At least that game had character. This one? It's like a soulless robot just going through the motions. Don't waste your time or money on this abomination. Stick to the classics, folks.\t\t\t\t\t
