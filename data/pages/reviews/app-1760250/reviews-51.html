I received this game for only $15 dollars. Lots of fun (sometimes) but compared to the next gen version, which also has the same bugs, this is a terrible version. First of all. My pc is very low spec (R33200g and veg8 integrated 2gb) and runs the game on ultra settings. This normally wouldn't be a problem, but consider that EA should've given PCs the next gen version as 80 percent of all computers can handle it. Over the course of playing this game (I upgraded from madden 19) I have noticed few improvements except for 3 new animations and you can throw out of a sack. Oh. And now you throw interceptions if your throw isnt complete and receivers never catch if someone is covering them. And the game makes you fumble and makes the defense better if you are winning the game in all madden mode. This makes running the clock near impossible. Some other glitches are: In Face of the franchise, the players house literally spawns on the practice field along with the entire city of los angelos. Not that hard to fix EA. Also sometimes coaches arms become bird wings. I was somewhat satisfied with the game until I saw the next gen version. I'm not buying another madden game until the give Pc players next gen. This game is sub-par for 2023. Graphics are dated, presentation is the same, halftime the same since madden 19, same emotionless comentary lines from madden 19. Another gameplay glitch that happens to me is I will have a player covered defensivley, and he will literally float through my defender (teleports essentially) to catch the ball. Also, now you can't throw on the run because barley moving the stick makes you inaccurate. It's a step over madden 19 (except for the graphics) but is lazy on EA's part and an embarrassment to John Madden.

Yes, I still play this game, but that's because going back to madden 19 would not be very fun. I have madden 22 but it crashes every time I play it.\t\t\t\t\t
