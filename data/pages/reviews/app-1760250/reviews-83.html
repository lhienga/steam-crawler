You wanted to invoke discussion, shattering the preconceived notion of clearly defined roles. You use this as a vessel to challenge gender roles, prejudices based on the way someone looks, misconceptions about other cultures and religions,...

You have bravely gone where few others dared to venture. I tip my hat to you!

Food for thought and a lot of money I will now spend in therapy trying to find my true, inner self.\t\t\t\t\t
