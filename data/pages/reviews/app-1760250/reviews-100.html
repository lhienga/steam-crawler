I haven't played a football game since NCAA '08 and I owned madden 2013 but never played it. For being out of the EA football loop for basically 10 years coming back to the franchise has been nicer than expected. I know people are unhappy with the MUT, player packs, and the monetization of video games. Unfortunately that's just the reality of gaming right now. End of the day it's a fun football game for franchise mode. I'm by no means an expert opinion but if you get this game on sale I can't say it's not going to be entertaining.

I'd give it a 6.5/10

Edit: I lowered my "overall score". After a lot more time, the game was less impressive than I originally felt. Still decent, but not worth the price if it's not on sale. Unfortunately the last time a madden title went on sale it was Madden 24, while Madden 23 remained retail price.\t\t\t\t\t
