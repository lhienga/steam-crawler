\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst Madden game for me in about 5ish years, got this on sale, didn't expect much to have changed, not much has. That being the case, I can't recommend this game at any price.

Obviously the focus is still on ultimate team as it is a cash cow for this franchise, but I'd like to see them improve on other facets such as be a pro mode, graphics, player movement and just realism in general.

Be a pro is really a husk of what it should be. The NFL is a multi billion dollar franchise. At the very least, try and incorporate some hall of fame careers into this mode, let us play out a season as a prime Barry Sanders with voice acting and the like, or prime Dan Marino. Obviously the rights are there to have these guys in the game, why not capitalize on it other than having them in ultimate team. That being said, it's probably a pipe dream for something like that, but please, at least add some decent acting and fun moments to the awfully drab face of the franchise mode.

Graphics, player movement and realism are generally tied together, so I'll just compare the graphics to NBA 2K. Most of the players in 2K have facial scans, why not have full body scans in Madden. Lineman look morbidly obese, linebackers look like lineman should and it just doesn't look right across the board from head to toe. Player movement is as robotic as it was 5 years ago. All of this attributes to the lack of realism in this game.

Overall, I'm just disappointed. 2/5\t\t\t\t\t
