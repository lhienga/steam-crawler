\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJohn Madden helped me fall in love with football and seeing his face every time I open this ♥♥♥♥ heap of a game my heart sinks knowing he'd be upset with how the series turned out.

Other than every other point I've seen made the game very routinely freezes on PC during games and when exiting the game not due to any fault with the PC but due to the fact the game is always-online due to some EA horsecrap. Games autosave while in progress after plays get recorded and often times this causes the entire game to freeze up. I tried Madden 22 on EA Play and encountered the same issue and by desyncing cloud storage I was able to prevent these frequent issues. Madden 23 no longer allows this. Sometimes I have to reboot my PC multiple times just to finish one game in franchise mode. Often times the game that is in-progress can't be salvaged, such as the time right now that just pushed me to write this review - I was late in the third quarter winning by two scores against a division rival with multiple challenges hanging in the balance. Not fun having to replay games and hope things roll your way again - they frequently don't. This issue persists on more than one computer so it is not this specific one at fault, it's the shoddy game itself.

Say what you will about the game being the same every year - I haven't bought a Madden in about a decade because of that - but nowadays they're not even stable enough to finish a single game. Awful product. I bet people who sailed the high seas to get Madden 23 don't have to deal with this issue.\t\t\t\t\t
