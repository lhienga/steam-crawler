\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnother year another release full of the same boring problems.

I have been a big madden player on and off for over 15 years. I do CFMs, online CFMs with 32 people, and even some MUT year to year. I have not played a madden since 2018 or 2019 after playing those versions way too much. I am glad it is on PC and it is pretty easy to run. Looks decent and I don't care about which generation we get ported. That is just backstory for me.

But the game itself.... I am so addicted to the CFM managing aspects... but every year they seem to make it harder and harder to play. There are now pop ups everywhere telling you to upgrade the coach, and upgrade a player, and scout this, and change your scout, and focus a region, and every single thing you try to do brings you to a menu. Each menu takes seconds to load (PC built 3 years ago). Then each menu has a submenu. You can not exit and enter menus quickly. It is clunky. This was an issue back when I used to play on PS4, and somehow it is worse.

The gameplay feels the same way. Picking plays isn't fluid. You use ask madden and its the same 6 plays all game. You use concepts and not all the plays are listed. You 'play the moments' and nothing feels like an epic moment. Sometimes you press A to skip forward, sometimes you hold it, its random! You have to use this a ton because there are 3 replay videos for every play. Does EA think we all love to see 3 angles of the field goal after every 3 point attempt? Like why? You can be sure that at least once per game, at the most important part of the game, you will be trying to skip and it will hurry up into a ball spike play or you pick the wrong play. The whole game is fragmented, every series, play, review, menu, during the game just buffers and makes it choppy. It is absolute nonsense. It should be pick play adjust snap repeat with no cooldown inbetween. No one wants to hear the announcers make the same joke over and over and see pictures of the team logos swirl around the screen. Pure nonsense.

The in game menus are somehow worse. Some simple and obvious examples of how to make it smoother. If someone pulls up a player card, they should be able to stay in the player card menu and quickly swap to their next player's card. Like players on the left and the cards on the right so you can scroll through them. If someone is in the depth chart they should be able to pull up a player card. No reason I should have to pull a card to see someones dev and non stat features from the roster menu when I am trying to figure out who to start in the depth chart.

If you are getting a scout for a region it shouldn't be enter scout menu enter region menu back out of region menu enter scout menu change to buy scout change to scouts set scout to region exit scout menu go to region menu verify scout region. THAT IS LIKE 20 STEPS FOR SOMETHING THAT SHOULD BE TWO STEPS. Scouting gets changed for no reason every few years. Each time it becomes worse. Unthinkable.

Wait till the combine comes. You are telling me to see the 40 times of the players, I need to bring up each player 1 by 1... which takes seconds to load, then RB the screen over to the stats, and I have to do this for all 200 prospects? Why? Why cant I just see the combine stats and sort by them? Why is having fun gatekept behind load times of pointless menus. Not only that but if you do this DURING THE DRAFT the ranks of the combine stats are only compared to the remaining players, so if the number 1 speed guy is gone, now the number 2 guy shows as the number 1. What is the point of that??? Its round 6 and everyplayer is top 10 in all catagories? LOL? Just too lazy to fix stuff?

The whole game is built like this. You can not simply compare FA to roster, of find people to trade for by using a filter with multiple options (the filter with multiple options in MUT mode takes minutes to set up it is so clunky) or check which players of yours have good development, every time you have to back out of menus and into menus with load times for every menus.

It really sucks that madden is the only player in town. They have such a good formula, and waste it with bad programming. Reusing code from decades ago and just layering more crap and popups on top over and over.

I only played because it was free. I only bought it because it was $15. It is addictive as ever, but it just isn't fun.

I will also briefly mention how they cannot even balance the gameplay. How hard is it to make the games vs a CPU challanging? It is the same old gimmicks over and over. If you are up 3 touchdowns, you cannot sim the game because any team will automatically score to catch up. If you are winning in the 4th quarter, better just kneel the ball because you will randomly fumble or throw picks if you even attempt to go for it. Spamming plays to the 60 overall 98 speed guy? No problem overall stats don't matter at all! Usering a DLine? Expect an instant throw which you cannot react too. Usering a LB in zone? Expect zero pressure no matter how many you blitz. Running a zone killing route on 3rd down? Your WR will just run into a cluster of 3 players and not run the route. Have perfect coverage on someone? The game will just randomly speed up some frames to you over. Throw a pick to a 85 speed lb? Your 98 speed wr cannot catch him. The core of CFM is team building via stats, and the stats only matter for simmed games. Everything usered is too strong or the CPU just says no you cant do that and messes up what you are doing. Nonsense.

Like these things arent fun, it isnt a fun way to play. It is basically if you can glitch better than the CPU... that isnt fun. You have to run glitch routes. The CPU randomly glitches your plays. This isnt simulation.

Fix your game. It has been like this for almost a decade. There are so many madden fans out there. 32 man CFM with a good group of people is one of the best video game experiences I have ever had, even with a broken game. PUHLEASE put some effort in.\t\t\t\t\t
