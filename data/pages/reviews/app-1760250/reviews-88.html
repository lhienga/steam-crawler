\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOK, I get all the hate for this game, especially for us PC users, and the criticism is fair. However, I have played a lot of Madden 22 and this game is better than Madden 22. I am not a super user, I am a couple notches above the casual gamer. I don't think you should ever purchase this game at full price. I picked it up at the $18.00 price and it is worth that price.

Here is what I find the most different, (this is after 10 hours of play) the DEFENSE and Star Players. The defense is so improved. Running drag routes for first downs is now contested. You no longer can throw any old receiver out there and they make catches and plays or any running back and get 100 yards. These guys don't get open and they can't punch thru holes. You need a good offense line or you will get wrecked! You need your star players. I now have to actually worry about the pass rush. I played against Micah Parsons and he was wrecking shop on me. I actually had to run plays to counter him!
You just can't run the ball and get 5 yards a carry. I use to run the ball on 3rd and 5 and always get a 1st down. Trap plays were like stealing. When your on defense you have to cover their stars or they will ruin your day.

The computer doesn't let you trick them into making bad trades, well at least not as bad. You can still trick them but it is harder. I had to give up more picks. I actually have to punt the ball! Well I am only 10 hours into the game but I find myself actually having to plan out my strategy. If I call a bad defensive play they will eat my lunch. If I don't pick up the blitz I am going to get sacked, In Madden 22, I went game after game never getting sacked. Well that is all for now, I hope this review sheds some light on Madden 23. It is worth 18 bucks.\t\t\t\t\t
