\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not going to go on about next-gen graphics, because that's a dead horse, and frankly I'm not a graphics ♥♥♥♥♥.

However...let's talk gameplay. I last played Madden '08, before purchasing this game. It is still a better game. Franchises were better, Superstar/FotF mode was better, and most importantly, the AI was better, somehow. I have literally ran in CIRCLES while on a route, 3 times, before my QB finally threw the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ ball. It's like the AI chooses the worst possible outcome about 30% of the time. It waits until you reach the sideline so you can't gain YAC yards, despite you being open for the last 10 ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ steps. It throws ONLY to the primary receiver named in the playbook, even if they are covered by 3 dudes...unless YOU are that receiver, then it will throw a no-gain swing pass every single play, no matter how open you are. Your blockers will STARE at the opponent running past them to tackle you.

My HB in FotF is actually too fast for the AI players, so I have to deliberately NOT sprint in order for the play to actually unfold properly, which is especially asinine since my HB is built for power, not speed. The player ratings do not matter, Josh Allen with a 95 rating is just as trash when controlled by the AI as Taigavailoa is with a 78 rating. The absolute worst part is that it makes any position that ISN'T HB a nightmare to progress in FotF. You have to rely on idiot AI to catch your passes and block for you as QB, and once the ball is out of your hands, it's all RNG. Play defense and watch even the best offenses in the NFL fail to score and capitalize on your good defensive plays. Play WR and get the ball thrown to you about 7% of the time it should be, the other 93% of the time you get to watch the AI perform like your teammates are crippled. Mentally. HB is the only somewhat palatable choice because, no matter how stupid the AI gets, I can just hand myself the ball and truck people until it all evens out again.

The physics are OK, but the players fall all over themselves like drunken toddlers from the smallest nudges, even by your own teammates, which, with the pathetic AI, happens far more than is acceptable. Changing direction is a stat now, because apparently only the top 1% of athletes in the world know how to turn around, leading to instances where your character SHOULD just turn, but instead runs a circle with a 5 yard radius. Whoever had that idea should not breed.

I get it's EA, and subpar is what they do...but how the actual ♥♥♥♥ did they go THIS far backwards? Everything else about the game aside, how the HELL is the AI significantly stupider that it was over 15 years ago?\t\t\t\t\t
