\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat can I say? I'm 10+ years late, finally decided to give it a go last week (before the sale started).

Played over 16 hours within 3 days of purchasing.

Extremely easy to learn, with all mechanics/controls easy to understand fully inside of 30 mins. Equally its VERY challenging to master (Anyone who's played Darkest Dungeon or Domina, think that style of progression/difficulty curve)

The game features a very wide breadth of variance based on broad RNG. Some runs absolutely hammer you and make your chances of success almost zero , often from the first 1 or 2 sectors (8 sectors per game) . Equally other runs randomly hand you copious currency and upgraded weapons right at the start, however if you take that for granted and get even slightly overconfident; prepare to meet your doom as fast as ever.

I feel like sometimes it should be frustrating, but yet I never really felt that. The perfect pacing combined with basically zero loading screens means that restarting and adjusting your tactics is very fast, quickly allowing you to reach your previous point of failure with a different plan in mind; sometimes a subtle change, sometimes a whole new ball game.

The only other thing I'll bother to say is this, if you're hesitating thinking its not your type/genre of game, try it anyway; you'll probably love it.

9.5/10 easily. Wish there was a sequel.\t\t\t\t\t
