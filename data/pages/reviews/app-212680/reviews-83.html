\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn extremely well designed *actual* strategy game!
It's a rougue-lite but with so many flavours. Every run,
1. Multiple ships to choose from
2. Multiple types of weapon
3. Varied systems (such as boarding another ship,
4. Manage a crew with different types of crew class and manning systems
5. Limited resource of energy to power all of the above, scraps as in-game currency, and ammo for weapons and systems
You have to manage all of the above simultaneously while both attacking and defending against enemy ship with many times and environmental hazard. All that you need to do for 8 sectors, each having 7-10 jumps, keeping your ship intact and powerful by spending your scraps judiciously for the final boss fight.

Each fight or an event requires decisions to be made constantly. You don't know what you'll get in each event, and you have to adapt accordingly.

On top of that, if you have got a good grasp of the system, you can enable the advance option to get even more systems and weapons!

In true sense, this is a very good strategy game. I highly recommend you to try it out and give it a couple of hours if you like any RTS or strategy game.

Also, it's notoriously difficult. I failed miserably in the first 3 runs. Then I switched to Easy and unlocked few ships and understand the game better. Along with some look ups in the wiki, I was able to clear the normal run after that. I recommend you to not skip the tutorial, and after 1/2 runs in normal, switch to Easy to understand the game mechanics better.

Hope you enjoy the game!\t\t\t\t\t
