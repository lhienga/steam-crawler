\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat roguelike concept, but awful RNG fest in reality. If you came from Into The Breach, you might be disappointed. ITB is extremely tactical and there is always a set of moves you can do to neutralize the Vek. Here, not really.

The fights are far too RNG-based (enemy weapons, targeting, miss chance). Fights will spiral out of control by unlucky misses or an unlucky breaching robot in your O2 room. The map generation is win-or-lose, the events are dialogue-based coin flips that can instantly make or break your run. Resources are too random. Shops are too random.

All of this together is way, way too much and ruins what could have been a very fun game, and I say this as someone who loves RNG-fest roguelikes and procedural generation.\t\t\t\t\t
