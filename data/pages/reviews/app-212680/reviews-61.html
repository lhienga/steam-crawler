\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I'm not someone that keeps up with Gaming or plays the big games every year, I can easily say this is one of my top 10 games of the last decade.

A seemingly simple game that offers endless replayability, even as you lose most of your runs. Winning will take everything you and your crew have and may be out of reach if you don't make the right decisions in the early systems.

At times the games feels tranquil and the beautiful soundtrack transports you away to an empty nebula. Other times your ship will be filled with fire as an enemy ship bears down or you battle with a boarding party; your crew fights for survival and the soundtrack adds tension and urgency.

I love the pause feature, which focuses the game on making the right choices at the right time instead of testing your reflexes and it allows the player to step away and come back in a way that feels more natural to the game.

For players like me who have put a lot of hours into the game the advanced content offers some new challenges and play mechanics. That said I'm sure I would have gotten to 700 hours and climbing with the base game as every map and playthrough is unique.\t\t\t\t\t
