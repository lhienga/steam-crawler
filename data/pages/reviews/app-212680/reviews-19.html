You progress through a star system, strategically jumping between various events. From fighting other ships, to answering distress calls and other space shenanigans. While advancing, you upgrade your ship and crew, all to prepare for the boss battle.

Insane amount of replayability due to many kinds of starting ships that reward different playstyles.\t\t\t\t\t
