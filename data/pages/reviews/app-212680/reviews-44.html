\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou command the USS Super Mario, but this time you know exactly which castle the princess is in. In order to beat Bowser, you need to upgrade your ship and survive many hostile encounters and quests on your way. The way is different each time and offers a ton of replayability.

The different ships really determine the play style early on. Once you have enough scrap to add systems, most ships converge a bit. Apart from the system and reactor upgrades, there are no guarantees. Everything is random. Unlocking some ships will happen naturally, others require a bit of digging.

Depending on your crew/ship/systems you find alternative solutions to quests and are given new dialogue options.

The first sectors feel genuinely fair for most ships. Playing with fully upgraded sensors reveals the beauty of those mechanics, as the same rules apply to both you and the enemy ships.

Strong recommendation for sci-fi fans and people who are looking for a game that requires no mechanical skill but a lot of thinking to go through.\t\t\t\t\t
