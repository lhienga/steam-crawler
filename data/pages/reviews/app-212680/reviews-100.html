\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFaster Than Light is an amazing game, and, in short, shines in its:
- Engaging combat
- Absorbing world
- Addictive gameplay loop
- Sheer Replayability

The biggest drawback in my opinion is the difficulty. This game is hard. Very hard. While the 200 MB games packs a surprising amount of nuance and strategy, there is also a fair bit of RNG which can feel very unfair at times.

In all honesty, about half my runs start successfully and then suddenly end with one unlucky encounter. The other half feel hopeless from the start, and my crew and ship suffer a slow and painful journey until they die.

But for some reason, I keep coming back. I would recommend this game if you:
