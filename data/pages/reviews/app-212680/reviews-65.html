\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the most high effort real time game I have ever played. The game mechanics are both effective and efficient- that takes a lot of mental power to make.

The game is great at easy mode with advanced content disabled. Replayable for a lot of time because the game has so much and such variety, none of it randomly put in, it actually simulates real randomness in random encounters, with depth in actual possibility… you can never guess what's coming… and… based on ship equipment, you may have any of 5-6 options to deal with them, if you're fully loaded in equipment maybe most of them. Further, each choice again has a variety of possible reactions from the enemies. Depth galore.

There is further depth in how you can approach the game from the start. 5 ships in base game with different tech (and different new options in random encounters based on them), with different weapon types installed that actually change combat approach.

Done with easy mode? Normal mode, hard mode is available. Played for hundreds of hours and had all the enjoyment you could get from it? You could enable advanced content and play more then, because it takes away a lot of stuff you could do to destroy enemy ships easily. This just keeps on giving. I am still only on easy mode, defeated the game with the 5 basic ship configurations, and hopped on to advanced for a taste. Advanced mode unlocks at least one more ship that I know of.

This game even has mods, at least one total conversion one which I have come by and noticed. The game is dirt cheap. It has not updated its pricing to be between double to five-fold its initial price in most regions like many others. Makes it one of the better deals on the store, considering every hour spent will not be slogged, endured, but it will be enjoyed.

Edit: I was wrong. I've just unlocked 3 more basic ships in base game. This is excluding variant layouts, each ship has 3 variant layouts which come with differing module location, upgrades pre-installed, crew and starting weapons.

Wow.\t\t\t\t\t
