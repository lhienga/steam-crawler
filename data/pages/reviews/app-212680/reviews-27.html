\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing FTL for many years now, though my steam hours do not reflect that. This is such a creative and fun game. I wish more games were made with the design philosophy of making something fun and "game-y" instead of cinematic and flashy. A good game needs good bones, not bells and whistles. FTL has rock solid bones, that make it an original and everlasting example of what a videogame should be. Just because the age of "Hollywood Big Budget Culturally Mainstream" games is upon us doesn't mean that we need to abandon what makes a game fun: fun innovative mechanics. Graphics don't make a good game, voice actors don't make a game good, self-indulgent qualities that make you feel cool don't make a game good. Only fun gameplay makes a game good and FTL has endless hours of fun gameplay.

Give it a try! It's a great roguelite with an arcade-y feel! If you are a fan of videogames you will enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
