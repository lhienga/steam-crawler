FTL is like that friend who insists on throwing a surprise party for you every five minutes. The surprises range from finding an abandoned space station to dealing with alien diplomats who seem to be in a constant state of existential crisis. I mean, if I had a credit for every time an alien asked me, "Do humans ponder the meaning of existence while making sandwiches?" I'd be rich enough to buy a fleet of spaceships.

In summary, FTL: Faster Than Light is a game that combines strategy, stress management, and the occasional existential crisis. So, buckle up, and prepare to question the meaning of space travel while frantically clicking on icons. It's a ride you won't forget.\t\t\t\t\t
