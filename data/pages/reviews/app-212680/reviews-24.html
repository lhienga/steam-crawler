My favorite Space Roguelike
  • Endless replayability (I've got 33hrs on record)
  • Great soundtrack and aesthetic style
  • Gameplay is simple but fun
Honestly, I'm not a huge fan of roguelike, but FTL is the exception. Now I get why people like them.
Every playthrough feels challenging but fair. I never felt cheated by RNG.
The campaign is short, sweet, and to-the-point.
The primary gameplay-loop is intuitive and engaging, while also allowing a surprising amount of freedom in how you deal with what the game throws at you.
I love trying various strategies and tactics and different playstyles. FTL just lets you find what works.
The simplicity of the game makes it easily accessible to player new to the genre, while the surprising depth of the possibilities make it enjoyable for even seasoned gamers.
I recommend Faster Than Light to both diehard fans of Roguelikes, and to those just getting in for the first time.
