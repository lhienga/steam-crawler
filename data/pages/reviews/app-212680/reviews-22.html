\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeautiful, engaging, and fun. This game had a lot of love put into it; it's well balanced, very replayable, and has wonderful music. 9.7/10 stars. My only gripe is how certain scenarios/choices are presented multiple times in the exact same way. For example, you will, on occasion, arrive at a beacon where scientists offer to reward you if you participate in a test. This is presented in the exact same way every time. Granted, there are multiple possible outcomes, but it just bugs me for some reason. It's an easy enough fix, though, and the devs could easily add a bunch more story choices in a later update. I know they're busy with Into the Breach. Another thing that could definitely make this gripe obsolete is the fact that I'm only playing with the base content. The expansion probably adds a ton of choices. I'm just reviewing what I've seen.

tl;dr: this game is great, and well worth the money. Despite being 12 years old, it's quite smooth and polished, and looks like it could have been developed recently. It has a lot of replayability, and tons to learn; I like to consider myself a somewhat decent strategist, and still haven't beaten the game on easy mode.

Well done, devs. Definitely gonna buy Into the Breach after I finish this game/when it goes on sale!\t\t\t\t\t
