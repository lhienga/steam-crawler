\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChoose your own adventure in a post apocalyptic bunker.
The goofy gameplay segment of 60 seconds free-looting your house once a run before going into the bunker is actually surprisingly satisfying.
During the game you have yes and no choices as well as which items to use in certain scenarios You can send people out to scavenge for resources.

The fun comes mostly from the RNG factor that changes outcomes on a dice roll or encountered random events, seeing everything the game has to offer. It is also a downside though, as there is no "perfect play" due to a large portion being influenced by RNG.

The game doesn't explain ailments well, so when it comes to Food & Water most new players will struggle while wasting resources unnecessarily.

Overall I enjoy the setting and the laid back gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
