\r\n\t\t\t\t\t60 Seconds! Reatomized is an inventive and challenging game that is sure to capture the hearts of both old and new fans alike. You assume the role of Ted, a family man who has just 60 seconds to prepare his fallout shelter and collect as much provisions and family members as possible before a nuclear blast. The game does an excellent job of setting up a heart-wrenching family drama in the span of a few seconds, where you must prioritize who you are willing to sacrifice for the greater good or who you are willing to risk to save.

Gameplay wise, the game is easy to learn but difficult to master. There are a number of tasks, such as gathering supplies and family members, keeping a track of supplies and food, and various mini-games, which all must be done within the limited time. Keeping track of all these tasks can be quite challenging but (fortunately) the game also provides helpful tips to help you out.

The overall art style is quite well done, mixing 1950s aesthetic and post-apocalyptic vibes together to create an interesting atmosphere. The sound design is also top-notch, creating the perfect sense of urgency and tension throughout the playthrough.

All in all, 60 Seconds! Reatomized is a great game that captures the tension and drama of the original game while improving its overall game mechanics. You wont regret adding it to your collection.\t\t\t\t\t
