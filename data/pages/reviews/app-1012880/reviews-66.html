\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis isn't a 0/10 thumbs down, it just doesn't quite rise to the level of reccommend. This sort of 'choosing your own path' game appeals to me in concept, but often let me down in that the number of choices seem so small. I only have a few hours in the game, and yet feel like I've seen some of the choices so many times that I got bored quickly. Simply, I think that in a game that purely exists to give players choices in an evolving story, that there are not enough variety in choices.

I will praise the game for the opening 60 second sequence though - it's goofy and cluncky, but I think the idea behind it as a differing prelude to the incoming story events is clever.\t\t\t\t\t
