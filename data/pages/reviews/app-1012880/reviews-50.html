\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd give this game 6 out of 10. I think the game is overrated. It's fine for a small game though.

I think the idea of the game is great but it's a bit poorly executed. Preparing for a nuclear attack, using rations accordingly in the shelter etc.

Somethings i disliked:

Preparing stage. You have 60 seconds. I think the time wouldn't be a problem if picking things up wasn't such a pain.

Transition to the next day is slow. I don't want to stare at the "Day X" title for that long. I want to be able to skip the waiting.

Expeditions feels bad. Some items are essential such as the briefcase to carry the other items, gas mask to avoid illness, i guess a gun or an axe to protect yourself... Once your briefcase get damaged you can't go out prepared. Also, the game doesn't really tell you what the items do in expeditions. For example, how would a map help in an expedition? Is it worth it? Most of the time our characters are gone for so long and come back with basically nothing. Why can't we look for a specific thing? What if we need medical items? Expeditions should have a purpose as well. Developers should consider this. Players could also send characters to look for specific items instead of random things... Ugh I don't want to talk about how great this game could've been but how it feels... missing. A lot of things.

I played for a while to be able to "beat" the game but honestly it wasn't much fun after a while. Should you try it? It's worth a try yeah. But it was somewhat mediocre experience for me.\t\t\t\t\t
