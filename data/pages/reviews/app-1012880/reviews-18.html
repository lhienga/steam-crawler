\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI first played 60 Seconds! back in 2017, a year after it came out on iOS. Despite the game having a lot of luck factors and also requiring knowledge of the various events within the game, I greatly enjoyed playing it back then and spent numerous hours seeing what all events could happen and ultimately seeing if I could beat it on Tsar Bomba difficulty (I was unable to back then).

Owning it now again on Steam, I can say the experience is still incredibly great personally. With the new changes to expeditions and various unique events that were not in the mobile version of the game back in 2017 from my memory, it only makes playing through it many times over just as fun as it once was. My only complaint after playing it again on Steam for the first time would have to be the challenges that are offered. Though the scavenging challenges are not that problematic and simply require good pacing and a tinge of luck on item placement, the survival challenges feel like an awkward split between providing a challenge and also complete luck rather than skill. As the items you are given inside the bunker can sometimes be randomized based on the survival challenge chosen, along with the events in the game itself, you sometimes want to hope for specific bunker objects to bother attempting it. To me personally, this makes it less of a challenge but rather a hope-and-pray scenario that you are given the perfect setup to succeed.

One gripe aside, this is still a game I found myself investing quite a few hours into upon obtaining it for Steam, and would recommend it for those that like skill-based but also random chance games.\t\t\t\t\t
