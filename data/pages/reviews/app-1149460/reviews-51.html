\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHi all. After having played many games for many years, I have decided to write my first review.
Icarus. Like all games, you will always find some thing, or many things that could be changed , introduced or removed. I try to play the game as is as the limitations in a game is part of the challenge, ...............mostly.
Icarus is for me, creates a truly immersive feel, the ambience created by the weather , the in game sounds keeps you looking over your shoulder and wondering what will kill or maim you next. I love the building aspect which is pretty seamless and is limited only by your imagination. Of course depending on the mission you are doing , the time allotted will determine how much time is spent building and the size of the build. So there is a nice balance between completing the mission and building.

On the , hmm, I wont say downside as its more a mild frustration than a negative thing. It seems weird that you can take items back into orbit that you brought down but can't take items you have built on planet??!! It is also a bit frustrating that you have to start from scratch every mission in terms of collecting resources and building items. Having said that, I sort of get why it would have been developed this way.

I have played solo and as a team. Solo can be very challenging and you have to plan and prepare if you want to live and finish some missions. Multiplayer is much easier as you always have backup and of course sharing the experience can be more fun, all depends on what you like.

In a nutshell, fantastic game, full of everything that makes a survival game great.
