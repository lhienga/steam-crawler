\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the better survival games that I have played. It's a good game to play if you like playing solo. There's a lot to discover, enough mystery (you have to actually find them), and challenging. I like this game better then Ark. It's less buggy and I like the added missions into the open world. At times infuriating when you don't plan for disasters.....You will burn you house down at least once as you watch it go up in smoke, standing in the rainy dark with no torch and you hear a wolf at your back....Fun to watch but heartbreaking. Good luck!
Only complaint is that during thunder storms the game becomes almost playable (especially if caught out in it and you are trying to fend of wildlife) it just might be my computer because everything else seem to operate well. Also once you are getting into higher tiered abilities you should have to have the previous tier ability to get otherwise it doesn't seem rewarding. For example if you want a steel weapon....you could simply wait to unlock it and not have the iron version of it. Sounds dumb but it seems more earned if you just don't simply have to wait for it.
Thanks DEV team, you are amazing on your weekly communication and continued improvements of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
