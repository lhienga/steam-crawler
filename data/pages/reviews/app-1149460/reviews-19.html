\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a few developer-decisions away from being a GREAT game. However, the bugs that exist and a few of the choices in game design have made this game too annoying to get behind and stick with.

I am playing the base game atm, 27 hours in, and with the amount of content they have released already, the base game should be DAMN NEAR perfect, but its not. I just wasted many hours of my personal time only to fail the second mission because of bugs. Im okay with wasting my time if i suck or fail, bc at least im learning, but this was infuriating. Several hours into the mission, i lost the scanner trying to protect it. Then the game glitches and the drop box where i would get another one is gone. 10 hours gone, boom, just like that. I restart the mission and THIS time the ghosts where you are supposed to place the triangulating beacons arent even there, I checked all three. Another waste of my time back to back.

Beautiful game, love the challenge, but I HATE wasting time for bugs and bad design, ESPECIALLY when you have released several content packs in the name of making money. You'd make a ton more money if your game was just fun to play and a lot of people liked it.

Oh, and as i was leaving to reset the mission, I loaded up by drop ship with all my workbenches and metal ingots so i could start the next mission strong, but guess what... You dont get to keep ANY of it. They let you load it up and then delete it. You have to start from scratch, weak as hell, every time. Thats ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, dont have a cargo bay in the ship if i cant keep what i leave with. Very annoying.

Bottom line: make the game less annoying and more people will play/pay for it. Not hard math.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo i picked up this game expecting a fun and interesting experience based on all of the positive reviews. What i got instead was a very very boring open world survival craft experience. None of the enemies in the first few hours pose a real threat to you. All you need to do is craft a stack of wooden javelins and you can 'one shot' any of the wildlife while sneaking.

One very important thing to note is that there are zero outstanding structures or interesting terrain - meaning you don't have any legitimate reason to explore. I spent most of my time after crafting my first base running across the map looking for anything and everything to explore. i found zero cave systems, nothing resembling a dungeon/delve experience, zero unique encounters and no unique wildlife besides one giant sandworm that i easily got away from by simply jogging away from it.

I judge this game just like i would any other - if the base game isn't good then its likely the rest of the game won't be either. Im at a complete loss for words seeing all the positive reviews. It was as they say "like watching paint dry."

I cant recommend this game, especially for the price of the DLC. Would highly recommend adding some unique mobs into the early game experience and more interesting challenges.\t\t\t\t\t
