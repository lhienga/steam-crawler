\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen playing solo since shortly after launch. The game was incredible then, and has only gotten better! Challenging gameplay (they have achievements now too) but I spend most of my time in Outpost Mode at the moment. Icarus has a similar building style to other survival games like Rust, Raft etc but with a lot more build pieces (like different roof slope degrees, 1/2 walls and 1/4 walls).

To those who are intimidated by the price of the game including all the expansions:
START WITH THE BASE GAME. Complete at least half of the missions on Icarus before purchasing Styx, and do the same on Styx before purchasing New Frontiers. I purchased all of the DLC in order to support the devs, and haven't even played half the Outpost maps yet or even completed all the missions on a single map. You don't lose out on a ton of gameplay, you just can't play the late game missions (which won't matter if you're new). That being said, the furniture packs are beautiful if you enjoy the game's building system.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't know what I was getting into after reading all the reviews on this game. I waited until they had their free weekend to test it and oh my god... I was instantly hooked.

I am a big follower of games like Scum, RUST, Tarkov etc... Which have certain things I love about them and have not been able to find in any other game, until now. The survivability aspect of this game is thrilling. There's so many things you have to keep in mind to survive, but it doesn't really feel overwhelming or like a chore like many other games do where you feel like you just play to survive with the least amount of fun time.

The developer of this game has done a FANTASTIC job at updating it (based on older videos and reviews) and many many of the things people used to complain about are now gone and replaced for something way better. Graphics are incredible (and my computer has 5 year old tech), I have had no issues with low FPS or anything like that, the music is great, the history is great, farming dynamics, fighting aspect... Everything in this game has been well thought of.

Yes, there are a few kinks here and there like with every other game... But I really want to give the developer all the praise in the world for actually delivering on something so complex and ambitious.

If you like harvesting stuff for survival, doing hunting missions, discovering new caves, nodes, adventures... Fighting bears, the weather and riding mounts over beautiful areas...This is the game for you. It's like RUST but 100x better in pretty much every way (And exclusively PVE of course)\t\t\t\t\t
