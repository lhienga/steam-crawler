\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will preface this review by saying that I am a huge survival genre fan and have tried nearly every well-known first-person survival game on Steam, therefore I've seen what works and what needs improvement. I have owned this game since the day it was released 3 years ago and all I can say is this is an incredible game. From the graphics to the interface to the gameplay, this is a completely polished game with one of the highest replay values on the market. The developers are some of the most responsive I've seen, so big kudos to them for the hard work and passion they have put into this game. At the time of writing this, I have put 76 hours into this game, with half of it being on the open-world setting, and I haven't even explored half of the base game map yet (let alone tried the multiplayer feature). With the number of updates constantly being worked on, I can see Icarus only getting better as the years go by, and although I might take a break from time to time, I will always find myself coming back and getting the same experience from it as if it was my first time logging in. I hope to try the New Frontiers expansion soon, but for now I am blown away by the experience this game provides and look forward to continuing my survival story.\t\t\t\t\t
