\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIcarus has had a lot of work done to it by the devs in the last two years since launch and it deserves your attention. If you like survival games, building bases, map exploration and a plethora of PVE options then it will certainly make you happy. Note that this game does its own thing so you can't expect it to be or behave like other survival games you might know.

The missions/operations are decent, open world mode is great and now 108 weeks of consecutive updates.
There is more than enough different gameplay mechanics for everyone to have something they will really like and it will take anyone many hundreds of hours to explore 3 distinct maps so you will be hard pressed to be bored. The game is not difficult to play and tough to master.

I strongly recommend you and a friend pick this game up and experience everything for the first time together. It will be a really fun experience as you climb the learning curve.

8 out of 10\t\t\t\t\t
