\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitaley not worth the price, this game is an absolute buggy mess.

MAJOR Bug Issues:
- Animal hitboxes often just disappear.
- Open world dedicated server performance degradation requires clients restart the game every day or 2 to recover FPS.
- Major P2P host performance issues, better to run a dedicated on same machine as a client.
- Supply stockpile missions sometimes complete themselves.
-Electrical wiring often doesn't connect to junctions properly
- Memory leaks.
- Viewing the map often plummets performance down to 1FPS.
- Movement and Mounts often get stuck on trees and logs.

MINOR bug issues:
- Chopped trees sometimes fly up into space.
- Wood chopped from tree logs often fall through the ground.
-Dropped objects and killed animals sometimes fall through the ground.
- Mounted camera angle bug requiring re-saddling your mount.
- Some areas of the map have invisible cliffs/boundaries.
- Animals sometimes stop spawning requiring server/host reset.
- Bows sometimes get stuck mid-combat and won't draw until you swap weapons.
- Arrows can collide with small tree leaves.

Gameplay balance issues:
- Game quickly becomes too easy with character level.
- Crafting progression of armor/weapons quickly becomes obselete after you gain access to workshop (loadout) gear.
- The best workshop gear and highest difficulty missions are locked behind DLC (Though you can join DLC maps without owning them, it's still a bit rude).

The developers seem more concerned about adding content than fixing major issues.
