\r\n\t\t\t\t\t150+hrs in this game,it looks spectacular,feels very fleshed out, different biomes look and feel great,especially how they effect your character and his survival.The survival mechanics are sound and not to micro mismanagement depth wise.The skill tree is huge,and allows for alot of diversity on how to flesh out your character. Weapons an armour are great and very fitting towards the story/game. You can progress at your own pace or timed in quest challenge scenarios. Building mechanics are good,some work towards snapping with some pieces would help but not game breaking or frustrating. Sure some animals glitch and get stuck on terrain or foliage,but not game breaking. Dev's are ongoing with updates and fixes. Single player or co-op...overall a great game with hundreds of hours of gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
