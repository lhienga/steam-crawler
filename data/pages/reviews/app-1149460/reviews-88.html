\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is amazing! By far the best survival game in my opinion. The punctual weekly updates, the amazing graphics (even on low end PCs), the down to earth and communicative developers, and the new unique perspective brought into the survival game genre personally makes this an amazing game.
People will complain about how the game didn't promise what it did before it launched, people will complain about the unique survival experience of doing individual missions rather than an Open World to exist and survive in, people will complain about how the game makes no sense that you can breath underwater and still need oxygen. But all of that has been fixed, explained, and made better. I know many friends that didn't want to play the game mainly because it wasn't an Open World survival with a story. And now it is. You don't have to restart the world to do a new mission every time, you can make 1 world and complete every single mission that there is to offer.
There are I believe almost 100 individual missions now which take anywhere from 20 mins - 12 hours which gives a good variety. This along with the weekly updates gives us new missions to look forward to along with a bunch of other content.
There are minor bugs just like with any game, which get fixed mostly within the week, and there are still performance issues for some people which have been improved A LOT since launch. But if you don't mind that (as with any game) you'll be just fine.
I gotta give it to the devs especially, they're amazing people, they constantly talk to the community, always livestream them playing the game and answer any questions in chat. The community and devs really do make this game fun and interesting to play and look forward to new content.
Definitely would recommend this game if you like games like Ark, The Forest, Valheim, or maybe even Rust if you don't mind the lack of PVP. But of course there's nothing stopping you from PVPing with some friends.
I've got over 700 hours now in this game, got the New Frontiers DLC and am absolutely loving it and I won't stop playing as long as they're still bringing out new content\t\t\t\t\t
