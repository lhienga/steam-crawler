The outposts and gear dlc is not really needed unless one is a fan and the 2 additional worlds simply give more room to explore and survive on.

So far spawned, died by pony and bear, explored a bit, we got a base going, slowly getting more and more mats and expanding the base as well as hunting more in the open world.
It is adviced when first starting to start at the default outpost, level up a bit and get a hang of the survival mechanics before jumping into the open world as it makes surviving the open world a bit easier.

It is an interesting game, love how the crafting, building and survival flow inside the game I would recommend it.

The roguelite esque way the character one creates slowly levels and has more and more options open as well as later on gear for harder challenges feels nice as well.

The game runs well on my 4070, one is constantly listening to telltale signs of certain animals that might give a problem while gathering materials to further upgrade or expand ones base or gear.\t\t\t\t\t
