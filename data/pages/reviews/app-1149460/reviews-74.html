You can repair early items from your inventory.
You can craft while moving and doing other stuff.
Picking up things in the world is fast and you can hold the button pressed while sweeping with the mouse.
Mining is showing progress on the stone, where individual voxels are mined.

Could use some work.
They also consistenty get stuck at rocks and fallen trees and don't seem to notice that their position doesn't change at all.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis open world survival craft game is challenging but rewarding at the same time. Just the simple act of gathering supplies is so satisfying and yet the game is infinitely more complex than that. I've found myself rage quitting more than a handful of times only to come back a few hours or days later wanting more. I've found myself thinking about strategies and the to do list for my next session while i'm at work or doing chores or whatever. At it's base, the game is simple. Gather, make, survive, level up, repeat. But having some sort of a strategy is crucial to your survival. Just when you think you've got things under control, you're confronted with a completely new challenge and sometimes forced to start over.
The scenery is beautiful and you're immersed in nature, being constantly reminded that you are both predator and prey. Harvesting resources makes you question your footprint on the environment of the planet. The music both supports the game play environment and is also haunting and sometimes downright creepy.
Bottom line 10/10 would recommend to anyone who wants a good challenge.\t\t\t\t\t
