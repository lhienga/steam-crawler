\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBUYER BEWARE!

This game currently has a memory leak or similarly major optimization issue which does not occur until AFTER the refund period is over. Evidently this occurs the more players interact with the open world environment and stacks slowly over time. I didn't start noticing performance degradation until about 20 hours in once I had built a couple structures and started exploring the world. I was running 60fps perfectly until this point. Now I have frequent sporadic drops as extreme as 25-30fps and generally in the 5-15 frame loss territory. I cannot recommend this be purchased in all good conscience.

Aside from the performance issues, I've been enjoying the gameplay loop a lot, but I've reached a point with only 30 hours where the experience is absolutely bogged by the performance problems.\t\t\t\t\t
