\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have put many hours int othis game with solo play and co-op. I have completed all 60 levels and I am working on a second character for specialization. I have put far less hours into other games so that should say something positive about Icarus to everyone. YET, there are lots of fixes needed for this game.

BUILD - It is not easy to build as the snapping tool is basic and difficult to work with.Build areas from ground to sky need addressing as you may not be able to stack or build over a space to achieve your plan. I ahve played other survival games where this has been address successfully.

There is nothing more annoying than trying to put a ladder on what you have built only to have to undo and redo a number of times to only get close. This pertains from the build point ground to structure. You can easly put a ladder on top of a build, but it will not snap to your target.

ENEMIES! - Basic enemies are easly dealt with until you get into the Arctic. Basic enemies become crazy hard to kill. For example, a brown forest bear is powerful but can be killed in 4-6 well placed shots to the face. A polar bear however may take 14 - 20 arrows to the face. You can succeed, but will more than likely be killed. Then add being attacked by more than one at a time. Rediculous.

CLIPPING - This game has some horrible clipping in it. Enemies can swipe through barracades without destroying them and kill you. Make sure you have plenty of room away from walls to survive. Needler enemy can shoot needles through rock and hit you. It would be okay if it were not so rediculously difficult to kill them without advanced weaponry.

INSTRUCTIONS - There are NONE. You must rely on the community to answer questions. There is no manual for this game that I know of. Look for Wiki's to answer your questions and join the community chat.
