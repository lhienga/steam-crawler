\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, almost 100 hours in, I think I can say something about this game....

So, I think the game is really great IF you like this kind of games. In my opinion this is more of a "colony builder" than a "survival" game cause while there are survival mechanics in place and they work well, the goal is to create a thriving colony that not only makes it possible to survive but makes you the owner of the planet you are on. As a prospector you will be searching for ores, plants, hunt for animals and build workshops which will make your life on the planet much easier . Additionally you will be searching for exotic ores which you can then sell to get some gear you can't craft on your own. The graphics are great, the planet looks very alive and you can make this world your own. There are also some missions to take on, I'd say they are "nothing special" but this is not a RPG game after all.

Saying all that, there are some small issues here and there with the biggest one being the massive grind in order to progress your "colony". There are some quality of life enhancements to make it a bit easier when playing alone but you will still need to grind quite a lot to progress. However, if you like to discover new places, great views, a lot of freedom when building your colony (and the building itself is really fun) and stuff like that, the grind is not that bad. But not gonna lie, it could be lowered a bit by making some of the building pieces and benches "cheaper".

Finally, it's really worth mentioning that developers are constantly improving the game, adding new systems, features and content that makes the game even better. So yeah, I do enjoy this game quite a lot.\t\t\t\t\t
