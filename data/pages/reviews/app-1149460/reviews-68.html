\r\n\t\t\t\t\tConsidering where the game was when it started I was pleasantly surprised to see how far it has come. I'll be upfront and say I waited for it to go on sale before I pulled the trigger, and for that price point I would say the game is absolutely worth it. Don't feel like you're obligated or need to get the dlc maps, you can just purchase the "base" game and you can play the missions and open world both in single player and with friends. I will say there is some definite glitchiness and jank with different aspects, especially when talking about combat and the mounts but for me at least it wasn't that big of deal. Caused a little bit of annoyance, but considering the state of most games I can look past most of what i've seen plus the devs have been consistently (literally weekly) patching the game so I'll give them the benefit of the doubt. Combat is pretty simplistic, hoping maybe they'll show some love to that especially everything outside of bows and guns, but beyond that the survival aspects of the game are pretty good. Kinda reminds me of a cross between Valheim and Satisfactory. I have seen some people talking about having issues with FPS drop, but I haven't seen anything dramatic - maybe the occasional stutter or jitter here and there when on a open world map with a group of friends. If you'll take the time to go in a tweak some of the graphic settings and not just set everything to ultra because "I have an awesome computer" you should be fine. The stutter and jitter might be directly correlated with how much you've actually built on the map, not sure on that one yet though. Overall, the games definitely not perfect but its a farcry from where it started so I have to give the devs their props for listening to the community and putting in the work to get the game back up and running. It's definitely not a fast paced, run and gun game, but if you enjoy slow burn games and the survival aspect then I would say give the game a shot when its on sale - especially if you have a group of friends to play with. If you're looking for a game that's non stop action and combat then probably not the choice for you. Also, beware of polar bears and scorpions...those guys suck!\t\t\t\t\t
