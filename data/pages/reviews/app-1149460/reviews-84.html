\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen playing the game since it released, and enjoying the QoL enhancements and progressive improvements they do to there game. This has been one of, if not, then is my favourite Solo/Multiplayer Survival game. The core gameplay being a mission based drop loop on new missions helps it not get stale as you find new ways to optimize and build up.

I love the setting on a terraforming disaster, fighting the Elements and the wildlife, trying to collect rare materials to upgrade you drop equipment and unlock new items. The new expansion has released a bunch of great QoL improvements as they lean more into allowing you to play the way you want and let loose on top of delving more I to the alien theme possibilities.

The only downside I'd say there is, is that the game is not very optimized. They've worked hard on it over the years to help, but still you normally need a fair decent PC to play it optimally. I wouldn't recommend having less than a 20 series card to get the most out of this game and here hoping for better possibility for lower end builds.
