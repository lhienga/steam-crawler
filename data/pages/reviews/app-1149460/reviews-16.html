"Exclusive to Prometheus" is the norm for new updates. The literal definition of bait and switch.

The game might get better, but I can't fundamentally support devs who rug pulled a community that supported them in early access.

If you don't give a ♥♥♥♥ about that then the game might be for you.

This is intended gameplay. If you skip the open world and just do missions, it gets incredibly boring with half a dozen enemies set on repeat.

Give it another year to bake. It's technically out of EA, but still for all intents and purposes still in EA. You'll likely have to buy a 3rd DLC by the time the game is in a state the devs promised when it first launched.\t\t\t\t\t
