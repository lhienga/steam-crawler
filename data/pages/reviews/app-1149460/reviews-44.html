\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the best survival games that I've played, especially with the addition of the New Frontiers expansion where they honed in on story driven mostly linear missions in an open world.

For me, the pros of the game are:
Amazing immersive environments
Co-op is pretty seamless
Crafting and building are solid
Open world with story driven quests
Storms add a variable intensity to the game

Cons:
AI/creatures aren't intelligent and are very predictable
Talent tree is large and cumbersome
Workshop items are annoying to repair in the world
Batteries don't exist, even though things like flashlights that use batteries do
It can feel a bit "grindy" when gathering materials, especially tier 3/4
