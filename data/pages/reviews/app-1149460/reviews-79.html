The deers and such do not agro, but there are horses that spawn in packs that do and 2 minutes to a mission (not yet even at the mission site), in "mild shower" that damages the character without cover, there is nothing you can do alone to fight them in packs. And when dying, you start with nothing and the cycle continues. Also you take damage from the rain when you are in a pod (fully enveloped in the pod I should say) returning to the planet after dying which for me was the straw that broke the camel's back. Apparently there were not enough dying in the game testing to get that lunacy crunched.

Open world is nice, but when you go to do a mission, you lose the progress of buildings, warehoused materiel etc. and to go back you need to choose to lose your XP/character lvls or the progress on the planet.

Game has great graphics and wildlife behaviours (apart of the killer horses) like the wolves howling and gathering and lone wolves staying away before getting their numbers and attacking. It's such a shame that the open world does not have same kind of mission objectives to call/activate than the actual missions (which are ruined by the earlier cycle) does.\t\t\t\t\t
