\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI read reviews for everything it seems and I get all points. This review is a recommend but there are points good and bad like most other games.

Pros:

- I play on an older rig. I used to upgrade mobo/cpu/memory and graphics cards usually every 4 to 5 years stretching things out. Been harder the last years to get good graphics cards. I run twin 1070‚Äôs and yet this game still looks beautiful. I do have ssd, i9 processor and a decent z390 mobo so maybe that helps.

- Tons of gameplay space. Reminds me of Fallout 4 in terms of linear space to cruise around.

- Being able to do missions breaks up the monotony of resource needs helps to keep the game fresh. Mind you, many of these missions are the same and not always good.

- Slaughtering animals with a bow is rewarding. Like lots of killing.

- Building snap is pretty good for the most part. I did run into multiple times corner pieces just would not snap the direction I wanted. Build ramp pieces, get closer and boom, they snap

Cons:

- Animals definitely spawn in on a cycle and that makes a schedule and yea, boring.

- When you ask for a mission towards the beginning after building the station, expect to be screwed. If you do not have cold weather gear, yep, that is right, that is where that mission will be. Kind of like a game giving the option for something but you need to make something. The issue is they already know you can‚Äôt make the item so they offer it.

- Kind of weird that I essentially walk right down large cliffs on animals. I can take my horse and just hit a small angle and slowly walk right down steep cliffs. Every now and then, yea they make get hurt but minimal.

- If they can make mammoths, they can make dragons. Just saying‚Ä¶.would be kewl.

The dlc, IMO seems to be overpriced for what you get. If they offered like an Apache chopper, my attention might be better controlled. WITH rockets and .050 mounted for my friends. Just in case.

I recommend this. My friend loads into my little server and it plays well. I have a newer Alienware laptop with a 3080 and I admit that I do not notice anything real crazy in the playability. I am sure my graphics settings are not the same but it plays in both places well.

For transparency, I am barely into the dessert and maybe I get more ideas later. I will update if so.\t\t\t\t\t
