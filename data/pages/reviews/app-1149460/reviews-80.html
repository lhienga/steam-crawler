\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIcarus is a great game to play both solo and coop, if you enjoy sandbox games
and like to keep yourself busy with building bases, stockpiling ressources or just enjoy hunting, fishing and exploring the beauty of it's landscapes.
It's especially great, since dedicated servers and a persistent open world mode have been added, allowing you to keep
your session running without a timer which would usually have you evac your mission and start up a new one.
(which was the original gameplay cycle the game launched with)

I've been with the game from the first announcement to day 1 and forth and found myself coming back over and over,
to see what the regular weekly updates have drip fed into the game, to enrich an experience which to me personally, is one of a kind. If you're looking for a chill open world game with beautiful visuals, great audio ambience, lot's of crafting and building while also having persistent character progression (both in terms of skills and workshop unlocks to optimize your loadout and playstyle) do give this one a try.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game since Alpha, part of all the playtests. I have watched the game go through its ups and downs. There is not really any part of me that can not recommend this game at its state as of this review.

The first complaint I felt most had was "I built a base and its gone" has been resolved through the Hypatia updates. You load in, make a base, accomplish objectives, launch out. But, where is the base and time I spent? Gone; no longer. RocketWerkz has made leaps and bounds to balance and keep us all happy. Not to mention, I have NEVER seen a gaming company do weekly updates. They have not missed a week yet.

If you are looking for a challenge they have their timer based missions. If you are looking for something a little more laid back, you have their open world operations. If you are looking for the ultimate lay back, just build, and have fun with no worries they have outposts.

This game is my #1 most recommended survival craft builder.

If I had any criticism at the moment, is that the first two maps of this game do not have the story aspect that is a huge part of the new frontiers expansion. Outside of that, this game is a masterpiece of survival craft. The work that has been done to balance every aspect of this game is remarkable.

I highly recommend to everyone looking for a game with friends, or even as solo play.

10 out of 10\t\t\t\t\t
