But.
1. When you get to Tier 3/Tier 4 game feels off. The amount of gold/copper ore you have to get is ridiculous. Why tf do i need to gather 10000 gold ore and 30000 copper ore for a slightly better gear and benches? 97% missions can be completed without building Tier 4 fabricator bench at all.

2. Its been 2 years since game released. Game has a lot of bugs. Don't get me wrong, im fine with most of the bugs in games, im a 2023 gamer after all, i've seen ♥♥♥♥, i just feel like i have to mention whats wrong with this game. Olympus has a lot of holes you won't notice while exploring. I mean... holes. Like... Holes? You are just running from point A to point B and you are dead. Why? There was a hole on your way in the mountain you jumped in because it had a texture but no collision so gravity killed you.
ALL stockpile missions are bugged for me, drop pod immediately leaves when i approach and i can just leave to receive my 1500 Ren.
Character model is invisible 50% of the time when im using third person view
Ore nodes sometimes are impossible to gather
And so on.

3. Missions design. This is the worst about this game. Lets take a mission i just completed 30 minutes ago as an example. You've got to get through the ice border. So you get there and ONLY WHEN YOU ARE ALREADY THERE Dude on the radio says that you have to use a quest equipment (some kind of a laser ♥♥♥♥). This equipment is located WHERE YOU BEEN 5 MINUTES AGO, IT JUST SPAWNED THERE. GO BACK AND TAKE IT. Fine, ok, not a big problem, maybe some kind of a script has to be activated for it to spawn, idk. But when im getting closer this dude says that i have to provide electricity for that ♥♥♥♥ to work. Why can't we know all of that when we start a mission? And all mission are like that. ALL. It just sucks.

4. Your dropship. 80% of the time, when you completed a mission, you've got to walk 500 miles back to your dropship to leave the area. 20% of the time its 200 miles, not 500. lol. But you can buy dropship beacon (yeah, sure, after you spent 350 exotics just to research it). Why can't we have an option to just call a dropship any time we want?

5. Game is too easy, i died only 5 times. Im playing on hardest difficulty with only one life. Died just because mission design is ♥♥♥♥♥♥/i was new to the game and understood no ♥♥♥♥.

6. Im gay. Game is still fun and you'll probably play hundreds of hours. Even with all those problems i really enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
