\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love OWSC. I bounce from title to title, and typically find that the majority are good, but also since they're in EA, they're jank as f***. Originally when I saw Icarus announced, I was hyped, based on what I saw in the teasers and trailers. Then I learned it was mostly broken on arrival, poorly reviewed, and the idea of roguelike survival didn't actually appeal to me.

During one of the past steam sales, this game popped up again. I was surprised and excited to learn it added an Open World sandbox component, giving it a more traditional feel. It didn't immediately click with me and I put it aside. I think the biggest issue I have is the weird disconnect between what I'm doing on the planet, and where I came from to get here. You're telling me that I can be sent to a different, terraformed planet, but when I get there I have to cobble tools together with the loose stone and twigs I find laying around? It introduces so much dissonance that it's really hard not to feel silly building a thatch structure next to a pristine landing pod with all sorts of tech.

With that being said: suspend your disbelief. Eventually I just threw up my arms and said "forget the space side". It's gone a lot further in aiding my enjoyment of this game. I'm able to appreciate the building and gathering system, both of which are very well done. Like Rust, but improved. The hunting actually feels really good, but I couldn't explain why. Where this game seems to really shine, so far, is in the progression/tech trees. I actually think they've nailed a perfect balance of experience and skills. There's a depth here that isn't exceedingly overdone, but just enough to spruce up the whole survival experience. I especially enjoy the various wounds and infections you can get, and the myriad ways to treat them.

I have not played the mission structured version of the game, nor do I think I plan to. With both Palworld and Enshrouded leaving a lot to be desired, if you're looking for something a bit different but still the same to the existing flock of OWSC games, I do recommend picking this up!\t\t\t\t\t
