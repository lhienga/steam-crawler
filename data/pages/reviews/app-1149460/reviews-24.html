\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to enjoy playing this game. It has so much potential but just falls flat at delivering a fun survival experience that doesn't feel repetitive or annoying. Albeit, this changes somewhat with friends but not for long. After a few missions, the game feel tedious starting from scratch again and again and again, where I never got past tier 3 tech. There are some "issues" with this game that the community has raised time and time again, with some being fixed but others hanging in "known issue" limbo on the featureupvote page.

I bought this game in Beta (for 24 iirc). I knew in my first play-through in August 2021 that the game would get a mixed review score at the time of release. I had mixed feelings about the game at the time of beta and I still have mixed feelings after playing today.

There are bugs/issues from beta still in the game. The wood/items falling through the world. Now I wouldn't mind if it happened sometimes, but every tree I cut down, at least 1 piece of wood falls through. I don't care for the lost materials, I care that it breaks the immersion, its a constant reminder that the game is unfinished.

Animals still see you crouching behind inside your base with the doors and windows closed. They have Superman's x-ray vision. The mounts are a super cool idea, but the fact that the mother's of these animals are roaming and constantly aggressive (more than wolves or bears) is annoying. The amount of times I'm just chopping a tree and then a horse is charging me or attacking my hut as I'm crouching inside. Then I'm left with a defense-less foal who gets eaten by wolves the next day. The idea is amazing, the execution in game is not. The starting areas, to me, are too full of animals. It's like I've dropped into a zoo enclosure.

Player movement has always been super clunky. Jumping feels like I'm on the moon and just drift and bounce off things as I move through the air. Walking over rocky terrain will get you stuck and small rocky inclines are impassable. Trying to jump over these just doesn't work and makes things worse. With the world filled to the brim of trees, rocks, bushes, rivers and lakes. Combined with the terrible movement and the sheer distance of places for missions. Makes it so frustrating to traverse through this world on foot.

Performance is another "issue" that has been back and forth from the start.

Character creation feels bland with no real options to chose from and just feels a "preset". I can't make a unique character, there just isn't enough options. Each one I make feels and looks the same, male or female with white skin tone and same bodies. No height, width, hair, skin tone, complexion, facial hair, etc, options. When I play with friends, a big part is making our own characters and the crazy characters they come up with. Here we look and feel the same, like NPCs.

The tech changes were nice to consolidate some of the tech unlocks, but still feels like a absolute grind to progress in this game.

After 2+ years from "release" there have been weekly updates, which is actually amazing. But, updating this game is anywhere between 5-10mins EVERY friday. I get home from work, want to fire up this game that I played and updated last week only to update it yet again before playing. Just release them monthly or quarterly please. This alone made me uninstall the game until more updates came out. I know its not their fault for how steam makes you update the game, but they are choosing to release updates each week.

Honestly, I don't know about this game, I want to enjoy playing it, but its a bit of a chore to do that. Maybe I'll try it again in the future and change my review.\t\t\t\t\t
