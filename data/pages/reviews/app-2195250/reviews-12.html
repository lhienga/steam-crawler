\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo start with, I would like to clarify for EA, that this is not a simple copy-n-paste of the last game. Because it is clearly worse than and probably the worst game in this series for the past few years.

This game has successfully inherited the continuing failing passing logic from its predecessors, and included a new system for interacting with the ball that makes dribbling/tackling experiences more painful than ever. The newly developed weather system emphasizes the effect of various field conditions, which helps to increase the pain of playing.

Apart from the physics and interaction system, the AI difficulty system is also problematic. It seems that this game is setting strong handicaps on the AI teammates, since it feels like playing along with 10 audiences who come on the fields just to take a closer look at the ball, instead of actually playing. Playing against more competitive opponents in a higher difficulty level makes sense, but playing with more incompetent teammates does not.

I feel sorry for the developers and designers who work for the art and graphics of this game. Because despite their good work for providing more realistic graphics, the quality of this game is still under satisfactory due to the work of their colleagues.\t\t\t\t\t
