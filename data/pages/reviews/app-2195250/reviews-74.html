\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely buggy after the last update. Crashes at different points. Ultimate team especially - very poor experience.

Generally the gameplay is fun but also a bit unintuitive at times. Like previous versions it's dominated by speedy players who also happen to have good strength so it's usually teams like PSG and Real Madrid that have an advantage. Difficult to play a passing game, interceptions are very unrealistic - there's no concept of body positioning and angle at all. This is also the case when taking shots due to which higher rated players can score from very abnormal positions and angles.

All in all good time pass if you play every now and then online or with friends but would love to see the gameplay improve significantly. Fifa was a very good title 10+ years ago\t\t\t\t\t
