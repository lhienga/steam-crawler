Long story short:
If you play offline modes, experience is the same every single match, all is fair, but if you are playing Online however, you will totally experience how it feels like playing in La Liga and its referees LOL

Gameplay and Controls
Honestly, shooting is awful especially in online-matches, sometimes it feels like the game really wants you to lose a match, there have been many occasions where players miss the goal from a laughable positions or should I say 99% scoring chance, they completely miss the goal.
However, if the game "favors" you, you can literally score some impossible goals with ease.

Graphics
They are ok, strand-based hair makes most of the difference, when it is visible players look fantastic, without it, players look pretty meh, so hopefully in the next game all of the hairstyles and beards will be strand-based.

But there is obviously a lot of room for improvement, Frostbite game engine is stagnating a bit currently, they must improve it a lot if they want to keep on using it, otherwise they will have to scrap the game once again and move to another engine (*cough*Unreal Engine*cough*)

Are there bugs?
Yes!
Simple camera bug from time to time, nothing major, or some in-game stat graphics bug, but nothing game-breaking.

When it comes to the game launching however, EA Anti-Cheat will sometime not initialize so the game wont launch, sometimes EA Play is acting out, so it will not initialize EA Anti-Cheat, which will not initialize game..................................
So, be prepared to sometime spend a minute or two to diagnose what is causing the issue, so you can fix it, its fixable, but still, there should be no issues at all.

Would I recommend it?
Well, if you are a fan of Fifa/EA FC games, then yes, its different enough from previous games to enjoy it, but is it completely different, no!\t\t\t\t\t
