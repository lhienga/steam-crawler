\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm gonna say this, and I need everyone to promise not to hurt me when I do...

But uh, Fifa is fun again?

I know, I know, blasphemy. Its not as good as it could be, but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ if FC24 hasn't held me, and its *especially* worth trying at 14 bucks on sale like I got it. I've already got my money's worth and I'm nowhere near done.

I will say, I absolutely am ignoring Ultimate Team because I know how insane of a money and time sink that can become, I used to go crazy with it from '13 to '18 and it just isn't worth spending the time with if I'm not also willing to open my wallet. I wish there was less MTX with that mode but thats a long lost battle at this point.

What IS worth paying attention to is whats there beyond UT, and by that I mean career modes. I have a player career as a CM for the Spurs looking to trade into my longtime favorite team, Chelsea (woot woot) and I took over QPR as a manager and I mean... maybe its just me but the management mode is just super fun now? I really enjoy all the visuals and upgrades that have from when I last tried Fifa in 2021 when it was still called Fifa, but its such a jam piloting the day to day and matchday of a tier 2 English club. I like that if I don't feel like playing a game too I can just simulate it and let it fly, but its quite a fun experience.

I can also 100% get behind how the game is moving away from how it was when I used to play where getting 5* skill moves players to just spam the right stick in circles to win was the meta. Tiki taka passing is very effective, shooting feels way better than it used to, the through balls work great, the defense took a tiny step back but overall the broadcast experience took a massive leap forward too.

I hate praising EA as much as the next guy, I mean its practically a ritual at this point to try the yearly sports releases (2K, Madden, Etc) and feel like it regresses every time, but FC24 is a genuine step up and for 80% off, if you're a long time soccer lover like myself thats taken a break for awhile, this is actually a fantastic time to pick the game back up.

If you're interested, I review all manner of games because I have far too much free time and like helping people find good games and avoid sh*t ones, so feel free to give my curator page a follow so my reviews pop up for you. Thanks for reading.
