\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPurchased the game this year on a 80% discount... Let's just say, it's a game not worth of even 14$.

Been a die-hard supporter of FIFA from 2016, the golden days of career mode, where I would sit down every day after school playing countless hours of career mode. Lastly, I've been playing FIFA 2023 and decided to "upgrade" to EA FC 24. Let's just say...

Performance? As soon as I entered, the settings were so bad that the frame rate was locked to 30 FPS. I decided to unlock my FPS, guess what? For some reason, the refresh rate was locked to 60, no hint as to why that was the case. Decided to play around with settings, removed vertical sync, any fps limit and the likes, still had issue with the game running at 50 FPS, on a RTX 4070 with 32GB of RAM, and an i9 13900hx.

Decided to play around with the settings more, even changing the resolution or display type would take around 10 seconds. A lot of times the settings were reverted back and forth because of how long it took to actually unfreeze the game after changing the settings. Forget that, I got it set up eventually... Still, even the menu of all things will actually stutter at some points.

(NOTE: This didn't ever happen in any other game, out of the ~300 I got, not even previous FIFA games, so it's definitely an issue with this game)

Skipping past the performance points? Like some other review mentioned, they up defense, literally you can blow past any defenses, be it world class or legendary, it's kind of a joke. I scored 9 goals in my first (and only) game on World Class.

Gameplay-wise? They kind of improved the passing and stuff over the last year, I think. I had fun playing around passing, but not shooting, at all. It's just weird.

Graphics-wise? .

Commentary? God, I wanted to kill myself during the game.
El Classico, one of the most anticipated derbies, Lewandowski scores the hattrick, what's the commentary like? It's as if you brought your average Joe from England and brought him to comment on the goal. "There's the hattrick, he's bringing it home". That's literally it, no shouting, no screaming, no anticipation of joy, nothing. It's as if he described it while reading the lines.

Refunding this game at once.\t\t\t\t\t
