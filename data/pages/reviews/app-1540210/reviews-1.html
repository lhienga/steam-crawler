\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had to refund this game. It ran very poorly on my computer and for such a simple game with bare bones VR mechanics that's a bad sign. I wanted to like the dog but he whines allot and there was no option (hope they add one) to turn the volume down on him or turn the whine off. There is a part in the beginning of the game where your dog runs ahead of you and starts whining at a door that brings you into the next area. I wanted to explore a side room and the dog never stopped. He just kept whining in my ears like he was right next to me. And the whole purpose was to show you where to go... There was no where else to go. It was an obvious straight line without deviation that is impossible to miss. Yet the designer felt the need to have an endless high pitch dog whine play on a loop to let me know the only door moving forward was the door to go through. There were other small issues with guns disappearing and strange references to current day american politics which were annoying. Unfortunately if the game gets really good after a couple hours (I hear its a very short game) I just couldn't get past the lag.
I have an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3080 Ti
AMD Ryzen 7 5800X 8-Core Processor 4.20 GHz
and 32 gigs of ram.\t\t\t\t\t
