\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a very fun, exciting, and hilarious addition to the Airzona Sunshine franchise. I had a blast completing the story campaign on the hard difficulty which took me 8 hours. The gun mechanics are good, although a few weapons could use a better explanation on how to reload them. I'm looking at you double barrel shotgun.

I took my time while playing the story mode, exploring the maps looking for items. You can craft your own items which is a nice addition. Unfortunately, you hardly have any inventory slots so most of the time I skip on crafting the items. Thankfully your dog buddy can store some weapons but only this smaller weapons that go on your hip. It would have been nice if Buddy could have held on to one large weapon.

Overall, this is a really fun game that really freshens up Arizona sunshine. It's got a few mechanics that feel quite dated for a VR game in 2023, but that is easy to look past while you're playing and having a great time.\t\t\t\t\t

i have to change my review to negative because how offensively bad the horde mode is. i wanted to play the game more, but being stuck in a square with zombies coming at me from all angles all the time is not my idea of fun no mater how good gun play and zombie animation is.
