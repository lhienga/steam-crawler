\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really don't understand the positive reviews. This game is a big step down from the prequel. It's very fiddly with the guns (e.g. reloading is just annoying and doesn't work most of the time, guns just drop on the ground even though they are supposed to snap back to the wrist after dropping). Also the combat just feels annoying. You can run through the game for 10 minutes and only see like 5 single zombies and then get into an area where 50+ zombies swarm you and the only real way to survive (without dropping the difficulty) is to teleport around the map for like 5 minutes and occasionally shoot at the zombies. The graphics are ok/good but the world feels very empty. Combined with constant disconnects and other problems in coop and some desync, the game just isn't fun.\t\t\t\t\t
