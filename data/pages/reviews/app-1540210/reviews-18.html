\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery good game, but not as polished as I would have expected.
Graphically, you can tell it's been developed for Quest first: lots of low poly models, even if they added more detailed models for PC. A little disappointing, but the good thing is that it runs very smoothly with max settings. Many clipping and collision problems, even though the physics are pretty good. Animations are a little too stiff.
The gameplay is a bit rough and sometimes immersion breaking: it's often impossible to get close to the environment and grab items, and it takes time to get used to the inventory management.
And, of course, there's the issue of the campaign duration.
But the atmosphere is phenomenal, if you liked AS1, you'll love it. The voice acting is great and the interactions with Buddy are a real plus. The shooting feeling is good, with a variety of weapons, and the melee combat is basic but fun.
Overall highly recommended, and probably one of the best VR releases of the year, hoping that the game will soon be improved. If you liked AS1, get it now. Otherwise, wait for the first discount.
Played on wireless Vive Cosmos Elite, with Index controllers, RTX 3080 Ti, i9-9900K.\t\t\t\t\t
