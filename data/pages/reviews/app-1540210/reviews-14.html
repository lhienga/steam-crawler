\r\n\t\t\t\t\tArizona Sunshine was a pioneer game that I really enjoyed. Slow paced head shot fun with a story and enough corny dialogue to be amusing. Arizona Sunshine 2 was underwhelming and proved to be just a overhyped sequel with very few, if any, new features. It feels older than it is and I dont understand how most well known content creators made such a fuss about it. It has strange weapon handling, finicy grabbing mechanics and idiotic ”musical chair” gun swapping that just felt really combersom. Grabbing a new weapon swaps out whatever the game thinks you want to swap. Not being able to a gun down anywhere, and pick up the one you want is really stupid. The dog is ok but mainly caused irritation by its constant whining. The game is ok at best and definitely not worth full price.\t\t\t\t\t
