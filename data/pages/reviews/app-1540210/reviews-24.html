\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall I give this game a 4 out of 10

PROS :D
- Having a dog companion is very adorable
- Dog can wear a hat :D and store single handed guns
- Deluxe edition cosmetics can be turned on immediately anywhere in the settings
- The 2 handed weapons from AS1 made its way
- Melee weapons was a nice addition
- Storing items in hand slots was nice
- Controls are easy to use (You can avoid grabbing areas with a setting XD)
- There's cosmetics for co-op but usually it's just masks and hats.

CONS D:
- The story is about 4 hours long sadly... Kinda obvious that the dog was patient zero
- Lighting in dark areas needs some work (I have max brightness on for the index and I still can't see some items in the dark.)
- You know when a big wave of zombies are coming when you get a TON of AMMO... and there is a crafting station nearby (I basically beat the whole game with a pistol..)
- THE MAIN CHARACTERS DIALOGUE IS JUST LIKE THE FIRST ONE cheesy and kinda annoying
- NOT ENOUGH WEAPONS
- 2 handed weapons are kinda lack luster in my opinion because there's not much weapons to use...
- All the weapons don't really have any damage numbers to them so you can basically use and gun you desire.
- Comparing this games price to other VR games... sadly there are better options out there. The game feels outdated and similar to its previous.
- Loading screens are kinda a buzz killer
- If you use Valve index controllers, pray to god the controller tracking for fingers is on point.
- THERE WAS SOME AREAS WHERE MY GAME WOULD STUTTER RANDOMLY and freeze ( I'm on a 3090 and i9-11900k )\t\t\t\t\t
