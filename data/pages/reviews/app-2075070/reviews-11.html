\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"KinitoPET" is a surprisingly creative indie horror game about a virtual pet similar to "Bonzi Buddy" and it's clearly inspired by the sinister version of it. Also the game pushes so many boundaries that my antivirus acted against it three times (signaling for url phishing and blocking it, not sure why).

I am honestly surprised by the uniqueness of this game and I can't recommend it more to those of you who ever had a nightmare of getting your location doxxed or losing control of your computer due to a virus. This game can even take control of your webcam which is really disturbing, to say the least.

It is totally worth it on full price and it will definitely give you creeps at some points. "KinikoPET" is a massive breath of fresh air from all of the horror games filled with cheap jumpscares, generic narrative and mediocre gameplay.

I can undoubtedly give this game a rating of 8.5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
