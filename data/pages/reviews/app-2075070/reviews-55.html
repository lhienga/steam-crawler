For experienced players of the genre, a relatively average first half with unfortunately a few cliché scares, but a fantastic second half in combination with incredibly impressive technical elements, brilliant music, ideas and scares that more than rip it off - even as someone who really has a lot When I played these things, I thought to myself several times: "How the hell did the game implement that?"

Unfortunately, the fear of losing YouTube viewers and impatient content creators too quickly at the beginning shows up a bit, but the last 30-40 minutes make you forget it quickly.. And the finale.. Damn.

9/10 - Pure genius and the strongest in the genre, but unfortunately lost the place for horror veterans in the eternal Olympus due to impatience\t\t\t\t\t
