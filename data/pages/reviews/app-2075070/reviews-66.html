Just listen, the only achievement that is basically an absolute MUST if you want everything, is the "Bliss" achievement (Basically, that's finishing the game, getting that achievement).

Anyways, overall... this game is a 9/10 for me. The only thing that bogs it down for me is the waiting times for some things, it gets a little frustrating. (Oh, and the fact that this isn't really that scary, but it still classifies as a horror game imo).\t\t\t\t\t

I don't think I'm in the target audience for this game. The target audience, I think, is young people who don't know much about computers, because those are the only people who would be scared by these mechanics. That's not meant as offense towards the developer or people who were genuinely scared by this game! It's just my thoughts. I think that if you don't have experience with games that involve file manipulation as a scare tactic + game mechanic, this game would be really novel to you and pretty creepy! I've just seen it and am apparently an old fart now. That mechanic hasn't ever really scared me, even when it was new. Neither does the other creep factor in kinitopet, occasional gore / mild shock images . Like, hypnospace outlaw (an excellent old-web-style game with psychological horror elements) doesn't do either of those things as part of its creepy features -- I think it was a lot more successful than kinitopet as far as creating intrigue and suspense just through the story.

Like....the game is fine. I can see why some people might really like this. I don't DISlike it. It just wasn't really my jam, you know? That being said, since the achievements aren't hard, I did go ahead and snag a 100%. :)

Thank you for putting your time and care into this project, developer. I hope it was a good experience for you and that your fanbase continues to grow.\t\t\t\t\t
