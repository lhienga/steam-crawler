\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKinitoPet offers a beautiful experience that tops many other horror games for way less! As someone who's almost completed the entire game (only 2 more things to get!), I can say its really is good. However, the main issue arises upon the post game, where certain sections become somewhat tedious.

[SPOILERS AHEAD]


Throughout the game, there are instances where you're required to reset, often resulting in a lengthy boot process. While initially charming, this becomes frustrating when aiming for the true ending. Additionally, I encountered some bugs that slightly broke immersion, such as Steam notifications appearing in Kinito's speech bubbles and/or the games inside of it (eg minesweeper, web world, etc).

On top of that, certain aspects of the horror lose their impact over time. For instance, the personal Q&A segment felt more comical than frightening, especially if your steam friends got jokey profiles pictures! (I got jumpscared from tails Get Trolled and laughed my ass off)

One notable flaw is the game's failure to acknowledge specific player actions. For instance, when providing genuine information, the game's response remains unchanged. This oversight detracts from immersion and potential narrative depth. Also I assume any game outside of Steam doesn't work, as when i typed Mario Galaxy as my fav game, it was changed with RPG Maker in the "personal world" section, which sucks...

Despite these shortcomings, KinitoPet delivers an enjoyable and genuinely disturbing experience. While it falls short of a perfect score (to me) due to bugs and repetitive post-game sections, it stands out from the mascot horror slop we've been getting with its unique story and unsettling atmosphere. I highly recommend it to horror fans seeking a fresh and memorable gaming experience!! 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthis game has an amazing idea but not the best story
(this review is quite spoiler heavy, if you want the non-spoiler opinion, it's that this game has an interesting concept that you should try, but don't expect the best story)

the things this game does to your computer is amazing, and i feel that the game should try and lean into that more, rather than "what if this digital companion was REAL??? and wanted to KILL YOU!!!"
trying to act like kinitoPET is real doesn't work, especially considering that kinitoPET is cute. in no way shape or form would i feel threatened upon seeing a real version of kinitoPET. to add on, the horror in this shouldn't be about a cute mascot trying to harm you - games like FNAF have done that before - it should instead delve deeper into the fear of what this thing can do to your device. it opens your search bar and gets your address, it changes the volume of your device, this program has gone rogue and has no limits. the concept for this works better if you keep it contained in a computer, not acting like it's behind you.

a different way of executing this game could've been going down the route Doki Doki Literature Club took (which i'd say has a fairly similar idea as this game)
make kinitoPET act like an actual virtual companion up until it invites you to the friendship club - then kinitoPET should gradually start becoming more and more intense, affecting your computer much more. im not scared of this funky little axolotl being at my window, i'm scared about what this he can do with my address, what he could do to my private information

the scare with the camera - although it didn't work since i had no camera - is an amazing idea and should've been expanded upon
i'd be startled if i opened up a webpage, and in the corner, my face was there
act like a virus, not a real life threat. kinitoPET isn't here to be friends with me, it's here to attempt to destroy my device. the gameplay would feel much more tenser and more interesting if act 3 was dedicated to you trying to turn off kinitoPET.
of course, i don't actually want a virus, this would all be self-contained, and it shouldn't be that you fail the game and ruh roh! looks like you have to put up with spooky pop-ups constantly until you can finally get rid of kinitoPET.
i just feel that if you kept the horror purely digital, and strayed away from "I'M BEHIND YOU!!! I'M GOING TO GET YOU!!!" the story would be much stronger and more engaging

regardless, i still really enjoyed this game, and really hope that more games have similar ideas to this in the future\t\t\t\t\t
