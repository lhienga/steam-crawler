This game is very linear, and not in a good way. the most amount of interaction you have is clicking and walking around, its more of a visual novel than anything.
The price is eh, For the length and amount of content you get, you should just wait for it to go on sale.
I was able to beat the game in almost 2 hours, and like i said before, the most you'll be doing is clicking.
Personally i didn't find the game all that scary, most of the scary parts was either just the music cutting off, kinito saying something scary, or some sort of jumpscares.
Overall i think the game could be better, the amount of content isn't just worth it for full price.
Like i said before, just watch your favorite youtubers play through this game, since you aren't gonna miss much by not playing it yourself.
