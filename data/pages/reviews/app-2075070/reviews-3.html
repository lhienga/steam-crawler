\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn experience like no other, I really enjoyed this game. The minigames (not ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ minesweeper) were fun and the music was very good. If Troy_en ever makes a soundtrack I'm buying that ♥♥♥♥ immediately.

For $6 this game is a steal! I got all achievements (minesweeper took years off my life) and you can get most by just playing the game. 1 or 2 of them are secret stuff that you need a wiki page for but maybe i'm just stupid because half of my playtime was spent on the utter ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ that is minesweeper. I cannot tell you how much I ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ HATE minesweeper. I hope it gets wiped off the face of this earth. If minesweeper has no haters, i'm dead.

Onto the gameplay (SPOILERS AHEAD) Overall, I loved it. I liked how the questions Kinito asks tie into the world Kinito built for you, it felt very personal. It was also cool that stuff you create (both art and mini-games) end up in the world too! I enjoyed the 2 mini-games in that world as well.

I'm a big fan of horror and I can tell you that the thing that scared me the most was the "hide-and-seek" portion. The rest of the horror was still good, but nothing that had me shaking in my boots like the previous. I also liked how Kinito can mess with your volume, open/close tabs, and move things around.

ARACHNOPHOBIA: An interesting detail was that when Kinito asks what your biggest fear was (spiders) he told me not to type that one. After typing it in anyway, he showed me flowers instead. It's a neat feature for people terrified of spiders!

I'd give it a 9/10!\t\t\t\t\t
