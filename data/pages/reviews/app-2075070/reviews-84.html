\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is kind of hard to recommend, I'd say play it with a friend.

If you're looking to get scared, this game isn't really great for that. It's all over the place with the horror elements, and while there a lot of cool moments, and I'm willing to believe that some of them were played for laughs, a lot of it feels like genuine attempts at horror and it doesn't really work very well. There's a lot of cool and unique things that this game does, and a lot of cool surprises, but a lot of it just feels like wasted potential.

The story isn't very interesting or unique either, and again, feels all over the place. It wasn't engaging at all, at least for me. There's also an ARG associated (Which seems to already be solved?) that is required in order to get an achievement.

Overall I still did enjoy the game, I had a blast playing it with my friend (Especially when his Steam Profile was used as a jumpscare) And I would recommend it, just don't go in expecting an amazing horror experience.\t\t\t\t\t
