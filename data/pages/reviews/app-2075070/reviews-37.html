\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo make a long story short, it's not a BAD game, but there a far better ones to play that do the sentient computer program thing way better.

It's cute! It wasn't truly scary at any point. It doesn't have much actual game play, just a lot of following commands. It was fun but not very impactful. The "story" moves by too fast and falls flat at the end. [SPOILERS after this point] A lot of scary imagery but it never actually leads to anything. It seems like they were going for a haunted game thing(with the messages and the body bag/organs) but it just does't become anything. There might be a story if you dig for it, but all the creepy imagery and mini games don't seem to lead to anything. If you choose to stay the game just, ends. Kinito never feels like a threat cause you can just ignore him and leave at any point. they are supposed to be malware but then makes you a game and wants you to hang out with him forever? I don't understand their motivations. If the end bit when Kinito makes a game for you was longer I really would've loved it! or if they had used your info for anything besides trying to get you to like them. If you want something that is immersive and emotional that breaks the 4th wall, I would recommend Buddy Simulator 1984 for something cute or Inscryption for something spooky.\t\t\t\t\t
