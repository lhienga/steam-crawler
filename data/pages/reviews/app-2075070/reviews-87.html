\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished the game with all achievements, and... It was quite the experience.

Firstly, the 4th-wall breaking elements were implemented pretty well, sometimes too well that there were some things I didn't except to pop up (I won't tell which one exactly to prevent spoilers).

Secondly, the 5.89 € price for ~3-5 hours of gameplay is okay, even though the game could be cheaper (at least it's better than exit 8, which is 4 € for 5 minutes of gameplay...)

Thirdly, the "story" was pretty good, and some creepy parts gave me a good laugh honestly...

So in conclusion, KinitoPET is a pretty good medium-lengthed story game about old desktop pets. Probably try to buy it when it's on sale, but you could say playing this game to it's fullest is like paying for an interactive movie ticket price-wise. (knowing most recent movies are slightly more expensive for a worse quality, heh).

Final note : 7/10\t\t\t\t\t
