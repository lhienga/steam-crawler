\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello everyone!
First i want to thank the devs for making a fun and awesome game. The game has a lot of potential and I look forward to it's future development!

There are a few things i would like to recommend for improvement.
1. There should be an option for bodies to remain permament throughout the whole battle. I understand it would put a strain on performance but there should at least be an option for it. Maybe not the whole size of regiment (225), but maybe 50% of the regiment, because it would add to the horrific feel of a WW1 battlefield.
2. I feel regiments get replenished way to quickly after battle and it happens instantly after battle. Also there should be an option to create new regiment just like there is for tanks and planes. If regiment loses 75%+ of it's men, it should be destroyed and removed from the campaign map, as they would be combat inefective as replenishing that regiment would take much more time.
3. Supply system feels too basic. We should be able to create supply routes like roads or railroads from supply hubs that lead to the front maybe?
4. Maybe introduce some kind of recruitable population pool.
5. Planes are too wonky, bomber sometimes wont bomb their targets. Also planes don't give field of vision which i think they should maybe?
6. Air baloons should reveal bigger area of the map.
7. Regiments should have ''weight'' so that you cant stack infinite number of them on a single tile, but improving supply to that tile should give more ''weight room'' for the tile.
8. Pre battle Siege bombardment should do more damage or it should be a mechanic that you use on the campaign map before launching your attack. It should weaken units morale and leave devastation on tile terrain that would be visible after launching attack on the same tile.

These are some of my suggestions I will add more if it comes to my mind.
I want to say that i really enjoy this game and I hope it will get only better as time goes on. 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't waste your money.

Units are buggy as heck and get paralyzed if trying to take a trench with multiple units already duking it out in melee, and then die because the enemy will fire at them and they just stand there and take it no matter how many time you spam click to get them into another trench.

You can literally spam artillery and win with 0 casualties when attacking and the AI has no clue how to counter even when it has bombers at its disposal.

That reminds me, bombers are buggier than a beehive. After several hours, I still don't know how to position them to actually go after emplacements. The AI can figure it out no problem as when attacking, it will forever go after your machine guns and nothing else.

The game feels very very grindy, which I suppose it was meant to be that way, but I would prefer that if its quite obvious that one side or the other is going to lose, I wish they would just lose faster i.e. lose will faster, or actually lose troops, which brings me to another point...

Neither you or the enemy lose troops. No matter how many companies get destroyed. You can lay waste to tens of thousands of dudes and they never get taken off the map.

Gas is too expensive and you need to spam it in a very large area to be effective or the enemy just runs away and you lose 80 supply on a wasted attack.

Siege artillery is only useful if you can buy enough of them and then at a certain percentage you start getting diminishing returns even when you research the tech that makes them a little bit more effective.

The AI can be stun locked if you stay on the offensive. They will never attack so long as you continue to apply pressure. This applies to the strategic map.

A massive, yet ultimately useless research tab. None of it matters. The only thing you need is to research some supply nodes and stay on the offensive.

The hard decisions and events are pointless and don't have any real ramifications on game play or how the game is affected.

It's expensive for what it is. Depending on the tax rate in your region, you could be paying 38 dollars for a game that's worth about 15 for how lacking in content and creativity it is.

It's poorly optimized and has memory leak issues which cause units to bug out even more and fall under the map or get stuck in trenches and map objects easily. Your computer will have a heart attack trying to keep up with the game for more than an 10 minutes.

No replay ability. Both factions play the same and the tiny bonuses they get to anything don't matter at all. If you play Allies, you know how to play the Germans and vice versa.

Cutting off the British port from the rest of France also does nothing. They still send troops and it's just a big waste of time to take. The strategy I used was just making sure more units couldn't make it to the mainland and no matter how many stacks they had, the AI never attempted a breakout.

Just go for the Victory Point. Really. It's an incredibly boring oversight by the developers. If playing Germans, just push through Rethel straight to Paris. If playing Allies, just go straight to The Victory point through Verdun. You win in less than 4 hours and you can refund the game, which I wish I could do right now.

Multiplayer is just a game to see who can waste more real life time the best.

The only time the AI will do anything right is in the Skirmish mode. It will optimize the crap out of its loadout to the point where it will annihilate you in a steady tide of bodies and arty shells, which...Iguess you're suppose to do the same? It just goes back to my previous point about multiplayer only the AI has no actual life to waste and you do.

Save yourself from all the gray hairs you'll get and ignore this game.\t\t\t\t\t
