\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBLUF: A fun and engaging foray into a horrific war -- 10/10 would von Schliefen again (even if the game started in August).

The dedication to historical accuracy and the generation of "atmosphere" is spot on. Exceptional work from the team on that aspect of the game. Also, the strategic map setup is straightforward and easy to navigate.

I have spent some time in tactical battles with an eye to early reviews and have immediately identified some issues. I offer the following suggestions to improve the game:

- Zooming out further with the mouse wheel would be very valuable to get a better tactical overview of the map. As it stands now, it's too close to the battlefield with the only option of zooming closer.


- Tactical alerts should be more prominent when your units engage. A few times I've scrolled to a battle just to see my units 90% destroyed.

- Consider making binoculars, snipers, and scouts a thing.

- Infantry should have some autonomy. Reacting to contact should make them dive down and return fire, not simply running until the unit evaporates.

- Moving troops into building areas, high vegetation, or trees should immediately impart cover, not just when they're stopped and proned.

- It would help if field mortars had more autonomy. Artillery too. Or at least the option for such. Or perhaps a hotkey to trigger fire missions against enemy infantry concentrations.

- At some point, the enemy should just surrender and the tactical battle end. Fighting to the very last unit is frustrating.

- Consider real-time bonuses to supply points for tactical prowess. Individual units could sustain morale bonuses or deficits based on what they see around them. As it stands now, the companies seem to fight in a vacuum. Watching a company next to yours be totally annihilated in an artillery barrage would probably drop morale significantly, even if one's own company didn't sustain many losses.

- Units should be able to go prone and crawl to a destination.

- FOR THE LOVE OF GOD, MAKE CORPSES, BLOOD, AND DEBRIS PERSISTENT.\t\t\t\t\t

Nobody till today did achieve such great historically accurate and authentic game.
Petroglyph Games once again surprises us with another masterpiece.\t\t\t\t\t

Game mechanics such as National Will, should be tweaked a little bit, since it tends to run out quickly, especially if you're winning Great Victories! left and right, on paper, you can reach Paris/German HQ, but extremely difficult to do so without gaining a National Will victory beforehand, but alas, it's not a big issue. Take the tutorial to learn the game, it's a bit boring, but it teaches you the basics which will carry you in the battlefield!\t\t\t\t\t
