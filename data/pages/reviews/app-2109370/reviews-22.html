\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhenomenal game. The combat has gotten better with recent patches and the troop movements on the battle map have become more fluid than they were a couple weeks ago. Some cosmetic changes have been made. The history is immersive and one of the most compelling parts of the game was that you want to be the malevolent general. There are units with faceless men, eyes shadowed by the brim of their helmets that occupy "Unit Cards" on the battle HUD. Personally, I know a bit about the western front and I wanted to conserve as many mens' lives as I could. Every advancement, technological and in ground, aided me in protecting as many lives as I could. The solemn music, the gray landscape, the drum beats as you begin a new turn. It's a fun game to play, and after a couple games, your learning curve is easy. I recommend completing the tutorial as there are dozens of mechanics within the game itself that, unless pointed out to you, you will not understand what is happening. And always remember..."Dulce et decorum est". Happy hunting.\t\t\t\t\t
