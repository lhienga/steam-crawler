\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was really enjoying this game for the first day, but the amount of frustration caused by absolutely terrible infantry pathfinding can ruin any immersion, fun, and sense of fairness. Troops can be ordered to attack a trench (with space in it mind you) and they'll either get to the trench and run a couple circles around it before entering (while getting massacred) or just not do anything to begin with (also, getting them killed). I've also seen infantry companies get too spread out and end up backtracking in on itself (can also be a death sentence in some cases). The AI also cheats in the campaign, seemingly having an infinite treasury and supply pool with which to attack you with its death stack every turn, while you can barely afford to attack every 3 turns. On the tactical map, the suppression effect is inconsistent, working really well sometimes and other times doing absolutely nothing but giving you false hope that you can move your troops up. Overall just incredibly disappointing because when I started, i thought i had found my new favourite strategy game but its truly an incomplete mess. Wait for patches!!!!!!\t\t\t\t\t
