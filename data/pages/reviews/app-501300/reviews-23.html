\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me tell you a mysterious story. An exactly same cave appears and disappears in 5 different continents and entered people never return, and my ending to this story is that people find that cave on the 6th continent Antarctica and you know what, entered people never return again. How's my story?

The storytelling of this game is amazing. I also love the controls and graphics. However, as a 30 dollar visual novel, you can't just be as much obscure from the beginning to the end and throw an open ending to players so they will explain for you. The motivation for players to play the game through is mostly the questions the game gives from the beginning. With more and more clues and details collected throughout the game the players are supposed to be closer to the truth or the ultimate answer. But this game does not even try to give an answer. It just keeps listing one and another unrelated clue and you are not even allowed to have the ultimate clue. A good answer does not require too much details, but a good story always need it to the most main questions that attract us to play it though. Without an answer or at least implied possible answers, the details and clues are just useless materials.

Everyone can create such stories without an answer like the one I gave. If I put more details to the cave, you probably gonna find an answer for me while I didn't even prepare one for it.\t\t\t\t\t
