\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is an adventure game, a walking simulator at core with excellent script and beautiful narrative from the perspective of Edith Finch, a young woman revisiting her old family home. The inheritance of property also comes with the stories of her deceased family members. It's in our human nature to try and find answers even if we can't explain them.

“If we lived forever, maybe we’d have time to understand things. But as it is, I think the best we can do is try to open our eyes, and appreciate how strange and brief all of this is.''

As we explore the house ,the game builds succesfully a tense and mysterious scenario. It's not clear what causes Finch's family extinction through the years. There is no physical manifestation of a monster or proof of supernatural, but we can't eliminate that assumption. Family curse or just misfortune there is plenty of drama and sad stories discovered through journals or clues left behind.

Why did they build the graveyard before finishing the house? My assumption is they already knew their fate is sealed so they prepared for it.

The game ending left a bad taste for me,because you can't do anything to change the past or make a choice for a different ending. However a dramatic story can provide a excellent foundation for an emotional response from the player ,something to remember and learn lessons about life and fate.

Being pregnant and being aware of what could happen to her,Edith's courage and determination to find inner peace is what i appreciate the most. She got her answers in the end but i don't know if we obtained ours.

Pros:
-attention to detail
-excellent story
-graphics
-music

Cons:
-short
