At the heart of the game is the Finch family, and players explore their colossal and eccentric home, discovering the stories of each family member through a series of interactive vignettes. The narrative is beautifully woven, and the game excels at delivering a profound exploration of life, death, and the interconnectedness of family.

One of the game's strengths lies in its ability to evoke a range of emotions, from joy to sorrow, as players connect with the characters and their stories. The pacing is well-executed, and the game maintains a sense of intrigue, encouraging players to explore the mysteries within the Finch house.

In conclusion, "What Remains of Edith Finch" is a triumph in interactive storytelling. For those who appreciate atmospheric storytelling and unique gameplay mechanics, this title is a must-play.\t\t\t\t\t
