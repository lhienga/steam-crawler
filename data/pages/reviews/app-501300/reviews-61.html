It's literally just beautiful story/visuals and nothing more. Gameplay itself sucks, but given that caveat it's nearly as good as it could be and will probably stay with me.

Ignore negative reviews complaining about supernatural ♥♥♥♥ and curses. There are none. You just play through children's imaginations and people's mental disorders a lot and some players aren't smart enough to distinguish between what actually happened and what some character believed to have happened.

One last, uncommon note. I was looking for something to play with my wife and she got scared and had to leave for a section of it. It wasn't marketed as "scary" and no one in the comments mentioned it, so I thought I'd add that. She felt uneasy most of the time. She has no tolerance at all for horror or even suspense though and I'm completely unaffected, so I really don't know how many ppl would be too afraid to play, but it's definitely too much for some.\t\t\t\t\t
