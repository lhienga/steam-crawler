\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was searching for a short game that my daughter and I could enjoy together. A game that was not fast paced. One in which we could each take turns deciding what to do, perhaps with our choices affecting game play.

What Remains of Edith Finch did not have the game changing decision making that I had hoped, but it wowed us with its visuals and unique ways of presenting a story.

The player explores the family history of the Finch family. Each part of that history can be discovered in any order and at any pace. We were drawn in from the start and found ourselves trying to anticipate how each family member's story would end.

It took us just over two hours to complete. A perfect "movie night" experience with an interactive twist.
