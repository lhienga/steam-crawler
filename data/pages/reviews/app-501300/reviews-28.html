I logged 3 hours in my play-through but had it paused for maybe an hour total, however I didn't hit all of the achievements, so that would probably still bulk it out to about the same.

It's simple but it does a great job connecting you to the characters and wanting to learn more about them. I especially love how Luke's story was portrayed.

The movement felt a little slow, however considering how quickly you can progress through the story, it definitely works out for the better, but something to bear in mind if you're thinking of playing it.

Overall, I'd say this game does a great job of doing what it was aiming to do and I would recommend it to anyone looking for something quick and laid back with a gripping storyline
