\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA nice walking simulator with a pretty awesome story (and more!)

You walk around a house which is very nicely detailed and click on things to hear commentary. Pretty chill. Some of these lead you to being able to relive the past as various family members - some short glimpses into their life (usually their last moments) and they were pretty cool and unique. Pretty creative.

One story involves you being a cat (trying to catch a bird), an owl (swooping down to catch some rabbit for supper), a shark (hunting some juicy seal) and then a sea monster (hunting humans). There were a couple equally good and creative stories like this.

Vegans be warned, I guess? LOL.

I don't know why anyone would cry or call this game depressing though (they must be the sensitive sort) but yeah, some people just don't handle death very well (in the game too - a lot of overreacting). I shake my head.

The game is just over 2 hours long but I enjoyed it and it felt longer than that. I wouldn't pay more than a cup of coffee for it though - around ¬£2-¬£3 is right. It has easy (100%) achievements and trading cards which is a nice bonus.\t\t\t\t\t
