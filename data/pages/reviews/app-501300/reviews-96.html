\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhy do we say, the apple doesn't fall far from the tree? What does "family history" really mean? This is a question I still don't know how to answer after playing this game. From generation to generation, every Finch had a different story. Knowing your family history is like knowing where the tree grows from. We use history to reflect, reflect on the "mistakes" others had made. But what does family history teach us? What is the meaning of life, death, pain, and fears? How do we even reflect on these subjective factors? I guess, understanding the stories of those who are related to you, is like understanding another version of you. Our blood has similar genes, but our stories are so different. Only by understanding others' paths, can we truly see ourselves better.\t\t\t\t\t
