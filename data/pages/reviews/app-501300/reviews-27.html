\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played 'What Remains of Edith Finch' on a rainy afternoon, looking for a relaxing but emotionally touching experience. For me, it was too short for what it contains. Let me explain... Within this 3 hour experience there is one story of a family, and within it more than 10 stories of the individual family members. Some of them are gutwrenching or beautiful, others are generic or surreal. The game itself doesn't give any single story more than ten minutes to shine. While two specific stories, one involving a bath and one involving fish, will stay with me for a long time, there is too much emotional disconnection between the different stories themselves and the main character to put this on the level of some other narrative masterpieces.

If you like the genre, I recommend 'To the Moon', 'Life is Strange', 'Gone Home' and 'Firewatch' all more than this.\t\t\t\t\t
