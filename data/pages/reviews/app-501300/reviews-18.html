I recommend you buy this game on sale


Make no mistake about it this is a walking simulator game and that is all the gameplay you will get so if you don’t like this kind of gameplay, this might not be for you, although I appreciated how even during the narration parts they kept you engaged by making you interact with the game so it felt like there weren't any cutscenes in the game.

The story is good but the way they tell it is masterful and I think the best part of this game is how they chose what to tell you and what not to tell you and when to tell it and leave the rest to your imagination, you might not at first get all of it but it is one of those games when you think about it later or on a second playthrough it makes more sense and you begin to put the pieces of puzzle together and think about how the story was told to you and how things really were.

Visually this is a gorgeous game and I don’t mean it’s just has pretty effects and such, I mean the details they put specially in the house you visit are next level, every room feels different and has a lot of little details, they clearly put a lot of time into it and not only it looks great but it actually makes the story even better because sometimes it feels like you’re there and a part of it.

What a wonderful game this is and although it’s kinda short for 20$ it’s a must buy when it’s on sale.
