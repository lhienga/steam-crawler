\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo not understand appeal. Short walking-simulator with characters that are not in depth, and a story that concludes poorly in a "your imagination as to what happens to the narrator" way. Maybe it was too over-hyped from what was heard from others in spoiler free contexts, but expected there to be more narrative and creative means of expressing that, which is why I purchased to begin with. While the graphics design was nice, very pretty art, it was not revolutionary. The story is poor as well, each character only getting a small look at their death and relationships before it ends for them. One character in particular felt like an afterthought, having only been given the title of "missing" in the same tell-don't-show style the rest were presented. Very notable in particular this one, as there were missing posters and such before in scenery, so it was my expectation to have more interest through connection to others in the story, only to be left with no development of character and relations to others. Additionally, the motion sickness for me was worse than Portal, wish I could have altered settings more for this. On a positive note, the voice actors did a good job, no bugs that I saw, easy to follow, and chill. Feel like its more enjoyable if one does not know anything about others' opinions going in, because was expecting something with more substance. All this to say, though, that it was not awful. It is obvious the developers put effort into it, and the commentary on life was one of good merit in addressing different outlooks to the cycle of it and how to cope. However, it is certainly not worth the price, nor was it worth the overwhelming positiveness in its reviews. Just want to state this dissenting opinion for others who have been led astray by the hype. Its okay, but not great either. Buy on sale, or better yet, watch for free on YouTube. Same experience.\t\t\t\t\t
