\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who spent most of my life in western Washington state and spent all of my childhood there, this game brings so much nostalgia. From the fox glove plants that you see, to the kid pool with the fish on it, the camera thing where you click it and it turns and you can see a whole story through it, to the wind up frog and old bathtub. The nostalgia hits hard. And the atmosphere of it being mostly dark and dreary, that is what Washington is so much of the time. This game is dark, but wonderful. It encapsulates the depression that many people who live there feel. Sadly its dark and rainy there most of the year, so depression is a very common thing there. This is the best game I have played in a long time, and the only game that I have played that game me so much nostalgia. If you wanted someone to feel the atmosphere and see the scenery that Washington state has, this game has it.\t\t\t\t\t
