\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is a touching tragical surreal story about one very strange family dynasty, which, they say, has a family curse hanging over it.

The game is made with great attention to details in location decoration: there are a lot of unique, interesting things, constructions in the secluded family mansion and around it. Graphics, composition, color palette, even fonts with their disappearance animations, music combined are adorable. Sometimes the game feels like you're inside a revived painting: still life, scenery, etc. Good looking, fancy, but dusty, a long ago abandoned interiors and exteriors help to maintain story's sad, but at the same time to a certain degree warm mood.

The plot doesn't make much sense (self-fulfilling prophecy with a touch of madness), but it creates an urge to explore family secrets, relive family members' stories, it makes to feel melancholy, nostalgia by Edith's childhood, by her deseased relatives.\t\t\t\t\t
