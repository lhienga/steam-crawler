\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first I was considering not getting this game at all. The price tag seemed very high and there wasn't too much extra content to offer.

After getting it, I was pleasantly surprised that it has dedicated server and crossplay. Played through the OC1 campaign and it worked nicely. Lag was way lower than in OC2, even though we had EU/NA connection. There were a few issues here and there, but nothing major, I assume they will be looked into soon.

Controller worked for me immediatelly, without any custom settings or anything. If controller doesn't work for you, first check your steam controller settings, it fixed it for many players. Devs are also aware of the issues with some controllers and working on a fix, so if it's still broken for you, try again in a few days. You can also play on keyboard.

If you're just looking to play OC2 levels with the same group of friends, there's probably not enough content for you to justify the price. However if you enjoyed OC1 and want to play it online, or had connectivity issues when playing across continents, I'd definitely give this game a try. There will be more content updates in the future as well.

I just wish this game had a level editor :)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy score: (8/10, due to some issues in general that can be hopefully fixed)

FYI: A friend and I found that to help with the controller issue (at least with using Xbox) plug it in before you start the game and when it prompts to either Press A or Spacebar, Press A on the controller to ensure you are playing with a controller.

As someone who has played both the first and second games, I still ended up getting this because of cross-platform multiplayer, access to all the content from both games in one package, and online multiplayer for the first game. Sure it still has bugs and some other weird issues, but besides that this is a good value if you have never played either any of the Overcooked games, never played any of the DLCS, or for any other reasons.

Edit: Now that the game has been out for a while, I feel that there are still some issues with performance. I usually run the game on lower graphics quality, however it uses a lot of graphical power where most of my GPU usage always with this game will be almost 100% (maybe its just me). I feel like this game isn't even that demanding specs-wise but it really takes a lot out of my 1060.\t\t\t\t\t
