The graphics are super cute and the gameplay is relatively simple, but don't be fooled; some of the harder levels will definitely get your heart racing. If you're the kind of person who likes to 3 star everything, it's got good replayability. Even if you aren't though, there's plenty of content to go through once. Really the only thing I don't like about the game is the story. I didn't feel it added anything worthwhile, but the game will keep forcing you through it in order to progress. That's minor though, and the gameplay more than makes up for it.

Overall, it's a good one to have in your arsenal, and keys can be found for like $9. It's definitely worth it!\t\t\t\t\t
