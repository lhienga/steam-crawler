\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game at half price during Team17's publisher sale. Before this purchase, I owned Overcooked! 2 without any DLCs on the Nintendo Switch.

This game is great fun with friends, definitely, just like the other Overcooked! games. The assist mode is a very welcome addition, especially because not all of my friends game a lot or are familiar with controller games. It prevents everyone from getting frustrated on a nice afternoon. It also makes playing with younger kids much easier, as the game gets much harder further into the story. I would recommend getting AYCE to anyone who hasn't previously purchased one of the games and want to have some (chaotic) fun with friends.

I personally don't regret buying AYCE on Steam mostly because OC!2 on my switch can serve as a more portable method of playing as I could bring it to someone else's, and for the price of the original Overcooked! plus a bit more, I get to play the original AND all DLCs (in addition to the OC!2 I already have) on my PC, which I think is better than on a switch. However, if it wasn't on sale, I might be a bit more hesitant when it comes to this purchase.

I probably will not recommend this game, especially when bought at full price, to someone who already has at least one previous game (OC!1 or OC!2) and on the same platform. Unless you intend to play with someone online and cross-platform often, or just want to collect them all, you could probably just stick to what you have right now, especially if you've bought DLCs before.\t\t\t\t\t
