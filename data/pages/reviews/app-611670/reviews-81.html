\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I have to preface this review with one thing: without mods, this is not worth buying, especially not at $60. I got this game at $15 on sale with the full intent on modding it. This is because the only thing you actually get with the vanilla game is standard male nord no customization in a still very buggy Skyrim mess. Now onto my review with mods.

With mods, this game is more than salvagable. I am talking character creation, visible body, being able to physically grab things, weapon throwing, etc. Most mods for Skyrim SE are compatible with this, either by default or with mod patches. Weapon play feels great other than bows which still suffer from odd controls with or without mods.

Now, I know most people would probably say that rating a game based on a non-vanilla experience is wrong, but I don't feel like this game should be passed on if you have the patience to mod and troubleshoot, I just don't believe the game is anywhere near worth full price. If you can, try to get this game on a QuakeCon sale when it is 50%+ off.\t\t\t\t\t
