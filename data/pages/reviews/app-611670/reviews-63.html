Get your Nexus mods account updated because you are about to get tweeeeeaked!

This is absolutely incredible! The feeling of life in this game I was not expecting. The visuals were just something you cannot describe without seeing it. I have played Skyrim and all DLC countless times, and here i have already put 96 more hours into just the VR version. I anticipate many more. The immersion of sneaking (turn on real sneaking in VR options) and stealth backstabs/archery is just insane. My first cave i was sneaking up to a set of stairs. A skeleton was walking down them and I let loose my arrow. The skeleton fell apart and i could hear each bone tumbling down the stairs. I almost cried! It was so good! Its a million more moments like this where you feel so connected to the game in a way you had not known you were missing in the non VR game.

Now that being said there are still some things... all the time you walk into a room and random items fly around like a poltergeist just got its first set of feels. Sometimes random doorways, and especially rotating gates in some dungeons, wont let you pass and you have to finger bang the thumb-pads to get past. Bridges sometimes do not let you land on them if you had to lower them into place. I did find a save and reload on that often fixed it, but it was still annoying. Once in a while you would hit a mob with an arrow and it would fly into the sky giant style. All of that stuff didnt subtract from the game enough to make me quit.

Would i recommend you pay full price? I want to say no but with the mods it really is wonderful. I would pay it. But DEFINITELY get this on sale. Dont hesitate, just do it. And follow the guide. Some of it is outdated and the mods no longer available, but 90% of it is still there, and some of it is to your own taste anyway.

Spend another couple hundred hours in Skyrim with me. You'll be glad you did.\t\t\t\t\t
