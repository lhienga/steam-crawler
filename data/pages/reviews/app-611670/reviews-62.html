\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI feel i should edit this review to actually give some REVIEW to it.

If you want this to be a great product straight out of the box, DO NOT get this. It's horrible on its own. Think Payday 2 vr melee combat, but even worse.

HOWEVER

If you are willing to mod/learn modding, this game can steal hours, no, weeks of your life from how much you want to play it.

Melee combat:
Honestly, this is the weakest part of the game. Your swords phase through enemies, you can bypass the swing speed by just swinging as fast as you can irl, and there's no epic finishers. The first and third issue can be fixed by installing the PLANCK, and the HIGGS mods. These give you actual physical presence in the game, allowing you to grab items and people in-game, allowing you to physically drink potions, eat food, throw garbage at people, and hold someone up by the neck before jamming your sword in their stomach. 5/10 system

Archery:
I hope you're not like me, your dominant hand is opposite of your dominant eye. I'm left handed, but right eye dominant, so i have to fire a bow left handed, which i'm not use to since with bows i actually am right handed. If you don't have that issue, congratulations, you have a fairly good bow system to work with. If you don't like the prospect of arrows magically reappearing in your hand after firing, there's a mod called Simple Realistic Archery that makes it so you pull arrows out of an invisible quiver behind your shoulder. solid 7/10

Magic:
This is the game's bread and butter here. This is about exactly what you'd expect. Magic spell in either hand, you can actually blast magic in either direction, and the aiming isn't that bad. It might be a mod elsewhere, but i'm fairly sure you can adjust WHERE the magic comers out of. by default it comes out of your fingertips, but if you're like me and want to blast fireballs out of your palms like a wizard, it's an option. no mods are REQUIRED for this, but there's some good ones out there that fix dual casting and the magelight being in your face all the time. 8/10

Immersion:
VRIK mod, enough said. There's a ♥♥♥♥ ton of mods out there that help this too, just visit the modding hub on reddit. great place to start\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter installing VIRK, HIGGS, PLANCK, and their dependencies, this game is one of the best I've ever played. First, I strap on some 5 lb ankle weights (why in a second), then I start up VRocker and configure it (found on Steam and allows you to naturally move), then I start up the game through the sksevr file, and finally enter the game. You can do all of this from inside the headset, by the way, using the virtual desktop (aside from the ankle weights). Time from initial setup to being in the game takes less than five minutes for me at this point.

Tip: you'll want to copy the sksevr.exe file to your desktop (I prefer directly on the taskbar, as it's right there). Then just start the virtual desktop from inside the headset, click on the .exe file, and you're in the game!

I also have several texture and weather mods installed: Onyx VR Weathers, HD Skyrim Overhaul, and Noble Skyrim. Yeah, HDSO and NS overlap a lot, but it doesn't hurt to have them both and they make the game look fantastic and very real. Seriously, the game looks like ♥♥♥♥ without texture mods installed to make it look like Skyrim SE. For some reason, they decided to use Skyrim LE as the base for VR, and it looks really bad.

Now, the game. Rule number one: Avoid close combat with spiders. AVOID CLOSE COMBAT WITH SPIDERS. They are horrifying in VR and always make me run in the opposite direction (literally with VRocker active). Fortunately, if you're a decent shot with a bow (and you'll become one, trust me), they can be easily dispatched from a distance. On the other hand, physical combat is really fun with VIRK, HIGGS, and PLANCK installed, and I find myself reaching for my sword more than my bow these days. Encountering bandits on the road now feels like a genuine threat, especially at a low level.

With mods (and a few outside extras listed above), Skyrim VR is simply incredible. If only I could import my Skyrim LE save file to it (never played Special Edition).\t\t\t\t\t
