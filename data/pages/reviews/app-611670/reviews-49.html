\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are willing to pay like 4 pounds for 1 month of nexusmods premium and you get a modpack installer like Wabbajack, then after downloading-installing you will have the best VR game yet. Fact. The amount of content is not really comprehensible. Don't learn modding from 0, honestly just use the modpacks/modlists with downloaders as people dedicating thousands of hours made them, why would you start from nothing?

The base game is literally unplayable, modpacks are fun however, sometimes crash, but they provide the best VR experience yet. I have above 2500 hours in steamVR, played pretty much every single main title (even some Oculus only ones), but nothing compares to the depths this one has.

The community is really helpful, any issue I had was solved for me on the mod's discord server within minutes, but you need to put in some time to understand things. Even if you never played a single Elder Scroll game, this would be a blast if you soldier through the first 3-4 hours when you learn the basics of modding, using the mods and the game mechanics.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's Skyrim in VR, read a Skyrim review if you want an understanding of how good the story, open world, systems and such are outside of VR. Due this being a VR port, I will review its VR implementation, and I will say it's raw to start out with its interactive ability. You cannot see your body, you cannot grab anything, etc. making this base VR game, as others have said, feel like an afterthought. However, if you put some time into modding the game with three main mods : VRIK, HIGGS, and PLANCK, your experience will be enhanced dramatically and make this an absolutely fantastic experience. As of course you are modding the game, achievements get disabled, however you can mod the game further to re-enable them as I have done. I believe that at the full price of $60 it is not justified to buy this when so many other great Titles such as Blade and Sorcery out there to play instead. Although the price is steep, waiting for a sale of 75% off as I did and spending just $15, My money has been very well spent, and I can actively recommend the game to others who may be inclined to play it.\t\t\t\t\t
