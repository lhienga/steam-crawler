So you may have heard people talk about how bad the vanilla version of this game is.

Some people exaggerate about that.

The vanilla version of this is a decent experience, although not a very immersive one. But when you mod this game with certain mods, it is a very immersive experience.

It's a bit expensive though, wait for a sale or buy it if you have spare cash.

Here is a list of the mods I would recommend:

VRIK (Adds a player body)
HIGGS (Allows your hands to interact with objects)
PLANCK (You can grab NPCS and Weapons hit them instead of going through them)
Conduit (You can infuse weapons with your currently equipped spell)
SKSEVR (You NEED this for VRIK and HIGGS to work)

How do you install these you may ask?

Go to nexusmods.com and download Vortex, it is a mod handler that makes modding Skyrim Vr a LOT easier.
After that, install any other mods you want!
