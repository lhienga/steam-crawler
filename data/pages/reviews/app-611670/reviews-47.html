\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm going to preface this by saying that the community fixed this game for the developers, just like the flat screen skyrim. There are mods that fix everything that I've listed below and add even more to the game. However, this review is for the base game, not the hard work of the community.

Alpha skyrim graphics, lots of stuttering and jitters, several bugs. Heck, the amount of bugs and glitchesI saw before I even left Helgen is astounding. My favorite was when (spoilers) ralof asks me to stay close as the dragon is attacking the town and then teleports somewhere else literally as soon as he finishes his sentence. He teleported a few times before and after that as well.

There are no VR elements aside from the ability to freely swing your sword. You can pick things up, but they'll be floating next to your hand, just like the non vr game. Lockpicking is done with the analog sticks, just like the console controller.

The visuals are horrendous, that includes EVERYTHING, especially textures except for maybe water in some places. The textures are especially bad. They look like they're from a ps2 game.

Controller mapping on the quest 2 is also awful, makes no sense at all.

The ONLY redeeming quality of the base vr game is that it's skyrim, literally everything about this is awful.

TL:DR: BAD. Literally everything bad. But very good with mods. Mods fix all, and make better. With mods game good.
