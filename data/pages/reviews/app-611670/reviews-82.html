\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery bad VR port made probably in less then a weak by 2 temps at Bethesda...
There is only a handful of ingame functions that has been VRized, and half of them are made very poorly.

With that said if you are willing to spend about 15 hours modding the game, then this is, at least for me, the best VR experience you can get at the moment. Mods add everything Bethesda didn't bother to add, full VR body, picking up things with hands, holsters on body to switch weapons without menus, gestures to switch spells, everything should already be in the base game.

It is a shame that Bethesda gets all the money while the modders did 90% of the work. They didn't even bothered to patch the game for newer controllers. You have to go to steam community and select bind preset for your controller to even play the game.
