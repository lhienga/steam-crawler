\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSimply one of the best VR experiences I had so far (and yes I played HL:Alyx).
Works flawlessly on my Valve Index (just wanted to mention it as I saw many reviews saying the opposite).
If you put some time in modding (took me around 1 hour), the visuals are stunning! Diving this deep in a fully designed fantasy world is breathtaking. There were already so many situations I had to sit down to enjoy the moment because of the beautiful view in combination with the typical Skyrim music.

Melee combat is not the best BUT the sneaky archer on a high difficulty feels so damn good in VR!
If you have a problem with spiders make sure to install a mod that replaces them with some other animals. I like spiders in general but screamed like a little girl when the first giant frostbite spider jumped on me during my nightly walk to Ustengrav.

I played a lot VR games so far but Skyrim is the only one that kept me hooked for more than a couple of hours. The whole concept of following the story, level up your char, do side quests and dungeons works even better in VR and is a nice escape after a stressful day.
I think one of the main reasons is the fact that it tickles a lot of emotions. There are calm moments where you just travel trough the beautiful world, badass situations when I FUS-RO-DAH an enemy off a mountain, a lot of exploring vibes (especially in the dungeons) and a constant feeling of progress when getting new items/ level-ups etc.

Yes it could have been better (melee combat, vanilla graphics) but for me these are peanuts compared to the extraordinary fantasy setting I can get lost for hours.
Thanks Bethesda (and the mod community) for so many hours of diving in a beautiful fanatsy world and many more to come!\t\t\t\t\t
