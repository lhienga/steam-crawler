Test to see which mod is crashing you or B. Restart the entire list SO ALWAYS keep track of what mods you download when and only download a select few at a time so that way you can seed out the ones that break the game easier. You will probably never stop modding this game I have about 100 but there's people with over 400 and even then if you reach that point you may still switch things up and replace a few or even add more. Me personally it's been about a day and a half of constant modding and testing and I am getting to a point where the game is beautiful and has all the VR essentials so it may take less then 2 days but 2 days is about average modding time I'd say. You can even create a mod collection idk if you can set those to private but what I do is just write them down and back them up so I don't have to wait so long to download it all again. Now one more thing I HIGHLY RECOMMEND it's called cherry picking look for high endorsed collections and sort through the mods find which ones you like don't download them all just get the ones that seem appealing to you. A GREAT START IS this guy named Habbie you can search Habbie Skyrim VR online find his most recent video go to the description and download all the mods he has because he has the bare minimum mods to make it actually playable then branch out and install your own mods. Gameplay review (WITH MODS):

The game is awesome I mean getting to slice my enemies with a heavy 2 handed axe pretty sweet. The things you can do to NPCS is quite immersive there is tons of roleplaying options. For example you could be a cannibal or a werewolf and just eat people and actually bend down to eat them or you could sneak into people's houses and hold knifes to their throats and kill them that way or be a vampire and actually sink your teeth in sucking the blood from them. And without mods the graphics are good but with the graphical mod lineup I have stunning when I'm tired and sweaty from walking around I sit down and enjoy the night sky look at all the stars and the moon. Maybe I look to the mountains in the distant see how they howl. Sometimes I will travel through a harsh blizzard in search of a way out or shelter. Or a dark cavern full of mysteries and dangerous traps. I can go assassins creed style and kill a villager pick their body up hoist it over my shoulder and drop them in some shrubs. The possibilities are endless for RP you can be whatever you want or do whatever you want. I love it stunning game and a wonderful VR experience (IF MODDED) and like I said in the disclaimer you gotta be ready to heavily mod it or you will be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
