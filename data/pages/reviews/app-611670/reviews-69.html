Even with mods, this game is such a technical chore to play, the bugs are relentless, even from the "essential" mods themselves. Using VRICK, HIGGS, and PLANCK, I ran into the following:

-Mod installation errors that you can apparently just ignore (for some reason the game/mod manager has a hard time telling the difference between sksevr and regular skse; a fourth essential mod that isn't mentioned you need for other mods to work)

-The horse carriage intro will be (hilariously) buggy AF and make you think you did something wrong. You didn't. It's just normal and nobody mentions it.

It really is a chore to play all around. I only recommend it if you have the time and passion to do so.\t\t\t\t\t
