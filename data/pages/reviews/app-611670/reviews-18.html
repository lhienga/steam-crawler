Skyrim just isnt a vr game in my opinion. Even with a ton of mods there are too many menus, too much jank, and i find im swinging wildly when fighting enemies. My issue is skyrim is at least a 100 hour game, and the novelty of vr starts wearing off pretty quickly.\t\t\t\t\t
