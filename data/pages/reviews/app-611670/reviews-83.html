https://docs.google.com/document/d/1qbh7GE30xLyso7MA6u3xpNJNdL5r2V2WP8SjZ4VpHLQ

Manual movement is best for immersion and roleplaying but after about an hour of standing in manual mode, I nearly blew chunks, so I switched to sitting. (if you play sitting, adjust your player height in game settings) I have a fairly strong constitution and I still feel a little queasy. According to people who actually suffer from motion sickness or vertigo, they couldn't even take 10 minutes in manual mode, they could play longer in teleport mode but still got sick. Taking a Benadryl or a supplement may help. There is also a SteamVR add-on for supported VR games called Natural Locomotion.\t\t\t\t\t
