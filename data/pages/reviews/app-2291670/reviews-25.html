"The Supporter" will add a bit of red to some weapons, most noticable on Driller primaries.
(Adds a red tank to Sludge Pump similar to "Megacorp". Often changes wires to red.)

"Megacorp" "Ghostly Pale" "The Company Special" for a more "Prime" white & gold look.
(Blue from the company special has minimal effect. "Sleek" also works but it has a metallic gray secondary with an unique texture.)

"Fiery" adds a red with an unique chrome texture to most weapons.
(It sometimes has a gray secondary tone similar to "GSG Guardian"s default gray.)

"Primal Blood" is mainly red with a gray secondary and some blueish gray tone.
(Blueish gray is minimal on some weapons)

"Salt Crystal" goes well with most of them.

"Lead Storm" Powered Minigun, almost every paintjob goes well with it.

All is subjective and varies a lot from weapon to weapon but hey.
