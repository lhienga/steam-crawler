\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far the best piece of dlc content GSG has released, period. They took the first dlc, threw it into a bedazzled, diamond-tipped blender, then hand-carved some of the most intricate and absolutely gorgeous cosmetics I have EVER seen in any video game.

The main meat of this dlc is without a doubt the love letter that is the weapon cosmetics. I deadass sat in the Equipment terminal for like 15 minutes just drooling over all of the frameworks. They are ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ amazing. Genuinely just beautiful pieces of art, they belong in a museum. The weapon paintjobs that come with them look great on most weapons. The pickaxe paintjob looks great and even the Bosco paintjob looks awesome.

The armor paintjobs however are quite egregious. They don't fit very well with anything. The fancy marching band hat, like alot of the dlc hats, fits with literally nothing. Without a complementing armor set, I don't see why anyone would want to wear it.

The second best part of this dlc is definitely the Oily Oaf mugs. The previous supporter pack mugs were absolutely abysmal and I hate them to this day. These however fit perfectly and actually improve the mug's design for the better. The mug still looks like it's for the Oily Oaf with the wood lining on the mug and the handle. Especially love the golden GSG logo at the bottom of the mug, that was a pleasant surprise.

This dlc is the right way to do a supporter pack. The previous one just slapped some gold onto everything and called it a day. This one has so much love and care put into every little detail of all the designs of the weapon frameworks, the pathetic hat I'll never use, and even a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ beer mug. I love you GSG, never stop being awesome!
