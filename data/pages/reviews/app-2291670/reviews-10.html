\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow, how fast time flies. It seemed like only yesterday I was buying my first supporter dlc. It took me exactly 3 days, from the moment I bought the main game in 2019, around the time of the anniversary in early access (completely accidentally stumbled upon when browsing the Steam store). Just three words in the title "Danger, Darkness, Dwarfs" sold me on this game! *(laughs)
The game looked almost like a legacy build at launch, except with more fixes, QoL improvements, and weapon models. And I immediately fell in love with it. Not without my love for the dwarfs, but I loved the dreadful atmosphere of the solo missions first and foremost. The game was just as good in co-op, in comparison it even feels like it was harder! And the game had a great community (except for the leaf lovers) and I occasionally like to run missions with the greenbeards!

It's the kind of unique game I buy every dlc for and will continue to do so in the future! You guys deserve it!
Guys at GSG, thank you for a great game and one of the best co-op shooters ever! I can't wait for future seasonal content and of course games from you as a publisher!
