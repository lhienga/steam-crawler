Happy 5th Birthday Deep Rock Galactic
(Give or take a concept project, pre-alpha and the like…)

You've come a long way from the ‘Project Dwarka’ concept.

From losing out in the Community poll to both ‘Project Karoo’ & ‘Project Knoxville’ to surviving the Press Play closure of 2016.

You had a Pre-alpha in 2016, with an unreleased 1st trailer and an amazing jump in development to the official first Pre-Alpha Trailer

⭐Then on the 28 Feb 2018. Early Access launch and the birth of Deep Rock Galactic⭐
When many of us discovered our inner hidden Dwarfs

Since then DRG has never stopped growing & not just the game but the fan base.
”I love you guys”

We've seen New, More and/or Reimagined;
🔸 Bugs both Glyphid’s, and game glitches,
🔹 Fast and effective Game bug fixes,
🔸 Biomes,
🔹 Mission,
🔸 Primary Weapons,
🔹 Secondary Weapons,
🔸 Grenades,

🔹 Deep Dives,
🔸 Infused Matrix Cores,
🔹 Cosmetic Cores,
🔸 The Forge,
🔹 New Machine Events,
🔸 New Assignments,
🔹 New Mutators (Warnings & Anomalies),
🔸 Seasons, with story elements & loads of other stuff

🔹 Weapon skins & Colour palettes,
🔸 Armor skins & Colour palettes,
🔹 Bosco skins & Colour palettes,
🔸 Pickaxe skins & Colour palettes,
🔹 Dwarf cosmetics, (Beard Physics rule)
🔸 A few, then more, then almost too many additional Cosmetics,
🔹 Loads more Voice Lines, “But who cares?”

🔸 Timed / Themed / Seasonal events,
🔹 Upgrades & Addition to the Space Rig, (Minigames, Bar & Beer Overhaul…)
🔸 Loads of miscellaneous (world building) additions,

🔹 Weapon Balances,
🔸 QoL Improvements, (Hud options…)
🔹 Additional Cave Variations,
“This place is almost nice compared to last time”
(I know I'm missing stuff but this list would be huge to do DRG justice)

⭐ And yet, not once have GSG ever asked for our Gold ⭐
🔸 This approach has kept all Dwarfs with the same access to every addition, preventing a split in the player base and a major reason why so many of us get the Cosmetic’s or the Supporters Upgrade’s.
(Even though it was a complete game on launch and not sold as a live service)
🔹 This steady influx of good additional content throughout all aspects is a major factor that has help keep Deep Rock Galactic alive, & not just from the Developers, but also the Community it spawned.

🔸 A Community I am proud to be a member of. 🔹

A community, that amongst so many other things:
🔸 Answered the call to Mine in droves, and stayed,
🔹 Creates Trailers & Recruitment Videos
🔸 Creates amazing Music, then even Remix them,
🔹 Cemented voice lines into history, ”For Rock & Stone”
🔸 Creates Mods,
🔹 Documents DRG History,
🔸 Loved Doretta so much we asked/received the ability to save her,
”No Dwarf left behind”
🔹 Spawned, and funded a DRG Board Game
(I got me the Collector’s Edition within hours, plus some extras) 👍
🔸 But best of all, we mine together vs the horrors of Hoxxes IV,
(There are ‘Co-Op’ games where players get along, then there's DRG, where were practically family)
“Rock and Stone Brothers”

& finally from myself, and many others,

⭐ Thankyou Ghost Ship Games ⭐

The first round of Glyphid Slammers are on me.
Here's to the next 5 Years and many more to one of, if not the Best Co-Op game of all time.
”Last one to finish is a pointed eared leaf lover”

”To Karl”
