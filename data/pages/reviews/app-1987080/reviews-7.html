\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I think this game is better than Escape The Backrooms, the performance of this game is TERRIBLE (Especially in VR)

My GPU is a 4080, my CPU is a 5700X, and my HMD is a Quest 3 (connected via SteamLinkVR on a wifi6 network). On the default resolution scale of SteamVR (150% in my case) the game will run at less than 1 FPS on low graphics with antialiasing, SSR, and Volumetric lights off.

The maximum resolution the game runs at is 1784x1912, a single pixel higher and the game will run at 1 FPS instead of the 72 I have it set to target

The game uses insane amounts of VRAM, so if you are using an older card the game may perform even worse.

Alongside this, the most recent level added to the game WILL NOT LAUNCH IN VR.
The old VR lobby was incredibly disorientating since it locked your view, the new lobby is even worse since it locks your view AND puts you in third person without a theater screen. There are also some points in maps where you will be teleported if you are playing in VR.

The levels themselves are decent (puzzles, environment), but the AI is a little basic and doesn't really pose much of a threat.

I give the game a 5.5/10 overall (3.5/10 for VR), most of this is due to performance issues and bugs (like join in progress not working EVER)
I personally believe that the devs added the workshop feature as an excuse to stop adding new content to the game, so I do not believe any of the issues will ever be addressed.

I would not recommend buying the game as it is today, but if you just want a quick few hours of horror with the homies it's ok, nothing special.\t\t\t\t\t
