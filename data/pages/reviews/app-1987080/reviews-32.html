\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am writing this review to discuss the topic of what game to buy, Inside the Backrooms, or Escape the Backrooms. They are both Backrooms themed escape room games but there are pros and cons to both. This review is coming from someone who has played both games with a friend (no singleplayer experience). If you are looking for a fun backrooms themed escape room game to play with a friend, Inside the backrooms is the game you should buy. Escape the backrooms seems to follow more of the lore of the backrooms while Inside the backrooms seems like an escape room game that is Backrooms themed. In Inside the backrooms each level is carefully crafted with puzzels that interlock with eachother. Escape the backrooms just seems to have an exit somewhere and plop you into a new area. Escape the backrooms has many more levels compared to inside the backrooms, but they can be short (15min). Inside the backrooms has many puzzels to each level and can keep you inside one level for sometimes 2 hours (there are also multiple endings). Inside the backrooms does not have a sanity meter like Escape the backrooms but if anything I think that makes the game better even though it is not as realistic to the actual lore. The walkie talkie situation in Inside the backrooms is a bit annoying so I would recommend using a third-party voice chat app. Overall I think Inside is the better game because it is more catered to the experience of the player (there are emotes) while Escape the backrooms is more trying to imitate the lore of the backrooms (no emotes), but if you have money to spare def buy both. Oh and playing with friends makes both games a blast to play.\t\t\t\t\t
