\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust played this game with a friend and actually had a lot of fun! If you liked Labyrinthine, you would like this game. The puzzles are difficult but very rewarding when you figure them out. If you don't have all the time in the world, I would recommend playing the game on easy. Sometimes there can be some bad RNG where the entities trap you in a corner of the map and end up draining a good amount of your lives on a level that already spent you an hour and a half in. The cons are that there really isn't a way to save your progress in a level from my understanding. You have to finish the level which could take upwards of 2 hours depending on the level, and if you don't finish it, you will have to restart the level in the next session. On the other side, there are some good jump scares and really good level design that makes the game really fun to play with a friend. The game is also replayable, as most of the combinations and clues are slightly different each playthrough. Recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
