The Gameplay:
Play yet another backrooms games with puzzles and stuff to do with friends. The puzzles in this one are often laden with too much pressure that honestly just makes it no longer fun. To that end, while you have a generous amount of health to be able to take hits, the amount of entities that you can encounter is honestly too much and makes the game less scary and more annoying than anything. The tension almost never gets to build up.

The Story:
You are John Backstreet Boy and you get sucked into the backrooms by a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ .jpg, It honestly awful that there is some weird ass entity at all there, it just looks dumb. It would be better if you just stumbled in there or if that scene were done with proper creepy tension instead of whatever that was.

The Visuals:
This is one of the best looking backrooms multiplayer games. Especially in regards to the characters and the way they look wearing the hunchback banana pajama outfit. The animations are still quite jank especially the way your character leans forward with the posture of a drinking bird in stasis.

The Sound:
The voice acting in this game is dog water. It's a level of bad where I feel anyone can do a better job than what was done. I'd offer to do the lines for free if it meant I did not have to hear that dumpster fire of a performance. The ambiance and sfx for the entities is one of the pillars to this game's sound work. Too bad the entitles get spammed so much that the sounds they make actually becomes obnoxious.


The Experience:
After coming off the heals of Escape the Backrooms, I expected that this would be a much more premium experience. While enjoyable, it certainly was not. It scratches an itch, but it also just feels like a chore. Here do a puzzle while I swarm you with enemies you have to run from every 2 seconds. It had a cool/scare factor for the first 5 minutes, then it just became just an annoyance. The party room puzzles were notably, neat in concept but mostly just a frustrating mess. The writing was also bad, and too confidently placed in the game for it to not be issue. Overall the experience was mid, and you would be better off playing a different backrooms game for now. Hopefully development does not stop on this game so I can change my overall recommendation.
