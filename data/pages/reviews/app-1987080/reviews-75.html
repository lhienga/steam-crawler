It has a pretty good shock factor for the most part and has really good jump scares for the most part. This may look like a lazy backrooms game at first but I would definitely say that this has to be one of the best if not the best backroom game out of all of the replicas being made so far. The Models are actually pretty good as the enemies in the sewers and the hotel actually look very well and the Dead Rats have very good movement and animations. The Bad animations I don't really mind because it is a small company and they make updates where they can and the game is still enjoyable.
[Pros & Cons]
Pros
========
-Cheap Price
-Good Puzzles and Pretty Good Game play
-Good Scares
-Very Optimal Settings And Very Little Bugs
-Great Fun With Friends
Cons
========
-Cheap Animations
-Repetitive Sound Effects

In My Opinion this game is worth the 15 hours i had with it and the price tag it has and I believe that it is honestly worth the purchase I had a really fun time with it. 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
