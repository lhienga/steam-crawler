\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInside The Backrooms takes you on a thrilling journey through the eerie layers of the Backrooms. It's a short yet satisfying horror game that captures the essence of the creepypasta lore. But there are a few things we wish we knew before delving into this spine-chilling adventure.

Stamina is a crucial factor, and running around recklessly will quickly drain it, leaving you vulnerable. Respawning is possible, but the door's location changes each time, so don't waste time searching for the same spot. Losing your flashlight means navigating in the dark or finding a replacement, as items don't respawn.

Puzzles are randomly generated, adding replayability and keeping you on your toes. Entities in the game may not follow the exact rules from the lore, so don't rely solely on your prior knowledge. The game's order of levels also deviates from the canon, offering a fresh experience. Beware of the birthday party section, as it presents the toughest challenge in the game by far.

Lastly, while the ending may not be as satisfying as the rest of the game, it's still a worthwhile journey.

Inside The Backrooms is a game that creates tension and offers spooky thrills, but be aware the game as of this review is prone to crashes and glitches. While not present on my end, it was apparent and fairly noticable for a few of my friends. But for $7, I cant really complain.\t\t\t\t\t
