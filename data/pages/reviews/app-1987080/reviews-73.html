This is because in the game's current state,

To start: The game's servers are basically dead.
There are no where near enough public lobbies that are actually public lobbies, and not "I forgot to set it too private" lobbies.

Sometimes joining a lobby would just softlock the game,
and if that wasn't bad enough:
I Don't want to install this game again because it has shown that it can be harmful to my PC.
Leaving a lobby I, myself, created, somehow crashed everything that was connected to my PC. Monitor, Keyboard, Mouse, headphones. As soon as I clicked that exit button, everything just flat out died, just from leaving a lobby, not going out of my way to break the game, just LEAVING A LOBBY.
