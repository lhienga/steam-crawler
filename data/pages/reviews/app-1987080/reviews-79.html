\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy group of friends usually play overwatch, but we eagerly wanted some other coop games that we can have fun and get scared (me) and not be mad at random teammates. And since this was cheap and early access, also watched some youtube videos of earlier levels and one of my friends got the game, we decided to try it!
The levels were big, with numerous puzzles that are connected to each other, a monster or two that dominates each level that we try to avoid. It's scary and I'm a scary cat, but I'm super excited to play this with my friends as they carry me and I focus on the puzzle solving XD. I like that vibe and types of puzzles in it, it's bad for someone who can't remember the map, but I can follow my friends so that's ok.
The one only complain rn is when we left the game midway (during sewers level) and went to eat dinner, the game froze for one of us, so we had to restart the whole level, but it wasn't too hard to catch up on the progress so wasn't too bad. Not sure if it's a steam issue or game but that's the only occurance so far. :)

It's kinda funny to say that the birthday room was one of the scariest experience but somehow I loved it even though it's scary and the idea is soo new and vibrant. I actually tough up and run around the corners to find the boxes since instead of hiding, it's better to find the boxes and pass each level.
There are some glitches like the rats try to chase a friend in the sewer and a group of them got stuck in the corner, which we happily enjoyed so we can run around to find clues when we get stuck XDD so we don't mind if you don't fix that haha.
I was also surprised that the birthday room level had multiple exits?? I still haven't tried the second way that I saw on youtube video, unless it's changed that you have to play birthday room?
We waited for long for the new level so we're super excited that level 5 is out and we'll try it soon! While waiting we got the labyrinthine and escape the backrooms. They were somewhat different, but I think we all enjoyed this game the most in terms of the map size (not too much tedious walking or trying to find the right path), puzzle types and solvability, monsters and how to dodge them, we enjoyed this a lot! Also, we don't feel dizzy after playing for many hours straight, since it's a really big bummer for some games. Please lemme know if you have any questions concerns! :))\t\t\t\t\t
