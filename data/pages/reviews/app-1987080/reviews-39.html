\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy first backrooms game I've played, it's pretty good for a horror game, especially with friends.

However it can be very tedious if you're not sure what to do, as you need to look for items that can be hard to spot. Puzzles are also randomized, meaning you can't watch a video or look up the guide to get all the immediate answers you need. Example, if you need to enter a password for something, it will always be different each time you play.

Also, entities (or just one) spawn randomly, which can be a pain if you're trying to do something and they keep chasing you causing you to run or hide constantly.

Despite the possible time consuming gameplay, I still enjoyed almost every second of it.

For $7 it's worth it's price imo with updates still rolling out. Overall, 7.5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
