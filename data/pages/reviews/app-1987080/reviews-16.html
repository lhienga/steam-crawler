\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's definitely more good than it is bad, it has real problems. It is very spooky and there are no proper jumpscares because the monsters roam around or appear randomly, they just make you jump which is what a horror game should be.
If you have 2-4 friends to play this with I fully recommend it, but only with friends.


The biggest issue is the inventory; it's so extremely tedious to equip anything from your inventory, the only things that have a keybind are your flashlight and radio. Everything else, you have to press I, scroll down if you have a lot of items, click it, click use and if you unequip it or pull out your light or radio, you have to do that all again. The game gives you so many items to use like snowballs, spray paint, breadcrumbs and even a motion sensor but it's just such a pain to use any of them it's not even worth it.
Why there isn't just a toolbelt that is bound to 1/2/3/4 that you put items in I don't know but it's really annoying.

Another issue is specifically with the party level(I don't know what level# it is but it's after the parking area). Maybe it was an issue with my system or settings or maybe it is part of the game but that level is so stupidly dark I could not see a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ thing. You get the motion sensor before that level and the monsters are all around you so it's incredibly useful to have, but without the flashlight I was completely blind so I had no choice but to not bother with the motion sensor.
Besides not being able to see, the level was just very frustrating in the gift stage.

For some reason every player only has 10 lives, if you die 10 times you are permanently dead and just have to spectate or leave. It never happened to me but it did happen to one of my friends I played with and it seems like a really stupid design choice. Nothing breaks immersion and ruins the enjoyment of a game like not being able to play it or having to quit out, invite everyone back and restart from the beginning of the last level.
Death is really not avoidable, especially on the party level where you just cannot avoid it because there are multiple monsters in extremely tight spaces and absolutely nowhere that is safe to hide.\t\t\t\t\t
