The game is reallyyy good. I love playing it. It is scary at first, but after a while, you will get used to all the dangers and you can improve your skill in this game. It A LOT of fun playing with friends and quite tricky to solve all the riddles. I really like the atmosphere of the game and all the levels, expect the party room , thats also the reason why "only" 4 out of 5 stars are given. I feel like the party room espacially with the labyrinth (maze) is more luck based.. I would wish for an alternative to gain the Red Key Card , as it would be a great opposition to the luck sided gameplay.

The Graphics are great, only thing I would wish for is a FPS cap above 120 ^^.

Great game, definetly recommended!\t\t\t\t\t
