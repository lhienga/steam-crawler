\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the greatest backrooms game of All Time.
I love Inside the Backrooms, it's fun and pretty atmospheric when you're relatively new to the game. The puzzles are extremely fun to go through and figure out (for the most part, there are a few that should be re-examined). I have but only a few complaints. Firstly the monsters. I feel as though some of the monsters (Entity #3 and partygoer) are kinda lame and hard to counter.
To go in more depth; Entity 3 (creepy face) feels like it has little to no counter. It can spawn on your face and have a chance to kill you even though you didn't do anything. Also doesn't go away even if it has killed someone. As for partygoer, I have a huge problem with both the fun room and the creature. The creature in the first section is the worst part due to the fact that it will freeze you in the claustrophobia-inducing maze. It makes it feel crappy, even if you're about to die anyway. I feel as though the whole level needs a complete revamp on how it works. Or make it optional.
Ignoring all of this; I would still absolutely recommend this to anyone who has an interest in backrooms related stuff or horror puzzle games in general.
7/10 would play again.\t\t\t\t\t
