\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started the game and already was too scared to play alone. So a friend joined and together it was hella fun! I love the combination of puzzle and horror and the feeling of being lost with the beginning of each new level. To find out what kind of monsters might be there and how they react on movement, sound or brightness, etc. was thrilling.

In contrast to what some other people said I did really enjoy the sewer level. It was way more relaxed than the ones before, so that one had enough time to search for items and figure out how the puzzles work.

Even though, I have to admit, that we restarted the game in easy mode very early. We just didn't want to be game over when being close to the end of a level. But I guess for gamers seeking a challenge, the normal or hard mode might be interesting.

The hotel level was my favorite so far! Please give us more of that! Loved the puzzles and the enemies were fun. Got caught by some pretty good jump scares. I liked that one was exploring different puzzles at the same time, so that there was always something to do and something to find out without the game feeling streamlined.

The only part that I found really annoying was the birthday party. If I'd have played alone that would have been the part where I'd have stopped playing the game. But with two people it is absolutely doable, just takes some time.\t\t\t\t\t
