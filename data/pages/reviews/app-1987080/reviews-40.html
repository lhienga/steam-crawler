\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDO NOT PURCHASE, EVEN IF IT'S FREE.
This pathetic debacle of a game is the most piss poor take on the backrooms that I've ever had the displeasure of running on my computer. The sewer and grassroom areas I thought would be a fresh new take on the game since there was an update and I'd attempted to play it twice with my friends already and despised the way I felt afterwards. I was hopeful and left with extreme contempt.

The puzzles are grueling and exhausting to play through, the steps to complete each chapter were tedious and unforgiving, and after the first 20 minutes of playing it feels like you've been playing for a hour. Playing the game made me wonder if the developers played it themselves before subjecting people to this reprehensible product. The stamina system is flawed and requires entirely too much time to refill if you're being chased by an enemy. It takes less time to cook a hot pocket in the microwave. Upon dying you need to find a door to respawn but the sound indicators such as the heartbeat and angelic harmony bug out and don't occur. Also the trail of light is very obscure and is seldom an accurate path to follow to reach your door. Often times I would respawn and be insta-killed because the enemy that killed me was still stomping around on my body to revel at what they'd accomplished (though it didn't take very much).

Save your time and money. You'd be doing yourself a service by staying as far away from this game as possible. It's not interesting, captivating, or rewarding.\t\t\t\t\t
