\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would like to provide some feedback on this game based on my experience and the opinions of my friends:

1. Optimization Issues: The game suffers from poor optimization, causing frequent stutters even on hardware that meets the recommended requirements.

2. Graphics: The graphics in the game are subpar and fail to create an immersive experience.

3. Jump Scares: The game's attempts at jump scares are lackluster and fail to deliver the intended thrill.

4. AI: The AI in the game is disappointing, as it can be easily manipulated by breaking line of sight.

5. Puzzles: The puzzles in the game are not necessarily difficult, but rather tedious and lack coherence.

6. Voice Chat (VC) Issues: The voice chat feature in the game does not work properly.

7. Lack of Save Points: Surprisingly, the game lacks save points, which is a significant drawback. In our case, after spending an hour in the first level and dying repeatedly, we discovered that the game did not save our progress. This frustration prompted us to look up a walkthrough, only to realize that we were not even halfway through. This lack of save points was the final straw that led us to abandon the game.

If you're looking for a better Backrooms game experience, I highly recommend playing "Escape The Backrooms" instead. It offers a more satisfying and enjoyable gameplay experience.\t\t\t\t\t
