A COD that could have been great...


Overall : 6/10

This COD is actually a rather good one on the solo side, with a nice and engaging campaign. The multi is really sub-par vs COD 2019, despite having exactly the same engine, but they went really too far for me with the kikoo-LOL skins, removing completely the feeling of a WW2 game.

But the killer for me is the fact that the achievements are completely broken for the ones requiring a count, and the Devs never cared to fix it. So thumb down!

Gameplay : 7/10

The gameplay is a copy paste of COD 2019. It's basically a reskin of the game, into the WW2 era. It's actually good, but there is absolutely no innovation since 2019, they just transposed, with the exception of the order mechanism, taken back from COD WW2.

Graphics : 8/10

The game is very beautiful and the environments are generally very well done, with beautiful lighting effects and superb FX.

Story : 5/10

The story itself is trying to tell the tale of some SOE operators in the late days of WW2, with some flashbacks for each protagonist. It's simple, efficient but not very original

Achievements : 0/10

For the hunters only. This game is unfortunately broken for many achievements requiring a count (kill x while using a capacity, etc). Hence the 0 grade. The zombie ones were relatively OK, except Grim Reaper which was a drag. On the multi (shared with zombie), the operator level up will be the most time consuming. But nothing complicated, just a grind. And this bomber achievement was a real PAIN to get. But keep at it and you'll get it.\t\t\t\t\t
