\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo.. After completing the campaign, the game is not as bad as everyone says it is.. but it's not good either. I'd expect that a game which came after Call of Duty Modern Warfare(2019) to look better but the graphics even on 4K Ultra were just atrocious.. It was a disappointment in that regard, not only because some of the face models and textures looked like polished rubber, but also because some text on paper which normally you should be able to read was too blurry to even make out the letters.. There were also a lot of blurry textures during gameplay, for example some rocks you have to climb over look like they're from the Call of Duty 2 era.. Blurry and untextured. That's not what you'd expect in a new game from a big developer like this. It just feels rushed in terms of textures. Also the cutscenes running in 15-20fps was a bummer. The combat was somewhat entertaining, although there's no explaining how most enemies take a headshot and just shrug it off, and you gotta shoot them again to kill them(yes, those without helmets). I'd say the only good thing that I really liked in this game was Lady Death(Lady Nightingale), Polina Petrova, the sniper. Her actress did a great job. Her story was awesome. Should you buy this game? If it's on sale, then yes. Is it worth playing? The story is good, no denying that, so I'd say yeah, but only if it's on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
