Have you ever bought something you knew was bad just to see how bad it is?

That's what happened when I saw this game was on sale. I knew this game was one of the worst Call Of Duty titles in recent years so I thought, eh how bad could it be?

One of the few positives is this game doesn't take 70% of my hard drive. But that's also a very big negative because there is no content on this game. Black Ops Cold War came out a year before this and has a great Campaign, decent Multiplayer + Big Team Battle, 5 Zombies maps, Outbreak (DMZ but with Zombies), Dead Ops Arcade, and Containment mode. This game has a bad campaign, a empty multiplayer, and 4 maps of zombies.

Though Call Of Duty vanguard isn't a broken messy experience it is certainly a lifeless. Things that make call of duty fun are just missing from this game. when you impressive things like get a triple kill or a headshot, in other games a medal would flash on the screen like "YOU JUST DID THAT" and it feels satisfying and rewarding, in Vanguard, nothing you get nothing.

The gun-play is pretty decent, though once more that's not saying much because its literally MW19's movement system. The only fun you can have with guns is abusing the attachment system to make some cursed creations.

I will give them credit though, they didn't ♥♥♥♥ up Shipment like MW22 did. But thats not a lot of credit.

I didn't even bother to play the campaign I knew from game-play footage that it sucked and wasn't worth my time. Just save your hard-drive and deselect campaign in the "Manage my DLC" button

Zombies is okay on the later maps but like I said its missing a sense of life/soul. Characters are also always talking in zombies, they never shut up but they never say anything useful either. It's all nonsense. But at least you can kill zombies and that's fun

The game has some really cool bundles and cosmetics but why would I want to buy bundles for a game that sucks? I did already buy the Terminator bundle and the Anime Dubstep gun for Warzone but the Terminator isn't even available for purchase so I can't recommend this game to anybody who missed out\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMany people consider this the WORST call of duty ever made. I certainly understand the problems with it and the reasons for the hate it got/get. But honestly i think this is a pretty decent entry in the franchise. It's definitely toward the bottom of the list, but there are very few cod games i find completely unplayable. There is enjoyment to be had in every cod game except the three first in the franchise for me. Zombies in this one however is complete garbage and probably the worst edition of zombies i've ever played. The campaign is actually pretty decent. The flaws of the campaign are more regarding the plot and writing, not the gameplay. The worst part of the campaign for me are definitely the extreme cringe of the characters, but none are WORSE on cringe factor then the character of Polina Petrova. But the gameplay and missions in this campaign is what makes it pretty decent. It's fun to play through the campaign even when it has one of the weaker plots and characters of the franchise. Still better characters then the ghost and black ops 3 campaign!

The multiplayer is half good and half bad. The main problem with it right now is the fact that it's pretty dead for a game that's not even two years old at the time i'm writing this review. But there's definitely entertainment in the multiplayer. Still, the game is a pretty mid tier cod in both campaign and multiplayer. Zombies is as said the worst ever made. Worth buying on sale.

Campaign: 6.5/10
Multiplayer: 6.5/10
Zombies: 2.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
