\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a soft 'not recommended', it was ok. I'm probably in the minority here, but I mostly bought this for the singleplayer campaign. That was fun enough, though a bit cheesy/they were going for some weird A-team thing. I did enjoy that it at least touched on several theatres of WW2: Europe, Pacific Front, North Africa, Stalingrad, the siege of Berlin, etc. Weapon variety was fun as well, it was kind of neat how each mission gave you relevant weapons for where the fight took place. Like in North Africa you can use some of the few Australian/NZ weapons from the war. Russia you of course get Russian weapons, etc etc. So if you're looking for the typical cheesy action movie COD campaign like I was, you'll get one.

However most are probably buying this for the multiplayer, which is the main reason this review is negative. It's fun enough, but my main problem is the game is already pretty 'dead' for something that came out this year (at least according to Steam). And that's *with* crossplay to other platforms, I still can't find a match on the bigger game modes. Which is incredibly disappointing from something that came out so recently.
You can still find a game, just not on the 'blitz' big battle modes that have lots of players (at least I couldn't find one). There is a bit of absurd cosmetics. Like my very first match, I was anticipating World at War style, where it's allies vs Nazis or whatever, but this one you have to choose an 'operator' (a little character with a backstory). But on my team this first match there's a guy in a purple pimp suit, I'm like... "is that Snoop Dogg??" It was Snoop Dogg. He had a black Doberman following him around too, and they got the man himself to record some voice lines. We're getting fired on and he goes "damn, y'all gotta chill". It was pretty funny/doesn't bother me too much, but quite different from past WW2 titles.

Also worth noting that I haven't played Warzone or whatever so I can't comment on that.
TL;DR: Campaign is ok, could buy on sale, but the multiplayer isn't populated enough to justify the purchase if that's what you're after.\t\t\t\t\t
