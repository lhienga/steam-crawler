I start the game and during the splash animations, where the developers can be seen, the game freezes and then crashes with an error message.

(*) BUT if I put Steam in offline mode (not friend list on Steam, but the Steam client in offline mode), I now get to the Activision Online Services, where on the one hand the shaders are loaded at the initial start. Because I never had this problem in the Battle.net launcher. (There was once a Free Weekend there, where I could test it!) Here on Steam, unfortunately, it does.

And since the problem can not be solved, I'm probably forced to refund the game - very sad.

Nachdem ich mir Vanguard auf Steam geholt habe, wollte ich es natürlich gleich spielen - vor allem wegen dem Zombie-Modus. Aber leider macht mir das Spiel [bzw. sogar Steam selbst (*)] ein Strich durch die Rechnung. Ich starte das Spiel und bei den Splash-Animationen, wo die Entwickler zu sehen sind, friert das Spiel bei mir erst ein und dann stürzt es mit einer Fehlermeldung ab.

(*) ABER wenn ich Steam im Offline-Modus versetze (nicht Freundschaftsliste auf Steam, sondern den Steam-Client im Offline-Modus), komme ich immerhin nun bis zum den Activision Online Services, wo zum einen ja beim Erststart die Shader geladen werden. Aber die Online Services wollen nicht laufen; auch nachdem die Shader vollständig geladen wurden, passiert weiter nichts. Also es muss wohl ein Problem mit der Steam-Migration des Spiels von Battle.net zu Steam sein. Denn im Battle.net-Launcher habe ich das Problem damals nie gehabt. (Es gab mal ein Free Weekend dort, wo ich das testen konnte!) Hier bei Steam leider schon.

Und da sich das Problem wohl nicht lösen lässt, bin ich wohl oder über dazu gezwungen, dass Spiel zurück erstatten zu lassen - sehr schade.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCampaign

The Campaign in this game isn't as bad as i thought it would be. They Have a focus on 4 main characters which get 2 missions each. you would think it would develop each character well but it didn't do well for me. i didn't come attached to any of the characters because i felt only 2 missions and some cutscenes wasn't Enough Character Development. I think COD:WWII Campaign was much better. It made you feel connected to the main character and small character build up about your team throughout the game. This game Just felt Rushed. It's like they tried to smash a bunch of characters into a campaign as quickly as possible.

Achievements

If you like getting Achievements, this game couldn't be a worse idea to get for that reason. Most of them do not work no matter how hard you try.

Multiplayer

Multiplayer is not worth the time of day, for one main reason. There's a perk called ninja which is just dead silence forever. You'll get into a match and you can't hear anyone's movement because every single person uses it.

Zombies i haven't played enough to give an opinion.

Don't buy it. Save your money please.
