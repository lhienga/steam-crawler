Characters were so cringe... I could not feel connected to characters. Their attitude towards each other was like high school kids. They are in a very important operation but they dont act or talk professional- unlike Modern Warfare games. In modern warfare games you feel the seriousness of the missions and you can see that characters are connected to each other and they respect each other. it makes you to feel more connected to game but in Vanguard, the team itself is weird that they all from different countries and working together... What the hell is Russian lady doing with american-British forces. it looked awkward.

Conversations in Russian and German parts were in English. it was stupid. I would prefer subtitles but original languages. them speaking 5 words in English but adding 1 word Russian into conversation makes it even more stupid. it was terrible

Story telling and cinematics are awesome, in all the COD games you see great cinematics. I liked to watch them. But for some reason in game graphics were not that good. it makes a weird transition from cinematic to game due to poor graphics. I played at highest available settings at 2k, not 4k but textures were so poor in many parts. It looked like Call of Duty 2 sometimes. For example the episode with aircraft carrier, episode stars in a class but you can not read the board properly because it is pixelated like hell.

I would never buy it if I had a chance to play some demo. I liked both new Modern Warfares and I recommend you to go with those instead of this one.
