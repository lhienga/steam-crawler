\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a fan of WWII history...I thought I would pick up this game to see what it could be about. I haven't gotten a COD game in many years, as they have declined so much. Sadly, this game is full of so many falsehoods, I am afraid it will make younger kids who play it think it was true. It's complete fiction and garbage. It almost seems this was intentional propaganda BS to younger audiences. The new "woke" movement I suppose. Some of you may be thinking, "Hey Bro it's just a game...". Well, just because you can "re-write" history in movies, shows, and now games....doesn't make it real. And we all don't need younger people to have these fake spins on our history. WWII had so many REAL heroes that there is no need (no matter what race or nationality is offended for being left out) to make up fake ones covering races or nationality's that didn't do "great" things. The real OSS never had a unit under command of anyone like this game has...period. Moreover, I found it comical that the game even makes the Nazi's move evil than they already were in real life. Do we even need this COD? I wasn't aware there was any question?

Now, take or leave my thoughts on the basis of the game. Now to the gameplay itself:

Graphics are good.
Game is decent when it comes to playing-like playing an 80's action film. Get shot 50 times and live while killing hordes of enemies. "They drew first blood...."
Story is amusing.
Weapons have some that are real and some that are fake.
Acting is...well, I didn't know video games needed acting and it shows.
Finally, you save the day and you realize you wasted your time doing so.

I give it an D-\t\t\t\t\t
