\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the worst COD in the franchise in my opinion stealing the throne away from infinite warfare.

The campaign, like always, has solid game play. Its the story & characters that suck. They have one intro mission and one ending mission and everything in between is just going into the backstory of the main characters. I believe the idea behind this was to make us like and remember the characters but it had the opposite effect on me. I dont particularly like any of these characters and after just finishing the campaign I dont even remember any of their names. They failed at making me care. These characters dont come anywhere near previous iconic cod characters like Mason, Woods, Price, Ghost, etc. The same could be said for the antagonists. They are so forgettable and cant measure up to villains like Dragovich, Krevchenko, Steiner, Menendez, Zakhaev, Shepard, Makarov, etc. They shouldve spent less time on backstories and more time on telling a good main story.

The multiplayer & zombies are also pretty bad. They yet again fail at making good maps for the multiplayer and I hate the new version of zombies that started with Cold War. Zombies needs to follow the WaW, BO1 & BO2 formula. Its just changed too much for me and I have no interest in playing it.

Feel free to pass on this COD you wont miss much.\t\t\t\t\t
