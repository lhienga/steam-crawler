\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom Eternal wishes it was this game.

Turbo Overkill blends the delicious, white knuckled, blood soaked combat tropes that Doom 2016 popularised and Doom Eternal butchered, improving on the design Bethesda brought to the mainstream and further refining it. No frills, no nonsense, this game is about one thing and one thing only: leaving nothing but pools of bolognese sauce on the ground where your enemies were.

If you liked the Doom reboots or Dusk, this game will be right up your alley. Some nice story touches too, and I've enjoyed all the boss fights so far (I'm almost done with EP2).

Probably my game of the year for 2023 simply for how right it gets its mechanics and how few people were required to pull it off. Can't wait for a sequel!\t\t\t\t\t
