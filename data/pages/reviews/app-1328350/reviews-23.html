Turbo Overkill (2023) represents a retro-inspired first-person shooter (FPS) game, developed by the company Trigger Happy Interactive and published by the company Apogee Entertainment.

Pros & cons:

++ What starts as a stereotypical revenge story with a cardboard cutout hero escalates into a full-scale planetary war, where you slide at breakneck speed, followed by a triple jump to finally ram your chainsaw leg through multiple heads. High octane FPS action from humble beginning to the spectacular finish.

++ The amount of upgradable body parts, skills and weapons turn you into a murder machine that would make the T-800 jealous. In addition, an aggressive playstyle is supported by allowing for armor and health leech in melee combat, which becomes essential at higher difficulties. It also results in more fluent battles and higher combos.

+ The Overkilled difficulty is a wet dream of mine. An endless spiral of excess with boss battles worthy to be remembered and timed sections that need perfect execution. Johnny Turbo demands peak performance!

Summary:

What is better than one chainsaw leg? A chainsaw on every limb and an unquenchable thirst for vengeance against the entity responsible for your unwanted cyborgization.

Recommended for aficionados of overclocked killer cyborgs with nothing to lose.

I got bored one day, so bored, that I decided to see if there was any action packed games on the Steam Store, turns out I had never played the beautifully crafted and intense game that IS Turbo Overkill. It's like if you took Doom Eternal and Far Cry: Blood Dragon and shook them up in an adrenaline junkie trademarked blender from a sketchy cross-fit store.
----
PROS:
* Doom Eternal Mechanics, Jump, Cut, Slide, Grapple, and KILL.
* Fast Paced Action, AND I AM NOT OVER EXAGGERATING, ITS FAST ASF
* Take control of Deadly Vehicles that you couldn't in Doom
* Sickest and Coolest Motorcycle chase scene ever
* The Villains are your classic Cyberpunk Comic Characters, and their designs are KILLER
* Boss Battles that will either break you, or boost your confidence
* Annoying Enemies that will make you want to go back and finish them off
* CHAINSAW LEGS
* Skill System and Weapon Upgrades that truly make you feel like a bad ass
* Beautiful and Colorful Synthwave Environments
* Story-line that mixes Movie References with Classic Futuristic Media
* CHAINSAW HANDS
* Robocop type Revenge Plot with a rage quit inducing boss battle (its still a lot of fun)
* Everything else. This Game is and was Brilliant, and I bought it on a whim cause I was BORED.
----
CONS:

I don't see a Turbo Overkill 2 yet.\t\t\t\t\t
