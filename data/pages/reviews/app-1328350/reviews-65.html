\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm only about halfway through but at this rate this game is just utterly spectacular. It's not only one of it not THE best boomer shooters I've ever played (overtaking the throne held by Dusk), it's full stop one of the best shooters in general I've ever played. I'm in complete awe that a game of this quality and length was made by what I assume is a pretty small indie team. The game makes it SO easy for you to jump in and start enjoying it, there are no barriers here. Mechanically it's practically perfect. I haven't played a game in a while that puts me in a flow state quite as well as this one does and I attribute that to how smooth everything feels. The movement is fast and very easy to control and understand, and the weapons are varied, interesting, and overall awesome. The level design is as good as it gets in this genre with plenty of opportunity for exploration and well designed shootouts. The visuals, sound design, and soundtrack are all varied and fit the game's theme so well. So far if there's one single minor critique I can find it would be the sound design for some weapons (more specifically shotguns) and how they sound pretty underpowered. I got used to it pretty quickly and it's extremely minor overall.

So far at the halfway point (and at this rate) TO is an EASY 10/10 and in my opinion one of the top 5 best games of the year. I seriously can't wait to see what this dev does next.\t\t\t\t\t
