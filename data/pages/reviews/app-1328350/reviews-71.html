\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOH MY GOD YES GET IT DO NOT HESITATE! Okay so to say more, this is high octane. Very well made, and very fluid. The story so far is amazing with like I think around 20 or more levels to play. All the weapons feel good and each of them have their own unique and surprisingly powerful attachments that you can upgrade. The story has more depth than I bargained for. I was thinking that the game was going to be more on the surface, but surprisingly has more depth than you think, the only thing the game doesn't have deep lore seeing as it doesn't do anything to help the story, but it would definitely help with the atmosphere. It is simply one of the most entertaining boomer shooters with high speed movement to what I'd compare to games like Quake on the Unreal engine. The game has a small range of characters that don't need too much to understand, simplicity and complexity done right (Audio logs you can find help a lot). There's definitely some pop culture references like LOTR and for example I found an Easter egg that was of a head (Of what I assume was one of the devs) kinda like the John Romero Head at the end of Doom 2, but in this game it was pixelated wall graffiti in a somewhat difficult to reach spot. If you are still reading this just go buy the game now. The game shines in every area where it needs to and *wants* to. It's very fast paced, but providing enough challenge and leeway to make it fun. Some of the weapons feel boring, but the game encourages using your other weapons and figuring out a few strategies to deal with some of the enemies, like enemies with a shield that can only be killed in certain ways (Like with a rocket, a plasma gun, or with the base shotgun's alt fire). There are augments, wepons, and skills to change the way each fight turns out like slowed time, a grappling hook that's inserted into your wrist, or an augment for your lock on rocket with the most satisfying middle finger for an enemy that will more than likely die from the first blast and will drop more explosives around all the targets, giving them of the biggest "♥♥♥♥ you and everything around you"s I've seen in this game. Overall 11/10 Masterpiece I would buy the sequel already if there was one.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcellent, colorful retro-inspired very fast shooty shoot that feels like it draws a lot of the right lessons from Doom Eternal without making as many mistakes. It has most of what I want from these sorts of games, which is to say a big arsenal of stupid weapons with silly alt-fires, a bunch of mobility tools to zip around arenas, and an almost comical amount of violence in the form of pixelated low-poly cyborg gore. As someone who initially dismissed the concept of a "Chainsaw Leg" allow me to say the Chainsaw Leg is very good and very fun.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome.

Combat loop is solid, character mods provide a surprising number of options.
Difficulty progression and pacing is great. It is challenging to juggle all the abilities you get!
The story is completely ridiculous and amazing. Dialogue is exactly as cheesy as it should be.
The levels start out fairly thin but escalate quickly, the design and setting change constantly.
A few fun gimmick levels that don't overstay their welcome.
Platforming sections are way more fun than Doom.
Completely over the top cutscenes, love it.


Very fast. When I played during early access I wasn't sure if this was really the game for me. I loved the doom eternal weapon combos, but the base speed in this game is like whiplash if you don't have your fps legs.\t\t\t\t\t
