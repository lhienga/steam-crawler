\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, but most people seem to agree so I'll throw in my mostly irrelevant opinions, especially since the credits end with alluding towards a continuation of the story.

1) I might just be clueless, but at some parts the game felt somewhat like a maze. Mostly in the first half of the game, but I still never fully knew where I was supposed to go on levels that weren't completely linear. I think a better system to show the player where to go would work wonders for players like me. CLUELESS PEOPLE RISE UP

2) Again, could be just a personal preference, but many of the weapons felt pretty irrelevant. I played on a difficulty where ammo couldn't be purchased, but rarely found myself running out of ammo for the first 6 weapons. The rest were too niche and didn't have too much of a use that couldn't be replaced by the ones I used too much. Additionally, I don't like playing my keyboard like a piano to use one of the 9 other weapons without using the weapon wheel, which feels hard to control in the middle of combat.

3) Most of the bosses were not great. I'm happy that the later bosses and the rest of the game was great, but the bosses from 40%-80% main game completion didn't feel that great to play. The final bosses of the game were great though.

Despite these issues, I still loved this game, especially the occasional vehicle sections. These issues might just be due to my cringe and ultrakill-biased brain. Regardless, game good and deserving of its rating\t\t\t\t\t
