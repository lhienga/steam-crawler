\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been jumping from old school shooter to old school shooter, forgetting what the genre even means and eventually landed on this gem.

If you like it over the top, then you are going to love this one. It basically tells an absolutely bonkers story about an AI fusing with a street cleaner and only you can save the world. You have chainsaw legs and later even arms and it goes from there. Expect face paced combat, expect a lot of gore and gibs and expect to slide around a lot, high speed. Very enjoyable. The story is voiced and even funny at times. I would say, that the game tends to be on the easier side, so maybe choose a higher difficulty than Regular Joe. Sadly, changing the difficulty after the fact is not possible at all and you need to create a new save game, losing all collectibles and augments that way.

Go get this game! :)\t\t\t\t\t
