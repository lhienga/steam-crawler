AND THE CHAINSAW LEG. In this game you have a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ chainsaw attached to your leg with a jetpack on it. I haven't obtained it yet, but I noticed there is a grappling hook somewhere in this game, and I cant even imagine how much more movement this game can get. No other game makes me feel quite like a crack addled rocket propelled spider monkey than this.

100% worth your money.\t\t\t\t\t
