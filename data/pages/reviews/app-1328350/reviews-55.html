\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10/10 ABSOLUTE GOD TIER GAME, A SWIFT, MOVEMENT BASED, HEAVENLY INTENSE ACTION FUELED CRACKDOWN. (w gorgeous tidbits and elements of Bioshock, Ultrakill & Cyberpunk/Blade Runner)

Having only played 3 hours, I can tell you straight away, right from the get go, I immediately wanted to 100% each level before progressing. Secrets are fun, Movement is godlike, action is intense, kills and gunplay is just downright sexual, Custom Splices (Think augments in Cyberpunk) to add as you please, Special abilities (Think Bioshock) to just absolutely ♥♥♥♥ up Someone's day. YOU CAN VIBE W YOUR AI BUD called SAMM. While SAMM pilots your hovercar, you are also free to drive it about n just absolutely light up any and all foes. YOU CAN RIDE ON TOP OF YOUR CAR HOOD AND PILOT IT IF YOU WANT. THE ENTIRE GAME IS SO DOPE SO FAR.

And get this...

THE STORY IS ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ AMAZINGGGG. At first I was like "hehehe, I'm gonna ♥♥♥♥ up SYN so bad, it wont know what hit 'em" Nahhhh I play on Hard mode and it's probably best on that so far, but lemme tell you, the enemy placements and all sorts are so well done. the instant you're like "haha, this is way too easy! 'That all you got?!" BOOM, NEW ENEMY OR LIKE..A BUNCH OF TOUGH ENEMIES. WALLRUNNING, AIR DASHING, MOMENTUM BASED SLIDES THAT ALSO DAMAGE ENEMIES DUE TO YOUR CHAINSAW LEG. FULLY FLEDGED CUTSCENES AND WELL VOICED CHARACTERS.

Real ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ talk. I left this game as a "ehh. Maybe I'll try it at some point" for FARRR TOO LONG. This is 100% Going up there as a 10/10 on my list. GORGEOUS GAME. I actually think this is what I wanted System Shock to be.

I'm not going to be the person that hints at story or ruins elements in the review. Trust me, you don't want to miss this game. It's the most entertaining, slick and ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome game I've played in ages.

And to those who made the game? Bless you. Bless you for literally covering all the bases of what makes an absolutely bonkers and fun game, creating secrets that aren't hard to get to and that actually make sense with a short look and little hints here and there, secret armour n Health pickups, introducing new enemies without always needing a character intro, instead having a codex for each enemy you kill.. Smooth and goofy ass-driving. Downright fire wall running and *ahem* certain weapon jumping. (if you know you know~) every weapon having a alternate fire just ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ slaps, stores w upgrades are great and force the player to kill more creatively for more dosh, etc etc.

Honestly, you should be proud. You did AMAZINGLY.

And in case you're worried as devs that "Oh maybe people might think of this as an Ultrakill wannabe or clone" Nah. This is it's own thing and it's absolutely stellar.\t\t\t\t\t
