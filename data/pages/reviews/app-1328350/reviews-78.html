\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving just discovered Turbo Overkill due to a random YouTube review last weekend this has been the best gaming experience I have had in years just out of the blue. As a hardcore fan of classic shooters like Turok, Doom, Half-Life & also new features from Doom Eternal the moment I saw Turbo Overkill I had to buy it. I did not expect the game to not only be good but beyond fantastic & with each new weapon and location the game just keeps getting better. Special mention goes to the incredible soundtrack some levels have music I would consider far superior to most AAA titles these days and pretty much loved every bit of music from beginning to end.
A fantastic mix of unique and interesting weapons keeps the game fresh all the way through and the movement and combat loop I found as good or even better than Doom Eternal which is no small praise. Also impressive is it managed to capture a pretty unique setting really well and got me decently invested in its story which I did not expect at the start, by the end you are fighting in some crazy over-the-top locations and battles. If you loved Doom Eternal or any of the classic retro shooters and want more but with a Sci-Fi cyberpunk theme and retro-style visuals you have to buy this game. I cannot recommend Turbo Overkill more highly, it was just a joy all the way through, and wish I could delete my memories of the last week to do it again which is how all the best games should feel when it's over.\t\t\t\t\t
