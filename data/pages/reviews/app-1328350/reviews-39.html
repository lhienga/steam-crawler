\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best FPS games I've recently played. The gunplay and movement are smooth, responsive and very satisfying which brings to mind games like DOOM Eternal which, in my opinnion, is deffinitly a pro. Bosses are also quite fun, varid and on higher levels may provide a decent challenge. Collectibles aren't tedious and provide rewards in the form of smaller challenge levels. The campaign, though a bit to long for my taste, suprisingly for a "retro-style FPS", tells an interesting story.
I think that it is a mustbuy for players enjoying frantic FPS games inspired by boomer shooters.\t\t\t\t\t
