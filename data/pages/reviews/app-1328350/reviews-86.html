\r\n\t\t\t\t\tModernising the retro shooter like many of its sisters revitalizing an gaming era decades past, Turbo Overkill does just about every ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ thing right.

- It doesn't show its hand too quickly, giving you a new toy nearly every level to mess with.
- None of these toys including the starting pistol becomes obsolete thanks to essential upgrades.
- The movement flows even if the path to progress is positively zonked.
- Never got lost.
- Augmentation system allows for many variety of playstyles.
- Its endearingly cheesy, authentic, and stylish.
- Soundtrack bops
- Game can be hard in some areas, but its not hard to learn. Game does a lot of the "cool" heavy lifting which is awesome.
- Doesn't have the funny shareware bit where only the first episode slaps.
- I'm profoundly surprised that the vehicle sections, as small as they were, had me wanting even more of them.
- A simple, but very engaging main character and sidekick.
- Antagonists that are actually really dope. The ♥♥♥♥ talking hits good in both "I'ma kill you" and "I kinda like you" ways.
- Campaign is lengthy, and doesn't overstay its welcome.
- Has a bunch of extra stuff like endless mode and map editor because why the hell not, its well made too.

Totally worth a hearty shot. Strong recommend.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the greatest boomer shooters of all time.

The movement capabilities in this game are the best I've ever played. The slide, the momentum you can keep if you hold slide while jumping, the dashes, double jumps, grapples, wall running, portals....bro, this game is like crack. Best movement of any FPS game ever made, hands down. I don't say that lightly.

The guns are all sick and they keep drip feeding you upgrades and harder enemy encounters until you literally feel like a god at the end. Gun upgrades are actually meaningful and powerful.

The power trip in the later levels is the best in recent memory. The level design is top notch and you are always exploring something new, shooting something new, etc...never bored. Stands face to face with DOOM Eternal and Dusk for best "boomer shooter" of all time IMO. The trailers do not do the game justice. What a huge accomplishment!\t\t\t\t\t
