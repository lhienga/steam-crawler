\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow! Now that was something else. I just finished it, and I actually cannot wrap my head around how good this game is! It starts rather slow, and at first I was thinking to myself "oh, cool little Doom Eternal clone", but after 2 hours it surprised me, then it wowed me and then the game kept getting better and better and better and it basically didn't stop, until the very end. It obviously borrows heavily from aforementioned Doom Eternal, but it's doing it's own thing, adding many interesting mechanics to the formula, and frankly creativity in level design, visuals and some of the mechanics is astounding. And did I mention the music? OMG the music is just fantastic, do yourself a favor and go listen to "Toxic Refinery" track right away. The story and cutscenes are also pretty good. To sum this up: Turbo Overkill is an absolute masterpiece and if you like FPS games in general playing this game is not optional!\t\t\t\t\t
