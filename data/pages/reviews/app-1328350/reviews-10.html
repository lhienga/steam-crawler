\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPart boom shoot, part character action FPS, this is the cream of the crop of the retro shooter genre. The gang's all here: incidental combat, lock-in arena fights, exquisite level design, an appropriate(ly cheesy) OST, and a story line that gradually matures past the usual drivel over the length of the campaign.

You don't just get a super shotgun, you get a super shotgun with a grenade launcher. You don't just get a railgun, you get a railgun that can telefrag people on command. And most importantly you don't just get a chainsaw, you get a chainsaw for each limb.

Turbo Overkill lives up to its name for better and worse. It's balls to the wall action from start to finish, but the finish line is about an hour or two past where it needs to be. Even so, it is well worth your time and money. You'd be doing a disservice to yourself as an FPS fan if you don't play this.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTurbo Overkill is one of the most frantic fast-paced FPS games I've ever played. Almost all of the guns are perfect; the pistols are still useful till the end of the game (unlike a certain game that rhymes with boom) and all the weapons have a use in combat. The only gun that make my balls crunch is the sniper; it doesn't feel useful during combat and feels jank, whenever I'm forced to use it (especially in the last level) minor amounts of blood surges through my balls in the worst way humanly possible. The enemies are mostly good; most serve a purpose in constructing a fight and all are instantly recognisable. The only enemies I don't like are the flying sentient poop holes, they never actually harm me they just annoy me and the buff ass TV head guys, they are essentially the same is the skinny ♥♥♥♥♥ that run at you but bigger and goofier. The only times I got lost in a level were more related to my soft pressed in skull rather than the game so I guess the level design is good. The game is a little bit too long; it feels 2-3 hours longer than it really needs to be. The bosses are kinda mid unfortunately, they all feel either extremely easy (i.e syn and that artifact ♥♥♥♥) or feel mildly ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ (i.e Maw and Ripper (Ripper only because I had to use the sniper and Maw because phase 1-2 are hard then 3-4 are piss easy)). Overall I give this game 8.94/10.01.\t\t\t\t\t
