\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBy far one of the best indie singleplayer boomer-shooters out there.

Turbo Overkill is more than a love-letter to the retro-FPS genre; it's a game made with soul and passion, and this becomes clear after every passing episode in the game: the tools in the gameplay soundbox, the soundtrack and the scope of the story keep growing larger until the end. It. Just. Keeps. Getting. Better.

In addition to excellent gameplay, this INDIE title features plenty of fully-voiced cutscenes (skippable; usually placed at the beginning of levels or during key boss fights).

Side note: in Early Access/throughout the game's development, there were a couple of levels/areas that just didn't 'click': the final boss of Episode 1 and the motorcycle level in Episode 2 come to mind. The developer took the time to rectify these levels in Early Access all the while developing the masterpiece that is Episode 3. This developer clearly CARES about their product.

This game is a STEAL at this asking price. Buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t

yet another fast paced boomer shooter. plays and looks quite similar to ultrakill altough unlike its graphics its MUCH harder to run dont expect to play on a potato DONT BUY unless you got 1070 on better, you will need a lot of frames to enjoy this game. atm i got 15hrs into the game played both easiest and hardest difficulty i could say this is enjoyable for both new to fps and hardcore ones alike. on the harder difficulties i find myself strapped for ammo and find myself using quickswap often, so this is a CON if youre expecting run n gun no think expirience, altough the easier difficulties offer that.

the cyberpunk aesthethic definitely comes across and looks nice, altough i got gripes with the level design not clearly telling where to go since its *not really* linear and also wants you to backtrack at some point without telling you, other than campaign theres custom user levels and endless mode. no comment on custom levels but given the tools i expect some really nice looking maps very soon, endless though? super fun albeit the ammo recharging every new wave instead of it being infinite from the start seems like a big hindsight to me.

story.... there was a story? lol. honestly its enough for a believeable background info for the world its not bad enough to joke about it but not good enough either for me to care on it

a solid recommend from my end\t\t\t\t\t
