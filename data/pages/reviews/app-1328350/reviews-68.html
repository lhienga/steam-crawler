\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great retro shooter. Movement is crisp and abilities make it incredibly fun to zip around your enemies juggling guns and alternate fire modes. Exploration is encouraged but not required, you can usually just find your way by looking for the next enemies or ammo.
I have gotten stuck a couple times not knowing where to go; sometimes it's easy to miss the hints in such a fast-paced game. And the driving section is borderline unplayable if you usually use inverted controls, as they don't invert during flight. Had to take that section extremely slow because of how ingrained the y-axis is in my brain.
Those two qualms aside, definitely recommend this. The movement is really close to perfect and overall it's just a really great, fun game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Easily a 10/10 game. An immediate must buy.
-The weapon balance throughout the campaign is extremely well managed.
-The enemies are varied and interesting to fight.
-The arenas are varied and never overstay their welcome.
-The bosses are actually fun to fight in an fps.
-The movement is incredibly satisfying
-There aren't any shoehorned political agendas.
-The characters are fun, sometimes humorous but never annoying.
All things considered, it may be the best fps I've ever played. It's certainly in the top 3. I eagerly await what the developer does next.\t\t\t\t\t
