\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't long like writing so imma just put down some random thoughts

What I like
-Movement options: Sliding, Dashing, rocketing jumping, and more
-Guns: All the guns are pretty unique and have some cool upgrades. Personnel favorites were the Upgraded SMG and Pump Shotty
-Art Design: You get to go to some cool looking locals throughout the game. Each felt pretty unique though they did sometimes blend in with each other.
-Story: I really enjoyed the story. It's not going to blow you away with how good it is, but I thought it was pretty good for this type of game
-Customization: You get a lot of upgrades throughout this game and a lot of slots to play around with. Nothing super deep, but its still nice to be able to play around with different augments

What I didn't like
-Honestly not much. I felt in certain places that game dragged a bit and some levels felt longer than they should've been. However, the game didn't stop being fun. It was just a feeling of "How much longer am I going to be in this area? I'm still having fun killing stuff, but I would like to see some other places now" type of feel.
-Weapon Sounds: A couple of the weapons lack a satisfying punch to them. Stuff like the super shotty and the sniper rifle don't sound nearly as powerful as they are.\t\t\t\t\t
