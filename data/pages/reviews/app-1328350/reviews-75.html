A good action FPS is one that flows and pulls you in, making you do cool stuff just because you feel like it. That is what this game does, the level design, the enemies, the weapons, your abilities, they all come together beautifully. Bonus points for having boss fights that do not suck and pretty epic scenery.

My only gripe, and I'm nitpicking here, is the vehicle sessions. They are few and far between (thankfully) but imo they don't add that much. If you make the main loop super fun you better have vehicular combat to match it or it will feel like an interruption instead of an addition.

But its a really small gripe. Go get this game, its superfun!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest boomer shooter in recent years. And behind this one I would put Postal Brain Damaged and Prodeus.

Its not because it recreates memories very well, rather opposite - because of indie nature it experiments with mechanics. For me this is mechanically better version of Doom Eternal.

Its quite unique because of movement, mostly because of chainsaw leg mechanics which can be combined with double jump + dash + grappling hook. You can stay midair most of the time, providing very unique flow to the game. Other game advantages - great enemy variety, cyberpunk environment.

However my major problem is that creator was chasing same thing as Doom Eternal so game is bloated with abilities. Therefore close to the end of the game engagements are too chaotic. Therefore you feel 2 things : 1 - you realize that some abilities you dont utilize, 2 - you spam same abilities all the time. Just for reference here is list of major abilities:

- double/triple jump
- regular dash
- chainsaw slide
- missile launcher
- grappling hook
- chainsaw arms
- turbo time
- weapon swap
- weapon alternate mods

Reducing number of abilities by 50% and adding depth to them would benefit gameplay greatly IMO. Great game does not need overcomplex rules to become popular or to have lots of room for skill development. For example chess - there are 6 types of pieces and 8x8 board. Very unlikely that if these numbers were doubled this game could become what it is.

Need to mention that while i believe that gameplay wise this game is more fun than Doom Eternal, production quality is no match for AAA title like Doom. So it would not be fair to say its overall better game than Doom.

Wishing dev all the best and hoping for polished sequel.\t\t\t\t\t
