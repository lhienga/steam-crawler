\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh yes...
Oh God yes...
So good... my God it's good...
I can't overstate how much I like this game. This is by far game of the year for me. Easily.
Fast combat with weapons that all feel viable and powerful.
A chainsaw leg that you can use to slide to cut down enemies... Do I need to say more?
I think the art style captures the Blade Runner like neon light dystopia atmosphere perfectly.
The sound track is freaking AWESOME. It really gives the mood an extra pump.

I started on hard and it has seem to be a good challenge until it became a bit too hard in the end; the game escalates, and then it escalates a bit more. It can seem a bit chaotic the first couple of hours but then when it starts to click... it's REALLY REALLY FUN...

The only criticism I have that sometimes you die quite fast without knowing what really happened. And TBH, most of the times it seems to happen because I got greedy with the chainsaw leg :D , but checkpoints are plenty so it is not really a big issue. And I played on hard which has 1.6x damage modifier, or smth like that, so I guess in normal it wouldn't even be an issue. But you also learn to recognize the enemies that are able to one shot you and adapt accordingly. You are so ridiculously overpowered in this game so it's only fair that enemies are too.

The boss battles are hard, IMO, and the weakest part of the game, but still very good for FPS boss battles. There is nothing inherently wrong with them, I think it just comes down to the fact that in these kinds of games it is much more fun to moan down hordes of enemies than take down one bullet-sponge with glowing parts you need to shoot, and by the way, floor is lava, so f*** you! The floor is always lava...

I have heard people compare it to Doom Eternal but in this game you don't run out of ammunition every 5 seconds. In many ways I feel like this game has took the good parts of Eternal and enhanced the whole "retro trow-back boomer shooter formula". I'm really excited to see what the developers are going to do next. This is basically a game that is done by a very small team and from the length and polish you would never guess it. Totally worth the asking price.

One of the best FPS I have ever played... Possibly even the best.
☑ And this time I mean it!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love shooters, but I've realized that the new wave of boomer shooters all have one thing in common: They are incredibly dull. They usually have extremely satisfying gunplay and nice hit effects and movement and it's just fun, but level design, enemy variety and behaviour or AI complexity and just the general pacing of these games is just severely lacking. The same applies to Warhammer Boltgun, Amid Evil and to some extent Prodeus (which atleast had great level design). The only exception so far is Ultrakill, which has such depth in its combat that it can carry itself entirely by that. When I think of Doom Eternal, the first thing that comes to mind are the badass iconic enemy types. When I think of most indie boomer shooters all enemy types just blend into one big wishy washy mash of cyber demonic thingies.\t\t\t\t\t

original review:

this game is genuinely amazing but equipping certain chainsaw augments straight up shuts my computer down. i'm only not recommending the game that causes such a massive issue like this but i know they will fix it. this is just a warning to new players that this could happen, and will instantly recommend after its fixed.

TLDR: games a wonderful 9/10, but this game loves its most miserable unfun thing, maw.

!!!potential spoilers ahead!!!




Review: you know that meme of a drawn horse being perfect all the way to the end being a child drawing? thats this game full stop.
I think this game is genuinely amazing. super fluid, i feel in control of almost all my abilities, and that bike combat is so smooth, i haven't played a game with a better hoverbike since like perfect dark. the music matches the tempo of gameplay, most the weapons i used almost throughout the entire game, and also the pistol stays relevant for once in a shooter!
there is genuinely so many small things i love in this, between small upgrades to your character making you feel more powerful all the way to the very end, unique designs, weapon utility, and enemy pool. i love most the arenas, this game never ever lets up, unless it needs to. at the beginning i was struggling, but by the end i was doing so good that almost nothing way that much of a challenge (dont worry though, if you stop moving they will absolutely kill you, its still very challenging).
the hoverbike level by far had to be one of my favorite missions, its just near perfect in everyway.
its graphically beautiful, while keeping the aesthetic of what an old quake shooter wanted to look like, almost like how n64 renders were what Nintendo wanted you to imagine the blocky characters to look like on the system. and the music is a straight banger, i've listened to the so much out of the game, like i did with dooms music. the color pallet stays consistent, only the ladders are hard to find sometimes though.

here are some things i'm iffy on, that doesn't really ruin anything but still takes me out abit. the car, its nice, but its kinda clunky, i kinda wish it had just a little bit more polish on the maps its in. cutscenes also feel way too floaty, and kinda weird (i know its a really small team making this and these don't effect the gameplay but i still wanted to mention it). some weapons don't really flow too well, like the ssg, i just feel it doesn't really warrant it in here, that being said i did use it alot, comboing the ssg+sg+ssg+rockets, but on its own just kinda felt weak. the sniper while really cool just never really worked well outside jut hip firing (ill get back to that) its still a wonderful weapon, but the crosshair is kinda bad for sniping in this game being so small. the assault rifle upgrade though? overpowered. literally best weapon, dealing so much more than anything else, the only gun that can make noticeable chunks on boss health bars(ill get back to that as well) it just became my most used gun, with using the others for rotation clearings. the venders, you cant mute their voices and it was irritating hearing them after awhile lol. also i'm not sure if there's any sort of map, but i couldn't find it, and some places could really use something like one. turbo time also while nice i used once and then forgot it ever existed. just abit too many things in this game, but you know? there may be someone who loved that mechanic more than the grapple, or other things. so while it didn't get used by me i could still beast the game with never using it.

Now here's the teetering thing that effected me the most. MAW, his boss fights are bad and tedious, and not really in a challenging way. if there were no checkpoints in them id actually have to not recommend. he eats so much ammo and has so many phases, with barely anything to replenish lost items. hes absolutely brutal if you try anything EXCEPT serious same back strafing away from him hipfiring the sniper. it mulches him, and not even the charged up shots, just the regular. it went from a genuinely brutal fight, to a braindead S+D+LMB simulator. beating the game, i have to say MAWs final boss level is garbage, awful looking character design, and miserable wannabe doom marauder. pure ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ garbage. its so bad that the last few levels with him he spawns in, in almost every combat encounter. hes not hard, hes boring and PADDING. his final fight sucks so badly. i genuinely hate that level with a passion just cause it literally goes on forever. you can only make an unintelligent knockoff of the marauder interesting for so long. and my god there being like what 10 phases to his uninteresting bossfight? its mind numbing. the only thing that dents him at all is that unbalanced assault rifle,. and i beg either balance the rest of the guns, or heavily reduce his health bar, cause its B O R I N G. i feel so bad bashing this lovely game so much here, but this game bashes me so badly in its last act.



that was a chunk of things i wasn't fond of, but they are such a small chunk of the game(except maw) and literally everything else has been p e r f e c t. i genuinely have high praise for the devs here. i cant stress enough how much fun i have had playing this. i plan on playing through again and again. I also appreciate you interacting the discussions and fans. thank you for the wondrous game\t\t\t\t\t
