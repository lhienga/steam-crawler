\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a good game. The first 2 episodes are very strong. finding new weapons and mods is exciting and learned the ins-and-outs of each mod and weapon mode feels very rewarding. Movement is fluid, fast, and just feels good. There's a nice variety of enemies and it does help to pay attention and prioritize which enemies will give you the most trouble. The vehicle sections are not horrible and help change up the pace, giving the player a break from the relentless assault on Syn.

The 3rd episode is sadly the weakest in the game. Its just a grind. Every time the player fights Maw its dragged out and gets stale fast, but Ep3 Maw is simply a bore to fight and by the end I was just glad it was over, only to get quickly tossed into the Syn fight which is also a long, drawn out, and annoying fight. Its not the difficulty of the fight, I beat it first try. Its how the game doesn't really tell you what to do, how many phases there are, and unlike the other bosses there is no health bar so the player doesn't know if what they are doing is working.

All in all if it wasn't for the 3rd Ep this game would be among my favorite FPSs but with how truly tedious the ending portion on the game is I don't really feel like playing it again. With the great art, awesome moment, and super satisfying gun play, I am sure I'll pick it up again eventually - just not right now.\t\t\t\t\t
