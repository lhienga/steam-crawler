\r\n\t\t\t\t\t + Interesting, simple concept that is far more creative than run-of-the-mill "I'm on Observation Duty"-ish horror games
+ Realistic graphics thanks to good lighting and textures
+ A variety of anomalies that – at least sometimes – send shivers down your spine
+ No over-the-top, cheap jumpscares

~ Few and (deliberately?) incomplete localizations: Japanese and English
~ Why are there only visual anomalies and no auditive anomalies? Those could easily be added. First I thought there would be audio-based anomalies but I noticed there aren't any after finding all anomalies.

– After playing for some time, the wall texture almost burns into your mind. It becomes annoying. Add some visual variety, please. Add more levels. Apply this concept on a dark street, an abandoned house/asylum/cliché location
– For a horror game there aren't many creepy, disturbing images. On a psychological level this works quite well but I did expect more horror elements to be honest

All in all, it's a good game with a lot of potential.\t\t\t\t\t
