The Exit 8
----------

At the time of this review, The Exit 8 is pretty popular with the streaming and YouTube Gaming crowd, which is how I stumbled upon it. Personally, I think that this first-person, "spot the difference" game is worth the hype that it's getting for 3 reasons. First, the game is currently super-cheap at $3.99 USD. Secondly, it's just a fun and slightly creepy, good time that seems to be accessible and enjoyably to a majority of the gamers that give it a shot. Finally, even though I know what all of the differences are and where to look for them at this point, I still love diving back into the game to relive the experience again.

There's only a couple of negatives here, including screen resolutions that don't seem to always implement correctly as well as a slightly disorienting introduction (as it takes some wandering around for instructions to show up, which adds to the atmosphere but also feels unnecessarily confusing.) On the flip side, the graphics and sound design ends of things are extremely polished and almost flawless.\t\t\t\t\t
