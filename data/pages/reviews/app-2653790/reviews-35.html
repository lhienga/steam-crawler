\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a cheap little 1-2 hour game time waster, it's quite entertaining. It does have some scary moments. Fun to watch your friends play through it after you know about all the differences (while you sit next to them)

The main gameplay loop is your in a never ending corridor that leads out of an underground Train Station. If the corridor looks normal you go forward, and it should be the exact same down to the details every time. However, if there are differences (Anomalies (I.E like a poster looks different)), you need to turn around and walk back from where you came (It is an endless corridor loop you'll end up back in the same corridor regardless) If you can make the right choice 8 times in a row then you beat the game. If you go the wrong way let's say going forward when there are differences, the count restarts. Can you escape Exit 8.

All in all there are 18 anomalies to discover. Have fun!\t\t\t\t\t
