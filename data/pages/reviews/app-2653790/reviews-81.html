Simple, Short and, Cheap

I really love the concept and the execution of this game. I bought this game however not knowing what genre it was. However, this made going into the game, for me, even more fun to experience.

TLDR +
A simple clean looking game. I personally didn't find it hard you could finish the game in arguably under 5 minutes. But i really took my time taking in the experience. The concept is fun and I'd really love to see it become or a new game in it's likeness really expand on an opportunity like this. I loved the eeriness of the silence and how the game starts . Like a first person observation duty ; I'm sure there's a few similar. The only shame is how short the game is. I really wish there was more.


My experience
I loved my gradual understanding of the game growing. At first I thought, maybe there's a pattern I need to follow and by dumb luck sometimes it seemed to work. But once my pattern was obviously ruined I really stopped and slowly walked around the passageway to take in the environment which thus led me to understanding the game.
Something to note about this I obviously got very lucky with the variations of anomalies as they were not as obvious. My first anomaly was actually the sign on the back of exit 8 which said "turn back" and that's where it clicked.

From then on going through the passageway was a cinch. Some of the anomalies were quite cool and a few gave me quite a scare. So overall my experience for this game was a positive one.\t\t\t\t\t
