\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe rules of the game are simple: look closely at your surroundings, if something's off, turn around and head the other way; If not, keep going forward. The tricky part is that you have to pay close (and I do mean close ) attention to your surroundings, because there's over 20 different anomalies that can appear, some are obvious but most are subtle (some incredibly so). It seems pretty random in how frequently an anomaly can appear, but fortunately only one anomaly appears at a time (at least that's how it is at the time I'm writing this), and almost all of these anomalies are benign— almost all of them, so as long as you don't let your curiosity get the better of you for the ones that aren't, you should at least be able to survive while you try to spot the remaining anomalies on your way towards Exit 8.

The subtlety is what makes this game scary, and it leaves you clueless on where to look and makes you second-guess yourself very often. It's like a "spot the differences" type game turned into a liminal space experience, but I think it's done very well. It's a pretty unique experience, and I'd love to see more like it in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
