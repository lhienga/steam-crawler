I also love how it seems to take completionists into consideration - it feels like the chance of finding new anomalies is weighted in the players favour which is brilliant.

It strikes a great balance of having easy to spot anomalies with more subtle ones like a small difference in the poster or the yellow tiles.

There are also a few patches that have been done to this game which shows that the game hasn't been released and abandoned which is nice to see.

63 mins for full completion, doesn't overstay it's welcome. It depends if you think supporting good devs is important I guess. It was a cool concept. I'd like to see them do more. Can you imagine if this developer had more concepts like this and blended them all into one game - that would be awesome!\t\t\t\t\t
