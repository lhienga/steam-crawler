\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShorter games that are cheap and have a fun little gimmick are underappreciated and underrepresented on steam.
Especially when it tells you how long the game is in the description.

The game is exactly what it looks like, you roam hallways to try and find anomalies. You either progress to the next exit, or restart from 0. It feels really unnerving to walk around, wondering if you might miss something while paying attention to a certain object.
The lack of noise makes that unnerving feeling even worse. The game sets you up to expect a jump scare, only to keep you in that suspense. With the biggest jump scares coming from flickering lights, bonking on doors or a door creaking.

The game is simple, and you can finish it in under an hour. But the game looks good and executes well on the gimmick it wants to represent. In my opinion, this game would've worked even better in VR.

All in all, the game is really short, which is fine since the price of this game actually fits for how short the game is. I can only recommend it!\t\t\t\t\t
