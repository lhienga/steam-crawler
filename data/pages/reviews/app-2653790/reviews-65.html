Some feedback to the devs:
I do agree with some negative feedback that the anomalies being removed from the pool after witnessing them once is a bit dumb and could be improved, whereas maybe have the unseen anomalies have a 66% weight vs 33% weight for already seen. Something like that.

Thanks for the game. Would love to see a sequel or a similar game in the future. Will be following!\t\t\t\t\t
