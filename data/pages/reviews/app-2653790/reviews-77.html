9. Announcements:

Anomalous Property: A intercom speaker is on the wall, it says anything OTHER THAN THE TIME

10. Cracked Tiles/Dirty Tiles:
Normal Property: All the tiles on the wall are pristine.
Anomalous Property: A very small amount of tiles on the wall are dirty or damaged.

11. Incorrect Number:
Normal Property: The sign on the ceiling will say "8"
Anomalous Property: The sign on the ceiling will say "9"

12. Crushed:
Normal Property: Ceiling stays in a fixed position
Anomalous Property: Ceiling slowly lowers until it crushes you and drops if you try to keep going forwards

13. Wrong Way:
Normal Property: You walk the direction of the arrow pointing to the exit
Anomalous Property: You walk the opposite way and start on the opposite end of the hall

14. Fire Exit Sign:
Normal Property: They are on the ceiling lighting up green saying "EXIT"
Anomalous Property: They are on the ceiling flickering once or twice displaying "EXIT"

15. Crowded:
Extra Info: Once you are to level 4 and past sometimes there will be some other people on which is normal
Normal Property: There are 1-3 people walking down the hall.
Anomalous Property: There are more than 3 people walking or one of the 3 people have anomalies such as smiling, staring at you, walking fast, having no face, or becoming giant.

16. Water fountain:
Normal Property: Someone is drinking at the fountain or no one is but its off.
Anomalous Property: No one is drinking out of the fountain but its still running water.

17. Bathroom:
Normal Property: There is a men and women's restroom at the end of the hall (The floor switches to a solid grey at the end of the hall and is not shiny near the bathrooms) it has two sides, one for women (Right Side), one for men (Left side):
Anomalous Property 1: The sound of a toilet flushing emits from one of them.
Anomalous Property 2: The sides that the bathroom is on flip (Men is now on right side and vice versa for women.)
Anomalous Property 3: The power is out inside the restroom and the walk way to it.
Anomalous Property 4: The walkway into the bathrooms get closed as a big metal gate comes from the ceiling and locks them.
Anomalous Property 5: A flood comes out of the bathroom, if you touch it you're fine but water pools everywhere right outside of the bathrooms.

18. Ripped Poster:
Normal Property: Posters are in tip-top shape.
Anomalous Property: One of the posters has a slight rip.

19. Swapped Posters:
Normal Property: Posters are in normal order
Anomalous Property: A poster swapped places with one of the others.

20. Shrinking posters:
Normal Property: Posters are normal size
Anomalous Property: Posters shrink ever so slightly

21. Lengthened Hallway:
Normal Property: Hallway is normal length
Anomalous Property: Hallway is slightly longer, you can tell this by counting the lights on the ceiling, this is one of the hardest things to spot, however.

22. Evasion:
Extra info: This will give you a slight scare if you're not careful and this only can happen on/past level 5.
Normal Property: Lights are on no other anomalies.
Anomalous Property: This happens with the power out issue. If the fire panel has a red light on while the power is out evasion is here. You will see a dull grey entity with maroon eyes chasing you, a loud shrieking sound will play as it chases you. Run as fast as you can backwards and keep going until the lights turn back on. However, when they do make sure to turn back around and go the normal direction otherwise he will appear right in front of you, jump scare you and send you back to level 0

23. Sign Error:
Normal Property: Sign displaying "^ Exit 8" will appear normally
Anomalous Property: The sign displaying "^ Exit 8" will change color or start flickering

24. Slippery Steps:
Normal Property: Once you reach the end the steps will appear normally
Anomalous Property: Once you reach the end, if the steps are very shiny and appear to have ice on them, turn back and then you will see another set of stairs that don't appear to have ice on them, go up those to win.

25. Cold Blooded Murderer:
Normal Property: The game will go normally
Anomalous Property: If you get 3 anomalies that each affect people in someway you might get the Cold Blooded Murderer anomaly later, once you get to level 8, if the guy walks slightly slower than normal, turn around. Otherwise he will run faster than you can run once you get near him and kill you restarting you at level 0.
