Stuck in the same passageway with the same guy

Can some please free the poor hard working dude from the passageway ? He seems stuck in his daily routine.
The Exit 8 takes the observe anomaly type of game principle and integrates this in a walking simulator. You walk the same corrider and if you notice something weird turn back otherwise walk straight ahead. Done this enough times and you are free to go.
Thats basically all and thats works great. As all observe type game they are easily beatable once you know what to look for. The same goes for The Exit 8. The game rewards escaping and finding all anomalies with an achievements. Which is completable in a realy short period. As the store page say 15-60min.
The only downside for replay value is that once you found and identify an anomaly correctly it gets crossed of the list. The list of all anomalies gets shorter and shorter till you found all and then it resets. For the achievement hunt great but if you wanna relive an anomaly you need to find all before and it makes the game easier for those who remember all anomalies.

Short entertaining fun with a horror atmosphere without having real jump scares. There are some encounters but they should be obvious enough.\t\t\t\t\t
