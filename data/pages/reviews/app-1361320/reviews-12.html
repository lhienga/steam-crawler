I really liked the hub world, multiple endings, and secret puzzles intertwined within the multiple endings. However, The Room 4: Old Sins does not continue the innovation. The game feels much more linear, with no hub world, no secret puzzles, and no multiple endings. Old Sins is not bad, it is just different from the third game. The puzzles are also very good, and I love how each of the rooms interact with each other through the objects you find. While I personally would have liked Old Sins to continue the gameplay of Three, it is by no means a bad game. To the contrary, it is very good. 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
