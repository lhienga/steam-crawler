In this The Room, the place acts as a hub, and you can go in any room of which you unlocked. However, some of the puzzles require you to go to another room carrying something to solve something else in another room. This would be fine, except 1. In order to get into another room, you need the eyeglass, and 2. you might forget where an item will go, so not only will you have to go through the animation of entering (which is long), you will have to go through the animation of exiting the room. Another bad thing I really don't like is the waiting time required for a hint. It feels like they extended it a lot.

So, should you get this game? An absolute yes, but get the bundle, because the first 3 games are amazing too. A solid 7.5/10 for me.\t\t\t\t\t
