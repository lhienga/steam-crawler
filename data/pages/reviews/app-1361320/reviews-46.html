Worth the couple bucks if you're a puzzle fanatic, definitely worth it in sale if you like puzzles and escape rooms.\t\t\t\t\t

Additional notes: after the 3, it was hard to top the content of puzzles and the overall layout. This one feels like a step down in terms of content. It is not bad, actually it is still very well done, very pretty, and full of little, smart puzzles. However, the 3rd one felt much smarter in terms of level design, with the huge hub - this one has a hub, but the overall backtracking you have to keep doing gets quite annoying. Also, the game can be done in one sitting... not that the Room series was famous for being hard, but still, one sitting compared to the 3rd one, and without a clue, and without any secrets or hidden things, or any multiple endings... this 4th episode feels a bit thinner and dry. It puts some good basis for the next step in the story, though - actually, if they keep working on the sequel story wise, then they should have named this something else than 4, as it really feels like episode 1 of a new series, almost. Still, as a Room enthusiast, it had to be done, and it was pleasant!\t\t\t\t\t
