\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely incredible as always!

It's precisely what you want in a puzzlegame. The puzzles themselves range from simple to seriously headscratching, but never to the point where you have to look something up. The story is simple and easy to follow, yet incredibly eerie. The way you get fed the story through 2 bookpages per room is a adequate amount, just enough to follow what's going on without feeling burdened to read again. Along with visual storytelling between room animations.

The one and only complaint i have with this one compared to the others is how it used a lot of "go back to that previous room with this new item that you got, and once you get there and activate that one thing you get the next item to progress in the room you were just in". In short, i think the rooms feel the best when they have their own little sections that lets you find an object that you can use somewhere else in that SAME room. Jumping between them takes unnecessary time and feels tedious.

Other than that, absolutely brilliant, 9/10 Can't wait for a 5th installment. Cheers!\t\t\t\t\t
