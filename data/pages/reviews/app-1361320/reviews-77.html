\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYes I recommend it, it's a The Room game as good as all the 3 previous ones. This one made it more engaging by introducing new rooms just as you feel you're almost done with the previous one, compelling you to start the new one without ever getting tired of the game.

The story aspect of it was very underwhelming (SPOILER AHEAD): Woman good, man bad, woman smart, man dumb, man gets f***ed, woman fools everyone. (END SPOILER)

I don't hate this trope, it's just that has become very common everywhere in media, and it makes everything so predictable, thus not interesting. I already knew where they were going with this from the start. They could have had a little more faith in both genders, the man to redeem himself, and the woman to be more heroic to save her husband, as a man would've done.\t\t\t\t\t
