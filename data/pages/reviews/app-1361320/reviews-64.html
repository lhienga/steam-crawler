\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs expected, loved the game. The story is vague but intriguing once you start piecing the games together. The puzzles are logical, and there's something to be said about being involved in the literal mechanics. I did encounter one glitch with the dragon jaws not closing all the way. Fireproof has a roundabout fix posted on their website: Pause the game, click on the "Game Paused" text 5 times. A new menu will pop up with save spots after opening each room. This was the only way I was able to reset the glitch without starting over. As with any save point, you're loading from an earlier spot, so you will have to replay a little.

My biggest disappointment is the next game is built for VR :(. Amazing concept, and I'd undoubtedly sell my my left arm for an immersive escape room like that, especially from Fireproof (not a lot of real life escape rooms can be done by one person). Alas it's not in the cards for me. I can't afford the platform. Hopefully they'll continue to make games for PC, so us lay folk can still enjoy their content :).\t\t\t\t\t
