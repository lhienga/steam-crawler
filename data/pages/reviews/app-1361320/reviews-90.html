\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy puzzle games, room escapes, and this is basically the best I have ever played. Beautiful ominous music in the background of very interesting well designed rooms with intricate interconnecting puzzles that had me taking mental notes to come back to. The actual design of the mechanical intricacies to make it all feel real, was just so satisfying to watch. Also, for anyone playing, don't be afraid to look at the first hints. They normally didn't tell me things I didn't figure out yet, but maybe reminded me of something I didn't remember or that I needed to take a second look at. Makes me wonder if I should buy the rest. Definitely recommend to patient people who love to see a puzzle come together, and come to life.\t\t\t\t\t
