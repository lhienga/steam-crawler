I have to say, this is the best the of the franchise; not counting the VR versiong cuz, you know, eating every day for a year or having a VR headset, and sadly since even "eating every day" is out of my budget, imagine the headset.

With that said, this one is the better one. Third was good, first and second were... experimental, but this, the simple idea of the compounding effects of all being one place with the main protagonist basically not really displacing at all from this one attic feels the most compelling, no space-time wizarding do-dads, instead of going out, we go in. If this was an action franchise I'd hope we'd see a Mausser C96 but ey, you win an excellent franchise of puzzle games, you lose on action using older weapons.
