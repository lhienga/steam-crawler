\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Room 4 gives all that you enjoyed from the first three games. While the plot isn't the continuation of the silent protagonist, you'll investigate one of the recurring characters in the game. Although the small twists were delightful, it didn't move the narrative forward. Those who enjoy the story will find it good but somewhat unfulfilling.

The puzzles are greatly refined with a lot of backtracking. Through the use of a doll house, players can go to each room quickly allowing them to find pieces and clues in other rooms without the annoying traversal time. The Room 4 is a bit shorter than the 3rd game and without the epic ending. It's still enjoyable especially if you want to scratch that itch.\t\t\t\t\t
