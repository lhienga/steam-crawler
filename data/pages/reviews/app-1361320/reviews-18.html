\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who has played all of The Room games, I feel like this is the weakest one. What I like about the other ones is that it feels like you are unwrapping the puzzle getting deeper into it. With this one it felt like I was just moving around pieces to different areas of the puzzle. For the puzzle solving to much of it was totally obvious just by looking at the object. Since it is so obvious what you just end up waiting for piece you needed to unlock so you could use it since game. It just feels like it is holding you back since it is very linear with when you can solve a puzzle, only allowing you to solve maybe one or two sections of the puzzle before letting you move on. This wouldn't be a problem for a single puzzle box in the game but it does it for the rooms making you jump back and forth to the other rooms to piece by piece solve puzzles. Moving out of the rooms was a bit tedious and I didn't like all the backtracking. This one did have more of a story going for it which is nice addition to the game.

If you haven't played the previous ones get those first, they are better. If you have played the previous ones and liked them, you will likely enjoy this one too, but don't expect an improvement over older titles. Overall its good but I am going to not recommend this one because if feels like a step back in puzzle quality.\t\t\t\t\t
