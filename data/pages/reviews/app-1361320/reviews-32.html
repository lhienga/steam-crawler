\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Room 4 is very good. I particularly liked how different components would help you unlock more areas of the Lockwood manor rather than it being a linear experience where the puzzles are left to the individual chapters. A stand out example is the use of the water system and how it was used to power one of the rooms through steam, to use the water to further unlock the kitchen and then to use that pipe to help with the Garden area, it was extremely well done. The story was also extremely well told. I really liked that as you unlocked more of the areas you found out more of what happened between the married couple that was there at the manor. The one criticism I have is I wish the developers encouraged using items between different areas even more, so that you have to bounce between the different areas, further adding to the mental challenge of the puzzle solving. Overall I highly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
