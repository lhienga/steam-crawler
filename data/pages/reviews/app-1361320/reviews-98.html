\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnother amazing game from Fireproof. I've played every one in the series, including the VR experience, and they are all outstanding puzzle games. The kind that I come back to years later after I've forgotten how to solve things so I can experience it all over again. I don't think there are any games comparable to this series. The way the puzzles connect from room to room. The unique key shapes, beautiful room decor, and interesting puzzles make for an enjoyable experience. Just enough plot to be interesting without being overwhelming.

My favorite room in the game had to be the Japanese Gallery, although all of them are pretty cool. The puzzles aren't so difficult that they are impossible to solve, and there are usually subtle hints within the game, not the hint tool itself, that make figuring things out a doable challenge. The game flows pretty well, and only took a few hours to complete in one sitting.

To any fans of puzzle games or puzzle boxes, this is a must play. Highly recommend it to those looking for a moderate challenge. Thankfully no riddles. 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
