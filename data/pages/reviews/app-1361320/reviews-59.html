\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe more I think about whether this game is good, the less I can reccomend it.

The storyline felt weak, it's there, but felt more like an afterthought.

The "puzzles" felt more like a "where's waldo" than actually solving puzzles. I beat the game in 4 and a half hours in a single livestreaming session without using any hints except for 1. Which wasted me 15 minutes of time because I overlooked a hidden thing somewhere. Again, "where's waldo".

If you do consider this game to have "puzzles" they are extremely easy. And most puzzles were even solved before you could solve them because the missing "key" is just hidden in a different area.

The atmosphere in the game is pretty great! game looks pretty as you'd expect from the series. But it did more to hinder gameplay than support it. making me feel as if the puzzling was nothing more than an after-thought when they were developing this game.

The room 1,2 and 3 were great.
The room 4 felt like it's a filler.
If you want more of the room, this game will work, but it won't scratch the same itch. If the other games were a 9/10. Then this game is a 6/10.
If this was a standalone game, I'd reccomend it. But as a sequel, it pales in comparison with its earlier games.\t\t\t\t\t
