\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOut of the 4 The Room games I've played, this one wasn't the most difficult, that would be the third, but this game presented five and a half hours worth of interesting puzzles contained in an environment appropriate to the current month of Halloween.

If you want an easy boost to your achievement percentage, all of the games I've played in this series are really easy to complete since you're basically getting an achievement at the end of each major section, regardless of whether or not you used hints.

My only major issue with this game (and the series in general) come from a handful of puzzles that required me to notice things that were really difficult to notice. Not quite pixel hunting, but it was close at times. The paper fan puzzle in the Japanese Garden gave me the most trouble, but it wasn't a hurdle that kept me stuck for very long.

I strongly recommend this game, and the other 3 The Room games. I don't have any kind of VR set up, but I'm aware there is a VR Room game if you're into that kind of thing.\t\t\t\t\t
