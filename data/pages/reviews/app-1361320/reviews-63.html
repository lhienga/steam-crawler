Short Game Description
The Room 4: Old Sins, is the last masterpiece of the franchise, bringing us great storytelling at the same time as new puzzles on this escape room.
  • Positives
    Discussing the positives, first of all, the game's dark ambience fitted really well to the storytelling, achieving a slow-paced story at the same time as interesting. Secondly, the whole escape room felt very well interconnected, being fairly difficult and quite unique, bringing a refreshing experience. Thirdly, like the previous games, the artistical design, it's flawless, just astonishing. Additionally, the soundtrack is very fine as well.
  • Negatives
    The unique flaw, could be that it would be great if it were a bit longer.
Conclusion
Summarizing, The Room 4: Old Sins is the best game in the franchise, with well-interconnected scenarios with a really well-written and slow paced story, that still maintains that great unique artistic style. The game is an 8.75/10.\t\t\t\t\t
