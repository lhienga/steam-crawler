A key in one room unlocking a lock in another is not typical of the experience the game usually brings. It was tedious to get a new component, realize its for another room, back out, put on the eye glass, rotate the doll house, find the room, click into the room, zoom in to where the component goes, rinse repeat. To their credit they closed off "exhausted" rooms.

It also felt like the pacing was off between the first few rooms and the last. LIke there were fewer details and puzzles, it almost feels rushed towards the end.

All in all, decent game for the genre, good chill relaxing time killer.\t\t\t\t\t
