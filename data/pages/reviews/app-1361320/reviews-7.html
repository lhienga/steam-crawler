They are never so difficult that you have to rely on hints or walkthroughs (though a built in hint system is available should you get lost) nor are they so easy that there is no challenge.
Graphically it looks polished, the sounds are fitting. Can't say I have experienced any bugs. The underlying story is interesting though usually I was most looking forward to unlocking the next puzzle than the story.
All in all, if you like puzzle games or escape room type games then I think you will have a good time with this one as well.
The only negative point I would give is that it may be a bit on the short side at around 5 to 7 hours.\t\t\t\t\t
