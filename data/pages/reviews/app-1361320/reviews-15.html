\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cant say enough good things about The Room 4,

Graphics - seamless and crisp, especially when you go into an ornament with the aid of the eye piece, so good

Puzzles - they are hard enough to be frustrating (at times) but not hard enough to make the game impossible to finish, you just need patience and to think outside of the box (or room)

Overall game play is top notch, if your a fan of puzzle games, this game is a definite MUST, hints come in at just the right time (when frustration levels get high - somehow the game knows) to nudge you in the right direction, but not so much as to wreck the game by making it too easy.

I honestly cant say enough good things about this game, play it and prove me wrong.\t\t\t\t\t
