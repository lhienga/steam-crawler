The game has multi step hints, so you can take the first hint to get pointed in the right direction, before using the solution itself.
This game does little things different. There are no different places and the variety of riddles seem to have increased again.
Graphics are great, sound is atmospheric. I struggled with right mouse button, but that may be because of games that use this button for zooming in.

I'm looking forward to the next installment.\t\t\t\t\t
