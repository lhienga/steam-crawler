Unlike in the previous games, this one isn't just solving one puzzle room at a time but solving puzzles in one room to unlock another piece of a puzzle in a second room which is the key to solving a puzzle in yet another room you opened a while ago. It makes you take your time and really think about how the puzzle rooms connect to one another and encourages you to pay close attention each area. This game also contains my favorite narrative of the series and the cinematic at the end is really cool. I hope to see them doing more cut scenes in future games.\t\t\t\t\t
