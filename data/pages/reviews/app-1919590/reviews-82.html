This game gets a 2/10 for the PC version.

To start with the obvious issues and downsides based on the actual game itself:

- The game is capped at 60 FPS in multiplayer
- The game has the absolute worst input lag of any multiplayer game you will ever touch; it feels like at least 100 MS lag.
- The game is stuck in old gen, so PC players with even decent PC builds aren't allowed to play the best version of 2K
- The Game is pay to-win; either you grind for 300+ hours for VC or pay $100 and build your player or MyTeam
- The Game will randomly crash every so often or lose connection because it's not made for PC
- Game is filled with cheaters, and I mean FILLED
- MyTeam is nothing but money-hungry overpriced packs filled with 99 overall players.

Now here are some upsides and the reason I Played/Play it:

- Offline mode My League is solid; you can run your own league run simulations (for the nerds) and customize a lot
- The game receives player overall updates every 2 weeks up until playoffs and will continue to in 2K24
- The game-play itself can be fun in offline mode but is pretty mid online
- If playing online, don't expect anything close to realism with how the basketball is played. It's just constant dribble spam.
- Fun to bring friends over and play offline 1 on 1 or 5 on 5.

TO SUM IT UP:
- Offline mode 6/10
- Online mode 3/10

This game would be an 8+/10 if:
- PC got treated like NEW GEN, as we should
- Remove Input lag
- Remove the FPS cap online
- Remove pay to win
- Game-play less cheesy (more realistic)\t\t\t\t\t
