I'm confident that my character build is still intact. So at least there is that. I really wish that bigger titles like this did more to release a functioning product they can maintain versus meeting some weak, predetermined release date. If you're gonna buy the game, wait for a good sale. Keep your backups up to date. And, simply understand, even though it has been tightened up mechanically.
