\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou start off rough with nothing but as you get good with learning how to escape, throw stuff at and juke/slow down killers. Combine this with objectives to do for each mission and finding resources. Its random as to how the levels will play out every time you start them. While it is fun, there are some technical issues that can sometimes get in the way, I think its a fun co-op horror game that is very enjoyable despite being repetitive.

From my experience, once you have a good build going you will do well in the game. You'd have to go through co-op to get more XP and stages completed. Complete with unlocking all of your good amps, tier upgrades, rigs and upgrades for those rigs. I recommend unlocking stamina and exhaustion upgrades ASAP. As for rigs, I recommend unlocking stun, blind and healer rigs and upgrading those to the max. As a good starter rig, I suggest healer, because you can keep yourself alive and your sanity full. You can do the same for your teammates. In this case, if you can help yourself; you can help others.

The key factor here is that this game a tedious Co-op play builds you for solo play. When you have good cards that you know how to play well; you will accomplish a lot. Also always explore the maps, look for items and a secret that be be unlocked (this is for co-op & solo).

Overall I recommend this game. I like it way better than Dead By Daylight.\t\t\t\t\t
