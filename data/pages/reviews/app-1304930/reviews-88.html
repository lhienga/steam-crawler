\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start by saying that this game is fun, only because I have friends to play with, i have not once played this and went, "I can't wait to play this by myself or with randoms" because anyone can agree a game with friends makes it more fun, that's why the industry has exploded in games you play with other people with, because you get more sales and people generally end up liking the product more.

This game lacks in a lot of areas, I think for starters the A.I is somehow less intelligent than a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ zombie, you turn around a corner and they lose you instantly, the enemies aren't even scary for a horror game and I never feel like I'm at risk I am just more annoyed at how animation based the entire mechanics are because this guy will swing at me and his hit box goes across the map and since you are in the animation you are forced to get hit despite you being far enough away from the attack that it shouldn't have hit. That COULD be a tick-rate issue but I still need to play some more to for sure rule it out, regardless any game with animation based movements and attacks are always going to be a problem when syncing to other computers on the servers.

I think the item management is a drag and could be better, like for example dropping and item, switching an item are all clunky although it is helpful when you can give a player an item, dropping and item or switching it out is pretty annoying especially when the item just drops to the floor and then you have to angle your cross hair right to where it is on the ground to pick the item up and sometimes it goes to where the item you picked up instead, I do not know if its dependent on if you were moving or its just a bug.

I think they need to have more enemy variety as well, its the same 5 types of enemies for every trial, big guy, girl with puppet, guy saying some schizo ♥♥♥♥ with a machete, guy who screams really loud, and scarecrow.

I hope that when this game comes out of early access after they stop getting concurrent players (i would say like 200 people or something) theyll take it out of early access to get hype or something and then the player base drops again. So I hope red barrels adds more meaningful changes instead of just pulling a call of duty and adding a bunch of bloat cosmetics and bare-bones "trials" that have the same 5 enemy types to drive up the file size. I would (as someone who wants this game to do good) like it if they improve the A.I. to be a lot more interesting and unique to one another and their own behaviors and interactions, make the trials more interesting, and make the game feel more rewarding.\t\t\t\t\t
