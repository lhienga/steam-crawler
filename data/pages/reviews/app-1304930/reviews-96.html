\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're looking for a good time with friends, this game can provide it. Simplistic in nature, it has just enough depth to it to make it challenging and enjoyable. The thing is, DO NOT come to this game expecting a normal Outlast experience. You are not going to have a good time playing solo. You are not getting some huge story driven narrative. It is a set of "trials" that basically equate to 20 minute matches of completing certain objectives with team mates and trying not to get killed. You're not going to be dealing with a huge over lying story line and you are going to STRUGGLE if you aren't playing with friends or at least other players in these lobbies you will be put into.

TL;DR
Game is a great multiplayer experience with little story and only shares the Outlast name and mechanics.\t\t\t\t\t

The game is decently fun with friends when you try to solve puzzles together as a squad. However, enemies usually camp objectives or have BS spawns and that turns it into a really frustrating and unfair experience. The game in it's current stage also lacks replayability, as such it forces you to replay levels you have completed in a harder difficulty. I've completed the game and 'freed' my character and see no point in playing the game any further.

All in all, the game has potential but can be really tedious to sit through. If you're looking to purchase the game, I would recommend you wait for it to be fully released or wait for a big sale to justify the purchase. I'm looking forward to new levels from the developers and I hope they'll improve the replayability of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
