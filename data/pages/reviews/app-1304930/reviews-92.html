The gameplay itself is a amazing, keeping me engaged and excited, ensuring that boredom remains a distant thought. The game has minimal bugs as well which is a welcome change with how alot of games have been recently.

However, the challenge lies in connecting with other players, particularly for specific trails. it takes ages and alot of the time will fail due to many different reasons and when you get into a lobby with a team the host suddenly changes to a completely different trail.

another issue is some players skill levels, even at higher levels, who seem content with staying in the background, leaving the heavy lifting to others. Unfortunately, the game fails to adequately reward those who actively contribute to completing objectives, reviving teammates, keeping people alive with rigs ect, a feature that could significantly enhance the overall gaming experience.

Overall, Outlast Trails shines when played with friends especially is a great game and i cant wait for the full game release, which i hope has more trails to explore with new enemies and challenges\t\t\t\t\t
