Oh boy…The new update is such a disappointment

I've been playing Outlast Trials since pretty much the beginning (early access) with a friend, and with this full release update, I’m so unbelievably annoyed. Program Genesis takes away everything that was fun and challenging about the game. It’s gone from stealth and strategy to a walking simulator where I don’t have to even use my amps or brain to get thought the new programs. I can run basically forever, most the enemies are removed (I hate that I actually miss the ugly bastards) from where they used to be and taking damage isn’t something I’m actively avoiding, because it isn’t consequential anymore. The boss enemies do so little damage now, it’s more of a minor inconvenience, like a mosquito, whereas before it was actually a problem you had to make a conscious effort to avoid.

Just to give you an idea of what skill level I’m at when it comes to gaming: I am not great at playing video games that involve any kind of directional ability or fighting enemies, I’m more of a visual novel kind of person, yet me and my friend managed, after much screaming and shouting, to succeed after a few tries on the hardest difficulty, level 5.

Speaking of the difficulties, they used to gradually get harder in each program (Program 1: Police station, Program 2: Fun Park, Program 3: Orphanage and the 4th Program within the court house which was released a bit before this final update), whereas now the difficulty goes form easy (easier than the easiest level in early access) in Program Genesis, to level three and one random level 5 in Program X and then the very hard in Program Ultra. There is no gradient anymore to get you acclimatized as you level up to difficulty 5.

On top of that certain trials now don’t have more than one difficulty level outside of the easiest level in Program Genesis. Program Genesis is all 4 of the old programs shoved into one and made short and easy. It’s not worth replaying, because compared to what the pre-full-release players were facing this is a walk in the park, a whole piece of cake.

Not even the weekly Program is as hard as any of the old programs that had level 5 difficulty trials. I just finished playing Program: Be Quiet and it was pretty easy. I mean I did get hit, but the fact that I only died once says a lot. Less enemies, less tasks, for instance where originally one had to fire up two generators in kill the snitch, now you only do one. Basically, the new level 5 feels like a level 4 or even a hard level 3.

It would be much better to create a separate program for beginner players and bring the old programs back. I hate these new weird programs.

I never thought I’d be missing the old programs but here we are.
