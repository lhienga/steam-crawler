\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best stealth horror game I've yet to play.

Solid stealth mechanics, amazing environments and atmosphere. Some minor build variety that gives some fun tools to use during gameplay. The atmosphere is meant to be disturbing, so be ready for giant penis man in your face. If you've played the single player Outlast games, then you know what to expect as far as disturbing imagery.

My only complaint would be build depth is lacking. While the mechanics are fun, they are not deep which hurts replayability. The maps also have a type of Warframe generation system where there are RNG pathing on how to get to objectives. All in all, I got a solid 50 - 70 hours before I played everything and then started playing just for the game and atmosphere.

They raised the price to $40 which I do think is just barely reasonable. This is a high quality product, but just falls short of having the seemingly endless replayability like Deep Rock Galactic.

If stealth and horror is something you want to try, then this is a solid buy.\t\t\t\t\t
