\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun unless you're trying to play solo. Interesting themes, and actually very well optimised (which is surprisingly a feat by today's standards when it comes to video games). I'm struggling to find any downside to the game aside from the semi-repetitive gameplay that pretty much only comes from the lack of content considering the amount of time that has been put into the development of the game, nevertheless a very enjoyable experience for just about anybody the first time around.

The two main villains of the game, Gooseberry and Coyle are well-designed characters that stand out, the downside being that they are pretty much the only villains in the game as of writing this, along with a few regular enemies that appear in most levels.

Plenty of difficulties that increase the amount of traps that are spread around the levels, more enemies, more damage, etc. that ensures that you'll face a challenge no matter what your skill level is, which is great to me as someone who enjoys a challenge.

If you're looking for a co-op game to play with your friends for a few sessions, there is no other title I would recommend more than this one.
