Yes, buy it (only if you have friends to play with)

Long Review:
Outlast Trials is a fun and intense horror multiplayer game, at first it's like creepy level of scary; however it later on turns into a survival challenge game where you basically try to get a good rank and complete objectives without getting hit every damn second.
This game with friends is a 100% recommended and should be a def buy if you and your friends like horror or intense games. The only downside is that they should add like more things to do after you finish almost everything but it is a nitpick because I have like almost 17 hours and I didn't rebirth yet. Last thing to keep in mind is that it still would be fun solo, but it's gonna be insanely hard (it's already hard enough while playing with idiotic friends).
