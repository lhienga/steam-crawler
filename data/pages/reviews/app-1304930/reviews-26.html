Being a exclusive multiplayer game it takes out the immersiveness, even if you play solo you can still obviously see how the game is intended for more players. If none of your friends have 20-30 bucks to spare to play with you, you can choose to play with real life players that act a lot like a broken ai. It's more of a 50/50. Theres very nice people that know how to communicate and help the team, then theres others who speak a language 80% of players don't understand. And bad team communication. Thats the troubles of online games.

But on the other side heres what i think is good about the game

The level design is stunning and has so many interesting props and things to look at
The lore is quite interesting if you look into it, but unfortunately thats not what most care about in this game
they care about the grades the coins and cosmetics more than a story you can piece together by finding files around the different maps.
The multiplayer IS a fun way to go through these horror thrilled trials with your friends or "new" friends
The different playstyles also brings fun into it. Go sneaky, go running for your life and getting it done as fast as you can, go intel collecting.
The ai has been improoved since the early access so a lot of the trials are A LOT easier than what was before for some a good thing for some a bad thing. Then theres of course the weekly challenges and program X that spice the difficulty up so dont worry about being challenged :)
It can be dreadful at times and thats good since it is a horror game!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe foundation of the game is strong with a solid ambience that contributes to the overall horror elements of the game. For the new player, it is one hell of an experience that had me scared enough to mute my friends on a discord call.

I just despise the way they went about adding smaller missions. Crappy small segment maps with not much thought put into it. Didn't really commit to the theme of the main missions they follow, and really just seemed like a way for developers to boast about adding new "maps" and "content" to the game.

It was most certainly fun at first, but as you level up, the game just gets easier removing elements of "team-play" as you essentially become self-sufficient enough to just blitz through most if not all objectives. Wait for a sale or buy it while 3 other friends are just getting into it to get the most out of it, otherwise, don't bother.\t\t\t\t\t
