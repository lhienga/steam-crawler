Don't get me wrong i love the game but it needs to be improved significantly.

First and main thing is the difficulty is way too hard when playing solo. I would say a good 80% of trial times are spent hiding and fleeing from enemies and sure its a rush being chased and scared but it makes progression and overall enjoyment pretty average. I enjoy playing games in singleplayer.

I'm not sure if developers read these reviews but a few suggestions for solo would be either less enemies in the trials or at least an adjustable difficulty level because i do not enjoy spending 45 minutes hiding in a closet and basically jumping from hiding spot to hiding spot.

I am recommending the game to people mainly because despite the difficulty involved with solo it is a well developed game and has little to no bugs from what i have experienced.
