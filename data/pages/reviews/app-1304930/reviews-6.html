I love a lot about this game, the vibe is cool, the audio is great, the graphics and performance are snazzy & snappy, but it falls short in the content and challenge department for our group.

After you complete the main missions and get enough tokens to leave, you're released. You get to make another character or remake your old one, you get a new outfit and some new skills, but that's about it. At first, it seems like there's a lot of replay-ability with randomized item and hiding spot spawns and the whole rebirth thing, but that gets cleared up really fast.

The weekly challenges are just the main missions and challenges, reworked with new modifiers, which is perfectly fine! BUT, there's so little content it's ridiculous. Took my group of 3 about 18 hours to do all the base missions and the trial that leads to your release. We were so underwhelmed by the ending and how short it was that we couldn't bring ourselves to play any more.

This is one of those "so much potential" games, but this one has a decent company behind it so it may actually go somewhere.

Also don't play with randoms, they suck and don't understand the concept of sneaking. Constant braindead C's and below from people running right into enemies and over glass lmfao\t\t\t\t\t
