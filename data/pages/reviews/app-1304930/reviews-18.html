\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved Outlast one and two, they are horror games that are actually scary and didn't need jumpscares to actually frighten someone. So i was excited when the third instalment came out.

Btw. this all just my personla opinion. And also im sorry for the spelling mistakes.

Story 10/10
Read Barrels did an amazing job with this one. It is really cool to see the backstory of the shady murkoff corporation and how everything ties with the original outlast. But it's also the smaller stories of the reagents, Dr.Easterman, the disturbed Mother Gooseberry or the sadistic Leland Coyle.

Atmosphere 9/10
Everything feels wrong, but in a good way. There's blood everywhere and the entire level is a run-down, nightmarish version of real places like a police station. The goals are usually pretty messed up and Murkoff forces you to do things you don't really want to do. Then there are the enemies and oh boy, like the other games these aren't the stereotypical "Oh I'm crazy" enemies. They all behave differently, talk differently and feel differently and you really get the feeling that their mind is already over the edge. The only problem is that once this shock is gone, it kinda feels dull.

Gameplay 8/10
The game feels... fine. Movement isn't as clunky as the previous games, and i don't know how good that is(i tell you what i mean in the last section). The rest is good, stealth feels fine and the objectives are usually clever minigames you have to do while being hunted, looking for something or activate a mechanism. So overall its quite good.

Coop 9/10
The game is extremely fun with friends. I have a ton of fond memories like blocking the door in my buddy's face while he is being hunted and him starting to swear at me, or seeing my friend running past me screaming at me to run, and the next moment some crazy woman with a hand puppet comes around the corner. In the hub area there are also activities such as speed-chess or arm wrestling, which can lead to funny situations even with complete strangers.
There was a time when my buddy and I started saying random nonsense while chasing a guy and suddenly we created some kind of cult around hunting this one person (don't worry he had fun to).

Horror/Dificulty 3/10
This horror game is missing one big thing: the horror.
First i thought it is the coop (something i predicted at the beginning) but even when i play the game solo there was never a moment where i was scared. That has multiple reasons.
1.The enemies are braindead, deaf and blind. You get spoted? Just run into the next dark spot and duck, thats it, they lose interest in three seconds.
2. To fast to smooth. Like i said you are to fast and to athletic, especially after the upgrades.
In outlast one and two it took some time to start running while you are ducking and you had very little stamine or you could not slide. Combine that with the very small rooms and and layout you don't even have to hide in a room sometimes. Just run behind a corner as soon as they spot you and the chase is over before it even begun.
3. Items. Items aren' bad, but with something like bootles ore a stun- granade you can litterly bully your foes. There are also hundreds of healing bottles. Plus you have WAY to much batarie power for your night vision.
4. Genneral vibe. It all doesn't feel thretening anymore, i don't know how to explain it. Yes it is more disturbing but even that feels forced and is, like i said, gone quickly. In Outlast one and two it all felt natural, that was what made it so scary and efficient for me.
5.at the end i have to say its not scary and to easy, like since 1.0 reset for the missions, the worst ratting i got was a A-. I even went back to outlast two and was beaten very often at the beginning because i played like outlast trials.

Finnal Opinion
The Game is very fun especially with friends. It is defenetly worth the money and i promise you you will gain a lot of fun memories that you can look back to. Just don't go in there if you want to have a the same scary and hounting experience like in previous games.\t\t\t\t\t
