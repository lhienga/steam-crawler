At times it truly scared me out of my skin and put me under pressure, made me panic & my heart race - something I haven't ever felt in a game before. The environments created in this game are astoundingly detailed and well thought out. The game mechanics are interesting and as you progress and unlock more it allows you to try and play in many different ways. Finding evidence and slowly uncovering stories and backgrounds of the evils in this game are wonderfully scattered throughout the programs and you slowly learn more, making you want to seek more and more information. I would recommend this 10/10, even if you aren't a horror game player you can team up with other players or friends (I recommend friends & starting together - it is a great experience as you figure out new ways to complete the programs & eventually get better and better) I will continue to keep coming back for more as weeklies get refreshed and new challenges get added. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tTense and Immersive. The difficulty curve is at perfect pace when getting into this horror game, because first timers will spend more time being super careful and sneaky until they are comfortable with the gameplay; and the game provides a difficulty to reflect that. Once you understand how the game works, understand the trials, understand the programs, understand item and rig management, build more skills, the more difficult trials are an amazing progression to keep it at your level. There is plenty of content here (plus weekly trials) to have something to progress through and see new things. The release protocol is like PAYDAY 2 infamy, unlocking new content, keeping your character progression but resetting your trial progression to be done again. Each release protocol completion gives you a new ending for your character when they leave the trials.

The gameplay is you actively completing objectives to escape the trial while warding off threats and using night-vision in dark places. You will be running away, hiding, throwing bricks and bottles, using rigs, sneaking around, creating distractions, and locking doors to slow or redirect pursuers. Objectives will encourage you to move towards dangers to progress, and the game will make you face you foes. Each trial is different in which map you play on, with programs determining your objectives, your challenges and your enemies. Always be ready to run and hide, but most importantly have something to get you out of a pinch.

This game is even more fun with friends and randoms. All work together to help each other avoid threats, use better shortcuts with two or more teammates for fast progression, and save others in the nick of time. Everything is against you, but your friends are there to give you a helping hand.

Even if I have seen everything in this game and not scared anymore, the skinnerman always makes me jump in my seat. So, never runs out on the spooks.

Highly recommend, even if you buy it just for yourself to play (I play it mainly solo)\t\t\t\t\t
