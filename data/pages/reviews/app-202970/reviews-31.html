Zombies: This is a weird one and I have a lot to say. It's a lot of fun, however for pro players. You'll be let down in the difficulty department. In bo1 the problem is the gun's are terrible. You can't get anything done quickly and high rounds are hard. Not for lack of skill but just the gun's don't do enough damage so it takes forever to kill the zombies. Even the majority of the wounder weapons are bad. Though the maps are amazing and a lot of fun. In this game the problem is the maps. The truth is you have one to two good maps. The rest are either not good or don't offer enough to be enjoyed. For the bad ones I'm talking about Tranzit, Buried, Nuketown, Die rise, farm/bus depot in green run along with Turned and Grief. Tranzit has a long list of problems but the big offenders are the lava and the fog. The fog was for coding reasons but the lava? Yeah that's just annoying. The wounder weapon, the jet gun, breaks after 5 seconds of use and you have to rebuild it again when it breaks. Shall I say more? Buried isn't big enough and Leroy is the opposite of George Remaro from Call of the dead but he's still awful. Yet you need him to get anything done even if you have the Paralyzer which is broken and makes the map not fun. Die Rise is just falling to your death. Though I love Die Rise, I can't ignore the fact that the map isn't designed well. For starters you don't need to ever go straight at the start. Everyone goes down the elevator to get to power. After you build the tramplsteam on round one, the map is a joke. The wounder weapon is just another way to get you killed after the nerf it got in 2013 and nothing more. Turned just doesn't work... enough said. Grief is bad for the maps its available on. God forbid it could be on maps with more space for 8 players, that's if you can even get 8 people in a game to begin with. Nuketown is too limited and the perk system is bad. If your luck is like mine. You'll never see pack a punch on a early round or jug for that matter. I've played that map 100 times and I've seen pack a punch on like round 15 at least on 95 of those games.

I know this seems like a lot of bad stuff but this is my unbiased review. If I list the good I'd be writing a book. The game is still amazing and more fun than all of the Call of Duties that came after it aside from bo3 in my opinion even to this day. If your looking to play for even the zombies alone. I'd go for it. This game may get a remaster in the future and if it does. Let's hope its not trash.\t\t\t\t\t
