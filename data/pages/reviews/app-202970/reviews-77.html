This is was my first ever experience with call of duty, driving an ASD and flying drones in a cousin's house around 2013.
I was always loyal to the Modern Warfare side of Call of Duty, but to be honest, after replaying the story to get every ending as well as playing Zombies, I can definietly say that it's up there with Modern Warfare 2 when it comes to great features. The story is a 10/10, of course if you somehow was able to complete it in one day while being focused, you can predict some stuff, but it's not a bad thing for me. Graphics basically went from mid 2000s to mid 2010s. The gameplay is just so good, I wish they remastered it with an anticheat.\t\t\t\t\t
