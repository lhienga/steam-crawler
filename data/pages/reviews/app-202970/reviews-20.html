\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is worse than all 3 modern warfares, game play wise maybe marginally better than BO1 which i wasn't a huge fan of in that department. The story is ok and the game play is tired.

After playing a few of the cod games in order i can definitely feel the fatigue that the original fans felt. This game was not fun, it was a chore to play. I played on hardened and maybe normal difficulty would have been more enjoyable. Every corner felt like a one hit trap. every section felt too long, problems that i felt in World at War and BO1 were present and perhaps even worse, the series feels like it isn't evolving at all and the modern knowledge i know it's not going to improve.

Also whoever thought strike force was a good idea is a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ idiot.

Good entertaining story, fun cut-scenes, ♥♥♥♥♥♥ game-play/damage scaling.
Objectively probably not a bad quality game, but my personal experience was miserable.

3/10.
