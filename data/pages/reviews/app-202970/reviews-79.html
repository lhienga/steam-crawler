\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBlack Ops 2 is an intense, action-packed game that takes players on a thrilling journey through a nonlinear storyline that spans decades. The game's multiple-choice campaign puts players in the driver's seat, allowing them to shape the story as they see fit.

The game's futuristic setting and weaponry breathe new life into the series, while the multiplayer mode provides hours of fun and endless replayability. Black Ops 2 also features a robust Zombies mode, which has become a staple of the series (although TranZit is a very controversial map to this day).

However, the game is not without its flaws. The campaign can be confusing at times, and the pacing can be uneven. One example of a confusing part of the Black Ops 2 campaign is the branching storyline that can be difficult to follow on the first playthrough. The game allows the player to make choices that affect the story's outcome, but these choices are not always clear or easy to understand. The player may not realize the impact of their choices until later in the game, which can make it difficult to keep track of the different storylines and how they relate to one another. Additionally, the game's nonlinear structure can be confusing, as it jumps between different time periods and locations without clear transitions or explanations.

The multiplayer can also feel imbalanced at times, and the spawn system can be frustrating. Despite these issues, Black Ops 2 is a worthy addition to the Call of Duty franchise and is sure to provide hours of entertainment for fans of the series.\t\t\t\t\t
