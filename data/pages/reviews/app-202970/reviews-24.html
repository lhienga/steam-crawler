\r\n\t\t\t\t\tin my opinion this is the best call of duty game in the franchise

i played 12 hours in 2 days when i first got it, and that was campaign only. the multiplayer is very good and this game improves upon bo1 in virtually every way possible. the campaign has to be the best in the series with fantastic storytelling, great characters, multiple endings, having your choices throughout the game affect what happens, the ability to create a class before each mission, all the fun little hidden features and overall being very fun to play.

the multiplayer is fantastic, with great maps and weapons, which are fun to use. scorestreaks are fun to use and the pick 10 system works quite well. no complaints here. unfortunately, all my praise of the mp goes out the window due to the fact that this game (and any other cod before bo3) are not safe to play online in public lobbies on pc. hackers can steal your information, passwords, rat your computer and more. activision is doing nothing to fix this, if anything they are making it worse by taking down ways to safely play them. i recommend either playing in private lobbies with bots or your friends, or using something like plutonium

i am not much of a zombies guy so i cant really speak too much on the zombies, but it does seem to be quite good

the price of this game is ridiculous. it is an 11 year old game that is being sold for 60 usd. there is absolutely no excuse for this insane price for an old game other than activisions greed and lack of care for their playerbase. i would only buy this game when it is on sale as there is no justifying spending this much money on something that isnt even safe to play online. im not even going to bother asking activision to lower the price because its painfully obvious they dont care about their players. it is very sad because this is my favourite cod of all time, with the best campaign in the series and great multiplayer. if activision made this game safe to play and lowered the price (and removed the assault shield), this game would quite literally be perfect in my eyes. it really pains me to give this a not recommended but i simply cannot justify the price and the fact its not even safe to play online.

tldr: great campaign, great multiplayer which is unfortunately not safe to play in public lobbies, ridiculous price for an 11 year old game\t\t\t\t\t
