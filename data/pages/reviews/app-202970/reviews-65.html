\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy personal favorite of the "Call Of Duty: Black Ops" universe, set in the 21st century it takes the "Black Ops" IP to a whole new level on both gameplay and narrative wise. I adore this game so much because of it's well written story with a variety of choices during the story which can result in multiple different endings. It's crazy just how many tiny things factor into an ending in this game. I love all of the environments that we are thrown into in this COD title. Whether it's a moving island filled with beautiful stores and plaza's or a war torn Los Angeles, they are all sights to behold!

This game is so worth your time if you enjoy narrative driven first person shooters, it's such a fun ride! Easily the best title out of the "Black Ops" saga in my honest opinion. The multiplayer was very fun, although quite toxic (as per any COD title). The zombies was definitely something else! Tranzit is to this day such a mixed bag of emotions, some hate it, some love it, I don't mind it. It's an OK map filled with stuff to do. The rest of the stand alone maps (non-DLC) are so damn forgettable it's pitiful that they were even added. However the DLC in this game is awesome! Mob of the dead is such a great DLC, (RIP Ray LIotta) it is so eerie when you realize that the inmates are stuck in a purgatory state just endlessly being consumed by the undead as they try to escape Alcatraz island.

All in all, such a great COD title with so much lore and an unforgettable story paired with an exceptional soundtrack. Give this game a shot! You won't regret it.

P.S.- Don't ever kill Harper in ANY of your play-throughs, it's just not worth losing Michael Rooker!!\t\t\t\t\t
