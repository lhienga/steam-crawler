\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Its decent. A 6/10 if not familiar with the og game, an 8/10 if familiar. 50$ feels a bit steep for the price though, so maybe wait for a sale.

So far its pretty decent. About what I wanted out of this game, a pretty decent remake and update of the GCN game I put thousands of hours into.
I am quite pleased with the artstyle and aesthetic, Im also very pleased with the diversity and representation update too. Some have complained that it doesnt go far enough, I dont really have much thought on that, I'll take any victories, no matter how small.
I also really like finding the small changes that have taken place. The shortcut by the waterfall, thank you so much.

As far as criticisms go:
1. Personal Gripe: Still cant romance best girl Flora. It is disappointing, although now I just tend to think of her as Aro-Ace representation in game.
2. The movement feels a little slippery. The original's movement felt clunky, but in comparison, I think I would like the movement speed dialed back a notch.
3. Im not a fan of losing a couple hours if you pass out, though I understand why its there.
4. The rise in prices of things in game from the original feels a bit...steep.

Overall, I'd say its gonna be a 6/10 if you arent familiar with the og game, and an 8/10 if you are. The Nostalgium hit is pretty good off this one.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the original Another Wonderful Life on my PS2 years ago, but never really got past Spring, as it is so difficult to earn money in that game, and I remember my cow and goat stopped giving me milk at some point, and I couldn't figure out what's happening.

So when the announcement for the remake came about, I was thrilled and apprehensive, as I didn't enjoy Another Wonderful Life much back then, but decided to give it a chance. And I'm glad I did.

Not only did the devs fixed a lot of the annoying stuff that is in the original, they made the game a lot better. Earning money is still difficult, especially in the first year, since the seasons are only 10 days long still. But raising friendships are a heck lot easier this time around to the point that I managed to get the free Seed Maker from Daryl in early summer! Several characters in the game however underwent a full 'makeover' like Marlin (or Matthew) in the remake to the point that I barely recognised him from the original (he's hot in the remake tho).

I played this game on the Steam Deck, and while officially, it isn't verified by Valve, you can still play it with some tweaks. Not perfect, but still completely playable.

I'm still in late Autumn of year 1 at the point of writing this review, but so far, I'm enjoying the game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all you should not consider buying this unless you plan to play with a controller. Keyboard controls are hilariously bad and while they can be customized it does not function particularly well.

Aside from that comment I'd recommend this game overall as long as you read the reviews and understand what you are buying. This is a remake of the Gamecube/PS2 game and it does a good job of that but it is NOT as fleshed out as, for example, Pioneers of Olive Town or others games in the series. There are some really nice quality of life additions such as increased walking speed (thank god), festivals (minimal), and better visuals for things like stamina, relationships, etc. There are also some bad things like choppy animations and odd design choices but overall, coming from a place of nostalgia and love of the original game, this was worth picking up and I'm enjoying it quite a lot.\t\t\t\t\t

Edit: (Changing this to not recommended for the following reasons: The game has been out for 2 months now and there hasn't been a single update to deal with bugs. It's been almost 4 weeks since I've sent an email about the game breaking bug I've personally experienced and I still haven't heard back either.)


I bought this game purely for nostalgia because Harvest moon A wonderful life and another wonderful life were my very first harvest moon games. This game is all about relaxing, running around to talk to townies and taking care of your animals. There is not much content here because the main focus of the game is around your family. Unlike most other farming games your not trying to rush around to get everything done in one day and your not trying to be the best at anything.
I'm playing this game with a ps5 dualshock controller, a ryzen 9, 32gb ram and a 2070 super and so far the game runs perfectly fine for me. No stuttering, bugs ect. so far. It started out in english windowed mode and I was able to switch to full screen no problem. One thing I really like is that It actually shows ps5 controller prompts instead of xbox prompts.
The color palate for the game is WAY to bright and doesn't reflect the darker art style of the original, I will say they did a great job with all of the house interiors though.
The horse is ugly as can be, it looks straight out of a nightmare, what were they thinking? Just look at the horse in the original game and then go look at the horse in the upcoming harvest moon game to compare. Seriously fix the horse model.
Lastly the cow model looks great, I love how they kept the original model in mind. That being said they clearly had different teams working on each of the animals. The sheep, horse and cows don't look like they even belong in the same game?? You have a bright pink round sheep with a realistic looking cow with a horse that looks like a 5 year old drew.
The new planting and watering system is the best I've used in a story of seasons game so far. It's so smooth with controller and it automatically goes from square to next square so you don't have to keep lining it up. Not only that but when you pick stuff up, you don't have to put it in the bag before picking up the next item. Huge improvements there compared to the original.
All in all don't get this game if you want a bunch of content. If you want a nice little relaxing story and to watch yourself, family and the townies grow old then get this game. As disappointed as I am with this remake it's still enjoyable to play and is ACTUALLY relaxing unlike other farming games.\t\t\t\t\t
