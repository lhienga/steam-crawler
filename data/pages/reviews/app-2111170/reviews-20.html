It was worth waiting for 20 years.

This game is like an original Harvest Moon: A Wonderful Life game updated with a lot of major game patches (60% base-game + 40% improvement) including improved graphics (from darktone of PS2 to half-Japanese anime theme) and performance which is significantly smoother than it was in the original (60 fps with no crash or lagging), extended cutscenes and storyline, NPCs are more lively than the original especially your family members, step-by-step tools upgrades and a way to earn money which got buff in crops and livestock products (treasures from excavation site also) but got nerf in fishing and foraging so you can't fish for your life like the original one.

Even though they change many things, they still use some mechanics like the original game (which I respected) including the weather affecting crops and livestock products. The game also welcomes all player gender (yes, this game support LGBT+) and marriage is not fixed in man+woman like before (but you can't live alone you need to find a partner to begin the next chapter).

Rating summary:
9/10 for the player who doesn't play the original harvest moon: a wonderful life.
9+(2)/10 for the player who plays the original harvest moon: a wonderful life. 1 plus from more lively NPCs and events and 1 plus from being me back to my nostalgia.

Warning
** This game will consume a lot of your time if you play it **\t\t\t\t\t
