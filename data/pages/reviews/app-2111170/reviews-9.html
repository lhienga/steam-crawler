\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a modern day Remake of the original Ps2 era Harvest Moon A Wonderful Life.

The game stays largely the same in terms of story and characters. A few mechanics have been changed, the game has alot more help and redefined interfaces and a few new mechanics and personality. But it largely stays the same to the original.

The game now has a notice board for requests, (Some) characters designs have been changed, Pronouns were added, Clothing hair color and eye styles have been added in its place. Events and activities were made easier to do than in the original.

Something truly new was added in year 2: Vinnie the talking plant. Vinnie allows you to combine two crops into a new sub breed and eventually 2 sub breeds into a unique Super Crop that can be named by the player.

The game also has a large Empathizes on Family, namely the Players Child of which will be the main focus of the game and has alot of sweet and heartwarming events to experience- Something truly unique from how children are depicted in games.

Overall a beautiful Modern Day take on the classic farming sim that started the genre. Story of Seasons Wonderful Life will bring back your Nostalgia of the original and the beautiful graphics will draw you in. 7/10\t\t\t\t\t
