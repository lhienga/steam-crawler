\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you liked the recent remake to Friends Of Mineral Town, then you're going to like this one as well. They took a beloved game from many peoples childhood and only improved it. If you're a fan of later releases in the Harvest Moon titles, then be prepared because this is one of the original games and nothing like the ones that came after.

This is a game that you can sit down and relax and not have to worry about impending doom or trying to save the world. There's no Harvest Goddess vs Witch Princess secret rivalry, and all the sprites haven't been sent to another dimension. You're just here to run the farm, start a family, and see your kid grow up.

The ONLY negative I have to say for the game:
The controls on PC are fine, it took a bit of getting used to for me because I don't play any other games that are keyboard controls. If you try to play keyboard+mouse it'll be a bit weird, but with both hands on the keyboard you're fine. The only thing is that you really can't rebind any keys, which has only been a minor inconvenience for me as my keyboard is a bit big so reaching [esc] can be challenging.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was SUPER excited waiting 2 years for this to come out, I was ready to get hit with nostalgia....and wow. I really wanted to love this but I can't. First of all, the game is practically unplayable with mouse and keyboard. On the deck the controls are better but it freezes, Typing names in is a pain and you have to press a bunch of buttons at the same time because there is no keyboard optimization. Took me 4 Minutes to type out the name "Rue" on my deck. Super fun. Looks like crap on the deck too.

The game overall looks too colorful and cutesy compared to its predecessor, it's completely lost all of the realistic melancholy vibes the original had. Everything is too bright and cheery with shoddy shading. The models and textures all look so cheap and lifeless. All of the uniqueness from the older HM titles is gone, everything they release is all soft and way too cartoony now. The original game was always supposed to be darker than the other HM titles. It always stood apart from the rest and was very special to me as a kid with it's aging system, it is one of my favorite HM titles. But they sterilized it all and turned it into a cutesy little fantasy town.

Yeah let's make the sheep pink puffballs like they came out of a magical girl anime.... let's also make it so they can't die anymore when the whole original game was based around life and death.... cows ALWAYS produce milk now. They altered a bunch of beloved characters and changed their names for no reason and the perfect town name "Forget-Me-Not Valley" to "Forgotten Valley", change the bar to a stupid cafe, didn't bother adding much dialogue etc.

They pretty much dumbed it all down or more specifically, modernized it.
The original game had its flaws but it was still meaningful & beautiful. It had more charm and was quirky. This is lifeless modern trash. Play the OG instead. I'm returning this for Rune Factory 4 S which I will be replaying for the 3rd time because that is an excellent game actually worth my time.
