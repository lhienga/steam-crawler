I am happy to have this remake that has all the features of the previous A Wonderful Life entries and has much better performance. It doesn't suffer with any lag, slow loading time and low frame rate like the Special Edition version for Playstation 2.

There are bugs & glitches that causes teleporting animals with super walking speed along with the menus scroll too fast though if your game is running higher than 60 fps but those issues can be fixed by turning off v-sync in the game settings or limit the game to run only up to 60 FPS.

XSeed for some reason never made any plans to fix any bugs & glitches that happens when the game runs higher than 60 fps even though those issues already been reported last year. You have to limit the fps to 60 or turn off v-sync to prevent those bugs & glitches.\t\t\t\t\t
