So, I bought this game hoping it'd be very similar to the game cube version, knowing that it wouldn't be EXACT and I am pretty pleased overall. I have a really great PC, so I'm having no lag issues or any of that - I'm also using an Xbox controller, so I'm not having too many control issues, but I will still say that there are some weird things:

When you're typing something in, it immediately reverts to if you were playing with your keyboard and it's a little confusing trying to get it back to the Xbox controller. You have to hit the ENTER bar and then you can go back to using your controller.

When you first open the game, you're in WINDOW mode, so you have to go into game settings and display and change that. And then ...

You can't partially exit the game to say, go on google for any reason - it will make you fully exit the game instead.

It would have been nice if there were more characters added ngl - maybe I'll get some hate for that, but I feel like there aren't very good romantic options in the game overall. Maybe there are more characters in the DLC, I haven't looked yet but, there's that.

OVERALL it IS good - the music sounds about exactly like the game cube version, all the sound effects are very nostalgic and I love them, the visuals are overall very good (BUT NOT THE MOTION BLUR). I'm really excited to keep playing, but I AM also hoping that the devs are reading the reviews and will fix things like this. The motion blur is really annoying. I know ya'll are trying to make it as close to the game cube version as possible, but it's a PC game whether we like it or not, and having motion blur is just annoying.

EDIT--

Previously in this review, I thought there was a problem with the motion blur, and there actually is not. There's a problem with the resolution and frame rate, lol. You can't have this game on max settings, it make it appear to have motion blur when in reality, it's just not able to fulfill the fps you're asking of it. I've got it on the 900 option and it's looking much better now.

Also, I thought there was no quick save - but there is! When you're in your house and you go to that side table by your bed, and click the option that says 'journal' then go to 'write in journal' THEN you can save - and that can be whenever the heck you want! So that's def nice.

Now that I've played a little more, I am really enjoying it. This is def a nostalgia game - if you're looking for the best farming sim ever made, this probably isn't it - BUT it IS nostalgic, cute, sweet, wholesome, relaxing, etc. If you want a really chill game to play at the end of the day, this is it.

I'll start by saying while I do quite enjoy this game, it isn't for everyone and despite the facelift its age shows. If you played Stardew valley don't expect that. This game is simple compared to Stardew however the Harvestmoon/Story of seasons franchise has set as a base of inspiration for Stardew Valley.

This game deserves the mix reviews. I already seen this happening from miles away. Those who played the originals will LOVE it and those who are new will have varied feelings. By the way, This game is unlike most farming games. It does have an ending. It plays through 6 chapters and ends with you dying of old age.\t\t\t\t\t
