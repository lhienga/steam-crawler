\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish I had found this sim before I had played Forza Motorsport *the 8th and latest*. I don't think I will ever play another FM Microsoft game again after finding this simulator. If you want a nice authentic racing experience with a very flexible difficulty and control setup. This is for you. Great visuals as well especially for a 3 to 4 year old game. It has a nice learning curve though, and cars feel a bit floaty at first. However if you get it down and learn your corners it is EXTREMELY satisfying! The customization is not as quite in depth as Forza's, but it does let you create your own team, car livery, and has many colors to pick from. Between this and F1 2023 I think my sim racing thirst is quenched after the let down of FM8 and Microsoft / Turn10. I am playing on a Razer V2 Wolverine xbox controller and after a couple tweaks in the input sensitivity it felt great. I will be creating a sim chair setup w/ my ol' reliable Logitech G27 for this though. I have not yet attempted using my RiftS for VR but after just driving in cockpit view I can tell this game should be awesome utilizing VR.\t\t\t\t\t
