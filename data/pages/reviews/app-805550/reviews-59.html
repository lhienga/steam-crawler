\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIm brand new to sim-racing so take that into account when reading this. I have played normal ACC, CarX, Dirt Rally 2.0 and F1 23, but this is definitely my favorite. Out of my current hours, 95% are just practice laps, hotlaps, and hotstint mode. But, this has got to be one of the most fun, challenging, and rewarding games I have ever played. I cant wait to get more time in this and learn more so I can become competent enough for online races.

The community is great and you can find plenty of advice starting off and for advanced drivers. This is one of the few games that exist where I genuinely believe that you take some of the skills you learned back into the real world. While I do not see myself driving a GT3/GT4 car anytime soon, I would love to actually have a try at it one day just to see what applies. Regardless, this is a great game with some great graphics, good optimization, and engaging driving.

Definitely recommend!\t\t\t\t\t
