\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinally, the racing simulator I've been waiting for.

Amazing physics, lots of top tracks and a selection of excellent cars. You can customize everything, and there are very detailed options to adapt the difficulty, assists, and rules. I have the full set of DLCs, and the tracks are absolutely stunning and worth it, every single one. It looks great and it even comes with a great default configuration for the Xbox controller, even though you should of course play this with a wheel -- but before I decided to buy one, I had to evaluate the game without one. I now played this with a Thrustmaster T300 wheel and pedals as well, and it feels even better of course. UPDATE: I got Fanatec gear and it's amazing to drive with a GT3 wheel.

The only very minor negatives I can come up with it that it's only GT3 (a minor issue for me, as GT is my favorite series), and that Nordschleife is missing. Most likely it's missing because this is the official GT game, and the track was not part of the covered racing seasons. I hope it gets added to a future season and then comes to ACC, that would be amazing. If you really need Nordschleife, try Automobilista 2. It's a very different game with a much broader focus and tons of cars that I also like, but for GT3, nothing beats ACC: it just looks, sounds and feels better.

Update: I have now played a lot more (both in this account and on a friend's account at his home), we are now driving in different conditions without assists and watching videos to get better. It's still a blast. I absolutely recommend this to everyone looking for a serious racing game. Like Dirt Rally, it's rather exhausting to play though, and requires lots of concentration. If I talk to others while playing, I find that I make mistakes and mess up. If you are willing to put in some effort, it's perfect though. If you're looking to relax after work and get some easy wins, skip this.

Now let me drive just one more lap! 9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
