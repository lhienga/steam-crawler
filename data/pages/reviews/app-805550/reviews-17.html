R for Racing mode
Note: I played ACC (Assetto Corsa Competizione) on my wheel, the G923, I haven't tried the game on a controller.

The Career
Another note, I haven't finished the career and I probably never will.
The career mode of ACC is... weird. At least to the point I have played in the career, most of it are races, often AN HOUR long. The races have been also difficult, I don't think I have ever gotten into the top 3. The nice thing though is that the career doesn't require you to win to continue. But, like Assetto Corsa's career, there are no rewards for winning, though this time you can at least choose which car to drive!

The handling
ACC is probably one of the best-feeling racing games I have. The cars feel different and good to drive. Trueforce has probably the best implementation in this game. Even if you turn your sounds off, you can hear the sounds of the car from the trueforce.

Graphics and Cars
ACC looks really, really good. The lightning, the track, the cars, the sky..
Even if you would delete the cars, the track and everything else would still look really good. The interior views are very detailed and nice to look at. One thing I like is that the interior view has the things that are also in the hud, so that if you play with the hud disabled, you are not missing important stuff.

Conclusion.
This review is kind of short compared to my reviews for other racing games, and that's because there isn't much to talk about here. ACC is just good at what it does and that's kind of it. It doesn't introduce anything shockingly new. The thing is that in my opinion, they should try to make the career mode better, and I don't think this is even close to what racing games were before. Gran Turismo 4. It had the best career mode I have ever seen, and it was a great game. It was long, it had great different championships, it had a bunch of cars from different manufacturers. But it didn't need tyre deformation or extremely realistic physics to be great. Even Gran Turismo 7 doesn't do the same kind of career mode anymore. Some game has to realise this someday. ACC gets a 7/10. It's great at almost everything it does, but there could be more content. Now I recommend you to check out other reviews. Thank you.\t\t\t\t\t
