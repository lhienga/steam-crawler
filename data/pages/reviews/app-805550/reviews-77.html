\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game. I have only been using my Quest Pro to play in VR mode. No problems. Too bad graphics don't match qualify on TV but still pretty good with 4090 card. I launch game with Oculus VR from Steam. Seems better than Steam VR. Finally found an easy way to record VR video. Before I launch Quest Link I start video recording within the the Quest. Once I see the red dot in the helmet I launch Quest Link, select my desktop, start Steam and then start ACC with Oculus VR. The red dot remains while in ACC in VR. It keeps recording very good qualify video. You can set you video recording prefs directly in the Quest prior to launching Quest Link. I have no way to stop recording while using Quest Link so I will need to edit/delete the beginning and end of video's. The only way I found to stop recording is to stop Quest Link and stop video recording within the Quest helmet. The video is stored directly on the helmet. If it is too big to sync with Quest app you will need to connect your helmet to PC to access.\t\t\t\t\t
