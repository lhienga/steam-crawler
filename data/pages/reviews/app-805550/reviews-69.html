While yes, there is muuuuch more content, that is completely dependent on how good a mod is (and more importantly, how much you're willing to pay). This game on the other hand? Perfect content, across the board. The AI for it as well, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥! They don't feel like they're too weak or too strong.
