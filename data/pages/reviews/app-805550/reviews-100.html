\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally wanted to like this game but, just can't. iRacing is expensive and hoped to replace it with this. I'm running graphics in Epic mode (RTX 3060) and it really isn't all that. I found the simulated bouncing to be extremely hard to adjust to, specially since you don't bounce with the car. Just gave an overall jittery feel to the screen. Force Feed back was disappointing, even after reading suggestions and trying every adjustment under the sun. (Fanatec CSL Elite) Never could get a real good feel for what the cars are actually doing. AI Racing is what it is and there really isn't much to do in the game. Never gave the online racing any kind of a try. Way too much effort to figure out and from what I've seen and read in comments, not worth the effort to just go get wrecked. So, yeah, going to look for something else.\t\t\t\t\t
