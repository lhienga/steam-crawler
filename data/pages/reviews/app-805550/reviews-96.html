\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs this game for you? Let's find out. I love racing games both arcade and sim. I have a love/hate relationship with this game due to the fact that it can be a ton of fun, but at the same time extremely unforgiving. This game will take your lunch money, throw you in the mud and take your girl all in one afternoon. It can also be very rewarding when you get it just right. I am what I consider a slower pace racer, usually between 10th and 20th for qualifying, but I have also learned so much. I watch racing, I enjoy racing, but I never got into the mechanics of it, like set ups, camber, tow, blah blah blah... This simulator makes you pay attention to all the little details that go into winning a race. If you load in, don't bother with set ups then don't expect to beat anyone. The top racers here are the best at what they are doing.
A lot of people complain about the force feedback, but again, you have to adjust settings depending on what equipment you are running. I have used several wheel and pedal combos and the set ups are all different. You have to find your sweet spot and that takes time. The games visuals are stunning, smooth frame rate, audio is amazing, (I love all the little creaks and squeaks of the car, engine, road...) and for the most part the community is amazing. You will always have your crashers but some of that is just inexperience.
If you enjoy GT class cars, this is THE simulator for you. If you want to invest your time and really become a better racer, this will teach you. You WILL struggle at first, don't give up, and when you get that first win, it feels amazing!!! Now, get out there and have fun! See you on the track.\t\t\t\t\t
