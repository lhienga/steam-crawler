\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is slow paced and can be boring when you are lure hunting and staking out a need zone or similar.
BUT
Here's the thing...
That IS hunting. It takes patience and while there are some QoL stuff behind DLC paywalls... cough tents, treestands, blinds. cough
The game is well worth playing even WITHOUT them. The stalking and tracking is almost meditative for me and is almost half the point of the game. The game is SLOW PACED, but there is a depth in scouting the area and doing the prep work to find the best place to wait.

I have run into a time where I couldn't sleep in a tent which I got as part of a bundle as I ran out of money and had to do some tricky night hunting to earn enough to get to the right time to hunt what I was after.

THAT SAID:
SPENDING that time doing prep and hunting lesser game to solve the problem was rewarding and let me practice and made me feel like the hunter Ranger or game warden that the game seems to be built around.

And that is another point. The game feels LESS like a hunting sim and MORE like a park ranger and game warden sim at times.
BUT THAT ISN'T A BAD THING
The game focuses quite a lot on 'Hunting as conservation' and 'ethical culls with minimal suffering' rather than the 'Only for a Trophy and toss the rest' style that some in the US might be familiar with.

I know where I live, hunting and fishing licences pay for most of the conservation and awareness stuff in the area and help KEEP the wilds..... well.... WILD

It is refreshing to see that reflected in game with the sort of hunts and missions offered:
Population control and culling species that are impacting the area negatively due to rising numbers.
Hunting invasive species.
Getting PHOTOS of wildlife for awareness pieces and articles.
Etc.

It is a slow game.
If you are looking for a TROPHY hunter with tag and bag mechanics..... perhaps look elsewhere.
BUT
If you want a hunting game where you can feel the full reward of slowly stalking and tracking game across the landscape or setting up and sitting in ambush in a routinely traveled route you identified through study...
THEN this game will probably be up your alley.\t\t\t\t\t
