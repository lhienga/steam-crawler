\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe maps are well made (for the most part) very big, not to mention quite a few of them (and props to host sharing DLC maps if the other players don't have them). The graphics are decent and you can really 'polish' them off with Reshade.
It's not a realistic game to the max, animals aren't spooked by light, sight mechanics are basic, tracks are fairly easy to follow with the bright overlay (though I'd prefer Classic huntermate system), but the game doesn't offer much beyond hunting the rares and doing SP missions. It does offer a way to burn some time and can be fun with friends.
Multiplayer co-op is basic, some actual co-op style random missions/events wouldn't go amiss here (and in SP).
It feels like something is missing from the game, there's no skinning etc, random events, those ATV's are horrible (parachutes, gliders for funsies? Better vehicles? Horses?), some flavour on the side between hunts?

A few glitches and bugs... Audio on the wrong side of your player to the call, animal rustling when there is no animals around, certain maps do not AI well, the Cuatro Colinas for example; In several sessions and 90% of the time the AI will freeze on the spot, run as if stuck in the ground and just otherwise just derp out... Having tracked down mythical+'s in this only to see them not respond at all and bleed out on the spot whilst you stand next to them is very disappointing.
Sometimes tracks disappear entirely, whilst I would expect an element of randomness with tracks, a vital-organ hit with a medium bleed not producing a single blood patch over 200 meters with no corpse in sight and the dog just as confused as you are, or the tracks missing entirely is a problem, especially since most animals don't bolt in one direction and keep going, running circles around the area making it impossible to find anything.
The Hunt Club BETA is dodgy... Still.
And at the end of the day when you're done with the game, hard reboot the PC because it's got some kind of memory access issue and locks up the system after long sessions, or even blue screens it (on multiple systems).

More fixes, more content please...\t\t\t\t\t
