\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing this game for a while now and I really love the combination of its beautiful environment and relaxing gameplay. However, I have noticed a particular issue that can be frustrating when I am hunting animals. Once I save an animal, I have no way of taxidermizing it later. Sometimes, I may not have enough money to do it immediately or I am hunting multiple animals at once, so I save the animal for later. Although I know that I can head straight to the lodge after a kill and taxidermize whatever I was hunting, but this can be difficult when the player doesn't have enough money as stated above. Therefore, I would appreciate it if the developers could add a feature that allows players to save the animals for later taxidermy. This feature would be a great addition to the game and would save players from the hassle of having to hunt down the same animal twice. Overall, I enjoy playing this game, but this one feature could use some improvement.\t\t\t\t\t
