Pros
* Great atmosphere and ambiance. Just walking around the countryside is interesting and relaxing.
* Good variety of weapons and equipment
* Pretty good animal behaviors makes finding game not too easy or too difficult.
* Tracking wounded animals is satisfying
* Maps are HUUUUGE
* Fun and well done multiplayer co-op

Cons
* Interface can be a bit clunky with managing inventory transferring items from storage to character
* Radial menu to is also a bit buggy at times but nothing game breaking once you get used to it
* Graphics are not breathtaking but get the job done
* Items are very expensive to earn using in-game currency, but frequent sales make getting some of the content pretty cheap.

All in all this is my favorite hunting game so far on the PC and can't wait to get into some of the other game reserves\t\t\t\t\t
