\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think I get what they were going for, but the onboarding and general feedback is severely limited and that makes the difficulty curve stupid hard. In more than two hours, I could barely find anything to hunt and when I did, I shot it only to get "very minor blood loss" and a multi-mile trail that led nowhere. There's probably a good game in here somewhere, but it's just frustrating rather than fun to learn.

I genuinely don't know if I'm doing something wrong or if that's just how the game should be.

Worse, on several occasions, I shot with my crosshairs perfectly aligned and missed. Is there a spread? Does wind matter? Does the scope have to be dialed in? Does gravity matter? No idea. The game just gives you a pathetically weak gun and no instructions for it whatsoever.

I assume if I watched a lot of YouTube this game would make more sense, but I'm old fashioned in that I like a game to explain its own mechanics, and this one just fails to do so.\t\t\t\t\t
