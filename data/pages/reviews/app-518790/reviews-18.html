\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttheHunter is an extremely slow paced but relaxing hunting simulator, which could have earned a positive review if the developers weren’t so greedy.

Overall the gameplay is calm yet engaging. You track animals, sneak up on them while paying attention to details like which direction the wind is blowing your scent, how much noise you’re making and so on, then eventually take the time to line up a shot and hopefully hit a vital organ. You are given a few guns and tools to play with and many types of animals to hunt. The mechanics are generally decent, but ultimately my experience was diminished by something else.

The base game, although at full price, feels more like a demo because most of the game’s content is locked behind a DLC paywall. A lot of these I could forgive, extra guns, clothes, maps etc. despite their abhorrent amount. However, without paying for some elements, like vehicles, the game is extremely tedious. The movement speed is so ridiculously slow that I’m being genuine when I say I can crawl faster in real life than I can run in this game. The objectives you are given during the campaign are at locations very far apart from each other and at times getting from point A to B was 20-30 minutes of nonstop jogging.

If you are very patient or willing to throw in a few extra bucks for a decent hunting experience then you could give it a shot on sale, however I can’t recommend a game that makes me pay separately for a feature that is literally on the store page banner. 5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
