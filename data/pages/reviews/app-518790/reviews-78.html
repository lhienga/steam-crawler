\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game I should've played back when it came out. Better late than never. While it isn't a perfect representation of hunting in video games, the overall experience is satisfying and sometimes great. There are plenty of challenges that await anyone who wants to jump into one of the many maps. The wide selection of animals and gear available makes theHunter a title you can spend many hours on. As an RPG, you have enough options to keep you interested, such as skills and perks. The variety of DLC gives you more options than you might ever need, providing even cosmetics to show off when playing online. While the game is fun solo, having a hunting buddy or an entire party turns this game into an absolute riot when things go well. There's nothing like ripping into a herd of elk to see them all go down for harvesting. Before I go on for too long about all the details, I will wholeheartedly recommend this game.

Get yourself in there and happy hunting.\t\t\t\t\t
