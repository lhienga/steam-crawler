\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt takes a while to get going on this game, but once you've managed to go up a few levels and get some skills under your belt, it's a really great game! HOWEVER, this game is not for the impatient types. This game requires infinite patience and stealth, but if you can do that, it's a very rewarding experience. Big hint, animals can hear you walking normally 200 meters away and will spook and run. You need to crouch and creep in when you're within 200 meters. Also, hunt around water sources. It took me a while to figure this out and I was frustrated that I wasn't seeing any animals. Once I started doing this though, I was able to drop animals consistently and leveled up pretty quickly.\t\t\t\t\t
