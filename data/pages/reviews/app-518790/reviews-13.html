\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a pretty fine hunting game, not perfect by any means but well worth a sale price.

The parks are attractive enough to be a pleasant time all on their own, the soundscape reasonably well done, and the hunting itself... probably lacking about the right amount of realism to make up for the drawbacks of being a video game.

But there's problems. Some of the maps have bugs, I've noticed too many, most minor, to remember them all but a few that stuck out include: signposts hovering several metres in the air, ground clutter that doesn't align with the ground, some sounds don't seem to position properly, and now and then a sound gets 'suck' for a little while.
There's plenty more others have mentioned but the real problem is that the company just isn't very responsive and many of these have existed for a long time. A bit more time fixing the game and back-filling existing maps with more diverse animals and behaviour and it would really become something solid.\t\t\t\t\t
