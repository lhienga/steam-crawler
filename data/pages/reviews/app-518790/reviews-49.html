The animals look fantastic and with their behavioral patterns, really makes it feel like they're actually there. I really like how this game challenges your patience, skills, and overall determination to hunt certain animals. One time, I was trying to hunt a coyote with a caller and ended up spooking it when it was within 10 meters of me. I'm a pretty stubborn guy, so I ended up tailing the thing until I got it. I've had this game for barely a week and I already got 24 hours in it, just because they offer so much to do. This game had me super immersed most of the time I was playing and I highly recommend it to anyone looking for something chill, but also exciting at times.\t\t\t\t\t
