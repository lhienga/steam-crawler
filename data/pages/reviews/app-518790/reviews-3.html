\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLot's of fun. Good variety of animals, some decent maps, and a reasonable spread of hunting weaponry.

The greatest take away from this game, however, is the graphics. Some folks could easily never pull the trigger and still find enjoyment in the scenery on its own. Gorgeous sunrise and sunsets, Aurora Borealis in one particular map which was a fantastic surprise when I discovered it, forests are diverse and the clouds are something else to behold.

Mechanically it's not perfect, but, all-in-all, as a non-hunter irl, I really enjoy this game. Easy enough to figure out as a 'new-to-hunting-games', no prerequisite skill set, and a decent community.

The DLC's can make life easier and its completely playable without them but it's worth getting these before looking at any additional maps; "ATV/Quadbike Saber 4X4" to take the edge off map exploration, "Tents & Ground Blinds" for custom fast travel locations, "Treestand & Tripod" enhanced position and reduce effects of kills in zones.\t\t\t\t\t
