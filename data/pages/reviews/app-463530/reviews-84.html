Don't get me wrong, I love the game but the several issues I have just make me not want to play. I have owned the game for 23 hours, and I'm tired of all the restarts because of slight errors I make.

One of the first things I would add would be to go back more than 1 save. I frequently realise my errors after like 5 saves and with my OCD, I just can't keep playing. I'm tired of hearing the same voice lines over and over.

Next would be adding tiles. I was in the discord and about everyone went off on one when I tried to have a civil discussion but everyone was hell-bent on trying to shut it down. From what I gathered, the devs do not want to add building as then players could make long corridors but I have 2 simple balances.
1. Must be at-least 1 route from the Queen to the Entrance / Exit.
This is so players don't just box themselves in at the first sign of trouble
2. A limit on the distance. For example, the distance between the Queen and the Entrance / Exit cannot be more than 20 tiles.
Everyone was going on about how much more advanced the AI would need to be to path-find all this however I don't believe it would need that much changing or changes at all. In the very first mission, I often end up with a tunnel about 40 to 50 tiles branching away from the Queen. .

I love the game but its these things above that make me want to stop playing. Devs, if you read this, please reconsider the whole building aspect. With the 2 things above, I don't think it would end up being OP. I only need this change so that when I tab back into the game and accidentally mine a tile, I'm not forced to restart my save and spend several hours getting back to where I was.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is no other game quite like this one. It plays like a strategy games (think Age of Empires) but revolves around ants. The gameplay depends on which species of ant you're playing as, with wood ants eating conventional foods like seeds and other insects, while leaf cutters require leaves, which they then cultivate into a fungus to eat. There are a plethora of other insects which pose a threat to your colony, so you have to delicately balance expansion and safety. Encounter a Devil's Coach Horse Beetle or Wolf Spider too early, and your colony will be devoured. The largest threat, however, is rival ant colonies. They forage and expand same as the player, and will launch attacks against your colony. The game is educational, and has loads of content (I've played 34 hours already). I give it a 10/10.\t\t\t\t\t
