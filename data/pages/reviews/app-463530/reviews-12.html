\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI did enjoy this game at first, but the story requires a ton of grinding and that's not something I'm into. The story missions have low rewards after beating them the first time and are very long, which kills replaying them for required grinding. The story missions is split between missions/scenarios where you play as different ant species, and formicarium challenges/boss levels where you make an ant hive with the resources you earn from the missions. Even on the lower difficulties, the formicarium challenges are nearly impossible unless you stick to very specific play styles and layout of your hive and army, which kind of kills creativity. Overall the difficulty levels are unbalanced, pared with an almost non-existent tutorial. It's insane that a game that has been worked on for years is so unpolished.

Game play is fun, but quickly turns to grinding which turns the game unsatisfying and exacerbates the faults of the game's design.\t\t\t\t\t
