\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, this is a tough one.

The game is fun. Building your little ants and eating the bigger insects to secure the future of your colony - its a lot of fun in the campaign.
However, the campaign is ridiculously short - Overall it adds up to roughly 8 hours of playtime. The campaign is split in two -
You control different species of ants in different surroundings trying to establish yourself in the environment. Those missions are the best part of the game.
Then you have your personal experimental specisies in a lab. That part of the game is where the actual "story" happens. Sometimes you get challenges, but they're so boring and long it actually makes sense to start the next step and just go afk for 4 minutes or so.
Another part of the overall game is a kind of useless arena where you can pit creatures against each other - it only highlights that the fight between different creatures really is kind of boring and same-sy.
You have the demo levels, which have the same restrictions as the demo for some reason.
It is so weird, because at every corner you have something really interesting, directly followed up by something very irritating.
The challenge missions (Just like normal missions but with an extra obstactle) can be infuriating at times - like enemies which require you to micro manage, in a game were you are literally unable to micro.

I liked the experience, that is why I am giving it overall a thumbs up. But if youre hoping for a truly fleshed out game stay away - or wait until its on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
