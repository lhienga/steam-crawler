\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCharming game, excellent entry into the RTS world. I'll say that honestly, I was kinda disappointed with the campaign. I read the words "persistent lab colony and evolve according to your preferred playstyle" and was mislead to believe that it added rogue-like elements to the rest of the missions you'd play in campaign. It's purely instance based. I haven't yet tried out free play, so I'm withholding judgement on that part, but the persistent colony feels kinda lackluster. You use it for two challenges, after which it gets wiped and you move.

Equally unfortunate, you unlock the new ants in campaign after one mission with those ants, but that's not really enough time to understand "rapid fire" vs "mortar" for the wood ants, for instance, and I made the mistake of selecting mortar. No way to take it back, so my persistent colony is SOL and I'm not going to restart the campaign to fix that mistake.

I wish there was multiplayer. I've definitely gotten my money's worth of time out of this game, but instance based maps only keep my attention for so long without others to enjoy it with. It's an extremely high quality RTS game, and I gotta admit I'm blown away by the optimization. Not a hint of lag. I've been following this game since it first got announced on EA, decided it was finally worth a try, and I'm glad that I did. I just wish the persistent colony part of the campaign actually mattered.\t\t\t\t\t
