\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is quite poor for an ant sim.
Firstly the difficulty curve is extreme, even on easy and it only goes up from there.
There is not enough levels to justify the cost currently, AND;
The Formicarium levels are EXTREMELY unbalanced. It makes me wonder what even is the point of this beyond unnecessary gating of the player to prevent progress.
The Formicarium is also frankly half utilised- they mention all these different types of ants and mechanics through the game but you dont get to use them in the formicarium. Leafcutter ants? Nope, army ants? not quite there yet but ill update (yes the player should have been locked into one breed of ant and the strenths/weaknesses there of- giving them the best of everything for variety sake is again uninteresting).


Simply put it feels as though difficulty has been used to drag out the gameplay time- and that is poor design.
The game should hold on its own without the incessant need to "throw more enemies" or "add more ints to damage and health".

This difficulty also makes it hard to enjoy the game, because if im playing the leafcutters and now im competing against other leafcutters that might be "interesting" for about 2 minutes, but the constant struggle to outplay other colonies is frankly boring and prevents me from exploring or doing other misc things.

My suspension of disbelief was ruined a couple of times, mainly on the formicarium levels, want me to fight another ant colony- ok, that sounds interesting, but then they have an extreme amount of spawns that does not seem possible for their colony as compared to mine. Want to go on the attack? The guy squishes your ants, want to stay and defend? He spawns large bugs. And this is compounded by the fact that after your first play through on a level you will then be SIGNIFICANTLY hampered on the replay, you will earn about 1/4 to a 1/3 of the first time rewards. Again grinding the player on the same handful of levels so that they can come back and retry the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ that is the formicarium levels.


TL;DR
Its an ant strategy grind game- not an ant colony game. If that was the intent they have half way missed the mark.
Further point it feels like the developers are not focused, they are trying all these different things and throwing it together but not really delving into them (the formicarium is the best example).
They just need to take something, focus on it and make it mechanically fun. My suggestion would be the formicarium- dont just pick a colour, also pick a species of ant, take it through the challenges and to the end game.
Also fix the difficulty, it should not be this extreme.
Oh and blocking up entrances/areas, why can I not do this? Why am I giving the other critters a free shot at my colony if they overwhelm my fighting ants.\t\t\t\t\t
