\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOn the state the game is is not worth it... Unless you are a huge fan of Dungeon Keeper and similar games.

The "campaign" is 8 missions that the game dev expect you to replay and FARM to get enough points to upgrade to beat a "Challange" that gatekeeps your progress every two missions.

The campaign don't intruduce new elements and on a new level than let the player understand how things works so after that they test your knowlegde on a new mission as you would expect on any game on this genre.

The same mission they get you new units, a new form economy, a totally unique resource, and propose a challange at the same time. You will fail on the first try, and you get nothing (xp for you "main" home base) for the time wasted.

Every level takes about 50 minutes to complete, if you like to take your time and enjoy the game.

So without the mandatory trial and errors the game could be finished on about 6~8 hours..

Some levels you will have to play exactly as the dev intended or you fail, with for me takes the fun on having a game about growing wierd combinations of ants and dominating the whole map.

On the first 4 levels you get rewarded for killing other insects, like spiders, and beetles. But on later missions those fights reward you nothing. As you are only playing with leef cuters, who will NOT take food (the main resource of the game) from enemies killed.

TLDR: I enjoyed the game. But having to grind and farm on levels i already completed to be able to progress on a mandatory challange that only purpose is to gatekeep the player, is not something I where expecting on a game like this.\t\t\t\t\t
