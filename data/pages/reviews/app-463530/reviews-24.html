\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen observing ants my whole life. The designers of this game have gone out of their way and paid attention to details. Only someone who has spent a lot of time studying and observing could have been behind the design of the ants in this game. The animations, the way leaf cutter ants climb up plants to cut leaves and even take back to make their fungus. The way that the army ant soldiers show off their mandibles in defense. The way ants swarm and climb on top of larger predators is satisfying.

The story in the game can be kind of hilarious at times. The voice acting is really well done.

As someone who spent a lot of hours as a kid in my backyard just observing ants, I have found this game to give me that same feeling from the comfort of my office. There are things that could be better but overall I can tell that a lot of love has gone into this game and I am finding it very enjoyable.

Please devs, keep going. I would gladly pay for dlc for more ant species and more maps.\t\t\t\t\t
