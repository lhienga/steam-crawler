\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is in fact more fun than you'd think an ant RTS/sim game might be. You get a sandbox ant colony in a science lab that lives in isolation but gets progressive challenges created by these scientists you hear the voices of, but lets you select the next challenge when you think the colony is ready. And to gain more bonuses in food and different forms of experience points you visit the wild and have these documentary style scenarios; first one for example is taking over a rotting log as a new black ant colony. Then back to your peaceful laboratory colony to upgrade things when you're done with it. There's also free-play where you cans set your own scenarios. It all is fairly realistic and not very cartoony, uses real ant and other bug species. Took about half an hour to learn, maybe an hour to hone out any special details for proficient strategies. Really good ant strategy sim quite honestly! Recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
