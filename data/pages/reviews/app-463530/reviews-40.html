\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like the game. I bought it to help support the devs. To be fair, i was warned that they have been very slow to add anything substantial to the game over the last 7 years. Yes, 7. It's fairly well thought out and pretty challenging. But nothing makes you feel more powerful than marching around, 100+ ants strong, and taking out everything in our path with ease. You can clear a patch of food or strip a plant in an instant, and plow through a group of competitor ants like it was nothing, leaving their bodies curled up and sinking into the earth and into nothingness.

My main gripe, and it's starting to grow over time, is that, the devs obviously care about their creation a lot, and want everything to look perfect, BUT, I wish they would spend more effort in creating and adding content and levels first and foremost. They can always buff and polish the models, artwork, balances or whatever with game updates along the way. But the main campaign, or story, or whatever, is lacking and sorely needs to be increased. 3 or 4 maps per level, instead of just 2 would be nice for a start. Then a person wouldn't need to replay the same maps over again just to build up enough colony and resources to advance to the next level. Then maybe 8 or 10 levels to work through. There are several additional maps to play besides the main campaign, and even custom games you can create, but most aren't very interesting to me. I really, really want more main story maps, and probably at least 4 or 5 more levels added. Please. I'm bored. I've played the existing ones so many times now. I just can't anymore. Please reward us early adopters by throwing us a little handfull of seeds now and then to keep us satisfied.

Overall, I rate this game an 8.5, BUT, this score is slowly dropping over time, without new content to satiate the hunger.\t\t\t\t\t
