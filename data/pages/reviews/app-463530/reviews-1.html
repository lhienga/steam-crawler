It's also fairly unique (much like SimAnt was) in how it plays and the fact that you play as ants. Unlike SimAnt, you don't control individual ants. Instead you issue orders and ants will get around to it. If you want them to do it urgently, you set them to "pheromone" control groups and set the pheromone marker somewhere and the ants will tumble over to that spot to do whatever you need them to do.

The simple and repetitive music has a good sense of urgent need for killing and expanding and gives a brutish tone to everything your ants do. Everything about this game is portioned just enough to make you want more but question is maybe more would make you bored with it. But I still want more.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRating 6.5 Ive been obsessed with ants since a kid and this game is right up my alley. Grew up playing warcraft, starcraft, and C&C. I fell out of RTS games when they tried to evolve the genre with complexity. Ive always like the basic harvest resources and build a huge army.

Empires of Undergrowth is a beautiful game and does a great job of making each ant species play differently and work similar to how they would in real life. My issue is its way too hard. The first several levels are great and there is a learning curve but there are so far two levels that have me throwing in the towel. The first one was a fomicarium (sp?) level which I just had to google how to beat it. Basically keep playing previous levels to get a bigger army (grind) and change my ant skills. The second level that I am at now that I think Ill throw the towel in is a swamp level. You cant save in the middle of a level I dont think and so when you loose its hours of play time down the tube. I feel like each level the game developer wants you to play a certain way and if you dont you wont succeed. This last level I have a great army, beat the "boss" but right after that you get swarmed and destroyed. I could look up how to beat the level but I feel like that shouldn't be how it is and i dont want to play another hour or two to get that point only to loose again.\t\t\t\t\t
