\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHow dare you. Hooded Horse? Slug Disco? How, actually, dare you...

This game, all over its store page talks about "strategy." I bring that up because I could excuse user generated tags. I could excuse assumptions I myself make. But no, this game is literally billed as strategy.

So how dare you call it that when you're going to have pile on player mechanics, to a degree I've never seen, before.

I'm not talking Stellaris-type stuff where the developer and apologists for the game can say "Oh, it's like super deep and stuff and you clearly just didn't understand. Git gud, scrub?" to cover up its failings. No.

How dare you introduce multiple competing ant colonies...that all gang up on me.

How dare you show off multip...no, numerous, creatures that love to kill ants, to a borderline suicidal degree they just NEED to kill my ants, but...not...each other?

And. Then. How dare you have multiple ant colonies team up...yes TEAM UP...with the ant killing monstrosities for the sole purpose of screwing over my colony.

All of this, on top of stun locking my ant groups so that none can disengage when you just manifest threats that bypass my wall of mandibles to assault my Colony...

Stop right there. If some of you were going to say "well THAT'S why you need to get the royal guards upgrade for your Queen!!1!" Well it isn't strategy if I need to rely on one specific upgrade to account for abject nonsense, now is it? Nor does it apply to the ants outside of the Formicarium, so just check your flex at the door, Skippy.

Believe it or not, I wanted to like this game. I tried to like this game. I know for a stone cold fact, however, that some jackwagons are going to look at my other reviews, see most are negative and then just dismiss my criticisms so they can feel good as they fire up their gawk-gawk skills to justify having spent their parents' money on the game while going "lol u mad."

But this is not strategy. This is a slap in the face. Challenge is one thing. Challenge is desirable, even.

BUT. COME. ON.

People, believe what I say or not, it's not my money being spent if you want to buy this. However, if you want strategy, this isn't it. I guarantee you that.\t\t\t\t\t
