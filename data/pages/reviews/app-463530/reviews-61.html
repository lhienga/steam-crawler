\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game. It has the beginnings to everything i would want in an ant game however there are still quite a few problems. Firstly story mode is severely lacking due to just a lack of missions and by far not enough royal jelly options. Right now the game has the life span of an ant instead of a campaign. They have a few simplistic arcade levels and a free play section that is functional that require saved slot for setting up a custom game that stays after you win for some reason. An introduction into the mad scientist actually being manic like when you play the reverse colony and he has hybrids would make more sense for the original story since he got escorted off. The player does not get a lot of reference to this throughout the campaign because its more or less presented as a boy being a boy ya know. I would suggest going a bit more mad scientist and maybe even outside missions through control where you attack outside world things or you escape and he makes another colony to hunt your colony through a rather large map like 4 backyards etc. I see the pieces but they just are not quite there. Also have some fun with this i appreciate the documentary aspects and learning about the bugs for sure but this is also an empire add some enchantment to it and don't just blast freaking tuba at people for 8 hours. All jokes aside music has a like background jazz angle that does a fair bit of tuba playing which is obnoxious. I mean we are small creatures give me like some dwarven empire noises or something suited for underground and exploring a massive world as an ant empire. I want to see this game super successful just honestly the things i mention break the fun, collude the story, or just make the game feel not the best. 100% love the insect noises they are spot on and not to violent on the high end. I can withstand their deaths and noises with out being dog whistled and that is very nice and a problem i am glad this insect game does not have. Kudos to the developers on that and the voice acting. Very pleasant and clear voices to listen to overall. No matter what they had to say, even background babble, was in no way overbearing or to much and you actually deem them useful in most missions. Good job on game mechanics. I would also appreciate stats and health bars for creatures and ants. Not seeing the values kinda just kinda leaves me in the dark with what im working with. Much potential if done well and carefully.\t\t\t\t\t
