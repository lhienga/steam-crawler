This game is not meant for children that are fascinated with ants, and want to learn something. My sons have lost time and time again, and when I stepped in...I was blown away by how difficult it still was on "Easy."

My opinion, it's a great game. However, if you are going to have an "Easy" mode...you need to tune it down to easy! Starting off the opposition with double or even triple your army is absurd for "easy," Maybe I just don't understand what I'm doing, but isn't that the point of "Easy" mode?
