\r\n\t\t\t\t\t8/10 (early access review).
First of all, STOP LOOKING AT PICTURES/VIDEOS. Encountering those surprise "bosses" for the first time and having that "oh " heart racing moments is one of the best parts of this game.

This game started developing quite long ago, but it is now an absolute gem of a base building RTS and feels so unique! I can't wait to see what else they add!
Very challenging (if you want it to be). But so satisfying when you complete the missions, only to be hit by an even greater boss and more terrifying creatures that make your spine tingle and skin crawl. However, you also get more options to hit them back with.

Very very strategic gameplay. There is always a way to win no matter how daunting the task may be if you are tactical about it. Reminds me of the good old RTS games of the 90s. I think this has the potential to be one of the all time great RTS if they keep adding more content. Get it!\t\t\t\t\t
