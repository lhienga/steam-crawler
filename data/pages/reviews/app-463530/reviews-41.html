\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDesperately needs a way to save inside a mission. Missions run 1-2 hours long, and oftentimes, you will lose 1-2 times before you win because you simply do not have the information to succeed, and it's annoying wasting so much time for almost no progress.

For instance, I have lost because I dug into a tunnel expecting only 2 enemies and making short work of them, only to discover it was actually part of a larger tunnel with several larger enemies attached, forcing my army into a protracted battle. Halfway through this battle, an enemy wave came and attacked my queen. Your army cannot deaggro during fights to prioritize more important tasks. My queen died. 1 hour down the drain. (Thought I was only going to fight 2 small coachhorses, but it was attached to a tunnel with like 4 more small ones and 2 big ones)

I have lost because I have sent out my main army to fight an enemy, only to have a brand new enemy type spawn right at that same time, and now my entire army is dead because this brand new enemy mowed down my army because I cannot tell my army to stop fighting or re-prioritize. My army now dead, I cannot generate the mass necessary to actually overwhelm the enemy, and then my queen dies. (My army attacked a small hermit crab just as the big wolf spider spawned. The small wolf spider was trivial, but the big wolf spider killed all my ants in one hit, and my ants wouldn't stop attacking the hermit crab to target the giant wolf spider brutalizing them right behind them). That one was maybe only half an hour lost.

Final day of the 2.2 beach mission. I have reached this point easily so far. I have 2 separate armies of ants, 20 fully upgraded melee and 20 partially upgraded ranged. I send each to their respective choke points, thinking that I should defend my aphids for the guaranteed food for ant respawns. So far, this army has been more than enough to handedly win each side. Turns out, this final day repaths the enemy AI, and now they have an infinite feed of ants heading into the aphid spawns, so this is a terrible strategy, and you are instead supposed to consolidate inside your base so that you avoid having to fight these 'gatherer' ants in addition to their 'army' ants. I didn't know, because it was my first time reaching that point, and now both my armies are dead, and I have very little food stores, and I cannot tell my ants to retreat to conserve resources because once they're engaged in battle, they stay in battle. My queen dies. I lose 1.5 hours of time.



I get that it's probably artistic intent to not have fine control over your ants, but losing these ways is not fun. It's annoying. It's fine to keep it this way, but give us some way to mitigate the time loss. Give us a way to save inside the mission. If you don't want us to be able to manually save, have an autosave at the dawn of each day (or however many minutes constitutes a day cycle for those missions without access to the overworld), and give us the ability to reload to that point mid mission.

For what it's worth, I do genuinely enjoy the game when I'm not being frustrated at losing so much time, and the game is very reasonably priced.\t\t\t\t\t
