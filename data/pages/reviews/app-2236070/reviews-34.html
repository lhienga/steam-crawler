\r\n\t\t\t\t\tas someone who rock climbs irl i found this a very enjoyable experience :>
the mechanics are easy to understand, fun to use, and have a high skill ceiling with the cool stuff you can pull off.
i found this a refreshing game amid climbing games that try to be rage inducing, it was nice to play a laidback genuinely fun game. i dont have much patience so i would sometimes put on some music to keep me focused so i wouldnt get too annoyed with a climb. me being annoyed at the climb though wasnt the fault of the game itself, but the fault of my own inexperience, and as i tried again and again at the levels i learned more about the mechanics and was able to surpass the obstacle. i feel that this is one of the best rock climbing games out there, ive tried "the climb" in vr and although it is a pretty decent game, i enjoy this a lot more because of how many levels there were, the time trials as well were also fun.
you can really tell a lot of love was put into this game and its so sad seeing barely anyone talk about it.

my only 5 complaints are as follows:

-i wish there were more levels, perhaps a dlc that adds another 3 books would be fun

-the game feels a little empty without music, perhaps if there were some peaceful old timey tunes going on while climbing it would help ease the spirit

-i feel the price is a little much, maybe bumped down to $20~ (aud)

-i found it a little annoying how the arms come out of the playermodel, and i feel as if it could be made so that they are more centered on the crosshair. although this was annoying i did get used to it eventually

-and finally, i found it a bit annoying how the button to put the telescope away is the same button that detaches you from your rope, on multiple occasions i found myself looking around at the environment and accidentally causing myself to fall when i go to put it away. i feel as if the game could be slightly improved if that feature was removed and the button for letting go of the rope was only "t"


finally, whoever is reading this. i highly recommend buying this game. its one of the few genuinely entertaining and challenging climbing games out there and is well worth its price!! (even if i feel the price could be lowered a bit as its proven difficult to convince my friends to try a $30 game ~.~)\t\t\t\t\t
