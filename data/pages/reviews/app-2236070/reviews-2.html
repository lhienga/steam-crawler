\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're reading this you're probably wondering if this is another "Getting Over It" fad game.

One defining feature of those games is that the mechanics are not fun. You are motivated to play both because of how ironically un-fun it is and how cheeky and fun the dialog is.

Peaks of Yore is the complete opposite of this. Its FUN to learn to climb. I don't want to climb just for the satisfaction of finishing the level, but because its an enjoyable experience in itself. The mechanics are also deep enough that after 100 minutes I'm impressed not only with the challenges the game throws at me, but at my ability to overcome them using tricky techniques.
