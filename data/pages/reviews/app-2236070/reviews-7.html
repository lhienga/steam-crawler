\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis Game is an amazing game to start playing while talking to friends or listening to a podcast. It has a very gentle difficulty curve slowly easing you into new modifiers and mechanics with every level, giving a rewarding feeling of progress with every new level and your skills as a mountain climber naturally increase.

The game strikes the perfect balance of difficulty and frustration, without making the game too annoyingly difficult. you may be dissuaded at times, but you should be comfortable to continue going all the way to each peak.

And then you realise why the credits roll before unlocking the last two levels.

The first is The Great Bulwark, a tutorial for new mechanics that will be present in the final level, it is a very difficult climb that you may have to come back to once or twice before you reach the peak. but as long as you are patient, you will make it to the top in a reasonable time.

The Solemn Tempest is an unrelenting gauntlet that not pull punches, its twice the length of every other peak in the game combined, the developer implemented a mechanic to be able to save and quit of exclusively this map, because it is just unreasonable to expect people to have the time to master the game to the point of climbing this mountain in one sitting. It will tear you up and spit you out. Your will has not been tested harder than this mountain will make you.

You will fall. Again, And again. And again. but each time you get just a bit further up the mountain. And eventually you will reach the top. This feeling of accomplishment far outweighs any achievement i have made in any other games, and many achievements in my real life. I highly recommend the first half of this game for anyone looking for a peaceful time as you slowly learn and get better. I also heavily recommend the second half of this game for people who want to overcome challenge and test their own will.

10/10!\t\t\t\t\t
