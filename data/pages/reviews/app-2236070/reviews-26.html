I peaked the first category of obstacles only to be brought a parcel containing the next category, bouldering. I thought, "Woah, a new type of climbing! Cool!" I finished the bouldering category only to be brought ANOTHER parcel containing the advanced category. I said "Oh no, here come the final mountains." That was only the path to get TO the actual final mountain, the tallest obstacle I have ever seen in gaming history... The Great Solemn Tempest. I cannot believe how insanely massive Solemn Tempest is, and here I am thinking, "Surely I can beat all mountains without rope, right? Checkpoint?" No... This game is for sure worth buying, but you must know that you have to be sane and mentally prepared for the final challenges if you even dare try to peak them, especially the final mountain...
