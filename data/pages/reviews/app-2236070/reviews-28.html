\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can see many people comparing this to "Getting Over It", but I highly disagree. GOI is a rage game at heart, instantly throwing you into a difficult game with little to no control of what you do. This game is the opposite. The Beginner levels teach you the basics and movement, the Intermediate teaches speed and accuracy, and the Advanced put it to the test. It gets to be very difficult at the end but the game perfectly eases you into it. The physics are perfectly janky without any speed limit allowing for crazy shenaniganery as well. Also, the charm is off the charts. I actually enjoyed meeting the people and finding items along different climbs, wanting to finish as quickly as possible to go back to the Cabin and shelve my treasures. This is easily one of my favorite games topping my list alongside Celeste and Enter the Gungeon. I don't know if it'll ever go on sale but if you ever see it do so, easiest snag ever. To the developer, congratulations on one of the best games of 2023. Also, please give me a gun so I can finally shoot those damn gulls. XD
