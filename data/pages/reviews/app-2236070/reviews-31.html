10/10 would recommend if these are your genre of games.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely great game!

Still yet to take the final mountain, but I have some big grievances.

There is this thing I called the "Rub Around" where the hands rub around everything but the Hold that you aimed for. In a sentence it would be like: "Man, you just got rub arounded" or "fekkin rub around!" or "I dont like that this game clearly has this rub around thing going on". This "Rub Around" made me fall many times, and it got really annoying especially in this final mountain.

Another thing is, playing with toggle, Ice picks dont toggle. And there is a host of bugs that prevent me from pulling out an arm that has to do with Ice picks. I kinda like that ice picks do not toggle, but it definetly ruins the "toggle feature" as a feature. The bugs dont help even further.

I also feel like CTRL jump is a bit too strong. It becomes overly relied on in many points of the game and you essentially can speedrun most mountains with it in an unsatisfying way that goes against the games climbing mechanics. I understand why it exists, its good in a way but it is totally too strong resulting in bumping my head on a ceiling (even really small) resulting in downwards falling sooner. I rather have the pull up mechanic/momentum be better, and I know there is even items for that...

Now, final thing that irked me and that is something to do with trends in games. Doing side-objectives and collectibles simply make the game easier.

The game is at its hardest at the start of the game, also because you are getting used to it. But as soon as you get most tools, the game is a lot easier. This keeps going until I started to ask the question "Why even make me limited in these items" at one point, spoilers: Its lifetime supply of chalk+coffee.

I really question the actual difficulty of this game. I can make the final mountain, its just a question of time investement at the moment. But having the side objectives make the game easier is what makes these mountains progressible. Not the players skill. Some of the mountains even require knowledge, and as such extra items helps explore these mountains so that one can do them without the tools after.

But it is a very pleasant, cozy game!\t\t\t\t\t
