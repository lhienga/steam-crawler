\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game I wasn't sure I'd enjoy. Saw a video of a dude playing it and wanted to try it out and am I ever glad I did. The feeling of overcoming a hurdle you thought you might not be able to, and looking back at where you came from is a great feeling. My palms got sweaty while playing sometimes with how nerve wracking it can be.

It's a game that encourages you to not give up, to keep trying cause as long as you don't stop, you haven't truly failed to climb it. You don't need to be a god gamer to reach the top of any of the mountains, just patience, practice, and persistence. There are several optional objectives to complete that give you more tools to reach the summit, and plenty of additional challenges to pursue if you actually are a master climber like time trials or climbing without rope.

As of writing, I'm about to tackle the final mountain of the game. With a bit of grit and determination, I know I'll reach it. And you can too.
