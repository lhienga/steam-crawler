Don't get me wrong, it's not perfect, and there are some flaws, like the occasional janky buggy hand movement that blocks you from grabbing a hold you're trying to reach, but it makes up for these minor flaws in beautiful fashion.

Each peak is it's own unique challenge, and each feels so rewarding upon reaching their summit. I'm only halfway through the second book with the bouldering challenges right now, but I am aware of the harder mountains such as Ymir's Shadow, and each peak looks better than the last.
