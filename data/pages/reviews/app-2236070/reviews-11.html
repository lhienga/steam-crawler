\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPeaks of Yore is a game I happen to stumble across and buy on a whim. It is outrageously fun with lots of content, it is one of those games that while the basic skills are relatively easy to grasp, the skill ceiling is in the stars. Just when you think you're getting pretty good at the game you'll see someone post a video of them doing the same thing but 30 time faster and more impressive. The game rewards you for going out of your way to push yourself, whether it be in the form of collectibles, more rope and other resources, or just a straight up buff. While some people may enjoy the game for what it is and be done with after scaling the mountain on their own time, others will become obsessed and strive for 100% by forcing themselves to complete insane challenges that require a solid grasp on all of the game's mechanics (and the rock wall you're desperately clinging to). Overall, a wonderful game and a fun experience for a very reasonable price. Looking forward to more content!\t\t\t\t\t
