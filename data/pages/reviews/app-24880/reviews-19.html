Do stealth missions for the Free French Resistance and blow up Nazi's, but most of my time as a kid was spent racing in events. So, I never really paid much attention to the story, I'll let you discover that yourself or let someone else do the explaining - they'd do a much better job than I can.

Grab it on the cheap, EA doesn't need to rob us anymore than they already do. One of my favorite games of the 2010's. Played it way back on the Xbox 360. It still holds up well and may remind you of games like Bully, Mercenaries 2: World in Flames, Just Cause, and even Assassin's Creed.

Widescreen issues could be an problem but setting to 1920x1080 seemed to work better for me, better than 2560x1080. You may have to fiddle around in video options before it looks good. It does start and play smoothly which is really good for a game it's age.\t\t\t\t\t
