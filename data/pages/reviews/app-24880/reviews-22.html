\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery interesting open world GTA clone game.

I first heard about this open-world WWII GTA-clone game The Saboteur back in early 2018 since I was familiar toward now defunct Pandemic Games, and this game was the last game that Pandemic Games had developed. I had quite of interest of seeing this game back when It was not here on Steam. I could see this game is underrated-ly good by some people. I also had plans to play this game sometime, even into the 2020's, even at the time that this game wasn't on Steam during time being.

And years later to this today this March 7, when this game makes a surprise release on Steam itself, I was very surprised. I was happy that it's finally here after not been available on Steam for many years since the release in 2009. I purchased it and I started to play this game just one hour after I purchase it, and I tried it out and I turns out to be actually that great. I really love the setting of German-occupied France during World War II in the early 1940's. It's a interesting concept, and I liked the idea off a Assassin's Creed-Wallenstein-GTA mixed up all around gameplay, and It's super cool to me.

At the end of the day, I'm nearly 100 minutes into the game and the story, and I have plans to continue on for more further storyline and interesting things throughout. If you're a fan of GTA clones, you'd might like this one as much as I do.\t\t\t\t\t
