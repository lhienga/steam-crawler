\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game. Probably played through it more times than any other open world game. The map is a good size, it feels like Yakuza's Kamurocho map, in that it's small enough to become familiar with, but big enough to give you plenty to explore, without it ever feeling like endless empty space like is popular in some other open world games.

However, it's only playable with keyboard. For that reason alone I can't recommend it. I bought it back on Green Man Gaming I think a decade or something back, and the controller worked fine. Steam's release removes that functionality. The face buttons work but there's no way to walk or sneak, which given the nature of the game is, yknow, important. And I never could drive with the keyboard.

I'm really disappointed. Maybe it'll be tweaked in future. I'm hoping EA one day remasters it. But this release? It ain't it, chief :( Also when I tried quitting, it crashed my whole computer and I had to restart. I'll be going for that refund now until I hear it's playable with gamepad.

In short, I recommend the hell out of this game, but not on Steam. I can't believe I'm saying this, but get it through the EA App, which feels better and has native controller support with no faffing required.\t\t\t\t\t
