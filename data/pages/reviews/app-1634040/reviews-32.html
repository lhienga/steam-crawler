\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoth of the survivors have really interesting perks that add more play styles to the game. Nemesis seems like a very noob friendly killer which is not a bad thing. He is pretty spooky and has loud footsteps. His perks are pretty decent and fit his character pretty well. His power is great but it has counter play because he needs to infect you first before the power can start damaging you. There are also vaccines around the map for you to cure the infection although there are only 4 per match. His zombie AI buddies are cool but they seem to get stuck on stuff a lot so even if they're on the other side of the gen, they'll just keep walking into the gen and not go around it sometimes. Overall, I'd say the Resident Evil dlc is definitely a hit in my book! All 3 characters it adds bring a lot of interesting things to the table. I think Nemesis just needs his AI zombie buddies tweaked so they don't get stuck on everything. Very interesting step forward introducing AI to the game though!\t\t\t\t\t
