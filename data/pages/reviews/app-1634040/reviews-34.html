\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter the disappointing buggy release of the Twins, and the really weak release of the Trickster, I'm so happy to report that the Resident Evil chapter is definitely one of the best chapter ever made in DbD, especially if you're a Resident Evil fan.

I'm sure Nemesis is a strong killer, not only that, he feels really good to play as, regardless if he's good or bad. His perks are really good, I think Lethal Pursuer is going to be one of the most used perk in higher ranks, though still not as much as ruin, pop and ect. Hysteria got a really nice buff which definitely made it feel good to use. Eruption is a good alternative of Surge though it's not as good as Pop or Hex: Ruin.

As for the survivors, I think this time I actually like the survivor mostly because I knew Jill and Leon. Their perks is really good though probably annoying to play against as a killer especially Blast Mine.

The Map is kinda awful but I don't care because it's very faithful to the remake.

Overall, this became my new favorite chapter and it beat my old favorite Silent Hill.\t\t\t\t\t
