What I wasn't ready for is the overall laziness of this chapter. I guess I was expecting a little more quality control since it's a licensed chapter:

1) From killer perspective, nemesis runs with both fists clenched at bottom of screen. Not only does this look foolish, but from survivor perspective he does actual run animation by swinging arms (see second screenshot above).

2) They've used the exact same survivor animation used to show healing for when you need to inject vial.

Some of this will (hopefully), get fixed. If history repeats itself they will bring these fixes to a future public test build, which is weeks away. This game can be fun, don't get me wrong there...but in the end we are paying for DLC that is unpolished, unfinished, and only gets partially fixed since devs will just throw in game currency at ppl as an apology and many are strangely fine with that.
