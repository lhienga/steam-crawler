\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome having Resident Evil characters in-game.
Leon's Bite the Bullet is nice & can be pretty useful, albeit nullified through A Nurse's Calling.
Rookie Spirit is also nice. I don't think it's particularly amazing, but it's useful; especially on maps where you can't find generators.
Flashbang is the star of the show, being the only way to use firecracker-esque items all-year round. Really wish they would make Chinese Firecrackers permanent, though.
Jill's Counterforce is alright. One of the weaker totem hunting perks, but it works well enough. Frustrating when someone cleanses the totem you're going after before you get there, though.
Resurgence is good. Halves healing time off hook. Quarters it if the savior has We'll Make It.
Blast Mine is just okay. A nuisance, but it doesn't matter most of the time; killers will just kick the gen again anyway.
As for Nemesis, he is one of the most fun killers they've added in quite a while. You really feel like a behemoth, with how his footsteps sound. The audio when you hit someone, whether it be with your fists or tentacle, is very satisfying. The zombies don't help out often, but they can lead to funny situations when they do help out. The tentacle breaking pallets & walls at tier 2 is the best part, one of the fastest pallet breaks in the game, and you don't slow down as much as, say, Demogorgon after shredding a pallet. It is also a pain in the rear for survivors to vaccinate after being contaminated, as there are a limited number of vaccines, & every time someone uses one, their location is revealed for a short duration.
Nemesis's Lethal Pursuer is easily his best perk. The start of a match is often the most crucial part, and this gives you much-needed information to go do something; even if it's not a guaranteed hook, you can at least injure/contaminate someone, or break pallets/walls.
Hysteria is mediocre. Could be good on someone like Legion, though..
Eruption is okay. It's a sidegrade to Surge with more potential benefit.

The only qualm I have is that Raccoon City Police Department is one of the worst maps they've ever added. I still like it for the visuals & audio, but actually playing on it as both survivor & killer is a horrid experience.
Overall, one of the best DLCs. Get it if you haven't.\t\t\t\t\t

Jill and Leon (the survivors) have some meme-worthy perks whilst the others are more catered for newer players. Their models are accurate to the franchise and even have some cool legendary skins too in the forms of Chris and Claire Redfield.

Nemesis is very accurate to his RE3 remake counterpart, albeit his power is a bit underwhelming, but the use of zombies can be cool/frustrating/funny as hell at times. You also get the RE2 Raccoon City Police Station map (Remake) which is also very true to form and accurate to the source game, albeit a few slight alternations to make it flow better as a DBD map. It's huge and unless you've played RE2 remake it can rather hard to navigate, but over all they did a good job with the map and characters.\t\t\t\t\t
