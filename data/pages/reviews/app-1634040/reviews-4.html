The Survivors (Leon S. Kennedy and Jill Valentine): 5/5
Both Jill and Leon fit well into Dead by Daylight's roster of survivors. Their appearance is true to their characters from the Resident Evil franchise. Leon and Jill's teachable perks are very unique (especially Flashbang and Blast Mine), providing new ways to fight back against the killer.

The Killer (The Nemesis): 5/5
The Nemesis is quite possibly the most unique killer we have in the game. His power is fun to play as and against - with the zombies and vaccines creating very dynamic gameplay. The Nemesis' visual appearance is good and he feels as strong and threatening to play as he looks. The Nemesis' perks are all good although Lethal Pursuer stands out as his best one (and is especially strong on killers like The Nurse). I find myself having fun playing The Nemesis even when I lose.

The Map (Raccoon City Police Station): 4/5
[Note: You can play the map without owning the DLC] Raccoon City Police Station is a BIG map with a lot of rooms. It is very different from any map we currently have and makes for pretty unique gameplay. The map can be rather like a maze if you (like myself) are unfamiliar with the Resident Evil games although I imagine it's easier for fans of the franchise. That being said, the map has a LOT of atmosphere and small nods to the source material (such as the ) which is neat. I really can't fault the visuals of the map - the attention to detail is unmatched.
