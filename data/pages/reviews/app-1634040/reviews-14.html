\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a pretty great Chapter pack, with some decent perks, and as to be expected with other Chapter packs, new maps, new characters, and a new killer. You have Leon and Jill for Survivors, and Nemesis for Killers. Leon has 2 outfits that you can purchase via microtransactions, his bomber jacket outfit from RE4, and a full model replacement outfit, Chris Redfield (RE5 face and hair on his RE1 outfit). Jill has 2 outfits that you can purchase as well, her blue dress outfit from RE3, and a full model replacement outfit, Claire Redfield (RE2 Remake model). The outfits for Leon are pretty good, but Jill's Claire outfit is really off to me, Claire's model just looks weird for some reason, her model has a really weak chin and it makes her face look pretty ugly from side angles. Nemesis has no outfits, which is a bummer because they could've made Mr. X as an outfit or even the T-002 Tyrant from RE1. The new perks for both Leon and Jill are decent, nothing amazing but they aren't terrible. Nemesis is fun to play as, and to go up against as a Survivor. The new map for this Chapter pack is taken out of the map rotation pool due to issues and crashes, so as of writing this review, you still cannot play the RPD map and I have not been able to play it myself. Apparently the RE4 outfit for Leon and RE3 outfit for Jill were able to have their pieces bought separately, but then the devs changed it to where you had to buy the whole outfit instead of certain pieces, which I think is pretty stupid considering the RE4 Leon head just has slightly longer and more blond hair, and the RE3 Jill head is literally the same head that is used on the default Jill outfit, no changes to it or anything. Overall, this Chapter pack is pretty cool, but with the issues it has and the fact that the map released with this pack is still taken out of the game, this pack should've been cheaper.\t\t\t\t\t
