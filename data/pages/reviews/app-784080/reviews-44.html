The AI in this game genuinely makes me enraged it is so bad, yes. THAT BAD. Mechwarrior 4 puts this game to shame.

Not to mention this game has the weakest atmosphere/acting/campaign in any game I've played in the 2020's. It's clear that Piranha games doesn't even have a fraction of the passion for BATTLETECH that FASA Studio and Zipper Interactive did. My first mech game was Mechwarrior 4 Vengeance, and this game doesn't have 1/10th the atmosphere, quality of characters, good music, good missions that MW4V has.

To top it all off? Awful area design. Boring repetitive maps. Maps with unbreakable geometry that can get you permanently stuck so you're left with no choice but to restart the mission.

Do I recommend you buy this game? No. Absolutely not. The ONLY instance I would recommend buying this game is if you have a friend who owns all the DLC, you can buy the base game and piggy back off of their DLC while you play with them and 2 other friends. Otherwise this game is absolutely not worth your money.

Right now it's on sale at $17CAD, normally it's $34CAD. At $34CAD I could write you a book full of better games you could buy for the same price or less.

I recently finished the Legend of the Kestrel Lancers DLC in which you work for house Davion to fight house Liao. I started in house Steiner and my reputation with them is flawless. My reputation with house Davion is in the second best tier. So You would think that when they unite into FEDCOM in 3041 that they would love me right? Naw my reputation with FEDCOM is -15. This is so stupid!

I should also mention that at the time of release this game had TERRIBLE performance issues and they were charging $50USD for it with no DLC.

I seriously hope the devs do a better job with MechWarrior 5 Clans. There is zero chance I'll be preordering that game and very little chance I'll be buying it until I can grab it for like $20 unless it's drastically different from MechWarrior 5 Mercenaries.

I tried this game back in 2020 about a month after it came out in December and I was very disappointed. Here I am playing it in late 2023 hoping that with years the devs would have fixed it but nope. They certainly have not.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a must for BattleTech and MechWarrior fans alike. First of all, it introduces a co-op mode for the first time in the history of the serial, which is great fun.

For anyone who isn't familiar with the MechWarrior games, you can expect a light mech-pilot simulator, that's gameplay focuses around an arcade shooter style. You are a mercenary in the 31st century, and you fight your way through a variety of missions in mechs, wrought from a historical fiction setting. BattleMechs. Ranging from 30 - 100 tons of armor and raw firepower. The base game offers decent customization for the newcomer, easy to understand and work with each new chassis you find.

If you are interesting in the potential of modding, Mechwarrior 5: Mercenaries offers full mod support, from a very passionate community. There are new challenges, and even more levels of customization waiting within the workshop here. New mission types, lore accurate and expansive customization, and plenty of new mechs and variants of favorites.

It is both satisfying in gameplay, and aesthetic. The only gripe I can have with the game is the biomes of the base, some are dreary, and uninspiring. Though, again with mods, there is plenty of opportunity there. In addition to other client-side HUD and appearance mods.

The main gripe I have with the game, is that the AI for any allied units, including your own NPC pilots withing your lance, are awful. There are also mods to help with this, but for someone who wants to try the game without committing to modding it, etc. Keep this in mind. The AI is garbage.

Multiplayer in this game is really where it shines. It's enjoyable without it, but if you want the truly best experience you can have with MechWarrior 5: Mercenaries is through the co-op. Not only do you have friends to laugh, and blast through missions beside, you can negate the AI and all of its faults with a full 4 person lance. The more the merrier, as they say! It doesn't absolutely require, or make things impossible with less than four people. I've played quite a bit of the game and made it through with just myself and a friend!

That said, with the newly added free update, Piranha, the publisher of this game addressed difficulty with a wide range of options to choose from. Making it more playable for the new player, or veterans alike.

Furthermore, although, there had been seemingly much unfortunately to be desired with the base game, Pirahna has delivered many enjoyable and game-changing DLCs for cheap, both extending the longevity of the game with challenging, and engaging campaigns. AS well as adding free updates that address quality of life improvements, and core assets to the gameplay.

DLC is also shareable in that if the host of a multiplayer game owns a DLC, it is shared with the whole lance. Pretty nifty.

So all in all, it is a fun game, it is something you can take as seriously as you like, and enjoy from solo. I hope this helps you understand this game, I do quite enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
