\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMechs are cool metal music is cool I guess but that's about all the good I can say about the game.
You get some cool moments where you feel like an unstoppable beast of a machine rampaging through buildings and mowing down tanks and smaller mechs but as soon as you reach a group of medium armored enemies the game slows to a crawl and becomes a chore.

Usually one has a way to outplay or use some sort of maneuver to get the upper hand in slugfests. This game doesn't give you any tool except running in circles around enemies the problem with running around in circles is your AI teammates will follow do pirouettes and stop shooting obviously this is a problem when your only options in outplaying is speed and accuracy/volume of firepower. There could be updates to the combat system to spice things up don't give me "muh tradition muh keepin it faithful" the game play is very dated and simple.

The worst things are about the game are the pointless time wasters like THE LOADING SCREENS BETWEEN PNG STARS WHY? I have to see the ship warp twice to fix after every contract just so I don't go broke because the game charges you double if you fix your mechs in a contested area of space. Another is talking to the "Indian lady?" on the bridge. I don't know if this is user error but when the game wants you to talk to her it spawns you 50ft own and down two flights of stairs and is just like GET CLIMBIN BISH sometimes it wants you to climb back down to talk to the "Indian SIR". THEN after it removes about 600k about a missions worth of credits to progress the story I'm not listening to.

The games worth like 10 bucks at most.\t\t\t\t\t
