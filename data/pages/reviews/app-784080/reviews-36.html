üìãMechWarrior 5: Mercenaries is an exhilarating action mech simulator that drops you right into the cockpit of massive war machines. It's a game that will transport you to the future of mechanized warfare, offering endless excitement and replayability. If you have a passion for commanding towering battle mechs, this game is a must-play, providing countless hours of enjoyment with all the content the game has to offer mods withstanding.

ü§ñ The year is 3015...War rages on...

üî∑In MechWarrior 5, you're thrust into a war-torn 31st-century universe, where factions vie for control, and colossal mechs are the ultimate weapons. The game's rich lore and backstory set the stage for an epic adventure as you lead your own band of mercenaries in this tumultuous future. As you progress, navigating the political quagmire of the Great Houses becomes more precarious as your reputation proceeds you for your services. This leads to any number of play throughs as siding with certain Successor State's can change the course of your campaign and lock you off from missions, quest and rewards depending on the side you chose to help.

üéÆ Gameplay üéÆ

üî∑MechWarrior 5 is a love letter to fans of classic mech combat. It not only captures the essence of piloting these giant machines but also embraces the challenging learning curve and the strategic depth of the genre. Master said mechanics takes time and dedication.

‚ö†Ô∏èWarning: Newcomers may find the intricacies of mech customization and piloting a bit daunting at first, but once you get the hang of it, it's incredibly rewarding.

üéØNote: For those who prefer a more straightforward experience, you can simplify the controls to play more like an FPS and customize numerous aspects to the difficulty to suit your play style and skill-set.

ü§ñGot to catch em' all!

üî∑ In the year 3015, the flames of war continue to engulf the galaxy, and the battlefield is dominated by an array of formidable battle mechs. These towering war machines include iconic models such as the Spider, Zeus, Battlemaster, Orion, Griffin, Archer, Blackjack, Hunchback, Grasshopper and many more, each with their own unique variants, combat capabilities and loadouts. Top of that, each mech has a common, rare and hero variant to collect with hero variants being the most sought after for their special paint schemes, quirks and loadouts that usually make them far stronger than their counterparts.

üî∑ With additional DLCs, MechWarrior 5 boasts an even more extensive roster of mechs and variants expanding the total count quite a bit to provide an even greater variety options to utilize and explore during combat.

üî∑What stands out with MechWarrior 5 to me is the sheer variety it offers players. Each mission feels unique, thanks to the dynamic mission system and the extensive range of mechs and loadout options available.

üî∑You start with a basic mech, but as your journey unfolds, you'll collect and customize more powerful war machines. Your choices in weaponry and equipment dramatically impact your playstyle and tactics.

üî∑After each mission, you're rewarded based on your performance and the objectives you completed, allowing you to upgrade you to take 'shares' earned to salvage new mechs and equipment which in turn you'll use to enhance your mechs, build new mechs and expand your mercenary company.

üî∑The sound effects for this game are outright incredible. Guns sound punchy, explosions sound crispy, hot, gassy and I swear I can even hear the air hiss as it ignites from super heated plasma lasers. üìñConclusion

üü£ MechWarrior 5: Mercenaries is a game that truly delivers on the fantasy of piloting massive mechs in a future where war knows no bounds. This game is perfect for mech enthusiasts and co-op aficionados alike. It's an immersive journey into the world of mechanized warfare, offering countless hours of gameplay. MechWarrior 5 may have its minor flaws, but the thrill of piloting a mech makes it a must-play.
