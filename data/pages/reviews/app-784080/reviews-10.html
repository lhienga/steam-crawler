\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I played a bit of MW5 over the weekend. I think they got the spirit of MW2 Mercs. That's a good thing. I like that they have pushed gameplay over pretty. The action feels like MW2 in how frenitic it can get the AI control of the rest of the lance is a lot better than it had been in the past in that it will try to flank and doesn't just run directly at crap. Performance is smooth which is to be expected with the lack of graphics complexity. The game world is a lot more open in that you can travel about and take on all sorts of missions. It is comparable to the Battletech turn based VG from 2018 (HBS). I could see it being daunting to people who just want a shoot 'em up game in that it requires resource management and strategy skills to survive past the tutorial. The depth is high. The only thing they should look to fix in the future is to get the voice acting beyond a few phrases like "I've been hit!". The NPC's are a bit stiff like XCOM2. Overall it is addictive.\t\t\t\t\t
