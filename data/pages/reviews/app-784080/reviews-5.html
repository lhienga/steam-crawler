\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolaris dropped and discovered i did not review anything in MW5 which is sad so the start of my reviewing spam.

Veterans and Old heads of battletech and Mechwarrior probably already have this game at this point, so I will not drone on about how freaking great it is to see Mechwarrior back. I will point more towards those interested in jumping into a Far future Science Fiction IP; in my opinion can rival warhammer 40k is awesome because: BIG FREAKING BATTLE MECHS!

This game is a good launch in point for those who never experienced the past Mechwarrior games, it does not continue what was trying to be continued in MW4 and hard resets us back to the start of the 3000 timeline. This is great as you can experience the Lore in a more sequence of events. The only bad part if you don't get to experience Clan stuff and anything from them for the most part. Yet MW5 clan has been announced so even more reason to experience MW5 mercs before the best event in battletech happens: Clan invasion

The game itself is pretty solid, the vanilla campaign hurts a bit as your player character seems bland. It gives good feeding of the lore of the world and some fun mission here and there. Yet the best part of the base game is the sandbox and merc SIM aspect of the game. You really run your outfit from planning financials to blow up mechs. There is a large pool of mechs and even more variants they you will find to find your way of battle.

So, if you are thinking of giving the game a try, please do.\t\t\t\t\t
