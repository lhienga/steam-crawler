Overall 8.5/10, I wish it had more reliable net code for co-op across the pond, and simpler ways to fix weird host-client de-sync where you need to save game, load into single-player, sell something, travel to another system, buy something, save and load back into co-op so your buddy can access the main menu of the game to interact with anything, including load into missions which requires a ready check.\t\t\t\t\t
