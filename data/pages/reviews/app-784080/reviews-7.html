\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a tough review. There are SO MANY THINGS WRONG with this game. But the pure joy you get from lumbering around in massive mechs is just done so well.

The good first. The mechs look fantastic. The guns feel good and sound good. The variety and amount of customization is staggering (Why do you need to be able to adjust the amount of armor on a single body part - but hey, why not). The graphics associated with the actual mechs and weapons look great too. Lots of loot to find and lots of side quests to keep you going through many hours of gameplay, which has been helped greatly with the expansions. The modding community is massive and has many upgrades already available. And for me, that has been enough to keep playing this game for 80 hours, and I have plans to keep playing and find more mechs and perfect my loadouts.

THAT BEING SAID, I will discuss some of the problems (not enough space for them all). So many of them are silly things that could probably be easily fixed. When in the cockpit - your gait is a certain speed. But when you switch to an outside view, that gait speed is different. When your leg is blown off, you walk with a limp and slower, but if you switch to 3rd person view, nothing is noticeable. This is the theme for lots of stuff that is wrong with the game - just not polished I suppose. Typos in the text. Maps used over and over with different weather or time of day. Procedurally generated missions have the same voice overs over and over again. The cities feel quite dead and lifeless (We know this engine can do better). The reputation system caps out far before you're done with the game. Occasionally getting stuck on maps, or allies getting stuck. When you "interact" with characters in your ship, they're completely lifeless. Then why bother putting this in? The list goes on.

But alas, its not enough to keep me from playing the game. Many things can be fixed by mods, or ignored. Hopefully the developers will continue to address this in expansions and updates, and make the clans game much better. Never has there been such a great way to destroy stuff with 100 ton robots.\t\t\t\t\t
