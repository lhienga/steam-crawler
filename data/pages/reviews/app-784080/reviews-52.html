\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game last year and went through most of the campaign and enjoyed it well enough but it really becomes amazing once you add some mods. After spending some time picking out mods, my new career mode has been a ton of fun.

PilotOverhaul - Darkest Dungeon like quirks with negatives and positives. They gain proficiency piloting the same mech. And it adds a few story missions with some newly voice acted pilots. This mods really fleshes out the pilot side of the mercenary simulation.

Coyote Mission - Since you are mostly just dropping into procedural generated missions, anything that adds more variety and more options is a good thing

Simple Zoom - I love the simple zoom install option that zooms in your entire screen instead of doing the tiny square window

Stacked Crates - Makes crates actually worth going for! Alot of fun to diverge and get good gear+cbills.

VonBiomes - similar to coyote missions this mod does some extreme heavy lifting for adding variety

YAML suite of mods - I am a little less sold on this compared to others. It definitely makes the game more enjoyable since tweaking your mechs is more fun with a more robust mechlab. It can make the mech variants feel less special since you can change them so much but I think overall this is worth adding. So much variety, additional mech variants, parts, etc.

Once you add all the DLC plus the above mods the career mode and mercenary company simulation become just so satisfying and the gameplay loop I cannot get enough of. Highly recommend!\t\t\t\t\t
