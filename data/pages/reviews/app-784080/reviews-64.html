\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game out of my love for Mechwarrior, but it was sadly disappointing. Its hours on end of grinding redundant missions in order to handle the more difficult, but very few and shallow, campaign missions. Eventually you will get sick of trashing endless waves of mechs that spawn all over the map forcing you to throw conventional battlefield tactics out the window. Then you find out there are tons of cool mods that you can get for free that fixes some of these problems. But then that begs the question, why are buying a shoddy, half-made game just so that more talented people can mod it to make it playable at best? We shouldn't, that's why.

I only wish that there were more handcrafted missions that were more varied, like in the previous MechWarrior games. Nonetheless, the game is a lot of fun. Also, shout out to the developers for making a game so mod-friendly. Seriously. MechWarrior 4 was much harder to mod (hence why MekTek was such a big deal) compared to this game.\t\t\t\t\t
