\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have fond memories of playing MechWarrior 2 in the late 90's on my uncle's PC. So, when I seen this game, I had to try it out and it did not disappoint. I will say that if you buy this game, I highly recommend adding Heroes of the Inner Sphere DLC. I told this to my wife and once she added it, she agrees that it makes it much more enjoyable to play. The Cantina quests add an extra way of getting rewards and cash--which are highly needed during gameplay. Without cash (in-game, not real cash), you can't repair your mechs, pay your bills (pilot wages and etc), or even travel to a new world.

Wish I could give you more details, but as you can see, I've not played a lot, but what I have played is really fun. The map in the game is monstrous! The one drawback I would say is that if you travel too far from your location in the space map, you can't see what type of quests are available. Again, not sure if I'm doing something wrong, or if that's how it is, but it would be nice to see what type of quests are available before spending 100's of thousands of money traveling, just to have to go back to an area because it's too difficult. My apologies if this is inexperience talking and not an issue.

Hopefully this review is helpful and enjoy taking on the worlds, one planet at a time!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved MechWarrior 2 Mercenaries. This game, while fun for a while, is overall a step backward and not a good value-- there are only like 12 truly handmade scripted missions; all the rest of the missions in the game are one of 5 auto-generated tasks against random enemies: destroy an outpost, defend an outpost, destroy a series of outposts, fight a random set of mechs, kill specific mech, That would be fine if this were a mobile game, but it's billed as an "A" title, and they didn't invest in the content I'd expect from that.

The map auto-generation is OK in that it provides a variety of different hills, but that's not really enough to avoid the tedium of playing the same basic mission 20 times. They really skimped on content and seem to have thought no one would notice. If you're really hankering for a MechWarrior game it's ok, but I'm not sure why they didn't just do an updated MechWarrior 2. If they actually had proper handmade missions the game would be awesome. Instead it seems they chose the team that bid the lowest total tonnage of level creators...\t\t\t\t\t
