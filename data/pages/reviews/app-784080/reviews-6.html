MechWarrior Online is based on CryEngine, and this was going to be as well, until Epic Games™ came along with their loose wallet drooling dollars. A Steam sale kindly reminded me this even exists and again: it's aight. Mechs are a bit less overly technical than what MWO offers. But if you really want to choose every bolt on your bot, there's a mod for that. On the plus side, mods are highly compatible. So, most can be used without much issue and they go a decent distance in helping to fill dev gaps.

Coop is a thing but the poor optimization almost ruins it, unless you have a GPU only corporations or God can afford. Maps are kinda bland, though thankfully a mod addresses that, but then again it can kneecap already-poor performance depending on which map.

Good enough, if you're into MechWarrior.\t\t\t\t\t
