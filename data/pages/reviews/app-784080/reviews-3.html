\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMechwarrior is unique interesting and detailed universe, that came from the tabletop games and books almost 40 years ago. All battle mechs are one of a kind, with their own style, names, history and characteristics. This game for me is at the same time good and frustrating. It is fun to play, but some fun-breaking features of it just ruined the experience.

Positive. Mech building/refitting system. It is very interesting to try and balance different configs by yourself. In my opinion interface in Battletech TBS game was better, but nevertheless this game mech constructor is good enough. Gunplay and movement feeling. It is really allows you to believe and imagine youself driving big badass robot, that is able to shoot anything in its way. Except other bigger or better robots.

Negative. No saves or checkpoints in mission. I failed one of the missions third time right before the end and its just SO BORING to start it again, walking through the same scripted scenes and enemies. The first fail though was twice annoying because operator just told me that I finished the mission and right after that in second mech of my partner was destroyed and mission failed. Graphics. It is outdated UE4 blurred mess with poping up LOD objects even on highest settings. Do not turn RTX on, it is very unoptimised and was added to the game not so long time ago. You mostly feel the difference only by the fact that your FPS dropped a lot without almost any noticeable visual difference. Also, mouse sensitivity spike bug on the base is very annoying. Story is so mediocre and not engaging that you will start to not care at all after listening just few character replics and just skip all dialogues in order to start new mech building and fight quickly. Objects in game are destructable, but at the same time they are sooooo copy-pasted that you will not be able to tell a difference between them from mission to mission.

Try it yourself and decide.\t\t\t\t\t
