\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game that makes me wish that Steam had a middling review option, HOWEVER.
This game with mods, is why I am writing this review. The YAML mod collection is more or less why I still play this game.
The base game you are very limited on what you can do with your mech designs, for example in the base game, there's maybe 3 mechs that can carry AMS, MASC, TSM, and superchargers. However, the YAML mod allows you to basically do whatever the hell you want. You want a Flea with a Supercharger and a ER PPC? and a bigger engine than it already has? You can do that. You want a Atlas with Hardened Armor, MASC, and a HAG 30? Yup you sure can!
The Base game has a pretty milquetoast story, and theres some pretty solid DLC campaigns, and those DLC campaigns tied into the YAML mod collection really makes this game shine.
Basically YAML will singlehandedly make this game from mediocre to something that I recommend people (with the YAML caveat)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start off by saying the amount time played is only my newest play through I have just a tad over 300 hours played through Epic games. So yes, I like this game enough to not only buy it twice, but to have all the DLC at the same time. Now what I think more in depth, the controls are a bit clunky but in a good way it feels more like you are really piloting a 20-100 ton mech. Plus you can customize the mechs in so many awesome ways especially with the YAML mods! The game only has a few types of mission and most of the time when that happens in games I get bored, but in this one I don't. Every time you get a new mech or some rare weapon drops or just get a crazy idea for a build (those can be good and awful) it is just damn fun. The story is alright, nothing to write home about and honestly I never finished the main campaign and went on to the sandbox merc for hire mode. I could understand why people don't like this game, it is absolutely not a game for everyone. If you like mech games then I think this one is top tier and highly suggest it.\t\t\t\t\t
