\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are good with several different compatible mods then this game can be very fun. In its base form its mediocre at best with a host of issues that can screw up gameplay often. The devs have allowed mod support ( to an extent at least ) for many of the better mods that you can get through steam here that make the gameplay much better and allow you a greater level of customization for things that can make the game worth the time.

I would say make sure to get it on sale as the cost of the base game plus all the official DLC is a bit too much for its original base form.

Pros: 1) Seriously destructible terrain ( trees, buildings, walls, rocks...etc )
2) Good graphics potential and the ability to have the mechs actually show battle damaged areas.
3) The AI pilots comments on how well you can target and take out other mechs can be very amusing
sometimes. ( A nice little amusing touch )

Cons: 1) They did NOT follow the rules for "legging" a mech....In previous games if you can remove a leg then
the mech is out of the game/dead...but not here...no matter how much damage you deal...they still
get to walk on it....Stupid stupid stupid.
2) The mechs are locked into how many of what type ( missile, ballistic, energy )( and critical location
) they can have which type of weapon/electronic system ( while some of the electronic or engine
systems are limited in their placement the weapon systems are supposed to be modular in nature
and able to be placed in ANY free critical slot(s) / NOTE: There is supposedly a mod that allows this
to be done correctly but it doesnt work for everyone.

All in all though id give it a solid 5/10
