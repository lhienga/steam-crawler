\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThese people did something I would not have thought possible. They took Pacific Rim, put it in a video game and somehow made it dull and repetitive.

In this game, humanity has colonized a myriad of star-systems yet there's only 4 different kinds of planets, Hoth, Mars, Earth and a poisonous-looking one.
The only thing more repetitive are the missions. Which are either defend this, destroy that or kill them, generated by randomly combining a very limited number of preset factions, targets and environments. The different factions have no lore and no identity other then paint-jobs and what they sometimes shout when their mech is destroyed.
What I found particularly absurd were the recon missions in which you pilot your mech behind enemy lines to spy on them. I mean even the small ones are tall as a house and weigh 20 tons, how are you supposed to be stealthy in one of those?!

They can't even seem to convey a feeling that your mechs are big. When fighting tanks, which themselves must weigh several tons, they speed and bump around like toy RC cars.

The game combines a business-running game with command of a mercenary team, customization and giant walking war-machines.
But for those aspects I would rather play Planet Coaster, Star Fox 64, Crimson Skies and Iron Harvest respectively.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a decent game and even better especially for mech fans. It has quite realistic mech gameplay and weapon management system. OST sounds quite heavy metal and rock which is suitable. No crash or froze encountered, only some lagging. I also appreciate its co op feature.

With that being said, this game which was released on December 2019 lacks some improvements in proportion, some are quite critical in my opinion:
1. Cockpit and HUD look are ok, but truly feel lacking. For reference, please check vonHUD mod and cockpit glass, it looks significantly cooler and has cockpit glass is visible. I am sure there are more other HUD Cockpit examples out there for developer to learn. In future release, I wish there is an option for better looking HUD and cockpit option in max graphical option by default.
2. Map graphic on weather, landscape, lightning feel lacking. For reference please check vonBiomes mod, it looks much better and should be an option in max graphical option by default.
3. Overton penalty. This is an unfair and a bit dumb system especially when player only has 4 mechs to face at least twice number of enemies mech and vehicles.
4. Reinforcement. Enemies can easily have reinforcement come anywhere, but player only has 4 mechs. I think there should be option to call mech replacement or call for artillery or air support with cost.
5. Transportation cost. This game can be quite grindy since player need to get money to buy better mechs. Sometimes there can be a distance between place that give good mech discount and place to find good money. Transportation cost is too expensive that I have to install mod to reduce it.
6. Salvage. Sometimes player lack salvage point to salvage mech or weapons, I think there should be pay option to salvage.
7. Related with cockpit, No compass. I installed compass mod and felt that this mod should have been in default game already.
8. Story is ok but lacks dramatic moments and explanation of mech universe.
9. Each map does feel bit different but at same time also feel similar, which can create repetitive feel. These should be improved to reduce repetitive feel.

Overall I feel this game like an underton mech, a bit skeletal for end of 2019 release, decent game but lack quite good of improvement especially on graphics. I would rate this as 7.8/10 and still recommend for decent mech gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
