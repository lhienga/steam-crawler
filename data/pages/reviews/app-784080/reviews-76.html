\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally fun with a few friends and it's still a solid game even by yourself. I've been waiting for something which has felt like Mechwarrior 2, 3 and 4, and this is the closest thing to meeting those urges. It's come a long way from when it debuted and everything I really wished Mechwarrior Online could've been. The level of customisation is great and I feel it's perfect. I was never really interested in the aspect of running a merc company (I was here for the story in the MW universe tbt), but everything that was great about MW4:mercs is expanded on, and it's fun.

The only grating thing I have come to face in this game is that (personally) things can get a little boring post-main story, while the combat is still there to keep you occupied, many of the non-main-story scenarios and missions become a bit predictable and uneventful. It's good that a lot of the DLC has added improvements to the game in various ways but I feel like a lot of those should've been in the main game from the get-go, like rivals and so on. This game is missing a more dynamic AI to make those missions seem more interesting, or chaotic!

Still, absolutely worth checking out if you're looking to get into it.\t\t\t\t\t
