\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt initial launch, Mechwarrior 5 was not as strong as it should have been; however, after continuous support and several DLCs, this game is really fun.

What I like:
- A focus on giant stompy robots. While fast, zippy robots a la Armored Core are fun, there's also a great satisfaction stomping around with 3 other stompy robots blasting everything in sight

- A sense of power growth. As you play the game, be it campaign or career, you will notice a nice improvement in your capabilities, both with the mechs you field and the weapons you have available

- Detailed lore. There's a lot of lore to read in the game if you want, but you don't have to if you don't want to; however, do make sure you pay your phone bill, or Comstar will get cranky with you.

- A great introduction to the BattleTech Universe. This game eases in players who aren't familiar with the BattleTech universe, or who have only played HBS' 2018 Battletech (which you should also go play!)

- Mod support. Oh man, mods REALLY make this game. Play through it once vanilla to get a feel for how the mechanics work. Then, pick up YAML (Yet Another Mech Lab) and YAW (Yet Another Weapon). These two mods will add a ton of new items and give you massive customization options.
================================================================
Things I don't like:
- Procedural Missions - some mission types aren't great, though recent patches have really improved the higher-tier demolition missions

- Weak story initially - The campaign is kinda rough from a story perspective; fortunately, each DLC has better stories than the last

- Inconsistent scripted mission difficulty - some missions are just HARD, and when they show up can be inconsistent; however, with the ability to go over tonnage, it makes it easier to handle those rough missions

- AI derpiness - While I have not had a ton of problems with the lancemate AI, some players have reported significant difficulty with them. From time to time, they will do stupid, stupid things, like your long range mechs charge in to give hugs to the enemy.

=================================================================

Overall, I have really enjoyed MW5. While vanilla can be a little weak, with 2 easy-to-install mods, this game is top notch.

85/100, 90/100 with rice mods\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not big on FPS-games since I have slow reactions. But in this game I can (mostly) keep up with the changing conditions on whatever battlefield I'm fighting on.

Never played any other games in the Mechwarrior franchise, but I'm having a blast in this one!

The controls are mostly intuitive, one glaring exception being the map-button being bound to 'B' by default and the actual map being called 'Battlegrid', which annoyed me until I read up on the controls online.

All in all, I'd say this game is a solid 8/10 with a fun campaign (with decent VA) and a sandbox career-mode.\t\t\t\t\t
