\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, overrated. The game feels poorly optimized, and does not look very good in my opinion. Feels like the VFX/animations are from the 90's and just reskinned with super high res textures. The weapons just don't look or feel that good and the gameplay is just .... bleh. Boring. I tried the campaign / tutorial, and immediately got bored. Then I ran some random skirmish type missions, and also got bored.

I understand it's like a strategic FPS type game but the gameplay is just very lackluster. I have no interest in learning all the weapon specs and tweaking my builds. Why do I need 20 variations of the same weapon? IDK. Would rather play Earth Siege from the 90's.\t\t\t\t\t

Yes, enemies slowly get harder. Yet nothing new happens in general. You drop to mission, you shoot all red, you go back. Money? You don't care, mission pays back needed damages.
