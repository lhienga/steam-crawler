That whole part of the game feels like an Xbox (2001) era game, even down to the graphics and the "NPCs" that never move, not even idle animations they're just figures for menus. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tInitially, I said that I wouldn't recommend this game. However my stance has changed. I have played a lot and it has sparked a lot of joy.

First, the bad. The Tone in the Cavaliers story is... a let down. It's a pretty standard father son revenge plot, that echos the Gray Death Trilogy even down to comstar shenanigans. But it lacks the charm of that novel series. Commander Mason is clearly a self insert character for the player, but the lackluster story just kind of pulls you out of it. Fortunately you can ignore this with Heroes of the Inner Sphere and the 330 pilot overhaul mod. It's just a shame that the MW series of games maintains this "dudebro" kind of tone, while stuff like HBS Battletech can be very poignant.

Second, this game NEEDS mods. There is a vibrant community of modders that have added so much to this game that really makes it shine. In some ways it feels like half a game, since there are mods that just really elevate it. However, the game is incredibly easy to mod, which makes it highly customizable.

But for the good points, this game is fabulous as a coop game. You can jump into your friends' games and make whole new playthroughs with friends. It works incredibly well and is a great game to play with friends. It's gotten my play group into Battletech the tabletop game, and we have had a great time playing together.

Finally, the DLC content is really good. With a lot of great content that spans the succession wars, and feels much more faithful to the tone of the setting. the DLC is really worth it, and it is great to see new content for this game, either as mods or as DLC.

I highly recommend MW5 now, as long as you are comfortable modding it. Also, just get Heroes of the inner sphere, and start your own career, and make your own story instead of following commander Mason. Here's to hoping that MW5 Clans will just let you make your character!

No guts, No Galaxy!\t\t\t\t\t
