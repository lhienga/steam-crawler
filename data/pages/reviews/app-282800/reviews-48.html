It's always a joy unlocking stuff (not via spending like $60 USD on DLC, plus buying entirely different games: That is quite stupid.) If you really want the full game, wait for a sale to come around and getting it then. I'm aware that there are mods for this, and as good as mods are, they are indeed modifications).

All in all, it's a simple and fun party game with a lot of DLC, RNG, and even more moe goodness. I'm still playing this game so obviously it's good.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYour anime-styled board game with RNG elements and dices. The cards can be bought through packs in the shop, which means you can build decks without paying any money if you spend enough time in the game. Of course, when you get killed by a duck, you realize that the game can be frustrating at times. Still, I definitely recommend this game for everyone.

Unless you hate ducks.

EDIT: After 4 days of playing, it's been nothing but sad experiences. Campaign's RNG is horrible, but you can tackle it with enough experience and strategies (which I don't think I can manage). In multiplayer, you play against 4 players in a RNG-stuffed playground. No matter how well you prepare, how careful you play, the game will still manage to screw you over out of nowhere. Not only that, streaks of bad luck actually punish you further, as you can be left behind for chapters and gradually lose momentum. I've had 10 games where everyone are at Norma 5 while I'm still at 2 or 3. Sure, it's meant to be a fun game, but when you experience it enough or look further into it, it's too nonsensical to put any time into this game, unless you are sadistic.\t\t\t\t\t
