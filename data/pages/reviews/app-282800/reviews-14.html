It's hard to be bored when entering the 100% Orange Juice franchise, what with it's colorful cast, but if you're not a fan or bullet hells, this might be the perfect game to dip your toes into instead. You'll get to experience the charm of the characters in a more playful (though not necessarily peaceful) board game. I recommend playing the game-- seeing how you like it, and if you enjoy it, go ahead and purchase any dlc character you like (I usually buy them on a good sale).
