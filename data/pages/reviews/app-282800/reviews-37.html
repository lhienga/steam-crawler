\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat board game to play with friends, at an extremely reasonable price on sale. The game may seem very complicated at first, but once you play a game or two, it's easy to at least grasp the basics. Most of the nuance isn't important to learn by heart and won't affect your enjoyment of the game.

There are a lot of paid dlc characters, but none of the game is pay2win since it's pretty much all RNG anyway. Of course, there is some strategy and player choice, but most of what you'll be doing is dictated by randomness, which makes for a good party game.

There's also a singleplayer component which, while not as compelling as playing with friends, is still worth trying out if you need to kill some time with something chilled and fun, or your friends aren't available at the time.

Additionally, the game has mods in the steam workshop. They're mainly just graphical/sound modifications, but they're still fun to customise your game with.

Overall, cool game. Would recommend even for those not familiar with video games, as the whole game can be controlled with only the mouse.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis review goes to a game named „100% Orange Juice“ from the household of Fruitbat Factory in central japan. Where you must walk in circles to get points to win, fighting mobs by throwing a dice at them, stealing and gifting cards to your opponents and mentally challenge your friends until they say that this game is too complex for their logic.

Graphics
First of you need a RTX 3000 series or lower to enjoy your view of these High end graphics this game provides you. The decision to choose an anime art style makes the game likeable, which help your eyes to calm down.

Gameplay
The gameplay is great and challenge every corner in your brain. You simply walk around circles on the map and collect either stars, you collect on yellow star places or wins over your hostile individuals by throwing dices at mobs, who are stationed on the map or on other friends, while they challenge you before you can even react to that, to level up your character.
The higher your level is, the more cards you can play which need an certain level and an endless amount of stars you collect on your journey in the match. I recommend you, that you survive at any time, because if you die you‘ll loose 50% of your stars and you don‘t want to pay for the insurance.

After every Match you‘re getting coins, which are needed to pay the items in the shop like new maps, card packs and more. And you get oranges and tickets which they are used to buy event costumes or collect a pet on one of the few pet catcher terminals in the arcade section in the shop.

Final Score
I hope you enjoyed reading this as much as I enjoyed writing it

After a few matches with my friends, i understand it‘s hidden purpose. The orange juices main goal there is to torture your mental stability or those of your friends, giving them social anxiety and turning your friendships into dust.
If you want to challenge your friends on a mental and pyschological level until you need a vaccuum cleaner for your friendship then your welcome to give it a try.
8/10 I recommend it. I like the game a lot.\t\t\t\t\t
