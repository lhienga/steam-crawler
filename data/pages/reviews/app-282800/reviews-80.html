\r\n\t\t\t\t\t100% Orange Juice is a digital multiplayer board game, as you can read in the description for the game. It is important to acknowledge that this is NOT a party game. This is NOT Mario Party. 100% Orange Juice uses a card system where you choose what cards to bring into the game, and then all the player's cards are all put into the "draw pool". There are unique characters with different stats, and each of them have their own "power card" that has an impactful effect on the game. The game operates practically entirely on dice rolls other than your ability to play a card before you move. This game is great fun, albeit you almost certainly want a couple of other people to play with.

10/10 would roll a 1 on revival 6 times in a row again.\t\t\t\t\t
