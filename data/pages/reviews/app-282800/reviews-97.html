From the studio that make most of shooter music from Japanese arcade, comes a fun board game (calling it Monopoly is wrong as the similiarity stops at “gathering resource to win”) where the goal is to either rack up resources (Stars) or beat up other character (bullying).
I can offer you 4 things from this game: 1) fun adventure. Literally walking around and traveling both in story and gameplay 2) single player with different set of CPU, practice to adjust your skills on a given target 3) quest mode, further enforcing the adventure with this board game 4) if you got group to play with, coordinating with people you know and feels like we are in same room
Besides the gameplay the story is funny with constant snark of the plot, music are light, fun and rotate between players depend on the characters. There are about 4 starter characters, 4 more to open for free and about 6 enemy characters by grinding. Other than that every other character require purchase. My friend recommend me to get “Game of the Year Every Year Edition BUNDLE” for the most value, especially if you havent buy the game.
