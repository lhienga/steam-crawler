\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan, this game is something else. Back in high school some of my friends really enjoyed weebshit to a concerning degree. While I tried to distance myself from the " uWu senpai I'm a catgirl with big titty witties " humor I still partook in watching anime (still do). This game, while it may seem very cringy on the surface... actually surprised me. I don't have any experience with the single player but the multiplayer for this game is insanely fun. There have been many times when I've had tears in my eyes laughing because me and my friends would make suboptimal plays just for the sake of screwing each other over.

The simple rules of this dice game make it super easy to learn, which definitely helps when me and my friends are throwing back a beer or twelve. On an aesthetic level, this game is exactly what you would expect. You get your simple drawings of anime girls & boys fighting chickens and sludge monsters. What you don't see on a surface level is the deck building and the potential of boning over everyone else on the field. It's a great time. With the amount of characters and cards in the shop you can find some absurd combos that are just broken. But again, if you're just playing this game as a way to pass by a Thursday night with your buddies, you'll have a good time with it. The games UI can be confusing and sitting through cutscenes when you open up the game are NEVER FUN but I think once you get into a game it's hard to have a bad time.

Had a blast breaking my friends' niko-niko-kneecaps as an anime girl
8/10\t\t\t\t\t
