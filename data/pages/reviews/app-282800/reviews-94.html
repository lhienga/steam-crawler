\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably one of the most replayable games I've come across. Got this from a friend's recommendation in 2017 or so, but just started getting back into it. It's great to see they still regularly add to this game with new events, cards, and playable characters. And I mainly just do Online because the community is fun and open. I've not even touched Single Player, but it looks like it's pretty thorough, so that might be interesting.

Basically, it's anime meets Mario Party meets what I'm told Hearthstone is like. So it has a hell of a lot going for it, and I'm glad the devs just keep working on it.

I'll say this - if these devs had the opportunity to bring a Monopoly game to PC, I'd have 100% faith that they would knock it out of the park.

...Sure the Monopoly games available on Steam are hot garbage, so the bar's pretty low, but you know what I'm getting at.\t\t\t\t\t
