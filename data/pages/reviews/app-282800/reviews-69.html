\r\n\t\t\t\t\t I BOUGHT THIS GAME AS A JOKE

Its the autumn sale, I saw 100% Orange Juice on sale. Honestly knowing nothing about it I thought it was a hentai game or something.

>See the 4 pack for like 6 bucks.
>Why not

Now its 1 am me and my roommates have been playing for almost 3 hours nonstop. This game is incredibly fun to play, which is weird; on paper this game-play isn't much. Roll dice, move your little anime dude and battle monsters. I was worried looking at it it card game elements aren't really my style on account of me being incredibly stupid but no, the card elements are straightforward easy to understand and pretty fun; at least to me because I like pretending I've setup the 'master deck' when in reality I still don't know what i'm doing.

100% Orange Juice is an amazing game that held the attention of 3 people who suffer from ADHD, as well as keeping them up for several hours. Love this game so effin' much and definitely suggest getting the 4 pack for all your friend-havers out there. Even if you don't have friends by the look of it there are tons of online lobbies to meet people in!

thanks for reading my ramble review, hope you enjoy this game as much as me and my roommates did\t\t\t\t\t
