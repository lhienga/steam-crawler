\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is actually much more fun than it might seem. The only bit boring part is to wait for the opponents to finish their turns. However if you're trying to do something else while waiting for your turn you find out that it came faster than you've been expecting it to.
The price is good with discounts and trophies are fun to collect.
There is also a great replay value in this game as it might not get old for quite a while to play with friends or even with the AI.
In the options there is a setting for the narrator's voice: you are free to choose from a really annoying anime brat to comment on certain moments to a mature Japanese woman with a soft touching voice. Voice actors have made a really nice job there.
The characters can be slightly customized as it usually is in J games (clothes, colors, etc.), but no extreme settings, which is good IMO.

Overall I was slightly hesitant to play this game at first and the navigation seemed a little confusing for the first 10 minutes or so, but it really quickly catches up and becomes semi-intuitive. In the end the game was fun and made me stay up and pull and allnighter... I really should have tried it out earlier on and not to wait for couple years after purchasing it.\t\t\t\t\t
