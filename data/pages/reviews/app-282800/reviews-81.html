\r\n\t\t\t\t\t100% Orange Juice more like 100% oRNGe juice

mario party but with cute chibi characters who beat each other up with dice

It's a fun but chaotic game, and one where I like to just turn off my brain and roll dice. It is very heavily RNG-based, so if you don't like games with a high amount of randomness, then this game may not be for you.

You don't really need any of the DLC to play the game, as the base game characters and cards are about the same strength or better than the DLC characters (most of the DLC characters are gimmick characters anyways), so no need to feel like you need to pay for the DLC.

I'm not really well-versed in the Orange_Juice universe and pretty much picked up this game because it had cute chibi characters, but after playing it for some time, it has definitely made me more interested in some of the other games produced by Orange_Juice.

Can definitely be a bit confusing as to what's going on with the board, especially if it's your first time playing, but reading the in-game guide definitely makes it a lot easier to understand all the chaos going on with the board.

All in all, 100% Orange Juice is a fun game that I like to play when I need something to pass the time.\t\t\t\t\t
