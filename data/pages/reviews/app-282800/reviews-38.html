\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game some might say is the anime Mario Party. In the beginning of the game you have one out of two objectives to do. You either defeat an enemy or you collect 10 stars. After doing one of these you must go to a home base to turn in your goal or your norma, You have 5 in total to do and they increase the numbers of stars or wins you must get in order to get all your normas completed. There are many character selections to choose from. Base game wise you have a few good choices. In my opinion the best characters base game are Suguri, Tomomo and Marc. Each character has a hyper which is an ability card that is unique to that character. Some of these hypers can only be used in battles, used as trap cards (put it on a space and hope someone lands on it) or you can use it before you roll the dice to move. These rules apply to the rest of the cards in this game. DO KEEP IN MIND that cards come at a cost. Whether it be having to have done a specific amount of normas and having to use stars to use the card. Some cards are free but most aren't. A big tip I have for new players is always evade when you are one health or if the enemy rolls a 1. Pay attention to your characters stats. If you have a +1 evade you will always evade 1 rolls unless your stats are debunked in a battle. Same goes with defense, if the enemy rolls a 7 and you have +1 defense you will have a good chance of defending IF you have enough health. Say I have 2 health and my enemy rolls a 7. If I have +1 defense and +2 evade I have two options. I can try taking zero damage and evade or I can try rolling a 5 or 6 to take one damage. Keep in mind if you evade and roll the same number as attack then you will lose all your health along with most of your stars. If you roll a 4 and get a 5 from your +1 you will still die and lose all your health along with most of your stars. If I had one health the only option would be to evade. Defending means always taking one damage at least. I know this explanation probably has you confused but once you try this game out you will get the hang of it. Thank you for reading my review, take care and have an amazing day ya'll!!!!\t\t\t\t\t
