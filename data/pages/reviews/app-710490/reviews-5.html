A pack so big it's Spooky

Spooky Pack is the first paid pack (not counting the construction kits) for Planet Coaster. Adding a whole lot of new scenery objects, special effects, animatronics, buildings, rides, avatar hats, and a new Spooky entertainer. This pack includes alot of creativity with it's items such as fire effects, actual fog, lightning, rain, clouds, moving books, ghouls, spiders,

Pros:
- Over 200 Scenery pieces (Many recolourable)
- Over 100 Building pieces (Many recolourable)
- Tons of new music tracks, sound ambiences, and sound effects
- 2 new blueprint rides
- 2 new avatar hats
- New Entertainer: King Ghoster

Cons:
- Considering almost all the objects deal with gothic, spooky, and halloween themes, it's maybe a pack that is best during the season of Autumn/Fall. (However alot of the new things can blend with other themes.)

My Rating: 10/10

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like this Spooky Pack for the most part. I am sure I'm not the only one aspring to make truly unique and very large parks. I wish with this update they would have setup better park management features and got rid of the spreadsheet style employee management side in favor of an easy to handle dashboard. I don't want to waste half my day (seriously 4 hours) going through and triple or quadruple clicking every single entry to promote up my employees. There should be a batch option to do this or an option to toggle that side off so your employees can stay at a certain training level always, or just not worry about it at all. I feel as though we are getting penalized for making large parks.

I know it takes time to load all the assets I put in my park, but what stinks and feels like REAL WORK that I do at my paying job is messing with the employee roaster. I have at least 100 shops and 50 janitors, 35 security people, plus 25 entertainers and 35 mechanics. I just end up quiting the game because I don't have patience to deal with employees constant need of attention. When I spend time perfecting a coaster, I end up quiting after that because usually my park starts sagging in ratings and my attendance drops by the thousands. I have over 125 coasters, rides and attractions, it has been great so far building such a piece, but again please work on the schematics of tihs.

I wanted to build Mystic Timbers from Kings Island (owned by Cedar Fair) but couldn't really make a good shed, until now. Wished they would have made a Zombie Princess to go with King Ghost. Also, I was thinking there would be a roller coaster added, but there isn't only the two track rides. I'm excited to build with them as it would be easy to create a simple path with the track provided. The added build items really add the realistic layer.\t\t\t\t\t
