\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCompletely missed opportunity. If this game was classic Rome Total War with 2020's graphics and some of the best, newest mechanics like multiplayer campaign (at least 3 player co-op Roman campaign was to be expected), then it would be perfect. They also failed in some obvious aspects like no unit variation (just faces and hairstyles, but everyone wears identical clothes and armor, something fixed in Medieval 2 in 2007), catastrophic mobile UI (original was far better, I mean, FAR better), no option to play as some of the most modded features like a united Rome campaign or historically accurate factions symbols and Greek Egypt, and other missed points.
Anyway, this is a remaster, and it is a good one despite the lost potential. It has better graphics, better AI behavior and diplomacy, bigger unit scale and overall better controls and pathfinding. It also gives the option to not apply the newest changes and feel like the original, which is a good point too. The game is still a 9/10 , far better than most newer Total WAr titles.
I hope they take note of the mistakes to remaster Medieval 2 is a better way.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game offers little beyond some improved controls, and graphics that are definitely higher in quality, but arguably, worse in style than the original designs. A personal opinion, but mine is that the improvements of this game, do not make it significantly better than the old one, in any way. The animations are same old, the new campaign UI doesn't feel to be any better than the old one, even with the improvements, this game feels less pleasant to play than Medieval 2, which i have been playing since 2007. While i loved the original Rome when i was but a child, this game did nothing to resurface that nostalgic feeling i had. It felt foreign and unpleasant as an experience, a missed opportunity, to do something great.

More than updated graphics, this game could really have used some updated mechanics and most of all, better animations. I don't know exactly what happened between Rome and Medieval 2, but these games just feel very different.

It feels that every remaster for the games that i have been waiting for, has been nothing but a dissapointment. At this point, i fear that a remaster to Medieval 2 would be given to same studio. If it's quality standard will be same as this, i don't want it.\t\t\t\t\t
