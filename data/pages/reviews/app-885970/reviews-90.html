\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Remastered"??? As someone who's owned the original Rome since it was new, and still have it in my library; I'm asking myself what has been remastered??? I expected to be blown away with 4k graphics and a whole new better UI, but I can't see an actual difference. I think the map view is a little higher resolution, but it's hard to say, and the battles look exactly the same. There have been a few minor tweaks to things, but overall, I feel it was a waste to get this edition already owing the original. At the very least, I was hoping for the "large battles" from newer titles, but you're still limited to 1 army while the computer is not.

I will say this is by far the best Total War game; Medieval 2 comes very close, but the original is still the best. If you don't already have the original Rome, then this is a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
