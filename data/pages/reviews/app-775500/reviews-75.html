\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an amazing game. I am so glad I bought it.
The art is great. It even has its own anime opening when you open the game and it's pretty rad,
The story kept me at the edge of my seat the entire time, and I've only played through one of the main characters' story.
The fact that there's two different viewpoints you can play from makes this game pretty unique, since it's not just a self-insert character but TWO main protagonists. Like, what could be better?
The story was a tad confusing at times though again I was still at the edge of my seat playing through it all.
The animations don't really show the emotions well but the voice acting does hit pretty dang hard so it makes up for it.

The gameplay was pretty epic. Most fun I've had in an rpg yet. God did it add to the story.
The game's not too hard and not too easy. It is challenging (especially if you're like me and running headfirst into fights and spammin buttons like its no tomorrow) but the challenge only added to my enjoyment.

I really do recommend this game. It's ridiculous how amazing it is.\t\t\t\t\t
