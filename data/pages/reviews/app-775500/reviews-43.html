Combat can be quite difficult, and even aggravating at times, until you really learn all your abilities and moves well. Overall, to me, the guys team has a more standard balanced team while the girls is more technical/tricky.

The story has some gaping plot holes, especially the end, but is interesting with good shocking twists. If you happened to watch the anime that gets about halfway through the game, but only from the guys view.

Best to pick up on sale, especially now that the discounts are usually 75%. If you are looking for action in a similar style, Code Vein is way better in my eyes though!\t\t\t\t\t
