\r\n\t\t\t\t\tScarlet Nexus is the most generic anime game I've ever played, to the point where it almost feels like a self-parody of the shounen battle anime genre (but isn't quite clever enough to actually be that) and instead is a super generic action RPG that utterly failed to grab me after half an hour.

The game focuses heavily on its plot, which is basically "You are a superpowered anime teenager who fights off brain-eating monsters from space", to the point where the first half hour of the game is almost pure cutscene. I turned my brain off for most of it because I simply did not care. The combat isn't really great either - you have a sword attack (which is 80% of combat) and a magic attack that takes roughly forever to go off and isn't that great. There's supposed to be some nonsense about chaining combos or something but in all honesty it didn't really seem to matter much.

There's better things to spend your money on, unless you really like listening to generic anime garbage.
