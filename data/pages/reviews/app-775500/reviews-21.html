\r\n\t\t\t\t\t【二编】
出个续作吧

特点

剧情文本量巨大且80%以上是用游戏内建模截图制作的纯对话视觉小说来体现的（好在重要剧情做成了cg动画）如果有足够的耐心认真阅读文本，观看每个人物的全部羁绊章节，会发现剧情质量还是非常可以的。特别是对于每个人物的成长与变化都有着很不错的刻画。

战斗上一开始会比较痛苦，不过越到后期越有感觉，羁绊处决特写也超帅~

关卡设计类似仁王的那种大地图选关，怪物的美术风格不错（有点蒸汽朋克的感觉？）。

流程推进上接近弹丸论破，每段主线之间会给你充足时间去刷羁绊好感度。

美术就不多说了万代的二次元懂得都懂。

音乐好！！！可惜steam卖的原声带是320k的，只能去mora那样的网站买高质量音源。

值得一提的是伙伴的礼物系统：你送给同伴的礼物是会在基地中有所体现的，人物也会根据收到的礼物有不同的互动，同时基地的装潢也会随之改变，可以说的上是非常用心的礼物系统了~ 玩健腹轮的花火太可爱辣

总结
整体看下来是部佳作，不过就是需要时间与耐心（笔者单女主线通关且看完全羁绊章节花了近40hrs，还省略了大部分刷礼物材料的时间），无奈这个体量很难迎合大部分玩家的游玩节奏......所以说这个游戏可能更适合那种看得下去大文本视觉小说的玩家吧。\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's really rare that a game gets a hold on me not with gameplay or story but its looks. I really enjoy this game's aestetics. Soundtrack is also one of the most varied collections of audio work I've seen, with it switching between classical, electronic, full on dubstep and rock/metal on a whim.

This is not to say that gameplay or story are bad, quite the opposite.

Gameplay is quite fast-paced, and becomes almost tactical by the end when you need to juggle and combine 8 different effects of your powers during combat (it's quite intuitive and fun, with game ramping up amount of things you have access to quite gradually). Gameplay also has minor differences between characters, even upgrade-wise, with Kasane having much better air play with double air-dash, while Yuito has a much better charge attack, for example.

Story - I have a minor issue with how it is parallel between protagonists up to a point, and after merging the parties everything from other campaign essentially gets spoiled to you. Yuito's story relies more on not knowing what's going on, but his story premise is weaker overall. Also I feel like his story does not set up the endgame as good as Kasane's. I would say that if you want your first runthrough to be the best it can be, pick Kasane (unless you don't like her as a character), but there is value in playing Yuito first (also he has better DLC episodes imho). Structure-wise story really reminds me of Persona games, with it having plot-heavy segments called Phases and spending time with your friend and side-questing in Standby's. I really enjoyed this structure, as it really balances story's more dark tone with moments where characters can be just simple humans with simple everyday activities.

All right, I kinda went all over the place, but in general I REALLY liked the game. Every aspect of it was enjoyable.
Arashi is best girl, Kagero is best boy.
Have fun playing!\t\t\t\t\t
