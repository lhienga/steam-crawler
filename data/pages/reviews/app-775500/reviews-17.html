\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWelp, I must to say Scarlet Nexus is pretty fun to play, yet there are some downsides, especially for the story. First of all, the story is pretty generic, an ordinary troupe you will find in an anime story, there is no actually interesting plot to hook you into play the game deeper.

Secondly, the character are nothing special, you can also easily found a generic anime troupe, such as childhood friends, older sister type, the cold one, the envious one, and so on. It practically can be said, you can't put much hope in the characterization.

Now let's get to the fun part. Scarlet Nexus offers a slight difference from the other action RPG where you could utilize the so called "psychokinesis" to move around the objects around you and literally throw it to your enemy, they also introduce "partner" where you can borrow their power in order to give you different strategies on defeating your enemies. The rest of the game play mechanic, is not that really new and you can actually found it in the other games.

Unless you are looking for the action on this game, I think you need to reconsider to spend your money for this game. Though, I might change my opinion throughout my time playing this game to the end.\t\t\t\t\t
