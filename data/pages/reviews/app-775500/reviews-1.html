\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMost fun I had in a while. This is the first game of this type that I've played, kind of like a visual novel style, but I gotta say I liked it way too much. It's a story driven game with lots of dialogue so unless you are intrested in the story and the characters you might not have as much fun but the story itself is very good.

Characters are also very good and likable. I like all their story and background, they are interesting, and their design are also catching. Overall visual style in this game is also one of my favorites. I especially love how the characters look in their brain drive.

That said gameplay itself is also super fun so no shortcomings there either. Game having 2 different routes depending on your character of choice is also nice. You can play the other route once you finish the game and play for that much longer. Routes are also fairly different, and the two characters' play style are also quite different and both of them are super fun to play with. You can pull of infinite midair combos with Kasane while Yuito has more of a grounded playstyle.

Above all bits and pieces of subte hints you catch on your second playthrough, realizing the secret meanings in dialogues between characters are astonishing. All in all I would say this was a very good experience. This is one of those games that you just kick back, relax and simply have fun enjoying the ride. One of my favorite games of all time for sure.\t\t\t\t\t
