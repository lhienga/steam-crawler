\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game needs a lot more work. But has the potential to be something quite great. As for now i'd say hold off for more work to be put in it. The road map looks to be a little disappointing in terms of game play elements and AI, i mean there isnt any AI in the game tbh.

Anyone found a need for the production of heroin? i mean whats the point? Cocaine really delivers enough money or too much money for you to be stuck struggling on how and where to place your cash (which is actually true to life) or even just spend the money like where do you spend it charity events that offer nothing more than to make the game easier than it already is? . Granted, i just played the sandbox, as that's how i like these games. Especially ones that have lack luster dialog.

As of right now the game is too easy and doesn't offer anything of a challenge. AI cartels don't build, they don't fight with each other. in fact you never have to deal with them once you learn the very basic dialog options for each character. It just becomes mundane having to every once in a while talk to each of them and click through the repetitive dialog that inst even voice acted for them to just leave you alone with their stupid most the time pointless attacks that done affect anything really. Okay cool this woman likes me now because i chose a couple of different flowers? Come on! Same with certain production chains. i need to order certain items from a depot and keep ordering them every couple of days.

The early game is fun. But just like a lot of these games they lack any sort of depth or challenge mid to late game. becomes repetitive and boring. You unlock more characters by making each character a 'Capo'. Like what? how is that fun, why would i need to go through that effort? especially when its easier to just cheese the game by creating a load file where you continuously load a save and recycle your lieutenants to 'Capo' to unlock that character.

My suggestion is instead of adding things to the game like items or characters. Use what you have and build off of it. I built my empire. Have all my lieutenants, can easily ♥♥♥♥ on all the cartels; but again, whats the point. Clearing the map. How is that fun. I can literally fill the land with cocaine farms but so long as i keep running charity events in a casino or cathedral the authorities don't care? what about all the countries these drugs are getting shipped to, they don't care?

The math (game play Mechanics) in this game are fun to learn. As soon as you learn them it becomes boring very fast and you realize you spent too much time learning something so elementary. The UI is weird as well. Some basic QOL processes are none existent even after their QOL improvement. Why are some pretty important menu's buried? Why don't they have a hot key. Even after a year of development or how ever long it makes no sense to me. Its almost as if the game hasn't been play tested for a long time. i cant be the only one annoyed by some aspects that seemingly wouldn't be hard to fix.

My suggestion is just allow modders to do their thing.

All in all i hope this game gets a lot more work done to it. i put hours into the game (past the refund that's for sure) already and that wouldn't happen if you weren't clutching at something special.

I'll change my negative review once the game starts to feel like it inst a mobile game with zero multiplayer.\t\t\t\t\t
