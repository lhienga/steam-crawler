It took me at least 10 hours to get a grasp on things and after that you could clearly see the possibilities for this game. Just go slow, adding 3 dopium farms might sound good but when you can't sell em or repackage em quick enough your full warehouses attract attention and the law will come pay you a visit.

You really gotta micro manage your warehouses and transport companies a bit but after that i was making money by the boat loads. (Small Tip: Struggling with the logistics ? Just make small virtual islands for yourself with one type of product chain and dont let them interact with another production 'island' unless you start to get your head around the way warehouses and transport companies works.)

One of the cooler things is that you can 'buy' the peoples respect with heavy bonuses by building churches and such. This game has so many cool and new ideas (not all perfect tbh) in my book that it certainly deserves a look when you love the early tropico or similar games.

I would like to mention that the soundtrack is a masterpiece , it perfectly fits with the game and has a very relaxing vibe to it.

Things i would like to see:
Fights just needs more to it then a simple numbers game.
Pay off cops, federales to come fight for me or send em after rival gangs.
Possibility for blockades so you can stop advancing police or rival gangs
No terror for legal goods
More things to do with your underlings: Train, fire them, have the possibility to don't die or be arrested, more options in general
Bridges or transport boats for logistics.
Laundering operations draw attention when the money is full (should be when its over full capacity)
The possibility to cancel a move or other order in transit.
When underlings are on transport and the site gets attacked they shouldnt die or get arrested as they aint there.
A possibility to upgrade all buildings from a type on the map or per province at once. (Later game this get tedious)
Why can i build only one building at a time. I' am a business man and time is money.
Possibility to 'starve' a rival gang (when taking away everything they own, the strenght should go down sporadicly).

There is only one thing i realy don't like and that is the fact that gangs get power when you take over a city. I got stronger and have boat loads of money, you guys stay the same and you get a big bump in power. I understand for the game why its in place but it doesnt make any sense.\t\t\t\t\t
