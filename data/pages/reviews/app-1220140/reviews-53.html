\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEarly game is fine. But it boils down to an extremely annoying micromanagement juggling.

The idea of automatic logistic is neat, but the AI is very stupid, so it's a serious pain in the ass. Most of your dirty money will accumulate in the resources, and you can't properly launder this automatically. The only way you can launder this automatically is by adding it to residence logistic which is then connected to laundry building like bank... but there's a major issue with this. The trucks will deliver the port haul to the residence which will cap it and increase your TERROR, which is dumb. There should be a way to deliver the dirty money from the port straight to the bank, it makes zero sense to need to visit residence as middleman. Worst part is you can deliver things from port to the bank, and this is the most efficient way to launder money from ports, and the only way to do this is to MICRO MANUALLY. Basically once you own 3+ cities and residences the micros get extremely annoying.

Game has potential but in the current state it is not a good one.\t\t\t\t\t

I hope this has the ability to keep us excited way into a play through, also how different is this going to be each time I play it, because that's going to matter A LOT with how much people like this.
