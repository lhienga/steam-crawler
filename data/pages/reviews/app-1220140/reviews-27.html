\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAug 3, 2022 EDIT: This game is getting way too many downvotes from clueless people. I see people complaining about certain things but they just simply don't understand how to play the game, so they think the game isn't working right. There are several QOL issues in the game, but many things aren't broken so much as unclear -- everything in easily manageable as long as you know what you are doing. There is negligible tedium.

This game has a number of very well-thought-out touches that impress me and catch me off-guard. For example, I found the terror system too forgiving. I wanted it to punish me more. Well, the sandbox mode lets you adjust all sorts of things like that. I set terror to 250% normal, and wow, it's pretty close to the experience I wanted!

Then I started feeling like it was too easy to smuggle things hidden in vegetables. After a few months of this in-game, I was so impressed to find that the authorities had started suspecting my vegetable shipments! Especially with 250% terror, this was strongly encouraging me to change up my operation and use something other than vegetables to hide things. Not only that, a few months later after I had pissed everyone off up to the CIA, they started outright confiscating any vegetable shipments I tried to send through a particular smuggling point. So cool! I was very happy to be placed under this kind of pressure.

So many little touches like this really make this satisfying to play.

***

The Story Mode is buggy, confusing, and terrible. You will be subjected to all manner of nonsensical twists - for example, you'll read a confusing story about your lieutenant shooting the mayor because he's aligned with the Guerrilla faction. It doesn't say which lieutenant it is. Then your leader executes the lieutenant. Once gameplay resumes, you are given the mission of executing ANY lieutenant. The reason? Because they're aligned with the Guerrillas. So you're given the story before the event happens, which is confusing. Then you're told to kill literally anyone, just because. And then whoever you kill, it's justified, because they were aligned with the Guerrillas. Huh?

Anyway, the Survival Mode is great. This game needs a strong UI and QOL pass on certain things, e.g. you should be able to set sell priorities at your smuggling points and you should be able to set the storage of your laundering sites below max if you don't want them hog all your liquidity (i.e. you need some funds outside of the laundering circuit to fund upkeep and construction).

Overall, a great game and deserving of a high rating. I trust they will polish in time, and I'd love a DLC or anything else.\t\t\t\t\t
