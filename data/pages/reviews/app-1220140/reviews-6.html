\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI made a negative review about the game before i finished the campaign mainly criticizing the transport routes being clunky. I wanted to finish some harder levels in the game and give it some more legitimate concerns, i can say with confidence that I still think the transport companies as well as the warehouses are very clunky when it comes to their inventories. After playing the game more though i realized that the police were much more of a pain, even at terror level 1 they send waves of cops to target and either seize or even entirely destroy important buildings. They also send any lieutenants to prison without bail for multiple years if they're caught smuggling from export hubs such as airfields and ports, which wouldn't be so bad if you could cancel their smuggling runs. This ended up killing about a quarter of my runs on harder difficulties cause the police would take an airfield and id have to decide between letting the police snowball or losing my best lieutenant. Fun mechanics but personally i wasn't enjoying the game anymore by the time i finished the campaign, the police combined with rival gang attacks in later levels just made everything tedious to the point of having to save scum your way through the game cause of bad RNG.\t\t\t\t\t
