\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey've got a really good balance with this game!

TL;DR: buy this game. It's brilliant...

I've played it since early release, but didn't partake of the betas. Some of the early releases were fairly typical of this genre of resource-management strategy games with some repetitive elements. However, this game provides what I think is one of the best balances I've seen in the various genres of resource-management/ empire building strategy games. It's well thought out and the multiple elements balance themselves well. It's actually pretty easy to pick up, probably will be a bit tricky initially for a new player (but there's good tutorial missions). I've not played survival mode, but sandbox seems like just the right level for someone like me who's looking to a game to chill out for a couple of hours after work.\t\t\t\t\t
