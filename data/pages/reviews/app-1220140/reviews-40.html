Overall, pretty good for EA. I'll take a break and come back in 3 months.

Things to improve:

- devs need to reduce amount of micro required. At a minimum, one should be able to queue multiple orders. Better would be an order to eg 'transport' within a selected area, so you dont have to worry about which buildings are being travelled to and watch every movement your soldiers are making
- Production efficiency at beginning of game, particularly warehouses, is way too low. This forces human intervention, which increases the amount of micro
- Need more challenge / variety once you hit mid game ... I've just accumulated millions and I can't foresee any real challenge ahead
- need less focus on money laundering. I understand why it's important but it feels like you end up spending about half your time on the issue
- some of the AI logic seems off ... eg trucks transport from farm 1 to warehouse, instead of taking it directly to the production facility next door.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally solid start. This Early Access is exactly how devs should be using early access. They've limited a bunch of available options - so for instance, there are buildings and factions and actions unavailable - so that they can focus the Early Access on balance, gameplay and interaction / information design. Very respectable approach. The devs are already making updates based on feedback from the community in those areas.

There are areas that need some work for sure but it seems that this is the focus right now - a constrained set of problems to fix. For instance, there is a bit of a learning curve around how to get a money making empire going that is missing some key information design to let players know where the problems are (mostly around transportation). Likewise, the Lieutenant's model for loyalty is an interesting mechanism but might be a bit heavy handed currently and needs some additional nuance. Finally, there isn't really any diplomacy or relationships between the rival gangs or other factions - I hope something like this is coming and it isn't just a paint the map-style game.

The art style is awesome, the soundtrack is phenomenal, this game is on the right track. Buy it now if it is discounted or put it on your wishlist. This is one that is doing it right!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good game! Enjoyed the story line and game play.

Possible improvements:
- Hotkey for moving characters. You tend to use this quite a bit; having hotkey reduces clicks.
- More cartel member slots or reduce the need for manning stations. 3 slots for manning docks, 2 for ports and if you have 5 cities, thats 10 right there. You dont end up using your members special perks such as cultivation speed improvement etc if you have to man each of the above points.
- Maybe have options like hire delivery trucks/ labor from city/village (which improves respect) as delivery trucks seem like a bottle neck even if you have 2-3 warehouses.
- Increase cost of freezing star, it becomes too easy mid game. You have constant cash flow, the only challenge is the police. But if you can pay them off for 25-50k while bringing in 500k+ per month, it becomes less challenging. Or add more deterrents such as crop failure etc to make it more dynamic.
-Lastly more avenues to spend money, as mentioned above after mid game it becomes too easy to leave money on auto pilot.

Looking forward for the rest of the story :)
