You soon realize the biggest limiting factor is your ability to launder money rather than produce it.
It only takes a few buildings for anything other than the first drug and you end up using only a tiny fraction of the resources on the map, before you are pulling in too much dirty money. So the economy, logistics and expansion side is actually kind of limited.

I expected combat to play a bigger role but other than mission requirements and a few key buildings to get things rolling its actually best avoided. There doesn't seem to be any way to hurt or take on the other gangs unless you already have more overall power, at which point its much faster and easier to just use diplomacy.

Still I think overall its a game worth playing.\t\t\t\t\t
