\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very broken game with great potential.

Spent about 6 hours on the game and found the game non-intuitive and the tutorial system is useless. Player tutorials are much better.

You will spend a lot of time learning which keys (alt vs ctrl vs shift + letter/number/F-key) do in order to get anything done. None of which is intuitive. This results in an unnecessarily high learning curve. Developers desperately need to hire a Human Factors Engineer.

Part of the problem may be focus on the part of the Developers. Players can use xBox/PS5 type controller (no alt/ctrl/shift/f-keys). This game may have been built for game controllers, which I don't have. PC players may have been an afterthought.

There are minor bugs and some of keys simply don't work when they should. This feels like a good beta.

For those who don't mind many hours learning the system, you will be rewarded. There is gold there. You just have to dig harder than you should have to.

Recommendation:
Keyboard/Mouse: Wait until they improve the user interface.
Game Controller: undetermined (it may be deserving of the 91% approval rating).\t\t\t\t\t
