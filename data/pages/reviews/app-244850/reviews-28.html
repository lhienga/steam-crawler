\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played this game for over 3000 hours in 5 years. I have every DLC (aka we need money here are some cool block skins for 5 bucks, not really complaining I get it, SE 2 just better blow my mind). Do I like this game? Yes, and no... For the entirety of owning this game I have had a love hate relationship with it. The base game for what it is, is amazing, I personally have not found a better sandbox creative building physics game. If you like to build and problem solve it will engage you for hours however I have found that the base game lacks the engaging features it needs to keep it interesting. There are hardly any enemies and most of the time you must seek them out. The only real reason to go to space is for uranium, the planets all have the same ores to offer. There are space stations that offer random tasks to do like bringing them resources or escorting a transport etc. Other than that there's no real sense of adventure or danger. Thankfully there is a vast community of people much smarter than myself that create mods to make the game more enjoyable, although using too many or the wrong ones will significantly hinder your FPS. All things considered though, if you like space exploration and building I would highly recommend this game.

If Keen reads this:
Your game has kept me entertained for hours, but as you read above it needs some work, and seeing comments on your updates I'm not the only one who thinks this. I stay up to date of on everything I can about SE 2, water is a great step it the right direction and the physics look like a lot of fun. I don;t know your plans but I would recommend a more engaging survival mode with more to do, fight and find. Also If it were a toggle or just part of the game I think atmospheric drag for planes and helicopters would be a hit and add a new challenge to flying in the new game.
Thank you for everything you do and keep up the good work!\t\t\t\t\t
