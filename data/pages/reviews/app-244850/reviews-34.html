\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm surprised how bad this game really is, despite many good things it has.

The performance is very good. The explosions in space are cool and cinematic. Ship weapons and other stuff are dynamic, and leave other games in the genre (looking at you, Empyrion), far behind. There are factions that feel alive. I've been actively hounded by ships and it feels like in a middle of a war. Some cool vistas, graphics are nice, and ability to just land on planets is amazing.

But that alone doesn't make a good or an enjoyable game. The most fun I had was watching other factions lose ships in hilarious ways. Mining? Wasn't able to do any mining. All ships I flew were immediately attacked and shot down by a faction that apparently wasn't yet even hostile. You will get attacked, but you will not avoid getting attacked, as you see now map, no overall layout of anything. The only thing you see is space and some tagged points. That's it. Scanning for enemies? Mapping territory? I don''t even know how many planets are there in this solar system. Does the game has a galaxy? Many planets? How can I know?

How can you not implement any map or layout is beyond me. Which areas to avoid? I don't know. Where are planets? I have no idea. If my friend with whom I played didn't tell me about factions, I would have zero knowledge about the world and its layout. Everything seemed to happen randomly. A ship appears and shoots things around me while the tutorial bot tells me that I'm hurt for the 100th time. You either treat the player like a hardcore veteran or like a newborn baby, not simultaneously. If I didn't get resources from wrecks and ships from my friend, buying for space credits, I would have exactly zero progress.

And then you have the most horrible UI I have ever seen, which was designed this way only to sell you overpriced and greedy DLCs. Yes, the only reason I am being shown 300+ blocks to pick from when moving things into my toolbar is to promote the DLC blocks which you cannot have until you buy.

How can you sell so many DLCs with singular reskinned blocks for such an unpolished mess that lacks the most basic functionalities is astounding.\t\t\t\t\t
