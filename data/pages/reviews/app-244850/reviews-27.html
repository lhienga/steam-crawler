\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell well wellllll...
How would I begin to describe my experience?
I'll start with a "Well well wellllll..." followed by :

Decent creative threshold. Has plenty of physics related content, oddities and block manipulation.
Space travel? Oh my goodness. It took days on Xbox for me to not have anxiety and panic attacks floating in space. Hahaha, so sad.

I do have some issues I would like to bring forward, followed by some interesting ideas for new ingame content.

ISSUES :

Server lag : The monotonous building of large structures and massive ships is great except when players PC's have to communicate with servers, the information isn't always saved and bits and pieces of built structures vanish into thin air as if not built. Generally an issue when there is packet loss between player and server... You need to find a way to balance the communication... maybe decrease packet size to just binary information, orrr.... have players builds store data into packets that get sent to the server every 30 seconds to 20 minutes depending on server host options/choices.

Thrust Power : It is hard to determine exact thrust needed for leaving planetary surfaces to orbit and space. Each player should have a Calculator that is built in game that allows them to input block count, power source and weight and have the calculator display exactly which thrusters are needed for each type of use. It would be really cool to add a feature that allows players to have access to Engineering tables. Tables that allow defining timing, autonomy of thrusters and guns, shields and refills from a single spot (the way its currently displayed is just too much in such a small space).

Wolves : Great to add an NPC that is "natural" to earth like Planet, but they are really over powered. When you start earthside and you land near wolves, you have 0 hope of survival. the couple clips of ammo and pistol will kill 1 maybe 2 of the 5 wolves that attack you. An endless cycle of needless death.

Cave systems : They dont exist, which is strange because theyre more common in reality than the ores that exist... Kind of a bummer really.

Exploration : There is little to none. I mean that literally. The surfaces of all planets don't have anything worth exploring. There is no ancient buildings, crypts, crumbling civilizations... notta.

Escape Pods : For some strange reason they don't exist, even though in real space travel in the future escape pods will be a crucial element to survival and is on every ship in space.

Gravitational Fields and Stationary Objects : All planets and most objects with mass in space have a field of gravity. If you go to space, asteroids aren't stationary, not in the least. All things with mass attract smaller objects with smaller masses. I don't know the science behind it but its true.

Turning Asteroids into Moveable crafts : Yes this is an odd thing to point out... one that won't make much sense until you finish reading this. If an object in space is given thrust, even the tiniest amount, the object can be moved. I should be able to encase an asteroid 50% with blocks and throw thrusters onto it and move the asteroid where I want it.

Gear : For all the things that can be built, why can I not make my own gear? All the required things are already available, except for dyes...

Food : The good ole sustenance angle. YOU CAN NOT SURVIVE ANYTHING WITHOUT WATER AND FOOD.... a seriously crucial element left out of the game.

NPC's : The worst of the worst. They are over powered right away when they should be matching the players threat level and power.... They shouldnt have drone ships if I dont have one... Makes the game a little unbalanced.


Now for Ideas :

Ideas

Food
Crafting station that allows new suits to be built
Hydro dependencies for sustenance
Fire - for damage and for lighting sources and cooking
Floods
Extreme Weather Patterns or Extreme Storms
Alien life - come on you don't seriously believe humans are the only intelligence?
Warp Drives - DO NOT EXIST AND SHOULD NOT BE IN THIS GAME
Animals - not a lot just a few to bring more color, life and depth to survival




I love this game, I just hate to see so much potential go to waste\t\t\t\t\t
