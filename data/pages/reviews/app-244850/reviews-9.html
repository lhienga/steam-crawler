\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis Game is fantastic for building Ships, Stations, and Automation for materials. While the Physics engine does have a couple bugs ( Klang is always watching and waiting... inside joke if you haven't played yet) its nothing that holds the game back and can even be harnessed if you have the creativity and know how.

The expense of Space does a great job of feeling Large without feeling empty. there is always another Asteroid, a rival factions ship or one of the many planets in the distance.

The building is great, in that while its most common to Build Ships and stations, you can do so much more. walking machines and crazy contraptions. Hinges, Pistons, Rotors and Conveyors, along with many different shaped blocks give a lot of freedom to explore whatever crazy design you can think of, and some you didn't.

The game is 10+ years old now, but it still holds up well. i'm very excited to see whats next from the Developers.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t
