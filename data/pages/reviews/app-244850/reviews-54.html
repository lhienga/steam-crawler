\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll preface my review to say, I have not completed any Scenarios in the game nor played on any public servers. I've played on dedicated servers with friends & single player, with easily 90% of my time played being single player.
The game has a high learning curve and because of this, takes a lot of time to get comfortable (i.e. 100+ hours due to large variety of blocks and build potential). The time investment to learn is why most my friends quit playing. I found the built-in tutorials rather useless and went to YouTube to resolve all the issues I've encountered along with teaching myself how to use blocks to automate remedial tasks, big thank you to creator Splitsie for the tutorials & how-to videos.
Overall, your experience with the game will vary greatly depending on what effort you put into it. Don't be afraid of adding mods to the game, they easily make the experience significantly better. Quality-of-life mods are a great place to start. I added more NPC encounters later on and got properly messed up by MES. Glad to say I've since returned the favor.\t\t\t\t\t
