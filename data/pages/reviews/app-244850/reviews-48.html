\r\n\t\t\t\t\t[TL;DR SE is a fun but empty sandbox game with microtransactions and DLCs.]

The game can be fun and immersive this is why it gets a "YES" recommendation.

BUT

It is also very inaccessible, you can and should play the tutorials and you can learn some of the basic stuff but the tutorial maps are also old and do not show you how to do other newer things.

Space Engineers lacks any kind of fauna or flora in planets, you have trees and endless terrain, planets are as
barren as the moon so unless you subscribe to the idea that "any game is more fun with friends" you'll be pretty much roaming empty spaces with a few robotic hostiles from time to time and nothing else.

Finally DLCs, Space Engineers has a lot of them and i'm not going to buy any of them, because buying them only encourage the devs into releasing more for the sake of "supporting the game" and cosmetics hardly justify any sort of money. Devs and the community will argue they are just cosmetics, that you also get vanilla ugly equivalents in the game every update but this does not dissuade the in-game menu from constantly pushing them to the front, because you know, having permanently grayed out paid items in your crafting menus is always fun.

SE is a fun game, but it is also surprisingly bare taking into account the time it's been around and the amount of microtransactions that supposedly fund its development. It has modding support but at that point it's the community developing the game.\t\t\t\t\t
