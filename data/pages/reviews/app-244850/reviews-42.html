\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith almost a year in this game, it safe to say I love it. Building is awesome, learning how to add more detail into things, and building things I love. Combat is unique to SE, and the block destruction system is just unparalleled. The variety of mods is insane! Being able to get mods to make building easier and faster or to change how core systems work, the modding community makes this game truly special.

Yes there are bugs, more than a few, but those honestly make the game *more* fun. It introduces an aspect of randomness that makes every adventure exciting. Multiplayer is fantastic. Building with friends, blowing things up, crashing space ships. The variety of things to do makes multiplayer one of the greatest things about this game. Its better with friends.

While I know they are somewhat controversial, after spending so long playing this game I am more than willing to pay out $5 or so a year to support a game I love so dearly. And while true that DLC blocks introduce no new functionalities, they do add cosmetic options that make building all that much better.

Buy this game. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
