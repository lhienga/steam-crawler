TL;DR:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

A Stellar Engineering Playground



Detailed Review:

1. Narrative and Visuals (5/5):
Space Engineers scratches a unique itch, standing out as the best 'space survival sandbox' available. Its design, especially for those with an eye for design and programming sense, is unmatched. A solid 10/10 recommendation.

2. Gameplay and Unique Features (5/5):
While entry may have a high bar, Space Engineers excels in gameplay, offering an unparalleled 'space survival sandbox.' The in-game tutorials are subpar, but after the initial learning curve, the game becomes a clockwork of creativity.

3. Optimization Woes (5/5):
Despite occasional oddities and friction issues, the physics engine works well, allowing for the creation of interesting structures. However, dedicated servers on personal machines may impact performance, leading to lag and physics interaction problems.

4. Soundtrack and Audio Design (5/5):
Space Engineers captivates players with its challenging yet enjoyable gameplay, focusing on building and overcoming unique challenges. The game's physics create a puzzle-like experience that evolves based on the player, the planet, and their friends.

5. Bugs and Technical Issues (5/5):
While some aspects may be broken, like the physics engine under certain conditions, the overall enjoyment of building and solving puzzles overshadows these issues. The game's survival mode may seem like a missed opportunity, but the joy lies in overcoming challenges.

6. Replay Value and DLCs (5/5):
Space Engineers offers an exceptional replay value, akin to Minecraft but set in space with the ability to build ships, space stations, and bases. The game's broken aspects don't diminish the overall enjoyment, especially for those who love building, solving puzzles, and automation on a large scale.

7. Community and Modding (5/5):
The game thrives in a multiplayer environment, encouraging collaborative building and making accomplishments more satisfying when shared with friends. Space Engineers' openness to modding enhances the overall experience.

8. Value for Money (5/5):
Considering the depth of content, cooperative multiplayer potential, and the satisfaction of building and exploration, Space Engineers offers excellent value for money. The game's continuous development and maintenance contribute to its lasting appeal.

If you just want to enjoy the experience pick up the base game, but if you feel like supporting the devs pick all the DLCs as they are only decorative blocks and they do not add anything new to the game mechanics.

Personal Thoughts:

Space Engineers resonates as an engaging space sandbox, providing endless possibilities for creativity and exploration. Despite its flaws, the attachment to self-built creations and the game's open-ended building make it a captivating experience. The lack of a conventional story might not suit everyone, but for those who relish the joy of designing and experimenting, Space Engineers is a stellar choice. Whether you're a meticulous designer or simply enjoy building and tinkering, this game offers a rich and rewarding gameplay experience.\t\t\t\t\t
