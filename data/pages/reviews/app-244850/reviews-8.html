I don't mean in terms of physics or scientific accuracy because it has a lot of problems in those departments, but in terms of the experience of being an engineer, this game gets it right. You have to think about every stage of the process, from how you're going to manufacture your vehicles, how they perform, how you maintain them, and how to do quality control if you produce more than one example of your creation.

The lack of realism in terms of science and physics is not necessarily a bad thing, either, since it lets you focus more on the big picture than details like whether your craft is aerodynamic, or whether the thrusters are balanced (though there are mods for all of these things if you want!)

I love this game.\t\t\t\t\t
