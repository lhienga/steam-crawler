There are already high-quality reviews that do a great job of discussing the solid reasons of why you should not get Space Engineers, so I'm not making one myself, but making sure my input counts. Simple as that.

I know your looking for a a idea of my opinion, as well as the state of the game. Please see the reviews of, Snow, and TJ_Taz. They might not be the best in detail, but certainly are *Hopefully* the least in potential provoking reviews that still do a good explanation.
Here's some conveniently placed links to mentioned reviews:
Snow: https://steamcommunity.com/id/kibbewater/recommended/244850/
TJ_Taz : https://steamcommunity.com/id/TJ_Taz/recommended/244850/

I'd also like to add to some reviews just a little bit:
Snow: Individually any one DLC might not be too much, but if you elect not to buy them for *insert your own reason here* - having discovered some of the more darker secrets of Keen, in my case - you miss out big time. Though if you do own the game, by my experience, eventually your hand will be forced due to the heavy limitations of zero DLC bought. (Mods will mitigate what I describe to a extent, I mean, just look at the "hours at review time")
Oh and one other thing, Keen's whole argument of "This content is not pay to win" is, for select packs, true, but with others.... (I'll let you investigate this on your own)
TJ_Taz: KSP is referring to Kerbal Space Program, specifically the first one and not the second.
