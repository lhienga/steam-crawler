\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCould have been a real gem and obviously it is for many players, but I personally can not recommend it. The lack of a real in-game tutorial for such an immensely complex game, which in many aspects is also anything but intuitive, is a no-go for me. I refuse to watch hours of youtube tutorial videos, just to do what I've seen there.
Moreover there are some veeery short story-based scenarios, which are ok at best, but other than that there is no story, no greater goal and nothing really to strive for...at least I didn't find anything. Yes, you can strive to automate more or reduce the grind needed to mine or to build a spaceship to go to space but firstly, you will not be able to do that without first having to see how another guy does it on youtube and then more or less just copying him and secondly even if you reach your goal...what was it for? You're now mining faster or you're in space. Congrats! What to do there? What's the big overall goal? I guess for many people it is just to build around and spend thousands of hours learning to figure out the mechanics and how to reduce the grind.
Thanks, but I'll stay with games that are fun to play, have an impactful story and playfully make you familiar with the mechanics instead of telling you to go watch other people do stuff so you can copy them.
KSP or Subnautica did a far better job at making their games accessible and fun.\t\t\t\t\t
