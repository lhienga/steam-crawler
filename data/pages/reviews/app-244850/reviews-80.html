\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot worth your time... I always wanted to try this game out, as I heard good things about it. However, 10 years of development, and this is still pretty much someone's highschool prototype project, and not a viable game that anybody can enjoy. Of course, for a very few with whom this "game" clicks, they will enjoy it. However, the survival mechanics are frustrating, the progression is shallow. And after 40 hours of play, the only satisfaction I get out of this game is when I reload the save file to undo whatever happened after something horrible has gone with game physics.

Overall, this game is perfect for individuals who like to play in "creative" mode and then smash things together while watch them go boom. For others who are looking for a "game", there is none here...
