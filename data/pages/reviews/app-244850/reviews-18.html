\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI truly think this is one of the best space builders around. As far as functional block builders goes (ie Minecraft, Feed The Beast, etc), this game is truly a marvel at what you can build and the physics required to make your creation functional. I am a huge fan of voxel mining, ore processing and refining, and then building from parts. SE doesn't disappoint. There is a great workshop community that supplements some shortcomings or adds additional needs to the game. Multiplayer is awesome on a hosted server if you want to set some automation to mine/process/build and walk away and come back later.

My only pet peave, and this really is stupid on my part, is that while there are several space suits/helmets/gloves from which to choose, you cannot take the suit off in an oxygen environment. I think that would make it more realistic but I digress.

TLDR; Anyhow, I highly recommend this game along with grabbing some of the more popular community items from the workshop.\t\t\t\t\t

Unfortunately I just don't feel like I can recommend this game, I've forever struggled with the building mechanics, unable to make a ship with a decent balance of thrust, armour, carrying capacity and fuel (and style). Updates are very infrequent and 70% of the fancy blocks added are DLC, the most notable update recently was the weapons one, it added much more customisation of turrets and weaponry but did it have any use in non-pvp gameplay? Pretty much... NO! NPC ships are pathetic, the strongest ships are equipped with a handful of machine gun turrets and a rocket launcher, this used to make for a pretty fair fight but with the new weapons you can just add an autocannon to your construction ship and snipe from a safe distance, the enemy doesn't stand a chance.

The physics of the game are pretty poor and incredibly laggy as well as unoptimised, a player on a server flying their medium sized ship into render distance of your character can briefly freeze the game. I've seen games such as From the Depths pull off pretty amazing things with its physics and yet somehow keep FPS around 40.
Mods barely patch up the emptiness, even if they do, poor compatibility leads to your game crashing followed by you needing to wait about 8 minutes in total for all the loading.

Would have liked to seen this game improve and increase its optimisation but now I'm certain that we won't see much more new and USEFUL content.\t\t\t\t\t
