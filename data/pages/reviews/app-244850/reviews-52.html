\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI originally reviewed this game quite negatively, and I stand by several parts of that negative review.

But my friend urged me to continue playing and I have sense changed my mind on if the game is worth buying.

The physics engine is quite odd in many places, downright bad in some, but for the most part it does what you need it to do, and more, very well when it works. You can build a lot of interesting and fun things with the engine, but it prefers space and has some problems with friction it seems like.

Your game with run worse on a dedicated server on your own machine, im not sure how, but it will. And it is a LOT worse, not enough to chug, but enough to feel the lag and the physics interactions seem to suffer for it being on a server vs pure client.

The most enjoyable part of the game is building things and overcoming the unique challenges your builds create, its like a puzzle that can't end, and it gets harder or easier depending on you, the planet, and your friends.

If you're into building things, solving puzzles, automation all on a large scale, this game is probably for you.\t\t\t\t\t
