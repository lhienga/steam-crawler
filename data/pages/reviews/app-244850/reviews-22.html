\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game for anyone who like creativity. You mine ores, and use it to build ship block by block.

I find it enjoyable to design ship with different sizes, stack it with weapons and devices, then send it to battle.

There's a bit short coming when the game doesn't have a clear objective. Once you get more ore than your need, build the biggest ship that you wish, there no campaign or end game content aside from doing some contracts given by base station around the system. That unless you play with scenarios, mods, or someone organized servers. That's fine since this game is more of a sandbox than an open world RPG.

In term of mod, there's plenty in workshop for you to try. You can download new blocks, more game contents or other player's ship/station blueprints for your use. Although, the game kind of heavy in term of ram should you install too many mods or GPU should you have too many objects in one place.

Overall a nice space sim sandbox to play. Perhaps more fun if you have friend or a nice server.\t\t\t\t\t
