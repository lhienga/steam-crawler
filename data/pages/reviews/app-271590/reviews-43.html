\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInitial 5-6 hours I thought this game was damn fun and addicting!!
Then after few more hours the game felt dumb. And boring.

** I just realized I only played the PC hard drive installed version and not the Online version. I have no idea how far the game has evolved since GTA V was released years ago. So my review below is for the classic GTA V on the PC and nothing to do with GTA V Online **

First of all, I can't catch that damn biker dude to repossess the bike. That's the mission I cannot complete. Tried over dozen times. Every time I get within few feet, game AI throws obstacle (usually vehicles) to make me crash or slow me down (or both). Is that part of the fun -- the challenge? Yea of course! But over a dozen times, it's more like P.I.T.A.!!

Anywho, anyone who has played this game knows this game is opposite of PC (politically correct).
That's fine by me! I was expecting some serious & creative roughness. But in actuality I am disappointed!!
- Not enough variety of assault methods against the public on the streets. I've seen only punching & kicking option aside from guns, bullets and grenades (if I haven't already run out of ammo).
- What about bad mouthing a**holes to pick a fight?
- What about snatching cell phones. food items, weed/cigarettes or purses??
- What about bad-mouthing or slapping women instead of just punching & kicking??
- Or surprise-touching their private parts?

Let's be honest, If physical assault & murder are a big feature of this game, why not include a few more creative assault methods to piss people off?? Why limit to only punching and kicking, or murder??
BIG DISAPPOINTMENT!

Like I said I haven't been able to catch the elusive motorbike offender to repossess his bike. So I don't know what more is offered in the game. Sure I can watch YouTube videos of other people playing GTA V and accomplishing all sorts of missions all day long...but what joy would that be for me? That would not be my direct personal experience.

Finally, along with the game purchase, I paid for the Whale Shark Card Pack (part of the $22 purchase).
But I don't see the benefit of that game cash anywhere in my game account. So my game character has had to resort to committing petty crimes (not my preference, but have to since I can't get past one of the missons to get paid better $$$).

Some of the things I actually enjoyed and kudos to the development team:
1. Nearly any bike, car, SUV, van, pickup, truck, big rig or bus in player's view is available to jack and cruise around town for a joy ride or get chased by the Po-Pos throughout the city.
2. It's super great fun to tear up a shiny new BMW or Bugatti on the Los Santos streets!
3. That awesome air ride over the entire city on the cool-looking blimp! Especially at night with the city lights - what a damn awesome sight!! Now THAT is creative and cool !!
4. City layout, details and the whole visual dimension are great eye candy!

Maybe if I can get past few more missions and see what else develops, I may change / update this review.

But for now, those few good things mentioned above are not worth $15. My recommendation is if you're on the fence, buy it when it gets to less than $10.
