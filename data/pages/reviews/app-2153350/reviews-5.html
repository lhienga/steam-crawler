If you have played this game before in its original state, I would not recommend buying this upgraded version. If you are brand new to this game, please buy this version.

I played the original back when it came out and this remake still holds up the magic from the first. The game is beautiful, as well as the story. This game is still just as short as the original, not really adding much of anything "new" to the gameplay and story itself. Although the story is beautiful and moving, it didn't create as much emotional turmoil for my (now third) playthrough of the game. I purchased it for the nostalgia and beautiful graphics. Like my title suggests, if you are like me, save your money. If you are new to the game, please buy it as it will be worth your time.

While this game is definitely playable by yourself, it does not come without its frustrations. The gameplay is fairly unique in that you are controlling each brother with a different joystick and trigger buttons for their actions. Some of the solutions aren't outright apparent, but for me personally it didn't take too long to figure out the puzzle. The game will definitely be easier when played with a friend, which I would recommend if you are not confident in your skills with difficult puzzle-like gameplay. As stated in some other reviews, if you are not adept at figuring out those puzzles it will become frustrating for you very quickly.

The gameplay is very short. While Steam says I played for 4 hours, about 20-30 minutes of that was spent on a combination of chore break or snack break or phone call. I purchased this game on release day with it being on sale for $16; it has a regular price tag of $20. With the original version of the game being $20 as well, I think it is a fair price tag for the graphics upgrade it received. I also only managed to complete 7/14 of the achievements available on a "blind" playthrough. The rest of the missed achievements seem easy enough to go back and accomplish on another playthrough.\t\t\t\t\t

Have you ever played a game that brought you to tears?
I mean really to tears?
I remember playing the Original in 2015 and it was an emotional Rollercoaster.
After completing it ... i was still, i was ... thinking about what i played.
Thinking about the whole Story and of course the Ending.
As i said before: An emotional Rollercoaster.
And ... here we go again.

Welcome to BROTHERS - A TALE OF TWO SONS REMAKE!

This is the story of two Brothers on a journey to help their father.
At the beginning of the story the father got ill and his sons bring him to a healer.
The problem is, he know a way to heal the father but it will take a very long journey.
The sons already lost their mother and don`t wan`t to loose their father, too, so they started the Journey.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3170023638

The Gameplay is the same as in the Original and that is totally okay. You can play with Keyboard, but i highly recommended a Joypad.
With it you controll the 2 Brothers at the same time. One with the right half and the other with the left half of the Controller. The same time! Most of the parts of this game you must do Teamwork with the Brothers to get through the it.
That sounds a little bit complicated, but it is very easy to control.
At the beginning it may take a little Time, but believe me, you are shortly into the Controls and then you can enjoy the game and it´s story.
And this Time with a Friend if you wish. Local Coop or Remote Play. Your Choice.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3170023465

The Graphics are really wonderfull overworked. At the beginning it is nothing special. You are in a small village, with some people, normal village life. But the more you play, the Scenerys are getting better and better and you can see how much work it is in this game. I don´t wan`t to spoil, that is something you must see by yourself. The World of BROTHERS got many nice details and all the characters are very well animated. AAs i said before, really wonderfull.
And that goes also for the amazing Soundtrack. Really wonderfull Music to totally dive into the World of the BROTHERS.
I highly recommended buying it, too. 25 tracks for just 4,99.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3170023775

Brothers also have his Achievements, 2 more then in the Original, but these are special ones. Each one stands for a little situation you must find and master. For example, you have to make a special person dancing, or helping a Turtlemom to get her Babys back. Very nice done.

So what can i say.
This is a wonderfull Remake. Approved Graphics, the amazing Soundtracks and this damn, damn, damn emotional Rollercoaster Feeling.
Yes, this game did it again. I have had some tears in my eyes, again, and i´m not ashamed of it.This is more than a game. It´s like a movie or like a book. You follow the story and you laugh and cry with them. Each chapter you wan`t to know how it is going on. You can`t stop. And at the End of the Game you sit there, thinking about what you have experienced.

A very big Thanks to the Developer and publisher for this "upgraded" experience.
It was really, really awesome.
Again.

So dear Reader, if you like a great story with wonderfull graphics and amazing music, this is for you.
If you are really emotional, don´t forget the tissue. I promise you ... You will need it.
