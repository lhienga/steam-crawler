One button to get into/activate all interactables and another button for exiting/disengaging for controllers, quick and easy. Keyboard users have one button get in/activate, like controllers, but 1 of 3 buttons to back out depending on what you are interacting with. This is more than counter intuitive and eats up time having to set to close out menu's or back off of interactables and can easily leave you scrambling to get on the right button in the midst of real time combat.

So, if you are a controller user go for it, if not be prepared to be frustrated as you find yourself locked into a barrel trying to pull cannonballs only to mash the wrong exit button as you take cannon fire to the face that you should have been able to dodge. Edit for clarity: You use F to interact with all as I stated, to back out of non-container items like cannon, sails, wheel, ect. you have to hit F again. If in a barrel or chest it's F to get in and X to back out. If you are in your ships armory it worst, you hit F to interact then Esc to back out, moving your hand completely away from your WASD movement keys. Controllers are A to interact, and X to back out, of everything. So GL if you want to use keyboard here as you also can't remap in any form that will improve this without macro's (I've not even gone there as I'm betting thats against TOS).\t\t\t\t\t
