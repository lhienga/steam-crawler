\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to start by saying I don't really hate the game, I just don't see a reason to play it. This game can be absolutely hilarious, the interactions and community can be great. But, that's it. The pvp is alright, I've seen worse, but it's nothing to write home about. I haven't played the game long at all, but already it feels incredibly repetitive. The only progression is for cosmetics, and it ends up feeling like you're doing everything for no reason. The game has its moments, and while those moments are amazing, they just don't happen often enough that the (imo) boring gameplay loop is worth it. I've played very grindy games before (I have 180 hours in Destiny 2,) but in SOT all of the grind is for nothing. Everything is built well aside from the core gameplay loop, but even something like defeating another crew feels very unrewarding. You get more money so that you can buy cosmetics, and if you don't care about cosmetics then your only progression is buying stuff that will make you make money quicker.

The actual ship control, combat, and overall art style and vibe of the game is great. I love playing various songs on the instruments in game. This game shines when you're messing around with friends and just having a good time. But if you don't have friends to play with, I assume the game would be much less fun.

In short, this game is just not for me. I want to progress in games, and SOT lacks that progression.\t\t\t\t\t
