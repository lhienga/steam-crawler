\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot good for busy gamers; requires a massive time commitment for any session that keeps me from booting it up.

This game feels like skipping to the end-game of an MMO where there's nothing left to do. You get unlimited free ships, there are no performance things to unlock or progress, everyone has the same gear from the start. It's all cosmetic unlocks. You have to REALLY like the PvP sea battles to spend any time in this game because the rest is awful.

The core of the game is ruthless PvP between ships and their crews in multiplayer, which is well executed and surely exciting for fans of the genre. There "is" PvE content but it's utter garbage filler to slow down the pace of PvP encounters and grinding for cosmetic unlocks. There are Monkey Island and Pirates tie-in experiences, but they are limited and boring, being so watered down compared to the harsh PvP game that they are like family friendly kid rides compared to a rough hockey match. I'm a big (big!) Monkey Island fan but couldn't even complete the first 'adventure' because it was such a boring and limited grind with no mid-game saves, despite there being a lovely 3d version of about 1/3rd of Melee Island to explore.

Fair graphics and sound, nice immersion, and sailing the ships around is quite enjoyable.
