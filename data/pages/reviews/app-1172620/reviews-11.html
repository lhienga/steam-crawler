\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is a 8/10 with your best buds, but probably a 6/10 by yourself.

8/10 : Rare has done an amazing job bringing the public a game where one can plunder to their hearts content, or get lost in a world of lore and cosmetic challenges that keeps the fashion-seeking pirate entertained. There are still plenty of glitches and bugs that come with every content update, but I believe Rare is one of the few companies that tries to keep the community informed on what problems they may experience. Getting a group of your close friends in on some voyages makes for a fun time. The vibes usually fluctuate between goofy shenanigans to "locked-in" grind mode with the boys.

6/10 : Rare has given the single-player side of the game a LOT of love. However, nothing is going to fix that slightly frustrating scenario of grinding for loot for over 30 minutes, just to have a bigger ship/crew pull up on you and set you back to square one. They do offer a "Safe Seas" mode that will allow you to go on the same voyages as you normally could, but at a reduction of loot and you cannot use all your cosmetics that are normally at your disposal. To me, this seems a tad disappointing, only due to the fact that there is no game-breaking edge that could be given to a player at a high level versus someone starting out fresh. So why give a repercussion to someone who doesn't wish to participate in online multiplayer?

Still overall a fantastic game to play and I highly recommend that if you are looking for a fun game to live out your pirating fantasies, this is the game for you!\t\t\t\t\t
