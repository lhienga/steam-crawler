\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMS ruins quite a lot of things, and honestly I didn't play this game before they bought it (when the hype was over) and thus, I didn't understand a lot of the appeal, and still don't. It's fun if you're 8, maybe even 9, but currently the game feels super bloated and yet extremely hollow, which it always was. There's no real upgrades, you continuously repeat the same extremely boring loop which consists of sailing to an island, killing a couple of enemies, digging up a chest and returning to another island with said "loot". It's honestly pathetic, as far as MP games go. The ONLY thing you can and will ever be able to buy with the "loot" (which always ends up being gold) is cosmetics, which are factually worthless. Also, MTX up the wazoo! There's pets, cool! Better pull out your real-life wallet for a game that cost me $40. Also also, there's certain limited campaign-esque modes which end up "rewarding" you with (you probably guessed it!) even more worthless gold and cosmetics. All-in-all, in 2024 there are so many games that are worth your time SO much more than this cashgrab MS immediately saw potential in and bagged, because this game is built on nothing but dragging you in and forcing you to play for your own amusement, and frankly, with no benefits gained ever, you might as well go touch grass with your friends, instead of forcing them to download 100+ GIGAbytes of bloat. Very disappointed, but not surprised, as my first 17 hours in this game were literally the same (during the great pandemic) and my final hour was spent configuring the EXTREMELY bloated settings menu (4 different sensitivies, really?) and sailing in a completely empty, playerless map. Dead.\t\t\t\t\t
