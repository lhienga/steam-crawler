\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started with Season 11, which, from what I hear, is radically different in terms of play and time investment. In moments, I can jump into my ship, queue up a quest, dive to it's location (or sail there, they tend to be close by to your position) and complete a handful in a relatively short amount of time.

I feel like what I put into this game, is what I get out of it. It's not wasting my time with nonsense, and losing a ship is scarcely a setback.

The constant threat of piracy, fighting of ships, and the unlikely alliances make every game a different experience from one session to the next.

This is an open world with a good time.\t\t\t\t\t
