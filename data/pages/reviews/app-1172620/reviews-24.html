Played the beta, have the day one eye patch, played the test server, also streamed it to half my family for the streamer perks I been around for a second. This game can be the most fun you've had in a video game ever. It can also burn your chaps like none other. It is the best pirate game in the market. Those that try and compete just don't. The game shines brightest when you and your best friends are on the same ship/server just playing the game having fun stealing loot finding loot doing mission and whatever else have you. There is a absolutely bonkers amount of new content if you compare it to how bare bones it was when it launched. It has its issues the EAC is a new big one for the community, I have not run into errors from the anti-cheat most of my issues stem from the servers and just Microsoft being the bloatware that it is with their inherent over complication of everything in existence and their ability to change everything with every new version of windows that never needed to be changed in the first place. (Have had quite a few problems with voice chat in game for a few months now with some of my friends and we just gave up and use discord now) After spending hours on a ship solo to get sank by the 10k hour vet that was bored and losing all of your treasure can be disheartening for sure but your a pirate there's inherent risk involved...that being said its an always on line game there's always going to be griefers, Rare has given you more than one way to stay away from people like that. I don't know where i'm going with this, the game is great with friends not so much if you play solo but there is still much fun to be had if you are playing by yourself. If you want to be a pirate, or captain your own ship sail the "7seas" this is absolutely the game for you. if you want a chill experience with no chance of losing your progression then i would think about the purchase a little more because you might find it a little more hostile than you may have thought. 10000/10 if you pick this up on sale, 9/10 if you pick it up at full price, 0/10 if you just want a safe sailing simulator.\t\t\t\t\t
