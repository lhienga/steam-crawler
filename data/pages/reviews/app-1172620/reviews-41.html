\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSea of Thieves has changed so much for the better. Season 11, current season as of writing this review, has changed the game significantly. I absolutely love the changes made to the voyaging system. The game-play loop is much much much faster and just as rewarding, if not more rewarding as I've experienced in most sessions.

The introduction of safer seas is a good idea but I'm not sure it's been perceived super positively by the masses. It has some heavy limitations, like 70% money and reputation earned. It's definitely for the brand new players learning the game at their own pace without the fear of other players. It's also for doing tall tales if you'd like. I think for those two reasons it's a good option to have.

Per some recent reviews, NOT EVERY PLAYER you run into in this game is toxic. Some definitely are and it sucks but you can just leave the game and start on a new server. It's a piracy game, expect their to be pirates who want your loot. Out side of those, my favorite thing about SoT is the social interaction with strangers on the seas. Sometimes you run into a brand new player just learning the ropes or a veteran who just loves the world. Outside of the game the community is very welcoming, accepting, and helpful. There so many videos on YouTube to help you get started and answer any question you may have.

Last point, the scenery. If you love pretty games with INSANE screenshot possibilities, this is the game for you. Experience the golden hours of sunset, the rising sun blanketing the seas once again, or the star filled night sky. Experience the beauty that is the shore of plenty and its bright white beaches, vibrant islands, and beautiful bright blue water. Head south the ancient isles and explore the rocky islands with their own beauty and history, head northeast to the wilds for the dark blue rough water. The islands have a rough and rigid look and I'm sure someone out there loves that darker theme, lastly, head southeast to the Devils roar and experience the threat of active volcanoes, geysers, earthquakes and fiery hot water. The seas are full of beauty and incredible ambiance that you will never miss out where ever you may be located.

TL:DR; Long post, sorry, just love the game. If you love PVE with optional PVP, or just love PVP and you've wanted to be a pirate building your story, your own legend, this is THE game. RARE has done some incredible things to make the game better and better. SPECIAL PROMO FOR THE NEXT 26 HOURS, 50% OFF, IT'S WELL WORTH IT. Have patience while you learn and don't be scared to reach out to communities if you want to find crew mates.\t\t\t\t\t
