\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL:DR at bottom

Sea of Thieves is in my opinion the best pirate game out there. Hits all the right spots and areas a game like this should strive to. PvE feels great, the combat on boats feels perfect, threats of the computer can be easy all the way to difficult. PvP is a hard spot for me personally to describe, I deleted my old review due to it being just ranting about toxicity when in reality it was frustration from the "meta". Pvp is amazing IF you know what to do and when to do it. Tends to be a lot of boarding and getting one shot by sweats but you can improve if you try. Hourglass is the PvP mode which is a lot of my issues with this game but it is unfair to judge with that since you specifically have to select it to even participate. Usually just ends up with some toxic people who are rude and taunt you even if you are new to PvP. If you can get past that the amount of experience you can gain is actually huge. I can somewhat handle boarders now and even board better now myself. The biggest bonus is the practice steering, firing cannons, and overall managing your boat. Which brings me back to PvE, almost everything you learn in Hourglass can translate into the normal gameplay. Skeleton/Ghost ships require you to sink them or escape them, hand to hand is different with real players but it still helps with situational awareness since in gameplay you have to be aware of things constantly to not get jumped by players or over run by computer enemies.

Best pirate game, PvE is amazing Pvp is frustrating sometimes but good learning experience with some enjoyable fights.
4/5\t\t\t\t\t
