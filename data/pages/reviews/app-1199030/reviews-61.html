I'm not a huge deckbuilder afficianado, but I really like roguelikes and its fun experimenting and learning each character class as you unlock them even if its a bit intimidating at first. Definitely worth it if you like dark fantasy, rogue likes, especially if its on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
