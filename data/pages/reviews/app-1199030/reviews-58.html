\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSlay the spire meets Dark soul. Outstanding roguelike deck building game.

Tainted grail is the best one that combines the story and the roguelike element. It provides extra layer of motivation to keep playing again and again. Lots of similar games only have plain conversions, which just add a little flavor to the game play. Here, the story telling is the main part. Deck building mechanism is not as special as Monster train but is still well designed.

----------
Pros:
Insanely great atmosphere. Every conversion is voiced and every card is animated. The best in this genre.

Art work-- masterpiece in this genre.

Interesting gameplay. There are enough combinations, synergies to keep every game plays differently.

The Soul-like story blends with the roguelike mechanism very well (also the best in this genre). The quest system is brilliant. Unlike Griftland, Tainted Grail provides much tighter and cohesive experience, which makes you want to play next game to see how the story goes.
--------------

Cons:
Not meaningful exploration element. It's only fun in the first few runs.

Enemy types or combinations are not enough. It feels like I have thousand ways to solve only a dozen challenges.
------------------

All in all, Tainted Grail is a great game. Definitely worth the money.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm having a tough time with this one. It has great reviews, cool voice-acting and a story. I should love this game. I am just so absolutely bored. It's not the repetition of content that's the problem. It's the quality of content being repeated that's the issue here. I've killed the 3rd boss. According to the achievements, only 38% of players have gotten that far.. clearly I'm not the only one who's bored..

In the description in the top-right of the store page the devs defined this (as of this review) as a roguelike. It's not. It's a rogueLITE. They're not the same thing, A roguelike means you carry nothing over into your future runs, you just try to do better or hope for better rng. Roguelite, is a 'light' version with 'elements' of a roguelike except you have have unlockables for future runs, and carry some items/skills over into future runs so it gets easier the more you play. That's this. How this is mislabeled by its own creator is beyond me. I mean, do you know the difference? Do you know what you even made here...?

Well anyway, with that out of the way, why am I bored by this game? With many rogue-type games you repeat content, a lot. I get that. But this is slooooow, very, very limited, content. It's the exact same enemies time, and time again with very, very little variation. Yes it's dressed up very nice, but it's same few, boring enemies, over, and over and over. RNG of new runs doesn't change the enemies, just where they are. So you still face the same ones, all over again.

I'm at almost 12 hours with some classes unlocked, and I honestly don't care about the other (unlockable) classes, because no matter what class I am, it's the exact same enemies, fight after fight, that I'm already bored to tears with. At 10 hours in, I struggled to click the play button. Finally after a few days away I made myself play just now, for an hour and was just not enjoying it. Yes, re-read that, I had to 'force' myself to play this.

Maybe others are more easily amused. Maybe I'm missing the 'excitement' of such a slow paced game. Maybe it's just me. What little action there is gets interrupted by something quest-related 2 out of every 3 fights, and many times is pointless until you randomly run into the 2nd half of it's quest, then back to the 1st part again in another future, random run. Repeating content is fine, that's the heart of a rogue-type game, but when it's the same slow, boring content for 10+ hours, it doesn't hold my interest, no matter how well dressed it is, no matter how well voiced it is..

And by the way, yes it still has some optimization issues. When I play this game, and only this game, on a mid-level rig, all the fans on my gfx card kick on and it vibrates - I mean, dayum.

Obviously I am in the minority, but this game is just going to sit until I see more content updates, which, you might be interested to know, it had just before I bought it. I can't even imagine how it was before... holy cow.\t\t\t\t\t
