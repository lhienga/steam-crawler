\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a lover of deck building games and dark fantasy games, I really enjoy this game. It blend the two so well. My mind thinks of this game as Dark Souls if it were a trading card game.
The six starting classes are basically different versions of three different play styles; melee, ranger, summoner. I appreciated the six different options.
The game play is challenging and engaging. There are even several quest lines to follow, which I enjoyed. After encounters you get to add cards to your deck, trying to create sick combos. After leveling up you get to pick a skill, more combo potential.
The replayablity is great. You get stronger and wiser with each run. Even after beating the game, you learn of a secret final boss. So now I, not only want to beat the game with each class, but beat that secret boss.
All in all, I recommend for card players. Great work developers!\t\t\t\t\t
