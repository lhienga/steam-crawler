\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello!
My names is Nils and i have spent almost 10h playing Tainted Grail when writing this review. Games is rogue like type. You build your deck of cards to fight enemies, upgrade your character attributes and build your village.
Will start with good stuff:
- Game design dark fantasy ( with J.P. Lovecraft vibe to it) - games atmosphere really does draw you in and make u feel abandoned in this world of horrors;
- All game is voice acted;
- Different classes;
- High replayablity;
Bad stuff.
- Game unfortunately is badly optimized;
- Has some lags;
- Game tends to drop error what make all game crash;
- Some voiced characters in game don't fit the narrative and their visual drawing.( this is my personal preference);
- Summon creatures for classes could be more unique rather then recolored version of themselves.( this is my personal preference);

Overall , by no means this is a bad game. Even tho there is big problem with optimization game is replayeable and for amount of time u can spend playing different classes and unlocking futures, price (16,79EUR) adequate.
If u are Rogue like game fun, then this game should give u at least 20-30h worth of enjoyable game time.
