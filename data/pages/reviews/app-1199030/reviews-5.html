\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't buy it. I just requested a refund for this game. The first game I ever try to refund on Steam. It really is that bad. The story is ridiculously convoluted, irrational, implausible and mundane. The enemies are hopelessly overpowered (I put it to "easy" and a common archer still fxxks me up - even though I leveled like crazy to technically outmatch those archers - while I have to go into the inventory to eat up every health pie and potion that I have), the fighting system is complete cxxp and on the level of games from the early 2000s (even Oblivion had a better fighting system... and Oblivion didn't have a good fighting system at all). This is not retro, this is just bad game design.

As a 3D & VFX artist, I have to shake my head over the badly executed animations as well as the mismatched lip sync animations of the characters. Also the design of the character models look like that of amateurs, or maybe from an asset they bought on the UE market place (could I do that better? Yes! Yes I could! And I did in the past - many times. Character design is one of my special abilities offered to clients. It's part of my job, and I tell you: They delivered a subpar result). The framerate is tanking below 25 fps at around the north horn - even with an RTX 4090 running on 32 gigs of 3600 MHZ ram and a ryzen 9 twelve core processor (so it's not a hardware problem, they just fxxed up the efficiency of the drawcalls for the given simulation cell - I never had such a bad framerate even with the notoroiusly resource hungry Cyberpunk 2077 with that same system I am currently operating).

The only thing that works kinda okay are the audio lines of the voice actors. They really try and do their best to deliver the lines given to them in a somewhat lively fashion. But that's really it - the number of the "pro" items for this game is very limited. It is a short list.

I am all for giving an indie game like this, with great aspirations, a fair chance. But this... this is so severly botched, that it almost borders on being a scam.

If you love Skyrim, Oblivion and the Witcher series and somewhat expect this to be similiar: Don't buy this game - it is not worth your time or your money.\t\t\t\t\t
