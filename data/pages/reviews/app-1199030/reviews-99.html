\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
Decent graphics
Intriguing story lines and progression.

Cons:
Of the 6 classes I've unlocked, the classes are too similar to each other.
Blood Mage is almost identical to the Summoner.
The Arrow users are almost identical to each other.
Individual classes (Sentinel, Pathfinder, Zealot, Wyrdhunter) are woefully underpowered compared to the multiple-enemy group encounters.

The only viable class to survive multiple encounters is the Summoner, and that's due to the free attack given each round.

Enemies are WAY overpowered and the player is WAY underpowered.

Considering the damage output of enemies, half the energy spent needs to be for defensive buffs, leaving little for damage output. If a group of enemies has a total of 600hp with barriers, heals and resummons, and my character does 8 damage per card, I don't stand much of a chance.

Interesting game, but it needs to be toned way down.\t\t\t\t\t
