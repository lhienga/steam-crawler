first the bad: The game gives you too much freedom, and thus runs can take a long time; basically you can go around killing every single enemy, which kind of NEGATES the meaning of having branching paths... they added some consumable candles to try and force players to RUSH through the levels I think? Doesn't really work

also annoying. THE UI. it feels clunky, and unintuitive... I'm still searching for a way to enter the menu with just the mouse, and I fear it does not exist. NOW THE GOOD:

Game is well made, FULLY VOICE ACTED!! (professionally so) although the voice acting is much slower than normal reading speed which causes me to want to skip it 90% of the time... Game also has quite a few options for replayability with the different classes, passives, & The NPC Quests (which REQUIRE replaying many times)

Graphics are good. Enemies look cool, SOUNDTRACK has some AWESOME tracks, especially some boss themes. Basically this game is totally worth it if it is on sale, and if you like ROGUELIKES, and deckbuilders, this game is ALWAYS WORTH IT!!! give it a try ~

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a review after i play about 700 hour and have 100% completion ( all cards, passives, achievements,...)

What i like in this game:
- Very deep and complex character building with cards / runes / passives
- 9 different classes, each of them is distinct and play differently
- You can comprehend all mechanics in an hour, and u can`t master them in 700 hours. Every run i find a new way to broke the game
- Soundtrack is 20 out of 10, voice acting, visual art of the card, beasts, fact u can use Bard as a jukebox and all other cool little touches
- Every run is different. Every run u find different cards, runes, passives, fights, bosses,...
- You start a run, u got to go, ALT + F4, you are back, start the game, you are exactly where you was. Priceless!

What i don`t like in this game:
- every new run difficulty is set to highest possible and if i want a chill run (or play a class i`m not good at) i have to click 30+ times to turn it down (minor minor issue only when you reach high difficulty)
- i go to sleep too late every time i play :)

Great, great game. I can't recommend it enough!\t\t\t\t\t
