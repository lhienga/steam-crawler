\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoyed the world that was presented to me, and how traveling from area to area had an actual dungeon crawl feel. I didn't like how easy the game was, the lack of real Roguelike elements. It would have been better if when you died you had to start from the first guardian rather than just heading to beat the next one in the next area. I literally only played the starter "Warrior" default deck and completed almost everything the game had to offer OTHER than the "unique classes." I did that in under 10 hours of playtime. I've seen a few of the other classes I've not unlocked yet played (streamers/youtube/etc) and their playstyles are drastically different, some of which are a true "hard mode" from looking outside in, but the grind to a harder difficulty doesn't feel worth it.

For the base price of $20 you'll get a solid experience and not feel cheated out of your money, but once you figure out the base of how the game works (which should take a normal person probably 5 or so hours) any difficulty the game has instantly vanishes.\t\t\t\t\t
