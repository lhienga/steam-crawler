\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile this game has some interesting characters and does fairly well in the deckbuilding mechanics, the rest of the game is just not very good.

It has a severe lack of polish for example: bad voiceacting, bad graphics (looks like a 16-bit color graphic setting is always on), does not run well and most importantly all the elements are implemented in a sluggish way.... they are just placed on the map like indifferent objects. There are buildings which are just sized too small, evil monsters stand next to civilians clipping into each other, character models which look nothing like the character in the dialogue window you see after speaking to them... it is so bad. I just have the feeling that the whole presentation is just randomly chosen Unity Engine models thrown into a lackluster game.
The devs could have at least tried to polish it up a bit. I mean once you enter the world map there are just some odd models standing around in place and you run around lightning up torches which have a silly amount of particle effects? Feels more like an alpha.

The characters and deckbuilding works mostly well. Eventhough some classes are smoother than others. Summoners are very complicated at first because you don't know where the enemy strikes apparently?

At last I also want to say that these body horror types of games are so overdone. I mean besides the background story (which is barely existent) this game has zero connections to the King Arthur myth. And seeing those grotesque and disgusting enemys I'd rather watch The Thing or Hellraiser then playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t

100% I'd recommend this game if you like roguelites with lots of combinations, more realistic graphics in comparison, great music with lots of replayability.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA Deep and Immersive Dark Fantasy Adventure

Rating:

Tainted Grail presents a rich and immersive dark fantasy experience that captivates players with its atmospheric storytelling, strategic gameplay, and intricate world-building. With its deep narrative, challenging mechanics, and stunning artwork, the game stands as a testament to the potential of tabletop-inspired digital adventures.

One of the standout features of Tainted Grail is its engrossing narrative. The game presents a sprawling and intricate story, filled with morally ambiguous choices, deep character development, and branching paths. The dark and mysterious world of Avalon is brought to life through captivating writing and detailed lore, offering players a wealth of lore to uncover and unravel.

The strategic gameplay mechanics in Tainted Grail provide a satisfying challenge. The game seamlessly blends RPG elements with card-based combat and exploration mechanics, creating a system that requires thoughtful decision-making and strategic planning. The combination of character progression, resource management, and tactical combat offers depth and variety, ensuring that each playthrough feels unique.

Tainted Grail's artwork and visual design are truly breathtaking. The hand-drawn illustrations and atmospheric landscapes bring the grim and haunting world of Avalon to life. The attention to detail in character designs, environments, and monster illustrations adds depth and immersion to the game, creating a visual feast for players.

The game's soundtrack and audio design are also noteworthy. The haunting melodies and atmospheric sound effects complement the game's dark themes and add an additional layer of immersion. The carefully crafted audio enhances the overall experience, further drawing players into the world of Tainted Grail.

While Tainted Grail excels in many aspects, it does have a few minor drawbacks. The game's complexity and depth may be overwhelming for some players, requiring a significant investment of time and effort to fully grasp its mechanics and systems. Additionally, the difficulty curve can be steep at times, potentially frustrating players who are seeking a more casual experience.

In conclusion, Tainted Grail is a remarkable dark fantasy adventure that excels in narrative depth, strategic gameplay, and visual design. Its engrossing storytelling, challenging mechanics, and immersive world-building offer a captivating experience for fans of deep and atmospheric games. While it may not be suited for players seeking a casual or straightforward experience, Tainted Grail rewards those who are willing to dive into its intricacies and immerse themselves in the haunting world of Avalon.\t\t\t\t\t
