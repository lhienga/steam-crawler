However, the game needs a lot of tuning: class balance, progression rewards, all of these seem out of whack and 'early-accessy'.

If the above issues would be sorted, this would be a game I would recommend to anyone. As it stands, I would recommend this game to players who are looking something new to play after games like Slay the Spire or Monster Train.\t\t\t\t\t
