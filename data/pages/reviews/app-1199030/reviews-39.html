\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I'm submitting a request for a refund because this game attempts to boil water with my CPU despite being incredibly unimpressive visually. If I turn everything to minimum and lock the FPS at 30, I can get the CPU temperature to hover around 80-90C, which is better than the default settings which push it all the way to 100 and trigger the throttle. It's just incredibly poorly optimized.

Now if there was a killer game behind this, I could endure the 30fps and extra terrible graphics. And there is - if you haven't already played Dream Quest, Slay the Spire, Monster Train, et cetera, et cetera. If you have, the only new thing here is the interactive novel that accompanies the generic roguelike deck builder. Obviously with less than two hours I have no clue what the whole story is, but it sure seems a lot like Loop Hero.

The combination of technical shortcomings with the "nothing special" gameplay (at least in the first two hours) makes this a no-go for me.\t\t\t\t\t
