\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasically this game is "slay the spire but...."

Now compared to slay the spire, I'd say the differences are as follows

a) much fewer opportunities for removing cards from your deck, but on the other hand your starting deck is quite good
b) every time you play you gain some currency which helps you upgrade stuff like your starting hand, remove cards, etc
c) there is this system where you have to basically use a torch that has ranks and can refuel it, at high ranks every couple of turns it can give you cards that can help you and at low ranks it will give you cards that will hinder you (even if you don't play them)
d) after every fight there is some sort of event reward, you can think of it in sts as having a question mark after each monster on the map, only they don't seem to be any negative ones
e) you get to add cards based on your character's level (which resets on every run) not for every fight you win
f) the equivalent of relics are passives which again you get to choose on each of your character's 20 levels and from certain events and bosses
g) it is quite difficult to have extra energy so you can't plan on "oh this card will be good when I have more energy in the future"
h) at lower difficulties (sts ascensions) the game is very easy and I think it was planned this way, the challenge comes from higher difficulties (for comparison I frequently get my ass kicked in ascension 0, but I'm pretty sure that with 2-3 characters I've learned how to play I can always beat the game on diffuclty 1)
i) There are 9 different classes, under 3 archetypes (melee , archer and summoner) , the classes have different passives and sometimes tweaked cards compared to other classes of the same archetype, so even though they are under the same archetype , they play quite differently, and it's been a fun puzzle figuring out how each one should work, but in effect picking a class is like saying here's how I will play for the rest of the run , it's like starting StS and saying, I will now play a claws defect or a powers defect or a poison silent


I'll probably update this if I think of more

overall it's a good game for a decent asking price\t\t\t\t\t
