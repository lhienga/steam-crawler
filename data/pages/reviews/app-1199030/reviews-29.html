\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great game, blending RPG with Card game elements. Decent story, but what is great is the addictive gameplay and deck building choices with a bunch of classes to pick and upgrade and a lot of cards to customize your deck through playthroughs. It also has great replay value. On the negatives its the grinding required to reach the "true ending"(i wont spoil but you dont miss much even if you dont reach it, kind disappointing) the OST although having some nice tracks they get repeated a lot and because of the huge re-runs required they can turn tiring and boring quickly, i just played youtube music on the background after a while. Also you can get screwed for a whole playthrough and its rest runs with some choices, that was a big yikes cause i was locked on from some NPC quests and couldnt finish them despite having done all the rest to unlock the true ending.

Give the game a try if you like card games and RPG with strategy feels with choices than matter.\t\t\t\t\t
