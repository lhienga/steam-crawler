\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:

- Art style is very nice with great atmosphere
- Soundtrack is awesome
- Gameplay is very good, if you liked StS, you will like this one
- Classes & Factions concept is neat

Cons:

- Very janky, the animations in combat sometime slow down, especially if that's a lot going on (chain, multishot with tons of cards in hand sometimes delay the animations
- Although the classes are nice, the cards and passives feels very generic
- Balance is nowhere to be seen, Apostate is so ridiculously op that's not even funny. Even a toddler could finish this game with an Apostate. And the other classes are mostly boring, especially the summoners.

7.5/10

Overall I got my money's worth and I recommend for rogue-like fans.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI picked this game up after a friend brought the board game over to play one night. I had immediately fell in love with the lore, and world building. I picked this up to experience the lore and world building. Voice acting is top notch, and most of the story plays out with a still image and a narrator. Its soothing and calming and very excellent to chill out and immerse yourself in in this dark world.

I didn't have much experience with deck builders going in. I will say the first two hours isn't a good representation of the game. The first class didn't grab me and the cards were pretty basic and boring mechanically. I refunded the game 60-120 min in. I seen later someone stating exactly what i was feeling in those first two hours, urging people to keep going a bit more and the game opens up and hooks you.

So i re bought it gave it a second chance, mostly because the themes, world building and lore is so strong. I urge you to do the same if your first impressions aren't stellar. I lost an evening to the game without realizing the sun had gone down. This happened once the game and second class opened up. ETA 2 hours in. I'm sure to lose many more evenings now. I have the Tainted Grail needle stuck in my arm, and I probably wont pull it out until I'm a few hundred hours deep.\t\t\t\t\t
