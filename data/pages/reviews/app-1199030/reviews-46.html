I'll start with some bad ones:
- The whole "walking your character" part and things related to it feels very off. You can skip fights to get to the "reward" behind them which seems intentional but the way you do it feels like something was missed and you are abusing it.
In general I feel like this walking mechanic was totally unnecessary, at least in this state.
- The hints or other parts that try to communicate to the player via text really throws me off. The way it is written disconnects you from the game and totally ruins the immersion. There are text that wish you good luck and such which are not said by a particular character but just thrown there by the devs and it feels really weird. - I don't think the game is balanced good enough but that's rather a minor issue given it's a single player game.

And some good ones:
- The three factions each with it's own pool of cards and three sub-classes each feel really good. Especially when you unlock them progressively and try them out one after the other.
- The card design is really interesting.
- A great pool of passive abilities for each class to make your character really powerful in different ways.
- NPCs and their intertwined quests feel really good.
- In general things add up to give a good amount of replayability.\t\t\t\t\t
