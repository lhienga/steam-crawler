\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIntriguing idea, the execution? less so.

First things first, what I did like about the game is the great voice acting (not gonna downplay it) and the setting which are top notch.

However, the gameplay loop gets repetitive quickly unlike Slay the Spire and some other games from the genre.

The UI is messy and unintuitive, some of the cards and classes have interesting mechanics but for the most part - just overcomplicated to a point where the depth comes from variety of boring mechanics instead of depth that can be found through simplicity and the deck chemistry itself.

You may give it a try because the game is pretty well made and the price tag isn't high, but I personally wouldn't recommend it.
