While I feel it does not offer as much variety in build for each of the nine classes offered (as far as cards go), it makes up for this in passive abilities and runes as well as the fact that the 9 classes play reasonably differently from one another. There are a variety of small quests to unlock mastery of the game, and a good balance of unlockables to power up your rogue-lite experience. While I would not rate this as highly as (for example) Slay the Spire, I would rate it as a very strong contender. Worth the price, and quite fun.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is really fun if you are a fan of the emerging rogue-like genre, similar to Slay the Spire. My main complaint would be the imbalance between melee/range classes and the very Strong Summoners. I feel so much stronger with the Classes that can summon versus the other classes , both in defensive and offensive capabilities, that also snowball in ways that the other classes don't.
The melee classes hit harder initially but the standard defensive ability block that melee classes have will only negate 1 hits worth of damage and remains on you until the end of the turn, this is good for enemies hitting you with one hard ability but most rounds you are commonly facing multiple enemies that may individually be hitting you 2-3 times. Summoner can apply shields to themselves and summoned companions that block 10+ damage and remain on you permanently until you take damage , you also can summon a Golem that will take a minimum of 70 damage for you dealing 1 damage to you each time they are hit , where you can rely on your shield to take that damage instead of HP. This is so much stronger than block, block doesn't even stay on you more than 1 round.
With all this being said I believe it would be pretty easy to fix if they simply add some life nice life stealing / DOT / Buff attacks and better mechanics to bock. Melee / Ranged classes have temporary defense that isn't effective or sustainable. I might be missing something or haven't invested enough levels into the classes to see the optimal way to have effective defense and offense within the same round for these melee/ranged classes so feel free to provide solutions if you have found any :).

All in all I still see great potential and replay ability in this game considering you can also increase the difficulty and look forward to seeing additional content/reworks with classes.\t\t\t\t\t
