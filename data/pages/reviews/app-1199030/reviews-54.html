\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I've been playing this on and off, and I just keep coming back. The world is interesting, and the in lore reason for the rogue-like nature of the game is rather clever. The classes thus far play so differently and definitely encourage the player to experiment. The summoner being a great example, as the first one you unlock after the starter of the Wyrdhunter, it plays vastly different relying on summons to do any damage and having to also protect them to protect your own health so it really makes you think which summon and which ability is the best for the situation. The animation and artwork are striking and leave an impression right of the bat.\t\t\t\t\t
