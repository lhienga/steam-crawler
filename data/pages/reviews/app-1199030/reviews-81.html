\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a delightful turn-based roguelike. Shares many similarities to Slay the Spire and the like, but with some major differences. First, the game is very story heavy, which is unusual for the genre, with plenty of lore and interesting characters and events to encounter. Second, the game carries a lot more progression than I've seen in other roguelikes, and the game is designed to be played long-term, with a lot of various progression systems for you to work through that provide permanent upgrades to your characters.

The graphics are great, and man, the soundtrack is just fantastic. The boss music is some of the best I've ever heard. Absolutely recommend, but be warned, you begin the game with only one class (out of 9) unlocked. For myself, personally, I hated the introductory class, and I'm really glad I pushed through my first couple hours to unlock some of the others, which I found vastly more interesting and complex. This game is still in production as I'm writing, and there's more to come, but I've already spent a healthy amount of time on the game, and there's still plenty more content for me to work through even in the current state of the game. This one is worth your time and money.\t\t\t\t\t
