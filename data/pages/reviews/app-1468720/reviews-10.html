\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA few rounds of varying units in varying formations and maps is quite fun and the game performs surprisingly well even on a tad older computer. My GTX 1080 Ti and i7-8700K worked OK (as in steady-ish 30FPS) with millions of units roaming the map. Hella cool!

But after a while you start to realize that the experience is very boring. You can get almost the same experience from all the numerous YouTube videos with clickbait titles and gasping person thumbnails. Watch a few hours of those and you'll get the core idea of the game.

The UI is bad, and planning battles on the map is very tedious and frustrating. I dare you to try and properly position a single unit army from the edge of the map to the opposite side without pulling your hair out.

The AI is very basic. Hordes of bots using the simplest pathfinding mechanism possible just to rush the nearest hostile bot. No strategy, no tactics. The RTS mode helps, but due to the clunky UI you just wish you'd never have to use it.

Hopefully the devs will continue working on this, the idea is good but the execution (heh) just isn't there yet.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great game. Truly. But there are a couple of points I want to address which I experienced:

- lag. Too much troops is lag. This could be at my end though, none the less, there is no option for me to optimize this game, and I partly also think that is the problem, the fact that I cannot optimize the game. I'm sure the game will run smoother when I can.

- Scroll option while choosing units. The scroll is too slow! It takes hours to scroll down. And when you use the scroll bar manually (instead of the scroll wheel on your mouse) it goes too fast with big jumps. There are two things that you can do here:

1. Speed the scroll sensitivity up, when using scroll wheel on the mouse;
2. Add a search bar so we can find the units quicker!

- Map glitch. Sometimes I start underneath the map. Why?

- Loading time. The loading time when initializing the battle can sometimes be rough, and often it even happens that the countdown when initializing the battle disappears, and you have a black screen, with only outside ambiance. When it happened I had to restart the game. a second time mostly fixed it. This happened twice, and this is now in my back head, when is this going to happen again, which it definitely will!

But this game is for the rest super fun! I would though still suggest, you would add some more maps. There is more ancient than modern, and I would like to see some more modern map variety! Too that some more castle maps could be cool too, although the existing ones are already super cool!

The world war 2 maps are cool too, but I wish there was some more of that too. Berlin, Stalingrad, Paris, something major battle. And these city maps you could combine with modern assets for modern warfare. Just a suggestion. You did a great job. If it keeps my attention, I play it! I got so far 4 hours. It is time killing and exactly what you want with a Total War like sandbox game.

I haven't played the campaign yet. But I will soon! Thank you for creating such a cool game!
