\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI know I've only played the game for 30 minutes (steam glitched out, i actually played for around an hour and a half) but I'm going to refund it and I'm fully confident about this.
The game is great. I love it. In a nutshell, it's TABS with emphasis on optimization allowing you to have 300k troops on-screen, easily. That's what I love most about it. If I love it so much, why a negative review?

The game costs WAY too much. If we're gonna continue comparing it to TABS, it's the same price for a slightly different gameplay style. The real issue is how LITTLE content there is. I bet I could probably enjoy this game for 5+ hours, but for a sandbox game, $20 is NOT it. TABS on the other hand has hours upon hours of campaign content along with a crazy high amount of variety in the troops and unique artstyle. I get that this game has mods too, but once again, there's just such little content.

Earlier I said that I love the game, and it's true. I just think it should be something like $10 instead, maybe even $5. For such a - and I hate to say it - "low effort" game, it would do great as a cheaper sandbox game.

TL;DR:
The game is awesome but way too expensive for how little content there is.

Wait-for-a-sale/10\t\t\t\t\t
