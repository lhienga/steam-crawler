\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter waiting for the release of version 1.00, it's time for my review of Ultimate Epic Battle Simulator 2 (UEBS2). As a player of both UEBS2 and its predecessor, I can attest that the game is enjoyable, albeit marred by a significant number of bugs. This is the main reason I hesitate to recommend it to my friends.

Bugs Galore
The game's official release brings into question the developers' understanding of a finished product. For instance, units do not operate as expected in areas indicated as usable. This forces players to spend upwards of 20 minutes figuring out the quirks, which can be quite frustrating. This issue is particularly bothersome for those with limited gaming time, and it seems indicative of the developers' own time constraints. Rather than addressing these issues, they've chosen to release an unpolished product.

Missing Features
UEBS1 had some great elements such as the cavalry that could wipe out units simply by running into them. It's disappointing to find that such an immersive feature has been omitted from UEBS2.

Unit Fielding Improvements
There's a silver lining though. One common gripe with UEBS1 was the limited number of units one could field, which seems to have been addressed in UEBS2. This improvement is a step in the right direction, allowing a wider range of players to field a larger number of units.

Future Hopes
With the release of v1.00, I suspect the developers are gearing up for UEBS3. My hope is for improved sound design, better AI for managing units, and a more polished, bug-free gaming experience.

Final Thoughts
Despite my criticisms, it's important to note my deep love for this game. It remains my favourite game of all time, but the frustration from its constant bugs prevents me from recommending it to others. The game demands an extraordinary level of patience to navigate its numerous bugs, which can be off-putting for many players.

UEBS2 has potential, but until the bugs are adequately addressed, it remains a diamond in the rough. I look forward to the day when I can wholeheartedly recommend it to my friends.\t\t\t\t\t
