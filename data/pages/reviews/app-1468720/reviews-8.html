\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts good. Not great, but good.

I didn't play UEBS 1, so I had no idea what I was in for but the price, it was good enough to take a chance. I also might have mixed feeling about it because this game seems more strategy, where I wanted to play it more like a first/ third person shooter like Battlefront.

It's a little on the buggy side. I start under the map more times than not which is annoying, as there's no way to get on the map except start the match over. Sometimes units spawn outside the boundary lines and there's no way to bring them in except... restarting the match.

IDK, to me, the controls feel a bit janky. Im not sure if there's support for an XBOX controller, but if there isn't, would be nice for the devs to add it. Would also be nice to not have to keep jumping into different modes to do different things. Took me forever to figure out you can only poses a unit in god mode.

Performance with settings on high and big number squads seems to run great on my PC. I'm running Ryzen 7 5800X 8-Core, GeForce RTX 3060, 64gig ram (I do a lot of video editing) and 2 onboard ssd drives.

Game gets a little boring after a while as map and unit selection is a touch scarce. Hopefully the devs add more.

Wish list for this game would include:
-A compass and or minimap, especially when you have control over a a NPC.
-A way of finding the squads during gameplay
-As mentioned above, more units/ maps
-The ability to change battle music/ add tracks. The song is epic, but not so much when you have heard it 30 times over
-Ability to jump while controlling a unit

Its still a fairly new game though, so I imagine more is to come. With this in mind, for the price, I say go for it.\t\t\t\t\t
