\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is more of a tech-demo than a game.
As such, expect a mountain of user-unfriendliness and jank.

Basic things this game is missing:

Maps
-No map creator.

Sandbox
-No tooltip that tells me which army is what when hovering over their army box.
-No way of specifically selecting one army when they are overlapping on the map.
-No way to sort your list of saved battles in game.

Character Customisation
-no way to sort your list of units in game.
-no way to rename your list of units in game.
-no way to sort your list of customised profiles.
-the drop-down window with the list of total units is abysmally tiny.
-the drop-down window's scrolling is abysmally slow.
-the camera does not scale to the unit size or height, meaning you will only see the legs of large units or nothing of flying ones in the character customisation screen.

Unit Stats
-ATTACK SPEED does not tell you the unit's base attack speed so you can't even tell what the hell are you even multiplying. No tooltip.
-SPLASH DAMAGE RADIUS does not tell you what the numbers mean. Wtf is 1? 1 meter? 1 feet? No tooltip.
-ATTACK RANGE does not tell you what the numbers mean. No tooltip.
-IMPACT FORCE does not tell you what the numbers mean. No tooltip.
-SPLASH DAMAGE does not tell you how it works. Does it bypass defences? Who knows. No tooltip.
-SPLASH DAMAGE RADIUS does not tell you what the numbers mean. No tooltip.
-MAX MOVESPEED does not tell you what the numbers mean. No tooltip.
-MAX RANGE BLOCK FORCE does not tell you what the numbers mean. No tooltip.
-MAX RANGE ARMOUR FORCE does not tell you what the numbers mean. No tooltip.
-No "Unstaggerable" tickbox option to negate impact force.
-No "Bleed" tickbox option to negate bleeding for mechanical units.
-No "Flying" tickbox option to negate melee damage and ground hitboxes (meaning other units can walk under it).

Projectiles
-No lasers (Come on).
-No laser beams.
-No high-angle artillery (e.g., mortars)
-No flamethrower flames
-No smoke/gas
-No projectile colour selection option.
-No "piercing" tickbox option for turning projectile piercing on/off.
-No "full-auto" tickbox option for shooters. Meaning, you have to click to shoot every shot when controlling the unit, instead of holding down left click.
-No projectile force stat, meaning you have no clue what MAX RANGE BLOCK FORCE or MAX RANGE ARMOUR FORCE even corresponds to.\t\t\t\t\t
