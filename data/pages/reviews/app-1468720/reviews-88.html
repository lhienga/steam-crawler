\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrankly this sequel is very disapointing to me.

TLDR;
Very limited functionality, for the most part the same or downgrade.

I was hyped when I heard that UEBS 1 would get a sequel as I really loved the game, but for the most part in the current state it's the same if not a downgrade to the first game.
The only true improvement is the amount of units you can put down, and the blood oceans are a fun gimmick. The size of the armies and maps make it difficult to micromanage, plus you are limited to 18 armies. These armies cannot be split into multiple groups. Now this might now seem so bad, but for instance on the new WW2 beach map, in the first game I liked to split up my defending troops between the individual bunkers to have them fire from a protected area.
In the sequel there are way more bunkers than I have armies, and they are not even functional as far as I could tell. Troops can walk right through them like they aren't there, and I'm pretty sure the walls don't block bullets either.\t\t\t\t\t
