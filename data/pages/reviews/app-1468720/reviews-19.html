\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are dozens of small issues that have crossed over from the first game that still haven't been addressed despite them announcing a full 1.0 release a few months back.

Scrolling on drop down menus is laboriously slow and zooms out the map for some reason.

you cannot walk through open doorways and in most cases can't even get close to them.

The maps are riddled with clipping props and invisible walls and have an asset rip vibe to them. very disjointed.


On the plus side, the framerate is much improved and the god powers are much better implemented compared to the original game. The RTS concept is pretty fun and helps to solve the clumping issue with the AI.

My question is this: Why didn't they just fix the issues with the first game instead of copy/pasting the code (bugs and all) to this one?\t\t\t\t\t
