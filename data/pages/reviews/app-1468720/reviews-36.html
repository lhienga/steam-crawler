issues that ive encountered:
  • deflection mechanics only seem to partially work with arrows - enough arrows can destroy a tank even though thats not realistic. Bullets should also be deflected but there doesn't seem to be a way to do that
  • 2 of the fields in the custom unit creator (max range block force and max armor block force) get reset after every battle, so any customized projectile block is basically impossible
  • a basic dropdown is used to select units in the various menus - after downloading a lot of mods or creating your own units, that drop down becomes cumbersome to use
  • max 18 armies in a battle, which means if you want to really customize unit placement, your options are limited
  • game crashes a lot after multiple battles, and when starting a battle with under 10kish units

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty cool and fun, but only for shorter play sessions as it does get boring rather quickly. It's mainly sandbox only, as the campaign is almost non-existent.

Beware, that even though the game is in full release, it feels very unfinished.
I really do hope they continue working on it. It needs more content and features!

Do I recommend this game?
Maybe! I'd say only if you're really into this type of entertainment.
I would give this a middle thumb for the state of the game right now.


GOOD
- Large scale battles (how large depends on your PC specs)
- Take control over any unit on the battlefield
- Can select units and give orders, like a RTS game
- Easy to customize any units stats in-game (dmg, armor, speed, etc.)
- Steam Workshop

NEUTRAL
- Could really need some sort of automatic action cameras that panned and zoomed in on the action hotspots

BAD
- Campaign; barely any content, but you play this game mainly for the sandbox anyway.
- Very limited selection of unit types
- Can't rebind keys
- Camera is generally way to slow and sluggish for the large battlefields
- Buggy! F.ex. sometimes units does not load on the battlefield.\t\t\t\t\t
