\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUEBS 2 (2022)
Overall, a gigantic improvement from UEBS 1. instead of only being able to do 10-100x the people for 200x worse gameplay and feeling like a tech demo UEBS 2 although still in very early development actually feels like a game now allowing you to place EVEN MORE units and with even more detail! apart from that UEBS 2 also features an RTS mode and improvements to AI and Performance allowing even the most potato computers to render thousands of units.
8.5/10 (In it's current state)

Pros:
- Simulation of GIGANTIC battles with many different armies and choices with pretty good AI too (If you know how to make a good one the game is totally worth it)
- Direct control over your units and an FPS mode that isn't as janky as TABS's (mainly due to the subpar physics)
- A pretty good roster of units
- Massive maps that allow you to make hour long battles
- Less demanding specs than TABS due to the optimizations
- Water physics that allow you to flood whole battlefields

Cons:
- Lack of workshop features or mods (In it's current state)
- A very much subpar campaign that doesn't work too well with the RTS style gameplay
- Very rudimentary RTS system that really only works with large battles where fine and precise movements don't really matter
- Guns feel janky and recoil overall doesn't work well
- Pretty janky physics with catapults and artillery making units flail around in the air
- Half baked UI and QoL features\t\t\t\t\t
