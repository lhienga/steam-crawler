It was good fun and I didn't push the boundaries of what my PC can handle in the terms of the size and scale of a battle. After a few months it then started crashing when on the loading screen to a battle. It would reach 99% and then crash. I thought that this may be because of the number of units I was using. I tried fights between two chickens and still the game crashed. So I left the game until I heard about the introduction of mods. This seemed to have fixed my issue and so I started playing again. After about 10 more matches of modded and regular units of about 500,000 each the game began to crash again at 99%. I thought that this may be because of mods so I uninstalled them and tried a fight between two chickens again. I do this because they're really small and shouldn't take much loading. Though yet again my game crashed at 99%.

In summary this game is a total broken mess. It's totally inconsistent to whether I can play or not and is totally unacceptable. I've heard about people having the same issues and this needs to be sorted. Otherwise many people are stuck with an unplayable game until it is resolved.

Unbelievably disappointing and shouldn't be happening.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun early access game, battles are quite slow with lots of units and don't expect to get the really high numbers unless your pc is good. Development progress is most of the time consistent with monthly patches with sometimes bigger gaps like now as they are working on implementing mod support. The community is alright but a bit impatient at times with regards to updates but I have good faith in the devteam that in a couple years this will be a fully fledged game that surpasses it's predecessor.

Some mechanics are a little rough around the edges, some of the main things that annoyed me during play time were the archers since they don't have a high arcing fire, same with other artillery units which means their projectiles get stuck on things a lot and they don't work very well on walls (if you can manage to get them onto the walls in the first place). The models and maps look highly detailed with the downside currently being lack of attack animations and the RTS and FPS features are currently quite rough and have a long way to go.\t\t\t\t\t
