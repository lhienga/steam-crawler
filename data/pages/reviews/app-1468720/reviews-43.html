\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely write reviews for games, maybe this is my first bad review of a game. This game, in my opinion, is good, but the UI development is very poor and you could say that this game is really unfinished.
The first couple of hours, you might enjoy the gameplay of this game. However, when you reach a point of boredom and try to explore new aspects of the game mechanics, such as creating scenarios for a battle, you'll encounter various difficulties. Considering the poor quality of the UI and the game's content, you might decide to abandon that idea .
The excitement of large-scale battles with numerous troops can indeed become monotonous. In this game, the ragdoll physics are noticeably poor, and there is a lack of variation within the factions and different types of units. It's important to note that purchasing a game solely based on watching someone else play it and finding it entertaining, rather than experiencing it yourself, can lead to quick boredom once you start playing it on your own.
I hope the developer will soon update the UI and game mechanics in this game because it is actually a unique and enjoyable game. It's unfortunate that I spent too long learning the mechanics and playing the game until I reached the point of boredom, and I missed the refund deadline.
