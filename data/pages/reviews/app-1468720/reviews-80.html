\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, I expected so much more from this game.

After waiting for god knows how long with delays, the game has huge flaws. Such as on mount olympus NPCs are just able to climb a mountain at an angle of about 80 degrees, this same effect happens on walls on the city of troy. This is not to mention the characters are also way less diverese than promised by the developers.

The graphics are also genuinely terrible, you look at a character about 20m away and he might as well be a triangle with how little polys they contain. The FPS part of the game is also bad, with bullet fire being super unrealistic. If you plan on playing with modern soldiers in a squad expect to hear the same droning voice line every 2 seconds.

Ways to improve:
- A better squad movement feature, like in the original commanding your squad war far far easier than it is now.
- Diversifying meshes and voice lines, if every character looked slightly different the improvement would be insane, same with the voicelines.
- Better scripting, game feels poorly scripted. Squad management system looks so horrendous.
- Make an option to remove music.

THIS IS NOT EVERYTHING EXPECT FAR MORE GLITCHES IN THE ACTUAL GAME.\t\t\t\t\t
