\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGOOD:
Campaigns look promising. This game really needs campaigns, as the sandbox can get boring.
Early days but It's fun moving armies out of the way of huge objects rolling down the hill. Then organising them to attack the big boss. Great idea.

BAD:
Audio samples for crowd noise I wish were longer, the sample they use on repeat for the groans and collective battle cries is too short and jarring. Needs toning down.
UI graphics are rough. Times font etc.
I'd like to see more fighting moves possible. When controlling a single character, the fighting options are very limited. Just button pushing on/off style block/attack fighting.

Edit: changing to thumbs down. Not enough progress, still the same old funky interface and janky vibe.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this little game. Yeah it's still lacking a lot but I've still managed to put 92 hours into tinkering with battles and settings of just the default units. As it was with the first game, I'm still waiting on glorious mod support for all of the wicked maps and units that come with it. I love the creativity you can play with in these games. The sheer volume of units on screen is mind blowing lol. A super super huge improvement from the first one. I would highly recommend this game to anyone that loves being creative and tinkering with battles. The RTS addition opens the game in new ways I didn't think possible. Be patient with this one, if it ends up anything like the first game, it'll be the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ bomb. Lol. (literally? hehe) I still remember when UEBS 1 added mod support and everyone went nuts with the mods, just take a look for yourselves if you never played the first game. Good game, good game indeed. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like the game. There isn't really a lot of content though, and that's understandable since the game is only in early access. However, I would like to make some suggestions which would make the game more fun.

1. Resize units in customization:
It would be really cool if there was a unit customization feature where you can adjust the size of any unit. You can make a
unit really big or really small. Looking at the mother of all hens made me think of the idea.

2. Workshop:
I know that the game will be compatible with the workshop later on, but they should definitely add it soon if they can.

3. Units from UEBS 1:
Some units from UEBS 1 aren't in UEBS 2. It would be cool if they added remade versions of UEBS 1 units into the game.\t\t\t\t\t
