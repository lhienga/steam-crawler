\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBack 4 Blood is a very good spin on the Left 4 Dead style game play, however, there are a few issues I'd like to address and you should know about before buying this game! (I've only played 7 hours at the time of review, but I still think these are important to know about.)

The Card System: In Back 4 Blood there is a random card system, you can build your own decks and also in the beginning of every individual level, there are corruption cards (benefits for the zombies). It works well and you can unlock and buy more cards but I absolutely hate that the game is so one sided. If you try to balance your deck out, you'll probably die. You need to buff the ♥♥♥♥ out of shotguns, or buff the ♥♥♥♥ out of melee or.. you get it. Buff something to really strong using these cards or else you're just going to get destroyed.

Weapon Upgrades: It was a disappointment to me when I heard that you can't upgrade your base guns and it was only in-game gun upgrades. Especially since the place where you equip gun skins is called "Armory", I thought you'd be able to upgrade them or something, anyways. The gun system works well but like I said, you can only upgrade the guns in game so if you were hoping for long term gun progression, sorry. Also there isn't a camo grind in this game like in games like COD. This is because like 70% of the camos are paid DLC anyways.

Anyway, those are my two big complaints. The game is still a ton of fun, super satisfying and gives the sort of old Left 4 Dead feel without giving the sort of old Left 4 Dead feel. (Am I making sense?) Thanks for reading.\t\t\t\t\t

The game offers more weapons and characters than the original Left 4 Dead/L4D2, which adds a lot of variety to the gameplay. While the cards may not be the most exciting feature, they do allow for different builds, which adds another layer of strategy to the game.

The graphics are nice, and the missions are well-designed, reminiscent of what Left 4 Dead offered us back in the day.

My only gripe is with the special zombie mutants; they don't quite give you that "oh ♥♥♥♥ tank" or "oh ♥♥♥♥ witch" vibe like in the original games.

Overall, though, this game is enjoyable and definitely worth playing.\t\t\t\t\t
