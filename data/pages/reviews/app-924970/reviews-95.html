Justified Negativity

I've been waiting for this game to go on a huge sale (%80) to buy due to negative reviews. Now that I bought and give it a shot I can easily say that I'm glad I waited for a huge sale. This game's only advantage over a decade old game (L4D2) is graphics obviously and nothing else. B4B's cons are:
  • The new card mechanic that B4B brought is just lame.
  • In-game store and weapon level system is really underwhelming.
  • Characters are so cringe and noisy. They just can't stop talking and their speech is a torture to listen.
  • Zombies pathing is just bad.
  • Mutated zombies are not even interesting.
  • Missions are not fun. Athmosphere is non-existent.
Devs claiming that they are the L4D2 devs is a gimmick to sell this garbage. They should have asked Valve's permission to remaster L4D2 instead, just like Black Mesa did with half-life. Because B4B devs messed up with game mechanics, characters and environment. They would have more respect and had a greater product than this.

Verdict: 4/10. Hard pass even with sale. L4D2 offers more and is a lot more fun to play even to this day.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a diehard Left4Dead fan, I didn't buy this game on release, but 2 years later. The reviews kind of deterred me away from it as I'm sure it did for other people, but it went on sale so I said screw it, I'm gonna get it. I'm REALLY happy I did. I even decided to get the annual pass as well after the first day because I can see myself playing this game a lot.

Back4Blood is packed full of action, it can get intense. I've been playing it with friends and my husband, and we've been enjoying the hell out of the game. Basically played it all night the first night. It feels somewhat familiar like Left4Dead, but it plays like a more polished and modern game. The FEEL is L4D as is the core premise, but the mechanics are a different game all together.

The class system is really fun to mess around with, as are the cards and cosmetics. You can modify weapons, modify perks. There's more depth to this game than just logging in, hopping into a match and just going. There's a feeling of progression and there's definitely a higher sense of achievement for completing missions.

The graphics are beautiful, and there are lots of things to admire about the game looks. The zombies are creepy and badass looking, the characters are awesome. I really like the custom weapon skins and changing up my characters appearance.

I like that there are hives you can jump into during a campaign. So it's not just the same old campaign over and over and just switching difficulties. You can choose to take on a bunch of difficult modifiers and hop into a hive. The feeling of communication and teamwork is really important in B4B too.

I will certainly get my monies worth out of this game. I really like where they went with it and it makes me sad it got the hate that it did. It's actually really fun and well done game. The only negative I have to say so far, is it would be awesome if there was mod support or at least player made campaigns, but maybe some day.\t\t\t\t\t
