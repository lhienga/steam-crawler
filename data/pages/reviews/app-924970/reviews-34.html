\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBack 4 Blood is an exceptional zombie shooting game that I have had the pleasure of playing, following in the footsteps of the renowned Left 4 Dead series. It sets itself apart by offering a completely different experience, boasting astonishing shooting mechanics and stunning graphics. The game truly shines when enjoyed with friends, allowing for the creation of unforgettable moments. However, the one downside to this otherwise impressive game is the absence of an option to select specific firearms. Instead, players must rely on the predetermined gun provided at the beginning, complete with fixed attachments. This lack of customization can lead to a sense of repetitiveness, as you are required to progress through the entire act with these unchangeable weapons. On the positive side, the addition of a shooting range is noteworthy, as it provides an avenue for practice with various guns and even allows players to transform into infected beings, adding an element of entertaining mischief when attacking co-op friends. Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed playing this game, although I have to admit that the lack of choice for users in terms of weapon and attachment selection is a noticeable drawback. I have not encountered any significant bugs or glitches during my gameplay. Although the game may feel somewhat short, it compensates for this with a wide range of diverse stages. If you have friends to join you, this game is highly recommended. I encourage you to give it a go!\t\t\t\t\t
