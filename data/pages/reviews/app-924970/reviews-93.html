\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay so I've had a couple hours to play this left 4 dead clone and regretfully I cannot recommend it. The 'tutoroal' section keeps talking about cards that you're supposed to create a deck in order to shoot zombies? I then was supposedly taught about how the cards are supposed to create randomness in each run-it happened in a supposed tutorial run where I got splatered with green muck and watched as I and my team bit the dirt. I decided to exit out of that instance and start another run, but then I started thinking there are other games I could play that are a lot easier to understand and do shooting zombies better. Left for Dead or World War Z.
So, I spent some more time with this game and change my not recommended. I just needed to spend some time with it to figure out how the cards fit into the scenario. I still think they could have done a better job of informing the noob on how the game is meant to be played.\t\t\t\t\t
