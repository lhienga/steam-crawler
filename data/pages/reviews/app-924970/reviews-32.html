\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere do I start? This game can be fun, but the more you play the more you realize why there are so many negative reviews. Alot of the campaign maps are recycled, and you'll be seeing the same levels over and over again, but with different side streets/paths. There are still bugs, like a special enemy jumping in front of me and falling off the map into oblivion, that made me laugh. The card system is annoying, and wants you to grind tirelessly to get good powerups. There are also 1 time use cards, which doesn't smell right, like they're gonna make it pay2win in the future. The playerbase is meh, people spend most of the time running to the finish line like they have to desperately go to the toilet. The last match one guy wanted us to 'wait for him' while he lingered in the saferoom. When we left, he started shooting at us. Friendly fire is quite a thing in this game, and even on normal its not difficult to kill your team mates. The enemies are also very spammy, and randomly spawn behind you, even after you've cleared the area/room.
