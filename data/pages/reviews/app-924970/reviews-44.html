\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst-off let me dispel the myth that Back 4 Blood should be compared to Left 4 Dead. Don't listen to nay-sayers with nostalgia vision and complaints about difficulty. I loved this game and I'm grateful I'm not friends with shallow idiots who would judge me just for playing.
With that out of the way, let me count the ways;

This game offers a fantastic campaign with a variety of shooting galleries and weapons in this apocalyptic shooter. The story is cheesy and modest, but the game play is the main focus. Some of the repetitive sounds are better turned off and ultimately squad communication is done in-game or through discord.

The card system is fun and engaging, and it was good before/after they decided to give players all their cards at once. I was playing since day-1 and I still enjoy playing with my friends and even random public games to check my reflexes in-game, as well as how my decks are holding up in the meta. My deck building theory guide has received a lot of visitors and genuine compliments as well, which just goes to show the player base is genuine and thoughtful.

As a heavily involved player, I did feel like my feedback was heard by development and was disappointed when they announced they were no longer going to support this game. I think this is inevitable for all games, but at least they did not kill-it intentionally. (Blizzard-Activision, I'm looking at you.)

I think the campaigns are pretty lengthy but you can save and quit mid-campaign to continue later if you like, which I think is a nice addition to this type of game. Sessions can be long-short depending on your mood. The guns have good action.

Melee and speed running is rather dull. I think they kept it in the game because they didn't want to upset players who preferred these tactics; to each their own. But they did balance player choices with the game they created, so in the end I was very pleased with the wisdom exercised by developers.

Overall, great game, will remain in my multiplayer rotation for a while. Made some great friends to play with too. Another great shooting gallery by Turtle Rock, underrated and sadly, underappreciated by many players!\t\t\t\t\t
