Survived a Wild 10-Hour Ride, Y'all!

'Sup fellow gamers!

Just got through a 10-hour sesh on Back4Blood and lemme spill the tea - the queue times are wack. Seriously, I spent half the time staring at the matchmaking screen. If you're into testing your patience, this game's gotchu.

Peep the stats - Left4Dead2's poppin' with 30K active peeps, while Back4Blood's struggling with just 2K. Not a good look - clearly this game ain't hittin' with the crowds.

Now the graphics, those are lit. But that's about all this game's got going for it. The zombies? Boring AF. Even the special ones disappoint. You'd expect them to bring some heat but nah, they as stale as the rest. And why's every level dark asf? Who tryna blast zombies you can barely see? Kinda whack if you ask me.

With the base game you get 4 chapters, 8 characters. DLCs add 2 more chapters and 4 extra chars. But trust, even with the extras this game gets old, quick.

Here's the tea - if you tryna mess around with your squad, you could cop Back4Blood for some laughs. Going solo? I'd skip it.

The Verdict

TLDR: Despite the glam graphics, Back4Blood flops where it matters most - gameplay. Hate to break it to y'all, but it's a bit of a letdown. Cop only if you keep your expectations in check, aight?
