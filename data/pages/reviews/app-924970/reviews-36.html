\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell... Okay... I have played both Left4Dead games quite extensively but not in a while. i have to say that in many ways Back4Blood does indeed capture the Left4Dead feel in a number of ways, now I have only played like 6.8 hours on Steam and some time on Xbox Series X I do prefer mouse and keyboard for this kind of game over controller simply because of the precision aiming that a mouse offers. I absolutely love the bow, so far the bow has made this game. I hope no patch nerfs the bow it's perfect just like it is. I don't know if they do more with it later in the game or not nut I would like to see more ammo types for it such as incendiary, acid, explosive you know some borderlands type stuff for the bow would be cool. I feel like Legolas in the Lord of the Rings movies fighting against zombie hoards instead of goblin and orc hoards the bow is really that good if you haven't tried it you must. Guns are cool too but I wish the muzzle blast sounds were a bit louder and more authoritative, especially the shotguns. I haven't really come across anything just yet that sparks the sheer terror of the witch in L4D just yet, there was something called a hag I think and I did die when I disturbed it so the hag needs more exploring.

I don't fully understand the card system just yet, it feels kinda like an unnecessary complication sure it gives some customization and when I get used to it I might like it more but part of the charm of the L4D series was the simplicity of it. I haven't gotten attached to any of the characters in B4B nearly as much as I got attached to the L4D characters.

Now the one thing that I do not like and I have heard others gripe about this as well, I absolutely do not like the limited continues. Frankly this almost kills the game. You make progress through 3 or so acts and everything is going well, then you get to the last one at least in the first part of the game that last act is a bit tricky at the very end of it getting out alive. I am trying to not drop any spoilers for anyone that hasn't been there yet. So far I have only had one continue when that continue is used I start back at the begging of that act just like in L4D when you failed an act. In Back4Blood however after that one continue is used you have to go all of the way back to the very begging of the episode, chapter or whatever it's called and do all of the acts over that you successfully completed already before you can get back to the very last act that you failed on. This need to change, they need to give you at least 3-5 continues (even in Pac-Man you got three lives right???) I can see people getting tired of replaying the same thing over and over again just for another shot at one area where they failed. I am telling you if one thing kills this game that will be the one thing that does it because it it doing a lot of stuff right. It has the potential to live up to the Left4Dead series just make the characters a little more memorable, give them more interesting dialogue, who can forget Bill and Francis from L4D always riding each other over something, the cut-scenes weren't all about the monsters but they developed the characters and gave them depth too. Ellis from L4Dead 2 was one of my favorites. SO I hope the devs are listening this issue of having to repeat whole chapters over is the biggie. I think it is the one thing that is most likely to make people get tired of this game and just stop playing it and that would be a shame because it's a great game game-play wise.Little polish on the audio for the weapons, give the Characters more interesting dialogue and fix that God awful issue with the limited continues. Why even have continues at all, just let the group start back on the act they failed on in proper Left4Dead style. Boring your players by making them repeat the same thing over and over again is a sure way to empty the room. Don't kill a good game over this tiny issue,\t\t\t\t\t
