\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a fan of Warhammer Vermintide 2, Warhammer Darktide, World War Z, Remnant from the Ashes and Gunfire Reborn. No offense here - game is not that complex on the battle side (like Warhammer games but i think it fits the gameplay that it wants to deliver)

Bought the game for 20 bucks on sale (including all DLC), because the reviews actually did go up into mostly positive.

Doing Act 1 took me around 3-4 hours on Veteran and was fun so depends on the DLC how much time you get out of it. I like the Deck-Building-system because from my point of view it gives you some options you don't have before you do a run in the games mentioned above (except for Remnant).

I didn't crash so far and playerbase seems to be healthy on PC and consoles - got into a match in under 1 minute. I can't see why people hate it because it is not L4D 3 but honestly it feels like it - purely from the PVE perspective so far.

After playing all the mentioned games i can say that i like B4B so far.

Go watch some recent playthroughs - maybe you get hooked.

Don't know about the "Devs abandoned" topic that i read over and over but for me and my 20 bucks it is enough content for now.\t\t\t\t\t
