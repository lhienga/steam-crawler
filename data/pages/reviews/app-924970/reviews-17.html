\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLong story short, I enjoyed the game during the beta, but felt it lacked a true fun factor that Left 4 Dead provided. Compound that with all the anti-solo functions they had at launch, no progress, no achievements, no offline, etc. I decided to wait to buy it and let it cook.

I'm happy to report that this game is now OUTSTANDING. Not only does it have the fun factor of Left 4 Dead, but it also 100% solo friendly. So far, I've only played offline solo with bots and have had an absolute blast. The card system, which is basically just a skill tree system, offers so much variety and play-styles that you'll be having plenty of fun just trying to tweak to find that perfect build.

There's no micro-transactions, no battle-passes and no in-game store currency bs. Literally, everything you can buy between cards, skins and other unlocks is all earned FROM PLAYING THE GAME. You really can't ask for much more, the game really is fun as hell and rewarding too.

All in all, if you're a horde shooter fan, that loves that loot system style of Borderlands, give this game a purchase. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
