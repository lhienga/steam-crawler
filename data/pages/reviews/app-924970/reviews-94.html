No, in fact I would probably be disappointed with spending what the game is worth when it is not on discount. For what it's worth I think it is a good pickup during this sale. The gun play is alright, but this is nothing like LFD/LFD2. Do not get me wrong, when you take into consideration as a stand-alone game it is good. But this is supposed to be LFD's successor. With that being said, the mutated do not feel that rewarding to kill in contrast to the originals. With that being said, pick it up, its worth it for its price, you can 100% get your money back with the time you will spend playing this game (with the discount) It's not the best, but it's just something you can switch your mind off and play for a while on autopilot without thinking too much.
