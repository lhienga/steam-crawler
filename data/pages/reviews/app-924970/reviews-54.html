\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI haven't finished the game yet, but I think I can safely say that the game is so much fun to play. In my opinion, it's not meant to replace Left 4 Dead, but to stand beside it. Left 4 Dead will always be the classic, iconic, and simple Co-Op shooter. Meanwhile, this game has deeper mechanics, because of the cards, variety of weapons, accessories, and Character Perks and Team Effects. Now, don't get me wrong, the game is not perfect, there are some things that I think could be fixed. If you played Left 4 Dead, you know how all Special Infected have their disctinct sound effects, like the Witch's crying, and the Tank's growl, along with their own music as well. But here, the Special Ridden does have their own sound effects, I believe, but it's not as distinct. If you want to be aware of them, you might as well just look for them than try to hear which of them is coming. And then there's also a debuff that I encounterd quite frequently when I enter the Ridden Hives, which slows down movement A LOT. To me, that debuff just sucks the fun out of the game when you do encounter it, I know other games does implement a similar mechanic, but it just sucks so bad, I wish that could be omitted. Overall though, if you're a fan of Left 4 Dead and shooters in general, I absolutely recommend this. I got this on sale, and quite frankly, if I had the money, I would buy this retail price and not regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
