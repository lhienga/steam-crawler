The Ai is wonderful, it literally does everything within its power to annihilate you. Even if it means it has to cheat. This is what made the game a lot of fun. But overtime, it gets predictable and with the right build/knowledge you can fly through no hope act 1 literally punching all zombies/muties, aside maybe the ogre, into oblivion. Which is still fun, just in a different way.

So many small stupid things helped bury this game. Hype/clout, aim assist issues (just turn it off, trust me), double mutie spawns, ghost rounds, god like melee, ammo eco, the most negative press you can imagine, and ultimately selling. A difficult game turned into an unfair game to most and it went downhill from there. It really seemed like, no matter how accessible they made it, people just ♥♥♥♥ all over it because it was the thing to do that year. Which I get, because the game is still hard as ♥♥♥♥ sometimes. Especially if you don't know how to stack damage cards, boost trauma healing, and copper eco. Which isn't described in game from what I can tell and is only really ever explained on Reddit by other players.

Ultimately, and in a literal sense, this game was left for dead...lol
But its still fun AF.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's pretty apparent that the developer's can't really make a well-rounded game even after only a couple hours of play. And by the time you've completed the campaign, you're left with a feeling of, "is that it?" because the only thing this game has on offer is decent gunplay. The characters suck, and repeatedly fail at making witty remarks. The campaign levels are incredibly one-note with no atmosphere whatsoever; so much so that I can barely recall a single level. And the card system is pretty unwelcome in this kind of game. I want my shooters to be a test of my skill at shooting, not deck-building, and that's coming from someone who loves deck-building games more than just about any other kind of game. But without the deck-building, then the game would have to rely on a good gameplay foundation, which this game does not have. The zombies are just not that fun to kill, and the special zombies are so uninteresting it's painful. Charging $60 for a game this unrefined should be criminal.\t\t\t\t\t
