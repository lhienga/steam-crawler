\r\n\t\t\t\t\tB4B is L4D evolved, with more options. I feel like it is an improvement from L4D2.

I loved L4D, played thousands of hours, bought it on multiple platforms, later on when L4D2 came out I was disappointed it was basically the exact same thing, I really didn't appreciate the new zombies. Now, B4B is very similar but clearly not the same. A few things I notice is that B4B is more chaotic, and the selection of ridden is pretty cool, I really appreciate the cards system, it allows for some more in depth customisation, and provides something L4D didn't have, L4D was very basic for customising and did just fine.

I don't care for battle pass ideas, so I am just not going to participate in that unless it drops to 10% value in the Steam Store. I am excited to check out the DLC next time it is on sale, to see if it really adds to the game.

The thing is, that the custom kit does add something to the game, but not all players are going to care about the feature, everyone likes games for different reasons.

A few things that would really make a game like this really pop and seem fun:

-It would be nice if there were videos for each of the characters, like some sort of intro video, this will help players actually care story wise, I know it features bonus stats related to the characters, but it really doesn't allow much room to care for the story.

-If there were vehicles you could drive, like on some large open field scenario, allot of people would like to shoot from the windows, I notice the Barge scenario in game, it could be like that, but allowing people to pilot the direction would be fun, maybe even a boat for example. Remember when WOW started making more dynamic quests in Cataclysm, it really helped the game.

-Another thing that may be interesting is something like having a pet, the pet's mechanics would probably be item fetching, maybe some places on maps with dig spots, or a raven that flies over head and gives you a zombie-revealing aura/glow on Ridden.

-A Trophy cabinet, people love gloating/collecting, and if there were a tent in the town to display everything some people would enjoy, maybe something cute like a binder sticker-book thing that features your achievements, titles, emblems... etc

The game is fun even without my suggestions, 9/10 score, 10/10 would be achievable with better emphasis on the story.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't get why this game got so much hate. Do not go into this game thinking it's "LeFt 4 DeAd 3". And just because it isn't like its supposed predecessor does not mean its a bad title. It's just hard to make people happy nowadays for whatever reason.

Anywho, this game is a great product. The graphics and physics are nice. It's very satisfying for me to sink a round of buckshot into a zombie torso and watch it do a back flip. There are lots of weapons to choose from and modify. The melee combat isn't as satisfying as vermintide but thats ok, i see plenty of good melee players in this game and I've noticed having a good teammate with a good melee build helps. (especially in harder difficulties).

The maps are nice and explorable for the most part so you can search for extra loot or cards to give you extra perks. I like this because it adds a good risk vs reward factor on the harder difficulties. Some maps are more open than others. Exploring a hive (DLC) is tense and frightening--hives are high risk high reward where you can find some pretty nice loot as well but your chances of dying are increased tremendously if you have a team that doesn't work together.

There are a lot of special monsters with different mutation variants to add to the variety.

Corruption cards (enemy and level mutations) ensure that no play through is ever the same.

Your team can suffer grave injuries which effect your stats and can force you to change your tactics or make the game increasingly difficult which really encourages you and your team to work together and communicate; especially on harder difficulties.

All in all, I think this game is a great product and doesn't deserve all the poor reviews. There is a lot to like about this game.
