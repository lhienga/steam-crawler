\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBuy it only on sale. A pretty okay zombie shooter game, and that's it.

The magic that makes L4D fun and replayable, is just not in this game. Campaigns are forgettable, once you've beaten a campaign mission, that's it. There is just no urge to revisit the mission again and again.

Special infected/bosses are generic boring big, bigger, and super big tough monster coming at you from a distance and will just smash & destroy your whole team easily.


Compared to L4D, there is no sense of dread like that of a Hunter that is gonna pounce on you when you least expect, or a Charger gonna charge at you around a corner, or a Smoker gonna drag you away when you are too far behind. There is no moment of short silence & peace when you successfully cleared an oncoming horde, impressed that you and your teammates are still alive.

B4B feels like it just throws waves and waves of oncoming infected and bosses at you until you made it to the saferoom.

Not fond of the card system or RPG elements. The weapons sound like peashooters, and do not project the sense of power against the infected/Ridden.

It's ok to play B4B once in a while, but my go-to coop zombie shooter is still L4D.\t\t\t\t\t
