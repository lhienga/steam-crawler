\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, its not Left 4 Dead. Going back and playing L4D 2, Back 4 Blood is a better experience, adding in a bunch of quality of life changes (such as not losing all of your ammo when reloading). What is missing from L4D is the personality of the characters, but it more than makes up for this in how much better the core gameplay feels. Whereas the shining points of L4D are its quirky characters, variety of mutations, and overall storyline, I think what really shines in B4B are the different builds that you can make in the game along with the level of challenge that it offers. In addition, it offers a fresh experience from L4D's core gameplay that feels like a party from an RPG (with various tanky, support, or damage focused builds) rather than simply playing through a campaign.

If you can pick up this game for 35-40$ then its a pretty good game. It would be worth the full price if the community was more alive, but playing the game with even just one other friend is worth it. After the recent updates, the enemy mutations are pretty good and add a lot of the variety that was missing on launch. The game seems to be really stable at this point. It just needs a community revival.

The character perks/cards are pretty neat, as they let you spec into whatever kind of build you want. Want to throw like 10 grenades and take down bosses in no time? There's a build for that. Want to zoom around the map like Tracer and never have to stock up on ammo? There's a build for that, too. Melee? Sniping? AOE Team heals just by popping some pills? Those too.

At this point, all the game needs is to revive the community with a major sale. If this happens, then I think it would be a blast to play more often. Just need to have more friends who own the game.\t\t\t\t\t
