Back 4 blood DID NOT live up to it's predecessors

It's a hard game to like, even with multiple replays of it's stories it has to offer. The story and the characters behind it are bland and boring, making it hard to keep interested in Back 4 Blood's game play. It's not worth a single drop of money even if you got it on sale.

Here is a list of the pros and cons to this game made by my wife and I..

Pros:
  • Very detailed graphics.
  • Evangelo (Best boy).
  • Gun upgrades were cool but could be overhauled.
  • Gore was okay.
  • Deck building allowed specialised classes to be made.
  • The idea of the game was good but executed poorly.


Now the cons...
  • Animations made the game feel very clunky at times. Notably when you get stunned.
  • Movement feels very "floaty".
  • There was a massive jump in difficulty between different difficulties (weirdly worded).
  • Characters felt plain and boring, not much about the relationship between the characters.
  • Characters NEVER interacted with eachother.
  • Like the characters, the story also felt really plain. There wasn't much life in the story.
  • Most of the special ridden felt like they copy and pasted making gameplay feel way too bloated. I felt confused throughout most my gameplay as it was very overwhelming.
  • The ridden hives were really repetitive, there wasn't any other objective to going in them unless it was for skull totems.
  • WHY IS THE UI SO HUGE!

I personally wouldn't recommend anyone buying this unless you're a masochist.\t\t\t\t\t
