But it's most fun to just play around with builds until you find the one that fits your play style the best.

Anyways, L4D/2 were some of my favorite games and as you can see from other reviews, some people loved B4B and other people hated it. I loved it after I got the hang of it and clocked in over 500 hours with the game. But by the time I hit 400 hours it was really losing its luster. I think what made the game so good was the community of devoted players. Which checking the reddit for the game looks like it has an average of about 1k players.

If you get all the DLC it really expands on the story and it's a blast to play from beginning to end. And once you have all the cards unlocked you'll find yourself doing multiple play throughs as you try different builds, cleaners and weapons. It's even better (again) with friends as you can further customize things by having synergy between cleaners/cards.

Guns feel good, sound is great, bosses and scenarios are loads of fun and there's just so much depth to the whole thing.

The biggest thing to know is the developers have abandoned the game, I think they confirmed this at the beginning of 2023. Which is a shame I think there was so much left to do with this game, there are still bugs and balance that should be addressed as well.

Bottom line, get this game when it goes on sale, it's a ton of fun and it is WAY more fun if you have a group to play with especially if you are all into it.\t\t\t\t\t
