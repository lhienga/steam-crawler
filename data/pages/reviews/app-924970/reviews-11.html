\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has one of the most replayability values I think I've seen ever. The "content cycle" (I guess?) is extremely replayable and hella fun. However, there are some things I have issues with.

Pros:
Game is really fun the first time you play it, especially with friends
There is a lot of replayability, with each game being different due to cards, nightmare cards or whatever they're called, the different difficulties, the money system. etc.
The game was definitely made with love, there are a lot of little details and great sound design throughout the entirety of the game.
It is visually amazing, especially for it's time, and not hard to run. (I can run with with a 3060 and so can my friend with a 1650)
The character system is fun to change each game and how it plays.
There are great cosmetics in the game, and ZERO MICROTRANSACTIONS!
There are multiple different modes, whether it be PvP or PvE for you to experiment with.
The training range is very fun with ranges to mess around in, and I've spent over 10 hours combined with the beta and now playing there with friends.

Cons:
The game is definitely not worth $60, I got it on sale for $6 and the DLCS for $4, so that's why I'm more happy with the game compared to others.
There is not enough content, and the devs game up on the game too early due to the hate from the game not being L4D3. (which is quite a shame, I could see this game being in my top 5 if it got more content.)
All of the DLCs suck. After buying them, I cannot see much change in the game besides the new characters & the warped chests. There is no new acts added or even maps, just little coves and gambling + overpowered characters. Of course, I got these with $4, so I'm still happy, but they're normally $15+.
There aren't enough weapons in the game.

Overall, still a great game. This game has sparked a feeling of fun in me that I haven't gotten since I was a kid. Don't get me wrong, this game is no where near worth $60, or $45, or even $30, but it's definitely worth $6. I am so in love with it, and can see myself booting it up in the future whenever I'm bored to just have some mindless fun. (Kinda like older Call of Duty games with good zombies?)

8.75/10. Do NOT get this game for MSRP or without a sale. It is NOT worth it otherwise.\t\t\t\t\t
