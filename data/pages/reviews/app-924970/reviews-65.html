\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the time of this review, the game is completed by the developers and there will be no further content added.
The worst thing you can do is listen to haters. There has been a hate campaign against this game from L4D2 veterans since the beta released, with the game getting review bombed for not being a reskin of L4D2, or for having certain issues with difficulty, bugs and issues with bots.
The issues with difficulty have been addressed, and all difficulties aside from the top on, are enjoyable and can be casually played. For those who enjoy a good challenge, the top difficulty is here to provide it.
The bots are good enough to allow for this game to be played offline in singleplayer mode.
If you are coming from L4D2, know that this game is more complicated, and has a steeper learning curve. You will not be able to singlehandedly dominate the game, you will need your teamates (or the bots) and teamwork to succeed. This game has 12 playable characters, each with their own passive abilities, but due to this, voice lines between the characters had to be simplified. The game is a bit less detailed than L4D2, but has interesting new mechanics to compensate for it.
Overall a very fun experience.\t\t\t\t\t
