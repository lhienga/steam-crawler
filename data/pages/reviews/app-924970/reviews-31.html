I will get right to the PROS and CONS...

PROS

1. Ok Graphics, but just ok
2. Expected Music, so ok music
3. A decent shooter, but not permanent looter.
4. Zombies are cool, but they come from out of no where 100% of the time, so there is little to know method to the madness you encounter, unless you are in a building.
5. Weapons are rather believable and sounds are ok.

CONS

1. Ok Graphics, but just ok
2. Expected Music, so ok music
3. A decent shooter, but not permanent looter.
4. Zombies are cool, but they come from out of no where 100% of the time, so there is little to know method to the madness you encounter, unless you are in a building.
5. Weapons are rather believable and sounds are ok.
6. Progression does not match the increased difficulty when it comes to gear. Difficulty will always make you feel like your weapons are pointless after the first couple difficulties... Even with the best cards!
7. You cannot start with the character you chose until the end of the current match and just assume whatever BOT character is unused (VERY LAZY DEVS)
8. Load outs are a complete joke! You do not change to your setup once you are able to switch to your preferred character from the "supposed" load out you setup. So do not expect to switch to your favorite weapon type as per the character you setup with skins and weapons.
9. No personalization. As stated above your LOAD OUTS are completely useless! Only thing you get is the Custom Skin and Cards! Only way to satisfy your load out is to find them in this RNG world...BAH!
10. No wander or AFKing penalty and Kicking feature does not work! Players are so bad at this game and literally wander miles apart. I asked people I play with their age when I hear a little kid and I come to find out almost every grouping, if not every other grouping there is a 10-12 year old playing this game! Also I encountered an 8 year old, yes an 8 year old hanging out with a group of adults...cringe worthy in the least! There needs to be proximity penalty because 80% of the player base AFK or just never stay with the group. They can get kicked and just rejoin the game they were just kicked from! Lets call it the "BABYSITTER-TETHER"! If players are a certain distance from group, have the game auto-kick with like a 10 second timer to boot and while that far away will also receive -50% all loots/currency/exp. (LAZY DEVS once again!)

Not even older versions of them to make this iconic and believable while attempting to be a continuation! They have similar characters but Ellis is now a Female named: Holly. One weird character is named: Mom who you can turn into her teenage possibly low 20s self which really creeps you out cuz the character selected portrait does not change to the new skin....ummm yeah your character skin does not match the portrait bottom part of the UI! (LAZY DEVS AGAIN!)
12. Matchmaking is a complete joke! Anyone at anytime can play any difficulty they want and do not need to beat previous difficulties or acts to progress. Thus first day and first match in veteran and dying endlessly because they didn't have to earn up to that difficulty at all!
13. You restart all over again with weapons and possible weapon upgrades every time you play. So it is just like Perma-Death! You keep nothing that could help you do later difficulties. Good cards are not nearly enough! This game needs to allow you to make custom weapons with upgrades that improve over time as you progress to even start to attempt to keep up with this crazy difficulty that this game offers! So referring back to #8 and #9 that we need custom load outs that actually switch your character to at the start of new level. Just like in Call of Duty MW I/II/III. Otherwise 90% of the player base will never have a chance beyond recruit or maybe, just maybe veteran...


CONLUSION:

Notice that the PROS are also the CONS #1-5 ....this is because they are both ok and very bad at the same time since BACK4BLOOD is supposed to be the better and newly updated next step in the Left4Dead series and it pales in comparison to the previous versions! Its all just ok or worse; which as a next in the series failure just makes it disappointing most of the time.

This is a playable game and you can have fun like 20-30% of the time, but the next LEFT4DEAD? I would say no and this is coming from a long time LEFT4DEAD player that still plays L4D2. So sad as it is, I cannot recommend. The game feels like there was no love and passion put into it and just a another game that is shelf worthy after one run through.

Hopefully in future DLC, they will have way more powerful CARDS to match the impossible later difficulties.
