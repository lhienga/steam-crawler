\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI thought this game gonna be so boring, but it turned out really great.
I had fun playing with my matey, grinding in the hive and finding duffel bags together.
Blaming each other on a small stuff and suffering to finish the game on the highest difficulty. It was such a great moment.

The card decks are confusing at first but I somehow able to learn about it.
There a lot of play-styles to try. You might want to mess out and mix your cards to your liking because it does really matter in the game.

The zombies are good, the higher the difficulty the tougher they can get, especially some special zombies are so annoying that it could easily wipe a whole team on a single bash in No-Hope difficulty.

The story is somewhat confusing as the Left 4 Dead series. But it seems makes sense if you play the DLCs, as you would able to see the other aspect on the story to make it more better (story and gameplay wise) and yup there's a good stuff too.
The characters are good, they have their own classes. For example, if you want to play as a support/medic, you may have to choose the Doc <3 or if you're kind of melee enjoyer you may choose Holly and whatsoever, you'll see.

The performance runs well even if you're playing on a lower end desktops, and it seems well optimized as I never experienced a noticeable FPS stutters and drops.
The graphics are decent for a modern game. I like the shading and the lighting is well immersed.\t\t\t\t\t
