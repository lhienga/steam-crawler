You can increase the intensity and excitement by ramping up the difficulty when playing with friends but this also is B4B' major flaw. I say 'flaw' because if you play this game using the bots and no actual players you are almost forced to play the game on a lower difficulty as the game becomes very hard and zombies harder to kill. just for an example i even struggled when i first got the game just to play on 'veteran' mode (2/4 difficulty) with the bots as they just aren't intelligent enough with teamwork and the use of the card system which act as perks. Despite that if you do have a squad to play with and know what they're doing then the game is a lot more do-able on hard levels and the game is still fun on lower difficulty. Ultimately, i would recommend B4B if you are a fan of the L4D franchise as it still has those redeemable qualities and similarities to the Valve franchise aswell as the game being on discounts often for as little as 8 pounds/dollars.\t\t\t\t\t
