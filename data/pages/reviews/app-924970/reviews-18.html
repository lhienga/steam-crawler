- 希望提供更简单的难度或选项，比如关闭友伤

**Positive Aspects:**
+ Excellent performance, smooth visuals
+ Gripping combat, top-tier gunplay
+ More variety of zombies compared to similar games
+ Some consideration for replayability
+ AI is decent, at least it doesn’t make mistakes

**Negative Aspects:**
- Wishes for simpler difficulty options, like turning off friendly fire
- Collecting or finding elements in levels is frustratingly difficult, such as searching for keycards, batteries
- Desires more diverse game modes, neighboring COD’s zombie gameplay is rich
- Wishes for more rogue elements, limited levels become monotonous over time
- Main storyline is relatively short, including leeway, 9h to complete the first playthrough is sufficient
- Updates have ceased

**Summary:**
Although lacking in content and not very large in scale, it is a qualified AAA game, recommended for fans of zombies and cooperative gameplay. (7.5/10)
