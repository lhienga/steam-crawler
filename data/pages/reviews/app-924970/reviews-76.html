\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA promising title that sought to overthrow the iconic Left4Dead. It unfortunately failed, in spite of some interesting decisions such as implementing the cards mechanic allowing players to create builds before loading into a map which showed some promise, but it was quickly overshadowed by the fact that many cards were almost never used, some were too overpowered and the developers failed to cater to their game's playerbase needs. In fact, once they felt dissatisfied with the how many people were buying and actively playing the game, they withdrew from the title completely, leaving their fans hanging and disillusioned. This approach can hardly be recommendable. They barely even tried working with their own product. They pushed a few lukeworm DLCs, promised continuous development and scrapped that idea completely out of the sudden.

In terms of the gameplay, it is definitely a step in a right direction in comparison to the clunky Left4dead controls. There are many weapons which have a very nice feeling when you use them, no matter if its a gun or a melee weapon. However, the iconic, almost comedic vibe to the game, which was prominent in Left4dead, is completely absent here and the main characters try very hard but appear bland and uninteresting in spite of the banter they resort to basically every 10 seconds of the game. Graphics are definitely good and the soundtrack is fine, too. The progression is at best mediocre and the rewards you get for playing are acceptable but that's that. The approach of cutting a generally big mission/act into small missions strip the maps you encounter off their atmosphere as these missions are so short you barely even care about their layout and it also makes it so that some maps are nice and some are very boring and uninspired. In terms of the difficulty- well I'd say without a good mastery in the card's system and relevant builds, the game may prove challenging in anything above the recruit level. There are many special infected zombies in here and in general I'd say they are nicely varied although the fact that you need to hit their weak points instead of just shooting them can be frustrating, especially at higher difficulties where the game is definitely not too forgiving, making it possibly problematic for people who exhibit a rather casual gamestyle.

All in all, a game with potential, a potential wasted, definitely not worth buying for the fact that it is quite expensive. Buy it at best when the price is slashed or stick to Left4dead, this game is hardly a must-play.\t\t\t\t\t
