Overall, I would recommend this game. You can become a pumpkin.
What I love about this game is that you can tell the people involved in making it were having a good time.
The art style is quite lovely and I liked the story as well. There are multiple outcomes (plus some silly cute achievements) so replayability is certain.
I even laughed at some of the puns! But honestly, I could not stand the constantly repeating "au revoir, lil Reaper" to the point that I would mute the sound or avoid the bartender altogether.
Most importantly, reading through files didn't feel boring to me. They are unique enough and some contain fun references. Buying a lamp makes for quicker judging in New game + (although you will still encounter new files).
I am a bit disappointed that the various characters you meet at the bar don't really play any role in the story (as far as I'm aware after taking 3 different paths). There is this big building full of rooms and you only get to enter a few. The lore is cool but it served no purpose at all.
Lovely detail: the title screen changes with some choices.
Lastly, the credit scene is awesome!
