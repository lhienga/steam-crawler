\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe concept, visuals, and music of this game is great. The gameplay is not difficult but annoying. After an hour of playing, the player does not really know much more about the world they are playing in, and there is not much of a point of talking to other people or buying widgets besides for decoration or for exploration for the sake of exploration. The main gameplay, choosing who lives and dies, became really tedious. The amount of clicks I have to do to make room, click on papers, and click the pen while doing it for multiple papers is really tedious. It would not be that big of an issue, but you get handed lots of papers at once. The decorations are more of a hindrance because they take up space on your desk you want, and your bedroom is feels pointless if all I do there is reset the gameplay loop. I am not exactly a reader, but there is also a lot of reading in this game. I was not under the impression that the game had aspects of a visual novel, but I think the reading got really annoying. It feels annoying to read so much dialogue or pay attention to small details when they do not have immediate effect or hint at future effects that affect gameplay. Going up and down the elevator is also very annoying to do multiple times in a row.\t\t\t\t\t
