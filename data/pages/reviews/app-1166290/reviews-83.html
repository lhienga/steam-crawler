Whoever had the pirate skeleton idea in the thrift shop, let me be honest and say it was bad, it made no difference, but delayed the gameplay.

The script was mediocre, boring, unfunny. In the beginning I was paying full attention to whatever it was shown, however later I've just realized it made no difference if I just randomly clicked at anything just to skip the dialogue. It surprised me how this minimalist game had voice actors for every conversation. Maybe must've spent half of budget on it? It is a plus, makes the gameplay more refined indeed, although most of conversation speed are waaaay to sloooow, like we already have subtitles on... why? let me suggest skip or 2x speed option during dialogues.

Furthermore, who made that box moving upward and downward? If you consider that an elevator, that's not how it is supposed to work, it was a lackluster job on design and program part. Just ride one in real life and just copy it? That does not sound hard, or does it?

If those sort of issues does not affect you after all, just let me say that this game is repetitive, very repetitive, at least it won't go further than 2 hours of repetition if you praise for your sanity.

I had a few more rants about this game in my mind, but I guess this is enough.

Glad I'm bought this on sale, at least I was not ripped off 100% on the price.\t\t\t\t\t
