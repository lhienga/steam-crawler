Along the way, you can also find items that help you increase your awareness of the situation of your workplace, your relation to the world, and Fate.

In other words this game is a battle for knowledge, you need to do a good job, but you also need find out for yourself what a good job is, who is telling you the right things, and what path you must take.

By the time I put an hour into this game, I was already invested, my mind was running around between figuring out what was going on in the office, and figuring out who should live/die. There was not a single moment where my mind wasn't running a mile a minute.

Finally, while I don't want to spoil anything, this game has a gimmick very similar to that of "Flowey" from undertale. (you can't lost unless you give up or intentionally try to)

Simply put, despite this games fairly high price, it is VERY much worth getting.\t\t\t\t\t
