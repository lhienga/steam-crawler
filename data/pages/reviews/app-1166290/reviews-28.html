\r\n\t\t\t\t\t⭐⭐⭐/5 - I liked it

I was looking for some Estonian video games and stumbled upon Death and Taxes. I recommend this game to anyone who enjoys slow-paced storytelling, quest-like gameplay with loads of text, and open-ended conclusions. In the game, you take on the role of a grim reaper, deciding who lives and dies based on brief information. Each decision you make ripple effect on the world and the story.
I thoroughly enjoyed the game's art style, sound effects, voice-acting and mystical atmosphere. I even played through it twice to experience different outcomes. I believe there are things I didn't discover, and I would be happy to play this game later, reconsidering my choices. Considering the team size that has been working on the game, it's terrific. However, I sometimes found the gameplay monotonous, and the ending left me wanting more.

Despite these minor flaws, I still think Death and Taxes is an excellent game I would happily recommend and play again.\t\t\t\t\t
