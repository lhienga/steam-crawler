I disliked the lack of player freedom in a game supposedly about choice, and lack of real consequences in a game supposedly about consequences. I disliked the incredibly repetitive gameplay without any sense of payoff. I disliked the writing, which felt like teenage fanfiction level. I disliked the overt political opinions purported as fact. I disliked the slander of Francis ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. I disliked the music, which at first seems good but on the 333rd repeat in 10 minutes gets a bit grating.
