\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed this game very much! Charming characters with wonderful voicework, a cool soundtrack, neat character customization, and a lot of replayability if you're the type who likes trying to go for a bunch of endings. I also really enjoy the little threads of ongoing narratives apart from the overarching plot. Actually a bit longer than I had expected it to be for my initial run, although you may find it quite short if you move at a fairly vigorous pace (you'll probably be going much faster as well once you start going for other endings).

The gameplay is really simple, and unlike Papers Please where you also need to find discrepancies, this one is really more about the choices you make. It requires quite a bit of reading, unless you want to play without doing much of that. I had a lot of fun playing with friends and debating over certain choices or dialogue options though. I bought this game while it was on sale, and am very pleased with the purchase. Overall, a lot of fun, and with a lovely style!

Undoubtedly, the best character is a cat, which is really the only reason I made this review.\t\t\t\t\t
