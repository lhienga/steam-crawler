The Aspect Of The Game
You are a grim reaper that has been "crafted" by Fate himself and given the task of saving and taking lives. Not through the traditional way of donning a black cloak and scythe but through checking boxes in an office that seals there fate.

My Two Cent Review
This game was a fun little story "paper checking" game if you can call it that. Every day you are given the option to save or kill things and you can either follow the quota you are given or go rogue and do what you want. Based on who you save or who your kill will change the fate of the world so make sure you read your news bulletin on your death phone everyday to see the effects. After you are finished for the day you have to meet your boss to go over how you did and they will give you pat in the back or tell you the straighten up and fly right. After that you can go to the store and buy trinkets which some of them help you and some of them being purely cosmetic item's. The game in general is alright it's nothing fancy and it isn't the best at keeping you hooked in the game. After the first play through I was done with the game I really didn't want to spend another hour and a half plaything through the game checking boxes again.

The Value Of The Game
It's an okay game to play but to be honest I wouldn't really buy this unless it was on sale.

Final Score 68/100 Ranking D+ Tier
