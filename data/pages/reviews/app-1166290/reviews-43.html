It's been funny, but that's pretty much it, funny. It's good for a quick casual sesh for a laugh- the kind of black office humor you'd get out of a Tom Holt novel or something if it were written for a younger crowd. I'm pleasantly surprised by it being fully voiced, and pretty well at that. BUT. The side conversations with people you can have are unfortunately dull and too long for a game that's geared towards making you chuckle. Script could have used another editing pass, because I think the chatty parts are what convinced me to put it down unfinished.\t\t\t\t\t
