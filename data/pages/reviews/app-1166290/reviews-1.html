8.5/10 Buy it if you like some job simulator game or some game that you can sit back and enjoy deciding what will happen next. if you are interested in this game concept and theme DEATH and TAXES worth it to try at least for 1st run.

Good things about this game are concept and art style. Story has an interesting perspective about death and world balance. A lot of gimmicks and choices to decide during your day until one point of the game. You will get bored so soon the more you play the more your game will be boring.(after 1st run) I found that I read most of the things carefully and decided everything as well as I could when I just started the game. after a while, I just get bored and "KILL" whoever's required for my ending and task. I guess this is how DEV wants us to feel. Being Spawn and deciding people's life tho documents is absolutely a boring "JOB SIMULATOR". but with this concept I guess this is what FATE and GRIM would feel during their job.

Pro and Con
+I love concept and art.
+A ton of ending.
+You will decide the death of something else not just HOOMAN.
+A lot of unexpected effects tho choice from day to day. (In a good way)
+A lot of possibility of 'Ending' with your choices.
-Boring repetitive after 1st run.
-The more you play, The more how boring the game is. (after a few runs)\t\t\t\t\t

This game has replay value and for the first time I might do a second playthrough of a game to get a different outcome. My first playthrough was follow the rules type of playthrough. Now I will try rebel playthrough whenever I get time. I had a nice time playing this game but if the game wanted me to have feelings about the choices I made, then I was not that engaged to feel the consequences of whatever decisions I made. When I got the ending it was like "Alright the game is done." The game did not manage to make me feel attached to the choices and outcomes. The conversations with characters are fine in solitude but had no impact on the gameplay, at least for me. The voice acting and music is decent. The game mechanic and gameplay feels repetitive after sometime, at least it did on my follow the rules playthrough. A different type of game but not engaging enough in my opinion. All the best to the devs for future endeavors.\t\t\t\t\t
