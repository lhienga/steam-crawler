\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeing a game that I had bought on a whim having heard nothing about I was pleasantly surprised to enjoy a very interesting story. The game could possibly go down different routes but I'm not too sure as I have only played through one game. The marking can get monotonous depending on how you see it and hence, the game should be played for the story and not gameplay. A nice collectable character customisation and desk item element is also a part of the game that is - whilst pointless - quite a nice aspect that I found myself wanting to spend my coins for, and it isn't too hard to save up that cash as long as you aren't going around buying a ton of pointless stuff. The design is really nice, I'm a big fan of this particular art style. Even better still, every character is fully voiced, which I love. Overall it's a nice game that I have enjoyed playing thus far, a true testament to the potential of a simple indie game.\t\t\t\t\t
