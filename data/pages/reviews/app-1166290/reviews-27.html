\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game if you are a completionist and love really odd and specific achievements. I don't however getting this game at full price, the sales were generous, especially this years Summer Sale, which is where I bought it.

Its a charming game, i'll give it that! Good artstyle, stories decent for the concept and the voice acting and characters are charming! [Fate my Beloved]. Its just that it feels kind of depressing, I get its the concept but maaann. There's a mirror that talks to you and its kind of, ewey? Tries to kind of demotivate you and make you rebellious, I get why, to get different endings.

SPOILER:
But I wanted to be a good reaper, do my job as I was told, follow the rules! I did. But this gave me probably the worst ending possible, seriously? That's pretty demotivating. I was expecting perhaps a more, neutral ending. But this...? Wow. Okay. You just end up killing the ENTIRE world by following the rules, and oh wow! Fate was plotting it all along. Don't get me wrong, i'd be tired out of my mind after aeons of killing and keeping humans alive, who wouldn't? But ending it for every other being in the office because the department is no longer needed, is kind of a yikes for me.
_______________________________________________________________________
I'm definitely going to do another run or two to try and get other endings. But in general, for a game I completed my first run in just under five hours.

6.5/10 - Could be longer, definitely love the art, voice acting and general concept. It just feels lacking in some departments. I hope we get more updates on this charming game, perhaps more endings and different sort of 'timelines'... The Plant Department?\t\t\t\t\t
