Death and Taxes

Important premise: this review is based on my personal experience.

Category
Notes
Score
Graphics
Good quality, considering the genre.
7
Plot
Good construction of the narrative universe and the dialogues, and good dissertation of the subject matters. It's a pity that many elements are only introduced (characters, places, dialogues) and not deepened.
7-
Gameplay
The mechanics that allows to decide if a person must die or live as the Grim Reaper is certainly original and sufficiently unexplored in other games. Howewer, there's not much variety in the tasks and interactions the game offers. Some gadgets can be purchased and put on the desk, but there's no explanation of their functioning (where needed) making their use potentially unclear. The map shows various areas, but some of them are and will remain unreachable.
6 ½
Sound
Good quality on dubbing (with a small, almost invisible imperfection) and sound effects. Some voices have a lower volume than others.
7-
Music
Faultless music in every circumstance.
7 ½
Overall
It's a good product that talks about an usually unexplored subject matter, especially from the point of view of the Grim Reaper. It's a pity that some elements are only introduced.
3.4 / 5
