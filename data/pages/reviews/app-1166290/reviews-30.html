The story is interesting. Just a lot of conversations get a bit too long. It's also extremely annoying that they only use "they" pronouns which often makes reading the files or conversations confusing and you end up having to do some double takes and it gave me a headache and an annoyance at bad grammar. The long winded explanation about why they only used "they" pronouns was also annoying and dumb as ♥♥♥♥ to be honest. It almost made me quit and ask for a refund.

All in all it's ok.\t\t\t\t\t
