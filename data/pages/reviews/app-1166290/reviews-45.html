The gameplay is simple: you mark profiles to live or die, and you then check in for a performance review. The writing is clever, and I loved the comedy sprinkled throughout. Even the profiles, of which there are a ton, were interesting and gave me a lot to think about in terms of getting the endings I wanted. Getting all of the achievements in this game was miserable, and there are a few quality of life updates I think would fix that, but until then, I recommend this game highly for people who want a fun and simple game with good writing, and not at all for achievement hunters like me.\t\t\t\t\t
