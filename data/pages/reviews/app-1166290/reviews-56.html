\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game left me very confused. Let me start of by listing the good things about this game: the soundtrack is amazing, I really like the artstyle, the dialogue is fun, and the morality of it all is a bit questionable (which is positive thing in my book). Besides that, props to the devs for creating a Linux port of the game, always nice to see that. Despite all of that I'm having a very hard time recommending this game, because of the following: I was just really confused after playing this game. And not in a good way. I had seriously no idea what was going near the end. There are certain items that are of importance and I didn't buy them early on in the game. This made it so that some of the dialogue made absolutely no sense. Also, you can follow multiple instructions, while they are supposed to contradict. I did exactly that and had no idea how it got to the ending that I got. Maybe I'm missing something, but I simply can't figure out how it got to that conclusion. I'm very sorry devs, 'cause it seems like you put a lot of work into this game, but because of my confusion I can't recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
