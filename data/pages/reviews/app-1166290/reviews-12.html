\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love it! It's immersive and easy to catch onto but it has soooo much more and has a lot of replay ability. The environmental? storytelling is great, using the social feed combined with certain profiles & Fates own snippets is great and definitely lends itself to variation on each run. I admit, If you don't like lots of reading it may not be great for you, but there is some wild stuff in those profiles! The actual story that your moving through is great and I like the subversion/ the interesting ways t=you can get to the end.

I'm a fan of the voice acting (Fate, Mortimer & Florian the most), Each characters voices suit them and I found they rarely overstayed their welcome on the annunciation and dramatics front. Very nice that the side characters got voices.

The games got some great style as well, the art is lovely and the colours are vibrant, which is nice to see for a death inclined game. Definitely unique to itself even if parts are similar to other games/styles this game has its own flare.


Some cons:
- Needs more music, the same tracks over and over does get a little boring
- Mostly empty NPCs - I know it's a story between Grim & Fate so they're the focus but I would have loved to get some more on the side characters we meet. Unfortunately, for most of them it only takes 2 runs to get to know all their bits. Even just 2 or 3 more snippets that suits the way you're playing on this new run would have been good.
-LET ME EXPLORE- It would have been nice to see a couple more areas, even if they were only open on certain days of the week, just to be able to interact with those other characters some more and, of course, LORE!! It would also help with the in game week pacing and give some more variety between the games main mechanics (Cerberus' den was great!)
- More outfits/trinkets, I bought out Mortimer in the first run and spent the rest only going back for Erasers & the achievement. Maybe having some more that you could only get after you've started a second chance or based on what kind of ending you got last run? Just a little more would have been nice ^-^


Overall I love it and can't wait to see some more akin to this from these guys!\t\t\t\t\t
