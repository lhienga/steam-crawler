\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think it's criminal that this game went under the radar after its initial demo.

I'm not usually a fan of simulator games but this game stands tall. Everything is brimming with personality and charm. They really leaned into the chaos. The game messes with you and demands your attention. I should note however, game would fall flat without Fate's performance. (Props to the VA.)

I think that your first playthrough is the most impactful, but I like how the game rewards you for striving for more. There are plenty of secret endings to help you piece together the whole picture. (A picture that is quite funny in reality.)

P.S. If you’re a completionist like me… just don’t. There are quite a few achievements that rely on RNG or a billion playthroughs. At some point I found my hunting became pretty monotonous and boring.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a wonderful game!! As a huge fan of "Papers, Please", I knew I was going to like Death and Taxes due to the similar gameplay; checking people and ticking boxes accordingly - or not, if that's how you want to play! The art style is great, I really love all the character customisation options you can get! Plus, the storylines and the interactions are all very well made and interesting to explore!

But for me, the highlight of this whole game is Fate. He is honestly just such a great character, his vibe and storylines are great, and his voice is so perfect. I love everything about him, he's genuinely brilliant. The only thing that could improve this game is if you could have epic sex with Fate as one of the endings.

Overall, just a really enjoyable game! I've already played through it twice as it's easily completed in a short amount of time, and i'm definitely going to play through it some more and explore the many different options! I'd love it if the game were longer, but I understand why it's the length it is and don't have too many qualms with that fact. Also, the soundtrack is fantastic. Props to the makers really, such a good game!! (also a note to the makers, if you ever happen to make a Fate dating sim, hmu <3)\t\t\t\t\t

In my 3.6 hours of gameplay, I replayed the entire game *many* times. This was to see different outcomes and to obtain various achievements (both of which I highly recommend). A worthwhile experience but come with the expectation that you will need to play it through several times in order to squeeze it for the content that it holds.\t\t\t\t\t
