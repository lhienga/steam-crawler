\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly I'm kind of disappointed with this game- at a price of roughly 13 US dollars, there isn't much replayability, and you can get a "perfect" run in 4 hours or less thanks to items which will quite literally tell you the "correct" choices to make. The art style and direction is fantastic, I'll give it that, but the story isn't very compelling, in fact it's quite simple, and that combined with the repetitive gameplay makes it feel lackluster. The achievements are meant to incentivize replaying the game but it's so tedious that I'd rather not go through the trouble. It's not "fun" is what I'm saying, you play it twice (once for normal, once for NG+) and you've pretty much seen it all. All in all it feels like a "nothing" game to me.\t\t\t\t\t
