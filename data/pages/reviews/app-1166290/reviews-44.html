\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDeath and Taxes is a wonderful game in an office job setting, each files have the summary of every single human who plays a certain role to society, it's your choice whether to let them live or die.

This game reminded me so much of Papers, Please but with a different type of pressure looming over your head- who are the people you'd want to exist in the world, informing Fate (and the lovely Lady Pawdington) of your work and hopefully doing a good job for the day, maintaining a "social" life and spending your money wisely via Mortimer, hopefully he has some interesting stuff for you that day.

A great game with a lot of replayability, the characters are interesting and the soundtrack is lovely to listen to, whether you're deciding the fate of humanity or enjoying a good drink at the Cerberus' Den.\t\t\t\t\t
