\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game. There's a lot of depth to the decisions you make and the conversations you have so there's a lot to pick up and change between different runs of the game. Not only that but specific items you pick up throughout each run carry over to the next helping you understand the mechanics and have more control over the outcome you want. My one gripe is that some of the harder achievements require a lot of RNG and a level of score precision that is not possible in the main game. I'm not against external guides and I use them quite a bit to 100% games but I would like it to at least be possible to 100% a game without consulting them. If you want the Egg achievement you're going to have to look up how to do it (and even then you need to get lucky) but if you don't care about achievements this game is pretty much prefect.\t\t\t\t\t
