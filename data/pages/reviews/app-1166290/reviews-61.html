\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game can be played a multitude of ways:

You can be as apathetic and misanthropic as possible, truly invest yourself into the impact your decisions will have, or you can "yes-person" your way through each day. Furthermore, you can make your daily judgement calls based on the rules for the day, do the complete opposite (though you may not last long), or just decide based on your own capriciousness.

For a game that appears rather simplistic, it really does have a number of layers to be considered (or contended with) as you deliberate over concepts that are particularly deep.

Very much enjoyed the music, the art style, the humor, and the characters.

I don't remember when I picked up this game, and only recently decided to give it a go. But, I'm glad I did. It was incredibly fun to push the limits of job expectations as much as possible and dig for any lore that may be hidden about The Office and its departments.

But for real, when do I get a vacation?
