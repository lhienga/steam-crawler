2. The game can't touch the player's emotions. I couldn't feel the impact of the decisions I made for the world. Many of the characters are inconsequential, so I can't understand why they have to live or die. Arbitrarily deciding whether someone lives or dies makes me feel no respect for life.
3. The story is shallow. Background of the game made me think that I could see good text, such as reflections on life and death, or criticism of social norms. But the actual content is so shallow and wordy that i don't want to read on.
4. About gender awareness. I bought this game cause the promotion of Women's Day, so I thought that the female game developers would bring equal gender awareness to the game. But this is not the case. There's only one female character till now, and her look is obviously different from those "male" characters. Know what, female characters in video game always need to be dressed or made up to look like "female"... Evermore, how pointless it would be to face a male boss every day.

Pros: Good art style. Concept reminds me of DEATH NOTE.\t\t\t\t\t
