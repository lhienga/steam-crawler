See what kind of consequences your choices bring, and revel in the destruction or prosperity you've wrought.
Or don't, maybe you just want to be done with the paper work...

A short and humorous story, featuring intrigue which would keep you second guessing your choices, and a dash of existential crisis sprinkled on top.

Gameplay can be a little boring as it involves a lot of reading and ticking boxes, but it really sells the "boring office job you would probably hate" vibe.
But seeing how your actions affect the world just keeps you going!

Interaction with most characters is fairly small, but they provide interesting insight on the world you're playing in.
And the music and art style really add soul to it all, making the experience that much more enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot my cup of joe, but it is a really good game if you like story based games. There are plenty of changeable aspects. I won't be marking it as not recommended because this is still a perfect game, I just didn't enjoy it much myself.

Story: Moderately long story, but still great. Plenty of choices, but I do think there is a major problem with keeping with a certain mood. For example I have the ability to be super rude to Fate (a main character), but I don't have this ability throughout the game's dialogue. So some times I will be telling Fate how much I hate this job and how I didn't miss him, but other times I will be listening to whatever he says without an option of I don't really care. Some characters are perfect like Fate and Mortimer, but others I get bored or annoyed of quickly. Especially

Gameplay & controls: The game has perfect controls, and the gameplay fits for what the game is about. The game just needs explanations on how to use some of the items Mortimer sells you.

Visual design: Some characters were designed creatively, but I found some to be pretty boring if I'll be honest. Lady Pawdington (the cat) isn't cute to me, especially in some scenes which is disappointing, I wanna see a cute cat.

Music and SFX: Music is pretty good, but I did get bored of it after a couple hours, there's only 3 song in the background which are like a couple minutes and loop. The sound effects worked well, I enjoyed the clock sound after making the decision to kill a certain person (no spoiler), but I was disappointed when there wasn't a special sound effect for saving said person.

Enjoyment: I enjoyed it for the first couple hours, especially being rude to Fate and being his pet. Got bored of the game once I started achievement hunting, I think it was a lot due to how lost I was while playing since I had no idea how close I was to achieving many of my goals.

Bugs & Other problems: I found 2 annoying things, first sometimes Fate's reactions to certain events were confusing, it was annoying because I had no idea if I was on the right track for the ending I wanted. Also the lamp just didn't show me all the results for a decision. Usually it shows outcomes, but sometimes it just doesn't show all of them when put on the table, I enjoy how I can leave sheets on the table so I can review them quickly, but the lamp sometimes didn't show all outcomes unless I picked it up and dropped it again.

Side note the community is lacking a bit. I guess it should be expected for a smaller game, but just know if you are buying the game to get 100% there aren't guides on everything. So if you are lost or need help, you might not be able to find it. There is an achievements guide (bless their soul, that helped a lot), but there aren't many on individual aspects of the game like the endings. I wish I could help bring up the community by uplifting the Fandom and creating guides myself, but I don't enjoy the game enough to do so.
The game also does have really cool streamer settings for twitch if your looking for that. Haven't had the ability to test it myself for obvious reasons. (Might eventually make myself stream the game someday and will test it)
One last thing, the game has an impossible achievement that requires you to either win the lottery essentially or download a cheat engine. That's just awful and insane if you ask me. Like look for a guide for the egg achievement, it is like 5 pages long with instructions on how to use the cheat engine.

Overall, decent game, wouldn't mind replaying in a year or two, but if you aren't already looking to get a game like this, I don't think you should get it.\t\t\t\t\t
