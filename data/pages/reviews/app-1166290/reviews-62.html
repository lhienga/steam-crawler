\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDeath and Taxes is honestly one of my favourite games in a long time.

If you enjoy games like Papers, Please or Peace, Death! this game is for you!

It's witty and charming, the graphics and voice acting is lovely, and from what I've played so far it has a good story.

Super easy to play this casually - say on a work break, you can do a single game day and shut it off and it's very easy to just hop back into later. It's also very easy to get sucked in and play it for hours on end.

Just like real life, the choices you make (Who should live or die) can either be super obvious, or much more complex and soul-searching. There is no one right answer.

Based on my experience so far, I would have to give this game 9/10 stars.
As I said at the start, it's one of very few games I have enjoyed this much in a long time.\t\t\t\t\t
