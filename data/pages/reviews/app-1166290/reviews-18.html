A wonderfully built game about morality, death and acceptance. While this is not the type of game I would recommend to everyone, I would recommend playing the demo. If the themes/humor/game play is to your fancy then I would recommend it, if not that its more than understandable skip on this title. Death and Tax's takes a serious look into the way we value human life, weather that is your own or a strangers. It has excellent gameplay, beautiful sound design and overall is extremely high quality.

Pros:
- Gender neutral pronouns. Not to sound like a ~blue haired libtard~ but I genuinely appreciate that a gender was not assigned to grim. Im an afab nonbinary person myself, and I always appreciate when my experiences are accounted for ^^
- Excellent story and gameplay, simple and repetitive, yet still carried enough to be enjoyable.
- Many different routes and areas to explore, adds a lot of replay value.
- While I would recommend waiting for a sale (the game goes on sale semi frequently) even at full price it is an accurate amount to pay for the length of the game.
- soundtrack comes free with game!

Cons:
- I wish we could've seen a bit more lore filled in during the mid section, but what we did get was great!
- Gameplay can be repetitive for some, and while I greatly enjoyed it I could understand how it might not be everyone's thing.

most of the endings made me tear up, especially the fate endings \t\t\t\t\t
