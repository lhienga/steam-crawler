\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the original release I loved this game.

Then the achievements got added.

The first time play-through of becoming a grim reaper, decided whether or not to obey orders and determining who will live or die while talking to a senile pirate every once in a while is all really fun. Then they added the achievements to the game.

I love the game, I really do, just not enough to play it 15+ times to figure out all the endings which are so delicate and complicated that you'll be lucky if you manage the right balance for whatever ending you want.

If you don't care at all about the achievements I recommend this game, the developers put a lot of work into it, and it's a wonderfully amusing (and sarcastic) look into what life would be as a paper-pushing grim reaper. If you care about achievements though, it will suck all the fun out of the game and make you hate your life, so maybe stay away.\t\t\t\t\t
