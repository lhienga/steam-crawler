\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI gave this game a retry when the airstrip dlc came out. Had fun to some extent and decided to get the texture pack too. I am happy I bought them at a discount.
I cannot recommend this game. It is really repetitive and has no really exciting content. There are a lot of bugs that never got fixed, and probably never will. The new dlc, actually, all the dlc for that matter, are cash grabbing content packs that don’t bring any new, lasting content to the game. The shady deals pack seemed interesting at first, but I am not going to buy it. The base game is as fun as it gets. Even now, the new Tidal Wave dlc, seems out of the blue. This has nothing to do with the base game. Looking at the images for that game, I get the feeling that the devs don’t take the original idea of the game seriously anymore.
So, if you want to get into this game, be sure that you have at least watched some gameplay videos. If you already played Treasure hunter or Food Truck Simulator, you know what kind of games they make, and you can decide if this would be worth your money.
