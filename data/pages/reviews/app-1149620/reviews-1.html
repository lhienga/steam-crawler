I'll keep this short and to the point though- this is a great game that'll be a blast to play for the first time. It's awesome to watch your gas station grow from a tiny, desolate shack into a sprawling, busy highway rest stop.
Mechanics for interacting with the world are very well implemented, and the mini games are fun. However, the game has unfortunately very little replay value; once you've unlocked everything, there's essentially no reason to keep playing, nor to restart from scratch.
I'll give the DLCs a try though, I did enjoy the game enough to spend money on those to extend new content for a few hours.\t\t\t\t\t
