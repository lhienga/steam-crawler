Should be NEUTRAL: An original, rare idea in the Simulation world, powered beautifully by Unreal Engine BUT there're flaws... my advice: save manually often.

I usually delay my gameplay, waiting for bug fix patches (and also can't afford full prices on releases too). Found out some glitches in GSS (now, May 2023) dated from Jan 2022 (Steam Community). This and other technical issues don't entirely ruin the game, but kind of demotivate, stress and irritate us players.

(+) Very nice graphics (UE); cars and objects are beautiful and detailed;
(+) Broom is the most powerful tool in the History of videogames (I would dare to say);
(+) Be aware of overwhelming non-stop action at all times (if business open): run like a maniac all over the places, even with employees. However, I'm not sure if it's positive or not.

(-) UI could be better. Glitchy and funky menus: scrolling the cart shopping list sometimes does not work properly; if click faster 2x on Decorations > Landmarks options they got stuck; etc.
(-) Too tiny font size, specially on employee screen and there's no option to change it;
(-) Painting is the most rare, slow, dull and boring thing I ever see in simulation/renovation games;
(-) Airbrush tool is bugged to me: can't deselect it, can't select any other tool nor TAB works anymore;
(-) Car wash is terrible: game almost freeze when fire water gun with nozzle option; automatic feature sometimes does not work properly, requiring quit to main menu and load again (or simply do not use it);
(-) Some areas are dark as f*ck and light decorations "may impact performance" but do not illuminate anything (or too little);
(-) Can't access the toilets for renovation or find trash. I only found some trash later on with the vacuum;
(-) Warehouse upstairs is pretty much useless, can't put anything there; can't customize either warehouse or workshop, it's kinda of meh;
(-) Employees get stuck sometimes, frozen in place doing nothing. Going back to main menu and load a previous save will fix it. But this kind of action is shameful.


A very nice game overall, original-rare idea, Unreal Engine beautiful, but also very grindy and repetitive. I can't see much more than 30h spent in this game, unless you want 100%. Glitches and technical issues don't break the game but can and will affect the gameplay (specially if you're a achievement completionist).

It's a 7.4/10.\t\t\t\t\t
