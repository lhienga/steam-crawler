\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a fun game, but has a lot of problems. If you catch it on sale, it's a pretty cool experience overall.
But, for a fully released game:
-it's scuffed as ♥♥♥♥
-full of bugs, I mean you get stuck on literally anything, NPCs get stuck, cars get stuck, hitting an item on the ground at an angle, sends them at MACH 5 speed in the atmosphere.
-at the release of airstrip DLC, all my in-game achievements got reset and I got bombarded with notifications the whole game.
-the game is incredibly badly optimised. It goes from 60 to 200 fps constantly.
-why bother adding decorations? If you place 20-30 decorations on your fully upgraded gas station, the game is barely playable.
-after mid-game (popularity 6-7) the game gets kind of grindy. You really need to put in those hours to afford things. Even though it's kind of stupid, as having employees and automation for some modules should mean an easier experience, but no.
-the mini-game with RC Car describes perfectly the state of the game. Try going through both courses without losing your minds, I dare you!


Overall I've enjoyed it, even though it was a pain in the ass from time to time. Buy it on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
