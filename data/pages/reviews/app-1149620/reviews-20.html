\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've put a lot of hours into this game.

Pros:
The gameplay is pretty relaxing.
You begin with manually cleaning cars, and eventually get an automatic car wash. You can toggle manual/automatic if you ever just want to clean cars yourself.
I enjoy putting the gas in the cars.
I bought the Ferris wheel and it is great! I mostly just manage the business and ride the Ferris wheel.
The reset cars and customers button is a God-send.
There is some online help if you ever get stuck.

Cons:
The game is so dark! Even buying and placing lots of lights doesn't really light anything up too much. Placing lighting in the apartment does not brighten the place up. I found that painting the walls white helps to see things a bit better.
Some of the minigames are very annoying and frustrating. I don't like the RC cars at all and the ice cream stand is annoying.
There has got to be a better way to determine what glasses you have on the stands and which ones to place. Visually, it's hard to tell them apart on the stand.
Party DLC: The party decorations cannot be used for the party. You can buy and use the decor for the gas station and your apartment above the warehouse.\t\t\t\t\t
