\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGas Station Simulator does a thing not enough other games do, just let you be in the moment. It's been my go-to game for a few weeks now when I just want to focus on a task and be rewarded with incremental progression. It is a buggy mess at times, but hilariously the devs included tools to help you combat this, including a broom that can flip cars, or a ufo switch that resets all cars and npc on the map. The decorating is fun without being too time intensive. The devs were even nice enough to give you the option to turn off a really annoying part of the game if you don't want to deal with it. There's a lot hiding under the skin of this game too. There's little mini-games everywhere, you can play guitar, race rc cars, or just try to set high scores by lobbing trash across the map. It really is a great game to help you zone out and just chill. Do recommend. I was pleasantly surprised at how often I think about playing this game. And if you're feeling a little overwhelmed mid game, my suggestion is to get an employee to run the register, and one to run the garage. All the other tasks are incredibly easy to manage on your own with those two out of the way.\t\t\t\t\t
