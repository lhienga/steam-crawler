\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally enjoyed this game at the start, finished the campaign and upgraded everything in base game. Installed the party DLC, finished that and then went for achievements. So far so good. I noted down which achievements I had left, mostly popularity based ones which are a grind (but that's OK) and RC car time trial.. The latter is just silly. After over 100 attempts at this race, researching strategies, attempting to alter the timer via Cheat Engine, I give up. It's demotivating and does not make me want to finish off the other achievements. The RC car mechanics are awful. The car defies the laws of physics and comes to an instant stop whenever it hits the ground in any other angle except the one the game wants it too...

You cannot upgrade the size of your shop or warehouse once you've hit max level on each, and therefore you have no choice but to buy stock, sell it, buy stock, sell it with no goal to work towards, just increasing your balance.

It's fun for 10-20 hours, after that you will either need to purchase DLC but you'll need to create a new save otherwise you'll finish it in an hour, or quit.\t\t\t\t\t
