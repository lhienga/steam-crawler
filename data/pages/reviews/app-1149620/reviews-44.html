\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game so much that I've bought it on multiple platforms. An imperfect but very addictive chore simulator with an intentionally bizarre narrative angle. The mini games can feel frustrating and obtuse to start, but I promise if you stick with it you'll get into a very addictive rhythm. Even the Airstrip DLC felt like a bridge too far at first, but once I figured out the rhythm it became one of my favorite parts of the game. I've been playing it for a few weeks and I'm still getting new import cargo items to buy and sell in my shop that I've never seen before, which I find to be super fun. It's still rather buggy 2 years after launch, but I haven't experienced any game breaking bugs. Just some quirky moments adding to the already weird flavor. I will say that once you've maxed out your gas station, there doesn't seem to be much reason to play anymore. There's not much of an endgame event, either. Just some brief text, and then the game just sort of peters out on an endless loop. It leaves me wondering (hoping!) that there might be more DLC. There are parts of the game that seem like they were laying plans to develop further, but never did. Why do you have a living space above the warehouse? There's nothing you can do in it besides decorate it. You can't even sleep in your very gross bed, which would have made a lot of sense since there's no way to elapse the time, and very few people come to your station in the middle of the night. I have read that their upcoming Roadside Diner Simulator somehow connects to your Gas Station Simulator game. I think the diner appears across the street from your gas station? No idea how that will affect either game but super curious to see.\t\t\t\t\t
