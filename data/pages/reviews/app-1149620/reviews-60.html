\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat gets my brain in a knot is the setting and era.
So in what era is the game setting?
My guess is sometime after 2004, since there is a glimpse of a 2004 Harley-Davidson Softail.
But where is this gas station?
According to a fan wiki, it is set in the middle of the desert between Nevada and California. But here's the rub; there are a slew of Fiat 126's in the game, but this car was never introduced in the USA. Icing on the cake: the Fiat 126 is actually banned from driving on the road in the USA for being unsafe. So for my brain to be more immersed, I set it in Cuba since in Cuba has a lot of old classics still driving on the road...plus the Fiat 126. But there's another rub; Cuba has no deserts in terms of vastness as portrayed in the game nor that populated.

But I digress. Despite some shortcomings of immersion for my part, this is a fun game. And if your brain doesn't get its panties in a bunch because of the conundrums, I highly recommend this.\t\t\t\t\t
