\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've enjoyed JOB and House Flipper, then you'll enjoy this game. This is a great game for us workaholics. As the name implies, you take over an abbandoned gas station, but as you upgrade it, you gain more option, like a mechanics garage that you work on cars with, a quicky-mart that you have to stock and keep the customers happy. Then general maintenance of the place in engauging. Even the pumping gas part is done through mini-games. There's enough going on here you need to set an alarm or get sucked in. I've only played for three hours and I'm already hooked. If you like occupational games, definately worth the money. My only request is that the developers find a way to have a Steam Friends invite multi-player option. Yes, there is that much to do that unless you can a black belt in time management, you're not doing it all. To their credit, you don't have to. You can shut down parts of the shop and focus on the stuff you want to do. Again, great game guys.
Still hooked, bought the airport expansion. I like how you're not locked into a set type of station. You can just be a mechanic if you want. Or just be a quickie-mart. THIS WOULD MAKE A GREAT FRIENDS LIST MULTIPLAYER GAME. Sort of an invite-only deal where your friends could take over as one of they employees. I'd pay money for that DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
