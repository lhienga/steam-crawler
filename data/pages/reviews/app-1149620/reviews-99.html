\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot a bad game, I have to say. The otherwise mundane tasks of working the cash register, filling gas, and fixing cars gets very addicting at times. Sometimes I'll get into a groove of running around during a surge of customers and it feels nice. It is very buggy though. There have been times when cars have gotten stuck driving the wrong way or flipped upside down. My whole gas station can grind to a halt as one car gets stuck in an awkward position blocking everyone else from entering or leaving. I wish it were easier to fix than nuking the whole lot and removing ALL cars and customers because ONE person got stuck. When I saw that button I knew it probably had to be a band-aid fix for the cars always getting stuck and being impossible to individually fix. The party bus gets a bit annoying at times too. I always know they've arrived because my frame rate TANKS making the game nigh unplayable. I'll try to adjust my graphical settings to see if that helps. Carrying objects will have them falling through the floor or just not following you properly as you walk around with them. This happens often but not always. Dennis is pretty annoying as well. When he comes around I have to run to find something to throw at him, but after throwing once or twice, his hitbox would cause the item to rocket into space so I have to run and find something else. It may sound like I dislike the game, but I do very much like it. I just wish these bugs weren't so frequent! If it's on sale and you're into these types of games, I'd say to pick it up. Just don't expect the most polished of experiences.\t\t\t\t\t
