\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, the graphics are absolutely horrible, the gameplay feels really quanky, the music is boring and repetitive and the humor is childish -- but despite all these downsides and quirks, it's still a fun and somewhat relaxing game, that can keep you busy and entertained for a long time.

My main issue with this game is that it's core gameplay "cannibalizes" itself over the time: When you start a new game and are left alone with that gas station, there is a lot of stuff to do: fuel cars, handle the cash register, repair cars, bring the trash out, etc -- that's the "weird job simulator" part of the game that is actually fun. However, once your gas station attracts more customers, you are more or less forced to hire employees, which perform these simple tasks for you -- and now all that you are actually left with is the ordering of fuels and goods (food, drinks, car parts) at the lowest price possible, which turns the game into an actual "trader simulator" and unfortunately, this economic part doesn't really feel fun to me and it is not possible to let an employee handle this trading stuff...

Still, I have enjoyed the game for dozens of hours and I can definitely recommend trying it out -- if you can live with the graphics, that is.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far, the game is fun. It's really satisfying to clean up and restore this old gas station and the surrounding areas. It's house flipper core gameplay with money-making minigames and the writing that frames it is really funny IMO.

That being said, the controller support is incredibly bad. I've only seen a handful of tutorial prompts that actually show the controller button instead of the keyboard. Some activities are fine with the controller. Some, like the checking out minigame, are impossible. You can't really navigate the main menu with a controller. Trying to go through the computer menu with a controller is so hard; selected items are very subtle so you don't always know what you're selecting, trying to order quantities of anything is a nightmare. It's obvious that the game was made with keyboard and mouse in mind and after it was kinda retrofitted for controller but not very well.\t\t\t\t\t
