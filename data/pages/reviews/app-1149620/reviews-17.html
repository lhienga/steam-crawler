\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter sighing immense relief over finally 100% completing this game I can agree it's something I'd recommend to people who don't mind a bit of repetitive gameplay.
Gas Station Simulator is worth the price, but even a small sale is something to consider it for! I got 43 hours out of the game and while perhaps a good 10-15 hours were dedicated to getting all the achievements I still think you'll get your moneys worth.
I can't say this game is for people who can't handle repeat actions. Once you hit, say, level 6 of your gas station you've really got a constant goal - restock, buy low sell high, keep it clean, steal from cars, etc. After a while it gets tedious and if, like me, you're achievement hunting? Waiting to get to level 10 can really make this consistency a slog. Invest in party nights as fast as possible.
Still, plenty of fun, oodles of gags and references, and the community and developer are active so it's always updating and providing more content. Invest if you get the chance!\t\t\t\t\t
