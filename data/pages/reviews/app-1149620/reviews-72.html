I've only been playing for a few hours and I've encountered several game-breaking bugs including being unable to level up my gas station (the button does nothing when clicked), being unable to unscrew tires and stuck in the repair animation even after "resetting character," being unable to clean the conveyor belt, dirt piling up inside of the terrain which impacts cleanliness, and more. On top of all of this the game has absolutely abysmal controller support and sometimes the game will give tutorials with half in controller support and half in keyboard support on the same window. This game feels like alpha phase. The devs pushed this game out knowing that it was incomplete. This is the second worst controller support I've ever experienced in a game with controller support.

Now with all of that being said, the game has nice features, good graphics, pleasant interaction and good action pace. Unfortunately I would not recommend playing it when you encounter a reload-or-quit bug every 10 minutes.\t\t\t\t\t
