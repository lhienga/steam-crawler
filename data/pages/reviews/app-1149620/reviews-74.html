\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor the price, one heck of a game.
- Lots of stuff to do,
- Lots of customization (like paints, decorations, tile, etc.), so you can really make your station your own.
- A good bit of humor.

But, there are some in game issues....

1. Until you hire some help, you'll literally be doing EVERYTHING at this place. It can be overwhelming; Pumping gas, tending the register, keeping up inventory, cleaning up the place/picking up trash, general maintenance (sometimes offsite a bit), ordering merchandise and fuel, washing cars, fixing cars, running vandals off the property, unloading trucks, etc. etc.

2. Graphics are a bit dated, but it is an indie game, after all.

3. Unfortunately, there are some minor bugs. Nothing super bad, just mildly annoying. For example, every once in a while, you'll get out of a menu, and suddenly your field of vision will get stuck at crotch level and you can't fix it until you reload the game. Sometimes, employees that work the register will just stop working and stand there doing nothing. I once got stuck in a dumpster for a few seconds, too.
---------------------
All in all, a good game, though. I can definitely see some replayability here.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis isn't my first time playing GSS(Gas Station Simulator)
I played on another steam account on my old pc; i7 7700 cpu/ GTX 1080TI gpu with 16g RAM. The game constantly experienced framerate issues, especially late game. I ended up writing a review at the time and even went to the GSS discord server to try to see what was going on- that was 2 years ago. I ended up buying a new pc; an i9 13900kf cpu, 4070TI gpu, Asus z790p motherboard, 32g Titan RAM, all on a firecuda ssd (8g read/write)

I say all this to inform you all that even with the top of the line pc, it really doesn't matter. The game still experiences frame rate issues, it constantly crashes whenever you get gas services going while having more than 2 parking lots(too many cars jam and just crashes the game), the game informs you too many lights will crash the game, cars get stuck on everything from the smallest pebble to the NPC that drives it. Sometimes car tires get stuck on the fuel pumps so you have to go to the warehouse to "UFO" the entire map away then wait 4 minutes just to have a decent amount of things to do again, just for the game to crash 10 minutes later.

Its a fun game, don't get me wrong- but the aspect of doing chores for a game then it crashing and deleting the last 15 minutes of progress every time sends me the wrong message- especially when the game is considered "Finished/complete" with an absurd amount of game breaking glitches. I usually don't write reviews, but people need to know what they're in for.

You have about 2-4 hours of fun game play before the entire game ♥♥♥♥♥ out its own ass. I give this game 4/10, buy it on sale for probably $5. You have to consider the infinite amount of free games on steam with infinite amount of replayability- all for the high cost of FREE, but GSS charges $20 so you can actively hate your life\t\t\t\t\t
