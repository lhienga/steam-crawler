\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLatest update after ~20 hrs: The game is fundamentally broken and buyers remorse is 100% with me. My saved game I started at the beginning will not load anymore. Prior saves that were automatic also don't load.

The number of little bugs/crashes here and there quickly accumulate into frustration. Stuttering between menus, loading/unloading characters in and out of the scene (the bus event), empty delivery trucks backed up as far as the eye can see. The game actually has a button to "fix traffic" which is nothing more than a clever way to cover up a huge bug they weren't able to fix that causes traffic to back up, cars and your excavator sitting on top of light poles for no reason, glitchy. If there weren't so many bugs there's actually the appearance of a good simulator progression. The most frustrating thing is there are small minigames to complete activities that RELY on smooth performance to do them correctly. if you don't, your in-game rating suffers. That's VERY annoying.

I continued to play after this review since I did pay for the game after all, which I regret. The stuttering performance in this game is so overwhelmingly frustrating, I'm done with this game. The dev team NEVER should've considered releasing any DLC until the core game works.

After paying off the final $5,000, the game froze so bad I'm not sure if I missed a cut scene that simply didn't load. Afterwards, a device appeared in my parking lot that just generates trash which doesn't make any sense at all.

There are also numberous moments where there isn't a customer in sight and suddenly a challenge appears to serve 40 customers in your store within several minutes. I can't quite remember the amount of time but it's laughable. Days will pass without seeing anyone until you "reset traffic". Delivery trucks get stuck miles away for no reason. This game is awful.\t\t\t\t\t
