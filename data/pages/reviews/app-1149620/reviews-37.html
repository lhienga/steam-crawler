Its not a bad game but it can be buggy, I have had it crash on me once but thankfully this games auto saves made it so I didn't loose really any progress when it happened. I love the charm the game has but really feel like it lacks a few things and I figured Id give some tips here also for the developers!

In short before I go into those tips I want to say if the game is ever on sale I seriously recommend getting it. Its a solid enough of a purchase now and I am having fun still 8 hours in. I would even argue it is worth it at the 20 dollar price tag. It may not be hundreds of hours of experience but its definitely fun for the 8 hours I have put in so far.

Now for the tips to Developers.
- Firstly the Employees (which I know are still in your updates and hot fixes) could use just a bit more work. I am noticing whenever my employees are checking people out they are getting negative responses a LOT. Maybe there is something to the employee system I am not catching but if I have to take over the register every time a tour bus or something comes in because my employee can't handle the line and is getting a lot of things wrong, then there is a problem. They are on working on the register but it seems they get a lot of negative responses from customers.
- Secondly I am going to suggest a simple Quality of Life change. Let us repair the register so we don't have to constantly clean it please. It was a interesting mechanic at first but it doesn't help the repetitive nature of the register later on. A simple upgrade later down the road in terms of progress could seriously help make this more interesting.
- I have seen a lot of car accidents, firstly thank you for the UFO resetter, its great and full of the same character the rest of this game has. All the same though, your AI for driving cars could use a slight rework and would solve so many of these issues. Something as simple as a check to make sure other cars are not on the same road, and making sure they stop a couple of inches sooner when there is something in their way could solve so many of the road issues. Moving the Gas tank is also something I recommend as it further causes issues with the traffic.
- Give us some more tools to mess with the gas station profits please. I have noticed that a lot of the prices of items and gas fluctuate. Allow me to also set up my own prices and control how much profit I get. This can also even lead to a mini game of updating the sign for the new price of gas if you want to add more to it. You could also add a competitor Gas station across the road or something if you ever get time to add more layers to the gas station itself. This happens a lot in gas stations across the US where you can have multiple gas stations competing for the same customers and would certainly fit the Route 66 vibe.
-Lastly, Route 66 is in the United States, I know we are only one of the like two countries that still use it, but in the US Gas is not measured in liters. We use gallons, so I suggest defaulting to gallons for gas. However Id also suggest having a setting to toggle between the two, it is another small change that makes the game more accessible.
