\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have really enjoyed the game. In this game you buy a gas station off route 66 in California. Your goal... make a successful business. As you play you unlock more services for your station that will will earn you more money. You can eventually hire employees to help you run the station. You start off with gas only, and one pump which you can upgrade up to 3 pumps as you go. As you progress, you will have in store merchandise to sell, such as, snacks, drinks, alcohol, tobacco products, toys, ice cream, and more. As you level the station you earn access to other merchandise as I mentioned above, however you start simple with drinks and snacks. You eventually get a car wash, which can be upgraded to a automatic. A car repair shop, which you unlock more repair items as you go. You start with tires, scratch removal, and mirrors. You can unlock, spark plugs, coolant, batteries, and oil. If you get the Airstrip DLC, you gain access to refueling planes, and trading items with them for imported items. Such as maple syrup from Canada, tea from UK and more to sell on your shelves. The game allows you to paint your station and decorate it as you see fit. There is a fair amount of items in my opinion. There are some game features like a kid Dennis who comes and vandalizes your station, and sandstorms which leave sand piles on the station roads that block cars. I found these features more annoying then fun. The options menu does give you the option to turn these off. I played with them on for the start to experience them, but again found more enjoyment in the game turning them off. Overall, I did enjoy the game. This is one I will come back to as DLC content comes out. Once you beat the story line, you can continue to run the station making money until your heart is content. Though as you do the content becomes stale. It is rinse and repeat in the end. Though like I said, during the progression of the story, and progression of unlocking everything, the game is fun to play.\t\t\t\t\t
