\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe sound of dust swirling horns honking will haunt my dreams for ages to come

I played this game after seeing it advertised and watching a youtuber 100% it closer to when it launched. It is an oddly addictive game and if you just play it to enjoy it, it is quite fun. I love how over the top the cut scenes are and it makes some of the early on grinding worth it. Also how the game just throws achievements at you for the first five or so hours of this game is fun as well. It is fairly realistic as you have to spend money to make money, sometimes with very little profit margin, rather than some simulators where after a certain point you are just endlessly making money. The AI pathing and things like that could be slightly improved however it is still a fun game and looks surprising well graphically for what it is. Decorating is super fun and I love the interactive decorations, it just sucks most of them serve no real purpose and just halt your progress. I would recommend this for anyone who wants a fairly mindless little game to play and have fun with.

In all my reviews on games that I 100% I will mention whether I recommend this 100% or not. If you are looking for a grindy simulator achievement, this is the one for you. It is not as grindy as it used to be with the free party time DLC (before that people were grinding well over 100+ hours for level 10 popularity). Depending on how you go about it, it should take you 20-40 hours to complete, and most of that will be grinding the popularity levels, grinding one of your employees to level 10 in all skills, and paying off uncles loan. Most other achievements are just thrown at you for completing certain tasks and buying upgrades.\t\t\t\t\t
