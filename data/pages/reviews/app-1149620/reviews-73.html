\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a person who enjoys simulations of all stripes and varieties, this one is pretty good for its cost. What you lack in story, you gain in mystery as you walk around and find random things that are part of the game. I like this game for the same reason I like House Flipper: simplicity. You run a gas station in a part of the US I'm very familiar with: outside of Vegas. Although still glitchy and not everything is customizable, enough of it is to warrant the price. Try this game if you like other simulations that aren't too complicated to enjoy, and yet could benefit from a spreadsheet on your second monitor.

A major con that can be fixed, is how often my game crashes. I don't have a slow rig, and take great pride in my computer's ability to play very large games (think Sims 4 including all expansions) very well. For this reason, I'm taking a break from this game and trying some others until the devs catch up and fix remaining bugs. For about $20 bucks, I got a good amount of entertainment and look forward to coming back to this game later.\t\t\t\t\t
