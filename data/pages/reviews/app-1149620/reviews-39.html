\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI reported a bug with one of the updates and it was fixed in the same day, Really impressed at the development team for being so ontop of things.
Yes the cars can be buggy at times but honestly at this point its kinda part of the games charm...nothing like calling in a ufo and seeing every car in the map getting sucked up and reset..
I would disregard any comments about the game being buggy as it's honestly not as bad as some people try to make it sound in the reviews, and they fix any MAJOR bugs relatively quick in my opinion.
This game manages to accomplish alot for your average simulation game and is put together very well.
The game can be tailored to almost any players needs in the settings,meaning if theres a feature you don't like or cant stand chances are you can just disable that feature in the settings menu.
The tutorial of the game and how it teaches you to play is also very well thought out and was easy for me to understand it does a grate job at not overwhelming the player with too much at one time. Until you get your first party bus...then your probs going to feel a little overwhelmed <3 have fun with that one lol..at least they warn you though

This game is amazing all around, and like others have said it really does keep someone with ADHD or OCD very entertained. However even if you don't have those conditions this is DEF worth adding to your steam library.

I will add that I did not pick up the airport DLC because of the seemingly endless amount of poor reviews revolving around the dlc but I DO recommend getting the DLC I did get which is Party Time & Can touch this.
This game was bought during a sale on steam at 30% off 13.99$ plus the cost of dlc.
