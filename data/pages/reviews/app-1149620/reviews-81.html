I understand that crashes were common when the party bus came by; apparently, there's been some work done there, because while I have a moment or two of very slow bogging, it snaps back. The game stability does still need some work, though; I have experienced several game crashes, though the autosave has generally been fairly close.

I see that ice cream vending is an option for the employees, but I don't yet see how to do that. I'm in search of the accolade where you get your employees up to level 10 on everything. BTW, I liked the hiring process and the backgrounds, too.

Overall, a fun concept and a fun game, and a nice break from my usual shoot 'em ups. Thanks so much!\t\t\t\t\t
