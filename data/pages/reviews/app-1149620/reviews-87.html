While I really enjoy the multiple different things available to do, the amount of times I've had glitches happen is frustrating. The party area with the free party DLC causes the party bus to frequently get stuck, depending on where you place your register the queue can very easily break. I had my line going through the bathroom stalls at one point. You can't place the shelves however you want to or customers or your own employees will get stuck walking into them, forever. I can always tell when a party bus is arriving because I immediately start experiencing lag spikes. And nothing is more frustrating than when the game crashes, again, for seemingly no reason. I would also love the ability to add and remove items from the shelf without having to remove and replace the shelf, because once you put something on the shelf there's no way to take it off otherwise. The amount of times I've accidentally pressed "place all" is absurd lol.

I really do love this game. There's so much to do and I love to watch my gas station grow and manage my employees, and there's a large variety of things to do. But because of the prominence of bugs I'm forced to put the game down for a while. Might come back in a few months. I notice the devs are releasing DLC here soon and I've wish-listed it, but I'm hoping they crack down on the bugs, crashes, and glitches first before releasing them.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a good game in all honesty but theres so many issues that bring the user experience down. Id have absolutely no problem with giving a positive review if they were fixed but they just arent. Theres issues that can range from being from the last update or can easily go as far back as over a year ago, if not more.

(These are not in the order of importance.)
First issue is that some settings automatically revert themselves upon a game reboot/startup. I have to shut the Car Horns option off every single time I load in, and yes, they can get pretty excessive at times. The options will even show that they are set to your desires but are still reverted.
Second issue, very random game crashes. It tends to be at low intensity moments, which of course seems weird. There are guides telling you how to lower the chance of crashes but I shouldnt have to do the Macarena and pray on my tippy toes that the game wont crash from something that hasnt been resolved for over a year. This is a issue for a large amount of players, not dependent on your PC specs.
Third issue, the Bus Party Events cause horrendous lag spikes lasting as long as 45 seconds. Doesnt sound horrible but its every time without fail. Yes, you can shut them off in the Gameplay section of the Options menu, which I suggest. I unhappily ran with them on because I needed as many customers as I could get for various achievements.
Fourth issue, achievements are random on when they decide to count to Steam. Its like Stevie Wonder is sitting there with a Yes button and a F*ck You button, and he just slaps them like crazy not knowing what they do. Theres an achievement section in the game showing players that they received them but its not well communicated to Steam, thus resulting in the game saying you have it but Steam saying you dont.
Fifth Issue, the Late Game Challenges are broken and very unbalanced. The challenges are great for helping with Popularity achievements but are about useless. Ol Stevie mustve had a hand in these too Some Challenges will just not track your progress whatsoever, others have such high demands with such a low of a time limit it ends up being impossible to do them at all.

There is probably more to say on the issues with the game but those five mentioned are the ones that really applied to me during my play-throughs. Do I expect every game to be a polished masterpiece? No. But seeing their efforts being put into DLCs, at least 3 new games, and not into long lasting issues in the base game causing some players to drop the game entirely, seems a bit off. Seems like they're putting their effort into DLCs/games that can get them more money and not attempting to resolve issues in the game that cause highly negative user experiences since that itself isn't profitable in the short term.
If you're wondering if you should buy this game, I'd suggest you wait for a sale or give it a healthy time frame so that maybe the issues are patched/fixed, if they ever feel to do so.\t\t\t\t\t
