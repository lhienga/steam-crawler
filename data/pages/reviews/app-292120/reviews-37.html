I can say playing this game in 2023 where the market is overstaturated with boring and bloated open world games, that Final Fantasy 13 is a BREATH OF FRESH AIR. Yes, it is a classic "hallway simulator", but when you are traversing through a massive opening world were there is nothing but grass and trees, this is so much more interesting in the long run.

I love open world games so I say that with great conviction.

The first two chapters of this game are very boring as you can only use 2 commands but when you hit chapter 3 and the world then opens up with magic and command forms, then it gets so much better. I have played through the majority of this game on my other account (until steam locked me out, those bastards) and I found myself clocking in 4 to 5 hours of nonstop playtime and wanting to continue playing even though I probably should be doing something else.
