\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell I've 100% it. So I suppose I should write a review. And it's going to be an honest, albeit short review.

If you play the game with a cheat 'trainer' running. To allow you to kill the enemies (and bosses) in 1 hit, and simply play the game as a 20-30 hour interactive movie/story, then I will admit that it's fantastic.

But as an actual game? Playing the battle system, and mechanics therein as intended? Absolutely the worst garbage I can begin to imagine.

Anyone else is free to think that the battle mechanics are fun. I don't. And never will.

I'll recommend it because I do actually like the characters. The graphics and sounds are brilliant. I personally find the story engaging.\t\t\t\t\t
