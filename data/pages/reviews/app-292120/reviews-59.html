\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave to agree with a lot of the other negative reviews: This is the worst Final Fantasy and not worth your time or money.

Starting with the positives:

+The game world is occasionally beautiful.

+The music is pretty decent. While not as good as some may hype it up to be, there are some real slapping tunes in this game.

+Late game, your Ravagers (casters) feel really awesome and powerful when they cast high-tier spells.

The negatives:

-Game is literally a corridor simulator for the first 20ish hours. And again for about the last 5-10 hours. In between, you have the worst kind of MMO simulator experience with faceless quest givers sending you on generic hunt-style quests.

-Combat relies on positioning to avoid powerful AOE attacks, but you literally have no control over the characters' positions.

-Game relies on speed (and judges you based on how quickly you can complete each fight), but the character and battle menu animations are slow, clunky, and unresponsive. "Go fast or we'll give you a bad score! Oh, can you kindly wait while this menu animation finishes before you can make your next selection. ;)"

-The combat camera is an awful, glitchy experience.

-Game events force party changes right before boss battles, giving you no time to prepare the new party before being thrown into a fight. Once you die and retry, THEN the game is like "oh right, here's the menu so you can make changes and tweaks!"

-Player cannot control two-thirds of the party members, but the player cannot rely on the AI to make smart choices about what skills to use and when during clutch situations. My characters are literally dying and my buffer prioritizes an attack buff instead of a defensive one.

-Awful dialog and shallow writing produce unlikable characters with no depth. It felt like SQEX couldn't even be bothered to hire a director to guide the voice actors in their performance.

-Obtuse story and awful writing with lots of plot armor and unexplained superhero moments where characters can suddenly do new things because the situation requires a solution.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnderrated and over hated.
Is it perfect? Nope. Awful game that doesn't deserve to a Final Fantasy game? Nope.
Music is absoluty amazing, the visuals in this game could pass for a modern AAA game in 2022, the character designs on even the meh characters are fantastic, some of the characters personalities are absolutely phenomenal (Sazh and Fang are two of my favorite FF characters, easily)
Hope and Snow can both be extremely grating, but I don't hate them entirely, either.
Give it a try with an open mind, and don't listen to anyone who says it's a hallway simulator, Final Fantasy X is way more linear than this game, and is widely considered one of the best games in the series.
The games only major flaw is the gameplay loop, especially combat, can grow tiring during long gameplay sessions. I think this game is genuinely the most enjoyable in short bursts, over a long period of time.
Give it a try, it's a genuinely enjoyable Final Fantasy experience, just don't expect too much from it.
7/10 game. It's fine.\t\t\t\t\t

Best father award.
