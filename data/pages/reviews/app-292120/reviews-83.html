\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll the criticism of FF13 over the years is completely correct. Not only is the story complete nonsense, it's also horrendously boring. Terms are explained clearly if you read the novel's worth of text the game spits at you as you play each section but the story is indigestible garbage on its own. Exchanges like "Is that a Gran Pulse fal'Cie? No! It's a Cocoon fal'Cie that will turn us into Cieth! But at least we're already Cocoon le'Cie with Eidolons ready to fight for Orphan!" happen constantly and there's no reason at all to care. Even if it made sense it would be boring.

It takes about 20 hours for you to even have any idea who the main characters are on any level deeper than "Angry girl" "Foolish guy" "35 year-old 'old man'" "Optimistic girl" "Child" and "Wears a blue dress". The secondary and tertiary characters are even worse. Several of them get literally no characterization at all and by the end of the game they have "heroic" moments that elicit all the emotion of a human in a Zac Snyder movie being caught in the rubble of a collapsing building. How anyone could care for anyone in this story is beyond me.

This game is only worth playing if you're trying to complete all the Final Fantasy games for some reason (like I did) or its the only game you have. It's definitely not the worst game ever made but it is well below the standards of Square-Enix (even a Square-Enix long past their golden age). Anyone that tells you this game is anything better than "okay" has terrible taste and should be ignored.\t\t\t\t\t
