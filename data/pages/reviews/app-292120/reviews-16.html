BEFORE YOU PLAY!
For the love of god, before you play, install the 4 GB Patch and the FF13 Fix, one of the most popular guides on Steam shows how to do that. Don't think "eh my PC is good", it's not good enough, this game ignores any specs you have as it creates "virtual" specs. These virtual specs were enough for the console versions, but on PC it's prone to crash because many things can make it use more RAM than expected. And the more you play, the more common the crashes will be.

Now about the actual game...
I'm not on the team "FFXIII sucks" and not on the team "FFXIII is an underappreciated gem". It's a fine game, it has flaws, it has its moments, it can be fun, it can be a bit annoying. It's kinda hard to talk about a game like this, like what do I say? How can I make a review about how "a bit above average" it is?
The story is confusing, but using the Datalog on the menu surely helps. The combat got me confused too, not because it's difficult, but I'm confused as to why they decided to make some things the way they are. But some boss fights are quite frenetic and exciting. The characters are cool, but a bit dumb at times.

Really, it's a fine game, I think it's worth playing if you like the genre. But given its nature, it's a game that reaaally divides people, you never know if it'll be your favorite game ever or the worst thing you've ever played.\t\t\t\t\t
