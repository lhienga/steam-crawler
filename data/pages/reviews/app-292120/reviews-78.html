\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI last played this game on the Xbox 360 8 years ago and just recently started playing it once more on PC. While nostalgia goggles are a consideration, I will aim to avoid using them.

General Review...
- The combat system is challenging, fast paced, and engaging. The paradigm system forces you to think quickly and each enemy forces you to use the appropriate strategy and paradigm.
- The story is confusing at first but quickly unravels itself as you continue throughout the game.
- While the game is very linear, it is clear how Square Enix wishes for the story to unfold. There is an open world moment towards the end of the game.
- The controls for the PC port are atrocious. This game is really enjoyable with a controller and is optimized pretty well for it. (Who the hell thought it was a good idea to use 'esc' as a button to completely shut down the game?)
