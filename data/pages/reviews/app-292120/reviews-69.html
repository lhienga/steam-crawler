The vast majority of graphics settings and all of the audio settings have been removed in this version. You WILL need mods to get this to work and actually look halfway decent.

The story is really all the game has going for it, which defeats the purpose of it being a game. It should just be an anime. Not only is it objectively the worst game in the series, it's also just an objectively bad game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t5.8 Hours: the visuals are still stunning and the gameplay doesn't get old and repetitive.

25 Hours: I still stand by this game having incredible visuals! Graphics still remain top tier despite their age. Although as I've progressed the gameplay does get a bit repetitive but still it's bearable. I often find after playing the game for a decent amount of time, I begin to 'zone out' during battles unless it's a new enemy. I love the story although Snow really gets on my nerves. Personally, I find it irritating how his character behaves and reacts to situations that unfold and new details they come to learn, he makes me roll my eyes and sigh. But that's also what makes this game great! Not having to make the player like EVERY character portrayed as the heroes. Although the female leads in this game definitely have my heart.
I used to play this game way back when on PS3 but I was very young and didn't understand the basic fundamentals that make this game playable. (The Crystarium's, Team Composition etc.) So if I have a tip for someone playing this game for the first time, DONT SKIP THE TUTORIALS! Once you understand the basics of the game its so much more enjoyable. Its not a game I can personally sit and binge the entire day but its great for some quick time killing or not sure what else to play.\t\t\t\t\t
