Cons: Gets very tedious and repetitive. It takes far too long to develop the core gameplay mechanics. Characters feel bland at times. It's too easy and holds your hand for far too long. Seven hours of pure gameplay time alone and I haven't even used the last character once.

This is my third time playing FFXIII and I know what I'm doing. Therefore the repetition feels gratuitous. Yet I find myself grasping the core fight mechanics better than ever. When should you use synergists and saboteurs? Do you build chains with Com/Rav(/Rav) or triple Rav? Is switching paradigms worth the time cost?
