\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan. I really gave this game a good try, played 30 hours and got to close to end-game. But there are so many small problems that add up and mix into Very Big Problems with the majority of the game's design.

For instance:
You can't position your party members, or your party leader. Which means your healer will be glued to your tank 70% of the time because their AI will just walk them forwards into the middle of the battle. Combine this with the fact that in the late-game every enemy has an AoE basic attack that can easily knock any party member not actively tanking down to 1/3rd HP, and that attack in groups of 2-5 at any given time, and you can see the problem.

There's a massive open-world section very late into the game, which sounds great! In any other JRPG the moment the world opens up you get much more freedom to choose what you want to do. But all of the quests in the open-world are linear. And the fast travel system requires you to run across the map over and over to unlock because the fast-travel points are tied to the quests. And you don't get a faster movement option until you've finished about a third of the open world quests. Which means you'll be spending precious tens of minutes just running from point A to point B on the other side of the map, only to have the next quest send you from point B back to point A.

Some of the boss fights have a time limit. A Very Strict time limit. And instead of health bars you have to impress them, but each boss has a random set of things that do this and they Will keep attacking you, forcing you to heal before you can do anything else because if your party leader dies, you game over.

Also, unrelated to the gameplay itself but I love party-based RPGs, the party is what Makes the game most of the time. But 3/6 of the cast members are so unlikable that I had to actively stop myself from skipping every cutscene they're in. And one of those characters is the MC!

Like I REALLY wanted to like this game! I love female RPG protags, Velvet from Tales of Berseria and Milla from Tales of Xillia are my 2 favorite RPG protags period. But god man, I couldn't even like lightning and I'm a lesbian.\t\t\t\t\t
