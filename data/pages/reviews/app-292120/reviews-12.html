The game is about as bad as everyone said it was back in the day, but if you can look past all that it's a good game, just not a great game unfortunately. FFXIII is what I like to refer to as a "game of it's time", and honestly it sucks compared to the likes of FFIX, FFX, and FFXII. It's basically, in my opinion, an amalgamation of all the games that came before it, or something like that.
As a side note also, if you get easily frustrated with games, especially when they explain nothing to you, then avoid this game for sure, this game is bad about not explaining things very well and expecting you to know what to do.\t\t\t\t\t
