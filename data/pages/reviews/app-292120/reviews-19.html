\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI consider this game to be good but I understand why people wouldn't, it's a very flawed game.
The first 20 hours are basically walking in corridors fighting enemies, although they're very beautiful corridors lol, then it opens up astronomically for the remainder of it, which can be 10-60 hours depending on how much of it you want to complete. I believe this design choice was intentional for story purposes but they took it a bit too far with how linear it is.
The other low point is the story, I loved it when I was younger, but now it's pretty corny and cringy at times.
It still has its highs, some bits of it are pretty good. Sazh's story has really grown on me since I have been able to see it from another perspective.

So if you don't mind the first 20 hours or so you're gonna like this game a lot, they're not even that bad tbh, just very mediocre for what you would expect from FF. I think the gameplay gets really good and it's super fun to play when you get past that point, also the soundtrack is godly and graphics really hold up considering this game is 14 years old. This is why I give it a thumbs up, but I'd be lying if I didn't say it was a tough desicion.\t\t\t\t\t
