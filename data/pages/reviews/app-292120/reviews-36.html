\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDISCLAIMER - I enjoyed this game but its such an awful port of it. Even if you use 3rd party software to patch it, it still runs really bad. Wait for the inevitable HD remastered trilogy or play it on ps3/xbox 360.

The first 20 or so hours of this game are pretty meh. It's crazy linear, there is so much universe specific jargon thrown at you from the get-go (I don't ever want the letters C I E in that order ever again smh) and pretty much every character is boring or annoying (Except Sazh and Vanille ;)). Once you get to Gran pulse the game finally lets you kind of do your own thing and it gets hella customizable.

This part is pretty great and has the same addictive nature FFXII gives you with setting up your specific party and paradigms. Everyone in the party gets a lot more likeable too once they decide to stop arguing and saying Cocoon or Pulse l'cie. I only wish there was more plot stuff to do there, you can literally just walk through gran pulse and ignore it if you like ( you will get killed a lot).

Overall I do think its a decent game. I can see why people like it and I can see why people hate it, especially final fantasy old heads. I love RPGs and I REALLY love old-school 90s RPGs which I'm sure many OG FF fans wanted this game to be but I don't want RPGs to have to be boxed into one specific style of gameplay and so I appreciate the attempt to try something kind of different. I don't think it was executed very well but I still think this games last 25 or so hours are really fun (longer if you want to mess about with all the great post game content). Trudging through those first 20 can be rough though, I mainly did it to satiate a curiosity as a long time FF fanboy. Its not my favourite Final Fantasy, but I do like this video game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Final fantasy 13 is a good game, but also an unbalanced game. First you are thrust into a world with an ongoing story that takes a bit of time to piece together as it unfolds through cut scenes between fights. It is almost confusing and hard to be interested in since you have no idea wtf they are talking about for a very long time. Aside from that the visuals are fantastic! Square-Enix worked really hard on this game and it shows. The character details and backgrounds really stand out. It fits into today's standards. The visuals, and storytelling is great so what's the downside? It's the game play and a few other things.

For a long time fan of the classic turn based rpgs in the series, I cannot stand playing this game. It feels more arcade-y than a final fantasy game. You have the option to preset your attacks with auto input, or you can select it manually. Somehow selecting it manually is a harder task, because you must confirm your actions with y (xbox) or triangle (ps), I don't know pc's key off the top of my head. This is kind of confusing considering your default confirmation button is the A button (xbox) or x on ps. You select with one and confirm with the other. However you can mash that same a button to confirm auto input. The good news is, you can play this with one hand.

Another downside in my opinion is the game difficulty. You will go through areas fighting your way through then get to a boss and things will go well, until they use one move and then it's over. This happens a lot. And sometimes it is not even a boss, it can be a regular monster. I know its no fun without a challenge, but it is kind of ridiculous at times when you grind and it still happens. There is no defense option unless your character is a sentinel. What's worse is you can't choose your other parties actions. Which trust me it will anger you like nothing else. You can't rely on the computer. It is dumb, real dumb.

The final letdown is the game itself. It is poorly optimized for PC. You will be smooth in some areas. then in others for whatever reason, you will chug like it's got nothing in the tank. Also there is no in game "exit game" option it can only be exited with escape on your keyboard. Pc got the stepchild treatment. If you can get past all these obstacles, there are end credits to see. Because that is what I am looking forward to after 40 hours.\t\t\t\t\t
