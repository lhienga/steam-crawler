But, I reinstalled this game after a week-long break. Ended up binging it to the endgame.
I'm still not recommending this to others because while there's a lot to like about this game, none of it is in the opening 15-20 hours (yes, that's the opening).
Gameplay is far too linear with almost nothing breaking up the constant grinding and cutscenes. If you're not engrossed in the story, characters or battle system, you're gonna have a pretty bad time slogging through up to the point where you can edit your party. Which happens just before what many consider the hardest storyline boss in an FF game.

The graphics, music, and art direction are all 10/10. But those aren't all you need to make a good game experience, which is what the creators clearly wanted to do.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReview not posted Final Fantasy 13 on Steam Deck
Game completed : April 23, 2023
Playtime : 54hrs
My score : 9/10

When I first played the game at its release, I thought this was the worst Final Fantasy game I had ever played, and I still think so at present, only now that I kept my mind open and gave it a chance, noting it got sequels and not all negative reviews. It also helps a lot with its upgraded visuals and portability with Steamdeck. I gave the game a chance. After a couple of hours (10 or so), it is excruciatingly linear, but it has its benefits, as you can now focus on its story and narrative. So I enjoyed this way more than the Tales series, obviously, as I finished it in 2 weeks for over 54 hours of playtime. So the experience was way better than expected. The gameplay overall also adds to its linearity; it has its challenges in its own way. When I get to the last part and watch the ending, it reminds me why Final Fantasy is still my favorite JRPG series. how their team doesn't tread lightly with their efforts. It reminds me of the core of the series, where they tend to surprise you and where it always becomes a blockbuster in the end, especially when it comes to its story, narrative, and character building.
I love the game; it will surely revamp itself towards my impression from a score of 1 to 9.\t\t\t\t\t
