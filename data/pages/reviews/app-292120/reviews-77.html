\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm in love.

I must say I was kept away from this game far too long by it's not-so-good reputation. Having decided to finally try it...I'm having such a great time I can't stop.

While it's true the gameplay is pretty much -follow the path- ...even more so than FFX... the trick is not to expect a "classic" FF (I to IX) but rather a story.....and boy, WHAT A STORY.

If you like high fantasy, distopias, mythology, adventure, sci-fi...this game takes a bit of everything and makes an incredibly well-thought literature. At first it confuses you, then it creates questions...the kind of questions you simply NEED to know the answers to...and as the adventure progresses, the pieces slowly begin to fit togheter bit by bit...creating a beautiful masterpiece. I highly reccomend it if you don't mind quite a bit of reading.

Add to this the aesthetics which are overwhemingly beautiful. To this day they remain high tier graphics. The vivid colors and high definition of ...everything, makes for an eye candy journey.

From a gameplay point of view, it is indeed lacking... The first 2 chapters will be almost entirely automatic. Walk forward and press action button now and then during battles. Ocasionally use a potion when needed, as much. The game will then very slowly open some actual choices and battle planning, and much...much later, at a certain point, it will expand a lot on the whole building/plannining/tactic aspect. By then the gameplay becomes quite enjoyable in my opinion.

Having not yet finished FFXIII I'm so hooked by the story that I cant wait to play the next two games as well. This game deserves more love , hope more people will enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
