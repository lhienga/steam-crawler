as an absolute final fantasy fan, i played the hell out of this game back in 2013, in my old ps3, finished it two times, fell in love with the ost, barely understood the plot because i barely knew English back then

i'm going to address the bad stuff first since they're whats fresh in my mind.

The plot is really, really rich and interesting, if you check the files, there's much to read, and some may even bet some hidden messages or hints about our current world
but, nevertheless, as far as i am into the game right now, the plot feels really silly

I'm pretty sure all three females are lesbians (tho i dont care about that, but, it feels strange that ALL are like that)
Lightning gives masculine vibes to make abby jealous
all the male protagonists are absolutely pathetic. Snow, the strong macho like guy of the group is just big reckless and stupid. I can't imagine someone of that age being that naive, i wonder how much troy baker was cringing when he was voicing for him
Sazh is unbelievably childish. i understand that it may be part of a personality, but for someone with such a purpose, an grown man with a son, its almost unbearable to watch or hear some stuff he says sometimes
Hope... a boneless wimp. Just that. He looks 14 but has the emotional endurance of a toddler.

not so long into the game and the strong wuma is already punching and humiliating the strong macho character multiple times
i dont remember another final fantasy where there was so much fighting and discontent in between the party, so early into the game

im only in a few hours in, but i remember some stuff, and i can get the sense of how things are going. if i change my mind about anything or decide to add another two cents, i'll be back

also, the game just doesnt run above 27 fps, doesnt matter your rig it seems, im going to have to look for a patch around the web

now, for the good part

sountrack? part of many spotify playlists to this day, and have been for many years. one of the very, very few albums that ive consistently listened to over time

graphics? if you manage to patch the 27fps cap, they are a joy and wonder to the eyes (remember that this game is more than 10 years old, and was originally released in ps3/xbox)

now then, another thing, the elephant in the room
is it boring/a hallway simulator? Yes?! No?! I cant say for sure. My guess is that square wanted to take a shot in a final fantasy that was more appealing to western players, or anyone in general. this game is much, much simpler (and, consequently, easier) than any previous ffs released
i liked it back then, i'm enjoying it now (though, honestly, i am quite a patient person)

anyways, ill leave the comments open. just try to be polite and elaborate properly if you disagree\t\t\t\t\t
