\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite the great critical dislike for this game, I really like it. I was doing a Final Fantasy marathon at this point in time, so the gameplay of this game was a really nice change of pace. Final Fantasy XIII is one of the best looking final fantasy games, even to today's standards which is absolutely wild to me considering how old this game is lol. I especially love the music in this game, it is probably some of the best tracks in this entire franchise done by Masashi Hamauzu. The plot is kind of all over the place, the world building is kinda cool with different planets, and I definitely do like the characters and themes of this game. One theme that I do not often see enough in media is the theme of forgiveness, and that is the main focus of characters in this game. While all the characters start off mostly bitter to each other, they grow to overcome their own grief and better themselves and grow as a group, to overcome the corrupt church and of course save Lightning's sister Serah. I still love these character designs to this day, and while these characters are not perfect, I definitely appreciate that they all go off on their own arcs, and have separate parties/conflicts and such. I would definitely recommend this game and I think it is worth checking out even if it does not have the greatest critical acclaim because I believe there is stuff to be gained from playing\t\t\t\t\t
