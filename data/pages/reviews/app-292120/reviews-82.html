\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinal Fantasy 13 is a Solid FF title. This game has absolutely astonishingly beautiful graphics, fantastically stylized character design, an interesting dynamic for how your teams work depending on how you format your combat team, the ability to hotswap between classes as a group (which is great and very unique to this game) and some very amazing visuals, and the score is fantastic. This title has some of the best music in the franchise.

This FF title is great in a lot of regards, its sole weakpoint is its story and how the first half of the game is bottle-necked. The first half or so of the game is very slow, and very much a series of corridors between you and the open world part of the game, which is in the last quarter of the game. It takes a long while to get through the first portion of the game and how straight line it can feel at times, but once you hit the open world and can fully travel around and such, the game gets MUCH better.

All around, Not the strongest FF title, but not a terrible one. I would definitely say to give it a try if you are an FF fan, and have gotten through most of the other games and want a new one with unique features to try out and experience.\t\t\t\t\t
