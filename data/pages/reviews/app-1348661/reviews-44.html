\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA quick summary of what i feel about this DLC

Railway system is interesting and definitely mechanic changing to the point it makes this DLC worthy of picking up
Same can be said about the army corp system and their special buffs
New focus trees are finally here, man the old soviet tree is like pitiful compared to the new one

However, the tank designer is an huge issue. Completed custom designs will remain to appear as a chassis and not a finished tank, a small immersion break but thats the least concerning.
The most problematic issue is however you design your tank, it will not exceed the performance of the default tank that u receive that is not customizable and only upgradeable. Being only able to upgrade engine and armor, the Inferior stats of the custom tanks all round makes the tank designer useless in its current form, and subtype is unable to be used either due to no possible modules to unlock role atm or just simply having even worse of a stat gap. The tank designer feels like a heavily incomplete feature that may update in the future, or can only be saved by mods.\t\t\t\t\t
