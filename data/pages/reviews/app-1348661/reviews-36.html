\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom a multiplayer perspective, this DLC is horrible. It is an unbalanced, buggy mess.
Long rant ahead, you have been warned.

Soviet Focus Tree:
WAY TOO BIG, WAY TOO MANY 70 DAY FOCUSSES, LOTS OF USELESS/FILLER FOCUSSES

In the industry part of the focus tree, you can spend hundreds of days to make your country literally worse. The five-year-plan national spirit is basically one level of American depression that you cannot get rid of – ever. Then you can do a 70 day focus that INCREASES the consumer goods while giving “buffs” I would not even pick up with a 7-day focus.
The purge part is also completely filler with focusses that give you ♥♥♥♥ like 5% Ideology drift defense + daily communism support while you have 100% commi support after 1 month into the game anyway. Then there is the political paranoia system which is a pain in the ass to deal with, and they FORCE you to deal with it because they time locked the purge focusses similar to the American depression focusses.
The worst offender on that part of the tree is probably the “war economy” focus, which… well… gives you war economy in exchange for stability. But you get it way to late. Remember when they said something along the lines of “we removed some war support from the USSR to make it harder to get to war economy”? Well, now you just sent an attaché to Spain and get to war eco as soon as the Spanish civil war starts – which is more than 1 year earlier than if you would take the focus and you don’t lose stability.
Killing Trotsky? Waste of time, you don’t get anything from it other than an event.

The army focus tree is not much better. You spent dozens of days on utterly useless focusses to occasionally pick up something decent. The Russian air force is ridiculously week as well because, once again, you have several 70 day focusses just to get rid of your horrendous starting debuffs. And its not like you have time for that anyway.

Also, there is absolutely no choice in the order you do the focusses. Stalin’s paranoia is a ticking time bomb, so you HAVE TO rush it. Then you go for a research slot because its now around 38 and you still have only 3. Then you have to do the claims on the Baltic and Finland which takes another 175 days just to be allowed to spend 225 political power to send the ultimatums. Claims on Bessarabia is also here, but why would you ever take another 70 day focus that effectively give you like 1 factory?

Then you do some army focusses because otherwise your army will just die. And halfway through its Barb-time already – no time to even think about doing air focuses, improve Stalin’s traits or do any of the other stuff.

Gameplay:
TANKS ARE USELESS NOW, they take way too many researches (you know, the stuff you get with research slots, the ones you get way to late and are buried under a mountain of garbage focusses?) since the guns are locked behind the artillery techs and you now also need to research, engines, armor and the radar stuff because tank radio is kind of required to get at least some value out of your tanks. And no, not having to research doctrines does not make up for it by the slightest. Tank stats overall are way lower than before as well now. Armor stat is still useless because even if you invest everything into it, your tank will be infinitely more expensive, slower and with lower reliability just to get pierced by a normal tank anyway. Heavy tanks are way superior in any way to mediums tanks once again. HT are barely more expensive and you can make then really quick for a really low cost now as well.
But even if you suffer through all of that and make really good tank divisions, basic infantry arty divisions are just superior now, especially with CAS – the thing Russia is really bad with.
The supply system is really great. You now you have pay attention to the logistics of your country, have to capture strategic supply hubs, etc… at least I would have said that if not for:
Transport planes. They are OP as hell. Like 500 of them basically let you completely ignore the entire supply system. They let you snake better than before, you never run out of supply, and they basically bypass the otherwise really interesting supply system.

ARMY EXP. One question... Why?? The amount you get from your military high command guys basically needs to be doubled. Changing divisions is still WAY to expensive and when the war starts, the entirety of the allies will have no more than level 3 doctrines, while Germany simply rushed through it. And I don’t care if they is historical of whatever, it completely ruins the balance. Even as Russia, being battle planned by the entire axis, it took me until 42 to get all the doctrines, which is ridiculous because Germany has their doctrines completed before barb.
Promoting general into high commands is bugged in many cases. Like the special forces guy from India, which would give you 15 attack and defense on special forces you simply cannot promote. The button does nothing. And I heard the same about other generals as well, some Russian general for example.

The different widths are really stupid as well. They changed how bigger divisions fight smaller divisions and now, focus firing is no a thing anymore, reducing crits by a lot. That means you spam out thousands of 10 width without even support companies as holding divisions, because they have extremely high defense and org while not being able to be focus-fired like before. This also makes the game even more laggy.

Performance:
The game became laggy as hell. Even in 36 the game is already noticeable slower than before. And if the German-Soviet war lasts longer than 2 years you better get yourself a NASA computer in order to be able to continue playing at a reasonable speed. This reminds me about the original stellaris endgame lag after the changes they did in the first economy update.
AI seems more competent than before. Though I am unsure if they just ignore some of the supply mechanics or if they are actually clever about using them.
Now for the biggest problem: During multiplayer, the AI randomly turns on and sends random lendleases out and keeps reorganizing the entire army. I doubt anyone ever playtests multiplayer over at paradox. How could you have missed AI randomly ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ up player countries? This keeps ruining every second MP game.

This DLC might be cool for single player only people, but for multiplayer? Hard pass, just revert to and older version and play TFB or wait for the mod to be updated.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis dlc is a unbalanced, broken and buggy mess. They have completely butchered the soviet focus tree. It's absolutely terrible.

SOVIET FOCUS TREE:
The five year plan industry focuses are completely useless, in fact they cost you more consumer goods while giving very insignificant bonuses. there is no reason to go beyond the second five year plan focus as it's required for the extra research slot. If you try to do them while germany is attacking, you will get locked out of them while having consumer good debuffs and by the time you can do them again, they become totally useless as the game ends at that point.

The paranoia system is also dogsheeet, you have to constantly do these useless focuses to keep paranoia down, while you keep getting massive debuffs. It forces you to waste time on focuses that give you no benefit as you ignore your army and economy. It would have been way better to put the purge focuses as decisions,that way you can focus on things that helps your army and industries. Also it takes way too long to get rid of paranoia like about 1939. Going for industry focuses in 39 is useless because you have to focus on your army then. In summary the focus tree is as wide as an ocean but as deeps as a puddle and not very well designed.

THE AI AND GAMEPLAY:
Gotta give credit where it's due, the AI has significantly improved which makes the game more challenging and fun. The new supply system is decent but still needs a lot of work. It will be significantly harder to play as a minor nation now and the focus trees for minor nations have become outdated, knowing paradox this could be deliberate, so they can sell more dlc. The new tank designer is decent but tanks have become very useless in general. the combat width change is whatever, it doesn't make the game any better.

BALANCE:
As I said before tanks have become very useless. They cost way too much in terms of IC but provide little benefit.. If you make Low IC tanks they will not be able push even basic infantry. You also can't design good tank divisions as they cost way too much xp and you need xp for way too many things (doctrines, advisors etc) while you gain very little even with full officer corps.even when your troops are engaged in cambat on every tile along the frontline, you don't get as much xp as you need. This makes for a totally unbalanced game as countries like germany will have a lot of xp while minor nations will have very little. the supply system also needs to be balanced. It costs way too much for railways, infrastructure, and supply hubs. they either have to reduce the cost of these or reduce civilian factory cost.

BUGS AND PERFORMANCE:
This dlc is more polished than many other PDX dlc releases, however the performance is horrendous especially after 1942, it's borderline unplayable.

VERDICT:
AVOID IT LIKE THE PLAGUE UNTIL THEY FIX THE AFOREMENTIONED ISSUES.\t\t\t\t\t
