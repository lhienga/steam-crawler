\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHearts of Iron IV: No Step Back is the best HOI4 expansion to exist. It adds so much new content in eastern Europe, for the nations that would inevitably fall to the communist rule of the mighty Soviet Union. Speaking of which, the Soviets finally got themselves a focus tree. You can now start a civil war between rival communist parties inside of the nation, bringing back Trotsky from exile for example, or you can try and reform the Russian Empire. It also has new war goals for the nation. In Poland, you can work with the allies from exile, and fight against the German invaders, sabotaging their operations in the region, leading to the downfall of the Reich. You could also support a communist movement in the nation, and work with the soviet union against the Reich from the beginning. You could also forge your own path against the Germans, with monarchy, leading to you having the option to reform the Polish-Lithuanian commonwealth, or perhaps starting a new union, or empire. For the Baltic nations, each has its own unique path, alongside a shared path. Each of these nations unique paths are very fun and good overall. I have zero complaints there. The army officer corps have been added, giving advantages to all of your troops if you use different army spirits. This expansion adds the ability to give your generals abilities, meaning every division under their command gets a bonus, or a buff. The supply system has been completely overhauled to be more realistic, now you must deal with your railroads, which will be the lifeblood of your supply. If the trains cannot make it to the front, then your troops will suffer mass attrition, which leads to mass casualties. These railroads can be bombed, and you can bomb other nations railroads. If your railroads are bombed, repair them as soon as possible or you may be screwed. Scorched earth tactics are now inside of the game, press a button on each state you want to scorch, and then retreat. If you do this, the enemy will have attrition, leading to an easier victory as they rebuild what you destroyed. Floating harbors and special units for supply are now in the game, meaning you can airdrop things to the front if your railroads are destroyed. The tank designer is the best part of the expansion in my opinion. It allows you to research different parts for your tanks, and attach them to chassis that you unlock through the research tree. You have to balance attack power with speed, as if your tanks are too slow, then they may never see any frontline action, also you must balance the price. If the tank is far too expensive to produce, then it will take forever to make. Railway guns are added as well for the railways. Meaning that you have bigger artillery that can move faster as they are on rails. You can use these railway guns in order to deal with opponents that have entrenched themselves for the purpose of defense. The railway gun is very good for that purpose. With the newest expansion By Blood Alone, the army corps menu has been updated with new parts to it, such as a system where you can recruit generals from your officers of your divisions instead of out of the blue. It also adds a ledger to see which divisions did noteworthy things to give them awards which gives bonuses to the division as long as the officer controls the division and isn't promoted. Overall this is the best HOI4 expansion to exist. Would recommend at any price, but as with most if not all paradox dlc, wait for a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
