\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI forgot about reviewing this DLC, but if you're interested in mods, but this. If not, buy it anyways or the game because chances are, you're too far deep to not want to.

Unfortunately, this is another dumpsterfire with the Soviet Union taking the stage as a shiny new rework. The branches feature a grand total of three communist branches, an unaligned branch, a fascist branch, but no democratic branch since Paradox forgot democracy existed or something. They also get a gimmicky paranoia system which forces you down a branch. This flimflam system right here needs some choice words Steam won't allow me to say, so use your imagination and remember this paranoia mechanic started the trend of forcing the player to do something the game tells them to instead of playing the game and picking whatever focus felt appropriate at the time. This is HOI4, a military sandbox, and Paradox just added a linear mechanic. Paranoia aside, the Soviets also get an economy branch which will get cancelled if at war with a major since just like in reality, it is illegal and impossible to finish a building constructed before a major war as this creates a powerful black hole. I truly commend Paradox for bravely implementing such a system.

There's also a foreign policy branch only the communists receive, propaganda only the communists receive since fascist propaganda obviously never happened.. dang it. If you don't choose Stalin and fail the coup from the right opposition, you get a mandatory civil war with terrible supply and long fighting. Paradox loves their civil wars, so here's a real example in today's HOI4 version:

I was playing as Finland to get my last two achievements. Suddenly, a civil war occurred, Stalin vs Trotsky. It was a final battle of annihilation. Stalin made a brave speech, convincing Soviet Estonia to white peace with Estonia, resulting in a north and south Estonia. Trotsky succeeded, promising a world revolution whilst Estonia would be forever decided because the civil war broke and current game mechanics force a minimum world tension on war declaration unless your country is fascist or communist and Soviet Estonia AI isn't interested in reunification. This happened until Latvia declared both Estonias as illegal and invaded them.

I think it speaks volumes to say HOI4 is a terrible game, but a great sandbox.\t\t\t\t\t
