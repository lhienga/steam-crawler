\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is hands down the best DLC. The tank designer allows for much more dynamic gameplay and custom making your own tanks is a ton of fun. The logistics rework is one of the best thing to ever happen to the game and now when you have no supply it's much easier to find out why and to fix the problem. The doctrines being changed to use XP instead of using up a research slot is a great change and should have been how the doctrines worked from day 1. The officer corps is a great addition to the game and provides an actual use to your command power. The cherry on top of these features are the outstanding focus trees added to the game. The Soviet focus tree is great and if RP is your thing it's probably the best in the game as you can choose to be offensive, defensive, keep socialism in one country as Stalin, spread the world revolution as Trotsky or just get rid of them both and restore the monarchy. Poland has imo one of the best focus trees in the game. You can restore The Commonwealth, make a union with Romania, join and then backstab the Germans, and my personal favorite put a bear in charge. The Baltic nations have good focuses like the alternate way to restore The Commonwealth with Lithuania, uniting with Finland as Estonia or creating Ostland as Latvia. These aren't as good as Poland and the Soviet Union imo but those are very high bars and these are the Baltic Nations so what do you expect. In general if you're new to the game and can only get one DLC get this one. It has the best new mechanics and it's focus trees are some of the best in the game.\t\t\t\t\t
