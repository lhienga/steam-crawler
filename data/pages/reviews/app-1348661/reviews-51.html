\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I'm still exploring this expansion this is a must get for anyone who truly enjoys hoi4 bringing a whole slew of amazing changes to the game. For this review I will run through them in a list.
Command Overhaul- The new system is superb. Its far easier to manage and allowws you to put your research slots to good use for other things. More importantly it allows for you to customize your army from special uprageds and unique traits of your army to doctrine to who you have in command. Its a Great rehaul and I look forward to exploring this more in depth.

Tank Design- The new version rocks. If you like Man the guns ship design you will love this. The tank design template synergizes well with the army rehaul allowing for you to now create the tank to support your infantry however odd in design it may look. Also thankfully the tank deisgner is far easier to use then the ship designer requiring less research to be able to build functioning tanks

Logistics- While I still have no clue what I am doing the new Logistics model is far more realistic and will now require skill and planning. For a strategy video game this cannot be welcomed enough as it add more depth and thought to each game.

Focus Trees- First out of the gate for this has to be the soviet Union. Did they deliver with the tree rework ? In short holy ♥♥♥♥ yes. The russian tree is easily the largest focus tree in game with more then 5 destinct leadership paths you can go down as well as a fully fleshed out naval, Cominturn, Industrial, Propoganda, Avaition and Army paths which will mean there will be tons of decisions to make in terms of how you wish to play each run through as well as the order in which you complete Focusses. Second we have the Polish focus tree. In short this is what always wanted from a polish focus tree. While it is very heavily monarchy foccussed the new tree allows you to quickly grow a solid industry as well as expand in territory. It also make the game far harder as the Cheeze you used tto be able to get away with has now been patched out. In short the New Polish tree gives you a strongr poland but also a harder run through. I personally Havent figured it out yet but im looking forward to cracking the code.
Lastly we have the baltic nations which you absolutley shouldnt sleep on. While they may be small and several parts of their focus trees are shared each one offers some rather interesting paths to take with some of them like Estonia being able to create truly powerful nations.

The new Railway cannon look beautful and bring great benifit to your armys and I look forward to using them more.

Lastly the Music for this DLC is on point once more. It has the Russian feel to it one would expect and each of the 4 tracks is very enjoyable to listen to.

Overall I would Highly recommend this DLC to all hoi4 Veterans even at the current 20 dollars but if thats to much for you grab it when it goes on sale. This is the first DLC I would really call a must have.\t\t\t\t\t

The overhauled supply system adds in an entirely new dynamic to the game where now you are not merely focused on encircling units and taking victory points, but also railways and supply hubs to cripple your enemy while also slowing down how fast you can go on the offense even if the enemy forgets to scorch earth. The new officer corps overhaul makes a lot more sense; now instead of Uruguay understanding the intricacies of using close air support in conjunction with ground units, you can only progress through your doctrines using army, navy, and air xp, meaning that you have to be at war before you know how to fight properly. The weather changes will make the Winter War a much less one sided fight, with severe attack penalties and defense buffs in deep snow combined with poor logistics making it a more historically accurate fight. Lastly, the new focus trees are absolutely insane for each nation, though truth be told the Soviets are the only one I actually enjoy playing as Poland and the Baltics are still balanced to be double teamed by the two most powerful nations on earth.
