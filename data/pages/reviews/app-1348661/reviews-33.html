\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like this DLC. It makes the gameplay much more realistic, and makes you face similar challenges to the actual armies regarding supply which makes the game more in depth and entertaining (you can really see this in the China war and North Africa especially, and when playing on the eastern front)

Several other updates makes the game play better, and the new focus trees are welcome.

There are bugs, performance and allot of balance issues however. Personally i think you should need more trucks to supply armies. I can supply 3 million men easily with only 3k trucks, which makes it a no-brainer to use trucks. IRL Germany had a tough time because it lacked enough trucks for supply, and the trucks used too much fuel. So some re-balancing could be good there (and in many other places esp regarding multy player).

Overall an extremely good DLC, hopefully the issues with performance and balance will be fixed soon.
