\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGoing to be mainly commenting on the actual update and the changes the Barbarossa update brought to HOI4.

So, I ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ love this update. Completely revamps the game in such a good way. You actually have to decide where to advance, it’s not just “paint line go brr” like it was previously. Playing as the UK and doing a successful D-Day, I then had to deal with a large German buildup on my front line. I brought in some tanks to cut off a railroad bringing supplies to the entire southern flank and to isolate Brest. It was so much ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fun. AI is much more competent too, I feel.

Getting to learn the new supply mechanics was fairly difficult, but I managed it in about 2 halfway-completed games.

Now to the actual DLC.

I love the ship designer from MtG, so while the Tank Designer is more complicated to get used to (components are unlocked not in tank research, but artillery research), it is very gratifying and allowed me to better design my tanks for the front.

Railway Guns are expensive, but as far as I can tell are fairly effective and are just cool as hell.

Focus trees are fun to play through, and I wish whatever focus design philosophy PDX has now would be applied to older DLCs, because IMO it’s great. (See Stellaris Custodian Initiative).

Overall? 8/10, counting the free content in the update. Definitely my favorite DLC and update for HOI4 ever.

Also, the music bangs.\t\t\t\t\t
