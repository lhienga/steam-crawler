\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo kick off the new release, I played as Japan and struck north into the Soviet Union. I immediately knew this update was something special when I cut off the Trans-Siberia Railway and watched the entire Russian Far East begin to crumble under low supply. Then, I played another game invading China instead. I found Chinese troops putting up a much better fight, and the best part was I could only go so far inland without my supply lines running out and putting my units in danger. These are factors that didn't exist before, and it's put a lovely breath of fresh air back into the game.

The tank designer and officer corps are both solid additions, great for roleplay and getting your army to fit a certain need. Tank designer especially has more depth than I was expecting with a lot of options for customizing your ideal design. Combined with the combat width overhaul, it leaves strategy much more open to experimentation than it's been in years.

My primary interest is seeing how mods will take these mechanics and run with them, but for those that enjoy their vanilla focus trees, these ones are pretty good, but I haven't played them much to say for sure. They're clearly well designed with tons of options to choose from. The only downside is that it makes the other trees, even relatively recent ones (China, Japan, Italy), pretty lackluster by comparson.

In any case this is a must-buy for any Hoi4 player, right up there with Man the Guns in my opinion. It's given the game new life for me.\t\t\t\t\t
