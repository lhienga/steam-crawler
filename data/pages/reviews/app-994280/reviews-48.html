\r\n\t\t\t\t\twhy am i recommending this game...
well it has some major points
*Combat is actually quite fun(keyboard is a bit more difficult than controller) 7/10
*Story is just ....wow it was surprisingly great(aside from some translation that doesn't make sense) 9.2/10
*crafting/farming is okayish(farming for materials is easy and somewhat fast,crafting for the first time can be
a bit different) crafting 5/10 farming 7/10
*stylish scenery(the entire game has a lot of just wow places) 9.9/10
*Character style(only main people can have different style clothes and weapons not alot of
options but hey it's something) 6/10
*Card game!(not as impressive as gwent"witcher 3" but i still spent a whole bunch of hours on it,takes a while to know how to play) 7/10
* own world with your won house that you can decorate(only your house) 6/10
*floor puzzles(is actually somewhat fun) 6/10

now for some disappointment
* fighting same monster just different abilities
*puzzles boxes(hintjust buy a sh1tton amount of seals from the grocery guy,you're going to need it ) 3/10
*Romance(ok going to be serious here, i don't think i had much influence over my romance
choice(i think i got the girl that i liked)) 4/10
*choices matter(once in a blue moon you get to make a choice choice,some minor changes to
the story,does changes some thing later near the game ending) 4/10
*ending(okay so you get a small unimpressive cut scene and a storybook to read at the end not very impressive,was expecting a scene with Yun but nah that wasn't happening not the best of ending) 4.5/10

Overall game is 6.5/10
Would play again, only for the scenery and the card game though\t\t\t\t\t
