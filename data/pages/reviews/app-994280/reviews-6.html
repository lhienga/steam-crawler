For example you can't say buy a level 20 weapon in the first couple areas when you're level 10 even through grinding; you get the level 20 weapon when the game says "okay here's a level 20 area, here's gear to scale you up".

Minigames come and go and are only there for a specific area, and there's no depth to any system in the game, not even the combat.
