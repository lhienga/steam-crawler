\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far the most British fantasy RPG you'll ever play. It also happens to be extremely fun. I honestly can't tell if this game is trying to take itself seriously or not. But I have loved this game since it came out on the original X-Box. Directed by the legendary king of false promises, and exaggerated claims himself, Peter Moleneux. A very unique, and anachronistic fantasy setting that at the time was revolutionary in it's depth and systems. One of the few games that has a soundtrack made by Danny Elfman. And you can definitely tell it's him. This is an updated version of the game. The textures, and graphic systems have been upgraded, but the overall gameplay has been untouched. You can still completely cheese this game in a matter of a few hours.\t\t\t\t\t
