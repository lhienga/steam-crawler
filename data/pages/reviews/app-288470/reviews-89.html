\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably one of the best games I've ever played in my life. 18 years later and I still come back to it frequently. The updated graphics make it very worth playing on any HDTV or monitor. The extra quests and new areas (compared to the original) are a great addition. And, let's not forget all the new awesome items. I honestly can't think of anything I dislike about the game except I have a tendency to crash the game from time to time when I move from one area to another too quickly; and I'm sure that's just my computer being a glitch.
In many ways, it's a very basic RPG that doesn't have any real grinding requirements to get somewhere. The quests are typically very straightforward and easy to accomplish with a little bit of work. The updated visuals and neutrality of the game itself makes being good or evil equally available without any real punishment for choosing one or the other. If you enjoy a good story with a minimum of grinding, this is a great game. If you're new to the genre, it's a great way to break into story-based gaming in general. No complicated button combos or tedious running around (unless you just enjoy taking down every villager and merchant to cross your path). I can't say enough good things about this game. But, as a child of the 80's, it may come off as "old" to some people. Great for beginners or old schoolers, or just a way to kill time for those dabblers out there.\t\t\t\t\t
