\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you grew up playing the original, you'll love to revisit it now. The graphics are updated and mostly look far superior although I will say the character models themselves look pretty horrendous. This game was pioneer of so many awesome features, some that still aren't regularly employed today. For example, your character's body and appearance morphs depending on your choices. If you invest in Strength, you'll get beefier and grow hair. If you invest in Skill you'll grow taller, and if you invest in Will (magic) you get cool tattoos. If you are good, you get a halo and blonde hair but if you are bad you lose your hair slowly and get horns. The armor and clothing also adapts to these changes which is incredible from a game made back in 2004. Highly recommend this game for that along with the awesome story, the fun, engaging combat, and especially the British humor.\t\t\t\t\t
