The keyboard/mouse adaptation is abyssmal, you can tell this game was designed with a gamepad in mind and they made very little effort to make it playable without one. The mouse movement is sluggish and feels like you're playing at 10fps, precise mouse movements are almost impossible. There's also basically no graphical options that actually matter, like framerate or again, cursor speed. That all aside, it's still a better game than fable 2 & 3, even if it looks worse. The story is super cheesy but at least if you want to play as an evil character you get some really evil choices and npcs will constantly remind you of them. All in all I'd recommend this game to anyone who hasn't played it and especially to people who liked fable 2 or 3 (if any exist).
Overall 6/10 good game, terrible port.\t\t\t\t\t
