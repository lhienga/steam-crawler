\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdate: I've played more, and it's not that bad! I like it much more than the original. The bugs in shopping menus are manageable. I'll leave the original review below though.

---

I played the original game a lot when I was younger, and I even have the non-Anniversary edition, however, this particular edition of the game, while it's visually better, it's a worse experience in comparison to original version.

There are a couple of bugs that makes the playthrough annoying like trader's GUI gets broken sometimes where the control somehow gets sticky and moves right or down indefinitely until you quit to main menu and go back again. In some places the event interactions aren't being triggered correctly (in White Balverine you have to approach more righter side or else second attack won't get triggered).

Menus while look nice, they are filled with unnecessary animations, because of that it's slower to navigate, and there are too many categories in which items get filtered.

I'm going to finish this game regardless, but I think for better experience it's worth buying the original version (Fable - The Lost Chapters) rather than this remaster.\t\t\t\t\t
