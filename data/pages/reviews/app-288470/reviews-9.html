\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoved Fable as a kid and this game is good if you can play it without issue, despite the MC having the facial expression of an oaf the whole game.

The main issue is that several players including myself struggled using the level up menu as the game would crash upon entering the skill/experience pedestal necessary to level up (also necessary to progress through the game whatsoever when you are at the end of the initial guild/tutorial experience, making it impossible to play this game even if you cheat/play non heroic with walkthrough/etc just to bypass the need to level up). When the game crashes due to this issue, sometimes it will give you a "Pure virtual function being called while application was running (GIsRunning == 1)." error message, sometimes you'll get no error message at all.

If you check online or search the steam forums regarding the issue above (both with error message or without), you will be met with a million different TS steps to take to resolve the issue, ranging from deleting the exp_spend movies from the files for the game, all the way to setting vertical sync to fast in the NVidia control panel for the game. Following the steps that were posted by Hohbes (community manager) or following steps posted by anyone else may resolve the issue, or it may not.

I was only able to progress due to pure luck as far as I can tell, because the next time I fired the game up to play and tried to spend EXP, it crashed despite doing exactly what I did the day earlier (setting Fable Anniversary exe compatibility to windows 8>disabling antialiasing - FXAA in NVidia control panel>setting Vertical Sync to fast in NVidia control panel>deleting exp_spend movies in Fable Anniversary/WellingtonGame/Movies folder>running the game as administrator>and lastly enabling Vsync in the ingame settings>

Regardless it took me almost 2 hours to reach the point where I experienced the issue initially, meaning that if you go AFK or take your time at all you may not be eligible for a refund once you experience the potentially game breaking bug.

Oh, and the issue occurs on the steam deck as well, giving the same error message I listed prior.

As this issue has apparently been ongoing since the game release, I would not expect it to ever get fixed.

With this issue in mind, I have to recommend playing Fable: The Lost Chapters instead as when I played that a few years ago I had no issues with that game.\t\t\t\t\t
