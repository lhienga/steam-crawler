\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI tried really hard to like this game. I get it. It's old. It's an xbox game remastered for Xbox 360. How could I really criticize it when it's so ancient? Well like this. There's fun to be had in this game. It's there. The world is cool, the humor, the freedom in choices that range from play style to deciding who lives and who dies. That fun is there. It's just unfortunately buried under a mound of frustration. Everything is such an annoying task to do, if it even works at all. There's bugs and glitches that literally forced me to load old saves to restart quest in order to complete them and some non quest bugs that just couldn't be fixed. The controls are awful and unintuitive, or at least on controller. There's an alternate option for controllers that fixes some of the issues but then causes others. The UI is bad too. It's probably because its age but its is an exercise in futility to try and find stuff in the menus or attempt to use the god awful map to figure out which is the best teleport to go to in order to be closest to where you are trying to go. I think this game needed more than just a graphical upgrade from its original. It looks great for the time but some quality of life improvements and quality control would have gone a very long way. I kept trying to play and like this game because I could see the fun there but at the end I was just overwhelmed with the frustration.\t\t\t\t\t
