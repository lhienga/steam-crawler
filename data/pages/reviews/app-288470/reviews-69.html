You can manipulate the market on health potions to make a massive profit. Buy up and improve property to make passive income. You can even grow horns and attract flies after slaughtering an entire community just so that you can buy the no longer owned houses and own all of Albion.

Or maybe you're the Tofu eating holier than though type, complete with angelic music, rays of light from heaving shining down on you and a golden halo.
