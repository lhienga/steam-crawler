\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDO NOT BUY THIS GAME ON PC!

Back in the day, when this Fable first released I was flooded by my imagination. I fell in love with medieval fantasy, pure vs corrupt, magic and assassins. The story, gameplay, characters, and audio were and are brilliant. It was a great time back on the original Xbox.

That said, this is a horrible game on PC. This is the worst port I have endured. The game does not want to open; but, if you can get it to do so after many attempts it will load at an extremely slow rate. The frame rate, for how pitiful it is, is all over the place. If I had epilepsy I'm certain I'd have a seizure from the graphical issues. The game does not want to respond to controls, will lock out options in the menu at random, and is prone to freezing and crashing. This being played on a modern gaming rig which has had no issues with any other game (except Elden Ring).

The developer seems to have long abandoned this game and have made no effort to resolve the issues that so many are having, as can be seen in the Community Hub Discussions. People instead coming up with wonky workarounds to try to get it to work.\t\t\t\t\t
