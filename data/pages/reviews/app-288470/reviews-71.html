It makes no sense how a game this old can get a "Anniversary" edition and still be in this state but I guess I expected to much. One bug I had made it so I was trapped in a loop quest and was unable to get out of because of an ill timed save I made. Reminds me of RES 4 if you miss one part you can never 100 % the game. So in closing my disappointment is immeasurable and my day is ruined.\t\t\t\t\t
