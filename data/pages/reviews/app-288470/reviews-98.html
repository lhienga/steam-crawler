\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnyone who tells you Fable still holds up is blinded by nostalgia.
Anyone who tells you it was a good game for its time is Peter Molyneux, ergo a compulsive liar.

Fable isn't good. For its time it was ugly, sparse and built on ridiculous promises.

The combat is terrible its slow, clunky and worst of all BORING. There is no challenge. Just mash M1 and occasionally hit F1/F2 to drink a poition.
It reminds me of Diablo, or Borderlands. But those games offset the boring combat by having tonnes of loot and hype abilities to use. Fable, even when you start getting high tier magic spells just never hits any kind of high note.

The story is laughable.
There is zero lore or world building. Everyone is a one note cliche with horrendous voice acting.

The games NPCs and karma system are a joke. At best its an over promise at worse an outright lie.
NPCs are entirely generic with exactly ZERO memorable characteristics so the game can just spawn replacements for them whenever it needs.
Morality is also a joke, either your a good guy in which case you can get access to all the interactions. The marriage system, recruiting random people as followers, getting any interaction out of an NPC that isn't them cowering in fear really.
If your evil then get ♥♥♥♥♥♥, you can't even get hair cuts after a certain point cause your character starts going balled. All the interactions you try will be ignored or responded to with the same stupid cowering animation and sound, you can't even customise your tattoos because your skin turns so ashen you can't see them.

About the only positive thing I have to say about Fable is that when NPCs open a shop they actually go and fill the shops displays with items.
But that only happens if you stole from the displays the previous day, and just like every other RPG once your past the front door everything is free for the taking with no redundant security whatsoever.

Everything in this game is just bad, it really can only be enjoyed ironically and probably with a few pints in you.
Once I sober up I don't think I'll touch it again. Maybe just to get the rest of the achievements.\t\t\t\t\t
