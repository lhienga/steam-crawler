\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing game when it came out, Anniversary edition keeps everything that was amazing while adding great new visuals and end game content. The world is fun to explore and discover new places, the soundtrack is absolutely perfect capturing the world whether you're in the Guild, out in the forest, or in the terrifying dark and spooky settings. Combat is simple, easy to learn and extremely fun. The meaty sound of hitting enemies with longswords or great axes never gets old, and the spells are all interesting and offer amazing variety in battle. The story is easy to follow and doesn't do anything crazy, but it keeps you interested and doesn't overwhelm you.
I must emphasize that this is a very old game with a fresh sheet of paint, so it is very similar to Oblivion or Halo Combat Evolved, in the sense that there's a pile of gold under some early 2000's jank, but like those 2 titles, it's more a charm than a negative most times. The lock on system isn't perfect but combat feels honestly better without it, especially against groups of enemies when you want to switch who you're hitting frequently.
I've got every achievement, beaten it without dying once, collected every unique weapon and armor set, and I've had barrels of fun the whole way through. While the beginning tutorial may be slow and boring admittedly, I would absolutely always recommend this game to any fan of open world RPGs, collecting loot, and wanting to go to a wonderful yet dark fantasy adventure. The game really picks up when you start venturing out of the Guild and Bowerstone and into the forests, and especially Blackmarsh will give you a feel of what this has to offer.
To anyone wondering if they should pick this title up, it is absolutely worth ANY price, even off sale, and is a MUST PLAY. At the very least, everyone has to give it a try for under 2 hours, and refund it if it's not your speed...... but I guarantee it will be!
10/10\t\t\t\t\t
