\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a fantastic action RPG!

I never played the Fable games growing up, so I don't have the benefit of nostalgia-but I can safely say this is one of the best games I've ever played. I'm so glad I gave it a chance all these years later. I was prompted by a Steam sale to buy it, as well as all the DLC, for a cheap price but I whole heartedly would have paid full price for it too.

The whimsical, charming, vibrant art style; the gorgeous soundtrack; fun combat, magic, and levelling; a great story; interesting items (and funny ones too) and characters; atmospheric locales; an astonishing level of detail in the lore, gameplay, and interactions; this game literally has everything you could ever want to have a wonderful time.

I adore RPGs like KOTOR I and II and The Elder Scrolls series so I wasn't sure if I'd like this game in terms of how "lightly" it seems to take itself. But make no mistake: this game gets dark at times, bleak even and for all it's storybook appearance, it's every bit as a "serious" RPG as anything else you'll play.

There's tons of replay value here. I just know I'm going to get lost in Albion's endearing world lots more...\t\t\t\t\t
