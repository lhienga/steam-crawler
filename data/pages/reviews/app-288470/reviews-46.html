The game's mechanics are smooth and intuitive, making it easy to get into the game and start playing right away. The game's graphics have been updated to HD, making the world of Albion look more beautiful than ever. The game's AI is also decent, providing a good level of challenge and engagement.

However, the game doesn't bring many new features or contents compared to the original version. It mainly focuses on the remastering the graphics, and the gameplay remains very similar. The game's combat system, while fun, can feel a bit repetitive after a while. Additionally, some of the game's side quests can feel a bit lackluster and lack depth. The game's modding community is also not as active as other games on steam.
