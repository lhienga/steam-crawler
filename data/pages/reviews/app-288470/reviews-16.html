\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFable TLC was the first RPG I ever played, back in my teenage years I remember having so much fun with the tons of options, emotes and choices this game has, the limited but diverse collection of spells and cartoonish characters. Having played the remastered I can say that I was able to relive that experience.

Fable is very well balanced, there's no need for grinding and you can switch from being a warrior to a wizard any time without much difficulty, the Heroic mode is definitely manageable and while just a few features are lost it's pretty fun. There might not be a thousand quests but that makes most of them much more interesting and different from each other. This isn't a 400 hours game and there is not much to do on the endgame, but your journey will be filled with tons of secrets and decisions from the very beginning to the Heroic end.

It's a classic beloved by many and a definitive must play, maybe a bit outdated but I don't think the Anniversary edition has aged that bad.\t\t\t\t\t
