\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are six reasons you buy and play a DD game. You love TD, RPG, loot farming, burning your life away, forgetting what the outside world looks like, doing the same thing over and over again, and have a tendency towards masochism. Every actual DD fan cannot deny the prior statement is true of themselves, and they know it. Moving on.

What is actually wrong here:
Content; believe it or not after 2+ years in "alpha" dev, the amount of content is sorely lacking. Namely endgame.
Microtransactions; exclusively costumes are pay only, while new heroes/heroines are either 20$ish or replaying the same maps hundreds of times.
Community; there are generally two types of players, those that have no clue what's going on, and those that are farmed and know what's going on. The two don't interact much and if they do the results are often less than fun.

It's not a new story for the series but it's reached thinly vieled to in your face bad, at least for me. I can't expect Trendy to make any different decisions at this point and I can no longer support their business model.

So, if you either have a deep pocket, or lots of time on your hands, I recomend you skip this one.\t\t\t\t\t
