\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI remember endless hours of fun playing Dungeon Defenders as a kid, and I distinctly remember DD2 being a massive disappointment.
For a number of years, Trendy completely abandoned the game, I came back to try it out again since there was new content added, and quickly I was reminded why I quit this horrid thing. Microtransactions.

Dungeon Defenders 2 is riddled with MTX, everything from power to cosmetics can be bought with premium currency, and they make this very apparent that you're suppose to be spending your money on it with how insane prices are for some things without giving them your credit card.

I don't know if they'll ever change things in the future, but as-is I'm just not interested in playing this anymore, and they have had years to fix it so I have zero hope things will change for the better.

Gameplay
It plays just like the original from what I can remember.

Visuals
Nothing unique, very generic and boring to look at.
Infinitely less appealing of an artstyle than the original.

Sound
Will put you to sleep with how soft and unrewarding the sound is.\t\t\t\t\t
