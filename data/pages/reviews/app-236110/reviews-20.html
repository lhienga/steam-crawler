\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt it's core the game is good, unfortunately since the alpha it's been in a steep decline, and seemingly has no hope of rising up to what it could have been.

To start with the item system is a complete mess. Every major update has completely reworked the entire system making players have to relearn how it works every few months, which is boring and takes too long compared to the benefits learning how to build you characters would give.

Speaking of characters, while the pool is fairly small each one (save for the adept and the initiate) are all unique and have strenghts and weaknesses that compliment each other, but unless you play solo there's no real reason to have multiple characters. All you need is one character who you just dump all your good gear to progress to harder difficulties.

Finally the playerbase is nearly noexistant and finding games is a joke. When the game first launched on steam there were several thousand concurrent players, and while the severs were a mess finding a full match of 3 others was easy and usually only took a few seconds. Nowadays the playerbase struggles to pass 1k, and unless you already have a squad good luck finding randoms to play with. There's no simple matchmaking. Everything is split up by level and difficulty and you have to manual serach for a room for each individual map and each difficulty. You can't combine modes, maps or even difficulty when seraching for rooms, which only serves to frustrate when playing alone.\t\t\t\t\t
