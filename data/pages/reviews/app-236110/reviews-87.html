\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Stunning graphics
- Completely online so I'm going to guess that it's a bit harder to hack than DD1
- Loot is per user, not free-for-all.
- Not able to sell other people's defenses.

Cons:
- Combat is a bit slower paced (but not as bad as DD: Eternity. That game was garbage)
- Maps are a bit more condensed compared to DD1.
- Combat is.. guided. (you can swing on the knight and kind of aim where you want to swing, but any time you start swinging it aims you towards a specific target. Don't get me wrong, hitting multiple enemies is still possible. It's just when you want to swing at a larger enemy that's hitting you, you're stuck focusing each one of your swings on a smaller one due to its guided system)

Overall:

Though it doesn't feel exactly like the first one did, it is quite a remarkable release. Well polished compared to most of the other early access games out there. I'd wait for the price to drop below $15 however, as it is only early access. Not sure if what is in so far is worth the current amount of $25. Regardless, Highly recommended either now or at launch! Devs doin' good.\t\t\t\t\t

####UPDATE####6/25/2015
---After thinking about it. I will say the game is good. But I miss DD1 art style, this new style seems isnt my cup of tea. But my biggest gripe with this game is this assigned builder nonsense. Every game I join or create everyone teels me how they can build the map better map. While it does require teamwork I think we can all agree that online players are usually jerks. And the game falls apart soon after that.
On the bright side when you get the right team mates who are intellegent and polite the game can be very fun.
Overall the game is really different and that isn't a terrible thing. But if you loved DD1 this is quite a step in a different direction. Hopefully the game will reach a more complete stride. But as of now the game falls short of what I believe the Devs are trying to reach.

####UPDATE 01/01/2016####
After coming back to the game, I have changed my review;
Over several patcher the Devs have fixed a few major gripes I had with the game;
-Party leaders don't really matter again, anyone can start the mission from the tavern (this was really annoying because of people who would afk).
- Stats matter again, each time I leveled up I really felt like they made a diference in whatever attribute I placed them in.
- The pet hatching system is now INFINITELY better, with no 12 hour wait time for it to hatch. Solid work guys.

There is still a ton of things I missed overall the game feels smooth and it seems to be growing into it's shoes very well.
This new experince has left me hopefull for the future of this game.
