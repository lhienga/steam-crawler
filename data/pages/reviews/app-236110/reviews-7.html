\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me preface this by saying, I originally bought this game when it was able to be purchased ($25 USD - May 3rd, 2015).

It felt quite good, the game was challenging but not overbearing. Abilities were part of the core-loop unlike DD1, and you had to use them if you wanted to survive.

The servers were absolute hell at first, but they figured it out.

The maps were new, and fun. Especially the in-map traps, they were extremely useful and enjoyable to use.

It was almost as if you got into a groove, springing around the map completing the tasks at hand to assure your victory.

Sadly, this wasn't to last. Trendy went a different route, one that I couldn't support.

Over time the game slowly degraded into what it is now, an extremely easy, grind heavy game, with nothing to offer other than the grind.

It truly saddens me, hopefully Awakened can pull the slack that this game has given. Only time will tell, good luck Awakened, and the team at Chromatic Games.

I haven't truly played this game since it's early days, only sporadically since. Most likely, I'll never launch in again, so.

Make us all proud, and make something worthy of your player-base.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best tower defense looting game.
If you like to play along your defense with your characters and the never ending farm/power growth, this game is for you.
Level, gears, enchant, ascension, reset,pets it have it all (it even had skins with level...).
There are a lot of various mods, some endless, some hardcore and "fair" (at least when it was released...) for ladder, no pvp thought.
Several classes, a quick and nice campaign, you can even trade and play with others.

So, why would I not recommend this game?

The devs are not respectful of the time players spend on their game. Before even releasing it, they did at least 4 hard wipes, 3 of which after they said no more hard wipe. After that, they learned their lessons and started doing soft wipe after every big update. Gears or features which took players a lot of time to get and have an edge on the other were either rendered useless or brought back to lower levels.
A lot of going back and forth between different aspects of the game (Hi orbs!) during updates lost a lot of time for everyone (but never attribute to malice that which is adequately explained by stupidity, we will go on about malice just after).
Now the game is mostly dead as no meaningfull updates are done.

As for myself I'm done with Trendy (the company behind this game) which I can no longer advocate. (But don't take my words for granted and google trendy lawsuit against ark for instance) .
I can't no longer count the amount of lies they gave to the community (check who's behind dungeon defender awakened).

So yeah, I had a lot of fun, sleepless night and pain but no more DD2. You should consider something else if you're looking for THE game to put a massive amount of your time into. If you wish to try it, eh why not...it's free anyways as trendy said it would not...

On a side note: I'm looking for fellow council defender members to know what happend in the end? I guess warframe founder pack had a better lifetime.

TL;DR : Good game, Bad company ruined it, and will do it all over again elsewhere.
