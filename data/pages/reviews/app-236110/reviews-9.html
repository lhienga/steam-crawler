First, they made the game much more complicated but failed to create any real documentation. Players are left to search the internet for information on character abilities and towers. Information that should be available in game. Some of the programming choices are ridicules. Example: At chaos 5 you get a mob that is invisible until it attacks, harasses you relentlessly, and serves no purpose other than to screw you over. We call it the griefers, or the anti-fun mob. Most gaming companies work hard to keep griefers from ruining the game experience for their customers. This company programs them into the game. Last of all, this game really needs someone to do some good videos. Yes, I know there is someone out there with probably 500 videos, but most are useless for new players. I mean, really, you start a series for new players and claim to be using a secondary account with no benefits, then do your first ancient power reset and we see that the character is getting 900 ascension point? A newbie series using a character with 900 ascension points? The game desperately needs someone to do a series of instructional videos on a normal account.\t\t\t\t\t

The game never gets stale, in that it actually gets extremely stale when you hit level 50 and need to start the tedious item level grind for about 5 tiers.
