\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a more modern take on DD1 (the first game in the series). A tower defence game where you have a Build Phase to plan & place your defences using a dozen Heroes (with mostly unique towers and abilities) then use those same heroes to fight alongside your towers during the Combat Phase. Grab loot afterwards to upgrade your towers / hero and repeat.

It's a free to play game where you can earn (grind) everything in the game except for some cosmetics. The grind is kinda long but not too bad. It's about a week to earn enough to buy a new Hero. 100% pay to skip at worst, no pay to win. You can also trade with other players using ingame currency (like most F2P games, there are multiple currencies, somewhat predatory "premium" currency packs/etc but it's never too egregious) BUT the grind can get quite arduous in 'end-game'.

That said, while other games, you have this '30 seconds of fun' that's looped? Here it's a 'plan and build', fight alongside your defences, collect your rewards then gear up for the next run. It's a '20 minutes of fun' that's looped.

If you like a tower defence game with very strong RPG elements and lots of action, complete with a stable of very unique heroes (flying witch? woodsy dryad? studious mage? spiritual monk? noble knight? sinister necro? and much more)

-----

The game is server-hosted. This prevents almost all hacking but means you can suffer from lag\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSadly, It's a game with bugs and issues that have never been corrected and fixed. After 750+ hours, I've never reach that end game and I've done nothing but grind and spend money to try and help that go faster. From maxing out your shards, to mods and gear, I never could progress.

Dungeon Defenders has always been a fun tower defense game with a ton of depth and interesting aspects to it with all the classes you can choose from. A large map pool and support for the game years after launch.

The problem comes for lack of guidance and being able to understand how things work. You'll have to seek information online, from user made guides to videos.

But even after all the work you put into the game, You're left with game breaking bugs that have never been fixed. I can build towers and for no reason at all they will just stop firing for a extended period of time and start again after it's too late, You lose now!

Being a Free to Play game, There are a lot of gates and restrictions new players are faced with, which is expected for this sort of game. Heavily limited bag slots, bank slots etc. So, to max out a class without at least spending some money you will have a very hard time.
However I'd recommend spending some money if you can put up with the game breaking bugs. If you can find fun in it, because that will make the long long..... long grind easier for you.

For my time and money spent, I have very little to show for it. You rely on RNG drops and rolls at the end of the day. You may get lucky or you maybe like me and can sink thousands of hours into it with nothing to show.
I have no doubt some people have put half the time I have in this game and can easily do end game content. But I know for me when or if I hit 2,000 hours I'll likely be in the same spot I am now and have been for the last 700.

If you like casual fun and have no interest in doing end game content or maxing out your classes you will find fun here.
But with the major game breaking issues like I see using the Elvish arty cannon's. I can't recommend the game to others that will likely see many other bugs that hurt the overall experience.\t\t\t\t\t
