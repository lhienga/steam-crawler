\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought that game before it was free to play and I had a lot of fun back then but the game was not complete. I waited a few years for them to finish the game and polish it a little bit. Lately, I came back to it and I had a lot of fun going through all the Chaos Level. Now that I am at chaos level 7 and the game becomes all about grinding... This is not a problem since I have a lot of fun but I have that annoying bug where I can't have the score menu at the end game ...

If you don't know, you need that menu to replay the map, go to a random map or back at your tavern... At the end of a map, I have to wait for open-chest animation because otherwise, it doesn't auto-loot the chest since the menu doesn't show. Then I can push G to auto-loot the stuff on the ground then I have to wait 40 seconds for the server to realize I am not going to choose an option, then I am teleported at the town, which makes even more loading time. Then I have to choose the map I want to play and I have to wait for another loading. I can finally play my map. ( Even describing this bug feels over annoying. )

Because of this bug, I Can't get the 4 win strick bonus that helps for the grinding... Most of the time I don't even get 1 shard for the Chaos level I am trying to farm ... It makes it impossible for me to grind what I need for end game... Also due to this bug, I can't make the challenges map. It seems like the menu confirms you did the 5-stars challenge without cheating or something like that so I have to forget about that part of the game too ...

I open a bug report and I post a video to prove I have the issue and after 10 days, my bug is still in review... 10 days and no tech or programmes or even an automatic message to say: hey we are looking into your issue... or: hey we are in short staff due to covid-19 but we don't forget you... When I look at another bug report with what seems the same issue, the only comment from the company is:
"Thanks for your bug report! We've investigated your report and followed your instructions, but we were unable to reproduce the bug on our end." ...

Then a long text about why this is so important for them to reproduce the bug if they want to fix it... I don't see any effort to fix the bug or try to reproduce it... They don't ask for a log, they don't ask about hardware, the OS Version or even if the user is on wireless ... They even ask the guy to open another bug report if he has the problem again what mean to me they close the ticket... They just don't care...

So base on my experience, this is a good free to play game until you hit the grinding portion of it. Don't spend any money on it and if you have any bug, just walk away... there is no point to ask for support.\t\t\t\t\t
