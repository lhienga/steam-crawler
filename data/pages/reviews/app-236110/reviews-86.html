Watching gameplay and dev streams about this game made me angry. All I wanted was DD1 with more content. However this is incredibly different from DD1. How the game feels and plays, level strategies, gear/money grinding, etc. It's all different. But I am here to tell you that it's not a bad thing! DD2 has a unique feel to it and from the moment you set up your first tower, you're pulled in. It's fun to play, easy to learn and a blast playing with friends.

Obviously it has flaws, I mean.. It's still in pre-alpha.

My advice is to go play your hearts content of DD1 and come back when this game is further along OR come join me in playing, learning and watching this game grow!
