Great sequel that is shaping to be as awesome as the first. Also, the F2P model should (in theory) be a better platform for this type of game, as the (overwhelming) DLC in DD1 fractured the community, which should not occur in DD2 as long as updates stay free and only cosmetics cost money.
    Pros:
  • Completely F2P - no P2W here
  • Great artstyle & fun gameplay
  • Player hub redesign greatly improved form previous versions
  • Holiday events are frequent
    Cons:
  • Store items are very expensive
  • Some modes are very grindy / boring (Onslaught)
  • Does not support all resolutions (no native support for 2560x1600)
  • Inviting friends to games sometimes gives an error (happens too frequently)
I can easily recommend DD2, but instead of taking my word for it - download and jump in game. Its free, so give it a shot!\t\t\t\t\t
