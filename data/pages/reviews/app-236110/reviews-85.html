\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my review when I had first pre-ordered the game on early access. Below that, is my current review (as of Release, earlier this week.)

The game is in pre alpha, but feels like a full release. This game keeps getting better too. There will be some glitches, that's why it's still pre alpha. The game's core mechanics are included, and there's more on the way. If you like action games or tower defense games, this is for you.



-----------------------------------------------------------

Do I recomend the game? Yes.
Do I recomend paying for the game? No.


The dev's can't seem to pick a direction for statistics, map selection, UI, Hubs, Markets, basically anything. It seems like every major update has a major overhaul forcing you to relearn the game. Honestly, based on what I'm seeing, I suspect Trendy is struggling.

Pros:
- NOT pay to win.
- Entertaining, if simple
- Free to play

Cons:
- Half assed UI.
- Classes change every month or two.
- Stuck on a loading screen.
- Get a good piece of gear? Wait a month. They'll rework it. It'll be vendor trash.
- Stuck on a loading screen.
- Don't report bugs using Steam forums. The top post says they don't check it.
- Stuck on a loading screen.
- We finally got tower skins. Chromatic shifting towers? That's it?
- What happened to that cool star wars gear we were promised at X number of subscribers?
- Stuck on a loading screen.
- Toxic Player base.
- No ignore function.
- Stuck on a loading screen.
- Impossible to tell whether a piece of gear is good or not. It shows as uncommon in your bags, but equip it, and it's suddenly legendary. Unequip it, and it's Uncommon again.
- RNG character upgrades, that only drop on early end-game content. Yes, You'll be going back to carry people. Regularly.
- Stuck on a loading screen.
- Bland UI
- UI is boxy.
- stuck on loading screen.\t\t\t\t\t
