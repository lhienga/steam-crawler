\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStill a game (like back in 2015) that you really want to love.
But it seems like it does everything to prevent you from doing so.

First the positive Things:
- after 7 years there are still patches and even new heroes
- lovely character- and leveldesign
- interesting ideas for towers (theoretical)
- enjoyable in multi- and singleplayer
- there are elementary combinations between different towers

The bad / annoying Things:
- 90% of the towers (and some may say most heroes) are useless in later chaoslevels
- ancient power reset seems to be mandatory for progress in onslaught and Chaos 9-10
- one-dimensional progress
- very grindy (feels like continental drift-speed sometimes)
- to much randomness in upgrading-process of mods
- the gap between trash und useable drops is way to large
- no variety in needed heroes (just count the monks on public maps )
- too much CC immune enemies on Chaos 9-10 (bye bye my lovely poison-shatter build :( )
- no fluid progress between chaos-levels - every next difficulty-level hits you like a truck

Conclusion
Still a 4 or maybe 5 / 10.
Would have been a neutral vote if allowed, but Chaos 9 and 10 annoyed me enough to be negative. Most strangely the old problems back from the Nightmare-era (until 2017, I guess) are still there.\t\t\t\t\t

Yes it's not exactly like the original, but thats the good thing about it, who would want an exact clone of DD1 after all this time? Now I am not saying that DD1 is bad, It's brilliant, but personally I think DD2 shines where DD1 failed to do so.

Yes it is a bit cheesy that they are selling the same charachters again for dlc, but remember, it IS free to play.

If you have played this game when it was pay to play, let me reasure you that it is almost a different game, everything is alot smoother and flows better.

One of the only things I am dissapointed in is the lack of upgrading items and pets at low level, which in my opinion let players get used to it before choosing a pet was the most important thing in the game.

This game is definantly worth getting since it is an amazing game, if i were to put a price on it, I would say around $15-$20 AUD. but since its free there is no reason not to give this a spin.\t\t\t\t\t
