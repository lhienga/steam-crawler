Just played a bit more - there might be specific weapons that are causing the AI to be worse like I mentioned above, whereas other weapon combos they behave better.

Played even more - Once you create a titan a lot of the 'problems' go away to be honest.

There is one 'bug'? The order in which parts are showing up in the head, body, arms, legs section used to be sorted highest mana cost to lowest mana cost I believe, now I'm not sure how they're sorted. The section with everything I think is sorted that way, but the individual parts sections don't appear to be, which makes selling parts kind of annoying. Adding the ability to sort parts by mana cost, speed, HP, etc would be ideal and needs it badly. Or an auto-sell parts where you can select "All Skeletons, Orcs" etc would be a super cool base upgrade.

Overall, very good fun game, pacing of upgrades seems like it'll take a while to get through. There's an upgrade in your base that is 150000 gold so...\t\t\t\t\t
