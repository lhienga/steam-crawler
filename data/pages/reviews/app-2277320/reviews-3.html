\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNecrosmith 2 has a lot of major flaws. It feels like you're playing a worse version of the first game with some strange mods installed. Maybe it was released too early? I don't know. I've beaten the game in almost 14 h, and don't get me wrong, I've had some fun, but most of it was pain in the ass.

My main problems with this game right now:
- Don't believe all those reviews, that say that this game is "hard" or it requires a lot of "grind". All you need is luck. Map generator sometimes will spawn all the body parts that you need, and sometimes you will get just junk, and you need to base your creations on parts from buildings. All you need to beat map 2 are dragons and on map 3 mechanical dragons.
- Spells are useless. I never felt any pressure to use them. Don't even bother to upgrade them in the tower. On my final run on map 3, I didn't use even a single one
- Maybe 6 artifacts from all of them are useful. Most of them are just fillers.
- There are 2 types of bosses: 1. They die in 5-10 seconds (like the final boss on map 3), or they kill your entire squad in 5-10 sec (3-4 semi-bosses on map 2).
- AI of player-controlled units could be better. Sometimes after 5 seconds from spawning, they will just walk into enemies, and die.
- Titans are a solid idea, but they are useful for only one task: Defense. They have powerful attacks at the beginning of a run, but they are too slow to use them to destroy lairs, and they lack damage in late-game when you have dragons/mecha-dragons.
- Why is this game even divided into 3 maps? Just create 1 map, that scales better. In the late game, you have a lot of extremely fast units, and you can easily uncover the ENTIRE map after 20-30 mins of playtime.

I still believe that this game will become better shortly because it has a huge potential, that cannot be wasted.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't hate this game, but the first game was simply better. It was more casual, more fun, less grindy, less expensive.
This game tries to be bigger and more strategic, which is great news, but fails miserably at it:
- you can now specify a role for your minions, which was direly needed in the first game; unfortunately the options available are incredibly limited and not customizable, might as well not be there. Cannot select to claim buildings, cannot select a defense range, cannot specify to move as a group, etc..
- Maps are bigger, but movement speed is...lower?! Minions spend tons of hours collecting resources, 1 by 1, forever
- Titans are cool new units, but the enemy uses them against you, and you lose in 30 seconds if not prepared (hello second level, I'm looking at you)
- Still no filters nor sorting for body parts: when you have dozens of them is difficult to select the best ones for a specific role
- AI is even more dumb than in the first game: minions will not kite enemies any more, they often runs straight at the enemies and get instakilled, objective prioritization feels random at best
- there are more maps, but the difficulty spikes way too hard, and requires hours of grinding to be able to play in the next map\t\t\t\t\t
