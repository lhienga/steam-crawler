\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove the game from Necrosmith1.
I've finished all achievements from Necrosmith1, revealed 99% of the recipes from fandom WIKI.
Now played few hours of NS2, got some satisfying parts and also disappointing parts.

Some things are new.
Now you can assign tasks for each minions, which you couldn't do in NS1.
Few maps are added, it had only 1 map in NS1.
You can't go over some objects and waters, and some special parts make it available to go over them.
New heroic minion called 'Titan' is added.
Minimap is added, easier to look around.
The enemy building is now shown through the 'fog of war'.

But! the rest seems pretty much the same.

So I think the game's not bad, but still got some disappointing parts.

First, I love clearing achievements and finding recipes.
Especially filling in the empty recipes in a crafting game.
So in NS1 I've found about 90% of the recipes by my self, writing down everything using excels.
But still, I wanted to know how much of the recipes I've revealed.
And like NS1 you still don't know how many recipes there are in NS2.
Just collecting recipes is one of the fun parts in a crafting game, but game doesn't know how to make it rewarding.
They still give you a bonus for creating minion based on recipes,
but I don't think players searching for recipes are doing it for that bonus.

The bonuses would be better if it was like giving skeleton parts 1hp per revealed skeleton recipes.

Just showing the names of recipes would increase the volume of the game for players like me.
Because players like me will try finding all the recipes in the game.

But this game doesn't even show how many recipes there are.

Next disappointing part is that they increased the number of weapons.
This is disappointing because I really don't think there's a big difference between an axe and the sword.
This just makes you look for more useless recipes, and also making recipes useless.
Because of the diverse weapons, now it's harder to use a specific recipe.

Although the part they increased the species are great.
They should've focused on increasing different monster type, not weapons.

Next disappointing part is about the AI.
The part you could assign tasks to minions were great.
But when you make them attack enemy buildings, they just go through the monster waves and die.
It doesn't seem like prioritizing their actions, it seems like forcing them to do only one action.
This might not be a problem in a latter part of the game when the minions are strong enough, but it makes me feel like their AI's not good enough

Also it feels like melee weapon got useless.
Minions with melee weapon die too fast.
Maybe it's because of the stupid AI, or it's because enemy got faster from the beginning compared to NS1.
Still it feels like minions using melee weapons die faster than NS1.

Final disappointing part about the game is the map.
Increasing the number of the map is great, but the maps got smaller.
It feels like map of NS1 is bigger than adding all maps in NS2.

Also you can't zoom out as much as NS1, and this makes the game frustrating a bit.

Haven't played for a long time.
So this is all I've got to say for now.

Still I think this game is worth the price.\t\t\t\t\t
