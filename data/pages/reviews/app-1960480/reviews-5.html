\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLooks great but the inventory has some issues.

**THE ISSUE WAS RESOLVED** (quite quickly i must say)

My main gripe with the inventory is the fact that you must be quite careful with where you click. Clicking on a hotbar slot with another item selected will just replace the slot with the item instead of swapping items. It's really frustrating trying to switch to another item and instead removing the item from the hotbar, requiring me to re-assign the item to it's slot. Selecting items with numbers on the keyboard works fine, but as a chronic slot-clicker it makes the hotbar very frustrating and near-useless.

beyond that one big annoyance, I'm loving the game. I played Crea back in the day and saw that a new game was in the works a while ago, and i was NOT let down. It feels great to play (mostly), and it definitely scratches that Terraria/Factorio itch at the same time. Very excited to see what is to come in the following updates!!\t\t\t\t\t
