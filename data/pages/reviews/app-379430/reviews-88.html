\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLOVE LOVE LOVE IT! if you want a medieval rpg that is extremely in depth, this is it. Even your clothes being dirty or having blood on them will impact your persuasion chances. You have to sleep, eat, bath, learn a extremely complex combat system. Haggle with vendors and get them to like you so you can get the good deals. As you level up skills or attributes by using them, you can put perks into an immense skill tree. Stealth archer build? Hah! Think again, from how its going it seem like the game intends to bully you and make you really grind to be an efficient player. You want a challenge that i would say is similar to Souls game but an entirely different manner of fighting, its this. Worry about your reputation (stealing is a good way to ruin it, if you get caught, but the money, oh the money). Your equipment loses durability over time so you have to maintain it. I cant recommend it enough. I always loved Ren fairs and medieval movies growing up and few games ever truly made you feel like you're a regular ass individual with no special abilities just trying to make something of himself after he lost everything. I wish there was character creation, but at least you can change hair and beard of the default one you play as. Perhaps a mod one day, i cant see it being overly difficult but i also don't do that stuff. Graphically Stunning. Ask any questions and ill try to get back to you. I will update this as i play further, but as i post this.... 13 hours i have been playing.... non stop.... The beginning is slow for sure but it definitely sucks you in. Do the main quest and get the combat training tutorial... i didnt till much later because i wanted to free roam and i wish i would have.\t\t\t\t\t
