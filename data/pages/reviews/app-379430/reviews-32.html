\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have over 500 hours on this game, to say that I love this game is an understatement. If you have an interest in medieval history, arms, armor and just the overall time period itself, you'll enjoy this game. This game is very in depth, and has a lot of mechanics to master and a lot of tiny things to manage i.e. you need to clean/repair your armor and clothing, eat, sleep, learn how to pick locks, learn alchemy, sword fighting, archery etc. I thoroughly enjoyed the story and have enjoyed every DLC that Warhorse Studios has released. The game is not perfect however. There are a few bugs here and there and unfortunately some of them can be game/quest breaking. Fortunately, they are few and far between in my experience. Sometimes they just become a part of the game, like not being able to run through large bushes, and some other weird stuff that doesn't really affect the overall game play imo. I love the in-depth combat system, i find it to be intuitive once you get the hang of it and your character's skill level increases! Overall I'd say this game is a solid 8/10, and I am really looking forward to a sequel, if there is one! If you like a game that has a decent learning curve, can be challenging at times, has a story that holds your attention, you'll like KCD. If you like a game that you can get lost in the world and the scenery for hours on end, you'll like this game.\t\t\t\t\t
