\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game reminds me of a time when developers truly cared about the games they were making. In fact, I can truly say that I have never seen a developer of this size put so much time, effort, and love into a game. This game is worth every penny at full price, even for the royal edition. The narrative is excellent, and the game world is really alive.

I know the game received some criticism for the combat system, but I felt that it made combat unique from other games. The combat system requires that you actually practice, not just for your character's in game skill, but YOUR real-life skill with the mechanics as well. This made "grinding" very rewarding and entertaining.

I would highly recommend that you set time aside over the span of a week or two to play through this game in its entirety. This is not the type of game that you will want to put down.

I don't really like pre-ordering games anymore, however, if/when a sequel is announced, I will pre-order immediately.\t\t\t\t\t
