\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game with a few issues here and there.

Visually this game is a delight to play.

There are frequent occasions when the controls get a little odd, like forcing the camera to track your movement on one joystick and completely disabling the right joystick and seemingly randomly giving control back to the right joystick after either side stepping or walking backwards for a second or two? not really sure why this is but also don't really care as I'm done with my play-through now.

Mounting a horse for some reason results in a few seconds of your characters head bobbling around like a rag-doll and you get to see the screen swinging around wildly. I'm guessing this was by design as I even turned head bobbing to zero... so assuming "realism" from a person who never got on a horse once in their life or something and they just assumed it would a head spinning experience every time? (maybe fire the person who programmed that aspect).

The combat is honestly terrible, however if you just get some good gear you can fix the majority of the combat issues, Look online, youtube, for some easy to dig up buried treasure spots around the map and you will be good to go for the rest of the game honestly. Also try keeping enemies in front of you will help a lot as well. Also be sure to pick up master-strike from Bernard in Rattay, makes the combat so much more enjoyable to play.

The alchemy system takes far to long to be enjoyable in any way, though you can play the game through fully without ever touching it. There is a quest near the end that required making 60 pots and even if you already have all the herbs in your inventory be ready to waste 2-3 hours making those 60 pots because the animations are so drawn out for every step of brewing it makes it an awful experience to be sure.

The tournament, and bar fights are a great way to rank up strength and weapon skill levels without needing to cheese the game at all, also just running around fighting bandits will work well too. There are about a million ways to break this game in various ways ranging from breaking quest lines, to making your character insanely over powered and thus making the game rather annoying and/or boring.

I originally played the game when it first released and it was extremely buggy and honestly terrible, a lot has been fixed over the years and I finally came back to it and beat it through fully.

Keyboard and mouse is a nightmare for this game, use a controller... trust me.\t\t\t\t\t
