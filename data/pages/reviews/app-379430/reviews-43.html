\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the most enjoyable games I've ever played.

The game style can be compared to Skyrim. An open world first-person medieval adventure but instead of "medieval fantasy" it is a "medieval historically accurate" game. Very accurate. Ostensibly, the team had two historians working full-time making sure everything was period correct.

The thing that I really appreciated was a ton of quality voiced dialogues and character interactions. There's a lot to do in-game, and you can see a TON of effort was put into details. The world is not gigantic, a 4x4km area but everything in it feels hand-sculpted.

I have especially liked how the story is set in Bohemia, a time and place not already-done by a ton of other games/movies/tv shows so I feel like I've explored a new world and learned some history along the way.

A word of advice for new players: if you're intimidated with the combat mechanics and find it hard in the beginning of the game - do not despair. It gets easier as you level up your skills and equipment. Besides, you're playing a peasant who has had a lesson or two in swordfighting with wooden sticks, what do you expect? :)

I understand that this is a crowdfunded game and was rough around the edges during early access but I've played it through in 2023 and it definitely feels polished and complete. Did not have any issues with bugs or crashes. The bugs were fixed, and the DLCs that came out are actually good and not some shameless cash grabs.\t\t\t\t\t
