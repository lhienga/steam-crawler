\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOther than straight-up D&D this is the best RPG I've ever played. VERY old-school in terms of difficulty. It was also written and directed by the guy who wrote and directed the first two Mafia games, so that's a plus. Stellar game. Great story, characters, gameplay, MUSIC, combat, everything. It does start off a bit slow for some people and some people are put off by how unforgiving the combat is in the early game (and the late game, for that matter) but if you can get past those things you will have an amazing time. Pretty historically accurate, too, especially the architecture. Sometimes the clothes look bad in terms of accuracy but that's okay I guess. Play it, please. We're pretty sure they're making a sequel, so, you might as well.

Spectacular game.\t\t\t\t\t
