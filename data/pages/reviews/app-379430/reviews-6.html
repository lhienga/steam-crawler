\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game! The story is compelling. The player character is likable. The actual gameplay is compelling and enjoyable. I recommend the game. However...after 35 hours I kind of dropped this game in the middle of the story because...the combat just SUCKS! Specifically if there's combat with more than 1 enemy. It gets real messy real quick as the enemy count increases. The combat is principally great; when you start the game, you're useless, but as you train and improve your skills, you really do become a lethal opponent, and you feel like you earned that! However, when you have 2, 3, 4, or more enemies in a bigger conflict, it really is the most disorienting and out of control mess I have ever played in any game. I hit a point 35 hours in where I just couldn't do it anymore, it was actually making me ill.\t\t\t\t\t
