Worth mentioning is how much a pain in the a** the Monastery mission is, St Benedicts Cathedral is such a ♥♥♥♥♥♥ place to get stuck for hours of the story, at first I thought it would be interesting but it almost made me stop playing the ♥♥♥♥♥♥ game - there is so many frustratingly annoying things to mention about the mission I can't even be bothered. It's just a crap mission. I busted John drinking and he tells me to join him and the other bloke gets up immediately then tells me I am somewhere I am not meant to be and puts me in seclusion? That whole mission is bugged and doesn't work properly.

I recommend the game I just feel there is a lot to be desired from the game mostly around gameplay. Horses can't even run through small shrubs or over bowling ball sized rocks on the floor, I thought the horse was glitching a few times to find it was just stuck on something small I couldn't see.

Another thing is the amount of timed missions in this game, so frustrating how often I have accidentally accepted one task too many and can't finish them all on time.

I think in all honesty if you haven't played it you could have a better time playing Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord over this game, combat is more fun, more stuff happens just not as deep a storyline but the gameplay makes it more fun overall than this game.\t\t\t\t\t
