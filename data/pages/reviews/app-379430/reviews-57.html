\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBe prepared for a slow burn, with lots of humbling experiences at the beginning in which guards, peasants and bandits alike will beat the ♥♥♥♥ out of you if you look at them the wrong way.

But there's a certain vibe to this game that is unlike anything I've experienced in other RPGs. The sound track is fantastic, the gameplay is unique and engaging, and the story is exceptionally written. With lots of twists and turns in the plot and multiple ways to complete quests, KCD is a truly immersive and underrated game.

There are some bugs that really turned me off of the game at first; I also learned that this is not a game to play casually, as you need to take time honing different skills, which can lead to grindy elements, but even that is enjoyable at times. Overall, KCD is a unique RPG with great voice acting, story and environment.\t\t\t\t\t
