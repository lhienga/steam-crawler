\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJesus Christ Be Praised!
If youre looking for a realistic medieval game this the best one youll find. It has an incredible story with lots of fun side quest and activities. The one thing about this game that shies people away from it is the patience you need to have when learning the game. Unlike many others this game will not hold your hand instead it will make you go through a lot to become good at anything from combat to talking to an NPC. At first youll struggle to fight a simple bandit on the road or have trouble trying to persuade a beggar, but when you work on those skills and youll able to fight off multiple trained soldiers with ease and persuading noblemen without any issue, It will feel extremely rewarding.\t\t\t\t\t
