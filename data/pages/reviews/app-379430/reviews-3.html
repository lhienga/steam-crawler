Now, 45 hours into my playthrough (as of writing) I have realised that I was entirely wrong about this game. It is not an easy, nor particularly forgiving game. However, the power creep of going from being utterly useless to rampant knightly killing machine all the while being a part of a rich and interesting story is incredibly satisfying. Would recommend if you enjoy the metallic *clunk* of beating someone's armoured head in.\t\t\t\t\t
