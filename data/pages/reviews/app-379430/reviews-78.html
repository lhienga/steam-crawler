\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere's my review of the game. First off Kingdom Come Deliverance Is a really good game and worth the buy. Is it still buggy at times sadly yes, its not uncommon to run into annoying bugs specially during combat which is fustrating. However the story's the side quest are really fun and enjoyable. It has good visuals, great audio and lot of fun rpg mechanics from alchemy to leveling up stuff like getting drunk or reading.

Now lets talk about combat. I actually got really good at the combat about 25 hours in and can say i enjoy it but it could be 10 x better which is why you will see people complain about it. Let me explain. Targeting multiple enemys is bad no other way to put it, hard to switch targets or the right ones and often you can get hit multiple times cause of it. However the game has great combat when it comes to 1 v 1's. The tournaments for example are super fun and took me a while to get it down pat but now I can do them without losing a single time. However one massive tip is talking to the trainer after you learn the basics and having him teach you how to counter hit enemys. Seriously its the most useful combat skill in the game cause perfect blocking is key in this game for its combat.

Now lets talk about the main character aka henry. First off i wish we could play as a character we create rather male or female. I think it add a lot of variety to the game. Henry is basically the most useless person to exist, you start off being bad at legit everything in life. However as you progress more and more you go from a weakling idiot to one of the most intelligent strongest characters in the game. In a way this is fun but i think having a character creator would add alot more flavor to the game.

Finally ill talk about skills and why i love it but wish they did so much more with it. Leveling up skills is actually super fun simple as that. However a big complaint i have is the perks are not always great infact there will come a time you get perk points and you don't even want to use them cause your going to hinder yourself more then anything. Rework perks to be benefiting not punishing and if they do make it punishing least add fun reasons to why that is. Also finally they need more, not enough in the game.

Finally the one big negative i have besides the bugs, the saving. The way you save in this game is bad. Its not the worst thing ive seen but there is nothing good about it and ill just be real. Nothing like doing 10 mins of work, only to fast travel get ambushed by 4 people and a dog and die then have to restart all over again cause only way you can save is by using a item or sleeping in a bed.

Overall Ill rate King Come Deliverance a solid 8/10. Id even be willing to give it a 8.5 if not for the bugs and its awful saving system. Combat needs improved however I don't dislike it but considering its a big part of the game it can be super frustrating at times. And it is super unfriendly to new players to grasp.\t\t\t\t\t
