This is hands down the best period themed game I have played. It's aesthetically pleasing, it's immersive, the directions to go are endless, and you genuinely feel a sense of accomplishment when you achieve something. It never loses that "exciting" feeling when you see a skill level up, or you notice you have subtly become better at combat.

It can be as intense or nonchalant as you want it to be. Want to fight enemies with your bare hands all day? Go for it. Want to lollygag and pick flowers for two days? Do it. Want to steal everything and die in jail? The pleasure is all yours. There is not a "correct" way to play this game, and I believe that is why it is so addictive.

Life is overrated. Buy the game and become Henry. I dare you.\t\t\t\t\t
