I truly do respect and admire what they created with this game, however, it truly falls short of being a really fun experience. What could have been such a knockout for this time period in game form, it just isn't there. I found myself almost in a coma-like state of trying to find the fun in the choices they made with combat, survival, and etc. but each of these are either just a frustration or such a chore to get a hang of that it really doesn't feel worth it. They seem to have gotten so committed to a ultra-realistic time period that they even made the game as fun as the entertainment they had back in those days too: f all. TLDR: unless you're truly a hardcore for this kind of period and ultra slow life sim progression, don't buy ever. Just not worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
