Maybe it had some difficulties in the past, but actually I really enjoyed Kingdom Come Deliverance.

It is a bit of an odd one, though. The most notable mechanic is the melee combat, and emphasis on using skills and swordplay. But that's also its biggest flaw - the system falls apart if you're engaging more than one or two opponents. Perhaps that's more realistic, as in a real fight you'd get pummelled if you were fighting a group, but it's not very fun to play in a game. I ended up cheesing the system by circling groups on a horse shooting arrows at their head. This worked, but I would have rather had some more of the melee system.

Anyway, buy Kingdom Come and play it if you like open world RPGs. It felt like Witcher 3.\t\t\t\t\t
