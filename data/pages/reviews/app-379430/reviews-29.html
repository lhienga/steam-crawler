For starters, your character sucks. I understand roguelike progression, but your character literally isn't good at anything to start at all. "Go level up, get better!" say the fans. Okay, sure. Let's get to trying out some combat....

COMBAT
When I say this game has the worst combat of any game ever created, I mean it. Not only is it terribly unintuitive for starters, it is filled with the worst controls I have ever experienced in a video game. You'll deal with jarring camera breaks that happen all the time, for no reason at all! You'll enjoy being clinched constantly by enemies with no way to break free from it. Most enemies just wait for you to swing and counter attack you; the other ones start unstoppable combos and 3 shot you. God help you if you try to fight more than 1 person at a time; enjoy the worst camera work and lock-on targeting system you'll ever endure. You can't run away; enemies don't have stamina and are faster than you. You can't fight, your character sucks at everything. Oh, you wanted to try mounted combat? LMAO. Enjoy your camera breaking so badly that you'll become disoriented, or in worst case scenarios, develop motion sickness from the god-awful controls and perspectives.

Do yourself a favor; never play this "game". A key component of interactive media is the interactivity, and kingdom Come Deliverance fails utterly at every interaction the player is a part of. And for whomever designed this world-class combat system, please don't work in games anymore. This is an abomination.\t\t\t\t\t
