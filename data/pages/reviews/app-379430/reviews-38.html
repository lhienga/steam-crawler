\r\n\t\t\t\t\tImmensely enjoyable eurojank that does a great job with the power curve of your character. Combat that's very engaging and fun to learn and get in the groove of. The game does a masterful job with its atmosphere, art direction and overall mood. If I had any complaints it's that after the halfway point the main quest gets a little too linear and tedious and doesn't take full advantage of the games plethora of RPG mechanics to circumvent the issue. Despite this, probably one of the best western RPG's of the last decade or so, highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
