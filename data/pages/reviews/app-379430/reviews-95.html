\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my all time favourite games - an actual medieval simulator (of sorts) with a great soundtrack and story.

A lot of folks complain about the difficulty, and yes for an average Josephine (the lord be praised), it may not be for them... as they say in the game, you need to train, practice and pick your marks. Trying to take on more than one enemy in the early game is suicide, picking locks without practising and learning the perks will result in multiple fails. Make use of trainers, learn to read and read skill books, train with Captain Bernard and (as in life), Henry becomes a force to be reckoned with! ...but you really do have to give it time, and play the story at least until you get to train with Captain Bernard - then go back to him and train some more!

Compared to the early days, most of the bugs have been ironed out, leaving a truly beautiful gaming experience that I keep coming back to on the regular (hardcore only... its by far the best experience) - can't wait for the next instalment!\t\t\t\t\t
