\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter two significant attempts to play through this game, I've given up the ghost.

You know how in most action games, yanking camera control away from the player during combat is considered a cardinal sin of game design? In come the KCD devs, here to insist they know better than the rest of the industry, because R E A L I S M

The AI robotically clips into you, stunlocking you with looping grapples/clinches, terrain clipping, attacks that send your camera reeling in every possible direction so you can't track the combat, and more. The combat takes itself far too seriously for how comically ♥♥♥♥♥♥ the combat animations are, too.

To top it off: mediocre voice acting, awful menus, and one of the most creepy bootlicking fanbases I've ever seen. Never ever mention complaints about the gameplay to the community; there's always some pompous neckbeard who thinks spending 500 hours picking flowers and getting your ass kicked by poorly-animated AI is the height of immersion and that you are a FOOL and a PHILISTINE for not recognizing it.

These devs aren't talented enough in the coding and animating departments to afford sacrificing gameplay for realism. There's a solid core there, and I really wanted to like it, but the bootleg For Honor combat with ♥♥♥♥♥♥ AI and self-fellating design choices wore me out. I won't be finishing this game and I don't recommend it to anyone who values their time.\t\t\t\t\t

Not to mention you can't drink any during combat. You end up riding 10 hours, killing tons of random enemies just to die to the last band of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ idiots that manage to surround you and kill you cause even your last stand or berserk can't save you when they hit you all at the same time, so then you lose a lot of time because you didn't save so you Alt + F4 and edit your review to reflect how pissed off you are.
