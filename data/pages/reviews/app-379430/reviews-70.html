\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA VERY immersive medieval themed game.

The combat is very fun(although some people find it quite difficult, I personally find archery to be the least fun), fighting more than at most 2 enemies at once is tough, if you see more than 3 you usually run (although this becomes more manageable as you get skills and armour).

The skill system is interesting, and immersive as you gain skill by using them (my favourite kind of system). I also like the fact that you start out weak (I mean what skill can a peasant have? You can't even read at the beginning :D) and gradually grow powerful (or not depending on how you want to play).

The quests are interesting, I loved Theresa's story dlc.

There are some performance "issues", altough I personally did not mind the frame drops as the game still felt responsive enough. I played this on nvidia 3080 on max, and the frames still dipped in the low 20s sometimes (I found out that the grass/foliage distance and density had a significant impact on this), which is odd for a game that came out back in 2018.\t\t\t\t\t
