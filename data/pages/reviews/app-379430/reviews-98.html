The first 5-10 hours of play time can be really frustrating and confusing since the tutorial sections are badly implemented, combine it with the complex combat mechanics it create a really tiresome experience.

But if you can pushing on and really learn the game it can be one of the most immersive RPG to date, the world feels alive and full of interesting things to do or you can just relax and take a walk to around an impressive towns and learn the history of said town (if you don't encounter any bandits or special events).

I really can't say about the story because I got side tracked but so far it's quite interesting. Really recommended if you willing to spend quite a time to learn this game and mastering it.\t\t\t\t\t
