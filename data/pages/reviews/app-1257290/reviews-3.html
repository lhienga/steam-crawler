\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's no ranking system here but straight out the gate: 11/10 would play again and definitely will.

I realized that playing this immediately after the first title, I walked into it with nostalgia and excitement. Seeing every little improvement from the much better music to the extra-hectic battle mechanics, a handful of game mechanics I was too overwhelmed to touch, and seeing a lot of old faces return with time-skip love and care (look it tells you this in the description, that's not a spoiler) I was primed to get attached to this game.

In my mind this is a complete improvement over the original title. Music was mentioned already, and the battle system adds depth where the first game felt afraid to limit its' audience. I didn't even start using the item combos or switching between characters until halfway through the game and it makes a huge difference. Story-wise there's a little more depth and some occasional darker tones but it's still largely the kind of thing you could have a middle schooler understand and enjoy.
I can give no spoilers and would never do that, but I do have to offer warning by way of experience; I cried for about an hour after I beat this and struggled not to immediately start a new save.\t\t\t\t\t
