\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn improvement over the original in almost every regard!

Much better combat, mainly due to the defense command and core charges needing to be built up, and separately tracked per character, meaning you have a reason to use alchemy on each character instead of leaving Ryza with your strongest tools.

Character graphics are very good, with working physics this time; The terrain and backgrounds are still somewhat PS3-esque, but they take advantage of it with more expansive areas.

They somewhat abuse a lot of modern post-processing effects, to the point where the game looks better with some of them turned off, but thankfully the option to turn them off is there. The PC port is a significant improvement over the intital release of Ryza 1; I'm very glad they've learned from it.

If you want a comfy, low-intensity RPG with a focus on crafting mechanics, I highly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
