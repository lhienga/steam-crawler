Pro's:
Improved battle system from last game
More ways to interact with the world
Tao is a Daddy now

Cons:
The running animation is dumb, it emphasizes certain... *assets*, and just looks silly
Skill Tree is wack addition that wasn't needed or asked for.
Can no longer quick command skills at any point in battle nor can you manually control Tactical level.
You can tell some of the level design on closer inspection is recycled maps from last, just re-textured with different assets and elevations.

Can Atelier series just return to its roots by being cute and wholesome again without relying on fan service to gain a following.... Overall the game is good as a JRPG... but as an Atelier game, it lacks the appeal it once had.\t\t\t\t\t
