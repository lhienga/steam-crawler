The four Legendary Lords are all amazing, each one feels unique, Settra is an amazing faction leader, I had one of the best campaigns as Khemri and his final battle against Arkhan was one of the most titanic battles in all my years of Total War, and I've always wanted Queen Khalida to make it in the game since she's my favorite Tomb King, Arkhan the Black also gives a nice twist to oppose the other Tomb Kings and uses some Vampire Counts units as well, while Khatep is a campaign for those who want a bit more challenge.

The canopic jars and the crafting options are great to spice up your Tomb Kings and agents with abilities.

Yes it's an expensive DLC, however the sheer amount of content available here is more than worth it for me, Settra's campaign alone felt so fresh and had me more engaged than most fully priced AAA games nowadays, not to mention the other lords will give me many more days of entertainment so I can't recommend this enough.\t\t\t\t\t
