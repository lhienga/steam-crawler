The one campaign I played on legendary was with Khemri and after I obtained control of the entire Southlands continent more or less the game became defensive with the non stop invading forces from Loremasters and Lothern and the problem is that you can't make too many armies as the price of getting new army slot is quite costly unless you don't grind until turn 200 and nonstop harvesting organs from the fallen armies. After you reach enough number of armies to maintain the non stop attacks of the elves sustainable you can finally send armies to retaliate. All in all, it took me like 250 turns to complete the campaign.

Tomb Kings are kinda poor units rooster in combat units in the early stage and they will depend more on archers with monster types in the later. Perhaps because of the free upkeep but at later game one army of well trained units cannot face single handle with equivalent later unit rooster of the elves which is kinda annoying. I get they are undead so they depend more on quantity before quality but still make them a bit more powerful so when I play against them they are actually formidable foe. I played another campaign afterwards with Lizards and wipe them without any problem whatsoever which kinda sad as I would like to see later game to have 1 great power of the elves, dark elves, lizards, skaven and nehekhara. Until this point all I have been receiving is mostly 2 great elf powers, 2 dark elves and 1 skaven maximum if the southlands are not sandwiched between the loremasters and lothern.
