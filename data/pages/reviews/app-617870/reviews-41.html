\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is top teir Warhammer TW DLC, perhaps the best they've released to date. The Tomb Kings are very original and play like a "for advanced players" faction should (a la Wood Elves). I was worried about the free armies being OP, but they come pretty slowly, and the lower tier Tomb King units are total trash (while the higher tier construct units are Glorious). Makes for a long early game with paper-thin armies, and even when you start snowballing it is held in check by unit caps for everything but basic skeletons.

This is also the first time I've not been able to decide which new Legendary Lord to start with, since they are all quite different and each have a unique and/ or challenging starting position.

Anyone giving this DLC a negative review soley because it was released a little late (due to Steam, not CA), is a child. Many of them hadn't even played the DLC when they made the review, so perhaps just ignore them. The Tomb Kings are well worth the money I say.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t*Problem has been fixed, first part was my original review and I like I said I would add to my review once my problem was fixed.*

So I just want to say so far I love playing as the Tomb Kings and once this has been fixed I'll give it a positive review, but none of the lord campaign or battle effect descriptions are showing up for the lords. So I have no clue what his starting buffs are or what his skill buffs actually do. I know roughly what they'll be from previous games in Warhammer 1 & 2 but I don't which specific units they'll effect or what the specific value of that buff is. Sure you could say I can just look it up and reference that but I shouldn't have to. I shouldn't have to research each skill everytime I want to pick one, It should just be there for me to see. So yeah, love the DLC so far, love the units and how the TK's play, but until this is fixed regardless of if it's client side or something on the developers end, this review will stay negative.

So I have no clue what happened or what was causing it, I wasn't playing with any mods but it eventually just started working so what the problem was I can't say all I know is I'm no longer experiencing it. I bloody love the TK's. They have a slow start due to their low income but thats to balance the fact units cost nothing to recruit or upkeep, hence the added unit cap that you raise via building military structures. Once you start to get a good economy going it really speeds up because your able to build all these military buildings and have full stacks with good units as oppossed to Skeleton swords & spears. They do struggle against other armies with mostly more elite or hardy units, so I usually had my a main who was there to actually win the battle and a secondary army filled with base units to support the main army and soak up the fire. The mortuary cult was a fantastic edition to the game and I hope they impliment that system to other races in some forms. I feel like it would really suit a lot of the races such as the dwarves. I'd advise trying to expand really wuick if possible because you can easily get bogged down in small terrority due to not being able to keep your buildings upgraded. Sadly you can't confederate with the other TK factions. Whether you can with the legendary lord factions I don't know because it seems like the AI doesn't understand how to play TK's and they seem to get wiped very quickly for me. My only problem with this DLC is the fact you don't get any info on how the fight for the vortex is going. I know it doesn't really matter for the TK's and they don't care, but would be nice to still see the other races progress. This is going to sound like the typical kid in the candy store but I always don't like how good it is. I know that sounds silly but heres why. This shows how good CA can make their race DLC. They felt unique, it added cool new mechanics and ideas to what was essentially a big Warhammer expansion. It shows just how good they can make their DLC games. And it makes all the other DLC's before just seem really weak in comparison. Now I know what they are capable of I hope to find more DLC like this, instead of the half assed ♥♥♥♥ we got with Warhammer. You've laid out your cards CA. It was a beautiful hand but now we know what you're capable of and I hope you continue to show this much dedication for future DLC for warhammer II.\t\t\t\t\t
