\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great DLC. It is probably one of the best DLC I have ever gotten for a Total War Game in fact besides Kingdoms for Medieval 2. The Tomb Kings are a really cool faction and I love pretty much everything about them. They play so differently than all of the others in that they don't spend gold to recruit units so you can get full stacks fairly easily. The thing is you can only have a certain number of armies and you have to unlock a lot of units in different ways than just buildings. Their early units are fairly weak but that is the price you pay for early game full stacks but they can come on strong late game. It's also kind of refreshing to not just be trying to mess around with the vortes in some way. Collecting the books of Nagash brings some needed diversity to the Eye of the Vortex campaign, I haven't played a Mortal Empire campaign with them yet but I can't wait. Great job CA on a very good expansion, it was well worth the extra hour we had to wait.\t\t\t\t\t
