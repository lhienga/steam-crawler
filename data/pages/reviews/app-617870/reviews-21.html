\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnfortunately this has gone the way of CA's previous DLC. Which is to say, great content that is well made.... and horribly overpriced.

As it stands, this DLC costs almost a third the price of the base game, despite it adding nowhere near as much as 1/3 of the content the base game offers (it is more like 1/6, if even that much). This means CA is not pricing this DLC due to how much it costs to produce, but rather they are price-gouging their fanbase for the most they think they can get away with. The DLC itself is well made and of high quality, but it costing almost twice what it should (which is $10, which is what some other race packs such as Norsca and Chaos cost) means I can't reccommend it at its current price. Wait for at least a 35-50% off sale before you buy. Which is a shame, because the Tomb Kings themselves are unique, fun to play, have a very interesting twist on Total War mechanics. But 1/3 the price of the game for 1/6 the content of the game means it isn't a good deal unless you get it on sale (in which case I would definitely reccommend).
