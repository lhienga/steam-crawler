\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't care for Warhammer much, don't know much about it
But I saw cute skelly army and decided to jump in.

After quite some time playing this faction, I can tell you it's the one of the most fleshed out (pun unintended) in the entire game.

Things I Like:
+ Skellies!
+ They're quite weak to start, then slowly build up large strength. You typically need at least 2 or more armies to score wins.
+ Tons of meaningful upgrades to purchase.
+ Long research tree.
+ Their gameplay. It's hard to explain, but for me they feel convenient to how I want to play. Slow build. Lots of armies and units. They dismiss some negative effects but are receptive to others etc.
+ Lots of skills for lords and characters.
+ Lots of unique lords and character models.
+ There are 4 different playable factions of Tomb Kings. Each have a different leader and long-game to play.
+ Lots of little events and other timed bonuses you can get that further push the game.
Another is a mix of vampire and so uses a bunch of different units from the vampire faction.
+ There are some funny lines like when opening diplomacy with another Tomb King faction, they say something like "I think my pyramid is bigger, but let's not get into that...". The VO overall is good.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHigh Queen Khalida is easily one of my favorite leaders to play in Total War: Warhammer 3's Immortal Empires campaign, and that is what this review is for.

Khalida receives poison attack buffs for the troops in her personal army, and some incredible archery bonuses for both Skeleton Archers, her foot missile units, and her Skeleton Archer Chariots ~ think missile damage, armor pierce, and so much extra ammo it is almost silly ~ and that poison damage buff goes for her melee units in her army too, giving some of your rank-and-file chaff an extra bite. Overall, the tech tree, which you unlock in blocks of options called "pillars," offers a lot of variety for building your faction out.

So far, I have played a campaign through as Settra, who was cool but, like, kinda Tomb King, like he's the regular ramen of the Tomb Kings, and Khalida, obviously, who is awesome, and I am in love with the flavor and style of this faction. What isn't to love about completely free troop recruitment?!
