\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRise of the Tomb Kings is the first faction DLC for TW WH 2, and it definitely changes things up a bit. The Tomb Kings play drastically different to the other factions. For example, almost NONE of their units do you actually have to pay to recruit, and almost none have an upkeep cost, but the amount of any given unit you can recruit is capped unless you construct buildings to extend that cap. They also have their own unique objective in the Eye of the Vortex campaign. Instead of competing for control of the vortex like the others, you're trying to gain entry to the black pyramid. As a result you're not under as much of a time constraint and can play at your own pace, but on the flip side, it does dumb down the difficulty a bit, depending which LL you're playing. Definitely worth the purchase if you're looking to expand your WH 2 roster with a unique faction with gorgeously detailed units and a unique play style, or even if you just want to play undead who don't take attrition when leaving home.\t\t\t\t\t
