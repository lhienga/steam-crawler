  • Unique faction mechanics that feel very different from the others (not the same car with an Egyptian coat of paint).

  • Completely different campaign objectives than other factions help it feel like a brand new game.

  • Very unique research system that also helps each play through feel different depending on what route you choose.

  • Freaking awesome special units.

  • $20 still feels like a lot for 1 faction. I am sure I will get at least 20hrs of playtime out of it so I guess I can’t really complain about the value.

Overall, I certainly recomend this DLC for anyone that likes Warhammer 2. Can't wait to try them in Mortal Empires!\t\t\t\t\t
