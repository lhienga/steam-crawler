This game is... a 7 or 8 out of 10 in my book. Certainly not perfect, but worth the price.

If I have to commend the game on something, is that it gave me a new perspective on a few events on my life. Certainly, while the story isn't perfect, it is illuminating on a certain view point. Also, it is really funny, and as my friend put it: "this goes hard ngl"

Now, I could go on about the theme, and for me, it hit me hard. I was somebody who managed to climb the social ladder all the way from the bottom, and the message of the game really reflects what my journey felt like without even probably being intended to be relatable to me.

Jeffery reflected me, and my beliefs, and Nicole's struggles* (not Nicole herself, I am a manifestation of hope and happiness) would've been relatable to me after I dragged myself out the hole of un-sociability. The argument from the 100% cut scene really showcases it to me. Unsociable people think pretty/sociable people are lucky, while pretty/sociable people are still miserable.

*NOT THE BEING ASSAULTED BITS!!! I WAS LUCKY ENOUGH TO NOW HAVE ANY PEDO'S IN MY SCHOOL! Just the being miserable despite everyone liking you bits.

Other than the theme which resonated with me, I found most of the characters really funny. Mr.White and the jock guy battle it out for who made me laugh more. Mr. White's name by itself got my laughing, but his racism was so not hidden that it was funny af. (I'm not explaining why a dumb jock is funny. It's too obvious)

This game, however short, is worth more than it's price in MY opinion. If I wasn't somebody who read into things too much, and because of how my life turned out the way it did, it still would've been worth the money just for the laughs.

Anyway, buy it, the funny parts are still worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
