\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a gal of the late 90s, so some of this hit home and can say I experienced some of it, but not to this level. This is a new stage of dark that I was (until today) blissfully unaware of and this covers a dark topic full of darker things. Yes, she's got some sass and that's good, but whomever was able to survive that IRL (if they survived that, if you survived that anyone...) you're royalty.... and I'm glad I chose not to have children.

Going to play the other one... but not immediately... I need to "Go Outside"

Get this only if you're good with a visual novel that covers dark topics that females deal with. If you're easily triggered, depressed or anything but perfectly stable. Maybe skip or play it on a semi-good day cause it might ruin a very good day and it will certainly make a bad day worse.

Wow... my gal... Nicole... wow.

My biggest criticism with these kind of visual novels is that I really only want one ending. It's a failing of the genre, not of this story. The story is great if you can deal with the darkness. I think... and this is just my opinion... but If you have a story or a message to tell. Stick to your message it'll have more impact than providing all the potential ways things could possibly go sideways.\t\t\t\t\t
