Pros
1. Fully Voice Acted with an amazing cast.
2. Clever and funny writing with well paced routes and thought provoking endings.
3. Extremely short play time and quick to the point.
4. Brings up ideas and themes people don't like to think about or ever are put into a situation to empathize with.
