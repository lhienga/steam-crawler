\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought after watching an animated clip that was made to promote the series. The voice acting is what drew me in. The VAs did an excellent job. No amount of voice acting can save this writing though. The writing is garbage. I'll admit, I went into this game mostly blind (I knew about the content warnings) because I was hoping for something a little more meaningful than the absolutely miserable experience this game has to offer. Constant edgy jokes about every man in the school being a rapist, racist, and/or a pedophile stop being funny after a while and just become depressing. Playing this game actively made me feel like ♥♥♥♥.

This game gives you the illusion of choice by having multiple endings, but in reality, bad things will just happen no matter what. Oh you rejected a guy? Now he wants you dead. You agreed to hang out with him once? Now you can't escape and your only choice is to kill yourself. At best, this is a piss poor attempt at social commentary. It honestly took me back to being majorly depressed and in highschool so I'll give it that.

Play this game if you're into roleplaying as suicidal highschool girls and/or wallowing in your own misery.\t\t\t\t\t
