It is definitely nothing like other visual novels, the cynical and depressing yet humourous and sometimes exaggerated dialogue, themes and plot make for a unique experience that just had me wanting to unlock every ending and explore every dialogue option.
I will say this much however, that this game borders on a little too ♥♥♥♥♥♥ up and socially critical for my taste. I think the plot could have benefitted from one "normal" character or story path, to showcase that not everything is that crazy or stereotypical.
Further, the level of cynicism mixes a lot with feminist ideology and maybe that's the meta-irony in this game, but Nicole, the main character, just seems quite unhinged in her beliefs sometimes.

Overall a very solid experience, 9/10, minus the one point for the level of craaazy going on in this game, a little over the top, definitely be prepared for that, definitely recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
