Anti-visual novel except that it's not?

Who's the target audience? People who haven't really gone to school in '09. I graduated from high school coincidentally in '09. This story is cynical and there is nothing of value to gain here, no life lessons just a lot of bad takes typical of EVN.

  • All men are not .pdf file,
  • Online gaming was still fresh and people weren't as addicted as they are today. (League of legends released in '09)

✔️The good thing I can say about this visual novel is that it plays as if you're watching a show, it autoplays at a decent pace and your choices do matter. It's very well made for a western audience who has never read a book in their life.

🛑 Do not waste your time on this. Go look up some visual novels who was made by people who want to tell a story with great music and art instead of whatever the **** this is from rage writing.
