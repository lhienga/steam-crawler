\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the funniest games I've played in a while, and one that's also filled with tons of references to the late 00s (remember Avril Lavigne and calling everything gay?). But beneath the humor and sense of nostalgia, there's some actual substance here. The game deals with heavy themes and subjects like suicides, bullying, sexual assault, and even pedophilia (why tf is the pedophile gym teacher something that's apparently so universal?).

While Nicole has a distinct personality she stays true to, there's still a lot of choices and 15 different endings. So far I've seen two of them, which took me a bit over an hour. It's certainly not the longest VN, but the endings are different enough to warrant multiple playthroughs.

I only have two negatives: the art and especially the animation are rather simplistic. They work well enough, but there's a lot of VNs out there with more drawings and elaborate animations. Secondly, the game occasionally does a poor job of showing which of the characters on screen is currently talking, as the characters often aren't animated and not everyone is introduced the first time you see them. Still, those are minor annoyances, and definitely nothing that'd stop me from recommending the game.\t\t\t\t\t
