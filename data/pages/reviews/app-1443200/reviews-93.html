\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a fantastically funny game. Very edgy humor that not everyone will enjoy, but that was a large part of the pre-2015-ish American high school experience that many of us grew up with, which the game absolutely nails. The edgy humor is done in a tasteful, satirical, social commentary way: the era around 2009 was f***ed up, and Class of '09 does a good job highlighting and condemning many of the reasons why. The voice actors all did amazing too and deserve plenty of praise for their performance.

Very minor critique: you do have to backtrack a decent amount to see all the scenes/get all the endings, and sometimes the paths do not converge into other scenes in the most fluid way. Again, not really a huge deal, and The Re-Up addresses these issues by making the paths longer and more divergent from each other--albeit at the cost of fewer endings and choices, but it's a worthy trade-off.

Highly recommend both Class of '09 and Class of '09: The Re-Up!\t\t\t\t\t
