\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really really wanted to like this game so bad. All the clips I saw of it online were so funny, an there were definitely some good jokes (especially with the character Jeffery), so I bought the class of '09 + reup deal for $16 during winter sale. Little did I know that the clips of this game online are actually not really representative of the actual game's "humor". 70% of the "jokes" are one of the female characters stating "haha [teacher name] SA'd me!" or other lines about SA/p-dophilia. No lead up, no punchline, no timing, no comedic value whatsoever. I was honestly kind of shocked at how much of the "humor" in this game was actually just very cringe-inducing, on-the-nose dialogue about being a victim/doer of SA/p-dophilia. Very possible I could just have a bad sense of humor, but this was just not super funny. I cant believe I paid ~$8 for this 45 minute long game that was just full of unfunny p-do "jokes", and another $8 for the sequel that I will never touch. If you want an actually good unconventional dating sim, just go play Doki Doki Literature Club for FREE. If you want some actually funny, not super politically correct humor, just go watch some episodes of It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia.\t\t\t\t\t
