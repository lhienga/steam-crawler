Pretty much a computerized, single-player version of the board game Agricola with a few added twists layered on to keep you clicking "just one more turn..."

Only criticism would be that the game has a bunch of neat unlocks which you get at a nice pace, BUT you pretty much can't actually use any of the things you've unlocked until you start a new game. The unlocks will keep the game feeling fresh for a few playthroughs as you'll unlock different buildings, rules, and effects that require different strategies. However, given a single playthrough last about 10 hours (if you don't die/give up), that means you've got a spend a LOT of time to see everything.

Seeing everything is by no means mandatory though, so you can just play it until it starts to feel grindy/repetitive (probably 2-3 successful playthroughs). That's plenty of value for the price.\t\t\t\t\t
