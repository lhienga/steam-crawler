2nd edit

when you just started playing it seems to be a fun game, but things don't add up with more memories you unlock.

more content and random events are good, but how come you give me an omen injuring 2 of my 12 pips for a total of 20 ♥♥♥♥♥ turns in the early game when I only have buildings to heal bleeding?! events like these occur quite frequently as more stories I restarted. not to mention that little of your rewards or random events with minor benefits by little means helped me either to expand my population or prepared me for the upcoming apocalypses.

the more your population, the more mouths to feed, the more people and turns required to spend on gathering/harvesting and the more tech points required for stable and high efficient food resources, leading to the lack in resources to prepare for the apocalypse. the less the population, food will be sufficient enough of course, but there won't be enough resource or growth speed to prepare the apocalypse to begin with.

once you've failed to fulfill one little prophecy, things goes south in light speed as more resources are demanded to make up for the punishments in time, otherwise a series of punishing events will catch you off guard and end the story before winter. even if you managed to make up for the loss, a significant amount of turns have gone, resources have run dry and the next apocalypse is standing right in your face with requirements more severe than ever before. there is simply no coming back

3rd edit

one of the developers contacted me almost instantly after I left the last comment. discussions were made regarding the 'injury' event and a patch (that and a few other) was made soon after.

its hard to hold a grudge over a balance issue with developers who listens actively and makes quick adjustments to make their game better. though I still find strategy games rather hard to play (couldn't get the hang of it even after hours), it would be unfair to leave a 'not recommended' for a good game like this.

it is worth giving a try, even if you aren't good at strategy games\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tdotAGE is pretty good and has a lot of potential, but is very punishing and can be challenging.

On one of my most recent runs, I didn't fail a single challenge until right near the end.
I felt I was doing fantastically and was enjoying the challenge of maintaining my successful run. Then I got unfortunately poor luck on a 76% chance of success roll even after fighting crippling sicknesses tooth and nail for an extended period of time.
ALL 51 of my pips instantly went to the shadow realm with no recourse. Needless to say, I am a little sore about that run. It feels too punishing at times, and can feel poorly balanced with a single failed event.

I don't have a problem with challenge and have enjoyed it at times, but my biggest complaint is that there is frequently no recovery from failure, which makes losing off of a high chance of success feel that much worse.

With all of that said, the game is interesting and well fleshed out, and has strong resource management mechanics. The unlock mechanics offer replay value, but can make progressing to a successful point feel a little slow, or make you feel set-up to fail in the beginning.

I think there is a ton of potential and I have mostly enjoyed my time playing, but I don't feel I can recommend dotAGE given some of the painful experiences I've had.\t\t\t\t\t
