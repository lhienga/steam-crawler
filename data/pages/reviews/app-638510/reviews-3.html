If you happen to not have enough production of something, the game is over. If some random events is not in your favor, the game is over.

The randomness factor is too high, the player agency too low.

The game that comes to mind that does this but better is Against the storm. Against the storm understood how to encourage players to make their own build and play their own way, by substituting one ressource for another with a penalty for example.\t\t\t\t\t
