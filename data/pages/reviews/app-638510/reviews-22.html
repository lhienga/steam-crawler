\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts one of those games that if you like them, you spend an insane amount of time on them. The gameplay loop is fairly simple but for those who like to "min max" or being very precise with resources, you will love the time passes each turn mechanic. If something goes wrong with your village its most likely your fault, either for not planning ahead or not being thorough enough. The events are pretty meh, I would love more variety in that regard.

I would compare it to Against the storm but way more chill. Also it rewards you more for caring about your village since my first (and only) village went on for 30(!!) hours (thats probably more on me than on the game). In against the storm you play for like 1 hour and then you move on to the next settlement which is nice and makes play session pretty different but at the same time it lacks the sense of growth and that feeling when everything just clicks. Here, if everything goes right you get to bask in the glory of your village and reap the rewards.\t\t\t\t\t
