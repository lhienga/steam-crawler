\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's kinda like a turn-based version of Rimworld, but without combat. The challenge is in balancing the resource generation, research, increasing population and combating the events that happen regularly. There are also synergies for builings. Placing certain buildings next to each other (or next to specific biomes) will make them generate more resources. So figuring out how to use the limited land is important. Some of the types of builds you can make also changes from playthrough to playthrough increasing the replayability. Most gameplay mechanics are also gradually unlocked (both automatically and by spending resources) which make for a less steep learning curve. All in all seems like a great game, but not sure if it will be for hundreds of hours though.\t\t\t\t\t
