\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has it all: Charming graphics, relaxing upbeat music, a gameplay loop that gets you hooked for hours after hours. You can play a short session of 15 minutes, just a few turns - or you can completly loose track of time and play for 5hours non-stop.
The early game needs to be well thought about: You need basically everything, and you need it fast. Keep track of the prophecy and start farming corresponding ressources for it, always in advance before the next event awaits.
Midgame might feel a bit tedious, but it's there to give you just a little bit of time to prepare for the dangerous endgame.
Endgame is where the real stuff happens. You thought your village is safe and sound, prepared for everything? Well, think again, because suddenly that omen made everyone sick, but your healer aswell, so you need to make a new one - BAM random event of sickness incomming, so you need him to do generate health, but sick people also generate sickness and suddenly you loose not only one event, but two - more sick people, more sickness generated and the cascade goes on - and you also forgot there are other domains and suddenly you loose on those aswell.
Do NOT get fooled in the midgame, where I personally think the game slows down a bit - I know now: It is the calm before the storm. And the storm is a big one to come!
Overall, at least a 9.5/10. It's not perfect in every single aspect, but it is in almost all of them.
You will notice all the love that got into this every single minute of playing it.

Do I recommend it?
Without a second thought: Yes, absolutely.
Does it deserve all the praise it has been getting lately? Yes, absolutely - and even then some.

Good job and well done, dev. This is a game many will remember and will continue playing for (dot)ages!\t\t\t\t\t
