So. Many. Roads.

This may just be the biggest map DLC yet for Euro Truck Simulator 2 in terms of total road length. The road network in this DLC is massive! On the other hand, this also ruins sort of a "balance" between highway and country roads. Beware, as the main issue is that there's just too many highways, which might make the DLC look boring over long hauls.

Other than that, an impressive job by SCS as always! New industries, new textures and a respectable chunk of realism in this DLC. Totally worth buying if you're wishing to expand your map beyond DLCs like Vive la France!\t\t\t\t\t
