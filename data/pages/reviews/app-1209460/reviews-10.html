\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was on the fence after reading reviews about stuttering, Iberia being a "highway sim" etc etc
It does stutter a bit for me when I get to Spain, nothing awful (I play on a 3 year old laptop that wasn't bought with gaming in mind). Clearly this is due to the amazing level of detail in the sky, vegetation, buildings, vistas, etc. Some of the most breathtaking views in ETS2 are here. I love the viewpoints and overall I would rank this up alongside Scandinavia as the best ETS2 DLC's. Also there are some amazing small road areas in the actual cities, there is a beautiful (and very narrow) run between houses in the city of A Coruna for example. I also loved the new roadside event additions, such as tractors on fire by the roadside. Excellent DLC!\t\t\t\t\t
