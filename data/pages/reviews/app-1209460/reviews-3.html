\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first was not sure to buy this DLC after reading so many negative reviews. Now after driving several hours in Iberia, I can say that the map is quite good, with many interesant nice details and features by the panorama and the topography of the region.
Yes, is true, There a lot of highways but also we must take in count, there are not so many tolls in Spain. If you drive from the French border by the Mediterranean side, Catalunya, until Algeciras, there are only two or three tolls. In France or Italy for such a distance you have certainly more tolls. Roundpoints, yes, you have with Iberia a lot, hunderts,everywhere.
In Portugal, yes, you have many tolls but also many are automatic gates(so like the weight stations detectors poles in American Truck Simulator)taking the money without reduction of your speed. There also polls stations, and there the lanes of pre-paid(Card, 30kph)are at the left. In Spain this gates are at the right.
In Spain AI traffic work more or less as expected, sometimes a jamming by the entry of the highway, but that is not exceptional and you can see also this situations in Germany or France.
By Portugal traffic is more unexpected. AI brake too few or nothing before engaging roundpoints and special precaution to take by the tolls stations if you are in the prepaid left lane and a truck is exiting the right pay line. If this truck is near, give him place or you risk a crash.
By Spain, Algeciras area and Andalucia in general, is quite nice and there some places, farms and others in rural area to pick and carry cargos.
I have checked also the graphics, and eventuell drops of FPS and I have finded not difference with France. A stable 55-60 FPS allround.
To resume, is a good DLC. Recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
