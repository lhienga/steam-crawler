I know many ETS2 players have low or average level GPUs and this DLC is not for them and "them" includes me as well. The difference to other map DLCs is very obvious and I can definitely say no other map DLC is so laggy. I hope SCS will look into the case and optimize it. All in all, if you own a powerful GPU go for it otherwise forget it. It looks and it is very good but since it leaves a significant portion of ETS2 players "outside", unfortunately, I cannot recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
