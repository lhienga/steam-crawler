\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom the first day I bought the game I had the desire that one day they would release this DLC.
Although in this Steam account it appears that I have a few hours, I have been playing this game since I was about 11 or 12 years old.
I was very excited since it was announced, and it did not bother me at all the day they decided to delay the map because, I suppose that like everyone, I imagined that this DLC would be perfected.

I will have realized that I was wrong. I do not live in Spain, but I have visited it enough to feel disappointed with the map.
First of all, why are there so few back roads? The whole country is full of highways, while I have only seen about 3 small roads. I have also noticed that there are some things that do not make sense, such as traffic signals and traffic lights.

The cities have also disappointed me. Madrid is not bad, the industrial zone looks very good and it seems decent to me, but I can't help but feel disappointed due to the lack of more things that really identify the city where you are, for example the lack of a recognizable Skyline (As they have other cities in the game)
Barcelona did it better, but there are still things that would be wonderful if it were represented in the game, such as la Sagrada Familia.
It seems to me that they have given very little love to the cities, and I would like them to touch it up.

In everything else, the DLC is fine, and at least I can feel (half, of course) that I am riding in my truck through Iberia.

I don't recommend it if you don't care if you have Spain or Portugal.

Primero que nada, por que hay tan pocas carreteras secundarias?? El pais entero esta lleno de autopistas, mientras solo he visto como 3 carreteras pequennas. He notado tambien que existen algunas cosas que no tienen sentido, como las sennales de transito y las luces de transito.

Madrid no esta mal, se ve muy bien la zona industrial y me parece decente, pero no puedo evitar sentirme decepcionado debido a la falta de mas cosas que realmente identifiquen la ciudad en donde estas, por ejemplo la falta de un Skyline reconocible (Como tienen otras ciudades en el juego)
