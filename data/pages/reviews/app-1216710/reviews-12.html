\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game feels too linear for a game like this to work. There is either too much hand holding or very little, so the design is extremely inconsistent and I have encountered a bug where one of the vital information piece was changed in text in my version, (probably due to an update) which made me hard locked and unable to progress without a guide/walkthrough. SPOILER! I got 18th May 1983 for Ito's birthday when the game only accepted 16th in the database search. The calculation wasn't wrong, the information given was incorrect SPOILER !

Voice acting is atrocious and so is many of the mini games/puzzles. It would be more tolerable had some of the phone calls didn't require some form of trial and error and worse yet, is unskippable and unbelievably slow. Bad voice acting is fine when its optional. It's garbage when its mandatory. I am still waiting for someone to perfect this game style (Browser/internet/hacker detective subgenra). But while this one is pretty decent as an attempt, it is still a bad experience.

I still play it from time to time but each time I relaunch the game with renewed excitement and renewed disappointment.

The closest game I can think of to this that is actually any good is Her Story, and it pales in comparison because Cyber Manhunt has twice the ambition yet half the execution of Her Story. Overall severely disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
