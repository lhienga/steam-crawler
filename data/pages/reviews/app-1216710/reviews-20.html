Also sometimes the game would prevent you from pausing/using escape key. I think that needs to be fixed. The settings/menu should always be available to the player. It just feels bad design.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: What it lacks in interesting mechanics it makes up for in repetetiveness.

Slightly longer review:

You google names, go to their facebook page, click on interesting info and then google that, or enter it into the same five programs while the UI makes you wait for 10 seconds every time you found a hint. Then you answer some exams to check if you paid attention. If you expect deductions or logic puzzles, you will not find those here. It is all simply about reading and remembering information.

Sometimes there are minigames but they are also not interesting. One in particular really sucks, because you simply aren't given the information necessary to make the correct decisions, so you just click on random stuff until it somehow works.

It is one of those games where I suffered through the last 3 hours because I wanted to see the ending, but I had my head in my hands a couple times because it was just so annoying sometimes.

The writing.. well first of all, from a purely technical standpoint, it is bad. Clearly whoever wrote this is not fluent in english. There are tons of typos and grammatical errors all over the place. Things which could have easily been fixed by proofreading. Funnily, it gets better torwards the end. Or I just stopped paying attention, which is probably more likely.

From a stroy perspective, it is actually *ok*. Not great, not terrible. There were definitely some good parts, some twists, and some hateable characters. The main issue is that the gameplay is so boring that torwards the end you just use your mouse to scan over text really quickly to find hints, and stop actually reading it.

Overall score: meh/5.\t\t\t\t\t
