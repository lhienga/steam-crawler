Good:
- The game's story and art is good.
- Decent interface (well coded).

Bad:
- Puzzles often have unrealistic time limits or forces you to guess the answer, and after the time runs or you guess wrong you have to rewatch all of the dialogue before the puzzle started.

- The 5th chapter can't be completed at the moment due to a bug,
- The game doesn't reward you for thinking on your own when collecting evidence or hacking people (with a few exceptions).
