\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaven't played one since 2019. Just as terrible as ever but now you have xG to actually show you how hard the game is f*cking you.

Graphics look like a game from 2000 but apparently these are massively improved.

The sound during the game is like listening to an illegal live stream through your neighbours wall.

The new J-League has no appreciation for its own rules, mostly disregarding players trained at their club and forcing them to have massively reduced team sheets from the second season. The league essentially becomes a joke.

Press conferences are as pointless as they were 4 years ago, assigned to the assistant after 2 goes.

It all comes back to the actual matches - I found that changing tactics to almost anything else works for about 3 matches, then quickly falls apart. This is in EPL, J League, la liga. Developing my own tactics with my players or downloading a "top" tactic, it doesn't matter.

Loads of games with 15/8 shots/on target vs 1/1 and losing 0-1. This should be the freak, not the regular. It seems to be punishing the player for playing the game. All the player ratings are basically tied to goals and winning the game, so when you lose despite dominating (key passes, key tackles, etc.) your players score low, their avg. goes down as does their value, not to mention your league position, and it all spirals.

It feels like nothing you do in the game really matters; everything is either extremely RNG or it's scripted, but the game engine is a blackbox and you have no idea what is making these decisions. Do shouts matter? Are my tactics good or is this luck? There isn't a "combat log" that can show you how each dice roll was determined with various + and - based on your players stats, you are trusting the game to be a reasonable actor despite the evidence to the contrary. You can't work out what works and what doesn't, especially when you can reload a game and get a completely different result.

Outside of the match engine, the game is too much and too little information at the same time. I'm inundated with useless scouting reports, but I have no idea how my avatars national team is doing unless I actively subscribe to it. Players playing for their countries? No idea. An under 18 *exists* at the club? Here is a detailed report. Champions League results? No thank you. It's like being trapped in a room with an Alexa who is subscribed to every detail of your club, but all you really want is to watch the sports news channel.

The game has a lot to live up to, obviously, compared to real world media coverage. But it doesn't create any kind of world with excitement or intrigue. There isn't a Sky Super Sunday type coverage option where you could see the highlights, hear the pundits, see how these teams form has been. It all gets lost in this information overload, every inbox item is just presented with equal ♥♥♥♥♥♥ value.

After so many hours on this series, I'd love for this game to be a chillout, have something on tv in the background kind of game. Or an intense, high focus, detailed tactical game. But it's neither, it's just boring and frustrating.\t\t\t\t\t
