\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSadly, I can not recommend this game.
Sure it works and is fun to play, but I'm starting to see a trend, the same trend FIFA started showing all those years ago.

For example; The Squadplanner is completely useless and they show no effort to even fix it.
"But I can use it? I haven't noticed any issues with it?"

If you haven't noticed issues with it, it is because you are not rigorously using shortlists.
Whenever you have really huge shortlists the performance of the Squadplanner gets ruined for whatever reason. When you open the Squadplanner, the game slows down to a crawl and in worst case scenarios, the game can even crash if the shortlists are big enough; I've witnessed it myself.

This is a bug that has been present since FM23 Beta. Worst part? It was reported on their bug forum way back then and I did my own post in FM24 Beta. But it hasn't been fixed, it's still in the game.

Even though they say on the forum that they will look into it, they don't. Hence, the same bad feeling that FIFA started to give me years ago, where it is all about fixing superficial issues, issues that the broadest range of players will encounter.

It's the equivalent of a car, they fix the frame so it looks all nice and neat but under the hood there are glaring issues in the engine department that they don't seem to care about fixing, because most players might not even open the hood. Which is why you see constant fixes to the match engine, because all players are exposed to it.

I don't know, I think it's a really bad path to be on for this franchise.
Despite how many bug reports we do, it doesn't seem to matter unless it is a widely experienced issue or superficial at first glance.

There are other issues with the Squadplanner as well, such as that it auto populates, absolutely ruining any usage it might have, since it is used to create plans, but if those plans are constantly altered, then what is the point?

They also pushed into it some really important stuff such as the Comparison screen that is important as well as Assistant Reports etc. But forget about using these if you prefer to use shortlists for scouting purposes and to target players for your club. The bigger your shortlists, the less performance all these things get, since they're all under the Squadplanner main category.


The game is playable yes, but for how long? Some features aren't working and it looks grim in the future due to negligence. Squadplanner was one of the flagship features of FM23 and it hasn't been fixed since the FM23 Beta.

It smells a lot like FIFA and I don't like it.
They need to change course and start caring more about the quality of their product, not how many sales they'll get, otherwise history will repeat itself..\t\t\t\t\t
