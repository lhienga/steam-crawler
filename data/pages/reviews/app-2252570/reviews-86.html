FM24 is really the best edition of FM ever. No annoying new feature. In the past, they often add new features simply just to add something new. Some new features didn't make the game more enjoyable to play. Very annoying. But not this time. There is nothing there to frustrate you.

Set piece specialist is a good idea. However it has been implemented as well as it should. More or less par on the course. No ood and aah. But nothing that makes the game less enjoyable, either. A seed for future development, I guess.

The most satisfying new feature for me is the ability to play FM23 saves. With all the tactical innovations that pour out in the real world each year, FM inevitably is always one year behind. Now I can play 2022-23 season with 2022-23 tactics rather than the tactics from the year before. Very satisfying, indeed. Hopefully this feature will be available in FM25 as well.\t\t\t\t\t
