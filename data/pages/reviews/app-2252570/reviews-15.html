\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far the worst iteration of the game that they have released. I have been playing since FM14 and I can say its the first time Im not having fun playing football manager. The game is bugged beyond belief, you can tell the care and attention is gone for this iteration (Im hoping its because of the focus going into FM25, which I will of course, like an idiot, buy).

It might as well be called "Set-Pieces 24". It is a major concern when they put something in a simulation game that you can not control or counter-act, every highlight from the opponent is a set-piece, you can try to organise your team, but there is no confirm button on the tactics screen, any changes have to be put in the hands of God. Obviously, after every other game, all your changes are undone from the tactics screen, thats of course after you have conceded 4 goals from corners, especially fun once you've told everyone to "go back" and be in the box defending, but 4 of your players who are supposed to be defending the corner, are in the opposition box talking to their keeper.

Its also great when your players are scrambling to get back into position from, lets say, a throw-in from the AI, by the time you look up, the ball in already in the back of the net. They really did not properly test the set-piece over-hall they graciously marketed. The game would maybe work without the AI 80% conversion rate (1 shot 1 goal, I know "it happens" in real life, but not EVERY game) compared to 30% from the human, its just not fair. "Football is not fair" I hear you scream, well its a game, even when you do everything right, the game WILL find a way to frustrate you for "balance". When my assistant says "it looks like they are playing more attacking", there is a whole 0.17seconds before there is a highlight for the AI, where they will have a change with 95% chance of pain. Then they switch back to a defensive formation, and you might a swell be playing Mourinhos 04/05 - Chelsea (they conceded 15? goals that entire season). Oh, and good luck if you try to attack yourself, that timer will tick quicker than you can say "useless",

Penalties are broken, VAR every other highlight, In-game role changes don not take affect, and much more...
The most recent update has so many issues that they could have addressed and just plainly did not, as a dev myself, I know how much work can go into a project like this, I would love to take a look at the code base and see the hidden "frustrate" attribute slider they have going, must be stuck on 20 for me._

Don`t get me wrong, you can still win games, as a matter of fact, in my current journeyman save, i have 18 trophies with 6 teams, the only problem is, (for me), I can win 10-1 against some bottom of the league fodder, but if that 1 goal is a Scam, it really doesn't make sense, against the bigger teams, they will scam you again, without letting you score 10. I can count on my hand how many goals i have conceded from open play, or it wasn't the AI`s first shot on goal. Its embarrassing.

Its bad, buy it if you are a fan, its sad that THIS is the last version of THIS game we will ever get.\t\t\t\t\t
