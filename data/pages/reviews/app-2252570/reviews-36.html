\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game but with some reservation; Let me elaborate

I only buy Football Manager every few years and the last one I bought was FM 2015. Every year the latest edition gets a bit more complicated without adding playability to the game. With FM 2015 I managed to get Hearts FC to win the SPL every year and eventually win the Champions League on a regular basis. That was fantasy football and great fun. The only downside was that the 3-D game mechanics weren't entirely convincing with the oft qouted "ice skating on the pitch" in some evidence



This version of the game is much more realistic which means it's less fun. I manage The Spartans FC and and managed in something like ten years took the club from the fourth tier of Scottish football all the way up to the top tier. However I spent nearly all my time in the SPL avoiding relegation which was so frustrating I decided to delete that particular game and start again using the youtube tutorials . However this hasn't made much difference despite following all the advice from multiple sources on youtube

None of this is helped by needlessly complicated tactics and elements such as keeping the players happy. No matter what I do the players attributes often drop to an alarming level which is a pity because the game visuals are fairly impressive

Sega have made it clear that FM24 will be the last "Traditional version" . I've no idea what this means for the future but I do hope this means the game will be simpler to play. I for one am rather nostalgic for the good old days of Championship Manager where you could play a whole season in a couple of hours .\t\t\t\t\t
