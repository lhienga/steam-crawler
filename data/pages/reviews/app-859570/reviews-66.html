\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-:Genre:-
Fun PvP Social Multiplayer Horror game

-:Gameplay:-
Similar to Dead by Daylight but with simpler mechanics and cartoonier graphics.
Movement is dynamic, so door-blockers, bunny-hoppers and parkour-ers can rejoice.
Gameplay is five kids vs a Secret Neighbor.

-:Objective:-
For the kids is to find keys hidden throughout the house to unlock and exit with the win through the basement. Keycards may be required to access all parts of the neighbor's house during your search for keys. You should probably search all drawers, cabinets, etc that you can ;)

-:Is it fun?:-
Game is super fun, easy to understand and get in to at its most basic level without any understanding or knowledge of the maps. Just...make sure you can throw a box with passable aim! For veterans of the game who know all the neighbor and kid classes, the secret shortcuts and the parkour spots, games can be speedrun or be highly competitive and come down to the wire after a long 1v1 goose chase and battle. SN is dope with friends that are newbie and even more awesome with those that know how to play. Honestly, solo play can be just as enjoyable.

-:Note on Classes:-
Neighbour classes are pretty legit, but IMO the newer neighbor classes have some 'power creep'. Having said that, I think the neighbor classes are not outrageously imbalanced compared to each other.
Kid classes are pretty unique too but we haven't had a new kid class in awhile. I think I count two new neighbors released recently without any new kids following suit.

-:Longer Gameplay Thoughts:-
As the neighbor: Feel the thrill of the hunt as you plot to capture the pesky children running around your house. Track, isolate, trap and catch oblivious children! Against veteran kids stacking and working together? Not a problem, the neighbor is always ready for a throw down! Time is on your side as long as no padlocks have been opened and/ or you are occupying attention.

As a kid: Trust no-one, everyone is a suspect until you hear the distinct sounds of the neighbor either using their voiceline taunts, or as they use their abilities to capture someone. Once you know which neighbor it is, or even better, WHO the neighbor is, tread even more wisely throughout the house with said knowledge for it is invaluable! Choose your allies wisely for even if they aren't the neighbor in his guise, an innocent kid may give in to his carnal fears and abandon you, regardless of whether you made every effort to save them. Oops, one child lost in sacrifice for another. We can only hope they survive in your place! Fret not though, brave child, for maybe in your next life (game) you will be reborn (spawn) as the neighbor and maybe... just maybe...the child who spurned you in your last match is ripe for a real revenge reveal! *Evil Laugh*

-:Downsides:-
~Game is not overflowing with hackers, but their presence in a game is very noticeable. Needs serious anti-cheat/ hack fixes and also requires report system that is effective.

~There is also no real party queue system to speak of. This is pretty lacking in a game that's been around for awhile and also why wouldn't you include such a simple feature that makes it easier for their player base (especially smaller parties) to play with each other. The only way to do so is to make a custom game and get your mates in that way.

~In-game currency for long-time players is pretty much useless as the new cosmetic skins that are released are mostly to be purchased with the real-world currency, which is fine, but i'm just saying that we need some way to convert or use the in-game currency that we earn because long-time players have a MASS of it.

~Item collision can be a problem. Sometimes you walk into a small object on the ground and your movement gets slowed, stopped or altered in some way. Not a problem under normal circumstances...but when it's dark and you're playing as Scout, backing up while aiming doze nutz at the neighbor and suddenly you get stuck, dead. As your screen fades to black you hear, "Get out of my house! I see you! Boo!" spam :'( Now you spectator child :D


Other than the above, the game is VERY solid and VERY enjoyable for Children and Adults :D



EDIT: I did nominate this game for the 2021 Steam Awards 'Labor of Love' Award :)\t\t\t\t\t
