\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I had to be honest, this is probably the second-to-worst Tinybuild game I have ever played. (The first one being Hello Neighbor 2 Alpha) Mainly it's because the game is just too gimmicky, repetitive, and on top of that, you have to grind so many matches to use the shop, which from what I know the gear is useless. I got the game for Christmas, and since New Year's 2022 I have played only 72 minutes and the game was boring. I tried to give the game a chance, but it just doesn't make the cut for tense horror like Hello Neighbor's Main Titles do, not even a little. Really I think this game is for kids for what it looks and plays like, and now doing some background research on the internet and hearing what was behind it, I would never have gotten it if I knew it was just a cosplay free roam game. That is just Garry's Mod in a nutshell but with hello neighbor assets and models. Also the game has so many abilities and tactics to remember that you wind up just behaving like you would in normal Hello Neighbor gameplay, so it's practically useless to use abilities. Also the item and map are just so messy and spread out that finding items just becomes too confusing and there is no puzzles really relating to the house like the Main Story, so it really gets tiring looking in circles for items you may not know are there or not, or if it is somewhere secret. Also if you have no friends the game will be even worse having to play with random people, as randoms are kind of just there, and people never communicate. Really the only pro of the game is just playing as the neighbor. I'd give Hello Neighbor 1 and 2 a chance, but this game isn't good. Sorry, Tinybuild if that hurts you, but it's just my honest opinion. I am looking forward to hello neighbor 2 though, and the demo looks good. But I have to refund this. I am glad I bought it on sale, because it's also not worth the full price.\t\t\t\t\t
