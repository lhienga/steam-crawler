\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game, Secret Neighbor is a cinematic and universal achievement and a testimony of how for us as mankind have come. This game is packed full with secrets (pun not intended) that only the brightest and thought provoking people could understand. The symbolism of these characters that you control provide heavy lore implications, more than the frequently compared other hit games such as Five Nights at Freddy's, Resident Evil 8 and No Man's Sky. These characters expand the interesting universe established by the previous two entries in the Neighbor series. The main villain/anti-hero himself "The Neighbour" can be seen as a hero by some, much like The Joker, Arthur Fleck himself. This is reflected by the Neighbor being able to disguise himself as a clown to stop children vandalising his house. The bagger is by far the most complex character in the game (besides the neighbor), his bag is able to hold near amounts of items and is able to gain a 4th inventory slot. This introduces the concept of magic in the Neighbor universe and the ablility of witchcraft. The Brave wields a bat that is capable of reflecting weapons, revealing that the Neighbor house is located in America and the brave is a leader in Baseball, a classic American sport. The Detective seems to be the descendant of Sherlock Holmes which implies he was imported from Britain to America to cause the events of Secret Neighbor. The Inventor however is clearly a young Torbjorn from Overwatch, establishing a clear connected universe between the two series, perhaps in future updates we may see other characters such as Soldier 76 or Reaper in Secret Neighbor as we already have the Neighbor being able to use a hook just like the fan favourite and all around best character, Roadhog. The leader possesses the ability to make all surrounding players run faster. This could be linked to drug use such as ecstasy to enhance the performance of nearby people. While there are lots that point to the ecstasy coming from Walter White (from the breaking bad universe) no one can really say for sure - another example of Secret Neighbor's interpretations, allowing anyone to view the game as they want. And lastly the scout wields a slingshot, now it may seem that I'm reaching at threads here but I propose that the slingshot is the same that is used by Bart Simpson from the Simpson universe, this one is more speculative, however do take it as you want. Overall, secret neighbor is jam-packed full of mystery and intrigue. Me and my friends have had a blast uncovering its dense lore and I'm sure that any game that combines The Joker, Sherlock Holmes, Overwatch, Breaking Bad and The Simpsons is sure to be interesting\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am not going to lie, originally my friends and I made an agreement to get this game when it was on sale for a joke. We thought the idea of a multiplayer hello neighbor type game was hilarious due to the relatively janky and strange nature of the main title. Upon purchasing I can safely say we were all pleasantly surprised. The multiple maps of the game are very large with lots of winding paths and corridors and they are all fun to play on save one, however this is an option to choose which maps you wish to play on making this complaint unimportant. Usually in murder mystery type games like this it is only fun to play as the killer however, with this game that is not the case. Each one of the 'survivors' have there own ability and by extension, role in the group this prevents boredom from setting in as you can always switch between the numerous play styles. There is always a strange sense of tension when playing as a 'survivor' as you never know whether you are being lied to weather you are about to go 'missing'. Now for the neighbor. Similar to the children the neighbor has 3 different 'classes': the neighbor, the butcher and the clown. These all change the abilities of the neighbor. My one major complaint is that there are a few bugs that can cause a game to stop which of course is very frustrating. To summarise- the game is good with a few bugs but thats to be expected, get it on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
