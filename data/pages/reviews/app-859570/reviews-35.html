\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSecret Neighbor is an interesting game where there are 6 players in a house, one of them being the neighbor. The kids job is to escape by opening the neighbor's basement. The neighbor's job is to catch all the kids before they do that. Now, talking about the actual gameplay in general, there is room for improvement. The game is kinda unbalanced on the kids side, since they have not only to find keys for the basement but keycards to unlock rooms on the house. The problem is that depending on the map you're playing and the level of skill of the players, this is a bad thing. In a big map, for example, you have to travel a lot around it and keycards slow down the gameplay a lot, as well as for medium maps. Depending on the map, the keys won't spawn everywhere, but on the places where there are keycard doors, which also makes the gameplay repetitive and make you bored. I also need to say this game has lots of hackers, so it still needs an anti-cheat. Although there are some flaws in the game, you won't regret playing it, since the game has a level editor system where you can create and publish your own maps. There's also a tab called brawl where you can play other game modes and on special ocasions, maps from the community.\t\t\t\t\t
