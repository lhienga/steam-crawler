\r\n\t\t\t\t\t A game where you can kidnap kiddos without any effort whatsoever because they're literally in your house already lmao

I'd recommend this game simply because it's fun to play when you're itching for a horror-themed Among Us. Sounds specific until you play it, I know. Queue times are fast, gameplay is fun on both sides & I'd say it's worth the money.

Though there are a few downsides.. such as (of course) hackers, can't really group with friends outside of custom games & teaming. Oh yeah, once you know what to do (specifically as one of the kids), games go quick and objectives eventually become a bit boring so keep that in mind. Fortunately there's a few different gamemodes if you get tired of the same ol same ol.

Overall I love the game so far and plan to put more hours into it. If you have a group of friends, stop reading this review and just go buy it already.\t\t\t\t\t
