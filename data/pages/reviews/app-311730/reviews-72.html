No online a launch

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBad Port? Yes
Worth the current price? No
I preordered this game at a much lower price and I have anticipated that TN is going to release another mediocre port just like Dynasty Warrior 8 XLCE and Dynasty Warriors 8 Empires. So I didn't regret my purchase.

Graphics-wise, as everyone had already known, this game is closer to the PS3 version. But considering that the PS4 version doesn't look that much different than the PS3 version, i wouldn't complain (google it). The game is running at solid 60 frame per-second except during cutscenes which I am sure was intentionally capped by the devs.

Gameplay-wise this game is very fun, fast-paced, and different from other fighters. For those who have never played DOA, the key into winning a match is to get used to the timing and look for opportunities to counter your opponent. On harder difficulties, the AI is capable to read your movement and counter every attack.

If you are craving for a fighter on PC, you should get this game when it is on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
