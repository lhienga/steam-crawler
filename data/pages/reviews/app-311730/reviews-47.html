I don't mind much about the extra content and the softengine. This game runs smooth on my entry-class rig (shadows enabled, 720P+FXAA). so I think that's an OKAY call. But then again, they should at LEAST add an option to enable or disable the softengine for higher spec users (If it requires more power, that is.) And they should really allow modders. Some people like myself, I'm contented with the game as it is. But please, Tecmo, at least give users modding support so that they at least can customize and configure the game to their liking. And the lack of online play (Which will be released this end of June) really is a bit disappointing. But all in all, the game might be missing some features, but it still retained its quality of action. My suggestion is for Tecmo to release a patch where users can modify freely their advanced graphics option.

PROS:
+ Zack.
+ High replay value
+ Combo chains are creative
+ You can play a character for 20+ hours before getting bored of them. And the roster is huge.
+ Community is "Alright" (Besides those who keeps complaining).
+ Taunts.
+ Camera mode is fun to play around with.
+ Tag teaming!
+ Megane!
+ Zack has an island.

CONS:
- Zack.
- Missing features, albeit not "Game-changing", can ruin the atmosphere for others.
- NO ONLINE LOBBIES. (Yet).
- Cutscenes could've been redone in 60 FPS tho, but wtv. Most console ports are like that anyway.
- EXPENSIVE COSTUME SETS.
- Rarely updates, but most crashes might have been fixed already.
- Lack of advanced in-game settings. No advanced graphics options for those who want to enable advanced effects.
