This is a PS3 port that needs user made patches to play with some resolutions and has a washed down look that can be fixed only with third party software. The DLC's are atrocious, asking 60€ for some costumes is just hilarious.
Also the game needs some configuration to work with Xbox controllers, the golden standard of gamepads on PC, so yeah that's fun too.
Oh, and online play is dead and you cannot fight with your friends online.

So why the thumbs up?
Because it's still DoA, it's a good game and a great buy when on sale below 15€ (the free version is just a demo).
Buy it if you want it, it's a good unique fighting game and you'll have a ton of fun with two controllers and a few friends. The next best option is buying a PS4, and that's not an option for most people.\t\t\t\t\t
