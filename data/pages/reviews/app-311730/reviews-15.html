Dead or Alive 5
For the first time that I recommend a game because graphic ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)
------------------
LOL, forget about the joke above, it's time for a serious review below.
Gameplay: Easy to play but hard to master. There are 4 main buttons: Puch, Kick, Throw, Block (Counter). The core mechanic is simple just like Rock-Paper-Scissors, in this game Attack beats Throw, Throw beats Block (Counter), Block (Counter) beats Attack, very clear and straightforward. The characters are specialized for your fighting style, you can choose from the ones can attack very fast, or the others can combo throw (Chain throws) or the ones that can counter in many style (Which is very cool to play).
Graphic (The serious one): It is beatiful, trust me, I have played the game since it came out the first time in 2012 (On Xbox, of course), then the Ultimate version, and then this version. I have to say when it first came out, the graphic was mind blowing, I remember back then on all medias people talked about the "Dust effect" and the "Wet effect" on female clothes, epecially ones with white clothes when they get wet, it transparents, just like real life ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°). That one special effect was one of a kind, which you are not usually see in fighting games. This game focus on visual aspect so everything look great, the "Blast effect" when hitting opponents is satisfied.
Music: Don't mind about the music anyway ... yes it has some music ... but most of them are not interesting. You can forget about music, I can remember the rap song they use in the trailer ... terrible ... (The one and only good music in a very good stage called: "Zack Island").
Sound: Opposite of bad music, the sound did a really good job, the sound of hit + "Blast effect" makes every hit feels impact ! And of course *Whisper: The female voice acting* ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°).
Story: DON'T PLAY THIS GAME FOR STORY ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°) WHAT DO YOU EXPECT ? A GOOD STORY ? PUT YOUR EXPECTATION ELSEWHERE. Let me make it short, the story is: "Nevermind".

Conclusion: Dead or Alive 5 is a good and fun fighting game for newcomers to fighting genre and professionals alike. With big (Enough) roster of 34 characters in the Full Game and 2 hide behind pay-wall (Naotora Li from Samurai Warriors and Mai Shiranui from King of Fighters).\t\t\t\t\t
