\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is actually a very competent and deep 3d fighter despite it being infamous for fanservice and for what it's worth, default settings and costumes have the game actually look pretty reasonable looking and indistinguishable from a typical video game. This is far from a soft core p*rn game although I personally wish the franchise moved away from its more sexual aspects.

Gameplay wise I cannot comment on the competitive aspect of this game since I have not delved into it at that level despite being a fighting game nut. All I can say is the game has a large amount of depth and is a competent competitive fighter. However, I have had great fun with this game casually as a badass martial art game. The game's animation and cinematic flare with stage transitions is shockingly good, better then Tekken by comparison.

Give it a shot. It's free so you don't exactly have anything to lose. It was worth my time to the point where I bought the full thing.\t\t\t\t\t
