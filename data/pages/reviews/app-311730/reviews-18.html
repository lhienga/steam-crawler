\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoA 5 completely abandoned what made 4 such an awkward game and returned to the core of great gameplay like in DoA 2U and DoA 3. The story isn't particularly phenominal but the fact that they've added it on top of the arcade is a big plus. Online play is decent but the amount of players is very low nowadays. That's not anything to hold against this game however. The triangle system of combat is one of the most balanced systems in place for fighting games today. Stages are incredibly fun with lots of interactive environments, all which help give an edge in fighting but not enough to throw the fights off. Character selection is great, and most of them are balanced with their stats and styles to offer a good variety of whom to play versus whom. If you can get past the stigma of what the series has become, you'll enjoy this game. The only con I can see is how the DLC is so outrageously over-priced. Having to pay for the story and pay for the characters is not acceptable and a major con, but if you are willing to spend $50 you'll pick up most of what you need there.

All in all this is a fighting game and series second only to Street Fighter for the PC. I only chose this over Street Fighter because I don't like SF's style, graphics, and most of their characters on top of the fact that it is a 2D fighting game. Definitely worth a buy if you like balanced 3D fighting games as this is the best one you can buy.\t\t\t\t\t

- Fighting mecanics are pretty good and simular to games like Virtua Fighter, there is also a counter system too
- Online multiplayer and a few offline modes for single player
- Decent keyboad and controller support for pc
- Constant 60+ fps and good preformane on all maps (this will probbaly depend on your rig)
- The girls
- Music is pretty good too, there is also tracks from previous games like DOA3
- Good training mode
- Good voice acting (Both Japanese/English Dub)
- lots of DLC outfits (mostly for the girls)
- Mods

cons
- Story mode is locked behind a $20 USD paywall for the F2P version (unless you pay for the full game version like i have)
- The DLC is Overpriced (usual ♥♥♥♥ for koei tecmo so i am used to that, unfortunatly some DLC go up to $100 USD)
- Unlike the ps4 version of this game the dlc outfits dont have a damage system
- No online lobbys (i dont think its due to a dead player base, nobody plays online no more)
- Some maps are missing from ps4 version
- No soft engine
- PS3 Grafics
- the developers have pretty much ditched the pc version and keep updating the ps4 and xbox versions of the game

While I do recomend this game, I still think that the PS4 or Xbox One versions are the best bet for people intrested in getting this game\t\t\t\t\t
