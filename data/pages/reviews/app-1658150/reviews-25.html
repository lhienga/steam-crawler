\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a deck-building battle simulator, this is pretty fun. There's a lot of things you can decorate your base with as you progress which is pretty nice.

I just wish NPC interactions were fleshed out some more. Currently you can date them, but dates are basically a few lines of text with a multiple choice question: one answer is great, one is good, one is bad, and you get or lose "reputation" with that NPC depending on what you choose. The dating system could use an update to make it more interactive, add more places you can go, etc. You can only go to each place 4 times, and after that it just feels like you're at a dead end and either 1) just can't do anything with that NPC anymore; or 2) you break up with them and you lose all your rep with them, plus they give you the silent treatment for a full in-game month. It's just not a good system IMO.

Finally, some seasonal events would be nice. A fall festival for example, or a gift-giving exchange in winter, etc.

So as it is now, I can only recommend this for the deck-building/battling and the base-building aspects of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
