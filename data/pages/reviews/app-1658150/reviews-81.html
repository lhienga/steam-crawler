\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEnjoying it so far. Only bug I've encountered is giving a gift and not seeing the text response from the person.

Summary: Reminds me of a mix of Zelda/Harvest Moon/Coramon. Randomly generated islands with main island. Random mini caves to explore. The only thing I *wish* I could do is have my spirits reduced in size so I didn't have this giant house following me around (Edit: You can actually do this in the medallian and ask them not to follow you.)

The health/stamina system is novel. Instead of an hp bar, you're solely focused on a stamina bar. This makes for interesting issues. If you're using a lot of stam in a dungeon, you don't have a separate bar to work with as you go around. Need to stock up a bit more.

I would probably give this an 9/10. It's solid in the sense that each mechanic is enjoyable. I will re-edit later as I go through the story as to whether it has the same replayability as something like pokemon/harvest moon.

Edit: I'm loving the deck building. "Talisman" can change the cards by adding/upgrading/removing from a spirit. It's a really cool idea that gives you control over how a deck plays. I can see a lot of possibilities with it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, I was completely put-off by the lack of character creation / character customization. The design for the main character seems to vary wildly from more masculine promotional art to more feminine backdrops in-game. I'm honestly confused on what they were going for in terms of the main character's design. In RPGs, I much prefer to make my own character in the world, but I can often accept a well-made, defined character instead. Moonstone Island has neither. You are just an avatar with no real personality nor identity outside of being an Alchemist. A very odd decision for a game with relationship mechanics.

In terms of gameplay, I can't really comment. The initial features seem promising, but I didn't play long enough to get a full grasp of the mechanics. My avatar in an RPG heavily impacts my personal enjoyment, so I simply didn't want to explore what else the game has to offer. Hopefully, the devs or dedicated modders can remedy the issues I have with the main character's lack of design in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
