\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a great cozy game buuuut... You get really into it and then... you hit a wall and it feels like the magic is gone.
I know that this is a newer game and the devs are still working on adding more and improving the game like finally having rotating gates! However, once you catch all of the sixty something spirits, close the rift, get a significant other, collect all the plants... then that's pretty much it. I wanted to get all achievements, but some of them are extremely time consuming and I decided to give up.
You spend a lot of time creating a relationship with some of the towns people but once they accept to be your significant other, nothing happens, no benefits, like, nothing....
Also, the decorating part is nice, but it's lacking a bit, you cant really create village things like roads and such, so it's okay, it would be nice to have a community custom made tiles and such. I am not a big decorator so i just created a bunch of chests and the "home improvement" doesn't really have any benefits. Maybe if it was co-op I would be more invested.
I wish you could customize your character!
Also, there is not really an incentive to keep leveling up your spirits aside from collecting all of them in the dark islands...Once you have them all the battle system is pointless. There are no other islands or anything like that or a way to go to a different town.

Overall I had a lot of fun playing the game, but I wish there was something else to make it worth playing or looking for resources/exploring.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNice idea, but the game is just boring.

-Takes Ideas from Pok√©mon but is as deep as a puddle.
-Forced Farming into the game (Because every game needs farming, ever since SDV), but doesnt really do much with it (You can find all the ingredients in the world and just run with it)
-You're an alchemist, except that youre not? Youre a monster tamer, at best.
-The game has a decently big world to explore, but it doesnt matter really. Islands are basically all the same (Except for *insert x-hazard here* that requires you to build the anti-hazard gear, but they're all just damage in different intervalls)
-The Monsters look cool and cute, but they are all basically the same? You just pick the one monster that fits the element you need, because they all learn about the same abilities, have the same stats (Because you pick their stats on level up) and therefore can all fulfill the same roles.
-Dungeons are inspired by Zelda, but as if theyre made by 5 year olds, they have 0 depth to them
-Since it's SDV inspired we also need mining, which improves the same tools as in every farming game, except that it doesnt matter, because its not like you can get new/rare materials, you just destroy the same materials, faster. And your bigger farming tools are also irrelevant, because you get sprinklers from the start of the game, so you never have to water/replan your farm and therefore never need a better watering can/hoe.
-The cast seems.. bland? First of all, its like 10-12 characters at max, and while its great that the game is queer-friendly (including characters that refer to themselves as they/them), it falls for the same tropes are non queer-friendly games do, except now we have non-heteronormative cliches. Instead of busty barmaid/barkeep, we have muscle mommy blacksmith. Instead of the "i love all nature"-flowergirl, we have the pierced alt girl. The designs look nice, but the characters lacked personality from the first second on. I havent played the game for a long time, so maybe this changes as you get to know the characters through cutscenes etc. but given how bland most of the experience so far was, i wouldnt bet on it)

The overall experience for the game simply isnt fun. It's bland and thats sad, because i went in with expectations of being able to spend 20-40 hours on the game, casually playing through it, but after 5 hours i have basically seen everything there is and now i can decide on whether or not i want to see the same monsters in different costumes, which i propably wont.

TL;DR: Bland, boring gameplay, no depth, no progress, nice character designs that are dumbed down onto 1-2 personality traits, 5/10\t\t\t\t\t
