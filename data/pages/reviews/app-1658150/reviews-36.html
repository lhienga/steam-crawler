\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a delightful game! Coming from Stardew Valley I didn't really know what to expect but it's definitely addicting. The islands are fun to explore, the overall design is cute, the monsters have really interesting cards and it's a nice deckbuilding/skill tree for beginners. The farming is optional but can give you really great boosts, many of the tools feel unique and the boss battles feel perfect for a relaxing experience (this might be a downside to many people, I don't really enjoy fighting).

There are a few things to keep in mind: the NPCs don't have typical dialogue, instead you learn about them and the main story through dates. They can also feel a bit boring when you're halfway through their hearts, and entering a relationship with them only gives you a few more dates. The main storyline is quite small, you can get an idea with a few comments from NPCs and the last boss battle. And some dungeons can be quite tedious, just fighting about 10 battles to get to the boss.

Even so, I really enjoy the game and will continue to clear out the map and get some holos!\t\t\t\t\t
