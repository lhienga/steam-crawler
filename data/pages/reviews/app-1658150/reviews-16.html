\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe core mechanic of this game is a card-based battle system. As a result of that the game is going to sink or swim based on the core mechanics of that card system.

And..... the card system is very, very boring and lacks anything clever or interesting about it. Basically either target an enemies armor or HP, and they're HP is nearly inaccessible until you wear down their armor. Sound boring and lacking any decisions? Yes, that's precisely correct.

You can tame multiple little critters to your side and each of them have a deck of cards that can be managed. However, the cards among all critters are generic and boring.

Would have been a much better system to have your main human protagonist do most of the fighting and have the little helper critters just provide some unique benefits. That way they could all have some unique and interesting reasons to exist. Looks like they lacked any creative ideas on how to formulate the core systems of the game and just tried to do a bad ripoff of Pokemon instead of anything compelling. Which is a shame because the rest of the game looks very cute and charming and compelling.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCute fun little game. Gather your spirits and fly from island to island looking for resources, battling spirits with your custom decks and building your base while forming relationships with your villagers. Good cozy game.

Pros:
-Giana
- cute art style
- nice soundtracks
- flying exploration is fun and interesting
- unique spirit designs with abilities and decks. The same two sprites can have different weaknesses which makes team and deck building fun.
- farming and tool upgrades and getting money comes pretty quick. Just fight spirits.
- Good on Steam Deck


Cons:
- tutorial is virtually non-existent
- super difficult to find moonstones early on, they're supposed to reset every season, but mine did not ther first season.
- limited use of furnace, one bar at a time, so you can stack a bunch.
- it's annoying to run from the observatory to the barn and the medallion to switch out spirits.
- levelling up spirits can be grindy, better off growing a bunch of eye plants and feeding them EXP fertilizer tk level them up.
-storyline is meh.\t\t\t\t\t
