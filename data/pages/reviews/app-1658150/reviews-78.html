I love the exploration from one island to another, the deckbuilding mechanics for the fights are extremely engaging and it has a well designed SKILL TREE which was a huge surprise, it makes every action feel rewarding no matter what you do in the game.

WARNING: don't expect this game to be another Stardew Valley or a Pokémon. It's clearly inspired in those games in many ways, but Moonstone goes beyond in so many ways, you can focus onn different activities and gameplays without breaking the game's flow.

One of the best games I've played this year, so refreshing after the bad taste Starfield left on me.

Congratulations to the devs♥

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is everything I’ve wanted in a modern life sim game. On a surface first impression, I was constantly thinking, “oh that’s a neat way to implement that mechanic.” It’s not just a regurgitated copy of every dated mechanic from games released in 2010. This game has fresh and new ideas and makes small tweaks to everything from the farming to the mining and combat.

Speaking of combat, thank the lord there’s finally a game where I don’t just smash A on a monster in a mine. In this game, you get a simplified version of a deck builder similar to Slay the Spire, as well as dungeons that will remind you of the classic legend of Zelda games with their puzzles and layouts. Combat actually has a rewarding value to it and is captivating rather than a half-assed thought out addition that was put in because “well every other farm game has it.”

The soundtrack slaps and is also reminiscent of LoZ. The graphics are vibrant with colors that make me happy just looking at the game. And then there’s the Pokémon aspect, because this game just kept delivering on making a FUN sim game. You get to capture creatures which all come with their own set of decks that you’ll be able to customize as well.

The NPC art is incredible and everyone in this game is hot. I want to marry everyone. You get to build friendship via a dialogue option that has 3 different levels (chat, joke, flirt) and different chances of raising or lowering friendship. It makes it feel less like a recycled chore and more of a game, because this is a game and every feature should actually be fun.

All in all, get this game. If you like turn based RPGs, or creature captures, or just farm/life sims in general—get this game. You won’t regret it.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCard combat system can feel a bit flawed early game since if you have a lower level spirit with your decently leveled spirit, it just feels like youre mixing your good cards with trash ones from the low level spirit. I recommend leveling up everyone somewhat evenly if you can to mitigate this cause once you have an even team, the synergies between spirits feel really good to use which makes combat fun.

I wish animations was juuust a touch faster, 1.1x maybe? I also wish when you fish you dont automatically face down cause I sometimes waste stamina by using the fishing rod on the ground.

Story is very light hearted and starts off really cute. NPCs dialogue start off a bit too flirty even when you just met them and they barely even have half a heart in romance.

First 2 hours of the game is somewhat slow but I like how you can immediately do your own thing right after the tutorial and the game doesnt hold your hand anymore after it. It makes it feel more immersive and adventure feels more rewarding.

Highly recommend if you want a chill game that has combat like pokemon mixed with some card RNG with gameplay loop of farming, romancing, and adventure.

My only cons about the game maybe is the art of the NPCs. I wish it was a bit more cute and anime. Less western-retro-ish. I also wish there was a character creator too.\t\t\t\t\t
