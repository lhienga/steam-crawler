I thought the monsters looks funny, kinda similar to pokemon. There are shinnies here too.

I stopped playing the game because the farming feels.. ehh. It will give your monsters buff, and even help you level up faster later on. In the end, if you want to chill, farm some pokemon like monsters and lvl em up quick, try different tactics and stuff. This game is for you.

Farming and dating, not so much.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing games on and off all my life. As we age, we have priorities that doesn't allow us to spend too many hours on our hobbies at home. Most of the recent games require some effort and time before getting into it, so I don't typically play games that I need to carve out chunk of my time for. This game was perfect for me, because it's not overly complicated and gets you straight into playing. It's a mix of stardew, pokemon, and a deckbuilder. I've always found stardew and pokemon to be lacking in combat aspect, and deckbuilder type combat gives it a nice twist. I tend to stat playing a game and then quit early if it doesn't hold my interest, but I've played this game for 16 hours and I still have an urge to come back, which is saying a lot. I'm sure the urge won't last forever, but it's holding up better than some of the AAA titles out there for me.\t\t\t\t\t
