\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game I never knew just how bad I needed in my game life!!!

-PROS-
It is a perfect blend of cozy, wholesome adventure along with some fantastic RPG. For those who love to decorate, you are in for a treat, loads of craft-able decor items, shop items and housing upgrades. Exploration is a huge bonus in this game, with 100 islands to explore, collect resources, treasure maps, dungeons and catch spirits. Making money seems to be straight forward, especially with some "sell items" that specifically state "for sale only" no more hoarding useless things in fear of not knowing to sell them. That is a game feature I wish all games had. This game also has a clear story line that is not only cozy but super fun.

The beginning tutorial is fantastic, clear and easy to follow and learn
Auto-save and a back up manual save along with 9 save slots for multiple play through
Opening any menu will PAUSE the game, such a FANTASTIC feature and much appreciated.
You have the options to truly play your way with loads of difficulty levels, from how easy, normal or hard your battles are, to how often spirits spawn. Truly a game mechanic that some tend to overlook, but this game DID IT PERFECTLY.
While some complain on the time speed, I have never found an issue with the length of my days, I am able to accomplish everything in my day. Its no less time than any other farming sim out there.

-CONS-

NPCS while date-able, are a little dull. The chat mechanic in game is an interesting one with it being based off of a percentage. However, I wish when we did talk to them they actually talked back. The only information we ever get is on a date and even then its usually one or two sentences.

Fishing mini game is IMO only good if on keyboard, I have tried multiple times with my controller and I can NOT catch a fish using my controller. Which is a huge disappointment for me since i love playing with controller.

Speaking of controllers, some mapping and buttons will only recognize as xbox, even though when I use my playstation controller it all works, except in battles when it tells me to use Y instead of triangle. That can be a bit confusing if you don't remember the xbox layout. DEVS, If you are going to have controller support make sure the button descriptions match that controller you are playing with.

All that being said, I absolutely LOVE this game, It is easily my favorite game. I wish other games that released were as fun and mesmerizing as this one. We have had such let downs lately in releases (cough cough some fairy like farming games) that this one knocked it out the park.

Would I recommend? I will say this, while I love this game, my opinion is not always like others, but for me with weighing both pros and cons and price point I would say yes. It really is a great game, the devs truly have a special love for it, you can see that in their discord and the game. I do not think anyone would truly be disappointed in trying this game.
