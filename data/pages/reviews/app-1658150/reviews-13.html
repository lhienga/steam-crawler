\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're hesitating to buy the game because of negative reviews, just know that most of the complaints I saw when deciding whether to purchase have been resolved. Bugs get fixed, features get added. So if there's a specific issue you're worried about, check the patch / update notes because there's a good chance that it's no longer relevant. This is one of the reasons I purchased this game, and I have not regretted it. Seriously, there were a few minor things I didn't like about the game, and they were added / fixed / improved in the most recent patch,

As far as game-play goes, this has everything I loved about playing Pokemon, and more. There's so many cute spirits to collect, and they can walk behind you, which I adore. The combat is turn-based and uses cards. NPC relationships I'm not sure about, since that's not an aspect of games I gravitate towards. But I can tell you if you're like me and don't want to talk to NPCs that much, you can just spend days hopping from island to island, taming and battling spirits. When you make some progress, you can use a glider and it's super fun and relaxing.

The farming is not a huge part of the game - at least not compared to farming sims so if that's what you're mainly looking for, you're going to be disappointed. But if you're into collecting cute creatures, exploring, dungeon diving, and battling, I think this game is worth playing.\t\t\t\t\t
