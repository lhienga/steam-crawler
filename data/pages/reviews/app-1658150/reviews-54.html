\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game, the gameplay loop is really addicting and the spirit combat is fun (so long as they don't nerf burn/poison bc thats my entire strategy). It feels like there are plenty of things to do each day, so if I get bored of one thing then I can switch to another. I do have complaints but they are more like suggestions bc I really want to see this game improve even more.

I think the main thing that's lacking is the NPCs, I wish there were cutscenes between them like in Littlewood, or mini storylines/quests like Rune Factory. The main 'storyline' was underwhelming at times since there wasn't much acknowledgement of it, I just want more of these characters bc I do like them, especially Magic Man. I liked getting them spirit friends, it would be fun if we could battle against them since once I've beaten all the dungeons there won't be much point in battling anymore.

Still I have a lot left to do in the game and I know it's a four person team so the game is incredible as it is, I just want to be able to play it more once I'm done with everything xD

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was hesitant at first with this game, especially when getting used to movement and the interactive box in the over-world. I am pleased however that I stuck with it.

The positives do outweigh the negatives: combat is satisfying; the quest-lines have rhyme and reason for WHY they need to be done; the dialogue is witty and engages you (NPCs have distinct personalities); and exploring the numerous islands in your seed is far from a chore. I can confidently say that you get your moneys worth from this game.

There are a few things to mention, though: the interactive box used to plant crops, break stones, hit trees, place furniture, interact with crafting tables etc is very temperamental on controller (likely avoidable on mouse and keyboard); depth perception when dealing with dungeon traps takes getting used to; and the music (which I enjoy) does seem to stop for what feels like forever, leading me to switching it off in settings and listening to my own.

I've enjoyed every hour spent in this game, however I seem to have a rarer bug where a main quest-line is locked from my game (reported in forums). I struggle to see myself playing this again if not rectified, however judging from the devs logs, I'm confident this will be sorted in due course. Recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
