\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed the exploration aspect of this game, finding new island types, delving into dungeons, finding new spirits, and discovering the cards unique to their types.

I also liked the dialogue with the island characters, some really cute moments. I also appreciate that they didn't just add a basic "marriage" button that does nothing (looking at you Stardew Valley), though I hope they do eventually add and flesh out this part of the game.

My main critique is that there isn't much to do after you've finished exploring what's there.

It's too easy to optimise your spirit's decks, and even so there's no need to since combat is easy after you understand the basics.

Making potions with alchemy feels like an afterthought, even though that's stereo-typically what alchemists do. It's simplistic, tedious, and with little to no reward.

It's a casual, cute, and exploration focused game, with a bunch of small gimmicks that add to the initial sense of wonder but unfortunately disappoints due to lack of depth.\t\t\t\t\t
