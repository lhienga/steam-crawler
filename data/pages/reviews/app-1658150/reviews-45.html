Pros:
1. The NPCs are fun
2. The storyline is cute
3. The vibes are INTENSELY cozy and comforting
4. Everyone on the island is queer af.

Cons
1. Leveling up is a grind, it's much faster/easier to just go to a higher level area and catch new spirits, as once you're past low levels you get almost no XP. That's fine, but its also not as fun as levelling up a "buddy" who sticks with you throughout the game.

Otherwise, overall had a ton of fun, but if you're a completionist it can be quite frustrating.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMoonstone Island is exactly what it advertises itself to be. And it is exactly what you expect it to be. And while there are a lot of negative reviews that criticize the game for lacking in a lot of areas, I am not going to give it a thumbs down. I agree that it is strongly lacking in some regards and there are a lot of things that can be worked on to improve the overall experience and longevity of the game, but those are not points in and of themselves to warrant a thumbs down or a bad review.

The game tries to do many many things, and while it succeeds in having those many many things, it falls flat on almost all of them in terms of engagement and length of play - but it is not without its fun. I do think the game is worth its price for what is currently offered and I do think that you should give it a shot if "Cozy" games are your cup of tea, the community is friendly and inviting and the discord is always active. The team takes feedback and suggestions and answers questions pretty quickly and they seem to be really passionate about the project as a whole.

I do think that maybe the game should have been marketed as "Early Access", but I do understand why they didn't go that route. But, sadly as Steam only allows for an 8000 character maximum review, there is not enough room for me to give my full thoughts on the game. If you are interested and would like to read a full detailed dive into the game as well as my own thoughts and opinions on it, then I have begun writing a rather lengthy review/guide on the game over on Medium.
