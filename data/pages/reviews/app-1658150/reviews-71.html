\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit after 42hrs:

I "beat" the game (aka finished one year ingame) and I loved my time with Moonstone Island a lot. I have more cons than pros, but they're more like suggestions, that and it's because I like this game a lot and want it to succeed, so I'm hoping my cons here can be addressed and make the game even better in the future.

Pros:
-BEAUTIFUL artstyle
-There's a lot to do. When you become tired with one aspect of the game (farming, fishing, etc), you can just go do another. It's hard to get bored.
-The battling is genuinely super fun.

Cons:
-So it's not just in the mines, but in all of the game there's hardly any music that plays. Most of my playthrough was in silence with just the sounds of my footsteps.
-The romance and villagers are extremely shallow. They all kinda feel the same. The dates you go on only last like 10 lines of dialogue and adds nothing new or interesting to the characters themselves.
-Decorating and upgrading my house was cool at first, but eventually there was no point in doing so. I upgraded my house to have a second floor and never saw it because I had no reason to go up there, nor did I have a reason to put anything up there since it's too far to walk to compared to the main foyer, wasting less time in my day.
-Fishing also doesn't have a purpose, besides the quest for Tobin, but the reward for that is just to make it easier to fish, which again, has no real purpose.
-I wish there were seasonal events in the game that could help flesh out the villagers more and add more of a reason to visit the village.
-Magic Man isn't an option to watch the fireworks with! :'(

----
I'm not a fan of farming sims usually. What brought me in was the monster taming element similar to Pokemon. But when I played the demo, I was pleasantly surprised and found myself really enjoying the package as a whole! It was easily my favorite demo from Next Fest and the same sentiment applies now as a full game.

As much as I like it though, there are some things that I would love to see implemented in future updates to make the experience even better:

-Explore the romance further. It's shallow as it is now with no option to actually engage/choose dialogue options other than click "talk/joke/flirt" and see a number go up or down. It doesn't make me invested or interested in pursuing the characters at all (though I may just be spoiled now from all the otome games I've played lol).

-Add music to mines. It gets pretty quiet in there when I'm mining, and I'm in there for a while.

-Add more tracks to the battle segments. I'm 5 hours in and though the one track we do have for fights is good, I would love to have some variety.

-Earlier (or just outright) implementation of fast-travel between islands. Again, I'm 5 hours in, and I haven't encountered any form of fast travel yet, which would be so nice right now.

-Character customization!! Not a must, but it WOULD be nice to spruce up my character with some fashionable clothes.\t\t\t\t\t
