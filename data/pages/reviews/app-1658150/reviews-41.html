\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCan easily miss out on a LOT of moonstones (one per island visited) per season. Not nearly enough emphasis is put on the importance of hunting down the moonstone on each island (at night) every season. A map indicator for which islands still have a moonstone to grab before the season changes would be a huge improvement. An audio indicator when entering an island with a loose moonstone would also be welcome. Apparently there's a moonstone detector you can get quite late in the game, but it doesn't do the things suggested.

Is there a way to see how big of a group it is before fighting wild spirits? With over a dozen islands visited, the game seems to jump from fighting a single level 1-6 nonthreatening spirit to fighting groups of 3 level 9+ very threatening spirits with no in-between.

The card system feels too heavy on RNG. Basically every spirit is going to start with a couple utterly garbage cards that need to be removed. Catching spirits above level 1 gives a very high chance that they'll have even more garbage cards. The skilltree increasing the chance of good cards also encourages basically abandoning your current spirits and starting with freshly hatched ones once you get all the passives increasing the chance of good cards unless you want to be constantly starved for a *very* restricted resource to improve/acquire/remove cards. Ease up and give more consistent access to talismans, so people can build the decks they want as they get new spirits.

Once in combat, because of the janky armor system, there's a good chance you don't get any armor shred cards, accomplish all of nothing, then finally once you get a couple armor shredders and stun them (with the potential for big damage) you get a fist full of armor shred the next turn with no damage cards.
Another emphasis on bad card spread RNG is the janky stun system. One of your 3 spirits gets stunned? ♥♥♥♥ out of luck, 5 of your 6 cards next turn are for that spirit. If hand draws are entirely random, that needs to be fixed. There needs to be weighting so getting 5/6 cards for a single spirit is impossible, and getting all armor shredders or zero armor shredders is also impossible.
It isn't interesting, fun, or challenging to simply get bad rolls of the dice to an oppressive degree.\t\t\t\t\t
