I rolled several of the new events while I was testing this as a child ruler, and can say it does add more flavor (which you need as much as you can get with your limited actions as a child). Most notable to me was that my child ruler had a favorite sweet that several events revolved around (in what did not seem like a connected chain) and an event fired with my self-interested regent deciding on how I dealt with his inattention.

I think the enrichment is obvious with these examples, and I would not want to lose it or the other parts of W&W.\t\t\t\t\t
