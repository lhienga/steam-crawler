\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI do recommend this event pack. For the price I think its worth it and I'd buy it again. However, I don't think it was quite what I was hoping for out of an event pack themed around raising wards. I think I wanted more family, child gameplay and events for me to bond with my heirs. Maybe more to do with raising your heirs in general, and I don't feel like I got that. If you are a big Sims 4 fan who wants more family roleplay in CKIII - then this isn't going to impact your game significantly, but if you want to just flesh out your game a bit with content around Wards, then sure, it's good! I'm still glad I got it.

POSITIVES & NEGATIVES
• The education events are nice and adding some variety here is nice, but I can't tell a huge difference personally, other than the fact that I do think I can have my kids' traits aligned sooner. And I do often have children with 4 traits now, which is kind of cool.
• University is neat, but won't be relevant in early play. You have to build up to access it and then have enough money to do it. At that point, you're normally already jacked from genetic traits. It's cool though.
• Regents were a great addition with the Tours and Tournaments Expansion, but i haven't really encountered that impacting me more when I play a child with this pack.
• Hostages aren't what I really wanted, but they've honestly changed gameplay the most. In the right situation, they can help tell a really interesting story and I like the events that accompany them.\t\t\t\t\t
