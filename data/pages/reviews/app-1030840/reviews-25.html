\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat can i say, only a masterpiece, amazing story. I didn't play original Mafia, the only game from Mafia trilogy that i actually played is the Mafia II, long time ago. When i saw they made a reboot I coudn't resist, sure i buy the game on sale and didn't know what to expect but darn it, it is amazing game. Sure it has some of the problem like linear gameplay which doesn't really give you possibility of chosing how you want to end game. It would be nice if the gameplay is more fluid, open world like and that you have options, instead you must go by the script.

Story is what is the best selling point of this game, at least to me it is well done, voice actors did a splendid job, and you had feeling like you are there, like it is real. Combat system is little clunky but still nicely done, it has realism, you dont need to spend entire clip by shoting your foe to head to die, one hit in head kill enemy. Driving, many similar games have big problems with driving, and they nailed it. You can feel the car and it is little bit harder then in GTA but its darn more realistic especially for cars from 1920-1930 era. You cannot expect fast acceleration on them but they act as they should, especially when there is rain. Overall fantastic game. It should be longer, more missions, side missions but it is okay as it is. I hope one day that if Mafia 4 is done, it will be done properly but still i am glad i played it. Do i recommend Mafia: Definitive Edition. YES!! If you play original, you will not be dissapointed by reboot version. If you don't want to buy it at full price, buy at sale and try, it is great. It lack in some areas but still, it is great.\t\t\t\t\t
