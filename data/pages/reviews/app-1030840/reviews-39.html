\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter the first four short introduction missions you get to play an old bucket racing simulator which is almost unbeatable without mastering very specific driving mechanics (I played on Classic but I heard complaints that lowering the difficulty does not help much), so you've been warned. But, if you win the race, here's what you'll get:

Pros:
+ Outstanding story
+ Unforgettable characters
+ Amazing music
+ Atmosphere
+ Decent graphics (city and transport)
+ Good gameplay (shooting part and probably driving because it's claimed to be realistic, while it may be frustrating sometimes, it brings some challenge)

Cons:
- Graphics (NPCs, Tommy and some scenes sometimes): the models and their animations look outdated (the cutscenes look great though), they just don't fit the realistic picture of the city.
- Gameplay: I would say that the missions could have been designed better because they feel outdated too. Like it or not, but gameplay is a key part of almost every game (except the interactive movie genre) no matter how great the story is.
- The race: it is a very questionable game design idea as by the moment the mission starts you didn't even get to know the game, so if you are not familiar with the original, chances are you will rage quit and uninstall the game.
- The credits song by Ice Cube. It's nice but what the f?

P.S. I didn't play the original back in the day but I've seen a playthrough and watched some videos comparing these two games, so I am aware of the differences but I cannot agree with those who say the remake is worse than the OG.\t\t\t\t\t
