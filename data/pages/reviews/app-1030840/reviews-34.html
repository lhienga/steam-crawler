\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMafia Definitive Edition is the remake of the classic 2002 game bringing the 1930 s to life with modern graphics, gameplay improvements and refined narrative. Without losing its essence this is a gift for the nostalgic fans and a great introduction to the Mafia games for those who are new. The game successfully balances action sequences,immersive storytelling and period-accurate exploration.

With themes of loyalty and friendship but also betrayal and revenge,Mafia offers a complex narrative with moral dilemmas and a strong accent on character development. By recounting his involvement with the Salieri, Tommy is confessing his past actions searching for redemption,a way to unburden himself from the things he done but also to make a deal to protect his family.

The narrative follows the story of Tommy Angelo,a cab driver that becomes entangled with the Salieri crime family. Drawn into a world of organized crime,Tommy climbs the ranks and earns respect and privilege to be part of the family. The retrospective narrative provides insight into the consequences of being part of a criminal group .

Mafia Definitive Edition is a substantial upgrade in terms of visuals over the 2002 classic, it's a stunning recreation. The game benefits also from improved controls particularly in shooting and driving mechanics.
For me it was a great experience, it felt like a polished version of the 2002 classic. I had so much fun driving and exploring,even if this doesn't feel like a complete open world experience.

In conclusion Mafia : Definitive Edition pays homage to the original release with a cinematic experience ,one to remember ,a gift for everyone who loves a good story and a excellent introduction to the Mafia games for a new player.

-Graphics

-Attention to detail
-expanded content

Cons:
-limited open-world exploration
-Linear mission design
