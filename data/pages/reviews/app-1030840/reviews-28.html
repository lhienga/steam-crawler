\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my all time favourite games from one of my all time favourite franchises. It was the first time playing this one, previously playing both Mafia 2 & Mafia 3. Like the other two, this one features excellent performances from its new cast, a fantastic driving model, and a beautiful and authentic city oozing with 1930's atmosphere.

It feels and sounds very authentic all though I found the accents of some of the players to be very off, yes its a small thing but still, an Italian American should sound Italian American and an Irish American should sound like an Irish American.

I enjoyed the story rich play that this franchise is famous for but I found the story mode to be very short, clocking the entire game and all its collectables in less then 10 hours and I felt like I was taking my time.

The game is so good I got sad when it ended that's how good and short it felt. I highly recommend this game if you are a fan of anything and everything to do with organised crime, the 1930's and the Mafia franchise in general. 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
