\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a avid fan of open world shooter games, like GTA V and RDR2, I decided to try out the Mafia trilogy as I heard generally good things about the series. I enjoyed this game very much. The graphics are beautiful in the Definitive Edition, although my PC struggled a bit in terms of frame rate (if your CPU is old like mine, you might struggle to play this game). As I am more used to the mechanics of the aforementioned Rockstar games, it took me a little getting used to in terms of shooting, since the reticle is a circle and not the most accurate for shooting. I was also a bit disappointed that this isn't a true open world game, since the environment is very beautifully designed and yet you cannot explore the world freely. It's pretty much a linear set game, one mission to the next. I liked the progression since I have less means to distract myself, but I also like exploring.

I found the story much more interesting than any GTA game, but that's just personal opinion. The characters were interesting, and the voice-acting was pretty good. The cutscenes look fantastic, too.\t\t\t\t\t
