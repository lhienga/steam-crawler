\r\n\t\t\t\t\t*This Review is strictly based on the fact that this game is a rework of original Mafia 1 from 2002*
Simple recommendation - If you like mafia type games, mafia story lines, you for sure want to check this game out. You get your old school vibes, old cars, mafia story line, high life etc.
More in dept review - Just because this is a remake of a legendary game, I feel like Hangar 13 went a bit too far away from originality of this game. Sad that Hangar 13 didn't even offer voice acting to original voice actors from Mafia 1, that really cut a big part out of the nostalgia. Overall game and missions felt great, the city, radio, just wonderful place to be. As far as missions themselves, everything was great, was not really enjoying end of the game. Missed the cut scene of the original of Tommy being in jail before getting old and spending rest of his life else were, but other than that, was a really huge fan of the game. Free Roam, in my opinion, little less to do in there, after you finish main story, only 12 side missions made game feel like that really quickly ends. Overall, happy to say that I have finished all Mafia franchise, and recommend this game for those who enjoy mafia and past in general.\t\t\t\t\t
