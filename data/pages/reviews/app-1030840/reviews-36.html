\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen a long time since ive had my hands on a Mafia game (first for me was Mafia 2) and honestly im sad I didnt play this sooner. The gameplay is smooth and enjoyable, the vehicles feel like heavy classics skidding around the streets and the gunfights are fast and dangerously fun. There are bugs in the game but nothing game breaking. for example: I found myself taking damage once while simply vaulting over a barrier without any combat present. Another bug I experienced was the characters awkwardly teleporting during a silent take down however it didnt cost me any stealth during the mission. The only bug that caused me to restart the game was when a cut scene failed to activate when driving to the checkpoint. Despite a few minor bugs I still found myself immersed in the story, picking out any foreshadowing and just listening to 1930s music on the radio (which is always good on the rainy nights). Ive seen a few people complain about the length of the game but I think I got my 14 hours worth especially when you take a lot at the $70 price tags for most games these days. I would highly recommend this game to any mobster fans out there determining whether or not to give this game whirl. Will definitely be playing the remaster for Mafia 2.\t\t\t\t\t
