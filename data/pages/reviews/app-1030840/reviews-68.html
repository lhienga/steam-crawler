\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's practically a rage game.

The story is flawless, nothing wrong with it. However, if you want to play to enjoy the story, i suggest you to play it on normal difficulty. I myself never play it in normal, only in hard and classic cuz I seek the challenges and thrill presented by Mafia II (classic), which I played long before this game was released. Sadly, i don't got the same challenge and thrill from this game,.

The shooting mechanics are solid, although not as good as Mafia II. Unfortunately, this aspect is hindered by minimal feedback system: enemies don't get knocked back when hit and can still shoot the player. Thus, players must truly understand enemy movement patterns to shoot them without losing hp. If the player shoots other than at the head, enemies continue to shoot as if nothing happened. This is exacerbated by some enemies aggressively advancing towards the player's hiding spot, especially on hard difficulty—they are unstoppable!

The game features a fast-paced action element. However, the character animation of Tommy (the main character) is exceptionally slow, making him vulnerable to enemy attacks. As an example, it takes about 4 to 6 seconds for the player to get into and start the car's engine before being able to drive. It really suck, especially in situations where the player surrounded by the police, like in the "Election Campaign" chapter.

Melee combat is utterly unsatisfying; players only need to blindly press the "Q" button (if playing with a keyboard) until the enemy falls. If the enemy attacks, the player cannot cancel their attack, and the only response is to press the counter button.

The fuel system is poorly executed. The game NEVER tell the player about it and gas station legend in map is hard to notice. When your car ran out of gas, you have to take it to gas station to make it moving properly, otherwise it will stay like that. However, since the game never tells you about fuel system, you will think that there is a bug that makes your car broke, at least that what happened to me, it's give me a hard time till i check the community tab.

Above all, the worst aspect of the game is its level design. Despite its Mafia-style shooting, the game heavily incorporates out-of-place racing missions. However, the real issue is not the inclusion of racing segments, which are common in games like GTA, but rather the extreme difficulty and lack of enjoyment in these races.

In the Fair Play mission, especially on the classic level, players are not allowed to crash or fall behind because the two leading racers are truly unstoppable. This makes completing Fair Play nearly impossible.

In a side mission to obtain the mutagen car, players must follow a series of checkpoints in a rally. However, the checkpoint scope is very small, and some placements are misleading (the next checkpoint located behind the one just reached). Additionally, traffic in the game is also problematic. When players are required to obey traffic rules to avoid police attention, cars on the road always disrupt this, often appearing suddenly in the middle of intersections when the player is moving at high speeds.\t\t\t\t\t
