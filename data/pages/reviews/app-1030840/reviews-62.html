\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou don't play Mafia for stellar gameplay. The gun-play is stiff with almost no variation in combat scenarios, all shooting gallery's with some pretty terrible AI to the point that some guys will just stand in the middle not taking cover at all. Driving is eh, it's set in the 30's so part of it is your driving cars that aren't far removed from the Model T. And a kicker for a lot of people, this is not a true open world game. There's a city and at some points you can drive around and not head for your objective. Thing is there is nothing to do in the city except finding collectibles.
So saying all those negatives, why do I recommend this game? It's the story, the story is fantastic. Now if your familiar with Mafia movies and pop culture, you'll be getting more of what you like. It's not state-of-the-art, it's not the Godfather, but damn is it good! Get this game on sale, and kill 8-12 hours with a killer story.\t\t\t\t\t
