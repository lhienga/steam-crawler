- it's short. i just want to get that out of the way, this game is extremely short. don't come in expecting grand theft auto or red dead level of interactivity or length.

- excellent graphics and decent optimization. i use a rtx 3050 with a i5-12400f, and i usually got 75-100 fps for missions in lost heaven, but dropped below 60 frequently on countryside missions, but i can understand, forest-y areas in any game can tank performance, from my experience at least.

- the story is excellent. one of the games that's really made me feel anything while playing it, the game is extremely immersive too, even if it's breakneck pace can make it seem like its jumping back and forth.

overall, should you buy it? yes. buy it.\t\t\t\t\t
