\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was there when Mafia 1 came out. I was at the occasion of announcement for the official release back in the early 2000s. When the game came out, i didn't wait and purchased it. It's probably the most "played through" game in my life till this day and I play it (Mafia 1) at least once a year ever since. I stopped counting after 24th playthrough.
The definitive edition is far away from "definitive", unfortunately. New visuals mean almost nothing to me. The game is underwhelming at best, annoying at worst. The interface and controls are very sad - non-responsive and stupidly frustrating because of the "take cover" mechanism. The game is missing serious sections of the original and feels very empty. Don't let me start about the physics aspect which was a showcase of the original 5 years ahead of any competition in same/similar genre.
No challenge getting new cars or doing quite challenging sub-quests. It's like a school run race, where everyone gets medal for participation. It leaves good impression of the cinematic story, but very bad aftertaste because of wasted potential. Seems the developers and the distributor wanted to appeal to modern audience, probably taking "it" for completely stupid, lazy and not able to value good challenge and better game mechanics.
Unfortunately it's not a definitive, nor good "successor" of the original. The storytelling is bit more dynamic (not entirely a positive if you ask me) and parts of the story changes make sense. The visuals are better (duh), but if it's for the cost of taking away from the other strong aspects of the original game - it's not a good deal for me.
If you are lower on IQ, new to gaming, or loving game consoles and some "standard" modern day "arcady" game mechanics, this is for you. If you expect a gaming masterpiece (especially for PC gaming) with strong story and exceptional game-play, rather move on.
It's still worth playing just for curiosity, but don't pay full price for it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t- Can't say the shooting mechanics were amazing, but it was fine, I did not find it special.
- Enemies are pretty hard to defeat if your playing above the normal difficulty (you will rage), the AI is sort of dumb at times. Fighting with fists or weapons were wacky and boring, too easy after you figured what to do. Killing feels epic.
- Quite a bit of driving in this game, if you can't handle the cars, refund. Took me some time to get used to the car handling but it was not horrible by any means, even started to do some sick drifting on turns, BUT THE BIKES, I don't want to talk about them, they feel like boats, can be seen in the "You Lucky Bastard" chapter.

I am really sad. This game was so close to being perfect for me, like a tiny inch away and I would fall in love with this game. Throughout the game, I just really wished this game was more open world, sure you can explore the world after, all alone :( but the story line is SOO straight, no side missions or anything, nothing, something special I liked about GTA games.

I was really skeptical about the theme and story, thought I will find it boring, HOWEVER it was amazing, and I am surprised considering my taste is really leaned towards modern things. This game is special because of the story, You will enjoy it.

8/10
