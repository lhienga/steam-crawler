\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst time playing a Mafia game, since this is a remaster of the 2002 Mafia game base on the standard of that time this game is great. I like the graphics, the map is so beautiful and each scene is well done. But I'm not a fan of the story is kind of standard nothing really too special. Overall I like all the missions but they are quite similar.
Very few weapons, and I really don't like all actions map to a single button, reload, open doors, first-aid or pick up stuffs are all button x.
And its actually a linear game, yes you can drive around town wherever you want but there is nothing you can do on the map other than driving or maybe get five star and get away, I will say is more like a interaction movie.
The driving system is amazing if you like driving around on a beautiful map this is the game to go with.
Graphic: 10/10
Gameplay: 7.5/10
Story: 6.5/10
Music & Audio: 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
