\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBorderless Fullscreen works about 50% of the time. Lose about 14 fps in full-screen versus borderless.
Experienced a crash less than 3.5 hours into the game.
Almost no accessibility options, but you can adjust key bindings.
No DLSS or AMD FSR.
You get ads in game to buy Mafia 2 and 3, but you can at least hide that message permanently if you want to so that's good.
2k Launcher is annoying.
No saving function in this remaster; you have to play-through a mission to get to an autosave.

In terms of the game itself, I liked what I played so far. The story was interesting and the gameplay was enjoyable enough. Lost Haven is nice. Sound is good too. There are all new voice actors in this game compared to the last one, and it isn't the original developer that did this remaster, but I did not play the original so this wasn't an issue for me.

If you have a PC that exceeds the recommended specs by a lot and can comfortably run modern games (released since 2020) in Fullscreen at 60+ fps, I would recommend this game, and I might consider playing this again once I upgrade my PC.

But this remaster was designed very poorly on a lot of levels for a game released in 2020, and the game is unplayable in borderless fullscreen, so I cannot recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
