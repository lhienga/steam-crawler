\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHavent finished it yet, but so far its pretty great, and does everything a remake should. And it succeeds moreso than the majority of remakes released in the last 5 years. It actually has a reason to exist, for one, the source material being so janky and dated that any half competent attempt at modernizing it would be a serious improvement. This is in contrast to something like the dead space remake, who's source material still looks and plays great, and who's genuine gameplay improvements are overshadowed by the (in my opinion) downgraded animations, sound design, and voice acting. Mafia: Definitive Edition not only goes above and beyond with its art direction, the writing and voice acting are top notch and without a doubt leagues above the original. Again, not all the way through, but (almost) every cutscene I've seen has been very tightly animated and acted.

Another thing I like is how faithful to the original it is while not getting all puritan. Nothing it adds takes away from the original vision and only serves to enhance it. The original's gameplay is archaic, pretty much busted. Definitive Edition is silky smooth. The shooting isn't anything to write home about, but it still feels good to land headshots, the guns sound good and the reload animations are very detailed. Driving takes getting used to, but once you get into it it pretty fun, which is why I chose not to skip driving segments, another improvement more games should include. To contrast with another remake, Demons Souls does the exact opposite. It did nothing to improve the gameplay. As far as I know, they pretty much copypasted the original game's code and slapped 'pretty' graphics on top. It improved absolutely nothing from the original. What we're left with is average gameplay that looks like a Raid: Shadow Legends ad, and a version of the original's soundtrack found on a stock music archive.

Overall, pretty great game so far, even if its not perfect. Stands up there with the Resident Evil remakes and is more than worth the 40 dollar price tag.\t\t\t\t\t
