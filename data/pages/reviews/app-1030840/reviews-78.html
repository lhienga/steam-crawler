\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat I liked in this game:
+ Characters - main character's development; chemistry between this big dysfunctional family; great voice acting
+ Plot - although short, predictable and 'generic' it does tell the story in a captivating way - I didn't get bored by it
+ Driving around the nicely designed locations
+ Action sequences - they're not anything amazing, they're rather simple but enjoyable - although I didn't play classic difficulty
+ Music and noire vibe in general
+ No major bugs

What I disliked in this game:
- The infamous race - I did have some problems with it despite me liking racing games
- Collecting all things may get boring and annoying without guide
- Bushes - don't drive into them, those are immortal
- Lack of things to do around the city, unless you just want to go on a rampage and kill everything that moves

Overall, I recommend buying this game.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMafia Definitive Edition is an amazing and immersive gaming experience that successfully resurrects the glory of a classic game. With stunning graphics and meticulous attention to detail, the game manages to recreate the dark and intrigue-filled world of the Prohibition era.

One of the most striking aspects of Mafia Definitive Edition is its distinctive atmosphere. The developers carefully crafted every corner of the city of Lost Heaven to fit the lifestyle of 1930s America. From vintage vehicles to period-appropriate clothing, everything exudes an authentic and deep sense of time and place.

The story is compelling and emotionally charged, with characters that feel truly alive. Players will engage in the tale of the protagonist, Tommy Angelo, and witness dramatic shifts in his life as he becomes entangled in the world of crime. The gripping narrative, experienced with genuine intensity, makes it difficult to relinquish control of the game.

The gameplay is highly satisfying, striking a good balance between action and role-playing elements. Diverse and challenging missions provide a non-monotonous experience, offering a variety of strategic choices to complete tasks.

Moreover, Mafia Definitive Edition updates its technical aspects. Updated graphics, high-quality sound, and an engaging soundtrack contribute to an overall captivating sensation.

In conclusion, Mafia Definitive Edition is not just a remake but also an artistic masterpiece that pays worthy homage to the original game. For gamers seeking a deep, emotionally resonant, and visually stunning experience, Mafia Definitive Edition is an excellent choice.\t\t\t\t\t
