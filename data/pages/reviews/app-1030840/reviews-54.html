\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who played and beat the original Mafia, I can't express how grateful I am for this remake enough. Though I did love the original game, the controls were just bad, and driving was an exercise in futility. This updating of what was an already good game, is vital to anyone getting into the series.

Now, this game is far from perfect. The story can probably be wrapped up easily in under 10 hours, maybe even less. There isn't any additional content either. Also, you're never really able to explore the open world too much because you're always going from mission to mission. I think there is a mode that let's you explore, but it isn't part of the normal game play.

All-in-all, it really is the definitive experience for the first Mafia game, and if you're looking to get back into the series, or start again from the beginning, it might be better to pick this up than play the original.\t\t\t\t\t
