Story mode: One mission after the other without breaks or free roam in between missions
Free Roam: Literally nothing to do but drive around, it has less features then the original (Where you could shoot the black dressed gangsters and gain points - no point system exists - no type of mini game nothing)
Motorbike mission + Race: One of my most annoying experiences just wanted it to end as soon as possible

No real reason to keep playing - you could play the story again on a few more difficulties but kinda pointless
