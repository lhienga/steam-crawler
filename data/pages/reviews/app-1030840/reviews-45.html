\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCompared to the other people, I didn't had much problem with driving. But the race episode, oh god.

Atmosphere feels awesome, and I can clearly say that it was the only reason why I played this game. Combats were nice as well. But the scenario felt blank. Episodic way of storytelling wasn't for me. Plot twists were too obvious. After some point, I just played it to finish it. I didn't like the ending either.

The game also made me realize how I got used to storied with multiple endings. I was expecting some choices. Felt like it should've choices. Also, an open world gameplay as a daily life of mafia could be nice as well. But after all, it is a remake. So you can't ask much I guess.

If you love 1930s, mafia aesthetics. Go for it.\t\t\t\t\t
