\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCirca twenty years ago, I watched my dad playing the original Mafia: The City of Lost Heaven when it was released. I instantly fell in love with the story, the characters and the "aesthetic" of those years. When this remake came out, I can't describe the joy and the hype to see my favorite characters and story recreated all brand new from the ground up!
Now that I finally played it I can say that they surely made some changes; some positive and some...not necessarily bad but still not great. Anyway, over all it's a well done remake! Even the infamous race was still doable on Classic. Just remember, the controls seem "crazy" because these are OLD cars, so of course it's quite challenging, mostly in Simulation Mode. But once you get the hang of it, it's a piece of cake (well, almost, you're still gonna crash but -hey!- not as much as before! :D)
Only thing I wish they implemented is being able to enter certain buildings and areas (not just Salieri's) in Free Ride; instead you need an external mod for that which is quite silly, in my opinion. :/

SPOILER

I wasn't expecting the dialogue at the end to be changed. From "Crime doesn't pay" to "Family is everything"...meh. But nothing is worse than the song you get right after you finish the game on Classic. Nothing prepared me for that and honestly, it quite ruined the mood. \t\t\t\t\t
