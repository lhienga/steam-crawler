\r\n\t\t\t\t\t 7/10

Pros:
- Visually appealing
- Pretty challenging
- Excellent story
- Authentic atmosphere
Cons:
- Traffic is awful
- Driving is not the best

Pretty atmospheric game, despite being short, gets you in an immersive and interesting journey to America in 30s. Somebody may say that it is too linear, but it is best perceived as a story or a ‘book’. The visuals are appealing, characters - interesting and the gameplay is pretty challenging. The only thing that got on my nerves was the driving and the traffic. Driving is kind of the same as in the original game, but traffic feels as if developers intentionally try to interfere with you driving. Apart from that it is a pleasant experience. Definitely worth a try.\t\t\t\t\t
