\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI regret not having played the original version. I tried a few years ago, before they announced the remake, because I had played Mafia 2 at the time and it quickly became one of my favorite titles, mainly because of the storyline. So, I tried playing the first part to learn more about its world and characters, but I couldn't; it had aged too bad.
To my surprise, a few years later, they announced the remake, and I was very excited. Finally, I could close the complete arc that I had liked so much.
Now, regarding the game itself: Graphically, it is beautiful; the vehicles, environments, and characters are sublime. Every detail is carefully crafted.
Unfortunately, it's just a pretty picture to admire because gameplay-wise, the title is very weak. They could have taken advantage of the remake to add more variety, something else to do in the open world, perhaps a shop to customize our character, some mini-games, anything that would make exploring the city enjoyable instead of a chore.
Once you finish the story, there's nothing else to do. The game could easily be a linear video game since the open world is empty and lacks activities. I think that's the worst part because, in other aspects, like the plot, the game is brilliant.
There are also some issues with the animations, a bit clunky, but I've just finished playing Red Dead Redemption 2, and there's no comparison; maybe I just need to get used to it.
To give it a rating, I would give it a well-deserved 7 out of 10, mainly for how gripping the story is, which, for me, is the fundamental pillar in any video game.\t\t\t\t\t
