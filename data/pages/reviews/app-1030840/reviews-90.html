Mafia: Definitive Edition is a full-scale remake of the original 2002 classic. While appearing similar in looks and style to GTA5, Mafia focuses a lot more on the narrative and story it has to tell, with the definitive edition including a free-roam mode, where you can explore the city of Lost Heaven in your own time.

Technically, the game runs fairly well. Aside from some abrupt texture and model pop-in at close ranges, the game is a visual treat, with ample detail to recreate the feel of 1930s America.

I was most impressed by the driving in the game. It feels much more weighty than you would expect, and I enjoy the feel of driving every vehicle the game features. The soundtrack is another stellar part of the game, using original motifs and melodies, but also a fresh true sound; the music really is timeless.

For a nicely crafted story, excellent open world, mobster themed gameplay and a sweet-as-♥♥♥♥ soundtrack to accompany it all, Mafia: Definitive Edition is certainly worth a try.\t\t\t\t\t
