\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, here I am, after 22 years, playing this game. The gameplay isn't too impressive, and if you've played the original Mafia 1, you'll understand what I mean. The original Mafia 1 is the hardest game I've ever played (except for the Souls-Like category), and it's really realistic for its time in 2002.

On May 13, 2020, 2K announced Mafia Definitive Series developed by Hangar 13. Hangar 13, hmm. I remember on July 18, 2015, 2K announced Mafia III developed by Hangar 13. After Mafia III was released, everyone hated it. The story isn't about the Mafia, the open world is awful, and all the missions are the same.

When Mafia Definitive Series was announced, every single gamer said, "F*ck that studio, that game will be awful." On September 25, 2020, Mafia Definitive Edition was released. It's not a AAA game, but it's working well and stays true to the original story with a few new mechanics. The graphics aren't awesome but look very good. Sounds are really good. Animations aren't as smooth as the original Mafia II, but it's not too bad, and the missions are faithful to the original. Cutscenes, oh man, they're like movie scenes. Every single detail is awesome in cutscenes. Hangar 13 deserves applause. They've taken a cult game and turned it into an really good one.\t\t\t\t\t
