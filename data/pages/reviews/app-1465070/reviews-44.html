Legendary Tales
If you are a fan of VR Sword fighting games with physics but also want to experience it with your friends, then look no further. Game is in early access so there are some bugs but its to be expected, for now, it's a fun game to play with you friends

Multiplayer
Of course its no Blade and Sorcery levels of physics and content but I would say it makes up for that by allowing co-op between 4 people. It's a version of peer to peer networking so no dedicated servers for this, you will notice lag and latency if the person you are connecting to have bad upload speed, you have bad download speed or you are just far from each other, your fps also plays a role in it so connect to the one with best fps. I have tried it from both ends when playing from Sweden with a person from Scotland, I have a decent connection(100 up/down) while the other person have less than ideal(20 up/down). The latency does not make it unplayable in this situation but you will still notice it.

Graphics/Level design
It has decent graphics and decent physics, and a big game changer is the stats and skill trees allowing for personalized builds making it more fun to replay as well as you can try something different.
The level design is decent, first time you enter the dungeon it will be really good but as you start wandering about everything will feel familiar. The randomly generated levels are true to an extent, the map it self does not change but everything inside it does change.

Looting system
Loot progression is fairly well made, with the addition of the Legendary equipment which makes you feel really powerful.
When you kill an enemy or destroy one of many objects it may drop a crystal, these crystal come in different colors which indicates rarity. The weapons/armor that drop will have random stats and buffs except the legendary equipment which can only get different values depending on your level. There is no equipment upgrade so when a weapon start become low level all you can do is sell it or save it for another character.

The price tag is a bit hefty but they also need to get the money to work on it from somewhere. If you don't like it, get it when its on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
