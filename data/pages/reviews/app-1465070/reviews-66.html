- The player characters have been nerfed massively again because the developer cannot forgo the pursuit of making the game as difficult and frustrating as possible for us to complete. (God only knows why they are like this)
- Skills are just a huge confusing number crunch with few useful and no fun abilities.
- Weapons are 99% throw away and scrap items. Not satisfying at all unless you get a 1/1000 legendary item drop that happens to be the 1/20 weapon you can actually use. So good luck. Even then they typically have bad RNG stats.
- The game play is dependent entirely on ping when online with friends so expect to be unable to parry, shoot bows, etc. unless you are hosting.
- They gutted the entire second half of the game (levels 50-100 are now gone), probably just to avoid letting players become strong enough to enjoy killing enemies. So you repeat the entire game again in challenge mode at level 50 (Enemies, dungeons and items are all lvl 50).
- The only thing you can gain is an extra 12 skill points (for sale in exchange for honor) that will probably take 20 hours of grinding to earn, and all for skills that are useless at that point. The difference is completely negligible. I would say Challenge mode is a complete waste of time unless you just want to play the game again without getting anything out of it.

Overall I don't know how a team of 5-6 Developers spent 5 years making this game unless it was a weekend project. It's incredibly rough around the edges and you can tell with out a doubt that they struggled to release it as soon as they did. To ask for $72 CAD for this game is absolutely criminal. I do not like the attitude of the developers, they keep patting themselves on the back and proclaiming greatness in a product that is still to this day just a mediocre asset dump.

The ONLY good part to this game is hanging out with other people. If it was a solo adventure it would be completely worthless.\t\t\t\t\t
