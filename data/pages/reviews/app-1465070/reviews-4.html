\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's got some jankiness and yes I would say it's overpriced but the game itself is pretty fun, especially with friends : )

You can craft your own weapons, there's a good selection of different weapons, there's different areas and dungeons to explore with enough checkpoint portals that you aren't gonna worry too much about retreading the same ground because a skeleton wearing timbs and brandishing a tyre iron keeps clonking you for loose change.

The combat isn't quite as satisfying as B&S but it's definitely not too far off either.

My only qualms are:

1. No character editor or clothing system. You get a static player model, and the female one is just a sexy elf, which would be fine if there were a clothing system, which is currently limited to gloves and helmets, neither of which apply any visual changes at least to the female characters.

2. A more vibrant world would be nice? Some animals you can hunt for meat, trees to cut down for wood. Currently you only get these crafting items from killing skellies with the boys. Hunting would give the bow a bit more usefulness as currently using a bow is much more skill based than a big stick or a big ball of fire.


Tl:DR: Fun dungeon crawler with frens. Ovepriced. Slightly Janky.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is one of the most "game-like" vr games I've played, by which I mean it feels like an actual game instead of a dev showcase of vr capabilities. The swordplay feels great, while it doesn't have fancy things like being able to cut off individual limbs the game more than makes up for it by having enjoyable parry mechanics and an exciting variety of enemy attacks. Personally, I'm a bigger fan of video-game style health bars than the usual realism that vr games try to go for and I'm happy the devs went in that direction. If you want a vr sword-fighting game that isn't just an arena sandbox, Legendary Tales does it best.

On that note, I'll start addressing some of the cons:
  • Calibration seems slightly off, my arms are goofy long for my character's body.
  • New quest system seems buggy, I just wave my arms in front of the quest thing and spam buttons until it becomes interactable.
  • I still wish you could reset stats more easily
  • In my opinion, weapon modifiers/skills could be more varied/impactful.
