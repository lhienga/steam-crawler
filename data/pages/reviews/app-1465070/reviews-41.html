\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe controls for the game are currently unsatisfying. Interacting with the inventory in its current implementation is a pain, and the tutorial kept breaking for me, which prevented me from completing it, and unable to leave the game since you cannot access the menu while a tutorial pop up is activated.

The game's largest offender is its over reliance on the joy stick. You have to push up on the joy stick to open the menu/ inventory, and push back on the joy stick to put items in the inventory. I believe joy sticks should have minimal interaction while in a VR setting, as it just simply feels weird.

I believe this game has potential, but in its current state I don't believe it is worth the price, even while it is %20 off from the summer sale. I will likely try this game out again in the future when more updates come.\t\t\t\t\t
