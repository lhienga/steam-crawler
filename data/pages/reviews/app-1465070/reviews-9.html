\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLooking for a VR RPG game that you can sink your teeth into?
If you dont mind a bit of "VR jank" - this game is absolutely a must play!

PROS:
Great crafting interactions
Great looting mechanics (white, green, blue, yellow and orange rarities like an MMO)
Full RPG levelling system (with secondary stats and passives)
Multiple build paths for different playthroughs
New game+ (challenging)
Co-op multiplayer CROSS PLATFORM WITH PSVR2 (I play with my brother - this is awesome)
Plenty of mini games and sidequests to keep you busy
The bosses in this game are HARD -> this makes the game FUN - you cant just flail your wrists and expect to cruise through this game
There's even in-game loot boxes for random gear you can buy with IN-GAME currency (NO cash shop thank god)

CONS:
Sometimes buggy with questing/interacting/hitting (this is the VR jank)
Price is on the expensive side (Note: If you account for $ per hour of gameplay, this game = incredible value - it WILL take you a long time to complete this game)
Some character buildpaths seem considerably weaker than others

100% recommend this game if you like VR RPG games. This will scratch that itch!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a case where I really, REALLY, wish there was a neutral review option.
Despite this being a negative review, I really love this game. It has a very nice gameplay loop, it has enough depth to keep you interested, and the multiplayer in it is absolutely amazing. The problem comes in with the price tag.
If this game was still 35 dollars like it was before full release I'd recommend it in a heartbeat, but I just cannot in good faith recommend it for as premium an amount as 55 dollars.
If you're super rich and have the spare cash I'd recommend it, otherwise wait for it to go on sale. The developers seem passionate (if somewhat out of their depth at times), and they're really onto something with this game, I just wish they hadn't increased the price by such a margin.\t\t\t\t\t
