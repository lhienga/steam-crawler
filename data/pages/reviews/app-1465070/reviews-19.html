Experienced on the Meta Quest 3

You can view my gameplay & early impressions here: https://youtu.be/2Rdtt_R4d4g

I wasn't the biggest fan of Legendary Tales in Early Access. I thought that the combat was good & the physics were nice, but the overall game felt more like a tech demo rather than a full game. Plus the game overall was very clunky with a UI that was bad.

The UI still isn't good, but almost everything else is much better. The visuals are improved. The game runs good on my RTX 3080. The combat is still top-tier and where the game shines. However, the game is missing the high production values that you'd expect from a game at this new price point of $54.99 USD. I also came across a game-breaking bug early in the game where I couldn't retrieve my items after I died (you're supposed to be able to pick up items like in Dark Souls after you die when you go back to where you died, but a tree was in the way and I couldn't reach my items).

Game is running on the Unreal Engine 4. On my RTX 3080, I was getting a steady 90 fps on the Quest 2. Outside of the major bug mentioned earlier, I did not experience any other bugs or serious issues. You do have full locomotion and smooth or snap turning available. You can technically play seated, but I don't recommend it as you'll be too low to the ground and you'll have a more difficult time with the combat as it does involve realistic physics (you have to do hard swings). You can basically choose melee strength (sword & board, axe, etc.), magic, or agility (bow & arrow, spears, etc.).

I think I may have given this game an 8/10 if I hadn't run into that major bug and the price point was a bit better. I do want to re-iterate that my initial impressions are that the combat is pretty satisfying, although the trash mob fights could be better. The first boss fight I encountered was pretty difficult (still haven't beaten it). I like the visuals. I like the new level design which is miles ahead where the game was. I do wish the game had voiceovers for the NPC quests, instead of just text reading. I do wish the character models were better, at least at the start they are pretty generic. Still, this is a good game and the combat is top-tier.

Rate 7/10.
