\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot quite ready for prime time, although I do like it and will try it again once there's an update:

The Good:
Combat is fun
Art direction looks great
Dungeons appear to be complex
Enemies are fun to fight
Physics are pretty good

The Bad:
Magic feels wonky. I think they know this and are about to redo it.
The menus are bad. I hate going into my inventory.
I cant find a way to just drop items. I have to equip them first, as an example.
The combat is good, but it also feels a tad too slow sometimes.
Climbing is not good. The games fades to black sometimes while im climbing, completely disorienting me, and then I fall. I dont need any "comfort mode". The game needs an option to turn this off.

My biggest gripe: VR games should steer clear of using the joystick buttons. Valve Index and Quest2 controllers are prone to stick drift, and constantly pushing the button degrades them even faster. I would stop using it and come up with another way of opening the menus.

Its off to a good start. If you are on the fence I would buy it, you can usually refund games within 2 hours on steam if you dont like it. I will not refund because I'll wait for updates.\t\t\t\t\t
