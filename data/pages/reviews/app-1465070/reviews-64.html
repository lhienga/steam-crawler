\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will start by saying I love this game, it is perhaps turning into one of the best VR experiences I own. Currently at the time of this review I'm opted into the new beta version where they re-hauled the entire attribute system and magic system due to player feedback. On top of that the new glitches were addressed in under 20 hours from the time of report and the inital patch. And further refinement in under 4 days beyond that. The love this game gets from the developers is great and I'm glad how much they listen to player feedback.

Pros of the game:
Great combat system with full customization with new primary attributes that climb based on your combat style. And secondary attributes that are chosen by you.

The skills- The skill trees are new and still a little bare but there is a wide variety of skills, passives and talents to chose from that can change up gameplay to your style

Map exploration- Map delving will be enjoyable on each of the current 10 levels as they are randomized and an enjoyable length with treasure and loot to be hunted down and found or challenge portals found. Hidden walls and breakable floors will have you tapping on walls from time to time. Climbing puzzles and lever locked gates placed just enough to shake up the wandering around the rooms without being overused to get annoying.

Enemies- Enemies have enjoyable attack patterns that can be learned and have actual sword play rather than just hacking from side to side at you. As you delve deeper the skeletons have different type of skeleton that change their sword style just slightly. Goblins chop at your ankles and make you enjoy plunging an axe or blade down into their skull and cause their other goblin allies to flee and run into a wall in the panic. Finally you have the champions of these current two races that have magic or abilities with buffed up health and damage,

Loot- Loot is plentiful and you'll be hunting out enchanted weapons and armor until you find your first Exotic(orange) drop before thirsting for each and every one of those unique items and armors.

Updates- Updates are always on the way and there is a clear road map that can be seen on the website of current, planned, and even considered updates.

Cons
There are cons to this game like every game. This one at this time is that it is currently Early Access, and while enjoyable in its current state once you beat the boss at the bottom it leaves you with little but loot hunting for what to do next.

Enemy variety- Enemy variety is currently sparse as you have variations of skeletons, mimics, bats and goblins only. While there are 2 new monster types in progress but currently you'll just have to enjoy bashing and slashing your way through floors of skeletons. The only exception is the boss at the end.

Single themed- By this I mean that the entire dungeon will look like a church or undead crypt as you make your way from room to room. And while there is an arena mode I do not believe the aesthetic with change until they add in the next dungeon.

Empty Hub- the main town is very empty especially with the magic vendor only selling potions now after the update changed magic to skill points rather than buy it. With only the blacksmith selling blue tier and lower loot he is more of a selling point for all the gear and items you collect. The only place you really spend your hard fought gold at currently is the gambling corner were you buy randomized loot hoping to catch a big win.

In conclusion this game is good now and one of the best Dungeon Delving experiences you can own currently for VR. If the game continues to be updated with the new weapons, magic, skills, rooms and enemies as planned this will become one of the greatest VR experiences on the market. If you enjoy delving into dark dungeons and gathering a party of friends to have a good time smashing crates and enemies alike you will love this game too.\t\t\t\t\t
