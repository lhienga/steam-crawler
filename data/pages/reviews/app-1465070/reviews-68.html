\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis might be the best purchase I've done for VR ever.

People who compare this to Boneworks, Blade & Sorcery etc. are all WRONG, this is WAY better & has much more to offer.

This game has the most realistic (in a very good way) combat & weapon mechanics I've ever seen in VR, the spells works great & there is plenty to do in the game. Sure it could use more variety when it comes to levels & enemies, but overall this game is the absolute best you'll get if you like using medieval weapons, spells & general dungeon crawlers.

The developers have thought about many things for the combat, the looting & the co-op experience, I have to say that I truly hope they will make another with slightly better graphic, more weapons (weapon types), enemies & more exciting maps, but keeping all the core mechanics & simply make them even better.

if you play this with just 1 friend, there is simply no way you'll regret buying this. Personally I can't play Blade & Sorcery anymore, because the mechanics in that game is just so bad & poorly made, compared to Legendary Tales.

WELL DONE DEVELOPERS! I CAN TELL ALREADY ;IF YOU MAKE A NEW GAME LIKE THIS, AND IMPROVE UPON IT, I'll BE THE VERY FIRST TO BUY IT.

If Bethesda made Skyrim VR with the mechanics of this game, from enemies, combat, spells etc. etc. then that would not only be the best VR game ever, but very literally the best game ever made.

Urban Wolf Games Inc. Good job! Please do make a follow up game that improves upon everything you've done here, without getting greedy & just milking your future titles, because you truly have something great & unique here and can become really REALLY big within the VR industry.

I rarely leave reviews, but this game deserved it 100%.\t\t\t\t\t
