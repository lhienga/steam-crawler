\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe combat in this game is great. Parrying the enemy is super satisfying, the weapons are cool, the legendary weapons look cool, have unique abilities, and are fun to use. I haven't really messed with the magic, but my friend uses it and enjoys it. The multiplayer works great, I've never had any syncing issues or random disconnects or anything. In fact, it works so well that my friend was able to catch an arrow I shot out of a bow (after plenty of tries).

It's not without its downsides. The movement isn't nearly as satisfying as the combat. You move super slow, even when "running". You also can't mantle over anything unless you're already in the air for some reason. It does feel like an early-access game due to lack of content. The variety in the dungeons and the crazy amount of loot you can find definitely feel fleshed out, but the main hub and enemies are lacking. It seems like that could change with the upcoming update that'll add smithing and whatnot though.

Overall it's a fun game, and super fun to play with a friend. What's already in the game is great, and it seems like it'll just keep getting better.\t\t\t\t\t
