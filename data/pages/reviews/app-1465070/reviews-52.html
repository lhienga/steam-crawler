The enemy types are a bit limited, but the devs have mentioned that they are working on a way to add more with the latest update, and they are pretty communicative. So let's run down the good:

Seamless VR
Good movement and tracking
Combat feels fluid - even the magic
Skill trees
GREAT multiplayer
VR parkour is a RIOT

The cons:
Environments are a bit repetitive (although at higher levels they seem to get more complex)
The music is quite repetitive too
Current lack of monster types (which is an acknowledged fact)

All in all, it's a good buy if you get it now, and it has future potential expansion. If you want to wait a bit, that's a safe bet too, but if you love VR and VR games with friends, then this is one of the current better games. Plus, who doesn't like grabbing a skeleton by the neck and yeeting it out a window?
