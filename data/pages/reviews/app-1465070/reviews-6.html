\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSummary:

Dungeon crawl with multiplayer capabilities. Their are 3 main attributes (STR, INT, DEX) and 3 secondary attributes (VIT, WIS, LUCK). Your player level goes up based on the primary attributes that you level up. Experience is given to the attribute based on the action and weapon stat. Currently max level is 50 and the quests/dungeons are set in a linear path easy to navigate. (I waited to post a review until after the extension and delay was put on the final release version, turned out to be a solid gamble).

Pros:

Typical level based skill point and tree system
You have both holsters on your body and 'quick bar' slots for each hand
You only drop the items on your body during death
Numerous types of weapons for both single hand and two handed play styles
Crafting system that immerses you in game play just right and not too grindy
Weapons are gratifying to use in combat
You have skills to hold/rag-doll opponents with one had while stabbing repeatedly with the other after a status effect
Very passionate dev with numerous updates.

Cons:

It feels like leg day when you fight goblins with all the squats you have to do to block with shields
Some of the two handed weapons feel clunky to play
Auto targeting assist has a high chance of hitting an NPC you weren't trying to include in the combat during pulls
