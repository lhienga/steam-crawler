Bought this with low expectations and found myself wanting to hurry home to play it. Co-op is solid, the developer(s) communicate well. Important positive things to note: climbing in this game is fun (hidden challenge rooms sometimes have a speed climbing challenge), combat is wonderful except magic can be tricky when AirLinking on the Oculus Quest 2, timing and positioning of attacks actually matters, loot is rewarding and not too stingy with the upgrades. Im enjoying the game and am quite surprised with the combat, everything feels impactful, the looting is great and cannot make me feel more like a goblin.

The dungeons currently feel a bit empty but im always surprised to find new room and skeleton variations as I progress through the levels.

The skills are quite varied, but may need a better tutorial, as I an never quite certain if I activated a weapon skill or not.

On the other side, game feels like it would need a great deal of optimisation, hopping between other games lets you feel the lower framerate.
At the current time I have encountered 5 different enemies, excluding the skeleton variations, but this is still an Early Access.
The town also feels barren, there is 3 shops but the gold I gather feel like it should only go for potions or gambling for a possible better item, even though the dungeons will quite certainly have something better, for the price of a few sword swings.\t\t\t\t\t

Quite a bit to a great game:
1. Optimization - I have a computer with 1660, and these are almost the minimum requirements, BUT the video card is only half loaded even with the virtual desktop on QUEST 2 (which also creates a load on the video card). Well, changing the graphics settings does not change the personnel cleanliness at all. Something must be done about this.
2. Handling with two-handed swords is very poor. Feeling like I'm using an ax instead of a sword. The game does not understand this (in games like blade and sorsery, battle talent or hellsplit arena, this works correctly) and reduces the attack speed (and therefore damage) by subtracting the movement speed of the auxiliary hand from the movement speed of the main hand.At least that's how it feels. The rest is not so critical, but it would be cool if it appeared in the future:
3. Customization of the character AND ESPECIALLY the display of worn armor. This is sorely lacking.
4. More control settings.
5. Using spells with gestures.
6. Versions for quest 2 (I heard that there are problems with this, but the desire does not cancel).
7. regional price for Russia, like other vr games.
8. Various pvp modes, if at all possible.
(duels, squad against squad, who clears the dungeon faster, dungeon master mode (where one player will control the appearance of traps and mobs, and he will be the final boss), but these are already dreams.

Doing the first 6 points will already make the game perfect.

Остальное не так критично, но было бы круто, если бы в будущем это появилось:
3. Кастомизация персонажа И В ОСОБЕННОСТИ отображение надетых доспех. Этого очень не хватает.

Выполнение первых 6 пунктов уже сделает игру идеальной.\t\t\t\t\t
