\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game a good bit. been trying to convince friends to play/ get it with me. Even in its current status it's a lot of fun. Granted the price is a bit up there. I bought it before the price increase for myself, but I got it for a friend after.

At the moment it's a really cool dungeon crawler. Unfortunately the dungeons don't have an aspect of randomness like they use to, but I also haven't gotten too far in the game to really gauge the others. The main area is outside, and right off the bat you've got a lot to explore. Fight your way into the castle? Fight your way into the dungeons and destroy a crystal that's been devastating your little camp. You can brew two kinds of potions with blood clumps and mana chunks, and apparently they updated it so dropping either of the bits into the cauldron turns it to the other, so if you need more mana than the average person but have a butt load of blood clumps, you're still good to go.

I love the crafting aspect added to the game, though It's slightly confusing at first, at least for me, once you get it it's almost addicting. You can buff your weapons with stones and magic powder as well. If you don't wanna sell your stuff, scrap it for parts. Fighting feels pretty good as well. Love chopping the heads of goblins off, and I love that if you get an arm or a limb you can use it to fight if you're quick enough. They aren't very good for blocking though.

The only thing the game is missing in my opinion is an aspect of randomness. The dungeons are set in stone, and it's really easy to memorize them. Before the update I would enter another floor, leave for the night, come back and it was different. I miss that.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't normally write reviews but I feel like it's needed for this game. Both the good and bad and maybe with the hopes the Dev's see this and maybe take it to heart.

Don't let the cheesy sounding name dissuade you, the game actually slaps and has a great SKELETON to be something FAR more. See the skeleton unfortunately is also the joke/con of the game. Enemy variety is BAD. You got SKELETONS, Goblins, big goblins and can you guess? BIG skeletons! WOW, what diversity -____- it makes the whole gaming experience depressing imo which is a shame because the RPG system and MULTIPLAYER really shines through and with a little more love the game could easily be a top tier VR title if polished. The game solo is fun but having a bunch of your pals join you while you gang up beating some poor goblin with chairs and then play soccer with his head is priceless.

The TLDR of it all: Blade and sorcery but can invite friends, better leveling system/RPG, rng dungeon crawl and only 2 enemies, 90% will be the same bone boi. If you're tight on money just wait for the next big update (as of now it's been delayed for a year+) but if you got the money to spare, you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
