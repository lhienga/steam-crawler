PC: i5 10400 cpu, rtx 3060ti gpu, 32gb 2400 ram, run off an oculus quest 2, via air link.
At first I figured this would only be doable via co-op, so I held off on getting this game.
Figured I would take a stab at it, and holy hell.
Controls are friendly. Lag is minimal. The combat is thoroughly enjoyable and responsive. As a solo act, I just progress slowly and take my time. Such a good time so far, and I will be spending a fair bit more. Now if I can just find my way to floor two...\t\t\t\t\t

Others are right to compare it to an old Diablo 1 VR like game, the ambient music and feel is there. I'm anxiously awaiting the new worlds update. Only my opinion but yea its a good buy.\t\t\t\t\t
