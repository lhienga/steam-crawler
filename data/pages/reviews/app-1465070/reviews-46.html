\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery restrictive combat (1 second soft cooldown between attacks before you can do full damage. makes daggers next to useless). You're essentially stuck hacking away at an enemy with a huge health pool while the others in the room get ready to give the unsuspecting fool (the homie playing) a bukkake of swords and maces. The dungeon layout gets stale pretty quick and the combat needs a bit more freedom to be a game worthy of sinking an unholy amount of hours into. A good thing going for it is it has a very large variety of weapons and the way damage types works is super neat and unique. Cannot recommend unless you're gonna jack around with friends, needs more content.

edit 2/22/024: previous review still stands for the most part so here are my current gripes. Takes just a tad too long to level up and feels kinda tedious, textures are REALLY dated, dungeon layouts are quite basic and boring until you get to the end (they look ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ cool at the end typically) all of the enemies look the exact same aside from the weapons they carry, and combat can still be really janky at times. Now that I'm done complaining here are things I like. Unarmed combat is really fun, throwing has been improved drastically so you can thundercunt a chair across the dungeon and dome an unsuspecting skeleton, the legendary weapons are really neat and unique, multiplayer is very easy to get going and feels quite nice.

TLDR; game is really fun, but wait for a sale. game's worth 20 bucks at most.\t\t\t\t\t
