\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is insane how underrated this game is. For years there has been an untapped market for a good VR RPG game. If you are addicted to RPG games like I am (almost 1k hours in skyrim and about 500 in fallout 4) you will absolutely love this game. Enough praise, time for the features.

Multiplayer- I am actually shocked that I haven't encountered an issue with multiplayer yet. The only slight thing that I think could be improved is allowing players to calibrate from an in game menu, instead of having to leave the game and rejoin.

Combat- The combat is simple but great, similar to blade and sorcery, but the enemies have a health bar that you can see and need to deplete to kill them. Consecutive quick strikes do less damage, and so you should wait about a second or two between each attack before hitting again to maximize DPS. The magic in the game right now is cool, and they are currently developing and planning to release more spells.

Perks and Skills- The main talking point of the game. The more you use certain fighting styles, the stronger their respective stats get, using strength weapons increases strength, dex weapons increase dex, magic increases int. In the beta branch, as you level up you can spend skill points on abilities, which you can use to learn new spells or new fighting techniques. These are the basics and they are bound to change in the future.

I would wholeheartedly recommend this game to anyone with a VR and in interest in RPG's, even if you don't have friends who have VR, this game is still great singleplayer. The dev team is constantly updating it and very active.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great with a lot of potential but that said, being early access/beta, it has issues. Not bugs either, just how the game works and its mechanics. The leveling system feels lackluster, and loot drops have crap items, but you can find legendaries/unique items more often in a pot then from hard enemies or chests. You can find a secret room guarded by high level enemies, fight your way through them (even die a few times) just to get to the chest that has a crap dagger that is so bad its not even worth grabbing. The end of each floor loot is the worse, you ONLY get yellow tier items. Being on floors 1-7 sure, thats whatever...... but near end game, and still getting THE SAME crap loot at the end of each dungeon is just insulting. When I play the game, i feel as if I HAVE to use a bow/magic, because 90% of the enemies in the game, have crazy ridiculous attacks that either go through your weapon so parrying is pointless, or they straight up walk inside you because they move slightly faster then you, and feel the need to hug you mid fight, so you can't block/parry, when their sword is already inside your stomach when they start the swing animation. About 90% of my deaths, are either from a archer skelly that never misses while I am fighting other things, or a goblin or shield skeleton hugging me and killing me without even getting halfway through their animation of attack. This game is great in the fact that its something new, and the idea is something I think all VR players needed, but thats about all it has going for it. (And its fun with friends, but thats just about any game under the sun)\t\t\t\t\t
