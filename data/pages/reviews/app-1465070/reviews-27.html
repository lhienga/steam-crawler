\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI LOVE this game! For what it offers it really is the best multiplayer weapons combat experience, and the stats and skills make it dynamic to play. I have played through the entire game with several characters and even created a "Battle Mage" that uses strength and magic, with the wand used as a parrying dagger.

The game has plenty of weapons to choose from and the AI battle animations make it challenging enough, but still allow you to parry and counter attack even while fighting multiple enemies. The stats and weapon levels are like many classic RPGs, but each weapon is unique and requires real combat techniques to get the most out of attacks (plus it makes equipping a bucker actually worth it in an RPG!).

The dungeons can be disorienting enough to loose your way and it seems every play through of a level is a little different. I do wish you could move faster, and it will be nice to have new levels and enemies to fight in future updates. But for where this game is at now I will continue to play it solo and with friends, and when full release hits it will be even more worth purchasing. This game has crazy potential but is 100% worth playing in early access\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far, as someone who loves dungeon crawlers, I absolutely a fan of this game. I did the tutorial and played with my friend who didn't ... it is important to do the tutorial for sure hahaha. It'll streamline a lot. The archery mechanics are pretty realistic, the sword fighting and melee is fun - oh and climbing ropes to access hidden areas is pretty awesome!.Is the game a little jank here and there? Sure, but that's what Early Access is all about! I look forward to watching this game grow!

My one and only complaint was that upon loading into the Abbey Courtyard for the very first time my friend and I were super lost looking for what door to go through. The portal to the front of the fort made it seem like that was were we needed to be until alas we accidentally stumbled into the correct door - on the church (abbey) hahaha. I feel like a sign or whisp would be really useful upon your moments in that area. Maybe explaining the merchants and highlight the abbey door.

Overall - great game so far!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame was cool, honestly the most fun i've had with VR. The devs said they "ran into trouble" while trying to upgrade the engine. Last update was in december of 2022 which summed up is they are upgrading from Unreal4 to Unreal5 and that "they swear they are still working on it". I cannot recommend this game unfortunately. Its an awesome foundation and has great potential...but if its just gonna fizzle out of existence don't bother. Wait for a cheap sale, and only buy it if you have friends who will play it with you.

2/11/24- Now that the game has released I can honestly say this game is not worth the new price its asking for. When I first played this game a few years back it was better then blade and sorcery,MULTIPLAYER, and a cool looting dungeon crawler. Now it honestly feels worse then it did before, AND its twice as expensive. This game was great for a 25 dollar sale grab but now I'm not so sure. I genuinely hope they continue to add to this game, but as it stands right now, it feels like the same game that was put on hiatus 2 years ago.It has a slightly new hub, new music, and a very cool and ambitious crafting system...but thats it.

2/25/24-After playing for awhile the combat feels worse then it did in earlier releases. The main problem of enemies walking through you, or walking through your weapons still exists., Moving around the main hub is slooooooooooooow.It is beyond tedious to just walk everywhere.Yes the portal system works, but that barely alleviates it as you slowly trudge from place to place on the map.Also, try as I may, I cannot get the dash to work on my HTC Vive controllers. This update feels like an update that would have come out two years ago. This update to me is not indicative to two years of work, more like two years of mistakes,backtracking (to appease console players and poor pc build people) and general incompetence.\t\t\t\t\t
