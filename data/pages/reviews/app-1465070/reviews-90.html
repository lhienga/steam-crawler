After finishing all the floors, my opinion has changed.
For the first three floors it is all new and exciting, between floor 4 to 6, it is repetitive. Same enemies and room layouts as always.

From 7 to 10 I just wanted to reach the last floor to see if there may be something special, and in fact it is.

The last floor was a bit frustrating at first, but in the end a good way to finish this game off.

I am not returning to it, but I think I have got my moneys worth out of it, still definitely recommend that you play this with someone else. I do not think I would have finished it otherwise.





Wow!

This is in my top 5 vr experiences.

I have been looking for a VR game like blade and sorcery, but with progression and multiplayer and this game brings me what I want.

Now is it perfect?

Of course not, it's early access and we all know how that can go. This could be abandoned and never updated, so I have to review it's current state.

Gameplay:

There is this simulated weight effect on the weapons, so no flailing arms in this which is good. Once I got the hang of the intended slow pacing in combat I started to enjoy reading the enemies attack patterns.

The enemies attack patterns are varied enough to keep you on your toes and it never felt unfair. When I died I had either took on too many enemies at once or I forgot to keep my distance.

About dieing, when you die you get transported back to town where you can buy and sell gear. Your equipment is left in a tombstone where you die and can be retrieved. The enemies you have slain will stay dead so you can just run back to where you died, though the enemies that got alerted and killed you will remain around that spot.

I don't know how many rooms there are in this game, but I got to level 12 of 50 in about 5 hours.
(Wow, the time flew!)

Graphics:

I run top of the line specs so I don't know how it looks on lower end hardware, but it looks good for the most part.

I like the general theme and setting, but the town looks very dead and boring. Luckily you don't spend much time there and the rest of the game looks great.

I do have one thing that I don't like, and that is the anti aliasing used in the game. It creates this blur even though I am super sampling att 300% and resolution at 1.3.

Bugs:
My healing potions do not fill up anymore except for the third one.
Kind of annoying, but it forces me to get good at parrying attacks.

TLDR:
BEST MELEE COMBAT VR GAME TO PLAY WITH FRIENDS.\t\t\t\t\t
