\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are several things about this game that make it really hard to recommend.
-Pitch BLACK in many areas. You can't see anything at all. I get that it's supposed to be dark, but there are light sources everywhere, enough that it should be dimly lit.
-Poorly explained game mechanics. The explanations in the game for many of the skills are not very straight forward, and other things leave a LOT to guesswork and trial and error.
-Not being able to see enemy HP bars except for right after you hit them. An argument for could be made for immersion, but he actually recently added an option to get rid of the bars completely for that.
-MAJOR LAG ISSUES!!!!!!!!! Lag issues that did NOT EXIST in the pre-release version of the game. My friend (in Japan) and myself (in the US) were playing this game before it was released. Of course there's going to be some lag, but the pre-release somehow handled it perfectly. But now, whoever is NOT hosting effectively can't use bows AT ALL, can't toss weapons and catch them, can't throw weapons, can't grab individual items out of items sacks without several attempts. It's really bad. I don't know how it was working so well before but it's not now.
-Too many "mechanics". It's a VR game. If I can swing non-stop and not run out of breath, each hit should do the same damage. Once I get tired, that's when damage should be reduced. But there is a "heavy hit" mechanic and it just seems strange in a VR game. Not the biggest deal though.
-One of the BIGGEST issues though.....You lose 20% of your gold when you die which is INSANE. Even if it wasn't for everything else, I CANNOT recommend this game because of that alone. When I mentioned it to the developer, this was his response,

"20k means you have 100k gold, why would you stack that much of gold for nothing?
You lose 20% of gold every each time when you die.
Use it before you enter the fight with a boss.
Basically you dont have place to use the gold except gambling.
We want you to use the gold before boss fight, make sure you find a gear for your level"

Basically he said "too bad" this is how I made it. He actually thinks it's reasonable. Stacking and saving gold is a normal play style.\t\t\t\t\t
