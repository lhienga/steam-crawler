You're a little boy, whose parents died long ago due to a car accident, being sent by your siblings to live with your beloved relatives: your uncle, aunt and two cousins down the countryside. Spend your precious 15 Summer days having an enjoyable time with your cousins where you enjoy warm days gallivanting with your relatives. Hooray for a family friendly time~!


Actual Context:
There's a H-patch from the Kagura Games website, surprise! Now the real fun begins.

Your parents are reincarnated to send you, a f**king dwarf of an 18 year-old high school graduate, to live with your cousins and aunt. Oh, and your uncle, forgot about the reject. This time, your parents disowned your degenerate ass for 30 days. The whole summer basically. So, sit down, unbuckle your pants, cause we're in for an incestual ride (if you know what I mean).


Opinions:

First things first, you're an 18 year old, but this game has it you looking like a child who just ate 50 pills of Viagra with an insatiable lust for people who lack the basic idea of 'modesty' and shotacon restraint. So, if you're not put off by that, you can try this game out. You have multiple women to satisfy in this game apart from the main heroines but 30 days is sufficient to win the hearts of all of them. Plus, there's actually a way to unlock everything without working that hard if you're just here for the gallery of H-scenes.


The sounds and visuals of the game are spot on, if you don't mind the little peepee. The UI of the characters are somewhat pixelated but fret not, the CGs are in full HD, though just not animated. So enjoy the sounds of shota peepee slurping and butt slapping coupled with highly lewd moans during CGs as well, though only for the main heroines when played without the DLC.

Gameplay is sort of grindy. You have multiple mini-games and they are quite essential if you're aiming for most of the women. You do have to repeat most of them to progress onto the next event for the girls. The rest are just strings of daily habits you have to do, like hunting insects each day or typing out stuff on the in-game computer. H-scenes are unlocked as and when you encounter them. Though certain conditions need to be met so either get a handy guide ready or be prepared to hunt down every single lewd scene there is, and trust me, there's a lot for the base game itself. DLC adds more so it's an investment at that point.

All in all, it was a good 10 hours spent grinding for almost 80% of the game the legit way! Now my peepee is sore and I am waiting for my next family event so as to establish fruitful relations with my cousins.\t\t\t\t\t
