Every character pursues pleasure, but they do so out of their innocent desire for deeper connection and acceptance. Their level of innocence is admittedly absurd, but at the same time, their absurdly innocent takes on pleasure and connection remind us that all our adult desire has indeed emerged from our childish innocence at some point. I would say this game's characters feel as if they skipped the entirety of their adolescent years and the bitterness that comes along with it; they have childish innocence and adult perversion but no adolescent bitterness at all. It is practically removed from their souls with surgical precision.

Therefore, you cannot expect bitter emotions like rejection, jealousy, and rivalry from the characters. Fans of some other mature contents might not like this, as these negative emotions do add realism to mature contents, but personally I believe the developers made a wise decision. Given the limited volume of a single game, focusing on a few specific aspects of primal connection allowed deeper and denser expression than covering all possible aspects of it would. As far as its delivery is concerned, this game is definitely a classic in its chosen theme, that is, childish innocence and adult perversion.\t\t\t\t\t
