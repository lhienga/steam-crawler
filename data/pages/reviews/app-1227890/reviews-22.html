\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a well-known adult game, I've heard of it before purchase, and now I find it "very direct" after I've played just 20 minutes.
The Plot
All dialogue and plots aim to guide the MC to sleep with girls. And the MC can have a love affair with more than one girl simultaneously. The story features numerous adult scenes, though not fun and a bit cliche.
The mini-games
You can do easy math problems, fish, wash dishes and catch bugs in this game. All of these will help you unlock the stories of girls containing more you-know-what events.
You may find the mini-games okay initially; however, soon, you will get bored. Mini-games in Yakuza 0 are much better.
The art
I don't like too big breasts, but the MC is cute!
Conclusion
It is a "Straightforward" game for players who don't care much about stories and enjoy hot scenes.

这个游戏好像很大名鼎鼎，低价购入后，感受就是：非常，非常的直接……
故事情节
非常简单，一切为了成人情景服务。
男主角来到了亲戚家度过暑假，可以同时保持大于一的亲密关系。
主要是情色日常及对话。没什么特别的。除去情色方面，很一般。
小游戏
系统设计比较丰富，内设做作业的小游戏，可以算数学加减法，洗盘子，还有捉虫子和钓鱼。
小游戏其实挺一般的，不是很有趣。当然，重点并不在小游戏上……
美术
画风比较男性化整体不太符合我的审美
但是，男主角的画风很可爱！
总结
总之，制作很用心，非常直接的游戏，推荐对剧情和玩法没有要求，主要为了特殊目的玩家；）\t\t\t\t\t
