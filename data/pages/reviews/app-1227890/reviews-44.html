\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, it should be obvious, this is a hentai game. though the base game may not include the H scenses the patch from Kagura games website does. (free btw)
Second, if you have any specific preferences against large mammaries and or large posteriors, this game is not for you.
Third and final, if you have any problems with a young looking definitely 18 year old boy as "you" the protagonist, please back away from this game.

Now then, onto the game. The first thing you should know is that in general unless you know what you're doing, you most likely won't get many scenes until around a week into the game. This is not a super baby game where everything is super hand fed. There are some mini-games and tasks that cost your time in game to manage, as well as certain things that you have to increase to unlock certain scenes with the heroines.
As for the mini-games they are simple in format, however not knowing your times tables/ how to manage you time and action points can make progress slower. Overall the game-play is solid and honestly engaging enough to not bore you.

Secondly, the "skill" system. This system allows you to accumulate different things you can do with and in the world. Some may not be super obvious how to activate so have fun with that. These skills are acquired through different actions and scenarios in the world and with the heroines which accumulates skill points. The heroine skill point route is slightly more difficult however if you were hoping to farm skill points from that.

Thirdly, gauge. As I've said previously, this is not a baby handhold hentai game, to get the scenes you want you have to handle MANY gauges in this game. starting with the most obvious.

Stamina: Most used stat in the game, very needed and always valuable, keep it high as much as you can.

Lust: Second most used stat in the game. needed for exchange into skill points, as well as for satisfying the lady's in player activated scenes (the ones as in the tutorial with Rio) Also very important, however hitting max will have the player wetting their pants, so try to keep in mid state at all times (except for before any scenes).

Affection: These points are separate for each heroine and are needed for progressing the relationship with them. honestly these come easy from the next gauges needs, though can be gained through spending time with the characters normally.

Homework: Ah the mini-game points, honestly this is the big decider for which heroine end up romancing first. basically any time the heroines aren't busy (on the phone, away from home, ect.) you can ask for there help on your summer homework. These are framed as small mini-games that can be decently challenging if trying to to them speedily for max points. when max points for any heroines bar is reached, the next affection scene can be activated. (IMPORTANT if you want to do scenes with Rio or more importantly Miyuki at the seventh day/first day trip, you MUST fully focus on doing the homework and affection points for them. Miyuki requiring the 40 affection scene and Rio the 20 affection scene. make sure to buy homework buffs from the candy shop for this.)

Vigilance: And finally for this "gauge" though represented as a number. whenever you do steamy things with a character, people in the rooms around you will gain some vigilance points, once these accumulate they will come and check on you, catching you mid act. this one is easy enough to manage as you can see vigilance states when speaking to characters. just be sure to give them gifts sometimes and it should be fine, especially if you are only going for affection scenes.

And finally, the art. though, i mean, it's subjective, only thing worth mentioning is that the affection scenes are more detailed.

Final words, this game is amazing to me, i fully recommend it to any hentai game enthusiasts out there, and hope you have a great time playing it.

(TLDR Great game. good progression system. not baby easy. Art is good in my eyes. heavily recommended.)\t\t\t\t\t
