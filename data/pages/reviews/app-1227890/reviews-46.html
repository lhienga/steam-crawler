\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSummer Memories, is how I would say for people who like thicc older women that spoil the protagonist. In Summer Memories, you play as a boy who is going to being dropped off at family member's place in the boonies because his parents have work to do for 30 days. Now, his family member is a rather attractive and thicc aunt with 2 attractive daughters and her husband with the word husband on it, probably. You the protagonist with a 4 letter name max, plan to spend your summer with your family because you want to make some nice Summer Memories, but also do some homework.

Now, that that has been introduced, you, the boy, has opened up his third eye and is now on a quest to make his attractive aunt and his attractive older cousins, his. But, the task is never as simple as it seems, as you must do your homework with them and spend some time with them as well. But, you can also increase your harem with other attractive thicc older women. They range from being a candy store clerk who has her own twitter following and onlyfans that is free (she becomes just yours and only yours afterwards); a very attractive PE teacher with red hair who is also thicc; a thicc bespectacled girl in the mountains doing research on insects and fish; a thicc pink haired girl by the beach, and a disappointing girl (that will be explained a little). But those are all of the girls to choose from. The side girls mentioned earlier don't particularly matter as the MC only sees them as pieces in the grand scheme of things.

But, enough about the girls as the gameplay is the main thing here. In order to progress one of the three main girls route, you must have them love you and force them to help you with your homework. Rio, the blonde girl helps you with your research on insects and fishes; Yui the purple? black? haired girl teaches you math which is like solving math problems in College, fast and in your head without showing your work; and Miyuki the aunt who you help clean dishes with with your swole muscles praying you don't break the dishes because you don't know your own strength.

That's out of the way now, you are here for the more simple thing like I was. The lewds, which as a man of culture I don't blame you. So, you can start doing lewd acts with one of the girls right off the bat, but you gotta be secretive about it, as if you are TOO LOUD will go south. But, there's hope! You can corrupt the other 2 girls enough to join when you found which is a major plus. Thus leading to 2 out of 5 possible endings. The "Sisters ending" and the "Mother and Sisters ending". To get these endings you must have all 3 of them at at least 80 and have them discover you taking care of business with one/two of the girls. Which is ez.

As for NG+, pretty alright. Same for gallery, you stuck in the house forever with no rest, but they can't rest either so its a win-win situation. Do I recommend this game? If you like Shota x Older women or just Older Women in general then 100%.

Also, as a side note, I learned that the true goal wasn't the harem; but the friends we made along the way...
Harem over Friends any day....

Edit: I have found out that there is DLC or more commonly known as Append, that came out recently that basically happens during Winter Break to my understanding. So if this game sells well, there could be a chance that they will release the Append. So here's to hoping it happens soon, because this is an incredibly enjoyable game that isn't like the other games that KaguraGames brought over.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou play as an 18+ years old manchild, who was dropped to his 18+ years old cousins' home by his parents during summer holidays.

Things you can do:
- Buy fish baits and honey from the 18+ years old shopkeeper who's standing in for her granny, then set up honey traps to catch bugs or fish on either river or at the beach.
- You can try to hike the mountain to increase stamina, and eventually the 18+ years old coach might deem you worthy to join along the other 18+ years old on the Track & Field Club.
- Do a basic math mini game aimed at 18+ years old with your gentle cousin Yui, who'll reward you afterwards the better you do your homework. With more allowance.
- Have your other cousin Rio help you note down all the bugs&fishes you caught on your 18+ years old independent study. Great way to gain Memory Points so you can learn more skills.
- Help your Aunt Miyuki wash some dishes in the conveniently cramped kitchen, as helping with household chores is also part of the independent study. And you get even MORE bonus allowance the better you scrub em!
- Bond with your uncle every now and then to keep things hunky dory. Not like you're banging his whole family behind his back or anything. hahaha.
- Go on a weekly picnic with your Uncle's family and create some memories! "MEMORIES".
- BE THE ULTIMATE MENKOER AND SHOW MENTAI-KUN WHO'S BOSS.
- ...plus many more secret side stuff to unlock.

In all seriousness, this is actually quite a fun game even without the "patch". One of the better, non cookie-cutter game on Kaguragames' list.

There are a bunch of stuff to unlock and some of the mini games can be quite challenging. Well, until you meet the 18+ yo tree climbing lady that is. At that point you can just pile up money really easily by selling all the bugs and fishes you catch, then buy a mini game skip item from a secret merchant that's worth 1000 Yen each. (spoiler about a certain side plot, you've been warned)

p.s. save *OFTEN*, the game semi-crashes(audio and control still works but the screen freezes) randomly, but thankfully doesn't happen that often. Press F5 when that happen to restart the game immediately.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have always wanted to play Summer Memories since its initial release last year on DLsite; however, I was always hesitant to start because I knew that I would not be able to stop. It only took a few hours of gameplay for Summer Memories to turn into my newly discovered obsession. I found myself waking up early on weekends to get an early start. Ill only ask Aunt Miyuki for one favor often extended to two or three and I start doing something that I usually never do: replaying routes to try different options. 100 hours later, Im finally ready to put my tissue down. What an experience. Never has a single-player RPGMaker game kept me interested for so long, which speaks volumes about how immersive this game truly is. DojinOtome has set a very high standard for RPGMaker games and a strong reputation for themselves.

As simple as that statement is, there's a lot that goes into it. At its core, Summer Memories is the ideological successor to Housemaid Ladies Disturb My Study! and Succumate. It takes the rock-solid foundation of the relationship and intimacy systems put forward in those two games and improves upon them in every way story, h-scenes, creativity, freedom of controls. However, this is much of what I expected simply from the announcement preview of the game on DLsite, given the improvements from their previous study simulator to Succumate and the prowess DojinOtome has shown in designing all their games.

This is by far my favorite gaming experience of the decade and I played it with only a mouse.

For a game that is developed in RPGMaker, Summer Memories looks really amazing. From the design of each individual character, to the different landscapes and backgrounds, to the animation. You can really tell that a lot of time was spent to make this game feel as alive as possible. I have never seen a game in RPGMaker be as interactive as this one, giving the player this much freedom. The biggest selling points, in my opinion, are the characters and story telling. Without the magnificent writing and voice acting offered, Summer Memories would not have been such an immersive experience. Given how many characters are introduced in the series, its amazing how many unique personalities there are. Literally none of the main characters are repetitive, dull, or lack personality. There are countless scenarios and wonderful moments that I remember vividly and am very fond of, for example, when I watched the fireworks with Aunt Miyuki and the rest of the family towards the end of Summer. That was truly emotional. More impressively so, you even feel a part of the game while in the over-world. Many NPCs can be interacted with different choices of actions. Depending on your relationship with them and the progression of the game, you have the option to engage in various activities together, such as doing chores around the house. I especially liked helping Aunt Miyuki with the dishes.

Dont let the mere 800MB RPGMaker game fool you. The content in this game is actually packed to the brim. The world of Summer Memories is so vast, full of side-quests with promising rewards, quirky and interesting interactions with various characters, and even fun easter eggs to discover.

Despite the game taking place in a small town in the countryside, it has over 10 different locations that you can explore individually. My favorite discoveries by far was when I visited the schools warehouse at night to buy stuff from some strange man and meeting the candy shop lady on Twitter. Other than that, to my surprise, I even enjoyed playing Kabutomushi, the Japanese rhinoceros beetle battle introduced as a mini-game. Initially, I ignored the minigame as a whole, but once I learned how to play it, it quickly became an addiction. I remember spending two full weekends reloading my save only to play Kabutomushi and collect new beetles. The fact that DojinOtome chose to implement Kabutomushi as a mini-game really goes to show how proud they are of their own culture and childhood.

The protagonist skill tree is fantastically simple. You have access to your perks, actions and passive buffs but in reality, how you decide to play the game is your greatest weapon. To interact with each individual character requires a different approach. Picking the right choices from the many selections you are given weighs heavily in your favor.

In conclusion, Summer Memories is a hell of an experience that will remain deeply engraved in my memory. I mean there arent many games out there that allow you to collect bugs, do math and wash dishes. I also cannot stress enough how much this game offers for a single-player experience - I could easily spend another delightful 30-50 hours on my current save file without replaying quests or starting a new game.

This game is a steal at full price, and even more so when its on sale! What are you waiting for?\t\t\t\t\t

Gameplay
I'd compare this title to a sort of "open world" visual novel. The game takes place inside a small home, brimming with interactive characters and props. The idea is to make the most out of each day, by chatting it up with characters and collecting new items and skills (to unlock new interactions). To learn new skills, you must purchase them using "Memory Points", which are acquired via character interaction (or intercourse.)

Speaking of intercourse. . . it's conducted by a skillful minigame. You have to manage a combination of arousal, noise, speed, and stamina during sexual intercourse. Too much arousal, you'll "wet yourself" and collapse (losing precious time). Too much noise, you'll be caught and it's game over. Too fast, you'll deplete your stamina and collapse (also losing precious time).

You have a grand total of thirty days to do as you wish.

Story
The game doesn't start until you enter a (four letter) name, so I opted for what was already in the text box, "Boku". Sounds a bit like "baka", I'm sure this poor sap had a rough childhood.

Boku is staying with his cousins, Yui-chan and Rio-chan. Boku is eager to see them again, along with his aunt, Miyuki-san. Boku's parents are leaving on a business trip, and they won't be back for thirty days. Until his parents return, he is to live under Miyuki-san's wing.

Visuals
I rather enjoyed the simple, painterly titlescreen I also enjoyed the nearby, [mysterious white goo]-stained room and bangin' couple. From "Options", you may adjust a handful of audio preferences.

When the application is fullscreened, it appears fuzzy. I sincerely wish this wasn't the case. Also, there doesn't appear to be a way to force the application into a borderless fullscreen, this also sucks.

- The Goods -
First of all, I haven't a clue why a game like this would ever be allowed on the Steam store. . . but I'm not going to talk about why. I like this game. It's great to see it in English. Expect male-on-female content.

Verdict
Heck yeah I recommend this titl twenty dollars?! Wait for a gosh darned sale, sheesh. A big sale. But seriously, as a fan of pixel art and morally obscene erotica, this software tickles my fancy, and I recommend that you give it a whirl. Honestly, the biggest buzzkill for this game is the lack of quality fullscreen. As a matter of fact, because of the poor fullscreen capabilities, I would strongly dis-recommend this game unless it really does go on some huge sale.
