Quite The Moist Summer.

Disclaimer:
So to start things off I will give a quick content disclaimer. This game does involve shota(everyone said to be over 18), incest(indirectly related), reverse NTR, and a lot of piss play. If any of those things sound like an immediate turn off, then this game will not be for you.

Story:
The story of this game revolves around you, a shota boy that is being dropped off over at his aunt and uncles place for the summer while your parents are on a trip. While the games start is relatively tame, it can very quickly fall into the depraved when you happen upon a pair of one of your cousins panties. Here is where your first major choice is made. Do or don't you masturbate to the smell. Yes, that is the level of story telling that is going on here. If you were coming to this game for any kind of complex or meaningful story, then I'm sorry to disappoint you. The story here is full of completely unbelievable scenarios, tons of character tropes, and really strange, but sometime humorous, dialogue. So lets get back to the panties as this is the first and arguably one of the more egregious examples or unbelievable scenarios. So lets assume that you chose to slap your meat around to the stink. You will quickly find yourself face to face with your gyaru-ish cousin, who instead of being absolutely appalled that she just found one of her family jerking it to her juice, is actually rather understanding and offers to give you a helping hand. A real saint if I've ever seen one. This is a common style that the game follows when designing its beginning relationship scenarios. Only some characters, such as the aunt, fall into a slightly more believable scenario, but it really is only slightly. As for the tropes, you have your sexually frustrated aunt who is in desperate need of a good pounding, your gyaru cousin who is more than willing to do lewd acts with you, and your honor student cousin who turns out to be the most depraved out of them all. There are of course side characters as well, but I won't be going into them here. These a frequently used character types, and while some of the latter sex scenes with them can be pretty erotic, the characters themselves fall flat. The conversations are your standard hentai level of dialogue, most being bland, uninteresting, or outright humorous due to them sounding so outlandish. A great example of this is the sex scenes, where you will have characters explain everything that is happening to them, literally shouting out "you're ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ me in the ass!" while you ♥♥♥♥ them in the ass. While I would say that you can skip most of it, there is a problem with getting into the habit of doing this. Certain things like tutorials and quest objectives can sometimes be hidden in standard dialogue lines and skipping them can leave you either confused or unable to progress a characters story line.

Gameplay:
With that being said, lets get into the gameplay. The game is really simple in its design; you gather affection towards the characters you interact with by spending time with them, helping them with chores, working on your summer assignments, and playing games. You do this via the use of a stamina, action, and lust point system all used for various actions and events. Actions will use one if not multiple of the three and can also result in time progression based on the actions size. Not all actions will build up ones affection towards you though, and certain actions can be used as a way of making money to buy various useful items. Now, as a characters affection builds up, you will unlock new scenarios with these characters. Their affection will cap out each 20 levels until the next scenario is gotten. Each character has their own prerequisites for triggering the scenarios. The main three girls all require you to study or work with them, by either collecting bugs/fish, or by doing a mini game. As you do them, the work meter will raise, and when it reaches full, at a random time during the day(it seems random) a scene may trigger with them, usually resulting in a time skip. There are also set events that will happen with you and the main cast, that take place on certain days. These events will usually ask you which character you spent your time with, leading to a special scene with them, though these scenes do have requirements that need to be met in order to select them. At the end of your stay you will also be given a similar option to pick which of the three girls you will remember the most, each one being considered a different ending, with two special endings requiring certain conditions be met.

Art:
This is subjective, but I quite like the simple, almost chalk like style the game went with for it's CGs. I think they blend really well with the pixel art style, of which is also very good. None of the art here seems copy and pasted from other games, though certain CGs and animated sprite scenes can get repetitive as you will find characters sharing poses, just with different backgrounds. It isn't a big deal, but it can get boring to see the same thing over and over again, especially if you are trying to get all endings like I was. As far as other complaints go, the uncensored vaginas can look pretty strange, and the semen that coats the floors and walls if you ♥♥♥♥ someone in an animated sprite scene for long enough can all look a bit strange at times.

Soundtrack:
The game has like maybe 5 or so songs that it recycles over and over throughout it. That coupled with the volume sliders only allowing you to lower them in intervals of 20% can be irritating. The SFX much like the music can often find itself being very distracting while playing and as far as sex scenes go, sometimes doesn't even loop properly, leading to weird silences and then a sudden startup again. Overall, not the worst I have scene from a game(looking at you battlefield), but also certainly not great.

Performance/QoL:
The game itself is fairly stable. I have had very few performance related issue with it, though I have noticed it will sometimes stutter when counting down/up large numbers. There has also been rare occurrences of characters/overlays/levels flickering in and out. One weird example I have noticed of this can be scene when a room becomes dirty after ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ in it for a long time. The semen stains will sometimes change their appearance based on where the character is located in the room or what position they are currently being ♥♥♥♥♥♥ in. This seems to be completely arbitrary and I have no idea why this is, and while not a performance issue, it does seem to be a bug. As far as QoL goes, the game allows you to save whenever you want, excluding during scripted cutscenes/events and loading can be done very quickly. That is about where they end. The games menus can be infuriating to navigate through. Each one requires that you click it once to select it then click it again to enter it. While you can buy and sell items in bulk, you cannot use more them one at a time, thus forcing you to use the item then re-enter the menu, select the item, use it, then repeat. This can get really tiresome.

Summary:
All in all, it isn't a bad game but due to it repetitive nature and sometimes questionable H content, it can be a hard recommend. If you are looking for something mindless with some decent sex scenes here and there, then this is probably worth looking at. I would recommend trying to get it on sale, but it does offer a decent amount of content for its base price.\t\t\t\t\t

Cons
The main character is a bit on the young side, which can be a MAJOR discouragement for many players. The animated scenes are repetitive, as they are all the same for every girl with the main difference being their models. This goes for the threesome and foursome as well, as it just consists of the same animations with the added detail of another girl being in the same room watching and can be swapped in place of the current “active” girl. The only difference between the girls are the events that occur after certain conditions are met, but those are just static images with text boxes below them. This game requires a minimum of two playthroughs to unlock all the girls, which can be a problem for those who just want to see everything the game has at the very start.

Final Thoughts
Personally, I only recommend this game if you got nothing else in mind of purchasing due to the repetitive nature of it. If you manage to get everything unlocked on one girl, then you have seen everything. If anything, pick this game when it goes on sale or if you are currently waiting for something else to release to pass the time.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood Fried Chicken Recipe:

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together buttermilk, hot sauce (optional, for added flavor), salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl. Add in chicken pieces. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate 4 hours.
When ready to cook, pour the vegetable oil in a skillet until it is about 3/4 inch deep. Heat to 350 degrees.
Prepare the breading by combining the flour, cornstarch, onion powder, garlic powder, oregano, basil, white pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika, and salt in a gallon sized resealable plastic bag or shallow dish. Mix it thoroughly.
Working one at a time, remove chicken pieces from buttermilk mixture. Shake it gently to remove the excess. Place it in the breading mix and coat thoroughly. Tap off the excess.
Place the breaded chicken into the 350 degree oil. Fry 3 or 4 pieces at a time. The chicken will drop the temperature of the oil so keep it as close to 350 degrees as possible. Fry each piece for 14 minutes, turning each piece about half way through, until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees F.
Remove from the oil and place on paper towels. Let them rest for at least 10 minutes before serving.\t\t\t\t\t
