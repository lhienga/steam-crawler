\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is one of the few gems that you can find in the sewer that is HRPGs. Despite its wholesome content and gorgeous art however, I cannot recommend this game especially for perfectionists.

The lovely designer for whatever the reason thought it was a good idea to implement a gacha minigame. Now the reward is nothing to write home about but whereas some of you will finish it in mere minutes, the others will save scum and finish it in half an hour as there are 12 items to draw. For comparison the game wont take more than 2-3 hours to beat without the gacha.

The other *special* design is the fishing mini game. Why put so many fish in a very limited area with random movement? Just why? Whether you will manage to finish this 5 stage event or not depends on pure rng that determines the placement of the fish.

I hope the upcoming dlc will be compatible with finished vanilla saves or iron out these idiotic mechanics for under no circumstance will I ever do the grind again.\t\t\t\t\t
