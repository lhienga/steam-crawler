\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIm actually impressed with this one. I had low expectation, that it would be another half-assed game with a short story and a couple of scenes but no, its actually pretty good.

Its basically a long incest (cousins and aunt) H manga, if you read one you know what to expect. If you've seen a scene in an H manga of this kind, then chances are it is replicated here.

We have our trinity of manga girls: the prim and proper girl, the gyaru and the ara ara milf.

It has some mini games that while simple they're engaging enought to break the monotony and make it a little more than a mindless clicker. It has a calendar system and a game over state that while is hard to trigger if you are careful, it becomes risky when trying to unlock extra content

But when talk about the main attractive is when i was surpised by how much content there. There are two types of scene, the still/semi-still images and the animated pixel ones.

Now at this point you should know that you need a patch to unlock the good stuff.
But much "recreation" we're talking about?

1) the main story progresssion obtained raising the affection on the main girls, this generally are still images that slowly escalated the relationship with the MC. There are between 4 and 5 for each girl.

2) the Free roaming scenes done with the pixel art animation that can be play at almost any time, provided that you meet the affection requirement and have unlocked the positions with skill points. How many positions? like 15 + some extra ones.

3) Stat scenes, this are a mix between pixel animation and still images. Each girl has three bars: lewdness, service and depravaty.
And while you fill this bars, new scene will happen. There around 3 or 4 for each girl.

BUT WAIT, there is more.

Thanks to the dlc you have 5 secondary girls wich add some variety to the cast. This are unlocked by completing a secondary quest, often involving a mini-game. I honestly expected to give you 1 still image and call it for the day, but no.

While they have less content than our main girls, they have free roaming scenes in pixel animation and between 3 and 4 still images unlocked by affection.

In conclusion this is a "recreative"game with some midly entertaining gameplay and plenty of "you-know-what", more than i was honestly expection.
