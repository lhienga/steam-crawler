\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-- GAMEPLAY --
It is a really short game. At least it feels like one, since you only have 30 days. So for the first playtrough you will not unlock too much stuff.
But, this is why new game+ exists. Because you can bring over your progress to a new game.
Otherwise, progress is really easy in this game. Just do the minigames and do every activity with everyone regualry, to unlock everything.
And you want to unlock everything. ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)
10/9

-- GRAPHICS --
Graphics are simple pixel graphics. Many of the naughty scenes, events are animated in this style, but there are a lot of CG style part there, so you can do the thing, why you are here. ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)
And you will do it a lot. Don't worry.
10/8

-- STORY & CHARACTERS--
Charactery are mostly onee chan archetypes (except for 2), but they are really cute. Story is kinda mid, but you are not here for that.
One thing to note, or more like a warning: there is some level of NTR in this.
10/6


-- REPLAYABILITY --
Well, mostly the new game + if you want to unlock everything, without using other methods. But other than that, not much.
10/4

--TL;DR--
It is a fun game, which will make you work on yourself. It has some good art, some nice characters. If you are into this kind of stuff, go for it. ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)
10/8
