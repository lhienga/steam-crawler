80/100 A Heartwarming Tale of Mending a Broken Family

This game is insane. Firstly, there is a patch for it to access all the goodies. This review is made with that patch installed.


+ Surprising amount of content
+ Actual game unlike most eroge
+ C*mpelling narrative
+ Writing isn't bad at all.
+ Skill tree and fun side quests and characters


- Most scenes are still frames with a weird stammer effect
- Shota
- A bit confusing at first


GAMEPLAY


This is one of the most fun adult games I have ever played. It has a ton to do. It has some really entertaining side quests with some interesting characters that you meet and meat. The way each game plays can be confusing but once you get them, they are easy to get hooked on. I wanted to collect everything in this game.


SOUNDTRACK


Some RPG inspired classic bangers- the fishing song being one of the harder tracks, in my honest opinion


STORY


You play as a young (probably too young) protag in the crescendo of his hormonal adolescence, vacationing with your Aunt, who is in a loveless and thankless marriage, and your two cousins, one who is a hearthrob and can oft be seen as only that and the other who is a studious but often forgotten sister of the two daughters. Oh, yeah. Your uncle is there, too.


It's up to you to unite the house once more.


GRAPHICS


The pixel art is endearing and even though not something super top notch, I actually like the art in this game. Some parts were definitely crafted with love. I just don't know why every scene had the lady's cheeks spread. Sometimes I like to see cheeks together.


Also a bummer that their wasn't a lot of animation in this game, other than the overworld scenes, which were dope. If I were to find anything to complain about, it would be that.


MULTIPLAYER


None, of course.




JO POTENTIAL


I give it 8/10 JO's


ANIMATED OR STILL H SCENES



Still framed big scenes, and animated overworld scenes


Conclusion


At the time of posting I have done almost everything so I probably won't be revisiting this, but I will definitely be getting the next game that has released at this time. This was definitely a must for any eroge enjoyer. Very shnice and shnazzy.
