\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, I can recommend this game as long as it is your flavor. That said, my game has the DLC AND the update to uncensor it from the maker so I can't speak to the game alone. Reading other commentary others wish they'd started out with everything.

https://www.kaguragames.com/product/summer-memories-expansion-dlc-patch/

The game is primarily in the map/more pixelated images that you see on the steam preview. Very little of the game is static hentai versions of illustrations. Those that do exist are static with the very little movement other than words describing the action popping up on the screen as well as the three text boxes. The pixelated images have some basic actions and sex moves and those are repeatable through the interface while the hentai illustrations will be one and done. The arousal portion of the game is heavily reliant on the text and the idea of the little dude doing everything in the most perverted ways, that gets more perverted as the game progresses and the girls become corrupted. If you need images more than concepts, the game may not be for you as the variance in images is low in each story (maybe like 4 or 5 for each "unique" scenario).

The style of game is mostly a map navigation with a little dude that wants to do everything that has three holes, but can't early on. The game play is broken into 30 days with a morning/Afternoon/Evening/Night play and an optional late night if you have spec'd into the skill. Each time period allows a certain number of actions points to accomplish what you need to in order to farm your stuff. Play is gated by resources. One resource is affection which you grow with the ladies. The three primary ladies will require homework to be maxed to advance their affection so it can begin accruing again. I found that my player was stuck until I maxed out his lust (or got it high), and then suddenly I had ladies interrupting my play all over the place. Another resource is memory points which you build up through sex or events in order to acquire skills. Sex is constrained by the players stamina as well as lust. You can have sex without lust, but it builds memory points which builds skills, so sex without lust is a waste of resources, at least in the first play through before stamina is maxed at 1000.
The beginning of the game the player is pretty constrained, but half-way to 3/4 of the way through the month it gets easier. It'll definitely take another play through in order to complete the game. When you play the game plus, save the game at the start because if you crash it won't start again with the game plus data. At least mine didn't on my third play through. I had to redo the last two days because of this (and omg the freaking sex scene with the foursome was a ♥♥♥♥♥ to get through).

The flavor of the game is heterosexual with some sleep play (repeatable), anal, urine play later in the game but neither are repeatable. Skills allows more pixel repeatable action, but all are pretty vanilla. The main girls are an aunt and two cousins so... be aware. Not sure what's up with family love in games, but they're not my family so whatever. I expect this to turn some off and others onto the game.

The pro of the game is the idea of a little dude pretty much doing everything and spraying it all with love juice. I discovered you can spray multiple times in the manual section and basically cover the room in everything. Entertaining stuff.

The cons of the game are few although they wouldn't cause me to not recommend unless it's not for you and you're into the flavor. The sex scenes tend to go on and require clicking, no auto play. You also don't get to opt into them. I suppose you can avoid if you don't build up the girls lewd/pervert stats, but where's the fun in that? The text is in three boxes with the lady, the guy, and description. If you're not a reader, you may not appreciate text like that all over the place. I didn't find a way to save out of the long ass sex scenes which could go on for some 15 minutes. The mini game requires a mouse and if you use a touchpad, like me, it's a pain in the cushion. The worst mini games are skippable through a book you can buy but you also miss some content, apparently. The instructions aren't always clear so I'll include a couple of tips below that I didn't feel were fully in the guides.

I know my con list was a bit long, but I honestly enjoyed the game, even with its flaws. At least after the first week or two when things start moving.

Tips:
Do the DLC and patch.
Keep lust around 60-80 if progression of affection is stuck. This worked for me.
You can progress time by clicking on the time of day at the lower right.
Arrows in the top right can help you navigate rooms.
Clicking on your little dude brings up actions/options and saves
You can take things to help with stamina/lust as well as Action Points. (AP).
If you're given videos or fish one up, it requires the nightwalker skill and to manually push night to late night to watch on the TV. You then need to learn/train the skill as you don't learn it automatically.\t\t\t\t\t
