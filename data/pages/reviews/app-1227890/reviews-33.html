I purchased this game when it was released as I thought the pixel graphics was quite interesting for a h-game. Summer Memories did not disappoint.

You spend the summer with your aunt and cousins. It does automatically assume that incest is not very taboo to this family, which means things progress quicker than they should, but there is quite a bit of dialogue to capture a natural progression. I personally would of preferred it to be non-incest but I guess this game is catered to this particular audience.
The gameplay is simple and frustrating at the same time. The H-scenes are decent. The cutscene scenes are usually still images, hentai style, but there are some minor animations that give the illusion of a moving picture. Outside of the cutscenes, all the H-scenes are fully animated, but they're only sprites, so it's more of a visual cue.

Overall, it is a fun and unique h-game, I just don't enjoy incest in general but I highly recommend it if that's your thing ;)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game as Summer Memories "Plus"(DLC) and uncensored.

I want to start out by saying I am sorely disappointed that its 2021 and there is no option to assault your thicc workacholic uncle on the toilet or in the tub.

Next, this game sold on steam is the censored version. There should be a disclaimer about the game content when its censored. You literally cannot progress through the game with this steam censored version. 18 days into my first game play through I downloaded and installed the uncensored patch from the developer Kagura Games' site. My eyes were now open and I could see the paths that lie ahead of me......

This game is very well put together and the game mechanics are fairly complex in how they interact with eachother. For example not every Heroine enjoys the same sex position or act or at the same speed/intensity. They vary, this variance could be RNG or it could be coded within the Heroines themselves. Because they have preference; it makes it worth while (imo) earlier in the game with lower Lust and Stamina stats to explore and find out what they like to efficiently raise their sexual satisfaction with the least expenditure of your stat bars. I found this out on my second game play through with Kagami sensei the Gym teacher, I was able to raise her satisfaction up to 80 of the 100 point cap with a specific set of positions and speed/intensity. Your affection levels with the main Heroines affect your Menko stats, as well as your lust and stamina max values. I only found out that lust could be raised on my second play through thus my progress was limited. Lust and Stamina are key, there is an algorithm for the satisfaction obtained by x lust and y stamina on top of Heroine preference for position and intensity. Long short of it is if you don't have lust you can't please anyone and you'll just be blowing stamina. Memories are your currency for skills.

The writing and dialogue itself isn't bad at all and fairly entertaining. There is a lot of character self awareness and introspection with the MC and supporting characters. They're aware of common tropes of anime genres, games, etc. as well as their actions and how they're perceived and call it out conversation. Alot of the themes surrounding the writing for the sexual acts and dialogue centers on a few key areas "Motherliness, Instinct, NTR/Pseudo -NTR, possessiveness. sexual rewards, and Shotacon". It may not be your thing but I went in blind and thoroughly enjoyed the writing, it can be polished up in a few places but considering this is a translated title i'd say it was very accurately conveyed.

The graphics themselves are very charming and well put together for the sprite animations. I do wish the character designs had more diversity in figure but maybe its something in the water or the food out there. Akari was the only outlier amongst all the Heroines, but considering the art style it makes sense from a development point to not have to draw so many different animations for each specific body. This was not the case with sex-positions and acts; you have a healthy array of skills to unlock and perform all very well animated and very enjoyable.

The voice acting in the game is pretty minimal outside the sex scenes, Miyuki's was the worst in my opinion its very generic of the genre. Rio was my favorite and everyone else is pretty decent. But Miyuki's moans and cries were annoying to say the least.

I do have a complaint about the scale of the game as you progress. Stamina and lust replenishment actions have fixed amounts that don't scale with the players capacity as it increases. This makes later game more reliant on buying consumables to replenish those status bars. Meaning you need to have money. Money in this game isn't the easiest thing to come by even with skills, I've rolled over my items and cash from previous games to help out with this.

There are 10 endings to this game and many many sex scenes/ memories to unlock. At the time of this writing I had secured 8 endings on my third play through. but i'm just over half of full game completion for the scenes. A big turn off for visual novel games for me at least is the replay-ability, some games are a slog to get through and can be intimidating given the amount of hours they require. This game was the first one I've really enjoyed without that concern it really left a better impression on me of the visual novel genre and has given me alot more confidence to sit down with uncompleted titles I already have such as Bible black, Starless NPD, The Fruit of Girisaia, Hitomi my step sister, etc. I would hands down recommend this game if you're looking to get into visual novels, the RPG aspects really balanced out the game play and the control of the character in the world itself was a really refreshing feature outside of traditional dialogue options. I was so impressed with this game I bought the Dojin Otome's other title Succumate which I think shares the same universe. I have high hopes for that game as well given my experience with this one.

All in all this game is well worth it, you can play with one hand and the impressions the characters leave on you are more than enough to crank a few out without a visual aid. It's a shame this game doesn't have achievements.\t\t\t\t\t
