\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbout 11 hours of gameplay to unlock all the content (including DLC).

There's some decent gameplay here between the minigames and time-management; and the generous NG+ mode means the game's ticking-clock element is really a non-issue.

There's plenty of (shotacon) H-scenes, and the translation is good, with no Engrish (and only a couple of typos or similar minor errors through the entire game). The dialogue's writing is good - it does get a bit repetitive towards the end, especially with the DLC content, but that's the case with all hentai. There's only so many ways you can describe the same thing.
There's also a load of posable sprite animations that can be unlocked and add additional sexiness.

The setting and setup's fun too.

The game invites comparison to the superlative Summertime Saga. There's some similarities, but this is far more traditional in its Hentai sensibilities; whereas Summertime Saga is far more westernised and innovative.
Bearing in mind that Summertime Saga is freely available, that title wins over this one hands down. It's got far more variety and interesting game mechanics going on; its graphics are far better (the art in this one is pretty low res).

However, if you enjoyed Summertime Saga and are willing to pay for a fix, this is a good substitute while you're waiting for the next content drop.

With a bit more polish this game could be a real standout gem. As it stands at present, it's a little over-priced. It's still a solid product though.

Note that uncensor DLC is available at the publisher's official site: The DLC uncensor (restoration) patch includes the base game patch. The base-game patch will NOT work on an installation that includes the DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
