\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is definitely 18/10 worth your money. because you're basically a 18 y.o "Shota"(don't ask.) having a summer vacation in cousin's house. and every activity u do, will be drawn in diary.

and yes i wasted 7 hour in 9 PM for this "Family Friendly" game to get 100% progress.

Detailed Pro & Cons

+Pros :

+ You played as a 18 y.o "Shota", and every "Activity" u did will drawn in the picture diary. especially when you teach your cousin how to eat a "creamy mushroom" or making a "cream pie".

+ Lots of hooge hungalahoobs heroine & sub-heroine, and they all attractive. (about 8 characters in total)

+ Flexible, you can make it Family friendly game when you don't install the patch from the official website.

+ Lot of things to do everyday like mini games, train yourself, giving offering, and etc. So its optional to you "what you should improve first".

+ The music is felt so innocent :D, especially while u teach your cousin to make a cream pie. and when your cousin playing piano, you can hear it in her room. Small touch but its really cool detail. and main heroines is voiced differently.

+ Lots of item that you can use depend on your situation and it only cost money.

Cons.
- Dish washing homework is pain in the bum.

- Because lots of things to do, you might missed lots of stuff in first run where you need to retry it like 2-3 times in game ( i did 100% 2 & half run)

- Money basically your skip on everything, and you just need like 18000+ to skip wasting time by spending time with main girls for their affection. or you can just farm fishing in river to get some expensive stuff if you're lucky enough.

- Every max affection and homework, you fulfilled the condition to proceed the affection event. but sometimes there's unwritten condition where you need to sleep with her, or you need to unlock this move first, and etc. So some people might think why i can't find the event or this game is buggy as f.

- as i said heroines are voiced. but not sub heroines they are silence. and when you teach them how to make a "Cream Pie", you only hear yourself clapping and spreading the cream.

- Every action even giving a offering or fishing except shopping. it cost 1 AP, so you need to wisely plan your move for the day.

- They did not tell you when to stop teaching your cousin making a "Cream Pie" so you always knocked up every session and wasted 2 phase for recovering 30 stamina. (click to your character to use item or stop teaching her.)

There's lot more Pro & Cons, but i only point out which i like or i don't in 7 hours of playing this game.

That's all of Review and Pro Cons from my 7 hours play. and i hope it helps, if you need me to help you to proceed this game, just ask me, i will be your tour guide. Thank you for who read this long review that took your important 7+ mins.\t\t\t\t\t
