\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI tried to like this "game", I really did. It is one of the first games in a long time I have given up on.
The game is mediocre in every single aspect except story and even then the story isn't for everyone.

Combat is easy to the point where you could effectively put anyone in front of it, gamer or not and they are getting through the game no problem, spam dodge attack, activate "focus" when its up and parry (with very generous parry timing), do not get this game if you are looking for anything other than a walking simulator.

Puzzles are all tedious and there is a huge lack of things that point you in the right direction, for a game that prides itself on being immersive things are not immersive when you have to google aspects of a puzzle. I chalk this up to poor game design and the game failing to inform you on what can and can't be done but it is also my smooth brain, looking up a tutorial is my fault and I can accept that but I shouldn't /have/ to.

Moving around feels really sluggish, the atmosphere whilst great, quickly becomes shallow when you realise the world isn't very interactive whatsoever and it becomes a case of walking through pretty scenery completing tedious puzzles. This game is also a victim of invisible walls being literally everywhere.

I can appreciate that this game is not for me and I gave it a fair chance imo, playing through roughly half of the game from what I saw online. From what I saw of the story, I found it to be enjoyable but this would be better off as a movie rather than a game. Unless you really, really enjoy Puzzle Games/Walking Simulators I would stay clear from this game.\t\t\t\t\t
