\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHellblade Senua's Sacrifice is an interesting deep dive into a world of Norse Gods, and Celtic Warriors. The main story is divulged from various Lorestones, not-so-hidden in the world, narrated by a fantastic voice actor. It's an excellent game, albeit short. I by no means speed-ran it, and I 100% completed it in a little under 6 hours. The combat is basic, although fun. I do have two gripes with the combat: 1. Playing on KB/M, and having 7-10 adds on the screen, and then attempting to switch targets, can be difficult. The default keys are 'Z' and 'C' 2. The FOV is insanely close to the character, and seeing enemies around you can be increasingly challenging.

Also, I must say, if you're going back to collect Lorestones, and you're using a youtube guide to find them all, just know that ALL of the lorestones assume that you are collecting them in order. For example, if you collect Lorestone 9, and it's an 'S' Symbol, and all of the youtube videos show that Lorestone 9 is an 'N' Symbol, and you're confused, it means that you missed a Lorestone previously in that zone. 10/10 Can't wait for Senua 2.\t\t\t\t\t
