\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt has been decades, that I last played a game this majestic.

Just as it says: fair warning.
If you experience mental issues, it might be good to inform yourself first.
I went into it, knowing this all too well, and it still hit like a truck.

Senua's sacrifice touches upon mental issues, and does an incredible job at it.

Gameplay:
You will be challenged by intuitive puzzles.
No markers or other HUD info.
You will be solving them with your senses and wit.
Moving through beautiful and breathtaking places while doing so.

Alternatively, you will run into encounters with a variety of enemies.
Plunging you into engaging and thrilling battles.
Combat is also intuitively, not using any displays of health.
It rewards your reflexes and abilities to adapt during a fight.
Eventually, getting a perfect parry, rewards the player with powerful counters.

Story:
Senua's sacrifice slowly submerges you into it's Norse mythology themed world.
Visiting many of it's well-known places, fighting powerful gods and beasts.

I was prepared for what had to come.
And still, I managed to break eventually.
Never before, have I struggled through emotions of this magnitude.

Now, I carry Senua in my heart.
Just like she has touched many others.
A beloved experience, that I will never forget.
Even making it difficult to write this review.

If you are going through hard times, know that there is always someone to help.
We're not alone.
My heart and soul goes out to all of you.
You are loved, and have your rightful place in this world.
Never give up, and don't be afraid to reach out for professional help.

We all need a helping hand now and then.
Love everlasting.
