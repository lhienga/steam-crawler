\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, I had fun with this game. I would recommend it to people who are into short, dense experiences.

PROS:
+ The story is very good. I never felt like I lost the plot anywhere, although a couple of characters disappeared after some time.
+ Gameplay is rudimentary, but has a solid foundation. Your standard fare of dodging, blocking, and attacking. A surprisingly wide variety of moves to compliment its simplicity.
+ Graphics are great. I spent a lot of time looking at the main structures of the game, which proved to be a welcome interpretation of Norse mythos.
+ The world, as mentioned earlier, interprets the Norse mythology very well, and I was very impressed by how different it ended up being.
+ The soundtrack and audio design are very good as well. Boss themes are very motivating and in general every aspect of the audio compliments the world.
+ Boss fights are good and have good foundations. Nothing was overtly unfair in my eyes, and they ended up being straightforward, engaging moments in the game.
+ Voice acting is also noteworthy, with the main protagonist being especially powerful and moving.

CONS:
- This game has a severe lack of indicators of any kind. I found myself having to check the pause screen to see which button does what quite a bit. Also, lack of indicators during combat makes it somewhat confusing at times. Health bars, attack direction indicators (besides The Voices), and more would be greatly valued.
- Story and world could have gone a bit more in depth in my opinion. Exploring the given landscape is cool and all, but I still feel like more could be done to tie this world into its real-life roots.
- Lack of enemy variety. Like 5 enemies total: there's the sword guy, the mace guy (who's basically just the sword guy but more tanky), the shield guy, the dual wield guy, and the big axe guy. I feel like with a little more variety, this game could be more interesting in terms of combat. They encourage you to use all of the game mechanics, however, but I still hope that more is done in the sequel.
- A few significant bugs. One of the ones I ran into got me stuck on terrain, forcing me to reenter the game.

Overall 8/10. Probably buy on sale or full price if you really want it (I got mine for $5 which was a steal IMO). Can't wait for the sequel!\t\t\t\t\t
