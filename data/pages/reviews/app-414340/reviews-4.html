For people who are like me with Photosensitive Epilepsy or have light sensitivity, proceed with caution because there are some effects that could bother you depending on sensitivity.
There's lightning in some areas and an effect in a certain area Helheim - when you go into the darkness. The screen goes red and images flash up .
I'm normally highly sensitive to flashing but these didn't bother me at all. The effects aren't fast and don't take up all the screen but of course proceed carefully since everyone's sensitivity is different!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOH BOY
I want to love this game SO much, but I hate it! It started with the really choppy gameplay, which turns out to be the directx setting (move it from 12 to 11) and/or ray tracing (turn that off). I was happy again when I figured that out. Then I started to get frustrated with the 'run' button: it's not a toggle, you have to keep it pressed down, and it's more of a light trot than actual running. SO slow.. but ok i guess i can adjust. Then it was the combat. I couldn't figure out what i was supposed to do, it's a lot of button mashing, but the most important one (the focus button) is not explained, nor how to get up when you're down. It was very frustrating, until I looked that up as well and then it was a lot better. Very frustrating though, especially when you can't skip the cutscene's you've watched so many times due to unnecessary dying in combat. And then it gets annyoing that I can't save when I want, i have to wait for the game to decide where I should save. And after 2,5 hours of struggles with the controls, choppy cutscenes (that you can't skip), and slow jogs I am done. I'm sorry game, you've bested me. I wanted to love you. but I hate you. I guess I'll go watch a gameplay video to see what the story's about, because that at least seems interesting. Or maybe I'll try again with controller, maybe that's better? sigh...\t\t\t\t\t
