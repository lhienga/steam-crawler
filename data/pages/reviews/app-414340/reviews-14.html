\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHellblade is an absolute masterpiece and a fantastic work of fiction. Beautiful in every sense of the word. The art direction is immaculate and the game is simply stunning to look at. The sound design is absolutely perfect. Everything sounds fantastic, from the wind rushing through the trees to the crack of thunder. The music is fitting and exciting.

Without a doubt one of the most impressive parts of the game is the story, which is both heart wrenching and uplifting. It is respectful of the very serious topics it address, and it is very hard not to empathize deeply with Senua and her quest.

The only real complaint is the combat, which is entertaining at best and merely serviceable at worst. At the final battle i just turned the difficulty down to enjoy the world and the story the game painted.

I honestly cannot rave enough about this game. I expected something good, instead I was greeted with something that is truly artistic in every way.

10/10, very much recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
