\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a game. This is my first real review for a game that I ever made to date. I have to say the story that is told throughout is beautiful with a deep message about life and love (doing it in a tasteful manner and not making it corny). The awareness this game gives to mental illness that some people suffer from and gives everyone the ability to see how they might be suffering throughout life is also just an amazing jaw dropping piece of art/experience. So happy that I found this game and played it. I might also be biased with my background since I studied Psychology in college and have a very well and in depth understanding of schizophrenia, but I feel everyone should at least play or watch a play through of this game to gain a understanding of how someone might be suffering from a mental illness such as schizophrenia or psychosis to share empathy with them. Once again if you are on the fence of buying this game... let me tell you, if you have any background in psychology or a family member that might be suffering from a mental illness like the ones that this game involves you will gain a new understanding and feeling of empathy for the field of study and that family member. Not only does this game have a great story the visuals and art style of the game are so well done and eye pleasing for a game made in 2017. My only complaint about the game would be the combat being a little hard to get used to but once you get passed that you will really enjoy it. The puzzles for each section were amazing and really challenging which made me think harder then must games I have played. Giving me a fresh air from the usual games that I play. Overall I would recommend this game to everyone that is thinking about getting it. You will not be disappointed with your purchase of this game. Cant wait for the new one that I saw that is coming out soon!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is the single best game i have ever played. From the gorgeous visuals to the story-telling to the fighting mechanics are all absolutely flawless. The price of this game really isn't what it's supposed to be. All my life since childhood no game has gotten me this excited before. Moreover, this game is the only one i actually consider 18+. I have played many games considered the best of all time like Elden Ring, Hogwarts Legacy, Cyberpunk 2077, thw witcher 3 etc. However this game stood out amongst them. If i would reccomend a game to anyone it would be this. A true 10/10.

This game has alot of pros, but also some cons.

Pros: Visuals, story-telling, voice-acting, the paranoia factor, transitions from cutseen to battle, fighting mechanics, character designs, monster designs, level designs, puzzles, lore, difficulty.

Cons:
the paranoia factor (This game creates such an memorable experience with the paranoia. This means it could be troublesome for people who has experienced something similar.)

Scary (this game is extremely scary. Therefore, if someone who has trouble with scary games you should play with caution. However, the way Senua's Sacrifice has implimented it in such a way making the fear a good thing.)
Pc-troubles (when playing my pc will begin to warm up more than other games which could be a problem for those with a not so strong pc, but not that huge of a problem.)

Difficulty (Without spoiling anything the difficulty is on the high-end. Not so much as souls games, but it's up there. This could be a problem regarding a certain aspect of the game.)

Even with all these minor setbacks for some, it still is a fantastic game i would reccomend to anyone with a will for a new experience.\t\t\t\t\t
