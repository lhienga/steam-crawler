HellBlade is difficult that’s no lie but once you get the hang of the controls and the movement you’ll be able to progress through the game easily and that allows for more time listening along with the great story line.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMagnificent game showcasing the struggles and tribulations of psychosis/schizophrenia. All be it, Senua has an extremely violent form that many if not most don't have, but it still doesn't subtract from the importance of this game.

My first play-through I almost collected all of the lore stones, and was left with the question of "Was this ALL in her head?" after finally collecting the last 3 I've seen it so. I'm excited to see what the sequel will bring; the story here was great but a bit on the shorter side, with myself only obtaining 6 and a half hours of time.

Getting this game on sale is not only a steal but still provides the great lesson of "Never judge someone till you've walked a mile in their boots". With all of that being said there are not many bugs or noticeable flaws other than one really. The game does tend to stutter a bit when loading in new areas/levels, but to be honest after a while you get used to it.

In the end I'd give this game a solid 8.5/10 the story was a bit shorter than expected but the combat was great and the visuals were amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
