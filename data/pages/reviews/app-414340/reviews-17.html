\r\n\t\t\t\t\tso i just finished the hellblade 1. there's some cons i'd admit. ofcourse there's no such thing as perfect game.

few cons such as the brightness of the game is kinda pita. sometimes its very dark and hard to see clear so i need to check the gamma or adjust it every time to fit on the game.
save feature should be applied on the game. not auto save. but a real save.
combat is not so great. not enough tutorials on it like i just discovered the kick and charge attack at mid game, and combat is a trigger combat system only, meaning you can only go combat mode on specific combat scene. that's kinda bad as it minimize the freedom of players. and the force autolock is kinda bad. when multiple enemies spawn and sometimes some of them spawn behind me, i need to click the switch autolock many times so my lock will go on the enemy behind me. kinda terrible. luckily im pro-gamer so ain't that pretty hard for me to adjust.
and the game mechanics is very much empty. simple combat and visual puzzle. that's all. although the story and the animation is superb, its lacking more interactions on the game.
also the music copyright on the ending. that's actually bad for business. as streamers and content creators will avoid the game resulting for low promotion. so they should apply streamer mode on settings next time.

overall, i rate hellblade 1 of 7.7/10. im fine with game but its kinda short and pretty much lacking of many things. i still recommend the game if you're onto the lore of the story.

what i expect on hellblade 2 are:
more character interactions and better combat system such as variety of weapons. range attack such as bows or throwing axe like the enemies on hellblade 1 are doing. anything you can add to improve the combat style of the character not just hack and slash. (parry is really great, don't remove it.) trophies collection ain't bad also. more quest paths means more reasons to play the game over again after the ending and also more reason for players to really pay attention on the game story. so players will not just rush it to the end without thinking about the story as there's no bad or good ending.
if you will going to apply lorestones again please allowed players to go back on previous chapter if they accidentally missed one of lorestones. or better put some indications like diary checklist of lorestones for that chapter.

and about the story, cutscenes, and animation i have no problem with it. its actually really good. but as a gamer, i am more concern on the actual gameplay mechanics of the game. so yeah im really looking forward on the improvement of it on hellblade 2.

btw i beat hellblade 1 in hard mode. but i don't feel its hard.\t\t\t\t\t
