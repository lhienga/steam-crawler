Game doesn't hold your hand, there's no "training level", "HUD", popups to explain what you must do or help you do it. I liked that aspect as it added something special to the experience. Each enemy has different flavour and requires different approach. I like how the voices are also incorporated into combat (not being random).

Story-wise, game is sometimes confusing, but that's just the point (mental illness representation).

Please watch the "Hellblade Feature" but do so AFTER you complete the game (they added a warning that it contains spoilers, thanks for that, otherwise I might have spoiled my gameplay).
It's a short documentary about making of the game and it is beautiful.\t\t\t\t\t
