You spend most of your time in this game walking around solving simple hidden object puzzles as the story is narrated to you. Senua will sometimes talk to the camera and give you some insight to what she's experiencing. The whole way the story is told is very abstract which is another thing that might not be for everybody.

I enjoyed the overall visual style of this game and the combat is also solid if too brief. Just know what you getting into before picking up this game\t\t\t\t\t
