That said, the simplistic puzzles made the experience feel very tedious - especially by the last third of the game. Once I realised most events were scripted and the only danger I faced was getting stuck at certain sections because of some unintuitive path I should've followed, the gaming experience mostly flatlined. I am still recommending this game, and I'm glad I finally got to play it after all these years. But due warning, the gameplay will make you feel like you're stuck inside a walking simulator after a while. Combat was fun, but certain areas, chokepoints, and even boss fights, felt all poorly designed at times.

Overall good experience, but I'm not doing this again.\t\t\t\t\t
