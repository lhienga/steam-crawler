\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is visually stunning. Even 7 years later its one of the best looking games i can think of. The art is excellent, the sound design is excellent, and the animations vary from good to excellent. I found the games depiction of psychosis to be quite engaging and it uses shock to great effect.

The game is let down by being a bad PC port. At 1440p with a 5600x, a 6800xt and 16Gb of high quality ram, i suffered with severe performance issues. Even at settings which provide me a reasonable average of 60-75FPS the game is a stutery mess. V-sync also seems to be broken leading to visual tearing throughout the entire game. The game has many glitches, which will force you to reload. I eventually resorted to editing ini files in order to remove the motion blur which the game forces on you.

The combat can also be quite frustrating at times, especially due to the locked camera which makes you not notice enemies that will lead to your death. Though the combat is quite satisfying when it does work.

All that said, i thoroughly recommend playing it. Don't worry about the short playtime and lack of replay value. This game makes up for the lack in quantity with quality.\t\t\t\t\t
