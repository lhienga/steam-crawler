\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is very interesting for some reasons. I usually don't play this kind of game and to be honest would have never played it if it wasn't on sale for a couple of Euros. But if it is on sale, you SHOULD buy and play it. I think I played the story in about 8 hours or even less. The fights were actually challenging for me. The game also combines mental illness and norse mythology very well. I mean, it was really well made. After the game I also watched the "Hellblade-Function" Movie, which can be found in the main menu and only then I really realised that the psychotic moments the main character Senua has is actually based on real life experiences. The storytelling is really, really good as well.
However to the gameplay itself: like others, I thought it was a little too monotone and linear (read the top-review by Heeb). Also the saving points were sometimes weird and once I got stuck on a stone (bug), couldn't move, had to restart and play like 20minutes again, including a fight which was really challenging for me.


So:
+ Narration
+ Music
+ Story
+ Character
+ Effects and Particles
+ based on real life experiences
+ loved the fighting (but I was happy enough when a fight was over. Death sometimes leads to refighting the WHOLE ENTIRE FIGHT AGAIN with like 20 challenging enemies)

- limited movement
- limited "exploration"
- limited interactions
- you don't really get anything from listening to runestones etc. (though very interesting if you like norse mythology and great addition to the game)

Thanks for this game. I do highly recommend it (when on sale) :)\t\t\t\t\t
