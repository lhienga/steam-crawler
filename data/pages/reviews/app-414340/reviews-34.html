Most of the time it feels like a walking simulator, save for a few interesting perspective puzzles. Even then you really just walk around until you find the answer. These puzzles would be way more interesting if you could interact with things in your environment to make the symbols you are looking for.
The fights are also not super interesting, and the way you have to play to beat multiple enemies is ridiculous.
While the "guiding voices" in your ear are an interesting idea, they are also annoying as ♥♥♥♥.
The environment is beautiful though, the models and settings are pretty darn good.
Overall, Not for me\t\t\t\t\t
