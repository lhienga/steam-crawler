\r\n\t\t\t\t\t11/10 Absolute psychedelic nightmare drama Masterpiece!!! With Vikings!!!

2024 - Still masterpiece!

This is another one of these games where you have to push forward to your goal, no matter what obstacles hit you.

The game puts you in the dirt multiple times, but you have to get up and FIGHT.

Hey, if you like instant fast action, open world, looting, micromanagement, super complex gameplay, THIS IS NOT FOR YOU.

This is a cinematic story game with nothing to loot. The world is ultra linear, but in a massive story game with playing a living NIGHTMARE there is nothing to blame about it. I don't miss any open world when everything around me is a flashing nightmare.

The story is about a celtic warrior woman going to hell (Helheim), to rescue the soul of her dead man.

If you're not sure about the game, go YT and search for "Hellblade - Sea of Corpses NO DAMAGE" my most loved stage in the complete game (WARNING THERE ARE SOME SPOILERS INCLUDED!!!).\t\t\t\t\t
