Initially, the game has you struggling with resources but IF you can get past that, the mid-game has you swimming in resources but no worthwhile weapons or armor to spend it on because you already got the best the game has to offer. In short: The enemies still get progressively more powerful with time, but your own forces don't. Caveat: I played the vanilla game. Against the most powerful "boss" enemies I might as well have brought BB guns and wet paper armor. I finally hit a wall where the difficulty and the lack of fun simply wasn't worth whatever conclusion the storyline would come to.

I still recommend trying it but I honestly can't see myself having another go. More of the same is exactly why I stopped playing.\t\t\t\t\t
