Helpful, bit OP weapons and missions that took good chunk of my gameplay (enemies with constant shielding and near perfect aim from opposite end of the map? HELL NO.) And grenades... why most of the time they felt more like liability than actual help?

I enjoyed the game but man... can understand why only ~6% of players actually got to the end credits.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot an easy game but very good.

Some people say it's too difficult or not well balanced. It wasn't well balanced at first, but many updates have fixed that and added many options to win the game and lore that's actually motivating to continue playing.

It's a complex one, I'll give you that. It's not only about field tactics, you need to play the strategy game too, take care of the economy, choose well your alliances and get to know them so that when they ask you know how to respond. Research is very important: you need to choose well what to research both for advancing the game and for your play style.
Most importantly you need to understand you MUST NOT research it all. The strategy layer is mostly a war against time, so poor choices on what to research, build, or not caring enough about your alliances will kill you later on.

The good news is that the game is very replayable, all locations are randomized at the beginning of the game. So you can take as many runs as you want to learn the deep mechanics and not get bored.

The battles are difficult too. Cover and positioning is key, and making the right decision on when to rush or when to stay back until the enemy makes a mistake will save your day. My advice, don't save every move and load when you fail. Play each battle till the end. If you reach the point where you know it's lost, then restart it and try a different approach. You'll learn faster on what works for each situation. Restarting a battle randomizes enemies and their positions which will be a real test for your tactics. If you pass you are ready for the next level. Loading saved games in this game is like cheating on exams, you'll pass now, but sooner or later the reality catches with you and you won't be able progress no matter how much you reload.

It has a long learning curve but it's fantastic if you enjoy both strategy and tactics.\t\t\t\t\t
