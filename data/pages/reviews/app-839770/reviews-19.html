The main deal breaker for me is the base management and geoscape. Xcom hits the perfect sweet spot of simplicity and complexity. Pheonix point cranks it up to the max to the point it becomes a chore. Almost a deal breaker, but the combat saves it. The resource management is fine, but the magazine system and some DLC unit maintenance really burns your resource. Yes, in this game you do not have unlimited ammo, instead you must craft/scavange magazines, If you have titanium DLC, your mechanical unit is almost not worth it. Tanky, but even the tiniest dent will burn your resource for maintenance.
