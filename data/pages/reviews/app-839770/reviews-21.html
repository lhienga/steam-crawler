\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was on sale so i thought why not it looks like XCOM, and i love xcom.
I really enjoyed this game in the beginning because it had a xcom kinda feel to it.
The amount of micro management was not too bad..

Until..

As you progress the game just throws too much at you at once.
The tech unlocking content felt unearned.
Research felt like i was unlocking new tech every minute of the game.
The game constantly forces you into battles by ambushing you.
Too many useless gun and armor options.
Too many micromanaged bases.

The game starts turning into Stellaris, Dont get me wrong, i love that game too, but they did it without too much micro managing.
This game where you have 100 bases and 100 army each you need to manage each character in each base.
The whole game just gets way too overly complicated to be enjoyable for me.

The worst part is the factions.
There is a massive pandemic virus outbreak and humanity must survive! nope! all the factions wanna kill each other. and they do. Each faction wants you to turn against the other faction so you have to baby sit each faction into not killing each other. or just pick a side. you need to micro manage them.
The early game was so promising.

I've given it 15 hours of gameplay and i already got to the near end game. But i was really not enjoying myself enough to keep going, i just feel the game lacks the long game aspect.

I really can't enjoy this game.

If you like Xcom, probably don't buy this game.
If you like Stellaris and more micromanagement you might like this. who knows.
But after playing i don't think i will play it again.
Might try again in a few years when i forget why i didn't like it.\t\t\t\t\t
