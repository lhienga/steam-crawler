\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoring for some reason. Shooting same alien repeatedly (because there is no other way to kill it) gets boring fast, unlike in xcom where i could create efficient squad and exterminate! (dalek voice). Here no matter what i try it is just boring shooting without much thinking. Yes i played just few hours, but xcom is way better. I recommend it for on sale etc. and for xcom fans.

Edit:
I read in some review "Game has no soul", and yes that is exactly my feeling, that is why it seems to be boring.

Edit2:
Played this game more, and this is how it went:

I started on ROOKIE difficulty to learn ropes and how all works. After some time i faced some well armored enemies (aka bullet sponge). They were not smarter, not harder, just more armor and hp to kill. I realized that all my assault and heavy units were useless, because crap damage of their weapons. Only snipers had chance to hit those new armored enemies.

So i restarted game again and used only snipers. Everything was fine i went trough first wave of those armored bullet sponges. But then there comes even more armored big artillery enemy. It produced enemies as fast as all my 6 snipers could kill them, leaving nothing for big enemy. I could win this (if i had enough of ammo) after 30min of shooting and kiting.

So i restarted game once more, looked on internets for solutions. And i learned how to get better sniper rifles (hiring snipers from other factions). This time i could (barely) kill that artillery enemy. But then next mission i was overrun by dozen of heavily armored enemies.

All this time difficulty did not increased, just my weapons were made useless by raised armor and hp of enemies. They still were as dumb as in first mission, i just could not kill any of them in time.

So I wanted to love this game and learn how it is meant to be played, i got some console commands (cheats), maxed out weapons and my squad. This time i went big further but again more armor more hp, same stupid aliens with more armor.

Luckily i got one faction to allied and they shared armor piercing rifle. However i see that even basic enemies have multpile armor pieces. They still randomly roam without any clue (used console to remove fog of war to see what is going on).

At this point i realized that tactical combat is no fun. shooting for 30min to kill single enemy, kitting it using cheesy tactics, all that is not smart nor fun. Game is booring, because instead of some challenging difficulty and tactical choices all we have is bullet sponges and artificial difficulty.

Old classic XCOM (1990s) was great game because it was possible to kill those aliens, it was possible to counteract their tech, most units died in 2-3 shoots. Then there was xcom from the depth where to increase difficulty they added armor and more hp. Same happens here, shooting single enemy for 10 or more rounds is not fun nor challenging, it is boring.

Changing to not recommended.

Ps.
Game would be just fine if there were difficulty sliders for armor and hp creep (how much it increases during gameplay). And "size" of world (number of siode misions). Yes i just wanted to see story for first gameplay (and learn ropes), however even with cheats in tactical, managing whole game is just turning into another chore, so many side missions, bases to build etc.\t\t\t\t\t
