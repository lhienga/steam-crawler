\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBase game has a pretty rugged learning curve imo. You're not even on your backfoot at the start of the game and around the mid of it, you're legs have been cut off and you're ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ crawling just trying to survive.

I love the aesthetic though, from the different faction weapons, armor, vehicles and personalized haven leaders to the splatter-pastel looking cutscenes. Crab people be funny too, even if there is way too many different forms of each for me to mentally keep track of weaknesses and strengths.

Somewhat into the story so far, i've restarted a few times due to the learning curve I mentioned, but with each one I feel like I am somewhat getting better.

I like the ability to have an army too, multiple teams across the world to react as necessary to the ever growing threat, it is something I felt lacking from the XCOM games and am glad to see it fleshed out here.

Definitely some bugs and UI Issues. The game likes to freeze and do a wobble with your characters if they get shot by return fire, takes a solid 10 seconds before it allows you to do anything again.

DLC wise, the robot one and the most recent one aren't worth it IMO. Roboto is just so underwhelming and a waste of potential, while the new one adds a creature that is fundamentally broken. The one that adds the lil air combat sequences is a fun little side on, but I'm not sure if i've started the legacy of the ancients content so I wont comment on it.

Get it on sale if you aren't too impressed by what I mentioned but think it might be worth a try.\t\t\t\t\t

The product of the original XCOM creator Julian Gallop and his team at Snapshot Games, Phoenix Point delivers what any series veteran has come to expect, but sets the action in a futuristic, post-apocalyptic world ravaged by creatures inspired by H.P. Lovecraft’s cosmic horror Cthulhu mythos.

To top things off, the whole game is paced differently than Firaxis’ XCOM games, playing more like campaigns in XCOM 2’s Long War mod to give players the feel of running a post-apocalyptic survival force to cooperate and unite with the wasteland’s many factions and rebuild humanity against the onslaught of nightmarish creatures from the dark beyond, wherever that may be.
