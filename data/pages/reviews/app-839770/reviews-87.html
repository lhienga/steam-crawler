\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe good:
- excellent turn based mechanics
- interesting and varied classes, factions and vehicles.
- excellent visuals.
- improved aiming system (no 99% chance to hit misses like in Xcom each bullet has its own trajectory)
- engaging story.
- good performance with only a few bugs. The most serious I have come across is very occasionally the game hangs when the enemy is doing their turn and it never passes back to you. alt+f4 is the only solution.
The bad:
- repetitive missions can feel "grindy" at times(urgh do I need to save another Haven... urgh another nest mission can't I just get the techs!).
- DLC doesn't make sense sometimes and can break immersion, "wait I am allied to that faction why would I fight them for an artifact and not just have the option to request it?"
- some clunky interfaces for example when you have lots of tech that you can manufacture the manufacturing screen can become unmanageable,
- becomes a nightmare to manage when you have multiple squads on multiple aircraft.
Overall:
recommend, very enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhoenix Point Year one edition is a strategy game from the creator of the original X-COM series.

It features turn based tactics and world based strategy in a fight against a terrifying, alien menace. For every mission will require you to deploy a squad of four or more soldiers in the battlefield

Player building base around the globe, recruit and upgrade soldier ability, do research to get more advanced equipment, helping other from the enemy attack to get more support point

From a visual perspective, phoenix point has really cool graphics, a map that represents the real world, and customize character models. Destructible Objects when vehicles pass through obstacles, the impact of gunfire and grenades makes the game feel more alive and realistic. also the meaty feeling when the bullet hitting the enemy is so satisfied

You can also change the costumes for each soldier as you wish, and enter codes in main menu to get more additional costumes

Overall Phoenix Point is one of the best strategy game i ever play in 2021, with a great story, challenging mission to tackle and really cool graphic make this game one of the most compelling turn-based strategy games i ever play.\t\t\t\t\t
