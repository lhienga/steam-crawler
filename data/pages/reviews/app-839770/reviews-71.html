\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile not as polished as x-com phoenix point is still a fun game. I really like the aim feature and picking apart enemies limb by limb. The grand strategy part of the game can be a bit overwhelming at times with so many locations to search and defend. You can also hit a point were you have to wait to advance the story because a random event wont happen. As someone that always wants to do all the missions and side missions this game was tough because that is just not an option if you want to beat the game. One of the greatest improvements in the last update was giving you the ability to set the game speed at a higher pace which in my opinion makes the long trek to the end game more bearable.

On a side note for the completionist out there some of the achievement are bugged and wont trigger. I am hoping they fix it in the next update\t\t\t\t\t
