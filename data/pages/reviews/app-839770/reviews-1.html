\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, it's impossible to review this game without comparisons to Xcom.
It's got a lot of cool ideas to set itself apart, but it's the lack of polish that lets things down.

The idea of free aim, love it. Targeting body parts, also interesting. The geoscape, managing bases, faction relations, exploring etc, all are a step above Xcom and added to my immersion in the world.

What killed it for me though, was the little frustrations. Managing dozens of ammo types, not being able to clearly compare armour/weapons, the classes being indepth but unclear.
The worst thing though that just outright ****ed me off, was the constant stuttering of time. On missions you'd send someone to move, and they'd stop several times as they spotted an enemy, with me having to issue the same movement order again and again. And on the geoscape, a popup pausing movement then not automatically resuming when I closed it.

So yeah, it had go ideas, but I just found the act of actually playing to be too annoying to continue.\t\t\t\t\t
