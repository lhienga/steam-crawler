The next bundle came in 2019. I got everything in a Mega Bundle deal, one being a Bundle of the Stuff Packs I was missing, and the other being a Bundle of the Expansion Packs I was missing. The total was $79.

(Also, this isn't me telling you to do it, but: They don't sell Katy Perry's DLC anymore. And for me, I wasn't accepting that. Let's just say... with a little Googling, you can get yourself a download of Katy Perry's Sweet Treats and install it into the files with the rest of the Packs, and the launcher lights up Katy Perry's Pack icon just like normal, and then you have the "Complete Collection")

Now, in today's age, I doubt you'll get that lucky anymore, with how hard they're pushing TS4. But honestly, if you ever find a deal like I did, snatch it up. The Sims 3 is far better than The Sims 4, even if it's a little choppy. Just get MasterController, and NRaas.

Those Mods handle cleaning up stuff around the world, and turning off stuff the NPCs leave on. And just generally makes it run a little better.

Then, personally, you should check out Fresh Prince's car mods for TS3. Because that open world is what makes TS3 better than TS4.

I tried TS4, and it just doesn't feel the same as TS3, even if it looks better I wouldn't take TS4 over TS3 any day.\t\t\t\t\t
