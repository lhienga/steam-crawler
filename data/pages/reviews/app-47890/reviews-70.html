\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to love The Sims 3. I first got The Sims 3 when DVD discs where still relevant, and my first expansion pack was Late Night and Supernatural. I have very fond memories of my sim (I think it was Lola) wooing Matthew Hamming (that rich 4/5 star celeb dude) and cheating on her bf, and in Supernatural's town I made a vampire sim bite most of the people in the town to make them into vampires. Vampire dominance for the win!

I also remember getting Island Paradise (which is very buggy on my pc now :( ) and having amazing houseboats and trying to make a good hotel, whilst also trying to make my male sim into a mermaid, and exploring the map as it was very cool to see all the cool little details on the map.

I then got all the other expansion packs, but other than World Adventures, Into the Future (I have my qualms with it, though) and Pets, they were either:

- Buggy (Island Paradise, and the Zombies from Supernaturals)

- Laggy (Island Paradise was SO laggy, i couldn't even play for longer than 20 minutes)

- Lacklustre (Diesel Stuff and some of the other packs, for the money they charged back at least 7 years ago is astonishing... even now. There wasn't very many items to make your homes unique, especially if you've played the game for so long that every item just looks... meh. Or you have to buy "SimPoints" from the ol' Sims website to access other item packs or other maps.)

- Prone to crashes, especially on maps like Isla Paradiso (from Island paradise expansion pack) and even Moonlight Falls (Supernatural)

It's a real shame because The Sims 3 was a staple in my gaming collection, as it was my childhood game. But coming back to this and seeing the state it is in, is depressing. EA has really milked it dry, and left it to rot.

If you have gotten down here and read through all that, thanks for reading. If you still do want to buy it, there are mods you can download to fix some of the issues in The Sims 3 and improve performance and adds in commands if your sim(s) are bugging out, though do be careful of Custom Content sites that aren't official or if they aren't from NexusMods (not sure if Steam Workshop actually works with the Sims? A bit strange, they're missing out there). Hope this helps!\t\t\t\t\t
