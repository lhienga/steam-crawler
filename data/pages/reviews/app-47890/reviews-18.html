The object of the game could be to satisfy the lifetime wish of your starting character but in a way the game has no goal except to enjoy yourself in a rich sandbox. It gets even more wacky and fun with the DLCs. Overall the game can be quite expensive with all the DLCs so I recommend wishlisting and waiting for a sale if you don't mind waiting.

Also, the saving and loading can be excruciatingly slow if you don't have a good SSD. The marketing is aggressive and EA will try to sell you things in game but you can turn off some of it so it isn't too annoying.

Despite those issues, it's an extremely fun game with tons to explore. I recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
