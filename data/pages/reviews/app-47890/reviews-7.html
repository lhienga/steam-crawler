\r\n\t\t\t\t\t The best Sims game.

IMO The Sims 3 just does everything that The Sims 2 did, but better. Creating your own Sim is so much better now that you don't have to worry about aspirations and random lifetime wants. Now you can just CHOOSE what your Sim's lifetime wish is. That change alone makes this game better for me. This is also the only Sims game that is entirely open world with no loading screens AND you're getting the most bang for your buck without any expansions. This is probably the most feature rich game in the series. The only major downside is that the game is very unoptimzed. But thankfully there's a really good guide on Steam that you can use to help optimize the game.

I have a lot of good memories playing the console version and now I get to experience that on PC (better late than never lol). Like the previous game before it, it does everything a sequel should do. If you ever wonder which Sims game to get now and days, I will always recommend this one.\t\t\t\t\t
