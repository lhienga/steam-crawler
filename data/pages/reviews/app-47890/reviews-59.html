The collection bundle is tempting (I know because it tempted me (steam summer sale)) but you will NEED to follow a guide to make it ***playable***. I knew this going into it, but I feel bad for anyone who spends 100+ bucks just to have the game not work. You can get it to work, but you need to do a good bit of modding.

I am only a novice at modding, but there are some very helpful guides from the community on steam. I spent about 3 hours following guides to help with the game's performance and I was able to make it functional - I believe that if I can do it you can do it. It will crash/freeze every now and then, but I could probably do more research and mod it more to fix it further. For now I am just saving the game... often.

Still a much better game than Sims 4. I'm having a lot of fun, even dealing with the glitches, and that's saying something.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the best of the Sims games because of Create a Style - for any clothing/furniture/thing, you can click the palette button, which lets you change the RGB values of the different parts of the items, select textures to apply, basically you can fully customise the appearance of everything in the game.

The big downside to this game is the very long loading times. Especially if you own all the DLCs.

Neighborhoods work differently in Sims 3. Your whole neighborhood is active at once, and you can send your Sim off to walk around the whole place. A saved game covers every household of this neighborhood. The downside compared with Sims 4 is you can't visit other neighborhoods (but you can move a household to a new neighborhood).

Sims 4 has a buy mode search function, which Sims 3 really suffers for not having.

Sims 4 has more customisation of the actual Sims than this game.

Build mode is pretty good. There are a lot of fun workarounds you can find on youtube (the barn roof trick is one of the better ones to know). I can't decide whether Sims 3 or 4 has a better build mode, they're both good in different ways.

Sim Autonomy - I like the Sim and pet behaviours of this game more than Sims 4. In Sims 4, guests visit your house and barge into your Sim's bedroom to play games on their PC for the whole party. Sims 3's autonomy is a lot better than Sims 4's.

If you like Pets, then Sims 3 lets you control your pets directly. In Sims 4, you had to tell your Sim to tell the pet to do something. Sims 4 - if you don't tell your sim to tell their pet dog to go to bed at night, the dog will stand there looking at its bed until it passes out from fatigue.

The Steam Store has a bundle with all the DLCs, which for some reason does not appear on the main Sims 3 page.
https://store.steampowered.com/bundle/1247/The_Sims_3_Collection/
The downside is having all the DLCs really bloats the buy mode. You cannot easily turn off any DLCs. If you untick them in Steam properties, the game will not launch.

Expansion DLCs:
1 World Adventures - vacation locations of France, China and Egypt.

2 Ambitions - Some great professions with fun minigames are added, such as Private Investigator, Architect, Ghost Hunter. Neigbhorhood - Twinbrook added, French Bayou theme.

3 Late Night - Vampires, Rock bands and Clubs. A Chicago/Seattle like neighborhood with high rise apartments.

4 Generations - Adds a whole lot more content for children. Buy this if you like raising families. Has no neighborhood, but has a lot of very nice prebuilt homes.

5 Pets - cats, dogs and horses! Neighborhood - Appaloosa Plains, a ranch town

6 Showtime - adds singing, magician, acrobat careers. Neighborhood is Starlight Shores, a Hollywood themed town. Starlight Shores has some nice retro buy-mode items and mid-century modernist architecture.

7 Supernatural - Adds fairies, witches, werewolves and zombies. Town - Moonlight Falls, Pacific Northwest / Twilight inspired.

8 Seasons - adds seasons. Also aliens.

9 University - adds a university campus neighborhood. Sims can't live in the neighborhood, it's more like a vacation location, they live there temporarily while they study. Upgrades smart phones and adds social groups (jocks, rebels and nerds).

10 Island Paradise - Isla Paradiso neighborhood, adds mermaids and lots of water-related activities.

11 Into the Future - Lets sims travel to the future neighborhood of Oasis Landing. Travelling back to the past will allow you to make changes that affect the future.

Most recommended expansions, depending on gameplay style: Ambitions, Late Night, Pets, Generations, Seasons, Supernatural.

The Stuff packs are all what they say on the tin. The Katy Perry Sweet Treats pack seems to no longer be available (basically tonnes of furniture that looked like cupcakes)

Mods: You need mods to solve a bunch of problems https://www.nraas.net/community/Mods-List
Error Trap, Master Controller, Overwatch and Story Progression are the most recommended.
