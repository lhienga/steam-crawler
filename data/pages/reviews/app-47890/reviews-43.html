\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn my humble opinion, it is the best Sims game on the market. The amount of content (when DLC are considered) is truly astonishing and can leave people who have spent years playing in awe of how many things there are to discover. There are many cases where people posted online about a certain feature, object, mechanic of the game that they've just discovered in spite of playing for so long.
But even without the DLC, this game is fairly content-filled as this is the only game with an open-world like gameplay which hits the jackpot. This adds so much to the atmosphere of a way a player perceives the town their characters live in. You get to see how it's changing, evolving, how families come and go, what legacy they leave behind (especially when you mod the game) and only the Sims 3 has ever done it.
Okay, indeed this implementation is not without its limits and problems. If you purchase many dlcs and launch the game, it is not optimized correctly and you pretty much need optimizing mods (which are thankfully easy to install and apply to the game) even if you own a very good PC this game WILL lag and cause you problems if you do not install mods.
If you can withstand this, you are in for a few thousand hours worth of content, the biggest potential in comparison to all Sims games (The sims 4 included) hands down. The graphics are okay, maybe not outstanding but good enough not to complain and they can be successfully enhanced by mods, too. Soundtrack is very sims like (who would have thought) but I'd argue it is the most fitting balance of a magical and optimistic vibe without sounding almost annoying like it sounds in the Sims 4.
And, what's imperative to mention, these were the times when EA still (somewhat) cared about their customers or at least showed them proper respect so the DLCs truly bring a lot of content that makes the game as the whole. It's not just a bunch of small add-ons posing to appear as something bigger than they really are.
If you are looking for a life simulator and you are thinking about the Sims 3- do not think twice, buying it (and managing its limitations and errors) will pay off.\t\t\t\t\t
