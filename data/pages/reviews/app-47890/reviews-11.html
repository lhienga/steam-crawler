Sims 3 is better than Sims 4.

Yes, I have tried playing Sims 4, while it does have a lot more fine tuning when it comes to the creation of Sims and Infrastructure, it's the absolute atrocity that is the UI and Menu's that kill it for me. I also spent about $150 (AUD) on Sims 4 DLC's only for none of them to load/work despite spending hours troubleshooting. Gave in and refunded the whole lot.

Sims 3 at least works for me - I like that I have a lot of control over the creation of my neighbourhood and the more simple UI and menu options. The game is outdated unfortunately, which means using the Online services is either very difficult or impossible, but there is enough there for me to have had plenty of fun... and getting jealous that my Sim/s are having much better lives than I am xD\t\t\t\t\t
