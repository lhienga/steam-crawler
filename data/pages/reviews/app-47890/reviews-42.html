\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhy this review is positive:

This has been my favorite game since I was 13. Sims 3 is the only open world game in the series, and the DLC gives you way more content for your money than Sims 4 does. (Still overpriced though lol) I have 2000+ hours and I'm not bored yet.

The two main issues with this game that you might want to consider before buying it:

1. It's buggy / unoptimized if you don't have the patience to install mods. The worst example of this is the lack of a framerate cap or vsync option, which causes it to run at 1000+ fps on some newer graphics cards if you don't get LazyDuchess's smooth patch mod. NRaas mods also help a lot with the bugs.

2. The character customization without mods is much less expansive than in The Sims 4.\t\t\t\t\t
