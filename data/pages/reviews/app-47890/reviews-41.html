\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love the game itself, but I question why the damn thing has to run my system at full speed constantly. I am normally a flight simulator (FSXSE), American Truck simulator player, both of which are graphically intense game. Neither of these run my computer half as hard as the Sims 3 does. I don't know maybe its poorly written programming or a genuine desire to blow out the cooling fans in your system as quickly as possible. It has nothing to do with a weak graphics card because it does the same thing on my old POS that has only a shared graphics card 512mb.
I really hate the over pricing done on the game and its add-ons, especially as old as the game actually is. I guess that's because the newer version, Sims 4, suks the big donkey so hard they have to make it a free to play game.
Would I recommend buying the game? Well it's better than Sims 4 except for all the things above, also it tends to be a bit glitchy from time to time as the game progresses and you start having failing saves and problems with the maps when building.\t\t\t\t\t
