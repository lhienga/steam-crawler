Since steam denied my refund, I decided to go back and try to figure out how to actually get the game to work consistently (might as well get my money's worth). Hours of Youtube and Sims 3 forums later.... I actually really love it!!!! I've managed to find some mods that really enhance my experience, and the gameplay is everything I remember it being now that I can actually enjoy the game. For anyone considering buying the sims 3.... Make sure to get the Lazy Duchess patch, the Nraas Overwatch & Master Controller mods, and if you can't get the game to launch, set the affinity to 1 CPU in task manager by right clicking the process and then selecting "go to details", right click on the detail and "set affinity". Select CPU 1. This will launch the game. Then when the game is visible in task manager, return to "set affinity" and select All CPU's. Your game will run like a dream. Happy simming!
