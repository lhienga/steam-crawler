\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has a really neat concept, and I really wanted to like it. I spent probably 30 minutes just trying to master the 3 basic moves, and while my consistency did ultimately go up, I did feel like my success was partially due to a dice roll. I did feel like this is partially due to the fist cleansing and bringing hands towards my core being very unintuitive; it felt like the game wanted me to bring my hands inside my body, so I ended up sticking my ass out just to achieve any success. After barely defeating the bot, I entered a game with my friend for a friendly duel. We ended up naruto signing each other for 10 minutes, while occasionally throwing rock discs. I will say, there was a lot of joy in us both frustratingly trying to kill each other with little to no success. Ultimately, the stances I think are a little to unforgiving and prevent you from rapidly shooting moves like you want to. Maybe if the game is trying to be competitive, the strictness is necessary, but I just wanted to throw rocks. A minor thing, but i find moving around, especially sprinting is quite nauseating, although this may be a specific issue I have with VR and not necessarily the games fault.\t\t\t\t\t
