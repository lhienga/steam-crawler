\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you, like me, think this is cool conceptually, but the videos on the store page looked kinda boring, I ask that you trust me, and buy this game. It completely delivers on it's concept of earthbending in a VR fighting game.

More than that, it is hands down the best fighting game I have ever played. It solves every single problem I have with normal fighting games.
The controls are intuitive and simple in a way that encourages creativity and expression. Instead of controls that are intentionally more difficult than they need to be like in a traditional fighting game.
There are no grapples, stuns, or combos that leave you unable to play the game while your opponent passes a skill check, every combo in this game is executed before the actual attack, as fundamentally the combos in this game are sending rocks at your opponent at different speeds and angles.
And lastly the knowledge floor is super low, there aren't 4 dozen character movesets you need to learn how to use or counter, hell there aren't even 2, everyone has the same moves, and understanding each of them doesn't take that much time.
Now, if what I've described seems very simple, I assure you it's not. the fundamentals are simple, but you can build on them to perform incredibly complex, difficult, and flashy combos, and the growing community is actively creating new ways to fight. Not to mention the complexity even at an early stage of counter-play in this game, and how the debris from you and your opponent's moves changes the arena throughout a match.
And if you're still not convinced to get this game after all of that go watch Shoeless's videos on this game on youtube.\t\t\t\t\t
