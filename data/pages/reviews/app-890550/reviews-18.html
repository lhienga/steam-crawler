\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a bit tough to get over the beginner's hurdles, but once you get somewhere it's honestly addictive. The gameplay is perfectly suited to VR and is fantastically executed.

The physics-based combos the community has discovered over time has made the skill ceiling absurdly high, figuring them out feels more like learning a martial art than playing a video game, but you really feel like an insane badass when you hit them and destroy your opponent.

My only criticisms are the peer-to-peer matchmaking, which can be very unreliable on high ping, and a lack of any cosmetic customisation, but since the game is still in early access I think the second one can still be forgiven, the devs seem to be working on it as well.

There are lots of resources in the discord with beginner's tips and intermediate to advanced combo/skill progression, and with lots of new players joining right now thanks to a YouTube vid about the game being picked up by the algorithm (shoutout shoeless_man), it's a great time to jump in since you have a better chance of playing with evenly-matched players .
