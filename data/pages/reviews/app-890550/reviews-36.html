\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst impressions after my first session:
This game has some really good potential to establish VR fighting games as a genre.

It is pretty precise with the movements you have to do in order perform your moves and will require a good bit of muscle memory just like any traditional fighting game.
although a bit frustrating at first i can already tell how much creativity this allows once you get comfortable with it! Buuut somehow Sprinting is harder than anything else.

It also makes me aware of how out of shape i am by making me sweat in the first 5min of gameplay!

The art style is nothing special sadly, i know it's because the quest2 is supposed to be able to run it but I feel like every VR game just has the same low poly cell shaded style and it's getting pretty old by now. The overall art direction is really good but it's not quite enough for it to form a strong Identity and stand out in the sea of low poly VR games.

I do think it's worth the money and deserves more recognition.
8/10 will try to bully friends into getting it

Edit:
I am now 10h in and in love with this game!
If you like fighting games, Get. This. Game.
The combos get crazy and are so satisfiyng to pull off\t\t\t\t\t
