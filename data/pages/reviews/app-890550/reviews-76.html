\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am changing my opinion and giving this game a thumbs up now. This last patch apparently did solve some of the inconsistencies and the game is performing better. Overall it's a great idea with a great execution from a dedicated team. It still needs a little bit of work to smooth the gameplay but having in mind the fact that it is still an Early Access title I really can't complain now. What I can do though is give some thoughts and suggestions.

- The starting stance placement of many movements seems to differ a lot in regards of forward or backward position making it a bit hard sometimes to know where you are messing up the movements, especially because the training machine kind of teaches some of them wrong.

- Running is still very clunky and inconsistent for many people, myself included. I understand the idea of making running into a sign but it's over complicating something easy, plus I don't normally run the way the game wants me to and having to guess the angle every time is a chore for something basic like running. I would either change it for a normal running position with big forgiving angles or just link it to a button in the controller.

- The body still seems to be anchored by the head and everything else, especially the waist and legs seems to rubber band a lot.
