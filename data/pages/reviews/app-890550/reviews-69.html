\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright I'm changing my review here post session. My headset can in fact handle it, I'm just not doing it right, after a few youtube videos of what the motions look like and a rather helpful comment on this review I finally managed to pull off pillar and disk somewhat intentionally. Now I just have to get to the point that I can do it without thinking and I'll be a fully licensed VR Earthbender TM. Contrary to what a lot of the other reviews are saying the poses are not overly hard to pull off you just have to overwrite muscle memory and not think you're some master of the art because I have found a lot of the times the moves fail because in a haste to do them I've not put my wrist at the proper angle for the pose and am usually a quarter or eighth turn off from the right orientation.

Practice does in fact make perfect even if you're left madly flailing about in your room for twenty minutes before it finally goes through but if I did have one request for the devs, instead of hand poses for the tutorial could you model a dummy npc to do the moves so players could see the fluidity you're aiming at for the moves to activate?. Just seeing green incorporeal hands pull back into my stomach threw me off a lot. If you could find a different demonstration method that could be seen from outside of the tiny little circle it wants you to pose in it may help new players with getting an idea of what their flailing is supposed to look like so they don't have to youtube that stuff. (Thank you Rumble Bee on youtube for actually showing such things!)

Overall incredible game play, I'm still being put in the dirt like Aang back-sassing Toph for the first time but I have learned distance and the first move you learn are very good counters as opposed to doing heavyset matrix bullet dodges that get me hit anyway and usually lead to leaning back into a wall at mach fat. Still going on the Iroh body building plan, still going to be practicing the poses into muscle memory and still waiting for my chance to metalbend a magnet onto Howard's cpu but thus far this is an awesome game... Once you know what you're doing.

Give this game an actual try and if at first you don't succeed, youtube to find out what you're doing wrong or watch a twitch stream and ask questions. And again folks, Early access means just that. You're currently on the base model of this game while it is pretty new. Be nice so we don't scare off indy devs from publishing something that when polished could turn out absolutely amazing.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing VR Game, I'm only LOR (I think its' called, it's the one with the blue belt around the tree) right now and I can't wait to unlock Mountain for full combos. The game is insanely fun when going against other people.

Of course the hand movements can be difficult and sometimes it can feel like it's not working when you're in a heated situation but it is just me being bad (After I watched Caradoc Lyigh I realised). People who are obviously higher ranked most of the time go easier on you whats nice, either by limiting themselves to your moves or just going slower

The game hasn't got MIC what has it's Pros and Cons
Pros, it doesn't turn into Gorilla tag
Cons, when trying to teach someone or when you want to get taught it's hard to communicate through hand signals
It'd be cool to talk to some of the people in game seeings it's only 1 on 1

Things that I wished were in the game;
Animated Tutorials for moves. I've having such a pain with the boulder and I can't reference myself against anyone offline
2v2: it'd give flick more use :D
Ghost hand for moves in the online public training area
Official Tournaments, I don't even think contenders would expect money, maybe just a separate ranking board for people to see.
More Maps (Maps where parts of the floor fall out, Maps where not all of the floor is usable ground


Things that I don't want to see
Too many moves, I don't want the game to get too many move to where the skill bar seems unreachable and unfun to play.
Overly expensive/Exclusive cosmetic packs (I think it's obvious that you're going to add some form of cosmetic to keep the game running but if it gets to "A Township Tales" levels of cost and exclusivity then it puts me off)

You've made a really fun game, I've been on and off the website download version of the game for time just messing about and I hope the playerbase rises and more people get VR, also are you planning to get into PSVR?\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to start of by saying it can be good

I have a few dislikes about the game

Tutorial
First of all, the tutorial. I found it to work fine enough at the the place with floating help hands but when i tried to use it in action, I failed miserably. I understand how this can be my fault but i want atleast a menu to bring with you incase you forget and don't have to run all the way back to the "tutorial place".

Howard
I found Howard the tutorial bot to be quite fine, but the arena was not helpful at all. First, if you "die" you just regain your health and then you continue. Fine in concept except the arena doesn't reset at all. If the arena gets too clustered it can get confusing to "run" around and can limit your movement.

Menu
The menu's are fine by themselves. They're fine but maybe a bit slow, i can get that for something you don't change a lot like movement, turning, etc. But when the menu's are still a bit slow in the tutorial, that's when it can get annoying. My suggestion is that you keep the big board menus but add a button for a pop up menu, it's not like you use the buttons for anything except measuring your height.

Movement
I would actually be completely fine with mostly all of these if the movement wasn't so janky. Walking around is just fine but super slow compared to the environment. This is were they implemented running. Running would probably be fine if it was a bit more responsive and easier.

There's still pros to this game

Environment
It looks pretty nice for it's price and fits the game.

Performance
It runs amazingly well even with tons of blocks spawned in. I have a relatively low end VR machine, [GTX 1070, 16GB RAM, i5-8600k, [strike] Meta[/strike] Oculus Quest 2 with Link cable] and found no lag.

All in all i want to recommend this but it just needs a few more updates to be pretty good for everyone

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDiscord: Alex Poland#3107
I wish Steam had a middle review button but I think this is sadly gonna be a downvote for me.

TL;DR: You have to play games with higher ranked players to have more than three moves, and the player base is too small to find people who are around your rank

The game is FUN. The community is great. But this has two fundamental problems that really have a bad, ugly synergy.

#1: You start with only THREE moves and have to work for the rest of them.

Why? I can understand working for "alternate" versions of each move, like a version of the move that is weaker and stronger in a different way. But it doesn't work like this. You literally are locked out of a bunch of moves for beginning the game. This makes it so when you go into a higher rank match with another player, they will not only be more skilled at the game than you, but you LITERALLY have less tools to fight them with simply because they play the game more.

"Bro, just play with people your rank!"

This is where the terrible synergy awakens and makes this problem worse.

#2: This game's playerbase is small. Very small.

As it stands right now, I tried playing yesterday at about 5-6:00 PM EST and I saw only 8 other people on the East Coast. About 8 on the west coast as well, and it dipped to about TWO other people at one point.

This is extremely small. And I wouldn't mind that honestly, as I could simply go looking for people to play with manually, but BECAUSE YOU ARE LOCKED OUT OF THE OTHER MOVES you have two bad choices:

-Play with people who are higher rank and get steamrolled to grind moves
-Wait a LONG time, possibly never, for someone who is your rank for a fair fight

I love this game. It's got great combat, very unique PVP, that I would love to enjoy.
But no other game makes me grind for things. Pavlov doesn't make you work for weapons, B&S doesn't make you work for weapons & spells, that's just bad execution.

Unlock all of the moves from the gate, and let people work for alternate versions of those moves, or skins, or whatever.\t\t\t\t\t
