\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl;dr: This game is uniquely hard and very rewarding, join both Discords also.
I've only played this game for 17 hours (and plan to play for much more) and I think my opinion has developed enough to leave a review. This game, is difficult in a way that is hard to put into words. There's no baseline for difficulty, as you play against people of varying skills, but in your first few matches, you will flail like a decapitated chicken. Then you rank up and unlock more skills, and can now flail like a decapitated chicken slightly differently. Even now, I mess up the simplest moves. When you get this game, you need to put in the effort to have fun. As many others have said, you can't get around it. There's no trickity trick or different method, you just need to face it on like a rock and break through. This game is incredible. If you get it and put in the effort, nothing feels better. Also, join the discord. It's a great environment and there are great tips on moves. Especially the unofficial discord, Rumble Kai. It has an incredible spreadsheet with lots of good tips and moves. Anyways, to review, this game is difficult but fun, even if you flounder at first (and you will flounder at first) push through and you can do some of the most satisfying things I have ever done in VR. Buy this game, put in the effort, and ROCK ON!\t\t\t\t\t
