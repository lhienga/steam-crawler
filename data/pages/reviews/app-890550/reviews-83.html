I wanted to talk about some of the other reviews that i saw for this game

there was mention that people dont like the system where you have to progress in rank in order to learn new moves, but after playing, i think it was a great idea. having all the moves at the start would to way to overwhelming for beginners to learn. instead with this system, players can master the basics before moving on with the more advanced. also it is not hard to level up and only took me less than a day to get to the top level

once again i recommend this game to anyone with a vr headset, it is very satisfying once you learn the moves and can execute them in a fight to utterly destroy your opponent.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery addicting gameplay, one of my new favorite vr games. One thing that many people are complaining about is the fact that new abilities are locked behind rankings, which is true. However, I've yet to lose rank in a match, even if I lost horribly.

I don't see it as a limit on your abilities, it's more like a progression system. When you play, you'll be matched with people above your rank, below your rank, and at the rank you're at. It's a good way to see what abilities come next, while also showing others what they have yet to unlock.

This doesn't mean you'll always beat people beneath your level either. At the beginning of it, you have three main moves: Disk, Spawn Pillar, and Straight. I've lost to people spamming Disk because that's a decent ranged move, easy to spam, and It's not one I use too much as I prefer different attacks.

Two things I would like to see updated however are how precise you need to hold your hands to perform a move and the ability to run. Running is possible but you just have to pump your arms like crazy. I wish it were a bit more like Blade and Sorcery in that aspect, just more fluid. And as for the moves it can be a bit of a hassle to pull up a boulder or throw a disk, it can result in weird (albeit pretty funny) moments where both people are just waving their arms around. 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
