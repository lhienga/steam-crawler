\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is an excellent implementation of earthbending in VR. It focuses on the poses you make to perform "spells" - essentially make stones appear in front of you and/or throw them around.

I recommend this for the type of player that likes to master a game's mechanics. You will feel very frustrated when trying to do some moves and even more frustrated when trying to combo moves onto each other - it's all part of the experience.

As for downsides... Well, the game could be improved in readability. For example, knowing when and why you failed specific spells would be great, maybe a "shadow" of the closest pose to the one you are making, sort of like the game has on the training zone.

The community experience I had was great too. Although the game has no voice chat (at the time of this review), sometimes me or and adversary stopped the match to help each other figure out why some moves were not working. I even spent 10/15 minutes being teached a relatively advanced combo before we proceeded to stone each other. It is a good showcase of the social affordances of VR and of the Teacher/Student relationships this game can create.

In the end, I just want to say: Go into this game as you would go into a martial art. Be prepared to fail a lot at the right moves, but when you start getting the hang of it it starts feeling great.\t\t\t\t\t
