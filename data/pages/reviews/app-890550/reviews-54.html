\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright so I have played for 12 hours or so. I have topped out on experience and learned how to do all of the moves. As far as progression in game mechanics, I'm maxed out, this only took about 8 or 9 hours of dueling. This might sound like a dig, but its not. It's perfect.

You start off with 3 moves, and you have to fight with only those 3 until you level up and unlock a couple more, and then twice more where you will be titled a "Mountain", having all of the earth-bending skills at your disposal. The amount of time it takes to level up gives you enough experience with the new moves that you actually get them burned into your head before you learn new ones. This is the ONLY reason you will stand a chance in a fight, as your form, which seems to be based around Karate, needs to actually remain upright, eyes forward and constantly returning to center to initiate another earthbending.

The heat of battle constantly challenges my skills and shows me there will be almost countless hours of training and fun that also helps work out your arms! The community seems to be pretty friendly too, for a fighting game! There are even mini games you can play with your friends in a private lobby. Loving this game, might be my new favorite.
