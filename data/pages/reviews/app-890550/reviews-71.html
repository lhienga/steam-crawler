my big tips are: Pay VERY close attention to rotation of hands and try and instead of thinking about it as two poses, get a muscle memory of arm motion. for example, with summoning a rock i act like im pulling back a bow string and it works for me.
second tip: distance is a very risk reward thing in this game, the closer you are the more damage you can deal, but its also very easy to take large damage up close. far away you deal less damage but you also will take less damage. so you want to find a sweet spot for your playstyle.
third tip: always fist bump before and after matches, and dont forget to thumbs up your opponents lose or win.

thats about everything i can fit, hope it helps\t\t\t\t\t
