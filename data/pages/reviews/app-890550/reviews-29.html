\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally fun, but there are some things that could be better for the $20 price.

Firstly, some people have said that if you can't do the moves it's strictly your fault. Yeah, you can get the hang of them but I think it could be slightly less strict about form (admittedly that goes against the earth-bending theme).

Secondly, I agree with the others saying that the community just isn't big enough right now. What's the point of a PvP game if there're only 30 people in game during peak hours? A solution may actually be just reducing the price, as I remember that being an obstacle for me buying this game.

Make these changes and continue to work on the game, and I think this is a really good VR title, possibly one of the first great multiplayer ones.\t\t\t\t\t
