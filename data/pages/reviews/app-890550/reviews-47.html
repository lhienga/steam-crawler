\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery cool game so far in my limited playtime. As some of the other reviews have said, the controls are a bit hard to get used to, but in my opinion, that's what makes it rewarding. I like that I have to train towards becoming a master earthbender, just as you do when training in real life martial arts. The only issue is that it is definitely unresponsive at times because you have to be extremely precise when doing the moves. At the same time, this can be a good thing when practising perfect form.

Now, in terms of multiplayer, I haven't had any luck in pairing with other people, as there doesn't seem to be much of a player base ( at least when I played it ). You have to play with other players in order to learn new moves which hinders your ability to progress, especially when the player base is still low. There should be an option to go up against computers instead to level up. This will also increase longevity in the game as you are not solely relying on other players keeping this game alive.

If that is fixed, and more varied gameplay elements are implemented, this game has the potential to becoming a top VR game. I look forward to progressing further and seeing the devs build it up into something great.\t\t\t\t\t

Mainly it is progression because you can only get more moves by ranking up, but it is hard to find a match in low ranks. This means you will be limited to a certain amount of moves compared to the other player. Literally gimping new players. Next, once you unlock big wall, it gets spammed non stop. For new players, we want to do the full motion but later you find out you can cheese or shorten it by half motioning, which for me kills the whole martial art/immersive gesture movements.
I recommend playing it and finding out yourself if the above does not bother you but for me I believe gimping already new players in a system that requires a lot of learning is too much.

tl;dr: You start with 3 moves.Moves are locked behind progression, you will fight players who have more moves than you. Prepare to lose a lot and get frustrated with gesture system. Lots of fun though!\t\t\t\t\t
