\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably the best competitive VR game I've played to date. The combat and move system feels well thought out and can lead to some insanely intense 1v1's in the arena. I've seen some people complaining about how hard it is for new players to level up playing competitive because they only start with limited moves, but you still level up even if you lose a match so don't stress! As an Australian, I haven't faced very long matchmaking waits either, 5 minutes has been the absolute max.

The community has also been one of the best parts of the game so far. There's a real sense of comradery as you and your opponent bump fists at the start of the game and inbetween rounds - as well as giving eachother a thumbs up when someone executes a particularly deadly combo or wins a round. They're also insanely forgiving to new players like me - willing to stop the fight mid-round to help you correct your form so you can execute your moves and they carry on fighting. At one point I even had to call a time out (using hand gestures due to lack of voice chat - a positive) to plug in my headset and my opponent gladly waited until I returned to continue whaling on me.

Fair warning though, the game will literally have you sweating out of your headset. Especially if you live in a warmer climate like me so you may want to take breaks in-between matches! It's an amazing workout though and I've seen some liken it to a form of martial arts which seems like a reasonable comparison.

Overall, the game feels so polished already and has an incredibly unique premise and combat system. I'm super excited to continue playing as it's developed further! Maybe one day I'll become a Mountain!\t\t\t\t\t
