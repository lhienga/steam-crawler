Edit: I got the hang of this game. You just need to twist your wrists. The running is still weird, but it's not hard to do moves. Just twist your wrists. The odds are, if you're not pumping rocks out like a 12 year old blind earthbender, you're not twisting your wrists. 12/29/22
Let me rant real quick.
This game is far from perfect. As a matter of fact, it's far from good. Far from enjoyable. I started this game and wondered why I was moving so slow. I did the running tutorial, tried running, and my Fitbit called the police because it thought I was having a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ seizure. Needless to say, I walked for the rest of my gameplay. I wanted to play with my friend. So, I did the tutorials. I was shooting walls and flinging disks like I'd been doing it for my whole life. When I tried to fight Howard, that piece of ♥♥♥♥, I had made about 3 moves total of about 11 attempts. I guess I did need to practice this game for my whole life, because if my movements are not perfect down to every last minute detail, I end up looking like a toddler trying to box my own shadow. Sorry I can't master this game based off of it's ♥♥♥♥♥♥ vague tutorials and my inability replicate an action down to the molecular level. Another thing; why? Why not incorporate buttons into the game? I don't buy a VR to only use the joysticks. That's like buying a car to sit in my driveway turning the steering wheel and pretending I'm driving.

Sorry. Real review now.
This game is very infuriating. The hand movements are incredibly hard to replicate in the heat of a battle. I can't seem to exactly figure any of them out. I kind of just try it a few times and sometimes it would work. The only thing I can seem to do is punch, and even then I'm only punching the pillars that Howard makes. And when I punch them back, they don't do damage half of the time. The running, too. I'm doing these weird spasms of running and walking, it was a little jarring, and super annoying. Needless to say, you should only get this game if you are a 10th degree Red Belt in Jiu Jitsu. In my opinion, it's not worth all the time and effort. Just go watch Avatar.\t\t\t\t\t
