\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally fun game! It probably is the most fun and unique PvP game I've played on VR yet. Instead of mashing buttons to use your moves, you have to use combo's of hand movements. This makes pulling of your moves feel much more intense and rewarding.

You unlock new moves by playing PvP matches against other players. Even if you lose you still get experience points. In the early stages the winner gets mostly decided by who can cast their moves the best. The later stages is when these battles turn into fights straight out of an action movie. Things like doing 7 precise hand movements in rapid succession to throw huge walls at your opponent like meteors becomes the usual.

Don't let the difficulty in pulling of your moves at the beginning scare you. Part of the fun is that you really need to practise and memorise the hand movements for each move. Some tips for beginners are to stand straight and look forward. Most people I've fought against are able to consistently cast most if not all the spells when they've reached 8+ hours (and oh boy does this time fly by.)

The community is really kind too. When more experienced players match up against new players, the match almost always turns into a teaching session. Which can be very funny to see because there is no voice chat yet. And if you have any other questions, people on the discord are happy to help.

It makes you feel like the Earthbender you've always wanted to be after watching Avatar, so give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
