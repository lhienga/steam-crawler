\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a refreshing and fantastic vr experience. I highly recommend this game at its asking price. I am going to be breaking down my experience as a new user, along with what i think it does good and what it does bad.
New Experience
To start the first thing the game does is drop you in front of the matchmaking area which is locked until you preform 3 moves and beat the ai. The game doesn't guide you to the tutorial area but instead allows you to explore and try to find your way around. The tutorial can be a bit frustrating especially if this is your first vr combat game based on gestures and motion. The key to preforming moves is very specific hand placements and rotation. The game does a decent job of showing you how to do this but it can still be confusing. After getting the first 3 moves once and beating the ai you can match make and get into your first game.
The Good
The graphics have this very nice warm feeling and the combat system is well thought out. The combat flows very easily once you start to master it. Combos are intuitive and feel really solid. Hits feel deserved and getting pummeled by some one feels like you lost due to skill, not ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ game mechanics. The park allows for a lot of creative freedom in your match style and playing with friends is really fun. Knock-back on your Straight gives a real sense of weight to your moves and i haven't played a game that feels this good in a while. The community is probably the best thing in this game. Everyone seems genuinely happy to have someone to play with and are all good sports. I got stomped by a player and they spent the next few matches showing me how to do some combos that i was struggling with.
The Bad
Personally the biggest issue for me is the lack of players. Matching can take up to 30 minutes and sometimes you can't get a match at all. the north America server is the most populated which requires people from other countries to play on that server. This leads to some very noticeable input lag. The lag isn't game ending, for me, i notice that some moves come out a little delayed. You start with just 3 moves available. while this in of itself is fine, you can't unlock more moves without doing the matchmaking(lack of players again). This tends to lead to the first rank of matches quickly becoming stale and repetitive. Especially if you match against players of higher level who have a lot more options than you do.\t\t\t\t\t
