\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest VR PvP game I've played so far.
At first it was a bit unsettling that you have to unlock abilities, but after starting small and working up to unlocking many more I changed my mind -- it helped me to learn to use them without being overwhelmed.
All of the abilities you can use (kicking up rocks, walls, sliding, etc.) are action-based irl and feels very fluid: like an actual Earth-bender.
I also like that there aren't restrictions (no cooldowns or limits on how many e.g. walls you can put it [that i've found]) making combat feel more skill-based.
Additionally, it's a great workout. Leaves me sweating and it's fun.

Improvements I'd like to see would be in some UI elements (like changing settings not being on an in-game tablet) and avatar customization. Would also be cool if I could import my character model to LIV for streaming. I also think other elements (like water) would be cool, but I get the developer's point in that it would be a lot of work and would make it harder to polish or balance.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tArguably one of the best games ever made, in the history of PC and VR gaming. No joke, no lie. The use of body movement to use moves in this game in a more sophisticated way than sword / gun / fist-fighting is one of the largest steps in VR gaming history since BONEWORKS and BONELAB. The game is difficult, but not so much it's infuriating which would put you off, and yes, I know it's tiring, but it's very fun. Multiplayer is executed very well, with easy-to-understand ranking systems and ring mechanics (fall off the edges, you lose a life), and the community for the moment is very chill and if you are ranked with higher levels (black belts etc.) such as myself, often they will help you rather than just win the game and continue. I haven't played in a short while so it may have changed, but for the most part this game is absolutely brilliant. Combat is smooth, sometimes there is tracking errors or moves come out mistakenly, but that's to be expected from a game with movement-based attacks, there are many features, the progression system is newbie-friendly, the community is non-toxic and kind, the devs are awesome, overall, great game. 100% recommend, worth buying if you can fork up the cash. I highly, HIGHLY recommend this, more than any other VR game.\t\t\t\t\t
