\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRUMBLE is EASILY the most fun and unique PvP game I've ever played in VR. If you're looking for that anime fighting game fix in VR, look no further.

I nearly refunded the game within the first hour, because the stances are pretty hard to begin with, but after 17 hours (over a week or so) I've now reached the top of the daily leaderboards.

At the moment it's a bit light on content, but there will eventually be more modes (possibly 2v2), more arenas and even character customisation.

Overall I highly recommend anyone with a VR headset to get this game - there's a lot to learn, a friendly playerbase with many willing to teach and there's no better time than right now to hop in ;)\t\t\t\t\t
