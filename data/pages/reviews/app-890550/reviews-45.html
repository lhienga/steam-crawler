\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't often leave reviews here (in fact I believe this is my first), but I felt I needed to because this is quite possibly the best VR game I've played all year. As it stands, the game is immensely fun -- who doesn't want to be an earthbender?? -- but the potential for growth is also pretty incredible. The gestures to perform the moves are precise, but consistent, which gives the game a strong sense of skill and makes pulling off combos and complex maneuvers very satisfying.

There are a couple things that could use improvements; primarily, I feel like the structures you create should only be able to take a certain number of hits (or perhaps have a pool of hitpoints specific to each structure) before they're destroyed. As it is, no matter how many discs you shoot at a stone pillar, it would never break; you'd need to use a boulder or another pillar, or some other larger structure. It would also be really cool to have 2v2 and 3v3 matches, though that could get complicated with all the structures getting created and destroyed at once.

In total, a fantastic game; would 100% recommend! I'm excited to see where this game goes!\t\t\t\t\t
