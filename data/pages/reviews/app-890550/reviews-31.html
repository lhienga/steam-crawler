\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRUMBLE is by far my top game of the year. Contrary to what some of the negative reviews have said, this game is extremely polished for the state it's in. The move system is strict, yet forgiving in a perfect balance, making the dopamine rush for getting a certain combo down that you've been trying to stick all the more rewarding. People that say the move system is "glitchy" or "unresponsive" simply didn't put in the care or effort to truly learn how to perform them. If something doesn't activate, 99% of the time, it is the bender's fault. The art style is simplistic, yet beautiful in all areas, from Arena Design, to the props and trees scattered around, or even to the small rocks that may sometimes fall into your path seemingly randomly. The devs also are more communicative with the community than I've seen most do, along with having pretty fast updates (for a small team). The player base may be small as is, but hopefully as more people become aware of it, and it gets more attention, it has potential to grow extremely quickly. The skill ceiling is endless, only being limited by your own physical stamina and speed. The community is easily the best I've seen. Nearly anyone you meet is more than happy to help in any way, shape, and/or form. Finding matches can be a slight pain due to current player counts, but the official discord helps by letting people "Ping To Fight", letting people easily connect even if it seems like no-one is on. I heavily recommend RUMBLE to anyone who is willing to learn, and has the patience to truly become an EarthBender.\t\t\t\t\t
