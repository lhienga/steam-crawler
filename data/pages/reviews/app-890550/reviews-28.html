\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who is 700 hrs in at the time of this review, I can say for certain that I love this game.
I love throwing big rocks at my enemies and every hit feels impactful.
Learning and improving from difficult fights and getting closer to beating my opponents the next time.
Pushing myself to move faster and deliberately to out pace them.
Getting sore and coming back stronger.
My fellow black belts all helping each other improve and learn new combos and tricks.
I could go on.

There are downsides too.
The low player base. As this is PCVR only for now. PSVR may be on its way sometime. And the Devs do want to port this to standalone VR in the future.
The tutorial is confusing for those who do not know much about this game. That too is being worked on. There are also community made videos on how to do moves and such. Highly recommend watching them.
The peer to peer connection means there is host advantage. This is only an issue over greater distances and people with poor internet speeds though.
I also have a couple minor gripes about the Pit map.

Despite all the negative, I still come back for more. I know it's still being worked on and I can't wait for new features like character customization, new moves, and special modifiers to tweak your play style.

If you are ready to rock, we are ready to RUMBLE. Don't forget to stop by the Buckethead Discord if you need any help or just want to partake in the community.\t\t\t\t\t
