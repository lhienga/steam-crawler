\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing this game for 180 hours during the last two weeks, I have finally decided to leave a review.
First - if you are unsure, then just do it. It is a very fun game. It seems a lot of people compare this to Theme Hospital which I used to play as a teen. At first I thought it was a blatant rip off until I learned the original developers were involved with this. And it shows. The same exact quirkiness, humour and game play style is apparent, if not even more honed. I love that the same elements of the original game were kept, while adding many more new concepts and ideas. I also love the fact that alot of tings that just didn't function correctly in the original are now working as they should. I had to relearn some strategy as I assumed things worked as they had long ago, and I had to learn the hard way that these things function now. There are still issues of course. Nothing I feel is worth mentioning. I love this game. If I had to say anything negative, I would point out that the option to link amazon/hospital pass advertised on the main screen is outdated, the interface is a bit clunky - especially adding items and scrolling for ages, there seems to be a delay/freeze when opening menus and after month ends, and some things just are not well explained when you first encounter them. Besides these things, I love everything else.\t\t\t\t\t
