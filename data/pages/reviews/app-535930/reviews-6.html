\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my second time playing this game. the first time was through Microsoft and I logged over 60 hours. When I lost access to that version, I bought it on steam because I enjoy it so much.
Two Point Hospital is a hospital simulator game that adds just enough silliness to keep things interesting. The illnesses are all fictional so there is no worry of triggering content there. And even if you fail to cure a patient, they just turn into ghost and (rightfully) terrorize your staff until a janitor sucks it up in a vacuum cleaner. Each new hospital has three levels of challenges to complete and there are plenty of levels to play. Don't worry about being overwhelmed early on, as the different diseases are introduced slowly so you have time to collect money to build your treatment rooms.
There are additional game play modes with Co-op challenges to get special items, DLC locations, and an impressive Sandbox mode.
If you enjoy other Two Point games, you'll like this one as well.\t\t\t\t\t
