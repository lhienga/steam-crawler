Its a game. Its fine. There are some nice touches to the overgame gameplay as it goes on (the epidemics are a nice active mini-game alongside the monobeast hunting). But its basically a nostalgia grab for fans of "Theme Hospital". It will certainly appeal to a certain audience, and I hope they enjoy it. I wasn't one of them. For me it was a casual time-waster that felt it lacked skill in favour of grinding paitence.

Play if you like. It wasn't bad. But its equally fine if you feel meh from the experience.\t\t\t\t\t
