The Game
Hospital simulator where you build and manage hospitals while completing different objectives and challenging scenarios.

The Achievements
Be aware that there are several long and grindy achievements, which also require multiple DLC for completion. There are some guides and tips available but as you progress throughout the game you learn certain mechanics and strategies that will get you through most, if not all, of the hospitals. Estimated completion time depend somewhat on skill but you can expect well over 100-150+ hours.

Recommendation
At its core, the game is pretty solid with good management, mechanics and scenarios. The problem lies within its length where you don't get much variation with every hospital and DLC. Due to the completion time required I wish I could give this game a neutral recommendation, but as it stands I will give it a positive recommendation.
