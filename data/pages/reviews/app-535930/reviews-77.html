First, the default prioritization of patients is a bit dumb. It's just first-come-first-served, so I frequently have to move patients around queues to treat the most unhealthy patients first, and this can be tedious. Second, the game is a bit slow-paced. I often wish I could run it at 5x speed or something because it'll probably take like 100 hours to get three stars on all of the levels. There are a lot of levels, and they aren't all that different from each other, so the game feels rather samey long before it's over. (Although each level has a quirk, the basic gameplay is the same for all of them.)

Overall, it's fun but I imagine trying to finish it would be tedious.\t\t\t\t\t
