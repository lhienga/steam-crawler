\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTwo Point Hospital is a fantastic medical simulation game, but I have to admit, I've had a bit of a rough start. You see, I'm not the most coordinated person, and I seem to have a knack for accidentally killing patients in the game. I've lost count of how many people I've "terminated" at this point, but I'm pretty sure it's over 300. Oops.

But even with my less-than-stellar track record, I still love this game. The gameplay is challenging and rewarding, and I love the feeling of curing patients and making the hospital run smoothly. The attention to detail in this game is also top-notch, with diseases and treatments based on real-world medical conditions.

And let's not forget the puns! There are so many medical puns and jokes throughout the game that it's hard not to laugh. I mean, who wouldn't crack a smile at a patient with a "cactus-itus" or a "pandemic flu"?

Overall, I would highly recommend Two Point Hospital to any simulation game fans out there. Just be careful not to kill too many patients like I did. Trust me, it's not a good feeling. But hey, at least I have a "mortality rate" for the record books.\t\t\t\t\t
