\r\n\t\t\t\t\t As a Septuagenarian ( or Caveman, as some have said ) with little game play experience, I have found the many hours playing this game rewarding and lots of fun.
I highly recommend this game. Fun for all ages and eases you towards the tougher levels gradually.
I definitely needed to read the guides and used the discussion pages often in the early days to help navigate my progress but quickly started to play and adapt to the positives and negatives of Staff, Rooms and Equipment etc.
I also now know that there is an easier way or a funner ( is that a word ?) way to play, but for me I prefer the option of not loading up on "gold stars" etc and try to build a "nice" environment as it raises this old mans happiness level. ( Although I have gone the quicker fix at times )
Having a Sandbox is great for building crazy stuff and the younger ones had tons of fun experimenting, creating havoc and destruction.
All in all a blast has been had by those who have had a crack at my game.
Good luck if you decide to have a go.
Cheers Al
