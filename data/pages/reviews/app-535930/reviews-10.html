\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very fun game, and one I (at the moment) prefer to Two Point Campus. Because the impacts of your choices, of placement of objects, amounts of doctors or rooms, are directly visible in the game play. It takes a bit of time figuring out on Wiki why happiness of staff or patients is important and how it affects the game, but once you spend a bit of time learning the basics, it goes uphill from there. (meaning it gets better).

The only 'beef' I have with this game, is that it does not clearly say what affects what or how. For example Two Point made a statement in not writing any numbers on any of the items. Which is annoying, but once you visit the Wiki enough, you will quickly find your favorites and will know what you are doing.

Copying items would be nice too, and I don't think they will implement it. But at least know that on the top left you have a "filter", so you can search for your items without having to scroll up and down to find it.

Overall, it is a very addicting game, with fun visuals and very funny 'diseases' and descriptions. But it is a good Sim as well, in a sense that it makes sense why certain things affect others and it is a great game for those who like to 'micro manage' their workers, as oppose to 'macro manage' (like two point campus).

If you like Sim games that are for fun but also make sense, then this game is for you!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter 250 hours playing and completing the game (no DLCs yet) I can say that this game is what any tycoon management lover would ask for. Fun, dynamic, plenty of missions to do and a sandbox. You can play this game for hours and you won't even notice. I like the funny sicknesses and the machines to cure them, very creative. Once you get into it, you know what to do for making a cool hospital. Even when each hospital comes with some new challenges, it's not something that you could manage once you learn how to deal with it.

If you want a game for having fun at your own pace and that you will be able to play whenever you want and come back to it, this is one great option to pick. Considering the competition or games that are in the same hospital management category, I don't think there's something better that Two Point Hospital. The OST is pretty cool, I love to play with it, but if you're into your own ♥♥♥♥, you can play your music instead. That's a nice feature. There are collaborative tasks if you have friends with the game that unlock items very useful for your hospitals.

I would totally recommend this game to any city builder's lovers, tycoon builder's lovers, management game's lovers, etc. And for the prize you can get this when there are offers, is a must.
