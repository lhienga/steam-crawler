\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen I heard there would be a spiritual successor to Theme Hospital I was extremely excited. Management sims like this keep me sane because I can manipulate every factor in a simulated environment, so a refresh on a personal favorite was exciting. It lived up to it's promises and has abundant charm, wit, and adds new mechanics and challenges to keep you playing. R.E.M.I.X. mode has been great for me as someone with 30 stars to enjoy a new game mode while still keeping the same basics.

Theme Hospital fans will love the room templates, the nostalgic charm, new illnesses, and new game mechanics (Epidemic, R.E.M.I.X., radio customization, HOURS of radio host content, etc). The vomiting sound is also far less repulsive.

There is some room for improvement, however. DLCs only seem to add new illnesses rather than changing game play, and getting new items is challenging if you don't have any friends who play. The global research mechanic where you complete tasks for items is, while interesting, a hindrance to those of us who do not want to add strangers to our friends list and wonder who "Bimbo Baggins" is after a few months when they change their display name and icon. My criticisms are minute compared to the praises I have for this game. If you enjoy tycoon games/management sims, this should be at the top of your list. Or you can get Theme Hospital from GOG and see if you hunger for more...\t\t\t\t\t
