\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you ever played Theme Hospital then buy this game! Many of the developers from bullfrog are now at Two Point studios so this is the natural successor to the franchise.

This game is kind of a game of contradictions, in a very good way.

It is both funny and cartoonish while at the same time being a very good management sim.

Most hospitals can become solvent pretty quickly and easily but if you don't develop them properly you can soon find yourself bankrupt either because you expanded too quickly or haven't expanded enough or efficiently enough to keep up with growing demand as more patients arrive due to your hospital being good and having a good reputation.

Like most management sims there generally becomes a point you have reached critical mass and have in effect suceeded and then the challenge becomes fine tuning everything and seeing how good you can make everything and there are plenty of options to tweak and improve your hospital to make it even better...tweaking the hospital to make staff and visitor happier, tweaking rooms to make them more efficient, tweaking prices and wages to make the most profit, rerarranging the layout to make the flow of the hospital more efficient with the advantage that the game style is funny and entertaining so not only can you train the best surgeon in the world you can train a feckless idiot who smells so bad he makes people puke when he walks into the room into the best surgeon in the world.

The game does have an option to copy and paste rooms as well as save them between scenarios and certain rooms in particular you will be making over and over again, even in the same hospital.e.g. G.P. room but at the same time some of the building shapes can make this challenging but again you can just create some basic cut and paste rooms you use over and over again to complete all the scenarios as there is plenty of building space to not have to worry about having to maximise space but you can also fine tune every single room to maximise the space used in every hosiptal and make it as efficient as possible.

The game does have a progression/gating system which provides additional challenges where you have to meet certain criterior to progress and/or unlock new things although the progress/essential unlocks are pretty easy (if your having difficulty unlocking them you probably aren't ready to move on) unlocking everything can be challenging (or time consuming depending on your philosophy).

Overall this game should be enjoyable by most people where you can just play it casually and laugh while doing a basic playthrough of the campaign or you can spend spend hundreds of hours tweaking and refining the perfect hospital.\t\t\t\t\t
