\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCons:

- there are a few very tedious chores you have to do to 3 star every hospital, such as vaccinating epidemic patients (you basically have to stare at your screen waiting for someone to give off a rare symptom) or getting the hygiene rating up to 90% (one of the most difficult and boring tasks in the game).
- the vast majority of items in the game just increase attractiveness of the hospital. very few actually do anything else useful. would love to see a better mix of item uses.

Pros: (everything else)

- Balancing your economy and cure rate is fun once you have a working strategy for doing it.
- The radio stations and tannoy operator are hilarious
- Once the devs added room copy-pasting and templates the game went from tedious to fun
- The game does a great job of keeping the feel of the old Theme Hospital while updating the mechanics for today's audiences.

Overall a fantastic game but DOES take some getting used to. A lot of working strategies don't make a lot of sense, but once you figure them out it will be smooth sailing. That having been said, you basically won't run into any of the cons I mentioned if you only want to 1-star the hospitals (which is all you need to unlock all the hospitals in the game). You will only struggle (with both reasonable and unreasonable challenges) if you're a completionist and insist on 3-starring every hospital.\t\t\t\t\t
