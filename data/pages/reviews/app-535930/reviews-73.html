\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTwo Point Hospital is an enjoyable sim game. There are many options to build up your hospital as well as an overall career story to play. New options continue to open up as you play the game and handy tips pop up frequently to give you some idea of what you might want to consider next. Its easy to get absorbed in creating the perfect hospital, there is so much to take in to consideration from staff, training, what facilities to build and upgrade and limited time challenges to bring prestige and currency awards to your hospital network. The more you play the more options that unlock including items and upgrades and even new facilities. I would definitely recommend this game to people that enjoy sim games, I find it a perfect way to unwind after a busy day.\t\t\t\t\t
