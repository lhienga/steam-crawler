Each new level I am excited to improve my hospital, and I don't feel like I am constrained from doing what I want to do by the map layout or the "meta". There is something that I don't like about the game though. Those Superbug Network projects require you to have a friend complete objectives for you to progress. Those projects seem to be secondary to the main progression system, but it was still fun having other objectives to work towards.\t\t\t\t\t
