\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a good game, fun and can play at your own pace. Pretty good successor to Theme Hospital though doesn't quite catch its charm.

I have three big issues with the game:

1: Every game plays the same. Despite the occasional map quirk every hospital can be built the same which means once you have completed 3 or so maps you will naturally want to design them the same. The game doesn't really provide a reason to change up your room designs or general layout of the hospital. This means for me I can only play 3-4 hours every couple weeks to keep it fun an interesting.

2: You are never in any real danger. Once you get set up the money just flows and you are simply waiting for enough to drop the next room. Some maps have specific events but there isn't any like a "mass cas accident you are forced to take" or "someone is sueing the hospital" etc. Its actually hard to lose a map and I have never come close. On that note killing patients seems to have little repercussions. I have 2-3 patients die a month and no one cares, I have fuming patients that leave because they haven't been treated in over a year and nothing happens.

3: Friend assisted research. I am guessing this is hear to encourage you to get your friends to buy the game and help you? Since none of my friends play this game and I have no intention searching online for random people this whole feature is walled off to me.

Other points: No ambient life outside the hospital, only one level no additional floors or basement, no real personality with the staff (they have quirks but none of it plays out in game).

I recommend the game, its really the only good successor to theme hospital, but it does have some big dings against it.\t\t\t\t\t
