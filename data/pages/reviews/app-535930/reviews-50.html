\r\n\t\t\t\t\t3 out of 5 stars

TPH is a very accessible manage-sim, with some quirky humor. It also looks pleasant and is very easy to control. However, it also is really slow and tedious. You are basically just expanding you hospital continuously, which requires you to copy-paste rooms, build new type of rooms meant for specific illnesses and hire and train your staff. The game isn't hard at all, but certainly will feel like a slog if your machines needs frequent reparing because of earthquakes (and for some reason many levels have those) and training staff takes a while. It also doesn't help that some rooms aren't high priority in certain levels but others are, requiring you to demolish and build a copy of another room and place every mandatory item individually again (like benches, plants and radiators). You'll feel like you're constantly fixing problems. I never really took the time to appreciate the little details of the game or even the unnecesary graph tab in the menus. It is shallow busy work.

However, as shallow as the game is, It is still decently put together with lots of content. The workshop is also a welcome addition. And for that, I find it too harsh to give this game a thumbs down.\t\t\t\t\t
