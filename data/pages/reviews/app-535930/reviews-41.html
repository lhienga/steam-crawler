\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you enjoyed Theme Hospital from the late 90's then this is definitely a game for you. If like me you sunk more hours into that game than you care to remember then give this one a go. It is called the spiritual successor for a reason.
Same humour (the receptionist, if anything, is only becoming more sarcastic with age), same feeling, newer graphics. Plenty of levels to play through, some frustrating if you want to get all three stars, different problems to overcome or location specific issues.
One fix I am so glad for is the infectious diseases. Thankfully now the infected can wander between your hospital buildings without triggering the health inspector visit (and then as rapidly as possible hunting everyone down and evicting them from the premises to avoid as much damage as possible). They are harder to find and vaccinate but at least it gives you a chance at success.

The only downside is I miss some of the old illnesses (bloaty head, invisibility and slack tongue especially).

Now if only we could have some more spiritual successors to Bullfrog games!!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally fun resource management game where you are trying to run a hospital. Illnesses range from silly to overly hilarious and require some bizarre methods of treatment with specialized machinery. Everything looks very cute, but this ain't the type of hospital I want to be treated in for sure.

The management part is quite light but it is still possible to screw up. Main focus goes to humor and the ability to create/decorate all the rooms. Although from a pure management perspective, this doesn't really add that much except for some prestigue numbers making your people more happy. Most cosmetics are purely optional.

I really liked all the weird stuff going on here. Bought all the DLC's and not sure what's in what anymore. There's a lot of hospitals you can build-up. Some require unlocking first but it's up to you to decide to go fully in on one hospital at a time or toy around from one hospital to another.

Going for 100% completion will take up A LOT of time and things might get a bit tedious in the end. The different zones on the map do offer a lot to stay entertained though, so you might want to mix it up a little.\t\t\t\t\t
