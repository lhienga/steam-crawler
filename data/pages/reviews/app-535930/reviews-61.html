\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Two Point Hospital is a very funny medical sim. Your staff will be curing a variety of weird illnesses, from verbal diarrhoea to inflated ego and much more unusual ones. You’ll probably discover one or multiple diseases which symptoms will make you chuckle. But giggles won’t pay the bills! You’ll have to play smart stay out of debt, keep an high reputation and retain a good cure rate even when the amount of patients increase rapidly.

The levels in the career mode will gradually throw more and more stuff at you to deal with, which makes for a really good way to learn the game. After having done a decent amount of hospitals, it’s very fun to go back to your older ones and improve them with your new knowledge.

I’d definitely recommend the base game, it’ll take you plenty of hours to complete. I have not tried any DLC yet, but ot tastes like more!



Favourite quote
“Please leave the toilets as you’d like to find them, not as you expect to find them.”

Little warning
The last hospital in the career mode will get laggy, be sure to lower your graphics a bit before jumping into this one.

7.8/10
