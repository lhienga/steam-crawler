\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot overly complex, but challenging enough to make you sit back and strategize your next moves in the best tradition of "tycoon" games. The game is a riot, from the types of illnesses your patients have to deal with to your methods of curing them, from the voiceover "PA" announcements or to the radio DJ making occasional comments, much of the material is laugh-out-loud funny. The graphics are also quite unique, and again, many of the characters are fun to watch, especially if they have some amusing diseases and are moving in strange ways. The game developers really did a fantastic job and -- as far as I can tell so far -- the game is bug-free. Well done! Moreover, the game is highly addicting, and I've barely scratched the surface since there are levels upon levels upon levels of new hospitals, new challenges, new room types and layouts, and treatment devices I have yet to unlock. Can't wait to see what comes next.\t\t\t\t\t
