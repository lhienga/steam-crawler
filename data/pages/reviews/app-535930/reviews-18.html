\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA more complex remake of the old Theme Hospital, TPH is a tycoon simulation game in which you build a hospital. Actually you build many hospitals in the career mode and with all the DLCs which add diseases, rooms and animations to the base game.

I think the most important thing about this game is that it's very, very funny. That's a rare thing among video games nowadays. You can't not smile when you see a cheerful Elvis Presley walking in the hospital halls. Add to that a hundred more funny disorders and hilarious audio announcements and you can get an idea about how funny this game is.

Otherwise, TPH is a classic tycoon game, with research, money and staff management and, of course, patient care and coaxing. The complexity is variable through the missions, some are hard or grindy, some are simple.

Pros:
- graphics
- humour all over the place, no spot left untouched
- very long game, especially with all the DLC's
- customizable rooms very easy to build with many, many hilarious decorations
- addictive: when you finish a mission, you can't wait to begin the next one
- relaxing and satisfying to see your hospital going about its business like an ant farm
- music (I love it, one of the best)

Cons:
- DLCs content is not integrated in the main game, i.e. it's available only in the DLC's missions.\t\t\t\t\t
