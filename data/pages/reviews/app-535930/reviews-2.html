\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRead some poor reviews of this game a while back and they put me off completely. How wrong I was. Very wrong. Huge mistake, because I bought this game recently and regret not buying it sooner. First game in a long time where I'm completely obsessed with it - put just about 500 hours into playing the game. Finished the base game, Bigfoot DLC and almost all the Pebberley Island DLC so far. The walk styles and behaviour of patients had me laughing out loud. Spat my drink at seeing 'Mock Star' and some of the dances and idle animations of staff. The humour is fantastic.

The different levels of the base game offer different aspects in a progressive way so you gradually become more skilled in your career. Haven't tried sandbox mode yet, but love the various challenges, whether it's related to fitting in the rooms in tiny/awkward shaped hospitals, or to do with hiring/training staff, or meeting cure rates. Every hospital, every level has something challenging and it's satisfying when you create good hospital layouts that work well. I also enjoy the hiring/training aspect for staff.

The interface is extremely user-friendly, with large icons and tabs. As my vision is affected by health issues, it's a relief being able to see everything. The tabs are straightforward and you can allocate each staff member to specific rooms or tasks, so great customisation of their roles.

The music is enjoyable and overall the gameplay experience is funny, stress-busting and relaxing. Hours go by without you realising.

I can HIGHLY RECOMMEND this game!
