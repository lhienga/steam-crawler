Gameplay: 9/10

Later Edit: After a patch, the game is not working anymore, whatever i do. Reinstalled, deleted saves, deleted registry entries, nothing fixes it. I have posted screenshots on the developer's discord and recorded my screen and posted it on youtube, posting the videos on the said discord again, but there is no info or fix from the devs for 2 months now. Clueless devs.

Later edit: 4th of novermber 2022. I was contacted by a dev via discord and in about 3 hours we have found a solution to the bug. The windows user did not have file rights on the savegame folder in app data, most likelly from a antivirus type software. They made a debug version of the game for me to download and to troubleshoot this and we talked back and forward until we fixed it.

Edit after playing the DLC: Completed the DLC with 100% achievements. The gameplay is expanded upon from the base game. It still has the same feeling, but with the new stuff you feel even more powerful and unstopable. I found it more fun to run DLC runs than base game runs. Waiting for endless mode.\t\t\t\t\t
