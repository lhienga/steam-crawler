\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: This is a good game. Do yourself a favor and just buy it.

You play as a silent protagonist who must, for some reason, slay the corrupted members of the populace, ending each stage with a Four Symbols-themed boss fight.

There are many minibosses, but there are only 5 main boss for each playthrough: the 4 officials (Pig, Crane-Phoenix, Turtle, General Tiger) and the Dragon Emperor.

In higher difficulties, the mobs (and their mutations) pose a bigger problem than the bosses, in my opinion.

If you are a Chinese mythology/wuxia/xianxia buff, this game is for you. If you are a Hades fan, you will enjoy it. I played the game and finished the base difficulty months ago. I came back recently. They've added tons of stuff which made me play and finish the game on higher difficulties.

I rate it 9/10, if for the fact that 10/10 would mean that they'd have to expound more on the story and probably get a better engine for the game. And at its price point, it's head and shoulders above the competition. Try it for yourself.\t\t\t\t\t
