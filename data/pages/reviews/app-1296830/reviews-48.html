\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've only played 2 hours but I've had so much fun in those 2 hours that I'm just going to write my review early lol.
This game is so much fun and easy to get into, there's not a big learning curve and after my second death I've yet to die again as I've found a really decent build since it's easy to catch on to how this game works.

The art style is beautiful and the mechanics are simple.

The boss fights are actually a lot of fun and though I don't find them as challenging they do make you use your reflexes which I enjoy a lot coming from Vindictus, Nioh, and Monster Hunter.

The build customization is what makes this game worth every penny imo; there are so many options to go with depending on your skill and play style and it makes you feel like you have control over how you fight which is very nice and makes you want to play over and over to see if you can get an even better build & weapon.

The soundtracks are also very interesting and usually I would turn music down to 0% but not this time as it's very peaceful to listen to while you slash away enemies.

This game really gives me old school Contra vibes with the art style and boss sequences and gives a nostalgic feel like I'm a kid playing on my Sega genesis again.

Overall I give this at 9/10 and will definitely support the devs in their future projects! Well done with this one!
I hope that one day there will be newer weapons & animations introduced into the game (i.e): a Staff, Nunchucks, dual swords, great sword, etc...\t\t\t\t\t
