\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great game, almost is I should say, the gameplay feels nice, there are a few bugs and a lot of details that need polishing, the game should be in early access because honestly it doesn't feel like a full game yet.

The translation is pretty rough and there are a few places where you can get stuck which is no big deal because if you quit and come back you only get thrown back to the beginning of whatever room you were in, the art is gorgeous outside of the ending sequence which feels more like a 2008 flash animation.

Pros: Great gameplay and variety of playstyles with the different statues that you can pray to which give you different classes I would say.
The controls are pretty nice and responsive, never once felt it lag.
The soundtrack is amazing
The artstyle is gorgeous.
The story and unlockables are interesting and fun to collect.

Cons: The difficulty levels don't offer any variety, the hardest difficulty level only makes the enemies (included bosses) have 50 times the health as usual it feels like, as a consequence a run on the hardest difficulty level ends up feeling more like a chore than something fun you want to do, I've ran blizzard difficulty twice and once I died, the second one I just quit because I couldn't get myself to keep going further seeing as in the third stage the turtle palace it was taking me around 5 - 10 minutes to clear each room.
The bugs; There is this one specific bug that makes the game rezise itself when you dash or "flicker" which even it if doesnt affect the gameplay its really annoying.
There are quite a few spots where you can get stuck, which is no big deal but it's still annoying.
The translation needs a lot of work
The game needs a lot of balancing, certain "classes" are obviously much stronger than the others discouraging variety at the time of picking up your statue for the next run.

All in all good game but I wouldn't consider it a "full game" more like an early access type of thing since there's a lot of work to be done for it to be perfect.

Worth the money? Probably yes, you can play the demo if you're not sure if you want to get it yet.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great game that suffers some annoying translation issues related to items and bonuses.

The gamplay is responsive and very satisfaying, the combos of excaliburs, items and perks are varied and unlock nicely along the way.
The game might ultimately not be as hard or balanced as Hades or Curse of the Dead Gods, but it has plenty going on for it, and ultimately I ended up prefering this one over the two others.

Yes it has some totally broken combos, and also some underperforming ones, but imho that is also one of the biggest charm of this game. Finding the ultimate combos are so satisfying, and playing the weak one proved to be fun and challenging. It all comes to your choices and how you wanna play the run.\t\t\t\t\t
