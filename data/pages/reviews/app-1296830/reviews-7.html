\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally good roguelite game, it's very similar to Hades. But with less build variety I would say. The builds rely more around elements such as lighting, poison, fire etc.

Before each run you choose a Dharmic deity to commit to which represent a element, and from there you can choose one of two abilities. You unlock new deities by defeating bosses.

You only have one weapon. A sword in one hand and a bunch of flying swords as your ranged weapon.

You can get new swords that change stats and compliment other things you have. But the main butter of the builds is the relics and scrolls which give you passive stat buffs, like buffing you abilities, atk dmg, health etc and also active buffs such as enhancing your dodge and attack abilities.

You also unlock new relics and scrolls by beating more runs and collecting story items which are randomly dropped by bosses.

The game is not very randomised except for the difficulties changing up some of the mid level bosses (what the game calls mini-bosses).

But as I said, this game is definitely below Hades and Dead Cells, they are S tier roguelites, but the combat has an addictive nature to it, and it is fun to jump in and do a run every now and again. And this game is cheaper so therefore I still recommend this to roguelite fans.
