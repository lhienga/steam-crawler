\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is Wuxia Hades.

I have two complaints and it's not much, the graphics are a little wonky and take a bit of getting used to but that's honestly nothing. The second complaint is just the subtitles kind of zip by, I can read them as a speed reader but I'm not sure if that can be said for the rest of the population. Same goes for the dialogue boxes when the NPC's are talking to you.

Overall a blast for the first 30 minutes I've been in.

The skills are neat, has your standard skill trees like in Gunfire Reborn. There's a ton of options for builds and weapon customization. For instance you do extra damage with "drunk" and if your build has fire in it, you start doing DOT damage while they're drunk and on fire. That made me laugh.

The boss fights are fun, some have some surprising mechanics but in the beginning it's easy and the difficulty only goes up from there until you wonder what the hell just hit you.

The real icing on the cake though I feel is the weapon/skill build "class" customization where you can either hurl wind swords or hoard your swords to be an unkillable tank with defensive stats through the roof. Have a taser fetish where you shock the hell out of enemies and where a shiny cape while doing it? You've got that too.

The mobs an AI feel extremely similar to Hades or Sentient K where they just bum rush you and enemy archers do the enemy archer thing. Where it kind of shines is the snap inputs in mini-boss and big boss rights where they pop in your face and timing is key, which makes it a little more interesting and fun.

Maps are beautiful and so is the art. You're just a Ronin/Wuxia warlord running around slaying evil townspeople that sucked up a bad batch of snowflakes in a House of Flying Daggers x Demon Slayer x Onimusha/Nioh kind of demon/zombie/cancer pigs warfare world.

I'll write a better review as time goes on but so far it's a solid 9/10 for me only because translations and dialogue speed is a little fast.

TL;DR The monkey boss is a B@$%@RD.

Edit: Upon further review, this game is a challenge when you decide to Russian roulette your build and pray for the RNG to give you what you need. It gets way more intense the longer I play it, to the point of infuriating. So it has that "5#!% I died again, why the hell won't this guy die" rogue-like feeling to it 100%.\t\t\t\t\t
