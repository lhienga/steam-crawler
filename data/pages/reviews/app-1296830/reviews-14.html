\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter 100+ hours, i can say the gameplay is fun and enjoyable, lots of builds to choose from, especially the system to get your relic in the lobby and block other relics so you can customise on a build you are going for. the same goes for the swords, you can block some swords that randomly drops, meaning it increases your chance of getting the sword that you want for your build

Gameplay, this is like hades, it is rouge-like and you will likely die a lot on repeat to get all the talent tree and get stronger, after you fill up the talent, any run you do is just experimenting on a build that you have in mind that the game throws at you, you pick them up and use them, some builds are easier to use like thunder and ice, and some require mechanical use like supreme bodhi or mother trinity

the mobs are different every level, or in higher difficulty some later mobs show up earlier than the level and has higher hp (basically bloated health). But your skill, sword and relic is adjusted to the difficulty to get that sweet legendary gear to kill those bloated mobs.

Bosses are challenging to a point where it can be easy or hard depending on your build, some builds eat bosses like a melted chocolate on hot summer using ice build or thunder, some builds take longer, like poison or infinite arhat. It does not mean poison or infinite arhat are trash builds tho. Each statues shine in their own way, and depending on your luck during a run you can get their unique sword that makes clicking the mouse feel so good.
overall recommended the warm snow with its gameplay and replayability and also the randomness of drops like relics and swords making each run different or controlled depending on how long you have played\t\t\t\t\t
