\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game would be like any other rouge like if it had not been for the near limitless amount of build options it presents you with. I had the most fun not using my brain but doing as chaotic builds as I could and it works. It works so perfectly that you realise this is the most fun and at the same time the most optimal way to play.

It takes a while to get used to for me it was around 2-3 hours but in which I could not even pass the second boss. But the moment you understand what you will do and you get a tiny bit used to the perks ever boss can be defeated under 30 seconds. Every enemy can be dodged and the entire game can be played by just pressing two buttons. When I was making a thunder run I did not even know what was happening on the screen.

Overall a very fun game if you are in it for the build potantial and gameplay. Other than that the music, story or the visuals are not really something to be impressed by\t\t\t\t\t
