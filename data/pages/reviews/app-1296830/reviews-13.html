\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChinese Hades is the best description of this game. With its own style and story and a very fast paced sword-throwing-and-slashing gameplay and a slightly different take on mixing things together. It's not as good as the "original" Hades but good enough (tbf nothing is as good as Hades..). It's definitely worth the price (and not just the steam sale price). A great game to spend time with while waiting for Hades 2.

It's also great on SteamDeck (with text a bit too small but it's not affecting experience). Cloud Saves work great, even mid-run and you can even change the controller button icons between steamdeck/playstation so it matches your current controller (I play with Dualshock on PC).

PS: A lot of people compare it to Hades, I hope the devs see it as a good thing.\t\t\t\t\t
