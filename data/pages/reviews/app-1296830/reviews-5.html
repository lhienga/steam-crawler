\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMeh. More bad than good.
So unbalanced that it feels like the only two upgrades in the game are "does nothing" and "you win". You either get an OP upgrade and easily blow through the game, or just get a bunch of nothing and lose. I died to the first boss 4 times then I got a few overpowered upgrades and leisurely walked through the entire game. Quickly beat every boss with no trouble at all, first try, with basically no meta upgrades.
First boss insta-kill mechanic is no fun. Makes it so certain upgrades/builds are not viable.
The game plays pretty well, looks good, and could be a lot of fun if they tweak the balance a bit. The big problem with OP stuff is that it's not fun, you just mash all the buttons and annihilate everything. So I imagine grinding to unlock all the meta upgrades will just be a chore. Instead of being excited to try new stuff and keep playing, it all just doesn't matter.
Would recommend it if they balanced the upgrades and removed insta-kill. All they need to do is make it so the useless upgrades actually do something, then it would feel better and be more fun instead of just hoping for good luck with RNG to get the good stuff or retrying because the game decided "not this run". Skill isn't a factor in this game, it's just luck of the draw.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely in my top 5!
This is Chinese Hades no doubts, so it'll be more approachable to Eastern fans.
Very strong rogue-like elements, lots of available builds, swords, skills, upgrades. The graphics is really good especially for this type of game. Story is somewhat meh, not that much explaining and not that deep, but who cares? The replay value is stupidly high, the only game I kept playing daily.
It plays really well on the Steam Deck and highly recommend over PC.
I'll probably ended up buying the mobile version soon.

If you dig rogue-like games with high replay value, pick this up, you won't regret it even at full price!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGuys, this game is fantastic. The number of available builds in this game is so significant that I could not tell even the approximate number. I played this game a lot. After DLC, I am playing it even more after DLC, as this is a new game with new mechanics and staff. I am not sure if it is good or bad, but sometimes, if you are lucky, the build could be insanely strong and kill every boss in seconds. The gameplay is straightforward. You have dash, melee, and "flying swords," which basically range attack; different weapons, different relics, different "cards" with modifications.
Beautiful graphics, scenes, and voice lines were done well (Chinese). English localization is excellent; I have no complaints at all.
Enemies have different patterns of attacks, strategies, and so on. I was surprised that they even go further in DLC, with new types of enemies there.

I highly recommend this game for all Rogue-like top-down action game lovers.\t\t\t\t\t
