\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely recommend this game even though I have a huge beef with the devs for the bad English translations. I would recommend to either find a good Chinese/English translator or ask the community for help in translation. Google translate can take you so far, especially with Chinese :/

Pros:
- A lot of fun, with a huge amount of variety and content for its price point.
- Graphics are pretty amazing for what it is.
- Fluid combat
- Boss fights are well telegraphed.
- Challenging but not too challenging.

Cons:
- Game is loads of fun, with terrible TERRIBLE English translation.
You got:
1- Misspelling.
2- Flat out change in terminology (e.g Relics/Elixirs are the same thing apparently)
3- Some properties or upgrades don't make any sense, and you have no clue if it would work with your build or not.
4- Incomplete descriptions (e.g Deal lightning damage to the.... to that what?).
5- And finally some things are not even bothered to be translated.

- Apart from that, the game also lacks visual cues for your buffs or debuffs. How do I know the new relic that I got [that's horribly translated] is working or not?\t\t\t\t\t
