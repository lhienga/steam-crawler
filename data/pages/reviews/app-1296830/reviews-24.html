\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a fun game.
The game feels very good to play. So it's not awkward or wonky, like some games in its genre.
It's kind of easy to complete, but there's overarching progress and progressively higher difficulty modes after you finish the game on one difficulty.

The game has a fun system where you're basically trying to break the game by getting an OP build.
If you play the demo, you might think: "Ah, this gameplay isn't super exciting or mindblowing." But if you play it for like 5 hours, you might end up learning how to put together a very satisfying, incredible built where you destroy enemies in one or two hits.

I'd say this type of gameplay & mechanics are more fun than those of Hades, but Hades has a better story, characters, aesthetic and such. But for people just into playing games for sake of gameplay, this game has is a great experience.

If you like this game, consider Hades & Curse of the Dead Gods.\t\t\t\t\t
