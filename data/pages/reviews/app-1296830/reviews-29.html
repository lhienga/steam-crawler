\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game. Far from perfect but definitely a good game for its genre.

All the builds in this game are quite balanced if you have the right weapon, sect, and artifact. Personally, the lightning Buddha is the weakest for me. It is really fast, can stun, but lacks the damage in the hardest difficulty. You need the x3 lightning damage artifact for the build to be viable. The rest of the classes are close in terms of DPS.

The best weapon in this game is the Butcher pair with damnation. 40% for the attack to deal 300% damage.

Seven Sword - all rounded, great DPS, life leech, great for bleeding.

Carousing Fire - fire damage, it's ok, nothing special.

Mother Trinity - poison damage, tankiest class in the game for its life leech but in exchange for dps. You can literally tank any boss in the hardest difficulty making up for your dps.

Infinite Arhat - flying sword baby! very long range, huge damage, great for some blood pact

Bitter River - ice damage, freeze enemies, great dps

Thunder Fury - lightning damage, fast but lack dps, can stun normal mobs, can’t stun boss

Supreme Bodhi - use flicker and shealth a lot, shealth doesn’t affect by shealth cooldown. It stays 30 seconds forever. Fastest class in the game, great damage

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was following this game before release and thought the art style was unique and the gameplay seemed fun and reminiscent of Hades, and to my surprise it was just as good as Hades in terms of fun gameplay.
The start is rather slow and you think it doesn’t run too deep, however when you have all your skill trees maxed out you feel each run to be exciting even at a base level regardless of what power you choose to start with (there are 7 currently and each have 2 different versions so 14 in total). The story unfortunately isn’t that interesting or even exciting and the poor translation doesn’t help. Another issue is there’s a lot of clipping with text in English and there are still some weapons that you have no Idea what they do by trying to read it in the game.
One great thing I liked about it is how increase in difficulty is actually more than just increasing HP and Attack, you have new enemies appear, harder enemies appearing and even entirely new mechanics for bosses or new minibosses.
TLDR;
Pros:
- Nice graphics
- Smooth Gameplay
- Great Harder Difficulties which add to the experience
- Good replayability
- Nice Build Variety
- Big Boob Giant Woman
Cons:
- Clipping text issues and incorrect texts
- Poor Story
- Slow start
- Not enough music or SFX
Overall score 8/10

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game feels really smooth and very satisfying to maneuver and fight in. My pros heavily outweigh the cons and have had a lot f fun exploring different build paths and items. I'll list a couple specific pros and cons for anyone who might be interested or is new to the game.

Pros:

-Variety: I have just over 20 hours and I feel like I haven't had any two builds be the same. With all the different weapons and relics, all with color coded tiers, modifiers, and attributes that are randomized (on weapons), the game feels like ther is a never ending combination of things to play with. To top it off, you get to eventually choose a bunch of different things to use before you even start the run so you don't feel like your success is completely rng based.

-Combat: The combat feels very crisp all from lighting fast, meat slapping atk and mvmt speed to slower but hard hitting curb stomping damage. Throw in a bunch of debuffs and status effects and you got yourself some very powerful feeling slicing and dicing.

Cons:

-Dialogue/ Narration: This game does not have english narration which isn't a huge issue, just would be nice to know what characters are saying without having to read speech bubbles. The real con here is the translation in item descriptions are not super accurate and sometimes it takes some depicting to figure out what it says, or doesn't even finish the description at all.

-Terrain: I really like all the little things they put on the floor to add to the density of the stages, but it's infuriating when there's a bunch of stuff going on and I get stuck on a little book or something and end up taking a bunch of damage, or sometimes the character stick to walls in very odd ways and have the same conclusion.

Overall:
Very fun game and plan to put many more hours into it :)\t\t\t\t\t
