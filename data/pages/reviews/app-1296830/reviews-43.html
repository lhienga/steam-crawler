\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInspired by Hades' multiple choice boon system or perhaps just an example of parallel thought, Warm Snow has very solid mechanics and game design. Nothing revelatory, but plenty of room for experimentation and build testing. Unfortunately, it's really let down by the moment to moment gameplay. Combat feels weak and bland outside of boss fights. There's no sense of impact or satisfying feedback, and enemy mobs are uninteresting in design and act mindlessly. This is not to hate on the developers, I don't think they've made a cheap or lazy game, it just isn't at a level of satisfaction I find worthwhile. If I were to compare it to another title, I'd compare it to Dandy Ace. Both have good ideas and solid mechanics, but to me, are let down by the moment to moment gameplay, level design, and game "feel".\t\t\t\t\t
