Serious I have had attacks that normally should be dished out within a second not be thrown out and hold that attack for another second while I finish my dodge animation just to then throw the attack and hit me. Serous sometimes you feel as if the programming is predetermined to hit you at times. I don't like that not one bit!

Of course I don't really know what's going on. I really enjoy the game as I stated above and I prefer this game over the binding of Issac to be frank. It's got depth and both games are fun but this is definitely more my style of game.

A simple fix to that problem stated above(it is a problem) is to indicate attacks using the red and white lines. Even having a better indication of the attack... have the arm or weapon follow the player until 0.2-0.5 seconds or whatever(more clear animations). Then stop the arm or weapons whatever in place. Meaning the attack no longer tracks the player.

That's what is really key here. There needs to be a clear point in time when the attack no longer tracks the player and it needs to be visibly clear so players can pick up and learn the queues. That's what makes games like these fun to master.

I really want to sink hours into this game but it's really turning me off and slowly diminishing my will to play the game. I mean we are talking if I knew these mechanics were present in the game prior to playing. Just for those reason I would of been more likely not to purchase the game.

I want to give this a thumbs up but if you have mechanics that ruin the experience and burn players out early on. Then I can't exactly give this game a thumbs up.

Assuming both the problems of reaction time between attacks in boss fights and unavoidable damage via the attack animation hitting and sometimes waiting for you to finish a dodge animation is fixed. I can't recommend this game to anyone. Will I continue to play? Yes from time to time but only because I can't refund the game now. It took 3-4 hours to notice these things. At first I thought maybe I just timed my dodge poorly or maybe it was a fluke or just a tiny bug form time to time. Then the pattern starts becoming more apparent as you spend more time playing and you realize it's not your dodging it's the game mechanics. When it's you it's frustrating but I love improving and getting better. It's infuriating when it's mechanics because there is nothing you can do to improve yourself or improve the game... it's out of your hands and that ruins all the fun!\t\t\t\t\t
