\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLegit one of (if not the best) live-the-life-of-a-wuxia-protagonist game I've ever played. If you ever read a wuxia story and imagine what it'd be like to throw swords around and slash like crazy, this game's pretty much for you. It fulfills that one fantasy, unlike most other games like it that spread around a bit, but it fulfills it perfectly. As many reviewers pointed out, it's quite similar to hades, but I'd argue a bit higher paced, if less varied.

Note that the balancing isn't the greatest, and that you progress through the game fairly quickly--but I actually enjoy these two points, especially having gone into the game blind. Stumbling onto an insanely oppressive build that was finished 2 stages before the last was extremely fun, and just playing around with various possibilities is equally so. If you're an end-game purist, perhaps these balancing nods might piss you off, but I mostly play for the entertainment and usually stop once I've hit that 'play for 200 hours' wall, so this is perfect for me.

If you want a game where you can dump around 30-40 minutes a night into and have a blast, this one's for you. Runs can go on a bit longer at a higher difficulty, especially if you don't get a good roll of luck early on, but oh well, you can always restart.

UI's a bit wonky at times as it clearly wasn't optimized for English language so sometimes you'll see words bleed beyond the border, but they are relatively rare. Anyway, if you're a fan of fast-paced, Hades-like games, this one's for you. Plus, if you ever wanted to feel what it would be like to take upon the mantle of a wuxia MC, look no further. Slash away and all that jazz.\t\t\t\t\t
