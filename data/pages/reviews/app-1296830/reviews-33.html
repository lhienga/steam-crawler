\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll in all the core concept is there and is fun, but there is definitely room for improvement.

You start out with one class and unlock more classes as you progress through the bosses and difficulties. Together with the relics and weapons you mix and match what you randomly get and see how far you go. It's somewhat unbalanced because some classes are flat out better than others and some relic+weapon combinations output crazy amounts of damage while others are useless.

The harder difficulties are hit or miss depending on how well your relics + weapon + class + acquired skills work together. Sometimes you'll clear at a snail's pace because you get no damage items. Other times you will obliterate floors because you managed to stack synergies very early on. There is no in between. In this regard I wish there were some way to guarantee drops for your class because if you don't get those few drops that work, you're basically wasting your time. The hardest difficulty (white ash) gives bosses additional mechanics that heavily favor ranged over melee, which is not great. Ideally both should be equally viable.

Improvements I'd like to see:
1) Translations - Not all voice lines have subtitles, and translations for the relics and weapons are confusing.
2) Transparent projectiles - Some attacks, relic effects, etc. take up large portions of the screen and make it hard to understand what's happening. Perhaps there can be a setting that changes their transparency
3) Camera focus - The camera should always be on the character. Instead it focuses on the boss. In a similar vein to (2), how am I supposed to understand what's going on and react properly if I can't even see my character? The final boss fight is especially guilty of this.
4) Auto-fire & Auto-dash - Some classes do not have this and it's a pain for the wrist/hands to play them.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is nice and all, but something about difficulty need to be addressed (to see where I'm coming from, I have beaten the game's hardest difficulty and unlock all 6 master skins):
1. Too many knock back, and getting back up just to be knock back again is getting tedious.
2. On top of that, some enemies (looking at you, some jumping assassins) are really annoying: they teleport to you out of nowhere, especially if you are in the fog area and knock you back near permanently, or rolling with F1 speed from the top of the map that you couldn't see on the screen and slamming right into you, knocking you back.
3. Some relic options are just strictly better than others in certain classes, namely blue dragon spirit and spirit jade for Infinite Arhat (or I may just be not experimenting enough).
4. Again, knock back. Bosses combo seems unfair, namely the A Qing and a sword man duo: the sword man telegraph a skill that can knock you back, so you have to dodge the line, but immediately after dodging, A Qing throw her weapon on you to catch you, leaving very little room for mistake.

Other than that, the theme is great, I've been enjoying the lore, the world and the gameplay. 8/10 in this state.
