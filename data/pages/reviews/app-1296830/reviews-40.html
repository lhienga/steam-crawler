\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL:DR, game fun, decent value, don't plan on wanting to play it for "fun runs." 3.5/5

This game is a lot of fun to play, and there's a lot of depth to the gameplay that really sucks you in. I'm very glad that I played this game. The translation is a bit off at times, and there are some polish issues. If I had to say this feels more like an early access game than a full release. I've fully gotten my money's worth out of this game though.

That being said, as someone who has cleared all of the difficulty levels; The more I play this game the less I want to play it. Higher difficulty quickly become hordes of damage sponges, only allowing for specific builds/playstyles/aspects to be successful. Worse yet, coming back to lower difficulties and trying to run specific builds for fun doesn't work very well either, as even if you get the items/skills that you want (which is unlikely to be frank) you're unlikely to ever get to "a full build."

My #1 complaint right now is the blacksmith. It feels really bad to pour 2k+ into trying to rank up your weapon only to have it end up at a lower level than what you started with. I don't know what the "failure" chances are when forging a weapon, but I feel like at the moment it is way to high. I would either remove the failures causing your weapon dropping a tier, or improve the odds that forging will be a success.\t\t\t\t\t
