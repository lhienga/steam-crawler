\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's missing some key quality of life items for me:
1. You can't use arrow keys to move. Games that have WASD allow us left-handers to use arrow keys also, or otherwise allow key rebinding. Dev said they would look into adding it in a January 2022 comment, but seems they haven't bothered in the end.
2. No 'gore off' option. Everyone you kill leaves a huge splat of blood on the ground. I like to reduce gore when playing because my youngest son will often watch me play games.

Aside from that, I wasn't keen on the item matching bonuses. This card does X effect if you have exact Y card. This feels pretty restrictive. Normally it would be you get a bonus if you have Y type of card to allow more open synergies, not naming a single specific card you have to come across.

On the plus side, I did like that you had a range ability by default in addition to melee ability - although so many effects were focused around 'if you do a melee attack after a ranged attack' or vice versa - so the game was heavily pushing that I had to use both rather than letting me focus on one.
I also felt that the roguelike permanent progression was somewhat attractive to keep me playing, although fairly basic benefits.\t\t\t\t\t
