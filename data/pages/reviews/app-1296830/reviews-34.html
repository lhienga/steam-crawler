\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very refreshing take on the rogue-like experience. Great art, simple, and satisfying mechanics. A few minor problems here and there, like the black smith being able to give lower grade weapons in higher difficulties. (I know that very well may be by design, it's just very annoying. I think it would be better if it adapted to the difficulty like the drops are.)

Edit: I'm stuck on blizzard difficulty, and feel not every class is viable on higher difficulties. The 4 best classes for the higher difficulties I've noticed are White Frost, Swift Flash, and either of the newer Buddha sub classes because of having no flying swords. One personal gripe is I've completed multiple runs and some how never found one note for the Hope Admits Despair achievement, specifically the second note needed.

Although the new dlc is very fun, and seems more for the speed runner type of players. The new relics can be considered better versions of others in some ways while having distinct play differences. The new skills in nightmare mode make the Seven Sword Sura Sect, the Ahrat Sect, and the new Buddhist Sect alot more versatile while trying to make a build.\t\t\t\t\t
