\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the better rougelikes out there. The art design is appealing, the sounds aren't bad, the combat is above average with some pretty nice bosses.
The best part about the game is the wide variety of builds that actually work at endgame. There are a lot of different sects to play, each with their own theme and mechanics and each having a melee and ranged variant that may be a little - or a whole lot - different. To add to that, even if you build a melee poison this run, there are multiple swords that can synergize with it each with a different unique passive.
One thing to note: the last boss has a rather annoying one shot mechanic - get to a random circle in time, while slowed, or die. Building speed doesn't help, but just using melee attack lets you easily get there, since that movement isn't affected by the massive slow. It felt real cheap to die to that.\t\t\t\t\t
