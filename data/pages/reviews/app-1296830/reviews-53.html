Otherwise really nice and solid game. Great work developers. Beautiful fluid art, fast paced fun.

Game is completely a giant troll. I mean that. The bosses are all complete pranksters.

At any point you think you have won. There's always another 3 phases to a boss and they just don't die.
They will probably one shot you out of nowhere. Always keep this in mind.

Clear inspiration from Hades, which people say a lot. - Dark Souls, Sekiro and Nioh.
Warm Snow feels like the Tale of Immortal version / rogue-like take on these types of games, all the while staying true to it's Chinese Mythology and Wooshoo/cultivation aesthetic.

Very satisfying combat and dynamic flashy moves in the way of skills/books and weapons bringing powers called Excalibur. It is not King Arthur's legendary mythic sword. But more of a unique passive that each weapon has, and effects attached that also sync/work with the items you pick as core moves, your dash, power, or utility, etc.

Makes for the fun and experimental creative side of the game.

Originally I was sceptical about this game, but I downloaded and played the free demo, upon completing it I enjoyed it and bought the game. No regrets. Good game.

Besides the common bugs & the time slow effects which I found way more annoying than helpful or "cool matrix effect."
It's still taking me time to get used to the slow.

Really solid awesome rogue-like.

Please put the slow time in options of gameplay settings because it kills the fast pace fluid combat, that's otherwise smooth and usually kills me! When things were going smooth! All because out of nowhere my character slows, so I can't react in-time properly and it's more like: wtf okay? stop this?

A good example is taking away the controller from me like an annoying big brother.

Anyways, I look forward to future updates and releases! Good work Developers!\t\t\t\t\t
