\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"I hate that game. I play it every day."

I feel like this quote pretty well embodies the community surrounding this game. I fell in love with Bungie because of Halo 2. My brother had turned me on to Halo 1 and I put in plenty of hours, but Halo 2 is what sealed the deal. Bungie has a knack at creating incredibly detailed and story telling environments. The set pieces themselves tell a story.

Destiny 2 tells some great stories, but lets be real. You're here for the gun play. The gunplay in this game is unrivaled, and, like Halo, sets it apart from everything else on the market.

If you can avoid treating this game like a grind filled part time job (Datto has a great video about this) it can be a lot of fun. Especially recently, the developers have been very responsive to the community and reduced the grind and increased the ability for your friends to join you. There is a STEEP learning curve to this game, but in the end it's worth it. Nothing beats the feeling of completing a raid with your friends. No other game provides collaborative high challenge group gaming like Destiny.

Simply put, there is nothing out there like this game when it comes to gunplay, collaborative gaming, and high level challenges in the PvE FPS environment. Take breaks and make sure you don't become one of the online ghouls that have lost their soul to the grind and now haunt the community.\t\t\t\t\t

The new updates are a joke considering you're paying what a new game would be worth and all you're getting is a battle pass and a new dungeon(one mission). Sometimes the new content is a raid or a new campaign, but nowadays they're all grind fests instead of being a difficult mission like the Outbreak Perfected mission and Whisper of the Worm mission. Now its silly events that just force you to play a lot instead of pursuing a challenge. Even the recycled content they barely tweak to make more challenging.

Its also a necessity to use meta weapons that have meta rolls on them or you'll be guaranteed to lose a fight. While also weapons like Cloudstrike that one hit anyone nearby when another player is sniped and the infamous le monarqe meta when people would sit in one spot and almost one hit just based off poisoning. Much more is broken for PvP especially when you start pairing two broken guns or a broken gun and armor piece together. I was a Top 1% player in trials and it got to the point of boring because it would be the same fight in PvP every time. PVE is the same though, you will be kicked from a group unless you use meta weapons and setups. It even got to the point where if you weren't a Starfire Protocol warlock you would never find a raid.
Realistically, you're paying hundreds of dollars to have cool emotes/skins and to use the same weapons/armor over and over again. The raids and missions you'll do will take days to weeks just to get one weapon because the RNG system in place for loot. You will waste time and money on this game because that's what this game was designed for, RNG system forces players to play something 100x over just to get one item and 100x more if you're trying for a specific roll. If this were reward based then this game would have died long ago since there's not much content to actually play.
Don't waste your time or money playing a game that doesn't offer you much content while stealing from your wallet and instead only offers to waste days upon days of your life trying to get one item(gun/armor/exotic/roll) that you should've gotten the first time you completed their content.\t\t\t\t\t
