\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, there is a lot to cover for this.... Let's start with the Pros
- A lot of very viable end game weapons can be farmed in the free version
- The actual DEVS (not management) care about the game and that shows with how the updates for bugs come out as well as the content they release for free such as the quarterly events every year
- The majority of the community is pretty chill in the PvE side of things
- Fun with friends
- Constantly on sale to counter a point i will make in the con list

Cons
- A lot of content is behind pay walls that have some more pay walls to get the rest of the content that should already be included in the original price. Example: Dungeon keys for expansions. These do not come with the DLC you purchase
- Content creators doing content creator things such as making everything a big deal acting like they'll quit or the game is dying (I do understand that the game is on a decline but for the first time in a long time the reasons are actually valid due to the lay offs and comments from previous devs saying the head honcho's don' t want them to over deliver)
- Lot of content is vaulted and not actually in the game, including DLC content

All in all, if you get the downloadable content at a discount (Epic has legacy for free right now) and have someone who has been playing for a while to help you understand more things story wise or how to play the content you bought and in what order, this game is an easy time sink of fun. Some parts get grindy, but hey, its an MMO.

I hope this was helpful and remember
