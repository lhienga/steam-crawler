If you are some kind of psychological codependent, and you're looking for that special narcissistic abuser in your life, then this game is for you!

All jokes aside (using the word joke lightly here), this game is not for everyone. Is it free? No, lol, not really. Is it well made? for the most part, yes. Is it fun? That is entirely up to you. This game caters primarily to the people who are already playing it heavily, and getting into the game for the first time is a monumental and unenviable task. You won't really know what this game is like until a few hundred hours in, and you'll be confused about the story for the entire time because about three years worth of story isn't even in the game anymore.

I don't recommend this game for new players, for returning players, or for current players even. I've met some amazing friends over the years by playing this game and Destiny 1, but I encourage you to find greener grass. Maybe some actual grass while you're at it.\t\t\t\t\t
