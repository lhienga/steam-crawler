\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny has had its ups and downs, but with the peak that was The Witch Queen, I was hyped, and hopeful for the future.

Sadly, Lightfall was an abysmal disappointment, in every aspect. Narrative, loot, gameplay. The servers remain unstable quite frequently, the new characters introduced have been insufferable, in addition to tearing down old characters. The guns are constantly recycled frames, with no interesting new perks on the horizon, all of this while you are nickel-n-dimed further while each season/expansion contains less and less content. And Bungie and their PR team do not care to address this or promise anything else. Season of the Witch and The Final Shape hold no improvements, but hope to reel you in with 'memberberries like Cayde and Eris (ruining Eris btw).

Instead of addressing the misgivings the community has about microtransactions, abandonment and intentional lack of quality; the team would rather promise you PvP maps, non-MTX armor sets (which they had already promised and then neglected) I'll believe it when I see it.

Your game is overflowing with microtransactions, and even the $100 bundle for the new expansion doesn't get you the new dungeon? Sorry, you can now put your disabilities in your Bungie profile so you can be matchmade with other disabled people.

The game has become a joke, and the community as well for not speaking out.\t\t\t\t\t
