\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a fast paced shooting MMO RPG style game with plenty to do. It is a matured game with many different progression systems and various playlists for PvP, PvE, and even mixed PvE/PvP events. Destiny 2 will keep you engaged with their weekly rotation of dungeons, raids, and other playlists. There are also events that appear less frequently but in a defined timeline such as trials. The downside to Destiny 2 is the learning curve. Some systems built into the game add some complexity that is not necessarily needed. The different planets are themed, immersive, and offer different rewards for completing them which will be worth your time. Graphically this game is amazing and I can appreciate the time that the developers have put into maintaining a game of this scale and offering a fairly stable experience for the amount of code that goes into such a behemoth of a game. Unfortunately the developers have seemed to err on the side of PvE which has caused some heartburn with the PvP community, which is a shame because it is hard to find a really balanced game that can make both PvE and PvP players happy alike. If there was a game that could pull this off I think Destiny 2 could be the game, if they invested some more cycles into PvP. The last downside to the game is the extensive monetization of events, their store, and season passes. I am aware this is common but Destiny 2 seems to do this on a larger scale than other games that are similar to this one. All in all if you are willing to put the time, effort, and investment for the DLCs then Destiny 2 will provide you with hours of fun and fantastic memories. Definitely worth a giving this awesome game a try.\t\t\t\t\t
