Original Review:
This game is quite possibly my favorite game of all time, though it is a love-hate relationship.

Destiny 2 in Universe, Concept, Art styles (yes, I mean plural), Gun-play, ability comfort, build-crafting, CUSTOMIZATION, world building, UI design, atmosphere, and most of all MUSIC, is masterclass.

You can customize an unbelievable amount in not only cosmetics but also build-crafting. (There's a good reason why there is an entire sub-fan base for this game that's just fashion.)

The Music is some of the best I've ever heard, some of it even graced by the man behind the Halo soundtrack itself, Michael Salvatori. There is unironically more than an hour's worth of high quality, unique music that came out of this game.

BuuuUuuUuut... the monetization of the game is atrocious, there is no argument against this fact. It's fine for DLC to be priced as it is (Unless it's Lightfall), year passes and stuff like that. However, Silver pricing is pretty steep, even for a "free to play" game such as this. On top of this, the fact that Dungeons are priced at 20 USD for 2 and not just 12 USD for 1 included in the season pass is just greedy.
It pains me to say that this is one of the worst examples of cashcowing in games, period.

On top of that, there are a plethora of development issues with the game that plague the community such as gamemodes like Gambit being all but abandoned, Various issues that could only be attributed to laziness/ time crunch, and a variety of other things I won't mention.

But even with it's flaws, I've been playing since 2019 (Shadowkeep) and participating in the emotional roller coaster that is this franchise every step of the way. (I think over 2,000 hours says it all) I can't stress enough how much this game has impacted me, so much so that for a while I even wanted to work with Bungie themselves... That ship has all but sailed.

Destiny 2 is a fantastic game made for a specific brand of ADHD brain with a horrible monetization scheme to back it. TONS of love is put into the game by artists, composers, programmers, and the like, but the higher-ups tarnish it's reputation by making poor decision after poor decision.

For those like me- 11/10, for those who want a great experience and aren't familiar with the game- 4/10.\t\t\t\t\t
