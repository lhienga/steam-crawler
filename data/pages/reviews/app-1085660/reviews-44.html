I WOULD STRONGLY ADVISE YOU TO NOT INVEST TIME OR MONEY INTO THIS FRANCHISE

I am really very sad to be leaving a negative review on what has at points been my favourite video game ever.

Great developer team that has been absolutely let down by the heads of the company. Sony acquisition hasn't helped anything. For years, Destiny 2 had the best communication on the market, spearheaded by dmg04 (Dylan Gafner) and the amazing community team. TWAB (This Week at Bungie) was deservedly a shining example for the rest of the industry.

This is a game that has been difficult to recommend for most of it's life, even after the base game was made "free to play", but I always felt that if you invested some money and time, that it was an experience unrivalled by many other similar games, especially with the company of friends.

2,000 hours isn't a lot compared to some of you, but this is easily my most played video game ever, and has regularly been my favourite game (although the quality of Destiny 2 has never been consistent for long, in my opinion).

I'm aware this may come across as melodramatic, but with betrayal after betrayal from the senior leadership of Bungie, my faith in the company has *never* been lower.

https://www.gamesradar.com/the-face-of-destiny-2s-community-dmg04-is-leaving-bungie/

^ This was a truly awful article to read. My deepest sympathies go out to all the workers and developers who laboured away in a famously crunchy industry, to provide us with a game that has brought so many people so much joy.

It feels like a childhood friend has moved to a new city.\t\t\t\t\t
