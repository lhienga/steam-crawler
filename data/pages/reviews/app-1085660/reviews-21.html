Bad for:
PvP, The absolute state of the "Oneshot" meta is atrocious in gaming. Bungie made Halo, they knew what good TTK felt like.
Story/Writing, the quality of D2 writing is like a rollercoaster between expansions.

Expensive, but what isn't nowadays.
Content Deletion, we no longer have access to a significant amount of old content. Filesize wouldn't be an issue if games were optimized like they used to be. Not saying all games do that, but Cod has been called out for it and I'm sure they're not the only one doing it intentionally.
