It's one of the best shooters of all time, wrapped around one of the worst anti-consumer practices portrayed by any developer or studio. It is not worth hundreds of dollars that they charge annually for season passes, expansions and all the content they've sliced up over the years that eventually become unplayable because the live service model won't allow for it. Older content is scraped, completely to be forgotten to time *unless* you were around at the time to experience it. The game itself? Once you get beyond all that egregious price gouging? It's one of the best shooters ever developed. It's been confusing, hard to follow and damn near impenetrable to understand. That doesn't matter anyways, the lore behind Destiny, while fascinating, never gets to fully explore any ideas past what Star Wars or similar Sci Fi archetypes haven't already done, but much better.

If you're looking for a quick triple A title to pick up and play with your friends, you better be ready for paywalls every 30 minutes unless you dump hundreds of dollars into the experience. Not recommended despite any praise I've given this franchise, it just isn't a title you can play without a subscription type model. It's too expensive of a game to enjoy properly, and it isn't for most gamers in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
