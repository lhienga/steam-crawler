\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have loved Destiny since before the first game even came out. Played both betas, watched all the trailers and ViDocs, pre-ordered both. I've always enjoyed both games for their gameplay and very interesting lore and worldbuilding. That said, this is still a negative review.

Some time ago, the devs decided that the best way forward was to completely remove the base game and first 2 or 3 expansions. There were lore reasons for not being able to play them anymore, the world state had changed kinda like Cata did for WoW, but it meant that new players were missing tens of hours of story and tons of lore. I know several people who won't get into the game because they can't play the whole game, even if it is free. After this, they then started doing seasons with a battlepass system and seasonal stories to play with associated items to upgrade and quests to follow. These too are short lived, so you miss lore and story if you take a break for a while.

At this point, the game is not only unfriendly to new players, but unfriendly to players who take any extended breaks.
