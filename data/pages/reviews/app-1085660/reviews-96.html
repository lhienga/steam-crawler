But I can't, in good conscious, recommend it to someone just starting out. D2 is a shadow of what it used to be. More than half the story has been gutted to remove bloat to keep the game download size smaller. It's still an enormous game with some great content, but what's there feels empty. If you're just starting as a New Light you have absolutely no idea what's going on or who the key players are. And all the biggest storylines happen in seasonal content that disappears after a few months, never to return.

I wish new people could have the experience I've had, but sadly it just doesn't exist anymore. I'm sticking it out for Final Shape, but only because at this point I feel obligated, because of the time and money I've put in, to see this through to the end.
If you don't care about story and lore or any of that and just want a looter/shooter that you don't have to pay attention to? Go for it.\t\t\t\t\t
