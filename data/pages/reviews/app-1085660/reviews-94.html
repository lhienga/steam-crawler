\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSince the release of Destiny 1, the franchise has enjoyed mountaintop highs and downright depressing lows. For all the time I've put into this game, a lot of it has been disappointing. But at least it hasn't just been a "whatever" game from the beginning. Destiny has one of the best stories of any MMO and is pound for pound the best feeling FPS ever made. Bungie has pushed to make a game that gives the player something new to do every few months while still offering a compelling loot hunt. They haven't always hit the nail on the head there, but at least they're trying new things. Personally, I think Destiny 2 is in a decent spot at the time of writing this review. It's not amazing, but it's certainly been worse. The biggest complaint I have is how the content is monetized. It costs a ridiculous amount of money to experience everything Destiny 2 has to offer right now. If Bungie makes substantial changes to how players access content alongside The Final Shape and changes to PvP and the seasonal system on the horizon, I think Bungie is poised to make Destiny 2 a truly great game again. If you're interested in trying out Destiny for the first time, I honestly recommend waiting until The Final Shape to jump in. Watch a few Byf videos on YouTube to catch up with the lore beforehand and enjoy the ride.\t\t\t\t\t
