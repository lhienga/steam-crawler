\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game used to be really good, on both the pvp and pve sides of it. But I feel as if ever since sunsetting took place, which made an otherwise grindy game even more whilst removing about 80% of its content, the game had been going on a downwards spiral of bad decisions. But as bad as sunsetting was, I'd argue that the shift to a "freemium" model might have been the worst decision Bungie had made as that inevitably led to all of those awful packs and bundles they charge you for almost as much as the latest DLC for the game (100 Euros for DLC and Season Pass AND 90 Euros for Legacy Collection + Armory Collection, which have about half of the content removed aside from the Exotics. What a joke!).

When you pair that with a lot of recycled content and loot from sunset activities from past seasons or straight out of the Content Vault, it really feels as if Bungie became creatively bankrupt with D2 and they are now milking the game's last few breaths before they pull the plug on it and focus on their next cash cow, which will be the Marathon reboot.
I will admit that this game has pulled me through some of my lowest lows I went through in my life, but the D2 from back then is not the same as the one from now. Will I be there when Final Shape drops? Yes. I will by watching videos of it because I no longer trust Bungie to do anything good to this game and I do not intend on spending any more than I already have on a game that should have died years ago, and I was stupid for not doing this earlier.

Do yourself a favor and please, for the love of God, don't get Destiny 2! All the cash you will spend on this game can go into something a lot more useful. Do not be like me and waste nearly 300 Euros on a game! Treat yourself to something nicer than this, you deserve a lot more!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat Shooter. Lots of great loot. Can be a lot of fun with friends. Graphics are near top-of-the-line. The story is not top tier, but not awful.

When the ads show to play the game free, you can. But that is mostly limited to some of the PVP, some of the Strike missions, and some other additions. If you play this game past a few hours, you will get hounded to purchase the new expansion (ads after missions, ads in menus, etc). You will be locked from accessing a big chunk of the current and fun content. You will not level anything close to the current pace if you had the expansion.

Bottom Line - Fun gameplay, replayable beyond belief, lots of loot (if you are into that kind of thing), good to play with friends. It is a game you will constantly pick up with the new expansion, then put it down shortly after until the next update.\t\t\t\t\t
