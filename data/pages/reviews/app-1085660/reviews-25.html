\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove this game, one of my favourites. I played the original Destiny with a clan and then moved onto Destiny 2 and have played all the DLC content. All the updates are good they release but I recommend getting some friends to play it with. Have tried on my own and got bored quickly.

You need to enjoy grinding for this game like most MMO but this is part of the fun. Raids are a great way to test you as a team and work together. Story line on Destiny has not always been the best and I do miss Cayde as he was my favourite but the game play itself will keep you entertained if you give it a chance.\t\t\t\t\t
