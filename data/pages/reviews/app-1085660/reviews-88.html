\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are a lot of reviews on the game with mind boggling numbers on record which are far better than this one; read them for details on the game. But if you are in the present year of 2023 and are contemplating if its worth it - then read ahead.

First things first, the game is free to play and you can clock a solid 10 hours of content before you run out of stuff to do and explore and will start craving for more grinds and more stories and worlds to explore. This is where Bungie starts with the microdosing of content and prods you to pay for the rest of the good stuff. From campaigns to dungeon keys everything good is now behind a paywall. This is where you will have the second dilemma. Is it worth it? Well some seasons are good but some are absolutely horrible like the latest one. But lets assume you take a deep breath and purchase a season; now what? Well you grind it out and you're back to square one. More seasons, more keys and before you know it you will spend money in the ♥♥♥♥♥♥ store for ♥♥♥♥♥♥ cosmetics.

My issues isn't with paying money for the DLCs and additional content. No, no no. Its with how Bungie has consistently degraded the game with subpar content and silently put out old missions and doesn't have a good enough server for the PVP mode. Its just sad that a game this old (7 years running) is now more of a cash grab, I honestly think they might be working on a Destiny 3 in the background and the last Season hopefully is the last of them milking the gamers for their money's worth. The game itself gets very repetitive if you have more than 300+ hours on the clock. Its always the same template at the end of the day with no variation. Not to mention, we need a serious update on the graphics to match up with 2023 standards of gaming.

For people playing this game for a while, there is no out. We will be the last guardians standing at the altar of Destiny 2 until the servers wind down. Simple reason - Sunk cost fallacy. Older players have too much time, money and effort put into this and they occasionally come back for seasons to play and spend money. For those thinking to buy this fresh and start in 2023 - DONT. Please dont. Its simply not worth the time and grind commitment. The enjoyability factor has considerably decreased and Bungie has already decided its not worth their money to update the game and its best they make a new one instead.\t\t\t\t\t
