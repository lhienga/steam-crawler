\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story, the world-building, the art style, and the gameplay are pure and utter perfection when it comes to looter shooters. The worlds are vast and interesting, the characters are deep and likable, and the character customization options are endless, but there is a very huge but.

The game on its own is holding on to the current player base, it is not for new players because they have vaulted all of the introductory content. That means that you can’t play the original 4 campaigns, campaigns that introduce characters, make you fall in love with them and the worlds, and tell you why the fight that you’re fighting is important. This includes the season content and the seasonal story quests that are hit or miss, but sometimes the seasonal stories are better than the stories from the core expansions, with great writing and character development moments that you won’t see because everything from the seasons is available just for a 3-month period.

I regret purchasing the Lightfall expansion and the annual pass. The hype was real and it was based upon great storytelling that happened within the seasonal stories, that no one will ever play again only because it is all vaulted.

Will I return for The Final Shape? Because of Cayde-6, yeah, probably, but this time I won’t invest in a seasonal pass and I kind of want to see how it all ends, if it will ever end…

TLDR: The game has way too much vaulted quality story content which makes it trivial for new players. Also, the gameplay loop feels like a chore.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 : By Triple F

I recently embarked on an interstellar journey like no other, courtesy of the spectacular masterpiece that is "Destiny 2". If you've ever dreamt of being a guardian of the galaxy, wielding incredible powers and exploring breathtaking landscapes, then this game is your cosmic ticket to an unforgettable adventure!

From the moment I stepped into the boots of my Guardian, I was captivated by the sheer beauty and scale of the "Destiny 2" universe. The attention to detail in the game's design is nothing short of extraordinary. Each planet, moon, and outpost is a visual marvel, offering a rich tapestry of environments, all with their own unique ambiance and challenges. Whether I was traversing the rugged, war-torn landscapes of the Tangled Shore or gazing in awe at the awe-inspiring structures of the Dreaming City, "Destiny 2" consistently left me in awe of its artistic brilliance.

But "Destiny 2" is not just about eye candy; it's a symphony of game-play mechanics that come together harmoniously. The combat system is nothing short of exhilarating. The array of weapons, gear, and abilities at your disposal allows for endless customization, making each firefight a thrilling and unique experience. The PvE and PvP modes both offer their own flavors of excitement, whether you're raiding with a squad of friends or testing your mettle in competitive Crucible matches.

One of the game's defining features is its storytelling, which has evolved dramatically since its launch. The Forsaken expansion, in particular, stands out as a narrative masterpiece. The depth of the characters, the intrigue of the plot, and the emotional weight of the story kept me on the edge of my seat. It's a testament to Bungie's dedication to delivering a compelling and immersive universe.

"Destiny 2" also shines in its sense of community. The game encourages cooperation and teamwork like no other. Joining up with fellow Guardians for strikes, raids, and other activities is a social experience like no other, and it's through these bonds that some of the most memorable moments in Destiny 2 are forged.

Furthermore, the game's live service model keeps things fresh and exciting. Frequent content updates, seasonal events, and the promise of future expansions mean that "Destiny 2" is a game that keeps on giving. Bungie's commitment to evolving and improving the game based on player feedback is evident and commendable.

Now, let's talk about the soundtrack. Destiny 2's music is a symphonic masterpiece that elevates every moment of the game. Whether you're exploring the mysterious depths of the Pyramidion or facing down a formidable raid boss, the music never fails to enhance the atmosphere and your emotional connection to the game.

In conclusion, "Destiny 2" is a breathtaking odyssey that seamlessly blends stunning visuals, exhilarating game-play, a compelling narrative, and a vibrant community. It's a game that constantly evolves and surprises, ensuring that no matter when you enter its universe, you'll find something extraordinary to experience. "Destiny 2" isn't just a game; it's a space-faring epic, and every Guardian's journey is an unforgettable story waiting to be told. Don't miss out on this cosmic adventure—it's a masterpiece that sets the bar for the entire gaming industry.\t\t\t\t\t
