\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I loved Destiny and have been off/on with Destiny 2 through it's life, I just can't recommend the game anymore. Disregarding the news around Bungie layoffs/drama at the time of writing this, the game just feels like a shell of it's former self. I'm not going to list everything good and bad about the game in my opinion. If you want a good synopsis then I suggest checking out the review by Daddy Muffin. I think they truly nailed what has irked most of the playerbase in recent years.

For my 2 cents, the game feels like it's on the wrong track overall. While there has been decent/good content put out it's overshadowed, in my opinion, by the general feeling that the game is no longer being driven by a leadership that cares about the game being good over pure profit margins. This feels like the, now all too common, inflection point for studios where they slowly become purely profit driven. The devs still care, you can tell that in the game and on socials, but overall the company feels like it's on the Blizzard/Bioware/EA/etc path to being hated by the wider gaming community.

I hope that Bungie steers the ship back on course but after the past 2 years I personally have lost faith. If they manage to turn things around then I'll update this review to fairly reflect it.\t\t\t\t\t
