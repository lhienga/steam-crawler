\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's one of those games where if you're in it, you're in it til the end.
However, the game is terrible for newcomers. There are so many additional content that you would never know what you are allowed to play unless you ask a veteran, and even they might not know.
PVE part of the game has always been amazing. The best gunplay mixed with fun abilities. The devs do mess with the subclasses/cooldowns/skills/weapons to change the meta here and then, and that may be disagreeable to some. Raids are a blast, and no one should go into this game unless they plan to play the endgame.
Now PVP on the other hand, is a mess. I know this isn't meant to be a competitive FPS game, but there are no semblance of balance anywhere. Since this is a looter shooter, the sheer number of weapons that one might not have access to further exacerbates this issue. Oh, and since the PVP playerbase is absolutely abysmal, the matchmaking simply doesn't work. Map spawns suck also.
Now, Bungie is working on making huge sweeping changes in the PVP sandbox to alleviate this, and we'll see how that goes, but I'm not holding my breath.

To summarize.
Story? Go play anything else.
PVE with cool gunplay and fun superpower abilities? Yes please.
PVP, nah.
Oh, gambit, how could I forget. JK everyone forgot about gambit, even Bungie.
