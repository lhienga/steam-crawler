Tons of lore, weapons, and scenic locations that was made all the better for players that played with friends. Post-Lightfall, the game has gone downhill. It is very unfriendly to new players, both with the excess of quests and the high price point just to get +5 year old content and the absolute investment of the Eververse store. Only one time did they ever make what was originally was an Eververse armor set as an in-game item that can be earned, and that was only because of the backlash.

They popped off with Witch Queen, they flopped with Lightfall, I pray that they at least tie Destiny's story, as a game and franchise, in a decent enough bow. I've been here since Destiny 1 Year 2 and it hurts to see how far Destiny as a franchise has come down. Bungie is truly riding on a make or break with The Final Shape.

If you willing to spend money on this game, I'd recommend waiting until after The Final Shape drops. Hope for the best, prepare for the worst.\t\t\t\t\t
