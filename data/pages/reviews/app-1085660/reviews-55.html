i took a longer break, but recently got the itch to check it out again, only having heard that the writing in the latest expansion was...rough
i'm not playing d2 for the story, so that didn't bother me too much.
what does bother me however is that to run the dungeon of the most recent expansion, you don't just need to buy the expansion...no, you need to additionally buy the dungeon key for another 20‚Ç¨ which would have been nice to know before buying the expansion itself
sure you can pay a 100‚Ç¨ a year and have everything, including every season pass that year, but this isn't about what you get and what you don't...it's the fact that the basic parts of an expansion (i.e. the dungeon) are hidden behind an additional paywall which, surprise surprise, forces you to interact with the ingame store, because you can only buy it with the premium currency.
but of course there's no pack that gives you the right amount, only slightly too much or not enough, opening a whole other can of worms, which is mostly cosmetics though, so i won't touch on that any further

tl;dr:
destiny is unfortunately no longer the game it used to be and to be completely honest, i would not recommend this husk of a franchise to anyone anymore.
i sincerely hope they reconsider their monetisation model, because it really was a one of a kind experience with so much potential, ruined by presumably greed or whatever other corporate buzzwords may come to mind
