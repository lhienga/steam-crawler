\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlay HBG with 4 shields and maximum defence stats, or abuse Clutch claw to the maximum if you're melee. TBH, you're probably better off not buying this and just playing the base game if you wanna play melee at all. The DLC has been created to basically destroy melee viability in favor of ♥♥♥♥♥♥ clutch claw cheese tactics. You wanna play Hammer? Too bad, everything moves at mach 5, hits like a truck and has almost no openings.
The tutorial for the Clutch claw says to use it when it's tired. I've yet to see an iceborne monster that actually gets tired and stops for more than 2-5 seconds. Barely enough time to even attempt to approach it, let alone try to clutch claw to it's head with the wonky controls it has. Plus all of them have small, quick moves that punish clutch clawing attempts too. So the one mechanic you don't really wanna use cause it's horrible to use but you're forced to, also gets punished. But if you try to play without it, you may not be able to even kill monsters or touch them. They just don't stop moving around. People like to pretend like there's "nuance" and that it's not simply artificial difficulty to pad gametime. There is no nuance in forcing you to do one of two things - clutch claw spam or HBG.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIceborne is a fantastic follow up to the base game, with a new story, returning and new monsters, and so much stuff, it's amazing.
Ignore the people whining about Fatalis and Alatreon, just git gud and slay the punks.
The slap on designs still carry on from World to Iceborne, even butchering some established monster weapon designs such as Glavenus(this guy I will get to...), Nargacuga, and Brachydios. It really irks me that they bring back some good monsters but yet they couldn't bring in all 14 weapon types.
Namielle is my favorite Elder Dragon(Nargacuga is my favorite non-elder dragon), when I first saw this monster, I wanted to have all of his weapons. That beautifully colored monster would have some fantastic weapons if they didn't shaft it with having a few unique designs, then bone weapons with skin taped on it, and even not having some weapons like great sword and hammer. I really, REALLY hope that this god-awful feature is entirely dropped in Rise.
Glavenus: A monster that I would not have at all expected to get in Iceborne/World due to it's design, but he got in along with a sub-species! Glavenus that was screwed with having some of it's weapons being bone/metal with parts on it. But one thing that sits on my mind, why isn't Astalos and Gammoth in Iceborne? I understand about the leviathan issue thing, but they sould at least but those two in. Gammoth would've been perfect, and Astalos is such a uniquely designed flying wyvern.
Iceborne is worth the purchase. See you guys in Rise!\t\t\t\t\t
