\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm surprised at how a Monster Hunter game could be pay to win, but here it is! The patient zero.

So now people who owns the DLC gets the all new and powerful Clutch Claw. You can grapple- oh I mean *clutch* on to a monster and either beat the hell out of em with your weapon or launch them into walls or other monster to really show them that daddy's home. This feature is so important that if you don't have it, it'll make your hunts roughly 75% longer. Thanks Capcom, you're the best!

Other than the clutch claw, there is absolutely no reason for you to get the DLC. Of course fans of this game is going to tell you otherwise, that it's a "fun, new and exciting world! Perfect for all new comers!" it is absolutely not. The developers decided that to make a new content they simply copy the monster, give it a bit of different coloring, and voila a new monster that'll make fans of the game simply COOM at the thought. To make matters worse, their whole formula to make a game more challenging or difficult is simply "more health, more damage, more speed." and nothing else.

Not to mention that some monsters have one-shot mechanics, and not the ones like The Behemoth's where it tells you a powerful attack will come, no no no, that would be too easy! This one is pretty much a normal attack, it simply happens and your hunter falls to the ground, leaving you scratching your head as to decipher "what in the sapphire star just happened?"

So if you're new to the series and you want to extend your journey in the new world, then please look elsewhere for this is not for newcomers, casual players, or functioning members of society. This is for the die-hard fans of the game who can't get enough of the game and wants nothing more than cheaply re-used content.

For the love of god, don't make the same mistake I did.\t\t\t\t\t
