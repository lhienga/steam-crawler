\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI see a lot of negative reviews complaining about performance and mechanics. I have played this game for hundreds of hours and never had performance issues. There has been some online connectivity issues but honestly I can chalk a lot of that up to my internet. It has been spotty the past few months.

As far as mechanics that I see people complaining about, they all have ways to deal with them built into the game. They just haven't learned how to do them properly. There are usually more than one way to deal with each problem.

I see a lot of hate on the clutch claw and some of it is fair. However, the clutch claw is more of a personal opinion thing. The claw takes some getting used to, but its not bad once you learn it. I have done relatively short hunts without the use of the claw but it does make hunts easier.

At the end of the day this game is amazing. Tons of new content for not a lot of money. Lots of new monsters. Twists on old monsters. New gear and skill changes. The game is just a blast.\t\t\t\t\t
