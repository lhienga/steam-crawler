Oh well... where to even start.

First, Capcom hasn't learned from trash design desicions they made in World.

* Bloated HP values for monsters in Master Rank. A quick comparison:
HP for G-rank Yian Kut-Ku in GU: 7,904 ~ 8,360 HP.
HP for Beotodus in Iceborne: 14,616 HP.

HP for G-rank Valstrax in GU: 10,847 ~ 11,424 HP.
HP for Velkhana in Iceborne: 23,040 HP.

Not only that... monster parts hp values also have been bloated beyond belief. Safi's head has 26k hp. 26. Thousand. Health. Points.

More HP =/= challenge. When. WIll. You. Learn. Capcom.

* Clutch claw. While the idea seems cool at the start... the implementation is far from cool. It's bad. Really bad. Not only you're forced to use it(you do want that sweet +20% damage boost, right?), but you also have to deal with horrible, annoying moveset of most 'new'(ie those who weren't in base World) monsters that cancel the clutch claw... that's like giving the player new tool and then making it useless/annoying right after that. And the cherry on top is: you can't use clutch claw + wall slam combo when monsters are engraged... and now they're enraged most of the time. And as the fight drags on non-engraged status is almost non-existent. Trash design at its finest.

* More trash RNG. Not only there are many more decos bloating overall deco pool, but also you've got a trash RNG system of Safi weapons. Remember Kulve weapons farming? Wasn't it fun? No, it wasn't, it was trash. Now you've got to get through more RNG trash if you want specific set bonus or extra deco slot. This is trash design at its finest.

* You're forced to go into multiplayer if you want to have fun or you have to use specific weapons against certain monsters. The moveset of many monsters is just trash for melee single player.

* Grinding lands. Who the hell came up with this trash? The idea itself is kinda cool, but the layout of grinding lands is absolutely horrible cluster♥♥♥♥. And the fact that you can't level up all areas to max level... who came up with this ♥♥♥♥??

* Remember Health augment from World? The one that heals you proportionately to damage you deal to a monster? Well, now you can't access it before MR 100! Not only that, you have to level Coral area of Grinding lands to max and fight Tempered Namielle there for the ingridients! This is just trash.

* Terrible flagship monster. Capcom, when will you learn, that cheap monster moves =/= difficulty. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like staring at your screen as you die without any recourse what so ever, you might actually like this. Taking control away from the player is a blight of a "mechanic" that shouldn't be in any game. They took the worst parts of the base game and turned them up to 11. The balance in this DLC is absolutely atrocious.

Monsters have twice the health of their base-game counterparts minimum, and fights start lasting between 30 minutes and 1 hour at times. It plays like the developers did not understand their own game, but as if they have never balanced a game in their entire life. It stands in complete contrast to the base game where fights were most often short and sweet.

In Iceborne the fights are all over the place. It has both the best and by far the worst designed fights in it. It is here where you will find monsters that constantly fly, monsters that will not allow you to weaken them, or monsters that will never stop writhing (which hurts you). It is here where you will find monsters which leave little to no gap in their patterns and will constantly throw some sort of debuff on you, or outright take control away from you with screams, tremors, paralysis and stuns. It is completely unfun to fight any of these monsters.

Monster takes 1 step? Time to heal the 80% health you just lost. Monster screams? Half your health goes away, and you get stunned. You land a hit? Time to watch the monster run around for 10 minutes until it calms down enough for your next hit. You actually have plenty of opening to attack? Enjoy whaling away for the next 30 minutes until it finally falls over. Want to engage in the clutch-claw mechanic to weaken the monster to deal reasonable damage? Well... you just lost 50% of your health grabbing on.. oh, and you've been immediately thrown off before being able to do anything.

Just avoid this and play the base game if you actually like the base game... This DLC completely sours the fun if flawed experience that the base game provides.\t\t\t\t\t
