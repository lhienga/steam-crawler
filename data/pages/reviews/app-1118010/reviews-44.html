\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat addition to the base game. Only one new map which is disappointing but looks good and has some fun interactions. Good monster roster and variety, I enjoy the variations here more than just a harder apex fight like you get in Rise. Safi raids are really fun, Altereon I didn't care for with the hard dps check and elemental changing, Raging Bracy and Furious Rajang are a great mixture of super difficult and fun. Fatalis you could use an essay to describe the fight, on the one hand it's a masterful experience with the level design, the phases, the animations, everything just comes together to make an unforgettable experience. The other side is I think it's tuned too hard and is too difficult to the point of being unfun. I spent more time learning/preparing for this fight than I did all of base world to put things in perspective. The guiding lands while I think is a great concept is just horribly executed in the inability to max out all the zones and the grinding is very heavy for augmenting out multiple different weapons. Fatalis armor is amazing with the skills you need and the multitude of decoration slots that you actually have some gear and build variations, I wish the game was like this from the start instead of being forced into certain sets/builds that are clearly the best. I really like the end game deco farm, you can get your build 90% of the way there and then when you get a great rarity 12 gem you can adjust things around to become a little closer to perfection. Some of the drop rates are terrible and could easily have 10x their current drop rates though, i'm looking at you attack +2. I like the weapon augment system but wish the costs were lower and it was available from the base game instead of very end game.

Overall it's a great game. I liked rise the least, then world, then Iceborne. You finally get the build customization I was hoping for, although it comes very late, and a lot of the endgame loops are a lot of fun so highly recommend this if you enjoyed World.\t\t\t\t\t
