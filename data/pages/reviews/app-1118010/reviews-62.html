You'll see other people say "This is where the game begins" and that's very accurate. It add so much depth, and really makes the game shine. Most of the negative reviews seem to be from launch bugs, but I've seen a couple of somewhat recent posts complaining about bugs and whatnot as well. GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1660 SUPER
CPU: AMD Ryzen 3 3100 4 core processor
RAM: 8GB RAM
Nothing particularly strong

(TAD BIT OF SPOILER) (POSITIVES :D)
This DLC is basically a whole other MHW game and you can get to truly experience what it means to
have end games items in this game, fighting multiple new monsters which prove to be really hard on multiple occasions but hey! That's the fun of the game. There is also the guiding lands that is basically a region on the map inclusive of ALL regions except the Elder's Recess which is fun if you are able to have some other friends tag along and see how fast y'all can kill a monster and some other fun ideas. You will definitely enjoy the new mission and the new story line.

(NEGETIVES ( ._.) )
However there are also drawbacks to this game no matter how great it is one of them which I can think off of the top of my head is the difficulty of the monsters as I said previously, as it IS rewarding to beat the monster you probably had gained PTSD from, namely Alatreon and Fatalis in which I have yet to beat Fatalis *sigh*. After beating Alatreon, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ did it feel great, BUT it took me a solid week and a few days to rework how I play the game AND mechanics to fit the hunt for Alatreon which may be fun to some people I guess. (P.S this is a review from a perspective of someone that never played any other Monster Hunter games, so I'm definitely not a veteran hunter)

Overall, I would recommend to a LARGEEE extent to get this DLC on a sale. It is a HUGE expansion to the MHW, but there will be no additional events as they have stopped updating it and the events would be on a cycle so there would be nothing you will be missing if you do not get it now. Spend time being immersed in this game and just enjoy yourself. This game gave me so many memories and so much fun, and would probably go down in my books as the BEST RPG game I will probably ever play in my life\t\t\t\t\t
