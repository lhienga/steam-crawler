\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIceborne is the natural evolution to MH:W. Everything that worked before is here, being done again, sharpened and improved. Some of the negative reviews here speak the truth, some don't. Here's some popular feedback and my thoughts on them:

"The game is grindier": It isn't, it's as "grindy" as the base game, which it wasn't. All of my armor sets were acquired in 2~3 investigations, and the fights are enjoyable and varied enough to not feel tiring. This is as grindy as eating pizza is grindy: sure, there's lots of slices you need to "grind" through, but is it really a grind if you enjoy every bit and save some for tomorrow if necessary? In other words, if you don't enjoy hunting monsters, then you shouldn't play monster hunter.

"The enemies are health sponges": They aren't. They buffed the enemy health pool but also added new weapons with higher attack values, as well as the ability to add weakpoints to enemies on demand + the ability to crowd control them and bash them against walls for almost a thousand damage. So yeah, they're tankier, but as long as you occasionally forge a weapon or two and actually use the clutch claw it is NOT a problem (most of my hunts are in the 19~24 minute range, which I think was standard for the first game).

"The enemies have unfair attacks with unfair blights": At some point you fight an enemy that causes Sleep as often as Bazelgeuse causes explosions. By the time I fought him, I had 17 Pep Jewels, and I only needed 3 to become immune to sleep. Also, its ability to cause sleep is warned beforehand as you unlock it. This is like unlocking Teostra and going after it with a -15 Fire Res armor, getting blasted and complaining the game is too hard. What's the point of decorations if you don't wear them?

"Most of the enemies are reskins": Totally valid criticism. The campaign starts with new content, and then there's a huge stretch of re-skinned enemies almost all the way to the end, and only there (and postgame) you see new enemies again. It's... kinda lame.

"Denuvo destroyed my FPS": Totally valid criticism. There's indeed a CPU-reliant FPS drop in this game.

Overall, many of the negative reviews are from underprepared hunters who refused to learn new mechanics and ended up being crushed by new monsters. I'm brand new to Monster Hunter (this is my first MH ever) and me and my significant other blazed through the campaign with no difficulty whatsoever.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey took a beautiful game and trashed it. This expansion is so bad it should have never been made. The idea is great, I love the thought of new monsters and a new story and the guiding lands is a great addition. The execution was absolute cockwater . Ramping up monsters to be bullet sponges with bazookas is not how you create difficulty. The whole master rank is a joke. Monsters become enraged within 1 minute of fighting it, and it lasts forever, meaning 90% of your fight will just be running and dodging, while 10% is time to actually do some damage. Hitboxes are janky af, its almost like the developers just said it i dont want to do this ill just put a giant box around the monster and thats where the damage will be dealt. Honestly, its like you cant even look at a monster without getting hurt. And the clutch claw. The clutch claw. This thing was a cool concept and I was excited for it when the game was coming out, but my god this thing plays awfully. Its got no range, you have to basically be hugging a monster to use it, the tracking is ass, gotta fire it multiple times before it connects to the part im not even looking at. And the fact that literally anything and everything can knock you off is so lame. The monster knocks you off with every attack, even just walking. Other players can knock you off. Even the little tiny monsters just chillin can knock you off. I mean honestly what the were they thinking when they were implementing this thing into the game.

Basically the new game mechanics are straight ass and it ruins the whole monster hunter experience.

Also the story makes no sense. ICE-borne? the "final boss" takes place in a sandy desert island, and the boss man is a rock monster. Velkhana is the flagship monster for this expansion, the promo monster shown everywhere to advertise, and its not even the main boss? wtf bro this is ass backwards. And then even after that, to unlock more endgame , you are just fighting a weaker nergigante??? Cmon bruh elementary school kids can make better stories than this.

Game mechanics suck. Story is boring and makes no sense and also sucks. Basically nothing good came out of this expansion except for the learning experience, so that they know that this is not how you make a game.

Dont buy this expansion. My friend and I just get as much high tier as we can and just go back to low rank and kill monsters there its honestly so much fun. Waste of 40 bucks. Base game is perfect.\t\t\t\t\t
