\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game but... terrible optimization leading to high cpu usage. Clutch claw keybinds can't ever be the same with ranged and melee weapons for some reason...(keybinds for mouse & keyboard are brutal in general even if you change them) And server disconnects are slightly better than MHW base game launch.

And with all these issues the game is still one of the best games I've played in my life which says a lot. Provides a lot of "wow" moments that just feel so good like smashing a dragon out of the sky with a giant sword.

I see a lot of reviews saying that some monsters make you switch your elements to be effective and they just want to play how they want to play. Even reading that now makes it seem ridiculous. The game is about mixing up your builds and skills to optimize to kill boss monsters. So to them I say, "git gud son."

I don't remember the anti-cheat thing causing massive issues but I do remember having the lower graphics at some point randomly so maybe that's it.

In summary, this game is great, some of the systems are bad/ported poorly but still one of the best games I've played in my life (in my 30s). GLHF

PS Play the base game first before buying this.\t\t\t\t\t
