\r\n\t\t\t\t\t This DLC package broke more than it fixed.

I could go into how the clutch claw was a bad idea implemented in a really strange way, or how the game's economy got busted by the steamworks, but that is all small potatoes compared to just what an unplayable, buggy mess this thing is. Even if I discount the really bizarre events that broke the game for several days, such as when an update in March rendered the game un-launchable due to an EX file being flagged as malware by everyone's computers, or the fact that it outright bricked a few people's PC or deleted their save data, the game regularly disconnects and lags when in an online session, the framerate routinely dips into single-digit territory, and the fact that the entire online session can outright crash from a monster losing its tail or someone carting to it. Even if you have the greatest high-end PC and a perfect internet connection, most of the server errors are on Capcom's behalf. Good luck getting a session without some sort of connection or framerate abnormality.

Did I also mention this thing is loaded with Denuvo?
