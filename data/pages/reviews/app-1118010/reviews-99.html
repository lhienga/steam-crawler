\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere is my little review after playing through MHW:IB

It'll be nothing to complicated or detailed, but let me tell you that Iceborne is pretty fun and I got so many more hours out of a 40€ expansion. Put that into perspective with DLC from Ubisoft for instance, you would get what? A slightly discolored pair of boots for you assassin? Maybe a ring with +2 attack for your lvl 50 character?

None of that bullshitery is here. You get all of the already present monsters ranked up to Master Rank, Some new Eldier Dragon, a fricking Black Dragon (although it isn't Fatalis, still hopes for big boy though). Of course there are new weapons and armor sets to deck out your hunter, from head to toe.

Weapon modifications, which are kinda like hunting styles in the older games. Not as much, but comparable.

Tons of new monsters, both as part of Iceborne and free LCs, such as Alatreon and Frostfang Barioth. With more to come in updates as well.

A con would be the connection issues mentioned in other reviews. While it wasn't as bad for me (like 2 or 3 in total so far) I can see how people can get annoyed by them.



So yeah. It's nothing new, just what most others already said. My word would be that you shouldn't get discouraged by people review bombing the place because of Alatreon. It's an optional monster, it's endgame, the highest of challenges. I mean, it is a Black Dragon THE strongest being in the MH universe. So it is bound to be hard.
Also if you think about the lore, it is flat out said that many hunters tried to test themselves against Black Dragons, but only a handful have managed to return, let alone kill those beasts.

All in all, worth the 40€!
8/10 (1 will be added when Fatalis and Valstrax make it into the new would respectively.)\t\t\t\t\t

+Looks pretty
+Many quality of life changes (removing unnecessary tools, no loading screen, tracks instead of paintballs, etc.)

-Monsters are annoying to deal with and newer ones straight up bypass core defensive mechanics
-Turning the game into a pseudo MMO with AEO Spam, instant death mechanics, and DPS checks

*nitpicks
the weapon design is lazy, most armor is lame and I want the lolita palico set without having to wait a year for the next event :(\t\t\t\t\t
