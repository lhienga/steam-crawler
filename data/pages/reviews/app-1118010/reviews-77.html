\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFatalis has 66,000 HP (For comparison, White Fatalis in MHGU has 29,250). While normally I wouldn't complain about artificial difficulty, since I loved EX Bloodbath Diablos, my main issue is the simple fact that the Fatalis weapons and armor are completely and absolutely overpowered. Pretty much everything else is made inferior by their mere existence. In no other Mon Hun game would this happen. Ahtal-Ka in MHGU had a similar armor set, where you had lots of decoration slots. However, it didn't have the completely ridiculous effect of "Removes the skill level cap for the skill secrets.". There are no reasons to make any other armor sets when this exists.

However, Fatalis is so hard that it must be difficult to get the armor set and weapons, right? Nope. You can easily cheese it with Plunderblade among other things to get all the resources you need without ever having to actually beat Fatalis, including Evil Eyes which are the rarest of his drops. If I told you that this was possible in any other Mon Hun game you would have told me that this is stupid, but here in Iceborne it's forgiven.

To be fair, I did forgive the game for Decorations, Clutch Claw, Grinding Lands and even Denuvo. But for this I won't, alas the game is practically over. You got the Fatty armor set? You got the weapons you want? Aight, have some fun testing it out and then you're done, since the only challenging thing left to do is Fatalis but you have no reason to fight him at that point. It's almost like they want to kill their game so people will move on to Rise.

If you're reading this and you're part of the minority wanting to start playing Iceborne in 2021 for some reason, then I honestly wouldn't recommend playing this game at all. The early game is dull since Defender weapons and armor overshadow everything else, and then the end game is also dull since Fatalis weapons and armor overshadow everything else. If you're not willing to take my advice on this, then at the very least please wait for a discount. Even as a Mon Hun fan since the PS2 days my money feels wasted. I'd play any other Mon Hun game for well over 500 hours, but here I'm struggling getting past the 250 hour mark. Do yourself a favor and play the other games instead.\t\t\t\t\t
