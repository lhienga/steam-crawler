\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis expansion definitely was worth playing because of how much content was added through it's lifetime. As others mentioned, the DLC had technical issues when it first released and was poorly optimized for the PC. But sometime since then that issue has been fixed (at least for me). This game is heavy on the grinding side as most JRPGs tend to be, and I understand if that's not your cup of tea. You could mod the game to change the drop rates of the most grindy part of the game to be easier, that option is open since it's all client side data and CAPCOM won't care if you do. Keep in mind that you're not obligated to grind much of the most grindy parts of the game (decorations and Guiding Lands), you just won't be as powerful if you don't. There is a difficulty spike but personally I like it since it provides a challenge. You will definitely feel powerful at the very end, but even the best hunters will get humbled by the Arch Tempered Elder Dragons if they're careless. Overall this was a great experience and very fun to play with my friends!\t\t\t\t\t
