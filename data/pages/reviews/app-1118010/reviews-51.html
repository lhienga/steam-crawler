\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're new to the series, or just play a few version of this series, then definitely play this one. It's the best experience of the whole series so far.

I'm a veteran hunter since the very first version, and have played many versions of the series in the last decade. Even though I love this game, and World + Iceborne is definitely the staple of the series so far, I kinda feel kinda empty. I really hope Capcom would stick to the weapon system for a few generations, and focus the time and resources on bring new, creative monsters to the table, instead of just bringing old monsters back time and time again, or just reskin the monster with the same skeletons. It has been very long since I see something completely new on the monster side that can wow me, like the first times they did with Rathalos, Khezu, Plesioth, Tigrex, Fatalis, Rajang, Daola, Devil Jho, Brachydios.... I would really, really love it if they keep the game as is now, and just focus on releasing more and more monsters with bundles or events, or the next version. I hate fighting old monsters over and over again. It's not exciting at all.

In my opinion, old monsters are welcome to return for a few version (like 2 or 3 at most) in the series, and that should be it. Move on! Create new monsters! Some of the monsters in the series are only used for 1 time and they can come back if the lore allow, but many of them have been overly used in almost every version of the game so far. Those monsters should be given a rest already.\t\t\t\t\t
