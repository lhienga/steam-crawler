\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWorld/Iceborne is my first Monster Hunter game. I have over 750 hours at the time of writing this review. If the hours don't speak for themself - I'm enjoying the hell out of this game.

The game performs very well on my PC. I had a 4GB GPU and the game did tend to crash from time to often on low graphics settings. After upgrading to an 8GB GPU, the game runs reliably on high settings.

A few pet peeves of my own:

- Multiplayer feels like an afterthought. Unable to pair with a friend until after waiting for cutscenes is longwinded and time-consuming from playing

- Further to multiplayer, servers are often unstable and most noticeable when trying to take on Kulve and Safi. I have been kicked/disconnected far too often, as have many other friends

- Having to click through NPCs repeatedly say random sentences before being able to complete a task got old very fast

The game is a challenge. Every single weapon is fun. The animations of the players and the monsters are incredible and captivating. All the pros trump the 3 complaints I've written.

Full 10/10 from me.\t\t\t\t\t
