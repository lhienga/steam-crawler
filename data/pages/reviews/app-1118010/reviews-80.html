\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe number of negative reviews that this DLC has got really confuse me. Though it has some negatives, overall this DLC is a huge improvement on the base game. This is mainly due to the large amount of content that has been added to the game, more than doubling what is in the base game.
I see a lot of complains about the new Hoarfrost Reach area as well, which I don't really understand. This is mainly due to cold mechanics and the monsters giving the Iceblight condition. Not only are both these thing returning mechanics of previous MH games, but also align with the key ideas of the series as a whole. You and your character are meant to feel out of place and vulnerable without proper preparation. And when it comes to preparation, both these conditions are easily avoided with items you can either find in the Hoarfrost Reach, or farm fairly early on.
Also, do be aware this DLC is considerably harder than the base game. Monsters are faster and hit harder, however this DLC is intended to fulfill the equivalent of G rank in previous MH games, which by design is meant to be harder and more challenging than Low rank and High rank. If you are not interested in more difficult game play I would not recommend this DLC. As well as this, Master rank is meant to be a new experience, meaning that previous armours and weapons will need to be quickly replaced.
One complaint that I do agree with however, is the addition of the clutch claw. I see what they were trying to do with it, but I think it takes away more from the game play than it gives. In addition, similar to what others have said, if you do not use the clutch claw, you are at a severe disadvantage to those that do. I think it just needed a little more though and balancing into how it was implemented.
I guess can understand why some people may not be interested with this DLC, but with what I said previously, along with a number of quality of life improvements, I would highly recommend this DLC.\t\t\t\t\t

1) Increase the health pool of all monsters

I hate using the clutch claw, i am always tossed off and when i do soften the part, its such a pain to aim for it. And using the slinger is too many buttons.
