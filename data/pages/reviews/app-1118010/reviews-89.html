\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis DLC feels like a new game to me. So many new stuff in here and the new armor looks pretty good. The new set of moves for each weapon also makes them even more fun to use.
I don't understand why people don't recommend this dlc, people say it's too hard or the monster is too aggressive and moves too fast. In my opinion this is difficult, but in a good way, if you have already finished the vanilla game then you must already have some skills to beat the monsters and of course you need more challenges. So it's nice to be able to fight again with monsters that you fought in HR but are stronger now in MR. There are also many new monsters in MR. The feeling is really good every time you finally beat the monster.
What people forget about this dlc is they are still using end game build from HR and think it will work well on Iceborne. You're really starting a new kind of game this way, because you'll need to craft new MR armor as soon as you start the ice born to help you out in MR. As long as you upgrade your equipment, you will be able to defeat the monsters just like what you did in vanilla game.
As for endgame content on iceborne, I don't know yet because I'm still playing the main quest because I want to enjoy hunting and making some builds of new armor and weapons and I have a lot of fun out of it. But I thought if it was really hard than it would be fun, because I was so desperate against the behemoth in the vanilla game and i scream in excitement when i finally beat it, so I expected something similar or tougher in this dlc.
In the end, if you are expecting some easy crafting games than this is not for you, but if you really like the challenge of hunting monsters than I highly recommend this game, one of the best games I have ever played.\t\t\t\t\t
