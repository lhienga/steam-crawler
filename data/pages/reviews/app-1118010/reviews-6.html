\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, Monster Hunter World is one of my favorite games, and the expansion was extremely worth it to me. While the story is a little lackluster, the gameplay with new monsters is fantastic and I wouldn't change a thing. I would recommend getting it on sale, because the normal price of 40USD is not worth it, but I would feel safe in saying that the game expansion is worth 25USD. Of course, get it for less if you can!

The expansion includes a whole new area to explore, as well as a few monsters and variants. There are new weapons to go with the new monsters, and an additional mechanic known as the clutch claw, which I enjoy as a slash-and-dash character.

While I do not enjoy the business side of Capcom and their lack of care to the modding community, these issues don't really reflect on the quality of the DLC and the game itself. I play this with all of my friends and do not regret a single cent of it. If there had to be a DLC to purchase, I'm glad it was one they put actual effort into. It is not a cheap cutout at expanding their newest moneymaker- the storyline is decent (if slightly annoying), the area is beautiful and stunning, and the new monster fights are thrilling and frustrating, just the right amount of difficulty. I regret nothing and highly recommend this to anyone who enjoyed MHW and wishes there was more to explore.\t\t\t\t\t
