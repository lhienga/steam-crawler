Monster Hunter World: Iceborne

Rating: 5/10 - Mediocre

The iceborne expansion is a lot of fun, the new locales are beautiful and the new monsters are ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome. It's more of the same, if you liked the base game then you're gonna like this. But the clutch claw sucks, Monster health pools are inflated to adjust for the tenderizing mechanic, making some weapons slightly worse in terms of DPS. Also the fact that many elemental attacks, like breath attacks, also have a physical component becomes really ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ annoying for rangers. The amount of times I raged as a bow main is at a number unknown to me at this point. And then there are the guiding lands, a terrible end game grind that is so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ boring it made me quit the game before maxing out my gear. But in the end it's still okay and Fatalis was a proper send off for the game.\t\t\t\t\t
