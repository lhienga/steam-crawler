\r\n\t\t\t\t\tlet me be completely honest here: after letting my thoughts about this game simmer, i have settled on the following opinions:
  • monster hunter world was a very good game, and a good step for the series. a little easy near the beginning, but overall, very fun.
  • this expansion straight up broke my enjoyment for monster hunter world for me.


iceborne continues to push the series' health pools even further, with some monsters having over twice the health that they had in earlier g-ranks, all to counteract the series' meta chasing getting worse and worse. this was only compounded with the next addition to iceborne's list of sins: the clutch claw.
the clutch claw was a godawful addition. the idea sounds interesting, until you notice that actually using it is incredibly circumstantial; so much so, that the developers felt the need to add an extra state for monsters to enter, named "clagger" by the community. when a monster enters this state, they step back obscene distances that completely mess up just about any combo. i can imagine many a longsword main missed their roundhouse slash to one of these. this is all to say that the clutch claw, and everything surrounding it, completely broke the flow of combat and ruined my experience with the game.

for a personal experience, i couldn't tell why i was feeling so awful about the expansion, until i was 30 minutes into a tigrex hunt, where i had mainly evasion and survival decos, with attack up 7 and some crit eye. up to this point, i had mainly been trying to ignore the clutch claw, as i just didn't like using it. my built was not optimized by any means, but it had been 30 minutes of me beating into this thing non-stop, just chasing and hitting it, chasing and hitting, and it was still going. even for a high g-rank monster, this fight should not have taken this long.

if you do choose to play this, i strongly recommend playing with the ICE mod. it's straight up just a better experience.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSpectacular DLC for a Spectacular game, adding double the content, Amazing soundtrack, bringing back old monsters and creative new monsters with a new map. Whats not to like?

Pros of Iceborne.
brought back fan favorite monsters like Nargacuga and Glavenus, gave them the shiny polish and little BS hitboxes that this series is well known for.
Majority of the returning monsters themes were done justice though there are some stinkers like Glavenus's theme losing its 1v1 feel that it had in Gen U.
Added some variety in the appearance of weapons though the original cast still suffers from the old weapon skins.
Overall added lots of content

Cons of Iceborne.
Clutch claw was a pretty clunky mechanic that felt like it could use some tweaking and often times made some fights real easy, Also don't like that it was given to those that bought world and iceborne for the first time, made the base fights easier than they already were.
To add to this the addition of the Guardian Armor was also disappointing as it just carried you through the base game without giving you time to develop skill and although it did its job expediting you to iceborne it also made base game real boring with no challenge.
going off the same vein the Defender tree was also Aids with super high damage Sharpness you couldn't get until the end portion of the game and blast damage, its true to its name in the sense that it defended new players from developing tactics with weapons.
And just a lil personal preference I wish they added some more older monsters, though I am content with the additions of this game.

Also to those of you complaining about the addition of the Enigma DRM I and many others have had no issues with mods or anything of the sorts. The few problems people did have with the new DRM were fixed. Ultimately Capcom has a right to defend their game from piracy as modern titles that you have the ability to purchase digitally and physically at a fair price should not be pirated, Im a firm believer that if you can easily buy a game on current hardware than it shouldn't be pirated, But to each their own.

Overall if you enjoyed Base World or have yet to purchase world but have a few extra bucks to get this DLC then you should definitely purchase this.
