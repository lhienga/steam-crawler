\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've really struggled to like this expansion. Really truely struggled. I have played this on PC and PS4 both, and I just cannot for the life of me enjoy this as much as I try to.

The Monster AI just feels worse than the base games, and on top of that have monsters that feel like they punish you for being prepared and using the system and items to your advantage. Flash bugs are horrifically nerfed, making flyers absolutely annoying to fight now. The Clutch Claw was a neat idea, but tenderization of parts was a terrible idea. Its hiding established weakpoints behind a janky control system and awful hit boxes for it. Even Gen U,

There has been numerous occasions where an update has completely busted the AI for some monsters. Glavenous jumping around constantly, Velkana doing nothing but his breaths and running away from you, forcing you to adapt a really boring hit and run tactic, ect.

The Decoration Farming is also abysmal, hidden behind several layers of RNG. Gotta hope you get the RIGHT Investigation(Layer 1 RNG), then hope you get the right kind of feystone(Layer 2 RNG) and then hope when it appraises you get the one you need for your particular build(Layer 3 RNG). The monster doesn't even matter anymore, its all about the Investigation RNG at this point.

The weapons and armor have some better designs but have still inherited the issues from the base game of being bland and repetative compared to older titles. On top of that, they honestly don't feel any stronger as you rank them up. The Base Game, getting a Rarity 3 weapon for the first time FELT like an increase during Low Rank, and getting a Rarity 8 actually felt worth it. The weapons of Iceborn at Rarity 11 and 12 just don't feel all that worth the effort, and create minimal difference in the damage you actually deal during a hunt(Unless you ferverently adhere to doing your tenderizing, such fun!).

And this probably has to do with the games "End Game", augmenting weapons for minimal stat boosts, and slapping on monster parts for more poor visuals. It is very unrewarding and really brings the joy I once had in this title to a grinding halt, either hoping I get the monster I need or farming for materials for a lure to bring out the monster itself. And even then the gains are extremely limited for the investment.

The fights feel like just pure attrition, and even G-Rank hunts I've done in less time solo than the MR quests and G-Rank in the older games was balanced for Multi-Player not Solo Encounters. Many of the monsters, are simply put, just not fun to fight as giant sacks of HP that also have extreme mobility, effectively doubling their pool, and adding to that Weak Points are now hidden under tenderizing makes them long, boring, drawn out affairs. Playing without tenderizing, is like having a permanent Debuff to all your attacks on the monster.

All around a very disappointing expansion for what was a truly masterful game.

Here's hoping Rise won't make the same misakes.
