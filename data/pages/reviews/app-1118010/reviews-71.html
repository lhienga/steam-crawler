\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you liked Monster Hunter World, Iceborne just adds even more to it. It’s as simple as that.

The reason you would buy Iceborne with World — other than being confident you’ll like it — is the ability to unlock Iceborne’s newly-added Clutch Claw in the base game as well. The Clutch Claw’s a mixed bag. Basically, you can latch right onto the monsters to hit them. Your weapon even gets new moves that let you hit the monster as you latch on. This tenderizes the part you hit and makes it more vulnerable to damage for 90 seconds. So while the Clutch Claw lets you hit monster parts more easily, it can also present the issue of worrying about not being optimal by not keeping parts tenderized. However, it’s never really NECESSARY to tenderize a part of a monster, so it’s more of a personal issue.

However, the best part of the Clutch Claw is the ability to grab onto the monster’s head, spin it around so it faces a wall, and launch it headfirst into the wall for big damage. It also knocks the monster over. It’s so satisfying and I would recommend the Clutch Claw for that alone.

Also, Iceborne’s hub is so much better than World’s Astera because it’s more compact and easy to navigate. Unfortunately, you can’t get that in the base game, but it’s something to look forward to.

My only real criticism with the expansion’s content is that a good chunk of new monsters are reskins or use the same skeletons as previous monsters. These monsters have different attributes and moves so the fights aren’t all basically the same as the monster they’re based on (and can actually be really cool takes on a monster), but it’s still very noticeable.

Like with the base game, a lot of the unfair content isn't in the story and is therefore optional. I haven't played any of it and still have had a great time with Iceborne.

If you’ve played previous Monster Hunter games, this one mixes in just as many returning monsters as it does new ones. I haven’t played previous Monster Hunters, but I hear it’s nice fanservice and that it’s neat to fight them again with World’s new moves and mechanics.

At this point, it’s kinda weird to think about the base game ending without Iceborne. So I suppose that’s as good as a recommendation than anything!\t\t\t\t\t
