\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasically more MHW with 17 new monsters (some of which are tougher reskins like MHW, or have different element/status effects), if you enjoyed the base game, you should enjoy the expansion.
Clutch claw
Learning how to use the clutch claw is basically compulsory if you want to fight as efficiently as possible: tenderizing monster parts results in more damage dealt over time; slinger bursts are basically free knockdowns whenever the monsters are not enraged, to create openings for big damage combos. Some people complain that this feature is intrusive to the initial game’s combat rhythm and those complaints do have merit, though it just becomes part of the new combat loop once you do get used to it.
Weapon changes
Iceborne adds new moves which I think are accessible in base MHW without iceborne, so it’s like a free weapons moveset update without access to the new story & monsters. The only thing base MHW don’t have access to is the slinger burst (I think). The new weapon changes results in different optimal playstyle of certain weapons (gunlance better with shelling rather than swinglance; savage axemode for chargeblade makes people actually use the axe mode rather than SAED pamming)
Steamworks
Is 1) a source of free resources and 2) terrible and tedious in terms of “gameplay”. It is a slot-machine/pachinko level gameplay where you basically mash 3 directional buttons randomly to exchange coals for usable resources, which is extremely poorly designed and absolutely not fun.
Defender’s armor set
I know it technically allows new players faster access to iceborne content to potentially play with friends who already finished the base game. However, this also communicates to new players (who only sees the higher defense values) that the base game can be skipped, robbing them of the experience of pre-iceborne MHW: The finely-tuned difficulty curve of MHW is made irrelevant by wearing this armor set; players don’t experience crafting High Rank armor and creating builds with them.
High Rank armor sets are of course once again rendered obsolete due to Master Rank armor, which I again feel should be linked between ranks rather than dead-ends.
