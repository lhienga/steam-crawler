\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIceborne is confusing, unlike the base game, it somehow made it less fun for both many 'casual' and 'hardcore' players. Its too harder for casual players, yet easier for hardcore players. It seems you're supposed to always use the clutch claw often, but it itself is another skill to develop, and its been difficult for new casual players like myself to get into it. But it seems that if you've mastered most other skills, clutch claw is a simple tool to learn that makes the game less challenging. My expectations in buying this was 'more base game', where I was able to finish the game without much optimization. I didn't have to play like a speed runner, its fun to watch and probably play, but not for me-- the base game let me do that.

Iceborne is confusing.\t\t\t\t\t
