\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly thought that this game was overrated, the story is a generic chosen hero that saves a village from evil spirits where whenever he fights he always ends up escaping with nothing truly bad happening or he ends up winning the fight in the end.

The protagonist was pretty much the typical bland, dense protagonist that has nothing interesting to his character. At least the other characters are kind of good, exploring their routes was kind of fun, except when the game pulls you out of their side stories and forces you to read the main story. The romance on the other hand had no impact on me, mostly because I found it very lacking.

Most of the story is spent trying to solve the mystery as to why the town is cursed, which boiled down to the protagonist and his friends fighting the evil spirits (multiple times in the same locatiton) and collecting their shards since it served as a key to uncover it, what sucks is that it constantly shows the protagonist having multiple very boring training arcs where he slowly becomes stronger and once they actually beat the spirits they show them going to the towns doctor so she can inspect the pieces, every single time.

The choices are just there so you can get laid by one of the girls (including the protagonist cousin), there's no risk of anything bad happening to you or the characters.

The reason for the main villain to curse the town was just out of some generic vengeance.

Music was mostly not my thing, since most of the music is centralized around the traditional Japanese music genre.

I would only kind of recommend to buy the game on sale for the characters if you are interested in them and not for the main story since I personally found it boring, even though I forced myself to finish the main story and Lena's route (which her route offers the most important parts of the main story). There are way better mystery visual novels out there like Steins Gate and Danganronpa series, and if you want a good romance visual novel then just get Clannad. Leave a comment if you disagree with something that I said, but please keep in mind that this review was mostly me sharing my opinions of the game.\t\t\t\t\t

TLDR - Another great story from NekoNyan. Wonderful story with a great set of main and supporting characters. The characters are well done and consistent. The translations are pretty good. The navigation in the game makes it so much fast to replay or jump to a specific scene or choice. If you love a great story or this style of game this is a must own game.

- -------
Story - Great story and the backstory for the MC does a great job of creating a strong dynamic for the story to keep moving forward while allowing for some pretty funny moments to happen. The characters are consistent throughout and gel well together it really feels like you are joining this community. They did a great job working to get you to care for all the characters. The one thing that I really enjoyed much more than some other games they have done most of the characters routes have a big enough and unique story that make them feel like its not just a side route that means nothing the main plot of the "cannon" romance.
Art - NekoNyan delivers on this area strongly as usual. Great design for the characters and their costumes. A nice blend of japanese traditional and other outfits that fit the story for the characters. The animations in the game do help to further enhance the art and they really do a great job.
Overall - The quality of the game makes this an easy one to recommend if you have ever played and enjoyed any of their other titles its a must buy game. You will enjoy this one.

The overall story is very good an interesting that it's worth it to finish all of the main routes to see the whole details of the curse. Brushing on the comedy as well, it's funny with actually good lines, not some cheap laughs of cringe or perverted jokes.
Their voice acting is also on point with voices that are pleasant to hear and not overblown so no need to adjust the volume, or at least that was the case for me.
The only thing I would consider as a let down is the length of the routes for each main heroine is a bit different from one another so I can't feel like there's some favoritism during development, but I digress.
All in all, I recommend buying this game, on sale or not, just to experience another good Yuzusoft game.

Humor: Painful trope/cliché moments.

Against my better judgement and instincts, I went into this VN blind based on the high amount of positive reviews. I almost never go into any game blind let alone a Japanese VN, but thought it would be a nice change of pace this time around. Unfortunately, this VN's story is very boring overall with annoying ecchi/trope "humor" moments (which is nothing new to VN's, or Japanese anime, but that doesn't make it any less unfunny, juvenile, perverse and sometimes outright creepy) but when the story is average at best, it makes the experience so much worse than it already had to be. This is a shame, since the initial story premise and some of the lore/plot points were actually kinda interesting, but few and far between, often let down by the rest of the story and average/cliché dialogue.

As listed above, the positive points about the game are the UI, Music, art, and voice acting which are all needed ingredients to make a VN even better, but sadly cannot make up for a at best average, but mostly garbage trope story.

I actually played almost all routes all the way through out of genuine spite due to how irritated I was I wasted money and time on this so I could review and rant properly. Never going into a VN blind again.\t\t\t\t\t
