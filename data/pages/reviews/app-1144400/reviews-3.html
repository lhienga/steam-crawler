\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, this game is cute af, heartwarming, all that feelgood vanilla experience one can simply relax to, and not think too deeply on.

There are points I do wish there were some more severe consequences, to make this a bit more dramatic, as you'd sort of expect from this narrative, but overall there is nothing offensive about experiencing this VN, the UI is well put together, the art is gorgeous, and the Characters are genuinely ones you want to root for, you don't feel their character growth isn't a given like a Mary-sue or atypical Shounen (for more relevant context), and they aren't magically close and in a Harem with the MC. There are some forced jokes in the beginning but overall nothing too weird, just your atypical over the top Japanese anime-comedy.

Definitely recommended on sale, but also I'd say it's worth the full price.\t\t\t\t\t
