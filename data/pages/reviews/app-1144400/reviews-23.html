\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, thanks for bringing Senren Banka to the west, NekoNyan.

Of all Yuzusoft games I played, this one is by far my favorite, for me it deserves being bought at full price, it has a long length for a VN, but that's just my personal opinion.

Pros:
-Beautiful and relaxing OST.
-Charming characters.
-The girls aren't constantly fighting for the MC.
-High resolution CG's.
-Panic button (it really saved me).
-Interesting story, and routes
-Easy system to get the route you want.
-Mako
-Murasame singing Kagome Kagome, and Toryanse

Cons:
-It takes some time getting to the H scenes, other than that, I have nothing else to say, I really enjoyed the whole VN


-The OST is perfect for this game, and since I love japanese instruments, I have nothing to complain about. I fell in love in the first couple of minutes

- I have a 1080p monitor, and usually for VN's, if I play in fullscreen, the CG's start getting blurry, and I have to play it windowed in a lower resolution (which I hate).
On the other hand, for this VN, you can easily play fullscreen without losing the CG quality.

-The designs are gorgeous and every character is really charming, it's the first time I can say wholeheartedly "I like every single character" in a VN. And the best thing about it, is that you have none of that sh*tty rom-com troupe of the girls fighting over the MC.

-It is one of those VN in which the more routes you play, the more you understand about the game, which normally, is kinda of bothersome (if you just play the routes for the girls you like), but on this one, I really enjoyed learning more about the world, and also, there weren't incongruencies you usualy see in VN's build this way

-And if you are interested in the +18 aspect, I will say, the scenes are really good, as for the amount of H scenes with each girl, for the main 4 girls, is 3(with the exception of Mako who has 1 more CG in which she is playing with herself). For the other 2, it's 2. You then have the after story which adds one more scene to every girl.
If you are wandering how long it takes to get to the H scenes, I will say it takes quite some time
