\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-Story: 6/10
The story is very generic with very convenient solutions. This leads to very little or short tension in the story. The common route is like a basic essay assigned to and written by a middle schooler. The branching routes let you explore the heroines and learn more about the curse from different perspectives. The branching routes are different in that they offer more insight into the lore of the legend, which i liked. However, how you "hook-up" with each heroine has exactly the same format. Which cheapens the replayability.

-ART: 10/10
The characters look great and the facial reactions do a great job portraying their reactions. I'm mostly impressed with the backgrounds. There's so much detail in most scenes that my eyes can help but explore.

-Music: 9/10
The music is very enjoyable and there are a few that I wouldn't mind playing on repeat on my playlist.

TLDR: Game has good art and music. Story is terribly boring and how you get with each girl is exactly the same: spend some time with her, they realize their feeling, confess, then sex in that exact order.\t\t\t\t\t
