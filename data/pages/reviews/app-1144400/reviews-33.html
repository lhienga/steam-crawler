\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPreliminary positive, but I've been waiting for this game since they announced and it's Yuzusoft so I don't doubt it'll be good.

========================================================================

Now, that I've finished the game I can give a proper opinion - I'm not disappointed. It's your typical release from Yuzuosft, which delivered exactly what I expected from it, and sets the bar up quite high for Riddle Joker.

Good common route as far as moeges are concerned, pretty good individual routes which don't vary in quality that much (unlike in Sanoba Witch), although the transitions to romance could be done a little bit better. Godlike art and soundtrack once again remain the highlight of the VN.

Overall, I enjoyed this more than Sanoba Witch, and I can recommend it without worry to any fan of Vns out there.\t\t\t\t\t
