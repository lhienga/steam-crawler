\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis visual novel has an entertaining story, relaxing catchy music and charming characters.
I also like that doing the different routes is easily accessible it has a nice branching system for other routes that I think should be a staple in VisualNovels instead of making you replay the whole thing to do every possible route/ending. There is a small menu in the bottom with some useful features such as quick save,quick load, skip backwards, skip forwards, go back to your last choice, settings, a backlog and more which are very useful in case you mess up and overclick a dialogue text. I only finished 1 route and i'm in the middle of another and it's very entertaining. My favorite character so far is Yoshino. This VN is very charming I find myself laughing at some of the interactions between the characters or their thoughts to themselves. Give it a try if this sounds promising to you!

Edit: I can't stop humming the music from this at work every once in a while it's so catchy.... After going on with Yoshino's route and finishing it, Jesus Christ this gets very lewd with time. There's also a patch in the developers website that makes everything uncensored 18+ (If you want the patch here's the link to it: https://nekonyansoft.com/shop/product/28 ) it's not really my cup of tea though I'm here for the romance story but that's just me heh.... anyways I'm doing another route now and I can't help but feel guilty/bad for Yoshino since I basically moved over to another girl now and it's like nothing happened at all....\t\t\t\t\t
