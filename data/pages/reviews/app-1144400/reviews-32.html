\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have so far completed Yoshsino, Murasame, and Koharu's routes. First playthrough took about 24 hours. The common route took me about 12 hours to complete, each main character route seems to be about 10 hours ± 2 hours, while the side heroine routes (only did Koharu's so far) took about 4-5 hours.

So far I am extremely happy with my purchase of this game. The art and character designs are phenomenal in my opinion and the music does a great job of setting the tone of the scene and really drawing you into the atmosphere of the story.

The comedy in the game was a great distraction from my loneliness on Valentines Day and kept me laughing for a large portion of what I read so far. The scenarios were also made very believable by the writing in the story. Though, I am usually a laid back reader, so if something is "bad writing" I wouldn't really care or be able to tell unless it really stood out.

The romance in the game was very well done, felt believably, and had really sweet moments too. At the moment, Murasame is best girl for me, but both Yoshino's and Murasame's routes were very good, and handled the drama/struggles aspects well. In Koharu's route, I also liked the love triangle that ensued between her and Roka; I thought it was cool to see the interactions between them.

Lastly, the technical features of the game are superb, some of the best I have seen in a visual novel, and I have read many. There is a voice bookmark feature which is improved from their previous novel, a customizable menu bar (the bar that is at the bottom of the text box), and bindable keybinds, which is rare (in my experience) to see in a visual novel.

So far this has been a great vn, and I don't regret buying it. I greatly enjoy the characters, story, setting, and fantasy aspect.

If you are still hesitant, try out the free demo first, and if you decide to get it, you can copy the data in the Trial Version savedata folder (can be found in Steam\\userdata\\[your user id]\\1221010) before buying the game, then pasting the saves into the main game save folder (Steam\\userdata\\[user id]\\1144400) in order to keep your old data, or you can just skip through until you get where you left off in the Trial Version (change ctrl skip from skip only "read text" to skip "unread text" in order to skip without needing to rapidly smash the spacebar or enter key).\t\t\t\t\t
