\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, so first of all, this was a really nice and extensive visual novel. So far I have only reached one ending. I like the graphics, the story is really nice and I do recommend this game... but :)

It might be because of the storyline/ending I ended up getting, which btw I am not exactly sure which choices triggered this one, but here is where some things were imho not contributing to the story and they were also not quite to my liking.

Mind you, I am in no way against romance and NSFW elements in visual novels, I even expect them to be there.
But the part in the storyline towards the end (or so I thought) where everything was lovey-dovey over the top I love you and can't live without you was (a bit) exaggerated. So much so that I had to contain my urge to speed click through it.

And then I got to the NSFW parts.... yeah, I did speed click through those. I am not really in need of those parts being almost as long as a part of the story, and it does not add anything to the storyline. Nice to see that they are enjoying themselves, but more than 3 minutes in one go, several times throughout the storyline, of said things in a visual novel is a bit too much to my liking.

Without spoiling too much, I got an overly detailed explanation, several times, of how good it is to make love to the one you love, how horny they are for each other etc etc. So yeah...

Still recommend it though :) The story is good, there are little to no spelling errors, and I might even check out the other storylines if I can figure out how to get to those.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the second Yuzusoft VN I've read/played, and it has the usual high quality interface and art that you can expect from them. This particular VN has a Flowchart, which makes backtracking and exploring alternate routes MUCH easier. I found it to be a fun read, and the various routes are diverse and engaging.

You're looking at 30 to 40 hours to explore all routes, 4 main characters and 2 "side" characters. I'd suggest Yoshino first, and Lena last.

Best story, probably Lena. But the best girl is the ghost girl.

I've seen a lot of comments about whether SB is better or worse than Sabbat of the Witch, and honestly I put them both on par with each other. I think the story is better with Senren Banka (depending on route) while Sabbat of the Witch has better character development overall. I will say the central character (Yoshino) in SB is the weakest main route, while Sabbat of the Witch definitely focused on it's central character, Nene.

The R18 patch is nothing special, and no achievements are tied to it.
