\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will compare this with yuzusoft previous work called sabbat of the witch.
meanwhile sabbat of the witch is your typical magic school settings, senren banka does everything differently.

Story :
The formula is new, countryside settings, there's a lot place, everything around it builds up to the story, and there's some plot twist but it doesn't too complicated since this is relaxing VN after all. Clearly better than sabbat of the witch imho, which is only school and go to clubroom.

Artworks :
yuzusoft is what you call kyoto animation of VN. The world is really vibrant, good looking, and a lot of unique buildings, everything is your candidate as new wallpaper, the mini CGI is good too which is animated when funny things happen.

Gameplay :
Now you must be confused about why there is a gameplay section in this review which is only click and read.
The flowchart feature is really nice, you don't need to replay everything, just choose the another choice for other girls route, without repeating another 15 hours playthrough. The voice bookmarks are really neat too.

Characters :
There's blue eyes white dragon hair girl, ninja girl, foreigner, and ghost that afraid another ghost heroines. But what i praise the most is the male cast characters they are not only standing there, they are as important as the girls to build up the story. There's lots of expression and every texts the expression changes which makes interaction even better.
The characters design are moe, good looking, and not bland, the girls personality are overall better than sabbat of the witch.
On my playtime when i write this review i still only got 2 out of 5 routes and i still want to get other girls because believeable characters, and other girls route is as important as the main heroine. Even though i only go for main heroine on sabbat of the witch.

Music :
Lot's traditional and acoustic BGM which is pleasing to ears, and there's so far unique BGM for different girls and the ending.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall well-designed, with a great starting point. The game also has a flowchart feature that you can use to change your choices quickly and allows you to experiment on the choices you make.

There are six heroines in total, although only three of them are the main heroines, as these three routes include the main conflict of the story (curse) into the plot of their story. Each heroine is unique, however, in their backgrounds and personalities, and their way of dealing with said curse.

Each main heroine route reveals a different side of the curse, and so by reading all three you can get a better picture of the true nature of the curse and its history. As such, the heroines have different ways to deal with the curse, which matches their own personalities.

The three others deal with a different problem, as they have little to no connection to the curse (except for one, but it doesn't really count as it doesn't really deal with the curse). Because of this, their routes are considerably lighter than the three main ones, with a more slice of life feeling.

Even so, each route is fun to read, The scenes are heartwarming and the comedy is great.

Lena is the best route
Murasame is NOT a main heroine
