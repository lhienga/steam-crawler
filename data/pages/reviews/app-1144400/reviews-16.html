\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs of writing this review i have just finished my first route in which i ended up with Lena's ending.

Firstly i'm very new to visual novels and have never properly played one. That being said from the very get go i was engrossed, from the masterfully executed music and the smart witty banter that had me cracking up multiple times throughout, there was never a dull moment. The in game menu provides a helpful flow chart that lets you see the choices you chose and the part of the story you are at, which lets you jump to any point in the story giving easy access to parts you might want to revisit. The characters in Senren Banka are crafted in a way that really emphasises the big moments in the story, sharing dialogue that feels meaningful only helped by being performed by some amazing voice actors that only enhances your immersion. In total the route i played took around 19 hours to finish which as a new comer was a manageable length never stretching interactions out longer than they need to be while at the same time never rushing important and memorable moments. As this was my first experience into Visual novels I was cautious of starting one as from the outside looking in they can seem intimidating to get into, however after finishing what i have of the story i am only ready to dive into the other routes and start the whole experience all over again. In conclusion Senren Banka is a story you don't want to miss out on and i highly implore you, no matter if your new to visual novels or a veteran to give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
