It was a good chunk into the game, by which I mean somewhere around 2-3 hours probably (it took me ~50h to get through all the content) before something clicked and I started reading up on things and I discovered that I had bought something that was rightfully tagged as NSFW and for which an R18 patch (freely available from publisher website) exists.
Not what I was interested in to be honest, however having been a few hours in and having had a blast at the puns thrown at me, and finding the characters vaguely interesting (really it's the dialogue and not the characters themselves that catch me) I decided "screw it, I'm doing this".

Yeah, 52.4h of this game and I can say that I do not regret any of my choices that led here, and I don't regret getting other games from the same dev/publisher combo either (however you can feel some.… recycling).

If you like cute characters who tend to act bashful, really terrible puns, want to learn Japanese,.… yeah, this is a great purchase.
If you also like hentai get the R18 patch, if you don't then don't, the game is good in either version.\t\t\t\t\t
