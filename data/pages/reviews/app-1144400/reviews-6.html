\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off I want to say this game is more buggy than Windows ME. This game crashed so many times on me, it was always when a video was going to start and i would literally have to spam save all the up to before the video and then just keep reloading the game until it decided to play and not crash, I am talking 5-10 attempts. Multiply this with 6 stories and it was frustrating and took the impact out of the end of the stories when it would fade to credit and then crash to desktop. Nothing in the forums about it so maybe it was just my PC.

As for the story. It's hit and miss. The common route sets up a excellent story which Murasame's route wraps up great, and just rating the story based off what I considered to be cannon and the story is a solid 9 and should be read for her route alone.

But the issue is I knew Murasame was the cannon story so I did her route last (As should you.) The other stories I can't be super nice with. Senren Banka is a painfully slow read and when 3 routes are pretty much the same from a different perspective, one route is unique but splits into the worst route, the slow read really shows it's head. This story just takes it sweet time getting anywhere, so when you're reading a scene that is similar, but different to another route, it's easy to start glazing over.

I don't want to ruin anything so I am going to just rate each route in the order that I did them.

Mako (7.5) - Started strong with a good personal conflict but it was easily and quickly gotten over and turned into probably the most sexual story? But at the same it's actually pretty funny what goes on in it. The story gives you a follow up to the history. It worked for me as a first route, but it would had been better as a second route since the lore was more about "motivation," while Lena's was about the straight scoop. But Mako was the girl I wanted to date first. Who doesn't want a pervy hot Ninja that reads manga?

Lena (8) - Probably the best route next to Murasame, I liked Lena, she was funny and this route is a huge context dump, it explains the entire history. (I recommend this as the first route) and honestly I felt this had the 2nd best ending behind Murasame.

Roka/Koharu - This is where the slow pacing starts to take it's toll, neither of these girls have ♥♥♥♥ to do with the main story and were not in the common route very often so this route turns everything into a completely new story. These stories are 100% slice of life and ditches the supernatural element.

Roka (8) - I really enjoyed the change of pace and her story was excellent. Nothing was recycled and it was a nice read.

Koharu (1) - Why? Why the hell was this a option? Was this a crowdfunding milestone or something? Not only does this route choice happen in the MIDDLE of Roka's story and it ditches it all together. it is the most eye rolling, boring ass story. I almost wrote a negative review after wrapping this route up. She is the MC cousin, they are having sex and want to date, will the MC's family and friends approve of this? <--- Yes that one black mark is the entire story, that's how dumb it is. The only saving grace is it's short, because of course it is. Koharu is literally for people who want a loli H-scene.

Yoshino (7.5) Ok we are back to the main story. I was expecting a little more from Yoshino, but since Mako and Lena gives the context dump there isn't much new addition to the lore. But Yoshino is so damn cute so i didn't mind. Her personal story and conflicts felt the most fleshed out, right behind Murasame.

Murasame (9) The cannon story as I mentioned. Also the longest. I won't say anything else.

The music and acting is really good, the backgrounds are nice and the models are a little basic but good enough.

OK, let's talk H-scenes. I actually don't really like H-scenes in these kind of stories, I do add the patch and the entire time a H-scene plays I sit there debating if I should just uninstall it as I put on headphones and try to speed up the conversation while making sure not to miss any important character development. Overall I feel they just threw them in last minute, because it becomes predictable when one was about to happen. But don't listen to me on H-scenes since I am biased, I feel they cheapen a good romance and it's hard to hear the MC talk all lovey dubby after giving a girl a facial after she performed oral sex in a public space. Don't ask me why I always install the patches, I just do.

This was story definitely not on the same level a Kinkoi: Golden Loveriche which just had more interesting story routes and much better character art (Subjective) but this story is worth it for Murasame's route alone

I recommend this. But unless you want all the achievements, skip Koharu and expect a super slow read, take breaks. I am a moderate to slow reader that turns auto read to about 45% speed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the first visual novel/eroge I've finished to the end. I've been trying to expose myself to these titles for quite some time now to understand the appeal behind them since I enjoy exploring various gaming categories. As of the time of this review, I've come to understand a lot more so it's only fitting I place my impression in this review.

First and foremost, Yuzusoft is amazing and they deserve all the love that they get with their games. I've tried penetrating the VN genre with surface recommendations from web searches and a lot of them are plagued with bad hardware support and modern features that games from other genres have been using for years.
I'm really impressed at the progress the studio have made with the development of their game engine. The interface is clean, software has zero performance hiccups, key binds are convenient, and accessibility features are there. You can also see them constantly improving the experience with their newer titles.

On top of that, all of the dialogues are fully voiced and the art is phenomenal in seemingly all of their games. With all that said, the only thing left to discuss is the narrative.

At first, I thought that the narrative for Senren Banka is a little lackluster. It's not bad but it's also nothing noteworthy. I mostly found the long aimless slice-of-life sections in the game to be boring. I thought it was out of place and wasted a lot of my time.
However, later I realized that it's a deliberate decision by the writer. The appeal of these games, and why they work, is the parasocial relationship that they provide. They need to prioritize the experience you have with the character you chose in your route. That way, the NSFW sections are more compelling. I used to generalize the VNs in one category but it seems like their lewd/dating iterations should be judged entirely different.

With that in mind, I would say Senren Banka has a very good narrative. I found the legends behind Hoori to be intriguing and I enjoyed all of the little plot points they have made in conjunction to it. While I would've enjoyed it if they had a stronger narrative to surround the world building they have, balancing it alongside the slice-of-life sections would result into a longer game which would not really be ideal.

I highly recommend this game for people getting into the eroges.\t\t\t\t\t
