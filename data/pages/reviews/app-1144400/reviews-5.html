Pro
If you like traditional Japanese culture such as kimono and shamisen, you’ll like this game. The music, the character design, and the aesthetic are all deeply inspired by traditional Japanese culture. It’s beautiful. The characters are all adorable too. It’s perfection. It’s almost flawless.
Con
This game is so beautiful and perfect as is that 18+ part feels a little unnecessary. I wish there’s a button to skip them but there isn’t. Seems like they did the censoring part simply by deleting two files from the game directory, which is kinda being lazy. But then the voice will mismatch with the subtitles due to the missing files, so I’m basically forced to download the 18+ patch… that’s the only bad thing about it. Other than that, an absolute beautiful game.\t\t\t\t\t
