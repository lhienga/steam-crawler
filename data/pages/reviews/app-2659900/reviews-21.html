\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very nuanced evolution of the idler genre that innovates with clever restrictions forcing you to create and improve your setup, with surprising and satisfying progression milestones.

While the game can feel vague and prohibitive at times, once more progress is made the reasoning behind each seemingly odd / inconvenience design choices become clear. As for whether they feel warranted, that's for you to decide based on your receptiveness for a challenge - of which this game offers in large but digestible portions.

Game can also be completed in a reasonable amount of time, which is a nice plus compared to other games in the genre, so if you have some time to kill and an itch to squeeze production rates out of small spaces, give this a try!\t\t\t\t\t
