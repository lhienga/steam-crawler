\r\n\t\t\t\t\t For now I'll have to give this game a thumbs down, I love the game overall, but my biggest gripe about it is the fact that there is ZERO accessibility settings Spoiler: at some point, the game flashes alot, and the only way to counteract it, is by making a building that comes much later, leaving you to deal with a ton of flashing till then.

The most you can do is adjust the sound/mute & unmute and enable a dark mode setting which imo doesnt look all that great,

Spoiler (again): your resource gathering/conversion machines make lines on your screen and as you can guess, the more machines, the more lines, you can see where this is going... the only way to reduce the lines is... you guessed it just like the accessibility issue, you gotta buy a buidling which for quite a long time doesnt actually make it better instead it makes it so much worse until you get to the 3rd tier of said building which just makes the line thicker basically combining a ton of lines into 1.

Main point is, if you have Light sensitive epilepsy or are just overall someone who struggles with obnoxious visuals do NOT play this game until (if) the dev decides to implement accessibility settings like disabling flashing, resource lines and such...

Quite disappointing to see a good game be so unfriendly honestly

EDIT: I can now happily change my review, dev went and fixed the really big issues we were facing pretty fast after the complaints started popping up! fantastic game and the dev seems to have plenty of more stuff to add to it, highly recommend :)\t\t\t\t\t
