\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a puzzle game with clicker elements.
Your goal is to build and produce in a way that solves evolving production puzzles.

It has a lot of micro management. A LOT...
Edit: *The developer has made a patch to reduce the micromanagement some. I am willing to adjust my review from a no to a yes, in view of their efforts. I still think this game is not for everyone.*

It is not a comfortable brain off, big numbers game.

I would not recommend for most players.
Gaming masochists would love this game.
Gaming sadists would love giving this game to others.

That said, here are some ideas that I think would make the game more fun, for most players.

1.) Add a sandbox mode. Give players a chance to experiment without punishing them for not adjusting to the latest mechanic. (If all achievements are locked to normal mode, I think most players will not mind the difficulty as much. You may want to consider adding several difficulty modes.)
2.) Create a universal fill button for machines. I get that it is a clicker, but do not force the players to wander a blank map to refill every empty machine. Your grid click system is not that great...
3.) Add a minimap feature!!!
4.) Add a way to customize the color palette. The dark mode is not enough, give the players some more control of comfort features.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish there was an "indifferent" option. I don't hate the game, but it isn't grabbing me, either. For the price tag, there's a lot there, but there are some issues I've run into - like not having any idea what you're supposed to do. You spend so much time just waiting for resources, they should really bill this as an idle game.

As I said, it's not a BAD game, it just sort of fails to grab me.\t\t\t\t\t

I agree with others: The premise of the game is great, but it fails at the execution sadly.

Out of 7 hours gameplay i have sat here and refueling machines 6 hours, 1 hour was understanding the game and actively playing and theorizing how to build.

I know this may not be in the spirit of the game like the dev imagined - but we need a one-time building that refuels everything automatically that can be toggled on and off.\t\t\t\t\t
