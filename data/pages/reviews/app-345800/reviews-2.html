\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis soundtrack is a masterpiece.
Despite never having played Darkest Dungeon myself, due to not being a fan of the turn-based game-genre, I feel that the soundtrack perfectly fits the game's theme.
Offering a mix of calm, creepy songs setting the mood for an exploration scene and flustered action tracks to add a hectic feel with rising tension to combat sequences, this soundtrack serves its purpose extremely well.

This soundtrack is money well spent if you are into the kind of music with more of a slow, dark and epic feel to it.
The Darkest Dungeon soundtrack isn't only one of the best soundtracks of any game I have ever heard but some of its tracks are even among my favourite music of all time, namely "Town in Chaos", utter perfection packed into a 6-minute track.\t\t\t\t\t
