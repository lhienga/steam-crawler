\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely not worth the £7 I paid for it, the files are not even remotely long enough to go on for a significant amount of time, I'd prefer it if they redid all of these to make the loops last longer so that it can actually last long enough to get into the songs.

I wouldn't have such an issue if this were only £1, but this is SEVEN POUNDS, I pay that much for far higher-end music albums that last a good 70 minutes!

Also, compare this to say, The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth OST, it's £2 for 106 minutes, EVEN if you take off however many songs that are just extras you might want to skip, and cut it down to 70 minutes, that's STILL cheaper than any off-the-shelf modern CD album you might reasonably get for the same content.

Don't waste your money on this, go buy the soundtrack of another couple of games you like or go buy a full CD album for this much money, hell you could probably spend this much money on any number of fully fledged games that won't only last 30 minutes and leave you feeling empty.

TL;DR: this purchase gave me syphilis.\t\t\t\t\t
