\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor me this is a chill strategy game which makes me feel powerful and in control. It has great music which you can set your favorites in the "BGM Settings" menu. On the surface, it seems really complicated with all of these menus and a dozen suggestion bubbles for actions you can take in your turn, but as you get the hang of it there are generally a few core actions you might take and ignore others.

I am not an advanced strategist and found the learning curve to be smooth and gradual. You can succeed without knowing everything, but as you play, you realize and discover ways to optimize your clan and domain which is fun. As others have said, I really think the AI is competent and fun and you can adjust how challenging they are.

The things they have you controlling, decisions, and implementation is really fun and relaxing in my opinion. I get excited when I march armies off to take over a clan.

This game is a great way to unwind at the end of the week. I would say it's just complex enough to be fun and a confidence booster since you get to be a boss and make fun decisions. But it's definitely not Europa Universalis or even Civ VI and not as complex as the original Nobunaga's Ambition I played back on the original NES. It has more of a casual board game feel to it if that makes sense and the AI is good enough that they almost can feel like friends playing online.

Great game, but I don't think it ever goes on sale and I'm not sure I'll go back to this game for awhile now that I've played ~140 hours and feel like I've gotten the full experience. That said, if you enjoy turn-based strategy and play games to unwind, I think you will have fun with this one;)
