\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlays great on Steam Deck! Bought it for my PC but on a whim tried it on my deck and it is great! The title screen has a janky colour splash that makes you think it’s not going to work but once past this it’s great.

If you want a grand strategy game with Epic battles the total war series is better. For everything else other than castle development, I prefer this. Battles are still very fun, just not as in depth or with as wide a range of units at a Total War game.

Diplomacy is better in this in that you have to send people off to build trust with rival clans and use this to ask for reinforcements if your getting stomped or form alliances or marriages. You can bully significantly weaker clans into being vassals and use them as meat shields to give you warnings of enemy encroachments into your area or use their troops in your alliances and coalitions of war.

You can build alliances with tribes and amalgamate their populations into your cities or use them as seperate helpful entity providing troops or provisions for your war efforts.

So much about this game is great. Having a heap of clans in a coalition marching all over the map, destroying enemy armies and conquering castles is a blast.

Read a review but I really like it\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll be completely honest: I went into this thinking it was a strategy game, kinda like the Total War series.
Unfortunately, It very much is not!

There is very little actual gameplay to find. You can choose to micromanage your cities, but not only does this get extremely boring after a bit of conquest - it also doesn't even matter! Just make the ai do it, it doesn't really change anything.

The wars too are boring. I haven't played any other games in the series, but in this game, it is almost litterally just "bigger numbers=win".

I managed to easily win my first game on normal, then on very hard, and I only found any difficulty on "extreme" mode.

The ONLY reason to get this game is if you really, REALLY like the sengoku period. As a game, I wouldn't even pay 10 euroes for not, and never 60!\t\t\t\t\t
