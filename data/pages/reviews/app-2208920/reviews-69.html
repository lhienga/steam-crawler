\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDecent game, Lot of squandered potential and insistence across multiple titles to force animus stories down your throat. I don't know a single person that gives a damn about the animus stuff stop making games that pull you out of the experience to interact with a soul less character nobody gives a damn about only to put us back in.

The story is okay, They squandered the main characters story casually skipping (18?) years? Their childhood could've had tons of content and they just choose not to do it you just timeskip randomly to adulthood. The raid system is interesting, Only ankle deep in content though it gets boring after first couple raids. The building aspect ain't revolutionary and the customization is a joke. Assassination might aswell be removed unless you specifically build for assassination you do zero damage to un-aware targets.

It has an in-game cash shop for cosmetics cause of course it does, The in-game (Non cash shop) stuff all looks extremely bland. The combat is fun, I really liked the combat did not like how useless my NPC allies were. Bosses have absurd scaling early with absurdly high health bars one hit from them does over half your bar it does not feel like you're this Huge Viking Warrior that people fear it feels more like dodge simulator!

Icing ontop of all this? Zero achievements, No reason to go explore all the side content and there is A LOT of side content. The main story is handicapped by the Developers whom gut all the interesting stuff while side story tends to be the stories that belonged in the main story (Main character fathers helmet for example)

Be better Ubisoft, This could easily be one of the best Viking games and you're dragging the quality down on your end extremely hard.

Exploration is the fun part of the game and Developer stops you from fully experiencing that with world states, If you're in act one you can't fully explore the frozen wastes it puts a wall infront of you and casually shows you (220 power required)

Bad design, Instead of letting you venture in and get booty blasted they assume you have no business being there.

The game is quite pretty, My rig can only run it on Medium thus i've decided to stop playing until i get an upgrade so i can run this game on Max i want to see how pretty it can be.


Overall, I'd give it a 4/10
Combat is good, Side content is good

Main story is not good, Exploration is not good and having no achievements after public outcry? Shows how they view their playerbase.\t\t\t\t\t
