\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved this game i think because vikings is one of my top 10 favourite series of all time.
now for everyone else!

Pros:

- Good graphics, especially the lighting, absolute eye candy
- Peaceful and epic music (Einar Selvik from Wardruna made some too!)
- Fun gameplay and combat mechanics, you can use a large assortment of weapon combos (I personally use hammer and spear, namely with Mjonir and Gungnir)
- Cool, creepy, funny, and wholesome mysteries and easter eggs are spread across the map
- Memorable characters and enemies with great personality, with a few irl people like King Aelfred and all of the Son's of Ragnar, and some fictional like Odin and the Aesir
- Cool armors and weapons to use for combat, or just look bad*ss.
- Regions have their own unique landscapes, so they don't feel copied and pasted

Cons:

- Audio can be very low quality
- a few clipping landscape like rocks that aren't fully attached to the map (where you could see right through it)
- Combat can be a bit annoying though, like faulty target locking
- Faulty and annoying climbing mechanics, like whenever you want to enter a house from an opening but you just keep climbing around the entrance.
- Norway can be annoying to traverse, it's nothing but cliffs and cold oceans with very little open lands (for the place that you start in
- Bosses feel copied and pasted, and very annoying, except for the Daughters of Lerion and Fulke
-Absolutely confusing lore and story, some irl characters shouldn't even be ALIVE during the time that it takes place!

7/10, good game but pretty stale sometimes and weird story.

Is it a good game, yes! Is it perfect? There is no such thing...\t\t\t\t\t
