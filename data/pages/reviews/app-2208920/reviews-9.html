\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI very rarely would review a game before it is complete... so why am I now?

Whelp the long an short of it is a lot of these other reviews sucked an I don't see the game play changing a whole lot from where I am. Getting passed the launcher an looking at the actual game in comparison to what came before...

TL:DR version is its a decent game. No chance it's worth over $100 DLC or otherwise. AC2 still holds the title of just "Best AC game" while Odyssey comes in at #2. Ac 2 just nailed everything - its a great all around game. Odyssey is huge but far less "assassin-y". This game is a lesser version of Odyssey in seemingly every possible way. Go buy that. This game is "ok" for a time waster but the price tag is ridiculous an Odyssey is just better in every way. Literally can't see one reason to pick this over that unless you just love Vikings I guess.

Explained (vagueness for non spoilers): As most know AC gradually lost more an more of the assassin play style over time an became more of an x combat with optional stealth. With that in mind Odyssey was so huge, tons of story, lots of skills, weapons, gear, quests, things to customize an so on.

I bought the whole shebang on a big discount so I'm like $40 invested into this $140 game... and frankly the $40 seems high. With this pack you get two item sets an a couple weapons. Largley what dictates what you want to use is the item set bonus. In this case the "inferior" of the two has a better set bonus... however it caps at 3 upgrades whereas the cooler of the two has I think 10. The only other item set I have got in game so far has a fairly crappy set bonus an also caps at 3.

Having fully complete the first two "areas" - gotten all side treasures an what not I have zero reason to use anything but the one "superior" set entirely because I can just dump resources into it an it makes it better - even though the bonus kinda sucks... same for the weapon... so it feels really boring that I have used the same items since minute 1 because everything is just so boringly the same.. but worse which is just... a lot worse than Odyssey.

All of the settlement/ship upgrades feel highly lackluster and pale in comparison to Odyssey.

All of my "choices" and tasking has really just resulted in "levels" / kill points... with the slight exception of the actual "abilites" are unlocked via the treasure finding system (AKA "books of knowledge).

The combat is Odyssey was better. The stealth was better. The abilities/leveling system. Equipment. Ship and crew.... the "cult" an investigation into it... the roaming "mercs/templars"... the boss fights... all of it better in Odyssey in the first 20 hours an I beat nearly everything in that game - even had desire to replay it. Hell even the quest markers / knowing what was DLC in Odyssey was easier.. you didn't just "take a nap" at the first settlement an end up mistakenly at end game dlc.. which a lot of people did here.

I'll still end up going through this whole game cause it is not a terrible game and I paid for it but that is the only reason. Get this game cause it's on sale, you're bored an you like AC/Vikings. Nothing more, nothing less.
