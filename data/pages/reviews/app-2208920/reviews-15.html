Presentation:
Graphics: great
Music: Great
World design: Excellent, I just love seeing all the different lands and city, exploration is the best thing about this game.

Story
It's average.

Gameplay:
Exploration: Excellent and vast, probably the best thing about this game.

Side activities: Decent and fun as it is tied in with exploration, it is your reward for exploring and you don't spend to much time completing them so it is a good balance.

Puzzles: Very good. Enough to have to think a little bit while also simple enough to not be annoying, a good balance, ties in well with exploration.

Combat: Fighting a few enemies is fun, fighting bosses and mythics is fun, but when you have to fight dozens of enemies, it becomes very repetitive and tedious, it's a chore, not hard, just turns annoying and boring, makes me want to avoid raids or becoming detected in a fortress, there is a lot of combat in this game and most of it is spending doing the repetitive grindy one.

Raids: First couple are novel, but then become very annoying, you are stuck having to do long tedious combat that's very repetitive, it offers the least amount of freedom in the entire game, it is a chore.

Stealth: Decent, can be fun to sneak around taking out fortresses, more fun than fighting dozens of enemies.

Items:
fairly good, you want to upgrade your gear, and your weapons change your play style, not a large variety, other RPG's have done a much better job with weapons and armor. I wasn't overly interested in find gear because the impact is just not big enough.

Character progression
Combat abilities: Learning combat skills by finding books through the game is pretty good I think, and they have a significant impact.

Gear: I have not been very interested in finding gear, there's not much drive. Upgrading the gear is the only good part.

Skill tree: I don't think it's good, I don't really care about putting a point into skills and building my character, the RPG elements is not very rewarding.

Overall
It's an ok game, the selling point is the world design and exploration. Everything else is a bit meh. So if you want a vast open world to explore and find some interesting things, you will like this. What made me stop playing is the long dragged out story and the tedious combat you have to do during the story and the raids, it was good a first but lost it's novelty after several hours and just turned into a chore.
