\r\n\t\t\t\t\tImage a game where you must complete all the side quests before progress the main plot: Here you have it, AC Valhalla. Contents are mostly fine, but the structure of this game is one of the worst I've ever seen.
The majority of the things to do in this game is conquering England for the Vikings, either by war or diplomacy. England was divided into many regions, each must be conquered separately and offers a long quest line. However, this whole conquest ties to the main plot in a rather LOOSE fashion (and kind of pointless as we all know Vikings invasion failed eventually). Why not make half as a mandatory milestone for the main quest, But the more you conquer, the better possible outcome by the end?
