DLCs : Paris : 5/10. Ireland : 8.5/10. dawn of Ragnarok : 9/10.
i was annoyed, angry, bored and pissed off every second in this game except the Ireland and Ragnarok dlc.
the only good fun thing is THE FORGOTTEN SAGA 9.1/10 and i don't know if it's DLC or not.
===
even if you remove all the bad stuff, and only keep the good stuff, the game would still be a normal good 7/10 game, it wouldn't even reach 8/10.
the best way i can describe this game is as if you were watching a movie but there are 10 second ads every minute, which mean it is no longer entertaining, it is annoying and torture rather than fun.
ac origins was 9/10, ac odyssey was 8/10, ac Valhalla 7/10 if it was made right, but in it's current condition it is a bad 5/10.
===
now to the long detailed review :
my BIGGEST problem with the game is that it takes 280 hours to complete 100% !
this is not because the dlcs or the game is a lot, this is because you have a VERY SLOW MOVEMENT SPEED in the game (on foot, on horse, or boat), so much that you really can't even tell if you are running or not most of the time specially on horse.
in ac origins or odyssey you have an upgrade that will disable fall damage so you can jump from any height to save time, but in Valhalla you have to CLIMB DOWN every time thus more time wasted !
and every time you go into cities or towns the game will FORCE your movement speed to be greatly reduced, basically going in and out of a town adds 5 minute to your game time.
i honestly think ubisoft refused to pay one of their employees and that employee took revenge by changing a few numbers in game to make everyone have a TERRIBLE experience to get back at ubisoft, because there is no way that a team can be so stupid to develop this many annoying things.
plus the sound, audio, music sucks too !
===
THE Second biggest flaw is ubisoft treating a single player offline adventure game as if it were an online MMORPG, which effects the entire game in the worst way imaginable.
the third flaw is that the entire game is based on your POWER LEVEL 99%, and everything else in the entire game is less than 1%, which means i LITERALLY played the entire game without ever using RUNES, hosting FEAST BUFFS, ENHANCING weapons quality or it's power level !
if you can play the entire game without ever using runes it means that it is a useless thing that only complicates the game without adding anything to the experience, just because ubisoft history team saw that in norway vikings used runes they had to put it in the game, and they did not bother to find a creative way to put it in the game, just add it with no purpose.
i collected all gears in the game and honestly other than a personal preference none of them add anything special, the gear i got for purchasing dawn of ragnarok dlc was more OP than all other gears which is a bad thing, basically the best weapons and gears are things that you must BUY in the shop, which i did not !
you get like 500 gear in game for free, only 5 to 10 of them are cool at most but the shop has 1000 cool gears that you must buy, the sad thing is they made these cool items but almost none of the players will buy or experience playing with them !
when playing AC games i am excited about the soundtrack, this game has no soundtrack ! the SEA SHANTIES has such low sound that you can barely even hear them.
the entire sound and music in this game is DEAD, MOST OF THE GAME IS DEAD SILENT ! in one of the dlcs in some fights with the druid sometimes a metal hyper music starts playing which surprised me, it was like the music of Prince of Persia warrior within, if you have played that game, and i was like why didn't i hear this music more often in the game if it is in the game ??!!
as a matter of fact there is a redhead singing in part of Ireland dlc, i was so thirsty for music, that i made a save at that point so i can load it and listen to that song. and on that point the randvi character is disappointing, i don't buy the romance between her and eivor. it would have been better to have something like the Ireland dlc in the main game instead of romance with Sigurd's wife.
the SKILL TREE which looks like stars SUCKS and is confusing since you can't see them in an organized way in 1 page, thus i ignored most of it for the most part.
the abilities are almost ALL USELESS or duplicate of each other.
this game is like a food, that every employee at ubisoft threw whatever ingredients they wanted in the mix, this game has NO CREATIVE DIRECTOR, not that it has a bad one, it does not have one !
nothing in the game is optimized, organized or thought about thoroughly.
i finished all the challenges, achievements etc, just so that i know :
i NEVER EVER EVER HAVE TO COMEBACK TO THIS ABYSMAL SIMULATION.
===
i will review dlcs on their own page but in short the PARIS DLC SUCKS because the game force and reduce your movement speed EVEN OUTSIDE TOWNS ! and it has a very dark sad environment which i don't like.
the Ireland dlc is fun and i like it, dawn of ragnarok dlc is also fun,
paris : 5/10. ireland : 8.5/10. dawn of ragnarok : 9/10.
i really think someone at ubisoft intentionally sabotaged this game.
===
i give this game 5/10 ! PLEASE DON'T PAY TO BE ANNOYED !
