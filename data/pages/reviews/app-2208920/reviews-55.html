\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is long and big. It's often very pretty to look at it. Some of the stories are good.

But this game is a disorganized, buggy mess. It gets repetitive quickly. I found myself out ranking zones very quickly. Combat became very easy about half-way through.

Then there is your main character. The male character design is awesome. Male "Eivor" has a good voice actor. There's a reason why he features on the trailers and cover art. But, he isn't really the main character. See, no matter what you do or how you skin your character, the rest of the characters see a female "Eivor." Which should have been obvious since Eivor is a female name. So, it gets a little awkward when male vikings start hitting on a male Eivor who then shrugs it off. It gets weird when kids in the streets refer to your character as "she." But, the biggest complaint is that it takes away from any role playing experience. If you want the character to be female, don't give players a false choice.

Ubisoft handled male and female lead characters very well in Syndicate, but they have been dropping the ball ever since.
