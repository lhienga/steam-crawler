\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty decent game from a vikings perspective. Graphics are really good, not much of a plus point these days, most games look great.
I however played with nvidia filters on to make the colors look more natural, felt like it suits the brutality of the setting.

Took 30+ hours to get hooked on the combat, after getting few great skills things really started to flow.
The skill tree is really good, despite the size it's not really that complicated. The anytime respec is a nice touch.

The side quests this time around are a lot less dumb, so that is quite nice, and you can usually finish them just right around wherever the quest was found.
The size and density of the map is not all too bad either. If there is nothing you can at least soak in all the beauty of the environment, the lushness is 10/10 for me.

I also quite enjoy the little things about the world we get to read here and there, most the time it will even lead to clues or other locations.

As an AC game not sure yet what I think of it, definitely don't hate it, however I do tend to enjoy a game more for the plus points rather than the negatives, which are many, (specially the micro transactions for good weapons, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ as usual).

Also I got just the standard edition for like 10.99 or so.
With a sale it's definitely a great buy, for full price? No chance\t\t\t\t\t
