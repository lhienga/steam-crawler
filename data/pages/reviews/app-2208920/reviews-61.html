\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the start of the game I have chosen the Pathfinder option, because Ubisoft games are notorious for having 10 000 POIs all over the place that completely ruin the experience. Its really nice to have this option.

The begging of the game (starting island) was really good and I thought I would enjoy the game. I got to the part when I have acquired my own boat and the boat gameplay was again very good. So I thought nice, first Assassin Creed game I might actually play and finish. But it turned sour really quickly. As soon as I have approached random village on different island, the Assassin Creed stuff started to ruin the game for me. Scanner? Going through houses robbing (scanner marked) stuff while no one cares. The 100 kg of meat viking running over roofs and ropes and jumping down like any other old AC title. It just didnt fit. It ruined the viking experience and within 2 hours I was annoyed and bored and refunding.

These games have little to no reference to AC anyway. Just title it Vikings - Fight for Valhalla and ship a proper Viking game, not this cross between vikings and ninjas with super scanner.

Shame, this game actually came close to being fun and staying in my library.\t\t\t\t\t
