\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy honest opinion is that the game is fun but for the first 20 hours AT MOST. Graphics, story and character design are really nice. But sadly those are the only few good things here. The story is long and pretty much nothing changes throughout the game. You are constantly going to conquer "new" lands which are pretty much the same all the time. Castles are the same. Outcomes are very predictable. Farming for resources is tedious. Stealth mechanic in this game is non-existent. Once you start its like a chore like you have to finish it although when you are playing the games you are actually supposed to enjoy it. As an Assassin's Creed game I give it a 0/10 but as a viking game I could give it 2/10 IF the story wouldn't be that long and repetitive. Like why even bother making a such a long game if the gameplay is pretty much the same from the start? Was trying to speed run it from half way through AND they even made that part irritant also by making non-skippable parts. This game defies logic (as the games should do) but its also made as being "realistic". Sorry but as an Assassin's Creed fan I can't recommend the game at all for other people. I would rather stare and the ceiling than start everything all over. Odyssey was one of the best games out there while sadly this is the opposite. Don't even try to consider buying it for full price. Maybe for a 80% discount I would consider.\t\t\t\t\t
