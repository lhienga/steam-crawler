In fact, you will not be surprised by much at all, especially if you've played any of the more recent giant, sprawling Assassins Creeds.

There's a lot more (kinda Souls-like) combat, with enemies signalling "dodge me" and "block me" attacks and boss fights against large health bars. Perhaps the biggest surprise is when, not long after the introduction, the game picks up and moves to England. Not just for a short bit of Viking-ing, but for a significant majority of the game; you're not going to spend dozens of hours exploring Viking Scandinavia here.

If you loved Origins and Odyssey and like Vikings, then ignore the "Not Recommended". Other than that, it's hard to recommend this when there are plenty of better action/adventure games to be played out there.\t\t\t\t\t
