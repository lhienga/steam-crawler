\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHDR is broken, there is NO way to configure it right, you can tweak some parameters in game and windows and then tweak a perfectly fine calibrated tv's parameters to get somewhat reasonable result, but would you want to do that for every game? Doesn't make sense. Then the graphics itself - I guess everyone who made a decision to post this game in production has severe myopia - the game is blurred so much it strains my eyes. You can, again, install mods on top of the game and tweak some videocard settings to improve it somewhat, but the sharp game picture gets blurred by the engine, then you try to re-sharpen it from the blur, but a lot of details are missing at that point and you get sharp but messy picture in the end and lose some frames just to get some eye relief. Be mindful that there is motion blur and depth of field stacked on top of this.
Then there's haze, I mean this also should've been designed and approved by multiple people during production. In conclusion - game looks terrible on good hardware even 3 years after the launch and is not fixable. Gameplay wise - it is too big and ambitious, the overall length of a game is strictly tied to the grinding of resources and microtransactions. which is unacceptable in full price game. This makes game boring after first couple of raids since you have to complete way too many repetitive actions just to move through the basic part of the story line. Regarding story line - if you got a DLC - it is implemented hideously, there are a lot of quests and tracking them can be tricky, but somehow the DLC starts without any explanation right in the beginning of the game without any story background or build up - you are simply thrown to the boss fight without any clue of who the characters are and what is the story.

In conclusion:
1) A lot of questionable design choices
2) If you as a game designer add a graphical effect - make it possible to switch it off, bloom, blur, motion blur, depth of field, TAA, chromatic aberration, haze - are graphical game DEFECTS, nothing more
3) Grinding is never welcome, never was, never will be
4) The size of the game is too big both storage wise and in the actual game itself.
5) DLC implementation is really bad.\t\t\t\t\t
