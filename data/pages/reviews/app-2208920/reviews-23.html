\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile the world is not as visually interesting as ancient Egypt or ancient Greece, the content available is much better than Odyssey. I liked the Eivor character. While the overall story is quite weak and not paced well, the individual arcs are quite interesting. It was more like a collection of short stories than an overarching story. I felt the map was a bit too big and there are too many collectibles as well. The DLCs are worth it, especially Wrath of the Druids and Siege of Paris, their stories are much more coherent than the main game. If you are not trying to 100% though it's a good game. 7/10\t\t\t\t\t
