\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen dealing with the game crashing left and right and couldn't find a solution to save my life. The fix ended up being simply turning off my overclock. Game has been smooth sailing since.

Valhalla is a great game so far, but it's barely an Assassin's Creed game, even compared to the last two. There is a strong focus on raids that are very much not stealthy. These raids and fortress assaults are a great time, so I don't mean to knock the game for things like that. Story missions are fun, and the story itself is decent. About half of the main characters have voice acting that is really not acceptable in this day and age, sadly. The world is full of life, and the villages and fortresses feel well thought out. The environment is beautiful, and the graphics compliment all of that wonderfully. Very well optimized, too, as my fairly low end rig can run the game at a very stable 60 FPS on high graphics. Would recommend, but Odyssey is still just a little bit better in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
