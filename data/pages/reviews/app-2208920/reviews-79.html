Too Long, still read it anyway -
As a huge fan of Norse mythos, as most Minnesotans are these days, I've been super excited to play this game for a very long time, but have been holding off until the price was more reasonable.

I've gotten about 6 hours into the game, but have been exceedingly frustrated to find that half of that has been getting my controls set up in a way that isn't miserable. More annoyingly, the functionality of keys being Held for one action and tapped for another is gross, especially with not being able to choose which keys are and arent set as such, and lastly setting [ALT] as the Dodge key is just dumb.

The Parkour movement is clunky at best, though I may just be spoiled from Dying Light 2. I've clipped through more walls accidentally in this game than I ever intentionally in every other game I've played put together.

Even with the strictly limited amount of time I've gotten to play between needing to micromanage my controls, I've somehow gotten severe motion sickness from play this game.
