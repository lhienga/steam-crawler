\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely loved AC Origins and Odyssey, and although Vikings and Norse culture/mythology don't interest me as much, I was really excited for Valhalla and ready to be pulled in. But sadly I have to say it's not a good AC game.

The story is basically non-existent. The beginning of it is quite forced and after 20 hours of play i still don't really know where it's trying to go. The characters have no positive motivation or goals and I in turn have no motivation to watch them develop (not sure if they do eventually, I have my doubts). You just go from area to area, conquering and making allies in long 'saga' quests.

The game play is good, but in my opinion a slight downgrade from Odyssey, especially the combat. Exploration is really fun at times, sometimes you come across interesting areas and riddles. But overall it doesn't save all the rest. It all feels a bit lacking the love I could see put into the previous games.

Not to mention technical stuff like issues with ubisoft connect, semi-frequent crashing and long menu loading time.

So if you love Vikings and fighting and conquering stuff, give it a go, you will probably enjoy it. But as an AC game, it is quite disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t
