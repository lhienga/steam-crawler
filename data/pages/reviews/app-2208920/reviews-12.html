As a comparison. I just had finished AC Odyssey and as deciding between this and Origins. Valhalla is good, its fine. But it is missing something critical that the other two definitely had, a strong motive for the player. It feels as though Eivor and Sifried just left to find adventure and settle the new world, and that's it. Both Odyssey and Origins started off with exciting plots that left me with a quest for vengeance. In Valhalla, vengeance was found early on, and well, then I just left home to go do stuff.

The open world mechanics are fine, and there a million different resources to collect, and tons to craft, and mini-games that are there own element. There is A LOT OF CONTENT, but more isn't always better.\t\t\t\t\t
