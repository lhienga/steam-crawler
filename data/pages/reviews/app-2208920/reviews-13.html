\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed from base game to all DLC. It is a 6/10 at best at certain point. Quite a bit of bugs that would require reloading a save or stuck between a wall that would kill you. I got it on sale, I would honestly recommended just to wait for sale if you really really want to try it out.
Story - 5/10 (Sweet Baby Inc touched their greasy hands on it), some relationship doesn't make sense since this 800+ AD in ENGLAND. A lot of plot holes and had parts where it felt rush.
Combat - 6/10 Having different type of sets and gears were pretty cool. I felt the bosses fight were alright at best but you will notice Ubisoft isn't experience in making interesting bosses fight. Also some fight don't even work as intended, one fight you have to drag the boss to a fire. But when you grab the boss, it bugs out and "throw" em into the same spot.

OVERALL, 6/10. Just go play Odyssey if you want a fun action from UBISOFT. I still don't understand the AC in this game. Should've just made it a original title and have Eivor live out his/her life with loved ones in England, away from the AC universe.
