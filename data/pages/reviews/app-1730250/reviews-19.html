\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was my first time playing a casual city-builder and I throughly enjoyed my experience.

The game has strategic elements mixed into very casual gameplay
The Tile Types,Buildings (Which require a certain Tile combination to build) and mission mechanics were to me, a much appreciated addition to the game.

The feeling of getting that one tile that fits just right into that one spot is very nice. On the flip side receiving a tile that just doesn't fit your current layout does feel quite bad but its alright, it might even create new paths that can be used.

I can see it becoming "boring" as one run does taking quite a while (mine was 4hrs) and at a certain point managing where to put your tiles to get points can become tedious. Maybe the dev's could create a 'Turbo Mode' which accelerates the gameplay loop in some way.
For me personally cutting my play session in half was fine, since its a casual game and I am playing it more for the aesthetic and simple gameplay rather than trying to get reach a highscore, or trying to finish a run.

All in all, just have fun creating your own little world bit by bit and take it at your own pace.\t\t\t\t\t
