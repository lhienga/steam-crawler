\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like city-building games? Do you like puzzle strategy games? Do you want to feel a sense of accomplishment and completion once you finish playing, but still have something to do afterwards? Do you want to just chill out, relax, and sit back? Well Pan'orama is the game for you! I have technically finished this game multiple times; however, the Beta Challenge Mode keeps me coming back. It's similar to Islanders if you've played that game.

I'm usually not the type of person who likes city-building games or puzzle strategy games either. Some of the previous ones that I played had a conquer and conquest agenda that would put me off (like Civilization) or they became too complicated far too quickly (like SimCity). I personally consider Pan'orama to be a happy medium while others would certainly describe it as almost effortless or low-key. It's also really easy to just mute the music and listen to a podcast or something on the side while I'm playing, which I love.

It deserves the Sit Back and Relax Award imo.\t\t\t\t\t
