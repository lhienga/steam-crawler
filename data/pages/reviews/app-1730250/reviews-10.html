This is a tile-puzzle game. You build a world with the tiles that you are given. Sound easy? Ah, but the catch is that to keep going, you need a certain number of points to be able to try to place a specialty building. I say try, because then you need to pick your spot and provide the required tiles. Doing so earns you more tiles.

When you run out of times, your game ends. You are playing against yourself for your best score.
