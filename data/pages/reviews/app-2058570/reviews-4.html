\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRogue Wizard 1 is in my top games of all time. Superbly designed game, 10/10.

Rogue Wizard 2 feels like a 1.5 version of the first game. Which is great, more content! There are some core design decisions that completely change how the game feels and how you progress, in very drastic ways sometimes. Sometimes the result is better and sometimes worse.

Free healing and getting spells back after every level completely changes the dynamic, it also removes much of the tension and some of the hard resource management decisions. It lets you become more wasteful and careless in some ways, and doesn't reward efficiency so much.

Getting upgrades and spells as level rewards... is interesting. The spell upgrades are completely different, you only can pick one of each. The items are kinda neat. Overall, I feel like the first game had a much cleaner and tightly designed progression system.

All and all, a good sequel to a superb game, although I would still recommend the first game over this one to someone new.\t\t\t\t\t
