\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall I rate this game very high!
It is not for everyone, but I'm really enjoying the mix of planned builds (spells/skills) with randomized enemy / item options. I'm not sure how many of the "nice to haves" are already available in the game (just started playing recently). I would love to see some rework on the UI/UX so the game can reach more players :)

Nice to haves:
1. It would be nice to have an easy way to link / search by text for a spell or skill. Many of the effects are depending or triggering other spells/skills and I find myself trying to guess which tags the new spell/skill might have or searching through the whole list.

2. It would be nice if mouse buttons could be used in the keybindings. I find myself switching my right hand between mouse and keyboard quite a bit. Note: I tried playing with just the keyboard for awhile and it works, but I liked the mouse interactions (at least the parts that are supported).

3. I've made it to realm 20, but there are still many times where things will happen and I have no clue why (even after checking the post round break down).

4. Some of the descriptions were not very intuitive initially (ex. raised vs conjured)
