\r\n\t\t\t\t\tToday I've spent like 8-10 hours playing the game and I didn't enjoy a single minute of it.
But I'm quite going to press the recommend button on steam because my heart would be bleeding if I didn't - just for the good old times.

Giga annoying cutscenes when hitting stuff in single player (was in the original as well but I just wanted to mention it again because this is one of the worst things they could implement in a racing game). Online Multiplayer is dominated by ppl with 0.0001kb/s internet connection lagging the out of your car and bugging through everything. Servers are doing exactly what you would expect ea-servers to do.

I used to love this game back then on my xbox 360 and single player is quite as it used to be, so if it's just for the single player I do recommend a purchase.
But regarding the multiplayer I feel scammed by this "remastered" and don't know if I would recommend this game to someone who is looking forward to play the "new" multiplayer. I believe they thought "well, we add one more level which needs 5 times the xp from the original max level (and therefore days of stupid singleplayer grinding or weeks of multiplayer grinding), give the multiplayer a crossplay and let every game be hosted on our personal office toaster." is enough to get paid a second time for a game I already own.
To be fair, the game isn't as bugged as unbound but sometimes the multiplayer is quite close to that.\t\t\t\t\t
