NFS Hotpursuit Remastered
A recommended game for fans of the original nfs games. Just speed & adrenaline.

What this remaster brings to the table are improvements to lighting and effects unlike the original (2010) including all dlcs in one game plus crossplay. The game does not include vehicle customization except for selecting the vehicle colour, some stickers and windows design (racers only) it's just jump into the action, race, hunt, do your best and unlock new vehicles. It is a game that is easy to understand and difficult to master. Recommended because it provides a unique classic experience.

If you want to buy it, do it when it is on sale. EA tends to sale older games or not so popular titles in their catalogue from time to time. Or go ahead and buy it at full price if you want to jump into the action now. Note: Uses EAapp (known as Origin before december 2022) as a launcher as it requires an EA account since EA is in charge of providing the service, servers, save game data, etc. Steam is only the distribution platform and is the same game as origin, xbox, ps and steam.
