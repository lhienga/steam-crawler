This game has all normal player of arcade racing cares about - choose a car, we give you a track, and some thrill in form of police chases and enjoy yourself. I can say only two words - thank you.

I cannot remember when I gave a damn about Need for Speed story.. probably second Undergrounds and that was a story of "you work up your way through ladder of random racers". I hate drifting. I don't need tuning. I liked simplicity of the old Need for Speed game in its rawest form - chose the side, vehicle and that's it, Keep going forward and if you crash nothing happens coz AI racers are tied on the rubber rope and always wait behind the closest turn. Good times, even the multiplayer is still alive and kicking which is proof this is the way they should be doing these arcade race games.\t\t\t\t\t
