Need for Cop™
Bust them racers!
Key feature of this NFS - you can drive as cop and eliminate racers! You either hunt a single car or a group of drivers. AI is impressive: they suddenly change direction, they using (abusing is more accurate) shortcuts, and drop spike chains with devilishly precision.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2822733982
...not today, doughnuts!
Of course you can drive as racer, just be ready to escape from police.
You share similar weapons (EMP, spikes), but have two others: extremely powerful nitro boost and EMP jammer.

Shortcuts are your saviours. They deduct precious seconds in timed trials, you can avoid road blocks and helicopter ambushes, plus - driving in one slowly generates nitro. So use them!

The game itself is very simple to navigate and races lists are easy to browse.
Racing tracks aren't city based (hallelujah!), meaning you play mostly in wide roads, highways and deserts freeway.
No collectibles or side activities either. Cool, right?\t\t\t\t\t
