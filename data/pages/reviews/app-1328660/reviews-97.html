\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOVERALL RATING:
5/5 Love it

GAMEPLAY:
This game plays more like Burnout 3, you have a map with a selection of events, you get "takedowns" for driving into others, as a fugitive you get some tools to keep the cops at bay or viceversa. You level up, unlock cars, go into higher difficulty races and you keep going until the end. It sounds basic, but it's fun.

GRAPHICS & SOUND:
This remaster improves the PC port quite a bit, though mostly on the technical side (more graphics settings). The game looks good and has lowish requirements. Sound has some issues where it kinda cuts away sometimes.

LENGTH:
Took me 27 hours to get all achievements, this includes some online stuff as well, though nothing too long fortunately.
You need to grind a few levels to unlock all the cars at the end, but it doesn't take too long.

CLOSING THOUGHTS:
If you have the original, there's no real need to get this.
Otherwise, this is a fun mindless game, with no story to follow or missable stuff, just sit back and relax driving some cool cars.\t\t\t\t\t
