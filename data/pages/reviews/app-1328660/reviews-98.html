\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNeed For Speed Hot Pursuit Remastered 2020

The game is great and it's originally from 2010 version. This game has stunning graphic back then in 2010 which also same the remastered version as well with slight enhanced of the game. The game is the golden age of NFS Franchise before it become pay 2 play as all new NFS games. The game primary divided you as racers and cops. You can play both it as it's a different perspective of you are a racer or a cop in each stages. The progression of the game is only complete all races and cop chases to the best, no story and it's arcade feel of Need For Speed type of game. The only difference is that it's getting more intense for you to finish all in perfect score. The game also don't use currency but only collect all your exp for all the races you finished. The more you earned the exp, the more cars and equipment unlock as you are grind the game level. I only played this game only to feel the nostalgia of the game from 2010.

For my review, buy this game during sale if you are Need For Speed fans. The game also welcome for all newbies players as well. The mechanic of the game is not hard but easy to adapt the control of the car. The progression can feel a bit bored as the levels are getting more harder. I like the game but not too much as some. The only lacking of this game are car customization and story. Other than that are great. The AI probably is not too good as it's from 2010 version. I give it as 8/10. Racing and pursuit is my goals. :)\t\t\t\t\t
