I have both the old hot pursuit and the new remastered version. The racing feels smoother and the graphics seem a little bit better. I have a little bit stronger pc then average, and the game ran perfectly at max graphics with no lag spikes or hitches in my 19 hours so far. The game is capped at 60 fps though so thats the only bad thing in the graphics area.
There are no new car added since the old version.

I love the customization although it is a bit frustrating because its very imprecise. I would've liked it more with a little more accuracy. I think it should have more presets for designs and the ability to change the secondary color like you can for the primary color

The racing feels very nice and overall this is a very tough but quite rewarding racing game\t\t\t\t\t
