On the other hand, you can also race as the police and you have to arrest the road pirates and much more… By the way, I once played Hot Pursuit on psx.
The missions will alternate from race to finish within a short time and overtake everyone.

The game also has good support on the steam deck: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4VZdas9VVy8

Playing as a police officer is also a lot more fun than you might think at first glance. The goal is to trap the speeders and ram them off the road if necessary.
Sounds delicious, right? And nothing prevents you from ramming the police in the role of racer yourself. It's nice to be able to get rid of your aggression without getting the police after you in real life.
There are different types of races you can participate in.
These are all distributed over the different courses in the game.
The first race of a new route is often a time trial to introduce you to it.
As a cop, these time trials are a bit more difficult because you get a penalty if you hit other traffic or the railings.
In addition to the time trials, for example, you have to stop the street racers as a cop or vice versa. The races are also available online and there you can level up as an agent or racer just like in the single player.

Hours of fun are guaranteed in a game that also looks very good graphically and at the same time Need For Speed: Hot Pursuit Remastered takes you back to a time when racing was super fast, nice arcade and above all very fun.\t\t\t\t\t
