\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNeed for Speed Hot Pursuit is the best American traffic cop simulator on Steam today. The use of force by police in this game is supremely realistic. Police in-game will regularly ram into speeding drivers, often rolling them 5-6 times over guardrails or cliff sides in order to issue a ticket for excessive speeding. Much like real life, players controlling police will have access to a variety of military-grade offensive equipment to use against the civilian populace in the name of upholding basic traffic laws.

My one small criticism is that during "rapid response" levels (requiring you to swiftly drive to the scene of a disturbance to aid your brothers in blue) you are assessed a time penalty if you side-swipe or collide into any traffic along the way. I find this to be unrealistic. In my experience, such collateral damage is considered to be acceptable in the U.S. (Source: was rolled 5-6 times by police on the way to the shoe store. Don't need shoes anymore, my wheelchair is awesome).

Overall, I recommend NFS:HP to any fan of police simulation games. Remember to back the blue and that all lives matter.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Good:
- Exquisite arcade handling model, easy to pick up and play
- A modern Need For Speed game that isn't open world
- Great sound (for the most part, see below)
- Solid lineup of cars
- Good variety of set-piece events that are a blast to play
- Cop/Racer weapons are fun to use
- Additional content from the console releases are included.
- Cross platform multiplayer

The Bad:
- Glitchy police radio chatter (especially in tunnels)
- The hitboxes around traffic and roadblocks are incredibly deceptive, make sure to give yourself extra space
- Texture resolution has been reduced from the original PC release, this can be addressed with mods
- Progression/Unlocks after completing all the single player races becomes a grind, no doubt to draw people into the multiplayer
- Needs the EA app installed despite launching from Steam

The Ugly:
- Zero anti-cheat in multiplayer


Anyone who likes arcade racers, you owe it to yourself to give this a go.
