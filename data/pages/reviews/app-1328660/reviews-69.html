\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis remains an amazing arcade racer. The gameplay is primarily focused on maintaining extremely high speeds by making use of drifting. In a modern racer that would probably be a bad thing, but in Hot Pursuit, the drifting feels similar to Ridge Racer Type 4 and Burnout, which feels very satisfying. The roads are varied and very fun to drive on, they're clearly crafted with the game's high speeds in mind. The car selection is mostly great, except maybe for the hypercar class, which could have used some extra variety. The online mode is still active and very fun, with no rubberbanding mechanic to artificially keep the players close to each other.
This Remaster also added the ability to create custom wraps for almost every car, which is always a plus.
Despite not having the most recent cars, the game holds up very well, and I recommend it to any arcade racer fan.\t\t\t\t\t
