Best Car Chasing Game

-Graphics: 9/10
Great enough for the year it was released.
There is no significant difference between the original version and the remaster.
I expected more, but as a "remaster" there is no problem.

-Driving Physics: 6/10
Garbage because you have control over the car, you crash with the slightest mistake!
On the positive side, it makes the game more challenging and difficult for the player.

-Car Customization: 5/10
It has almost nothing but the ability to change color of your car.
But it is not necessary for this game, because the combat system is fun enough to cover this defect!

-Car Tuning: -/-
Unfortunately you can not tune your car!

-Artificial Intelligence: 8/10
Clever cops, stupid racers, but who hates easy prey? :D

-Gameplay: 11/10
Perfect gameplay, even more funnier than today racing games!
Pleasant car chase, super funny combat system.
Especially if you have friends to play with.

-Audio: 8/10
If you ask me what car engines and exhaust sounds, i will not answer you.
But if you ask me about voice acting, all I can say is that it's perfect.
The special sound effects make the game look exactly like an action movie with pure excitement.

-Musics and Soundtracks: 10/10
I will rock whatever comes my way!
Great in-game radio music, much better than today's in-game radios!
The main menu soundtrack is on another level.
It has brought me a sweet and fascinating memory.
Listen

-System Requirements: 10/10
It runs well on most computers, you don't need a battleship to play it :D

-Story: -/-
There is no story section.

-Price: 8/10
It is cheap, buy it on sale, you will not regret it.

-Bugs: 10/10
"didn't see any bugs in my experience, so I don't care about your experience!"

-Singleplayer Mode: 10/10
"Painkiller"

-Multiplayer Mode: 7/10
It does not work properly It is also difficult to find a match.
It is funny if you have a good internet.
But the game is restricted in my country and does not look interesting with a VPN.
In fact, I have to use a VPN to connect to my main account!

Overview
I definitely recommend buying the best car chasing game ever made,
Total Score: 8.5 ★

Preface

I've never played the original Hot Pursuit so I can't compare how the remaster stacks up, but this is a pretty enjoyable arcade styled racing game. Also I don't really have any experience with the NFS series, but growing up I had many friends who were into the original Most Wanted, Underground 2, and Hot Pursuit. With the racing game sale on steam, I decided to pick this up on a whim since it was heavily discounted.


Thoughts

Overall as I said before it's pretty fun and easy to just jump into events. It's fairly simple with no complicated progression system, no customization in terms of tuning your vehicles, and no vast overworld to drive in. You just select a race, choose a car and go which can be a plus if you don’t want to waste time with other stuff. This is done through a mission oriented system where you can choose between standard race missions and cop missions. The race missions are as in the name just races with the occasional cop interference thrown in for good measure. Also sometimes there's time trials as well in this category. Then there's the cop missions that usually involve taking down a racer as quickly as possible. The driving feels decent, not the best, but still enjoyable. The car selection is good enough and will grow as you beat challenges and rack up points. Graphics wise it looks really good if you can crank things up to high or ultra, although environments look a bit aged. Lastly the OST is pretty decent and is representative of music popular at that time in the late 2000s to early 2010s. Side note, apparently this version also includes all previous DLC from the older version as well just to note.


Conclusion

I think if you're mainly looking for something linear and straight forward this is actually a good package. If you want something in the simcade field where you can really customize and tune up your cars, roam an overworld, or have some sort of story then you'll be disappointed. Setting the correct expectations will affect the potential enjoyment you’ll get out of this. I dunno if I would pay full price for this, but on a discount this is some no fluff straight to the point fun.

Unrelated but this game does remind me quite a bit of the Burnout series, but a little less aggressive with the chaos (I've played the 3rd one before on PS2). Although it might not be that surprising because while this remaster was done by Stellar Entertainment Limited the original developer was Criterion who worked on Burnout so seeing similarities between the two seem natural.\t\t\t\t\t
