Introduction
Need For Speed Hot Pursuit Remastered is a re-release of the original Hot Pursuit (2010) which includes all DLCs, visual enhancements and cross-play support.

Pros
  • Slightly improved visuals.
  • Handling is fun, although unrealistic.
  • Can play as both racer and cop.
  • Car sounds are great.
  • Lots of of different multiplayer modes.
  • Cross-play support.
  • Decent soundtrack.

Cons
  • Denuvo DRM. F*ck that!
  • Improvements to graphics are minor.
  • Inupt lag makes cars and even the menus feel sluggish.
  • Crash cams. I hate them.
  • Losing control of the car whenever a new cop car shows up and during crash cams.
  • Rubberbanding. It's been a problem in racing games since forever.
  • Roadblock gaps are too small, most of the time I crash into them even though it looks like I can make it.
  • Poor customization. If you like a particular color on a car you have to always select it.
  • 3 cars were removed (Carbon Motors E7 Concept, Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren 722 Edition and Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren Stirling Moss Edition).

Conclusion
Need For Speed Hot Pursuit Remastered is a good game with fun singleplayer and multiplayer modes but the improvements are minor.
If you already have the original and don't care about the DLCs or the multiplayer then I can't recommend it. There's too little here to call it a remaster.
If you don't have the original, like me, then I recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
