\r\n\t\t\t\t\tas title says, it's just a "remastered" graphics edition, so all "annoyances" of original are still on. I completed it on PS3 years ago, bought this because of sale and wanted to play anew on PC. Rubberbanding is what ruins the fun not only here, but on any game because stats become pointless; choosing the BEST car for any race won't make ANY difference as AI cars (even the worst) will ever catch up, for the most skilled and perfectly you can drive. They'll always reach you quickly and probably pass like a rocket, even if you're 9 sec ahead. Wall bouncing is another flaw, and these collision physics are ridiculous. When YOU crash, AI rams into your car and rockets ahead all fine, but if THEY crash and you touch... "CRASHED". If you slam other cars, yours loses speed/stability and they barely move, but they can ram you and right after pass just fine; never try to ram anybody at high speed, because all you'll get is either a crash or full speed loss. Same for cops, even if stopped there and you ram at 200MPH, barely half of HP will reduce instead of an instant takedown; best way to damage is heavy side slams or at their "wheels" if you're behind, as hitting rear bumper won't do much and if your car is half past theirs, they won't get damage. After any "cutscene", AI is dumb and will direct your car straight into walls, spoil your drift, avoid shortcuts, etc; it may reset your car at an awful place (after a crash) too. if you're patient enough to ignore all this crap, good luck. 'til the end of career you might've figured out how weight/drift physics work, and it gets actually cool after learning (when none of flaws above spoils). Car selection is good, except those unnecessary "copies" (roadsters), environment is beautiful, some car sounds are great, and Pursuits are fun with these weapons; I'd just like to have some of those from NFS World, then this game would certainly be very addictive.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tConsidering the game was "remastered", it really doesn't look all that great. Otherwise this game is amazing. I've only spent a couple hours on it but I'm sure I'll be clocking in more. Drifting mechanics are the most predictable and satisfying of any need for speed game I've played so far. It is an older game, so car customization is basically non existent and it is but also isn't open world. You can choose to free roam the map, but the game doesn't really let you do anything other than drive around while in this mode, no races can be initiated and cops can't be found, from what I can tell. Again, the races and drifting are awesome. Kinda reminds me of rivals with slightly better handling, although everything else is lacking a bit. Definitely wouldn't pay the full price on this one, but absolutely recommend on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
