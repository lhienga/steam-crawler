\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe experience that I had with this game was already enlightening as the same way the original predecessor from 2010 has brought to me while playing. However, I would like to point out some things that didn't seem right to me while playing the game. The problems I would like to report on is that sometimes when selecting the car I want during a event, it glitches a bit but doesn't really seem to effect my entire game which I would like to call a "sprite error" during these kinds of glitches. Another thing I would like to point out is that sometimes when I finish an achievement, its processing would take a long time for it to be featured in my trophy collections, however, I am not sure if this is normal to games or not, but maybe it would be a good idea to fix this bug. The last problem I would like to report on was some missing cars removed from the original game from 2010. I noticed that cars like the Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren Stirling Moss, and the Carbon Motors E7 Concept have been removed from the game. And I don't know if you people hadn't had the chance to add these cars due to licensing issues, but it does kinda break my heart that these cars were not added. However, even if the game sounds as if it doesn't preform as well as was, it hadn't stop me from doing the missions between the cops and the racers, which now takes us to the good parts of the games. The game featured something new that modern Need for Speed games features, which was unlimited customization, and I was really impressed by this new feature since the original predecessor had a limited choice of colors. Another thing that got me interested in the Remastered edition of the 2010 predecessor was the new feature of wraps for your car which wasn't really in need for this game, but it would have been for people who really like to make their cars that make it seem like they are tuned. The last good thing I would like to point out on this game is the new cars that have been added to this game and the events that came along with them giving a preview on how well they preform! Which concludes the ending of this long-lasting review from a veteran NFS fan who has been playing for 10 years! :)\t\t\t\t\t
