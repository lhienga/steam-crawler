Unlock fast car. Pick fast car. Go fast (from point A to point B). Simple. And I actually enjoyed this.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall really great game. It's basically like i'm back in 2010, but just with better graphics and optimization. The racing is fun, playing both sides is fun. Even the multiplayer is fun. I expected to need to search for ages for lobbies, but no. Quite allot of people still play this game online (as of this review on 20 nov 2022). I actually met some really nice folks, which is actually something i don't really do on most nfs games (because i rarely play multiplayer on those). Here it feels rewarding to get better. So overall, especially for the price, it's a MUST GRAB, atleast if you like cars/need for speed.

For the achievement side... ooh boy is it a pain in the ass. For the most part the achievements aren't bad. The ones you can do in singleplayer are actually very fun for the most part. The problem i have is with the online ones. As of the time of this review, it's still quite doable to get these. If you can't find a lobby for a specific mode, just make a lobby and wait for a bit. Usually people join. What i am worried about is the future though, online achievements are real killers of games when it comes to achievement hunters. If you buy this in the future and don't find lobbies, atleast you will have some luck. Because for most modes you need at most 1 extra person (yes 2 people will be fine to complete most of the achievements).

Also rubberbanding... like ALLOT of rubberbanding on the ai lol. Sometimes they even crash extremely hard because of it, which is super amusing.\t\t\t\t\t
