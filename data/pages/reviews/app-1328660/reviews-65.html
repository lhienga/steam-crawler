\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played every NFS game since the first one, and this one is the perfect homage to the classic issues of NFS games. Don't pay attention to the hour counter on the side, I've finished the old iteration of this game, and the Switch version as well.

This is possibly, the best definition of the name Need For Speed. There are no fancy upgrades, no wildcard cars, no nonsense - You get a beautiful mountain/countryside plethora of tracks, all the legendary vehicles, fast races, aggressive cop chases; you sit, you fire it up, and you drive!

Now, keep in mind this is an arcade racer, and not a simulation. The steering mechanics are "break to drift" and they are very polarising - some will love it, some won't. I personally think that they fit the pacing of the game nicely, although pretty much every car handles the exact same way, regardless of FWD, RWD.

The only thing that is missing, which would make the game absolutely perfect, are the Ferrari cars. I guess the licence was probably too expensive, or unavailable at the time. Also, the soundtrack is mildly average. It won't annoy you, but you won't really pay attention to it; albeit, there are a few awesome tracks (Underground 1 OST is still the best ever).

All in all, buy it, kick back, ramp up the volume, and have a blast with some good old arcade racing.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is is more like mario kart than forza, in a good way. Easy pick up games in single player and multiplayer, no cringe story, and cross-platform multiplayer are all great.
It's not a sim, and is a bit too simple, and has no customization except color, and that's fine.
If you liked Need For Speed Underground, but like me, wished it had cops, or if you liked the burnout games but wish they were a bit more like Need for Speed, you'll enjoy this game.

I just wish the single player didn't have so many "time trial" races where you race alone without crashing. But that's maybe because I suck at them.

Hackers do exist on PC side though, and no cross-platform voice chat is a bummer.
EA has the power to fix those two issues.\t\t\t\t\t
