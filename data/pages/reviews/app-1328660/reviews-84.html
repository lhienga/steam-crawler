\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI know it says only 47 hours but I've played the original version of this game for more than a decade on PS3 and all I can say is it's my favorite racing game (Not saying it's the best racing game cause it's not) It's just charming and fun.

Pros:
Combat racing with easy to learn weapons
Customize Cars
Unlock All cars via "Story Mode," Online, or both.
Remastered of previous game with virtually no changes
Variety of Race options (Race with no weapons, Race with weapons, Cops Vs Racers)
All cars are balanced so really picking based on steering and handling as well as cosmetics
No Additional purchases in game (Since it includes the original games DLC)
A send of power and SPEED
Haven't met a single hacker (Except one Suspicious person who won every race and I can say with confidence I'm good enough to at least catch up but they weren't moving fast enough to tell if it was hacks or a speed glitch)
Cross compatible (No system has any advantages over the others except for loading times lol)
Music gives 2010 bop vibes uwu

Cons:
Linear races
Linear progression
No weapon choices, you get what you get. (4 Options)
Original Game glitches remain
Not a HUGE player base but again cross compatible so plenty of different systems playing at a time and I've ALWAYS found a lobby using quick match. Specific searches may vary in popularity and that brings me to my next point...
NO PLAYER COUNT FOR GAME MODES (WHY DON'T MORE GAMES HAVE THIS!)
You either have a perfect run or the worst runs of your life.
No steering wheel support (From what I've read)
No Mods

Don't buy for $30 wait for a sale. Put it in your wish list. you won't regret... or maybe you will. can't ever tell with you people.\t\t\t\t\t
