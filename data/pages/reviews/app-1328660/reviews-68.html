I waited till it was on sale for $2.99.

(Take these pros and cons with a grain of salt cause this game is from like 2010/2020)
Pros:
  • drivin mechanics are easy to learn and understand
  • driftin is smooth and easy enough to maintain
  • good music
  • online servers still work for an old game and people still play
  • can customize color of the cars and the car's wrap
  • able to toggle sirens/headlights
  • lots of different cars to try
  • graphics look pretty good

Cons:
  • color/wrap is about the only thing you can customize and I think you need to pick your color each race
  • No real story just instance based missions in a pretty much linear unlock and play single player
  • Online doesn't let you customize searchin for game queries that much, so you have to pick a game mode and a racin class you can't pick a mode and search for a game in all classes at once
  • A little too straightforwards on car choice, you can just pick the highest top speed car and pretty much always win because NOS gives you plenty of acceleration.
  • I couldn't get a controller to work on the game but that might just be me. 100%ed it just fine with the keyboard. usin the numpad's 8456 for the weapons and 0 for the reverse/regular brake.
