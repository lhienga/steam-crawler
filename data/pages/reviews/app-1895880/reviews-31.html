\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I've played this game to completion four times on a borrowed PS5. I haven't played the PC version enough, obviously, to know how OK this game is from a technical perspective on PC. But, I love this game. I've played Ratchet since the PS2 days, and I think this is one of the best entries in the series, apart from Crack in Time.

I know some Ratchet fans have problems with the retcon of Ratchet's personality compared to the earlier PS2 games. But, I honestly don't mind the change. The writing and acting in this game isn't the best in the world: it's sort of on-par with a good Saturday morning cartoon. But, that gives it a lighthearted and pleasant feel, I think.

That all said, man this game is just so much fun to play. I love the new characters, Rivet and Kit. I love how fast and seamless the game is during action and traveling. The weapons feel amazing to use. The mini games and side-missions are great. The platforming is satisfying and the easter-eggs and collectibles are fun. This is the first game I've ever played where I've seriously considered learning to speed-run it.\t\t\t\t\t
