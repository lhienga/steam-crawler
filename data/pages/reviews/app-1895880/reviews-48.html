\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExceptional game.

Has the Playstation 2 era fun & difficulty mixed together with ultra HD graphics. Plus the technical feat of traversing through different worlds instantaneously (via rifts) makes this game extremely satisfying.

I only have two issues. First the worlds you travel to have minor design flaws (floating grass & square terrain) but this is quite rare/not that annoying and will hopefully be fixed. My second issue is the boundaries, which prevent the player from going to areas (only encountered on the swamp planet as of writing this). The walls preventing me from exploring areas are intensely frustrating as I don't want to miss any collectables, but also do not exist for areas which I should definitely not be in, which causes soft locks that are difficult to escape from but not impossible (if you die you re spawn in that same area you're trapped in).

I know my complaints seem longer than the good things I've said about the game, but honestly the whole experience is flawless, which makes the minor issues stand out a lot more.
