\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoved this on PS5, it’s a fantastically fun and bright game with a great vibe and wonderful particle effects. It’s a great adventure with a ton of polish and charm.

The weapons this time out are very good - there’s nothing as fun as the disco glove from a previous outing, but enough variety that the shooting galleries don’t get stale.

The settings are all wonderfully designed, and overall Rift Apart is a great entry into the series if you’ve never played a R&C game before.

It does run well on Steam Deck OLED albeit with quite a few crashes. I had to tinker a lot to get 40fps but it made the game feel unstable. 30 is more straightforward. Looks incredible on SD OLED and I was blown away that I could play it handheld.

PSA: Do not enable ray tracing as it crashes the entire steam deck and the game wont reload. If that happens, delete the proton files and it’ll let you boot the game up again.\t\t\t\t\t
