\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best Ratchet and Clank games in my opinion I played almost all of them since the first one on the ps2. Its not a perfect game, but it has lots of fun guns, good replay-ability, and a good story that respects other games in the series.
The PC version is not as good as the PS5 version, but its still a fantastic way to play if you dont have a PS5. Everything is basically just as smooth and optimized really well, despite my PC not having a solid state drive. The only real issues I faced were 3 occasional bugs. Audio would sometimes cut out/ become distorted, but restarting the game fixes it. Sometimes my weapons would not aim/ fire properly when using the hover boots and going back. Switching weapons fixes this, but its the most common bug. Lastly, when upgrading a weapon, sometimes my mouse would not be able to select a few nodes, but you can use the arrow keys and the enter button to make it work.
Despite those issues, this game was a lot of fun to replay. Other than the fun ps5 features and the bugs from the port, it almost feels like the game was made for the PC. 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
