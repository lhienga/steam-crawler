Yes.
Do I enjoy the game?
Not really.

It's good, don't get me wrong, I want to like it. The port is good, I love that Sony released it. And you can even play this with mouse and keyboard, better even than with a controller. Kudos!
But this is just not my Ratchet anymore.
Do you remember the original Trilogy? Did you like the characters?
If so, better ignore this game, because you will not recognize them, all bright and fluffy and bland without any edge or dimension to them.

If you can look past this, I'm not blaming you, I applaud you, I wish I could just enjoy this more.

This game is nice, but bland, so very very bland and it didn't have to be.
Press F to pay respect, Ratchet died a long time ago and there is an imposter on the loose.
RIP in peace, you were a great Lombax and certainly not the last one.\t\t\t\t\t
