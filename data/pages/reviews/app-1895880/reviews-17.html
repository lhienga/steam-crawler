\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: 8/10 (Trophy Difficulty: 2/10)

After almost 20 hours and 100% of the achievements, I'm now ready to review this game. Before I dive into that, it's worth mentioning that I played this game on a Steam Deck, so my review might be particularly useful for anyone else planning to play it on the same console.

All in all, I really loved this game. I haven't played a Ratchet and Clank game in a few years, with my last experience being the remake of the first game, which was "decent" at best. This game, however, is truly something special. It takes what was already great about Ratchet and Clank and puts it on steroids with incredible graphics.

While some people seemed to dislike the story, calling it weak, I thought it was pretty good. The gameplay remains the same as always, just slightly improved. The rift mechanics are amazing. Essentially, it's a more polished and improved version of what already works. If they had done something truly different, I might have given this a 9 or even a 9.50/10. But because it still sticks to the same systems, I'm going to give it an 8/10. Truly a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
