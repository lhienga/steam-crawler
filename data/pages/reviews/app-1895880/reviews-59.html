\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Field of View in this game is very different from the games of the past. I can barely see anything outside of my peripherals, making combat fundamentally different from how it was in the past. However, enemies seem to act the same way as in the past (swarming all around you from the front and the back, projectiles forcing you to keep moving but guaranteeing you'll be flanked, etc.). The combat in Deadlocked was vastly superior by comparison. Maybe because this is the first R&C game after Deadlocked that I've played, I'm not used to whatever combat has become in these games since the first four of the series.

There is a mechanic that keeps you from "accidentally falling off the map", and you can only choose "during combat" or "at all times". This also has the inadvertent effect of trapping you between swarms of enemies and the borders of the arena. Severely annoying, makes it impossible to move around so you just end up immobile and getting struck over and over until you die.

At least once, during the Zurkon's "Bugtrax Done Quick" mission, the fifth portal just isn't appearing at all for me rendering the mission impossible to complete.

There are a ton of issues with this game that make it aggravating to play, rather than fun. It hasn't been optimized for PC to a degree that is satisfactory for me. I really love this series but, for me, this has become slightly more chore than recreational activity. I'll try to play it again at a later date, but thus far I'm really disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRatchet and clank: Size matters on PSP was one of my favorite games of all times!
This one looks so much better (one of the best looking games in my library), it has more mechanics, more action, more everything. And thats the issue. You get completely overwhelmed right from the start of the game. Explosions everywhere, just action, action, action. You immediately get guns, multiple guns, tools, upgrades. The currency is super easy to obtain so there is no saving up. Also on the standard difficulty it is way too easy, even the first few bosses take 2-3 mins max with 2-3 attack patterns
To be fair I only played for two hours but it overwhelmed and bored me at the same time. Returned it.\t\t\t\t\t
