\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen it comes to the Ratchet and Clank franchise my experience is very minimal to say the least. A snip it of a demo here, a level or two of the full game there. So with Rift Apart being my true first Ratchet and Clank game I have to say these are some great first impressions. This game was fantastic and I was addicted to it for the whole week I had it for. Now of course with this being made for the PS5 originally it’s going to need a lot to run it at peak perfection. My computer is pretty good but it’s no RTX Minecraft guzzling masheen and it’s certainly no PS5 stand-in, so most of the graphical stuff had to be turned down. But even with that the game still looked pretty good and still ran pretty well. Funny enough the only time it ever ran poorly was in the skill tree menu for your weapons, and it only crashed once out of my 16 hour play through. So yeah this is a great game that you should totally check out if you're interested… And Rivet is pretty hot.
