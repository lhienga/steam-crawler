\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA visit to my childhood, experiencing this franchise again was a treat. Excited about this finaly coming to PC and bought it the minute I saw it on steam. I 100% the game fairly quickly and will play a NG+ playthrough in the near future. Like all early Ratchet & Clank games the visuals are great for its time! It looks so good even on medium graphics settings. It runs smoothly and the gameplay is fluid and feels like a tribut to the original games while still having new mechanics. It feels fresh for both new and old players alike while keeping the Ratchet & Clank feel to it. The story is fairly simple but thats ok! The characters are amazing they dont feel forced and the jokes are not oneliners shoved down our throats it feels natural. They still like in the original games have a bunch of adult innuendos that used to flew over my head as a kid but replaying as an adult you easily catch and can have a laugh at. The interaction and relationship between characters feels natural and seeing their alter ego in the other dimension was interesting. The gameplay while easy depending on what difficulty you play on is a blast but I would like if they polished the flying it feelt a bit clunky and frustrating at times. Sadly there is no space battles like there were in some of the games and thats a shame I really loved those with all the uppgrades and stuff you could do. This is kinda the downside of the game it lacks a bit of varieties. Enemy varieties, planets, railsurfing, space battles, parachuting while dodging missiles some of these are downright missing, some too few instances off or lacking in varieties. This is the games biggest weaknesses. Despite that I would still buy it again and will probably buy the next one if they make another one. Hopefully they improve and expand on the base they have laid here and maybe we can get something on the same level of the original games. Excited for the future 8/10!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis franchise means my childhood to me.
Even though I've only played the first two games (aside from this one) back in the PS2 era, I loved every second I spent with them so this franchise near and dear to my heart. So naturally when this game first came out in 2021 to PS5 I was so excited to play it but unfortunately I didn't (and still don't) have the money to buy one so sadly I had to shelf the idea... until 2023 :D.
I was tempted to watch a playthrough of this but I decided to stay far away from any spoilers, which was the best decision I have ever made. When it was announced that Rift Apart comes to Steam, the hype train was instantly fired up in me and I knew that I HAVE TO PRE-ORDER THIS. I have never pre-ordered a game before, mainly because the fact that games that come out nowdays are usually a buggy, unfinished mess, but at that point I only heard things about this game, and I was fairly certain that the PC port will be good so I went ahead and Pre-ordered it. And I wasn't disappointed. The game ran fairly well on my mid tier machine and I barely noticed any bugs during my playthrough.
To sum it up: The game lived up to my expectations, and for me the Pre-order was worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
