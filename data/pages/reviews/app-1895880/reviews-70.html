\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere to begin.

Well this has been the number 1 game i have wanted to play since the teaser was shown back in 2020. I absolutley love the Ratchet and Clank games. Only problem was Rift Apart was a PS5 exclusive and i don't own a PS5.

I never thought in a million years that any Ratchet and Clank game would get ported to PC. The Franchise was Playstation only, so i was caught very off guard when Insomniac announced that Ratchet and Clank Rift Apart would be coming to PC July 2023. I was Immediatley excited.

Now for the game.
As expected. I love it. Its not my favourite in the series. The original PS2 trilogy have a special place in my heart. especially Ratchet and Clank 2. However this game delivers in all the best ways. The gameplay and gun fights have been updated to just be more fun since the 2016 Reboot Game. The story continues off from where the PS3 games left off (Tools of Destruction, Quest for Booty, A Crack in Time and Into the Nexus) and it introduces some new faces with alternate dimension gender swapped versions of Ratchet and Clank named Rivet and Kit who are more than just alternate dimension versions of Ratchet and Clank as they have have their own unique personalities, back stories and motivations. They are some of the best characters in the franchise in my opinion.

As for performance, i had a constant 60fps. However i did run into a few bugs and glitches. First off some of the cutscenes lagged and were put of sync with the voices and seconds off the first level didnt load and i was stuck falling into the void.

I highly recommend however you will need a good SSD as the game has you jumping around dimensions and obviously it needs to load that quickley or your kind of taken out of the expeirence floating between dimensions waiting for it to load and open the rift. That happened to me a couple of times but it wasnt game breaking or anything.

Im on New game plus and its still great.\t\t\t\t\t
