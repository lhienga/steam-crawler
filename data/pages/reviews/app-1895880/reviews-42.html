\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a classic fan and a purist of that original quadrilogy. I've played through those games collectively hundreds of times, I replay them every year. Ratchet 1 is my personal favourite game of all time, with deadlocked, GC and UYA close behind. But I never clicked with the PS3 games, though I did play all of them. and the PS4 game was an insult to a much better game's legacy that at the time was the final straw to turn me off from ever playing a new entry again. But I heard high praise for this one, and I gave it a shot. I'm glad I did. While much of the issues I have with the PS3 game are still there, they've definitely faded. The story is still a little self serious, but has a bit of the original's charm in there. The characters are still pretty bland compared to those original games, but they're just a little bit closer to those ps2 games than before. The gameplay picks up right where deadlocked left off, and while I don't think the weapon levelling is very satisfying as it still relies on the raritanium system from the ps3 games that effectively delays getting your actual upgrades until you go back to the vendor to spend crystals on it - the gun play combines the best elements of those future games smoother shooting and movement mechanics with the long forgotten feeling the original release had, where guns feel more like answers to a puzzle than just picking whatever needs levelling and not noticing the difference, thanks to some stellar, highly varied combat encounters that allow all of them (except the dog cannon, which seems to just suck no matter what) to shine, on their own or combo'd in interesting ways. While the characterisation and the themes have nothing on the originals and are quite boring, the gameplay is worth it. For just that gameplay, I would rate it a little below GC and deadlocked, but higher than the rest of the series. As a full package, It's just below the original quadrilogy, but the best of everything that came after. Optimistic that future instalments will try to renew the character and charm of the originals.\t\t\t\t\t
