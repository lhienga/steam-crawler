\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBuy it discounted, at least it is pretty and can make for a decent VGA benchmark.

The story is boring, Ratchet is not Ratchet, and Clank is not Clank either. The game is short, very short. There is only one arena in this game and it is boring too, how hard was it to copy/paste from the Arenas of previous games? How can they mess up the simplest part of the game? Well they had to add a story, of course, games have to be movies now rather than games.

I had to skip most of the cut-scenes from mid-game onward, they are very cringe. The game tries to be cool without earning it and you spend more time playing as the female character than Ratchet. Furries will love this game I guess. Clank is broken the entire game (missing an arm and broken legs) it gives one OCD and it is incredibly annoying they don't even try to fix him at any point of the story. Like most modern AAA games it suffers from EGS shenanigans of course, Clank is now a cripple and Ratchet is full of fears and self doubts.

The dimensions thing is a gimmick save for a couple of stages where it is used in a very mediocre way, the rest of the time it is used very unimaginatively, basically a portal from where enemies come from or extra bonus stages.

The game was made to showcase the PS5 "as a next gen console" and it shows, there is too much detail (yes that is a thing) and things going on in the screen all at once resulting in a screen that often becomes a busy mess.

Audio effects are serviceable there is too much emphasis on the explosions, music is so meh you often do not notice it. The characters don't shut up, they spend the entire time making remarks like: "I guess I should activate this, or charge that, well done, good job" and so on and so on, when they aren't making comment's like these they are dumping exposition, they never shut up.

All that technical PS5 progress is for naught in this game, it's gameplay, structure, story and and mechanics are much inferior to the original 3 PS2 games. But hey Rivet is a furry's dream isn't she? (facepalm)

It's actually absurd how goods this game looks and it makes transitioning from a cutscene back into gameplay feel so good and so immersive because there's no change in visual quality what so ever. Overall I would rate this game a 9/10 due to the lack of enemy variety and weaker story for me personally. It is my favorite R&C game just barely above "Up Your Arsenal" and decently above "Tools Of Destruction" which was previously my 2nd favorite R&C. Buy the game.\t\t\t\t\t
