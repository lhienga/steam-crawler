\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo glad Insomniac decided to port this to PC! I've been a big fan of the series since the beginning. And if you're a fan of the previous games, Rift Apart will not disappoint. It's more or less what you're familiar with. Which could be a bit of a drawback. It follows the familiar formula of buying and upgrading weapons, blowing stuff up, collecting Gold Bolts and other collectibles to unlock extra features, hopping from planet to planet completing objectives leading up to the final boss. There are swingshots, there are grind rails, there are hoverboots and magnet boots. There's not a whole lot of new features here. But it feeds that run-and-gun platforming itch you've come to know and love from this series.

Performance-wise, I'm running a 3080ti and 32GB of RAM from a SSD, and it ran very well. Occasionally a little slowdown (to be fair, I was running Netflix on my other monitor), and the odd sudden crash (I counted less than five the entire time I was 100%ing this game) and that probably has something to do with my setup and usage habits.

Keep it up, Insomniac! Keep bringing your Playstation games to PC. In fact, how about a remaster/remake of the entire R&C series? I would happily buy them all over again.\t\t\t\t\t
