\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy first Ratchet and Clank game ever.

I enjoyed it a lot and beat it in 2 sessions. The game story is very focused for kids but I didn't mind it and found it very relaxing to play. Graphics, animations and art style are simply amazing.

Enemy variety could be better, it was definitely lacking from the half point of the game but at the same time at least the enemies are funny and kept talking nonsense. Same with many boss fights... except the main ones. Those were only 3 I believe. Other ones were quite recycled and you were fighting the same enemies multiple times.

I absolutely adored the characters, all of them. I wish we could get more R&C games on PC now.
I didn't expect this game to be packed so full of run and gun action. It felt like playing DOOM at times. The weapon variety was amazing but unfortunately some of the weapons felt the same or very simillar, though many of them were quite unique as well.

I'd say 7/10 at worst or 8/10 at best with the potential to be a point higher with more mature story and higher enemy variety, Still an amazing game despite of that.

I did not encounter any major bugs. I had no crashes. I played on Very High without RT at 1440p with 5800X3D and RX 6800 XT (with major overclock) with FPS lock set to 75FPS and it basically never dipped bellow that.

With all RT options at HIGH I could play at locked 45FPS without any upscaling but the game looks fantastic even without RT so I thought it's not worth the performance hit.

FSR 2.2 implementation in this game even at Quality at 1440p looks kinda bad and the XeSS looks great at Quality and Ultra Quality but XeSS has an issue where moving objects leave behind very noticeable ghosting trail so I decided to not use upscaling which in turn turned me off from RT as well otherwise I could get solid 60FPS+.

Got this game on Winter sale for 40eur I'd say it's definitely worth it at that price or lower. I'd personally not pay 60eur for this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGameplay is solid, with a few nitpicks for me (camera is a bit to close for me
and i find it annoying that i NEED to use the rigth stick outside of combat because the camera will not follow).
Combat feels great, a bit easy with the addition of the "dodge roll".
Weapon wise a bit of a strange focus on "turrets", with 4 instead of the usual 2-3.
Encounter design is good BUT it really likes to spawn enemies behind you.
Enemy variety is a bit lacking.

The Story, in my view is the second weakest story (only nexus is weaker) of all mainline R&Cs i have played to now.

It just feels like one big side adventure, the ingame timeframe seems really tigthly writen but then just as a timecritical event takes place comes a line "oh we should hurry there because that planet is under attack but the new arena levels just opened and we migth need the bolts for gear".
It just establishes nothing, even though two "new" heroes are introduced they spent next to no time establishing them just handwaving it away with parralel dimensons (which is honestly infuriating, before it was dimensons now its parallel dimensons.
what happend to Rivets dad? was he also the keeper of the dimensonator? what made the lombaxes flee this or rather ALL the other dimensons?! Why are the Lombax-lore bits writen as if its not parralel dimensons?!
making the Dimensons parralel dimensons was probably the WORST decision they could have made, maybe instead spend 1-2 Planets developing rivets charater set a few lore entries to explain her and make her a lost lombax that got dropped in that dimension or something.
This just opens up so many Plotholes with the amount of events that happend in this franchise.)
And it feels like one reference after the other, but what gets me more is the inconsistent character of ratchet:
When he speaks he often acts like Remake/Movie Ratchet with his confidence/insecurity problems
and then in body language scenes the old ratchet shines through.
For example in the tutorial:
Why have the "what if we are washed up" line setting him up to be a nervous wreck that saved the galaxy ~3 times and the universe twice completly on accident?
followed by "we havent done anything in years" which does fit with old ratchet and new one.
But as the tutorial mission starts with enemies and that smile and nod towards clank before combat starts fits sooo good.
Then the few character development scenes we do get seem just so over the top rushed through.
And one of the final cliche setup got me legitimately angry as that could have been handled alot better, especially as that leaves you without a talking backpack on most planets in the final grindstage:
At the Prison break Rivet comes clean about Kit being the one who took her arm, but its not going to stop her from breaking her out of prison and forgivenes being complettly up in the air (this i like), then after the rescue shes ready to forgive her because clank suggested it?!(WHY?) That should have been draged out a bit, her being willing to work together to save the universe but wait with the forgiveness till you beat the big bad.
BUT the BIGGEST PROBLEM here is that Kit goes home, just refuses to stay because we need the cliche of her coming to the rescue, leaving rivet solo in the final stage which is also the one your in after beating the boss and working towards 100%.
And im gonna be honest their banter during the figth with Dr. Nefarious (Act1 end) was fun and i would have liked more of that, them slowly growing to care for each other, buidling a friendship.

Overall,
the Gameplay is SOLID.
the Weapons are not as wacky as in previous games but still FUN.
the Performance is SMOOTH, only had a few audio bugs on my second challenge run.
the Story is meh to infuriating at parts (especially if you are a bit invested in the franchise) BUT still Enjoyable.
the Writing could be better, there are a LOT of References, A LOT.
is this Game worth 60, ehhhhh maybe.
is it worth 45, yes.

I pray that more of the older Games get ported to PC, especially the original trilogy as my PS3 is getting on in years.
To myself, i have played R&C 1, 2, 3, Gladiator, Size matters, Tools of Destruction, Quest for Booty, A Crack in Time, Nexus and now Rift Apart. AND I WOULD DO IT AGAIN!\t\t\t\t\t

Let me be honest The only reason I could Recommend this game is because I really do Like the Ratchet & Clank franchise.

Do you remember Metal Gear Solid Sons of Liberty? You eagerly anticipated playing a great game With Solid Snake, but instead you spend most of your time with a nobody blond guy and a tacky cast of villains. That game could have been great... but it was destined for the towelette. Well that is how I feel about this game.

It's not that the New characters are bad, but the very predictable WOKE writing dose nothing to make me care about these Ratchet & Clank Clones. It dose work great with Dr Nefarious. He was the star of the game, but the level design and puzzles were short and simple. If your target audience were 10 year old's then great job. If not then fire the woke writers and bring back your skilled level designers. The game was often very boring. That being said the final boss fight with Ratchet & Clank & Dr Nefarious was amazing.

The Armor collecting was pointless and added on to make this short game a bit longer, Armor should affect stats and buffs, but this game is lacking. If for some reason there were buffs ect. then I could not tell.
