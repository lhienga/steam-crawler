\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoyed this game. I am not even a Ratchet & Clank fan either. This was actually my very first R & C game played even though I had obviously heard of the series before. I simply had a smile on my face for the entire 20 hour journey!

This game feels like a playable Pixar film. The visuals are absolutely stunning and the game ran flawlessly in ultrawide with Ray tracing and all that good stuff.

Gameplay was easy but a good time regardless and searching for all the hidden collectables was actually entertaining (I normally hate collectable things). Game length (about 20 hours) was good for this type of game as well.

I will say this, if you can swing a PS5 Dual Sense Controller, you absolutely should get one for this game. It actually makes a huge difference. The Haptics and Special Trigger sense that this game employs is really very cool and worth the price of the controller IMO.

All in all, a very fun action packed romp that even non-R&C fans will enjoy. If you ARE a R&C fan this of course is a no brainer and you've probably already played this!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been a fan of the Ratchet & Clank games since the original on PS2. This is definitely the best entry in the series as it improves on the designs, animations, graphics, and weapons. They were able to expertly weave realistic textures and animations together with the classic R&C cartoon style which was a real showing of the skills of PlayStation Studios' artists.

While this kind of story has been done a millions times before, it still has a bit of charm from the comedic characters, but there's pretty much no surprises except for the maybe very young players. You don't go to many interesting places. Each location could be any other with a generic coat of paint like the pirate land, dinosaur planet, etc. The only interesting place is the starting city planet, but it really only serves as a tutorial location. It would have been great to come back to that place a few times and see it really expanded.

The gameplay is pretty good as usual for a R&C game. Nothing spectacular as it's just a shooter with some platforming, but it's quite smooth, very quick paced without feeling overwhelming and, in my play through at least, very stable with very few bugs / glitches to note. There were a few instances where I got stuck, or an enemy (emperor nefarious in one case during the final battle) making it easy to throw missiles freely at him for a bit until the next health-cased animation trigger.

Overall a very good entry in the series. Maybe the best, but I haven't played them all. The graphics and gameplay are good reasons to check this game out.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game with terrible issues.

Gameplay is incredibly fun, guns are awesome.

I found a bug that prevented me from getting the best weapon in the game, when I had 98% completion and had lost the opportunity to load a save for it. rip 16 hours I guess (playing on the hardest difficulty). I want to do ng+ but I feel like the experience is ruined. This bug has been known for at least a year and no fix. (tl;dr don't set the hoverboots to "toggle" because of cutscene bugs)

There's one arena challenge early on that is BY FAR the hardest part of the game. I have no idea how that got past playtesting, unless it was intentional for some reason? idk

The hoverboots deserve their own game. DOOD those are awesome.

Voice acting is great, very goofy B movie attitude like the old ones. OH and the Zim VA is in it, ++ for me. Story is meh but it doesn't matter for a game like this imo

+++ hoverboots
++ gun variety
++ platforming feels good (especially after hoverboots)
+ lots of customizing
+ lots of collecting and upgrading things
- some forced hand-holding at the beginning
-- difficulty spikes
-- stupid bird riding collectathon with hidden checkpoint and (temporarily) unreachable (but very reachable) collectables
--- speetles
--- extremely patronizing side characters demanding that I pursue the story objective at all times and never explore anything or look at the cool world design or they'll repeatedly scream in my ear that the objective isn't done yet or to go push a button or whatever
----- hoverboot cutscene bug

I recommend it, especially as a fan of the series, but I also don't. Get it on sale I guess\t\t\t\t\t
