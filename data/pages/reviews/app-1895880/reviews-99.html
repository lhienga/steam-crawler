\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was a lot of fun! I've been a fan of the series since the first one, and I was hoping they'd bring this to PC. As soon as I beat the game for the first time I launched right into challenge mode, just like the old days! I definitely would love to see more colorful cartoony action platforming out there. The only game like it I can think of in recent memory is Psychonauts 2

Not to mention this is actually a really good PC port, in a year of absolutely awful PC ports.

I had a mostly solid framerate throughout my playthrough, any time I had drops it was because it was the heat of battle with ten bajillion particles on screen. So it was like "fair." This game really lays into particles.

Would only recommend if you've got a 20 series or higher, because I suspect DLSS was saving me a lot of pain. But apparently this runs on Steam Deck so your mileage may vary. SSD helps tremendously too.

It runs circles around Jedi Survivor, even after all the patches so, hope that helps you.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have never bothered to write a Steam review before, but I felt this game warrants it. My PC config is an i7-11700, RX 6600 XT, 32 GB of DDR4 3200 memory and a WD BLACK SN850X. Certainly not a powerhouse PC, but I've gotten a solid 60fps on a variety of games by tweaking settings. Prior to the 1.808.0.0 patch, I was able to get a (mostly) locked 60 with mostly high settings. I was disappointed to not be able to try out the ray tracing, but at least the gameplay was solid. Now, even with ray tracing off and the settings dumbed down to medium across the board, I struggle to hit the high 40s. Just to make sure that it wasn't the updated AMD drivers causing my issues, I reverted back to the previous version of R&C. No surprise, I was hitting 60 with higher settings. Bug fixes and adding the possibility of ray tracing shouldn't have tanked my performance.

Bottom line, I'm extremely disappointed in this port. I'm able to run Returnal with ray tracing at 60 with decently high settings. Nixies has done a horrendous job at optimization with this game. And new updates shouldn't result in worse performance with lower settings than the previous version. Maybe by the time it's on sale for $15 it will finally be fully playable, but I'm not holding my breath.\t\t\t\t\t
