I had no issues playing on a GTX 1080 ti. However, playing on another pc with the newer AMD 7900XT it does crash frequently at certain spots in the story. This is the case even with raytracing disabled and with the amd driver that recently released for this game to enable raytracing. We hoped it wouldn't crash with raytracing off but it does, so playing on the older gtx 1080ti provides a better experience even though you'd have to lower the settings. So for AMD users I would wait for them to patch it, if you can't wait: it is playable thanks to the auto save feature, but expect some crashes where you have to restart the game in order to continue.

So right now I can only recommend for Nvidia.\t\t\t\t\t
