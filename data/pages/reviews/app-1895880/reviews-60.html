Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart - Otherworldly Joy. 9/10

Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart is a continuation on Insomniac's leading franchise. They, along with Nixxes have created a fantastic PC port of the formerly PS5 exclusive title. Rift Apart is a juicy one, running at a low/med settings config for my RTX 2060 to get up to 60FPS, but it still looks incredible running in 1080p. Fun, colourful and with a beautiful art direction, this feels like what AAA games should aim for on a visual level.

You play as the titular Ratchet & Clank character duo, on yet another classic adventure to put Dr Nefarious' plans to rest, this time travelling through time and space on the way. The game eplah itself I found to be very enjoyable, with an array of weapons for you to use and good movement controls, it felt fun to blast through each world.

I was amazed at how well each level loaded. There are segments where you will walk through world rifts into smaller realms for side content, and the transition between them was flawlessly smooth where I expected a slight stutter from the game. Absolutely incredible.

While shorter than most major releases, Rift Apart is definitely worth looking into I feel.\t\t\t\t\t
