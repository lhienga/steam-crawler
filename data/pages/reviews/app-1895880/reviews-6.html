\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best R&C game since Up Your Arsenal.

Pros:
+Visual spectacle, and will age gracefully
+The best gunplay in the series. It truly captures the best aspects of the PS2/PS3 games and modernizes them to a fine point.
+Solid level design with some great set pieces. Maybe not the highest in the series, but still solid.
+Engaging story - Rivet and Kit are great additions to the series, and Dr. Nefarious is as enjoyably hammy as ever.
+Loads of PC customization options

Cons:
-I don't remember the music
-The gun selection isn't my favorite. You have some good weapons, but there's no equivalent to the morph gun, a series staple. Furthermore, there are a few guns that are either redundant or simply bad. That's been a problem in the previous games, so it's not a new con. But it is a con nonetheless.

Overall:
8.5/10 - Very fun game that probably won't become a modern classic, but I can definitely see it being a cult hit with a loyal fanbase. I recommend the game to fans new and old, as the story is easy enough to follow without much background, and the characters are dynamic enough to where you can easily get a feel for the personalities without needing to play prior games.

The gameplay is very solid and fun, but I wish the weapons were a little more consistent and the music to be more impactful. If the series continues, which I hope it does, I really hope they bring it to PC because this was also a very solid port that offers loads of customization options to tune the game to your own liking.\t\t\t\t\t
