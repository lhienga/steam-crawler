\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay is sometimes fun, the weapons are cool and it looks nice, that's the extent of my praise.

Everything else is soulless, sterile, bland and generic, the music sounds like it's all been lifted from some royalty free website, compared to the original trilogy nothing here is memorable, the characters are all boring and lifeless, the dialogue sounds like it was written by a chatbot or some humorless out of touch corporate executive. I recall maybe 2 or 3 moments I thought were written by an actual human with a pulse, which is saying a lot since there's rarely a moment of silence. Story is similarly boring, I will likely forget it entirely in a few weeks. Their are only 9 levels all of which are incredibly short and linear, there's very few branching paths, no sense of exploration, optional content consists of jumping puzzles, arena battles on a flat grid, and perhaps 2 or 3 actual missions. You shoot the same 3 or 4 minibosses and the same 3 factions of enemies for 16 hours and you're done. This isn't a Ratchet and Clank game, it's a safe, market tested, passionless, corporate product.

Get an emulator and play the original series.\t\t\t\t\t
