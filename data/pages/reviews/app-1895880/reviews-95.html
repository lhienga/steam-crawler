\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn an age full of political agendas being pushed in most mainstream media, I'm glad to say that this game is just like a classic, but kind of corny Saturday morning cartoon. The story is quite tropey, but they handled it fairly decent. Rivet is a decent addition to the franchise as she isn't overbearing like many have feared. This game is appropriate for the whole family. The gameplay is really really fun. Lots of spectacle and and cool graphic effects even at low settings too. You do need decent hardware by 2023 standard though to have a smooth experience. I have an Intel i7 13700F, 32GB DDR5 RAM, RTX 3070 and running this off of an NVMe Samsung 970 EVO PLUS. The game loads in assets fairly decent and the whole portal mechanic doesn't really choke up my hardware. It's a split second longer than the PS5, but this game was built for that hardware and features that PC simply don't have access to yet. Their is technology being used at hand that tries to mitigate that, but it's not as fully baked as a hardware solution. I would recommend this game if you like lots of action and a light hearted story.\t\t\t\t\t
