\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game really made me miss the originals.

The pros first,
The game is quite good visually.
Guns are mostly ok, some sucked and some were no brainers i must use this the whole game. RYNO was kind of disappointing seeing as you got only 8 shots at max level and didn't hit bosses as hard as a RYNO should.
For what it was worth, the game was very enjoyable, i got 42/47 achievement on my first play through not really trying to 100% it first go. Although i don't know when i'll replay it as replaying it doesn't seem appealing atm.

Some Cons,
The characters do not stop trying to hold your hand through the game, I wanted to explore some planets and every 30 - 40 seconds the characters would tell me where to go. The game is very linear please let me explore the scenery at least?

Rivet and Ratchet's ... relationship? Feels quite forced. When Ratchet finally hears from Clank about who Rivet is. He almost immediately falls for her as does Rivet to Ratchet. Like you people haven't met yet you only heard from Clank that you are both the same race why do you care for each other like you have been married for 3 years? And when they do meet its almost like a high school crush like i don't fully understand why its forced so much let the characters develop through the story

The game is very short. Had i been full focused on the story it could easily be beaten within 8-10 hours. I don't know whether its because of the original story's but i remember the first three games being very long with a whole lot to do in them.

Id say get this game on sale and try it out but i still want Sony to give us the original 3 preferably the exact same but just on pc.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall fun game play. If you've played before it's exactly what you would expect out of a Ratchet & Clank game. TONS of soft lock opportunities. Playing this game for a total of about 14 hours now and way more than a handful of times I've found myself completely stuck in the graphics and having to restart at a check point even several instances I've been exploring and have had to restart because I ended up in areas I was never intended to end up which isn't a huge deal by itself but I didn't do any fancy jumps or glides to get to those areas I just got off the speedle at the wrong spot and there's no way back. Still lots of glitches and over sights to be worked out. Upon startup the game asks if you consent to them harvesting your game data to "improve game play" etc. however, if you select "learn more" it explains what data they're looking for and explains why then throws you into the game without ever asking again so I have no idea if they're harvesting my data for testing and development purposes or not because the choice was taken away from me for wanting to know more. Another detail I don't like regarding the game play is the forced 'tutorial' if you will. When you unlock weapon up grades it explains them then forces you to make several upgrades on a specific weapon rather than letting the player choose which upgrades to make. I admittedly got stuck in the weapons shop longer than I'd care to admit because the game forces you to go through all interactions in the menu - including testing a weapon out but doesn't flat out tell you that to get out of the weapons shop you need to do certain things, the tutorial screen pops up and simply lets you know what you can do and makes no indication that you need to do everything it lists in order to continue rather than let you learn on your own.\t\t\t\t\t
