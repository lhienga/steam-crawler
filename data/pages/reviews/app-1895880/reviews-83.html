\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere is the neutral recommendation option when u need it?
Look, it is a relatively good game. However, if you are a long standing fan of the franchise/originals than you are likely going to be more frustrated than excited with this game.

Graphics 10/10
(it's truly a next gen game, with not too bad performance. only issue at launch is the massive frame drop during combat. due to particles)
Game play 8/10
(overall a fun platform-er, great mobility from the player character. however, upgrades/ gear are given quite randomly/rushed. this feels somewhat out of place. a slower step by step means of obtaining these upgrades with better puzzles dedicated to the gear would have been better.)
Game length 4/10
(relatively short, but likely the cause of the incredibly fast transport options which makes the worlds way smaller than they actually are.)
Weapons 4/10
(the weapons aren't as unique as they used to be. they have less interesting mechanics that in the end often work rather similar, just with different graphics)
Story 7/10
(great cinematic cut-scenes, but lakes some personality and humor)
political messaging 1/10
(too much specific political messaging, it's fighting for equality while suppressing the other group. which is questionable, especially in a child's game. making them repeat political standpoints as part of a mini-game has similarities to indoctrination.)
puzzles 7/10
(generally fun, but rather simple.)
Characters 7/10
(Rivet is a cool character, it was always fun hearing her lines. Ratchet however... He has been changed too much from his original character. Generally, he is acting scared with anything and extremely shy. Listening to him is just generally frustrating.)
