\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's everything a classic fan would want. Gameplay, visuals, humor, and fan service are all top-notch and exceeded my expectations. I've heard some complain about the game being short, but I think it's just long enough to avoid overstaying its welcome. Each destination is stunning and incorporates a perfect mixture of exploration and linear pathways you'd expect in R&C. There is an abundance of weapons, most of which are fun to use. Enemy dialog will change depending on your weapon; it's an attention to detail that I particularly appreciate.

The only aspect of the game that I'm indifferent to is the soundtrack. The PS2 games always had a badass song for each destination playing in an infinite loop, whereas this game uses a dynamic soundtrack that changes depending on the action. Although this provides a more cinematic feel, the music overall is quite boring and immemorable. Another small gripe: I miss the long/high jump use of the Heli-Pack! Although the hoverboots and tether are functional (and subjectively better) replacements, I do miss that classic movement.

I'm glad that Ratchet and Clank are around after all this time. This game would've been the only reason for me to get a PS5 so I'm grateful that it has been ported to PC. I can only hope that Insomniac gives us another great R&C experience soon!\t\t\t\t\t
