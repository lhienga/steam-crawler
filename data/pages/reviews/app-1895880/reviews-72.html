\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely adore the Ratchet and Clank series, and played most of the games over the years. The different guns in Rift Apart are great and whimsical, as always; the story is fine, the voice acting is great!

And in this installment the two positive outliers are the Set pieces and the movements of the models. The characters just have a natural fluidity to them that I hadn't noticed before, and the setpieces make you feel like a GD superhero - even though they are not particularly challenging, they FEEL challenging, if you know what I mean.

The challenge level is one of the weaker points, honestly. On the standard difficulty, the only fight that gave me any real challenge was the final boss. It is a little better in NG+, and maybe on higher difficulties but I haven't tried those yet. With a lot of exploration, it took me approx. 30 hours to complete the story with 96% completion. So it's not the longest game, but the NG+ just tacks on more hours of fun.

Oh, and lastly, I am Dutch and the Dutch voice acting is fantastic! The fact that it is easily available in Dutch on Steam is great and the translations are really well done.\t\t\t\t\t
