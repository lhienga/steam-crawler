\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's Ratchet & Clank, but now on PC. If you haven't played one of these before, definitely give it a try.

Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart is an action 3D platformer with a focus on spectacle, but not too difficult to finish. Expect to finish the main story within 6 hours. More if you try to find all the collectables. The game feels a little shorter than some of the previous games in the series, but the quality is pretty high. I personally don't need all my games to be 80 hour epics (that I don't finish anyway). I'm a bit disappointed that the new playable character Rivet doesn't have her own unique play style. That's a bit of a missed opportunity.

On the technical side the PC port is absolutely great and a real showcase for how console ports should be done. The game will also play well on the Steam Deck, though there is the occasional glitch (particularly in the weapon upgrade shop and when opening Steam menus). Controls are fully configurable through Steam input. There are plenty of settings to adjust how the game runs and plays.

Very much recommended. Playstation is clearly taking console ports more seriously now.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaven't finished yet, about 35% of the way to 100% the game in around 8 hours, so 30-35 hours seems about as much as you'll get out of this game. That being said, if you use to like the original Ratchet and Clank games on the PS2 then you'll like this one. It's got it's gimmick like all the other games, but just like those this one works well, the characters are great, and the combat is fluid and fun; even on the highest difficulty I don't feel overly punished when I mess up. If you haven't played a Ratchet and Clank game before but like arcade action shooters this will be right down your alley, all the guns are great, they feel different and you can upgrade them into super weapons that function fantastic in the situations they are suited best for. My only real complaint is that I wish this game was a bit longer, it's been so long since I've gotten to play a Ratchet and Clank game I'm really dreading finishing it.\t\t\t\t\t
