\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(STEAM DECK VERIFIED) No it’s not, I know it says it is, I have an OLED and it doesn’t work.
I don’t know where to begin with this game maybe the good parts of the game? Just the guns, it’s nothing new and exciting and that’s ok, it’s a Ratchet and Clank game, the gunplay hasn’t really evolved in 20 years and it works. It’s fun. Except when the enemies are too easy. The graphics are nice, very pretty game. Well done there.

The game feels like a PS5 tech demo rather than a game. It was just entirely about the graphics and really nothing more. The enemy variety was sparse, I couldn’t believe I was fighting the same bosses 5 times, every mob I fought looked the same. I can’t believe how lazy or understaffed every team was other than the graphics programmers.
The level design is awful, I could skip entire sections using the hover boots and I wasn’t even trying. Who thought this was a good idea to use on other planets other than the one you get them on? The game is short at around 12 hours long and that’s if you’re slowing down and trying enjoy it.
The game is bland, it’s just the main story and nothing else, there’s no side missions at all, so if you enjoyed the older stuff like the quark minigames and hoverboard riding or collecting crystals in that huge desert then forget it bud, you get a short movie with some gameplay in-between. I guess you could count the arena? Except there’s only 15 missions and they’re all genuinely not interesting or challenging.

The writing is even worse, there is no crude humour, no jokes. The target demographic is for 6-year-olds. Where’s quark in the nurse outfit, where’s Courtney GEARS!?! The game is too safe, it’s boring, I legitimately zone out when dialogue was happening because it was genuinely cringe, weak little man babbling or just words with nothing of substance.

They’ve assassinated Ratchets character completely, he’s normally a witty goofy and fun and a genuine hero and in this game he’s just an INFERIOR, WEAK, INSECURE, LITTLE MAN Lombax that is scawwed of meeting other wombaxes!! >w< Compared to the STRONGER, HEROIC, SAVIOR, Rivet. He’s scared of riding on a water snail thing and terrified of skeletons with swords. In the very first Ratchet and Clank the guy was blasting at top speed on a hoverboard and doing tricks with it. He was also fighting way more terrifying creatures than a skeleton. Clank even whines about these skeletons so much I was genuinely cringing. What the hell are they doing to my boys!?!?

The writers really want you to love Rivet more in a game called Ratchet and Clank despite having no reason to. She has no history, she’s just a girl Ratchet Also, if the game is so dumbed down for children why are there political agendas being pushed, for example there’s pirates singing something along the lines of “of women in respectable positions of authority”. Why does this need to even be said? It’s 2024 not the 1960’s no one thinks women are inferior anymore. It’s just bizarre to have this kind of thing wedged into any media.

Maybe keep Ratchet and Clank crude and good. Focus less on the graphics and get some good writers, try harder on the level design, you’ve been making these games for 20+ years, the recipe for a successful R&C game is in front of you but you chose to ignore the entire history of the game and “start fresh” so to speak. If you want to keep making games aimed towards toddlers and push women’s agenda onto them, you can make a spin off series for that audience and call it Rivet and Kit that way you don’t alienate the original fans who grew up with the game.

I paid something like $50 for this game and I felt robbed. I feel bad for whoever bought this game full price for $110 AUD\t\t\t\t\t
