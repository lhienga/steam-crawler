\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery good game, the gameplay and design are all on par for Ratchet and Clank, which means really well done, the only con is that I wanted more.

I completed the game and collected all of the weapons and upgrades in about half the time I actually wanted to complete it. I maxed the weapons out very quickly, and the upgrades themselves weren't that exciting. They were pretty much just slight iterations on all of the other weapons in the old games, and sometimes even simpler. This game also seemed to remove a few mechanics from other games, such as high jumping and the suit upgrades are all purely cosmetic and not too interesting to collect. The gold bolts are always fun to collect and get new abilities, but again, just re-used the abilities/cheats from the other games.

Overall, I had a lot of fun playing it, but it's just about on par with the other games.. It's a good second installment for PC, but when comparing to the other games just on PS2, I hoped they would add some new mechanics or creative weapons, or more to explore and collect in general.

If you've never played a ratchet game, it's definitely going to be fresh and fun, if not just a little short to 100%, which can also be seen as a positive to some people.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you enjoyed the other Ratchet & Clank games, you will also like this one. A fun game as always. However, I think that the difficulty in this game has become "easier" since I last played the series. The most difficult setting isn't as brutal as previous games. The platinum achievement is much simpler than previous games. I remember you had to get all the gold bolts before, and turn the game on hardest + challenger mode. This time you just had to get 5 gold bolts and beat the game on whatever difficult.

I think they could have done more regarding upgrading the guns, as guns is a big part of the series. There were also to many collectibles (bears, gold bolts, RINO and armor) for my taste, but I did get them all.
I think previous games "tools of destruction" and "crack in time" had a better story and was more fun in my opinion.
Game performance: overall good performance, played on low settings to get more fps. Note the character can get stuck some places if you try.
