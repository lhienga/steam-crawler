\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI thought I would like this game because I grew up playing Ratchet & Clank, but it is not as good as the PS2 games. I'm not saying that because I'm nostalgic, there are genuinely a few things I noticed that seemed missing or not as good. The area mission series is lackluster or interesting, there is no racing circuit/series, most of the gadgets are just given to you and the ones you earn aren't really a mission (however Ratchet is an experience hero so you can argue he shouldn't have to earn it again). But most importantly, the plot and characters are not as edgy as the originals.

In the first game Ratchet was an eco-terrorist fighting to save his home planet. In the second game Ratchet fights an evil corporation that is covering up that one of its products kills people. In the third game, with Dr Nefarious, it is a little more of the generic 'save the galaxy' plot, but you can argue he is stopping a genocide of organic lifeforms. In this game it feels like the plot is to stop the bad guy because he is bad and it is hard to care about the story.

The game is fun if you haven't played any other Ratchet & Clank games, but a really disappointing game if you have played them before\t\t\t\t\t
