\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a masterpiece and one of the best video games ever made. I don't normally try to 100% a game, but this one was a blast so I did.

The progression system is very fun and there are tons of skins/extras to unlock. There are no arbitrary paywalls or microtransactions, which is great. Everything can be unlocked in-game.

The animations and graphic quality are movie-like and reminiscent of a Pixar film.

The story is very good and I love the characters. Ratchet, Rivet, Clank, and Kit are all adorable and I love the interactions they have with each other. Dr Nefarious and Emperor Nefarious are great villains and can be funny at times.

The gameplay is also very good. There are many weapons to unlock, upgrade, and use against your foes.

Overall, this is an amazing video game and I highly recommend it!
