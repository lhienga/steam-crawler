Cool graphics, great music

Creative use of different (too easy) minigame elements, but the main gameplay itself was unintuitive and way too detail oriented. Also, to find the different endings, I had to use a guide. I would have never quessed how to complete the different tasks without outside help. So don't waste your time trying to figure it out yourself...

Also the game glorifies being a stalker: "Taking the time to take to know me" means stalking her profile and liking all her posts? I would think that relationships require communication, and not just reading through each others social media to obsessively learn everything the other likes and dislikes..?

At least the DISTANCE doesn't force a right ending. Both "So Far, So Close" and "So Close, So Far" seemed to be around equal. Even tho the game doesn't underline this. Sometimes love is letting someone go, and not being selfish in a relationship requiring them to do all the sacrifices while you do none.

I really wanted to like this game, but I just can't say with a clear conscience that this is worth a while. Sadly, I do not reccommend.\t\t\t\t\t
