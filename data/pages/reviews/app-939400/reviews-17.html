\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe designer of this game is obviously female. Moreover, she clearly lacks any romantic experience. She simply designed a so-called romance game based on her fantasies. Both the content and design of the game are just a stack of simple mini-games, lacking any novelty. Too many elements are hidden and triggered outside of the dialogues, and the game lacks proper guidance, essentially reflecting a common issue in today's female perspective on romance: "You have to guess, if you guess wrong, it means you don't understand me, and if you don't understand me, it means you don't love me." The entire game is filled with unrealistic fantasies, even if you fulfill 90% of the designer's demands, you still get an unsatisfactory result. In short, it's permeated with immature ideas and demands on men to unilaterally sacrifice for women. Looking back at the levels within the game, they all demand men to think and sacrifice for women, while women just need to enjoy these benefits. Personally, I suggest not buying it because it's really foolish.\t\t\t\t\t
