\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI never been in a relationship, so I had no idea about the norm/standard when it comes to dating. In my first playthrough, I had a bad ending, so I decided to repeat the game and made other choices to see different outcomes. The more I played, the more I realized that there weren't any good choices for certain scenarios as I always ended up with a bad ending. I then proceed to wonder that maybe there can be another choice that best fits the scenario. As a result, I was playing around with my mouse then discovered that I can actually interact with the images.

The lessons that I could learn from this game are that:
- Love is simple and it just us that makes it complicated.
- Trust and constant communication is a must have in a relationship.
- True Love and Friendship will last no matter the distance.
- There is no standard when it comes to love, as it may vary from one person to another.
- If you truly love someone, you shouldn't expect anything in return and just wish the best for them even it means no longer being part of their life. You may not end up with them but at least you had change them for the better.

I was expecting a short visual novel type of gameplay but this game exceeded my expectation. Thank you very much developers for the awesome game!\t\t\t\t\t
