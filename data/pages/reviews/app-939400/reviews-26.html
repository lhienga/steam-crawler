I still wouldn't recommend it as a good game but feel free to do a speed run yourself and return it. If you don't want to go through the hassle, the "lessons" in the game are this:

----First Story----

1. Good first impression and make it natural

2. Like and be aware of her world through her social media

3. Using tip #2, find out her likes and dislikes on foods, movies, activities, etc.

4. Using tip #3, plan a good date

5. Read the mood correctly, sometimes it's better to not talk or ask questions and just go with the flow

6. Share your interests, passion, beliefs, goals, dreams. Big and small.

7. When you get closer with your partner, you both will find annoying things about each other. But during those times when things are about to escalate, don't insult each other. Instead, remember that your relationship is stronger and it means so much more than the little argument you guys were having.

8. Be a good listener. This means being by her side when she's stressed out. Agreeing with her, instead of saying remarks that will belittle her frustration. Counseling yet prioritizing her feelings first.

9. You both pick up after yourselves and each other. You're both not soloing life anymore. You're not a burden and she's not a burden. There will be different days where one of you two will have to pull more weight than the other person. Do it without holding grudges. Do it with the appreciation that you have each other.

----Second Story----

1. In a long distance relationship, stay up to date with their likes and dislikes.

2. Explore and enjoy the things they're interested in.

3. When you both have different dreams you two are pursing, let each other pursue those dreams. Even when it's hurts not being with the person physically, it hurts more to have one of you give up your dream. Giving up your dream also means giving up a part of yourself.

----Third Story----
I didn't pay too much attention on this one but here's what I think it was trying to teach

1. Trust your partner. When it seems like they're isolating themselves, trust them. When an ex is trying to contact them, trust that they can handle it.

2. It's okay to investigate, but not okay to doubt them or to accuse them unless you have actual evidence.

3. Remind yourself of the love you share with them.\t\t\t\t\t
