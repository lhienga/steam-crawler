And as you'd expect, these morals and attitudes mirror the Asian Dating culture and some things might not make much sense to westerners.

The game is more like "I wanna be the guy" x visual novel. You're supposed to find hidden options instead of choosing the seemingly 2-4 limited obvious choices. (I suppose the game is trying to say actions are better than words.)
