\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoveChoice拣爱 is a visual novel-esque game with 3 short stories jumbled into a game, from my initial point of view yeah, it's cute overall game at first glance, boy was I in for a ride.

I was so baffled when at the start I got the bad ending straightaway, but as I kept on replaying Love · Game on my 2nd and 3rd play through it struck me.

Sometimes it's not just words you need, your actions matter.

Simple really but it really struck a cord in me, sometimes giving generic replies and answers isn't always the case. You have to show it that you mean it. It surprised me when I could not just gave a reply back to the person, but actually interact with the moving background. It was so cute how it broke the 4th wall by making you not just give a reply in the text box but actually interacting with the environment making you an observant person.

As with how things goes too there's a moment in the Love · Game, where you are arguing with your significant other you would assume and click on the dialogue insinuating you to bite back and get even more frustrated but really the correct thing was to let it go and cool off. Just by not interacting back at all you really are doing the right thing.

Generally when it comes to visual novel-type games there really isn't much to say, you read, you click, you save rinse and repeat, but the twist in this visual novel is that you can either continue with the given prompts or interact with said background. Minor thing but it can puts things into perspective with you missing cues or whether or not you're supposed to ignore it.

For the story, I've completed all 3 mini-love stories that are included in this visual novel. Below are my thoughts.

In Love · Game, I've loved every single aspect of it from the start to the end. How it was handled, how the characters interact and of course the endings I've gotten. Good or bad, they're both important valuable lessons that were taught here and there.

In Love · Distance like it's in its name, distance can and will change a person. Without spoiling too much, time will inevitably change certain aspects of an relationship whether people agree or disagree. There are plenty of choices where you think you would've done or could've done but ultimately it is best if we go our own separate ways, to find what truly matters for yourself.

In Love · Detective, I already went in with a raised eyebrow assuming as what the previous titles meant for it's story, and you would've guessed it is... detective work alright. I didn't know what I had in store for me when I realized.... sometimes choosing not to know is the right choice, not saying to be ignorant of the choice you made but choosing to believe your person.

Music is quite subjective, so from what I've listened it's generally fits the theme of this game and mood. When you first met, when you're talking, when you're arguing, when you're being sentimental. The simplicity of the melody brings out the best in what's happening currently. So, yes I do love the music, thank you MIDIPanda.

All in all, I'm very happy how the game is and I'm suuuuper satisfied and gave me tons of fuzzy warmth feelings in the inside.\t\t\t\t\t
