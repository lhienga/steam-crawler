You have 3 stories: love story, long distance relationship and love detective

I recomend to go blindly on the stories, especially when playing the 1st one. It will show you want you believe it's a relationship to you and how you value your partner. It is interesting that this games teaches you with the gameplay how important it is to listen to the person you love... or not say a thing and be at their side; and how relationships are built on strong bonds that you have to cultivate (even if it's friendship) by supporting each other, even if you don't like what the other person is doing or it's a bit of a inconvinience to your "story" or "desireable end".

Game, with all three stories, lasts round 60 - 90 mins. Totally worth it, Gameplay is a click & drop in most cases, but you'll figure it out while playing.
