If you're looking for a Zelda-like you might get that, but there's extra steps that made the game very interesting to me however this may bother someone looking for something more straight forward.

You might have scanned the suggested product tags, saw "puzzle" and you're wondering what that means, you're in for a bit more than you might have expected. In a very pleasant way.

I want to stay pretty vague because the switch in experience was really exciting to me. If you enjoy really deep puzzles, pick up Tunic trust me. If you're looking for a Top-Down Adventure game, you'll possibly get that too, but there's a lot more to Tunic if you're willing to scratch the surface.\t\t\t\t\t
