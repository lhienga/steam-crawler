A fusion of a puzzle game with souls-like combat. With a great soundtrack and cute art style.
During your playthrough, the game open ups to you not only by granting you new traversal abilities but also by revealing to you previously hidden knowledge about the system of the game. Parceling out the knowledge in the form of pages of a retro-style instruction booklet is such a unique and genius idea.
The combat is comparably the weakest point but still fun. The bosses are quite difficult and some may find the spike in difficulty jarring. I personally enjoyed the boss fights and found them to be a welcome change of pace. If you're just here for the puzzle and exploration, there are options in the settings to make the combat more accessible.
I would recommend this game to, honestly, anyone who likes games.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I'm past the return point and can't refund therefore I have a right to write this review.

First of all I want to list the positives about this game. There are a lot of people who can and will enjoy this game.

1. It's got really nice visuals and feel. The game runs smooth and the colors and lighting are all excellent.
2. Simple to play, easy to run around and the running speed of the character is good.
3. Combat was actually good in this gaame.

Negatives - Why I don't recommend.

1. Navigation is annoying. While I get there is a manual you can use to get your position as long as you find the pages, I find that this game requires wandering around everywhere in order to sometimes figure out where to go next. Doorways aren't well highlighted and neither are pathways. The isometric camera also doesn't help because I remember being in a dungeon for 30 minutes until I realized there was a doorway on the lower left hand corner of the map. Then there are a lot of pathways here and there.

2. I don't like the game not using a real language to give direction. While you can figure it out, it's not fun to figure out how to use something.

3. Too much of this game is trial and error. A lot of things require you to know or try many different things to figure something out. The path forward isn't always clear either.

4. Bosses have way too much health and it annoying to fight with the stamina gauge.\t\t\t\t\t
