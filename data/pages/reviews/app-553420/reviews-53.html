\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTunic is a challenging but ultimately delightful game. Much like Hollow Knight, it's a labor of love from a small dev team. And much like Hollow Knight combined classic Metroidvania gameplay with charming graphics and Soulslike elements to create a work of art, Tunic does the same with a Zelda-like format. Like most Soulslike games, one has to figure out the story and what happened to the world, and boy are there moments where your mouth will drop open. I enjoyed Tunic immensely and cannot recommend it highly enough.\t\t\t\t\t
