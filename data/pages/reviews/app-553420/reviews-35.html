\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like Zelda with a little bit of souls in it, play Tunic. Tunic is very reliant on secrets and puzzles so try not to look anything up if you can help it if you're stuck, some of the game's hardest puzzles are far too rewarding to solve to skip. The puzzles range from zelda puzzle difficulty, to some of, if not the hardest puzzles I have seen in gaming so if you know you don't like it when you have to think too hard and take notes in a notepad, you might not finish the game.
The world isn't very large but it makes up for it with good design and how dense it is with puzzles that you wont even know are there until later in the game, and then some more puzzles later. The combat it souls-y but very simplistic and is probably the lowest part of the game, but still passable at the very least.

All in all 8/10. Great throughout but wished it was a bit longer and had more, in a traditional sense.\t\t\t\t\t
