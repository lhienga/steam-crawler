\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInitially, I wanted to recommend this game for its beautiful and mysterious Zelda'ish game world. Nearing the end of the game, I've decided to give it a thumbs down, instead. The biggest offender is the absolute horrible boss design, which does not fit the rest of the game. I guess the devs kinda knew this by giving you an option to toggle god mode, when you don't want to bother with the fights. This poor design is just a major red flag I cannot overlook. That's coming from someone who likes souls-like games and enjoys a challenge.

Going a little bit more into the details of the boss fights. The "spider" boss is badly designed because you can just hide underneath it and spam it to death. The bosses after that have a horrible camera angle that keeps rotating. Some bosses move too fast compared to how slow and clunky you move. In the ghost world you'll have to fight bosses, while you have lost all your power, which is another annoying mechanic. Lastly, the cathedral boss with all the waves was an exercise in futility. [EDIT] Also, just beat the final boss, which compounds all the aforementioned issues. Camera rotation is even more nauseating, especially when locked-on. This boss (some others too) can stunlock you to death. Except, now you have a newly gained insane mobility buff, which allows you to dodge almost every attack with ease. What's even the point then? Man, I don't understand how the devs could have screwed this one aspect up so badly.

Just a waste, really, because the rest of the game is great.\t\t\t\t\t
