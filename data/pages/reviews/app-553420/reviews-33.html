\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love it! If you have ever played the original NES Zelda game this is a must play.
I love that there is really no way to be sure whether you are doing things the "right way" but there certainly is as many guides say so.
By that I mean this in a good way. Because you most certainly do things out of order right away. As for me walking in areas where I felt like there was a huge difficulty spike but in reality I was technically not be there. And the game makes it all feel like the world is truly one.
You will find paths that you probably did not realize were even there before and feel dumb that you did not even look for when you are on the "correct" path.
The game manual is so filled with love and it feels so nostalgic when looking at it as if I am 5 years old looking back at my NES game manuals.
The gameplay I would describe as Zelda combat. You can lock on to enemies to focus on them to help fighting much easier similar to as most 3D Zelda games are.
Overall the art style, the gameplay, and overall feel of the game are all so refreshing on this.
10/10 for me!\t\t\t\t\t
