\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game lacks overall balance and fairness near its end... It kinda just goes off the rails. The beginning has nice cues and hints for how to progress that makes the middle feel really good, but the endgame relies on mechanics such as non-cued invisible bridges, poorly choreographed attacks, enemy spamming, and not resetting consumables on failed boss attempts. Achievements suggest 70%+ of players agree that the middle of the game is the correct time to put it down.

There's a lot of minor cuts that make endgame play less fun. Your 40+ pages of "ingame manual" cross references itself plenty of times, yet there is no way to "seek to a specific page". Instead, you need to hold left/right to flip through the pages and wait. Not an issue when you only have <10 pages, but annoying when you have all 56.

Lacks the cryptography cue quality of FEZ and the combat tightness/fairness of Hollow Knight. Check those out if you haven't yet instead.\t\t\t\t\t
