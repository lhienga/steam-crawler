However, Tunic is far more difficult than anything you might find in Zelda, especially in terms of combat. Particularly annoying enemies that appear in the later part of the game did hurt my enjoyment of this game. Fortunately, there are a couple accessibility options if combat seems too difficult and you'd simply like to progress.

Despite that, Tunic is one of the best games I have played, if not the best, when it comes to a sense of discovery and adventure. Much of the game will be spent exploring, finding hidden paths and rooms, or deciphering the game's manual, which which is written in a strange language. Tunic never spells anything out for you. The story itself seems to be a puzzle to solve with what little you are shown.\t\t\t\t\t
