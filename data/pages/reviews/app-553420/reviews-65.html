\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've never in my life soured on a game as fast and hard as I did at the ending segment of this game. About half of the true ending puzzle segments in this game are just so overly convoluted for seemingly no reason. The game goes from being one of the most brilliantly designed games I've played in forever to the most overly obtuse nonsense I will never touch again in one fell swoop. It is so, so unfortunate that it decides that it needs to be every bad puzzle you remember from point and click adventure games all rolled into one right at the climax of the game. I'm telling you, I went from the giddiest, "I can't wait to recommend this to EVERYONE!" to complete apathy with it so quickly. I solved about 3/4's of the endgame puzzles by myself and then shut it off. My only guess is due to one of the pages saying "Share your wisdom" that the game wanted people to go on the internet and talk about it to try to emulate something from days past, but unfortunately for me I would like to solve my own puzzles instead of looking them up and ruining the whole point. Frustrating stuff.\t\t\t\t\t
