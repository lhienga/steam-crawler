\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is fantastic, I loved the mystery/intrigue of the world and was hooked until I completed it. Honestly one of the better, more compelling games I've played in recent years. And definitely amongst the most clever and creative.

(++) Awesome world filled with many, many secrets. It's an absolute joy to discover stuff, and was an even greater joy to see all the layers I missed after I completed it. I got addicted to coming back to it to see what was next
(+) Music is excellent. Gets stuck in your head
(+) "guidebook" is really cool, and adds to the nostalgia for an earlier era of gaming. No real tutorial, and it works great (mostly)

(=) A lot of the gameplay is typical zelda/metroidvania stuff. Not terrible, but a bit rote as a core gameplay loop (i.e. you see heavy bushes, you know you need to unlock some item and come back later to go down that path).

(-) I did not enjoy the combat all that much, and its a major part of the game. I think they either needed to reduce the importance of combat (and focus on the puzzle/discovery side); OR make combat feel a lot better. Even having a longer sword might go a long way. A few of the bosses were of particular difficulty for me (which works better in games like Elden Ring or Hades, where the combat is actually decently fun)\t\t\t\t\t
