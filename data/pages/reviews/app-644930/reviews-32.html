\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really interesting take on an existing system, but ultimately poorly executed.

This game reminds me of Starcraft and Red Alert 2/3, with a new exciting twist of surviving a 'big final wave' each map instead of constant small-to-medium battles. I really enjoyed the idea of the resources and the developments tracks you could spend points on.

But ultimately with no save game ability and extreme punishment for small mistakes even on the suggested first timer difficulty far too often did hours of effort result in no progress. A few seconds of inattention could take you from feeling as though you were well on your way to victory, 30 minutes in, to sudden unstoppable defeat. Or even worse, after setting up what seems like an impenetrable fortress having it razed despite taking out 80% of the final wave. Or not correctly navigating the research tree even at early levels. Call it a skill issue if you want, but be warned ahead of time.

A lot of potential, sadly wasted. I won't be back.\t\t\t\t\t
