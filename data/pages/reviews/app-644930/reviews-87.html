\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm trying to like the game. The premise is cool, the controls are fine, we have a neat tech tree, the way you select and get missions is fine. What is not fine is the game just straight up feeding you misinformation on top of having a terrible system to do it with. When the game tells me an attack is coming from the east, and the east only, do not also send just as many from the west. Especially when this game does NOT have a save/load feature. On maps that can take you literally hours to try and complete. This is not told to you when you start the campaign. you get punished for failing maps for some reason. Oh, did you just spend three hours on a map only to have the game lie to you at the end and then screw you over? Well you, there's no load system. Researched a new building that you want to try out? Well if you misplaced it, screw you. All in the name of 'spirit of the game'. Well if this is the spirit of the game I'll call an exorcist because unless you're a masochist that likes losing hours of progress as the untold premise of the game, I think it needs an update to let you load progress at least on lower difficulties. Or it needs to stop lying to you and be clear with its information.

Edit: I've played it a bit more (I can't refund it so I might as well) and there is a good game here, it's just held back a few major flaws on which I still just cannot recommend it to people. An RTS simply -needs- a save/load system if there is a campaign. Especially this game where if you are going into a map blind, you have no idea where you need to expand to for resources, where the enemies will come from or how hard you need to prepare for the waves. It's great that they want to have you feel 'consequences' for doing it wrong, but all that happens here is, you're doing 90% of the map correctly, fail the last 10%, restart the map, and do the first 90% in exactly the same way. It's trial and error in a genre that has a save-load system needed to facilitate it. And sure, if you want people to do it right on the first try, you can. Just give a failure punishment whenever a previous save is loaded. Just don't make me repeat 90% of a map I almost cleared, because right now every single time I fail a map I quit the game out of sheer frustration of having to do it all again.\t\t\t\t\t
