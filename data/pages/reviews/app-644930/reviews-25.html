\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCore game is good, but the devs have been lazy on so many things I need to give it a thumbs down.

First of all, campaign consists of 3 things: Regular missions, Swarm annihilation, hero missions. Regular missions are why you play the game. Swarm annihilations vary from being a pain in the neck to just being utterly boring time wastes (most of them being the latter), and the hero missions are even worse. But, the hero missions give the same amount of research points as 2 regular missions, so you feel obligated to do them not to fall behind on tech level. In my opinion, the two latter modes should have been entirely optional because they are subtracting heavily from the experience of the game, and are extremely underdeveloped as game modes. I mean, the extermination thing is just "you have an outpost in the middle, and you'll be zergrushed from all sides" over and over and over again. There were so many other possible options for how they could have made that mode, and they just did one and called it a day.

The UX is also woefully underdeveloped. You end up muting half the interface sounds because if you don't, you won't hear anything else once it gets to a certain point. Sound mixing is kinda bad in that all sounds are as if they're right next to you, despite being a bird's eye game. There's no help in placing buildings in an optimal way, which is needed as the game forces you to build insane amounts of huts to get the necessary population.

And this is a shame, because the main game mode is enjoyable. But I'm just so tired of game devs being this sloppy and getting away with it. This game would ironically enough be better if it tried to do less. And a bad UX is unforgivable after this long.\t\t\t\t\t
