\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever in my life have I suffered such advanced mental anguish from a game. You ever see that episode of Seinfeld where George tells the guy "...the jerk store called and they are all out of you!"? That is this game when you lose a mission and then you think of a strategy to win, you go back into the mission get back to the same point again, then get destroyed by some special you didn't know about and all of a sudden there is just a chorus of "Whats it matter, you are their all time best seller!" This game is masochist simulator and was more addicting than the child of vape pens and cigarettes. The game also gave me ADHD because after playing for multiple hours I was completely out of dopamine. This became especially bad after multiple days in a row of playing which have caused me to develop holes in my brain leading to memory loss likely similar to the NPCs that occupy this game. Please don't buy this game for your own sake as I have lost multiple friends since beginning the campaign.

Finally that counter in the beginning of the game, despite what the devs tell you is not the amount of zombies killed in total, it is actually the combined amount of times that everyone who played this game wanted to end it all IRL.\t\t\t\t\t
