Building, researching, resource managing, unit control, etc. are similar to others in the genre, but zombies give it a special touch. You have to expand your base carefully while clearing surrounding monsters, who get more powerful further you go from starting point and defend against progressively larger hordes every few in-game days. In campaign there are two more types of missions - hero clearing the map and defence against hordes without base. Game has mostly steampunk aesthetics, even though there is oil present. Audio is okay, music and sounds are fitting, and you'd probably remember some tracks as you'd be hearing them often and as they are memorable. Plot is rather predictable if you're into sci-fi and have read\\watched some classics, but it's still doing it's job of setting the world and explaining the situation, how it all started, etc.\t\t\t\t\t
