\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great RTS game. It is very well made, looks beautiful and runs smooth but more important it's really fun and challenging. I've never played the campaign so I can't comment on that. I really enjoy the survival mode so all my time has been on that apart from a few hours recently spent on custom maps which add a whole other level of endless creative maps to play.
This game is stressful if you increase the difficulty along with your learning curve but that's what makes it enjoyable. It requires alot of micro-management but can be paused when things get stressful and you can still build and give yourself time to think.
Watch some videos on Youtube before you play if you are easily stressed because it's not for the faint of heart and one mistake can cost you hours of play as the game is unforgiving.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is very fun, but if you do not enjoy difficult games then this might not be the game for you. This is an RTS game where you build a base to withstand big waves of zombies that get larger over time, until the final wave where they come from every direction. The map is full of zombies as well that you must clear out throughout the game, unless you want the final wave to include all the zombies on the map. There are different maps that each generate different ways every time you play, but have the same general setup. The game has a lot of little things that make it difficult, but it's well worth taking the time to learn and play, as I have had a lot of fun with it.
As a side note, the campaign is not the most fun thing in this game, and I would recommend not stressing about playing it. The survival mode is the main reason to play the game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEven after so long and less updates, the game is still a great game after all this time.

Pros:
- Flexible playing times being able to pause at any moment
- Challenging because if ONE zombie gets into your colony, youre basically ♥♥♥♥
- Sound is surprisingly distinctive, with every building and unit (zombies included) each having a different set of sound effects
- Ambient music can put you on edge, until the wave music hits, then youre just jammin
- Have I mentioned that the models are really high quality? CAUSE THEY ARE

Cons:
- Normally will play in bursts, because unless at some of the highest difficulties and challenges with normal maps or campaigns, then the replayability kinda goes down
- As said before, this far into development brings very few if any updates, so you wont get much past this point for this game :I

Overall: 8/10, would highly recommend to those who like a combination of progression, RTS, and city building.\t\t\t\t\t

The main maps are great with tons of replay value as you learn the mechanics and push toward your favorite level of difficulty. If you enjoy RTS games I highly reccomend They Are Billions, with all of its warts because it truly is a stellar game!\t\t\t\t\t
