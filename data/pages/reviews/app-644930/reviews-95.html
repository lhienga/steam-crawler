\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game. Hardcore with a bit of a learning curve. Most addictive RTS I've ever played. So much more nuisance to this than most RTSes. Love the post apocalyptic zombie steam punk theme. Appearance and sound design is sharp and well done. A lot of effort was put into this and it shows.
Only non-game play complaint, which is very minor in my opinion especially since everything else is tiptop, is the voice acting. Bad voice acting on more than just the delivery of lines, the atrocious dialogue is obviously written by a non native English speaker. A good voice actor would likely add their own input to improve the lines.

I mostly want to ask the DEVS to please for the LOVE OF GOD... please fix having to collect empire and research points on the hero missions. Those missions are a welcome breath of fresh air from the normal ones, but it is beyond infuriating clearing the map of enemies only to have to TEDIOUSLY scan with your mouse pointer for tiny, barely noticeable missed items, that could look like anything. There is absolutely no rhyme or reason that this even remotely adds to making the game any more difficult. Just a big pain in the ass.
Also, one other somewhat major issue that needs tweekingm is unit pathing in conjunction with other units. They run in place against other units for way too long, sometimes indefinitely if you let them, before going around.

Otherwise, good freaking job on everything else in this well crafted game.\t\t\t\t\t
