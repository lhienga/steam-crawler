\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely love the concept of this game. The steampunk designs, the predictable story, and the general campy feeling of the game. There are tech trees for the RTS portion where it'll improve your base; however you'll need to choose which upgrades you want or think it'll be a waste. The other portion involves taking 2 characters to explore a base which breaks the base building portion. It breaks the sameness of a building and go for horror exploration; at the same time if you are wanting extra tech or units during a survival, you'll spend an extra 5-15 minutes pixel hunting every room for that treasure or tech.

That being said, I cannot recommend this game to any sane person. Every game either takes 45-2 hours. Yes you're able to save but you cannot save scum if you mess up. This is including ALT+F4. Anytime you may thing you messed up or seeing your base being rampage, you have to retire or watch it burn as all those hours you spent on the base are gone. This would be acceptable if game lasted at most 45 minutes with no saving. The game having no loading a file before is nightmare stuff even at the easiest difficulty. if one zombie makes it to any building it'll infect and destroy that building while amassing a horde. Again that goes to if you mess up for one slight thing.

I have been on the end of the level and of course it should be hard, but me being a perfectionist and liking to make my base neat absolutely dunked me and I am partly to blame. But I am not wasting hours of my life building a base just for it to be torn down and tell me try again from the start.

I have looked at the ending for this and yes it does have a sequal bait and yes I am interested in another game like this but please improve it and fix it. Have some sort of Iron Man difficulty for those who don't want to waste their life just to fail.\t\t\t\t\t
