\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolid 8/10. I'm a big fan of strategy, city builder and zombie games, so naturally a game that combines all 3 elements sounded perfect. And while it's truly impressive how the game can run literally 10 000 zombies on your screen without issue, and burning through them all feels like you are actually battling a horde, there are some flaws that I'd like devs to fix:
- "Recommended technologies": the game features a campaign with different objectives and challenges, and every mission requires you to have specific building or unit to make it beatable, without it that specific mission is very hard. Many times I've found myself being happy to finally unlock a new unit or building, only to find the next mission is a very cold map which requires technology invested in energy production to be feasible. I wish that game gave you hints on which tech to prioritise unlocking, otherwise you can really get stuck on campaign if you unlocked the wrong tech (there is no respec btw).
- Unit collision and pathfinding: Really annoying that my soldiers would rather walk outside through a horde than through a city, just because some building is blocking 1 tile he needs to pass. Also if you have a lot of units and order them to move, they will get stuck in each others collision boxes, trying to walk but those in front not moving. Sooo units in the back are walking, not going anywhere and not even shooting the dead in front of them. You have to manually select them and order them to shoot to fix this.
Formations - There should be a formation kinda thing, micromanaging units to distribute themselves on a wall is a pain in the ass, same reason as above
- Hero missions - those feel kinda meh, first 1-2 times it's interesting, but then it just becomes a kiting simulator, since you only have 1 hero, and occasionally you find help like turrets or other units, but have to kite like 100 zombies for 10 min one by one. Aaand you can't even skip these missions because it's your main source of technology. :/
No 2x/3x speed - seriously, there are times where you have to wait 3-4 in game days to get the resources you need or for your units to clear the infected from an area, but you can't speed up the game so you just stare at your screen for 15 min doing nothing. Seriously devs, add speedup please like all normal RTS games!

Well that's about the meh stuff. None of it really game breaking, just really annoying to deal with. Otherwise the game is extremely fun, base building rewards pre-planning and properly layering your defenses and segregating your colony into smaller defendible chunks, a lot different than usual "stack everything in one place behind 1 wall" like most strategy games. The dead come in truly terrifying numbers, sometimes up to 10k, and watching your units shoot, burn or explode the horde feels very satisfying. Solid 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
