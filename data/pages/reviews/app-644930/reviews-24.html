\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are so many things wrong with this game. I'm shocked it's rated so highly.

There are some decent ideas going on here that I enjoyed like handling the possible spread of zombie infestation and the general feeling of defending massive hordes of zombies, but the game does not deliver these moments in a way that's enjoyable or rewarding. Every victory in the early parts of the game provided me hope that things would get better but they only got worse. If I could refund this game at full price I would do it in a heartbeat.

This is what happened every time I tackled an average mission where you needed to defend a swarm of zombies (these types of missions were my breaking point):
- start building up a base and gathering resources like a regular RTS match
- explore the map, expand my colony, and start building defenses
- reach a point where I don't need to expand much more because I've placed a ton of the same building all over the map to gather a couple key resources
- think I've done enough but realize i still have over 10 in-game days left in the mission, and i have to wait for that last swarm without the ability to speed up time
- mess around but build up defenses a bit until the final swarm comes
- oops, I underestimated the final swarm
- get wiped almost immediately

And then I have to repeat the entire process from the start and hope that my new approach is good enough, because if not I'll only find out hours into the mission when the final horde comes without ANY prior indication of how large it will be.

I know the game is supposed to be hard, but baiting players along a multi-hour mission to get to a finale that they will almost definitely not be prepared for feels so pathetically cheap.

What I'm feeling now seems vaguely similar to how I felt when giving up on X-COM, but I at least have respect for X-COM. However, I do not respect this game.It's just frustration, disappointment, and many bad game design decisions.\t\t\t\t\t
