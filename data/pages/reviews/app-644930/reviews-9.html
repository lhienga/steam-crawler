I played the campaign about half way through before I simply got too bored to continue. I did a couple of restarts to try other techs and strategies. All in all the game has some potential but lacks in depth and repeatability. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed the game till I started playing campaign mode. Campaign mode is so bad and time consuming it's terrible. Probably the worst mistake you can do is playing campaign mode. If you want to enjoy life and be happy don't try campaign mode. It's worst design ever. Story is kinda decent. It's small but it's enough for a game focused on survival and rts.

Good Part of the game:
1. It's probably the best tower defence game out there.
2. Shocking Tower is so satisfying.
3. At first I thought zombie reaching tents and losing all of the population bad but I don't think it's that bad
now and it adds more interesting scenarios to your game.
4. Units are too fun to mess around. Except Lucifer. I hate Lucifer.
5. So challenging all the time. As an RTS player I really like moving around all the time. This game gives me that with Soldiers. I love Soldiers.
6. You can pick which part will be hard or which part will be easy. I LOVE that. Let me cook my friend. I want short games with lots of zombies. Gimme.

Problems
Campaign Side
1. So you failed the mission and want to quit? Nah wait till they destroy everything you have. Have fun waiting 5 minutes just to die BECAUSE THERE IS NO RESIGN BUTTON. Why??? WHY????????? (Edit: Actually it's in the menu where you can read the victory conditions. My bad. Thank you DoubleJo for info.)
2. Every mission is different so you don't know what you'll get. Always read the description and expect more. Because you wouldn't want to have thousands of mobs to attack you without any indication and have no defences to keep you safe.
3. Harpies... Just who thought of adding them to the game? Seriously. I don't look at my soldiers one second and I see them torn in half by 4 harpies that come from the fog in mach 10 speed. If you're unlcuky enough to attract 10 harpies it's just game over for you. They have high health, high speed, high attack and high attack speed. Only way to beat them is to get engineering station units.
4. Map is super important here so my advice is to check the map beforehand from a video or sth. Because there is no restart if you don't know the map and go something wrong you're dead and you'll get less points.
5. Colony Size Condition. It's garbage. 2k+ population is such a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. It's only makes me play the game longer. It's super boring.
6. Hero Missions. PAIN. Straight up PAIN. It's boring and time consuming never and ever had fun playing it. Finding items is so stupid. I had to watch a video to see where it is.

Normal Game
1. No speed up button: Don't tell me the but it'll ruin the RTS bs because you can pause as much as you want. In some campaigns I just sit there and wait for game to end.
2. Pathfinding: Sometimes it's so terrible and since it's so terrible it's good for you. You can literally kill 800 zombies with 2 soldiers with ease. To battle this you can increase the ai pathing by level.
3. Not knowing what's coming is kinda sad: I built the wonder to unlock the map but till that point I can already see 90% of the map. I wish that wonder could show me the types of zombies I'll face.
4. Too expensive. Normally I wouldn't get mad at simple things in game but for that price tag I'm expecting premium quality game but I didn't really get that. This game is a great $15-20 game but $30 is a bit too much.\t\t\t\t\t
