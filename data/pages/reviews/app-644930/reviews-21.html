\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really liked this game for the survival mode. I came back after years to play the campaign and while it is really fun at times, there are a few extremely frustrating mechanics built in. Not being able to save/reload makes sense in the survival game but good god is it infuriating when playing the campaign. There are parts of the campaign where you play solo missions with a chosen "hero". These missions can easily take an hour or longer to finish due to how slowly the hero kills zombies and how many zombies are jammed into them. So, to spend an hour tediously clearing a base room by room, zombie by zombie, then die to an unexplained instant-kill mechanic really kills any enjoyment I was having. I could care less about losing "score" by failing the mission and having to do it again, but to spend an hour slowly clearing out the whole base just to die right at the end and lose all progress downright pisses me off. I don't know that I'll even come back to try and finish the campaign, I'd just get angry having to replay that mission again.

I give it a thumbs up for the survival mode. The campaign seemed great and I was enjoying it but the save system really ruined the experience in the end.\t\t\t\t\t
