\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall quite enjoyable. I was looking for a game that felt thematically interesting and this scratched the itch for something like Starcraft/Warcraft, but neither. Loved the music, loved the colony management.

That said, it isn't either of those games, and does have some notable drawbacks.

The biggest drawback is the game is ironman mode only, which makes some (early, mostly) levels very rough before you've unlocked enough options to really feel set. Getting to the final horde wave only to realize it's going to swamp your defenses really makes you wish you could either load a save from earlier in the level, or more keeping with the spirit of the decision, give us a "Give Up and Restart Mission" option in the files. Having to wait while the horde finishes off your base and not being able to manually choose to give up can be tedious. A time acceleration option would also be nice, since some maps I have my defenses fully set and just have to wait for the horde to trigger from there.

Second drawback is related, military units are too hard to access early on. The training building requires you to build up enough infrastructure before you can even train the basic Rangers, so you're going to spend the first game week or so with only your starting units. Better hope you don't lose them, and better hope the AI doesn't decide to get a bit frisky with wandering packs, because they're not enough to fully guard your base while clearing expansion room and you won't have the resources to build walls if you're expanding aggressively, which feels necessary.

The final main drawback is also related to the second, it's too easy to snowball into a loss early match. A single zombie you missed on your radar or walks juuuust outside your pickets' patrol paths/sight range can infect a tent in only a few seconds, which immediately spawns more zombies than your starting mercenaries can handle. Once they're in, it's extremely hard to stop a loss from there.

To wrap it up, I recommend it if you're patient and love horde clearing strategy games. This one left me wanting more.\t\t\t\t\t
