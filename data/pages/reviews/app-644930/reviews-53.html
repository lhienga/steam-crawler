\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery unbalanced for beginners. I really wanted something I could get into and this game sure does seem like it would be fun. However, if you only have a few hours per week to play I would say skip on to something different, there is much better competition available. The difficulty scaling is atrocious.

No matter how fast you build you will find that in the early campaign you simply cannot compete with the mechanics of the game. You'll either find you need more food to make archers or you will be far behind on stone to build a garrison. Your housing for your team is woefully inadequate and the moment one tent falls they all fall. One lone zombie can destroy your whole village which sounds like how it should be. Except that it's a game, you have no opportunity to learn the mechanics, and on top of that there is no save game feature to go back when you realize you did make a mistake. You're simply required to start the map all over again and sadly, waist your time. Finally, those mistakes cost you not only time, but also every fail counts against you for upgrading your game making it doubly difficult to learn.

I'm sure later in the game things would become more balanced as you get more ways to protect your colony but in the early game when it's critical to help players learn the game it's just simply punishing and therefore takes away the fun. I highly recommend not playing the game in the first place.\t\t\t\t\t
