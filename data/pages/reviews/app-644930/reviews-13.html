\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI WANT TO BREAK MY MONITOR. That said this is an amazingly built zombie survival game. One mistake and you lose your entire colony which is entirely reasonable considering its zombies. One big issue is that the aoe units are way too far down the tech tree for the amount of hordes that pop up and getting resources for high end units is a pain if you have a bad starting spot. But those issues really emphasize how much micromanaging you have to be doing at all times which is a good way to brute force learning awareness. Another issues is the horde alert mechanic. I don't mind the direction being revealed last minute but it would be nice if the attack actually came from that direction instead of weird pathing around terrain causing it to come from an entirely different direction where you haven't just placed your entire army and bulked up defenses temporarily.\t\t\t\t\t
