\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is not for everyone. It is not a mainstream game. It will not hold your hand. If fact it might bite your hand, and you should probably get that hand looked at.

Note: I have been playing the game at the brutal difficulty. Which is about halfway up the large difficulty list. I have no idea if the below hold true on other difficulties.

CONS:
- You will screw up and lose.
The game is ridiculously unforgiving. A single zombie can and will destroy your city if it manages to sneak
past your defences. In the campaign, if you do not research technologies wisely, you will be unable to
defend yourself and have to start the whole campaign again or at least at a backup save you have made.
- The save system is weird.
The campaign auto-saves and only keeps the latest. You can manually make extra back up saves but they
basically act as separate campaigns that start from the save point. So they will auto-save and only keep the
latest if you play from them. If you want a save at a certain point you have to do a manual back up every time you start from that point
- The cut-scenes and plot.
Some of the most cringe dialogue I have heard in a long time. The plot itself is not worth mentioning. Some of the character voices also suffer from this but not to the same extent.

PROS:
- Its fun.
Legitimately, a PvE RTS doesn't sound amazing but it is actually really fun. It feels like something between a normal RTS and a tower defence game. The thrill of pushing your luck and having it work out is awesome.
- The campaign mode is really interesting and has replay-ability in which style of play you attempt via what technologies you unlock and when.
- The hero based missions are interesting, though they often act like slow moving puzzles. Its a nice break from the main game play loop. Also you can animation cancel with your hero.

CONCLUSION

While it has its down-sides its a surprisingly unique game in a genre that doesn't have much representation these days and manages to do well in the areas it needed to. Definitely a good time sync. If in doubt pick it up in a sale.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, although there are some 'buts'.
Base building is satisfying and intuitive. Exploring the map is great too, there is actually a feeling of danger there. Setting your defenses and fighting against oncoming hordes is the best part. Extremely fun and rewarding!

Tech tree is very varied, and tech choices matter a lot, although some upgrades seem almost mandatory. Without researching soldiers the game seems impossible, for example.
Hero missions are somewhat dull, as your hero can hardly do anything but run and shoot. They would benefit heavily from having active hero abilities rewarded on lvlups.

System requirements are very low, it runs well on an old acer swift laptop with integrated graphics.

Modding is somewhat lackluster, which is a shame for such a game. You can create your own maps, but that's it.

All in all, recommend this game. It's a great strategy game with zombies, what more do you need?\t\t\t\t\t
