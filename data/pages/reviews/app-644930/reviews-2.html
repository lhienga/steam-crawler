\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game excited to play a base building/tower defense/zombie apocalypse game. I did get this, but several things make this game VERY unpleasant to play.

1) No save/restore. I read some reviews and apparently this is to make the game harder. Okay fine. but...
2) No surrender button. You can't even quit a mission once you started it. So if you mess up on day 30 out of 60, there's no "surrender". You have to play all the way till the end then try again. VERY annoying.
3) No speed up button. This combined with the no-surrender button is brutal for long missions where you just want it to be over so you can try again
4) Some missions require specific research. If you don't have that research you can't beat the mission. But you can't get the research because the research points are non refundable after 1 mission. So you'd have to start completely over to make sure to get that research. (again no save/restore)
5) Replaying missions on harder difficulty apparently has no effect.
6) You can't tell where you can/cannot build buildings. Some buildings have a "range" to them and you can't tell where that is except by hovering until it turns green. No indicators of where you can build.

All in all i'm disappointed. Its a great premise and the gameplay itself is fun. But I'm only 15 hours in and so far only about 2 hours of those have been fun.\t\t\t\t\t
