\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like brutally hard RTS games this might be for you. Its fun, and I do enjoy it and, sometimes I even enjoy just how absorbed i get by it. But at the end of the day the devs requiring you to replay the entire campaign because of a tech tree mistake is absurd. I get it you're awesome at your own game and can beat it with any tech combination but i am sick of this toxic dude bro in the gaming industry, not everyone is good at a game, not everyone can be good at a game see my 930+ hours in the game and still not having finished the final level of the campaign or the desert survival mode. All because the game has one save that gets over written meaning if it decides to get on its (not sending horde notifications for some reason for example) you're screwed and hours of your life are gone. Or if during the campaign you invest heavily in a tech path you don't end up using, now at the end of the game you're severely crippled.

This game and its devs do not respect your time. Add in the ability to reroll your tech tree and "earlier save states" Hell even just ONE earlier save state 10 days a apart would solve the problem a lot of the time.

I only recommend this game if you're very good at RTS games or are willing to spoil yourself, look at the tech tree ahead of time, formulate a plan.

As a point of clarification the horde audio cue is highly likely not to play if any other audio cue/voice line from the announcer occurs simultaneously, anything from "defenses under attack" to "construction complete" or the like.

This review brought to you by someone who is terrible at games but damned stubborn. And yes, I will keep trying but that is hardly the point.\t\t\t\t\t
