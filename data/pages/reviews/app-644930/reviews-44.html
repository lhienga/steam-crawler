\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNo save in mission makes this game extremely frustrating. You'll spend an hour building up a base and an army/economy only to realize you underprepared for a horde and as soon as a single zombie makes it through and starts converting all your structures you're screwed. Be prepared to waste a lot of time only to lose all of your progress And unlike other roguelike games where failure happens frequently, there are no rewards for a failed mission.

The campaign also has hero missions which are generally pretty boring and tedious and suffer from the same problem - you'll accidentally step on a landmine after 30 minutes and have to repeat the entire boring slog from the start to get any rewards.

I can see why people would like this game but I can't possibly recommend to the average gamer.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a review mostly related to the Campaign. If you like survival modes, then this game might be for you, but the Campaign is just really bad.

The campaign has to many aspects to it that make it not worth it. The hero levels are just a chore, but you have to do them to get the tech for the other levels. The horde level are the same, you either don't have enough units to succeeded and they are impossible, or you do and they are boring.

Replay ability is low because so much of it feels like make work drudgery that I just cannot bring myself to play it anymore when I'm not streaming. It's good content, but not a fun game.

Which is sad because the base game, the actual RTS is really good. I just hope that TAB 2 is a lot better as there is a lot of room for improvement in this title.\t\t\t\t\t
