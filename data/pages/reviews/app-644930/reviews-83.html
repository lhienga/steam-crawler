\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far I am enjoying it. Definitely a learning curve as the later waves definitely require more than just a wall and some towers, requires more of a tower defence set up to survive it seems.

That being said there are a few things that rub me the wrong way..
-Random map can leave you crippled right from the get go. As well as many starting positions are much harder to defend then others. Provides more replayability which is good, but I would have liked if it would let you clear some things out.. random debris, tiny stone mines and trees can be a nuisance and should all be removable.
-Similarly, why the hell cant you bulldoze research buildings? They are massive (especially the first one) and serve 0 purpose once the you have researched everything. I even tried researching both wonders, still cant bulldoze it.

I will give a thumbs up because these are minor issues.
