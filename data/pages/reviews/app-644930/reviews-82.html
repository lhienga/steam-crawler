\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game's a mashup between a single player RTS, tower defense game and a colony management game. Since it's a single player RTS, you can pause the game which is very helpful for colony planning and precise maneuvering.

Its an immensely fun game, but the difficulty is quite ruthless and leaves little to no room for error, so be ready for a serious challenge. That being said, once you have a good set of defenses, there's nothing more satisfying than seeing legions of un-dead crash into your defenses and get obliterated.

There is a campaign and it's good if you're a new player that's learning the ropes since you have limited options each level and more become available every subsequent level. This means you can learn all the different stages of a regular 100 day colony through the campaign. However, if you are already familiar with all the mechanics of the game, the campaign can feel slow at times.

TL:DR: If you like tower defense games, resource balancing and management and don't mind the room for no error, its a very fun and unique strategy game to kill a lot of time and zombies with.\t\t\t\t\t

Ever since the game received the campaign, the devs just completely stopped giving it love.
The game is fun, it has a lot of replay value, but there could be so much more;
- better balancing
- more unit types
- more building types
- BETTER MAP LAYOUTS (like elevation or anything)
I know there are custom maps for that, but workshop should be to let players use their creativity, not to replace the devs from doing anything after releasing what everyone would've loved to be ''a great campaign''.
Not this half assed campaign that has no relevance to the core gameplay mechanics of this game.
I'd say go watch some videos of how it plays out before you make up your mind, this game has a lot to offer, but it can be super unforgiving for very little mistakes.

The game is brutally difficult.
Even though there are lower difficulty settings, I feel that you will still have quite a challenge ahead of you taking this on.
That aside, the game can be quite addictive in the same sense of something like Dark Souls - that 'I have to try just one more time' itch.

Spend some time watching Youtube videos on build layout first.
Story campaign is disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t
