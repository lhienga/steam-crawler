You can't save-scum your way through this. The game uses auto-saves, and wont even let you alt+f4(it saves over your auto-save) to load from a prior auto-save.

If that sort of thing is off-putting to you, then you might not want to play the game.

If you like a more hardcore challenge, the game is awesome. The progression is smooth, and it's fun to have to evaluate a map and determine where to spend resources.

For the most part, the game feels fair, and you can feel it when you're getting better. When you die, there's usually something you could have done to prevent it. The campaign has a nice mix of levels, and you have to tackle each one differently.

I give it a 7.5/10. Recommended if this genre is appealing to you.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis might be the most infuriating rts I've ever played.

The campaign relies on an Ironman system for its missions, not allowing you to save. Frustrating, but nothing worth popping a vein over. Well, there's also a mechanic wherein any building that has taken enough damage from zombies will instantly spawn more once it hits a certain threshold. And oh boy, is it easy to miss one little zombie shambling its way into your colony. You'll hear the narrator warn you that your buildings are under attack and by the time your four archers run back to base, one zombie has turned into fifty and your base is overwhelmed.

Edit: I gave it more time. I want to love this game. All the rts mechanics are great, but the god awful ironman makes it impossible. There's no point in it. Having no checkpoints isn't a challenge, it's a restriction, and shows a complete disrespect of the player's time. Why would I play on a high difficulty when it's just a waste of my time? I'll spend four hours on a mission only for one zombie to erase all of that.

'Git gud' I can hear the jobless tryhards say. Even the most challenging games have a checkpoint system so that the player doesn't have to potentially replay through hours of progress due to a minor mistake.

I've found a save mod called TABSAT that I'll be trying out to see if it makes this game enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
