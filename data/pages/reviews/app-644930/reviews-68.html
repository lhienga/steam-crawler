\r\n\t\t\t\t\tControls:

- No grid keys
- Cannot use modifiers such as Alt or Control when binding
- Laughably bad control menu, won't tell you about conflicts and is harder to use than needs be
- Every other button press my units randomly abort their movement midway through as if someone stumbled and fell
- No hotkeys or bindings for half of the commands in the game (including every single building and unit)!

Tutorial:

- Doesn't tell you about the sawmill (which is necessary to build other buildings it does tell you about)
- On my first attempt did not spawn a stone resource even when I'm told to build a quarry

Gameplay and campaign:

- Easy mode is what I would call hard mode on other similar games
- Cringe-inducing D-tier anime writing, including a "bad guy villain laugh" in the very first minutes, causing tonal whiplash to rival a sucker-punch to the groin
- D-tier voice acting, in particular for the unit feedback which worn out their welcome in about 5 minute flat
- While in better RTS games I can micro-manage two battlefronts at once while macro-ing my base at the same time, in TAB I literally do not see that my base (which is literally my entire screen) is under attack until a building explodes out of nowehere - probably because zombies and workers are too tiny to distinguish (horrible visual information delivery and zero aural feedback even with "visible warning" toggled on)

Conclusion:

Feels a lot more unpolished than RTS games from 25 years ago it is obviously derived from, which you can buy for a few dollars on sale on Steam or GOG - not to begin to compare to Starcraft II (now free) or Northgard!

I'll keep playing because I love the idea of a longer match of Age of Empires II, with deeper mechanics built around playing against the AI but so far left me a very poor initial impression. Were I not looking for something this particularly specific I would have liked to refund and go back to playing Age of Empires II DE.

If you know another game that fits this particular niche please leave a comment below. TAB probably isn't it.\t\t\t\t\t
