\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game needs a sequel so badly.

It's very fun. The campaign and sandbox are both enjoyable and the difficulty curve is mostly well tempered, sometimes skewing a bit on the difficult side of the equation.

The campaign tickled a part of my brain akin to Starcraft Broodwar or Command and Conquer (C&C95/RA1). The main thing that can rub the wrong way is that it's forced ironman on campaign levels (saves are restricted to game exit and loads).

I'm okay with Ironman, its how I play XCom, but there probably should be an accessibility or "very easy" difficulty option that folks can access to enable mid level saves of some kind (limited slot or not). I can understand folks not wanting to replay an hour long campaign map from minute zero because they overlooked something critical in their fortifications.

Regardless, a sleeper hit! (if you'll forgive the late review). I periodically check the developer's website to see if there's any news on them creating a new game, but it's totally dark after a small blog post in 2021 about the music of this game.

I wish them all the best, the left the game in a good state and released modding tools, not much else you can ask for! I hope a TAB2 gets created.\t\t\t\t\t
