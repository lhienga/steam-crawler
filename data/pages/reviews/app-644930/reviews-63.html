\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave really enjoyed real time strategy (RTS) games for 30+ years.
Sadly the difficulty of this game, on its easiest setting, is punishing and beyond frustrating.

Any error and you've pretty much lost the level with little hope or recovery.
Try a slow and steady approach to mitigate this risk and you'll get overwhelmed by a horde for not expanding fast enough.
When you do lose it then reduces the XP you'll gain from a future victory thus ensuring you won't unlock all the tech.

My observation of RTS games is a lot of the pleasure for a lot of folks is winning :-) base building and expanding out to conquer a map is as much about catharsis as it is challenge.
Having harder modes for those that seek that pixel and second perfect challenge is great but punishing people seeking an easier ride seems daft.

Fair warning then, these folks have built a beautiful game with lovely mechanics, a great tech tree and a lovely twist on RTS with literal hordes coming for you...but sadly no option for people seeking a gentler ride to pay money and enjoy it without forcing you down a permadeath horror show.
Know what you're buying and if you were looking for a gentle escape from work this will leave you angry and frustrated.\t\t\t\t\t
