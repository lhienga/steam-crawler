\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game makes me RAGE but I love it so much.

The campaign is straightforward but the different maps and different infected types kept me on my toes and I never felt like it got boring or repetitive. The only part I really hated was finding all of the objective items in a couple of the Hero missions, as they're so small and hidden next to other objects at times which might as well have made them invisible to me for all the time I spent looking for them (I still eventually found them, but jfc were those some sleepless nights).

As for Survival mode...
Most of my hours spent playing this game has just been building up the most rock solid colony—the best defense, the thickest walls, ballistas, wasps, the most watchtowers full of veteran archers, snipers, and soldiers; nothing is getting in, victory is absolute—only for the next wave of infected to utterly annihilate through it all and send me spiraling into despair as I watch HOURS of hard work succumb to fetid decay and leave me a hollow shell beneath the glow of my screen and those big, fat words "DEFEAT" mocking me.

Highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t

But I don't recommend the campaign stick to survival or the other modes they where a lot more fun in comparison to the campaign. I still recommend the game because there is a lot of good things about it but those are just some of the things I hated the most from it.\t\t\t\t\t
