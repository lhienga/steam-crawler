Take "The Age of Darkness" for example, a medieval variant of "They're Billions" without such fatal scenarios -- it's a decent game, but it gets boring after a while, its only real difficulty being surviving the last onslaught (and I mean it -- once you've mastered "Age of Darkness" to a certain degree, no other challenging aspects remain; maybe the devs will do something about it -- the game is in early access, after all).

All in all, the game is definitely recommended as a jewel of "survive-against-hordes-of-zombies-base-building-colony-sim" genre.
