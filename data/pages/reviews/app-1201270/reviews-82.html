\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow. This game will forever have a special place in my heart. The story and characters were amazing and the ending was so powerful. It made me cry but left me full of hope. As someone who has struggled with depression on and off for my whole life, the themes of self-destruction, isolation, and healing resonated with me. The music was amazing and supported the story in such an enchanting way.

There was times in the middle that get a little bit of a slog, but the beautiful art and characters you meet are enough to keep it interesting. The endings of the middle chapters make you forget about any tedium you just experienced and keep you going toward the most inspiring ending. If you are thinking about quitting halfway through the story -- keep going. It's worth it, I promise.

I've heard some criticism about this game's gameplay -- I just want to point out that this is basically a point-and-click game (but in such a way that works great with a controller) and this game is about the characters. There are small combat mini games that are worked in in a clever way and puzzles that are mostly easy but I did have to look up a walkthrough a couple times.

Play this game. It's worth the full price. It's one of those games I wish I could wipe my memory clean of and experience again.\t\t\t\t\t
