I really wished I played this game when I was younger, back on school days.
A great game of self discovery and life lessons. The depiction of the daily ups and downs is done beautifully and accurately. The funs and the struggles we experience with the characters really sucks me in.
The ending also done right and give a satisfying and melancholic end of Atma's journey through Loka City.

The story and plot isn't something new for some, but the atmosphere of the environment and flow of the story is nicely executed that it would still make a fresh experience out of it from start to finish.

There isn't much to say about the gameplay as it is not the main focus of the game. The puzzle and quest can be a bit confusing or mundane sometimes. But everything else in my opinion made up for the lack of it.

Hope you enjoyed the game as much as I do!\t\t\t\t\t
