\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe plus:
+++ Beautiful combination of pixel art and color, thus maikng a super comfy atmosphere (mostly).
+++ VERY good soundtracks of different genre.
++ So many references to the 90s Indonesian (pop) culture.
+ Cool mini games.
+ Sensible pricing for its length.

The minus:
- The (Bahasa Indonesia) dialogues seem bit too stiff and do not flow from time to time.
- Moderate amount of backtracking.
- Certain "mechanics" can be unbearable to do, especially towards the end where you have to avoid the falling debris.

The middle of the road:
* The plot is actually very ordinary but the devs present it in such an amazing way so it kinda cancels each other out.
* The sidequests are hit and miss, be it the puzzle or the story, but I can understand they're there to flesh out the side characters even if it's just a little bit, and it works.

Overall it's a good game to play when you need to take a break from your high-octane games.
Just go in, enjoy the atmosphere, and immerse yourself. Totally recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
