Gameplay is mostly fetch quests, QTE, and algebra, and could get a little cumblesome after a while, but personally for me, the story is worth it. The last chapter was amazing. One of the best story driven games I played yet.

Extra props to an indie studio in Indonesia for this amazing piece. Being in SEA and an indie dev myself, I do understand how difficult to acquire resources and talents in the region. And the exposure for studios and video games from SEA itself isn't very big but it's growing thanks to great studios like Mojiken and Toge Productions.\t\t\t\t\t
