\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn absolutely fantastic side-scrolling game that I wasn't expecting for me to be taking a few or so days on! I am glad it had a warning at the start, as indeed, the game does have some heavy bits here and there. It's quite emotional, but I think when a game makes you feel, that's when you know it has been totally worth playing! Throughout the gameplay, you experience loss, romance, depression, anxiety, su*c*de ideations, bullying, family issues, but furthermost, a strong friendship and helping one another - wasn't quite sure if that was a spoiler or not, but I censored it anyway just in case. The game also has a feel of the 90's (kinda like those 'Street Fighter' vibes), and I really enjoyed that aspect, plus all of the puzzles were really fun to do and they get you thinking about making the right choices for yourself or whoever else. There may be some scenes that are uncomfortable/eerie (but thankfully no jumpscares). Overall, such a beautiful game with amazing pixel graphics full of gorgeous colors, and nice music to listen to. I definitely recommend it and having some tissues near you!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the demo and felt this game is a charming one with some potential. However I see the storytelling being very problematic when the plot in the demo so far established a somewhat serious undertone: establishing rapport with an anxious young girl who faces domestic violence from her father. The plot of the demo ended with a huge cliffhanger where the protagonist faces mortal danger… yet all these are forgotten when the main chapter starts. There’s a girlfriend who is basically a new character, and no attempt to address what happened in the demo is made. Essentially, the game starts telling a new story without resolving the existing plot. Even more egregious is the undertone change from serious to flippant, and then arbitrary superpower was introduced, and the game lost me when it started to demand me doing random chores to please the girlfriend.

I want to like this game. The art style is gorgeous and the animation is stylish. However the story seems to be a train wreck that sapped every ounce of my desire to keep going. A lesson to be learned: Nostalgia isn’t a panacea, because it’s not material to those who can’t be nostalgic to the backdrop of the game’s settings.\t\t\t\t\t
