\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a love letter. And the amount love being put into this game is insane. Every single component of this game, I can feel the love from the developers. More people needs to play this game.

If you like Makoto Shinkai's movies, I think you'll like this game. Thematically speaking, there are some story components and direction that I feel heavily inspired by Makoto Shinkai's works. Namely... Your Name, Weathering With You, etc. So, if you love those movies, I think you'll have a great time with this game.

There is so many layers to the game's story. The game has one of the most ambitious writing that I've ever seen. Kuddos to the writers for being able to pull this off beautifully. The storytelling beats are done wonderfully. No moment in the game ever feel boring and it kept me on the edge of my seat. Every single moment felt special and meaningful. Every single moment drove me with emotions. Sorrow, shock, and happiness. Thank you dear writers for giving me such an amazing 10 hours.

Music. Oh... Such amazing music. It's the kind that will immerse you deeper into the story. The kind that will tug every last bit of your heart strings. I don't think I'll cry that much without such amazing music.

Design. Every single game mechanic ideas being put into this game adds so much to the game. The minigames are never boring. Pure love for all the classic games. Love em.

Art. Beautifully done. Beautiful aesthetics throughout the game. Dear artists, well done for bringing back early 2000s Indonesia. And the animations are impeccable.

It is very hard to find any fault with this game. And it have the potential to be life-changing for many people. Thank you so much Mojiken.

To end it all, I have to say it again. More people need to play this game.\t\t\t\t\t
