\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe most beautifully heartbreaking game I have EVER played!

A Space for the Unbound was the best 10 hours I've spent gaming this year.

Some of the puzzles in the main story became unpredictably tougher and chaotic towards the end of Chapter 4, which maybe fun for some players, but slowed down the story progress in that segment. Still, nothing that can't be enjoyable.

This game is a walk down memory lane for anyone who has struggled with trauma, pain and forgiveness, and it handles these issues with such careful detail, that even those who haven't seen such trouble will be able to understand the lives of their peers who's struggles they may have failed to understand.

As someone who is learning to write for story-rich video games, this is one I will always remember, and come back to. Nothing is more important in a story game, over and above the story. And in such a game, because it is interactive, and choices matter, a powerful and well researched story hits home much harder than anything else.

Thank you, Mojiken and Toge. This game is truly a treasure.\t\t\t\t\t
