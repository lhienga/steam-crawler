Between memories and future

A real close call for me to almost cry like a baby, the one bad thing in this masterpiece crafted with heart visualy and narratively was the amount of backtracking later on. Like one "Dive" was not enough you will do multiple inside one location and sometimes then backtrack 5-6 maps because you forgot something or just uncovered something. Delivery boy...

It's not hard in terms of riddles and achieving good ending. But it is frustrating and realisticly speaking, if you would cut out most of the running around picking up items at the far ends of the city, then backing up you would have 5 hours long game not 10 hours long. Avoiding road blocks was annoying as hell (yes these are happening too! taking even longer road back to a point)

Anyway enough bickering from me. The game is really impressive especially with city vibe, art, characters. It is easily soaking me with it's story, detailed with pixel art. it's almost a life experience. Because it is about life, about people problems Atma & Raya and plenty of others! Sealed with imaginary fantasy. If I was to compare "Space for the Unbound", the closest call would be "To the Moon". It's that much creative with it's story telling and engagement. Even if I dont like all gameplay designs it is still incredible childhood journey experience.\t\t\t\t\t
