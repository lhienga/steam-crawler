Amazing game, amazing story, great visuals.

I really love the game story, very touching. Relatable to some extent (yeah, growing up sucks ass). The game visuals honestly looks fantastic, it is 2D but it has life in it. Very cool. The OST is very good as well, really well made.

The gameplay is take it or leave it kind of thing, it could be a visual novel and it still going to be a good game with solid story. The "Spacedive" and "Magic Wand" is a cool feature. BUT, the fact that sometimes u need to go back and forth with the "real world" feels more like a chore. I wouldn't mind going back and forth on some cases, but towards the end of the game i feel like u guys want to keep the player playing as long as possible while making the back n forth part nothing special. The combat system is just there, u could take out the combat and it will still be a good game. I get the dev want to make a type of minigame for combat but its not engaging enough.

The best feature of this game of course the fact that u can pat every cat existed in the game\t\t\t\t\t
