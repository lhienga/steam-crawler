\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have mixed feelings about this game. The graphical style is enchanting, the story is interesting, the final twist (no spoilers!) is unusual and nice, the foreign (to me) Indonesian culture is fascinating (maybe it would be better to show even more of the culture, Indonesia is definitely underrepresented in games), and the gameplay provides some diversity and does not distract from the story. That's the good things, and I honestly recommend this game (I did finish it, the playtime has been logged incorrectly from my steam deck).

On the other hand, this game is so... childish, for lack of a better word. It's probably a design choice, as the game is meant to represent the events from the point of view of a troubled teenager, but still... I feel that the heavy topics that the game touches are not really treated well; the heroine has a lot of serious problems, the whole game is an exposition of these problems... and then suddenly we are shown that all problems have been magically solved, the heroine is recovering, and all is well. I can't articulate it well enough, but I do have a feeling that something is missing in the game's treatment of its own topics.

In any case -- great game, please play it and decide for yourself!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNormally, I don't write Steam reviews, but after playing A Space for the Unbound, I had to make an exception. This game features a fantastic and emotional story where nothing is as it seems. Honestly, I never thought a video-game could touch me so deeply and make me think so profoundly.

Graphics: The game is incredibly well-crafted. You can tell how much effort the developers put into it. Simply beautiful: 10/10.

Story: Just play it yourself. In my opinion: 11/10.

Music: Usually very calming, until the track "Within the Dream" drops. "Within the Dream" + visuals = guaranteed tears. 11/10.

In short, this game is a definite recommendation.
Would I buy it again if I had to? Absolutely!
To anyone who purchases this game, I hope you enjoy it as much as I did.

Best regards to all the existing Space-Divers and anyone who becomes one!\t\t\t\t\t
