aight i am writing this down because i waited long enough for this game release and when i am playing it its just so worth it to buy and play it so go ahead and buy it.

back on 2021 when i play the prologue i have massive expectation to this game, why? no no no.its not because this game made from my country and being overproud of it.
this game is somewhat i can't believe this is not made from a japanese developer(you know what i mean),the artstyle is gorgeous,the environment is so much alive and really picture the feels of indonesia in his late 90s,soundtrack is pretty good,the story itself is well written,lot of plot wist but don't worry i won't spoil it

the cons itself its bit repetitive to get a list done from atma and raya,like for example you need to collect YOMAN letter (if you don't get the reference,well its basically a bubble gum leter called YOSAN that if you collect all the letter you will get a prize, and yes this is real thing in indonesia but even myself can't find all the letter when i was a kid) now this isn't really necessary to progress but if you don't complete it you won't get a paper to get story book to complete.but overall you can skip this part and just read the full red magic book story on the internet (no it will not affect the ending i think)
and for non indonesia player who play this game will get a bit confused for pop culture references just like the YOMAN letter i am talking about,i think i am indonesian enough to understand all this pop culture references

for the puzzle itself its bit annoying at some point but it's pretty worth it if you don't look at guide on internet like seriously,if you can manage not to just don't

9/10 will recommend anyone who likes this type of story game and beautiful art\t\t\t\t\t
