Bought this game a year ago and finally managed to finish it this year just a few hours ago.

With a total playtime of 10.7 hours, spending money on this game last year when it was newly released was not in vain to play "A Space For The Unbound," even though I just completed it today because there was no time, hehe.

So, "A Space For The Unbound" is a game created by Mojiken Studio and Toge Production, released on January 19, 2023.

Here, Mojiken uses pixel graphics, which I am comfortable with, given the art in this game. You can see it on my previous instastory, and that's just a small part. You must try this game to experience other eye-pleasing pixel arts.

In terms of gameplay mechanics, we are presented with a 2D side-scrolling mechanic that makes the game easy and more relaxed to play. Additionally, there are some simple puzzles and side games that add variety to the game.

As for the story, we follow a high school boy named Atma to solve the mystery of his girlfriend, Raya. At the beginning, I was a bit confused about how the story would develop, but as the story progressed, many unexpected things happened, such as Raya also having supernatural powers, among other unexpected twists.

So, that's my short review. I apologize if it seems messy, and I also apologize for any inaccuracies in my writing because I still have much more to learn.
