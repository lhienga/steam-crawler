\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"A space for the unbound" is a game with many strengths and weaknesses, but which rests on the solid foundations of a well-told plot and a very successful graphic style.

Contrary to many reviews, I won't tell you that the plot is super innovative, rather the way in which it is told is masterful and even the use of twists and turns is well done and keeps you glued to your seat.

However, I would like to underline that, especially in the central part of the game, the pace becomes boring and the many backtrackings require a bit of willpower to get to the end.

Despite everything, however, also thanks to its not excessive duration, I recommend the game 100%.

For fellow Steamdeck users, it won't surprise you, but the game runs perfectly without any particular tweaks.\t\t\t\t\t
