\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very great game. 100/10

It has good visuals of sprites, and the background as well as the animation. The background featuring rural Indonesia and the fantasy-like, hearts of people honestly absorbed me into the world of ASFTU.

The mechanics are easy to understand and the puzzles are not too hard but can still leave you satisfied.
My favourite parts of the game involve the quizzers, they are one of the best characters of people with a lot of trivia knowledge yet look like delinquents. This so called "gap moe" made me like them a lot.

Another reason to love this game is the in-game music. Especially by Bambang Iswanto. I love the piece made for Erik's theme. I was jamming along to when Erik beat me up. It was awesome!

Cheers!\t\t\t\t\t
