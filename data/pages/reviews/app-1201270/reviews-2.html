타인을 괴롭히지 말고, 스스로를 괴롭히지 말자.

하지만 게임플레이는 너무 따분했다. 뺑뺑이 도는 것 싫어하고 미리미리 챙기는 것 좋아하는데, 미리미리 챙기지 못하게 해놓았고 강제로 뺑뺑이 돌게 만들어놨다.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I had to describe this game in one word, it's marvelous.

The story is absolutely astounding, the music is great, the characters are great, and although the gameplay is standard, it's nothing bad by no means. Make sure to go into this fully blind if you're set on buying it, it's one of those games where experiencing everything blind makes it even more enjoyable. The best aspect of the game is definitely the story, which I don't want to give away in the slightest as it's that good. The only piece of advice I will give is to make sure you complete all of the Bucket List, and the best way to do that is by checking/exploring every area in each chapter. Talk to every NPC, pet every cat (Dead serious), and check the ground, all furniture, and all objects to gain bottle caps. If you're able to get everything checked, trust me it'll be worth it. The game is formatted in five core chapters with a prologue at the start and an epilogue at the end, and from my play time the game's about eight to maybe ten hours long, so not super long.

Overall it's a very solid 8/10, which to me means it's great. Highly recommend! I hope this game gets more attention and for the development team to keep making high quality gems like this.\t\t\t\t\t
