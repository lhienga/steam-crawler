\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo so so beautiful..

The setting and premise reminds me of a time when i was a kid, living in suburban Jakarta, trading YOSAN candies with friends, watching people collect bottle caps, walking everywhere and visit the cinema, the red clay roofs, the street peddlers that sells magazines, and small trinkets, the hansips, it's all very true to life and authentic Indonesian 90s-2000s experience and i love it.

At least for the first few chapters you can still take in the atmosphere and enjoy yourselves within it.
The puzzles are quite nice and makes you think outside the box, it's not boring, Atma's story with Raya is very reminiscent of high school love that we all know and familiar with, can definitely relate to them as well :>

It's a gorgeous piece of art that rarely comes out nowadays in the world of lootboxes, battle passes and action triple A games.

Definitely get it if you want a cozy game where you want to enjoy the music, art, and story, and also get a full fledged video game experience with no MTX and Battle passes :3
