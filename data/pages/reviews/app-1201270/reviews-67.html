The best indie game I've ever played. This game costs $15.99 but I can buy it for $30.
A touching story told in a very creative way, a sad story with loss, fears and deep loneliness. But it also has a cure in itself, just like Raya and Atma overcome everything to complete the bucket list, do what they want, create small but unforgettable memories.
The ending makes me want to cry. Goodbye Atma, rest peacefully - "In the storm you'll find your sunrise" and welcome to the new path of life, Raya.
In real life there are probably many people like Raya, hoping that all will find a cure, or maybe find their own "Atma".
Thank you Mojiken for bringing this masterpiece to life. Thank you for reminding us to keep chasing reality even though it's not as beautiful as a dream.
