You never know with this type of games.... what letdown or what epic emotion rollercoaster is going to take you for a ride.... but what an experience!

The gameplay is enjoying this pixel graphic novel, carefully tailored for you to start piecing together whats your purpose and your role in the whole story, piece by piece... action by action. With flashes of different events that revolve around the core for this master tale. This is sprinkled with puzzles and minigames that expect you to make full use of that you learn that you can do and your observation of the world that is presented to you. Amazing work on this game, thank you for the exquisite experience!
