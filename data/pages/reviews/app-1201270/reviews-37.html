\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game helped me reflect on my own inner turmoil and reminded me of a very close friend who has been by my side, no matter what, for about 8 years as of this review. I saw myself in Raya and saw my best friend in Atma, especially during the final act. The themes of self-conflict, escapism, and the undying love and support one feels for another are conveyed excellently. I almost shed tears a few times since I could heavily relate to some of the events in the narrative.

My only critique is the saving system being a little wonky and how running back and forth to complete quests can get kinda tedious. Other than that, this game's narrative is very well-written, and the pixel art style and soundtrack enhance it even further.

I highly recommend this to aspiring creatives, especially artists and writers. I can vouch for that as an artist and writer myself.\t\t\t\t\t
