\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably one of the best of the newer Musou style games (not counting collabs like Hyrule, etc) all in one package with all DLC included (minus 2 or 3 Japan exclusive DLC's and Sophitia's costume).

In my opinion it's way better than Warriors Orochi 4 Ultimate by a large margin, better story, better character interaction, better selection of music with no bad remixes, so many stages both from DW7 & SW3 and new ones (WO4 felt like it only had 10 stages max), way better end game side mode that's actually a bit challenging. And unlike WO4 there is actually a reason to max out characters if you want to play the hardest difficulties and get their best weapons, this game doesn't mess around when you change the difficulty to chaos, like it should.

It also has a camp in between levels where you can walk around which they took out from WO4, it doesn't sound that interesting, but it does make the world of the game feel more alive than going through a bunch of menus like in WO4, not to mention that you get curious about some characters interactions thanks to it, you might see Ryu Hayabusa standing in the camp and be like "Hm I wonder what he has to say about what's happening" something that in WO4 you would have to go out of your way to look for in a boring menu.

If we are talking about the port, I would say this is one of the better ports Koei has made on PC, up there with SW5 and... I think that's it. It only crashed once (WOW, an achievement for Koei) in my 100 hs playing while on 4k 60fps. Now it can't only be good things, this is Koei after all, the in-game cutscenes play at 30 fps no matter what you have it set on, it also can stutter while playing them making the sound of the voices delayed to what's happening in the cutscene. There are weird graphical glitches that seem to be tied to framerate, you can try different options to fix it, but every setup is different, some people couldn't fix it that way. Oh, and it doesn't have any English voices only Japanese, since I know that's really important to some people.

I would put it up there with the best of the best like DW8, DW7, SW4, SW2, WOZ and DW3 (personal favorite). It's worth it on sale or without it.\t\t\t\t\t
