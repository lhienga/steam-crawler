My favorite part of WO series is the characters from every koei IP and stuff you can do in different modes and just get lost farming and having fun. Im very glad hayabusa got in 4 but still hoping we see more ninja gaiden love especially for the girls! Going in to 4 before 3 got to PC my expectations were high since a new orochi game shouldn't be rush or looked over without making sure it atleast lives up to past games which 4 did atleast when it bought for the switch back when it came out. I would say that only thing 4 has over 3 is the updated graphics and for the most part improved combat system. But everything else 3 is the superior game and might even be the best mousu game ever to this date.
I highly highly recommend this game if you the genre or looking for something to really invest time into and get lost in. Sadly has been any mods so far but hopefully we get some in the future.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor the uninitiated, Musou games are grinding games at heart for those of us who like grind. At over 100hrs (soon to be more, going for 100% cheevos), this has been a great game to unwind to. You can easily pick up and play a 10-minute battle or fight demons for much longer. Its simplicity hides a cruel, tumultuous endgame capable for wiping your team at a moments notice if you get caught lackin'. You're in no way ever forced to interact with the endgame that's there and can enjoy the game and story at your own pace. One of the best, if not THE BEST, Musou game of all time. If you're ever interested in this franchises' history of Dynasty Warriors and Samurai Warriors, WO3U is a great entry point.

The game runs at a hard 60FPS cap on release, which in itself could be tolerable but the longstanding "black-boxes" and missing effects glitch rages til this day with no official patch rectifying this issue, with the only solution being to lowering your monitor's refresh rate back to a hard 60hz every time you load the game to get the full particle and graphical effects within.\t\t\t\t\t
