\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCAUTION: Warriors Orochi 3 came out on PS3 and Xbox 360 in 2011 and Warriors Orochi 3 Ultimate came out on PS4 and Xbone in 2018, so the game is going to look old. It also if from a Japanese game developer porting to PC.

Please keep this in mind with your purchasing.

My Thoughts: It is not a bad port for me personally so take this with a grain of salt. The game is fun for the Anime God Simulator that is this hack and slash. You have over 145 Warriors to use and contend with, in the form of grinding out levels, weapons, items, skills, character bonds, guntlet mode, etc.

For an example: Every character has 100 levels before you are given the option to promote them beyond that. There are also 9 promotion levels to grind out. Now this would be 100 levels x 9 promotion levels = 900 levels in total, now times that by 145 characters. 100 x 9 x 145 = 130,500 levels in total. This is worse case scenario if you are a 100% completionist.

Now, if you wanna chill and just play the story then by all means go for it. This is also going to take you like 40 hours at the least even more so if you are not used to the Warriors gameplay.

Also, if you like to play games by just paying attention to the voice over work, I have some bad news. The voice over work is only in Japanese, so you can't just do this missions they give you on voice alone, But they do show where you need to go on the mini map so just look on that from time to time if you can not be bothered with reading the subtitles.

I am used to this stuff from Omega Force in regards to all of this. So I don't mind it.

I would recommend to buy this on sale, because at the moment it is $56.95 AU. it is still a little too high even with "all of the DLC"

Happy Trails In Slaying the Hydra.\t\t\t\t\t
