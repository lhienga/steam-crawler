\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, the ten-year wait has finally paid off - congratulations!
I've spent hundreds of hours playing it on PS3, PSP & Switch and no doubt it's one of the best musou game.

Recommend to play on 60 Hz because some graphical glitches appear at a higher level (black shaders in Ma Chao's musou for example).

ATTENTION! If you plan to play together on the same keyboard like in the good old days, then you will not succeed. The co-op is implemented with two gamepads (yeah, many people have them, but some do not). Giving one player a gamepad and another a keyboard is not an option.

It's very nice that you left achievements (that didn't happen to DW7XLCE), now waiting for new arrivals of the classic: Orochi Z, SW2 HD Ver., SW3, DW6 and further more...!

Remote Play Together is a great feature, but I would like to have classic online. 0o\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, comparing this to their previous console to PC ports (coughs Dynasty Warriors 7 coughs), I have to say Koei did a really good job of not f**king up the port this time.

Pros:
- 60+ FPS gameplay.
- Roughly 50+ hours of content.
- All WO3 contents for the price of under $12 (in my country currency).
- MOUSE SUPPORT? FINALLY THE FIRST TITLE IN THE SERIES WHICH HAS MOUSE SUPPORT.

Cons:
- Horrible controller support (seriously, did Koei even properly test the controller input before releasing WO3UDE on PC?). I had to turn off DS4Windows to be able to even use the controller properly, as the game suffers from what's called "double input", which have never happened to any other games that I played using a controller.
- Forced 30 FPS during in battle cutscenes. I wonder if this is intentional of if this is impossible to change. Nontheless I still enjoy the 60+ FPS gameplay.
- Keeping the same poorly translated English subtitles at a noticeable amount of lines whist Koei had like, 9 years? to fix this. I don't have much of a complain to this but you have the time and manpower to work it out though?

I'll still keep this as a recommendation though. Keep up the good work Koei.

Also please release Samurai Warriors 3 to PC.\t\t\t\t\t
