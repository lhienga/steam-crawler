\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had the original on consoles and is a bit better than I remember now that I have all the "extra" content with it. Here is my list of pros and cons.

Pros
-Bigger character selection
-good ost
-more weapons
-story is fine
-Gauntlet mode is fine
-battlefield editor is a nice start.
-mounts can still strafe

Cons
-Crashes on occasion
-AI by default is not aggressive enough. I much preferred the DW2-3 aggression.
-Gate captains (now base) do not show up as its on icon on the map. You can also no longer clear maps as there is one checkpoint always left open and the trash that pours out of it takes your checkpoints.
-AI dies way too quick off screen regardless of difficulty (failed a stage a few times in under a minute.)
-No way to gauge morale
-battlefield editor is barebones (Tried looking for mods no luck yet.)
-long load times for an in battle cutscene
-arbitrary limit of 8 weapons a character
-No English voice acting

Overall though still a fun game, would recommend during the next sale.\t\t\t\t\t

1. before you play the game go to its library settings.
2. set game resolution to what in-game resolution matches (set mine to 1600x900)
3. set compatibility tools for Proton experimetal
4. Now play the game. in-game, (after the intro cut scene) go to options > graphics, and set resolution to what you set in steamdeck
5. Also change display mode from Full to Borderless
6. select Apply and Save.
done. enjoy playing!
But before you exit the game, in-game, go back to the options>graphics. set the resolution back to a lower setting (ie: 1024x576) and display mode to FULL because when you wish to play again, you need to redo step4 to 6, otherwise, you will experience the frame-sync issue.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are a lot more cons than pros, but I think the pros outweigh the negatives.

Pros: There is a ton of content, a ton of characters, and the game play is fun. It does feel like a typical Warriors Orochi game. So far i've played story mode, and honestly I don't think there is any way I will get to see all the content.

Cons: Some of the battles are annoying. Example, a random NPC will die on the other side of the map and it causes the battle to be lost, or a random officer will just chain me to death while surrounded by my allies because took my time killing him. The game has built in ways to stop you from grinding and its annoying asf. You are basically forced into not messing around at all, and have to go all out on your enemies or you'll auto-lose the battle because of the anti-grind measures they put into the game. Because everything is subtitled, you REALLY have to pay attention to the victory conditions and whats going on all over the map or you could just randomly lose. The UI / Controls are really bad and not intuitive, and not seeing a save button made me forget to save several times already thinking it was auto-saving. Having to go back and redo battles 5 times because of no saves and deaths on easy mode really makes me annoyed.\t\t\t\t\t
