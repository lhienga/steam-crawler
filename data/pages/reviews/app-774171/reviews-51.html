\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like cute things and you like rhythm games, you'll like muse dash. Whether you are an advanced or casual player, Musedash's forgiving timing and score system combined with its simple gameplay style allows for the existence of challenging songs by incorporating visual and technical elements to any track. The DLC adds a ton of new songs and even after 60+ hours I am less than halfway through the entire list.
Characters/muses are a fun way to enhance your game in ways you may not think. Changing your muse allows you to make the game easier, harder, or completely change how the game can be played like in the cases of the touhou characters and miku.
I highly recommend the base game for all rhythm game enthusiasts and the DLC is a worthy investment in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
