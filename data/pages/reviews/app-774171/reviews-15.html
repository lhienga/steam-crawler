\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMuse Dash is a great rhythm game made accessible for all skill levels due to the 2-button nature of the game with a great selection of music.

Pros:
- Each level has it's own enemy designs/bosses, though this is purely for aesthetics.
- Great and varied character designs.
- Massive amount of content, even more with the DLC.
- Music is varied, covering all different types of music.
- Very accessible due to only needing to use 2 buttons to play (3 if you include the fever button).

Cons:
- Rarely when doing long note presses, the input drops out, more than likely a me issue.
- While there are a lot of characters you're more than likely going to be locked into 3 characters if going for high scores.\t\t\t\t\t
