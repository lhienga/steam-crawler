\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI LOVE Muse Dash so genuinely.
I really enjoy rhythm games, but often find myself overwhelmed by a lot of them because of the number of buttons. I always get all mixed up when songs get faster or more demanding. This problem has effectively been done away with in this game, since only 2 buttons are required for any song. HOWEVER that doesn't necessarily dull down the gameplay. The songs range from being almost painfully easy to absolutely horrifyingly difficult. Each has 3 difficulties, so most songs can be played by anyone at any level in at least one mode. (Excluding some more ridiculous DLC songs that bottom out at a 7 or 8 lmao)
I enjoy playing so much that I bought this game 3 times. Steam, mobile, and Nintendo Switch, and I don't regret a single one. It's a great game to occupy yourself when you're bored but don't wanna commit to anything longterm, and it's also good for long car rides too. It's a game I can always pick up for a bit of quick fun. I'm listed as having few hours here, but I assure you between all platforms, it's a significantly higher number. I play via steam least because I usually play when on the go.

OH! I also wanna praise the accessibility features. You're able to dim and darken the backgrounds. As a visually impaired player, this helped me a LOT to make the note pieces stand out more against brighter backgrounds. This feature is a HUGE HUGE deal for me because it's so rare to find in games.

Over all phenomenal game. Glad my favorite streamer introduced me to it!\t\t\t\t\t
