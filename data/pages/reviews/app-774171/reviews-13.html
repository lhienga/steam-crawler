TL;DR - Just “Okay” on a discount, fun and easy to learn with anime music/aesthetics, shortlived without paying for extra music packs.

I wish I could give this a Mixed review;

The pricing for extra songs is ludicrous - it was justified with the previous ‘Just as Planed’ DLC that is basically the current MuseDash+ except you’d also get all future DLCs for the price of $33.99/32,99€
With MD+ you won’t get the Miku and Rin & Len DLCs (and all future ones that will be coming out) but you do get a 20% discount on those.

With that said - the game itself is a reimagining of Taiko no Tatsujin just with anime girls doing cute things presented in a flashy, cute-pop fashion instead of hitting Don and Ka on a taiko drum.
It’s easy to understand and easy to follow at times, very fun to play when you do have found the right tracks from different music packs but given the steep pricing on extra packs it’s very hard to recommend to anyone who likes variety in their rhythm games without digging deep in their pockets.

It’s a good game to dip your toes into when it’s on sale as some songs from the base game are quite fun to listen to and play, but don’t expect a long lasting experience by only paying the $1-3 admission fee, most of the fun is locked behind a $30+ paywall and then some (like DLCs that aren’t included in MD+).\t\t\t\t\t
