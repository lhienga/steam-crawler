\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was pleasantly surprised by this rhythm game. Nice graphics, good music and anime girls.
Even without DLC, you can enjoy the available tracks and get all the achievements.
Easy control in two buttons, nice to play both on the keyboard and on a gamepad (but I still prefer a gamepad, as for me it`s more mobile).
If you like rhythm games, then Muse dash will attract you in and you might even want to buy the DLC.

I think Muse dash can be a good first introduction to games of this genre, so I can recommend it even to those who have never played such games. <3\t\t\t\t\t
