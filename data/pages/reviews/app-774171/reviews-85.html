\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhilst I have not purchased the add on to the game at this point (I am waiting for it to go on sale), I think that this game is extremely fun, especially as one of my first rhythm games besides Geometry Dash. It has some amazing unique game-play twists with all the characters and choosing which character to use for which level is quite engaging.

If I had a few critiques, I would say that the game-play can sometimes just be mashed through as there is no punishment unless the level design is clever enough to place obstacles, but most of the time they're forgotten. Additionally, I think that the Silencer elfin that turns off the passive powers of the characters should be free. It literally does nothing and removes the abilities, so I'm confused why it would be locked behind a paywall besides greed. Please, I just wanna use the succubus on easier levels that I like the music of.

Overall, the music is absolutely amazing (if you don't like J-core it does have some songs you may like but be warned it is the main amount of the OST), the graphics and beautifully detailed backgrounds and enemies makes it such a blast to play. I'm in love with the game and can't wait to purchase Muse Plus whenever it goes on sale to get some more songs and characters to use!

TL/DR: Good music, great art, cute girls. That enough for you?\t\t\t\t\t
