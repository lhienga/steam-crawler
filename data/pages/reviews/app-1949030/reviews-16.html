\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA wonderful, well crafted and beautiful game from Frogwares. I have never been disappointed in a Frogwares title, and this one really takes the cake.

Challenging gameplay that doesn't hold your hand but still requires you to do a bit of thinking, beautiful graphical design and a large array of options, and a compelling story through every twist and turn! The characters were all interesting and unique, and I love how being able to play Watson as the voice of reason helps ground all of the happenings and provides a unique perspective, and I liked all of the interactions between the NPCs and location based characters.

My heart goes out to the team for all the loss suffered during the development of this game, and I hope the future becomes better for them.

10/10 Eels, would play again, ended up buying it 3 times as I kickstarted and then bought it for 2 friends.

Definitely recommend it if you enjoy other puzzle games that are in a world of their own.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI feel like we're back at Testament Frogwares, it's so close to being really good but just can't quite make it over the hump. I think this game was pretty much designed to appeal to players who made it through the original. I loved almost every minute of it despite all the clunkiness, shallow gameplay, and bland deduction system. They prioritized the cinematics over gameplay which hurts but is definitely newcomer friendly. I miss the process of the older games, going to the archive, tinkering with chemistry set. A lot of missed opportunities imo. the side content was okay, most of which came from the DLC which padded the game maybe a whole hour. It's a bit of a shame to see such wonderful areas made that serve no purpose in the game. At least there was no egregious QTEs or wonky action sequences that disrupted the flow at any point. I still highly recommend the game but we're 20 years on in experiments with sherlock holmes that only really landed once with Crimes and Punishment imo and that's because it's one of the only games aside from the original Awakened and Jack the Ripper, that made you feel like Sherlock Holmes. They've had a lot of good ideas over the years with poor execution and while this game has much better presentation and execution of its constituent parts, it was too dumbed down to be truly great. I scoured every area, did all the side quests and dlc and got 100% in under 11 hours. It's worthwhile regardless if you've played the original but works best if you get that hit of nostalgia.\t\t\t\t\t
