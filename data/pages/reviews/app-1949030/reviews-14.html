Overall the aesthetic design of the game was fantastic, however, instruction on the game seems very lacking. Clues are all over the place with huge maps, So if you walked through a clue without noticing it it might take you a long time to proceed with the game (unless you are going through with a guide or something). It can be VERY frustrating to play from times.

Player sometimes would be forced to pick clues that fits the story's narrative instead of from logic. Feels like the story comes first, then the clues are just an afterthoughts.

Also they added a feature where you can pin your evidence on the interface and ask questions about it to NPCs for answers. While this is a cool added feature, 95% of the people you ask have nothing to contribute to the answer you seek, yet you are forced to freeze screen and listen to voice lines from the same actors repeatedly narrate the same line from different NPCs (ex:"sorry, I cant help you with that question"). A massive waste of time. (kinda reminds me of Skyrim Oblivion where they use the same voice actors)

Also personally I am not so big on pointless puzzles, but there are several in this game that forces you to go through. I play this game mainly for the story.. so if you are like me.. this game will give you a massive headache.

I really really liked the other games from Frogwares (majority), but this one definitely is a miss.

Conclusion-

Easy to navigate - 3/10
Story - 7/10
Problem solving satisfaction - 3/10


I think this game could've been finished a lot quicker if you wouldn't need to waste your time running around finding clues on a massive map. (very very frustrating)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tConsidering the circumstances in which the team made the game, it's a tremendous accomplishment and they really should be commended for their efforts.

It breaks my heart not to recommend the game, but I've got to be honest. I really wanted to like the game... but, honestly, it's pretty bad. Aside from the clunky controls and many bugs, it's completely lackluster. I loved the other games, but sadly, this one missed the mark. The story was roughly half the length of Chapter One (which was excellent). The Awakened felt linear, rushed, and much simpler than its predecessors. Aside from some scene rebuilding, you're not doing much detective work this time around either.

Sherlock was the best part of the game. He's just great! The voice actor really brings Sherlock to life. Watson, however, was incredibly annoying in this game. He should have added much more emotional depth, but he was just irritating. It could just be me, but he was *always* in the way of where I needed to go. There were countless times that I'd try to interact with something, and then Watson's big, fat head is just there -_- Oh, and the gameplay in the final mission is truly awful, very anticlimactic.

If you can afford it, I'd still recommend buying the game to support the people at Frogwares. I'd also like to recommend Sherlock Holmes Chapter One, it was a brilliant game! As were the other Frogware Sherlock games - check them out!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely love the story! And I'm going to start with the negatives because I'm a glass half empty type of person. Things I didn't like: 1) The game mechanics felt a bit clunky starting out (Probably me getting used to them and the tutorials didn't feel very helpful lol). 2) The stickler to the order of operation you had to do could feel a bit forced. 3) (my biggest problem I had with this game) I felt there could have been more story. There is so much potential and I'm sure DLC's will fix this complaint later. 4) The mind palace feels more of a step backward from Chapter One, more forced. 5) The last villain felt like the least impressive villain after the previous ones. But I love this game and the way the developers are telling their version Sherlock Holmes! I liked the different clothes that you have (and you aren't required to use them, just if you want to). The game is beautiful. Definitely had two bad guys that I absolutely hated (as intended). Despite me saying the main story needs more (and I don't mean fluff), it still has a lot to offer and took me on a great adventure. I'll probably play it again before any DLC's come out just to enjoy it again. I honestly like the developers version of Sherlock and Watson more than some of the tv shows. Great Game!\t\t\t\t\t
