\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is alright and can be enjoyable, but it doesn't let you fail and the puzzles are easy. Story wise, this is NOT Sherlock Holmes dealing with a Lovecraftian cult, as I had thought it would be. This is a game about a detective in a Lovecraftian universe where madness and meaninglessness are a given, Cthulu is for sure real, and your detective is not very stable.

Ultimately, the name Sherlock Holmes was used to grab attention and sell a Lovecraftian game that is pretty short and not actually scary.\t\t\t\t\t
