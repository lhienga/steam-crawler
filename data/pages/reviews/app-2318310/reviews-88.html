there are also such sparkling dialogues as:
"I just had an anxiety flash back to when Hillary Clinton lost to Obama last summer"

"I can die fulfilled cuz dying together with another girl is a bigger commitment than some soft ass ♥♥♥♥♥ getting married to jack the construction worker or some other boring white guy"

or

"marijuana is a baby drug. you can't even die off it"

This game is everything i could ever want it to be and more. The way the characters are so absolutely abrasive to each other and are mostly completely irredeemable is what makes it so fun to watch them. The writing, although may come off as rather extreme, is completely believable in most parts. My mid 2000's schooling experience may have been slightly different, but i still see myself in most of the characters and dialogues (side note. there is no H content in this game, so don't go in expecting it)

This may very well be my new favorite VN of all time. Topping Somnium files... well.. maybe toppling it... that is, if i can figure out what the ending of this game really means...

This was absolutely what i needed and when i needed this. I am saddened the first game managed to duck my radar; but I pray this one won't duck yours.\t\t\t\t\t
