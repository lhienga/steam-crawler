Getting the star lacrosse player killed or maimed

Accidentally inciting hate crimes
Getting teachers fired

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was shorter than the first one but still as good IMHO. I liked how Jecka became the voice of reason while Nicole got more and more evil in a way. It reminds me of going to school at the time in the UK and while it is American centric, some parts were relatable. It is the first VN i have played in a while and it is easily the best. It is easily playable in a few days and you can play it again whenever you want and it will still feel fresh. The voice actresses were believable and truly showed emotion like in the cop scene when Nicole got... well i'll let you see it for yourself.
