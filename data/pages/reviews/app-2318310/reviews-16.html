\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn the original Nicole was an ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ in a sea of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. In this one she is an absolute psychopath in a sea of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, for better or for worse. All of the characters behave kind of differently this time, which is weird. The main thing for me was Nicole being much more aggressive with Jecka, which makes their friendship seem much different and IMO actually made their dialogue together less fun. I feel this less captures "mid to late oughts teenager" vibes and is kind of just more modern cynical humor. This a bit darker and more depressing than the original in overall tone for sure, which some may like more, some less.

I will say that story paths are waaay longer this time, with each run taking a half hour or so while the original had some come in under 10 minutes. My only issue is that in that half hour you only actively make 3-5 decisions, about the same as the original had in 10 minutes, which makes it feel more like a passive experience. Also the branches are kind of confusing as to what leads to where as the selections are more vague in context this time; it also feels like a lot of the scenes don't actually really jive with the main story that gets created, and characters that should be mad or untrusting based on something you did show up again shortly after like nothing happened because the different story paths overlap so heavily. It just makes the game feel technically a bit messier, with the much longer playthroughs relying on not that much more actual content.

Still, if you liked the original you should give this a go.\t\t\t\t\t
