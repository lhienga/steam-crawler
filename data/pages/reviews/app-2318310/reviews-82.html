\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIncredible, funny as hell, and amazing voice acting to wrap it all together.


Almost everyone in this game have some atrocious attribute to them that makes them terrible people, but they're often the funniest characters.
The "worst" characters in this game are only the worst because they're boring. They don't have a fatal flaw that makes them undesirable. Often its just that they're uninteresting, or bossy, or annoying. They have the least to hate about them, but you just do, because they just feel too normal for the world they're in.

Nicole is just an ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. She has some reason to be, so you don't immediately write her off, but she just makes it so hard to root for her. Almost all of the bad things that happen often only happen because she lets her view of the world and the people around her ruin any opportunity that she gets to make some friends. For the beginning of the game, you try your best you lead her to an ending that will hopefully make her happy. Something to change her view on the world. It just never happens, and its always because she ruins it for herself. There is not happy ending because of the way she thinks.
Eventually you give up on trying to help Nicole, and you just start riding the wave the game is setting up, and its a blast.

10/10 make another one I'd buy it
