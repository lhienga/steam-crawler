Buy it if you want some unexpected laughs but don't expect a redeeming story about a misguided youth. Nicole is an irredeemable ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ and she stays an irredeemable ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ no matter what you do. Is it fun? Yeah. Is it a visual novel about love and acceptance? Absolutely not. Maybe that's not a bad thing, but I felt horrible trying to fit myself into the shoes of someone who probably *is* a sociopath who doesn't feel empathy for anyone but herself.

I hope it gets a sequel just to see how her life spirals out of control after high school or if she ever learns from her actions later in life. I don't know if it's one of those games where you could do an "all endings are canon" kinda thing... it's not like Henry Stickmin where continuity is loose and you can retcon even the character's death in one of the endings if you find a way to make it cool and/or funny.

Agree or disagree with me in the replies, I have a lot of mixed emotions with this game, and idk if I'm understanding all of them.\t\t\t\t\t
