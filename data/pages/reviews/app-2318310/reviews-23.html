\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game made me feel understood. specifically me at that 16-17 year old age. I saw an uncomfortable amount of myself in Nicole and kin her hard. Just drift from social circle to circle being calloused, cold and dismissive of others, get whatever i wanted outta them and then drift away.

"They are just walking clichés and they are so unobservant they can't even recognise that." I'd often think. I didn't take them seriously. But there i was, maybe 1 or 2 genuine connections in my life. Connections I was more dependent on that'd I'd like to admit and connections that were fading with the divergence of life paths. At least they were genuine in who they were, they didn't carry the baggage of self hatred. I resented them for that. I couldn't engage with them in good faith. I resigned myself to wallowing in self pity about not having any genuine connection with people all the while dismissing anyone I found not quite as sharp as I'd hoped, a little too sincere, a little too overbearing.

I was a deeply hateful person, and frankly, I still am to a lesser extent. By the time I recongised this, all who I had hurt were long out of my life. I am now alone. Everyone moved on. I am still right here. My Jecka and Emily are doing just fine without me, I'm incomplete without them.

Ridiculously funny, Incredibly witty, heartrending, and dare I say profound. If you are like most people, you'll have a great laugh with this. If you are like me or Nicole, You'll be in equal parts cathartically validated as much as you are ruthlessly criticised. Emotionally powerful either way.

Emily better get a good amount of screen time in the anime. She ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ deserves it.
