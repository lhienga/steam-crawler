\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great game for fans of the genre. Chained Echoes has a wonderful story, great character development, and plenty of side content to keep you busy. At it's price point, this game is definitely worth it's value and more.

My only complaint is that I wanted the fights to be more challenging and complex; for the fights to really make you think and consider a strategy in order to best counter the enemy. In the beginning of the game fights seemed dynamic in this way though this was likely artificial, for lack of skills. Late in the game I found myself repeating the same tactics to completely overpower the enemies. Even on Hard Difficulty with Aggressiveness at Hard, I did not need min-max crystals or equipment to fell the, supposed, super boss. This was disappointing.

Additional content that forces players to max different skills and use different strategies would be amazing for this game. The amount of characters and skills that you have access to late in the game are so vast and it's all wasted because it's just unnecessary for any of the content.

Loved the game, loved the story and lore, loved the nostalgic old school vibe.\t\t\t\t\t
