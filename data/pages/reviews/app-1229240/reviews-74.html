\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChained Echoes is a top notch turned based RPG. Nearly everything about it oozes quality and the creators clearly put a lot of care into the game's design. Because characters fully heal after every fight, enemies don't hold back and even regular fights can pose a challenge at times; luckily, fights are not randomized and are not too frequent. The story ensnares the characters in a typical world-ending plot with epic powered being(s) as the final boss.

The crystal system unfortunately brings the game down slightly. Crystals replicate the dozens of passive skills characters can have and they can be equipped to weapons and armor similar to Final Fantasy VII materia. However, each crystal occupies one to three slots (equipment maxes out at 4 slots) and the system actively punishes you for using crystals by making them "big" (3 slot crystals) and reducing their purity to 0 (limits leveling) when you unequip them. In all, you get a *lot* of crystals and 2/3 of them are useless based on their stats (level, slots used, and purity) and it becomes busy work to manage them. Even a basic sort/filter would improve crystal management. Fortunately, crystals are not necessary for progression.\t\t\t\t\t
