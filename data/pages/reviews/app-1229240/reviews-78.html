\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChained Echoes is a beautifully-crafted JRPG with a lot of content. It gets a lot right: The artdesign and music is beautiful and the story gets you hooked really fast. The game is full of surprising, sad and funny moments and has an interesting and challenging combat system. It also provides a lot of different party characters with individual strengths. My first playthrough took me about 70 hours. What I especially liked, was, that although combat-heavy, the game isn't grindy. So- Chained Echoes is a great game!
Even the elements where I partially thought that they were a little too much - weapon upgrading AND modification AND a guild AND a clan - although not working so well, still contributed to the game.

Chained Echoes really, really rocks, and I'm looking forward to a successor. I'd love to see more of the world and the story.
