I cannot recommend this enough, the singular developer has risen to minor level of worship from this staggeringly complete work of art. I want to see more from this universe. I will pay to see more from this universe.

This the probably the best love letter to late SNES RPGs written by a Final Fantasy Tactics and Xenogears fan ever created. This man shouldn't be hired by Square, Square should gift him a studio.
