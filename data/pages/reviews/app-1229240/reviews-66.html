\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's solid, though I got my expectations too high in advance.

This is coming from someone who trolls Steam for RPGmaker games and their ilk, someone who dares to consider himself a connoisseur of the genre, so to speak. And this one is fine. I can't give that high of marks when I have no patience to keep up with the actual storytelling going on beyond the major plot beats, but It's been awhile since I had the patience to get invested in any jrpg storytelling, so take that as you will.

The actual mechanical systems and game you're playing, though, that's pretty solid. I'm enjoying it quite a bit, though it takes its sweet time actually giving you enough party members to feel like you have a choice in your party composition. If you like turn-based jrpg combat, this is some solid turn-based jrpg combat. I wouldn't call it a gold standard or anything, and there are definitely times where there's too much going on all at once to keep track of, but but it's got me rolling my eyes through the storytelling to enjoy the gameplay, so that's an accomplishment.

Though take it from me that the game is slow actually letting you off the leash- you're like halfway through the game before sidequests become a thing, and they're handed out pretty sparingly despite that. It's fun when they finally do, and the game isn't so much unenjoyable before, as taunting you with the fact that they clearly are eventually going to open up and let you off the leash, but not yet.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery good JRPG.
Unique, interesting, and fun combat system.
Tons of quality of life improvements to the genre as a whole.
The story and writing isn't cheesy at all, which elevates it above most anime games I've played recently.
The crafting system is largely irrelevant sadly and the game would probably be better if it was heavily toned down or removed entirely. I tried messing around with it but a lot of the marginal increases to power just seemed not worth the time investment to sort them.
Impressively, game didn't overextend it's welcome even with most sidequests done and had a satisfying ending.
8/10 I would look forward to an (admittedly unlikely) sequel.\t\t\t\t\t
