\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game ! Soundtrack is amazing, it definitely made my playthrough more atmospheric and also nostalgic. I think I heard a few tunes similar to ones from games I loved, like Haven or CrossCode. I liked art style a lot. Story is very good, has a few interesting twists and make you want to play more and more. I liked most of the characters. I believe only one was not compelling to me. Exploration - your characters move very fast what's a bit weird at first but later on one's grateful for that decision. Chests have meaningful loot. A while later you get certain mechanic allowing you to fly throughout places. Fighting in it becomes quite boring with little time but in terms of exploration it's extremely fun. Combat - overdrive bar is an interesting idea in classic turn based JRPG combat system but it can be a bit troubling, especially when 1 type of action is repeatedly chosen as the one able to decrease overdrive bar. From my experience, there are specific difficulty spikes in the game, mostly at the beginning and the end of the game. The former when you don't have a full party of 4 yet and trying to adapt to the overdrive system. The latter is about optional endgame bosses who have mechanics to literally one shot your characters. I was a bit mad about it at first but then I read on the Internet about stacking agility buffs ( This stat is responsible for picking how fast certain character's turn is, with much agility one character can have even 2, 3 or more turns before the enemy). Without this mechanic it would be really hard for me to beat the optional but game didn't really teach me about it itself. I'm saying that because most of the game is relatively easy so I didn't have to learn about it until the endgame. Most of the main story is balanced about person who didn't do any side quests ( at least that's what I think). Some of them can give you learning points for skills and permanent stats' upgrades. But it can be easily forgiven by other great aspects of the game. Overall I had a great time with this game, definitely worth a full price for somebody liking this type of experience.\t\t\t\t\t
