\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI usually play JRPGS for the story, exploration and the characters. The combat, especially in turn based games is the boring part which I have to endure to enjoy other good aspects of the game. Ironically, CE was the opposite for me. The combat is the best part of the game, whereas the story/characters are okay definitely not amazing.

The combat is absolutely superb. There is no stupid QTEs involved (Sea of Stars... that game the combat feels like a chore) but yet the combat is fast paced, challenging and engaging thanks to the overdrive gauge, and how the SFX and combat animations are as crisp as they can be. I usually hate swapping party members in and out mid combat, but this game it was done in such a well thought way enjoyed doing it. This game truly offers the best combat among any turn based JRPGS I have ever touched. Strongly recommended.

Not just combat, this game grasps the essence of what makes JRPGS great, put a handful of nice modernizing touch on top of it without breaking that essence. It really showed the direction that all future JRPGS should strive to go. This game will be the standard that I use to evaluate all future JRPGS for years to come.\t\t\t\t\t
