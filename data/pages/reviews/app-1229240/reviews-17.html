Dead Space Remake was amazing (aside from occasional stutters), but it's a remake...
I'm also not going to count early access titles because they aren't "in a finished state".

I played some other good games this year to be sure, but some of them were games from prior years (waited for sales); or just passing fads - I look back and have nothing more than a "Yea, that game was fun for a bit (bored yawn)" feeling at best, with a few I wish i hadn't purchased (and realized too late for refund).

Looking back at Chained Echos though? Sure, it had some flaws or annoyances (crystal farming >.> RNG $#^@!), but of this fiscal year's released games that I've played - Chained Echos was the only one (aside from DSRemake) that actually makes me look back and feel : "Yeah, that was a good ride."\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t One of the best RPGs I've played in a long time. It really checks all the boxes for me: Exploration, character customization, good combat system (even if some fights become trivial due to builds), enjoyable story, and plenty of secrets to hunt down and complete.
My only main complaint about this game is that the crystal system feels very trivial in the sense of getting the right crystals is inherently too random. Along with having to upgrade a piece of gear to use your crystals, only to get a new piece of gear to replace it in the very next area, causing you to have to repeat this process over and over again. I found myself just saving my crystals until I was near the end and having all the final gear I needed to upgrade first before even really getting back into the crystal upgrading.
All this being said, I highly recommend getting this game. Get it on sale. Get it for normal price. If you like a good old fashioned 16 bit styled RPG that reminds you of the days of Final Fantasy VI and Chrono Trigger, you owe it to yourself to play this gem of a game.\t\t\t\t\t
