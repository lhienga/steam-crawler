\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat to play with friends, great to play solo.

Loved exploring the maps. Played through the game to get 100%. The variety of guns, abilities and builds is incredible. I kept wanting to play more, to try out different builds. Didn't find all trinkets and armor sets but I got most of them. Getting the one item you want might be an ordeal since maps are randomly generated.

Shooting mechanics were very smooth even in multiplayer. And a total of 40 bosses most with alternative ways to kill them gives a lot of replayability.

Multiplayer browser is a little lacking, because it does not allow for browsing based on ping. When playing multiplayer I joined a host with a lot of lag, so much in fact that I couldn't unequip my melee weapon. Tried to unequip it like 5 times before game decided that 'attempted cheating detected' and kicked me out. My character in the main menu shown gear level 1, a little bug that almost gave me a heart attack, thought I lost my save file.

It bothered me a little that almost ever single boss would spawn additional enemies during the battle, but nothing a coop partner wouldn't help with.\t\t\t\t\t
