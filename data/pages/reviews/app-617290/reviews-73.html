\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I tried the demo for this game a couple years back but I didn't get hooked and I forgot about it. Then I heard that there's a sequel on the way and I was intrigued and decided to pick this up while it was on sale and give it another try, and boy do I regret not sticking with it back then.

I'm nowhere near completing this game but I just wanted to write a quick review because of how the story and the lore hooked me instantly this time around. The game is very linear, the enviroment looks more repetitive than anything I've played really and the gunplay is fun enough to keep me busy but the lore, oh man.

I guess the similarity of this world and the world of Darksiders is what got me hooked while exploring and reading every single journal I came across. The backstory is pretty obscure but it's still so interesting - I'm looking forward to discovering more of "The Founder" than the actual game. Just following the story of this one man and how he tried to find out how to stop the "Root" that is the antagonist of the game (far as I know as of yet).

I know I'm very late to the party with this one but if you happen to stumble upon this review and are in the same boat as me; definitely try it before the sequel comes out, the lore is worth it alone.\t\t\t\t\t
