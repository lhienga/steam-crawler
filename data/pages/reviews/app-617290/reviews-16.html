Labyrinth armor
Galenic Charm or Soul Anchor
Spirit Stone/Burden of the Follower or Soul Link

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this on sale. 10 hours in and I confidently say it was worth the price I paid. The game itself is really fun. Guns and abilities are satisfying. There is a bit of the Souls-like exploration and challenge. Traits and weapon mods allow for a decent amount of build diversity. The game runs well and coop is a smooth experience. No bugs encountered. I'd give it a solid 8/10.

My biggest complaint is that the campaign is on the shorter side (10 hours for a first play through). The replay value comes from their level design being semi-randomized, so in order to fight every boss you either replay the campaign, play in a mode known as "adventure mode" that allows you to fight bosses quicker, or jump into online coop with randoms/friends to see if their world contains bosses you have not seen. That is the stage I am at currently, and I could see myself getting another 10+ hours at least before I move on to Remnant II.\t\t\t\t\t
