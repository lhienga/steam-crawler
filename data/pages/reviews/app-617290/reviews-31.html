\r\n\t\t\t\t\t7/10 - It's a great game, but it's not perfect.

There is a lot of content in this game. There are two gamemodes: Campaign and adventure mode. Campaign: pretty self explanatory. Adventure mode lets you choose a world from the campaign with a couple dungeons and bosses. The purpose of adventure mode is to look for and earn more gear, scrap and xp. The worlds and dungeons are procedurally generated which have its pros and cons.
Pros:
- This offers a lot of replayability in both gamemodes.
- It extends the game, since it's random what bosses, dungeons and random events you get.
Cons:
- It extends the game, since it's random you will have to rerun adventure mode until you get that specific boss or dungeon to get that last item you need for your build.
- It gets really repetitive after a sertain point.

The adventure mode makes a great alternative if you're playing the campaign on a harder difficulty and get stuck on a boss since you can play adventure mode on a lower difficulty without losing any progress on the campaign. This allows you to acquire better gear and level up before returning to the campaign.

The combat is really fun but can feel clunky in some situations, mostly in close quarters.
You get a ton of different weapons and a lot of different weapon mods which offers a great variety to character builds. There are also many traits to complement your builds and I absolutely love that you're not gonna get level capped so you can level up all traits. When writing this review my character is in trait level 824 and you can put up to 20 points in each trait (you get a point for each level). I love being able to change build whenever i feel like it and this game really gives you that freedom.

One really important thing you should know is that the campaign doesn't have an end. In order to play through the end of the story you'll need to buy the subject 2923 dlc. That is a big negative on this game. You shouldn't have to get a dlc to complete the main story. With that said, that dlc is actually really good imo. I just wish it wasn't a dlc... maybe buy it with discount.

Oh btw, don't get swamps of corsus dlc unless it's discounted. It only adds one of the campaigns worlds to adventure mode with some additional gear, adds skins to armor (don't worry, no micro transactions) and adds a new gamemode: survival mode.\t\t\t\t\t
