\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is fun, surprised me that there is still people hosting online coop session, and not only high levels but also people starting up (or at least starting with a second character). Although I recommend joining online session after doing a normal campaign run first on your own (don't recommend starting on hard as it will be frustrating and you will lack knowledge on synergies, even if you understand them, you wont have the needed items for a strong build)

As it says on the tin, is a sort of shooter with souls-like style combat, the start will be rough due to a lack of experience with the enemies and lack of understanding of builds/items that work best together. There are also a few mechanics the game does not explain (for example, rolling to remove fire damage quicker) so its not a bad idea to check for a few starting up tips before diving in.

There is a quite a variety of items and a lot of builds you can aim for, the trick is, loot is randomly generated (and a few items are tied to certain maps of the game, or are obtainable only after beating a puzzle or defeating a boss with a certain condition) so there is a good chance you will get a mixed bag of rings, weapons, armor sets and even traits that don't really compliment each other so you will have to improvise as best you can while you accumulate more skill points and items.

Its fun, the campaign was nice and I had a good time doing adventure runs with random people online, I didn't find hackers or trolls playing and currently I am waiting on a sale to get the DLC so I can complete the achievements and enjoy it a bit more.\t\t\t\t\t
