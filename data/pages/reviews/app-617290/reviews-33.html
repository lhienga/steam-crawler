\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolo experience review.

I don't like replaying games, even if it adds a new content in NG+.
The only reason I do this is if it's required for the 100% completion.

This game is different, you actually start playing after your first play-through, once you understand the game mechanics.
You just want to replay and replay and replay to find new gear and try new builds.
Once you like the build, you want to perfect it by finding the build specific gear and it makes you to play again and again and again.
You eventually become stronger and want to try harder difficulties that may reveal flaws in your build and it'll make you play the game even more to improve the build and your skill.

The guns feel great, the gun mods are interesting and fun and you'll have to play a lot to get and try all of them.

The only complain I have is the enemy scaling system, but it can be easily "fixed" by stopping upgrades at +12 or so.

Don't pass by this game, it is really good.\t\t\t\t\t
