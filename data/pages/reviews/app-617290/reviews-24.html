\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is aggressively, frustratingly-bad. I can only assume the people who have left it positive reviews
1. Played with friends to mitigate any shortcomings with the design.
2. Played on the easy difficulties (Normal, Hard).
3. Didn't really think much about what they were playing and just enjoyed the fact they were playing a co-op game where the shooting felt good and the environments are cool.

-Level-scaling. What this means is when you reach a certain area or upgrade your gear, the zone levels up with you. You never 'progress' - you will always take 5 shots to kill that enemy with your pistol at level 0, and with a gun at level 20 shooting a level 20 enemy, the same is true. It is kind of baffling why they even bothered with this system at all when they could just remove the levels and nothing would change
-Some of the worst bosses I have ever seen. EVERY boss spawns adds that swarm and attack you from offscreen, sandbagging the fight, and making an otherwise easy to kill and dodge boss an annoying headache. Every boss that is not an act or 'main' boss is the same model and attack pattern of regular elite enemies you fight in the overworld, except now they spawn adds and have a particle effect phase 2 gimmick. I SINCERELY cannot stress how awful EVERY single boss is. The other reviews and comments here cannot do it justice.
-Difficulty is horrendous in its design. You have two difficulties: Normal/ hard (you will fall asleep, one-shotting every common enemy with unupgraded weapons, breezing through levels, never coming anywhere close to death, and beating bosses on your first try. Nightmare/ Apocalypse (You will die in 1-3 hits from any boss or enemy attack, even with hp maxed and wearing heavy armor. Bosses have an enormous, spongy amount of health. You will die randomly and not make 'progress' learning a boss, you will instead get lucky that they didn't spawn adds because the game bugged out, or the ads spawned from a different door so now you can progress. Or you get grazed by a stray hit because you were dealing with the 5 enemies who leap 15 feet offscreen at you and die instantly because you didn't perfectly dodge everything with all the insane variables at play.


Go play Remnant 2 instead if you are interested in this game. PLEASE. Trust me. Unless you have no standards and don't ever perceive the 'bad' in game design when you play.\t\t\t\t\t

The combat is alright, but the bosses are all very dull. They mostly resort to swarming you with adds, and several of them will kill you in 1-2 hits.

It makes for a frustrating experience and culminates in the final boss which is the worst offender. With the wrong loadout, it is borderline impossible and because of how the upgrade system works you would have to farm for hours to try scaled up alternate weapons.

Skip this and go buy Remnant 2, which refines and fixes virtually every problem this game has. It's more varied, more fun, and far better balanced.\t\t\t\t\t
