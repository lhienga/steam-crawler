\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely leave game reviews, but this game is incredible. Just snagged it on the summer sale and worth every penny. I can't believe I waited this long to play it, but with the sequel coming out I wanted to give it a shot.

My favorite thing about this game is nothing is overly complicated, all of it is "just enough"..

-Character customization... enough to feel like your guy is unique but not excessively complicated.
-Gearing... rewarding but not so confusing that you need a calculator to make a decision.
-Maps... big enough to feel like there's things to explore but still linear enough to not feel confused at any time.
-Gameplay... . It's a souls game with guns. I'm ~3 big boss fights down and the last one caused me to pause the game and come write this review.

I haven't even played with other people yet and I am loving it, just doing the campaign. There's "adventure" and "survival" modes too. Pretty pumped on this purchase.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am a 45 yo father, probably one of the oldest people playing this game. I am a single father to my Son, who is 14 now. My son got this game for Passover in 2022 from his uncle, so we installed it on his computer and he started playing. By the end of the week he had 24 hours on this game. This was horrible for me, as it was already hard for me to find ways to spend time with my son, as he is always out with his friends or just watching YouTube. So i decided to make a Steam account and get this game to see if I could maybe play alongside him. I loaded into the game, picked my character and world and started playing but I was stuck on what you where supposed to do. I asked my Son for help and he hosted a game for me to join. I loved it as it was the best time I had spent with my Son since my wife had died. This game has ever since brought me and my son closer again and now we actually spend time together outside the house together as well. This game reminded me that there's fun to be had in everything, and it has brought both me and my Son many happy memories.\t\t\t\t\t
