\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery pleasantly surprised, a hidden gem. I haven't heard much about this game until one of my friend played this game. It is very addictive due to replayability factor, random generated maps, events, bosses and items to acquire.

The third-person looter shooter and soul-like gameplay are very well designed on this one. Regardless of the difficulty, frustrations is there all the time due to very challenging enemies and bosses. Grinding is very easy, unlike other souls-like games, if you die, you die along with all of your looted items and exp, not on this game. If you die, you'll re-spawn back to the latest check point and you get to keep everything you've looted with exp. and no penalties, monsters will re-spawn aswell.

Co-op multiplayer is so much fun, the good thing about co-op is the loot progess. It is shared to all players. One thing to take note about co-op, enemy damage and health will scale depends on how many friends you have invited or joined the game.

And last but not the least, about the story. It is not that great, at some point you will find that the story is out of the line and too many questionable aspect but it's alright. That is the only downside of this game. Remnant 2 is coming out this July so, don't miss Remnant from the Ashes to give it a try then get ready for the next one.

Rating: 8/10\t\t\t\t\t

The Random world gen is cool for going back to previous worlds to unlock the items you want. For me, it manages to perfectly tread the line between being tedious and fun. I also like how they handled the gun aspect. Usually anything with guns I'm immediately using mouse and keyboard. For this game I actually preferred to use controller. If you like looter shooter or souls/ rogue-like games this game is definitely not one to skip.\t\t\t\t\t
