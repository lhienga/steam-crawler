\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dig the idea but the execution leaves a lot to be desired. Endless mob spawns during a boss fight already scream bad design crutch. Mix that with tight corridors, clunky movement, and very slow, deliberate character animations, and it just doesn't gel. If you're going to have mob collision on you can't box players in. Sprinting away to gain distance to heal/reload/reposition flummoxed by multiple mobs ghost spawning out of sight, knowing exactly where you are, and charging in to block you in and explode just isn't what I'd call a good time.

If they spawned at clear health intervals, if the boss personally summoned them, if they didn't psychically know *exactly* where I am at all times and chase me down like a stripper wearing a Snickers bikini, if they could be reliably evaded -- any single one of these would mitigate the issue, plus a dozen other very easy fixes.

I don't hate it, but it doesn't give me confidence that it'll be time well spent.

If this sort of thing doesn't bother you definitely check this game out. There's some cool stuff here.

Maybe they'll fix it in the upcoming sequel. I won't know, because this convinced to take that right the hell off my wishlist.\t\t\t\t\t
