\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has to be one of the most mind-numbing games I have ever played. Gunplay was alright, but it didn't really matter since the core gameplay does not change at all as you "progress". New weapons and armor are extremely rare to come across unless you go out of your way to look for them or get lucky. The maps are pre-generated using cells so lots of the environments lack any detail and remains stale. There was a point where the same cell showed up until my friend and I finished the level and I swear my brain was melting. These cells are also generated upon starting the campaign so you cannot even farm certain equipment if you are unlucky with your campaign and have to restart to unlock those items. All enemies really boil down to either charge with melee or spam projectile and only look a bit different. Bosses are simply designed and are often just variants of the minibosses or overworld enemies. Every single boss has an ads phase without, with the end boss even having it as a core mechanic. The traits are not anything cool either, as they are just stat increases like crit chance or revive speed which progress at such a slow rate, that if you do not invest multiple points into them they feel negligible. The story tries to take itself too seriously, but really just comes across as silly. I normally can see the positive of most games, but I genuinely do not understand how people enjoy this game, aside from just using this game to shoot the ♥♥♥♥ with their friends.\t\t\t\t\t
