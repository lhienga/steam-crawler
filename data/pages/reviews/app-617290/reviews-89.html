\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile typically labelled "Dark Souls with Guns", Remnant: From the Ashes is honestly entirely its own beast. It actually leans more towards looter shooter than anything. The gunplay is super satisfying and the aesthetics are top-tier in my opinion. Enemies and weapons are varied enough to where it doesn't get boring. various playstyles are accommodated (melee, shooting, summons, "magic", etc.) and builds are simple enough to make as they are mostly just dependent on gear. Multiplayer is expectedly a blast, although sorting games by ping would have been appreciated (hopefully in Remnant 2 fingers crossed). The art direction is great, and the enemy designs are beautiful.

Remnant: From the Ashes is a solid recommend to anyone who needs their shooter itch scratched. It's a great time to get into it too because Remnant 2 is around the corner.\t\t\t\t\t
