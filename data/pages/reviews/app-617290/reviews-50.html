\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start by saying I've already bought Remnant 2, and most of my complaints in this game are addressed in the second game. I got this game because I was interested in Remnant 2, but I didn't want to be lost in the story so I figured I'd play through this one before.

I actually picked this game up originally for free through the Game Pass, but didn't really spend too much time playing it. I think not long after that it was removed from the Game Pass. Ultimately, this game is good, but I wouldn't go as far as to say its great. I'd give it a 7/10. I'm also not much of a Souls-like fan - I play them if they're accessible to me. This game really is perfect for people that want a challenge but also don't want to die on the same boss 15+ times. 2-3 good runs and you generally figure out what you need to do, that doesn't mean its "easy" but combat feels very rewarding and when I did die, I never felt like it was something beyond my control.

Like most souls-like games I encounter, the movement mechanics can feel a little clunky, but for the genre this game is actually really good comparatively. I absolutely love the gunplay in this game, feels very smooth on either Keyboard or Controller.

I know a few games from Gunfire, and I don't remember the graphics looking so "dated". The game was released in 2019 and honestly, I would have expected it to look a little nicer. The textures are kinda basic, some things look cool but some worlds can feel a little empty-looking.

The UI is not the greatest, the mini-map is like trying to read blueprint schematics and even on a 4k monitor its really small and hard to see the details of what corridors lead to where.

The leveling system is ok. It's pretty simple. Knowing what I know now about Remnant 2, it makes this one look a lot worse in my opinion.

Those are the only real complaints I have and they honestly don't detract from the game as a whole. The game is fun and Im having a really good time playing it. It's great at keeping my attention. I recommend this game for sure, but if you are thinking about getting Remnant 2 and you don't care about the story - that game is just objectively better in almost every way.\t\t\t\t\t
