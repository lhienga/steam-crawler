\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDue to the release of remnant 2 being only a month away I wanted to write a review for its predecessor.

Remnant for the Ashes is a Action Shooter Rpg Rogue-like. With my 33 Hours on record I have successfully leveled myself up to the degree that I no longer fear fighting bosses. I have leveled my perks and gear to near max and have re-rolled my campaign about 3 times.
On the start remnant feels a lot like a "souls-like" Action Shooter. The class option is deceiving unimportant with only the starting equipment a perks being changed. The game play was responsive however the movement always felt a little slow and clunky.
Running through worlds felt engaging with constant exp gain and level ups keeping a sense of progression. Fighting enemies was Very fun. Building your character correctly to Steam through a horde of enemies was satisfying enough to keep me hooked throughout. Finding new gear and upgrading it was massively rewarding and worth the experimentation.

The Story of Remnant is its largest flaw. I rarely understood or cared to know what was going on. The characters in the game are significantly un-chatty, Leaving the story empty and just a formality for running through a town and mowing down the inhabitants.

In All If you are looking for a fund FPS RPG Shooter with Friends I would highly recommend this. Keep your eyes peeled for Remnant 2\t\t\t\t\t
