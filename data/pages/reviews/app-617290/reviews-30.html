\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good Dark Souls genre game. This is more of a shooter though, especially early on. You can acquire new weapons and armor and upgrade them as you go along. You also earn traits and level those up. You pick what you want to concentrate on depending on your playstyle. There are lots of different weapons you can acquire, and you can equip one pistol and one rifle. You also have one melee weapon.
The game is set up to run on UWS and its very nice. No mods needed! The graphics are quite good IMO and the sound is great also. It had very few bugs and glitches and overall ran very well.
I played on normal difficulty and some of the bosses are pretty tough, but if you co-op with someone you can beat them without too much trouble. One boss took us about 10 tries I think because he kept knocking us off a bridge! There are optional bosses as well, and those are extremely hard to beat. One boss is nearly impossible, but you dont have to fight her to win the game.
Controls are good using xbox controller. The game has no block ability, and so you roll to avoid getting hit. There is a stamina bar that will deplete, but you can train some traits to make that last longer.
Great Souls like game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game with various mechanics and play-styles. I strongly recommend you checking the devs answers on Reddit on how the scaling works before starting the game!

Another tip for the new players, don't listen the guides. They will tell you something like 'the main Earth both is +5, so lvl your equipment to it'. Man, I got to +6 only for the final both during my Normal game and I DIDN'T UPGRADE ONCE (except the traits, of course) during the whole Hard re-roll.

The only guides you can check is a boss mechanics. So far, I had to check 3 bosses for all my three game walk through. I simply missed some small details, which you can find by yourself.

Now, when I re-rolled my world to the Nightmare difficulty I do upgrade a lot. But still, I am +10 in the third world. Nightmare and Apocalypses difficulty is no joke even for my character, so please don't start with it. The game is pretty challenging itself, so don't play a tough guy.

Interesting story, various enemies, fun to re-roll once you finish it. Basically everything in this game is well done and interesting to experience. I really love that I can't use the same weapon for different enemies. Sometimes you need a full auto with bullets, sometimes you need a beam of radiation, and sometimes you need a single shot shocking rifle.

You also not limited to the class you chose. You can take any gun, any armor and any traits after you choose the class. Class only gives you the first equipment.\t\t\t\t\t
