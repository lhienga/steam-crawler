Repetitive combat & levels... like super-repetitive.

Wonky leveling system that's too spread out.

Truly awful Bosses... like the only difficulty is the fact that there are ALWAYS regular enemies from the area in the Boss room... which means there aren't really any cool/fun Boss mechanics to learn like in a proper 'souls-like' game... they're mostly just tedious & annoying... but in the end most are pretty easy.
