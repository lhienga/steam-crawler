\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I will start off by saying, that I outright ignored this game at the beginning. The gray filled color schemes of the early screenshots and the chunky armor designs just didn't hit for me. Being someone who gets grabbed by aesthetics and visuals first and foremost, I put it on my ignore list and moved on.

So imagine my shock when I see a trailer for Remnant II and realize it's the sequel to From The Ashes. And this trailer showed worlds exploding with color and life and adventure and featuring planar travel, a theme I didn't know the game centered around. A theme I don't see all that often and actually love wholeheartedly.

Knowing the new game wouldn't be out for a bit, I picked up From the Ashes and dove in and then felt sadness, because I realized I was getting into this experience right at the tail end of it's life-cycle. Not nearly as many players to explore with, so I pushed through on my own with three different characters.

Even at only 24 hours of play before moving to the sequel, I was stunned. Diverse environments, tight shooter controls and intense battles, as well as bizarre alien planescapes that nonetheless felt vaguely familiar. Influences ranging from Fallout, to Alien, to Hellraiser, to Black Sun and more, all of them somehow both tight and linear and claustrophobic, yet expansive and beckoning you to explore all at once.

The game has it's shortcomings, like any other. Charms and upgrades felt really narrow and a bit too simple, NPC models sometimes looked a bit too recycled and yeah, this game is best played with friends and if you don't have anyone else join you, it can be a lonely and difficult struggle to the next checkpoint, but I can also see how much love was put into this game and it's setting and I'm really glad it I picked it up.

Just to say too, the sequel improves on pretty much everything and I'm playing that now, but if you're looking for a focused, fun and unique adventuring experience, both this and Remnant II are great titles and well worth exploring. (My only complaints about the sequel so far are that it's got some spawning glitches and it's graphically more intense, so I can't run it on max settings like this one), but again, definitely worth giving From the Ashes and Remnant II a go!\t\t\t\t\t
