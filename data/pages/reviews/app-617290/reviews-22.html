\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRemnant 1 is one of the few 'souls-like' titles that I've hopped on to play for minutes, just to check the time and realize that hours had passed by instead. My initial expectations have been met, although there is only one complaint that i have on the surface of the game design. And its boss fights having some extra AI enemies helping them out. So, if you don't prioritize them, you will likely die. But in a one on one scenario, its both easy and hard. If you've played any of the dark souls series or elden ring, you'll probably be decent at dodging. This game is a 8/10 for me, and bordering on 9. Plenty of content, and the combat is fun.\t\t\t\t\t
