\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRemnant is one of those games I always wanted to get into. With the sequel just around the corner I figured now was as good a time as any, so I jumped in with a friend - and while the game started out a little slow, awkward.. perhaps clunky? What I found was one of the better attempts at the 'souls-like' genre.

At first the lack of build paths seemed concerning, and I found myself struggling to find any decent loot. What I didn't realise was that the game was meant to be played through multiple times. Each play-through will differ slightly - the path you take through each world may look similar, but the dungeons and bosses/events surrounding you will change. You'll encounter different stages which will offer their own unique loot, your traits list will expand as you play, and your builds will open up.

I went on a roughly 3 week journey with this game, playing mostly with a friend, but also playing solo at points. I came out the other side with another 100% achievements gathered, and an excitement for the sequel that I didn't think I'd gain during those early few hours of play. Definitely a fun and worthwhile purchase if you're into shooters, looters, and, simply put, having your balls stepped on.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game since release, First on PS4 then on steam. To this day, this is one of the best modern games to keep having me come back and play it. The game was viewed by most as a souls like game, in a way it definitely is. The game play you could say matches dark souls, but its wildly different games. The game puts you in an unforgiving map with bosses that cant just be killed by going around them ,it takes constant action to kill the boss and not die in an instant. The melee is fun but basically unnecessary as you tend to get swarmed quickly when trying to melee. There is three starting classes but it truly doesn't matter as any class can use any weapon, you can buy the other starting class mods too. Its not a typical looter shooter in a sense that you just do a mission and get random loot. This game has a set amount of weapons and armor, and its by finding and defeating the boss of that level to get the armor or weapon material. Sounds simple but this game does a pretty unique thing where the map is randomly generated, so the layout and paths are changed from your last play through. This is the true randomization of when and how you'll get loot. Some play troughs, you will not see a certain npc and will have to reroll the world. There are game modes like adventure, which allow you to just farm bosses and side quests, and survival mode. there's plenty to do and with friends you will have a lot of fun.\t\t\t\t\t
