\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is like Resident Evil (shoulder aim switching) but has Dark souls gunplay (roll).

Campaign without farming is 8/10 cause of the co-op, shared loot, etc.

Finding armors, weapons, weapon mods, traits, etc. is a 4/10 because of the RNG grindfest.

I gotta start with the CONS:

- Grindfest if you are looking for basic armor sets, specific traits and specific weapon mods. Why? RNG of course, randomly generated worlds that need to proc specific events/places. You have to reroll, reroll, reroll and reroll until what you are after spawns.

- Puzzles you can't figure out on your own unless you look up a guide online. Armor sets and such are locked in puzzles or RNG. Don't think you can change your look even after visiting the next world.

- Huge beautiful environments but there is no point in exploring them since most are empty, you think you are going to find secret stuff? Nah man you only find a set amount of items like: trait book or a random item the spawns in that world (if you picked it up before it becomes money automatically). The beautiful huge environments are misused and I feel very bad for the 3D artists. Basically the environment feels empty. Even Fortnite Zero Build has better environments with NPCs and loot.

- You don't know where to go next, `Find Ford's Trail` you have to go to the next area, go back, next area, go back. There is a lot of annoying back tracking.

- Cosmetic change of the same armor (just a different color/texture) needs a specific resource called Glowing Fragment which is VERY hard to farm. Good thing there is workaround on the PC :D I feel bad for console players.

PROS:

- Shared loot, you don't have to race to the item to get it first.

- Up to 3 players co-op and it's such a fun adventure since all of you can die and feel the danger of enemies.

- You can go to the DLC worlds even if you have the base game but you can't get DLC specific items, hop into multiplayer to find out.

- Enjoy the Campaign but don't enjoy hunting for armors, items, weapons, bosses, etc. That RNG is very bad.

Hopefully the cons get addressed in Remnant 2.\t\t\t\t\t
