\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverrated and I can't call it Souls-like. Just different vibes. It's just has dodge roll, boss hp bar and boss' phases, which other games far before Souls games had. Has almost nothing to do with the genre.

Narrative is ♥♥♥♥. It's just gibberish. NPC open mouths and start to spit out random words. You can make nothing out of it. Who would've thought lore from equipment would be much better.

It's not (very) difficult game. World is pretty empty and uninteresting to explore, but here and there several beautiful sceneries to be enjoyed.
Bosses also have some nice animations, but again, challenge-wise nothing close to Souls games. Almost all bosses have one same recipe - adds swarm. And accordingly the strat is the same - kill adds, shoot the boss and it's weakspots, repeat.

I didn't buy any DLC, you can check Mixed reviews on your own and in vanilla version there's almost no armor. We've completed the game and had maybe up to 5 armor sets, 3 of which for sure wasn't suitable for the class (game style) you play. Situation a bit better with boss weapons, but unfortunately you needed to somewhere grind the resource to be able to buy them all, because same resource is being used for upgrades. I've got most weapons, as always some may be fun, but not enough for your purposes. Low amount of "class weapons". I've played with Sniper Rifles and there's maybe 4 of the in the all game. It has 20-30-40 times less gear than the Souls games.

I was baited in this project with all the Souls talk and expected good amount of content. This game is pretty short ~15 hours. For gameplay (not story) oriented game it's nothing.

This project need so many QoL features. Maybe they've made work on mistake in the next game, but I'm not really interested. Maybe someday at -75% :D

It's okay-ish game, just don't buy it at full price and don't expect it to be Souls-like. Wish I could've give mixed review, because that's exactly how I feel.\t\t\t\t\t
