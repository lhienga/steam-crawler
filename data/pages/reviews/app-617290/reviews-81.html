In Dark Souls series bosses and their arenas were thoroughly designed, almost each boss had it's own unique moveset and fights felt hard and fair, and victories felt well-deserved. It was achieved with unique one-on-one fights (mostly).

In this game every boss fight I encountered so far was plain ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. You get boss alright, but you also get endless swarms of fragile enemies at the same time, they constantly respawn. It looks like developers didn't care for design, they just put uncomplicated bosses in a copypasted rooms and make it difficult with big numbers.
It's frustrating when played solo. I hope that sequel will be more enjoyable in that aspect.

Game is playable though and good enough to spend some evenings in it, which is rare for souls-like genre.\t\t\t\t\t
