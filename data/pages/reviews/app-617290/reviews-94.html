\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinished base content.
Don't mind the plot. You don't play this for the plot.
Recommended as an action game.
Not recommended as a soulslike.

Since guns are the main focus of this game, boss design has to revolve around that. Most bosses therefore have trash mobs spawning left and right so they can drop ammo. If you take away trash mobs, boss design is exposed as weak and too easy. The thing is there's already ammo boxes that you can consume to replenish ammo and consuming that would be enough to add to the hectic nature of boss battles without throwing trash mobs.
I heard that this was a common complaint among the playerbase and the sequel is said to address this so that's good.

World design is below average. It feels like a sequence of linear Skyrim dungeons linked by loading screens than a world once lived in and decayed. I can compliment the details and art direction but the map layout of the randomly generated corridors doesn't make the game's world feel engaging or concrete, another trait Souls games have excelled at.

Item progression relies on your playstyle. You don't drop a weapon/armor because a better one dropped. It's all about upgrading and what feels good to the player. Some players might prefer semi-automatic fire, some may prefer fully automatic, etc. and trying out weapon mods that make the weapon feel better is up to you but you have to unlock those via boss fights.
Armor sets have their own bonuses and you can synergize that with your gun build.

Amazing art direction.
Great gunplay. Guns feel and sound impactful. Really. The animation of staggering enemies further enforce that.

Co-op is fun and great especially with friends but there are problems:
1. You don't see the latency so joining random hosts is a dice roll
2. Boss fights might be considered spoilers because the host has progressed farther than you but you don't really play this game for the plot...
3. Friendly fire can be used to grief players
4. Cutscenes only play for one player so while that's happening the rest can use that time to heal each other. It's funny.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNo clue how this game is regarded so fondly. It's a buggy mess with bad boss fights. And a lot of weird traits to put points into which are clearly never gonna be usefull make it seem like they were out of ideas to come up with... Like increasing vaulting speed? Why?

I do like the general concept though, and the gunplay is rather satisfying. Although i'd have liked bosses to drop weapons themselves instead of weirdly named items just to go through an extra crafting step for no reason. I dislike those "professional game reviewers" so much for always slapping games with like an 8/10 when it's clearly a buggy product that hasnt been play tested properly. I'd say this game is a 5.5/10 at best, NOT AT ALL worth the 40 euros (whered they get the guts to ask that amount of money!?), but can be quite fun with some friends and a drink.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like souls like games and or like shooters then this game is for you.

This game is awesome in co-op, and pretty good solo as well. That being said... please do not play this game on the first difficulty or even the second unless you're a very casual gamer. The game will be over very fast if you play on one of the first two difficulties because there will be very little of a challenge. The last difficulty will be too hard for most people and will just drive you crazy unless you really like to grind then go for it. The third difficulty, Nightmare, is what I would personally recommend for most people because it by no means is easy but doesn't make u grind as much as the hardest difficulty will make you do.

This game has replayability because you cannot beat all the bosses in 1 play-through because the game will only give you a set amount each campaign run and if you want to find all the content the game has you have to reroll the campaign or do adventure runs to find the other bosses and side-quests that you didn't find the first time.

Oh and if this game ends up being too easy for you on the max difficulty there is a mod out there that makes it even HARDER! :)

- Side note, don't mind the low amount of hours on my profile, I played this game for many many hours on epic games before it came out on steam.\t\t\t\t\t
