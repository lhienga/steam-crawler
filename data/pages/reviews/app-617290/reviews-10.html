\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPurchased this near its release after playing Dark Souls for the first time and was on a kick. Really didn't like it at first but standards were out of whack at the time. A few months before Remnant 2 came out with a ton of hype I picked this up again and played the crap out of it. Parts of the game are a mess. The story is a weird hot mess and doesn't really make much sense when it exists, the graphics while fine are a bit too cartoony for the tone the game is trying to set.

BUT THE GAMEPLAY IS BEYOND. This is the definitive Gun-souls and Co-Op-Souls game. Controls are tight, weapons are cool. The adventure mode is a really interesting and cool take on things.

TBH I have a number of hours in Remnant 2 and so far while Remnant 2 is amazing and expands on the first game, so far I actually like Remnant 1 better. Will update when I have more hours in Remnant 2 though.\t\t\t\t\t
