\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTownscaper is a fantastic and beautiful game, with relaxing gameplay and good looking, comic-style graphics, which can be perfectly used to let your creativity run free and relax while letting time pass.
After initially buying it, I just wanted to try it out and without me knowing half an hour was already over...
And after not playing it for over a year I just got back to it and it still got me for over 20 Minutes, after which I decided to write this review. Even though I don't have like 500h of playtime, it is still a great game to kill a few minutes and calm down.
I definitely recommend it to people who want to do something senseless whilst creating something beautiful.
Regarding the price: 5,99 € is more than fair and gets you this great chill experience!
Performance wise it should run just fine on literally everything, including the SteamDeck.

But I have some suggestions for improvement & feature requests (not only from me, I took some inspiration from other reviews):
  • It would be nice, to have more building types, like Bridges or Shops
  • Also a cool feature would be the possibility - in addition to the automated decision - to be able to choose which type of building topping is used, like roof structures, rooftop terraces, lighthouses, towers, etc..
  • Humans walking around would also add a nice touch to the game, because this way the towns / structures would be more alive (besides the birds).
  • Also multiple brush tools would be a nice addition, for example to arrange houses in shapes, recolour houses, etc.
  • For nice screenshots, alternative backgrounds would also be very cool.
  • For more building customisation different roof types would make room for new possibilities.
  • As an alternative to the normal camera view, I saw the idea for a 1st person view of one of the humans walking around your town, which you also can control, to go around the build town.

All in all it is a wonderful game to just chill out...
Definitely recommended!\t\t\t\t\t
