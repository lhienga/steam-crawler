Townscaper

Really fun! I've enjoyed my short time with it. I likely won't return to it again, as I feel it's a little too straight-forward & limited for my creativity; I'd love to be able to build boats, and flags, and plantations, and benches, and lamps... and those minute details to truly elevate one's desired vision of a town-scape. Yet I can tell that's not the envisioned design for the game itself.
Nevertheless, it's still really fun to goof around in for short while. Very chill, very relaxing.
It's truly enjoyable.
The sound-design is fantastic as well, the sounds are so adorable, smooth, and bubbly. Plus I adore how you can see birds fly around & land on your buildings; it makes it feel truly within the world, as the world interacts with it. Very cool, would love more of that.



Conclusion

I'd utmost recommend this to nearly anyone, as I can basically see all kinds of gamers enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTownscrapper is a great toy. The macro building effects like hooks, and gardens, and light houses, and flags are a lot of fun to build something fun. The micro details like windows, and support pipes, and benches, and tables, and looking glasses are superb. It's a fun toy to make a few little towns.

There are some opportunities, why is there not a fill bucket? If I make an outline I want to immediately fill the center. Why is there not a paint brush? Do I always have to destroy and rebuild? Click+Drag and delete would be great.

The real fun is making a game with the toy. My brother, co-op co-pilot in all games, and I made challenges. Make a university, a holy place, a wizard's tower, an obstacle course, "build something that represents resilience!" the winner was whoever made the best building.

This toy would absolutely up it's game if it became an actual game. Combining this with something like Totally Accurate Battle Simulator (TABS) where players could make an arena then have their pawns fight each other? Or incorporating elements of a tower defense game. Making sniper towers, or enemies behind corners? This toy could do so much. Defending a flying island from enemies? Yes please. Triggering a tsunami? or earthquake? or a fire?!

Townscraper is a fantastic building block but only a building block thus far. It's a good toy and has so much potential to become a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
