\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLong story short: it's just virtual plain LEGO bricks, not an actual "PC game".

It doesn't contain features to increase the repeatability, increase progressively the experience along the time, and the worse: do in PC what you can't in the reality.

It's all the same houses all the time, with an exciting mechanic about having minor variations depending of how you build the blocks, however poor explored because there's only a dozen of such variations (eg. a lighthouse, garden, ...) , so you have to rely 100% in your imagination (just like plain LEGO bricks).

That being said, I still give it a positive review for the idea and execution (both visuals and audio are great), but I'd just buy it in a sale. For a full price I'd expect this game progressively receiving updates, and increasing the experience with more options to be "live" and actual fun PC game.\t\t\t\t\t
