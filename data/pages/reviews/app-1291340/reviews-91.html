I would have to agree with the majority on this one. This "game" is just a massive waste of potential. I thought I'd come back to this a few months later to see some kind of improvement. Alas, it is more of the same... Without any kind of objective, any gamer or HUMAN for that matter would be out of their mind to spend any longer than 1-3 sessions playing this... If you are looking to be creative, go pick up a paint brush or a drawing pencil before you waste too much time in this...

I respect the dev for holding their ground on what they obviously want this to be, but it just looks like a tease to anyone who would seriously endorse this. Hard pass until there is a point to this little time-waster. If I want a therapeutic art-application, I'll go somewhere that has objectives and some sort of reward system.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSteam said players like me love this game and it was right! I would wish for a few more "comfort" features and maybe a color that doesnt get turned into houses but instead stays "mountain" or "grass", but otherwise its so relaxing and once you start to build towards the sky, its essentially only the size of the brush that stops you from creating a monumental hive city in the best 40k manner, places that dont ever see the light of the day and high rising spires (maybe not exactly what this was meant for and it looks like a beautiful town with tall "coastal town houses", but nevertheless - for me it works even for Hives!).

- exporting your town into an "obj"-file, although I lack the skill to really use it, I made my town blow up in Blender - I imagine people who are into this can create amazing flights through cities
- sharing your creations with a clipboard code

I would like stuff like naming certain buildings, but honestly - for 5 Euro this is one of the best products I have ever bought on Steam, I highly recommend it if you are into casual clicking - the underlying algorithm is doing a very very good job at making it look so much more than just that. Or you try to build a Hive, because why not.\t\t\t\t\t
