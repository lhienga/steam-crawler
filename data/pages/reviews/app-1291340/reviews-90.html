\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this game is very fun. The simple aspect of just following your imagination is something that I will forever find amazing, the game although being basic with many simple actions like clicking to remove and add blocks is very fun.

I don't tend to write reviews but I think this game deserves to be given a good rating. Now there are things I do find to be a bit annoying like having to place every block by yourself and the grid not being strait I do find that this adds to the charm much like playing with LEGO.

If I were to suggest some design elements to be added to this game I would say allow for a flat plane to be selected so that players could build in straight lines, NPC's walking around or doing simple actions to make the world feel more alive, a bit of a larger music selection, the ability to add decorations to your world like trees, ships, walls or even little shops for the NPC's to use, a multi selection tool in order to make placing/removing blocks faster and easier and possibly the ability to add color gradients in order to make more colorful buildings.

would I recommend this game?
YES, it has great potential and is fun to play if you want to relax or blow off some steam and in my opinion is worth the price you pay for it.\t\t\t\t\t
