Unfortunately, the game makes my head and eyes hurt and strain. Something about the FOV, the almost fisheye effect, and the warped grid makes my brain think a building that's more a parallelogram shape is a rectangle at an odd angle, when I already know the angle, so it fights against itself. Weirdly enough my eyes also felt like throwing up.. just my eyes, yes.

But all in all I'd say give it a try for a bit - you can always refund if it's uncomfortable to play, and if it isn't, you've gained yourself a relaxing and simple town builder.\t\t\t\t\t
