\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen enjoyed as intended, Townscaper is an effortless stress-reliever and an easy buy at this price point.

However, it could be so much more. Many players (including myself) see the immensely useful applications Townscaper has for map creation, worldbuilding, and even role-playing. Unfortunately, the game's intentionally minimal interface only allows you to build in the confines of a mid-20th century coastal town. What if there was an option for a grass base instead of water? Or medieval buildings? Or different types of blocks that created unique features like shop signs and display windows? What if the rooftops could be weathered and old? or covered with snow? The possibilities are infinite.

This game's community is bursting with ideas on how to improve and expand the game, and I dearly hope the developers don't let these ideas go unheeded, because this game is truly a gem.\t\t\t\t\t
