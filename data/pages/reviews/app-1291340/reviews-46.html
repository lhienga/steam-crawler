I, myself am not a very creative person, so I usually end up being a little bit frustrated, but not with this game. It is very relaxing to place your houses/towers and other tiles and think about what might look good.

A perfect game for everyone that wants to have a relaxing game and escape reality.\t\t\t\t\t
