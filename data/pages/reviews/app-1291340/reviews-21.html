\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat is Townscaper?

Well, Townscaper is more of a creative tool than a game, I would say. This app lets you build gorgeous and serene town, Mediterranean style(French? Italian? Something like that). I like it, but at the moment it's very barebone, just a bunch of blocks that you can slap together to create quirky architecture. If you like that, then go ahead. Unfortunately, it bored me out after the first 2 hours.
Additions I can recommend: people(I want to see humans living in their buildings), animals(the Mediterranean without cats and dogs running around, no seagulls flying overhead, no fishes leaping out of water in the distance: is not the Mediterranean at all), different kind of businesses(at least add a fish market and a dock, you know, the game is literally surrounded by water).
Otherwise, it's literally a tool. Maybe if you're an architecture looking for ideas, sure. I'm a general Joe, so i'm not much immersed in it.\t\t\t\t\t
