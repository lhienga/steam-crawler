\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs you sit back on your chair, feeling relaxed after a hard day's work or school, you wonder which game should you play? One that uses the least amount of brain power but gives some enjoyment value...that game could be Townscaper!

Townscaper is literally a colourful indie game which allows you to build settlements on islands in the middle of the sea. It's like the old movie Waterworld but with more colours and no defence. You can have any shapes or sizes of your islands. Just be creative. Remember to relax and turn on some soft music!

Please don't expect a lot of functions because it is a simple game, developed by one person. I like this game because the graphics is nice and colourful, and it helps me relax in-between other games. No achievements, no stress. For the price during sale, I think it's worth trying it out, if you like simple-building games. Please help support the developer.

Note: This game is also available in AppStore and Google Play, which means it's a great game to entice your kids to create and build with colours.
