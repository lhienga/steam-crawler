- Very laid back game play
- Simple building mechanics
- Pleasing range of colors
- Serene graphics
- Phenomenal sound design

Negatives:
- Not a lot of depth
- Not much angular fluidity

Opinion:
Townscaper is an exceptional game if you just want to sit back and relax, building up a quaint coastal European town filled with pastel houses and grassy courtyards. And it is absent of all the frustrating or complex city building mechanics in Cities: Skylines or others such as traffic flow, zoning, or citizen needs. You can instead simply let your imagination run wild as you construct marvelous island towns. Truly a one-of-a-kind game that I 100% Recommend.
