Thumbs up, but my not my cup of tea
Is this a good game. No. It isn't a game (There are no challenges, timers, etc. )
Is it at least good in the respect that it entertains or inspires somewhat? yes

It is a creative landscape builder, and unless you are actually in the mood to build without challenge, then this is for you.
Especially for all you Lego builders out there
I like building, but not Lego, I am more RTS or Base Building strategy
That being said I got 20mins into this before I was bored.

The only thing I found annoying about this Builder is that you cannot remap the mouse buttons, (you can with keyboard) not with the mouse, and I am use to moving around with the opposite buttons ie from graphic design and function the other. Something I wish I could reverse on the mouse setup... but cannot.
I see the appeal and cuteness of this "Game" but honestly i have 3d Design home programs more interesting than this.

I will give it a thumbs up simply because, there is nothing clearly wrong with the game. It is just I personally do not feel like it. And it isn't my thing\t\t\t\t\t
