Despite what the devs might think, adding different colors to a single building type doesn't add nearly enough customization. Additionally, the tedious process of placing tiles one at a time, contrary to popular belief, is not relaxing but rather a chore. Even IF you spend two hours getting the tiles exactly how you want, the town is almost completely lifeless without any people walking or interacting. Lastly, your creativity is severely limited by the middle-of-the-ocean backdrop.

As it stands, Minecraft or even Roblox are better "town-building toys". Not worth $5.99, would only recommend if it was free or at most $1.99.
