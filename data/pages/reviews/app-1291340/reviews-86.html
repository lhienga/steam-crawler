\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs Oskar (the developer) stated: this is a toy, not a game. It's a toy where you click click click to create a block, then a little tiny house, then a two story house, then a tower by clicking on the same spot. There are conditions when you click that will create a slightly different building, so if you create a 6x6 "wall" (of houses) and you destroy the middle block at the bottom, that will create a ark. Yes, that's it. How long this will last it's up to you, but I'm quite confident that even the most relaxed player enjoying to build a town one house at a time will soon get bored because, once you've find out about the different behaviour you get by using different colours, or the "flying" cities, or about gardens, the discovery is pretty much over. While this game/toy explicitly has no real purpose other than be visually pleasant, It lacks of some advanced techniques to make it last longer: you cannot chose anything else than the colour. They could at least add the ability to chose the type of the building, perhaps from just 3 options to keep its simplicity, like house, church or palace. Or maybe chose a prebuilt building. Or textures. Or at least to fill a f** circle with blocks without having to create one by one. I mean, something to give just a bit more control. It even has no music. Since the developer clearly stated that the development is basically over, this game is pretty much dead and there will be no further addition, it will likely be just this very limited clickclickclick forever. Someone has started to add mods (multiplayer and "first person" walk) but that requires to install a loader and a bunch of other files so that's not available, for example, on mobile. But it shows it would be simple for the devs to include that and much more. Some more effort from the developer, which is apparently surprised his game (toy) is selling so well, would be really appreciated, and it would definitely change this review to a thumbs-up because the game on its own deserves it, but in this moment it seems to me just a dead project\t\t\t\t\t
