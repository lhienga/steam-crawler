\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNeat little game. I enjoy city builders and zen games, but this might be an example of taking it too far. There is enough content to last about 30 minutes. The game is charming because of its simplicity, but it is also severely limited by it. I bought this game as an adult, because I like this sort of game, but this is really more like a toddler ipad game. The game lacks any refined building control, and there is no variety really from game-to-game. Every instance of the game is essentially the same. Design and color options for the buildings are very limited, and there is only one "type" of building. Because of the random shape of the tiles, its difficult to create a design with any sort of plan or intention. This is more of a "click and see what happens" experience than a game.\t\t\t\t\t
