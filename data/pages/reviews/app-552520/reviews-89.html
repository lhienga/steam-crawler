Far Cry 5 for me is like a movie in which, no matter how strange it may sound, I played.
Wonderful outstanding photorealistic graphics. The wonderful atmosphere of America is conveyed in the game. A fairly ordinary plot, as it seems at first, but then...
I came across interesting and fun side quests. Large selection of weapons and their upgrades. A large, beautiful open world where you will immerse yourself in an atmosphere of constant action and joy.
I played this game myself and enjoyed the roleplay, but I think it would be more interesting and fun in co-op.
Now IMPORTANT, Far Cry 5 itself is not enough to get the final pleasure. I advise you to buy a pack of two games at once: Far Cry 5 and Far Cry New Dawn. This way the game will be more interesting and cool. (In Far Cry New Dawn the plot of the Far Cry 5 is literally continues in the same place) Then it can be called a whole complete game.
