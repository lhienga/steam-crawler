\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinished the game review:
TL;DR 5/10 game at best. Tries to ape FC3 formula badly and fails. The story is meh at best and fails at multiple basic storytelling concepts. Unoriginal and unsatisfying with Not really worth a buy even on a low sale.

You'd be better off playing 3 or even 4 (again if you already did) FC5 desperately tries to bring back the most popular moments of 3 without any understanding that it was variety and exploring something new that made the game interesting and enjoyable. Do you remember the memorable moment of Vaas kidnapping you and having the crazy monologue? Well in FC5 we've repeated the same thing 9 times, isn't that great? No it's not, you're getting kidnapped more often than Louis Lane and April O'Neal combined and it gets old really quickly. And there's very little you can do about the railroad of the story. Ever heard of player agency? Well this game's devs didn't. And let's stop silent protagonist is artistic choice, no it's just easy way of saving some extra money on writing and voice acting.

Mechanics are okay, your usual FC stuff, some weird difficulty spikes and some ways of cheesing almost everything. Half of the weapons feel pretty underwhelming and the mayhem gets old pretty quickly and your main motivation to get through will likely be the story. Which is pretentious and failing at understand why FC3 was good. It tries to be smart and deep but instead it ends with depth of a emo teenager's first attempts at poetry. It's about as smart as if I told you there's prophecy that you'll get punched in the face, then punched you in the face and then acted incredibly smug about being right. But don't worry nothing you do matters at all so you'll finish the game with feeling that you just spent 20+ hours doing nothing.

FC5 probably could've been good game if the devs were given some freedom to be creative and make new stories, new gameplay mechanics and write decent stories. Instead we got gaming equivalent of two day old cold leftover pizza because execs will insist of just repeating the same thing over and over.

We know definition of insanity and with new FC games we've also learned the definition of mediocrity, which is repeating over the same formula over until everyone is sick and tired of it.\t\t\t\t\t
