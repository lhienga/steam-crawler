Guns have garbage accuracy and egregious bullet travel time.
NPC AI is dumber than a bag of rocks.
Air planes have some of the worst controls I've ever seen in video games.
There is no obvious indicator which seat you are going to move to when you hit the button to move to another seat in a vehicle, resulting in you getting shot to death in a fire fight.

The developers had MANY games before this to iron out a lot of these kinks, but simply didn't put in the effort. I often find my self feeling immersed, only to get yanked out of said immersion with the punch of a heavy weight boxer because some very stupid little thing happens that completely ruined what I was planning or trying to do with me yelling a visceral "ARE YOU ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ KIDDING ME!?" for a millionth time.

I play a lot of dark souls, I understand the 'git gud' mindset. And I got very good at these games, often playing them on hard right out of the gate because it was so much fun. But then #5 comes along and spits in your face at almost every opportunity. I'll play through and finish the game and come back and update this review if the game manages to redeem itself, but I have gotten very good at seeing the programming on the wall, so I'm doubting much will change.

Edit: I have completed the game. Nothing got better. It all stayed ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.

The devs felt like it would be a cool twist to take away all player choice in a open world game. None of your choices matter, and not in a good way. You will either predict what's going to happen ahead of time and be really disappointed when it happens, OR you will get blinds sided and STILL be disappointed. My ratings listed below should give you and idea of how bad this game is comparison to its previous iterations.

Far Cry 3 8/10
Far Cry 3:Blood Dragon 9/10
Far Cry 4 8/10
Far Cry Primal: 8/10
Far Cry 5 3/10

The game gets 1 for good music, 1 for good graphics, 1 for awesome side stories/characters, but nothing for anything else, because anything else that was good at one point will gets soured by something absolutely, mindbogglingly, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ stupid.

What ever the devs were trying to get at with the over arcing plot of this story just falls flat in every conceivable way. The reason? Pure laziness. Its clear a lot of work went into a lot of this game, but none of it was where it needed to be.

If you are thinking of buying this game on the next sale for 90%, just don't. Go spend that money on a cheap bottle of booze, you will get get 70 trillion times more value out of it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUbisoft Connect



My first thought going from Far Cry 4 to Far Cry 5 was a disappointed "oh".

What a downgrade Far Cry 5 is! I mean the music is stellar, especially the cult music, I liked the villain and his lieutenants, but somehow it just didn't come together. The removal of animal skinning animations is a little example of the things missing from Far Cry 5 that bring it down. The vehicles in this game are a downgrade as they each have much less personality then the rides found in other Far Cry games. This game is by far the most tedious Far Cry yet. Sometimes I would die and respawn, only to have Adelaide get insta-ganked by some peggy with a plane. Sometimes I would fly a plane, die, then respawn FAR away from where I died, but without the plane! Sometimes I wish I didn't start playing Far Cry 5.

I had some fun here, but damn I've got no intention to play any Far Cry after this at all.

Also got to add that the plane controls SUCK. Yaw and roll is the same button and you cannot remap them! Don't bother adding planes if you can't be assed to add proper controls! And one more thing, why have a custom character when all the face options blow? Hell let me just steal a random Peggies face, it'll be better then anything I can "create".\t\t\t\t\t
