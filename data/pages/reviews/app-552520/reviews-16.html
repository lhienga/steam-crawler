\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEh. It's alright.

The bugs are horrendous, the gameplay is fun, the antagonist is FANTASTIC. And I mean fantastic. The game *really* makes you hate that guy and all of his lackeys. It might just be because I'm a left leaning American who lives a lifestyle that gets my life threatened by the religious nutbags fairly often but I've never felt genuine hatred towards a video game antagonist before. Which, I mean, he's the bad guy. You're supposed to want to kill him in games like this.

That said, the gameplay is basic Farcry. Have you played any of the Farcry games since 3? Then you've played this game.

But the bugs! Gods. They're everywhere and they're both hilarious and pathetic. I knew about that going in and I played primarily co-op to make them worse and the experience was utterly hilarious. That being said, it's not in a good way. We're not laughing with it like the Nord Space Program:tm: in Skyrim. We're laughing at it. It's bad. It reeks of Assassin's Creed Unity level Ubisoft garbagio.

TL;DR: Good villain, entertaining gameplay, horrendously buggy mess. I'd pass on this one.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay itself is pretty good. Combat feels slick and the scenery is gorgeous. Clear out a region so you don't have to deal with baddies and just turn it into a walking sim to take in the scenery and explore. Taking helicopter tours of the area is nice and chill as well.

The story, on the other hand, is unsatisfying garbage. You're railroaded into periodic encounters by getting kidnapped with no real way to avoid the instances. So you run around being a one-person army, dodging bullets and sniping out enemies only to take an arrow to the knee on your way to the outhouse so they can force you into a cutscene. It's like your character goes from bada** to dumba** when it's required for the story.

Then you don't even get the satisfaction of truly taking down the villains yourself. I was really looking forward to putting a bullet between each sibling's eyes because they are sooooo effing annoying and you don't even get to do that. You do fight them in indirect ways, but then you get cutscenes where they just sort of fizzle out on their own.

And they monologue soooooo much! Seriously, I wound up skipping cutscenes a lot about halfway through because they just go on and on and on and on and on and on with their insane religious claptrap. If I want to listen to the ramblings of blathering christo-fascist cultists I'll turn on Fox News. I got this game to put the smack down on simulated WCNs, not listen to them drone.

Great scenery and general gameplay but ultimately the story and forced cutscene mechanics make this game fizzle rather than pop.\t\t\t\t\t
