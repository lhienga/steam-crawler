Its kinda fun to experiment with different tactics and weapons to clear out a base of cultists, but the game will punish you for it.
With a boring plot, even more boring side villains and their boring magical powers.
Wasn't it dull that I kept resuing the word "boring" over and over?
I'm merely paying an homage to the plot of the game by resuing the same word.

So you are the Rookie, the fresh agent far down the chain of command, your also the only competent fighter in the entire resistance. Only you are sent out to actually do something, and only you is called whenever something important is happening, you are still however just a rookie. Which people are quick to remind you of.
The Edens Gate cult can by magical drugs incapacitate you, use a musical song to mind control you and also oneshot you with a drug bullet.
You keep getting captured but the only reason you survive is that the cult leader wants you alive, he has forgotten to mention this to the rest of his following that will try to kill you on sight.

I did not finish the game, more than 2/3's in and I got stuck in an unescapable bugged mission that killed the last of my fun in the game.\t\t\t\t\t
