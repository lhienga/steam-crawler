\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou get to be friends with a bear named Cheeseburger and you get to pet the bear.

Real talk, I need to play Primal but this is the only Far Cry game I've enjoyed from beginning to end and into New Game+/replays. Dan Romer's score adds a lot, but there's solid world building and count this as one of those rare Ubisoft games that actually gets the narrative and narrative pacing broadly right.

On top of which there is just so much room to ♥♥♥♥ around while you're at it.

Plus Cheeseburger. He's my special guy.\t\t\t\t\t
