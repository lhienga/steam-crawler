\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFar Cry 5 has one of the most interesting cast of characters in the series and an intriguing story-line that backs it up. Then why am i not recommending it ? Because these good things about Far Cry 5 are buried 6 feet under the most tedious and uninspired gameplay loop i have ever seen.
%90 of the game seems to be filler missions. Fetch missions, escort missions and every annoying mission structures imaginable is constantly thrown at you back to back. Very quickly you will wish to progress through the story-line and avoid these boring tasks but too bad for you, you have to keep doing mundane tasks to fill a bar that lets you play the actual campaign missions.
In the previous Far Cry games, i loved exploring the world. Even Far Cry Primal had points of interests and charm that made exploration fun. Far Cry 5 however has a vast empty open world that takes too long to traverse, has nothing but foliage for miles and its just isn't worth the hassle.,
If Far Cry 5 didn't have a strong enough story backing this horribly sterile gameplay, it would've been the definition of an average game.
I really see no point in playing it. If you are curious about the story, just watch it, you will have a much better time that way.\t\t\t\t\t
