\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started it after a long time without playing any game of the same serie, and..... It made me regret it !
Don't get me wrong, the gameplay is fun, like as fun as most of the good FPS game I played, some features are some good additions (like the companion thing), BUT ... here comes the catch : the story ... It's starting really well, and you quite quickly end up being the marionette of the targeted opponents. I mean that I don't care to lose controls and be abducted sometimes, I can deal with some magical trick as soon as it doesn't give up on logical explanation from the player POV, but with Far Cary 5 it's a real nightmare !! You are told a story that makes non sense, scientifically and even physically speaking ! Drugs ♥♥♥♥ can't hold any explanation please !! Too bad as the starting plot was a great idea, but according to me it has been unpleasant and unrealistic from the first hours to the very last minute ... I read some player actually loved it, being forced and "conditioned" to do things the way it was written, giving them feeling they hadn't felt before, and I would say Far Cry 5 is sooooooo far away from other game that does that perfectly (ie RDR2 ...)
Anyway I guess it depends on the kind of playthrough the gamer like, but with this opus of the Far Cry serie I ended up hating this gross and foolish rendering of the Far Cry usual ADN (at least since the 2nd one...)

PS : Good thing is I played FC Primal after this one and.... way much better, reconciliation is on its way :)\t\t\t\t\t
