- The most beautiful environment I've played in so far
- The sounds of the environment were just as amazing to hear as they were to see
- Plenty to discover and do, but not too much
- The weapons are very satisfying to use
- More vehicles, and you can more easily call in a car or chopper when needed (instead of hunting for one)

- The DLC is very fun so far, maybe a little more fun that the main game...

Cons:
- The base missions feel very generic and don't really challenge you as you progress.
- Although there are more vehicles, they seem to handle worse in this game than in previous ones
- The story is kinda "meh". Some things didn't really connect with me until the end.
- Parts of the story and general dialogue can be a little too disturbing
- Many of the side missions are just boring (go here, shoot this, come back, do it again)
