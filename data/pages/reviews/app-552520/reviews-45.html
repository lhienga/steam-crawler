FC games always look good, and this one continues that trend, with the environments being incredible my only complaint being they are not varied enough. The Story and mainly the voice acting from the main cast is top tier and falls off as you go down the tiers of side characters, again as usual for a FC game. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want a redneck-infested Montana to wander in, hunting and fishing and saving country folk, with an adorable pet dog assisting, this is the game for you.

The first time I played I got stuck early on, wondering what it wanted me to do. There is a lot of freedom here, and that can feel like a lot of nothing if you aren't sure why you're there. There are also strange restrictions on things like how many weapons you can carry. But it appears these systems sort themselves out as you "level up" and build towards what's important to your playstyle. The world also reacts to your actions, which feels like progress. I can see a bit of The Division in this game (or vice versa).

There's definitely some weirdness because it wants to be an MMO, but it isn't. You might die blowing up a silo, and the game will save that you've blown up that silo, like an MMO would in a persistent world. But then it'll respawn you right next to the burned silo and instantly recreate enemies you'd killed, so you die again immediately. It'll also spawn in enemies around you when it feels like that's required, but it's sort of random. Try to do something sneaky, whiff one arrow, and you'll end up with two dozen identical NPCs shooting at you because they're all psychic clones with teleporters. Or, do something sneaky and it will work right away and you'll complete a massive challenge no time so it felt like nothing. It's hard to tell what's coming, either way.

The game is "always online" I guess, and threatens to fill your world with other real life players. But it never has for me. It feels like a single player game with MMO failings and the constant threat of other people without the payoff of other people. At least there's no battle pass?

You can pet the dog. And do, because he deserves it.\t\t\t\t\t
