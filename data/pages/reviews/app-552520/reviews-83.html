\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought this game back in 2020 and just managed to get around to it in 2023. Beat it today.

I rate this game a solid 8/10. It's strongest points are the soundtrack, core game play, side content, and some interesting characters and possibly the best setting in the Far Cry series.

However, the story and some questionable game play choices are ultimately what hold this game back from greatness.

The perks and upgrading system is pretty good. Good variety of guns to customise and upgrade. Buying and selling system is really good and the fishing and hunting is fun enough and will be the main source of your income. The map is littered with a healthy variety of activities to partake in. In summary, most of the traditional Far Cry elements are here for you to play around with.

This game will keep you busy. I took my time and beat the campaign along with some side content in about 28 hours. I have probably another 10-15 hours worth of side content to complete which I may or may not do. The multiplayer is basically dead but there is a solo arcade option which can give you almost endless challenge.

Overall, great game, but not without its flaws. Story could have been better. It's probably worth the price tag of $39.99 at this point in 2024. Been about 6 years since this game came out.\t\t\t\t\t
