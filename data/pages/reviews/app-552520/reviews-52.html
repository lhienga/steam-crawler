\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played a fair amount of open world First person and Third person shooters. This was my first Far Cry game.

Why I purchased this game:
A friend and I were looking for a FPS to play (with Co-op), and this was on sale.

The good:
- Great graphics
- Gun customization
- Good antagonists
- Arcade: Solo with custom maps is a great mode, haven't tried out the Map Editor
- Flying Planes and Helicopters feels great
- Good music

The bad:
- The linear story is fine, but having little control over some actions of the player feels too limiting
- SPOILER: Set interactions with the antagonists after gaining points feels outright stupid
- SPOILER: There is a little variety of enemy NPCs - was starting to become bored after liberating one region
- Co-op has a lot of delay, sometimes the players/NPCs rubberband in Co-op, connection also drops (in spite of having a fine network connection)

I would suggest the reader to purchase this game on sale.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty enjoyable. Has a really good plot, great graphics, well developed characters, and the action is great. Now, I don't know if this is just me, but I feel that this game is a bit too hard, in some spots. Maybe it's just because I simply suck at it, or maybe because I'm not used to it. I raged quite a few times at a few sections, which I feel would be common, and it's been a while since i got pissed while playing games, so perhaps that's why I think so.

Though, the variety is spectacular here, there is so much to do, and sometimes, I just get stuck at what to even do, as there is so much stuff to explore. You can easily play it for 50 hours, and not even get remotely bored. The developers really paid a lot of attention at making the game as good as possible.

Soundtracks, on the other hand, are just so great, I'm even thinking about adding some of the tracks to my playlist, and this stuff ain't common.

The particles, and effects are pretty realistic, and it's wild that this game was made 6 years ago. Character models / Entity models were highly detailed, and the voice acting was top-notch.

UI Designs were pretty solid, and overall, just very well polished.\t\t\t\t\t
