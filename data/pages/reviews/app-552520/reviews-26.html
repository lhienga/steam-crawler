\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(TLDR = Extremely mediocre for $60, 4/10):
Graphics: During the day, everything would be super pretty if the bloom were not so blinding. There is no option to disable or lessen bloom in any way, and it can get so bad you can't see an enemy ahead of you in a clearing if the sun is behind them. During the night, everything is a washed out grey, but at least there isn't any insane bloom.

PvE: The NPCs all feel bland to fight. After you've played ~5 hours, every fight starts to feel the same. All enemy NPCs share a hive mind and go on alert mode even if you assassinate one. The weapon menu is the same way, not good but not bad either.

Story: There are some pretty major plot holes in the story and illogical actions your character takes. If you look past that, the story is just okay. There are some parts of the story where it just forces you somewhere else through a loading screen with no clear reasoning. There's no feeling of connection with the characters because you never see any of them frequently or in-depth enough.

Transportation: To travel the map you can use waypoints, walk, fly, or drive. Driving is very mediocre, I dislike it but on a preference level but it's functional. Walking is only an option for short distances, and is boring. Waypoints are unlocked once you've visited a place, and have no drawbacks. Flying has planes & helicopters is fast and quite fun, helicopters are kind of OP.

Weapons/Store: You use currency you get from playing and use it to buy weapons & vehicles. I only used the store to buy a basic car (which I rarely used), and a helicopter that made most fights trivial. As far as weapons go, they are nicely varied but still generic to what you'd expect.

Audio: Audio is consistent & good at telling you how far away something is. It's intentionally used during story missions to draw your attention at times, which I appreciate.
Perks: You get perk points from completing different challenges, which are pretty fun. The perks are all direct upgrades that feel useful but not OP. Very well done system.
Crafting: Literally didn't use the crafting system once. You get all the ammo & items you could need by looting.
Roster: Roster lets you take some NPCs with you as allies. They tend to just be there to res you. Very bland.\t\t\t\t\t
