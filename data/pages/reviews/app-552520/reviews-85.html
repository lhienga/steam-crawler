- Companion system is great, adds a feeling of "you're not fighting alone"
- Cute and pet-able companion animals
- The visuals look great even for lower graphic settings
- No actual "Good" ending, works well for the kind of story that Ubisoft wanted to tell
- Kinda fun with co-op

Cons:
- John, Jacob, and Faith - who cares about these guys
- You can't visit Jacob's mansion/fortress after defeating him
- The easiest game of my life; play at hard or the game is not challenging at all
- Silencers, which can also be attached to shotguns and and LMGs, make the game even easier
- Special ammo can just be ignored the entire game
- Flamethrower just sucks ass; you're more likely to kill yourself with it than kill enemies
- Lazy and lame boss fight for Faith
- Lazy and repetitive scripted scenes for Jacob
- No replay value
- Not being able to go far from your co-op partner at the Nuclear ending makes the race against the clock lose all sense of urgency
- Not fun playing by yourself\t\t\t\t\t
