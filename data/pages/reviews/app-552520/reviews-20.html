Story -2 of 10
The story is absolute garbage, my gawd it had the most garbage story i have EVER meet in a game or a movie.
This games story makes the so-called movie "Sharknado" look like a masterpiece that should win minimum 4 OSCARS.
My gawd, the writer of this story should NEVER be allowed to EVER come close to a keyboard or pencil EVER again. Handcuff the people who wrote this, and let them work with their feet from now on. Holy hell a trash story..


Graphics 5 of 10
Graphics are okay, i know the game is almost 6 years, but did you see the game Crysis back in 2007? No destruction of houses or trees anymore, Its mediocre

Gameplay 3 of 10
The gunplay is good. The impact the bullets make is great, i love the RPG.
The flying missions was awful, they boat was never needed.

But the biggest problem with the gameplay, if you have done 5 mission, you have pretty much done them all, then its just a huge grind, and this really make the game boring as hell.
But its a typical trashy UBISOFT way to dont give a damn, and only care for the income. That is why i NEVER again will buy a UBISOFT game for more than 50% off sales and then the game need to be good, which again, UBISOFT is not known for to make anymore.
There are NO passion in game developing anymore.

My 3 of 10 is PURELY for the gun play. if the gun play wasnt good, the rating would have been in the negative numbers.

Sounds 4 of 10
Sounds are pretty okay, the voices is annoying as hell, and i must admit i killed every damn kid standing at the boring arcade machines, because they were telling the most GARBAGE lines at every machine. Just awful.

Gun sounds were great.
Cars, planes and choppers just awful.

Controlling 4 of 10
Infantry controls, or should i call it ground control? Anyway, when you walk around the controls are good, nothing to complain about here,
The cars controls okay, not the best, and not the worst.
The planes and helcopters, MY GAWD!! was the programmer here a total console first year developer??
Those controls was just the most awful controls ever. Let me control my plan with my mouse and accelerate with my walk keys. Speed up on space and slow down on control, what the hell were you thinking??

Fun 1 of 10
I had fun the first hour, after that it was just one huge grind and that is NEVER fun. It was a huge let down.

Price 1 of 10
Seriously, even though i only paid 8 euro for this game, i still feel the price was too high, the quality and price do absolutely not match. The quality is low, the price is high, again, typical UBISOFT!

Over all 3 of 10
Far Cry have NOT been a good game since pretty much the first Far Cry, i didnt like Far Cry 2, 3 was okay because of the villain, 4 was trash,.. etc etc.
NOW, i dont get why the hell UBISOFT keeps making us do these STUPID capture the location missions, its soooo boring after 2 or 3, after that its just another grind. And the dumb checkpoints and incidents on the side of the roads are way to many.


Conclusion
This game is NOT fit for a replay, its full of grind and i am so disappointed how UBISOFT have completely fokked their consumers with high prices and very very low quality and absolute ZERO passion have been put in the game.
The "Suitmen" have completely destroyed UBISOFT,

Final Score 3 of 10
I cant give this game more than 3 of 10. There is NO passion or quality in the game. The guys that made the sounds saved it a bit. And the gun play raised the score, but i will not give it more than 3 of 10, its not worth it.
IGN probably gave it 9 of 10, but they dont have passion in their ♥♥♥♥ anymore either, they rank the games depending of how much money the publishers are willing to pay for a good score.
