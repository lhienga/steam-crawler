\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall i had fun and i think its worthy of the far cry series. The arcade mode is a nice addition, so is Co-op.

Graphics are very nice, weapons feel mostly really good. Performance could be better, especially the HDR implementation is lacking. But ill let it slide since its an "older" game. You get a huge map with lots of tasks / outposts / random encounters. Exploring feels a bit less rewarding compared to far cry 3 tho.

Story was okay, some of the boss battles were really goofy or just straight up dumb, especially the one against Joseph. The story is missing a lot of subtlety, most noticeably in the beginning (You essentially have info on the entire map, positions of antagonists and who the are).
What annoyed me most was the story telling "devices". I understand its hard to build a bridge between open world and story but using the same mechanic to force the player into a confrontation with one of the antagonists and then playing most of it as a cutscene is not only immersion breaking but also really comical.

I didnt play coop but you can tell the game really wants you to. The companion AI was mostly doing fine, with some expected jank. The revive animation is annoyingly long and leaves you vulnerable for way too long...

Worst thing was by FAR is the skill and weapon shop system. How they implemented the progression is fine.
Buy why would you remove the signature weapons from FC3 which needed special conditions to unlock and just put them up for sale in the shop (IG currency or real money)?!?! Takes the fun away completely. Also most of the weapons in one class are really useless since they often have the exact same stats.

Lots of the skill are really boring or useless. So is the skill tree layout. You can just get everything from the beginning (given you have the funds), with like a few exceptions. A real step down from the skill system in FC3. The take down mechanic used to be way more complex and layered in skills, now squased into a single perk. That opened up space for 6 individual perks for larger ammo capacity and like 10 for companion cooldown?!?! Really? Really?!?\t\t\t\t\t
