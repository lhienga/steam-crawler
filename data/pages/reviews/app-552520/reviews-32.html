Enemies: They do spawn more and it feels more like a living world.

Hunting: here it is thought as farming money, no crafting objects for increasing your ammo bags etc...

Fishing: ok, nothing more to say. You can play the whole game without doing it.

Drugs mission: I loved in farcry3 and 4 those missions where you used to face a completely different enviroment (shangri-La) and to see some funny stuff. Here there is a kind of it, but it completely lost its irony and funny purpose.

Guns: as in farcry 3 and 4. You can customize them.

Main story:
I did really love it. The main antagonist is very well written and the end is absolutely amazing. In my opinion it seems sometimes to watch a movie.

I prefered farcry 4 and 3 as they are masterpiece, but I do suggest to play this farcry\t\t\t\t\t

1. the regions do not show directly where the control thresholds are like outposts and what not
2. no more radio towers for extra rewards
3. the overhaul of experience points I just think that a more kill to kill based system for the upgrading of perks was a pretty essential thing to making a far cry game good
4. Perks I don't think it suits this game well by making you have to finish the game once and play new game + in order to get more of one skill
5. the "you have been marked" moment, it just kinda annoys me when I'm in the middle of a mission or a side quest and a unbeatable squad either cuts me off from the mission or soft locks me by making me fail the mission over and over due to being hit by the bliss bullets

as for things I personally think are better in this game it has some good parts
1. the graphics, obviously this is a reason better graphics make you feel more immersed in the world you play in
2. weapons I feel like the weapon selection is one of the best in all the far cry games
3. the side quests and other non main missions, I think that all of the side jobs you can do are fun and unique in their own kind of way but this might not be as strong for others and I have enjoyed the more capture outposts feeling of far cry
4. the companion feature, I think that this was a big part of the success of this game and although some of the animations were taken straight from far cry primal I personally love the ability to tame animals and befriend people to fight alongside me in the constant war for the world

overall good game\t\t\t\t\t
