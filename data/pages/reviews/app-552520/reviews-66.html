\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played a few of the other far cry games and its safe to say that this one doesn't quite reach the expectations I had, I got this game as it was on sale but I would have been fuming if I bought it full price. The game is buggy and controller adaptations don't all work. For example there is literally no button for reversing a vehicle and descending an aircraft which is essential for some missions. In order to complete the mission I had to figure out the keyboard controls just to land an aircraft. In addition the missions are mostly all the same thing which makes the game quite boring quickly (go there, kill a bunch of people, free the hostages).\t\t\t\t\t
