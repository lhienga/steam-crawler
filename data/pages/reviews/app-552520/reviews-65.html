The diverse range of activities is what sets Far Cry 5 apart. Whether you're liberating outposts from the menacing cult, casting a line in the serene rivers for some fishing, or stalking wildlife in the lush forests, there's always something exciting to do. The variety in open-world activities adds depth and replayability to the game, making every moment feel fresh and engaging.

Montana serves as a fantastic backdrop for the game, providing a unique and captivating setting. The attention to detail in the environment, from the sprawling landscapes to the small-town charm, adds an extra layer of authenticity to the overall experience. The freedom to explore this open world at your own pace is a testament to the game's design, encouraging players to forge their own path through the chaos.

The charismatic and sometimes eccentric characters you encounter contribute to the immersive storytelling. The blend of intense action, thought-provoking themes, and unexpected twists creates a rollercoaster of emotions that make Far Cry 5 a standout title in the series.

In terms of technical aspects, the game boasts stunning visuals and a well-optimized performance on PC. The cooperative mode seamlessly integrates into the single-player experience, allowing you to share the thrills of the campaign with a friend.

Far Cry 5 is not just a game; it's an experience. Whether you're a fan of the series or a newcomer, the captivating open-world, dynamic gameplay, and cooperative features make it a must-play title. Ubisoft has truly hit the mark with Far Cry 5, delivering an unforgettable journey through the heart of Montana.\t\t\t\t\t

NPC-s aren't ugly like in the new dawn
More outfits than new dawn, but not enough
Enemies are not bullet sponges

Some weapon modifications
