\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI’ve purchased and played every installment of Big Ant’s cricket game series and unfortunately, as much as I wanted to like Cricket 24, it’s plagued with a lot of the same issues we’ve seen since Cricket 19.

This review is a message to the BigAnt team, you can and should do a lot better than this. This game is essentially a repackaged version of 22 with very minor improvements to things like the menus, music, and having licensed teams which are all great additions but when it comes to core gameplay, there’s little to look forward to.

Fielding is atrocious. They might have made improvements but in my first couple of games I had fielders running parallel to the ball, fielders standing outside the boundary rope before the ball even got to them, fielders kicking the ball over the boundary. This is absurd. And again, this was my SECOND match in the game, so if you actually tested the game before release, this should’ve been so easy to catch (no pun intended).

Commentary: it’s ridiculously bad. Hit a shot for 6 “poor shot!”, score 20 runs in an over “batters were kept quiet that over”. Like come on guys, how can your system not check “if runs scored > 6, do not use any low scoring commentary”. Like again, these are just the basics.

Bowling feels arduous, you can hit perfect lines and lengths all day, deliver tons of variations, and it feels like playing to a random number generator where hopefully one of them gets you a wicket, otherwise you’ll be sitting there for 50+ overs or simulating. Batting however, does deserve praise, it’s pretty enjoyable and I do feel punished when I have a lapse in concentration but otherwise in control.

If you’re looking for a fresh game from Cricket 22, this isn’t it. Skip.

For BigAnt, I’m all for you guys having minor regular releases with incremental improvements, but you absolutely cannot pull an EA Games FIFA on us when you haven’t gotten core gameplay out of buggy, just bizarre mistakes mode. If fielding continues to be a joke and the commentary is blatantly wrong, this isn’t even a finished game in my opinion, you’re missing your foundation and basics.

Your success with Don Bradman came by listening to the community and wanting to make the best game out there. Please take your time and do it properly, heck if you need a kickstarter because you’re still an indie studio making the only legitimate cricket game out there, we’re behind you. But please don’t drop releases like this that quite frankly cheat a community when the basics are still very raw. I’m excited for the next proper iteration, but for now, this isn’t it.

My Honest Review for all my Community Folks...

As many of you aware that i requested some features on behalf of the community from Cricket19 to include in Cricket 22 and they did it.

Now about
Cricket 24

Pros
1st things 1st :--
Cricket24 is the most Licensed Cricket Game till now

⭐ Licenses
- 8 IPL Teams ( Delhi Capitals, Mumbai Indians, Rajasthan Royals, Lucknow Super giants, Sunrisers Hyderabad, Kolkata Knight Riders, Gujarat Titans, Punjab Kings )
along with their Official Stadiums.
-International Teams : Australia, England, New Zealand, West Indies, Ireland, Scotland, Oman, Thailand, Russia.
-T20 Leagues : IPL( Indian ), BBL ( Australia ), WBBL, The Hundred (England), CPLT20 ( West Indies ), PSL ( Pakistan ), WCPLT20, Super Smash ( New Zealand ).
🚨 Pakistan Cricket Team & PSL also confirmed license but will be updated Later as per BigAnt's Tweets.

⭐ Graphics are polished and look stunning compared to past title:
-Grass blades are More realistic & Mesmerizing.
-Pitch is more looking lively.
-Sun with light flares give feels like you are in middle of the field.
-Lighting is a huge upgrade in Cricket24 like in afternoon you actually feels like its afternoon.
-Addition of Stadium Night Lights Celebrations same like T20 World Cup 2022 (Australia)
-Awesome Pyrotechnics Upgrades.

⭐ Audio :
-Menu music playlist is good and mood booster.
-Stumps sound which were asked from developers finally here real stump hitting sound from ashes 2023.
-Addition of Adam Gilchrist (male), Alison Mitchel (Female) Commentators with their spot on Commentary is the best part i am enjoying in this Cricket 24.
-Indian Stadia ( Licensed ) Homely Crowd Noise gives authentic Indian Cricket Feel chanting "Ganpati Bappa Morya, Boom Boom Bumrah, Jeetega Bhai jeetaga India Jeetega".
-One of the major upgrade in audio section is ball hitting bat sound is pure, not awkward loud sound now.
🔥Commentary name of KOHLI, KLRAHUL along with major INDIAN SURNAMES like SHUKLA, CHOUDHARY, RANA is now there in database.

⭐ New Additions
- New Batting style number 7 included considered to be of Great Sir Sachin Tendulkar.
- 4 new Bowling style added :
For Pace - Shoaib Akhter, Trent Boult.
For Spin - Muttiah Muralitharan, Harbahajan SIngh.
- Players appearance with licensed players, especially from Licensed IPL teams, resembling their real-life counterparts.
- Pull shots, Backfoot defence mechanics, is too good in C24.
- Fielders are now quicker and more proactive, improvement from Cricket 22, though need more patches to make fielding more realtime looking and not robotic (suggestion)
- Short Cover Fielder is no more "GOD" slight mistime also just land in front of the fielder.
- Frontfoot Defence is lovely Batsmen lean forward to reach to the ball but this should be random (suggestion)
-Various Number of New Cricketing Shots i discovered so far:
-----
>Late Square Cut.
>Sachin's Classical Straight Drive.
>Joe Root's New Ramp Shot ( Reverse Scoop shot ).
>Cover Drives & Slogging over covers ( Similar to that of Surya Kumar Yadav's ).
-----
-Player Creator Now have More Sets Of New Hairstyle Customization.
-Players New Entering Cutscenes as per the Format You are playing in.
-Players do Huddle before starting the match.

------
⭐
Cons
will Edit once the feature gets available with patches

-Players still overlap, with runners sometimes phasing through bowlers.
-Frequent glitches with runners can lead to undeserved run-outs. At times the runner in place of completing the run, turned around went half way and then turned again to complete the run.
-Shot power is high simple drives leading to boundries although i made my custom difficulty to make it more harder to play which feels good during career mode also.
-In Pro Cam Mode Grill graphics are broken & when entering the Ground fixed picture is shown not any movement of head as per the player.
-When Fielder takes a catch camera goes inside player body see through which is awkward.
-No forward diving catches, Slip Catches still not carrying comfortably to fielders.
-Post Match Winning Celebrations are all same as of Cricket22.
-Player playing no look shots.
-Fielders exhibit strange behaviors, like unnecessary throws and odd shimmies.
-The roster for team India in nets practice lacks recognizable names.
-Gameplay on ‘Hard’ difficulty feels easier compared to Cricket 22.
-No Rain⛈ still.
-No Covers coming up Cutscenes.
-No Dynamic Flags in Crowd.
-No New Celebrations for milestones for Batsman.

------
⭐
Edit
-Only 3 New Celebrations for Batsman seen so far (M.S. Dhoni, Iftikar Ahmed & Ben Stokes)
-With Latest Patch Update Dated (24-Nov-2023), Bowlers have New Wicket Celebration.
