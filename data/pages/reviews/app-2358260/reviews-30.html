\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was eagerly anticipating this game, but the initial release has been disappointing so far.

Negatives:

Fielding has regressed significantly; players don't respond well to the ball, and catching windows appear for catches that aren't intended for the players. This was supposed to be a major selling point, but it's taken a step back.
Players sometimes appear idle and get run out while just standing around.
The Career mode consistently crashes after only one game.
The 100 draft mode crashes every time I finish the draft.
There are numerous bugs in the game, and it's surprising that it was released without a "beta" version for testing.
Positives:


The addition of licensed teams and competitions is a welcome addition.
The user interface has a sleek and appealing design.
The grass, gameplay, and graphics are visually pleasing.
There may be more positives, but I haven't been able to experience them due to the career mode and Hundred draft crashes.

Desired Features:

I would love to see the opportunity to complete a full cricket year in a 30-year career mode, where you can witness the entire cricket world in action, not just as a player but also managing a county team and England. This would be similar to what's available in other sports games.
It's frustrating that you can't view any in-depth statistics at the end of the game, such as 4s and 6s per player or wagon wheels. Having access to detailed stats would greatly enhance the gaming experience.
I hope the developers prioritize resolving these issues with the game in future updates.


Overall, while there are some positive aspects, the current state of the game is marred by significant issues and bugs that need addressing.\t\t\t\t\t
