\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs the Cricketing season buzzes with excitement, Big Ant Studio presents its latest offering, Cricket 24. This game, while thrilling and detailed, stands under the looming shadows of its predecessor, Cricket 22, echoing many of its strengths and weaknesses.

Having crafted four predecessors, Big Ant Studio’s approach towards creating a cricket game is seasoned. Their dedication to delivering a game that’s engaging and lasts a considerable amount of time is commendable. Unlike many sports game adaptations which require players to find joy amidst obvious flaws, Cricket 24 offers a smoother experience.

* Game Features and Licenses:-
Cricket 24 unfolds as a vast game encompassing a plethora of features. From globally renowned tournaments like The
Ashes to the rapid T20 leagues, the game has it all. This edition also steps into the colossal Indian cricket market with
some, albeit not all, IPL teams. The availability of character and team creation tools allows players to bring their
imagination to life.

* Career Mode – A Step Back?:-
Cricket 22 introduced players to an intriguing career mode teeming with narrative elements and training minigames.
Cricket 24, rather disappointingly, has chosen to trim this feature rather than expand it, creating a void for players who
loved the narrative gameplay.

*On-Field Gameplay:-
The on-field gameplay is a testament to Big Ant Studio’s expertise. Batting stands out as a joyous experience, allowing
players to choose between arcade and simulation modes. Bowling, although detailed, loses its charm due to a sense of
detachment from the real-life spontaneity of the sport. The AI, particularly while bowling, often makes the gameplay feel
predetermined, stripping it of the excitement that real cricket offers. This makes matches against AI less challenging and
somewhat predictable, contrary to playing with a real opponent.

*Visuals and Aesthetics:-
One of the standout features of Cricket 24 is its visual representation. From the intricate player models, thanks to the
photogrammetry process, to the expansive stadiums, the game offers a visual treat. However, the movement of players
lacks the natural fluidity observed in real-life athletes. Despite this, the overall visuals, including the player kits and
broadcast-quality camera angles, enhance the gaming experience.

*MY IN GAME EXPERIENCE REGARDING CRICKET 24:-
As far as gameplay goes, Cricket 24 is great fun. With different bowling and batting styles to entertain, it has a level of
depth that will appeal to both newcomers and existing fans of the series.
Cricket 24 has some flaws and bugs that needs to be fixed especially in the Fielding department and will be fixed as the
developers are constantly listening to the Cricket Community giving patches which is improving the gameplay day by day
making it more flexible and quite enjoyable too...

POSITIVES:-
* Control options to suit newcomers and experienced players
* International licensing including men’s and women’s leagues
* Licensed player appearances and kit attention to detail

NEGATIVES:-
* Some new gameplay mechanics are not fully polished
* The game can be prone to occasional bugs
* The presentation is somewhat lacking in my opinion

FINAL NOTE:-
Cricket 24, while commendable in its vastness and core gameplay, seems to have missed the mark in capturing the essence of a full-fledged cricket match.Big Ant Studios are a team known for their dedication to constant improvement, and we may see these things fixed through patches and updates in coming weeks, months.As Big Ant Studios continues its journey, there is hope that they will dive deeper into the nuances of cricket, bringing to life the ebbs and flows of the game that make it so revered globally. Until then, Cricket 24 offers a fun, immersive experience, even if it doesn’t bowl a perfect yorker.

Last But not the least I would like to thank specially Kapcha42 and CP (Community Pro) for recommending this great game and Hoping that future patches and updates will make the game more versatile and enjoyable...Thank You Big Ant Studios for these masterpiece of a game hope you guys will not stop developing Cricket simulation game in future times.

My Rating Regarding Cricket 24:- 7.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

1. Graphics: Amazing graphics, licensed players look real. Animations are sharp.
2. Gameplay : Gameplay feels a lot more smoother than before. Fielding has improved. Fielders are more proactive. More shots have been added to batting. And pretty refined overall.
3. Licensed Players/Staduims
4. Some aspects of AI are good : while chasing big totals the AI tries to hit hard in the slog overs and tries to hit 4s and 6s.

Areas where the game needs improvement (If anyone know how to reach out to the developers of the game for user feedback kindly let me know)

1. AI NEEDS IMPROVEMENT : When I am batting and hitting boudaries, I expect that the opposition will make changes in the field and bowl on the areas where it will make it difficult for me to hit boundaries.
2. FIELDING NEEDS IMPROVEMENT: Like I meantioned above fielding is way better that cricket 22 but still there is scope for improvement. The wicket keeper dives uneccesarily while collecting fielders throw. And overall it can be imrpoved slightly.
3. SPINNERS ARE VERY EASY TO PLAY : The game should make it more difficult to play spinners.
4. Some animations can be added for sledging or bowler and batsmen eye contact.
5. Post match presentation animations can be added.
6. Need ned post wicket and post winning celebrations.


Overall it is a very promising game and I would recommend it. Hope that Big Ant studios improves on some of the aspects of the game im future patches to make this one of the best cricket games ever.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCricket 24: A Masterpiece Unveiled in 2023

Cricket enthusiasts, your long-awaited dream has come true with Cricket 24. This game, announced months ago, has finally arrived, and it's a true game-changer. Let's dive into why this game is an absolute must-buy.

The Menu and UI: Cricket 24's new menu sound, background music, and UI are a visual treat. It's a promising start, and we can anticipate more exciting changes down the line.

New Commentary Package: The addition of fresh commentary lines is a game-changer. It's a massive upgrade that transforms the gaming experience, and Gillie's commentary is a standout.

IPL Inclusion: The inclusion of IPL with all eight major teams is a monumental step. This alone makes Cricket 24 a significant upgrade. Those who clamored for IPL in the game are getting their wish granted.

Licensed International Teams: Pakistan and PSL are now part of the game, a testament to the developer's massive efforts. More nations are on the horizon, promising an even broader cricketing experience.

Visual Brilliance: Cricket 24's visuals are top-notch. Player models are sharper, grass is more vibrant, and the ball physics are incredibly realistic. The game truly brings the stadiums to life.

Lighting Effects: Day and night matches are finely tuned for a lifelike experience. If you have an OLED TV, the game's magic is even more pronounced.

Player Motions: Player movements are more realistic and fluid, immersing you in the action. The attention to detail in player motions is now becomes hallmark of BA's games.

New and Improved Shots: Two new batting styles and a host of new shots within existing styles are a delightful surprise. Style #7 is a particular favorite, adding to the game's allure.


Here's why this game is a must-buy for users who still plays Cricket 19 :

——————************———————

Prepare to be blown away by the wealth of new features in Cricket 24. This game has raised the bar with innovative additions that redefine the cricket gaming experience. Let's explore these thrilling enhancements:

New Batting Style: Introducing the seventh batting style, a tribute to the legendary Sir Sachin Tendulkar. This style is a game-changer, allowing players to emulate the master himself.

Revamped Bowling Styles: Cricket 24 unveils four new bowling styles, featuring the blistering pace of Shoaib Akhtar and Trent Boult for the speedsters, and the magical spin of Muttiah Muralitharan and the crafty Harbhajan Singh for the spinners. These bowling styles offer a fresh and challenging dimension to the game.

Licensed Player Appearances: Expect lifelike player avatars, particularly from the IPL teams, where players bear a striking resemblance to their real-life counterparts. It's an impressive level of detail that enhances the immersive experience.

Dynamic Batting Moves: The game has perfected pull shots and backfoot defense, providing players with even more control over their shots. Hitting boundaries or defending skillfully has never felt more satisfying.
Responsive Fielders: Fielders in Cricket 24 are more agile and proactive than ever before, a substantial improvement from Cricket 22. However, there's room for further enhancements in future updates to make fielding even more realistic and less robotic.

Balanced Fielding: Gone are the days of the "GOD-like" short cover fielder. Now, even a slight mistimed shot may find its way in front of the fielder, adding an element of unpredictability.

Frontfoot Defensive Mastery: Batsmen showcase beautiful frontfoot defense moves, leaning forward to reach the ball. This provides a more authentic cricketing experience, although introducing a random element to this skill is a thoughtful suggestion.

Expanded Arsenal of Shots: Cricket 24 introduces a variety of new cricketing shots, including the late Square Cut, Sachin's Classical Straight Drive, Joe Root's innovative Ramp Shot (Reverse Scoop shot), and a range of Cover Drives and Slogging shots, similar to those of Surya Kumar Yadav. These shots add depth and excitement to your gameplay.


Enhanced Player Customization: The Player Creator feature now offers an array of new hairstyle customizations, ensuring that your in-game avatars truly represent your unique style.

Immersive Cutscenes: Experience the thrill of players entering the field with cutscenes that match the format you're playing. It's a small but impactful touch that adds to the realism.

Pre-match Huddle: Witness players huddling up before the start of the match, a detail that adds authenticity to the game's atmosphere and showcases the team spirit of cricket.

In conclusion, Cricket 24 isn't just a game; it's a love letter to cricket fans. It's the embodiment of dedication, innovation, and authenticity, and it promises to deliver an unmatched cricketing experience. If you're a cricket enthusiast, this game is more than a purchase; it's an investment in the love and passion you hold for the sport. Get ready to be captivated, challenged, and thrilled like never before with Cricket 24.

Note - Let me be clear, this is not a paid review. I am a grown man, employed with a respected organization - BlackRock. My assessment of Cricket 24 is entirely honest and intended for those who, like me, have dedicated a significant amount of their time to playing BA games since DBC14.

The development team is actively listening to the community, considering suggestions and improving fielding realism. This commitment to feedback ensures that the game keeps evolving. Future looks good.\t\t\t\t\t
