\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeing Dutch and therefor probably 1 of the very few people that plays this game in my country, the lack of Dutch players and the Netherlands not being in the T20 World Cup mode despite them being in the last one and the upcoming one is a big fail for me.

There are also some BIG issues with how players behave. When the batsman hits the ball straight ahead the bowler never tries to stop the ball even when it rolls right by him. When the batsman hits the wicket on the other side it's not given as an out. Batsmen will swing at the air in the completely wrong place. Short balls are impossible to hit because the batsman will never raise his bat high enough no matter what shot you are telling him to make.

Taking wickets seems to be completely at random and has nothing to do with how well you bowl or what bowling style you use. Fielders will let high balls bounce before they grab them even when they are right underneath the ball and it should be an easy wicket. Fielders also start running well after the ball has been struck so they usually are too late to stop a ball from getting to the boundary. Fielders also have no clue where the boundary is and will grab the ball and then casually walk over the boundary with it. There doesn't seem to be any animations for fielders to throw a ball back before they step over the rope.
The commentary is repetitive and at times completely wrong.
Run outs are impossible when you are fielding. The fielder will throw the ball way too slow to actually hit the wicket in time or even worse, will throw the ball at some random fielder somewhere else entirely. I have played for over 20 hours now and had ZERO run outs.
I also had Steve Smith going out because he stepped over the line well after the ball had been caught by the WK and you have 0 input possibilities to make him stay behind the line. I even yelled: WHAT ARE YOU DOING and he just walked away from the wicket and was run out.
LBW is never called by the umpire unless you review it.

Now there are positives. The customization is extensive in Career mode. Kits, gear, name and logo of your team, player names, everything can be made as you want it. I obviously chose to play in the Dutch league where random Dutch cities suddenly have cricket teams they don't have in real life and they even play first class cricket which is completely absurd but I can forgive that. The fact that the Netherlands is even in this game should be noted as a plus. The number of licensed teams, players and competitions is pretty good but the IPL being not fully licensed would be a problem for a lot of Indian cricket fans no doubt. Visually the game also looks pretty good and a lot of the big players are instantly recognizable. The umpires could need some work. I feel like some of them are just reused looks from random players.

There is a lot to like here and I had some genuinely exciting moments in the game but cricket is all about rules and when the game doesn't follow these, it becomes a major problem. I don't understand how you can make a cricket game and not be absolutely sure that all aspects of the game are correct. For all its flaws, EA at least doesn't make a football game where a defender shoots the ball over the sideline and his team is given a throw in. That would be inexcusable. So is a cricket game where the wicket has fallen and the players just keep running and the WK is trying to knock the invisible stumps. I feel like a lot of these flaws are not even that difficult to fix. This sport deserves better and I WILL change my review if Big Ant actually fixes these things.\t\t\t\t\t
