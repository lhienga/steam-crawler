\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish I could say this game is worth your money and that it's finally the cricket game we all want, but it's not even close.
TLDR: Pretty much no improvement in any areas over prior games, and some areas that are worse, ends up being a slog sometimes, not worth your money, if you want to play a cricket game get one of the prior ones.

GOOD:

Graphics seem improved although not much, the bowling feels a bit better but has a lot of issues which I'll mention later. More teams is always good and the amount of facial scans they have was a pleasant surprise, especially how many English counties have been done.

BAD:

As per usual with big ant the fielding is janky and feels awful, players will still take absolute blinders, before then proceeding to consistently forget the ball is going towards them and just letting roll past them.
The bowling still feels way too random set ups rarely seem to actually affect the batter, and batters will frequently feel like the best players in world even though they're a number 10, plus even when they get out it makes no sense, I've had more batters outside edge in swingers than out swingers, as well a had more batters beaten on the inside edge by out swingers than in swingers, it makes no sense. Pace feels like it has no affect, the AI treats a ball bowled at 80mph the same as one at 98mph, it makes being a fast bowler feel boring.

To add insult to injury, the only good part of the previous games, the batting, is worse, sweep shots (using arcade batting) don't work half the time instead your player will play some weird clip to leg-side where they played sweeps in the previous game. Playing spin is no awkward as hell because sometimes your player will block to leg-side when you aim in to off if the ball turned quite a bit, missing the ball and getting you bowled.

Commentary is bad still, just this time the dialogue rarely even matched what just happened in-game, and on the topic of sound, in my opinion the bats sound worse than in prior games, that one is just a personal one.

And last but not least, player ratings, the default ratings for most players feel completely wrong, with test batters having 25 defence, this results in some international players not even making the first class teams, which just makes the teams you play against at that level fell really generic and dull.

If you really want to play a cricket game, save your money and get one of the older ones they are just as good if not better.\t\t\t\t\t
