The physics of the game also leave much to be desired. The ball seems to fly off just by touching the bat, which doesnâ€™t align with the real-world dynamics of cricket.

The audience presence in the game is disappointingly sparse, which affects the overall atmosphere and excitement typically associated with a cricket match.
