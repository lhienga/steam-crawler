A Promising Innings with Room to Improve
Rating : ★★★★☆

Exploring the World of Cricket 24 as a Passionate Fan
As someone who is deeply passionate about cricket, I recently decided to immerse myself in the exciting world of Cricket 24. Let me tell you, the experience was absolutely thrilling! This game has everything a cricket fan could ask for, making it the perfect choice for enthusiasts like myself.

Visual Aspects of Cricket Game
The cricket game offers an immersive experience with its reasonably detailed player models (although the complete model is not as detailed as the face, it still does the job well) and realistic stadium graphics. Although the animations may not be groundbreaking, they still enhance the game's authenticity. Additionally, the game's visual experience is elevated by the presence of daylight and weather variations, adding a touch of dynamism.

Intuitive Gameplay Mechanics for All Levels of Players
The gameplay mechanics are intuitive, making it easy for newcomers to grasp the basics while providing enough depth for seasoned players to strategize and implement effective game plans.
Suggestion for Improving Mechanics in Pro Controls: Spin Bowling
One area that could benefit from some attention in pro controls is spin bowling, while aftertouch is available in other control methods; Length adjustment is missing which was present in C19.

The Joy of Test Matches
Indulging in test matches is like a stress-relieving meditation session - just with a bit of added excitement! Playing your favorite sports game, and having real-lifelike players, teams and stats! With a massive roster of current international players, it feels like I’m a part of the team!

A Great Game with Room for Improvement
However, no game is without its flaws, and Cricket 24 is no exception. While the gameplay is generally smooth, there are occasional glitches that can disrupt the flow of the match. Specifically fielding AI that could use a bit of refinement. These issues, while not game-breaking, do take away from the overall polish of the experience.
Despite these areas for improvement, I must commend the developers for their ongoing commitment to the game. Regular updates and patches have addressed some of the initial concerns, demonstrating a dedication to refining and enhancing the overall gaming experience. In the grand scheme of things, it appears that their efforts are aligned towards creating an unparalleled experience for their beloved fans in the coming years.

Few suggestions to the development team for the future of cricket gaming experience
  • Competitions & Tournaments: Individual match still feels an independent game, adding more context in match of being in a tournament will be awesome; e.g. Added tournament stats in between games, rankings of players and standing of the team.
  • While ball physics are awesome, player model physics can get more details which will improve the overall experience.
  • Apart from injury by fatigue, ball impact injuries and accidental injury can be included; Also in tournaments & competition injured player management can be a great addition.
  • More team management mechanics & team stability can give a nice touch in the tournaments and career.

Final Thoughts on Cricket 24
Cricket 24 is a remarkable addition to the virtual cricket world, offering an immersive and genuine experience for cricket fans. By continuing to incorporate user feedback and prioritizing support, there is no doubt that this game has the potential to become the ultimate cricket gaming experience. I am eager to see how the developers will continue to enhance and polish this promising game in future updates.\t\t\t\t\t
