Sims 4 has removed one of my favorite features from 3, and that is Custom Worlds as well as being able to place lots where-ever, personal nitpick though as the worlds in TS4 look pretty good.
Of course there is more, Custom Swatches is gone. Even if this was cut in favor of performance, there are so many workarounds to this, even if there are a decent selection of pre-existing colors that you can choose for each separate piece i.e. independently color the sheets and frame of a bed. I question the game performance aspect because the PAINT TOOL exsists on pets, werewolves, ect...
And to tie it all together, you don't really have as much movement freedom going around the world. What happens is you have 1 active lot at a time and you may only move around the pre-determined neighborhood (1/3 of the current world). Doing what sims 3 might have had a performance impact for sure, but either allow seamless lot transitions in an active neighborhood or just open up the world to wander if you so choose.

TS4 has some good qualities it can be commended for.
Moods and Multitasking make the game quite dynamic, giving sims more liveliness in everyday life, much needed change.
The game runs smooth as far as I am concerned, the older games are much harder to run and were never optimized well to be honest. Still think some much needed features like custom swatches/color wheel can be afforded.
Most aspects of the game look good and were designed well for what I feel the sims is. This can vary between people but the cleaner style is quite the step up from TS3's honestly hard to look at visuals.

DLC... Only good thing I can say is the content adds some decent variety, if it were a reasonable price. TS4 recently went free, but that does NOT excuse the NUMEROUS $40 expansion packs $20 game packs and $10 stuff packs. Oh that's not enough, they decided to add $5 of useless BS that is the kits?? If EA had just bundled many of the features from between the several packs like TS3 they MIGHT be worth their price, let me list a few examples;
City Living + Get Famous
High School Years + Discover University
Cats & Dogs + Horse Ranch + My First Pet
Werewolves + Vampires + Realm of Magic + Paranormal
Growing Together + Get Together + Parenthood + The various kid/toddler stuff packs
Honestly some features should have been in the base game from the start, Especially core features such as Seasons, the Generations type packs, and even the more generic packs, which most packs are especially as you get lower into Game and Stuff packs. Bare-bones features like Lunar Cycles, infants and more get added in a free update, but they have near zero uses without their corresponding packs. The packs all together are about $1,000 off sale (Please do not pay full price for these packs). There is no use in saying boycotting them will do anything because EA has several egregious money making methods. This series is fun, there just needs to be a bigger uproar to stir up EA...\t\t\t\t\t
