I haven't played very many Sims prior to this game (I played one of them on the Nintendo 3DS a couple years back for a short time), but I definitely knew this was my type of game. I love controlling every little aspect of their lives, and with tons of clothing, personality, and action choices to choose from, that certainly vibes great with me.

I can't say I know what I'm missing with all of those DLCs I've never thought about purchasing. Other than the most obvious (for me) being the worlds that they can live in. Only starting off with a measly three worlds seems more than a little money-grabby when there are, I think, about ten locked in the uber-expensive DLCs. Other than that, I don't really feel like I'm missing much. I feel like they would be great for people who really love the Sims and wouldn't mind laying down more than a little bit of money, but I think the base game works for me for what I wanted to do from the beginning. It's just a silly little game that I don't mind wasting my time on.

7/10\t\t\t\t\t
