\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really don't know how to describe this game; I never played one like it on PC before, and only played the original Fate/Extella on Switch for a little while before I had to stop playing the Switch permanently due to recurring tendonitis. (Getting old is hard for a gamer; not recommended, not that you have a choice...)

Anyway, it's a really fun action game, especially if you are a fan of the Fate series of Anime, which of course I am. You control one of the Fate characters (my preference is one of the strong female leads, like Saber) against a veritable horde of enemies. Most of these are really just cannon fodder for the other side, and you can wipe them aside casually to build up your kill meter and power for strong moves. There are a few "real" enemies in each room, which are harder, and occasionally a real "sub-boss" that is a challenge. After defeating these "sub-bosses" in the various rooms, you eventually get to one (or occasionally two) real boss fights.

There are RPG elements, in that your characters can be upgraded and such, but the game lives for these action segments. Furthermore, the action segments have tactical and strategic decisions - which rooms you go to, and in what order, and how you avail yourselves of possible powerups or treasure options.

Overall, I enjoy this game, and although it does get a little repetitive at times, the Fate setting keeps the interest level high.
