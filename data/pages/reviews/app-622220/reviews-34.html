The game seems to be endless which thoroughly justifies the price in my eyes. But not only because of that. When you finished the story you can unlock several more extra battles and constantly level up your servants.

The only thing I don't like about the game; when playing a chapter of the main story that you did already play, you can't skip the text in one go. You have to skip through the whole of it again and again which can be quite of a bother.

But all in all I can say this game is one of my favorites so far.
