\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo im revisiting this after it has gone dormant for multiple years now, the playerbase is long dead and gone, and us "blessed few" remain here amongst the game's faithful. Let me break this review up into 3 parts: the singleplayer experience, the multiplayer experience, and the content load.

-single player experience: if you played the extra or extella line of games the story telling method should be very familiar and the plot should come to you relatively easy. If you played extella, it basically picks up from altera's arc where she gets sent back to "larva altera". you hack and slash through npc enemies and big bads along the way to claim her back after she was kidnapped by a new figure trying to rule over the moon and youre assisted by what could only be described as their "counter part". i will not spoil the story but you meet plenty of chars along the way, pick whoever you like and play as them, progress them, build them with special stats, skills, etc. and then just beam through the story and unlock everyone. the story is alright. not amazing, not terrible. to be point blank the story is entirely carried by the characters they brought in. Astolfo, Gilgamesh, Scatach, and Zerkalot are staple characters in the fate ip bringing a lot of character fans to the game for the chance to play as their favorite char but i digress. the story is a little lacking and did not pack the same emotional weight as games like the first extra, CCC, or extella but it was serviceable and the gameplay was passable.

-multiplayer experience: o ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ boy, as an avid multiplayer enjoyer i will not sugar coat it, the game feels like ass. for how fast paced the game wants to be the camera is p slow, the comboes feel chunky and clunky with not much room to improvise, everything kills, and the amount of variety in your code options is basically "take the heal and damage one" and nothing else. the game has these pylons that give your noble phantasm gauge (you can only get noble phantasm from this) that is tied directly to the class of char you are playing. there is one assassin in the game so if you have li, then youre going to be the only one able to claim unlike every other class that has at least 2 in it (yes even ruler). the game is king of the hill style where you hold a hardpoint on the map and get points or rather you deplete the opponent's points (idk why this is but whatever) and both sides compete for ownership. it becomes readily apparent how outmatched one side is verse the other from a character and player standpoint almost immediately after first engagement. i myself have had matches where playing lancelot ive managed to hunting dog knight 3 people into a juggle combo off rip and win a game before it really got started. unfortunately, for those trying to play multiplayer these days, youll more than likely see only bot matches and never get the full experience unless this game revives like jesus.

-content load: okay so for a while there were costume dlc being made for the game which initially gave i and many others the impression more was coming to the game in the form of characters, paid dlc map packs, etc. and that we may get balance updates. nope. what you see in the costumes is still available afaik and does give you unique 2d sprites that come on screen when you use NP, overcharge, etc. but aside from that, they do nothing. for those coming from fgo, no, bikini scatach does not make her an assassin. yes, im dissapointed too. still the dlc is nice and if you enjoy attractive anime chars, by all means. i will stop talking about dlc and talk about the actual CONTENT LOAD of the game. it is a p repetitive hack and slasher in the context of its single player gameplay but does offer a good amount of content to placate. in terms of multiplayer, it has a really nice amount of variance and content that when played with a full lobby of people can lead to really funny and wacky moments (mostly breaking the game but i digress) and can be a really great and wholesome experience despite its aformentioned short comings.

My final verdict on this game: unless youre here for the characters or here for the multiplayer, youre not gonna play this longer than 100 hours without fiddling yourself beyond that point. unless a playerbase comes back like someone waking up out of a coma, there is no point in playing this game other than saying you did. If the devs see this, I hope youre doing well, and if youre going to make another game like this, give players a reason to stay besides hot outfits. cheers.\t\t\t\t\t
