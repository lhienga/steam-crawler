\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFate/Extella Link is a worthy sequel to The Umbral Star, if only in gameplay. Probably your best chance to play as some of your favourite heroic spirits, unless you are insane enough to face the power of gacha in Fate/Grand Order

Pros:
-the gameplay got a major upgrade, and fighting off massive armies has never felt better
-the game is well optimised, since even my potato laptop can run it in 50-60 FPS
-the music continues to be phenomenal, so get ready to jam out to the 5986th EMIYA remix
-the roster got expanded, and all the favourites are here. From the original cast from the VN, to newbie servants like Arjuna and Charlamagne, there is a servant for everyone here....and yes, Astolfo is in the game.
-Gilgamesh is still broken beyond compare, so bow your heads low, mongrels

Cons:
-what the actual f happened to the story. The og Extella had an amazing plot, while Link over here...not so much
-as much as i like the combat changes, i still find some combos lackluster compared to the original
-as a fan of both the JP and ENG voices, i'd really like an english dub.

Overall, if you want the satisfying warriors combat, play this. If you want an amazing story, play The Umbral Star. If you are a Fate fan who's fully embraced the hell that is this franchise, get both.
