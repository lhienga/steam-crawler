It's simple, Fate/EXTELLA LINK is a really bad sequel to Fate/Extella, but it's still a good game that's a lot of fun to play.

One of the things I liked the most in the previous game was the story, seriously, it was great, heavily focused on romance, so I understand that it's not for everyone, but I personally loved every second of it.
In this game, the story goes from non existent to OK to "it could be good too bad it's not".
The story is about Charlemagne and Rex Magnus Karl, and Altera (again), and it starts just after the true ending of the previous game.
The problem is that there's more story in the first hour of the game than in the entire rest of it.... between each missions it's like "I have a plan" , "I have another plan" "Master please make your choice". and there's nothing else.
So yeah you have a little bit at the start and a little bit in each of the 3 endings.

The game is also super short it took me 8 hours to finish every endings while also doing some extra battles along the way.
The previous game was like 30+hours and even the side missions had more story than the main story of this game

The way the story is told is also is huge step back. It's classic "visual novel" you see the character portraits as they talk and the text but that's it. There's nothing but a blurry background behind the characters portrait, no 3d cutscenes aside from maybe 3 times where you have a few seconds of 3d animated story telling. Another huge letdown.

Then there's my room, if you expected something like the first game it's not the case, you just got the same portraits you have during the visual novel parts and that's it, also nothing happens when you level up your bonds. When you talk to your characters they never say more than 3 lines of dialogues. They completely ruined it once again.

The gameplay. That's where this game shines. It's great, playing a character is fun even level 1. you can even choose which skills you can use during battle.

All of the "equipment system" is better .
There's a decent amount of Extra battles (side missions) too.
There's more diversity in the main objectives of each battle and the fact that the "master" is physically on the battlefield change a few things.

Overall I had fun playing it and I'll probably continue to do so just to do all side mission, but this game JUST CAN'T COMPARE to fate/EXTELLA, don't expect much and you probably won't be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
