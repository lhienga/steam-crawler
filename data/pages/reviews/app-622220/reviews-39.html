Edit: Added a gameplay video.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fBuEIPCckPI

It's very fast paced, super colorful with nice graphics, the character design is great and the gameplay is pretty good too.
There's a lot of different characters to chose from and it's an absolute blast to play with all of them.
Lot of content and high replay value here.

Probably the best musou game i played since N3 : Ninety-Nine Nights !
Highly recommended even to non fans of the series.

Pros:
+Interesting universe.
+Lot of great characters to play with, each with their unique attacks and abilities.
+Great gameplay.
+Fights are incredibly fast paced.
+Nice art direction and character design.
+Really good soundtrack and sound effects.
+Tons of missions / extra missions / unlockables.
+Unlocked framerate (only in borderless fullscreen tho).
+The game have multiplayer (only pvp sadly).

Cons:
-Graphics may look outdated in some areas.
-Can become a bit repetitive after few hours.
-No coop mode.
-Fights can become a bit confusing when there's alot of action going on.
-Failing missions because an npc dies is frustrating.
-I'd prefer cinematics instead of fixed dialogues boxes.

Rating 8/10
