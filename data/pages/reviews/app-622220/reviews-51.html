\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is probably my first "musou style" game. I really like the increased character roster and gameplay albeit I did find a few things that I disliked. Battles can be too fast that its really hard to follow and the camera can be quite annoying. I hope there would be an option to zoom out a bit and also add an option to lock the camera viewing angles from time to time.

The costumes per character are quite few IMO and some require the previous game to unlock them. I'm not sure if you need to restart the entire game from Fate/Extella The Umbral Star first then start a new game from F/E Links. I'm guessing the increase in servants limited the number of available skins to publish.

Control-wise I had no issue with using both an Xbox360 wireless controller and also an 8bitdo SN30 gamepad but haven't tried to use the keyboard.

Fate Extella/Link shows how story line can be great and funny at the same time. You will fall in love each individual character when you're progress trough out the whole campaign. There are extra hours to the game with New Game plus and extra difficulty which is for advanced players too.

Multiplayer is also included in the game. Testing out a battle against others or your friends online competitive match of 3 vs 3.


Game play feels smooth and sometimes sluggish due to enemy AI clustered and if might lag some PC users as well. But you'll get used to it.

The music. I love it so much
The characters. Fun and lovable. Sometimes weird as well.
The environment is colorful and variant. If the game had an expanded lore. The Fate series might be the best to expand more variety and color vibe of all the maps and world.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe previous game, Fate/Extella, was far from a masterpiece. Despite of that I did enjoy it much more than Fate/Extra. First Fate/ Extella had lots of small things that diminished my total experience. Such as loose mechanics and too little variations between different missions and servants' play styles. However, apparently the creators really listened to players' feedback concerning the previous game's issues. Link's gameplay and combat itself is much more precisely planned and it is much more fun to play than the previous game. Playing different servants and using their different abilities is much more fun in this game. I found first Extella's story more epic and intriguing than Link's but I don't mean that the story is boring.

Tldr; If you liked first Extella even a little, then I truly recommend Link, since as a video game this one is a lot better.\t\t\t\t\t
