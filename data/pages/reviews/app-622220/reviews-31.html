\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing fanservice! And I'm not alking about anime tiddies, although the game doesn't lack in that department. No, I'm talking about all those gorgeously animated Noble Phantasms and opportunity to play with the iconic characters of Fate franchise without the need to fish for them in gacha, unlike in a certain other Fate title. Gameplay is extremely basic hack-and-slash and almost every mission boils down to "capture designated points, then defeat the boss", sometimes with a timer on top of that, but timers are usually long enough to complete the mission.
So, if you're a Fate fan and want to hear Kawasumi Ayako yell "Ekusukaribaaaa!" every 15 seconds or feast your eyes on the Rose of Rome - then this game is awesome and a must-pick. Variety of playable characters (20-something) and branching storyline will provide you with enough entertainment to make it worth the purchase. If you're not a Fate fan, however, you'll likelyget bored of it after a couple of hours of repetitive gameplay.
I had a lot of fun and enjoyed the game immensely, so I rate it 10 missed Gae Bolg strikes out of 10 and would go to Nero and Lizzy joint concert again.\t\t\t\t\t
