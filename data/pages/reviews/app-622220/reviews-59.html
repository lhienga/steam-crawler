\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay so... second review.
First of all, the Fate/Extra series has an incredible story line. Extra was good. C/C/C was good. Extella was good. This? Not so much.
Extella's story was slow. Long, and slow, but full of emotion and left a huge impact on me when I finished it. I legit cried several times during my playthrough. Extella LINK however, has a much shorter, and seemingly rushed story. It feels like this is an extension to the previous game (hence the name "Extella" LINK), rather than an actual sequel. Which is fine on its own, but when you compare it to the previous game then... it's a big let down. I really expected something better.

Other than that, the gameplay (and graphics) is really improved compare to the previous game. This game has more servants, more stuff to do, an online mode (which probably doesn't matter anymore), more costumes (which means more money, Jesus), and Altera lily. Towards the end however, it kinda lost its charm, since you most likely have all servants leveled and stuff, you're left with pretty much nothing to do, but this is like every RPG game so... yeah. I'll say if you come for the gameplay then it's probably worth it. But if you're still hyped up from Extella's story and looking for a similar experience, then... prepare yourself.

My only other complain is that the game needs internet to play. Which... for a game where you play offline most of the time, is kinda weird.\t\t\t\t\t

If you like a good soundtrack
If you like a good story

Eventually, I hope the developers realize how much American fans want more content for multiplayer and release more. I plan to 100% this game, and completely finish it.\t\t\t\t\t
