\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf the point of Extella was deepening your relationship with Saber/Caster, then this game is nothing like that.

Link reduces both of them to caricatures for the sake of letting more servants have their 15 seconds of fame. This was done presumably to rectify the non-gameplay of Extella, yet not only does it fail in this regard, but now the writing has been axed too.

Nasu nor Sakurai is the main writer for this game and it is blatantly obvious. I dont know whats happening in this game, Charlemagne (both of them) dont know whats happening, Saber and Caster especially dont know whats happening: you wont know whats happening.

The characters all play incredibly similar and the few wildcards like Caster were nuked: that cool explodey kick thing she did is now a spell you have to cast and it barely works half the time despite being fairly consistent in the previous title. The increased number of playable servants thus fails to be meaningful and you'll probably just be playing the same guy the whole time.

There are a couple brief scenes that will maybe have plot relevance for FGO down the line regarding the supposed "foreign god", but they is not worth buying the game over.

Link lacks the soul Extella had. I thoroughly enjoyed my time with Extra, CCC, and Extella, regardless of the jank, but Link failed to give me a similar experience. In a strange twist of Fate, Link feels like the devs were focused most on the gameplay, yet it plays worse than Extella- which is really saying a lot.

Games worth playing if youre a fate fan just dont buy it lol.\t\t\t\t\t
