\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Gameplay is improved as compared to the sequel with the addition of skills that you can use in combat in addition to normal/heavy attacks.
- Great characters.

Cons:
- Game is too damn short for the $40 price tag. You can play through the main story in ~5hrs. I bought it on sale for $25 and even then, I feel like I wasted my money. A $15 price tag would be more fitting. In comparison, Fate/EXTELLA has ~40hrs of content and can be bought for ~$15 on sale which is way more worth its purchase.
- Cut-scenes are too short and do not flesh out the characters. It almost feels like the Devs did not want to pay the voice-actors more to record more audio for these scenes. My favorite character, Nero, is barely in the game and if I were to collect all her voiced dialogue, I doubt I could fill even 1/2 hr while her voiced dialogue in the previous game can amount to hours.
- Story was meh: big bad guy invades, our heroes stop the big bad and they all live happily ever after. A 8 year old could write a better story. I did enjoy the story of the previous game and compared to it, this story is hot garbage.

Overall, 4/10. This is the Dragon Age 2 of this series. A OK game on its own but compared to its prequel, hot garbage. Too many smooth-brained weebs are giving the game a free pass just because it has Astolfo.\t\t\t\t\t
