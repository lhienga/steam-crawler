\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf your Fate waifu is in this you probably already bought it. If your waifu is not included, you probably still bought it with the hopes of your waifu getting some love in potential dlc or a sequel.
If you have no idea what Fate is, this might not be the best starting point, but nobody can agree on the best starting point anyway so if that's whats keeping you just go for it anyway.

Anywhoo...

Positives...
(1) Much cheaper than trying to get your waifu in F/GO
(2) 26 characters in the base game
(3) High quality waifus with quality environments for them to play in
(4) You are never forced to play as a specific character for any level after the tutorial
(5) Gameplay is smooth with some really good looking combos and noble phantasms

Negatives...
(1) A main story that will make you question if it's truly over after you see the credits. But have no fear! For there are two more routes that will lead you to the very same confusion at the end
(2) Lack of a challenge. You can finish levels while being under the recommended level on the highest difficulty. The Extra missions might have more challenge, but I have still not played enough of them
(3) 26 playable servants means a lot of people won't get the chance to play as their waifu this time around. But don't lose hope!\t\t\t\t\t
