\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will note right away that I am not familiar with this genre. I am speaking solely from the perspective of a Fate fan, not a musou enjoyer.

Disappointingly short, combat is a little repetitive, the story is blatantly underdeveloped and doesn't feel like it matters much, the side tasks in the extra missions feel like they were shoehorned in at the last moment (Getting the "Take over 3 sectors in 420 seconds" task after having taken over every single sector especially stings.), and it's generally pretty mindless. However... if you're into the rest of the Fate universe, this is a surprisingly good game to decompress with. Its mindlessness plays into its favor, at least for me. I usually come back to this game for a few hours after reading through the bad ends in F/SN or F/HA and just chillax for a bit. The environments are stellar, the soundtrack is good (Although CCC's was way better), and the servants are all animated very well and I enjoyed playing with every single one of them.

Plus... This game has the best husbandos in the series so far: Iskander, EMIYA, and Gilgamesh. Darius III is a most welcome, if niche, treat as well. The obligatory Nero and Tamamo are included as well and are most welcome; however, Liz love triumphs over all other waifu candidates.

In all, I wish I could give this game a neutral rating. I don't hate it, but I'd never pay $50-60 for it. If it goes on sale and you want to see more from your favorite husbando and/or waifu, then by all means pick this up and kill some time with it. Otherwise, you're paying for a 12-15 hour long story at the absolute most. Don't get this if you prefer Fate lore and canon over the characters, you will regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
