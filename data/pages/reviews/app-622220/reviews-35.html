\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo be honest, I was quite surprised that Fate/EXTELLA LINK actually has good combat gameplay that rivals that of the Dynasty Warriors series (and is different enough).
It's fast-paced visceral action, simple to pick up and add its own twist to the genre.
In fact, I'm going to have to say that the game has a better formula so far with its inclusion of Active Skills abilities.
Characters are varied enough that, chances are, you'll find something that you'll feel good playing.
There's also a quality of life option where you can level your character with currency, up to the level of your highest leveled character - making it a lot easier to start off on new characters.

If you're not acquainted with the Fate series, I doubt this title will make you anymore interested.
But if you're a Fate series (more specifically, Fate/EXTRA) + Musou-style lover, this might be right up your alley - there's a lot of fan service here.
Nevertheless, looking at the whole package, I do recommend getting this (only) when it's on sale - got mine at 40% off.
One advice if you do purchase the game, is that the really fun part only starts after you complete the main storyline where you unlock more characters and the Install Skill Synthesis option to make powerful upgrades.\t\t\t\t\t
