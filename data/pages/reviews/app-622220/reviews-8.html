\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIm saying this as a Fate fan and Dynasty Warriors fan. If you like musou or fate or both, buy it, especially if its on sale. But i cant really recommend this to you otherwise

The battles is fun like most musou games are, the situation in the battlefield is constantly changing so you wont be bored just button mashing. In addition, the characters have good variety in their moveset. I have played with some and i have fun with them. Although for me the camera sometime confused me and the shaking is a bit disturbing, this issue is quite minor.

A major issue that make me can hardly recommend this game is what outside the battle. The story is alright, but its only done in visual novel style unlike the previous installments, even the psp Fate extra use 3d models and environment for story. The hub is a nice idea as a place we can see our servants hanging out and interacts with each others, but it did not offer more than what a menu can honestly (as you can just access your "rooms" via the pause menu anyways.

As a fate fan i like seeing my favorite servant Lu Bu and Lancelot in the game (very fun to use as well), but the lack of new original servants aside from Charlemange and Rex Magnus is kinda a let down.

Overall its a fun game if you are looking for a game to destress with the bonus of anime waifus/hasbandos, especially if you are a Fate fan. Aside that it has not much to offer. But i still recommend it because i have fun with it, and thats what important.\t\t\t\t\t
