Combat feels more fluid and you can change more aspects of the Servant's skills.
Noble Phantasms have been greatly improved as you can now fill the gauge in Moon Drive mode instead of relying solely on NP Chips and the controller vibration during NPs is 10/10 accuracy to what you see on the screen.

The story is far less cringey in this game as well and you aren't forced to complete all 3 routes to see the true ending unlike in the previous game.

Overall yes I would recommend this game if you are a fan of Fate or a fan of Dynasty Warriors.\t\t\t\t\t
