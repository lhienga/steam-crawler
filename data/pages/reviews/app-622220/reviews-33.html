\r\n\t\t\t\t\tso the short version is, I played the previous game and enjoyed it, I played this game and enjoyed it too

the long version....
the gameplay feels more polished than in the previous game, I would overall rate it as an improvement in terms of gameplay
the visuals are comparable to the previous game, in some cases improved
music is still great
the customization system is....mostly the same, but maybe streamlined a bit, I found it easier to max out certain things, but maybe that's because I had some idea of what to do from the previous game, hard to say
the story was...ok... it wasnt great but it wasnt really bad either, there was really only one part of the story that overtly stuck out to me as a "wait what?" moment, which I will attempt to explain as spoiler free as I can... and that is a character from the previous game shows up and the other characters act as if they had never met him, which they had, there are potential reasons why they would have forgotten this character but none of them are ever expressly called out and everything else about the story would suggest it is a direct sequel, thats my one thing about the story, and honestly thats probably the worst part of the entire game, for me at least, minor continuity error...potentially, so thats not too bad
dlc is mostly cosmetic with one actual extra character at time of writing this review, it looks like they maybe planned to add more but at time of writing its been over a year since release and no new dlc, maybe it was planned and abandoned, not sure
it felt shorter than the previous game, but again not sure if thats due to having more of an idea of what I'm doing or it is actually shorter, maybe both
at time of writing, this is the only fate game with charlie, so if he sounds like a neat character you wanna check out then thats a plus

biggest complaint I've seen is that the active skills invalidate the combo system, and for some characters this is completely true, but not every character, there are many counter attack and buff skills, and some characters they feel more appropriate, at least in my experience, maybe that's sub optimal, idk

I havent tried the multiplayer so I can't really comment on it

overall I enjoyed the game, so yeah\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk returned to the game and finished everything here's my breakdown:

This game was obviously made for Fate fans. If you're not a fan of the franchise or musou games ( dynasty warriors ) this isn't for you don't bother save yourself the time/money.
It's a direct continuation essentially of the first game in the series and the netflix fate adaptation.

Pros:
-Tons of story like 90% of the game is diallogue, you're almost buying an actual LN here with dynasty warriors thrown on top of it.
-Lots of characters from the Fate universe including some lesser known ones (unless you play the mobile version ) which is nice.
-For a Musou game the combat is good and is fun and it's what kept me playing to be honest.
-Each Character feels pretty different
-Lots of side/extra content unless your me in which you end up speed clearing the game in no time at all.
-actually has a unique and interesting multiplayer although its probably completely dead in 2021.
Cons:
-Graphics need improving. What I mean here is the way the color pallet they used for this game and all the particle affects don't really work well. I find myself not really looking at my screen but my mini map to run around. You kind of get blinded by all the effects and bad colors happening. I especially hate all the fog maps and then they throw in global effects like Andromeda that just make everything ugly and you can't see ♥♥♥♥. I hope this gets improved drastically for next one. Id like more camera zoom out and things to be clearer.
- Ui is horrible. Not that it doesn't look good its ok but the fact its so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ big its in your face and in your way half the time. This coupled with the graphical issues I mentioned really annoyed me and if you're a streamer or content creator this isn't gonna be a great game for you since it'll look all pixelated and a mess of colors on stream/recording.
-It's essentially a console game/port so you get all the negatives that come with that.
-lots of DLC that I would of rather have seen implemented in the game as unlockables. They charged the price of a AAA title for this and still wanted more afterwards which is just taking advantage of the fandom because you know we can resist.
-There's no difficulty. Like outside of maybe your first run through? The games easy as hell. You eventually get to a point where you're borderline unkillable and even one of the end game relics gives you invulnerability on cooldown whenever you want it. I enjoyed the content because of the combat/characters but it deff wasn't a challenge. You might be saying well its a musou game. But dynasty warriors on its highest difficulties is no joke while this game never felt challenging.
-Some characters are borderline useless ( looking at you Joan ). Idk why they designed some of them the way they did but quite a few of them really aren't good and you'll mostly be playing the same 4-5 characters that are actually good.\t\t\t\t\t
