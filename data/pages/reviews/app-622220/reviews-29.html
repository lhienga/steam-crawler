STORY
This takes place in the Fate Extra universe which is a different timeline than the original Fate/Stay Night timeline. There's currently 4 games in this universe:
Fate/Extra (A remake is coming called Fate/Extra Record)
Fate/Extra CCC (only in Japanese, takes place during Fate/Extra)
Fate/Extella (Direct sequel to Extra, retconns the ending of Extra)
Fate/Extella Link

If you're completely new to Fate the best introduction into the series is reading the original Visual Novel Fate/Stay Night and then watch the Fate/Zero anime. Make sure to get the Decensored Realta Nua version of the VN.

That said, even if you have read it you should start the Extra games with Fate/Extra and then play Fate/Extella if you are interested in the story. Fate/Extra plays perfectly on a emulator by the way. The problem with Extella Link's story is that it is more of a inbetween game, kind of like filler in anime. I really liked the story of Extra and Extella but in Link I honestly think it sucks, so much that I think the game would have been better without it, just battles with added fluff text and bonding events would have been preferred.

GAMEPLAY
While it is clear that they spent a lot of time on improving Extella's lackluster gameplay, including adding an additional 10 playable servants, the issue I have with this game is the lack of balance. It is simply too damn easy. I play exclusively on hard and even when I am 30 levels under the recommended level (on hard) I breeze through the levels. I'm also not that big of a fan of the sector maps, I prefer the battlefield maps of the (old?) Dynasty Warriors games. One thing they added was active skills (basically powerful attacks with cooldowns), these are not a bad idea but the cooldown is simply too short. If you cycle between the 4 you have equipped you can more or less use them indefinitely, it makes normal attacks and combos meaningless. The AI is very weak as well, it is very easy to juggle enemy servants by timing active skills.

PORT QUALITY
Almost perfect. No FPS cap, I play with 140 fps, and you can use high resolution as well, 1440p for me. I remove a point because exclusive fullscreen is capped to 60 fps so you have to use window/borderless mode, but if you use window your mouse cursor is visible and constantly appears on the screen. I fixed this with by using the "Lossless scaling" software which has an option to hide the cursor. The game also requires you to be online even when playing singleplayer.

MUSIC
Better than expected. There were definitely a couple of songs that I really liked. Your milage may vary.

If there's anything to take away from the Fate Extra universe it is just how good Tamamo is. After playing through Extra, Extella and Link and can with confidence say she is the best (servant) girl in Fate.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, i'm not a fan of Fate Series. I only watched Fate: Apocrypha for Astolfo. Yes, that's the main reason why i bought this game; it's because i saw Astolfo was playable and he is an often character in Story Mode. The fact that this game is a Hack n'Slash and got a RPG Style (LvLing your Characters, adding/removing Skills, creating Mystic Codes) gave me another reason to buy it. Sadly, when i saw full price 50€ (Normal) and 60€ (Deluxe Edition) i wasn't really sure to buy it. Why should i paye that much for a game that may not like? So, i waited Black Friday for having an huge sale on it and i bought it and i'm really not disappointed! If i knew that i will love this game that much i would already payed it since a while even in full price.

The game working perfectly. I never had any crash or drop FPS. You can run it in all type of PC even on the most badest ones. You won't get any troubles even if there is ALOT of ennemies and skills effect on the screen it won't crash or reduce your FPS. It's very nice optimised. As i said earlier, this game is a Hack n'Slash and you got a bit of RPG type. It means that you got multiples characters that you can LvL UP, also adding/removing some Skills on them. The Max LvL is 150 but don't worry about it, it's not that long for reach it. Same for the Bond (Support) LvL that is 30. This game own two main game mode: Story Mode that you can play in Easy, Normal, Hard and Very Hard (unlockable when ending the game once) and Extra Battle that got only one Difficulty call Extra. It's basically what giving you new Costumes and to LvLing your Character super fast. There is also a Multiplayer Mode but i never tried it because i can't found any game.

All DLC for this game are only Costumes for your characters. The only DLC that you could have to buy is: Young Altera. That giving you a new character to play with, and trust me, she is worth her price (10€ full price). Talking about characters, i can't tell if the game really giving the main Fate Serie's Heroes because i only watched Aprocrypha and this game got: Jeanne d'Arc, Karna and Astolfo from it.

Conclusion: I know nothing about Fate Serie and that did not changed my opinion and gameplay (understanding story and knowing characters) for playing it so you don't need to know the Anime/Mangas. Only thing you need to know is; do i love RPG and Hack n'Slash combine? If yes, then take this game. If not, then pass your way.\t\t\t\t\t
