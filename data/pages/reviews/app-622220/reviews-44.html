\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMusou gameplay, with the Fate series characters. If you do not like Musou gameplay, this game will not be a fun time for you. The DLC for this game is all purely cosmetic, if you absolutely have to have the swimsuit sets, wait for a sale. Overall the animations are great, the style is very well executed for its series, and it is an enjoyable game.

It can be pretty short, you can run through a playthrough rather quickly, getting the 100% will take some time. Like all musou games the game is not terribly difficult, but can have some challenging maps / missions. There is some thought into your planning, since you can only have 5 side missions, that will get the affection of your spirits up. This affects gameplay and unlocks more customization/strengthening.

If you're a fan of the series, and don't mind button mashing Musou games, then this is the game to play, however if you're not a big fan of the button mashing mindlessness, avoid at all costs.

Oh and the tiddie jiggle.\t\t\t\t\t

Things it did better than Fate/EXTELLA
  • You have more attacks(every chatacter now has 5 ground and 3 air combos with 8 active skills)
  • More install skills
  • More servants(tho only 2 are original)

Things it did worse than Fate/EXTELLA
  • Story mode is very short(5 hours to reach the final ending), boring and bog-standard
  • Game is much easier, AI can't combo for more than 3 hits, seemingly never uses active skills
  • Command Seals give you such a strong boost that it's silly not to use them
  • Install Skills are weird now, all you need to do is put red skills and you win, no need for defence, no need for movement speed or increased xp like before
  • Item crates very generous, a lot of the time giving you max Drive or NP bar(without any buffs to drop rate from Instal Skills)
  • Addition of Rush attacks and Clashes just lets you ♥♥♥♥ even more on enemy servants
  • Enemy AI in general is braindead, they just block and wait for breaks, attacks randomly
  • Small enemies(called attack programs) now pose 0 threat(archers could sometimes hit you stronger than servants!) and you don't even have to fight them because as long as you let your allied servants take their scripted fields, you never have to "spawn" stronger mobs(called Aggressors)
  • Aggressors were nerfed it seems as they die really easily from 1 or 2 active skills which changes combat from a Warriors spinoff to a drive-by simulator, you just pop into an arena, run up to an Agressor and hit it with your active skills because that's the most optimal way of playing, no need to do combos on them, they are too weak, just run in, pop a skill or two, move on
  • Combos are only worthwhile on enemy servants
  • Active skills are too strong and up too often, they turn the game boring, instead of diferent combos with different purpose like in Extella, you just decide whether to use a close range skill or a long range one, maybe a rush one if the game allows it to work on a servant
  • No Extra Story! Just Extra Stages, half of them are just stages from story mode! In Extra Story every servant except Tamamo, Nero & Altera had their own intro, ending and unique dialogue and objectives. Maps weren't very different from Story Mode but there were some edited ones like Nameless vs Archimedes. Original Extra Stages are okay but not when you consider what they are replacing.
