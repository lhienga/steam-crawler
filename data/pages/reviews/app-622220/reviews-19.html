\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game! From what I can tell, it's related story-wise to Fate Extella, which is in turn related to Fate Extra, the first introduction to Fate media I had. My favorite servants both from their respective stories and here are Tamamo no Mae (Caster), Nero Claudius (Saber), Artoria (Saber), and Jeanne D'Arc (Ruler). Fate Extra was a good introduction to everything for me years ago, as well as the original Fate Stay Night visual novel. I really like how if you play as a certain servant who canonically interacted with each other in another Fate media (VN/anime/game), they'll have a unique conversation regarding it upon encountering each other. For example, Artoria vs Gilgamesh or Artoria vs Medusa will have said unique dialogue in battle. It's a nice touch of detail. I'm not too concerned with the story of this game here; I preferred Fate Extella and Fate Extra for their superior stories, as well as the vn. This is essentially a fun way to play as your favorite Fate characters but in a much newer 3D fighting game with very nice graphics. The story is decent enough to make you care about said characters here, but for me I prefer the original stories and their lore. It would be nice if we could get a remake of Fate Unlimited Codes, but with this game engine so we could play as Shirou, Rin, Sakura, and Saber Alter. Although, being able to play as Saber (Artoria), Archer (Emiya), and Lancer (Cu Chulainn), and Rider (Medusa) is good enough for me here.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery enjoyable game in the fate franchise, but some things were better in the previous one:

Umbral star had better plot, story in this game is very straight forward and most of the characters lost their charm. Link is called a "direct sequel" to Umbral star but there is no connection to the previous one whatsoever.
Extra content was also better in the Umbral Star, servant side missions gave few interactions between characters and some depht to them, although I still enjoyed the fact that you can talk to every servant in my room.

However, Extella Link has some good points that I would like to see more of if we ever get another game, to name a few:

Gameplay is 3 times more enjoyable, you no longer have to chain normal and strong attacks to do combos but you can have fun with customisable active skills, npc servants actually help this time with defending and attacking sectors, and there are no more sector traps that were annoying.
Music was always a strong point in fate games, this soundtrack mareges both the old umbral star soundtrack with the new additions from link.

Overall I had a really fun time with this game, 100% it was way easier then the previous one without much grinding. 6/10 would recommend\t\t\t\t\t
