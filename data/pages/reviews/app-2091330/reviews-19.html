The game is fun to play for a long period of time
The last time I gave money to the devs was back in 2014 when the Early Access hit. Ever since then, the game has been always here for me. And year by year, it has provided me with new content every time. Only this year, before the DLC launched, did I reach 500 days in Survival - it was difficult, but very very fun.

Negative reviews - why?
I get why some people are mad and are giving negative reviews because this DLC isn't the full package, but I can also get that they are horrible at reading comprehension. It was clearly stated that this DLC is an experimental project for the devs, which project's end is after 12 months. So 12 months of getting updates with promising content that everyone wants, for the price of 20 EUR? It sounds like a good bargain honestly. If you want to get a DLC with the full package, then (as it was suggested in their pre-release notes) wait out the 12 months. It's incredible that they are actually working on the game still, and I have to say: they're doing a great job at it, since the game is still pretty entertaining to play.

Is the DLC worth it now?

The game doesn't feel that different, other than having a new region to explore, female deer (doe) and some new variants of already-existing items.

Should you buy RIGHT NOW?
Is it worth to buy it right now? Yes and No.
"Yes" if you are supportive of the devs and wish to experience the release of this new content month-for-month in small chunks. AND you wish to pay the least amount of money for this DLC for this and next year.
"No" if you are not supportive of the devs and wish to experience everything at once after 12 months of waiting. AND you wish to pay 30 EUR when the DLC drops\t\t\t\t\t
