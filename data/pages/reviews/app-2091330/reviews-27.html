\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDevelopment of the DLC has now progressed to a point where I can strongly recommend it to any players who haven't bought it yet. The latest update to the DLC and its tale is, in my opinion, one of the best updates to the whole game, very immersive. The design of the new region is excellent, I really enjoyed playing it this time.

I will leave my original review below, but its arguments are no longer true, and my view of the product is now VERY POSITIVE.



=======================================================================
After playing for a few hours, I sincerely believe that the DLC is missing most of the gameplay features one would expect, based on the information provided by developers prior to launch. In my opinion, the only thing that this adds to the game is one region and one affliction. No matter how much the developers boast, adding a doe to the game is not a particularly strong selling point. I experienced a few minor technical issues, but those were quite easy to fix or work around. However, missing gameplay features seem to me almost as a form of false advertising, even if the marketing division carefully chose their words. As a non-native English speaker, I was not able to immediately determine what is and what is not coming to the DLC at launch.

At this point in time, I cannot recommend the DLC for purchase; it needs at least another solid three months of work to be worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
