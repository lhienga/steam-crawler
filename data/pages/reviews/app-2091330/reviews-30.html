I'm playing on Stalker and it's like the developers forget a development-toggle on, released the game and went on vacation. There also seems to be a lot of players that are not able to start the game, I'm on a PC, AMD processor and an Nvidia Graphics card, on Windows 10 and have not had that problem.\t\t\t\t\t
