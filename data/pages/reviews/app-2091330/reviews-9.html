\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm writing this when only the first part of this DLC is out and I think this DLC will be a good addition to the base game once it's fully released.
That being said, in its current state, there isn't much to it.

The new regions are some of the least inspired ones that currently exist. I agree with everyone that are saying the walk to the Forgotten Airfield was too long but it mostly felt that way becasue there wasn't much to do or look at on the way there. Once you actually make it to the Airfield there once again isn't much to look at or explore. Some of the rock formations have weirdly square shapes and there are plenty of places where you think you can walk but you can't, and others where your character will get stuck on little nooks which I hope will be fixed.

Again, I think the developers are intending to add more content to flesh out the existing areas but as of now this DLC won't bring anything new to the existing TLD experience.

The new fog and insomnia affliction seem pointless and for some reason tanks your FPS if you're near the airfield structures. I was worried when I first got afflicted with insomnia because I was already in a pretty bad state, only to go to bed and wake up with a full nights sleep and the infliction completely gone... The fog is pretty much just fancy fog, now with sound effects.

I think Hinterlands shouldn't have called it a DLC with a "12-month campaign" and simply pushed it for what it is - A very early-access DLC with almost none of the promised future features currently in it.

The only reason why I'd recommend someone to get this DLC now is because the price will go up later down the line and I believe it WILL be good once everything on the roadmap has been added. But if you're looking for something to spice up your gameplay right now, this currently isn't it.\t\t\t\t\t
