It's essentially Early Access:
You can either pay a smaller sum of money at the beginning of the development period for the promise of content throughout the year, or a larger sum of money at the end of the development period for the whole deal.
If you pay from the start, you get the full value of all future updates, not just those released at the time of your purchase. Doing so is purely to support the developers, and if you are risk-averse and do not trust them, you can simply wait until everything is done to purchase with your mind at ease.

That being said, the update has been more than worth it for me.\t\t\t\t\t
