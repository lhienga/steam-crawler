\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was torn on writing a review for this, but there's three things that pushed me to state that I would not recommend this DLC at this time:

1) This is early access-esque DLC that cleverly on the store page advertises all the features you'll get... eventually. You get a small portion of the promised content now and will supposedly get all the advertised content later. This is Hinterland's MO, so par for the course here and probably not a deal breaker for most fans of the game. With that said, though, at the moment the amount of content is not worth the asking price (in my opinion) and it's unnecessarily confusing to know what you'll actually get when you get the DLC at a given point in time (please don't link me the road map, I've viewed it).

2) You lose your survival saves. Yes you can time capsule to get them back and also lose the content you get with the DLC, but then why get the DLC if you're going to do that? This is as well something that I've never seen outside of early access, and it's surreal to see save wiping in a released game. This is a feature that should have been advertised on the store page, as you will get this feature if you purchase the DLC now. (please don't respond about how we've been "warned for months")

3) The responses from the devs and parts of the community on the above points are not what I expected. I shouldn't have to follow dev diaries or do in depth research to know what this DLC gives me when this could have just been communicated more clearly. Sometimes it's not the volume of communication that's important but rather how that communication is handled. Put a disclaimer for save wipes on the store page for this DLC. Have a concise and updated list on the roadmap page to clearly show what's actually in the DLC at its current release.

All in all, if you're excited for more content for The Long Dark then this is it! Just make sure you understand what you're getting with this DLC and decide whether it's worth the price you'll pay for it.\t\t\t\t\t
