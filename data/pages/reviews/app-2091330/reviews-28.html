\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReviewers should keep in mind that this is a year long expansion pass! Do I think that what was released today was worth $20? Not at all! Because this is just drop one of many to come over the next year :)

Yes, there are bugs. Yes, that's disappointing. The devs have done such an amazing job thus far with the Long Dark, we have these very unrealistic expectations. Bugs and issues at launch are normal, and the team did a great job at fixing them within hours. I didn't buy or launch the game until 3 hours after launch and experienced zero issues. The bugs I've come across are annoying, but not game breaking. Yes, there is a trailer in Mystery Lake with all of the new variants lined up on a wall, and that is said to be in the works already of being patched out. Yes, there are graphical issues with blurry lock picking and items. Yes, there are weird changes to loot tables I don't like. All of these are minor and I trust the devs to have all of this fixed soon.

What you are buying and able to access immediately, is not a lot. It's two new maps you can't start in, a handful of item variants, some new music, a new weather type and a new affliction. That's all you get right now. Yes, it's not a lot. That's why the price is lower now. It's a purchase of trust, telling the dev team "Hi, I believe in you! I support you!". If you want a huge valuable update of content all at once to play right now - wait for it. Buy it when it's all been released. It's that simple.

If you love this game, buy the dlc. Support their ambitions in delivering this huge, long project. Buy it because you have hundreds of hours in the base game that's been on steam since 2015 and provided you free updates for five years. Buy it because you are a fan, a supporter and you appreciate this team for the world they've built for us.\t\t\t\t\t
