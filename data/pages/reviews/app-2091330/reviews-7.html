\r\n\t\t\t\t\tQuick note: around half of the reviews (as of jan. 2024) are from before the signal void update, so if they complain about there not being anything in Forsaken Airfield, it's outdated.

Overall a very good DLC. It's not worth twenty bucks right now, but the price will increase with more content being added to the DLC. They probably should have just made all the content, then released the DLC, since it will get extremely repetitive if you have to go out of the Far Territory to complete one of the "tales" every single time a new region/tale is released. The content is definitely enjoyable, and you can sink a lot of hours into the game easily with this stuff. Great if you want to recapture that feeling of exploring the game for the first time.

The second part, Buried Echos, is somewhat disappointing. It's short, easy, and wasn't worth the 6 month wait. The map is amazing, it's basically 2 in 1, but the story for it is just a bit too short in comparison to the first chapter. It might be because the first chapter had you exploring more of the map, but it feels like you spend more time wandering around than actually doing anything in chapter 2. It was still enjoyable, but it wasn't quite worth the wait. They probably could have done better with six months, but they are setting things up for the third part which should be interesting.

8/10 get it when it's on sale (or when you just stole someone's wallet)\t\t\t\t\t
