\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am unsure why the negativity surrounding The Long Dark: TFTFT

Impatience and inattention to the press releases I think is the main cause and that is decidedly unfair on a studio such as Hinterland led by someone as in touch and in tune with their customer base as Rafe is.

The expansion map itself is excellent and is a real challenge to survive in. By design there are large expanses of open and unremarkable terrain and getting caught in severe weather such as blizzards is a hazard to survive in. The map is varied in terrain and has a spread out places to make a base.

As far as new items go, as of right now, there are variations of the rifle and lantern. I know the rifle variations have different stats (shout out to Vaughn's Rifle - my personal fave) but not certain if the lanterns are different.

Not everything has been released yet - far from it. But Hinterland well prepared us for this and has provided a roadmap for game releases.

Overall, if you currently own The Long Dark and aren't part of the "I want everything now" crowd this is a solid addition to the game and I look forward to the story mode and additional items/mechanics when they come.\t\t\t\t\t
