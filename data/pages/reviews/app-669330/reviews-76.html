- No 100 Cost Mass Unit should be able to have a shield tech

- Tech disabling should have a strong reduce effect (like 80% slower, less effective or sth), not a nullfy effect

The game is supposed to be a rock-paper-scissor style game where unit placement/overall composition also matters.

As of now, the higher in Elo you get, the less variance there is what is good in the lategame.
