\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGenuinely surprised to see the hours I have on this game. Guess it means I should probably say something. Things I find outstanding: Devs make careful and frequent balance changes to units (not nerf or buff into oblivion), frequent updates per month, games are short and test your big lizard-rock-paper-scissors brain, no game is exactly the same with different card and comp picks, lots of tools available for creative strategies.

I don't have many complaints about this game. Sometimes the state of the meta gets stale but these frequent updates are keeping me playing it.

Edit: Feb 3 2023 new "random chaos" update introduced and removed a large portion on strategy from this game. Very heavily set luck as a factor to your win/loss and no option to play it as a separate mode. The heavy handed RNG is just part of the ladder and matchmaking. Those looking for strategy look elsewhere.\t\t\t\t\t
