PVP games take between 10-20 minutes (the longest I've ever played was <30). The built in spectator mode for high rank games is a very fun community building feature. The player base is friendly and interactive.

Best value I've gotten from money spent since 1993.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was very promising and shaping up to be one of the classics. Unfortunately for some unknown reason the developers decided to introduce a patch that doesn't change the game slightly but completely turns over the fundamentals of the game.

This has lead to a big rift within the community and decline of competitiveness as most higher ranked players did not enjoy the change.

I am myself also opposed to the change. It drastically alters how the game is being played and turns it into something else than I enjoyed for many months. The game changed from slower paced deep strategy with a lot of nuance to a chaotic unit spam fest. It might appeal to people who only want to do random stuff and watch big battles regardless of the outcome but I can no longer recommend this to anyone looking for a bit of strategy or a "thinking man's game."
