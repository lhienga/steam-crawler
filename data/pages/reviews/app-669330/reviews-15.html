\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game since the beta. It was a really nice round based strategic game until the last patch. This patch increased drastically the difficulty of the game and with it the difference between good and bad players. But the player base isn't that big. Also, it won't attract any new players because they just got beaten up for the first 20 hours playtime. All of this was now approved by the devs in their last Q/A. And this brings me to the last argument. The devs don't tell you all you need to know about the game. One example: Leveling up units is important and therfore they need to earn xp which they get by killing an enemy unit. But until the devs where asked about a bug in the xp earning system they didn't tell by themself that a unit gets earns also xp for a kill assist. This lack of communication forces you to invest more time in the game than necessary. Which wasn't this big problem until they drastically increased the difficulty of the game.
So sadly, I can't recommend this game any longer even it is not a bad game.

PS: Some money quotes of the last Q/A of the devs:
"Players who have a deeper understanding of the game have a greater advantage than before. Judging from the already available data, the winning rate of high-level players is even higher than before."
"In the past, new players had to be concerned with only 3-4 types of deployed units. With the free unit card system, the players need to have a certain level of understanding of all 19 types of units."
"In the past, we didn’t explain the appearance mechanism of free unit cards clearly enough. As a result, many of you didn’t know how to predict and counter the appearance of free unit cards."
"We always believed that the unit positions of the first round were a relatively weak part of the game. The reason is that all units in the first round can move at will, so it is difficult to predict the opponent's tactics." - My comment: "This is why we need a "Chaotic Fun Patch" with more random unit spawns then ever before?"\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, an autobattler which requires a good amount of skills/wisdom, between positionning, unit choices, tech choices, etc.
There's a part of RNG due to the card selection between each round (same selection for both player) and the fact that each player plays their turn without seeing what the other does.

A lot of people cry about those free units which appears instead of the normal cards, but personnaly i find it mostly great. Yes it requires some adjustements, but it offers more diversity from match to match and it requires more adaptability during a game.

Yes, it adds RNG, but at the same time it's a non card turn (and sometimes those ones can be far worse/great than the "free units" (nuke/lightning storms when playing aerial units, javelin when you rely on some important units while the other guy is mostly building mass, giant hunter when you already have giants on the board, etc. )).

The patch will stay, the game is even better now.\t\t\t\t\t
