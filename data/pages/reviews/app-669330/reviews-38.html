So the game itself is fun, its a battlefield thats always evolving with each action either granting you the upper edge or the losing opening in your army. Its all about mechs ranging from small infantry to tanks to flying killing machines. The ai is also super good for just being an early access game. Normal is what you expect, its a little easy when hitting end game, hard is what i'd consider normal as it provides a challenge from the start to finish, and insane ai is difficult but yet not impossible.
Now for multiplayer... which I want to be fair I'VE NEVER TOUCHED IT YET. Now I am gonna give my input about it bc there are somethings you can just see when playing vs the AI, again the ai can act differently then a real person. But from what I notice is that an easy strat to win is to normally go with heavier bots, skip the upgrade cards, and only upgrading your units makes a pretty good army but yet I dont want this game becoming a "meta" game as it has so much to offer. I just hope multiplayer isnt like that. But for now personally I will be sticking with the Ai game play for a bit as ofc I'm still exploring the game.

Over all if you love rts games, but hate the whole micro manage and resources etc this is the game for you. You put units down, then figure which unit does what and how to counter the enemy. All armies are UP TO YOU TO PICK besides the start, but after round one, its whatever you can imagine for your army. For only playing for an hour, I am really satisfied with this game.\t\t\t\t\t
