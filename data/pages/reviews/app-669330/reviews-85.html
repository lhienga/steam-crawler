\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cannot recommend the game even at its price point. It has its upsides, but first the down.
1. You cant zoom out to see the whole map. (And its a small map)
2. The units are really really unbalanced.
3. Flanking needs to go. (cannot zoom out to see your flanks you have to scroll to the sides to even take a look.)
4. You get card buffs and what not but there is nothing indicating units have them, so when you have the same upgrades you can see on units but the enemy units have more range you have no idea why.
5. In some of my matches it was very sketchy and was almost like the opponent could see my placements of new units would be and counter placed all game (AKA. they could see through the pre round fog.) Even when i went to flank 1 round units somehow just popped up right in front of them to counter my flank. (possibly cheats already at play in this EA game)
6. Feels like a phone game more then a steam game.

Now the good
1. Its a very unique take on a tactical auto chess style game.
2. Getting into matches is fast
3. They have a discord
4. Graphics are not terrible
5. Its fun to watch the battles play out each round.
6. They have in game tournaments so you don't need to sign up for tournaments on 3rd party websites like in other games that don't support tournaments.

Now ill come back after some time and if improvements are made on these things ill change my recommendation as its still in EA. But there was more frustration with the game then fun while playing. And inst the point of the game to have fun? Was just my two cents everyone, if you enjoy it im glad for you. No need to troll.\t\t\t\t\t
