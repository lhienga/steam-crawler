Had some random card elements, which was not great but ok. Enjoyed the game for some time.

Then suddenly devs after a long time add random units every couple turns that completely alter the game balance in a huge way, and negate a lot of planning since you or the opponent can just pick up the perfect counter to the board. Then went on vacation.

If you want a casual game where you dont really care if you win or lose since every couple turns is just a gamble - great. If you want a strategy game where it is skill vs skill - not so much.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's a lot of good in the game and I enjoyed playing it a lot - if this was before the latest big patch, I'd definitely give the game a glowing recommendation. However, with a patch that was dropped very suddenly and labeled "experimental", features were introduced that make me enjoy the game a whole lot less.
I'm pointing out that the patch was called "experimental" because I feel like the devs were a bit disingenuous about their intents - reading between the lines of later communication, they made it quite clear the "experimental" changes were here to stay from the beginning, even against major push back of a significant part of the player community. That's not "experimental", that's "we're altering the deal, pray that we don't alter it any further".

I do understand that the game is in early access and it's legitimate for the devs to implement major changes in order to more closely achieve their vision, and I get that they cannot cater to *every* player - some people are bound to get disappointed when the game changes in ways they dislike and it's not something that can be avoided. However, communication goes a long way and I feel the concerns of people who dislike the changes aren't actually addressed beyond "this is how it is now, deal with it".

Anyway, I'm sure the game is fun for some players, but I'm disappointed about the direction, which is why I'm leaving a negative review. If I could give a "meh" rating, I probably would, as it's not all bad - just bad compared to what it was.\t\t\t\t\t
