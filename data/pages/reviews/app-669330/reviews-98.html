\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEpic. Best auto-battler already with tons of customization and option between rounds.

I found very few post about the quality of this game, and after 20+ hours, i have to tell: this game is a gem, and the best auto-battler i have every played.

The MMR works! As i climbed higher, i got more skilled players with very interesting builds and tactics - that rekt me, and thats the point of the mmr - to get you to a place, where u are forced to learn.

The game has BIG BRAIN MOVES, so when a plan click, it is so satisfiing. This is the "just one more match" type of game, trust me, absolutely addictive and fun. Even when u lose, you can learn interesting compositions and tactics, i do not get frustrated by this game at all, and its a rare saying in multiplayer.

2v2 - This is my absolutely favourite. Many people claimed, its chaotic... Whats chaotic about that? With a friend its an absolute blast, two 1v1 but with many ways to help each other - and if one of us win, the remaining forces go marching to help the mate. Works well, and feels epic. I would really consider 3v3 as well, becouse the system is there, if enough player will play the game, thats a must have!

Variety: i have to say... i almost never see the same comp/tactic on the next game, there are SO many different ideas and compositions to choose from. Zerg rush, heavy metal dreadnought fleet, shielded hacker army to steal you, afterburn toxic radioactive microbots, early fortresses.... so many things to learn and to choose from.

Some reviewer wrote the game has no variety, and he's got 3 hours in it... man... are u crazy? After 20 hours of playing iam still dont match the exact same tactic again and again. The key is in the builds, the tech tree, it can be very personalized and its adaptive. You have to adapt in every round, so you cannot just force your imaginery "meta" which is not exist here and its cool.

The tech tree is a nice START, i would say, there could be many more options to choose from, so there are way more space to develop on that area.

Right now... there is BALANCE. Yes... i said it... in a multiplayer game. Said no one ever before. But hey... there is no meta, everything works, its more about positioning and upgrades then the units picked. And its cool.

Also... in the first 20 hours i was NEVER told to go **** myself as in any other multi game. The chat is way more mature here, as of right now. Of course, that can be the result of the paid early access (which in my opinion should stay, i mean, the "pay once to play" )

------------
This game has so many areas to improve - but its already a totally fun, and balanced game with huge variety. Iam surprised to actually write this on EA, but i saw so many negative stuff here.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game in the lower tiers. Rock, paper, scissors format with upgrades and additional variable play options. That said, once you reach upper tier, they match you against people way above you, that play cheese...

Expect unbalanced game-play followed by relentless curb-stompings.... You may go a day where you win back to back matches. You may go a full week without winning any. Played 5 games a day for four days in a row, losing all 20 games back to back.... I don't mind losing, and I consider myself to be of average intelligence, but once something becomes overly skewed, it ceases to be fun. If you are a masochist filled with self loathing for yourself and others, you will love this game. Otherwise, invest your time and money elsewhere. This is one of those games, that after you've uninstalled it, you'll feel glad you did. All the best.\t\t\t\t\t
