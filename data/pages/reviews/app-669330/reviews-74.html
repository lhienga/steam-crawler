It may seem like simple rock-paper-sciccors at first, but unit positioning, unit upgrading, unit customization, flanking, measuring long-term vs short-term gains ... all of this can lead you to victory or defeat. Exceptional game for an absolute steal of a price. The learning curve can seem quite daunting and you will very likely lose a lot of your first matches, but don't lose heart! If you like tactical thinking in your games, pick this one up. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
