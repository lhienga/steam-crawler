\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDifferent autobattler, that doesn't leave most things up to random chance.
Every unit you get, is something you choose to unlock. You and your opponent each have access to the same type of units, however you are able to chose 4 techs for each of your units, that you can unlock throughout the game. These 4 techs are something you chose before you look for a game, so the same unit could have 4 totally different tech options between you and your opponent.
The only 2 factors that are random in this game, is your starting loadout, in which you get to pick between 4 options, and the start-of-round 4 options that are the same your opponent has to chose from. Everything else is solely up to you the player, which I for one really like about the game.\t\t\t\t\t
