The gameplay is simple enough for anyone to pick up - you set your units on the field, then they automatically march towards the opponent's forces. Mastering the gameplay is another matter entirely, as unit skills, flanking, and pseudo-rock-paper-scissors matchups between types of units add quite the skill ceiling to this game. You'll often find yourself needing to think 2 moves ahead, trying to predict and counter your opponent's reaction to your next move.

The only downside is that the game doesn't have much of a progression system, nor a lot of depth to keep you coming back. That said, I'd say the game is an easy 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t

There is undoubtedly, going to be some negativity due to fierce competition and balancing issues, but it seems like the developers are systematically working on balancing the game and removing sure-win exploits.

I greatly recommend this game, and it’s a bargain even at full price. This game is going to be huge in the near future.

Btw, I did not even notice it was Early Access, I thought the game was completed. The game is very polished, in its current state compared to most other Early Access titles.
