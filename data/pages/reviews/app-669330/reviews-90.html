\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a fantastic game and it may be again someday, at which point I'll change this review but recently they've decided to undercut a lot of the game's strategy with poorly balanced RNG that is now dividing the community. This was originally supposed to be a fun 3 week test, but they seem to have just made all those changes permanent now with only minor balance adjustments that fail to fix the underlying problems of the new system.

And lets be clear, you'll see positive reviews here of people who like the new changes and cite a stagnant meta and yeah at the top end things probably were stagnant and that is a problem that likely needed addressing, but this doesn't do that in a healthy way, this changes the game for everyone at every level of skill with unbalanced randomness.

Personally, I hope they either realize these changes are bad and revert or do a much more extensive balance pass so that these changes don't completely break the time/cost balance and randomly swing games, in either case I can't recommend this game anymore until they find a solution to this problem.

I don't normally change my reviews for early access games where such things happen and large changes can and should be expected over time, at least not prior to their actual release, but this one.. part of its that it is such a large change, not just a slow rebalancing that might eventually lead to one, but a sudden change that fundamentally changes the entire game, and part of it is that the devs are really failing to communicate on this one, they aren't listening to the community and addressing concerns, their next proposed balance pass fixes very few of the problems they've introduced, and they've bait and switched us by turning a 3 week test of multiple game altering things into a permanent feature instead of reverting after the test it done, evaluating, and then slowly introducing the features that they liked bit by bit in a balanced way.\t\t\t\t\t
