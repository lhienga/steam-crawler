\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe by far best aspect of this game for me is that Multiplayer is actually overwhelmingly fair and fun.

Because it is an autobattler and a well thought out one at that, you don't get paired up against
micro-management-monsters that make multiplayer unwinable. Since you don't need military grade stimpacks to lose slightly later in the match because Gamertron is at it again, you can actually learn the rules and win or lose based on the choices you make and slight rng mechanics.

Doesn't have much in terms of singleplayer at this time but multiplayer is viable. Bots can be quite a challenge too.

So yeah, good game and the price is low at full price.\t\t\t\t\t
