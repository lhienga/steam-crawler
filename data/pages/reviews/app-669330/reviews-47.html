\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is perfect for old timers like me, whose StarCraft twitch reflex micro days are over but still want that strategy game feeling. I would highly recommend. It captures the feeling of playing an RTS but with the twitch reflex micromanagement and base building element removed.

It's an autobattler so each round you invest your limited resources into building and positioning a few units which you get to add to the existing units on your side of the battlefield from the previous round. Since the fights auto resolve, it's all about positioning your units then hitting ready and watching the spectacle and explosions. Once a round ends, unit positions are reset so the units you picked and positioned carry over to each future round creating a bigger escalating battle. The game is all about assessing what your opponent added to their side of the battlefield then trying to out think them by picking good unit counters or upgrades to add to your side to give you the edge in the next round.

Games only last a couple of rounds so a whole match takes about 15 to 20 minutes.\t\t\t\t\t
