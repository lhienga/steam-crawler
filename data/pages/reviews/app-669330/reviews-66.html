\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe launch was promising, but a few months after the start of Early Access there is no progress in the game. The MMR system is atrocious and feels like a matchmaking system from the early days of multiplayer games. Every now and then there are updates, but there is nothing new, just minor tweaks to damage, range or other unit abilities. For a game in Early Access, this is far too little. Small changes are necessary for balance, but at such an early stage of Early Access you would think the developers would have more ideas than changing the speed of a unit by 1 metre per second. The user interface also needs some attention. The menus are clunky and not very intuitive.
Player numbers are also steadily declining. Where 4k players was normal in the first few weeks, we are now at less than 1.5k and I can't see the decline stopping until they fix the MMR system and bring new interesting content like new units or unit synergies.
I will probably take another look at Mechabellum next year. I hope the developers realise that the current state of the game may not be enough to keep most players motivated in the long run.\t\t\t\t\t
