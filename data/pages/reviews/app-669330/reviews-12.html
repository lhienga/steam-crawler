The latest update did change things, but not as much a people complain about. People saying it's only RNG are too lazy to adapt to new ways of evaluating the state of the game(which the devs said in the patch notes of the change will only last 3 weeks, reading is hard). Some people also hate change and don't want to lose their previous way of thinking.
The patch reminds me a lot of how drafting cards to make a deck and going to a build a constructed deck and playing in a tournament.(think magic the gathering, hearthstone, etc.) a lot of skill overlap but both scenarios require to evaluate the same cards very differently, the the ability to know WHY they are different is a valuable skill.
Predicting how your opponent will play is still valuable in this game, and you still get the same info as before, and now people are seeing way more units than before(more niche units are showing up which I find to be fun). It definitely favors people who play the game more and understand unit interactions. Maybe the change is worse for new/inexperienced players and might make the learning curve higher.(something for the devs to consider?) it is harder for newer people to judge what is going wrong if to many things show up on the field at once.
TLDR new patch is fun despite what people say. adjusting is not hard if you want to play. might be harder for new players to get used to unit interactions which more units showing up faster.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was really great until the last update. There was a bit of randomness that sometimes decided a game. But that was completely within reason. Now it seems to me that the strategic factor has been lost to some extent. You build up an army of allrounders in the first few rounds without following a specific strategy. Then you wait for the random unit cards. These largely determine the further course of the game. Long-term planning in advance is virtually impossible because you never know which units will be brought onto the field by the random unit cards. These cards have also increased the size of the armies many times over. For me personally, this has made it impossible to keep an eye on everything and strategically level up units or assign certain upgrades. As a result, the appeal of the game has been completely lost for me. I cannot recommend the game in its current form. After spending a significant amount of time with this game before and after the update, I have stopped playing it for the time being. I hope after this test phase there will be the possibility to choose between two different game modes; the classic mode and the "chaotic-fun" mode. Otherwise I don't think I will return to the game.\t\t\t\t\t
