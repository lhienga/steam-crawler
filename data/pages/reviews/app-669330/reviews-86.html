\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA highly addictive auto battler. I'm just shy of 100 hours playing this game and it's hard to believe I've actually put this much time in and will be putting in much more. It's an army of mechs destroying each other, what more do you want? It also has 2v2 so you can play with a friend!

The graphics are clean and the models are well done. The sound is filled with explosions and gunfire so there is a lot that gets lost in the chaos most of the time while some don't feel like it has the impact it should coming from the cannon that is being fired. Although you won't hear much of it as you watch your successful attack and ponder your next move or due you yelling at your marksmen for not targeting Wasp spawning Overlords or the Stormcaller RNG.

The unit list is fairly big and well balanced, each serve a specific role or general purpose. Proper planning, unit placement, countering, predictions, and unit upgrades are key to having a successful match. At this time I think there still needs to be some slight changes to power and balance. Just keep in mind that everything feels OP until you understand how to counter it.

It's worth it.
