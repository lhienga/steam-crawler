\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, I want to say that the price is maybe a little high (by really just a few dollars/euros/pounds/whatever).
The actual gameplay is good. I like anticipating what the enemy will throw at me and trying to defeat other people. I have a few problems though.
The UIs, tutorials, basically all of the text, though, have many errors in grammar, spelling, capitalization, which isn't a problem I would have with anything except for the fact that this is a game I paid money for, and the homies who wrote the text couldn't even bother correcting a spelling mistake. That's a mistake I would expect to see in a high school presentation, not a polished, ready-for-public experience.
Plus, I don't like the fact that the troops you can buy every turn are randomized. Why not just have all of them be purchasable every wave so you rely more on tactics and not RNG? I have no idea. Then again, this bad thing is also a good thing since it makes each game feel different, since you have to adapt to what you are provided with each turn.

Basically, the game itself is good, but it has some questionable choices that make it meh. Once again, like many, many people have said before me for many other games, I wish there was a mixed option because that would more accurately explain how I feel about this game. But still, it's above a 5, so i'd give it a 6 or 7/10.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasically, this game could be amazing, but there is too much chaos that isn't being balanced. This game actually might be impossible to be balanced, actually. Not to mention the player count is abysmally low.

There are too many things that are not being balanced. You can lose before you even start round 1, because you get an unfavorable matchup and then the momentum is too heavy against you to counter. Some units don't have direct counters, but counter tons of other units. (I'm looking at you stormcallers and skorpions.)

I could go on for days and days. Long story short. You're gonna be frustrated and it's not possible to get 'good' at this game because there are so many unbalanced 'cheese' tactics that make this game not chess. It's more like 'find the most overpowered cheese tactic that can't be countered early then snowball' over and over and over.

Oh, think you've figured out every unit and the counter? They can just buy a mobile beacon and change the movement of a unit and mess up the ENTIRE AI of your board.

Oh, you have a bunch of range units and the game is fairly balanced? The opponent gets offered a smoke card and now you lose HARD two rounds in a row and just lose the game. No way to remove it once its down.

Oh you're winning really hard based on actual counters? Game offers wildcard unit and the entire board swings away from you due to an unlucky choice.

Oh lets not forget that every unit doesn't have the same abilities. YOU HEARD THAT RIGHT. You can modify your units so that even if you and your opponent have the same unit, each of you can have vastly different abilities on those units. So you just straight wipe the board with them based on that mismatch.

Let me state that again. You can have abilities that are just flat, out, better, than your opponent even if you have the exact same board. And you wouldn't know that before the game even started.

The game needs to figure out how to make the 'auto-chess' fair and balanced, at least for ranked or competitive play. Right now, you're just gonna get frustrated over and over and over because you just wasted 20 minutes of your life constantly losing every round because you lost as soon as you started.\t\t\t\t\t
