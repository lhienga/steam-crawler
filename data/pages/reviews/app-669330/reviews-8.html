\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game used to be great and it actually developed very well - until the devs decided to completely mess up the gameplay by making it way too random. Now its just chaotic and mostly lacks the need for strategic decisions that kept me hooked for a long time.

Heavy RNG is not fun in a game that was all strategy and outplaying your opponent.

In my opinion the card system provided already a good portion of randomness before the update. The result of adding free unit cards are matches that require almost zero strategy or long-term planning because the game will probably just bail you or your opponent out of any mistakes that were made and would have been game-deciding prior to the update. Tweaking RNG a bit to potentially make games more diverse is one thing but these changes instantly cranked the game's randomness just up all the way to the maximum.

I could see the chaotic version as a separate game mode but it definitely should not replace the version that made people start playing the game in the first place. That's just a terrible decision.\t\t\t\t\t
