\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
+ Mech auto battler with a variety of mechs to choose from.
+ No pay2win mechanics. The only microtransactions are for cosmetics.
+ Some PVE content.

Cons:
- Unbalanced meta.
- Match making can and will match with people far outside of a player's MMR (due to low player count most likely). This leads to players that have spent hundreds of hours in the game going against people that are brand new to the game.
- No way to play this in a casual/unranked way in PVP.

Overall, I would say to skip this game unless you are desperate for a mech auto battler that you don't mind spending many hours to learn and master the meta to handle the players that have been playing since launch.\t\t\t\t\t
