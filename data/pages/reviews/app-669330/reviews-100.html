\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a fun game with a lot of strategy elements; resource management, unit placement, unit upgrades, reinforcements, bombardments and power-ups, counters for every unit. And it's pretty cool to watch two armies go at it. The tutorial and survival modes are good for teaching you how to play, and PvP has a strong learning curve and very high skill ceiling. They have a functioning 'Tournament Play' if you want to test yourself. You can even spectate games and try to predict who's going to win.

The dubious system of combat power and MMR is strange, as there's no real point to combat power, as they both seem to represent an estimate skill/experience of the player. There is no customising of the units beyond tech choices between matches; no skins or paint jobs like that.

It's worth the purchase if you enjoy auto-battlers with strategic depth, and if you're new to these kinds of games, just prepare to have your s*** rocked for a few matches before MMR sorts you out.\t\t\t\t\t

Every part of this game feels extremely thought out and there is a new update every couple weeks to make sure any slight imbalance gets addressed and they are constantly adding new features.

My only complaints thus far are that the insight system seems a little pointless (voting for who you think will win), and the credits that you earn in game at the moment have nothing to spend them on (the shop has yet to be implemented.) Hopefully these can be addressed in the near future.

This is a hidden gem of a game and is my favorite game of 2023.\t\t\t\t\t
