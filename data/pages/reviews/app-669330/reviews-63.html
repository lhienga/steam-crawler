\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to push the yes to recommend but there are still too many flaws to be addressed. Gameplay is symmetrical, which means variety game-to-game is all about the players. There are decent strategies and counter-strategies going on which is what the game is about. Unfortunately - games start to run together due to the lack of variety in matches (and overall lack of uniqueness in units - other than hacker they all move and shoot or move and melee). With the timer you don't have a lot of time to look about and get a feel for where you are at. Do not get me wrong - THE TIMER MUST STAY. It would be way too slow otherwise. My issue comes in that you are playing the same match over and over and over. When you play 5 in a row I find myself questioning "didn't i take that earlier?" as the matches are mostly the same.

While the mechanics seem stable, this game is rough until they can do more to differentiate the feel match-to-match. Also - I am not a fan of the manual pathing mechanic. It can be used as a differentiator between good and bad players but it ruins the atmosphere of the game. I mean what squad of units is going to chase the retreating (or non-engaging) units rather than engage what is attacking them? Not a bad gameplay mechanic but it makes your units feel stupid.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a fun game that can be sometimes frustrating. The main issue beyond balance issues and smurf, is that its very unoptimized.

When playing on a 780m (RDNA3 iGPU) at 1080p, it barely does 45fps (and that drops to 30 with more units). There seems to be major issues with the poly count or rendering, as static "pre-battle" even results in such bad performance. You may say, but, that is a iGPU. Yes, but its the same iGPU you find in the Ally and other devices.

For comparison, a game like World Of Warships runs at 1440p 2k, with mix of low/medium/high and still hits 60~70fps permanently. While its not a big company, you see their focus being a bit too much on the monetization aspect (shop, game currencies, skins all linked to real world money).

I feel this aspect is slowly starting to hurt the game in the long run.\t\t\t\t\t
