This game could be great, but the current balance just makes it an obnoxious mess.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDEV RESPONSE EDIT: SO HAPPY TO HEAR SKINS ARE PURCHASABLE WITH IN-GAME CREDITS. This is a positive feature and you should absolutely display this somewhere where its easy to see. For those that dont own the game, the cosmetic store refreshes every 24 hours. The skin shop displays 3 skins that have small timers in the top left of their icon and a USD price tag at the bottom. One would assume that this timer is when that skin shop item refreshes and displays a new item, but according to the devs this is when the skin becomes available for purchase with in game credits. Super confusing BUT I'm glad to see units CAN be bought with in game currency.

Actually incredibly frustrated to leave this wonderful game a negative review. This is a game that struggles with player retention due to lack of progression, so to entice their player base to stay engaged they introduced a credit store. Credits are earned by leveling up past the default tech level cap- basically prestige = cosmetics. GREAT. ABSOLUTE W ADDITION. However, only a few months into the cosmetics store update they changed it so credits can be obtained with irl currency instead of playing, AND removed the ability to EARN UNIT SKINS. I REPEAT YOU CAN ONLY GET UNIT SKINS BY PAYING REAL MONEY. Player retention is decimated.

There's no incentive for casual players to keep playing as you no longer really earn anything.. Credits are only used to by little emoji's and player borders/icons. Which makes no sense anyways because your player icon just defaults to your steam profile pic instead of their in-game purchasable pngs that are super generic. Competitive players will stay for the competitive matchmaking, but the demographic for that in an auto-battler is pretty small. Fun game but honestly I don't think the devs should be rewarded for trying to milk their already small audience for more money, when their game is already fairly priced for the content it provides.

2/16/2024 EDIT

Switching my review to negative because of the "Free Unit Card/Chaos" Update. This update has completely sucked the core elements of the game that I enjoyed out of it. The game is no longer a slow buildup of strategy, but more a match where the victor swings wildly from round to round and game play is focused on reacting to what free units are picked. When my opponent can drop in 7+2 units, but I can only buy 2 to counter per round, I better just hold on until the next free unit cards drop so I can get my dramatic counter. The game is good if you like a more RNG focused approach to strategy games, but thats just not for me. This game used to shine because it had minimal RNG elements for an autobattler, now it feels more like a less polished version of TFT.\t\t\t\t\t
