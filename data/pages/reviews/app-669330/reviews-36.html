\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPRE-February review:
Excellent up-and-coming autobattler. Players each play up to 3 units per round, so the armies build slowly and in reaction to each other. Matches are decided by who is better at outsmarting, planning and understanding the units. A thinking gamer's game. My personal favorite Turn-Based Strategy game of Jan 2024, with 100 hrs played in that month alone. ~85% review score on the Steam page, and the discussion board is full of players discussing strategy and proclaiming the game excellent. Recommended!

POST-February review:
The devs dropped an awful patch that destroyed the meta of the game (before going on vacation, no less!) and intentionally made the matches dependent on RNG luck, "for fun". Players still play up to 3 units in a round but also sometimes get up to 6 additional units or 1-2 incredibly powerful units. The game is now about waiting for the RNG cards to arrive, and using all your money to snowball the giant free cards. It's not often advisable to try to react to your what your opponent is doing, instead now it is better to wait for the RNG cards to completely change the board for you. The Steam Store page rating is now at 54% and dropping, and the Discussion board is a wall of people screaming that the game is now awful.

I've decided to change my review to NOT RECOMMENDED for now. It really pains me to do this, as I still have high hopes for this game. But I just don't enjoy it now. After the match, I'm just mad. Watching a really great game that I'm excited about, turned to a mobile-app mess in one day, is really sad and frustrating.

I will definitely change my review to RECOMMENDED if the devs can figure a way out of this mess they've created. But as it is, this game is basically ruined.\t\t\t\t\t
