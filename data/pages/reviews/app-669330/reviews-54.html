\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly this is one of the best tactics pvp games I've played.

It captures all of the cool strategy/counter-strategy rock-paper-scissors stuff you always wanted from classic RTS games like Age of Empires or Starcraft, but were too ass at microing your villagers to maximize unit production.

This game always makes you wonder how you can counter certain comps that whooped you, and I've really enjoyed the balance overall. Even stuff that seems busted has an answer and it's fun to customize units with their specific upgrades to suit your particular playstyle. Really enjoying 2v2s, especially.


edit: As for the "controversial" new update... the whole game is adapt to your opponent, and I think this is just more of that. Adapt to the adaptation to your comp, etc. It's just some units now instead of bombing runs; it's the same stuff. Maybe they could tone down the high levels on the units or stop unit unlocks, otherwise I'm pretty OK with it.\t\t\t\t\t
