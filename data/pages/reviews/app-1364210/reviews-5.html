\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have honestly been looking for a game like this to be developed for absolutely ages, and considering this game is still in early access, it's still a damn good game.

Firstly, you have complete control. You can select your local town, and recreate real world services and routes, even following the real world timetables, or you can create your own routes and timetables, and create 'What if' scenarios.

Secondly, you have complete customisation control over the appearance of your buses. There is an in-game editor that allows you to select the base colour of the bus and the roof, as well as an additional 'Drawing' tool where you may draw your own shapes / hand-write words onto your buses. Failing that, there is a steam guide for obtaining vehicle templates and creating your liveries in the photo editor of your choice.

Thirdly, Mods + Steam workshop support - If you're like me and want to see British vehicles and liveries in game, steam workshop is your go-to place. There are many vehicles available, from the Optare Solo to the Enviro 400 MMC, from Arriva to Stagecoach.

With all the fun stuff out of the way, the management aspect of the game is very good. You have to hire Customer Service staff, Engineers, Cleaners, Drivers, Researchers - And you can choose what their wages are - and they will become unhappy if their wages are too low. You must ensure that your vehicles are clean and fuelled up ready for the next morning. You can also send your staff on training courses to improve their 'Skill' level, as well as having full control over 'Short Distance' 'Single' 'Day' and 'Week' ticket prices.

At the time of posting this review, I had only bought the game 24-48 hours ago, and have already obtained 11 hours of play time, which yes, perhaps isn't much, but it would be a lot higher had I not had other life commitments. I have to admit I was sceptical about purchasing this game at first: I didn't feel a management game of this nature would be worth the price - I was completely wrong.

I really look forward to the future development of this game, and look forward to seeing what may be introduced in future updates.

* Despite being in early access, I have not yet currently experienced any huge/game-breaking bugs.\t\t\t\t\t
