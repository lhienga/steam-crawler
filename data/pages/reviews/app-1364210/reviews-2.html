\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCity Bus Manager is a game that makes the hearts of bus enthusiasts beat faster. PeDePe GbR has created a solid management game that is a lot of fun and will keep you hooked for many hours. As a fan of simulation games and public transport in particular, I was looking forward to this game and for the most part I was not disappointed.

The graphics are appropriate for a game of this type.

The actual gameplay is where City Bus Manager shines. The management aspect of the game is in-depth and offers a variety of challenges. From buying new buses and setting timetables to managing staff and dealing with unforeseen events such as traffic jams or repairs, there's always something to do. The financial aspects of the game are well balanced and you need to plan carefully to ensure that the bus company remains profitable.

Unfortunately, there are a few weak points. The user interface could be more intuitive in places, especially for newcomers, and there are some translation errors that can make it difficult to understand. The AI of the passengers and other road users could also be improved to make the experience even more realistic.

Overall, however, City Bus Manager by PeDePe GbR earns a solid rating of 4.1 out of 5 stars. It's a fun game for anyone who enjoys management and simulation, especially if you have an interest in public transport. With a few small improvements, it could be even better.
