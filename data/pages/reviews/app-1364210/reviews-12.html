The fact of being able to download the map of your city and create your own lines is very interesting, as far as the game is concerned, after about twenty hours, I can say that it becomes very repetitive.
The graphic aspect is really embarrassing, buses that overlap in the depot, people move like robots, etc. They should have added some element of more realism. The point is that when you understand the game mechanics, like a bus fleet of 100 vehicles, you will spend most of the time waiting for them to return to fix / wash / clean and refuel.

In short, fun at first but then falls flat. To be tried at a much lower price than the one requested

At first it was alright, had a few issues but it was playable. The game got more and more laggy as the game progressed and it was starting to get unplayable. If you like buses and / or management, chances are you'll like this. Well done to the team.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was quite skeptical when I first found this game when I saw Aerosoft as a publisher, given my experience with their titles being "okay-ish, but not great". But I bought it and... didn't like it in the first early versions as it was more of a second work than a game. But now, the development is going nicely and the devs are actually listening to the player base. You can even report bugs and suggest features in-game. Selected features will then go through voting by the players and the most requested actually get implemented. It's really joyful to see an Early Access game with a healthy development and devs communicating with the community (looking at you, LOTUS!).

The gameplay is still lacking some features to reduce overwhelming amount of micromanagement when your company gets bigger, but I am not afraid to say that all this will be fleshed out soon. You can now create new buses from scratch and upload them to Workshop, which is amazing. And the amount of vehicles already released is astonishing.

While the game is now basically still going to be interesting for transport enthusiasts and the "common folk" will find the game too tedious, I will still go out and recommend this. I am kind of addicted to the game at this point.\t\t\t\t\t
