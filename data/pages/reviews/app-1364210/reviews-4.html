\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, this game has a lot of potential thanks its simple and approachable nature. Its a good game, though its value to you will massively depend on your personal interest in management and/or transportation, I imagine those lacking my interest will quite quickly become disillusioned after pressing the clean and wash bus button for hundredth time, and before I get comments I know its automated after a certain milestone. Personally, I think that the best thing for the game given its small development team, would be to further expand the workshop of the game to be able to mod more than just adding bus models. Clearly, the devs know of the importance of modding from experience, but if the game wants to maintain relevance this is must. This could see the rise of map mods, custom driver-swaps, mass repaints, and so much more. As if it wasn't for the replay-ability of game across the world using OSM data then I wouldn't recommend it given its price.

TL:DR:
Its a good game, if you like transport and management you probs enjoy it, I think modding needs more access, its replayble though at £33.50 for everything its a bit dear but acceptable for the niche its in. Thanks for reading.\t\t\t\t\t
