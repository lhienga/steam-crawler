\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt was a tough call on if to recommend or not recommend this game.
Early impressions were good. Allowing a player to use real life street maps (especially with most transport games ignoring Australia, and focusing on Europe or the USA), ability to paint buses, hire staff and allocate them to duties.
The developers were also pretty quick initially on in-game bugs, such as some buses stuck in the depot or articulated buses appearing sunken into the road.
Unfortunately, in the end I can't recommend the game due to performance issues.
After playing for about 10-12 hours (and playing the game well), doubling the size of my depot, expanding my coverage area and hiring 70 staff, the game started to lag, badly (especially on 3x speed).
This made is frustrating when trying to select things in the depot menu, having to wait more than 10 seconds for the system to catch up - similar when trying to get simple things done like a bus cleaned.
The game has potential, and was addictive early, but performance issues limit the enjoyment.\t\t\t\t\t
