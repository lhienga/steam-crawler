\r\n\t\t\t\t\t--Edit--

As promised I came back to have a look at this, it seemed quite promising with all the positive reviews and there has been a bunch of updates.

However, I did notice an awful lot of these reviews seem to be from brand new steam account which is interesting.

Not willing to try it again just yet, not with the price it's still at.

--Original review--

First impressions is what counts for me when it comes to EA games, seeing the area I live etc as part of the game was exciting at first, also had some fun designing the bus however that's where the fun ended.
  • Guide at the start is not really a tutorial into the game, it's just telling you everything you should buy with no explanation. Also doesn't tell you where X items are. In ways this could be a positive but a tutorial should be informative and more than just "Buy X,Y and Z"

  • Route creating, honestly had no idea how to use it (effectively) the only tips you are given is that a route should be 10+ stops. Doesn't tell you how far apart they should be, how big the area of influence a single bus stop is (how far people will walk to it), doesn't tell you if the bus stop is in a good spot...nothing.

  • Kinda sad that you can't follow the bus on it's route or see anymore of your local area but maybe I am asking too much there so fair enough.

  • After route is setup the next goal is to get X amount of passengers, zero information on if my route is decent until the driver does it and I Find he picked up about 8 people from what I imagine would be a pretty busy route (town centre, shopping, doctors etc on the route). Great, so I guess my route sucks? Honestly no idea, didn't get told or given any advice.


Anyways, could be great but those are the negatives I got whilst playing which made me stop and just have a nosy at steam discussions which is where I found some questions (Like the point I said above about the area of influence of a single stop) just going completely unanswered by the dev, some that they'd replied to asking a question and then just never returned to the thread again leaving people still asking.

Lack of dev responses to pretty important questions was the final nail for me, i'll be refund for now and at this time saying negative but it has the potential to be good but yeah, a dev that can't answer what should be simple questions for him is a major red flag for me.

Maybe in the future? We'll see, but at the current price (even with %25 off) I can't really justify keeping it currently.\t\t\t\t\t
