\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is superior to Minecraft as a sandbox exploration game. The former has much more content that the latter doesn't have, for example:
+ producing poo from a toilet and use it to craft fertilizers
+ flying kites
+ secret special world generations that isn't restricted to a single, outdated version like Minecraft
+ vanity items
+ Halloween and Christmas themed enemy swarms
+ various weapon types from yoyos and sentries to G U N S
+ a rod that allows teleportation
+ unique rewards for fighting certain extremely hard enemies
+ a special fluid that can transmute or decraft most items
+ a sprayer that lets you create and destroy biomes as you wish

My only complaint is that the game can sometimes be grindy, for example I had to farm for Chaos Elementals, each of which has 1/500 chance to drop the teleportation rod I mentioned above. It took me approx. 1800 kills iirc to finally obtain one. I also saw complaints about having to search the wiki to know what to do. I can relate to that, because it took me a few days of trying to farm Chaos Elementals to realise from reading the wiki that they spawn only if I'm constantly moving.

Overall, Terraria's not perfect, but is still quite an awesome game. 9.5/10, would recommend it to other people.\t\t\t\t\t
