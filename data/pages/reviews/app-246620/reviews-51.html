\r\n\t\t\t\t\tcharging almost 20 dollars for a mobile game port that originally cost a dollar not including dlc is pretty absurd. all that aside, i DID enjoy this game, played it for a while, until i realised the different types of diseases are pretty deceptive, since as other reviewers have said there is one single strategy that works, and the only thing the different categories/types of diseases do is make completing that strategy easier/harder.

its not worth it to buy it on pc, its a good mobile game, but a mediocre repetitive pc game. its heavily reliant on rng which is the only thing that makes different playthroughs different. if it didnt have community created challenges this might be one of the most boring games ive ever played (once you realise you are literally doing the same stock run over and over).

there is no real strategy making in normal gameplay, since trying something "different" (not too possible) results pretty quickly in a loss, or a long drawn out loss. a loss nonetheless.

buy the mobile version.\t\t\t\t\t
