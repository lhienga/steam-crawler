This game is mostly good, although there are some downsides I'd like to mention:
  • There isn't a massive amount of depth to this game, with most disease types/scenarios being beatable with the same or similar strategies.
  • Most disease types are almost identical, usually differing only in a single ability tree.
  • This issue seems more egregious in the game's cure mode, with every disease playing almost exactly the same way.
  • The cure mode itself seems quite unbalanced. There have been multiple times in which I have achieved the highest rank on a disease type despite tens of millions of people dying and my authority dropping below 10, which doesn't feel rewarding.
Despite all of this I would still definitely recommend this game to anyone who hasn't tried it. Playing through all of the disease types for the first time is quite enjoyable and you will likely get at least 10 hours of playtime before the game starts losing its charm.\t\t\t\t\t
