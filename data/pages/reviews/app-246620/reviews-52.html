\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou play as a disease, with your goal being to wipe out humanity completely. If one person in the world is left surviving, you lose!

The game is quite strategic, allowing for a multitude of different approaches to the various diseases you can play as. As more people become infected or die from your disease, you gain 'DNA' to spend to 'mutate' your disease to help spread it, make it more deadly, or both!
You have the ability to adjust your difficulty, which is crucial to unlocking different achievements. There are also a lot of different scenarios available to play, which add a fun story to the game play. There is also multiplayer available where you can either work together or compete with other players.
There is also a mode called 'The Cure' which came out around COVID. This mode is the exact opposite of what you would normally do. Instead of destroying humanity via disease, you're attempting to save it. It is quite a bit different than the main game, but has many similarities at the same time. Overall, it's a different experience worth trying.


8.5/10- With a bit of a learning curve, the game is a tad complex but quite fun and rewarding once you develop different strategies to wreck humanity. Quite a bit of game play available, and I always find myself coming back every now and again to tackle different achievements.\t\t\t\t\t
