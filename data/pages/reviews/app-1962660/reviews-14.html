\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of those games that makes me wish there was a "mixed" option for Steam reviews. Buy only at a VERY steep discount.

Don't get me wrong, I found this game fun and the story mode to be great. The issue is that the most fun parts of it, and as such the parts you'll be spending the most time in, are free-to-play anyway. As such, for £60 it absolutely is not worth it, especially given that the game has microtransactions up the wazoo as well. They're not required to enjoy the game by far, but ActiBlizz waves them in your face as much as they can.

Oh, and do not bother trying to play Raids if you have fewer than 2 friends who play this game. 3-way communication is a MUST for those, so don't even try it with randos.

Addendum (04/01/2024): MWII has effectively been sidelined in its entirety in favour of MWIII - so more or less, this game's only worth picking up for the story and Raids now.\t\t\t\t\t
