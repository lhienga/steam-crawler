\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat COD game. I was super excited for this one to release as soon as it was announced as I loved the 2019 Modern Warfare for its campaign and the bit of the multiplayer I played. I've got to be honest though, I was let down by the campaign, although beautiful graphically and fun to play I found its story lacking and its gameplay somewhat unpolished but what I didn't expect was for me to get addicted to the multiplayer. I had always played the previous Modern Warfare's for their singleplayers but this one was different for me, I spent little time in the story and most of my time playing the multiplayer and co-op with my buddies. The amount of content this game has is staggering, although the quality isn't always perfectly distributed you cannot complain that there isn't enough to do or that there isn't something here for everyone. The co-op missions and raids are super intense and really fun. I don't personally spend quite that much time playing Warzone and DMZ (they are cool just not really my thing). Regular 6v6 is just awesome and I have really enjoyed maxing all the weapons in the game, this is where the majority of my playtime has been put and likely will continue to be put. I will also say that the live service has been pretty consistent (when compared to other AAA games) and its always refreshing to come back and see what they've added. I like that they have given a lot of customization options with the skins they have added to the game but a lot of them break immersion for me (I have always somewhat viewed COD as a military sim) which is fine, just annoying and a minor complaint of mine. I see myself coming back to this game for a couple of years to come (I don't buy every COD and I will enjoy grinding to max rank over time). While the game is pricy I actually surprisingly think the price is worth it, you are getting a lot of game for your money and if you play even somewhat frequently I imagine that will be the case for you as well. You can't see it on the review but I currently have around 350 hours in MW2. I also love that the newer COD games are being put on steam which is my preferred launcher. This is Bravo-06 going dark.\t\t\t\t\t
