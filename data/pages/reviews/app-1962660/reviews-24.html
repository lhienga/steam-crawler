\r\n\t\t\t\t\tModern Slowfare II

If you ever buy this game, buy it only for the campaign and while it is on sale. DO NOT UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES PAY FULL PRICE FOR THIS GAME!

This game's multiplayer is designed with slow gameplay and campy with guns that visually kick harder than my 06 civic stalling in first gear when I don't give it gas. Modern Slowfare II was supposed to be a massive improvement from 2019, but the only things that genuinely improved was the general map design, and the first-person animations.

Here's what sucks about this game:
  • Update requires restart
  • Slow movement, no slide cancelling
  • Guns with high visual recoil
  • Abysmal user interface (completely different from older COD, Hulu lookin')
  • Riot shield with overkill
  • No dead silence perk (Moving around sounds like you have concrete bricks attached to your feet)
  • SBMM/EOMM (which will likely never be addressed for this game)
  • Shadowbanning system that can completely kill any enjoyment you may have with the game
  • Worse graphics than Modern Warfare 2019 (this may be art style/authoring idk, 2019 just hits different)
  • Worse performance than Modern Warfare 2019 (with not great visual uplift)
  • Timed release perk system (You don't have all perks active when starting a match, they unlock during the match)
  • No red dots on minimap when firing an unsuppressed weapon
  • Call of Duty HQ (since MWIII released, you have to launch MWIII COD HQ to launch MWII)
  • Ignorant developer (Infinity Ward doesn't listen to the community, nor do they care about it)
  • At this point, no more major updates to fix these issues (Infinity Ward doesn't care and they didn't even care when this game was current either)
  • Boring multiplayer announcers (I'm reaching at this point, but this bothers me)
  • Terrible cosmetics (This is of course very subjective, but they threw the entire art style of the game out the window for the sake of bundles, which is apparently content)

Here's whats good:
  • Better general map design
  • First-person weapon animations are a genuine step up from Modern Warfare 2019
  • Scorestreak option (over killstreak)
  • Firing range (super basic, but better than nothing)
  • Revamped gun camo system with seriously relaxed mastery camo grind (unlock a camo and use it on any weapon, there are only 4 base camos for a single weapon and 4 mastery camos)
  • Fun campaign

In short, this game sucks and there is no reason to buy it at this point for multiplayer.
