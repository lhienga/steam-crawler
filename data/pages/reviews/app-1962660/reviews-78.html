I love Call of Duty, though priorities need to be put in place. I hope Activision reads this, because I have been a large player of Call of Duty, since World and War. The campaign is great though, with a good amount of Easter Eggs. DMZ was a great addition. I just wish the multiplayer aspect could have the same love and care that it deserves.
