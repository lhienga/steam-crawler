\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn MWII, only playing tactically stealth is your choice!
Campaign
Mediocre plotline, worst level design, no memorable music track.
  • Prepare to experience a level of Warzone vehicle training session with virtually no BGM and annoying QTE to jump across ground vehicle tops failing multiple times.
  • Or have fun with junk collection levels to play hide-and-seek with heavy armored enemies - your only option is to make traps and not using your knife seen in the ending cutscene but create a make-shift one with scraps! - and also their dropped weapon only giving you 3 ammo with no reserves!
  • Price to Gaz: I'm the Captain, you're the Sergeant. You go down in the huge sandbox and carry on the mission alone! I give you limited Sniper assist!
  • No significant graphic improvements. Could only say the Amsterdam part is great.

Co-op (with Raid)
No fun. Only way is tactical and stealth. Friend standed no longer with multiple failures triggering alarms of enemy forces. 2 player limit (3 for Raid) is a great decrease in fault tolerance.

Multiplayer
Core 6v6
ABSOLUTE TRASH.
  • Movement - a CoD:Ghost experience of sliding, a stiffing dolphin dive (tho no fall damage this time), a huge ADS penalty from attachments, a slightly annoying ledge-hanging. Oh, forgot to mention you can finally swim in an IW CoD game (aside from Ghost).
  • Weapon - still enormous visual recoils. Fast-draw pistol grip is a good addition.
  • Map - Still has large as hell maps, but overall slightly better than MW2019's.
  • Leveling up - You're forced to max level all launch weapons to unlock all attachments, but I view it as a benefit - it decreases the overall weapon level count significantly. You no longer need to unlock the same - maybe never used - optics, lasers, muzzles over and over again. And it also means DLC weapons at their own are easy to max level. The result is one single weapon only have like 18 levels average, around 28 at most. A bit sad they didn't quite work out the weapon platform tree.
  • Challenges - the easiest ultimate weapon camo unlock ever in modern CoD history! and is also my very first ultimate camo.
  • Visuals - no visible improvement from MW2019
  • SBMM and network - Booooo

Ground War / Invasion
Invasion is kind of OK with the addition of AI players. Sadly unlike Zombies, killing AI won't give you another set of challenge rewards and does not count towards MP challenges. Only useful when leveling up weak weapons.\t\t\t\t\t
