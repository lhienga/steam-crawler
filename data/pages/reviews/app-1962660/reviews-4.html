\r\n\t\t\t\t\t=================
MODERN WARFARE II
=================

Campaign: A fun single player experience, continue the story with characters that kick ass! Many people say the plot of MWII doesn't really make much sense, but you can have a genuinely great time in the campaign.

WARNING! The armored soldiers will mess you UP. DO NOT GO HARDER THAN REGULAR DIFFICULTY! (unless you like CBT)

Multiplayer: Loved this, slower movement was nice. I know people hated it but I honestly enjoyed being able to react to half of the gunfights in this game without someone slide cancelling or bunny hopping everywhere. Weapon progression is actually enjoyable and not grindy with 70+ levels PER WEAPON.

Co-Op: A massive step-up from MW2019 in this regard, with an offering of spec ops missions and even Co-Op RAIDS. Shame you can't queue solo, but great fun with a buddy or two.

==================
WARZONE SECTION
==================

DMZ: Goated as hell. Its a crime that DMZ didn't get a second year of support. DMZ was Call of Duty's first (as far as im aware) and pretty successful take on the extraction shooter genre, as it was fairly forgiving after unlocking more insured slots and other upgrades.

That being said, it isn't all great. DMZ after they discontinued live service support on DMZ has left it in a terrible place. It's almost impossible to find friendly people in game and you'll almost always be gunned down instantly as a solo player.

They re-used a lot of the old missions over the seasons, but at least the rewards for doing these missions were new. They introduced quite a few factions across the life cycle of DMZ (Crown, REDACTED, Phalanx, Shadow Company). The post-launch support was pretty great, as Infinity Ward introduced new overhauls to the progression inside and outside of the match.

I miss DMZ, I miss what it was. Ever since Season 2, it just kept getting more aggressive and PVP focused.\t\t\t\t\t
