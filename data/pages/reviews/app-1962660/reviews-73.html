\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought the base edition of the game on sale, figuring that if I liked it enough, I could just upgrade to the vault edition for $30 later, since that's what was listed in the in-game store. A little pricey, but still, it could be worse (considering the price of literally anything these days). Imagine my surprise, then, when I enjoyed the campaign and went back to upgrade, only to find that it now costs 100 DOLLARS TO UPGRADE. Activision? Are you kidding me? This is CALL OF DUTY. It's really not worth that much, especially considering what the upgrade entails. I don't play warzone or DMZ, because I don't like battle royale games, but I do play the base multiplayer on occasion, and I thought that the Red Team 141 skins would be nice to have, especially since I love the original MW games/characters so much. However, I don't care about the warzone operators or real-people-based skins, I don't care about the battle pass, and I don't care about tier skips or double xp. I just want Soap, Ghost, and Price with cool-looking masks. Is that really worth 100 dollars? Absolutely not.

Such a dumb move, making everything for a franchise that's already under such hate so expensive. This is just punishing the franchise's fans, charging outrageous amounts for the story content, and leaving the warzone game (Activision's focus) free for those that don't actually care about COD, and just want to play another cookie-cutter battle royale. The change has killed the game and taken the magic of what COD used to be away entirely. I yearn for the old days, when the story was the main focus and multiplayer was an extension of it, rather than coming up with new gimmicks to make the game more profitable, hiding behind the argument that 'the game is free! It's the only way we make money!'. The campaign isn't long enough to justify a $70 price tag, and the extra crap they tacked on for the vault edition shouldn't be another $30 either.

So, while I DO recommend the campaign (or rather, I would recommend it for anyone with a PC that can actually run it, since the game is so poorly optimized. If not, just watch a play through on youtube), I don't recommend spending more money on this than you can handle. It's certainly not worth what they're asking for. And, if you are serious about it, and you want the skins and extra stuff alongside the campaign, don't be cheap like me, and just buy the vault edition off the bat, but only if it ever goes on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
