\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey unceremoniously did CoD:MWII dirty and don't care if the game is hurt by their decision. Even the now's CoD:MWIII has one of the worst reveal trailer possible and to think CoD:MWII's reveal were the last of good times. Looks like we're back in the dark ages for Call of Duty franchise. To put it simply, I will not be buying another new CoD title from now on.

Now let me paste my original review's text content for CoD:MWII (now rebranded as simply CoD) so the message doesn't get lost to time. Bottom part will remain unedited. Further edits will remain on top of the quoted review in separate paragraph.

They disabled family sharing because there were players abusing this Steam feature to avoid getting banned by cheating in the game on their main accounts. How is this even a thing if there's a "functional anti-cheat system", developed from the start by Activision themselves? My brother can't even play this game anymore, which sucks because he enjoyed this game as much as I did. Please bring Family Sharing back as soon as possible and actually fix the cheating problem once and for all.

EDIT: It's ironic that the first thing you do to play multiplayer is "accept the terms of the fair multiplayer experience for all players" before being able to actually hop into the game. This proves that it serves no other purpose than threatening the normal players. That's cool that Activision is making players aware of the ban conditions, however cheaters will always be breaking those rules, and I hate them for that because they took from me the best feature available for this game.

EDIT2: Activision, why do you use aggressive marketing on your games to promote one game mode which has yet to arrive? The current game title is making a lot of confusion. Original title should be brought back after the consequential update so it's clear it's the latest installment in Call of Duty video game series. There's CoD: Warzone 2.0 game page already so why Activision thought it'd be a good idea to make this game's title obnoxiously long?

EDIT3: They've broke symbolic linking so I can't use my external drive for better performance while using the same folder for accessing game files. Shame.

EDIT4: They haven't decided yet to add static crosshairs option back to the game. They've introduced such feature a while back somewhere on December or January I think and this feature is gone since then. I hate the motion sickness that the floaty crosshair gives me. Bring this feature back, Activision. They brought back static crosshairs in S3.

EDIT5: While they fixed one issue, they introduced another problem. They removed the "Upgrade to Vault Edition" option as of Season 3. Instead, they charge you a full price for the "upgrade" while before S3, you just had to pay extra $30 in order to upgrade your game edition. I just wanted to do this on S3 but looks like I am not going to play this game until such upgrade makes the return.

EDIT6: Okay, Season 4 just made everything worse. There are no gamemode filters now, where are they? Why am I forced to play gamemodes that I don't want to? Alright, they just updated playlists and brought back gamemode filters.

EDIT7: I am honestly disappointed and disguisted by Activision's decision of Faceoff gamemode removal. I liked that personal 3v3 encounter style without killstreaks which also was fast paced, became quickly my one of favorite gamemodes of all time in MWII, where is it now? Not to mention the now unpresent 3rd person gamemode.

EDIT8: The new weapon, Tempus Razorback, is a wonderful toy, executed really well. However I have one issue with it - It's with the charms. First, why does the charm float slightly above the weapon instead of being attached to the weapon? In first person it's especially distracting. Second, the charm on Tempus Razorback may clip into the gun when held in certain angles, when the charm is supposed to jiggle on the gun, not sink into the gun while jiggling. Fix your weapon because it's good and ruined by this customizability issue. They fixed charms for Tempus Razorback as of Season 5.

EDIT9: Oh ok... so this is interesting. After many, many Seasons, in the Season 5, they merged Warzone and MWII back together... however - now the title is dumbed down so much they renamed the game to just Call of Duty. This makes impossible to tell which game exactly do I play but requires from the player to look at the game details in order to tell which game is currently played. Thank you Activision, very cool.

EDIT10: Alright so my theory has proved to be true - They are going to make Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III a large DLC rather than its own game. Good job Activision at making the worst decisions possible which hurt this somehow good game in its own way.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been on and off this game from launch all the way until mid-season 3. After that, I played the game on a daily basis, except for mid-season 5, when my PC broke. I put 400+ hours into this game. I did Orion, Ghoulie, and 90% of the calling cards for MWII MP. I beat campaign and spec ops. This game is by far the worst modern warfare game, but it is a mid-cod game and is better than BO4 and Vanguard, two of the worst cods, in my opinion. Besides the ♥♥♥♥ launch, like I said, this game is mid. Here are my pros and cons.

Cons:
- Perk system: 2/4 of them are based on time, which was stupid; no one asked for this.

- The weapon level system was dumb as ♥♥♥♥; I don't want to level up my shotgun for a red dot.
on my AK. This feature is annoying at first, but once you level up a category, family, and/or all weapons it's fine

- SBMM

- Servers: from seasons 4–5, this game would crash my whole system basically whenever I would get any sort of latency or connection issue and was extremely annoying.
- Optimization: again, from seasons 4–5, this game ran like ♥♥♥♥. I went from playing at all high settings 90-100fps to putting everything to all low except for textures to get the same fps. This game looks worse than 2019, and in that game, I get better performance at max than this ♥♥♥♥.

- Spawn system: Whoever added squad spawn is a moron. If I'm playing domination and I cap A and B I own those areas, and only me and my team should spawn at A and B; enemies shouldn't spawn next to me. If I'm in a gunfight with the enemy teammate, the ♥♥♥♥ should spawn at C, not next to me, not at A or B.

- Adding the Lachman shroud and ISO 9mm was a lazy addition; they could've been attachments for the ISO 45 and Lachman Sub, not a new gun.

- Lack of 2x xp weekends and doing a 24-hour one was dumb as ♥♥♥♥.

Pros:
- Ranked
- Weapon tuning
- Maps
- Consistent events and content
- Gunplay
- Movement after the update it got in season 4 or 5
- The number of weapons and operators

From launch till now, the game has improved, but this by far has been the most lazy and least cared for. cod I've seen this franchise for years. This game is just a cash grab. Was playing 400+ hours worth it? No, was Orion worth it? Yeah, was spending money on this game worth it? No, except for that, it carries over to MW3. This is not a game I plan on going back to. I was extremely hyped for this game because I loved the OG MW trilogy, and I ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ loved MW2019, and this game being a sequel to 2019 had me hyped. but it was a huge disappointment to me. I lost a lot of trust in the infinity ward because of this game, and I am keeping my expectations low. when it comes to them for now on.\t\t\t\t\t
