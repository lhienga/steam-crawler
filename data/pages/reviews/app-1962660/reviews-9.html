The SBMM is pretty crazy and I think they can tone it down a notch on it, but besides that I had no troubles. I only encountered 2 crashes and a few bugs here and there but all the bugs I noticed have been patched in the Week One patch that came out yesterday. But the main highlight of the game in my opinion is the Campaign. It is really well done and I think is a worthy follow up to Modern Warfare (2019). If you haven't had the chance to play this game or you plan on buying it I highly recommend you play it!
December 2023 Edit: Since this has been out for a year and won't receive any content updates here is my final review for the game. The Campaign is really the only good thing that came out of this game the multiplayer doesn't go well with the slow movement and Warzone 2.0 didn't work well with it either. I really do think the movement works well with the campaign and how that goes and all but as a general movement for the whole game it just doesn't work well. I also feel like a lot of it is just a cash grab now for battle pass and bundles like it genuinely feels like it takes 20 years just to get up a full tier in the battle pass while on Warzone Caldera (Warzone 1) and Fortnite I can play 1 round and go up a lot of levels. Honestly I wouldn't get this game for multiplayer but if you are just a campaign person then I would probably recommend it for that but as a whole game don't waist your time.\t\t\t\t\t
