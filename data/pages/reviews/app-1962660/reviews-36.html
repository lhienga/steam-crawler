\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's get this straight, this isn't the MW2 we fell in love with back in 2009 and this isn't the proper sequel that MW 2019 deserves in terms of the online aspect, campaign wise... Fantastic.

Buy or Wait

Wait for a sale... It's not worth $70.

Online

It's crazy how Infinity Ward can go from a masterpiece of MW 2019 where it was fluid and easy to understand… then went into the mentality of making MWII 2022 into this.

How do you go 10 steps backwards? Everything changed. From weapons, maps, lack of customization removed, gameplay mechanics to the perk system. It's horrible.

P.S - 19 years later of Call of Duty and somehow they still haven't introduced a way to give penalties for leaving matches.

Gameplay/Misc

First off, why did third person make a comeback? Corner peeking corners to know an advantage of other player is just dumb and trash. Why is this in a competitive game? Yet alone Call of Duty.

- Perk system is horrible, no idea why they changed it from 2019 to this.
- Operators in-game aren't even in the campaign. All of them are made up characters.
- No Operator customization, completely removed.
- Operators are lame, most of the 141 Task Force has masks… Wtf. Is this Halloween?
- "Home" screen just to show ad's
- Horrible UI, MW 2019 had a better UI, while this UI looks like it was catered to mobile users.
- When switching between players when dead. It takes long to load to the next player.
- UAV got raised from 3 kills to 4 kills.
- Tactical Camera should be in the 'Tactical' section like how Black Ops 1 had it.

--- A lot more wasn't added in from previous MW 2019. Read it here, https://www.reddit.com/r/ModernWarfareII/comments/yfi3o7/okay_iw_time_to_release_that_day_1_patch_now

Game Modes

As you can tell Hardcore makes 0 comeback in MW2… It's in every Call of Duty title except in this one. All I want to play is Hardcore SnD and Infinity Ward doesn't have it.

Revive mechanic is pretty lame in both Knock Out and Prisoner Rescue. I don't know why they added in a feature like this. If you're dead, you're dead. Why can you revive people? Even then this mechanic they added in, when holding reviving you will pick up the weapon of the teammate. Brilliant idea (sarcastic).

Knock Out is just TDM SnD, no one cares for the objective. As usual, TDM is filled with sweats. It doesn't put you with the same 'level' of people. Most game modes still reward people for getting kills rather than doing the objective. Call of Duty still hasn't found a way to reward people yet… Go figure.

One game mode that had promising was Prisoner Rescue. It's just like Rainbow Six Siege but lame… 0 tactical'ness was added in. They could have turned this into a tactical game mode like Siege and make this a very competitive game mode to split away from the casual modes.. But IW isn't that smart as you can tell.

Maps

Nothing from the original MW2 game… And I can say nothing from the campaign locations was added in here. I don't recognize any of these places. But locations aren't the core issue to the probably, map layout is. I mostly play SnD this entire layout is horrible. Some maps are catered to Defenders and some are Attacker focused. Nothing meets in the middle or is natural to both teams like MW 2019 or even the original MW2. Nothing is balanced for anyone.

Weapons

Game has no UMP, Intervention, TAR-21 or FAL. And other weapons like the shotguns.

How are they going to remake MW2 but not add in a UMP or even an Intervention that literally made the series into what it is now?

Campaign

Campaign is filled with 17 missions each one has movie like cut-scenes they draw you in each time you play with almost 0 loading times between each mission and cut-scene... It's remarkable. If you have a good aim, the campaign can be beaten in a good 7 hours on Regularly difficulty. As for Realism, the enemies have 'Body Armor' from Warzone so killing enemies will be a lot tougher and more bullet spongey. I haven't touched this difficulty yet but I guess it will be a lot harder.

At the post credits scene, Makarov made a comeback and had a 'No Russian' in the text message. From my understanding they reworked the MW2 campaign to remove the killing at the airport but instead hijack an airplane instead? ... I guess we will see how that plays out in MW3.

Buy MW 2019 (Modern Warfare) on Steam, go enjoy that game instead.

Overall great campaign. Disappointed we didn't have the airport scene from the original MW2... I guess Infinity Ward wanted to drag on some of the characters a bit more into future titles so Warzone can have more Operators.\t\t\t\t\t
