TLDR: 6.5/10 campaign, but far too many bugs and technical issues to be actually enjoyable so I'm giving it a 2/10

I only got it for the campaign, so don't expect anything about MP or Warzone because I don't give 2 ♥♥♥♥♥ about either of them. I also haven't played the whole thing at time of writing so I might edit this at some point idk

Pros
- Cool story
- Cool mission settings
- Nice to see old characters in a new light, especially good to see Ghost actually doing something instead of being just a cool guy
- New characters have interesting backstories and are actually involved in the story
- Characters feel more like people than in previous games
- Guns feel good
- Crafting and backpack system is a fun new addition to certain missions

Cons
- Just to get to the campaign you need to go through 3 menus and a client restart
- Enemies deadass aimbot prefire you on veteran, I dread to see what realistic difficulty is like
- Far too many armored enemies, to the point where they're not special anymore and end up being annoying
- Stealth is broken: I can break windows and gun down 2 people right next to each other, and someone in the next room won't care, but if I throw tear gas through a vent enemies 500m away will start shooting at me
- Takedowns are inconsistent: I'm standing right next to an unaware enemy and the takedown prompt won't show up, so I end up beating him up with my gun instead and failing the mission
- Performance is good until it isn't: I get above 100fps consistently on max graphics (12600k+3080+32GB RAM). HOWEVER if you restart a mission checkpoint too many times (could be twice, could be 10 times) your FPS will drop to 10-20 for about 10 seconds which makes it unplayable
- Lights will sometimes be visible through walls which makes certain missions completely unplayable
- Repetitive story beats ("It happened on my watch, I need to fix it") we don't need 3 whole characters to have the same story arc for ♥♥♥♥'s sake
- Jumping on vehicles in that convoy mission is atrociously inconsistent, I could practically be inside the next vehicle and when I jump and spam space to grab on Gaz will just fall to the ground and die
- The convoy mission is boring as ♥♥♥♥
- AI companions are utterly useless and will straight up let you die to someone you didn't see because they're too busy shooting at nothing
- "We heard you liked Clean House from MW2019, so we're making every mission Clean House!" we don't need to be clearing buildings every mission, it gets repetitive after the 3rd one
- The fight vs Graves is also extremely unfun since you die immediately if he shoots near you. Also armored enemies everywhere and Rodolfo is completely ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ useless (as are all the NPCs)
- The survival/crafting bit in the last mission was completely unnecessary and was just kind of a nuisance
- Sometimes you will have to sit in a black screen for up to 2 minutes after you die because reasons I guess
- Having to restart entire missions to fix some garbage bug is unacceptable
- I had to restart prison break because it wouldn't let me go down the rope even after the objective changed. The AI would sit there and get shot at and I'd die to the invisible sniper

If this were any other studio I'd say wait for the bugs to be fixed and get it on sale, but since it's Activision they're never gonna fix anything (also it launched last year and the lights are still broken lol). If you really REALLY wanna play the campaign wait for a sale, absolutely NOT worth full price\t\t\t\t\t
