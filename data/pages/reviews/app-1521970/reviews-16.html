I am lowering my score to a 7/10 as its got all the basic foundations for a phenomenal game, but simply needs another 6 months of polish and content updates to feel like the game should have felt upon release. Still recommended if you're looking for more content similar to Darkest Dungeon.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDO NOT BUY.

This game has a bug that is causing many to get soft locked at the end of chapter 1 and even if you try to do the campaign over again it is likely you will get soft-locked again. Why not roll the dice regarding this bug? Well there are plenty more game breaking bugs past this one that will put you outside the return window but the main reason is that the developer is being shady AF about the release timing and not active on the boards of their official discord regarding the issues.

1. The game was supposed to release at midnight and instead of admitting that it was delayed by 15 hours to fix issues, they said it was so we would all go to bed at a reasonable time (apparently we were supposed to sleep until 2 PM).
2. You can move rooms, but you can't move stairs, some people can't even place stairs to begin with,
3. Many people are experiencing loot disappearing upon equip.
4. Action cards aren't acting as they should according the the descriptions.

I waited for years to play this game, and love the demo and loved everything about the concept. After seeing how they're treating the community, I bought the game and played just enough to leave a review and hit the refund button and I suggest you do the same. I don't mind a rough launch, but I would like some transparency regarding bugs and it would be nice to let us communicate with one another regarding issues.

March 12th should have been the first day this game went into EA.\t\t\t\t\t
