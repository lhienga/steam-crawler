\r\n\t\t\t\t\tArt and general aesthetic are cool.

Gameplay seems fairly simple. Early game fights could be beaten with random card selection. Want to access higher difficulty, and I believe it is gated until much later and I doubt it gets very hard. Tactical game feels incapable of losing at it.

I dislike the general containment of comments about the game. They had a demo, it is nowhere to be found now, yet numerous sites listed it. Steam forums have several categories, they are not being used to communicate at all. For example, the launch was delayed, and that was only posted on their discord. Not steam, nor the reddit for the game......which is controlled by game publisher and/or developer and has limited posts to it. It seems like they are funneling traffic to their discord now, but as someone that does not want to join a new discord for every game I try, that is not a good place for me to join.

Other people mentioned bugs. I have not had major ones. I just think the difficulty is way too easy. If you like roguelikes because of the tacticalness of them, I would suggest looking elsewhere. If you like this aesthetic.....well I wish I could recommend a demo, but the devs removed it for whatever reason.

Edit: I am going to add this quote from a dev in the forums: "Yes, some of the reviews are from us. We believe there's nothing wrong with reviewing our own game. As a small indie game studio with a tight-knit team, we are also a players, eagerly awaiting its release. Thank you for understanding."\t\t\t\t\t
