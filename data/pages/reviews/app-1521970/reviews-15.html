\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinetly doesn't deserve a 50% review score. It's a lot more fun than many modern games
Great game for the price, it's a turn base RPG game with base management and gathering resources.
Later you can buy better items for your goblins based on their class and breen goblins with specific traits so they become stronger. You also gather resources in missions and have alternate pathways.
Sounds familiar? yes, it's influence a lot by Darkest Dungeon https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YX3xVlin_tE
Pros: looks great, charming, hand drawn is timeless, combat is interesting since you can't brute force your way, gathering resources feels rewarding, base management is overwhelming but becomes fun, a lot of variety in gameplay because of the turn order mechanic, decent story
Cons: combat takes a while if you do it right, confusing and conflicting mechanics when it comes to abilities since you have a lot less control over your party rotation, might be too linear, takes a few hours to get started.
But, overall, definetly one of the best indie games ever made\t\t\t\t\t
