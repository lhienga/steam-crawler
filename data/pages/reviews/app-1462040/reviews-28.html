\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the original FF7 back in 1997, while this story is primarily the same, there are a few minor changes that make this game a completely new experience even for us older players of the original game. I'm not one to pay $70 for a title, but I caught it half off on spring sale. Def recommend if you have not yet played it or if you have and want to relive the experience again.

I had only a few bugs so far, found at one point I couldn't open up vendor item lists, but a save and restart fixed it. A few music game bugs where the music was playing from a previous fight overplaying the world music, but again save and restart fixed it. The voice acting in the game is amazing. These people really brought the game to life and should be commended. (Looking at you Bri, amazing job on Aerith!) Def worth a watch on her youtube channel of her playing through the game for some back story and behind the scenes stuff and her own reaction to the game. -https://www.youtube.com/@StrangeRebelGaming

Graphics are amazing as well. Many screenshots and a new desktop background have been taken. :)

I almost forgot to mention that they also pretty much kept the original music as well only way more amped up. From combat, to just the simple overworld music, it gives you the vibes from the original title, and brings it to modern level. I found myself just pausing the game to listen to the music and remembering what it was like the first time through.

I'm really impressed how well they've taken to keeping to the main original game's story here and while things are different and expanded on, nothing takes away from the story IMO it just enhances the story and makes it worth the play through.

I think it was kind of a cheese move to split the game into 3 parts though because now I have to wait for rebirth to come to PC cuz I don't have a playstation and I don't plan on getting one just for the next title, and I'll have to wait even longer for the final part as well which has yet to release. Its hard to stay off youtube and not just watch the game clips. I'll just have to go back to the original game and play it through again with new eyes after I finish rebirth and then remind myself how its supposed to end because I kinda forgot. Its been a few years...

Anyway. I was skeptical about getting the game myself because I really didn't want to play through the exact same game with better graphics, and square enix did not disappoint here.\t\t\t\t\t
