Now the story isn't 100% here because it's going to be in three parts and this is probably only about 5 hours overall in the original game, but there is a lot of good content and new stories here. Cloud seems more like he can do anything and is someone you don't want to mess with. Barrett is a badass guy that's just trying to protect his daughter and hearing him actually talking about her shows more emotions than just text can justify. Tifa is amazing in this game. I completely love how she looks in this game and how her attitude is towards everything. She actually looks like she can defend herself but still be friendly at the same time. Aerith is that type of girl that you would want to take home to your mother. It is she is sweet and down-to-earth but also funny. Biggs, Wedge and Jessie are also really good at this game. I can learn a lot about them and do invest in their backstory. I love the chemistry between all of them. The world building in this game is amazing. I feel like they just took the game from the PSX days and made it work today. Love the appearance of the world. The biggest issue is waiting for more of the story. It is up to you if you want to buy it at full price, but definitely, but it is on sale Grade A
