\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGot it 50% off recently. This game runs and looks fantastic on my PC, and was well optimised. This is what should be expected for a PC port and not whatever is going on with Dragon's Dogma.

Aside from the performance and visuals, the game is a pretty good re-imagining of the original Midgar story (or as I call it, CD 1) with some new characters thrown in. I really enjoyed the re-imagining of the Wall Market in this game, and look forward to seeing how the overworld and other locations look in Rebirth. The combat is pretty fun and can be quite challenging until you learn how to use every character's abilities and co-ordinate attacks. That's another change, the combat has dialed down on being as materia-heavy as in the original with the introduction of unique abilities you learn throughout the game (aside from limit breaks)

I'd recommend this to any RPG fan, not just people who loved the original\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs much as I'm inclined to vote "yes" on the merit of getting to see some of your favorite characters and sets from the original game in 4k, the real answer is "no" because the game advertises itself as a "remake" and it absolutely is not. The plot is almost entirely different and often feels like it has more in common with Resident Evil than it does Final Fantasy. The plot veers way off course into "we can make our own fate this time" territory with spoopy dementor spirits that seem more like an attempt to distract you from all the content that was cut than anything else.

The biggest question I come away with after playing this is "who was this game for?" It isn't for newcomers to FF7 because they wouldn't understand all the "fate" aversion talk around the dementors, and certain cues in the plot (especially around Sephiroth) just wouldn't make any sense. But it's not for OG players either, because most of what you wanted to come back to is cut out and replaced with the same three areas and lots of lazy backtracking mechanics. No exploration, no overworld map, no sense of high adventure. Just the same linear roads to the same three areas.

This game is a multiverse/side story, not a remake. I can only hope that one day modders are able to utilize the assets and AI train the voices to produce an actual remake that doesn't cut out the heart of the original.\t\t\t\t\t
