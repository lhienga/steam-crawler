Another reason why I don't recommending buying the game at full price.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so bad it's not even funny.
The first couple of hours were ok, but then you reach the Slums and the game presents a bunch of very bad quests.
Quest #1 Go to a location A. Kill N gators. Find a door that cannot be unlocked.
Quest #2 Go to locaion A again. Kill the same gators. Find a key that is now present in the location and open the previously locked door, Kill a drake.
Quest #3 - Find and click on 3 cats.
Quest #4 - Kill 3 rats in ocation B.
Quest #5 - Kill 3 a bit more dangerous rats in location B.
Quest #6 - Kill 3 soldiers.
Quest #7 - Kill a rabid hound. I think it was in the same location as quest #6? Not sure.
Several hours of running across one area, visiting the same locations again and again, completing the most boring quests I've played recently. What a joke.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinal Fantasy 7, although incredibly long & tedious to play through, was the most nostalgic game of my childhood. I have a tattoo on my arm to signify just how important this game was to me. Everything has been elaborately recreated, allowing you to re experience the FF7 world in a fresh, modern way that breathes greater life into the characters. Even the ability to play with Japanese audio was a huge bonus! This is an absolute masterpiece in every sense of the word. I don't often play video games anymore, nor do I have the time as a new father of 2, but this is a beautiful game & I am still finding a few hours here and there to play through this an relive my childhood again.\t\t\t\t\t
