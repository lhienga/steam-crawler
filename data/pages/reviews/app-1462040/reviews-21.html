\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall: 9/10

Never played a Final fantasy before and this one even thought its not the original and have a different fight mechanic, I was amazed by the gameplay, story and characters, would recommend this to everyone. The final fantasy franchise can be haunting to start but tanking into account each one of them is an isolated universe if feels less overwhelming.

Pros:
- Excellent gameplay: The game offers a variety of combat options, including a real-time action system with dodge and parry mechanics, as well as a deep skill system.
- Insane RPG mechanics: Each weapon has its own mastery system, and materia allows for interesting builds and customization.
- Interesting teambuilding: Each character can be tailored to different roles.
- Great story: The story is engaging, with twists and turns that will keep you on the edge of your seat and character development on some degree.
- Smooth mobility in puzzles: The game's platforming and puzzle elements are well-implemented and enjoyable.
- Variety of enemies: The game features a wide range of enemies to fight, each with their own unique abilities and weaknesses.
- Boss fights: The boss fights are challenging and exciting, and each one is unique.
- Weapon detail: In almost all the cutscenes, you can see the weapons you are using on each character, which adds to the immersion.

Cons:
- Repetitive shops: The shops in the game all sell the same items, and there is not much variety.
- Motorcycle missions: The motorcycle missions are boring and repetitive.
- A part of a triology: I know the game is meant to be a third of the story, but ending the story in such state is kind of depressing only knowing that now it is only possible to play this and the second game on ps5 (rebirth).

Final thoughts... Jessie best girl\t\t\t\t\t
