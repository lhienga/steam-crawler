\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJank Fantasy 7 Remake.
I hope the next one is better.
I don't understand at what point the FF7R devs thought they'd done a good job with this port.
Without the fanboys/nostalgia googles the game is average, I don't understand the reception.
I think watching a "FF7R the movie" video is more interesting.

++++++
The story is nice, they manage to stretch 10h of gameplay into 30 without it being (too) shocking, respecting the original game.
The remake twist is good
It's pretty, with nice camera shots and cinematography.
HD Character design
Voice acting
Cloud in HD dress
Some of the music is really good
No bugs (just once or twice, I reloaded the save and it was fine)

-------
Keyboard/mouse controls are basically non-existent.
No graphics options
No accessibility options
90s console menus
The camera in combat is horrible and doesn't do what you want it to, horrible in combat
Character/movement/animation slow/clunky for no reason
Lots of hidden loading screens (slow animation on a ladder/going through a narrow corridor/etc), more than necessary.
Fetch quests
The "puzzles" are boring and do nothing but waste time
The pacing is a bit random and there are new chapters that add nothing

The audio mix is janky:
- you're walking down the street next to a bunch of people, and all of a sudden you're deafened by all the simultaneous dialogue at 100% volume
- Combat music that remains off-combat at 200% volume
- Or just music that doesn't fit the mood (always at 200%)\t\t\t\t\t
