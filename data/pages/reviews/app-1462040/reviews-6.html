\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan, I've been in my head about this remake all week. There is no denying they put a ton of effort into this. They actually made Midgar fun and interesting, which I wasn't sure was possible after countless replays of the original.

The fleshed-out interactions, character building, and story were so welcomed. If you played this back in the day, it will make you feel beyond nostalgic. I was glued to my screen far too often, in awe of how pretty or cool some parts were.

Being transparent, the game is very linear and at times feels like a corridor simulator. A small part of me is screaming that they didn't just put the whole game into one remake, but it makes sense that they split it up into three games given the scope of how big it is and them fleshing it out considerably.

It pains me that we will probably have to wait years for the sequel "Rebirth" on the back of PlayStation having exclusive rights, followed likely by Epic Games on PC. LOL, but what can ya do.

Fun to be had for FF7 nerds, enjoy!\t\t\t\t\t
