\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI watched Advent Children when I was very young, and I've rewatched it throughout the years because I thought it was a really good movie even though I didn't play the original game ^^; So when I started playing the Remake, I was already familiar with many of the characters. I'm happy that they kept the same voice actors from the movie as well as some of the music. The game also gave off major Advent Children vibes especially at the end, and it made me so giddy~ XD

I also thoroughly enjoyed the Yufie DLC. Her character design is probably my favorite. The DLC started off as really cute and lighthearted, but it turned quite dark at the end. I think the DLC made Yuffie's character really compelling, and I'm excited to see her meet the main crew in Rebirth when it comes to PC XD

-I think it took me around 60 hours to finish the base game and around 10 hours for the DLC.

-The mini games and side missions were actually a lot o fun and engaging

-I like that there's still a turn-based element in the combat despite it being mostly real-time.

-I liked the materia system as well but it felt very limited/restricted. I think the best part about it is that you can link an elemental materia to your weapon and it'll have a nice effect in combat.

-The story, narrative, characters, and music are the best parts of the game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs much as I wanted to love this game, and as much as I loved the original FF7, I just didn't find this game to be very deep or fun. The graphics are kinda cool on my steam deck, but it really does feel just like a hallway simulator running from point A to point B and killing things along the way by going tap tap tap. The side quests are pretty boring. Some of the battles require a bit more strategy, so those are fun, but the overall level design is just so repetitive and linear. Just feels too stretched out and slow paced. I will say that I loved the Wall Market chapter. That was probably the most fun section.\t\t\t\t\t
