\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnrailed is a really interesting and unique game: a mix of arcade-style isometric movement and building/management with a rogue-lite foundation: a train is running from the left of the screen, and you have to build track to the next station. If you don't build fast enough, the train crashes and you lose. You can get upgrades to your train that speeds up building or gives special abilities, and the level gets harder as you advance. It's a simple premise but it's lots of fun and can be addictive. An especially good game if you have friends to play with, though random queueing and soloing are definitely do-able.\t\t\t\t\t
