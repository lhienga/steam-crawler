\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnrailed is a simple, primarily multiplayer co-op mini-game. It is NOT an open world survival crafting game, it should not have that tag.

In Unrailed you have a train that is slowly moving right towards the edge of the screen while you and up to 3 friends, mine rocks and chop down trees to both make way and build more tracks. In the "endless" game mode you start in the first, easiest world and work your way up to the final hardest world, getting upgrades for your train along the way. In "quick" mode you pick a world and play a 15 minute round. There is also versus, sandbox and time, but these modes feel like an afterthought and most players will stick to the first two.

Unrailed is basically a party game. You boot it up with friends, play a few rounds or see how far you can get, laugh at the hi-jinks, and then move on to something else. Its not set up for a long campaign you return to multiple times or single player. So keep that in mind. While it does allow for saves and for you to pick up where you left off, its clear that is not really the intended way to play. It took my friends and I, a 2 to 3 hour run to beat the game, though we did that after several unsuccessful runs. If your goal is only to beat the game, and you don't look at a guide, expect to spend maybe 6 to 10 hours depending on whether you use checkpoints.

That said, it is very fun for what it is. The simple mechanics are easily picked up by anyone but the challenges it throws at you late game can be difficult. I played all the way through with 3 friends who are all avid gamers and we struggled at times. But on lower difficulties you can still give the game a try with kids or people who don't game much.

Lastly, I did enjoy the art style and the music. Seriously, the sound track is better than it has any right to be. Its all very polished and cute.

In the end, I would recommend this game on sale. $20 is a bit much considering you and all your friends need to pay that, and its not really worth buying for solo play. But for $10 or less I think its well worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
