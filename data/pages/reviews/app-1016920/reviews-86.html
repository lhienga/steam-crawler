The Gothesis
I adore simple, fast learning curve puzzle/casual type games. Unrailed! is def that, it's cute adorable graphics and simplistic play still coupled with co-op makes it a super fun group of friends type game. It's very quick to master but with a long list of unlockables and random gen maps to keep you busy forever. The only two down sides that I can find is one that a lot of co-op/multiplayer games suffer from, no text chat or communication feature. This makes it hard to sync up or join a discord quick call or anything. Lastly is single player, I hate to say it, but I just can't see myself playing this as a single player at all. Aside from that, love it!
