\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game! From the starting menu I knew it would be something unique (to me). Really, from the start menu alone, the simplistic attention to detail is impressive. I have only played the demo, about one hour, so far, and ended up making the purchase because: 1) It's high value to me. It's a different kind of puzzle game that offers several modes, including sandbox, endless, timed, and daily challenge, as well as single-player/co-op/online match options (for now through the discord), difficulty levels ranging from "Kids" to "Extreme(?)", cute style and little character/AI enhancements; 2) For about $7.50 US for the supporter pack, (~$5 on sale for game only), it's well worth it, as a little game with a strong dev path going forward. Upon starting the demo I immediately latched onto it, and am happy to support the development. Maybe it's me, but it's quite challenging, especially as a single player. I can see it being a fun game for a group as well. I think the devs built in a unique system, which enhances the experience for solo players.

This has been on my wishlist, but I never really looked much into it prior to today. Sometimes I see "local co-op" and pass over things. Today I saw the "free weekend" update and gave it a try. If I didn't have other obligations (one of which I already shirked, not wanting to give up playing just yet), I'd still be playing. Hopefully anyone reading this will also give it a shot during the free weekend and enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
