\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnrailed is the definition of raw, unadulterated multiplayer fun. The constant ticking clock of the train moving forward creates a lot of tension and beautiful moments where you and your crew are fighting for your lives to get rail extended at the end of the line. The way that resources are managed - i.e. requiring you to cut down trees and rocks to create new rails while also creating space for new rail - is just incredible for such a simple game. Balancing your wood usage for rails and for bridging out to collectables or new areas is fun and engaging in a way that is difficult to describe. Fighting off the enemies with your pickaxe with friends is funny and creates opportunities for all sorts of hilarious moments. The fact that your character cannot walk through other characters or through terrain pieces or the train makes for an additional challenge that you not only need to make room for the train and its rails but also yourself and space to work around the rails. The game is challenging but fair and repetitive but engaging, a complete package for a simple indie-game. Don't bother picking it up if you can't con any friends into playing with you!\t\t\t\t\t
