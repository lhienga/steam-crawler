BUT! the gameplay is really addictive and hilarious at times.

To make a short story, you have a train that only stops at stations and 1-4 players must build the track fast enough to keep it from derailing. You have your tools (axe/pickaxe/water bucket) to harvest the materials to build said track. As the train moves forward, so does the camera and anything left outside the camera is lost.

Can you manage harvesting ressources, feeding them to the crafting wagon, grabbing the tracks from said wagon and placing em, clear the path ahead and all this while carrying your extra ressources by hand along the track?

The game definitely isnt an idler, be ready to get stressed and proud of your work while laughing at your friends blowing themselves up with dynamite.\t\t\t\t\t
