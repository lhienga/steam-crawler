\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMasterpiece
Seeds and procedural generation for huge replayability
I expected to play for 30m remote with a friend to kill time, but then 3 hours passed...
SINGLE PLAYER + BOT
You can EVEN play in single player with a pretty capable bot you can give specific commands to, like to mine any line of sight visible wood, iron, place dynamite here, auto gather and combine all visible materials to place new track near you or build track in this basic shape I designate!
VERY fun with friends in endless mode to unlock new wagons or versus mode.
CONS
I enjoy the challenge, but be warned that the difficulty even on easy is quite high to beat the game. Probably not like Enter the Gungeon, but up there. To see everything in the game, you have to master every biome and get there in a single run. Only problem might be considered the difficulty of perma death, (you can't checkpoint and respawn there on death to quickly see everything in the game, Only to save and come back if it's going on 1-2 hours maybe) but with permanent unlocks for the sandbox aka practice mode.
So snow biome for example is quite hard even on easy because the snow slows down walking a lot and you need to fill the bucket with water constantly and make sure you have a cleared path to a water source. And then the yeti constantly knocks stuff out of your hands - he can be killed but there's a lot going on to time manage.\t\t\t\t\t
