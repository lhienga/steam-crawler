\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnrailed is a really fun time if you're playing the game with other people. The game is simple enough. It's not too complex and a great way to spend time with your friends. The goal is simple, get your train out as far as you can for as long as you can. The visuals are simple enough and convey the best amount of information possible in a meaningful way.

I do have a few criticisms for the game though. These are probably just my own thoughts so whether or not you consider this an issue is up to you. But Unrailed I find is both too long and somehow too short at the same time. I feel like the amount of "biomes" is fine. I do wish there were more to keep the runs feeling fresh outside of "layout" being different. Even if the mechanics stayed the same, swapping out the desert to something else randomly per run would be nice.

If you're playing the game for mars, I find the game takes way too long. I know that there's nothing stopping me from moving onto the next biome sooner than later, but you can't "complete" a run of Unrailed until you collect the "special" biome item. But those only show up on the third section of any run and nowhere else. If you don't collect it right then and there you're effectively screwed, and you have no idea that you would know this until the very last possible biome. This is rather frustrating. It would be nice if the special biome trinket would spawn later in the biome. Or at least more frequently in the final biome.

Also some of the carts are significantly better than other carts. Something like the milk wagon for example is extremely powerful when the train is going no speed at all, but at higher difficulties where the spaces are cramped and the speeds get really high, it becomes worthless. The light wagon is basically only good for the snow biome and for people who can't navigate at night. Maybe it would be more useful in the high difficulties? But in the normal difficulty it's basically pointless.

But that's enough rambling. I think the game is fun and I would recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
