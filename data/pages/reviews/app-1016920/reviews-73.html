Very fun to play solo, but by far is better with friends. The game at first seems like it could get boring quickly which is what I was most worried about at first when buying it, but playing with friends makes it a fun game that isn't too repetitive due to the multiple different biomes as well as it being procedurally generated level with many obstacles in the way. It's great for bonding with friends or just for a fun passing the time/party experience.
Pros- doesn't feel repetitive, can be challenging if you want it to be, can be more laid back and easy if you want it to be, playing solo with a robot means you don't need friends to enjoy the game nor do you need to be or play online
Cons- single player is much more tedious with the robots current control state, plenty of times I've lost because of the robot not following the path I marked for him or from him picking up the tracks and not moving

TL;DR- great game, by far worth the money (especially during a sale), fun to play solo but definitely better to play with friends!
