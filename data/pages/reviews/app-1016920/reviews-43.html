Pros
- A great game to play with friends. Fun, teamwork-reliant, chaotic. While I've only played 2-player for the most part, even 4-player groups will have stuff to keep everyone busy.

- Lots of wagons with interesting effects. Reorganizing the layout of your train is a big strategic element, especially with the wagons that affect nearby wagons.

- Lots of interesting areas to progress through, with unique challenges, and changes to the existing mechanics.

- Host can choose whether to save at checkpoints or not, potentially providing a pseudo-roguelike challenge.

- As of writing this, the devs are still updating this game. When I bought it, I totally thought it was complete, so the new features added afterwards have been a nice surprise!

- One of the areas might be annoying if you have motion sickness, so the devs added a helpful accessibility option that disables the effect! However, they made sure to give it a fair downside, so it doesn't feel like you're cheating by enabling it.

- Very few (if any) issues with the netcode. Should be just as good with local mutliplayer. Splitscreen isn't even necessary, as the camera is fixed on the train.

- Has crossplay, I'm told!



Cons
- Only one button for most necessary actions. This means said button is VERY context-sensitive. You'll get used to it, but sometimes it's annoying trying to pick something up when your character keeps picking up the thing next to it, or when you're trying to place the track going right, but it goes down instead, forcing you to put down the stack, pick up the one track you misplaced, and move it.

- When the train gets REALLY long, wagons closer to the back can be obscured by the edge of the screen, especially when the track is completely straight for an extended period of time.

- Sometimes the level generation just screws you over. It's never impossible in my experience, but sometimes an absurd amount of planning in advance is necessary, especially when you have a split path or a 1-block-wide gap.



Conclusion
Nice fun co-op game, no idea how good it is singleplayer, but other reviews say it's still fun. Very few issues, and they're only minor ones that can't be easily fixed. Definitely recommend the game, especially if you've got a friend who's interested.\t\t\t\t\t
