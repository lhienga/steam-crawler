\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn Unrailed, you're tasked with cutting down trees and breaking rocks to get the materials to build train tracks. You then have to clear a path for the train and build the tracks so the train doesn't run off the rails or hit anything. If you can manage to get the train to the next station, you get a small break to buy train upgrades that'll make your life easier. There are small challenges you can complete during levels that will give you more money (For example: Only one person can pick up the axe during the level. If only one person picks up the axe, you get bonus money) that are relatively easy and add a fun twist to the game. As you progress, the levels get harder and the train moves faster.

Overall Unrailed is very chaotic but lots of fun if you and your friends can work together as a team. If you have someone trying to troll I think it would really dampen the experience for the other players. So if you aren't an ass, get this game and build trains with your friends!
