\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good fun, but has lots of flaws too. The narrator's goofy enough not to make you feel awkard while slaughtering hundreds of innocents sea creatures and humans, the world's pretty beautiful and the story, which is mostly centered around your nemesis, is pretty captivating. But collecting things on hidden and hard to reach places isn't least bit fun, the controllers are pretty clunky when you have to jump to a high place (and you do that a lot), and the leveling goes too fast, making every enemy pretty underpowered halfway through.

As for the DLC, well, they give you a taste at the end of the game, and that combined with its reviews are enough for me to call it a day after 100% the base game.

Overall, I'll give it a 6,5/10. Playing as a shark is plenty fun, but it had many game mechanics I wish were not implemented.\t\t\t\t\t
