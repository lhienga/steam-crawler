1. Game is fun when eating vulnerable fat humans.
2. Gives you quests / objectives to collect / kill and discover, keeping things in game more interesting to explore further.
3. The world itself is very pretty featuring a day and night cycle.
4. Customisation for optional shark perks to level up your skills and stats.
5. The voice acting is well done, as for its screams is horrifyingly funny.

The Negatives:
1. Game has a thing for crashing at intense times, being it fighting a orca or the hunters boats clashing into each other too much it shuts my game off.

Things id wish would be added:
1. Colour options for your shark.
2. Multiplayer PVP matches shark vs shark.
3. Other maps of the world to explore and feast upon.
4. More characters such as Scaly Peat that are memorable as him.
