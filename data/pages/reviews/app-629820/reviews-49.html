\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played and finished the entire game within about 11 hours or so. From start to finish. This includes all the additional side quests and collectables.

Basically the game is alright, it's fun to play, at least at first, but later in the game you, as others mention, become basically OP against the majority of the enemies, with a few exceptions which are at levels above the max level allowed if you don't own the DLC. Mind you the mutation sets will basically compensate for that, but it feels kind of basic, once you find out what is the most OP, and the game just gets very boring.

As for the gameplay itself, it is quite lacking to be honest. It is basically the same thing in each area, but with different enemies. Every area consists of the following:

Population control, or basically go eat 10-15 of certain fish in multiple areas. This unlocks the Apex, or basically a mini-boss that you must defeat.

Alongside that, there are the "revenge" missions were it's simply go eat 10-15 humans in various areas, but mind you you'll gain a "threat" level where you then have to tear away at enemies that get in bigger boats as time goes on, and more and more. Again, with the right mutations you're basically invincible, but still it gets tiring.

The only other missions in each areas are basically hunted predators, or basically certain fish you have to hunt and kill. Typically a little higher in level than the other ones around.

And that is pretty much it, minus 2 actual boss fights. Both with the antagonist, but the first is basically a glorified hunter fight, and you can easily cheese it by ramming it again and again. The second is a bit more complicated, but still the same in concept.

Of course there is a "story" where you follow the antagonist and his son, but it's super short, doesn't really make much sense, and is very lacking.

So if you want a game to just kill time and mess around with, this is it. If you're looking for something with substance and then some. Look elsewhere.

Overall the game is decent though, especially for not being a AAA title, so I'll give it that, and it looks pretty good too.\t\t\t\t\t
