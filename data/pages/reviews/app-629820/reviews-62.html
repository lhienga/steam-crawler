I don't want this review to change your mind about the game itself because the playthrough you get is very fun, the graphics look pretty good, a good-sized open world, and a decent storyline. I would only recommend playing this game if you want to play as a shark that eventually takes over the ocean, but just keep in mind that the game will no longer feel fun after your first playthrough.\t\t\t\t\t
