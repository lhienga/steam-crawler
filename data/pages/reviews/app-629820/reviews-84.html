Addicting Grind, Hilarious Storytelling, But It's Not For Everyone

Pete has to have the worst luck out of anyone I've ever seen. This guy tries every way he knows how to hunt a shark but life just insists that everything he does must go horrendously wrong. I've never laughed this hard at another man's misfortune. If you like level grinding and upgrade systems, there's some of that here and I like it a lot. It is largely go to this area and complete these tasks, go to the next area and complete the same kinds of tasks again etc, with not a lot of variation outside of a new locale to explore, new kinds of animals (bigger and badder), and new skills/gear to find. The story missions do get some variety as time progresses, but you either like what you're doing or you don't. Which brings me to the crux of the matter. You'll either love or hate this game. It is what it is and if you like it, there's a lot of it. If it's not enough for you, you won't find much else. I loved exploring the areas and treasure hunting, maxing out my skills. The areas grow a lot bigger and more interesting from swamp waters til you make your way out to open sea where there are a lot bigger and stronger fish to fry.

As for the DLC lmao, If you're familiar with the illuminati, the conspiracies surrounding them and people like Alex Jones, you're gonna enjoy this one. Even more so if you're a believer, or just someone who likes to troll the believers. Both groups will find a lot to love here. You'll be laughing at everything the narrator says, or agreeing lmao. They use a lot of actual real life conspiracy talk as well so it's gonna be stuff you're very familiar with. It's not a very long game either unless you want to collect everything so you don't have to drag your feet if you don't want to.\t\t\t\t\t
