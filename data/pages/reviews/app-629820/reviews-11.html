Fun but not worth $40

The game is nice for what it is: play as a shark, kill things, ♥♥♥♥ up everything.
It's good at that part, its satisfying just attacking things at random (though it does get repetitive). The different parts you could equip were probably the best part to me, putting on all parts of a set makes you feel invincible lol.

..but everything else is average:
The humor (mostly from the narrator) borders on cringey (especially in the DLC), story is fun but not much to it, and not much post-game unless you wanna do the Infamy stuff. The game isn't long either, at least not as long as you'd expect a $40 game to last. At least the game is easy to perfect.

Verdict is it's good, just wish there was more. Wait till it's on sale to get it. Same with the DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
