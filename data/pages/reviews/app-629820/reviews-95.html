\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, I recommend it. But on sale. It's too short of a game for the initial $40 and the DLC doesn't offer much more time invested either.

The base game is actually extremely fun and is very open. The entire way it is portrayed as a reality tv show about shark hunting is actually pretty spot on for most shows like this lol.

The gameplay itself is actually a lot of fun and the collectibles aren't boring to get either. The bounty hunters are fun to fight and eventually the evolutions at max level are all incredible fun. Honestly though the DLC one is my favorite but the DLC has some flaws that bring to the base game.

First is the frame drops. There are a lot more frame drops with the game in the DLC.

Second is the time trials. Some of the time trials are extremely difficult if not preformed perfectly. Sometimes the controls for launching yourself in the air are a bit difficult. I used a controller for this and had some minor difficulties

The best part of this game? For any achievement hunters this game is pretty easy to 100% with the 44 achievements and its not boring to do either.

But as I said, I would only really recommend at a lower price for anyone initially interested.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThings I really enjoyed in this game:

1) The work put into making a shark level up, and growing into an elder aged shark. The only thing I didn't like about this part is that they gated the progression of growing up, so if you wanted to fast track to an elder, you would have to progress quite far into the story. I was too eager to do all of the side quests and try and grow up much quicker.

2) The varied shark armor skins, and upgrade system made the game quite enjoyable. Different armor skins would change your gameplay experience, and you can tailor the way you do damage if you wanted to (i.e. Your bite doing extra damage, giving a shock or critical strike chance, etc. All the skins even change the way your shark looks in a unique way as well). Being able to stack damage passives on shark equipment made it very entertaining.

3) The level of detail that went into making the different areas of the game. I really enjoy the contrast colors of the water depending on the time of day, and weather conditions. Swimming in the deep sea with all the hidden chasms was very cool, and very fun to explore for collectibles.

4) The amount of collectibles made it very enjoyable to just swim around and explore the different parts of the game. There are also plenty of secrets to be found, while exploring.

I'm still not finished this game, as I really enjoy it when I am in a relaxing mood, and will play it if I'm brain dead from a long day after work. I highly recommend this game as it really shocked me on how fun and entertaining it is, and the work, and content that they put into it.\t\t\t\t\t
