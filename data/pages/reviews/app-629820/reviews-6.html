\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fun rampage as a man-eating shark!

Set up as a mock TV series following a ridiculous Cajun fisherman and a man-eating shark, the game doesn’t lean too heavily on its story and just lets you have fun. That framework is mainly used to set the mood and keep you swimming straight ahead towards anything you can eat. Since the gameplay is so fun (at least for ~10 hours) that works great here.

Positives
- Addictive, fun gameplay.
- Story doesn’t take itself too seriously or get in the way.
- Looks great.
- Fairly easy to get 100% achievements.
- Only takes 10-15 hours to beat everything.
- Jumping onto a beach and eating all the panicking tourists.

Negatives
- Can become a little tedious towards the end.

Overall 8/10
Would recommend along with Truth Quest DLC, especially when on sale. Very short game at full price.
