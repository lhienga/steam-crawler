Main/Side quest missions and the loot: They all are good and interesting, i loved mostly the DLC part where narrator is pretty much going insane in a way but either way, it's hilarious. At start of the game you'll have the good ol 'Kill the Target" or even multiple targets, getting some loot in areas to get the needed materials for leveling up your stuff, collectives which is the license plate on your car. But once you finish the main game and start off the DLC, it gives you more of targets to kill along with the "Race" which is something new.

Now what really happened and what forced me to write this damn review even tho i'm sure none will take this serious: As i mentioned the "Race" is what screwed me over and tilted me so bad that due to the mechanics, time limitation was forced to change the tactics and do something unorthodox by bitting water which gives me a good speed boost but not how much i would've liked to have, or i just simply had the wrong items on me which didn't made me fast enough. There wasn't that much of a problem. I was legit ready to play it full 100% till a single race made all of the motivation, willingness drained all the way to 0 and just in overall gotten so frustrated that i've rage quited due to failing on reaching the blue ring which extends the time or the very last ring, red which would've ended the whole race but it was placed on a bridge and along with the metal bars which forced me to try and leap higher as much as i could along with the combination of biting just to reach it but ended up in water and on last couple of seconds tried to make that jump again only to fail again with the menu popping out "Failed. 19/20, Retry or Leave?" even when i click Retry.. the narrator of course mocks me for my failure... sure... my fault because i couldn't get my nose the right way and i was not heading or rather jumping on the ring where i was suppose to, but to only go the either full stop or going the opposite way.

In overall: The games is alright and is playable, thumbs up there, just word of advice. Stay The Hell Out Of "Race" on Truth Quest DLC. If you want to put your sanity or your pure skills on them, then proceed with my fair warning because i'm not doing Sparkle 3 Genesis thing again. I finished the game and i call that as a win with however much % i was done with and for the rest of this eternity. Oh and buy it on discount, i recommend that rather than buying it on full price, less regrets and more fun in a way i guess.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game that is insanely easy to pick up and put down.
The humor reminds me of older games like Destroy All Humans and the gameplay itself, while grindy, is fun. I never really felt myself getting bored as trying to constantly hunt and throw myself into more dangerous situations to get the most amount of resources proved challenging and heavily rewarding. The narrator sometimes repeats the same things but it didn't happen too often with me.

However let it be known that this game does have an audio stutter, it seems to get really bad when there's several things going on or in cutscenes, so it's wise to put on subtitles.

All-in-all, it's a pretty fun game. One I enjoy playing and I recommend it to anyone wanting a simple game to just relax to and turn your brain off for a while. Go swim, eat a fish.\t\t\t\t\t
