\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolid 3/5 game. Pretty fun gameplay and the ocean design adds to the immersion. I like the variety of animals included in the game, plus the land mobility feature is quite entertaining.
Some areas of improvement though:
1) Lack of replayability. After completing all optional and main missions, since there is a level cap on the shark which means no more skill increases, there is really not much to do post game. It would help if the level cap was removed so it feels like you are embarking on a journey to become a true monster shark, if you get what I mean.
2) Lack of character variations: I like that you can upgrade your sharks using these genetic modifications like bone armor and shadow skins, but I think it would be cooler if you could play as other sharks. There are many different marine predators shown in the game so I think it would not be hard to include them as playable characters as well!
3) Combat is not smooth at times: This might just be me but I find the fish to fish combat to be hard at times due to the camera constantly moving away from my target. Sometimes in the midst of an underwater fight, my shark would breach the surface during an opportunity to attack the target which makes me miss the chance to damage.

For its price, I would say I am a little disappointed from the content provided by the game, After 17 hours being put into it, I no longer want to play the game anymore due to the lack of replayability.\t\t\t\t\t
