\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a lover of the mobile game "Hungry Shark", Maneater is without a doubt a game to have in your Steam library.
The world building is beautifully polished, there are plenty of Easter Eggs, like "Moby D i c k" and of course there is a variety of challenging enemy fish.

I also really like the additional superpowers you get throughout the game.

The only thing that disappointed me a bit is that you couldn't play any other kind of shark or fish other than the bull shark (apart from the atomic hammerhead shark in the DLC).

As for the DLC, I can say that it's ONLY worth it if you enjoyed the previous gameplay itself and want to fight the Atom Leviathan and the modified versions of the boss sharks.

All in all, Maneater has been a great game for me, and I've had a lot of fun playing it 👍.\t\t\t\t\t
