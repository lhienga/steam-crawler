\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fun concept and some good writing and voice acting, but the core gameplay is unpolished and frequently frustrating.

The hardest enemy in the game by far is the camera, everything that I attack responds by swimming beside or behind me, and spinning to be able to lock target over and over gets old.

The big thing was that the game doesn't really tell you how to do anything from mid game onward, from not explaining chained jumps to not telling you how to open gates (bite another predator, jump, and tailwhip to throw - absolutely not intuitive either), to all the license plates require jumps that seem insane until you go on youtube and see how easy they are, none of that is clearly shown or explained in-game.

The narrator lines include some surprisingly good jokes, but they repeat far too often with a relatively small number of possible lines, and the story is surprisingly good for a mayhem simulator, but the frustration in the core game coupled with the tedium and grind of the DLC make this a "wait for the next big sale" title.\t\t\t\t\t
