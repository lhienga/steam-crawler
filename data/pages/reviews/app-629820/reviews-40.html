\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely a fun game. I just finished it, 11.3 hrs. I defeated every infamy hunter, and got every evolution but 2. Most regions at 100% completion.

Unfortunately, that's one of the issues. This game was great, but way too short, especially for the initial price. This game isn't worth 40 dollars now either, but on sale at 13, it's a deal. And while the gameplay is great, it is very repetitive. Mind you, it has to be expected, considering there's not much you can do as a shark besides swim around and eat people.

That being said, the game is still great. The rivalry between you and Scaly Pete is a constant source of tension, and the little cutscenes where you check up on him and see what he's doing while you've been evolving is great too. There is a great twist as well, that I shall not reveal for spoiler reasons, but it's heavily hinted at throughout the game, which just pumps some excitement into the story.

Overall I like this game, but the gameplay will get repetitive and make you put it down after an hour or so of play, then you'll pick it up again. Definitely recommend while it's on sale. Do not pay full price for this game.
