A fun turn off your brain extravaganza of munching, screaming and rip and tearing your way through wildlife, then human populations, then the military... and then friggin aliens to prove that you truly are the most terrifying powerful Alpha creature that ever swam in our oceans.
It's an absolute treat to complete 100% and the commentary from your narrator slowly losing his mind as you become more powerful from little pup to bordering megaladon is enjoyable.
Sure the combat is simple and sure it is repetitive at times. But so is Fast and The Furious and they made 10 of those films. This one has so much cheesy action that makes you smile you half expect it to look at you and mention something about "nothing is stronger than family." Give it a go, its well worth your money for a rainy day quick pick up and play!
