But while Unleashed was a guilty pleasure at best, Man-Eater perfects the formula with an engaging game that has a nice dose of tongue-in-cheek humor. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI HIGHLY recommend this game to anyone looking for a fun, casual, RPG-style shark beat em' up that ACTUALLY FEELS LIKE A GAME. No stupid political commentary (just jokes that are actually funny), just a silly and entertaining story that has its moments of intensity. This game works as it should and is definitely worth the money. Although not an original concept, it takes the style of Jaws: Unleashed and fleshes it out incredibly, making its entirely own thing with the evolutions and other RPG mechanics. Unique enemies with different attacks, interesting locations with enjoyable side quests, an infamy system with the humans comparable to that of GTA or Saints Row, and challenging yet rewarding apex predators to defeat in each region. One of my favorite games in YEARS.

Also want to say that I originally bought this on the Xbox One X, and do not regret buying it again.\t\t\t\t\t
