It runs a lil questionable sometimes(FPS drops and game chugging to get its life together), and crashed quite a few times until I disabled the in-game steam overlay. My computer is more than powerful enough to run this game, so I just blame Unreal Engine 4

Let me start by saying, the playtime here is not good. I 100%ed the base game + DLCs(all side stuff, hidden truth questers, and max evolutions) in 10.8 hours. Of which I can say atleast an hour of that game time was me AFK smoking/cooking/eating. It is NOT A LONG GAME, BUT IT IS A GOOD/FUN GAME!

Armors: Bone Armor, the best armor, invincible tank, but slow. Shadow Armor, also the best armor. Did everything but infamy using Shadow, you're just so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fast it makes everything easy and fast. Slap on Sonar and those collectibles are toast. Bio electric....idk man, people online say its good but I just didn't find it good/fun, reason being right below this sentence.

Combat: Frustrating until you get bigger and also figure out how ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ BROKEN tailwhip is. you're big enough to thrash an enemy? get in a couple bites and chuck that som'♥♥♥♥♥ at the nearest wall/fish to repeat the thrash phase. Doing end game fights where you're deep in the water? Thats fine, chuck it at the floor instead! It's an infinite thrash lock and it's hilarious

If you're like me, you do all side content and go for the 100%....if you have the DLC, Truth Quest, DO NOT DO THIS!!! LEAVE YOURSELF STUFF TO DO!...The max level is 30 in the base game, and you reach that very quickly all things considered. I got max level with 2/3 whole areas left to explore, doing all side content and eating everything in sight, but you can't get the max size until endish game. When you reach end game and want those tasty achievements, the easiest way to get resources is slap on that sweet bone armor + farming mutagens, and kill humans until a bounty happens, then kill hunters forever.

DLC time. If you have the DLC, please for the love of god DO NOT 100% THE BASE GAME. get your ass to 30, beat the game, and get to the DLC. The new Atomic Set is the most broken OP thing and its beautiful. The DLC is the same gameplay mechanics, with added Time Trials(not too hard, finished them all without Shadow speed armor), hidden people to eat(use guides, they arent on the map), new APEXes which are really cool! new infamy people, and Chris Parnell going absolutely INSANE. Rush getting the new Atomic Set and then start having dumb fun. The body gets charge as you DAMAGE ANYTHING, bite, instant kill low fish, thrash seals, all builds the meter. Get the charge ready for anything you do as its the silliest ♥♥♥♥ in the game. You activate the ability and when you hold bite, time slows and you start charging a nuke...its not just ONE nuke. you can SPAM this ♥♥♥♥♥ for the whole duration. Activate the ability and reign down hell upon anyone that crosses you. you can shoot out well over 6 nukes just by spamming the base nuke damage(way more than enough damage btw) and not charging the shot. gotta go kill some people on the beach? jump out of the water, activate the ability and nuke the hell out of the beach goers. It makes those missions so much faster and way sillier as people are just getting blow away by nukes. Infamy is the same damn story, get the ability by eating fish...start a rampage...go underwater and spam nuke the hunter boats with no charge nukes. you can easily kill all 3 hunter boats in 1 activation of the ability and then when it runs out....just eat all the dead hunters just floating there since you just nukes all their ships and you get the ability back...it's by far the most broken and silliest way to play the game but it makes everything SO MUCH FASTER AND FUN. Infamy ranks climb so fast by nuking the boats and those pesky APEXs/Targets just get shredded by the nukes. 10/10\t\t\t\t\t

This somewhat open world ShaRkPG may be repetitive in that it's kill 10 these, find all the items, fight some stuff, and do it again... but it's incredibly fun. The base game is slick and very well executed. The shark powers you evolve are all fun and increasingly ridiculous.

I got the DLC with the game on sale as well and that was a lot of fun too. However, the DLC is clearly not as polished or optimzed as the base game. There are bad character-text issues in the narration, the map loads very slowly and feels like it's on the verge of crashing.... but it doesn't. The DLC also introduces time trials which I'm normally not a fan of but with one of the full shark sets and one of the bonus organ evolutions it's easy and a lot of fun.

10/10 might massacre the rednecks and secret agents again.\t\t\t\t\t
