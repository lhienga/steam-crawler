Gameplay wise, this is a really fun game. At the start it can be challangeing, but as you grew bigger and stronger, the carnage becomes bigger, bloodier and more fun.
The big downplay here is the lack of content and challange. After you reacvh max level, fights become easy. The point where you get every evolution is basically the end of the game, so there is nothing else to do after.
Higher difficulty and New Game+ would solve these problems.
10/8

-- GRAPHICS --
The game looks really great. The artstyle really fits the theme.
10/7

-- BUGS --
This is the part, which makes me mad and sad. The game is a crashfest currently. If you have an i12 or later gen CPU, the game will constantly crash, making it unplayable.

-- SOUNDS --
Sounds are cartoony, but they fit the game perfectly. Music is... there is music?
10/6

-- STORY --
There is some story, but really minimal and meh. I did not expect much anyway.
10/4

-- REPLAYABILITY --
No New Game+, no different difficulty levels, so there is not much. There are no exciting bossfights or anything, so if you want to play more, you can just freely roam around.
10/2

--TL;DR--
This, would be a short but fun game, to spend time on. But, thanks to the crashfest it is, it is unplayable. So i do not recommend buying this game, until it is fixed.
