\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs much as I love being able to play as a shark and cause havoc in the water. The game is buggy and refused to pick up my PS4 controller no matter what I tried. So I was forced to play on janky as hell keyboard and mouse controls.

Threat builds too fast, hunters hit stupid hard, not nearly enough information on how to open buttons for gates. I had to google how to open the gates because my shark would not bite onto and HOLD onto the thing. It just kept biting until I found something sturdy like a human or a turtle. The fact you require an "evolution" to have predator creatures lower than you to leave you alone is stupid. No predator Barracuda or Musky fish would be stupid enough to attack a full grown shark without an entire school to back them up.

The narrator is annoying as hell, spouting misinformation every five seconds and I didn't turn it off until I was finished with the story because I wanted to hear what he had to say. But there should be an option to turn off the constant "blurb of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥" whenever you enter an area you have already been in. Because sometimes a fight takes you in and out of a border and having the narrator repeat himself ten times over because you kept going in and out of an area during a fight with ships or a whale or w/e was exceptionally annoying.

Controls: I can't speak for how this works on a controller, but I was forced to use keyboard and mouse. Jumping I would not keep my momentum, I could not get the shark to turn how I wanted. Dashes would just turn off even if I'm still holding the dash button down to keep my speed up. My camera would randomly switch directions when I hit the land after a jump, forcing my shark to go in an entirely different direction even though I hadn't moved my mouse. I would get stuck on ships, props, people, anything and everything and couldn't get out sometimes for 5-10 minutes. It was rage inducing.

DLC: Time trials are stupidly difficult when using Keyboard and mouse. Jumping is inconsistent on a good day, but trying to get over a bloody bridge with a timer!? Forget about it. The military ships were kinda fun at first, but the torpedos were nearly impossible to catch unless I was in my atomic body and dodging them. Any other body? Just trying to catch them? Nope. Impossible. Also why is the atomic body forcing you to be a Hammerhead shark?! Did you lose ideas and cop-out to a lame "well you're mutated so you gotta look like the weirdest shark species, the Hammerhead!"!?!? Like come on! They made the other irradiated animals look like themselves and cool! Why did we have to suddenly turn into a Hammerhead!?

Overall: Play this game if you like being a shark and just killing everything in the ocean. It's pretty fun! I enjoyed the difficulty of the fights with alligators, the Apexes of the area, and even the story with Scaly Pete. Getting my Shadow evolutions as early as I did because I wanted them so badly allowed me to just breeze through some things in a super fun fashion. Original hunter fights were fun, then got annoying after Pete because almost every ship had that stupid shield and refused to drop bombs. Tail slap targeting when you did have a bomb was inconsistent and frustrating. Narrator is annoying, keyboard controls are a nightmare on the wrists/fingers and for progressing game-play.\t\t\t\t\t
