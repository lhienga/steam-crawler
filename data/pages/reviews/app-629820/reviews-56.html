\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly really enjoyed the game. This is also the first game I've 100% completed, so there's bonus points lol.

Although there were some troubles. The controls were a little funky to get used to. It took me a while to learn how to launch myself out of the water without screwing it up, though that's probably a skill issue and not a game issue. The beginning of the game (after the tutorial) was probably the most difficult, but makes sense since you're just a baby.
There were also some bugs; there were a few times where I was trying to move forward but my shark kept moving to the left or right (there was no invisible wall).

(Truth Quest DLC SPOILERS)
Something that bothered me (and some others) was the sudden difficulty spike in regards to the apex targets in the Truth Quest DLC, and ESPECIALLY the Leviathan battle. I understand that it's basically the FINAL final boss, but man it took me forever to beat him and the atomic preds he summons. And I don't know if it was a bug or not, but he kept bringing his armour back up which was just annoying af.

But other than all that, it was genuinely a fun game for me, especially as an avid shark lover. The narrator was also hilarious (voiced by Chris Parnell, who voices Jerry in Rick & Morty), and I loved the various pop culture references. The graphics (I had on High) were pretty good.

Overall, a solid 8/10 for me!\t\t\t\t\t
