\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is just okay. I bought it on sale because I really wanted to play it and like it, but the game mechanics leave a lot left to be desired. Too many times I found myself getting stuck somewhere. The auto-surfacing/jumping really got annoying throughout the game. The inconsistency with wiggling forward, jumping, and lunging. The game doesn't do a whole lot so it's tough to excuse. The story was non-existent for me. I didn't play it for the story, but I initially tried to at least pay attention to it but by the end I was just skipping it. The RPG elements are alright, though there really isn't a lot of variety. I pretty much used one set the whole game.

All in all, it's a fun experience if you think you'd like the game and it's on sale, but I would not buy it at full price. If you're going into this thinking "RPG" you should dial back your expectations because it's barely that. At the time of this review I've 100% the achievements for the main game and DLC so there really isn't a lot of replayability for this game and it's easy to 100%.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've ever had fantasies of what it would be like to be a great bull shark, or have a repressed urge to maul and thrash unsuspecting beach goers as a Megalodon after watching Jaws, The Meg or Deep Blue Sea then this game is for you!

PROS
- A fun evolution system that, while not super deep, still gives you a good reason to get all of the collectibles and complete all the missions
- The evolution themselves look phenomenal and make your shark look absolutely terrifying and baddass!
- The combat, while somewhat limited, was still fun and plausible given that you're playing as a shark. Also as you unlock more mutations you gain more abilities, thereby making the combat more fun & interesting.
- Great visuals and audio/music that really help to immerse yourself within this aquatic, savage world
- I'm not from Louisiana so I can't attest as to the quality of the human antagonist's accent but it sounded great to me and the voice acting was quite good. I however speak French and after visiting New Orleans it was really cool to hear & understand some of their French/Creole expressions in-game!

CONS
- It took me ~16 hrs to complete everything and during that time I experienced 2-3 crashes. Not awful, as I've experienced worse, but still a little annoying...
- The narrator did a great job at delivering his often-humourous voice lines but sadly they began to repeat themselves quite often about 1/2 way through the game :(
- I was disappointed that none of the NPCs commented on my shark's crazy-looking evolution and massive size as I attacked their boats or their beaches. After putting in so much effort into designing distinct & impressive evolution sets it's a shame that they go unnoticed within the game :(
- I was looking forward to the DLC and its new mutations but after reading the many negative reviews in regards to its many technical issues, grind-like combat and missions, short length and frustrating final boss I unfortunately gave it a pass\t\t\t\t\t
