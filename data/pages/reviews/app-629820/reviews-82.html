\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game really. It starts off slow and challenging to take on big game twice your size, and then evolving slowly into (an overpowered) Apex to take on almost anything your size and even bigger, fearlessly. It makes you feel like you grown from Shark food to well, shark.

My only gripe is the lack of a minimap in the HUD. The low 3rd-person perspective and similar looking bodies of water makes it difficult to actually tell where you are when navigating. Opening the main map every few seconds is pretty annoying, especially because distractions can cause you to turn away and then you suddenly have no idea which way you're facing now without - you guessed it - opening the map each time. Really would've helped with the sense of direction just by having that in the corner. Why really?

Otherwise, good game.\t\t\t\t\t
