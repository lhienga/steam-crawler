\r\n\t\t\t\t\tManeater is a bit of a guilty pleasure of mine. It is the essence of the cathartic, visceral violence I crave in combat; to the point that I can just zone out and enjoy the grind. It can also be pretty comical and the plot is not a bad one (though a narrative game, this is not).

I certainly enjoyed it enough to purchase and play it twice. Once through on the PS4, and now on PC for Truth Quest. It is also surprisingly good on Steam Deck.

This all said, if the thought of a super mutant shark rampaging across a port city does not sound like pure dumb fun then Maneater might bore you. You eat, fight, and find collectibles across several map regions. That is the game, cutscenes aside. So no grand RPG here, just an oceanic mayhem sandbox.\t\t\t\t\t
