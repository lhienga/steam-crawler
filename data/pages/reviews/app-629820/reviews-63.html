I played the base game to completion twice and the dlc to completion too and I have to say I really enjoyed my time with the game. Not super difficult in fact some quests are SUPER easy but the boss for the Truth Quest DLC was a real stress lmao.

Overall, 9/10 if you love animal games, and don't have a problem with copy and paste type gameplay\t\t\t\t\t
