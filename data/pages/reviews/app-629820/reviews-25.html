\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's if they were trying to copy Burnout Paradise in style, but you're a growing/evolving shark monster thing. Exploration is bland in general, there are landmarks to visit that are all parodies or references to other things, but they don't really add too much to the overall game. The navigation and combat on mouse & keyboard is weird but better than gamepad controls (for me). Combat is fairly repetitive, most of the time you clamp down on something and shake them until they're gone. Fighting with boats could have been more fun, but it's just 'there'. You have to flop around on land as well, which is a bit of variety, but it's only really to collect collectables, or have a feeding frenzy with beach goers. I got to the last boss, after multiple frustrating attempts, I decided it wasn't worth the annoyance to finish the game. Also, the DLC adds extremely overpowered evolutions that essentially make all other evolutions useless. Not recommended unless it's on sale for about $10.\t\t\t\t\t
