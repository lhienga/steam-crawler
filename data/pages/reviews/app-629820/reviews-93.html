\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game needs to stop pretending that this is an ultimate power fantasy designed game because it is not in any possible way. I do believe this company do deserve to go bankrupt for misrepresenting their product but first, let me go into a bit of details why this game is absolutely a mockery of its own game and your time and effort as a player.

The game starts out in the prologue as a power-fantasy show to start you off but it quickly turn into a lie when the real game starts after the mommy shark is killed and your real protagonist daughter shark takes her place. It's not only directed sarcasm at the take of feminism, with its title, the gender of both sharks and protraying how masculine and powerful feminism really is in the commentary but the reality of the mechanical aspect of this game is not. If you value your time and effort, you will definitely skip this game. You soon hit a grind where you find your upgrades entirely meaningless in boasting your own stats that matter like your attack power, def power and speed. The amount of skills you get in the beginning are miserable and they are not effective at all, even against the lvl 8 alligators. The problem is by the time, you reach lvl 8 on your female shark, you can still fail to produce reliable results of downing one lvl 8 alligator yourself. Out of more than 10 attempts each session I tried, I only managed to down 1 each session. It is this bad and clearly show why this game is an absolute failure when it comes to its gameplay and its marketing. If you think you are into the comedy aspect of this game, you won't get it either as you can't even move past the area to the next story quest for a long time and let's face it, the constant mockery commentaries become annoying fast as it keeps reminding us everything that is of the opposite nature to what is really happening in the gameplay. An example is how powerful and confident your shark is terrorizing the area when you really can't do that even to the next difficult mob you see, which is the lvl8 alligators and there are plenties of them to deal with to move past the area.

Worst of all, this is constant mis-expectation to the player create not a fun game where you can experimenting with crazy, fun, and overpowered antics because you are mostly safe but instead, create a stale boring grind which really don't pay off and serious mode that is so bad that kills the entire fun out of the game. Do not buy this game even if you think the comedy(which is almost non-existent to some players) and mockery aspects are to your liking, because the gameplay will fail you miserably hard to even hold your attention.

This is definitely the clearest example of how not to make a power fantasy game. If you buy a power fantasy game, and you are not curb-stomping as you put in the efforts and the challenge bar is too high without giving the ability to curb-stomping your opponent's abilities even better, then it is a failure from the perspective of game mechanic design wise. Look somewhere else if you want a true power-fantasy game, which is barely non-existent these days and if you are looking for its comedy and mockery aspects, don't bother. You will get bored quickly with nothing else better to do beside grinding for a long time. You can't even move past the area to the next story quest, I remind you politely again.

Let me repeat it again. This is definitely the clearest example of how not to make a power fantasy game.
I'm done.\t\t\t\t\t
