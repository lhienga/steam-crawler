\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome game, I bought this game one night and played for 8-9 hours nonstop, went to bed for a couple hours, got up and finished the game with 100% achievements within 12.5 hours in total. Yes I actually did that. Why? No clue. The point is that this game is awesome, open world shark game where you can endlessly murder animals to your hearts content. Sounds evil but kinda the point of the game. Would absolutely recommend to those people who are into being the villain in games.

There were some things that bummed me out when I finished the game.
- Only 4 shark armor sets that were available (assuming you also have Truth Quest). I really wanted more shark armor sets because the game was really awesome. I know you can mix match the armor but that's besides the fact there were only 4 armor sets.
- The max level was capped at 40. Why? I know it also has to do with the size of the shark, but I really wanted to gain more levels despite the size cap for the shark.
- The max size of the shark. I know that you get even bigger than a great white, but I wanted to be the size of a whale. Even bigger if I wanted too! I bought the game not really knowing much about the max for everything so it really disappointed me when I saw that level 40 was the max level and the size of the shark wasn't growing anymore.
- Repeated dialogue. The narrator kept repeating the same dialogue like 100+ times every other time. I know it ran out of dialogue to say, but it could just be quiet. I also know you could just mute it, but I love the dialogue too, just not when it repeats the same one over and over.

But in the end I really loved the game and would love more content!\t\t\t\t\t
