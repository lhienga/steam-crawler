Be the Shark. Explore the Waters. Eat the Humans.
Maneater is an Open World RPG-like game where you be an ever evolving Bull Shark. Spiritual successor to Jaws: Unleashed and developers from Depth, Maneater allows you to quest and collect in a vast array of different regions with their own unique sights and layouts, you can evolve and upgrade your shark as they grow through stages of life and get revenge on Scaly Pete, the fisherman who killed your mother shark.

Pros
- Awesome Gameplay
- Lovely Graphics and Artstyle
- Lots of upgrades
- Very Distinct Regions especially Underwater
- Fun Story
- Cool Unique Builds with Body Armours
-- Bone Shark set to become a tanking battering ram shark
-- Shadow Set to become a metal-like zooming poisonous shark
-- Bio-Electric to become a glowing blue and lightning shark
- Lots of Collectables

Cons
- All the objectives, outside collectables, are all "kill this" type of objectives, which is standard what else are you going to do as a shark. The good thing is the large array of different sea creatures you can face against.
- While i love the fantastic designs of the body armours you can select, i do sometimes wish to make them invisible or at least choose to roll back the visuals to the lower grade versions of them, because the level 5 upgrades of a full set of armour can easily make you look like an alien/non-shark like.
- Even after 100% the objectives and collectables, it does require a bit of grinding to gather enough of the nutrients to upgrade all your items, specifically the minerals.

My Rating: 8.5/10
