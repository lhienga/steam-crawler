\r\n\t\t\t\t\tManeater has been a pleasure to play. It managed to be super fun and challenging without being utterly insane and frustrating. The only downside I could say is that the end-game replayability isn't as strong as I had hoped, as at about 27~ hours I've managed to beat the base game and the DLC, with a handful of side missions left and one more Steam achievement to earn. Of course one could always try new playthroughs, but I tend to be a stickler to my original playthrough, and plan to just relax in the game on and off after I collect everything.

But while it may not have hundreds of hours of content, I've thoroughly enjoyed every minute of playtime! The combat system, the abilities, evolution mutations, etc make it a very unique experience. The game also rewards you for being both creative and exploring the available mechanics. Here's an example;

When I was faced with ships that fired torpedoes at me, I was delighted to find that the unique electric-based dodge ability I had disabled the torpedoes and was basically a counter to them. And later on in the dlc quest, it finally clicked in my head that I could bite onto those disabled torpedoes and fling them with my tail at boats to make taking them down less tedious.

I really love that the game's combat system is so fleshed out and rewarding to play around with. And I'm really hoping there's more planned for the game! I did wait for a sale to buy Maneater and its dlc, but after how much I've enjoyed it, I'd buy another dlc in a heartbeat!\t\t\t\t\t

From Guppy to Megaladon
Great open world exploration game where you get to eat everything in sight, ocean life and humans alike.

Level/world design is really well done, especially under water. I had a few legitimate jump scares form predators hiding out of sight; so I would say atmosphere and sound are spot on. Mutations and perks keep the game refreshing as you go through each location, evolving and growing in size.

The missions in each new zone are repetitive (like far cry games). DLC added some cool mutations but the level was small. The final apex predator was very awesome and a lot of fun to fight. Overall a very fun enjoyable game.

It has achievements (with guides) and trading cards.

All in all I would definitely recommend even at full price\t\t\t\t\t
