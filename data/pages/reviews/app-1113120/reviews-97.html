\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a well-polished choices-matter kind of builder/farming game in space with a pretty decent storyline.The graphics and animations are great, the gameplay is solid. The storyline is compelling.However, a big bottleneck is created when you have to start finding and thawing thousands of people. It feels like a huge timesink for nothing. They basically MAKE you wait to win while your people get more and more anxious about getting out of a system you're basically STUCK in until you can thaw thousands of people out a few at a time (or dozens if you're like me and used a whole section just for that purpose) but, it still takes hours of doing NOTHING ELSE... which seems really pointless given that the rest of the game just about everything you do feels like it has a purpose.So, other than that portion of the game, I definitely give this a thumbs up.The tedium does start kicking in around chapter 3 though. Launching the same probes for the same resources to be mined by the same ships does get boring at some point.\t\t\t\t\t
