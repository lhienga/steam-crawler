\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, before I start, I rage exited the game like three or four times before I finished it. You gotta brace yourself for that. But hey, it was worth it!
Like a lot of developers (developers!, in general, not just the game developers), these guys have made a mistake, assuming that everyone is thinking the way they do. That's not the case in 99 % of the cases.
No spoilers ahead, just the instructions as to how to play this game:
1. Technological upgrades are VITAL. First things first, open all branches of the tech upgrades, then, upgrade the Tech Lab, in order to be able to upgrade stuff even when you do not have enough science points (slowly, but surely)
2. Do not skimp on people: keep your work conditions OPTIMAL and people happy
3. Do deep investigations (the ones that require resources) to gain enough science points to facilitate the upgrades
4. MAX OUT the solar panels (and build a nuclear plant just in case)
5. Do not push people too hard: if you find yourself relying on PROPAGANDA or Stability buildings too much - you did something wrong.
6. In the end, you should have one sector open and stable, but not occupied with anything (I did it with 3.5 thousand people aboard).
7. EVA Airlocks for at least 5 sectors (so that the HULL would be maxed out, always).

As for the game itself - it is a very VERY interesting adventure, and a beautiful one. I absolutely recommend it!\t\t\t\t\t
