\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed the game. Comparising it to Frostpunk does not do justice to neither of the games. If anything, the games should be compared to "triggered strategies" like Alien Legacy or Deuteros. Or even better comparison, criminally forgotten Iron Seed or Star Flight.

It is journey of the last remnants of humanity across the universe in ark falling apart looking for planet to settle and survive in high sci-fi environment. The focus of the game is on efficient building and rebuilding to keep your efficiency high. And some exploration thrown in.

Although rough around some edges and with usual issues of triggered strategies (very low replayability and exploitability of non troggering events), I enjoyed the game.\t\t\t\t\t

Replayability is probably low compared to something like Rimworld or Factorio.\t\t\t\t\t
