First two acts feels like "WOW" but later... 3rd act of the story feeling like a partly done and last act also looks like made in a hurry with no enthusiasm. I mean, it looks like developers have too many interesting ideas but they couldn't done them at all bcs of something (hurry in release, investors rush etc.) and they released what they managed to do. Also, there too many gaps in a story which left player with questions and have space to image story to full end. And actually it's not bad at all to have understatement story lines, but not as much as this game has.
Ending looks like undone and pointless at all chooses.

2. Gameplay: As this is a city builder with restrictions and survival aspects i don't really awaited from IXION something like as Cities Skyline, Surviving Mars or Ostriv and yes - it is not like city builders, really not.
And if we look on this as a survival RTS (mheh) we could say it has too many issues: in logistic(it's annoying), in resource management(if you grind - u always has no space for everything and everyone), in logic of some mechanism. But it's not critical, you could handle it and used to click on different menus and make a right decisions and after in some period of time u start to understand how to abuse different mechanics for getting better management of whole ecosystem.
On the other hand many aspects of the gameplay feels like developers trying to increase your playtime by non-interesting activities and grind. And btw, if u grind whole game u literally have no problem with any type of resources.
Also, management of hull integrity really interesting mechanic, but after 2nd act u have no problem.

3. Immersivity: 10/10. Nothing to say. Really good.

4. Support: Absent. I mean, developers fix bugs, for sure, but nothing else. I hope in future we could see some DLC bcs this game really required them.
Also i hope we could see second part or spin-off.

5. Cost: fair enough but i think it better to buy it by 30-40% sales off.

6. Do you need to by this game?: yes, good ambiance, space atmosphere and space RTS entirely.
Score: 7.5/10 + requires DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
