The game has huge potential, but it drowns in routine.
Interesting gameplay throughout the game is constantly replaced by routine operations. And this substitution reaches its climax by the end of the game (up to 99% of the routine gameplay). You already start to doubt the game In the first 2 hours and think about requesting a refund. And you might think: "Maybe the game will become more interesting next?". No, it won't.

The main problem of the game is manual resource management. You will hate the resource management screen. You can set up logistics, but it will only work in a static system (when you don't build anything). But you will constantly expand and rebuild everything around and constantly move the sliders on the resource management screen back and forth. Devs, it's easy to fix - when specifying the construction priority, just automatically request resources from warehouses from other sectors.

The second problem of the game is also its feature. When you destroy a building, you fully return the resources spent on it. Thus, in order to destroy a building, you must have enough space in the warehouses to return the resources. And if there's no enough space, then you can't demolish the building either(: This brings you back to the first problem: you have to manually transfer some of the resources to another sector in order to free up space in the warehouses.

Apogee of manual resource management.
When you have to fill an area of 1000 cells with generators in the last mission (of course you have to free space for them), you will forget all the good things that were in the game(:

One more spoiler
I was scared by Devs for a long time of the Piranesi ship. I built 3 air defense systems. And it didn't work for me at all. The most disappointing thing was that I didnt even see the Piranesis attack animation and how this air defense works (: This Piranesi was completely useless, although the developers could make it slowly chase Tiqqun, forcing a player to constantly run away. That would be interesting.

And another one
I found a planet that Tiqqun's AI "didn't see" in the last mission. I suspect that it could led to the alternative ending of the game, but for some reason I could not interact with that planet in any way and had to assimilate with aliens. Perhaps for such a scenario, it was necessary to agree to the replacement of the onboard AI of Tiqqun with another one.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIXION - A Captivating Sci-Fi Adventure

Review:

IXION is a fascinating game that immerses players in a captivating sci-fi adventure. With its futuristic and visually stunning environment, innovative mechanics, and intriguing narrative, the game stands out as a remarkable experience in the genre. Here are the key elements that make IXION a must-play for sci-fi enthusiasts:

Sci-Fi Environment:
The environment in IXION is visually breathtaking, featuring detailed and immersive futuristic settings. From spaceships and orbital stations to exotic alien planets, the game transports players to a vibrant and wondrous sci-fi universe. High-quality graphics and impressive visual effects contribute to the sense of immersion in this futuristic world.

Innovative Mechanics:
IXION introduces innovative gameplay mechanics that explore futuristic concepts and advanced technologies. Players get to experience the use of high-tech devices such as exploration drones, exoskeletons, and augmented reality systems. These mechanics add an extra layer of immersion and make the gameplay more engaging and thrilling.

Intriguing Narrative:
The narrative of IXION is intriguing and immersive, with surprising twists and thrilling discoveries along the way. Players embark on a space journey filled with mysteries, uncovering the secrets of a distant future and facing ethical and moral dilemmas. The rich and complex narrative keeps players immersed in the story, eager to unravel its secrets.

Space Exploration:
One of the most exciting aspects of IXION is space exploration. Players have the opportunity to travel to different planets and moons, uncovering their secrets and facing unique challenges. The sense of discovery and wonder while exploring new alien environments is unparalleled and adds an element of excitement to the gameplay.

Customization and Progression:
IXION offers a comprehensive customization system, allowing players to personalize their ships, and equipment. As they progress through the story, players can unlock new abilities, technologies, and upgrades to enhance their gameplay experience. The constant progression and sense of evolution keep players engaged and motivated.

In summary, IXION is a captivating game that immerses players in an engaging sci-fi environment. With its stunning visuals, innovative mechanics, intriguing narrative, and the thrill of space exploration, the game is a must-play for sci-fi enthusiasts.
