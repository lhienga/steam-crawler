\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a survival city builder, like Frost Punk or Against the Storm. Involves juggling various resources to keep from dying and grow your population. Emphasis on tile by tile space management. There's also an overarching adventure style story with mission objectives and chapter progression.

Really fun, very intense on challenge mode.

As somebody who doesn't like too many tutorials, this game had the right amount of them.

Interesting lore and story, tho I admit I was skipping it after the third chapter, since I became more engrossed in the gameplay. Really nice soundtrack - downtempo edm stuff.

Some wonkiness with the UI, it took me an hour or two to get the hang of it. Of course, in a game like this, you want to compare the "population stat" to "homes available stat", and you want to compare the "workers available" with the "workers needed", but the former is only available through a sub menu, and the latter is in a tooltip or you can also find it in that menu. This is a lot clunkier than some other city builder games, for example, Timberborn gives you easy access to all this information.\t\t\t\t\t
