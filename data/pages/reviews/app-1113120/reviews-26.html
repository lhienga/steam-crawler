That's why I'll recommend picking up on sale, though the regular price is also worth it imo.\t\t\t\t\t

I take back the previous comment regarding the guard railed strategy but will say that after playing the game for a while and realising what research options were available to me I felt like it was best to restart and plan some research early on especially around the research generation speed. I do admit that I unnecessarily horded resources on my run which really was a time waste in the end. I do feel that if you got to certain points in the story and you were not already well provisioned there could be disasters due to some story sections having a real time aspect to it.\t\t\t\t\t
