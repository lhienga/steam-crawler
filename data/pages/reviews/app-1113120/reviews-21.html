\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, it's a very interesting game. Great main and side stories. Excellent soundtrack. Nearly fully customizable difficulty that allows you to experience the game the way you want to. Parts of it feels underdeveloped, and other parts definitely needs to be explored further.

The idea of the game is what makes it interesting. They're sitting on top of a very promising one that they haven't fully explored yet; a city builder with survival elements, but the real gimmick is that the city moves. However, as there is no endless mode, you'll play it once or twice most likely.

If they ever add an endless mode with a touch of rogue-lite mechanics, like a path selection and system randomizer, this could very well end up as one of my favourite games of all time. The building blocks are definitely there, and if you play the game you might feel the same.

There are other parts of the game that feels lacking, and somewhat unpolished, but even though I absolutely hated some of the stuff, overall I very much enjoyed this game. If you are into survival city-builders, highly recommended. Otherwise, wait for some sale to grab it.\t\t\t\t\t

I think if you are looking for a fairly challenging survival city builder this is a good bet. If you want a sci-fi city builder then like me play on the lowest difficultly and be sure to pre-plan your sectors with paused buildings. Overall I do recommend this game though it is a more neutral review as I think that a large part of the game is just not something I enjoy, but it does still accommodate what I wanted with very fine difficulty controls, and this makes it positive.\t\t\t\t\t
