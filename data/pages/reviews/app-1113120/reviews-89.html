\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMasterpiece. This is a genre I know and love and this is hands down the best that I've played.
I never review games, but the steam rating it has at the moment is misleading.
It is difficult yes, if that is a turn off for you, at the time of writing this it looks like they're going to be adding the ability to change the difficulty soon so I'd say maybe wait for that. The story is really worth experiencing.

This is not a game to rush through, you should take your time with it. Read the tech descriptions, read through all the events (the results aren't random and there are usually clues).

The gameplay itself is a great mix of the Stellaris map nav and Frostpunk. Each chapter introduces new mechanics and the progression is insanely cool (can get a bit overwhelming if you're playinig for a long sitting, I'd suggest sticking to a chapter per play session max).

All in all, if you're into management games and sci fi, you need to play this game.\t\t\t\t\t
