\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm tired of games that try to replicate the success of Frostpunk but end up being mere wannabes. We all remember the challenging gameplay of Dark Souls, and I understand that developers want to capture that "Git Gud" feeling. You can do a difficult game and an easy game at the same time.

However, this game disappoints by falsely advertising itself as having an easy mode. The so-called "fun ride" quickly turns into a constant struggle to progress and survive. It's frustrating that even with a difficulty selector, the game only makes superficial changes, completely neglecting the necessary adjustments like addressing the random penalties.

It's as if the developers wanted to capture the essence of Frostpunk but fell short in their execution. The city-building aspect feels boring and lacks meaningful interaction. The space theme seems to be mentioned sporadically, serving no real purpose other than reminding players that they are supposedly in space.

Overall, I'm utterly disappointed with this game. It aimed to be a fun experience but failed to deliver. Instead, it became a time-wasting attempt to imitate Frostpunk. My advice would be to hold off and wait for Frostpunk 2, which is bound to provide a far more satisfying and worthwhile gaming experience.\t\t\t\t\t
