\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is genuinely fantastic. It's an amazing piece of sci-fi city building with a good narrative arc that kept me hooked from start to finish. If you are someone who enjoyed games with rough decisions that can be avoided through careful management like Frostpunk. I think you may enjoy IXION.

Obviously the game isn't perfect but the story is well paced, the game play fair and creative with the mechanics they have. Without going too much into spoilers, I was not expecting a BOSS battle in my city sim, but I was 100% for it. If I had to throw some critique upon the game, I'd definitely say the UI can be a little clunky at times. I recommend going into the setting's right away and binding gamespeed up/down to some easy access keys, as you'll be using it plenty. Also if would be rather nice if they implemented a select tool in the space map so I can set a group of resources to be avoided, instead of having to click through them individually. Either way the games strengths more than make up for it.

Please if you liked frostpunk or enjoy good hard sci-fi try IXION. Games like this don't show up enough and I hope to see more! (Also note if you really want the Frostpunk experience, set your starting difficulty to Challenging. The base difficulty is rather forgiving.)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis Game is Amazing.
If youre on the Fence whether to buy, watch some of the playthroughs of the beginning,
to get a feel for the Game.
Dont watch anything from episode 1,
Itll ruin the Element of Surprise, that makes this game so much fun.
When you are shocked by what happens in Episode 1, and youre totally thrown for a curveball.
It is insane, you dont know than what to expect.
And i wont say more.
As for the Gameplay it is a City Builder Game.
But it is more like a Survival City Builder Game.
Where things feel like at any second they will fall apart
I played the Game Twice, and barely got past the First Mission,
And at that point my station is falling apart.
Than i read a Walk-through and realized everything i did wrong, which is very not obvious.
The Game will punish you for your mistakes, and often wont tell you when your making them what they are.
Kind of like real life.
So heres my Walkthrough Guide.
I recommend you play at least once, twice, or as many as you can before you want to give up,
Before reading the guide.
Because that feeling, of What Am I Doing????
Why is everything on Fire??
Why is the Station about to Tear itself apart??
Why are people Rioting, and Starving all the Time???
Who made the mistake of letting me be in charge are priceless.....
And once you read the Guide,
This doenst happen as much, but I think, Failure, learning from Failure, Trying Again.
Learning its ok to fail and try again, even when making decisions that you dont know what will happen, are core parts of the Game.
And to me Life, some really important life lessons.
So without Further Ado, i recommend you buy the Game.
Play the Prologue and First Mission twice or as many times as you can stomach failure before reading the Guide.
And if youre thinking of buying, dont watch any video for the first mission only the prologue which is shown in the store screen.
Youre welcome to ignore my advice, but i think youll miss out.
Any way this the walkthrough im using now, and it helps me alot.
