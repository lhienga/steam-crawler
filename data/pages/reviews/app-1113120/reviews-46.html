The Good: Graphics are great, Music is excellent, UI is well organized. Story is weird and wonderful.

A bit more detail:

I have played for almost 140 hours and finished Challenge mode. The gameplay loop is an interesting take on the logistics game. On top of managing your supply chains, you have the constant time pressure of acquire/smelt Alloy -> continuous hull repair, and a social engineering mechanic where you have to see to the needs of your progressively more depressed populace. The game is punishing. If you make a mistake you pay for it immediately. Power flickers? 40 people will get injured. Refuse to turn off factories for 20 cycles? Maybe you get a general strike and your entire industry district stops functioning. Did you research the wrong things and now you are out of science? (Upgrade your tech lab as soon as you can). If you keep on top of things, you should be able to manage your populace well enough that they don't space you. The music is excellent and adds a lot to the vibe of the game. I love the graphics! The level of detail on the internal and external views is great and I enjoy the galaxy view as well. The UI is well layed out and I found it intuitive.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo... Where to begin?
First of all I'd like to say : despite my positive review I have A LOT to say about this game...and not always in a good light.

I'd like to ask for comprehension on my writing as I am not an english speaker from birth

First the pro :
I find it a good survival game. I seen some review on the difficulty of it and all the reset people had to do. My opinion? It's alright. Sure the game is hard (Played on normal difficulty tho) and I had to say that I nearly died at least 4 time by the end of the second chapter. Lack of some ressource or poor management of them. I think the system could be upgraded, but if function well enought for me to save it last second. It can get pretty intense sometime and give you some good scare about whetever you will die or no and honestly, great, that's what you like in a good survival city management game. I only had to reset against the Piranesi (That I will not speak of to avoid spoil)

So where? Where do I find the problem in that game?

First of all and probably the most annoying for me : Bug...bug bug bug bug bug everywhere. There is probably more but let me tell you all the one I can think of at the time I write this :

1- Sometime you ask for a ressource to be brought to one sector......and it just doesn't move, checked if the sector with that ressource coule bring it, it does, does it have enought of that ressource, it does, can it bring it to stock at the right sector? it does.....so why? The solution I found? Ask him to bring the maximum amount or just turn the option to send it on and off and on and off until it do

2- Sometime you ask for a building to be destruct. The building have access to the road, and have access to a workshop...and never get deconstructed, normally just turning off the request for a deconstruct and turning it on again work....but by the end I had a building (a monument if I am not wrong) that said to me that it wasn't even enable to reconstruct it as there was no workshop available...so I constructer a second one right beside it....and no change, great

3- The game permit you to deconstruct a building that produce trash...problem? If you did not turn on the recycle policy the trash don't get burned while it is deconstructed. So at the end you still have that building, that don't work and take place because you can't do anything of the trash withing it UNTIL you accept the recycle policy, do something with it (Policy that can take as long as 15 cycle to recharge before you turn it back off again)

4- Same as the first three bug, sometime when deconstructing a building you have a ressource left...it can be taken back to a stockpile, there is one available and with some places left....the worker don't have any other task....and it never get brought back. Only option is to reconstruct that building and deconstruct it again while hoping that this time it'll work

5- The game let you deconstruct a ship that explore something, after some testing I discovered that normally the mission finish then the ship get brought back....unless it doesn't, just stopping the timer on the mission not letting you move the ship with for only solution a reset

There are some minor one like the end starting the audio without having loaded the image....but my review is starting to get long

What I would like to add is....man what is this story? I liked the main plot and tbh I can understand the author of any story to have some loose end to let people wonder and speak about it...but here? Half of the planet you visit have a little story associated with it...and half of them let you with that only thought at the end "what was that?"...not like the story was bad....but worse, the little story seemed interesting but you are left with 0 answer on what happened or anything wich from a narrative standpoint can be good one or two time.....not ten and more.


In the end, I still liked it. It does not have a great replayability but I still spent 23 hours on it while getting it on sale (half price or more I believe). I had some god laught, good scare and genuiely enjoyed the main story (Thought even the end on that could be upgraded).

If the bug get fix and the story have a re writing it is totally worth the money.....if it is not...then you may want to wait on a future sale before paying it or you might have a bitter feeling left.

It's the first time I left a review that long, I hope I may have helped some people. May everyone reading this have a good day\t\t\t\t\t
