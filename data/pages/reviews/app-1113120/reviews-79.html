\r\n\t\t\t\t\t5 hours in, somehow already failed my first Voyage. At first I thought the game wasn't really that challenging (Im usually bad at management games) but they neatly split the difficulty into different chapters. Despite failing it was still fun and I plan on beating the game.
Positives:
The way you can exchange Resources between your multiple "cities" so to say is really nice.
Despite a lot of Views and unique things nothing really gets overwhelming and you're kinda guided through (in a non intrusive way IMO).
A few negatives:
Constantly sending a Probe out for finding resources kinda gets tedious (might change later I didn't get to the final tech trees).
Constantly fixing the Hull is also kinda getting annoying because of the constant resource drain (Probably solved later though).
The way the Storage system works can get kinda annoying and cause lockups / force you to place down a Structure you're gonna deconstruct 5 seconds later(Probably mitigated if you don't suck at planning as much :) ).\t\t\t\t\t
