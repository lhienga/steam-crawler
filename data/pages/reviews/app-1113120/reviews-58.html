\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll start by saying I love the storyline, it's actually pretty interesting. However the actual station management mechanics seem extremely basic and boring. It's just not engaging, It's oddly too simple and yet more complicated than it needs to be in the wrong areas. The best way I can think to describe it is poorly balanced.

Once you open a new sector of the ship suddenly you realize peoples issues are specific to different sectors. So you can have your original sector perfectly happy, work load balanced and awesome. But your second sector has major morale issues and strikes every 5 minutes, it's tediously annoying.

I found the Solar System fleet management to be an interesting concept. Quite honestly this game reminded me a lot of Frost Punk, but you know... in space. I feel like that's what they were going for anyway.

Frost Punk however did a much better job of smooth resource management and overall "vibe".

This game is interesting and I can see it's potential, but the areas that it lacks just kill it for me. It's so strange how they knock it out of the park in some areas and just flatline in others.

If I had to put a price tag on what I feel this game is worth.... Maybe $20 or $25 tops. But with it's potholes of lacking engaging gameplay I don't see this being worth $35.... Buy on sale or pass in my opinion. (At least until they make it more engaging)\t\t\t\t\t
