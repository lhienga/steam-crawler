\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall i'm pretty happy with this DLC, maybe there are better DLCs out there to purchase if you don't already own all the DLCs, however, if you do own all of them then this will be a nice addition to your collection especially if you enjoy exploration and story content.

- The new civics are interesting and add more flavour to empire builds
- The new Astral Actions are a nice touch, a few could use some work but most offer some nice additional bonuses
- The new relics and bonuses for completing Astral Rifts are pretty strong and worth your time
- Lots of interesting events and additional story content unlocking over the course of the game making the exploration part of the game more enjoyable and last a lot longer.
- Astral Rifts can branch off in different directions meaning when you encounter an event during another play through the outcome will be different (if you choose different options of course).

Maybe it could be a little cheaper but either way i feel I'm getting my money's worth.
