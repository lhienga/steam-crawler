\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor the price of 20 bucks you get 4 - 6 more anomalies per playthrough.

You get to make some choices, most of which don't really matter as far as I can tell.

100% of the rifts I had played out like this: There is a new planet. There is this situation. Choice A or Choice B. Rift resolved.

I'm really underwhelmed. This was sold as Parallel / alternative Realitys. That was basicly false advertising. They did nothing with that permise. This could have been another anomaly pack.

There is a new resource you can use for VERY LIMITED BUFFS. Like, popping up a hyper relay for DOUBLE the influence cost. Yeah, no thanks, I'm never using that. There is no reason to buy this DLC just for the buffs. The game plays fine without them and they can be safely ignored if you wish to do so.

There are no new technologies. There are apperently relics, but I haven't found any of them. I have found a new unique commander.

There IS some ingame lore being wraped up and I like when devs bother to do that, so there.

If you want to buy this for the story content, I strongly advise you to check out the Distant Stars Story Pack DLC and the Ancient Relics Story Pack DLC first.

This is a 5 buck DLC at most so buy at a large discount. Or don't.

Buyer beware.\t\t\t\t\t
