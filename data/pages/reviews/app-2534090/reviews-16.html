The number of localization/text lines is roughly equivalent to all of Ancient Relics archeology sites (~1200) and trades off the quantity of archeology sites for the depth, risk and reward and branching paths potential instead.

- Storylines that have alternate timelines to 2 of the precursors.

- A civic that gives the ability to play a hyper-defensive empire that can defend planets with 6 digits (100,000+) defense numbers that will take 100 years of bombardment or a planet cracker to break and guard starbases and territory super hard.

- An alternative take to the pre-FTL exploration civics in First Contact without the tech loss and challenge.

- A civic that lets you play spiritualists that worship weird space portals (l-gates, shroud tunnels, astral rifts or wormholes)

- A civic that lets you get dark matter tech without having to beat fallen empires. It also grants unique edicts that use dark matter to achieve research, espionage bonuses, alloys and energy credit bonuses. (You can also spawn this on AI empires for an alternative method of getting the dark matter tech debris without going through FEs)

- An origin that has a unique archeology site and rift, allowing you to jump start both minor artifacts/archeotechs and astral rifts at the same time. Within 40 years, I had 5 astral rifts done and hyper relays before even getting the pre-requisite tech.

- Various actions from researching astral rifts including but not limited to: Making more astral rifts so you’re guaranteed 5 (and all the actions) if you find just 1, research hyper-relays and quantum catapults, give a permanent boost to quantum catapult effectiveness, ability to instantly generate forges without rare crystals and alloys, being able to prevent wormholes and l-gates from being accessed by closed border empires, instant jumps travel to your territory, the ability to summon extradimensional fleets for a time, removing relic cooldowns, and access to astral thread edicts.

- Astral thread edicts that let you get a 10% bonus to all resource production, +5 minor artifacts per month for a consistent source of minor artifacts if you get screwed on map generation or if you’re tall, cloaking strength and a massive bonus to shield HP and hardening.

- 8 relics, which is over half of the amount in Ancient Relics including the custodian patch that added relics to precursors!

- A new vassal type that cuts the cost of physics research tech in half. (aka research physics techs twice as fast).

- A new legendary paragon leader that can give military fleets, random physics techs or a bonus against certain crisis enemies.

- 2 Physics technologies related to astral thread extraction and exploring astral rifts.

- Several new ways of gaining leader immortality without psionics or synthetic ascension.

- New scientist leader traits and bonuses.

- Some new buildings.

- Ways of accessing various technologies before you’re supposed to.

- Replayable fun and things to do during the post-expansion midgame phase for those high skill scholar scientists.

Definitely recommend this, it’s been a blast so far and the scale of what goes on in the rifts stretches well past the already weird existing in Stellaris. There’s a universe full of blood out there. Another one full of trash. Find out for yourself what happens.\t\t\t\t\t
