Civics: They are good, but only for normal bio empires.

Origin: It is mediocre

Astral rifts: Astral rifts are basically upgraded archaeology sites and they generate a currency called astral threads which are the equivalent of minor artifacts. If you are lucky you can get some crazy rewards including new relics. If you are not lucky you can still spend the astral threads in a special shop that actually has some really good stuff. Some of the things you can buy in the astral thread shop include boosts to physics research, sublight speed, and getting a guaranteed research for hyper-gates/quantum catapults. Last thing of note is the true end game for astral rifts will lead you into the center of the galaxy where you will have to fight a mini crisis and honestly is what made it worth it.

New Relics: They are strong.

New Music: It is good.

Price: It is not good.

If you play spiritualist empires you should get this DLC. If you play non-spiritual normal bio-empires get this DLC if you have all the other DLC. If you play machines or hive minds you might wanna wait for a sale. If you are a sweaty well you have to buy this dlc cus you need the PvP or Tall civic. :p\t\t\t\t\t
