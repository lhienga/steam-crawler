\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo be frank, I'm not sure what my money has paid for; this is a full priced expansion but it compares poorly to the smaller story packs. It's a shame, because the narrative content itself is actually quite good and I have enjoyed it - but there's so little of substance to warrant it being the most expensive full priced expansion.

The 'Astral Plane' mechanics essentially boil down to a new type of Archaological Site that require a tech to access, a series of boons that function in a similar way to Relics, and a new resource type 'Astral Thread' obtained through research stations to purchase them with... and that's it?

Outside of the mechanics, there's a new Origin, a couple of Civics, and a few new species portraits that for some reason are low resolution compared to the existing ones.

As it stands, I'd have trouble recommending this at the current price to anyone. If it were reduced in price to match the other Story Packs, then I might suggest it for the more hardcore Stellaris fan, but even then it's a bit of a stretch.
