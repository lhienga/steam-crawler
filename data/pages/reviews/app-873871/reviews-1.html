\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great DLC all things considered adding a ton of new missions, mechanics and unit types, brand new nations with their respective voice acting and units, you even get the chinese for a single mission.
That being said i found a number of issues, the doctrines are bugged in the diary entries, like you choose the army and the diary has the navy entry and vice versa and the Japanese battle dialogue is in English with English accents even though the unit voice lines are in Japanese and the diary narrator speaks with a Japanese accent. Also even though the game adds several new pacific war aircraft, for some reason the american aircraft support is the same as in the base game with their late european counterparts in their late european camo and id markers.
The missions are very enjoyable and diverse in their objectives with both offensive and defensive missions. You have a japanese land campaign something rare and two naval battles. Naval battles are a big selling point but there are a few things to be said about them, you can not play naval battles in skirmish, the two campaign battles are all you get, the game's take on the naval aspect is unique and interesting, i do recommend replaying them with each doctrine as they have vastly different play styles, but it shouldnt be the sole reason for purchasing the DLC. In comparison to actual naval games these here are underwhelming and come out lacking, after all this is a land based game and the naval missions are a bonus, i just feel this is something that needs to be said given what i have read online.
The two campaigns feature flamethrower units which are absolutely deadly in taking out structures or groups of infantry. This is something that can absolutely ruin your mission progress and force you to restart. Finally i have noticed that in a number of missions the ai sends combat patrols through the jungles that may attack you from behind and sneak through your frontline, the japanese have access to tunnels that can spawn troops that will try to overwelm you while you are in a firefight near them.\t\t\t\t\t
