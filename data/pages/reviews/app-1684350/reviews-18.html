The story is nonsensical, most if not all game mechanics are superficial, it's just an absolute mess of a game.

The game itself looks awful, 2005 graphics vibe (look at freakin Luka, he's a Half-Life 1 model looking fella designed by a child).

A team of people worked on this, not one person trying to create a game on a bet in record time?
