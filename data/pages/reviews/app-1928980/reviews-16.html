\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEARLY ACCESS REVIEW
I wanted to like this game, and I put in 50 hours so I liked a bit. At the end of the day, it was unnecessarily complex but having so many different flavors of the same item and not being able to combine them when needed for crafting. I got to end game and at that point, it seems like it's going to just be continually grinding 1 of 3 available dungeons to get your gear score up for... what? I don't know. So, I got to the end, played one more time and uninstalled.
There is a serious issue with load times for realms and with a game file this size, what exactly is it that I don't already have downloaded? Why do the loading screens take so long?
The building mechanics leave a lot to be desired coming from a lot of other survival building games. There seems to be a limit to how many structures you can place. I never quite figured that out and coupled with the approximately 1 million augments you have, it becomes a challenge.
In character creation, you can to select other stock characters for your lineage, all the way up to your great great grandparents I think. I don't think you are required to do this but what is the point of that?
There are probably more complaints that I had along the way that I don't remember now. It is not an entirely terrible game, that aesthetics are nice.

My overall review: it was okay. I can't in good conscience recommend it right now. Maybe once it's at full release some of this will be fixed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to like this game but the developers are not making it easy for me. So at the moment don't buy it.

I love the art style, I love the graphics, the overall atmosphere and crafting games in general. I also know it's an early access and not everything works like it will in the end.

But I really dislike the crafting!
It feels like there are lots of good ideas in theory, but the way they are working in the game is just bad.
The basic idea of being able to control the stats of your crafted weapons is nice, but having a chest filled with 6 different types of the same material that you cannot combine is just crazy.

The next blocker that annoyed me so much that I had to stop playing because I was just so annoyed! I really dislike how upgrades for different workstations work.
You can control what your workstation is able to craft by placing upgrade items near it.
BUT you are not able to craft everything at one station. So you have to build many workbenches all with different upgrade items near them to build everything. That is just annoying. It makes no sense that you are limited on upgrades on one crafting station.
I don't want to build a skyscraper with a floor for each work station.
Also it is really hard to see which upgrade goes to what station because the indicator icon is tiny. Another point if you want to craft something that need different pieces the game won't tell you where you can craft it or get the recipe from. There is no explanation in the description text and no search for the essence traders.

I really want to like this game, but at the moment I will rather wait for an update. The thing I like most in these games, gathering and crafting is too annoying at the moment.\t\t\t\t\t
