\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has a ton of potential. I have been following it for quite some time. I was bummed to see them have to push back the release a few times, but now that it is out, I wish they would have pushed it back until it was ready. I have sunk 30+ hours into NG, and I don't have a lot to show for it. I enjoy the building, the game is beautiful, and the music really sets a tone for hours of fun solo or a group, however, I find myself waiting for loading screen for 5-10 minutes at a time, just for the message that I have disconnected or were unable to reach the realm. It takes me 3-5 tries to connect every time I log in, and without the offline mode, it is hard to keep any momentum going. Overall its just a big buzz kill that will leave you frustrated with little solution. I have no issue playing any other game online, with a hardwired connection. I can play virtually any game other than this. So for now, I would keep yourself form getting too attached, or from diving in altogether until they can get the servers stable. Its maddening. I hope I can review this in the coming weeks and say that all is well and the magic of the Fae has returned, but for now, the candle wont stay lit.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCan't recommend this enough. Nightingale turns the survival genre on its head by providing an interesting story, clever dialog, in-depth character customization, and complex crafting opportunities. It's lacking a bit of polish and the servers are VERY unstable, but I've been absolutely obsessed with this game since its early access release and can't put it down. Beautiful biomes, immersive SFX, interesting enemies that keep you on your toes. The ranged weapon shooting even feels good. I'm really thrilled to see what this studio has in store for Nightingale's future!

Almost forgot; this is online-only, and once you break through a certain point in the storyline, you quickly discover why. It's very MMO-esque, which is strange as you can play solo for over a hundred hours with no trouble. I'm curious how the upcoming offline feature will turn out.\t\t\t\t\t
