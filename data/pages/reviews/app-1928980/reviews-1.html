\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI tremendously enjoyed the first few hours playing this game.
That it captivates me more that Conan Exiles roleplay, says a lot..
Personally, this amalgamation of Steampunk, Magic and Fairies, is 100% up my alley, so I am not surprised, that I enjoy the story aspects most.
But... as someone who usually will play survival games only for their roleplaying communities.. I am utterly astonished, that I even played it as long as I did.. and plan to continue playing it.

I have only a few other games to compare it with... those being ARK and Conan Exiles..
But off the bat, Nightingale feels a lot less convoluted and complicated than those other two. That might be due to the fact, that the quests so far, explained most of the more intricate game mechanics and the crafting system feels very intuitive.
After eight hours.. I am still a bit overwhelmed, but working through that, is actually quite enjoyable.. opposed to tedious like I experienced with Ark.

Most of the puzzles are more on the easier side.. but as this game is still in developement, I am sure that might be fixed at some point in the future.
Same goes for other aspects I found.. meh, like the character model in 3rd-person-view o some quality of life tidbits..

The aesthetics... are just mindbogglingly beautiful.. and I dearly hope the game can be adapted for roleplay in the future, for it would be the most amazing setting for a High Fantasy story~

Soooo if you are into fairies, dazzling shimmering magic, victorian fashion and feeling like Mary Poppins, flying with an umbrella, this is your game!\t\t\t\t\t
