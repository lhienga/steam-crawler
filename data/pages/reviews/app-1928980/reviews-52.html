The main thing that lacks for me are the companions, they are lifeless to a degree that astonishes me considering this game is being made by ex-BioWare people. As someone who will play this almost exclusively in SP/offline mode (when that gets added), good companions are a must.

That said, I've had a lot of fun with the game so far! If you're into survival games, I recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
