\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are a lot of redeeming qualities here. The art and music for the game, and the singular voice performance are all wonderful. The building mechanics, fantastic and can only get better. The realm card idea for seeding new maps, and changing existing ones is really cool, but it isn't implemented in a compelling way. While overwrought too early, the complexity of the crafting system is chewy and interesting.

In short however, this game takes some bad ideas from both survival games and MMORPGs and makes them the core pillars of gameplay. IE: gearscores and content gating. Skinnerbox collect-&-kill quest design. Trite, repetitive POIs. Dreary, chaotic combat.
In its current state, most of the gameplay gets in the way of the harsh whimsical survival-adventure I think this game wants to be.

If I could change one thing right now to improve that experience? Remove the gearscore gating to content, possibly remove gearscores altogether. Find a more interesting approach to gating content, and balancing combat. The robust itemization on resources and gear are wasted because of it, especially early on. You've got the realm card system, I think that's all you need. Lean much, much heavier into that. Go crazy with it. Rather than finding the place in your abeyance realm to get the card you want, then crafting the gear to get into it. What if you summoned it into your abeyance realm? Or what if you had to travel to a different realm.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would love to recommend this game but I can't finish the tutorial because of a game breaking bug that hasn't been fixed. Yes, I've tried waiting in front of the swamp portal for over an hour. Yes, I've made multiple attempts.

Adding new servers is great and I do understand what EA means, but even in EA the game has to be playable and if bugs break the game they have to be fixed, of which I see no evidence. Have fun with your twitch drops, I guess.

Update: After another fix I have finally made it past the tutorial. The game isn't perfect but exceptionally pretty and I do like the approach the devs took. I wish I could port to the portal in my Estate realm, crafting from chests would limit the annoyance factor and some updates with controller input are needed but overall I am enjoying myself very much now and it is, after all in EA. Def looking forward to offline implement!\t\t\t\t\t
