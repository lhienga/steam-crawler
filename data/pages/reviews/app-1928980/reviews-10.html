\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor still being in Early Access, this game has great potential.

The realm walking is a fresh new take on the survival crafting genre, really letting you have a central home base, with adventure just a few steps away. Similarly with friends, you can build in wildly different areas, and not have to trek for twenty minutes to reach their homes.

Good lore and world-building is a big selling point for me, and this scatters it about the place, and in the level design, for you to uncover.

The RPG systems baked into the crafting is a nice touch, allowing you to craft tools, weapons, and armour to suit your play-style.

I think it still has a ways to go in certain respects, but overall, I'm excited to see where the devs can take this one!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFifty-plus hours of game time, here are my thoughts so far: great game! solid gameplay, the grind isn't insidious, hostile creatures and bound can be a bit frustrating sometimes, but that leans into the challenge of it. the customisation for building is amazing; for clothes and weapons, not so much (clothes especially are super disappointing atm and i'm hoping and praying there are better options in later game). there are LOTS of bugs, but it is still early access so i'm not surprised; i haven't run into anything game-breaking beyond network connection issues, which is a known problem that's actively being worked on. honestly, my biggest gripe is the load times. tbf, the higher your graphics settings, the longer it's gonna take to load a realm, but sometimes i'll start loading a new realm, go to the bathroom, get a water refill, pet my cat, come back and it's STILL loading; it really gets ridiculous sometimes. especially if you're grinding resources to build a new/bigger house or upgrading all your gear or something and you have to do a lot of going back and forth between realms. all in all tho, great game, love it, looking forward to full release.

tl;dr biggest problem is load times but otherwise a really solid game that i'm always excited to play and you should definitely try it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't like this game, an extreme amount of hand holding that reduces your game play to a series of checklists. Not what was sold with the idea of infinite open realms, you are constantly bounced between irrelevant NPCs that throw walls of text at you and that's considered story building. Such a gated experience, and the progression is a real grind. You are sent from realm X to realm Y to realm Z, and eventually you get thrown into a public realm with other players. Too late to make anything easier, but altogether unwelcome for anyone who was just playing with their friends.

Every realm is just a small island with a few of the same POIs that spawn, and you can loot them but there's no point mostly since you just need enough resources to upgrade your gear to open the next realm and then loot the next POIs with the next higher level loot. Anything left over is trash. Eventually you end up at the Watch and do the dungeons over and over to slowly upgrade your gear. Just not a fun loop for me, no sense of adventure or exploration.\t\t\t\t\t
