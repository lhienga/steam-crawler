\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can see what people don’t like about this game, there are quite a few QOL type issues, but I love the setting and the crafting too much. I’m hoping being early access means it be seeing more improvements as we go, but so far I feel like I’m getting my money’s worth.

The combat is fun, or at least passable in the sense of Fallout 4’s combat. I enjoy whacking the enemies with my stick. Also I love the crafting, although it needs a way to sort your storage ASAP.

More than anything though I love the atmosphere and feel of the game. It’s the perfect companion for listening to podcasts and just chilling. There is tons of room for improvement, but as an early access I don’t have a huge issue with any of its faults. Performance is probably the biggest issue so far for me. I get lots of screen tear, and the realms take a tad too long to load at times. (For reference I have two year old high middle range rig, 3070, SSD, and 64GN DDR5). Looking forward to seeing what they do as they transition to full release.\t\t\t\t\t

I like the different realms and their different difficulties that you can put them in. I like that you have a risk reward system with it. I also find that the replayability is given. I like the at least for me unique magic system. The fighting i find solid enough already. Building can be improved, thats were enshrouded is far more polished. But i feel like you can get there.

Currently loading times are extremely long, but as soon as offlin mode is there, i feel like this game will be truly awesome. Even in this state. Enshrouded i played through nearly until the last part of the map and i dont feel like i want to replay it until the final version is out. This game? I see myself playing every 6 months just to see whats new. Oh and i am 45 hours in and not in the (apparently non existent?) endgame in. Also i played solo, and it really works very well. I am very grateful for that.

I also think that this game is very pretty and the clothes you can get are awesome :D\t\t\t\t\t

YOU CAN PICK UP AND MOVE A FULL STORAGE BOX WITH EVERYTHING IN IT!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI usually don't do reviews on Early Access especially if I do not like the title.
Its my belief that if you control where you put your money and if you made a bad investment then, that's that.
But I want to delve into why I think my money is worth the value this go around.

Firstly, I like Steampunk and I like Cyberpunk. Probably because I'm a punk!
Also visuals mean a lot to me. I like art. I'm not an artist.

That aside you should examine if you like "Steampunk." before purchase.
Some cons for me:
Customization of PC - I'm grateful it is there put it doesn't appear cutting edge. Seems unfinished.
Character animations - are unfinished/EarlyAccess. Going to bed might as well be you activating a water dispenser to refill your sleep bar.

The Pros:
I feel that, for what I paid, I got my money's worth alone.
Granted I upgraded my GPU and RAM in september to better enjoy Bethesdas most recent debauchery and so what I am seeing if fantastical and really fun to explore.

With the combinations and RNG of worlds I find new things within hours of game play and I'm enjoying every morsel.
Sometimes the RNG does something really unexpected like load a bunch of enemies in behind a wall so when I knock a wall down they pour out and scare the mud out of me.

So I see bugs, I see unfinished content. I see I forked over money for it.
But I think my money is well spent. Its a beautiful world they created and I look forward to it being finished and then added to.\t\t\t\t\t
