\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI just can't recommend this game in its current state. There isn't a single thing this game does that isn't done way better in another survival game. The maps are boring and uninteresting, the combat is awful, the crafting is abysmal. It looks pretty though even as un-optimized as it is. The early game is such a slog with all the different material types (There are like 4 or 5 types of leather that are not interchangeable with each other.) Breakdown with my issue with the game:

Maps:
There is nothing to do the map that I was on was relatively devoid of enemies so all I had to do was mine several type of rocks and chop down trees. Speaking of mining and chopping trees who thought it was a good idea to not auto loot what you chop down? Nothing like shopping down a tree then chasing a bunch of rolling logs. The weather mechanics were a nice touch I like that you need to take shelter from the hail or you take damage.

Combat:
You basically just mash the attack button no real strategy and the enemy AI is terrible. There are stealth mechanics that I am going to guess get better at higher levels. I fought some goblin type guys, some wolves, some boar and I manged to snipe a baby dear. You need to attack the enemies corpse in order to get the mats from them which I find annoying. On top of needing to attack the corpses they will drop different types of materials so you need to just keep wandering around until you stumble upon some animals. But wait after killing them you may not get the hide you need because it may be a pelt or a skin and if it comes from a predator or prey (the different animal classifications) they wont stack.

Crafting:
There are too many materials. There are at least 4 different types of leather that I have found and they are not interchangeable so anything you craft needs the same type. This would be fine if the animals were always around but you need to wander to find them. Also why are there so many different rock types I already have several chests filled with rocks/mats that have no purpose. The crafting menus are super click heavy, you need to click to add each material separately having it in your inventory won't do it (bonus points though for the autofil materials option), and the benches have no storage and are not linked to your storage bins.

Overall there really isn't anything that this game does good it is a neat idea and the visuals are great but there just ins't anything that makes me want to play the game. The crafting sucks, the construction isn't great, combat is uninteresting, and there isn't a whole lot to do. I will certainly be checking back in a few months to see how it is doing but for now it is getting shelved.\t\t\t\t\t
