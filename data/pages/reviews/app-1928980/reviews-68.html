Puzzle actually puzzle me at times, Some are so easy a 4th grader could knock them out, some are quite frustrating but once you do one there all the same so hopefully they work on that.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game honestly was not ready for even early access release. Here are the issues from my perspective.
1. The combat AI is not challenging. There really is nothing more than getting zerged.
2. Gear level increase significantly outpaces the content making it even easier.
3. The minor cards are more of a nice to have, and currently content can be completed without ever using them.
4. 3 base biomes are not enough variety. The amount of MOBs is also too limited.
5. It appears more time was taken on making the game graphically appealing (which it is), rather than design.
6. The vault dungeons are another example of too easy and quickly boring. Running them over and over to get T3 essences can get you most of the needed recipes in a few hours.
7. Building materials for the better designs are needlessly tedious.
8. The augment system needs a revamp. There are way to many, and no good system to manage them.
9. Crafting overpowered gear at the T3 level is a significant issue. No real checks are built into the system.
10. With all of the materials that a farmed, there is not a good system to manage them while crafting.

Right now the game feels like a rushed cash grab. Hopefully the developers will listen to the significant amount of feedback they are getting and make some needed changes.
