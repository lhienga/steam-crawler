\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall - I enjoyed playing this game and would recommend it. I think it's worth the money for the hours I have enjoyed playing it. I look forward to seeing what comes next and I do hope they plan to expand and advance the story line and quests.

I have been playing the game for 100+ hours, I am fairly new to the video game world, previous games I have played where Skyrim, Hogwarts Legacy, Avatar Frontiers of Pandora, Skull and Bones, and Anno 1800.

I like survival crafting games, fantasy open worlds, it's not all about combat for me.

Some things I would like to see change or improve.

Crafting and Resource Gathering and Inventory
Not having the ability to stack crafting jobs at the same bench is tedious, but I have heard this will be addressed in the future.

Cloud storage is also being addressed in the future, and I am hoping for the ability to sort items by Tier level, and maybe have a filter? It would also be nice to able to multi-select items and transfer from your pack to storage, or your recruited follower.

Follower

I would like to be able communicate better with my follower. Currently, they will add any type of fuel I give them to any campfire, I can't say how many times I have lost Eoten lumber, coal, and hardwood before I figured out my recruited follower burned everything that can be used as fuel in their inventory. It would nice if their equipped clothing items didn't take up space in their inventory. I really like that they don't seem to have a weight limit for carrying.

When I am collecting specific resources I would like my follower to collect the same thing. For example, when I am mining level 100 ore, I don't want them mining sandstone.

They walk into the campfires and light themselves on fire constantly, although it's kind hilarious, it's still something that shouldn't happen.

Game Mechanics
I would like to be able to group or move multiple items at a time when building or re-arranging furniture.

When building, I would like the ability to zoom out to a higher perspective so I can design the floor plan from an aerial view.

I hate the language "Give Up" after being injured. Maybe something more neutral like Try Again?

It would be lovely to have multiple sets of toolbars configurable for your carrying hands 1-0 and Q. I would like a mining resource option and a fighting one.

Clicking on augmentations should populate information about what craft bench it supports and a crafting bench should list what augmentations its currently utilizing. It gets incredibly chaotic trying to figure out what augmentations go to what. I would also like the guidebook combined with the crafting screen so I don't have to jump between screens to track items, learn about the augmentations required, etc

It would be lovely if the Building tab had more filters, I would like to be able to filter out the Simple, Refined, Excellent etc and I would like a count of how many items I have currently built.

I hate de-foresting trees, I wish there was a way to ensure they were replanted.

Coal is insanely hard to find and it's used to craft gun powder, steel ingots, or as an option for fuel so itâ€™s a precious commodity. I have spent hours looking for caves to find it and I get maybe 3 per map. \t\t\t\t\t

Survival games need to feel immersive and this just completely removes you out of it, as your character doesn't grow and adapt with the realms and creatures. It just somehow turns it into plain boring already seen in history books of the Gilded Age.

I'd rather have just played a survival craft as someone lost in America wilderness after the civil war with this progression system. It would have made more sense.
