\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is not without its flaws. but its pretty great too. Dont approach it like a Valheim, or an Enshrouded. This is a different beast. Approach it like a long lost sequel to Myst, or an elderscrolls spinoff. Its a great elderscrolls game.

Pros
-Its gorgeous. the aesthetics and design are on point
-satisfying combat. Look, I know that its a common complaint but I think too many people are trying to jump directly into 3rd person and they warn you OUTRIGHT when you turn it on that 3rd person mode is experimental. Fighting in first person feels visceral, things react to being hit, your swings have weight, the glass breaking crit sound is so satisfying. Wailing on a Bound's dome and hearing CRASH CRASH CRASH while it stumbles around trying to regain its footing just. feels. good.
-crafting depth, find better materials to augment simple recipes. Thats smart. I like it, search out new augmentations to improve your crafting equipment, decorating improve crafting. Feels good.
-"Infinite worlds" The realmwalking here offers more in early acccess than what Starfield did on release. Enough said. Does it all start to feel a little samey after a while, yeah sure. but it does a better job than some other entries are currently doing.

Cons
-Always online, yeah.. its not great. getting lag while youre playing alone and being kicked out of a server just kind of suck, but they may be doing something about that.
-character models are ugly as hell. I think they were going for the same charicature vibe dishonored has but they kind of botched it somehow.
-UX, they need to hire a UX specialist STAT, the crafting menus, UI and general crafting experience are gargabge. Which sucks cause you'll be spending a lot of time with it.
-AI companions, need some serious care or to be removed from the game entirely. Theyre a step below worthless now, they get in the way, they get distracted, they cant handle even the simple singular tasks theyre set to do (by equipping the right tool) they'll waste your materials unless you exploit them not to, and they'll just straight up disappear in the middle of play and never return if you look in the wrong direction. The equipment they were holding will show up outside your doorstep and you will never see them again. Dont recruit them and dont get attached until the devs do something about them.

All in all though its a great experience. a nice world to simply exist in and wander and discover. which really.. what more could you ask for here. If they take the care to clean it up it will be an A+ experience. Its just a shame it had such a rocky start.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositives:

- Ability to play with friends
- Initially cool worlds to see

Cons:

- Absolutely the worst time trying to connect to the realms
- 8 times out of 10 it will disconnect and give you an error when trying to travel forcing you to exit the game and reset
- Very short early access game and very mid end game material
- Just another grind game to get mid end game gear
- No real reason to grind for end game gear because there's nothing to do afterwards unless there was a big game update which won't come for a long time.
- Damage balancing is a big struggle apparently for theses guys because you can farm end game realms with currently equipped gear running solo if you wish.
- So early access that most of the crafting doesn't have an explanation as to what the tiers of crafting material or prefixes mean. Higher leveled prefixes the come with the material in the endgame give higher levels to the items you craft. But nowhere can you find that out unless you experimented yourself.

Overall, I would not recommend this game to anyone that wants a crafting open world game. It's way to in depth for no instructions. Either wait for big game updates or find another game. This isn't it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: the game is boring and mechanics are awful compared to other games in the genre.

What a disappointment. I wish I was still eligible for a refund. The crafting is in-depth, but somehow still limiting. You can choose from a wide array of materials, but can only craft a handful of items.

The building mechanics are poor considering the innovation we've seen from many of the new games from the same genre.

The overall game loop is also painfully boring. Running from POI to POI solving the same 5 puzzles over and over again to increase my arbitrary gear score to unlock the next realm, which is just 1 of 3 aesthetics, to do the same 5 puzzles over and over once again.

Combat is atrocious. That is all.

Oh, and don't go anywhere dark; you can't see 2 inches in front of your face. I'd say use a torch, but all that seems to do is cast a red shader over the landscape and doesn't seem to illuminate anything.\t\t\t\t\t
