\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is like a survival game mixed with an art gallery. Anything cool looking is just that, cool looking. You can't interact with the majority of structures. It's a beautiful game with a very interesting concepts, but the survival aspect bogs the game down. Its should of been more of an action adventure game instead of your run of the mill survival game with nothing special about the building process. I love the creatures in the game, but all the creatures have the same bad combat where they either charge or lung or swipe. The attacks are really easy to dodge. The combat on the player's side as far as animation and movement feels good. Overall, the game feels like a grey area. I don't like it, I don't dislike it. Playing it feels like you're waiting for something cool to happen, but rarely it does come. As of 7 hours in, I wouldn't recommend the game but, I'm gonna keep playing to see if it gets any better. My recommendation for the game devs would be to scrap the whole survival part of the game and make it more focused on the portals and the adventure. The intro was great; I want more of that.\t\t\t\t\t
