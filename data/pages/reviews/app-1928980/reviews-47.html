\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like the theme of the game, also the fact that it's much deeper than direct competitors like Enshrouded.

But, there is a lot of issues:
- Game is not polished.
- I don't like icons of items/objects in a game, they are not clear enough. Why different grades of items have the same icons? How hard is to create different outlines around the icons to show different item grade so player straight away can recognize what he is looking at.
- When looking at items in the bag, number shows weight instead of counter of items.
- Interesting idea of realms but effectively it's jumping between instances of the just few maps with different mobs/objects on it.
- Fighting seems very clunky, not pleasant and comfortable to play.
- No dodging ability when fighting? You have to swap to a different weapon like knife in order to be able to jump away?
- Climbing....where do I even start... just rework the whole thing because its entirely broken. You can only climb up vertical walls, if there is a slight angle ... no you can't.... You can't go out of water.... You can't jump over small obstacles on the ground, etc.....

Till that point I was still quite ok with the game but...
Last patch broke performance of the game and added couple more issues. I believe there is some massive memory leak issue. Before last patch game run flawlessly, no stuttering, no freezes, perfect...
After last patch when I play my processor starts running like crazy every here and there... for no reason... game freezes, stutters when I look around... 5 seconds freezes when I click M (to see map)... long freezes during fights. Basically the longer you play the worse performance... Game is practically unplayable and I can't imagine how it runs today on lower specs machines as mine is quite high end. I have been monitoring memory parameter on my system and it constantly goes up, never down, what means objects loaded to the game are not being freed correctly after use, what causes memory to accumulate.
Even when you want to close the game, it doesnt... it freezes(it takes ages to free up consumed memory-have been watching it on memory monitor) on logging off screen and I just had to close the process to close it - and that kind of explains memory leak issues and over draining machine resources.
Also, to clarify above issues are not dependant on video settings, they are just there regardless.

That last reason made me not to recommend the game.... yet....

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted to like the game and in a weird way I do but I cannot recommend it at this time. The graphics in the game are wonderful, I really enjoyed running through the different environments and exploring. I also enjoyed the music which highlighted different situations in the game wonderfully but that is about where it began to lose me.

The building system is nothing to write home about, if you enjoy building then this game is not the one for you. I found the building to be simple and easy but expect to build your box and dare not try to deviate from that. At least it was easy to understand unlike this next part.

Crafting in the game is an over bloated, overly complicated mess of crafting stations, augments and materials. Trying to understand this system on your own will likely end in frustration which is what happened to me. I resorted to watching videos to understand what I was doing and just watching the videos annoyed me. Why make the crafting so complicated? I just did not find this fun in the slightest.

Adding to the above is the inventory and encumbrance system. I found myself easily over weighted trying to gather the resources necessary to craft. This got slightly better as I upgraded my backpack but its very limiting. Using your companion is another helpful work around but again, why have this in the game? Just an annoyance which doesn't add anything fun to the game and is just frustrating as you find yourself very often over weighted. Inventory management was also not fun for me as I like to organize to my liking which you cant do in this game. There are preset ways to have the game sort your inventory including chests at your home base none of which allows you to put things as you like in them. Its nice for the people that don't care about that sort of thing but I do and I found this an annoyance.

Exploring at first was fun but it quickly got boring as things started looking familiar...seemed to me like I was exploring the same places with slight variances over and over again. This lost my interest quickly.

First few days running around and fighting I found repairing my gear to be fine, having no issues. Then a patch happened and come today I cant keep my gear in tip top shape because I cant gather enough essence to keep it fixed up! What happened here? Maybe a bug? I am not sure but yet another feather in the cap of this game becoming more of a chore then a fun game to play.

Last part I reserved for the character graphics and clothing. I find it lacking in this department. I think I understand what they were going for here but its not my cup of tea. The character graphics I can over look but the clothing is just terrible! How about some more choice in this department? More styles would be great here.

In the end I found the game to be frustrating at times and down right boring at others...The story is interesting and I think that is what kept me in the game for as long as I have been but its time for me to hang up my hat on this one. I hope things down the line get better but for now I choose not to be frustrated and bored when I play a game.\t\t\t\t\t
