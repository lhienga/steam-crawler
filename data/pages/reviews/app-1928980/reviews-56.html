\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am loving this game so far!!!!
It's early access so I don't know why so many negative reviews for a game that is not complete yet. I think this is a great game now and know it will be an amazing game when they are done with it. I've read reviews that said the game was bare bones and I honestly don't know what their talking about. There is so much to do in this game in early access and yes there are parts that are extra grindy but that's to be expected in a survival game. The worlds are beautiful and theres so much to do in this game. The combat is lacking a bit, same over and over again and the weapons you can use are lacking as well. I was expecting more variety but it's just not there yet. The building is amazing and I'm loving unlocking all the different types of structures and themes but I wish there was more decorating options early on. My husband is much further along then I am and he is just starting to unlock more decor items, I think they should be available a lot earlier so your base isn't just crafting tables. The NPC's are a huge help, think pack mules but they can be janky as well. If you don't unequip their axe, they will cut down every tree in the immediate vicinity of your base. But if you do want their help cutting down trees, they will just stand next to you and do nothing. A bit annoying. They will revive you in combat which has been a huge help to me but only if they themselves haven't died as well. The loading screens for the portals and loading into the game are long not game breaking just annoying. Hoping they address this in a future update. I have had so much fun with this game so far and for $26, I've gotten my moneys worth so far. If your on the fence, don't be, it's a great game and I've got almost 90 hrs in.\t\t\t\t\t
