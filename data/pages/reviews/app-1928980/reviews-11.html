For anything lower then that or ultra quality TSR and you will feel like you'll have to go to an oculisit because you could swear video games looked sharper just yesterday. There's also the problem with Lumen, the lighting system the engine uses. If you don't go for Lumen - Quality option you'll be forced to witness shadows flickering in between each leave for every tree in sight. Any shadow quality lower then Ultra and every time you turn shadows tend to take a moment to disappear as the game simulates eyesight adjustment, because apparently humanity lost the natural ability to do that or something.
BUT if you go for higher Lumen Quality it will eat through your FPS, dropping them by 10 or more and you'll feel like you're playing in molasses.

Again, I want to like it, there's some compelling ideas, for me it's the fact that each creature in the genereted world drop a different type of hide, meat bone and so on that can allow you to craft items to give you the buffs you want. It's reminiscent of older MMOs and the developers seem to want to do a MMO-light with its focus on the late game being about dungeon and world delving with other players from a shared hub. Think of it like the Warframe stations but with a bit more of a social use. Which again, I like.

In the end, the devs ambition to play with the new fanciest tech toys has bitten them in the ass hard, the game shouldn't need things like TSR to be playable. Those things should be the cherry on top in order to allow even ancient graphics card to run an expensive game without exploding. But no instead modern developers want to use this new tech as a crutch to skip on perfomance tuning, so in the end only the top 10% of players can expirience the game in its native resolution at 4k while the rest of us have to deal with worst perfomance.

I'll be back in the future when the game gets some performance fixes and maybe even delete or change this review, but at this moment I just can't tell you to support this Early Access, in spite of its good art direction, creative ideas and potential.\t\t\t\t\t
