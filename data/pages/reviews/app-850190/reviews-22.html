\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat choc chip cookie recipe

125g butter, softened
1/2 cup caster sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 cups self-raising flour
1 cup choc chips

Cream butter and sugar, then add egg and vanilla, and beat well.

Mix in sifted flour and choc chips using a wooden spoon until well combined.

Place heaped teaspoons of mixture onto a lined tray, and press gently with a fork or fingers to slightly flatten.

Bake at 180C for 10-12 minutes or until golden in colour.\t\t\t\t\t
