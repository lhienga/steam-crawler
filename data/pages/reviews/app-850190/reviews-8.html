The Goat of Chaos in the Open World
Goat Simulator 3 allows you to return playing as Pilgor the wacky and insanely powerful goat, wrecking havoc around the world of San Angora; and now can be done with friends. Explore a large map with a bounty of quests, unlockables, and items while you serve style of your choosing. Complete quests and rise the ranks of the goat tower to gain more benefits and quests as well as instincts to do for fun objectives. There are many different themes and areas from farmlands, to cemeteries, woodlands, vineyards, and city districts.

Pros
- Great Graphics
- Crazy Gameplay
- Lots and Lots of Quests
- Fun Instincts
- Fun Abilities
- Lots of Customisability
- Drivable Vehicles
- Co-op up to 4 Players
- Lots of References and Easter Eggs
- Lovely Soundtrack

Cons
- In the first game abilities were an addition that could be stacked via mutators, and in this game it's tied to clothing items. Because abilities are tied to certain clothing items for the character, you're stuck with certain items if you want their utility. It's fine for certain things but there are lots of hats with cool abilities that you might not like the look of, or if you wanted to have two abilities that are cool but both of them occupy the same clothing slot or button, you have to switch between them.

My Rating: 9/10
