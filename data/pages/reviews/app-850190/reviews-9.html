\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen i first booted this up i thought it was going to be a really obnoxious, reference filled, expensive piece of fan-service.
The first elder scrolls joke was so "GET IT?? GET IT!? REMEMBER SKYRIM?!!" i kinda just gulped and expected the most obnoxious unfunny experience of my life.
However, I am pleased to tell you that the first joke was probably the worst i encountered. The farmer remains extremely annoying all throughout his appearances but i guess that's kind of intentional since he ends up being the final boss.

This game feels shockingly fresh and original for something i expected to be a milk of the original franchise. my only complaint is the lack of "goat" characters. There was no customisation in the original, you just went around unlocking more playables. The item shop feels like a pretty piss poor cheaper alternative to just letting you play as lots of different animals and joke creatures instead of just.. like.. being able to buy a new hat or shoes.

The eleven pound DLC I've seen is obnoxiously expensive since it only realistically includes one more playable model (Pilgor). I would however buy a pack of just the original goats from every Goat sim game before Goat simulator 3.
It takes place mainly in a city?? why cant i play as Old goat (Tyrannosaur) from Goat MMO??

The price tag is pretty justified, its funny and very full feeling, Its a complete parody game.\t\t\t\t\t
