CA has created an updated horde army for Warhammer 2, and this fulfills the fantasy of rampaging through enemy lands, looting and smashing anything in your path. The new sea battles are a welcome change to the autoresolves of the past, and make terrorizing the coasts a fun new strategy as opposed to a terrifying prospect that could see your entire army destroyed. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have to say, I find myself really not liking Vampire Coast armies.

EDIT: THE GAME ALSO CRASHES A LOT WITH THIS DLC! Graphics glitches also occur, such as a faction symbol next to settlements being replaced with a big grey block, and the symbol over foreign factions settlement window also freaking out (can't quite remember what it looked like, but it was weird).

Like the Dwarfs, you are meant to rely heavily on your powerful missile units. However, unlike the dwarfs, you have nothing suitable to hold the line against enemy heavy infantry. Depth Guards can slaughter trash, true, but Har Ganeths, Temple Guards, Swordmaster Hoes, Greatswords etc will easily out-DPS The Hunger (the stuff that regens them whilst in melee).

Sireens have a pathetic hp pool.

Hulks have trash defensive stats and nonexistent leadership.

Morghuls are glass and are meant as quasi-cav.

Crabs hold well, but are hella expensive.

Your own armies are very vulnerable to missiles themselves, and your regular handcannon mob is outranged by archers. You also have no mobility to chase down anything, and to harass enemy archers. Your only hope is to out-shoot them...out shoot Elves....yeah, right.

Managing your armies is micro intensive since you need to ensure all your gunpowder inf has firing angles, but they are slow as BALLS (much slower than thunderers or handgunners) and take forever to relocate and form up.

Other than that, it just all feels like a meme-army. Certainly very cool, some even funny (I'm looking at you, bloated corpse) but ultimately are not competitive on higher campaign difficulties or in multiplayer battles. Yes, I am aware that some youtubers have won multi-battles with them, but that is likely due to the novelty of the faction, once people figure them out they will be very easy to beat especially with Bretonnia.

Edit: With time, it was proven that they can be effective in MP. I still think they are a 'meh' campaign faction, with your best bet being a mass necrofex colossi spam.\t\t\t\t\t
