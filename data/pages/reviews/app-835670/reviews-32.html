First of all, if you buy this DLC, you have to know that you are paying for a race that relies on artillery as well as in monster units. It has good magic powers as well.

It has similar features to Vampire counts, but plays a way lot different. Vampire counts had to rush enemys with their good mounted units, and monster infatry. Here your infantry is almost everything zombie, and the elite infantry can't hold the line, so you have to use wisely your gunpowder units, as their are your best way to deal real damage.

I love to see Creative Assembly really making the Total War Warhammer into a really good variety game. Tons of factions, each one really unique, and making little gems like this faction.

I hope they keep adding small factions with the same level of detail as this DLC. Every unit is so much detailed, It's so freaking awesome to see the zombie pirates, and the monsters.... they look absolutly amazing.

For the price, some people thinks they are overpriced, but wich each DLC they made since Warhammer I, i spend almost 30 hours on each faction campaign. So you are paying about 0,5 euros for each hour of new gameplay. Not many games offers this great value.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat job CA! I always think these DLC's are a little overpriced until I play them. There are a lot of new mechanics and although the Vampire Coast was technically already in the game, they weren't like this. It's basically a completely new faction with 4 new Lords. I honestly had a tough time choosing which one to go with for this first run. The roster is new and definitely fun to play with. The new pirate mechanic is great! Your heroes and lords have a "Dig" ability to search for treasures from maps you get due to quests or battle victories, the new cove addtion is a great touch, as well as being able to operate as a pirate horde and build your unique ship up! This DLC adds a lot and makes the game feel fresh again as there are pirates from all different races on the high seas you have to contend with and now the added infamy level all make it a blast. Trying to knock off other pirates so I can jump up the infamy category gives me just one more reason to hunt these rat bastids downnnn!

Also, wanted to say kudos to CA for the release of Lokhir Fellheart with this DLC. His starting placement on the map gives me a reason to play with the Dark Elves again, as I was tired of basically staring in the same region with them. In addition to that, he seems to play into the whole pirate theme better than the others so even if I didn't pay for the DLC, I could still enjoy the new gameplay to some extent. Good job, all is highly recommended!\t\t\t\t\t
