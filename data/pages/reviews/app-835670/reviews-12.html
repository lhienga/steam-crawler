\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnplayable mess of a faction in campaign.

Your main income building is with ports - setting up pirate coves. A main way to do this is to attack the port with your army. Except that for some inexplicable reason you set on the nearby shore whenever you want to attack. It's up in the air whether you can then attack the port the same turn as you have lost all your movement. After attacking the port, you can't leave either because you're still on land. Next turn, the ports influence comes back and you've got to attack it a second time (sacking and damaging the port's income you need as the best building gives you 50% of it) just to get back out to sea. If you're lucky.

And hope you never want to change your mind. You only know if you're going to be able to make the attack after declaring war unless its skaven. And you're even more stuck as you're inside the port's influence and MUST attack.

You're also not allowed to replenish at sea unless you control that region (Horde-fleet encamp is only for buildings), nor can you Raise Dead at sea so you're likely to have very few units starting out too. (Since updated)

It's a shame because the faction itself has tons of flavour but it's just unplayable with the way you attack ports.\t\t\t\t\t
