\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis adds a great campaign and cool new mechanics. The units are very interesting too, particularly if you like factions with a lot of monsters (squid-troll creatures, giant crabs, and bloated corpses etc.). I like that the new factions are not necessarily focused on creating massive empires, but instead focusing development on the coasts and where possible establishing pirate coves and hideouts. There is also treasure to search for, which makes the campaign really interesting since it's a really important component of the Vampire Coast economy. You can always raid and plunder too, but I find in the long-term it's better to establish pirate coves buildings.

The only real downside is that the DLC did not add more in terms of non-vampiric/living pirates. I think the Sartosa faction should have been more oriented to be a living faction and less undead, but it's easily fixed with an overhaul mod since they did add some pirate models. I don't mind playing evil, but it would be nice to have a pirate faction that isn't necessarily always evil and maybe could help out other humans against Tomb Kings, Orcs, Dark Elves, and the others.\t\t\t\t\t
