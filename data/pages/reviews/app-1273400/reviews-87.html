From the time I got it became apparent that I would enjoy the game if not for said problems with the camera.
While off googling, reading forums and whatnot, lo and behold the developer issues a hotfix that remedies some of those problems. So I buy it again.

While setting things up I realize that I can use my dual stick setup in addition to steering wheel/pedals. Oh my. This is a gamechanger! It's a bit of a hassle to configure, but once done it's magical.

There's something incredibly zen about excavating some plot of land and then build houses, stripping the asphalt off a road and lay on a new layer, or simply driving materials to and from construction sites.

I will say though, this game is in DESPERATE NEED of TrackIR support!! (edit; TrackIR has now since been implemented along with eye tracking as an alternative!)
