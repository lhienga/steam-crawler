\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEven though I'm having fun with the game playing with friends i honestly can't recommend it in its current state.

The game is just very buggy overall, here are some examples.
- Vehicles can glitch out and just flop about if driven over a small pile of gravel for example.
- I have problems with pop-ins and other graphical glitches.
- Vehicles loaded on the mixed trailer refuse to secure even though there are lots of room, the ramp on the mixed trailer gets stuck when lowering etc.
- The traffic system is horrendous. Other cars drive super slow, the red lights seem to last for ever.
- When driving the tractor with mixed trailer fully loaded the equipage feels super light, kinda like the flatbed truck.
- Controls are janky at best, but you kinda get used to them(toggle camera should be default, not timed)

There's just so many small things that are annoying which is a shame because at it's core the game is very enjoyable.

And i got even more irritated when they released the roadmap, because there are the bugfixes and improvements to traffic etc. This means they they knew the game was buggy and needed fixing but decided on releasing it anyway. I hate that kind of behavior from game devs.

My recommendation is to wait until the game gets a few patches, i think you'll have a much better experience.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDirt digs like dirt.

This developer team nailed the concept of scooping dirt in a video game. Dirt behaves like dirt, from trickling out of a bucket, to moving with the bucket. Wheel Loader, excavator alike, dirt digs like dirt. First video game i've played where you can take the side of a excavator bucket and actually smooth out dirt mounds. Not that you have to get that technical, buts it's pretty neat.

I've seen the word 'arcadey' in other reviews. Sure, there are some arcade aspects to this, if you want a more real to life simulator just turn up the difficulty, it's there. (Higher difficulty, more money reward, win win)

A few cons

Playing with multiple controllers, I.E. Wheel, Joysticks, Pedals, I don't have time to mess with a mouse. Unfortunately at this moment, First Person View controls are not bindable. You can not look around in FPV without using a mouse. (Unless you use Joy2Key or other key emulating software).

Axis Deadzone. You can not adjust them, with a wheel there is currently a 10-15 degree of deadzone (Which, with the help of third party software again, you can reduce steering angle and take away some of the deadzone)

Job Completion. This is more of a nit pick, because i'm a nerd who likes to be challenged, I would like to see a hard-core mode of some sorts that requires 100% of the work. Currently on the highest difficulty, if you are given say 100sq meters to dig, when you dig 80sq meters that is considered 100%. I would like to dig all 100sq meters, and even have to level and grade if it's for a foundation. Again, this is just me, not Positive Review breaking.

Should you buy this?
Do you have at least one or more of the following: Farming Simulator 2011-2022, Construction Simulator 2015, House Flipper, American Truck Simulator, Barbies Big Adventure, etc of the like. Buy it.

Remember, peripherals make simulators fun. Go get some sticks!
