\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn one line:
Well done, bug free & enjoyable - although a mild consolitis permeates the game.

There's plenty of variability in the jobs, and it's your choice if you want to invest time in doing things or you prefer spending a some money instead ( good example is the quick travel ).

The tutorials are good, the game eases you into it very nicely, and it give you progressively less help.

My one big gripe; a lot of information about the machines that could have been included in the "showroom" is missing, eg bucket sizes, pipe lengths, general size information, etc... it's really not obvious that the first crane is barely enough for simple "home" bilder jobs, for example. Some size information would have cleared that up.

It's almost a necessity to test every machine of a type during a job or two. Lots of hiring expenses but still cheaper than buying the wrong one.

Smaller gripe is the default grouping for machines by manufacturer. And I never need this; the other grouping - by machine type - is ten times more useful. But, alas, no changing that.\t\t\t\t\t
