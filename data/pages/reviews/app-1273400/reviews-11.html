\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing, I now have a spit cup in one cup holder, a monster in the other cup holder, have attached a CAT license plate to my 02 Duramax and pay no attention to bridge weight limits.

The bad:
On road driving is equal to or worse than farming simulator, especially the traffic. I fast travel everywhere because it is not enjoyable.
Some machines like graders and dozers are limited in functions, only offering blade up and down and side to side movement.
Spreading material with a dump truck and loader is impossible.
The helper doesn't offer much help.
Limited range of equipment, there may be 5 excavators, but they'r all the same size except for one or two.
Some jobs require multiplayer to make sense, think milling a road without a dump truck to load.
Lastly, there is no mod support. Why Astragon? I guarantee you would sell as many copies as farming sim if you allowed mods.....

The Good: Remember playing in the sandbox as a kid?
Excavators are pretty good.
Crane and concrete work is also pretty good if that's what you're into.
Dirt physics are good for the most part.
Decent graphics.
plenty of different jobs to choose from.

In conclusion, I give it a 7/10 because the driving needs improvement, unpolished world and physics, and most importantly the lack of Mods. Buy it when it's on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
