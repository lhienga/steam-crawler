\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have mixed feelings about this game. The reason is because it is the best construction game out there, but they are missing out on some stuff. I get some things could be hard to add to the game, but still they could invest a bit more.

The digging is in general good but at the same time terrible. I mean, you could literally just shove the bucket through the ground, and when you dig, you dig in one type of mass the whole time. There isn't any stones, gravel or clay in the ground which makes the digging kinda boring. However it is the best digging I have experienced ever in any type of game.

If you are familiar to Construction Simulator 2015, the ordinary game basics there are completely different. You don't have to change between digging and driving mode on the track excavators anymore which is superb. But the wheeled excavators NEEDS its supports to dig, lift and do whatever. You can not move the machine while holding stuff for example, which I find very frustrating.

They do add new stuff but you have to be prepared to pay for some of it. Year pass is the way to go. Hopefully they get a some kind of partnership with VOLVO and/or Hitachi sometime soon.

Overall: 6 / 10 score. Hopefully it will get better but right now it is meh. (Very fun if you have friends) i don't\t\t\t\t\t

I have checked the discord and website and I have not found a location to report bugs or suggestions.

One major issue I have encountered was during missions. The sprint function (shift key default assigned to run) during the mission would glitch to making my character stop instead of run. A fix was rebinding the run key. Another major issue was after prolonged game play, the vehicles would become low powered and not reach speeds above 30 when traveling on the road. The biggest annoyance is some devs need to learn that in the US we can turn right on red if there is a opening and none of the traffic that has the right of way yields to let people out of side roades sitting at stop signs or yield signs...

Anyway, they just need to get the priorities straight instead of dumping out massive amounts of DLC. They can do better, so save your money for now and wait till it goes on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
