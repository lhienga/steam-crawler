If you are looking for a game that can give you a break from other games and some hours of enjoyment then this game is for you. I would say your best bet is to watch what the community forum is saying about the game and make a more informed decision about the game from there. It still has plenty of potential and needs improvement but they are actively working on it so it gets my approval for that.
