There are some gripes with the American map:
1. All the bridges say 12' 6", which would be way too short for any standard truck to pass under let alone construction equipment.
2. I had to disable traffic offences because I am tired of losing money for making a right turn on a red light which is legal in the USA.

I have noticed minor bugs with equipment (ie: when pouring concrete with an extension truck, the cement won't always feed into it from the cement truck, at times the hoses get stuck and I have to reset the machine.

The game needs more standard American equipment specifically trailers.

Some excavation jobs are also bugged, specifically the parking lot job, I spent hours excavating the land and only ended up with 10% complete.

Overall, I give this game a 6.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd recommend this game if it's 60+% off, it's not worth full price.

The game mechanics are solid for the most part, although there are a few UI issues which are annoying. All of which have been reported on the forums by multiple people but nothing has been done. I suspect the developers don't even read customer feedback, they certainly don't act on it.

The graphics are good, the gameplay loop is ok. The character creator is super lazy (extremely limited customisation), but that's not too drastic as you don't really need to see your character.

Some of the goals don't make sense at all, for instance you need to drive a vehicle to a local garage to refuel/repair 20 times to advance your company to the next size. Why? I can just refuel/repair at my depot. I've literally never needed to drive to a garage to do that, and yet it's a goal? Why?

My main issue is that once you've bought all the equipment you need for the various jobs rather than renting them (took me about 30 hours and I was taking my time) there's no motivation to complete any further jobs. There really needs to be something to spend your money on other than vehicles to complete the jobs to earn money.

This game is largely abandoned. Since release there haven't been any fixes/improvements based on customer feedback, just quick-buck DLCs including cosmetic packs....in a simulator game..... Shame.\t\t\t\t\t
