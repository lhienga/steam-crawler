\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame has too many bugs to be worth full price. Half the time, the context menu doesn't correctly register what you choose, so when you click reset it tells you that you have to reset before you can enter drive mode. There's little to no help figuring out what a mission wants so you need to physically walk the job site to determine if the concrete forms you need to place are magically spawned or if they're the ones you have to buy at the building supply. The travel helper is the most useless part of the game because it constantly tells you to go in circles instead of a shortest route to travel, so half the trip is taking 3 right turns and circling the blocks instead of one left. Keyboard commands only work some of the time, so that handy list of commands on the help menu, forget it. Fast travel a truck to the construction site and have it spawn on top of a parked car, rendering it stuck and unable to reset since it resets right back on top of the same car. Fast travel another truck with a load of gravel only to have it launch into the air upon arrival, throwing the load all over the site. Utter garbage with these bugs and glitches rendering the game almost unplayable.
It's a fun additional to the simulators out there, but it's not ready for a full price release.\t\t\t\t\t
