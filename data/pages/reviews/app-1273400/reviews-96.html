\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy main problem with this game is that 70% of your time is spent doing the most boring thing in the game. Moving objects from A to B to C, with a crane.. It's a shame because all the other machines are fun to use, but the way the jobs are set up you're forced to spend most of your time operating the crane.

And if you think maybe operating the crane in multiplayer will make it more fun, then you're sadly mistaken. Watching someone operate the crane is not fun either. Because all the jobs are created for singleplayer. 4 players at one job site are 3 players too many. So you either get one player doing all the work while the rest of you'r friends twiddle their thumbs, or you'll have to take turns while twiddling your thumbs when it's not your turn.

Well why don't you all just work on different job sites? Good question, you actually can! After the 19+ hours it takes to unlock you'r second job slot!

I can only recommend this game if you enjoy playing crane simulators.\t\t\t\t\t
