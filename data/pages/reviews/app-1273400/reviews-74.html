\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my first game in the Construction Simulator series and I have to say I really enjoy it. There's a huge variety of construction machines from different licensed brands and the process of seeing your projects come to life is very rewarding. While my overall review of gameplay is very positive, I know most people trying to make a decision based off reviews want to here the critiques as well so here are some things that could use work:
- The driving is not a pleasant experience. I know the meat of the game is the on-site construction work, but unless you want to pay to fast travel and if you want full immersion, you have to drive on-road a lot. The handling is very loose with several ton vehicles going into a skid in the slightest turns, AI traffic going extremely slow, and long traffic lights. I skip driving as often as I can.
- There's some inconsistency in finishing tasks. Some tasks like asphalt paving and cementing will force finish the task when you still have quite a bit of obvious work left, while others like removing topsoil or excavating will have you driving back and forth over the same ground several times wondering what you're missing. Hopefully autocompletion can become optional once you reach a certain percentage of the job
- There's no in-game descriptions of the difference between machines of the same class, such as difference in size or load capacity.
Those issues are really the only major ones that come to mind. Overall, still a great game so far and I hope more is added over time.\t\t\t\t\t
