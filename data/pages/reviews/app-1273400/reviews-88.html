\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL/DR;

The games well worth the money, its not perfect nor really complete, but its playable, fun and a blast with friends. what it needs more than anything right now is MOD SUPPORT, look at farming simulator, the game practically lives and dies off of its mods. this games the exact same, it needs it, because what content there is, is going fast.





THE BAD:

-Games riddled with bugs, none of them are totally game-breaking and honestly the first couple times your truck gets launched into low orbit its pretty funny. but after a while they get very tiresome. that being said the games just been released so i'm sore this will get polished up.

-Traffic lights are very poorly done. if you're playing with traffic enforcement on you will catch every red and you'll soon be turning it back off again. having realistic traffic could be a neat idea but presently the lights don't have any deep coding into them that i can see. they just arbitrarily switch from red to green which can be extremely annoying that your sitting at an intersection for what feels like a full minute each time while no traffic passes.

-Character/vehicle/company customisation is.....non existent. you pick from a couple of presets and that's your lot. i would like the ability to change the look and colour of my base,buildings, character and vehicles. custom paint jobs, decals, uniforms etc. the whole point of this game is to create your own successful construction firm, so why does none of this stuff feel like mine?

-gravel spreading/topsoil removal are really annoying jobs. not because they are tricky and challenging, but because the game is so ♥♥♥♥ finnicky about what does and dosent constitute spread/removed. literally i spend 30 minutes with a dozer removing topsoil and ive taken a layer off the entire area and its still only showing as 60%. this can be done right because when youre using a soil/asphalt compactor the games able to recognise when youve gone over a certain piece of ground. please make removing topsoil work like this.

-the machinery in general does not drive well at low speeds. at high speeds, fine. but at low speeds in a truck, when you are trying to do something like follow a cold planer or dump gravel on the ground, its verrry difficult to keep a slow and steady pace, the vehicles jerk around like i have lead boots on even when im just tapping the keys.

-some missions can finish very abruptly, like the house barely looks out of the first stage of construction, no cladding or anything and suddenly PING. brand new fully furnished home with blue painted walls, a driveway, grass lawn and white picket fence, mission complete music blares as the camera swings around saying well done in an almost condescending way. it dosent look anything like what we built. i get the feeling that there were supposed to be more construction stages but in the run up to release it got canned and they just skipped over them. hopefully they go back and flesh it out a bit more.

-Feels a little ridiculous that you need to rent a mobile crane, one of the most expensive pieces of equipment, just to pick up some panels from the sawmill/prefab/steel workshop. this is in one of the earliest missions too. why cant there just be a fixed crane at the sawmill, surely that makes more sense? how does it make any sense that i cant just drive a truck down, i need to drive a mobile crane over too just to pick up some damn wood?

Machine trailers are suuper janky to load/unload. despite the largest one being the size of a 100M sprint track it can only hold one excavator despite there clearly being more room on there. and when youre playing in a more immersed mode where you're not allowed to fast travel, going back for your dozers and excavators one at a time gets really hecking annoying. and then occasionally, once you get everything loaded and hop in to drive, the entire thing glitches out and refuses to move, forcing you to reset it, which empties the trailer for you to start all over again. its a shame because i really love the idea of setting up a building site/clearing it out at the end being one of the biggest stages, loading up all your heavy machinery and driving it across town to where it needs to be is way more satisfying than just teleporting it there. just need to make it actually work, because right now it does not.


THE GOOD

-for a sim the game is really pretty. you find yourself cruising around and just noticing it sometimes, really good sim city.

-machines in general feel very satisfying to use. big excavators, tower cranes, concrete pumps, rollers, all work as intended and really makes you feel like a construction worker.

-Job Variety is quite good. the story missions are always special undertaking with unique projects, meanwhile theres enough repeatable houses, parking lots, mini jobs etc to make sure you can always bankroll yourself if you spend too much too fast.

-theres alot of discussion regarding the game feeling more like an Arcade than a Sim, but in my opinion i think the game for the most part, strikes the right balance between doing enough work to feel like the jobs done, and actually covering every square inch of it.

-this is also a game that dosent take itself too seriously. there are easter eggs, whilhelm screams, and some references that made me smile, its clear the developers loved making the game.

-theres a fixed number of "campaign" missions in each map, this means there is a soft content limit, but the games well supported with a roadmap already up and running, and i am sure there will be content updates too, maybe even new maps.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think the game is decent. It really is fun in a lot of parts. 75 different licenced vehicles are very awesome. I dont mind the arcadish vehicle behavior, because I think its fun to drift a heavy duty car. After playing hundreds of hours farming simulator I can assure you, I am happy to play more easy games.

But what bugs me is the missing respect for the consumer. I have 3 big issues:
1) There are 3 different menus.
a) A general menu you can access with ESC.
b) The manual menu which let you also access and sell your vehicles at the same time on "1"
c) the actualy ingame control menu on M where you can scroll through all your jobs, players, map what so ever.

I mean was there no quality control when developing this? 1 and M could be at least one menu. Even ESC could lead into the same UI frame. You only need to quit and save. The whole UI is just a hilarious amateur approach to gaming.

2) Excavator are yet another mess like nearly in every game. It seems to be a problem to determine from which direction the excavator shovel hits the dirt or something. But I can tell you that this game has the worst excavator mechanic. If you managed to get a good handful of dirt in your shovel you also produced a little heap of dirt in front of you. And you cant pinch through that heap anymore with the excavator. This is mega tedious.

3) The roadmap in my opinion is disrespectul to all players that own that game already. I have my empathy that Weltbauern Software GmbH isnt big enough to bring a bug free, perfect working game. But I would expect to see some of the obvious annyoing points to be on the roadmap. Instead the roadmap basically says "Multiplayer fix for all plattform" and "Modding".

In conclusion I think if you dont mind to play a decent game that can get tedious at some points, if you have a borderline relationship to games with funny glitches and bad design. Just buy it. But if you feel pissed about being a labroratory rat that was put in a bad made testing area. Then you should defenitely wait at least a year and some patches.\t\t\t\t\t
