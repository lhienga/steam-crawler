\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI give it 3/5 stars in its current state as of 9/27/2022.

The construction aspect of the game is great, very enjoyable and only slightly repetitive. The amount of vehicles is good and has multiple brands. I'd like to be able to paint my vehicles different colors or make a custom logo for my company/clothing/PPE, but maybe that'll come in future iterations.

Where this game loses stars are the traffic, driving, and camera aspects of the game. The traffic constantly cuts you off, even if they have stop signs or turns. The red lights take way too long and are horribly implemented (I strongly recommend you turn off traffic tickets to not spend half your game play sitting at a red light). The vehicles can't make normal turns (think a mountain road) at faster than 25MPH without skidding and losing control. 25 MPH throughout other than highways is drastically slow, and unless you spend money to fast travel anywhere, it takes forever to move around the map. ALSO, you cannot look behind you/over shoulder when backing up in in-vehicle camera, making it almost impossible to attach to a trailer or fully utilize the 1st person camera. Additionally, you cannot look down while in a crane, making you have to use 3rd person view. Updating these things would make quality of life so much better and make me change from 3 to 5 stars.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKeybindings are abysmal. Like many other posts. I tried getting a refund but was unsuccessful as I had attempted to play for "too long" (4 hours).
So the purpose of this review is to save others from my fate.
I'm an avid simmer. From DCS to MFS to the Farm Sim franchise, I've spent a few hours in the seat tilling crops or just doing startup and taxiing. This was by far my most infuriating issue. Not not being able to load or start the game, or have crashes.. nope. Literally not being able to interact with the environment.

I'm able to start a campaign however once I enter the world I'm unable to interact with the first NPC, or the truck I am supposed to take. (I once randomly interacted with him by alt+Tabbing to the internet trying to troubleshoot my issue.) The prompt for the interaction pops up weather I'm using an XBOX controller or Mouse and Keyboard, however I'm simply unable to use it. The inability to rebind keys in this game severely
limits the ability to remedy this problem as well.

If they ever fix this issue I'm sure I'll jump back in, but I cannot give a positive review of this game until then. $40 CAD better spent toward renting an excavator from your nearby Home Depot, and digging up your neighbours lawn. #callbeforeyoudig\t\t\t\t\t
