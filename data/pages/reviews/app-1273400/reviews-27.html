\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really do want to give this game a positive review, but there is just one big issue that prevents me from doing so.

This game is pretty good. It looks alright (my main issue is that the vehicles look a lot better then everything else, making them stand out in an odd way). It is surprisingly fleshed out with a bit of story, voice acting, and some character customization and a whole company to run. But my issue is this: The game seems to focus 90% about vehicles. Although modeled and animated very well, they drive pretty poorly (Takes off like a race car, but has no brakes and cannot take a corner at 25km p/h without sliding.) On top of that the vehicles get stuck very easily. I happened 5 times in the 80 minutes that I played. And the controls are very annoying. Why? Because you need to use the arrow keys to control parts of the vehicle, meaning you can no longer control the camera. You need to switch between holding your mouse and using your arrow keys every 10 seconds so you can see what you are doing. The worst part is that the 2 controls you have on the arrow keys could easily have been scroll wheel + left and right click. But this is not the case, neither can you rebind them and there is no mod support.

Overall this just ruins the game for me as, like I said, vehicles are 90% of the game. And with poor driving, getting stuck often and controls like these is just doesn't play comfortably in my opinion.
If you can live with this, I highly recommend you try the game, but for me it just didn't work.\t\t\t\t\t
