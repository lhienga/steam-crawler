Hello together dear gamers and workers,
I like the Construction Simulator very much so far. Of course there are still some things that need to be improved, but I am already excited and if you look at the roadmap I think it will be even more great.

What I like:
- The new graphics in general as well as the details of the vehicles.
- More construction machines and more brands than in construction Sim2015.
- Road construction
- 2 new maps, EU and USA .
- Day night change.
- More different tasks, more construction phases, unpacking of material e.g.
- better earth motion system. ( Dredging or the behavior of the earth when you drive along the construction pit, so that it changes as eg a small ramp in the construction pit is formed or also when rolling.
- No annoying earth spikes when dredging (one still knows from the building Sim2015)
the inspector mode, so you know how much you still have to dig or which materials belong where put.
-Sound
- Rent vehicles, refuel and service.
- new game menu ( Map, Profile, Company, Vehicle Control )
-Change the color of the truck (with new update from 22:03.23)
-Reversing camera in the cabin view (with new update from 22.03.23)
-Seasson Pass with new DLC vehicles and map expansions ( from April 2023)

Which I don't like:
-With the Pritschen truck you have the problem not to put the pallet all the way to the front.
-when loading bissel more freedom.
- Missing data on the vehicles, horsepower or load capacity as well as in the gravel group the load quantity.
- that the rest of the vehicles from the construction Sim2015 are not already included at the beginning. (Tipper, small MAN with trailer. Crawler crane)
- The hours of the day go by too fast.
- Missing multiplayer chat ( Roadmap 2023)

Conclusion: One can recommend the game to anyone who has played the Bau Simultor 2015, as well as those who like to play simulations on the construction site. I think you should give the game a chance, it's not a champion yet :)

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish there was a "meh" rating. This game is neither bad, nor good. It is playable, fun in part, frustrating, repetitive and infuriating in others. If you have a construction itch to scratch this could be it for now. Multiplayer contracts are odd as they are still gated and you still hang around waiting for the next job. It is more a take it in turns rather than a multiplayer game.

I don't regret buying it, and it is max a $20/£15 game for me, not full price. 15 hrs in and I am done with it.
I had more fun with Demolish and Build as it was a bit more hands on.

6/10 for me.\t\t\t\t\t
