My thump is up now!!!!!
This is the best Construction Simulator I've ever played on Steam. It reminds me a lot about Farming Simulator from the early years with the kind of funny bugs. No problem just repeat a section or reload and continue with a little frustration. The view from the inside of most vehicles is very, very bad. The STEAM Achievements are broken!!!!!!!!! I played 345 hours and still don't get the 160. Time froze twice at 145. Only 0.1% or less have done all Achievements, which should tell the Developer to FIX IT --- and not only making new DLC's.
I like to get 100% STEAM Achievements and wait before I buy the DLC's.
In hope the game even better (less bugs) I still think it's full with good ideas and the best Construction Simulator. Each of the two maps (Germany & USA) comes with its own campaign and this is why I have to recommend it.
If you are not sure maybe try CS 2015 to get a little taste for less $.\t\t\t\t\t
