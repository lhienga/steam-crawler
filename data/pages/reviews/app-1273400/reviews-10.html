Somewhere Satan is looking down and smiling. Someone needs a slap for that with a haddock.

Its the A.I. again! moving the camera around against your wishes, as you continue to move it back to where you can see what your doing. "oh you moved to the right slightly? This is a game for young kids playing with a mouse and keyboard.
Steer clear.
