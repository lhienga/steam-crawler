\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has a long way to go in terms of qol improvements, balancing and bug-fixing. Nearly half of every construction vehicle has some major hickups, from perfroming the task badly to plain buggy behaviour. Since there is no price difference between these vehicles I'm assuming these should perform similar with minor difference.

E.g. the escavators in the 250k Range: One simply doesn't load more than 1/2 a shovel, the other performs the task flawlessly. Another example: Dumpers dump in different styles, some fast, some slow (i guess this is intended behaviour) but some (same price range, so same capacity?) swallow 1/3 of the load without showing up ( and one let the load visibly clip though the wall of the container, but thats just bad design).

This list goes on from not plaining the ground as well as the other, over having somewhat smaller capacity than the others, to refusing to work as intended ( cranes f.e. ).

This and other bugs things like 50/50 suicidal/shluggish traffic or pedestrians, getting flashbanged from light sources while evening approaches and needing to finance a whole construction while getting only half of income in multiplayer ruined this for me.

It's somewhat a good game, but in this state not really playable for me. Maybe later.\t\t\t\t\t
