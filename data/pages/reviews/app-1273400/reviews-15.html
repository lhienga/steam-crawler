\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs an architect with over five years of experience in various real-life construction projects, I highly recommend Construction Simulator for its immense fun. Having dedicated over 100 hours to this game, I can confidently say it's the most enjoyable gaming experience I've ever had. While the realism in the construction process isn't entirely accurate – a compromise for gameplay reasons, with almost everything involving prefabricated parts – it's still incredibly entertaining. As a child, I always dreamed of operating heavy machinery, and doing so in the game is thrilling.

I would love to see the addition of visual NPC laborers for added realism, which I believe would enhance the gameplay significantly. Further detailing in facade construction would be a welcome improvement. Also, the inclusion of larger industrial or commercial projects, such as constructing a skyscraper over multiple contracts, would be fantastic.

Overall, Construction Simulator is the most fun I've had in a long time. P.S.: As a self-confessed workaholic, this game has been a delightful escape\t\t\t\t\t

The tutorial was not bad at all and overall performance of the game is pretty good on my setup. I can honestly see myself putting some time in this game. If I have overlooked something and there is a way to get rid of it, please let me know.

Overall, I would recommend getting this game if you enjoy building simulations style games. If you can deal with the mouse cursor issue and ignore it, it is not a bad game at all.\t\t\t\t\t
