\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame has potential, but is simply unfinished. I was looking forward to this one, but lost interest because of quite a few immersion-breaking issues. There is a roadmap where some of these issues are supposed to be addressed, but this should have been done pre-release and not afterwards.
- Traffic: traffic is just simply unfinished (I just tried EU). Signals are a joke and AI traffic doesn't stick at all to rules or signs. Mix that with all cars going under the speed limit and after a short while you are quick travelling everywhere or just going rogue GTA style.
- Progression / Economy: There is no real challenge in this game and no way to adjust your experience. Some people like the grind and the sense of achievement, when you are finally able to buy a new machine. In this game you are completing a few minor jobs and are basically able to afford every machine twice. There is no need for planning on what to buy next, instead you can mindlessly spend it on everything. I am sure, other people are enjoying this, but that's what different difficulties are for. Also, there is nothing like a mid- or late-game which would keep you invested for longer.
- Cockpit View: Again, for some degree of immersion I prefer the 1st-person / cockpit view, which is realistically unusable right now. You are unable to look back and the quality and angle of mirrors is way too bad. I tried a setup with a wheel instead of a keyboard but quickly gave up, because there are no settings at all for e.g. Force Feedback and the virtual wheel of all machines just turns by 90 degrees either way, which just feels ridiculous at some point.
- Optimisation: A 3070 isn't enough for high quality because of frame-rate lows.
- Bugs:: Seen my fair share of flying cars and other glitches

All-in-all this game feels way too arcade to put more hours into it. Again, there will be people that like exactly that, but there is no way to customize this experience. In my opinion, it's just not finished yet. It's a shame as you already feel, that a lot of work went into this game and the idea/foundation is great.\t\t\t\t\t
