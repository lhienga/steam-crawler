Plainer gets stuck at base of trailer ramp. while using Cold Plainer on a slight up hill grade it is very very slow and on a down hill grade the vehicle moves super speed. Most of the time the vehicle won't move at all, you have to tease the accelerator by spamming.

2.) The waypoint system is horrible. If set a waypoint most of the time it won't take you the quickest rout, it takes you a scenic rout. As your following the waypoint path, the path keeps changing on your mini map.

3.) Asphalt Compactor- When activating compaction with only the front vibrator roller on pavement a pop up window stays on the screen for your entire compaction process telling you that compaction is disabled when in fact its working fine.

4.) No need for most of the driving everywhere. If you fast travel in a tractor with trailer and heavy equipment on trailer the fee is only about 100 coins for everything. If you fast travel inside a piece of heavy iron (NOT!!!) on a trailer you get price gouged very badly. As I've compared the pricing it can cost about 1000 coins to fast travel a heavy piece of equipment as opposed to fast traveling it on a trailer for 130 coins.

5.) No need to waste time unloading your vehicles from a trailer, Just hop inside the trailered vehicle and go to quick setting wheel and hit the reset, you'll magically wind up next to the trailer in the vehicle ready to do whatever you were going to do.

6.) Graphics are horrible, very primitive human figures.

7.) Very little content. The jobs you can except are very repetitive. The same jobs all the time. There is a very very small rotation of jobs.

8.) The campaigns are too short. There are only 4 campaigns. 3 of them have 5 projects and 1 of them has 4 projects.

9.) The map is too small.

10.) A lot of the jobs in the campaigns require special delivery that require you to pick up, then deliver the materials to the job site. But........ half of the time you don't know where to get the item, so you go to your map and choosing dif product manufactures and you still wont find the item for sale so you have to go into the job page and skip that task.

There is no real point to this game, The rewards are fruitless and it's a poorly designed and not very thought out. It almost seems as though it was done with a lot of haste in development and planning and it's definitely not worth $40 U.S.

11.) In the end there is no real point and the game gets really old, really quick.

There are many more things wrong with this game, I just can't list them all. Just do yourself a service and don't waste your money.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like simulations such as all of the FarmSim games and like those this game is a real treat when it comes to operating heavy equipment. In the game you basically take over a small construction company and take on different building projects to make money and purchase additional heavy equipment to make even more money. Most of the construction is pre-fab or concrete work, so you do spend quite a bit of time loading materials and then lifting them into place using heavy cranes or using a concrete pump truck to poor concrete for making buildings. Building projects are considered complete when the shell of a building has been completed so there is no interior work such as plumbing, cabinets, drywall, trim, brick work or even painting. Landscaping is also mostly limited to parking lots. Most jobs involve stripping the top soil, digging foundation trenches then delivering building materials and last using the crane to swing pre-assembled walls, floors, rafters and such into place. While I personally find this fun and relaxing and at times even interesting is can get dry after a while. The most fun is learning to handle some of the heavy equipment and man is the equipment great looking. They have done an excellent job modeling all of the equipment and making it fun to play around with. While the graphics are not earth shattering they do create a nice realistic looking construction environment that is easy on the eyes and believable. there are currently two maps one in Germany and the other in the US. I played a little on the German map but like the US map much better as it seems a little more realistic and more open and scenic. The game is not without its faults and bugs. Traffic lights are way way too long, the moving violation system is basically broken and there is a graphics glitch associated with hauling heavy machinery on flatbed trailers. You can work around the traffic fine mess by turning them off in the options menu. You can also adjust street traffic and turn it off completely in you want. The graphic hauling glitch is more difficult but can be mostly avoided if you limit the amount of equipment to one per trailer and default to the largest trailers for equipment transportation. I think the one thing I would change if I was on this games project team is the amount of projects that are auto completed for you after you have finished a small amount of work in a given section or task. I understand the developers were having to walk a fine line between keeping the game fun and not letting it get too tedious but sometimes the game completes too much and it can take away from your sense of accomplishment. The Job system already has a easy, med and hard ranking system, why not make hard ranking eliminate all of the auto complete stuff so every step has to be fully completed before moving on to the next. Folks that don't like that can lower the job tier or maybe leave the current ranking as it is and simply ask the user before auto competing so it is their option to do so.

Anyway, negatives aside, this is a fun game and one you will enjoy if you like simulators and driving heavy equipment. You will also learn a bit about heavy construction in the process.
