game has an official night and day cycle which the 2015 game sorely lacked. this atleast makes using headlights and work lights a requirement and adds to immersion which is a plus

soils physics is much better then 2015 in it atleast somewhat behaves more realistically to an extent but there remains some room for improvment.

not only do you get a wide range of vehicles there are more brands to choose from such as cat doosan atlas etc. which is nice especially if you want to set a colour scheme to your fleet of vehicles though the road map indicates that in the fuure pleyaers will be able to customise their vehicles colouring.

nursery for plants have not been included which i think to be daft so now you buy a small quantity of plants from the building meterials dealer. i don't like it but hopefully they will return the nursery at some point.

player can create and pave roads in 2022 giving asphalt pavers compactors and a dumptruck which in and off itself is awsum but it would be nice if they would now add a way to paint on the road markings, hoepfully this will become a thing when they release the update providing manual labour elements.

you get access to the cold planers asphalt pavers compactors and dumptruck allowing you to both update dirt roads and repair broken/worn down sections of road which is a huge plus.

typically you can't customise your home bases which i hoped you would be able to do i hope that in future the devs will let us set things up the way players want things. also it would have been nicer if it would let us set designated parking spots for machines and even have specially esignated spots for tower cranes.

irritably they still seem to count tilors as a single vehicle meaning it takes up a slot away from other machines you could have out

the one main feature 2015 has is missed in 2022 there is no reset to base feature which would have been nice especially with nearly your entire fleet in a area that miles from the home base.

has 2 maps and both are quite a respectable size.

this isn't much of a reward for the players to complete the main campaign in truth all one would have to do is complete 2 campaigns and leave it at that since it doesn't make mother difference to complete all 4 plus the finalie. an i deal reward would be granting the player a place to build their own house without restrictions on that map. but complete the campaigns on both maps and it should give you access to a 3rd map which could be a blank canvass and the player can then design and build a brand new city, building in a place designated by the player.

the metal barrier fencing can be a hinderence to the vehicles at times.

trucks and other vehicles can go flying miles into the air if to much soil accumalates under them from digging or missing with te excavator bucket at first it was funny to watch but can quickly become annouying (if you've played skyrim think dovahkin being slammed buy a giants club and being launched on impact its that only with construction machines)

can turn ai traffic on and off this is useful since there is virtually no speed control.
wheeled vehicles aren't to responsive as in it can take time between button/trigger press to vehichles starting to accelerate.

ofcourse there is more job types to cover and both maps use different meterials generally. building meterials dealer has scaffolding but so far as i can tell its only benn used in maybe 1 job i've not seen it as a requirement to often makes me wonder why its there.

trailors are rather limited unlike 2015 even if some of the trailors was from dlcs there was atleast a veriety base game also comes with the rotary drill which was a dlc vehichle for 2015
present trailors can present problems when trying to load some vehichles, some is bercause the ramp not dropping all the way to the floor regardless of how level the ground is and thye other being because of certain machines width being to wide but you atleast can tie the vehicles down when you do get them onto the trailor. loading cargo for a job however is different you can tie it down like you could in 2015 but it alteast has a snap to feature that allows the sstacking of items in a more orgonised fashion.
anouther issue i have with trailors is were there is space enough fo 2 machines the game seems to stuggle to find the harness points despite them being present and usable.

other bugs are present one such bug i came acrooss in the european map were after starting a specific section of road building that required a large deal of excavation once shifting the rubble and the new stage preparing the trench ends up being auto dug and while under most circumstances i've always foud the excavation to be the more fun part and the rest were merely arbitrary.

weather dynamics don't exist but i hope they add it at a later time.

generally the games good for passin the time when you can't be bothered with anything else but it can get pretty stale after awhile hopefully future updates that is planed will serve to fix major issues and add pretty decent content\t\t\t\t\t
