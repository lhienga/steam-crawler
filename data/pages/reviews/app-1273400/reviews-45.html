I think it's also quite realistic and players can be quite free how to carry out construction in each mission. e.g we can make stock piles with a wheel loader, do cut and fill with a bulldozer or do lifting with an excavator. No light construction work such as welding, installing windows or plumbing work.. all job's need to be done using heavy equipments. There are still some bugs which they will disappear by simply resetting the vehicle's position or restarting the game.. but I believe the dev's will always take care and develop this game through the updates that have been described in their roadmap.
