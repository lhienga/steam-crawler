Loader has cosmetic-only z-bar linkage and bucket follows radial rotation of arms, does not follow a partial leveling path or even a shortcut true-level path like they do in Farming Simulator. I'm really torn whether to keep and hope for improvement or return for refund. It took nearly 2 hours to establish that this just isn't quite right, and I want to like it, I really do, it really IS better than before, but it still falls short.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI could write a long eloquent review about this game, but let me sum up how bad it is in a few easy bullet points:

- Graphics are sub par even on max settings (seriously it looks like I'm playing with children's toys and anti-aliasing doesn't exist).
- Audio is so ungodly loud that I have all my system audio settings turned way down.
- The GPS sends you well out of your way any time you set a waypoint, and I thought GTA was bad....
- The steering wheel visually turns like what, 45 degrees in each direction? Making it feel really awkward when trying to use a steering wheel.
- The AI drives at 15mph no matter what... and I thought Farming Simulator was bad (Why do sim games keep doing this???)
- The physics when digging look pretty cool, but good luck getting a 100% scoop every time. Also, sometimes I scoop in an area and get a huge chunk taken out of the total, but then I move a little bit and dig again only to get 1% taken off......

Inconsistent, ugly, slow, not very sim for a sim if you know what I mean. There are better sim games out there to spend your money on, bypass this one.\t\t\t\t\t
