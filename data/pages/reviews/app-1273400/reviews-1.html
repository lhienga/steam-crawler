+Better graphics and more vehicles
+Upgraded progression system, perks included
+Multiple maps
+Manual Tasks that require you to walk and... press a button
+Dump trucks can spill over if poorly handled

As for the downgrades:
-Dump trucks can spill over if poorly handled (the driving isn't good)
-Can't precariously stack cargo to your liking anymore, it just all snaps in place on your truck
-You still can't coast with your vehicles, they always slightly brake whenever off throttle making keeping to speed limits very annoying

-Whenever loading more than 1 vehicle on a trailer having to by the millimeter precisely park them or else the game refuses to secure them

It is basically fancier CS2015 with the above mentioned changes and everything is still very much on a linear rail. You can still have fun with this one but I would've got expected a little more than a graphical update\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game promises to be a solid simulator with visuals and mechanics that you would expect from similar games like farm sim and american trucking sim.

It is not that.

The controls are janky at best.
Even singleplayer sessions are hosted online only with no option to play offline.
The driving physics are comparable to a 2010 GTA wannabe iPhone game.
The jobs feel like you are being led on a leash.

Overall, I am pretty disappointed with this game. I could tolerate the physics issues and controls, but quite frankly it's just not been that fun for me.

I'm sure there are some out there that will enjoy this, but sadly I am not one of them.\t\t\t\t\t
