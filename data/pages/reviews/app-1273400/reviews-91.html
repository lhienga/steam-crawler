\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game. Lots of content is going to make you busy playing this for a long time.

Nice graphics, nice sounds (I love the sounds when you are digging and hit some rocks). Gameplay is very nice. Some tasks can be boring but you can skip them if you want. The dirt looks real enough and its a pleasure to dig or use the bulldozer.

Some positives and negatives about this game:
- feels a bit like a Construction Simulator 2015 update. They could have done more to the european map.
- missing animations (boards on trailers for example)
- controls are messed up (excavator has QE and YC instead of QE-ZC, easily fixed in settings)
- some bugs related to terrain. You could fall through or get stuck a few meters below the grass.
- bugs related to people walking around (I saw missing shoes). Not really a problem.
- tractor and trailer locks up, making it impossible to turn. Its completely stiff.
- multiplayer sync issues
- its almost impossible to drive anything if you need to adjust a little (driving 0,5 meters is not possible)
- camera (I went to manual camera because the dynamic ones were crap)
- some controls could be explained better (like space for the pump)
- some parts looks like the devs got very cheap. Animations bought on Wish
- Hape looks like he is Napoleon's relative with his hand up all the time when speaking to him
- water looks extremely cheap too. There are water assets for Unity that costs about 10 USD better than this.
- DENUVO! I hate that!

+ nice lighting
+ I love some of the sounds
+ the little icon on ground that shows you where stuff really are when using a crane
+ fast travel system (thank you devs!!!)

All in all, its a game I would recommend. The bugs are going away for sure. You can play with friends. It may cost more than a lot of other games, but if you look at number of hours you will play its not that expensive. More DLC's are planned. Workshop / mods too.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is not a finished game. I have tried to give this game the benefit of the doubt, but there are so many debilitating problems that I cannot enjoy it. This game has potential but feels rushed and unfinished with every key aspect being affected by poor workmanship.

The UI of this game is clunky and aggravating, you NEED a controller to play this game otherwise the impossible mechanics are even worse. This game requires you to enter the menu between minute long tasks. The gameplay is short, choppy, and repetitive and would be therapeutic if the mechanics were not the quality they currently are. The tasks, while amusing, are lacking in fulfillment and do not incite the same level of satisfaction as other comparable simulation games on the market today.

This game starts to take a turn when the controls are introduced. Each vehicle has its own control pattern, while this makes sense between vehicle types, each control input is counterintuitive and all around poorly thought out. Control operations do not work well with each other and the entity hitboxes of vehicles and objects are odd and not properly sized. This leads to an aggravating time as you will fight with the vehicle the entire time you play.

While vehicles and the map look appealing and the map does have some life, there's an emptiness and lacking feeling throughout. One of the biggest problems that you will encounter is the camera functions, or lack thereof. The camera does not work and there is no option to change settings or set specific views in the menu. Players cannot even rotate the camera around their vehicle as they operate or drive. This leads to very frustrating camera angles and unplayable mechanics. I have read some forums about changing camera options outside of the game, but in my opinion this is a fundamental game mechanic and should be functional out of the box.

On the road vehicles are clunky, slow, and have a tendency to drift on their own. Vehicle hit boxes are large and not centered in travel lanes and using equipment that will switch between drive and reverse like a pavement roller, there is a noticeable lag between the switch of the gear where the player will hold the accelerator and the vehicle will roll backwards until it moves in the correct direction.

Overall this game has a lot of potential but in its current release state is not worth the price or aggravation of playing. I recommend the practice of buyer beware with this game and encourage them to research before committing the money toward this half finished and frankly rushed game.
