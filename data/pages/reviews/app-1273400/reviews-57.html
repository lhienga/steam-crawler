\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlthough you get alot of gameplay, this game still has some issues.
The camera is one of the worst I have seen in 30 years of gaming.
The cranes and the concrete pump should have an operation circle visible before positioning the vehicle.
The bull get's stuck on sand. Yeah, that really happens.
Some bulls drop a part of their load when lifting the bucket.
It might help to have an idea how many trucks of asphalt or concrete is needed before choosing a task or to partially load a truck with the exact quantity you need. You can lock these behind skill upgrades.
The asphalt scraper should be locked onto a truck so it moves with the scraper. Make it more fun for solo players.
When the game suggests vehicles for the best job, make sure to grade it from best to worst.
It would be nice to know in advance, when picking up heavier objects, what crane can lift them.
Make the crane on the truck that pulls the large trailer reach the back of the trailer.
That's it, for now.\t\t\t\t\t
