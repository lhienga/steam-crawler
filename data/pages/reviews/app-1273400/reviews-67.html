\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game for a mobile platform. Unfortunately, for PC it is weak. There are many elements that are unnecessarily simplified, many that need to be refined. I've played a lot of Construction Simulator 2015 and I can compare. I skip Construction 2 because it was an obvious android copy, and 3rd because it was typical for consoles. Of course, the new game is graphically much better than the 15th. But the 15th was 7 years ago. In terms of mechanics, the game is poor. On a PC, we do not need a circular menu, only keyboard controls. We do not need so many tags and automation, e.g. when loading a pallet onto a truck or unloading them. Or unloading with a crane. The player wants to control what he do. And now the game does it for us. Simplifications and compromises are a bad idea. This game is great, but for the phone and console. For now I will give this game back and wait until the creators think what they want and take PC players seriously. I think it's time to come back to Constructor 2015.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome game! This game is very well designed and WAY MORE FUN than I thought it would be. Getting started and making enough money to start buying equipment is much less of a grind than I thought it would be. Every job is a little different, although many tasks are repetitive, the projects and variety of construction is different quite different for each job. So I do NOT get board and feel like I'm just stuck in a grind to progress. Progression is straight forward and with a little planning and common sense choices you can soon have your very own construction equipment fleet. I like how I sometimes have to be logical and creative to accomplish certain tasks efficiently, to spend the least and make the most profit. In other words the game makes you think, Just as you have to do in real life. And every job is different, just like in real life.
I was quite impressed with the detail of the projects and variety. I play on PC with Xbox controller and mostly I never touch the keyboard. The machines work amazingly well considering the complexity of some of them. While there are some very minor glitches, there was nothing that crashed my game or that I could not easily work around. If you do have an issue or just can't complete a task, just go to jobs and skip that step. You just won't get paid for that one step you skip.
Much credit to the developers!! Very well conceived and designed! I look forward to whatever new and exciting updates and maps might come in the months ahead.\t\t\t\t\t
