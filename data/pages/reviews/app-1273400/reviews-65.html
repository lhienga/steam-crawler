\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Game is a time killer and has a "okayish" multiplayer experience.
- Major improvement from the older construction simulators.
- If you get annoyed by the lack of Pros this game has just go bug hunting with friends and you will have more fun then in the actual gameplay of the game.

Cons:
- Game lacks detail and misses the "simulator" vibe.
- Contract/Mission issues, you can spread gravel maybe 1/5 of the way and the game will say u completed the task and just 100% finish it, making the game feel super arcade and annoying. ( This goes for all missions, paving etc )
- Excavators work better then before but still they are a mess.
- "transforming" a vehicle e.g backhoe loader to use the backhoe you have to stop completely be on even ground or the game will freak out or just not change the controls.
- trucks accelerate crazy fast even with 20000 KG loads.
- traffic is still bad and the whole environment feels empty.
- Tutorial is literally 2 hours long and you can only play multiplayer after you have fully completed the tutorial.
- Missions are literally mirrored copies of each other. i understand that variety in a simple field is hard, but cmon, same objectives same type of building/project, same pay IN EVERY MISSION.
- Vehicles/Equipment lack variety and for some reason on ultra graphics they still look like plastic.
- First person view freaks out the server if ur playing in multiplayer. ( this was a bug in the 2014 one aswell )
- Gameplay is dull, repetitive, full of glitches and bugs.
- Overall physics feel like they are just mildly improved from the 2014 one.
- For 35€ the game is not worth it ( or 40€ if u want some freaking skins )

Conclusion:
Grade: 1.5/5
Reasoning: Gameplay is clearly not finished, missions are 95% bugged or the game finishes them for you e.g spread gravel and u throw 2 buckets worth and its 100% and then the game just adds all the missing gravel, the amount of issues with the game ruin the gameplay.

IF you buy this, buy it in a sale for 10-15€ its not worth any more then that.\t\t\t\t\t

Pros:
- Ground deformation mechanics are great
- Decent selection of machinery
- Machinery is branded and modelled well
- Multiplayer is fun
- The AI traffic is pretty good, not like other sims.... looking at you FS22
- The map size is (in my opinion) perfect for this type of game
- Most of the sound design is good
- Each contract you accept, you choose just how much detail you want. Build a warehouse in 10 steps or 40.
- Lots of options, difficulty, finances and control settings (and trust me, you need them..)
- Most vehicles operate pretty accurately to their real world counterpart

Cons:
- If you drive over a 4 inch curb you may be thrown 1000ft into the air.
- Ambient sounds you can't turn off or lower separately.. A cricket looping at night, for example
- Small variety in jobs
- Run a red light = get a fine. Stop at a red light = also get a fine...
- You have these big construction sites with immovable fences 1m from the work zone...
- The camera control. You have to click L3 every time you want to move the camera. 3rd to 1st perspective in.. 3 clicks?
- Playing solo is pretty grindy (ok if you like that, I dont mind it too much but others may be annoyed)
- Tracked vehicle controls, forwards and backwards on each track is a separate button. This isn't a new thing but in this, it is so frustrating and unless I'm missing an option in the menu, you cant change it.
- Game forces you to use particular machines, want to take the top soil off? Cant use a backhoe. You MUST use a dozer. Want to compact dirt with your CB44b roller? NOPE, thats for asphalt only. Concrete a 6sqm slab? Concrete pump. Want to stand up a 2.4m high prefab? Tower crane. "Tower crane? I could just use the backhoe or bobc...." TOWER! CRANE!

Here comes a rant lmao
- Loading machinery onto a truck (or float) isn't required (you can fast travel) but the game encourages it and I really enjoy doing it. But in this it is the most frustrating thing I've ever seen. Vehicles get stuck on the slightest bump, like the end of your ramps, you have to use 64 wheel, steerable heavy hauler because its the only float wide enough for most vehicles and then.... THEN.. if you get the machine on the float, it may or may not be allowed to "attach" to the float. If it doesnt attach you have to keep swapping back and forth trying to find the sweet spot where it wants to attach. And if you cant get it to attach, the ramps wont go up and if the ramps are down the trailer is locked in place. So f&#k you for trying to be immersed, use fast travel like a normal person. Honestly this is a rant over something pretty minor but I got so frustrated during this that I tried to refund the game xD

Alright I've lost my mind a little. Basically it's an ok game, but its JUST too frustrating for me to recommend, the controls are garbage, its too janky, the looped sounds make me mute it, the camera.... oh god the camera. If it was cheaper I'd be cool with it, but it still feels like a ipad port. Not worth $50.\t\t\t\t\t
