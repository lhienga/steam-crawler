The large selection of vehicles and they all handle really well as well as sound awesome.
The campaigns are great as well as the standard contracts plenty to do and plan ahead for in which vehicles to invest in and expand your fleet or grow the business.
The environment is beautiful and the town really does feel alive. I have only played the European map so far and there is so much to do. If you have always to to dig a huge hole with a hydraulic excavator, build a stunning hotel or just burn through town in a 40 ton semi full of iron support beams then you really need to give this a bash. First time I have paid full price for a game in a long while but it honestly is the best 30 quid I have spent in a long time.

Congrats to the Devs for an extremely polished game.\t\t\t\t\t
