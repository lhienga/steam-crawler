\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game needed to be finished. Crashes, bugs, and unoptimized UI make for a frustrating experience.

I really wanted to enjoy this game and after completing one of the 4 campaigns and getting one of every vehicle type (except for the civilian vehicle) I think I've had enough. I'm not big on reviewing games and cant be bothered to write out a nice neat categorized list, but heres a few points.

Idk what the deal is but when i launch the game it freezes requiring an ATL-f4 to close, its always fine the second time.

The buy menu Is auto sorted to MFGs and not machine type so I had to switch every time and there was no saved settings.

Tracked vehicles are almost undrivable, they only move in straight lines and require a complete stop to turn even on controller will varied imputs.

The concrete pump controls are so bad, I'm sure they are hard to use IRL but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ after spending hours using it, it has not gotten any easier at all, and while Ive seen reviews saying all the controls are bad I think a select few are the real culprits. I guess the real problem is no uniformity between different machine types.

I think I could go for a while actually, but the last one I wanted to mention is that it was way too easy getting all the machines. There was no progression, I never had to use the backhoe, or buy a smaller excavator or crane while I built the company. I Always could afford the biggest and baddest machine that I needed. I understand people may not like the grind but you could always just rent them if that was a problem.

Would not recommend sadly.\t\t\t\t\t
