\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe reputation about Astragon games under delivering, in this game is also unfortutenly true. Let's dive in.

I had suffered many bugs, and in the first 50 minutes of the game I did 0 constructing. The game is called Construction simulator, not "run errands and repair this city supermarket simulator", the initial experience there was terrible.

Let's talk bugs, many bugs. Engines not starting, menus not opening or not clicking and the best one, performance. I have a pretty beefy computer which has no problem handling games at 1080p 70+fps on max settings, but this one barely went over 40fps, even on medium quality. Playable, but not enjoyable.

"Construction" simulator. The tutorial is terrible. Instead of teaching you how to use the machines or how to play the game, instead it's this terrible jokes, terrible story-telling missions that teach you the basics of using 2-3 machines. But the best part is, that it's not about constructing, no sir. It's about literally trying to fix a city after some sort of storm. Doing chores around, fixing a supermarket and driving cargo. I mean, I would understand all that if this was not a construction simulator, but I'm here to make constructions, build places, not be a 'fetcher' quest game. PS, after the tutorial you are still in this crappy terrible jokes story thing and you get to construct after hours. Maybe they did this so when you actually get to the terrible construction experience, you can no longer refund this :)

Engine. I don't know how they managed to make the game so unoptimized but it's not enjoyable. There are also no physics engine involved. You'd think for a construction simulator, things like dirt or concrete and stuff would have some basic physics, it's not that hard after all, if minecraft mods can do it in Java with terrible performance, I'm sure you can use UE4 physics. But nope, insteaed what you get for the lovely price of 35 euros is basically a very bad model change. You 'scoop' dirt but you actually just remove parts from the model of dirtpile and add parts into your scooper thingy. Hilarious.

TLDR; No. Do not waste 35euros on this junk.\t\t\t\t\t
