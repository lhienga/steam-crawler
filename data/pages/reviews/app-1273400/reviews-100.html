[2022-09-21] After launch
tl;dr - I would recommend this game but not yet. It needs work.

Things that I observed from scratch:
+ game has some analytics, so they will check data and upgrade
- camera model is bad, like super bad, in third person when you walk it should just follow you, and move around on pressing mouse or alt with mouse movement (but it's not, it just always moves in freelook mode)
+ graphics are okay, they look nice with some good details
- game is lagging on every settings, pls optimize your game, it shouldn't run this bad
+ there is traffic settings, and how much there is around
- you can just drive into other cars and people walking, no penalty no nothing
+ nice expanded tutorial that teaches you everything in little steps
- ...but the tutorial is literally 2 HOURS, I wonder why... (you can only refund game back within first 2 hours so I think I know where is it coming from), and it explains stuff simply but at the same time GPS runs you to random street to discover gas station and ride another 3km to point. Literally they are making sure you stay in game in tutorial first 2 hours
+ you can drive vehicles, colide with stuff, load things, unload and do stuff just like in trailers
- driving model is bad, your truck going 40 kmh is forever in tokyo drift mode slipping tires and going sideways, this needs fix. With game lagging it's terrible, I kept crashing constantly
+ detailed sounds
- some of the sounds are broken, like loaders hitting ground do a lot of loud cranks, it hurts my autistic self, treshhold of this sounds should be based on tool speed when touching ground not on constant sliding, you don't expect BONK BONK BONK sounds when you are shoveling dirt


Please fix, I know devs are reading comments. If you fix it I might change my review. I might - but I haven't tested MP yet and campaign so I don't know how it will go and how stable game will be.

I wish it would have mods support in future, with more custom vehicles, maps and tasks it will be really good game.\t\t\t\t\t
