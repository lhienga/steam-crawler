Why you would set anything other than "Toggle" as the default for gamepad is beyond me but Germans are always doing weird stuff and getting away with it! Ich liebe dich wirklich

Anyway, I held onto it and gave it a few hours for the benefit of my young son who is crazy about vehicles of all shapes and sizes. Now having trouble tearing myself away from it as once you get through the tutorial and get over the really stiff dialogue "cutscene" parts, like talking with Hape, the underlying construction GAMEPLAY (the important part) is pretty solid and enjoyable if you like sorta repetitive motion with a satisfying end product for your efforts.
It can get tedious occasionally but most of the time it is pretty cool, enjoyable and, dare i say, relaxing!
Certainly beats football games that's for sure and is on a par with similar Sim titles like Farm sim and Train sim world etc.
So much better than the publishers other dev with their police sim, that game is just woeful and nearly stopped me buying this one. Astragon take note!

8/10 - Would dig again

Things I would change -
Needs option for brake intensity,
Needs smarter traffic bots though this can be disabled if it becomes an issue,
Colour scheme needs a major reshade but i can probably do this myself,
Not clear if modding is supported? Could really use modding/workshop support.\t\t\t\t\t
