\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdate after 10 hours of gameplay: far better than expected; dirt, gravel, etc in dump truck actually moving around during transport, take a hard corner and the load moves to one side or spills over, it is amazing. Also, truck interiors is just gorgeous, you do not notice it first, but after trying several truck you start to notice edges of plastic as it makes up pieces of the interior looks like the real thing, it is nothing I have ever seen elsewhere. It is so fun to drive from interior view (driving physics are also good) that I do it despite of a fairly good teleport system (driving to locations saves some money, but not that much compared to what you earn, nevertheless driving with these awesome trucks just fun, also instruments work and look very cool). I think this is a newer game engine that is why all the surprise for me. Also the city and country side, the road and highway system is huge, GT5 like, nothing we are used to in farm or construction simulations to this point (apart for size it is also very well built and interesting, in a way it reminds me of cyberpunk 2077 map). My recommendation is to get it even without sale, it is that good, however make sure you spend at least 10 hours before you move on to something else, the good stuff start become more apparent once you learn a bit about gameplay.

One more thing; vehicles feel heavy (but not slow), excavation physics, dirt deformation is simply amazing a super realistic, and vehicle suspension deforms to terrain and road side curbs. What I have seen so far is my dumptruck's rear wheels hanging into the hole in the dirt I had just excavated as I have backed the truck to close to the hole, wheels spin for traction, etc. Not exactly snow runner but close enough for me. Given all the other game mechanics I do not believe I go back to my 300 hour snowrunner save any time soon.


For that matter, vehicle exteriors, object in the world appear much smoother, especially around the edges. If I had to guess this is a new game engine, but I am not going to bother looking it up (too busy playing the game...:)

So far I like it. It does some things better than snow runner or farm simulator. What it does better than snow runner? It has a much livable map, NPCs, you can get out of the vehiche, which I always missed in snowrunner. What it does better than farmsimulator 2022? Construction Simulator allows deforming the ground, excavating, etc, and does it in a pretty impressive way. Also, the map is by far much bigger, and better populated, the world just more interesting to me.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have to say that I'm impressed with this game. Generally the construction process is correct. Obviously things are missing or are oversimplified, but it's a game that it's a fun game for most of the time. Unloading operations can be a bit boring though.. Also there are minor jobs (such as moving stuff), which don't add any value as they don't pay much.

Pros:
Graphics, variety of machines (I always wanted a concrete pump sim..), variety of projects, physics of the ground during excavations.

Cons:
Crane physics (ropes are way too flexible), dirt effects are missing on machines, mud is missing from the excavations, duration of the day could be longer, can't skip night, cant assign tasks to a fellow worker (ie to unload materials), can't repaint the vehicles (at least with factory colours). I'd also like to see construction personnel on site along with welfare facilities, just to add some life.

The cons might seem more than the pros, but these are minor things that could be added, along with the ability to mod the game. A free paly mode could be added in a way, that we can select a project, machinery, and build it.\t\t\t\t\t
