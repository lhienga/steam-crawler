\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDO NOT BUY UNLESS ON SALE

Seriously, over-hyped, under delivered. Its not a bad game but....

Graphics cranked all the way up, looks like good graphics for an xbox 360. Stuff pops in when you're on top of it. Ghost items to crash into. Traffic spawns right in your path.

The traffic system is beyond bad. city streets move at 11-13 mph. highway at 24-28 mph????? can walk to your job site faster.... The red lights are terrible. One of 4 lights turns green (other 3 red) for 10 seconds, then the pedestrian walk light goes on (all 4 lights red) for 30 seconds, then a different green light comes on for 10 seconds, then the cross walk again.... Traffic simulator would have shorter red lights, but if you play with traffic offenses on, be prepared to wait at a light for 2 - 3 in-real-life minutes, which is about an hour in game.

The physics engine is just as good as the traffic. going up a slight hill? this Mack semi truck pulling nothing seems like 15mph max speed... right??? Driving a 8 ton, 600 hp dozer? Be careful, that 2 inch curb lip will either get you stuck or launch you into outer space.

The overall gameplay is okay, if not extremely basic. Theres no real challenge just time and money, which is fine, just wish there was some sort of economic challenge aside from rental / repair costs vs your bank account. Certain gameplay features are missing, like a way to fill up machines that arent "road worthy" aside from loading a trailer and driving it to the station. Certain things can only be done by 2 or more players which is super annoying.\t\t\t\t\t
