\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will have to be the one here who says that this game isn't good.

I got this game on a sale because it was a game I really enjoyed as a child.

But unlike a lot of games I played from nostalgia who lived up to what they were (some more and some less) this just didn't do it for me...
For instance, the lion king and Aladdin are some games I really enjoyed as a child and again as an adult, in this case the game was just insanely easy (even for a kids game!) and the lines were so cringey for me I just couldn't sit and play anymore.

Every time you move between screens you cant skip the animations, half the game is just sitting watching that.

I wanted to say that at least the game would MAYBE? be fun for kids but I had to read the manual just to figure out how to save load and exit the game because there isn't really a menu screen you can get to with esc or with your mouse and I doubt every kid will want to read the manual.
Unfortunately I played the game for the first time over 2 weeks after buying it so I cant ask for a refund (first game I actually want to refund on steam)

I dont think this game is worth a buy even on a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
