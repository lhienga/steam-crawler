Black Ops III: A Thrilling Trio of Action, Zombies, and Multiplayer Mayhem

Let's get right into it!

In the campaign mode, players are given more choice than ever before, with customized loadouts and larger, more open missions. The addition of Cybernetic Core powers brings magic-like abilities to the battlefield, making the game feel like science-fiction meets fantasy. The story is a wild ride, starting traditionally, then getting confusing, and finishing with a bizarre finale. Players can enjoy the campaign split-screen or online with friends, choosing to play as either a male or female character.

I truly believe this game to be the pinnacle of Zombies. It has it's own fun and flare with "Shadows Of Evil" which takes players into a 1940s film noir world filled with Lovecraftian monsters.

With Hollywood talent like Jeff Goldblum and Ron Perlman lending their voices, the experience is both creepy and fun, providing plenty of challenge for players.

It even introduces GobbleGum which essentially a power up load out you can customize to your own liking. Now for the true fans of OG zombies, it even takes those old maps you know and love. Zombies Chronicles is honestly the best thing to ever come out of this game. In fact that dlc bundle has...

While the visuals may not always be as impressive as previous titles, the game makes up for it with its engaging story and freedom to play how you want.
