Quick Notes

• Confusing, long-winded and often downright boring and repetitive story.
• You can tell some story choices were inspired by the studio's political ideology. It's not to a disturbing agree, but I'll be careful to screen for this in next iterations. If it gets worse, there'll be no more Call of Duty for me.
• Really interesting technologies (cyborgs, star wars war machinery, brain chips, et cetera).
• This game is a little more flash than substance. Not a fan. They did a far better job on the first two games in the series.
• This felt a lot more like a hack and slash game. You get TONS upon TONS of mobs thrown at you and it makes sure you're playing for longer, but I'm not sure it's more fun. I ended up dying a lot, because there'd be some guy spawning in behind me and slashing away at my neck.
