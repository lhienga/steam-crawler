\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have purchased this game on every console I have ever owned that could run this game. Call of Duty I believe is a dying franchise, but what I can respect is the game it put out in 2015. Yes, the campaign is not super good, but everything else is just perfect. Multiplayer is fun as hell and my personal favorite exo COD to date. I enjoyed the seemingly endless amount of weapons that were added during this game's lifecycle. Yes, the supply drop system is dated and pretty trash, but it was fun for a little bit.

However, the real meat and potatoes of this game to me in my opinion is zombies (I am very bias to zombies). Zombies is one of the first things I ever really nerded out at. Every video online I've watched. Every secret and easter egg I've seen. The lore I can recite like its the back of my hand. So imagine my surprise when one of the most unique storylines I have ever seen came to a close. BO3 zombies is without a doubt one of my favorite times of my life. The maps were amazing. The story was amazing. It was so amazing that they even remastered eight classic fan favorite maps two years after this game had come out. The dedication the team at Treyarch had for zombies really showed and it was a time to be alive. The Z House. The ending. Zombie Chronicles. Man, good times. Time really does fly.

This is a very bias review for one of my favorite games in my entire life, mostly just for my favorite gamemode in all of gaming. I won't ever forget the excitement and fun I had/have still playing this game to this day. When I built my PC thanks to the help from my friends in 2023, one of my first purchases was BO3 AGAIN. The custom zombies maps had always been a dream of mine to play and let me tell you it is just fantastic what people can come up with when given the materials to create. That alone is a selling point for me. Endless fun even when the game is done. Yes, this is a love letter to this game, but I hope this love letter finds the right person when considering buying an older COD title. If you are to buy any, buy this one. Do yourself a favor and play zombies, especially custom zombies. You will not be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
