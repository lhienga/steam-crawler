\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSettlement Survival is a gem city builder after the likes of Banished and Patron, which in fact were the two builders I played the most before this one. It had only minor bugs, is not as slow paced as others such as the upcoming gridless Foundation; kind of standard fare but enjoyable to expand and conquer your maps, and the achievements can be a challenge to get, for the most part not the tedious kind. Even on the hardest difficulty it is a rather easy city builder, however, after youve gotten past the initial formative years for your settlement, and even with disasters. But there's a sense of accomplishment in this quaint game that persists as daily life goes on. There are blueprint buildings that act as "Wonders" you can build, tied to the factions, including a Castle, often with very large bonuses to your town, but that require massive amounts of materials. One of them, and this is cute, is an Aztec Temple where your villagers will periodically and methodically be sacrificed to the pagan gods. If you leave it on you will soon find your populace dwindling. As I kept playing I found myself hooked and loving this game.
Because it is not very unique however;
and not as monumental like other city builders that are on the horizon
I give it 8/10 stars
Still a very solid showing and a Large B+ Rank
