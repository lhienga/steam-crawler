\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere are my first impressions and criticisms:
+ Although the game arouses great curiosity and excitement at first, then it turns to statistics only.
+ It's really difficult to assign people to jobs one by one later (if there are no idle workers) since new people don't automatically fill the jobs of because of deceased people.
+ I cannot assign workers to repair and construction sheds from the main management screen. Bugs I guess.
+ The characters are soulless, they have no personality, they only exist as statistics. There is no difference between 10 people and 100 people. People are just statistics.
+ Families, as if members of families were only randomly assigned. They don't have a link or relationship I suppose. Also no wedding.
+ Town administration policies, politics, decision-making mechanisms, culture, not exist.
+ There seems to be 5 tribes in the game for trading, but I couldn't find the 5th one to trade with.
+ The variety of commercial products is low.
+ Food variety is good, but production supplies are difficult. Especially dairy products are very difficult to produce, and the benefit is relatively low. Fertilizer production is unbalanced I think. High turd amount, low output.
+ The characters don't seem to prefer the best quality of the two separate products. (I'm talking about tools and clothes).
+ Very few options for building upgrades. There could have been more options and more slots.
+ It is difficult to plan a city because the shapes of buildings such as churches, bathrooms, theaters and heaters are very variable according to the grid city structure. I wish they weren't in so many irrelevant shapes.
+ In the late game, the unhappiness effect that occurs just because the population is too much is meaningless. There are countless beautiful things in the city, but people are unhappy just because the city is too crowded. Excessive growth in the game was tried to be prevented in this way, but an unrealistic unhappiness emerged.
+ No leadership nor leader exist.
+ People who give buffs to the city live much longer than the normal population.
+ There could be special foods that have no production but can only be obtained by trade.
+ The causes of death of people are not very diverse.
+ It is very difficult to maintain the population after a certain time because high level of unhappiness. So continuing is meaningless.\t\t\t\t\t
