\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMany have compared this game to Banished and for good reasons. Some hate the similaries, some love it.
While the core concept is similar to Banished, there is enough differences to make Settlement Survival able to stand on its own. While Banished is washed out and rugged in graphics, Settlement is cartoonish and cute.

It's definitely a game worth buying and enjoying, but I would say; enjoy it for what it is. Don't compare. You will do this game injustice by comparing it to other similar management games. You don't go watching Game of Thrones, only to then compare it to Lord of the Rings. Both may be fantasy, both may have dragons, both may be books written by old men with R.R in their names, but there the similiarities end.

Is Settlement Survival Game of Thrones or Lord of the Rings? You decide!\t\t\t\t\t
