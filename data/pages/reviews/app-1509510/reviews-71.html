\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've not played Banished, but I'm a big fan of city builder and other building/simulation games, so I had been eyeing this game for a while. It's pretty decent for an Early Access game, but it still needs some polishing to do before it can be launched publicly.

There are some slight audio and grammar issues in its contents that are a bit annoying when you come across them. The 'Help' section can appear like huge walls of text at times, so it's sometimes difficult to find information on what to do next to evolve your colony.

All this said though, it's still a comfy fun game to relax and get lost in. The graphics are charming; music is good (though it can be repetitive); and the mechanics feel smooth to play. I was looking for a relaxing medieval type city-builder game to play when taking a break from MMORPGs, and this seems to be it. I look forward to future developments.\t\t\t\t\t
