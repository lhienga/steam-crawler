In-Depth Review

Settlement Survival is the hidden gem of city-building, simulation, strategy game. Which offers a lot for someone who enjoys spending his time building and decorating every single part of his city. There isn´t particular story in-game but after you pass tutorial you start your first city on map generated by your preference, creating your "own" story.

The Game

Gameplay
Game itself is pretty explanatory and not by any means hard, if you find this game hard in the beginning, I recommend to switch it to easiest mode in which you basically cant lose. There are I think under 150 buildings in the game in which you need to unlock throw mission tree with time. You start from year 1 and you experience all 4 seasons throw the year. During the game you need to meet the basic demands for your villagers. After some time you can improve the happiness and work efficiency of your villagers by crafting some luxury items such as wine, cakes or make some warm coats to keep them warm and fancy during the winter. You can also find yourself in a trouble because sometimes natural disasters such as earthquakes or floods can strike your city.

I found this game to be pretty explanatory and not too complicated. If you are not in a mood for something challenging and you want to just relax and build something, this is the game for you.

Conclusion
I can´t remember correctly but I think I got the game under 10 euros. But this game is definitely worth the money. There is a lot of place for improvement or/and some new buildings, etc. You won't be disappointed by buying this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a cute game that's very welcoming for new players of the genre, but also for fans that like to manage a complex economy. I play those kinda game more for the creative aspect to it.

I wish there were more decoration, customization or mixing option as basic as switching house colour or having more pathway options, for example. This would give a little bit more freedom for making beautiful and unique villages. Reaching max level in trade with different faction should unlock a theme for basic structures like house, gathering stuff or stress/health relievers.

I only say that because i've played this game a lot and I wish I can enjoy it more. They are offering ongoing update and I feel it might be possible eventually.

8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor the colony-builder junkie, this game is amazing. The tech tree is well thought out, but not overly complicated. Management of citizens has just enough depth. Yeah, I love city/colony building, but I don't love managing each of the 500 citizen's minute actions. That just ain't me. I also enjoy that the game doesn't include wars or military whatsoever. All of the pressure of this game is growth, improvement, expansion, wealth, and making sure that the masses don't suddenly drop dead due to neglectful leadership... Or forgetting to build your first well until it's far too late.

As an added bonus, this game gets updated regularly, and every update has been great. I'm very excited to how this game will develop and progress!\t\t\t\t\t
