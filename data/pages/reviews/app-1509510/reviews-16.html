\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYa know, normally I don't give up on this type of game so quick but this one just didn't chime with me. A nice paint job but I'm having issue with the play style, though I probably just suck at this game >: ] I am noob after all.

Still though, I think I'd recommend against this one as it is. It has a good, interesting setting, it's honestly not terribly made. For me it's a game pacing issue. Even by my standards, this one is just too slow. I play games like Banished, Pharoah, Children of the Nile, Cities skylines, to name a few of similar genre and I'm normally content to just sit in the standard speed setting but in this one, even I have to speed up and it STILL feel slow. Most people love speeding up the game to max. If you're one of those, you're probably going to have major issues with the game. It's very much a patience is needed sort of game and the learning curve seems... probably higher than average right now.

On the flip side I do see it as more of a challenge to balance, especially at the beginning. Some people like that.
Building/construction takes wayyyyyy to long but I'd say the most irksome parrt of the game is actually the initial stage where you're just waiting on the second generation to get into the educational system. It might take you until the fifth year of the game to start churning out new workers. There is the concept of migrants though.

In part it's because I'm not quite sure how to balance my start out that's giving me the rough time but I for one am not patient enough to learn in it's curent state. I will probably give it another go in a years time to see if balancing improves it for me a little.

Other than the personal preference issue's I've outlined it's actually a pretty good game.\t\t\t\t\t
