And a warehouse should perform the additional function of a supply terminal and a delivery station. Having three separate buildings is insane and overly complicated.

The worker pathing is very buggy. I've seen people walk right next to my cut-stone and brick roads rather than on them. I've seen them leave a road, walk around a building next to the road, then get back onto the road. The worker did not stop at the building and neither dropped anything off nor picked anything up. I guess they just wanted to say hi to the workers in that building?

I should be able to specify what percentage of workers within a building can go on break at the same time. I once saw every single one of my builders go on break at the same time and so construction came to a screeching halt. Same thing with other buildings. I have a transfer station with two employees. Both are on break and have been for months. I tried to close the building and open it back up, hoping different workers would take the job slots, but the same ones came back.

There are many other things, but these are the main ones. And for the record, I have no mods installed.\t\t\t\t\t
