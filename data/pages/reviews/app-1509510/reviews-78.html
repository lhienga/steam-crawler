\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not sure if I can call this game bad. It has some neat ideas. Like I enjoy that crops are things that can be discovered by your villagers. However, there's just too much that I found too frustrating to play beyond one settlement.

It's very slow.
Now colony management sims are a slow genre in general, but it feels like the developers presumed that you'd never have the speed below x2. This is beyond the villagers moving at a snails pace. Population take many years to grow. This is realistic, and could be said part of the challenge, but population growth is constantly outpaced by tech and construction expansion. Plus, there's the habit of events taking many months to complete. The timeline will tell you that a merchant ship is coming and you can watch the three months it takes to come down the river.
The tech is just a mess.
The tech tree is split into several categories, which is neat. However, the techs themselves are all over the place and seperate techs seem like they should be consolidated together. For example fertilizer/fields that use fertilizer/orchards that use fertilizer are all seperate techs. There's a bunch of examples of a tech that gives you a specialized resource but are unable to actually use it until you get other techs. While choosing your next tech is a genuine tough choice a number of times that's simply because you have to try to calculate which tech will pay off the soonest three techs down.

There's other stuff, but they're more of nitpicks. Ultimately, this is not the worst game. It's just that there are better options on the market\t\t\t\t\t
