\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has an immense amount of potential, and is a ton of fun to play. It has great mechanics, great ideas, and a strong foundation.
It's a very deep game, and I can't overstate the amount of ideas, and further depth that could be added into the future.

it takes the very best of its' inspiration/influences, and adds positive mechanics to them.

The content currently available, is already enough to give hours of fun. if the developers continue to output quality work, this game could be a masterpiece on release.

Of course the game is not perfect, no game is.

- There are a few spelling issues, and minor bugs.
Note: I haven't encountered a single game breaking bug or crash.

- The tech tree could be more intuitive, and better arranged.

- More map customization, scenarios, biomes, and other custom sliders would make for even better replay-ability.
Note: there is random generation, and a modest amount of randomness to the maps.

In conclusion: This game is great, and can become a classic if the devs stick to it. It has plenty of upside, a lot of modding potential, and is surprisingly well polished for a new early access title.\t\t\t\t\t
