\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have mixed feelings about this game, but overall I gave it a negative grade due to there being better games in the genre. I played on both Easy and Moderate settings.

On the positive side, if you like settlement building games, this has a lot of aspects that are enjoyable for all. I liked how trade was viable in the game - you couldn't research everything in the short term, so you were better served by specializing in some areas, and trading to get items where you were deficient. There were, however, some aspects of it that detracted from it.
1. UI had some issues. My biggest complaint was trying to figure out how to split a herd. They have 2 buttons where one was needed, but even then, the key was clicking to the left of the view icon, not on the view icon itself.
2. Upgrade system was flawed.
