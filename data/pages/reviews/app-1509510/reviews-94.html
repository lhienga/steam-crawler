\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlease note this review was written during early access.

If you like Banished, you will like this game. It's almost an identical copy from what I can tell; however, I'm excited to see where it will go. There are some comforts I'm used to, having played Banished for hundreds of hours, such as pinning buildings to see their radius of effect, that are not here yet, but may be added in the future.

The graphics are a bit more cartoony than Banished was, but they are stylish and enjoyable.

Some people have complained about the dumb AI; however, I haven't had any critical problems. It seems that saving and reloading your game can correct some issues such as people working half way across the map from their house or getting stuck in the marketplace, etc.

I have already put 25 hours into this game and will continue to follow it and play it as it grows.

If you like citybuilders, I definitely recommend this game. I'm very excited to see it continue to be polished.\t\t\t\t\t
