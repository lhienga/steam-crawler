\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS:
- An updated and honestly better banished
- Nice graphics that look good despite the simplicity
- Many buildings
- AI is generally good
- Good gameplay loop
- Can place landing zone of settlers!
- Good trading

CONS:
- Map generation is sometimes frustrating as boosted resource points
are often in useless locations or close to the edge of the map
- Boosted resource points are only labeled on game start
- Rivers can go in weird and unnatural angles
- Rivers can be diagonal, but river buildings can only face N, S, E, W
This means sometimes river buildings are plain un-placable
- AI will go do their job freezing and die, even when firewood is plentiful
- Happiness and health seem very difficult to fix, even with all the buildings

SUMMARY:
I know I put a lot of cons but it is a good game! I just want some things fixed
or better in Early Access. Worth a buy if you liked Banished for sure.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\ti do love these type of games and i understand that it is a EA. I have tried several different starts with altered settings to change difficulties etc but the same issue persist. I am hoping this gets up dated in the future.

The population doesnt grow as quick as you need to cover the jobs that are available for the buildings that you require to keep happiness up on the off chance that the population maybe actually re populate with children otherwise your doomed. The immergants dont immergrate to your village as fast as you would like either so constantly micro managing building staff and turning them on and off to move workers around to different buildings. becomes quite tedious after a while.

Also because this is a grid based style game( little squares on screen when putting buildings down) instead of free placement, room can become a struggle too when unlocking more buildings via research as there could be a gap where your new building could fit but it wont because its grid based which again can be annoying when you see a gap and want to keep all industry stuff in one area, which means that aint happening or its time to start knocking buildings down again to start moving them around which again can be a little unwanted when you have a village as you want it . This can also be a little fiddly when placing mines on the tiniest of mountains lol as its grid based so can only place them in straight lines which is a pain when the little mountain has rounded edges lol!

it was the price that pulled me towards it instead of a couple others on EA but as it is i am not reallly enjoying it as i thought i would be after watching vids on youtube. maybe further down the line when it has had more things pushed in to it and tweaks.\t\t\t\t\t
