The tech is all over the place and research is very slow. You start with far more production buildings needed than settlers, even if you staff one per building. That situation compounds even 5-10 years later, no matter the difficulty setting. Still in early release, but the game play has become more broken as more content is added. The game was enjoyable at first launch in early release. Some things improved and others worsened over time so far. Hopefully the final product will be polished. I understand that this isn't an English native game so ignoring the numerous grammatical errors is plausible. Banished is unforgiving, but fun. They Are Billions is hard, but fun. Settlement Survival just isn't fun. I'll try it again months down the road when the game is nearly ready for a full release. If the mechanics are fixed and I find the game enjoyable, I'll edit the review.\t\t\t\t\t
