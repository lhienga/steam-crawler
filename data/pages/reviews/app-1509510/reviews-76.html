This isn't an early access issue, if this is the kind of game for you, then this game is in good working order and is pretty. It's just boring is all.

As an additional note, you can only name your villagers with six letters or less. Even if they have a longer name, when you click to change it, it will shorten that name to six letters.
I was going to name a character "Bernard" after a friend. But it would not let me. Seven letters is too much. This isn't a major issue, it's just a silly addition to this, but I mean... that's pretty dumb. Dumb stuff like this will likely be outed after early access is over though.
