\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought the game when it was still in EA; I played it for a bit but could not get anywhere, so I abandoned it. I reinstalled it a month or so ago and this time I had no such problems.

Except that, unlike some other city builders, it does not grab and hold me. Perhaps that's because it's a Chinese game: I did not figure that out when I played it the first time but now I have been looking for such a game, taking place in China; I went through some demos and noticed the similarities. Most obviously, it's terribly slow - I'd like to know a hero who dares to play it at 1x speed (luckily, others up to 10x are available). Also, UI takes some time to get used to - everything you need is there but sometimes in strange places. There are other pointers, e.g., people actually care about how the dead are treated: they don't just bury them and forget all about it.

But these are not real problems. The only one I encountered (and I am not alone in this) is that I have yet to see the day/night cycle and seasons. although I set graphics right. Other than that, no bugs encountered and the game runs just fine.

I guess the problem here is with me and not with the game; even if it does not grab me it has enough original ideas and twists to be recommended. Hopefully, it will grab you.\t\t\t\t\t
