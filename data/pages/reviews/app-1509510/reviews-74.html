\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is very similar to Patron, which I love, so I picked it up. It has a very similar feel, so if you've played that game, you will probably like this one and pick it up quickly.

I really like the music and the graphics in this game, even though the graphics are more simple/stylized and less realistic. It does have a few issues that bug me, but aren't game-breaking:

There are a lot of grammar errors in the game, which is jarring, and sometimes makes tips or info in the tech tree hard to understand.
Tech in this game goes VERY slowly- my game is 13 years in and I'm still missing a lot of tech that I would consider essential in similar games of this type. I like the idea of having to be carefully selective of which tech I choose, but I think it could be sped up a little.
Happiness is a little too difficult, imo- church, alcohol, candy, and theater are said to increase happiness, but I'm 13 years in and only have access to church/alcohol and my town is miserable. Related to the above, I'd love it if tech went faster so I could unlock these things earlier, or if there were more ways to increase happiness.
I have no idea if Decorations have any use in the game for your villagers at all or if they are purely cosmetic.
The storage system is a little confusing- sometimes I will have huge amounts of a resource, and then suddenly my town will start using way more of it than before and the resource goes down to almost nothing. Also, despite having several production buildings for certain resources/products, I have almost none of these resources stored.
Finally, a *very* minor issue: there are a lot of buildings you can make along the water, however most rivers are very curved and it's hard to find places that are straight for long enough to place the larger buildings like the Dock.

Despite these issues, I do enjoy the game. It is very satisfying to slowly spread across the map and build a giant settlement. I would just love it even more if it was easier to keep my villagers happy in the process. Looking forward to seeing the game update and grow!\t\t\t\t\t
