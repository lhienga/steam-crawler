\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely adore this game!
I'm a huge Banished fan and when I saw this game available I had to try it.
It's a healthy mixture of Banished and modern city builder. It's basically Banished with variety.
There is a lot to it but once you get the hang of the different processing buildings and how to maintain a steady population, the world is your sand box.
I'll admit you need to have a decent level of intelligence in order to grasp the game fully because a few bad mistakes and it punishes you with famine, disasters, sicknesses, and just sheer brutality from wildlife.

In my honest opinion, its a 9/10.

Could've been 10/10 if they had some sort of story driven campaign mode instead of constant freeplay.

Overall, I highly recommend this game to any city builder (how far can I go?) kind of player.
