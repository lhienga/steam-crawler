The tech tree covers all facets of survival including trading, education, smelting, food processing, medicine, etc. There is a ton of different resources that can be refined into higher tiers. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tCompared to many other early access games the game is very buggy at the current moment.
During the first two hours of playing i encountered several gamebreaking bugs;

Wells not working so all my villagers died of thirst.
People not building the buildings i ask to be prioritized even though i have plenty of builders, and materials.
A absurd amount of herbs required nonstop. I had 12 people gathering herbs all the time in a village of 45 people. They could not supply fast enough, leading to sick and dying villagers, and as a result, unhappiness.

I also encountered a large assortment of other bugs;
Buildings not working in general
People not using the paths i make, rendering them useless.
Constant deathwaves, some of them due to the gamecrippling bugs mentioned above.

Also encountered a lot of smaller issues such as very obvious spelling mistakes and grammatical errors.

The game has potential, and i will probably try it out again on release. But for now it is not worth spending your time nor money on - It promises Banished, but does not deliver the solid base of it. It is currently a game in dire need of quality assurance.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUPDATED DUE TO SOME OF MY ISSUES BEING FIXED BY THE DEVS IN THE ALPHA BRANCH!
Thank you devs for reading my suggestions and implementing them!

TL;DR - At around a tenner, it's good value as you'll get at least 10 hours out of it. Be warned though! It is a SLOOOoooooow game. The kind you leave idle while doing something else.

Yep, it's definitely heavily inspired by Banished. You could even say it's Banished 1.5 though not quite 2.0 yet.

The theme is brighter and cheerier than Banished. There's not so much a sense of foreboding that Banished has as you can see when disasters are coming on the year progress bar.

Others have complained about the artwork but I actually quite like it. Settler's coming from elsewhere in the world will already know how to build such fancy structures so you shouldn't expect them to live in mud huts and dark and dreary wood cabins. We mastered big wooden beamed houses with wattle and daub style walls hundreds of years ago. It does beg the question though... Where are they getting the clay from for the lovely tiled roofs? The devs should perhaps start us with a grass/straw thatched roof that can be upgraded later after getting clay. I haven't got far into the upgrades yet but it seems most (all?) buildings are upgradeable, though not much visually.

Gameplay is a slow and ambient enough to write this review, pausing occasionally to tend to their needs. It's the perfect game to have on a second monitor while you work or watch videos. Just like Banished you'll be waiting on your settlers to breed or for immigrants to turn up in the beginning. This I don't mind. What I found quite annoying though is every spring having to remember to pull everyone out of their jobs and allocate them to farming. Because farmers only seem to work for 5 months of the year and do nothing the rest. So I put them to work elsewhere. To the Devs: Idle workers definitely need to become labourers as the micromanaging is annoying! (EDIT This seems to have been fixed in the alpha branch. Farmers now don't hang around in their empty fields and seem to go off and do laborer work. YAY!)

I have to give a LOT of praise for the graphics auto-reduction when it detects a frame rate drop. My PC isn't the strongest these days. As my village grew into a city the game automatically adjusted the visuals to give me more fps. Nice!

The music is good but when it inevitably gets repetitive and you turn it off there's just nothing. No sound effects at all which seems really odd to me. I should be hearing the bustle of a busy village. I should be hearing the sounds of the buildings when the camera is near. I should hear nature! The sound effects are there so it's not a case of them needing to be added, you just only hear them when you click on a building. (As of the alpha branch on 13th March, it's still silent without music)

I've unlocked 7 of 52 achievements in my 8 hours of play time so far. I can see this game going the mile, especially with mods. It just needs to get some polish before being called "done".


My suggestions to the Devs:
--Can the farmers (and maybe every job in general) turn into labourers when they have no work? It's so annoying having to remember to assign and then remove farmers for just 4 months of the year. - FIXED! Woohoo!
--Is there only one growing season in game? I've not unlocked all the crops yet but some crops should grow in winter.
--It would be great if I could assign workers to multiple farms so they move to a different farm when it's time to do work there instead of having to manually take them off a harvested farm and move them.
--Need building sounds near the camera! How about having the sounds of the buildings play when the camera is near them so you'll hear multiple building sounds depending on how close the camera is to them. I should be hearing construction when I'm near it too.
--I've heard nature sounds in the game on certain buildings. Why not use the nature sounds when the camera is above large groups of trees?
--Stone cutter sound at the Smithy has electrical machine noise in background. Don't think they have electricity in this game?
--Water pouring/bucketing has people speaking which may throw non native speakers off a bit. - FIXED!
--No noise to announce a fire... no screaming etc.
--Needs more transition between rain/snow. It stops and starts too quickly at 5x and immediately at 10x. It's a bit jarring. - FIXED!
--On the season indicator bar: Perhaps use sun icon for midsummer, leaf for mid-autumn, snowflake for midwinter and a flower for mid-spring?
--Spelling mistakes in the challenges that appear in the upper right corner that gives you silver when completed - FIXED!
--People have no tools in their hands when the working animation plays. - FIXED!
--To me 1x speed seems more like 0.5x - people walk and act slooooowly - Is this necessary? I'd much prefer faster speeds like 20x. - FIXED! 1x now looks more like normal speed
--Please can we have the option for the game to PAUSE (not just slow to 1x) when a disaster happens? This is the kind of game you leave unattended so this feature would be great. Also the option to pause when a trader arrives. - STILL NEED THIS FIXED
--Cannot de-select build priority button - why? - STILL NEED THIS FIXED
--Please keep the settings window open when hitting apply. It's annoying having to go back in to adjust something. - STILL NEED THIS FIXED
--Camera angle control please. I'd like to angle low while zoomed out or get right in and take a nice screenshot.
--Farmers shouldn't be harvesting from least grown. Should harvest from most grown. - FIXED!
--On Town Details, I'd like to know what percentage of the adult population is educated on the graphs. Maybe this comes later like in Banished with a government building that I've not unlocked yet. - NOW FOUND ON TOWN DETAILS (I)
--Minor gripe but the stone path texture really looks bad compared to everything else. It really sticks out. Maybe the edges are too straight or the pattern repeats to much. - STILL LOOKS UGLY
--Last one: Do houses consume more wood in the winter (as you'd expect) because I seem to be running out of firewood even in summer when you'd expect demand to be lower as they don't need to heat their house, just cook. - CAN NOW SEE PRODUCTION VS CONSUMPTION IN TOWN DETAILS (I)\t\t\t\t\t
