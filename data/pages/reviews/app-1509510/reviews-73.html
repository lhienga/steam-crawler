\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSadly the latest patch came with more issues for me than fixes.

The green seed icons were there when I started the map but then all disappeared so I'm not getting new seeds and the chosen placement of the gathering huts are now useless as there's not special seed finding boost.

The alien ship seems to show up, but I only see the 3 colored lights on the floor and no aircraft, so there's nothing to click on.

I'm not sure if the ferry is supposed to be unlocked at the start of the game of if you need to unlock the dock in the trade system and the ferry is completely gone, because the dock refers to the ferry in the description yet there's no ferry to be found in the buildings. I wasn't getting any flax seeds and they needed clothes so it just became a bother to find out and I gave up.

The boxes of people saying something like 'It's a red packet' (or something, I played it yesterday briefly) wouldn't go away and stayed the entire 20 minutes of playing. I'm a little sad there's so many bugs so I stopped playing and I'll wait for some more patch updates.

What can also be fixed:
Custom inventory settings in the marketplace, I don't want my people wearing certain things and only sell them, but the market can't be adjusted like a storage place can.

If you do use the custom settings in the storage places, they barely work. Beverages is off yet its still storing water. Everything could be off just to test what filters worked and what not yet it would still store certain things like stones and iron and fill it up when I didnt want it to be stored there.

If you remove gravestones it leaves the graves there. Simple hack is to draw a road over it and then delete the road.

When changing from dirt roads to any other road, let's say you work on a grid, you can't cross lines. Let's say all vertical lines are already stone and you want the horizontal dirt roads to also be stone, you can't drag big lines because once they cross over the vertical already stone ones it becomes red making you have to draw each grid line separately.

Sometimes (a lot of times) the March notification doesn't show up, mainly when you're using the information or help tab, meaning it's been multiple times where I didn't notice it was March and missed the harvest.

Why do standard fields have the option for 6 workers when only 2 maybe 3 are needed? I couldn't get enough food for the live of me because I assumed 6 would be needed. It took only until I started reading the info about a simple basic field that's in every game and doesn't need explanation, for me to see that 2 workers is enough.

I get that fertilizing the fields is different than sewing or harvesting, but even after 100% fertilization the 2 fields were still out of sync with the others that weren't fertilized, all having only 2 workers but the latter 2 were done with harvest by the time March was rolling around again. So then you do need more workers, 3 or 4, but this is then not in the information.

The added music in the festive spirits..... it's horrible. All the music is super relaxing and amazing, pretty folky, and then this turns around... had to turn it off.

I love micro management games and I've played so so many of them, so it's easy to point out things that aren't finished.

Besides that, I do love a good learning curve and I'll want to play this game in the future, but it's just a bit too buggy for my liking. Then again, it's early access.\t\t\t\t\t
