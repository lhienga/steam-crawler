\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is definitely worth recommending. It stands out from the competition with several interesting ideas. Very nice graphics. The balance of the game is quite fragile and you have to be careful with it, but it does not cause immediate disasters and in my opinion it is very good. The problems the player must face change as the game develops. I like the building upgrade mechanics. The invention tree is also interesting. The order of technology development has a key impact on the gameplay and you can adjust it to your style.
Typically, games of this type suffer from a lack of documentation and a clear description of the game mechanics, but this game is a notable exception. The help explains a lot, which is a big plus for developers, although unfortunately there are some shortcomings here too. For example, it is difficult for me to assess the impact of increasing the number of employees on the increase in production. This is not a linear relationship. Or how does a trap work? I also miss the ability to move buildings more easily, change the size of farms or pastures, and the ability to build them in a shape other than a rectangle. Despite these shortcomings, the game is very enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
