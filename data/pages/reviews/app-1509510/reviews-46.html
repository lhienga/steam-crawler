\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCompetent little city builder, but it adds nothing new to the genre. Once you get basic needs met and setup the basic supply lines for building materials, there is really nothing else to do besides go after luxury resources and buildings, but there isn't really any incentive to do so. The game could really use a campaign to push you towards going after things like the late game trade only buildings/items or meeting certain health/happiness metrics. As it is, I suspect most people will just keep progressing until the micro-management starts to outweigh their personal taste for such things, then move on. For me, this was around 7-8 hours in.

I also really wish there was a "move building" option. I hate when any city builder doesn't have this, but in this game in particular it is extra bad because every time you build something, you have to wait for it to be built, then remember to come back to it later to set up the number of workers, production item, etc. It makes re-arranging things extremely time consuming and frustrating.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave had Settlement Survival since the first week of Early Access.

Now its gone into live, its time to say I highly recommend the game to anyone who wants a city builder game.

Over the course of the Early Access period there was ups and downs on updates where we loved some and we hated some. But the Dev's listened and what is released today is very enjoyable, and a ton of content for the price point.

Graphics are excellent, though cartoon style.
Music and sounds are great.
Game play is initially easy, but can ramp up in difficulty depending on choices at customization phase before starting your game.
Basic tutorial on game start to follow (plus main menu has extra tutorials) to get you going in the right direction. Simple easy to follow.

This is a city builder game, where you can chill out for 5 minutes and accomplish something or have a binge session of a few hours and create a large township.

Well worth adding to your games library if this genre is something that interests you.\t\t\t\t\t
