\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay I do actually like this game, and would change the rating if some of the issues others have pointed out got fixed in a patch:
1. The absolutely necessary items should be more consistent to find/harvest. Why am I not drowning in wool and feathers with alpacas and sheep? Oh, because apparently I have to hope it falls off of them rather than my people shearing it regularly. K.
2. The amount of population/harvest structures necessary to support a single industrial structure is imbalanced, which worsens the population balance issue that others have mentioned
3. SO many technologies before you can really upgrade or advance anything. It makes the pace of the game feel like molasses. I'm sitting there forever waiting to accumulate enough whatever to research and build whatever to accumulate enough whatever to research and build the next thing to maybe finally get to upgrades for my structures 8 technologies down the line
4. The keybinding interface is awful. I don't understand why there isn't an activate-able overlay menu like in other build games. The number of build categories (like 18 of them, once you figure out there's another menu of aesthetic building stuff) makes it so that the keybinding is awkward and difficult to remember what key goes to what category. The number keys getting taken up by categories you can't remember could go to time clock control (which is imbalanced itself)


Nitpicky gripes but the things drive me nuts but I wouldn't have downrated it for these:
- the sounds for the structures keep playing on a loop, so as I'm looking at the structure, or even better another menu I've opened that overlays stuff, the sound keeps going and going. So as I'm trying to look at stuff, the insistent clanging of my smithy is making me lose my mind
