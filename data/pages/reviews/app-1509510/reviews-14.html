on to the good stuff. as a city builder its fantastic. its not too hard of a learning curve to build a large settlement. graphics look pretty good. theres a challenge but from what ive found its mostly from the rng drops than anything else. its fun to decorate your settlement and bring it to life. seeing it in day/night is also very cool to watch your settlement light up.

overall i quite like the game but the rng factor for basic need stuff that will collapse your settlement if you dont get it early enough is what makes me on the fence about it\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's fine, but tough to recommend with the issues I've encountered.

- After unlocking and using "farms harvest the whole reap at the end", villagers just sort of find other stuff to do and the food just sits there uncollected well into winter while people starve.
- There's no blueprinting available, and if you try to plan ahead and just place things + pause their construction, villagers won't collect the trees in the areas they occupy - so there's really no good way to plan ahead.
- The UI and unlocks are sort of unclear, and it makes it really tough to try and properly prioritise tech unlocks. They aren't really laid out in a way that makes a lot of sense, with unclear unlocks referenced frequently in different tabs. It's very difficult to just figure out what you need to do to get the one thing you're after.

A lot of effort went into this game, and the free updates/DLC, etc, show the developer really does care about the players, so it's tough to give this a thumbs down but it really does impede gameplay quite a bit. I'm not really driven to get much further since I've already encountered these QOL issues, I'm just expecting to find more down the road.

Let's be real, if you're going to model it so closely after another title, certainly try to make sure you've learned from all their mistakes! There's no reason there should be issues from Banished in here, such as citizens being so hilariously unreliable while people starve to death lol.

Best of luck with future developments! Will keep an eye on updates.\t\t\t\t\t
