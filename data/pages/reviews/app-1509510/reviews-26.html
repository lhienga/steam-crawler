\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove this game ! I bought it because I completed Banished and I wanted more from this kind of game. Settlement Survival is a very good game inspired by Banished but it is also different and unique.

What I like the most :
- You can configure your game to be very difficult or very easy, the disasters are sometimes very hard to recover from
- It's a realistic game, for exemple, the output of your farms depends on the number of farmers but also depends on the farmer's equipment, it's health, the distance between houses to the farm etc.
- Lot's of seeds and animals to acquire, you can find some in the wild, win some with your tradings with factions or you have to buy them
- Love the buildings architecture, the way you can customise your city and map
- The fact that the hapiness and health of citizens are as important as the survival of your population with enough food or materials. If you don't take care of them, you won't be able to expend as much as you wish !

The only thing I wish was different in this game is the stress management : even with a low evolution of the number of inhabitants, the stress level is super high as soon as you reach over 400 citizens. It could be great if the bonus of health and happiness were more effective so you could have a stressless population.

All of that said, 200% would highly recommend this game\t\t\t\t\t
