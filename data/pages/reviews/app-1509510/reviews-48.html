\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery promising but ultimately unsatisfying. The rigid, linear tutorial sequence that introduces you to building nets you tons of coins but ends up a boondoggle (I don't want to build a church OR a distillery) which inevitably overcommits all your citizens. Meanwhile winter comes and they freeze to death because the tailor shop is so far down the line.

Also the tech points are essentially useless in early game because each advancement is dependent on something you can't have developed yet. Only after you earn and spend about 10 of them does it start giving you benefits -- but by then you've developed a zillion inefficient units. Upgrading when you're that stretched on resources feels like a slog.

So if you don't do the tutorial it's better, but because trade lies so far down the tech tree good luck getting coins in the early game.

Finally, immigrants = plague is offensive in a 21st century context.

I wanted to like this game so much!\t\t\t\t\t
