Settlement Survival builds on all the things Banished does well, and adds improvements of its own. There are many of the same mechanics and buildings, and you still need to be wary of the weather and plan out how your settlement will expand so you don't kill your villagers. Like Going Medieval, there's a trade system with other near-by settlements, and a research tree that you need to unlock in order to improve or build helpful buildings to keep your villagers healthy and happy.

The only thing I'd love to see improve on here is the map. All the maps seem very similar, and more variation in the terrain would help make the experience even better. Aside from that, this game is pretty much perfect.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is actually the first City builder game that I have played. As a first timer, there's a few things that took me a long time to learn. Mainly, as the games not been out long there's not many guides on the content and it can be frustrating to work out, but still workout-able with some video guides.

I really love playing this game, frankly already put in too many hours. It can be frustrating to start again and again to get the right feeling and it can feel like you have won the game so its less enticing to play.
I have played from easy to extreme and find hard mode to be the most enjoyable, on the large map. Its still challenging but I don't spend as much time micro-managing game starts within the first hour, like I would have to do on extreme. Mainly because you load the game on extreme and your builders break their ankles before you even set a house down for them to build. Or someone freezes to death because the house you built happened to be a second too slow.

I find the beginning game pace to be really slow, like so slow I wish the kids would just grow up and get to work already, and often dreaming of when the refugees will arrive. Once its about Mid level settlement, it starts to speed up and you really have to make sure your on top of your supplies.

I haven't quite understood the trading system in the game yet, mainly because I'm too lazy to send out caravans. Or find myself in dire need of herbs because I researched herb finding and haven't found a herb seed for years and its all going downhill fast but that's also kind of fun.

I would recommend this game! Its a great game to have for the days where you just can't work out what to play.\t\t\t\t\t
