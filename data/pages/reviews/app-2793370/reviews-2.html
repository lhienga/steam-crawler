The visuals and sound design are great, and the premise of being set on a looping Shinkansen train is also pretty good. There's even a bit of a story weaved in as you navigate from carriage to carriage, identifying anomalies as you search for the game's two endings. Speaking of which, the anomalies themselves range from the mundane to the downright zany. They can be as simple as one of your fellow passengers behaving ever so slightly differently, or they can be overtly weird. But a lot of them can also be very subtle and easy to miss - so pay attention to your surroundings and take your time! Overall, a definite recommendation from me.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright I'm gonna be real honest here.
It's not like I don't like playing Chilla's Art games until it feels like the devs are putting in Quantity over Quality in their games. The 3D model has been improving so much in the recent years, that's one quality. Gameplay is also frankly fine with the spot the difference style, but I felt like this one is so empty in terms of storytelling, the ending feels... rushed and I don't feel anything attached to the character in the game. Compare to Parasocial or the kidnap, this one is a big downgrade in terms of the story. Personally, I don't really care about graphics and stuff, I really prefer the game is good in storytelling and gameplay, they should avoid being like the big AAA game devs.

Chilla's Art dev must step up imo, I'd rather buy a $15+ horror game but have long hours and good storytelling rather than purchasing $5 but I feel crappy and then regret buying it. All aside, you still have my support Chilla's Art. Thanks for your hardwork.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t🚅 This is my first experience with a Chilla’s Art game AND observation duty type game! The anomalies were fun to explore and gaslight myself with :'D Naoki and his sis' story were the icing on top. Their interaction was heartwarming and I genuinely wanted to help them <3. The dialogue was well-written and voiced too, some of the conversations were quite interesting, like when chatting with grandpa in the rest area. I wish I learned more about him and his disdain for suits lol.

My only issue was getting a bit nauseous during the first half, but I started to feel better once we hit the green cars, maybe because the cars felt wider?

🐱 Gameplay: 8/10 (a few things i thought were anomalies were most likely bugs, like pixelated shadows and a headrest clipping oddly into the seat)
🔁 Art/Graphics: 7/10
🛤️ Music: 10/10 (an actual bop and great fitting outro!)
✨ Story: 9/10
💙Game time: 2-3 hours
