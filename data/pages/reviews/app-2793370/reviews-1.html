\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is incredibly reminiscent of a game I recently played called "Exit 8". The principle is the exact same: Go through 8 levels and find anomalies. Nonetheless, I gave the game a go and the first few anomalies I found were very reminiscent of Exit 8. This made me incredibly sceptical.

However, as I made my way through the game, I realised that this was not an imitation, but an expansion of a fantastic concept, bringing the idea of anomaly spotting to a fantastic conclusion. With Exit 8, I had a looming sense of unease, but with Shinkansen 0, I thoroughly had an overwhelming feeling of dread. Many of the anomalies are truly terrifying and creepy and, once you reach the green car, you essentially have an entirely new set of anomalies to memorise.
Speaking of the green car, I feel somewhat let down as I luckily was able to spot all the anomalies and got through the green car first try; I still have 19~ green car anomalies to find. This is not a discredit to the game, it was pure luck.

The two endings of the game are also fantastic. Piecing together the story was very rewarding. One element I wish was expanded on was the "don't mention the men in suits" dialogue. I was constantly on the lookout for clues pertaining to it, but it didn't have any pay off, ultimately.

This game is 100% worth the 6 and I highly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
