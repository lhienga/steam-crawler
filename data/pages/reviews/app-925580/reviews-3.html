One of the standout features of Beyond the Baltic Sea is the incredible attention to detail in recreating the diverse environments of the Baltic region. From the lush forests of Finland to the picturesque coastlines of Estonia and Latvia, the visuals are breathtaking. The meticulous craftsmanship shines through in the stunning landmarks, road networks, and the overall ambiance of each location. Whether you're driving through quaint countryside villages or bustling city streets, the scenery feels authentic and transports you to the heart of the Baltics.

The expansion introduces six new countries to explore: Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Finland, parts of Russia, and a small portion of southern Sweden. Each country has its own distinct architecture, cultural nuances, and road networks, providing a truly immersive experience. From the iconic landmarks of Helsinki to the historic old towns of Riga and Vilnius, every destination is lovingly recreated and exudes the unique charm of the Baltic region.

Beyond the Baltic Sea also offers a plethora of new missions and cargo types, ensuring that players have a wide range of engaging challenges to tackle. Whether you're transporting timber through the dense Finnish forests or delivering goods to bustling ports, the missions provide a great balance of difficulty and variety. The expansion breathes new life into Euro Truck Simulator 2, providing hours of captivating gameplay.

With its stunning landscapes, authentic representation of the Baltic region, and engaging gameplay, it is a must-have for both fans of the series and trucking enthusiasts. Whether you're cruising along the Finnish coastline or navigating the busy streets of Tallinn, this expansion offers a captivating journey that will leave you in awe of the majestic beauty of the Baltics.
