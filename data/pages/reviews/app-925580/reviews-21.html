This DLC is simply put expansion of the gameplay-available European-map.

So if you're looking for more content in general, this update provides it.

---
---

However, as a person living in Finland, I would like to deep-dive a little further to the Finnish-content.

To put it bluntly, this expansion barely scratches the surface when it come to Finnish-roads and especially the routes.
So for "Finn-boom-maniacs / Finnomanias", there is simply not enough.

Southern-parts of Finland ( as of writing ) are simply desolate. And there is perhaps a little bit too much artistic and other liberties taken especially when it comes to surrounding areas of Turku
( but this is also primarily due the design-philosophy in this game in which they try to avoid city-centres as much as possible ).

"Turku-Helsinki"-route is simply put "nothing outside Ring III".
This is not to say there wouldn't things next to the road: Very opposite since there is seemingly a lot of handiwork done with the cleared-rocks and such; it looks nice.
But primarily I am referencing to lack of cities, especially "Salo"
( yes, there is a "Salo"-named transit-company; not the city however ).
Alongside the lack harbour-ferry-terminal in Turku this causes some very peculiar detour needed to be taken to rest of the north-side of the Turku-harbour.

Here are some visual-examples:

Helsinki-Harbour - Turku-Harbour according to Google Maps

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1583146407&fileuploadsuccess=1


Helsinki-Turku - route according to Euro Truck Simulator 2

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1583147839

---

In conclusion, we're going to need a dedicated update for Finland alone.
Or we might need to get a completely stand-alone-game for Finnish-trucking (or otherwise road-cruising).

---
---

So good expansion with loads of attention to details;
just don't buy this for Finland alone
( at least if you're into studying road-routes that is ).

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have owned all map DLCs of ETS2 and ATS. So far, Oregon is the best map DLC SCS has ever released on this planet. Seeing Beyond the Baltic Sea was coming after Oregon, i was expecting it to be on par in terms of the level of details. Unfortunately Beyond the Baltic Sea does not have the level of details of Oregon. But it is still a very good map DLC, and i would recommend to anyone.

I have watched some reviews on famous Youtube channels after i bought and played this DLC. Please be warned that some famous Youtube channels (and some of their comments) really underrate the quality and value of this DLC. Because most of the Youtube reviews show you just only a small portion of the map, and not even a drive through, they show you only some spots and say "so plain", "so same same", "Promods did better", etc.

On the opposite, the drive through experience is very good. The DLC is full of new objects everywhere to see. It is not a copy & paste map. It is a handmade job. Even trees and grass are new and carefully placed so that they look like they grow naturally. The forest areas are very dense and denser than the other map DLCs, so dense that they feel naturally grown. I'd say this DLC has the best forest areas. I was never a fan of forests, but in this DLC i enjoy driving through its forests very much.

I have never been to that part of the world in RL, i have only been to Sweden in RL. So, i cannot tell how well the DLC replicates those countries in RL, but i can say that the DLC really feels different than the other DLCs. New buildings, new AI vehicles and introduction of passport control stations add up to the new feeling.

CONCLUSION:

Although it's not on par with the Oregon DLC, but it's still a very good map DLC, not copy & paste, different look and feel from the other map DLCs, best forests. So i see no reason why you should not buy this DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
