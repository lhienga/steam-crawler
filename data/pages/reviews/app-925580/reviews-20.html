\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe three major characteristics of the Baltic region:

1. Countryside: flat and green
Not the majestic scenery of Italy or Norway or the Alpes, but a more modest countryside with fields and forests and rivers.

2. Road network: few divided highways
Huge difference from Italy, France and the base map, which have an extensive network of divided highways. In this region, even routes between major cities are often on undivided highways.

3. Architecture: post-Soviet
Cities are where this region is at its best, in my opinion. Trams and flower decorations, but mostly the post-Soviet apartment buildings make some cities really stand out.

(The second and third point don't really apply to Finland, which feels more like Sweden than like a part of the Baltics/Russia region.)

Overall, a nice DLC with an area that feels different from earlier expansions.
