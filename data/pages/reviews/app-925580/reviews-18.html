\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this DLC is great, but not excellent/surprising.

The map looks great, but the road network is kind of "meh".
There are just not that many breath-taking bridges and tunnels.
It does not have highways with beautiful scenery like in the Scandinavia and Italy DLC.
It could be because I don't like two-lane road and prefer highways. It could be just what countries of the Baltic Seas are. But I just feel it's not as exciting or interesting to drive to as compared to other DLCs.

Helsinki and Daugavpils can be improved by completing the circle around it, it feels kind of dumb to drive around the city to get to load/unload the trailer.
In some cities like Riga, Panevezysn, and Vilnius, etc. many companies are too far away, which makes taking jobs from them quite time-consuming.

However, Saint Petersburg alone is definitely a 10/10.
It shows us what a big city should looks like in ETS2. It is also made with great details and it's really enjoyable to drive to it. I hope when SCS remake London, they will let the same guy who build St. Petersburg work on it.

I would still recommend you get the DLC, maybe when it is around -33% off or more then it will be worth getting.
