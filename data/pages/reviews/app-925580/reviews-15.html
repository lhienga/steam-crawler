\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am torn, wish there would be an option to choose something between "recommend" & "not recommend".

My fellow countrymen released latest DLC a year ago and I only got to it now, I am always playing for a month, then stop, then come back and so on... I did couple first jobs in Estonia, Lithunia, Latvia and I cannot say I'd like it very much.

Pros:
- new companies, new trailer designs
- architecture - somebody really spent a lot of time making this look genuine
- random events (crashes, police checks on the road)

And that's about it... I don't see anything else positive about this DLC. The trend of introducing lot of detours, huge intersections you have to cross 3-4 times to "uncover" them completely for your GPS remained but what is also new here is almost every rest stop or petrol station in OFF the road so you have to stop, go there, mark it yellow, get back to road and 30 seconds in you repeat this.

Driving here is like "intersection, roundabout, village, intersection, village, intersection, roundabout and town" - yes, it looks nice and all but all that is at expense of continuity. I drove from Poland to Estonia and I was so tired by the time I finally got there. I was circling roundabouts, going to petrol stations and rest stops OFF the main road to mark it for GPS progress and this just wears you off. I don't like it. I prefer simple Poland or Hungary where you just drive 50-100 km on boring road going 70 km/h but at least you don't have to pull over at every mark on the road.

I am afraid what's to come with new expansion that's about to come...\t\t\t\t\t

This was the first DLC that i buy. I don't have any high expectations before. I thought an extra map DLC will only extend the map size, add some city landmarks, and that's it. Then i started a trip from St. Petersburg all the way down to Poland. This may sound overreacted, but oh boy that's one of the best trip i've ever had in ETS2. The map feels so freshly different and even better than the base game. It creates not just an extended map, but a completely new different vibe, with a bunch of detailed stuff i never saw before. SCS Software is really dedicated by creating a lot of these, keep up the hard work.
