\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best map DLCs out there, imo. Worth every cent, as I was a bit sceptical concerning the price. No need to wait for a sale. The landscape and environment is very detailed, the towns and cities are huge - not really comparable to the base game and the base nations. Even smaller cities seem to have more companies than the bigger Western European ones. Trucking feels a bit like in Scandinavia. Lots of single lane roads, but with lesser changing speed limits. The highways are up-to-date. Don't know if it's also part of the DLC, but it looks to me that SCS even added a few more standard cargo jobs.

10/10, would truck through the Baltics again.\t\t\t\t\t
