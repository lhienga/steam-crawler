\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely underwhelming. You know something is wrong when you're driving in the new areas and you can't even distinguish them from base game Germany. The cities are very small and have a lot of repeating assets. The store page mentions how they have brand new unique 3D-assets but I can't see any tangible reflection of this in the environment. It almost feels like they've tried to make a completely new area with vanilla Germany assets.

To be fair, many landmarks and smaller details are beautiful and well integrated. Especially Russia was a positive experience since the area is more distinctive and has character. Some of the Latvian and Lithuanian cities are quite good and they actually feel like urban areas. Sadly, this isn't enough to make the overall environments of the DLC stand out.

Especially as a Fin I found Finland to be very disappointing. Weirdly enough, driving on some of the Russian country roads felt more like Finland than Finland itself. Finland's cities are abysmal, tiny and contain minimal variety making them feel more like tiny towns. The country roads fail to encapsulate the feeling of Finland; thick forests and winding roads broken by sudden open fields, giving a weird and almost claustrophobic feel.

I thought SCS had an upwards trend in terms of quality. Newer DLCs like Vive la France, Scandinavia or Italy were amazing and had a lot of character in the respective areas. Beyond the Baltic Sea is quite disappointing and almost disgusting since mods that add these areas have existed for years and they do a much better job. Sadly the price to content ratio in this DLC is a bit off. You're almost better off getting Promod's map mod.\t\t\t\t\t
