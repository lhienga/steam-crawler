It's really exciting to drive around places you've been. HOWEVER...

Also I've noticed that in Baltic States you can see replicas of such cars as LADA, when in real life you don't see them at all. In best case scenario maybe 1%. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tSCS Software really outdid themselves with this one. There's a certain point, if you drive northeast of Olsztyn up towards Kaliningrad, where it suddenly stops looking like a video game and starts looking exactly like it did heading out to the summer cottage when I was a kid. The same flowers and vegetation along the country roads, the same golden rapeseed fields, birch trees swaying in the breeze... It's just as I remember it.

The level of detail in this extension is amazing. Everything looks hand-made, every intersection is custom made, the houses you drive by have little greenhouses in their back yards. Not to mention the excitement of driving through Russia and exploring St Petersburg... and the bright Finnish summer nights when the sun barely sets below the horizon makes it an absolute delight to drive.

This is absolutely my favorite extension - the Italy extension coming in as a close second.\t\t\t\t\t
