Their doesn't seem to be a freeway in place between cities so its mostly single lane roads your driving on. The scenery is nice. The new job sites look very cool. My issue is with the cities and the lack of side roads. Their are very few side roads in the 5 cities I visited. So towns feel very cramped. Sure it all looks great but it feels as though your driving down an alleyway. I think because they had so many cities that they cut some corners on roads inside the cities. I still think its worth buying. I do think in the future perhaps they might make smaller maps and make the road system inside the cities more open. So tons of roads and cities to visit.
