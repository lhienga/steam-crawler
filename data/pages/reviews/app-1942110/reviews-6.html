I can only imagine that the core loop is the way it is to hide the fact that there is no game. I'm so disappointed in this game that it nearly seams like fraud. There you have it. Buy this at your own risk. You have been warned.
Oh yea, someone said that the performance and graphics are bad. Totally agree. Rocking a 4080 and HP Reverb G2 v2. Horrible. Literally nothing about this game I would consider good at this point. The idea is pretty cool and that is it.

Game looks great. Gameplay not so much.

--The bad--
You get a few minutes to explore before being hunted by some night creatures. There's no tutorial so you have to figure out everything on your own? After 30 minutes of play I had to go on youtube to figure out how to survive a night. After watching those videos I realized there's not much to do in the game. Trailer doesn't show anything either. Like I said, it looks great. Opening coconuts is satisfying. That's about it.

I wouldn't recommend this game as-is but it's $10 so if you're curious just try it. Maybe I'll play again another day but it's just not that fun to me to go back right now.
