\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is an amazing immersion into the world of early access. After I spent some time with it, I was struck by its appeal. The depth of the work and priority systems is simply mesmerizing, and the tutorial, though it requires some effort, turns out to be extremely important for full immersion into the game world and avoiding disappointments. But the game needs to be given time to reveal all its complexities - this is a real pleasure for those who love details. This is not just another civilization simulator - it cleverly adds new mechanics and offers interesting twists, such as generational knowledge and other unique elements. In general, this game promises to be exciting, and I look forward to where it will lead us.

The drawback can be called a slightly cumbersome interface, which can cause initial inconvenience. But over time I got used to it, and it no longer prevents me from enjoying the game. Perhaps, as the game evolves, the interface will become more convenient, which will make the gameplay even more enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
