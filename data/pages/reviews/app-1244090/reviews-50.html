\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is not a masterpiece.

The writing is bland and sounds like a poorly written YA novel; as soon as the characters started talking, I regretted my purchase. Combat is one-note and tedious (granted I only got a couple hours in, but from what I've seen elsewhere it doesn't gain depth). The "puzzles" are minor roadblocks that aren't even close to mentally stimulating. Exploration in maps may as well be linear, with side paths leading to a chest with underwhelming loot.

Visually it's nice, but that's about the only thing that I liked. I mainly picked this up because I was nostalgic for Golden Sun, and wanted to support an indie dev making what looked like a nice homage to old JRPGs. That was a mistake.\t\t\t\t\t
