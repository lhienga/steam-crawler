All I can say is the game is good enough to own on every platform I own which is why i decided to support the Developers and buy it on steam for full price and also order the physical copy for switch from playasia.

It's been a really long time since i enjoyed playing an old school style RPG this much and It will definitely hold a special place in my heart just like Chrono Trigger and Final fantasy 1-6. The story is incredible and very heart warming in many areas, Game play is very reminiscent of the aforementioned games but has great innovations that make it more involved and keeps you engaged as the player to try new strategies.

Visuals are also stunning and use very modern rendering techniques to add more detail and has some of the best looking pixelated water i have ever seen in a game to date.

Lots of side quests to keep you busy and the wheels mini game became my new addiction for my entire first play through. it Definitely is this games triple triad.

overall a very well crafted game and you can tell the Developers put a ton of love into this project just as they did with the messenger before it, which is also another favourite of mine.
