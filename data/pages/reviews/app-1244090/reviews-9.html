\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSea of Stars is a great blast-from-the-past that also innovates on the 80s/90s JRPG formula to bring it into the modern day in way that I don't think any other game has done. It really feels like what you would get if you sent a modern computer back to the 90s and told them to make a JRPG with it.

The art is absolutely beautiful. The entire game looks incredible. It's some of the best pixel art I've ever seen in a game. The sounds and music are also great and really put you in the mood of the game.

The combat system is simple and fun. It all feels very well-polished. Navigating around the world is also a joy, with lots of rewards for exploring.

The story is a bit generic at times, but there's a lot of emotional moments and love put into the characters that just makes it work. There's also a lot of parts of the story that are very creative that overshadow the "generic" parts.

Overall Score: 9/10

Positive points
- Gameplay and combat
- Art
- Music and sounds
- Characters and emotional moments

Negative points
- Story somewhat generic at times
