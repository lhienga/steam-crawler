\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow, I did not expect to like this as much as I did. The world-building in this game is absolutely incredible. The animations and graphics are great too, and the music is for sure the cherry on top. I actually really liked the combat as well, it was engaging and I personally never got sick of it. And okay, sure, the two protagonists are rather bland and forgettable, but who cares? We all know Garl is the main character anyways.

Chrono Trigger was one of my favorite games when I was younger. I won't compare the two since they are obviously very different games, but I had to mention it because of the way Sea of Stars made me feel like a little kid again. You can definitely feel the inspiration in this one. It's such a wonderful thing to still be able to come across games that are able to pull off that kinda magic like this one does :')
