Story: 9.5/10
(Story is wonderful that it has so much lore for every character that there are some that are still very mysterious.
I even almost "cried".)
(-0.5 because i want MORE)
Gameplay: 9.5/10
(Interesting and Cool mechanics that makes the game very unique and fun.)
(-0.5 because, I do not know, I feel that something is missing. Maybe, I want more varieties of attacks i guess, but i can't complain about it because it is enough for what is given.)

(Clean and Wonderful art, love it. Animations are clean and cool.)
(Why missing 0.3 because, I would like to see a bit more cut-scenes in certain parts of the game.)
Duration: 9/10
(A almost perfect duration but i was hoping for more, just because i enjoyed it so much.)
(-1 because I want MORE)
Difficulty: 10/10
(The difficulty is very balanced that if you lose, it is indeed your mistake. Otherwise, unlucky.
I love the addition of Relics to make the game more enjoyable by helping people who is struggling or wanting harder challenge.)
Music: 10/10
(Love everything that the game offered)
