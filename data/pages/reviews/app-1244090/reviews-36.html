\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll of the reviews, both positive and negative, are essentially correct here:
- it's a beautiful game
- a lot of the characters are bland, and there's a lot of Chrono Trigger DNA in here
- it's pretty easy, combat-wise, especially once you figure out what the overpowered strategies are
- You have to get 100% item collection to get the hidden ending...like a lot of retro and retro-inspired games
- the level design and traversal mechanics are great.
- the "last boss" is a wild genre shift for no reason whatsoever

Frankly, I got my money's worth. If you kinda want a lighter-hearted Chrono Trigger with not as much to do/explore, but also not as much time commitment, this is probably right up your alley.\t\t\t\t\t
