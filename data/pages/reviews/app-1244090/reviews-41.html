\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHow to begin ! This game is truly very beautiful and touching, I was always invested in discovering more, finding secrets, advancing in the story and adventure with these colourful characters. But, I have to admit , I found myself often times just stopping there, listening to the music, admiring the gorgeous retro-scenery, thinking back to me playing Golden Sun and Dragon Quest games ... Its truly a Gorgeous, vivid, world, in which I found myself wanting to find more things, all the areas are distinct and detailed, yet the whole lot makes for a cohesive world in which I wanted to be immersed, scored by one of my favorite OST's ever now.

Gameplay-Wise I love the fluidity of transitioning between exploring the different little contained zones and their puzzles into these fights which are very involved for a "turn-based" game. The lock system is great, the bonuses you get from proper input timing rewarding but you're not mad if you miss a few, its a very simple, effective and brilliant system which creates challenging fights where I can still enjoy the artwork of characters, ennemies, animations, abilities, and ultimates. Fights which actually showcase the character's abilities and personality very well through combination attacks.

But, speaking of characters, this may well be where my only gripe with this game truly stands. I think the story fails to flesh out those characters even more than I wanted them to be. A few scenes before the true ending fight ( which I have not completed yet mind you !) do wrap up everything very nicely in a satisfying bow, but I will admit, I would hope some points of the story were more detailed and took the time to be important moments. I feel as though the weight of this very interesting world and story would have been more impactful if the story beats hit even harder. Some do ! of course, and very deeply so, but I think the two main characters kind of go along in the story with a motivation, but they are not as fleshed out as the other characters.. And I wish they had more detailed and deep relationships with certain characters in the story which may have made some events even more impactful. Although I have to say that the other characters accompanying the two main characters are all truly lovely characters which I grew to love very easily and seamlessly though the story and their interactions. I guess I'm just sad the story didn't let me grow too close and feel invested enough in the main quest of the two protagonists as it didn't flesh out the story beats relating to them enough for me to feel fully invested, even though I wanted to be.

Regardless, If you enjoy games with retro graphics, a banger soundtrack and gameplay, and a very neat story of adventure with lively characters, who you want to see grow and interact with the truly gorgeous world that surrounds them and that go on a quest to save said world... Then only the adventure awaits, and I could not recommend this game enough , which I am truly glad I played, as it is one of my best gaming experiences ever.

( Garl is the best character in any video game ever, if anyone dares to lay a hand on him I will defend him for ever and ever xD he is the purest thing to exists )

Thank you for an amazing game Sabotage Studios, Hyped to see what you release next that will probably reference in some way this amazing game haha <3\t\t\t\t\t
