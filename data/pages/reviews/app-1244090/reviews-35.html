There's no need to grind for anything in this game - you can 100% the game without ever needing to engage in combat encounters that aren't related to your progress though the story. It's worth it to do all the side content (collectables, optional boss fights) to unlock the true ending. Puzzles are pretty simplistic and don't require anyone to really look up a guide IMO.

The story has some good plot twists that kept me interested. The side content's a tad boring, which is probably my only real complaint.

Overall, would recommend. Kept me engaged for 40+ hours.\t\t\t\t\t
