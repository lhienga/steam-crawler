The map guiding design is very confusing and there is no journal log or whatsoever to give players a clue for the next step at some point, especially for casual players who play from time to time.

6/10

The writing is amazing, the characters are both relatable and inspiring (one day I hope to be half as wholesome as Garl is!), the music is fantastic, and the combat gives me massive Chrono Trigger vibes, which is great!

I don't cry easy, but my eyes filled with tears several times during the time I've played it so far, and I'm not even finished with it yet! Even more importantly those tears have been tears of joy rather than sadness, this game is such an amazing, soothing experience somehow and I will love it forever.
