\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWholesome, joyful, sad and beautiful. Love it so much.

Edit after completing the game at 100%: Sea of Stars is a beautiful pixelated adventure full of humor and love that actually made me tear up a few times.

I did find the combat too easy (I only ever lost when not paying attention), and some mechanics can be exploited to make any fight a joke. I'm usually the kind of player who will discard a game that is not challenging enough, but not here.
The music, the characters, the sound effects, the pixel art... Everything is so enjoyable. Easy combat does not hinder the game, especially because it offers options to render it harder as you progress, though I would have liked a general difficulty option nonetheless.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game draws heavily on the 90's Squaresoft games that I played as a child. The music is even done by the guy that did Chrono Trigger! The combat is a shining aspect, and super fun to switch characters in and out to try different combos. The story is pretty great, but the characters within the story make it shine. The game is somewhat linear at first, but eventually opens itself up to exploration with lots of little things to find and enjoy. Just a solid game with a great ending and plenty of nostalgia to make it worth buying and playing, right now. If you liked Chrono Trigger, Final Fantasy VI, or any of those 90's Squaresoft games, give this one a try.\t\t\t\t\t
