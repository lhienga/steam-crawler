\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best JRPG I ever played

What I loved :
*Music, sfx, graphics, visuals, maps teleported my Fran to my childhood while entrataining my adult self,

*Combat is really fun, a little repetitive later on but, still fun!

The meh :

*Story : we have a lot of interesting plot twists, i even dropped some tears, but nothing new and lots of plot holes (hated the ending, felt so.... "that's it?"

Things I didn't love :

* level up system,, just put a couple of stats point and that's it, no talent, no skill point, no new skills to gather around or buy, nothing

* character development : they felt to me a little generic, didn't connect with them at all.. And I must say I completely hate one of the characters (GARL) he just talks toooo much

Overall : a solid 8.5/10 this gamea best value is :combat, music, world building, maps and tons of fun gameplay and simple yet cool puzzles\t\t\t\t\t
