Sea of Stars - the most disappointing RPG of the year for many JRPG fans.

Is the game bad? Absolutely not, the game is great. But go in with tempered expectations.
This isn't Chrono Trigger 2, do not expect quality on the level of it.
For an indie team of only 23 full time staff, Sea of Stars is an amazing game.

The Good:
The pixel art is gorgeous, combat is fun, boss/enemy design is pretty good, and Yasunori Mitsuda composing some tracks.

The Meh:
Characters and story are unnoteworthy.

Solid 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSea of Stars is my game of the year of 2023.
Some things could have been better, but overall I love this game.
Having played The Messenger before, increased my enjoyment.
The game is great on its own, but where it really shines are the connections to The Messenger :
  • The familiar areas with remixed soundtrack
  • The lore connections
I recently completed every achievement and it took me 56 hours.
Pro's :
  • Soundtrack
  • Art style
  • Customizable difficulty using the relics
  • Backtracking for items is made easier with an upgrade that tells you where there are chests remaining and which area is
    done
  • Fishing minigame
  • True ending
  • New game plus (not required for true ending)
Cons
  • combat is fun, but can get repetitive at the end
  • Playtime (20-30 hours for first ending, 40-45 for true ending depending if you look for collectables)
