Overall – Sea of Stars is great throwback to some of the greatest RPGs of all time. Sea of Stars plays sort of like an amalgamation of Breath of Fire and Super Mario RPG and will flood most players with nostalgia from the days of amazing SNES RPGs. Starting from the music, it’s classic, it could slot in right next to games like Chrono Trigger and Secret of Mana, the soundtrack feels like it was created as a love letter to those games. The combat initially feels a little shallow but does start to add more dynamic portions as you unlock combos and ultimate abilities. The storyline starts a bit slow but ends up being really well done, I was actually surprised with how much I enjoyed it. Sea of Stars is a worthy purchase of any fan of old school SNES and PS1 Jrpgs.
