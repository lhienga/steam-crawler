\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShort answer this game is good and I recommend it at full price if you are a messenger fan who likes Chrono Trigger style RPGs otherwise I would wait for a sale even if its just a small one.

Long answer this game is all over the place.

The Story:
This is by far the highlight of the game as the main story is engaging even if it takes a second to get going and makes you want to keep going and find out whats going to happen next with a lot of twists and turns along the way.

The Characters:
This is a mixed bag. Some characters feel flat and one note such as Garl while others seem to have bipolar such as Zale, Valere and Resh'an. Serai and Aephorul are the standouts having really cool backgrounds and more to them than is immediately shown on the surface. Overall I really enjoy the writing for most characters but here and there the dialogue can get bland and repetitive in certain areas.

Subsection Garl:
Garl oh my god I hate Garl. I know he is a main character and he is supposed to be a lovable character who does no wrong. But that man is nothing but annoying and throws off the entire feeling of the adventure and he is also the main reason I feel that Zale and Valere are so bipolar. It feels like he acts just like he is still an 8 year old throughout the entire game and interjects to take the lead in most conversations when it really doesn't feel like its his place. His entire character is part of the reason I despise some of this games scenes so much as he acts like he is the centre of attention and everyone makes room for him to have the spotlight at any opportunity possible.

Overworld Gameplay:
This games extent for puzzles and traversal is very akin to zelda as most puzzles are solved by moving blocks or finding a chest with a key. This isn't an issue as I enjoy zelda puzzles but sometimes the constant climbing of walls can get tiring sometimes. Also just like zelda there is a very tedious water dungeon where you need to mess with the water levels. Overall traversal and puzzles don't usually take a lot of thought but are engaging enough and encourage exploration to a point that makes the world fun to run around in.

Battles:
Okay this battle system has a lot of highs and lows. First of all I enjoy the creativeness of the battle system overall as it allows for a lot of dynamic engagements and methods to attack your enemies. The issue I found was that once the battle system was truly understood it is insanely easy to exploit and make it so its almost impossible to die and you can break every lock a boss throws at you making them easy pickings. Also with the limited inputs for action commands and defence easy battles can feel very bland as you just press A for 10 seconds as everyone does their flashy animations. There is a lot of fun to be had in this battle system and it can be assisted by its simplicity but its way to easy to find a comfy combo of people to use for every battle and only swap characters for locks and heals as some characters lack powerful attacks. Overall its engaging enough to keep you going but there is definitely room for improvement.

The Messenger:
This game being a prequel the a game I love means I need to mention this game relation to it. Overall this game doesn't show references in your face (other than mesa island) nor does it overly hint to the future events. However, it provides a nice amount of lore for things that end up happening in the future and how they came to be in a way that makes it all worth it to me.

Music:
This games soundtrack is great. There are so many songs that get stuck in my head and convey all sorts of emotions. Some things here and there are lacking such as some things getting repetitive in areas you have to visit repeatedly but overall a great listen and I highly recommend.

Art/Visuals:
This game is stunning, and some of the best pixel art and sprite work I have seen in a game in a very long time with amazing shading and love behind it. I will note how some animations feel like they are missing a few frames and in some parts they lack any form of animation. But overall this game a treat to look at from beginning to end.

Overall Rating:
I really loved this game and I think you will too if anything positive I mentioned above as the positives far outweigh the negatives in my opinion. But I could also see someone not having a great time with this game so I would say if you are not sure I would recommend a sale as the price is rather high for an indie game even if I believe it is completely worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
