\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSea of stars is a great new game of Sabotage Studio. I saw a video of it before it released and it got my attention right away. Once i could play it finally for myself i was really impressed with how fluid the gameplay is and how great the timed attacks and blocks are. The story is in my eyes at least really good, only downside are the translations which seem in conversations sometimes really rough but you can for sure with a bit of creativity understand everything. Battle mechanics and level up mechanics are new in a own way but also are mixed together by some features i loved in other games already.
Now the short version:
pros:
-Nice Battle mechanics which are easy to learn but hard to master
-You are very limited with capacity for heal items but you can easy cook new ones
-Even when some say that at parts it seems like they are emotionless. I think they did a nice job for how compact they made the story
-Level up system gives you also a bit of own deciding how to form your characters
-Nice hidden secrets
-The MP system is really interesting and limits you without being too punishing
-You can adjust difficulty with quite some relics in both ways harder or easier.

Cons:
-The translation of conversations is at times not entirely well. At times the grammar is poor or some words seem off.
-The wheels game i understand that maybe some people might not enjoy too much, for me it was fine.\t\t\t\t\t
