\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very intimate and beautiful experience that outshines the game's flaws. Sabotage studio continues to impress with their retro style game design, following The Messenger. As a gamer that enjoyed past titles that are now considered retro, I fully believe they managed to capture the same charm to bring out the nostalgic notes in me.

The positives:
- Unreal visual style, unlike anything I've seen for a pixel art game.
- The amount of detail you notice when paying attention proves how much developers loved making the game.
- Music is complimentory to the visuals and very distinct from one another.
- Story has some twists to keep it spicy
- The game is running super efficiently on Steam Deck. Barely lost any battery when playing. Major plus from my side.

The negatives:
- Character development is tricky. It's there, but it might not make the most sense.
- Combat can get repetitive after a while

I highly recommend trying this game out. It's very special in my opinion and absolutely deserving of the awards it will get this year.\t\t\t\t\t
