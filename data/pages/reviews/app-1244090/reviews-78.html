\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a difficult one. I almost dropped my playthrough and was not going to recommend the game, however now that I have finished it, I'm glad I did and had an overall positive experience. Let's start with the cons and why I almost abandon it:

- The first part of the story (this was ~10-12 hours for me) were a drag and boring, nothing interesting happens and the events of the main quest are super cliche. I understand that the world and the plot were being introduced, but you can do that and still be interesting to progress the story.
- The two main characters have really poor writing (and this stays throughout the game, not only the initial part), they add nothing to the story and simply follow the plot along, replace them with a couple of dummies and in the end I would fell exactly the same way about the game.
- The combat system is interesting, but until you have a full party and more options in your skill set it is frustrating and repetitive.
- Overall I found the game to be easy and I did not use any of the relics to make it even easier.

Now the pros and why despite all this I still recommend the game:

- The remaining characters (apart from the main two) are actually very interesting and contribute a lot to the story (maybe not so much the last one) and it is fun to find out more about them.
- Story does pick up after that initial drag, won't expect a masterpiece of a storytelling but it does get good and I ended up exciting to find out what happens next.
- The combat system....again. As I mentioned above once you have everything at your disposal it is enjoyable to enter combats and try the different combinations available.
- The dungeon maps, they're fun to traverse with simple puzzles, beautiful pixel art (this applies to every map in the game) to admire and a lot of verticality that explores every corner of the map itself so you don't end up simply travelling in 4 directions.
- Probably the best thing in the game for me....the music, such a masterpiece, it creates a tone for the game and the different maps and it fits perfectly well in most of the situations.

My conclusion is that the game is definitely flawed and there is a lot of room for improvement, especially in the writing sections, but for me the game brought that felling and nostalgia from playing pixel (J)RPGs from more than 20 years ago, so in that end I call it a success. I now hope the studio carry on creating games like this and improve because I for sure would like to see what they can do next.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cried three times while playing this. The story is absolutely touching, and will leave you wanting more.

The timed-action combat is a fun approach - hit the action button in time with your animations, and you deal more damage or take less damage. It's nice to have an aspect to the combat that requires attention, rather than queuing up actions and waiting for things to happen. The exploration system is also great - you have a surprising amount of freedom when moving, despite being a 2D game.

Cannot recommend this game enough. I am forever a Child of the Solstice. :3\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHate to say it but this game is just boring. I thought I'd grab this as it looked like a chill steam deck game to chip away at during evenings, but I've put it down and don't really feel the urge to pick it back up again. I have never played Chrono Trigger, FF or anything like that so take what I say with a pinch of salt.

The art style, animations and music all blend together well, creating a pretty package but for me it just falls apart the longer it goes. I've played for 8 hours and feel like I've given it a decent go, with the last 3 feeling quite forced, waiting for a 'light-bulb' moment that never really came. It was once I began actively sprinting past combat encounters that I realised I was bored - I packed this game away shortly after.

I do however see the appeal for those more seasoned JRPG enjoyers, and appreciate the work that's clearly gone into the creation of this game, but for me personally I found the experience pretty middling at best.\t\t\t\t\t
