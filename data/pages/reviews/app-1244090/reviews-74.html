\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10/10. Easily one of, if not the best game of 2023.
As others have said, it's very Chrono Trigger inspired, though it does bring new elements to Chrono Trigger's combat in the vein of the button commands from the Mario RPGs, and a break system similar to that found in Octopath Traveler.
The story's great. It's a big world filled with colorful characters and funny dialogue.
The OST is also, as expected, fantastic. It starts out with a banger right out the bat with Mountain Trail, which gave me major Pokemon Mystery Dungeon vibes. And it just gets better from there with Battle On, Dance Of 1,000 Suns, and Volcanic Pursuit. They even got Yasunori Mitsuda, known for Chrono Trigger and the Xeno series, to do a handful of songs.
My only complaint is that there isn't more, but apparently there's DLC on the way, so even that'll be fixed soon enough.
Sabotage is currently 2 for 2 with their games, and I hope that continues!\t\t\t\t\t
