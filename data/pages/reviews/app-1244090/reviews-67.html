I never felt like I was out of my depth or needed to grind like most other RPG's, and with the in-game relics you can tailor your experience to fit. Want an easier time playing, with more forgiving with the timed blocks? Stick on the relics and your fine! Want to get used to the timing with the attack/blocks? There is a relic for that to help you train up.

The story is just a delight to play through. The NPC's are richly designed and have some amusing lines.

Honestly...my expectations were smashed, and I can't recommend enough that you should play this game!\t\t\t\t\t
