\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is everything that I wanted from a turn-based RPG inspired by classics. If you enjoyed playing Chrono Trigger, you most likely will enjoy this game as well.

- Graphics 10/10: Impeccable art direction, best pixel art I've seen in a long time. The 3D dynamic lighting gives a modern touch well appreciated.
- Music 10/10: Stellar sound track, always well fitting with the situation. I love how the pirate band will play any of the song with their respective instrument.
- Story 8/10: Overall very good story. Garl MVP, that's all I have to say.
- Exploration 9/10: Traversal is fun and the level design is really well done. Most of the puzzles are a bit too simplistic for my taste but still nice to go through. Lots of areas to explore and secrets to find.
- Combat 7/10: Some good ideas. The timing bonuses make to combat more dynamic. The lock system is interesting but it gets a bit stale after a while.\t\t\t\t\t
