-Level design is incredibly bland compared to Pokemon's complex overworld and dungeons
-Sticker system is way too customizable to where it makes monsters feel less unique and learning new moves is not exciting or rewarding
-WAY too many filler battles, even compared to Pokemon and the filler battles take way too long
-"Ranger Captains" are just regular overworld trainers and don't come close to the feeling of fighting a gym leader
-Team Real Estate is not an interesting villain and their "hideouts" consist of walking in, having a single battle, then leaving
-No "legendary" monsters which are a huge highlight of Pokemon
-Dialogue is very boring, just a lot of nice positive people giving you constant positive affirmation
