\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cannot recommend this game enough. From the soundtrack and artwork, to its battle system and abilities, this game exceeds a rating of 10/10. I bought this game on a whim after seeing someone play it for a bit and I don't regret it, not at all. This game is an absolute joy to play.

First of all: the soundtrack. The game really lives up to its name and theme by having some of the catchiest songs as its soundtrack. I even added them to my playlist and listen to them regularly. I don't think it's possible to play through the game without singing along to a song at least one.

I loveeee the art direction in this game as well. Not gonna lie, the art style of the game is what enticed me to get it in the first place. It blends multiple different styles; pixel and 3D in the overworld, Illustrations for the characters in the game, and a unique art style and design for every boss battle, which pushes the unsettling feeling of the encounter even further. I have goosebumps every time I see a new boss. The boss battles really are one of my favourite parts of the game, especially since the best song in the soundtrack plays during said battles. And also, did you know that every fusion in the game, which can happen between any two beasts from a roster of 100+, is unique? That's just crazy.

The battle system is super fun as well. Every beast element type interacts differently from one another, not in the way it's usually portrayed (ie: strength vs weakness) and instead focuses on status effects brought upon by those interactions. The movesets of the beasts are highly customizable as well, enabling interesting builds, gimmicky moves, and personalized combat. The levelling system is a unique one. Only the characters level up while beasts gather stars to evolve into more powerful forms, which I find beats the grindiness of most monster-collector games.

Although there are a few annoyances in the game (like the physics of some puzzles), I still think that this game is one that can't be missed because there really isn't anything to dislike about it. It's just really fun.

Just buy the damn game. You won't regret it\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally good game, one of my favorites ever played. Worth every penny. To sum up:

- The game is based on a Pokmon-alike combat system with unique (and cool) features. Most of the battles are 2v1 or 2v2. You can freely change your monsters' movesets, and there are many bosses, each with their own strategy to beat you.
- Despite having a main quest, the game offers a bunch of primary and secondary quests that are enjoyable.
- You are free to explore the map on your own and decide which objectives you want to focus on. You will need to unlock some skills to reach certain parts of the map.
- Achievements are doable (I mean, they don't require insane feats or following a guide). Two of them could be considered missable, but the odds of this happening are very low. In case it happens, you can obtain them quickly in another save file.
- I don't usually care about the OST of games as long as it is decent, but the one in this game requires special mention. The music is so nice, especially the parts where the female voice singer is involved. Such a sweet voice.
- The main story gameplay lasts 20 hours, and if you decide to do the post-game part, it will increase up to 40-50 hours. The post-game is fun at first, but it can become somewhat repetitive.
- Developers are still updating the game. A DLC has been announced, and multiplayer is coming.
- In case you care, it's a perfect game for SteamDeck.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing the game for 30 hours in about 3 days, I can wholeheartedly recommend this game.

The game itself plays a lot like Pokemon, though it has a distinct charm and feel that makes it so much more enjoyable to me. Being able to customize movesets to the level you can in this game certainly helps the depth of the gameplay, but on top of that the level of optimization, exploration, and overall uniqueness of each beast species being able to be any element is incredibly smart.

On top of this, the game has a lovely cast of characters, enjoyable bosses, amazing references, a fantastic soundtrack, and overall just a really incredible feel to play.

If you are a fan of turn based RPG's, I STRONGLY suggest this one. Genuinely one of the best turn based games I've played. The only real downside I'd have to say is that the postgame, at time of post, is sort of grindy. Outside of that, everything about this game is a 10/10.\t\t\t\t\t
