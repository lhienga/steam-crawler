\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is amazing! This is a very unique monster capturing, turn based combat, and visually pleasing game on pc in awhile. Unfortunately its always compared to Pokemon and I don't feel its fair to do that to this game. The time of me righting this review is literally minutes after 100% this game.

I recommend some small thing that could improve this game:

Sorting System: when it comes to Stickers or "Moves" there is no sorting system for the types of attacks or passives or anything. just A-Z. wish there was also a sorting system for ranges and melee attacks.

Beast types: there is so many types of beast and there isnt technically a type advantage but it does buff and debuff in the game. There is certain types you just never really use (at least i never did) like Glitter or Glass. Once i finally got these types they werent all that powerful either. Just interesting since you find them later in the game.

"End Game Content": at the very end it was a little wonky to actually beat the game and then technically go back to do end game (this is the most spoiler free way of explaining it) and even when it was end game it was an uphill GRIND. i beat the game within the week of purchase and it almost took me on and off play for about 3 weeks to finally grind to get level 6 bulletin board.

Now i do want to go into everything good about this game

Capturing: You arent actually wrestling these beast down and catching them like poachers, your copying them and i love this idea! Its still a Monster catching game and use them to fight for you but not using them.

Moves or Stickers: Unique concept of moves and how to equipped or remove moves that you can do! It was confusing for like 2 minutes and then i realized how amazing and large the customizing options are. Also having the same stickers in uncommon and rares gave it the extra edge but your still using them same moves.

Creatures: Every time i played this game with my wife we always had a chuckle or giggle or a desire to capture or kill these uniquely named monsters. im dyslexic so butchering these names was even funnier to me too! all in all the names and monsters plus their designs make this game feel wholesome.

Multiplayer?!!: Drop in and drop out Co-op, need i say more?! Playing this with a wife or friend who just wants to play games with you but isnt fully investing into the game (yet ;D ) is amazing. I found a perfect balance of story play, capturing time and exploring time. Once you and your co-op partner get that down, its great!

Bootlegs ( Shine's!!!! ) : Boot legs are just shiny Pokemon but they are different types of whatever you catch, for example, a Triaffickrab is a plastic type that you find normally but if you find a ground type you are more likely to learn higher rarity moves and of that move type! So your Traffickrab ground type has a Rare sticker now because he is a boot leg. I love this system.

The only somewhat negative part lies in the design of the creatures, some fantastic with excellent inspiration, others that vacillate between the incomprehensible and evolution that do not follow the previous form, thus not perceiving a logical continuity with the monsters. Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
