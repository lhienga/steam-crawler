Originality- The game is very original you can literally transform into monsters and fight with them. There are also HUNDREDS if not more fusions of monsters you can experiment with for days.

Story- The story is the best part of this game by far. You spawn into a world where people from different timelines have all been sucked onto the same island outside of space-time. You will embark on an incredible adventure, make friends, fight monsters, and uncover secrets. Without any spoilers this story is incredible.

Combat- Cassete beasts has similar combat to Pokemon, but I think it surpasses that stereotype. There is a lot of strategy in the combat, and it has some incredible features such as fusion.

Art- The art and animation in this game are top-notch, it makes your experience so much better.

Monster taming elements- In cassette beasts, you will capture monsters into cassettes and then transform into said monsters once you go into battle. Cassetes can even evolve and some have split evolutions! There are also bootleg cassettes wich are like Pokemon shinys.




I have written on only some points of the game, there are many more for you to discover. And the story is just so good! You need to get this game. No seriously buy it. Right now.\t\t\t\t\t
