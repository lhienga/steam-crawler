\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had brought this game for $14.99. Imagine Pokemon, but with more NPCs with personalities, better story and better quality of life.

I also am a huge fan of the battle system and the way that you can customize your character.

The battle system works by you having a cassette and cassette player. Imagine the "cassette" as your Pokemon, but instead of you throwing a poke-ball out which releases the monster, you instead place the cassette into the cassette player to "play" the music of the monster, and then you transform into the monster. To add new monsters to your collection, instead of attempting to catch them with a "Pokeball," You have to "record" them onto an empty cassette tape.

As for the customization of your character, it allows a few different hair styles, outfits and hats to choose from, as well as the ability to customize the colors of each item. There is a cosplay pack that you could buy for more customization options if you wish.

I would highly recommend this game, even at it's full price.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the best creature collectors out there. Simple as.

The visuals are fantastic, both the pixelart, character portraits, Archangels and the rest. You will fall in love with one of the creatures and people for sure.
The Gameplay is dynamic, changing whenever you decide to change your own attacks via stickers you put on the creatures you use, allowing for two of the same beasts to have different styles of fighting. Fusing feels amazing in fights, as the outcome of the fusion is always surprising for the first timers.
The chemistry system aka type matchups leave so many doors open for strategies with your allies. Using a water attack on yourself as a Plant Type to give yourself healing, boosting your AP by using certain type attacks on Astral monsters and more! Experiment for best results
The music is one of the best parts of the game, both the overworld tracks and in battle music. You can fuse during fights and depending on when you fuse, the vocals in the track will play from that moment, not resetting the music track from the start. Once you first fuse in a certain fight, you will not forget it.
The story is incredible, basing itself around the chaotic world and the methods of getting there. It makes every character's unique drive stand out by reminding you they aren't superheroes or anything, just the average person using this world as a second chance at life.
The monster designs are amazing, with them having unique inspirations you can learn about by levelling them up. Some of my favorites are; Tokusect, Sirenade/Decibelle, Glaistain, Ripterra and Snoopin. Bootlegs change their type and color palette too so I love different type'd ones too.

It's a creature collector game so obviously you will need to grind levels to get some creatures, but the soundtrack and gameplay makes up for that for sure. The entire game oozes charm that has not been captured by other games of it's genre. Seriously, the devs are legends and they deserve awards for the amount of love that was poured into this game.

TL:DR, game is so good I'd run up to Ham and make sure there is no moths around.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSimplesmente o que Pokemon devia ser em 2023. O gênero Pocket Monster é fantástico e divertido e com bastante capacidade de desenvolvimento, jogos como Persona, Tem Tem, e Até Dodge ball Academy demostraram desenvolvimentos significativos a antiga formula. Amo Pokemon, mas talvez ele devia baixar a arrogância e aprender com outros novas formulas de jogar e de criar.

Falando brevemente de Cassette Beasts, o jogo inventou uma ação e reação com "tipos" de monstros complexa que exige pensamento. Como exemplo: Um monstro tipo Fogo, tem vantagem contra Gelo, porem se usar um ataque de fogo no tipo gelo, beem, Gelo derrete então durante um período esse monstro de gelo se torna tipo água, que tem vantagem contra tipo fogo.

Por ser um jogo single player existe um foco em um roteiro interessante onde um misterio reina todos. O mundo parece vivo e constantemente você usa poderes para resolver puzzles. habilidades são mais fáceis distribuídos alem de terem um processo de raridade. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tHoly cow did this game absolutely blow me away.

So monster tamer, right? Creature capture, whatever you may call the series. I personally recently came with a big love for the genre, and I came into CB(Cassette Beasts) with SUCH low expectations. Why? Because to me bonding with your monster is half the fun and story telling, so I thought I wouldn't like it, well, I did!

As seen from the screenshots, in this game you don't catch monsters, you transform into them. So the story focuses a bit less on the monsters themselves (with the exceptions of 'bosses') and more on the human characters. Kayleigh! Eugene! Viola! Meredith! Felix! All of them tell such good and excellent stories.

The fighting is butter smooth. You can even set the scaling! The type chart is very unique and fun! The monsters are cute and scary!(See what I did there?)

The overworld is vast, the stories sad, the ending made me silently tear up because I got too attached to a bunch of human idiots and a dog.

I would say give Cassette Beasts a shot, Steam has a 2h refund policy, and who knows, maybe you'll find something you really love in it.\t\t\t\t\t
