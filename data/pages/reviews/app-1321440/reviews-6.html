\r\n\t\t\t\t\toverall, i like the game, and you should play it

(I played the game on max difficulty settings)
I really like the adventure and the overall difficulty of combat, as well as the synergy and movesets you can make. The characters were okay, I would've liked maybe more dialogue, the kind of idle chit chat you can make with your companions in other games.

The bosses are neat. Only one of them made me want to gouge my eyes out (status spammer), and I actually would've liked more bosses of that calibre, especially with the maximum difficulty setting.

The finality of the game.. I personally didn't like it, and would've preferred an actual difficult fight. You do fight, but for all intents and purposes, it may as well be an interactive cinematic.

Post game, there are things to do, but unfortunately as far as I know, there isn't any place to limit test your team or game knowledge, like Pokemon's battle towers.

*edit
I actually like the post game lol. Leveling up the noticeboard and getting more Bootlegs is pretty fun. I can see myself getting all the achievements, to be honest, and the Custom Game with randomisers spice the game up enough to make for another playthrough.

So again, all in all, I highly recommend anyone to play the game! It's definitely worth the money ,especially when it's on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
