I've played practically non-stop since I bought it, and it has been super fun. whilst a few of the fusions are a little underwhelming, and the names feel a little on the nose, there are plenty of gems. my favourites include Robindam, Arkidd and Djinnentonic to name a few. even after beating the main story, the noticeboard quests never got boring. i would find myself completing hundreds of them without them ever feeling repetitive. and the custom games are great fun too, I love that there are built in options for a randomised hardcore nuzlocke already in the game for anyone ready for a challenge.
all in all, I find cassette beasts to be a fun and enjoyable, that puts a fun twist on the turn-based monster-catching formula.\t\t\t\t\t
