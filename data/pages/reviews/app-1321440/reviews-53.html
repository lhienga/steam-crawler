\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is awesome. Not to bring down the game by comparing it to other things but it's like all of the good openworld improvements of pokemon Scarlet and Violet, the fusion gimmick from the pokemon infinite fusions fangame, and a whole lot of pixel and retro charm.
The soundtrack is banger, the sprites are fun, the game has a ton of content, and the companions are cool (except you Eugene).

If I had to give some complaints:
-I wish there was some sort of speedup for status effects in combat. Hitting multiple enemies and having to watch their defenses increase for 45s is annoying, especially when the attack defeated them anyways.
-Also I guess I wish there was at least one more starter cassette beast.
-Physics can be really janky sometimes. It can be funny, but also very frustrating.

edit: just coming back after finishing most of the post game and doing the final ending. It is amazing. It's consumed a lot of my life in the past week and I'm glad to have done it. A bittersweet end having to leave behind the characters I'd befriended (in more ways than one). It's crazy to me that such a small team can create such a cool experience that imo is better than any pokemon or monster taming RPG I've every played. You guys keep going and doing your own thing. Well, I found the vocal soundtrack on spotify, so I'm going to go listen to that for the 100th time on loop. Buy the darn game.\t\t\t\t\t
