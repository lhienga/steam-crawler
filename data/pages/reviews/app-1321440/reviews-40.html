Cosy and Wholesome Monster Melding (?) Experience
Cassette Beasts is a relaxing and fun local co-op game to play with a friend. It is pretty amazing to see how many variations the game allows you to combine different monster types together when you go boss-mode, and overall the game felt very straight forward and easy to pick up.

There are a few quality of life improvements to be had e.g. sorting cassettes, items, status effect library and maybe even a chart to indicate each elements match-up. But all-in-all this is a solid game that delivers a lot of cosy and chill vibes.\t\t\t\t\t
