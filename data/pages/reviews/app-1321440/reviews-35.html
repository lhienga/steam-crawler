\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl;dr--too much! The audio was inexcusably high, even after adjusting down to fifty percent music and forty percent cues before beginning gameplay. For a game that's called cassette beasts which ostensibly is meant to have good music, this was just a wall of sound, and for some reason had no/crap transitions. I tried to escape out to the menu to lower volume, the game wouldn't let me until the tutorial ended. And nothing against the VAs, but yikes.

And the visuals. So many flashing lights, images popping up suddenly on your screen, etc. I wish I had played this as soon as I purchased, as this would have been an instant refund for me. I really liked the idea of this game, but clearly should have watched some gameplay first because this is a big pass for someone who gets migraines from flashing lights and jarring noises.\t\t\t\t\t
