Gameplay is smooth but I wished there was an option to skip animations to make late game grinding a bit smoother.
The music is beautiful and better than what most AAA games have. The monster design is hit or miss. While there are some really cool ones, there are also some more strange designs.
The difficulty is odd. The early game felt hard, but that eased over time. The game felt very easy over all and I think I've only lost a fight once. But there are sliders that affect if and how much the enemies scale with the player or not and how smart the AI behaves, which is welcomed a lot.
Then there's also a New Game+ mode that is unlocked after beating the game, where you can play the game with your cassettes (your monster forms) permanently destroyed if they get defeated, permadeath mode, random distrubution of monsters, random types for the monster or random move learning. You can combine any of these things to create the mode you want to play, which is awesome and greatly increases replayability! There's also an ingame speedrun timer if you want to go that route!

Great game, definitely worth the 20€!!\t\t\t\t\t
