\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an amazing, really cute and charming game with surprising depth and good enough story. I have streamed this game and me and my chat got obsessed with the Trafficrab as soon as we saw it. And Candevil. But mostly the trafficrab. Which we renamed Traffiscrib and did really well from basically start to finish. I really appreciate the diverse cast of characters and I grew particularly attached to Ashleigh. It was really sad that we had to send her back to the troubles because she didn't deserve it. The fusions are also a lot of fun! A really great take on a pokémon style game and bringing on cassettes as the medium (I have a soft spot for cassettes, essentially grew up on them along with CDs)

There are a couple very minor gripes I have and they are as follows: The overworld sprites are too chibi because I really appreciate the full body ones seen in the battles. They are nowhere near bad, however. It's just wishful thinking and fawning. The second is the dog. It's mostly personal for me, as I am afraid of dogs and don't like dogs in the first place. But the introduction and the story of the dog felt very shoehorned and.... well... there was nothing to it. I could've ignored the dog quest entirely and not have lost anything storywise (and probably gained a more satisfying ending, ironically).

Anyhow, this game gets nominated for basically all the positions on the steam awards this year and it is well ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ deserved. Well done Bytten Studios, excellent game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have yet to finish this quite frankly beast of a game do to my habit of I must 100% everything not locked behind post-game when it comes to games like this.
While i do have some nit picks, such as bootlegs feeling a bit to rare and to easy to miss (accidentally get them off screen and they're gone, which i did when i boosted on accident when i saw one and over shot it when i meant to just get beside them) This may be a little grip I have thats fixed post game some way though I'm not sure. Again, i'm insane and feel like I must 100% all I can before the final boss. (though that bootleg collection is going to wait til post-game, i can't do that to myself with just how rare they are. Maybe my RNG just sucks)

Also the romance? Yea romance is a small part, just a foot note and thats fine, not the reason i decide to play this (only found out about it after looking at the achievements to how many i do in one play through) and all but only one of the party members feel like that having a romance option wasn't in mind with their dialogs leading up to their fifth heart. (not counting the barkely cause thats a dog and there for does not count. He is a good puppy though) All the others kind of just comes out of left field in their five heart convo or not all even though its a option. It just feels flat with all the characters other then Meredith who's fourth heart sets a nice stage for romance to seem like a real option (at lest to me) come her fifth heart. Which for me isn't one of the two characters I would like to romance, a lovely lady, just not my type. At lest from what I know other then a achievement and a slightly different ending romance doesn't matter at all from what I know. So it's more of just nit pick in my regard but still.
Another note on the romance, I had no clue about it til after I got Kayleigh to 5 hearts and didn't choose the romance dialog option as there was no indicator that you could romance at 5 hearts. The dialog option that i didn't choose just felt out of place to me so i didn't choose it. To bad it was the romance one. Then i looked at the achievements and now I cant date Kayleigh who is my first choice for romance now that i know that it exists. She's my favorite party member cause i love her game play with her cassette beast and like her personalty the most. There is no way to date a member if you don't choose the right dialog option in their fifth heart convo. No way to redo it without cheats or mods.
At lest I found out before accidentally friend zoning Eugene too.

On the note of the lovely dog Barkley, he is by far the worst party member and that sucks. Maybe I just don't know how to use him that well but when there's Kayleigh, Eugene and the others the poor dog just is so weak it feels. May just be my play style but from what I can see from other reviews and talks about the party members I'm not alone in feeling like Barkely isn't all that great sadly.


But over all I do recommend this game, maybe not to 100% cause it is a grind (the bootlegs and the rouge fusions grinding is hell) and I think I am driving myself a little mad with it. I am having a blast over all though.

I'll probably beat it before hitting that 100% pre-post game cause I am burning myself out with the grinding and getting everyone to five hearts and 5 staring their main cassette beasts. You can only do the same repeat fights of rouge fusions so long before it gets dull when you really just want everyone caught up and powered up plus grinding out those Fused shards. (though Fused shards grinding apparently gets a bit easier post game)\t\t\t\t\t
