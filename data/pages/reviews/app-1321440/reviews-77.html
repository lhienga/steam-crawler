\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSigh, Another Pokemon clone.

Is what most people say / think about this game upon first glimpse, myself being one of them. Decided to give it a look after a friend strongly recommended it to me on a number of times and it was on sale. I tried it out, first 15-20 mins i was like "mm ok yeah this just another pokemon clone" then i started noticing i couldn't put it down, i got addicted to the games story, design and sprites. It's beautiful, its so much more than just a clone of pokemon. It has its own combat system, UI style, Story and Unique Element to it.

The story itself was brilliant, i've finished it fully through and am currently working towards 100% achievements and impatiently awaiting the DLC release, because of how damn good this game is. The combination of Funny names and Sprite combo's, as well as the well thought out dialogue of each character, it truly is a masterpiece of a game.

The Music is a key element of what makes the game so good to me, i have two of the main songs on my spotify playlist and listen to them daily as both are so well made and so well thought out.

TL;DR

Whether its on sale or not, this game is 110% worth the price they ask, it is a beautiful game and has a brilliant story behind it, hoping the developer team keeps this same energy for the DLC and future potential DLCs...\t\t\t\t\t

This game is not better than Pok√©mon
It is better than Scarlet and Violet by a longshot, but it really lacks the depth a 20 years franchise like Pok√©mon has. Not necessarily the devs fault, Red and Blue had more or less the same issues.
If you are looking for an alternative to modern Pok√©mon games, look into emulation and ROM hacks, that's were the real quality is most of the time.
With that settled:

GAMEPLAY - 8/10
The combat is good, it's a turn based game with a lot of breathing room for personalizing your combat style, even allowing some pretty overpowered set-ups, which are always fun to figure out. The game lets you roam around freely from pretty much the get go and is built on non-linear progression (you don't need to progress the story to unlock parts of the map you just need to catch specific monsters to unlock "field powers", similarly to Pok√©mon's HMs). A good variety of option is given to you to make your experience more or less challenging, like bosses level scaling up to you, hard mode (which is essentially better AI) and a bunch of other settings to tweak the difficulty to your liking.
The fusion mechanic is interesting on paper, but ends up being redundant and taking away the nuance of double battles. The mechaninc could be completely scrapped and the game would probably be just as, if not more, good. But I guess they needed it to convey some weird metaphore about sex.

GRAPHICS - 7/10
The art style has me torn: I despise the characters' designs, but I somewhat like the monsters' design; they're pretty hit or miss, with great highs but equally great lows. The sprites for fused beasts all look too similar to each other and they don't even look that good to begin with. I get that spriting 10.000+ fusion wasn't feasible, but they are probably the lowest point of the this game's design. The pixel art and the different techniques used for bosses (each of them has a unique art style that range from vector art, to 3D art, to traditional drawings) are great and leagues above the cartoonish style they used for human character. They not only cater to a very generic millenial taste, but they are even poorly drawn, with some characters eyes even crossing...
Given that there is only a handfull of human characters and 120ish monsters I feel good in giving the game a positive score for graphics/design.

STORY - 4/10
This is what really brings the game down: the writing. It is really moot. I didn't resonate one bit with any of the characters and found the storyline pretty boring. This game really makes you feel like you have no agency on what happens around you, you are a cog in the storytelling machine and that's it. This becomes overwhelmingly clear in the finale. I was surprised by all this because the reviwes all praised the great writing and characters. There is barely a plot and close to no character develpment. There was clearly an effort, but this department of develpment could have used either more time or more talent.

Overall a good game, 6.5/10.
Either the reviews are from people that have been personally abused by GameFreak or I really stumbled upon something that was really not written for me.\t\t\t\t\t
