\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan...should have listened to the reviews sooner. This game is phenomenal.

I haven't finished it yet, but there is absolutely no way this will get anything other then a massive thumbs up.

The game is better then any pokemon game, EVER, period. The combat system itself is simple, but by using all the customisation options you have at your disposal, this quickly turns into the most complex turned based I've seen. There are so many abilities, so many monster types, so many ways for the different elements to interact with each-other; it's just insane. Things like air putting out fire element, so lowering it's stats, or other elements turning things into glass and then you have abilities that shatter the glass killing the monster instantly, or spreading shards that hurt melee ones; or monster that are asleep having access to very powerful dream-walking spells, but they have 1% chance to hit, so you use a buff that gives them 100% for 1 turn etc etc. You could write essays on how many things can happen.
The game is also chock-full of references and has some amazing names for all the monsters.

Other then that the game over-world is a really cool, puzzle filled place. where you have to use abilities and a bit of brain power to traverse it and find treasures/quests.

The story is also very interesting, if a bit of a slow burn.

To top it all off, the music is just amazing and it gets EVEN better when you use your fuse move.

Probably the only mediocre thing is the animations. There are ton of monsters, so each only has like one animation.

Overall, it's one of the best games out there. A must buy at any price.
Every time I find a gem like this, it makes me happy to see indie devs still pushing the limit of what games can become with little money, but a ton of soul.\t\t\t\t\t
