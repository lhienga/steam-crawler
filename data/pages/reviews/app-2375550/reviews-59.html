\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story is pretty darn good, the combat not so much. It's buggy, the cheap-looking animation and camera work as well as snapping from early Dragon Engine games is back, and a lot of the heat actions feel impactless.

Agent style is unfortunately terribly undercooked; it's limited, stiff, and buggy. However, the sound design is really satisfying. Tiger drop sounds everywhere, it's great.

Yakuza style is just, ugh... it's strong stats-wise and it "works" better than Agent, but it's honestly not fun to use. It's basically Kiwami 2 DoD again. The combo animations still look and feel really disconnected and samey.

It just seems like so many of the advancements to the Dragon Engine that Judge Eyes and Lost Judgement have made, have got thrown out again or forgotten about. Even the UI looks like it got downgraded. It was neat getting another chance to play as Kiryu, we got some amazing match ups for him this time around, but the fighting and overall feel of the game just was incredibly underwhelming and I think it wasted heaps of potential.\t\t\t\t\t
