\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great fan service game for the RGG fans. Only contains 5 chapters, so some plot points and characters are not developed enough. Although, if you are a yakuza fan, the last chapter will probably make up for the all the shortcomings this game has.

Combat is kinda clunky. I like it better than K2 and 6, but it is still not that fun compared to Yagami from Judgment. The agent style is way too op if you max out everything, but it's fun to look at Joryu using these crazy gadgets. The yakuza style is alright. It's good for 1v1 but that's all I have to say about it. I recommend using mods for this. It's a lot more enjoyable imo.

The substories are on yakuza's standard, but the other main side content is meh. It's just fetch quest and "go beat this group" over and over again. Some of them require you to find a certain place and take a picture. Overall it's there to pad out the game time.

Coliseum, on the other hand, is the best one we got so far. The variety of fights. You can play as other characters (with a very limited moveset.) Also, there is a nice surprise for those who are a fan of this series if you clear almost every fight.

Soundtrack is alright. There are good tracks there, but not a lot imo. Might be due to not many memorable scenes. New karaoke song is good though.

The only thing I'm super disappointed about is that, there's no new game plus and legend difficulty. Only recommended if you are invested in Kiryu's story and have played Yakuza 3 and 6 (Although I urge you to play them all before this one.)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a big fan of the games but this one is just shallow and left me unsatisfied. TLDR I do not recommend it for current price (50$) and would recommend waiting for sale . Can easily recommend it for 30$. It just feels like a dlc turned standalone game and reminds me a lot of current COD MW3 situation
- There's only one city (Osaka) and it's reused from Kiwami 2/LAD , the first chapter does feature yokohoma for like few scenes and goes unused for the rest of the game. I was expecting them to have 3 cities (Yokohoma, Kamarucho and Sotenbori) but only one goes used.
- The minigames suck for the most part. Pocket circuit racing (I seriously don't get people hype behind it) is just reused from Y0/kiwami with barely any changes and just overall reusing minigames in Like a dragon games IMO is bad idea since most of them are "oh that's cool" things that overstay their welcome in the same games they featured in. This includes pocket circuit racing and it's annoying enough that they are mostly tied to substories and you have to play and beat them for Amon fight. I think if you reuse minigames, reusing it more than one time makes it extremely boring and just lazy. One thing I will say that they did good job on is hostess club. The fact they using FMV add to the experience and also they made it not grindy at all like in past titles. Don't even get me started on another fighting ring that's again is boring as hell and has substories tied to it so you must do it.
- The main story is extremely short. Even though my play hours said 20h, 4 h was actual story content while rest was filled with grindy boring minigames. The overall story also felt relatively weak, it did had some good characters but yeah.
- The substories are extremely boring and uninspiring. Again, there are very few good ones but most of them are "THERES THIS GANG AROUND TOWN, BEAT THEM UP" and then after you do that "THERES ANOTHER RIVAL GANG, BEAT THEM UP" and that's like legit 75% of substories, just pure filler.
- Amon fight sucked ass, but atleast it wasn't as bad as Inshin

I don't know if this is true or not, but to me, this feels like it was made by the same B team that made Inshin remake and it shows. Again, fine if it's on sale and if you are a fan of series, but this is not a good "entry point for a franchise" like SEGA is promoting it and honestly, I would skip this one unless you're a fan or wait for sale. I can't justify 50$ for it and probably the second like a dragon game that left me unsatisfied and I just did not enjoy fully (first being inshin)
