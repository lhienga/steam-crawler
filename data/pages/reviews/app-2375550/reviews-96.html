\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game. Just more of the Yakuza that I've come to love. Kiryu's new Agent fighting style is really fun. The main story length is definitely far shorter than the other games, excluding Yakuza 1, but it's still packed with a lot of fun side content. I would say the only complaint, and it's really more of a nitpick, is that Sotenbori seems to be lacking in restaurants and bars compared to the previous entries. I was always going to refill my health at Wild Jackson and Smile Burger. I barely ever got Kiryu drunk to take advantage of the heat benefits since I had to go down into a basement to access the bar and go through a loading screen to get in and out. Also lots of running back and forth between Akame's hideout to get missions and collect rewards.
If you've never played a Yakuza/Like a Dragon game and you're thinking about starting with this game, DON'T. This is not for a new players, just start with Yakuza 0 and take your time, and save $45, then play through the games in order, it's worth it. This game is a reward for long time fans and the emotions have been built up by every other game.\t\t\t\t\t
