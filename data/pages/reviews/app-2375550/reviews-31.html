\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRelatively short for a Yakuza game (hence the Gaiden in the title); probably 1/3 the length of Yakuza 8 (also known as Yakuza: Like a Dragon). However, it's almost all killer, no filler. The ending sequence had me in tears. 10/10

I liked the return to the traditional Yakuza combat; I didn't mind 8's turn-based combat as much as some die-hard fans but it was definitely a slog at times. It definitely fits the more standard Yakuza theme of "Kiryu keeps punching things until the world is as it should be" that this game has.

My only complaint is that there is a specific fight that overlaps in this and 8 (The fight between Kiryu and Ichiban) that I would have love to have played from Kiryu's perspective, but that was just shown in flashback form at the end. I was really looking forward to it, and was bummed it wasn't there. Not a huge deal though, as the rest of the game is fantastic.

One thing that I liked / disliked was the Akame requests system. I do like that it was really easy to find and complete all the substories without wandering around looking for the triggers or relying on a guide, but it also felt less "Yakuza-y", in that there wasn't an equivalent of, for example, randomly getting roped into a Michael Jackson music video. It certainly streamlined things though.

All in all, it's a great game, and whets the appetite for Infinite Wealth without burning you out on the formula.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn terms of content it's definitely lacking, overall story length as well as only 2 fighting styles is very meh. Game itself was great tho, story had very cool moments and all the characters that made an appearance were great. The combat was kinda boring, partly because of only 2 fighting styles and the fact that normal attacks and movement are very weak, meanwhile you can kill a boss with just 2 heat actions (like seriously?). But besides the main story, the coliseum fights were very fun and had a big surprise in the 6 boss boss-rush battle ;) . For 50 €/$ i definitely wouldn't recommend the game, wait for a minimum 25% sale.\t\t\t\t\t
