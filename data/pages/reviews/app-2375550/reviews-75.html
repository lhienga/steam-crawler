\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSatisfying continuation of Kiryu's story, I love Yakuza 6, so this is a perfect instalment for me. Story was very short, but sweet, dramatic, funny, interesting and overall emotional like the rest of the stories in this franchise. Gaiden introduced new characters, who became almost iconic to me, they fit into the world perfectly, are awesomely voiced/acted and they just feel like real people.
Side content wise, it is again stupid how many activities you can do. Fan favourite Pocket Circuit is back, still holds up to this day and it is enjoyable as hell. Substories are not weak at all, I was surprised in how many new situations main protagonist ends up in. Even a few nostalgic callbacks made an appearance. Playing a Yakuza game only for the main story is almost like not playing the game at all. Of course I need to mention customization: hat, glasses, clothes, rings, earrings, gloves, shoes, and so on, pure awesomeness.

Like the original title of the game tells us, it is a side story to Yakuza 7, don't expect Gaiden to have a nuclear impact on the whole franchise, but at the end, you will certainly tear up at least. Thanks to this game, I understand the story of Yakuza 7 more, Gaiden takes you behind the scenes and some things becomes clearer.
And combat was super awesome, you could maybe compare it to Lost Judgment. Great game, I can certainly recommend it to all players who want more of Kiryu Kazuma/Kuroda Takaya.\t\t\t\t\t
