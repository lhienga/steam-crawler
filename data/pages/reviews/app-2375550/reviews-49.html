The graphics look very beautiful and gameplay overall is solid and style switching in this game feels comfortable, though in my opinion Yakuza style needs a tiny bit of a speed touch up since at times it can feel like I could've pulled off very good looking juggles and combos. Agent style is very fun combined with faster attacks and also the gadgets give you the ability to juggle and re-juggle well.

In terms of story, this game met my expectations and hype when it was first announced. To me it is very much worth the money spent however I was a little disappointed to find out that there isn't a New Game+ feature for this game as I was planning to redo the story battles with more upgrades than the first playthrough.\t\t\t\t\t
