Gameplay and Story wise, it has everything you would expect from the previous games, maybe even better, but I might be biased. The new fighting style, Agent, is equally as good and even compliments the usual DoD style, as both styles cover each others shortfalls, but not being directly better than the other, making gameplay constantly fun and engaging.

New fighting style, agent, feels pretty solid with gadgets, but the combos you can do with it is kinda bare bones. Yakuza style just felt broken and it was the only style I used after mid point...

Ok let me get this out of the way, if you're a new player this game is not for you. This is where it feels like the story ends. Every other game we played so far was building up for this. Kiryu finally going on a break after all those adventures. Bosses feel like bosses we fought in older games, especially final boss. Game does an amazing job at ending the story. I teared up and was just staring at the credits scene like I watched an old friend go away.

Fighting is bare bones, but the story is just amazing. Not for new players, and definetly older players that played all other games will like it.\t\t\t\t\t
