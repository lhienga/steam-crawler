TL;DR - The true and final closure of Kiryu's story before he passes the torch to Ichiban
In other words, this is the (most likely) the last time we'll ever play a Yakuza Like A Dragon game as a beat-em-up as Kiryu

Disclaimer: The game was played through Professional/Hard difficulty blind, having played all of the previous major games and not being spoiled of the story

Loved the gameplay and combat, especially since I recently got into fighting games (mwah, Mortal Kombat) the juggle system was a chef's kiss. I know Lost Judgment was the first to introduce it but that's not the point. Although I will admit, I did grind all of the upgrades before half of the story (my bad, Shishido), it kinda ruined the game but was still fun nonetheless.

Coliseum was a straight upgrade, thank god

And the story, well, it was very good. I'll give you that.

Cheers to Yakuza Like A Dragon 8: Infinite Wealth\t\t\t\t\t
