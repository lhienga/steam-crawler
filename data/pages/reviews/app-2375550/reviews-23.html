As for achievements, this isn't your normal Yakuza game as it's one of the easiest to complete 100%. You only need to play the game with minimal mini-games. That means, no mahjong, shogi or 100 collectibles to slow you down. It's a great change, but kinda miss doing a lot for that 100% completion ya know?

Story wise, they really went great lengths just to create a compelling one in order to connect to the events of Y7: LAD. The ending is one of the best I've seen among the series and can seriously compete for top 3.

Long story short, buy this game! A little pricy, but worth it. Looking forward to LAD: IW!\t\t\t\t\t
