\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to start by saying as a fan of the franchise the ending to this game is so cathartic and unbelievably emotional. Its a roller coaster ride. Its a kick in the chest. It genuinely feels like a reward for playing the last 7 games and It makes me all the more excited for infinite wealth. I can not wait.

How in the world did RGG studios make such an incredible game in only 6 months? Hell, they gave us more game in 6 months than most devs give us with years of development. What once was meant to be a DLC side story, came out as its own feature game at a reduced price, that barely feels reduced in any way. There is so much on the plate with this game. Returning to sotenbori was a treat, Pocket circuit us back with a vengeance and the Colosseum mini game is better than it has been in like ever. I thought i was going to hate the agent fighting style, but my god its actually fun. Dont let the over the topness of it all steer you away. Game play is king and agent style is super fun. The story is amazing as i expected, it was great to see the events of 7 from kiryus perspective, but as i stated before the ending of this game takes the cake and is probably the best in the franchise. I would not recommend this one to first timers though. This game is the culmination of YEARS of games and the impact of the story will be greatly lessened by not having played all of that first. go play 0 or kiwami first.

The performance is out of this world. The game Looks and runs incredibly. How does it look better and run better than all the games before it? So many games get released broken these days and get fixed as they go, but i didnt have a single crash or bug in my almost 50 hour playthrough. SEGA remains my goat, and they do no wrong in my eyes.

I have one complaint. I wish the mid fight cut scenes had more QTEs like they use to. There are like 4 in the game and i think the QTEs made the scenes feel 1000% better and more involved.

Please SEGA. More of this. More Gaidens between major releases. You have a wealth of amazing characters to make a wealth of new stories. You know a Majima game would sell like crazy.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very nice conclusion and wrap up of Kiryu's story at least until Infinite Wealth hits. The story is easily right up there with Yakuza 0 with a very good mix of comedy and serious moments that everyone has come to love. The quality of cut scenes and cinematics have improved so much and it was an absolute joy to watch.

Combat feels nice although it definitely feels a bit lacking when compared to previous games. There's a smaller variety of heat actions which is a shame and combat is significantly easier as extreme heat mode is a very easy way to cheese most fights. Boss fights are definitely much less fun than compared to previous titles where each boss has a quirk; most of the boss fights in this game feel the same. However, the introduction of Agent style more than makes up for it. I've had way more fun beating people up and throwing them all around than in any other previous game. Coming from Yakuza 7, it was great to beat people up in the combini again.

Another change from previous games is that substories are all now triggered from a "HQ" location which is kind of neat as it is easier to clear through all of them rather than hunting them around town. I can understand why some people might prefer the older method though.

The coliseum was pretty cool but kind of underwhelming due to the ability to cheese fights with extreme heat mode. The team fights also felt kind of forced in and at some point, they become very annoying due to the insanely high health pool of some of the "boss" and "mini-boss" characters can be.

Overall it was a really fun game and well worth it's full price. Steam Deck compatibility from launch was also a really neat bonus.\t\t\t\t\t
