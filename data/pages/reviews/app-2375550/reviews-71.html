If you know what that is already then you know what to be in for.
If you don't know then I suggest starting with at least Yakuza 0 / Yakuza 7.
The game also includes characters from Judgement / Lost Judgement.

The game-play starts off mediocre but quickly ramps up once the player gets access to upgrades and can get money easily at the arena. The new agent fighting style very quickly becomes fun to use.

The DLC was worth it for me to play Majima again but it is not really necessary. In all honesty they should have made a Majima game since we kinda know what happens to Kiryu already.

If you do not have cash and / or time available you can skip this or buy it on sale. It is a good game but there are better in the series.
