No but seriously, this game is among the best, even though it's the shortest mainline game in the series. The story's pacing is faster than usual, which is a nice change. Cast is a 10/10.
I have not played Lost Judgment before this game so in terms of combat it's different for me since I'm experiencing stuff like juggling for the first time. Regardless, it's extremely satisfying, especially once you get all the upgrades and gadgets with Agent style and this is the best Kiryu Dragon Engine combat hands down. Pretty much everything else that a Yakuza game has, this one has too.
Should be played after 7 at the very least, ideally after all the other mainline games though.
