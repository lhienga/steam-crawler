\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was originally intended as DLC to LAD... and it shows.
Apart from the absurdity of Kiryu being brought back from his definitive retirement for the 6th time, this game is just filler content. There is a story, but it's a very uninspired one with characters you don't care about, and the rest of the game is just a set of minigames like colloseum, gambling or fetch quests with random items. While mechanically sound, graphically it's nothing to brag about. Dotonbori area is good, but the tiny part of the game when it takes you to Yokohama, it's terrible. You really can tell that the only reasoning behind this game was being a cash grab rather than bringing any value into the franchise.

It's just a glorified ad for the newest LAD you pay full price for and nothing else. It reaches peak absurdity towards the end where some random guy is easily more powerful than all bad guys Kiryu had faced up till that point and when in the last cutscene sequence he reminiscences a character that hasn't been brought up even ONCE for the last 6 installments.

tl;dr
PROS:
- more beat'em up content with Kiryu I guess?
- you get to see Dotonbori
- certain red haired character looks good
CONS:
- repetitive and uninspired content aimed to extend the playthrough
- boring story with major plotholes
- really bad graphics in ceratin areas
- cringy attempt at making Kiryu an Inspector Gadget\t\t\t\t\t
