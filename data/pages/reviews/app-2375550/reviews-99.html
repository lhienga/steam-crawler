\r\n\t\t\t\t\t100% Steam achievements with 38 hours clocked. I had a great time with Gaiden and loved almost everything about it but I just wanted to quickly highlight the very few things that I didn't like:

1) Akame is a great character but ranking up her network often felt like boring busywork which was clearly designed to pad out the runtime of what is a fairly short game. This is especially true of the requests that require you to run all over the map talking to various NPC's placed on opposite ends of the map or fighting groups of gangs scattered across town. These were definitely a chore to get through and they frankly don't do anything for the overall experience. The substories were great though.

2) I wasn't a fan of the live-action hostesses at the cabaret clubs. They felt awkward and forced and frankly unsettling. I had to power through those by skipping through every scene. Fingers crossed they never bring that back.

3) JUSTICE - the Third King bossfight found in the Colosseum - is completely scuffed. Anyone here who has fought him knows exactly what I mean. Not even Amon comes close to the level of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ and unfairness found in this fight, almost to the point where I can't quite believe it was intended to be this way. I guess time will tell. In any case, this was the lowest point of the game for me and the only time when I felt frustrated and angry while playing Gaiden.

Other than that I universally loved everything else. The game is short by RGG standards but by every means sweet and the story is up there with the best this studio have made. I'm more excited than ever for Infinite Wealth and I'm desperately hoping that Kiryu gets the ending we all know he deserves to have.\t\t\t\t\t
