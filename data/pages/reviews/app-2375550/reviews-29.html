Great game, great combat, great story that appeals heavily to fans, Side missions are not much of a chore and more a nice way of engaging with the world and leveling up Kiryu. The castle is a fantastic addition, with all kinds of gambling and a very well deserved dress-up system, so you can finally customize Kiryu drip your own way with little but pretty good designs. Making money is a lot easier and way less grindy and much more fun, so its very much appreciated. I trully can only recommend this game for people who played ALL of the main RGG games (Yakuza) because its the only way you can almost personally feel for all these awesome characters. I played the Infinite Wealth demo that came with the game and i gotta say, they reaaally improved the turn-based combat to give a dynamic and "Beat 'em uppy" feel and so far the graphics and the open-world is jaw dropping. The story as well grip you by the balls and seems promising. Truly RGG fans are eating good.\t\t\t\t\t
