\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLike a Dragon Gaiden is a filler game starring the series protagonist Kazuma Kiryu. It explains what happened between Yakuza 6 where he went into hiding, to his unexplained surprise cameo appearances in Yakuza 7.

Despite being a smaller title than the rest, the game is filled with charms with modest game length. The new fighting mechanics is great and fans will notice the various throwbacks to the previous games here and there. Unlike previous game however, most of the game is set in Sotenbori and a new place called 'Castle', which function as a huge high end gambling dams and a Colosseum. Still there are lots of contents to go around and looking at how the Yakuza era ended from Kiryu's perspective is rather interesting.

The ending is one of the most emotional endings in this series, and it also leads into the opening to the next game: Like a Dragon Infinite Wealth. Although it is a gaiden story, I highly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
