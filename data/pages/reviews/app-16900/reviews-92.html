\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game for 6 hours and i'm still far from playing every map and testing all play styles, though i don't think there is many play styles yet.

This game is good. Very good. A bit buggy, though i haven't had much problems with it at all.
You can pick an assault rifle and make it the worst abomination ever seen in the world, make it weight a ton just from useless attachments you might never use and still do great.
It has some weird controls (to me), but you get used to it.
Multiplayer is *FUN*. Really fun. and playing stealth missions when you're alone feels like you're playing an horror game.
The only major mistake i have is that the enemy AI is a bit goofy, ontop the fact that the enemy AI always likes to scream when they see you, which means you can always tell when you're going to get popped.
Night vision makes my eyes tear, i dunno if that's how militaries feel in real life, but for the people out there who had to use theses, i am sorry for you.
I will continue using it 24/7, tho. Add thermals plz, can't see the heat.

If they add more guns in, more maps and make movement/controls and AI better. I'll eat this game for breakfast.
Seriously, this is up there in my list of best FPS, just as it is.\t\t\t\t\t
