\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has a huge amount of potential and it has been awesome watching the devs continue to build on it. They are constantly adding content. The fps foundation in this game is almost unmatched when it come to bullet metrics, weapon customization, kit building, and planning. The only one that really comes to mind is Escape from Tarkov.

I agree that the overall point of the game seems dull because there really isn't any story or anything to strive for, but that is where you can get creative. They made this game to be modded and to be played with friends. Me and my group of friends get together and set up challenges, make it super difficult. You can also create your own server with your own rules, or join one thats up already. I love jumping into the milsim servers and role playing with a bunch of people.

If you are a fan of a great fps shooter with good mechanics, weapon customization, and tactical missions that you build, I would recommend this game for sure. If you are looking for a COD or Battlefield experience like level grinding and unlocks, then this is not the game for you.\t\t\t\t\t
