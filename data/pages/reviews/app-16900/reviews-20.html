\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most underrated tactical shooters ever. Been following it for a long time and updates continue to release at a steady rate.

The customization in this game is literally unmatched. Pick your face, your outfit, your camo, your guns, what specific rail you want to put attachments on and it actually effects how the weapon works. You can place sights as close to your face or as far from your face as you want. You can choose what kind of belt you want. Vest, rig, plates, etc. You can choose what specific pouches you want on the vest. You can build your vests out how ever you want or however you can think of. A smart idea is to always include a AK pouch on your kits since you are likely to need more ammo on missions.

The game features the most realistic night vision system I've ever seen. Great for rolepaying and challenge.

The game features a very good amount of maps, and they get updated and upgraded all the time. A level could be a city block, that features scaffolding for sniping, entire multi-level buildings to clear, and an entire subway system to work around. You can choose the time of day for mission. Most every building includes places to cut power before entry. The you can even cut power to the subway.

The gunplay is challenging, yet realistic, like most aspects of this game. It features a good variety of weapons. Weapons can only take attachments they can take in real life. Every gun still features a long list of every kind of attachment, and since the game lets you pick specific rails to put on attachments, you can really make anything you can thin of. This goes for rifles, SMGs, LMGs, shotguns, snipers, and DMRs.

If you can think of a real life soldier to try and copy/larp as, chances are you can 100% mimic most kits.

Only complaint is the AI. Wish they were smarter. Wish they had varied looks. Wish I could create kits for enemy AI. Wish they were ofc a little smarter.

Game is still entertaining enough playing solo, but getting a few friends this game takes it to the next level.

Love this game, and hope it has a great future.
