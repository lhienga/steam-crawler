\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want Ready or Not with an "Operator twist" and less of the evidence gathering/ spending 15 minutes to circle the map and make sure you ziptied every civilian and dead suspect before you can finish the mission stuff this is the answer. The attention to detail is top notch. Awesome customization and gun porn comparable to Tarkov. Best implementation of recoil and weapon handling I've ever seen in a video game, by far, which means it's one of the most satisfying games to move around and shoot in.

I'd say the game is best experienced with a group of friends, but the smaller sized playerbase is active and you can get on servers and play with randoms as long as you're not being a dickwad and are open to communicating. Everyone I've met has been mature and welcoming.

Biggest draw back is the AI, but you can tweak them with the in game settings to make them tolerable.

The developer has their roadmap posted for everyone to follow and there are a lot of things coming that you may feel are lacking. Development is pretty slow but worth keeping an eye on if you grew up on games like the original Ghost Recon and R6 series.\t\t\t\t\t
