\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would recommend Ground Branch for those who loves fps games, and Swat games/simulators. Even thou perhaps the game lacks perhaps of somewhat bit more graphic effects, and details, at current state look quite good. But at the heart of the game, are the many options of setting up both the gear. That's weapons with flashlight, laser, scopes, nightvision helmets and much more. Plus also a fps gamers wet dream of custumizing everthing when it comes to numbers of terrorists/suspects, how tough they are eg. how good aim they have etc. And also for those who likes to play maps with some variety, theres even possibliy to set time of day, so you can play either morning, evening, afternoon, or night. Down to the hour of the day. And each map, one or the group - can select spawn location on the map. Think it's a total of 4 different spawn locations/sites. And Ground Branch has many maps to choose from. There's also many players that are custumizing MODs to the game. But haven't personly digged much into that. Game right now, I think it's still alpha, or early version at least. But looks very promissing. The fundamental structure seems to be more or less in place. But could be cool to see how this game could evolve in the future. Like I mentioned, so could some of the graphics be improved. Higher textures, graphic effects like RTX reflections, fog/mist, weather system (light goes trough skies down to the earth) vulmeric light (dustparticles you see in a ray of light etc. what modern games uses. Some of the sound samples could also be improved I think. When walking, the sound of the shoes inside eg. an airplane, are a bit like tap-dancing shoes. Most improvements in the game, would be cosmetic changes in graphic and sound I think. There could maybe also be some changes in the game, but kind of hard to put my finger on what those changes should be exactly. Maybe some helping guides when inside the loadout, so you perhaps get a text or small pop-up if you eg. custumized a weapon, where the flashlight are not working because it's pointing directly to a scope or similar situations. So one can't custumize weapons with errors like that. Kind of guiding-system. Maybe also inside the game, it can sometimes feel like the older games, when it seems a bit binary if an enemy hits you or not. If you get shot at you often dies. Would like to feel also, there's something in between either get killed or you got lucky not to get shot. I'm talking about if there were also hand-shoulder-leg etc. wounds. So it feels less binary, but more realistic with you also get wounds. You have a energy/damage bar in the game, so you don't dies instantly of only one bullet. But often it feel like you can't get much more than one bullet. Don't exspect that one should be superman and survive a lot of bullets. But maybe a bit more energy or less damage than what it is in the game current version. But overall this game is very interesting. And will keep you on your toes. Thanks. GG.
