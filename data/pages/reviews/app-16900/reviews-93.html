\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am on the fence about Ground Branch. I like this "game" but unfortunately it is hard to recommend. It still feels like a tech demo more than a finished game. The game has PVP, Terrorist Hunt, and Intel Retrieval. Terrorist hunt is the same (mostly) as R6 Terrorist Hunt, and Intel Retrieval is Terrorist Hunt but you have to find a laptop somewhere on the map. There's no campaign, in depth mission structure, mission planning, or AI team mates. Also the enemy AI is pretty bare bones, and they haven't implemented a prone stance. If you are looking for this to be a new version of old R6 you are going to be disappointed.

What Ground Branch has going for it is decent graphics, in depth weapon customization, and in depth kit customization. Weapons and gear are also rendered with a sense of authenticity and detail rarely seen in many "milsim" games. But for me this only takes the experience so far, and leaves me wanting a more complete product. I would trade many of these customization's, if it meant adding in a campaign or structured missions.

This is in early access and all these things are subject to change as the game nears release. If they flesh out this game with missions or a campaign, then my opinion is likely to change as well. For now however I can't recommend Ground Branch.\t\t\t\t\t
