\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHits the spot just right!

This game is a true hidden gem.
The only downside so far is the performance with many other players.

The AI is as difficult as you (or the server host for that matter) wants it to be. It can be from totally retarded to near impossible. There are surely improvements to be made though, but the devs are working on it.

In regards to movement speed there are 3 ways:
Walk, which is really slow for those tight spots,
Run, which is running with your gun up, so you can still fire if you get surprised,
Sprint, not much faster than running, but it takes a while for you to be able to shoot

The Controls were easy to learn despite the game offering quiet a number of things you can do with your operator. It's not overly complicated, but the interaction Icons appear to be Xbox right now. No problem though, it's only the "interact" button, so you won't get confused.

Graphically Idk what some people are mad about. It looks good. The lighting is a bit crazy at night, as anything where there isn't a direct light source is just pitch black. Surely there can be some trickery to illuminate dark rooms just a little. Or it may also be a deliberate Design choice, so you will use those NVGs at night.

Sound quality of Teammates was generally good, I never had trouble understanding any of them. Most players are working together so far and don't pull solo numbers in Coop. Usually the Coop Servers are more on the challenging side too.

All in all, I enjoy this game a lot! You can just go ahead and gun down some AI, or have yourself a challenge! Whatever suits your current desire.\t\t\t\t\t
