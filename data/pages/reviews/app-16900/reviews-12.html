The Good:

The gun play is nice and the animations for everything from shooting to reloading are exceptionally well done.

Being able to adjust your stance and your carry from alert to high carry is a neat little feature.

Leaning is a functional aspect that does actually help, unlike many games that have it but you find yourself never using it because it is just a gimmick.

The sounds are good (suppressors sound real and just suppress) and the graphics are beautiful.

The customization is really nice, you can even customize the pouches on your gear, which have a real world affect on your load out (like changing a grenade pouch to a mag pouch gives you more magazines and you lose a grenade), which is one of my favorite features.

It requires tactical thinking and is pretty realistic in the ways a game should be.

The Bad:

It could definitely use a better mission briefing. The missions don't really give you enough information on what exactly you are there to do. In real life, before an operation, you get what is called a 5 Paragraph Order. It is a several hour long brief with everything from infil, exfil, plans B through H, layouts, the 5 W's. This gives you a map with a sentence or two with extremely vague information like, find the intel and extract. What intel? Am I looking for documents or computers? Are they in a safe or is a guy carrying them? No layouts for structures, no intel on what the enemy force is comprised of or where they are concentrated, just a simple map showing the area you will be.

The AI isn't the best. You can adjust its "intelligence", but it isn't that smart, and on easy it doesn't hardly react to your presence at all other than to say "It's one of them!"

Grenades... oh my God, are grenades bad and by far the worst aspect of this game. I can't tell you if they work like they would in real life because aiming and throwing them is absolutely broken. In a dozen missions, I have never successfully thrown a grenade through a door. You will stand a foot in front of an open doorway, aim right in the middle of the open doorway, and the grenade will still manage to bounce off something in the doorway and blow up in your face. Please, for the love of God, fix the grenades.\t\t\t\t\t
