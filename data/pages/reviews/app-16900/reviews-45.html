I should have done more research before purchasing this game. The game has been in development for over 12 years (Kickstarter project 670743543) and yet it is still in early access. I don't believe this game will ever be completed, and I am of the opinion that the game is correctly described as a glorified "dress up shooter".

I should have done more research and discovered that the game is over a decade in development without full release, and had I done so I would not have made the purchase.

PREVIOUS REVIEW:
"Right now I must give a negative review as I can not recommend buying this game as it offers very little at the price point ($42.95 AUD), and I could only recommend buying the game for the sole purpose of supporting the development team. To put this price in perspective, Arma 3 (base) is currently $44.95 AUD before discount and, whilst it is an older game to be sure, at least that is both a complete and popular title.

Full disclosure, I did try and refund the game, however the refund request was rejected. The game Q&A states that the game will realistically be in Early Access for 18-24 months, however to put this in perspective Early Access began on the 14th of August 2018 while it is now December 2023.

Body armour is still just a cosmetic, AI is dubious, hit detection is dubious, feature release time frames on the Ground Branch website seem meaningless, and there is really no clear indication as to when anything will be ready for us; the customers."\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood, but development is agonizingly slow, and seems to prioritize fluff as much as core gameplay aspects. New weapons and character models are put into the game before systems like armor or first aid, or new gamemodes like survival or raid; combined with the lengthy development cycles it means that it feels like it takes forever for anything meaningful in the game to be added.

The game itself is decent, with some of the most satisfying gunplay I have ever had the pleasure of experiencing in a videogame and an expansive customization system for weapons, but the otherwise authentic and satisfying experience is unfortunately ruined by the enemy AI. They are essentially dumb-as-rocks terminators, who will stand around and take bullets from you until you enter their engagement range, at which point they will instantly headshot you. If you are in a building, they will all march in the same exact doorway their compatriots and get shot, at which point more of their friends will repeat the process. They will not flush you out with grenades, suppress you with automatic weapons, or flank you. They will simply walk towards you and die. V1034 appears to fix some of these issues, but I will reserve my judgement until the stable release of the branch comes out.

The reason I am giving this game a recommend is because I did, in the end, have fun with my friends. The experience was enjoyable, for me, even despite the complaints listed above. I would, perhaps, wait for a sale though, 30 dollars is a bit steep for the current state of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
