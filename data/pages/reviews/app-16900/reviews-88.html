\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGround Branch is currently the best tactical/milsim experience out there currently. In a world full of Ready or Not's, Zero Hour's, and a ton of other offshoots, GB just does it best. That's not to say the other games are bad, most of them are very good and worth a play, but the open-endedness of GB makes the replay value skyrocket. It lacks in content, but the content it has is A+. Amazing weapon and character customization, great open ended maps with harsh CQB environments (GB is the only game where i can swap from a high magnification optic to a holographic and still wish i had the other), stellar gun SFX, near perfect reload animations (really, its almost on par with the newer modern warfare ones), and an arguably satisfactory community. You rarely run into children who want to play this like a high octane shooter and most of the people who populate the servers are nice and regulars (aside from a couple, biodiversity seems to host the younger immature players and abusive admins). Updates are infrequent, but are very dense and i can appreciate that. There are usually a handful of game altering changes alongside a map rework or a new gun. No complaints here, but it can be aggravating for people accustomed to the "Games as a service" format that ground branch doesn't really follow, updates are released when there is enough content and it is ready. The only gripes i have are 1. AI, which is getting a complete overhaul and to be frank, its not bad enough to ruin an experience in the first place. And 2. Performance. Its just not great.

What I love most about this game is i can launch it, click on a server with about 5 people, and have a great time 99% of the time. I cant get that from ready or not, which is a game i still love, but that community is so horrible and full of john wick enthusiasts, you simply cannot get into a lobby that isn't modded and has 11 people in it and god forbid you get into one where people know what they're doing.

You'll see arguments about how it "isn't ready" or to wait on purchasing and honestly if you know what you want from a tactical shooter then you will have an absolute blast with this game and i dont see a reason to wait on playing it.\t\t\t\t\t
