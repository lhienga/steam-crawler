\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally nails the mechanics and feel on the shooting aspect, but that loadout editor... Whoa. What a great idea. Half the fun I've had with ground branch has been getting all my gear just right. Of course the gun customization is on point, but I really love all the options for clothing, colors and customization all the way down to the pouches on your carrier or belt. That's the depth I'm looking for.

All this game needs is GSG-9 boots and some drippy desert night camo shorts so we can operate with our thighs out. And some MCX Spear variants. I don't know if they have thought about it, but I hope the devs really go all-in on the gear editor and give us some sick vintage kit like flight suits and tactical vests so we can live out our 80's Seal Team VBSS fantasies. No gay skull masks or clown suits, just cool, authentic gear. The shooting mechanics are really on point in this game, but that gear editor is really something special.

I highly recommend Ground Branch if you enjoyed the real OG Rainbow Six games.\t\t\t\t\t
