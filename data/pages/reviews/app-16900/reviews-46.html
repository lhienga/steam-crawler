\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're on the fence between RoN and Ground Branch: (tldr: Buy RoN.)
- Ground Branch doesn't have AI teammates and you will not want to play this solo (reasons below)
- Ground Branch's enemy AI is notoriously bad. They can not take cover (because they still haven't made any cover animations), have very basic enemy behavior (when you fire your first shot, they will look like robots as they all walk towards you in unison via the quickest route possible, a straight beeline), are incredibly stupid (sometimes their beelining offensive strat will cause them to walk into a corner, resulting in them being stuck walking in that same corner ad infinitum), yet have impeccable accuracy and superhuman perception which allows them to drop you through foliage from half a kilometer away.
- Ground Branch, despite its kevlar vests, doesn't actually have a functioning bullet mitigating armor system (AFAIK it's purely cosmetic)
- Ground Branch runs like hot ass, albeit consistently, and makes RoN, despite its own performance/stuttering issues, look well-optimized.
- Ground Branch, despite running like complete garbage, looks like a high school student's blender demo which is even more infuriating as you get a rock solid 50 fps on your mid-range PC.
- Ground Branch has probably two or three enemy models in the entire game. (every soldier in any Eastern EU map looks exactly the same)
- Crouch leaning in Ground Branch will offset your front sights slightly off center (not a big grievance when using rifles, but good luck aiming with pistols; you can't even see your front sight)
- Aside from its most cutting edge mil-sim dress up mini-game, Ground Branch has no meaningful SP content. Although it is available, it will probably keep you entertained for maybe 5 mins then put you in a brainrot stupor due to cognitive paralysis and sensory deprivation.
- Ground Branch's maps are horrendously ugly and a plain bore to play due to the tedious and incredibly un-engaging missions. Most of the time, you will have to make up your own fun via Tier One operator ERP with other like-minded people. There's a reason why most of the community servers play that Neptune Spear map 24/7.
- A complaint that is frequently echoed by the Ground Branch community which perfectly encapsulates the Ground Branch experience, paraphrased: "Whenever there's a new update, I find that I hop on for 10 mins, customize my character, and quit the game. I don't touch it again for months until the next update." (Why would you even keep something like this installed?)\t\t\t\t\t
