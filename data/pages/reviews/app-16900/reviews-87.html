There's a huge emphasis on teamwork; if you go solo you're much, much more likely to die. The attention to detail on the weapons and gear is spectacular, and the transitions between weapons, reloads and optics is seamless. Even NVGs function as they would in real life with bloom, unable to look through certain optics, etc. Extremely realistic, it's very clear the developers did their research.

The only con I can think of is the AI. I haven't had terrible experiences, but the voice acting and AI mechanics leave something to be desired. Overall it's a great game and I can't wait to see how the developers continue to build it.\t\t\t\t\t
