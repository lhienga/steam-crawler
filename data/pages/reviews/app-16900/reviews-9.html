\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I want to make it clear. I love this game. I've played it since it first came to steam. But, as of V1034, the game has just gotten worse. The problem begins with the pistol. It jumps around like a wacky waving inflatable arm flailing tube man when you fire. Then, we have the SMGs and carbines/assault rifles. Which, fire exactly the same. Barely a difference in feel. One just shoots faster. Though, for some reason I find a bit of AK bias, as the AK guns in this game are disgustingly accurate. Not like the shotguns which have infinite range. But, still accurate enough to really not represent just how an AK actually fires. I'm not talking single fire either. You can full auto targets from the same distance you can shot bucksh... I mean snipe them from. The m16a1 has way more recoil than it actually does, and the AR platform weapons along with the UMP feel like you're firing a BAR. Yet, I'm convinced a bug-a-salt gun (the ones that literally shoot salt) have more killing power than the SMGs. The rifles on the other hand have this stupid artificial feeling jolt when they shoot (along with the UMP.) I get that they're trying to simulate shooting these guns while aiming down sights. But, they really simulate aiming down sights when not holding your gun correctly. It's like you gave a five your old that somehow has dementia a rifle. The operators literally feel like they haven't fired a weapon before. You're playing as Special Activities Division operators. It should feel like it. Prone is nice, though.

That's my thoughts. TLDR: Guns feel terrible. Prone is nice though.\t\t\t\t\t
