\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHeld off on giving this title a review but after half a decade of little progress, the devs seem to be increasingly focusing their efforts on inconsequential things, such as removing flags from the game (as if the game didn't already lack enough of an identity) and copying Ready or Not's aimbot AI (that no one likes).

At it's core, it's basically just a knockoff R6 Vegas 2, but with no campaign (no single player at all really), just co-op terrorist hunt. This game's claim to fame is supposed to be it's customization but after countless updates, only AR15 variants can fully take advantage of the weapon customization system. You can also customize your uniform, but like I said, they're actively removing features from it so who knows what else they'll remove later down the line. The enemies have no personality and just sort of lay down when they die, no blood or anything, so it's not even fun to kill them.

Ultimately, the game has no identity and the fact that the devs are censoring any criticism of the game indicates that it will likely be in development hell forever. For the price of this game, you could probably buy all R6 games that were made in the 2000s so just do that instead.\t\t\t\t\t
