The Good:
This game is great, the base mechanics are solid, weapon builder is on point with the over all operator builder being well built with an acceptable level of customization.

The Bad:
-This game was released in 2018, I started playing in 2022 it is now 2024. I can't really say I see a difference in the game at all, and that's a problem. I want this game to succeed but there seems to be no real traction on the dev side. They put out steam updates posts every now and again but I still don't see a difference. No new attachments, weapons, camos, equipment, maybe a map or two , and recently one mode.
-The AI is still a non threat 85% of the time, barely able to navigate the map for longer than 4 seconds. They have near zero reaction to the player shooting at them or their buddies dying. They are painfully slow to react to you even when you don't try to play it safe. This game would be god tier with reactive AI that communicates and moves like those of F.E.A.R. the poster child for great gun play AI.
-The game has "mod support", but this would be drastically better if they made it steam workshop support, making it simple to install mods and maps and allowing the community to do the dev work the devs are lacking in.
-The player base on public servers tends to go for a sprint through mentality with zero communication which breaks the dismal AI further. This game plays best when you have a dedicated group to take the game slow and smooth.
-There's no real tools or equipment to use so you cant really play a breacher role or others.
Also why do the highcut helmets look like bowls?

If you fully understand what this game currently is and have a number of people that want to seriously play it, then get it and have fun. Otherwise don't get this until there's a full game.\t\t\t\t\t
