\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's alright. As it is now I'm already bored of it. There's 12 maps and two game modes. PvE AI is absolutely garbage but the devs are working on adding a whole new system which should be good. Pick up games with strangers are beyond terrible as you'd expect, the maps are clearly made for 4-6 player teams and the vast majority of servers run 16. The missions are over in two minutes every time.

Messing with gun customization is fun. It's even more advanced than Tarkov in certain points to be honest, which surprised me. Right down to where the magazines are in your carrier, you can have it set up however you want. Impractical or otherwise.

If you have three like-minded, autistic friends you can convince to buy this you might have fun for ten hours or so. Ready or Not is a more complete package and for $10 more on sale I'd recommend that over this any day of the week. The developers mean well, and they're a small team, so I can't hate on them, but it seems like this game is just a hobby for them that they put a couple hours into on the weekends. I can't see any other reason they made this little progress after so many years.

I wish there was a "meh" or "mixed" review, because it's not bad but it's definitely not good. This game needs a PvP scene so, so badly. I can barely find a single server running that game mode. Instead it's just running against brain dead bots while playing tactical barbie. Insurgency Sandstorm ISMC is way better for anybody that wants that kind of gameplay.

Keep in mind, however, that while this is just a skeleton for a great game, once it's fleshed out and adds the essentials to maintain a steady playerbase, this will be _the_ tactical shooter to play. That's why I haven't refunded it, because I have faith down the line it'll be a mainstay in my game rotation. As it is now, it's barely a tech demo.\t\t\t\t\t
