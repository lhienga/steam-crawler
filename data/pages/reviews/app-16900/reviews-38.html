\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun to play the maps the first time and build different classes. After some time the game gets kinda boring, reasons why;

- Game modes: There is only 2 game modes, terrorist hunt and Intel retrieval. Since the bots are super basic and dumb t hunt gets boring fast. Intel retrieval is basically t hunt, nothing special. There is no story, lore or progression that comes from doing missions, basically you gain nothing from playing. FIX: Implement more game modes like bomb defuse, hostage rescue, active threat or defense. Progression should be implemented so you have something to gain or unlock more game mods/maps.

- Bots: Its the most basic form of AI's, they are complete dumb asses with aim bot. The default maps are great and some large too, but the AI don't utilize the map. They just stand around like dumb asses and when alerted they just pile up and all of a sudden you have killed whole 30 bots. FIX: Give them Combat drill scripts like suppressive fire (and the longer you are static the more accurate the shots are), flanks or fire and movement (where "they communicate" suppressive fire team and one moving team). Also giving them better concealment like mounts and prone.

- Mods: You have to replace items for a different skin resulting in less in depth mods. Some mods require a tool to change in game code. There aren't any custom maps yet. There is a lot of mods but they aren't really game changing or making the game better, its more of the aesthetic. (I know mod support is promised)

The rest of the game is what you expect; great gun play, sound, realistic movement (prone is coming next update V1034) and it looks great. You could buy it for the early access price or discount and play a bit then wait for more content or full release. It also works great for a cqb sim to play with friends, but for solo players it will get boring fast. Just dont expect a full game.

(Most fixed after update)\t\t\t\t\t
