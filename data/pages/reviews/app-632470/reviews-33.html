\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBackstory -

The first question that came into my mind when writing this review, Where do I start ? After spending a lot of time thinking, I thought why not start from the beginning ? The first time I heard about the game was when i was watching all the previous game awards and I saw this game won several awards and even the most important ones like 'Best narrative' and 'Best role playing game'. I initially didn't pay much attention as i'm not really into indie games and prefer much more complex gaming mechanics over simpler ones like Disco Elysium. Then some time later during the steam winter sale, I saw this game at a 75% discount and was instantly convinced to buy it ; and so I did. I bought it, and to this day i never have once regretted that decision.

Game play and Features-

To begin with, Disco Elysium is not really like your typical indie games which are straight forward and the player has to progress through the story or so called levels and there is no going back to the previous checkpoint unless it's part of the story. Disco Elysium is a role playing indie game that is why it gives us freedom to roam in the featured open world and interact with characters to progress through the story. This game has a greater emphasis on the character abilities. The game also has some presets for the character which you can use directly or you can customize the character abilities as you see fit. Throughout the game you can you can upgrade the character abilities with experience points that you gain. The game also has a feature called The Thought Cabinet which takes in game time to solve the questions that your character has and provides the solution when you spend the required amount of time in game. It comes at an disadvantage when the thought is still in process and reduces some of your abilities for a temporary time but once the thought is processed, it provides the character with additional perks and abilities. Clothing is also an important part of the game and with it you can customize the appearance of the character and it also comes with a lot of upgrades and downgrades which are equally balanced. Game play mechanics are quite simple.

My Thoughts-

Coming on towards the actual review, this is where i actually share my thoughts about the game. Disco Elysium for me was one the best gaming experiences I have ever had. Quite a few games live up to my ideals and this is one of them. Although i had thought very little of the game when I first started playing it but to my surprise, it far exceeded my own expectations. I mostly play single player story driven games and from that experience I have observed one thing about single player games and it is that the game which succeeds in bringing new game play mechanics those unknown before and a new way of story telling are the ones that attract the most attention and provide us with a new experience never known before. Disco Elysium has included all the attributes that are responsible for the actions a person takes. The attributes listed in disco are all responsible for the functioning of the human body, and to bring such a feature into a RPG sure is a work of art. The person who designed the attributes sure is an extraordinary one to bring such features to the game, I'm obliged to say that no sane person can ever achieve this, he has to be INSANE. Most of the game is in our head and i haven't experienced anything like this before. The voice actors that voiced our character's inner voice really have breathtaking voice's, all of them do. Hearing them talk is like therapy for my ears, So deep and beautiful the voice is . This game is one of the games that really challenged my conscience and took a pick at it in a amazing way. Every choice i was given I chose it with great care, it was like i was in the protagonist's shoes no even better it was like i was the protagonist. The other NPC's from the game were also great and fun to talk with. One of the other features that i liked the most was the dice system where you had to perform checks with a possibility in order to advance. This made the game more fun for me though sometimes a bit frustrated. The actual story was overall very great but the side quests and the way of advancing through the story were unlike anything I have ever experienced. Never did I ever felt tired or bored while playing this game. I recommend not playing any other games while playing this one, although this does not hinder with the game play experience, it is really hard to stop playing this game once started as it is strangely immersive. I never thought of playing any other game once i started this one and only wanted to experience more of it. I quote it as "One of the most philosophical game ever made" and it really makes you question some things about your life. The protagonist is on the task of solving a crime but I think that's only the half of it. He actually is on a journey of self-exploration and even YOU can join on with him and this is the best thing about this beautiful game.\t\t\t\t\t
