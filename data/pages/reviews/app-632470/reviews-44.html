Everything is great, the only things that kept it from a 10 for me were these three points.
The killer - I won't say much for spoilers, but didn't seem well done.
Navigation - Knowing what you can and can't interact with is a bit of a hassle, even with the investigate mode.
Objective Tracking - The game wouldn't remove a update or mission when the mission or check became impossible to complete (like breaking down a door I already opened with a key), making it seem like I had more to do than I actually had and making me backtrack to things I couldn't do.

On the good things, the writing and voice acting are absolutely S tier. Music as well. Art is amazing as well. The character building is super unique too, your friends play-through may look very different than yours, which is so cool in a game that is so story driven.

I recommend this game to anyone who can lean back and just enjoy the ride - and also roleplay a bit into a character.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a masterpiece. Introspective, hilarious, melancholy, absurd, Disco Elysium is one of my favorite gaming experiences.

Despite its excellence, this game is not for everyone. At the minimum you must like to read (or be read at), because you will be doing a ton of it. You must be patient, because you will enter a world about which you know nothing and your classes will be taught by a gaggle of pretentious political science post-grads. You must be willing to empathize with trauma and suffering, because Revachol is full of it. And on a practical level, you need to be okay with point-and-click style gameplay - you will run around, inspect objects, and talk to people. Even then, the game has basically nothing in the way of mechanical puzzles, and the entire experience hinges on the strength of its writing and story. That writing is excellent: frequently unhinged, aimlessly encyclopedic, flippantly spiritual, somehow across everything it drips with the most flair I've ever seen in a disco-themed detective mystery.

There is a lot that could be said about everything the game does right, and so for the sake of brevity I will end with this: Disco Elysium is the best version of itself. 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
