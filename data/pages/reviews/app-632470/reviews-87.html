However being a ravish drunk, high on speed, you kinda left the corpse out back rotting while you were blackout wasted. From here you gain control over the main character and get to play, shape and mold them into the detective you find most appealing (or funny) to play.

You will then begin your investigation by examining and talking to people to gather clues, travel to places to look for evidence and so on.

What makes this game stand out as an RPG and a detective game is both the world and its vivid and interesting characters. You will be talking with people that are almost always a joy to talk to. This is a game where talking to characters, as the main gameplay, is incredibly fun and rewarding. The dialog choices you get to make are insane, and they will both affect how someone views you and how you are able to extract information from suspects and witnesses.

You know a game is good when you can divert an entire conversation to talking about your favorite boxer. Or how you can go on a date and be ranting about politics non-stop.
