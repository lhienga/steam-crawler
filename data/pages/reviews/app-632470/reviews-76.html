\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is probably the best game I've ever played. It undoubtably has the best writing of any game I've ever played. It's a game for people who enjoy exploring and unfolding the world before them. This game is light on puzzles and has no combat mechanics at all. But... you're a detective. You're all about investigating things, solving the mystery.

So it's probably not for you if you place heavy emphasis on watching numbers go up, watching yourself improve on a scoreboard, or running around and killing everything. And that's OK. But for people who enjoy the narrative, exploring a world and unraveling mysteries and secrets, this is the game for you.

This game is incredibly dense and dialog heavy, but with the release of the Final Cut, all of that is voiced so you don't have to read an entire library's worth of words anymore.

Disco Elysium isn't a game for 'everyone', and it's not trying to be. But what niche it fills it fills better than any other game I've ever seen. And if you're even remotely interested, I recommend playing it whether you get it on sale or not. It's absolutely worth the 40 dollars.\t\t\t\t\t
