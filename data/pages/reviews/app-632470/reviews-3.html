A really fun and immersive RPG in which you take on the role of a drunk and depressed dude who used to be supercop.

There is a lot of dialogue, and I mean a *LOT*! Your thoughts can actually *speak* to you and influence the way your character thinks. Also you might get political thoughts and in this case ignore them and be a proud compromise, the centrist!!!!

What I like about this game is that almost everything you do matters! The coat that you wore for its stats? A random NPC will comment about it! The really important personal stuff you said to a random person? That person will then use it against you!
When you find yourself in a certain event, there is an option to do something with a dice roll. Even when you fail the roll, something interesting and sometimes even useful stuff can happen! Failed rolls aren't actually *failures* but more like "I fell down from running away from a manager, and now he's making me pay less for the damages I caused!"

I haven't found a single thing to dislike about this game.. yet. (will probably edit later)
