\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn times when there is abundance of mediocre writing, Disco Elysium, with its exceptional narrative and incredibly well-developed characters, feels like a breath of fresh air. Despite considering myself quite jaded by now, the game thoroughly captivated me. Disco Elysium marks my first foray into CRPGs (at least from what I can remember), and never I have been so engrossed in a story.

While it's labelled as a detective RPG, personally, the experience felt very relaxing to me, almost akin to a visual novel. The game's heavy reliance on dialogue is expertly executed to ensure it never feels monotonous. The dialogues are a perfect blend of hilarity and though provoking moments. The game is cleverly paced so that, despite being narrative heavy, it never got overwhelming to me. All the lore and ideas in the game feel very novel and immersive. The choices in the game has real weight into them, and there are real consequences for your actions. The mysteries within the game unravel seamlessly, providing a satisfying conclusion. You will end up finding yourself reflecting on your experience with the game once you finish it.

For those seeking an experience that challenges their psyche and leaves them feeling intellectually simulated, this masterpiece is a must play.\t\t\t\t\t
