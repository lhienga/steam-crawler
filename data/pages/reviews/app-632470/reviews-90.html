\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a work of art. This is also the closest a game has gotten to emulating how my ADHD brain works. Caution if you have troubles reading or focusing... this is a LOT of reading and focusing. I advise taking this one slow, taking breaks here and there. Some people can do this one in a few hours but it took me a few days because I wanted to experience it, and it was definitely an experience.

It also wrenches my soul to know the creators of that sentiment were robbed of this creation... yet it almost validates this games entire point. Yes there's more to the story but flat out the people responsible for leading this project do not profit from your purchase, nor will they be involved in any future installments. If there's a way to profit Kurvitz, Rostov and Hindpere, the original creators, instead then do it if you want. If there's a way to play this game without profiting ZA/UM then do it if you want. I'd already bought my copy long before I learned and in the end I don't think any of them NEED your money... but we can't seem to have successful art that calls to people without vultures picking at it's bones AND each other and lapping up your sentiment as their "comfortable living." Those with their hands directly on the paintbrushes already got paid for their work, be it fairly or not, and everyone else is now snapping at the IP pieces.\t\t\t\t\t
