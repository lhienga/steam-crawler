DISCLAIMER: The game is amazing. The politics behind it are not. The best way to obtain the game would be in sailing the seven seas. Not gonna talk about any of that in this review, just the game.

Right. I usually wait until I've put a good amount of time into a game or 100%ed it before I write my review, but Disco Elysium is different.

This game is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ amazing. There's no other way to put it. While it may not be the game for everyone, I highly recommend and encourage you to try it. It is an experience that no other game delivers, and one you definitely won't forget.

Visuals are superb. This game has such a unique visual style, and one that leaves just the right amount to imagination. Voice acting is top notch, genuinely some of the best voice acting I've heard in a game for a while. It'll make you want to not skip dialogue just to listen to it. The story will grip you in and make you want to finish the whole dang thing while savouring every moment. I really can't do justice about how good everything in this game is. There's just so much choice, everything feels like it has so many different outcomes, the setting is amazing with a gritty world, set in a completely different 50s to us, executed perfectly. Characters portrayed perfectly, with everyone being very different from the last character.

Play the game. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
