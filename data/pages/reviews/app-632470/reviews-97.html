\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA captivating story heavy game with endless amounts of writing.

I play games to have a sense of control over my character. I've played through many where I get to mold my character into the person they are, and this is one where you get to decide what happens. You choose your actions, and every line that you say will have a consequence in the future. Because of your background, you still get some freedom to choose any line you want - whether it be some hilarious line or a serious inquiry into the investigation. All of it matters, and all of it is so well connected together, even the side quests that may not seem like much at first.

The world may be small, but it is DENSE. It is packed so tightly that you can go to the same place ten times and still have quests and hidden secrets to do there. There is just so many incredible moments and a-ha! moments that will make you feel ecstatic about every discovery you make. The sheer number of dialogue and content, especially with the final cut voice overs, makes this an immersive and completely engulfing experience that makes you feel raw emotion for every character within.

There are some publisher issues with ownership, yes, but if you enjoy these story games - like a choose your own adventure book, then this game is a must play.

**Also, don't be afraid to fail a check.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted to love this game. I was fascinated from the very first scene (I managed to die there and I am like WOW! Naaasty detective hardcore!) But then..
all the way, you hope that this mess will end, after which the main plot will finally begin, and then the credits appear. that's it.

this game is kinda like a sandwich with
- a perfect world with solid design and charismatic characters
- an interesting beginning story with a discouragingly weak middle and the end
- not an RPG at all. the cake is a lie. No matter how you set up and play, it's always the same personality (just admit it to yourself, admirer Tequila Sunset)
- gameplay consists of text windows for 90%
- a lot of 'choice' but it doesn't change anything, I mean dialog branches
- dialog branches refer to non-existent (in your version of the world) events, it's like a text bug (wtf!)
- some scenes are still bugged either
- throwing dice for nothing
- a perfect world with solid design and charismatic characters

TLDR a boring, tedious beautiful dummy in an insanely attractive world

and such a huge number of positive reviews say that people are ready to sell their soul for a raw product with a perfect world, solid design, and charismatic characters. ok. if this is your choice, feel free to get it.

P.S. Don't listen to these stories about za/um etc. it's just the Pale.
