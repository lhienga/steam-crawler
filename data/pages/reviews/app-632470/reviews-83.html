\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most moving games I ever played. Main protagonist is absolutely unforgettable character and I literally laughed and (almost) cried as he went on a journey to rediscover himself. His personal story and murder mystery are incredibly well interwoven and both are equally engaging. Detective story unravels at a satisfying pace, with well written and credible twists and turns and enough drama to make it all feel worthwhile. I loved that game allows for all kinds of ridiculous lines and self-sabotage attempts (disco superstar and doom prophet being my personal favorites). Other characters in the game are also well-written, with sharp and fresh humor, mix of satire and hard reality, all together building deprived yet goofy world.
The only weakness of the story are failed attempts to describe the Ravachol and its surroundings in detail. There is simply too many descriptions (meat), but no solid base (bone) to put them on, so they mostly fall flat. I felt that those parts are unnecessarily long and don't bring any real value.
RPG element is original and super fun. Both internalizing thoughts and basic characteristics blend well with protagonists development and is exciting. Choosing to be a dumb brute with sixth sense or highly intelligent but emotionally fragile soul, both paths are equally fun and rewarding.
This is 10/10 for me, particularly on the storytelling side, which I value the most in general.
