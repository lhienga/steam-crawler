Before playing this type of game, one should prepare for the lack of traditional game-play and instead be ready for deep dialogue, processing vital information, and discovering memorable characters in an unforgettable setting. DE made me laugh, conceptualize and understand different ideologies, while keeping me focused on the task at hand. You are detective and there's a killer on the loose. Your task is to find who killed the victim. There are options to use your intellect, psyche or strength to overcome the obstacles the world will throw at you. All have their advantages and drawbacks but the options are what make this journey riveting. Overall, I must say I really enjoyed this game, but it will take considerable effort especially if these type of games are not your cup of tea.\t\t\t\t\t
