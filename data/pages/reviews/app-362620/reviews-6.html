\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will edit this later to give detail but in short, I don't even know how this game is POSSIBLE, much less created by a single developer.
The game has EVERYTHING!

You can play in so many different ways. You can be a service provider of sorts, you can invest in companies, you can invest in the general stock market that's unrelated (as far as I know) to the companies in game...

You can get pre-built buildings or you can build/modify your own. The build mode is as complex as it needs to be without being overwhelming. It reminds me of The Sims but seems more versatile.

The things you can do in this game are too many to list so I'll just say, dive in and try to get into it bit by bit.

I've been playing for about three days now and I've lost too much sleep and rearranged too many priorities to keep playing.

This game is ADDICTIVE if it catches you. Proceed with (delightful) caution.\t\t\t\t\t
