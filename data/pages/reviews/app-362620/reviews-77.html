\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely love this game, even more so as time has gone on.

I bought this game in June of 2015, at that time, it was so simple and still, I loved it.

The Dev has done an amazing job to create a compelling little software management game that, while still in development 6 years later, has a lot to offer at an extremely reasonable price. Not to mention, the modding community is pretty active with a lot of very nice content mods that help patch up some sections of the game you may feel is lacking, or simply add some much needed customization in the form of additional wall elements, decorative elements like kitchenette sets and so on, just a lot to offer overall in the modding scene.

There are obviously issues, some glaring problems and much needed "quality of life" features like better ways to show rooms that lack fire alarms and sprinklers, difficulty tweaks, and I'm sure other things I can't think of right now.

I hope to see this game released relatively soon, if for nothing but to see what Otto has to show next. No matter what it is, I know I'll be giving it a try, based on the quality of what I've seen so far.\t\t\t\t\t
