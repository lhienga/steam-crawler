This game has always been one of my favourites, I love the aspects of managing businesses and the challenges that come with it and this game, in the early stages at least, is perfect for me. I find myself coming back time and time again and finding new ways to innovate and create new businesses and take them huge. However I will give this game some credit is still in beta but there are some short comings, one of my biggest problems with this game is the lack of companies/competitors/products and product lifecycle you could create an antivirus to last a million years and it will die within 10 years despite me updating every time no bugs no problems with it and it never gets past a few million active users. Same with operating systems they never get past 10 million active users at the VERY most which puts a cap on how far you can go.

One thing I love about this game though is the various ways of making money like I find myself creating online platforms and taking a cut and still making a profit. However there aren't enough companies in the game and they just go bankrupt after you reach 2 billion no one can catch up everyone is stuck under you if you wanted you could sell 99.9% of your company and you'd have no threat of taking over because no one could afford to. After a company reaches at most 1 billion all of a sudden despite releasing products that do 10x better than yours it's costs just go crazy high for no reason theres no taxes salaries couldn't go high the game just dooms them for no reason other than it can and that has always annoyed me I love the fight for the top spot but after I get it no one can take it from me.

I also feel annoyed about there seems to be a cap on the number of users that can use your product which is just annoying I want to become like as big as apple or microsoft and with every new release millions more flock to my product and as more companies come I have to really innovate to compete.

You can create incredible products with small teams which means you don't need to expand and have multiple offices which is annoying I wish I could have multiple offices for my empire. I feel there also needs to be political challenges and more challenges that could bankcrupt your trillion dollar empire and you don't just get stuck with no reason to carry on.

Small issues that bother me but not much:

Rented buildings are out of date for the newer updates which really sucks.

There is no CEO position.

Automation becomes tedious after a while. (You have to keep hiring new team leaders and can't automate it)

Lag

However despite all of these short comings and so many more I haven't mentioned I still ABOSULTUELY love this game and it is my favourite game of all time my second most played in terms of hours and as this game gets more updates I will continue to play even if it never got another update I don't think another game could top the experience this game gives I RECCOMEND this game fully to anyone and everyone this game will keep updating and keep going which is incredible and it's incredible it's a one-man operation which is just insane to me. I beg anyone who wants to get this game to just do it you're not gonna regret or dislike this game.\t\t\t\t\t
