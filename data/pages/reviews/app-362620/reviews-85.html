\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy first ever review on Steam – simply had to show my appreciation of this great game.
After recently rediscovering Roller Coaster Tycoon I had developed a sudden urge to play a newer business sim.

Software Inc. looked extremely interesting with stellar reviews, so I checked it out despite the early access stage.

I was completely overwhelmed by the sheer amount of details in this game right out of the gate.

Whether you want to rewrite history by starting Microsoft out of your garage in 1970 while revolutionizing the tech industry, be the next Epic Games and create the best game engine that other developers simply cannot avoid or just run the most successful video game company ever seen – this is the game for you!

As you go through the years of the technological age you have to build your company from the ground up while making the correct decisions, hiring the best fits, and managing your projects and your employees. Watch as you grow your business empire, build and expand your headquarters in rich detail while competing with other companies for the throne in your field.

Is a new upstart suddenly booming and taking your customers? You can buy them, take out the competition and everything they have made is now yours. Or do you need an influx of quick cash? Put some stock on the market for capital.

This game does not feel like a game in its alpha phase to me. I have put in many hours in my first week and have yet to encounter a single bug and I am only in year 1999. Graphics is not mind blowing but more than enough for this type of game. The project development takes a few hours to learn but once you got it, you’ll get rolling quick. I did have my first three upstarts fail so it was a steep learning curve.

A tycoon style game with a sprinkle of Sims and distant remnants of sports-management. Could not recommend enough if your drawn to the business sim genre like me. Possibly the best, most comprehensive I have played to date. There are so many options and routes to take and the list get only more expansive as you get further in the game\t\t\t\t\t
