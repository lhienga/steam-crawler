\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game a while ago. Every now and then I played it for a couple of fun hours but eventually it stops being fun because of some big problems.

First some pros:
- This game has very good dev support. It's just one dude but for the past years he has been updating and fixing the game. Something like this is quiet rare even for a EA game.
- The game is very detailled in it's mechanics. You can compare it to something like Game Dev Tycoon but this gives you so much more to do. It's not just games, it really is a software company.
*You can design and build your own office or just rent office space.
*You can handle your own distribution if you want. You can even distribute other software from other companies on your servers. You can also
*You can invest in other companies and they in yours
*You can have multiple teams, who work on different projects
-In my play time I have also seen very little to no bugs at all

Now some cons:
-You will find that past the mid game its very limited. While you can manage different teams and even apoint people who will lead these teams and hire new people, it's impossible to make make them do exactly what you want. You see, employees have certain skill in certain areas. When they have high skill in something they are doing its very good, but likewise when they have no skill it will affect the quality of your product. This becomes impossible to manage in bigger teams, let alone many different teams. It is this kind of micromanagment that makes it no longer fun. In a game like Factorio you are continiously upscaling but meanwhile you wil also unlock tools that help with the upscaling and mm. That is what makes Factorio so good and what Software inc. lacks. \\
I would love it if the dev would create a system where you no longer need to mm your employees to such a extent. Otherwise it is impossible to create an office with 500+ people.
-The gameplay is inherently repetitive. Once you find the right setup for a product and you are profitable, you can easily make million, and by upscaling billions. There was a time where you could just buy up new companies everytime they were created. Because out of every couple some would be very succesfull it was a no risk investment with high reward. This was patches but if you came to that point you were already set for life so to speak. There is really not much different to do. You can create different software, but in the end its just ticking different boxes.


While I like to start over every now and then with a new update I find myself walking into the same problems every time. It can be worth your money, and I don't regret buying it, but unless you have cash to spare I would come back in a couple years to check in on the game.

I do play this on a laptop so what could be a glitch to me or whatever performance issues I have may/may not pertain to you. I do however have a couple of things that could perhaps be added to the game before final release or perhaps on an update later.

How about the possibility of setting up "Executive Board Members". Every fortune 500 company has the aforementioned "Executive Board Members" and I feel like this game could use this. Especially when you surpass a certain amount of company worth. Somewhere around MULTI-Millions/Billions

Example:
President/CEO(Chief Executive Officer) - Since you create someone at the start of the game, they are essentially "president". However CEO should be added as well to make a President/CEO title. Or leave this as 2 different positions up until you strictly have to/forced to hire for the CEO position. CEO's are people that are usually responsible for the operation of the business. Could be used in day-to-day operations. Use him as the Project Manager since this person keeps certain projects going. Project Manager is the definition of CEO.

CFO-Chief Financial Officer - This person is usually in charge of everything pertaining to finances whether it be education, month-to-month income/risks, any kind of stocks/bonds, loans, etc. Anything having to do with money.

You could also add "Vice President" which would take care of and step up for the absence of the President. Having said that, I think the person you create should be sick in order for this to work. There's no "perfect society or situation" in-which yourself doesn't get sick. Lets be real about this.

I feel as if the pay for employees should be looked at. Perhaps bump the pay up as the company makes more money. At first the base pay is fine. However, the pay should be bumped up based on the employee education or/how long the employee has been there.

This is just silly at this point but what about adding helicopters or at lest limos, for the aforementioned "Executive Board Members". Do you seriously think "board member" are the type of people who drive themselves to work? Let's be real about this as well. Maybe add a helipad or valet service which now would employing a valet service.

There are a lot of things I do like about this game hence the reason why I have almost 400 hrs playing this game.

Another thing I think could be looked at is the option to get rid of any ponds/lakes that may show up in a custom map. I understand the "challenge" to having ponds but sometimes I just don't want to delete them and I'd like the option to delete the pond or better yet add the option to add ponds before your custom map uploads.

You may ask yourself if this game is any better than games like "Startup Company" I have to say, It does. However, each game has their strengths and weaknesses. Is it any better than any other "startup Company" type game? I'm not sure about that either. This is an amazing game though and I do find myself playing it more than any other game I have if that tells you anything.
