\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought Software Inc. a while ago and have about over 100 hours in this game. Now it's time to review this purchase: It was definitely worth it, why?

Reasons to buy:
- It does what it promises: An huge simulation of a software company
- Not only the software development is crucial, it combines a lot of needed fields and hence, feels very realistic. The HR system, the building system with all the (functional) assets and the hardware system
- Very relaxing to play, good to take a time off and dive deep in
- Train real skills, mostly in the fields of understanding what it all takes to lead a company and just giving some inputs on why employees and the market responds to something in a certain way
- 3D Game with nice graphics and controls
- There are many features adding to that that all in its mass make it a huge buy

Some negative points to consider:
- Recently there is a lot of focus toward hardware production, which is not my favorite topic in the game's context (just personal opinion)
- Some features need some small improvements
- The tutorial can be improved a bit as the game is very complex

All in all still a huge buy, I can only recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
