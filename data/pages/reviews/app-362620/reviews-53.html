\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing this game on and off over the last couple of years.

Really neat business simulator. With a great UI and lots of options that make building your stuff easy.
It's honestly mind boggling how a single person made such an accessible game with a nicer UI than a ton of other way more expensive games.

You got a couple of options on where you want to take your business like just doing contract work for other companies when one of your teams has some downtime. I honestly haven't tried out much of the hardware manufacturing or patenting you can do and never really got far enough in a game that i wanted to automate my sequel releases but you can scale up your company to huge levels and the game gives you the tools to do it without much micro (hiring and sequel releases can be automated and building new offices or buildings is really easy too once you got some blueprints).

I really liked how your software isn't done when you release it. You have to provide a support team for your users and have your developers fix the verified bugs and even update the software by increasing it's tech levels in these updates to increase its shelflife . And on top of that you can port it to different operating systems to reach a bigger market once something new becomes popular.

Marketing and printing of the disks can be outsourced to a publisher who gets a cut or you can hire the people and do it all yourself. And once you have the infrastructure you can keep them busy most of the time by doing contract work for other companies. They even have a digital distribution system on which you can release your competitors software for a cut of their profits.


I got to admit that i barely had any problems keeping my company afloat after the first releases so it feels a bit easy. But i only played on the default difficulty and i'd expect that the other difficulties will make it more challenging once you know what you're doing. If not there is always the workshop support which might help.

I rarely encountered any bugs in the early access stage and am really looking forward to play it with the workshop support and what ever else this mad man can come up with before the full release.
Would highly recommend for anyone interested in business simulators. Well worth the budget price.\t\t\t\t\t
