\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I bought this game not really sure if I was going to like it and being skeptical of it being a fun game. I was absolutely surprised once I gave it a go. I love tycoon/business management games and honestly this one is now near to top of my list, maybe even top of the list. Was blown away at the level of depth to this game, the amount of choices that can positively or negatively effect your outcome are immense. There is a learning curve but the tutorials are quite good in conveying how to play the game and manage all the pieces. I'm only 30ish hours in and can see that I will likely go well beyond that likely into the 100's of hours trying different strategies for my company. My first company almost went bankrupt as I misjudged how much of a financial cushion I needed before developing my first piece of software, while rapidly depleting my finances I made a major bet that my first piece of software would save the company. My bank balance dipped down below 10K barely covering payroll for my employees as I neared the finish line of releasing my first product. I thought I might have to fire employees and potentially go bankrupt, then at the last minute was able to release my first product and within a month of that low balance and edge of bankruptcy, the product began selling at a rapid pace and ended up with over 1 Million in profits the first month of release and saved the business. From there I began updating, patching, and porting the product over the next couple years and made over 20 million. This money laid the foundation of a hopefully bright future for my organization and my employees, allowing me to say goodbye to the garage I was renting, purchased a plot of land and built a nice HQ with plenty of room to grow as the future unfolds.

This game offers leaps and bounds of choices that affect your business prospects, really deep business simulation. If you enjoy business sims/tycoon games you definitely need to give this game a try. Seriously a hidden gem.\t\t\t\t\t
