I can't stop playing this game. It's so good! I greatly underestimated how much depth there is, but it's good depth; all fairly straightforward, and I haven't had to look anything up yet except for why the fire brigade didn't turn up when my office set on fire.. yeahh, maybe they're on strike or something - If you do get this game, invest in sprinklers!

On my first run after doing contract work for a bit I decided I was ready to start making software. it did not go well. Bankruptcy followed and I started again, this time choosing a different path. I decided to try making a research company, growing larger and larger to conquer several patents each yeah, and it worked!

This is by far my favourite tycoon game since OpenTTD.
