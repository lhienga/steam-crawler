Unfortunately, the game has an extremely steep learning curve and the tutorials aren't newbie-friendly. I need a tutorial that makes me want to invest my valuable time learning the game, and doesn't just vanish into thin air, leaving me awash in a sea of unfamiliar icons and half-understood concepts, with no idea how to continue.

Soooo... every few months I set aside some time to learn the game, "ooh" and "ahh" at all the new stuff (which, incidentally, make the game even more complex and more difficult/intimidating to learn), create a new character (the only part of the game that I've mastered) and then spend the next few hours navigating a patchwork collection of mini-tutorials that, while fairly adequate, don't leave me feeling like I know what I'm doing. What I need is a very basic overview that takes me through the overall flow of the game, not just how to do this task or that.

In the end, I'm just left wondering whether the game is worth investing my time in.\t\t\t\t\t
