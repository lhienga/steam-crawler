\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs is evident from the amount of hours I have in the game... I like it a lot.

It has come a long way since I originally bought it way back when. The pictures and trailer on the page don't do the game justice. Not only have graphics improved a lot, the mechanics also get deeper with every update. And there's a public trello board where you can see what the developer is working on and you can give your ideas and comments on those items.

Pro's:
- deep and addicting gameplay
- A developer who cares about his game and has worked on it with regular updates
- Interesting graphics (by no means a AAA-game, but very fitting to the game)
- A lot of replayability
- a small but active modding community

Con's:
- Every update usually breaks your savegames, so you'll have to start over

Neutral points:
- a slight learning curve to new players
- because there's only the one developer major updates take a bit of time\t\t\t\t\t
