\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has grass so there is no need to go outside and touch it as you can touch it in game. Allowing you to play more Software Inc.

Jokes aside on a more serious note this is an incredible game with an incredible developer that if you at all enjoy tycoon/business management style games (wow I couldn't of made that sound any more boring could I) I can guarantee you will enjoy this game.

Especially with the recent release of the first beta version of the game, there are limitless ways to play this game, start solo in a garage and develop a small indie game title, take out a loan and get some co-founders so you can start off big with making a console, start all the way back in the 80s with an easier market to get into but have limited tech or start in the 2010s with all the modern technologies but have to fight against bigger companies. There isn't really an incorrect way to play, as long as you make sure to play your cards right you can really just do whatever you want.

If you get bored of vanilla, just download mods from the workshop or make your own. I've been following the game for about 5 years now and loved it since my first play through, and if your not sure about getting it watch some people play it and you'll get a great idea of if you like it or not.

TLDR- brilliant gameplay, infinite replayablity, simply a fantastic game

9/10\t\t\t\t\t
