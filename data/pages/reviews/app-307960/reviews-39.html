\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeginners - stay away! This product is strictly for the hardcore experienced simulator pilot who also is expert in internet connectivity. The developers spent their time making beautiful graphics, incredible realism and extremely complex controls, but no time whatsoever in making this product user-friendly. My friend and I bought the game thinking we could help each other learn, but of the 7 hours I have in the game, about 4 were spent just trying to figure out what firewall was blocking what ports so we could play together. Other games make connection easy but this one won't even tell you what ports to open. Eventually my friend could connect to my server but IL-2 would simply freeze if I tried to connect to his server -- what nonsense! The other 3 hours were spent trying to figure out the absurdly complicated controls. There are no tutorials, there is no beginner mode and only by chance did I discover that you can set certain functions for automatic, and that was little help anyway. They don't even give you a beginner runway to practice on -- the runway is the same color as the surrounding area. It is umarked and utterly invisible from the cockpit, but you can't build up enough speed to take off anywhere else. The simulation was even erratic beyond my own lack of ability. One day I was actually able take off and fly several times and the next day in the same plane and same scenario the plane would simply wheel over and crash over and over. I've watched so many videos it makes my head hurt, but all I've learned is -- you can't learn this from a video. If I could join someone else's server, maybe someone could help me, but between the connectivity issues and the fact the few other servers are all LOCKED precludes that. I only thank God I bought this POS on sale instead of wasting full price on it.\t\t\t\t\t
