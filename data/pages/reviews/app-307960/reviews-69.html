\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely love this game and the continuation not only of content but improvements makes this game an absolute no brainer. If you enjoy WW2 warbirds you have to pick this game up. I was torn for a long time between DCS and IL2, bought both, started with DCS but after all the improvements, added content, and time spent playing, this is now the game I play. Now this game looks just as good to me but much more friendly on the FPS , so many more options in regards to aircraft if you are a WW2 fan, more assists when you are starting out if you want to allow yourself to ease into the mechanics a bit more. Where this game really shines over something like DCS is all the play options. Want to play a variety of scripted campaigns for any of the warbirds? No problem, they are there! Want to just do a quick mission for practice? Got it Want to start a career mode as a new pilot and progress through the war with your squadron, read the daily newspaper, track your medals and kills amongst the aces?? Got that too!

The depth of the game is what makes it playable for a long time and I've hardly even started to get into the game but could love it more and more with every hour I put in. The game is beautiful, well optimized, great sound, and has a depth of material you wont find anywhere else.

If you are only going to pick up one warbird game, skip DCS, skip War Thunder and go straight to this.\t\t\t\t\t
