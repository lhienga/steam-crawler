Most of the career missions are 15 minutes to fly to the objective, mainly with autopilot and fast forward time, reach the objective, miss every shot on the objective and fly 15 minutes back while enemies intercept you. I would like a faster fast forward than 8x because on some missions it just takes way too long to fly to the objective and back.

To sum up, it's a great game to relax with. Air battles can be really tense and because of the long time it takes to get to the objective you really want to watch your back and stay alive.
