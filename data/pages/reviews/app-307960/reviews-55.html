\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdate:

One more thing that started to bother me is controls bindings. There is no way to filter out the controls by plane type. You might be flying a fighter plane and there is no way to tell which controls are relevant to the plane you are flying, unless you know technical specifications of that plane.
Does it have ruder trim? I do not know!, Airbrake? try and guess. bomb bay door? I guess that is for bombers. Can I fire a flare gun from a bomber? nobody knows.

And there are a few controls that are specific to one of two planes, that might not be available (the planes) to you at all. Those actually say the specific plane model.

And why only the pilot takes care of bombing controls in a multicrew plane, where you have other players as a part of the crew?

__________________
Carved content much?
Go into Scripted Missions - get a screen where everything is locked.
Go to Campaign - 3 locked, 1 available
Join multiplayer and you will get to see a wall of planes that are locked and you will have hard time finding the one that is available. Or you will not be able to play, because all of the planes are locked.
Game shamefully shaves it in your face that you don't own all of DLCs.
You will absolutely need Battle of Kuban DLC (49$) to play multiplayer.
The base game should be free.
Setting up a multiplayer server is a blast from the past, but in the wrong way.
Port forwarding? seriously? It is not the 90s anymore. Have you heard about Steam API for easy multiplayer ?

Change one setting in graphics - needs to restart. Again it is not 90s anymore.
The game takes to load longer than my average combat engagement.

Throttle controller gets swapped with joystick, so keybinding stop working... sooo expecting for your controller to work is too much.

Other than that, it is ok flight sim I guess.
When on solid sale I'll get the damn Battle of Kuban DLC, and I'll enjoy every second of it, because I fly with friends, and it is awesome experience.

I just paid for 2 planes and 2 campaign DLC (with -60% to 75% discount) more, that than I paid for Cyberpunk 2077. Let that speak for itself...
And I did buy those to be able to play multi with my friends, as otherwise you might have no plane available to play.\t\t\t\t\t
