STRONGS:
- Amazing immersion, specially in VR
- Great sound, campaigns and missions
- MP is a MUST! But remember that people on MP have been playing this for a while, so you'll have a learning curve.
- WW II AIRCRAFTS!
- You can play with players that already own different packages even if you don't have them (Moscow, Stalingrad, Kuban...)

Weaks:
- Different packages means that you'll have access to the maps, but not campaigns, aircrafts and missions.
- Not SUPER realistic (if you want THAT level of realism go for DCS)
- Joystick and a head tracker is a MUST, without those the game is just meh.
- Lots of tears because people claim that as the game is made by Russians some aircrafts are nerfed or OP. Don't listen to this BS. Just learn how the aircraft fills YOUR WAY of flying on a better way.
- No comms, meaning that flying with friends must be done with Discord or TS.

As a aviation lover, and as a real AB115 pilot I give this game/simulator a good 9.5/10. If you like aircrafts but you're still thinking how hard would be to learn the basics THIS IS THE GAME FOR YOU.
