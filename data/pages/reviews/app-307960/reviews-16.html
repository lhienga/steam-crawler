\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt may not be perfect, but its by far the best Combat Flight Sim on the market today.

An incredible value (an entire battle with 10 flyable aircraft [with field modifications representing different variants and roles the aircraft family flew], maps representing multiple seasons and the course of battle, ground units, missions, campaigns and dynamic career mode) for the price of a AAA game, not counting the very regular sales where the older battles can drop by as much as 85% off. Compare that to other sims where you pay AAA prices for a single early access aircraft with no addition content.

The game also offers a nearly unlimited amount of content, aside from the scripted campaigns and missions available for download on the internet, there is the vanilla quick mission builder, Soon to be released (at the time of writing) advanced quick mission builder which will generate full missions for the player, and the dynamic career mode that tracks your pilot through a historical recreation of the title battle in a historical squadron, flying from the real airbases and the real types of missions they flew actually participated in, in said battle. There are also several excellent third party tools such as Pat Wilson's campaign generator and Vander's Easy Mission Generator. All this without ever even needing to learn to use a clunky mission editor.

Unlike most other flight sims, the game is also very well optimized with great graphics, and is friendly to low end hardware. Pretty graphics don't mean anything if no one can run at high settings! IL-2 manages this with ease, even in VR.

Being a survey level sim it lacks clickable cockpits, but don't think that makes it any less immersive, far from it. Aircraft are simulated to a high level of accuracy, with the proper engine management requirements and the unique quirks of each aircraft simulated. You'll still feel like you're flying a Mustang, or a FW-190, even if you aren't clicking the buttons with your mouse. And when you find yourself Sitting on a historical runway, part of a real squadron participating in plausible recreations of battles, keeping track of your victories in a perma-death campaign where every sortie could be your last, I promise you will be more immersed than you ever have been in a Flight Sim.

The game is constantly being improved and upgraded, and issues are being fixed as they appear. Most recently at the time of writing an issue with the .50 cal modelling was fixed improving the experience for all Late war western front pilots, new clouds are on the way promising improved visuals with improved performance, the Aforementioned Advanced Quick Mission Builder offering new ways to fly the game, new visual damage modelling, fuel system modelling, decal system, Along with the new battles and new aircraft that are on the Horizon.

Long live IL-2\t\t\t\t\t
