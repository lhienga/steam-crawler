- PWCG aka Pat Wilson Campaign Generator (available for Bos and Flying Circus)

Some NPC pilots can nearly dies, injures, or even capture by the enemy. Also you can edit your fellow pilot portrait, create a bunch of waifu and arrange a rescue mission when one of your waifu got captured by the enemy.
