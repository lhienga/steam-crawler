-cool graphics, sound & campaign.

-Stable fps in VR. ( note : gpu 1080-ti & cpu amd 1950x )

dark side : ( main reasons )
-no clickable cockpit.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is simply amazing.

My first real flight sim, came to it using the mouse and Keyboard as i didn't had a joystick. The First thing i noticed with the mouse and keyboard was that 90% of the hotkeys were unecesary so i took a good hour or so to revamp them it was boring job and i was with just about 20min on my refund window by the time i finished.
Then i went out to try my first career mission. Wow it was so much fun and all i had to do was escort a few bombers to their location and back. the plane felt great it was like almost flying it for real.... I decided not to refund and invest.

Then i Bought the Logitech 3D Extreme Pro joystick (Cheapest Good Quality Joystick in the market) Main reason for this one, is because of my limitations of space. Oh Boy... was i in for a treat.

Suddenly all the artificial Helps that the game provides with the mouse were off, and boy this planes are no longer a child's game. They have their own attitude and behaviors each plane feels different, and you are truly in control of the plane. YOU ARE FLYING IT... or crashing it, but its you. and it feels great.

If you love flying and planes, (if not what the hell are you reading this for) YOU SHOULD GET THIS. The joystick and keyboard combinations make this game 90% playable, and i can't wait till i move so i can get a proper thruster with it.

This game gave me joy, Makes me fulfill a dream that i'll never be able to achieve the WW2 Planes are so much fun to fly they are old, and you have to battle all their systems to make those things fly and as the campaign goes on and technology advances you can really feel it.

TLDR
10/10 Buy this.

And as a side note - This and DCS are probably the best Combat sims out there, and when you consider that DCS with its full content will cost you more than 1k USD (On sale), and this game giving you many more planes and unlimited Mission to play around with for about half the price I just want to Support and Endorse developers like this one that make quality content and charge FAIR for what they worked for.\t\t\t\t\t
