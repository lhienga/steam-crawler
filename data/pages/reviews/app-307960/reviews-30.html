\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll in one: IL-2 Great Battles is a nice Game and worth it for all WWII (Warbirds) Fans. In some points out of date, but it is still the leading WWII Air combat Game/Sim. (stongest competitors: War Thunder, DCS World)

Gameplay:
It feels very realistic in most points but has for sure his quirks.
Every flight instrument seem to be working. Cockpit is not clickable (Hotas, Keyboard or Buttonbox needed). The Planes cold start is in semi automatic mode "press E".
Some controlles/functions not working? Example BF109: I can't open my air cooler manually. (Or I'm to stupid, if someone know it better please tell me how)


Beginners:
Trail and error.
At the beginning you can switch on some helpful options (unlimited Ammo etc..).

The carreer is cool. But I dont feel, that my kills or attacks have really an impact in the war. (no simulatet resources etc.) I miss a good written cinematic Campaign who teach and entertain also new players.
Just for example, games like Hawx or Ace Combat lifes from the dialogs ingame. The Missions in this arcade Games are very generic but the dialogs fill it with life.

What brings me to the next point, RADIO. Short: The Radio communication is not contemporary.
It is such a pain to listen the Luftwaffe radio voices. Damn is that crap! It is so artificial and stereotype!

Sound: Yeah is good. I miss maybe a bit dramatic WWII music.

Graphics:
Beautiful graphics. The planes are super detailed and the Damagemodel looks awesome. I can't really complain at all.
Maybe spoiled personal taste: The Artstyle and ground is a bit trist. In VR, the heaven has a kind of cartoonie blue (I personal dont like it much.) I play it 95% in VR.

System: rtx2070, Ryzen 5 5600x HP G2 or Oculus Quest 1
A good CPU make the Game more stable and smooth in VR. :)
With Motion-Reprojection on, also in high details no Problems (medium clouds). 45 to 90fps

AI:
I cannot judge the AI combat skills. Im not a IL-2 Pro, for me is challenging enough. When I startet to play Multiplayer I realized, that there is some thing what I don't like. It is much more work to shoot down the AI-Pilots. First I thought my Bullets dont penetrade at all. But now I believe its because the AI fly like normal, when the plane get some strong hits. They just go done if they burning or lose the wings.
I lose the control of my plane after some bullets stroke me , like my human opponents in Multiplayer. The AI don't have problems with overheating engines? They climb so fast.

Mission Editor: Damn I never used a so confuse Mission editor in my live. Operation Flashpoint, ARMA, DCS, thats are easy editors! :D What a luck a guy exist, (SYN_Vander) who code a support software ("Easy Mission Generator") to help us. Its free, but you can donate a coffee for him (he deserved it). I recommend it, because he made a damn good job.
