\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I wanted to play War Thunder I would of played War Thunder. Flight physics extremely unrealistic. Yak's and P40s on your tail and you pull a high-G barrel roll to get them to over shoot but they just dump full "super" flaps and hang in the air like the physics defying game mechanics allow them too.

On top of that you have sniper AI gunners that can shoot you like gods. Even with 3-4 hundred KPH difference in speeds between aircraft the godlike sniper gunners will hit you out of nowhere. Attacking an aircraft from underneath, the split second instant you pass from under to above the aircraft the AI gunner insta snaps and kills your pilot. The gunner issue is not limited to only a certain subset of planes but all planes with defensive gunners.

Issues like these have been raised time and time and time again with the Devs. But they dont seem to care.

I wanted a realistic flight sim with a bit of multiplayer. And you will get that sometimes maybe, when the "Tactical Air War" server is up. But don't expect it from the busiest server "Wings of Liberty". Its heavily russian biased server. Exceedingly difficult to attack bombers from offensive angles as all they ever do is run along the deck. And not to mention the rammers. So yeah. Its basically War Thunder with a different name at this point. Rather play 1946 or if you really need something more modern get DCS. Still yet to try Cliffs of Dover Blitz Wings over Tobruk, So will see how CLOD pans out. But can already tell you CLOD looks much better.\t\t\t\t\t
