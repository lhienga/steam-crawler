\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn my well-spent youth, I played a lot of Aces Over Europe and Aces of the Pacific. I wanted this game to be a modern, more realistic version of those games.

I'm happy to report that I got what I wanted. There are a few problems I have with this game, however.

Foremost of these is that the friendly AI is bad. Really bad. There's been a few times that my wing was attacked by enemy fighters while we were circling the airfield and landing. While I broke away to deal with the incoming fighter wing, the rest of my wing continued circling the airfield with their navigation lights on, oblivious to the incoming threat. In the most recent instance, this resulted in 3 of 5 fellow pilots being shot down. I've also been in scenarios where my wing has decided to fly off, leaving me to deal with the bandits alone.

Nevertheless, I'm enjoying this game thoroughly and would recommend to anyone looking to relive their Aces Over Europe / Aces of the Pacific nostalgia.\t\t\t\t\t
