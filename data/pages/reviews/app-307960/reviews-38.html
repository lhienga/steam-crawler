\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun combat flight simulator especially in VR with HOTAS and pedals. I have no clue how realistic the flight model is exactly but the planes are pretty unstable and hard to fly aggressively during combat. This is not an arcade game, though there are lots of assists and automation in the custom difficulty settings, such as RPM governing, manifold pressure, pitch angle and fuel mixture.
The damage modeling is fantastic and very detailed. It never gets old seeing your enemy getting shred to pieces by machine gun tracer fire, maybe catching in flames and spiralling violently to the ground. The damage is not only realistic visually but also physics-wise regarding flight dynamics and mechanical damage.
Runs pretty smoothly compared to most flight sims but still has nice graphics. I actually got it to run stable 90fps in VR with a Reverb G2 native resolution, GTX 1070 and Ryzen 5 3600 and the graphics settings aren't even the lowest possible.\t\t\t\t\t
