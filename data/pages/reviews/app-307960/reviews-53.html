IMPORTANT! If you encounter any loading errors
go to your steam library-->right click on Il-2 Sturmovik Battle of Stalingrad→ Properties-->Local Files→ Verify Integrity of game files→ then restart computer

This review is a list for people trying to get into multiplayer which I have most of my playtime on. Currently the three most populated servers are Finnish Virtual Pilots, Wings of Liberty, and Combatbox.

Hardware
Make sure to check if your computer can run the game and internet connect is decent. Scroll down to system requirements on the Il-2 Sturmovik steam page. If you have an old or damaged computer of over 5+ years then it is not recommended to buy any video game due to lag, crashes, etc..

Joystick (with twist so you can control the rudder); a joystick is necessary to make this game fun because most of the multiplayer populated servers require a joystick to player such as Combatbox and Wings of Liberty. Additionally the planes are frustrating to fly without a joystick. I recommend the “Logitech 3D Pro” joystick at price ($30-40) but pricing depends on the vendor or person who sells the joystick. This joystick is relatively cheap and works well for Il-2 Sturmovik.

Headtracking/TrackIR; TrackIR is necessary for a fighter aircraft or planes that shoot other planes because tracking an aircraft with the mouse is just frustrating and with the mouse you tend to lose the target often leading to a quick death. For the reasons listed above flying without TrackIR is infuriating. Recommend TrackIR 5 Camera ($150) + Track Clip Pro ($30) ordered from the website. This device is pricey but works well.


DLC
The DLCs are expensive at base price but on sale they are worth it. I will list my recommended order to buy the DLCs. Additionally I will give my opinion on some of the aircraft. Also listing the price it is on sale at this time (Autumn Sale 26 November 2020). You should read this list from TOP to BOTTOM with TOP being most enjoyable.

1. Il-2 Sturmovik: Battle of Stalingrad Deluxe ($11.99)
this expansion gives you aircraft such as
Yak-1; extremely nimble aircraft and quick at turning
La-5; 2 x 20mm cannons and fast in a dive
Bf-109 F-4; quick aircraft with fast dive and climb
FW-190 A-3; 4 x 20mm cannons and fast dive
Most common planes you can find in Multiplayer

2. Battle of Bodenplatte ($24.99)
P-51; late war American fighter which is good at Boom and Zoom
P-47; late war American fighter famous for fighter-bomber missions
good DLC for CombatBox server because they focus on late war scenarios

3. Battle of Kuban ($12.25)
Yak-7B; better and more nimble Yak-1
P-39L-1; American plane with a 37mm cannon
Fw 190 A-5; German plane with 4 x 20mm cannons fast in a dive
Good planes for Wings of Liberty because they like to do Kuban scenarios often

4. Battle of Moscow ($12.49)
Bf-109 E-7; 2 x 20mm cannons and 2 machine guns
IL-2 model 1941; fun ground attacker with big rockets
Pe-2 Series 35; ; fun ground attacker with big rockets
Finnish Virtual Pilots server mainly does Battle of Moscow missions using Battle of Moscow planeset

Collector planes
P-38J-25 ($6.79); excellent ground pounder and fighter for Combatbox server 4 x 1000IBS bombs
FW-190 D-9 ($6.79); excellent fighter which is extreme good in boom and zoom attacks with great climb and dive especially at high altitude for Combatbox server
LA-5FN ($4.99); arguable the best soviet plane with great climb and dive as well as 2 x 20mm cannons
Bf-109G-6 ($4.99); 30mm cannon + 2 x 20mm cannons + machineguns this plane is a gun platform and has good dive
Yak-1B ($4.99); extremely nimble plane with a good turn

Conclusion
Overall price for the best experience:
Logitech 3D Pro ($30) + TrackIR 5 Camera ($150) + Track Clip Pro ($30) + Il-2 Sturmovik: Battle of Stalingrad Deluxe ($11.99) + Battle of Bodenplatte ($24.99) = ($247)

You should consider what you can get out of this game. This game is excellent if you can see yourself playing this game for 247 hours and more.

You have bought the game. How do I play it?
*It is recommended you should watch videos, read guides, and set keybinds to understand this game’s mechanics.
*play BF 109
*E (default key) to start engine from parked on runway
*Right Comma (default key) to control break left
*Right Period (default key) to control break right
*Right Slash (default key) to control break right and left at same time
*find runway
*align plane with runway and take off
