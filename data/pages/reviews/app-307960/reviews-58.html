\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLooks good, clouds, planes and sunsets all look great. The flight model seems very good, but the battlefield is weirdly empty and the AI is absolutely terrible.

Generated mission I just had sent 4 bf109's against 9 bombers and a 5-6 fighter escort. They all line up slowly behind the bombers and get shot down one at a time. When the friendly 109's engage fighters they'll get into dumb turning fights with slower more agile enemies (they should be using their speed and altitude advantages).
But then the AI almost never aims to hit other AI planes. They fly straight behind each other firing to miss.
Against you they're a bit better but tend to all do the same set of manoeuvers meaning they're not much of a challenge at all once you've learned the pattern. They also always see you even if you sneak up in their blind spots.
Enemy fighters usually don't break off and run if damaged or low on fuel, they just follow you home and shoot you if you ever pop up from the deck, if you stay low they just hover around behind you, Airfield defences and scattered friendly forces make little to no attempt to shoot at them, no fighters will scramble to deal with them and you can't rearm to go back up yourself. Even set to dense activity there's not much on the ground.

Enemy bombers always come with an insane escort, whereas if you fly bombers you'll rarely ever get an escort. Friendlies rarely kill anything which leaves you to knock out the 3/4 bandits your ammo allows and hope the others drifted too far away to continue the fight.
The single player experience is very hollow. 1990's AI in in 2020 sim. Very disappointing.

If you want to play online or with friends maybe it's OK, but DCS has warbirds now so is a much better choice for that. It was OK on sale for <10, but I'd feel cheated playing full price.\t\t\t\t\t
