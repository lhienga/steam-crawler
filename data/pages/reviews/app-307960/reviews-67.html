\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you own a VR headset you owe it to yourself to buy this game. It wasn't designed for VR, so you can't flip switches with the touch controllers etc but nothing in VR has come close to the thrill of sticking my head out of the cockpit and looking down from 8,000ft flying a P51. No longer am I hitting a FOV button on my stick to check my six, I just turn my head around and have a look for myself.

No VR headset? Still a great combat flight sim, in fact I'm not sure there is anything better. Just fair warning it's not War Thunder, this is NOT an arcade game it's a simulator. That's kinda sorta my only complaint about this game - there's not many populated easy-mode multiplayer servers for the newbies that are lost trying to figure out controls let alone visually identifying enemy vs friendly planes without the little marker. But that's not a fault of the devs, the multiplayer community is just mostly people like myself that have been playing flight sims for years.\t\t\t\t\t
