\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe one huge thing that is not addressed with this game is the error in the flight model. Planes have 3 axis. The centre of these axis is inside the plane around the wing root leading edge area and the plane rotates around this central axis. In Il2 this is modelled underneath the plane so that it wobbles around in a most unrealistic way. Those without an interest in how planes work will be oblivious but to others it's so obvious it spoils it. Add this to the daft damage models that have a wing fall off almost every time its hit (and the plane then rotating the wrong way) and planes falling in slow motion and then bouncing when they hit the ground and it becomes an arcade game not a sim. Fire the guns and you immediately swerve off your aim and attempts at tight turns put you into a stall where the AI can continue to make tight turns with no problem. It is also impossible to bounce an AI. They ALWAYS know where you are what you are doing even if you are in their blind spot. If you are just after a bit of fun then great but it's too pricey for that. I would prefer it if they concentrated on the dynamics of the flight and damage models instead of making it look pretty and making yet more maps and aircraft to sell us.\t\t\t\t\t
