\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is REALLY good, but it is harder than I expected; BY FAR!

So to start, this game is unlike previous war machine games that I have played. Some of those games are the tank and airplane part of Battlefield 1 and 5, War Thunder, World of Tanks and World of Warplanes. Those games had the mechanics such as gravity, terrain, weather and angles affecting them aswell. But this game takes it to the next level! The amounts of individual keybinds are I N S A N E! The amount of keybinds needed to know to play a bomber plane and drop the bombs, knowing what you are doing requires a ton of practice. Like I had to google what it all mean in the "Bomber sight" mode. The game NEEDS a thorough watch in the key binds to know what you are supposed to do.
But other than the fact that the game is really difficult to learn, it is hella fun. When I learned how to fly and aim correctly I found myself really enjoying it! And I don't see myself staying at 10 hrs played for long. The game has a great depth and feeling or you really being there (which sadly relies on the almost too many keybinds).

Even though the game it is hard to learn, it is really fun when you get the hang of it! As previously mentioned, I see myself playing this game for a lot in the future! So with that said I would like to guve my personal rating (opinion) 7.8/10\t\t\t\t\t
