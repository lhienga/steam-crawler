The high quality dogfights make this game worth it, despite all the cons I list below. It is tough to learn coming from arcade games like WoWP and war thunder, but take it slow, and learn it bit by bit. It eventually becomes a blast.

Not a hug fan of the campaigns, though. 50 minute missions, with 1 dogfight, but mostly flying in a straight line, turn around and land.

VR mode is really cool. However, it is not optimized for VR. After scouring forum posts to optimize settings, and buying a new GPU, I still only get 45 fps when the ground is in view.
The desktop view is also a big disappointment. I'm not sure why they went with the 2 eye view, but I wish they made it optional.\t\t\t\t\t
