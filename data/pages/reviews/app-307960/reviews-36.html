\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt this point, it provides the most solid multiplayer experience in any ww2 aircraft game. Benefits from having a nice community and inventive server admins. The devs are constantly improving the game. It's neither too complicated nor too simplified. Good enough so that the player may depend on gunnery experience, plane knowledge and fight maneuvers in order to win. Teamwork can be critical.
There are single player missions, scripted campaigns and a somewhat dynamic career mode. The career mode has a very nice interface, your virtual pilot may earn medals, prizes and promotions depending on performance (and survival). The AI aircraft is modelled just like the player's, unlike other games of its kind. However, the AI is too easy too defeat, as it is only able to do very basic horizontal turns. That eventually ruins the immersion as a fighter pilot. Ground attacking in this mode also feels wrong, as your flight always runs into enemy fighters. There is a community SP campaign app that improves this aspect, although it can't save the mode from its uninteresting AI.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is one very important thing to understand before buying this sim.
If you are willing to spend lots of time, money and effort into learning the game, buying the equipment and aircraft as well as not giving up learning the ins and outs of this sim then go ahead and buy it.
If you’re looking for a quick game to spend £15 in the sales on to play for a few hours then don’t, it’s just not worth it. Buy IL-2 1946 instead :)
With that out the way I can proceed to give a review.
If you like WW2 air combat and fancy spending hours learning to fly in an authentic flight simulator with realistic flight models, damage models, locations, ballistics, ground vehicles, missions and scenarios then this game right here is the best on the market right now and probably will remain so for a very long time. There is literally no other game that has the content as well as the realism that this game and it’s expansion packs bring. This expansion has a decent set of planes, remember if you’re using steam then you HAVE to buy this one in order to use the others. I recommend purchasing this one anyway as it has a decent mid ground set of planes which are good to use to learn, buying the premium addition with the La-5 and Focke Wulf 190 is also not a bad idea. This game scratches an itch that not no others can, if you’re feeling that itch then do yourself a favour and buy it.

fixed view uses pre defined views that you press buttons to go to. the "Fixed view gradual" mode works like a pan, but moves in large increments.
