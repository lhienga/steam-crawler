\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you wanted a newer and more modern IL-2 game you will get one. It is awesome. It is just as hard as you want it to be. Career mode is very nicely done and feels like you are telling a story of a WW2 pilot. Pilots have backstories and all that neat stuff. It feels alive, not just lifeless flying and following a line on the map. Multiplayer is very good, many servers and lots of activity. Most of the populated servers are on Expert settings though so be prepared to learn basic mechanics of your planes. All the radiator controls, mixtures, prop pitch etc.. It takes time but is worth the effort. Also you can be a gunner of a human controlled plane if you don't want to fly yourself.

Flight models are great and even a lower price joystick(mine is Logitech Extreme 3D PRO) will do a very good job and feel good unlike for example in War Thunder. Controls in IL-2 are very rough in a good way. It feels like I would imagine flying a WW2 plane with steel cables as a control system would feel like. All the damage models are great and seeing a weakened wing bend off because of air resistance after you have shot at an enemy plane is very realistic and immersive.

As a life long IL-2 series fan I highly recommend this game for anyone who likes planes and WW2 history.\t\t\t\t\t
