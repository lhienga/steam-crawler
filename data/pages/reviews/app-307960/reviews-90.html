\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor me this was the first IL2 since 1946 to really feel proper. The planes fly wonderfully, and there is a large amount of flexibility depending on if you feel like playing in a more action arcade way or a true to reality simulation style. Also, and very surprisingly, this game ran amazingly smoothly on my last computer, which had a horrible intel iris GPU. If you like WWII flying simulations, the IL-2 series is the best available, and I believe this is one of their best games. (DCS world is possibly a more in depth simulation and offers a number of WWII aircraft, but its complexity and system requirements, along with an appropriately high price tag per plane, puts it behind IL2 in terms of WWII sims, but it is undoubtedly the best for more modern areal combat or if you are someone who wants simulation down to the last rivet at the cost of flexibility and ease of use.)\t\t\t\t\t
