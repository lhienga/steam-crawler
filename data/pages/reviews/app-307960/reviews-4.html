\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTHIS IS FLIPPIN' BRILLIANT!!!!!

I was initially put off owing to what appeared to be a combat flight sim for the more serious sim player.
Well, ok there is that, BUT! You can disable all those fancy dodads and ignore most of the wobbly needles in front of you. Alright, SOME of the wobbly needles ;)

The other thing that put me off was the abandonded ginger step-child of a game that is
IL-2 Cliffs of Dover:Blitz
I'm not buying a game, then have to pay £30 for essentially a third party DLC to make it playable.
Boo sir. I SAY BOOOO! (Hats off to the supporters work on "Tobrook" though)!

Anyhoo. The aircraft are wonderful.
I was initially dismayed at how few I got with one of the base games, but the differences in layout, control and performance really makes you want to master flying your favourite WWII birds properly!
...or, like me...
Hit all the idiot switches.
Don VR headset.
Drop your MKIX Spit into the middle of a wave of HE111's and blaze away with the Battle of Britain blaring out the stereo....
So I had to buy the "Bodenplatte" DLC to get a couple of aircraft I wanted. Bargain in the winter sale!
Loads more available for download from the store but sadly no 4-engine bombers to my knowledge. :(

ALSO!
Runs sweetly enough on my absolute potato-even in VR! (If a little jaggy)
