\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was absolutely overjoyed when this DLC was announced - I love trying out new cars and the style of GT4 racing is so different from the GT3 cars that we are all used to. With little reliance on aero by comparison, the racing can be much closer, due to the lack of dirty air. I spent the first two weeks after release, trying every car and eventually falling in love with the KTM. I still absolutely adore this DLC, and it is by far my favourite aspect of ACC now.

Sadly, not everybody feels the same way and the servers are largely empty. I would say that the GT3 or Mixed racing has around five or six fully populated servers at all times, and many others with similarly large player counts. However, the player base seems to be favouring the GT3 cars and even the mixed servers rarely have more than one or two GT4 cars. Because of this, I can't recommend the GT4 DLC if you are like me and don't have the time to join formally organised GT4 racing leagues, because of family life.

I really hope the population picks up again - I can't wait to get back on track and enjoy the close racing again!\t\t\t\t\t
