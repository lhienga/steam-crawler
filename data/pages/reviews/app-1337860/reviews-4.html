\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest car class for door to door racing in the whole world in my opinion. Those cars are keeping a lot of their ''street character'' and feels alive and not too perfect. This is some real car racing my friends. GT3 are cool, but they feel too perfect and precise. You must drive them with all your attention. With the mighty GT4s, you can literally turn off traction control, rock some lift-off oversteer or power slides and legit pull out a good lap time while feeling like Jeremy Clarkson reviewing the Reliant Robin.

Car models are perfect. In VR, the scale is absolutely on point and the interior feels too damn real.
But lads. The sounds.

Gosh

I mean, Mozart might be impressive, or maybe you prefer listening to some old black eyed peas, but those cars sounds are just like any real-life In-board you can expect except without the microphone parasites and wind.
The engine note feels good all the way up without digital-like transitions.
The wastegate woosh and Turbo Flutters are all up there
The very distinctive race car cabin rattles and ''can-sounding'' cockpit is the best i've heard in 10 years of simracing. I am lucky enough to be part of a Touring Car Racing Team IRL, I know for sure that ACC is the only one offering the real raspy sound of a race car cockpit with all its rattles and rocks hitting the fenders and marbles flying off and what not. It's the only game that makes me feel like the whole thing is actually LOUD. Other game's in-car are all sounding dull and muffled (iRacing, really bro ? ) A race car cockpit is chaos. Rocks hitting the steel, reverb, rasp, engine notes, roar, transmission whistles... ACC got you covered and the whole DLC costs the same as 1 car in iRacing. Oh, and you can slide the cars like you would in real life without launching your rear axle into oblivion !

If you got this far you can tell I like car sounds. But as a real life motorsports Mechanic and amateur Driver, I'm so Moist to actually hear something that makes sense out of a simracing cockpit !!

c'mon, go buy the pack for yourself and get 2-3 more for your friends !
