\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositives:
  • Adds the 2019 season of the GT4 European Series with most cars, liveries, etc., replicated in great detail.
Negatives:
  • The Aston Martin V8 Vantage GT4 (previous generation) and Ekris-BMW M4 are not included (though the factory BMW M4 GT4 is).
  • Unlike multiplayer, in singleplayer the GT4 content can only be used for GT4 European Series races, not mixed with existing content (exception: with release of the British GT DLC content only British GT modes allow mixed class races offline). Therefore combining the GT4 and IGTC DLC to replicate the real GT3-GT4 multi-class racing for example isn't possible offline.
  • Doesn't add anything to the Career mode.

Recommended because the 2019 GT4 European Series season is a great addition. I would prefer less restrictions on how to use the content offline when the multiplayer allows custom grids, however.\t\t\t\t\t

Can't say I use them that much and not many people use them online but for single player races or hot lapping they are great fun to drive. If your new to the game they can also help you learn the basics far quicker than jumping into a mid engine GT3 for example. At full price its a little steep for how often I have ended up using them but on sale it really is a must own. Compared with other games/sims Kunos pricing is very fair but if you don't have the 2 other DLC I would recommend those over the GT4. Overall more great content from Kunos but out of the 3 so far its 3rd best for me at least.\t\t\t\t\t
