For $27CAD, you get 11 spicy cars. A great price (compared to iRacing) and each car is genuine fun to use, with distinct handling tropes for each. My personal favourites are the Porsche and the Alpina, but I've tried racing each new vehicle and have enjoyed all (except maybe the Camaro and the Maserati). If you're scared of the transition, the McLaren and Aston handle more or less like a GT3 and your braking zones won't shift too much.

If you like some speedy boys and a slightly looser driving feel, get your understeer on and snag this pack to s b i n n a l a your favourite ride.\t\t\t\t\t
