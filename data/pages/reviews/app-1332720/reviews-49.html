\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have had a great time with Thief Simulator 2. It has had a lot of fun improvements when compared to the original, which I also greatly enjoyed. Given, there have been a number of bugs reported and there is still some content being added, but I feel the developer is very responsive to posted bugs and has updated the game five times in the first week of release alone addressing numerous fixes.

Basically, the idea of the game is to case houses, figure out the routines and schedules of its residents, and prepare the right tools and timing for entering the house, grabbing some good stuff, and leaving. This is the same premise as the first Thief Simulator game, but there's more options for how you go about your thieving. Moreover, there's a lot more equipment choices in this game, up to and including the ability to actually render people unconscious temporarily. There's also new systems for stealing known as "Heists" that take you to a completely new map for the job. And, if you are caught by police, there is now a jail system where you can either serve your time or break out (While grabbing goodies of course).

I played through each house in the presently available two neighborhoods, satisfied most of the Thief Contracts and Blackbay item sets, and have decided to hold off on starting the third Heist for the time being. But, I am looking forward to seeing more updates and continuing with this game soon. Fully recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
