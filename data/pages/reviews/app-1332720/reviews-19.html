\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is exactly what a sequel should be: everything we love, but more and better. I've played (and loved) a lot of Playway SA games, Thief Simulator 1 was one of their best, and so far 2 is even better. Loved the first one, found it early in it's development and played the ♥♥♥♥ out of it. I've gotten so used to sequels being something COMPLETELY different from what I loved about the original, but they nailed it with this one. More and better neighourhoods/houses, more and better tactical options, more and better mechanics, et cetera.

Playway SA is known for making a certain kind of game, and Thief Simulator 2 is a textbook example of the kind of work they do. The mechanics are so solid and engaging that you can see past the horrible graphics (and they can be quite horrible at times), which really just add to the whole eastern European "the world is a cold and ugly place" vibe. Sound is good, controls are solid, story is interesting enough, and the thrill of sneaking into houses at night to steal frying pans and vacuum cleaners is always dandy. If you want to try living the daily grind of a petty criminal then give almost any of their games a try, and if you only try one of their games it should be either this one, Drug Dealer Simulator, or Contraband Police. You're a bad guy in all of them, but you're a sympathetic bad guy and you get to go through people's stuff.\t\t\t\t\t
