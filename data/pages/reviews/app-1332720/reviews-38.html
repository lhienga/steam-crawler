\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThief Simulator 2 is unpolished in it's current state. I respect the dev for working on this solo, and for a solo dev, it's not bad work. However, there are some things i'd like to address.

First, when playing the story, some things don't trigger. The game instructed me to go to a marked spot at a certain time, but i got there an hour earlier. When the time came, I was in the spot the game instructed me to be, but nothing progressed until I actually moved around quite a bit for the game to recognize that I was there. That's kind of annoying.

Second, there's no proper "intro" to the hideout. I just drove there while escaping the cops, who got called on me for.. illegal parking??? and it was like "oh yeah this is your safehouse" once i selected the option.

The story is more "random" than the first game and I dont really understand anything. It kind of just drops you in, like "oh yeah by the way theres a lot of criminals looking for you, and im mad so steal something from this guys house and I know everything". It's just weird.

The time is also strange. Started to get dark at 4PM.

Overall, it's not a terrible game, just don't think its worth more than like $12-13.\t\t\t\t\t
