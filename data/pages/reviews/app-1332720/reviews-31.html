Game Play
From someone who has played The Original Thief Simulator (TS1), this is a huge improvement compared to the previous game. It introduces a variety of new mechanics and features such as pick-pocketing, sleeping gas, masks, and customisation.
Graphics
The graphics are amazing. Running it on max settings gives you lovely night views of the little towns you are robbing.

Audio and Music
I am not much of an audio enthusiast, but they do provide some nicely crafted music and effects that complement the crime-themed game play, which is always nice.

Story line and Narrative
The story line is interesting with a lot if hidden LOREE (I sincerely hope you got that) to be revealed. A lot of dark and mysterious vibes are being created in the story as you play. Also the random notes you read as you explore...

Overall
Personally, I like the game a lot and have been playing it for quite a while. I highly recommend checking it out if you have played TF1. If you are new to exploring games that involve stealth with mischievous action, I would also recommend giving the game a go.
