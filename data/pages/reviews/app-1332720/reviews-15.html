\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall: 9/10
Gameplay: 9/10
Graphics: 7/10
Story: 5/10

If you're a fan of stealth games like Thief, Hitman, Dishonored - then this is the game for you. I've played the first game and the sequel is as good, if not better as some mechanics are improved. This game deserves way more credit because it is mostly developed by one person only, and it is still more fun than a game developed by a multi-million dollar studio.

My only gripe is that the AI are kinda stupid and also too smart, so take that with a grain of salt; but you will be able to adapt to their behaviour after a while. Looking forward to more content for the game!\t\t\t\t\t
