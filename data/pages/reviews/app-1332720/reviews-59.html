\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit: The dev has been positively receptive about all the comments about bugs and all. I'm switching my review to a recommend cause they've been working on fixing a lot of the issues so far.

ORIGINAL GAME RELEASE REVIEW:

(Note original review was quick and dirty as I didn't have time. This is a slightly fuller review for the dev response.)

The game has multiple flaws and weird graphical elements that make it feel like it's more of a mid to late stage early access title then full release one. It honestly feels more like thief 1.5 then a proper sequel to the original.

Yeah the basics are there that made the game fun, yeah there are neat new concepts to it. Better lock picking minigame, drones are interesting (though introduced way too early IMO), enjoy the on location pawn shop. (Even it breaks immersion a bit with the way it's handled a little.)

Just to name a couple things. Cops have caught me on the other side of a building through a fence. Weird shape of roads and sidewalks not lining up and the ends of sidewalks just end with a tiny wall coming up. Not being able to ping people when on their property. Asked every single time I return to the main menu about controller support. Difficulty not being clear on what's different other then saying that hardcore sends you to jail and easy is easy. NPCs not walking around you still and calling the cops simply for standing near the sidewalk. Objects in your truck bed are physics based and a computer tower went flying when I parked.

There are other problems, but you can just go into the discussions to see them.

Overall it seems it should be as fun as the first. Sadly a couple flaws that I noticed give me a soft pass on this game.\t\t\t\t\t
