\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you liked the first game, you'll love this one.

So generally, game play feels smooth, graphics are nice, story is over all ok and the heists really spice some things up. However, I have one complain and one thing that felt "wonky". So first, when stealing big objects, it was a bit of a trick to get them out the window or over a fence. At the end I just ended up opening the garage and just walk everything out that way.

The part i felt as a negative was at end game, a lot comes down to hacking. And hacking works really well, there is no game play issue here. Its just that hacking is a bit "too much" in the end.

On the plus side, the disassemble of cars did greatly approve.

I feel its a fair price for what you get, I really enjoyed it. I liked the first one, I loved this one. Hoping for more content for it in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
