\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved Thief Sim 1 and despite my initial concerns that a different developer was working on this, Thief Sim 2 really is a worthy sequel. It is just as addictive and exciting to play as the original and adds some great innovations such as an associate, drones, heists, ATM hacking, dogs, parcel codes, a police station and plenty of new tools. The contracts and payphone missions add more replay value and the two neighbourhoods in the base game have plenty of houses to explore. Some gameplay elements are much slicker in this game, especially car disassembly which is a breeze compared with Thief Sim 1.

The game itself is harder than the original as tenants seem to have keener hearing and are onto you like a shot if you make too much noise. The heists are an interesting addition, improving upon the simplistic ones seen in American Theft 80s, and the third one of these is a proper challenge.

My only minor gripes are that some of the gameplay elements aren't properly explained, for example the car hotwiring which is different from Thief Sim 1's minigame. Nevertheless, it isn't a major problem as you'll get the hang of the different gameplay elements with a bit of practice. Also, house customisation is nowhere near as good in this game: you can change the walls and floors, but you can't add furniture or put your swag on display.

Overall, Thief Sim 2 is great. It goes without saying: if you enjoyed Thief Sim 1 and American Theft 80s, this game is an essential purchase. The fact this game was created by one person is incredible; what an effort.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbysmal story. Someone's after you, but you have to steal goods and piss off people before the voice assisting you (in the game) will lend you a hand.

Abysmal voice acting. The voices in the game, especially the dialog sound like Windows 11 voice prompts.

Better than average graphics. Decent job on the graphics.

Hacking ATM machines makes no sense. There's 3 colors that define the 'hot and cold' method for choosing the correct number. I've multiple times run into 5 being yellow, but 4 and 6 being red. This makes no sense.

The game is decent but it definitely feels like an early release not an official release. It has a lot of bugs. For example the player cannot cancel what they are doing to run from police or hide; such as cracking a safe. The level of AI all together is plain stupid, games like GTA V have better human AI quality out of regular civilians.

I played for 4 hours and the game crashed. When I went to re-enter it froze. I give up for now.

For the price, 6/10, if it goes down to $10, it would be worth it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit: Came back in after several updates with the intent of getting 100% on this game by completing a Hardcore run. If you have played Thief Simulator or similar sorts of stealth games, I would highly recommend starting on hardcore difficulty. The challenge is considerably higher and the AI is substantially less exploitable. The whole game took me 47 hours total to complete twice over (once on normal, once on hardcore) with the content as of October 19, 2023.

For a one person dev team, very impressive work. Builds on many aspects of other games in the series with enjoyable efficiencies. I wish I hadn't waited as long as I did to progress in the main quest, though. I got sidetracked on sidequests and, had I been making use of the companion mechanic to handle almost all of the heavy lifting during a robbery, I would have saved probably a third of my total hours played.

That stated, those hours weren't wasted. The AI is imperfect and I personally had fun tricking it in weird, counter intuivite ways. It can't quite seem to grasp rooftops at the moment, so if you're able to launch yourself anywhere on the z axis that the bot isn't on, they're going to have a bad time.

All-in-all, glad I bought it and looking forward to seeing further updates. As it stands, this recommendation is based only on the ready feedback we've been seeing from the developer. This is a very well made theivery game, even if you'll likely only currently get 20-30 hours of gameplay out of it unless you get SUPER completionist.\t\t\t\t\t
