\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe good:
Not a fully fledged sequel, but a decent continuation. As in, it does not iterate over the first game a whole lot, but is a really playable second game in terms of story. You grind a lot and they can get boring, but if you're a simulation/indie game lover and especially a stealth game enjoyer, you will spend at least about 5-10 hours, and 20 hours in the long-term i.e: the lifespan of the game. The graphics and sounds are sub-par, but mostly what you would expect from an indie game like this.

The bad:
It is still a bit dull like the first game in terms of action, adrenaline and fully-fledged stealth enjoyment. I would expect Drug Dealer Simulator levels of adrenaline and stealth from the sequel of a game that has been very well perceived and accumulated a substantial amount of budget for the next games.

The ugly:
I may be wrong, but it sounds like they used the same male voice actor for every character and even radio stations. After a while his announcer-like voice really started bothering me. Also there are some intonation mistakes that are very evident even for a bilingual like me. The voicelines seem so rushed. My opinion on the budget applies to here as well.

6.25 / 10 - Could be cheaper, could not be rushed this much, but I really enjoy playing it. :)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile a bit janky, if the in game messages arent a lie(they dont seem to be) this is largely done, if not entirely, by a single person.

I "Thief simulatored" Thief simulator 1 and noticed this came out the same week so not long ago, bought it asap as I was enjoying the little I played of the 1st game. As a huge stealth fan, this game is very very fun. The jank exists, but its not bad. The content is a lot of fun, the gameplay loop is very satisfying, the dev is continuing to add more and more content and mechanics without charging(though it seems some DLC is planned which is great) It says I only have 6 hours or so at the time. I clocked in an easy 18-20 so steam kinda up, enjoyed it all for the most part. It seems to have been more updated since I played on the release patch and was internetless until very recently. My current issues seemed to have been fixed in general (Baton being 1kg to 0.1 and range is now gone) The stealth is kinda its own thing, but the new mechanic where you can pick someone or a dog in the house and see its pathing+schedule is a joy. A wonderful stealth mechanic for a game such as this, as only 1 at a time can be used.

My issues mostly come from the heists(ive yet to do the halloween one) The first one is FANTASTIC. The other 2 just feel meh, and its such a great addition but I hope the dev can find ways to improve them. I found the 2nd one very very mediocre and the bank, the "finale" has so much potential but its biggest issue is just getting inside, it got to such a point I didnt give two exploring the inside because of how much time it took to get INSIDE.(apparently it has a new entrance ive yet to see)

$20 for 20 solid hours and more free content and DLC to come? Games like this is why I hardly bother with AAA anymore.

Im running a i5 from 2012, an external SSD, and a 1050ti. The performance is shockingly good at 1080p, I turn a few things down, but nothing that makes me feel like im sacrificing anything serious. Im usually at a constant 45-60fps, if the dev can continue optimization I expect a full 60+ easily.

The story and VA are enjoyable enough, nothing mind blowing, nothing amazing, but it doesnt drag itself down, gives things context but isnt that it makes it annoying. The car radio stations are pleasant and some of funny dialog or flat out hints, I always like the station where the guy keeps complaining someone keeps stealing his . The extra side mission content such as the individual house challenges and black market sales keep the loop fresh.

Things id personally like to see change.

More consistent AI, I dont really know what or doesnt trigger them noticing missing items or such, sometimes I ransack the bedroom they are in and they wake up eventually and dont give a , sometimes I steal at $10 pot and its the end of the world.

A change to the micro camera system, especially on hardcore. Remove any ability to purchase tips for tenant schedules(hardcore). As in changing the micro cameras, let you install them dynamically in the house and have access to them while near it. Forcing you to still stake out of the place without it being "Wait in car, get all info" it could also double as a great tool on hardcore for keeping tabs on where tenants are. If the dev reads this, look at splinter cell chaos theory micro cams

Id like more car stealing but I expect it will happen.

This is just kinda a little fun thing ive thought of, but if you keep stealing from a house, lets say a rich tier house, eventually it starts having objects because of the amount of $$$ they spend to replace them. Nothing major, it would be kinda funny if you steal top tier computers/phones/etc and they just get some junk because youve gotten their like 10 times.

Overall, a solid 8/10, it seems it will only get better.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, it is the continuation that I was waiting for. It is pretty much the same game but with new mechanics and completly new districts. There are a lots of similiar mechanics from american theft 80s, another great game from this studio that is a spinoff of thief simulator 1.
New things added:
1:Now you can paralyze tenants or guards by using buton, sleeping gas or tranquilizer gun.
2:You can prevent calling the police by paralyzing tenant fast enough
3:Now tenants may have dogs! You can also stop them by feeding them meat
4:Tenants sometimes may change their behaviour from usual and go to different room, so you have to be more careful.
5:You can use phone from which you can buy lockpicks or meat, make photos, teleport to your car, or invite associate
6:You can invite associate to lockpick doors for you, decrease level of police stars, bring big items to your car or check behaviour of the nearby tenants.
7: You can use drones to discover tenants behaviour or disable cameras
8:You can now start heists that you could see in american theft 80s, but in this game they didn't include changing disguise mechanic which I thought was really cool.
9:Now you can upgrade your equipment on the laptop in hideout
10:Around the map you may discover new feature to dig loot from ground using shovel or find crates that you may lockpick
11:Now instead of having to instantly load save after getting caught by police now you can go to jail

cons:
1: The story is rather straight forward and doesnt add much
2:There is new mechanic of delivery crates in which you can hide yourself and have godmode where you cannot get caught because the guard or police cant open them even if they are open.
3:Maybe drones could also have ability to hack panels because with micro camera all part of studying tenants behaviour is pointless and the only usage of the drone is really only turning off cameras that I think is too powerful because with drone you can turn off 3 cameras at once where in most cases this is exacly how many cameras there are.
4:I think that the thing the games misses the most is workshop where you may create your own houses and compete with players to get the most money or best time
5:Getting on the roof is usually the best spot because you are nearly undetectable and with hacking laptop you can hack everything beeing completly safe. I don't know if it is a good con but at least in 114, the last house on Madison street it is the easiest way to get all loot while staying undetected.
4:Not con but rather information that after level 30 you cannot learn anything new but you can still gain level points that you cannot use.
I may expand this review if I come up with something else

Overall I think this game is great and it is worth the money. Shoutout to the developer that made this game, thank you!
