\r\n\t\t\t\t\t8/10 Fun sneaking game, but with a very small world.

The original Thief Simulator had a number of issues that compelled me to refund it and leave a pretty harsh negative review. However, I am happy to report that Thief Simulator 2 is a vast improvement, and I thoroughly enjoyed this game.

Most notably, Thief Simulator 2 has an actual "free mode" in which there is no story at all, allowing you to freely explore the neighborhood without any of the story mode's forced objectives. The car handling is also much improved. While there are little annoyances here and there, overall, this is an excellent sneaking game.

The only real problem is that the world is tiny: there are only a handful of houses to burgle, meaning that you'll run out of fresh experiences pretty quickly. And once you've learned the house layouts and residents' routines, the exhilaration quickly fades.

Still, I'll gladly take a game that's fun but too short over one that overstays its welcome any day.
