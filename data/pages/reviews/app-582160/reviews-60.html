\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEXTREMELY clunky parkour and combat mechanics that get boring quickly and the story does not make up for it.

NPCs say the exact same things and there's a lot of problems with multiple voice lines layering over each other that makes them an incoherent mess to listen to.

Facial expressions on characters (especially Bayek, the MAIN CHARACTER) look absolutely horrible and ruin any emotion and immersion in all of the cutscenes.

Lots of problems with geometry poking out of the floor and awkward mountains to scale and come down that makes it more frustrating to move around than anything. Oftentimes you will hold forward to go down small ledges automatically but some slightly larger ledges make it so you have to manually jump. It's hard to tell with some ledges and this lead to me holding forward then realizing after about half a second that I have to manually press to jump down a little more. There are so many specific problems that I could go into more detail about that just completely ruin the whole feeling of being an agile assassin that can maneuver around any obstacle quickly.

There's no feeling of being an agile stealthy assassin at all later on in the game really. Every interaction is made completely trivial once you put some ability points into the good abilities.

The lack of diversity in the enemies made all of the clearing of the camp locations around the map an annoying chore that felt the same every time. Being able to scout whole camps worth of enemies with Senu just made it all the more simple. You can literally see all of the enemies through the walls. Berserk made it so even if there was a tough enemy in a camp you could just berserk them and they would fall over and die after a minute or so without you doing anything.

No matter the situation, there was always a mechanic that made the situation simple and uninteresting as opposed to being interactive and requiring any thought at all.

The graphics were great and I loved the setting of the game but I was severely disappointed by the same old boring enemy archetypes reskinned based on the location and given no thought beyond that whatsoever.\t\t\t\t\t
