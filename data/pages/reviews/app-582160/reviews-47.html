\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCombat is so janky. Enemies alternate between perfectly attacking you the second you attack their allies and watching you fight while doing awkwardly doing absolutely nothing. Enemies can attack you while looking in the other direction as well. Not to mention the game's awkward attempts at hand-holding make it difficult to actually play combat in your own way.

Oh, and forget trying to change your controls. If you don't like the defaults you'll have to be prepared to wrestle with a convoluted system that only contains about half the relevant controller binds and manages to show conflict errors for binds that cause no issues while not showing any conflict errors for ones that do actually break things (try binding primary interaction and climb to A, then try to Leap of Faith... you'll just end up stuck in the synchronisation animation while enemies take pot shots at you).

I am a big fan of the earlier games and figured I'd give the new era a try, but this has been an utter disappointment.\t\t\t\t\t
