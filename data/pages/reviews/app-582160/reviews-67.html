\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my first Assassin's Creed game, i didn't know what to expect from this. I was looking for more stealth games after playing Hitman trilogy. And i'm so glad i bought this... Because I love it. Here is what i loved about Assassin's Creed Origins as a first-time player:

I loved the music, because of the Egyptian-inspired instrumentals, with NPCs singing songs in the streets and fields, it was cool to stand there and watch them dancing, and the occasional contrasting of Egypt's mystery, beauty, and diversity.

I'll have to say, more than most games, it's incredibly easy and simply fun to get lost in this game. You'll find yourself weaving in and out of taking out assassination targets for the story, completing hundreds of side quests (complete with arcs, cut scenes, voice-acting, and varied gameplay), raiding enemy forts for loot, exploring hidden tombs and solving lite puzzles, and on and on. This game keeps giving and giving, and I'm 230.3 hours in at this point with still plenty to do.

The writing is fresh, the plot is simple and story is not full of itself. Bayek is, in my opinion, the most likeable and relatable character. He's strong-willed, charismatic, and full of heart, and with lots of funny quotes.

The details in this game are honestly mind-blowing; the gameplay, the world-building, the story points, etc. This is a game you should NOT rush through. Do not miss out on this game. Glitching and freezing issues aside, this is absolutely worth every penny and will easily keep you entertained for at least 100 hours.
I cannot wait to get more of this game and its sequel(s).\t\t\t\t\t
