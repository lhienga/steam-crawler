\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHands down one of the best Assassin's Creed games, it features a very likeable protagonist, who is widely considered one of the best in the franchise along with Ezio and Edward.

The story is well-written and compelling, and it rarely, if ever feels drawn out or unnecessary. The world itself is vast, exotic and beautiful, rich with culture and life.

In this world there is so much to see and explore, with an equally abundant amount of side content to complete.

Assassin's Creed Origins introduces an all-new combat system which adds a lot of depth and challenge to the franchise. You have many weapon types to choose from, with different move sets and applications for different encounters and enemy types. These weapons also have different stats, perks, and rarity which have a notable effect on gameplay.

A new skill tree and level up system is also introduced in an RPG-like fashion. This may add another sense of progression and accomplishment by giving you the ability to acquire new skills and equip better gear, but it may also be quite frustrating when you are under-levelled and unable to complete certain activities because of hard-hitting enemies that you are unable to defeat.

All in all, this game gives a much needed breath of fresh air to the Assassin's Creed franchise by being an all-around enjoyable experience in every way. If you are a fan of the AC universe, you will be sure to enjoy this title.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAssassinate, Pillage, Repeat.

I know I'm late to the party, but here is my review.

This game starts out very interesting and then becomes very repetitive. I honestly don't hate repetitiveness in this case, because the game mechanics were just so fun! My main motive in the game was upgrading my stats for new skills, armor, and weapons.

Visuals were stunning, like I could probably just put on some lofi and watch the water. Just gorgeous!

If all you care about is fun game-play and visuals, then stop reading this review and play this game! You will love it!

On to the biggest negative of this game, the story. It has an awesome starting plot and your very interested in what will happen. You and your wife are also beasts, and absolutely awesome as characters. But man, the story lines suckkked. I always felt invested in the beginning and then let down by the story endings, or I didn't care about the story at all. Its almost like they hired a writer in the beginning to have awesome plots, but then fired them half way through.

Anyway, that was my only frustration with the game. Everything else was on point, and the maps were huge!

Themes: Adventure, Action, RPG, Historical - Egypt, Greece, Rome.
