\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought this on sale for $8.99, but can't recommend it even at that price. Trying to launch the game from Steam opens an old Ubisoft app called Uplay, and then you're down a rabbit hole. The Steam forums point in a promising direction - apparently Uplay has been soft deprecated in favor of Ubisoft Connect, and with a bit of work, one can uninstall Uplay, manually remove uninstalled folders, uninstall Ubisoft Connect, reinstall Ubisoft Connect, log into Ubisoft Connect and presto, the game will launch from Steam. None of that worked for me, but I really appreciate the effort folks on the Steam forum have put into reminding folks who post about this issue that they could've found an answer simply by searching the forums.

I imagine this is a great game, and probably a great deal at $8.99. But for $8.99, I'm not putting in the amount of effort it seems to take to get the game to do the one thing all games must do: run so you can play them.\t\t\t\t\t
