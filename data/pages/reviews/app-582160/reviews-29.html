\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI hated this game. I detested it and wanted to throw it out of my steam library. I was a ardent fan of old AC franchise, It was a shock to me how they have reworked everything to fit into an RPG. I hated every hour playing it, the monotonous grind was too much that I almost gave up on this game.
Then after investing a considerable time on it leveling my character, I started enjoying this game. I finally understand there is a method of playing it. So this took time for me to adapt and find my comfort and technique of playing this game.
Here are some tips to help you play this game better.
1. You will not complete this game in 10 hours. The whole mechanics of the game is to gain xp and level up, which takes a lot of time and you need to invest in various side missions and clearing out bases to up your xp. Only then you will be able to handle higher level enemies.
2. Get used to the RPG elements. I feel like this game is hugely inspired from Witcher 3. Just like the withcher, the main guy Bayek has to investigate and solve different mysterious, find clues and complete missions. You will be riding on camels and horses a lot... i mean A LOT. So get used to it.
3. Once you begin the game, your character Bayek is very weak to a point that you cannot go hack and slash every enemy you find. You will get killed by higher ranks very easily. The main point here is to level up your character first in your first playthrough. After that when you complete the game, Newgame + will be unlocked and you can play on higher difficulties which will be a breeze in the wind. It is in this second playthrough you will enjoy the game... the most. Your character will be the strongest and you can one shot any enemies right from the start.
Overall the game is pretty good, only if you know how to play it. Take your time understanding it and this game will offer many things.\t\t\t\t\t
