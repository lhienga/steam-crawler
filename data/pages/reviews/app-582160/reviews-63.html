\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story is mostly boring, the narrative is full of revisionist PC garbage and the gameplay quickly gets stale after a few hours.

However, there's a ton of eye-candy. Like, a TON. Exploring egypt and seeing how much detail is put into the cities, temples, tombs and environment is pretty much the primary reason to play this. Lacking any other immersive adventure game with ancient egypt as the background, this tickles that particular niche for me. 10 hours in, I started skipping cutscenes and running through quests just to level quicker so I can properly explore more areas without getting curbstomped by higher level enemies. But the exploration is well worth it. The setting is that well made.

Wait for a sale if you honestly feel like giving Ubisoft your money though. If you don't feel like giving them your money however, I highly recommend you get this through whatever other means are available, if just to give it a try. I highly recommend playing this on a decently modern system however.

Side note: You can pet the cats. But no Bubastis and therefore no Bastet temple, therefore no huge house of cats to feed and pet. A genuinely incomplete Ubisoft experience.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: Assassin's Creed Odyssey is a better game, but Origins is right behind it.

I have not yet beat the game, but I can say this so far: it is absolutely inferior to Assassin's Creed Odyssey in pretty much every way. Odyssey sets a high bar, so that doesn't necessarily mean that Origins is a bad game. If you are looking to play these games and have not yet tried Odyssey, go for that one instead. The main benefit in my eyes is that Odyssey has far more customization as far as your gear goes, and having more customization to hone your play style is a crucial part of a modern RPG. The environment is also far more beautiful, and the Atlantis DLC adds a massive amount to the game's story. Side characters in Odyssey have stronger, more memorable personalities.

Take my word for it. Get Odyssey if you haven't already. These games frequently go on sale at significant discounts, so keep an eye out!\t\t\t\t\t
