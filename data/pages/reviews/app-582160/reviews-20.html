ORIGINAL:
I am quite disappointed. I have the presets on HIGH. Not on VERY HIGH or even ULTRA. No. On HIGH! With a freakin 4070! Single digit frames!

My Disappointment is immeasurable and my day is ruined.\t\t\t\t\t
