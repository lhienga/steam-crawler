\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThoughts halfway through the game.

Would definitely recommend to AC fans, Ancient Egypt fan and anyone who likes bit of a grind and getting lost in an open world.

Various types of terrains and cities with their unique personalities.
Like the rich Memphis, some poor villages, swamps and islands, pyramids of Giza and no man's land to east of it.
These are just few things top of my head that I visited recently and enjoyed.

Getting killed by travelling bandits or ambushes in no man's land many times, and then able to plan and striking back was whole new level of satisfaction.
Stealth is decent since AIs are not very smart. I personally don't do much of melee attacks and like to rely on bows/stealth/tools. Like throwing a fire bomb, then sleeping dart over it and then assassinating everyone is pretty funny.

I would recommend to play the game at the highest difficulty with level scaling, although hard initially it really makes you rely on stealth with better rewards.

Other plus points-
The game looks gorgeous and I get full framerate on my medium level PC.
Steam Achievements (unlike Valhalla)
Simple skill tree and weapons
