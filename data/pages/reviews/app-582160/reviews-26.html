\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn my opinion this game has the best game play out of all the assassin's creeds.
Combat:
When it released it was the only combat system in the series where you could do combos and could execute enemies with full adrenaline.
Stealth:
The stealth game play is my favorite in the series. I preferred sneaking into guard outposts as opposed too areas of a city. The stealth sections of this game are better designed to use the crouch mechanic and take cover behind stuff. With the last two games before this I felt like the stealth game play was very linear with not a lot of choice, where as this one is much more open and allows you to approach the mission however you want. Also this is the only game where it is much easier and quicker to move bodies. What is also nice is that they give you a bow in this one. I did find that using the bow would make the stealth game play too easy at times so I stopped using it so much. It does have cover assassinations in the game but the animation where he pulls the target in does not always activate, but he does still kill the target though.
Story:
The story in this game is one of the best in the series. Bayek is one of the most bad ass protagonist in the series. The story is not like any of the other games and really is a breath of fresh air. I don't want to say too much about the story to avoid spoilers.
Other:
People say that this game changed too much to be a assassin's creed game, but a lot of stuff they changed they made better. And this game still felt like a assassin's creed game. It still had parkour, stealth, and assassinations. And the parkour in this game is not bad at all. The problem with RPG assassin's creed games parkour is more with how the map and cities are designed as opposed to the parkour mechanics. But for this game there are a lot of areas where it is better designed for parkour. It is not so well designed for parkour in some of the bigger cities though.
