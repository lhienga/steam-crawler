\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have never played any of the Assassin creed Games, and my friend Talked me into purchasing the bundle that came with all of them, and here is quickly how i feel about it.

the Good: Controls like Butter, Sound design is amazing, Graphics are beautiful, especially with Reshade, Gameplay is fun

The Bad: The one biggest issue with this game, is the Quests, the Mainline Quests are fine, its all the Side quests that are the issue. They are only filler quests..

By filler quests, I mean they are all the same thing with the same objective... you eather have to carry someone to safety, Assassinate someone or something, or Escort missions.

Those are literally the ONLY 3 objectives every side quest in the game is, so far anyway 21 hours in, it is EXTREMELY repetitive, the mainline quests are fine, they throw in some different objectives,but they are also guilty with following this pattern, just not as much as the side quests, they just feel like copy and paste quests with altered short stories with them.

The mainline quests level requirements are far apart, 1 quests would require you to be level 20, when the next one will require you to be level 28, and you cannot complete a quest if you are 3+ levels behind, so you are FORCED to play the repetitive Side Quests to level up to continue with the main story.

Its like it was made to make enemies that are 3+ levels above you to be impossible to kill, and are a 1 shot to you.

I personally would rather the game be shorter, and have only main quests, or just a few side quests, rather than 70% of the game being the same 3 objectives.

The game is fun, looks good and runs extremely smooth, just the horrible side quests which are the issue. I recommend giving it a go, just expect to be bored with all the repetitive side quests.\t\t\t\t\t
