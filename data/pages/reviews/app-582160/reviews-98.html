\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGraphics: Really good, Egypt looks amazing in this game.

Music: Almost no ambient music, the soundtrack is boring only has 1 good song and it's Ezio's Family.

Gameplay: Kinda fun, stealth is viable in the majority of cases but not very deep, direct combat is always much faster and easier and are kinda fun. RPG stuffs are there but not too much. Parkour is non-existent, you hold one button and Bayek moves, that's it. You can do back eject and catch ledge (the game doesn't tell you about it) but they are hardly ever useful. There is a lot of side content and most of them are pointless and doesn't add anything to the characters and the world, they feel more like random tasks you do for money and XP than stories actually. You can still have fun with it if you enjoy the gameplay loop, but don't expect anything. Also no replay value.

Story: The second weakest aspect of the game, behind music, imo. The story is too long and not very good, there isn't really any memorable moments or characters. The voice actor, especially for Bayek, did a really good job in this game, dialogue feels natural and has emotions. However they are sometimes overdramatic (especially during post assassinate interrogations) and emotions shift unnaturally quick too. Modern day introduced a new protagonist but it really hadn't add anything yet. There are also a few retcons to the lore are honestly quite weird and dumb that I'm really not a fan of.

Overall: It's an OK game if you just want to kill some time. It should not have been an AC game, since it doesn't really add anything to the franchise, but if that was the case Ubisoft probably could not have sell it very well. Don't expect to get a good Assassin's Creed experience out of this. And if you are considering buying this because it's an AC game, I recommend you DON'T do that, it is NOT WORTH your time and money.\t\t\t\t\t
