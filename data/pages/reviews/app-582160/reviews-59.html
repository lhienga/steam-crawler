\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAssassin's creed origins is a pretty good game. Ubisoft went in a different direction after Unity and Syndicate, and tried to make this one an RPG. It is obvious that they took inspiration from Witcher 3 with some of their game design. There really are a LOT of side missions and it would take a ton of time to finish every one. However, most of the missions lack in depth and the game can really make you grind a lot in order to progress. Regardless, strangely enough, the grind is not too bad. The fact that they have build a marvellous recreation of ancient Egypt might be the reason. There were actually a few memorable quests which left an impression, but for the most part, the game is a bit monotonous. Despite all these criticisms, AC Origins is one of my favourite AC games.\t\t\t\t\t
