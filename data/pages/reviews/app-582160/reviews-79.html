\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlaytime : 107 Hours Played
Achievement : 100%
Short-Review : Totally Recommended


A. Game-Score (1-10)

- Story : 10
Betrayed, Losing everything, including yourself.. You tried to become the true protector of egypt as a Medjay, whilst you seek for a revenge as a father. The more story goes, the more you understand Bayek, and the phylosophy of how and why the Assassins is created. An ideology of seizing a freedom. Remember, "Nothing is true, Everything is permitted"

- Gameplay : 8
During your playthrough, you will understand the love that the Dev had for this game. A mixture of the Culture of Egypt and video game made this game so beautifully made on every aspect that the open-world game should have. You will find yourself learning about the Ancient Egypt, its Gods and Culture, while playing this game.

Adopting the newly RPG-element to this franchise, made this game even more challenging and fun. Although pretty much all of the missions is so repetitive, where you just have to clear a bandit camp, save someone, or kill a certain person. The size of the map is also big enough for you to explore, which is also a part of the reason why this game took long enough to beat. The minigames concist of a race and gladiator arena.

There is also a "discovery-mode" in which i find it so intriguing that you can learn the history of Egypt from this game.

- Completionist : 9
Only if you care about completing 100% achievement, this will took +100 hour of gameplay

- Graphics : 8
Everything that you see on this game make you believe that you are in the actual ancient egypt.

- Audio : 7
The music really felt like an ancient egypt, though the voice actor of most of the characters including bayek doesnt really "tune-up" for me.

- Difficulty : 8
Since this game adopting RPG-element, you have to raise your level in order to have a fighting chance against the enemies who are most likely had more level than you.

- Grind : 10
Since youre gonna have to raise your level, you have to complete the side missions and any side activity/collectibles that you can find. The map is so big that you will find yourself taking more time for exploration and with your horse/camel than with your own wife.

- Bugs : 2
I hardly find any bug/glitch on this game. Theres a few, but none of them are the game-breaking bug.

B. Review
It is highly recommended to play this game if you love Open World - RPG games. Even more if you really love Assassin Creed Franchise. And even more if you really curious about the Ancient Egypt history, culture, architecture and the gods. But get yourself ready to grind the hell out of yourself because that is how you have fun with this game.\t\t\t\t\t
