\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts been 6 years and this game still holds up to this day.
The RPG style of gameplay with crafting, leveling up and looting for gear really has you coming for more not to mention the vast recreation of Ptolemaic Egypt will make any lover of history exploring the world for hours in search of loot and secrets hiding in the game world. One major addition in this I find my self booting up from time to time is the Discovery Tour mode which as a Historian myself highly recommend for the educational value it provides by putting you up close in a immersive environment along with actual photographs of material as well as recreations of historical environments such as Roman Forts, a walkthrough of the Mummification process as well as showcasing everyday life in the era.
I cannot recommend this game enough if you are new to the franchise or are looking to hope back in.
I often play on the Steam Deck which does work if you can get around the Ubisoft Launcher which is a easy fix.

Rating: 10/10
If you love Egyptian History or Culture, this game will have you hooked.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAssassin's Creed Origins stands as a testament to the evolution and refinement of the beloved franchise, offering a captivating journey through the sun-drenched landscapes of ancient Egypt. As a long-time fan of the Assassin's Creed series, Origins not only met but exceeded my expectations, delivering an immersive and revolutionary gaming experience.

First and foremost, the attention to detail in recreating ancient Egypt is nothing short of astounding. The world of Origins is a living, breathing canvas that captures the grandeur and mystique of the time. From bustling cities to sweeping deserts, every location is meticulously crafted, making exploration a true delight. The level of historical accuracy is commendable, creating an educational yet entertaining experience as players traverse through a rich tapestry of Egyptian history.

The narrative of Assassin's Creed Origins is a compelling odyssey that delves into the origins of the Assassin Brotherhood. Bayek, the protagonist, is a character of depth and complexity, and his personal journey is interwoven with the broader political and social upheavals of ancient Egypt. The storytelling is masterfully executed, keeping players engaged with a perfect blend of intrigue, emotion, and historical context.

The introduction of RPG elements in Origins breathes new life into the series. The progression system, skill tree, and customizable equipment add layers of depth to gameplay, allowing players to tailor their experience to suit their preferred playstyle. The fluid combat system is a joy to master, offering a satisfying blend of strategic planning and visceral action, making each encounter feel uniquely dynamic.

The introduction of the eagle companion, Senu, is a stroke of genius. Senu adds a new dimension to exploration and scouting, providing a seamless and immersive way to survey the sprawling landscapes of Egypt. This not only enhances the gameplay but also strengthens the connection between the player and the vast open world.

Origins also excels in its commitment to providing ongoing content and support. The post-launch expansions and events have kept the game fresh and exciting, ensuring that players have a reason to return to the captivating world of ancient Egypt long after the initial release.

In conclusion, Assassin's Creed Origins is a true masterpiece that rejuvenated the Assassin's Creed franchise. With its breathtaking visuals, engaging storyline, and innovative gameplay mechanics, it sets a new standard for open-world action-adventure games. Origins is not just a game; it's an immersive journey through a bygone era, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of players and standing as a shining jewel in the Assassin's Creed legacy.\t\t\t\t\t
