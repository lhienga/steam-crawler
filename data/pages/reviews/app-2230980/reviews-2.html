\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game ran surprisingly great on my Steam Deck... until I reached the Dutch Gambit quest line and beyond. Now essential NPCs are failing to spawn, quests are bugging out hard, and the game has started crashing more and more frequently. The only way I've been able to progress is by installing the game on my PC and completing bits that are insurmountable with the Deck.

(Seriously I tried for several hours to get one (spoiler related) part of the main story to work, even validating my game and redoing large parts of the quest before. Only for it to work flawlessly on my original save when I loaded on PC.)

According to the developers, the game is completely playable and enjoyable on the Steam Deck and was extensively tested, I can only assume they play tested it for about 8 hours and just assumed the rest of the game would continue to function.

Honestly, I want to give this game a thumbs up, by given how I bought the game hoping to be able to play through it completely on the Steam Deck and can't, I am forced to not recommend it until the developers keep to their promise and make the ENTIRE game playable on the Deck.

Edit: Having put many more hours into the game on the deck, after occasionally having to use my PC to get quests to function properly, I have ran into a "bug" that's much more game breaking. I have no clue the cause but sometimes the graphics will just start to completely break and become incomprehensible (the screen flickers wildly, menus start popping up for no reason, the camera randomly shakes.) I have no idea what causes it, but it forced me to stop playing, I tried changing Proton version, reinstalling the game, restarting my deck, and even reinstalling Proton, but nothing fixed it. I thought my save was cooked for the deck (it still worked fine when loaded on PC.) However, I turned off my Steam Deck, went to bed, and for some reason felt like trying it again, and it ran perfectly fine. I have no idea what could cause a bug like this to occur, but made the game unplayable for period of time, so be warned if you do want to try to play this on the deck.\t\t\t\t\t
