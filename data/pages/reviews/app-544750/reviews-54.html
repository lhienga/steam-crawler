\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLots of mixed feelings on this one, especially on the character creator.
Soul Calibur is my favorite fighting game series. This one is probably better than SC4 and DEFINITELY better than SC5.

But really, I'm more likely to recommend this specifically for SC fans than fighting game fans in general. There's a lot here to enjoy, and some of the story content is surprisingly well done. I just don't think it really has much draw unless you're already emotionally invested.

As far as the character creator goes, it feels incomplete. There's lots of different kinds of creatures you can be, but it doesn't effect anything besides giving you less clothing options than if you were a human. Lizardmen hardly have any options, and the few things they can wear clip horribly.
Most of the clothing options can't be worn with options outside of their own set, so you're really limited creatively.
And most of the options are designed as fetish gear or modern clothes rather than armor or outfits relevant to the time period.

That said, it is a fun game. Just manage your expectations and wait for a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
