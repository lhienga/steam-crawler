Solid 10 from me.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith friends I strongly recommend this game, most of the issues the game has can be circumvented with in house rules, and friends.

Alone? You should get any Soulcalibur older than SC5; There isn't much content, and game mechanics are abused by the bots, I would pass on this game playing purely solo, as the game plays nothing like previous SoulCalibur games, and is greatly lacking in content compared to previous entries, though the character creator remains strong you cannot use the stronger features of the creator in the singleplayer libra of soul mode.

If you are wanting Character creation go for it on sale with dlcs, if you are wanting a "Soul Calibur" game I would recommend getting an older entry instead if you can; This entry feels more like Tekken 7 than Soulcalibur, and is far more guarding, and meta characters focused when it comes to fights compared to previous entries.

The character creation is great as always, but the rest of the game feels lacking in comparison to previous entries, no tower, no ability customization, and less equipment to customize with compared to prior entries. Quick nitpick, you can't do fancy custom characters in Libra of soul, kinda making the whole custom character thing less cool in that mode since you have no access to textures/patterns, stickers, and shapes in that mode.

The newest and most annoying addition to the game by far is the meter which allows for unfun cutscene super attacks to be performed frequently allowing the user to escape just about any attack, this is a problem most fighters have already had and solved by now; I don't get why they felt like SC needed a meter and cutscene supers that can't be punished. I do however love the new cinematic like rock paper scissors like thing, it's a nice spectacle during fights and it builds hype.

Soulcalibur 6 is far more unforgiving in its gameplay compared to an entry like soulcalibur 4 which felt far more beginner friendly and pickup and play where you could just about use any character and do well. SC6 feels far more like it is trying to be tekken or street fighter than soulcalibur with how little freedom there is in combat compared to previous entries(most moves feel like a hard commit, and landing an initial hit is pretty difficult at times, and if you aren't constantly blocking you are just going to get combo'd to death which is something that I haven't seen in the series up until now.), recovery is also pretty difficult in this game and just altogether clunky feeling, whereas sc4 I never had any issues with combat, mechanics, or recovery. All these issues are more pronounced when playing the singleplayer modes, though against a friend these things don't seem as bad as they are.

Character creation seems to have less parts than previous entries even with the dlcs....this is rather disappointing considering development for this game appears to have stopped; Though on the bright side the character customization here is the best in the series with patterns/textures, shapes, and textures to help you make your character just the way you want, though you may have to get creative with the parts.

Get the game on sale, enjoy the character creation, but don't expect to enjoy the core game alone unless you love learning entire movelists/combos just to be able to play a game, soulcalibur 6 really doesn't play anything like the older entries. It's still fun, but get it on sale if you aren't the combo fighter type.

Honestly it's still a lot more fun than a lot of stuff I've played recently and it holds my attention quite well.

I still hate the supers though despite the recommendation I suggest you keep this in mind, unless you are lucky enough to be blocking at the time you just eat damage and it is ridiculous, my brother took full advantage of the super meter and would super like twice every fight, we've sorta made a silent rule not to use the supers as often, though I wish there was support in game for disabling the cutscene super attacks; With this in mind if you can handle spammy attacks and you are getting this for the singleplayer content I'd recommend waiting for a sale. if you can't handle spammy attacks and getting cutscened I would recommend buying the game when you run out of fighters to player, or if you have a friend to play with, bots use the supers relentlessly and it is really annoying.

TL;DR Customization is the best in the series, libra of soul doesn't let you use all the customization stuff, the game is really fun with friends, the bots are really annoying, cutscene supers are unpunishable; Buy it with all the dlc on sale, or be disappointed by the low amount of content.\t\t\t\t\t
