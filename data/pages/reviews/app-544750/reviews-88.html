\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSoul Calibur is finally on PC and it feel good. It has been a long time since I played a Soul Calibur game since Soul Calibur 2 on the GameCube and it is still the same great gameplay, as I remember. Four button with 3 attack button and guard button.
A easy fighting game to get into but hard to master. I enjoy playing all the the characters but I still a favourite on nightmare. The story mode is a retelling of the past games with a creator a character story mode as well. the classic are there like arcade mode, training mode, create a character mode, museum and of course online and offline VS mode.
I do have my problem with the game mainly with DLC characters which more fighting games are going into now. Personally I want finished game with all the characters than a half baked game which more modern gaming suffers today. And SC2 had a survival mode which SC6 is missing.
A part from those problems I really enjoyed the games and I would reccommend Soul Calibur 6 when it is in a sale.
