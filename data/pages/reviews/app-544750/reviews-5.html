Good amount of fun characters to play with and the character customization for custom characters is great. The main story of each character is good enough I guess, pretty short you can sweep through many stories in a few play throughs, won't take you long to discover each and everything the game has to offer, local co-op versus mode is fun with friends, the combat is easier to understand, button mashing helps.
