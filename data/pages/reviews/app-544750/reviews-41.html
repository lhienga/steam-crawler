\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGREAT AND SOLID SWORD-FIGHTING GAME

I think I have enough experience venturing the fighting game genre. I only have played a bit of Street Fighter and Tekken and kinda get what this genre aims to be and what sort of fun factor there is that made people coming back. Although being a competitive-centric game, there's alot of love and bond between different games and franchise in the same genre that kinda create this sort of brotherhood or community. This is refreshing from where I'm from where most of the conversations are just "A is better than B" or "B is underrated, therefore you are wrong." Ahem... Final Fantasy... ahem.... Monster Hunter... ahem.... other RPG games....

Soul Calibur VI is 3d fighting game where people fight which weapons rather than fists. You get to play warriors from the medieval period which is 1500s from a variety of countries. Although it took place in history, this is a fantasy game where there are dark forces, monsters, demons, and the lot. So the combat will be flashy and not as grounded like Tekken. But still every hit feels heavy, steel meeting steel or flesh.

The story is fine. But I can't but being dissapointed in it. The story tells a tale about the two magical swords that is the title game and Soul Edge. From what I know it is a reboot of past Soul Calibur games but still have new parts that make it a sequel, hence the number 6. It retells the story of Soul Calibur 1 ONLY. I kinda got duped by marketing (or just internet in general), that it is a retelling of all past Soul Calibur games which is 1 - 5. The post-first game story is just a standalone character story which takes place after the main story. Keep that in mind for those who have the same expectations as me.

There are two story mode. First is Soul Chronicle which is the main story between the main characters of the game. The main story is just two hours long which is sufficient. Other than the Soul Calibur 1 retelling, there are standalone character stories which are thirty minutes long each. This is fine to know more about the characters you want to know. Unfortunately, there are no characters that interests me. I kinda found them all to be bland. Honestly, Ivy is the most interesting for me but only on the surface level. I don't want to be horny anymore, I just want to be happy, In harsh words, I found the story to be boring.

The other story mode is Libra of Soul which you play a character creation of your own and venture the medieval fantasy earth in order to dispose Astral Fissures thats been poppin' up. These fissures are hurting people. This mode allows you to venture through points on the map, and some locations requires money to travel. In different points of the maps there are sidequests you can encounter to know more on the lore or grind some money. You also have access to a variety of weapons with different stats which is the money's for since enemies will get harder depending on their level. This mode is fun and very unique in general. I like these choose your adventures stories. Unfortunately, I will keep on playing it if the story and lore is remotely interesting.

On to the combat, this game reached the same high for me as Tekken. You have 4 attack buttons which is horizontal attacks, vertical, kick, and guard. There also multi button inputs that will give counter, ultimates or supers, and special attacks. I have fun with the combat and is very easy to get into. Probably one of the more accessible and beginner friendly game with a low barrier entry which is good. There are also stuffs to get into for veteran players... maybe (I don't know).

Other modes in the game are the traditional Arcade Mode. This mode will give you ten characters to defeat and try to get the fastest time. Good ol' Arcade mode but much better than Tekken 7's arcade mode. Unique to this game are Creation which you can make your own fighter. The customisabilities of this game are various and I pretty much spent a lot of hours in the creation mode. After creating, you can choose which weapon do you want to use. Keep in mind this Creation mode is not for Libra of Soul mode which has you made another Creation. Libra of Soul is pretty much its own game.

Soundtrack is good. High fantasy vibes but nothing for me to go wild about.

Overall a great game but I expected more for the story and lore.

4/5\t\t\t\t\t
