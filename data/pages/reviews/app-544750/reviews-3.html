\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHow is the game:
-Feels a lot closer to SC2 as far as fluidity and freedom of movement which 4 and 5 really restricted for some reason.
-Get it on sale.
-The game has next to no tools for learning. So if you want to learn a bread and butter for Hilde (hey that's me) you are better served watching a YT video and trying to dissect the goofy ass lingo. Which for what is a pretty dead game it may not be worth the hours in the lab. I would hope the next entry does have some combo trials and better tools but I doubt we are getting one anytime soon. It seems Bamco is in the Tekken boat and I don't blame them. It would still be nice though.

Netcode and online:
-The reality here is that this game has a super low population and while I would love for that to change (so I could play more people instead of supergamer6969 who grinded the game) the netcode is delay based and say it with me "Delay based bad, rollback good." Please plug in a cable if you can.
-The best thing you can do is to have a friend who is wired as well suffer with you or play local with a buddy.

DLC:
-So...DLC and season passes are in this game and it is what it is. I would recommend purchasing the characters individually especially if you just want a couple of em (Hilde my beloved). It really is not worth shelling out the insane prices of the season passes unless you want the music packs from older SC titles. So I wouldn't bother tbh.

Characters:
-Ah yes the selling point! Geralt and 2B are worthy inclusions especially Geralt. I will say 2B is fairly busted but at this point I don't think that is going to change so complain to the clouds if you want but I wouldn't bother.
-Balancing from a casual glance seems fine. 2B as I said already is a little goofy and Nightmare still can take up a good chunk of the stage with a beeg swing but hey...that's just SC for you.

TLDR:
-I wish this game didn't get buried and maybe if the netcode was better it could have had legs and ran for awhile but it really did come out at a funky time right before the pandemic. Tekken 7 was just plain better as far as popularity and while the netcode was not the best it manage to hold its players better. SC's rep had gone down after SC3 and SC4 while SC5 didn't have a lot of the classic characters a lot of fans enjoyed so why bother buying it? I mean shoot, when SC6 came out it I did not even bother trying it out at the time since I really fell out of it after SC4 and was not keen on giving the series another chance. So yeah if it is on sale for an attractive price and you want to goof around with some friends go for it. You will definitely have fun. Too bad the game just doesn't quite hit.\t\t\t\t\t
