\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is difficult to say I don't recommend the game, as honestly Soul Caliber II was a classic favorite of mine and the devs tried to maintain the characters and their abilities.

Unfortunately, they still haven't learned to make a proper single player story that doesn't involve random long-winded cutscenes that are rather unnecessary for adults, who primarily buy this. Its similar to Dragonball Z games: they may be fun for a bit, but aren't ever as good as we can imagine.

In addition, I memorized the classics and despite being similar, the fighting movements aren't all the same. There were combos I could do by muscle memory in SC2, but now most of them have been changed which is, disappointing.

I got the game on sale, and glad I did, because it is nowhere near worth its full price. 90% is graphics, 10% is plot, story, voices, and game options. Unless you can't live without this game, I'd look around for something better.\t\t\t\t\t
