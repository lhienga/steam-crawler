\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed this a lot but not on Steam around 2 years ago. Recently got it on Steam thanks to the big sale. Soul Calibur VI is AMAZING. Very satisfying for fans since the early games. The story modes are magnificent and it's incredibly satisfying that it particularly touches plot points that have only ever been mentioned in the past through text, background information, or easter eggs. What would make it complete is if it had literally all the classic characters (Charade!!! Yun-Seong!) and I personally wouldn't mind characters from SCV (though I don't know how that would mess with the story lol). DLCs suck so bad, especially when the characters you main are DLCs. Though, continued support for the game is very nice. Libra of Souls is fun, it brings back the adventurous story-mode feeling from Soul Calibur III though this time, you are the main character. The Character Chronicles' execution was very good, the timeline approach makes it easier to piece everything together and it's awesome to see how everything is connecting, and what has been happening. Gameplay might be the most fun in the series so far, Soul Calibur V even though the negative reviews had a very fun gameplay and VI just made it better. The fast pacing feels satisfying and fitting. Though, a VERY BIG SHAME that there is not even an option for controller vibration. Online matchmaking is hell and takes forever to match so I'm very unsatisfied about that. And in my experience so far, when you do match, the lag/delay is terrible. Also, for some reason you can get matched with someone way different from your rank. Beginner VS. Someone in the 20k, totally ruins the fun and it happens so often. Graphics are amazing, and the stages are very beautiful and well designed. Character designs all look nice and fresh. Music is just hands down beautiful. The homages to the previous games in the series is wonderful.\t\t\t\t\t
