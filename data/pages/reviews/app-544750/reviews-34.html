\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSoul Calibur 6 is a well rounded fighting game that is accessible and easy to learn. That is not to say that it is without its complexities, but one can pick up the game and start playing the story content with little to no issue at lower difficulties.

The roster is quite good, especially if you can get the DLC characters (which I would recommend buying on sale, as the game goes on sale frequently), and each character feels pretty unique.

The writing for the story missions isn't amazing most of the time, but I have certainly seen worse, and it manages to evoke emotion every once in a while. Everything is voice acted which is nice.

The game runs well, looks fine, and is not very resource intensive, which is also nice. In my opinion, the good parts of this game very much outweigh the bad parts of this game.\t\t\t\t\t
