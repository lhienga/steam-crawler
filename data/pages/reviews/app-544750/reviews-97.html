The game has most of the classic SC1 characters + a few more new and later characters by default, several more are DLC. The create a character mode is good although I feel they could have included more costume parts from older titles by default. The worst part in my opinion is the stage design compared to SC1-4, the majority of designs are much more plain and boring with an annoying depth of field effect (which can be modded away if it bothers you enough). Perhaps all the interesting stage design went into Tekken?

My recommendation is to buy the game when it and its DLC go on sale to maximize the value of your purchase.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust don't buy it.

Arcade mode doesn't even reroll encounters, and AI is dumb af, always uses same juggles and attacks, counters your attacks because it can handle frame calculation betters, and then proceeds not to block and stand on idle for few seconds.. Sadly, there are on avarage 6 people playing online so if you like to wait for 10 minutes for one fight with 270 ping, then be my guest.
I got it for 5$, but I end up simply annoyed every time I try to play it.

The worst Soul Calibur game I played, and I played all from Soul's Edge up to SC3:
CONS
- reversal edge are scissors rock paper game, not needed in a fighting game
- super attacks look like naruto fighting games
- lots of bad gameplay outside of battles
- Arcade mode has no boss at the end... oh wait, look below
- supposedly you get to fight inferno boss only on legendary - why? im a ♥♥♥♥♥♥ player so I won't experience whole game?
- Legendary mode is always Kilik juggling you for 15 seconds and then you loose
- Both story modes are boring, meaningless dialogues and loading screens split with 1 round battles. I don't really care who is who and when, I want to use cool weapons from third fight and have 8 round battle with astaroth
- Geralt music is not the fight music from witcher 3
- Areas are all similar, plain and somewhat empty
- No force feedback
EDIT:
Oh my lord the main story mode is a disaster.
Boring meaningless dialogues... you try to fit into the game, try to sink in.
You get to hire a mercenary. First mercenary i could hire was SPONGEBOB.
Oh ok. There goes the immersion. Lets try to use him.
Mercenary fights are 100% AI controller. You do nothing. You just watch as 2 dumbnumb AI dish it at eachother.
Fk this game in particular.
Pros
+ Fighting is ok'ish

Got the game for like 5 dollars so Im not refunding it. Not worth more.\t\t\t\t\t
