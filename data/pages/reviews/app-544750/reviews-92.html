\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy favourite fighting game. The focus is mostly on neutral but combos do exist without to high of an execution barrier for most characters, pressure exists but isn't oppressive, movelists are massive yet fairly simple once you know what you're looking for (every character has a guard break on AB and a launcher on southeast B, and there's a wide variety of defensive options each with their own upsides and downsides. Oh, and the roster is fantastic.

Where other devs are dumbing down their games to make them more accessible, Soul Calibur 6 has added new mechanics instead, and I'd still argue it's one of the most accessible real fighting games on the market. No complicated inputs, The move variety helps show off where your timing may be wrong, dedicated block button. Hell, reversal edge is a new defensive option which exists to ease new players into understanding the systems by giving them a simplified version in every clash.\t\t\t\t\t
