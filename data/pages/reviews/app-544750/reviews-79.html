\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis iteration of Soul Calibur is great when bought on sale and looked at as a budget title. There's not really anything new here, but if you know you like Soul Calibur then you'll probably like this. It looks good, it plays smooth but ultimately, with the way other fighters are growing and evolving this is a pretty forgettable spot in the franchise.

Soul Calibur 6 is a pretty bare-bones package for a $60 title with all the cosmetics and extra characters being sold as microtransactions. I feel like that would justifiable if there was more depth involved, but the single player story mode might be the worst of the series and no other game modes on offer other than online VS. At this point online is pretty hit-or-miss though, rarely are there ever enough players online to make the wait times worth it.

That said, I bought it at a steep discount and I can definitely recommend it to anyone else doing the same. Getting $10 of value out of this game is pretty easy to do.\t\t\t\t\t
