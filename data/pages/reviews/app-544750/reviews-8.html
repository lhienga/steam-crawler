\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSoulcalibur is an excellent weapons based 3d fighter that I've had quite a fun time with. I haven't been able to engage with the franchise since 4 due to switching primarily to PC gaming so seeing so many new and familiar characters has been a wonderful experience that has been worth every penny. Especially considering that this game is a soft reboot of the franchise. I think that makes it a perfect entry point for anyone looking to get into the series as well. If you like fun 3d fighters with excellent characters, fun story, crazy levels of character customization, and unrepentant fanservice to boot, this game is right for you.

My only criticism would be that you can't customize your weapons to be soulcaliburs or soul edges. That was one of my absolute favorite parts about customization in 4. I really hope they bring that back either in a dlc or the next game, which I'm eagerly awaiting.

Other than that, solid 5/5 game. Don't delay, pick it up today!
