\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdate: Yeah I've been playing it a lot recently, but that's because I got a friend interested in it and it can be fun to play from time to time, especially if you have all of the DLC like I do. But I still don't recommend it unless you get it all on sale, including the DLC.

This game is a huge disappointment as a fighting game enthusiast and as a Soulcalibur fan. I don't recommend it because it doesn't look or feel like a current gen fighting game compared to every other franchise out there. Here's a more detailed breakdown:

Presentation
First of all - presentation, there is none. The game has no intro CG video or even in-game graphics intro like the previous entries. It has nothing hyping you up the same way that Tekken 7 does.

Story
The story in this game is a visual novel with fights in between them, but there's very little "visual" to it, it's just portraits talking to each other. If that's your thing, cool, but it doesn't fit a fighting game at all and overall feels like a fan made game, especially since the dialogue is cringe AF.

Visuals
This game just flat out doesn't look as good as any other fighting game out in the market and it came after most of them. That's just depressing. The textures and models don't look anything like their artwork counterparts (which are great btw), there's no finger animation at all so the fingers just go through your weapon at all times. Again, Tekken 7 looks better and it's the same company, wtf happened?

Progression
There is no progression, there's nothing to earn. There's like 5 joke items locked behind in-game currency you can earn, but they're so awful that I doubt anyone cares. Everything is unlocked from the moment you purchased the game or the DLC for it.

Character Creation
Did you play Soulcalibur 3? Soulcalibur 4? yeah it's still the same, a few enhanced features and a few new items, but overall very lackluster. It's not even that varied and most of it looks bad mixed together, so you spend way too much time trying to make something decent out of a pile of trash. Don't be fooled by the meme creations, the character creator is not as good as it seems. Also, in order to get access to all of the pieces available you have to be willing to drop an extra $50 or more on the DLC to get all the pieces. This is a very expensive character creator game.

Gameplay
It's still Soulcalibur but with supers. I mean, it's whatever. It performs well, but it's hard to get into a game that's supposed to look way better than this. It just feels like they gave Soulcalibur pity money so they could do another entry to gauge fan reaction.

Music
It's still great, it has some new tracks, but nothing that is so memorable you find yourself listening to the music on youtube all day long. Tekken 7 has more memorable music and the Tekken series doesn't always have a good soundtrack by comparison.

If you go to steamcharts.com and compare any other triple A fighting game to this one, you'll know all you need to know on whether this game is successful or not.\t\t\t\t\t
