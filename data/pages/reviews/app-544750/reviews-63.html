the story mode is long, you need good hours to finish everything even rushing with fastest possible time, also a lot of content that older games not had we see here, who are saying that ended the game with 30 minutes are lying grotesquely, because the story even if you try to jump all the dialogue, it look more than 3 hours, but if you see everything, it will took much more time. with 12 hours of gameplay playing only the story more, i not made everything, i need to finish 2 characters yet. even the fights in story more beign easy and last 5 to 10 seconds, the story mode take time to finish.

every character show a different point from the story and zasalamel beside we not play any battle, is very needed to see, he explain with details the facts about the story.

when we finish some stories, a continuation opens sometimes for other characters that we already finished. so, is better to get back and check other characters that you already made and see if unlocked more chapters

but now i'm playing more the other modes, is better to do the story more little by little, there is a lot of information to read, things that was missing or bad explained in older games.

SUPER EASY TO UNDERSTAND THE MECHANICS, a player must be retarded to not understand it. is one of most clean and easy too get used, maybe the easiest game in existence to understand, after 10 minutes you already take it, and every character can beat every character with that, the is no super weak one to others as some games do. people say that soul calibur have it, but definitely, this game doesn't have this at all, under any circumstances.

as always, soul calibur got the correct colision and hit box that tekken never had. people attacks soul calibur as if was tekken and praise tekken as if was soul calibur, i really can't understand why they reverse the strengths and weaknesses of these games, also i really don't know how the same team made both games but they fail so hard with tekken but they made soul calibur absurdly perfect, i think only 2 moves are wrong here but is really few, when tekken the animation reset like 90s bad done arcades, soul calibur the mass and physics are well done. tekken is good for sure, but soul calibur is perfect, still infinite years ahead (until they fix the tekken 90s arcade hit to define how many years tekken got to evolve enough to reach soul calibur)

aaaaaaaand, sure, fight game with swords, shields, spears are missing most times, soul calibur is one of few, and is one of best fight games.

the mission mode is nice but not that easy to understand in begin, after some time you see that is simple, the mode is like power up your level, complete quest, advance the story by maxi crew view.

the soundtrack here some times has less presence than soul calibur 4, but some tracks rocks much more than any older game had. better rise the bgm sound a bit because the sound is lower here compared to other games from the saga.

about the performance, this game is not bad optimized, is normal. i'm using a FX 6300 with 750Ti, even with 8gb ram still run ok everything set to the maximum, the game runs fine, high fps and no frame drop. and well, with win 8.1, with w10 maybe you will need more machine...

about the characters move changes...

ivy is easier to use here, but seems weaker, i need to use her more times to see, but looks like she is better to take the basics but the advanced is harder, when in SC4 was opposite, hard to understand even in begin but the advanced from her is not that hard if you already know the basics.

nightmare is faster, but some moves i miss, is stronger some ways but weaker in others, they balanced him with others.

mitsurugi for me is weaker, the SC4 version was a bit harder to use but stronger, the character still worth, but is not that boss like anymore...

sophitia is almost the same, just one or two moves changed a bit

so, few examples.

custom characters here is one of most complete to build the body, but the equips are less than SC4, so, is much betten to make the character itself but is weaker to equip, but yet way better than SC5

neutral things by my view:

the raw game came with a good number of characters, but the fact that we need expensive dlcs to take many others is a bit abusive, yet, how the game is really well done, to times like this that we live, i can forgive. but is better to take the packs with discount... unless you are looking for steam points... then it's stonks

bad points:

just two... the control config, even being easy to set, the menu controls are different than in game, even if we configure, the p2 side will affect only in game, but menus we need to find it and we not have a way to configure. the other problem is that steam crashes if the start the game with other player on remote play connected with xbox controller, the game still runs but steam close, so is impossible to "boot" the game with controlled connected with correct port, as p2 (because i'm playing with kb now)\t\t\t\t\t
