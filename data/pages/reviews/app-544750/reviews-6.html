The story is basically the same as SC1. But with more anime things, it doesn't feel like a SC game. *Having some weird "X-ray' type thing when you get your soul gauge filled is weird. Though you can block it, it feels like it should belong in an MK game instead. *There is a rock-paper-scissors thing in the game that also shouldn't be in the game; it removes you from combat and feels unsanitary. But it's still a fun game. I'd still recommend it, but it's not without its flaws compared to the older ones like SC & SC2. However, I still want Ivy to dominate me.

If they make another SC game, then they should go back to the basics and do what made SC popular in the first place: less anime-style things, keep the character creation but change it up, and make it so you can earn more stuff from playing instead of like 2 characters and a few items. Though it's not like saying any of this really matters as they're not going to make a new SC anytime soon, or at all, SC is like NAMCO's forgotten child that was left to die. After they took its soul out, if I'm wrong and they do make one that is good, I'll eat my own words.

It is still better than SC3, as your save won't corrupt if it does something wrong—also, there are no Star Wars characters to worry about. That leaves me with 3rd party characters, in my opinion. No, SC doesn't need them to be a great game; that might not be the most popular opinion, but to get SC where it needs to be, they either need to have no 3rd party character or one that will get people excited about the SC series again.\t\t\t\t\t
