\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWas just looking for a random game to waste my time on, and SoulCalibur VI just fitted the vibe I was looking for, and after nearly 50 hours of playtime, it became my favorite fighting game.
The campaign is divided into two parts (Libra of Souls and Soul Chronicles). You get to create your own character to play the Libra of Souls. Soul Chronicles is the main story and it also has character stories.
and also ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥... the character customization in insane!
95% of the story is told in a graphic novel way (so lots and lots of reading), and there are a few cutscenes here and there so they feel special.
The story is not the best, but still better than most of the other fighting games.
Online mode is great but it takes like 5 minutes just to find an opponent.
SoulCalibur VI shines best with it's single player content, and it takes around 40+ hours to beat everything.
The fighting system is really impressive, specially the combos. It's so satisfying to press the right buttons at the right time and see the character hits the perfect combo.
Character design and level design are also impressive, and whoever decided to add Geralt and 2B as playable characters should definitely get a raise.
Easy 9/10.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnderrated. Fair warning: I main Sophitia/2B.

Low execution barrier with good breadth of options and deep enough game play to stay interesting. Some characters are easier to start playing than others and I would say even are unfair vs. newbies not accustomed to them. But the game is balanced enough so that the fairness curve levels out the more you learn to play and delve into mechanics/moveset options.

Steam summer sale's a good time to buy in (season passes 1 & 2 contain all the other DLC). I recommend having a friend or discord group to play with, as the online Steam community has a relatively low player count and is likely filled with highly experienced players. Some of them are not so kind, but there are those also looking for more people to play with and learn.

Great visual effects and presentation, the music from previous Soulcalibur entries is available, and you can do many great things with the character creation system.

As with any fighting game, you get out what you put in.\t\t\t\t\t
