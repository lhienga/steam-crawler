\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started playing Soul Calibur when it released back in the PS2 with Soul Calibur 2. I mained Yun Seong at the time and have always mained him in the succeeding Soul Calibur games that came after. I passed on the 5th installment because of his absence. When this game came out and he was nowhere to be seen, I was still planning to pass on it... Until they announced 2B and later Hwang. 2B is one of my favorite SE characters and Hwang, while I did not know anything about him; I saw that he played like Yun Seong and decided to give the game a shot. Now I have to say that I like Hwang way more than Yun Seong, lol. Gameplay wise, SC is super fun as usual with a good roster that also includes Geralt of Rivia and Seshomaru. I like the amount of customization you can give to your favorite warriors. It's too bad with the story though, I really did not like the visual novel style of story-telling. Nothing wrong with visual novels, I just didn't expect it for a Soul Calibur game. But overall, the game is totally worth it and if you've never tried out an SC game before, now is a good time.\t\t\t\t\t
