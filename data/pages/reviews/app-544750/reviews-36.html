\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTbh... I tried to like this one.. I really did.. Even had it on PlayStation & decided to buy it again on PC to give it a second try. As a huge fan of the Soul Calibur series & fighting games in general, this has to be the most soulless money grab attempts of a game Bandai Namco has put out. The combat is amazing - don't get me wrong, but the lack of content put behind paywalls pulls me away a lot.
Soul Calibur V had so much to do in terms of arcade, roster, online battles, and definitely the character customization options. The character creation is what made SC unique compared to other fighting games. Here, it feels stale. It's almost like they took SC V and simply dumbed it down by a lot - even visually. Less maps, less characters in the roster, the story is eh, and there's literally nothing to unlock for character creation or the character roster (unless you wanna pay for season passes lmao).
I'm not hating on the game though. It's not bad at all. If you like simple competitive fighting games, then this is definitely a series you should get into, but if you were coming from the past SC's (especially V) and expecting this to be better or just about the same, then you're gonna be wildly disappointed.
I know SC V got a lot of hate and it "almost killed the series" as some may say, but it really wasn't that bad. I'd recommend V over this.\t\t\t\t\t
