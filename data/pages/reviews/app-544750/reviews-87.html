Definitely not as high quality as Soul Calibur 2, 3. But lives up to what I enjoyed about 4 and 5.

The Good:
- Visuals
- Game Feel. The game runs smoothly and works great and is fun to play.
- Sound Track is solid.
- Playing 1v1 with friends over Steam Remote play works really good. Is a lot of fun!

The Mixed:
- The Roster is a love it or hate it. The fact that characters that SHOULD be part of the base game are tacked on as paid DLC is stupid. The only one worth buying is 2B.
- The single player content is weak and not alot of fun.

The Bad:
- The Menu's are awful.
- The classic Arcade feels more underbaked then in the first game.

I still think it's a good game, definitely worth it at around 20-30 bucks on sale. Full price? Not a chance. They need to step up their efforts in 7 since Soul Calibur is easily my favorite fighting game outside of Smash bro's.\t\t\t\t\t
