CONTENT:
There is loads of content for both single-player and multi-player.
The single-player content includes story modes for each character and for the overall game, which show that the developers have a love for the game they made, as the story is brought in a strong new direction, fixing the missteps of the last game. There is an extensive gallery and museum, for much more lore content.
Multi-player is also much fun, though it lacks some of the bonus modes like team battle that were available in the series' best games. Additionally, it can be hard to find a match online, as there aren't many players actively playing online.

Graphics:
In-battle graphics are stellar, and set and character designs are vibrant and clear. Story modes are mostly told through still images and/or text, which is a letdown.

Audio:
The game contains almost all of the songs in the game's history in its museum. New songs aren't as noteworthy, however. Individual characters' story modes are fully voiced.

Controls:
Controlling the character is responsive and fair, once you get used to it, and the training, museum, and Libra of Souls story mode do give much to learn how to play. Many characters have their individual mechanics, which combined with the overall gameplay mechanics can be a bit much to learn, but it is still fun.

A strong recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
