\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed this last year. This game is either underrated, or lacking some elements that makes other FG like Tekken 7 shine. Story is okay tho I didn't finish it. Female characters are prettier like Xianghua. I wished they could make this game better tho. I just want the two sisters (Cassandra and Sophitia) be the guest chars in Tekken 7.

Gameplay wise is quite unique. There's some mechanics that I like that are not existing in any other FG I played. Characters get half-naked after getting few hits. Oh my god. The music, tho not fitting for a fighting game like this, but I think it fits the story quite well.

For online match, I think the netcode is okay tho I only play a few matches and didn't continue (was addicted to MGSV at the time) coz I have no time to play it more. Might try to play some other day.

Customization is god like. I heard u can play Asuka Kazama in this game. Lol.

I recommend it if u want a variety of flavors in FG u play and decent story mode.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWorst SC game in series.

I'm an old SC fan so I just want an online version of SC 1/2/3/4 etc which used to be a fun competitive game. This is now as very simple game with a lot of complexity removed. Many options are gone, move lists are smaller and simplified so a lot of combos are the same button, can't low parry and the reverse edge minigame interrupts the game.

Everything is tailored to new players so if you want a casual fighting game this could be for you but for old SC fans this is a big disappointment.

The graphics are mediocre and the levels are limited too.\t\t\t\t\t
