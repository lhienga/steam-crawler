\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great game. The roster is balanced really well and every character is tournament viable. Many people complain about the Reversal Edge mechanic but there are counters to that. For example there are moves that are Reversal Edge safe where you can side step afterwards or you can break the RE itself with specific moves.

That being said I have three complaints.

1. Single player options are mediocre. The stories are rather uninteresting and are conveyed in a boring text based style. When you do fight, most CPUs just stand there and do nothing but get smacked by you. It's really really boring and it's why I've never and never will beat the main single player story mode Libra of Soul

2. Created character hurtboxes are not aligned with the character models of the style they originate from which is the reason why most serious players frown upon using custom characters. This is a shame to me because I love my custom Lizard characters so very dearly.

3. This game is at the stage in it's life cycle where there are very few players left and the ones that do play are super strong. The PC version of the game has the toughest player base out of all three versions as it by fare has the least amount of input lag both offline and definitely online. It's rather staggering that I can get substantially less lag playing with people in the UK from the New England region in the US on PC than playing someone virtually in my own state on PS with a LAN cable, and I live in one of the smallest states in the country.

All in all this is a great mechanically accessible fighting game, the players are very nice and likely would be willing to teach you how to play if you know where to find them which usually would be discords for particaular characters.

If you are interested in learning the game yourself, I can instruct you if you PM me. I don't bite much haha

Oh yeah and FYI I have around 2k hours on PS version I only recently got the pc version

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnly get this on sale. Good party game if you want a flashy fighting game to play with friends. A lot easier to do impressive looking stuff in this SC compared to the older ones. The Character Creation is pretty good with minor flaws (some clothing floats on characters, like belts). Online is pretty dead and most players are pros. Single player is lacking, I wish they had survival, team battle, and other older SC single player modes, but what is there isn't the worst. I don't know if I'm missing something, but I can't find a way to adjust the amount of rounds in arcade mode, which is something you used to be able to do in older SC's. Hopefully Namco comes back to this game and gives it some much needed updates, but to be honest, they probably wont. I'd probably give this a 6/10, what's there is pretty good, but it feels like more was planned for this game than what we got.\t\t\t\t\t
