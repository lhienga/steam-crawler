\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so much fun- this is my 2nd SC game after SC1 and I'm absolutely loving this.
I'm really bad at fighting games- the pinpoint accuracy and timing of combos and directional movement keys always throws me off, and it was really disheartening for me cause I absolutely adore fighting games from my childhood, but whenever I try any such game I fail miserably.
Well, SC6 fixes all that with much simpler combos- it's very easy to play but also it can be hard to master. So, this truly is the perfect fighting game for me and I highly recommend this to any beginners or anyone like me who's afraid of fighting games but want to get into it.
Now content wise, there's a lot- there's 2 story focused campaigns, one is Libra of Souls where you make a custom character and start a rpg like journey. It has no voiced dialogues whatsoever but the stories within are interesting and diverse.
The other campaign (which I haven't even started) is about stories of each of the main SC6 characters.
I can't really say anything about it as I haven't started it yet but from what I've heard most characters have a good story.
Besides that, there's the usual arcade mode, the training mode which is really helpul and also just a fun place to fight without time/ health constraints and I've spent a lot of time there.

The online mode I haven't tested much- did 2 fights and then got disconnected but I know that the playerbase is definitely small- so I won't recommend this if you're looking for an online fighting game.
Personally, I don't care about online component as I mostly play solo.

And the last but definitely not the least important part of SC6- the character customisation- Jesus Christ it's so much addicting- you can pretty much make any character you want. Here just take a look at my TR Anniversary inspired Lara-
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2523870062
And I'm even that good at this stuff- it's my 1st custom character but there are some truly amazing characters I've seen from other players.

Another thing I forgot to mention regarding all these dlcs and season pass- well, thing is from what I've heard the devs made SC6 on a very small budget and it is kind of a miracle this game actually came out. The core gameplay itself is great, so I guess they cut some corners in the cinematic videos, extra characters and all that.
The base game itself has 21 chars and all the dlcs combined have 6 more. And tbh, 21 characters for me is already so overwhelming- I've only played using taki style for about 10hrs and I'm just starting.
So 21 characters is already a lot to me but I do understand if any of you are returning SC players and maybe your favourite char is not in the game. I got the deluxe version with 3 additional chars (including 2B), you guys can get whichever version you want- it's just that buying individual sp or chars might be more costly so I slightly recommend deluxe version more.

But anyhow, I think Soul Calibur 6 is absolutely a 10/10 game and I especially highly recommend this to any newcomers to fighting games!\t\t\t\t\t
