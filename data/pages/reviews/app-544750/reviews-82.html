\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell this is something I never expected.
So I got this game and DLC around 70% off during the NAMCO sale.
So here's my review.
I got to agree with the other reviews that this game is most definitely severely lacking in single player content.
It comes to something when I can say the 3rd and 4th part beats the 6th part in single player content.
The Story mode point blank does just like the Reboot Mortal Kombat did where you don't select your character and instead plays assigned characters through a story.
Them you have another one where you create your Character.
Making the Single Player come off as even more weaker in content.

Then you have a generic no story Battle mode.

Now we have the Character Creator.
Yes just like the last ones you use existing characters weapons and move sets. But now you can recolor the weapons and attack effects.
Except now some clothing looks to big depending on what options you have equipped.

But yes the Character Creator is the highlight of the game.
Don't I repeat don't pay the full 89.99 nor the over 33.99 per a season pass! It's not worth It!
I'm a veteran Soul Calibur player dating all the way back to Soul Blade and I feel a little ripped off. But not so badly to get a refund. Because this fills my need for a create a Character fighting game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSoul Calibur 6 ? More like...Souless Calibur 6.

By any means, it is not a Bad game. It is good, I loved it even. Played it from time to time again, just to recreate some character creations, doing a little Versus mode.
That's it.
Remember in Soul Calibur 3,
Story Mode had In-game cutscenes, giving the players more contexts of what happened, even a QTE which change the outcome in some fights. The story told through wall of Texts, but atleast there are choices to pick, and each choices will had different outcome, even encounter some new characters.
There are also unique Movesets like Lancers, Dancers, Ninja, Whip, Iron Fan, Sword and Shuriken, even a Magic staff.
Even a Created Character Story mode (Chronicle) which had its own story, but still in the same Soul Calibur universe. With a bunch of memorable characters (Abelia, Strife, Chester, Rudiger, Luna, and more...) despite being a created characters themselves, there are plenty of reason to like them and even care for them.
Also an ability to buy all extras through In-game Shops, using in-game currency.
Soul Calibur 3 is like the peak of Soul Calibur series, the Gold Standard.

Soul Calibur 6 however had None of that.

NPC characters are forgettable and unimportant, 98% of unlockables are paid DLC, story mode sucks and Im not even care to revisit or even end it, Libra of Souls is bland and meaningless since all weapons you get cannot be used in Arcade or any fights in the game, all Arenas in the game is boring and none of them stand out(funny how day and night variant from some of the levels took 40% of Arena slots lol why...), and there will be no more Characters coming

RIP Rock, Aeon, ZWEI, and all unique movesets from SC3...

The only good is the Combat has been improved and even more cinematic than it used to be.
The character creation worked way too well, and the only unique things in the game.

I loved this game, but at the same time I hated it. I could not recommend any hardcore fans to get it(you'll be dissapointed) but Im not gonna lie, there are some Fun to be had in this game. Versus mode still entertaining to play, even with friends.

At this point this game is pretty much abandoned by Namco, outclassed by their "Gold Boi" Tekken 7.
Its gonna be 6.5/10, only saved by the super fun combat and unique character creation.
