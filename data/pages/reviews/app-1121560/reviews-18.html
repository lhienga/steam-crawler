++ Alchemy is fun and deep.
+ likable characters (including NPCs)
+ There are many side activities to do
+ bench characters get battle exp
+ can fast travel to everywhere
+ classic press a button to advance dailogue
+ Overall experience is good and fun
+ The greatest thing in this game is Ryza herself. especially her boobs, I am not into thick thighs that much.

+- combat is fun and new to me, but too easy for my taste. Alchemy is great, but I don't have much incentive to upgrade my combat items that often.
+- everything has a slow progress : story, gameplay elements unlocking. maybe, some people will like it. Story in the beginning is kinda slice of life thing, just Ryza and friends want to adventure and help her people task. Slow gameplay unlocking means you will have constantly something new and looking forward to it and that keeps things fresh.

cons
--- no English voice. There are only subtitles in story and talking. and characters talk a lot outside that : combat, exploration, menu, Alchemy. They are all meaningless sound to me. sometimes, that's kinda annoying.
-- Role description translations are very confusing. example : from my testing, the game "row" actually means "column" in normal people sense, You should see the Steam guide by Okamiroy.
- map is lack of info, so I write my own version of the map. It's kinda a fun thing to me, so I don't mind that much.
- boring side quests (help islanders tasks) and there are many : just talking, craft an easy item, give it to them --- This is the form of fetch quest of this game.
- When you visit places around the Island and Atelier, you will trigger a mini side story event. many times, the side events are not fit with the time being of the main story. like, charA angry, you walk to the next map, charA happy and thankful for your deed
- Personally, I think the price is kinda expensive, mainly because of English voice lacking. compare to other JRPG of the same price range, other games have English voice and longer story content (many hours of my playtime come from Alchemy)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcelent game!

The crafting system is deep and fluid, with ways to absolutely break the game very early on if you have a lot of time and patience, that gets progressively easier as the time goes on, to the points that it's much easier to just finish the game first then destroy any balancing possibilites later. It's a pretty nice system for the hardcore grinders that want to min-max from the first step, and to the people that want to enjoy the story first and then tackle end-game content with OP characters.

The story is pretty good and I'm happy there's no world-ending conflict like what happens in most JRPGs. It's just a light, well developed story, with interesting characters.

The OST is pretty nice, if a little repetitive early game. The visuals are usually pretty good, but some of the animations are janky (the "cut-scene" running animations kill me every time), The voice actros did an amazing job (Ryza's and Klaudia's being outstanding, but all of them very good).

The combat is pretty fun and fairly complex, though you can break it super easy late game to the point that the "hardest" DLC bosses can be easily killed with basic attack spamming and nothing else.

There's a very shallow pool of enemies that repeat themselves constantly with re-skin variations, and that's a little disappointing. There's also a lack of boss fights (the Elementals being reskins are also pretty sad) and boss fight music.

All in all, the pros far outweigh the cons and this is an easy recommendation and a 10/10 for me.\t\t\t\t\t
