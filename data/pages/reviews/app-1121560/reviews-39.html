\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my first Ryza game, I'm happy I picked this one. Atelier Ryza is a cute game one can spend hours just farming for loot to make better loot. Alchemy is your friend and the UI for doing the deed is fun and easy to get a hold of whats happening while at the same time can get very advanced to get the very best out of it, strongly recommend you make your own gear, leave the store gear alone as much as possible. Alchemy is not the only good thing about this game, the fighting system is new turn based while offering real time actions. The fighting style similar to the crafting system is simple yet takes awhile to master.

Visually the game is really pretty, a kind of anime style with an appreciation for the human form, with a warm European type architecture, with wide fields. The art style of the game gives this idea of wonder and encourages exploration, you will yearn to see what's over each hill, what resources each bush will produce. However the music is a bit so-so, its not bad but it not all that.

So Its a good game. give it a try, not only for the thunder thighs.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAWESOME chill game. If you like crafting and alchemy based games then you will love it. Story wise it could be much better. It's just a basic coming of age tale and of course, friendship. Combat is active but fairly simple if you know what you're doing, so you need to be active with your crafting and use proper items as well in battle. Not counting the endgame, you should have no trouble going through this game. As far as the endgame goes, it's not really time consuming.

I finished the main story at 34 hours and took me 10 more hours to 100% it, so not really that much of a grind. Keep in mind that you'll spend a lot of time in the menu creating items, duping items, and farming certain materials which by the end of the game isn't hard as you can create unique worlds which contain items and mats which you specify so you can farm them easily. Also, the game is super breakable if you know what you're doing with synthesis and not just relying on automatic synthesis.

All in all: Chill weeb game that you'll enjoy if you like spending lots of time crafting weapons, items, and recipes.\t\t\t\t\t
