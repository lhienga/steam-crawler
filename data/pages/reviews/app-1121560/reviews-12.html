\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI just only have one word to describe this game, amazing.

Truth be told this is my first Atelier game I've played but I loved just about everything about it! The gathering, alchemy, combat, characters, story all of them were fantastic. The combat especially was very interesting as It features real time turn based combat where the game doesn't wait for you inputs so you have to choose your moves rather quickly or else you get overwhelmed. As someone who usually despises turned based combat, I loved it! My only complaint would probably be side quests as they aren't really that interesting to be honest, that however didn't stop me from getting every achievement.

Overall this is a fantastic game, that I recommend to anybody that likes gathering, crafting and real time turn based combat.\t\t\t\t\t
