\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"That's a gold coin! My father is fond of trading people for them!"
Umm... ok, so how many people do I get for 50 gold coins?!?

Game is great *IF* you can get past the horrid control scheme. If you play with a controller, it's no problem. If you play with keyboard and mouse (and the mouse support is GREAT), it takes a while to figure out all the keys, since the tutorial is NOT converted to keyboard layout.
Best out of the box mouse support I've seen. LMB - Use/collect, RMB - Jump, MMB - engage in combat/special collect.

BUG: W and S do not "wrap around" in menus, but "Up arrow" when at the top goes to the bottom instantly and reverse for "down arrow". I didn't find this out until the last hour playing. -_-

Also, down arrow pulls up the local map. This is absolutely critical for navigation. And once you get further in the story "N" will pull up the teleport map.

This is one of the extremely rare games I've took the time to 100% achieve. The further along you get, the more fun it gets.\t\t\t\t\t
