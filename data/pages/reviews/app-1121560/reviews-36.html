\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMeh game. It has so much wasted potential... Let's get to this quickly and divide it to make it easy to understand:

-Game looks good, aesthetics are not something out of this world but they are fine.

-Story is meh. You can get to love the charas to a certain extent but there's not really much more to it.

-Now what is interesting about the game are the unique mechanics. The combat system and the alchemy mechanics concretely. But they are just too hard to understand. The game is a bloody mess, the learning curve is definitely too steep. I learned more about the game mechanics out in the internet than in the own game... needless to say, that is not a good sign.

-And last but not least, the game needs a lot of quality of life changes, like a BLOODY LOT. So many thingies are just so tedious when they could be so easy. Making something that could be done in 1 min take 10 or 1 hour isn't a very good concept of fun, for me at least.

All in all, I'd say this game is a decent grab if you can get it somewhere over the 20s~30ish$, but definitely not worth the 60ish$ tag, it is just not that good, but it is a decent game, it definitely was worth the play.

P.S. After fighting the last boss for 2 hours and a half with only one character and beating it I take back what I said. This game is trash and its combat mechanics were not thought at all, or that thing couldn't happen
