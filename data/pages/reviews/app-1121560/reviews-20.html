How to run on steam deck
This is a copy of my review for Ryza 2. The difference being the game requires special launch options for Atelier Fix to work (see step 3). The launch options were posted by several people on Proton DB, so I'm not sure who specifically to credit.

1. Google "atelier fix" and look for doitsujin on github. Should be one of the top results.
2. Open local files in the game's properties and extract the fix to where the exe is.
3. Go back to the game's properties on the General tab and paste the following into the "Launch Options."
WINEDLLOVERRIDES="d3d11=n,b" %command%
Otherwise, the game will crash on launch (The rest of the guide is for steam deck only).
4. Look up how to get the latest GE Proton, there are several guides and it will help get better performance out of most titles on the deck.
5. Ryza 1's graphics options can be found in the launcher. I found turning off shadows makes the biggest difference, but the game does not look very good without them. You may have to experiment a bit.
6. Hit the "..." button and bring up the performance tab. Toggle on per game settings and set the deck to 40hrz. The game runs totally fine at 40fps, it gets you a much longer battery and the vent won't kill you anymore. Stable 40 is also way better than unstable 60 in some of the larger areas.\t\t\t\t\t

So the game itself took me 59 Hours to 100% anyways the Main Quest line was really great however I have a few gripes with the game so here they are

1. Why are the collision boxes of stairs so bad? I can get stuck on them without effort unless I'm sprinting

2. The pacing of side quests seems really poor and I understand they are meant to be played in batches as some overlap however its really awkward to get them to unlock without any prompt as I went through the entire game without figuring out how to create wind boots and so did all of the quests I could up until a certain point and didn't know how to unlock them without searching it up

3. My finale gripe with the game is during the ending scene I panicked thinking I couldn't complete some quests due to being at the end [It was in fact just not showing any and wouldn't let me do them as it was the final goodbyes so I lowkey had a heart attack

Anyways getting on to the good stuff about the game THE ART STYLE omg it's so pretty to look at it's made me want to start trying to use this art style myself. The main story is great and will definitely make you want to play the sequel(s) [Just found out a 3rd is coming in February]. The characters all have a clear intention behind them I.E the token muscle head and the go getter but I still loved how they broadened there characters especially Lent and Bos. Hoping to 100% the 2nd and 3rd games soon too :)\t\t\t\t\t
