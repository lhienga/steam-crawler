\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is RECOMMENDED for those who really LIKE jrpg and anime. Just not in general.
The good:
-Graphics. It just a standard anime post processing, the player model and the color hue saturation)
-Combat is actually quite fun. It looks fast at first glance but I get used with the pacing.
-Simplified recipe hunting. Reduced pointless wandering.
-Alchemy system is fantastic. You have more control to what you want to synthesize.

The bad:
-Storyline is just dull. Not much to talk about. I know it is suppose to be "summer memories" but it is lacking something.
-Side quest is mostly boring but the rewards sometimes good

Overall, I can't find anything to justify the price tag other than the fact that this is a niche game. I only recommend this game for those who likes JRPG.

Graphic: 6.4/10
- World (7/10)

Storyline: 6/10

Audio & Sound FX: 5.5/10
-Music (7/10)
-Voice Acting (6/10)
-Environment (4/10)
-Battle SFX (5/10)

Combat : Battles are not that difficult and usually doesn't take a long time. Each character has their own skills that requires synergy with other character. Some battle requires you to be strategic. (7/10)

- Enemy HP and DMG vs Team HP and DMG (both side equal lvl) is mostly on average side sometimes very hard
- Time taken to eliminate enemy (both side equal lvl) is normal sometimes very long
- Skills are limited and exclusive for each character
- Roles are interchangeable
- Some monsters have debuffs and skills that requires you to be dynamic.

Extra (8/10)
Alchemy system (8/10)
-Recipes are not difficult to find
-Gathering ingredients are limited but plenty
-Easy to tweak your traits
-You can use Item rebuild to increase quality or trait
Weapon Forging (8/10)
-Useful to increase stat


Final Rating = 6.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
