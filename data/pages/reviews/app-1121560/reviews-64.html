\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd never played an Atelier game before. Was rather good.

Alchemy system is fairly deep though a bit tedious, it'd be nice to have some extra auto-fill buttons and filtering for when you want to go for alchemy level ups, otherwise it takes awhile. The combats very enjoyable. Great anime graphics.

Story ended a bit abruptly and I wasn't expecting the character interactions and overall mood to be quite to childish. Not anything I would 'take points off' for. Just not my type. But the rest of the game was great as an rpg.

Overall good, would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
