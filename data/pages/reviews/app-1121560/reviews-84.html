\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a shame to see bad reviews based on issues with the game running on Laptop. it runs perfectly on my PC and honestly its a fantastic game.

The new combat system adds some complexity and excitement that was missing in previous titles in the series, the graphics have had an overhaul (which I assume is causing some people's problems) but other than that at its core its an Atelier game, cosy, fun and wholesome just like always.

The new take on the Alchemy system is fun, not quite as good as the systems used throughout the Mysterious series but far better than the basic system in Arland.

The series has gone back to the free roaming more open feeling that they introduced in Firis and honestly this is the biggest selling point of the game for me, Firis was my favourite Atelier game and this was a big reason why.

As for the characters I'm not completely sold on them yet, but they are likeable enough, I'm not even close to far enough in to provide a final decision on them yet. The voice acting seems a bit more mature than previous games which makes for easier listening too (I loved Firis, Lydie and Suele from recent games but they grated on the ears a bit after a while).

In total this feels like a game that fans of the series will continue to enjoy, and that people new to the series can get in on because it is the start of a new subseries. 100% recommend.\t\t\t\t\t

Con

Lots of gimmick tools were given later on to make alchemy even easier.
Some end game content for you to challenge.
Haven't had a single bug though out the whole game play.

Con
Not enough artwork for Jrpg.
Warning, lots of money grabbing DLCs and post game side story are coming.(although if u are willing to pay it might be a good thing)\t\t\t\t\t
