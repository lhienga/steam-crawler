\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played all of this game on the Steam Deck and I had no issues whatsoever with it (make sure to install the fix for it, check ProtonDB for more info: https://github.com/doitsujin/atelier-sync-fix).

This game got me to play JRPGs. This was also my first Atelier game. I kept reading opinions online about what "the best Atelier game is", but the opinions are really split. The first Atelier Ryza is a good starting point, even if it doesn't have the QoL implemented in the second one or other Atelier games. This game is good in its own right.

I do recommend playing it with a controller, as the PC controls on this one are very bad. I got the game on a sale, and I spent a lot of time in it and I loved it.

You do get to a point where grinding is needed, which that can be a big minus for some people, but you can push through and finish the game. Or there are "means" to speed that up, wink wink.

The story is nothing superb or unique, but I liked it. Granted, if you do not like the ansty teenager trope, which happens for the first hours of the game, you will not enjoy this. I REALLY enjoyed the alchemy aspect of this game and I really enjoyed crafting all the items possible and gearing up my team. The battle system took a while to get used to and it's not my favorite type, but I got used to it.\t\t\t\t\t
