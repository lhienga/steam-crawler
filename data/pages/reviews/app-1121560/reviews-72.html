There is a lot of information to take in at first for players new to the franchise, but the game allows for a "brain off" experience. You'll have to do a bit of digging to make the items you need later down the line, but generally the progression system doesn't feel as grindy/taxing compared to other JRPGs.

This is a very CHILL game, I wish I got this on my switch instead, but the PC version doesn't appear to have any issues (must use controller), this just feels to be a end of the night chill game to unwind to. There is room for improvements in terms of clarity, but overall the game was a great experience.\t\t\t\t\t
