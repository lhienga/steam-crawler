\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe most disappointing game I've ever enjoyed.

For me the failure of this game is not in it's shooting mechanics or level design. If you're playing solely for the gun play I'd say they knocked it out of the park as it feels even better than BL2.

No, for me what completely killed my interest in this game and franchise really was the way the story and characters were handled. If you don't care about that stuff have fun, but legacy characters being removed/omitted from the story to prop up this annoying teen girl character was unforgivable to me. They were trying to recreate a genuinely sad and emotional moment from BL2 and it was not deserved, and they didn't do it once, but twice. Also Axton and Sal don't even show up in the base game or the DLC I played so if they were your favorites, tough.

I was so hyped for this game I played BL2 to death and BL2 is probably in my top 10 of all time honestly. BL3 on the other hand, while being better on a technical level, just don't have the soul or humor the 2nd game did. Handsom Jack was an incredible villain and these 2 streaming siblings are lame and annoying in comparison. I find myself playing BL2 on VR these days if I play Borderlands at all. Will probably try out BL1 at some point too.

You can pick this up if you want, like I said the gunplay isn't bad, but for me, I kinda hated this game if I'm honest, not because it wasn't a competent game, but because it ruined a story and characters I was beginning to become invested in.\t\t\t\t\t

It probably wouldn't be the worst. But comparing it to the rest of the series its a pretty significant drop in story quality. And the god awful character writing. The gameplay is incredible don't get me wrong, the guns feel awesome and are extremely varied with the corporations and alternate modes. But good god the writing is like scraping a chalkboard with a shovel. Its just awful in all the ways. The voice acting is great, its no discredit to the talented voice actors. But they are just written so pretentiously.

I love to inclusion of new voice actors accents and leaving them as is. Gives more depth to the game.

But seriously, who approved this many poop jokes. Like jokes genuinely about poop. And Im sorry, but the main villains are just absolutely boring. Like social media influence villains? Really? We couldn't have a unique villain like Colonel Z, Jack or the destroyer? We get two social media twins? I think its taking real life a little too on the nose.

All of what made B2, B1 and PS great comedy and story wise is ripped out. Leaving a very fun combat experience, but utterly boring story. And also, the shop menu is total garbage. Bring back the B2 variant.

TLDR: Game is okay, but barely making a positive review.

♥♥♥♥♥♥ story, Poorly written Characters.

Great gunplay and gameplay, great voice acting.\t\t\t\t\t
