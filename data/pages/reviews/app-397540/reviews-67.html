Borderlands 3
is a looter shooter that delivers a fun, fast-paced, and addictive game play experience. It features a huge variety of weapons, enemies, and planets to explore, as well as a charming and humorous story that doesn’t take itself too seriously. The game also offers a great co-op mode that allows up to four players to team up and blast their way through the campaign.

The game’s main strength
is its gun play, which is refined and satisfying. The weapons are diverse and creative, each with their own unique attributes and effects. You can find guns that shoot rockets, lasers, grenades, saw blades, and even other guns. The enemies are also varied and challenging, ranging from bandits and beasts to robots and aliens. The combat is dynamic and chaotic, requiring you to use cover, movement, and skills to survive.

The game’s main weakness
is its concept of "humour", which is often crude, immature, and outdated. The game tries too hard to be edgy and funny, but ends up being annoying and repetitive. The jokes are mostly based on poop, sex, violence, and pop culture references, and they quickly lose their charm. The characters are also shallow and forgettable, especially the main villains, who are a pair of annoying streamers. The story is not very engaging or original, and it just feels like an excuse to move from one planet to another.

Overall
Borderlands 3 is a game that excels in its game play, but fails in its writing. It's a game that is best enjoyed with friends, who can share the loot and the killing. It's a game that is worth playing for its action, but not for its story. It's a game that is fun, but not funny. This game is stupid mindless fun, which i very much enjoyed.\t\t\t\t\t

The storyline itself = Doesn't captivate as much as it should. However, played through to the end because shooting NPCs until they explode is so satisfying.

What I don't like a lot is = The game forcefully injecting WOKE elements excessively is annoying. It's full of women and gay characters. Some guys might not like it. Can't dev create some good male characters?

In summary, if you ignore the mentioned downsides, buy it on sale and give it a try.
