This slight improvement is not worth the massive hit in quality that the main story got. Slow, unnatural and unengaging characters and dialogue. Absolutely predictable story beats. Even old favourites like the BL1 gang and tina's inclusion doesnt make them interesting. As all their character quirks have been toned down and reduced to being a standard quest giver.

Sidequests quality and quantity have also gone down. BL sidequests have always been one dimensionals with occasional interesting story. But most of the sidequest premise in BL3 tends to be around a meme that was dated even during the game's launch.

Progression and pacing in the main campaign have also been weirdly balanced. After completing the main story with a moderate amount of sidequesting. I am almost at lvl 50 with all enemies being trivial. Legendary weapon drops being incredibly accessible during the playthrough also seems to break difficulty somewhat.

Not recommended, as even a game to boot up and absentmindedly sidequesting it does not offer as the challenge is not present. And sidequests become uninteresting even at the earlier stages.\t\t\t\t\t
