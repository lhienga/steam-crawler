\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever felt so much like a side character. Intermissions and video sequences only feature NPCs talking to each other. These are sometimes many minutes long. NPCs have super cool abilities and talk about them with each other. Some critical game moments happen exactly where the players are in that moment, but you cannot interfere. You're not even visible. You don't say anything either. You just watch the REAL main characters (Lilith, Ava, Tannis etc.) further the story while you do repetitive missions.

Apart from that, you are ridiculously powerful. At some point you can just ignore enemies and walk past them. Most miniboss fights tend to last just a few seconds. Ironically, the special abilities look nice, but do next to nothing compared to simply firing your gun. The siren's stun skill option doesn't even work on bosses.

Lastly, the importance of vaults is driven into the ground. While it's shown as reality-bending and massively important in earlier games, it's used way too much here. For example, Borderlands 1 ends in the vault as far as I remember. During Borderlands 3, however, you open four or five vaults over the course of the game, which are all filled with very mediocre loot. The outhouses literally contain better loot than the vaults.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen Borderlands 3 first came out I was deeply disappointed, it was still an OK game, maybe 6.7/10 however compared to Borderlands 2 which I would rate 9.3 or Borderlands 1 which I would rate 8.2 it was a huge let down especially after waiting so long.

Now 4 years in and with all the content included, the polish it is an amazing game, an actual sequel to Borderlands 2. While I would still say it only manages to chase the tail of it's predecessor I'd say it's right on BL2's tail. With BL2 being one of the best games ever in my opinion that is still a great achievement.

1000% recommend getting it on sale. You can usually find the base game for 5-10 dollars which still includes 100+ hours of gameplay. Alternatively buy the Super Deluxe Edition (usually around 15 dollars) and the season pass 2 which is usually also about 15 dollars on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
