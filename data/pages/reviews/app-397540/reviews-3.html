\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEngaging gameplay and interesting worlds are held back by terrible writing and oftentimes frustratingly slow level progression. It's hard to take the story seriously when every character is trying to crack another dumb joke in the middle of an already middling plot. The Calypso twins have none of the charisma that Handsome Jack had, and there's almost no reason to get attached to any of the other characters in the game either.

However, the gunfights are intense and genuinely fun, and the varied environments are a huge step up from the Pandora of previous series entries. For fans of the series this is a solid game that's worth playing (which is why I picked it up), but it doesn't really offer anything new otherwise, and new players should probably just play Borderlands 2 first.\t\t\t\t\t
