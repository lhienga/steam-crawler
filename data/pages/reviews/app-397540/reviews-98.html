\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game improves on pretty much everything apart from the story. The main bad guys to me come across as cringey instead of comedic.

It also felt a bit lazy that we are doing the evil siren main bad guy again like in the first game.

The highlight of the story for me was the rivalry between Rhys and Katagawa, i think their rivalry should of been given more appearances throughout the game.

The best thing about borderlands 3 though which is an important factor is the gameplay, the movement is so much better, the gunplay is fluid af, the other improvements and additions like vehicle customization, weapon skin unlocks and more in depth skill trees is what really makes this game worth it.

Conclusion, if you're willing to deal with some bad script writing but great gameplay, you will enjoy this game.\t\t\t\t\t
