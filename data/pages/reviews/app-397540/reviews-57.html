\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBorderlands 3. I don't know if I'm to say that I am disappointed or impressed by the game. There was many things that I liked that they added to the game, but a lot of parts of the story line was very disappointing. I mostly played this game for the story, because I loved borderlands 2. If you don't want to get spoiled, then don't read the next paragraphs that I am going to write about this game.

-First, I will talk about the positives of the game. It is really fun, game-play wise. I love that they added a fourth skill tree for each character and that you can have up to two skill trees active at a time, in exchange for your grenade slot, which I feel is a valid sacrifice. I played as Zane and went with the Blue and Green skill trees, which I found was a great combo is you used cryo-weapons. With this combo, you can always have your abilities active.
-Another plus is that they choose for this game to be across multiple planets, which I love the concept of that. It was so cool looking at the areas that we go across over the planet, unlike Borderlands 2 where the map is a flat map of the entire game. Also, it was cool working with multiple gun companies too.
-The new vehicle the added is also amazing and I love how we have a lot more customizations with the Catch-a-ride then we did in the previous game.
-Lastly, I did like that this game has a lot of playtime overall to complete the game. Completing the base game, including completing all the sides quests (except any dlcs) was almost 50 hours. That is a lot of time for the base game, and I haven't even started any of the dlc or True Vault Hunter mode yet.

-Now for the negatives. I was extremely disappointed with the story. I felt like a lot of parts with the story was not fleshed out good enough, especially the final boss fight at the end of the game. I expected just as a cool final boss fight as we had in Borderlands 2, where we fought Handsome Jack solo, then the vault keeper after. But in borderlands 3, Tyreen just combines with it, and there is no talking with her after we kill it in Borderlands 2. That was one of the good things about Borderlands 2, at the end it tries to almost make you feel bad about Handsome Jack before ending him, and it shows him in denial as we finally defeated his plan.
-Also not to mention, the ending cliffhanger was disappointing, as Lilith just left and we don't know anything about what happened. At least with the ending of Borderlands 2, we had an idea with what was going to happen with the next game, as it shows Lilith and the team getting the vault map.
-This is a small problem I had, but I didn't like how much easier they made the game. I didn't like how much more common it was to find purple and gold rarity weapons, I don't think there was ever a point where I didn't mostly have all Legendary weapons. Also, the bosses are so much easier to kill. Bosses are supposed to be hard to kill, but I easily swept all of them, including the final boss.
-Lastly, I don't understand why they decided to add certain characters in the game, especially Tina, Mordecai, and Brick. I expected them to have a lot bigger role in the game then just seeing them break out of prison and then some side quests with building Boom Town. I was very disappointed, I would rather they not even added them at all, and seems like a lot of lost potential, especially coming from Borderlands 2 as when they got introduced, they were in the game helping the vault hunter to the end.

For my ending verdict, I would say that the game is definitely worth buying and playing... only when the game is on a sale. I really like a lot of the things that they added about the game, but the story was just too disappointing to say its worth buying at full price, although the game-play is amazing. Thankfully, I was able to buy the game on sale, I think I got it on a 70-80% sale when buying it with all the dlcs, so it was very worth it for me. All in all, I'm around the middle for this game; it's definitely not as good as Borderlands 2, but it isn't a completely terrible game. I'm hoping that by the time Borderlands 4 is out, that they improve on the negatives of this game and keep all the positives.\t\t\t\t\t
