\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, what can you say?

It is the fifth game in a franchise with several practically identical titles, which have the dubious distinction of adding a boring numerical progression system to the mechanically raw and visceral activity of hitting a target with a gun.

Yes, although there is still some room for skill within this game, the final result is decided by numerical abstractions. It's a grind-based collectaton within a Call of Duty template.

Yes, they had the genius idea to pervert the purity of shooting in a shooter. Reflect on this for a moment before you ruin your life.

I think someone should be punished for this.

It's the kind of game people call a "time vampire". It enriches your life with a mass of empty experiences and easy little meaningless rushes of dopamine, whose only satisfaction is derived from watching your character getting better (Not you), as your life-time evaporates, and you don't learn anything from mastering complex mechanical systems or overcoming challenges.

It's great.

It's such a generic and canned game that you already know how to play before you even open it.

Oh yes. There are jokes too.

A moment of disturbing distrust here:

This seems to have come out of Brendan Schaub's head.

The jokes are so absurdly forced, poorly written, and miss the mark so much that I think I would like to be hit in the head with a battle axe instead of continuing to play.

Believe me, if you like this type of humor and are over 15 years old... Accept that you won't get far in life.

But its alright. That's the kind of hook we willingly put in our mouths. The acceptance of mediocrity as the norm. Let's jump into the abyss together while looking for the next weapon with a different color.

After all, it is an extremely polished and competently executed game.

The maps are now bigger, more detailed and have some sense of design beyond just being open spaces with enemies being thrown around aimlessly (Although it's still a bit like that). You have a little more closed areas.

Weapons sound a little more powerful now and have genuine variety thanks to the unique weapons you can find along the way. Feedback is divine.

The controls, as always, are excellent.

The visuals has never been so good. This is a game that will be beautiful forever thanks to cel shading.

And it's an invariably intuitive game from top to bottom. Very easy to pick up, play and enjoy.

It has a considerable amount of content too. If eating fast food every day could be considered content, I would say that Borderlands 3 is like eating the same ultra-processed burger every day for the rest of your life as if it were impossible to get diabetes.

A long time ago, this world seemed fresh with its outrageous sense humor, full of dumb caricatured personalities and weak satirical vignettes. A reskin of Call of Duty with a dash of Diablo. It had enough charm and personality to make up for the lack of substance. Today it is an existential dilemma.

I feel like chalk being rubbed against a rough surface every time i finish playing. It is wonderful. Time is meaningless.

I'm going to keep playing because the game is fun, i guess. I have hundreds of reasons not to do it, so many better and more rewarding games to waste my time playing. But I keep doing it.

It's like an unbearable urge to scratch inside my ear.

It's the best Borderlands yet, I'd say. But it's good? Yes it is.

I bought this game twice and i'm already hooked again, just like the previous times with the other games. There is no escape.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo ive played borderlands 1-3, and most of the DLCs. Looking back and comparing all these games with each other, one thing clearly stands out: Borderlands 3 EASILY has the best gameplay/gunplay of all those games.

Something i want to point out specifically is that weapons in general are way more unique and it feels way better to use them than ever before. I feel like this game has by far the best worlddesign, with some really pretty and interesting areas, combined with a really good soundtrack ESPECIALLY on eventmaps or DLC.

This game is a lot of fun to play with friends and also its a pretty big game with a lot of content that will keep you entertained for many hours. Concerning story id say the game is a mixed bag, the main story is alright but also often tends to be cringe and over the top (i mean thats pretty normal for this game and should be expected - its cringe on purpose). Aside from the main story, there are some really hilarious sidequests, and the DLC actually have some pretty dark but also funny stories.

My recommendation is, if you really like getting dozens of guns and just are here for the gunplay and shooting things, paired with some weird and funky characters, then this game is probably a good investment. if you expect an amazing and deep story, i doubt this will tickle your nipple. Its definitely more fun to play this with friends, but i think its totally fine for soloplayers too
