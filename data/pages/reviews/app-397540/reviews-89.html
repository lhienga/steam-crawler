I got it in the sale complete for £15 and it was a waste of money, its worth £5 complete.
You will just die for no reason at odd moments, one minute you're standing there and then boom, one shotted by some mysterious force, the excessive explosions take up the screen so you cant see anything, enemies just spawn behind your back, the enemies are even so small that you can hardly see them and they can just pepper you with bullets aim bot style from the other side of the map. They never miss.
You get rinsed on mayhem 11. You just die constantly. Grenades become useless.
The gun play is ok but its over hyped, some guns are just not practical to use, why bother making an impractical gun? Feels like half the guns have no real function and merely end up in the trader.
Some of the side quests are just so difficult, it will be a shooting or jumping puzzle and it will take you an hour because you just want to play the game and you're not an expert at jumping using the unreal engine which is dreadful for jumping to slightly higher, off to the side, narrow objects.
I completed it on normal and TVHM mayhem 11. mayhem 11 was just a slog, it promises better loot but its a lie. Its the same base damage guns but the enemies resist everything you throw at them, some guns you wont get until the final act and those guns are ok and you will wish you had them sooner.
Moxxis tip jar gives you a gun thats better than 90 hours of grinding, its just a pain in the backside to use. Imagine that for a moment. Its Borderlands 3 and you can buy a gun from moxxie after an hour for about $20k that is better than all the guns you will find in mayhem 11 after 90 hours.
I enjoyed having two guns, the baby maker and the laser sploder. Both completely redundant by mayhem 11.
They dropped the ball massively with this game. Having enemies who can jump, roll, dodge, teleport, dash duck and slide, all moving at the same time shooting at you while you can duck, jump or run is just silly, fighting 5 badasses all armed with some super weapon that never runs out of ammo with precision aim to take a hair off your head which does splash damage and elemental freeze, shock and corrode just drains you to zero hp in seconds while your skill cool down can be 30 seconds. Its dumb and not very well thought out.
On mayhem 11 you do no damage to the enemy, you must spec for your action skill but the cool down is too long, you can reduce it but you mainly die in the process. It becomes: take out an enemy, die, take out another, die, take out another, die and repeat this until all the enemies in an area are dead.
On mayhem 11, the enemies shields just absorbs everything you dish out, even in vehicles so when you have to do the vehicle parts of the quest you must get out of your vehicle and fight them because your vehicle dies in 3 hits and you don't want to have to run to a catch a ride again... No thought went into making this game.\t\t\t\t\t
