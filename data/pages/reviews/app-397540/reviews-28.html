\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBorderlands 3 is by far the best in the franchise in terms of gameplay and fun factor. The game isn't perfect but it certainly isn't bad either.
Story 6/10 - A few funny moments but honestly quite predictable.

Gameplay 9/10 - Being able to climb buildings and slide makes the whole pacing much better than bl2

Graphics 9/10 - Classic Borderlands art style but looks amazing when cranked up to max.

System Requirements 7/10 - The game will run well on low end systems and is fairly well optimised.

Price 7/10 (10/10 on sale) - Borderlands 3 is absolutely worth the full asking price. Having racked up over 200 hours between consoles and epic games and to this day still enjoying the game I can safely say it's worth every penny! Do beware this game goes on sale alot so try and wait to get the ultimate edition for less than the base game!

DLC 7/10 - Borderlands 3 has 4 story DLC's and all are fun, my favourite being the handsome jackpot. Each one takes around 6 hours to complete.

Overall 7.5/10 Would recommend to anyone looking for a fun fps looter shooter and definitely to fans of the borderlands series!\t\t\t\t\t
