\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted to wait until I played through BL2 again with friends this winter before making a firm choice on this. TL;DR Play BL2 instead.

The gun-play and movement feel great. I got way too much use out of the Doom tribute double barrel. That's about the most fun thing in the game..

They kill important characters with no real good reason. Most of the new ally characters are so terrible I would kill them all and switch sides if I could have. Some old characters come to help in a few missions, but I didn't even seem to notice they were there, because they were reduced to "slice of bread" charisma levels. Vaughn and Ava were both especially terrible.

Some areas of the game drag on for hours longer than they should. The new planets are gorgeous, but a mission based off saving a frozen yogurt stand for Rhys counts on me caring about him, which I didn't. A couple missions like this wouldn't be too bad.. but it seemed like the majority were problematic. I barely remember the story now that I think of it.. Deleted the majority from my brain.

The villains cannot hold a candle to Jack. I get that they are supposed to be a satire of a toxic online community (which could have worked), but they come off so cringe it ruins it. Jack was a corporate overlord. These kids are high school bullies.

BL2 isn't perfect, but it's far better than this.\t\t\t\t\t
