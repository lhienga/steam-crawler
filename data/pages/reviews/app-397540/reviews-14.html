\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game on SIX different occasions, from Playstation to PC, and every time I think it will get better, but it doesn't. But don't let that mislead you though. I actually ADORE this game outside of the story.

I consider myself a fan boy of the Borderlands series. Yeah, this game has some of the worse writing in the BL serious and the dialogue is cringe, and I often get mad at myself because I laughed at a cringe joke or diss. I mean if you came from BL2 into BL3, there's a dramatic change in tone in terms of charisma. I won't go into my quips about the story, but I honestly believe what makes this game so enjoyable are the guns and end game content.

BL2 introduced raids and "The Invincible". BL3 keeps to it but quadruples any HP bar. It's fun. Between the Arms Race and Raids, there's a lot to enjoy in terms of gun play. It's hard to change out a weapon because it doesn't scale, but it's worth to grind guns that go brrrrrr. What the game lacks in story, because I do be a fan boy, I think the game makes up for in other ways.

Definitely worth checking out ON A SALE. At reg price? Meh. I just completed my platinum journey and I have to say I did enjoy it more than BL2's platinum. It gets better after the second act. I swear. In which I mean seldomly tolerable.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t
