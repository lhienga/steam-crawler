The villains aren't terrible, but they have the unenviable task of having to follow Handsome Jack, arguably one of the best video game villains of all time. But they aren't even the main problem: the real problems are your friends. Rhys and Vaughn from TftB are shells of their former selves, Tannis is overused (to the point of being annoying), Lilith and Ava's arc together makes no sense, and Ava might be the most unlikable character in the series history.

At the same time, the DLC stories are actually really, really well written, and many of the side characters in the main story (esp. the B-team who should've been used WAY more imo) are awesome. Also, from the cut/changed content in the Director's/Designer's cut, I get the sense that the main story was changed drastically from the original, and was probably much more coherent and sensible than what we got (in particular with respect to Ava and Lilith).

Main story: 2/10
DLC and side stories: 7/10

The Gameplay
The gameplay is absolutely excellent. The gunplay feels great, and getting loot is fun and rewarding. Also, unlike OP10 endgame in BL2, it actually feels like there was some consideration to balance. The removal of slag as an element doesn't make much narrative sense, but dear god was it a great game design decision. The raid boss level enemies are super fun to fight, the takedowns (mini raids) are awesome, and the ability to skip directly to Mayhem 11 as soon as the main story ends as opposed to having to do some Digistruct Peak equiv. 11 times is great (although maybe having us do something like that once to unlock Mayhem would be nice).

The invnetory UI is really bad though. Gearbox has always been bad at this, but Jesus Christ man, it's been how many years at this point? And it's somehow WORSE than the BL2 system?

Gameplay: 9/10
Balance: 7/10 (might be a biiit too easy at the endgame
UI: My God, my god, why have you forsaken me?/10

How to Buy
DO NOT BUY ALL OF THE DLC PIECEMEAL! Paying full price for Season Pass 1 and 2 (at $50USD and $30USD resp) is a scam, and you should absolutely wait until the ultimate edition is on steep discount. If you already know that you're a big fan of BL, and will play all (or most) the games, wait for the Pandora's Box to go on a big sale and just get all of the entire series. I think that, already owning most of the BL2 DLC, I paid ~$36USD for the Pandora's Box bundle, getting me BL3, all the DLC, BL1, BL1 enhanced, all the BL1 DLC, TPS, and all the Tales games... so yeah, do that if you can.

Value at base price: 2/10
Value at sale price: 7/10
Value at price I got: 10/10
