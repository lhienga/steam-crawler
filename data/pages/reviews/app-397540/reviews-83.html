\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBorderlands 3 it's... alright. In certain parts of the game I had fun (mainly DLCs) and in others I didn't really enjoy as much.

Pros:
-Guns are fantastic in this game. They sound amazing and you can feel how powerful they are.
-The game is beautiful. They really did it with this game, especially with how most of the settings in this game is either a desert or a Cyberpunk type city from far away.
-Most of the DLCs are so much fun. Spychokrieg's DLC managed to surprise me with how much lore Gearbox had manifested onto a Borderlands 2 character, and other DLCs that can make it to the same tier list story-wise.

Cons:
-The main story of Borderlands 3 cannot come close as to what Borderlands 2 story had established. You spend a few in-game hours with them, just for them to be killed for the dumbest reason imaginable. And your character (even though they refer to you as an important protagonist in the story) has no impact on the cutcenes that so happens to be important for the plot, almost like you don't exist in that moment.
-The beginning of the game is exaggeratedly boring. I cannot explain to you how hard it is to have fun in the beginning of the game. Enormous turn off when I played on PS4, still is on PC.
-You can get epic to legendary items very early. You barely get to try what uncommon and rare items provide before they already throw you a weapon you will definitely spend more time using before it does no damage.
-This game is pretty easy. If you're planning to play Borderlands 3 on the long run, turn on Mayhem mode on the highest level. At least with Mayhem mode, the enemies, bosses, and the loot become stronger and fun to fights against or try to master.
-In Borderlands games, it felt amazing playing with other characters and try builds for them. In the moment I am writing this review, I maximized Fl4k (Beastmaster) with the best build there is and now heading for Zane (Operative). I believe that this are the only characters I will play on the long run because I feel that the other character are just not interesting. I will update on this matter if I do play another character on the long run.
-The Arms Race DLC. THE ARMS RACE DLC!!!!!!! ABSOLUTE GARBAGE, STRESSFUL ASF, NOT WORTH YOUR TIME!!!!! I ONLY DID IT FOR A TROPHY AND FOR THE PLASMA COIL, WORST MISTAKE OF MY LIFE.

Overall: 5.9/10
I'll still play either way cause somehow I enjoy it.
