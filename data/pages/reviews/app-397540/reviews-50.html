\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite what my hour count would tell you, I've got a decent amount of experience with this game, and the others in its series.
There is debate about whether this game holds up to the quality of the others in its main game series, and to be perfectly honest while the story has a bit more filler it isn't at all a bad game by any means. You can read descriptions on what the story entails generally, but if you don't mind that bit of filler you'll be fine.

The gunplay is fun, with countless (and I do mean countless) weapon varients, all randomised in a part system (being varying stocks, barrels, grips, etc.). You could spend many hours just grinding for the perfect parts or just go with what is strongest.
That leads into the fact that it, like the other mainstream Borderlands games, is a looter-shooter. If you're the type to get attached to specific weapons then you may have a tough time as the game requires you to use the strongest weapons you pick up. That said, there are places to grind for specific weapons which online guides can help you with if you're willing to do that anytime your current weapon needs an upgrade for a certain boss or just because you want to.

The bosses are varying, not too much to say here. they can be difficult in the higher difficulties but generally they aren't 'samey' but they aren't all totally unique either. There are raid bosses in the game to fight with other people, though.

Playing with friends is very worth it in this series. There's a lot of things you can make fun of and try out together in the game. It also alleviates the slight repetitiveness of enemies teleporting in out of literal thin air. Other than that I find it hard to explain why this game excels in the multiplayer experience other than it does.

The encounters for lower level enemies (not bosses) can be a little repetitive to be honest. There is the game's stronger enemies called "baddasses" and subsequent levels of strength but they do just pop out, and there are areas where enemy waves occur. This isn't so bad though, as ass-whooping is very fun anyway once you get into it.

Now I will say I probably missed parts (definitely, there's a lot to unpack in this game) but overall if you like first-person shooters and you like wacky and occasionally dark humour, then you'll have a blast with this game.

8/10 I reckon, to put it into numbers.\t\t\t\t\t
