\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Crash on launch, BROKE my PC

I was actually having fun with this game. it's a bit of a slow start, but really all of the sequels have been since the first one threw you into the thick of it as you left the first city. i think the story is better than BL2 and the villains are believably cartoonishly evil opposed to Jack's "Joker[Joaquin Phoenix] is literally me!!1!!!1!" phase.

That was, until, i tried doing the DLC. For some reason picking up one of the DLC's questline on Sanctuary III gave me a weird glitch where every single time i loaded into a new location, Amara would say the quote she used when she got the questline. Something about being a bounty hunter in a western, idk i can't remember and i can't get it from the game for reasons you shall see.

I figured, "hey maybe if i go finish the quest she'll stop being annoying every time i load into a new location?" and so i went to the planet and started actually doing the quest. This actually seemed to work as when i entered the second area on the planet, she didn't say it! I even quit the game and went to bed and reloaded the game the next day and- she still didn't say it! WAHOO!

i figure "hey while I'm here why don't I finish the quest, I'm sure it's probably not that long and everything is way under levelled for me anyway" and so i load into the third area.

This was the first canon event. the game took way longer to load this area than normal (I'm on a HDD so it's normally about 30-45s but this was over a minute) and when i had loaded in, NONE of the textures were fully loaded for several seconds and most would stay unloaded. That is because the game hard crashed my computer.

"aw geez ah crap" i think to myself because i'm no stranger to crashing games, I play Forza Horizon 5 lol. So I press and hold the boot button on my PC... and nothing happens. okay.... So I go to find the power switch on my PSU and flip that. Nothing happens. Excuse me? A game crash has cause a hardware malfunction? what on earth? I flip it back to the "on" position and instantly my case fan (just one of them!) starts spinning at maximum velocity. {Rhys will remember this.} I try flipping the power switch again and thankfully it works.

I wait a few seconds and reboot my PC, making sure to restart it normally in case anything was affected from the unexpected shutdown and go to boot up the game again. Everthing seems to go smoothly, it even loads me into the area relatively normally unfortunately with the unloaded textures but they do all load in properly this time. I run around the starting part of the map a bit looking for frame drops or anything that could have caused such an immediate heart attack on my system but find nothing so i hop on the new quest vehicle and start driving around. I am able to go an talk to a character and pick up a side quest, and then even engage in some combat before CRASH.

the exact same thing from before, only this time the power switch didn't malfunction and my Fan speed hasn't changed. Oh yeah, that's been at 100% since the first crash. It has NEVER slowed down. WOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOSSHSHSHSHSHSHSHSHSHSH is all I can hear while typing this.

Reboot computer. Restart Steam. Relaunch game. Crash. Wait, what? I can't even get into the main menu? that was weird let me try aga- CRASH. OK. This time we didn't even make it to dancing claptrap loading screen. Try agai- CRASH. Literally just a black screen for less than a second and then Steam says i'm no longer playing the game.

well. I wanted to recommend this game with some snooty remark about how it launched as Epic Exclusive and I think that's a bad thing for gamers, but I cannot in good faith recommend this product. It will no longer start and is effectively a Digital Paperweight, but worse because it BROKE MY COMPUTER. It's like a paperweight that you dropped on your foot and it broke your toe and you'll never walk quite the same again.\t\t\t\t\t
