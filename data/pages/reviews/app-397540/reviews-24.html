this game's great but it would've been better if it wasn't so slow to get through the campaign and into the actual content i'm here for
even with the level skip up to 13 immediately i have to deal with the slowest feeling campaign to date. Sure, the bosses are easy and can be dealt with just by breathing on them if you've got even slightly decent guns but that doesn't help the annoying segments, like Fort Sunshine, walking with the Jabber, returning to Lilith and Tannis in sanctuary EVERY. OTHER. MISSION.
God forbid you don't have any DLCs either and you have to do the mandatory bits of the campaign before you launch sanctuary off the planet because then it takes even LONGER. I had to mod this game just so things can progress faster by making every bit of dialogue in the main campaign completely silent, just so I can try to get to Mayhem quicker.

But there's a few positives at least. The gun-play is the most solid its ever been in a borderlands game. My personal favourite guns are the Jakobs brand ones, but all of the factions have amazing weapons such as Maliwan's dual element and unique firing patterns, with either you ending up with a stronger projectile if you charge it up or rapid-fire. The multitude of action skills available are also great, including the 4th skill tree they become amazing.
Zane for example gets the mantis cannon, which isn't too special until you get to the skill that lets you activate your kill skills by using your action skills. Meaning, you can keep your movement speed bonus that triggers upon killing enemies up almost 100% of the time if you build him right. Which is why if you have plans to get every character up to max level, you should do Zane last so you're not drained of the dopamine hit his kit gives you.

Overall 7.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
