\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dislike how people rail on this game in their reviews, yet give it a thumbs up, so I decided to write one myself.

Short Story:
Don't buy it if you loved BL2, you'll only get disappointed

Long Story:
Hoooooo Boy
First of all, I refunded my Steam version and only ever played the Free version that Epic offered and even then I felt ripped off. And even that one I didn't finish, so people might call my review BS for not "experiencing everything the game offers"

Sorry, but having to slog through hours of quests and dialogue that are at best uninteresting and at worst just painful, doesn't sound like something I wanna spend any more of my life on than I already have.
Gods above, the writers need to be taken behind the shed like Old Yeller.
Unfunny, cringey, disrespectful and just tone deaf in the majority of the game is what the story is.

If you came for the BL2 story and humor, just keep walking and do it fast. If you only want the gunplay and are willing to mute dialouge, remove subtitles and skip cutscenes, this game MIGHT be something for you, but only at a steep discount.

The environments are nice and the music is pretty good as well, that's at least something I can say, along with the playable characters being decent in their abilities/design as well.

TL;DR: "Look how they massacred my boy..."
I am saddend, offended and horrified, by how they managed to bastardize and butcher the franchise with this entry and if there will ever by a BL4, I will outright refuse to acknowledge it's existence.\t\t\t\t\t
