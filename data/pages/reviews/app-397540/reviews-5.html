Recommended: Yes
Rating: 9.0/10.0

Summary: I highly recommend this FPS game for people who enjoy replayable, collaborative multiplayer games. In fact, I believe Borderlands series is much more enjoyable in multiplayer than singleplayer. Being the 3rd in the series, this bring back memorable characters we have seen in the past who were treasure seeking vault-hunters. Playing with others enhances the immersion of the borderlands universe. The story itself seems attractive enough, especially since we have seen some of these NPCs before. I do hope the series continues on. I have not tried the mods, but there seems to be a large collection available on Steam.

Pros:
  • The graphics of borderlands 3 has a funky, unique sort of feel to it which creates attachment.
  • Multiplayer is also super easy to set up, and is usually rock stable outside of the occasional crashes.
  • There's tons of guns which can lead to a massive arsenal of unused weapons in the personal chest.
  • Fast-paced game with good scale on difficulty, and has extremely high replayability.
  • It has intriguing bosses that kids will always remember by heart.

Cons
  • Maybe some characters feels less powerful with special abilities, can use some balancing.
  • I don't think anyone will get stuck at this game, though they might get curious about the various modes available to the game such as TVHM (true vault hunter mode). In case they do, a simple google should usually bring up some hints that answers these questions. Here's something that might help Borderlands 3 wiki
  • One of my favorite character, Maya from the previous series, dies. That is disappointing
