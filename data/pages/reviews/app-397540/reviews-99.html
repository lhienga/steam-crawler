Writing is generally abysmal, DLC practices are predatory, a couple of jank glitches were never patched and it's inexcusable at the on-paper price-point (Only spend like 10$ on sale in a bundle, but it's turning into Civ6, permanently 90% off because nobody's spending what they want to charge for it)
I put in 22 hours, pretty much doing just the main story, and a few DLC missions to level when I was like 4 lvls behind, and even then it felt like too much of the game was spent meandering through a poorly written slog. I don't even mind the "Woke" stuff people like to complain about, it's the soulless delivery. It's childish, but not in an Axe-cop or Catbug's Soap Opera charming kind of way, felt more like an 80s kids show being played so straight. BL2 was WAY more cohesive, despite being less fleshed out on the gun/grenade/skill variables front.

That being said, the gunplay was fun for like 3/4s of the game\t\t\t\t\t

at the end of the day if you liked the story for BL2 and didn't care about the gameplay then don't get this
