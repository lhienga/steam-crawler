\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's pretty fun gameplay wise, but it doesn't hold up story and character wise to what Borderlands 2 established over 10 years ago. If your a die hard fan of the franchise I'd say definitely get it for the sake of finding out whats going on currently in the Borderlands overall story but I guess you can just go on Youtube for that.

The only real grips I have outside of story and characters is that the UI is kind of, for a lack of better words, "Glitchy" for M&K. I sold some gear accidentally because the UI will bring you back up to your loot list after selling something for some reason. Not a huge issue if you just pay attention, or favorite the gear you want to keep. Other then that its fine I guess.

Also, the cutscene after killing the final boss fight froze and my game became unresponsive. So all the loot the boss dropped despawned because I had to relaunch the game. That sucked because I saw a legendary drop from it but whatever.

If I had to give it a rating I'd give it a solid 7/10, and I'd definitely wait for this game to go on sale to grab it.\t\t\t\t\t
