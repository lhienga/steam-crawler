\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR More Gold from previous Borderlands Games, Less Garbage from previous Borderlands Games

Borderlands 3 takes what the previous Borderlands Games did and runs with it.
Everything you want from a good borderlands games? It's here in crazy amounts. Zany Guns, Quirky Side Characters, Hilarious Missions, and a healthy amount of Grotesque, Crude Borderlands Dialogue. In my first dozen hours of BL3 I've had a more distilled, perfected Borderlands Experience than I have in my hundreds of hours of Borderlands 2 & Borderlands: The Pre-Sequel.

Everything you hated in previous borderlands games? CONSIDER IT GONE. Movement is SO MUCH better than BL:TPS with the addition of Mantling and Sliding. Confusing puzzles? In BL3, your mission-giver will nudge you through puzzles with hints and eventually solutions. Don't throw away your Dahls and Tediores; BL3's dozens of new weapon perks and combos makes the previous worst manufacturers on par with the likes of Maliwan or Hyperion. Every negative quirk from previous games was identified, addressed, and completely removed.

If you liked Borderlands 1, 2, or TPS at ALL, this game is going to blow you away. The price point is a little high, but it's definitely worth it. Regardless, BL3 goes on sale absolutely constantly for huge discounts: there's effectively no barrier of entry to what is honestly the best looter shooter to date. Gearbox cooked the ♥♥♥♥ out of BL3; I can't tell you in a simple way. You just have to play it.\t\t\t\t\t
