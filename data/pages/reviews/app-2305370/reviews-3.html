The early hours of the game are extremely slow and repetitive, at times there's actually nothing for you to do during the day so you go back to sleep after watering your crops, chop a few trees and fish... speaking of fishing you can only fish 2-3 times before it dries up, they don't respawn for some reason. The main gameplay loop seems to revolve around you cooking with the ingredients you gather, but there is no option to cook items in batches... Why? The amount of time you spend in the cooking menu is pretty ridiculous and i don't understand the decision behind this. The 'story' didn't hook me either, it's just going through the same motions of meeting a new character and they ask you to cook a dish for them.

In summary it's a pretty bland and disappointing dlc but this was priced at like $3.5 so i feel like i shouldn't have expected so much. You're better off spending your time playing stardew or any of it's other clones\t\t\t\t\t
