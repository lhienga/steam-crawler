\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's been a while since I've seen a good FMV game on steam. Breakout 13 is a well-made Chinese full-motion video game with an exciting story. Keep in mind this is the first part of the game and you still need to play the DLC which will be out on the 5th of February to uncover the whole story.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2915538956

The story is about a young teenage boy who skipped school to play video games in an internet cafe, he was expelled from school for that reason so his mother decided to enroll him in a behavior correction institute. After leaving her son there, expecting him to learn how not to be addicted to video games and the internet, it turned out the headmaster is using an illegal method of electric shock to force the students to say what he wants to hear. It's up to the player to trust or not listen to the other students and try to escape the institute.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2915532816

The gameplay is very interesting, the conversation choices you get depend on your responses and what you discover in the school. There is a relationship meter as well as QTE (quick time events). The escape room puzzle part is very well made.

The actors did an amazing job, I have played one of the routes and enjoyed it fully, you can choose which part you want to reply and you can also skip the previously seen scenes.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2915538995

Pros:
+Interesting story. (Based on a real story)
+Choices and consequences.
+Good soundtracks.
+Good character personality development.
+Good game length.
+Multiple endings and routes.
+Steam achievements.

Cons:
- The subtitles are white, I wish there is an option to have a dark background behind it.

It is sad to know this game is based on a real story, thinking about how people went through this. I still highly recommend this game to players who enjoy FMV, investigation, and mystery stories.
