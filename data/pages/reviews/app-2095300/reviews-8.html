那么，我为什么要给差评呢
欠佳的角色好感度系统
在游戏中，如果想要达到某种结局，就要让角色的好感度符合某种条件，但是如果玩家想要尝试某种结局，他就很有可能去到非常靠前的剧情中去刷好感，这样的情况较大的影响了玩家的沉浸感和代入感，并且很多的选项与好感度的关系不大
较为单一的剧情

部分尬到扣脚的剧情
真心，部分剧情真的太尬了，男主的话有些时候太中二，特别是那个重新认识的剧情那部分，我天，编剧要是你想不出更好的方案可以把这部分删掉。还有其他很多的此类情况（在打算逃离学校之前的“道别”、结局一在推翻学校以后）真心觉得编剧是可以在这方面在改一改。\t\t\t\t\t

Yang Yong'en (the principal) is particularly well-known as an expert in internet and video game addiction, and this is precisely why Zhang Yang has been confined to this campus.

It doesn't take long to figure out that something is awry in the Gratitude International Education Center. Shortly after arriving, Zhang Yang witnesses an act of serious violence by one of the security officers. Soon thereafter, he is dragged off and confined against his will. The staff is full of corrupt brutes. Principal Yang is a somewhat unusual villain for a story from chiniese movies. He really feels more like a Western bad guy.
Overall, the game is good, it's worth giving it a try if you love interactive story type of games.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, I do not condone the Chinese "practice" of splitting FMV games into two portions and making players buy the second half as DLC. The developer explained that this is their way of coping with development delays and to have something to sell on the planned release date, and the DLC wasn't too far off, so maybe this is believable. I would still prefer to have the whole game released only when it is ready (Feb 2023), and a demo if they really want to showcase part of the game before then. I was piqued by the story premise so that was the other reason that convinced me to buy "half a game". Apparently it was based on true events and people were curious how it would be portrayed as a video game.

The overall pricing is comparable to a typical FMV from Wales Int, edging towards interactive movie with easy puzzles. Decision branches were not completely trivial, and clear notch above similar games, especially Chinese-made ones. Acting and subtitles are above average as well. There are also collectibles which help to extend gameplay value and reason to explore the different paths.

Please make more quality games like this and never try to sell "half a movie" or "half a game", have some confidence in your quality work!\t\t\t\t\t
