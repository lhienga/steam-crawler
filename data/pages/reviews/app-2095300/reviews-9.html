\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat an amazing FMV game. This game has many features which makes it stands out over other full motion video game, which includes:
- An engaging story with a clear objective.
- Interesting characters and mysteries to solve
- Huge quality of life improvements such as a skip function, fast forward option, chapter select etc
- Choices are interesting and you are given time to consider each choice; fun to play with friends to discuss each choice
- Each choice actually matters and have consequences that make sense. With a bad ending, you are able to figure out which choice matters and go back to re-do it. All without having to look at a walkthrough
- Gameplay mechanics such as relationship meter, escape room puzzles, clue searching game etc
- Overall quality ie: great soundtracks, acting, cinematography etc

Really enjoyed my playthrough, it was a fun night playing with friends. My minor gripe is that I wish it has controller support. All in all, highly recommend it for someone who loves a choose your adventure type game.
