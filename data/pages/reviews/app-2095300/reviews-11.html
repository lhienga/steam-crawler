I play pretty much all of these choose your own adventure/interactive fiction games. I've played all the ones by Wales Interactive and have enjoyed them all to various degrees with the exception of Night Shift. Id' say this is probably at the very top of the list so far.

The production is higher than any game I've seen in this genre. There are lots of actors/actresses and none of it feels cheesy. I absolutely love the fact than you can look at your character relationship tree at any time in the game and see things you have unlocked and there are ? listed that indicate there is a story branch you did not unlock (due to choices you made.) So this actually makes me want to go back and try different things.

It's hard to talk too much about it without spoiling the game, but I'll just say it's definitely the most polished game of its genre that I've played so far. Also the way the scenes transition from choice to next scene is completely seamless and I have never seen that in a game before. Very well done. If you've liked anything from Wales Interactive you'll find a lot more here to enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
