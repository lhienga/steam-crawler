Though it's not too hard to get them to be amicable or friendly to you.
I enjoyed the story, interesting premise with a bit of mystery and in my opinion well acted, so plays a lot like an interactive movie with branching paths based on certain choices. It can be a bit tedious and sometimes difficult to get every single path unlocked (most of them are easy but there were 2 or 3 that were a bit confusing).
I saw some other reviews mention needing to go back and replay sections if you don't meet certain conditions to progress or want to unlock something different. While this is true, the timeline makes it quite easy and you don't need to replay an entire chapter again if you want to change something earlier on. You can skip through sections that are unchanged or use the timeline to directly go forward again to a point where something new happens based on your change. Personally, I didn't find this to be a problem, it actually made navigating the timeline quite easy, especially with the markers on each section telling you where a relationship change can happen.
Overall was a fun experience with only a bit of frustration trying to get 100% completion.\t\t\t\t\t
