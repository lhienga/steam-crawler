Like if you fail the game tells you to go back on the timeline and change things. In Detroit become human is not like that. If a character dies, it dies for good. The consequences really matter. In this game not that much, at least the big ones. Unfortunately this is only a part of the game. The game is not fully released. They will release a dlc with the continuation of the story. But this part has two endings good and bad. Bad where you leave the facility and good where you stay and fight. The acting of the characters is bad to okayish. I think the dlc will have the same price or a bit smaller maybe with the actual game, just because is half of the story. Anyway I guess for this price the game is ok.
