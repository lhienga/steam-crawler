\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this with a family member after we previously played Unsolved Case and enjoyed it.

I think this one was a good game too, we liked most of the puzzles and I did appreciate the working together elements. It wasn't just that you had to communicate with your partner, you actually had to do the puzzles at the same time for some of them. And it was nice that for the majority of the game one person wasn't sitting idle while someone else solved the puzzles (which has been the case in other co-op escape/puzzle games).

But, it was too short. We completed it in around 3 hours. I expected it to be a little longer, another hour or two maybe. But for the price I guess it wasn't too bad. And I suppose it's meant to played in one sitting, so it probably shouldn't be too long. I just wish there was little more to it I guess.

Puzzles: Most of the puzzles were fun and there was a good mix of easy and more challenging. A couple of times though we had to use hints, and it was quite annoying that things like "you'll have to work together" were counted as hints. Because, well thats kind of obvious, yeah? And there was some repetitiveness with each chapter leading to 4 digit codes etc. I think we disliked chapter 5, as we didn't quite get the semaphore puzzle until we used all the hints. But the previous chapters were all pretty fun.

Also, just as an extra note: I bought two copies, one for myself and one for a family member so we could play together. But that meant making two seperate purchases. Would have been nice to have a bundle so I could just make one purchase and gift the second copy.\t\t\t\t\t
