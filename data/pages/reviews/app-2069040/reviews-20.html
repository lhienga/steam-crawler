\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI first played the prologue of this game called "Unsolved Case" with my friend and we really enjoyed it. So we straight away decided to buy the main game and to play it right after.

I really enjoyed the puzzles, they weren't too easy which was important to me.
On the other side most of them weren't insanely hard which was good aswell. So by thinking and exchanging more thoughts for a longer amount of time you were able to solve most puzzles without even taking a hint. Only on two puzzles we decided to ask for hints.

The hint system worked well because it doesn't give away too much from the start. Like that you can decide if you wanna ask for a further one because you already had this step or if you wanna continue to guess before asking for more information.

I really love the comic style of this game and the narration was lovely.
While I wish the length of the game would be longer (since we finished it after 3.4 hours) the price is well spent for what you get.

Thank you for this interesting game, we had a lot of fun playing it! We even had friends watching our screens on discord (laughing about us struggling to communicate what we can see on screen to eachother) and sometimes they also joined the guessing! Therefore it is definitely a game that can be played by more than two people! You could use the extra people as your hints or you could form groups. We had a blast!

Looking forward to the next game called "Parallel Lab" from you guys, which should come out some time this year!\t\t\t\t\t
