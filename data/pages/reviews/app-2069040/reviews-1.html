\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove this game. I specifically love how the clues are given. Most of these are very sound puzzle games. I've played the game as both 'Ally' and 'Old dog' so I guess that shows how much I enjoy this game. It's a good thing that this game doesn't involve finding tiny little objects like in those hidden object games.

There is one tedious puzzle that my friend and I do not particularly enjoy though. It's the N puzzle in 'YOUR'RE ON YOUR OWN'. This one is just pure trial and error. Wouldn't really qualify as a puzzle though.

Anyways, I would highly recommend this game if you love puzzle games. The game might take on average ~3-4 hours so just savor every moment.

Looking forward to the next one in the series.\t\t\t\t\t
