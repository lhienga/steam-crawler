\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI pretty much never leave reviews but me and my girlfriend who date long distance just got done playing this game and it was awesome! I wanted to counter a few reviews i saw about this game. The intro game gave us a taste and we have had this next game on my wish list. The puzzles were challenging and took teamwork with the difficulty ramping up each stage. Just wait til the end which gets intense! The comic book graphics are awesome, the story is interesting/hooking/flows well, and while a few of the puzzle are a little confusing it's not unsolvable like some reviews make it seems. We got almost 4 hours of game play out of this so I paid a dollar for each hour i played pretty sweet deal. Some said the story was too short but there is already another game in the works that we wishlisted so the story isn't over. Obviously would love to have one giant game but for a small developing team I bet its way easier to manage their mystery franchise like this and i bet it will be cheaper long run, especially with sales. The hint system is very helpful and allows you to get small nudges without giving you the solution right away. Or the hint system can help you narrow down whats gone wrong if you think you ahve solved everything. Definitely, the best co-op mystery game we have been able to play and one of the already better mystery games outside of the Sherlock Holmes franchise. Would definitely recommend and give it a go! The cryptic killer needs to be stopped!\t\t\t\t\t
