Great game for price!

For it only being $6 and it having 2-3 hours of pure, puzzling game play, I'd say this is a highly recommended game. Both parties will need to own this game but the co-op aspect works flawlessly. If you get stuck, they offer hints that range from a small shove in the right direction, to giving you the direct answer if you are really stuck.

Coming from someone who enjoys murder mysteries, escape rooms, and everything under the sun, I say this is probably one of the best co-op puzzle games Steam has to offer.

I wish the game play was a little bit longer just because I wanted to keep playing until the killer was caught. I think in the future, having the game play from start to finish to where the case is first brought up to when you catch the killer would be stellar.

There's no save option in the game, so if you leave in hopes to pick up later, good luck. You'll have to start back from the beginning which is one of the only negative things I could think of. Another thing I'm sure some people would prefer is having a type of in-game chat. You'll need a 3rd party application to communicate to your partner whether it be Discord, a phone call, anything for verbal communication. Adding high definition in-game chat through a radio would also boost this game to a close 5/5 stars.\t\t\t\t\t
