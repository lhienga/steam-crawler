\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDidn't enjoy this, especially if you've played the prologue this is massively disappointing.
In the demo the setpieces and scenery changed a few times, and interaction with the environment was kept up throughout the game.
With this, the actual game, its literally just check box contents -> find numbers -> enter numbers in keypad -> new box contents. There was a single puzzle which required you to leave the box and check your surroundings, apart from the beginning of the game. You never change location, instead stay at the same place at all times.
The funny thing is in the cover art you can see the protagonists hunched over a box. Maybe a warning that that is the entire content of the game would be nice...

I would definitely skip this.\t\t\t\t\t
