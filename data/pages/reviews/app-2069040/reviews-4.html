\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWent on a game searching spree for something to entertain me and my brother. We played Unsolved Case last night, and we enjoyed it so much I had to get us this one! My expectations for the puzzles were raised after reading the reviews, and sure enough it was challenging and incredibly engaging. I adore the graphic art style, and though the voice acting isn't necessarily the best, the atmosphere from the music to the visuals really help to solidify the ambiance. I won't lie - we definitely brute forced portions of the puzzles. I don't think this is the fault of the devs but more of a communication deficit between me and my brother as we were nearing the end. I highly recommend this game for a fun night of puzzles, and I look forward to any sequels that may be produced as well as replaying the game to get the other perspective of the puzzles. The games not over yet, detectives.\t\t\t\t\t
