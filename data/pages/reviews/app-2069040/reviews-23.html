\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing the free demo with my friend, we immediately wishlisted the full game and bought it as soon as it came out! This co-op puzzle game is a breath of fresh air between all the first-person 3D escape rooms (which are also a lot of fun but can get a bit annoying when you're more busy battling janky controls instead of solving puzzles...)
Unboxing the Cryptic Killer has a moody and distinct art style that fits the detective genre well, and its 2D format allows for a pleasant and smooth puzzle solving experience. For us, the puzzles were challenging enough without being super frustrating, which made it a lot of fun! The voice acting is also nice, I love Ally's accent hahah :)
The only downside is that it's not a very long game (it took us 3 hours to beat it), but I think the price is fair for the quality and the amount of time you get out of it, especially considering it's an indie game created by a small team. And if you're a completionist and want to get all the achievements, you can decide to do a replay with the other character.
I'm already looking forward to the sequels!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love puzzle games, I love playing them with family and friends and this game met all the expectations. Unboxing the Cryptic Killer is an escape room puzzle game where you must have someone else to play it with you, keep in mind that both of you must purchase a copy of the game and share a room ID to start playing.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2968637768

Ally and Old Dog arrived at the warehouse only to find a huge mystery waiting for them, a box with a ticking bomb where they must solve multiple puzzles as quickly as they can to deactivate the puzzles. Could the Cryptic Killer still be on the loose? The comic strip cutscenes and voice-acting are really good. I enjoyed the gameplay, although You'll only be in one location unlike the demo, but the puzzles were very challenging and fun.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2968638670

Pros:
+Interesting story.
+Fun gameplay (Require two players and two copies to purchase)
+Great voiceovers.
+Good soundtracks.
+Chellenging puzzles.
+Good hinting system.
+Steam achievements.

Cons:
-Too short.
-Incomplete story.
-The level will restart when you exit midways.

There is still another chapter of this game and the story is still ongoing, I do wish whenever someone purchases a copy of the game they'll get a gift copy to send it to a friend or someone to play with instead of paying for two in order to play it. I still recommend the game to people who enjoy challenging puzzles.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2968637996
