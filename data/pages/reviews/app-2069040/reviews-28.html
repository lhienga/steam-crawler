A fun, EXTREMELY SHORT assymetrical cooperative puzzle game:

Pros:
The art's alright, the puzzles are a good combination of ok to actually clever and make good use of the format. No issues with the internet connection at all.

Cons:
The writing and voice acting is awful (although interestingly the art is consistently much better during these mini-cutscenes). The game is about an hour and a half long on a first run-through (Which, for $4 on sale, not the worst thing). And there's a couple nitpicky puzzle design things that could be tweaked - the sound effects on the farm puzzle are waaaaaaaaay too loud, and my partner was unable to the TV image puzzle because of how fast the image flickered?

Overall, it's a fun little jaunt to play with a friend, if you're both into puzzle games- short-lived, but the puzzle design makes it a good time worth experiencing.\t\t\t\t\t
