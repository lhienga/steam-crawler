\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLike space? Like exploring every inch of the universe without restriction? If you answered yes to these questions, this is the program for you. It is a massive program. Explore every planet, asteroid, star, nebula, comet, galaxy, and more. Essentially, it's the entire universe on your desktop. With all that endless exploration, what could make it better? Modding. Lots of mods. Want to explore fictional worlds from film and game franchises? Go right ahead. There's Avatar, KSP (Kerbal Space Program), and even Star Wars. Want more for Earth? Add rings and a multitude of moons. All with the power of modding.

This is the best space sim out there. There are some flaws, but they are not serious. Not much variety for planet textures, clouds are not fluffy (Just a flat texture above the planet), there is no 3D vegetation, no 3D cities, or even night lights for planets with life. Also no real spacecraft unless you want to drive it. All sci-fi too. No real ones except for an Apollo lander.
But none of these are issues that ruin the experience. In fact, I would like to suggest that some of these are added in. Fully textured 3D cities are too much, but some spacecraft would be nice. Also please add vegetation beyond just a texture on the ground. Lastly, add volumetric clouds and city light to planets with multicellular ground life.\t\t\t\t\t
