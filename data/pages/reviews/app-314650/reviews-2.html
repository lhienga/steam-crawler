\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty fun game. The views you can get are pretty amazing and the sunsets are pretty realistic and quite fun to look at. It's fascinating how many galaxies, with star systems and individual planets there are. When you zoom into the sky at that one random galaxy, you aren't just looking at a decoration, it is an entire different world inside of the game that has its own history, system of stars and planets and its own special view of the universe.
Also, what is really fun to do is to go all the way to the edge of the universe where the entire thing cuts off and then go and land on a random planet in the galaxy and see one side of the universe be filled with light and objects and the other side be the unforgiving empty void of nothing. It really kicks in my flight or fight response in a way when you go to the very edge and just see an unexplainably large and towering wall of all of the galaxies separated by millions of light years of nothing before you enter the empty void.
One thing that does annoy me is that the camera settings specifically for how much exposure certain objects look in the night sky do not save when you close the game and then open it again. Maybe I'm missing something.\t\t\t\t\t
