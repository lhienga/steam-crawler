\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like space?
Do you like exploration without limits (or with very little limits)?
Do you want to see what is on different planets?
How about exploring full AI generated planets full of volcanoes and cool space stuff?
Do you want to be able to click on any star or planet in the distance, and be able to go to it?
You are in luck, SpaceEngine answers all of those needs! This game is awesome, i've played 2 hours (not alot) and every time I play, I still find crazy new stuff! While i did say there was no limits, there is the limit of after going too far you get to straight darkness.
Little tip, if you want planets to look way bigger, go on the planet (like fly into it) then fly out. This might just be in my head but I swear everything looks way bigger. Anyways this game is absolutely worth the price, no doubt. Even if you arent interested in space, this game still is amazing.

Get this game.\t\t\t\t\t
