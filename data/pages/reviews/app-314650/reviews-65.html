It conveys the fascination, that is our universe to the average person and really sparks curiousity for what's out there. I like that it is based on scientific findings and extrapolates on these to let you explore from the edge of the universe down to the surface of any planet, moon or asteroid, which gives a glimpse on to the vastness of the universe.
It uses real star catalogs and presents a view of our universe that is realistic enough, that I think it should be installed on every computer at every school. A marvellous planetarium implemented in software, with great VR support, which I highly recommend to everyone that has a capable machine and can get his/her hands on a VR-Headset. The best VR App out there in my opinion.

While it may not be for everyone, If you're interested in space, love visually stunning graphics and don't mind tinkering a bit with settings and menus, or even like that, this is right for you.
