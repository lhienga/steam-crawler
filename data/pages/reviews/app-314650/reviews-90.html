I had some serious problems with my Radeon card and received a refund the first time around. However, the developer has since fixed a number of AMD-specific issues and I'm glad to report that the software runs perfectly this time. Graphics and background music are fantastic. Spaceflight is a little clunky and hard to use, especially with a HOTAS. It's only in alpha stage at the moment and I hope they continue building on it and improving it. In working with their support, it is obvious that the folks who created this are very enthusiastic about it. This is great for the future of the project and I hope they continue forward with the same level of enthusiasm. Overall I highly recommend, especially if you're curious about the cosmos or have kids who want to learn about the universe.\t\t\t\t\t
