SpaceEngine

SpaceEngine is basically a 1:1 replica of the whole Universe.

It has every little galaxy, system, star, planet, moon, asteroid, and even to the point where there's tiny little particles!

The Graphics are insane, the size of the game is huge (almost infinite), and the views are superb! Although you certainly do need a good graphics card to play without 1 FPS, if you already have a good graphics card, then this game could look pretty insane. It gets to the point where it's so realistic, it's so hard to spot the difference between Real-Life photos and SpaceEngine photos.

Ever since I discovered this game, I've been so interested into space that I brought a full telescope! ( Orion SkyQuest XT8 for those who wonder.)

It's abnormally detailed for a game, and would seem like you would need a NASA Computer, but no, you could run this on your OWN Computer, as long as you have a good graphics card.

I totally recommend this to anyone who has a good PC and are interested with space, those $25 are gonna be worth it, trust me.\t\t\t\t\t
