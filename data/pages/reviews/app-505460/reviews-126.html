\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTake Planetside 2 (massive map with tons of players), give it a top/down perspective. Add a boat load of extra mechanics and set it in a world war 2-esque era and that's the game. You level up by people commending you (delivering supplies, reviving them, exceptional team work etc). There is a bit of a learning curve. But its a Planetside 2 scale map where there's always action to be found and 'games/wars' take place over multiple days or even weeks until one side captures enough objectives to win.

There are no pre-defined class systems. You spawn at a bunker/spawn point and pick the weapons that you want to use, provided someone has been delivering materials/supplies to it.
Gunplay is not a basic 'aim in direction and shoot'. There's a stability system impacted by your movement speed, posture and left/right movement as well as the gun you are using.
I'd strongly suggest playing with a mic and maybe finding a group of people to play with because solo it can be very monotonous. It takes more than one soldier to capture a strategic locale after all.\t\t\t\t\t
