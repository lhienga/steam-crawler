\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHold up there. Yes, you. I know you are reading this and wondering how in the name of our lord and savior Gabe am I not recommending a game that I have put over 1K+ hours into?

To be honest, I want to do this not as a piece bashing the game. Foxhole is an extremely niche game that is very unique in its concept that very few games have attempted and Ive enjoyed near every minute of it.

So why I am not recommending the game? Because I want to provide a general warning about this. Foxhole is not a game for the general gaming community, let me explain.

Foxhole is completely player driven. Everything that you see that isn't apart of the map itself is player made from the trucks, trains, bunkers, weapons everything and I mean everything. Something I give the developers credit for the extreme amount of tools given to the players its something that very few games can match. You really do feel like you are part of the war machine. But thats the thing, Foxhole reminds you, you are just a cog in the machine. The game minimizes the actions of the individual for the actions of the whole of the two factions (Wardens [The vaguely french looking ones/Blue guys] & the Colonials [the WW2 American GIs/Green Men]). To get somewhere in this game requires you to devote the time and effort to not only build and produce everything but to also communicate and work within a structure to really get anywhere. This is why I am not recommending the game. I love this game to bits but I can't seriously say this is a game that appeals to a wider audience.

If you want to fight on the frontlines and feel like its WW1, go for it. If you want to do some casual logi go right ahead. They are very much niches and jobs that a casual player might enjoy. But for things like building a bunker base, building a facility or even building a rail-line, these are not things that the average person can do. I highly recommend if you want to do these, find a group of players to work with, trust me when I say this is arguably the best decision you can make.

If you still feel like the game is the right one for you. Strap in its gonna be a while and let me be the first to welcome you to the front. Be patient and listen to those of higher ranks (Game has military ranks as a cosmetic next to player names [Just joining means you will have no-rank hence the name "no-ranks"])

If you don't feel like this is the game for you. I'm sorry. It is a really fun game, but its niche is too small to really capture a larger audience. Don't forget you can always try it out and refund if you don't like it.\t\t\t\t\t
