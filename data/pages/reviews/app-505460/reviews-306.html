\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the most unique games you'll probably ever play. Everything in this game is centered around the players, they are in charge of everything. All the ammo, guns, grenades, bandages, vehicles, trenches, and bases are made by players. This creates a system where players have to farm materials, refine them, craft them, and finally ship them to the frontlines for them to actually be used. You are just a cog in this great war machine, and it can feel daunting. But you're not alone, you'll have comrades with you side by side whether you're fighting off hordes of enemies or gathering materials in the backlines. The gunplay takes some getting used to since its a top town shooter but you'll grasp it fairly quickly. The community is a huge part of the game, communication is everything. You can join clans to accomplish big operations together, and despite what some negative reviews say, you won't get permabanned if you do something wrong or if a clan decides to report you (as long as you're not doing anything malicious). Although I'm not saying the community are saints, this is a video game so there will be some bums trying to ruin it for others, but in terms of toxicity, it's definitely a minority. Give it a shot, this game is honestly one of the most unique experiences you'll ever play, and while it's not for everyone, it's certainly not a bad game by a large margin.\t\t\t\t\t
