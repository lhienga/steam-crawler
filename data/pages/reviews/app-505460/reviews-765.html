\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has great features and fun game-play. If you have the desire to sit down and play the game for hours and hours you may see this game as a fun experience. However a casual experience is almost surely to leave more to be desired. Much of the features and content is behind grindwalls (hours spent) to get access to features and content that used to be much more accessible.

If you want to be a "cog in a wheel" grinding away then this is the game for you. However, if you want to have some effect on the battlefield with your skill for the short amount of time you have then I feel you will be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
