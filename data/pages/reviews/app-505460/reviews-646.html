\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere to begin with Foxhole?

In a market dominated by FPS and 3PS shooters, Foxhole sets itself apart by being the only massively multiplayer top down shooter. It's a crazy combination of RTS viewpoint and tactics and the typical "Battlefield" style of shooter.

The game offers a wide variety of playstyles and is very friendly to new players - just make sure you're willing to listen. It does have a pretty steep learning curve due to the aiming mechanics / line of sight gameplay. But once you get the basics down you'll find yourself immersing yourself in learning more.

I highly recommend this game if you want a different vibe of battle, while still sharing enough of the same basic features to seem familiar.\t\t\t\t\t
