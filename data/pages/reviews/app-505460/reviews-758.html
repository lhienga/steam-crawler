\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne game that i really Enjoy for the community, or for the fact, you can go off on some adventure. Now what do i mean by that? Well, one moment you will be in the thick of the fight fighting for your life. And the people besides you, while another you find that enemy tank unlocked and by its lone some self. And just so happen to take it back to base.

Or run logistics, and talk and greet and meet new people along the lonesome roads. (Side note. My respect goes out to anyone who runs Logi in the game, since those guys keep the war effort going) Or be a small sniper team that goes off and kills the Enemy with bloodthirsty intent. Unlike most games, where the limits are the NPCs instead.. The limits are you and the people around you.

Now i have had no bad interactions in the Game between people. And to be honest.. the community is actually not that toxic. A lot people form up squads, run logi, hunt in small groups or charge with the infantry. Or hell some times get the tank core up. Their's a lot you can do with in the game. And i recommend it to anyone looking for something new.\t\t\t\t\t
