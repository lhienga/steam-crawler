\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA brilliant game, definitely overrated.

Since I am a slow learner, I was hesitant at first because of reviews talking about how clans mass-report to get people banned. I took the chance anyways, and do not regret it. So far, I have seen two cases of clans calling to mass-report a single player, and even though it was for a good reason both times, the players were not banned and I saw them both the next day(s). FYI: There are different types of reports. For instance, build-griefing reports result in their build ability being temporarily disabled, and excessive friendly fire will eventually result in a weapons lock.

The gameplay itself is big enough to make my current 60 hours of gameplay still classify me as a noob. The last games I got so addicted to was Factorio and Sea of Thieves, and this games has parts from both, so my personal life is officially screwed at this point.

Despite being 5 years old (with a 1.0 release only this month), the game's community is growing.

There are only 3 servers for the game, regardless of the country you live in, so you play with people from all over the world. I really enjoy this aspect ever since I logged in at 5am central time and experienced a squad of Russians breaking into enemy lines and pushing their borders back. This is a game of war, and it does not stop. Even server patches are rolled out per map region.

The game is not without its issues, but none of them come close to making the game frustrating to play.\t\t\t\t\t
