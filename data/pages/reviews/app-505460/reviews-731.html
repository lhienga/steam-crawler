\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHighly recommended.

You have two factions (Wardens and Colonials) constantly at war in a fictional WW1-WW2 like setting. Wars have a beginning and end, with victory conditions over a huge single-shard map, and typically last around a month, after which the victors get a commemorative plaque for their win in their home island, listing the top contributors and the map resets.

There are constantly fights over 1 to 3 fronts in the map, and three distinct roles: soldier, builder and logistics. There's gunfights, vehicle combat and naval combat, you can be a footsoldier, a partisan, a medic etc. It is classless which means the player can do whatever they feel like or their faction needs at the moment. There are static buildings and cities, but apart from these the players can build trenches, defenses, bunker bases, FOBs, howis, vehicles, watchtowers etc and fight over them.

Very nice dev team too, watch any of their devstreams to get an idea.\t\t\t\t\t
