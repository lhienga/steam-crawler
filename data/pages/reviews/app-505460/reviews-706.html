\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a cool concept, that is poorly executed. Is it a bad game? No, I don't think so. Could it be better? Definitely. The logistics system can be frustrating at first, getting boring at around the 100 hour mark. The combat is clunky, with the recent changes making it a contest of who has more HE grenades. The vehicles can spazz out at any second, flying off into the sunset. The community is hit and miss, with the new player experience differing quite significantly, depending on the player. You could get adopted by a vet and taught the basics, or get yelled at and teamkilled for digging trenches. Even with all these negatives, I'd recommend the game. Turning the tide of battle with a heavy tank you built, armed and fueled yourself is an experience that is, in my humble opinion, worth all the grind and adversity along the way. The devs might be out of touch, the community may be toxic af, the bugs numerous, but at least for me, the experiences you will go through and friendships you'll form are well worth it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t If you just bought this game, I would suggest you get a refund. Do Not waste your money on this game. I say this out of frustration and that I hope hitting the developers in the pocketbook will get their attention.
There are several reasons why: First and most importantly, is the fact that the colonials keep using alt accounts to stack the teams and to steal supplies and vehicles. In this last war there have been hundreds of alt characters doing this. I shall give some examples, one guy named Salvcal007 was wrenching Silverhands then driving them straight over to the colonials, he also was filling shipping containers with the wardens shirts and supplies and driving them to the colonials. I had already used my reports on other characters and upon seeing him doing this I mentioned it and he started reporting me for offensive language. He was not the only guy doing this, there were several that I witnessed. I mean why should they have to salvage a salvage yard then haul it all back to the refinery then wait for it to refine and then wait for your order to complete at the factory when you can just take all the ones your opposing team has made and drive them straight to your enemy.
Next example was me going into Callahans Passage on Thanksgiving. There was a queue to get in and I was just delivering supplies to the depot in Lochan. Once I had dropped off my supplies, I drove the zone and found only 8 people including myself that were actively fighting, building etc. there were a few afk in their vehicles, but there were a bunch of alts with clan tags like 404, SOM, RAID etc and some with oriental symbols and russian text, that were sitting in our bunker bases pulling out all the explosive supplies like HE grenades, mortar shells, howie shells etc, they were also pulling out Gsupps. There were 50 people in the zone and only 8 active but 40 or so alts. So you can at best be at a 5 to 1 ratio when trying to fight them, while they take all your supplies and you have a queue to deliver any new supplies.
During this time in the zone I let my niece and nephew play for a little while, I showed them how to use a mortar and they set upon destroying some enemy defenses. My niece did well even destroying an enemy bunker base but then during my 7 yr old nephew's turn a group of colonials showed up and started killing him, that was to be expected but they language that one of the guys used was filled with cursing and personal attacks against my nephew, named Mr Squiddy or Dr. Squiddy or something like that. Trying to get this from a crying 7 yr old. They said he told them" You ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ suck , you are a worthless piece of ♥♥♥♥, how did you even get to that rank? You know we are kicking your ass, why don't you go die on the frontline or something" I guess there was more he said, all of it using foul language and had my little nephew in tears, probably partly because the other kids saw it happen and he felt very ashamed. But there is no recourse, no way for me to report him or anything and he traumatized my little nephew. I partly blame myself for letting them play,( I repeat for those who have trouble reading this part) I BLAME MYSELF TOO! ( Who am I to think that children should be allowed to play a "Game" especially on a holiday like Thanksgiving. Next they will expect to get "Toys" at Christmas.....) I know how toxic many of the players are, esp. the ones that fight on the frontline. ( I have been advised that being able to report your opponent would be something else that people could exploit. We find it unacceptable for a child to bully or personally attack another child at school but I guess it is acceptable for an adult to do it in an online game.) I found most of the logi on both sides to be very helpful and respectful.
The second reason is that the Devs are stacking the tech against the wardens, some examples: colonial motorcycle gets a machine gun, warden one has no gun, colonial transport has a machine gun, wardens one does not, Colonial jeep has a RPG which damages structures, Warden one has no gun and later a spitfire with a light machine gun which does not damage structures, then there is the Colonial Tank Destroyer, which has a 54 m range, and drives faster than any other tank, Wardens have a 30 M range on their tanks. The devs also slow the wardens tech if they are progressing too quickly, one day I am getting 2-3 % per research, the next day I am only getting 0.5% per research on the same tech just different day, they must have patched it in that way while I was offline.
I came back to playing this game right before Dead Harvest, which I had alot of fun playing the tier 2 and tier 3 zombies. The next war myself and around 50 wardens went to the colonial side to help them and I personally taught maybe a dozen new players on the colonial side how to play, spending hours of my time helping them and even placed at number 15 for my contributions. I did meet some nice players and some clans that seemed really helpful over there but there were plenty of toxic players too. For example the Blemish Bandits, my name for them, a group of a dozen young kids with squeaky voices probably early teens, that guarded the road by the Blemish town Hall with half tracks and armored cars and ordered you to stop and unlock your truck and they would take what they wanted. If you resisted or did not stop they destroyed your vehicle and despawn your things. I also had a guy from 404 satchel my harvester for no known reason unless he was jealous that I had one. But I had gone through the work of farming my rmats for it just to have him destroy it for no reason. Because of these guys I went back to the wardens the following war. For the record I never saw any warden alts at all during my time playing on the colonial side, I did see a few partisan wardens but that is normal.
I normally spend a large amount of my time helping to teach new players how to do logi and the basics of the game. But not anymore I am done with this game. The Devs punish you for helping others and for working together as a team. But they continue to reward the toxic players and the people cheating using alt accounts. You would think in a team base game they would reward people for working together as a team and not punish them and reward the cheaters. The Devs need to desperately find a way to track IP addresses or something so that people cannot use alt accounts like this. It is killing the game and ruining the gameplay for the people who want to help others. I sincerely hope the Devs take notice of this problem and do something to stop this exploiting of team limits and the use of the enemies resources to supply your troops. This problem has gotten far out of control. (Some possible solutions would to make green and blue based gear that cannot be used by the other faction, however then they will just start despawning the items, so perhaps after an item is dropped to despawn at the end of the timer it reapppears in the portbase or depot that it came out of. Thereby removing the ability to despawn enemy supplies. This would not affect Logi drivers too badly that are intercepted by partisans because they will still have lost the travel time and their vehicle to the partisans but the gear will respawn back in the place it was withdrawn from after the appropriate time limit allowing for players to recover their shipment.)
Unfortunately I cannot take it anymore and I will not be playing this game. To all of the people I have met who are helpful and respectful of other players, I will miss you all, I feel like I am losing dear friends who I have been playing with.
Respectfully yours, Grimm.
P.S. PLEASE Devs make changes (hotfix, update etc.) to solve this problem so I can give you a POSITIVE review again......still waiting.....\t\t\t\t\t
