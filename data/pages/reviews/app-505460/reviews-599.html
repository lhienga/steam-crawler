\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe devs do constant updates and seem to communicate with their community, but this game's grind and larger communities will take any fun out of this.

If you want to buy this game and play in a small group, maybe even attempt to have your little tank crew then forget about that. The grind for any war machine far out-weights the benefits of result because you will have to compete for resources with every other group or loner within your own faction:
-You need rare resources and fuel which is constantly picked up by bigger groups, which leaves you to pick scraps.
-If you somehow get any sort of tank, it can be stolen from you either by someone picking your vehicle while you are away or by a larger group from your faction that will give you the ultimatum of either giving them the tank or them going to blow it up. If you fight back, you will be mass reported by them and permanently banned and it's very unlikely you will be able to have that ban lifted.
-Everyone steals from one another if they have the option. If you leave anything of yours at hand, well somehow both factions seem to be under communism, so that's ours kommrade.

The only time you will even remotely be able to have a chance at playing with anything a bit advanced is if you join a group, but you might just end up becoming the resource dog of said group and never play, because 'The ones before you did more resource gathering than you ever will.'

This leaves us to the only remotely fun aspect of the game if you are a loner or a small group: Being infantry. It can be fun at times and you can get enough resources relatively fast for the bigger infantry weapons if you work together with a couple of yours friends. This will also allow you to be a very good defense unit if what you are being attacked by isn't a tank or some game vets. Just dig a bunch of trench lines and make a couple of foxholes and you should be able to last for a good while with the help of communication and your set up defenses.

Attacking in this game is quite stupid. All you do for the greater majority of the time is spawn, grab some , go on the front and get pushed into the carnage grinder of either side, because some mentally handicapped kid blew up the only bridge there was for that area, which leaves both factions to swim in the water and get picked off by the people on the shore.

Don't buy this game, unless you want to be a resource gathering dog for a communist regime or a boot.\t\t\t\t\t
