\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs you can clearly see I've played this game many many hours. 1.5k at the time of writing this. I would absolutely love to recommend you the game, but I cannot honestly do that.

The game is a unique creation that, for me, filled a hole left by HeroesAndGenerals. Persistent battles and RTS. It filled the whole so well i never once considered going back to HnG after discovering this. Not only is there a persistent war and an RTS element like in HnG but there's also base building, backline sabotage, naval landings, strategic ballistic missiles and many others. It is in essence an incredible creation.

However. And this is the part where i'll explain why i don't recommend the game.
The biggest problem of the game at the time of writing this is the problem of alt accounts. Basically explained, nothing stops bad actors from purchasing multiple accounts and using those accounts to join the enemy side and wreck havoc. Drive vehicles into water, take screenshots of vulnerable points, screenshots of intel, modify bunker complexes to make them worse. There's many ways to damage your team and it is done en-mass. There is no way of efficiently getting rid of these bad actors at the time of the writing of this review.

Another considerable problem is the huge cost in time of many aspects of the game. Building a decent base can take real life days, and maintaining them can take a couple of hours per day. This is not necessarily a deal-breaker however seeing how wars can last months, this causes people to burn out and leave the game. A slightly less punishing way of building and maintaining what is built would alleviate this problem.

My personal last problem with the game, thou compared to the others it is a minor thing, is that the developers of the game basically don't ever communicate with the community at large, nor do they take a stance, pro or against when it comes to contentious subjects such as hacking, using of exploits, 3rd party software use and others.

All in all this could be an incredible game, the love of my life game. But as it is now, it simply isn't. Thank you for reading\t\t\t\t\t
