\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game can be really fun. There is a somewhat steep learning curve that for your 10 - 20 hours of play time it will be difficult for you to do anything. And the tutorial though is helpful it's not very detailed, and doesn't explain a lot of things. The community can be rather toxic, but also really helpful to new players (depending if any sort of ingame drama is happening or not). So it can be really easy to get help from other players. One question is asked; "There is only X amount of players, is that enough to enjoy the game?" Yes, even when player population is as low as 200 active players, you'll still be able to get into pretty large front line combat.

The game does have a few problems. Bugs, glitches, connection/lag issues. These can be overcome and not all that game breaking. Some of the more severe problems are community related; Lack of clear understandable rules, alt account spying problems, griefing, biased/unfair game mods, and many others. Of which often the Devs will just ignore which hurts the game.

Other then that it's still worth a play.\t\t\t\t\t
