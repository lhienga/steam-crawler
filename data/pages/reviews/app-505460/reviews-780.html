\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is currently my favourite on Steam, and I can't recommend it enough. I purchased this game on the 23rd October this year and I've already sunken over 500 hours into it.

It's the only game which offers logistics on a huge scale and strictly multiplayer, where every bullet, weapon and vehicle is made the players, there really is no other game like it.

The game could probably use more co-ordination between players from what I have experienced playing Colonial only, there are clans though that you could join which would offer me more co-ordination.

Stealing can be a problem, you must keep your eye on your vehicle at all times. I left my truck down the road while I carried out building work with basic materials that I had spent a considerable time producing, only to have someone steal my truck containing all my stuff!

I like to have a go at all aspects of the game. Gathering resources, refining resources, making weapons, ammo and vehicles. Delivering good with a flatbed truck, using cranes and shipping containers. Captaining a freighter through the various waterways, building up trenches and other defences and fighting too. This game has it all!

10/10.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my updated review after the Arms Race update and the ALT account fix.

I welcome most of the changes in the Arms Race update. The new way resources work is awesome and is a great fix to resource scarcity and the fuel changes promote more armoured combat compared to the old mostly infantry combat. The new vehicles are great and some may need some balancing but as much as i've seen they are pretty balanced.

The one problem I have with the new update is the tech system. It really promotes rushing the big boi tanks and not getting the other tech like armoured cars or tankettes. I suggest that we revert it back to the old system but with a catch, you can use the new tech stuff that was added to boost your research time, that would be awesome but we are stuck with this new system that can be abused, i don't think they learned from the relic update a few months ago.

Now the combat system, where do I start. The combat in this game is pretty weird if i say so myself. Bullets fly over peoples heads while aiming at them, always have to turn the camera and did i mention your at a disadvantage if you use smaller resolution/low graphics? Fix these problems and maybe ill consider going to the frontline more than once a war. Armoured warfare is pretty fun tho.

I have played both factions now. I can compile that the Colonials are much more toxic than the Wardens. The Colonials are full of Clans that abuse the report system and harass other players for their benifit. I suggest playing the Wardens for the first time since the community on their side is much more laid back and chill, with alot of friendly players except the WN clan (we wont get into that).

In the end I wouldn't recommend this game to new players. The combat system is a mess, and the tech system is still wonky, but give it a shot if you want.\t\t\t\t\t
