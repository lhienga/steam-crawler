\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game mechanics are wonderful, particularly because they allow the construction of trenches and bunkers. As you might imagine, trenches are very effective at protecting soldiers, as well as pushing a front. You can advance by digging them in a zig-zag formation towards the enemy, allowing you to move in cover.
I could talk for hours about why trenches are an excellent choice on the battlefield, such is their utility.

This miraculous ability is stifled by one factor, however. Not the enemy, no, they are courteous and honorable. In fact I have had excellent experiences, and once even got a ride in an enemy apc. No, the threat against my beloved trenches is my own ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ team.

♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ who have high ranks (read: try-hards) constantly destroy my trenches because they are "NoT EfFeCtIvE!111!!!". They are married to their idiotic dogma of charging forward without cover and dying, so much so that they will literally expend friendly resources destroying trenches during a pitched battle. I have had my trenches bombed, smashed, and burned by the entrenched (but not really) elite. For reference, these people are the kind of people who will shoot you for not calling them sir when speaking to them, and they are everywhere.

To sum it up, the mechanics of this game make it very promising, but the so-called old guard who thinks that they know best make this game unbearable to play. To put my feelings mildly, I find the player base to be vaguely unpleasant.\t\t\t\t\t
