\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe idea for this game is awesome and the mechanics actually work well, if not perfect in terms of aiming up or down. It really doesn't take a lot of time to pick up each part though it can take longer to really master any of it. The community is a mixed bag between the usual online personalities so you have to be ready for that. But I would still recommend, either with friends or own personal group, or alone. No mater what way you play, you usually end up with stories to remember later.

Update:
So after playing the game more I would still recommend it. But warning going in, the game is not balanced.

Wardens hit hard, but all their stuff is heavier. They also tend to have lower rate of fire, more recoil, and shorter range. Warden vics have more armor and health, but are slow. While infantry have to pick between anti-infantry or anti-vic weaponry when grabbing gear because of weight.

Colonials on the other hand don't hit as hard, but their stuff is lighter. They also tend to have higher rates of fire, less recoil, and longer range. So while Colonial vics might have lower health and armor, and don't punch as hard as Warden, they move quicker. But the big thing is the infantry can carry both anti-infantry weapons, such as the top tier in the game ar the dusk (which has a 40 drum mag with highest rate of fire, longest range, and lowest recoil of any ar in the game) as well as their stick grenades (which have the longest throw range of any grenade in the game), but can also carry anti-vic weapons such as their rpg (basically the bazooka which has the longest range of any rpg in the game).

This amounts to the Colonials being a well rounded flexible force with their infantry able to do more than what Warden infantry can. They can start an assault with a grenade bombardment on enemy trench lines before rushing them with their high rate of fire, accurate, long range dusks and quickly clear entire front lines. Combined with being able to carry an rpg at the same time giving them the ability to rocket spam any Warden vics or even defensive buildings. And that is on their own. Combine such flexible infantry with their equally flexible vics, they might not be able to take on a Warden assault tank head to head, but they can clear any additional units from the area around the Warden tank before hitting it from every side.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a top-down MMO shooter set in a persistent world war 1-2 era fictional universe. The Wardens (blue men) fight the Colonials (green men) over a map spanning the country of Caoiva (the Warden homeland). The game has a lot to offer, player logistics, frontline death, artillery, tank gameplay, trains. There's a lot of fun to be had and overall the game's improving slowly. There are still issues like the general community paranoia over alting and other foul play, the devs are slow to address issues and thanks to the length of some wars (longest war so far was 42 days) along with the dev's quarterly update cycle means you can be forced to deal with one state of the game for a while. The tutorial is bad, ask in world chat for help if you need it, if you want to do logi just find a logi buddy. Facility gameplay will take all of your free time, don't do it alone. I love this game and the devs do sometimes listen but they still have a long way to go, also if they stopped adding tank variants every single update they might actually have time to work on the important things.\t\t\t\t\t
