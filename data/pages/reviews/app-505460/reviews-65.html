\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first glance the game seems like it should be a ton of fun... but it's just kind of boring?

None of the fun strategies to go around the choke points, flank your enemies or ambush their support lines work.

The trench warfare is just absolutely boring. Why is there not some peek mechanic, where if you aim and shoot out of a trench, you also get vulnerable to being shot from another trench?

Logistics is sadly a one man job. There is just absolutely nothing to do for a passenger. No loading the crates onto the truck, no shooting roadside bandits out of the window. Nothing.

The game is very fascinating in the way that it simulates an actual war as it would be in real life.

But as a game... it needs a lot more work and balancing.\t\t\t\t\t
