\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have always dreamed of a game like this. On paper, it contains everything I have always wanted in a multiplayer war game. Not just the shooting and killing part, but also the resources, the logistics, etc.

Unfortunately, the game is extremely tough to get into. Not only do the tutorials offer a poor explanation of the game's mechanics, the community is also not very newcomer-friendly. Most things are communicated in extremely specific abbreviations, which you first have to look up in a guide. Menus and inventories have mechanics which are poorly explained and it is hard to keep track of all the different things.

I'm sure the game is amazing once you have a feel for all the different mechanics, but if you want to head into a game without reading guides and getting headaches, then Foxhole basically only offers very simple tasks: gathering raw materials, or picking up a gun and running to the front line to kill and be killed. Both is more fun in other games.

So, unless you are dedicated to really learning the game's mechanics and spending the required time on it, this is not a game I can recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
