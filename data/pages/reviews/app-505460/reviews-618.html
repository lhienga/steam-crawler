\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want you too imagine a Thanksgiving dinner, a grand table filled with food. A bit of it is DAMN goood, Most of it is good and the last bit is bleh/meh not bad but kinda close, that's Foxhole.

You can do some of everything. Fighting on the front line in that range from hellish trench/ bunker warfare with some Stalingrad on the side. to light forest skirmishing or open plains filled with armored vehicles, If you're a real bastard or sneaky you can get behind enemy lines and wreak havoc on supply lines. After all is simpler to win a war when the other side runs out of respawns & or gun/ammo

Now that's just fighting, Are you one of those people who like farming games? Play logistics, instead of plants you make supplies and gun and everyone loves you for it. You get to see the fruits of your labor being put too use (then again sometimes are dumb asses and use the tools you gave them incorrectly.) Let's say you like building? Great make ALL THE FORTIFICATION and then watch as your side uses them or the other dies of days and sometimes WEEKS to brake them (A solid defensive line is amazing too have and hell to fight)

Lets get too the down sides of the game. ill go over this in bullet points with some context if needed.
*obscure mechanics- when start there are a lot of things to know and the game does piss all to tell you them. Watch Youtube (channels you can learn from I Saw A Bear [] or Drew Erny []
*Trench warfare is not what you think-Trench warfare can be great however. because of the current meta is a pain most of the time to understand read this .https://www.reddit.com/r/foxholegame/comments/na39ao/improving_the_bunker_experience_for_all_infantry/
*Late game balance is kinda ♥♥♥♥♥♥- Now i may be biased here since im A Warden. but to summarise both sides are balanced evenly in the early war. both have their strengths and weaknesses in the mid stages of the war. HOWEVER in the later phases of the war the Colonials have a clear edge.
*Building tools while nice lack some common features, like a undo button E.S.T.
*Because of how Tech works you will rarely get to use some fun tools- Long version short there is a tech tree that gives factions their tools & weapons because of it you will rarely get to use a shotgun or a sniper. Insane I know but true.
*Slow updates- the team is small so the updates are slow sadly
Bullet points of strengths
* surprisingly no bugs like what so ever.
* Bloody fantastic community all around - Each faction has a large discord with many sub communities wanting to find a group that does behind enemy line operations? Look for partisans. Want to join organized fronts? there are a lot, Green guys got the Arm-co and the 27th just too name 2 while the blues got the
Warden Unity Hub on discord. its our area to meet up.
* Big battle feels- This game really sells the hole this is a massive war feels and does a good job.
* A play style for damn near everyone.
* Simple to learn hard to master kind of game
* In-game party tools and solid. As well as proximity chat
