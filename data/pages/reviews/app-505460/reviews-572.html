\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game intermittently over the last few years since it just came out and it is by far one of my favourite games. It's basically a military roleplaying game set in the interwar period between WW1 and WW2. You can essentially choose whatever role you want. Want to be a combat medic? Grab a trauma kit and first aid kit and start dragging the wounded to safety. Wanna be a tank gunner? Get a crew together, build your tank and blitz that trench line. Artillery? Get your spotter and gun in place and start raining those shells on the enemy. Feel like just driving? Get yourself a transport and make a convoy to deliver supplies to the front or materials to and from factories. Theres lots of experienced players always looking for someone to give them a hand with something exciting so if you don't know what to do just ask someone. The developers are always adding things as well. The game has been constantly getting better and more detailed every time its updated so it's only improving as time goes on.

Cons: Some toxic players, team killing happens and usually it's accidental but other times it's by trolls. Depending on what front you're at it may be boring at times but I promise it makes up for when things get heated. You will die, alot. Much like a real war, there are also stalemates and meat grinders where it may feel like you're just throwing bodies at an enemy bunker or bridge just for them to be immediately cut down but there's nothing like taking that hard fought fortification from the enemy.

I cant recommend it enough but if youve only played FPS the top down approach takes some getting used to.\t\t\t\t\t

Now to the good points of this game and there are many. It has a steep learning curve, but i like those games because they make you more and more to learn all the things. The fights can give you a really immersive feeling, very atmospheric, You also have really funny moments, not least of the community. When there are no funny moments, there are heavier warfight moments where everbody is supportive and trying there best.

The game is developing more and more and i think it will become better and better over the next year. Expect fighting there are a lot of other things you can do for example building a base, trenches, tower defense or just producing ressources which have to be transported to the front.

I´m not really good in english but i really enjoy this game and try the convince all the gamers wo love war simulations, MMO, teamplays and base building.
