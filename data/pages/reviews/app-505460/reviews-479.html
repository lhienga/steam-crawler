I would even more aggressively not recommend this game. The developers are grossly incompetent and outright unqualified at their jobs wholesale. The Inferno update is not full launch ready. Fundamental mechanics, like the fire that the entire update was named after, were grossly unbalanced and incomprehensibly out of form. The basic loop of tech-ing was broken for one faction. I will give them the credit due, they did fix the tech issue within an hour of us the players posting to Reddit and alerting them through aggressive pinging. The new super tanks are woefully unbalanced against each other.
