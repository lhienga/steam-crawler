\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally enjoy the game. Persistent, always revolving world where everything is player made is pretty neat. It has it all (mostly), whether you want to build, run supplies, run and gun, gunboat, or tank...it has it! However, running a tank or gunboat can be difficult for solo players. The on-foot combat definitely takes a moment to get use to.

Some people would like to see airplanes, but I don't see that being realistic in how the top-down works. Full release brings trains, player made automated factories (Factorio, but nowhere near in-depth), and trains!


Cons for me are the travel to "regions" in the massive world (you can choose the area/battlefield you want) when you first start up multiplayer), you have to get to the border, wait a few seconds (if the region is full you just sit and wait), then spawn in on the other side to continue to where you're going. I would prefer a fluid open world, but I know it's for performance since there are only 2 servers (everything is on one server, there are no "EU/NA/RU").\t\t\t\t\t
