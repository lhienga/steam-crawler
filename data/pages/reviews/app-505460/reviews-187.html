\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is an amazing product and with the number of hours I have put into the game and the hours I have enjoyed I should really be putting a positive review. I have almost 8k hours in the game even though the majority of that is idle time and I will admit I have definitely got my moneys worth out of this game.

However, over the past two years the developers have shifted in a direction that doesn't respect the players time.

And the best way for me to demonstrate this is to walk through a very common process that is required which is refueling salvage mines.


Before the release of Foxhole 1.0 this is generally what the process was like:

  • Go to a garage. Build a fuel truck using basic materials.
  • Drive over to a refinery, access the refinery from the front and pull diesel.
  • Drive over to a salvage mine, lock the truck, Hit the refuel button on the salvage mine.
  • If an empty fuel truck was already sitting at the salvage mine when you arrived. Drive it back. If not, you can go back to home region and spawn somewhere else.

After the release of Foxhole 1.0 this is generally what the process is like:

  • Go to garage. Build a flatbed using components. Unlike basic materials. Components are harder to acquire.
  • Drive over to the construction yard and build a fuel container with basic materials.
  • Package the fuel container, get into the crane and pick up and place the container onto your flatbed.
  • You now can drive to two different places for fuel:
    \t
  • A. Drive over to the refinery. Get into the crane and pick up and place the fuel container onto the loading dock. Pull diesel.
    \t
  • B. Drive over to an oil facility. Find the mobile crane or structure crane. (if the facility doesn't have either of these you need to build one at a nearby town hall or relic). If they have a crane pick up and place your fuel container next to the small train rail. Use the small train car to pull petrol into the liquid transfer rail car and then transfer that fuel from the liquid transfer rail car to the fuel container. If they don't have this setup, place the fuel container onto the liquid transfer station (if they have one) and pull fuel (which takes about 10x longer then the first process I explained).
  • Alright, were almost there, just hang in there. Now get back into the crane, repackage the fuel container and put it back on your flatbed.
  • Drive over to a salvage mine.
  • See if the salvage mine has a nearby mobile crane or structure crane. (if the salvage mine doesn't have either of these you need to build one at a nearby town hall or relic).
  • Get into the crane. Pick up your fuel container. Place it next to the salvage mine. Get out of the crane. Unpackage the fuel container. Hit the refuel button on the salvage mine.
  • Drive your flatbed back to nearby storage/seaport and put it into your reserve. That thing cost components and you will want to save it.

Notice how the process went from 4 steps to 10 steps and if you need to build cranes you might as well take those 10 steps and make it 12-14 steps.


This is the problem with Foxhole. You can take what I described above and apply it to other areas of the game where the developers took something that was simple and straightforward and added layers of complexity and time consuming steps.\t\t\t\t\t
