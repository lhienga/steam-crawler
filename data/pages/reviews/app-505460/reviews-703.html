\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are debating buying this - Just buy it. I sat on the fence about this game for years, as the promo pictures and videos - show a weird game, that looks more like an arcade spam game (aka Foxhole: World of infantry), then something worth playing. I rarely play a game for more then 40h, as most games lack depth or distances - and I have sunk over 975 hours into this game, and if you look at other reviewers - huge numbers is not uncommon.

This game is a mix of FPS and MMO. Reminds me a lot of WW2OL with a splash of COH and Iron Harvest aesthetics. You play as either Warden Baby eating scum (Blue/Blueberry's), or Glorious PC master race Coli Army (Green/Green Goblins). The combat/aiming is a bit wonky but it all works out in the wash. The main drive of the game is logistics, personal combat skill, base design and group skill and morale.

There is really something for everyone in this game, from non combat roles, to full medical, to camping rifle sniper, base builder, driver, tanker, farmer, hoarder, clan leaders and even just pick up and play - and die 30 seconds later, kind of people.

What this game doesn't show is the people. This game is very social and win and loss really boils down to how good your side can work together and get 100-1000 people in-game to play together and work together. IF you are that kind of person, this game needs you and will reward you. This game is the ultimate for persistent world team play - so much so, even after grinding battle after grinding battle, you'll still come back to WW1 like conditions - even well after this game gives you basically combat fatigue. It is a real this - this game oozes attrition warfare. It takes effort to make breakouts, maintain logisical supply and pressure to overwhelm or cut resupply of your penitential opponent.

Look past the silly reviews and the odd buthurt people who get upset because they can't be a troll or flamer in game, and understand this game has good depth (even if the devs might have no community manager or vision on gameplay, they also cater to whomever whines the loudest on reddit (see: wardens)). For the price, this is one of the best purchases for longevity have made outside of Witcher 3, BF1942/BF2, Rome Total War and Rainbow Six 3: Raven Shield.

I look forward to seeing you on the field. You'll see me in the chat box for sure if I am online :)

action (3 hours of scrap farming) -> action (delivering supplies to frontline, 3 pity commends) -> action (3 hours of scrap farming)
