\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWelp, played an additional 3285 hours since my last review xD

Foxhole is a one of a kind MMO, or even a dream MMO for some (like me). Many features of Foxhole simply can't be found in any other games such as the persistent warfare, player driven logistics and free building. It has immense potential, yet falls so short of its expectations.

The team behind Foxhole is an indie team, and no one expects an AAA quality game from them with so little budget. However, Siege Camp (the developers behind Foxhole) simply do not seem to care about player feedback.

Faction imbalances, griefers/alts (ruining your hours of work), server issues and core gameplay bugs continue to exist. Increasingly tedious and time-consuming gameplay loops continue to be added (such as Facilities) that make you spend more time maintaining your virtual base than your day job.

The century war (100th war) only served to exemplify these issues. Fresh accounts with **20 ban records** continue to be free to go around ruining people's gameplay. Server lag/crashes are frequent and the last dev message was 3 weeks ago on something unrelated to Foxhole.

Foxhole has been a blast and I write this review with great pain as I really hope the issues are one day fixed. However, I am unable to recommend this game as of now
