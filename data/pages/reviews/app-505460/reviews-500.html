\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn the words of Shrek... The game is like an onion. It has layers.

There's a lot of something for everyone here, and all can be useful in the great war machine!
I've had fun building up the supply chain and running logistics to the front.
I've had fun fighting as an infantry on the front lines.
I've had fun sneaking behind enemy lines to raid their trucks and resources.
I've had fun constructing defenses and repairing vehicles to hold the line.
I've had fun manning artillery with various crews; bombarding our enemies from afar.

I'm 37 hours into this game and there is still more for me to do and learn, which is both exciting and a little bit of where I think new players run into a problem...
Which brings me to my biggest concern with Foxhole: the almost complete lack of info that is provided.
When you first spawn in you start on a tutorial island that helps you learn the most absolute basics of the game, but it really just guides you on how to do addition while the veterans are off doing calculus.
There is serious depth to the game, and a lot of it you won't figure out unless you check the wiki regularly as you play or have a mentor who is very experienced teaching you as you go. So fair warning if you ever want to be great at the game, there is a lot of little things to learn.

With that said, I really enjoy this game. Clearly, as I am giving it a positive review.
The game heavily depends on teamwork for success, and to my surprise, the people playing often deliver.
Sure, you'll see a tank finally arrive on the front to save the day... just to drive straight into a minefield and explode because the crew was so excited to be there they didn't look where they were going.
But there are plenty of times where everyone makes mistakes, and for the most part the community is great. A lot of random people I have played with are helpful, friendly, and willing to cooperate to make sure that the war is a success and that everyone can have some fun.
Even if there are occasional a**holes. Such is life.

There is far more to this game than I can talk about here, so check it out. Foxhole is great.\t\t\t\t\t
