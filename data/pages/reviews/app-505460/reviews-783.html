Nothing matches the pure brutality of Foxhole.

Watching someone being incinerated in front of you, watching a tank's ammunition cook off, charging toward an enemy position with gas grenade in hand, suppressing pillboxes, all of these experiences are some you'll never forget. If that isn't your thing, try the back line. Ferrying goods to and fro, running risky supply missions, and assisting a greater cause, these are memories you won't forget either. Foxhole is well balanced, a masterclass in video game economies and most importantly, feels real. The scale of the war is unfathomable. Even as you sleep, individual players are coming together for a common goal; and it's not just a few of them. There are thousands of players active at any time. Foxhole is easily one of the most immersive games I've had the chance to play. I highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
