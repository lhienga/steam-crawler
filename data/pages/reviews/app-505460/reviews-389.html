\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole has something for everyone.

Small scale stealthy infantry combat, sabotage, espionage, high explosives demolition, large scale and chaotic battles, base-building that directly impacts the flow of the game, logistics, tech trees, and everything in between.

Players can guide their own experience by finding regiments that aim to serve specific purposes, or go it alone and tag-along with squads of their own to assault the front. The community, in my experience, is pleasant, helpful, and courteous to new players. Rude players and toxic team-mates are often shouted down by other players who want to preserve the heavy context of team-work and prevent inter-teammate conflict. This allows for new players to easily approach a ranked veteran for guidance and they will often feel welcomed to join in new experiences when some vehicle needs a gunner or has a left-over seat for manning a machine gun.

Content is completely player driven other than the physical layout of the map at the beginning of the war. Developers reset the map each time a team conquers the majority of the map's primary objectives. Each small part, done by each individual, adds up a massive team effort to move resources and manpower across a expansive and dynamic map hosting hundreds of players in each of its divided regions. The whole thing is a loud, chunky, black-smoke-billowing machine that grinds men and materials into war machines.

Seriously, this game is amazing. If you are a fan of Big Team Battle type games, like SQUAD, Post Scriptum, and Hell Let Loose, then this game will scratch your itch for large-scale cooperation. The top-down camera and aiming system is a bit frustrating at first, but with some practice quickly becomes a fine-tuned mechanic used to offer certain weapons particular benefits and adds additional benefit to having a dedicated team-leader to peer forward and provide intelligence for their squad.

There is too much to compact into a single review, so I will end it simply: This game is great. If you want to experience absolutely massive cooperation, and an opportunity to serve your team in your own way, through many different avenues that offer some sort of value to the war effort, you will find a place in-game and enjoy each minute of gameplay.

Absolutely epic game.\t\t\t\t\t
