\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't buy this game. TL;DR is it's not fun if you want to do anything other than run around shooting people, but if nobody wants to do anything else you don't have anything to shoot with.

I've had it for less than ten days now. 88 hours in ten days. Why don't I like it when I have that much playtime? Because of the grinding. I'll go over it piece by piece.
Logistics. Everything you use in this game (except the free pistol, two mags, and hammer you get when you spawn in) is made by players. Yes, you heard that right. Sounds interesting/fun, right? Not really. You need to grind scrap fields for materials. You need to refine those materials to make usable materials. Then you can use those to make guns, ammo, medical supplies, spawn tokens, fuel, tools, and vehicles. Each factory can only produce on order in each category (small arms, heavy weapons, equipment/tools, medical supplies, spawn/maintenance tokens) at a time, so you have to wait for everyone else's orders to finish up in a queue like you're at a fast-food restaurant. You then have to load all these supplies onto a truck and spend up to 10 IRL minutes driving these supplies to the front, where you then unload them and put them into a building that converts them into usable weapons and resources before driving back. You may or may not be shot along the way and have over half an hour of work stolen.

Tech. For some reason, the devs have made research manual. This would be fine... except it's a "pick and choose" system where you have to invest huge amounts of special resources into it and you only get one of the options based off which had more people produce prototypes for it in that tier. Once one option becomes officially available for production that war, the others are locked and everyone fights over what they want in the next tier. Those resources can only be found at salvage fields and require you to spend hours or even days working away to get enough for one prototype kit. You can't even get sledgehammers until a week into the war, if not later, which makes logistics work more painful.

Base building. Yes, there's base building in the game. No, it's not fun at all. How the devs decided they wanted base building to go is you build a central structure and support/defenses for it. Problem is, the garrisons, automated defenses meant specifically to keep the base safe when nobody's there, don't activate unless you have a small/large garrison (which works at tier 2 and 3 bases, respectively) or meet the conditions for Provisional Garrison to activate. How long does it take to get the small garrison upgrade? About 18 IRL hours if you have one person with that base set as their spawn who votes to prioritize small garrison in the slow upgrade unlock. What about Provisional? You need ten people who have set that base as their spawn. For bunkers, this is more painful: you need to set up infrastructure to allow you to unlock garrisons (AI defensive structures, not the AI activation itself), better bunkers, the ability to spawn there directly from the home region, basically anything. These only work if connected to the central base, which loses structural integrity if it gets too big, so you have to make separate bunkers connected by trenches. All this can take hours of work. Then you get a rifle garrison, which the base can automate to defend itself later. You want to build one? Well, it has to be linked to a BB that has the unlock, either as part of the bunker or through a trench. More work. You go to sleep, wake up the next day, and find out that one guy with HE grenades blew up your BB while you were asleep because Provisional Garrison AI doesn't turn on if there aren't at least 10 people who set that as their spawn and why would they if it's not on the front? Three days later, the front gets pushed back to where your base was and everyone's screaming for defenses now. By this point, you don't care anymore.

In fact, anything other than frontline fighting is like this. There's more grinding in this game than any MMO I've ever played before, but if nobody does this painful, repetitive, thankless grinding, nobody else would be able to do anything. The game literally functions by sucking the life out of people. I've inverted my sleep schedule to try to ensure what I work on doesn't fall apart, and it still does. I've spent days doing nothing but play this game to try to find some enjoyment, only to hate whatever I was doing for being a waste of time. Don't buy this game, it's nothing more than a waste of time and money.\t\t\t\t\t
