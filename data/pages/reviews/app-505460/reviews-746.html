\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen I first saw this game in my discovery tab, I had no idea what it was, but the part where it mentions a war that can go on for weeks or just days, a game that relies on each player to either produce resources needed on the front line, or be on the frontline yourself protecting towns that You, the player, can set up and defend properly.

I enjoy the idea this game presents and it does it well, the shooting mechanics in my opinion are a little weird to get used to but easy to learn none the less. Sometimes other players can be helpful, toxic, or just rude, because again this game is community driven and player reliant. I've built defenses for a total of maybe 15 or less minutes due to other players disagreeing with what I place down, and you'll find that anywhere. because everyone has different ideas of how to defend.

Overall I have played with great people and I feel more times than not you'll find several people willing to educate you based on any question you may have. Community can be a bit sour at times, but the gameplay and core fundamentals of this War game, is not like any other game I have played.\t\t\t\t\t
