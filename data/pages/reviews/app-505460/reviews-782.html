From small fireteam sabotage missions in the cover of the night to full blown combined arms platoon assaults. This game has them.

Two sides fight over control in sectors inside bigger sectors, which do have a rather quick loading screen to transition on a persistent world map.
Featuring a decay mechanic. All the encampments, weaponry and outposts degrade over time and collapse on themselves. Understandable, the map I played on was 342 in-game days old(1s realtime=min in game).
Also there's an automation mechanic where houses, foxholes, pillboxes, bunkers and turrets appear to be manned by deadly AI around garrisons and encampments, a bag of mixed feelings and suprises for everyone.\t\t\t\t\t
