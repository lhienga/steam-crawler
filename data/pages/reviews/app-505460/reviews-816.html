\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: Game is more about hoping be able to play, rather than actually having game play.

The concept is interesting enough. Reminds me a little of if Planetside and Men of War had a baby.

The issue is the mechanics of how this works seems to be really bad. You pick up your inventory... hopefully you can find a rifle around otherwise its pistol time. You run to a frontline this can take 5-10 minutes unless you are fortunate to find someone trucking. Then hoping you see the enemy and kill them before they kill you. The issue is no matter what I seem to do I never see the enemy before they kill me. I am playing pretty high resolution and pulling the edge of the screen as much as I can in the direction the shots are coming from but I never seem to see anyone. So I die. I do not mind so much the dying part I am new bound to die. But it is the lack of capability to do much about it that really sinks the enjoyability. But again if I could respawn and get straight back into the fight even after a short walk that would be nice. But no you wait 45 seconds to respawn a further amount of time to spawn at a HQ rather than in the middle of nowhere and there is a chance that if you were in the middle of a large battle the HQ you spawn at will be over run by the enemy who just spawnkill you and you have to wait to respawn all over again but in any case you can expect a long haul back to the front, there probably is a better way but I lost interest. Oh! By the way, if the long haul itself was not bad enough you lose all your stuff so wherever you spawn you have to reacquire your stuff.

I can see the game may have potential so this review might get scrapped for a positive one in the future but the mechanics are just so rubbish at the moment I cannot possibly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo foxhole? I have been playing this game since its Alpha days, where there were only a few servers and not a very big community it has now, i fell into a clan pretty much straight away, and that improved my play not only because i had others to play it with, but because the game developers listened to players desires and wants for the game.

There are problems with this game much like with every game but the main issue at hand is this game has a huge learning system..its much more in-depth from how it used to be and its great like this, but the problem is many people don't have hours upon hours of learning systems in which they may forget tomorrow...However all that being said the community of clans and just you're solo players are very nice, and will teach players who are new to the game, and i speak from experience because i led a few classes to help people learn. I also bought the game in August 2017, back then it was the basic principles such as B-mats and guns, now after 2 years its much more complicated and its nice seeing how things grow. and the game is still growing.

If you're a casual to a hardcore gamer that on weekends has like 3-7 hours free just pop on hell even having 30mins free helps the war effort,

There is no greater feeling then storming an enemy town with a couple of APC's with a motar in the back with a spotter and just watching people climb out. or doing a tank push of multiple tanks, its great thing to see, and its a great thing to be apart off.. and hey as a added bonus...you may meet you're online best friend here lol.

so if you do check it out. i highly recommend joining a clans discord, running with them.. you dont have to join the clan but merely see first hand what organisation looks like, as most of the clans work together for similar objectives to apply pressure at different spots to the enemy, weather its behind the lines or the front lines. its tactical whilst remaining fun. its competitive whilst remaining fun.. and there is so much to do to aid you're war effort.
give it a try :) its on offer most of the time!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWorth it on sale? no, worth it out of sale? no. I have a great many games but as a multiplayer or even an MMO, this is far beyond broken to waste your time with and whatever you do will usually be reset in 3 days thus there's no progression.

The game hasn't got enough balancing mechanics as yet and needs a lot more work. One person can start team killing everyone or dump resources and upset the balance of the entire map by doing it at a key point which is against the rules but the rules themselves are somewhat meaningless when there's no way to track such things unless seen or reported directly.

Major bugs with items getting stuck in terms of boats and trucks which cause you to sometimes lose hours of work with a single glitch and mods can sometimes take over an hour+ to respond if you need help with such. While the game seems somewhat good mechanic wise in terms of general gameplay, i can entirely say that the ability to just choose the winning side of the last war causes a lot of strain as there's no population numbers for sides nor balance between them with one side reporting being maxed out in slots for the region while the other never having such issue.

Many things are not transparent or exactly clear and i feel a small popup of controls when holding / using something would be best since newer players require watching a video or being told how to use things from others when there's not always people who speak the same language around to explain such.

There's too many ways to sabotage your own team members entirely and some glitches that allow you to change sides at any time have been found but as yet not patched. I do however feel that any side currently overwhelmingly winning shouldn't be able to be joined. In this case 90% of the wins have been taken by the warden side and it does feel too one sided.

If you have or buy this game, I highly suggest telling everyone to join one side until it's entirely unbalanced that they need to do something about it. Force their hand at fixing the population issues as while the front line population limits can be capped out, the logistics behind such is also getting there which means that no matter what, they may always have the upper hand on purely people doing things.\t\t\t\t\t
