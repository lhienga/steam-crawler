\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is amazing for several reasons:
1. No Individual Matches: The war is always ongoing, you can join and leave as you wish without any penalty, which is great if you want a full afternoon of Foxhole or just 15 minutes.
2. Multiple Possible Playstyles: You can go get shot in the frontline, run logistics, build bases, drive vehicles, participate in partisan ambushes to ♥♥♥♥ with the enemies' logistics, and more! There are hundreds of ways to contribute to the war effort depending on what you like the most about the game.
3. Complete Freedom: No one is your boss here. There is no "Liutenant ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥" that can get you banned for not doing as he says. You do whatever you like whenever you like as long as you're not stepping over other players doing so.
4. Crowded Chaotic Battles: There is a ton of active players which are friendly, cooperative, and communicative most of the time. Battles are an absolute blast where every inch is gained with blood and guts (both kinds of guts).
5. Items and Logistics: The combination of everyone having an inventory, dropping all items on death, and battles being pure carnage results in a very interesting gameplay of "working with what you've got", and because the game's resources run on player-driven logistics, you will not always have top-of-the-line equipment available to you. Scavenging is a common thing in this game, and it makes it very different from other shooters.
6. Construction: With bunkers, trenches, turrets, sandbags, bridges, barbed-wire, and, of course, foxholes, the battlefield is fully malleable by players, which create very dynamic environments that constantly change as new elements are built or destroyed.

This game is unique in many special ways, and there is really nothing like it. If you dig gritty authentic WW1-2 warfare you definitely should give this a try.\t\t\t\t\t
