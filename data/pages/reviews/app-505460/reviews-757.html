You get put into the fight against the enemy, but you're fresh. You've got no experience. I started with logistics. It's a great way to understand the game. Anyways, let me continue.

I was driving supplies from the North all the way to the front. Collecting resources, asking people what they needed and where, and coordinating with other Logi drivers on production, collection, and distribution. I was on a run to a frontline province, dropping off some spawn supplies and guns/ammo. I was in the midst of dropping them off when an attack came. I picked up a rifle and fought alongside the infantry for the attack as we repelled their assault. They came very close to taking that post. In the fighting, my truck got destroyed. I couldn't bring myself to walk all the way back and decided to stay with my new found friends.

Shortly after, we were rallied for an assault and made our way towards a town under the cover of friendly artillery. Wave after wave, we made the push through the gates into enemy territory. Our attack stalled at the entrance of this town.

Sat in a building with a few other guys while one guy played guitar, while both sides shelled the hell out of the city and fought within.

Pretty fun game. 10/10 would nostalgically reminisce about my old comrades again\t\t\t\t\t
