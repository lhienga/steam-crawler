\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a pretty enjoyable game for the most part, but if there are/is something(s) I would suggest to the developers, it is to continue to add more new vehicles, new weapons, structures, create a system to allow players to create/place personal way-points on the map (Client side) and a directional pointer on the user's screen/interface so that the player can visually see where to go without having to constantly open the map (Sometimes look for your current location on the map) every five seconds (For myself, bad memory and the need to check if there is any enemy activity on the map.), and maybe extend the "end-game" for those that want to utilize the technologies that get unlocked towards the end of the war.
As a side note, I've discovered a few bugs, both new and old (As well as some additional issues):
- Getting "yeeted"/launched about 20-50 meters away in less than half a second, when respawning. I believe the issue occurs when a player spawns on top of another player. Which also leads to this: I would recommend making players/player models ignore the collision physics with other players. Although having players collide with each-other adds more realism to the game, it is not needed and can cause a number of issues to deal with...
- There is a slight issue (Barely noticeable and is not in any way important) with the map. The road in the game world does not always match that of the map, but like I said above, it is not important and is barely noticeable.
- When exiting a vehicle with the driver side parked right up against something else, the player can become stuck between the vehicle and that other object. I would recommend having the player spawn next to the vehicle where there are no obstructions, thus allowing the player to not get stuck.
- When a player locks their vehicle, the vehicle locks with the ability for the owner of the vehicle to use it normally... but only for their current game session (At least, that was the issue I faced before this update...). When re-entering the game after quitting it, I attempted to re-access my vehicle (It was mine, it was the only one within the area, and I know I locked it to prevent other players from getting it) and rather than being able to access it, the game stated that the vehicle was locked, along with everything within it.
That's all I can remember for now... It's a great game, with massive potential... And if I have any questions, it would be why are there nukes/missiles, but no air vehicles? In reality, the first ballistic missile was created during World War 2, a time when there were aircraft already existent, as well as other vehicles such as submarines, larger battleships, etc.
I suppose it is probably due to either cost or designers, or both.\t\t\t\t\t
