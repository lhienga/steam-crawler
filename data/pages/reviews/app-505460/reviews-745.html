\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe American themed Greens vs the German themed Blues. This game is essentially a boots on the ground version of Eve Online.

All the weapons, ammo, vehicles and structures are all player made, the logistic mechanics are very impressive. Mining scrap to refine in to materials, taking said materials to a factory to convert into gear and then transporting the newly crafted gear to where it is needed.

The top down combat is interesting and can be approached in many ways but it is ruined by the continuous need to rotate the screen to position your target into the top left/right corner of your screen (known as corner shooting) by corner shooting you are able to see further into the distance allowing player with widescreen displays to have an advantage over players without. It's in early access so I'll edit my review later down the line.

I rate this game a Bmats request out of ten.\t\t\t\t\t
