\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was in search of a new game to play and was looking through the Steam store when i came across Foxhole. At first the top down view was a bit off-putting but the whole idea of a massive war going on where your whole team must work together to slowly advance and capture enemy territory was interesting and i ultimately decided to give it a try. And although I only have 18 hours so far put in, every one of them has been super enjoyable so far. It is a game that feels unique to any other and I can now say I think the top down view works excellently for a game like this.
Some of the minor negatives I have to say are that it can take some getting use to spinning the camera around as well as running into a constant language barrier. So far it seems that the majority of the people i run into speak and communicate in French. I unfortunately however, cannot speak nor understand french so at times I find it hard to communicate with teammates around me, whether that is looking for a medic or just organizing a plan of attack. Maybe in the future having dedicated servers for French speaking and English speaking could do wonders and I hope as the player base grows for this game it may become more of a reality.
All in all, if you are on the fence about this game, I say absolutely give it a try. The community seems very willing to help new players and I have had a blast so far and cant wait for what future updates may bring!\t\t\t\t\t
