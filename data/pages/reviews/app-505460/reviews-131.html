You can totally find an epic tank battle going on where the men in the tanks are emotionally shouting at the battlefield in full sweatlord mode given orders to the nearby infantry to charge, while simultaneously some dude in a >bicycle< tries to charge the enemy tankline while playing the soviet anthem and ringing his bike bell.
The big grindy logistical and very strategic gameplay followed by intense combat gameplay(running in between artillery bombs, sneaking into enemy terrain stealing stuff, trying to heal people while being shot from all sides, trying to put out a wildfire spreading in your base) alternated by some of the most ridiculous memes ive ever seen make the game a must play.

Starting off, you have the obvious classes like infantry, tank and artillery, partizan(sneak around kill logistics, steal tanks), (limited naval), builder and logistics and there is alot of subclasses i.e. for infantry you can do shotgun/smg/different rifles/sniper/flamer/anti tank sniper gun/suicide anti tank bomb runner/anti building(grenade/rpg)/medichealer/medicreviver/grenadelauncher/mortar etc etc
and even in the subclasses you can be creative, there is a weight system that makes you choose between carrying more ammo or running faster and you are free to multiclass perhaps you want to take a simple handgun(too keep weight low) add a medic healing kit(not the heavy revive one) and 1 anti tank grenade(because why would a tank expect a medic to suddently suicide run at them? big brain moment)

Besides the obvious classes their are hidden subclasses that are literally made up on the spot, one day ive noticed alot of tank teams are driving back to refuel and restock at a nearby base so i figured i take a small car with a few fuel and materials and drive to our tankline refuelling them by hand so they can fight longer. Which is stupid fun cause fuel is very heavy and sometimes your tankline falls back a little and you just stand there with the fuel while the enemy tanks charge in....haha dont shoot please, im just fuelmen :((
