\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty much what Planetside 2 should've been. (Of course PS2 retaining its 1st person view)
I have a surprising amount of fun with this game, whether i'm in the front lines, or running logistics, to even memeing around and trying out different tactics, I've had a absolute blast all the same.

-PROS-
*Graphics - Simple, yet effective, everything looks high quality.
*Gameplay - Gunfights feel fun, no spray and pray in this game, this game HEAVILY encourages semi-auto/burst fire when in combat. (Personally I like sniping people with the Revolver.)
*Soundtrack - Pretty much sounds exactly how I expected it to. Reminds me of War Thunder in all the good ways.
*Content - Lots of stuff to do in this game that can turn the tide of battle. Whether you and many others go into the frontlines and take that strategic area of interest, to resupplying the front with Shirts, (Or Soldier Supplies, which are used to respawn) weapons, ammo, explosives, or even arriving with a armored car or tank. People will commend you for delivering the right supplies to the right place.

-NEUTRAL-
*Animation - Infantry animations are pretty nice, and for the most part, fluid. However, I find the prone animations to be kinda wonky, trucks feel awkward to use. (No opinions on tanks/treaded vehicles has I haven't had the chance to try them out, will update, when I have proper experience.
*Bugs - No opinion, never ran into any.
*Mechanics - Not really a problem for me, but for some, but be prepared to die. ALOT. This game is kinda punishing (in a good way imo) when you die. From spawn timers, to respawning at a far away base, you have to prep yourself well.
*Friends - This is ALOT more enjoyable with friends. Being solo is fun too, but nothing beats rushing the front with bayonet charges with the boys.
*Camera - The game is birds eye view, so certain situations can be good, or downright terrible.

-CONS-
*Sounds - Honestly my only negative take is the sounds, most don't sound that great to me, and makes me feel like, for example, shooting a rifle is like shooting a pellet gun.

Overall, if you want something with a bit more depth to it compared to games such as PS2, i'd heavily recommend getting this game.
