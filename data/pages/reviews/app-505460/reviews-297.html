\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEuro Truck Simulator meets Factorio meets Train Simulator meets (insert base builder game). And that's just the logistics side.

This game has something for everyone, truly. The combat is satisfying, but does take time to get used to.

I have never played a game that captures the pure chaos of being on the front line until Foxhole. You have randos screaming at you grab grenades and kamikaze into the enemy tank. Then you realize that you used the last of your grenades. So now you're screaming at the logistics folks to restock you. Then amazingly, 10 or 20 minutes later, you get a resupply. Or you don't, because a small group of enemies snuck past your front line to ambush the supply lines. Now the logis have to switch to naval resupply. It's incredible to watch unfold.

I'm generally a solo player but the community has been quite welcoming. There's tons of activities for solo players if you don't feel like being social.

There's truly no game quite like Foxhole. Big thumbs up.\t\t\t\t\t
