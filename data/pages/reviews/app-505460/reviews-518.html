\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a game of many things, it can be relaxing, scrapping for weapons and supplies. Or it can be intense, on a bridge with people exploding around you while bullets whiz by.
To start, I'm going to list the good things about this game. It's being worked on a lot, with the devs updating it regularly. People are (generally) nice, and you can find someone to help you with anything if you look enough. The combat in this game is amazing, and I've seen my fair share of heroes turning into dead bodies, people suicide charging, desperate defenses, and huge offensives. It's a big pleasure with this new update for the camera, variety of weapons ( although you get stuck with a rifle most of the time ), and powerful vehicles that are fun to fight and to fight with.
Now, the bad. The bad mainly stems from the community, mainly alts and greedy players. Most people in this game are nice, sometimes going out of their way to help new players. However, people will occasionally get another copy of the game, and throw out supplies, weapons, and whatever else into the nearest lake. This has caused a second problem, witch hunts. Whenever someone seems to be going against the group, a person may call them an alt. This has happened to me and my friend on both sides, but is a rare to happen. Greedy players like to horde loot for themselves, draining stores, and making equipment for themselves that them or their clan never use, depriving it of other players to use it.
Overall, this game is very good. The devs just need to solve the alt issue, and ensure that people aren't hording valuable supplies.\t\t\t\t\t
