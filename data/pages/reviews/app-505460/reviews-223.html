Original Review:

I have had great moments with this game, but I can't, in good conscience, continue to play it.

The amount of player time that needs to be spent doing the busywork so that people can engage in the main attraction of this game (actually fighting) is absurd. Never before have I seen a community so comfortable with managing burnout or expending hundreds of man-hours to build, maintain and defend structures that may not even last a day. Either I am wasting my own time on this busywork, or forcing someone else to do it by my own refusal (read: desire to have fun).

I'm not saying there isn't fun to be had here. I'm saying that the fun is better had in a game that respects everyone's time.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the most rage inducing MMO I ever played. And I played War Thunder so it is very difficult to top that one. You will develop love/hate relationship and you will enjoy all different aspects that game offers...for some time. I consider it a pretty highly addictive game, and believe me, it's like a drug. And drugs are no good for you buddy. You will ask yourself why i just found this game now, where was it all along? This is the greatest MMO ever man! But... after a while you will start feeling consequences of this bug infested glitchfest of a game. Unfinished product that still HAS great potential. Unfortunately, I think it will never be something more than that. If you ask me, this is still an early access game.

And yes, everything is good and dandy until the burnout kicks in. How to avoid burnout? Impossible. Btw, this is the only game that I regularly exited with Alt+F4. No game can beat that! On top of that, balance issues, toxicity and teamkills is a regular, especially in the situations when your faction is losing a region. Then pure chaos ensues and... well, things happen.
On top of that you will die in all the ways possible you could imagine 10 times in a row in a span of 30 seconds, just to look 45 seconds of that annoying death screen after every death. On top of all of the above add the fact that most of the time you died, you died because of the bugs and poor design decisions. Some of these things are impossible to fix. I'll just mention a few things that are horrendous and will never be fixed (probly):

-queue bugs (waiting to enter another region for 10-15 mins in a truck full of bmats just to get disconnected and thrown back to the menu screen. You have to redeploy, and probably lost everything)
-queue time (because, time matters, life is too short to watch youtube adds and wait to join in game FOXIHOLE)
-respawn time (especially after a teamkill or some other death you could not control at all, you should have shorter respawn time)
-stuttering (minor nuisance but happens quite often)
-getting stuck in trenches, foxholes, trucks, bases, literally everything.
-getting stuck in jump animation after getting unstuck from trenches, foxholes, trucks, bases, literally everything. (You basically have to go back to the previous spot where you got stuck in the first place and start all over again)
-If you switch to your secondary or tertiary weapon after use of primary weapon too fast it won't load. Basically you will not equip it. (very annoying if you need to throw a grenade pretty fast or use binos after mortar fast)
-Make WIDER roads.
-Inability to check activity log while dead
-I want to be able to check map when driving without stopping. I didn't use a brake for the love of god, just wanted to check the map for one friggin second! (Especially annoying when trying Logi)
-REMOVE BARBED WIRE from game!
-friggin LAG, this thing is the worst. This is the main reason for that rage inducing part I mentioned.(And it's not on me).
There's more stuff but this is the most annoying thing.

For now this game is a NO NO. If devs fix some of these things i'll change my opinion as well.
