\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you have ever wondered what it would be like to come home from your non descript job at the office and enter into more work than you've ever dreamed of, but this time it's in service of the Colonial Republic, and you are building artillery shells and shooting blueberries, then you are in luck. Also probably fictional. But still in luck my friend!
Foxhole is what it says it is. You join up with an army of other soldiers and fight a battle in real time on a constantly changing map. What makes it different from games like planet side two and other MMOWG (Massivly multiplayer online wargames) is the little conversations, the massive tank battles, the minor skirmishes and the almost racist hatred the players from each faction have for one another. The war feels real, because the players want it to. And by golly is it fun!
Highly Reccommend !\t\t\t\t\t
