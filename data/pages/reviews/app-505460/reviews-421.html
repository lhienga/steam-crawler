\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the more unique games i have played, foxhole is a simplistic looking top down MMO shooter. Premise is simple, you fight to win the war. The execution however is some of the most unique and most realistic i have ever seen.

You are not the hero here, nobody is. You are a poor pleb who got dragged into pure chaos, you are nothing. You will have to dig trenches, get blown up by artillery literally fired from a different server, get scrap metal to make those sweet sweet rifles, drive trucks full of war material in circles while everybody is giving you confusing and contradictory information, AND YOU ARE GOING TO LOVE IT.

The point of this game is mass scale teamwork, you will have to coordinate that 80 player bayonet charge, you are going to have to organize the transport of these brand new tanks from the factory to the front, just for the boat transporting them to be blown up by guerilla commandos, and you are going to have to literally scream to your teammates to get ammo for your machine gun when you fire on waves after waves of enemy soldiers while getting pummeled by heavy artillery. ANd that is what makes this games great\t\t\t\t\t
