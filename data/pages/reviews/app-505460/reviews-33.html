\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't even know were to begin, for starters it is one of the best war games I have ever played. Most other people have probably already talked about the mechanics in the game so I'm gonna go over some other points/suggestions for new players:

1: Play on Charlie shard (shards are servers). Able shard is full of toxic tryhards and really not worth playing on (and this is coming from somebody who has roughly 50ish hours on this game)
2: Build trenches, but build them well. Research IRL trench formations and try to copy that, alot of IRL tactics/defenses actually translate well over to Foxhole
3: When fighting tanks, aim for fuel tanks, turret and tracks. Taking out the tracks makes them nearly immobile, damaging fuel tanks means that the tank looses alot of fuel and taking out the turret disables the turret from rotating.

And the final tip (or rather an answer to a question most newbies ask): Which Faction to pick?

First off, lets start with the Wardens. The Wardens (Or the Caiovish), culturally, are based on the Celtics while most of their equipment is inspired by the French (with a little german inspiration sprinkled in). In terms of lore they are fairly conservative and value individuals, often making statues of great leaders/people in their society. They were once a massive empire that is now in decline, and while they weren't an empire in name, they were a trading nation and there is evidence to suggest that they held a monopoly on trade in the region.

Second, the Colonials (or Mesean Republic). The Colonials aren't actually one peoples, but rather a collection of smaller cultures, the main one being the Meseans (Italian) with smaller countries in it such as the Velinians (Greek), Kraunians (French) and Estrellans (Spanish). The Colonials use a variety of equipment, guns from Estrella, tanks from Kraunia, ships from Veli and general gear/equipment from Mesea. In terms of lore, not much is known about them with people either calling the Republic a Democratic Liberator or a Tyrannical Oppressor, all that is 100% confirmed is that they are a highly militaristic state, with a collectivist mindset and little care about individuals unless they made a great contribution to the Republic.

In terms of gameplay, both factions are somewhat identical though the Colonials have better Infantry weapons while the Wardens have better tanks\t\t\t\t\t
