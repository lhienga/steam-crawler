IN THE WORLD OF FOXHOLE, THERE IS ONLY WAR!

Foxhole a game that demands the player base to pick a side and choose how you want to contribute to the war effort. Ever-changing warfare every hour, redraw the border maps. The game sparks a shocking but amazing look into how wars are fought. Through the hardships of the logistics players, to the soldiers manning the defenses, to the partisans disrupting logistics, to the naval commanders either bombarding or d-daying, you the player can make a difference in this game.

The player base on both sides often show a form of camaraderie and humanity. A good example:

You, a soldier bleeding out in a foxhole, dying slowly watching your squad and your friends all being blown to bits to kingdom come, maybe some of them abandon you to your fate. You cry for help, no allied medic in sight to save you as you close your eyes and get ready to die alone. With one last hope, you put in the all chat for help. Then an enemy medic comes and heals you. You are grateful yet confused, the enemy medic tells you to run back to your base and wish you the best.

This type of game offers some strange bond amongst enemy factions. It truly does bring out how a player would want to play. So now you have a choice ahead of you.

The Warden Enclave or the Colonial Legion, Which will you choose?
