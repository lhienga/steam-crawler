Foxhole is great.
playing in the front line, making big pushes with people you only met feels like pushing with people youve know your whole life

the feeling of taking out another tank with your tank crew is exhilarating and always makes you feel proud and accomplished after hours of making a tank from scratch.

However thats where the good ends. i have been playing this game for years now and with the release of 1.0 i was hoping that good changes would come.
except it didnt.

Logistics and many areas of the game still retain old and know issues that us, the community, have pointed out time and time again.
Alts, Logi, base building, etc.
We even had a in game strike that was left unanswered. Facilities wasnt the fix we needed.

I love this game, beyond measure and reason but a lot of changes needs to happen.\t\t\t\t\t
