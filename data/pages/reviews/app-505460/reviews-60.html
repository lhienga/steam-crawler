\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs well stated by Freerk, this cooperative game is great not because any particular phase of it is great (although the economic balance is really, pretty good to great) but because each phase of the game is good to very good, and other than the occasional glitch, not much is really bad - it is in fact that the game HAS all these aspects together and the sum of the parts is far superior. Worth a look if you don't mind top down FPS gaming (which I would say generally I do not like, but like I said, the other things just compel me to play)

Only Full MP, and only one instance where there may be thousands of people playing over the course of an hour or two. Cooperative play is rewarded and uncooperative play is generally frowned upon. Sure, there are knuckle heads (I don't know of a single game community that doesn't have some) but they are far fewer in number here, are certainly NOT encouraged as they are by some game designs, and the general populace of the playership does not support idiot behavior very well. Worth a look at a lets play. If you watch a stream, try to watch one with multiple people on it. Watch for more than a few minutes, or you find yourself wanting to find more vids, you know what you need to do! :)\t\t\t\t\t
