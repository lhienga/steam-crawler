War is Complicated

What I Liked

Some of my favorites games are persistent war games. My go to a long time ago was Planetside and Planetside 2. I had wondered if I could like a game set in this era of time, and surprisingly I did. I was shocked by how the war has so many moving parts and how they worked so well together. Every role I enjoyed. I first learned to take logistics support to the front line, and it was hard to learn, but the more I learned the more efficient I became. I learned to play a medic and I enjoyed healing people. Lastly, combat is a lot of fun. I, of course, was bad at it at first. Then I learned to slow down, aim, and shoot. I had some great shots which made the game that much more enjoyable. Oddly enough, one of my favorite things was to go on the battlefield and scavenge from the dead. Using what they had and adjusting on the fly because of that. Sometimes I was a medic, sometimes I would lay down covering fire, other times I would just be radio support. It was a ton of fun.

What I Did Not Like

I don't care for the commendation system. I do like it, but most times when I run logi support I receive little to no commendations. While I try to give commendations when I can, including medics who heal and resurrect me. While I know it's not the point, I would like to receive some for running logi.

The game is still a bit buggy. I get stuck on terrain, usually a bunker, once every hour at the front lines. It's annoying, especially if you get killed because you are stuck.

The snow. It took about 16 hours for me to encounter snow. It really made me hate the game. I lost my vehicle, bmats, and basically all I was doing within the last hour. I get it, it's part of war. But it really ruined the game for me. Depending how often it snows, it could affect how often I play the game. I never understand why devs add things that makes the game worse, and not better.

Why I Stopped Playing

I'm playing it until 1.0 and then I will see. 1.0 is changing a lot, which has me worried about being a solo player. I hope it turns out well and I play for a long time though!

Obviously, I never came back. I just lost interest after the snow!

Disclaimer

I have been a gamer for over 30 years. I tend to play mostly PC games. I also bounce around between games A LOT. I may purchase a $60 game and play it for 2 hours or spend $10 on a game and spend 50 hours on it. Even if I played a game a short time, it does not mean it is bad. It just means another game caught my attention. I also complain about flaws in games a lot, even about the littlest of things. Keep that in mind when reading my reviews. If I do not have a complaint, then the game must be special!\t\t\t\t\t
