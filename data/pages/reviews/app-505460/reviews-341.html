\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a semi-positive review because I have a lot of complaints.


Logistics gameplay feels like a second job, even more so ever since facilities were added. Gathering basic resources (collecting scraps from scrap fields) is a point-and-click adventure. Takes too much time and work to build enough materials to fuel a frontline. No wonder half the bases are devoid of resources. Oh, wait, regiments hoarding resources is a second reason. Speaking of which:

Much of the content is only attainable by the monopolizing regiments who usually may not share their toys with outsiders. Why? They fear it'll be wasted by the "simple minded randoms". They hoard anything from mass-produced tanks down to simple Basic Materials. Therefore, the rest of the world is reliant on their local regiment feeling particularly charitable.

Big moderation issues regarding false report spams and alt accounts. Be careful who you cross, or you might get banned for doing nothing wrong. Don't expect the devs to help you about it.

6/10,
Game definitely needs work. I'm probably gonna play it anyways, because I'm a masochist (apparently).\t\t\t\t\t
