\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game feels like an idle game which you can't alt-tab out of or you lose all of your progress.

If you try to fight in the front lines, you'll realise you should have bought an ultrawide monitor so you can see and shoot enemies farther away from you. Ultrawide or not, you have to constantly be rotating your perspective so the enemies are in the very corner of your screen so you can attempt to have a fair chance. You'll also run out of resources at the front lines and starting realising not enough people do logistics runs.

Then you'll try to do logistic runs to make up for it to support your team.

Then you'll realise why no one does logistics runs. A single enemy can easily walk into your territory (holding W for 20 minutes) and then sit on the side of the road with a grease gun and completely disable your vehicle in under two seconds.

You may ask yourself... how many people really spend hours of their time wandering around and camping for enemies?

In my experience, most of them. Anyone can kill you by spending more time doing a boring task, forcing you to do an even more boring task to beat them. The cycle continues until you realise you need to invest more time into this game than your actual career if you want to become the best because the majority of the stable player base is people who play for the majority of their day, every day. More time than they spend working or sleeping.

Even experienced players tell me the game doesn't become fun until around 1,000 hours in. Some said it was closer to 800 hours in. Either way, that's a lot of time to invest in a game before it becomes a game instead of a job.

I don't want to spend that many hours in a game before it becomes fun. I play games to relax, not to stare at a bland palette and hold W for 15-30 minutes at a time, or go prone in a bush for 30-45 minutes at a time.

Edit: I forgot to include the multitude of teamkills that I've been on the receiving end of. Why do people team kill? Because the community is toxic. The game even has local voice chat with enemies so they can refer to you in a derogatory manner after they kill you. Every time.\t\t\t\t\t
