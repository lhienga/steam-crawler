\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe scope of this game is unlike anything you've ever seen, but it is an early access, and it does have problems. But it is FUN and unlike anything I've ever played before.

The Good:
- Unique experience with great gameplay depth
- No classes, no level ups, everyone is on even ground.
- Incredible scope, and really shows how wars are reliant on logistics and strategic positions.
- Generally good combat, fun once you understand.
- Doing operations with a plan is pure bliss.
- This community will try to teach new players. Often, when you spawn into the home region, there will be someone running around teaching new players. Particularly when the war is about to end.

The Bad:
- Building. It sucks, it's not fun for most people, and it's easily griefed.
- The research and tech system needs an overhaul (new players don't need to worry about this facet of the game)
- If your team in the region you're fighting in is a bunch of dumdums, there isn't anything you can do to turn the tide of the fight generally.
- The "tutorial" in the home region that you always spawn in is downright garbage for new players. This is a complicated game, and the dev's need to step up the practice area to be easier to follow and understand.\t\t\t\t\t
