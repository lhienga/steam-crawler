\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game would be extremely cool if not for one major issue.

Developers can't possibly grasp how the balance of things should be in their game. How this looks:
Add something op to one side, the other one cries, nerf it add something op to the other side, now the first side
cries, go to step one. Repeat the process for the last couple of years. Root of this issue is devs thinking that both factions should have everything different and it can be magically balanced. To more deeply understand the way the devs think - imagine mines and ant-tank grenades, both are against armored vehicles, so devs think hmm let's add mines to one faction and grenades to another, now they both have ways to deal with armor so it is super balanced right? That's obviously wrong and both factions should have at mines and grenades as they fill different sub-roles.

There are of course some other issues, but not as impactful to the game.

Lots of minor bugs like falling through terrain, stucking, being unable to get out of some trenches, shooting from the side of your character after getting revived, invisible enemies and allies.

Infantry combat is annoying. First issue is that it takes literally 3 seconds for your crosshair to adjust when aiming even if you sit. Second is related to character movement set which allows instant 180+ degrees direction change, combined with the movement prediction system to compensate lag(like in most multiplayer games) people just micro teleport all around which ruins the immersion and forces experienced players to use this clown-step to be combat effective. Also you can't just aim and shoot, a lot of the times you have to aim behind a target or a bullet won't hit.

Now the weather system: imagine living in southern Italy and having 2-3 winters per year where you freeze to death when being outside for 3 minutes or 5 minutes in warm clothes. And every vehicle freezes with the same speed as well including cannons(you can't move or shoot from a frozen field cannon). Or being caught in the rain somewhere else for 10 seconds and suddenly you weight two times more and move accordingly.

Tech system is meh...

This sums up the flaws of this game.

To devs. I could write write a few more pages about the current balance, but I'll be short.
ISG and Cutler fill two different roles so both factions should have them.
Both factions should have 40 mm field cannon and 68 mm AT guns including heavy variants (but you could merge AT cannon and AT gun into one by adding 40/68 mm HE and AP switchable shells for them, so the light version is 40 mm and heavy uses 68 mm caliber).
20 mm AT rifle to both factions as it was before. Replace Mounted bonesaw with Bane 45 and Venom with regular bonesaw, so both factions have bonesaw as T2 AT weapon and Bane 45 as T3. Hydra's Whisper is almost useless throw it away.
Make a vote to Wardens if they want their Aalto storm rifle to have the exact same stats as a Dusk.
Sniper rifles should also have the exact same stats based on Warden or Collie rifle up to you.
Outlaw is obviously broken in this update, with its range, speed and anti-structural damage it should've had paper armor as an LTD.
Adjust MPT price as Wardens want.

TL DR: dev man bad, can't into balancing things.
Also lots of bugs, infantry combat isn't anywhere near good, weather system is crap, but the game still could be fun if you don't take it too seriously and possibly switch to op faction with every major patch.
