\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo my current 61.1 hrs has been just 1 war and I would like to share my first impressions.

When you start you will have a ton of questions. I was told there was a bootcamp, but I never took it. I instead was taught by the players themselves, they worked me through how building works or how you should aim in the corner of your screen as it gives you more sight than anything else. Any questions I had were solved by players in game and being a hands on learner this really helped.

The players are pretty friendly as far as my experience goes. Although I have noticed a LOT of what I call "Faction Pride" which is really just people insulting the other team, most of it is just trash talk but there are some people that take it too far but I can't really fault the game for it's community.

The combat in this game is very rewarding, although agitating when you start because you believe its mostly rng and that's why you miss, but I didn't know to hit someone you NEED the cursor on the target, not in front, not behind. The more you play the more tricks you learn about the combat, like any other game.

Building/Logistics in this game is surprisingly VERY entertaining. I can imagine there are players that only build or do logistics, they tend to go hand in hand. Everything is built by players, every foxhole, every gun, every sand bag, everything. It's impressive how the war functions as you need logi's but the player decides if they want to do that or not. There are no roles you are what you want to be. The building is simplistic enough to not be too hard yet has the depth to be entertaining and not drain you mentally while you support the front line.

This game has a problem with team balance. I played as a colonial during my first war, and it wasn't really even a fight. The wardens had tactics, strats, and a much better logi team as they were probably two tiers of tech ahead of us the entire time. Every time we'd make any sort of advancing push, they would bring out some sort of new tech that just deletes us because we weren't ready.
EVERY fight as a colonial is "Just push the frontline :)" which most of the time isn't an effective strategy. I found it more fun to be a partisan, which is just someone who attacks or disrupts the backline making it more difficult for the frontline to receive resources.

According to https://foxholestats.com/ colonials have lost 6 times in a row, this includes my war. Looking into the past it seems it was balanced at one point but according to people I've met in game, the population is dropping resulting in team balance issues.

TL;DR
The game is really fun but has team balance issues\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere to start. I am pretty bad at writing reviews. So I rarely write them. Especially if it is a negative review. I hate leaving negative reviews, but if there was a dev team deserving a negative review. It is this one.

I only started playing this game this year, just after their big update "Arms Race" I believe it was called. So I have no idea what the game was like before, but honestly, that doesn't matter, because now it needs some serious attention from the Devs. I have been in a clan since Day 1. They showed me the ropes, and I guess they have been playing the game basically since release. Avid fans for sure.

I thoroughly enjoy everything about this game. Mechanics wise, but balance wise, this game is utterly screwed. Me and my clan have tried speaking to the devs, and were completely disregarded and treated, quite frankly, like ♥♥♥♥. The Dev team were extremely disrespectful. But hey, they are game creators, not customer service agents. I get it. This wouldn't be a problem, but oh my god, this game is so unbalanced in it's current state.

The game is set in a persistent battlefield/War, sort of like Planetside2 or Heroes and Generals. Except every single thing in the game is literally built by the players, you have to mine the resources and then refine them, and then build it. Every clip of ammo, every vehicle, even your spawn points. Are mined and refined somewhere down the logistics line. It is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fantastic. But for some bizzare reason. The **Wardens** have a way better tech tree, (you have to also research every item in the game) **There are two teams in the game "Wardens" and "Colonials"**.

The game is also plagued with alt accounts, people from the other side logging onto your team and teamkilling you, throwing away resources, blowing up vehicles. The ammount of times I have been hunted down by Alts after having a really successful run in a tank is not even funny. They will lose some fights and then just log onto your team and blow you up, and if you try and kill them, you will get banned. It is a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ joke. And if you report them. Guess what? You get ignored. Spent hours mining and refining (literally around 5 hours to solo build a tank) and then an alt comes along and blows it up. Guess what 5 hours of work gone, nobody gives a ♥♥♥♥. It is so bad it has become a bit of a meme.

So now the game is in the state that it is in. The Wardens have won 7 consecutive wars, with ease. The Colonial team is basically dead, with hardly anybody playing it, meanwhile the Wardens team is literally full. And rather than aknowledge that their game is dying and trying to work with people, the devs just treat people who complain like garbage and act like everything is fine. It sucks, because I love this games concept, I juts don't like paying people to treat me like garbage. SO heres the thumbs down.
