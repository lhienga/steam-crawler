\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game offers an experience unlike any game that is even remotely similar to it in concept.

Now dear reader, you can find many an in depth review about mechanics and various nuances in the game on other reviews so I will not attempt to retell these things when others have done a far better job that I

So what will I say, well you can see there are some people who are very unhappy with the state of the game. I am hear to encourage you to ignore them. Does the game have issues? yes certainly, many are rather unavoidable given not only the scope of the game but also the fact that it is developed and managed by something like 10 guys. The fact it actually works at all is a massive achievement and is testament to the commitment of the dev team.

Many feel the game is unbalanced, this is not actually true. we just had a war which lasted 52 days, during which time we had a stalemate of several weeks. The only reason that is possible is because the game is actually well balanced. The Wardens however are dominated by a monopoly of large clans who are generally elitist and strategically unimaginative. They also have a tendency to become very defeatist if things don't go their way, like say a few major advances being held back or even routed. The truth is fellow gamers that the recent review bomb of the game happened at exactly the same time that the Wardens began to lose. This is obviously very sad for the warden players who enjoy the game and continued to play out the end of the war. Props to you guys it was a hard won victory. The issue with the warden string of losses is actually a cultural issue for the big sweaty warden clans. Obviously they are not all like this and even most of the actual players in these clans are pretty reasonable people. The issue is that their culture is dominated by whiny man children who make a lot of noise and spread low moral.

I've read a few things about the new facilities being and issue with big groups claiming resources and monopolising the access to all the good toys. This is a warden issue, I have never experienced that last more than a single day when a Colonial group attempts this, which they no longer do because we don't tolerate it. several collie clans make public facilities where people can trade resources they collect for the tools they which to use. Furthermore, setting up a small facility is not even hard to do, you must simply have focus production on what you intend to build. When the moment comes when you want to try different things, switch out the buildings you no longer need for the ones you do. It's that simple.

This is a cooperative MMO war sim, play cooperatively\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really, really, really don't want to give this game a thumbs down, as it is one of my favorite games i've ever played. However, I simply can't give it a thumbs up in its current state. Balance is a big issue in this game, and rather than making an attempt at actually balancing the game the devs instead just swing the balance pendulum back and forth. One side will have a win streak and suddenly the devs will nerf something on the winning side while simultaneously buffing something on the losing side, give more favorable tech to the losing side, give less favorable tech to the winning side, etc. It does happen to both factions, however, devs do generally seem to have bias towards one faction over the other (won't say which).

Quality of life is almost nonexistent in this game, building, logi, and maintaining structures are all tedious. With 1.0 they introduced a plethora of new and very destructive toys, putting further strain on builders and logi. 1.0 also introduced facilities, quite possibly the worst mechanic that has ever been added, it adds a whole new level of grind and tediousness to the game; building and logi were already pretty bad before the update, yet doable. Solos and small groups are discouraged from playing with the new (and old) toys as many are locked behind the aforementioned facilities. People will say "bUtT ITs a TEeM gAMe U haVe 2 woRk 2gEthUr!!!!" yeah, you do, and that is still achieved no matter what you do. You are always contributing even if its indirectly, and also small groups are still groups, we should be able to have access to the cool stuff without the game being a full time job.

I don't want the devs to rework the current system, rather i just want them to add some quality of life changes to make it bearable. On top of all, sometimes big clans will "claim" resource fields and make it hard or impossible to access those resources unless they are kind enough to allow you to access them. You also risk clans mass reporting you because you do something they don't like. Both factions are largely swayed by their respective community groups where all the (mostly major) clans organize, vote, and decide the outcome of the faction. I wouldn't mind those points so much if these clans didn't simply prevent/ make very difficult for non big clans to do their own work.

However, it's not all bad. This is such a unique game that gives an experience like no other (basically Battlfield on steroids). Massive battles with hundreds of people on the same battlefield, tanks of all sizes spearheading attacks, constant artillery shelling creating no man's land or weakening enemy fortifications for easier assaults. There is a role to fit every play style, medics for support, heavy gunner to suppress enemy push, basic rifleman, tank crew member for epic armor combat, partisan for those who would rather sneak behind enemy lines and disrupt logistics, and so many more. This game has so much depth and so much to do it can keep you entertained for hours; with it being as epic or relaxed as you make it.

I have no plan to stop playing this game for good anytime soon because it truly is an amazing concept and amazing game, but until some of these issues are addressed i cannot give this game a thumbs up because they are pretty big issues in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
