\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI hate MMO's. They are unimmersive, boring, and feel like a just worsened singleplayer game that had to strip all the immersion, and add a bunch of grinding to make it fit in a "multiplayer format".

This game really fits all of that (except the first one). I bought this in 2017 and it was a much better game back then, because they had servers like normal multiplayer games do. You could actually make an impact in the war because it really didn't last that long. And i was actually able to witness the end of one.

But now they removed that old server system and replaced it with just 1 giant map with all little server sectors. The wars now also last waaaay longer. Before you could actually have changed the outcome of a war with your squad but now even if you played all day but only that one day it basically didn't achieve anything.

The reason the devs gave for removing skirmish mode was that only 5% of the playerbase's playtime was spend there. I don't believe it could have been that extreme but still that it is pretty logical. The people who would spend huge chunks of time on a consistent basis to actually make an impact on a world wide war would ofcourse dwarf the play time of more normal players.

I really loved this game. I just wanted to keep playing it after those first 2 hours. But after they removed those servers it was just boring and i wouldn't recommend it to anyone with a life or who likes to actually make an impact on a war instead of being another nameless soldier.\t\t\t\t\t
