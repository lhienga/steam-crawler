\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a unique game that offers something not a lot of games of the war genre do. Persistent world warfare with massive teams, hundreds of different battlefields, drop-in drop-out conflict (don't want to fight on the frontlines? Go do Logi or build a base!), and campaigns that take weeks. Actual Operations planned by your clan / friends / whomever. Ever wanted to plan, fund and carry out your own D-Day? You can! But despite all that and despite how much I love it ... I can't recommend this game. At all.

Why? It's simple. The Devs don't care. Let me explain.

I won't rant about 'communication' (although theirs practically doesn't exist) or 'balance' (the factions are different by design). What I mean by this is, Foxhole is an Massive Multiplayer game... with practically no moderation. There are automated systems in play, yes. But they're able to be abused... and not effective in some cases anyways. This war with 1.0 my friends group and I have spent the past few Weeks struggling with the most persistent enemy we've faced constantly attacking the facility we set up. Not a Partisan (enemy backline infiltrator).. not an 'Alt' (a enemy faction player on an alternate account). No, this man had been a Colonial player, actually played.. and got angry for some reason. Got a chip on his shoulder. And to take it out, he decided to run around trolling and griefing any and all players be could find. Driving friendly tanks and vehicles into the water to destroy them. Using mobile cranes to pick up trains, fuel tanks and other parts of facilities and destroying those. Pulling supplies from facilities and bases and destroying those as well. Changing his Steam tag to impersonate people in-game and griefing as 'them'.

And despite the fact that all seven of the people I play with (myself included bringing it to 8) reported him both in-game and on Siege Camp's actual help website every time we saw it happening. Despite the fact that he was doing this to at least five different regiments (that we know of) and two independent small groups of friends/players and all of them have also confirmed constantly reporting him... Nothing. Sure, he would get the auto-ban that timed him out for a day or two at most. But NOTHING beyond that. He has kept coming back for Weeks now, and the war is practically over, and nothing has been done.
