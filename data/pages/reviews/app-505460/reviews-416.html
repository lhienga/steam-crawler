the infantry aspect of the game is also fun, however, i do not mostly do infantry so i do not have a lot of say in it.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt feels like you are playing as a single person in a RTS game, the closest comparison might be Men of War, but with more logistics. This means you could personally take on the role as an infantryman, recon, base builder, resource gatherer etc. You could also team up with a squad, a regiment or random people, to then be incorporated more into the whole. The game takes place on a giant world map, where 2 teams fight over strategic locations, over a long time.

So far, I've seen multiple combat roles available(infantry, tanks, apcs, jeeps, medic, artillery, anti-tank cannons, weapon crews and so on), offensive and defensive recon(structures and certain vehicles/inf radio equipment being able to reveal things on the map), logistics(harvesting raw resources, turning those into materials, which then are used to make other equipment like supplies in factories and vehicles in garages) and building(making spawn bases with bunkers, pillboxes, trenches, obstacles and so on).

I think it's a fun combo, but logistics does play a big part in it. Also with it being a sort of topdown isometric view in a 3D world, it can sometimes be hard to move around and shoot without getting stuck or blocked by random things. The combat feels a bit awkward due to this and the controls, which often are not intuitive. In a way, I think this awkwardness reinforces it as a team game.\t\t\t\t\t
