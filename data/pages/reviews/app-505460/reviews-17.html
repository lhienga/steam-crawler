\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun sandbox type game with a pretty chill player base considering the type of game this is. You can hop around different roles and work in different areas and it makes the game fun since all roles are extremely important (logi, infantry, artillery, armor, facilities, etc.) Regiments also will help you and most people are surprisingly newbie friendly.

I will say if you do start (and well even for vets), just know every piece of equipment you use is created by other players. They mined, crafted, and transported EVERYTHING so treat equipment well and try to avoid stealing if someone clearly is trying to save something for their own operations.

That being said, there are some clear flaws to the game that include:
-IMMENSE amounts of alt accounts used that has been actively ignored by the developers that steal, grief, and ruin queue : in it's current state, so many parts of the game become unbalanced and unfair ON BOTH SIDES since of course alternate accounts are not designed into the gameplay and is technically banned by the rules, yet there is no form of ip banning or any type of system to fight this so it is inevitable unless developers actually want to lose their alternate account game sales
-Server instability
-Bias of developers and interference
-Quality of life for logistics and bases (maintenance supplies can be produced 15k+, yet can only be transported in small batches; logistics in terms of gameplay is severely lacking more complex systems and streamlining that make it nearly unplayable)
-Collision problems with terrain and jankiness with water (you will get stuck often)\t\t\t\t\t
