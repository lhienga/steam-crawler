\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so addicting and I can't stop loving it, despite its rough edges.

An excellent fusion of factory game, trucking simulator, and slower paced (well thought out) shooter this game merges three genres and thus has something for lots of different people. The persistent warfare means you can always login and contribute to the war effort to whatever extent you want before calling it a night. It also means that your contributions trickle into your entire team's effort, making everything you do part of a greater effort (or completely wasted!).

This game isn't for everyone but it is certainly a lot for those who enjoy it.

The Good: Persistent large scale war, combat creativity; patience; and teamwork are strongly rewarded, supportive community, tons of different roles and niches to fill
The Bad: It's got a gnarly learning curve, the tutorials are frankly awful, a lot of the UI choices are baffling, many of the gameplay balance decisions feel rushed and are arbitrarily restricting, factory "chores" can make gameplay extremely tedious at times for all but the largest clans.

Despite the bad I love this game and hope the devs continue to support for years... I could certainly see myself playing for that long!\t\t\t\t\t
