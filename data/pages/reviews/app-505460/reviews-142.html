\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs the game good? Yes.
Is it fun? Yes.
Can you sink 10s of hours into this game each time you open it? Absolutely yes

But it's very niche. The gameplay is fairly slow for a solo player and you can't get much done without at least cooperating with others. Sometimes that means you'll have great experiences on the frontlines with natural leaders taking charge on the mics and the newer players following orders, and the whole game becomes a well oiled war machine simulator. Other times that means pure chaos in silence as your enemies encircle your bases and your friendlies are only able to spam nades with no comms being called.

If you are a player that is looking for something where your actions truly matter in day-to-day activities and you want to work with others on achieving common goals and you view the dedication required to the game as a positive, then this game is right up your alley. On the flip side, if you aren't able to at least dedicate a few hours each day to fighting/gathering resources/building, then you don't really get to see the macro picture of the game which is where the complexity and fun is really at.\t\t\t\t\t
