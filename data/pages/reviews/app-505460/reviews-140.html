\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don’t usually write reviews but for this game I feel like I should. Foxhole as a concept is a fantastic idea for a game that gives you a truly one of a kind gaming experience, unfortunately being a great idea is the only thing that is good about it. Foxhole has been so catastrophically mismanaged by its developers that its own community has actively tried to make the game fail.

I have played Foxhole in every war from WC71 to WC101 logging over an entire week of playtime during the 1.0 update war, that’s almost exactly 2 years of consistently playing this game and watching it develop so believe me when I say that this game has seriously gone downhill. The developers of this game have allowed themselves to be stubborn and petty to the point of destroying a revolutionary game.

Here are some reasons that you may want to rethink buying Foxhole
•Foxhole is filled with long standing bugs, exploits and hacks that the devs have ignored in order to push out bloated content updates.
•The new logistics system in game is overly complex and takes hundreds if not thousands of man hours to maintain a single facility for the duration of the war (usually a month or so).
•After several years the developers have only recently made changes to reduce the amount of ALT accounts easily destroying/sabotaging your hundreds of hours of work.
•There is an incomprehensive and outdated tutorial that is generally skipped, causing unintentional griefing.

The developers refuse to balance the game. There is a clear faction bias. The last several updates have heavily favored colonials, giving them many cheap yet very powerful weapons and vehicles to add to their already strong arsenal. The new weapons and vehicles have given the colonials a massive advantage over the wardens, a faction whose weapons are generally described as gimmicky and not as useful as their colonial counterparts. Evidence of bias also comes in the form of colonial buffs immediately following the recent 8 month long colonial win streak.

For the sake of keeping things as concise as possible I will stop here but I'd like to note that these are not all of Foxhole’s problems, these are just some of the biggest and most relevant that come to mind.

In conclusion I would highly recommend against buying Foxhole, Anvil Empires or any other game touched by Siege Camp.
