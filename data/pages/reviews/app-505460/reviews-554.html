\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSince there isnt a "Depending on some stuff" kinda review. Ill give my fair shot here.

Foxhole is what i would call semi realistic game (semi because of some systems9

Positive things:
-Good community at times, lots of communication.
-Fun gameplay at times with player made events.
-Alot of different weapons to explore

Negative things:
- Lets be honest, the aiming system is a "git gud" system as many other games, but it is also dependent on angles, wind directions (on some weapons) and the position you are using. The problem with this system is the curve ive seen at times, a bullet curving down in close/semi close range, a tank shell curving down into a trench (not at the edges). I would like this system to be remade but i do understand that alot of players like it.
- Artillery: Fun in small sizes, not fun when a clan has stocked up on Artillery shells and fire it away at a base for 2 hours straight, my recommendation to fix this is adding a cooldown. That way taking over bases wouldnt be as much artillery dependent as it is now.

-Mass reporting: A problem that is controversial within the foxhole community is clans abusing the report function to essentially ban players over and over again. There are many proposed solutions to this but i would say it needs to get fixed (along with alting).

All in all, foxhole has certain positive aspects to it, but the negative aspects is just systems that requires fixing or remakes in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
