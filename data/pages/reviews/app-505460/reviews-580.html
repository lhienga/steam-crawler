\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really regret purchasing this game.

Foxhole wars last that long because it is slow, grindy and full tiresome loops. Companionship and cooperative gameplay is the only rewarding experience you might get out of this title because the game itself won't show you any meaningfull progress or achievement for your personal efforts.

The best:
-Scenery and map alterations.
-Companionship and cooperative tasks.
-Vehicle, weapon and tool variety.

The worst:
-There is no static vertical control over the aim system, which makes uneven terrain a headache.
-There is no feedback when you land a shot, which makes shooting unrewarding.
-There is no player markers in the deploy map, which would make deploying easier and faster.
-There is no player inertia so zig-zag sprinting is a thing, which is annoying and looks ridiculous.
-The chat design is too chaotic.\t\t\t\t\t
