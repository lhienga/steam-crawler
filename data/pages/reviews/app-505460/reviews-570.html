\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositives:
- Updated Regularly
- Even though it is repetitive, doesn't feel like it
- Time flies
- Kinda one of a kind
- The community has it's own culture
- Can run on potato computers
- Has a promising future

Negatives:
- It is quite a social game so in the end the community matters a lot. There are a lot of annoying people in the game however. People who claim they know it all but don't know ♥♥♥♥♥, hoarders, nationalist regiments, just generally toxic people.
- In most cases it demands a lot of time, long queues, a minute to spawn, another minute to gear up, 10 seconds to die. Don't get me wrong it is natural, but it is not the type of game you can hop in, play for 40 minutes and have casual fun; in most cases.
- Each region can have 200 players in it. 100 players from each faction. In some cases it can be near impossible to play as you like. Doing logi? Good luck taking those soldier supplies to the front when there are 18 players ahead of you.

Now I didn't explain the negatives with details because the game is flawed or bad. I simply wanted to explain because I didn't want to say dogsh*t community and put a dot. There are tons of friendly people who wants to help new players for no reason, but negatives can effect you a bit more than positives in this case, depending on what you want to do.

Conclusion:
It might be a cancerous game but what can I say, I like it. Probably you will find something that peaks your interest whoever you are. Want to be a loner? Run logi or scrap. Want to play with a small group of friends? Do artillery or get yourself a tank. Want to babysit? Build a base. I believe there is something to do for anyone in this game. I will give it an 8/10 I really hope it never dies and continues growing slowly.
I also think the developers should sell a mini DLC, something cosmetic like a small supporter icon or something, because I really believe they deserve more money for their hard work. Some if you compare it to other games, the amount of updates this game gets is incredible. If you are curious about the game but afraid to buy it; I say just go for it. Probably you won't regret it, even if you regret, you will probably end up playing it anyway.
