\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs fun and unique a game as foxhole can be its held back by a lot of very poor features/issues

As a positive the community is a lot of fun and probably the best thing going for it, having someone call out for a medic in the middle of artillery strikes is really enjoyable and as far as I can tell so long as you're willing to listen and learn a new player won't have any problems.

Another positive is building structures is a lot of fun and you can do a fair bit with it, plenty of times have I been covering someone who has been building sandbag walls and crates below so MG gunners can sit on top and defend a position.

Logistics is a lot of fun, driving around from A - B bringing supplies while trying to dodge the occasional partisan. You can help a lot in a war without even fighting in it by doing logistics. Only issue is clans hoarding the salvage points but there's usually a few public ones around too.

As for negatives aiming is a struggle especially when it comes to aiming up or down hill, you are supposed to use the yellow line when aiming to determine how high your shot will go but sometimes its very difficult to gauge if your missing because of the spread of some weapons or because you're aiming is slightly off. When on a bridge this hits the hardest because sometimes there is a small spot you can aim to get your shots on target but it feels far more awkward than it needs to be.

Character movement is for the most part fine until you have to clime where if you're too close to the side of a trench your character will refuse to climb it, couple this to how trenches are sometimes all over the place means its frustrating to get anywhere.

Going from prone to crouching/standing takes far longer than it should and its a death trap a lot of the time. If you get flanked while prone or you press the key by accident you're already dead a lot of time time.

When you're getting your load out you can't cancel if you picked something by accident so you're stuck waiting which when you need to get to the front line asap it just becomes annoying.

What's worst of all if when you get shot you are slowed to a walk and while this could be a decent idea in theory the amount you're slowed down to here is so severe you're very likely to die afterwards anyway making it even more frustrating.

TLDR: Game is fun but with too many flaws, I can almost recommend it but its just not quite good enough.\t\t\t\t\t
