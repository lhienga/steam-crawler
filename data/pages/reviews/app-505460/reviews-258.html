\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe preview video left me with the impression this was somewhat cartoonish. I didn't have huge expectations. Those expectations have been simply blown away. This game not only has very decent graphics, but amazingly in-depth gameplay. Once I got a handle on the aiming mechanics (difficult at first), combat has been utterly astounding. Combat is just a part of the war though. Players need to coordinate scavenging, refining, crafting, building, and transporting to ensure smooth logistics to the front lines. Every gun, bullet, outfit, gas mask, bandage, tank, artillery, shell, truck, trench, and bunker are player-made. Vast networks of player-made factories, refineries, and bases sprawl dozens of zones.

Every new player should spend some time at the front and learn the basics of combat. If you identify needs of your team you can then try to figure out the best way to meet those needs by playing in the backline zones to prepare supplies. Foxhole has a huge range of options for gameplay. This lets players take a break from certain aspects and try others if/when they want to try something different.

All in all 10/10. I would recommend this game to everyone to at least give it a try. I promise you will not be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
