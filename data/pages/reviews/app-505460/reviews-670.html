Update: I now have 1600+ hrs in this game. It is highly addicting, lots of fun. I've played many hours on both sides, but always find myself leaving the Wardens for the Collies specifically because of issues of toxicity with teammates. While the devs are worthy of praise in many aspects of this game, one area that is lacking big time is mechanics to keep teammates from sabotaging your work and progress. This is by far the biggest problem with the game, so much so I can't even think of any other major complaints. I can not recommend this game until mechanics are put in place to keep teammates from so effectively sabotaging their own side, especially since this game is all about teamwork and cooperation.
