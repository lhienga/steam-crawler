\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReview as of update v0.52

Foxhole is one of those games that it is a hobby unto itself. If you've ever gotten into table-top wargames before, this is worth a try for sure. It's also way cheaper and labor intensive than table-top wargames. The best part is because of the MMO aspect you can play as much or as little as you want, but every soldier in the field helps.

PROS:
+ Early Access game that is very well done.
+ Always something to do. Always something going on.
+ Playable as social and solo players.

CONS:
- Tutorials only teach you the basics and there is a LOT to learn in the game. (I recommend watching some videos on what you want to do in the game.)
- You'll need mods if you're not great with color. (RIP allied soldiers I blew away before colors registered)
- A lot of the biggest features requires coordination across multiple players, so without a clan or a dedicated group you likely will miss out on a lot of late game stuff.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tToo in-dept into resources and economic for me.

At first I thought the game would be focusing on the frontline aspect with a little side of logistic.

Like tanks, cars, boat, etc etc would be spawned and ready to use as long as you feed it with resources. And to get resources you just to go resource drop off station, carry it to frontlines, and it will be turned into spawn points for cars and guns and ammo and such.

But nope, you got to collect specific resources (this is where I draw my line) and turned them into different crafting materials, then you got to go to car factory, choose blueprint you want, and start building it.

When you craft guns and ammo and medical supplies and stuff, you can't even use it outright. You got to put them in crate and have tracks ship them to frontline base where god knows who can issue them to soldiers.

(you can't crate/uncrate them as you wish, btw. You got to specify it when you make a batch of it, which is the most BS part, think if you try to put something in crate IRL and you magically can't because you're not doing it when you manufacture it.)

Also vehicles uses fuel, which you got to craft.\t\t\t\t\t
