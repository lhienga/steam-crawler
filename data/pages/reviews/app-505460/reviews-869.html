\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wouldn't recommend you to by this product if you're going to stay here for a long time (and you will). The concept of the game is unique and you'll never see any project with such idea of such big scaled MMO. That's all about the pros.
About the cons. There's ton of em.
1. Game's 1.0 is coming in next summer and it doesn't even feels like pre 1.0 game. It feels more like midddle of developing. Devs are still *testing* things and trying to figure out more what they can implement. Apparently they add a lot of vehicles but they didn't manage to balance them properly. It made a huge drama in the community.
2. Game is still crude AF. Bugs and weird mechanics without hotfixes included. Exploits also included.
3. Community is the main part of the game. In some way it's unique as the game's concept but in its own way. It's not like: devs do the game independently and players play it and report some bugs or call out some stuff. No. In this game devs are very submissive: they listen more only to the one half of game's community which's basically loyal to Colonial faction and then implement overpowered vehicles and mechanics calling it as "the vision". Second half wasn't taken in account for some reason. Conclusion: dev team is biased.
4. Mod team and Community manager. Oh boy, that's something. These guys are not dedicated specialists which would get paid and be truly neutral. They are players. Sometimes they're clan members. Sometimes clan leaders or even one of the admins of clans' association. And guess how balanced and "neutral" mods are? It was rhetorical question. Most of them are infamously know as colonial players. Forget about such thing as free of speech on discord or reddit. If you wear blue uniform and you're known as a loyalist - your opinion doesn't count however valuable it is.
BTW Community Manager is a quintessence of incompetence and infantilism. The guy who's living in a couple of blocks from devs' office became a CM and brought his internet friends in mod team letting them do whatever they want. That's ridiculous.

I think that's not all but you have to think twice before you will buy this game because one moment you will be in centre of sort of scandal or "drama".
Good luck, soldier.\t\t\t\t\t
