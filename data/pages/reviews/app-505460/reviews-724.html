expand each tier to have more vehicles(3-5) and limit the number of researches per tier to 2-3 vehicles

soldier (combat).

The game is designed to disallow any kind of private property (both for single player and for clan). It allows you to store SOME items to private storage for up to 2 days but it still puts hurdles before you even with this. It allows storage of some materials in refinary but it again puts hurdles in front of you (not as simple as it sounds).

You can try to make vehicle far from front, lock it and hope no one will take it, so you can enjoy it the next morning. I tried, it was "stolen" of course.

This lack of private storage kills the game for me and at the time of this review, I do not plan to play any more.\t\t\t\t\t
