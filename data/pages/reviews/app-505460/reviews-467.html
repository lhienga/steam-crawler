\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat concept of an indie style game. Requires teamwork and in game communications to get the most out of this game, the game itself will absorb you, its very immersive,hours seem like minutes as your scrambling to recover a lost sector. Sounds and atmospherics are pretty good, however the main issue with the game lies with the in game reporting systems.

The game has a steep learning kerb, controlled by the clans of the very few servers available. If you don’t play the way the clans want, you will get baited into traps that will easily lead to lengthy bans. You will find yourself hiding in hopes that you are not spotted by your own team mates only to be quickly banned once spotted again until eventually the server is unplayable.
The game is controlled by the players, not the game designers or moderators due to weakness on their part. The game has systems that can be easily abused and manipulated.

My experience was great to start with, helping to push forward and eventually taking the foundry with force, set up by my own incentive to push out and destroy several bunkers by my own doing, however, they didn’t want this for some reason. I got toyed and followed for hours, raciest abuse via game chat suggesting I was feeding the enemy matts and weapons. Attempted to bait me into team killing, yet no cigar 😉 so they of course mass voted me out resulting in a ban.

Second count; An hour into a match, spawned at a new location, helping to build defensive structures until we get fired upon by artillery. i die several times trying to build through it, so i switch to our artillery to fire back on the near by enemy town when i get shot and removed from the artillery gun by my own team members, so the group members dominant in this area can use it. i retaliated, then took all their ammunition away before being ganged up on, killed continuously multiple times then mass voted and banned again.
if you don't mind being walked over, then sure, just walk away, but i for one wanted a game full of great team efforts and a great community to bring the game together but instead, we get this.

Unless you’re a sheep, willing to follow immature, elitists “leaders”, then of course, this game is for you. If you want a game where you have actual freedom of how to play the game you want that you just paid for, then this isn’t for you sorry to say. The game developers need more time learning better in game systems and design.
Definitely needs a better in game guide and not expect the community to show new players via YouTube as the game currently has 2 pages of hints but noting in depth.\t\t\t\t\t
