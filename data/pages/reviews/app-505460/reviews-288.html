\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, a sandbox you say...

It would be terrible if a majority of the game was just moving inventory from one box to the next, right? But, it's actually worse: each item you move has a delay associated with it. You will spend an unhealthy amount of time waiting for a ticker to count to 100% while you could be doing literally anything else.

The combat and aiming system is atrocious. There are RTS games where you take control of a single person that handle this concept better, and it's only a fragment of their game. In this one, it's the whole game, yet it still controls like absolute ♥♥♥♥. There is no precision control of your aiming. If a dude is at a different elevation than you just blankly aim over or around them in a straight line with no nuance.

And it's a sandbox, so imagine the core hook being doing these things millions of times. The core play should be so fun I want to repeat it.

I'm fairly certain this is just a chatroom with a game attached for most people. The problem for me is I remember playing chatroom games on AOL that had saved progression, stats and, frankly, more inputs required. Why can't I have more feedback on my gameplay session? Time spent in enemy territory, kills, wounds survived, highest single damage dealt, etc. there is basically no information on how you've conducted your session.

Sincerely, someone who used to slide nade you in Chain of Command.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game, you gotta communicate to get the most of this game. I highly recommend joining the Foxhole discord and joining a clan if you want to experience large scale operations. That is where this game truly shines, like yesterday 28 of us did an infantry operation and it was absolutely wild. A little chaotic but in the beat way possible, some of the most fun I have had playing a video game in so long. Also I have never come across any negative people in this game so far. Everyone has been nice and very willing to help, dont let the reviews that say otherwise scare you away. Those experiences are the minority and if you do come across someone who is a jerk just mute them and move on cause its not worth letting a small few bad apples ruin this great game. Also a heads up there is a lot to learn in this game and it can be a little over whelming, just focus on one thing at first like front lining or logistics and after a while you'll just start to learn everything else naturally. It is a much more relaxing way to learn the game then trying to learn everything at the same time. Honestly don't take my word for it, just buy the game and you wont regret it.
