\r\n\t\t\t\t\tConceptually Foxhole is one of the best games I've ever played but that concept isn't the reality. It has the potential to be revolutionary but I don't believe it can achieve that with the current attitude of the developers. In this game there is a serious lack of moderation or effective tools for player moderation (players can vote temp ban but no matter how many times this is done the offender can come back and just grief again the next day) there is a ticket system that often does not get listened to by the devs. On top of the standard griefing you would expect in a game like this there is a phenomenon known as 'alting' where players from one faction buy alternate accounts and join the opposing faction with them, they then set about ruining hundreds of hours of other innocent players time and all that can be done is to hopefully get enough votes to get rid of them for a bit, however, often this alting goes under the radar because the activity logs we have to check aren't sufficient. The issue with the alting not being addressed ever by the devs has lead to a lot of paranoia towards new players by the veteran player base and can make the experience of being a new player miserable but the devs don't care, they don't communicate and frankly they profit from the alting so their silence is deafening. The devs do not respect the players time investment in this game, logistsics and bulding (as important as the combat) are tedious and dull experiences and were only made worse by the factorio esque updates in 1.0 where we received a whole new logistics system (that soft locks causal players out of half the vehicles in the game btw) instead of the quality of life for builders and logi that we've been asking for for nearly 2 years now.

tldr: dev man bad, dev man likes to profit off of their player bases misery and refuses to communicate with them.\t\t\t\t\t
