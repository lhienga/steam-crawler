If the average war is around 23 days and the average intermission is 2 days, that literally means that 8% of the time there is no point in playing the game, they need to rebrand as the 'mostly persistant' war lol

Whoever decided that a 'forced' two day break would occur between wars is my least favourite person. As a new player it has destroyed my momentum and enjoyment.
