\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like this game, so It gets a thumbs up but.... It has SERIOUS problems.

You ever spend 40 hours putting everything together so your small regi can have the tanks and ammo it needs, only to log back in and find all your tanks were taken and stored in the seaport for everyone to use?

Play this game, and find out.

Do you like not being able to privately lock your facilities producing things so randos come steal it before you can move it to the next facility down the line?

This game is a perfect fit for you then.


Add into it the two sides are NOT balanced. Just look at the battle tanks of each side.

Wardens big tank has 2 cannons on it. When it upgrades it has 2 cannons and 2 quad Mounted grenade launchers. (8 grenade launchers...)

Now the colonial Big tank. It has 1 cannon of the exact same size as the Wardens tank. And a Machine Gun.... And it upgrades to... Having sandbags on it and tank tracks wrapped around the turret for a little extra armor...

Let's look at infantry weapons now.

Warden Anti-Tank Rifle can be carried normally by an infantryman.

The Colonial Anti-tank rifle requires a tripod to use (AKA you need someone to carry a tripod, and you carry the gun)

Still don't believe me? Let's take a look at the logistics trucks.

There are two upgraded trucks for each side.

The wardens 2: 1st: Loses an inventory slot for extra armor so it takes twice as much to disable it
2nd: Gets a half track so it can move resources off-road faster.

Both of those are worth getting in the right circumstance.

Colonial upgraded trucks, 1st: Loses an inventory slot for a nos boost(ONLY 10% btw, not enough to escape a fight) that drains your fuel at 5x the speed. 100% useless. if you spam boost, your tank drains in 3 mins (can't even cross 1 hex)

2nd: Gets a machine gun on the passenger seat, but is disabled after 2 small arms rounds hits it....

And Finally, let's look at the Rocket Arty vehicles....

The Colonials get a TRUCK. It's slow af off road, but truck speed on road. And is disabled like a truck ( pistol will disable you)
Wardens get an armored half track. Decent on and off road speeds, requires AT to put down, AND IT'S CHEAPER BY A LARGE MARGIN.

This game is fun, but highly unbalanced, and needs fixed.
