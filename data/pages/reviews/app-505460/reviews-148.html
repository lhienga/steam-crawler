\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA summery of this game for new players.
Foxhole is a very one of a kind experience, that I have seen no other game do. The amount of different ways that you can play this game and help the team out is awesome, and has a role for any kind of player. You like action, get a gun or tank and go to a front and fight. Do you like chill gaming sessions, grab a truck and harvest some resources, or craft some supplies deliver them to a point of interest. Want to mess with the enemy, get behind enemy lines and try to steal stuff or just cause chaos.
There are so many more ways to play this game that it will be able to appeal to any kind of person.

The game isn't perfect though far from it. There are many frustrations, people creating alt accounts to hurt the enemy team while on the enemy team, building being so fickle with how you can build in certain places (if at all), the voip errors making where you can't communicate with people, and many more problems.

Would I recommend this game. Yes, this game is just too unique and fun, you will not find another game with the same type of game play as this, and while the issues are annoying they are not bad enough for me to say that you shouldn't play this game. Give it a try, play different roles to find what you like, and remember to ask in chat, in game if you have a question or need help. The community over all is very helpful and kind to new players (usually).

I hope you find foxhole as fun as I have.
P.S. There is a lot more good things, bad things, and roles you can play that I didn't mention. This game is up and down a lot of the time but truly a one of a kind game.\t\t\t\t\t
