Pros:
- Diversity of what you can do
- Fantasic ingame Community
- Unique Production Mechanic
- Replayability

Cons:
- Part of the community can be Meh (like all communities)
- Somewhat steep learning curve (offset by helpful communuty)
- Occasional bugs (but it's early access so hey)

Overall, i cannot really fault the game, onto some information.

You'll spawn at the home resgion, you might choose to go do the training, learning about Combat, Logistics, Vehicles and even Artillery.
If you don't you'll spawn at either a frontline, or a backline you may feel lost if you don't know where to go or what to do, being Early Access the game lacks a indepth tutorial as the game is changeing, but ve vocal; the community is your tutorial, example:

Often I'll be doing my normal logistics and see a player with no rank stood or wandering about, I'll call out to them asking if they're alright; they'll say "they're trying to get a truck" or "they're new" my eyes light up and I stop what I'm doing to help them, I get them some materials for a truck or tell them where to find one, or I explain Logistics to them.

The key Unique aspect of this game is except for world struxtures e.g. Roads, Houses, Bridges; everything so every gun, vehicle, wall, defence; is player made; someone (maybe not all the same player) harvested the metal, refined it, manfactured it, and delivered it so when you spawn you can shoot at the enemy.

This is a game that I stress; give yourself the time to learn, find helpful people, join your Faction's community hub, find a clan, learn and integrate into the community.
You will love it.

There are different things you can do:
- Logistics:
-- Scrap
-- Manfacture
-- Deliveries
- Building (can be frontline too):
-- Bases
-- Defences
-- Offensive Emplacements
-- Massive Cannons
- Infantry (equipment taken, not loadouts)
-- Rifleman
-- CQB/Suppression (e.g. SMG)
-- Building destruction
-- Vocal commander (spot enemy and cordinate)
-- Tank Hunter/Anti Tank
-- Medic
-- Looter (loot the dead to save logi backache)
- Vehicles;
-- Motocycles
-- Jeeps
-- 2 Man Field Artillery
-- Armored Cars
-- Tanks
-- Transport Boats (gun barge)
-- Gunboats

In a single spawn you can do multiple of these: Grab a rifle on spawn to push back the enemy (Rifleman), and then find a medpack on the ground (Looter) and keep that with you and heal a few guys (Medic) then some enemy armor turns up and you find an Anti Tank Rifle and pop some shots at it. Perhaps you run out of ammo and head back to the forward base and a guy asks for a gunner in his tank.

TLDR:
I love this game, the way everytjing is plauer made is super-unque; find a community, take the time to learn from others, and you'll find what you love to do easily.\t\t\t\t\t

However all of that worry went away when i bought this game to play with my friend, the community is in fact, one of the most friendly i've seen, allies are helpful, and enemies are respectful. Although we shoot each other, there's always that small window of peace.
