\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has potential to be so fun. Whether you are running around trenches, hunting for weapons and ammo, or grouping with 7 people to rush an enemy structure so you can all throw 4 HE nades inside, or if you are relaxing whilst driving around delivering supplies to the front, it has potential to be fun.

But the devs just cant quite grasp it. Yes, the balance is and warden is objectively better tech wise. But you can overcome wardens having better tech, and we were. But suddenly every colonial in Clahstra gets kicked, all their AI gets disabled, and wardens are just able to undo hours of progress with no effort. They were on their heels, losing ground, and they were saved not by their ingenuity, not by some amazing call or plan, but by the devs. Dozens of people quit the game on the spot, many claiming they didnt plan to return because it simply ruins the fun and feeling like you have an impact when the devs allow this.

2 hours post the event, and no dev has responded, not comments from any staff on the official discord or in game. I have lost many times in this game, and all of those sucked. But this was not losing, this was getting cheated. No fault of our own saw us lose hours of progress and resources. Until the devs are able to respond to this situation, i cannot find the motivation to continue, or recommend this game.
