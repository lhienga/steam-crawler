\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the only games where it feels like you are actually contributing to a war effort with everything you do. You can dedicate yourself to building defenses, or gathering materials for people to use for war. You can be a frontline soldier, or a medic, or even a partisan -- behind enemy lines, disrupting logistics and preventing supplies from getting to the front. The dev team is very active, and there are still new updates coming out all the time. Hell, there was an update today that released two new tanks and four new guns. The lore is fairly interesting. The gunplay is a little clunky at first, but you get used to it.

People complain about toxicity, but I haven't really noticed it. I did have someone steal the truck I had just built, but to be honest, that's not really that bad.

Really excited to see where this game goes in the future. This is already an awesome game in early access, and with the game releasing its 1.0 patch later this year and being a full release, I can't wait to see what they have in store for us.

EDIT: Unfortunately I have to change my review to a "Do Not Recommend". I haven't played the game since April 2021, when the game team told me that neo-nazis have a right to spout their hate and extremism in-game.

So if you're a neo-nazi degenerate, this is the game for you!\t\t\t\t\t
