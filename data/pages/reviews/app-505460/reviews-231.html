\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a very immersive war simulation game with logistics, player coordination, and everything. I remember my very first time playing it, I struggled just to find the frontline, only to realize it was just across a bridge somewhere across the map. At the frontline, I heard deafening gunshots and artillery fire. It was horrifying not knowing what was going on and seeing explosions and bullets whizzing by. Soldiers were shouting and dying everywhere. It was the immersive gameplay that I'd been looking for for a long time.

The gunplay is hilariously slow-paced. This is, of course, intentional and is part of the immersion. For example, sniping requires you to wait around 5 seconds for the crosshair to be pinpoint accurate. Rifles have horrible accuracy when moving, therefore, you have to go prone for them to be effective. Submachine guns are just spray-and-pray guns. Bringing a bayonet to a gunfight is a surprisingly solid strategy in this game. Seeing a bayonet-wielding-madman charge at you at full speed does things to people. It makes you feel like a stormtrooper fighting a Jedi, except, it's 5 riflemen vs 1 bayonet boi.

The MAIN issue I have with this game is the QoL features... if there are even any of them. I understand that this might be intentional because, after all, this is a teamwork-oriented game. You are not expected to be able to do everything by yourself.

Doing logistics for the first few runs is fun, but later down the road, it becomes repetitive and frustrating. Managing inventory is a pain. You have to constantly click on things, or hold down the mouse button to do things. Other features of this game should include giving players Carpal Tunnel Syndrome. I had to resort to using autoclickers just to do basic stuffs and not risk hand injury.

The dev team has been working hard on this game with lots of great updates. They finally added trains! You can now get addicted to Foxtorio. The devs haven't shown any sign of stopping updating this game and this is good news.

7/10. More QoL features please\t\t\t\t\t
