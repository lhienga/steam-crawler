\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's pretty much everything I've wanted from a team based multiplayer shooter, only lacking the first person view. Fully player and team driven war for a massive map. love the concept and the game play is pretty fun. it seems that there may be some balancing issues as the past few wars have been a bit one-sided but i'm having fun.


EDIT: so a few more thoughts having a few hours in now: I think the choice to change the tech trees every war is an odd one. I'm still learning so how much this effects the game is something i'm still getting used too. A more major issue for me is that your screen/aspect ratio has a major effect on gameplay. you can see further at the corner of your screen than the top or sides, meaning that you're constantly reorienting your view to try to get the maximum possible range on enemies. I'm used to it now and not sure what a good solution to this might but it is rather offputting.
I do love that every gun, grenade, vehicle, and bunker is entirely player built or produced which means keeping supply lines protected is essential to winning the war. this system creates a lot of emergent gameplay that you won't see in other shooters. Like for example when supply lines get cutoff, defenders often resort to scavenging the battlefield for munitions and weapons so they can keep the fighting going.\t\t\t\t\t
