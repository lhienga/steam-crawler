\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun game, but be ready to find friends or clans. A lot of content requires ton of resources and custom facilities (player-built logistical structures for advanced in-game stuff) buildings which need to be done and maintained (regularly or else structures will decay with time) to produce everything else that isn't done just in "normal" logistical structures. (world structures that are avaiable to everyone and don't require any processing on custom facilities)

You've probably heard it a lot already but this isn't your classic MMO or war game as everything here is done by players - defences, weapons, ammo, fuel, resources, tanks, ships etc. which sounds super interesting but also means you can't just have it all and to get it you'll need to grind and often wait hours to get some more advanced stuff like basic tanks (dont forget about fuel and ammo for it) as most players and clans value them and wont gift it away even though you are on the same team.

You still can enjoy game solo even somewhat casually being at fronline as infantry guy roleplaying a bit and having your PTSD from constant shelling and mg fire or play with "classic" tanks (tanks/armored vehicles that aren't a special gimmicky variants that can be done in facilities) if you grind resources and find some random players (or friends) to man it.
But if you want to experience all content (advanced tank combat, artillery operations, rocket artillery operations, naval combat with large battleships (since naval warfare update)) then you'll need to join clans or bring friends that are gonna be as dedicated because otherwise its very hard to do alone.

tl;dr This game is heavily clan based and teamwork is what its buit on and what makes it so fun. You can play game solo but you won't be able to experience all content without communications.\t\t\t\t\t
