\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun, yet very player involved game. Every aspect of the game is player driven. From resource procurement, to supply production and transport, and the obvious front line combat. If you want to have a somewhat relaxing gameplay, you could play a logistical role, sitting far in the back lines, gathering materials and making supplies to help in your respective war effort. If you want intense combat (especially now with the trench warfare update which turned this game into World War I simulator), you can join the front lines and fight for your respective causes.

Whether you are a Colonial or a Warden, teamwork is required to play this game efficiently. So sit back, relax and hop into the trenches with your comrades for some of the best carnage and complexity to ever grace the top down genre.\t\t\t\t\t
