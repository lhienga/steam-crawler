\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn Foxhole, there are many occupations you can pick. Be it a logistical driver in the back lines making guns and artillery shells or a medic trying to heal the wounded of the front line; each has its costs and its benefits. This game is relatively easy to get into, as it is often or not obvious where the front line is, thanks to the in-game map. Just spawn, press E, grab a gun, two mags, and a bandage, and you are off to the races. However, if you are not a fan of being a common (often disposable) grunt, you may not like frontline combat.

Some of the good factors I found while playing in the last three wars.

1: Every day is an adventure. You will most likely do something new and meet new people every time you log on and go to a front.

2: The audio design is very good. Despite the camera being the way it is, guns feel unique and memorable.

3: Plenty of jobs that you can pick from and get good at

4: A job for anyone, and often any mood. If you want to fight in the frontline, go for it. Do you want to build Verdun? Go for it. Do you want to be a tanker? Go for it.

5: The lore (despite being a mess at times) is very cool, and you can find yourself immersed by your sides traditions. Like the Wardens (Also known as blueberries) often say, "Praise Calahan."

There is plenty more probably that I can't think of right now.

Some negatives about the game:

1: The map is constant. There is little to no change to it, and eventually, you will see it all. Of course, there are unique landmarks and towns, but eventually, you will probably see it all, and I have not seen much change.

2: The war is constant, which means you build a base that needs defenders due to a lack of AI defence. And players don't spawn at your base to help you; chances are the nighttime players (Often Asain and European) will steamroll the base. This also connects to my next point.

3: Player count for each side can quickly become unequal and can stay like that, which means that you are often outproduced, out teched, and outnumbered in these situations because the other team simply has the manpower to do things. It can be rather demoralizing.

Apart from all this, I would rebuy it and recommend it to just about anyone. ( Updated ) (EDIT FOR 2023 game reward)\t\t\t\t\t
