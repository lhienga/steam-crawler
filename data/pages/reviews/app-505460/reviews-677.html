\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery nice game, it has some downsides, but overall my first 34 hours with it has been fun and immersive.

Pros:
- Incredibly immersive, sure you sometimes have the moments where an enemy alt is trying to ruin your day or an enemy on a bike just runs up to you and starts bayonetting, but those arent that big of a problem IMO. The flow of the game is great, though respawn timers can be annoying, overall, hours went by fast playing this game.
- It provides a LOAD of unique experiences, every front can be different, every fight can bring up a new situation. Sometimes you are a tank crew trying to push an enemy emplacement that has been slaughtering your infantry, sometimes you are a group of partisans trying to infaltrate the enemy base. Wanna be a logistics guy? Go for it.
- It is PERSISTENT ONLINE WAR BABY. Not only is the war 24/7 online, fighting is 24/7 going on. You can wake up to a vastly different map, and be shocked, and rush to a frontline to help a push or defend. It is incredibly fun to see the community do stuff around you. You are a cog in the machine that is war, and i find that AMAZING.

Cons:
- I have been constantly reminded of balance issues while playing this game, while i had no problem with these, as losing was a fun part of a war too, for me at least, but if you are a person who cares about winning all the time, this might be something you wanna consider (i'd check in to see if they fixed issues such as "Colonials spamming 'Mass-Produced Tanks' before buying it since its apperantly an problem by the time im writing this.")
- Enemy Alts may be really annoying, as they might sabotage you. But this isnt that common, at least not for me, i only saw this happen 2 times in my 34 hours of gameplay.
- While being a basic game, it sometimes struggles to run smoothly, especially overheating my GPU. Idk lol. Just play it at low settings and it wont be that big of a problem.

Overall, GOOD GAME. 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
