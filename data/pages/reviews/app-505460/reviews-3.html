Please Give This One A Go
I honestly wasn't sure if this game would be right for me, I saw the tiktoks and other creators praising this game and showing off unreal battles but I was just not sure I would get into it. I say that only because I thought it would be difficult to insert myself with such a niche community and that I would end up only playing solo. I was wrong.

The foxhole community showed me the ropes with anything I wanted to do or learn about, they answered all my questions about the game without hesitation and random players would stop to ask if I needed help when I clearly did not know what I was doing.

On paper this game ticked all the boxes for me: large scale map, multiple battlefronts, multiple branches of combat, REAL LOGISTICS, communication and coordination like I haven't seen before and lastly a unique art style and set piece.

In my time playing I have almost exclusively played logistics, as it makes you feel important, that you're contributing to the war machine and without players like you the war is lost. I loved thinking about how the bullets, weapons, bandages, artillery pieces and materials that I ship to the front via truck or boat will eventually be used by another player to help win the engagement. That is a level of immersion I haven't felt. Seeing players roleplay basebuilders, factory workers and seamen makes me really happy because it shows the games unique roles are all being filled by someone.

There are several roles I have yet to play that I'm excited to jump into like being a crewman on a battleship or tank, joining a massive assault on a town, mass producing bigger equipment and building my own base. This is the ultimate sandbox that offers a playstyle for anyone interested in the genre. Please give it a chance, have some fun, this is a breath of fres air.
