\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot a good game, the odds of you getting into one of the "magical wonder squads" where people work together is... Way less than zero. Population for the game sucks horribly most of the time, it gets repetitive and teamwork does not actually exist. Logistics is a horrific nightmare grind that is utterly thankless, most of the time you play in total silence clicking as if you are one of those who mindlessly clicks away watching tik-tocks on their phone. Being in combat is just... Endless suicides without coordination, what squads are listed are always 100% locked at all times, you just, shoot. Shoot, die. Respawn, shoot, die. In total silence. No matter how absurd things get, total silence, no one willing to work together to stop a simple problem. Gets even WORSE when dealing with anyone who has "rank" in the game. The only communication in the game is a scrolling text blog that has no meaning 99.99% of the time and you can get that anywhere online for free.

Before you buy this game, go outside, touch the grass, drink a beer and realize your time and money was better spent. Whatever glory this game once had has vaporized. This game is just mindless tedium made famous by youtubers, believe me, what they show aint gonna happen for you 98% of the time. What you see is cherry picked.\t\t\t\t\t
