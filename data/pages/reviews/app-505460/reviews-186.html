\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy story first time playing this game: So after install I loaded up the game and selected Wardens as my faction. After messing around a bit on the home island I asked in chat "where is a new player needed what area needs men" and in the text chat "GO FIGHT FOR THE HOLY LAND" and "GO TO ABANDONED WARD IN THE DEADLANDS AND FIGHT FOR CALLAHAN" appeared. So I spawned in grabbed a gun and helped push back the Colonials to Iron's End and we almost pushed them back to Callahan's Gate before they counter attacked and a back and forth battle began before I knew it 8 hours had gone by. My first war was war 87 and it was glorious wish I had gotten the game sooner since I got the game about 2 weeks into 87
Met some really cool people in this game willing to help a new player out especially when it came to the logistics side of the game.
-Pros-
*Theres alot of ways to contribute to help your side win by doing logistics, combat or by building stuff and the thing I like is that no matter what your doing it feels like what you do matters and has impact on the game every little bit helps *everything in this game from guns, tanks, artillery to ammo and so on was built or sourced from other players.
*large persistent war with 30-40 players on each side is cool being on the frontline for the first time theres no experience like it the joys of taking that first town or point with your alliance is amazing.
*the play area is pretty big and there always something going on with 6 or 7 front line zones you'll find combat pretty fast
*taking it easy and doing logistics helping supply the front is also a cool experience

-not really cons but stuff to look out for-
*I recommend watching Moidawg on Youtube and watching some tutorials on the game so your not totally lost on some of the more complex parts of the game
*this game takes alot of coordination from other players while the game is fairly solo friendly some late game tanks and Artillery takes dedicated clans to build and maintain.
*people complain about alts all the time and I think I can count on two hands how many times I've seen a player alting or trying to sabotage there own alliance don't be a paranoid Badman Larry and think every new player is an alt.
*the community has its toxic and hateful players but by and large the Foxhole community is great, welcoming and friendly.

-CONS!-
*FACILITIES this adds another layer of unneeded complexity to an already complex game adding its own specific vehicles and upgrades and its own special mats and if you not in a sizable clan then your basically locked out of its content. its just a headache to interact with and its almost a full time job on its own to maintain facilities and bases players will either join a clan or just quit the game when they start to learn about facilities its basically the Great Filter of Foxhole.

*The combat and aiming can be a bit jarring and clunky at first but the longer you play the more you get used to the 3rd person overhead view.
*theirs still alot of holes and gaps in the terrain that will get you stuck the !unstuck command will be your best friend.
*trees need to be removable they just get in the way and in some areas just random or blocking stuff unnecessarily.
*Weather specifically snow in this game is annoying just no just play another game or switch to a front without it just a headache to deal with or mitigate its affects

This game is fun but not ready for an official release and is probably the worst thing they could have done this game feels still in beta to me so go with that in mind when buying this game still needs some basic polishing up.\t\t\t\t\t
