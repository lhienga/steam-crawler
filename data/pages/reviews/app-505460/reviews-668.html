\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to like this game, but it needs to get tighter on execution. For example - multiple times I saw references to players logging in on alternate accounts to steal logistics material and give them to the other side. Why allow this to happen?

Here's my biggest gripe: a pretty severe lack of coordination and communication. I guess I can join a clan, but generally speaking I really had no idea what my side's goals were besides either 1) walk towards enemy base or 2) ambush enemy logistics. I guess logistics grinding is option 3.

14 hours in and going through the tutorials I really don't have a good sense of whether we're even attacking or defending on a territory. When you have multiple languages it's even more of a cluster. Had a few cool organic moments working with people nearby, but it took a long time.

Here's the rest of my whinging:
*Hitting enemies on elevation is hard, almost impossible if they're in a house. Maybe there's an easy way to do it, but it's not intuitive and no one I talked to nearby knew how to knock that MG on the second floor out. After 20 minutes and spamming gas grenades we just went around, including the two tanks...

*It's jarring to pass lines of empty vehicles and boats in the middle of nowhere, all usually locked. It makes the world feel less alive and more dead.

*There should be an option to automatically join a squad of your language in the region you spawn in. French and Chinese plays are cool, but doing anything that requires basic coordination ("can you hand me a med kit") is impossible.

*I can't stress enough that communication is rough in this game besides the local voice chat. Any sort of command role that gives directions and cascades info would be welcome. It's frustrating watching person after person walk into the same MG kill zone even as I'm warning them.

I think the game has potential and I hope it can solve these problems.\t\t\t\t\t
