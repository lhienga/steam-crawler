\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdating review: Still give it a positive recommend.


Main recommendation:

-- I really think a command system would improve this game greatly whereby people vote or take roles so that senior players can help coordinate and direct newer players. For a game this massive in size, it seems absolutely impossible to coordinate a team to win the war. It really seems wardens have a better clan system working so they seem to do better, at least to me, a colonial. Like I am taking General to Squad Leaders, etc. Logis seems to have their organization down, but the front line fighting seems to be too disorganized.


Pros:
-- Great concept, very fresh, really gives a different perspective of war. This gives me a great feeling when I run logis because the war lasts very long, which means people can make a difference. It has been good to see the game grow in scope to include trenches, bunkers, new vehicles, weapons etc. This game truly has that teamwork feeling that all games are lacking nowadays
-- Tanks, Vehicles, Boats, weapons, all authentic feeling. Love it!!
-- People generally try to help each other out. I know that if someone asks how to do something, people will show them how or tell them.
-- Battles are fun and logis feel rewarding.
-- There is progression of the war with unlocking stuff over time. A cool feature IMO.
-- I would rate this game 9/10!

Cons:
-- I am not opposed to new players at all. With the recent sale, the player count went up which is good. My issue is that they do not generally listen to advice, and just charge into gun fire more or less ruining entire battles by wasting shirts and taking too much ammo, etc. One issue will illustrate my point. Yesterday or the day before, I was throwing a gas grenade. I said over mics, watch out, get back, etc etc multiple times. A swarm of newbies just then jumped into my trench and ran in front of me. I got shot, killed, and dropped the gas grenade which killed most of them. I got like 5 TKs which were absolutely not my fault. I was banned from weapons for 30 minutes? I got reported for tking, and was furious. I did cuss them out, which brings me to my next point. I understand banning tking on purpose but this? Why not just disable team killing if that is such an issue?
-- No foul language. I got reported for this immediately after. I will say, that I do not think the warring sides of WW1 and WW2 were polite on the battlefield. I can understand banning hate speech, absolutely. But if I call a newbie 'a ****ing idiot' for being a ... idiot, I feel like that should be fair game, especially if they will not listen.
-- It seems like it is to easy to 'spy'. People constantly complain that someone is on wardens or colonials that is feeding the other side information.
-- With the new trench system, which I generally applaud. It seems like people over build trenches. Every battlefield has become an absolutely clustermuck, and not in the authentic, cool way you would want to represent the Great wars.\t\t\t\t\t
