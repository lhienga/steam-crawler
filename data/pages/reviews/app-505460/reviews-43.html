\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've only played in one war, so my review is purely based on a fresh persons view on the game. The game overall I think has a lot of potential and can be a very fun game at times, the sandbox aspect of the game and thousands of players working together to fight/build/run logistics to the frontline is such a great concept to the game. However there are some major flaws that cause the game to have issues:

1- Clans and groups taking control of major resources and hoarding them, or preventing people from using them for other uses causes major issues and limits other players to enjoy the game. This issue actually occurred to me my first week of playing, as I was looking to get some components and after harvesting a good number I had also gotten some broken ones, so I went to the groups facility (Who even put a sign saying will trade broken for fixed comps) and did the math and took them from the PUBLIC facility. As I was leaving I was then shot by the group accusing me of stealing.

2 - Server issues caused quite a number of big issues during the war, causing rollbacks to happen in which concrete bases (which are the toughest available) were reset after being destroyed multiple times for Wardens. Not only that, the Colonials had an issue with their login servers to where they could not get into the game, and losing a zone because the Wardens could join in and push through without any issues.

This may be pure speculation, but based off the things that have happened this war, I can only help get the feeling that there is some biased hotfixes and rollbacks that only seem to favor one side. The wardens had multiple tanks stolen recently after a server crash, and immediately the developers rolled back the server, restricted Colonials to get in the server around that area, and moved the tanks back towards the Wardens. After that a hotfix was made to restrict tanks being taken after a server crash. However as I previously stated the entire Colonial army could not login to a zone to try and push back an assault, but nothing was rolled back in that instance.

Will I still play this game? Of course. Even with the issues that have came up, I still managed to have a fun time doing it all from frontline combat, to logistics, to just harvesting resources.\t\t\t\t\t
