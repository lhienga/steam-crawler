\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's closely related to "Running with Rifles" except with logistics. You can either fight on the front line, or do logistics (get scrap, refine it, and send it to the front). It has a learning curve but not too huge. You cannot run and gun on your own, you need to push a front with a team.

In some ways its unqiue (the logistics) and is fun. IT surely has a different game play than Running with rifles as you can run out of ammo, etc. Everything is made by people in game (defenses, trucks, tanks, guns, ammo, structures, etc). The greatest feature this game has is the battlefront changes and will never be the same. BEcause people can build defense structures differently, it will always be differnet on the same map even. You can fight in the front and then get bored and do logistics. and vice-versa.

In other ways its not fun though. I dont feel rewarded by anything. I dont know if i killed someone or not. There are no stats. IF i build a defense and it gets destroyed, i feel like i wasted my time. Everyone has levels, but it doesnt mean anything except to show other people you know what your doing. I would rather get rewarded by XP immediately or something than just the sheer knowledge that i helped my team because the next time i play might not be the same war even. Because i dont feel rewarded, i have a hard time keeping interesting playing it. I get sick of dying, picking up weapons and ammo (or making ammo if there is none available), then going to the front to eventually die at some point to do it again. Logistics is fun for awhile, but it also gets boring quickly. Another thing i do not like. There is no no single player. Sometimes i dont want to play with other people and play against AI. Running with rifles has a mode to do this while foxhole does not. And the devs have no intention of adding one as of now. There is some AI in foxhole but it is embedded with human players. (the base and what is connected is AI protected). This i think could be modified into a single player campaign that i think would be very fun.

Im on the fence about whether to thumbs up or down to be honest. I probably wont see myself playing this much in the future unless a few friends pick it up that i know, to play with. Otherwise i would rather play running with rifles. I just feel more rewarded when i play that game VS foxhole.\t\t\t\t\t
