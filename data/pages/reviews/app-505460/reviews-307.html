\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was very bad at this game at first. The community is fantastic. I spent an afternoon keeping people alive as a medic and someone offered me an invitation to their regiment (guild) and I've just been having a blast. The real-time nature of this game means there is always a need to move from one front to another. So far in the war I am currently playing in, the factions are evenly populated. I can't really describe the joy I get from listening to all the guys using the game voice chat yelling over artillery and gunfire and grenades. It's by far the greatest multiplayer experience I have EVER had in a video game. EVER. Been playing games for 25+ years and this one is easily in my top 5. For $30 you can't go wrong. I'd go so far as to say this game could easily be worth every penny at $50.

The learning curve of this game is steep and shallow. There is a LOT of stuff you can do to support the war effort. Infantry, healing, running heavy guns up the battle field, logistics (making sure materials go where they are needed), building defenses, etc etc.

The only real complaint I have is that this game has been out since 2016 and I just found out about it with the release of 1.0 and that was because a friend stumbled across it and gave me a heads up.

It's amazing. Would recommend.

EDIT: Voice feature decides when it wants to work. Really inconsistent and seems to cut out at the WORST possible times. Meh.\t\t\t\t\t
