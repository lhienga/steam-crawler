I won't get into it now because it's way too long but, several integral parts of logistics and buildings were cut out because they were "superceded" by new things. Too bad they didn't, they added palettes for transportation of shells and heavy equipment, you'd think that's good but it isn't. To add this they removed the small shipping container, a mobile container that was used to construct player built storage depots (which were integral in areas where you couldn't bulk store supplies). They removed a good feature and replaced it with a worse one. Tier 2 and Tier 3 forward bases were also removed because they got "superceded" even though theres no replacement and railways are near impossible to keep up because of the vulnerability of the t1 encampment. Not going to go any further into the horrific system of upkeeping structures because it is the definition of mental torture.

A little bonus too, saying "FREE HEIMDALL" on their twitch or the reddit often ends in punishment after he had pointed out how flawed their systems were. (lovely guy by the way)

Foxhole as a concept is an incredible game, it allows a player to not feel chained to their role and let them swap from fighting on the frontline as a soldier in a simulated war and switch to a peaceful time scrapping materials within minutes. The world is vibrant and over time you can see the ravages of war by visiting old battlefields that have been ruined by artillery and destruction. Watching player built infrastructure develop overtime to support base logistics, halftracks and eventually tanks and then even bigger tanks. However the 'small shipping container' was removed with 1.0
