This game is based around WO1. i'l just list the pros and cons to make it easier for u to choose if U like it:
Pros:
-GREAT community
-loads of ways to play
-great variaty of weapons and loadouts
-this game is getting updated every month and will still get updates for a couple more years
-many community tools (a site like globalHQ this is a tactical map for planning an operation)
/b Cons:
-some bugs (not gamebreaking or annoying)
-sometimes there are grievers that annoy everyone
-the team picking system isn't always great and sometimes makes it so a war is unfare due to a team with an overwhelming player amount

U can play many roles but the most known ones are:
-Infantry: a normal soldier who fights at the frontline with normal weapons like machine guns and rifles
-Medic: a soldier equipt with medical supplies, u carry a medkit (heal other team mates) and (possibly) a trauma kit (revive team mates)
-Logistics (truck driver):a soldier who drives around a truck (build in storage) or a flatbed (containers or (small) vehicle transport) logistics is the blood of any war, u can also be a tank operator
-Heavy soldier: a HMG (heavy machine gun) carrying soldier.
-Engineer: a soldier who builds up defenses to support other soldiers
-Tank crew: a soldier who is part of a tank crew (tanks aren't driven solo), ur a driver, gunner, commander(maybe something else later on...)
-commander: u lead operations to a good end
-(ship)captain: a soldier who drives a boat around the map
-and many more u can just be whatever u want(/need) to be
Important elements:
-EVERYTHING is done by the player so this means in short u wan't a weapon to shoot well u need to get it driven over to ur location, but to get the weapon u need to get it made in a factory, but to get it made u need materials, but to get materials u need to refine raw materials,...
-The most important structures are stationary but for the rest the building system allows u to place the other stuff everywhere.
-NO PAY TO WIN
-Holiday events(halloween=Dead Harvest)
-Clans, these are not included in game but are communities of players who group together (mostly on discord)
-Many many many items,vehicles,ammo types,weapons,...

My verdict?
Buy this game if u like to play with a community (solo is possible, but not as fun), buy it if u like to play war type games. I realy advise to give this game a try it is just to great and variated to include everything in a short review.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole came out of nowhere for me and filled a niche I was craving.

My lack of play time is due to loss of a PC and moving around a bunch; and as of writing I am downloading on my new PC which is a laptop, I might not be able to run it; but while here did want to leave a good word.

I've had only positive experiences so far, community is good to work with and helpful, everyone enjoys a different task in games and Foxhole capitalizes on this; so really if you just run in shooting and think that is it; you will not have really understood how free for all this title really is.

I enjoy the fluidity; and not being ASSIGNED a role or boxed in after a class selection menu to specific roles.

I can strike out on my own as a sniper, hook up with a squad for a ride across the map; get dumped in the woods alone again to provide cover fire as they roll in from another place; run into exploding everything; have to help reenforce some kind of bunker; hunker down in a machine gun nest; then go back to roaming as a sniper all in one life.

Even something like a transport truck running out of fuel part way adds the kind of unscripted mayhem that really help create situations outside the norm; which is what makes game play fun; too many games now feel like ants swarming hill after hill; while Foxhole is more about the total chaos of war; chains of command break down; machines take time to move about; resources run out; sometimes you just get killed by a grenade bouncing the wrong way. It's fantastic. It's that gritty; insane BS of war that makes this title shine.\t\t\t\t\t
