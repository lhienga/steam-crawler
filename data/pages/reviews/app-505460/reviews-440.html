I am sure you can find something equally or cooler to do.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCheaters, bad (horrible, the worst I´ve seen in a long time) weapon balance, faction spies actively throwing and spending resources and overall ruining everything good and-or fun you might encounter in the game.

I seriously think that only if you are a full casual player or like to play as a Warden (good luck with that) you can give this game a positive feedback.

Gunplay is horrendous but mainly because of ping difference and cheaters - you will get killed by people running and landing every shot while yours will miss in prone position. Also, again, the weapons oh god the weapons.

Developers actively ignore balance issues and spies throwing wars and BLATANTLY DENY that hacks exist - but you won´t be able to play a single day without encountering at least a few.

Everything in the game is a chore. Want to revive someone ? you need a trauma kit. Want to Heal ? that´s another kit and also bandages. It all goes completly to ♥♥♥♥ when you factor things like GAS WARFARE (broken) and WINTER FROST BITE that will make your experience absolutely miserable any time you play it.

For a war simulator, it´s not realistic, just cumbersome. For a shooter, it´s clunky, slow and sloppy. For an rpg there is absolutely nothing to keep you playing aside from gaining rank.

Anyway, I´ve played it for 40 hours with friends, and I had my fun...until I realized how broken the balance in the weapon and vehicles was. Also, if any faction fills a map you won´t be able to join because each province works as a different server-so the game can be a QUEUE simulator often.

Overall a very bad game kept alive by an addicted community. Stay away from this game if you value your time.\t\t\t\t\t
