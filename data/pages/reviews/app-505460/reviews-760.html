\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSometimes I take a look for new projects and I make decisions to support them early access, even if I don't really play them.

I have this game from the beginning and I keep an eye on the development very closely.

This game is definitely one of the so called "hidden gems", no doubt about that for any second. I am proud to be one of the early supporters of this awesome masterwork of art!

From my experience so far, people were extremely helpful, the comradship is real!
I believe you need a trained team to really get the most out of this game, but even the occasional frontline charge or loggi support is very entertaining.

What I would like the devs to gift this epic game and its community it already is:

I personally wish that this game gets a map which emphasizes more on maritime warfare too, by maybe having several strategically important islands, or forcing one side to take the other sides continent island, the main homeland, by either a major coastal invasion or by successive taking of multiple islands to create a supply chain.

It would be a blast to have players manning different water combat vessels like battle ships or destroyers (maybe even submarines) and so on, to guarantee sea supremacy and enable their faction to invade the coast with massive ship artillery support.

The current way it is, there are basic ships to have simple coastal artillery support and some very repetitive sea combat, due to the lack of variety.

The water plane could be another field to make further expansion and actually increase the relevance of dominating the sea, rather than focusing on dominating the ground only like it feels for now.

Even oil rigs or things like small resource islands might be a reason to want to get the advantage over the sea too, rather than just clustering the coasts with artillery defense.\t\t\t\t\t
