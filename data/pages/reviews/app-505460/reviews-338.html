\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you’re a strategy type who likes risk and axis and Allie’s type board games, this is your drug. However with any drug there’s side effects like lack of a life outside the game. It will suck you in and spit you out if you’re not careful. I’m the latter so I can’t recommend this game. Everything is made by players, all the people you fight are real people, and those that fight along side you are also real people. The game has a lot of exploits the other team will always use and there’s lots of people who will rage quit mid war if things are going badly for your team leaving you to fill the gap of their absence and it’ll piss you off too. The game isn’t balanced between both sides. I wish they’d just give everyone the same weapons and tech and let players decide which tech to get. That way people can’t complain that one side has the better stuff. If everyone has the same stuff it’s all even. If the devs would flip the map around as well where one team is north and the other south, then flip the other way every few wars instead of trying to force people to play the same sides all the time and have to fight the same uphill battles or the same flatlands or the same mountain roads… it might be a far more interesting and even game. Plus if they could get rid of the ability to alt and play on both sides… it might actually be a good game. You can’t please everyone, but in all honesty the lack of direct interaction the devs have with the community now and their inability to actually fix some of the broken mechanics that have been going on for many wars now is unreal. They’re talking about releasing version 1.0 soon, but this game is far from ready in its current state.\t\t\t\t\t
