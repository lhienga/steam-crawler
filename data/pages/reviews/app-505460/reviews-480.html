\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnique game.

Description:
Two factions (blue and green) fights on the big map for dominance.


Gameplay:
Battle units:
Frontline, Partisans, Anti-partisans.
Divided between Infantry, Artillery, Tank units and some limited Naval forces.

Logistic units:
Mining resources, war production, transport and technological advancement.
(almost everything used in-game needs to be produced and transported)

Builders:
Responsible for building, upgrading and repairing trench fortifications. That helps in times when the game got a low population (war is a 24/7 job). Bases can be manned by Artificial Intelligence units (aka pillboxes shooting on their own).

The graphic is in a fancy 3D isometric view. Quite charming design (with architecture from the European 1920-1950 era). I don't have any problem running the game on AMD 5600X/ RTX2060.

Positives:

Sandbox game
Complexity (Logistic, Production, Battles)
The player can see the developers passion for the game.
Non-toxic community - Shard 2

Negatives:

Time-consuming
Slow-paced
Currently faction disbalance on Shard 2
(I suggest not to start on Shard 2. and go directly to Shard 1)
Every technical problem can hit your regiment/faction harder than in other games.

Conclusion:
I Strongly recommend try this game. Its catchy concept.
