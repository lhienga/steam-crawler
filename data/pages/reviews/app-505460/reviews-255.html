It feels like you're working a second job instead of playing a game. You have to wait hours and hours for things to be made, you have to then load those things into stock piles or material storage which literally take half an hour plus depending on the quantity of items you have. then to unload those items and put them on crates you have to wait an other half an hour plus. The games too serious, too complex and honestly not fun at all.

What would be best for this game and make players stay is adding less materials, speeding up construction/building time, adding an auto loader to crates so they instantly have items instead of getting bored waiting hours and hours . Also making it so that vehicles don't require rare materials to build them.

You need to make the game actually fun to attract players because so far as developers you're pushing your only audience away

I don't recommend this.\t\t\t\t\t
