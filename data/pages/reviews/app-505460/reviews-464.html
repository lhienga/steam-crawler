Sadly i can't recomend it on its current form, for all the highs the game has to deliver they can't make for the lows it constantly pushes to you.
-The game excels at having a full logistic line and backline network to keep things varied but all the backline jobs are plagued with bugs and QoL issues that just deter almost anyone but the people near the masochistic threshold
-The game offers a plethora of guns and vehicles to fullfil all sorts of roles but then pushes a tech tree of sorts that leaves most of them to stay on a sideline never to see actual use when they migh've been relevant
-That same tech is a whole debacle of its own and to sumarise, its counter-intuitive and unfun, rewards people exploiting game mechanic and still favors the side with more people into a snowball for which the majority of the players have no control or desire to have it

The game is fantastic and delivers hours upon hours of guaranteed fun, but in its current state i can't recomend it as once you get better aquainted with the game itself you'll see all the flaws that sap the fun out of it and make for a hell of a burnout in record time\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI keep thinking about playing more, and there are two things keeping me away.

The first is that the battles can be a ton of fun, but most of them are just sitting at a bridge shooting at anything that moves on the other side of the river and dying constantly. The bridges in this game end up being where the most fighting happens and those fights are boring as hell.

The second reason is that the people who play this game can be incredibly toxic elitists. The first of these I ran into was a high ranking guy who ran up to a squad I was part of as we were organizing to get behind the enemy and potentially break a major stalemate. He wanted to have us do something but we already had a leader and a plan so we told him we were busy. Then he followed us as we attacked, and as we made contact he shot half the squad in the back because we didn't do what he said. The last toxic player I ran into was using region chat to tell the entire map that anyone who didn't have 200 hours in the game shouldn't even think about crewing tanks. Like, that's the level of braindead elitism that some of these people operate at. There isn't a mechanic in the game that can't be explained in five minutes, yet there are people gatekeeping everything from using vehicles to what guns to use.

It could become a pretty awesome game, but not without some serious moderation and some simple design changes.


EDIT: If you're thinking about playing logistics instead, the state of logi right now is so boring and jank that there's a literal cross faction logi strike going on right now because the devs have ignored the community for so long.\t\t\t\t\t
