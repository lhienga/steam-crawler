\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDay 1: A little overwhelming, end up helping some guys gather scrap to make weapons and ammo to go to the front lines. End up getting in a truck and delivering said stuff under fire to a frontier base just inside an enemy province.

Day 2: Defending a Safe house in a town, street to street fighting and its pretty hectic. A guy is gathering troops to go behind enemy lines to help cut off their supply lines, end up blowing up about 5 enemy trucks full of supplies before the damn wardens send tanks after us.

Day 3: Wardens are pushing into Scurvyshire, see a guy making a new bunker system to spawn from to the flank of the wardens, another dude is here and we set up in foxholes with LMG's and defend the guy making the base, its a tense hour or so but it gets on its feet. 3 hours later we have pushed the wardens back to Endless shore. Feels good man.

Day 4: Spend the day scrapping and doing logistics, delivering to the bases that I think need it most. Though not in direct action it feels extremely rewarding.

All in all this game is super fun to play, if a little infuriating at times as you cant throw in a heroic performance and wipe the enemy team and save the day. Its a team game and you need to work with every single person you play with to ensure victory. Help the guys who know less than you and listen to the guys who know more than you, they generally just want to help out the new players. Get past the bewilderment of the first few hours learning the ropes and you'll be sinking your free time into this, worth every penny.
