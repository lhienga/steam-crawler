\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dont write many reviews unless its something I think is particularly well done and deserves said praise. That being said this game is so unique and fun I had to go out of my way and say something. I've heard people complain about the possability of a volatile public making the game experience less than desirable. I personally havent experienced anything of the sort and rather have always experienced other people who dedicated to capping a certain town or building a howitzer to defend a push. It's truly a unique experience and trying to compare it to something else is difficult to do.

Graphics arent anything to cheer for but they arent bad. I mean what do you expect with a birds eye view.
The game is a MMO and the only gripe I have (mind you this doesnt matter in the end) is before you go through your bootcamp tutorial to get the gist of the game and the different jobs you can do (mind you you dont sign up for a class as you would in other games, if you want to be a medic, go get some bandages and head to the front etc) But you cannot change sides once you make your pick.
So you have two to choose from, and they use their own made up countries but its pretty much Nazis vs Americans. And once you choose your side, thats it. Because it's considered a war thats unending (presumably) you cannot switch sides. I would like to see how the other side lives.
But because the game is so much fun, who really cares. I really hope they expand on this idea though. They have such a cool idea and the possabilities are endless for such a cool war game. Imagine a better UI for platoons and dishing small missions out to capture a city. I only hope the dev doesnt cash the check and just do bug fixes and wait to cash out for a foxhole 2. :/\t\t\t\t\t
