Well I hope this helps


As you probably know this game is a mass multiplayer sandbox “role-playing” game or in another words.


You play as a soldier and you have the responsibility of making yourself useful.


What I mean by this?


There aren’t any objectives that the game sets for you other than win the war. You are the one that must choose what to do and has a newcomer it can be very daunting at first what the **** you will do and more importantly what there is to do.


Of course when you log in for the first time there will be shown to you a training course were you will learn the basics of the game like gathering refining and converting raw resources to material that can be consumed by the war machine and how to use guns.

But like everything he does not show you all the possibilities, now that we talk about possibilities you can play this game solo but as always playing with even a friend is way better. Of course you can always rely on randoms but there will be always that missing piece called coordination.

If you are one of the bunch that don't have any friends no problem. Role-play! You might have the occasional neighbour asking if everything is ok but that is a little sacrifice for your social anxiety. In time you will be able to get a group and then do some **** together. Like being useful in a tank.

In time you will discover what the game is about and then you can ***** about a faction being favoured by the devs or grenade spam or you can just log in and enjoy the game.

As I conclude this review the things that I really want any newcomer to know is these 5 points.


Don't be afraid to ask does not matter if is for work or a simple question of what is this or for what is for most of the community will be happy to answer and guide you.

Variety is the spice of life so don’t always do the same things over and over again, I don't care if you think logi is too lame, try everything.

It's normal to take a break; since I started playing since open beta I have made a few breaks and every time I came back the game was even better.

Role-play! That's is the way I play and it’s by far the best, it's from there that I meet some memorable people and made some of my most memorable moments on foxhole.

And finally don't be mean to your team mates, like stealing vehicles, blowing defences without a clear reason etc etc ... Just don't be a ♥♥♥♥
