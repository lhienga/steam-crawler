\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSome years ago the game used to be excelent. No complicated logistic system, some sort of balance etc. Now it's all about one faction beating another. There is no balance left. One faction gets a super-duper tank whilst the other gets a thing called a siege tank, which is completely useless though is believed to be somewhat equal to the other faction's tank when it comes to balance (not even close lol) The thing is, developers don't really think about balancing the game, it gets worse every time, as people don't want to waste hours producing things and farming. There are some people who do love that, though I doubt they are that much into waiting for hours just to let other people play and waste it all in several minutes. The problem of balancing is it's assymetry as factions now don't get the equal vehicles as they used to, now they get completely different ones which are not even close to be balanced. E.g. one faction has an Anti-Tank truck which is to some extent may be even better than the real tank of the other faction. And the problem is, while that one faction has all these vehicles, the other has no "deadly weapon" except for a cool battle tank which is actually very similar to the basic light tank but with more armour (which is not enough after the release of the "super tanks" mentioned earlier and the AT trucks), more damage and a machine gun on it. Speaking of balance in general, one faction gets all the high grounds in the game and it seems to me, that developers just love one faction and actually develop it and have the other one just to be and it also looks like the models created for the devoided of love faction were also made out of the idea that very must be something done.

This might seem like a cry of a baby. But I am actually bored. Balance has been a major problem for a period of time and it was never solved. That is also my personal opinion on the things going on with this game and it's okay that you might have a different one. Though it is not okay to insult other people for having an opinion and I really hope that we are all civilized people here and can solve our problems in a civilized way.

All in all, that is what I realized after quite a time in the game and I really don't think I am going to play it any soon again untill they do something about balance, because there is a lot to do\t\t\t\t\t

Edit: Edit:

After a Year ive come back and many of the issues have been fixed and addressed. No more tech in-fighting and it seems that the Devs are finally listening to what the community has to say. Their are still many issues with this game but it has come leaps and bounds from when i originally made this review. I have changed my review from not recommended to recommended because the Devs have consistently updated their game with good updates, aswell as bug fixes and changes that have been requested by the community. They plan to keep making updates to this game and therefore I think its worth the purchase.

I LOVE THIS GAME. Needs a Better Player Reporting System. and Vehicles should have similar Variants for each factions. Game also needs a Tutorial for New players. Not always Noob Friendly

Edit:

This game will never fix the problems it needs to address, The devs have no idea how to fix their game with clear bias towards one side. Ive wanted to hold off changing my review to this game for as long as possible but update after update with the same massive glaring issues that dont get addressed, ive come to realize that Balance being the worst part of the game. The maps favor one side, the vehicles favor one side, the player base favors one side. You can still have a great time in this game and meet some pretty awesome people. After so long the devs dont have any excuse why these bugs that have been in forever dont get fixed. The Devs joke about being called bias towards one side or another claiming to be non-biased but they don't listen to the large number of complaints and feedback about integral parts of their game. They are afraid to equip both factions with the same weapon or type of vehicle because of "asymmetry". They could easily add 2 different types of the same vehicle to both factions that are special to each faction but still have the balance of having both sides having a variant of the same vehicle. MG Jeep, AT Jeep, Standard Jeep, etc.. They also make changes that directly impacts gameplay based on "Lore" aka most Colonial Vehicles are open topped but open top has no advantages at the time of writing this.

Overall: I want to love this game and recommend it, but i cant do that anymore. i tried to play thru the issues but its just stressful and not really fun anymore. I've met some awesome people during my time on Foxhole, the community is Great and very supportive. Its also a very competitive Collies Vs Wardens which i enjoyed, but some people take it way to seriously. This game will always have a place in my Heart, and i truly want this game to excel, but until the Dev's start addressing the bigger issues i have every single time i play i wont play and cant recommend it anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
