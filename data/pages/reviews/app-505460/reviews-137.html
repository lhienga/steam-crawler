\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust starting playing this game and it is really fun! The epic skirmishes immerse you in intense battle as the fight between both sides makes you feel the high stakes of war. The world is massive and evolves as players build and defend bases, set up supply chains, and improve technology. The community is very helpful to new players and everyone works together to build, supply, and fight. There is so much to learn but even as a new player, I felt like a valuable asset to the team by helping dig trenches, build defences, or fight the enemy.

My first time playing as a Warden, we had just captured a city and were pushing the enemy out so we could build a better defence. We would skirmish with the enemy and push them back into their territory only to have them gain back the land we took. Then we would push them back again, and they would push us back. It was a constant game of tug-o-war, but slowly we were able to push back long enough to build a defensive base. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a game like no other. It has its ups and downs with game balance and the developers building out the game following what is referred to as "the vision" which does not always match up to what the community wants, but generally it offers a pretty rewarding experience for players that enjoy the type of gameplay that Foxhole has to offer. The asymmetrical equipment for each faction makes for interesting engagements, and allows a lot of room for countering enemy attacks and being creative. The game's art style is really cool, and really allows you to be immersed into each faction's lore. Community engagement is a must in this game, and while there are some toxic people like in any other MMO most other players are very friendly and are there to have fun.

There really is a lot that could be said about Foxhole. I would recommend checking out some videos on YouTube before deciding on a purchase. The game is not for everyone, but if you enjoy the type of gameplay that is available there is nothing else like it.\t\t\t\t\t
