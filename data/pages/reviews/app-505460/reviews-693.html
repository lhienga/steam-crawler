\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played a lot of games in my time. Only 30 mins in and I can already tell this game is needlessly time consuming, tedious, slow paced, clunky, and probably isn't going to be terribly fun. The tutorial, or "training grounds" already had me cringing. Oof. The problem is that everything in this game is just so ridiculously unintuitive (especially for new players) with things like the menus and player inventory, the HUD elements and their positioning on screen. I can't even begin to explain the things wrong with this game, otherwise this review would turn into an essay. The tutorial (training grounds) alone would probably be an essay of all the things wrong with it.

The short and simple of it is the idea behind this game is neat and has a lot of potential, but it needs a big re-thinking, which probably isn't going to happen. There is just so much excess "fat" that needs to be cut and it just really needs to be simplified and maybe even a bit less teamwork oriented than it currently is so the individual player can actually have fun. This game's mechanics also need to be more intuitive and feel natural and smooth and not like you're neck deep in the mud whenever you want to do one simple thing.\t\t\t\t\t
