\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole has come a long way since initially launching on steam. I've played on and off since it came out and while there is still a lot that I'd love to see in the game, it's definitely a unique kind of game that allows for people to embrace many different kinds of gameplay. If you're a logistics focused player, you can get materials to make weapons, tanks, ammo, medical supplies etc and if you want a top down style shooter, you can run to the front lines and hop on a gun placement or sneak up to an enemy camp at night and lob grenades to cause chaos. If you don't have a particular draw, but you like to just float around and be helpful where you can, this might be the best game for that mindset. You can just ask people what they need and go help them. It's totally up to you and I love that it has that level of freedom.

**Please be careful about friendly fire in this game though, it's scarily easy to take out your own teammates by accident either by running them over in a vehicle or shooting blindly.**\t\t\t\t\t
