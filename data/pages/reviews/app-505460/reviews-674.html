\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLTR: don't buy it! Even if you have it for free: don't run it! Learning the game's basics (movement, aiming, gearing) takes some time, then you just realize what am I talking about below.

The game is becoming an unbalanced mess.
- Technology takes ages, and sometimes one side get's important thing way earlier than the other.
- Hit-reg is a joke. Movement prediction is also a mess. (Look up for Foxhole shadowdance).
- Countless _confirmed_ exploit cases on both side.
- Terrain collider can give you a headache.
- Terrain height can give you a stroke. Somebody on top of the mountain (they glitched up there, no worries) can kill you easily.
- The winter aspect gave a huge dept to the game: in the snowstorm you can freez to death in less than 3 min. While also moving slow. Vechicles broke down because of it. Sometimes you don't see the trenches, because of the rendering is a mess.
- Most of the players plays in clans, and if you open and fill their trucks, you can't take the stuff out. They barely ever communicate to you. Mostly about your _grave_ mistakes, or for your bino.
- "Tank battles" like... I have no words. It's just silly. Have a look on YT.
- Everybody is on the edge. The first slightest mistake on your side, like driving on the Right side of the road, resulted in yelling, and swearing.
- Everything takes ages: if you want to arm your soldier with a gun, ammo (3), a granade, it's about 40 second. And you do nothing, but clicking on UI buttons, and watching the timer. You can die in 2 shots, sometimes 1, then taking a 20-40 sec nap (based on unknown factors) then rearm, run to fight zone... In a good scenario you can have 3-4 good shoot in every 3 minute. Endless fun! And please don't stand in the door while arming!
- The game tells you when and how to play it: you don't like snow, but the normal regions has 8-10 person queue (which also screws up the logistics, which is a Huge deal btw) or fight in the snow anyway. Which is fun! For 2 min then freez to death. Or you can join the fight on the other day, but it's resistance (for 1-2 real day) phase, so even less people care about the game, and just mess around.
- The new shader system sometimes "forget" to render the barbed wire. Another great opportunity for a stroke!
- Some people exploit the hell out of the game. Trading advanced tech stuff with alt characters, tanks, or anything with their main character (on the opposing side).
- Most of the time I feel like we just run head on to brick walls, and nobody communicating clearly. Sometimes we have an "earrape" radio guy, who is runing around. Because it's funny! Takes a minute to find him/her in the playerlist.
- Idiot people (or just youtubers, who knows) waste your factions stuffs, because it's funny. And also since they didn't had to do _anything_ for it.
- Building is rigged, slow, booring. It's holds a realy great promise. Try to do it for more than 20 min.

The entire game design is so rigged and slow, I realy can't explain it. You try to do anything "outside of the box", like try to do some partisan stuff, but you have 1 life, or you have to re-travel for _minutes_ to do something. AI buildings are so random, and dumb! Some _fanatic_ people chees the heck out of the game, and waits you on the spot, where literally nobody else, and they have nothing to do!

The game is much better than use to be. In terms of fire weapon variations, and the driving is realy nice. But everything is working against you, and to you from having fun! Slow, booring tasks, minimal benefits (if any). No sense of progression for the player. Maybe if you are in a bigger clan...
Waste of time. Realy.\t\t\t\t\t
