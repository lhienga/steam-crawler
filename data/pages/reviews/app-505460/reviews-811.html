\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to have a positive review of this game. The developers communicated with their community, they gave biweekly dev streams, and any problems we would have with the game would eventually be fixed.

However near the end of early access these positives became rarer, dev's communicated with us less, problems started to escalate more and more, and our feedback was starting to feel more ignored.

I decided to give the dev's until their full release out of early access before giving my new opinion, because I still had faith that the dev's would eventually return to how great they were in the past. But the inferno update (1.0 full release) finally arrived and not only were the mass of problems still around, the update broke the game in several different ways.

Issue's that we have had for the longest time have been ignored, and the player base is outright sick of it. The game is currently plagued with cheaters that buy new accounts just to sabotage the other team by making the public new player experience miserable. The game is littered with bugs and lacks any form of quality of life while having the most grindy mechanics that don't respect players time in the slightest.

Understand, that as a new player you will more than likely have a great time playing, but it will be all dependant on other players throwing hours and hours of their life away working with the games horrendously grindy mechanics just to give you that experience, while also having to constantly deal with these cheating players who sabotage all of their generousity just to either troll or help their "real team" win.

Maybe someday this game will be great again, there is a large amount of potential, but the dev's need to actually start listening to the playerbase again. We don't want new content, we want the content that exists to not feel like torture. We want quality of life and ways to protect ourselves from griefing. And we want the game to not feel like playing 12+ hours a day is a requirement to get anything done.

Until then, give this game some time before joining in, show the dev's that several new players would rather wait at the gate for quality than rush in immediately. Maybe your restraint to try this game out could be the incentive for the dev's to finally fix the problems the game faces. We want your experience to be as great as ours was, but we can't promise you'll have that experience if you join now.\t\t\t\t\t
