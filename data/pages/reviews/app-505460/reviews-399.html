\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is NOTHING like Foxhole. If you read the reviews, I cannot find a single negative review that's under 40 hours of play. I mean, that's a pretty good amount of gameplay for something people say they hate. One guy has 800+ hours and hates it?!

Anyway, the developers are very dogmatic about what they want this game to be. Players are... spicy about what they think they want this game to be. But in reality, I think the devs are doing just fine with their work and roadmaps. The complaints people have about this game are very much about the sort of gameplay aspects that you wouldn't even notice until you were an advanced level player.

That said, this game is awesome. Players run the whole war, top to bottom, building bullets, bunkers, and boomsticks. It's a terrific actual simulation of real war, and the fact that the two factions are not the Germans and the Americans makes it that much better and freer to be linked to what's fun rather than what's historic.

This game is quite amazing. it might not be everyone's cup of tea, but if it sounds like something you'd be into, and you like open voice chat with people from all over the world, this is the game for you. Get it and play it.

I will comment that my only complaint is that recent patches have made it crash on my machine, but I expect a patch to fix these issues. The developers are very active. I do wish they'd sell cosmetics to enhance their income so they could hire some more bodies, maybe. Maybe hire a community manager?\t\t\t\t\t
