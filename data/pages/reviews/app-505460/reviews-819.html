PVP doesnt even win you the game, you have to PVE and night cap to actually gain ground effectively so essentially you can only win when everybody is offline.

Logistics.This game might have some of the worst grinding issues ever in a game, to actually help in the war effort your gonna have to do logistics for 8hrs straight , the process of making supplies is very tedious and painstakingly slow, you hold lmb for 20 minutes to get a raw material then drive another 2 minutes to the refinery then wait another 2-5 hours for your resources to be refined then drive all the way to the factory and depending where you are that could be another 10 minutes then if your lucky the factory might have no ques so you only have to wait another 30 minutes for you to create all your supplies.Then repeat this another 10 times to actually contribute to the war effort.
