\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWould love to recommend this game to other but as it is now i cant do that.

Main reasons for that is the community and unwillingness of the devs to see whats is happening. The basic explanation of this is the game is designed for the team in a two sided war to work, gather, research and produce together for the war effort. Which on paper sounds like a good idea but its far from that. What ive seen in game is people on your side will turn on each other to steal and murder anyone for what they want. Got a good amount of resources, be prepared for someone to steal them. Have a nice tank, watch as someone on your side breaks the lock and steals it. Want to stop them, be prepared to be banned for team killing to stop the theft.

Basically the devs think the community works together and leave everything up to the "community" to behave and not basically raid their own allies. But that dosent happen, as you cant lock or store anything at your player built base that someone cant just come and take. And it dosent help many people have "Alt" accounts on the opposite team, and will eaither spy, steal, or scout for the other side.

In my 24 hours in game ive been killed by more allies then hostiles.This was becasue me and my friends where trying to build a base and get a long range artillery site setup. But a large logistics group wanted our resources. So the stole, attacked up, and destroyed parts of our base constantly. But you cant shoot back, because if you do and happen to kill one of them, their whole group reports you for team killing to have you banned just so they can steal your stuff. The Game really dosent let you and your friends have anything private owned, and what little it does is very small and even then easily stolen from.

This is the kinda toxicity and clan bullying, control that is ruining the game. Basically its either fall in line, bend over, and let them have their way with you, or be banned.

With only being able to report 3 people a day it nearly impossible to stop the greifers, especially since it take multiple reports on the same person to get anything done, and those that just want to steal and break what you have. And lets not mention the large 'Clans" of player who abuse the report system to get people they don't like banned.

Maybe in the future this game will get better but as of now i would stay away from it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of those games that I want to like but so far I don't. Biggest gripe? The UI. It's atrocious. I don't recall hating a UI this badly until going back and trying to play old 1990s RTS games where every single action required a different key on the keyboard. This is a gigantic exercise in tedium.

Spawn in. Press E to open base inventory. Transfer hammer, rifle, and ammo to inventory. Close menu. Press TAB to open personal inventory and backpack, because letting you see your inventory at the same time as the base inventory would be too easy, you know, like every other survival crafting game ever. Transfer rifle from personal backpack to personal inventory. Close menu. Now press 1 to have your character choose the rifle that's in your personal inventory to actually equip the rifle. Now after 3 seconds your rifle is equipped. Now press R to load your empty rifle. Congrats, 5 minutes later you are now ready to leave the base and go somewhere else to play the game. This is what you do when trying to fight on the front lines, EVERY TIME YOU DIE.

Who designed this UI? Don't even get me started on trying to build stuff. Take the above formula and multiply it times 4 or 5 more tiers of ridiculous menus of swapping hammers and shovels in and out of your backpack to your personal inventory, closing the menus, then pressing 1 to actually hold the item, then any combination of E, F, B, and mouse clicks just to build a structure skeleton. Now go grab materials from a truck and put them in your personal inventory which is now encumbered, and lug the crap back to the skeleton structure, which now disappeared because no one started putting materials into it yet...

Could you please add like 27 more steps just to do one single thing? I know you could squeeze at least 15 or 16 more in there jfc.\t\t\t\t\t
