\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a game that relies upon player-player interaction as everything you see or do is player made and/or built with extensive time sunk. With each update, more and more features are added that simply do not value the aforementioned players time.

The most recent update added facilities, an unique design, but with VERY poor implementation. For a game that has been years in the making they tested the game-breaking facilities (as shown by the HUGE dip in players in the past handful of months) in only a couple weeks on the dev branch. Facilities have added a monotony of maintaining buildings that place needed items such as upgraded tanks, artillery, and other actual fun items like Battle Tanks or Super Heavy Tanks to where only a small portion of the player base will actually enjoy them.

For $30, this game will provide about 100 hours of actual fun gameplay if you run with a squad of friends or in-game regiments. After the 100 hours? You'll be lost in the litany of poorly designed bases costing fronts, game-breaking bugs such as queue bugs that wipe entire regions, and the toxic cesspool that is the community surrounding this game. There is a reason why the game was released to 1.0 in October 2022 with 10k players and with only one-third or one-fourth of those players still playing.

Recommendation: buy this on a sale with friends and enjoy the time you have, but don't sink your teeth in because the devs do not care about the veteran players.\t\t\t\t\t
