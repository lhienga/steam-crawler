\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has consumed alot of hours from me, i've been playing since there was only 9 maps in a 3x3 space where you had to physically push a push-machinegun from the rear inorder to get it to the front, i was there when trenches were first added and made hell trenches advancing through roads towards enemy town halls to rifle fire the building to the ground, i've fought hopeless wars inorder to gain War Objectives; achievements of having held sweet bugger all objective towns but still survived several days inorder to gain bragging rights even when it all comes crumbling down.

Single player games have nothing on the high of socially interacting upon the battlefield with your friends to win a month long fight over digital space, i've spent hours in the wee morning discussing philosophy with blokes while manning a post, digging trenches, or moving supplies back and forth moving the industry along, i've joined clans for logistical movements and streamers zerg rush clans to support pushes on the fringes of fronts only to have the logi be useless in the end or the territory gained be lost in the ensuing hours because everybody eventually had to log off to sleep.

Its a great game, but the communications are ever failing; Voice Chat errors that prevent you from speaking or being heard happen without announcement and are only revealed when you go into options.
There used to be Inter Team Communication; you would be able to talk locally to your enemies as you could to any of your teammates, mocking them or sweet talking them into not throwing a grenade into your trench... This is replaced by text only excused that the network couldn't handle it when in actuality it was probably because you could abuse enemy players or get them into trouble with their government (BingChilling). While I did abuse this system (I blew into my mic when near enemy players to annoy them into trying to hunt me down) its no excuse when we have auto recording devices to account for any law breaking that may occur between people playing video games on the internet.

Here comes the last bit of criticism that brought me to writing a review upon request by my friends who have been enjoying this game for months if not years; Cheating!

Cheating in some cultures is perfectly permissible against others if they leave themselves open; sitting invisible in a bush while enemy soldiers pass by inorder to murder their medics, standing atop a cliff where the enemy cannot easily get at you or even aim at you has been an age old method of securing some particular fronts (Jade Cove has a stupidly defensible cliff north of it for the wardens to hold off colonial attacks), creating terribly indefensible trenches infront of your bunker base that your opponent will have to hop over several times in awkward manners only to be gunned down by your automated machine-gun bunkers is terribly time consuming to deal with or must leave that area ignored as you seek another front to push.

This is unsportsmanlike like behaviour that can hold the enemy off the area long enough for them to quit the fight in exhaustion or bring material up to deal with; bush tactics can be countered by putting Watch Towers that act as stationary UAVs to let those with the equipment know about them & also is given for a second upon accessing spawn points so it doesn't allow for a long hold off point, Cliffs can be mortared with shrapnel shells as there is no cover atop those forsaken crags and then captured in the night to turn the high ground against your enemy, stifling trenches can be destroyed the same as any defenses by utilizing explosives to wear them down as the most frontline trenches cannot be effectively repaired without exposing engineers to great danger and risk losing all their supplies.

And here's the rub with people cheating using Alt Accounts; Every tactic can be countered, motorized anti tank guns are near on defenseless against infantry with an anti structure explosive, the most robust and perfectly placed bunkerbase can be wiped out by dedicated artillery crews manning entrenched guns replacing their counter batteried crews via nearby dedicated garrison base, bridge battles are over when a location behind them is conquered leaving the dedicated defenses facing the wrong direction.

A person on your team driving a flatbed with 60 stacks of basic materials into the ocean is uncounterable and unnoticed, A person on your team upgrading your bunker base with weakening spots (a tier one trench added and destroyed removes the entire structure) or making generator rooms which lower the integrity of the entire structure is uncounterable, A person waiting behind the front with a vehicle and coordinating with the opposite team to rush his vehicle forward and trap an allied tank into being destroyed is uncounterable.
All uncounterable options are banworthy, but so what. You win, your team gets the upperhand and demoralizes the enemy team while morally supporting your own team.

Rust has this problem, they solve it by Banning The Entire Clan related to the activity thus moralizing those people who have been wronged in a uncounterable manner. We don't get any such satisfaction in this game.
What can be done; vote with your reviews, hold them against the developers, they are public servants accountable to their player base.

And unlike in real life where nothing good happens for the people; if this ends up ruining the game the developers who failed their player base get to hang the shame of it around their necks to whatever job they go to next.

Their Move.\t\t\t\t\t
