\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst 2 hours: No idea how to fight, get shot and naded constantly while missing at point blank.

4 more hours: Start doing logi runs of guns ammo and shirts, join the front line, learn the joy of screaming MEDIC!

20 hours: Fold laundry while crafting mortars for half an hour, drive to the front line and start making people fly, still screaming MEDIC!

This is a game for people who enjoy gameplay over rewards. Progress is slow and collective. Community is incredible and hilarious.

Tip for new players: Make it a routine to drive a truckload of supplies to the front line every time you deploy and you will watch your team succeed.\t\t\t\t\t
