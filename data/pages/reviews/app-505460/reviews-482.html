\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are so many positives about this game, but in my experience the level of toxicity far out ways any good. If you want a game to play with the finer mechanic of war, this is it. The need for combined arms and the way each is relevant to a successful operation makes it very enjoyable to play. If you want a first person shooter and just want to blow sht up, this isn't for you. The toxicity comes into play when you get involved with some of the most technical parts of the game, building and logistics. The game rewards logistics to the point where people don't care about doing it right they just want to do it and get the rewards. So you end up getting supplied with worthless sht. Building, there is no incentive to build unless you are a masochist. It can be appealing and even fun, but on a daily basis you have to deal with other players coming into your master plan and forcing themselves in it. When I say forcing, I mean they want to add things in it and no amount of polite talking, cussing, or explaining will persuade them differently. You need to have an overall master plan for the defense of a town for a seamless defense. However, someone wants to build in the middle because they are going to single handily save the faction with what they are building, there is nothing you can do about it. You also can't compensate for it either usually. In the end, you are better off being one of the many front line players who is only there for the mere pleasure of playing a war game and is satisfied with that regardless of the repetitive, at times pointless, nature of their effort.\t\t\t\t\t
