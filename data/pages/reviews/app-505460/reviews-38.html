\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played quite a bit of foxhole.

Don't be fooled by any cool posts or videos you'll see of this game. That kind of interesting, video worthy stuff almost never happens.

You can join a regiment sure, but nothing will happen. Most of the time even concentrated team efforts are just like solo ones. Either way you'll just end up ramming your head into a wall and the wall wins 95% of the time.

And the other 5% where you beat the wall? Nothing. Most of that time, the enemy comes by when you're not around and undoes everything. None of it mattered.

Unless you are willing to sink huge chunks of your limited time on earth into this game, and then also manage to team up with the right people by chance

You will never do anything of note.

Tank crew or ship crew? Forget it. Everything but the smallest boats and tanks is ridiculously expensive and takes many real hours of waiting and grinding to get. The only people that can ever afford to do that are the large regiments who will rarely use their toys anyway because they're so expensive that they can't afford to lose them.

Not to mention, a lot of interesting vehicles or resources are blocked behind facilities. Which suck.

There are facilities that offer public resources out and are willing to help you with what you want. Can you find them though? There's literally no way to know if a facility is public, what it does, or who runs it unless you happen to walk into it and find someone in there. Most of the time, their someone in a regiment who treats all randoms with suspicion and will try their best to get you out of there.

The communication tools are maybe one step above barebones. You have map posts which easily disappear or a world chat that offers nothing of value 99% of the time. That's it.

The core gameplay is terrible, and is missing so many quality of life features that the community has been asking for for years.

The worst part of it all is the developers. These are some of the most out of touch, slow paced, and uncommunicative developers for any game I have ever seen. Developer interaction with the community is almost zero. Developers listening to feedback from veteran players who've played the game for years? Also zero.

They come out of complete silence after several months, announce some content that they've put a miniscule amount of thought into, then go back to ignoring everyone and every thing. The content is admittedly impressive and looks shiny and interesting when they reveal it. But in practice ends up being locked away from the vast majority of people and is often even grindier than the last peace of poorly thought out content they made. They will add more and more content that is completely unfun, poorly thought out, and broken instead of trying to polish what there is.

You will get no quality of life features, the enjoyment of this game is all down to the players and how willing they are to deal with your early game questions. The tutorial of the game is downright awful, and hasn't been touched in the slightest in years. It's literally missing content from several major updates.

I desperately want to like foxhole, hence my playtime, but the game is missing so many tools, so much polish, and so many little fixes or tweaks that it just feels terrible to play.

Please do not buy this game. The developers have only made it worse overtime and refuse to listen, interact, or even acknowledge the complaints that much of the community has.\t\t\t\t\t
