\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPersonally, I can acknowledge this is a really cool game. The premise is unique, the scale of what is happening is staggering, and the degree of coordination is impressive.

That being said, at no point during this game was I engaged with the game mechanically. Playing it never felt fun. I sat under artillery for a hour straight, firing thousands of rounds at wave after wave of infantry. It was cool knowing every shell and round flying through the air was made by people and I found the situation to be interesting and memorable. However, I did not find the gameplay of fighting fun, I was never immersed. I have some minor complaints about some design choices, for example the tanks feel more like RC cars than a Tank. Though those are minor.

Overall, this is a good game but if you are expecting to really get engaged and immersed in the fighting, it may fall short. Hell let loose and planetside were my gateway drugs to this game and if youre coming from a similar background, be a bit more cautious than I was.\t\t\t\t\t
