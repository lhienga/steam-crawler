\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me preface this review by saying I like the game, quite a bit actually. I'll boot it up every now and then and have a blast, but I can't recommend anyone buy the game for a couple very specific reasons.

Firstly, the staff team. They're incredibly abusive of their power, and pick and choose who they punish for ingame actions such as griefing, and trolling. It's very easy to damage your team, by design. You need to be smart with your grenade throws, and where your shots are landing, but this means that it's very easy to be a ♥♥♥♥ as well.

If you have tenure, and are a "Veteran" of the game, you can do pretty much whatever you want. Very recently a 'vet' had used an alt to grief other players, when other members of the community demanded on reddit and through their Discord (which we'll get to soon), that the player be permabanned (An action that any other member would receive), the staff team essentially said "If you knew who they were in reality, you would have demanded leniency, so we're only temporarily banning them".

Their Discord is where abuse runs free however. The community believes that since the game isn't in full release yet, that the devs don't give a ♥♥♥♥ what the staff team do, nor how professional they are. I'm beginning to believe this quite strongly. The main admin, 'Sir' is allowed to mute/ban people on a whim, usually muting them 'indefintley' as he would say (Which is just another word for permanent). Being muted like this doesn't let you send ANY messages, or contribute to/interact with the community in any way. It's gotten to the point that there is a separate Discord server with more than a hundred members of people who were muted/banned, who want the staff team to have a reformation.

My personal story with the staff team involved me posting a gif of another game, Exanima. The gif displayed the games impressive armour layering system. In it there were naked breasts, I was blurred out images/blacked out texts (Discord's spoiler system) aren't allowed, so I just posted the gif, to show to another member. Tasteful nudity or not, the staff team didn't appreciate it, and muted me. When I asked how long it would be, hours, days, weeks, months, Sir, the admin, responded with "Until I decide otherwise" (Verbaetum). I've come to terms with the mute by now, it's been a couple days, however this is still incredibly unprofessional behaviour for a staff member to display, especially for a Discord partnered server.


TL;DR Don't buy the game if you want to be a part of the community in any way. Wait until the staff team have been changed, or at LEAST, Sir is out of office.\t\t\t\t\t
