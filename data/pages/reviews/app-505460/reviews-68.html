\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever went to the main front-lines. But I can provide a POV of what it feels like to be on the flank of the enemy at the very far side of the map. Less chaotic, less resource to deploy to destroy pill boxes, and more concern for enemy if they start getting numbers. Its a push and pulled back situation most of the times. Medics are rare. But if you're the type to enjoy at least least chaotic battles, you should definitely come. Worth my money too.

Another opinion. It's up to you if want to take this, especially for people who are always/mostly busy, those who wants to play casually or play only an hour.
1. If you intend to buy this game and play for an hour, expect to stay in one position. Let's say you intend to push the bridge with your teammates, in an hour, the result you can achieve there is by killing 2-4 Warde- I mean, enemies. You can expect to shoot down 2-4 enemies. And mostly at those times you'd be sitting to cover for your team to prevent them from pushing and taking over your spawn base. You'd be dying and dying over just to realize you have to take this game slowly.
2. You can get kills under an hour, and sit there doing nothing since you realize you can't push because of automatic pill boxes.
3. You can't get as much result as you expect it to be. Take this game like real war strategy in real-life. It WILL take you almost a day to even get a proper support from the Logistics Guys and some other Squads who handle artillery or armored vehicles(Shout out to these guys!). In short, you can't expect to push through territory and take the land in a day, it will probably take a week or more.

My conclusion as someone who played the game in 4 hours total and a newbie, it's peaceful and good for casuals. But for people who only have an hour or two free time, you can't expect the game to be fast paced. This is extremely slow-paced! if you get interrupted because your mother told you to do chores, it's fine. You don't get punished in this game but you just spawn back at your town base or bunker and ruins the immersion. Overall, it's a good game and good community that helps out!\t\t\t\t\t
