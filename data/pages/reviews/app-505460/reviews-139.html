\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game shocked me, I tried this game a few months ago and hated it, didn’t have a clue what I was doing so I got it refunded.

But OMG, I’m so glad I gave it another chance, I saw a YouTube video and thought I’ll try again and I’m so glad I did, the game itself isn’t the most gorgeous game graphically but it’s good enough and runs ultra smoothly at 144fps for myself.

The thing that made me love it is, I met a random guy at a base, who asked me what my plan was as I was just wondering around, and within seconds i was added to his group and we was off on a trip to the front lines.
Maybe I’m sad, but hearing grown men and women scream “medic” with the occasional group of Russians in a tank singing go past me made me feel amazing, the intensity of the firefights and gun shots and burning through ammo is just amazing, I’m really not too fussed about the amount of time I would die or get left to bleed out in a trench, to respawn and go back to try and avenge myself was amazing.

The great fact that everything is built by players is crazy good, I joined maybe near the end of a war or midway through so there’s lots of tanks and everyone is stacked up, I would love to see what it’s like at the bare bones of just trench warfare.

If anyone loves war games and just having a blast with a group of mates BUY this game, holding a town for hours on end is crazy, slightly disappointed when you hold it and log off and then a few hours later your team has lost it to the enemy but that will teach Me.


I recommend this game and I can’t wait for the sequel they have planned. Huge fan can’t wait to play more\t\t\t\t\t
