\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very difficult game to review. On the one hand, you get a lot of value per dollar. The developers are continuously developing the game and never charging for expansions, dlc, micro-transactions etc. If you enjoy the idea of a persistent war that continues to develop while you are offline, this will certainly be an interesting game for you. I highly doubt you will regret spending $30 on the game.

On the other hand...it is difficult for me to "recommend" this game based on the grindy, addictive nature and the generally toxic community. People love to hate players in the "enemy" faction and it gets pretty bad. It's just a game, but not everyone thinks that way. Many people joke that Foxhole is a social experiment.

The balance is a very contentious issue as the asymmetry in this game is more extreme than any other game I've ever played. Any frustration is exacerbated since you have to pick a faction for the duration of the war which can last months in the real world.

Just be careful to enjoy the game in moderation, and work as a team player rather than as a solo.\t\t\t\t\t
