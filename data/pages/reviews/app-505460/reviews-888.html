\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was great, then it was good, then it was ok, and now it is bad.

It's not the game I started playing a year ago, and I'll attempt to explain how the devs of this game have ruined every aspect of it I enjoyed.

IMO gameplay can be broken down into three areas (combat, logistics, and building), and I enjoyed each of these roles.

COMBAT:
How combat was: You had the ability to dodge enemy fire and, immediately after, return fire. To effectively dodge, you had to know each weapon's firing speed, anticipate your opponent's movement and actions, and attempt to control the distance between yourself and your opponent. While dodging, you could--with practice--fire very quickly and mostly accurately with very little interruption to your movement. All of these things happened very quickly which made the combat feel very fluid and fast-paced. You could 1v5 enemies somewhat regularly if you made the correct choices and had the mechanical skill to execute them.

How combat is now: You can no longer fire your weapon accurately right after having moved around. Dodging is pointless now, because you'll never hit the person whose attack you dodged. With dodging being taken out, the combat feels much slower and much more boring. There is no additional skill to learn. There are tactics (flank here with overwhelming numbers, lob a grenade at this position, etc.) which you can use to gain an advantage over your opponent, but these options existed previously. The devs, by their own admission, have stated they don't want combat to be skill-based.

LOGI:
It takes longer to gather materials than it did previously (resource yield per node has slowly gone down over time). It takes longer to deliver equipment than it used to (map is more than 9x larger, and they removed production from some maps).

BUILDING:
It still takes just as long to build things, but--due to a decay system that was introduced--you have to rebuild your structures every 36h or so.

Since new players wouldn't notice these changes I've mentioned, you might think I'd recommend this game. However, on top of these issues, there is the big problem of low player population. Sometimes only about 100 players are online (less than what is needed to fill one map let alone nine or more). The devs put the game on sale to boost player pop every few months, but I'm not sure they'll be able to keep this up very much longer.

TL;DR the devs have, and continue to, ruin the experience of solo or mostly-solo players. And they take the worst aspects of the game and exaggerate them. Invest your time elsewhere. Most players have uninstalled this game.\t\t\t\t\t
