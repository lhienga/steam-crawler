\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the few games that does logistics right. This game is an absolute sandbox: you can run logistics; you can battle in the trenches pushing the front line; you can join an armored crew and eliminate enemy hard points; you can be a spotter for artillery; you can engage in partisan actions behind enemy lines; there's a lot that you can do and the only thing stopping you is time and manpower - which leads into one of the downsides of all this freedom.

While you can engage in many actions and have a some success you will never be able to change much unless you work together with your team. Everything - from the rifle in your hand to the ammunition you fire - has to be made by another player. This is not a game you can jump in and play solo. I highly recommend getting involved with a group - be it a clan or discord server or what have you. Planning of larger actions, offensives, or logistical operations is up to the players - Foxhole only has a basic squad system and it's up to the players to coordinate beyond that.

There is also quite a steep learning curve and few official resources for learning the game. Again, getting involved with a group of players will be helpful here. Luckily, there are plenty of helpful players that volunteer time to help newbies; some even hold "Basic Training" at each factions' home islands to acquaint new players with the basics of the game.

All in all it's an excellent war game, with a scale to match. Get involved with a group of knowledgeable players, and you will have one of the best war game experiences you can get.\t\t\t\t\t
