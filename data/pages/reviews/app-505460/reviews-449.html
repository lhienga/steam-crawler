\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: Still haven't come across as many toxic players as some reviewers say. We just come to dislike non-ranked, privates and corporals due to THEIR toxicity (usually due to ignorance, not necessarily malice).
Are there bad apples? yes, like every MMO. Are there many? No... Are there a LOT of people just ready to chat and have fun? hell yes

At first, after reading a few reviews, I was worried about toxicity...

After 12 hours of gameplay over a little more than 2 days (since I bought it), I met a few weird people, but not a single actually toxic gamer. Granted I am with Wardens and have no idea how it is with Colonials (heard there are more streamers there right now, and so more toxicity by default).

A guy saw me wandering around town when I wanted to put down my Medic role and pick up a Logi role to try it out a bit. He asked me if I was alright and I just told him I was new and getting used to the game. He told me to hop on his truck and spent the next 2-3 hours showing me the ropes, teaching me tricks, walking me through various tasks (not doing them for me, actually letting me drive and try it all out), driving various truck types and boats. A few other Logi were super helpful and even often kept ressources on the side to teach noobs like me.

On the bridge that I fought on for 4 hours straight as a medic -wielding a gun with a single loaded magazine in it-, We all acted like brothers, screaming "BROTHERRR" (not all the time, but enough times to make it memorable) when we got downed and saved each other, carrying the wounded back to get treated, charging the enemy all together to push them back. We got to know each other, making me want to go back there all the time since they are all almost always at the same front. Now, whenever I'm Logi, I'll be resupplying them with what I now know they always badly need.

THOUGH, I have seen people ask others to move... But those people WERE blocking roads, ports, factories, etc...
Some would stand in front of moving vehicles. Only Team Kill is if you break some player made rules in the game, like don't take/steal tech ressources because that ♥♥♥♥♥ over the whole faction (their usage is voted on, so don't take them). Trolls will also be put down and reported. Be careful of the ban hammer. If you are an ass (or if you get to close to some clans I heard, depending on what is being done) or a troll, you will be killed and then reported.


I love this game already and will keep playing it, now only to find a team to join and have even more fun.\t\t\t\t\t
