\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game with so much potential, that I have had many excellent experiences on, being murdered by it's very creators. A mother, smothering their own child believing that they are saving it. As the new Naval update is about to be release, I prepare myself for arguably what will be my last war on Foxhole, and what I believe is about to be the death of the game. A game, where content, and new experiences should be celebrated, and I find myself dreading it.

The Devs, who previously I would have supported in taking time to make good development choices, have shown themselves to be almost wholly blind, and lacking in any thought when it comes to choices they make. Asymmetrical balance so bad, that guns/vehicles WITHIN a faction are made completely redundant by virtue of other weapons in the faction (ignoring progression); not to mention that some weapons are just simply pointless to make. Not to mention the bugs that have been present for years still go unfixed.

Do not waste your money on this game, this development team, or this company. You're money is better spent on Overwatch 2 in comparison.

To any Devs reading this I have the following comment to make.
It speaks volumes to create a game that you don't even want to play; that forces the dedicated players into horrible game play loops for the 'greater good' of a fictional group. It tells a terrible story, that the community on your own game commonly states 'dev man bad' because there is no other explanation for why mechanics are the way they are. You've successfully managed to take a genera leading game, and have failed on game play loops already proven to succeed. Your 'vision' is so blind, that I now question if you ever had eyes in the first place. In a weird twist of irony, I can only truly congratulate you on one thing. You've made a game, that is as hopeless in real life, as is the situation in the world of the game.

Here is a summary of game play loops/mechanics.
New player experience
A) You found a vet who happily teaches you the game. Victory!
B) You were unable to find a vet that teaches you the game, and combat + Logi is forever a mystery.
C) You ran into one of the toxic clans, and looked at someone the wrong way, promptly being accused of being an Alt and banned.

Communication/clans
Operations used to exist, allowing for randoms to group together effectively to do things. Still waiting for this to come back.
Squad/regiment system is easily the worst system that I've ever seen. Foxhole clans exist on discord, not foxhole.
Voice comes will randomly disconnect, and you will need to restart the game to fix it. Pray no-one kills your sleeping body so you can return to the same spot.

Infantry
80% I shoot more bullets than you/luckier than you, 20% game knowledge/position/skill
If someone is spamming grenades, then they win.
Infantry AT is actually pretty nice to use.
The only sniper in existence isn't the sniper (worst weapon in the game). It's the anti-tank rifle.

Vehicles
I heard you like line battles?
I heard you love RNG even more than infantry
I heard you love several hours of logi, 20-30mins of arming the tank/yourself, only to be killed in 5mins because of a bush 94.5mm AT gun.
If you can really good at tanks and have a well coordinated crew, they can be really fun.

Artillery
Arguably the only balanced area of the game. Good sounds on the guns too.
Rocket arty is extremely fun to fire, but generally worthless.

Making defences
2-3 people with autoclickers + 1 person designing the place + 2-3hours = bunker base.
You will have no defences at your base (unless its literally the front) for 1-2 RL days, during which point its very vulnerable to attacks.
You will pay your several hours of logi game play each day to keep it alive, otherwise it will decay.
Also pray that someone doesn't come and destroy your plan because it isn't "meta"

Logi
Do you like no QoL? How about your QoL being removed?
Do you like taking 30mins to make basic materials for basic guns?
Do you like artificial timers and autoclickers?
Do you like taking 20-30mins to drive to the front?
Then logi is for you!
Also, There is also a 50% chance, you'll meet people who exist to deny your ability to do anything for fear of wasting 'their' resources. There is also a 50% chance you'll find the most amazing and helpful people in the game.
Note: Devs have never actually played logi, and it shows. Seriously devs, you actually just need to play logi on both teams for a way as normal players, and you'll understand 99% of the problems of the game.

Facilities
So, foxhole is literally the ideal place for some Factorio/Satisfactory game play. Shame this is the Foxhole devs version. This is not a factory game, this is a Idle phone game with a side of logi.
For the uneducated out there (including the devs), complexity is a GOOD thing, and should REWARD players that engage with it successfully. Instead, foxhole punishes the entire community to access what should have otherwise been basics.
Could have had actual tank factories with actual ability to produce variants.
Also, you will pay your 1-2h of forced labour every day to keep your small factory alive.

Partisan
Good game play loop (for those who like this sort of thing). It's been made more difficult to do, but still doable. Also, the asymmetry of the two stealth buffing uniforms, could have been made really interesting, but was deployed terribly, rendering the benefit of using one of the uniforms redundant.\t\t\t\t\t
