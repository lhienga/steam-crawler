\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA Chore than Entertainment.

People who want quick action need to stay away from this game. The game simulates a real war requiring way too many hours to do little influence; later to be taken from you when you are offline.

In my opinion the game would benefit from Automated Resources and crafted items, heavily focusing on the Fight than the maintenance and establishment of War Fronts.

Unfortunately, the game is as advertised. If your chosen alliance refuses to transport resources, there will be nothing to fight back with. In addition, the freedom to do anything is the biggest flaw.

From the casual "Sims" player building bases away from the war for a personal achievement to the dedicated Truck Driver, or idiot thieves (Allies) in your hometown robbing Materials in places that truly need it, the game suffers greatly from the sandbox Freedom. Although the game puts up 2 Factions against each other, the real enemy is the arrogance of squads within sections of the alliance itself. People will claim territory and shoot friendlies on sight to avoid shuttling Resources to the War Front, or putting up Warning signs to deter friendlies from entering particular bunkers.

The game is a child's playground with a greater Quantity of kids doing anything except fighting the enemy team. No resources, no fighting.\t\t\t\t\t
