\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTitle: Timezones, Building and Play Experience

Don’t know what could be done if anything but foxhole is degrading into a typical survival mmo game where the moment the majority of the player base sleeps, certain groups come out and do massive damage. There is no massive battle to hold. There’s no great struggle showing out the fun parts of the game. It’s people waking up to see their AI defenses did nothing. Days of effort wiped out over night, literally.

I’m reading messages in other discords where people are done with your game. They put in effort and time into your game and are getting nothing out. Just pain. I like the game but building a bunker base that takes days to make somewhat defensible only for it to die within less than a day is a joke.

I’ve been saying a recent joke. “Foxhole is too persistent.” It is. Besides games like Rust and Ark Survival, there is no other game that requires so much time from me to defend my ingame possessions around the clock.\t\t\t\t\t
