\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like the WW1 experience of being shell shocked and mass charged then this is the game for you! Some good game mechanics are that you can literally make your own defensive base at the frontlines or in logistics zones. You can also bring troops at the frontlines supplies to keep up the good fight like small arms, ammo, grenades, even tanks and artillery. This game also has a very nice community and if you ask for help in chat you will get help on really anything. There are 2 factions that are in a persistent war that will last for real life weeks, these factions are called The Colonials (the best) and the Wardens. There are also Regiments and Squads. Squads are like a group that Regiments could make or you could just make a squad with you and your friends. Regiments are essentially big squads that you join from an invite from the Regiment commander of that Regiment they can have their own discord servers for better comms and planning spec ops to go behind enemy lines either to setup a secret base or to disrupt enemy logistics. There is also a tech tree in the game to unlock better guns and equipment to better tanks and field guns, it's really your imagination from a soldier fighting in trenches or a general directing troops to the frontlines.\t\t\t\t\t
