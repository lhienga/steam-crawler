\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe devs need to act professional, start acting unbiased and change their mindset. It's not professional, it's not right. I can't recommend the game in this current state.

There are two factions in this game and the game developers are clearly biased towards one faction. Almost everyone agrees on this, even the opposing faction.

The "dev favorite" faction has had their overpowered weapons (pve monster cutler, hv40-45m, 45m outlaw that can PvE Eats etc) without any nerf for nearly a year, even when everyone agreed that it's unfair and needed rebalancing.

The "step-child, unfavoured by the devs" faction is announced to get a weapon (75mm cannon) with the upcoming update. Before even having the chance to use the weapon for one single war (let me remind you that the dev fave faction has been using their OP weapons for almost a year without any intervention, nerf or rebalance work), "the biased devs" decide to nerf the gun of that said faction they dislike, because a couple "dev fave faction" people didn't want the opposing faction to get those weapons. A couple people can tip the balance further into their side, while one faction is already hurting.

It even shows in the lore of the game. This game does not have much of a lore but whatever they have, its all about "the dev's favorite faction".

If you like playing an uphill battle with the unfavorite faction or playing an unbalanced easy mode battle with the favorite faction, buy the game.

What the game developers are doing is even worse than abandoning the game, ignoring to balance it or taking their time to fix it. Everyone that follows the updates or plays the game knows about these issues, the dev's are getting a ton of feedback and they in fact "Do know" about these problems.

The devs are actively not adressing these problems and making it worse for the weak faction (colonials). The state this game is in right now and the devs attitude is the biggest indicator of the path this game is on.

I do not recommend it. The game is not balanced and the devs are adding fuel to the fire (on purpose).\t\t\t\t\t
