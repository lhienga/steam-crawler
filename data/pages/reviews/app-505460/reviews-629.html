\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have experienced things in this game that no other game has even come close to. Ive played plenty of milsim experiences like Squad and Arma with thousands of hours between them, but this game so far has been one of the most immersive and interesting experiences.

It feels like if Squad was all in one server and was a top down shooter. The aspect of needing logistics plays a huge roll in any players playstyle. Supplies being low directly contribytes to no respawns or a lack of ammo or anti tank stuff and so on. Logistic players feel like heros and can entirly change the battles they participate in

I played in a multi day defensive siege in Savages recently. It was one of the first battles I really have played in and it was incredible. A territory cut off from supplies, being repetedly shelled by the enemy while running low on everything. I remember being drafted into a rescue mission to repair a bridge just to let some supplies in, only for the bridge to be rushed by tanks. I got in a boat with a group of privates and we stormed the towns nearby. We mostly ended up dying but we took people with us. I remember having a french captain tell me to come over and that he had a plan, he had lined up a bunch of boats in the river that let us swim across. I guarded him as he threw grenades and destroyed enemy logistic buildings behind enemy lines. I decided to try out logistics and eventually worked up to making a tank. I drove it up to the frontlines and ran into tons of players giving me advice about tanks and helping me out with fuel and such. When i made it to the front i joined up with 2 other tanks and we formed and armored line where we pushed the enemys back and ran over there trenches.

Theres so much to this game thats incredible. But I will admit its community feels toxic at times. While almost every individual player i have seen has been nice, there is a lot of times I have been told off because of my rank. I had a captain demand I give him my tank that I grinded for just because he didnt think I knew how to drive it. In fact driving around with a tank almost always leads to players getting pissy with someone who has less hours than them using a tank while they run around as infantry, This isnt speculation I have litterally had veteran players tell me they have 10x more hours than me as justification for ordering me around. The overal community itself is almost always complaining about game balance and attacking the devs as well. In the like 5 days I have played this game I have seen both sides get called OP. I personally dont really even know the game enough to understand the balance, but the nonstop complaining is a bit obnoxious when it takes up 90% of the discussions for this game\t\t\t\t\t
