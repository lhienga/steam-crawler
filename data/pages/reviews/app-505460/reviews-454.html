\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo there's a lot to say here about Foxhole and it's community.

In this game there are two factions: The Wardens and the Colonials.
Wardens are defense oriented: Better terrain, heavy armor, and winter gear.
Colonials are offense oriented: Flatter terrain, mass produced tanks, and mud/rain gear.

The Wardens are a mix of EU and US players mostly split between large and medium sided clans.
Colonials are a bit of a mixed bag - US, CN, and Russia as far as I can tell - mostly fragmented into smaller groups.

Wardens are horny weebs. Colonials are furries. Take your pick. You'll die all the same.

ALSO: COMMEND THE LOGI. THEY MAKE SUPPLIES SO YOU CAN WIN AND HAVE FUN.


EDIT as of 2/11/23.
While the core gameplay is still quite fun, remember that all the equipment you use in this game is produced by logistics players. Logistics is often a mix of base building and truck simulator where people run around the backline collecting material to send to the front. The recent facilities update has put a lot of strain on the backline players to the extent that you'd have to no-life the game to maintain anything you build. That kind of commitment requirement isn't sustainable.\t\t\t\t\t
