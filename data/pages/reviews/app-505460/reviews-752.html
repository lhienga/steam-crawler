Then logistics is going to get really ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ boring like everything else. The game is overpriced for an early access game that has huge problems in it's very core. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis review is my honest thoughts on this game. After being split, on whether or not I would enjoy this game, I took the plunge and got Foxhole in the sale. After being a bit overwhelmed to start with when i first started the game , I decided to do the training on the map. I stumbled upon a high ranking player, and he was using his mic and going through everything in fine detail, and even going through the tactics of the game. When we all went through the training he then took us to the front line and we stayed together a good thirty minutes defending a bridge and keeping it repaired. Since then, I have found more and more people who i have teamed up with and pushed our frontline on, all be it i think we will lose the war eventually. Dont expect to get the recognition for doing something in this game like you would most other games, if you kill someone it doesn't come up with a little icon saying 'this player has killed this player', in fact alot of the time you arn't even sure it was you who killed the enemy . Same goes for building and Logi. You do however get to commend players for their efforts towards the war. The whole idea of this game isn't to go out and do things on your own, you need other people with you to achieve anything in this game. Logi players are required to transport the materials and supplies to the front line so builders can build defenses while the infantry push on the front line. It is a fantastic game and it hasnt been fully released yet. There are a few bugs, i have fallen out of vehicles when entering water and died , only to be left by my newly found friends who hadnt even realised, and i never saw them again. That being said , I would thoroughly recommend this game it has its downs, but its mostly ups.\t\t\t\t\t
