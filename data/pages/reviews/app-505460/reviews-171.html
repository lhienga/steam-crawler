Balance issues, many game mechanics like facilities being for no reason incredibly annoying and draining, alting causing distrust among players, the insane amounts of time and energy needed to maintain defenses and facilities. Which take easily hours to make the stuff to maintain them. If you get invested into this game it demands that you treat it like a part time job.

6: Servers are often unstable, with entire servers going down out of nowhere, as well as lots of lag.

TLDR: I love this game, but please dont get yourself invested in it.
