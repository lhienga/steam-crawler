\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was between a positive and a negative review on this game but ultimately decided on a positive review. There are some positives and some negatives to this game. A positive is from what I have seen the community is very helpful in teaching new players how to play and most everyone on this game is pretty chill. The negative however is that if you play solo you can get very bored very fast. This game is really built on community and proximity communication. There's nothing wrong with that and the game itself is pretty unique with unique features that is a big positive. The issue I have with it though is that the aiming system is frankly just frustrating and makes the game not very fun to play if you play by yourself or get separated from your squad/regiment. I personally would really just like to see the aim steady much quicker than it currently does and just give players like one more hits worth of health. Randomized aim is frankly just pretty tilting and takes a lot of fun out of the game. If you're just looking to chill with your friends or find a good regiment there's still fun to be had, the game would just be much more fun with more of a skill aspect than relying on rng. I also think the negative reviews I've seen make a lot of sense. If you're a solo player and don't try to actively get involved with other people and tasks/strategies you will not have fun on this game. This is a game you are definitely going to want to communicate with other players in some way and probably will have more fun with if you look to join and communicate with a regiment on discord.

All in all it's a decent game for the comms and unique features/wars but you might have a hard time wanting to stay on it all day due to the low skill rng fights. Especially if you are playing by yourself and either don't have friends playing or don't look to make friends on the game.\t\t\t\t\t
