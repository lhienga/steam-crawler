Doesn't happen often but happens.

The anti-cheat system is non-existent and just needs to be implemented. Full stop. Even if the cheats do not exist then at least the players should have some confidence that they are not being cheated.

Alts are a problem - I would solve it with making use of the in-level ranks system so that perhaps the new players don't take the more expensive tanks and drive it alone in the middle of the enemy bunker base. Is it an alt or a newbie - no clue, but the number of fire trucks disappearing just before major fires is just ridiculous (event though they were parked in the same spots for weeks).

People are cool and helpful. Of course there is some tribalism, but less so than in many other games, and generally speaking you will always find someone to help you out.

I would wait with buying this game until:
1. At least some re-balancing
2. Introduction of anti-cheat system
3. Solution to alts problem

There were some big updates last year and I think that things need to settle a bit before fighting on the front line will be a bit more enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
