\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe concept of the game is very good with base construction, player gathered resources, player built supplies, logistics managed by the players, and this is where anything nice to say stops. With a top down view most if not all weapons seem to function the same not including rate of fire and magazine capacities. The construction or emplacement of structures can either assist or completely hamper the other structures around them or players, friendly or enemy, just in general. The top down view, given the right angles, makes you either invisible or a sitting duck despite a wall or ceiling coverage, how a wall that appears to be twice your size cant stop bullets is beyond me? Gas attacks ignore elevations unless they are drastic changes in height such as a cliff walls don't count. Aiming is a nightmare even if that dot/cross hair is lined up perfectly you can still miss the non-moving, standing, target that isn't even aware your there because you've used the right angle of approach to make your self invisible despite being in their field of view. If your looking for a team build/fight game this isn't it because people WILL take vehicles for their own personal/group needs despite being in an army. I found myself on the war front and despite heavy losses we couldn't get more ammo brought to us no matter how much we begged. The teams are heavily imbalanced as from what I've heard and read there is bias in the two choices and how players are distributed between the two as well as sorry this side is full notice when choosing between them. When you load in the map displays the overwhelming disparity between the two teams. Resources thankfully seem to be distributed evenly but again the favored side usually takes 2/3 of the map rather quickly. Research is done to progress your sides technology and weaponry but who wants to do that when you can troll players by blocking them in or running them over with a vehicle you stole while it was being fueled up. The game overall is trash. One day if this company were to rework the imbalanced and unrealistic approaches to the combat, logistics, productions, and character vision while maintaining the goal of the game's basic concept I would gladly give it another go but within the very short time I played this game I knew this is a terrible game.\t\t\t\t\t
