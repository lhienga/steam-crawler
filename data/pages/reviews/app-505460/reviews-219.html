\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe front line battle aspect is pretty realistic. Apart from that, the logistics field is also very much in depth. It may tempt you to grind resources and manufacture supplies for the front line, and you may do it on a small scale. There's no use in trying to mass produce or try and build combat vehicles unless you have a big squad. Most materials (like coal ) need time for refining even after mining them. Be careful to set resources to private if you intend on using them later. To properly mine or gather basic resources, you'll need big vehicles (flatbeds), not the puny R1 Hauler type which you will have used a lot and then realise what a waste of time that was. You'll make the big trucks, only to be stolen after you logout. So, unless you've got a big squad, just have some fun in the front line or do small scale logi related grind and deliver it to the front lines. It's immensely satisfying. Then, you might ask, if I've gotta play in the front line, why should I do it in a silly top down manner where there are plenty of realistic front line experiences like Squad? Well, it's a different experience. And the front line is just chaos (unless you've got a good command), with you trying to aim and shoot, only to realise that a teammate has come in your "line of fire" and that you've shot them accidentally.

That said, if you have a squad, then there are a lot of things that you'll be able to build and supply. The manufacturing process is unbelievably in depth, realistic and complicated too. There are trains (both small gauge supply and normal gauge ones with cannons fitted) which I feel is a pretty cool thing.

In short, unless you join a clan or have a lot of friends playing this game, you'll just feel exhausted after putting in a lot of effort.

Just treat this game as a way to pass time. Do some small scale logi, deliver it to the front lines, get commends, try to take out some enemies. Don't just stick to logi or fighting, experience it all.

Update : The servers, being based in EU, are pretty laggy and it isn't a fun experience for a player from Asia. I get disconnected 20 times a day, that too at crucial junctures. Most of the times, it says, "connection refused". I've never had such a consistent problem even in other MMOs with EU only servers. Lost a lot of resources because of sudden dcs.

Final Update: The game has become unplayable because of too much alt activity. Players from the opposite faction just come during low pop. and dump vehicles into the sea, steal material and ammo from facilities, etc. Basically, whichever faction has more alts is bound to win. Not gonna play unless the devs decide to do something about it. Countless tanks and other vital machinery are being dumped into the sea / destroyed by alt. accounts. ♥♥♥♥♥ with regimental facilities on a daily basis is a whole another chapter.\t\t\t\t\t
