\r\n\t\t\t\t\t At this point I have to say I can't do a review at conventional standard.

But just let me tell you the story of my first hours in foxhole:

As I started I spawned at the basic camp...just choose a location where to go and then I was there...totally out of ideas what to do. Someone who noticed that i was a bit confused gathered me up telling me this position will be overrun very soon and we had to retreat, so we have to get the supplies out of there. This dude gave me the order to pick up a truck and run them supplies out there before we blow up the bridge that will cut of the enemy. And you know what...I did what he told me...don't know why but I did it. Long story short we drove those boxes and ammo out there and blew up the bridge. Again I had no idea what to do. So a guy came around collecting some volunteers for a risky mission. I grabbed some medical supplies...a gun and some ammo and jumped up the loading area of this his truck with a hand full of other soldiers.
At our destination we found an infantry bridge destroyed, but those guys made up a small camp with supplies to build it up again. My job was to cover the engineers while they were doing there job. Over that bridge we took down some defensive structures and build up an offensive bridgehead. A bloody hell it was...I was just busy keeping my guys alive while the enemy tried to push us back. We were defeated after a couple of while and that sucked...I was kind of depressed because we did a huge afford just to lose this bridgehead again. But then my commander...I call him like that because he lead the mission...told us this was the distraction for the main offensive down the river.

By accident I became part of the distraction mission that lead to the success for the main offensive to take back an city that was under siege for like 4 days.
And this sh@t is not scripted...all the players do this by themself.

Imagine standing in this city knowing you were part of this, one of hundreds of player that made standing there possible...

Today was a good day!\t\t\t\t\t
