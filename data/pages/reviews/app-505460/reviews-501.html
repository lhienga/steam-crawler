\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game 5 days ago, and I have to say that it is the best MMO games I have ever played. at the time writing this review I have sunk about 35 hours into it and I have no plans on putting this game down any time soon.

my favourite aspect of this game is the fact that the war you and your faction are fighting continues while you're not online, it continues 24/7. you might spend your time one day fighting on a front line, and when you log off, that fight will still be continuing without you, you will come back online and see that your buddies pushed the front line and gained ground, or were forced back by the enemies.

Everything in this game is player built, every bullet, rifle, tank, bandage... and you can sink hours into collecting materials in the back lines in order to keep the front line infantry supplied. it almost feels like a truck driving simulator game, right up until the point when your truck is riddled with bullets and explodes.

That being said there are some issues, some reviewers have complained about clans mass reporting people in order to get them banned, It has not happened to me, but be aware that some people have encountered this. I also see clans Hoarding vital materials in giant bases far behind front lines, making it slightly more difficult to resupply the front.

These issues are nowhere close to enough for me to give this game a thumbs down, this game has an amazing player community that is welcoming and helpful to new players like me. and the moments of excitement storming a trench with your new found comrades, shouting battle-cry's and bayoneting an enemy because your base ran out of grenades is thrilling.\t\t\t\t\t
