Some things I'd like to see fixed...

1. Can't stand up once you start cocking a grenade (I've seen tons of people blow themselves up because of this), HUGE delay for crouch after throwing one as well.

2. Both your team and the enemies buildings under construction are blue, it's literally impossible to tell whose is who. Which can be hugely important. Also the flags over the buildings are very hard to discern, I think wardens look yellow and blue and collies are pure yellow? I'm not even sure, they don't look distinctive at all in my opinion.

3. Everything surrounding building, maintaining and upgrading trenches is bad. It's very difficult to build them or connect them. You can't shoot through the rope as they are being built. You need building materials to fill them in or remove sandbags / barbed wire upgrades (I think it's dumb you use a wrench to remove barbed wire as well, they should just make wire cutters).

4. It's extremely difficult to climb in and out of trenches as well, you spam spacebar in every direction and it frequently takes 5 secs or more, which is certain death when your trying to run from a grenade.

5. I think it's dumb you can't right click to assemble and equip items from your bunker base.

6. The logistics system is bad too, it's really annoying. There should just be a separate tab at the bunker base where players can request items from the logistics people. Right now it's just a million people making posts on the map begging for basic items. I would build them myself but after nearly 170 hours of play I still have no idea how to do that. The tutorials are horrible. So many new players don't even know how to use to a bandage, much less use construction vehicles, or build a tank. I think the only realistic way to learn how to play this game is join a clan. There aren't even good youtube tutorials.

7. I think it's dumb that you win the war and like nothing even happens. Some guy in France probably knows but I still have no idea despiste winning two wars. I went to sleep after playing for a long time and next time I log in it's just like ok wars over. I don't think it even says who won, you just infer it was your team. There should be fireworks in the spawning area and a parade or a video cut scene or SOMETHING. It's really a bummer, I don't know if you can even check your stats or anything. It would be cool if it told you how many kills / deaths you had, trenches / items built, literally something to reward players for such a huge time commitment.

8. The game is heavily dominated by armored vehicles because there is absolutely no way for infantry to get past the endless number of automatic gun turrets that instantly target and kill you. Really dumb, you log in at the start of a new war and there is basically no point in even playing until tanks or artillery are unlocked. Mammons have ridiculously short range, it's basically 100% suicide if you try to use them to destroy a vehicle or a automatic machine gun, they even insta kill you if you are standing in a trench.

9. Another thing that's very bad is the aiming system, I was shocked when an enemy was 4 ft away from me and I spray fired 30 bullets with a sub machine gun only to have all of them miss? Ridiculous. Or how you pull out you pistol to finish an enemy lying still on the ground 2 ft away and to have 4 shots miss? You have to stand there and aim for like 2 seconds to shot them. It's really a bad system. Also your frequently shooting at people and it you gives you the red line that means you can't hit them, and surprise you can! People somehow turn immortal when they lay down as well, the whole system is pretty bad.

10. Lots of toxic players, people saying all kinds of slurs and teammates screaming into their mics. If a teammate kills you on purpose you can't report them while your dead, you can't commend people either (The commendation system is really bad, you can alt + right click to mute / report / commend people but I'd wager roughly 85% of players don't know that). People can kill you and instantly close their game to avoid all punishments. There should be an immediate pop that asks if you want to report them - and if there's excessive team killing other players should be able to watch clip of the incident while there dead to ask if it looked intentional and give them a 90 min ban for first offense and escalate if they keep it up.
