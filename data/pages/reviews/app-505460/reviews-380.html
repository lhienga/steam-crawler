\r\n\t\t\t\t\tConcept, I think we have all dreamt of something like this, it's a fun game, you can easily sink hundreds of hours into this game, definitely a unique experience.

The biggest issue that has been apparent since I started in World Conquest #83 (The longest war yet!) and til now is the balancing of the game.

So in the game each faction gets unique tools, which is fine, but the wardens have got the best equipment in the game and is meta, you can't get this equipment if you play on the colonial side, so you are basically always going to be fighting a hard war where the enemy power spikes massively and you have no way to challenge it, the developers don't communicate with the players at all and that results in players waiting months for these 'updates' to only add more stuff without balancing the previous stuff!

It's depressing to have to fight against a faction that is basically the equivalent of the saying 'Born with a silver spoon in your mouth', the devs coddle and give the wardens buff and no nerfs, soon people are going to be quitting, and I know many in the game that are taking big breaks until things change, that reminds me.

Not only is the balancing all over the place, the gameplay can be heavily improved and isn't even when the entire community wants and screams for it! We ask for logi updates, they make a few changes which are really amazing and basically QoL updates, but we also want improvements to logi overall as a system and also the terrible building system which is so buggy and finnicky it makes people want to blow their head off, still not communication on that, really is annoying and demoraslising to all players.


TLDR: Balance is atrocious, there maybe potential bias from the developers, devs do not listen to community, devs take so long to make changes, the game has big gameplay issues which results in less fun!\t\t\t\t\t
