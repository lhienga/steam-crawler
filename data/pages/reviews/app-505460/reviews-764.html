I tried many times to form groups to help establish coordinated attacks, ambushes, defenses, and many other strategic ideas, but no ever wants to commit to someones plan, unless theirs someone, who is a super high rank or a big popular clan conducting plan. It seems like a very fun game, but you can't get anything done by yourself and not many people are willing to help, like I've stated. Foxhole would be better, if everyone had a squad of bots to follow them around and be their own squad leader. Except we all know the A.I. would either be way too good at shooting people first or completely useless. I would say it would be a game to buy, when it's on sale or maybe a free weekend, but never at full price. It's just another game that has so much to offer, but sadly the community is just idiotic and always doing the 1 man army mentality. I'm all about teamwork, but never seem to find many people who are in the same mindset or willing to follow someone new or unknown. I could keep going on about things I don't like, but it's just not worth it; because I already know this post will be seen as "You're just trash", "Get gewd", "You never got with a good group or clan". Maybe I'll come back to the game again in the future, but I've uninstalled and reinstalled the game, like 3 times now and I'm just done with the community, not the game. I don't think it's me either; because I've had multiple people want to invite me to their clans, but it's like no one is ever on at the same time to do much. 2/5 Stars game\t\t\t\t\t
