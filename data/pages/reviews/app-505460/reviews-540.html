\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat an amazing game! I'm blown away. I sank 56 hours into this game in about a week - which is NOT normal for me. At all. I couldn't stop. And with so many different ways to play - as an engineer, as a medic, as a grunt, as a tanker, and so on, (not defined by rigid classes but simply by what equipment you choose to pick up and how you choose to play) no two sessions were ever the same. Often one logs on, deploys to a region, and soon gets roped into some compelling situation with other players that becomes a choose-your-own adventure lasting hours.

The game design really emphasizes the logistics of supply that make battles possible. Production of arms and munitions behind the lines, and their subsequent transport, is a huge part of the game, something not really represented well or at all by most games in the genre.

Cooperating with hundreds of other players, working together as cogs in a machine rather than individual action heroes brings a very refreshing feeling to the action. Knowing that every round fired, every piece of war material destroyed represents the effort of some player who built armaments behind the front line brings a sense of mutual responsibility to members of a faction who look out to see that equipment is not wasted and to ensure that attacks count for something - not merely Kill:Death ratio shootouts within no larger context.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlmost everything in this game is too convoluted to be fun. If you have lots of time on your hands and are just looking for a big time sink of a game to lose yourself in, then it might work out for you. If you want to have actual fun consistently with your game time, maybe look elsewhere - there are other games that do logistics/truck driving much better and other games that do combat much better.
Positives: The world map is huge and fun to explore. The in-game community is friendly for the most part.
Cons: Pretty much everything. Resource gathering is a time consuming pain in the ass. Combat seems like a mess to me because the front lines are just so built up with all the crap everyone is building all over the place. Too many different types of ammo and guns. Game isn't newbie friendly and you can insta-die in your first few minutes in the world (by freezing to death) if you're unlucky without any real way to avoid it.\t\t\t\t\t
