As seen below, I loved this game, It has since been ruined by developers who refuse to listen to the community and solve important problems while prioritising issues that nobody wanted/needed solved. Please, if you buy this game, join the community of many who are fighting to get the players a voice and genuine solutions to the problems that face this innovative and interesting games. Here is my inital *Positive* review, many points still stand but the development team have failed us:



*sigh* I wish I could give this a neutral review or sommin. The game is great, the 13 man dev team are working their assess off to give a game that's loved, while they make blunders in their decisions sometimes and really piss off the community the game is still good. The real problem is the community, I'm a *very* solo player and have argued with a lot of clans in my time playing the game this is not unusual for games, but the community in general needs to improve, Toxicity is very easy to find with both factions having it. If someone is Looking to pick up the game:

- If you're solo, good luck to you I genuinely hope that you don't decide to quit the game like so many have.
- If you're coming from a clan, I don't care what clan it is but when you join don't get pulled into fights or arguments between clans, it's a waste of time and the people having the arguments are usually idiots
- If you've got friends who play you're in a perfect position to play the game, Roam around in a squad from 2-10 players doing whatever you guys enjoy, this game is perfectly geared for that play style and it's what the game needs more of.\t\t\t\t\t
