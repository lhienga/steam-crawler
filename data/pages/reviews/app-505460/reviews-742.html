I could easily invest hours into fortifying a road to secure logistics or farm and run a truck with high explosive granade's to make a breach into enemy defences. It was fun, I felt I was having an impact by myself which is extremely satisfying. Later on i'd even play with a few friends and join a group, even though we were usually only 6 to 10 people we felt we had a big impact on what was happening in the war, fighting, flanking, sabatuering, building an unholy amount of howitsers, even doing logistics, it was fun.

Now though I don't have fun, the game isn't satisfying anymore, the dev's keep pushing away from gameplay for solo and small grouped players towards groups with hundreds of players, to the point where by yourself or with your friends you cannot do anything to contribute to the war without spending multiple hours doing mindless tasks preparing for something which is probably going to fail because your going to get dunked on by a larger group or a ridiculous amount of defences which can only be breached with 50+ people coordinating and another 10 supplying them.

I would not recomend this game in its current state, the dev's and mods haven't been acknowledging the player bases critism for a long time now and are taking the game in a direction which is making alot of dedicated players leave, making the community toxic and leaving some scummy people in influentual places, in and out of game.\t\t\t\t\t

The gameplay is rewarding in that you will see the progress or decline your side achieves throughout the life span of a war. The devs are doing some really cool things here, they are constantly adding and changing features and trying to shape the game into something very cool and fun.
