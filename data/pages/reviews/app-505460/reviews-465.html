\r\n\t\t\t\t\tabsolutely astonishing how professional the playerbase is in terms of co-operation and attitude.
you barely to never hear anyone having a hissy fit over a death or arguing with teammates or enemies either in chat or voice chat, maybe because the majority of players are young adults.

if you like arma or any other realistic or complicated military war games, GO HERE! the only differences foxhole has between arma and other hardcore battle games is that it has a more interactive atmosphere, its based off ww2 times, the community isnt just filled with hardcore tryhards, and best of all, it has consistent wars which the outcome relies on your team of hundreds of players co-operation skills, researching skills, logistic skills, and battle skills over real-time months of battle.

download this now, not just for the realism factor but for the sense of family and community. bring your friend along the battle and feel like youre in a battle movie like band of brothers.

this game has little glitches, although the existing glitches can be fatal to your gameplay, they are very rare, as in my time of 74 hours of gameplay i have not experienced a single detrimental glitch. along with that, foxhole has little to no problems in general whatsoever, but i will list the pros and cons so you know what youre getting into:
PROS:
+: massive community,
+: big maps with barely any lag (as long as you arent playing on a nokia)
+: consistent wars that make you want to win (months of battling in the same war, two different war servers you can choose from)
+: top down view that eliminates the casual first person shooter effect,
+: vehicles cost your time and valuables but are worth it when you go into battle with a hard earned tank of your own with your crew
+ much more pros that you will encounter over time!

CONS:
- noobs might find themselves having trouble mastering the controls, understanding your options in terms of your job type (you might have trouble learning how to be a logistics driver or a tank commander, but i suggest staying as infantry whilst watching youtube videos on what you can do in foxhole and how to do it)

- MODERATION: when i encountered an extremely toxic player that teamkilled me in rage for the first time, i found myself struggling with the reporting system and when i finally figured out how to report the person, i never figured out if he got banned or was given any other violation log, a couple months later, i checked his violation logs, and he was given no ban or anything, i even gave the mods a clip of his username and info with video proof of him shooting me and cussing me out, so the moderation could be better.

- MICROPHONES: i dont know if its the playerbase or the voice chat, but you will often be trying to get a situation report from someone on the frontline or just generally talking to someone, and when they activate their microphone in the foxhole voice chat, it often can sound loud and unintelligible, this isnt for all players but for some its a very bad problem and for others, its a minuscule or non-existent problem.

you also might come across a small amount of other problems ranging from rare bugs to grumpy players, but the game is really fun and, at the moment, is thriving.

thats my overall review of the game, now, finally, go help the wardens (and only the wardens) win all the wars!!\t\t\t\t\t
