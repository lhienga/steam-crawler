\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm still pretty new to the game (17hrs at time of writing), so I haven't been able to explore all the features the game has to offer. From just looking at the game it was clear I shouldn't immediately run to the front and start hip firing madly until I figured it out, that would just be wasting the effort, resources and manufacturing other players had put into it. So I decided to explore the logistical side and see how the sausage was made. I recommend any new players do the same! There's not an insane amount of production materials, which makes gathering things pretty nice and you don't have to go looking up a 2 billion stage recipe with all kind of specialist parts, anything you produce can be used in a number of places.

I was absolutely blown away by how welcoming everyone is in this game, you ARE still on the internet believe it or not!

The tutorials for the game are sadly lacking, but there's plenty of sources to learn this game outside the tutorial, both in the game and out so don't worry too much. Vehicle driving physics are a little odd, but you get used to it. I might appreciate a wider FOV when well behind the front lines, but I can't think of a way they'd implement that without introducing a meta conflict, so I can't reasonably expect them to add it. I wouldn't try looking ahead logically for a new player, for example you get diesel from salvage and petrol from an oil well. Look up what you need then go get it!\t\t\t\t\t
