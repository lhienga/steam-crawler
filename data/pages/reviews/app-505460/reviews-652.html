\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOriginal review: After over 400+ hours in this game I just cannot recommend it anymore. I love the game and there is some jank to it that you kind of love and gives it a special place, like the buggy terrain. I've over looked all of that and I still cannot recommend the game. The main reason being the community. Hoooooly ♥♥♥♥ the community sucks. I typically play solo, even in a tank. I am actually quite effective playing solo in a tank. I farm the RMats, fuel, bmats, and rounds, so my choice to do what I want with my tank right? Wrong. The biggest enemy on the battlefield was my own team. MUETE is a clan that especially ranks high on this offense list. Constantly being told to not drive my tank up by myself, being told that "I'm an idiot.", even to the point that they were willing to destroy my tank and any and all fortifications I set up. I mentioned that I farmed for the mats, I bought that game, and I'll play any ******* way I want. Then they threatened to get their entire clan mass vote ban me. As for a side note I've never had a problem with French people in other games and I generally have a positive view of French people as a whole, but the French in this game. Bruh, I've never seen a group of people that would get soooo pissed off with me being solo in a tank than the godd***** French dude. Even the non-MEUTE guys were still really bad. The French weren't the only ones to do this, Americans, Germans, etc all did as well, but no one took it to the level the French guys did. It seemed almost like a personal insult to them if I was allowed to solo tank. Constant b******* and complaining. Even though it cost them nothing if my tank and resources were lost. To this day I have yet to have a tank stolen from me. I would have people run up to my tank and tell me to unlock the tank that they would gun for me. I was polite and said, "No thanks, I solo this b****." People would become irate with me for not letting them in my tank that I worked for. I was solo in a mortar truck shelling some front lines when a MEUTE guy drives his tank behind my mortar half track while I'm trying to retreat to safety and ensures my halftrack is destroyed by placing a nearly point blank shell into the rear of my halftrack. I was farming for some RMats when a French clan, PARS I think it was, rolled up and threatened me, they wanted the component field for an operation and if I didn't let them have it then I would die and get vote banned. I witnessed 2 Chinese men, smoke some poor fool in the garage for his RMats, killed him when he returned while his friend built a tank from this dude's RMats. Large clans will farm for days and hold on to thousands of tech just to be the first one on the field with a prototype kit, just make all tech perma public for f**** sake. I'm going to try wardens next war. Perhaps they are bit more appreciative of people just trying to have fun and do their own thing.

Additional Review: After some more game play I've noticed some glaring issues. The population balancing is trash. Every front line on the weekend has a player queue. Expect to wait constantly to get into the action. Even moving supplies to the area is hampered by the population balancing issue. Infantry weapons are woefully inaccurate while tanks, rockets, and cannon are lasers. The gun play is horrendous. For example, I can load up an RPG and fire at a stationary target and boom it hits 100% of the time so long as I'm in range. All small arms in this game feel like spitball guns. The new assault for the colonials is an absolute ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ trashcan of a weapon. 40 round of not hitting ♥♥♥♥. The warden smg, the liar, is far superior to this so-called assault rifle. The only thing it assaults is my common sense.

An enemy player can weave through gun fire to charge tanks while 3 infantry are spraying at him but nothing hits! I've been in cover, standing, prone, kneeling, behind cover, in front of cover, kissed my bullets and tried throwing them at the enemy (sadly it's more effective), and nothing is accurate. It just ruins the game for me. Pistols are better at close range than most of the smgs, or at least comparable, what the ♥♥♥♥ is that kind of design? The gun play is full of so much ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ it's dumb. How would you like to flank an enemy, get the jump on him, fire 4-6 well placed and timed shots, watch all miss, then get 2 tapped by a pistol? This game punishes you for wanting to play for skill. Do you want to go out and kill multiple people because you have better aim and tactically smarter? Nope, prepare to be humbled by your inability to hit nothing. I was in the army and if I was handed 40 rounds and I didn't hit ♥♥♥♥ at 35m they would have kicked me the ♥♥♥♥ out. Gun play gets a fat 0/10 for me. The small arms in this game are worse than ass cancer.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe only games I have played that can even SLIGHTLY compare to this title is Planetside 1 & 2 , and WWII Online. Both titles being decade or decades old. There is few or no games in this genre. The MMO war genre. This game is basically a top down view ultimate dynamic fully player controlled " Battlefield " like game. Land, sea and naval combat even. There is a ton of weapons, items, vehicles and assets at your disposal. Not to mention many avenues of play to go down. From your basic rifleman, SMG , AT man and Medic all the way to Snipers Engineers and other specialty classes. And if your into vehicles, there is tens of them. From light vehicles, transports, to medium and heavy tanks and the like. Not to mention there is also mortars, artillery of different varieties and sizes too. There is almost unlimited ways to contribute to this games persistent player driven war. Every gun you use, bullet you fire and vehicle you drive was at one point crafted by a REAL player. The engagements rage for hours, sometimes tens of hours. The battles for certain areas can go on for extended periods of times for even days on end. The wars themselves can last weeks and weeks on end. And all of it is impacted, controlled , coordinated and communicated via the playerbase on either factions ranks. I've been playing PC games and gaming in general since the 1990's and there has NEVER been a " War Simulator " like this as far as I know. Hundreds of people on the battlefield at once (500+). It's no FPS so if your looking for a Arma or Squad like experience you won't find it here. However it has the teamplay, organization and mass orchestrated tactical play that I don't think you can experience anywhere else. Planetside may have the battles raging with aircraft, tanks and infantry and such but it cannot touch what Foxhole has to offer. Foxhole as a game has 1000% more depth, features, and is way more challenging than Planetside 2. In PS2 you may see a battle with 150 people. Tanks battling, infantry rushing into the domes to capture them and all that. But in Foxhole you have so much going on at once. There is people fighting with SMG's and nades at the front, battling CQB. Just behind them a row of riflemen fire at the enemy. And with the riflemen are player constructed machine gun and cannon nests, bunkers and sandbags. Behind that line once again lies another. This time it's a line of armored light tanks and vehicles, providing fire support along with the infantry at the front. Now behind the light tanks are the heavy tanks and mobile AT guns rolling up. And once again it continues. Behind those tanks are now siege artillery, mortars and other fire support players lining up to help the advance with their barrages. And it doesn't stop there, the depth of the war never ends. Behind even those siege cannons, and mortars and such is the base. Housing huge howitzer cannons and other powerful equipment. I could go on and on but even with under 10 hours in the game I can tell it is unique, insanely detailed and challenging as well as fun. Would strongly suggest having a microphone and joining a clan. This is the last game you want to play solo. It's almost impossible to play by yourself in any effective manor in fact.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game surely has its faults, it's still being developed and new features break the game on occasion, I still remember the first relic war. There are toxic players but those exist in every game and over time I have seen less and less of them (probably helped by the fact that you can't hear the enemy's voice chat anymore). That doesn't mean that there aren't exceptions and sometimes even good players can be ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ but there are plenty of folks who are just there having fun and are willing to teach new people.

Don't be afraid to try Foxhole, but if I were to say the best time to join it would be during the Resistance Phase, basically a time between proper wars where players can just run around in a conflict that won't affect the next big war. In there you can get a feel for the game and learn from the few veterans who are running around. Vehicle combat is definitely something you should figure out in this time as teamwork between spots in a vehicle really makes a difference when the war starts.

This game has a ton to offer people and I would really like to see it grow and become more healthy, maybe not all at once though. The servers can't handle sudden huge growth very well.

PS. This game is a huge time dump, the battles never end if both sides have good logi running around keeping the fronts fed so be careful or you might end up fighting over a bridge or a barren field for four hours.\t\t\t\t\t
