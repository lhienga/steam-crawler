You are just one person in the war, and lots and lots of people die, constantly. That includes you, a lot.

Walking back from spawn point takes a long time. Building takes a long time. Supplies takes a long time. Everything takes a long time.

Long range combat is somewhat difficult due to the top down nature, but once you get the hang of it, its cool. I think its a great mechanic, some people hate it. If you can't deal with the inherent nature of top down orthographic view, why get the game?

Requires selfless teamwork selfishly. Almost everything requires a huge amount of team effort... You can't just get equipment or vehicles and run out and waste them without hurting the team.

The glory is in the battlefield, but the real significance comes from the logistics. Sadly, logistics people never get enough credit or praise so they eventually get jaded and toxic.

One of the best games, but needs lots of players due to the logistics.\t\t\t\t\t
