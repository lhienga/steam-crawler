\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite needing some QOL improvements well documented by the community, the scope and execution of the game generally hits the nail on the head for me.

If you're into playing solo, there is some sneaky partisan work to do, or deliver supplies to the front line!

If you're into teamwork, find a Regiment, and conduct multi-discipline attack operations. Or find some randoms and just tag along.

Tasks available:
Scrooping (gathering materials)
-Use a combination of vehicles and tools to get that scrap

Logistics
-Haulers, flatbeds, shipping containers, freight trains and bulk naval transport. Lots of ways to move goods.

Partisan
-Disrupt the enemy operations. Destroy buildings, steal vehicles, dump enemy materials on ground to despawn. Block a bridge with a boat.
Blockade a necessary supply line!

Combat
-Rifles, pistols, fisticuffs
-Grenades, grenade launchers
-Mortars, artillery, don't forget your binoculars!
-Tanks, half tracks, jeeps with RPGs!
-Trains? Yes. Train combat.
-Anti tank field guns
-Gun boats, amphibious APCs, landing ships.

Not everyone is going to like the game. But. You can invest 20 minutes or 16 hours and never run out of things to keep you busy.

Has very few bugs.
Occasionally get stuck on terrain
Queueing to another region can take some time.

I think the purchase price is a bit steep, but I would purchase it for the current price, because I play it and like it.

No micro transactions.
No subscriptions.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's...fun, but not for casual gamers or anyone who likes to see any progress in general. Kinda fun at times but definitely wouldn't recommend if you have literally anything else to do at all in your life. The main tips you get in this game are mostly how to have fun despite the game design. Logistics is BS, frontline is wasteful, satchels actually remove fun from the game, the whole game is just a pit to throw your time into. You could EASILY spend legit hours and hours just watching your little guy swinging a hammer or driving a truck literally across the map because the facility layout doesn't make any real-world sense. You refine your stuff into material and then make items to stock frontline bases, but oh wait, *2* guys just waltzed up and detonated all your hard work off the map, which could then take real-world DAYS to get back. All this just adds up to drag you out of the game into realizing you're wasting your one precious life on some make-believe 'war.' The dopamine loop is strong in this game but don't let that trick you into thinking you're having fun.\t\t\t\t\t
