\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA unique experience that really captures the chaos of war. A true sandbox, you make your own missions here. Want to "Run Logi" and supply the run lines for commends? Get yourself a supply truck and have at it. Want to group up with friends and slip past the front lines to sabotage the enemies logistics? You can. Build a tank, crew it with a few guys, head to the front lines, get to blasting. Or maybe build a mortar truck, and blast away at the trenches with your mortar team.

The community is pretty great so far, lots of voice chat, helpful people, and the commend systems works fairly well. Though I wish the commend system involved combat a little more. Like if I just put down loads of bunkers and trenches with bmats, it should pop-up for a commendation just like delivering logistics does. Or you just landed a mortar barrage that took out 10+ guys, same deal, a commend pop up box should come up for those around you. It's very difficult to get commends for anything besides logistics currently.

It can also be a bit laggy at times. I've died a couple times to lag spikes and it is annoying for sure.

I wouldn't sleep on this one, get it!\t\t\t\t\t
