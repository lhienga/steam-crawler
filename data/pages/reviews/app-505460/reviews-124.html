\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst, I love playing this game with my friends because there's so much opportunity for teamwork. Even better, teamwork contributes to the long-term goal of winning the war! There are so many awesome roles and tools to use that we can switch things up all the time. When my friends aren't able to play, I can team up with almost anyone because 90% of the players are friendly and looking to work together.

I could leave it there and say it's worth a buy, but my personal favorite part about the game is the economy. The idea that every single gun, bullet, grenade, medkit, car, tank, artillery piece, shell, boat, train, building, and more is mind-blowing and adds a weight to what you use in a fight. The idea that players have to maintain a supply line to keep the war machine going was something I was attracted to. So, I play in this corp of players (shout out FMAT!) that deliver supplies to the front lines so that everyone can keep fighting. The cheers as I deliver ammo to a desperate front keep me invested in their success and I convince myself to stay locked in for hours on end. It's such a unique core game mechanic that I really struggle to find anything similar elsewhere.

I recommend this game to a lot of people, and I'd recommend it to you too.\t\t\t\t\t
