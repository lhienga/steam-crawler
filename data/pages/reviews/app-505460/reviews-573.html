\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had a friend recommend me this game, but after looking at gameplay, I thought this game would be slow, and boring. I was also worried about combat. I thought that I would just be shot by another player who I could not see because he was off screen.

I'm glad that I can say that I was wrong. Combat can be slow or fast; it depends on your combat situation. Sometimes you're pushing through territory in a massive Blitzkrieg, sometimes you're engaged in static warfare, with both sides building trenches to try to engage each other instead of heading topside above the trenches where you would get shelled or mowed down, sometimes your running spec ops, destroying enemy equipment and logistics behind enemy lines, and sometimes you're running errands to deliver groceries to your local bunker. There is a lot you can do in this game, from the common foot soldier, to running logistics, or building bases.

This game has 2 factions, the Colonials or the Wardens. Both are inspired from WW1 and WW2 armies, Specifically, the U.S. Army and the French Army. The Colonials (My faction) are the U.S. Inspired faction. m1 helmets, rifles that look like M1 Garands or M14s, etc. The Wardens are inspired from the French. Their Helmets, rifles, tanks, etc, are all influenced by the French.

So what faction do you join?
Join the faction that you think is cool. They all have their own pros and cons but in the end, join the team, that you think is cool.

Once you join one of the factions, you will be deployed and here is where your efforts can change an ever changing war. Fronts are always advancing or retreating. always work with others around you and play as a team. The community is a very friendly group, and you're likely to meet some friends along the way.
I do recommend that you have a mic but if not, text chat is viable solution but generally listen to your superiors, they will help you.

There's so much more to this game but for me, the fact that this game is a living persistent war is amazing. barely any games do this and the only other game I can think of is Planetside 2 but Foxhole is NOT Planetside 2, I personally think it's better. there's more to do and there are plenty of ways where you can help others out.

So, what are you waiting for, ENLIST TODAY!\t\t\t\t\t
