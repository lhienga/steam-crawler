\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely a game where you should NOT invest your time.

If you're playing casually it's a good time. The combat is passable and the cooperation which the game encourages feel really good when it pays off.

If you stick around and devote your time the shortcomings of the game really start to become unavoidable. The lack of substantial punishment or moderation means that griefing could potentially ruin large amounts of progress made and toxicity can run through the games communication systems, but the main issue is it's development itself. Balance is wack and the new features seem barely tested at all making the players the games Q.A (Quality Assurance[Game Testers]). Finally the core fundamentals of the non combat side of the game are legacy systems with no Q.O.L(Quality Of Life) making it both frustrating and a monotonous grind.

As said at the start foxhole can be enjoyable but only if you take it casually.

TLDR(To Long Didn't Read) do not play this game unless you have cash to spare. Even if you do take it casually unless you 'play'(sell your soul to) games like EVE in which case still don't since it's just worse.

Addendum: Griefing is now more handle able and balance is slightly less wack(for now). Non combat is still up too 2000's level standards 4/10 Help I can't stop playing.\t\t\t\t\t
