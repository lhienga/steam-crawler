Imagine sitting in a trench or bunker for 10 or 15 minutes at a time just staring at the empty field between you and your enemy's trenches while nothing happens. Or dying if you so much as pop your head up in sight of the 5 guys doing the same on the other side. Imagine almost never having basic, essential things like ammo or grenades and having to scavenge enemy corpses because their side does have those things. Or spending hours driving a truck around to gather raw materials and then waiting 10 minutes for the queue in the factory to clear so you can finally produce supplies for the front and drive them up.

I think by automating more of the production process and making the setting up of supply chains more engaging, rather than requiring players to spend their time driving back and forth and starting the war with some simple weapons unlocked the game would benefit immensely. As it is now, it's basically just a chore to play.\t\t\t\t\t
