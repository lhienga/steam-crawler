WhY WoUlD AnY oNe PlAy ThIs GaMe?

this game is not Hyper balanced
this game is not fair
this game will kick you in the balls

so why play it? This is a WAR game you dont have to have have the reflexes of a squirrel on crack to do well in this game, in fact you can play this game for hundreds of hours without even firing a rifle, and still be contributing to the war effort. How is this possible you ask? to put it simply you are what you wear in this game.

There is no "Medic/support" Class in fact there are no classes in this game so lets talk about roles

ROLES

First things first Fighter role
what does some one in the fighter role do?

Fighters go out in the front lines and....fight, they stab they shoot they blow ♥♥♥♥ up and then they die.

Medics

what does a medic do? well unlike in real life whose job it is to make you more comfortable as you die. a medic in Foxhole is able to get people back on their feet and in fighting shape in only a few short seconds

they are able to heal people with First aid kits FAKs as i call them use Bandages as a resource to heal

What happens when some one gets downed?

a medic is able to pick people off the ground using a Trauma kit . trauma kits use blood plasma as a resource to pick up wounded troopers

Combat Engineers

Combat engineers are the backbone of the front line, without them building foxholes (AI and maned firing position to help keep the area secure for Logistics to bring things in and fighters to respawn.

Combat engineers are able to build and even dig modular bunker systems making outpost for more info please check on youtube to see a more details about how building things work.

Logistics or Logi

what is the logi role? they are people who like playing games like truck simulator or watching videos while playing with one hand and use auto clickers (since the game does not have a cruise control....STILL)

Basically they pick things up from point A take them to Point B. Take things from Point B back to Point A. Some logi players even mine their own resources to produce weapons and ammo for fighters Medical supplies for medics and building materials for combat engineers . if a front line has little to no Logi that front line WILL fall. now you are thinking what dangers do Logi drivers face being so far behind from the front lines? that leads us to our next role...

COMMANDO

what does it take to be a commando you ask? (no i am not calling them ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ PARTISANS) well if you want to drink the tears of logi drivers blow up bridges, respawn points, factories and even call in a NUKE strike?

Boiled down commandos go behind the front lines to make life hell for the poor bastards who live back there (careful if you make to much noise behind enemy lines they will send a quick reaction force or QRF most likely a armored car or halftrack to come deal with you!!) who drives armed cars and halftracks you ask? well that leads up to our next role...

Crewmen

what is a crewmen? when they operate weapons of war such as little tanks, big tanks, half tracks, armored cars, armored personal carriers and even gunboats!

They run people over and blow things up!

Crewmen can help a front line that is in a stalemate by crossing no mans land and breaking through the enemy!

what would create such a dangerous place such as no mans land? that is covered in our final role...

Artillery

as the old saying goes Artillery is king

what does arty do? they blow ♥♥♥♥ up that is wayyyyy over there from over here. you name it they got it. 80mm mortars, 150mm howitzers or even the 300mm STORMCANON nothing says BIG. ♥♥♥♥. ENERGY. like hearing this storm canon fire from over 700m away and then it landing in front of you and watching over half a dozen men die enough said.

now does this mean you only have to do one of these things at a time? NO. you can mix and match these roles till you are blue in the face, this is only the stuff i could come up with off the top of my head.

i have played this game for over 400 hours and will continue to play it until it surpasses as my most played game (ARMA 3 2,826 HOURS as of writing this)

what are the Cons it cant all be BADASS right?

You will die , not only in real life but in this game as well. but unlike in real life when you die in the game you learn a bit from that life and can apply it to your next one, you will learn how to shoot well, when to advance when to retreat and when to cook your nades

this game is not for every one if you cant handle it i would recommended trying some of these games listed here

-Hello kitty online
-shower with your dad simulator 2015
-Disney Princess: My Fairytale Adventure
-My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic (video game)
if you have any suggestions please feel free to put them in the comments below

I have no issues teaching new people on how to play the game the best way for me to teach you how to play is joining me while im Live streaming foxhole on twitch @

Nothing you do has any impact, everything interesting is locked behind grind, every single one of your lives is boring and meaningless, and the community is awful.
As I see it, there are 2 main problems, and they're both very common.
First; the game tries to do everything. It's got a resource mining, refining, and manufacturing element, a logistics delivery system, and front line combat. By spreading itself so thin, it does all of them badly. If you have a desire to play any of these things, there are specialized games that do it lightyears better.
Second; even if it weren't spread too thin, the game is badly designed. As just one example, take a look at its early-access schedule. It says the game will be in early access for about 1 year. That was 3 years ago. Is it three times as good as they had planned to make it? No.

I bought it to try, and thought I'd make a decision in the 2 hour refund period. But then I didn't really get to experience anything in the first 2 hours, so I exceeded my time limit. I thought it was because I didn't know how to play very well yet, and (as I mentioned above) the game is always hinting that there might be some fun if you just go a little longer! So I decided to keep trying. Now I've played this long, and I can tell you. There's no fun there. It would actually be an interesting con if I thought it was done on purpose, but I don't think it was. I think the developers just caught on to an idea for a game that could be fun, but they don't really know how to make a good game.

So why are some people so committed to it? Well, the game has a splash screen when you launch it that says something to the effect that "the players are the content," and I think that explains it. If you can convince someone that they're doing things for other people, if they haven't got the judgment to evaluate the claim, they'll just bond straight away, and endure all sorts of things for that "community." The fact that the game experience isn't very good is actually helpful; it's the hazing phenomenon that binds together fraternities, and real soldiers. If you endure something painful, the mind likes to decide "well, then it must've been worth it." Because if it wasn't worth it, that means you were an idiot who was tricked into suffering.

But the game isn't torture. It's not like having bamboo shoots shoved under your fingernails. But it's not fun. And every time I've finished playing and closed the game, I've immediately regretted spending any time on it. The point is, there are better things to do with your time. Better games. Better communities. Better pursuits. And we're all gonna die some day; do you really want to spend 1000 hours chasing some illusive promise of possible "fun" in a mess like this?\t\t\t\t\t
