\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm making this review after my first play session (of 2 hours) for the people who'd like a newbie's view.

The game is pretty fun. I got so into defending a single bridge that I forgot that my fight was only a small part of the whole map. It's like Planetside 2 but, like a real war, there's never someone wiping out entire platoons on his own.

I got kind of confused about how certain things work, like how to get weapons out of barracks and the resources that uses. Had to figure out on my own how medic bags work while under fire, turns out they work like guns with how they reload using bandages after they've been emptied.

A lot of people use voice chat, which is good for communication, people like to use it to call for a medic, but nobody will blame you for not using it since chat messages show up above your head as well. Everybody I've met while hopping battlefields have been pretty nice, explaining me what I had to do and press without ever complaining about my lack of knowledge.

The game's mechanics don't go very deep and it still feels kind of buggy to climb things like rocks, getting out of the water is a ♥♥♥♥♥, but that's to be expected from an early access game.
