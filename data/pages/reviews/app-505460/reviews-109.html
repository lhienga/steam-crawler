It is sort of like actually being in some kind of war, in a sense. You are free to do pretty much whatever you want, awesome!!! There are no leveling up, no super powers, no BS. Many types of held weapons, mounted weapons, and vehicles. Machine guns, RPG's, mortars, you name it. One of my favorite things to do is grab an anti-tank rifle and go punching holes on the front lines. If you are looking for a super hero action packed game, this ain't for you. You could be sitting in a pill box for hours waiting for the enemy to show up. You could be in the trenches on the front lines, popping heads. You could be manning the turret on a tank, or using the machine gun to mow down the advancing enemy. Maybe you are not enough grunt to murder digital soldiers. You can always mine and manufacture weapons and vehicles and other supplies for the war effort. Maybe your thing is to hunt down enemy snipers and then run up to watch them die in bloody gory agony. You can do that in this game.

The only thing it is missing is the corner hut brothels and the bars. Add that in and they would have a really fine simulator, lol.\t\t\t\t\t
