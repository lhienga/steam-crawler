\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn my time in foxhole, this is what I've learnt:

-Very accepting community, with the few hints of toxic players every now and again.
-Even if you're low level, you can still lead a small group. It just takes some confidence and a little bit of a raised voice and next thing you know, you're sending lads off to their untimely deaths.
-A lot you can do, regardless of rank or status. You can go from Infantry to a tanker to a logi driver and then back to infantry in the same play session because there's never enough of anything, especially brave infantry.
-Those who complain about the community, one hundred percent earned that animosity and this is coming from someone who has never had beef, never joined a regiment and never interacted with community beyond a few memey map posts that I'd delete if I was blocking actually important info.
-The people WILL help you without a second thought, they will point you in the right direction or outright teach the basics, all you need to do is ask and make sure to listen.
-Wardens and Collies, while they do "Hate" each other, they don't actually go on witch hunts to find a collie player who swapped to wardens for one war so take those loyalists very lightly.
-There is a good amount of small bugs, i.e getting stuck on destroyed trenches, barbed wire, and foxholes, but they are spread out and not at all game ruining or breaking.
-There is no shortage of action, if you head to a dead front, just simply head back to a home region and try again because there is always somewhere where the fighting is tough and they need bodies.

If you've read this far, firstly, thank you for your time and attention and secondly, you really should try this game, it's fun and always has something for you to do. There's no shortage of job openings for tankers, logi drivers, and scrappers. so strap up your boots and get ready to get bloody because this game is definitely worthy of your dollars and time.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very fun, deep dive, high skill ceiling game. There is so much to learn and so many moving pieces. Fox maximum enjoyment, join an active squad with vets and put forth an effort to learn. You can definitely play Foxhole casually, but I would highly advice that you at least practice some etiquette.

1) Do not take a vehicle or supplies from a bunker base. Odds are someone spent hours harvesting materials and driving it to the front line. Public vehicles can be found in seaports. OR you can make your own!

2) Do not take take mass supplies from a bunker base. They were placed there for the sole purpose of defending that base. Public supplies can be found in seaports and storage depots. OR you can make your own!

3) Do not build bases or dig trenches without some guidance first. These actions can very easily harm the integrity of a base... they can also assist the enemy in attacking a base. You may think that digging a trench or foxhole is the name of the game.. but they can easily be used against you. There is a good way of doing it, and a bad way.

The MAIN problem I see in this game is espionage. Whether it be alt accounts from the enemy side... or just ignorance from a new player... there seems to be a LOT of sabotage. I have witnessed with my own eyes, brand new players parking vehicles and equipment in front of pillboxes, taking down barb wire, and removing base supplies in order to aide the enemy attack. I have seen teammates drive vehicles and supplies to the enemy front and then bail out to gift them our equipment. When we work together to report these TOS infractions... WE GET BANNED AS WELL (This is NOT OK). The devs MUST fix this issue. It has bloomed to epidemic proportions. We NEED a way to lock certain supplies into a bunker base. We NEED a way to report espionage. IRL, spies would be killed... in Foxhole, the same spy comes back after a 3 day ban. And they usually bring friends the next time. This game will be unplayable if Foxhole continues to be relaxed with TOS infractions.

PS... the border base system sucks.\t\t\t\t\t
