\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole has been a heck of a ride since i first bought it. I had originally gotten it back when they still had legacy (star) bases, but shortly before they moved to the newer hex system.

The game itself is face paced, heart pounding, one wrong step can get you poked full of holes by the enemy.
The design is inovative, intuitive, fun, and very interesting. It very much reminds me of Command & Conquer: Red Alert games due to its top down visual style mixed with World War gameplay.
The gameplay itself is interesting, in Foxhole, being a lone wolf can get you a ways, and is good if you dont mind dying over and over, or if your the lurking type (spy, sniper, scout, ect)
But the gameplay really shines once you hop into an operation, you dont need to have friends, mind you, just hope into an operation and work with the others in said OP to achive your goals. When working as a team the game gets more complicated and more interesting and more fun.

Combat not your thing? Well OK! Join one of the Scrapper battalions, or run Logistics yourself in a truck.
You can sit on a backline base an collected needed materials for the front line bases, Scrap, Explosives, fuel.
Or collect upgrade parts, used to upgrade structures making them tougher and more dangerous.
If you dont want to sit around hammering on a pile of scrap you can always build a truck, load it with supplies (ammunitions, medical supplies, spawn kits which are called soldier supplies) and haul it to the front lines to keep your allies supplied and able to continue to fight at top form!
