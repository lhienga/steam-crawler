\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played Foxhole for quite some time now and have put in a lot of hours in ever aspect of the game. The game has enormous potential and because of this you will see a LOT of people around with thousands of hours in this game. Unfortunately the developers haven't been able to make it work. Foxhole is a very intricate game with a lot of complex systems for building and logistics, all designed to support the combat of the game, this is what makes it unique. Unfortunately the developers have not just failed to address their failures to make the systems work without causing massive amounts of burnout, they have outright refused to.

Every update builds upon existing problems, adding more content and thus more workload. Due to software limitations they cannot make the servers handle more assets than they already do, which means builders have to fight the game itself in order to keep the assets they built alive. Logisticians are at war with the enemy and their own team for control of the limited resources. Both groups eventually burnout throughout a wars duration because the hours and effort they must put in to help their faction are both numerous and boring.

You might ask why it is people continue to play when the game and its developers don't respect the time of the player. The answer is that Foxhole is a one of a kind experience that most of us love the idea of, and have had a lot of hope for. We all constantly keep trying to bring in friends to experience it with us, because we have put so much effort into this game and have have had amazing moments. Ultimately though, the longer you play the fewer amazing moments occur, and it just becomes a mental and emotional drain.

As a last note, the game has a significant problem with griefers, alts, and plain abysmal behavior by the player base. The cross-faction and intra-faction is not getting any better, and the developers can't seem to find out how to keep people from making accounts on the opposing team to destroy their painstakingly constructed assets in cheap and unsporting ways. As of this time, unless significant changes occur to improve quality of life I cannot advise anyone purchase Foxhole. If you are still curious, I would suggest staying away from construction or logistics for the sake of your time and mental health.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR at the bottom. The game has had its ups and downs throughout the year, but the last 2 years have been slowly adding on to the down part and I cannot recommend it in its current state. The game used to be built on the principle of nobody's actions alone were significant, but as a whole they all mattered. Now the only actions that matter are those of organized in-game clans.

These clans have both the capability and the wherewithal to monopolize most of the game's activities outside of holding a rifle and charging towards a trench. Activities that could reliably be completed in a single play session and/or with a small group of 5 or fewer like building a base, building and driving tanks, assaulting a flank, or doing basically anything that is tangible longer than a few minutes has become virtually impossible due to onerous game mechanics being added that can only be surmounted by collective, organized, and large group effort. The time of the solo and small-group player is over. And the game is not fun without that capability without dumping 3-4 of hours into it every day, which people with schedules can't maintain.

TLDR: The game requires either huge groups of players or 3-4 hours of daily play to accomplish anything making it virtually impossible for people with jobs to play in small groups or by themselves. Do not buy.\t\t\t\t\t

Why is my review negative? Because the devs have signaled that they do not care about the playerbase at all. Due to how the game works you have to have a high trust in your community to not tamper with equipment produced for the fight or you have to have moderation tools in place to fight against bad actors who are looking to play the game in bad spirit. Foxhole has neither and its killed my enjoyment of this once great game.

There's evidence a of accounts with no stats dumping tanks into the ocean to destroy them or dumping supplies from a base out right before an attack or leaving gates open so enemy partisans can have access to a base.

The worst punishment that person will face is a temporary suspension between 3 and 7 days. That's it. Hours and hours of work from people playing the game legitimately undone all for that person to only be temporary banned. These temp bans don't ever escalate to being permanent either. Users violation logs are visible and multiple accounts exist with 20+ temp suspensions. It's a complete joke.

The worst part of this though? The devs haven't said a word on the situation. They control the official reddit and will ban you from the reddit for calling out alters with clear evidence. They are quick to ban there but not for their own game. They absolutely are aware of the concerns and do not give a ♥♥♥♥.

My recommendation is to not buy this game ever. You'll get invested like I did only to be completely let down by a developer team who would rather sweep the issues under the rug than address them head on.
