\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I'm still fairly new to the game at the time of review, but I've really enjoyed my experience so far. It's certainly the most unique war game I think I've ever played. There's something special about seeing and being part of a large group of players working towards something, be it trying to capture a strategic point or loading an artillery gun. The logistics element to me is what really makes the game stand out, how pretty much every resource players can use in the game are crafted or gathered by other players. Truly there are many roles to fill besides shooting on the front lines. Truck driver? Yeah. Artillery gunner? Sure. Builder? No problem.

There has been a little toxicity, but in my personal experience it has been completely negligible compared to the helpful and welcoming members of the community. In the end I think this has been money well spent and I'm confident this will be a game I will continue to come back to in the years to come.
