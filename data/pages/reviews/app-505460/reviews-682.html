\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL,DR: Great game if you like being a small cog in a big war machinery where your individual contribution is nearly unnoticable, every tool used has to be worked for and everything depends on collective collaboration.

A great world war 1 simulator. Two factions, colonials and wardens, standing off on a persistent world map divided into regions, fighting for dominance on the map until one faction holds all victory conditions (static bases on the map).
The game is not quick! If youre looking for a lobby shooter experience where you just hop in and start fighting you won't be happy here. Everything has to be done by players, you collect basic resources at nodes, have to ship them to a refinery, from there ship them to factories and then ship them to the front. Logistics is an important part of the game.
And during fighting everything is not only a question of tactics, but also resource efficiency, since every weapon, clip of ammo, grenade, vehicle and static defense has a cost and needs to be delivered by logistic players and everything is shared by all players.\t\t\t\t\t
