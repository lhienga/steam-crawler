\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am changing the review to a negative one.

Short reason: game has no respect for player time and has a severe lack of quality-of-life features.

Long reason:

This game still has large appeal and what made it originally good is still there except it is now watered down and hidden behind repetitive, boring and mind numbing tasks.

Logistics is critical for success in this game but developers appear to have tried their best to make the entire process as tedious, annoying and time consuming as they possibly could. As a matter of fact it feels like they did their best to make anything you do in this game take longer than it should.

Only way to automate logistics a bit is locked behind a rare resource (oil) that everyone wants because noone wishes to do logistics so there are constant internal fights within the faction. There is theft. There is teamkilling. Only thing there isnt is a way to prevent it.

Players that wanted to build tanks and do armored pushes on the frontline now need to beg strangers for advanced tank variants or need to invest ungodly amount of time into planning, building and maintaining a facility and even then they could simply be robbed and not even get to use their tank.

If you wanted to relax a bit from the repetitive job that this game's logistics is and head to the frontline you will be met by a 10-15 min queue if you are lucky. Hell, even if you are just delivering the stuff you built there is a decent chance you will sit in a queue only for the game to crash and send you to main menu with you having lost all your equipment during region transfer.

Problem with queues is, ironically, that population is too low so developers artificially restrict number of players that the more numerous faction can have in a region which leaves regions empty yet locked behind a queue.

Meanwhile, game has 3 servers running, as Tolkien put it 'like too little butter spread over too much bread'.

In essence, BUYER BEWARE.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing concept, great community, great visuals and sound but it seems like the game was designed around having the player do as much tedious tasks as possible. Generally in games i like building, logistics, strategy, etc. All the before mentioned are just a tedious autoclicker simulator in this game which feel like i got a worse second job. There is no automation in the game which is a huge negative for this type of game and there are huge balancing issues between the factions.
If you join the collies there will be a lot of tryharding but not in the good way as the community refuses to build and use their PVE tools because it's easier to complain about not having the cutler/outlaw/silverhand/chieftain.
If you join the wardens on the other hand there will be a lot "skill issue" where the infantry is slaughtered constantly with no way to retaliate (because the collies PVP is just better in every way), so you are just left with waiting and hoping to reach late game armor tech which will win the game because collies won't have more than 2-3 tanks on a front at a time(again easier to complain about warden armor being op instead of building smelters and bardiches).

I played both factions along 5 wars and I think that the asymmetric balance sucks in PVP and logistic/builder work are just a tedium that should be changed in pretty much all aspects.

Update: Facilities changed nothing in terms of tediousness of the logi/engineering activity. They just made more sinkholes for resources that need to maintained for no reason(because they are utterly useless until mid to late war)
Small gauge railway makes for a slow and underwhelming way to transport resources and is just tedious to drive around manually(unless trains can autopilot to destination they are yet another tedious thing).
Proper trains are locked behind late game tech and non-clan or casual players will never have any reason to use them.
There is still no automation possible (using pipes is 0.5% of the possible automation let's be real).
Building as ever sucks and is composed of autoclicking and with the addition of facilities and rails you just have to autoclick more.

Update: Since the latest update, an attempt was made by the dev to address most of the quality of life issues, with debatable results. Overall the game is improving slowly but surely\t\t\t\t\t
