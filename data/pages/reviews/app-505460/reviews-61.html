\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played the game for about 10 days. First thing - the community there is AMAZING. People are genuinely nice, responsive, explain the game, it's easy to form groups and have some nice team play. Team play is actually everything in this game, you cannot do ♥♥♥♥ without other players. It's complex and demanding and there is no good entry point, you will learn the hard way. It's not immediately pleasant, and you have to invest tens of hours to develop skills and understanding. In this sense it is a truly great game.

But, having said all that, it is hard to enjoy it. If you want to do anything it just takes a lot of time. The game loop for catching a gun and running towards an enemy is good for a couple of sessions, but then it becomes boring. Most of the time you spend running, choosing equipment, waiting to spawn. If you want to take part in the logistics operations, then you will be driving from one side of the map to another. The game looks good, people are nice, you feel like you are taking part in an important and large operation, and you want to contribute, but then you realise it takes good 30 minutes to build a truck, then to process scrap material, bring it to a factory, make some guns and then another 15 minutes to deliver it to a front line. And this is only for the most basic items. Anything more complicated is like playing Factorio. It is not fun if you don't want to dedicate some serious time into it, and coordinate what you do with others. It's a big game, and a big world, it takes a lot of effort to build a tank or anything bigger. Building trenches and other structures is incredibly boring - you essentially have to hold a mouse button pressed down for 2-3 minutes to create anything, and that's the time you cannot spend on doing anything else. I'd say it would be a little bit more enjoyable if you could just press a button and leave for the necessary time for your soldier to finish the job. It is simply boring. It does get better when there is a team with you, that cuts the time down, but still it is brutally slow. The game is highly addictive, but after spending 40 hours playing I really feel I am wasting my life not even clicking, but holding two buttons - one on the mouse, and one on the "w" key to move forward. I do feel like joining again and going to that stupid bridge in the east of Endless Shores, but I feel it is pure waste of life. Because of that I don't feel like playing anymore.

Another aspect is that the game is not new, and you will play with a mixture of total newbies and veterans, and there is a palpable tension between the two groups of players. Again, people are super nice, but I understand they want to play their complicated operations, they put tens of hours to prepare a naval attack, and suddenly a new player comes along and wants to build something that goes against the big savvy group's plan and it's just not entirely pleasant. It is also hard to join as a casual player if people are really treating the game with dedication, like a serious sport competition.

I don't regret buying the game, but after playing for 40 hours I don't want to play it anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
