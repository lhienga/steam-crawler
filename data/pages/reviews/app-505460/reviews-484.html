\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has great multiplayer interactions and that is the only reason to play it.
Unfortunately the developers make heavy use of wasting player time with tedious, repetitive tasks.
And that is why you shouldn't play it. Player time is limited and valuable. The foxhole developers do not respect players' time. To make the game play work requires each team waste thousands of real-time hours each war doing tedious, mundane tasks.
It can be so tedious that the developers have even condoned using third party autoclickers to help you.
These tedious boring tasks have a purpose though, wasting player time is the mechanic the developers use to balance certain aspects of the game.
It seems the developers haven't figured out how create balance in this game, and so they experiment by wasting human players time as a gaming mechanic while they try to figure it out.
Given how long it takes to play even a single round of foxhole (days to weeks), and how much of this time is simply busywork, I could not with good conscious recommend it to anyone.
Its a great concept, and intriguingly social, but until the developers learn to balance the game and respect players' time, avoid it.\t\t\t\t\t
