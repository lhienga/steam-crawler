\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing game! So many great ideas. What Foxhole achieved is quite remarkable. They designed a successfully MMO without classes, xp and quests at its core and it is really refreshing. There are lots of different type of gameplay depending on what players want to play at the time, easy to go from working on Logistic to using a tank or being in a infantry squad in trenches defending a bridge or going deep behind enemy lines. It is also awesome to see a game where players can enjoy themselves if they only have 30 min to play vs a few hours ahead of them. There is a learning curse: it takes a few days, perhaps 20+ to get adjusted to the game and understand how some aspects of the game works. I am sure I will still learn a lot when I pass 100 hours played. There are a few negative aspect to the game but there are ways to go around them. It is a MMO and like all MMO, there is a minority of annoying people, griefers will be unable to arm friendly people, players abusing voice chat will be easily muted etc. I highly recommend this game!\t\t\t\t\t
