\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole...

Very good game. Its a very easy game to get lost in but great fun. If you have seen videos talking about having to spend hours and hours in the game for it to be fun you don't. Spending an hour on logistics and an hour on the frontlines is a very fun way to play the game. The community is great and learning is fun and not a drag. You may feel like you aren't doing much if you don't have a massive tank or if your not shooting off artillery but each kill or each truck of logistics helps. As you learn more you can make doing the basics more efficient. Joining a clan is also a very fun way to play the game. Joining a clan does not mean you have to be on 24/7 and joining a clan makes the battles feel much bigger as you will find things you have not seen before.

Foxhole is a very fun game and definitely worth the 30 bucks and even more worth it during sales where it is only 20-25 bucks. Best game I have bought in the last 2 years.\t\t\t\t\t
