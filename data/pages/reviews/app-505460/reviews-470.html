This company refuses to do the most basic of communications with the people who play it. Their big shot they hyped up for years, and it came and went with a large sigh from pretty much everybody.

Honestly, the people who continue to play this game are mostly masochists and psychotic people who want to find meaning in holding m1 and validating the insane amount of hours it takes to do anything with a hollow idea that it somehow makes it all more interesting.

You win a war, what happens? ♥♥♥♥ all.

Which makes you wonder why everything is designed to be played by robots instead of a living, breathing, thinking human being.

Sure, its cool the game hits so many different genres and niches. But there's a better game that does each of them better.

1.0 was just more crap that was exactly what everybody complained about for the prior 4 years. The Naval Update is the same exact story. Trailers we were excited about for years -- incredibly underwhelming. Naval ships and mechanics for 1% to ever experience. Brash last second changes to live servers without any testing or care with Rare Materials.

They don't have money for Moderators. But they have money to produce and pay people to make trailers and updates for a very select few to experience. You learn to accept this and move on. "Greatest Idea Ever." "Worst Game Ever Made." -- Einstein blueprints created by a tribe of monkeys, enjoyed by the brainless dead.
