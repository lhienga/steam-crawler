\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: If you have 3+ people to play with consistently then yes its fun. If you do not and are shy then the answer is a HARD NO.

Had this for awhile its a real mixed bag folks giving it a no based on my experience as a solo player.
Toxicity:
This was something that I was really worried about when I first got the game most reviews were complaining about this, but the player base wasn't that 'toxic' from my encounters. I mostly played Colonial (green man) they tend to have smaller clans that work together in coalitions and from my experiences do a whole lotta public stuff. The Wardens (blue man) tend to have larger clans that tended to gather and spend their resources on those larger clan operations during the weekends apparently there are civil wars going on who gets to harvest at the rare resource nodes. The only time I've really seen toxic elements is while building, especially as a newer player since poor building placement can really mess things up for your side.

While both sides can be annoying at times, if you hate people spamming ♥♥♥ or talking about femboys/techmaids then don't join the Wardens it personally drove me away from the faction and I liked almost everything about it weapon/aesthetic wise.
Colonials apparently have large numbers of Furries but I haven't seen any so I cant comment on that, the players talking about that stuff might be talking about out of game fan art or it might be a big meme about being nicknamed Collies.

Optimization:
The game can at times feel like it isn't super optimized. You can run into some real lag even on the lowest settings when players have large construction in certain areas. Facilities has made this feel worse as you need to build lots of them due to dev vision of specialized facilities trading with one another, not so great for performance power generation seems to be a big part of this.

Asymmetry:
Factions have some shared neutral gear but most gear you use will be faction specific, Colonials have recently been buffed as of 03/30/2023 (MM/DD/YYYY) in regards to their lunaire grenade launcher allowing it to destroy buildings more efficiently. Colonials had been asking for something like this for longer than I have been playing (~4 months at least) so don't expect quick balance changes.

Colonial:
As a mostly solo player I think the Colonials have the better infantry gear in the form of the bomastone grenade a very long range and causes bleeding which forces Wardens to use lots of medical supplies.
They also have a large variety of rifles each serving a purpose close range to longer range all of those early game rifles take the same ammo making logistics better.
Wardens:
The Wardens on the other hand have more specialized gear, a heavier longer range rifle the Loughcaster (my favorite), a light SMG that uses different ammo great for close quarters or at night fighting, a short range revolver rifle that does a lot of damage but once again uses different ammo. Vehicle differences do exist but I cannot comment too much on them beyond wardens vehicles tend to have more armor but lower hp and might have extra guns that use different types of ammo from the tanks main gun.

Do I recommend it? Will you have fun?

Yes: If you are willing to join a clan, join their stupid discord and participate in their attempted operations then I suppose you will have fun...provided you and your clan can get past the queues and are in the right timezone my warden experiences have been massive 10+ person queues in which I could be forced to sit in for 30 minutes as I attempted to participate in the organized operation. Even if you manage to get in you or clan members could disconnect at any time and be forced to sit in the same queue all over again.

Yes: If you have a group of THREE or MORE friends that are willing to play the game with you then I can also recommend the game and will vouch you will have fun. Why? Because almost all vehicles require three or more people to function properly and that is not even counting the gathering, processing, construction, AND DELIVERY of the vehicle and ammo. You can also do partisan activities which consists of blowing up the people trying to deliver supplies to the Frontlines. I mostly stuck to using mortar weaponry/vehicles when I could get teams together. Just be aware you probably wont be able to do anything at night if you are in a tank.

Yes: If you are willing to herd random players into actual plans, this takes a special kind of person though and if you are on the shy side it wont happen. You could potentially push a frontline this way but all my experiences with this stuff has been early game with disorganized mammon rushes where some random will gather a bunch of people together to suicide charge enemy fortifications with anti structure grenades.

No: If you want to do anything combat related as a solo player for vehicle reasons mentioned above and because as infantry your will spend most of your time looking at a 10-60 second death timer while fighting over a stupid bridge that will not change hands until an organized clan rolls in to push the front forward. If you are not on a bridge then you are waiting in queue for the fun regions, if you are not waiting in queue then you are undersupplied because using the weapons + respawning in this game relies on logistics slaves driving across entire hexes to put shirts guns and bullets into your base and guess what? They sit in almost the same queue you do. The charm really wears off quickly once you 'get' combat and can actually get kills.

No: If you want to do logistics AKA a boring second job for no pay. They are basically the slaves of the game who sit there playing a terrible grindy Old School Runescape esque mining and moving simulator so people on the front lines can have all the fun. The only upside to this is you get commends from other players (ONLY IF YOU DELIVER SUPPLIES TO THE FRONT) which will increase your rank in game which is mostly cosmetic garbage, I wouldn't bother with it especially since rumor has it a rank reset is apparently coming.

No: If you want to build, building is akin to logistics you are once again playing a slave forced to sit in place with a hammer or shovel holding down the left mouse button for minutes at a time while other players have fun. The worst part by far is the maintenance which involves doing the slave logistics stuff as mentioned earlier but you are only building supplies to stop your stuff from decaying into rubble if you are far enough behind enemy lines and the war lasts long enough then you may be doing this for an entire month or longer.

No: If you cannot tolerate reporting players that can have up to 30 violations only to have them get banned for a few days.\t\t\t\t\t
