If you want to end up with severe paranoia, trust-issues and PTSD that is.
Foxhole is ruined by both its community and its devs, I'll be giving you some examples:



-Firstly, you'll love this game if you have a year of time to spend on it, however if you always have limited time, don't even bother playing it.

-In Foxhole, if you are a Colonial or a Warden, your enemies aren't the other team, but actually your own team. Piss off some hardcore no-life player? He'll wrench your truck full of RMATS and BMATS then throw it in the river then kill you, you took a single stack of RMATS to go build a tankette but a dude was gonna take said stack to build a jeep for no reason? He's gonna follow you in a truck yelling the hard-R at you for 2 hours, waste your tankette's fuel and ammo then drive it to the other team's frontline and blow it up.


-In Foxhole, teamwork is a neccesary element to any war, sometimes it needs to be coordinated, so clans have been created, and this led to almost every big clan being full of power-hungry no life hardcore basement dwellers that if you even DARE to piss of once, they'll cry to their clan and tell them to mass report you and you'll be banned within an hour. When oil was added in-game, big clans at the start of the war imediately did a bee-line for the oil fields then killed anyone who even DARED to try to take a single jerrycan leading small clans and regular players with no way to fuel their vehicles, this led to literal civil wars in between big clans controlling all the oil fields for their own stuff and killing anyone approaching the fields, small clans leading attacks and sabotages on the big clans oil wells and regular players just trying to win the war.

- The devs are very respectful to critisisim, if you even dare to leave a constructive and backed up negative review, the chances of you being shadow banned from the game and discord are close to 100%, they also listen a lot to their community, main example is the famous L.O.G.I strike, basically, logistics player were complaining to the devs that logistics was tidious, boring, unrewarding and riddled in bugs, they made a letter to the devs and gave them a deadline to either respond or fix the issues. They, of course, never fullfilled the deadline and this led to logi players going on strike for the next war, guess what the devs did? They removed half of the refineries and emptied world storages instead of listening to their community in order to punish EVERYONE leaving both teams needing to swap out their frontline duties for logistic ones, I guess I don't need to mention the fact they are ready to ban a new player with 3 hours of playtime if a high clan member asks them to, but if said clan member sabotages their own team or even hack, you can be sure nothing will be done against them.

- Foxhole has the amazing ability of sapping your morale away faster than spending 5 minutes on Twitter. Picture this, you've been supplying a frontline bunker base with shirts and BMATS and other resources for 3 hours, you help build defenses, barbed wire, mines, AI bunkers and so on, you team up with other logi players and build a battle tank, you take it to the frontline go fight with it and your teamates and capture a town so quickly the enemies don't have the time to take their vehicles with them and end up abandoning them, after a hard day of work, you decide to log-off and go to sleep, when you wake up you hop on the game only to see your team has lost both the town, the battle tank, the vehicles, the bunker base and the seaport, all of that within 6 hours. How? Simple, a Warden logged on with his Colonial account when most players were sleeping, took the vehicles you captured and brought them back to his team, dismantled a part of your trench so he could sneak his Warden account in and burn it down and did the same to the Town Hall and gave the areas with no defenses to his team, worst thing? This happens ALLLLL the time and the devs REFUSE to do anything against it, they refuse to take any action despite the community literally begging them to do anything. This caused a majority of players literally ending up with depressed or with no morale because anything they worked hard for could be rendered meaningless and useless within 2 minutes by some basement dweller.

- The moderation in Foxhole uses a bot, logical, most games now use bots to moderate right? Well, Foxhole's bot does it differently, does it base itself on the evidence, on who is getting reported or on your actions? No. It bases itself on how many people report you, you decide to report a hacker once? He'll never be banned since its not enough, however if a clan decides to report you 254 times for bullcrap reasons? Expect to be banned in the following hour because logic is that "If there's a lot of reports coming from lots of players on the same clan on a single person it HAS to be real" But it doesn't stop here, the Foxhole devs in order to stop people yelling slurs in the chat have introduced a "Communication Rating" system, basically the Reddit upvote/downvote system but the devs have somehow made it WORSE. Upvoting someone gives you no rewards whatsoever, downvoting someone however screws them over as if their Communication Rating becomes "Bad". They'll simply and casually be PERMANENTLY MUTED FROM GENERAL, REGION AND LOGISTICS CHAT leaving you with only Local chat access, now it might not seem like a big problem until you realise something. In order to get your Communication Rating up, you need to be upvoted, but the thing is, you CANNOT upvote messages in Local chat effectively leaving you muted from the general chats for the rest of the war and since downvoting messages for no reason don't give you consequences, you can effectively shadow mute anyone from communicating in a game where teamwork and communication are key.


Overall, I wouldn't recommend Foxhole at all, the game's slowly dying with dwindelling player numbers because the devs just refuse to listen to their community, useless update adding more and more useless vehicles and mechanics (who thought decay would be a good idea? And who seriously uses the ambulance?), the community is a toxic mess full of power-tripping clans, alts and hackers that get no punishements whatsoever, the game can go from the most fun experience you've ever had until, Uh oh, a bunker base needs shirts and BMATS, time to drop down your rifle in a war game and go scrap for 5 hours straight while being constantly griefed by big clans, only for your progress be meaningless because some guy with an alt account waltzed past all your defenses and burnt down your storage and up with severe paranoia resulting in you shooting anyone next to your storage because they seems suspicious and end up shooting a clan member that then tells his entire clan to mass report you only for you to end up being banned.\t\t\t\t\t
