\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe "release" state of this game is an outright lie. There is nothing release-worthy about Foxhole, nor do I have any reason to believe the developers would stop active development on this game which is nowhere near finished.

In addition to that, an entire half of the game the logistics and building side is a ceaseless grind wholly designed around unskippable, un-queueable waiting periods that sacrifices your time to potentially lessen others' ability to grief. There are other ways to mitigate griefing, but it seems the devs have gone all-in on time-wasters. If you have any respect for yourself or your time, you won't play logi. I also don't think this will improve anytime soon the latest "Inferno" release update was meant to overhaul parts of logistics, but only added more tedium rife with micro-management. There will never be a quality-of-life update, and the game will never be balanced against anything but wasting your time.

The other half the actual fighting is nice enough to sometimes pop in, but it is entirely dependent on that prior logistics and building half, so you'd be better off playing Squad.\t\t\t\t\t
