\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll be blatantly honest, and take this as either a warning, or a incentive to actually get this game. This is a game that you will pour hours into, and loose track of your time. You will probably fall in love with this game, you will probably have a blast in this game, and I am willing to beat you will end up pulling an all nighter over this game.

Foxhole has something for every type of gamer. Like trucking sims? Do Logi... Like MMO's and the grind? Do another form of Logi... Like Grand Strategy and managing large forces? You get the point, do another form of Logi. But that is the beauty of Foxhole, ever aspect of the game is contributed to by players, and thus no matter what you want to do, it exists. So wether you are fighting on the front, sneaking behind lines, building bases, or doing one of the various forms of Logi, Foxhole has a niche for you.

That does bring me to a couple of things. Prior to the latest update, I think my biggest criticism of the game would be combat grinding to a stop. While realistic, it was kind of stale 90% of the time. While we are still early in the war, the overhaul to the system of defense seem to have improved this issue (at least for now). Encounter now feel like a battle of wits and skill, and it's a vast improvement.

Every game has it's flaws, but for Foxhole, I still think it is worth it. In truth, the game in fun, and I can confidently say that you will get your money's worth (especially since the game in Early access).\t\t\t\t\t
