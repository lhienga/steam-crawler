\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou either hate this game. or lose your life to it. It is worse than world of warcraft for me.

Edit: yeah. Still either play 10 hours straight by accident or refuse to play for 3 weeks straight.

Edit: The last edit was at 100 hours of play. I now have nearly 1700. I am known for my work in the builder community. The games balance is all over the place, and not just in the intended asynchronous ways that the devs think its at, but some pretty glaring issues.

Lets start with late game anti structure.

150mm artillery, The guns between factions are identical, however the colonial one has more range whereas the warden one has a blast shield that helps keep the gunner alive (by roughly 1/3rd chance) within the emplacement. Cool difference, recently modified, but slightly overtuned towards colonial favor.

250mm Siege tanks.
Chieftain and ballista. The ballista comes with a total capacity of 11 of these hard hitting "mortar" shells whos entire purpose is to crack whatever its shooting at the sacrifice of the tank and its crew. Its slow, but holds quite a bit of ammo.

The Chieftain, while quicker, holds a total of 6 shells, but also has a 12.7 Machine gun to go with it. Good vic to kill border bases at 2am for the 5th time that night. if this was the only difference, we'd be okay, everything would be fine. BUT: Post 1.0 update, Chieftains require T2 facilities and the silverhand as its base vehicle, requires silverhands to tech in order to produce, and no fast way to do so, not including the extra infrastructure requires to turn silverhands into chieftains.
The Ballista? Unlocks two entire tiers earlier, and can be made in the MPF. If you wanted Scorpians (also known as the infantry support tank) (also a really fun tank) You would have to go through a cheaper, but yet similar process to turn the siege tank into a support tank.

What this means is the colonial big boi of concrete killing comes out two tech tiers ahead of the warden medium boi of concrete killing. This can be up to 4 or 5 real days of one side having siege tanks before the other gets the equipment to effectively defend themselves from them (the other tech tier, if you dont know what im talking about, thats okay, just know its a struggle for awhile)

Infantry equipment. In my personal opinion, colonial arms are easier to learn and use, lowering the requires skill floor to use effectively, while warden has a higher skill floor and ceiling, meaning more tricky to use, but once mastered it is more rewarding. This is okay and fine, but my biggest gripe is the handheld anti-personnel grenades. Harpas, with less standard range is more likely to instantly down an enemy may sound good, you vastly underestimate the extended range of the instant bleed Bombastone with a much wider blast radius. And as easy it is to throw two of these grenades (with the damage saturation of 4 harpas) colonials also get 25 per crate compared to 20 per crate wardens get. For the same cost.

Another piece of infantry equipment is the Catara, its just 12.7 handheld light lazerbeam of death. this is your typical salt from wardenman. yada yada yada i dont have numbers it just makes me the deaded.


Alright now onto universal issues instead of "that side has better toys than my side >:[ bs"

Fire. 1.0 update included the lovely aspect of setting things on fire. If you have ever built anything in this game, you know it literally going up in flames and nothing you can do about it, even with a fire truck or two or three or seven at times. Oh and dont ever count on buckets of water saving you. The amount of damage 3 people, regardless of team, two with flamethrowers and 1 with a machine gun for suppression, can destroy entire bases, facilities, and towns if the response time is slow, or its perhaps middle of the work day Europe and 2 am United states and not as many people are online to notice the issue. 3 people can take out days worth of work done by dozens of people.

Speaking of work of the many undone by the few.
Alts.
Some of you may know what an alt is. If you dont im going to explain it. It is when you use another account to play the game. This is sometimes beneficial for you, or even explicitly something developers want, in some games it allows you to do multiple classes or crafting recipes or whatnot at the same time to help progress your character. Sometimes it allows you to do 1 task by yourself instead of needing a team. In this game some people use them to have more factory queues or even solocrew a tank.
THIS IS LOOKED DOWN BEHAVIOR HERE.
and heres why. Not only does it give you an advantage over your enemy in many scenarios, or just deny your faction queue slots without having the player presence to justify it, But mainly, it is used for the cases of sabotage. People buy other steam accounts or just buy a brand new copy of the game and have their level 1 no rank guy go around as the other team, taking stuff, and throwing it into a river. Destroying defenses, despawning things from bases, disconnecting bunkers to slow the rate of tech, teaching tanks to swim, using trucks to block AI defenses (allowing the enemy to come closer than they had any right of being and killing whatever needs deading). But since on the map it doesnt show up as an enemy, unless you are in close proximity and catch them in the act, you just notice everything from your base gone, 15 trucks parked perfectly in front of garrisons, the 15 tanks you had prepped for the operation you and your buddies had planned for an hour from now have went missing, your base now has grown engine rooms and t1 pieces off the back (making it much much weaker for you newbies out there, please dont add pieces to someones base without their permission).

Okay so we catch an alt in the act. what now? Well you can try to voteban, where you have enough people in region to report them. but thats only temporary. What does the developers do? Nothing. Even the ones they have gone out of their way to ban, its only a year long ban and you see these accounts, come back after 372 days, and go back to doing all this damage.

And its always interesting to see an invasion of your territory happen, but all of your staged counter artillery, and ammo, flatbeds to move the ammo, Extra guns, Quick Response Force tanks, all go missing just right before the attack.

Or the PVT who knows all the tricks required to acquire and steal a stored Super Heavy Tank, that some group has gone out of their way to store as secured as possible, and starts driving it towards a group of partisans who just appeared in your backline at the same time.

But theres 0 investigation of foul play by the developers or any sort of team. I dont personally think a developer should be dealing with these types of reports but maybe a community manager or someone. Anyone at this point.

War 100 has been particularly bad. At least from the warden perspective. Able shard seemed to have a less amount of Alt behavior since 1.0 but thats to be believed that the people who like to see the world burn went to what was considered the new player friendly servers where there is less "tools" in place for handling griefers and alts. Those servers are now gone. All condensed back into 1 shard (or server).

As much fun as this game was at one point.
Building needs a quality of life for the love of callahan.
Power creep is insane, PvE needs a rebalance (no the cutler is not that OP)
Bunker supplies go BRR (i understand why this exists, but i can still cope seethe, and in fact mald.)
Facilities. ruined. it. All. This requires its own rant.
Train bros who wanted a logistics simulator A, not working together, B, not working with the faction, C, not working with the game. The games not a logi simulator with a war going on, its a war game with a logistics simulator in the back, i love you logiman, but train man please stop destroying the frontline defensive fallback position just so you can get an extra 50 meters of trains.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a war game made by die-hard pacifists, and the most unfun game I have ever played. Accurately models world war era combat, up to and including you wanting to be anywhere else, doing anything else, but playing FOXHOLE.

Suffering from QoL issues of smaller games like Dwarf Fortress, a reliance on an inaccurate and outdated wiki, a terrible tutorial, learning this game is as unrewarding and frustrating as playing it. Gone are hitmarkers, kill messages, KDR stats and any other such convenience. Instead, players bored of fruitlessly dying from artillery, snipers and gas can pick up a second job as a truck driver, shuttling materials back and forth for hours over the same road to die to enemy partisans.

It's deep and complex but confusing and counter-intuitive mechanics are never explained and the game outright states that the indifference to your confusion is part of the experience. A necessary part of the game is in fact resource grinding, explicitly executed to be tedious and soul-crushing. As you time your clicks loading up the fuel tanker, you can't AFK, or even talk in chat - but click the icon, not too slow, not too fast. If insufficient mules commit to this drudgery, your faction's soldiers end up having to take on tanks with pistols. This game does not respect your time, cares little for your experience and requires a massive investment of effort.

This is not a mainstream game, nor a fun game, nor a good game. But if you are that particular hardcore gamer who takes pride in bloodied hands from handling this cactus of a game and is looking for something that makes you feel punished, powerless and confused, your masochism will find fertile ground here.

This game explains why warfare never caught on as a hobby. Because despite the movies, it's not an adventure, it's not fun - it's a job, a boring one, a hazardous one, a confusing one, and all of it sucks and then you die. FOXHOLE captures this perfectly. Whatever sins you may have come to atone for by playing FOXHOLE, your efforts are better spent elsewhere.\t\t\t\t\t
