\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has its strengths and weaknesses.
But since its the only one of its kind I decline to not recommend it even if I had some major bad experiences in my last 100 hours (mostly interaction with toxic clans and players).

Strengths:

- Very unique, niche game
- Strong team play (also for solo players)
- Casual, but addicting
- Fitting graphics
- Masses (250+) of players on a single map, thousands on the whole world map
- Wars go from 10-60 days and then everything resets
- In general a nice community
- Dozens of different vehicles and buildings
- Dozens of weapons, gear and other stuff
- Lots of cool mini features and functions
- Faction vehicles are not 100% mirrored and also some weapons differ, making it interesting to switch sides from time to time

Weaknesses:

- Servers (Been a major issue since early "early access")
- Clans can get toxic if youre single player
- Players can get toxic due to sandbox and building features
- No real tutorial: Which can be frustrating for fresh players
- No real tutorial: Which can be frustrating for veterans when interacting with new players
- Music and sound lack a bit in this game in general
- Lots of bugs relating the terrain (getting stuck, cant build etc)
- Due to world wide 1 server expect some lags when interacting with other players
- Shooting feels a bit janky due to server issues and world wide mixed players (high ping)

Still in general its worth playing it.
Last words:
I really recommend avoiding toxic clans as much as you can, as they can mass report you if they do not like your behavior or play style (ultimately resulting in bans).\t\t\t\t\t
