\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is such an unique and amazing game. When you start you're completely open to do whatever you want, whenever you want.

Want to jump straight into combat? Join any front line and push with the infantry mass.

Want to organise an artillery battery to demolish an enemy emplacement? Grab some people and arty pieces.

Interested in sneaking behind enemy lines and stealing/destroying enemy equipment? Build a boat and bring a wrench.

Maybe you want to chill and support the war effort without dirtying your own hands? Spawn in the back line and start organising some logistics.


The battlefield rewards creativity, flanking and teamwork which, on such a large scale, is beautiful to witness when everything fits together. The game absolutely isn't perfect, it has its fair share of bugs and toxic players like any game, but the feeling of being part of something much bigger is intoxicating.\t\t\t\t\t
