\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly I didn't know I'd like this game at first, but after seeing the vastness of the world, I wanted to know more about the war effort. The ability to make you feel like a little part of something greater is magnificent. You wouldn't think that being a "Top-down-shooter" game it could be immersive, but the goosebumps I get when sitting in a trench with a couple strangers after a big battle, and talking about what we'll do when we get home never gets old. It's a very social game, so I wouldn't recommend it for a solo player, because you'll be talking to strangers a lot. The game has its trolls like any game, but the community is actually very good. On countless occasions I've asked random people in the midst of a job if I can help, and they usually will show me the ropes. It's a bit of a learning curve, so don't get to frustrated when you first start out, just ask around, ask for help, and use the community to learn the game, it's what keeps that immersion at its best. This is rated lower then what it deserves, because I imagine people get frustrated. Be patient, once you learn, it becomes an experience.\t\t\t\t\t
