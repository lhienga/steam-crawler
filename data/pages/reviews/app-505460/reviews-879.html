\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a game that involves alot of things, in brief it is a strategic top down shooter that revolves heavily around the logistics portion of a conflict. It doesn't matter necessarily how many enemies you and your team are defeating if the enemy is able to supply and amass a tank column that will break through your defence line in minutes, even if you've been able to hold it for hours. This game values the back line alot people mining/gathering resources to refine and turn into useful items and weapons that in the best case scenario will get used efficiently by team mates, or in the worst never put to use and captured by the enemy. Whatever you end up doing in this game it will be fun and rewarding (A notable example being having a 10 man squad thanking you and inviting you into a clan simply for keeping them supplied with rpgs and machingun ammo). Fair word of warining however this game is still in development and in the past and just recently many of the systems have been changed around, mostly for the better. But if your a person who likes getting used to one way of doing things, this game will occasionally pull the rug out from under you forcing you to change your play style and causing the community to come up with new efficient strategies for breaking the game again ;). Not to mention there are still several bugs, most involving trucks and vehicles (you will learn to fear trucks) as well as getting stuck in various cockamamy ways but usually these are more funny than annoying. Overall I find the game an enjoyable team based combat logistic sim with great feeling controls, and a way of making you feel extremely immersed in fighting over extremely small patches of land.\t\t\t\t\t
