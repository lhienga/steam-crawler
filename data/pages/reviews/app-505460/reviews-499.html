\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis here is a game with a brilliant premise - a massive-scale war between thousands of players on a series of massive maps. The most important thing that sets it apart from other games, however, is logistics. Everything you see is made by players. Let's say you sit in a trench and fire a rifle at the enemy - some other player had to gather materials to make said rifle and ammo for it, another one had to deliver it to the front and someone else probably dig that trench you're now sitting it. It turns both sides of this conflict into a clockwork-like machine that requires hard work, coordination and dedication to achieve progress - and that in itself is enough to make Foxhole great.

But unfortunately, the people playing this game are probably the most toxic, cancerous gaggle of asshats that you will ever see. For example, the first major problem are so-called 'alters' - people who play for one side, but buy another copy of the game to go to another side and sabotage it from within. Now imagine for a moment that you spend several hours gathering resources and producing supplies your friends at the front desperately need. And now just know that at any moment, your hard work may be invalidated by some insecure moron who will just grab all effects of your hard work and just dump them into nearest lake so he could earn bragging rights in a video game.

Next we have clans. You'd think clans playing one one side should work together. Sadly, that is not the case here. Not a day goes by without some drama going on between some clans and they sometimes even sabotage each other just to... hell, i have no clue what they're trying to accomplish. But the only thing they achieve is hurting their own side. And the thing is, clans are needed because they're the only ones who can organize big operations. I'd really wish they did that, instead of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ and griefing all the time.

And finally, it's not uncommon for a new player to suddenly hear a barrage of profanities from some 'veteran' just because the new guy is not doing what the old guard wants. Nothing more demoralizing than being yelled at by your own side just because you're new and you have no idea what to do in this very complicated game.

Now, there's plenty of friendly people around, but it seems they're being slowly but surely phased out by rampaging ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ and alters who treat this videogame way too seriously. And since developers of the game don't seem to care at all about moderating their playerbase (and they even lock and ban all discussions about it), it doesn't look like things will improve any time soon. If anything, griefing and overall cancer will probably just get worse.
So do yourself a favor and steer clear.\t\t\t\t\t
