\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole is a really fun game, one moment you can be on the front lines fighting an intense battle and then the next you are running some logistics to get more ammo for the front line.

the game is in early access, but I don't think that really matters. the game has a fair amount of things to keep you engaged, such as building bases, making supplies, fighting, scouting, being a medic, transporting supplies and whatever role is needed on the field.

The game can be hard to pick up at first, but if you have a mic and feel comfortable using the built in voice tools, you can easily ask for help from other players on your team. I suggest finding a small group doing something you wish to do as well and tagging along with them for a while, you can easily learn what to do that way.

The game can make you feel like what you do is pointless at times, especially if you captured a town while playing then go to bed and get up to find it has been recaptured by the other faction. but do not worry, it can be recaptured again. The game has a strong sense of you are just a cog in a greater machine and this can sometimes be a negative thing on the grander scale of the whole war, but on the field of battle with a couple other soldiers, everything you do matters, that building in the town you just captured, can help create the break through required to take the entire town, that truck full of supplies that take a while to make can help lift the morale of the soldiers at whatever front you delivered to, helping them carry on fighting to capture whatever objective they are after.

The wars can go on for weeks meaning that if you can only play on the weekends, you do not need to worry about missing out in a war, as you can hop on and still participate.

All in all, I highly recommend Foxhole and to give it a good try. I feel like I definitely got my moneys worth out of Foxhole.


Edit: don't listen to all the negative reviews, they are just salty veteran players who don't like some of the changes, the game is amazing so give it a try, yeah the game has a low population problem, but that can only be fixed by more people playing.\t\t\t\t\t
