\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very good game especially considering it's still in early access.

The interlinkages between combat at the frontline and logistics is fascinating. I recommend switching from one position to the other. Playing as infantry, you'll notice that what you are supplied in your bases really makes a difference in what you can achieve. It's a relief to see the logistics truck arrive with the needed supplies or a desperation to see that your base is out of ammo/weapons/grenades.

In turns, that makes the logistics experience very rewarding. Even though logistics can be a bit repetitive, you know you are making an important contribution to the war effort by supplying the front what it needs. Also, you'll to watch for ambushes, determine what are the best routes considering that the front will move over time and that some road that were clear at a given time might not be so anymore.

One of the strength of the game is that you can switch from one role to the other whenever you feel like doing so. Stressed out of fighting? You can just pick a truck and go mining/transporting for a little while and vice-versa. Also, while I totally believe the reviews saying that playing in a clan or with friends is key to live the full Foxhole experience, I've been enjoying my time playing solo and I think the game is totally worth playing solo as well. The fact that there are no level progression system hard-coded in the game makes it a very accessible game for the casual player. If, like me, you are working and can only play an hour or so so days in the week, you'll still enjoy your experience. You'll contribute to the war effort for the time you can invest in it.

There are a few downsides to mention also: the game saw a brutal rise in popularity and this lead to the game servers to be overpopulated creating delays and queues to access the places where the fight happens. The devs plans to increase capacity so it might be a temporary issue only but it's something to be aware of.\t\t\t\t\t
