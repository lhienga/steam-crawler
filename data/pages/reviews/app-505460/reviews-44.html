As a solo individual you have the opportunity to fight on the front lines all the way to mining the materials used to support those on the front. Grab a truck and move much needed supplies to where they're needed.

The game is definitely best experienced with a group. This gives the support needed to run larger production facilities and coordinate larger combat operations with larger equipment.

It can be enjoyed as a solo player and while playing in a group. There's a ton to learn though, so don't get overwhelmed in the start. In the least, grab a bolt action rifle, 2 magazines of ammo, and find a trench at the front.\t\t\t\t\t
