\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHas some good qualities, but the game needs something more to keep most people motivated.

Edit: I like to play for the whole group, not just one clan in the whole group. It used to be that you got a great feeling about picking where to support the group, mine resources, make stuff or/and bring it to the front get commends and personal thank thank yous. Public storage was great because everyone could see what is needed for the whole group and supply that and feel good about helping. Now, hardly anyone uses public storage, because 1) Alt's (even a very small number of them) can ruin 100's of hours of peoples work by taking out stuff and dumping it or leaving it in the ocean. This leads to a much wider distrust of everyone and really taints the whole process 2) public storage is 10x slower to pull resources from and it can take literally an hour to fill 6 containers full of public stuff (vs 6 minutes from personal storage), then spend 20-30 min driving the boat to its destination. 3) Very few people give any thanks or commends when you have worked 10 hours gaining resources, 2 hours gather and producing stuff, 2 hours shipping to the front, then drop off 600 crates to have 5-8 trucks gobble your stuff up and not one of them says thanks...

I'm sure if I joined a clan they would understand and appreciate the time I put in, but I like playing as a solo. I want to be like an uber driver and do what I want, when I want and where I want with no one else giving me direction, just me and the whole group.

If the devs make it better (more rewarding) for the solo player it would be a huge bonus for us and for the whole community as a whole.

Edit #2: I changed shards from Alpha to Charlie and I feel that the community is much better at helping and thanking others. I still think that the Dev's should make some changes, but I am enjoying the game again and would say it's a Buy. Just be aware that different communities will give you different experiences.\t\t\t\t\t
