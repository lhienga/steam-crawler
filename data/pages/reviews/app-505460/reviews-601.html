For more information, I fell in love with this game because the more i played and provided support for my colonial front lines, I found myself personally invested in the success or failures of the infantry I was providing support to. Unlike the "norm" for these types of games like BF5, Call of Duty, etc. where a tank represents "i was the first one to spawn in it now BRRRT" any sort of armored vehicle represents a tremendous strategic foothold to be applied. You are required to communicate in this game to discuss strategies and request help, it is simply impossible to charge into battle with a tank without the proper supporting supply line including fuel, ammunition, and the infantry support you'll be fighting alongside. This game really is the whole picture of the wartime genre. ABSOLUTELY RECOMMEND.\t\t\t\t\t
