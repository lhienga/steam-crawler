\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReviewer info: 800 hours of playtime before writing this. Rank: Major. Played in both factions. Actively playing as a Warden at the moment.

Let me start by saying that Foxhole is a unique and highly addictive gaming experience.
It's positives:
1) Huge scale wars, cool lore, an extremely helpful and newby friendly community.
2) Entertaining combat mechanics.
3) Infantry, tanks, artillery, gunboats, trains and advanced logistics.
4) Advanced building mechanics. You can even build fortresses and huge Storm Cannons.

HOWEVER, i CANNOT recommend this game anymore.

The unresolved issue of alt account griefers cannot be ignored. Both factions have had a number of ''bad apples'' who use their alt accounts on the opposing faction, in order to grief on backline facilities and harass their production. Hundreds of hours of work can easily be razed to the ground in minutes.... Thousands of complaints and reports have been submitted to the dev team but they choose to ignore the issue... Even when alts account get caught on video, they get a 2 days long ban and then they can go back to their evil habits... On the contrary, RPing situations like checkpoints or fist fighting arenas can get you permanently banned...lol...

In summary, this game has been fun and could be great. However, you should stay away until the devs decide to actually solve the many issues that ruin the experience. At it's current state, it aint worth it....\t\t\t\t\t
