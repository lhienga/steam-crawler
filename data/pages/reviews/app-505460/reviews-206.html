\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish I could say this game is worth your time. I had some amazing moments that I would not have had anywhere else. Thing is, the opposite is also true. Logistics in this game is a very important aspect since everything is made by players. From rifles and bayonets to tanks and artillery shells, everything is made by players. Playing logi S U C K. It's an absolute grindfest nightmare where gouging your eyes out might probably enhance your overall experience. Player facilities got added to 1.0. Guess what? If you don't have slaves running supplies 24/7 just so your installations don't start decaying, well, sucks to be you but that's your job now. Oil fields are so good because, with the right upgrades, you can have power 24/7 without having to ferry coal all day. They are so good in fact, both factions early wars are also fought internally in what I call the "oil wars 2.0". You have a bunch of players shooting each other trying to secure and claim an oil field of their own. The more patches comes out, the worst things get. All those hours you put in that facility over there, yeah just two guys with flamethrowers and a bike can burn it all down, where it would have taken much more effort and actual explosives before. I could list much more things, but I'm sure many people already did before me.

TL;DR Foxhole is a colossal online waste of time. The amount of fun you can get out of it does not compensate for the absolute grind fest it has become. And I don't expect it will change anytime soon.\t\t\t\t\t
