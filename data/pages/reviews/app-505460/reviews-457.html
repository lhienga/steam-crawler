\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDear Mother,

Its early in the day, some say too early. The fields are boggy around Ash Fields and there are gunshots in the distance.
The chat feed fills with the words of male expulsion from the groin area.
Why are the men shouting these words in their darkest hours?
To my amazement, we have taken a key river crossing and the men are happy.
What a victory.

Alone, I entered this war.
No friends, but the people I stand alongside and will eventually die alongside.
At last, a common man appears and offers aid.
Will he be my humble comrade and allow me to join him on this adventure.
He accepts me into his regiment, and I too become a member of the march towards victory.

I make many friends within this humbling regiment of many.
We plan to take on a bunker base to the south. We are not alone.
Another Regiment has decided to join us. A mammon rush is being formed from the captain.
We grab two to a man and rush the enemy lines. I get my two off and manage to get away.
Many did not make it back. I will see them again, once the cooldown timer allows them too.

Tomorrow is a new day mother. I looking forward to the sunrise.
I just hope its not my last and I can come home to you and papa.
But tomorrow is just another penstroke in the chapter of this here foxhole.
We will be victorious. As the men in the chat feed said so.

Yours truly,

Bravo
Ash Field Frontline
Behind a Logi truck.\t\t\t\t\t
