\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would prefer to leave a neutral review.

The game itself is great, the community is not. If you like to go online and pew pew on the frontlines this game may be for you. If you want to build a facility / base and do logistics it may not be which is my negative experience with this game.

TLDR; I had to stop playing after having all my trucks stolen from teammates for the 3rd time.

Logistics in this game is a long and tedious process. It takes a long time to harvest resources, build trucks, drive them to where they need to be. If you leave them on the field when you logoff they will be stolen by your teammates when you wake up.

Every day when you login you need to assemble new trucks because of the thieves. Even if you leave them locked, they can still lockpick them.

The solution to this problem according to the community elitists is storing it in a storage depot. The problem is, when your facility is not close to a depot, and you need 5 trucks to manage the facility (haulers, flatbed haulers, fuel trucks, cranes, construction vehicles, containers etc.). You simply can not spend 1-2 hours a day driving the trucks back to the storage depot every day to avoid thieves. You literally would spend over 2 hours driving back and forth, running back on foot to your facility just to store everything in a safe depot.

When I login, I want to play the game. Not drive my trucks from the "garage" or make new trucks 1-2 hours every single day before I can start with the actual gameplay. Which for me was building a facility that create materials, equipment and resources for soldiers on the frontlines.

Thanks to thieves this became too tedious and a massive job every day.

The solution would be personal locks on vehicles that last around 3 days which are not able to be lock picked.

Oh, I forgot to mention all the dumb people who run in front of your vehicle when you are driving around so you get teamkills. It's like they never crossed a street in real life. Imagine having to re-educate people on how to behave when crossing the road.\t\t\t\t\t
