\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to enjoy this game as the playerbase & countless war activities were great fun, and there were a ton neat mechanics for awesome gameplay and environmental cue's showing the game came a long way from what it used to be despite still being VERY grindy & time consuming. Although, since the last few updates devs have promised new features (or returning features originally removed for reworks) and the 1.0 version of the game (despite still having many old flaws players are still complaining about in the official Discord such as bugs and the clear unbalancing between factions) for about a year or so now but have gone almost completely silent. As the playerbase is complaining about lack of communication (& balancing or simple fixes) from the devs, they are simply leaving the game until 1.0 finally arrives while the devs are likely more focused on their new WIP medieval game called "Anvil" currently undergoing beta testing. For a few of the players sticking around there are "salty vets" that accuse most new players of "alting" (some of which actually are as the devs have to guaranteed method of preventing this) for not knowing an OUTDATED TUTORIAL when you first join the game of which the devs also abandoned, but they'll be sure to update the game with "fixes" the moment a well-known Twitch streamer points something (ignoring their playerbase completely) of which I simply can't support. The devs treat the playerbase more as guinea-pigs than respectable players supporting their game as they control most of what happens at the start of each war such as the map, faction tech trees, possibly the weather also, and implement bizarre in-game stat changes sometimes without any patch notes or answers to player's questions about it. So, if you want a game that you can't really see an outcome and anything you do might get you yelled at or left with more questions or sorrow, this is the game for you (honestly I'd prefer War Thunder for that though).

Update: Tried again recently for a war and with the latest updates post FINALLY getting 1.0 and all the patches after it I gotta say while few parts did get slightly better (e.g. item pull time & another few promises added), the game is in a now limbo-like state which imho I would say is probably worse for one reason: Facilities. This new feature never asked for pretty much locks a lot of the game's content behind a higher grind wall for current, new, solo, and most definitely casual players, pretty much forcing you to join a clan or suffer the grind for the most part while worrying someone might take or ruin your facility throughout the entire time of managing it and gathering the resources for it. What was once (dare I say) a simple grind has been pipe-lined to a longer and more tedious process that forces you to make a facility for most vehicle types (some of them having to wait 1-2 days REAL TIME to complete, and that's AFTER getting all the necessary resources) and even on occasion ammo types with the false promise that it would had made grinding more idle (in other words it's NOWHERE NEAR as satisfying or comparable to Factorio or Satisfactory, especially in terms of throughput). Luckily, although there's still some bad apples in the community, a lot of people make facilities that offer some of the necessary resources publicly (at least on the Colonial side from my experience) to soothe the experience, though there's also opposites that exist with the same notion that they suffer the same grind as you will. The devs said they won't abandon this game's development for ANVIL: Empires, but I would not hold them to it knowing their history of only 1-2 devs on this small team still caring for this game kept alive solely by its 1-2k player community.\t\t\t\t\t
