\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst and foremost I have played for years and went through quite a few updates. The game overall has to be one of the BEST persistent multiplayer games out there but now with some recent updates it has become increasingly difficult to enjoy like once before.

Before there wasn't much to worry about if you wanted to do something new like work as artillery or drive a tank or even work as a builder or logi, but now if you have no knowledge its almost near impossible due to the hoops and hurdles you have to cross to even be slightly effective. I agree that adding detail to make things more challenging is fine, but adding additional steps is maddening. I can't enjoy artillery like I used to nor enjoy building like I used to. There's a line between being too realistic and too simple, if you make things too realistic and add steps in order to complete one task, then no one wants to do it anymore. Make it too simple and it gets spammed out of control and abused.

With the rant out of the way, the story behind the game itself is forever changing due to player wars because it IS an ongoing conflict and no one knows truly who is "good" and who is "bad" cause sometimes in war, there are no Good guys or Bad guys, it's as simple as that. (Sometimes) The maps are great and HUGE! The ability to craft your territory with friends and allies is a HUGE plus, also being able to conduct and organize attacks is great. The game gives you an abundance of freedom, just make sure you don't team-kill.

If you haven't yet tried it, I would HIGHLY recommend buying and playing for a bit, make sure you watch some tutorials on YouTube before you do though so you don't go in blind.\t\t\t\t\t
