Im using a gaming laptop circa 2020 build so its running this game okay. Startup is a bit long and worried me the first time.

The game is persistent and easy respawning. So you can play out long arcs of adventures over many real life days/weeks.

Ive noticed not much greifing or audio bombing. Thanks to the quick respawn people can quickly be doing something again so unlike World of Tanks or World of Warships there isnt the BLAH BLAH of the dead folks.

Also the game has a huge map with likely different battle zones so you could just move to a different area anyway.

There is various jobs to do and its relatively straight forward once you learn the controls. Do think youtubes are good to watch. Im not an expert in the game but I did find that was able to do the following things:

= Medic bandaging comrades
= Assault medic with rifle and bandages
= Supply wrangler organzing supplies from dead guys into useful locations
= build fort improvemnts
= Truck driver
= Rescource hauling
= Supply shells to an artillery battery
= Assist building a bridge

So a huge amount of things to do
