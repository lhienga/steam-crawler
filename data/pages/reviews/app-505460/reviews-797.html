\r\n\t\t\t\t\tＯｖｅｒｖｉｅｗ
Foxhole makes you learn a lot about the "War Effort" in the way you wouldn't seen in another game or genre. From the boots on the ground to the backseat in the chain of command. Foxhole gave you an endless opportunities in your playstyles.

For example you can play as a rifleman pushing over the no man's land , an officer leading the charge , arty crews from 1km deep behind your lines and even a truck driver driving for the logistic corps. With no fixed role, you can be everything you wanted.

Ａｅｓｔｈｅｔｉｃｓ

The world of Foxhole is magnificently crafted by Devs. There is two faction in the game, The Colonials (Collies) and The Wardens. Both of the team have the same level of technology for the balace of gameplay. The world is mostly based on Interwar Europe, with riveted tanks and armored cars. Towns sometimes looks lively even it is abandoned and used solely for war. Sometimes you'd feel the scent of solitude while driving down your guns to the front. With rotten tanks lying around.

Ｇａｍｅｐｌａｙ
You're one of the many conscripts stepping into the realtime war with fellow friends (and sometimes foes) on your side. The war takes place in a continent maps with its own distinctive features. There is several types of environment you'll encounter. The Front , Middle and the Rear

Frontlines is the most busiest part of the game. Everywhere on the map can be the frontlines. Most people choose to go into the quick action here through garrison spawns. As the name suggest it is where the action begins, both sides send their waves and waves of poor conscripts and decent trained officers into the field, the killing field. If you lucky there will be a tank helping you push your objectives.

Middle Lines is where you second line located. It is a transportation hub for delivering goods and weapons to the front. Be cautious here too because enemy might send their elite troops often led by high ranking officers to destroy your logistic lines and bridges. But you can raid them back too, with good combination of troops and vehicles you can even open a new front in their rear !

Rear Line is one of the most "Boring Part" for action packed players, but it is also the most VITAL and IMPORTANT part of the army. If you have tried playing as logistic crew (Called Logi by players) you'll learn a lot from this. You must gather resources, turn them into Goods , Weapons and Vehicles. And ship them to the front via trucks and boats.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tToo slowly the devs will adapt to the playerbase while still ignoring several bugs.

6/14/2022: New review, but still the same old. If I thought the game was buggy before, it is a lot worse now. Lag is crazy from squads lists, to massive bunker bases, to just poor optimization of certain functions in the game. The land is still littered with rocks that you can't climb, blackholes from trenches, or trying to climb out of trenches being buggy unless you find the "sweetspot". QoL is awful for players unless you just pop in for an hour to play infantry on a frontline IF you can get through the queues. The devs have done a great job with such a small group to add so much to a game like this, but it feels like they are trying to rush to 1.0 and just leaving so many things broken to fix later if they do. Many things have been broken since I first played. It's just scummy in my eyes to ignore so many bugs and continually creating new bugs with new things being added and expecting the playerbase to just truck through it. This game solely relies on the player base to even function in the first place, and its the reason many players quit or take month long breaks from the game. I'll end off with saying at the current state I would rather play a different game to waste hours of my day and time to lose to alts or a volatile front in 30 minutes or go back to previous versions of this game.

11/20/2023: The game is still exactly in the same state it was over a year ago. Sure flashy new updates come and bring in a influx of new players who leave after a few wars and the population is back to the same usual number and devs do nothing for the ones that commit their time to the game. The newest naval update was a great concept and long awaited, but this update war has by far been THE worst war i've been involved in. the home region of all places being unable to get to from the world conquest, several regions crashing consistently, the ships introduced acting like floating fridges on water with how bad the handling is unless you use blueprint turn, creating a terrible asymmetric gunboat, and the drama from their flawed input of the next logi grind of rare metals. I come back every few wars and check in to see if they ever fix the things that are broken, but never do. The community is even more toxic than before from reddit to discord to in-game to faction specific streamers. The developers used to have very consistent updates letting the community know what was going on and created fun events to break up the monotonous of the wars. Dead Harvest and relic wars were great. The devs say they dont want any monetization in-game, but focus more on flashy new toys and a new art for foxhole store page to bring in new players, but wont improve the QOL in the game.\t\t\t\t\t
