\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has the potential to be something unique and amazing, with its large scale battles that you can take part in, to being part of the war machine and drive your faction to victory!

Except however the fact that most of this would be you if you joined a clan and played the game from beginning to end, which by the way could take months of non-stop grinding and dying. A solo player with no clan affiliation is basically just someone who gets yelled at by large clans for playing the game, and then friendly fired for doing nothing wrong, in a small group you might be able to destroy a small outpost or town, but without any large clan assistance, the entire push is just made useless and you basically wasted time doing it because large clans aren't interested.

This game lives and dies by large clan organisation, if any clan dissolves or moves factions, the entire game is just a land slide for who ever has those clans.

Pros:
- Interesting gameplay and mechanics
- Decent visuals
- Interesting new additions and content

Cons:
- Large clans domination
- Logistics is boring and tedious and makes you feel insignificant
- Solo / small groups are pratically useless
- Trolls and spies everywhere to spoil peoples fun
- Toxic high ranked players (not all of them, but some)
- Camera can get you killed if the enemy is below your line of sight due to hot bar getting in the way

I would personally give this game a 6 out of 10, it would need to really change how solo and small groups can get involved without an army to constantly back you up. Then I would probs give it an 8 if it achieved this.\t\t\t\t\t
