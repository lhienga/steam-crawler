\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is fun game, my life is gone, only foxhole now.

^^^ See this? I made this review just about a year ago and though it's kind of a joke review I truly meant that it was a fun game if a little time consuming.

I come back to this review a year later to update my opinion on the game after the release of 1.0, and unfortunately I have nothing good to say. The full release of the game gave us many fun sounding things like rocket artillery, trains, super heavy tanks, and more. Sadly it ended at "sounding" fun, all these features came out in broken or extremely unbalanced states, rocket artillery is more effective against one faction than it is to the other because of broken fire mechanics, train tracks decay so quick there's no point in using them, super heavy tanks are extremely unbalanced with one faction having a much better tank than the other to a shocking degree, and the list goes on.

Now I'm going to be honest with you, I have my own personal opinions about the balance of this game, but I won't get into them because to be frank it's tiring and you can look at any other salty vet's review for that, and more importantly that's not what changed my opinion of this game.

So what did? Well... factories, player built factories sound great right? More places to build your stuff is never bad. But unfortunately that's not the whole story, factories are high upkeep, annoying, and gating buildings that suck the joy out of this game for many people. The fun variants of most vehicles are locked behind these new factories for a hefty price in most cases. You need specific materials to upgrade them, which are made with other specific materials, which are made with 4 more raw resources all taking a minute or more to make at every step, AND EVEN WHEN YOU HAVE ALL THE MATERIALS IT TAKES AROUND TEN MINUTES TO UPGRADE THE VEHICLE THAT YOU HAD TO MAKE BEFORE ALL OF THIS, it's not fun, it's grindy and needlessly complicated for new players and completely ignores players who like to play without a mega clan.

And that's another thing, clans. I've been a clan man for all my time playing ([XXTH],[FSL][CMC]), and I still think the factories are bad for us clan people. To make some of the high resource and fun tanks like the battle tanks it requires a lot of time to gather the materials for it, and then it takes 18 hours to construct, yup 18 real life hours. This wouldn't be a huge problem if it wasn't for the fact that other people on the same faction (or opposite) can steal it. All it takes is one alt account from the enemy faction (of which there are many cuz the devs don't address the problem), or one low rank player that doesn't know any better to take all that work away from you. This causes clan men to be more hostile to other clans and solo players who pass by because if they don't they might get their 18hr long effort stolen.
