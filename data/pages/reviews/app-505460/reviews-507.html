\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing this game for at least 4 years, I can definitely say this is one of my favourite games.

Yes, there's a lot of problems that one is expected to face (i.e, Alts, report abuse, balance issues etc.) and a lot of them are most likely going to stick around for a while. Thats common with lots of other games. But what I like about this game is the concept behind it; the idea of you being a cog in the bigger machine of war that is Foxhole, not some super soldier or special forces operative. Just an ordinary nameless soldier that, with enough teamwork and dedication, can really turn the tide of the war.

There's definitely plenty of problems that devs need to address and they really do need to listen to their playerbase for feedback. But I think this game has a lot of potential and really sets itself apart from most other shooters.\t\t\t\t\t
