\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a fun concept, but it's held back some game mechanics.

Playing with others with voice chat is fun. Most are helpful and they help you get through some of the more mundane parts of the game, which there are many instances of. Making gear/supplies takes to long to make. It's like a job. Its unfun. Then some resources are harder to get because there is a learning curve and you need to invest your time like a job. You need higher tier stuff to actually do things on the battlefield. If not, you're cannon fodder. You can only be cannon fodder for so long before it gets boring. You improve this by joining a clan, but if you like to be solo, your options are limited.

Also, this game was a big problem with people having alternate accounts and sabotaging/griefing the opposite side. The developers have not done anything about it. In fact, a common compliant of the regular players is that the developers aren't really engaged with the community.

This game has potential but a lot of parts of it are a drag. I don't play games to do busy work.\t\t\t\t\t
