\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoxhole isn't for everyone ill tell ya that much. The game isn't so much fixated on extravagant action or superbly sandbox building. If anything its an amalgam of the two to create a sort of slowed down RTS except you have control over one unit. Foxhole is all about collaborating with members of your chosen faction in order to try and win a war that tends to drag on quite a bit. The game in a sense tries to add historical aspects to wars by having to hold territory, make vehicles from materials, transport supplies to bases/the front, etc... Even if a war in a sense is lost by one faction the game never truly ends and the world will just reset to start another war anew. While normally in a top down third person game or some other games its possible to perform the art of carrying your team in foxhole it isn't as simple. Because complicated tasks take time it is best to work with others to get things down. If anything a group people may carry but only in specific parts due to the large regions of the game.

TL,DR: Third person game where you need to co-op to succeed. Carrying is almost non-existent you will get pwned trying to do things.

Finally a little advice: The game can be a bit difficult to take in so it is recommended that upon spawning at the Home Region you ask players to help ya out.\t\t\t\t\t
