\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs you can see, I'm very new to the game, but I already love it! I had the luck to join on the last day of War 91, and it was the prime Foxhole experience. Fighting the battles at Ash Fields was a small slice of Verdun, as we desperately fought to push the enemy back from the Ashfort while being pounded with heavy artillery and gas. Entire squads of men worked to simply keep our bunkers from collapsing under the onslaught. I worked to charge enemy trenches and defend the outer perimeters as Colonial infantry washed over us in waves. Every ten minutes or so, we would receive the notification that yet another rocket had been launched. I fought at Ash Fields for more than four hours, and I had a blast. When I finally did have to leave the region, however, I went to an adjacent one and explored a town that had been hit with at least one rocket and probably several volleys of artillery. I really got a sense of the scale of the conflict, as there was literally nothing left in almost the whole city.

There are a few cons, though. Queue times can stretch on for hours to enter regions where the fighting is heaviest. If you die and there isn't a base close to the front, which happened frequently, you may be stuck in a situation of spawn, walk five minutes, die, repeat. If you're not a teamwork person, I really can't suggest this game, as any of the really fun parts of the game require multiple people to do. However, if you're willing to push past the problems, this really is a very, very good game.\t\t\t\t\t
