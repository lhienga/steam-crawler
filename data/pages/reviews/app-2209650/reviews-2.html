In worst-case scenario, job offer expired on arrival.
Despite Tulsa is the second-most populous city in Oklahoma, the city within itself has a garage, but without a truck service station, gas station and resting area are located 50+ miles away from the main city area is a major inconvenience to access service in Tulsa.
McAlester also missing service stations similar in Tulsa. Idabel has a truck service station, but why are located 140 miles away from the city?

Oklahoma DLC is small, lack of interesting stuffs and feels overpriced at this time. There are 10 cities, 4 achievements, 3 truck dealers, 5 garages, 2 recruitment agencies, 3 special transport routes, 10 viewpoints, and 11 photo trophies on this DLC.
As smaller states are approaching in the near future, you should start consider to make at least two states in one DLC or the price needs to be reduced based on number of cities and state size.

I'm feeling more satisfied after developer just added a truck service station in Tulsa, McAlester, and Idabel after 1.49 update, but they forgot to add resting area nearby just like many other existing truck service stations.\t\t\t\t\t
