\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeing a native to Oklahoma was really my only justification for owning this DLC and the base game but to say the least it is a very surreal feeling to be able to drive around your own state in a game. Though a lot of things are scaled down and some highways are sort of mapped weird, like the stretch that goes from Sallisaw to Idabel which was really jarring for me, it is really true to heart of what Oklahoma is and that's lots of greenery and long open roads surrounded by plains and farms. To say the least Oklahoma in reality is not the most exciting state in the US so I can see the negative points that this is getting but I can't say you should expect much from a State that's not really shown much in the spotlight.

I do give kudos to places such as McAlester which driving through the main downtown area is almost exactly 1 to 1, it's almost like more time was spent there than anywhere else as that's the most recognizable area I've been in so far besides Tulsa and OKC.

I would hope maybe a revision to this DLC could be made and the Devs could come back and sort of re-arrange things and add more to it like the lack of there being Muskogee which is a huge thru-way for trucks in real life.\t\t\t\t\t
