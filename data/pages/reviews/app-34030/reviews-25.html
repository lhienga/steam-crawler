\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me preface by saying my hours on Steam are not reflective of my actual playtime, I have more like 100 hours.

Napoleon is a good game, but it has many flaws. Shogun 2: Fall of the Samurai is a better version of this game in almost every way. Artillery feels good to use and actually decides battles. Cavalry have physical impact when they charge units (I.E. knocking over soldiers). Regiments are responsive and have good path finding. The battle AI can be very challenging. These are all things Napoleon lacks. Fall of the Samurai is better in every way mechanically. If you get this game, it's because of the time period and location it takes place in. That being said, the Napoleonic Wars were freaking awesome, so that is a totally valid reason to buy the game. I would highly recommend getting mods for it if you do, though. DarthMod, for example, increases the scale of battles and allows ranks in a regiment to fire; it makes the game unbelievably cinematic and much more fun (and in some cases tolerable) to play.\t\t\t\t\t
