\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst and foremost I will state that Napoleon Total War i one of my favorite Total War games and i have played it for many years, with as double more hours in Offline than the ones shown in Steam.

I had a great time playing in Single player, either Vanilla and in the recent years with Mods ranging from Darthmod, NTW3 and others and also in MP.

Did countless MP campaigns with friends and despite not being perfect they were innovative in what they introduced to the Saga, and therefore welcomed.

I would recomend this game always and got several friends to actually buy the game and play it with me or for themselfs.


I cannot however and with great sorrow recomend any of the OG Total War games as they stand. CA has sold its soul, focusing on the Warhammer TW´s and new titles and have not only abandoned but sabotaged their older games.

Not only have they ruined the MP experience by forcing players to procure a 3rd party communication platform to play such as discord or ts3, their "updates" of games like Napoleon,Empire,Shogun,Rome and others broke the Single Player Experience by not only breaking said games but also breaking the modding behind them.


Never in all these years playing, in this specific case NTW, have i had problems running Napoleon, either SP or MP(battles or campaigns) other than the rare crash but now, having picked it up again this last week, in the same system i cant play no more than 6 or 7 campaign turns in Vanilla MP Campaign without some sort of game breaking bug or crash.

My friends experience the same especially in ETW,NTW and Shogun 2. These games are now unplayable, thus forcing the us, the veterans who grew up with these games to move on to newer and "cleaner" TW´s.

The Thing is, we wont, these games are our essence, the OG´s as well, Rome and Shogun 1, both Medievals made us and now we can barely play them.

CA will keep making its money and therefore ignore our voices regardless of what is written here but i cannot in good conscience recommend any CA titles anymore as they are broken and the newer titles are a DLC money grabbing fest(as its costumary nowadays overall).

A sad time to be a strategy fan and a CA fan even more.
