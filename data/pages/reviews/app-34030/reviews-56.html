\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was one of the best total wars to ever exist. Beaten really only by Shogun 2 and Rome 2 is a close 2nd place. (Of course modding it to not have black female generals in literal germanic tribes or Rome itself. Sorry laws of the land didn't magically stop because it's what if. Add an edict that explains why then I will roll with it.)

Long winded sentences later, this game allows you truly to have probably one of the best Napoleonic war experiences. March of the Eagles is also very damn good. But this game really took the time for every event to still be squeezed into it. Only few things this game lacked was the fact Austria didn't exist as a sole entity nation in the later 1700s. Basically the Holy Roman Empire was obliterated by Napoleon and economic factors outside which lead to it's break up. Leading to Prussian states, the Hapsburgs and even the Netherlands. Which was crazy come to think of it.

Regardless. This game covers everything from 1795 to 1815. 20 solid years of Napoleon into one game, this game also gives light on his Marshals which really is over looked, usually only Ney gets a spotlight but this game gave Davout, Murat, and Soult a real examination.

It's worth full price. QUADRUPLE if sale.\t\t\t\t\t
