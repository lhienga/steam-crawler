I thought that this one would be different since the period is so interesting. However, TW:Napoleon suffers from tedious management issues, chaotic battles, and a general dullness that is hard to endure for long. If you are going to make the mega-game and strategic issues boring, then at least have cool tactical battles. But alas, another lackluster entry in the Total War series.

Although it's a bit old, I would highly recommend Imperial Glory (2005) as an alternative to this game. Its campaign and management system are much better put together, and the battles are more straightforward and easy to follow.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNapoleon was always a fascinating historical figure to me, I read anything that I got about him.
He brought hope to my people of Poland and we fought on his side to the end.
The game is great, great mechanisms and is very fun. The voice actor for napoleon is well-chosen, the story is great and generally enjoyable, fun.
I am a bit disappointed of not finding a single Polish general in the game, not even Marshal Poniatowski, who was one of Napoleons favourite generals alongside Dabrowski, who was vital in the Italian campaign. I am glad that there are the two Polish units though.
There should be Polish lancers at waterloo, I must add!\t\t\t\t\t
