\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasically Empire without all the bugs and localized to Europe, which makes it one of the best Total War titles to date (only beaten by Shogun 2 imho; also, no colonizing so yaaay). The land battles are amazing. Get the right mods and the landscape will be blanketed in smoke, lit from within with sporadic bursts of red and orange from cannon and musket fire. The naval battles are also very well done. You actually have to utilize naval tactics to achieve victory vs spamming the best ships (I'm looking at you Rome 2 and Attila with your "rams go brrrr" bs). Campaign-wise it is great for a TW game. you move armies around the map while dealing with political and occupation wrought unrest and building up your cities to be military or economic powerhouses (or both, but I rarely recruit from more than a couple cities myself). There is also a technology tree that does everything from boosting economic and political power, to giving you new units like steamships. All in all, a solid 8/10, but that can easily be upped to 9 with the right mods.\t\t\t\t\t
