\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll of the total war games I have played have been 7/10-10/10 games in my mind but this one is a bit different. While it is amazing and it builds upon the last game quite well I do think that the main fun comes from playing with real people less the campaign. This isn't to say the campaign is bad; it is clunky in parts, and has a lot of bugs. Some of the audio bugs are very annoying to the point of unplayable. None of this is a problem when playing against friends. I also think that the slow paced extremely cinematic nature of this game allows for more strategy to unravel as the fight goes on. Which is a lot of fun to feel the tensions rising as you sit in a discord call swearing at units with your friends vs waiting for dudes to walk a long way to kill some ai. If I am alone and playing a total war game then I am just gonna play Shogun 2. I rate it 7-6/10 alone and 12/10 with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
