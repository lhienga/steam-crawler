\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed a few dozen hours on a cracked version SP so I decided to buy the game with the autumn sale. It's worth it only with a discount (it's a 12 year old game after all). Although what I've said previously, this doesn't negate that this game holds up very well even now. Troops positioning and attacking might bug seldom but for one of the old TW games, it's very enjoyable to this day.
-The SP is ok, the campaign can become a bit boring (as it always is in any TW game), pretty straight forward systems on your nation's management and income producing
-The AI is stubborn and pretty rigid, and might make stupid decisions, but it's passible.
-I recomand to play "Napoleon's battles" with hard AI for some enojoyable and thrilling SP experience
-The real that all people wanna hear about, however,are of course the battles and the MP.
-I had a pretty enjoyable MP experience, although there are some toxic sweaters as there are in any MP game.Some form of "meta" exists, but the simplicity and enjoyablity of the comes from the fact that within that meta (that you can ignore and don't follow) you have creative freedom in deciding how to operate and maneuver your troops. Discuss with other players before a battle the plan, and from there try to put it in action, but expect many chances in situation. Bombard the hell out of the opponets with artilery and march your troops as effective as possible in good positions to tear down the enemy units. Again, might be a bit buggy time to time, but it's hardly intrerupting with the good gameplay. Recomand it if found with discount.\t\t\t\t\t
