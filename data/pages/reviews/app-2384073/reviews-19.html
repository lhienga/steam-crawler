\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTheme of the new region is great, but could use better direction and incentive to explore every nook and cranny like I did with the base game. I don't think it's worth the 10$ unless you really desire the new status themed subclass.


Re-used Losomn assets galore, some areas literally layed out the same as regular Losomn, vast spans of areas that maybe have 1 new item (sometimes just base Losomn loot generates), 90% of loot is Iron/scrap.

New enemy types are very stagger resistant. The multiheaded sewer spitters for example; 1 shot with high speed projectiles that they then follow up with a second in 0.2 seconds leaving no time to react or keep distance, while they also tend to be in groups so you are dealing with 2-6 at a time. I utterly despise having to use Song of Aefir just cause the enemy is not tuned for any form of counterplay other than slow+fire tornado from a distance.\t\t\t\t\t
