\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly super disappointed in this one. I thought Losomn was the best new world Gunfire Games added to the Remnant series and they made it completely unbearable with the addition of this DLC. They introduced a total of 3 new Elite enemies which 2 of they turned into bosses. They added a couple of normal world enemies that are horribly scaled and can one shot you on normal difficulty. The general world mapping is very confusing/maze-like and has many areas with 0 loot or purpose. The Ritualist class is very underwhelming as DoTs do very little damage and have much less viability in this game compared to Remnant 1. The cherry on top was the fact that once the first play through of the DLC content, you are susceptible to encountering base game dungeons in the DLC world... Gunfire couldn't even produce enough content to make the DLC only DLC content. So without further adieu, I have to give this DLC a 2/10 and a very negative review.\t\t\t\t\t

FPS drop do happen but luckily only in the main big area ( for me at least ).

Overall mixed experience with this one but new gear is always welcomed addition in my books.\t\t\t\t\t
