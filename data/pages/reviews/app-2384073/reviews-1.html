\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't initially intend to post a review for this DLC, but seeing as it's got mixed reviews I think I need to. Nowadays it's extremely common to release DLC for prices of an actual game. For a tenner this DLC is ridiculously good value. The overworld is visually stunning, there's 26 new rings, 12 new amulets, 7 new weapons, a new archetype and more. The rings are very tailored to the new archetype, as you'd expect and to partner this Ritualist is so much fun. Playing coop, if someone uses the 2nd ability and then the other person uses the first ability it one-shots most enemies. I do feel there could be more bosses (Only 3 new bosses + the world boss) but because you can get the Losomn dungeons (This DLC is basically an extension to Losomn) there isn't a significant lack of bosses you can face. I have a mixed opinion on the final boss, it can be quite easy but also quite difficult even on the same difficulty. It completely depends on your build and the suffixes the boss gets (Specifically the stagger bonus makes it hard to get the dodges right). But overall it's a pretty fun fight and can provide a challenge if you really want it to.

For the price the DLC is definitely worth it, but if you're not super into the game it doesn't add that much content (time wise), but I find it has really good replayability and is really fun especially in coop and with the new crossplay update it's even better (Becareful of desync, Idk if it's crossplay or my friends terrible WiFi (He literally lags just as much in solo)). But yeah it's fun\t\t\t\t\t
