Plus really great quality of life features for multiplayer. If you own this DLC and a friend doesn't, they can still play with you, they will just get generic rewards rather than the DLC exclusive loot.

Events, Quests, and the overall story are solid. What was most exciting was how the DLC would react when you had base game dungeons in the mix post your first run.

The new archetype is a big success. It's strong in concept and gameplay and all the status effect gear in this DLC add a lot to help mix up your builds.

End boss is well designed, matching the quality of the base game and excelling its peers. Though it is a touch easier for those who have smashed through the main game.

Solid addition. Well worth the money. Hope Gunfire can keep this up for the other Docs.\t\t\t\t\t
