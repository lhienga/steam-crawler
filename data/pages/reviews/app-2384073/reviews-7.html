The Green/Purple bloaters have very high stagger,very good health,high speed(on par with the spear Fae it seems) and are often fought in tight areas where you either fall to your death,or get ganked because they just stagger you down.

The purple bloaters have a ranged spew attack that functions like a root rot that you can't see though because it takes up your whole screen. With either armor or DR that makes them tankier.

Boss fights are fun. It's just Lossom again,and it honestly feels like the finale to the Lossom arc. Lossom Archetype I haven't found yet. But I had to take a break after getting frustrated on my second run because the stupid bloaters are just so damn annoying.

I do but don't reccomend it. I wish I could put 'Buy on sale' but it's not worth the full price imo. Needs some balance passes.\t\t\t\t\t
