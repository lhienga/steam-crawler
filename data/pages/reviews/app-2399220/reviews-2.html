And vice versa if you think about Nukitashi as about some stupid hentai I recommend you to check Nukitashi 2nd op to give you an idea of the atmoshere and what you can expect in this work.

Because it's... a fun comedy battle-shounen.

It's ironic that h-scenes kinda weak despite the crazy setting in vn, like almost all of the h-scenes involving main heroines on average moege level. So this should sound like a joke, but I really read this for story only, except couple h-scenes.

And I was going to give Nukitashi 8.5\\10, but... true route just too long and went in some kind of simple drama with cheap shounen crap.

I like characters and especially antagonists, they are well-written and interesting to read, I like setting, great ost, funny jokes and plot overall, but in the same time I just found Nukitashi too repetitive and too chained to the shounen rails.
Also as for battle-shounen, battle scenes here forces you to use your imagination way too much, which is not a good thing for vn of this kind imo. They are still fun to read and maybe devs were out of budget so they can't make them better, but still, there are so many battle scences that I can't avoid criticizing them.

7.9\\10
