\r\n\t\t\t\t\t“Nukige Mitai na Shima ni Sunderu Watashi wa Dou Surya Ii Desu ka?” also known as Nukitashi. This is the very first title developed by Qruppo and was released in Japan back in 2018 and has won 2 awards in the Moe Game Award 2018, both being New Brand award and Scenario Award. While its next sequel, “Nukige Mitai na Shima ni Sunderu Watashi wa Dou Surya Ii Desu ka? 2” has won the Grand Prize in the Moe Game Award 2019. Seeing Nukitashi 1 and 2 achieving these 3 awards in just 2 years might mean nothing and I used to think that too. But the truth is that receiving awards from the Moe Game Award is a pretty big deal.(at least in Japan) Back when Kinkoi came out, I was not interested due to the averageness in the art and synopsis, but given that they won Second place and Gold Award in the Moe Game Award 2017 I decided to give it a try and it blown my mind upside down inner out and now Kinkoi is one of my favourite visual novel of all time.

Oh right, I forgot that this is a review section, and I am supposed to tell you if Nukitashi is worth it or not. It’s definitely a BIG YES from me. Although I haven’t finished the entire game, let alone even started it, but, I have read the manga version of Nukitashi up until the latest chapter. All I can say is, I got cliff hanged and it really piqued my interest, and I am dying to know what’s going to happen next in the story. This game is probably not suitable for you if you don’t like how the story setting is which is pretty much going to happen throughout the entire story, but otherwise, try it out and give it a go, you will definitely love this as much as I do. I am really grateful for what Shiravune has contributed to the VN community, releasing a bunch of good titles into the global market and letting more and more players know the charms of Japanese visual novels. Cheers for Shiravune, and hopefully they will bring the announcement of Nukitashi 2 and Hentai Prison(currently the latest title from Qruppo which won Grand Prize/First place/User support award in the Moe Game Award 2022) and I am willing to sacrifice my wallet for them.

Also, the story is definitely not just 2 hours, the entire game length is about 40-50 hours depending on how fast you read, Shiravune said it’s 2 hours long because you know, I know, we know. Get the patch from Shiravune’s website(Johren), restore everything back and enjoy your long awaited journey, unless you weren’t even waiting for Nukitashi’s release nor knew anything about Nukitashi in the first place. Another good news is that as Nukitashi 1 released, Nukitashi 2 has been confirmed on its release and is currently being translated by Shiravune team as well, can't wait for it.

终于啊，拔作岛1来了，所以我的拔作岛2呢？随着1上架，Shiravune也宣布2正在翻译中估计2上架应该也是几个月以后的事情了吧。其实我还蛮犹豫的，如果我买了1然后推完了要等2，如果太久我又会忘记剧情，到时候我不就又要把1推多一次？尴尬死了。。。不过我还是打算先把1推完因为之前已经读过漫画版本了所以有点按耐不住想要了解整部故事。绝对不是涩涩的部分被漫画砍了所以只好冲原作来满足自己

我个人还蛮推荐这部GAL的，主要原因是角色画风很有特色也很对我胃口，故事也很特别，OPED歌曲也喜欢，而且这部GAL根本就是每个男人的梦想，想干就干谁不要谁就是没出息+你就是在说谎因为没有男人能拒绝这样的故事背景and say NO！！再说了，Qruppo真的有在关心海外市场，他们这段时间有在推特上放一些有趣的中英配音，都是声优们努力尝试说中英来拉满各位海外兄弟们的兴趣。我是真的被感动到，我还真的没看过那个GAL公司会出来宣传自家作品给海外玩家们，只能说Qruppo诚意真的满满。不废话了，买吧别等了。
