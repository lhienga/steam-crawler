\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVisually speaking, Rune Factory 3 Special got a much better treatment than Rune Factory 4 Special did. This time the UI and 2D sprites also got major resolution improvements (instead of sloppy filters), making for a much, much better experience. It would've been amazing to see the backgrounds get redrawn, but they are not as bad as on RF4S. Overall, this is the best looking isometric Rune Factory game ever.

However, just as it goes with most re-releases, it's not perfect. The script and dub cast changes are considerable drawbacks to a lot of people who played the original game, and even for those who didn't, there will be an underlying feeling of not getting the same experience people did back in the day. I'll still give it a recommendation because I think they did a really good job enhancing the graphics. Still, there'd be much more value to it if they added new content/features and kept the original intro, scripts, and dub cast people love.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy favorite of the Rune Factory games

As a port of a DS game it works well, all of the functionality was preserved but it's quite bare-bones as ports go with the most notable thing being that the original opening song of the game was changed to a new one so we lost the amazingly haunting song that is "HAPPINESS" by Yazumi Kana (which you should go listen to right now if you never have).

This game is a farm-simulator RPG that leans heavily into the RPG aspect with multiple weapons, weapon types, spells and level-ups, you have to engage in combat quite frequently to progress in the farm aspect so it is good to know what to expect. I like this game quite a ways more than Rune Factory 4 (although that one would be the best one to start with if you're new) this one is more compact with a shorter story, incredible characters, amazing soundtrack and smooth gameplay with tons of quality of life features.

All in all give this game a chance if you're into real time action RPG's and want to be surprised by how in depth and addictive the farming element is.\t\t\t\t\t
