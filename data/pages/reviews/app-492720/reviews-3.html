Being a city builder, i would expect the fun of optimization being a matter of experience and knowledge as opposed to confusing and often misleading status messages, such as the "efficiency" value attached to all buildings. All in all, it's definitely worth the price but it's not as great a game as it could be.\t\t\t\t\t
