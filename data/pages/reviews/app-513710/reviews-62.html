\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst I'd like to say this game has a ton of potential and a vast gaming experience for the survival and FP shooter lover. It's a gorgeous game and some really well thought out aspects on survival, hunting, FP shooting etc. I will look forward to future developments. However, in the 40hrs I have played SP mode I'm not sold on the game. Yes it's in early access for 5 years now, yes it's still being developed and a lot of work has gone into making this game, there's no doubt about that. For the SP that is not into competitive multiplayer, I'm not sure this game is worth it at this time.

Day One-Getting a feel for the game, start crafting tools/weapons, live off cactus and aloe plants. Look for loot and anything I want to craft doesn't exist through 3 towns. Thread and food being a main focus along side killing the same puppets that spawn within 30secs of killing them. Ok fine, tweak some settings. Once in a while I heard chickens and goats, look into how to hunt them but was never successful at ever finding them. They may be a myth.

Day 2-Saw a patch with crafting update, I get excited. Yes, I found some thread! Ok cool, so now I'm thinking I can make more stuff. I find a wheelbarrow and start stashing loot in it so I can use it once I find all the mats I need. Run around a few towns with this thing gathering mats and call it a day.

Day 3-I find a drill press, excellent go get my wheelbarrow and wheel it over. Maybe I can craft some cool stuff! Now I hear a wolf. If you don't go hunt this thing right away the howling will drive you insane. So I go kill it, finally. It was right outside the house I was looting where the drill press was. Grab the goods and head back to my drill press and stash. Well WTF?! it all dissapeared. So disappointed, I google what the hell happened and this is normal. There are spawn timers on the items, which an explanation was given as to why the devs did it this way. There is also no option to turn that off in SP. So I'm thinking well how the hell do you craft anything if it takes you 3 towns and many hours of game time of looting to get all the mats you need to have it all vanish. Cool stuff...

The more I read into forums, the more I found out that SP is not a main focus of this game. Yes, I understand this game is still in development, but I'm not interested in just running around killing puppets. How boring. I'm more into survival aspect and crafting. If the devs want to focus on multi player, then just make it multi player. At this time I do not recommend it for SP. Maybe a year down the road this will be a better gaming experience for the SP, but the 40hrs I've put in all I got was this stupid wolf hat!\t\t\t\t\t
