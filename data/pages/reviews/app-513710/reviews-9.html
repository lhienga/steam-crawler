\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my favorite survival games of all time.

The level of detail is mind-blowing. The UI is abit clunky but I find its OK, I've looked up tons of tutorials and helpful tips to learn the game.

The healing function IS abit confusing. I initially put that here as a caveat and turns out they DID fix it awhile back. When you're trying to heal, click and highlight your rags or bandages, and go to Health tab and select treat after highlighting the injury. Easy as that.

I'm also a fish enthusiast in real life so seeing such a detailed fishing mini-game is awesome. I was excited to see Orange Ruffe here, and I caught a Bass and a Pike.

The combat is difficult and thats OK. The assets for the houses add abit of charm, but there could be more variety... in sims like this its neat to see what civilization looked like BEFORE things went bad. Considering its a small team of developers, I'm impressed that they've created a whole world.

I love this game and I've loved it for a few years now. I look forward to seeing what they come up with next.\t\t\t\t\t
