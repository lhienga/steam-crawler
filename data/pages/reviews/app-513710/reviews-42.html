\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've recently come back to the game after a long hiatus and I've found that the recent updates have been mostly positive. I have absolutely no complaints from the survival aspect, a lot of the effects and crafting options make as much sense as they can within a game.Generally I love the direction that Scum is heading in, although I do have some concerns that I hope get addressed.

The first issue is with the "puppets" (zombies.) The new horde mechanic is generally a welcome addition, although I think there needs to be some further troubleshooting to prevent them from spawning everywhere around the player. It can be easy to get completely surrounded and whilst yes, it does make you have to think about things, sometimes an area can go from being totally safe to a total nightmare in a matter of seconds which does seem a bit silly.

The second issue, for me at least, is to do with PvP servers. I know the developers aren't responsible for the actions of players in game, but right now the game actually rewards griefers and trolls. PvP is too difficult for new players to get into when they're playing solo. PvE is great but can get a little boring if there's nobody really communicating. I wonder if there should be a mechanic whereby you're given grief points or something when killing other players, and when it reaches a certain threshold their location appears on the map for others to see. It might help prevent one or two players absolutely dominating with the best equipment.

Additionally I wonder if player factions might help the situation, so as a for instance, when you create a character you either select or get thrown into a team. The map could then be divided in half and then players could fight over or capture territory from the other team?

Just a few ideas. But generally I'm happy with the progress that has been made. Hope that V.1 gets released soon!\t\t\t\t\t

One of the worst games I have played years after it's been updated from it's Alpha Release.

The melee is as bad as early 7 Days to Die. You swing at a zombie that is right up against you and the first 2 hits connect, you get the audio cue and they stumble. 3rd hit you get an audio cue and they act like nothing happened. 4th, 5th, and 6th hits your arm goes right through them, no audio cue, and no stumble animation.
