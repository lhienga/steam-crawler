Also I will speak for Single player only.

+Graphics-With an RTX 3050 You can play mostly on Epic to high settings its optimized good although Shadows is where it performs the worst set to low or medium, The island is really beautiful, the textures for every item is really nice. Overall 8.5/10

+Gameplay-The Most important in every game. The Zombies in this game can be a tough challenge to beginners cause they really love to rush at you and without a gun (which will take some time to find) 1 zombie can be hard to deal with melee, let alone if you get swarmed you DEAD!!! Maybe it was meant to test your skills?. Gun head? then you'll love what this game has to offer, the mechanics to shooting is great 9/10.
Vehicles are okay not to clunky plus more Inventory space for you assuming you can find one in top shape, P.S you will be walking a lot my friends!!!! The metabolism system is not complex as it first appears but is done super well,to know what you ate and how it will affect your character is a nice touch to the survival.
Game has crafting as well not complex but will take some time to figure out, base building is a bit tedious personally for 1 person it will take a lot of time and resources but its worth it in the end.
OVERALL 9/10

+Sound-Island got lots of funny noises to keep you on the edge ,mainly birds and wind gusts :) keeps the island alive i guess, guns sounds are pretty decent and loud watch yourself. 7.5/10

+++++CONS+++++
-Weather could use an upgrade it rains but nothing actually gets wet other than you.Lightning storms are blinding they are like flash bangs MY EYES!!!!
-Zombies AI weird as heck and lack any variety(they added some new type but its restricted to specific bunkers)
-Hunting is lame, its use to be better but now its a strange mini game.
*Most important CON*
They tend to do sever wipes when they add or change something in the game and if you work your butt of on base building that's frustrating as heck you lose every item you have,sometimes its broken my game !!!

OVERALL 9/10 :)\t\t\t\t\t
