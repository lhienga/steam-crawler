\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo seems I purchased this right as they updated to a version that makes the game unplayable. You can not survive for more than 24 hours as a horde of puppets (zombies) will spawn surrounding you. Every hit stuns you briefly, and as you will be surrounded and being pounded you will have no chance to attack or even run.

Guns are overly complicated, you have to go into inventory to reload, manually dragging bullets to a clip and dragging clips to the weapon. This makes reloading in combat a death sentence. Hitting R just unloads the current clip to your inventory. I had to go online to find out how to load, check to see i you have any bullets in your gun, switch fire modes for the gun, aim. This is not intuitive or explained anywhere in the menus.

I only "found" a gun because of the Christmas event going on in game that, rarely, spawns a ghost Santa that gives you a present while looting a random thing, food plants for me. Not that it did me any good.

I want to like this game, it has alot of potential, but the horde spawning in and mobbing you to death makes it pointless to play.\t\t\t\t\t
