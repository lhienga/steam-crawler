\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to recommend this game, I really do. However, I just cannot at this time. The concept is something great but the execution is just not there. One thing to keep in mind is that it isn't finished. They are still working out a lot of the issues but sometimes for better and sometimes for worse. A couple of pros and cons:

Cons first - Almost every major update has me asking WTF did they do that for?!?
- All the major updates are followed immediately with hotfixes almost like they didn't even bother
to test the update for shipping it
- The game has gotten more and more painful to play. (i.e. You need to go into abandoned bunkers
to get good firepower, but without already having great gear and good firepower the abandoned
bunkers are almost guaranteed death. Which out of 25 ish bunkers in the game they made 17 of
them abandoned.)
- It feels like the devs wake up each morning and ask themselves how they can make it even
harder to survive, taking mechanics that were challenging but good and making them insanely
difficult

Now to give some of the good points and why I still play and have hope that it will be great when it's finished:

Pros - The concept. The PvPvE inmates vs other inmates vs puppets (zombies) in a contained zone
fighting over survival gear, weapons, food, vehicles, and for fame is pretty interesting.
- Map. The map is very large and with different biomes, weather and themes.
- Setting adjustments. In single player and on private servers the setting can be adjusted to create
a play experience the way you like. This is a pro but the limited ability and confusing explanations
for some settings is a definite con.

Final thoughts:

This game has a ton of potential. In its current state I cannot recommend it for everyone or even the casual survival game player. If you are the kind of player who likes to build yourself up only to lose everything because a bomb wearing "beeper" spawns behind you 10 seconds after clearing the room. And you can get it on sale, then by all means go for it.\t\t\t\t\t
