\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're a fan of DayZ, you'll enjoy it. this game is pretty complicated and takes a decent learning curve. It has pretty hardcore survival elements and is more in-depth than other games in the genre I've played. There's dozens of servers to pick from (like ARK), and some have special modifiers.

A couple of my problems with the games, I'm not sure if it's server settings but the enemy spawns are a problem. I'll be clearing out a bunker, only to find that the way that I cleared has enemies who already respawned. Sometimes the the enemies are glitchy and float around in the area. Like most games in this genre, you can spend hours wandering around to try and find items. I have 8 hours in this game and I have yet to find another player. I play on servers that have increase loot, but yet there's hardly any loot.

This game is a real barebones survival and is unforgiving. If you enjoy DayZ, ARK, and others, give it a shot, I don't think you'll be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
