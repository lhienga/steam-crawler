\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUPDATE (still do not recommend): I continued to play for a bit assuming the backlash from the previous update would elicit some serious action by the devs. To the dev's credit a fraction of those bugs were resolved. But they have been replaced by new ones. Not only that but now I get fatal error crashes that did not previously happen. Does it sound like the game is getting better? 5 years in early access? Funny enough, 6 months ago I would have recommended this game when I was playing almost everyday.

Aside from the fact the game is barely playable at the moment, these fixes also tend to make QOL changes that remove enjoyable features like riding a bike. After the 30 seconds it takes to deplete stamina when riding a bike, you simple stop pedaling. My character can run for twice as long on foot with 45 kg of gear. I can run faster and farther on foot than on a bike. How does that make sense? Just remove the bike instead of making it useless. I'm actually irritated at how sloppy that change is. And finally this experience has helped cement the idea that if an EARLY ACCESS game has multiple DLCs beyond reasonable supporter packs, then the game is fair game for higher scrutiny. I don't mind supporting EA releases, but the ones I've played with DLCs give me a sickly suspicion that the devs are actually just ivy league MBAs figuring out how to milk their market rather than an artist or a storyteller.

This game, Scum, feels less and less like a passion project and more like the product of some corporate think-tank.

FIRST REVIEW (did not recommend):
I loved this game and played basically non-stop since last summer. I feel the horde update is so jarringly different that it has spoiled the fun. Stealth and camouflage skills are absolutely useless now. The puppets spawn right on top of you, and even if not, there's always one that spawns in and no matter where or what happens this one puppet will find you even if you run through bushes. When it finds you, it triggers a horde. Doesn't matter if it's a silenced gun, bow/arrow, or melee. Once you kill it, it triggers a horde. And if you've managed to clear a POI, you only have a few seconds before the puppets start spawning in again. Bears, wolves, and boars also spawn right on top of you.

This update brought about the worst bug so far too. Pulling items from a storage chest have a chance to despawn the items. Yes, bug reports will say the item goes under the storage chest and to move it. I do every time! I move the storage, see the item, and it falls through the ground before I can do anything.

I love the server that I'm on but this update has broken me out of the game. The devs focus on realism in really innovative ways but this update has basically undone that by giving it an arcade feel. Is it going to be *immersive survival horror* or another arma 3 copy? In the game's current state, being a solo player is impossible. Stealth tactics don't matter even with adv skills. So it's a team-oriented shoot em up game with a sprinkle of survival elements.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's so beyond me how anyone enjoys this game. I can not find one redeeming thing about it. I think I bought it on a deep sale. I played single player only.

On the technical side, I'm getting so much pop-in from flora textures, at varying distances too. Some of this is happening almost right next to me. I'm getting a decent amount of stuttering too. I've tried on different settings as well. I'm playing on a RTX 2070. So right off the bat you have this distracting happening that messes with my head and makes me anxious.

The gameplay is immensely boring. Project Zomboid moves at a much better pace and that moves slow but it's fine. There is a steep learning curve that will involve doing actual work and research well beyond an average complex game. The game tells you little and you don't even get all the keybindings in the options so you have to look online for all the hotkeys. This is crazy and shows little respect for the player.

There is no narrative whatsoever (this is probably obvious but still, there is zero, so more boring)

Combat is horrible but I didn't find any ammo yet. Melee combat is low tier jank, I can't imagine gun play being fun.

Looting is horrible. I get it, there's scarcity, but you search soooooo many containers and usually get nothing and carryweight is very tough. Item size makes no sense sometimes. Small items can be way too big. So when you do find random materials and whatnot you're not carrying it anyway. I came across two bikes with no front tire. Wow, really making me work for that, huh? The map is huge, give me a break. You also find stuff in random places, I assume scripted, to help create game flow but it feels artificial and unimmersive. Like ok, you played long enough and suffered so you can get a backpack now, or whatever.

Enemy variety is weak. What's the deal with these giant mechs that guard what I assume are military bases? You can't really tell what they are, just large points of interest. They feel way too out of place. There was this random laughing guy that blows himself up on you. What the heck is that? Dumb. The zombies themselves have a low variety as well. Like man, if that's the main enemy make more clothing.

Script spawning feels off. I'll look around an area, see and hear nothing, and then I'm in a house, crouching and being quite and then I have four zombies running towards me out of nowhere. They spawn randomly in the field too. I cleared a farm house and then another spawned within minutes right there, and then another. Bad scripted spawning is a game killer for me. Just like State of Decay 2.

Map design is uninspiring and bland. Relating to loot, what happened to all the stuff?? Buildings are barren and feel so random.

Crafting. I never got to craft anything and it's another game that has you chopping down trees. Crafting looks tedious and the menu navigation needs help.

Skills. I didn't have much chance to use any skills. This system seems fine enough for a survival game.

Sound design is serviceable.

Even if you like the slow moving survival zombie genre, I can't understand enjoying this game.\t\t\t\t\t
