\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing this game for a few years now, since about 0.2 if I remember right. I've seen things come and go, and although I realise it's Early Access the development of side content rather than addressing some of the basic mechanics has been problematic at times. I was very excited to have cars come, then more types of vehicles, then they got removed, and only 2 types of very bugged car were available. There have been a number of fun additions, like new points of interest, but some of them (like the nuclear plant) have me disappointed. I'm also an avid S.T.A.L.K.E.R. fan and it honestly seems like they were wanting to wrap that into the ever-creeping scope of the game. And it destroyed one of my favorite corners of the map, I loved having a base up by the now-gone campground.
I've still kept playing, even with not being interested in the new bunker mutants or the broken fishing process. However, with the 0.95 release I am finally out. It has been uninstalled, and I have left the island. I was here for a survival game and what was, in its original dynamic (and from what I could tell, intent) a survival game with zombies mixed in until real NPC interaction could happen in future updates. In its dynamic now it appears to be headed more for a zombie game with some survival around them. Walking through the forest I have a puppet appear 8 feet in front of me, and so I fight it. Another one hears it, screams for backup, and within 15 seconds I'm dead. Respawn, and it drops me right North of the nuclear plant, which is toxic without gear.
Speaking of gear, I did buy supporter packs 1 and 2 when they came out but there now seems to be many DLC packs for a game that is still in progress. Given the cool features that have been introduced then taken away I'm not keen on spending more money on something that might get deleted at some point.
I'm sure there are many who love this game, and if you do, fill your boots. As for me, I'm finally off the island.\t\t\t\t\t
