\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn interesting game which i just kept trying to enjoy myself but ultimately failed. I tried and tried and tried but ultimately when they changed the horde mechanic, that was the final straw for me. I play single player only and now there is simply no immersion whatsoever.
You can turn around and then behind you things just respawn. Look one way then look the other and wham, that area you just spent ages carefully clearing is full of zombies (they call them puppets) again.
Been a long time since i've seen such an amateurish change in a core mechanic for a game. They literally just pop up in front of you now. Bye bye what little realism there was.

For positives it looks very pretty but the game lacks variety and everything just becomes a rinse, repeat as, at least in my view, the building assets are very limited in number. It's the same building again and again. I eventually thought i'd try out the main city but it's extremely limited as you can't access many of the buildings. Mile wide inch deep isn't fair but that expression does loom for me.
The map is huge which is great, but you really need a vehicle. I've played over 80 hours and only seen one bicycle wheel. Just one. And guess what, you need two as you only ever come across an empty frame, at least in my experience. And yes i loot EVERYTHING and EVERYWHERE. Same with dirt bikes. Cars are somewhat easier but still a real pain in the backside finding the minimum parts you need to get one going. Last time i did this they then wiped the game as part of their update procedure. Ok EA i get it but in a game where doing and getting things is a deliberate struggle it's a real pain to have EVERYTHING wiped just because it's easier for you devs. Gees, thanks.

So in conclusion it's just another DayZ with some more fleshed out mechanics with respect to crafting and your medical fitness etc. If you're into single player like me then i wouldn't bother, it's really just MP as SP is just gonna waste your time.

Hope you have fun - i tried really hard but just did not, and they're close to full release now as i understand it.\t\t\t\t\t

It is very easy to survive. After about 2 hours you have the basics figured out and it is not hard.
Water and food are very easy to find.
Zombies are extremely easy to kill with the very first bow you craft in the first 2 minutes of the game.
PVP is easy to manage and avoid until you are ready to engage.
However, what is absolutely broken is the core of the game.
It has a huge map but almost zero vehicles.
I spent 10 irl hours using scum map to try to find a fixable vehicle and never found a single vehicle.
I even turned spawns up to max and just searched for hours....NOTHING!
What is the point of looting if it will take you 2 hours to run to a trader?
Why build a base? Traveling to and from your points of interest take hours.
I enjoy a very complex survival game but this one is not at all that!
the player base might think it is but it isn't.
I'm actually shocked that anyone plays this game but then I'm aware that some people also enjoy cutting themselves for pleasure as well!
save your money, this game is a complete waste!
First game I've ever requested a refund for!

There is no sustenance in this game, there is no more fun, no more laughter with friends, no anything. This game went from being interesting to fun, to a complete waste of time and space in my library. It has been unplayable for over a year now and no updates have tried to fix any of this issues present in this game, but lets keep adding things that don't matter and no one asks for I guess?

This game at this time makes me want to fist myself with barbed wire, while parachuting out of a plane while being eaten by a zombie bear. That would be less painful than playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
