\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not sure where to start. Firstly, I've noticed that the game takes quite a while to load on my high-end system, equipped with an Intel i9-9900k and an RTX 3070 Ti. Even when playing in single-player mode, I've encountered significant lag issues. To put it in perspective, I tried comparing it to a game that's over a decade old, 7 Days to Die, and Scum still has a long way to go in terms of performance.

One major concern is the limited variety of items to search for, as well as the challenging starting area. Being thrown into combat right from the beginning without any weapons or means of self-defense can be frustrating. It feels like you're thrown to the wolves with no way to craft weapons for protection.

After my initial experience, I'm left questioning whether this game is worth the wait or the price tag, especially considering the frustration I encountered in the first 10 minutes of gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
