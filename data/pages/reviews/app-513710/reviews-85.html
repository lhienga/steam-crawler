MY "NO VOTE" IS TEMPORARY AS OF NOW, SCUM HAS INSANE POTENTIAL,
BUT THE DEVS ARE GOING IN A BAD DIRECTION.

After 422 hours (and feels like longer) of SCUM, I would like to compare it with it's biggest opponent, that which is also my favourite survival game and have over 5000+ hours in, that game is DayZ.
Firstly I want to say that yes, I know this is not supposed to be exactly like DayZ, however I find that there are many aspects that can be improved in SCUM or taken directly from DayZ and implemented here to make this game a very good one.

SCUM used to be better in the past in my opinion, before all the changes to the hunting and the bunkers especially. I see that the Developers might have done those changes for the PVE player base, but those changes made a significantly negative impact on PVP servers, especially OFFICIAL or VANILLA loot servers, which by the way, it's horrible in this game, worse than DayZ's, on vanilla it could take incredibly long to get even the basic weapons and gear to defend yourself, and let's not mention food, especially do to the hunting rework, which was a stupid change, and was perfect before or almost was. Where you would run into animals naturally across the map without having to do a dumb mini game.

Like I said, I know this is only 0.9v and that is why the game can still be fixed. A few changes you need to add in this are:
- Change the look of towns (might be work in progress) every town looking the same is boring. DayZ’s cities are all unique and all offer a different vibe and scary feel to it.
- Add weapon world spawns in homes (important)
- Require less base building resources to build (annoying as a solo)
- If you will keep the hunting system this way, at least make pray animals or maybe just chickens, spawn near all or most towns, as an easier way to get food, similar to DayZ, again because vanilla loot can be rough.
- FAME Points are dumb the way they are now, you should be able to buy everything from trader from Fame Point 0, but an idea would be that more FAME makes items cheaper to buy.
- Add more Weapons and Clothes (I know this is still work in progress)
- Need better world spawns, vanilla is rough. Make more items spawn on the ground especially in military places.
- Generators to power up electrical stuff is too much. DayZ's way of getting in a home and cooking with natural resources is a way better system and straightforward.
- The Bunker rework was too much, or maybe not most of the bunkers should have been changed and only some. There aren't many military locations besides bunkers (as of 0.9v) so making all the bunkers this much more difficult, was an unnecessary change and only for PVE playerbase.
- The Map is too big, you rarely come across people even on high pop servers, either cut the map or somehow increase total player count.
- Cars spawning without an engine shouldn’t even be a thing. In official servers especially and those that are high pop, it’s near impossible to get a car going. The whole vehicle system needs to be looked at carefully.
- Vanilla loot is broken, you can search boxes for hours, which gets terribly annoying, and barely get geared, that is why I mentioned adding more world spawns to loot, like DayZ.
- As for Mechs, I understand they are part of the game and Lore, but having them in every military location gets a bit annoying, even knowing how to dodge them all. In military areas, you should only be concerned with whom you will PVP with there, not always having to worry about mechs. In DayZ walking in certain areas you can always expect PVP or be on alert, a feeling you don’t get in this game often.

I could go on and list more things, but like I said, SCUM is an alright game but if you want to make this game fun and last long, do not make things super complicated, and tweak a few others. Those above are some crucial changes in my opinion, and if you can somehow work on them, trust me it would make a great difference.
