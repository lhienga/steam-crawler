even though people compare this game to DayZ and say that it's not as good, I completely disagree, just imagine DayZ without mods.
I think that devs already added enough stuff to keep people busy for a long while, but the true problem here is not lack of fun, it's the fact that many aspects of the game are incomplete, not that they don't work, they just don't work like they are intended to, for example the melee fighting system, you bash a puppet(zombie) with a melee weapon and sometimes they don't even go into the "knocked" animation and immediately respond with another attack that not only hurts so much, that it will interrupt your next attack, which means if you're fighting more than one puppet you're going to be stucked in getting your cheeks clapped.
on the other hand they started a new system for (stabilizing the performance) in which puppets will only spawn in a certain radius of you, that makes the game less immersive and it will make having a long range gun unnecessary, in the other hand forcing players to go hand to hand with puppets with a broken melee system.
I think the devs only need to organize and polish this mountain of details instead of injecting more stuff to it.
sorry for taking your time and hope this review is helpful.\t\t\t\t\t
