\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTries to be a jack of all trades, master of none. Game is fun with friends, but can get boring after a while. As of 0.95 and the "the horde" update, AI is all over the place, walking through walls, spawning 2 metres from players. It's certainly better than the initial 0.95 version, but has a LONG way to go. The "would not recommend" doesn't mean I won't play the game, but it's not a game I could recommend to others in good conscience. It purports itself to have an extensive metabolism system yet lacks sickness from drinking dirty water, or broken limbs, or limb based symptoms (get hit in the arm and it causes you to limp). It feels like every feature is held together with duct tape and glue, that no one feature is ever brought up to at least 90% before a new feature is added. Feels like every feature is 40% complete, and bugs are fixed in the most janky ways possible. (their anti duplication "fix" isn't to just tell one player no you can't have that item, it's to kick one of those players from the server).\t\t\t\t\t
