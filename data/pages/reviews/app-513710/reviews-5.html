\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInitially I said I did not recommend this game in its current state. Well after playing it a little longer, I have changed my mind. This game has a way of sucking you in the longer you play. The game is challenging, and rewards patience.

Here's what I'd like to see after the full release:

1. Living NPCs walking around the world. Friendly and unfriendly. Maybe NPCs doing random things like hunting an animal, fighting puppets, attempting to rob the player, or repairing a vehicle. Things that give the illusion of a living world.

2. Perhaps some tweaks to the hunting system. Although I like a good challenge, currently it feels broken. Though this game does challenge you to get better overtime, and I do like that about it, I have serious issues just tracking an animal. Maybe a higher encounter rate would suffice? I'm not sure.

Anyway, great game overall. I am looking forward to the full release. I'd like to see this become a $60 game eventually. I want this game to be the best open world survival game on steam, and it does have things going for it.

Good luck devs.\t\t\t\t\t
