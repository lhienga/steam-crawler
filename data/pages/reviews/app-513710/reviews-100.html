\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust want to give this a complete thumbs up from the perspective of a female gamer. It is a social game where you can just drive around and not be serious, or the opposite, paying attention to every bit of food you eat and what weapons have the best stat like science level commitment to best possible outcomes.

Base building tools are fun and easy and the map is expansive with so many beautiful spots to linger and appreciate. Trips to trader depending on the server settings are a blast and it is super cool how private servers can really exercise many preferences of their own, like being able to sell puppet eyes at trader for cash. I would say that the wrong server preferences can make the game a drag so make sure you try out multiple servers for what suits you the best (or start your own) as some want a horde of puppets to play with and some want high loot or both.

I think the only frustrating thing for me as it applies to the game is how many times I have put days and days into base builds to only have them wipe due to updated content. That being said I have the same issue for just about every game I have in my Steam Library.

Another thing to keep in mind if you are having issues is that you could be trying to play on a rig that does not meet the minimum requirements to run the game. So if you find yourself falling through the world or losing vehicles you may want to do a quick double check of the system requirements and see how your system compares.

I am going to stop writing now so I can go play the game! Thanks for making a fun game with female character options and maybe female tattoo options in the future updates?\t\t\t\t\t
