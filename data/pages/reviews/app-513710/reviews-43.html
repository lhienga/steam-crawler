\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want to go into negative $$$ every time you die because you're just starting and you have no gear, this is the game for you.
If you want to travel for 3 hours straight in the largest map in existence to get back to your base because you have no option to respawn close to your base, due to lack of funds, then this is the game for you.
If you want to then die just outside of your base because you have to swim in a river to get there, and your character doesn't know how to swim even though his stamina is at full and you've dropped all of your gear in the river in a panic, then oh boy, this is the game for you.

This game looked so good before it came out. I was stoked for a hardcore realistic survival pvp game. I love difficult games. It makes the game last longer and it builds on your skills. My friends hate it, lmao.
I give this game a shot every now and then, a good healthy shot as you can see from my hours, but every time I'm slapped in the face with horrible and buggy mechanics. (Usually results in me rage quitting from losing everything due to bugs)
It's such a shame. This game has such potential to be good, but I just can't stick with it with the direction it's going. Wasting an entire day on this game just to have all of the efforts ripped away by a broken mechanic is just not worth it. And the money system they're using currently just doesn't work. I'm sure it's pushing new players away. You just can't get anywhere anymore. Starting a game on a new server and starting up a base was hard enough before I had to pay for everything I did. (Fame wasn't perfected either though...)

Like I've said, I've given this game a fair shot, but honestly, most of the hours spent in this game was just me TRYING to establish myself and then losing it all. I unfortunately can't recommend this game in it's current state. I really hope it gets better. It could be really cool. Even the option to have Dedicated Servers with custom options would be huge for everyone. Oh well. No one is going to read this. Wubba lubba dub dub. Squirrels and Potatoes.\t\t\t\t\t
