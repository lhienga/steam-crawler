\r\n\t\t\t\t\tafter playing this game I got to thinking. Maybe this is a game about magical puppies (zombies). they can teleport and warp suddenly near you. At first I imagined I could do stealth and finish off the pupets one by one. Turns out I was wrong. A room that was originally empty can suddenly be full of pupets that appear in the ground. Putting the zoom on the rifle doesn't seem to be useful against the pupet that comes near you.

regarding the direction of the sound is also frustrating. This game is quite promising with outdoor experiences such as hunting and gardening. Unfortunately, it is difficult to detect the prey animal and the direction the sound is coming from. Initially the sound came from the front after we approached it suddenly changed to the back. Come on, an FPS game that is simpler than this can provide reliable 3D sound.

I can give a suggestion, the pupet will be triggered to be at the location when we touch the circular area of 500-100-200 meters for close range pupet and 500 meters for the pupets on guard at the location so that players can feel the sensation of sniping pupettes from a long distance. After the trigger area, pupets will appear at the location so players can carry out stealth movements to finish them off one by one. The horde will be triggered 100 -200 meters from the trigger that triggered it and run towards the location so that it is realistic enough to create a sensation of covering fire for teammates on the team.

The small map in the bottom corner (like in the Zomboid project) is quite useful so we don't have to open the map often. we can assemble this tool or get it when looting. Like GPS. A magnifying glass can be used to zoom the map and a ballpoint can be used to make marks on the map. We can save location points in GPS so that we can easily return to that place. It's very annoying for forgetful people like me to lose boxes of things because we forgot where to bury them.

Hopefully the developer will not abandon this fun game and continue to develop it well.\t\t\t\t\t

UPDATE
I've gone ahead and made a reacquaintance post in regards to the game, to bring in some more detail about my experience. After a bit more time from this review, I've found that there are somewhat solid workarounds to the issues below, mainly the respawn bug. However, there's a caveat. It requires two people in order to fool-proof it.

Essentially, your body will despawn as soon as you respawn, I.E. hit random, sector or shelter respawn on death. Your loot despawning can be avoided if your teammate searches your body before you respawn.

This has made the game significantly more bearable and enjoyable to play, now knowing how to work around this. However, it's still not the solution, as this can plague solo players. Though, I have noticed in some casses my body would still stay even after respawning, so the bug comes and goes in some cases but most of the time it stays.

Here's my post about the game in more detail if you're curious to reading it.

Original Review
As fun as this game can be, it is a game plagued with bugs that continue to reappear even after 8 years of development. Unfortunately, a lot of these bugs are game breaking, which somehow do not receive immediate attention or have a temporary hotfix pushed out to resolve them.

Aside from the hair ripping frustrating game bugs, the gameplay is great. It is definitely one of my go-to OWSC zombie games that I will probably likely return to once a lot of the bugs have settled down. The 0.95 update seems to be quite buggy, so once this moves off of that and potentially releases, I.E. the 1.0 update, I would recommend not playing seriously, or avoiding trying to start in the game.

One of the worst bugs by far in this game is the consistent body/loot despawning issue that is all over the place, regardless of what happens. You die to an infection? All of your loot or body despawns, essentially not allowing you to go back and pick it back up. Die in a bunker but had a buddy with you? Your body despawns right in front of their eyes before they can even search it. Voila, all of your hard earned gear and loot is gone just like that.

Once the game comes out with a few updates/hotfixes, I will return and update this review. When and if 1.0 ever does release, I will give a new review.\t\t\t\t\t
