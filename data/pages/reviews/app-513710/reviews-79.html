Everything went absolutely wrong since the version 0.95 when the new unrealistic spawn system has been introduced and the game received incredible amount of bugs which exist for months and months now. Yet more DLCs are released and there is no focus to fix bugs.

The new Steam Family feature was the only hope left for me as I could bring my partner to this game and potentially have some fun together. But at this terrible stage I'm not buying this game from another account.

Overall, it's quite sad that such a nice game will be close to dead before the official 1.0 release.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is alright and has interesting metabolism mechanics, but most of the other mechanics are pure trash. The fame system blocks users from using the trader unless they use two accounts and farm fame with an alt that never dies. Death penalties for it are severe and realistically the game seems like it is designed for no lifers. Running across the map takes upwards of 1 hour real time and the entire game skirts the line between tedium and chores. Obtaining a vehicle is another aspect that is considered "end game", but the previously mentioned alt characters negate this for users with multiple accounts. There are severe imbalances between have and have nots and cheating is rampant on the official servers. Drastic inventory and weapon re-balancing occurs constantly, but servers are not wiped which skews the imbalance even further. In addition, there is simply one mechanic that the entirety of the game focuses around and that is the "lock picking" mechanic. It is a quasi rip-off from Skyrim, but worse. I practiced this for quite a while and was able to master it, but for those that cannot or are unable to master it, the game is simply unmanageable. Overall I'd give it a 4/10, with some potential for improvement if the fame system was completely removed. The only reason it exists is to pad game play hours due to lack of content in the game. In fact, most of the mechanics seem to simply exist just to pad game play time as there is really no actual content to be heard of.\t\t\t\t\t
