\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSCUM is a really fun and extremely challenging game. This is DayZ on steroids and way more polished. The only thing I think was a bad idea, was removing the prefab cabin blueprints. This game is already very challenging and grindy, and having the ability to throw down a small cabin blueprint and fill it with the required mats was great for early game, allowing new players to start something pretty quickly and have a place to respawn/store loot instead of having to backpack all over the map constantly dropping loot for "better" loot because you've run out of backpack space.

The new Horde system is a bit janky but I believe the devs will address/fix it in the upcoming patches. This game isn't really mean't for solo play IMO, it can be done, but you will have a pretty rough beginning. This game will definitely test your resolve and if you have any weakness in you, you will fail...lol It has made my friend rage quit and uninstall the game multiple times...lol

The last thing that I think is terrible, is playing with your friends. It is pretty difficult and time consuming trying to stay alive long enough to meet up with your friend. I don't care about spawning in with my friends, I know its not that type of game where we can join a lobby and then jump into the game, BUT what they should implement is a steam friends list system where, the game checks steam IDs and then checks if any of those steam IDs are friends. So if my friend and I are on the same server, we can automatically see where each other is on the map. That would make it so much easier. If that is too hard, maybe make it to where clans can send invites to whomever they want and once the other player accepts clan invite, they appear on the map.

Anyway, great game. Buy it if you're brave enough and don't if you're not and just going to refund it after 10 minutes of playtime.\t\t\t\t\t
