\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame felt exactly as I was afraid it would...

I was dropped into B3 (middle of map) began looting a farm shed, no real inventory space unless you can modify the parachute pack, which seemed to have disappeared upon landing. Then the "No item found" messages started.

I had a single fight bare handed with a zombie, killed it, no real loot of value, another in the distance, avoided him. Looked at map, went to the safe zone out post, and it took an hour of walking. More "No item found" messages 90%+
of the time.

- Controls need help, feel clumsy
- Planets unless previously harvested should contain something.
- If you want some plants to be empty or full, then have different models.
(example of this would be the Yucca, and Prickly Pear Cactus.)

By the way, no starting way to make a fire or process these items as you would have to eat them in real life, that I can tell from initial game play starting out. Eating raw seemed to have little impact, maybe 1% increase in food/hydration.

The density of activity in the game map seems to be an issue for entertainment value. I did find a knife in one of the kitchens, and a can of spam. Arrived to safe zone outpost half starved and dehydrated after a very long and boring walk.

I understand the game is Early Access, that these developers may also be Indie, that said not much different than someone buying prefab assets throwing them into a game level and then placing a character rig.

I would support this game for around $10 in it's current state. Best of luck to this team.\t\t\t\t\t
