Melee, add a player shove when a puppet is too close to be hit with either a punch or melee weapon, like every good zombie game. Make kicking and combos require player input (Look at Kingdom Come's combat)

Puppets just need a massive overhaul, and NOT spawning-wise. That's already borked and should be reverted. Give them the same physics as the player character or something similar, they shouldn't go from prone to vertical mid-swing.

Crafting needs some higher level stuff and more crafting stations. Ever heard of blacksmithing? Take scrap metal, make stuff. Higher skill means better looking (and more durable/functional) stuff. Humans can make better things, we're not limited to trashcan armor and animal-skin pants IRL. Crafting could also have a mini-game for things like blacksmithing or maybe tailoring. Player draws a little stitch mark or something? Hits metal with hammer until it's to shape (with re-heating?) Vintage story does this very well in blacksmithing.

Cooking just needs an overhaul, the current system makes no sense. I guess skewers can be expanded to anything logically skewerable, soup/stew as well. Look at any other decent survival game for ideas, Vintage Story is a great example.
