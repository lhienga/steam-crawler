\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith the update 0.95 things are looking better. The inventory looks a lot cleaner, with quick pick options to what u would like to craft on the go now. Its quick and easy to look at, but things are still a little too small to identify for new players. The crafting items are just too small to identify with ease for new players, u almost have to question ur self - if the item u would like to craft - is what u are looking at. Of course we are neat picking here, but it is important to mention this small but rather annoying flaw that new players will encounter.
Things are coming along nicely with this latest update to Scum, it is a fun open world survival game - that in my opinion is somewhat over done when it comes to character (Health, metabolic rate and well being)
But if u would like a game that goes in to deep detail on such gaming parameters, then Scum is for u.

I will start naming some of the Scum features that i think need to be added to the game, in order to make the game more realistic and immersive.

- Fix the character movement, it is too robotic and snappy at the moment. The movement has to be more fluid and life like.
- Get rid of the wall that is present along the map, its very unsightly - can i call it ugly? Make the map in to an island all
around would be a better option. More beautiful beaches to look at :).
- Get rid of the robots (The big Mech robots are not needed and add a very unrealistic, futuristic paradigm to the game)
Perhaps NPS-AI- Human soldiers guarding the high loot areas is a better option. Perhaps they have dogs that can sniff u
out of bushes - if u get too close.
- Add new images for crafting items that are easier to identify (Perhaps larger more simple images are needed) Its a small
detail - but it is needed before full release.
- Get rid of the microchip on the back of the characters head, once again it is a very futuristic/unrealistic feature of the
game - that in my opinion ruins the game. Perhaps an ankle bracelet that inmates usually get is a better option.
- Make the game playable without any onscreen HUD, some games need to have the HUD on at all times in order to play
the game. They might have a turn off feature for the HUD - but when u turn it off - u cant really play the game the way it
should be played.
- A game of this caliber has to have a down state for the player (wounded so badly - that a long time has to pass by before
u can get up off the ground again) Knock out state - when hit by a large caliber round in the helmet. A Winded state when
hit by shotguns in the bulletproof vest from close range - center mass. Losing an arm or a leg in combat, surviving the
longest has to be rewarded somehow - so players wont quit after losing a hand, foot, arm or leg - there has to be some
reward to survive as long as possible even if crippled. The game has to be a struggle - it is a survival game after all.
- When killing another player - being able to see how long they survived for and how many kills they have. Perhaps on the
ankle brace that all prisoners should have when let out in the world. U can see the time they survived for on the ankle
bracelet (Digital ankle brace). As for the kills - perhaps players collect a tooth from every kill - that is placed on a necklace
the players wear around their necks. After a kill is confirmed a player will want to look at two things - the ankle bracelet
and the necklace - seeing how many teeth are on it. Full necklace being something like 50 teeth - beyond that point u
know u killed a bad ass player when u see a full necklace and a ankle brace that reads 500hrs.

Those are some of the features i would like to see Scum add to the game. One of the features i like the most in Scum is Gun play - having to load up all ur bullets and see it live in the 3d world is very nice.
Fixing a jammed weapon is nice as well, however it could have been simplified somewhat - Hold R to check mag and bolt, click R to reload. If weapon becomes jammed - spam R multiple times until the fix animation finishes.
Some things need to be simplified and some things need to be added in order to make Scum more immersive in my opinion.
We can right a book im sure on how to make the game better and what parts to tweak in order to ease the Scum experience. Making the most immersive online survival game is the dream of every creator im sure.

After the Update 0.95 - i give Scum a rating of 6.5/10 - The game is much better polished now - the inventory is a massive improvement with this update. Solid 6.5/10 !!!
