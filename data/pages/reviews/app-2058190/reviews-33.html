I will keep this short and sweet
What this game has on it's predecessor is a solid amount of implementation that increase the choice to focus on gaining all the extra stuff through doing all the side quest and school stories but what it lacks is the lack of motivation to do it because of how freaking strong you are.

The Extract recipe is more numerous and they are OP!
The early fights are extremely easy to the point that if you grind your SP skill then grind SP you'll unlock with ease all (95%) of your skills.

Honestly some chapters are cut scene heavy and that's perfect but it makes the action part a bit easy and underwhelming. The Final chapter doesn't disappoint in that regard. It's some 100 vs 4 type deal and it's super fun, action pact, and the ending, while extremely predictable by then end of chapter 6, is very solid. The MQS is way too fast versus the grinding around it. You can finish the game with 30h at most with all the skill grinded, almost no side quest done and only the important stuff.

I put 107 hours in it with 44% done, all the other stuff I need to complete, I couldn't care to finish it at this point, I am kind of burned out with Judgement here. I may return to it but it's not Yakuza 0 fun.\t\t\t\t\t
