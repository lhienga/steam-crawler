Game allows you to play in two sandboxes, but features mainly in Yokohama (where Yak:LaD takes place)
Heads up there is a spoiler for the mainline games, but if you aren't looking for it you won't notice it.

Some sidegames are not improved and are just copy+paste (baseball game) but others do feel like they touched it up some. The following people and chasing people games are more fleshed out.

All-in-all, very excited to do the remaining 27 hours to get to see this story's end. A great mix-up within the Yakuza (Like A Dragon) universe if you are looking for something different than the mainline, but still made up of the same great Ryu Ga Gotoku DNA.\t\t\t\t\t
