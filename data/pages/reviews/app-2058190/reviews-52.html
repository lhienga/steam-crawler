\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLost Judgement
If you played a previous game from this studio you probably know what to expect. This is a massive improvement on the previous games’ gameplay wise. 4 different fighting styles make a return, and you can switch between them on the fly. I would get into random battles on purpose just to test out new things.

The game feels like it is 90 percent sidequests since there are A LOT. I recommend taking a break and checking them out. Especially the boxing game and the dancing. The choreography and motion capture for the dancing is on point. An issue is accidentally triggering story when doing sidequests.

Skateboard / hoverboard is a good addition for getting around town. Should have been in the first game.
Tailing missions are slightly better and there are less of them.

The Kaito files is REALLY REALLY GOOD. Strongly recommend this DLC. Very few sidequests. About 8 hours of pure story missions.

One issue I have is that stealth sections are just badly thought out. They mess up the pacing plus the main characters can just beat up thousands of guys.

Well optimized too runs Great even on a 1060.

Currently almost 100 completed the main game and DLC. I only have the “girlfriend” missions left which is honestly kind of a drag.

My time including DLC is 82 hours. I may have the game 100 percent done in about 85+ hours. Note this is with me playing on normal and using a guide. I have not touched new game plus. You get your money’s worth.

I would give this a 90/100.
