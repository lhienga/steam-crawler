Story
-----
Unfortunately, the story is not as good as before. Even though, the theme is great, there are some twist, but the way it unfold is not as compelling as the first Judgment.

Gameplay
--------
This is the strongest poin of this game.
(+) It has the best hand to hand combat with different styles, even better than Yakuza 0.
(+) So much thing to do in the game. You can play school activity with its own stories and mini-games, playing retro console games, playing arcade games, etc. And one mini-game also contributes into one of the combat style.
(+) This game also fixes many annoyances in the first Judgment, like better mechanism in tailing mission.

Music
-----
The soundtrack is great. From opening music, to the battle music, every music is very good and make the game more engaging. It enhance the situation greatly.

Graphic

The visual is good, but nothing special. FSR and XeSS help pushing the framerates for the low end card, but beware of the rain.\t\t\t\t\t
