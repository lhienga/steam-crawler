Lastly The Kaito Files was basically what the Majima Saga in K2 should've been, an actually interesting story. I really enjoyed the story and the combat was also really fun, so good to see Brawler and Beast being brought back again. This game showcases the godlike writing RGG is capable of and I really hope the next games in the franchise are as good as this one.
