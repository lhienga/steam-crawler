\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great sequel to the first game - Judgment. Really enjoy the unique high school elements in the detective mystery plot as well as the many returning characters both in the main story and side cases as well.

Combat wise, it was welcoming to see a new snake style being incorporated as well as reworking crane style to be a more effective way for crowd control. It was also interesting to see more varied gameplay elements such as the stealth and parkour missions being mixed in to add flavor.

Another aspect that I thoroughly enjoyed was the school stories which had a nice plot (twist) that tied everything together as well as brought back the prior side content villains from previous game to evoke some recognition and nostalgia.

In addition, the Kaito files expansion was also an interesting extension as you get to play Kaito as the main character and experience his unique fighting styles as well as learn more about his past background. Really delighted to see that they gave him a happy ending for this expansion as well.

Overall, Lost Judgment has been such a fun game to play and I really enjoyed exploring/skating around the new backdrop of Ijincho to solve all the cases. Very much looking forward to the next Yagami installment when it is released.\t\t\t\t\t
