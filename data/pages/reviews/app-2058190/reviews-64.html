\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy all-time favourite RGG game, surpassing even Yakuza 0. You have your run-of-the-mill Like a Dragon character-driven criminal conspiracy in which nothing is as it seems. I loved the courtroom sequences in the first Judgment as they really ground a story which (in all RGG games) can get a bit out of hand. As expected, some great story twists and a great cast and even greater 'villain'.

Sub Stories in this game are a blast and the basically second game they call School Stories is a bit to get through but if you can commit they're great.

Combat has been drastically improved and makes for what I believe to be the best combat in the whole series. Each of the 4 styles have something unique and useful so they don't overshadow one another. Tiger has fantastic offensive 1-on-1 capabilities. Crane makes for a fantastic crowd control method. Snake creates great openings to counterattack (also the parry is straight OP). And Boxer has the easiest juggle initiator, also with some great counterattack potential. Overall, combat just feels so much smoother than its predecessor, quicker style changes being the stand out change.

If anything were to be improved it would be the stealth and parkour sections. Difficult to control and overall unnecessary. Still a fantastic game. Really feels like RGG have nailed the use of the Dragon Engine. 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
