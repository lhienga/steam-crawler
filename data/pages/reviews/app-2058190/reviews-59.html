\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is decent, but compared to the main series and first game it feels pretty mediocre.

I've played all the Yakuza games avaialble on pc, so my review is compared to those
Negatives:
The main story is kinda meh, the plot is stupid in a boring way.

The side cases (not 2nd story) are bad aswell, they are too serious and not fun as in previous games, also there is barely any, most of the side stories are related to secondary content like gfs or detective work. There was even 10 out of 54 side cases being about the same subject!! and then a few is just a bad sequel side story from the first game.

Yagami speaks SOOOO slow, at least that's my experience in the japanese version. I can't tell if the game is long or it's just because the conversation is so slow paced. Maybe i've watched too much anime, but i don't recall it being this bad in previous games. With the story also being mediocre it was a real pain in the end to not press the skip button, as conversations are slow paced as well. (They talk a lot and take time to get to the point)

The judgment series mechanics are poor, stealth is unnessarcy to avoid 1-2 guards, beating them up would be quicker. Tailing someone is boring and slow. The parkour thing was cool, but it feels overused and sometimes you're just like, just use the door maan.

The last sidecase is just a boss fight, however this boss is straight out of darksouls and puts the final bosses in the main story to absolute shame. I gave up on fighting it, as i'm not that interrested in learning all the patterns and mechanics just for an achivement.

The game is expensive, it's an old game and a spinoff. It dosen't bring much new compared to the main series, so not worth the price imo.

In general just too many tedious tasks and also feels like they lost the humor from the previous games. I also feel like despite being a spinoff, the only relevance was the world. The yakuza and story of the cities was pointless or non-existstent.
Positives:
Skateboard - The skateboard was awesome, quick to get through the map.
Taxi - The taxi are perfectly placed on yokohama, you can get where you need instantly.
4 Different fighting styles was nice too
Secondary story (School story) was a nice idea, the minigames weren't the best but i liked there was so much different.

I would probably give this 3/5, but when comparing to the other games i cannot recommend it.

I have yet to play the DLC, but not into paying 21$ for only 5 chapters and nothing else.
