Side content is good, but less organic than J1- the friend system in kamurocho was really engaging and it was easy to lose hours chaining those quests together. the school is a cool setting for yagami and he's generally really likable in this one. This series is a really cool contrast to Y:LAD but i doubt we will ever see a sequel. oh well. worth a play\t\t\t\t\t
