\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust like the previous game (Judgement), you get more of what you enjoyed from that game here in lost judgement. Insane combat with a return of the tiger and crane styles but with an additional two styles, characters that you will vibe with or hate, an engaging plot, outlandish side quests, emotional roller coasters, and yes, four girlfriends. Now a harem might be condoned IRL but, this is why we play video games :V This time we get two maps to stomp around in, Kamurocho and Yokohama. The theme of this game is influenced by bullying and how it can affect people. You join the local high school in Yokohama as an advisor and help troubled kids in their own mysteries in the form of "school quests". This is entirely optional to beat but I recommend that you do. Besides, the best way to teach delinquent children is through pain.... I mean constructive lectures.... But as you progress through the story, you will find out why the game is called "Lost Judgment" because I was torn between what was right and what was not. My cons are the same as the first games, no Karaoke and you can't romance Saori-chan and Mafuyu chan because of plot. Like the previous game, all DLCs are included. All in all a worthy addition from Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio. So do yourself a favor and BUY IT!!! WE NEED A THIRD GAME!!!! >:V\t\t\t\t\t
