\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright so this game is an even enhanced version of Judgment. Few points which I would like to mention here:

1. The combat has become better, more fluid and more styles added, they hardly removed anything from Judgment's combat but definitely added more.
2. The soundtrack is on par for sure, but in my opinion Judgment's soundtrack is better (by a bit so this game still got banger music).
3. The plot of this game is also top tier but again, Judgment's story for me was better than this (I feel like I keep comparing it to its prequel because Judgment gave me very high expectations for its sequel so I keep it as a base to compare).
4. The message of this game is really great and I loved it.
5. Graphics has surely improved and ♥♥♥♥ ton of side missions are there (some i don't even feel are needed here).
6. Map has increased and that's good coz in Judgment, after some time of playing, Kamurocho started to feel small since we can just reach anywhere by just running but this game fixed it by adding one more location which is even bigger than Kamurocho.

Overall this and its prequel are in my top 5 video games now simply due to its plot and combat itself. This game is a serious recommendation from my side and a big YES if you are thinking of buying it. IMO this game's worth it.

PS:- If you have a steam deck, I recommend playing there. I completed it on Steam deck and it was a wonderful experience.\t\t\t\t\t
