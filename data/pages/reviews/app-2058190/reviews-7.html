\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJudgment/Lost Judgment have very easily found themselves sitting comfortably in my top 10 games of all time, just astounding games from top to bottom that give me genuine laughs, genuine tears and gods honest enjoyment from the moment I picked them up to the moment I finished them. I feel like I could go off on an absolute tangent at just how much I feel that games like this can shine such a positive and impactful light on gaming as a whole...amazing story telling, crazy fun combat (that improved considerably from the first title), deep characters that connect everything together in a perfect unforgettable package...and the side stories are just so funny, they even slipped a "these nuts" line in there that took me completely by surprise and had me cackling for a while. There's just SO much to do but it doesn't feel like an overwhelming chore like some games make side activities feel either. The Kaito files DLC was just the absolute cherry on top, Hes definitely the more likeable character and I'm incredibly happy I got to play as him in his own story that, honestly, was just as good as the main game!

All I will say beyond that though is, if you're considering buying it just get it, you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
