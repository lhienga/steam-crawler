\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall fun and worth playing. The only Yakuza game I actually had enough fun with to get to the end. It's really good for mindlessly clicking while watching a stream or listening to something on the other monitor.

Good port - stellar performance with a good amount of options and proper button prompts (unlike Yakuza 0, for example).

Despite a lot of cringe, cliches, corny action scenes, plot conveniences and completely pointless actions that add up to pointless plot points, the story's engaging enough to keep playing, even when it gets real bad.

The combat is pretty bad; mostly just button mashing with some easy combos where the enemies are either dummies or have ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ moves. Either way, you can cheese most of them and even if you get owned, you have hundreds of healing items and boosts. In a lot of fights, the problem is actually getting the character to face the right way.

The minigames are fun for the most part.\t\t\t\t\t
