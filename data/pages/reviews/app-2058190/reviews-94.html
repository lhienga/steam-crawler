\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLOST JUDGMENT BEATS YAKUZA 0 GAMEPLAY.

THE END OF THE REVIEW. BUY THE GAME.

Haha... So anyway lets actually begin the review, though thats the biggest thing you need, other than dont worry, trailing missions were mostly removed, and actually made insanely better. They only appear like twice in the main story. However, stealth has now taken its place, but it only shows up like 3 or 4 times so, good job Lost Judgment.

So, the biggest first thing to address are these new gimmicks, along with other things that made Judgment, Judgment.

1. Investigation no longer is annoying and sucks. What did they change? Nothing. They just made it enjoyable by making it much more intuitve, unlike in Judgment, where you would be rubbing up against every wall and sliding your camera around annoyingly trying to find out what clue you are looking for, Lost Judgment one, makes this not happen every damn mission, and is much rarer, and ALSO always has direction in what you are looking for, this was just way better game design in its direction.

2. Uncharted wall climb/parkour Yeah, Yakuza got it now. Welcome to Lost Judgment, its about what you expect but it can be slow when its not a swing or a wall run. I REALLY wish they did more with this.

3. Lockpicking and the silly key ring thing was removed, and the trailing was made good, no longer having boxes to snap to you naturally in a very Assassins creed type way get behind any surface. VERY GOOD. Plus, you can now blend into the environement when under suspicion.

4. Stealth. The new trailing missions of Judgment. They suck, seriously they are really dumb. Throw a coin to break away attention then stealth take down. OR THROW A SMOKE BOMB AT THEM. Problem being its super linear and basically forces you to throw it. It basically almost plays itself just you interact with it. Its SUPER BORING. though like I said, it happens like 3 or 4 times. It isnt plagued with it like traling was in Judgment. In fact, ALL THIS STUFF IS RARER IN LOST JUDGEMENT. VERY NICE!

Lets cover stances, where Judgment in a way revived the concept after long wait since before DRAGON ENGINE, (0, K1)
Now, we have them back, and 3 of them this time in Lost Judgment, as well as a 4th you can obtain called Boxer.

Snake, and Boxer are two new stances. Snake is a defensive, quick, grapple style thats block parries enemies. Its smooth as butter and my favorite stance in the entire series. Boxer, similar to Rush from K1 and 0, but no grab and its all boxer rules. Its over powered because when upgraded allows you to basically make the enemy die for even daring to try to hurt you, its dodges and counters are absolutely insane. These two stances are the best in the series honestly, with how powerful they are, they are VERY VERY FUN.
THIS IS PEAK COMBAT FOR THE YAKUZA SERIES, EVEN CRANE AND TIGER FEEL BETTER HERE.

The side missons are amazing, and not just the files you get in Judgment. Since the story has links to a highschool, you get a lot of interaction with school clubs and school mysteries now that Yagami works part time there. These are super good and some of the deepest side quests EVER in the series. Some argue to feel like they could be main game. Another think I love is that Judgment had the audacity to do forced filler, which Lost Judgment got rid of. Beautiful once more Lost Judgment. You also are given tools like a listening tool, and a detection tool to locate situations in the world, this grows into all kinds of secrets and side content, on top of all of them the best "tool" or goodest boy.

THE DOG. THIS IS THE BEST YAKUZA GAME, BECAUSE YOU CAN WALK THE DOG.
Beyond the point of DOG you can take him for walks, he will detect trouble afoot, and bring you to some more new mysteries. Its actually incredible how you seemingly investigate some side content, not just "take a case" or "run into an event." I LOOOOVE THIS CONCEPT! KEEP DOING THIS RYU GA GOTOKU ITS SOOO GOOD!

There is also a skate board you are given to travel around, its basically the first vehicle in the series, its super fun even if a little jank, and I found it great for moments of getting somewhere that would be much necessary for a taxi, or just zooming around faster or EVEN doing tricks which were fun. there are even coins to collect that are all around as you ride around that can be grabbed for rewards.

The only other thing I can pick at is that the skill tree still isn't as good as past games. Seriously, can we also please stop hiding EX's in the skill tree Ryu Ga Gotoku? It makes the game ooze less coolness when everyone will want to go for meta or abilities that make the game more fun, give more power, strategy, or abilities. Keep all of the EX moves open from the start.

This game is an ABSOLUTELY STELLAR GAME IN THE SERIES, MY GOD.

The only down side? Somewhat the story....
Its REALLY good, don't get me wrong, but it feels like it under delivered compared to Judgment a little. Ill never forget Judgments finally and all its segments to get there.
(Especially the ♥♥♥♥ ton of annoying investigation, and trailing missions...)
Overall, Lost Judgment OOZES with perfection in every field, I still loved the story, even if it was a bit of a mess in my opinion, it undershot Judgment.

9.7/10 - Lost Judgment, is my third favorite in the series. I have found it to grow to be special to me, its the best combat the series has ever had, its what Yakuza 6 wanted to be, with the quality of Yakuza 0. It is the master class dream, its only downside being a story that could have been better. Beyond this, the game is nearly in most departments the peak of the series, absolutely do not miss Lost Judgment. If you didn't like Judgment, try this game still. Judgment, you stick around for the story among the annoyances in design with good combat. For Lost Judgment, you stick around for it all. The best combat of the series, (beating K1 and 0 which I think is the peak.) Insanely deep side content, a lot of mini games, and things to do and experience, drone racing is even back on top of all this new stuff! School cases and clubs, the two cities, the 4 stances, vehicle... dog.... WOW. It may not be perfect, it may not be the best in every angle. Though, I believe the Lost Judgment, is nearly the greatest in every department, and in a good amount THE BEST.

DO NOT MISS LOST JUDGMENT, BUY THIS FRICKEN GAME! THERE IS THE FIRST DLC EVER ON TOP OF THIS YOU CAN PLAY A KAITO JUST GET THE GAME, PLAY AS KAITO, AND WALK TO DOG!

THIS IS YAKUZA 6 IF IT WAS YAKUZA 0 BUT ITS JUDGMENT 2. \t\t\t\t\t
