\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReview for fans of the publisher because you will understand this 100%:

changed my review to negative after one moment in the game when I finally fought that certain cop "boss battle" that was when i realized this game kinda sucks. Remember in 0 when there were so many fights and the coolest bosses and Kuze was like 10 boss fights...yeah right? Not here.

40 hours in this game and I only remember 2 boss fights and both were a pain in the *** because of the stupid button prompt. I play on hard and always wear the binding and training inners which puts my attack down -45% I think but I get an SP gain and it makes every fight require a little skill. Well I'm in a groove then the button prompt shows up and I fail every time because why tf is this here? I was fighting so well wtf. then the game locks difficulty on easy and I always fail that stupid part and game over. They added so many things to the game which is cool but took away from the fighting... why? School Stories are just minigames and where tf are the boss fights?

And an issue I have with this game is all the dialogue. In Shenmue and Yakuza 0, you were always moving and story was a quick cutscene most of the time. I hate mashing the A button because I am not here to read. That reminds me that for a detective game, there isn't much detective work on your end. You just look around and observe nodes which is so lame. And the need for them to explain every little detail and hold your hand. Honestly, why am I still learning tutorials this far in? tf

Also, the squirrel thing was annoying because in Shenmue and Yakuza 0 I could physically train to get all my stats up quick instead of an egg hunt and after we all looked up a guide online and finally get the last squirrel what do we get... a ******* acorn?! In Yakuza 0 I could steal everyone's money and throw it in the air...that was how you obtained everything and it was awesome. We all loved fighting cool bosses but you can't say this is a different game than Yakuza because it's really not.

It was always annoying in the Yakuza stories that the character always had to be the good guy when they call the game yakuza but it was fun. This game is not fun. find me in the arcade playing Virtua Fighter because f it atleast I get to fight.\t\t\t\t\t
