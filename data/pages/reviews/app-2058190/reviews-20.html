Played the game on my PS5 when it originally released, so I was looking forward to playing it again on my PC. In addition, it was a great way for me to finally try out the Kaito Files DLC. The overall gameplay of this game was superb, with incredible combat that's probably the best in the entire series. It has an amazing story, great characters and story themes, as well as amazing battle music. Throughout this game, I was always on the edge of my seat.

Kaito Files
The Kaito DLC is a short but sweet ride focusing on our side character Kaito, who has been with us since the first game. His fighting style is impressive, and he's an incredible force to be reckoned with. It was great to learn more about Kaito and his past through the story. It has one of the best endings in the franchise. Overall, it was definitely worth picking up with the PC port.

I definitely recommend people to pick up this PC port since it's well made. This game is definitely worth checking out, but you'd probably enjoy it even more if you played the first game in the series, AKA Judgment, which is on Steam as well.\t\t\t\t\t
