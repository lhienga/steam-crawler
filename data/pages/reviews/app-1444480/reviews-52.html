\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBottom line: $20 is steep for the content as it is now. If you don't have any computer science background wait for it to go on sale. If you love computer science, get this game to enjoy what it does have and support the developers.

I've been programming for 40 years. I attended college for Electrical Engineering. Digital circuit design was the one EE class I got an 'A' in. I love this game because it reminds me of what I learned and presents challenges that I really enjoy solving. I would recommend it to any programmer who doesn't have any background in computer science because it gives insight into the fundamental functioning of a computer.

That being said, the game is pretty rough around the edges (compared to, say, Exapunks) and can be frustrating to work with. Also, i don't think there's enough information contained in the game to be able to complete it without some background or an external reference source. It would be helpful if the game provided this content or references to it for those who are interested but maybe not equipped to learn this stuff on their own. But the bones of the game are good.\t\t\t\t\t
