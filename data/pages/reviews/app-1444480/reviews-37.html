\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot sure what the negative reviews are talking about. This is a fantastic game that very quickly explains going from a single NAND gate to a full-on 8-bit computer in just a few hours. It's a beautiful game and teaching aid at the same time.

The instructions are a little vague at times, but this is a feature. You're supposed to figure it out yourself. That's the whole point. A few times I had to click "give me a hint" or "show solution" (which opens a Youtube video that explains the solution rather than just showing it to you). In both the hint case and after just a few seconds of the Youtube case, it was pretty clear how to move on.

This is an incredible game if you're wanting to actually understand how gates are used to make CPUs. Really, really well done.\t\t\t\t\t
