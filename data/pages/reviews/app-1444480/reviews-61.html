\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Kerbal Space Program of Zachtronics games. Blurs the distinction between "Puzzles" and "Studying". If you haven't played Zachtronics games, try them first -- if you don't like them, you certainly won't like this. This game is intended for people who played Zachtronics games and said "I want more complexity"

Like KSP, this game attempts to take something very, very complex (for KSP, orbital mechanics; digital design for this game) into something that the layman can perform. And, also like KSP, this isn't going to work for everyone. But where KSP simply throws the user into the ocean to sink or swim on their own, this game uses puzzle mechanics to teach the player what they need to know and is therefore somewhat superior.

There's still stuff that's missing that should be added (leaderboards are only accessible via a third party website, for example), the UI could use some tweaks (a notepad to write down opcodes that is attached to the computer you are building would be helpful, for example), but its Early Access, so this is to be expected.\t\t\t\t\t
