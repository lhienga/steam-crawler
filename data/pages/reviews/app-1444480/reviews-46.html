\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall Recommended 5/5 Stars. No experience required. This game is fantastic, be prepared to think. As a professional software developer for almost 2 decades, this game definitely filled in some gaps in my understandings of the internals of CPUs. I also really enjoy puzzle and logic type games. I picked it up quickly, but still struggled with a few levels and did a little googling to help understand what I was trying to do. There's also a fantastic discord community that can help with answering questions and providing guidance on those difficult levels. I would strongly suggest working things out on paper when you aren't quite sure what is going on.

The levels build in a very logical format and introduce you to new concepts one at a time. Outside of a few levels nothing is timed and you can build at your own pace. Finding optimal solutions is not necessary, only meeting the requirements of the level. I really enjoyed seeing the pieces click together and getting excited about thinking about what each new component I unlocked was going to lead to.

The interface can be a little confusing initially, but take time to play around with it and figure it out. Interactions and control mechanisms are similar to games like factorio.

Music is really great as well.\t\t\t\t\t
