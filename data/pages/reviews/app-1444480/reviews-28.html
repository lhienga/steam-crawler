\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was really attracted to this game when I saw it, although I have to say that I was a bit disappointed by the inconsistent spikes in level difficulty, vague level prompts, and a bunch of other stuff that left me scratching my head and feeling frustrated more than actually learning and improving. I feel as though this game is more suited for people who are already acquainted with circuitry and logic and want to see their knowledge put into practice, and are more comfortable with the ability to fill in the gaps that the game regularly leaves.

That being said, the game is just as functional as advertised and holds true to it's claim of starting you from nothing and taking you all the way to a functional computer. Just expect the pace of learning to be similar to a college program.\t\t\t\t\t
