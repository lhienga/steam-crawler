\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, overall, the gameplay is fun. Bad UI, difficulty is pretty low even on the higher difficulty setting, but mostly it's a fun tactics game.

But the story/characters are bad. Like, bad in a way that actively hampers my enjoyment of the game.
Some of it's just bad dialogue.
Some of it's bad pacing and predictable story beats.
Some of it's unearned story twists like 'oh, he was the long lost king all along' even though there's zero lead up to that moment whatsoever.

But then there's also the creepy stuff, like the support where two characters interact for the first time ever and the woman immediately falls in love with the formerly evil (still sort of evil) guy where he literally gives a 'no one liked me when I was a nice guy, guess girls can't help themselves around a bad boy' speech.
Or the plot point wherein all paladins of the church are female and have to be forcibly bred before they can become part of the order so they'll 'have an attachment to the world' or 'something to fight for' or something like that.
Etc.

And to top it off, while I appreciate that the dev is active in the forums, they give the least professional responses of any dev I've witnessed. Oh, and they continually delete comments that they deem too critical of their work, especially anything that questions their obvious fetish for giant women or remarks upon the poor quality of writing (even in a constructive way).
They also have left a number of harsh responses on other negative reviews which reads like them trying to convince/bully people into only leaving positive reviews, which again feels unprofessional.

Anyways, all told, while I had fun with the actual gameplay, everything surrounding that has soured the experience.
If you don't care about the story (and/or if you really like seeing women objectified and share a fetish for giant women) then you'll still probably have a good time.\t\t\t\t\t

First thing first, i hate RPG maker. It took me a lot of considerations before i actually bought this game.
Which is not a hentai game, mostly looks generic between products.
The game used a lot of custom assets i think, it looks refreshing. Characters also pleasant to look at.

Sound, while not much to hype, it is decent. It isn't like using generic music you're listening over and over. Like Maou damashi's asset etc. It is original, and quite atmospheric on that. I consider the sound equipment I'm using decent enough to judge.

The combat isn't like anything made from SRPG maker or Rea-Lity engine.
At SRPG maker, level up is random. It also random at this game, but not at the same randomness. I mean, at SRPG there's a chance level up doesn't increase anything.
Each combat unit isn't singular, but a team. It is like a certain hentai game long time ago.
Be warned tho, losing a single unit at the squad can bring down the entire team. I mean, vice versa, also applies to the enemy. In such why assassin or archers have a great role for that. They're not a powerhouse, they're not Gundam like what they're at FE who can evade any incoming attack. Also mage has distinctive role unlike the similar genre, which usually just an archer with a staff or book.
The equipment is similar to the classic Langrisser i think? Unlike the Fire emblem weapon with durability issues. All equipment have infinite durability, which i prefer this way.

Story, i don't read story, but i think the writing is good. Judging from what i see on the fly...
Looks like the classic FE3-4, the plot is nothing new but the writing is nice to read. The story cinematics were quite spectacular and meticulously made for an RPG maker game.

Unit Job/Class specialization could have 1 step deeper IMHO, even the DLC won't fix that. Not saying the DLC is terribad.

The native game resolution looks like around 720p. I suggest to use Loseless Scaling program with xBR filter so it will look much better.

For an RPG maker game, this is 10/10.
For a tactic game in general, 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t
