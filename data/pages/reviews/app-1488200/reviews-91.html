\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn indie Gem

+ Fun build/combat system. The customization is pretty much limitless and it's deeper than it looks. Your input is in how you design your team and how you use them on the battlefield but you don't micromanage the engagements. It's very fun.

+ The story is surprisingly good. I've seen better in video games but it beats easily any western AAA. The story isn't straightforward.

+ You can alter some minor aspect of the ending

- The graphics are really basic. It does the job but it ain't pretty especially playing on a 4k monitor.

- Inside battles your main characters speak. The voice acting is hilariously bad. I'm not sure it's a negative point though.

- Control between battle are kind of clunky.

It took me 35 hours to complete the game on the medium difficulty (captain)

7/10\t\t\t\t\t
