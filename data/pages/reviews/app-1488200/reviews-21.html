Progression mechanics are fun and offer engaging hooks. There's individual progression for each unit, as well as whole-army upgrades. Nothing feels poorly balanced, but there is a noticeable increase in power from battle to battle.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great game for lovers of the tactics RPG genre. Rather than developing single units, you build squads with units that you can mix and match. The units each have their own individual stats and behaviours, and they can be promoted down branching promotion paths. It's really fun to build specialised squads and use them to great effect for the purpose which you built them for.

The story is not that great, but tbh the gameplay is so good that it doesn't even matter. It's pretty similar to Ogre Battle: March of the Black Queen, which is a game I have a special place in my heart for. But there hasn't been another tactics rpg quite like this one, so if you are a fan of the genre, you owe it to yourself to check this one out.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my favorite strategy games I have played.

Loved the combat and unique focus on squads on map rather than individual units, which incredibly fun layer to managing units in them. The class trees are expansive and very diverse even more so with the DLC. The spells you can cast later on on the map are also a great addition.

The pixel and animations for units are phenomenal. Art for character is also great and even better in my opinion with the visual update. Although maps and cutscenes visually are notting to write about I personally didn't mind.

Sountrack is good and pleasant to the ear, but it's not something I would listen to outside the game.

The story is rather standard high fantasy story think something simular to Fire Emblem, but it has few good twists. As for character I wish there were more support conversations between them or more sidemissions that focused on them. However even with that I found charaters to be enjoyable, especially those who were involved with the main plot to greater extent.

To sum it up. Great game if you like strategies you should give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
