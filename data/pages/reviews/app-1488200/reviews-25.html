\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a pretty fun turn based strategy that fans of the Fire Emblem or Advance Wars series would appreciate. The difficulty is a little strange. I found early game easy, mid game the most difficult portion, and the late game also easy on the normal difficulty. I've started a game on Warlord, the hard difficulty, and through chapter 11 have been experiencing a similar difficulty curve. Another sort of negative thing I might say is that the units that were good were really good, and the rest felt like youre gimping yourself by using them. Knights felt weak in mid to late, the entire female recruit evolution tree felt weak in comparison to the tankier male evolution tree, and only fire mages for magic units felt good to use. Also every dragon I tried by the end I was only using reds.

A final point, and this is kinda a side note, you can very clearly tell what the writers fet ish is. A large number of female characters are considerably larger than male characters, and a certain main character only extenuates that with a power up later in the story. So if you like that then that may be another positive factor for you lol
7/10\t\t\t\t\t
