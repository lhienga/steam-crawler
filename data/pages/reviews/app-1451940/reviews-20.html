\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game gave me an experience, and made me feel things I haven't felt since I played Undertale (and since that's one of my favorite games of all time, that's a compliment). NEEDY STREAMER OVERLOAD is a game I would've gotten into, and adored during 2014-2015 (I am actually surprised the game is only 2 years old). And that era of 2014 - 2015 was one of the best when it came to games, shows, and fiction in general, so that's how much I liked the game.


GAMEPLAY

The gameplay itself is simple, but fun. I heard it's like Tamagatchi, though I never played or seen it, so I wouldn't know. The gameplay is basically a management game to keep Ame /KawaiiAngel happy, (or to break her depending on the route you wanna take). Throughout the game, you can spend time together (and even have sex), get ideas from the desktop items, and stream where you can choose comments for Kangel to read and see her reaction to it, and delete any negative comments if you choose to do so. You can get different endings from the different streaming topics you can go for (like making Kangel a porn actress is the first ending I got), or to fill out the task managing bars. You can also text Ame(don't ignore her though).

SOUNDTRACK

Maybe, it's just me, but I really didn't take notice or care that much for the soundtrack.

COMEDY

The comedy of this game, reminds me a little bit of the humor Undertale had. It's subtle, but noticable enough. Lines like KAngel says that a woman's childhood flash game not being playable enough is a rightful instance for a woman to cry, or her saying a viewer is a grown adult who thinks their a little child, gives me the type of humor and vibe that Undertale had. I don't know why, but I wonder if other people see it. Anyway, regardless I liked the humor of the game, it did make me chuckle at times, and hell, made me question the developer(s) of the game at times.

DIFFICULTY

The difficulty is basically non-existent in this game. At most, it could be a tad tedious if you mess up once, (like I kept increasing her affection to 100 indirectly, and kept on getting the love overdose ending), but that's about it.

STORY

The story for the most part is simple, it's a streamer girl, who wants to get a million followers. The rest is up to you, whether you want the story to be her descent into madness, her becoming famous, her having her eyes opened by conspiracy theories, or her rise to becoming a porn star, is all in your hands.

CONCLUSION

A nice game, which would've been wonderful and something I would've adored back in 2015. The game itself makes me nostalgic for the mid 2010s era of games, and I really liked it. I also liked the different routes you could take with it, and the game is easy to understand.

9/10
