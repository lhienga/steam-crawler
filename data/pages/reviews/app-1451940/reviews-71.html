\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is fantastic, Point Blank.

I haven't even gotten through HALF the endings and can say that this is genuinely one of the best indie horror games (Or horror games in general) I've played, This is the first game that's genuinely left me feeling DISTURBED from the levels of realness it hits.

Normal players may think Ame-Chan is a very far-fetched, idealized version of a youtuber/streamer, but she's VERY REALISTIC, AND THAT'S WHAT MAKES IT DISTURBING.

When you're a content creator, playing it off stream or off camera is the best choice, as it allows you to soak in the story without the need to entertain an audience. And when you do that, you might find yourself relating to Ame on MULTIPLE LEVELS. Despite how noticeably off she is, you WILL get attached to her (as you do in most dating-sim type games,) and as you play more and more and see her descent OVER and OVER, you can't help but feel bad for her.

It's a very real game. With a very real, and very important message, and it executes said message PERFECTLY.

I would ABSOLUTELY recommend.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn absolutely fantastic game fully deserving of its overwhelmingly positive reviews. It feels like a love letter to the old internet in thanks to how accurately the screen is made. Everything is a nostalgic reminder of old chat boards, the ways you would message people, and even the sight of downloading images is just the same as I remember(but faster luckily). It also feels like a love letter to the web in its current days with how accurately the streaming services and chatters react to everything. The many endings are all fantastically well crafted where I felt Ame's mental degradation and her self improvement as a person. The sound track was fantastic and fit the theme of the game the whole way through, and I loved the touch of it deviating in tune depending on what was happening in game. There were so many tiny details I noticed through my play time that really showed off just how much love and effort was poured into this game. I am sure there were many many tiny things I missed, but the things I did find always made me smile. Some of the more extreme ending whether it was the bad or good ones drew me deeper and deeper into the story. The true ending was not my favorite, but I loved how it ended the game despite it feeling bittersweet. Needy Streamer Overload is a master piece and I can confidently say that this is a 10/10 recommendation even at full price or more. BLESS.\t\t\t\t\t
