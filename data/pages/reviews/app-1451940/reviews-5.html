\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like the music, the wacky ways the game can mess with you, and the artstyle is super cool. I like the retro vaporwave windows aesthetic, and some of the endings are fun. I still have a serious annoyance with the game's resolution settings being non-existent so the beautiful pixel art is completely ruined by the fact the only official sizes are too small or too big.

The grind at first, was kind of annoying, then pretty manageable, then completely infuriating with getting the 10 mil subs because of how picky the game can be with things (even if you use a guide, random events and small changes to the game can make them unreliable). It helps knowing that to avoid Flatline, just send Ame an emoji or whatever to basically every event that happens, and if she sends an emoji back, you should be fine. She'll tell you, at least.

I have a love/hate relationship with this game. It gets super grindy if you want to get everything (honestly not even worth it), but it has nice moments. I really like the graphical style and the idea for the UI, but the lack of better resolution scaling (as well as the game window resetting when you have to restart), is very very annoying. If you're versed with vtuber culture, I think you'll get a kick out of this. I'm not particularly into vtubing but I still liked the game, even if it has some serious rough patches. Good enough off sale, better on sale. Would make a decent stocking stuffer for any weeb friend you got.\t\t\t\t\t
