It's a little upsetting with the dark mental health topics and the fact that there is a literally a mini game in which you cut Ame's wrist when her stress/mental darkness is too high .

Psychological horror comes in as you are literally in control of her every action and are directly responsible for her mental health and whether or not it declines or gets better.

The music is a little annoying to me but it's catchy and fits Ame's manic personality, just super repetitive.

All in all it's actually pretty fun, and a decent game to check out if you liked Doki Doki and want something more interactive and overwhelming.
