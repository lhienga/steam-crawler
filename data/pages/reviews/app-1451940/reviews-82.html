\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Art is super stunning, and I love how it takes you into her life as not her but as her friend/boyfriend "P-Chan".

But unfortunately overall this game was not my cup of tea. I didn't like how fast pace the game was, it got very confusing at times. I also didn't like how I felt like the game just revolved around making sure she didn't die. Like i understand that some people find this enjoyable but to me it felt like i was taking care of a tomogachi pet. It took away the whole concept of the story i felt like.

If you're into tycoon-ish games like that, this game is definitely for you, but if you're more into story rich games i wouldn't recommend this.

I personally rate this a 5.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
