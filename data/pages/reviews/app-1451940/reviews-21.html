\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: If it looks interesting to you, I definitely recommend picking it up! It's a game unlike any I've ever played before. 🙏 BLESS 🙏

- Graphics
The pixel art is really pretty, and there are a range of pixel art CGs to collect and view anytime. The UI is really cool, with a vaporwave, Windows 98 style theme and aesthetic. Make sure to also check out the music videos for theme songs of the game, Internet Overdose and Internet Yamero!

- Gameplay
The gameplay is like that of a visual novel, with some simple management aspects. It's fun to experiment with things to find what works best to achieve the goals you need to meet to get different endings. After my first few runs with no guidance whatsoever, I used this guide to help me get all of the endings. It's spoiler free, and only gives you minimal direction, which I liked.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3017419281

- Story
The main draw to the game. It paints a very interesting psychological picture of the main character, Ame. When I had to make less-than-wise decisions for her to get all of the endings, it made me question what I was doing - the game was good at making me feel for her. The most emotionally impactful ending for me was INTERNET OVERDOSE. I definitely recommend at least going for that one, if you're not planning to see them all.

Overall, a unique, cute, funny, and thought-provoking experience of a game. Definitely give it a try if it looks interesting!
