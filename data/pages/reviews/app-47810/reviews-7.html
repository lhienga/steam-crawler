\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHOOOO BOY.

This one has been on my list for a while, first off. I loved my time with this game, there was a time in game development where Bioware seemed unstoppable, and man it shows here.

The world building and story are easily my biggest takeaways from this game, from the companions you meet all the way to the problems you solve as a Grey Warden on your journey, This game perfectly encapsulated the struggle of politics and war. And also hits you with some seriously tense dilemmas as you work to fight the darkspawn back while shaping the land of Ferelden.

The Combat is fine, It's good for what it is but certainly becomes trivialized once you really build up your character and party. I played as a mage in my playthrough (something I don't normally do in rpgs) and had a blast seeing the variety of builds I could have gone for, was definitely glad I went for it

Only negatives I can think to talk about in this game is the bugs.
I only encountered a few within the main story but MANY in the awakening expansion, so beware of those.

Also might I recommend if you are going to give this game a playthrough it might be wise to install a repsec mod as the game does not offer the ability to do so and at the rate you unlock subclasses in this game you will most definitely want to change up your build or a party members build at some point, and without a mod installed that is not possible.

Overall, HIGHLY recommend if you are into games with super deep and rich stories, I'm excited to get around to the sequel soon and see how my choices carry over.\t\t\t\t\t
