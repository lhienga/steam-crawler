This game is dragged down massively by the absolute abomination that is the combat system, sections like the 2+ hour long trek through the deep roads of orzammar where enough to make me consider quitting the game until i was roped back in by the amazing story soon after.

Maybe one day this game will get the remaster it rightly deserves so that its excellent story can be showcased without the burden of its clunky outdated combat.

still an absolute gem of a game that is worth experiencing in spite of its combat and bugs.\t\t\t\t\t
