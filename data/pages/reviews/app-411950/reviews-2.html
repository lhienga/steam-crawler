\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe first thing I bought for DCS was the F-18 module. There is far, far more to learn about this plane and it's capabilities than you might think. It's rewarding every step of the way. Carrier ops are the most fun thing in DCS in my opinion. There is nothing like taking off from a carrier (especially when the battle group is under fire), intercepting a target, and coming home. With the F-18, you get a plane capable of

1) Air to air, air to ground, HARM, and anti ship capabilities. Depending on how you spec the plane out, you could theoretically do all of this in one mission.
2) Taking off and landing from carriers. The Stennis comes with the F-18, and it's a good carrier.
3) A very capable BVR and dog fighter.
4) A capable carpet bomber. There is a lot of satisfaction with sending a JSOW CBU on an enemy formation.
5) A precision striker with JDAMs, Mavericks, and even JSOWs.

If you wanted a single plane that can do it all in DCS, the F-18 is probably it. It's every flight and combat simmer's dream aircraft.

With the mission editor, your options for missions are endless. I've made dozens, and don't plan to stop any time soon.\t\t\t\t\t
