\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis plane's an absolute joy to fly. There's a bit of a learning curve to her, thanks to all the electronics, but once you understand how they work (I definitely advise reading through the manual and using the practice missions) it feels very rewarding to successfully operate. The fly-by-wire system means she is very stable in flight, and with her fuel tanks dumped she can easily reach supersonic speeds on full afterburners. I haven't tried her air-to-ground weapons yet, but that is one of this plane's biggest selling points - she is multi-role, proficient in both Air-to-Air and Air-to-Ground roles. She's also a carrier aircraft, so there's a lot of value for money here. It should be noted though: THIS PLANE DOES NOT HAVE ILS! She had ICLS, which is a carrier-only variant of ILS. This means any airfield landings you make will have to be done under VFR.

I would definitely recommend this aircraft, even if you're new to the genre. Yes, you do actually have to take the time to learn how she works, but she really rewards you when you do.\t\t\t\t\t

I purchased the F-14 before purchasing the F/A-18, after some successful long range intercept missions, I was finding that for certain missions, I needed a more Multi-role capable fighter/ attack aircraft rather than a long range interceptor.
