But if you want cracked radar and datalink as well as the ability to lock targets up to 40 nm with HMD, while carrying up to 10 AMRAAMs in your loadout, this is it. Not to mention currently a clean wing Hornet smokes any and all fighters in terms of BFM. The downside obviosuly is its' kinematic performance but it's not like you'll be flying subsonic or anything. It's very modern and if all you are used to flying are 27s and 29s it can be overwhelming at first, but it doesn't take too long to learn. My main issue with the F18 is how it loves locking onto the first thing it sees with TWS and ACM modes without asking you, and in TWS you can't even break lock manually, causing you to waste precious time while your enemy takes advantage. I don't do CAS much so I can't comment on how well the Hornet does on that front, but I imagine with the weapons this thing can use it can't be bad. Overall, it's very good, I recommend it.
