\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood if you like being scammed.

Edit: Not anymore. Hornet has developed into a highly detailed aircraft that incorporates a near-full simulation of 2000s era sensor integration and half-digital battlefield as a navy fighter. It is true multirole and easily the best simulator of a legacy Hornet in the market. The only reason I am keeping this review as negative is the fact that the aircraft is not yet capable of projecting FLIR onto the HUD in game. It is true that Navy aircraft couldn't utilize NAVFLIR, however Marine jets were capable at the given timeframe and even the ATFLIR pod features a camera set in terms of 3D model and texturing. Other than that, aircraft should be able to L&S (Launch and Steer) on D/L (Datalink) targets without using its own radar. Same should go with FLIR LoS. No sources on that however. Gun dispersion is still off, real thing fires more precise. Flight model (how the aircraft handles) should be tuned as well, they acknowledged it anyway. Radar is overperforming AFAIK.

Looking forward to seeing a F/A-18F lot 10+ from ED. Most of the code will carry over, and I bet many will purchase it as well.

Despite changing this review to positive, I am still waiting on an update on APKWS, MSI, and NAVFLIR. A 2007 Hornet is well capable of these.
