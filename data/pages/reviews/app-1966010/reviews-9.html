\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: Never edited a review so fast - I'm an idiot and was doing the Chaos Theory not the Campaign. The Campaign is absolutely fantastic so far. So I'm not goign to leave my review as bad. Still ... very pricey.


I mean, it's good. Expensive af, I'd rather not had them pay Sam Neil and what's her face and keep the cost down.

They spent a lot of money and time on the very last thing I wanted to have to do... drive the slow, clunky, horribly accelerating ranger trucks into "rustlers" (thieves). I hate sabotage, and I hate this much much more. You guys all know what it's like trying to drive those things... can't get anywhere near a dino or you deadass stop but the dinos can knock you over with a fart. And now, you have to get a running start to even put a dent in one of the 3 attacking ranger jeeps, dodge the dinosaurs, dodge the water, dodge the trees, hope you get up enough speed. The traps and fences do absolutely nothing because they break through them so fast AND can just shoot through them anyways lol.

I bought Jurassic World Evolution - the park builder. I did not buy Twisted Metal, or Sabotage Simulator, or any other kind of game. I want to build a park and manage my dinosaurs. Not play the movie.

Frontier has this terrible disease where they think everyone wants to do stressful, violent things. They ruined Elite Dangerous Odyssey because they thought everyone wanted FPS combat instead of being able to walk around the bridge of their ship (in the space ship simulation game... noticing a pattern here?) after waiting to do just that for 6 years.

Look, I don't want to sound so negative because it's not something that I want to do. The DLC does add some awesome stuff for sure. But I have no interest in doing the rustler thing every 3 minutes. I don't feel like people playing these types of games want knee-jerking intense action lol. We want to manage finances and fine-tune exhibits and create beautiful things.

For this, on top of all the other annoyances (like explicit compatible dinosaurs that WANT others of their species around fighting constantly), I just can't recommend this. especially at this price. IDK - maybe I'll find something wonderful like the ability to to send ranger teams to deal with rustlers instead of having to do that myself in those horribly controlled jeeps.\t\t\t\t\t
