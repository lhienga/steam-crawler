\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat DLC. It has an entertaining campaign level and chaos theory (campaign much more) and some great new animals added and structures. The new species are very impressive, the variants are too impressive, and especially the new map with how large it is and the new biome is great.
Only minor nitpick regarding the animals is the lack of descarring on the Giganotosaurus, which feels unnatural seeing scars cloned with the animal itself.

I did enjoy the quality of the Campaign and the Chaos Theory with returning actors, felt like Return to Jurassic Park, which I loved. It has an engaging story and only wished it could've gone on longer or in more detail if there was a chance to do so.

The actual content itself is worth the price, I do recommend to get this DLC if you like the animals, map, and like a new fresh campaign. It is a must-have for JWE2, even ignoring all of the new animals.

Edit - with the announcement and release of the Malta Expansion pack, my paragraph on the missing animals is removed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's not much to gripe about within the pack in a vacuum besides the Quetzalcoatlus being a massive, homogenized missed opportunity and the Chaos Theory not being very fun. The creature designs are well-realized for the most part and have some very fun animations, the extra campaign is short but inoffensive, and there's much more content here than in the Camp Cretaceous pack (which I refuse to buy until it's half its regular price, I can't wait to tear that thing a new rectum after a price drop).

All of that said, after the precedent set by how the last game handled Fallen Kingdom and Battle at Big Rock-related additions, I must say that Dominion-related content being locked behind a premium paywall is very disappointing, especially given the fact that they've chosen not to include a healthy amount of content - namely the Atrociraptor, extremely prominent dinosaurs in marketing and arguably the most glaring omissions here, and Moros, which are exclusive to the chunk of the movie this pack represents.

I'm in a tough spot with where to rate this; though I think that what is included in the pack is generally very good, the fact that this is a $20 movie content pack that doesn't even represent everything from the movie does not sit right with me in the least. If you're dying for pyroraptors and therizinosauruses, waiting for a moderate price drop shouldn't hurt too much.\t\t\t\t\t
