What can i say, this entire franchise is a waste of potential. I keep giving it chances, but this DLC is the worst money-grab I've seen from Jurassic world.

The campaign is ass, ASS I say! The goal of part 1 is essentially to get to 75 animals in your park, and the new buildings are focused on having a corner of your park with facilities, and infinite easy mode with invisible fences your rangers can drive through, power and paths underground, no guests to please, its a joke. When you hit 75 animals they burn down your park and start you over on the same map for part 2, where you remove the burned down forest for 20 or so animals, and as soon as they reach 90% comfort the game is over.

The mechanics, imo, are all bad. As mentioned above, fences and the underground network are not interesting, it just removes all logistics from your park, basically making it an open-air dino island, too easy. "Dino wrangling" is push/pull for your rangers, which resets the dinos territory and I guess serves to push your new hatches through several gates into newly built enclosures? Not really sure what the theory was there. The amber mine is exactly the same as digsites, and replaces them, so that's not a new feature. To be fair, I haven't played the chaos theory for this DLC.\t\t\t\t\t

Frontier arn't learning anything, but they have so many little kids / brain damaged individuals defending them now this just ends up being a drop in the well so eh, here goes.

In with the pros first:

Wrangling dinosaurs is ok. You arn't EVER going to really use it outside of 2 missions but it's at least a neat little feature. Pitty nothing else is gonna be done withit

The new snow map is huge with lots of area to build in it, that is atleast nice. The biosyn map however is boring and suffers from what alot of maps do in both the JWE games ((Frontier make you look at MILES of land you can never, ever build on lest their engine fall apart)).

There are some more props, still NOTHING for the dinosaur paddocks to give them some uniqueness though. You have been given some light up statues...modern ones! that are just gold rectangles with lights....oh, muh, GURD!

Claire and Owen's dialogue did not want to make me rip my ears off and sit in perpetual silence, their existence is tolerated once more. I got some more of entertainment guy even though he's making bad decisions with his life now and above ALL ELSE, I got more Cabot. The game needs more Cabot.

Onto the cons:

The 3 new missions are pathetically boring, to go along with the rest of how boring the game is. First mission: build some paddocks, you are done. 2nd mission, wrangle some dinos, rebuild some paddocks....you are done. 3rd is the chaos theory mission, build barely anything, have to deal with CONSTANT airdrop dinos whilst dealing with poorly implemented "poachers", annnnnd you are done! You may now use the biosyn buildings that can't really be used outside of the campaign to build with in sandbox... oh what's that? They are only really useful on 1 map that has the giant biosyn building in it and the hyper loop system is a pile of crap and can only be used in industrial looking parks or else you'll have guests walking in and out of power-stations to get around? Oh well, whatever f*** it you'll buy it.

The new dinosaurs are to be honest boring and we have a new contender for the most boring, angsty, annoying "I DON'T LIKE ANYTHING IN WITH ME" dinosaur yet... the Therizinosaurus. For some reason Frontier think nothing got along back in the day, I've already complained about NONE of the longnecks getting along anymore so I can no longer make my gigantic long neck paddock, but now we have this giant raptor-bear abomination saying nope! nothing, screw your plans for a park with lots of things to look at, in a game we are enforcing you ONLY look at things, you pick and choose now. It's a shame because again from what they know this thing lived peacefully with other dinosaurs around it, but due to Frontier making a game where you shouldnt REALLY breed more than 20 dinosaurs, they make sure it hates everything, or the AI's pathing system stops working. The pyroraptor is just a raptor with feathers and likes forests more, for the kids who adore them fair play but really they are JUST raptors, same behaviour. The rest made so little impact on me I have already forgotten them.

Gonna' complain about the new buildings once more. They are utterly, stupendously useless. After you have finished the new missions there really is no use for them. Most if not all people will want to use more buildings, and the "parks" you build with the syn stuff has no life in it what so ever, it is for people who want to sit and watch these dinosaurs DO NOTHING! They end up looking like lifeless nature reserves. They haven't put anything new for the dinosaurs to do in them, they have no new AI quirks, fights or territory marking, its just a big pen and you LOOK AT THEM....WOOOOOOW!! Oh yeah, you don't get to use the new buildings outside of the Chaos Theory mission either, as they are tied into ONLY that mission and ONLY to use against the poachers. Pathetic.

That's it, this could have saved the game in my eyes but it's just another cash in with some dinosaurs to take advantage of kids wanting movie stuff... and when they complained they didn't put enough in, here is Frontier with those dinosaurs in hand and 1 new map, FOR ANOTHER 15 DOLLARYDO / POUNDIOS! Here's the new Malta DLC! Again, whatever, f*** it, you'll buy it. Also for those thinking "well Im sure mods can fix it", no they can't. Since the new update a few things have been changed in the way props and other bits work which has completely broken EVERYTHING. Until someone figures out how to refix that all you have are reskins / dino replacements.... sad.\t\t\t\t\t
