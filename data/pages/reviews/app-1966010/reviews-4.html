\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis DLC is....meh? Mid? Barely average?

Don't get me wrong, the new additions to the game as a whole is pretty cool. But what I can't agree with is the campaign.

I played it for nearly three hours. Doing every little thing the campaign demanded. Silly me for thinking that this campaign would resemble the others. Three hours in I'm struggling to get things unlocked. Dinosaurs start to drop due to age. Income starts to tank at break neck speeds and maintenance costs spike. And I still have the three big dinos to synth and release. Eventually I get what I need to get the rest of the fossils needed. Cool. Now lets see about taking down some stuff to bring my profits back up.

That's when chaos ensued.

I deleted a generator to try and cut costs while also making sure that the other generators in the park still had connection to everything. But if anyone has played this game for any time, you know that that's not always a certainty. Whether its a minor bug in the game, or me just being a @#%~! idiot, the connection didn't work. Dinosaurs got out. My profits were so low I couldn't even do anything. And I almost turned my monitor into a folded screen. Everything went wrong.

Compared to the other campaigns, this one has sooo much going for it with all the super cool stuff it adds. Then for some reason it violently soils itself by becoming a waiting slog that slowly morphs into a hair pulling stress mess. It's a drastic and insane curve from the rest of them. So for that, I really don't recommend this DLC and I hope the human who let this pass QA accidentally slams their hand in their car door exactly 37 times.

My suggestion? Get the DCL at whatever price you think is fair (I'm not your real dad) and ignore the campaign like it's a [Instert red headed step child joke or equivalent].\t\t\t\t\t
