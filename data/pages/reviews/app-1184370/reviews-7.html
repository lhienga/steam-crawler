\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a gem! One of the best RPGs I've played, and I've been at the genre since the '90s

I'll try to avoid spoilers.

The sweet

* Intriguing story.
* Extensive character progression.
* Choices matter greatly,
* Replayability.
* Looong game. About 100h per playthrough for me.
* Amazing music.
* Amazing voice acting.

The sour

* Steep learning curve, which is not for everyone. Grasping the class progression and the spellbooks is the real difficulty setting.
* Somewhat unforgiving, The choices you make may clash with the path you've chosen for your character.
* Lots and LOTS to read, combined with a somewhat unhelpful quest log and lots of puzzles is not for the faint-hearted. Luckily, the internet it full of helpful people!

The salty

* Loot is static (not random), and key/best items can be in unguarded pouches on the floor.
* Unbalanced "kingdom" mechanics leaving that part of the game wanting.

All in all a really good game!\t\t\t\t\t
