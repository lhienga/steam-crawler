An extra layer of min max character development that adds layers to what you are capable of. Pathfinder is my Favourite CRPG series to date and OwlCats are my favourite developers. As long as they keep pumping out quality love crafted games like this, they can just.... shut the hell up and take my money!!!\t\t\t\t\t
