\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall a really good party-based RPG. Excellent story, with some very weighty choices. Interesting companions and good combat, even though you might get tired of fighting demons by the end. I set the "campaign management" bit to autopilot, so I can't comment on that. Difficulty is a little uneven, but I feel like that's almost par for the course with this type of game.

The only real negative is the "Abyssal corruption" mechanic that has you constantly travelling back to home base to clear it off, often resting a few times along the way. The whole world map travel part of the game is a bit of a time sink, and the corruption mechanic makes it much worse. Just give us a good reason to return to base rather than forcing us to.

Still, I had a lot of fun and if you like Baldur's Gate, Divinity, Pillars of Eternity, DA:O, etc.... then you will probably like this game.\t\t\t\t\t
