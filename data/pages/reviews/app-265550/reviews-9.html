Well, this one really didn’t want to get perfected. Every time I thought I was in the clear I got smacked by some BS. The glitches in this game were never ending. If you are just playing this game for fun and to mow down zombies then awesome. If you are trying to 100% the achievements, be ready to get frustrated, VERY FRUSTRATED.

I have fond memories of this game because I got it with the Xbox one bundle that I got for Christmas one year and this game was surprisingly fun and offered a formidable arsenal with a relaxed and casual play style. I could not recall having any issues or glitches while playing in the past.

This time was not like that time. We had falling under the map, enemies falling under the map or getting stuck in the wall. There were times I lost my saved data for no reason. Sometimes the collectibles I picked up didn’t even count, I couldn’t even drive anywhere for more than a few minutes without flipping my vehicle on some invisible nothing. I had to do so many load last checkpoints and sometimes complete restarts of sections because of getting stuck in ridiculous spots and having no way out or some other random thing.

This is not to say that the game is unplayable. I put a lot of hours into it and enjoyed many parts of it. I like the game, just not all the BS glitchy stuff you come across while perfecting the achievements for it.\t\t\t\t\t
