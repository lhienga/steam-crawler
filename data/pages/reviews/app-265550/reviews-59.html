I think the open world system, running function that provides a dynamic feeling rather than a hard movement, transportation system, combination system, and physical fitness system prove this.

But I think the most felt was the change in graphics. Strengthening the light source system and shadow effects, more overwhelming supplies of zombies, more sophisticated modeling and effects, and textures have had a significant impact on graphic changes.

There have been many good changes within the game play system. Unlike "Weapon Combination System" that was possible within a certain space in the previous work, combinations are possible anywhere, and more diverse and fun-causing combination weapons appear.

dealing with psychopaths, dedicated theme songs, cinematic, and directing that support the bosses gave a stronger impression of "The Seven Sins," which was the motif of the bosses.

Unlike the existing Drunken story, the story is dark and heavy. I think people may like or dislike this point. But I personally think I made good use of this dark feeling.
