\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I remember this game coming out back in like 2014 for the Xbox One and always wanted to play it. I’ve played every Dead Rising game except for this one up until now here in 2024 and maaaaaaannnnn this game is an absolute gem!!! I hate myself for not playing it any sooner. I just got a Steam Deck OLED recently and stumbled across this masterpiece for $8. Dude… $8 freaking dollars. I would have happily paid $70 for this game in 2024 if I had known how damn good this ♥♥♥♥ was. So for the $8 I paid I have gotten way more than my moneys worth.

The physics, movement, way guns feel when you shoot them, all the weapons you can craft, the deadly vehicle weapon hybrids you can make, the massive hoards of zombies, running over zombies with a make-shift bulldozer with spikes on it, just all of it man. It’s a bloody good time. Literally 😂. And the story is very good too, very well written. It’s hilarious what some of these characters be saying. I wish I could ruin it for you but I won’t. Just buy this game. It’s a lot better than most modern day zombie games in my humble opinion. The quality is 10/10.\t\t\t\t\t
