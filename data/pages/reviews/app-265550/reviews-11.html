\r\n\t\t\t\t\t4/10 with my performance issues!

The game consistently gets stuck at the black loading screen right before the final part of the game, following my encounter with another character from the same series. I've diligently attempted every suggestion I could find online, and I assure you, I've tried absolutely everything. This is the sole reason I'm giving it a negative review.

Regardless of what happened to me, the game is quite enjoyable. It's quite impressive for killing the time. Despite the fact that Dead Rising 3 has various weapons, it will still intentionally force you to keep changing it by adding the durability of each weapon, making you unable to stick with your over-powered weapons all the time. This system could be a bit annoying for someone, including me. The story is not good but not that bad. The main story of this game is just to leave the town that is full of zombies and battalions, with your friend. Additionally, during the process of leaving town, you will confront some secrets about Nick (the main protagonist of Dead Rising 3) which will explain the reason why we need to play as a Nick, not some other random dudes. You may also confront some funny bosses in the game that require you to fight. The ways of presenting them are diversified and enjoyable somehow; you can still surely avoid them if you just want to finish the main content of this game it has nothing to do with the main progress.\t\t\t\t\t
