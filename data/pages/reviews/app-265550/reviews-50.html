\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really really liked the first Dead Rising. I loved Dead Rising 2. I liked Dead Rising 3. While the main gameplay is as good as ever. There are quite a few little nagging issues in here though. The world is not easily traversable. Every other corner has either a giant wall or the classic bridge that doesn't connect to another area. That I would say is it's biggest issue. The world either should've been smaller or easier to navigate. The story was very strong though with really fun psychopaths. The time limit barely affected anything. I had plenty of time by the end that it almost felt pointless. Killing zombies and collecting collectibles was super fun. Side missions also kinda sucked however. Mostly fetch quests. This is so far my least favorite Dead Rising game but I've yet to play Dead Rising 4 so we'll see if that changes.

7/10\t\t\t\t\t
