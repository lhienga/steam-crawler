\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWasn't thrilled with the launch of the game on PC back in 2014 but figured I'd give it a try and see if anything improved since then.

It has not and might even be worse.

The game's a cake walk compared to the previous titles. In their effort to up the zombie killing they've removed the challenge. If you're looking for a simple zombie slaughterfest then I suppose this might scratch that itch.

However, you might not get too far as the game still suffers technical issues. Frame rates, screen tearing, to name a couple.

The biggest issue is the game is a random event where saved data, namely ranks unlocked through the course of playing, will be reset to zero without any clear reason. I literally stopped playing one day, with a rank of 39, only to boot it up the next day and everything is back to zero. Hours of work gone for seemingly no reason.

So buy and play at your own risk. Though there are better open world zombie games out there.\t\t\t\t\t
