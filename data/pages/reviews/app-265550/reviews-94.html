\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fun take on the zombie apocalypse genre. What elevates this game to new heights is the whacky crafting system combined with the equally hilarious outfits you can find in-game for all your murder needs. Want to rock a banana hammock with a fire-breathing dragon battering ram as your weapon (also doubles as your helmet)? Well, you can. Even after completing the main mission, there's still many side missions and collectibles for the avid completionist. Game is a little dated and there are newer versions of the series, but it still holds it own and is quite an enjoyable experience.

The only gripes I have with this are that the game doesn't save your settings once you exit the game. You'll have to reconfig them every time you launch it. Also, you can only save the game by finding toilets, bathrooms and portable toilets around the map. If you don't have a habit of saving frequently and the game does crash occasionally, prepare to lose some progress. Lastly, the camera angle/tracking is really jank sometimes. It will work against you sometimes but you can somewhat get used to it with a bit of finesse.\t\t\t\t\t
