City of the dead

What I liked about the game
  • \tYou get to choose where to spend your experience points, instead of the game choosing for you.

  • \tYour character can now sprint.

  • \tThe developers have added in some of those challenges, which were seen in Dead rising off the record sandbox mod.

  • \tYou can now create combo vehicles which are awesome to use, especially the RollerHawg .

  • \tThe overtime mod was different; it wasn’t your typical fetch-quest.

  • \tThe game has a nice large selection of combo weapons which were fun to use.

  • \tI liked the new location; Los Perdidos. I liked that they went for a City of the Dead.

    What I disliked or was disappointed with

  • \tThe story wasn’t particularly interesting.

  • \tCharacters weren’t very interesting.

  • \tI didn’t like the new camera angle. I thought the camera was too close to the player. I preferred the camera angle in 1 and 2.

  • \tI thought they could have made the normal mod a bit more challenging. Boss fights are way too easy and you don’t need to worry about survivors when escorting them.

  • \tThe game has way too many fetch quests. I didn’t like the idea of having to do a fetch quest for a survivor.

  • \tI wish they kept the workbench, just to add a bit more of a challenge. I didn’t like the idea of being able to craft anywhere; it’s the same thing with the vehicles as well. I wish that you had to bring the vehicles to a garage.

  • \tSoundtrack wasn’t anything special.

  • \tThe game tells you where all the collectables and blueprints are, which seems pointless.

  • \tI wish that there was more variety of objectives within the challenges.
Conclusion

A good game, but not as good as the previous installments. I didn’t like some the new things they added like being able to craft a combo weapon anywhere or being told where the collectables are on the map. I also didn’t like the new camera position on the main character. I did really like the addition of combo vehicles and the game being set in a city, instead of a mall-type place. If you enjoyed the previous games then I would say give this one a go.

7/10
