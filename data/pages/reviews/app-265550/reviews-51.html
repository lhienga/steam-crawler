\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter a e3 trailer that made it look like a generic xbone exclusive zombie game, Dead Rising 3 got cast aside but thankfully it's a worthy entry in the series.
Has what you expect from Dead Rising, racing against time to finish the serious story that contrasts the stupid silly stuff you do when killing zombies. Now featuring better controls and a bigger (but still small enough that you actually want to explore) world.

Only problem I have with it is that it is ridiculously easy, upgrades are overpowered and time limit is a non-issue, you can and will get the best ending plus all the collectibles on your very first playthrough.
This game sacrifices the tense moments for the casuals who just wanted to kill zombies and ignored how the difficulty made the game replayable.

The pc port is also pretty shoddy, but it took me less than 5 minutes to look up the fixes and apply them.

3 is so close to being the ultimate Dead Rising, but it's so easy. The series' charm is still there, and it's still worth the money. Just don't expect to play past 2 or 3 playthroughs unless you really love it.

(The included dlc is garbage btw, don't touch it. The only good dlc is xbone exclusive.)\t\t\t\t\t
