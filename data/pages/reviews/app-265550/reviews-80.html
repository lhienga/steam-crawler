\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile this sequel of DR is leaps and bounds closer to the actual idea of dead rising compared to 4, the game itself doesn't exactly hit the nail on the head. This game struggles to run well even after years of being released on PC. Locked at 30FPS and just visually buggy as hell. While there are work-arounds to get the game to run at a higher frame rate, you lose stability of basic mechanics such as aiming and crafting. The story again is just eh, but honestly, I can't say the story has ever driven the gameplay in a dead rising game. Navigating the city is a CHORE, the design is okay but just getting to a simple objective that is right next to you takes so much longer than it should. Simple complaint about pacing but that's one of my gripes with it. As a sandbox zombie beat em up it is great, but lackluster at best. I prefer the 4th in some regards which may be a bit taken aback to some but all-in-all, I just can't bring myself to finish this one. I'm going to try my damned hardest to but with all the bugs playing the game just feels like a waste of time.\t\t\t\t\t
