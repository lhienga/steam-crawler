But the map is boring to traverse. And even if it wasn't, each neighborhood is separated by highways forcing you to get in a car and run over dozens of zombies. Sounds fun? Well guess what, it isn't. For some god forsaken reason that will forever be unknown to me, there's barricades ALL over the place. Your ass is gonna be hoofin' it for much longer than you'd like.

The story is also, in a few words, utter cat ♥♥♥♥. You watch Nick whine about something for a bit then he goes and does what he's told. Rinse. Repeat.

Middle thumb because there is some fun here. If you can get it for a tenner it'll be worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
