\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was originally going to give this game a positive rating, but the more i played the more i realised it didn't deserved a negative one. it deserves two negative ratings.

The combat is pretty good, weapon designing if fun and so is coop. The mini bosses are absolutelly hilarious, and so is running around in lingerie as a hill billy. Where the game fails is that it makes you run from one end of the city to the other every single time you complete an objective. to make it worst, there are road blocks everywhere. Why add cars if every second street is blocked?

The ending though, holy was it bad. It makes you run from one end of the city to the other and back and back and back and back and back and back and back and back (have i said it 9 times yet?). NINE times doing the SAME thing to be able to fight the final boss. If it wasnt bad enough, coop players get kicked and cant finish the ending. The game is so badly designed that i couldn't even watch it on steam streaming. So i ended up wasting 14 hours running from one end of the city to the other for absolutely nothing. Don't even know how the game ended.

P.S the entire game is fetch quests. So if you like those youll like this game.
