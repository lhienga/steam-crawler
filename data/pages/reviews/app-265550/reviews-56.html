\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been wanting to play this game ever since it came out (but couldn't on account of the fact I didn't own an Xbox One and the only computer to my name was a Windows XP.) But after all these years, was this game worth the wait I waited? Hell. ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. Yes. This game, well I'll have to admit there's not as much charm as the previous ones, it is so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fun. The story is about what I'd expect, albeit, again, some charm is lost, but holy ♥♥♥♥ are the weapons fun, is this game good, play it. Just play it, give it a try. Everything in this game is good, and if you haven't already, play the earlier games, they're even better.

EDIT: After further play time, I've come to discover the game has glitches. Really nasty ones, and it almost feels unfinished, especially after going back and playing Dead Rising 1, 2 and OTR again. When I started up the first DLC pack, I discovered I was level 1. Naturally, I assumed it was a normal for that to happen. Until I went back to the main game. What I discovered was that all of my progress was completely and utterly erased. No way to recover it, nothing. I'm building myself back up, and it's fine, but it sucks man.

I'm a bit confused about the DLC packs anyway, they're certainly fun and all but they just feel like they don't....really need to be there. They're like fluff, you know? Something else I've noticed is how long it takes for things to load in. I saw an old playthrough from the Xbox One where this stuff happened, I think it was worse on that than it is here on PC, and I've seen that THIS is supposedly a mediocre port. I'd still recommend the game, but don't expect perfection. If by chance you've played the first and/or second games, don't expect this to be anywhere near the quality of those. I've yet to play Dead Rising 4, or even all of the DLC packs for this, I've only completed the first two as of writing this, but I dread if Dead Rising 4 is gonna disrespect the franchise more like I've heard.

One final note: I recall a DLC from the One that was simply not here. This minigame I've heard was fantastic, where you could play as Frank, Chuck, Nick or Annie with a variety of costumes. I'm not quite sure why it wasn't included, or what it was about. I think it was a kill as many zombies as possible type thing or something, but it's a bit confusing it's not here. Not to mention one of the DLC protagonists only appeared in a smartglass (which is just on the One I think?) EXCUSIVE mission. Which obviously is not here! Confusing, eh?

EDIT 2: ok I started the third DLC pack and the game reset my levels again ♥♥♥♥ this game

EDIT 3: I am over my moment of saltiness and I have found how to get around the save file being deleted ♥♥♥♥. It still bugs me, but that doesn't change the truth. This is a good game, but god can it piss me off.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo in Dead Rising 1 and 2 it was designed like a mall meaning that shops were front and center and you could easily see what was what from a glance and if you really needed to find a special item you could open up the map, hover over a store and you could assume what might be in that store depending on the store.

In the open world map where a lot of the buildings can't even be entered yknow what brilliant idea they had? To remove that feature, so when you hover over a building/shop it doesn't tell you what that shop is. So I'm on one of the first side missions and I'm asked to find a chainsaw.

...

♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ where? I have no idea where a hardware store is and with the limited timer I don't want to waste time just looking around at different shops manually.

Oh and the game is extremely overt with its political messages and it's not about me disagreeing with them cause I can disagree with a lot of things but still enjoy them on their own merits. Like I'm not religious but I can get into a religious story. But the problem is when that politics is handled by what I can only assume is a 14 year old thinking they're hot ♥♥♥♥ at social commentary. There's political satire and then there's bad political satire. Dead Rising 3 falls in the camp of the latter.

And then we have the protagonist. I remember people being pissy about Dead Rising 2's protagonist being a wooden plank so they decided that Dead Rising 3's protagonist was gonna be a whiny crybaby who perpetually has an expression as if he's going to burst in tears.

I'm desperately trying to give this game a fair chance for the third time now and I don't know if I can do it. Even if I can overcome these problems these are not problems I should HAVE TO overcome in the first place. ♥♥♥♥ political satire, whiny protagonist, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awful map system and I can't uncap the FPS from 30 despite following the guides

[edit]
So I decided to play the game just so I could finish up a map of my own (which i have no idea where to upload for others to enjoy (if you got a suggestion, please tell me cause I listed all the store's names and numbered all the buildings)) and the game actually throws a curveball when it comes to the political stuff. I figured it was gonna be very PC and overly left leaning to the point that it is portraying right-wingers as actual nazi's but no the game takes the piss out of everyone. And the character you're playing as starts out as a total whimp but he actually has a character progression where he gets more macho as it goes on.

However, while I can redact those two negatives it comes with other negatives instead.

So we use vehicles to get around right, but there are blockades on the road which we cannot remove and they just downright piss me off but worse still is that as the game goes on it sets up MORE roadblocks just to screw with your travel even more.

The game is outright too easy. My personal experience is that I had access to a minigun in my gunlocker which I could take out 8 of them in a row and when I reached max level and I got all the upgrades ranged weapons are insanely useful because bullets penetrate zombies so just a quick DRRRT and you've cut down everyone in front of you.

There's not that many psychopaths this time around and because of how easy it is to get weapons (even outside of the weapon locker) I could easily just craft myself a powerful gun and just gun down the psycho with ease.

I would change my score to MIXED but since that is STILL not an option because Steam is staffed by absolute tits I'll leave it as negative simply because of the imbecilic change of how the map works.\t\t\t\t\t
