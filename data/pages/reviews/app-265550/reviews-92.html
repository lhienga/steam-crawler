There are some fixes that work really well to combat the poor design choices like the nauseating FOV and capped fps, however, there is one specific glitch where some of the included dlc is no longer showing up and I can't find a fix for that. Other than that, I have currently got the game working really well actually, running it on max settings. There is a way to get this game to run really well it just takes some tweaking.

If you are looking for a review of the game itself then I have to say I LOVE this game. The story, humor, and gameplay are awesome and, at its best, it can feel really immersive. If you are looking to have the most fun: dont upgrade your health too much, only fight bosses with items from their environment, and use guns over melee weapons!
