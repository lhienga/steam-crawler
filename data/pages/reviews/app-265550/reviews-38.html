Despite the rather ugly color pallete for the environment, I found running around Los Perdidos to be fun, mowing down zombies with an absurd arsenal of weapons. This addition comes in the form of Combo Vehicles, where you can do the same sort of 'put two things together, get something new' thing for vehicles. A scrappy car straight out of Mad Max? \r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of a few games that made me miss the Xbox One. As i wasn't planning on buying another, I was super happy to find this on the Steam store.

I never got on with the first few games and am not planning on buying the online grind fest of the next one. This is by far my second favourite zombie game, closely behind the first Dying Light.
The insane combo weapons that slaughter hundreds in mere seconds make this game so fun. I haven't tried the DLCs yet as I didn't have them on Xbox, but am looking forward to trying everything out.

My only bad point is the countdown you get to do everything. While it's not a massive pain, as you have plenty of time to do everything on normal mode. I still don't like the feeling of being rushed.

Works amazingly with my controller (although like with all games, would be nice if the buttons could be switched for Playstation ones, but that's not the games fault I guess)

Would recommend to anyone who likes zombie games and wants a laugh riot while they are at it.
