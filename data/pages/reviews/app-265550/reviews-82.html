\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDead Rising 3 isn't nowhere near the level of irredimable trash that 4 was, but this entry was a sign of things to come.
I picked it up blind to play with a friend after we enjoyed our time in 2/OTR. I have a long history with the series, with 400+ hours in GFWL (don't miss it) and completing the first game to the point of Gone Guru giving me PTSD.
So we picked it up and went into it. Finished it with Overtime, and honestly, every complaint of 4 can be traced back here.
Dead Rising was built on three pillars, a juxtaposed setting, urgency and mastery of the game rewarding you.
The setting is a grim, colorless and completly miserable enviorment, Nick is hardly charmisatic and just wimps all over the place, and the story is just as serious as always without the contrast to aliviate. To make it worse, none of the shops, locations and areas are unique, they blend with each other and end up becoming a uninteresting blur you don't want to explore.
I dont think explaining why urgency is needed. The entire POINT of the timer was making every second count, you had to plan ahead, make the most of your mastery and especially encouraged replaying the game with your upgrades kept and put your knowlage to beat the game. At no time do you feel any pressure, the game is so generous with time that you could 100% it and still have spare days.
And finally mastery. This game is just easy. We never died the entire way through. The map is full of guns, chuckfull of healing items and worst of all, every safe house has a locker to spawn any weapon as much you want with a generous timed limit, even OP DLC content that breaks the game entirely. The enviorment doesnt have unique content scattered, you dont get rewarded for learning where stuff is, it just hands over the victory and doesnt even bother to challange you.

So no, I don't recommend this incredibly mediocre game, unless you're just starving for more to play.
Just get or replay the first and second title, they are way more enjoyable games with better to offer.\t\t\t\t\t
