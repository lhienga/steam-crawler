\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a hardcore Dead Rising series fan, I can say with full confidence that this was a great game. I played through the entire story and all of the Untold Stories side missions and enjoyed all of it. This is definitely different than its predecessors in that it is more serious and not as cartoon-esque as the others, and the map is much larger than what you'd be used to. I see these as positive things though. Gameplay felt clean, I love the huge array of weapons and vehicles, the map gets kind of annoying to drive frequently and there is a lot of driving in this game as opposed to the others where vehicles felt more like a novelty than a necessity. This game still includes combo weapons and the ability to throw almost anything you can find at zombies. Some people mentioned game breaking glitches like cars going through the map and such, but I played all of the game's content and did not experience one game-breaking glitch.

So if you like the Dead Rising series you will enjoy this game, take my word for it. Avoid the fourth release though, I don't even consider that a part of this series. It's literally a superhero zombie game that takes place during Christmas with a ton of copy-pasted content from this game.

TLDR;

Any DR fan will enjoy this game. It's different than the other ones, but give it a chance.\t\t\t\t\t

not to mention in the map i kept getting lost over how confusing the layouts were. i get it it's supposed to be open world and all but it doesn't mean i should look at the map and ask myself where the hell am going and if it's the right way

overall if i had to rate this game out of 5
it'd be a solid 3 out of 5 definitely would play again if asked to play with
