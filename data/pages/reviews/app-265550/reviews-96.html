\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so much fun to me and is really unique in the series of Dead Rising, for better or worse.

Compared to other Dead Rising games, things are a bit more serious, both in tone and game play. There's less straight up nonsense, much less colour in the environments, and even your movent is less robotic like previous Dead Rising games and instead more realistic with momentum. I'm fine with this because the core game is still there: Massive violence in a depressing world that does NOT take itself seriously.

It's just a freakin' massive shame that someone really does not want you to play this on PC. Modding it has only gotten harder over the years, and with CAPCOM's recent DRM additions, getting the game to run at 60 frames is even harder than ever! Good luck friends ;w;

Also don't ignore the DLCs to this game! They're pretty funnn! The gameplay is pretty basic but the story is hella worth playing through once. Each chapter is rather short, whilst providing stories of familiar + new faces from the outbreak. Was pleasantly surprised by these.

ALSO: If you ever have the time... Check out the Dead Rising film. It's a bloody good time :D\t\t\t\t\t
