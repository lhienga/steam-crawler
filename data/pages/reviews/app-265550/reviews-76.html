Pros
+ There is a lot of content here. DLC of which there are
more missions and characters to play as.

+ No loading times, and more optimize even in lot of zombie still 60fps in old vga like mines GTX750Ti

+ They still add Chuck in game and his son so lovely :)

+ Los Perdidos is an interesting enough locale that is filled with collectables. There is literally so much to do
and collect, so if you are into this, this is a good thing.

+ Survivor AI is much better this time around. I do not feel like you have to babysit these individuals as much.
Add it in with the fact that you can even summon AI to help out out or to take them out of the world when
they are not needed is even better.

+ The newly expanded crafting system is better than what we had in Dead Rising 2. There are so many
combinations of weapons that it becomes pretty exciting to try out new weapons. I also appreciated the
fact that it is much easier to craft items on the go as you do not have to find a specific work bench to do it.
With that said, there were a few weapons that seemed to have too many variations (I am thinking about
the toy bear here).

Cons
- Frame Lock on 30fps but its okay just watch youtube and problem solved

- No frank west in game but still had chuck

- Opacity hud is to low u can't see the radar on mini map or mission list

- Driving handling so hard to control

- Boss is too easy\t\t\t\t\t
