BORING PREFACE ._.
Well..
I've beat this game three times, and have something to share.
(I'm sort of hotline miami fan, looked for some games like hotline, many times encountered this one, finally got it, and did not regret for sure.)
Beyond these visual effects, action-platformer combat gameplay, and level design i saw way more than a simple indie video game. I gave it a status of small-yet-worthy-masterpiece, and for the certain reasons. These reasons are: plot, how it served to the player, atmosphere+soundtrack which is inseparable, setting and the art of it, and few other details. I could describe this one probably as magnificent movie in cyberpunk genre, but slightly extraordinary cyberpunk movie. And i mean here we have the combination, the combination of involving yet fictional story, sad but neon bright vibe, quick yet discomprohensive combat. I'll try to note all importances one-by one.. Firstly i want to tell that katana zero got me not for the reason of gameplay, but for its world (or universe).. I don't want to tell that combat or any action was a bad experience but it was for me not at the first place to note as the main points of its greatness.

HERE WE ARE
Anyway.. Here are the moments which makes this particular game a masterpiece in my eyes:
STORY
> You are the lonely killer in the dismal cyberpunk city, but not the regular one, you are a samurai with supernatural powers (I never thought that i would like samurais stuff). You are getting contracts for ordered killing targets it's your job, you are living in a dirty yet real apartment seeing nightmares everynight, for some reason you are visiting a doctor who at the same time your contracts dealer and he gives you a dosage of unknown drug everytime after completing your killers daily routine. Every level the story and situations getting way tensiful and involving, don't want to reveal much but i'll say that you will try to figure out what has happend in the past alongside getting familiar with story of the games world, this character and his destiny in game seems quite fittable for me to fall deep and feel myself as the hero of the game itself. I think that's the thing made this game great at the first place..
HOW IT SERVED
> How the story was served for the player is undescribable, well it's served in the weird sort of mysterious routine of samurai killer that meets everytime things that were clandestine for him for the certain reasons (plus the loss of memory adds up logical explanation at first time), again alongside with visuals, sounds and other efforts that worked so well.
ATMOSPHERE
> Oh, thats perhaps my favourite part, it's raining, it's dismal, it's gore, it's nightmare, it's ahh, i consider the music as the main core that made this atmosphere so profound, music is just stunning, starting off with main menu theme and so forth, separately track SNOW gave me a lot of goosebumps. For sure i cannot forget the graphics, these visuals are not the best but all of the details, art, colors, and mood through these graphics is just another dimension that i would like to visit.
FINAL WORDS
There was a lot inside me, i just could not held it anymore, the game absolutely worth the time, this story and all of the inner surroundings it's just an ART, sometime i guess i will return back to this city of New Mecca...
P. S. Sorry if it was dull.
