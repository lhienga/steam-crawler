I have 30 hours of playtime with it(this is a very short game, you can complete it in like 4 hours if you don't know what you're doing...like 2 if you do know what you're doing. I have by now completed this game more times than I have completed almost any game).

Narratively this is the best game I've ever played. I'm not being hyperbolic as far as writing is concerned this is as good as any game has ever been imho. The gameplay is good too, combat is more like a puzzle, you're a psychic and when you "die" you just say "that won't work" and go back in time to plan out your attack again. Everything is one touch deaths with the exception of bosses but they usually go down in few hits while still killing you in one hit. The way this game blends story with gameplay is in such a way that this is a really great story that also would only really benefit from being written for a video game. Also you can flirt with a weeby hotel employee who thinks you're hot because you have a katana, which as we all know makes this game a masterpiece objectively.

The only downside is waiting 2 million years for the DLC but I do want shorter games with worse graphics and for them to take longer to develop so people aren't literally crunch culture-ing themselves to death just to make games so...I support Justin in his long process of continuing this story very very slowly and am mostly just happy that a game this good even gets to exist in the first place.\t\t\t\t\t
