\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, let me break down into multiple titles and get to my conclusion. *spoiler alert*

Story: As an Indie game, this story is so GOOD. like we start of with the main character called Zero, doing his killing job from his physiologist, in return that he will give him his treatment called "Chronos". So, Zero does the missions, and he met a little girl whose name is not revealed in the game. Then, Zero met is former allies named "fifteen" and "headhunter". Zero ended up killing the headhunter guy in the story and he learnt that he's one of the operatives in the new mecca operation, where the people of 1st district and 3rd district fought to get rid of eachother. Zero met two heads, silver and gold during his 9th mission. the silver head said if he chose him Zero will live and the gold head said vice versa. They said they would take the little girl if he chose to live but Zero didn't hear that and he ended up choosing to live, where the little girl got taken by those two heads. that's the overall summary of the story.

Gameplay: The gameplay was super good. You can slow down time for certain amount of time and repell bullet that come at you, AND THE EVEN MORE CRAZY GOOD THINGS IS THAT you don't need to use the perception of time power to repell the bullets! You can find secretly hidden keys to get cool new katanas after finishing the game and you can do a SPEEDRUN mode where you can skip the whole story and just do the gameplay.

Characters: Zero is a cool guy who's suffered from amnesia and he recovered everything at the end of the game.

Fifteen: He's the former ally of zero and he can also do the same thing as zero but he got a better ability than Zero where he can dash through any amount of enemy units if they are in his dash range.

V: The main villain of the game. He's Russian and he hunts down people like Zero who uses cronos to have perception of time. He eventually gets caught by fifteen and killed by him.

Conclusion: Overall, the story is super nice, the gameplay is amazing and the game is like 3 hours long so, for me personally, I give it a 9/10 only because the DLC isn't out yet😢.\t\t\t\t\t
