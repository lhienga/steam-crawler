You play as a time slowing one-hit kill samurai, Need I say more? The story is great and I love the interactions I had with the characters. With all these glowing positive things being said, why am I reluctant to post this? This game just does NOT feel finished. I beat the entire game in about 7 hours. I am planning on coming back and 100% completing this game which will add onto my time, but that doesn't effect my first play-through, and in my first play-through, I felt betrayed. I am not exaggerating when I say, just when the game is at its peak, the point where you really feel you are in control and its time for PEAK GAME PLAY... it just... ends??? When I got to the ending I literally laughed because I thought it would be one of those fake out endings, but NOPE, IT JUST ENDS LIKE THAT. I still love this game, and everything surrounding it, but my god this DLC that has been in the works better be POPPING to make up for how I felt at the end of the game because if its like another 2 hours of gameplay I am coming back to this review and it is not going to be changed to something nice I'm telling you that much. The developers clearly love the game and are hard at work making this DLC great, I just am really salty about the sudden ending at the peak of the game. Now, with all this being said, PLEASE PLAY THIS GAME! Getting sick as ♥♥♥♥ one shot kills, slowing down time, parrying BULLETS, some of the most fun I've had in a while. If you are still reluctant, wait for the DLC and buy it together so you don't have the same salty ending I did, but by all means, PLAY. THIS. GAME. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
