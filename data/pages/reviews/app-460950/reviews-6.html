\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou're a time manipulating, ex-soldier turned assassin with amnesia.
You uncover bits about your past as the story progresses.
A single play can be completed in under 5 hours but there are more secrets in the game than you'll find the first time through.

Gameplay is great. You dodge attacks, deflect and throw projectiles, jump from wall to wall, and slow down time. Enough enemy variety to keep the game interesting. Levels are created in ways that make you utilize your different skills. You will have to think about the consequences of barging through a door without first planning. There is a timer for each level but you would be hard pressed to exhaust it before clearing the map. There is an emphasis on clearing levels quickly and efficiently. There is even a speedrun mode for those interested in that kind of gameplay.

There is a DLC that has been in the works for some time now that was advertised as free. Stoked for that to release so we can get some more of this story.

Definitely worth picking up, even at full price.\t\t\t\t\t
