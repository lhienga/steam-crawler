THEY LET U PET THE CAT AT THE START THAT'S AMAZING


Fairly priced game although it's not very long. Soundtracks and the artwork is amazing, the story was better than i expected, i thought it was going to be pretty boring but i enjoyed every bit of it. Gameplay is nice, repetetive but doesnt feel boring, different enemies has different moves which is nice for a game like this. Story is more on the darker side, so it's not really good if you're looking for something to relax. Also you restart the stage everytime u die, so it feels like a souls game somehow. Overall gameplay and story is basically perfect compared to what i expected. Good experience, probably you wont regret. ALSO. U. CAN. PET. THE. CAT.

LOVE THE SOUNDTRACKS, seriously

MOST ANNOYING THING IS THE SOLDIERS WHO HAS SHIELDS, THEY KNOCK U OUT AND SHOOT U BEFORE U GET UP.\t\t\t\t\t
