Katana Zero is a fantastic game.

The story is excellent, ive played through it 3 times now, excluding the "Katana Hero" mode (I usually get bored of replaying a games story after the first time) The game-play itself is easy to learn and not even necessarily hard to master, you just kind of stop slowing down time as you play more and more.

Hunting down all of the achievements was fun too, even if I needed a guide to figure out most of the keys, I don't have the two speed-running ones though, but I will one day!

I believe that Katana Zero is worth the money, and the dlc will be free if I recall correctly, so going off of that logic, it'll be under-priced?

Fight the secret boss, it's a good challenge.\t\t\t\t\t
