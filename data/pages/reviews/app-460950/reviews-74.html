\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's an underestimated pearl with nice pixel art and a really cool soundtrack. You are a samurai assassin addicted to a drug called 'chronos' which allows you to manipulate time. In platform levels you hack and slay through hordes of enemies but be warned the game mechanic emphasizes every microsecond of your actions. Every single misstep is punished merciless and at times it's really hard to withstand the strong impulse of rage quitting and there will be levels where you die a thousand times without any foreseeable progress. On the other hand i was quite surprised that the story telling is well done for such a kind of game as it never gets boring until the great finale is revealed and although the playtime is not that long (i did it in ~7h) there's still a lot of stuff happening. For speed runners this may be interesting as well as the difficulty doing this without errors is at some points really insane regarding perception, respond time, adaptability and dexterity with your gamepad. For me it was fun to play!\t\t\t\t\t
