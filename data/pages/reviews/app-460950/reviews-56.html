\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Katana ZERO is an intense real-time fighting game where you, a contract killer, travel through facilities taking out goons and toppling a secret government conspiracy. The meat of the game is the backstory of our unnamed protagonist, and his ability to perceive time at a slower rate (allowing for split-second decisions like reflecting bullets).
WOW WHAT A GAME! The story is phenomenal, the dialogue portions of the game are almost as fun as the combat itself. It is SO satisfying to cut-off a villain mid-monologue, and it's REALLY COOL to get to watch a replay of your battle at regular speed. It really shows how much of an animal the protagonist is.
The story shouldn't take you longer than 4 hours, and is a wonderful adventure to play through. There are a lot of things to explore once the story is over too, and I appreciate the inclusion of a Speedrun Mode. Obviously, the art is amazing, and the music does an incredible job of setting the futuristic-techno city of New Mecca. If you like platformers and think reflecting bullets is cool as hell, you NEED this game in your library!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI consider this to be the perfect game. Everything in this game is refined to a tea, including its game play, story, mechanics, humour, music, characters, art style and animations.

Many challenges take careful coordination to complete, and leave no room for error. This game is frustrating, but it is not annoying. Every success feels satisfying and leaves you wanting more.

The story has very emotional moments, and some abstract moments that the player must interpret. It does not throw a bone to the player to make them feel good about what they are doing, it plays out realistically to how the story should progress. The music is well suited for each scene, and amplifies the emotional moments.

I cannot stress this enough, this game must be experienced with audio, and optimally headphones.

The game is short (6-8 hours I would say), so make sure to space out play sessions with that in mind. Although, there is also a hard-mode which will take you much longer to complete.

I have played many popular games on steam including the portal series, half-life series, hollow knight, celeste, terraria, etc. So I do not say it lightly when I say: [/b] this is the best game I have ever played. [/b]\t\t\t\t\t
