\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnce again, I hate that I cannot leave a neutral/informative review.

Personally, I found the gameplay to be very challenging and fun, with tight controls and great level design.
But I feel that what made this game so strong took a backseat to the lackluster storytelling.

Throughout this ~4 hour experience, I kept hesitating to skip through dialog as I was unsure what might affect said story later on. Cutscene after cutscene of nonsensical back and forth constantly gave me more questions than answers, and [personally] these took away from the fantastic gameplay that kept me engaged.

I feel this game is far to short for the pricepoint, and ending with a "to be continued", so abruptly as well, just confirms my suspicions that a hefty focus was placed on the story instead of more missions/boss fights/etc.

I don't personally recommend it, but if it's on sale I do believe it's worth checking out to see if the story works for you. If not, I recommend setting a timer for 1:55:00.\t\t\t\t\t
