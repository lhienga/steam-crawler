\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFlashy, punchy, stylish, and enjoyably frustrating. I simultaneously felt like a superhuman action movie hero and the victim of a sadistic experiment. But I endued the punishment and became more powerful, spurred on by tantalizing worldbuilding, lovingly-crafted pixel art, and a groovy soundtrack that fits the mood perfectly. The game gives you a mix of challenges - sometimes there are numerous ways to make your way through, and other times you have to figure out just the right trick to squeak through in one piece. I won't spoil anything here, but the finale of this game is a real treat. The story and the game mechanics build up in a crescendo of novel challenges leading to an awesome payoff in the end.

This game has heart, but it also has teeth. Expect to die a lot. It's your job. Now get out there, and don't leave any witnesses.
