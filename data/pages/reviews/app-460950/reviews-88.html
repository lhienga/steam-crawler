\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, fun game play with nice soundtrack with it

Love the little detail of main character putting cassette with each level start and it's was especially exciting when I realized how game mechanic was tied to the story, it was pretty eye opening.

The difficulty were not that bad in normal mode. Struggle with some part of the level and boss but overall it was pretty easy and fun to complete.

It leave me little surprise how it ends with twist and cliffhanger but not sure if the different choice I made will leads to the different ending or I have to wait for sequel or DLC for more..

And other thing I wish was the game to be little longer. The first playthrough only finished in 4hrs so the story itself is not that long. But with $9 I purchased with discount, it well worth the money imo. And re-playable game play with hard/speedrun mode make this well worth the price.

Overall, I enjoy the game very much :)
