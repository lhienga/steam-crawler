\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeatable in a few hours of playtime.

The bosses are challenging but after a few deaths you get the hang of their movesets( except the secret boss, that thing is psychedelic ).

The gameplay is fun: each enemy has it's own attacks and it is up to you to learn what those are and how you approach them. Everything dies in one hit here, including the player, but you have plenty of time to learn since you can respawn at the beginning of each level.

I still remember the story, so it must be pretty good. The characters are also memorable.

The music is good, I still have some of those tracks in my personal playlist and I listen to them from time to time.

Hard mode is not for the faint of heart. I also do not care about the speedrun mode, I do not enjoy playing a game in such a way.

In conclusion, I liked the game.\t\t\t\t\t
