\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKatana ZERO is a highly stylised fast-paced action-platformer where you play as a super-soldier hired assassin on a quest to solve the mystery of their own past.

The gameplay is extremely smooth and rapid, taking evident inspiration from Hotline Miami. It provides quite a difficult challenge but in a fair and rewarding way. The graphics, presentation and visual effects are all mesmerising. The story, although confusing, is very enticing and creative. I particularly enjoyed the fun dialogues and the subtle yet effective humour that seamlessly blended with the tone of the game. Overall, both mechanically and aesthetically, a very rich and gratifying experience.

My only complaints are that the game is rather short and the story ends on a cliffhanger. Fortunately, however, the developers are currently working on a free DLC, which I am very much looking forward to. 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKatana Zero has got it all to make all you little gamers squeal in delight!

A banger vaporwave sound track by talented artists LudoWic, Bill Kiley and up and coming artist Robert Sylvester Kelly, great job guys! The soundtrack is enough to get anyone in the mood to hack and slash their way through a compound filled with goons with guns, automated turrets, and Strong Terry (watch out for Strong Terry).

The game play is a one hit kill system that makes you feel like a lone Ronin, a one man army, slicing through all those appose you, staining the folded steel that glides through the bodies of your enemies, forging the path to your Destiny(2)®.
Or it could also make you feel like a helpless infant wielding a plastic toy, facing up against your drunk uncle Dennis... but no worries! That's where the game's coolest mechanic comes in! The revive function! every time you die you rewind to the beginning of the room you enter, knowing a little more each time, learning more and more how enemies work and where they're positions are. It might take some time... but you'll knock down good ol' Unc' Dennis is no time!

The story???
THE STORY????
I have played a number of games, some could even say I am a "Veteran Gamer." but I have never played a game where the story was as amazingly crafted as this one. It conveys such a moving narrative that compliments the game play even working in tandem with it, making a game that rivals story telling from games like Metal Gear, and John Madden Football (1988 video game). And even after the game is long finished, there is an incredible amount of replay value, with the speed run and hard mode game modes, which allows you to face off against Father Colton (more powerful then Uncle Dennis)

In conclusion, I'd recommend this game to anyone who likes hack and slash games, and if you played Hotline Miami, you'd really enjoy this game! Play the game yah turkey! It's worth the 14 big ones and 99 little buddies!

Get her done! and God bless!
