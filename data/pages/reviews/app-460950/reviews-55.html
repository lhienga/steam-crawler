\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWARNING: THIS GAME IS ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ BRUTAL! REAL LIFE REAL WORD LEVEL BRUTAL!
EXPLICIT KIDNAPPING, IMPLIED CHILD TORTURE, & CSA DURING ONE OF THE ENDINGS.










Quite possibly one of the most uncanny, and hauntingly beautiful cyberpunk action games I've ever played. It's a retro side scroll hack n slash bullet hell / time reset game. Its genres are time travel, sci-fi, psychological thriller, horror, and it's a G** D*** F***ING MASTERPIECE! I had a feeling this game was going to be amazing the moment I saw the trailer, and it still went beyond my expectations in every way. Everything from gloomily depressing, but lovably sleazy old timey hard boiled noir detective movie setting. To the piano music, to the most electronic that electronica has to offer in terms of the most retro futuristic music style a cyber punk dystopia can bounce into your eardrums. The music is one part of what makes this game PHENOMINAL.

Another thing is that takes you through a heart-warming, and gut wrenching roller coaster that has you questioning the main characters sanity, the continuity of the timeline, the nature of reality itself, followed up by the most emotionally upsetting and real world ♥♥♥♥♥♥ up bleaker-than-bleak cliff hanger ending I've ever had the displeasure of reaching in a video game.

No. I'm serious. I don't have another one off the top of my head that comes close for me. It's probably not most people's, but my background probably makes this game hit some nerves for me. . . I am chewing my fingernails for more hoping I we can all do something if we're careful, quick, and clever... Thing is. . . no matter what happens, the game is a emotionally razor sharp dagger to the heart in a masterful way. Most people aren't looking for that in a video game. Most people don't care what a masterful stroke of writing it takes for that to be possible. The game isn't bad because the ending isn't happy, satisfying, or ends on a cliffhanger.

Also, if in the sequel she's dead or worse . . . & trust me I hope not, or I hope there's at least ways to avert those endings if so... but this is the devs game/work of art. Not our power fantasy. Kids all around the world every day get violated, and snuffed. I was almost one of the ones that didn't make it. We live in a cynically bleak, and nightmarish world. Always have, and this game doesn't shy away from that. I find that to be oddly comforting. As a trafficking survivor, a recovered hard drug addict, as a veteran with ptsd, and an orphan this game had me in tears. . . It's not for the faint of heart, and the point will be missed by most edge lords too. Kid characters are not safe from harm in this story. Just a fair warning without spoilers. If that's not for you, I understand. Katana 0 was everything I never knew I needed in a very small, and precisely perfected work of art. Worth every penny, even full price, because this game is priceless.

Spoiler: Honestly one of the most painful parts of the game is bonding with this adorable little girl who gives you this stuffed toy she's named LEVIATHAN, which is OLD TESTAMENT. The girl has dark eyes, and hair, and looks Asian/Jewish coded as ♥♥♥♥, so losing her at the end in such a grim way really hits close to home, and I'm chewing my nails for a sequel hoping to God we can save her in the nick of time/or that she can defend herself.\t\t\t\t\t
