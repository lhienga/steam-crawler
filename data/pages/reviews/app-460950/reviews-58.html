I'm going through my library and playing games to 100% to revive my love for gaming rather than mindlessly playing video games.

This game stands separate from many due to the mechanics and the smoothness of gameplay. It can be quite difficult to grasp the game however when you finally find that strategy to beat a level there is a huge satisfaction. This game also showed me how difficult speedrunning truly is due to one of the achievements. I had a very hard time with the speedrun but once I nailed the gold medal I felt amazing. The secrets that were in the game were fun and entertaining rather than just simply a single easter egg with an achievement. I know there was supposed to be a DLC released and we still have not heard anything else but I wish I could have had more time to learn about some characters that seemed they were going to be more relevant.\t\t\t\t\t
