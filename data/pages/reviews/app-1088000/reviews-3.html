\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell I feel a bit divided on this one.
On the one hand it's nice to continue the adventure and to explore the world/lore, and the new mutant is pretty cool. Plus there are some nice or funny ideas here and there, and the Ark remains a great place to go back to.
On the other hand, it's somehow much more repetitive in terms of maps, the latter and most items you can find are less interesting, and enemies mostly just had a skin change. Plus, although it picks up where the base game left, it pretty much goes on a side story rather than giving answers to this very intriguing ending with the radio call (even adds questions on the long run), so it's a bit of disappointment for me because it left me wanting for more. You can add a clumsy processing of floors (camera sometimes not showing the upper floor when you need it to, and characters falling when they take cover on edge), which sometimes makes exploration or tactical preparation a bit tricky.

Overall this dlc could have been a bit more diverse, considering what the base game offered, but it does not deserve a thumb down from me.\t\t\t\t\t
