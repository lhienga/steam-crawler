Unfortunately, I can't give the expansion a passing grade. There was simply nothing of value added apart from a small story-progression in the very beginning. Everything else is a side-mission and it doesn't even come with anything particularly interesting.

A new mutant to play with. He didn't tickle my fancy unfortunately
More health on enemies, compensated by (only) 1 stealth gun and a few more or less equal loud weapons (no advancement in add-ons).
Almost nothing new in the ark. And the new things were not interesting. Iridia "added" new stock with every 5 or 10 levels. But only medpacks, grenades and other one-use items. Ended the game with a few hundred weapon-scrap and around a thousand normal, had nothing to spend it on.

Plus side, new HATS (felt underwhelming)
Also plus, Föroreningsparbanken (and a few other Swedish jokes, these keep being hilarious)

If you liked the original wait for a sale, this is a slower grind with less rewards/progression\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA short, barebones expansion to a flawed game. Worse, the flaws are carried over; no quicksave, limited key rebinds, camera jankiness, lackluster skill tress, and recycling of assets - it's all still here. Probably because this was cut from the base game for whatever reason, which would retroactively explain its unsatisfyingly abrupt cliffhanger ending.

It's hard to recommend this unless its on a deep sale. There are simply so many other, better. and cheaper turn based squad games out there more deserving of your money (I Am Not A Monster says hello)

Hopefully these Devs up their game if they get around to a sequel.\t\t\t\t\t
