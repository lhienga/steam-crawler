Enemies are the same as the original other than adding pod to their names, changing the visual a bit and tweaking some numbers like health and damage which is the same with leveling enemies. Only new enemy is a pod which can spawn enemies. Game gives you some new side missions for old areas which is again same enemies with a name.
As for the new character, he has two new skills which are not very useful and he is very behind of your characters. Therefore, you do not want to use him. Only new leveling you can do is some upgrades. Most of them do not feel very different other than the vine ability which is great. The upgrades are pretty weak again which only improves the consumables (if you play for ambushes, they do not even matter).
Lastly the story, it isn’t really interesting, related or detailed; just there to be there. I have finished everything in 8 hours with a little bit of AFK and when you check the price it does not worth in any aspect, with its lazy design.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRecommend- 8/10

The Good: New equipment, new character and a few new enemies help to keep the new maps feeling fresh. The extra skill enhancements are also appreciated. Gameplay is about the same, but your play style could change depending on how much you incorporate the new character. It’s more of the main game, which is great. The story does continue a little bit in this DLC, which I was not expecting.

The Bad: The DLC is too short to really get much out of the additional abilities and character. I wish there was a new game+ mode where you could play through again but with this DLC content already unlocked.

Overall: If you liked the base game, you’ll get more of it here. Setting up for a sequel?\t\t\t\t\t
