\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very slow developing game that still has a lot left to be desired. I got this game during the beta not expecting much as it obviously wasn't fully released at the time. I played it and especially during the beta it was really buggy. The main mode I played was Police and from vehicles not pulling over properly, the pursuit mechanics being ♥♥♥♥♥♥ and no AI back up it was really frustrating. On top of that the characters, vehicles and peds have all been the same since the beta was released. As someone who has played LSPDFR and knows how in depth that mod is for GTA I'll continue to play that over this game any day. Its even more irritating because the more you do play the game more whether its Police, Fire or EMS you'll soon realize that there is potential here. Now when I had seen that this was gonna be fully released I was very excited hoping the full product would've had huge upgrades and a completely different feel from the beta and I was heavily disappointed. The vehicle physics suck, the weapon audio is atrocious. It felt exactly like the beta with a different menu screen. The game is extremely buggy and with how this game is slowly developing I would definitely not recommend this at all specifically at this time. Maybe later that changes and they should be pushing the envelop as fast as possible considering there is potential here but at the rate at which this game gets updated the game might be actually good by 2026

My wishlist for this game (specifically Solo Police mode)

- Good AI backup
- Multiple agencies/jurisdictions (ie: City PD, County Sheriff, State Police)
- Ambient events
- AI partner
- The ability to see other AI officers out on patrol driving around and or responding to calls
- New characters/peds variety
- AI traffic variety (more vehicles)
- Better traffic control (Stop traffic, slow traffic down, redirect traffic)
- Better vehicle physics
- Better weapon audio
- Better traffic stops
- Field Sobriety Tests (Walk and turn, HGN, One Leg Stand)
- AI responding better to lights/sirens
- Gore (blood etc)
- Map development
- More Callouts
