\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this when it first came out and don't get me wrong, it's great that the devs are still putting work into it. The problem is, they are building on a base that was never fixed up. It's little things that cause the overall experience to be low and instead of going back and improving on these they just seem to be adding onto it.

Things such as animation, some of the animations are terrible and buggy and are the exact same ones they've had since the beginning.

Car spawning is also terrible, cars often times spawn in front of you or take a while to spawn in general, if they do spawn in places they have no speed and ruin the flow of traffic such as on the highway. Not to mention the driving AI is terrible.

They have missions and ect and have finally given users control over making their own which is a great step, really they should add mod support better than textures and sounds as I believe this would expand the game widely because people would be able to add many hot patches and fixes that are very needed. It would also take the work load off the devs where they could focus on the base things and improving them.

It's sad to see that after all this time we have the same base with no real improvements other than stuff being piled on the bad base. I am still optimistic and this isn't to put the devs down, I just want to see real changes.

I'll come back and adjust my review in the future if these ever do get fixed.\t\t\t\t\t
