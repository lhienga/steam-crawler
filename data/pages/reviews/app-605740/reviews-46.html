\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've probably owned this game now for around 3 years now and when I first brought it, I thought it was an interesting project and was excited to see the development and the potential interesting game play the game would offer as the time comes. However, its been 3 years now and with the announcement of the 1.0 I cant say that it was worth a purchase.

The game play just isn't interesting enough for me and multiplayer doesn't really match up as well. I've had so many troubles with bugs and missions just not working that I've given up even playing for long amounts of time because I get so frustrated and bored. The gamy play just doesn't feel great either and compares to a cheap mobile game rather than an actual police, fire and EMS simulator. usually, I'd often find myself just driving round aimlessly because of the lack of AI vehicles, AI pedestrians and enjoyable missions.

I think the low hours I have on this game for the amount of time I've owned it really shows how I feel and I cant say it was worth my money at all and wish I spent more time and money on other police simulators rather than this one. I really enjoyed the progress the game was making over a 3 year span and maybe i'll come back when they've added AI back up and increased the amount of cars on the road and pedestrians on the street but I really cant say it was worth a purchase looking back.

I think some improvements that would make me or someone like me be more interested in playing would be adding AI back up (which their hopefully gonna do soon), increasing the number of AI cars and pedestrians, adding a way point system on the map (like in GTA) so i can follow the line on map which tells me what roads I need to drive on rather than opening the map every 30 seconds just to make sure i'm going the right way, make game play a little better so it doesn't feel cheap and unfinished and of course fixing bugs.

Overall I thought the project was really interesting and enjoyed looking at the road maps they brought out each year and seeing the progress they were gonna make to the game but I cant say that any of the updates have really changed how I feel from when I first played the game. The cheap feel to the game, the lack things to do and the bugs have just ruined it and I sadly cant say I'd recommend any of my mates to buy it, which is disappointing as I had high hopes that 3 years from now, I'd really like the game. Hopefully the developers can keep improving the game as the concept and ideas they have are really interesting and would be nice to see the game actually finished rather than rushing the 1.0 release.\t\t\t\t\t
