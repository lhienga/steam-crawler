Really Fun Game, I Love It!
Its a really fun game love playing it as I have always been a fan of first responders & this game has it, play as a officer or a firefighter or a paramedic & they have mods to make it even funner if you want to play as CHP or LAPD or NYPD you can, they even have better looking skins for the cars like UIPD or UISD & UIFD & UIEMS vary nice looking skins but I do have 3 issues.

Issue 1. Some times calls or just random stuff around the map is buggy & glitchs in MP & it's not me as I have 32 GB DDR 4 ram & Intel i5 10400 & two AMD Redeon RX 580's

Issue 2. I hate when i'm a city cop & get call half way across the map in the middle of no-where I wish we had some setting from the game devs or a mod that would let us set how far we would like to respond to call like no calls farer then a 300 mile radius from the player.

Issue 3. The map I wish they had icons on the map where the gas stations are & you could zoom in on the map & like double tap an area on the map & then would get directions to get there on the mini map even GTA & RDR have this.

But after all the issues I had, I still really enjoy the game mainly MP mode get some people on the pd , fd & some playing as ems & then roleplay is so fun made some memory's there that I wont forget, Give it a try!\t\t\t\t\t
