\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has been in development for 5 years, and the lack of progress is honestly disappointing. And seemingly not taking the RP side of the game seriously when there clearly is an RP community is shocking to say the least. You're letting a part of the community down by only focusing on casual play, making the RP'ers resort to less ideal solutions such as using discord for 50% of gameplay where in my opinion there should already be features in place which let's us do most of those things in the actual game. Instead the focus is releasing DLC with a new vehicle where that time could be spent on other things. Releasing DLC for a game which is still plagued by bugs, server issues (not even any dedicated server support) lack of features which makes the game repetitive and boring while it still lacks accessibility for different types of playerbases. The game has left early access somehow and still feels like an alpha project.

I can just go ahead and let you know right now I'm running it on an RTX 3060, Ryzen 5600x and 32GB of ram, and 10 player servers causes the server to bug out and lag out after only an hours time of gameplay. Yesterday we had to taze a guy 20 times before we ultimately just decided to shoot him. The graphics are not flattering, the map is big but lacks road names proper GPS system instead of looking at the map every 15 seconds to make sure you're driving in the right direction. I mean, there's several parts of the map where the traffic lights don't even work. The AI is all over the place, their driving is horrible, the cars only spawn where the majority of players are, meaning if you have 3 players in beach town and one in the city, the city becomes a ghost town. The AI spawn randomly in the middle of the road to the point where you can watch them drop onto the road from a few meters height. And my point stands, the RP players are being let down. The game is out of early access, but there are beta games that are worth more in terms of gameplay. Earlier I saw an AI running around shooting at the ground. The AI tow truck and transport seem insistent on ramming into other vehicles when arriving on scene sending the other vehicles flying. I mean, there just isn't a game here to justify it leaving early access and that's a fact. Look at any other games in 1.0, they for sure don't lack as much as this game. I could go on and on about the many things this game lacks. My point stands, it feels like an alpha build of a game with potential, and I would have been able to overlook it had the game not been in development for 5 years already, it seems to me that this game is sadly going nowhere..

No customization for character model, no integrated way of playing as a civilian. No proximity chat, no radio filter on the radio channel. In absolutely no state for any serious RP'er to have fun, it's clear to me that the developers value the causal players over roleplayers. If you're looking for a good roleplaying experience unfortunately I'd have to point you in the direction of LSPDFR or FiveM on GTA 5.

The state of the game right now is horrible and I would recommend waiting for another 5 years before considering to buy this game, at the pace it's currently moving at maybe it will look somewhat like a game worth paying money for or even playing.\t\t\t\t\t
