\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI’ve had this game since December of 2020, and I’ve seen a lot of great change in the game. I fully believe that this game has so much potential, since it’s getting more features and things are smoothing out. Sure, it has some bugs, but I brush them off, as they are to be fixed. It does not bother me constantly that they’re there, it’s just part of development. It happens. I focus on playing the game, and not on the bugs constantly.

Updates to the game usually come every 2 months, as the team making this game is small (it could increase at some point, but not sure). But, it gives me something to very patiently wait for when an update comes.

At the time of this review, I have played for 530.8 total hours since December 2020. I did get it on sale for $15, it was the best $15 I’ve spent for a game. I have 0 regrets getting it. It’s that great of a game I cannot stop playing it. It is definitely better if you play in a group, and you play on multiplayer mode. I also have done modding for the game, which makes the vehicles look better to my personal liking.

Performance can use some work, it’s not always 60 fps for me in multiplayer (i7-9700k & 2070 super), but it’s acceptable for what I get.

Sure, it’s in early development, but it’s such a amazing game (to me, by far). If you don’t mind a few bugs, want to get into modding and some photoshop, like to play with other people that really want to play, and are serious about role playing, you will have a great time if you appreciate what it offers.

I highly recommend, 12/10.\t\t\t\t\t
