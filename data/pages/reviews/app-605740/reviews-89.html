\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCant recommend to everyone [hence the "not recommended" vote] but if you can handle bugs and alot of issues, the game has potential. In my opinion, the devs are focusing on the wrong things at the moment.

They want to get content in, thats good. But what good is more content when whats already in the game barely functions, if at all. Here's an example, they added a new vehicle and more call outs but alot of the call outs currently in the game just either dont work or work just enough to complete it, though you're gonna struggle to do so. The one part of that game that does work [mostly] is ALPR [Automatic License Plate Reader] type traffic stops. Meaning your just cruising while checking peoples plates or even doing speed traps works alright. Same with traffic stops, ignoring the fact if you call AI backup [Tow, transport, EMS, ext] they tend to have major issues spawning or arriving.

Another part that seems to work [again, mostly] is Towing. I rather enjoy just getting into the tow truck and going around enforcing parking laws and such. Ignoring the rubber-banding and clipping issues, the towing works rather well. You can mostly control the rear arm on the tow truck, just be carefull when hooking up as if the arm is too low when you attach the car, it'll start to spaz out as the hitbox clips into the ground. Just raise it up though and it'll fix itself.
As stated at the beginning, I can't recommend to everyone but if you like smaller type games and can get over bugs and such, it has potential.

TL;DR - Game has potential if the devs can focus on fixing the game and polishing it rather then adding content.\t\t\t\t\t
