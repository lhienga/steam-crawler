\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun game with a lot of realism.

I usually choose firefighters for my campaigns, and most of the missions I participate in are vehicle fires, building fires, dumpster fires, etc. Between the fact that my tour length isn't too much, I haven't tried any other missions yet.

Some suggestions:
1. Add a recruitment mode to form players into squads and communicate via squad voice, which will help to complete missions efficiently.
2. Increase the hierarchy and create different positions in the unit, thus constituting a logical model of an emergency unit.
3. Add items to the building, the furnishings in the fire station are too simple, perhaps add something interactive.
4. Increase the complexity of the mission by requiring multiple law enforcement units or other emergency services to respond to the same individual mission.
5. Add randomness to the course of the mission to make things better or worse.
6. We need navigation on the map! Just like in GTAV!
7. Increased mission types, map complexity of mission locations, smarter AI (like RON), more relevant injury animations (dead bodies at fire scenes, injuries and realistic injury feedback)
8. More equipment! No matter the department, more equipment never goes amiss.
9. Dispatch Center, set up this department to allow the player (and possibly assign tasks via voice) to dispatch units, so it's more immersive.
10. Looking forward to the next big update! All the best to the dev team!\t\t\t\t\t
