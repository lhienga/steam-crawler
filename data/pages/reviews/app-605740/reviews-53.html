The lack of ai backup to lend you a hand (and when you are able to call in paramedics or tow trucks you don't even see the animations at all, they simply despawn whatever you requested which was just frustrating considering when you play as them, they have animations like stretches and defibrillators) the awful and weightless shooting (zero blood or feedback, the enemies even have healthbars which just kills the immersion) as well as the feeling of a ghost town should make you just go donate to LCPDFR mod for GTA 4. Has incredible potential and not everything about is bad. You can probably tell from my hours played it is addicting, but the price needs to drop until the content is suitable. If this is the current state of the game, I'd hate to of seen what it was like in 2018. I'll pray it gets better and update my review if needed but I'd wait a bit.\t\t\t\t\t
