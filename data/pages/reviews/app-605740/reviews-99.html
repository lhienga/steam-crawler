\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, for someone with no EMS experience this is probably really fun, but for anyone working in the EMS side of things IRL... it needs a lot of work. I get where they have a "set protocol" for how you are to inspect a patient, but if my patient has no pulse, unconscious, not breathing. I'm gonna begin CPR first, not finish a full inspection that I can already see for the most part walking up. At that point its an Emergency Interventions and I need to make sure the patient is breathing before treating and has a pulse back before treat a left arm deformity distal to the elbow. Like before, game is fun for those with extremely limited to no EMS experience. Game wise, there are a lot of bugs. Trying to transport a patient is difficult with the cot not being able to be carried up stairs. Also not being able to contact additional resources until the patient is ready for transport is a very large downside. Like walking into the house, before even going hands on I could have radioed for additional ALS support. As of now, this game needs a LOT of work on the EMS. I highly suggest contacting local or even going to online forums for help with research on how EMS is done. This seems like a great opportunity to make a fun but accurate game to put EMS is a good light and enlighten the public, but as of right now, this needs a lot of TLC to get this close to a simulator.\t\t\t\t\t
