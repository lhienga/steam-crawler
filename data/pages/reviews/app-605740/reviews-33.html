\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun game. It is buggy and the performance isn't the best, but it still runs better for me than Police Simulator (and I have a high-end PC).

It's nice that you can play as police, fire, or EMS, something no other game has accomplished. As of writing this review, the game lacks AI backup, a core singleplayer gameplay mechanic which I feel should have been focused on earlier. I believe, however, that multiplayer has been the dev's main focus (though I do not follow their Discord-it's just what I've noticed from playing for months).

I purchased this game a few years ago and returned it immediately because it was very bare bones and didn't run well. Now, the devs have added many new features that I haven't seen in any other police sims, including GTA's LSPDFR.

Though development may seem slow at times, the devs are responsive in the forums and provide clear road maps of their future plans. I recommend purchasing if you're looking for a first-responder sim that has multiplayer and singleplayer, and is being actively developed and fleshed out.\t\t\t\t\t
