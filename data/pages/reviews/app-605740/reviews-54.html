The graphics are OK at best, The game handles - especially the cars like a free car driving simulator game on your iPhone 5s when we were all 12. The car sounds in this game remind me of a poorly executed Pakistani car simulation game (although previously stated).

The police radio dispatch plays the same 5 second loop sound effect found in most flopped LA based cop TV shows that give us all forks on plate scraping cringe.

During my gameplay, I encountered the Nabi tablet style CAD/MDT system. I wish it would be more realistic by making it only accessible from the squad car.

The Q on the firetrucks sounds like an special needs rubber duck being dragged across asphalt from a 4 wheel drive pickup truck then forced to make an elongated "squeaky noise."

The sheriff liveries are decent. The police peds in this game look cool, But not enough.

for the cherry on top, the world is so densely populated yet so barren at the same time, I see people on YouTube - TheNorthernAlex doing 4 hour streams of this monotonous game doing Tow Truck patrol with his 9 viewers...

The World is so empty - very few places to enter and to add immersion. The only thing I really see all too often are brick buildings skyscraper eye sores with 1920 x 720 resolution, flat window and siding textures on the buildings when I play this game at 1440p.

good luck to all who buy this game, Just get LSPDFR and you'll be more satisfied than this game...\t\t\t\t\t
