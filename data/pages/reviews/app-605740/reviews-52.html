\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a small development team, the game has came a long way.
Whilst I understand working on the visual side of things is important, its a bit sad when the call outs and actual NPC behaviour / combat is so glitchy and limited. Calls are repeated and get boring when you do the same call-outs over and over. Quite frequently call-outs fail to load in properly, and NPC's can be found on-top of buildings or inside non-accessible buildings. The customisation of the cars is my only favourite part of the game, but even then the handling of the car itself is not the best and makes your driving look clunky and not like an actual car driving at all.

Maybe in a year or two I will revisit the game, but honestly for now just use LSPDFR on GTA.

only reason I am recommending this is because it can be quite fun if you play with friends and the customisation aspects.
