\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHey Fellow Steam Users,

I would firstly like to inform you that I RECOMMEND YOU NOT TO PURCHASE this game.

As I have 169.4 hours on this game, the developers are not worried about fixing the known issues with the game. The game developers are worried about pushing out new and broken items, when there are game breaking bugs, that are not being fixed.

Currently, the game has Police, Fire, EMS and Civilian, however, there is no proximity voice chat or text chat. The voice chat is global therefor causing issues regarding differentiating who you are talking to. The game only has 3 male and 3 female ped options, with very limited options when customizing. There is no options to customize Civilian Vehicles (as a civilian_ or Player Ped, you are stuck with a white car or truck and you cannot change your character or vehicle through-out the time as a civilian, you must request a new civilian invite from the server owner, each time.

There is a maximum of 20 vehicles on the online servers, and if the server is open for more than 1 hour, the vehicles stop spawning. The vehicles are very buggy and glitchy when driving or when they are running from you, causing you to hit the vehicle, and fly into the air. When you call for AI tow the tow driver, exits the tow truck, comes up and despawns the vehicle.

There is currently a maximum of 12 pedestrians that can walk around, and you wont see them at all! You may see 1-2 peds walking around, but nothing more than that, after 1-2 hours, there are no peds.

I have been asking the developers to add more light bars and lighting setups (Valor Lightbar, Nforce Lightbars, Visor Light Bars, Customisable Traffic Control Lightbar) and they insist on adding new and buggy items to the game.

Some areas, do not have working traffic lights (no lights on at all), no peds walking around, no vehicles driving around.

We need new uniforms, more lighting options, fixes to the buggy custom light patterns, fixes to AI Vehicles, Peds, more vehicle options for CIVs and Clothing Options. General fixes to AI Vehicle handling.

The game has potential to be something good, but as it stands, the Developers arent interested in fixing the known issues, or adding essential features to the game, they are just adding random things. Currently were waiting on K-9 to get here, when there are so many issues they could be working on and fixing.\t\t\t\t\t
