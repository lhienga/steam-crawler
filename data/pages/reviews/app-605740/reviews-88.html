\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game (Flashing Lights) is very detailed with its calls, map and graphics. The graphics are pretty good and there are many options in the settings for controls, graphics, audio and languages. The map recently got an update, as well as the traffic stop update, I really think this has added a lot of fun to the game. It is priced at a reasonable amount of money for what you get. There are a few bugs that still need to be fixed, like I can't load up a world on my laptop, even though it meets the required standards. So I have to play on a third party app that lets me play games on a cloud. Another bug is the cars just randomly stop when I'm playing, and I can't get them to start going again until I disconnect and reload the world. I hope this is helpful to buyers and developers!
Edit: The new roadmap has made me even more excited about the future of this game!\t\t\t\t\t
