\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had a good time for the first week of playing this game. Development is at a snails pace and the content releases seem like they are getting more underwhelming as they drop. Unfortunately this is the only good standalone sim of its caliber and I can only hope that one day I can come back and enjoy it again.

Update / 2/21/23 \\

Game is fun for an hour or two at a time every once in a while. But there are TONS of bugs and multiplayer isn't so stable as a lot of these reviewers make it out to be. After about an hour running a server the civilians will stop spawning, vehicles will stop spawning and any vehicle left on the map will just sit there. Once this happens, the city is dead. You will still get radio calls and when this happens instances will still spawn, but other than whatever you are doing the town is lifeless. Server needs to be restarted at this point.
This games development is extremely slow. New content that is introduce, when it is introduced, isn't "major" as the devs make it out to be. I'm not going to downplay the amount of work it takes to do certain things, but there have been ENTIRE games developed in the amount of time this team takes to release something as simple and almost useless as releasing a "mod" manager in-game. This manager literally only allows you to download skins, which is possible outside of the game very easily.
This game will not receive the attention it deserves from this development studio because they are busy making other half-ass games to release to squeeze a few dollars out of their unsuspecting audience. When they do this, they will release minor updates every one in a while to keep people engaged, while they work on their next half-title.
This game has a ton of potential, but will never live up to expectations.\t\t\t\t\t
