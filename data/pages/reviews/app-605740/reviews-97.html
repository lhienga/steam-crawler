\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI say no because for what it is at the moment it's not worth the price stamp.

At first it is fun but, I have played quite a lot both single player and Multiplayer with my friend and it gets very boring and repetitive in the end.

We bought this game on release in the hope it would develop quickly into a more immersing, smooth game. It's been a long time now and unfortunately, I don't see any real updates that would make me start playing again. The shooting mechanics are dreadful, your gun is just a laser that u point and click at an NPC standing in the open pointing and shooting in your general direction.

Pursuit mechanics are pretty crap. Only way to stop cars is spike and ramming until compliance reaches 100% (I say ramming when I mean blocking. If you ram your car usually just stops) atm and it's been ages. There is no wheel damage from bullets, no damage to cars from anything and the driving physics and gameplay feels clunky and just rubbish.

Criminals it's just the same boring story. You get there, they run, you chase and then take them to jail. It's just a one trip pony atm do everything once and that's all it's ever going to be, the movement is clunky and horrible.

Game I could compare it to is Roblox, not phantom forces but the old prison escape game.

Forgot to say, my friend and I modded it to make it less boring which it did but once you add mods you have to redo the entire process each update which is a pain in the ar**.

Development is going way too slow, updates coming in when they haven't even finished the basics not to mention feels like it's an update every quarter if you're lucky.
