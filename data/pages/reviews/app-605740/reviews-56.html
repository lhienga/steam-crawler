\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a good game, however, here is what i have gathered since i started playing:

1. The game needs some minor optimization fixes as cars will just insta pop into existence or will be stationary on a roadway until you get sort of close.

2. Call outs need some work, i cant tell you the amount of times ive got to a callout as a LEO only to have the person i was chasing or responding to fall off the map or the callouts not end properly even after i arrest the perp and register them at the PD. (Shoplifting callouts for example)

3. The roadways need some work as well. There are a few roads that have some gap issues in the textures, fine tune this and the driving will be pretty good. Physics already feel decent with driving, but some of the roads need some texturing work for sure.

4. Please add a proper dispatcher and add some life at the PD, Fire Station and Hospitals. Adding some NPC's at these locations will add a bit to immersion, right now it just kinda feels empty.

5. EMS mechanics need some work. I was running as an EMS last night and after i hooked up an EKG and tried to remove the leads, the game glitched out and acted as if i didnt remove the leads, even though i picked up the bag and placed it near a different patient.

6. Singleplayer could do with the ability to call in pursuit backup or just the ability to have AI cops drop spikes or a roadblock. This isnt necessary in multiplayer however, as you could just have your friends set these up.

7. Certain traffic signals do not work within the city and beach town. This is more of a minor issue, but fixing all the signals would be a nice quality of life improvement.

8. Optimize traffic and NPCs so that there can be more people on the roads. This will add more life to the game and also increase immersion.

9. Parked cars on the side of the road need to have proper license plates and also if illegally parked need to be able to call in a tow truck for.

10. Traffic needs improvements on driving. I cant tell you the amount of times ill be driving behind someone on the highway in the 75 MPH zone and i get brake checked going around a corner. Or even just driving straight going through different speed zones and they stop and start abruptly.

11. Have gas stations as a marked location on the map..

These are a few changes i feel can really improve the game and add more immersion. Quality of life will also improve. I can still recommend the game as it is a great project that has a lot of potential. It definitely isnt without its issues and could use a lot of work. But, if there is ever a sale of the game, or even if they adjust a few of my issues, i could easily recommend the game.

Edit: Please fix multiplayer. Whenever i am playing with my friend and he is hosting, even on US East servers, whenever i enter a police chase, the game has a stroke and where the car i am chasing is visually, server side he is actually way ahead of that position. Multiplayer could really use some tweaks on the servers for sure.
