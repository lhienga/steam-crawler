The car physics - if a player/npc is able to push a vehicle by themselves then it breaks the realism and the role play situations players are trying to maintain
Path-finding - calling in ai tow trucks/ems will result in two different scenarios
1) its works just fine
or
2) the ai will get stuck and endlessly ram into a wall or block traffic

Possible additions to the game
1) the ability to vote to change time/ amount of traffic/ or kick/ban people from a session
2) k9 units(by tracking npc/civs down or slowing them down for officers to arrest them)
3)air units(helicopters in my opinion will fit perfectly with the large map by assisting players to locate suspects)
4)adding a element of centralize crime like gangs/drug dens(ai or players selling drugs to ai that can change the level of aggressiveness)
5)a dedicated casual and hardcore rp servers(so new players feel more welcomed to multiplayer)
6) adding weapons to civs instead of just having pistols
7) they should add pd fd and ems trucks to the game

However, one of my main concern with the game is its starting to lock vehicle behind dlc, even though its cheap at only 2 to 4$, its still a really bad idea because the main map of the game isn't even finished, and its not a skin that doesnt offer any actual benefits, but its gives a different max speed and offers a larger or smaller hitbox. The concern with this is that this game is starting to turn into a pay-to-win type game\t\t\t\t\t
