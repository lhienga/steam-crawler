very pretty game, nice city, good game pacing, if you like racing games you can just play for hours without worrying about progress. Customization is awesome, paints and decals are all very beautiful and thoroughly modifiable. but has its problems... like if you just want a solid pattern wrap you have to put it on 5 different surfaces. That is, select the surface, select the pattern, size the pattern, position the pattern, colour the pattern, tint/chrome/fade the pattern, repeatx4. Its a full time job but there is a slider memory that helps make the process 'slightly' less inefficent.
Tons of really cool well known cars and the overall driving style of the game is fun and very customizable.

Overall this game kinda sucks tho compared to old games.
Its a boring ass racing game.

Bonuses in this game suck. You have to choose only ONE way to receive NOS bonuses. Its like the game makers said "how can we make a game with less challenges, less technique and skill required to dominate, less inscentive to have fun and take risks, and also less reward for your gameplay and skill"

For some reason there are NO sprint races available at day for me anymore. Theres a lack of content compared to what I remember in NFSU2. Police AI is a bit wild, very over powered head to head but it's balanced with easy exploits like: driving into a river will make the cops chase you into the water and they die, or you can drive off a series of cliffs and they will follow you to their deaths.
I don't know if its because I play on hard but there are SO MANY CARS on the road in this game... and one ambulance for every 5 citizens.
and the AI of the cars is so very very annoying.. they just stop in the middle of the street , most of them stop as soon as they pop into vision

I dont know if tuning exists in this game, maybe it is introduced in the late game? but... I'd really like to be able to change my gear ratios and suspension and stuff... they made a system where you need to create different cars for each driving style (rally/track race/drift).
You have to configure downforce and TCS - WHILE DRIVING
