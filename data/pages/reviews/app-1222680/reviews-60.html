\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can recommend the game on a very small basis, but it's still good nonetheless.
First, the horrid aspects:
Cops are absurd, you will hear that many times over in these reviews. They can keep up with you going nearly 300 MPH. IDC what you say no matter what happens I will not believe that a crown vic can go 300 mph in the span of 10 seconds.
The price. I got it on sale mind you, but that's honestly the only time you should get it, other than that it's a ♥♥♥♥♥ scam.
The story. It was actually so good.... for about 20 minutes. 20 minutes because that's about how long the story line is, there is only 4 chapters and if you grind you can complete the entire story in under 3 hours, it's idiotic as every NFS before this had an amazing story line with length and GIRTH to it.
The damage dealing/taking. It is damn near impossible to hit a cop without taking 20% of your cars health and maybe like a .1% of the cops health. There is a VERY small margin that you can hit without taking damage and unless you are 100% dedicated to this game, killing a cop is a fleeting dream here.

Now for the good.
The graphics. I love the moody art style to the game, even when it's bright out in the game it still has this darker aspect to it meaning that pictures with the sun in the background make you feel phenomenal. Also the rain physics on the cars, on the ground they are pretty ♥♥♥♥. But if you're taking a picture and zoom in you can see that the rain is dripping down the car and it's actually pretty cool. I don't know any other games that do that.
The car selection. The car selection is actually not half bad, nothing like Gran Turismo, Forza or even The Crew but it does have a very good selection of cars for what it is, it has a selection of older Volvo's and the likes. Some nice Porsche's and Ferrari's. All kinds of goodies
The tuning/modding. The amount of ♥♥♥♥ you're able to do to some cars actually blows my mind, in my opinion they have Forza Horizon beyond beat with customization. You can tune even the exhaust sound (An old Volvo's stock engine can sound like its a 2.4L I4 with a bit of rasp). Now there are some cars that have ♥♥♥♥ selection, like the 458 Italia, highly popular Ferrari so you expect good mods, not really. But it's still fun to spend a while modding a car. I wish the car show community on this game were bigger tho.

All in all I would give this game a solid 6.5/10, only get it on sale and youll get your moneys worth.\t\t\t\t\t
