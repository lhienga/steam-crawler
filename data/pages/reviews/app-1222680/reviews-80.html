\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was okay. I was actually enjoying the story at one point when the cop took her car. But it took me an hour or something to get to get to level 7 from 2, just to progress the story. I can see why gaining rep makes sense, but did I really have to race so much?


The Good
The graphics are really good and the visual effects when driving are a sight to see.

The story was okay at the part that I stopped playing. I made it to 7 to continue the story and then I thought about how I didn't want to potentially grind out more levels just to progress the story a little more. So then I stopped.

Voice acting was actually alright.

It was fun to customize my character and car with visual effects.

I like that you make small upgrades to your car by buying parts. Parts require certain levels to buy.


The Bad
The game requires you to be online and connected to the internet to play. I was downloading another game and tried to play nfs heat at the same time, but nfs heat would crash because the connection wasn't stable enough, because steam was using all of the networks speed to download the other game.

The game uses high cpu.

The game feels too grindy for me.

Cops were annoying. just trying to progress the story and was forced to spend so much time trying to get away.

I don't like the black faded tint around the edge of the screen. It's always there.

Can't turn off vibration.


Conclusion
The game was okay from what I've played but the pacing and flow stopped me from wanting to play more.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(ENG) General outline.

There are 4 storylines in the game, for completing one, you get a car, for completing all, you get a car! So I don't see any point in ignoring them, hurry up and go through)

During the day for all the activities and victories in the races, you get money, at night you earn face, thereby at the successful end of the night, increase the level, which in turn opens up new cars for sale at the dealer, technical improvements and tuning. (For the animation of the transition from day to night, a special respect)
FACT: You can change day to night, but not vice versa. The night ends when you get home.

Activities.

From the activities, the game offers us long jumps, track drift, billboard knockdowns and speed traps. You get a car for completing all the activities in your group, you get a car for completing all the activities! So... don't rush, because you will need a car with 220+ level and nitro to complete. But this is not a problem if you have purchased the Deluxe version of the game and have reached the 18th level of your account, because you will have a packed Corvette waiting for you. Activities that have been completed with 3 stars are considered completed. Collecting items will be easier if you have purchased a map key that shows the location of each flamingo and a can of paint, otherwise, welcome to the guides on Steam or on the Internet)

Now let's move on to tuning.

There are 3 mechanisms, each of which has parts that can be upgraded: the engine (which you can swap onto another), suspension and transmission, which are all upgradeable. ULTIMATE class upgrades can be earned for prize money from Heat 3 and Heat 5 night races, which are unlocked at the 50th level of your account and after reaching the 3rd and 5th wanted level overnight, respectively.
I chose 3 types of tuning for myself:
- Fast and Furious - for long jumps, speed traps, and races (all TRACK parts)
- Ken Block (R.I.P Legend) - drift (respectively)
- Colin McRae - rally car (rally parts, because an SUV for racing is trash if you have a Subaru Impreza).
You can also change the behavior of the car on the road through the "On-the-fly tuning" menu - a feature that allows you to edit the steering sensitivity, clamping force, ABS and classic drift brakes, and in general, the great thing is that you can change the characteristics of the car on the go through the TAB menu and in a matter of seconds turn a drift car into a time attack rocket.

Style.

As before, you can play with stickers and paints in the classic way, but if you don't feel like it, use the community designs, I'm sure you'll find something for yourself from the hundreds and thousands of options.

I got a special pleasure from the car customization (which is not available on cars with a star on a purple background), and the 1x15 effect of nitro and 1200+ horses that accelerated my Pagani Huayara BC`17 (6.2 V8) to tears reminded me of carefree times on Play Station 2 with parts of this cult series: Underground, Most Wanted, Undercover, and Carbon.

I'm grateful to Ghost Studio for the project, and to you for reading my review.
