Its much better then Both payback and unbound. It obviously shares some similarities being made by the same studio but i found that its way better handled in Heat.

The Day and night cycles are a really nice feature and i think they balance the game quite nicely. Game can get repetitive but if you manage switching from night to day time well, then you can erase some of that repetitiveness

Bye bye the horrendous parts system in payback

Visually very nice , especially during night time with the neon aesthetic

The Bad :

It is very easy to get a way too good car , way too fast by grinding a bit. I got to a point where i had a car of 340 tier going against 220. I wish there was either scaling difficulty or at least a way to choose. Easy = lower rating , hard = equals higher rating then your current one.

Ea launcher existing, sometimes i click start on steam, sometimes it starts, sometimes it dosent. Sometimes i click trough ea launcher , sometimes it launches , sometimes it dosent. Ehhh its not really a complaint to the game itself i know but its still something to be noted.

Physics can be questionable at times, much like 2015 and payback


Overall, considering how ♥♥♥♥ Forza horizon 5 is, this is a very nice alternative\t\t\t\t\t
