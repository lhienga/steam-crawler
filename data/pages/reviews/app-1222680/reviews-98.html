\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNFSU vibes, paired with a touch of The Crew. Not quite as customizable or as grand as either of them. Open world, day/night cycle, visual and performance tuning, drifting, police chases and neon colors. At day you farm money in legal races, at night you farm heat in illegal races - both are necessary to progress. In between you can farm collectibles (pelicans, graffitis, stunts) in the open world, which honestly is more annyoing than it adds anything. Has a cringe-ass story as always, in a city full of power hungry cops where driving races is a way to rebel against the system or whatever.

It lets you drive a BMW M3 Evolution II from 1988, an oldschool Volkswagen Golf GTI, a Chevrolet Camaro SS 1967. In other words, the car selection is an absolute banger.

You have to tune your cars in specific ways for specific types of races (drift, race, street, offroad). Younger me would have loved it, but the tuning system seems like unnecessary complexity. I'd prefer to just have a premade loadout "for the job", rather than just selecting all the BiS pieces for the job manually - the choice here is an illusion.

Solid racing game for playing with a controller, sadly has no cockpit view. Then again it's a mainline NFS game. You can play it with friends. 8/10 Can't go wrong when it's on sale for 3,49€. I'd say anything up to 15€ is a good deal.\t\t\t\t\t
