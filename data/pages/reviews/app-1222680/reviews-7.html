\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs an arcade sim racer, i would give it a 7.5 out of 10. The biggest con that comes from this game is simply that it leaves a bit to be desired. The story, main campaign is a bit short along with being bland (i think we can all agree that NFS isn't popular because of its story). There are a few issues, such as the fact that you cannot access a full inventory of sorts. If you have more than one engine for a car you just sold and if said engine isn't compatible with any of the other cars you have but you would like to sell it, you would need to re-buy the car you just sold or buy another car that is compatible to have access. Also (at least as far as i am aware) there is a level cap at 50, personally I would've liked it if we could have unlimited levels. At the very least for people to show off.

On the other hand, racing in this game is quite fun, i have mixed feelings about drifting because it isn't the most easiest to learn, and putting a drift car together'll make you grow a few grey hairs but once you've gotten the hold it is quite rewarding. Rally is also enjoyable and customizing vehicles could be a small standalone game by itself.

Unfortunately, there isn't much diversity in what you see in online competitions, the Porsche 911 Carrera is one of if not the best racing car you can get, which while somewhat nice for causal gameplay, completing the story, ect. It makes racing online somewhat stale for if you're going up against one you'll likely lose, leaving you to either use one of the most expensive cars in the game to be about the same or use the same car.

I recommend only getting this game if it's on a heavy discount (as did i)\t\t\t\t\t
