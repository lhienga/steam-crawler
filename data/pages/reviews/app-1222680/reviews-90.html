\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a fan of older NFS games, this one doesn't hit my expectations.

Graphics are somehow dated compared to older games in the series.
The UI is designed for a console so it feels awful on PC.
"Hard" difficulty is easy and I am saying this as someone who isn't good at racing games.
The handling of cars feels pretty bad, you are going to be drifting all the time.
The customization of cars isn't great, e.g, buy spoiler 1-6 and you can't do any adjustments on it.
Story didn't feel very gripping... Probably suffering from all that drifting.
Music in the game wasn't very memorable.

Overall, it felt like an NFS spinoff rather than an actual NFS game. Even with the 95% discount, it's hard to recommend this. Maybe NFS Unbound is where it's at when it's on one of those huge discounts. Unfortunate, as I went into this game hoping to like it.
