\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI simply love this game. The gameplay and mechanics are gold, the vanilla handling is pretty decent (though I would suggest playing with Unite 3.0 and optionally Simon & Rattler's Handling Overhaul), ditching the 24h cycle for a seperated day and night and how the game plays differently was very sick to see.
The soundtrack is one of my favourites OSTs as of now ever. I love the spanish-esque/festive music during the day (especially for a map based on Miami/Florida) which fits really well with the day-side of things like the Showdown and feeling of the map in general. I love the genre and tone change when playing at night, I love to see the return of Drum&Bass compared to Payback, and of course banger hip-hop tracks with various artists that I now listen to thanks to Heat.
The story is a bit bland but whatevs, it's a bit too over the top.
And nothing to say about the customization, it's insane.
