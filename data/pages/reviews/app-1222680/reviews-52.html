\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a fun game to play casually, but the cop AI is so simple and dumb.
They don't react to breaking the law; you can smash into other cars in front of them, speed, ignore red lights, anything--they only really pursue you if you already have a heat level, and it's quite easy to get it back to 0. Once you get 5-7+ cops chasing you, it starts to get a bit more fun, but even with the helicopter at heat level 5, it's stupid easy. With lower heat levels and fewer cops chasing you, they lose you so easily that it's like they aren't serious about catching you. You basically need to play at higher heat levels in order to get any stimulating chases. It makes no sense that I can crash 2 cop cars and be at heat level 0 so fast, let alone that they don't seem to care about anything as long as you aren't hitting them. The speed they go is fine, I just wish the cops in this game didn't have the attention span of a 4 year old.

I originally gave the game a positive review, but I had to change it because the game performs so poorly on my laptop. I have double the amount of minimum RAM required, the game at minimum requires a GTX 760 and my laptop uses a GTX 1050M dedicated graphics card and a 7th gen i5 Intel processor, I run it on an SSD, I've tried hardware acceleration on and off, I've tried running it at low settings, it just performs terribly no matter what. This laptop should be able to run it. Upon further research, the game is poorly optimized and the CPU takes a hit from Denuvo. Denuvo is such crap and developers need to stop including it in their games. It's a good thing I got this game for almost nothing because it's unplayable on my laptop, only on my desktop. If it weren't for the fact that I do have at least *a* system it runs on, I'd have returned it instantly.

Optimize your game better. And it's 2024, stop putting Denuvo on new games unless you at least plan to release an update without Denuvo later. This crap is tiring and hurts gamers more than it hurts your company. Piracy doesn't hurt your sales as much as Denuvo hurts your paying customers; many people pirate games before buying them or wouldn't have bought them regardless. Please, please, please, fix your game.\t\t\t\t\t
