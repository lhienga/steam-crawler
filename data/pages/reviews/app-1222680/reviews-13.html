\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this on sale for 3.99. Which is the price this game should be. Odd way to start off a recommendation but it's the truth. Do not spend 70 dollars on this game or you will feel like you've been completely ripped off in my opinion by a game that should probably be free to play. I like this game and i want to like this game but it stinks of EA. If you can get this game for cheap, go ahead if you are looking for a *decent* racing game but be aware this will feel nothing like street racing and lacks a lot of edge that you'd find in actual street racing when it comes to look, design, feel, and character of the people around you. Everyone is the most plain jane random NPC you could imagine that probably goes to church on sunday and bakes cookies for grandma every week, including your character(no matter how much you customize them) and nothing at all what you'd imagine someone who street racing for a living would look like. Sure, don't judge a book by it's cover but if the cover of this book had a color it would be beige. Which is strange as the design and color of the world is actually pretty great. Props to the designer for that(but not EA)

I also find that the music in this game is straight garbage with no option to listen to different genres of music. I am serious when i say the soundtrack is one of THE most bubble gum rap avantcore experiences you could come up with. The music design for this game is a -5/10. Please, do yourself a favor and go straight to the settings and turn the music to 0, go to youtube, spotify, apple music(whatever you want to choose) and play your own phonk/hip hop/EDM music as the music in this game will subtract from the experience. If you take nothing else from this review, do that. I did not think that music could take away from the adrenaline fueled experience of driving at 150MPH but this game has proven me wrong on that account. I would rather have no music than listen to the soundtrack to this game.

Additionally, i want to give props that the cars are really customizable as they should be and there is always a goal to upgrade your car with new parts, a new engine, wraps/paintjob, and decals as well as a large list of cars to choose from. It's great and you can lose time just with that which is fantastic for the game.

Overall, i give it a 6.5/10. Oh and the story? What story? Who cares? Are you here to race or read a romance novel involving a nameless NPC simping after a cuban girl with a racecar you manage to beat by the end of chapter 1?\t\t\t\t\t
