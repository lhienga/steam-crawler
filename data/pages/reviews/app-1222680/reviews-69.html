Need for Speed™: CRINGEFEST

So, I kept hearing this was the NFS' "Listening to the feedback" title, got excited and thought I'd give it a go.

You have a decent rooster of cars, you get excited, only to discover that they all handle like boats.
To make matters worse, that game expects you to drift around all corners, can't drift? Well, tough luck buddy bro!

The game is practically a platform for product placement for fast fashion brands.
Then there's the story, which has the most cringe worthy writing I've ever seen in a videogame in a very long time.
Bland characters, terrible voice acting, and to top it all, the most cartoonish antagonists ever.
Take a look:
https://youtu.be/qpKcapbD7_c?si=vWsjQCaojFDrs-EZ&t=70

Like, I get it, police department is corrupt, but this has to be the funniest rendition of police in NFS' history. Well, at least it made me laugh.

At least Cross from '05 NFS MW had some character.

What else? Oh yeah, the soundtrack, they should fire whoever picked up the tracklist for the OST, it has like less than a handful of good tracks; at least the game introduced me to Bomba Estereo, so there might be some hope.

Pros:
  • The pursuits can be challenging, prepare for some action.
  • Lots of customization options.

TL;DR: Epic Cringefest, bad car handling and cartoonish characters. Oh and EA wants 70 USD for this when not on sale. Yeah good luck with that.
