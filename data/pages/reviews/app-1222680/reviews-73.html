\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnly recommending it because of the steam sale (Got it just for +3Eur).
Handling is abysmal with the whole approach of the game to drift every corner, together with car upgrade/ money grinding progression that is reminiscing of online only games. Car selection is jokingly small too with some cars having 4 variations just to bloat the total count.
Mostly it feels like Heat was created to copycat Forza Horizon with its day events and collectibles (4th or 5th with the latino theme and horrifying 5 similar-ish songs on repeat that feel like torture).
Main story is barely a point to recent NFS games, but this one actually starts at act 2 and quickly ends on act 4 which is quite disappointing.
Optimization is horrible as well, lots of artifacts, pop-in and you have to run the game at +150% resolution for it to not look pixelated on a 1440p screen.

Biggest W is the night racing for reputation as it gets the Vibe of older NFS games, there are some better songs that only play at night, but cops teleporting in your face is a big disappointment.

If you want some filler racing then get this game on the cheap.\t\t\t\t\t
