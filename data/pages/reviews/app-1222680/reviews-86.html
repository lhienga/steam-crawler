\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOVERALL TLDR: got the deluxe edition for less that 4 bucks and i regret my purchase. The crew 1 is better. NFS underground, carbon and most wanted are better. If it was free and i knew what i was in for i would still not get it probably.
Bad game experience all round. Starting off:
-Music is absolutely irrelevant. It sounds more like background noise. Played 3 races with it on and about half an hour on free-roam and i simply turned it off, never to be turned on again.
-Racing comes down to "powersliding" properly. After you get that one single driving mechanic the game has to offer, everything becomes streamlined. I finished the main game story having bought only 1 car. Being over-leveled or under-leveled doesnt really matter. You get overtaken on the straights but take back your position after a turn. Played the game on hard difficulty and I lost about 6-7 races on my first try.
TLDR: Too easy and straightforward.
-Story is nothing. Absolutely nothing, generic character has a problem, i go solve it with my magic racing powers that make real world problems go away somehow. Thats it. I do not care for the characters, i do not like them talking to me, i would not help them if i could. Theres this thing called the shadow/legend/masters league or whatever that I do not get to see/race or interact in any other way. Remember when you drove enemy racing gangs out of their territory in NFS Carbor by racing every single crew member they had, in their territory and then gaining the right to fight the boss? Yeah...No. Just playing the game makes you good enough. Or perhaps buying the game makes you a better racer.
-Multiplayer: Hahahahahahahaaha no. Thought i would take a shot at it after finishing the main story. But i finished it with a lotus exige and then had no incentive to race a bunch more to but buy my favorite flex car like a lambo. So im guessing it would go like this: I join online with lotus, i play with same level drivers (around 300 magic points or whatever) i lose, i buy better car, i modify it, i lose cause its ranked differently then i go online, check best car, grind to buy it, max it out and ONLY THEN can i try to get better and actually feel like winning or losing comes down to my driving skill. Dont know dont care.
TLDR: If i wanted to play online i would play a game that does it better.
-Graphics: Freaking nice, things are pretty (even though they went a bit mad with the particle effects) cars are nice, world is utopic florida im guessing.
-Cops are a joke, they do massive amount of damage by touching me in the wrong place and i do no damage, so i HAVE to run away. To do so i just have to find a jump. Nothing else works for heat lvl 4-5. They talk about spikes, rhinos etc but they are nothing. You dodge them easily all the time. The cop countering upgrades were a nice idea but the cop chase execution doesnt require you to buy and/or use any of that.
-Off road, drifting etc are not important so i dont feel the need to get a new car, mod it and beat races with it. Its not important to the main story so i do not need to do it, that means i wont. Besides if you go out and beat a drifting race lets say, you then free roam with that car .... Which mean you cant go fast, you cant run away from cops or enjoy free roam in general, you have to fast travel to garage (if it day out), go in the garage (so go through a second loading screen), change car and then go out. Otherwise good luck driving your car to the next race and change car there. Have fun i guess.
-The game doesnt teach you how to play. Did you find out on your own that if you double tap right arrow button on controller you go to a special menu that has some extra driving setting that are not available in the garage? So tuning a car for something has to be done in two parts: go upgrade in garage, go free roam and touch extra thingies. You can can visualy modify cars in like a million stupid ways. Want tire smoke colors? have em. Want to change mirrors? change em. But want to edit body parts/kits like bumpers and stuff? No thats out. Theres also a bunch of simple same-y stickers to put on the car but they dont really do it for me.
Also do you want to sell your cars? YEAAH GOOD LUCK WITH THAT. I aint telling you how to do it so go search for it like the rest of us. Im sure the devs wanted for it to be a secret so there you go. Having more than 8 cars in your garage, means you have to do some hefty swaps in order to find the car you want to sell in the proper list that allows you to sell cars. Had a deluxe edition or whatever which meant i got some pre-beefed up cars that filled my garage. Bought an BMW X5 to try of roading with it. It was crap. Wanted to sell it. Had the starting car and the exige in garage also. Pick the X5, go to the correct car list for selling, it isnt there. Pick the starting car, it isnt there. Pick the exige and there it is. Mind you, the X5 was there not the exige. So selling multiple cars means that i have to go back out of the list, change active car, hope the proper cars made it onto the selling list, go to selling list, sell and repeat if i want to sell another one. Im sure theres a reason to go about it that way, but AT LEAST MAKE IT KNOWN TO THE PLAYER.
