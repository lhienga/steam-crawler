\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA step up from Need for Speed payback.

Pros:
You can choose to play in the environment you want. Switch to daytime to earn money, go to the night time to earn rep.
Upgrading mechanics for the cars is wonderful, you will get a chance to swap engines for the cars.
Cops are competitive, not completely ruthless. (Unless you decide to mess with cops with low ranked rides)
Decently paced story line
Good amount of cars
Night time graphics are a plus

Cons:
Daytime graphics are meh. Not too good, not too bad, could've been better
The physics engine used for this game is prone to instability, resulting in unexpected behaviours of cars at top speeds.
McLaren F1 LM is locked behind a paywall, i.e. you need to pay 409rs to get it as a DLC, shame it is not included in the deluxe edition. (but to be fair, even without it its worth the discounted price that i bought it for)
The biggest letdown is the Koenigsegg regera tops only 394 km/h and doesnt touch anywhere near 400km/h

Worth my time and money.\t\t\t\t\t
