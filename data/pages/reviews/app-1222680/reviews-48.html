Introduction
Need for Speed Heat is the 24th installment in the Need for Speed franchise.
During the day, you compete in the Speedhunters Showdown, a sanctioned racing event held on the streets and surrounding area of Palm City, while trying to raise your reputation and fend off from the cops during night time.

Pros
  • Good graphics.
  • Good handling, albeit a bit too twitchy.
  • Good car variety.
  • Good car sounds.
  • Good customization.
  • Pretty good map.
  • Agressive cops during night time.
  • Risk/Reward system taken from Rivals.
  • Cross-platform multiplayer.
  • No microtransactions, unlike Payback.

Cons
  • EA launcher.
  • Denuvo DRM.
  • Poor optimization.
  • Horrid soundtrack.
  • Drifting sucks. There's no countersteering.
  • Map doesn't really match the physics. Some corners will send you flying, others will make you lose control.
  • Story is short and not that good.
  • Porsche 911 RSR is way too overpowered.
  • Collectibles.

Conclusion
Need for Speed Heat is a step forward from the sh*tshow that Payback was and although it's not as good as some of the older NFS games, it's still a fun and enjoyable game.\t\t\t\t\t
