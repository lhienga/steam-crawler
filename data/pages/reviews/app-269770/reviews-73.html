Quirky
Funny
Great combat
Difficult bosses
Good devs
So much content
Genuinely ridiculously enjoyable, particularly if you have ever liked collecting things, from leap of faiths to 7 red starred golden balls, you'll probably enjoy collecting in this game too, as long as you arent too anal about it, that is. (I am, but im a bit of a masochist too apparently)

Cons:
Ridiculously slow development. Doesnt take away from the fun, but if you are thinking its close because its v0.8, you would wrong. If you dont have the patience, keep walking. Its probably still got 2-3 years at this rate.
Incredibly imbalanced. Some magic is broken, some is useless. Using a melee weapon is often more hit and run than magic is, which feels bad to play. However balancing comes after completion of the game, so give em time.
Sometimes the collecting gets a little bit too tedious due to the combination of a lack of a group of a certain enemy, and low drop rates. Not the end of the world though, they are totally optional at this point\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is truly one of the best gameplay/graphics/story combo i have ever played.
Gameplay:You are not bound to specific typical playstyle like healer or tank because of amount of skills, weapons and abilities this game lets you choose from. There is also an arcade mode which acts like a standalone rogue-like game. There is also activities like fishing, house building and side quests you can take while progressing on the main story.
Graphics:I always wonder, how people manage to do such amazing pixel arts and games, because you need to manually draw every location, animations and sprites,by "drawing" i mean adding each pixel individually (except terrain, i guess) which is, i'm sure, very time consuming.
Story:It's not that special at the beginning - You're just someone's son with his friends who seek adventures, but the whole world with teleports, cyborgs, time travelling, fairies, some strange season temple and giant cute slimes tells me that this game is obviously isn't boring to say the least.
This game is oriented for co-op gameplay (2-4 group),but you can play it solo if you want.
Overall this is a great game, very interesting, long main questline, but isn't complete yet. Still being updated by devs, so final of it can be close, referring to recent desert update,but i don't know for sure.
