*ORIGINAL NEGATIVE REVIEW*

Ahh yes. Secrets of Grindea. A homage and love letter to both grind focused games (hence the GRINDea part) and the old JRPG favorites like Secrets of Evermore and Secrets of Mana.

This game suffers from what I call "Scope overload" there is just so, so, so much to this game. On top of the typical JRPG stuff, there is farming mechanics (like Harvest Moon or Stardew Valley) there is Zelda-esque dungeons, there is fishing, there are mini games, there is multiplayer, there is monster capturing, there is skills and trees to unlock, there is a Colosseum, there is a wave/horde mode! Jeezum word no wonder this game is taking forever.....

I would recommend this game but for now I'd really wait until it's full release or at least until it's
on a good sale.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit: Lots of the negative reviews are just complaining about development time and it "being abandoned" it is definitely NOT abandoned, there are weekly dev blog posts on the game's website. Would be nice if they were here too, so less people complained in negative reviews, but. Development time is a fair enough criticism, although I believe it is only three people working on it in their spare time and the game started out as only a school project... so...

The game is fantastic. It plays really well, and is very satisfying for those who like a fruitful grind with lots of visible rewards and upgrades. Game is also perfect for completionists, and secret hunters/explorers. There are many places to go, things to do, quests to complete, collections to fill out, equipment pieces to upgrade, etc. I haven't played in years at the time of posting this review, but I've been patiently and happily waiting for the small dev team to finish polishing this Diamond. As of currently, they're on version 0.949a and only have a few minor things left before fully launching the game to v1.0 and I'm on the edge of my seat waiting for the day I jump back into this masterpiece. Even still, there is more than enough content to keep you busy for a few dozen hours and there has been for years.\t\t\t\t\t
