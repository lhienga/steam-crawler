Debated whether I should change my review now that the game is actually complete. I will probably never buy anything from these devs in the future, based on how this project went. However, I know that when I look at reviews I never give a ♥♥♥♥ about negative reviews that talk about the devs or the development process. All that matters to most potential buyers is how good the game is.

With all that in mind, I'm giving a positive review. The game is a charming little action rpg with fun characters and pretty clever writing. There's some interesting character building elements. Co-op is super fun because you get to see different builds interact. For the price, it's an indie gem.


Original Review

The game has been in early access for 6 years. The game likely won't be at 1.0 for another 2-3. I've refrained from making a negative review for so long because the base gameplay and current story is very good. It's a well written, beautiful arpg with pretty good combat.

However, the devs made the decision to include a separate-roguelike "Arcade Mode" in the game partway through EA. This mode is not fun. I've tried it. It's a mode full of the weakest part of the game. It was fun for about an hour. From reviewing their EA blog posts working on this Arcade Mode has added at least 2-3 years to development time. Every time a new story/dungeon section is added to the game, they then work on the Arcade Mode for 6 months to add the new enemies & items to it.

If you're going to buy the game, wait for the full release.\t\t\t\t\t
