I believe it's well made, and the devs (when they actually do put out game content that isn't just grindy slop a la arcade mode) are very talented. The main story is well written and the world is beautiful and well mapped, but it's so genuinely infuriating that from the time I first played this game (in late 2015) to now they've put out approx. 2 new main story progressions. It's crazy because half the game was essentially already completed and released in 2015 and I've been waiting over 8 years just for them to finish the game. It's so sad and disappointing because I genuinely believe if the dev team had actually worked on the main game instead of the random filler and side content, and they were able to complete it in even ~5-6 years that it would have the same acclaim as other small studio classics, but instead it's just an example of why people shouldn't trust games in early access.
