\r\n\t\t\t\t\t13 years...
I mean, the art is gorgeous (with the caveat of the faces looking strange) but that is about it.

Played with a Xbox Gamepad.

I made it to the pumpkin area, where it became apparent what the future of the playthrough would hold. Enemies, constantly respawning, often right on top of me, with their first action being an attack, charging enemies, invulnerable in their charge, homing in on my position to deal half my health in damage.
My own charging attacks absolutely useless because standing still or slowly strafing is not an option if you want to survive.
Lining up of range attacks is problematic because of perspective, visual clarity and hectic movement of enemies.
Movement feeling awkward, too fast (maybe only in the diagonal?).
Often, because of bad visibility and oversensitive inputs, standing slightly wrong for a block.
Visibility behind objects is zero, visibility with a couple of minions, pets and enemies is just bad.
The skill system woefully undercooked in general, boring 1% bonuses everywhere, the active skills range from gimmicky to useless with few exceptions.
Repetitive music loops of maybe 45 seconds per area.
Cutesy Anime writing that has nothing to say at all.
The menus a chore to use, consistently needing to many inputs.
Fishing, as boring as can be, of course.

In conclusion: A janky collectathon, stale and repetitive, with a frankly terrible combat system.

Should not have bought it...oh well.

This game is obviously a gigantic labor of love for the devs, and it shows in every single aspect of the game. I don’t think I’ve ever played another ARPG with the level of polish and charm Secrets of Grindea has to offer.

Do you like chrono trigger? (Obligatory yes from everyone, obviously). Buy this game.
How about Secret of Mana? Or any other SNES era RPG? Yeah? Buy this game. You absolutely won’t regret it.

It looks cutesy, because it is. However, don’t let that fool you. The challenge ramps up VERY fast but in a good way that keeps the game from getting stale. Boss battles aplenty so far, and I’ve only scratched the surface.

Oh, and you can customize every single aspect of your character’s style ranging from attacks all the way to haircuts. can pick from tons of level upgrades, spells, buffs etc. No class restrictions unless you choose to build it that way.

This game is fantastic, do yourself a favor and don’t pass this one up!!

Oh, and you can whack the chickens to your heart’s content! :D\t\t\t\t\t
