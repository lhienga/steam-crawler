\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs many a reviewer has said, I wish there was a neutral option, as the game isn't bad, it's just not what it appears to be.

The cartoony graphics and action RPG like gameplay suggests that this would be a causal experience but it seems that's not the case, at last in solo play. Even the inaccurate aiming and controls overall suggest this isn't a game that requires a whole lot of skill. However the game is very hard, especially during boss battles. You take huge amounts of damage and the only way to heal is by picking up health globes dropped by fallen enemies. The issue with this system is they only seem to drop when you're at low health and during boss battles, they don't drop at all. This mean you cannot heal during an entire boss battle. It took me 6 tries just to take down the second boss.

If this were a hardcore souls-like game, that'd be fine but that's not the case here. The controls don't allow for fine aim or perfect movement, causing you to often take damage or miss attacks, something you can't afford to do when a boss will kill you in only 5 or 6 hits. If you're into very challenging games, then this may be the game for you. However if you're like me and you saw the silly graphics and causal looking gameplay and thought it'd be an easy game to sit back and mess around with, you'll be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
