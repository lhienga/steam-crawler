-Story is great.
-Many Items and things to collect

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNormally I wait to buy pretty much any game only when it's on sale but after playing the demo for this, I really didn't want to wait and it has been worth every cent. This game is absolutely everything I love and have been looking for lately!

[PROS]
+ Charming pixel graphics - really lovely NPC animations that are smooth and really show their personality. A really lovely and immersive atmosphere with different zones that have an entirely different mood and feel to every single one! My favourite so far being Pumpkin Forest.
+ Interesting and well done enemies/bosses in both graphics, animations, effects and gameplay.
+ Great music that doesn't feel annoying or repetitive!
+ Simple gameplay, the tutorial is integrated really well in that it doesn't feel like a chore but you also don't feel like the basics are a mystery. You have everything you need to know at your fingertips, the gameplay is straight forward and fun
+ I really can't stress enough how much the audio and visuals are an absolute 10/10, everything right down to the sound effects
+ Feels like a mix of zelda and ragnarok, two of my all time favourite nostalgic games. It's so reminisce of what I loved about both of them mashed up into one adorable, rewarding adventure
+ I would recommend this game to literally anyone who likes adventures and hoarding/collecting. My favourite thing so far is the fact that you don't have to constantly go back to town to sell, there seems to be no limit to your bag which makes gameplay feel so seamless and easy

[CONS]
- The only thing I can think of that doesn't resonate with me is the fact that I can't rebind the movement keys. For me, moving using the arrow keys feels uncomfortable and I would've highly preferred to be able to rebind all of my hotkeys but it doesn't appear to be possible. Maybe in a later update? Not MUCH of a hindrance, just a preference thing. It also would've been cool if the mouse could've been integrated in some way as that's what I'm personally used to in most games and it feels weird not using it, again such a small thing that comes down to just personal preference.\t\t\t\t\t
