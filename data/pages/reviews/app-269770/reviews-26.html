\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, I don't think I can recommend this game anymore.
Yes, it is in EA for some time now and we were told they will take some time. BUT it has been close to two years since the last public news talking about content.
There ARE blog posts as recent as last week, but what do blog posts do for me? Nothing if they don't translate to anything I can do.
So, as of now I don't expect this game to ever be finished.

With this out of the way, is the stuff present any good?
First things first: The graphics are ok, they look cute enough but are simple. Even for a 2d game.
Sound design is the same. It is simple. Nothing fancy.

The story telling even more. There is no surprise. There are some lightly funny scenes, but mostly it is just ok and at the lower end of that bar, too.

So does the gameplay redeem itself? Nah, not really.
The controlls (I mainly played with the controller) are not overly precise, hitting feels laggy at times (especially with two handed weapons where the games laggyness is just increased)
I often missalign my blocks and also often the attacks.
Facing in the wrong direction when attacking is frustrating when using the bow which has limited ammo or if you use a skill.
This issue is increased as it seems you are locked into the way you face when attacking, this results in the next attacks also being in the wrong direction.
It is worse if you block. You can not block, stop for a moment and reblock in a different direction.
But your enemies don't care, they attack anyway.
They attack from diagonals that are not evidently one direction or the other resulting in being hit regardless (you are only able to face 4 ways, but ranged attacks and charge attacks from enemies are not limited like that!

So if the combat is bad, the story is nothing to write home about, the graphics and sound design are lacking, why did I put almost 50 hours into this?
Hm, I don't really know to be honest.
It has coop, which works fine. You can even play coop with two instances of the game on the same pc (because lack of splitscreen)
But the story does work even less with two players so yeah. Also combat gets even more chaotic (but in the bad way sadly)

All in all, no bueno. Sorry.
