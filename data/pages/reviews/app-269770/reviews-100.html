\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHoly what in the hell....yeah, this game is full of charm, the Grinding in Grindea isnt actually that bad, and grinding for tons of collectibles is fun! the battle is tight, with a few minor things and the graphics, the design, heck, even the missions that have depth to it, all of these components are really awesome and this game,. even in early access, is starting to already shape up as one of the better rpg games that ive played this year.

TL;DR: This game is AWESOME! you like nostalgic zelda-like exploration games, but with more customization, collecting and crafting, really fun skill progression, abilities that vary greatly, and enemies that are designed smartly and with a challenge??? Then buy this game!

Graphics: alright, its a top down 32 bit looking game. and man, do they do a wonderful job at making sure every pixel counts, and every animation is fluid! The graphics are nostalgic, and they are done well

Sound: Man, the music is really suited for an adventure game. i really was not diaaposinted by the music at all, very well done. the sound effects were plenty, and they had depth and actual weight and distinction to each move you make. Of course, its nothing too fancy, but its not done poorly, they made every sword swing, every fireball, and every crate breaking sound good

Gameplay: Man, I am very impressed with the combat. its quick, its fluid, the enemies variety are very challenging and require to think about how to approach each one, ESPECIALLY WHEN THEY START WORKING TOGETHER AND F*** YOU UP!! oh man, there were some parts where i was sincerely triggered cause i couldn't get past some enemies when i really wanted to blow through a part of the stage to complete my mission. I played this on the hard difficulty, so im sure that why they were much more difficult to kill. Anyways, what everyone will definitley enjoy about how the combat is done is by the way you character is...

Customization: Yes, this game is very customization friendly! You want to go nothing but tank? well choose the corresponding stats to boost you defense significantly, dump your points into two handing that tree bracnh you get when you first start out, and sponge away the many slimes presented, all the way until youre three hours deep into the pumpkin forest, trying to hack away at ghosts that keep friggin disappearing in front of your face, and even though you know thats not the way to kill them, youre already soo hellbent on getting the last hack on his ectoplasmic butt, because your a tank, with the brain of the sponge, and YOU DONT KNOW ANYTHING ELSE!!!..... But, if you do have a moment of awreness, you could totally respec you're guy on the fly! you can totally dump those pointe that went into tanking into something like a snow buddy. A cute little snowball dude who's totally loyal to the cause. And hey, why stop theree? You can throw your invstment into four different magic categories, earth, fire, ice,. and wind! And best belieive that your armor, and your clothes are customizable too! I REALLY appreciate the developers for going this route, AWESOME!

Look, im gonna stop here and give you fair warning. As of this review, there are somethings that are still locked in the skill tree, because they are still indevelopment. Which leads me to one of the biggest troubles of this game. THERE IS LITERALLY NO HEALING. the only way to heal, is to pick up healt orbs from enemies (which drop pretty frequently), go back to town, or lvl up. Nopne of this was really an issue......until you start fighting bosses. OHHHHHHH man, this is the biggest flaw of this game.

HOW THE HELL DO I BEAT THE DAMN BOSSES THAT ARE OP, WITH NOT A SINGLE HEALING POTION OR SKILL!!!!

This, my dear developer, is THE MOST infuriating thing in the game, I pray to the flying spaghetti monster that you include something, cause this has got me beyond vexed. And the only other thing i gotta complain is the way blckin is handled, it seems real unforgiving, and even a bit unresposive at times. But hopefully, these things will be fixed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game with lots to do, good story, and a good variety of skills and spells. If you're a fan of old school action RPGs, you NEED to play this one. Because people seem to like these things:

Pros:
- Good gameplay with solid, customisable controls.
- Lots of items, armour, and weapons.
- Gameplay is very flexible. You can play whatever kind of character you want, and you'll be able to win any fight as long as you practice.
- If you decide you don't like your character's direction, you can just grind for cash and buy back your skill & talent points.
- Well written story & dialogue.

Cons:
- Some parts are missing, and that's kind of disappointing.
- I feel like some bosses & enemies need to be scaled down a bit, and others need to be a little tougher.
- I'd like to see more acheivements, because I like getting acheivements.
- Most of the music is a 15-30 second loop for most areas. While it's well composed, its too short and gets repetitive.

This is the first early access game I've bought, and all in all, I'm happy with it. Other early access developers should take notes.\t\t\t\t\t
