\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTHIS IS EARLY ACCESS DONE RIGHT! First off the game is amazing as it is, blending 2d Zelda, RPG and leveling mechanics together beautifully. The combat is very satisfying (and very challenging in arcade or hard mode) and the versitile ability system is alot of fun to use as trying out different builds can drasticly change how you play. There are also many different enemies and bosses to fight and (most) of them are fun and rewarding to play against and learn their patterns. The gear and drop system is basic but does it's job very well, the enemies make the best noises when they die and it is invigorating whenever they drop their Card, which is the rare one time drop every enemy in the game has.

The Puzzles, Dungeons, and Story are equally good, with the puzzles serving as well designed brain teasers to break up the combat but aside from optional ones they aren't too difficult or frequent. The dungeons are nicely made gauntlets that put what you've learned in each area to the test and overall pretty much everywhere in the main story is well paced and fun. Speaking of the story, that is why this game is called Secrets of Grindea, not because YOU have to grind for items, but because that is the name of the world and nearly everyone in the world is obsessed with grinding (Grindea is even the name of the Goddess) grinding is NOT a major gameplay mechanic unless you want it to be. Rather it is a storytelling device in the fun and suprising story that this game has to offer and while I don't want to spoil too much there is some tragic foreshadowing (backshadowing?) in the first temple that you shouldn't miss.

Now even though this game is in Early Access It still has alot of content and is well worth it's current price, with pleanty of fun side quests, 15-20 hour story, a full Rougelite mode that you can play for hundreds of hours. Not to mention that everything in the game is extremely polished with hardly a bug in sight. That gets me to why I said that this it Early Access done right, and even though this game is taking alot of time to release, that time is being well spent by the Devs who are working hard to make this the best game it can be. Do not ever belive ANYONE who say this game is dead or a scam, there is a weekly blog that updates every monday that keeps us informed in the developement process, and the devs are active in the forums and will listen to your feedback. If you are sceptical about early acces nonetheless then just wait for this game to release, the Devs will tell anyone the same thing and that is why this game NEVER goes on sale, because they don't want anyone feeling ripped off for buying an unfinished product. I wholeheartedly reccomend this game as is and would strongly suggest that you buy this if you are interested in it as it is now.

A game being in Early Access may be a turn off for some but Honestly, for me every major update feels like another wonderful topping onto this decadant sundae of a game and being able to give feedback to add a few extra sprinkles onto that make me very happy.\t\t\t\t\t
