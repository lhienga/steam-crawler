\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is pretty fun, I really like the variations in the skill trees and further in the game you can get rid of active skill points to just build up the passive tree and vice versa which makes your build style very flexible. It's very nice that you can change your skills at any time (costs gold), particularly for bosses because anytime I was having difficulty I would stop and think about the boss' patterns and what build would be able to exploit their attacks. Playing on hard mode most of the bosses were a fun challenge, though some were a bit easy. Final boss was an extreme difficulty spike and drove me insane so went to normal mode and then it was way too easy, so I do hope devs add a third middle difficulty at some point.

As others have mentioned if you want the final ending you have to grind a lot, for the most part this isn't too bad because most enemies will infinitely respawn in any given room and will start respawning really fast after a while making grinding go pretty fast. There's a few enemies though where there's only one in a room and it only respawns if you leave, so you're constantly walking in and out of the room to farm the card and that's very frustrating.

Story was surprisingly good, nothing mind blowing but certainly kept me engaged particularly in the later stages. NPCs are cute and it's fun walking around town talking to them. The artwork for the cards you get from monsters is adorable.

Controls overall felt good, my only real issue was I wish the parry was a bit more snappy, it has a small delay which is very annoying when some of the bosses are bullet hells and you're trying to parry different directions in a row.

So overall the game is cute, charming, fun, and fairly challenging. I definitely recommend it. If you strongly dislike grinding I would still recommend playing through it and just not grinding for cards, but be prepared for a challenge because the cards help a lot.\t\t\t\t\t

The story while simple is rather enjoyable and the combat although obnoxious at time due to jumps on difficulty for certain bosses is very good overall.

8/10 definitely worth your time and the price.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed it when it released 1.0 this year, and it's probably one of my all time favorites now.

It's got it all. Combat, music, story, puzzles, and multiplayer!

It feels like a supped up Zelda game with Ys-esque bosses.

We played it on hard mode first time, and don't regret it one bit. The bosses have more difficult patterns on hard, and I love that. Be warned though, at a certain undisclosed point, it REALLY tests you. So be prepared for that :)

Don't let the name "Grindea" fool you. It's only at one point that you "need" to grind, and it's not that bad. It has a great drop system that is very forgiving. We only decided to grind a couple levels once to help with a boss otherwise.

Haven't touched Arcade mode yet, but I expect it to be more of the same with more replayability!

Game is worth full price, especially with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
