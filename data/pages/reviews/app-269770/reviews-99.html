\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing the beta non stop for around 2 days now, and I've got to say that I am very impressed. It has all the charm of older RPG's like 'Legend of Zelda- The Minish Cap' but with a multitude its own little flourishes. The story line (so far) has kept me interested, and the side quests aren't just dull fodder.

It took me a a short while to get used to navigating the large menu, and I wish I could banish my pet to the farm (not the metaphor) instead of just "hiding" it.

Sometimes the graphics makes it hard to distinguish what is background art and what is moveable or collectable items, especially when first starting, but it doesn't take long to become used to it.

Altogether I think it's a fantastic game, although I am very biased towards that particular RPG style its a real throw-back to old school gameboy games. I will definitely be getting the fully completed version and I'm glad that the developers are taking their time to make the most out of this game. I hope that the game run time isn't too short in its entirety but from how long the developers are taking, I'm sure these fears are unfounded.
