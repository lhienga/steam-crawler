I played through what was released of the story at the time (still around 15 hours of playtime for that one run and they already released more) and I had to learn so much more to get better at Arcade.

Heavily recommend for anyone, at least give it a decent try since it is still in beta :)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR fun hack n slash with two game modes and co-op support. I like it. You might too.

I bought this game I don't know how long ago. It's progressed very nicely into a game worth buying at full price. There's plenty of story mode content and the NPCs have character and don't feel generic. The writing is pretty funny -- I think both adults and kids will find it enjoyable. Combat feels a little like a hack-and-slash game with lots of enemies to kill and you're rewarded for killing hundreds of the same enemy with cards that confer passive bonuses, but it's fun the entire time. There's a skill system that lets you buy melee or magic skills and passives and you can buy back skill points and tweak your build. Nothing is stopping you from using two weapon types, technically. It's very free form.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSingle Player Campaign: The combat is pretty satisfying and every zone offers unique enemy varieties. The difficulty of some areas does fluxuate, but it is never unfair. I got about 80% completion in 30 hours of gameplay by the 0.91 version of the game, which is really good for $14.99. If you base value on 1$ per 1 hour of entertainment, this game has you covered. For a singly player experience this game is very worth it.

Multiplayer Campaign: I played this game with my girlfriend, who is new to video games, and she and I had a wonderful time. There was only one instance where there was a de-sync, but I just left and rejoined and it was fixed.

Arena: This is the rogue-like aspect of this game, and is worth playing after mastery of the base game. It does boost the value of the game, and adds a lot of extra, valuable playtime.

In all, this is a worthwhile game to purchase, plus the devs are still working on this game.\t\t\t\t\t
