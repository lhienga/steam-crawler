\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe art is crisp and polished. If you are into the pixel style 2-D games it's very beautiful to look at.

What I have seen of the story so far is cute, not deep. It's a very simple story where you collect artifacts. There are some good funny moments and references thrown in. Don't expect a very deep story though.

Gameplay wise - lots of customization. You can build your character however you want. You can mix spells and summons with magic etc. Also better with friends for sure. I got it to play with my husband and it was fun. Some of the bosses are more challenging and there were some near rage quits happening, but they are not impossible.

My understanding is that at the time I write this only the final dungeon is not completed - but the devs are taking a lot of time and working hard to make sure everything is perfect, so when there is an update you know it's a quality one.\t\t\t\t\t
