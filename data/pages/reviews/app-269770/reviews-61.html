\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is BEAUTIFUL. It is amazing to see that a game that is so jam-packed with retro feel can feel so fresh and rewarding to play. I love the different questlines and the areas of the game really have a nice sense of immersion. This game NEVER makes you feel like you have any advantage in an area so you will have to (haha) grind up to a point where you feel confident in fighting the new area's foes. Bosses are a lot of fun and collecting, being a big part of the game, feels very balanced if not frustratingly so (collecting cards from enemies can be a real hassle but so rewarding to hear the chime when a new one is FINALLY discovered!). The game is still in development, yes, but they post weekly updates on new features and production on their blog so you know that they are hard at work turning this masterpiece of a title into a full-fledged game. Best money I have ever spent on a game in Beta. Seriously. If you love old-school JRPGs like Secret of Mana or early Tales series games, you will not regret this one. I've sunk nearly 45 hours into this game grinding for 100% completion (for now) and I haven't even TOUCHED the other game mode available.\t\t\t\t\t
