\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA tale of two games.

I do and do not recommend this game. I bought this game knowing full well it was still in development and what stage it was in.

If you are picking this game up because you love single player JRPGS then I CANNOT recommend this game at this time. It is simply too short for what it is and there is not enough content yet. Do not get me wrong, this is a great playing game. It is a blast to play. The graphics and characters are charming. It has a bunch of deep customization. It can be an really amazing game. Its just too short, there's not enough content yet.

Given the amount of time this game has been in development, there should be more. Even for the amount that is done, the stages feel too short, the amount of quests and items too little. The areas go by in the blink of an eye. They need to not only expand the game, but also the areas that are finished. Each one is only 6-8 screens and the map as a whole is tiny.

If you however, like playing with friends and want to challenge the games gauntlet mode then I do recommend it. You could sink a lot of hours into it.

A few nit picks with crafting system. Its partially broken. What i mean by that is it takes so many of certain items to make something, by the time you have them all you are farther in the game and the item is obsolete. Unless you sit there and grind before moving on, it doesnt make sense.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMost fun I've had in a co-op game in a long while. We bought this game on a whim during a 3-man lan party and and grinded up to the latest possible point in game (due to it not being fully complete yet at the time writing this), taking roughly 24 hours. Lots of fun content to be explored, granted we did -a lot- of item/card farming. Still, I feel we left some content uncovered, but hopefully the rest of the story will be out soon so we can dig out the rest.

It's mesmerizing how much content is in this game already, given it's made by three people. The graphics are beautiful, dialog is surprisingly funny at times (though maybe I was just tired enough).

In short, I loved it, my mates loved it, I hope to see the full game soon and maybe see what the people behind this game can come up with afterwards. I require more games like this!
