\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game so much, I simply cannot recommend it. The game itself is charming, amusing, nostalgic, yet unique in a number of ways, implementing both retro RPG and new-age game mechanics and expectations. The story is interesting enough to read the dialogue, and the gameplay is simplistic yet challenging and rewarding. Furthermore, the music and visuals hold up very well against games of similar nature. Unlike a lot of developers, the team is open and constant in their communication on how the game is progressing and developing, but it's been a number of years since I've purchased and it pains me to see that we're still not there yet. That's where I cannot recommend buying the game.

This game is beautiful in a variety of ways, but its incompleteness and dragging development has been its hindrance from the get go. I understand that it's a small studio, and watching the dev videos, it's clear that a lot of time and effort go into making it an enjoyable experience. However, I wish they had waiting further into their development to have released the game, so fans of the game would not be waiting in suspense for as long as we have. It's been approximately 3 years since I first purchased the game, and I expect that it will be at least another until we have a finalized product.

I love this game, and pain myself every time I go back to play through what's there of the story/arcade mode. Don't put yourself through the same pain, wait for the full release.\t\t\t\t\t
