\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy roommate and I put in 30 hours into the game since Friday night (It's Sunday night now)... It's definitely worth it!

The game has a surprisingly fun to follow story. When I started this game I wasn't expecting to actually follow the story at all (As I'm not one who plays games for the story), but there were moments in time where I was completely surprised by what was going on and how uniquely things were happening. It kept me playing and wanting to advance to see what things were upcoming.

The game describes everything extremely well. During the 30 hours; not once did myself or my roommate need to open up google or some other lookup page to find out how to find something or how to do something. Enemies, quests and puzzles are really well put together and seem to be really thought out for how they're expressed in-game. Of the 30 hours, we both basically 100% our characters, with the exception of the unreleased content. So if you do buy the game, you won't be needing to keep a tab open of where to find items or how to do things.

"Grindea" This game is made for doing some grinding. But when you are grinding, it's not a snooze fest of mashing buttons. Enemies drop "Cards" and the cards can give you bonuses towards your stats in game. You may find a card that gives you bonus damage for collecting it, or one that drops extra gold/loot when you kill something. You don't need these cards, and you don't even need to grind to level up. But the compelling aspect of collecting all the cards is actually quite rewarding.

A lot of fun is to be had in this game and you need to check it out for yourself!

Looking forward to the future content :)\t\t\t\t\t
