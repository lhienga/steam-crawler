\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSecrets of Grindea
Some may call it a scam, some may call it development hell, some may even call it lazy devs.
Myself? I call it a project of love.

The games very cheap for the amount of playtime you get out of it. Would have gladly dropped 40$ on it had I known what I was walking into.

In short, this is basically Zelda, with RPG Elements, and best of all, Co-op.
My god the co-op is good. Like... I was able to pick the game up, start fresh, with a friend, and play straight to the end, with his character being factored in.
We started the game on Hard mode, and let me just say, No regrets.
Hard mode made every boss fight an ordeal, and my god was it satisfying to beat.
We both would go in and likely die 10-30 times before we beat each one, learning more and more of the boss mechanics as the deaths racked up. Swapping builds frequently till we managed to find something that worked.
Each boss fight was a breath of fresh air, you can tell the devs are true fans of gaming, as they've taken many references from other games, and added their own unique touches to them. Each boss fight is something special, none of them felt like rehashed fights (minus maybe slimes? xD )

All I really have to say is, Thank you Devs. Seriously, was so much fun, the story (or well what there is of it) kept me invested from start to finish. Personally can't wait to see the conclusion, as the end tidbit shocked both me and my friend.
Grab a friend or two, Grab the game. Work out times to play together. Pick Hard mode, and sit back, enjoy the humour, and enjoy the game.
Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
