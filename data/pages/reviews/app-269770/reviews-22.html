I don't know how this game's combat system manages to hit such a perfect sweet spot for me, but it does, and i can never get tired of it.

Does it have flaws? Yes, everything does, but they are not major enough to stain the beautifully pure soul of this game. A personal 10/10 for me.\t\t\t\t\t
