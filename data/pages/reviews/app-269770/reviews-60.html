\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSecrets of Grindea is somewhat unusual when it comes to Early Access titles. While many Early Access titles you find on steam are rushed and unpolished this is the complete opposite of that. For better or for worse.
While the game is incredibly polished Updates are very infrequent. As I am writing this review the frontline (as in unstable/testing branch) has received an update after a one year (!) break.

While you can read the developement blog to see what is currently being worked on, this is something to keep in mind in case you plan on buying this game.

Apart from the update situation this is a incredibly good game.
The boss fights tend to be challenging but fair, the artstyle, is atleast in my opinion quite beautiful and the skill system allows for a variety of build while being straight forward. Many items allow for playstyle customization by providing special effects and the story is quite well done while somewhat predictable at times.

TLDR:
The game is well done, fun and polished but do not expect frequent updates.\t\t\t\t\t
