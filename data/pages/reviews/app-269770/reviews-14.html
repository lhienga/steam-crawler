This game starts out fine. Not hard at all.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, but I want to bring up a few grievances, ranging from minor to major. The minor ones have easy fixes, the major ones have more involved fixes. These are just some things I would LOVE to see the dev work on adding to the game.

Minor grievances -
1. Diagonal movement is faster than left/right movement. Very minor issue, but also easily solved. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0cYjreg7dpg
This video explains it, but basically diagonal movement should move you about 1.4 pixels, currently it moves you 2 (1 pixel left/right and 1 pixel up/down)

It has been brought to my attention by SoyAntonio that this can be fixed with a console command, so thanks to him.

Moderate Grievance -
1. No diagonal attack/aiming. You can only aim/attack/cast in the 4 primary directions, as opposed to the 8 cardinal directions. I imagine this would mean new animations/sprites, so maybe more trouble than it's worth. Personally I think it would be worth making the attacks/spells rotate 360 degrees around your character even if the actual character doesn't have diagonal-facing sprites, and this method of fixing the issue is significantly less work.

2. The blink spell has a slight delay after you cast it before you can start moving again. Easy fix, and would make the spell feel 10x better to use. Without a sprint key, I got the blink spell thinking it would be a suitable substitute, and it SORT of is, but that slight delay before you can start moving again makes it BARELY faster than just not using it.

3. Channeled spells have directional commitment. These spells would feel 10x better if you could change direction while casting them using the right thumbstick (or better yet, with the mouse, which is a grievance I will bring up shortly). This becomes especially annoying at the higher levels, when you gain the ability to move while channeling, which still feels really clunky without the ability to aim the spell after channeling it. Would feel 100x better if combined with the change mentioned above allowing attacks/spells/aiming the bow to be a 360 degree affair that rotates around your character rather than tied to the 4 primary directions, although even just adding the remaining 4 diagonal directions would feel much better too.

Major Grievances -
1. No mouse support. In a top-down action RPG, lack of ability to manually aim my spells/bow by simply clicking on the screen just feels wrong. This might be an issue for the same reason that diagonal attacking/aiming is an issue, but on the bright side, solving this issue inherently solves that issue. I realize there are other issues to consider besides this one, this is not a simple fix.

That's really it, great game, nothing else to complain about, but I definitely think it would be better with these changes.\t\t\t\t\t
