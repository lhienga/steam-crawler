\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm happy to recommend this game. Admittedly, it's taking awhile to develop, but it's still a gem. I've played it so much more than the other complete games that I have just because of the sheer replayability and excitement that the game offers.

The story is an absolute masterpiece. I've always enjoyed the art. And the combat is unique in that noobs can in enjoy it, but people who like a significant challenge can enjoy it as well. You can choose easy builds or choose the ones that make you put several inputs in as you use canceling techniques. Actually, the game has one of my favorite boss fights... I may like it more than anything I've faced in the Kingdom Hearts series or in Cuphead or in Hollow Knight. (Yes I beat all the radiant bosses.)

Most of the negatives you see here are because the game's been taking a long time to develop, but that has nothing to do with the quality of the game itself. Seeing as this is my second most played game, I'd still consider it a good game worth buying even if the devs did abandon it. But they won't abandon the game. From my experiences, I can guarantee you that they're putting an unimaginable amount of time into focusing on development. It's just taking some time. So you can buy it now...or you can wait until later. Either way, I think it'll be worthwhile. But... if you're the impatient type... consider waiting for completion instead of buying the early access only to add to the unfair development-time complaints. :)\t\t\t\t\t
