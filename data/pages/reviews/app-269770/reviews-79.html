\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor being in early access, this game is ridiculously polished. The characters, music, artwork, and gameplay is smooth, pretty, and you can tell the devs "suffer" with a desire for what's close to perfection. That's obvious in how long it's taking for the rest of the game as well. The wait is excrutiating, do not get me wrong, but the devs are open and up front about how much they're putting into their final product, and I like checking their weekly update blog from time to time just to peek. All of that said, in its current state, the game is great fun. I'm slowly tackling story mode so as to not run out of content so quickly, so most of my play time has been in arcade mode with a friend, and we really enjoy it. If you'd rather not take the risk, that's definitely up to you, and I would not recommend it unless you're willing to wait for quite a while. Personally I'm pretty pleased with what's available at this time, and hope to see the end of this project, whenever that may be.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly an amazing game. The writing always has me gigling, the chracters are vibrant and lively, the art style is amazingly colourful and detailed, the combat is very fun and fluid with diffrent playstyles, the abilities alow for a real customisation of how you want to play with attacks as well as buffs. I constantly look fowards to the next patch on hopes of just a few hours more story content. the recently added (as of this review) update to arcade mode really fleshed it out and made it a lot more fun.

one problem I do have however is the time this game has been in early acess for and seemingly how little has been made so far. I understand the indie development is hard and I greatly adore all the love and effort that clearly goes into this. usually I would say that a long time between updates or release on a game is better than rushed and sloppy, and while yes it is very much true that the time put into this allows for all the things I praised above this game has been in early acess for years but there's not eactly much story to do and you can beat it within a days if you focus on just this game, which is easly to do seeing as how fun it is. I resoect the developer and all the hard work that goes into this but I think that there ought to perhaps be a speeding up of the development process seeing as this is a product for sale that has been out for a long time now. early acess buyers buy into the promise of future content and while that content very much is coming and is great it's coming very slowly

I'd recomend anybody who likes RPGs to pick this game up but understand that if you get through the content it can be a good few months before the next update\t\t\t\t\t
