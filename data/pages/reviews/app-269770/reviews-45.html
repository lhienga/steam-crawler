This game is the definition of EARLY access. I had no idea that when I got it 6 YEARS would pass and it's still not complete. And what makes me a little angry is that the developers decided to spend a bunch of time on the arcade mode rather than finish the main game. I understand that they are a small team, but they need to finish this bad boy once and for all! Especially after 6 years.

All that being said, they have a great game on their hands. I have over one-hundred hours into it. I would HIGHLY suggest this game to anyone. It's more fun with multiple people but single player is great too. But BUYER BEWARE: You could get to a very interesting part in the story and have to wait 5 years to find out what happens. But hey, go play arcade mode I guess\t\t\t\t\t

I just want to start by saying I haven't reached the end of all of the content, but as someone who has played since the release of the beta I'm amazed at the quality of development this team has put forth. The puzzles, stories, and characters are absolutely awesome, and there is a ton of content. Despite what people say about the game not being finished you'll find so many hours worth you'll start to wonder why the hell anyone was ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.

I think it would be really fun to add more relationship options with characters and other skills. Sort-of a sim spin on things, I mean we already have houses. I could definitely see Concerned Ape partnering up with Pixel Ferrets to create one of the best games of our time.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got to give it to them, this game brings back some memories. There are a couple of things that I simply love about this game:

Character customization is fantastic. You can stick to a melee build, go full magic (like I did) or go with a hybrid build. Up to you as you are not married to one singular choice.
You can always restructure your talent points if you want, but it can become costly
Hours of gameplay for an early release (currently have about 38.5 hours into the game)
Did I mention multiplayer? Who doesn't love a co-op RPG game with progression.

Cons:
To me, the map is a bit on the small side. Wish the world was a bit more expansive.
Would be nice if the shops offer items according to your level
Can't leave a room if a player is downed on co-op. If the hordes are too big you just have to die.

I would definitely give this game a 9/10 and seriously can't wait for a finished product. Me and my brother are sitting on our hands anticipating it's release.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll just start off by saying this game is decent. The game play can be fun and while there are certain builds that are definitely more viable than others, in my experience you have a decent amount of freedom with your play style. There are some side missions and things to collect that enhance the overall experience. The real reason I wouldn't recommend this game is simply the timeline for development. The devs have become pretty transparent that buying the game now it isn't in its finished state, but when I bought the game at least 5 years ago, I would have never imagined it taking them this long to complete. I really don't think that the amount of content available at this point justifies the timeline, and based on the rate of development it could still be a year or two (minimum) before the game is done. I understand that games can take a long time to create, but even when you are clarifying exactly what a steam early access game entails, I think there is a reasonable expectation for completion. I believe the devs have exceeded that.\t\t\t\t\t
