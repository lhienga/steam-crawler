\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOriginal Review Down Below for Posterity, but...

Yesterday, I decided to boot the game up just to see if anything new was added and to see if the game could respark the excitement I had for it when I first bought and played it, and I can honestly say my view has changed since I wrote the original "Do Not Recommend" review.
This is legit a Very fun game.
It plays on many different RPG tropes and has references to so many different games and various other things, that I can't help but love it.
I decided to just delete my old save and start over and I can honestly say that, after starting fresh, my old love for this game found its way back to me.
Is it taking forever for the game to be put together? Yes.
Do I think it will be finished in the next year or two? Nah, not really.
The Most Important Thing: Do I think the game is worth it, even though development is taking for-freaking-ever? Absolutely.
You can tell how lovingly this game is being worked on, and how much thought and detail is being put into it.
I have to say I truly admire that. The devs (who are a small team) are taking their time, making sure everything works great, and that the game is honestly fun, and honestly I can't complain about that.
Make no mistake: They are taking their time, and on the surface that feels frustrating and disappointing... But the fact remains, what has been finished truly feels finished. You can tell that each update, however big or small, has been tested until there are no bugs and no massive errors when they decide to release it.
I'm glad for that. I'd rather play a game super slowly over a course of literal years than to have a messy, buggy, not-fun-at-all piece of garbage rushed out.
So yeah: I DO recommend this game, and I DO recommend giving the devs your money to help them finish the game, because I genuinely believe that, once this game is inevitably finished someday (some year?), it will all have been worth the wait.

OLD REVIEW, which does Not reflect my current thoughts on the game:
"Bought the game over 3 years ago.
Very little at all has been released in that time.
I once thought it might possibly be one of the best games out there once finished...
Now I don't think it will ever be finished, if I even think of it at all...
Trust me: Wait ten years and if the game's finally finished and fully released, buy it then.
The only reason to buy it now would be if you for some reason only like playing a game once every six months or so."\t\t\t\t\t
