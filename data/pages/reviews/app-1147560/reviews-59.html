\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably thinking to yourself. "Oh I played Dead Cells, this is gonna be easy!" Hahah!

No. You will die, an equal if not a thousand more times then you would in dead cells.

But that's okay, because Skul The Hero Slayer splits itself from its brothers and sisters with a more interesting concept of using forms to your advantage.

Some forms you find may feel weak at first, but a few upgrades into a run, and you'll easily be finding forms that suit you the most.


PROS:
The ability to have 2 forms at any time.
Buff sets: Get enough of one type? Congratulations, you unlocked a set bonus to make you even more powerful.
Artwork: Yup, it's wonderful to see a sprite game with this much polish.
Gameplay: A perfect mixture of what makes all other roguelikes good!
Dark mirror: A whole new game to be honest! Each level adds new enemies!
EZ MODE: It exist, use it if you're new to these games.
CONS:
Bosses are unforgiving. If a boss catches you in a panic, more then likely you will die very fast. Some even doing more damage the more you move without thought.
Sometimes RNG can be the bane of your existence where you get a magic skull, but all that drops are physical-oriented sets.
Cursed items. [Exclusive to Dark mirror] Many cursed items that are supposed to give you a huge advantage with some drawbacks, unfortunetly, they aren't powerful enough to bother for the moment.

All in all, I had a wonderful time playing this game, I do not regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
