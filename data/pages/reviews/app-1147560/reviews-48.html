\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSkull is one of the best rogues on the steam list.
Fast paced, strategical and rewards technical execution.

Vanilla had an issue of simply not offering enough challenge after you won 2~3 times, last boss became easy.
Dark Mirror solved this problem with the increasing difficulty level wich works like a charm: it's hard as hell, but also really rewarding.

From gameplay viewpoint, it brings tight controls and very clear enemy attack indicators so you can react correctly and learn patterns - you will face some unfar situations, but those are very few (first boss placing a damaging attack after you just dashed from the previous one without time for you to recharge, a few rooms on Dark Mirror later levels that put larg hitbox enemies together) and you can recover from them.

Visually, the game is gorgeous, and I could keep looking at it for hours (as I have) but the soundtrack is just fine, not excellent - sometimes I'll just have spotify on the background.

All in all, amazing game, respectfull and hard working devs (with a recent giant expasion, so come join as a lot of players still try to get their DM10 wins)

(Also of note, modding is just getting started on the community so if you're an experienced player and want MORE replayablity, check Mr. Bacanudo work and get some silly runs under your Little Skul)\t\t\t\t\t
