\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo put it bluntly: This game will chainsaw any confidence you have in roguelikes unless you have very good reflexes, patience, and an understanding that even the most broken builds will blow up in your face if you don't have the skill to get them up. This is a very satisfying and snappy but very skill-oriented roguelike, and the developers did not around with the bosses. Health is at a premium, you must carefully weigh when you need gold, bones, or items, when to risk it on a drastic change, when to play safe and hope for the last piece of your build's puzzle to find you. Understanding of risk versus reward and sharpening your skills will lead you to victory, but even then...The Dark Mirror awaits. Have fun. I'm hundreds of runs in and still have only completed up to the 3rd Dark Mirror of ten at the time of this writing.

Do not let the game's sometimes infuriating difficulty scare you away. Sometimes a single badly executed room can cost you the run. But this game can be exhilirating, pulse pounding, and damn satisfying to beat. It has a lot of variety. But it requires a lot of patience, buildup of knowledge, and polishing your mechanics to a shine.\t\t\t\t\t
