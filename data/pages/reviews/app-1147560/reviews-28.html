\r\n\t\t\t\t\tToo buggy. Barely runs. You have to configure command line options just to get it to start.

The screen is black for the first two minutes. You have to click around blindly until it starts working. A lot of textures appear corrupted or transparent.

The game is just full of bugs. Too many to mention here. During my first playthrough, I wasn't able to open the main menu or use several of the controller buttons. They suddenly started working the second playthrough.

The equipment you can pick up in the game has extremely complex functions, with very long incomprehensible explanations. They seem like they were written by someone who did not understand English well. Once you pick up an item, there is no inventory mechanism to see what items you have. There is no character sheet showing what active effects you have. Do any items persist after death? I have no idea, because I can't see what I have after I die.

It's unclear how to make progress. I went through a few levels, and then died to a boss, and then went through some totally different levels and died to a different boss. Am I making any progress? I'm certainly not learning anything, because the game and enemies are completely different every time.

I hate the excessive DLC advertisements.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very fun roguelike, I like the variety of skulls/item, but if there was a unique and legendary function(not 100% of the time so it would be less broken) so we can run into more skulls(like chance to play a favorite or easy access to challenge runs) or at least get a chance to find a new one(maybe a chance that a skull door would let you choose between three skulls similar to items). My only real complaint about the game is enemies with hitboxes that make zero sense visually. The thief's little dash that hits behind him, the executioner's swinging animation(the worse of them all). the twins duel landing slash that will occasionally linger for too long causing to walk or land onto an attack that was suppose to be over. If these hitboxes weren't so atrocious this would of easily been a 10 out of 10. Imagine doing a level 10 mirror run and one of these things ruined it for you doesn't seem like a honest loss.\t\t\t\t\t
