TL:DR
If you liked dead cells, buy this. Lots of RNG, lots of skill still needed, level 3 makes my cry.




This game is easily one of my favorite rougelikes, maybe even favorite games of all times, 100% pure peak fiction. I love not only the gameplay, but the art and story as well. While the main twist wasnt exactly the twist of the ages, I was still compelled to care for not only the story, but the whole world. Just about all the skulls control amazingly, all with unique gimmicks that I mostly understand. Most of the maps are really good, with most of the enemies feeling pretty fair and balanced for the area. The Dark Mirror is also quite fun, being a challenge while feeling mostly fair (I'll get back to that). I really enjoy fighting nearly every boss, all with fun gimmicks that really challenge the player to be on their toes and not play like a dumbass, amplified by the dark mirror which adds further moves and challenge to the bosses. The inscription system is really creative, making the player have to think about whether an item is worth taking and how it can fit into the set with its regular buffs and inscription buffs. Most, if not all the quintessence are good in their respective tiers, each serving a clear purpose that makes them fun to use (Except Balrog). And no spoilers, but the final boss is actually so much fun, really keeping the player on their toes and all. The game feels really snappy in the best way possible, and most of the skills feel really impactful. I dont think I dislike a single final stage evolution all that much, and I love most of the designs to death. I think my only complaint about the skull system is that some skulls just dont come up enough, I cant remember the last time I saw the Lich skull and I ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ love that thing, I wonder if its still in the game sometimes with the little amount of times I pull it. The Market is really cool, the concept of switching out your skull for another really makes you think sometimes, very creative. All the trapped NPCS you find all feel equally good, each serving a purpose in their own specific ways. The Adventurers are usually fun unless you get Archer with the tanky guy, they are in cahoots and know exactly when to be together and how to perfectly align attack so they hit you just perfectly. Level 1 and 2 are so much fun, I have not a single complaint about either. Level 4s curses are really annoying since fountains come up every 3rd to 4th stratum so you just cant rid yourself of them until you get lucky or get to a boss. Other than that, 1, 2, and 4 are really good.


Now while I do like the game and everything, it sometimes makes me want to walk over rusty nails until I get tinnitus. A good lot of the skulls are really weird, feeling as though they need specific items to just function. Not only that, but a lot of the game just feels like its a roll of the dice and praying to god you dont have to fight the ninja adventurer with a power skull build. The healing system also feels really weird and "Relic" doesnt come up that often since you arent going to be healing all that often off of orbs. Finally,

(Giant rant)
LEVEL 3 (The Lab) ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ SUCKS. I want to know who designed it so we can have a discussion because ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ its never fun to play. Every time I enter that stupid level, I pray a god that clearly isnt listening to me, is dead, or is laughing at me and my plight from his place in the sky. The enemies feel so much more powerful then the last level, as if im jumping from fighting cool enemies and having a good time to purely surviving giant ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ monsters and praying to the gods that my 1 big aoe attack and spawn THOUSANDS of little ghosts that will eat your hp. The traps feel unfun, making fighting these already hard enemies nearly ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ impossible with so many spawning. Even with all these problems, I can live with it, I can take a hard level and "Gitting Gud"; However, THE DARK MIRROR MAKES THIS PART OF THE GAME BORDERLINE UNPLAYABLE. First of all, ALL THE PROBLEMS FROM LEVEL 3 ARE ALL STILL THERE, ALL THE TRAPS ARE STILL STUPID AND DUMB, ALSO ♥♥♥♥ THE WHIP ENEMIES, the range is so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ stupid and oh my god they hit you from so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ far away WHILE buffing any allies it hits. SECOND OFF, YOU GET TO THE STUPID ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ SUMMONING GUYS WITH THE 2 SCIENTISTS THAT SUMMON EXODIA WHO DOES A GIANT ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ LASER THAT COVERS HALF THE MAP. ITS NOT EVEN HARD, IT JUST TAKES SO MUCH TIME ITS ANNOYING TO DEAL WITH WHEN ITS ALREADY BULLET HELL WITH GEM GUYS AND TREE DUDES AND THE BIG ONE WITH THE HAMMER. We finally reach my final problem with level 3, and that is Chimera. ♥♥♥♥. Chimera. This boss is just a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ drag, its such a step down from all the other interesting bosses before and after it. Yggdrasil was slow yet powerful, a good introduction to dodging and hitting, Leonidas sisters had you moving, keeping you on your toes at all times, The Priest had an interesting gimmick, with the first wave having you defeat 2 small pillars to fight the boss, and the final boss was peak of course; But then Chimera just feels so out of place, like it and level 3 were just slapped in here for the sake of showing off the kingdom and how they use dark quartz. Chimera is always so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ boring to fight, either being super ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ easy or being such a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ drag as you slowly chip away at its giant hp pool while it throws out attack after attack that are just ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ BLAND. Also, its drops are never ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ good, poison is bad and you're bad for liking it.
(Rant over)

The level after this has some problems, but only if you're in the Dark Mirror. ♥♥♥♥ that guy with the big ass weapon who just jumps in the air and does a giant, undodgeable aoe attack that just gets in the way and its ALWAYS in a giant crowd, thats all for complaining.

All in all, this game is really worth it. Is it annoying at times? Yes. Are some of the skulls really bad? Yes. Was this review just to rant about how much I hate The Lab? Yes. If you like Dead Cells, buy this game. Thank you for coming to my Ted Talk\t\t\t\t\t
