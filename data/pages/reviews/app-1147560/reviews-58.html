\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo be honest, this is hardly a thumbs up, but there is no "neutral review" in Steam, so i rather support this one..
I only played for 9h, but these where 9 straight hours which i enjoyed most of the time, but it was also frustrating.

Pro:
- gameplay mechanics are good, most of the time
- it's fun to try different builds
- difficulty is hard, but managable.. unless you get unlucky with your gear
- interesting and fun story
- very nice pixel-artwork

Cons:
- levels could use some secrets and stuff to explore
- no checkpoints, no shortcuts
-> you have to do the whole run, over and over again
- success is very luck-based, due to random generated shop items and room order
- no heals before bosses
- bosses are insanely strong
- if you're not good at this game, it takes forever until you see something new

If you're looking for a challange, this game might be right for you, but make sure you can take some frustration, because this is what you get, when the game decides to offer you a run with a shield skull and only magic boosting items before the first boss... ;)

Other than that.. i think it's ok, but I would rather not have paid for this game.. ^^
