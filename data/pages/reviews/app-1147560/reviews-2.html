\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a fun game with good mechanics. Sadly, it went with "difficulty over depth" route. It's just way too hard, even on "easy mode". In other words, play time is extended by making the player die way too many times instead of making more level variety, more enemies, more encounters, more adventures and ability to choose where you go, giving player ability to recover health or play their way. Your only options are memorizing every enemy, every item, item combos, what does what, what skull benefits from what and so on, and praying RNG is nice to you, or trying again.

Playing this game, you'll be doing the "trying again" part primarily. Unless you're very good, in which case you'll probably find this boring and repetitive pretty quickly.

This is one of those games I'd rate "neutral" if I could. Some parts are pretty good (graphics, gameplay in general), some parts I personally find way too limiting (lack of ability to set up difficulty "my way", and the general over-reliance on RNG). It's fun for a while but if you know this kind of game well enough, it gets boring quickly.\t\t\t\t\t
