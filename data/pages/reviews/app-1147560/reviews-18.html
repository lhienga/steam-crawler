Combat is fast-paced, movement is responsive, exploring the game and build potential is fun and learning to overcome the challenges is immensely satisfying. Especially with the DLC and final story chapter dropping last month, it's all the more worth the price. 100% recommended.

With that being said, there are a few things worth criticizing. The lack of guide or in-game library means newer players will be more often than not a bit confused on builds and different mechanics in the game at first, it has a relatively steep learning curve (as with all games of this genre), and a few bosses in the game is extremely unforgiving, not to mention the opening timings aren't exactly obvious so that could be a point of frustration for some.

Overall though i seriously love this game and can't wait to see what the future patches bring.\t\t\t\t\t
