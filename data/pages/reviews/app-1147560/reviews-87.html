This game didn't pull me in at all. Don't get me wrong, it's by no means bad, but it's also not that good. It doesn't have the excitement of beating a boss flawlessly like in Enter the Gungeon, it doesn't have the ridiculousness of TboI or Slay the Spire, it doesn't offer a clean, brutal challenge like Spelunky 2 or Dead Cells, it doesn't have a fascinating story like Hades - it just doesn't excel anywhere.
