\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dont do review except for games that i really like or dislike.
This one is clearly my favorite roguelite game and by far.
I've played so many roguelite/roguelike, and this one gave me the most fun and the most excitement!

There is a super huge amount of content, wich make the replayability so good, there 38 differents skulls, they all evolve wich modify their gameplay, they all have multiple builds that are all good and with the DLC you have 43 skulls.
A run take around 1h, so with an average of 2-3 differents builds by skulls, it'll takes you at least 100H of pure different runs, without doing the same run...

The challenge is genuinely well designed, you start playing the easy road, to learn about the base game and unlock a tree skill wich helps you going further until you finally beat the game for the first time.
During this first part of the game you learn how to dodge, how to build, you can experiment tons on items, perk...

When you finally beat the game for the first time, you'll unlock the hardmode called 'dark mirror', which consist in 10 levels of increasing difficulty, something quite common you might said, BUT here its a different matter..

Every time you finish a level on dark mirror, you'll unlock an upgrade which will allow you to reduce the random factor of you runs, from choosing common to rare starter skeletons, to lock item trait... when you reach the level 10 RNG is still present a little but you can build practically any builds you want with high rates over any runs and thats amazing if you decide to try skull or build that you never tried before

On top of that, every 3 level you got new ennemies, whose become very challenging from level 7
And that's what's incredible, the feels of a truly challenging game wrapped in a huge amount of differents ways to play.

This game is cheap, made by creators who really love making interesting video games, they made a masterpiece that every fan of roguelite should definitly try

Then I got to the dark hero phase 2 on mirror 3, died around 40%, and I can't seem to get much traction going again.

Now, I'm basically save scumming to learn the encounters(quit when you trigger Reassembly and try again), because I don't want to spend 40+ minutes getting to a boss to practice it. This is especially pertinent with the buffed adventurer encounter in act 4. If you're not already an expert at the game and you don't do well enough in an encounter, your run is basically over, so might as well just restart the adventurer encounter/map/boss if you didn't do it well enough.

Making it out of an encounter with around 40HP is basically a death sentence, since you have to play extremely well to recover, and if you're extremely good, you probably aren't beating encounters with 40HP left.

If you're not already amazing at the game, you need to figure out how to make a broken build, which is RNG and youtube videos, basically. When your build is bad, you're basically locked into it if you have all your items/synergies/dark abilities and you're in act 3 or 4.

I liked rookie mode to fill out the initial skill tree, and to learn the "normal" encounters. Honestly if you could turn it on in the Dark Mirror, I could see myself actually trying to play the game without restarting maps constantly, which gets un-fun pretty quick.

In conclusion, if you're wanting to play this game to have fun, and see all it has to offer, look elsewhere. Just play it a bit, refund it, then watch it on youtube.

If you're wanting to play this game because you need a sense of achievement in your life and still count abusing the crap out of a game as achieving something, this is the one for you.
