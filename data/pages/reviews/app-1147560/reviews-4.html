\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game I really do but I cannot recommend it.
After a while it just feels like there's no way to progress other than getting "That one run."
And don't get me wrong Rougelikes are all about "That one run." but Skul just asks so much of you to get there.
You need the right Skull. Enough Bones to max it if it's not a Legendary. Nine correct items that you either looked up online or through hundreds of runs found for yourself.
It's just too much to ask in my opinion and you have very little control over what you get.
You'll afford maybe *one* shop refresh. And not per shop either. If you refresh a shop you are now so down on gold that you won't be able to refresh any more. Oh but go for the gold rooms! They give tons! But you have to give up going for bones and getting the bones to upgrade your skull is the harder than getting the gold to reroll the shops consistently to get even a chance at seeing the items you need.
It's balanced oh so perfectly to make me feel starved. Even when I'm ON "That one run." I don't feel anything. The numbers are numbery and the animations are cool but I'm just numb to it.
If the game had more lenient resources I'd be able to think about my choices but the resources are so tight I can't do anything with them in a meaningful way. It just makes me sad and frustrated.\t\t\t\t\t
