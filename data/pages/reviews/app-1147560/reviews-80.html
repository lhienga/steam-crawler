However, easier doesn't actually mean easy since the enemies get challenging as you progress in the game. I like a good challenge therefore each run feels better than before.

Plus due to the variety of skulls you will never get bored there is always a new skull to try and different item comps to build.

The best thing about skul is that the devs provide us with updates instead of telling us to purchase DLCs. If you are thinking about the price before buying then don't worry it's more than worth your money. 10/10 devs.
