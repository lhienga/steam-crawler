\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best rogue likes I've played in a very long time.
So to start of positives:
- It is a decent challenge. Not the most difficult game around for sure. What I would mainly praise is the fairness. A lot of the difficulty doesn't come from enemy hp or damage. But from their movesets. Learning how to work around the enemies and get through the rooms in safe maner is truly satisfying experience.
- Developers do honestly care. Dark Mirror update shows as much very clearly but the devs are also on the various bug fixes and balancing issues.
- Story is very interesting. I very much like the setting and characters. Don't get me wrong this isn't some great complex story. It is rather simple, but well executed and the way it is delivered is unusual but I very much enjoy it. Only wish I could just play the story panels from somewhere in game again without full reset.
- The skull system is excelent. It keeps the game fresh and the system of artifacts compliments this very well.

Some cons:
- There are some reocuring bugs that seem to pup up with almost every update till the devs fix it. Luckily it's nothing gamebreaking, just annoying.
- This game is all about the replay value. If you're rushing to the end this game really isn't for you.

So in summary: Excelent core gameplay loop, high replay value. Good story that doesn't intrude on gameplay. Active development team who is trying to make the game good.

I very much recommend it to any fan of rogue likes altough probably not as an entry due to the challenge. It that's something you're up for then it's an excelent starting point as well.\t\t\t\t\t
