The characters you bring back to the castle are cool, allowing you to start new runs with a second skull and an item, but why can't I upgrade these characters to make me start with a rarer item/skull, or give me more choices to choose from? The chalice gives you a temporary upgrade that becomes meaningless after it wears off, typically after the first world depending on how fast you are. I also thought the dark knight would do more than upgrade the cosmetics of my castle. When he said "I'll upgrade the castle", I thought that would unlock better upgrades. But no, it's just cosmetic.
The bosses are good, but the mini bosses are stupid. By the third area you wind up fighting 3 of the humans at the same time, which makes your screen chaotic, dodging difficult, and focusing on one enemy at a time difficult, especially if you're fighting the ninja, archer, and Leeroy Jenkins at the same time. That one fight made me lose a life and most of my HP in a run that I was DESTINED to complete.

Don't play this. Play Hades.\t\t\t\t\t
