\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDark Mirror (DM) update is really challenging. May not be the same for everyone but I got stuck at DM4 (difficulty level in this update, which you unlock progressively and the highest being DM10).

There are definitely more viable skulls which severely limits how each run progresses. You pick a starter skull you're familiar with and pray a specific stronger skull drops later on. This also means that each run becomes increasingly similar to your last. Personally, I don't stand a chance of getting to the final boss if I don't get Prisoner.

However, the base game is pretty forgiving and allows for greater variety - you can mess around with different skulls and items. Enemies and maps do get repetitive though.

I feel that it is a wasted opportunity not increasing the cap on number of items in DM. I've seen this brought up on multiple reviews so I'm hoping we do get that in the next update. It would definitely make the higher DM levels more balanced.

Still fun though, just nitpicking :D\t\t\t\t\t
