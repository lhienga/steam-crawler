\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA phenominal sequel to the original, worth the wait. Its GR1, but with better mechanics - a block feature along with perfect parries for executing melee units, a stamina system instead of dash charges, and an upgrade tree that doesnt require you to mess around with Tetris for 5 minutes to find the optimal skill set. They also added a rogue like mode which I havent tried yet, but i've heard nothing but good things about it so far. The story isnt top tier, like the first, but its better and keeps you engaged. Theres no lull in the story that gets you bored, its just consistent action.

A lot of the levels are almost psuedo open-world style, requiring you to de-activate nodes on the far reaches of the map to progress to the main objective. A nice breath of fresh air compared to the very linear style of GR1. Also, they added a bike, and another fun feature that I never thought would fit in a Ghostrunner game, but it makes the second-to-last level INCREDIBLY fun.

A final note for my review, optimization. 2023 has been plagued with horribly optimized games with their developers/publishers saying stuff like either its time or you to upgrade your PC, or that 60 fps is "unneeded" (looking at you, Cities Skylines 2), or that it was intended for an upscaler (Remnant 2). The GR2 developers knocked it out of the park- I have the recommended spec of an RX 6800XT (16GB) for my GPU, and I'm able to run High-epic settings on 1440p at 120 FPS no problem. Dips in some areas (primarily the larger maps), but thats really only when loading in assets for the first time render. 11/10 thank you guys for actually optimizing your game, the industry really doesnt need the push towards unoptimized games like it has been for the earlier part of the year.

TLDR 11/10, better than the first, optimized great.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEARLY ACCESS REVIEW

(No spoilers)


I can not praise this game enough. After playing the first game I had high expectations of the sequel. And it blew away the first by a mile. One of the best games I have played in ages.

10/10 for me, without any questions. It managed to become one of my most favourite games ever in 14 hours of gameplay.

Incredibly much has been improved from the first game. Here are a few examples (no spoilers, all gameplay aspects I mention are seen in the trailer already):

GAMEPLAY:
The gameplay is just as engaging, if not even more fun than the first. Many new mechanics, new enemies (a few recycled from the first game but that's completely fine), absolutely great and challenging boss fights and much more. Controls have been updated to make a lot of things easier to handle, such as interacting with certain objects.
I also love the bike, it is way more than just driving, there are a lot of skills you have to use while riding it which makes it so much fun it's hard to even describe it.

STORY
The story is very well written and brought to the player in a way more natural way than in the first game.
The interactions between the characters are more fleshed out, the villains feeling actually alive, their personalities evolving over the course of the game, just like Jack's did over the course of both games.
I love how the villains were given a background that ties in very well with the lore established in the first game and they're even quite understandable. Many other side characters also had good times to shine. The humor also took over this game and a lot of dialogues have very funny aspects to them. And I just love the characters. Jack especially, now one of my favourite protagonists in the video game industry, had such a great character development, I hope to see more of him.

VISUALS
The game is absolutely beautiful. The first game already had great graphics and beautiful environment design, GR2 sets the bar even higher, inside the tower as well as outside. It has a great cyberpunk feeling as before but even more with holographic trees and other japanese aspects. The outlands are also incredible. They're detailed and even though it's mostly wasteland it has something pretty at it. Studying the landscape was a must in my playthrough.

MUSIC
Banger. There was not one track I didn't like. All of them were hardcore and gave such an epic flow to the gameplay. They gave so much motivation and epicness to the rest of the game, feeling cool with every enemy you slice up while the beat dominates the audio.
The music artists did an amazing job and captured the game's atmosphere perfectly.

CONCLUSION
A few smaller bugs and glitches, that's all negative I can say about this game. The game is in my eyes perfect with so little and few flaws you can only ignore them compared to the rest of the game. I recommend anyone liking kill and run games to get and play this in full. It is addicting, it is super fun, it is just great. It managed to become my second favourite game of all time.

I hope there's a third installment coming one day, the franchise is amazing and there is still so much you can do with it.

One last time: Amazing and exceptional!

10/10\t\t\t\t\t
