If you like Ghostrunner 1, you will like this game.

A lot of the "fat" from GR1 has been cut out of this game, but levels are overall longer (with SOME levels being very long [you know the ones]). I really enjoyed the motherboard rework with the expanded abilities and ultimates (you can pull off some crazy movement with full upgrades).

The final 2 levels are miles above the rest of the game, and are some of the most fun I've had in video games in awhile. I recommend playing this game for those 2 levels alone.
