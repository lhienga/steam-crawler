\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIll be honest, this game isn't as good as the first one. The first one set the foundation for a fast paced linear action game with parkour and 1 hit death schemes like Hotline Miami, it is such a shame we had to wait for over 2 years for this. I completed this in 6.7 hours. 2.7 hours faster than the first game and then spent another 7 hours 100%'ing it and getting all the achievements for it too. Speaking of achievements, there is one that says to complete 5 levels without dying, it is unclear whether or not you can do boss fights for it but in my case I couldn't and it was such a bloody waste of time.


The bike missions are boring and hella slow, the "open" world bit is awful and just feels so bloated yet empty at the same time. The ending was predictable by the end of the second mission, its predictable because story telling nowadays is so unrealistic and uninteresting now I can guess what happens halfway through. Its nice having Jonah Scott in this, suits Mitra more than Aiden Caldwell easily. Rougerunner is just the wave mode from the first game. Also a season pass? For an offline singleplayer game? Why does EVERY game have to be a live service model game nowadays?


Anyway, as long as we dont have to wait for well over a year for some form of expansion DLC then we should be fine (Project Hel is god awful and not worth it),

The only reason I still would recommend this game is because the core mechanics of moving are in this game and the combat feels better than the first. Although this may change.\t\t\t\t\t
