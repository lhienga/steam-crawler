\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been wanting to play this since it was announced cause I really enjoyed my time with the first game. I finally got the game when it went on sale, yet I still regret buying it.

The first game had fast-paced action, fluent combat, and parkour, which made up for what the story lacked. In this sequel, the combat is pretty much abusing deflect and dodge, which I understand is the entire point of the game: to counter whatever your enemies throw at you, but it's not fun.

The first boss does nothing but dodge the entire time and can be beaten by spamming grapple until the shock comes back. 

IMO, it is not as fast-paced as the first game, and the story is somehow worse.

Try it when it's on sale. You are pretty much paying for the graphics.

I've played games with better combat and storytelling for far less than $40.\t\t\t\t\t
