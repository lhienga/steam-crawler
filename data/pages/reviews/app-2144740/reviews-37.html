\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGhostrunner 1 but better

Sure, the game is easier but I think the overwhelming difficulty of GR1 was a downside at times, obviously GR2 could use a higher difficulty mode but I think making GR2 more accessible was a good thing

The game I think is a bit platforming heavy and carpal-tunnel inducing on the "oh you just unlocked this new ability lets use it 50 times the rest of this level!" sorta deal, but the combat is still excellent. The block functionality was a great addition, and new ability setups made it really cool to make proper builds for what you wanted to do. Being able to block the giant wall laser robot attacks was a GODSEND. I think I used ultimates like twice the whole game however, it felt kinda pointless

The motorbike sections, idk, I didn't like them very much. The bike controlled like ass on KBM and it might have been a skill issue or a settings issue but it felt too jittery for a game about precision. And don't get me started on the extremely long open-world towers level

The story here is a great continuation of GR1 and the boss battles are so, so much better than GR1. The final boss is a bit lacking but still way better than anything GR1 had. Character interactions are hit or miss, a lot of references to Project:Hel stuff that some players wouldnt fully get if they didn't play the DLC

Overall a solid 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
