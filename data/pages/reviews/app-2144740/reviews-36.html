\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a 9.5 for me and this game was a big step up from the first Ghostrunner.

While both have small unique quirks and Ghostrunner 2 carries on a lot of gameplay elements from GR1 while introducing their new enemies and mechanics really well. In terms of story we still get the characters talking throughout gameplay but getting to actually meet some of the voices we heard throughout GR1 and Project HEL.

I won't lie and say it is perfect as some smaller mechanics could be a bit smoother & while the motorcycle was really cool to have, I felt as if a bit more could've been done for it, a button to recall it if it got stuck, the turning felt a bit static especially around sharp turns and personally I think there was a missed opportunity to have atleast one of the cybervoid levels be a mix of parkour and the bike but that last one does not impact my rating of the game.

End of the day this game was a successful sequel and for its price tag and I'd recommend it along with the first one and it's dlc if you haven't played it, top tier game.\t\t\t\t\t
