\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYes the game isn't very well optimized yet, but if you are running a decent SSD you should be fine. besides that it is a much better version of GR1 IMO. They have added a handful of mechanics that are very fun and satisfying and the AI is more life like and less like turrets. Also IMO the story is much better this time around. The majority of the hate i see for this game (besides the optimization) is about the "one hit death" mechanic and how people dont like it, the only thing i have to say about it is this: one hit death is the center point of the challenge for this game, you go to a knew area and learn that specific areas combat loop then when you get it down you execute what could be the most satisfying string of violence with out guns. If you need instant gratification from games or you dont like games where every new area presents a new problem that you have to spend time learning and solving, then go play something else. Just remember it is okay to not like a game because one mechanic frustrates you, but that doesnt mean hate on that game.

TL;DR:
-Very good squeal to the first game.
-Needs optimization.
- If you dont like souls-like games you wont like this either.\t\t\t\t\t
