\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've had my Steam account for damn near 20 years. I do not write game reviews very often. I'll keep this as short and to the point as possible. For background, I very much enjoyed Ghost Runner 1. The cyborg ninja aesthetic is nothing less then awesome and I don't think that's very far off from most opinions. It's just cool. Points there. The game itself is one of preference. If you do not like fast paced games where 1 hit can will kill you - then these types of game may not be for you. It is a very fast paced twitchy experience.

With that said; if you enjoy Ghost Runner 1 then you will in no way be disappointed in regards to its sequel. In my honest opinion GR2 has improved in every possible way over the first game. For what the developers set out to make I'd say they absolutely nailed it, minus one simple thing which was most likely out of their control.

I think Ghost Runner 2 is an absolute blast and the addition of the motorcycle elements along with the improvements to the cyborg levels was pulled off incredible well. (those who did not enjoy the added motorcycle level are insane people not be trusted) The only reason I am not giving this game a 10/10 is because in my opinion it is too short and lacking more story. It's an easy 9/10 though. This is most likely a budget issue but I just couldnt help but wanting more. I was very surprised when the credits rolled. Overall I had an absolute blast.\t\t\t\t\t
