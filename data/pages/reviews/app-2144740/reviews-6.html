You get a motorcycle and can ride it vertically down the tower. You get a wingsuit and can skydive enemies. The level design is great and the boss designs are far better than the previous game. Worth.
As for the controls, Gap Jammer being mapped to X took getting used to, but it is manageable. L3 to block, however, I'm sure was murdering my stick.

Old: Fun but glitchy. I won't not recommend it, but this game needs serious bugfixes from vanishing enemies to incorrect player behaviors from ability interactions.

For controllers, the control scheme is WAY too bloated. The previous installment had smooth controls for controller, but this game realistically needs 6 shoulder buttons rather than the 4 that are standard.

Block comes with its own button and functionality, which contributes to this problem, and was completely unnecessary as it worked perfectly fine in the previous game mapped to the attack button, you just needed good timing.
There's way more use of skills, like shuriken, in this one. This also contributes to the problem. It's not really viable to slap this onto a face button because the precision you need at the speed you need just isn't there if you're not aiming as you fire.
This leaves, attack, gap jammer, jump, and dash for your shoulders--which is the GR1 BJ layout. I ended up mapping block to L3 which feels awful and gap jammer to X/square which doesn't feel right.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game really feels like a husk of itself. They pushed an uninteresting story and new mechanics that you use once and never again. I beat the game in 7 hours but realistically this could've been completed in probably 5 or less. The level designs were rough and they focused on padding out a lot of it with the shoddy motorcycle gameplay. As a fan of the first game and the idea of a fast paced first-person action game this game really struggles to satisfy what the first game did right. The HEL DLC in the original was rough because of how it altered the gameplay and became clunky and it feels like they doubled down on that feeling for this game.\t\t\t\t\t
