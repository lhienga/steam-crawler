\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished 100%ing the game and I gotta say, I've got mixed feelings about the game. I think I enjoyed the first one much more than this sadly.

I've wrote some notes about the game when I was playing, and surprisingly I still agree with them even after finishing the game, like:
  • The bike is cool but the controls are bad and camera is sometimes. Also, it can get stuck and I'll need to restart from the checkpoint when that happens which sucks.
  • There is no quick way to restart the challenge, gotta die to restart. (Actually I found it in the settings while collecting achievements at the end of the game, this should be a feature that is always on tbh)
  • The machine enemy just feel bad to fight without the proper upgrades. especially since the shuriken range is limited.
  • Jack feels extremely slow comparing to Hel from the first game DLC.. There is few upgrades that grant speed based on kills/combo but still, he feels kinda slow and it's probably due to dash air distance.
  • A lot of the fast paced missions will 100% get you killed just because you don't know where to go and have to rely on memory, forced/cheesy deaths just don't feel good (mostly bike mission and some cyberspace ones).
  • Speaking of cyberspace, it's actually not that fun overall... genuinely just wanted to get them over with ASAP whenever I reached one. Same thing for the rougelike mode.
  • The characters facial expressions are almost non existent, coming from Spider Man 2 to this is sure something lol, but it's not a game about that, I don't know why they tried to introduce the hub, game was fine without it breaking the pace every time.
  • Lastly, a note about performance: the game crashed on me a couple of times and the performance overall can be improved (lots of stutters here and there in some high density areas).
I'll still give it a "recommend" because it's hard not to, But overall it could've been better.\t\t\t\t\t
