\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositive:
---I love Bear and Breakfast because it's got a little bit of everything: adventuring, collecting, crafting, arranging/organising, funny and different quirky characters (some I want to listen/read their text boxes all day and others I want to give loving hugs with 'there-there' pats to), and dark mysteries that snap you out of the sunshine and rainbows world you build (in a fun and not interrupting your resorts kind of way).
---There's also a 'lost and found' inventory that pops up if you've lost or crafted more items than you have inventory space for. Sometimes you might need to wait a minute for it to pop up, but it's usually quite reliable (and thankfully so otherwise I would've lost a fair bit of stuff).
---The visual style is very charming, silly, and artistic. Between some of the sounds and the art styles, the areas in the game really come to life and feel very different and special. A24 and Darkgrove made me feel like I had to watch out for enemies or other scary things... was there anything? Who knows...

Negative:
---Especially early on and mid game, the running around for whatever and all reasons can feel a bit tiresome or too much (I would heavily suggest players invest early on in bus stops if this is also something you're noticing or want to minimise). But you do also get 'items' by doing quests which help... still can feel a little tiresome going around everywhere, but then I also get impatient sometimes.
---Currently, you also need to be careful when moving the artifacts you can collect later on in the game, because for me they would disappear if I moved the pedestal while the object was still there. Sometimes they'd pop into the 'lost and found' inventory which pops up at the bottom of the game screen if it has contents, but one time an artifact I forgot to collect before moving its pedestal didn't. Artifacts seem to act more like items that are just moved around your inventory instead of furniture. But then I don't know how the programming works there or what choices led to how it is. (This could be something that's affecting just me, but it's an input I have).
---Cian needs warm hugs (not a complaint, I just like him).

Other:
---I had a small glitch/problem with one of the artifacts that you can collect later, but the support team were wonderful and helped me out with that.
---Super looking forward to the next part/s of the story once it's finished. I hope the team is doing well and I really appreciate you guys doing so much for this game.\t\t\t\t\t

So unfortunately, for the huge lack of insight, I cannot give this game a thumbs up (unless the creators fix said issue; in which case I will go back on my review.
