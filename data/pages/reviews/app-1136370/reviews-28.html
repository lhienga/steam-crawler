\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthis game's gorgeous art style is carrying a lot of weight at the moment.

there's lots of little things and issues that seriously bog the game down. mainly ui issues as well as balancing

quickly becomes overBEARING (pun i suppose) with the amount of quests and systems the game weighs you down with
for example, the game encourages player freedom, but then places objectives that force players to play in specific ways. i wanted the A24 to have 3 rooms with a smallish bathroom and sizable bar for residents, but the game gave me an objective to fit 5 rooms in a very small space, which would have been very cramped, as well as a bathroom for each room since having one per room is a requirement for some reason.

a ui example is when you build a new room, the room requirements are listed in the above bar (beds, chairs, ect.) but if you haven't pre-crafted the items you have to exit build mode and lose the room blueprint to craft them first.

i really love this game's style, but it's in DESPERATE need of a QOL patch/large update. if such a thing comes out, i'll definitely change my review to positive. the best way to describe it is by sating "artists made a game"

devs, please give us a big update, and have a good day!\t\t\t\t\t
