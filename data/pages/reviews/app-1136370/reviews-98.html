\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou are a bear, a good bear, a naïve bear, a trusting bear who is just trying to run a successful B&B (Bed and Breakfast) for humans.

The game starts up simple enough with lots of quests to complete. It can get very tedious. I'll be honest, I was a bit bored, but I stuck with it because I waited a long time for the release, and the artwork is adorable. There is a lot to do and to unlock certain things you need to work on multiple quests.

You will create spaces, cook, and clean for your humans and their trash can be turned into treasure to increase the decor score of the rooms.

There is a lot of potential for the game. While there are some bugs, spelling errors(but this review might have some spelling and grammatical errors too), the developers are quick to respond and patch as needed.

It's a management game, so you can proceed at your own pace.

Peace and all Good!\t\t\t\t\t
