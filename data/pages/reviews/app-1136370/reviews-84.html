\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely adorable, chill, hotel-building and management, sandboxy game without any pressure.

The art is adorable and cute, the characters are lovable and the attention to detail is amazing. If you're looking for a game to chill to and enjoy a beautifully crafted world, you're in the right place. You can take all the time you want and need, there is no time limit to anything and gathering resources does not get tedious, even though you have to do it often, because the landscape is so lovely.

What others have criticised - that you have a "tutorial" until almost all is done, I can't support. I liked that I got introduced to all mechanics by and by, to not be overwhelmed by everything at once. That adds to the chill factor for me, others find it tedious. However, I did not felt too much hand-held, so to speak.

If you're looking for a challenging hotel management game, you're in the wrong place, though. If you want a fast-paced game with risks, then this is clearly also not for you.

The only thing that really is a chore is inventory management, as you have to drag and drop items from one inventory to the other, no quick button-mouse combo to get stuff from A to B.

Otherwise, can totally recommend!\t\t\t\t\t
