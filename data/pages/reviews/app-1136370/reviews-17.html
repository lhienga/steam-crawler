\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like the game but the two main issues I have with it make it frustrating enough to not recommend it to others. The game functions on a mechanic where you get paid from your customers in the morning, but there's no way to time skip to the next morning if you genuinely have nothing to do. I understand it's set up this way so players don't just spam time skips but it gets annoying. I also think it's a problem if your tutorial is 1 to 2 hours long, since I genuinely don't know if I'm still in it or not. I've also dealt with a lot of lag while the game quicksaves (while your character is walking this happens) and seem to have encountered a bug where a level 2 blueprint just doesn't work.

Overall it's a cozy management game that you might find some enjoyment out of if you're seriously interested in playing it but I would recommend finding it on a sale (like this is honestly not worth it for AU$29 compared to other games).
