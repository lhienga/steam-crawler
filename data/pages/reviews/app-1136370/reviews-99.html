\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuper cute and cozy game :) Love management sims, so this game was right up my alley -- went hard to get all the achievements. The game has its rough edges, but the bugs never felt frustrating (they're all minor inconveniences and the devs have been super steadfast at fixing them asap). I wish some of the furniture items would fit the size of the rooms a little better (you can't perfectly align some beds, for example) and I wish we had things like room dividers or inner walls. The cooking menu wasn't my favourite (it felt like I needed to press way too many buttons, even with the convenience item), but I applaud the devs for trying something new. Wish there was a way to recycle old furniture or unused mats (felt like I had to throw out so many things).

None of what I'm saying are complaints, moreso suggestions; because regardless of it all, I still had tons of fun :)\t\t\t\t\t

There are a lot of quests, and they run parallel to each other depending on which location the quests originate from. At times, I felt like I didn't know how to progress on one, so I tried working a different one first, and eventually it looped back to the one I missed, so don't worry if it feels like you're stuck. The only quest I had trouble so far was the one where you had to build 2x level 1 bathrooms in the A24 motel, but by the time I reached that quest, I had 2x level 2 bathrooms and it didn't recognize that I finished the quest lol...
I can't wait to keep playing! I have to pace myself because I lose track of time whenever I play.\t\t\t\t\t
