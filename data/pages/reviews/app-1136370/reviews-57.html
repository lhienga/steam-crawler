\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a simulation game, this one is a little different from others that I've played. While it has the core game mechanics of management here that one might expect, there is also a story to play through which spices things up a bit. I won't spoil any of it for you, but I will say that the story present here does get sort of... strange towards the end. The 'end' of the game felt a bit odd to me, and it is a game that even after you finish the story you can continue to play if you have other achievements to finish. But, there was never a credits roll or anything like that. I got the final story achievement and then that was... it. Nothing else happened to indicate the story had even ended. I actually had to look it up, like, "is that it?"

The game is good overall, but is not without it's issues. The inventory was sort of a strain on my patience the further I got in the game. It wasn't until a friend of mine that was also playing finally figured out where the "Delete" option for items was that I could clear things out that I clearly didn't need and didn't want to clog my already overflowing storage spaces (since selling items isn't an option, even old furniture you replace can't be sold). The pacing also starts to get a bit janky towards the end, and could use improvement; for much of the starting bits, money doesn't seem to matter all that much. Then, very suddenly, it matters a lot, too much, and trying to get it then sort of becomes an interesting balance to find the best ways to generate wealth to further the story, while also paying the rather expensive fees that come with keeping customers happy in the buildings you restore in the last few areas in the game (particularly maintaining food and temperature).

A positive I can say about it is that there are no alert systems or really anything to be stressed about during the course of this game. It's pretty laid back, and let's you progress at your own pace. So, if you're looking for a slightly different take on a management simulator, with some cute characters and genuinely funny dialogue, then this one might be the game for you. Just be prepared to accept a few flaws.\t\t\t\t\t
