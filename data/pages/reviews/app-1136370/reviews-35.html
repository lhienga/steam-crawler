\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI binged this game so hard over the holidays and unlocked all the achievements, so safe to say I enjoyed the game! Super cozy and chill, easy to play, great art, lots of fun!! Overall loved the game, though now that I have completed the quests I dont see myself continuing to play much, but will keep an eye out for any future expansions if any!

Pros:
- The game is really easy to follow and continue to progress
- Loved the art, story, and characters
- Experienced very few bugs. Had one bug where a door got stuck after removing a room, but was able to fix it. Sometimes I noticed when I picked up an item and it would give me like 5 of that item even though I only picked up 1 (which I gladly kept lol)
- Loved the cute outfits you can get - wish there were more!
- Decorating and placing rooms was so much fun and easy to do. Trying to balance maximizing the space, planning for future rooms, and making something that looked cute was a good challenge!
- Managing the rooms and guests was fun too, especially when you unlock the ability to do that remotely
- Having the option for some characters to help remove some daily tasks when you wanted was incredibly nice!

Cons:
- It's hard to move rooms after placing them so plan ahead!
- It's hard to know who has open quests without periodically checking every location and character which was a little time consuming. And traveling to certain locations that didn't have a bus stop was a pain sometimes
- At some point you need so much hardwood to upgrade levels, but they're harder to get than the other woods so you will need to grind at times for that. Also you will need silk sheets a lot too, but the only way to get that item is from the shop and it can cost a lot (overall wasn't a big deal once you realize you need these things; you'll just need to forage for wood more and save which is no biggie)
- Some trash gets into places the bear cant get to, so you either need to move items if you can or enable Wade to get it for you

Suggestions:
- I wish you could add the special rooms to other locations as well and add floors to continue to expand every location. Once I upgraded a location enough for the guests, there wasn't any reason to continue playing in that area and I really wanted to continue to expand!
- Once I had Julia help with the cooking I never really touched that part of the game except for a few quests. Would love to see a potential expansion around food farming and more cooking! Maybe even fishing off the ocean or something too.\t\t\t\t\t
