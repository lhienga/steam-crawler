\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd like to start with that the game still has some issues. I clipped out of the map, got stuck in loading screens, and got stuck in building mode. All where I had to restart the game in order to fix the issue.

Having said that, the game itself is very pretty and relaxing. The art style is very pleasant to look at and the core gameplay is simple but fun. You manage a couple of inns all with slightly different traits and upkeep types. You can really let your creativity flow with the layout of the building and the decorating of those rooms.

Downside is that the game is pretty badly paced. There will be moments you are running around, doing and collecting 101 things, and the next you will have to just.. Wait 10 in game days. There are some side quests that can entertain you in the meanwhile, but for most you will need to have certain blueprints unlocked or progress made. So end game is a lot of waiting around.

It would seem the game was very well play tested for the first 2 inns, and after that it becomes a bit.. Wonky. Even tho with all it flaws, I enjoyed the game a lot. Just be aware that it is not a perfect game. Especially not nearing the 'end game'.


I'd give it a 6.5/10. Not bad. Good core gameplay. Pretty visuals. Some bugs. Bad pacing.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would definitely recommend this game as it was really fun and reminded me a lot of the Gravity Falls tv show. As you can see about the amount of hours i have on this game, i really did enjoy it and have a lot to say:

Pro's:
- The music and art style was on point !!
- Loved the characters and their different personality's - especially Hank ;)
- Really liked the idea of managing the different cabins and resorts along with the decorating.
- This was the perfect game to play when i just wanted to relax.
- The funny remarks and dialogue was great and really made me laugh :)

Cons:
- The game had too many unnecessary quests that was really irritating. And i got confused as to how i would be able to continue with the story line.
- The management aspect of the game wasn't really present for me.
- Wish we had a button to speed up time.
- I really hated the heater idea for Winterberry as it just didn't work...
- There were some stuff that didn't make sense, like the diner in the A24. It could have been used for guests but instead was just for decoration. The Highlake radio station and the Lighthouse go-carts is also an example of this.
- One of the quests forced me to craft a lower quality item just to complete it, as i was already one step ahead and have already finished decorating one of the resort rooms ???
- The story line was a little weak as i expected a lot more, and the game feels unfinished, it started of Great and then slowly got less exciting - but it still has a lot of potential and room for growth.

But in spite of everything - it really was a cute little game and will always have a special place in my heart. The discord community was also very cool and helpful, and i really appreciate the developers taking time to chat about the game & answering my questions as well <3\t\t\t\t\t
