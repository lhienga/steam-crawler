\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a huge fan of Stardew Valley and other farming/building/management sims, I really tried to like this game. I even forced myself to work through the entire plot line. Key word: Forced.

Under the cute aesthetic and intriguing twist on a building sim that this game presents lies:

- A building game play loop that gets old after the first two hours.
- Extremely unbalanced financial system that showers you with money, whose only real use is to buy your way out of the now extremely boring game play loop.
- Interminable hours of fetch quests that increasingly stop even trying to justify themselves, until they just literally turn into "walk back and forth across the map to advance the plot to the next fetch quest"
-One dimensional characters that sometimes disappear for the whole game, only to pop up again so you can do a fetch quest for them.
- A disjointed story that takes seemingly random turns multiple times, until you are startled to remember that the game has a management mechanic at all, and isn't just a vehicle for sophomoric philosophical musings.

Don't buy this game, even if it *is* on sale. There are better things you can spend the currency of your life on.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is really a cute game! Enjoyed almost everything about it. Relaxing, not stressful. It's fun to make all the different food recipes and decorate all the rooms constantly. The only downside is that everyone is pretty negative towards Bear. He puts out a lot of effort to make all the BnB's, and nobody is encouraging at all. There is definitely a negative spin to the story line about humans being disrespectful and destroying everything around them. Yet, the Bear still works to bring them into the forest again. There were a couple of plot lines that seemed unfinished as well. The big bird that is locked up is never freed or told why he's really there. The "gods" story line seemed a bit out of character with the whole rest of the story line as well. It was also a bit annoying to lose so much money to the old lady. It takes so long to earn the money! The only really frustrating part of the game is gathering enough fuel to keep up with the cooking and the heating. You are constantly out searching for fuel and you can barely keep up to the minimum you need to scrape by. And paying for Jax to heat the buildings is outrageous. The winter biome that needs it most, only has one fuel source. That could definitely use some improving. Also, the lighthouse steps are never repaired and you have to zig-zag back and forth to get fuel at the top. The broken stairs should be a quest since you have to go up there every day. Really just some minor adjustments to the game play, and it's really a great little game! I wish it went on longer! :)\t\t\t\t\t
