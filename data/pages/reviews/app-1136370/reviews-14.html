The game is really slooooow and sometimes you have to wait a few days for a quest to be done, for example if you need to house a certain amount of guests.

I got it to play it mainly on steam deck but the controls are a nightmare. Probably a better fit for PC.\t\t\t\t\t

- I personally did not have any issues with the game glitching and having to close the game.
