\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love the art style of this game, and the whole vibe of it. I went in thinking it would just be cute, relaxing, and enjoyable... and I easily sunk HOURS into it right away. It's such a great game. You have so much creativity in designing the B&Bs. Want to change something or start over in that area? Sure, just remove the rooms with one click.

This game is great after a stressful day. It's so satisfying, and wholesome. I also love the characters a lot, and the whole world just looks so good. It runs super well too. Great sounds, though the door squeak when changing locations is sharp on the ears if switching between several in a row. The story isn't anything huge, but it's peppered in nicely and adds to the experience. It's all very well-balanced, though I would like to see a bit more diversity in the guests -- body types and styles are nice, but not a lot skin colour variation.

There's a real sense of progression throughout the game, and a surprisingly nice amount of strategy to max potentials. You start with the basics, then unlock new features at new locations. Then new furniture or decorations, of which the options get better over time. The grind is minimal; I recommend constantly picking up stuff, like wood, whenever you see it. You need a lot later on, and early collection makes all the difference. Everything constantly respawns.

You can play at 100% your own pace, even expand when you're ready to or put off things without impacting gameplay or story too much. I will say, it was a bit misleading in that it seems like we have one place but in the game you actually have multiple B&Bs with differing requirements. As things get further along, there's a lot of running back and forth to maintain them. I stuck with it and once again that progression kicked in; you unlock new ways to manage stuff, and simplifying tasks. If an aspect gets tedious just hang in there, as the game will absolutely help you out.

I highly recommend this game. It's a bit of a hidden gem, to be honest. Not perfect, but the devs are amazing. There's more updates coming, and they really listen to player feedback. A lot of love went into this game, and I have a fun time playing it. This game is a perfect blend of chill without being too easy, visually pleasing, excellent progression system, and creativity that allows your own style with lots of options. If you're having a rough time or looking for something chill, this is the game for you.\t\t\t\t\t
