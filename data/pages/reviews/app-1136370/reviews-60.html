\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like management games and I love bears! This game does not disappoint.

Positives about the game - The pacing of this game works very well. The sense of progression feels very rewarding and the new requirements for each bed and breakfast keeps things fresh. Also, the game has a charming sense of humour and a lovely art style.

Negatives about the game - it can be a little buggy at times but it seems the developers are aware of the issues and will help to resolve them. Also, if you have a stack of items it can get a little annoying when just trying to select a specific number of that item.

(Spoilers!) Tip for beginning tutorial - I almost got soft locked on the tutorial when building the first bed and breakfast. I spent all my money buying crafting recipes I thought would look nice, but only after doing so did I realise that I didn't have enough money to build the first bedroom. Because I couldn't build, I couldn't progress. After looking it up, it turns out if you are low on money you can go back home and ask your bear mum for pocket money. I don't remember this being communicated in game, so I was very confused for 20 minutes.\t\t\t\t\t

I've had to restart the game multiple times just because it'll get stuck in a menu and I can't get out of it. I prefer using a control but trying to design and reorganize, etc., with a controller is very hard. However, I've found I can't craft with a mouse so I'm having to switch back and forth just to be able to do a core part of this game.

There's also large parts of the game where you just literally wait. Luckily the game still runs in the back ground so you can tab out but that shouldn't be necessary. Supposedly, you can sleep but that function never worked for me (and I do mean between the right sleep hours.) Then you're hampered by needing to build rooms which costs gold. Your mom will give you gold but only 100, and only if you're under 100, so you'll still be waiting for some of the other types of building things.

This is cute and I wanted to love it. I just really, really, really don't love having to restart the game multiple times in a session and having to switch back and forth from controller to mouse and back.\t\t\t\t\t
