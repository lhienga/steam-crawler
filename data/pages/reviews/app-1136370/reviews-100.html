\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is deeply, deeply charming. The art and the adorable characters are the biggest reason for this, but getting to fix up BnBs and decorate them is pretty fun. I genuinely love that money and trash are both currencies, and used for different things. There are some elements that make this feel like an early release, such as the repetitiveness of the many fetch quests, the difficulty with certain mechanics (trying to view a room's value and ending up clicking on the furniture, for instance). Personally I'd really like it to be easier to view room stats compared with current guest desires- maybe a dedicated page (unless I missed that, I spent a lot of time clicking around comparing guests to room stats). I think some of these things could affect replayability, but they absolutely did not lesson my enjoyment of the game.

I was more intrigued by the themes of the game, and how it explored character arcs as intertwined with environmental concerns. Makes sense for a game about animals, but I don't think I've seen a game essentially discuss environmentalism and mental wellbeing as interdependent concepts before. It's a fun management sim, and a surprisingly deep exploration of the impact of environmental exploitation on the individual psyche. Definitely recommend, and I'll be checking back to see if there's updates I want to play.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was hoping the game would be as good as it is cute, but I wasn’t expecting it to run on my laptop.
It runs without an issue on my laptop, even though it barely meets the minimum requirements.
The game is adorable and has a lot of fun, witty dialogue. Why none of the animals object to the big dumb lovable bear bringing humans back in droves, considering how awful humans are to the environment, I don’t know. Humans litter EVERYWHERE. With that said, I think it’s a fun mechanic that the litter you pick up is a currency to trade to a local raccoon for decorations.
There were some balancing issues with the game in the first couple days. Primarily, nails wouldn’t spawn frequently enough and the heating system was…hard to fully understand and didn’t heat well early on. The devs have been pretty quick to address things, coming out with a patch to fix bugs and balancing within the first week.
They also took away Gus’ knife. Poor Gus :(\t\t\t\t\t
