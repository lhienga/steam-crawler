DO NOT BUY THIS GAME IF YOU WANT TO PLAY IT ON A CONTROLLER OR STEAM DECK

The game is cute. Running a hotel is satisfying when it works. BUT.

The controller support is absolutely awful. Sometimes when buttons are displayed as the ones you should press, they are wrong! The tutorials were clearly designed without thinking of controllers first; it took me ages to figure out that I needed to press "y" first before doing left and right trigger to even navigate the tutorial pages, so I never got past the first page of a tutorial and kept accidentally closing them until halfway through the game. What controls do exist are really clunky, which was mostly just slightly annoying at first, but then I spent a whole day attempting to cook unsuccessfully because I would keep accidentally closing out of the whole menu.

Also, this game is not very accessible. There's no option to change text or icon scaling, and things are way too hard to see in the UI or minimap at 4K.\t\t\t\t\t
