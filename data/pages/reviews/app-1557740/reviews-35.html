Rounds
Rounds is a 1v1 rogue-like shooter where you gain an upgrade after each round lost, these upgrades scale off of each other to form some crazy builds, there's a lot of potential with this game but the devs are set on being done with it which is a shame, modders have took it upon themselves to implement what the community has asked for which improves on some of the lesser parts of the game. It's definitely just a game to jump on now and then and play a couple games with friends or randoms.

Gameplay
You can play with either random players or with a friend in a 1v1 battle where the first to 5 points wins, each round is split into 2 halves that you must win to get the point. Maps change every round so the gameplay is always dynamic with environmental damage sources to switch things up.

The upgrades are really cool with some that give you basic stats like more ammo, reload speed or damage, others that change the way you play like bullets exploding or bouncing, as well as rare upgrades that can cause some crazy chain reactions if chosen with the right upgrades. The only downside to this is that because you only get an upgrade at the start of the match and when you lose a round, you end each game with only 5 upgrades, it never let's you chain anything far enough to make insane builds.

A game like this is inherently replayable because every game has a different player, different map and different build but I found in my first 40 minutes of playing that I seemed to see quite a lot of the same upgrades, maybe it's just bad rng and confirmation bias given my small playtime. This is another thing that modding solves it's just a shame its 3rd party and the devs didn't add workshop support.

Achievements (11/11)
The achievements are extremely easy as most of them can be completed in sandbox or with a friend, the hardest probably to get 3 rare cards since that's jut rng. I honestly wish they had more interesting achievement ideas given how many unique upgrade combos there are.

Music/sound
Rounds has a bunch of different music, great intense background noise.

Settings/Accessibility/Misc
Cross platform. Controller, keyboard and mouse support. Left stick aiming option for controller. Local and online co-op, with twitch matchmaking integration. 9 languages supported. The game does not have workshop support but there are many mods on 3rd party services that will make the game experience a lot more fun with friends.

TL;DR
1v1 roguelike shooter where you gain an upgrade after each round you lose. Upgrades scale off of each other to form crazy builds. Cheap. Pretty replayable. Mods can add what devs haven't. Pretty good music. Easy achievements.

ROUNDS: A Fast and Furious 1v1 Shooter with Rogue-Lite Elements

Pros:

Simple and addictive gameplay that requires quick reflexes, smart tactics, and clever combos
Pixelated and retro graphics that create a nostalgic and arcade-like atmosphere
Tons of power-ups and modifiers, with over 11.2 million different combinations to experiment with
Frequent updates and support from the developers, with new features and improvements
Fun and competitive multiplayer experience, with online, local, and remote play options
Cons:

Short and shallow gameplay, especially for solo players
Some power-ups and modifiers are unbalanced and unfair, especially in the late game
Potential frustration and rage from losing streaks, especially against skilled or lucky opponents
Lack of customization and progression, such as cosmetics, stats, and achievements
Summary:

ROUNDS is a 1v1 rogue-lite shooter where each round gets more intense. The losing player of each round unlocks a random power-up or modifier to create a better build to counter their opponent. The game is very easy to pick up and play, but hard to master and put down. The game has a lot of replay value and variety, as it offers a lot of randomness and experimentation. The game also has a lot of challenge and excitement, as it features a fast and furious combat system, a pixelated and retro art style, and a lively and competitive community. ROUNDS is a game that will make you sweat, swear, and smile, all in the same session. It is a game that will keep you hooked, and keep you coming back for more.\t\t\t\t\t
