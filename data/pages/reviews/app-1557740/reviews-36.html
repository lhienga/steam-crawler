\r\n\t\t\t\t\tROUNDS Is a great and fun addicting game and in my opinion is better than Stick Fight The Game.

ROUNDS At the start of a match allows the two players to pick a card out of 5 and each card gives different and unique power ups that can help you win the rounds. If you win a round the losing player will get to pick another card to get stronger to counter your abilities. Sadly there is only 5 Rounds and that means you can only ever get up to 5 cards in one game. The only way to get more than 5 Rounds is getting mods which some have broke with the recent crossplay update in I think 21 Feb 2024.

CHANGES THAT (I BELIEVE) SHOULD BE ADDED
1) An option to play over 5 rounds with a maximum of 25-30
2) More cards
3) An option when playing with a friend to get more than 1 card at the start like 1-5
4) More faces and details to add to your character
5) 2V2 Mode or 4 player free for all
5) Different colours for your Rounds Character (just cant be same as your enemy colour)

If you have a friend and they are either on PC or Xbox I recommend this game as its very fun to play against friends and if you have ever played Stick Fight then you will probably enjoy this even more. Hopefully the features I want and A LOT of the community wants will get added! And thank you (if you did) for reading my review!\t\t\t\t\t
