I would highly recommend this game for people that like to play quick rounds without having to spend hours just on one game.

One problem I do have with this game is lag and glitches that have messed with some of my gameplay experiences throughout my games, such as not being able to "hit spacebar to ready", other players choosing cards for me (or vice versa), as well as general lag, even when my computer is running quite smoothly on any other application or online game.

I would comment though, that an official four-player option would be a very good addition to this game even though it is unlikely for this game to get anymore major updates, as currently the only way to play with more than one person at a time is the mod "Rounds With Friends".

I would rate this game with a 7/10, and I plan to have many more hours on this game.\t\t\t\t\t
