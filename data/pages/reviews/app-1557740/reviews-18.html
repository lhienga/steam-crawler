Its a bit clunky. Crashes on close currently. Bluescreened my pc attempting to load mods once. I cant blame Rounds for that, but the game itself doesn't run well, whether its mostly server issues or what. Multiplayer games will freeze up or glitch on game start in 1 out of 5 games.

Even with its quirks and slightly broken state I still love the game and keep coming back to it. Its quite my favorite rogue-lite by a fair margin.
