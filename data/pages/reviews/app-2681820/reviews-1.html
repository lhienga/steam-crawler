\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlease note, I play 8B and this will be a short initial bias thoughts.

This was an okay pack.
Would I say it was better than Extension 4? Not even close.
It had a few good songs, but what really turned me off was the overall difficulty of the pack. Like sure, git gud, play more.

My issue are the patterns. A lot of times I find myself having to use my thumbs and fingers in sync. I'm fairly weak in that aspect. Combine that with fast sporadic patterns and its just not fun, but maybe I should have given it more time. I plan on continuing to play these songs and hope that it changes my mind.

My overall impression: Okay, but VERY challenging.

To conclude, I would recommend getting this pack during a big sale.\t\t\t\t\t
