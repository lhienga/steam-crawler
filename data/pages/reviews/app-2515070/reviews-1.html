\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou should play this if you are a Hololive fan. You must play this if you are an Aqua fan (though that should go without saying really). You can also just enjoy this as a cute short story without being a fan but all the references and most gags might fly over your head. I was kinda hoping this would get a translation but I wasn't expecting a PC port and a translation, so thank you whoever allowed that to happen. Although there are a few problems with it, not in the quality of the translation, but words and punctuations have weird line cuts. And there was an entire line untranslated left in there which made me laugh.

This is also the most expressive VN I have ever played. Not that I have played many, let alone modern ones. Lots of customizing options, characters constantly changing expressions mid line and moving around, stuff like that goes a long way. But for interactivity there isn't much here. You won't get choices outside of two gag endings . For me I do not mind, but I know some really care about this sort of thing.

The voice performances are pretty stellar here; Aqua, Marine and Fubuki all knocked it out of the park, not a single complaint from me. Would've been nice to get a voice for the main player too but I get why they didn't.

Getting a regional pricing would've been the cherry on top but seeing as this is ENTEGRAM's first release in the west (if I'm not mistaken) I understand they probably didn't expect this to sell a ton. So it's understandable.

I love Aqua, thank you.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk ngl the ending was surprisingly depressing while also simultaneously being happy and heartwarming at the same time. Akutan and her team really poured their hearts out to make this game a joy to play. The beautiful art adds to my wallpaper collection further and I had an absolute blast with it. There are also other routes but I didn't do them. However, Akutan's route alone was the cutest route I've played in a while and I am an avid VN reader. This can be considered a moege and nakige combination but the nakige element was a little weak. Despite being a little depressed, I didn't actually cry and the VN was too short to actually connect with the characters but it was good effort in Akutan's part. I can't switch to Japanese for some reason but it's aok. The ending song tho ;-; So damn good and beautiful. Thank you holomems (Especially Akutan) for all your hardwork and making fans' dreams a reality. However, I did notice that the price was a little steep for something that I completed in 6.4 hours, but if you wanna support AKUTANNNN go for it babyy!!
