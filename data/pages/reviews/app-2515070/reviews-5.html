Negatives:
*For some, the runtime of this game might be too short to justify the price. Personally I think it was fully worth it, but it's understandable if that shields some people away.
*This game has some minor bugs in there that might disrupt the experience. The most common example of this is the inproper formatting, although I did also count two other problems where the dialogue was just completely left untranslated in JP once (lol), and another where the audio clip was missing.
*No trading cards/badges at the time of writing this. Bummer.
*Eroge players will be disappointed to find out that this remains wholesome (for the most part) and that there aren't any lewd scenes. If that's a dealbreaker for you, tough luck.
*It has an ending :(

Overall, this game was just super enjoyable and I can't recommend it enough. I'm certain that people who like VNs will also enjoy this, and Hololive fans will adore all the references the game will throw at you (and of course, the girls in action too). For me it's a clear 9/10. Whether it's on sale or not, get it when you can! You won't regret it!\t\t\t\t\t
