Also don't jump into alliances, because if Beryl declares war on one of your allies you'll automatically become at war with her, and dissolving the alliance won't stop the war with her.
I had to dump my first campaign and start over because of her.

Also the game without any DLCs is 91.5Gb, so be mindful of this before buying.

Otherwise this game is stunning, I love how it's not overwhelmingly easy to maintain a good production balance, I love how you can spend hours making your city beautiful, I love the museum and zoo system where you've got to collect animals and artefacts to open new exhibits. It's an incredible game.\t\t\t\t\t
