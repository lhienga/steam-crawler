\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Late Cretaceous DLC, probably my most "Disliked" DLC for this game as my opinion on the species are a 50/50 split.

This pack features 4 prehistoric creatures from the late cretaceous period;



Alamosaurus wasn't a very needed animal, and its design looks egregiously inspired from stock art.
Its sound design is also just copied from Brachiosaurus and Dreadnoughtus. The only main positive are its skins, which can be pretty damn good.

Australovenator is amazing, Megaraptorids were much needed in the game and this Aussie was a perfect pick.
The skins range from colorful, natural to messy and beautiful, the sounds and animations are top notch too.

Barbaridactylus is a shame, what could've been an amazing Pterosaur has been dummed down to a generic looking one.
The hooked bill is a nice touch but the crest having a sail and not having the iconic speculative antlers is a massive shame.
Also WHY does it have hands!? Nyctosaurids are famous for not having hands or fingers!

Styxosaurus is a surprisingly good addition, feeling like a lovely apology letter for the travesty that is the base game Elasmosaurus. the Lux patterns look beautiful and while the species pick could've been better, it's still pretty good.



Steam really needs a neutral review, as this pack is in between pretty good or complete dogwater.
If you fancy one or 2 of the species in this pack, I'd say go for it when it is on sale, it's not horrible, but not amazing either.

6.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

Another strange choice is the lack of Austrolovenator's ginormous claws that it had on its fingers. Austrolovenator had huge claws on its hands that was a stand out feature of all Megaraptorids. Alamosaurus is a pretty good sauropod, dare I say one of the most popular and loved in the game, and Styxosaurus' addition of bioluminescence to aquatic species are really, really good additions. Even both species that have innacuracies are pretty good too, though, Barbaridactylus is the weakest link as it really doesn't stand out in a crowd. Unfortunate, since the real thing was a weird anomaly for pterosaurs in a lot of ways. I'd say pick it up if you like the species. It's not a must-have, but it's still a pretty solid pack.\t\t\t\t\t
