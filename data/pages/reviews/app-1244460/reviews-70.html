\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've never played the original Jurassic World Evolution, and it was a good thing I waited until this magnificent and superior sequel was produced.
This game was worth every penny; amazing graphics, varied interactions between different species, all sorts of first-person modes from on foot to vehicle modes. The game has perfect obstacles, whether in adjustable sandbox mode or actual challenges.
The only things I have to criticize about is what's still missing. I still hold hope that Microceratus will show up to complete the menagerie. Maybe even a variant for Mamenchisaurus that resembles the model from the Lost World.
Who knows? Maybe we'll actually be able to swim in first person mode instead of using an underwater drone.
In terms of analogies, here's how I compare it to my previous gaming experiences with animal park games.
Jurassic Park Operation Genesis: Zoo Tycoon Complete Collection
Jurassic World Evolution 2: Zoo Tycoon 2 Ultimate Collection.
