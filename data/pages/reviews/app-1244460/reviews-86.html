\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't understand all the positive reviews here.

I played the game for almost an hour and was bored out of my mine, irritated with the gameplay and eventually I just couldn't take it anymore and had to get a refund.

Remember how in the last game all you had to do was build a building for expeditions and a science facility to extract DNA? Well now all of that is 10 times harder and more complicated. Are the graphics a lot better at least? Nope!

No change in the sound either, exactly the same as the last game.

They decided to take the really legit format gameplay and U.I. from the last game, throw it away, and turn it into crap while keeping all the other stuff. Then added some new buildings and dinosaurs. I can't believe they're trying to sell this game for 60 dollars when it's barely an improvement over the last one. New locations, a LOT of locked off content unless you pay extra money. Horribly complicated and annoying gameplay mechanic. You need scientists to do literally everything, but can only have so many at one time. Also they have skill levels, so if you don't have the scientists with the appropriate skill levels and can't get more, then you're simply out of luck.

Sure they gave you a speed increase/decrease button but then went and made everything take longer than in the last game. The only good thing about this game is the new locations, the few new dinosaurs, and the improved sandbox mode. But even then the game is just basically the same as the last game but with a couple new things added in. And omg that storymode...holy cow was that boring and annoying and slow as hell.

Again...I don't know how people are giving this such good reviews. It makes no fricken sense to me. This game is slower, more complicated and besides a few new dinosaurs and locations, it's the exact same as the last game but with worse gameplay and U.I. If that's what you want then be my guest. I'd rather go back and play the original zoo tycoon.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo tedious. Such annoying forced camera movements.

This game has potential, but needs UX improvements.

This might be fun if you are REALLY into dinosaurs, the tedium of caring for them and the opportunity to relive the cinematic moments of the movies. For the rest of us, the tedium of forced tasks just gets to be too much, combined withe forced camera movements and the need to constantly manually zoom/pan.

For example: every building needs constant refueling through manual effort.

Worst, is that dinosaurs need vet inspections, then transfers and treatments. This should be a simple right-click action on the dinosaur, but it's not. Here's where the tedium and odd camera work just steal all the joy. Click on a dinosaur -- boom, forced zoom out. Then manually pan to find the facility and click on it to summon their care -- boom, forced zoom in -- manually pan back to select the dinosaur. Compound my frustration clicking on each building to figure out which one is the correct -- boom, forced zoom in -- followed by disabling all other actions until you escape key to be left zoomed in on that building...mnaully pan to the next building -- click on the next -- forced zoom in... It's just super tedius and annoying.

The last straw was a random tornado damaging four buildings with no clear way to order the repair.\t\t\t\t\t
