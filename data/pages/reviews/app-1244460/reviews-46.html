\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's mediocre. Buy if you deeply enjoy dinosaurs and don't care about much else. The first campaign was a waste of time and effort. It goes nowhere and serves as a tutorial that greatly overstays its welcome. I am playing the highest star chaos theory mission and it is beyond mundane. At best it's a checklist of things you would be doing anyway. There is no creativity, it's just collected a bunch of samey looking dinosaurs and throw them in a pen with visibility. Sometimes a tornado makes you engage in their horid driving mechanics to fix walls quickly. Honestly people are brain-dead that enjoy this that heavily. More than half the player base hasn't finished a single genome which means they haven't played at all and left a review.

It's the baseline for all zoo games, meaning it is absolutely terrible, like all zoo games:
- Terrible management mechanics and 0 real understanding of how people navigate a space. No amusement parks were consulted in the building of this game.
- 0 Animal dynamics. Predators just go after whatever is nearby. Mixing predators and herbivore has no meaningful dynamic and is a chore with you now having to manually clean out the predator's kills while paying constant attention to the population of the herbivores in the environment.
- Dinosaurs are done entirely with cloning, so you can never set up a somewhat self sustaining feeding systems.
- At least it is easier to actually set up a zoo than planet zoo, which is the hallmark of how desperate zoo game fans are

Play with a tiny zoo, don't try to go big. The more dinos you have the more dinos you have to replace as they die of old age. Enjoy spending 90% of your time navigating the windows between synthesizing dinos and incubating them and then slowly releasing them 1 by 1 from your incubator that you can have 1 of. Add in a sprinkle of having to send those people on vacation manually as well and you will simply be paralyzed getting nothing done construction wise while trying to replace dinos and every now and again having to tangle with a tornado.

The game is simply unplayable if you try to actually collect a big park of dinos.
