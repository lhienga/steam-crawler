\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA masterpiece IF and WHEN it gets out of its own way.

The glory of this game is in the sandbox. There are so, so, so many aspects of this game that NEED to be cut away before you as a player are allowed to have 'Fun'.

Some people genuinely enjoy micro management but not me.

As far as I am concerned the entire concept of 'Power' should be removed from the game entirely. It's a complete waste of the developer's resources that could be better used making other things.

Likewise, with concepts like 'Staff Fatigue'. Adding 'tedium' to the game is not the same as adding difficulty.

Emergency Storms a nice idea in theory but rest assured, are implemented with pure tedium in mind.

This all takes away from the time YOU the player spend with your dinosaurs. AKA THE FUN PART!

You don't even NEED to add ANY 'difficulty' (tedium) in this game. Maybe I am in the minority of the player base, but I could care less about, mindlessly clicking adnausem to keep energy bars full.

Let me spend more time with the dinos! Flesh out the research and medical part so we are actually more involved when they are born/ sick. I love the small details like how it plays dino facts in the enclosure pov. The more time I can chill with my dinos, the happier I am, the more fun I am having. The more time I have to sweat over staff fatigue I am not having fun. Yes, I can turn it off in sandbox. But the real question is why are you putting it in the first place if it SUBTRACTS FUN?

How that is rocket science for game developers to figure out.... is beyond me.

Anyways, beside all that lame stuff is truly a hidden gem of a classic.
