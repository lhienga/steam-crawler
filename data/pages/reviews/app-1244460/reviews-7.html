\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou'd think that Jurassic World Evolution 2 would be a step up from Jurassic World Evolution, but appearantly not. JWE2 is severely lacking in content: I had expected JWE2 to have MORE landscaping options, MORE decorative elements and tree types to use, better graphics...etc None of that.

Sure, there is the addition of the lagoon and marine dinosaurs and the addition of scientist reqcruitment...but that's it really. The aviary was already part of the Jurassic Park 1993 expansion pack for JWE.
There are a number of maps in biomes new to JWE players, but that does nothing to improve the game. In fact, that just makes JWE2 further remoived from the scenery of the original movie.

And that is exactly what people want: To build a park that resembles the actual Jurassic Park from the 1993 movie. If Jurassic Park doesn't take place on one of the "la cinco muertes" islands, it's just going to come off as off brand JP. And why were the classic Jurassic Park Touring cars removed? Why were the classic 1993 Ranger Jeeps removed? They were iconic. You can't make a JP game and leave those iconic vehivles out. You just can't.

Then they overhauled some things in JWE that really didn't need to be changed: They changed the powerplants to Backup generators. Would have made more sense to add backup generators to turn on when the main powerplant shuts off, as an actual backup generator. The scientist recruitment option doesn't really improve the game either; It's just the research center and the fossil extraction center merged together with extra steps.

They had a chance to make JWE2 into a very good Jurassic Park buiulder & manager, but they failed big time. The Add on content that can be bought for JWE2 doesn't fundamentally change that.\t\t\t\t\t
