\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a lot better version of the 1 game. This game has flying and marine reptiles that make this game even more fun and interesting to make. The story is short but interesting. It is kinda like a little tutorial game which makes it easier to understand for new players I think. Sandbox had a lack of things that you could change, for example, you did not need to send a ranger to check on dinosaurs, but of course that is already implemented. It was very cool that developers added more animations for dinosaur battles and other interactions with the environment, vehicles etc. But this game still has some things that I would love to highlight. Like T-rex model is still too big in comparison to other large carnivores. Park needs some more realism like benches and trashcans. I think that is really important since people have to walk around big islands and there are 0 benches to sit and rest for a bit and also people always litter places, so I think that is needed to make guests and parks more realistic. That way you could have cleaners who would walk and get tired of walking too long resting and continuing their work. Maybe also could be added security guards which could be available from the security center to make sure people would not go to the dinosaur enclosures and keep the park safe from burglars which could be also added like an event. Guest's AI is very bad in my opinion, and it needs a change. They act dumb, and it feels unrealistic, JPOG had better AI and even had guest personalities which you could view. Another change that could be added is the weather. It is so random there is no broadcast of what kind of weather to expect. Weather textures are awful it does not feel like raining sky is clear when it is raining and bright it just does not feel real. I understand that weather could damage buildings, but only when it is extreme which as I said does not feel like it could do that. There is 0 love for the previous JPOG game when it comes to skin patterns and especially models. The biggest examples would be Carcharadontosaurus and Achoranthosaurus. I, for one, really like the Carchara model from JPOG, and it is the best one. The last thing is hybrids. We really need some hybrids between dinosaurs now that there are flying and marine reptiles it is even more cool and exciting.\t\t\t\t\t
