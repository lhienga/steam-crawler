The only thing better in the first game is the campaign and its replayability, this game is for people that not only love dinosaurs but also love sandbox, while i dont think the Park managment is bad in this game, its certainly not the best which it doesn't need to be, this game is a quality of live improvement of the first game, the animations are way better, same goes for the dinosaur models, there are also Flying and water based reptiles (they werent in the first game, and the flying ones were terrible) so I would say that anybody that loves sandbox game and is a fan of dinosaurs should by this, if you want good park managment more than anything else, I dont think this is the wrong choice, it can still be fun, but I think Jurassic World Evolution One or Jurassic Park Operation genesis is better in that category, and people saying that theres barely any improvement from the first game, thats not true. From the Maps, the Dinos, the Settings, the Gameplay, the Look, the Animations, almost everything is better in this game, it feels a lot more life like as the dinos dont need to lock into animations when they do something, of course its janky sometimes, it looks way better than the first game and it still gets regular updates and dlcs which the first one of course doesn't, so if you have complaints, they still might get changed and the devs listen to the community. Would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
