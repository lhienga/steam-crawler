\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI find it so difficult to believe that a company that made Planet Zoo can get it so wrong with a dinosaur "zoo" game,.This game has gotten a lot of praise, and with it being dinosaurs I decided to give it ago but found it disappointing. But I'm getting ahead of myself, so let me start with the positives first.

Positives:
DINOS! Well obviously, but the dinosaurs in the game are stunning. I so enjoyed just watching their behaviour, making sure they are happy, and laughing when they attack a vehicle or escape, eat a guest and then need medical attention for a phone being stuck inside. Driving the vehicles, flying the helicopter and darting dinos is another element that is enjoyable. Initially the game was fun and the "tutorials" help to ease you into everything without spoon feeding you, and there is deep sanctification getting to that research node you really wanted or getting your dino's DNA to 100% so you can breed the perfect one.

Negatives:
There just isn't enough besides dinos to make the game fun. You can't see a guest individual stats (or anything about them), the way you need to build amenities every few sections to keep them "happy" is annoying, and the fact that you can get a high rating by just building the same attraction building over and over and over till you earn enough "browny points" to win is dumb.

So in my honest opinion the dinos themselves are fantastic, and maybe if you play in sandbox mode and all you want to do is build a pretty zoo by buying all the DLCs then this game is for you, but if you want a game that gives you true management and in depth info on guest and animals, and control elements like Planet Zoo, you will be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe positives are numerous. The game pays tribute to both the original trilogy and the Jurassic World series, with park fittings and species from all films.

The negatives are too much micromanaging, which the original game did not have. Having to resupply generators and make sure scientists rest gets very annoying very quickly, as does the more elaborate research process. But the main drawback is space is limited, meaning you can't use the whole island to build your park. Hopefully this will be alleviated in future updates.

The developers also need to address the fact that the Ranger teams seem to be mentally deficient. I have gotten numerous messages saying they are unable to get to ranger posts in paddocks even when there is a gate DIRECTLY ACROSS FROM THE RANGER STATION.

The Chaos Theory mode is more challenging than it needs to be, particularly the Fallen Kingdom portion. Being forced to manually control the Jeep is not enjoyable for those who would rather delegate the assigned tasks. Instead, we're forced to drive the Jeep ourselves and thereby greatly increase our chances of mission-fail. MAJOR thumbs-down on this.

Despite the mandatory Jeep driving and the mentally deficient Rangers (maybe someone can create a mod where you can discipline staff for incompetence?), I HIGHLY RECOMMEND this game (and its predecessor) to anyone who's a Jurassic Park/Jurassic World fan!\t\t\t\t\t
