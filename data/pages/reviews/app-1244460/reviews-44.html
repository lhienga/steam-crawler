In all seriousness though, caught a lot of flak at launch, but has grown to become one of my favorite zoo sims! At $167 retail I'd say the Premium Edition is a bit steep, but if you wait for it to go on sale it normally comes down to less than $100 which; with all DLC included, is actually pretty worth it for the amount of hours of playtime you can get from it. I personally have clocked 140 and still haven't really scratched the surface.
This game is visually impressive, the scenery, textures, and weather are all gorgeous; and of course, the dinosaurs look fantastic! I can spend hours just following them around, and watching them do their dinosaur thing, completely neglecting my other duties as park manager!
I would honestly love to see more comprehensive footpath building (ramps, bridges, tunnels, etc.) make it's way in to the game; as well as the ability to build free-form marine and avian enclosures like we can with regular dinosaur ones, but even if we don't see this I would still give a T-Rex sized 10-4 on buying this game!

TL;DR- JWE2 is a great zoo sim with a fun, and unique theme/vibe. Frontier has done a marvelous job with it, and it can provide near infinite hours of casual entertainment.\t\t\t\t\t
