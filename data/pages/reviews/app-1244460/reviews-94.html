\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm addicted, I lose track of time playing this game trying to keep up with managing the parks.
I loved the original Jurassic World Evolution, and in some ways I liked that one a little bit better, but this one is great too, with the Chaos Theory modes revisiting the settings of each of the movies.

A couple of the things I liked better about the first game were how you got to build parks on each of the 5 islands, and the research carried as you moved between them, but in JWE2, with each story mode and challenge mode, you start from scratch each and every time. As far as game play, 1 had amenities that counted for multiple ratings, like a bar that counted more for drinks but also gave some food and entertainment ratings, but JWE2 has dedicated amenities, so food amenities only counts for food, etc.
I also liked the story of JWE1, how it carried from start to finish, and the dynamic of balancing the entertainment, security, and science divisions in keeping them all happy or else risking the neglected one sabotaging you. JWE2 had sabotage, but you just have to rest your scientists after a number of tasks to keep them happy.
One thing I'm glad they implemented for JWE2 is the ability to run faster than "normal" speed, going back to 1 felt like watching paint dry at times. I also like the genetic modification interface better than 1's. And the amenities in 1 felt like I had to micromanage everything (what to sell, for how much, how many workers to hire), but 2 feels a lot more streamlined.\t\t\t\t\t
