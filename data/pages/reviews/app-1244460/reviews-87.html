\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe first game is better and more fun, stable and has less bugs. I recommend going to play it instead of this.

I just finished the campaign which wasn't even 6 hours long? Meanwhile I spent 44 hours on the campaign in the first game getting all islands to 5 stars for all rewards + Secrets of Dr Wu.. Anyway, will just be comparing the games;

Pros
Dinos now have more cute idle animations and interactions, you can favorite them and then you get a lil icon a few seconds before they do it.
Think there's more dinos in this, didn't see em yet tho but you can see all in the avatar showcase thing where you can also color dinos with like several colors which is neat.
Ranger team and ACU are now in the same building.
You can now select multiple dinos to transport at once.
There are only 3 feeders; fish, meat and live bait; herbivores just eat what's growing around.
The territories and needs are more understandable.
Game tells you which dinos get along now, don't have to read guides and wikis to figure it out.

Cons
It's like a rescue shelter version of the old game but you move to a different place every hour.
Now instead of ACU/ranger team tranquilizing/healing/firing flares and checking what's wrong with the dinos it's 10 steps more complicated; There are now employees that you hire to research/capture/medicate the dinos and you have to assign them every single time to every single thing.. so if 2 dinos fight and both get injured you have to go send the ambulance to scan them, then tranquilize them with the ACU, then pick them up with the transporting tool and put them into the healing building, then assign them a doctor and then watch as some random 2 robot arms spray the dino for a minute to heal them... and then transport it back yourself.. there's also some observatory stick you plant in the middle of the enclosures now to check up on dinos, but you have to assign previous 3 vehicles mentioned to it by hand. This may sounds like I'm lazy or something but it gets so annoying cuz just when you get used to your setup you are moving to a new place :l
If you complete tasks fast then you'll just be listening to the movie characters talk for like 3 minutes about how you need to do the thing, it's like that in many games but the thing is that you don't know if it's the last task or not and at the end of it it will just throw you onto another place :l
Power stations are no more, we now run on fossil fuel and food that you need to refill and it gets wasted real fast if you are doing a quest taking pics of wild dinos
There is no tourists/hotels or like 90% buildings from the first game, understandable it's not a part anymoer it's a rescue shelter but there's still ppl visiting or soemthing????????? I don't even understand how you get money, like one time I went bankrupt and had to restart but the other I was swimming in money and the only differences being locations and dinos..
There is only 1 mission which is like rescue 5 dinos to get points and done.. Dua, Issac and Lambert are just background characters that have maybe a minute of voice lines and that's it.


Dinos do not interact the same as in the first game, small and medium (sometimes even big) carnivores just stand murdering each other if their icons don't get along. In the first game they'd run away from each other or fight (if matched then neither dies, if not one dies) It's way more strict about territories and enclosures, I had like 5 different carnivoes (2 small, 1 mid and 2 large in the old game in the same place and only the big ones fought until they were both liek 24% hp and then ran away) In this game they will murder anything they dont' get along with almost instantly if the other doesn't run away to the edge of the first one's territory.



I'll play the game more and will edit the review if something changes in other play modes but I'm rather disappointed in this game :l\t\t\t\t\t
