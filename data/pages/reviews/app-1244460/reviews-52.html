I want to recommend this game to others but there are sooooooo many bugs on the PC version. I would have thought by now with over a year released that these bugs would be addressed.

1) dinosaurs disappear during transport
2) bugs where dinosaurs of the same species (herbivores) wont pack with the same species of dinosaurs causing them to be persistently lonely. This definitely effected the return to isla nublar mission as I had to restart the mission multiple times for it to finally work.
3) my allosaurus's were able to magically jump gates like across the map and eat my dinosaurs.

5) Rangers somehow saying they cant gain access to an area eventhough they are right there checking the dinosaurs it says they cant access
6) what is the point of monitoring stations in the enclosures if the rangers dont status check the dinosaurs?
7) my mosasaurus unable to leave the hatchery?
8) transport helicopters just sitting in the air with my dinosaur and not actually moving for an extended period of time

Honestly the list goes on

Overall the graphics are good, i love the original soundtrack and the optimization is OK but the bugs really need to be fixed.

Ill change my review if the bugs ever get addressed.\t\t\t\t\t
