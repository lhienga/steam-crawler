\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought this on sale for $17 and returned it a few hours later.

I have already played this game. I played it 20 years ago and it was better when it was called Jurassic Park: Operation Genesis. Stop making the same games over and over and charging us for them! LAZY!

But all the voices from the movies are in the game. Who cares! You wasted money on that and forgot to put any money into updating the gameplay!

I want a park management game. This feels like a fish-tank simulator with dinosaurs instead of fish. SO BORING.

My first thought was, "Wow look at all these new buildings, they have added so much!" Nope, they are all just reskins of the same handful of buildings from 20 years ago. But what if I told you you get different skins of them from the different movies instead? Did anyone want that? Or care? I really feel tricked. What a lazy cash-grab of a game this is.

I guess I am just spoiled from Planet Coaster. I wanted a themepark game management and got a dinosaur viewing game instead with about 1/1000th of the options.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYes it is a decent game, I still enjoyed this game although I still prefer Planet Zoo for my personal taste. The management mechanism a little bit easier than planet zoo (re: Planet Zoo Lite), but both game share similar gameplay that you will expect in Frontier Devs game. With an additional feature that you can use the helicopter and jeep manually to tranquilize, using flare, taking photo, medicate sick dinos, etc. I personally don't use this feature beside the tutorial mission because you need to pause the game sometimes to manage your park, while using manual feature will resume the game and could possibly break everything (especially in bad storm).
I'm super happy with the performance. Thanks to DLSS, and maybe because this game doesn't track every guests, I rarely have FPS drops when it's super crowd. In planet zoo, I played with 1-30 FPS if I have over 1500 guests. But JWE 2's guests not tracked one by one, so I think this really help not making the game CPU Bound.

For people who likes the theme of dinosaurs *jurrasic* park and not very heavy in managing (it's still not easy tho, but easier) like Planet Zoo, definitely recommend this one.

PS: The 'campaign' mode is just tutorial (and that wasn't covering all of the task you need to manage, it's just basic). THE REAL CAMPAIGN is Chaos Theory Mode. Don't get baited if you just finish the tutorial in like 2-3 hours lmao\t\t\t\t\t
