Introduction

- Despite me not being a true dinosaur fan or something like this, i found this game realistically astonishing because it offers real fun many people wish to have. Lets say simple, for the price this game asks you and the PC-requirements recommended the first thing that comes on your mind is...the graphics this game will have. Well starting with graphics, this game has really good graphics in terms of global Detail Quality Level, the Textures in General and the Quality..
>Good Detail Quality Level
>Extremely good Shadow Quality
Starting with the sweet reflections on water and ending with the good looking objects on map, even during a simple Rain, everything looks close to realistic.

IS Map design good?

- The map design is very well made, lets say almost every map its designed so well, you always want to build something crazy good looking on it, every map has its own size, Weather, There can be Storms, rainy maps, snow maps, and stuff like that.
- In terms of building your own kingdom on these maps is also a pleasure because you in this game actually you get enough place to find yourself how you want to see the Park to look. (the previous game has maps that were alot smaller for me) personally.
- I personally liked so far every map i played on, so from me personally i would say, you'll absolutely enjoy it.

How the dinosaurs are like?

- The dinosaurs infact are well shown, when i say 'well shown' i mean how they look, the textures, the AI behaviour of theirs, how they live with each other, how they are a part of your Park.
- The dinosaurs in your park are the
Key Element of everything.
They live, they die when they get old, they fight each other, you need to build the perfect eco-system for them, because as every creature alive, you'll have to make sure they got the food, water, and especially the Comfort, because comfort matters alot depends tho.
- The animations of dinosaurs are good, they move very realistic and its really cool to look at them, especially when you put some carnivores in the same place to watch them fight LOL.

Vast sandbox, absolutely recommend it 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
