\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing game. Faithful to the Jurassic Park and Jurassic World franchises, the Parks look beautiful. Does a good job of teaching you the mechanics to the simulation. Story modes come from a wide variety of points in the Jurassic Park timeline. It's great to hear classic characters and see references to the movies.

Fun, entertainment, beauty and dinosaurs. Highly recommend.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPRO's - The element of creating/managing a Jurassic Park is very well done. Sandbox mode is excellent fun with lots of replay value, the career/challenge modes are tricky and have some replay value. Dinosaurs are good to look at and have a good amount of animations. Lots of options for buildings in your park.

CON's - Too much DLC content which could (and should) be included with the original game, the DLC is only recommended to be bought on special offer. Some career/challenge mode levels require you to control a vehicle and perform tasks, this is a management sim, the vehicle control element is badly done and was obviously not properly planned. All enclosures require ranger trucks to enter them and check the status of the animals, whenever the ranger truck enters the aquatic/aviary enclosures it gets stuck and has to be manually removed.

VERDICT - You should get this game if you like management sims, its a bonus if you've enjoyed the Jurassic Park/World franchises. Original game and DLC should be bought at discounted/special offer price\t\t\t\t\t
