\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, if you want to play a solid game, go with this if it is on sale (i.e. less than $20). The annoyances with the first game are usually solved here in this game. It looks beautiful, and they have the actors from the latest movie add to missions.

Now why do I not recommend it? This is a glorified DLC for the original. Improvements added, U/I cleaned up, new dinosaurs added, missions built based off of the latest movie. But the whole campaign took less to finish than one of the islands in the first game. I literally found myself asking out loud, "that's it???" So many of the extra dinosaurs locked behind separate packs ranging between $8 and $20 each just screams the publisher looking to milk a bunch of whales. It seems they wanted to play off the latest movie, throw a mini campaign, and just have it as a sandbox park builder.

Do yourself a favor, grab the first game, and if you find this on sale under $20, go ahead and snag it. Just realize that this is a 4 hour expansion, and not a true new game.\t\t\t\t\t
