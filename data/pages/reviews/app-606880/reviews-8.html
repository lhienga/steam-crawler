\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly think this is a fantastic experience. There are some elements which are a little clunky such as the combat (at times) or getting stuck on the odd piece of landscape, but absolutely nothing game breaking. Any of these small flaws are greatly overshadowed by a truly immersive story, brilliant writing and characters who undergo developments as you progress through the story. The setting alone was truly unique and the 17th century-esque setting is one which I'd been pining to discover in a fantasy setting.

I'll admit that I'd never even heard of the game until a month ago, but been hooked with my first playthrough up until completion of the main story. I can tell that there is a good level of replayability here to try out different skills and make different choices which impact the game as I go along.

If you're unsure, give it a shot! With GreedFall 2 coming in the near future, I can only spread the good word of this game.
