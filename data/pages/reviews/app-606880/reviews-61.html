\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR at the bottom
I was really looking forward to this game, it looked like it would be a great sort of colonial fantasy rpg with a touch of the dread of Dishonored and a druidic side, but from the beginning I'm already fairly disappointed, though mostly from just the cosmetic appearance. The voice acting is stilted and unnatural, the animations are clunky and repetitive (seriouly, who the hell goes from crossing their arms to having them down at their sides, back to crossing them, and then into a freaking superhero pose while talking, in the span of about five seconds?) and the facial animations look like whoever did them just used the speech sync function in Blender and didn't go back to make sure it came out alright. And the teeth, oh God the teeth, every character has obnoxiously visible teeth regardless of the brightness settings that are slightly spaced apart like a six year old whose mouth is too big for their baby teeth, but is too young to lose them in favor of their permanent teeth, and they aren't attached to anything, they move completely independently of everything else, the lips, the jaw, even their entire face moves in different directions from those teeth. It's almost as bad as the original design of Sonic for the films. Add on how your companions each have only a SINGLE battlecry that they shout out every single time they lock onto an enemy, and everyone seems to have an obsession with nodding with every word out of their mouths, and the graphics downright refuse to run smoothly on a computer that would have been top of the line when the game released, and it's pretty difficult to keep playing long enough to care about much of anything.
TL;DR animations look like they were made by an AI, voice acting sounds like everyone came fresh off Fiverr, and every character's teeth look like a sleep paralysis demon. I wish the devs could fix this, but I doubt something this systematic and individual can be fixed or replaced without just remaking the entire game from scratch.\t\t\t\t\t
