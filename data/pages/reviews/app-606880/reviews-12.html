\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first, I was quite skeptical about this game then after watching so many gameplay and reviews on YouTube and i bought it.

The prologue will give you some glimpse of what's ahead, it will show you how things going to happen and how many diplomatic choices you will need to make later down the game. You can't be righteous in this game since you are a diplomat, at some point you will think violence is the answer but no your choices will have greater consequences on the story later (even in the ending) so tread carefully.

Combat:- it's janky but still enjoyable, since I prefer parry over dodging, it's satisfying in this game. Guns and Magic are a nice addition to combat for ranged attacks.

Environment:- Visuals are nice both in cities and in forest areas.

Armors:- There is a wide variety of armor sets belonging to different factions and upgrading them will give you nice visual changes which I love.

Story:- Some point feels really good and some are quite meh but overall it's good.

Characters:- one of the main things about such games is their characters and their development and it was beautifully done in this game, the way you build relationships with companions and their missions is exceptionally good.

Enemies:- A good amount of monster species and human enemies.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Things I didn't like

You have to Walk/Run too much which sometimes ruins the pacing as there are no other ways to explore.

That's it.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Conclusion

PR:- 8.7/10

For a small studio, this game is awesome, they did a great job with Greedfall and I think Spiders is heading in the right direction, really enjoyed every single minute spent in the game. I also really liked the DLC it's awesome it also changes the ending slightly so play it before the final mission.

Now I can't wait for Greedfall 2 lol.\t\t\t\t\t
