\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will be honest, I did not think I would like this game as much as I did. Many of the reviews make it sound boring.. Boy was I in for a ride of a lifetime! The story is fantastic, spite the running around you do. There are so many choices and different dialog you can receive based on your party members, who you romance, and who you make friends with as far as factions go. I was surprised that even completing side quests could make or break your main story outcome!
The only things I found to be bothersome were the fact halfway through my game launcher stopped working and I had to do some weird work arounds to get it working again (there are many mods to help fix these issues thankfully). I also had a few instances of getting stuck on map terrain and falling through the floor twice during the final boss battle, which reset the bosses health bar (terribly annoying). Other than that, I had a smooth experience and after 60+ hours on this game, I can say I thoroughly enjoyed every minute.
If you love unraveling story, diplomacy, and really neat battle specs/weapon choices, this game is definitely for you and I hope you disregard the voices of negativity given to it.\t\t\t\t\t
