\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI went into this game with low expectations given that it was from a smaller developer that I was unfamiliar with. Let me tell you now that this game is a hidden gem well worth the price, especially while it's on sale. I enjoyed the main story and all the side content and never got bored or detached from anything. The combat is quite fun, it felt to me to be a more refined version of The Witcher 2's fighting system. I played as a Technical class character, meaning my guy was specialised for science, alchemy, and firearms and I had a very fun time crafting loads of things and blasting the Island away with my muskets and pistols. I also enjoyed getting to know the companions and completing their unique quest lines as well, and I found it very refreshing that this game didn't feel the need to force in an awkward sex scene when you complete a romance process with one of the romance option companions. Overall if I had to give you a quick summary of what this game is like, it's as if someone took Mass Effect and The Witcher and combined their features into a single game. I would gladly enjoy this game again for several replays given the branching quest paths and diverse actions you can take to affect quest resolutions and the state of the world itself in the game. I also enjoy the fact that our character is a person of repute because they hold an official position, meaning this game stands apart from all the other RPGs out there where the other characters treat you like crap even after you've saved the world! Instead, your character is received and treated like someone who actually matters and is deserving of respect and deference: I found this to be a neat change of pace from other RPGs.

If you enjoy RPGs, give this a try. I would be very shocked if you were disappointed. Thank you developers for giving us this gem and I look forward to buying and playing GreedFall 2 when it is released!\t\t\t\t\t
