\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt has a little bit of a learning curve but once you get used to the mechanics this game has plenty to offer. The world is intriguing with plenty of lore and several companions that you can customize and swap out frequently. The class system is a little rigid along with the attributes and skills but feels satisfying when you find a good combination that suits your playstyle. Though the world feels a little constrained, the various regions offer some opportunities to explore and find some neat areas if you have the right skills to access them. The story and side quests can get a little tedious but are satisfying overall and offer some replay value depending on your choices. Overall I'd recommend as a solid game and an enjoyable experience.\t\t\t\t\t
