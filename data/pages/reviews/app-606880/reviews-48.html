\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreedfall is a story driven and exploration focused RPG set in a fantastic version of colonial France with heavy indigenous themes. It plays out like a lower budget version of the Witcher, but with a three person party and more faction related focus and minor shades of Fallout, Mass Effect, or Dragon Age. The combat is repetitive, but very manageable once you learn how to properly dodge. There is a lot of reuse of assets both in map layout and enemies and there are a surprising number of typos for a game that came out three years ago. Despite the game being a little sloppy around the edges and a little slow paced at times, the story is top notch as is the questing system that allows charging through events but rewards a more careful approach. The creativity of placing your character in a role of an ambassador is an excellent choice for this style of narrative. Push past the middling reviews if this is your style of game.\t\t\t\t\t
