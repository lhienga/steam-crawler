\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
The winner of this game is it's dialogue, talking with the characters by itself is rewarding and even the language of the natives it's easy to understand because the words are very contextual and persistent throughout the game. Completely voice acted and story is fun and immersive!
Combat is fun, though i recommend only playing on expert otherwise it can get a bit dull. If you play expert you have to use everything at your disposal as well as be conscious of your spent skills. Traps, Guns, Magic, Heavy/ Light weapons. Upgrading of weapons & armor, chemical concoctions for the blade... etc.

Cons:
So much walking, it's tedious, presented as an open world game but sometimes after walking back and fourth on a linear path between trees during quests gets pretty dull, especially when the assets and areas are basically recycled.
You aren't forced to fight, there is usually a passive option for some quests, i don't mean that. I mean if you step 5 meters away from who you're fighting they de-agro, walk away and reset health. Really annoying actually, mid fight if you happen to walk a bit away you basically have to do the fight again. That being said you can walk past non-quest enemies.

Conclusion:
It's a fun game, I'm annoyed that to get all of the achievements you'd have to replay the game a few times literally just for romance options with the companions, that means all of the missions, three F-ing times. I'm taking a long break from the game because that's a crappy way to make your game replayable and i don't respect it. I'm burnt out from grinding a good game just to get slapped in the face.

If you don't mind that though, listen to the story, sometimes you can just sit back and watch some dramatic cut scene and then get thrown into a bada$$ boss fight with demonic plant gods.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThought this would just be an interesting adventure game. After my first play through, I realized I am a bit obsessed. Originally I found the game's battle mechanics to feel a bit clunky and odd, but once I got the hang of it, they are actually pretty fun because they're so different. (playing with controller, so I'm unsure if the keyboard mechanics are odd or not).
I like that there is the option to truly play how you want, as well as the option to pick sides. I enjoyed playing that I actually want to unlock all the achievements and try each romance, play style, and endings.
I will say this game is good for people who like intrigue and can obsess about a good story. The dlc adds just a little intrigue to the story, and some unique items... but I'd definitely wait to buy the dlc on sale, while it added hours of playtime to 100% the story is pretty obvious from the beginning and really you just have to run around the maps to finish it.
Overall I'm thoroughly happy I got this game.\t\t\t\t\t
