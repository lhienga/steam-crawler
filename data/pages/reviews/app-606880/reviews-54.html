Boring gameplay. Bad animations, the 3 steps just for your character to stop walking are boring. Boring fights. Closed and partitioned world. Absence of freedom. Limited movements.

If you want and like to play a video game for its story and lore, then this one may be for you.

I'm not sure of the state of it at launch but from what I remember of reading the reviews they did not do it justice, at least compared to the 3 or four hours I'm into this game now. I bought this with the DLC on sale and it's so much of a bargain I honestly feel as though I've robbed the people who made it. Not often I get to write something like that about a video game in 2022.\t\t\t\t\t

The DLC is also good but a touch short although the main game has plenty of content, especially if you explore areas and do side quests/missions which do affect the main storyline interactions.

I also really appreciate the generally realistic weapons and armor. I also like how your character fights in a believable way with the various weapons while still having some fantasy flair. Magic, guns, traps and bombs also make give good variety in how you fight since it's easy to combine melee with magic or guns, etc.

Companions do have unique things to add to various quests which does happen with decent frequency but I wish it happened more often.
