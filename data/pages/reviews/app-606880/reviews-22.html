\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreedfall was a solid game for me, although it had some negative things that kinda lowered my opinion about it especially the more i was playing it.

Pros:
- Nice and unique setting/lore
- Solid combat system, that reminded me Witcher III, offering various ways to fight
- Nice character customization both in appearance and builds, having a plethora of combinations
- I liked the NPC companions, they had unique personalities and backstories/personal quests
- A lot of intrigue and plot twists that i really liked.

CONs
- For a 2019 game the graphics were kinda meh, not bad, but just seemed a bit outdated, nothing that will ruin your enjoyment though.
- A lot of repeated assets in environments, almost everything looked the same, especially in palaces and forests, this was probably the worst part of the game, it seemed really sloppy and kinda decreased my immersion.
- Quite a few quests were fetch quests and go to point A and then to point B and then back to point A, which after some time it made the game tedious and boring and i was just waiting to finish it asap.
- Music and ambient sound weren't anything memorable.

All in all i had good fun for about 35 hours, i liked the game but it could have been much better. Definitely give it a try, its a solid RPG.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is not a good game. Its not the worst game i have ever played, but i'm glad i got it on sale. It is not worth the full price tag. I did finish the story, however i did not want to. I had to force myself to finish this game so i could mark it off my backlog list. I finished it in about 48 hrs and got the best ending, apparently. So here are my main pro/cons.

CONS:
- It has this weird brown filter that happens sometimes, i assume its supposed to be for a cool effect but it honestly makes the world ugly and hard to look at for someone who has bad eyesight lol

- Story issues. The story was just kinda boring, sometimes it was hard to figure out what was going on or simply difficult to stay invested in what was going on. Most of the quest were forgettable and didn't always add to the story or feel like they mattered at all. Felt very linear, and even when i could go wherever i wanted, i didnt really feel the need to explore or do side quests.

- Companions lacked character. In all honesty, i didn't like any of the companions. They were all somewhat useless in combat too. I couldn't figure out how to start a romance with any one either. Like romance options showed up some of the time, but sometimes it was the same option over and over. Also the screen to view ur relationship status was just not great, since it simply would tell u how they felt about u using a single word and not some form of measurement where u can visibly see the point system. Every character in this game was ugly, but that's more of a personal opinion lol

- Combat was god awful and not satisfying at all. Maybe it was because i used a controller, but the fighting felt like it didnt matter what i did. It was clunky, slow, and repetitive.

- All the ending were anti-climatic or disappointing. Didn't feel like most of the choices u made matter, only a handful really did anything. Maybe i just sucked at the game, but that's how it felt.

PROS:
- Magic looked cool and vibrant when in use. Some of the ideas for magic in general were interesting

- The whole plant ppl curse thing was a good idea, just not executed that great. With a few tweaks and some more imagination, i think they could have made it even cooler and darker.

- Some of the creatures/monsters had cool concepts/designs. Most were basic ideas. but some of the boss monsters had a lot of potential

- Female or male protagonist. Your character, imo, is always kinda ugly but at least u can somewhat choose what u look like from the limited customization and whether you are male/female.

I would give this game like a 5/10. The idea seemed really cool, i just dont think it was executed well. This is all my own opinion, so if u loved this game dont hate me lol\t\t\t\t\t
