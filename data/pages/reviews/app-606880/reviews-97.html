Gear is mostly for fashion (except weapon), character builds are narrow in scope, loot is nothing but mats / junk, most exploration and most locked chests reveal nothing but mats / junk. On the other hand, I had a blast playing, combat is hectic and fun (on Extreme,) and the skills are interesting and impactful even if their progression is liner, and the story was actually enjoyable. Once you've walked in the office of the governor's house in one city, you can 100% predict the layout of the next govenor's house. Same goes for taverns, barracks and the port. The story is mediocre and requires lots of back and forth walking just to trigger the next poorly lip synced dialogue sequence. The voice acting it bland. The combat is passable but uninspiring. If only the devs had taken a step back and made a smaller world with fewer characters and locales it could have created a unique world with a real story.
