\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't get me wrong, the game is Fun and with a few changes could completely change my review. I have only played a solid 2 hours so I could just be tripping, but already that was enough time to find 2 very major flaws this game NEEEDS to fix.

The first thing I noticed immediately is that the giant has almost no mobility other than physically moving around your play space due to the game not having any joystick movement controls. On some maps you are provided with some portals to change the side of the map you're on but these are very rare and didn't really feel like they were helping in most scenarios in my experience. Now for those that have a big open space to play freely, this is not an issue, but for players just playing from their own room would quickly find that they are constantly hitting, or in my case full force Falcon Punching a bottle into my wall, as the size of the maps demands A LOT of reach that the giant simply just doesn't have other than to physically reach that far, or throwing something which can only do so much. This makes having a small play area a game ruining issue and is the sole reason I will not be playing this game unless it is resolved.

Second issue is the game gives plenty of visual cues for the knights to tell what health the giant is at, but once again absolutely nothing for the Giant to see the lives or health of a knight. This I could get over as it is a great game otherwise but with this combined with the fact that I am constantly at risk of breaking something IRL makes the game as a giant player feel like fighting a swarm of flys with guns that from what you can tell can't die, and in other cases a firing squad where you aren't given enough reach to actually stop anyone from just camping the other side of the map and being nearly untouchable.

All together makes a terrible experience for what is otherwise a great unique game.\t\t\t\t\t
