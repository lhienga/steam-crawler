As Giant, I would recommend very ample space in order to cover most of the levels, although using projectiles and ranged-attacks helps you in most situations, countering rockets is essential for long range.
As Knight you have a wide range of mobility actions and uses for your rockets and maces. Rockets are a double-edged ranged attack, serve as an evasive move, or even both! Maces also work both-ways, this is great for switching up playstyles.
