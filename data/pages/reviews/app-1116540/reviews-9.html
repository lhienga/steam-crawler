\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy venture into Davigo VR as a VR giant left me with a mix of emotions. While the game caters well to free-to-play players I feel as if it is outright neglecting the player base that actually pays money certain aspects, particularly the controls and gameplay mechanics, have left me reluctant to return without significant changes.

Controls and Blocking Mechanic:
One major gripe revolves around the janky controls, notably the blocking mechanic. The small shields on the fists proved ineffective, especially when confronted by multiple opponents. To address this, I propose a dynamic shielding system: a single-handed shield remains, and activating both hands erects a shield screen covering the face, serving as a HUD for strategic insights.

Dodging and Visual Indicators:
The dodging mechanism, reliant on head movement, became problematic when facing multiple threats. An improvement could be the introduction of a visual indicator for incoming projectiles outside the direct line of sight. This adjustment aims to enhance the dodging experience without compromising the core mechanics.

Shooting and Throwing Projectiles:
Throwing objects like boxes and statues and trees felt unrewarding, often resulting in minimal impact. To fix this, I suggest a charging system for the projectile ability that is always active. Gaining charge when blocking attacks would remove the reliance on opponents to initiate charging, and a cooldown period after using the projectile could balance the mechanic.

Additional Gameplay Enhancements:
Several minor grievances include inconsistent movement mechanics, the inability of giants to temporarily disable cannons, and the absence of power-ups. Introducing power-ups, such as armor, brief invincibility, larger hands for increased damage and range, eye laser abilities, and clones that mirror my movements but cant cause harm confusing players, could add depth to the giant's arsenal without deviating significantly from the original concept.

Fin:
While Davigo VR shows promise, addressing control and gameplay issues, along with introducing subtle enhancements, could significantly elevate the gaming experience. As it stands, I hesitate to recommend this game to VR enthusiasts unless substantial improvements are made to enhance overall gameplay satisfaction.\t\t\t\t\t
