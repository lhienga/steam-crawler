\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRecently geteting back into vr and picked this up after memory holing it for a while.
Haven't had a workout like that in a while. In short the games a blast and there isn't much like it on the market.

But it has its flaws. At least from my beginners perspective the Knight seems far to strong and the Giant very weak. Unless you're throwing yourself around the room like an expert beat saber song you'll get destroyed. At least for me I really needed to nerf my friend playing the Knight for it to be a fair fight. The fist charge you get after blocking a rocket just doesn't go where you aim it at all. It also took me a while to figure out how the warping system worked where it just wan't clear at all how you were supposed to gesture. Eventually I figured it out to be as if i'm pulling the target towards me. I did also notice some odd slow downs the longer the game went on. I think I played for almost 3 hours nonstop and the game started stuttering after a while. Maybe a memory leak or something i'm honestly not sure.

Again the game is a blast but you really need to spend time finding a good rule set that plays well for you and your mates. Otherwise its just WAY to hard.\t\t\t\t\t
