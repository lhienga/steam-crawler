\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdited review because the devs added locomotion.

Solid game, fun with friends. Grabbing your friend mid air and launching him into the sun is always a good time. The devs are also responsive.

I have 3 minor gripes. First is the cannons feel a little bit unbalanced in their current state, they have a large aoe and even if you sacrifice a hand to avoid the brain damage, it sometimes destroys both your hand and damages the head. I feel like it shouldn't do any more damage than a rocket especially since it auto aims.

Second is the locomotion switches the controls (at least on the index) so now it is grip button to grab and trigger to hold on to the ground, which can be pretty confusing if you use different movement on different maps.

Third is the charge the giant gets from blocking is pretty inaccurate at any sort of range, and slow considering how fast experienced knights will be zooming around. Maybe it should be changed to a finger gun sort of thing, and show an outline of the beam while aiming so the knights can try to aim dodge, but when it fires, it should be hitscan.

Outside of these pretty minor things though, the game is a blast with your friends, 7/10 (but on the normal scale where 5/10 is an average game).\t\t\t\t\t
