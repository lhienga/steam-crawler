\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLeaving a positive review because this game definitely has potential. The game itself feels very much like an alpha concept, from the simple geometry, bareboned menu options, and minimal sound effects and music.
The good news is, the gameplay feels really good. I'm not sure if they meant for matches to be quick and easy, but I think the length of it is in a very good spot. This is the type of game you just want to load in, play a few matches, and log out to catch your breath.

I'm VERY glad they made the KNIGHTS free to play for PC and I hope it stays that way, since I think A) it's great advertisement and B) will keep a nice player base going. The price is definitely a little high for what is in it so far, but if they flesh the game out more and continue to develop, I think it can definitely deserve the price tag. Maybe flesh out the graphics a little more, even if the low polygon count is an aesthetic choice, to make the game feel less.... alpha. More/better game modes, a few tweaks to their menu options and keybinds, and maybe even a few different weapon choices that maintain true to their current gameplay.

Consider it an investment, and what you get so far is still plenty of fun. Please don't let me down devs!\t\t\t\t\t
