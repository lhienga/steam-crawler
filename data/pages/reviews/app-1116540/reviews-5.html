\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFull Review

First I want to highlight how releasing the PC client for free is so incredibly smart when it comes to games like this. A lot of people than own VR sometimes don't have friends that either A have VR for other VR multiplayer games OR B cannot convince people to pay full price for half an experience (which is very fair)

I've been playing VR games daily/weekly since 2017 and recently have been felling a bit of a dry spell in the SteamVR space. This game has been such a breath of fresh air.

Playing as the Giant:
This is the paid experience and requires a VR headset but is an incredible amount of fun, especially for a game only 2.5 months into early access. There are robust game customization like number of lives, hp total, etc as well as 4 game modes. They recently updated to include grab and pull locomotion which has been a game changer both in accessibility of the game (previously it was a static position and the PC players were dangerously far away from the VR players IRL position) If you don't might some EA jank here and there and don't mind the low poly environments it's a blast.

Playing as the Knight:
This is the free experience (also playable if you paid for the game), and you're given a fun number of tools to attempt to take the giant down. Rockets you can ride, grappling hooks, and a giant hammer to smack the giant with. There are several button triggered cannons on the maps as well as plenty of nooks and crannies to use to you're advantage. IF the VR player as the giant is just spamming attacks it can be frustrating and they clearly focus on balance tweaks regularly.

The Devs and the Discord server:
The dev's are extremely responsive in regards to technical issues and I had a great experience. The server itself needs some cleaning up and better organization as a lot of incoming users DO NOT READ and immediately make their way to general to complain about the game being dead or looking for group.

Overall I've had a lot of fun with this game. It's like playing as Giant Man in the Airport scene from civil war, it's very fun to smack people around but you can be taken out by clever knights. I highly recommend this if you have a steady group of friends to play games with. It's pretty easy to recommend a free game vs a paid one.\t\t\t\t\t
