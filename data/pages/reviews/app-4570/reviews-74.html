\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best RTS games out there, amazing attention to detail, graphics and gameplay for it's time. Set in the massive (in every literal sense of the word) universe of Warhammer 40,000 it is a great starting point for people wanting to get in on the franchise. Even tho it's old, it still holds up really well, the soundtrack and sound design overall is extremely good. I was not bored for a single second playing the campaigns, and also had lots of fun playing against the AI in Skirmish. 4 playable races, 5 with Winter Assault all of whom are very unique and have their own playing style. There are a few annoying things in the campaign, especially in Winter Assault where the difficulty feels like the AI is cheating but it's not to serious and if you're a seasoned RTS player you can go past this. No bugs or glitches to speak of, game works great on Windows 10 and modern systems (for me at least) but the screen resolution is gonna be a bit awkward cause of the game's age, still it should not keep you away from this game. There is also an Army Painter so you can create and paint your own units much like the real thing and play with those units in Skirmish or Multiplayer which is one of the most unique things about these games. Totally recommend, 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
