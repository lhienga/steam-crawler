https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=205040803

That said, this game is amazing! It is the brother game to the Starcraft series. I still own the old CD box version of this game. It's easily one of the best RTS games of all time. It's well worth the money, especially if its on sale.

All of the other Dawn of War games messed up. All they had to do was just add to this game. Instead they tried going in so many different directions that they lost their community support and gave up. Well, if Dawn of War 4 wants to be a smash hit, then make it like this game! If any game should be remastered, it is this game.

If you are looking for a Starcraft break, but still want to play and RTS, this is the game to play. All other RTS should learn from the Starcraft series and this game. This is what RTS players want, imo.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn the grim darkness of the future, there are never enough power generators.

This game is a must have for those who like real time strategy or Warhammer. Being as old as it is, it runs on any modern system and often goes on sale for only a few dollars, so there's no reason not to pick it up. Its graphics and sound effects are somewhat dated but still look fairly good, but by the Emperor the soundtrack and voice acting are still some of the best in a video game. The gameplay is still some of the best in any RTS game, and is easy to pick up but gives you lots of room to try new playstyles and strategies. The campaign is supremely enjoyable, acting as a sort of extended tutorial for the Space Marines. Skirmish mode has essentially infinite replayability, with dozens of different maps and several different gameplay styles to choose from.

Go, and earn your salvation. Salvation paid for with blood - yours, or that of your enemy.
