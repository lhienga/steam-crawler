An absolute must have for both RTS fans and Warhammer 40k fans.

Battles are frantic and intense, keeping you constantly engaged and invested. Base-building is a key strength of the game allowing for loads of player freedom. The general gameplay is also deep with different types of units that have a range of match-ups and abilities and multiple possible upgrades for said units and structures. Plus the game is chock-full of Warhammer 40k charm that fans will love.
Overall, if you are a fan of RTS's and/or 40k, this is a great game to add to your collection.\t\t\t\t\t
