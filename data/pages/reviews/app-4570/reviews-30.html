\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe IP of Warhammer can be said to be relatively popular in the world, and there are a large number of Warhammer fans all over the world. The world view of the game is also relatively complete. I have to say that Warhammer is an excellent IP, and I can almost be counted as a fan of this IP.
Now let's get to business. This is an RTS Warhammer game. The game was released after StarCraft 1 and Warcraft 3. Compared with SC1, it is much inferior. Whether it's the way the units are operated, or the AI's pathfinding capabilities, it's terrible. The resource acquisition method seems novel but very cumbersome and cumbersome. This makes the overall rhythm of the game very procrastinated, players cannot complete a game happily. Many elements in the game are imitating SC1 and WAR3, and the game's own unit squad gameplay is extremely awkward under the poor pathfinding system. The balance between units is also poor and cumbersome. Many units are completely useless decorations, coupled with poor combat rhythm, which is why Warhammer cannot become a popular competition item in WCG.
In general, no matter in terms of graphics, gameplay, and plot, compared to the popular RTS games of the same era, Warhammer seems very mediocre and bad. Whether it is StarCraft, Warcraft, Red Alert or Age of Empires, it is far more fun than it. If I go back in time to that era and let me choose an RTS game as my daily entertainment to pass the time, Warhammer is definitely my last choice. I believe that most of the people who have a good impression of this game are for its fame, not its gameplay.
This is the truth, it's a garbage RTS.
