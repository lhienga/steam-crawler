\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the title that introduced me to the Warhammer 40k universe. It was here that I discovered the treachery of chaos, the honor of the Space Marines, and the legion of Orks. I contribute this game to the the armies of Space Marines, Imperial Guard and Tau that I have gathered sense I first played, and beaten this game.

This is a wonderful RTS game that offers you base building with resource management but endless amounts of carnage and violence that rivals many other RTS games. Combat is the emphasis in this game, with unit creation, placement and counters core at the mechanics as units both have ranged and melee capabilities, not to mention abilities that give them and other units in your army advantages.

Though this game only offers 4 factions, the story is fantastic. Winter Assault's story is just as good, where you focus on the Imperial Guard. Dark Crusade is okay, along with Soulstorm, though I would argue Soulstorm is the most complete game as you get all the major factions, minus Tyranids (unless you download mods).

All in all, this is a fantastic game series and one that I would recommend to those who are fans of RTS games. You don't have to know the lore of Warhammer 40k or even be a fan to enjoy this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA good RTS that stands the test of time and that can be enjoyed if you temper your expectations and accept it's quirks.

It has decent unit variety, multiple factions, an interesting mechanic where you have to capture Strategic Points and Relics to gather the main resource, cover spots that provide defense bonuses to any troops placed in them and a good variety of upgrade choices for your units.

Unfortunately your experience may be soured by poor path-finding, units getting stuck and forgetting to go where you told them to.
It also has limited resolution options that don't pair well with modern wide/ultra-wide monitors.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played the entire campaign and therefore I think I can say, that I can't recommend this.

Firstly, the campaign.
It is sludge. Firstly, It starts out quite nicely, with the introduction of various mechanics, it makes for interesting gameplay and I would go far to say it is "fun".

However, the game does not innovate on its level design and past the first couple of missions, things get a bit... stale and tedious. It boils down to conquering a map for each mission which gets boring and quite frankly, it's bad gameplay.

If you want to play it for the multiplayer... yeah, pretty sure its dead and has been for years.

If you want to play it for nostalgic reasons, yeah nah. Save your money with this one. You will buy it like I did, play an hour of it, get bored then just not touch it again for several years, hell, I don't think I will ever play this game ever again in my life and neither would you.

Its a really old game by this point, and it definitely shows its age. Controls are clunky and the graphics (while perfectly acceptable to play with in my opinion) are really bad.

I suppose it does have some interesting mechanics, such as upgrading and modifying your troops and upgrading your base etc like any RTS game out there, it really just doesn't make up for shoddy gameplay and a dull formula

Save your money.\t\t\t\t\t
