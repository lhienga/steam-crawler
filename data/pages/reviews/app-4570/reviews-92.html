\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a masterpiece and a classic RTS. It has a great emphasis on squad based combat with weapon upgrades to individual squads (giving your bois a rocket launcher, plasma gun, etc) and a morale mechanic that encourages you to pull squads out if they break, as well as giving you an alternative way of influencing a fight through the use of flamers, snipers, and other morale breaking weapons. The game features four factions: the Space Marines, Orks, Eldar, and Chaos. Eldar are my favorite, with elite specialist units, webway gates that allow for teleportation, and lots of mystical abilities for Warlocks and the Farseer. The orks are a horde army with lots of cheap units and a unique population mechanic. They have fast light vehicles, tough heroes, and swarms of mostly melee infantry, with some inaccurate but high damage ranged units. Space Marines have flexible heavy infantry, high morale, and a wide variety of vehicles. Chaos is...admittedly very similar to the Space marines with a few demonic units and far fewer vehicles. The campaign in extremely well presented, with stellar voice acting and music, and graphics that, in my opinion, haven't aged too badly. I love this game and can't recommend it enough, my only real gripe is that the campaign difficulty is off, and I'm pretty sure it just alters unit damage and HP, which generally just makes the higher difficulties frustrating instead of challenging, but it is well worth the price, especially if you have friends to play with online.\t\t\t\t\t
