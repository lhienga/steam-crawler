Yeah apparently by going into the game files downing some things and copy pasting it over the original ones, it§ works. Yep it worked for me the day I did that. By the next day it wasn't big screen anymore, I had to play with a black zone left and right. The only good thing about the gruelsome time I had to invest earlier to make this game somewhat playable for me, was the fact that at least the text was readable. In the beginning not even the worlds greatest decipher could decipher for it was not readable. Now don't get me wrong, although I say readable, it really wasn't either since it was microscopically small.

By moving my desk just in front of the TV and watching some YT vidz and reading some guides, I was able to play and enjoy this game. I only hoped for improvements with the DLC's.

Sadly Winter Assault had an even smaller campaign, instead of 10 missions, you only get 5. Whereas in Dawn of War you play with THE space marines, in Winter Assault you don't play the space marines, no you play the Imperial Guard (yeah F the new term Astrum Militarum, it's called Imperial Guard here anyway). I did it anyway, because after playing those 5 missions, I could play the bad guys containing the Chaos Space marines. I was hyped for that, but that hype soon died out when it was clear you play the same short 5 mission campaign as the bad guys now. How original... djeezus what a disappointment tbh.

Up to the next then! Dark Crusade... yeah Dark Crusade doesn't even have a campaign, it does have a conquest mode which looks appealing and cool. But playing these skirmish battles wasn't my thing really. I soon discovered I'm a F noob that doesn't know how to play this game. While I didn't looseeasily, I just wasn't able to win, I couldn't breathe, I was relentlessly under attack, wave after wave after wave. So I had to look up for some tips and guides + I started a new on easy. Easy is just way to easy, the AI barely attacks you. All while my readable textlines are still microscopically small. I expected to find some YT channels fully explaining everything etc. but sadly it seems like the game predates the current way of YT coverage of a game. So atm I quitted playing, but I did enjoyed it and I'll pick it up later since I still need to play the last DLC and try out Dawn of War 2 and the 2 DLC's it has. Even the infamous Dawn of war 3 will be reviewed 1 day.

Even though the serie got dropped, I hope some studio will pick it up again and bring us a Dawn of War 4 in all it's glory! Stronger & better then ever, for the Emperor! RTS will make a comeback, I'm sure it will, it's time for RTS to make a comeback. After all Starcraft 2 got killed by their own developers lol, F Blizzard, bunch of corporate men in suit only thinking of milking out their playerbase and pleasing their rich shareholders.
