\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOn sale for 75% off so I picked up the base game and each of the expansions. I've played through the first campaign and I'm on the 2nd to last mission of Winter Assault. Over all it's a solid RTS game and when it goes on sale it could make a worthy addition to your library.

What do I say about a playing a game 17 years after its release? The unit animations are good. When a dreadnought picks up an infantry unit and shakes it around like a rag doll it looks good. The unit pathing is 'ok' given the need for individual soldiers to spread out and assemble like a parade. Vehicles can get stuck at points and need some micro management to get back on track. The campaign is relatively short and sweet in contrast to some other RTS games but there isn't any annoying escort quests or fail states like having to restart a mission if a named character is downed. Reinforcing/replenishing your squads is handled at the squad level so as long as there is one survivor you're a minute or two and some clicks from being back to full strength. The other side of it is you need to peruse your units to reinforce and repurchase whatever heavy weapons they may have been carrying. It happen often so there's a lot of reinvesting in your existing units. The UI is a little clunky and janky but nothing too bad.\t\t\t\t\t
