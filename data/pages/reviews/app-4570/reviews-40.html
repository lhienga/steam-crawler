It's a simple RTS where you set a base, grow a small army and go to the enemy territory to kill its units. Simple as that, but, one factor here that some will find very interesting and some won't, is the difficulty. Even in Normal in a couple of missions if you're a first sailor trip, you will struggle. Some missions you can't just wait for your base to build an army, you have to attack and be multi-task asf. Some missions if you don't destroy their tank factories you will have to restart, because manly the orks get you with their tanks pretty fast.
