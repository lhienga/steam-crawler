\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe 41st Millennium is known as many things, including the Age of Space Empires.
Dawn of War is a classic entry into the RTS genre and if you're here as a Warhammer fan who likes strategy games you don't need reviews. The game works, just buy it.

Gameplay: Exactly what you expect and want from a base-builder RTS.
Story: Simple and predictable, wasn't a cliché back then but totally is now. But who cares, it's fun.
Sound: Plenty of Dakka noises.
Compatibility: Still runs well on modern system with little to no compatibility issues. Doesn't natively do high res/ wider aspect ratios. Can be tweaked but takes time and may not be worth bothering.

Bonus points: Still has the best intro cinematic ever.\t\t\t\t\t
