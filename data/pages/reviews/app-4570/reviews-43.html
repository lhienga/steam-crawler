\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very good game with excellent game play. On the downside it shows its age. Graphics are okay but not great. Units sometimes become log jammed with each other and stop progressing to the destination. The map screen is odd in that there is an outer map and an inner map. You can not click to a location on the outer map from the inner map. You have to scroll there. In one mission you start with 3 whirlwind missile launchers and 2 jump jet squads to take out an orc base and establish your own. However, the Whirlwinds will not fire on the base so you have to figure out a way of defeating the whole base with just the 2 squads. I was able to do after I realized that there was no way to get the whirlwinds to work. Time consuming but doable. The characters in the cut scenes wear not the smoothest in movement or form but funny in a strange way. This series could use a face lift like Microsoft Definitive Editions and it could be great again. This is the first of a series and the later games make strong improvements. Still thumbs up.\t\t\t\t\t
