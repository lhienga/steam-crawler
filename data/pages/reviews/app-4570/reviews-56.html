\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, this has been a long long time coming, as I have played DOW since the beginning. And to this day, it is hard to top the original DOW series.

Yes, the graphics my be getting a little long in the tooth these days, but is more than serviceable for any RTS enthusiast. The sound however is exceptional, the voice acting in particular really embrace the themes of 40k. But the game-play is RTS 101+1. Building, troop and resource management just like many RTS games that came before it. However, DOW really perfected the overall experience by making these mechanics very streamlined, with some fantastic upgrade systems to boot!

I revisited the first title only a little while ago and I was surprised with how much I enjoyed the single player campaign. I like Warhammer, but am far from a die hard fan. So it was a relief to find that the story was engaging from start to finish. With an over arching conspiracy hook lived through the stoic Blood Ravens, I enjoyed it very much.

If you are a RTS fan and haven't played the first few games in this series, you are missing out on gaming history! For anyone else just wanting to revisit this classic, my gosh, the price is stupid low and well worth it!\t\t\t\t\t
