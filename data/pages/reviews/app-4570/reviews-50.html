Its resource based skirmish gameplay should be complemented with its DLC (Dark Crusade for Crazy fun and Soulstorm for MP and Balancing), without it is overly simple. Even with the DLC after a couple dozen games, it leaves the player wanting more which they will not find replica gameplay in both of its sequels. Unfortunately it parallels to acting as a life support for a dying game. As much as I love 40k, Dawn of War will not survive another year or two without a new game.
