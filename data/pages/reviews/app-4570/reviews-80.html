\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a few bucks, sure I'll buy this game. But if I had bought this when it was released I would have been absolutely furious. For the first game set in the 40k universe, the RTS gameplay mechanics are absolutely standard. There are very few things that set this apart from any other generic RTS of its time.

But really, the biggest crime is 4 teams. Again, bringing the 40k universe to the PC for the first time, and you release the game with 4 teams, at full price, with every expansion costing full price from then on. If the game is unfinished (don't get me started on the blandness of the campaign missions) then it's pure dishonesty to release this to a loyal audience of Warhammer fans.

I played this for the campaign and put it away. At this point the story is the only thing actually immersing me in the universe with this game. What a shame.\t\t\t\t\t
