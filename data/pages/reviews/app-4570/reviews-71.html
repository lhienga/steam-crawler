\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is along with Company of Heroes 1 pioneer in a new way of playing RTS. This game is better than Dawn of war 2 and 3 combined. Even though it is very old, it still has one of the best atmospheres in the games, and this revolutionary way of playing RTS. So, there is no more resources that you need to mine. You collect resources by holding strategic points.

Also, you do not command every soldier, you command the squad now. Squad will act on command, in formations. Also, when soldiers die, you can reinforce the squad even if you have only 1 soldier left. You can also assign a lieutenant to the squads, and he gives them extra morale and courage, plus some other minor benefits.

You can play as Marine, Chaos Marine, Orcs and Eldars. I think that you need to buy some of the DLC's in order to play Eldar, but I'm not 100% sure. It was long time ago when I was buying DLC's. This game also brought the whole Warhammer 40k Hype, that escalated in every possible way, gaming wise. Every genre of games has now W40K game, but I rarely meet as good W40 game as this one.

Purge the unclean, burn the Heretic. You won't regret investing money and time in this game.\t\t\t\t\t
