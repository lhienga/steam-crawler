\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed the Demo and loved it, just finished the game on launch day and it was the best $3 I've ever spent! Super cute art, simple but fun management gameplay, and overall just sweet li'l street builder. Finished all 5 story quests in 2.5 hours but there is creative mode as well which adds to potential playtime, and honestly for a couple bucks I'm not at all mad about how short it is, because I thoroughly enjoyed playing!

Overview:

5 Main Quests - each quest has a handful of objectives + additional optional objectives to achieve. 2-4 hours playtime (as stated by developer + my own experience)
Creative mode - unlimited playtime!
Street Builder - 12 buildings to unlock, all are customizable & upgradable (lots of potential combinations!)
Management - Build shops to give your street an income to build more buildings and keep your neighbors happy buy stocking their favorite things. There is a puzzle aspect to figuring out who likes what, and sometimes you'll need more than one of each store to keep everyone satisfied. Keep your streets clean and your neighborhood beautiful to attract furry friends<3\t\t\t\t\t
