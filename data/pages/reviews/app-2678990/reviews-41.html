\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI purchased my first game on Steam and it turned out to be a delightful experience. The art style of Minami Land is incredibly charming. It's a small game where you can build your own street, customize buildings, and manage your shops to keep your villagers happy. The missions offer just the right amount of challenge without being overly difficult. The appearance of adorable cats adds to the enchantment of the game. The background music is pleasant and adds to the relaxing atmosphere of this enjoyable little game. Despite the developer's statement that there won't be further updates, I still hope for more content after completing the game. Looking forward to Doot & Blibloop's future works.\t\t\t\t\t
