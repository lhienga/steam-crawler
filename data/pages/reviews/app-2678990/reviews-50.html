\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10/10 - highly recommend. It's a nice little management game that has a good balance between demanding my attention, upgrades that let me stop paying attention, and cats that force me to slow down and let the game progress a little slower so I can get all the petting in. In other games of this ilk, I like to speed time up to just get the days over with, but the pet the cat mechanic here has me letting the in-game time tick by at a normal pace, just so I make sure I don't miss a cat. As the street gets longer, this gets harder, but it's something to obsess over while the rest of the game does its thing for the day.

Pros:
+easy to pick up
+great game for the price point
+pet the cats
+art style is exactly what I want from the game
+music is chill

Cons:
-what if I don't pet every cat?!
-the emotional damage that would cause me\t\t\t\t\t
