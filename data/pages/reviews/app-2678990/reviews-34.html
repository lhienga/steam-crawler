\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is really cute and adorable. I love the artwork, aesthetics and music. There are many issues that I have with this game though:

1) One straight line instead of allowing players to build in more directions
2) Not enough missions
3) A few fixed colours. Red, yellow, green, brown. That's it.
4) Shop pricing is difficult. I do not understand why some customers say the price is "low" and some of them say it is "fair". Is that a good thing? Or is it not? I have revised my prices to experiment and cater to both cases, yet it is still very unclear what the real price should be.
5) Inability to allow players to swap existing buildings. I don't want to demolish the building. I want to have the option to just change their location or swap it with another building's location.
6) Getting 10/10 recipe (or selection) and price satisfaction is actually REALLY, REALLY difficult. Unnecessarily so, and very confusing. Even with the survey help, which I found barely helpful. That last achievement will be out of my grasp. I highly suspect that this 10/10 thing is an actual technical bug because I never seem to be able to get 10/10 despite changing based on survey feedback and changing many, many times.

As much as I would love to support this game, I am unable to recommend this game at its current state. I feel that the price tag, even with a discount on a sales day, does not justify the limited content and technical issues in the game as I have stated above. It is quite a repetitive game that does get boring fast a mere 3 hours in and has low replayability. I was left desiring for much more than its current state that feels a bit too bare bones for me.

If there are more updates eventually that adds more buildings and features, as well as resolve the issue in the last bullet point that I have stated above, I would be willing to reconsider my review again.\t\t\t\t\t
