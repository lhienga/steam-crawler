\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMinami Lane is a cute and chill but short and minimalistic game. You contruct housing, business and leisure buildings. Housing increases your population, business sells products to your citizens and leisure increases their satisfaction. There are two types of residents, youth and seniors and they have their own product preferences. Which in practical terms, means you build 2x of each business to cater to either youth or seniors. Leisure increases the population's satisfaction which is required to build certain buildings. Very simple mechanics and not much depth.

It doesn't take too long to complete the five missions in the game, ~3-4 hours should have them and achievements done. Overall, there's no actual challenge. The missions are straightforwards and easy and optional challenges are not difficult to finish. There's no concern in losing money, you'll always be gaining money. There's also a sandbox if you just want to build your own cute little lane - there's a normal option with standard restrictions and a creative mode without any money or building restrictions.

Didn't notice any bugs or glitches during my playthrough, everything seemed pretty smooth.

It's a nice experience if you're just looking for a relaxing game without much depth and isn't particularly expensive.\t\t\t\t\t
