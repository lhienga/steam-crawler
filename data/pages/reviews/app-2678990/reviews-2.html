\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMinami Lane is a really cute simple management game with 5 missions (levels) to complete, each being increasingly more complex than the last, and two endless sandbox modes. I truly enjoyed playing this game and I found myself easily sinking hours into it without realising. The mission objectives are straightforward so you know exactly what your end goal is, but there are often many ways to go about achieving those goals depending on how you prioritise things which does allow replayability (especially in the later missions!) I especially loved how every building had cute colour customisation options, and the overall aesthetics of the game had me in a very relaxed and wholesome mood. Plus, in later levels you can just automate things and spend your time petting cats ^-^ I do wish that there were more missions to complete or a harder difficulty level, but overall the game is super satisfying and adorable!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an incredibly sweet game! It brought me so much joy in the short time I had with the campaign. There are just enough mechanics to keep it feeling fresh without being overwhelming. The addition of the "daily life" interactions also mean you don't have to disengage and idly sit through the animations of the day, though you could if you wanted to. The art style, animations, music, interactions, character behavior and thoughts, all of it is wrapped up into this piece of art that truly made me happy to play. Absolutely recommend this game, especially to anyone looking for a bright and uplifting escape.\t\t\t\t\t
