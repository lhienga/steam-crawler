\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is really cute! I really enjoyed the visual look and feel of the game. For me though, the gameplay was not what I expected. From reading the steam description, I expected to have more control over the resturaunts on the street and have it play more like a street/resturaunt manager with cozy asthetics and little challenges to complete with customization and decorating. When all I could do was buy a few buildings, change the color of them, and set prices for food/pick up trash... I was kind of surprised and disappointed. It didn't feel like there was enough to do. I've read other comments stating that the game opens up a little more with a few more customization options and mechanics as you progress further through the levels. But for me, I just personally couldn't make it through level one. I really enjoy the look and feel of the game. But the gameplay just isn't for me. Maybe if more depth or content came out for the game, I'd reconsider. But for now it feels too bare bones and feels like something I'd play on mobile.\t\t\t\t\t

I appreciate the up-front transparency on this--Steam has us so trained to expect games as a never-ending live service in which devs are beholden to players who expect them to constantly churn out new content and updates. If the devs do choose to update/add to the game in the future, I'd love to experience a new level or two. And if not, I hope it's because they might be working on something completely new for us to enjoy. <3\t\t\t\t\t
