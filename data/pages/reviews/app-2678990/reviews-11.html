\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo much potential but overall, really lacking a lot.

I'm on the fence. Whilst its a great game, great idea, with so much life in it and really polished, gosh it is lacking so much.

Why I don't recommend it is:
> Huge lack of creative input with houses and placement - you can only place houses in a row, one after the other; lack of colour and decoration choice for buildings; cannot edit any other aspects (roads, bikes, people, etc.) besides the buildings you place
> Replayabilitiy is limited - only 5 short, easy mission (it took me under an hour to finish the whole campaign. That is... kinda insane for me); once you have completed the campaign, no other routes to take or to try out; has sandbox, but whats the point when you have not much else to do besides repeat what you did in the campaign?
> Having only two kinds of citizens - forces you to repeat the same actions, reducing creativity and challenge, and making it out to be a chore which results in the same outcome every single day

What I can suggest is to possibly hold out for the devs to add in more content, which I really hope they do, as this game deserves loves. But coming from someone who loves having some kind of creative hand in a virtual world, this is a game I'm going to quickly forget about.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, this game was really fun and the style and art of it is so cute and yes it's very cozy. But it wasn't what I expected at all. I suppose it's fine for the $5 I paid but the game is awfully repetitive and I wish there were more challenges? I beat the game in 3 hours and while I can technically keep playing, I think I'll only touch it again when I'm in a cozy mood just wanting to play something where I can turn my brain off.

Basically you're given challenges to complete but all you do is just place a building and repeat the days over and over while trying to tweak things like a ramen/boba shop's menu or the books they sell at a bookstore to everyone's liking. You don't actually get to do anything beyond that. You just hit begin and watch people go on their day, occasionally picking up trash they leave behind for extra money or click on people to see their shop reviews. Eventually though, you get a management building that does this for you, so you don't get to do anything except fast forward through a day to end it to see if you completed the challenge or not, rinse and repeat.

It gets boring extremely fast and I was so excited to play this. I guess if you have $5 to spare you can pick this up, but I'll give it a pass.\t\t\t\t\t
