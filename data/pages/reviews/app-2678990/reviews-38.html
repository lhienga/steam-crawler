\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAdorable game. Worth the price for something to just sit and relax with for a few hours. The artwork is very sweet and the color pallet is soothing. My only complaint.. and hopefully I'm the minority on this one. After about an hour of play my neck gets really stiff. Since the street is illustrated on a 45 degree angle I end up tilting my head to level my eyes with the sidewalk. If each new mission would reorientation the street or if I had the ability to change it's direction on my own it would be a more pleasant overall experience. I hope they expand on the missions to allow for more game play!\t\t\t\t\t
