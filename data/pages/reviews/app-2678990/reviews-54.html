\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe world needs more games that are perfectly curated experiences like Minami Lane. I'm not the kind of person who enjoys management games all that much, but before I knew it, I had been playing for almost 2 hours and even restarted a mission so I could complete the optional objective I had missed. The art style is cute and charming, which is wonderfully complemented by the great music and sound design. The gameplay itself is just challenging enough to suck you in, yet not too much that it ever gets to the point of frustration. No performance issues to speak of, even on my decade-old potato PC. The price is absolutely fair for the amount of content, and I appreciate just how tight of a package Minami Lane is overall. Even if you think management gameplay is not normally your thing, I highly recommend checking it out anyway. The developers really knocked it out of the park with this one!\t\t\t\t\t
