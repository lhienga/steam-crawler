\r\n\t\t\t\t\tsuch an adorable game, the design is stunning and i love the fact you do have some creative control in your street - picking the roof shape, colour and base colour might not seem like a lot but it makes for the most adorable streets

you get to pet cats which is amazing

sadly i do have some negatives, especially with navigating your street once it grows - it's too big and you either play on the slowest speed or miss a lot of things like trash, petting cats or waving at cyclists

the fact you can only buy one building per day is a great challenge at first but once you have more money daily it gets frustrating, you can however update how many buildings you please if they are still up for upgrading

it's a shame the sandbox wasn't really appealing for me so once i finished the challenges i was like "welp, that's it" but i would love it if maybe there were more challenges or maybe someone made them like people do for the sims

the game is truly so cozy and adorable and worth the money even if you just play the challenges and leave it to rest, it's an afternoon well spent\t\t\t\t\t
