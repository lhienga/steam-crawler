\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMinami Lane is an absolute gem - I discovered it through a wholesome games account on Instagram and couldn't be happier. The visuals are stunning and drew me in right away. It's a delightful management-simulator that strikes the perfect balance of challenge and relaxation. I adore customizing the buildings, and petting the cats is the ultimate joy! The music is delightful, and the cute sound effects add an extra layer of enjoyment to the gameplay. The price point is fantastic for what you get, but I'm eagerly hoping for more updates in the future: It would be fantastic if we could move buildings around freely, especially as the lane grows longer over time. Nonetheless, I haven't even delved into the sandbox mode yet, which seems perfect for those seeking a laid-back gaming experience.\t\t\t\t\t
