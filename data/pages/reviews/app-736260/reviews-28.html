\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRating 7/10

Pros: Baba is You is an ingenious puzzle game, however, one that may be too good. It's puzzles are clever and ramps up the difficulty somewhat well although not perfectly, which is impossible for a puzzle game with such a large scope. The game is mostly non-linear meaning if you're really stuck on one puzzle you could switch to a different one although I rarely did this. The best part of the game is when you get that eureka moment on a difficult puzzle and it all finally clicks into place.

Neutral: The game is definitely aimed more towards very hardcore puzzle gamers which I am somewhat. I wouldn't recommend this game to a puzzle novice as it gets quite extreme quite quickly, Even for me, sometimes I thought I had solved a puzzle and then realised that wasn't even close to the answer. At times it felt like I had to dig five layers deep to find the actual solution to the puzzle which can be quite exhausting if you get a bunch of brain-numbing puzzles in a row. I managed to push through slowly but surely all the way up to the depths area. At that point, I just felt like I had had enough of the game and didn't bother searching for how to enter the meta area. The game's length also exacerbates this fact being lengthy by puzzle game standards.\t\t\t\t\t
