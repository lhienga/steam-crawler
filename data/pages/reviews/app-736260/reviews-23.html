\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBaba is You is a great game. Baba is You is a ruthlessly hard puzzle game where almost every puzzle seems impossible at some point. After the early game, there's always a point in every level where I feel like I've understood the situation and I think "Wow, so this level is just genuinely impossible." Then I end up having a eureka moment later and solving the puzzle.
I bought Baba is You about 3 years ago, and it honestly changed my perspective on how I see problems and obstacles. Whenever I feel stuck, or incapable of accomplishing something, I think back to my time when I used to play Baba is You, and every obstacle that seemed impossible became possible.
There's a level in Baba is You titled "Insulation" that I spent about 4 hours on across 4 days. I reopened the level the next day, and solved it within 2 minutes of having it open. The joy I felt wasn't a sudden rush of euphoria, but rather feeling of fulfillment and competence. I had been working on this level for so long, I was genuinely worried that I just wasn't smart or creative enough to come up with the solution. Then, I proved myself wrong again.
So yeah, I would recommend the game. You can play on controller, and you can pick it up at any time. It's a great game to just open for about 10 minutes and then close. Have fun.\t\t\t\t\t
