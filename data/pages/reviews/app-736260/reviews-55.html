\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBaba is You is a puzzle game with a simple premise, which works out really well. Something that this game does really well is that puzzle solutions are generally very strongly connected with an idea or concept that you have to discover to solve the level. Some puzzle games have levels where you it feels like you have to stumble around trying a ton of stuff to solve a level, which feels pretty unsatisfying even when you do solve it. There are many levels in this game where I have to stop, take my hands off the keyboard, and think about the puzzle, or I might figure out the solution while taking a break from the game.
The developer also added in the ability to create and distribute custom levels, which makes the possible play time effectively infinite. Baba is You is definitely my favorite puzzle game: if you like the genre or are new to it, I think it's worth buying for sure. It's one of the few games that has a really new and unique thing to offer.\t\t\t\t\t
