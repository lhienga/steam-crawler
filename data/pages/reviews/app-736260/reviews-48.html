\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBy far the most innovative puzzle game I've ever played. Portal redefines movement and transportation of objects, Superliminal makes illusions come to life, and many other great puzzle games have incredible main mechanics. This one tops all of them. Easily.
This game uses its clever mechanic of text setting the in-game rules of the world and lets you interact with and rewrite the rules of the world. Baba doesn't have to be You. Rock can be Win. Flag can be Death. Skull can be Sink. There are zero conventional limits. The only things that can stop you are the map geometry and your own imagination.
You'll use this incredibly cool idea to solve some of the toughest puzzles I've ever seen, and progress through the heartwarming, fun, and strange world of Baba. Tons of fun to be had here with a game with theoretically infinite potential. 10/10.\t\t\t\t\t
