The playstile is really simple and self explanatory with some creative game mechanics.
In order to be successful you have to combine words into commands which change the way a level behaves and your goal is to reach a flag or anything you want.
This game is definitly worth playing and has a lot of content and possibilities of what you can do in each level. In contrast some level might seem a bit too dificult or confusing by their structure which can be frustrating (except if you use a webpage in the aftermath to solve too hard levels).
