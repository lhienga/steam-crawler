\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI haven't watched any smart game design video about Baba is You but when I saw the meme that goes:
Level Too Hard
Baba Is Done
Better Watch Out
Baba Has Gun

I knew that I had to get it. And it's pretty fun. Very much programming puzzle with in-game scripting. Actually really fun at the start. Chill music too.

But it does do the puzzle games thing where as a reward for beating a puzzle you only get even harder puzzle which isn't a gameloop I can do. Plus I am too dumb, being more busy with job and lazy outside of it. So yea, I only got through 1/3 of the game.

Probably the weakest part of the game is same as some of weaker problems of programming gamedev: unclear order of things per each frame. Will WALL IS STOP take priority or WALL IS WIN? Will I get pwned or saved by a skull? Who knows, it's pretty unclear each time.

Still, very proud of Finland dev boy, most excellent, it's fun, cute, but really, this went over my head too quickly.\t\t\t\t\t
