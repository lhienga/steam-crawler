\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBaba is you is a simple puzzle game where what you write becomes the rules of the game. You can push words to create new combinations and change the world around you. If you change "water is drown" to "water is float", suddenly you're able to walk right past a previously deadly obstacle. It's an easy concept that can be explained even to someone who's not familiar with videogames.

And this is exactly what happened to me! As i played, all my family gathered around me and we brainstormed how to finish each level, which was the most fun i ever had with a game for a long while. At one point, we had been stuck in the same level for about three hours. I desperately wanted to look up the answer to be able to move on, but my cousin refused to let go of his honor, and convinced me to try for a little longer. We then found out that if we just looked at things from a slightly different angle, we would be able to see the answer that was right before our eyes since the beginning!

Baba is you is full of these "A-HA!" moments that make finishing a level so enjoyable. The puzzles are the perfect balance between challenging and doable. The new levels are increments of knowledges you adquire with each past one you finish. No game has ever made staring at the same screen for three hours so much fun for me, and i think Baba is You deserves credit for that.\t\t\t\t\t
