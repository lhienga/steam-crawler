\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoving this game, the design is so simple and unique, while being very cutesy. This is definitely the best puzzle game I've ever played, even if I've put it down a few times and had to come back later.
Playing it with my bf right now and we love it so much, the difficulty is definitely hard and my bf got his brain fried a few times but we've managed to beat the first 4 stages completely(all the extras).
If we can do it, you can too. We're not overly smart thinkers, you just need to constantly understand that this is a game where you're supposed to think outside the box and use the mechanics in a way you never thought of before. Hypothesize, test, fail, repeat, until success.
Anyone who complains that it's "too" hard are critiquing that a puzzle game is too puzzling. It's totally fine to take a break from a level and come back later with a fresh mind, that's sometimes the only way to make progress as it can feel like smashing your head against a brick wall otherwise.

Only gripe I have about the game is that most levels in a stage we breeze through, only taking 5-8 minutes at most. But then we get to a couple where we can be stuck for a half hour to 2 hours.
That said it's a great game and I can't wait to play it again, and eventually be among the 4% who have beaten the whole thing.\t\t\t\t\t
