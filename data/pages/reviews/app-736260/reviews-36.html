\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is based on an old sokoban game. But instead of having objects with fixed properties, such as you can only push rock, you have special text blocks, which placed in particular order define rules. The name of the game "Baba is You" actually shows three of the blocks that when placed correctly mean that you control Baba. What makes this puzzle interesting is that by moving the text blocks around, you can change the winning condition, or what objects you control, or can walk through. There are several different interactions between objects and more varieties of text blocks introduced in latter puzzles. This keeps the game interesting and requires you to come up with new and unique ways to solve the puzzle that have not been used earlier.

Most puzzles range from moderate to very hard. I'm still stuck on a few of them. But you are not required to finish every single puzzle to progress or finish the game. You can actually skip a lot of puzzles and return to them later.

I definitely recommend this unique game to anyone who likes puzzle games.\t\t\t\t\t
