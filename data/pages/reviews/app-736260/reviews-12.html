\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's charming and fun, I enjoy it a lot. It's very satisfying when you finally figure out the solutions, and I think the mechanic of being able to change the game rules is very unique and fun to mess around with

Unfortunately for me, I've had to look up quite a few hints/guides because this game's difficulty curve is more of a wall. It was fine for the first 2-3 worlds, but it very quickly introduces unexplained mechanics and solutions that the average person probably wouldn't think of. Sometimes I don't even feel bad looking up the answer because I see it and think "I can do that??" since it had some obscure mechanic that was never introduced.

Even so, I think most of the puzzles I've played thus far are well designed and creative. I just wish some of them had more than one solution... with a game that allows you to bend the rules, you'd think that would be the case more often, but no.

I'd recommend it if you're good with puzzles, because it's anything but beginner friendly\t\t\t\t\t
