\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't let the 8-bit artwork and music fool you: this game is hard, and at some points it gets really hard.

After 1-2 hour of tutorialish gameplay difficulty increases drastically, there are new rules all over the levels and you cannot even think your first moves to progress. For me some levels take hours to complete, it could be even more if I didnt say forget it and look at the youtube walkthrough. And if you think normal levels are hard there are secret levels unlocked some point in the game, which might makes you hate this game.

I understand this game wasn't made for casual gamers, but rather hardcore puzzle game players. But that "Hahaha look at that cute doggo pushing texts with his head!" vibes are kinda deceiving, and I guess I am not the only one since there are so many others who found this game difficult after playing.

Good game, but I recommend only if you are good... no, expert at puzzle games.\t\t\t\t\t

A brain breaking puzzle which I absolutely love. I love being able to change the rules of the level and the HAUYGEUYIGOSDUYGYU HOW THE HECK DO I WIN? feeling that eventually turns into HUGASUYASUYDGEWUI HOW DID I WIN AND HOW DID I NOT THINK OF THIS EARLIER satisfying sensation I get when I finally do complete a puzzle.
