\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game reminds me a lot of coding (and that's what it is in a nutshell). You're presented with a few codes (Baba is You, Rock is Push, etc) in the form of short sentences and it's your job to rearrange these codes to achieve your goal, which is to get Baba to the flag. Changing the codes changes the rules of the game.

Rock is Push
becomes
Rock is Baba.

Now Baba is a rock. (Not helpful, but that's an example.)

Gets more complicated as you go along where you can have longer codes/sentences (Rock is Key and Win) and makes you think harder for some than others. Some you think are supposed to be hard, but like in a math test, you're overthinking and the answer is simple.

Great for people who like to code and/or do logic puzzles.\t\t\t\t\t
