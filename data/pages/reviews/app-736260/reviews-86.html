Baba Is Fun, Sintag Make Review

Baba Is You is a puzzle game where you play as Baba*, a ewe who must reach a flag* to win the level.
*You're not always Baba, and it's not always a flag.

Plot Is NOT Here.

Gameplay revolves around rules. The game's rules are malleable, as in they're physical objects that you can shove around to alter rules, make new rules, break rules, add descriptors to rules, and more. This is the central conceit of the game, and it executes it masterfully. From the trailer, we can change Baba Is You to Rock Is You, and now you're a rock that has to go to a flag. This is just the bare basics, and the game's exact wording, loopholes, unwritten rules that are active unless NOT, and the puzzles all of this makes, mean Baba Is You is a fiendish little puzzler. I won't spoil any details here, but just know that it gets patently insane. Thankfully, there's an 'undo' and 'reset' button on standby if you have irrevocably screwed up (usually by breaking 'Is You' with nothing to be You)

Graphics are pixellated goodness with a sort of 'wobble' to them, with playable characters having just two states of motion, similar to old Mega Man titles, with both states having said 'wobble' to them. This is good. They're simple, but the graphics lend a cutesy charm to the entire game that makes it adorable and innocent.

Performance-wise, you could probably run this on a Samsung fridge. Moving on.

Multiplayer Is Empty.

Baba Is You is well worth the asking price. It's a difficult, but fun puzzle game that rewards lateral thinking with the rules in play, and it's charming as heck besides. Even if you're like me and you absolutely suck at puzzle games, it's a simple, fun game that wants you to have a good time.\t\t\t\t\t
