\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInsanely difficult minimalist puzzle game. It may not be the best game for those who get easily frustrated when stuck on a level. However, you usually have various levels available so you can try a different one if you get stuck on any particular level. Price may seem a bit high given the simple graphics, but you are getting hundreds of levels and a level editor, so it's worth it in my opinion. The game concept is great and well executed.

Update: after completing the first four chapters without guides or any external help, I've got to say this is by far one of the best logic games I've ever played. Some of the solutions will blow your mind when you find them. Other puzzle games are more frustating and will have you looking for the solution on the web, but this game is worth of your patience, it's very rewarding.\t\t\t\t\t
