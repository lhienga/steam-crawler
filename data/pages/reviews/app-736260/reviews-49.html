\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not going to do the usual "BABA IS GOOD AND CLEVER" word arrangements to describe the game because everyone's probably already done that in all ways imaginable and I've already done it to some of my friends. But chances are my praise of the game will blend in with the woodwork too because of that "Overwhelmingly Positive" by the review count! Which is well-deserved, by the way.

The game is so simple to pick-up that the bare-bones trailer tells you everything about how it's played through a few simple movements and no descriptions whatsoever; how can I possibly beat that? If you've ever played FLUXX, the card game that's all about altering the rules and win conditions to your advantage, this game is kind of like that! You create simple phrases by pushing word blocks together which change your OR other objects' OR even the geography's appearance, traits, and movements on the fly to create pathways, traverse obstacles, and form other phrases, ultimately working your way to (or even creating if necessary) the goal.

It's so approachable and easy to jump into or out of at a moment's notice because it loads quickly, and while it can take you hours to figure out how to solve a single puzzle, once you figure out the solution, it can be done from start to finish in less than a minute. And if you make a mistake, you can undo as many movements as you like with the press of a button. And you will use it a lot, because rearranging phrases in ways that breaks your control or removes an essential object from the field is all part of the experimentation process.

It's a ton of fun priced just right. And don't be daunted by people saying "IT'S SO DIFFICULT" or the associated tag, I assure you the puzzles are clever and fair-- some just have an extra amount of cleverness to them. And my advice is if you're ever feeling like a puzzle requires an extensive, complex Rube Goldberg machine of words and objects all synergizing with precision timing and positioning to solve it, chances are you're overthinking it. But smart is Baba and Baba is you.

Crap, I did the word thing.\t\t\t\t\t
