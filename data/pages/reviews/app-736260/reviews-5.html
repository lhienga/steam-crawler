\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBaba is love!

If you like puzzle games, especially challenging ones, I highly recommend you Baba is you. Idea is simple: you move around blocks with words to create rules, rules defy what you can or cannot do in a certain level: what will defeat you, what will grant you victory, what can be moved and how and more... And of course - who are you! Baba is you, or is it?

This game makes you think outside the box. I haven't completed the whole game yet, but I've already encountered a few levels in which solution felt almost like a bug! I even went to check on the internet if my solution was really correct once, because I was sure that I somehow tricked the game... Yep. It was, indeed, the correct answer. So, in other words, solutions are very creative sometimes.

Every area grants you new blocks and new rules, so there's always more to discover!

Brain is hot
Baba is you!\t\t\t\t\t
