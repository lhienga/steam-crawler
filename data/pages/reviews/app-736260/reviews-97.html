\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have made it my life's goal to finish the game and get all the achievements in it. I am 68.8 hours in (in 6th level of meta), I have repeatedly felt like a massive moron, because I have spent HOURS on a single level and its solution only to find it by accident because OF COURSE I am too dumb to find it on my own.

BUT, in the rare case you do figure out a level on your own, it is SOOOOO satisfactory. Unless it is an elementary solution which most of the time it is, and you still feel dumb.

This game will cure ANY arrogance out of you. It will make you feel humble LIKE NO OTHER. It is a monstrosity of a game, the hardest and demotivating puzzle game there is on Steam. And I will f0king finish it ON MY OWN without looking at hints and I will PROUDLY put it on my CV and cherish this achievement above my only child and all my accolades at work.

p.s.
If you are like me, take a break. For me these 68 hours have been distributed across ~2 years or something. To keep your sanity you need to take a break once in a while.

edit: As of July 28, 2023 I have finished this game. I have got all the achievements and I know there is one more level in Meta, which is the last level I haven't accessed and I suspect where it is, but I will access it later, since it isn't tied to winning the game/getting the achievements. Enough for now. What a monumental task. No other puzzle game even comes CLOSE to the level of difficulty this game has. Unbelievably hard. I was stuck in Meta level 3 for like 5 hours (and I have to admit, I found the solution mostly by accident, but what is important I PERSEVERED) and I was stuck trying to get to extra 1 in meta for around 10 hours or maybe even more. My main issue was that I was trying solutions that are more complex than what the game intended me to do. Fresh restarts also helped. I took almost an 8 month break before I attempted to finish it again. What a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ game, hats off to the guy that made this, what an absolute legend.\t\t\t\t\t
