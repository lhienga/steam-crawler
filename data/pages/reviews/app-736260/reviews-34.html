\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhenomenal game. 8/10

- Many levels can be solved in multiple ways.
- Has a good curve for the most part but worlds 3/7 have a few large difficulty spikes.
- Most levels don't focus on precise movements as if you were entering a code, but allow plenty of space to move freely and change sentences. The hardest levels are probably the ones with restricted space.
- Has a level editor and many custom maps available to play.
- Music is decent but I got tired because it was very samey across worlds.
- I have a few minor gripes with some levels having somewhat inconsistent logic with what they allow.
- I burned out near the end and spent hours staring at levels unable to solve them, then I'd sleep and immediately wake up with the solution in my head.\t\t\t\t\t
