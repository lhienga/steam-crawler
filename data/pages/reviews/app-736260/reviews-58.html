Though I admit that this is a very fancy idea, but I have to say that it's TOOOOOO hard. Even though I regard myself as a talented player of puzzle games, many levels of this game still make me frustrated.
Further more, because there are so many hidden levels (and areas), it's hard to find missed levels. Even though I've unlocked all achievements, there is still one level I haven't beat (and have no idea where it is).
