\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is probably the best puzzle game I have ever played, with only the first portal game a close second. The graphics are... not great. Ok, honestly, they're pretty bad. The music is ... um... some people love it? The premise of the game seems simplistic and limited at first pass. All this kept me from playing the game at first, but, once you're in, it keeps getting deeper and deeper. New ideas appear frequently to keep you ever more engaged. If you have any hint of a desire to play a puzzle game, buy and play it. It's on essentially every platform so there's no excuse.

One final thing needs to be said. The difficulty is high. If you want to get the basic ending (which only encompasses about 1/4 or less of the puzzles) it's not too bad. But if you want to complete this game, prepare yourself. The puzzles get brutal. If you don't cheat, you can expect some puzzles required for completion to take you hours. Good Luck.
