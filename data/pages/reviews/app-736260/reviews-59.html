\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRecommended – 7/10

It’s hard to describe what makes this game stand out. Mostly, you are trying to complete each puzzle level by logically manipulating the rules/properties of the level itself. As the game progresses, you are introduced to more rules allowing for even more changes in what you can do. I’ve never really played any game like this before and found it very captivating. The only rule I found confusing was the EMPTY rule and making combinations like EMPTY IS YOU. Manipulating things as empty space was not very intuitive.

Overall, I’d recommend this to anyone who likes logic and puzzle type games.\t\t\t\t\t
