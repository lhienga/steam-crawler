\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have to recommend Baba Is You based off of its beautifully original concept alone. That being said, this game is really, really hard, and I'm not sure if if falls in the Venn diagram overlap with "fair". It just barely provides any help to you. Normally, games are criticized for giving you too much assistance. I criticize games when they give me too much assistance as well, but at least assistance normally doesn't result in me being straight-up bored. I haven't beaten this game and probably never will unless I decide to look up the solutions on the internet. This is because the game insists on you teaching yourself how to play while providing you barely any hints or direction outside of the level title. I really just don't have the patience for this game to play it for extended periods of time. Most of the gameplay is in your head as you brainstorm how to beat the level. I frequently found myself not having fun while playing this, and if the game's not fun, why bother?

Notwithstanding all of that, I still have to recommend Baba Is You, as, like I said, the concept really is just great and perhaps I'm not the right kind of player for this unconventional genre.\t\t\t\t\t
