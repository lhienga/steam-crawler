The game is hard and fun. Most of the puzzles range from pretty challenging to mind numbingly difficult. I'd get stuck for hours on a puzzle and I've dropped the game countless times, taking brakes for days and weeks between play sessions, only to come back, finally solve them and feel great (or stupid) afterwards. The game keeps adding new rules as you go to new zones, which stops the game from getting boring or stale. The rules are simple, but the hard part is figuring out how you have to twist them to reach the Win goal. The music is pretty chill and the art is cute. It's worth the price.\t\t\t\t\t
