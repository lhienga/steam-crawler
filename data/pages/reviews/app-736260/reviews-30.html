\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBaba is You is definitely the best puzzle game I've played. It takes a basic concept and turns it on its head, and forces you to think outside of the box, or like the box. The only obstacle in this is yourself and your inability sometimes to think about other solutions. You may know how a level is won, but are too focused on a way to do it you can't think of anything else, so an effective way to solve some of these are just looking away from it and thinking about it again.

But I would lie if I said I didn't use any hints at all. Some of the levels seemed impossible and there are great ways to get spoiler free hints as to not ruin the accomplishment. That's to say, this game is not easy, and even without testing some of the player made maps you'll find it challenging. The difficulty ramps up gradually as you'd expect, and it does a great job introducing the new mechanics. Some of them are niche and reserved for only a few levels, but most of them are used throughout the whole game.

It's not a short game, either. They really made the most of it. There's more levels in this game than you can ever expect. And I mean that in a very literal sense. Each time you think you've beaten the game, you're proven wrong. None of the levels seem out out place either, but some of them are tougher than others when it feels like they shouldn't be. But generally speaking, you're going through them in a very logical order and your understanding of the game increases with each level completed.

If you're at all interested in the game, do get it. Even at full price I think it's more than worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
