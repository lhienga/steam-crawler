\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlants vs. Zombies is a definitive high quality and no doubt nostalgic tower defense game which has the player defending their home from hordes of zombies with the use of plants and lawnmowers. To this day, one of the best tower defense games of all time, possibly the founding father of the genre. PvZ would later spawn a franchise and gain a following of loyal fans, further cementing itself as one of the best video games in history.

Plants vs Zombies primary action takes place in its adventure mode. The game has no narrative apart from defending yourself and your goofy neighbor Dave from the zombies, instead wraps the player in a unique game-play experience challenging them to counter a variety of zombies with different plants in their load out.

After adventure mode is complete, there are a generous amount of achievements to collect (none too begrudgingly difficult), mini-games with twists on adventure mode, an endless/survival mode which pits the player in an endless wave of zombies, and a garden you can interact with to collect plants and gain money which can be spent for upgrades at the shop.

No known bugs or glitches, epic soundtrack, and Only $4.99! Recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
