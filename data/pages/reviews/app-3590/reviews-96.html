When it almost seemed like boring plain mobile games like flappy bird and angry birds was in the same battlefield as THE last of us. And when mobile games seemed to be bombed out of its trench it called on its final bombshell, that bomb was PVZ mobile. Of course the game we know nowadays, and the one I am reviewing is PVZ for steam, resembling one of now PC gaming territories just like South Korea is. But PVZ changed the market of gaming and despite this game looking like some stupid forgotten game, I have to remind you that this game, despite having mobile standards almost, is known as the best strategy game and as well as the most respected in all gaming communities. This game was like a peace treaty. I have never seen a “mobile game” that managed to be on almost all modern platforms. The game is fun, memorable, and the soundtrack of the game is unforgettable. The characters plants and zombies, have all interesting characteristics and all have their purposes in the game. Except cactus. Never really used that. 8/10
