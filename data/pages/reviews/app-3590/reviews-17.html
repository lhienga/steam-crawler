\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game I have a lot of nostalgia for. I first got the full game on the Nintendo DS way back in the day, and have loved every version of the original Plants Vs. Zombies I have gotten since. The PC version is the original, and most well-known, release, offering the game in its most unabridged form. While this does mean many of the additions from later releases of the game - examples being the DS-exclusive minigames, and the versus mode from the DS, PS3, and XBox 360 versions - are not present, what is provided is still a fun and silly romp through a classic from the late 2000s. Even as this game nears its fifteenth anniversary, I still consider it the peak of the franchise it would spawn, with later games being those with great potential, but tainted by greedy business practices that are not present in the original or most of its ports. Still, the original Plants Vs. Zombies remains a beloved title that has withstood the test of time for a reason, and I urge you to get at least one version of the game and see what made people love it.\t\t\t\t\t
