\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWidely known throughout the game industry as one of the greatest tower defence games of all time, PVZ remains as one of my favourite video games that I’ve ever played. After beating the campaign a decade ago, I returned to kill more zombies and had even more fun the second time around. Crazy Dave, the taco-loving, alien language-speaking, eccentric botanical hero of the town, teaches the player how to defend our home against an army of zombies. With the craziest variety of plants at our disposal, it’s not long until we realize that our natural military force is unstoppable. PVZ is extremely addictive, very funny, intensely challenging, visually appealing, and has one of the most iconic video game soundtracks of the modern era. My favourite mini-games PVZ has to offer include Wall-nut Bowling, It’s Raining Seeds and Beghouled. It’s a damn shame that no sequel lived up to the standards set by the original.\t\t\t\t\t
