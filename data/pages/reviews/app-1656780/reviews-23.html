\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore I begin I'd like to mark I haven't planned on playing it for so long. One might think it has a lot of common to do with HoMM III or so. In my opinion - yes and no.

Hero's Hour
Boy is that game punishing when you touch it as a total greenhorn. So many faction with its own strength and weaknesses, heroes that over time can become stupidly powerful and it really is all about abusing skills like Devour or Potency to make oneself stronger or your summons absurd. Hero's Hour is a game you can love and hate at the same time. It doesn't even hide a fact that a Hardcore or a Hardcore+ AI is literally cheating. I cannot imagine seeing any other army marching through a postguard, killing entire garrison only to learn around 8 different spells or getting their ass whooped so the next week they come back right under your doorstep with an even bigger army. Seriously, how the ♥♥♥♥ do you recruit 72 mindmelters in a bloody week!

It isn't deprived of its flaws like AI rushing right at you, making stupid decision like trying to sneak past your army so they can grab your mines and possibly city, unguarded. Often time they avoid gathering resources or even artifacts laying, ripe for taking, UNGUARDED. There are some annoyances I'd hope to see fixed or removed after a while.
It's fun to see how these HoMM III stacks of hundreds or even thousands of units clash with one another in one huge battle only for you to be really confused as to what the heck is going on while looking on your screen. Needless to say, your FPS is also a victim of such spectacular combat. And heavens forbid you or AI ever cast a spell Dire Die. You've been warned.

Rogue's Realm

You'd think that this "DLC" adds some content to the original game and is for some reason even more expensive than normal game. This ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ addon topples that game upside down, adding a lot more cities, heroes, spells, even remakes what's already there, adds few more constructs and even units to the existing ones. Well ♥♥♥♥ me!
My issue is that several Rogue heroes have skills that repeat themselves twice or even thrice. Imagine having Hoard (x2) or a Healing (x2). You add one point, you raise two skills in one go and they ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ stack! Not sure that's intended but it tends to be devastating. Also Komera from Earthen has that Scholar skill that if raised to lvl 4 or 6 gets +1 in all skills minus morale and luck for each rank EACH WEEK. Komera?! More like Nasus!

This game is a mess! You can love it or hate it but it's there. Completely playable (if you play your cards right) just don't challenge an unfair AI difficulty or you'll lose fun. That is unless you're a masochist.

In overall, Hero's Hour has a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ of flaws and is a one huge mess but I do highly recommend it!\t\t\t\t\t
