The combinations are amazing. and if you beat the Hardcore+ difficulty, then you up the anty by facing 2 on 1 and eventually go for 7 v 1 on hardcore+. Honestly I wish I could write out all the fun crazy stories and experiences I've had in this game. I want to thank the developers for making something that not gets boring and is always fresh. maps are random generated which makes it an adventure every time. you pick the style and it randomizes the map around that overall layout. Never two identical maps, ever. Anyways, this was worth every penny, and if I didn't work 50-60 hours a week with a family, I'd probably have 1000 hours in by now.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: I am changing my recommendation to Yes because I like the developer. (They put "Merry Christmas" on the game's homepage.) On sale, I would say buy this game. My only real qualm remains, though, per below.

I am trying hard to like this game, but I am the type of player who desires mostly to create my own maps and mods. And because I am almost fully computer/programming illiterate, then I need something that a 5th grader can use. Unfortunately, the map editor in Hero's Hour constantly crashes, and I am finding it impossible to set-up a game how I would like it. As far as modding the game goes, it should be super-simple for a dummy like me to create or modify a faction, it's buildings, artifacts, etc. It's just not. So this negative review is based mostly on those two facts. Also, I have noticed in the Steam Workshop that mod downloading has been disabled by the developer. This makes me think that they are aware of the problems and will update the game to address these...? Fingers crossed. And if so, then I will gladly change my review to a positive one with an awesome map editor in place!

That said, as you have guessed or observed, Hero's Hour takes a great deal of inspiration from the Heroes of Might & Magic franchise. The big difference in the combat system is that it is basically auto-combat but with very few tweaks you can make for troop placement and spellcasting. It's fun, and I have no problems with the basic game itself, which is why I am hoping for a wonderful map editor update to change my review!\t\t\t\t\t
