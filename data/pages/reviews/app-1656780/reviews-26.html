\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game does an amazing job combining strategy, tactics, and role-playing. The individual elements--town building, resource management, hero customization, exploration, and massive real-time battles--seamlessly integrate into a whole experience with ridiculous levels of depth and replayability. Thanks to the many factions and maps, I expect to sink hours and hours into this without getting bored. At the same time, it's quite easy to learn with simple, intuitive controls.

There's still room for improvement though:
-Once one player wins a major battle, the loser is unlikely to ever catch up, because the winner will always have a bigger army. Would it be possible to add some kind of catch-up mechanic?
-The exploration starts to feel predictable after a while. There's always a similar collection of mines, treasures, and troop production. It would be great to add more unexpected, game-altering discoveries on the map.\t\t\t\t\t
