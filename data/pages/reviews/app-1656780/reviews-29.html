\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo here's an honest review.

This game is fun until you realize how little the game actually relies on the Units. It's more like "Who has the better spells and more mana wins" I figured this out when I attacked a watch tower that had the "impossible" difficulty and just kept spamming magic and utterly destroyed them with only a few guys lost.

Magic is far too overpowered and unbalanced. It's more like a Hack-n-Slash game rather than strategy. All i have to do is Comet Rain my opponents ranged units and when they send in more ranged units, no biggie, just Comet Rain them again. That spell costs 8 mana though... Oh you think that's a lot? Nah I have 400 mana lmao. Even if it was a lot, I'll just go back to my town and insta regen all of it.

The magic system needs a complete overhaul.

That aside, one of my Hero's got a bug where whenever he attacked anything that i couldn't skip to the outcome, it crashed the game. I had to disband him because he was utterly useless.\t\t\t\t\t
