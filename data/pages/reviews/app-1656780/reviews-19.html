\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like the Heroes of Might & Magic series, this is a really great game.

The combat is in real time and automatic, but you have a degree of control with simple general commands for the troops to move or retreat and spells/abilties from heroes. Heroes have skill trees in which you can plan ahead your build order for an overpowred strategy.

There is also a nice variety of fatcions to choose from, 12 for base game and +1 (which splits into even more subfactions) in the Rogue Realms DLC. Each faction offers different ways to play the game with unique mechanics that spices a lot the crazy and fun strategies you can do. Like Decay (Necropolis like faction) where you can turn living things into undead and fuse them and also gather souls from fallen units to transform it into more troops or resources. Or the Stronghold/Barbarian like faction "Horde" where the troops evolve through combat instead of upgrading them with gold and the heroes having different abilities that costs no mana. This and much more for all the factions.

Amazing pixel art and soundtrack too

10/10\t\t\t\t\t
