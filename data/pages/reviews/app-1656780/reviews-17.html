\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHero's Hour : A "Total War(TW)-Lite" Lacking Any Depth and Strategy

Hero's Hour had the potential to be an engaging, TW-lite experience but ultimately fell short in delivering depth and strategy. While I completed the tutorial level, the game left me unsatisfied and uninterested in exploring its procedurally generated worlds.

The tutorial fails to provide comprehensive guidance, and it wasn't until later that I discovered the similarity to the Total War franchise, where you can upgrade towns without visiting them. Once I adapted my strategy by focusing on resource generation, recruiting heroes, and upgrading units, victory came easily.

Despite its procedurally generated nature, the game feels formulaic and lacks nuance. Speedrunning resource optimization guarantees a win with no real strategic depth or challenge. The video (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fwQnKcwMgC0) confirms that unit placement is arbitrary and devoid of strategy, further emphasizing the lack of depth in the combat system.

The game offers interesting artwork and diverse factions, which could provide some enjoyment for players seeking a time-killer. However, the simplistic formula of build, gather, and conquer without strategic depth falls short of my already lowered expectations for this game. The game has potential with character and town development mechanics, but the lackluster combat phase renders these features moot.

In conclusion, I cannot recommend Hero's Hour unless the developers commit to polishing the game's combat, strategy, and AI. As it stands, the game feels like an arbitrary, formulaic experience that lacks the depth and challenge to sustain entertainment over a few hours. Better yet, if the developers surpassed the "breakeven" point and fixed these issues from the ground-up in a sequel, I would actually consider supporting it.

If I compared this game's price per hour, I was entertained, I got terrible value. I don't see myself playing this much more than I already have. I might pick away at it some more. If I change my mind, I'll change my review accordingly.\t\t\t\t\t
