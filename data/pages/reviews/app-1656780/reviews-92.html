1. It's full release. Very cool.

-Undead boys are doing undead stuff.
-Holy bros are shiny.
-Serpent squad be sick.

3. The world map rocks. I probably seen 30 buildings that all did something different, just as random events to try out. RNG loot, and resources. I like.

Cons.

2. The enemy is fierce, but... I kinda like that. I put it here because I suspect they may be TOO godly. A little godly is fine.
3. It's a bit buggy. I'm pretty sure there is a building on my world that won't let me upgrade it. Stuff like that. (but it came out 1 day ago, so no worries)

Personally, I love it. I think it has insane potential. 8/10 Possible 99/10 down the road.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome game!

Even if the graphics are kinda simple, the accomplishment of this game to give us the dream come true of HoMM-like troops being deployed one by one in the battle really makes me happy!

Now, I hope the developer gets enough money to make the next game in the series!

The next game in the series, I want better sprites (detailed sprites) with more frames from each sprite animation (I really love the sprites [graphical sprites] from HOMM 3, at least in my infant-memory they were the best). I really like to watch the chaos of battle happening in this game, each battle is a Lord of the Rings Battle all over again hahaha

For the third game in the series, I wanna see 3D graphics and everything that comes with it!

And last but not least: better music. Not that the actual music of the game isn't good, but I don't like much this retro style music that makes us in contact with music for the first Nintendo video games and such. They have their charm, but I don't like it too much. And maybe have theme music for each faction. I really-really loved the music themes from HOMM 3, for each faction. Maybe add the option for the player choose if he wants to listen the music in the thematic fashion or if he wants random musics from the roll of musics playing randomly.

Anyway, I love this game and wish the best for the developers team!\t\t\t\t\t
