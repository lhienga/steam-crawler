\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLooks simple and shallow. Actually has TONS OF HIDDEN DEPTH. Lots to explore and learn about this game. Theorycrafting galore. I find myself spending a lot of time musing over the mechanics and that's not what I expected from an "autobattler" that should just be a simple homage to Heroes of Might & Magic.

HOMM fans will love this of course but everyone else may find themselves surprised by the level of mechanical complexity in this supposed autobattler. There's more manual control in the battles than I thought there would be. The hero leveling system is deep and offers lots of real choice. The AI is challenging but not too hard. Coop is good. (Supposedly network multiplayer is still in the plans but not yet, although playing through parsec with a friend works fine.) There's even a PVP scene on discord.

Getting way more than I bargained for out of this game. If you like theorycrafting your builds, give this one a shot. You may be pleasantly surprised too.\t\t\t\t\t
