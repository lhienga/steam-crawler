The gameplay is pretty simple each hero has a specific play style or two so there's not a lot of variety for the specific hero as there are definitely optimal paths in the limited skill tree.

The game itself the difficulty tends to be either boringly easy or unfair and infuriatingly hard with not much in between. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is absolutely fantastic and I recommend it to anyone who likes strategy rpgs, particularly if you're one to deep dive into mechanics.

The gameplay is like heroes of might and magic but more developed, and the combat system is massively improved. Instead of controlling stacks throughout the fight in a slow, turn based situation, your whole army is represented in chaotic and absolutely massive battles that happen in real time.

The skill trees are great, each hero and town type has a variety of strategies available to it and the builds are interesting enough and different enough that each one I've played has had a distinctive feel. I am particularly a fan of Hardsoul with the decay kingdom. This representation of necromancy is the most interesting execution I have come across in fantasy gaming. You create the necro army of your dreams in a way that is very tactical and rewarding (remember to very carefully manage your mana!).

Another really fun hero is Tono from the pillar faction. He creates mercury based slime monsters that are really powerful. Very cool.\t\t\t\t\t
