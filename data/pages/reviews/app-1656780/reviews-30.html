\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely love this game. The art is nostalgic and astounding, the generated maps look great, the diversity of factions is so unique, the strategy is simple despite the high learning curve, and the gameplay is just overall satisfying.

HOW TO WORK AROUND THE REALLY HARD DIFFICULTY:
1.) Be aggressive really early. It is tempting to turtle your army and wait till you can overwhelm the stations without losses, but doing so makes you fall behind in resources rediculously. It is better to claim mines and such ASAP so that you can accumulate wealth enough to pull ahead. It is better to loose an army and gain consistant income, rather than wait 7 turns for you to take that income to save a measley 25 units

2.) Challenge ratings are a lie. There are challenges above army heads that say "easy, moderate, challenging, hard, near impossible, or Impossible". I have beat an impossible difficulty before, because the calculation doesn't account for body guards or your hero's (militia leader ) power/magic capability, which can easily flip overwhelming armies on their heads

3.) Tactics matter. Part of how you win(in skirmishes), is scanning the army's specific enemy stats and skills. I was able to win a near impossible round WITHOUT a hero simply by using advantagous enemy placement. (i can go into great detail with this, but basic principles. Units are stronger bunched up, keep the units that regen each battle on the front lines, organizing so you surround the enemy force, ect.)

4.) your hero is a god. Heros are the units that lead the entire force, and for some reason, the forces get BUFFED BASED ON THE HERO'S STATS. The more battles you do, the more exp the hero gets. PLAN which hero you want depending on your playstyle. Get a skill-upgrade order to make them the most powerful.

5.) Know your faction. Every faction has something overpowered about them, you just have to figure out what it is. They each seem to have a unique way of generating excess gold, besides spamming gold mines of course. They each have niche units that you need to understand so you can use them with the best advantage.

6.) PLAN your build order in your city. It looks very linear seeing as how cities have pre-set buildings. But KNOWING what to get first in less turns can get you the most financial success, and the biggest armies early on
