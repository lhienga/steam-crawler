\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt takes some time to get the hang of it, but once you do it's totally worth it. Many of the complaints about this game are centered around difficulty, but this is just a learning curve issue. This is not the kind of game that makes it obvious what you are doing right and wrong, so it takes time to figure out your builds and play style. After a few dozen rounds you will figure out all of the tricks and can crank the difficulty up.

With all that aside, Hero's hour has a LOT to offer. The auto-battler combat is surprisingly fun, and there are 12 unique factions each having multiple viable strategies. The game world is one of the best parts as well because there is so much to do while you are building up your base and army. The random generation also ensures that you will encounter different situations each run. My only complaint is the UI sucks pretty hard.

Overall I highly recommend this game\t\t\t\t\t
