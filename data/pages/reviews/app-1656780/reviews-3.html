\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun game like homm3 or other turn based 4x games. Lots of unique factions with unique mechanics. Great music. I especially like the fact that you can't wield your entire army if it's big but instead choose a subset. During the fight if you or the opponent incurs losses you can choose units from your reserve to reinforce. This way you can counter what they are fielding at that moment.

Only downside is the AI is a bit wonky and if you play a big map you'll have lots of units which make the combat slow down. Also not all of the controls are explained fully. For example you can give some basic orders for movement and flanking. Also you can upgrade skills to higher levels instead of taking new skills all the time. Still a good game for that price.\t\t\t\t\t
