\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDid you grow up playing Hero's of Might and Magic? Did you want to have that same feel, with procedurally generated levels and screenplay for your friends? Well, here's the game to scratch that itch. This is a cool gem to pickup for your friend and discord groups - easy to jump into, turn based - battle sequences are "automatic" with you acting as a spellcaster/ability user.

Definite pickup if you enjoy TOTAL WAR at all - imagine playing that with 4+ friends, minus directly controlling your armies and building on tiles.

Addictive as it is!

Wishlist so far - Ability to name characters and towns - More textures on the map in general (missing a "3rd" palette to mesh the grass/snow/other with the "dirt/gravel/other" - would add more depth to the world) - Ability to "destroy" and "rebuild" resource nodes

Fantastic!!\t\t\t\t\t
