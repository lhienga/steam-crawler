\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, the obvious: this game has similarities to the HOMM series, yet it also brings its own uniqueness to the genre. (I've wondered why we havnt had more developers do this, would probably cause a certain company to raise the bar on their games.)

The factions and such are similar to that of HOMM, yet not quite the same. The developer definitely did their work to keep the game original. You have the overland map, a hero with his army, artifacts, resources, etc, all similar to that of HOMM.

The main difference lies in the combat. At first i wasnt sure how i liked real time combat in place of the turn based from the HOMM series yet after playing a few games i came to enjoy it.

Overall, if you are a fan of the HOMM series, you should at least give this a shot. Not everyone will like it as the units are different and some just may not care for the real time combat. I have enjoyed it and hope to see more games like this in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
