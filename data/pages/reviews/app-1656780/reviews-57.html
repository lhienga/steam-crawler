\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInstantly addictive game. Must buy for any HoMM fans.

Essentially it's a love letter to Heroes of Might and Magic with cute pixel art graphics and real time auto-battles. Games aren't as long as HoMM and you can clear the largest maps in a couple of hours, but fun is tightly packed.

There are many unique factions to choose from, each with their unique gameplay quirks, and heroes have extensive unique skill trees, allowing you to spend hours trying out different strategies.

Graphics are simple but pleasing and the chiptune music is pretty catchy. Each town has its own unique theme, which is nice.

I don't even have anything negative to say other than there's not a huge lot of content, but I hope more will keep coming. Besides the replayability value is high even if you keep playing the same random map over and over. For a small one-person project this is 10/10 as far as game design goes.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is almost a complete rip of Heroes Of Might and Magic II and III, if you've had the pleasure of playing those ancient games and enjoyed them. Odds are you'll like this game. You have a lot of different factions that have different play styles. There's a Skirmish game mode were you fight in various situations that's pretty fun. Of course you also have the random map as well, a lot of choices there and its well fleshed out which is good considering that was the most popular part of HOMM. Only thing I would like is some Campaigns like HOMM games in the past had. Preferably HOMMIII's where there is a main and multiple sides.

I will also address some of the Negative reviews I saw.
AI cheats/Solves whole map
I truely don't even understand where these complains are coming from. In my experience I'm shocked people are complaining that the AI is to hard. So far the AI has always been easy to beat back, I solve my area faster than it 80% of the time and whenever I do get beaten it's due to clear misplays on my part. For reference I play on normal always for now. (which is what most complains are on) Most people are saying it auto battle cheats, spawns units for free and solves the whole first area they start in.
Auto battle cheats.
I have yet to see literally any evidence of this one.
Spawns units for free
Alot of HOMM games do this, because the AI tends to lose alot more than the player. As for this game. I haven't seen any ridiculous amounts that I can't surpass with ease. So I dont see a issue.
Solves first area
The reason I don't understand this complaint. 1. I tend to solve it faster than the AI 2. The whole goal of the early IS solving the first area. if the AI couldn't solve it the game would be over before it began.
Skirmishes are too hard
I have beaten all skirmishes, they are all possible. They are designed to be difficult, if you don't want to deal with being challenged as a player. Don't play the Skirmishes.
This faction completely destroys my faction.
This is like all HOMM games, factions counter other factions. They all have strengths and weakness. There is no God faction that can fight all of them perfectly. If it wasn't this way the game would be inherently unbalanced.
The Game itself is to difficult.
easy is just that easy, normal is normal. The difficulty is fair. Anyone crying that easy is to hard either A gave up after like 3 games, B didn't change their strategy at all (skill issue)
and if anyone is crying that Hard is to Hard then... you simply play on lower difficulty.
Game is unplayable
more than 3 players cant work on my system
I really dont know what they are using at this point. My PC is far from top of the line, and I can play with 7 players at once.
Lack of strategy
This is a HOMM game, you build armies, solve you area as fast as possible, use spells and win. Its unfair to say this specific game doesn't have any. It has the same amount as all the HOMM's before it.

Some reviews that are true
Game crash
I have had this happen literally once, but since it happened once I can't say it's false. The map was clearly not Generated correctly. I played it anyway. The nomad heroes had such massive armies it lagged the game and crashed it if you engaged in combat. So crashing is possible, I've only seen it on a bad map though.
Death stacks (1 hero having a huge army and all the others having no army)
to be completely honest with you, all HOMM games have this, its a part of this game if you don't like that and its game breaking for you don't get it.
No campaign
yes at the moment there is no campaign.
Doesn't show actual stats of spells
This is true, not to sure why they dont.
Pixel graphics
I find the pixel graphics have their own charm, but alot of reviews dont. Yes the game is styled with pixels and intentionally lower graphics.
No in game graphic settings.
yep this one is true. I will say though. The graphics are plenty low enough, if your PC can't run it. Consider upgrading. Cause at that point a list of games you can play would be smaller than the ones you can't.

Overall this is a good game that can use some more content. I'd say a 10/10 just because I want to see more HOMM style games out there. (real scores is 8/10 needs missions and online multiplayer)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts good. It Heroes 3 with a multiplayer focus, so things that made the game slow have been changed. They want you to be able to finish a game or two in a single night. The change to combat is the biggest example of this.

Hero skill trees are more flexible and they fight in battle. You can have larger armies, but battles have a max amount you can field, you deploy reinforcements as units die. 6 creature tiers but more flexible choices. Only 5 resource types. Artifacts are less game breaking but more abundant. Better ship mechanics.

The art style is what it is. I don't particularly care for it, or the music that much, but I don't hate it either, and it all fits together really well. Its very sure of what it wants to be, I just prefer a bit more detail.

The biggest problem is the AI, It feels like they cheat. Even on normal, multiple times I've had the AI roll me before I can break out of my zone. In larger games one AI usually stomps the others by week 5 or 6 then its just me slowly chipping away at them.

This game was clearly built with PVP in mind, which is where it excels. I'm hoping they can make a more balanced and enjoyable AI to fight against, then it will be a complete package.\t\t\t\t\t
