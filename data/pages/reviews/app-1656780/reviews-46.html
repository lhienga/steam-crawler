✓ 11 factions to choose from
✓ 177 different unit types
✓ There are so many new mechanics available you’d find yourself struggling to see and do everything the game could offer
✓ Large-scale battles
✓ Gameplay is fairly easy to learn

CONS:
- No real campaign
- Few glitches and bugs during play
- Graphics and audio can get a little repetitive over a long playtime
- Battles are automated bust-ups that only give you a small role to play
