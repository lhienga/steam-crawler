It Also it executes them pretty good. I mean every stage has a few mechanics and themes which it carries with it the whole stage. To avoid boredom Celeste also introduces new mechanics mid-stage and also lets it combine with multiple previous mechanics. It's really fair too. If you messed up it really feels like You messed up. Also it's really atmospheric and the story really surprised me with how good it was (I bought it just for the gameplay). The atmosphere is just made better with the glorious soundtrack which is literally a spiritual experience. At last I should also mention that this game is very hard and although I had a lot of fun with it, I don't think everyone will have fun with it. But if difficulty is the only problem for you with this game then this is (potentially) no at all problem, because there is an Assist-Mode which helps players which are disabled or can't progress to have fun with it all the same. Anyways I love this game and it deserves to be played.\t\t\t\t\t
