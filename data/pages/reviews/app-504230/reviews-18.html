\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is absolutely AWESOME :D

Not only is it mechanically super fun and interesting, but the story is wonderful too and really well written! I'm not a speedrunner so I played the game pretty casually, but even in this case the game was still great. It does a thing I really like where, while the levels are super challenging and difficult, it's not punishing like a lot of platformers sometimes are. If you mess up, you don't respawn at some far away checkpoint, you don't have to redo the level, and you *certainly* don't have to redo the entire game like CERTAIN classic NES and Genesis era platformers. Instead, you just respawn at the beginning of the screen you were at. One can think of it as Savestates: The Videogame. But unexpectedly, designing a game this way actually works super well! It allows levels to present really creative challenges while minimizing the frustration factor. I bet even someone who isn't very good at platformers could still love this game.

Also, the music is incredible. I will confess that I played a chunk of this game at school, which meant muting my laptop. I REALLY wish I hadn't done that, and plan to go back to the levels whose music I missed, because what I have heard is unlike any game I've ever played. They're lovely atmospheric tunes I could listen to plenty outside the game (perhaps I should purchase the soundtrack)!

And of course, the story was so captivating. I will disclose that I do not have anxiety nor depression, so I can't directly relate to Madeline's experiences. Nonetheless, I do relate to her in some scenes a lot, and those scenes really hit hard. But even if I couldn't, I just really loved the few but well-carved characters, especially Theo, who puts up a very goofy demeanor but is hinted to go through an internal struggle distinct from Madeline's. The message of self-acceptance is also really well done. To be honest I wish I'd played this game when it came out, because I really could've used it! But hey, better late than never. I won't go into any more specifics about the story because YOU'VE GOT TO PLAY IT YOURSELF OMG!!!

In conclusion, I LOVE THIS GAME AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA\t\t\t\t\t
