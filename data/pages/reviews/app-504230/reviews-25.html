The characters are fun, compelling, and psychological. Much deeper and more interesting than you'd expect from this genre. However, the main draw is the gameplay.

This game is challenging. But more than any other game I've played, Celeste will make you a better player. When you die in other games, there's usually some time wasted while the player navigates back the challenging part of the level. Celeste respects your time. When you fail, you immediately respawn close to the difficult part. No time wasted. You try repeatedly until you can beat the challenge. It might take 100 tries, but once you've beaten it once, you will often be surprised to find you can now beat it every time.

You can complete other games without becoming a better gamer. Celeste improves your skill in a way most games don't.\t\t\t\t\t
