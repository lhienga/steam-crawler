This game is great. The difficulty is really accessible and refined (apart from the hotel, the 2-shaped jump near the end was a right pain and the room just went on and on after that and then another room or 2 for the chapter...) so average joe/jo/jane can finish the game without having to be some hardcore speedrunner. That said, the shortcuts are usually quite visible (especially for those who know what to look for) so those inclined can go back and run through the chapters in minutes. There is also a bunch of handicap options for those who can't manage the difficulty, and you can mix-and-match to get the playstyle you want (and they don't even affect your achievements, because this is ultimately a story-driven game disguised as a platformer and having fun is what matters)

There are also remixes of the levels in the B-sides and C-sides which provide even more content to those looking to push their skills.

The soundtrack is also great and uplifting at times, with remixes for every alternate chapter as well.\t\t\t\t\t
