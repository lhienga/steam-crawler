\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat's that clich shared between lovers?

"You're not perfect but you're perfect for me."

Generally I try not to make those kinds of bold statements, positive or negative, about any media. How do you define perfect when part of the beauty of art is your unique perspective and how that affects your interpretation? Nonetheless, I've found myself using the word "perfect" to describe this game, and this practice has only increased in frequency as the years pass.

Of course, this game isn't perfect. The average player won't get past the Celestial Resort, some will consider the visuals archaic, Lena's soundtrack may be a style that some will not enjoy, and some will not appreciate the complexities hidden under what initially comes off as a very simple movement system. Run-on sentences aside, I understand these people. I can't relate, but I understand.

A little context: this is my favorite game. Period. My top 3 is rounded out by the likes of MegaMan X4 and Metroid: Fusion. It's not hard to draw parallels.

If you're like me, you'll find yourself thinking about this game well after you've played and finished it. Not only is the gameplay exactly the kind of thing I love, but the presentation and story are masterfully done. All of these elements make for an experience that will leave a significant impact on your heart and soul. Playing the game again, I can't believe it's real.

I can't believe it still has this effect on me after 6 years.
I can't believe how vivid the memory of getting those golden crystal hearts is, and how I'll never forget how that dopamine rush felt.
I can't believe that to this day I can't listen to In The Mirror without tearing up a bit when Lena's dialogue plays.
I can't believe that this dev team loves the Celeste project so much that they released Farewell, an entire new game's worth of content for free.
I can't believe a studio like this exists, one that has so much passion for what they've made that it's impossible to hide.
I can't believe this game exists.

But it does. It exists.
It may not be perfect, but it's perfect for me.
Please play Celeste.\t\t\t\t\t
