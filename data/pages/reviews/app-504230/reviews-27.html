Mechanics:
Quite neat, but at times not very intuitive.

Level design:
Acceptable, similar in fashion to playing yoshi's island levels, however, it suffers from a lack of respawn points which makes progress tedious, redundant and difficult.

Music:
Beautiful yet repetitive, especially after dying for the 80th time. Just about drove me insane listening to the same freaking music loop over and over again to the point where I muted it . . . and all was lovely once again.
(Not to tear down the OG Leina Raine, I actually am a rather avid fan of her work, but anything can be annoying at certain point for me if looped too many times)

Characters:
Some are an upright annoying pain in the ass (like that creepy hotel owner, my goodness) and others are kinda meh and snarky

Sound design:
pretty neat, I liked the way the sound changes when you duck your head under the water

Story:
Couldnt bother to finish it anyway.

Visuals:
Quite charming to say the least. Pixel aesthetic is well executed. Only nitpick is that it's not completely consistent at times. (ei. the main menu level selection lookin something out of poly bridge 2)

Replay value:
Not much, unless your a speedrunner

Difficulty:
Yes.

Strawberries:
Yes (don't get me started on those F***ing strawberries)

While we are on that note: why are the strawberries in this game? They don't add anything to it at all except wasted time and frustration.
Like you had any friends to flex this feat on anyway to begin with, so why bother!? (Yo, check out all the strawberries I collected in this game!)
Plus it's confusing when you're trying to look for the next entrance to progress the plot only to stumble onto another pointless level with ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ strawberry in it.
Personally, I think the devs should have made some sort of incentive to collect them, but I digress.

Gameplay enjoyment level:
About as enjoyable as playing the ground levels in Drakengard


Overall verdict: 10/10 strawberries



In all honesty I'm glad other people liked this game, but for me personally this game isn't rewarding to play at all.
Instead it just feels like beating your head into a brick wall over and over again and it constantly punishes you for it.
At a certain point I simply refunded and put it down because I simply did not enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
