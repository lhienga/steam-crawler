\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving been turned into a gamer by Super Mario Galaxy and having a massive amount of hours on Mario games, and CSGO surf/kz/bhop you could say I am a huge fan of expressive, tight and fun movement. I can say without a doubt that Celeste offers such an experience in spades. The depth achieved with simply 3 possible move inputs (jump, grab, dash) is astonishing and ends up being one of the best platforming experiences out there in my opinion. I don't want to get too in-depth into specifics on complex mechanics as there are thousands of videos on the internet displaying the movement of this game, and I also feel the game does an incredible job of teaching the player relevant mechanics.
I am a huge fan of challenging levels and am always happy to improve my movement skills to complete hard levels. The feeling I get from hitting clean movement is hard to explain but I'm guessing if your anything like me you know what I mean, happiness is probably the closest thing. Thankfully, Celeste offers challenges of all difficulties including extremely difficult, and these difficult levels are not rage inducing or gimmicky (like some other games), they almost always offer you a new way to express the mechanics you've learned to that point in a creative and fun way (and introduce new ones even 15hrs into my playthrough).
The level design and story display a level of attention and detail that only comes from developers that truly love the experience they are crafting. The story does an amazing job of giving the journey more depth than simply "climb the mountain", and beautifully incorporates itself with the gameplay, just play through it I promise it's awesome. And the music, OMG the music. Every artist on the soundtrack did an incredible job and the music matches perfectly with the atmosphere. The remixes on the B and C sides are awesome too and just add to the amount of love this game has gotten.

TLDR: One of the best platforming experiences out there. Extremely tight and rewarding movement with an extremely high skill ceiling complemented by a beautiful story, and an awesome soundtrack. You can tell the developers poured all their passion into this game.

If you are on the fence about picking up this game I highly suggest you give it a try and if your curious about what high-level gameplay looks like in Celeste I recommend you check out any speedrun you won't regret it.
