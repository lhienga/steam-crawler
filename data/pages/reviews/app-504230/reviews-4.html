\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOVERALL: 7/10
MECHANICS: 6/10
STORY: 6/10
MUSIC: 9/10
VISUALS: 7/10

Celeste is a good game to play with friends and if you love games that reward practice. The mechanics are very precise both timing and control wise, but after enough tries it is possible to master the mechanics. Each new mechanic is unique and some are more enjoyable than others. The story feels disconnected from the gameplay for most of the game except for the ending chapter. The main highlight of Celeste, in my opinion, is the music. Playing Celeste without music is pure pain; playing Celeste with music makes it feel like a challenge to be conquered. The music is so immersive and was the most memorable part of playing. The visuals also create a rich environment for each chapter filled with unique atmospheres and colors. This game is a challenging but rewarding adventure, and extremely enjoyable with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
