\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor those who enjoy precision platforming, this game is definitely worth playing. The levels are thoughtfully crafted, and the difficulty curve is quite reasonable. Some players might find it challenging, but the assist mode provided by the developers is very helpful. However, I suggest trying to beat the game without it, as the struggle is integral to the overall experience. Investing time and effort in overcoming challenging obstacles in a game will hone your skills and equip you better for similar challenges in the future. The music in the game is exceptional and holds a special place in my heart. I could go on and on about its brilliance and how much it resonates with me.
There's an incredibly talented group of modders out there. They've created tons of mods that greatly enhance the challenge of the game beyond what the base version offers. It's always exciting to tackle new challenges, and there's certainly no shortage of quality content available. One mod that I highly recommend is Strawberry Jam - the levels are exceptionally well-crafted and it's a must-play for anyone who enjoyed the original game.
Easily a 10/10 game for me.\t\t\t\t\t
