Each chapter has its own gimmick adding variety for each zone and you can blast through the main story without noticing anything repeatable.

After the main story, you can come back to any previous chapters to finish your collectible, some of them unlock harder versions of a chapter that reward another collectible which unlocks something else which in total would unlock significantly harder chapters that in total might take double or triple the time it took to beat the main story.

These collectibles are tracked in chapter selection and each chapter has sub-sections that let you replay starting from a specific point, simplifying the collectible hunt.

As for the difficulty, the main story is somewhat simple, it can become finger hurting in the optional harder levels but you can only blame the failures on yourself and try better.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter finishing all the achievements and over 100 hours in the game I can say that I was pleasantly surprised by this game. The first time I saw it I thought I wouldn't cope and that it was too hard but after I started playing it and making small adjustments to the controls (z for jump, x for grab and c for dash) it was very good to play. I really had many times when I wanted to quit (ch 7, ch 9 and ch 7 C side) but I didn't give up and I managed to complete the whole game. Right now I'm thinking about playing a few mods to make the game more difficult. The story was decent, the characters were good and funny and the soundtrack was PERFECT. What I liked less was chapter 8 which I found a little unimportant and very short. Don't get me wrong, I liked the gameplay itself, but I didn't think it had to exist in this form, chapter 9 was much better but also much harder. My opinion is that if you want to stop after chapter 7 you can do it, the other 2 chapters seem optional plus the main story and the goal of reaching the top of the mountain happens in the first 7 chapters. In conclusion, Celeste is a game in which you have to reach the top of Celeste Mountain , it will not be easy but it will be a beautiful experience that could make you want to play more after chapter 7 ends.\t\t\t\t\t
