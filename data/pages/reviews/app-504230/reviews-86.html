\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game really helped me through a tough time. Madeline's story with mental health, and the overall tone of hope really resonated with me. Seeing Madeline going through a panic attack, and her self doubt throughout the story made me feel so understood. (Especially during a time in my life when my mental health problems were being ignored by family).

Normally I wouldn't consider 'difficult' games enjoyable for me. I'd just get too discouraged. Celeste was also my first ever platformer. Despite this, the gameplay in Celeste was so enjoyable, even when failing, it wasn't a problem. The feeling of accomplishment made after beating a tough screen, or the hardest levels, and the Farewell DLC was unlike anything else.

The music is stellar, Lena Raine is a force. Years later and I still listen to the soundtrack now and again. Starjump, Confronting Myself, Heart of the Mountain, Resurrections, and Farewell go so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ hard.

I cannot recommend this game enough. \t\t\t\t\t
