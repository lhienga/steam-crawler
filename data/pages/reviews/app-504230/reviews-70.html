\r\n\t\t\t\t\tImagine Satan designed a Mario Maker level.
This is that. A precision platformer that just does not feel rewarding at all.

It is not even fun, I was looking forward to an experience that could be somewhat enjoyable. And wow, it sets up a game I could really love, and them subverts every positive emotion and expectation I had around this game in almost every conceivable way.

Expect to learn pattern to perfection in an annoying room to room puzzler that turns into a high intensity, anxiety pumping, unenjoyable loop of dirty gameplay that absolutely destroys and otherwise fun aesthetic and presumably cute story that could come out of this. I am do not have enough self hate to work my way through this mass annoyance.

It is just hard, and ridiculously so. There are so many small approaches that could be done to make this somewhat enjoyable - but it is clearly not in the vision or wheelhouse of the designer.

This game was an absolute insult. I wish I would have played it early on enough to get a refund - I am that disgusted by it.\t\t\t\t\t
