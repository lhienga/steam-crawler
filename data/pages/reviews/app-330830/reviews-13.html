\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this when I purchased the Pandora's Box Collection and damn; I wasn't expecting Borderlands, of all game series, to tell one of my favorite video game stories. Awesome music and intros as usual, and great, fun characters you grow to care surprisingly lot for. By Episode 5, I was invested to the point I had goosebumps from the Episode's beginning right up until the end of the credits. And that credits song... amazing. I'm not afraid to admit there were certain moments that affected me emotionally; again, something I wasn't expecting from Borderlands, a game series I've regarded up until now as nothing more than a fun looter shooter time waster. Somehow, it was all just so relatable and the game's friend group very much like mine and many other people's as well, I suspect.

However, as a video game, like most Telltale Games, Tales from the Borderlands hasn't got much gameplay vise. Hell, a few times at the beginning I was thinking this story should have been saved for the Borderlands-movie rumours say is in development. Then again, when the story ended, I was glad I got to control Rhys and Fiona, influence what they did and said. So, idk.

I love this game, 9/10. You can find games with better gameplay and games that utilize that better gameplay far more creatively to tell their narrative - utilize the medium of games itself - but stories as good as this one are rare.\t\t\t\t\t
