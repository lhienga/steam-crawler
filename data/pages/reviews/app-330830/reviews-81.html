\r\n\t\t\t\t\tABSOLUTELY FANTASTIC!!! Just finished this game, and my goodness, it's such a nice change to see Telltale take their signature storytelling style and apply it to a light-hearted story, the results here are spot on.
The thing I loved most was the humour, at times I was laughing uproariously, and at the very least I was always smiling. Second to that is how all the characters are so effortlessly likable, you can't help but become attached to ALL of them.
Also, if you've played any of the Borderlands games then this game helps expand on the universe and your existing knowledge, but if you haven't, don't worry, I'd say this game is also geared towards newcomers to the universe.
Overall definitely pick this game up if you're looking for something that's fun and easy-going. And if you're a fan of other Telltale games, this game is a welcome change of pace from their other serious offerings (The Walking Dead, Game of Thrones).
Since there's no way I can't have persuaded you to buy the game at this point...ENJOY!!!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHands down one of the best "games" I've ever "played". I used the " " because I don't know if I would consider this as much of a video game, as I would an interactive action adventure sequence. It still gets a 10/10 as one of the best Borderlands installs that I've ever had the joy of playing. It is honestly worth its full price on any given day simply for the enjoyment factor of the first time play through. The whole adventure is a riot and minimal player input isn't boring by any means. The story line keeps you on the edge of your computer chair and begging for more. I almost played through all the sequences in a single go, which is several hours of binge gaming. Each episode is about 2 hours long and the story line only gets more out of control the deeper you get, eventually you end up piloting this rolling disaster of an adventure that is hilariously epic. I almost don't want to play it again lest I ruin an unforgettable play-through. All I've really gotta say is "CATCH-A-RIDE bro", and buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t
