\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game takes the best from the Telltale game mechanics and the Borderlands lore.
The story is really good, with a couple of twists.
The new characters don't feel out of place in the Borderlands universe. Some of them even return in Borderlands 3 as NPC. Of course, there are also plenty of cameos made by main characters from the earlier Borderlands games.
The voice acting is really important in story-driven games, and Tales from the Borderlands has a great cast of actors that do their job perfectly.
The music is an aspect of Borderlands games I've always enjoyed. This one has a pretty good score, even if not stellar.
I can't say anything about the replayability and the importance of choices as I've only played once (I prefer it this way).
All in all, Tales from the Borderlands is worth playing, if you don't only play Borderlands for all the guns, but also enjoy the lore.\t\t\t\t\t
