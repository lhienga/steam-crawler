\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Good: If you enjoy the Borderlands then a playthrough of the entire series, especially Borderlands 3, is not really complete without Tales from the Borderlands. Coming in with trademark Borderlands humor, it allows you as the player to sort of dictate which direction the narrative goes in. The game allows you to explore a different side of Pandora, where instead of murdering bandits and corporations with amazing skills, you are placed in the shoes of Rhys and Fiona who are essentially normal people trying to survive on a planet whose ruthlessness built a franchise. It was sad when Telltale Games went under and this game was in legal limbo but I was very excited that it became available on Steam again. Extra credit for not turning it into Epic store exclusive BS.

The Bad: While a great game on its own in its original form, I am disappointed that 2K has essentially made no changes to the way the game looks graphically. The game is treated like a new release even though it came out on console and PC platforms almost 5 to 6 years ago. Despite the time 2K spent to reacquire the rights to this game, I still feel a little time could have been spent updating the game to more current interface and graphics standards. It also had many button mashing sequences that were essentially fixed and a few sequences that had the appearance of more difficult than usual for comedy. This is simply not appropriate for players with motor disabilities who may have become frustrated because they might've thought they were actually struggling with the controls despite that not being the case. At the very least, Tales from the Borderlands could have been made more accessible. For instance, button mashing could be toggled to button holding.

The Ugly: Two words. Skin pizza.

Conclusion: Despite the game's age and 2K's apparent lack of interest in adding some minor touches of modernization to the game's graphics and interface, Tales from the Borderlands is a game I can happily recommend for a few reasons. One reason is that its got lols and another is that narrative can take many different turns and leave you satisfied with your choices. Again, a must-have if you enjoy the Borderlands.\t\t\t\t\t
