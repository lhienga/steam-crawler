The story balances aspects like humour and surprise elegantly, and also features a surprising range of emotion, which I was definitely not expecting given the cartoony, generally irreverent theme of the rest of the Borderlands franchise.
My biggest complaint is that the graphics (in particular some of the characters' animations) could be better, but despite being somewhat aware of their shortcomings, they never got in the way of the storyline and I quickly got used to it.
In summary, sick game: would absolutely recommend. I only wish it had lasted longer!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like Borderlands games, Borderlands 2 especially. I like the humour presented in BL2 and I think Handsome Jack is one of the best villains ever created in video games. BL2 has an almost infinite replayability.

And that's where I've got a problem with Tales from Borderlands: is more of a interactive 3d movie than a game. It has its moments, especially when Handsome Jack is around, but mostly it only ask you click one of the answers from time to time. And few times you have to walk around to click on few objects, that's it.

On top of that the ending of the story is disappointing.

Since I cannot give it a "mixed" score which it deserves I will have to give it a negative note. Only for hardcore BL fans, but even them will be bored by the end(ing).

PS: I didn't played it for 25 hours just left it on pause overnight ;-)\t\t\t\t\t
