So, you want to hear a story, eh?

The Tales from the Borderlands, a Telltale Series game, is a story-driven, dialogue-heavy instalment to the Borderlands franchise. It plays a significant role in the Borderlands universe; events occurring after BL2 and before BL3, something the lovers of the franchise certainly won't want to miss out on.

This is a conclusive story between the relationship of Hyperion and Pandora, you play as various characters on a mission, which turns and twists in all manner of directions, always keeping you on the edge of the seat, engaged and captivated; true Telltale Games fashion.

While the classic Borderlands shoot and loot approach isn't present, this game allows you to enjoy the raw setting of the Borderlands at its purest, with the same calibre of humour, sorrow and horror we grew to love with the franchise. During the game, you encounter new loveable and valuable characters in the Borderlands universe (Rhys and Vaughn being my favourite!); and the old ones, who are kenned and loved by many, such Zer0, Athena, Jack and much more. You'll also recognise a grim cameo from characters, such as Commandant Steele.

Telltale Games are known for unforgettable stories and adventures, and this game certainly lives up to that expectation.\t\t\t\t\t
