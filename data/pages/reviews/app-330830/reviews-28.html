\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood story, very fun! There's not much I can say that won't spoil anything, and I'm not finished with the story myself. The good news is your actions DO have consequences! There isn't just the illusion of choice, at least not always. It's hard to tell which actions will have permanent consequences and which won't, but maybe that's a good thing.
A few things I wish were different though (although it's definitely not gonna change for this one, and the second game seems to be nearing release, so... oh well):
Biggest one for me personally first, is the subtitles aren't accurate or consistent! It's especially bad if characters are talking over each other and you only get the ONE subtitle. Not as bad as the straight up wrong subtitles but also annoying is that sometimes the subtitle isn't centered - it looks kinda like it WAS centered, but then they moved that word to the next line. Not a fan.
If you want to change a decision you make, it sometimes takes FOREVER to get back to it if it's not near a save point. Maybe that's intentional, but I find it pretty annoying. Also, if you have to leave suddenly, same deal because you can't save at any point of your own volition so if your power goes out or you need to leave suddenly for some reason and don't want to leave the game running idle, you have to start all over from the save point that might be 15 minutes ago or something
Your mouse does not start in a consistent position on the screen at all. QTEs sometimes take way longer than I'd like because I have to start by finding my mouse! I haven't failed one because of it, but it's very annoying. And I have ALMOST missed a very quick important dialogue choice because of it.
Also personally I'd like a little more info on what decisions are entirely irrelevant (it just changes the current dialogue, nothing more) and what could have an impact, especially if it's really big. Maybe make it optional, so people don't have to see it, but it would be cool\t\t\t\t\t
