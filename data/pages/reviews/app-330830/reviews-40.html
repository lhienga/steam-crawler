\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI sadly found this one extremely mediocre. I really liked Rhys, Vaughn, Fiona, Jack and Vasquez, but I wasn't really a big fan of all the other characters and most of the characters I do like get written out of the story in Episode 3.

The story is kinda a mixed bag, but still overall mediocre. Episodes 1-3 are decent, but Episodes 4 and 5 are painfully mediocre. In Ep. 4 and 5 a lot of the jokes just don't hit, and there's multiple moments where I'm confused as to whether or not certain scenes are supposed to make me laugh, or feel sad, which is a big problem. I think the writing took a plummet because they got a different guy to write the last two episodes. I will say that all the jokes in Ep. 1-3 and a select few jokes Ep. 4 and 5 are extremely funny and the opening title cards for Ep. 1-3 are great. I think if you're a Borderlands fan, you'll probably get much more enjoyment out of this game than I did.

6/10\t\t\t\t\t
