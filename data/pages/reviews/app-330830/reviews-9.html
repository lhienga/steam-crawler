\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI usually don't leave negative reviews, but this game was miserable.

I experienced clipping during cutscenes, texture overlapping, frozen animations, really awkward pacing during dialogue choices, and I was hard locked twice. I usually don't care about stuff like this, but when the entire game is on rails, this is inexcusable.

I know that it's a story - driven game, but the actual gameplay is abysmal. It felt insulting how simple it was, and sometimes missing an input doesn't even do anything. Also, they literally have button mashing as a core game play feature.

Also, half of the main characters are sarcastic, condescending ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ no matter what dialogue you choose. What's worse is that the other half act incompetent no matter what you do, so you're constantly getting berated. There is even a moment where the other characters get mad at you for not telling them something, even though there was never an option to tell them.
