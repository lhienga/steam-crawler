The main characters are fun and likable. The side characters are fun and likable.
The story is well written and fits perfectly into the borderlands universe. The jokes are actually funny.
I've heard the game is still fun without having played any of the borderlands games beforehand, although personally I recommend you at least have played borderlands 2.

My only complaints are that one of the villains (Vallory) is a bit plain, but they make up for that by having all the other characters just be so fun and interesting. One of the romances feels a bit forced and cheesy, and my final complaint is that the game is simply too short. I would have loved to see more of this fun dynamic.

Absolute amazing game. 10/10 Could not recommend enough\t\t\t\t\t
