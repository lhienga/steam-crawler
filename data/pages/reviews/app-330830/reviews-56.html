\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGet this game if you like Telltale games and Borderlands. Otherwise, skip it. I'm just a Borderlands fan and this was my first Telltale game. I'm not sure what I should have expected, but I didn't really enjoy it. This game is filled with Quick Time Events, which I hate. There's often long dialogue scenes, which is fine, but then without warning you'll be expected to press buttons in an action scene. Also a lot of repeatedly pressing a key to fill up a progress bar to continue, which is a waste of time. There are several scenes where you need to walk around and the movement is excruciatingly slow. The choices you make can change future dialogue, but have very little impact on what happens. The main positive thing I will say about this game is that the story is good and integrates well with the other Borderlands games. I actually think the story is better than any of the other Borderlands games. There are several memorable parts and funny lines. Unfortunately the game feels like a chore, and after completing, I'm not interested in replaying for different dialogue.\t\t\t\t\t
