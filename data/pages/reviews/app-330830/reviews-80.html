\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter replaying this for the second time, I once again realise what a perfect little gem of storytelling and comedy "Tales..." was. Even though I'm not a huge fan of Telltales' take on the adventure game genre in general, "Tales..." managed to use the post-apocalyptic space-western setting of the Borderlands franchaise in a way that is almost perfect. The style, the tone, the writing - it all lends itself so well for a classic point-and-click game that you'd be excused for thinking "Tales..." was where it all started, while at the same time expanding upon and honoring the world of the original games.

Of course, a lot of the main plot points and references are probably best enjoyed if you've played the earlier Borderlands games (especially Borderlands 2), but at the same time "Tales" has such good writing that it most likely could be enjoyed on its own as well.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot a fan of Telltale Games, but as a fan of Borderlands franchise, this game it's definitely worth it. Also it's enjoyable even if you didn't play other Borderlands games.

TFTB keeps the atmosphere and vibes from the main Borderlands games, but also introduces a new level of storytelling and a more immersive and intense story.
The structure is similar, if not the same, as the other Telltale works. Splitted in 5 chapters and under the rule "decisions matters". This game won't be a challenge for anyone, it's just meant to be a interactive story. But that doesn't mean you'll be disconnected from the gameplay, thanks to the decisions and quick time events it features, the gameplay will be as entertaining as conventional videogames (at least it was for me).

To sum up,
This game is for you if:
>You enjoy the Borderlands franchise lore and want to know more about it.
>You like story-rich games.
>You enjoyed other "decisions matters" or other Telltale games.

This game is not for you if:
>You like the Borderlands main games but you're not interested in the lore or the characters.
>You don't like point and click games.\t\t\t\t\t
