The writing is honestly, not very good, and that only became more obvious as I picked some of the available options. Why was everyone so shocked when I (as Rhys) told everyone Handsome Jack was in Rhys's head when every other time Rhys was asked I answered truthfully? The Echo Eye gimmick for Rhys's parts don't really provide any new information, it's all filler text pretty much. I feel like the whole game is at least 20% longer than it needs to be.

The performances by the VAs are great and the songs are great but if it wasn't for the fact that I wanted all the achievements for just playing through the game I would've dropped this during episode three if not earlier.\t\t\t\t\t

The Gameplay:
Choose things, do QTEs when not choosing, then choose more things. Maybe walk around to look at things. I can see this being a game that is not E for everyone in terms of gameplay.

The Story:
This is the mule that makes this experience work in any fashion. The story is enjoyable and has some very good funny moments.

The Visuals:
Very faithful to the actual source game it comes from, which for me is a little bit of a detriment cause I am not that huge of a fan, but I can't say its bad.

The Sound:
The voice actors are on point being this story to life which the entire game hinges on. Everything else works as intended and reflects properly on the source material.


The Experience:
A safe fun experience for anyone who wants to play through a borderlands game for its story. Has a good bit of replayability to it, so it will be a fun time. If you’re a min/max completionist, this will be a little bit of a nightmare as you would have to replay this so many times more than a person would normally want to. One of the better Tales experiences.
