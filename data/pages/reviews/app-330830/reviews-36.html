My Review
The best Borderlands story... despite being made by Telltale and not Gearbox Games.

For me, the other Borderlands games feel a bit weak in their stories, probably the best out of the trilogy being BL2. BL1 had no story, and BL3 story was decent, but just average overall. This game though, the story is amazing.

I like all the characters, they are all unique, have their own quirks, strengths and help each other, the game even makes you befriend robots and want the best outcome for them.

There are also some characters that make a comeback from previous games like Handsome Jack, Scooter, Zer0, Brick, and Mordecai.

The good:
    \t
  • Amazing story
    \t
  • Very good character development, whichever route you decide to take with dialogue options
    \t
  • Satisfying ending (for me anyway)
    \t
  • Remains true to the Borderlands series and world already built
    \t
  • Unique characters
    \t
  • Great audio and music choices throughout the game
    \t
  • Good voice acting

The bad:
    \t
  • No PS dual-shock controller buttons, although you can use a PS controller, the buttons displayed in-game are Xbox buttons, which is odd as this game is available on PS4. So I ended up using keyboard and mouse instead which can be annoying at times to select an option quickly
    \t
  • I kind of wish it was a bit longer, although some people have said they found it boring after a while but I disagree
    \t
  • Graphics obviously aren't amazing, but the art-style of a Telltale game saves it
