\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat an amazing story. You MUST play this game if you like the Borderlands universe and want to know more about the lore. The characters are amazing (you can't go wrong with Troy Baker voicing one of the 2 protagonist), the humor fits just right in with the Borderlands games (and I must say, I even prefer this kind of writing than the one Gearbox does, but that's me). You're also going to meet a lot of familiar faces from BL1, BL2 and the Pre-sequel and while you don't need to have played the first game, you absolutely HAVE to play Borderlands 2 and the Pre-sequel before this one, because of story reasons (especially BL2).

My only gripe with this game was chapter 5: it felt rushed and way less polished than the other 4, lots of low effort animations, bad lip-sync, weird sound effects and other stuff, truly a shame. That didn't stop me from enjoying the hell out of this great game, so don't worry too much about it, it's absolutely worth it anyway.

Bonus points: after the revel trailer of Borderlands 3, Gearbox basically confirmed that Tales from the borderlands will be canon, because we already saw characters from this game in that trailer, so if you're planning to play the next game, you're going to enjoy it even more by knowing what happened in this one.

Have fun :)\t\t\t\t\t
