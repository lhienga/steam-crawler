\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTales From the Borderlands gets a lot of bad press from people for not living up to the expectations set by TWAU and TWD, but I think it's a perfectly solid game, it gets the job done. In fact, I think Tales From the Borderlands is better than TWAU. It's got really strong characters that are all likable and funny (except for Janey Springs. Just had to put it out here). Rhys and Fiona are great main characters. The story was also really interesting, yes, it was weaker than the past games, but it was comedic and picked up pieces from the mainline Borderlands games in order to flesh itself out. The Telltale choice-based formula was kind of modified in this game in a weird way. Unlike TWD, choices felt less linear, but, just like TWD, choices felt like they didn't have much of an impact, like at all. I can't remember any examples of it happening, but I remember trying to prevent things and then, regardless of what I did, it would still happen. I see why it got bad press, but I don't think that makes it a bad game, just a bad Telltale game. 7/10\t\t\t\t\t
