\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, incredibly overhyped game which adds very little to the world of Borderlands. Humor is good and actually pretty funny at times, but as soon as a "serious" scene comes along, the writing falls apart completely. At several points in the game the amount of blatant "hurr durr corporations bad" soapboxing gets extremely tiresome.
Gameplay consists mostly of annoying quick time events and pointless dialogue choices which have zero impact on the plot. In the very limited number of scenes where the characters get to move around somewhat freely, their movements are ridiculously sluggish and feel like everything is stuck in slow motion. It takes forever just to make a character walk a few meters.
Autosave system which they have implemented is extremely frustrating and dumb for this type of game. In case you accidentally choose the wrong dialogue option, or you just wanna see what happens if you choose something else- you're unable to replay just that single choice, but instead you're forced to replay literally the entire chapter, which can waste you up to half an hour to get through.
I suggest getting it on a big sale, but only if you really want to play every Borderlands game, and you hate watching walk-through videos.\t\t\t\t\t
