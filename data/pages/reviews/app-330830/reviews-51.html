\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are a fan of the Borderlands series (which I assumed you would be to play this game), then I think this game is one of the best in the telltale series by far. In fact its one of my favorite game in borderlands series as well. As funny as B2 was and lot more fun to play then boring B3. It does everything right so well. You feel like you're right back in the world of Borderlands but instead of seeing what you've seen while playing the games it really tells a much different story. You get to see new locations and worlds, meet new characters, and see the return of older ones. I won't go into detail of which characters return as to not ruin the game but I feel like they were gratifying and chosen wisely.

The game does a wonderful job nailing the humor of Borderlands. The one liners and character interactions feel like they were ripped straight from the other games. There are plenty of other funny gags to read through the game as you're scanning locations from clues.

All the characters you meet are very well thought out and different from one another. They all have very different personalities that make their evolving character arcs very satisfying.

My character decision-making felt impactful in this game which as people will tell you in Telltale games that's not always the case. It is very obvious however which interactions I'm having that are going to be more important than others. Sometimes when the game says 'This character is going to remember that' you could very much count on the fact that it wont really make a difference.

I think the most surprising part of this game was how world changing the game is. Like there are couple scenes that I couldn't have imagine coming because it just effects everything. This game is not a side story of Borderlands, it's 100% a sequel to Borderland 2 and I highly recommend you play it.\t\t\t\t\t
