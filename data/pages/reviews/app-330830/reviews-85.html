\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh boy...I don't know where to start. I finally got to play Tales from the Borderlands after waiting for an entire year. I decided to play the rest of the Borderlands games first to understand the lore. The story of Tales from The Borderlands is extremely good. The characters are well written, all the interactions among them are so real and natural, the game is serious where it has to be but there's a very generous dose of comedy too and I don't even have to talk about the amazing cast. It was nice to see cameos from fan favourite characters and they fit in perfectly without feeling like they were forced. One of the minor things I loved about the game is that every character had a purpose and a part to play and weren't just fillers. The game is so full of little details and shoutouts to the original games that I almost felt like I was playing another Borderlands game except without the weapon farming. The game makes you care a lot about most characters and you wish you had more time to make decisions. I honestly ended up feeling sorry for a lot of characters, even the ones who wanted to kill you except Wallethead (screw that guy).
A Borderlands game without much of the mechanics of the original games was pretty much impossible but Telltale nailed it and proved otherwise. Man, I miss them so much. It was the iconic finger gun scene that got me to try the Borderlands series in the first place and now that I'm done with the game, I'm depressed there probably won't ever be a sequel to Tales. However I do wish the characters and the plot of Tales have a part to play in the next Borderlands game.
I've come across people who refuse to accept it as a Borderlands game and well, they're missing out since the game is a nice break from constant looting and shooting and you get to see the struggles of being a normal, random human on Pandora (or Helios). It was nice to not be a skilled vault hunter for once. It doesn't matter if you've played the original games or not but I highly recommend Tales from the Borderlands and in my opinion, it's the best Telltale game out there and you'll have a blast.
LONG LIVE ATLAS (I really hope they return in Borderlands 3) and finally... RIP my man Scooter..... CAAATCHH A RIIIDEEE!!!! \t\t\t\t\t
