\r\n\t\t\t\t\t7.3/10

I wanted to like this a lot more but overall it sort of fell flat. Still really solid and a great story but it definitely missed a few beats.

If you've played a Telltales game before you know exactly what you're getting here, if you like those and Borderlands I think you'd get a real kick from this. The story is fun and engaging with the perfect amount of Borderlands humor. If you haven't yet played a Borderlands game though, I do think that you'd need to, especially to understand some of the things happening and of course the references.

The bad come in with some performance issues, not sure why but there were plenty of strange dips, sometimes even just the background frame rate not even being close to the foreground. Lip syncing issues happened relatively frequently and while Telltale games normally have an issue where character emotion is a little bit harder to show off due to the graphic style, this one felt a bit worse than most.

Worst of all was the ending IMO, it definitely wasn't bad, but it didn't finish the story off where I'd like to have seen, too many open ended things left hanging.

Overall though, it's solid and if you like Telltale games and Borderlands, it's definitely worth your time. If you aren't into Telltale games, well this is more of those so you'll likely not get into it, even as a Borderlands fan\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a devoted fan of the Borderlands series for over a decade, I recently had the pleasure of delving into "Tales from the Borderlands," and I must say, it exceeded my expectations. This episodic adventure game from Telltale Games and Gearbox Software offers an incredible narrative experience that bridges the gap between Borderlands 2 and Borderlands 3, providing invaluable insights into the events that transpired.

One of the standout aspects of "Tales from the Borderlands" is its exceptionally well-crafted story. The writing is clever, witty, and true to the quirky humor that the Borderlands series is known for. The game explores the aftermath of Handsome Jack's demise and introduces a fresh set of characters who find themselves embroiled in a thrilling and often hilarious adventure. The storyline is packed with unexpected twists and turns, keeping players engaged and eager to uncover what happens next.

What sets this game apart from the mainline Borderlands titles is its focus on storytelling and character development. You'll meet a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique personalities and motivations. The choices you make as the player have a tangible impact on the story, allowing for a personalized experience that immerses you deeper into the Borderlands universe. The character interactions are brilliantly executed, offering memorable moments that blend humor, drama, and even moments of genuine emotion.

Visually, "Tales from the Borderlands" captures the distinct art style that fans have come to love in the series. The cel-shaded graphics bring the world of Pandora to life, with vibrant colors and detailed environments. The voice acting is top-notch, with talented performers breathing life into each character. The soundtrack complements the gameplay perfectly, enhancing the atmosphere and adding to the overall immersion.

While "Tales from the Borderlands" may not offer the traditional looting and shooting gameplay of the mainline titles, it more than compensates with its engaging narrative and memorable moments. It serves as a delightful complement to the Borderlands series, deepening our understanding of the lore and events that transpired between Borderlands 2 and 3. Playing this game after finishing Borderlands 2 is highly recommended, as it provides a satisfying continuation of the story and ties up loose ends.

In conclusion, "Tales from the Borderlands" is an absolute must-have for fans of the Borderlands series. It successfully combines an excellent story, well-crafted characters, and the signature humor that has made the franchise so beloved. If you're looking to expand your understanding of the Borderlands universe and embark on an unforgettable adventure, this game is a worthy addition to your collection. Prepare for a rollercoaster of emotions and an experience that will leave you eagerly awaiting the next chapter in the Borderlands saga.\t\t\t\t\t
