TftB made me nostalgic for the peak of Telltale's games and this one just might be the best. Playing this reminded me of the greatness of that early to mid 2010s run of the Same & Max games and The Wolf Among Us but TftB beats all of these classics on its own.

It does walk the line between energetic visual novel and adventure game but the writing is so great I never really noticed the genre. No cliffhangers, no post credits scene, just a big thick ass pile of resolution and stuck landings. Plus that end credits song is on point. First Aid Kit - My Silver Lining.

An instant classic and I'll be back for a second playthrough, there seems to be a depth here that was missing in pretty much everything Telltale published after Walking Dead S2 and after.
