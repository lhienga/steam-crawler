\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn original story set in the Borderlands universe, Telltale attempts here to craft an original story while still maintaining the bravado and style of the original games. I must admit I have yet to play any of the Borderlands games, but if this series is anything to go by - Il'l definitely steer clear!

Getting lost in a muddled plot that jumps aimlessly back and forth like a ping pong ball in a hurricane, Telltale tries to tell a spirited, funny and thrilling story but ends up just confusing the player. The sad irony of it all is that after five 2.5 hour long episodes, the conclusion ends up a woefully underwhelming experience akin to eating a perfectly average ham & cheese sandwich, only to discover that tucked inside the ham and cheese are tiny pieces of crushed eggshells. Not a savoury experience at all. If you happen to be a hardcore Borderlands fan you might enjoy this lighthearted romp of an adventure. All else need not apply.

VERDICT:
6/10\t\t\t\t\t
