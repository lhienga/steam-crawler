\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was my very first introduction to Borderlands and Handsome Jack and I loved it! The witty humor and the style of art (even if the animations were off/glitchy at times) were appealing. I also gotta love Troy Baker--I mean who doesn't? He's in everything! Even though I'm such a noob at first person shooters, this game got me into the Borderlands saga and I really appreciate it. It's a very easy introduction and gives you the small taste of the series without making you go loot some guns and listen to some boss ranting while you search for the right weapon.

PROS
-Plot: Very good with the interesting perspective of one of the notorious gun companies employees as a main character--Rhys! They did a great job showing its inner working from a finger-gun fight, to Handsome Jack fan boys and the cut-throat sassy environment of its prissy inhabitants. I'm going to go ahead and say Fiona was disappointing for me--she just seemed like "wanna-be Vault Hunter story," but she at least wasn't a Mary-Sue. The actual story is rough in Chapter 1, great in Chapters 2, 3, 4--and well--Chapter 5 is good--and also kinda not so good during the last fight. If you're fan of Voltron/Power Rangers you might like it.
-Characters: Nice relationships! Telltale is hit or miss with them, but they're definitely a core feature of their games. I think their strongest was Vaughn, Rhys and Yvette--and seeing Janey, Scooter and Athena after BL-PS was good too! Sasha and Fiona were...good-ish?

CONS
-Dialogue: Not "Glass him" bad, where you'll end up murdering someone by choosing the wrong choice. But the awkward conversations and lines sometimes are cringe-worthy.
-Animations: Oh buddy, facial animations are hit or miss with the game and so are the rest--they're stilted and awkward at times, and passable and not note-worthy at best.
-Game-Play: Glitchy--very glitchy. What's that? You clicked in the target area---you didn't. The game refuses to sense it. There's also the Chapter 1 game-stopping glitch at the break into the dome--have fun googling how to get it to work. While you Q and E and arrows are never an issue, clicking at the correct target---yeah, it sucks.

WOULD I RECOMMEND?
-Yes, if you're a fan of Handsome Jack or want to know some info on Rhys before BL3 or like most telltale/Borderlands games. If you're in it for a funny and cool story it also works well too!

-No if you've had serious issues with Telltale game mechanics--they're not better. If you're also bothered by the Stupid Protagonist trope--yeah you'll probably want to pass.\t\t\t\t\t
