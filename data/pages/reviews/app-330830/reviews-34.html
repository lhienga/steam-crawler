\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore my brief review just know that there is a Steam achievement bug that makes earned achievements pop in the wrong order or slightly delayed. I think in the first three episodes I experienced issues with this but ultimately I still got all of the achievements within the episode, just at weird times. So don't worry if you don't get the first three chapters of achievements in episode 1 and then they all pop in the finale chapter.

For the game itself, Tales from the Borderlands is one of the best games released by Telltale Games -IF- you are a Borderlands fan (which I do consider myself). If you have never played a Borderlands game, I think a lot of the fun jokes and lore will be missed and therefore the game might not be as great for you but as a fan, what a treat this game is! Between cameos from main characters in the series to jokes/humor that some of the early Borderlands games are well known for, Tales from the Borderlands fits perfectly into the Borderlands/2k universe and is absolutely worth a play.

In terms of some of the normal issues with the Telltale engine I actually think the art style of Borderlands helped a lot with not noticing the normal crashes and hiccups that I am used to in a Telltale game. So have fun, play Borderlands beforehand if you haven't already, and be ready to experience some classic Borderlands fun.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a big borderlands franchise fan, finally i had a chance to play this game. The writing in this game is top notch, the best thing that has been added to borderlands universe. Its really fun to choose your decisions and affect how to plot will be progressing, either you want to be a really kind person or the opposite, its up to you. Also because this is a borderlands game you will experience the typical borderlands jokes and atmosphere, the world building, and how this story connects the gap between borderlands 2 and 3 (well presequel and lilith dlc also count). I don't really have much to complaints, i just wish it was longer, also because this is Telltale style game, the reason to replay the story is pretty small unless you want to play the game with different series of decisions or just for nostalgia. I'm having a blast playing this game, especially when the emotional part of the story. although this game is mostly interactive story telling, you get to do some actions to which is really fun. Overall, i love this game, not really recommended if you don't like this kind of game, but if you want an amazing story telling, you might want to check this. Last thing, a sequel for this game would be cool. 8.5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
