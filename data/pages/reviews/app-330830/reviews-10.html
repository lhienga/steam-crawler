\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, I've not played 16 hours, that's due to me trying to play it and falling asleep twice while running it all day. The actual time I've played was probably 2 hours max. It was just that boring to me.

This is pretty much just a long cinematic with a few choices and clunky at best "gameplay". By gameplay I mean "walking" around in small locations, looking at stuff and analyzing it, using the cursor to aim at things and "shoot" or in predetermined directions to dodge. That kind of stuff along with the most common one of choosing between 4 options of dialogue.

So, it's pretty much just a slightly interactive video story rather than any sort of game.

Nothing that wrong with that, of course, but it's too slow to be enjoyable. Everything moves gratingly slow, pausing all the time for no good reason, long walking sessions with almost nothing to see and dialogue. Even when you quickly choose dialogue, the pause time is jarring.

So, my opinion is that this would probably do better as a small movie with tons of unnecessary parts cut out.

As for story, it's nothing that good. A bit amusing at times but it's so damn easy to just go online, find a novel or book and read that instead for a much more immersive, funny and more satisfying experience with faster developments or any cartoon since this has nothing much to add despite its lengthy duration.

Very disappointed that I spent money on pandora's box. Borderlands pre sequel might've been worth it but I dropped borderlands 3 and tales from borderlands since both were disappointments, so it was wasted money for me who already has borderlands 1 and 2.\t\t\t\t\t
