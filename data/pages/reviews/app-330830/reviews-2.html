\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wasn't expecting much going into this but it's actually really good.

For anyone who isn't already familiar with Telltale games, they're basically "Choose Your Own Adventure" stories. Some are heavily puzzle-based, this one however is not.

The gameplay of Tales from the Borderlands is mostly driven by timed dialogue choices and simple quicktime events. Sometimes you're able to roam freely around relatively small areas but these segments are rare. That's fine though: you play a game like this for the story and the well-written characters, not for the interactivity.

It took me a little over 11 hours to beat the game with each of the five "Episodes" lasting 2+ hours. None of the Episodes are particularity worse than the others, although I thought the first one dragged a bit due to having to get the plot established.\t\t\t\t\t
