\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHad this in my inventory for a long time, really glad that I finally decided to give it a go. Better late than never, I guess.

What's not to love? From the world's most resilient Loaderbot, to scenes which remind you of Power Rangers; this game has it all. Compelling story, charming characters, and abundant with quirky jokes / references that fans of the franchise have grown to love. Plenty of surprises and plot-twists as well.

One con that comes to mind is the quick time events. Some of it felt quite unnecessary and awkward, but oh well... Guess it was needed in order to pad out the interactive aspect of the game. Other than that everything is quite alright.

All in all, a solid game in the Borderlands franchise.\t\t\t\t\t
