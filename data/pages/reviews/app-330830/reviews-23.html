\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy god, this is good. I remember being interested in playing it when it first came out, but I thought it was too violent. Well, if you consider decapitations, peeling faces off and wearing them as disguises, hacking people up with every weapon imaginable to be....

Well, yeah, I guess it's pretty violent.

But what I didn't realize back then was how hilarious it all is. Dark humor, absolutely. And not for all tastes. But I was laughing out loud all the way through and completely fell in love with the lead characters, as they tried to bluff and BS their way through this nightmarish world. The gameplay itself wasn't nearly as hard as I was expecting. Most of the time you're given dialog options and have to pick one before the timer runs out, and the biggest problem was a cursor that didn't always make itself obvious when you were under the gun.

The action/fight sequences were mostly having to hit WASD keys, Q or E as they flashed up on the screen, and the timing wasn't bad, even for my slow reflexes.

Oh, and Chapter 3 (I think) wasn't saving properly, so if I quit before nearing the end, I had to repeat whole scenes the next time I started the game. That was annoying, but not too bad.

Overall, I'm very glad I went back to this classic. It runs fine on Windows 10. It started in the old 5:3 ratio and I was afraid I'd be stuck with that, but it was easy to switch it to 1920 x 1080 without any distortion or graininess. Highly recommended!\t\t\t\t\t
