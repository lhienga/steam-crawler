\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a great story.

Most of the negative reviews on here will tell you it has no gameplay. And that is true. If you're looking for a GAME game - just don't play Telltale stuff. It's just dialogue and QTE. If you find this stuff boring and just wanna shoot at things do yourself a favor and play the other BL games...

Some of the negative reviews on here will tell you it has bad writing. I would disagree. The writers in Telltale were phenomenal back then and of all the Borderlands titles this one has the best writing. Plus the writers here really did their homework when it comes to the lore and worldbuilding. They give an interesting story with interesting characters that make sense in the world of Pandora and Hyperion and it really feels natural.

A lot of negative reviews about Telltale in general are that choices don't matter. True AND false. If you judge it as if it were an RPG you'd be right, but considering this is nothing more than an interactive movie, it does one thing and it does it good - storytelling. Some of the choices do matter a lot within the confines of the narrative. You can't dramatically change the plot with your choices without expecting the story to completely fall apart. Could it be done better? I'm almost certain it could have. But it is pretty good as it is too. This isn't Detroit Become Human and even if it wanted to be, there were huge issues with Telltale regarding crunch and there still are with the gaming industry in general, so I would say nobody that came into a game like this with realistic expectations would end up disappointed.

And there are issues:
- on the technical side this game, like other Telltale games, is a mess (minor annoying performance issues, controller input issues, issues with sound, etc.)
- trusting the option for cloud safe is a bad idea (but you can still play the game offline without an account)
- too overpriced for a game that came out 10 years ago

But nothing game-breaking, just stuff some would find annoying which is kinda unfair considering a lot of the "AAA" games that have been coming out the last 5 years are 5 times worse than any Telltale title...\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a game that came out nearly TEN YEARS ago, it has held up amazingly. For most games nowadays, it's a toss up on whether or not it'll even be worth a single playthrough, let alone worth buying in the first place. I got this game through the Pandora"s Box Bundle while it was on sale, and it was well worth it. I recommend that if you are on the fence about getting the bundle, get it when it's on sale. However, if you are wanting to get this game standalone, absolutely get it.

Telltale Games absolutely crushed it with this one. The magic of Borderlands as a series was on it's fully graphic, comedic, and overall crazy display here. It has honestly felt like I had gone back ten years and immersed myself back into the adventures of playing with my friends, collecting loot, telling absolutely outlandish stories. The characters themselves are legends in their own right. Side characters are the same way and are as memorable as what most characters are as what you would expect in Borderlands.

The story is incredible. From start to finish, it is hilarious and holds the charm of what Borderlands is all about in its psychotic grasp. This game captures almost exactly what it is like playing with friends in Borderlands.

During my playthrough, there were some scenes that buffered a little bit, but nothing too drastic to where I would need to close the game out and load back in again. That could just be something on my end, so it's practically flawless.

If you are an achievement hunter, this game is relatively easy to complete in one playthrough. If you want to play this game to see the different choices, whether or not to see if they make a difference, or if it is just to hear how the voice actors deliver certain lines, go for it.

It is an honest one-and-done kind of game for me, but it is a worthwhile adventure.

Honestly, this game is like a time capsule. It is what games used to be. This game has charm. and those who made this game possible had a great passion behind this and it shows.

I wish I bought this game ten years ago when it came out. Yet, at the same time, I'm glad I waited so long until now to actually play it.\t\t\t\t\t
