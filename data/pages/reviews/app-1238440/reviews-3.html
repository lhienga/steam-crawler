\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhether you like a DLC for Planet Zoo basically depends on 2 things:
1. Whether you're okay or not with getting four or five new animals and...
2. How much the building-theme of the expansion pack suits your fancy.

The fact is, most negative reviews are because people feel they should get more new animals for 10 bucks, and/or their favorite animal wasn't in it, and/or they didn't like the fact that an animal was a re-skin of another animal, and/or they feel the DEVs spend too much time making building-props. Don't get me wrong, these disappointments are perfectly valid. Unfortunately, "4-5 new animals + 170-250 new props for 10 bucks" is the format Frontier decided on.

If you feel 4-5 animals is insufficient for 10 bucks, you're probably going to dislike all of Planet Zoo's DLCs. Unless the pack happens to have your favorite animal, of course ;).

For me, however, I'm a builder, and I love the fact that each DLC gives me so much new stuff to work with. And, after four DLCs, South America is my favorite by a landslide. I love the temple theme, I love the bamboo/tree-house theme, and I love the ability to pretty much recolor everything! The new plants are beautiful and vibrant and the new animals fit in just right. Making my ruined temple, half overgrown by the jungle, and placing three new habitats to match gave me a zoo which looks amazing! Creating a massive ant-hill in my ant-eater habitat was a ton of fun, the capuchin monkeys are enjoyable to watch as they zoom all over the place (though their screeches get annoying pretty quickly =_=) and watching the llama's is relaxing. Still haven't had the time to create a jaguar-habitat.

So, again, out of the four currently available DLCs, South America is my favorite.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not a huge fan of these tiny animal DLCs. Honestly, this pack still feels pretty weak for the original price, but it is far better than the Australia Pack.

PROS:
+Fantastic variety of building materials that look great and can even be used for other zoo areas. (Bamboo walls for East Asia perimeters, for example)
+New plants for your jungle and tropics look lush, and add beauty to every (related) habitat.
+Music is wonderful, as usual
+Animals are very pretty to look at, as usual

MEHS:
=There isn't any new Career campaigns, or other game-modes added. This doesn't bother me really but might be important to players who like doing more things than just work on their own zoo in Sandbox.etc

CONS:
-Not many animals, again. You get 4 main critters: Jaguar, Capuchin, Giant Ant-Eater, Llama. You get 1 Exhibit animal: Red-Eyed Tree Frog.

Much like Australia, which has a wonderfully diverse ecosystem, South America has a large variety of animals that could have been put into this pack but weren't. I find the pack fantastic for scenery building and bulking out existing jungle/tropic zones of my zoo, but when I tried to make a South America zone exclusively it just felt increasingly small. This, again, is similar to how I felt with the Australia pack: it's good, but not great. I like the new building parts, but hate the lack of animals.

Will it add diversity to your zoo? A little.
Will you reuse the parts it comes with for other builds? I think you would.
Would I recommend it? Yes at full-price if you really like the theme. Everyone else though, only on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
