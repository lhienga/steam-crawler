\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my biggest issues with Planet Zoo DLC is how little we get for $10.
I understand that times have changed, but for $20 each, Zoo Tycoon 2 was able to supply roughly four times the amount of animals. It will endlessly upset me how the focus of Planet Zoo isn't the animals, the thing that make up a zoo, but is instead of the scenery. As much as I love scenery, we are EXTREMELY limited in the animal selection. I know I'm not alone in stating that we're always upset with the lack of content we are getting.

The Amazon is one of the most bio-diverse areas in the world. There are plenty of animals to choose from: Caiman, Capybara, Golden Lion Tamarin, Kinkajou, SLOTHS. In fact, this DLC would have been worth $10 alone if included the amazing avian animals. As happy as I am that I have the Giant Anteater, and I adore the Llamas, I just cannot express how disappointed I am in the lack of new animals.

I'd wait for a discount if you're looking to buy the DLC. I'd prefer to pay $5 for this.
We NEED more animals, not more scenery pieces.

This game is called Planet Zoo, not Planet Park.
Please, fill up our animal selection.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy the game and one does get a decent amount of content for the price. Its okay, it isjust disappointing and frankly very underwhelming.
The arctic pack was lacking and this is not the big redeemer.

When they started the announcement with "chirping of birds" I was expecting some parrots based on exhibits in aviary style. Honestly I would not even care if they dont really fly but wiggle with their wings a bit due to engine restrictions. However, even after the DLC South America does not really raise the desire for building a specific zoo for it.. there are simply not enough animals to make it interesting and without the parrots it is utterly lacking in flavor and atmosphere. The DLC should have added at least double the habitat animals - its pretty much an exhibition show and the DLC did nothing to change that.

The new building parts are nice but fall in the categories "Temple ruins" and "Indiana Jones style jungle holywood style".. there are tons of bamboo which is a blessing for Asian zoos, but nothing there gives me a South American vibe.
The addition of new plants is quite limited as well, an area where they should have really stepped up.

Maybe a DLC adressing jungle biomes in general, adding animals from all continents would have been a better approach.

TLDR: Fair pricepoint and a bit better than the Arctic DLC, but overall disappointing and lackluster.\t\t\t\t\t
