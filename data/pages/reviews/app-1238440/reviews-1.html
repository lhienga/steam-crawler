\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe South America pack gives us a taste of the continent, with five diverse and unique animal additions.

Pros: five new, iconic and unique animal species; excellent new rainforest foliage!
Cons: overly themed build pieces, domestic species addition in the llama.
Recommend for: anyone who wants more South American species and themed builders.

The Jaguar is, of course, going to be the main attraction to most players - an iconic must have for your zoos (and it has a black Jaguar variant).

The giant anteater was my favourite habitat animal in the pack, a surprise choice and so much fun to watch in game.

The treefrog is beautifully modelled and is an important inclusion, representing rainforest conservation projects around the world.

Monkey lovers also get a fun addition with the charismatic capuchin - although they do tend to spend more time on the floor than they probably should.

The llama is an odd choice, being the first domestic species in Planet Zoo. It does come with a lots of colour variations though! I would have personally preferred the wild vicuña or guanaco for our zoos.

The foliage additions are excellent, certainly we can never have too many rainforest plants! Lush, vibrant, realistic and beautiful, you will get a lot of use out of these items!

The build pieces are strongly themed. As someone who prefers more generic zoo scenery to theme-park style decorations, I don’t often use the pieces myself. The temple pieces and bamboo pieces are versatile though, and clever builders will find use for them outside of themed decor.\t\t\t\t\t
