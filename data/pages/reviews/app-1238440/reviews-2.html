\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a pretty strong DLC offering. Though it lacks a Timed Scenario or Career missions, it adds a great deal of useful build pieces and Tropical foliage to the game that make up for it. The South American representation in vanilla Planet Zoo is severely lacking, so the species added by this pack are extra welcome.

+ The jaguar is of course an extremely iconic animal, and it is rendered beautifully in PZ. In my opinion this superstar species is necessary for any Central or South American themed areas in your zoos. The black panther variant is an extra point in favor of this addition.
+ Giant anteaters are a personal favorite of mine. Bizarre, unique, and beautiful, I'm super grateful that I didn't have to wait long to get this species ingame.
+ Black and white capuchins add a lot of diversity to Planet Zoo by being the only small, arboreal monkey as well as the only Platyrrhine in the game so far.
+ Llamas are one of very few species in Planet Zoo suitable for petting zoos, which some players will find super valuable. I don't care much for them, but they do take advantage of a dynamic color system which is pretty neat.
+ The red eyed tree frog doesn't really fit the theme of the pack as it only exists in Central America, and Planet Zoo launched with a handful of nice frog species anyway. Still nice to have, even if there were probably better choices for an exhibit species.

Overall, I'd rank this pack an B+, totally worth grabbing during a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
