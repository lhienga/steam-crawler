\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to start by saying I was excited for a new pack and hoped for improvements after the first taste from the Arctic pack.

The lack of content in a $10 pack is astonishing, not only that but they hid it with content added in the free update. Almost half the South American plants came with the free update. The update gave us more content than this pack did. Speaking of the packs content brings me to the topic of the animals inside it; Jaguar, Llama, Giant Anteater, Capuchin and Red eyed tree frog.

The Jaguar

A very pretty animal, though most of it was most likely made through reskinning one of the other big cats animation rigs. I don't have a huge problem with this as this animal was highly requested for a pack of this subject. I especially love that it is possible to get a melanistic jaguar, aka; Black panther.

The Llama

This choice is odd for a South American pack, especially when everything seemed to have an Amazonian theme. I would expect a Llama in a pack dedicated to the South American Andes. This animal is cute, but a disappointment when in this pack specifically.

The Giant Anteater

I have no problem with the Giant Anteater, I love it a lot. This animal seems to have a unique model and unique animations. (skipping over the Capuchin because I have a similar opinion about it as the Anteater)

The Red-eyed Tree Frog

Now I don't hate that this animal is in the pack. HOWEVER, this frog is a box in a $10 package with only four other animals. Cute but practically useless when it comes to interacting with it.

What would I do to make this pack feel more worthwhile?

I preface this segment with this all being my personal opinion (like most of this review).
This pack could be better by being just $5 dollars OR add more content besides just building materials. The building content is not the problem, the animals are. The pack would feel more worth the money is if there were seven habitat animals and three exhibit animals. Yes, this would take more time and possibly be priced a little higher, but it would be a lot more content. Another fix would be more interesting animals, like a capybara, south american coati, three-toed sloth, black caimen, maned wolf, rheas and plenty more.

There is so little content for one of the most lively environments in the world.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely love Planet Zoo and this is my favorite theme so far.

Plus an amazing beautiful NEW MAP!

However, I do agree with the others in that it feels rushed and should include more items. But I will say that I have thoroughly enjoyed the pack, I am more than fine with the price because I want to support these developers.

I hope people take into consideration that recent world events may have also had an effect on this pack. Maybe they have been understaffed in the last few months, but still wanted to stick to their well known release schedule. I find it hard to believe they didn't plan to include new challenges when they gave us a new map. I feel like they just didn't have time, but wanted to give this to us anyway.

Overall, I am in love with the new map, all of the animals are adorable (that llama strut), the foliage is gorgeous, building pieces (more free placing panels), theme pieces, and the beautiful paths!

If you genuinely like the game, this pack is great and well worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
