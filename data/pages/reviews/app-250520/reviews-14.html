\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't let my short play time fool you, I have spent around 2,000 hours on this game and I don't regret a single second. It might be one of the greatest, most addictive RPGs I've ever played.

It's a hardcore single player combat focused build-porn RPG pain simulator with an endless re-playability due to almost infinite build possibilities.

Instead of writing review I'll just leave some pro-tips:

-get mercantile to 105 effective as this will unlock all the best items at the stores and you need it because...
-crafting is the king, all the best stuff is crafted. You can't dominate with the junk you find and you need high quality materials that you can (pretty much) only get from merchants
-speaking of, once you get your home you can buy benches that will provide 15% bonuses to crafting skills so keep that in mind while spreading skill points
-use Underrail Character Builder to plan your build ahead because in this game you can't respec and that's important because...
- you must specialize. Jack of all Trades is a dead Jack. In this game you go all in. You build a character and playstyle around one skill
-Stealth makes this game much easier. Position yourself and ambush these bastards
-Traps and molotovs will let you force any build past Depot A
-Don't be afraid to cheese it. This game is ridiculously hard so abuse line of sight, lay down dozens of traps to lock your enemies, use gas grenades and any advantage you can find
-Crawlers are ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. Remember to use flares, traps and molotovs to deal with them
-Tanking sucks. Glass canons are gods. Kill your enemies before they kill you. Optimise for damage. Besides, they can't hurt you if they're dead or you are out of line of sight already.
-Time magic rocks. Every single build benefits from Stasis (get out of jail free card), psycho-temporal contraction (more AP and MP) and Limited Temporal Increment (cooldown reduction)
-Crowd control is awesome. Traps, molotovs, traps, PSI... Did I mention our lord and savior traps???
-Different kinds of armor work better for different situations because there are various types of damage and weapons. Same goes for Energy Shields.
-When crafting Energy shields craft two and go LOW-LOW and HIGH-HIGH. You'll know what I mean. You can't block crossbow bolts or knife attacks with high frequency shielding because they're low speed weapons and vice versa.
-TNT explosion can lure all the enemies from the map into one place for easy nuking.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnderrail is about making your character's build, looking for synergies and optimizing skills, overcoming tough enemy encounters and exploring the Underrail. It's made in OG Fallout, Wasteland series fashion. Top down game, small areas seperated by loading screens. Players and enemies have turns and spend their action/movement points. Every weapon type and Psionic school can have build made around it. It is possible to mess up your character and there are no reset/respec option. But that doesn't matter much if you just want to test and see the gameplay (and there is Underrail character builder online if you are a game nerd like me and prep in advance).
1. New players might struggle with sense of direction because there are no quest markers or similar stuff. Directions are given in written dialogue. At the start of the game certain areas are blocked off and some of them have enemies that just instant kill the player. That is the best way of the game telling you where you're not supposed to go yet. Players will need to memorize how different locations connect. As of now there are global world map that uncovers as you explore, that helps a little.
2. There are no voice acting.
3. Ambient sounds and music are pretty good.
4. Enemies have diversity and diffulty progression is reasonable.
5. Lore is explained in written dialogue, computer messages, item descriptions. It's ok.
6. In RPG fashion players have a choice which factions to help and which to destroy.
7. Underrail world is quite big.

It's an interesting game if you like the style of it. Total I'm currently on my seventh playthrough and planning atleast one more.\t\t\t\t\t
