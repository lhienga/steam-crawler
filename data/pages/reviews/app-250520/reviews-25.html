\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnderRail is not a game for everyone, it attracts a certain type and repels everyone else. It is an extremely difficult and unforgiving game that suffers no fools kindly and expects you to figure things out for yourself. If you played Fallout 1 & 2 then you may like UnderRail too or so I have often heard (I have played neither). Your first playthrough will be rather frustrating as you figure out where to go next and often what you are supposed to do, contrary to what Sseth said, the places you want and need to be are not often easy to find. Good luck getting anywhere in Deep Caverns or obtaining the Ethereal Torch and figuring out you can use it to destroy the Shadowlith without a detailed guide. Same goes for finding the Acid Hunters and joining Oculus.
That said, I wouldn't be my most played game on Steam if it were bad and I very much enjoy it. If you like a challenge, RPGs, and experimenting with different builds in every game you play then I strongly recommend UnderRail.

Also, ♥♥♥♥ Todd. You can convince his "mother" to let him go by looking at all of the family paintings in the area then keep asking about Todd's wife. Convince her he was happier when he was with her. If you're a far less patient person than I however then by all means just murder that dipsh1t and enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
