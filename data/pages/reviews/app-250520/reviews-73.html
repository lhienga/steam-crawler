underrail is an excellent game with absolutely wonderful writing with satisfying progression that ticks all the switches in my head

HOWEVER, I want to emphasize that this game is not for everyone and (from my own experience) you’ll be having a bad to mediocre time early game on your first “real” playthrough. On Top of that, even on normal difficulty the game will be exceedingly difficult at certain parts in the story to the point where you’d have to be a masochist to enjoy it (this only applies on your first play throughs, where you don’t know how to metagame hard enough to win)

Also, a lot of the directions you are given are completely verbal; there aren't any pin pointers on a map or anything of the sort. If you simply skim past npc dialogue or forgot what they said you will very easily get lost and not have a good time.

Finally, the combat (especially early game) can feel extremely clunky and unsatisfying at times. This game’s combat is almost entirely dependent on dice rolls so at times you can get unlucky and feel like you got cheated out of a combat encounter.

Because of this, I cannot recommend this game to anyone else I know, as I fear that they won't be able to overcome these hurdles that the game has and enjoy underrail for what it really is.

But don't let my words stop you from playing this game, the writing in this game is astonishing and I felt like all the npcs (including even the most minor of characters) were real, living people with goals and ambitions, this feeling only broken ONCE in my time playing this game

As a word of warning, DO NOT TRY TO MAKE YOUR OWN BUILD ON YOUR FIRST PLAY THROUGH, FIND ONE ON THE FORUMS. If you cant find one, use the one in the link https://steamcommunity.com/app/250520/discussions/0/1697169163406743418/. If you do choose to use the build I provided, for the love of god, DO NOT GET THE 44. PISTOL THAT YOU CAN GET IN THE BEGINNING OF THE GAME.
