\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame's hard bre. RTFM because even then you will get zone'd quite alot on your first playthrough. If you want to play something that would be the equivalent of having lots of charisma in other cRpg make a psionic character with the Will attribute through the roof, you'll get lots of bonus on the persuasion and intimidation skills AND you'll get story elements you wouldn't get otherwise. With high perception you can detect secret hidden passages though. Again, this game is meant to be replayed multiples time with different build and its way quicker when you are acquainted with the mechanics, know the map and skip through the dialogs because youve already read it the first time.

So yeah, just expect unavoidable combat and don't build anything else than a badass killing machine. You can do it with crafting and intel and other stuff but this requires knowing the game quite well so for a first playthrough just focus on having a guy that has decent offense and defense.

Overall it's a very good cRPG that does things right but is somewhat punishing if you go into it blindly with a very bad build. If you find yourself quick loading way too much don't shy away from starting again from scratch its way quicker the subsequent times and its fun to play a different build\t\t\t\t\t
