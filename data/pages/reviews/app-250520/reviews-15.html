\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is without a doubt one of the best, if not the best, crpgs I have ever played. The level of depth to the gameplay and the world, as well as how well they are tied together, is truly amazing. The atmosphere is bleak and claustrophobic. So many mysteries surround you, but poking around in the dark is never safe. Combat can easily kill you, and the way you build your character has to be seriously considered. I would not recommend a jack-of-all trades, nor a glass cannon.

Survival is brutally difficult in the world of underrail. For some gamers, that makes achieving victory all the more satisfying. Every now and then you find little spots of light-hearted humor, a touch of kindness, or a deeply satisfying hint at some lore that has been out of your reach.

If you enjoy a slightly more casual experience in your games, I would highly recommend going on Easy mode or following a build guide. Underrail is not a peaceful world, you will not be able to roleplay/persuade your way through the game without fighting for your life. If you find yourself motivated by challenges, rather than frustrated, then dive into normal. My first playthrough was over a hundred hours and an amazing experience. The Expedition DLC is also absolutely fantastic and features some of my favorite parts of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
