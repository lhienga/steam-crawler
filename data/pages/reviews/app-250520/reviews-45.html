\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like Arcanum and Fallout 1 & 2, you're probably gonna like this one.
It's a turn base RPG with lots of crafting and lots of TEXT dialogs (so be prepared to read), and a somewhat unforgiving difficulty if your build sucks. My recomendation is to start with something easy like a heavy armored dude who uses assault rifles in normal dificulty. Most of the game is easy but some parts are as hard as MY FIST! (when i keep dying to the same guy, over and over again).
The story is actually pretty good with a nice built-in universe and characters. It has that deep immersion of classical RPGs that makes you uncanny in dark places.
Overall, it takes some time to learn and understand the base mechanic so here is a tip:
- Buy both the base game AND the expansion if you really want to give this game a try. The expansion offers a lot of new content and some quality of life improvement that are really necessary for the game.
- Crafting is essencial. You might do Okey without it, but you will probably regret it a lot later if you don't put anything into crafting.
- Either follow one of the many many builds in the forum, or dadicate some time to build your own. This game does not have rollback level mechanic, so if there is a skill that you really wanted but you needed 5 extra points in X attribute, you're gonna regret it A LOT.
- In general, builds that focus mainly on one thing are way better than builds that focus on lots of things. Not that hybrid build are inherently bad (not in the least), but you need to know what you're doing.
- Don't be afraid of the difficulty, just keep in mind that you can throw stuff like grenades, put traps on the floor like bear trap (very reliable in early game), try different approaches to beat a hard situation. Also don't forget to make some saves from time to time, the game can sometime throw you into "no way home" or "no return" situtations without as much as a warning.

Bonus tip:
- Psycho-temporal Contraction is an incredible support psi ability that is viable for most builds (especially non-psy builds).\t\t\t\t\t
