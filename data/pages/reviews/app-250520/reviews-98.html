\r\n\t\t\t\t\t9/10 Exceeding all expectations for an indie CRPG - an homage to Fallout and Immersive Sim-like emergent gameplay

+++ Sniper/Assault for first playthrough/psionic second run. ~100 hours+ each including expansion.
+++ Game requires some investment to really get going.
+++ Psionics are very fun once understood. Quite a different style of gameplay.
+++ Game is kino in all aspects sans barrier to entry. By prepping through playing Fallout 1/2/Arcanum/DoS it was easier to get into
+++ The world is alive, varied, dark, cyberpunk-ish with a touch of "magic" and a dash of sci-fi
+++ Wonderful narration and story that scratches the wanderlust itch just right. The novelty of what's out there...
+++ Hoarding mechanics will produce many extra to-and-from. Do make sure to drop all your loot at merchants feet due to limited demand tied to time (~2 hour refreshes)
+++ Possibly the most amazing Act 3/Main boss I've ever experiences in gaming yet.
+++ Real life sequencing/decoding mini-games were quite surprising when encountered though had to look up solution via a solver website; Thanks whoever you are
++ There are fast forward game speed shortcuts (NumPad+/-) which are better remapped elsewhere that I discovered only many hours in
++ Do review shortcuts; most time-saving ones are the bottom bar, shift modifier, ctrl/alt click item movements
++ Skill/build quantity impressive; Enjoyed my time as a stealth sniper with assault rifle backup
++ While stealing is "fun" in hindsight it seems like wasted points specing into it/losing time pilfering every NPC I encountered/not much noteworthy gained
++ Difficulty curve is quite challenging. Lots of anon stories getting stuck somewhere due to difficulty/respawns/lack of saves; Save scumming is part of mechanics in this gem
+ Ski ski ski ski ski - quite a wonderful additional to spruce up the game
+ Expansion adds a good ~30-40 hours first time content though at this point the game was growing old
+ Crafting produces the best items in the game
+ No real reason to raise lockpick/hacking past effective 100-110

--- Depth south eastern map was bugged and crashed the game for me every time; lucky the map could be bypassed
-- Very difficult to understand how to progress forward on a few quests. Sometime the directions can relate to dozens of maps in an area - had to look up guides. Frequent moments like this add to the barrier of entry filtering prospective players
-- Carry weight insufficient/perks increase subpar - item management frequently a chore; Weapon durability/repair per shot ratio is horrific. Endgame had to repair every few fights

If you an get past the first few hours this game really opens up and delivers. If fond of post-apocalyptic genre/CRPGs the game's mechanics are a wonderful blend of strategy, tactics, prep and exploitation of gameplay mechanics to allow glorious moments of 1 v. 20 to work in your favor\t\t\t\t\t
