\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI finished it on my second playthrough because I "softlocked" my first one at the final area.

This game is not for the faint of heart and you should have a high stress tolerance before you try it. The dialogue and the lore is extremely good and the combat is very tactics-based and is unforgiving if you screw up. If you expect it to be like the older Fallout games, you will find a mostly different game.
I went without any pre-done build from the forums and it is possible to beat this game this way. But if you're going for this route, a word of caution: Specialize for a combat discipline (either Melee/Guns/Crossbow, not a combination of these) and invest points in throwing and crafting. Trying to be a "Jack-of-all-trades" WILL ruin your experience and you have to plan a lot in advance. Preparation is the bread and butter of this game. Use resistances correctly for the situation and (quick-)save often.

Aside from these rough patches it is probably one of the best games I've ever played. And I still have to start the expansion.

I didn't mention the perks. Before you level up and select them, do wise decisions. If you did your selections well you can take anything, otherwise you will suffer.\t\t\t\t\t
