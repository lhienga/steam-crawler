\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is unique, addicting and ambitious, but also very flawed. There are multiple problems with it, coming both from limited budget and the developer's inexplicable superiority complex. Graphic and animations are not too bad, but sometimes they can be a bit off-putting. No voice acting is a problem, because the amount of text in this game is overwhelming and you'll definitely experience heavy dialogue fatigue. Combat is pretty great imo, mostly thanks to the many options offered to the player through crafting and feats, but the best strategy, like in most crpgs, is the turn 1 wipe, because most enemies will 1 shot/stun you if you let them move. There's also that superiority complex I mentioned. Styg refuses to put a respec mechanic in a game, in which a single build mistake can completely ruin your playthrough and make you restart after ~20h into the game. There's also the pitiful carry weight, backtracking and merchant bs, where you can barely take any loot with you and chances are the merchants won't buy it anyway, or even if they do, they won't have enough money to pay for half of it. For those reasons I don't think this game is playable without using cheat engine to alleviate some of the idiotic design choices. Finally we arrive to the difficulty discussion. Personally, I don't think the game is too difficult, however I do think that the lack of any build freedom is ♥♥♥♥♥♥ up. I'm a hopeless min-maxer so it doesn't bother me that much, but for anyone who prefers to make their own choices and mistakes - you can't and I sympathise, because I had to start a new playthrough 40 hours into the game and look up a "meta build" on the forums, because I wasn't able to progress any further on NORMAL difficulty. With that lengthy rant, I'd like to say, that for every bad there's also good and you won't find any game quite like this one. If you can forgive the bad, you'll have a very unique and mostly enjoyable experience. It's not for everyone though.

P.S. I really don't like the fact, that the main character is doomed to be an incel. Every potential love interest is killed off-screen, or discarded and I hate it.\t\t\t\t\t
