things to consider:

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts really good; It feels like a more polished, more advanced, and more modern version of fallout 1 and 2, which i have both played and enjoyed. If you are on the fence about the game, consider your skills, consider if you have brain functions, and consider your ability to read guides. If you are lacking in any of the departments, this is not the game for you. It is rather hard, but quite engaging and really fun.

My only issue with the game is a lack of scaling in some pathways in the late game. For example: if you want to be a psychotic grenadier, like me, the game is great until you hit the limit for grenades. at some point, you run out of options for making grenades, and you have a really hard time defeating end-game enemies. For other pathways, this is not an issue, but it is a little restrictive for some of the unique playstyles. (I also may just be bad, so take this with a grain of salt)\t\t\t\t\t

One of the best CRPGs ever made.

This game is an homage to Fallout 1/2 (maybe a bit too much, but I don't mind) and has cyberpunk elements.

Dystopian future, humanity lives underground in vast systems of caverns and railways.

The scope of the game is huge. I'd say larger than Baldur's gate 2 in terms of exploration and things to do. Really decent crafting system. The only downside is the brutal difficulty where "easy" should really be "normal" for most new players.

Having said that, because the game is old, build guides exist, and so does an unofficial character builder. Kinda glad I waited to play it now because there are so many resources around it.

Soundtrack is VERY FO1/2 inspired, and is super immersive.

I strongly recommend this game if CRPGs are your jam, and especially if you liked Fallout 1/2.\t\t\t\t\t
