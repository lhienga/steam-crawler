\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCan't really give this game a good review for a few reasons... The concept and mechanics are cool. But the way they are implemented right off the bat are awful. First of all... The combat is way too clunky. My first encounter in the metro tunnels I get jumped by like 5 bandits which normally isn't a big deal. Except this game punishes you for using any kind of guns. Why is it melee gets 50 turns and 50 movements while I fire 1 shot and suddenly I'm out of action points and can only take 3 steps? Talk about clunky as hell and unbalanced.

As well as the annoying buzzing that's constantly radiating through the entire beginning of the game. It's incredibly annoying and makes me want to lower my volume in order to avoid getting a headache. This game seems like it has a good story and a lot of potential but it's very unbalanced and the combat is extremely unfair and punishing without giving you a chance to really do much of anything.

It wouldn't be a big deal sometimes in certain encounters like a boss fight or something. But right away at the start of the game is a bit overkill and way too frustrating. It's way too punishing and just kills my interest in playing entirely.

This game has potential but it needs some serious patching to polish it and make it much better. I don't mind the graphics and the music seems kind of cool. But that buzzing is awful and the combat is so bad I feel like I'd have to mod or hack the game just to have some kind of balance and I don't want to do that just to progress.

This game has a lot of potential and is a cool concept. But just badly implemented in a lot of ways. It needs some more attention to detail and testing. If I were the dev of this game I would be asking my staff what they would change if they could. Sometimes opinions from a lot of people give some good criticism to create a master work of art.

I won't say I hate this game because I enjoy turn based RPGs. It's a nice break from constant fast paced fps and third person games and I just need to give my reflexes a recharge and my mind an exercise. But when I feel like a game is unfair at every turn it's just boring at that point and feels dull. Please consider heavily updating this. I feel like it would perfect this game into a masterpiece!\t\t\t\t\t
