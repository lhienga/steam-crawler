\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-Extremely well done crpg worth buying and trying out for it's many builds, huge content and quests, deep lore and mechanics.
-Provides several ways to approach problems making your build really shine while on other times you have to crunch up with tactics which makes it extremely engaging and will never get stale.
-Feats are amazing compared to other crpgs, they give you more tools to dominate gizmos instead of just upping your damage and hit chance or some other passive stuff. Some feats are broken but it keeps your build tight enough so that you can't pick others.
-The story is extremely well written and scattered across the underrail not shoved into your face, you can also learn from it and interact with other characters you've met in the past who will probably give you directions or give you their opinions on the matter, the npcs feel alive and have huge amounts of dialogue (not like generic quest-giver gizmos).
The lore is also fully connected to itself, plot-holes are nigh and everything relates to something else which happened outside the gameplay or inside for you to experience.
-The expansion is truly magnificent for it's content value, a new world you can embark to which has new enemies, hidden lore, a huge map and more gigs to try out which will also appear in the normal underrail.
-The difficulty is hard but extremely well done and fair, once you get going you'll only want to go higher and higher which means more enemies, new spawns, tighter more clean builds, smarter npcs, greater bossfights and survival tactics pouring down into your gameplay.
-Overall this game is an amazing experience, it scratches every itch a player would have and for someone who loves crpgs this is pure utter gold, an example of a game done right which is engaging, fully replayable, balanced (even for your enemies who are just like you), deep and extremely fun.
-At last if you're new to the genre it may be quite hard at first so I suggest you try out a build from someone else, expect bad rolls, evade some encounters to come back later when you're stronger and to also keep your build tight and never spread thin. Cheers.\t\t\t\t\t
