Ok, so I do like this game, Id like to start off by saying that, super unique Rouge-lite game that has a ton of potential. Offers a little more of a hardcore concept, monsters are strong, your guy isnt.
The reason i say not recommended at the moment is because one, the game feels really short. There are only 3 unique zones that just cycle in the same order over and over and over again, even though I get it's a Rouge-lite and yes, by definition they repeat, something about how this is done makes it feel like Im just re-doing the same thing over and over.
One thing i personally don't like is once you unlock the repeating mode, you have to sacrifice 3 boons and its difficult to choose the one you want to part with, it would be nice to just choose 3 instead of hope you don't get screwed.
However the BIGGEST reason I don't recommend this game ATM is BECAUSE IT CRASHES CONSTANTLY. In a rouge lite game, especially a 3 hour run to just have it close out of nowhere is a HUUUUUGE bummer.\t\t\t\t\t
