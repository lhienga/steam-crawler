Long review: I got into this hoping it would be the next coming of Nuclear Throne. I almost bounced off because it's not, but it's got lots of other interesting features. There are progressive storytelling and base unlocking elements like Hades, splorchy sound effects and bizarre art of Atomicrops, an action shooting roguelike main game loop in the vein of Nuclear Throne, and chasing (or being chased by) bosses around various segments of the map almost like Monster Hunter. It doesn't go as hard in any one direction as any of these games does, but the sum total is something pretty cool.\t\t\t\t\t
