\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am not exaggerating whenever I say this may just be the most underrated video game I have ever played. Virtually everything about it is flawless, in my opinion. I am still early, but I’m talking mostly about the fundamentals here. It seems like one of those rare cases of somebody having a vision AND executing it perfectly. It takes a lot of aspects of games like Enter The Gungeon, and makes them significantly funner and more accessible. One of my biggest complaints with Gungeon is that, aside from being ridiculously difficult even for a roguelike veteran like me that’s put thousands of hours into the genre, I felt like there was TOO much RNG. This game feels much more forgiving in its approach, especially in how you acquire weapons and power ups. The art style is one of the very beast I’ve seen, as well. I’d put this right up there with Hades as one of the best roguelikes ever made. It truly deserves so, so, so much more praise.\t\t\t\t\t
