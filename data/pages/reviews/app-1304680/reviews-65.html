\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn insanely atmospheric, and uniquely stylized roguelite in the vein of Nuclear Throne and Enter The Gungeon. Seeing as this game took inspiration from those games, I just simply had to try it. As it turns out, I love it. It has such a unique atmosphere, and adds a breath of fresh air to this little roguelite subgenre. As well as the unique atmosphere, and eye candy of an artstyle, this game has some fresh mechanics to spice things up. You have to "hunt" the boss of a stage, finding it, weakening it, and chasing after it flees. You can also stomp on an enemy's head after jumping, and chain stomp with multiple targets.

Super glad I picked this up. 8 years ago, I got super addicted to Nuclear Throne and haven't really had anything feel like it in particular since then. This is giving me some HUGE NT vibes in some ways, like the walls of the levels and bosses charging through them. It's also unapologetically bizarre which is really kin to NT and adds to the atmosphere. There's a lot of charm in this game, and you can see it as you play. I caught myself noting that voice lines of various things just simply had enthusiasm and life to them.

I wish this game a great development cycle as it comes out of early access into 1.0.\t\t\t\t\t
