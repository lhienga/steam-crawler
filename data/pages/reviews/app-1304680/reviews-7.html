\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like Nuclear Throne, Gungeon, Isaac, Hades or Risk of Rain: play this ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ game. Its up there with recent hits like Atomicrops, Going Under and Tiny Rogues in terms of how fun and addictive it is. I've been looking for something to scratch my never-ceasing roguelike itch, and this game is one helluva backscratcher. I just wish I discovered it earlier.

It's got hella style and presentation, reminding me a lot of Atomicrops, but with a more quirky, modern Cartoon Network show vibe. Like Ren & Stimpy meets Adventure Time or something. Love the voicework as well. It really adds to the charm and personality of the game. Music's okay. Nothing bad, but doesn't really stand out. Catchy motifs tho!

While the gameplay may take some getting used to initially, it becomes crazy fast-paced once you get comfortable. A jump instead of Gungeon dodge roll or a Hades dash may seem weird, but by GOD does it work. It rewards aggressive and risky play (especially once you know what you're doing), while still giving enough leeway via health and ammo drops. Like Nuclear Throne, the game seems to know when you're low on health or ammo and adjusts its drops accordingly. Great weapon variety, excellent upgrade variety and really good enemy and boss variety. The unlock system is really good and rewards repeated play and using new weapons and upgrades.

Don't wait for a sale, just buy it! Please support these devs!\t\t\t\t\t
