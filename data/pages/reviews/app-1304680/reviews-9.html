\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game just feels awesome to play. The animations are wacky and energetic; the attack indicators are precise; almost every weapon feels unique and great to use; and the dodge jump, it's just brilliant.

Instead of the usual dodge roll, this game has its unique dodge jump which not only makes it easier to determine when and where the player will land but also allows the player to perform the stomp - a mechanic stolen from Super Mario himself - that can be chained into even more satisfying combo stomp. The jump dodge also works well with the melee weapons by allowing you to perform jump attacks.

Speaking of melee weapons, those are absolutely goated in my opinion. I see someone complained about melee weapons being useless, and I see that as simply skill issue. You see, melee weapons in this game have high risks and high rewards. They require you to stay close to the enemies, but their AOE damage are crazy. To compensate the high risks while playing these weapons, the block mechanic was implemented only for these weapons. However, you don't really need to block most of the time, as you can simply dodge attacks by jumping. What makes jumping better than blocking is that while you can't block while attacking, you can jump and jumping doesn't cancel your attacks. Aside from that, jumping also allows you to move farther when using certain melee weapons that make you move when attacking. My favorite combo is the mace, which's second jumping attack makes you jump across the entire screen when timed right.\t\t\t\t\t
