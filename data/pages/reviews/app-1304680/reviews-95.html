One suggestion that I have for the game is to add a colour blind option for the "lootables", the yellow that flashes over the objects is hard to catch for me. Perhaps an option to re-colour this will do.

This game is amazing, and I 100% recommend this game for people who enjoyed Binding of Issac, Risk of Rain 2, or Downwell.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most fun and refreshing roguelites I've seen in a long time. Full of charm and challenge, this game is a true indie gem.

A fusion of Nuclear Throne and Monster Hunter is something I never knew I wanted, but Voidigo provides just that and is even more fun than I would have expected. The weapons all feel unique and satisfying, providing good variation. The melee weapons feel especially satisfying, allowing for close-range combat that packs a punch.

The highlight of this game must be the boss fights. Each of the bosses bring unique challenges to your runs and the telegraphs of their attacks match up perfectly with their action speed to test your quick thinking and reaction time. The boss-roaming mechanic can make for interesting sticky situations that will spice up each run. There are also many difficulties to cater for players of all skill levels, from people new to action roguelites to people wanting to push their skills to the limit.

Even in early access, the little design decisions that make a huge difference have been meticulously designed for maximum enjoyment. From how much ammo you receive to how quickly the enemies attack, you can sense the polish in design to make the player feel the perfect amount of badass when you take out a boss in a clutch moment.

Voidigo is definitely one of the most exciting under-the-radar roguelites out on the market right now even in early access. This game is definitely worth your attention, and I look forward to following the journey to full release.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game really is something special. It's got great humor which is conveyed not only through its text such as item or power descriptions, but also especially through its art. The attention do detail here is insane.
When your character gets knocked down, they do a mad scramble to get back up, wildly swinging the gun to where you're pointing.
When you kill a boss, their ghost comes out of the corpse, starts crying and flies away.
One of the power ups is a ghost orphan. When you get this powerup, the ghost actually starts flying next to your character. If you smack the ghost with a melee attack (and this has nothing to do with what the powerup actually does) the ghost will get knocked to the ground, make a sad noise and then fly back to your character.
The guns are insane, the powers are strange, the enemies are weird, I really like this game.\t\t\t\t\t
