\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is such a great game that 15 dollars for it seems unreal. How can a game be so polish in this state, gameplay, artwork, graphics and ideas is out of this world, literally, and I mean OUT OF THIS WORLD. There is so many interesting weapons and powerups that even the lower tier ones are just really fun to use. There is a turtle gun, animal bullets lotions, cat bat and so many other cool weapons and not just the wacky ones. The animation is outstanding, probably one of the best of all time, pixel arts is also very solid. But most of all, the gameplay, it is just amazing. Though a rouge-lite, it is the most balance and less dependent on RNG, compared to Enter the Gungeon and Nuclear Throne, this game doesn't go heavy on health and ammo instead it all comes down to the players skills and especially reflexes while Voidigo also introduces just a new twist on this type of genre, you can sprint, you can stomp which can used as an attack or mostly dodge the enemies bullets, when you get hurt too bad there is a shield to protect you so you don't get one hit. I mean what more could you possibly want?

However, there is a couples of things I want to talk about. People said the only downfall of this game is the lack of content, while this is true, I would like to point out that this game is getting constant update, It should be taken into account that the game is in ver 0.1.5, that is a long way from a full release. Judging from the version of this game alone, I would say that there is alot of content already.

This might be a PERFECT game, out of all the reviews, 9 of them are Not Recommended. I would like to point out that these Negative reviews are just inconclusive, one of them only complain about the 60 FPS cap, another think that Early Access game only need a couples touches before being released and the current state of the game is basically 85% of the release version, others complain there is no Chinese translation, some people said "It a good game but it is not their type". Based on these things that you gave Voidigo a negative review???

And oh, this game works pretty good with proton if your are using Linux, like myself :)

All and all. this is a solid game and for the one who like Enter the Gungeon, Nuclear Throne and other simliar games should be a must buy. The only thing that stops this game from being a massive hit is TIME.\t\t\t\t\t
