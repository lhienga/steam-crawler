\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played a lot of rogue likes and.... this is a masterpiece. I actually said that playing this "this game is a masterpiece"
Then I saw it was recommended by the steam curator gaming master pieces and was like "yeah, that fits"

Crazy upgrades, crazy characters, crazy bosses, crazy challenge. I think the character who controls two characters at once, him and the robot shooting is probably my favorite character in any game ever.

You get in the game and get upgrades, get new guns, and chase around bosses on map zones like monster hunter. Every upgrade feels unique and fun. There are almost no boring upgrades like just +stats everything seems to do something unique. You can goomba stomp enemies then rats explode out of them to leech their hp, shoot a minigun that fires snakes, get a scythe turn into a tornado and spin around. It's all there, and sometimes when games set out to do whacky and goofy upgrades the gameplay isnt that tight or it feels not as polished with balance/difficulty. This game everything in regards to gameplay is done extremely well polished.

It is quite possibly one of the few roguelike games that accomplishes perfectly every single thing it sets out to do.

There are almost no flaws for a roguelike twin stick arena battler. Other developers will copy gameplay mechanics from this game because it does new fun things, and it does them exceedingly well.

Last year we had vampire survivors that was 10/10 new and unique, this year we have voidigo.\t\t\t\t\t
