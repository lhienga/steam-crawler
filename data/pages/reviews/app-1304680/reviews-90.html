Update: After a few months, the game have gotten even more challenging and also more fun to play. More variety in play style is definitely nice to have and even the map is nicely spiced up as well.

However, I do still think the game should have something like a theme (events, unique modifiers and even NPCs that drastically change how you approach picking weapons and powers) for each run to make each run stand out from others even more. As of now, although players are presented with decisions, it does not have much of an impact on the run itself. Most power ups are invariably good or bad and doesn't have much of a conditional or contextual benefit or demerit.

Overall a good purchase and while its still flawed, I believe this game has a lot of potentials. I've been with the game almost since day one, and all the updates that the dev pump out are of high quality.
