\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVoidigo is an amazing game that doesn't show what is have to offer for the first hour or so. Even after that it relies on player perseverance but for those that do the game opens up and becomes a true gem. It mixes elements from the great Roguelites like Binding of Isaac, Nuclear Throne and Enter the Gungeon, while still remaining fresh and new with many new ideas.

The strongest aspect of the game is the sheer amount of items, synergies and strategies. Pair that with a risk & reward system that has been executed in a great way (similar to BoI) and you just have a great formula.

Any downsides? As of now I'd like to see a bit more enemey variety, especially in the boss department.
Also the game is very scarce in the information it gives. In a similar way that BoI does, you're not really sure what you're getting until you tried it. One of the reasons why the game is relatively hard to get into.

Still, I can wholeheartedly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
