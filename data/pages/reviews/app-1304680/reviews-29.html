\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving just finished the main story line on Rogue difficulty I am ready to say how I feel about this game.

The only way I can explain how I feel about this game is telling you that whenever I start a new run, I feel that optimistic curiosity I used to feel whenever I started a new run in the original Risk of Rain.

I feel this is the best roguelite game of the last few years taking the best from the genre defining titles and leaving behind the bits that dragged them down.

This game wouldn't exist without Isaac, RoR or Gungeon however it elevates them because of how much they have contributed to this formula.

Here are the little things to know:
- To reach the true ending, you need to unlock a lot of things; the game will tell you what things you're run into you already have
- The game is totally fair. You will lose due to your own greed
- The music from the last fight is the best boss music track there is and I will die on that hill
- Always visit the shop last, the contents will be rolled based on what you've picked up

TL: DR 11/10 Would use this game to teach myself impulse control again\t\t\t\t\t
