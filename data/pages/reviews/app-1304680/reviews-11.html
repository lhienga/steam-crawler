\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLook, I own a LOT of rogue-likes, I guess you could call me a fan.
I've always appreciated the details in games and I can at the very least say this... The details in this game are AMAZING.
I mean the animations are all wonky and wacky and are insanely entertaining, from a weapon reloading itself to a screaming sword holding guns shooting swords ?
It definitely holds true to some of the best rogue-likes out there like nuclear throne and enter the Gungeon with a little dash of crack cocaine introduced in there for *dramatic effect*
it's chaotic, with a lot of content, it's fun and most of the time hilarious, you'll undoubtedly get used to the little shenanigans after some time but with 12.2 hours played within 24 hours, I'm not even close and i'm always pleasantly surprised, that's enough to warrant the price of this game. Support these amazing devs for bringing us quality games.\t\t\t\t\t
