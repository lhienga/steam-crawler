\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has to be my favourite game of all time!

Because it is a roguelike, it has randomness. However, the game never felt like it was unfair to me which could result in me being underpowered, but instead every mistake felt like it was my own skill issue. And finally beating the impossible difficulty final run had me more excited then I've ever been in a game.

The gameplay feels like enter the gungeon, but with more and faster movement options. However the map system, while confusing at first (for me at least), really stole the show:

To activate the boss you need to clear 2 beacons in different sections of the map, BUT you can't leave a section of the map before clearing an active beacon, ensuring you won't just clear everything before the boss spawns. After the boss spawns it will follow you across the map until you clear all beacons before being able to take it down.
This system really shakes up the traditional format of a boss room which keeps you on your toes.

No spoilers, but my favourite moment of each run has to be the final boss encounter (phase 1 specifically).

The gameplay rocks.
The music rocks.
The animation rocks.
Lots of replayability

It's overwhelmingly positive for a reason.
Do yourself (and the devs lol) a favour and get it.
