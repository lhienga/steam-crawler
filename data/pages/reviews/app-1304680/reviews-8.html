-Emphasis on intractable power ups over stat increase items with a pretty fun elemental synergy system

-Very flexible dodge mechanic that incorporates aggressive play that further synergies with existing items

-Incredibly amount of QoL be it through it's quick travel mechanic, knowing precisely where everything is and how streamlined the whole experience feels

-It's weaponry is incredibly varied and even the lower rarity guns feel satisfying to use

These points above are just a few compared how grand the whole game feels, I always thought this genre was generally solved but Voidigo clearly proved me wrong by evolving past its predecessors and carving it's place as one of my favorite action roguelikes of all time.
