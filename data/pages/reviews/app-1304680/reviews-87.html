This game was on my wishlist since day 1. I decided to buy it because of the new "Companion" update and it's probably one of the best game I've ever played and this year's most insane game!

I love everything about it.

The gameplay is super fun, it's like Enter The Gungeon but a bit better in my opinion.

The music and sound effects are truely INSANE, every bits of sound is so enjoyable. There's a different kind of feeling in every weapon and the minions/bosses's sound effects are sick.

The universe is so well made. I love the narrator's voice. The minions, the bosses, the characters. the guns, etc... Everything is amazing! The Shop Guy is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ hilarious, it reminds me of some kind of character from Rick & Morty.

There are alot of different power ups and weapons for unique builds. I somehow stacked 2x bullet speed and range, 2x frog bullets, 1x fire bullets and 1x bouncy bullets in my first run and it was truely wonderful.

The animations are literally perfect. I also really enjoy the color scheme of the game. SO PURPLE & PINK! <3

The options are stacked. If you dislike camera shakes, just like me, you can slide from 100 to 0%. You can toggle sprint, change loot color, turn on enemy bullets outlines, etc... There are plenty of options for you to adjust, and I'm all for it!

This game deserves way, WAY more recognition than it has.

If you're hardcore into roguelikes, you'll love this game. Once you unlock Impossible difficulty, no other game on the market compares to the fast-paced "just don't get hit" action offered by Voidigo.

Unlike many of the other reviews here I'm not a fan of the art style, but that never detracted from the incredible gameplay. Engaging enemies, optional challenges with great rewards, and a difficulty level that constantly ramps up as you clear and loop the game keep things fun all the way from your first run to your Impossible loops 50 hours in, plus the game is frequently receiving high quality updates so it'll only get better from here. You will die plenty once you get to Impossible, but I guarantee you'll have a great time doing it.\t\t\t\t\t
