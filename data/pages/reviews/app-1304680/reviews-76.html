\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is absolutely incredible. Like, I NEVER write reviews for games, but this game is just too good for me to stay silent. The developers have done an absolutely outstanding job making a funky, vibrant, and bustling world for you to explore, shoot through, stomp in, and just generally vibe with. This game is pure roguelike excellence with satisfying progression, funky, stylistic, and vibrant gameplay, and a visual style that makes you just never want to leave. Every gun and weapon is unique and fun to use, every enemy fun and fair to fight, every powerup and item cool and valuable in their own way, and every boss just a generally awesome time(especially with the almost monster hunter esque boss chasing mechanic)! Oh yeah, and despite how the game may become an absolute cluster at times, all attacks are very clearly telegraphed, and there are plenty of accessibility and qol options for you to tweak and ease your gaming experience(and increase visibility of enemy attacks). The devs have clearly put an insane amount of love and passion into this game, and it shows.
As an aspiring game dev, this game is phenomenal, and I admire its style and polish so much!
LIKE SERIOUSLY, if you're a fan of roguelikes, and havent given this game a shot yet, do so....like RIGHT NOW.
IT. IS. AMAZING.\t\t\t\t\t
