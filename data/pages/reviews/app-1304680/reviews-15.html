\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI usually dislike roguelike games, but this is one of the betters.
Before they created this game, I used to watch some animations made by these devs. You can easily see the same goofy & eccentric humor of theirs has carried over in to Voidigo.

The good:
+ Unique artstyle, sounds and environments
+ Good soundtrack
+ charismatic narrator :)
+ Funny gunplay and weapon designs

The bad:
- Can get a bit too hectic and incoherent, by that I mean the screen will be cluttered in effects and bullets, especially with more than 2 bosses on scree (trying to react to anything that's going on will be extremely difficult, even more so if your own weapons are spammy and cover a wide area)
- Story could use some work, the small bits of lore you get is mostly through item descriptions
- The challenge is either too easy or too hard, it doesn't feel like there's a middle ground, but this isn't necessarily a bad thing depending on what kind of player you are

8/10 in my book, it has a good amount of replayability, though I'm not one to replay these kinds of games much after finishing once or twice.
I wish semiwork lots of success, and I'm hoping they'll be inspired to continue working on this game or make a new one.\t\t\t\t\t
