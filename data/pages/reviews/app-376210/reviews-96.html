\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore diving into my review, I want to state that I have only played official EU servers. So pretty much what you can expect if you buy this "game" and try the experience for yourself.

I don't get why people like it.. I mean sure, the dinos look great, sounds and animations are alright, the map itself isn't bad at all, just a bit large and sometimes pretty empty. The gameplay, however...

Let's start with the fact that every start of a new dino is immediately a coin toss wether or not food actually spawns (carnivore). You can easily waste half an hour just wandering the giant map with basically no stamina lookin for food that just isn't there until you die. And if something *does* spawn, more often than not it's something that is physically too fast for you to catch. This will repeat itself until you get lucky and don't die in your juvenile state for once. *Content*, what a marvel.
---
Next up, combat. Horrible. "Go run into that dino with no collision box and left click until one of you dies". Not to mention accidental friendly fire that happens this way. Easily a dino you spent hours on down the drain, yay.
---
What happens if you die? You guessed it, you spawn faaaar away at the other end of the island. Choosing a spawn location isn't a thing. Did you have friends you played with? Good luck, you can now spend two hours painstakingly making your way back to them. And surprise, you will no doubt die again of various reasons mentioned in this review, resetting your travel progress yet again. Again, *content*! :3
---
Next up, basic functionality. The GUI and social features don't even work the majority of the time. Want to see information on your group, maybe leave it? Good luck, the button doesn't work again. After all, why would it? :3 Please either kill yourself and reset your entire progress or waste 10min logging out and back in again and hope that resolves it.
Also, the bars for hunger and thirst are a nightmare. It looks full on your screen, doesn't it? Surprise, you're at least 20% lower than you thought! Gotta get some not spawning food quick or you die, haha :3
---
Lastly, the community is a toxic dungheap. Communication isn't a thing, "bite-first-ask-questions-later" is an alltime approach apparently (even within social species like the Utah Raptor), and dinos 20 times your size kill you not for food, but for ♥♥♥♥♥ and giggles. I know killing is a part of this game, I am aware, but what's the point if you're not eating my corpse? That's just senseless griefing.
___________________________________________________
I have owned this game for years, and I reinstalled it multiple times because what it wants to achieve is just such a great thing. But every time again after wasting a fear hours of my life, I get disappointed. I think this review is me drawing a line and making peace with this game. I'm done. I can give only give so many chances to something that could be so great but fails so horribly.
This game is great if you want a useless timesink, but if you actually want to get stuff done I can only advise to stay far away - especially if you plan to play something with friends. It will only bring you stress and frustration.\t\t\t\t\t
