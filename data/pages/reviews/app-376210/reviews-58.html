\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell I did like to think that I had fun playing this title. The novelty of being a realistic dino grower is pretty charming, but man does the push for "realism" just get in the way of player convenience. Whenever I boot it up I spend more time struggling to find food, getting lost, and or getting jumped by someone as desperate as I am. The game's also had a huge hacking problem you've probably heard before, along with horrid ping and frequent boots off the servers. Devs kinda seem like they're focused more on adding stuff rather than fixing stuff which is odd.
This was my experience with the Evirma branch btw. There's another version of the game called Legacy. It's more stable but waaaay more boring.

Other times were spent sitting my fat Triceratop's butt in a berry bush till he got thirsty and I needed to get that lumbering butt over to a lake to drink then went over to another bush to sit down again with a slow heavy satisfying thud. Did that for like 7 hours. Peak Snorlax simulator game play.
In conclusion, the game will always look better when I'm watching someone's highlight reel of hyper specific moments where everything's working and the role play of a dinosaur is at it's best, but playing it for 70 plus hours now, I just see so many of the cracks that feel like they'll never be repaired.\t\t\t\t\t
