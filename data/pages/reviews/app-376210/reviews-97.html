\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has become unplayable recently. They nerfed night vision so you can't see anything on your screen except your own eyes at night now when using it. Almost no active servers unless you play legacy. Every server requires you to join a discord, and most of the dinosaurs are locked behind a paywall in those discords because they choose what dinosaurs are available to play. Can't even play the Deino (my favorite dino) anymore because you need to pay server admins to be allowed to play as it now. Going near water often causes you to be kicked from servers now. They recently changed diets so some creatures require a special diet you can only find in a "sanctuary", which seems to be too far away from where you spawn to even travel to before dying now. Most servers have rules (which i like for realism), but unless you run an outside program to record every second of your gameplay, then you will never get help from any admin when someone breaks the rules against you. Only way to even play the game in it's current state is in Legacy, which is missing a lot of features and only has one map that all of the servers use. I've not once seen a server running any other map even though there's supposed to be a few they can choose from. Official servers are trash since they allow mix-packing of herbivores and carnivores and have no rules, there's absolutely no realism when playing them. High AI servers are still extremely easy to starve to death in, you can travel for days in the game at points without seeing or hearing any food when playing carnivores. If you play certain dinos like a Giga, you will be singled out and murdered for no reason whatsoever by every creature under the sun, herbivore or not doesn't matter, they will hunt and kill you relentlessly. It's literally impossible to become full grown unless you have a huge pack to protect you when playing as a Giga, even then you will be singled out if you are the only Giga. This game in extremely unbalanced and until they find a way to change that, end paywalls for creatures, and enforce rules using code instead of admins for servers, this game is not worth playing.\t\t\t\t\t
