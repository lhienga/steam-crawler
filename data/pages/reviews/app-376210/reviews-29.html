\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLegacy was decent for what it was, but I cannot in good faith recommend Evrima.

Nearly everything about it is not enjoyable, with the only good perks of Evrima being the unique dinosaur abilities and customization. The map is unbalanced and most times you either end up starving to death, get killed because the only source of food is being camped, or get bugged out of existence. Visibility is so terrible that you'll starve before finding a single AI to eat. And when you do find food after a frustrating 2 hours, thanks to the new scent system, everything in a 5 mile radius will know and come straight to your location to kill you. And no matter how good your computers specs or internet is, expect to be rubber-banded right into the jaws of death.

The stamina system was reworked as well, which never needed to be changed from what Legacy had in the first place. Now not only are you playing growing simulator, you are now also playing walking simulator. You have to wait a full 10 minutes before you can sprint again. Good luck getting anywhere and have fun "playing the game" sitting under a tree for the majority of the time.

What also made Legacy more fun was the ability to play with friends. You still can, but now you can't invite anyone from the menu. You have to find them without name-tags in the large map, granted you don't die first. There is no global-chat now either-- so good luck making any new friends as well.

Evrima is littered with hackers, but I can't exactly blame them. It's probably the only way to make the game fun. It would be a lot better if they kept everything from Legacy and expanded on it. The new skills make growing a dinosaur for 8 hours actually seem like it might be a fun idea-- if only they didn't ruin every other aspect of the game. Granted, they don't have access to the Legacy files anymore, but they could have at least taken inspiration from it.

Alas, the developers clearly do not care about their community.\t\t\t\t\t
