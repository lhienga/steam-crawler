\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's been tragic to watch the direction The Isle has taken from the move to Evrima and abandonment of Legacy. I had a lot of love for this game and the community was the saving grace that kept me coming back. Unfortunately, you'll find that the community is gradually pulling away the further along the devs continue to go on Evrima - actively fighting against community criticism and implementing changes that were not asked for or openly spoken out against by the community they are attempting to build this game for.

Each (far and few between) update, I have attempted to play Evrima and run into the increasingly common issue of maxing out at 25-30FPS regardless of the server density. The mechanics of some of the dinos seem genuinely fun but if you can't play the game without massive framerate drop or stuttering, that takes all the fun out of it. The stat balance leaves much to be desired as they consistently have swung far one way or the other and the diet system still needs a lot of tweaking (although these are both issues I can see being remedied in the future, provided the devs actually listen).

Legacy The Isle still holds a dear place in my heart and it will be sad to see it and the communities I've come to know and love within in go as the vast majority of players either can't run or don't see the benefit of swapping to Evrima due to the performance issues and hard-headed devs turning a deaf ear to their community. I hope that one day I can change this review for the better, but this remains to be seen.\t\t\t\t\t
