\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe isle is probably the most impressive and by far the most polished Dinosaur survival game on the Evrima branch.
The game is immersive, each Dino feels unique and well implemented. Obviously nothing is perfect, there are kinks here and there but overall, the quality you get for 20$ is a steal.

So why am I not recommending the game ?

Recently, just a bit before Christmas of 2023 the team behind the game release a content patch that added two dinosaurs and some balance tweaks. Which at first was praised and adored by the community and myself to be quite honest.
The issue as to why I cannot currently recommend this game is that ever since this patch was released, the AI system of the game is completely broken. Just before the Christmas vacation, the patch was let wild on the servers and a storm of starvation followed. Ai was so scarce it was practically impossible to survive outside the hot-spots (Where players gather). This phenomenon is an ageing meme in the community, Migrations, Diet were added especially to fight off Hot-spots and encourage travelling zone to zone, alas if you can't go around to hunt anything like Deer, frogs, fish, goats, boars or any of the sea animals, there is literally no purpose to exploration and travelling, in fact you are punished for it with death due to starvation.

You might think: ' Well, they'll fix it ? Right ? As I write this review, they have release a handful of unofficial hotfixes to try and repair the AI spawn system which the first attempt was a colossal ♥♥♥♥ up despite being funny due to an extreme abundance in frog spawn, so much it caused servers to crash and the game was entirely unplayable.

And right now ? (2024-01-10) Another hotfixe was released and while servers are stable we are back to step 1: Starvation.
These attempts to fix the AI system were so brazenly a failure that I don't understand how can a team ♥♥♥♥ up so bad ? Call me rude or heartless but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ this is so frustrating. We paid for a game and right now we are being punished to play it how it was technically meant to be played. Just 20 bucks, yup you're right. But this is probably the most frustrating 20 bucks I spent ever because if the AI was actually functional and properly integrated, this game is/would be so much fun.

I will alter this review whenever they actually repair the AI system but this issue is going to be remembered for sure throughout the community.\t\t\t\t\t
