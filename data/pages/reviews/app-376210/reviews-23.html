\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs far as the game itself goes, you probably wont find a better realistic dinosaur simulation game on the market. The servers are filled and the dinosaur selection - while restrictive - is acceptable with an expectation of future additions. The map is vast, the mechanics are interesting (altough it is at its core a walking/fighting simulator) and the visuals are very pretty.

Despite this, I do not recommend the game. While it checks out on the surface, it is rotten below.

The core issue with the game is the developpers. They communicates poorly their intentions for the game, are not reliable regarding future content direction and actively encourage an echo chamber of supporters on the main channels by banning anyone that does not agree with their views.

If you have the misfortune of enjoying this game, you'll be stuck with a dev team that does not appreciate - or even respect - their own community and will actively go against their wishes.\t\t\t\t\t
