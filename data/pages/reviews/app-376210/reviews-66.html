Too bad, you're a target now cause someones upset on Discord and wants you dead. Get ganked.

Seriously, you will have to experience someone living out a power fantasy on you. Not joking, its inevitable if you get this game.

Seriously, I'm bent about these people lol. Go on their Discord and you'll get flash-banged and nerve gassed at the same time. Some of these people ARE dinosaurs.

The recent gameplay fallacies introduced in the November 2023 "Gateway" update have left a sour taste in my mouth, and have ultimately driven me away from this game as a whole.

I get the idea of wanting to lean into a realism standpoint, but at some point, it goes from being a game to being an interactive graphical showcase. A game should, first and foremost, be a game, with entertaining gameplay in mind. This game does not have entertaining gameplay in its current state. It has frustratingly lacking gameplay, on par with the self-proclaimed "stupifyingly realistic" gameplay of Desert Bus.

Overall, if you buy this game, do not play on the active development "Gateway" branch. Play on the Legacy branch or the "Spiro" branch. It may be disappointing to miss out on new additions to the growing roster, but it will ultimately be a better experience overall until the active development branch receives some changes.\t\t\t\t\t
