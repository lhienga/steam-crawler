\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm gonna say this game is 50/50.
Is it fun to run around as a dino and chew on grass? Definitely yes.
Is it exhilirating to fight another dinosaur so that you may live another day? Absolutely.
But is it fun to wait on hours to grow a dinosaur to 100% only to be stuck on some weird rock? No.

The game itself is great fun and it can be good to just turn your mind off from things and go full dinosaur but at the same time it has some obvious downsides too.
For example the official servers are usually full so you may need to find a community one which is a really big hit or miss.
The game is stable enough on lower end pcs as well although for the best experience you may need a beefier set up.

(As a footnote if you read this long, the trailers and pictures on the store page are from the Legacy version, the game since have overcome a complete recode and is looking better. Although the dinosaur selection is just a fraction at the moment but new dinos are constantly being made.)\t\t\t\t\t

A Mesozoic Masterpiece

The Isle is a game that truly brings the Jurassic era to life in a way I never thought possible. From the breathtaking landscapes to the incredibly detailed dinosaur models, this game immerses you in a world where survival is the name of the game.

The attention to detail is simply jaw-dropping. The realistic behaviors of the dinosaurs, the dynamic weather system, and the day-night cycle create an environment that feels alive and constantly evolving. Whether you're roaming the lush forests as a herbivore or hunting for prey as a carnivore, every moment is filled with excitement and tension.

The game's mechanics are intuitive, allowing for a smooth learning curve even for newcomers. The progression system is rewarding, and the variety of playable dinosaurs ensures that each playthrough feels unique. The multiplayer aspect adds an extra layer of thrill as you interact with other players, forming alliances or engaging in intense battles for survival.

In summary, if you're a dinosaur enthusiast or simply someone looking for a unique and immersive gaming experience, The Isle is an absolute must-play. It's a testament to what can be achieved in the world of gaming when passion meets innovation. Strap in and get ready for a journey back in time like never before!\t\t\t\t\t
