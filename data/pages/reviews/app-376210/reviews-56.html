\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, its good and its bad.
its hardcore, which if you like hardcore. its good in that aspect, the game isnt gonna hold your hand with anything.
i like the concept, i like the play. the lack of dino diversity sucks. its like here, you can play one of these 10 dinos. and dont get me wrong, they are all very unique, and play completely different than each other, which is great. but, need a lil more selection, now this is being addressed. slowly.
HOWEVER.
the biggest gripe with the game, isnt the game itself. its the server lag. like FPS is fine, but server lag and rubber banding is out of hand. and its not like its uncommon, you can go in a area with like 10+ people and EVERYONE complains about the server lag. sometimes, (often) its so bad, you cant fight or play really at all. so that should be addressed, either issues with the code to the server, or the servers themselves. dont care. fix it. you could have 1000+ dinos for a selection, and the game will still be bottom end rating, until the lag issue is resolved.

another gripe, and this might just be me on this one. but what the hell is up with the maps? like ya, they look nice. but the size is ridiculous. i appreciate the biomes and the places to adventure around. but, its a pvp driven game. if your a predator, there is like 1 or 2 places to find other players. like, the entire population in condensed into 1/10 of the map and not like... crowded either, just you will run into people as you are doing stuff, and the other 90% is unused, and if you go explore it, you die of boredom. like they could easily cut the map to 1/3 its size and improve game play just because of that.
the long grow times on some dinos i don't actually mind.
the AI is.... there. its probably one of the dumbest AI i think ive ever played with. but its there, it serves its purpose i suppose.
the game could be great, with a lil time. right now, its ok. the lag sucks. but its medicore. in its current state.
ALSO just as another gripe, and this is for new players. like there isnt any tutorial, right, like 0 hand holding in this game, which ok, not a huge deal. thats something youtube can supply. BUT, where do you go? when i was new, i spent the first 4+ hours wandering the map, and didnt see a soul! because i didnt know where that 10% of the map was and that everyone typically just hangs out there. so after playing on a full server for hours, i actually tried to refund, because... i couldnt find anyone. its a little different now, since i know where they are. but for a new player, thats frustrating as hell.\t\t\t\t\t
