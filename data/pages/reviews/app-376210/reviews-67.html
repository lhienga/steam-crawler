\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom my 1000+ hours in this game, this is a fantastic game, both versions of the game like Legacy and Evrima, still it is a good game. However, my first Gameplay in the isle was really rough, was by myself, had no idea what i was doing or how to hunt. I wasted a lot of hours trying to grow dino's and i die from pitiful deaths, like starvation, or falling to my death, or getting attacked by a player and i do the wrong moves. But i eventually learn from my mistakes, and i became a really good player at both surviving and fighting in this game. When i'm a Giga, Rex, Sucho, Allo, sneaking up on a player, my heartbeats fast, dispite me being use to this kind of thing XD. This game is very fun in PVP, or connecting with players in teamwork at surviving.


But i still think i couldve had a diffrent path at playing this game with a friend first then by myself. If you are into Survival games and you are willing to play for hours. This game is for you, however i recommend to the new isle players you should play with a friend in your first gameplay, so you can get a much loose beginning at this game, be aware that if your 4 hours and 37 minutes Allo dies, you die, you have to start again from scratch. Some of you probably watch The Gaming Beaver on Youtube and hes having the best moments and all the entertainment in this game. I feel that way when i play the isle and it truly feels fun, and if you feel like your in the game and nothing is happening and its peaceful, feel free to multi task, but you MUST keep your guard high, i have had close calls with being ambushed while AFK, but i managed to see it coming right in time to dodge the bullet.

Note: IF you want to play the newest version Evrima, you must have a great laptop or PC designed for Gaming, if you have an old laptop or it doesnt run stuff well, it most likely wont run Evrima bc on how advanced the game is in everything, a lot of players have this problem DirectX 12 is not supported on your system. Try running without the -dx12 or -d3d12. I had this issue in a bad device but i got it sorted out. Evrima has a few requirements to be completed before you can play this version.


Coming to the end of this, The Isle is a really good game in terms of graphics, PVP, teamwork, and definitely fun to play with friends. This game first came out a while back and i'm a bit of a late isle player, but it is still developing to this day, and i'm really happy to see that people still enjoy this game. Good luck to the new players who play this game, welcome to The Isle ^^\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tit still has some work to do in order to be great but is overall a very fun game that will eat up hours of your time. can't wait for a proper elder system, pat/matriarch buffs and adaptations to really make this game the most addicting thing ever. if you're a dino-lover like me than you'll be disappointed by the unrealistic designs but the behaviors caused by the game mechanics will make you get into the mind-state of a dinosaur trying to survive in a hostile ecosystem and that get's me right in the childhood. If you're a fan of survival games I highly recommend giving this one a try as it certainly is a constant race to survive and manage all your needs in the game. Graphically this is the best in the genre in my opinion, again the designs for me personally are a little disappointing, they look great but are not accurate to their real-life counterparts. This game is in beta much like almost every other game in this genre but the updates, while slow and typically lacking in much new content, continue to come out and the dev team is very dedicated to continuing to improve this game as it grows. In it's current state I wouldn't say this game is the best, but the groundwork is all here for one of the most mechanically and conceptually unique gameplay experiences of all time that I and many others wouldn't be able to stop playing.\t\t\t\t\t
