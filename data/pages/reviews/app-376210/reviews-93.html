Even with 60+ fps and ~40 ms ping I'll experience significant rubber-banding and random choppiness so bad that at times I've been unable to sprint, I just rubber band in place. The game is close to being what I'd consider unplayable the lag and crashing is so bad.

Also, just very unfinished. Food only spawns on specific areas of the map, only on certain areas of the overland and only certain rivers/lakes, so despite the size of the island 90% is empty and 10% is thunder dome.

That said, the various dinos are fairly fun and the environment looks and sounds nice. Overall, a lot of potential but with its glacial development pace and major technical challenges even after this many years I can't recommend the game.\t\t\t\t\t
