\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love living out my dinosaur's life in this game! It has a nice combination of realism, immersion and action that keeps me playing for hours. I have had a lot of fun exploring the Gateway map and learning how to play the different dinosaurs and there is still more to be discovered. The map and game as a whole are beautiful even on low graphics (which will probably be needed if you have a laptop like mine). I only have performance issues here and there and they always clear up within a minute or two but server crashes seem to happen a lot more often. However I never seem to lose much, if any progress and I always get back into the server just fine so it is not really much of a problem in my experience.
One thing I would love to see in the game would be that the growth system continues after adulthood until old age and the eventual death of your dinosaur. They always say that death gives life meaning and I think that knowing that even if you get to adulthood you will not live forever would both encourage you to make the most of the life with you dinosaur but also give players something to work towards even after they have "made it". (This might be more applicable to herbivores who in my experience lead a lot more peaceful lives, especially after reaching 100% growth, and therefore have a harder time dying of natural causes.) I very much recommend this game!\t\t\t\t\t
