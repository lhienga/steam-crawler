\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy opinion on this game is currently quite mixed, every major update the game seems to get worse (Such as the terrible stamina changes that make the game pretty tedious to play compared to before, and make some of my favourite creatures such as Pteras completely unplayable), the devs are arrogant and horrible when it comes to taking criticism and new updates are slow though they do still come and I presume that the devs are trying their best (Or well trying enough) to get quality updates out. But even with all that negativity and more I still love this game, while it is noticeably worse than it once was it still is a decent enough game and every now and then gives great experiences. The feeling of spawning in as say a Raptor and struggling to find food only to pick up the tracks of some prey and after a successful ambush feasting, then meeting up and forming a pack which you stay with until you are an adult, then after surviving for a bit and maybe losing and gaining a pack member or two then ending it all with one amazing hunt can be great, though every now and then your life will be cut short by a sudden drop, a lack of food, a glitch or a hungry Dino, and of course not all of your lives will be so exciting, some will just be aimless wandering until you slowly die. If you like the idea of playing as a Dinosaur in a proper survival simulation then I'd definitely consider getting this game, though I'd probably wait either until a sale or until the game improves a bit to actually get it, I'd still keep my eye on it.\t\t\t\t\t
