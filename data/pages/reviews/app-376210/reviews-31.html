\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who loves dinosaurs, loved this game, and has spent almost 3000 hours playing it, it saddens me that I have to resort to this, but the Developers have shown their hand, and have continuously shown their hand since the creation of this game, so it’s time to speak up.

If you love dinosaurs and love survival, then I can confidently tell you this: DO NOT BUY THIS GAME. All you will get out of it is suffering. The Developers do not care about the player base’s enjoyment and have stated so several times in their Official Discord. They have a vision and intend to see it through. If you disagree or attempt to give constructive feedback they will ban you and claim it was due to misinformation.

The head developers of this game, Dondi being the most prominent, are egotistical and narcissistic. They do not want you to have fun. They want you to suffer and they’ve done this via several game mechanics that make even the most masochistic of individuals do a double take and ask what the hell is going on.

Growth times continuously fluctuate. The ecosystem and combat is horribly unbalanced, the diet and migration systems are like a bad joke you keep hearing/reading on a YouTube comments thread. The zones rarely work and they are the only area where this dietary food will spawn. Get to a zone and find no food? Too bad. Get to a zone and have it immediately switch up on you and then switch up again when you get to a different one? Sucks to suck. This is the mindset of the devs and they will tell you to your face to “get good” if you even ATTEMPT to suggest a better alternative.

On top of this, the AI system designed to keep your carnivores fed is busted. AI won’t spawn. It spawns in hot spots on the complete opposite side of the map and it’s all small creatures that won’t even feed your big carnivores past 2%. You will die continuously and you won’t have any opportunity to enjoy the game and your dinosaur. The map itself is atrocious and I am amazed that for a team that spends half a year to release an update, they miraculously still manage to dish out a half-@ssed, broken, buggy mess with each update that will inevitably be followed up by “hot fixes” that fix one problem while introducing a dozen others. Truly a talented bunch. The audio glitches on the new map, and for a game that is heavily reliant on the player’s ability to hear, this is a detriment. To make matters worse, the new weather system is also busted and more often than not you’ll be playing your game with thick perpetual fog that NEVER clears up. So not only can you not hear, now you cannot see as well. You’re blind and deaf.

Despite ALL these flaws, the devs continue to sit on their rumps and twiddle their thumbs, conspiring new ways to further break their game instead of fixing the numerous issues the game suffers from. The most recent example is the developers wanting to ruin an already fully functional 3rd person perspective camera that we’ve had since the beginning of this game’s creation and replace it in favour of some weird fish-eye lens slapped on the back of your dinosaurs head like some bizarre GoPro camera perspective. Mind you most of your herbivorous dinosaurs have tail attacks that would essentially be rendered useless by this new perspective. Despite the OVERWHELMING NEGATIVE outcry from their community to not go through with these changes, the team has dug in their heels and have blatantly declared that the camera change is a fact that is going to happen. They’ve even gone so far as to state that the player base has no say whatsoever and that the people actually playing their game have no business telling them how to make their own game. Anyone who makes a suggestion gets banned, as stated above.
To sum up, this game is not worth your time or money. The Developers are not kind people and are not even worthy of the title of game developers. They are clearly terrible at game development and are a disgrace to other developers who care and listen to community feedback on their games. They are egotistical individuals who are simply on a power trip. They don’t want to make an enjoyable game. Rather, they just want players as Guinea pigs for their pet project. Updates are slow and far in-between, break the game when they come out, and the Devs couldn’t care less about your enjoyability. Save yourself the headache and look into Path of Titans. You’ll get more bang for your buck there. This game is a joke same as the team behind it. The game advertised on the Steam Page isn’t even what the current game is. They long since trashed that version and have been ‘working’ on a different version that would allegedly be better. It is not, and that’s why the current version isn’t what’s being advertised on the Steam Page. Let this be a testament to the kind of people they are and their skill as developers. They should’ve just cut their losses with the original game went belly up and they had to start from scratch on the version they’re working on now but haven’t advertised on their Steam Page\t\t\t\t\t
