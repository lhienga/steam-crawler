I used to play legacy a long time ago but eventually got a bit bored of it because the lack of decent graphics at the time and the large groups of mixpacking carebears roaming the map.
But i came back wanting to check out Evrima, and see whats the fuzz is all about.
I've been playing Evrima for the last 2 weeks now, and have to say so far it was a blast!!
And yes i noticed some nerfing have been done on stamina, camera view and nightvision, and i feel a lot of these negative reviews originate from these changes pulling players out of their comfort zones.
But in my opinion these changes are making the game more thrilling then ever before, along with jaw dropping graphics, animations and the special abilities, it also feels way more balanced now.
That being said there are still quite a few bugs in the map/game, but this game is a simulator and i never came across a simulator that is bug free to be honest
Anyways i feel like i came back to a real survival game!\t\t\t\t\t
