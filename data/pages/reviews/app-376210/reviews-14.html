Waste of money.

Got Anti-Cheat to work, yaaaay. All of the main servers are swarmed with trolls who think ganking on juveniles who just spawned is some idea of fun. The community servers are fine, but most of them have such extreme rules that the main servers start to look fun again. I just wish people would let others experience the game a bit before they go and just hunt them down over and over and over again, I have no idea how to play and can't learn when (in game) I'm a literal child.

Deino is broken (Crocodile/alligator). The extended water vision ONLY WORKS if you have an NVidia card. Basically paywalling an entire dinosaur behind NVidia. Seriously guys? If it was that bugged out don't add it to the game.

Stamina regen for most creatures is appallingly slow. I understand realism, but when 80% of the game is "I'm gonna sit here and stare at some grass" I would've bought grass simulator. Especially with things like the PT where its a flying animal. Your stamina is gone in seconds and then you're sitting there for 5 minutes waiting for stamina. Please please please make this less excruciatingly painful.

Making a nest is awful as well for PT, please rebalance them. You put a stick in and its like 5% percent, so you have to take off (which uses stamina), land (which uses stamina) sniff, find stick, take off, follow, land, grab stick, take off, fly to nest, 5% percent Give them a bigger stamina pool with the same regen rate so that its more realistic (as in tired at night, flight during the day). Or make a mechanic where sleep doesnt auto log you, just increases your stamina regen tenfold. You should also be able to (slowly) regen stamina while gliding, because that is exactly why flying animals glide. To catch their breath

Too much weather that entirely blocks your vision for the entire day, only for it to clear up during night time where you can't see anyways, and then for it to come back when its day again. Just give us a clear day man.

The compass needs a very big improvement, or we need a minimap. Every single person in the game uses an external map anyways. Can we please just have the basic QOL features even though its not as realistic? Especially with most dinos having bad stam.

The game is fun otherwise, it has some severe identity crisis issues where the devs want it to be uber-hyper realistic but sacrifice any of the fun the game has. One of the only games I understand when people use things like gamma hacks at the least so they can at least compete at night.\t\t\t\t\t

In 13 days, I put 200 hours into The Isle, and can say one of my best experiences would have come from the gamer community surrounding the game ie: discord. You can play this game solo but its so much more fun to get into a group and coordinate attacks and survive together. I've made more friends in this 200 hour game then all my games combine. With the new release of the Gateway map there is still a lot to come with the game development, as far as adding more dinos with different abilities. There is a learning curve that'll take around 300 hours to learn roughly everything there is to know about the game. Typicaly theres around 100 p[layers on official servers, so despite the map being 3x the size of GTA 5 you still run across players quite often weather you wanted to or NOT. Even with 200 hours in game theres still a ton of the map I haven't seen yet. If your a fan boy as I was of ARK, you'll definitely want to give The Isle a shot.
