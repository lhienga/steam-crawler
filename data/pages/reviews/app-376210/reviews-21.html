First off this review isn't a "is this game good or not" because that's subjective. I'm gonna tell you all you need to know about this game.
Alright so basically before you get the game there's a few things you need to know. Firstly the game has two main branches of content. Legacy, and Evrima. Legacy is the branch the devs abandoned a few years back due to the game not being imagined how they wanted it to. Therefor abandoning it for Evrima, a much more mechanic based branch with much better graphics and immersion. The game is automatically set to Legacy and if you want to play Evrima you need to change the game branch in order to do so. For Legacy there's no official servers unlike Evrima due to the devs abandoning it like I said before. So you'll need to do some research and find a good community hosted server to play on, most servers on Legacy have rules to suit others' playstyles and more. Once you get it figured out and test out the playables you'll be good to go.
Evrima is a different story.. There are many different official servers unlike Legacy that suit many different regions of players to help deal with high amounts of ping. And if you don't like no-rules official servers you can also find community hosted servers like Legacy, that also may contain rules or not.
That's pretty much all you need to know if you're looking to get the game and are confused.
Some other things to note, no you don't need to buy The Isle to play the different branches; you can just switch between them whenever and it'll save your progress.
Other than that once you get used to all the playables on Evrima and Legacy alike, the game is great if you like dinosaurs and immersion.\t\t\t\t\t
