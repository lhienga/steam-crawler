\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll try and be impartial and leave developer politics out of things, and just comment on the gameplay.

Pros:
- One of the best dinosaur RP games out there (when it's working as intended)
- Sound effects are amazing, audio cues are fun and exciting
- Dinosaur models while occasionally clunky typically hit the mark, and customization features let you be unique while still within the realm of possibility and reality

Cons:
- The game is in a perpetual state of change. Just when the "alpha" game reaches a state of semi-stability the devs decide to make a game altering change, entirely reworking a system, or re-imagining a concept. While this is typically good in most alpha games, The Isle's changes are based off developer choices and rarely involve player feedback. The never-ending cycle will likely see this game in Alpha longer than Star Citizen.
- Lag. Lack of good servers, funding to the servers or other issues result in constant issues, which... after you've grown a dinosaur for 3 hours to die from lag, can be irritating.
- Growing a dinosaur. The system was highly non-intuitive years ago, saw positive change, then after a year, was changed again.
- Camera system...... will have to edit this after the current dev change to camera views, but it seems you will be staring at grass if you play anything shorter than 4 ft.
- Official Servers are plagued with cheaters, and mixpackers (players abusing various dinosaur strengths that would never realistically associate with one another.
- Unofficial Servers are plagued with mods with authority issues, elitist groups that ban or kick players learning server rules or players equally as disgruntled.
- Maps and systems around them don't really reflect coherent systems and result in players slaughtering one another around a central location or dying alone after seeing no other players or AI for food after 30 minutes of searching.
- Official Discord where players go for news or updates and to provide input/feedback is plagued by admins or mods who ban any dissenting opinions from the status quo.

Overall it's a great game, but until it releases based on what I've seen in the Alpha I cannot recommend this game to anyone because what it looks like now will likely be entirely different from the game that will release (if it ever releases).\t\t\t\t\t
