Tbh I see the devs catching a lot of hate for their game not being perfect and it is just that, not perfect. You need a pretty decent computer to get consistent good frames and certain balance issues come and go. The bottom line is that this game offers a very good dinosaur survival experience and is intended to be difficult. It is one of the only games of it's kind and is overall a beautiful game and a very fun game.

PS: While there are several flaws the game has (like all games) most of the negative reviews I see are people who either aren't that good at the game and get frustrated and decide to seethe and review bomb the game or simply they just aren't compatible with the style of gameplay this game has to offer leaving me saying "then why did you buy the game?" My point here is not to hate on these people they have their own opinions but to say that these devs work very hard on the game and are making good progress as time goes on.

So maybe we should appreciate a good game and the dedication and work the devs have put in to it as good devs and good games get rarer and rarer every year. Appreciate that there are still people who care about entertainment instead of income.\t\t\t\t\t
