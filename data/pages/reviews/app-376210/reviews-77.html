Don't get me wrong, this game can be one of the most fun you can have in pretty much any game, but man, when you have an off day, and you sure as hell will, they are OFF. Like you could die upwards of 10 times within an hour!
Look, I would recommend this to anyone who is a massive dino nerd like myself, but if you are just an average gamer looking for some mindless dinosaur fun, I can't fully recommend this one. If you have a high tolerance for janky mechanics and (most of the time, but not always) toxic players, you should feel reassured that you could squeeze at least some fun out of this game.
