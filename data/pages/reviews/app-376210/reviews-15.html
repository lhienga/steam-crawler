\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl:dr - Community bad, game buggy, hackers go brr, not worth your time. Evrima graphics kinda nice and dinosaurs are neat tho.

Right so I've been playing this game for several years, and while I've had fun, this game has gone down hill in so many ways over the last year.

Over the last year, I've taken breaks and come back to this game every few months, and every time i come back, it seems like there are either brand new issues, or old ones that remain unfixed. Several of these issues include, but are not limited to:

- getting food stuck in my dinosaur's mouth and being unable to eat, drink, or use any attacks to defend myself
- falling under the map and drowning
- logging in and immediately taking fall damage or flat out dying
- starving because food that is physically there cannot be interacted with
- getting stuck in little holes or crevices in the map with no way to escape

And yet while those issues and many more remain in the game, the devs insist on pushing out updates that no one wanted. The stamina changes and, while not in the Evrima branch at the time of posting this, the camera changes. Literally no one asked for changes to the way stamina or the camera works, and there was a lot of negative feedback regarding it. Yet the changes made it to the public branch anyway. The devs do not listen to player feedback, and more often than not, they will actively argue against it.

There have been countless cases of people being banned from the discord for giving constructive criticism, and instances where the devs themselves have been extremely hostile towards people giving constructive criticism.

As a final note, one thing to know if you still plan to get this game, is that hackers run rampant on official servers, and rarely is anything done about it. That being said, I was killed by several hackers, and then 10 minutes later, I was permanently banned for hacking. I attempted to reach out to several admins, and I have yet to receive any sort of response from any of them, even though I had recording evidence that I'd done nothing wrong.

This incident was the final straw for me, and frankly it is a testament to the game's community as a whole. While I have had a decent few good experiences with people in this game, overall I've encountered more unpleasant people than not. The developers themselves are guilty of this as well. As mentioned before they have, on multiple occasions, been hostile towards people offering constructive criticism, and even banned people from their public discord for posting said criticisms.

While this whole thing, especially the end, might sound rather biased, this is my honest experience with this game. I've enjoyed it, and frankly I'll probably still play it every now and then just for the nostalgia, but for anyone considering buying this game, don't. If you really want to play a dinosaur game, Beast of Bermuda or Path of Titans are both much better options than this.\t\t\t\t\t
