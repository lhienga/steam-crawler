\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaven't played that much yet, still I'd say the game is pretty cool.
It looks good and it's interesting to live as a dinosaur.
A few things you need to prepare for though:
- Running around ages to find food (AI animals spawn pretty rarely in a lot of areas)
- Not seeing many herbivores (from my experience)
- It's a bit laggy on a lot of the official servers (Don't have the best internet)
- Waiting for the servers you can play on without big performance issues to have a free spot for you.

I highly suggest playing this with a friend or more to atleast see one other dino (The map is pretty big, servers only have up to 100 players though)
Definitely install the EVRIMA experimental version (just more content + AI animals for food)
Also use a interactive map on a browser for orientation.

Some dinos i can recommend:
- Raptors for the start (pretty easy to play and have decent speed to get away from enemies)
- Beipiaosaurus (Spoiler: it's a duck, pretty cool water movement)
- Deinosuchus (Beware for cannibals)
- Herrerasaurus (Can climb which is OP)
- Pteranodon (Just kidding this one is so hard to play because flying needs too much stamina to be effective)
- Dryosaurus (Personal favourite Herbivore, relatively small and fast)

Overall I'd give this smth like a 7.8/10 in it's current state.
I enjoy it.

Only player I saw was apparently drowned in the middle of a lake and inedible for me. Only other sign of players existing in the server was a roar in the distance about 45s before I logged out, again as my screen faded red.
