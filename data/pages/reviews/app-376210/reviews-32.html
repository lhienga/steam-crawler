\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn a list of pros and cons, the cons seriously outweigh the pros that the game offers. I have played The Isle for several months now, in fact, it used to be one of my favourite pvp games despite some of the things I experienced in game including interfacing issues and hackers that the Devs really do nothing about. I have just shy of 700 hours in game and here are some things that I believe are worthy of noting.

The Good —
The graphics and artwork as a whole are stunning, including seemingly banal animations like how the dinosaur's foot splays out while it is walking to illustrate the weight and action of movement.
"Realism" and I use this term lightly as the official servers have no real established rules or regulations beyond no hacking. I do like that the playables come in colours assumed by the paleo-community instead of neon/flurescent colours. While there are some creative liberties taken on certain species (hypsilophodon, for example), and some inspiration brought on by other dinosaur-adjacent media (dilophosaurus having venom), I personally enjoy that the designs look like believable animals. The animations of the dinosaurs, au, and the environment of the game as a whole is very immersive and sometimes just beautiful to look at.
The Gateway map is really something fantastic.
The community of players, not the game developers themselves really make it a good and interactive community.

The Bad —
The game constantly crashes, while the ping issue has been addressed (and I say this lightly as everyone I have spoken to still has rubberbanding issues) the game is still always DCing.
This game has been in beta for how long now? It seems like a feeble excuse to continue to be experimental without addressing issues/concerns players are having with the game because "keep in mind, the game is still in beta mode. Bugs are to be expected". Beta removes onus on the devs.
Aforementions hackers. If you don't play on officials, you are less likely to run into them, especially on popular and regulated servers.
Gateway was supposed to have migration zones to keep players moving and there is no happy medium, AI spawn points are almost completely within only a handful of areas in the map, making them hotspots and the entirety of the map nearly irrelevant to players.
Frequency of updates and how some patches seem to be slapped on like a proverbial band-aid, sometimes making things worse than what they were originally.

The Ugly —
A crew of grossly unprofessional game developers. Whenever the community, at large, posts an opinion in their feedback that the developers do not like, ultimately the response seems to be to ban these people from the official discord instead of taking note of the complaints or suggestions that they have and addressing them professionally.
Game developers will actively insult their playerbase for disliking changes. It has been posted in the official discord on text, and can be heard in several videos.
Concerns regarding hackers are constantly ignored and if they are not ignored they are aptly addressed with a dismissive wave of the hand to those who do choose to report it.\t\t\t\t\t
