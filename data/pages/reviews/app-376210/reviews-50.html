\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am going to skip all the drama around the game. All of it. What does the game offer right now in 2024?

Things I like: The diet system- Eating certain dinosaurs give different buffs, The migration system- forcing herbivores to move around the map, the sound design, graphics, and animations are all superb. Theres real time growth, weather effects with day and night cycles. You only get 1 life then you have to start over as a baby, making the combat and sense of danger feel weighted, it got my heart racing hunting other players.

Things I dislike : Poor roster of dinosaurs. So no T-rex, Triceratops, Spino, and so many other fan favorites. Also the map isnt great. Both of those I assume will get worked on more in the future. It also feels like night time for way too long, and im not sure why that is.

So in conclusion, Whats currently on offer is awesome and decently fleshed out, the problem is there isnt much content on offer and the map is rough in places. There are some seriously talented people working on this game. This game requires alot of patience on its updates, more than most games, which ironically is the way the gameplay is too. A game of patience.\t\t\t\t\t
