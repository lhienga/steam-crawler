\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOff to a cracking start:

First time loading in as a Deinosuchus, played it very careful and spent a lot of time grinding. After about 4-5 hours in, I decided to go and explore away from my spawn area and meet up with a friend. I was longing a fight since I hadn't been in one yet and was excited to meet with my friend to group-up.

After making my way halfway down the river to my friend, I accidentally pass over a small waterfall, and then fall into a nook which I couldn't get out of. I died of dehydration within 10-15 minutes after.

What a waste of 4-5 hours, I am completely spurred from playing the game now, as I feel like any bug or glitch will just get me killed long before an actual player. The main thing that annoyed me was that I wasn't killed by falling damage, or being in a fight. Instead, I was killed by poor map design that I couldn't get out of.

I love this game but it's just too frustrating to enjoy regularly.\t\t\t\t\t
