\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI do not recommend buying or playing the isle.

The game is pretty, the animations are clean, the dinosaurs are pleasant to look at but the developer cares so little for the community he has built up it is not worth losing your money over.

With the newer map in evrima (the only version of the game being worked on) the isle has gotten so difficult to play it is not worth it. Growth times do not allow you to progress if you have a job or classes, the hunger and thirst were not properly balance to compensate for the new huge map, stamina punishes those who are not perfect with combat. Not to mention there is so little to do besides walk around and eat then die- that its not fun. there is no gameplay loop, and the gameplay gets harder with each update because the developer thinks that's how to 'fix' how boring his game is. (Not to mention the HORRIFIC things he wants to do to the camera system.)

I used to play the Isle for HOURS, one of my favorite games when i was still in high school, but that was when i had free time and lower standards. Now walking around for 8 hours waiting for a mid-teir herbi to grow isn't fun, nor is starving to death as any carnivore. I used to main ptera, but now with the stam updates I cant even enjoy flying anymore.

Legacy wasn't perfect either, but at least when it was out there was variety in dinosaurs, now unless you wanna play a carnivore you get to be one of 5 options. (three of which, die instantly to most things and take an hour to grow)

If you love a horrifically hard survival game you might like the isle, but it does not deserve its spot among the top three dinosaur survival games anymore. It has fallen far from greatness.

Do not buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t
