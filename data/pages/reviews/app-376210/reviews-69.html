\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUp until the last update, this would have been a definite recommend. But I can't say that anymore with the Gateway update. It has introduced too many things that make it impossible to enjoy as a casual game.

Stamina takes over 3 minutes now to get back to normal and seems to run out in half the time it did before. It might not be draining any faster but the recovering being so slow, sure makes it feel that way. Not to mention that you can't regain stamina even slowly while walking. So its endless, run and sit, run and sit. No strategic walking and running to allow it to refill but while still keeping on the move. Stamina management is no longer a thing. Just run and sit.

Injuries can take 20 minutes to heal, and again won't heal at all unless you sit. So more sitting time.

The map. The map looks amazing but it does not lead itself to exploring or going anywhere but 2 places. And those 2 places have one lake and a long river right along the length of them, which is endlessly full of Denos. So when you want a drink, you have to run half of the map to a safe spot, get a drink, then run back again. And that is most of you life as an adult now. Running back and forth for water. Sure you can go other places, but guess what? No one is there, and even that would be fine, I love playing solo, but there is no NPC prey there either. So you can barely survive. There is a reason why everyone is hanging around the only semi flat area of map, that contains most of the drinking water and 90% of the prey. So whats the point?

And my last major gripe is the fog. Oh dear the fog. The weather added to the game is great, thunderstorms look amazing. But the fog. Everyday, Every single day often twice a day, a complete blanket fog you can't see more than a meter in-front of yourself. And with the gateway map there are endlessly more up and down areas and paths that have to be followed as they are the only way to go, so add in the continuous fog, the up down map where everywhere has somewhere to fall, break a leg or die and it is no longer fun, its tedious.

And now that I think about it. Something has been bothering me about the map that I couldn't quite work out until i wrote this down. Now that they are preparing things for the humans, there are lots of gates and fences and paths/roads. This all makes sense, and I didn't think anything of it. But now that I'm thinking about it, that nagging feeling that has been at the back of my head the whole time I've been on the new map, has suddenly started to make sense. With all these additions the island/map is no longer open. You can't pick a direction and make your way there, however you want, with complete freedom. No you have to follow the road, or you will hit a wall or gate. You have to go over the bridge or you will fall into a ravine. You can't set your own course anymore, you all have to follow the same routes, prey is in the same areas, water is in the same areas, so everything no matter what dinosaur you play feels identical.

The joy before was surviving to adulthood, hunting, getting into a fight. Winning or losing felt OK and you would think, "lets go again". But now with the terrible stamina, walk sit, walk sit. Taking forever to heal and regain stamina, the fog and the endless places to fall. Instead of lets go again, it has become more. "I can't be bothered doing it again." And I know i'm not the only one. Everyone is completely gun shy in all the servers, only Denos attack things now, or people in packs, with fights they know they can win. The solo side of the game is done, because its just too much work and effort to start all over again because of that one mistake that got you killed. Like i said, I was used to that one mistake getting me killed and would think lets go again. Now there is no again. I used to get to sub-adult and then go on the offensive, I'd nip adult raptors with my Cerata, I'd attack full grown Carno's when i was the raptor, bleeding them, running away and coming back to wear them down. If i died, I'd start again and do it again. It didn't matter. Now when I die, its a big sigh and a thought of "can I be assed again"

The other night we had a pack of 8 raptors. A few months ago that pack would have roved around the map, threatening Steg families. Chasing carnos down and for a while being the map bully until some stegs or a group of carnos got together and took them out taking over the throne. Now on the new map with the new balance, the pack did not take a single fight. No one wanted to lose their character. A mix pack of 2 Dilos and 2 Cerata walked passed. and both groups postured and threatened. But neither attacked, because it was not worth the risk. A few months ago that would have been a fantastic rumble, the noise of which would have brought even more people, and everyone would have had fun, win or lose. But now, even if a side had won, they would have spent the next 30 minutes sitting and healing. Hoping enough food was around not to loose their buffs. The joy of the fight has left the servers. Instead the pack sat around for the next hour, killing NPC prey until one by one they got picked off by Denos when they went for something to drink. A real night to remember.

If that was any of those players first time, I don't think they will be back. And even those that love the game will soon become apathetic if nothing happens every evening, and they will soon go somewhere else to find their evenings dinosaur fun.

Hopefully they will balance things out, lower the fog, flatten some of the map, open a few more routes, but that could take another 6 months just to put the game back to where is was 6 months ago. I hope I'm proved wrong and balances will come out quickly, but based on passed experience I really don't see that happening. And that makes me sad.

I guess its back to Spiro until things get fixed, hope that there are some servers are still running it and that they aren't dead. Update - Nope, there aren't\t\t\t\t\t
