\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game with a lot of potential with a surprising amount of disappointment. If you're ok with a game that is still quite rough around the edges, I'd say it's worth a shot. However, be warned that there are many MANY issues at the moment. Such as:

1. The map (Gateway) is simply too big for the population they desire on each server, meaning the map is mostly dead except for 1 or 2 spots where everything lives because...it's the only places to get food.
2. AI, the main source of food for anyone not existing in hot-spots (AKA battle zones) are practically non-existent.
3. Migration pathways are meaningless because, as a carnivore, there is usually very little if any AI, so you will often starve to death even if you are in your migration area.
4. There are so many spots to just fall in and get trapped in, and with no one really around to help, you often just lose hours of progress and have to starve to death at the bottom of whatever prison you fall into.

I WANT to give this game a positive recommendation, however in it's current state, I can't in good faith give that recommendation UNLESS you are ok with how rough this game can be. :(\t\t\t\t\t
