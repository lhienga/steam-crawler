This is probably the best work ATLUS has put out in years- it at least definitely tops previous Persona games overall in terms of gameplay, story, and - absolutely - it's characters. This series hasn't had characters I felt so strongly about since Persona 2 IS/EP, but with the new changes and additions in Reload, P3's cast is now tied - if not all the way at the top - of my list. I could sing this game's praises forever... ATLUS though, not so much.
ATLUS has been borderline malicious with how they've handled dlcs. Not including The Answer and it's DLC in the deluxe editions of the game is almost blatant false advertising with how they were labelled to "include all dlc", and especially when you consider the fact that the company was denying the existence of The Answer in Reload back when pre-orders opened up.
TLDR: This game is absolutely amazing, the best in the series now- but the pricing of the game and it's dlc is awful. \t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy absolute favorite Persona game. This game is a faithful remake of the original Persona 3 that released in the year 2006. Even after 18 years, the games theme of death and what it means to live still hold up to this day. For newcomers to the series who started with Persona 5 or any new player, this version of Persona 3 should be considered the definitive edition. For veterans who played the original, this game had a lot of love given to it. The development staff behind the game made sure to not change anything major in the story and only improved upon the game play from previous Persona games. The games soundtrack is just as great as the original and with new songs included (The new battle theme being my absolute favorite). The new voice cast also did a great job and honor the original performances from the original game. The ending still gets me even after playing the original so long ago. A must play for any JRPG fan and one of my all time favorite games ever. Thank you to the team behind it for making this happen.\t\t\t\t\t

This is my personal favorite of the Persona games I played. I love the story, the extremely relatable cast, the social links. The gameplay is vastly improved from portable, with quality of life putting it on par with Persona 5's gameplay. There are moments in this game that made me very emotional, and for me it hits so much harder than Persona 4 or 5. Not trying to badmouth them, because I do enjoy both games, just my personal preference. The soundtrack for this game is also my new favorite, with tracks like Color Your Night and Full Moon, Full Life that will own my Spotify Wrapped once they get added. Now one thing I will say, is I don't believe the game should be 70 dollars, unless the Answer is included. I really don't agree with it being locked behind an expansion pass, especially for those who paid for even bigger editions of the game. If I were the average player, and not a persona fanboy, I'd say wait for a sale to get the game, and hope the Answer can be bought on its own.

Another disappointment is the lack of FeMC, or the Female Main Character. While it isn't the worst thing ever to not have her, the fact that her version of the Persona story isn't remade in this when it isn't too terribly different from the Makoto storyline feels like an oversight. That said the community is making a really good mod to at least have her model and new animations/music added to the game, which is wonderful. Seriously the pink ocean main menu and reimagined tracks sell it.

Now final thoughts on the game, if you want a very thought provoking story about which the primary theme is death? This is one of the best games I think to tackle it, I just love the formula of Persona games. You're given a story that takes place over the course of a year. And just like real life, the days go by so very quickly. This story and these themes resonate with me more than the other two, and that's why I'll say its my personal favorite. I doubt that'll change after I play the other few, but that remains to be seen! Thanks for reading, and if you get the game, enjoy the ride. Vibe to the music, spend time with your favorite characters, and for Thanatos's sake, don't use Armageddon on Elizabeth.\t\t\t\t\t
