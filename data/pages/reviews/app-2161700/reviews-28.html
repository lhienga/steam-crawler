\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who really enjoyed the Persona 3 Portable first and kinda going with the expectations of losing things that it had. I can say compared to that version as far as i remember the only part that is missing is the Female protagonist option which has its own set of stories in there.

Besides that tho i think that this game does an excellent job of making the same game just look much prettier AND actually does add some new story related things with main story and characters with some side things you can optionally do, which is really lovely to see.

If you are a casual and newer player to the series i'd say the combat is taught pretty well, that and the other gameplay things in the game does handhold you a bit when first learning about things. As for if you decide to play on harder difficulty i can say the grind is just as long and tedious as it was on the older game but that is honestly to be expected, that only applies in combat tho, other aspects are the same in any difficulty. The newer additions to the Tartarus (The main dungeon) In both exploration feel and look is something i was concerned how they did and i think all in all it was a lot nicer in this version. The combat has a lot more features which both builds on how you can choose to play while also building on the characters themselves.


I play these games in english so can only speak for that part of Voice acting but hearing a different crew at first was kind of weird since i was expecting same game but honestly now i can't unhear that these are the characters who are a part of this role. Really great match and great job.

The new music added are bangers which can either be remixes, remasters or just straight up new songs.

Unless you are a big fan and can't wait tho id say maybe wait for a sale at some point since its pretty pricey, even tho for the money you do get a game which lasts you easily 100+ hours or so. They did also release and expansion pass which as of writing this only added music from persona 4 Golden and 5 Royale to listen to in exploration in dungeon. I really am more waiting for the final bit which adds more story which was only previously playable in a different version of persona 3 which wasn't available on PC which is commonly known as "The answer".

Huge thumbs up for me to this banger of a game!\t\t\t\t\t
