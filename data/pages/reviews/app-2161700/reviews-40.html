\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPersona 5 Royale is the game you play when you want to feel hope.
Persona 4 Golden is the game you want to play when you’re feeling homesick.
Persona 3 Reload is the game you want to play when you’re numb and want to feel something.

This game is far from perfect. There was the tone of dread present in every version of Persona 3, including the animation, which is missing from this game. The darker atmosphere and cinematography was dropped in favor for a more dynamic one. This is most prevalent in the MC’s persona awakening. I think the Original did the scene the best.

Despite my issue with the tone, I still think this is my favorite interpretation of Persona 3. My main, non spoiler related feature is that Direct Control exists and is on by default. Theurgies allow for fun, unique cinematics which can dish out some nice damage, provide debuffs, and/or buffs.

Besides gameplay, I think this game does the best in certain scenes compared to its previous incarnations. Without going into specifics, I have felt more emotionally drained in this game compared to FES. The main reason being someone’s specific theurgy being absolutely gut wrenching.

It’s late at night, just finished my playthrough of this game, will probably edit it later.

Hoping that they do something more with P3R besides adding a $35 DLC bundle on the side. Maybe add a new dlc in which resolves what was mentioned in P4AU. Idk.\t\t\t\t\t
