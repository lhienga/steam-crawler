I would recommend this game but only on a good sale. It’s too expensive and representative of corporate & executive greed that is destroying game development and consumer enjoyment.

This is a very good remake. There are some issues that persist like the writing in the social links being inadequate compared to the likes of Persona 4 & 5, and the lack of a female route that was in Portable is a bit of a missed opportunity. But the quality of life updates, new events, graphical overhaul, and the fully voiced social links and new voice acting are very good and mostly make up for those factors. And the story still holds up extremely well with its cast of characters and narrative events/elements.

But the biggest issue is that this game is ridiculously expensive. £60/$70 for this game is already pushing people’s tolerance when considering other big releases these days, but having to pay an additional £30/$35 for a standalone expansion pass that contains important story details (that isn’t included in the other expensive premium versions) is a really ♥♥♥♥♥♥ move. Like I anticipated it being released after the initial release but it being this expensive as well is just pure greed.

On top of the other day 1 DLC in this game makes it just egregious. It’s what gaming executives like to do – ruin games for everyone for the sake of unsustainable profits.

The price tag hangs overhead like Tartarus during the Dark Hour which is a shame because this is a very good game and a very good remake with lots of details and improvements. They have been doing this for a while now and I don’t want to see ATLUS follow the likes of EA, Ubisoft and 2K anymore. The egregious DLC and expensive price tags, plus re-releasing games like with the upcoming Shin Megami Tensei 5: Vengeance at full price has to stop. But they won’t because executives are going to continue pushing, and we will continue to buy them anyway.

As someone who genuinely likes this game and this franchise it annoys me to ‘not recommend’ this game. It just stings quite hard because this isn’t a complete remake to begin with. And I’m part of the problem buying this game at full price. Don’t let yourself become part of it as well. ATLUS and SEGA are better than this, and we are as well.

TLDR: Very good game, but it is way too expensive to recommend right now. Wait for a good sale for the base game and for the expansion pass.\t\t\t\t\t
