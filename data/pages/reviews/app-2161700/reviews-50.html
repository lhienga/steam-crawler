\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest game to have ever gamed.

But seriously though, here’s a review.

Before this game came out, it felt like almost everyone was hoping for it to fail. I think mostly purists or select fans of the other installment. As a persona 3 fes and p5r fan, I, too, almost believed this. However, I’m glad I didn’t let it sway me because boy oh boy it’s probably the best game I’ve ever played.

The graphics, UI, and soundtrack are obviously top-notch. But the real best highlights of the game are its story and characters.

When I first played persona 3 FES, I was impressed by the story but felt the characters were a bit lacking. However, persona 3 reload fixed it for me. The characters are now well-fleshed out and their development can be seen throughout the game! Aside from being relatable, they’ve become more lovable too. The cast is phenomenal, fun, and memorable. The additional interactions really helped, imo.

Also, Koromaru.

The story has got to be one of the best stories in jrpgs. It tugs emotions, makes you think about your own life (lmao). Seems like an exaggeration but it’s true.

Also, voiced social links???? Amazing. P3r also has some of my favorite social links so I’m glad they’re voiced now.

The new game mechanics also make tartarus a bit more fun. Grinding is still there but it feels slightly less ughhh than fes. But when I get my favorite personas, it’s worth it. Although i did miss when shuffle time were cards actually getting shuffled.

Overall, it’s an amazing game that smt fans and jrpg fans or even newcomers should try at least once. It’s a unique experience that won’t leave your mind for months… maybe even years. I see myself replaying again after I finish processing my feelings and thoughts haha.

I am also aware of Episode Aigis coming and how sega is handling it. It might not be favorable to everyone but I kinda think it’s unfair to take it out on this game. But that’s just me.

Tldr: Excellent game, great remake 9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell Persona fans, I think we've finally "hit that point". This is a negative review simply because Atlus (and Sony) have effectively entered into the over-monetization phase of this franchise. $35 DLC for a few songs, costumes and maybe cut content? Ugh.

The original Persona 3 on PS2, Persona 4 on PS Vita and Persona 5 on PS3/4 were ALL great games and I highly recommend them. In fact, they've been doing a great job all the way up to Persona 5 Royal.

But all good things must eventually end and so...here we are. Charging $70 for a game from the PS2 days with just up-scaled graphics, a few minor gameplay changes and dialogue modification for the worse to "align better with today's audience" (it's that other W word that I dare not say here).

Can't say I've beaten this version as I couldn't stomach some of the "modifications" made to align with today's audience. If this is your very first Persona, you'll love it...get distracted by the shinies and never understand the excellent writing and music you've never seen/heard.

If you're a long time fan of Persona from the PS1 days, this is a slap in the face.\t\t\t\t\t
