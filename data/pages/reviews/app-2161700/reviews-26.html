\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter finishing the game, I have never felt so empty in my whole life. This game just hits the right spots for a remake.

The story and side stories for certain social links are amazing. The themes and messages this game tried to convey was spot on and I still think it hits hard for people today then it did 18 years ago when it first came out. The characters in this game were fantastic. Sure maybe the EN cast is a bit hard to get used to for OG fans since it doesn't feel the same. But since I never played the old P3 but heard good things about it, it was actually pretty enjoyable.

The UI in this game is absolutely top notch. Knowing that it took inspirations from Persona 5 Royal, It helps fans who started off with that game to help get used to this one (I still got no idea how Atlus one upped the UI after P5R, still mindboggles me). I also cannot skip the soundtrack, like cmon now this is some of the best music I've heard in a long while. I really hope when Persona 6 comes out the music becomes an absolute hit (I know it will tho cus Atlus xD).

The combat in this game feels more nicer and polished compared to P5R (hot take I know). But given that it uses the Unreal Engine, it makes it feel smooth and not so "jagged". AAnd I gotta say, grinding for different fusions in this game and Tartarus feels fun, doesn't felt too repetitive (once your near the ending tho, then it will start to feel that way). But overall, a great revamp of the Tarturus grind from the older games and its more enjoyable now.

The only downsides I can see is that one, while it does look appealing, there are some moments where the graphics and UI start to look hella weird a bit, could be just my perspective but idk. Even tho I praised he combat, there is some parts where I think it could do better. But overall those are the only complaints. Another would be that the DLC that is gonna come out on September isn't included with the Digital Premium Edition of the game which absolutely sucks and I wish Atlus just puts it in with it and gives out to those who actually bought the thing for 70$. Oh and no Femc, Atlus still not wanting to give us the gud stuff as usual xd

But overall, I highly recommend this game. Sure there are some flaws with the game, but for what it offers it is a very amazing game. And after my review for Persona 5 Royal, I think this would be the best starter to the Persona series now since it just has the right elements. Amazing story, amazing characters, amazing UI, amazing turn based combat and also that absolute banger of a soundtrack. Now hopefully they recreate The Answer (Episode: Aigis) which is gonna come out on September does it justice (even tho its disappointing that it isn't included as stated above). But overall, This game gets a 9.6/10 from me.

Now I shall sob in bed for a week after that ending ;-;
