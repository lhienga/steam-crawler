\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWanted to wait until I had fully beaten and 100%'ed the game before leaving a review, but it took a bit longer than I wanted because I messed up and didn't finish a single social link in my first run, lmao.


It's pretty simple, if you like JRPGs, and the newer persona games like 5, you'll love what they've done with this remake. That said, the game is definitely much easier now than the original; I beat this without a guide on Merciless with a few hiccups in certain spots, but really just power levelled through the game lmao.

Overall, while not as challenging as the original, I'd still say it's a faithful remake that does a great job of making the game accessible to people who maybe played P4 Golden and P5 and hoped they could go back to the earlier entries but found the OG P3 a little dated.

Honestly, my personal opinion is to wait until The Answer releases before you choose to buy the game. Knowing Atlus, they'll bring out a version you can buy that'll be the same price as the current version now, and the Answer is the continuation and wrap up of the story that they didn't include this time around.

JRPG fan? Buy it.
Persona fan? Buy it.
Like flashy games and kick-ass soundtracks? Buy it.

Hardcore challenge fan? Maybe skip this one, or play on Merciless and limit your grinding so you maintain the level gap with the bosses as you progress? That's up to personal preference, I guess.\t\t\t\t\t
