\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy very first Persona game was Persona 3 Portable way back in 2012. I was instantly hooked on everything the game had to offer: the music, the characters, the story, and the game-play. I still vividly remember staying up all night to finish the final boss on the night right before my very first day in high-school. I cried watching those end credits. Since then I've been a massive fan of the Persona series and was ecstatic when I saw a Persona 3 remake announced.

In every way has this game exceeded my expectations. I wasn't sure if they'd be able to do it justice, but they managed to recreate those same feelings I had all those years ago and make me care even more about the characters. The additional linked episodes with the male cast, and the new hangouts with all the party members in the dorm really made me feel connected to them. The combat feels faster and more visually appealing. Tartarus is much less of a grind and has fantastic visuals. The performances from all the voice actors are incredible and they've made me fall in love with the characters all over again. I thought since I'd played through Persona 3 FES just last year that this wouldn't hit me as hard, but man Persona 3 Reload is just incredible. I can't wait to come back to this game and re-experience it all over again someday.

If you've never played a Persona game this is a fantastic starting point, and if you've played through a version of Persona 3 already I still highly recommend picking this up. At full price this is more than worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
