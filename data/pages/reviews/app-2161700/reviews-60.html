\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA beautiful game from PS2 era being remade to modern standards with the lots of improvements included. If you happen to miss the experience of playing it, like I did, this is the great opportunity to pick it up and enjoy it. The upcoming DLC called Aegis Episode, also known as The Answer is coming in September so you can get the complete Persona 3 Original Experience. The female main character, which is favored by many, so far will remain the PSP exclusive, so if you are curious about that, there's a Persona 3 Portable port available on PC. Other than that - the long conflict of which version to play in order to get the most of Persona 3 experience is over with the introduction of this remake.

A small note for those who will try this game for the first time - there are certain things that are different from other Persona games, like Persona 4 Golden or Persona 5 Royal. One such thing is inability to carry over the most of acquired content to the Merciless Difficulty Mode. This means whatever you earned while playing the game on that difficulty can only be used again if you choose difficulty one step lower, Hard or Below. In other words - Merciless does not support the New Game Plus. Be informed of that before you start playing the game for the first time. As for the rest, it's mostly the same experience.

P.S You can enhance your experience even further with Persona 3 Reloaded AI Companions Mod ( available online via Google ) which makes AI Team mates use more features and generally perform much better than what native in-game AI can do, making the game feel even closer to original Persona 3 where your team mates where always AI controlled. It's optional thing, of course, you can always assume control over the rest of the team and do everything by yourself or even go solo if you really want to. The choice is yours.
