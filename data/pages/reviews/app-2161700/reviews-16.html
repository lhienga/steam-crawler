\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a really beautiful game! I've never played the original, so I'm really happy I got to experience for the first time this way :). I think I'm about halfway through, and I'm definitely hooked on the story and characters! I've played Persona 5, which is pretty great, and this one hits all the same marks for a really enjoyable experience! Part of me hopes that they'll remake 1 and 2 in the same way, if the stories and characters are just as good as these ones :)

It's hard for me to think up any criticisms, and the ones I've seen from other reviewers I just don't agree with. Someone said that Tartarus still felt "too empty" with the updated graphics, but I really think they couldn't be more wrong! Each level has been eerie in its own way with really cool designs, I guess sometimes the enemies are just a bit easy to run away from though? With all the space. So they're easy to ambush and avoid being ambushed yourself, you basically just run around a corner and they immediately forget you were there. But that really isn't the point of Persona games anyway, its more important to be able to prepare good element matchups and stay on top of your types and weaknesses when you're trying to fuse your collection and get stronger. Always having the jump on enemies isn't gonna make you automatically win.

It's hard to tear yourself away from the game, despite you being able to save whenever you want, so be warned! You boot this game up, you might come to your senses hours later and realize you havent eaten all day LMAO\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR a remaster that does the original justice. If you want one of the best games persona has to offer in HD, this is your game right here.


Persona 3 Reload isnt a game to me so much as it is an experience. I think its not a stretch to say this game is phenomenal, even with its flaws. And because of that its very hard to put my feelings into words for this game, half of this game is growing bonds and making memories. And the other half is fighting other worldly monsters, typical persona stuff. But where i think this game shines is the first half, a lot of these characters are very well written (except you nozomi, literally one of the worst S Links in all of persona) and emotionally compelling.

Another great thing about this game is the music, a lot of the songs are phenomenal, there are some songs that are misses (in my opinion one of those misses is mass destruction). Besides the very few misses, the soundtrack is phenomenal. Its going down now is unironically one of my favorite persona songs of all time, and both, memories of you and when moons reaching out to stars (listen to the lyrics, or read em to see why) never fail to make me cry.

Gameplay. ill admit, gameplay is its weakest strength. Dont get me wrong, im not saying its horrible. Im just saying out of this games many strengths, this is the least strong (still strong tho!). I found a lot of the battles way too easy (was playing on hard), i mean i literally beat the last boss without a single member going down and was bullying that shirtless fraud like we were both in grade school and i wanted his lunch money.

Lastly, the story. Holy ♥♥♥♥, the story. Ive never cried over a game until this game (both original and remake). Even though i knew well in advance what happened, i still sobbed over the ending. And it wasnt just the ending either, seeing all these characters battle with the inevitable and overcome it in their own way was really poetic given the story. And the moments leading up to the climax where all your social links give you their energy was another thing that got me in the feels, made the final moment all more impact. A beautiful end to a beautiful game
