\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a Persona fan that started with 3 and FES, I wanted to like this game. I wanted to see an updated version of Persona 3 with all the FES additions that included some of the QoL and systems we've been introduced to in 4 and 5. Instead I got an extremely beautiful disappointment. What's worse is that with the addition of Denuvo, this single-player JRPG isn't playable on the go with a Steam Deck. Atlus also made the absolutely disgusting decision of removing the FES content and The Answer for a DLC that bumps the game's cost over $100. That's for a game that has basically no additions outside of looking prettier.

This game is so dated and lacks the nuance and features of the more recent additions. No where is that more clear in the extremely boring Social Link system, that adds absolutely nothing to the game outside of increasing Persona levels upon fusion. Would have really liked to see this updated with the additional passive abilities that P5 gave us that made Social Links more interesting and dynamic. This complaint is indicative of the rest of the game. It's better looking but there haven't been any new additions, in fact, it's a worse game with less content than Persona 3 FES.
