\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPersona 3 was my introduction into the Persona series, so naturally the story as a special place in my heart. I’m usually neutral on the topic of remakes of older games since I either never played the original or find the remake and the original are practically the same, except for updated graphics or similar cosmetic changes. Technically yes, this game does not differ from the original. It has some added elements from the two later variations Persona 3 FES and Persona 3 Portable such as some additional gameplay, but overall this is a very similar game to the original. However, this is still a lot of more content added to the story in this version that no other version had. There’s simple things like a bunch more spoken dialogue and new mechanics added that were introduced in Persona 4 and Persona 5. However there is some new story content added. Nothing major that changes the plot of the game, but it just adds to it. Personally, despite P3 being my favorite in the franchise, I was never too attached to the characters. I thought they were interesting sure, but none of them were super memorable to me. The added story content added a lot of characterization and context for characters that, honestly looking back on it, really needed it. Some of the characters that I thought as forgettable before are honestly some of my favorite characters now. This by far is my favorite version of P3, the only downside is that I do wish the female protagonist from P3P were included so that this would be the definitive version of the game. I also wanted The Answer from P3 FES, but that has been since announced as dlc.\t\t\t\t\t
