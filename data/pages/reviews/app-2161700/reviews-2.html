- Incredibly fun cast whom is equally as well written. I had so much fun playing through all the SLs, I dont think there was anyone in the main cast I didnt like
- Combat can be fun at times
- An ending that is incredibly satisfying and thematically beautiful
- THE MUSIC
- Storytelling and narrative, almost perfect
- Fun inbetween events such as festivals and holidays that keeping me invested and reducing the insanity of almost 300 tartarus floors
-

| What I Don't Like |
- Tart becomes boring incredibly fast, I had to multitask to keep myself sane playing through it
- Party should all be kept at the same level, yes theres a way to level up party members who are far behind but it never worked for me
- So many bosses that are just damage sponges
- Sent back progress when MC dies rather than the whole party
- Money hungry Atlus leaving 'the answer' dlc out
- It's persona, so obviously there is ped0 stuff
- Game presents us what it thinks is a difficult choice but its for a character we met only a few hours ago...

Favorite Character - Aigis
Personal Time to beat - 60 hours

10/10 - Even though the grinding aspect of this game I despise, almost everything else about this game was perfect.
