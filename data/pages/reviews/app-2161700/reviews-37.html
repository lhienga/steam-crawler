\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Base game's alright, definitely an improvement over the original (not FES). Now they have an "Expansion Pass" that's an extra $35 bucks which adds The Answer from FES, better cosmetics than the ones purchasable with base game and better ost from previous Persona games (music you'd actually enjoy listening to over the Reload OST). I bought the $100+ edition of this 'cause I was originally very excited and what I got was awful and it doesn't make sense that this "Expansion Pass" isn't available for free to the people who paid $100+ (not trying to sound like a spoiled brat but I've given this company too much money already, money they don't deserve at this point). The digital version of the soundtrack and art book are cooked into the game launcher so you have to start the launcher to choose between the game, art book and soundtrack (which you can't listen to the ost out of the application either for some reason which makes no sense considering Steam has a built in Soundtrack player for SPECIFICALLY SPUNDTRACKS). Apparently the Aegis Episode dlc is cut content from the base game (from what I've heard) and that just makes me even more upset considering I paid $100+ for this mediocre turn based rpg.
I don't think Sega is being greedy, I fully believe this is entirely Atlus milking their fans just because they can. They couldn't care less if they're upcharging content that shouldn't be more than $20 because they know their fans will buy the garbage they put out regardless of the price. I'd say this is absurd but honestly I'm not surprised. I don't recommend this to anyone, just get an emu and play the FES, it might be slower and more monotonous than Reload but at least you get the content that should have been in base game of Reload.
If you bought this game, I'm sorry for your loss but you probably bought it with a similar thought process I had (but at least we get some pretty cool mods, there's a FeMc mod coming to the game which looks pretty good so at least we have that to look forward to). Can't wait to see how predatory P6's marketing and pricing will be.\t\t\t\t\t
