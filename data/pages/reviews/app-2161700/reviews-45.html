\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game and my favourite of the modern persona games.
In my opinion this is the definitive way to experience The Journey (original Persona 3 story) and will most likely be better than FES for experiencing The Answer (additional story that takes place after The Journey added in Persona 3 FES) since actually playing it in FES was kinda ass.
The story is in my opinion the most interesting out of Persona 3, 4 and 5. The characters are great and the less fleshed out ones are expanded on with the new feature of Link Episodes and hang outs at the dorm.
The new music fits like a glove and the voice actors do a great job (the biggest winner here being ♥♥♥♥♥). Fully voice acted Social Links are also greatly appreciated.
The omission of the female protagonist is a shame, but not surprising since it would probably be quite costly to redo all the voice acting and the entire soundtrack, all of the UI, animations and the animated scenes by Wit studio.
Truly my only gripe would be the pricing, 70€ with 30€ of day 1 DLC is mad. On top of that 35€ for the expansion pass where the only worthwhile content is The Answer expansion (the new outfits and more music is nice but ultimately who really cares about it). So unless you really love the series I would wait for a sale.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the best way to experience Persona 3. The visuals are gorgeous, new tracks are all bangers, there are a bunch of QoL features that bring the game up to current standards and the gameplay has also been significantly updated. The new Shift and Theurgy mechanics, combined with all the flashy UI and animations, make it so the combat always feels cool and badass. Tartarus received a noticeable glow up with some new activities to keep the climb engaging. The Link Episodes and new Dorm Activities gave some much needed ways to spend time with the SEES crew. The ending was a serious emotional punch.

My only major complaint is that the Social Link schedule could be slightly reworked to allow spending time with schoolmates on off-school days and maybe shift a character or two for night-time Social Links. Because while I understand that the game stays faithful to the original, there ends up being a lot of wasted time that would otherwise make maxing all Social Links in a single run less of a stressful mess.

I've played the entirety of the game on the Steam Deck. Initially the performance was less than great and I played around half of the game in 30 FPS. Over time, though, the updates made the game run pretty well at mostly 60 FPS. I can wholeheartedly recommend playing this game on the handheld.\t\t\t\t\t
