\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst time playing Persona 3. Writing this one day after completing the game.
Let me just say: what a ride. Can't remember when a game made me this emotional at the end. It has one of the best Social Links I read as of now (still need to play 4) and I prefer some story beats over 5.
I only played Persona 5 Royal before this and I remember somebody clowning on all of us Persona 5 players for having to deal with Tartaros. He was right. What the hell was that? The remake probably made this section of the game even more bearable. I can't imagine how torturing it must have been in 2006 lol.
The battle theme made up for it though. The music in Persona 3 generally goes very hard and I think it even topped 5, which already is impeccable.
Characters are aight. I think this one will probably go to 4 tbh, but they definetely have their merits, their designs are pretty to look at and Kirijo driving a motorcycle makes her superior to Makoto in my eyes.
I related more to the themes of 3 then 5 and think some story beats were just better here. The way it all ended. My god... it still sends shivers down my spine. Beautiful!
I don't know how to feel about the Expansion Pass situation. Part of me really wants to play it, because of what everyone has been telling me about it making the game feel more complete and me just wanting more Persona 3, but also 30$ is just shameless...
Anyways this game in itself is great enough. You'll get sucked in. It's more Persona. What more do you need?\t\t\t\t\t
