There's a lot of features in here that add a lot of quality of life to where I feel it's easily the most accessible Persona title - provided you can get past the abysmal pacing that not even the remake could fully fix. The difficulty is pretty managable, and the game even adds a rewind feature in case you want to fix a mistake you made earlier, some might see this as making the game too easy, but I think it's a great couple of additions. My only complaint with the difficulty is it felt a little too easy, even on hard. I'll have to give merciless a try and see if that changes things though.

If I had any other complaints, it'd be the occasional points where you can see corners were cut. Mainly in Club Escapade where there's very little NPC animation.

Overall a great Persona title, just wish ATLUS wouldn't have put it as pricy as it is, and then sell Episode Aigis as additional DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
