\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPersona 3 Reload was one of my most anticipated games of this year, and now that I've played it it's definitely cementing that spot for my own personal GOTY. With the all new added story elements and inclusion of full voice acting for many of the important story beats and the side content, a lot of the in-dungeon gameplay mechanics have also been overhauled to match Persona 5s as well, leading to a more streamlined combat experience - (mostly) without all the BS.

The new voice cast definitely took some time to get used to, but even the more questionable voice changes Yukari definitely won me over, with wonderful performances from the excellent cast of characters that had even left me going as far as missing them well after the game was over. The music too - oh man! Lotus Juice and the new vocalist knocked it out of the park with many of the tracks, with ones like It's Going Down Now and Color Your Night that have been stuck in my head well after the game was over.

Persona 3 is a must play for people that enjoy JRPGs. The story is definitely a lot more darker than the future entries, and may leave some players uncomfortable, but I cannot recommend it highly enough and to just stick with it. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
