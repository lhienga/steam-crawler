\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has been my favourite way to experience the story of Persona 3. The voice actors killed it, they've done an amazing job polishing up not only the graphics of the game, but also the Quality of Life in the battle and dungeon crawling system. Its a premium way to experience the story and gives me hope that other remakes come out as beautifully from the team.

There are a few areas I had hopes for that maybe down the line they'll hit, some are unreasonable, some not as much:
1) Introducing the Female MC in P3P back on their first swing at re-releasing Persona 3 created an implied precedent that we'd see FeMC in the future but that wasn't he case. I would've loved to have the option to play through as her.
2) The Answer though divisive in the community would be great to see in the future perhaps reworked a little bit. Many folks won't know canonically there's more to the tale told, unless the team wanted to get away from the narrative choices they made during that bonus game on the original FES version of the game.
3) Far smaller reasonable thing, but there were just some moments where the clean art style took away from the scene. Its only a few times and it toned down the comedic effect, but without context of a prior version of the game these would've been fine. (i.e. Yukari's sass level has been brought down a little but, but I really like her delivery in this.)

All in all, great experience, would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
