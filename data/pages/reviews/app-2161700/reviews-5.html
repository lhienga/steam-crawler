\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Wait for a sale

Never played the original, only played P5/R
Music is S tier, story takes FOREVER to get moving (like 25-30+ hours) but if you can stick it out it kinda makes getting to know the characters more organic, really feels like you all have lived together. There's only one big dungeon that changes its scenery every now and then, and it turns into a real chore to get through. That said, as long as you keep a Persona/item to nullify insta-kill moves on your main char, you can get through it fairly quickly without having to start from a previous save (there's no autosave).

The life sim parts feel super barebones, I know it's a remake of an old game but considering the $70 ask they could have done a lot more. The cast around the main story is fantastic, the side activity cast ranges from decent to forgettable - most of them have interesting circumstances rather than interesting personalities, if that makes sense. Some of them have neither. Imo it's a decent game but not worth the price tag, esp with the DLC nonsense.\t\t\t\t\t
