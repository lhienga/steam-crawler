\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA true masterpiece of a game. I am unfortunately one of those gamers that have a hard time getting into games with bad graphics, so when I heard that they remade an older Persona game I was intrigued, thought it would probably be good, but not better than Persona 5. Boy, was I wrong.

This game spirited me away into it's universe and I was immersed. I found some of the characters somewhat annoying at first but the growth and development they accomplish throughout the game just made me fall in love with them in the end.

If you are a fan of games that are JRPG, turn-based combat, and story-driven, I highly recommend Persona 3 Reload.\t\t\t\t\t
