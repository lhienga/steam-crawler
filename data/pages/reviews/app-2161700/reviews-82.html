\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally great remake imo doesn't live up to the original Persona 3 Fes (which would be hard since its my favorite video game of all time) but still a Fantastic remake and a great place for someone who has just played Persona 5 to be able to jump in and experience the best the series has to offfer imo. The game looks beautiful and all the social links being voiced is really nice. FEMC not being included is fine since that is literally making another game since her route is way different from the male protagonist and im okay with it because Minato fits the game and the themes of the game way better then Kotone. Overall having a blast with the game and would recommend this game to anyone even if they dont really like RPGS because that is how good this game is.

TLDR
9.5/10
Pros:
-Amazing Story
-Beautiful Visuals
-New Persona's from P5
-Amazing Cast and Interactions with the Characters
-Great Dungeon Crawling
-Tartarus is way better then P5s dungeons or Memonts
-Game is more Balanced then P5.

Cons:
- Voice Acting mainly Akhiko, ||Nyx|| Ikutsuki, Shinjiro, and Takeharu Kirijo. I could live with Ikutsuki and Takeharu but Akhiko, ||Nyx|| and Shinjuro are just really big misses to me and the original VAs captured the characters a lot better.
-Whole push with Akihiko and Protein. This was never in original Persona 3 thanks dancing and P4AU.
- Antique Shop was way better in original Persona 3 FES.
- No Answer but this is coming in the form of DLC.

I want to end with this game is amazing and GO AND PLAY it if you never played persona or if you never even heard of the game go and play it will leave you with unforgettable memories. Even if you have played a version of Persona 3 still Play this game since you can never get enough Persona 3
