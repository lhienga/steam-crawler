\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI almost never write reviews for games but this one I had to.

This game is absolutely beautiful. I streamed the entire thing on my Twitch YouTube and Kick of the same name as my Steam.

Persona 3 Reload has made me laugh, scream, cheer, and most impressive of all. Tear up.

The gameplay is a nice mix of life Sim and jrpg. The animations are fantastic. The graphics are beautiful. The cutscenes are amazing. And the voice acting. My god the voice acting. I felt for Akihiko when he cried over Shinjiro's funeral knowing how he feels being through something similar.

The only problem I had were the voices they had for the animals. Instead of using animal sound files they're just people making terrible animal noises. However that is the only problem I had.

This game is beautiful and is definitely one of my favorite games of all time. I look forward to personally taking time to 100% it myself.

If you guys wish to see someone's first experience with Persona 3. Part 1 of my journey is here. https://youtu.be/AaN2Vb57IZ8?si=aXGjAaD8s_i6RKvT

I highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
