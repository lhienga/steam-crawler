\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor context of who I am and why my review is the way it is. I am an amateur Persona artist who aims to mimic the style to the best of my ability so it wouldn't be a stretch to say the game as impacted me heavily. But I want to be objective and honest in my review, this review will not be comparing P3, P3FES, P3P, P3Dual, I am reviewing this as if it's standalone with a bit of acknowledgement that it is a remake costing $75(tax). I 100% the game even side objectives that do not give Steam achievements such as 100% compendium, beating Elizabeth, and seeing the bad endings. The only thing I did not do is play on merciless mode as I doubt it would make a difference in the overall experience besides certain fights. No DLC btw(I guess when Expansion fully releases with the Aigis story then I will have to buy it).

TLDR: Biased Opinion 9.5. Actual Review 8.0(3.0 if u care about price)
**Positives**

- If you like Persona games then you will most definitely enjoy this, the same core aspects are in the game with social links, fusing, and combat.
- The character designs in-game are very appealing while being different despite most of the cast being highschoolers. Even a slight change in face shape and proportion can leave a first impression instantly. Many of the social links deal with difficult topics such as bullying, self thoughts, and injuries. These aren't hidden messages as they are told to the player so you'll have to be as dense as a brick to not know.
- Most of the characters are likable and even if you don't like them then skip their dialogue. I'm sure they don't have anything important to say such as becoming handicap, abused, or that you became their favorite person ever :D. None are too overbearingly annoying and simple hangouts become vivid memories.
- The main cast actually feel like friends that hangout. Unfortunately with the ending it kinda ruins it a bit when the cast have their mind wiped it is told they were no longer friends with each other. So I can't say they would've been friends even without the Persona stick. P4 is a better cast in this regard.

**Neutral**
- The difficult is too easy. No surprise it's a modern Persona game so the difficult is naturally toward the casual fan base that is here for the story with a bit of action. I found myself mindlessly running through Tartarus once I got a decent AOE Persona. Theurgy doesn't make this any better either since you will naturally build it up overtime, if you get stuck then quickly pop the "get out of jail free card" like a uno reverse onto the opponent. In the final fight of Nyx every turn I had Theurgy rebuilt. It borderline became a Pokemon battle with press one button to win. Nonetheless I don't think this is bad for the casual audience.
- Gun. For His Neutral Special, He Wields A Gun.
- Vague quests/guide. There are many scenes such as giving Junpei the white flowers for Chidori that would not be seen if the player did not go out of their way to check the store. Especially the quests Elizabeth gives are way too vague sometimes such as cleaning a bathroom or collecting an item. These can only be done AFTER accepting the quest to "in progress" then the dialogue option will show up. Yeah imagine every batch of new quests, sitting in your chair selecting every quest manually into progress. LAAAAAAAAAME

** Negatives **

- The music. I'm not saying the music standalone is bad but when you're in the game it gets very tiring especially exploring Tartarus it's actually brain numbing. If rats make me crazy and trust me they do, this is actual torture. The same music on loop forever and they are NOT GOOD(subjective...nah they suck). I can enjoy some of the songs on their own away from the game but when playing it's impossible to ignore. Personally unplug the speakers we going raw.
- Sound Level. I don't know if this is an issue with my speakers or headphones but the sound equalizer is HORRIBLE. The music is too loud so I reduce it but then it's too quiet so I mess with settings and no solution. It's either too loud or too quiet which if you read my previous negative then maybe it's not so bad. Also the sighs or moans in this game are ear blasting, first it starts of having a normal conversation "yo Yuka-tan I misplaced my detective coat, any ideas? :D" SIGH "ace defective alright". A jumpscare when it happens and mainly on Yukari too which is really weird.
- Tartarus. That's it just a boring bland gameplay loop. Much better than Mementos in P5 but not that much better each floor has a very similar layout that becomes very tedious quickly. If you cannot bear to reach the blockage in one day then I feel bad for you spending an extra day to explore the wasteland they call "interactive gameplay".
-Yap. There's a character that said Yap as in yapping(talking too much)...ugh minor issue but it hits you at the end of the game. Right before I shut off the game it's there...yapping.
- Lack of Romance events. I believe there are maybe 2-4 in total that are actual romance events where you select your duo. For a dating sim game I would prefer more,
-
PRICE WTF FOR A REMAKE? MAKE A NEW STORY IF UR CHARGING THAT PRICE
HOOLAY that is highway robbery at it's finest and you already know us Persona fans will keep coming back. "Your life, literally, is as valuable as a summer ant. I'm just gonna stomp you, you're gonna keep coming back.. Why? Cause you keep smelling the syrup you jolly person" I will be buying the Expansion pack only in September and not a moment before to see if the outrage will make them separate the DLC. Just for that cause this game will cost $100+ for something that is a REMAKE I cannot recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
