\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't care that it's not the most definitive version of P3. This game was literally all I could've asked for and more. I loved every second of it. The game so perfectly captures the dread of death... the realization, the build up, and then the release when you finally have to let go. I, of course, wish we got the more complete version of the game and I think the "Expansion Pass" is pretty scummy, but I didn't get to experience this game the first time around. This game, in its most bare bones form, is better than any game I've played that's released this decade. The combat is amazing. Never has it felt so good to hit an enemy with debilitate, grind buffs and de-buffs and go in for the kill.
