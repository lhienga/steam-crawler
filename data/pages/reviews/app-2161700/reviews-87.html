\r\n\t\t\t\t\tpersona 5 royal is the first persona game,i played...and i liked it a lot...its art style to its ideas,incredible music,snappy combat system,and a good story....so coming from a place of love and the incredible hype surrounding it saying it has the best story in the persona series i was beyond excited....but after playing it for 25 hours i could not be be more let down by it...motivations of the characters in persona 5 were established very early and done very well....in persona 3 however,there is no real incentive to explore tartarus or be a part of this world,beyond extremely shallow reasons...mystery/story aspect of this game is handled very poorly and hinted at after hours and hours of meaningless grinding both in tartarus and in real life social links (which are,for me, very badly written and not being able of evoke any real emotion, very flat and forceful in trying to make you feel for these characters....not saying persona 5 had great characters(although confidants or individual character*s story were so much better) but what it had lacked,it made up for it through other aspect of the game and i cant say the same about persona 3),the art style(except for character designs) are not even comparable to persona 5,it is just a code of paint/added pixels over the dated ps2 game without any reimagining, the architecture/environment designs in this game are very shallow cartoonish and dated especially in the dorm,school,tartarus,the music is hit or miss..there are some great songs but also a lot of misses,...combat is fine..it is turn based and is very similar to persona 5 royal,the main dungeon in persona 3 is tartarus which is very similar to mementos which was for the most part an optional dungeon in persona 5 and tartarus like mementos is very tedious,repetitive and soulless but persona 5 had stylish and interesting main dungeons to make up for it....in the other hand tartarus being the main dungeon for persona 3 only amplifies its bad qualities...i quit playing it after 25 hours while in the end of june (ingame calendar system)fans of persona 3 claim it get better much better in september(ingame) but that is after 40-50 hours of playing the game i find mediocre at best
so in conclusion,for me persona 3 was a very bad experience for me in gaming....from its dated narrative , to its shallow characterization ,its souless artstyle,to its hit or miss music,to its badly designed and reptative dungeon crawling i found them all very lacking
but there is a new found appreciation for what the franchise would become in the future and the groundwork the was established with the original persona 3...despite all that i very much regret spending 70 dollars on a game i thought i would love...even if its cope,i think there is a lesson in disguise,for me as a new gamer to not let hype ever influence me into spending 70 dollars on a game without engaging in multiple perspectives and careful considerations(money and time are god after all)
i don*t know if anyone ever reads this and i kind of hope not...i wrote this in order to come in terms with my feelings with this game and be able to say goodbye to it without being compelled to play it for the sake of justifying 70 dollars i spent on it.
if you do infact read it sorry for bad grammer and spellings.
final rating-6/10
also i am very excited for persona 6\t\t\t\t\t
