Buy this game
If you have played the OG/FES/Portable. Reload is a great, faithful recreation of the original which adds a great deal of quality-of-life features (e.g. "Rewind" feature to roll back a few days without requiring a pre-existing save, new special attacks, and returning direct control of the party members) and also adds new events with the male party members that were previously "left without love" in FES.
Buy this game
If you have played Persona 5 and/or 4 before. You are likely to experience the most meaningful and compelling story and characters out of these three. They might not become your favorites, but SEES is definitely on par with the Investigation Team and Phantom Thieves at worst and is superior to both of them at best.
Buy this game
If you have not played any Persona before. Historically, Persona 3 was the part when the series took a major shift and distinctly branched off Shin Megami Tensei, introducing mechanics that have become the series' trademark since. Thus, I believe Reload is now the best entry to the series to experience. If you don't like it, then you've at least tried it and now know Persona games are not for you. But if you do, then I guarantee you this is a game that has a high chance of ending up in your personal Top-10 of all time, easily.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was my second mainline Persona game after playing Persona 4 Golden back in 2020. P4G is one of my favorite persona games, and I did find myself constantly comparing this game to P4G, even if I didn't mean to. The activities in Reload weren't as extensive or exciting as in Golden, but the key parts of any Persona game were all here, all of which I loved. There were times that exploring Tartarus felt a bit like an unnecessary grind, eventually I got into a pretty good rhythm and enjoyed all of my ventures into its many many floors.

The biggest strength of this game is its cast of characters, which have to be one of my favorites from any game that I've played recently, possibly even more than Golden if I'm completely honest. I genuinely enjoyed all of the time that I got to spend with all of the characters on this game, which culminated in one of the most beautiful and bittersweet endings that I've experienced in a game in a long time. I know a lot of people that said they bawled their eyes out at the end, and while I didn't personally cry, the feelings were very real in me. Overall, i would recommend this game to just about anyone, be they newcomers to the series or series veterans.\t\t\t\t\t
