\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow.... where to begin.

As a new player to the Persona series having just beaten Persona 5 Royal a little over a year ago. I had high expectations for this game, and I was not disappointed. This game blew every expectation I had out of the water. The score, the characters, the combat, the level design, everything about this game was phenomenal.

I understand there is some hate for some aspects of the game (i.e. Tartarus), but I can see where some of the criticism can come from. It seems like quite the grind and can be intimidating but becomes very important the later in the game you progress. I feel that Mementos in P5R were done much better, however, each one is a unique experience tied to either game. However, Tartarus can feel more like a drag at times than Mementos did.

Overall, I think the game was fantastic, it would be difficult to say which one I enjoyed and loved more between P5R and P3R. I love them both for different reasons.

I could get behind Persona 3's characters much more, and the weight of the story could be felt at all times. This game truly does a great job of making you question your own morality. The concept of death (which many people struggle with), and the idea of being on borrowed time. And what you would do with that borrowed time . Never have I played a game that made me think as deeply as P3R, and made me feel for the characters like this game did.

Thank you, Atlus, for a truly wonderful experience. This only makes me more excited for the other Persona games to be remastered in the future.
