\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat an amazing game, I wish the rest of the industry would take notice, because this is how a remake is supposed to be - they remained faithful to the original story, but they improved gameplay, mechanics, visuals, soundtrack.

ATLUS spared no expense when it came to this remake and it really shows, gameplay is fantastic, the characters are well written and expanded upon, while the visuals down to the last UI element are absolutely stunning. The entire game has that special shine that conveys that this was a labor of life project for them.

The story is beautiful and had me captivated, even if the storytelling shows it's age a bit here and there, it doesn't detract from it's value at all. I also love that the writers weren't afraid to tackle topics that are mature/dark, but still an inevitable part of life.

I wanted to give it a 9/10 personal score but after witnessing the ending I feel like a 10/10 is more appropriate, and honestly, I never expected to be so conflicted whenever Persona 4 or this game should be at the my number one spot, but I think there's room for both of them at the top.



Thank you, ATLUS, both for giving this story the shine it deserved, and for bringing it to other platforms as well.\t\t\t\t\t
