- remixed songs that are so-close to the originals but have an uncanny-valley effect with altered lyrics, inflections or instrument changes
- some of the most impactful animated cutscenes of the original game were scrapped, but the anime budget still got spent on other less-important scenes instead. Did we need one for SEES and their new armbands?
- the new voice actors are mostly fine, some are better than the originals. koromaru's now voiced by a person though and makes me cringe every time i hear the fake barks and awooos. Especially if it's during cutscenes that are trying to be emotional.

that's all whatever. it's still a good game. i'm mostly just tired of this company that charges above regular retail price for a remake of a 15 year old game and plays coy, then announces paid DLC for content that was included in the base game before.
The Answer was pretty grindy and forgettable the first time around, so I won't buy it this time.
Allegedly this is the last DLC for the game, but now I'm in this weird state where I don't even feel hopeful about FEMC or P3P content. If plans for it exist, I can only imagine a second full retail release, USD $70+ again. I'm just done lol\t\t\t\t\t
