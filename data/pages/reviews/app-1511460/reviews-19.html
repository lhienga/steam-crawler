\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd rather give this a "Maybe", and EA titles should have a maybe button because this city builder has some good mechanics and some game breakers.

The game breaker I'm going to advise about is that the "traffic" system is broken. Factories crank out product, and it gets delivered 1 piece at a time. So instead of hauling 100 sand, a sand delivery truck delivers 1 "sand" and then has to drive back to it's building (this is the "hard" mode). There's nothing hard about it except that it's artificially overloading the transportation system. A meal delivery vehicle delivers 1 "survival" pack rather than 10. Now some items should be delivered this way, large items that are produced slower, fine. However, in the current iteration, every item large, small, or tiny is delivered 1 at a time, which means you have 1000x more vehicles than citizens in your cities, and you WILL find yourself in gridlock due to this system. The traffic design just currently can't handle large scale cities in it's current version. Definitely has potential, here's to hoping for an update!\t\t\t\t\t
