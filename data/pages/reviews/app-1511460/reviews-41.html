There's no rush, no pressure (good or bad).
Graphics are ok even though some things like road connections and building entrances need some love.
More mechanics would be nice and they are comming in roadmap (fingers crossed).
Game is worth supporting and has potential.
Logistic part is interesting. You only have storage for construction materias and other stuff like iron is stored at drills and supplied to whatever building needs it via truck. I'd like to see a dedicated storage unit to have more control over what and where is stored for further (closer) chain supply.\t\t\t\t\t
