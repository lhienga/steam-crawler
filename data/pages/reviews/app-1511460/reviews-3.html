\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game does what more city builder-types should: gives you an objective (in this case, repairing your ship). To do that you have to do the usual management sim stuff, like build production chains and keep people happy.

There are (as of this writing) some issues or things that aren't always explained, or that you don't know are happening. For example, getting your habitats levelled up requires a certain amount of resources (food, consumer goods, robots, etc.), which is fine, but the amount you need to keep people happy increases. So you build your production chain to meet the demand, but before you know it that demand has skyrocketed and your habs are getting downgraded. Which, again, is fine, but there needs to be some kind of notification mechanism to let you know that's going to happen. Things like that make it really difficult to long-term plan anything (like industrial area layouts, transport routes, etc.) and you end up spending a lot of time going back to add production, balance its resource needs, and so on, rather than progressing.

Still, it's early days at this point, so we'll see how it all shakes out.\t\t\t\t\t
