\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe city building aspect is almost exactly like the Anno games. (Not a problem honestly) Logistics in the game is all about making the next product to upgrade your population buildings. And also to manage truck traffic efficiently.

Infraspace has a research system which I like but it mostly serves as a lock for the next building tree you need to upgrade your population buildings. It would be nice to also have tech to increase factory output. You have to build an excessive amount of electronics buildings. Maybe this was done to add more building synergy for bonuses. I would personally scrap that system and add research.

It's good that the buildings are low poly to save on processing power. But they could use some texture work to try and hide the low poly. After the core development phase is over the game needs a good deal of polish.

I like the road system in the game but I don't like how trucks travel on the roads. Especially how the trucks jump lanes and don't transition. But also change lanes in unnecessary ways causing traffic jams. You can mitigate this problem by setting custom lane transitions but the default setup is problematic. The ability to control what trucks do at intersections is great and reminds me of a traffic mod for Cities Skylines.

All of this can be fixed but some of these systems seem like intended features. I'm not sure over time how this will shake out. But as it is right now it's very meh.\t\t\t\t\t
