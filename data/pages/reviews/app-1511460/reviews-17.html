\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is very addictive and clearly deserves more attention. I hope the developers will release additional content in the future to attract the attention of more people (surely many content creators will return to the game to watch and show new features). Personally, I would like more gigantomania. Larger locations with an uneven concentration of resources, so that it makes sense to create many cities with specialization. Planetary logistics (so that several maps with cities produce components for some global construction, for example, an orbital shipyard or/and some kind of warp engine). I think a larger scale is something that can attract the attention of a wider audience. There are already quite a lot of construction and production strategies on the market, but there are much fewer games with global infrastructure (I can immediately remember only Dyson Sphere Program).\t\t\t\t\t
