\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wasn't sure of this game at first. I'm always on the lookout for good adult sandbox games this one was always recommended, but the price seemed a bit high to take a chance on it. I ended up pulling the trigger and initially... eh, mixed results. The actions you need to take are not intuitive, often incredibly stupid. Patrick and Frank will get on your nerves very quickly. But the sprites are pretty good and some of the dialogue was funny, so I kept going. Eventually I felt like the results were really not worth the effort.

So why the positive review? The fan-made DLC. Seriously, get an EEK! Games account and download the House of Lust! It unlocks what you really want out of a game like this and puts you in the driver's seat for a straight-up s3x party!

PROS


- Pretty good quality 3D sprites.
- Control is decent and mostly intuitive. Narrow corridors can sometimes result in a logjam, but the characters will give you room if you smack into them.
- Choice as playing as a male or female.
- Good variety in the scenes.
- Voices are all well-captured.
- Vickie. She's just awesome.
- Fan-made DLC. This is where the real potential of the game comes alive and becomes the 0rgy-simulator I really wanted.


CONS


- The "Original Story" is so forgettable, not even the Game Grumps can salvage it. Don't bother with it unless you're interested in the achievements.
- Patrick. You will hate this character with every fibre of your being. At least he's regularly beaten by the other characters.
- The characters are not fleshed out very well and hew to time-worn s3x-comedy tropes. Many are downright unpleasant to each other, particularly Madison and Ashley.
- Certain choices cut off others and scenes are often not repeatable. Many require you to do terrible things to the other characters.
- What you need to do next is not always clear based on the given cues. In addition, it's really to accidentally click on a choice that makes a character hate you.
- Item hunting is a chore.
- Grinding through the game twice as the two available genders just to get all the achievements will wear on you.
- Wish there was a bit more variety in the sprites.
- The uncensored version costs extra.

SUMMARY


The only thing that really makes this worth the money is the DLC. But it does succeed at doing that. Otherwise, don't bother.\t\t\t\t\t
