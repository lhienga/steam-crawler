\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOthers may like this but I found myself to be uninterested. While I wouldn't recommend, I don't want to dissuade someone who might enjoy the game. It just wasn't my flavor. My bar may also be different than others as I go through the adult games and find that I'm not a fan of most of them.

The game has you entering a party where you need to talk to your friend and then chat other people up. Each conversation is a bread crumb trail to potential sex relationships, but if you say the wrong thing you tick them off and sex may not be possible. You also have to go around the house to locate items that you can use on other items or give to people to help progress the story.

If you find enjoyment about puzzling out the right things to say for each individuals and finding all the items you need, you may enjoy this game. Without a guide I expect it'll take multiple tries and lots of saves. While I usually play with the sound off, this game has sound and you may want to use during your sex scenes. During sex scenes you may have some options, but teasing didn't appear one of them for me, which means you're going to end it quickly as opposed to any kind of simulation. Once you figure out the right paths, .

The negatives for me is that this isn't really my play style, at least not with this game. The graphics were pretty bad or dated. There is a TON of clipping issues. The being a jerk stuff, you can't really have fun with it due to realistic consequences. Part of the fun of a video game is to be able to get away with doing things you might not normally do, at least when it seems like it's encouraging playfulness. I was a little disappointed that the game was straight laced with all the females as opposed to being playful. If you talk to people out of order, you also risk saying the wrong thing because your character is apparently an idiot without the right piece of knowledge and will insert their foot rapidly. The camera scanning seems to lock up on occasion, and the game is sub-optimal on a touch pad. A mouse would likely improve the experience for me, but that's not currently a real option for me. The saving takes longer than I'd like with a few second pause.

If you want to quickly know if you'll like the game, I'd recommend one of the short stories that game comes with versus the main game. Look up the control configs so you know how to navigate and then go play. Even with a little trial and error they won't take long and you can decide if this is a purchase you want to continue with. It's not a horrible game and I'm positive many will enjoy it more than I did.

So, the game will be for some people and it's not an adult novel which some may appreciate, but ultimately it's not for me. The story wasn't really compelling, the fun/non-sense wasn't what i thought it would be, the player being unable to say something that doesn't tick a girl off if they didn't talk to someone else first drove me nuts. So, not my cup of tea. Others may appreciate the challenge and to them I say kudos and go for it! I may poke again, but i mostly feel done after four hours.\t\t\t\t\t
