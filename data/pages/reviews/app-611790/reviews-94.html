\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're already into this type of game (open-ended/multiple endings, adult themed, mission-based), then I think you'll probably enjoy House Party, and for you, I'd say: don't even hesitate, get this game.

If you're not into these kinds of games, then probably don't bother with this one.

A few caveats:
- This game is Early Access and feels like it. It has a few bugs here and there, it might glitch out on you and crash in unexpected/unpredictable ways.
- Some of the missions have oddly specific progressions that can be frustrating. One example of this: There's a mission where you have to open a locked door, and there's an option to "Unlock" the door, and a separate option to "Open" the door. If you "Unlock" and then "Open", nothing will happen. But if you simply "Open" the locked door (for which you have a key), then the door will open, and the mission will continue. Funky things like that are minor, but still annoyances
- Some of the pathfinding looks kind of awkward and clunky. E.g., watching NPCs navigate their way through an open door, or around obstacles/other NPCs, can look funny... But it generally works. And pathfinding is hard - even in 2D. This game is 3D and the paths can go up/down stairs. Overall, the devs have done a commendable job here.

But on the flipside:
- The dev/discord community is active, and it feels like the game is definitely being maintained.
- There is no shortage of walkthroughs/forums/wikia documentation to guide you through.
- The jokes in this game are funny; the adult scenes are well-done. Animations, interactions, etc.. All of it's pretty solid (Aside from the stuff I mentioned above)

Don't try to figure this game out by yourself! Use the Force! Er, I mean, the community!

One other note: Apparently the Steam version, even though it's uncensored for most NPCs, was patched to censor NPCs that are based on people in real life. That seems to piss some people off; I bought this game after that patch was implemented, so it doesn't bother me all that much. But if that bothers you, then consider getting this game on itch.io or GameJolt.\t\t\t\t\t
