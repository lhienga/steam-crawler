\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlenty of games offer you an interesting story while you try to figure out what's going on. This game contains quite a few story lines and lots of different missions.

House Party is about you going to a party at someone's house. You start out by helping a friend, but later you seem to be in the middle of gossip, stories, and helping people accomplish their tasks. There are plenty of people at this party who want to be your friend and are willing to do "ANYTHING" to get you on their side.

The gameplay is is fairly straight forward. Walk around the house talking to people and getting hints on tasks and quests to get them to like you. Each person has 3 needs to be met if you want them to like, love you, or just out right get drunk. The yellow bar is a trust bar, means they will talk to you about anything and even share their secrets. The red bar is the love bar, the bigger the bar, the more they are willing to have sex with you or at least get naked. The last bar is the drunk bar, the more they drink, the more drunk they will be.

Each time you talk to a person, you get choices on what your response can be. Some choices are bad while others are good. Obviously you want to make the right choices so they will like you more and offer you more options. Some will even give you some personal favors in bed, on the couch, or even in front of other people.

Various items can be found around the house as you walk from room to room. Items include but not limited to keys, beer, food, electronics, adult toys, even sunglasses. People will from time to time ask you about an item and you will need to see if you can get that item for them. Not all items are visible, so you may have to talk to some people who are willing to give up those items to you. One warning, be careful not to take items while certain people are watching, or they will get angry and they will beat you up and you will lose their friendship.

Some people don't like other people, so you may have to choose sides on who you want to be friends with. There is no real way to get everyone to like you at the same time. You are going to have to make choices that will get on one side or they other. This is one of those games where choices can have consequences.

Some of the DLC are okay. The holiday DLC's just adds festive decorations with a few small extras to keep the game interesting. Liz Katz DLC is based on the actual cosplay model, who will hold a mystery murder at the house. Doja Cat DLC is based on the actual person, but with a few surprises and twists to the story.

Overall the game is entertaining with a fairly interesting story line, outside the sex and nudity. It's not much different from a typical house party you might have been to, only not as dramatic, at least I hope not.

My only personal gripe is that you are limited to a small confined space so don't expect to travel much further than the house you are at.\t\t\t\t\t
