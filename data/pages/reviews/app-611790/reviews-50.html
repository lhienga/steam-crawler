\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought into House Party a while back.
To be frank (dood), it was the game grump's original play through that made me want to play it, but also my love for older Leisure Suit Larry games that made me actually hit the buy button.

Is it good?
All I can say is, yes, there's sex in this game and full nudity if that's all you want, but to not talk about the character missions, diverse cast, clear love this passions project has received, different outcomes and constant hard work the devs put into this, not to mention the voice actors, both guest, which now also include the Grumps themselves, and the OG cast, would be vastly underselling how fun this game has been.

Multiple play through sessions worth of content, choose your own adventure game with ridiculous caricatures who end up being more depraved than you could expect, but with a... slightly less vile core made of good intentions. Its tongue planted firmly in cheek helps keep the player character going (who is also a bit of a sleezy ball, but can have a slightly less gross core of like, about 7 carat to 16 carat gold. If I had to say anything negative, it'd be that there can be run ending bugs that stop mission progression all together and despite these being minor annoyances that can prevent you from completing a mission chain,

House Party is a fun adventure game with lots of characters that get fleshed out through your own choices. Puzzles sometimes are nonsense and you can never escape that this house party is not much more than a means to get your player character laid, but having said that, this game is absolutely about the journey, not it's destination.Whether you want to admit it or not, Eek games managed to make a raunchy comedy adventure game that deserves the praise it gets. You don't have to like it, but if you know what's in store, can handle some full on nudity and sex scenes, I highly recommend attending this house party... Just be careful of the alcohol dood.\t\t\t\t\t
