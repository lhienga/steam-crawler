\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, I want to give this game a "meh" or "it's ok". Overall, the base story line is enjoyable and has enough variety and replayability for multiple playthroughs. The 18+ content is good and for the most part the game itself is fun. Unfortunately, the base game is still buggy in certain situations nearly a year after release which makes you have to replay a whole bunch of the story unless you are regularly saving (autosave doesn't happen often enough for this to be viable). It also feels like the beginning of the story is the exact same each time around and the different paths don't really begin until mid-late story.

DLCs: I'm not going to make a fuss about the 18+ content being free before and now sitting behind a paywall as I didn't play the game prior to the paywall. I think the price is reasonable but I understand people not liking having to pay for something that was previously free for them.

The Halloween and Winter packs are ok. After a couple playthroughs I got annoyed with having them on and haven't turned them back on since.

The Doja Cat expansion pack is amusing but shouldn't be priced at a full DLC price since there is no 18+ content like all the other characters.

The Liz Katz DLC is well done and definitely the best out of the available DLCs. This was the most fun story to play through in my opinion.
