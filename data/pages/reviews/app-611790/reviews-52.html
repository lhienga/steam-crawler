\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of my favorite games but probably not for the reason you would think. It feels less like an "adult" game and more like a comedic intractable point and click adventure. You move around a house and have a variety of story lines you can pursue, all while collecting and finding things around the house in order to complete objectives. Each character feels unique and fun to talk to, and enjoying their interactions with others as they wander the house is hilarious. You can make enemies just as easy as you can make friends and it's a game where screwing up can cause some really funny moments that will make you rethink loading a save spot and trying again. Also you can just randomly pee on the floor because why not.

The smut scenes are not really the focus of the game and for that they definitely feel a bit weird. The PC just kind of exists in a soulless scary way while the girls look into the camera with these glassed over dead eyes. They kind of play into the comedic tone of the game. I would not purchase this game if you're looking for a high level sexy experience.

Overall huge recommend!! I do not recommend paying full retail price or purchasing any of the DLC which is very expensive in contrast to the games price, but it's a fun experience for sure.
