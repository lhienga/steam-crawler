\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, the gameplay is a lot like a detective game where you walk around and find items to pick up and interact with. You use those items and selecting different dialogue options with NPC characters to progress the game, as well as sleep with almost any of the NPC players.

Even with the sexual content aside, the story line is hilarious. I love how items can be collected and combined. It has a lot of replayability to achieve certain outcomes. Playing as male or female characters have different actions and outcomes, so if you are an achievement collector, then *hint hint, wink wink*.

There is such a wide variety of ways to interact with the characters and items. You can combine items in your inventory, combine inventory items with other items in the world, and give certain items only to specific characters and/or at specific moments (after following the right quest line or sequence of quest lines). *hint: Some items can be grabbed and moved but not put in your inventory and they still produce changes in quest lines or items to give to characters.

It is a puzzle to keep the NPCs happy or to make certain of them friends. Sometimes one character will like you more for something and it will cause another to like you less or to completely dislike you. This makes it challenging to sleep with everyone in the game or collect all of the achievements.

As far as the sexual content, I think the skin texture on the girls hands is pretty hideous but the rest of the base game is nicely animated. There is a filter for anything past being topless for m and f characters on the base game, unless you purchase the explicit patch. This game definitely delivers more than other games in this category with its style of realistic graphics, if I have yet to consider the explicit patch. I think its impressive how much body type variation there is between the female characters and they all look decent, including when topless. I wish there more archetypes of male characters.

I bought the explicit patch... I haven't really played it much yet but I am excited about having some extra interactions after playing the game a bunch of times.\t\t\t\t\t
