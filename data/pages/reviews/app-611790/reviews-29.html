\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHouse Party is a good CYOA, comedy, sex game. The characters are realistic & they have depth, personalities & unique dialogue. Indeed, the characters sound authentic & their reactions are believable. Furthermore, all the female characters are attractive & sexy. Also, the writing is good, good enough that the game sometimes feels like a visual novel. There are a plethora of story paths to take.

As well, the game world is small, consisting of the party house & its backyard. Despite this, the game world is dense & detailed, with lots to do & interact with & a lot of activity. In fact, one of House Party’s best features is that the characters react to you & almost everything you do. Moreover, you can interact with almost everything in the game world. It makes for a fun, very immersive experience & makes each playthrough unique.

Plus, with so much content & so much activity going on, and with the ability to interact with almost everything, House Party has high replayability. It’s almost impossible to get bored with this game! For example, I have over 50 hours of playtime in the game. The Liz Katz DLC added several more hours of fun & I had a good time romancing the charismatic, sexy & hot Liz Katz!

Also, I mostly credit House Party for getting me into adult visual novels (AVNs). To be clear, House Party is a video game, not an AVN. But House Party has many AVN elements like a focus on story, lots of dialogue, choices, different paths, and sex scenes. I played House Party a lot & noticed how much I enjoyed it. This made me reconsider playing AVNs.

But unfortunately House Party has some major flaws. The game's biggest flaw is that the graphics are outdated or rather mediocre, especially the character models. This hurt immersion & made the NSFW content disappointing & mediocre, especially the sex scenes. Seeing a blocky female thigh clip into her body was a big sexual turn off. This was especially a problem with Liz Katz. And the low-quality animations only made matters worse. It's axiomatic that mediocre NSFW content in an adult game like House Party is especially problematic.

Another major flaw of the game is that it lacks a gallery (unlike most adult visual novels). Hence, to see your favorite sex scenes, you have to create a ton of saves & it's a pain in the ass. Worse, every time the game updates, your saves no longer work. So in that case, you have to start over & redo everything, including your plethora of saves, just to be able to experience again your favorite scenes. It blows & I didn't bother. Hence, the sex scenes were a one & done experience, which royally sucks, especially for an adult game.

Finally, the sexual content is optional. If desired, you can bypass all the sexual content & simply play House Party as a comedy game. In fact, you have to buy an inexpensive DLC & enable adult content just to see the sexual stuff. So don’t let the label “adult game” scare you off. In sum, I recommend House Party. However, if you're looking for good NSFW content & a good story, adult visual novels are a much better option than House Party.\t\t\t\t\t
