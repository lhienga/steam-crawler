\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYes:

Great voice acting
Good characters
Good story
Art is good, even though some of it looks a bit weird (to me)

No:
This game. Is so. ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. Frustrating. At times. You will do all of a series of steps to achieve a goal and because you didn't pick this one thing up at this one random corner of the setting at this one small point in the story, you get a different outcome. And a few of these instances are "well, ok, I can see how that works, and I missed that, whoops" But in other cases, it's pretty arbitrary, and I have no idea why my character would go there, do this, at that time.

This is my only real complaint.

So, my suggestion: when playing, don't play to be a completionist (unless you are really into that). Because you will have to replay plenty of times before you can access the content. And these replays will, in some cases, be enjoyable because they feel natural. At other times, you just get screwed by a chaotic narrator who's really mad that you didn't go into that room and lock the door at a specific time because DUH.\t\t\t\t\t
