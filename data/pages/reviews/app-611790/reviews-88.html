My Review For House Party

The Positives
  • Choices actually matter, if you choose one you may fail another which makes you want to replay the game.
  • I actually thought it was pretty thoughtful the way the Devs put in the AI to talk about already feeling like they been through the same spot where they are already. (meaning replaying the game)
  • You can download custom stories made by the community.

The Negatives
  • I have come across bugs of course, one where I had to make a new game because the AI stopped interacting with me.
  • Frank can knock you out and if you are new to the game you will have to start over as I did if you haven't saved so beware of that.
  • It seems that the Alcohol can run out at-least from what I was playing, I know it is normal for a House Party but maybe have someone sneak out for a beer run?


    Overall

    I have been to alott of house parties, the one thing there has never been is a person guarding the liquor cabinet. Normally someone like Frank would be sitting near the beer pong table talking about how many girls he has had sex with during the week, when in reality that isn't true. Anyway though, the game is pretty well wrote no Bruh-ploms from me Bruh 7/10
