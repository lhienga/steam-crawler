\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this thinking it would be a fun college movie comedy type game, it isn't.
It's a classic point and click adventure in a 3D artstyle.
In just under 4 hours of play I think I let out 1 chuckle. the majority of my time was spent trying to discern how to get alcohol flowing in this "party".
There's guy, who seemingly nobody invited, who doesn't live there but is preventing people from drinking like they're kids in highschool.

Graphically the game looked quite nice but was riddled with screen tearing and character's both clipping and bumping into one another. Aside from that I found the music to be very annoying, having 1 track on loop for the entire time, even one of the characters comments on how bad the track is and how the girl throwing it can't seriously only have 1 song. So the creators decided to purposefully put this "joke" in there rather than just pay for some music - or even use some free use tracks.

On the subject of character's talking about things, I heard the same npc's say the same things over and over again, only for them not to come into play when addressing them. One continuously asks if anyone would help her play a prank on her sister. When you speak to her it's not in her dialogue. Strangely however I did get an option from her sister to play a prank on her.
Oh, and as for the characters, of the 12 or so that I met, I think 3-4 were ok and the rest were outright annoying.

Overall I found this game very boring, unfunny and monotonous. 3/10. If you like point and click adventures then you might get something from this.\t\t\t\t\t
