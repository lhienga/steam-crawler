\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHouse Party is...marginally OK. The explicit content is what drew me in and it is fun to get the girls naked and have a good time, though it can get kind of old. Unfortunately the overall gameplay and quest design is very weak. Playing through the main story I had to look up walkthroughs numerous times because it was too easy to get confused and lost by what to do next. There are also many story choices that will cut you off from progressing the intimacy with a character but when faced with these forks in the road it is rarely obvious that this would be the logical or reasonable outcome or that it's even a fork. Whoever did the writing doesn't seem to really know how to write and develop a fictional person beyond an extremely superficial level. Because of this you will likely find yourself failing multiple times along the way and restarting or reloading a save. I don't know about you but that is not very fun and just makes the whole experience tedious.

And then they hyped things up with the Doja Cat expansion, yet we come to find out anything sexual is very limited and completely censored. Really Eek! Games? All of the advertising for her inclusion is highly sexualized. Only an extremely dishonest person or someone unaware of what the base game contains, would see those advertisements and not expect Doja Cat nudity and so forth. They could have put a disclaimer on the games store page telling us there would be no Doja Cat nudity or even their website. Yet there is nothing on their website, Steam or Twitter account to alert the buyer to this. The most you can find are third party hearsay references from writers for various gaming website articles saying that the Doja Cat expansion wont have nudity but without direct links or quotes from Eek! Games.****Eek! Games did actually put a disclaimer on their store page. That is a good step by them, though I would like to know when it was added. I definitely don't remember seeing it when the page went up and I checked back from time to time looking for a note regarding if there would be new adult content.****



****Edited review after another commentator pointed out that there is a no new adult content disclaimer on the Doja Cat expansion page.\t\t\t\t\t
