Colors are bright and pastel and every angle rounded as is the style these days.

It is close enough to the original to interest you in purchasing it.
A long and slow tutorial to waste your refund grace period.
And will disappoint you enough to quit it and forget it before finishing the tutorial.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Evil genius was Austin powers, this is Minions."

I don't know where I heard that, but its the best summation of this game i've heard.

Especially in the 'something to say' department. This isnt a love letter to spy movies. This is a game made to be like another game, its meta-meta, and its bad at its job.

It fixed the, frankly, awful world map mechanics of the first game in an adequate way, and fumbled everything else. Almost all the individual mechanics from the first one are back in theory, but are implemented in inferior ways. (Tagging enemies requires clicking on each agent, individually, and for each then entering a sub menu where you can click to assign a tag, for instance. Building a room and placing its items are different tabs for some reason. Rotating while building assigns those keys to other actions when you need to move the camera most. Just clunky, constantly)

Technically, its ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. Its been out for YEARS now, and im still getting crashes, not bugs, GAME CRASHES doing trivial things. Want to move a room? Simple, select your stuff to be moved, and where you want it now. Nice. Now mark the room for deletion, once everything is moved, they will destroy the roo.... NOPE CRASH.

Like, WHAT. The building is the WHOLE fundamental mechanic of the GAME.

And yes, if they messed that up, you best believe random little things will crash you also. This game would actually be unplayable without the autosave feature. Thats absolutely wild.

Its not clever in the slightest, and treats you like a dribbling idiot. Lets make funny faces, now click the door button, now click the bed button, now click the.. yeah. No you arent expected to learn what 'place beds' means. Click the bedroom button, now click the bed button, now place the bed right here on the flashing spot.

The art style is great, for what it is, but is obviously a departure from the OG spy movie parody, but I mean its fine if you enjoy it.

Sound design and everything else seems very well done, and while the direction/writing they were given is absolutely childish, the voice actors give a very competent performance of the outright embarrassing lines, and you can tell the equipment they had is excellent.

As a long time lover of the first one, and really trying to get into this one, I cannot recommend this game. I've refunded it despite giving it a go both on console, and now on sale 75% off. Its still not worth it, sadly.

There are too many games out there right now to bother fighting this one, and if you want this experience, Evil Genius 1 is still for sale here, dirt cheap, and despite the awkward world map, is even so the far superior experience.\t\t\t\t\t
