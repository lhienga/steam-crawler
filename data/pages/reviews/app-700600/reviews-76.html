\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEvil Genius 2: World Domination if you ever wanted to be a super villain now here's your chance the game is a huge step up from the first with new traps rooms, minions and more evil genius to take over the world! The game is one of the best base building games I've ever played! That saying here are my pros and cons for Evil Genius 2: World Domination!

Pros

+ New Evil Genius
Each evil genius has it's own story line and ability's as well as it's own custom room and dooms day device and ending it's interesting to see where each one will take you and how you can take over the word!

+ Super Agents
Super agents keep you on your tows if you try and rob a region with them in it you will get a visit by them each one is unique like Symmetry who will spawn in your vault and try and steal your goal or Wrecking Bola who will destroy your doors, traps and minions each one can be killed in there own story missions.

+ Visuals
It's cartoon style surprising really fits the game to the minions to the evil genius to the super agents it's just all very nice to look at.

+ Loot
Loot are a new unique item which can give your minions a buff in certain ways like being able to see though better disguises. Loot can also be a trap like Flame of Prometheus shoots fireballs at enemies in range. Or could be the Doors of Fort Knox each one is useful to have in there own right.

Cons

- DLC/Content Drops
As much as I enjoy the game the DLC just seems to be a bit lacking for what there asking the Henchmen don't feel just right or needed a bit more time. The Lair Items Pack feels like it should have been a free content drop or should have been in the game in the first place. I don't know what to say about the Minion Packs but I feel there kind of the only good DLC currently at the time of writing this the Campaign Pack has not came out yet but I'm really hype to see where it takes up and if there will be anymore new content after that.

Evil Genius 2: World Domination is a amazing game that brings you as a evil genius to take over the world it's a great strategy and build game with it's great story and visuals that will keep you playing for hours on end. It's a must play!\t\t\t\t\t
