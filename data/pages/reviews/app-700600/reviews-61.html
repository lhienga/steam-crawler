\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNowhere near as good as the first one, it's basically hot garbage with idiotic mechanics and as others have mentioned, a "mobile game" feel.

An example of the stupid mechanics would be something like unused equipment suffering durability loss to the point of breakage even when it's not being used. Take interrogation chairs for example. They just sit there steadily breaking even without anyone being interrogated. Maintenance makes sense for certain things like generators or whatever, but last time I looked chairs don't just break without a fat ass sitting in them.

One of the best parts of the original was creating elaborate traps for pesky agents. Well, forget about doing that this time around, the traps are all worthless. Even basic investigators (even the literal FIRST group of level 1 guys that show up at the start) are basically immune to them. They show up in groups of 3, the first guy triggers a trap, it misses the rest of them completely. The first guy gets pushed along into whatever fiendish combination you think up and....nothing. Pushed him into knockout gas, he apparently didn't inhale. Pushed him into a shark tank, he bitchslapped them and got out with minor health damage. Pushed him into some flamethrowers, apparently he's wearing asbestos. Pushed him into a LASER GRID and he got slightly tanned. And these are the first, most basic, weak guys.
The other 2 investigators? Well they're just fine because traps take like 2 minutes to reset. Seriously. They just continued on. You're better off swarming enemies with worker minions, however many you lose will freely respawn before your giant fan gets charged back up anyway.

Base building is a bit different this time with more places and more floors, but that's it. You'll dedicate most of your rooms to stat recovery or power generation. You're basically designing an evil resort / spa for minions rather than a secret base to take over the world. My minions were ALWAYS hungry so my cafeteria was always full and HUGE. I had 4 of them placed all over the base. Same with barracks, it's constantly nappy time. For whatever reason they practically never used the rec rooms to recover their smarts, they preferred going for naps.

There's more but I can't be bothered writing it or even formatting this thing to look better. It just sucks.\t\t\t\t\t
