\r\n\t\t\t\t\t28/12/2022 Review Update 3 - Recommended? Yes,
Alright, much like many released too early titles, this one seems to have come around at last... EG2 was released too early, likely at publisher demand, clearly wasn't ready for release, and the rushed updates to fix things caused more issues... so, all in all a bad launch experience. A year or so on, its playable.
I HATE that this is the norm for games now... its why I will not pre-order again.

I have now progressed notably further into the story with no discernible bugs at all...
SO, I am once again restoring my "Yes" recommendation,

Well done Rebellion, for getting there at last...

20/02/2022 Review Update 2
Nine days on since my last review update and I thought my efforts were worth an update.
It's a good update (I know right!), looks like moving the install to my C:\\ resolved almost all issues...
However, I do not run games on my C drive, personal choice. So an alternative was needed... So I created a "Junction" (google how to make a junction in Windows) to so the game thinks its on my C drive, but it's actually on my Games M2 instead.
In this configuration the game is currently playable and I have been able to enjoy multiple hours of game play... I do have some intermittent graphical issues, but as I have recently updated my drivers, this could be related.
I'm taking it slow, but still, I've been having fun, I believe as of right now I'm up to over 180 hours now and only one crash that was likely related to another application.

I will switch this to a "yes I recommend" IF, and I stress, IF I can actually complete the campaign as Polar and one of the original Genius'.

Whoever from EG2 support suggested the move to the C drive of the game data, thanks. I have modified your solution somewhat to make it work for me.

11/02/2022 Review Update - Recommended? No
171.8 Hours, (so about 2 hours on top of last review time)
New update, new content, nice new ideas, new issues, same broken game.
A clean Installation, a clean start, all DLC, the new genius, on the new island, on medium. Lets do this.
It looks nice and I really like the thermal regulation element, given its a frozen island I would hope for it to be cold...

Can I select guards? Yes! My god there's hope!
Can I actually get through this game now? this could be exciting!
Will I have to change my review recommendation?

No.

100% repeatable game crash, when trying to start the mission "Cover Operation" Click the start side mission button, game crash. Reload, wait a few minutes, do some base tweaking, wait for another auto save, select the side mission, hit start button... game crash, progression entirely destroyed.

Clearly still no QA testing has been undertaken, I would ask for a refund from Steam at this point as this product is simply not fit for purpose, but doubt I would ever get anywhere with that as I have 171 hours play time logged and 95% of that has been playing the same first 1-30% of the game. I have never seen the doomsday weapon complete or beyond the first stage that is how broken this title is.

I'm not even running weirdly specific hardware - Ryzen 9 3900X, GTX 1660 Ti, 32 GB Ram, Installed on a Samsung M.2 SSD, all running on Windows 10.

So, after 171 hours, thirty or forty restarts, and almost a year after release I still cannot complete the game. Save yourself the pain and suffering, the damage to memories of a simply epic game. Do not buy this game. Do not waste your time.

Nostalgia destroyed. Faith in Rebellion as a studio lost.

Original review exact date unknown - six plus months ago.
Well... after 169 hours, I've called it on EG2...

Unfortunately, I no longer recommend this title and wanted to share my thoughts on why.

EG2 was released with so many progression bugs, it may have been released before it was actually finished, another instance of a developer releasing a game before its done (why is this a trend now?), I found it was impossible to finish no matter what Genius or Island I selected.

Every major version (1.1, 1.2, 1.3, etc) I have been required to start a new game, (the afore mentioned progression bugs) this has left me so bored with the new game experience, I haven't even been able to complete a doomsday weapon... ever.

1.7 broke it entirely, yet another new game, but this time if the guard arrives on any island, with any genius, in any order of recruitment, game crash. I go to an older save and hope the progression issues on those saves are fixed? nope. select a guard, train a guard or send a guard minion on a mission... game crash.

I have a save where there are four immortal, super agents in the base, who don’t leave. Ever. They just shred the base and end up killing all minions and the Genius eventually…
I have another where the story is stuck at “dig down to the bottom layer of the doomsday cavern” but it’s so early in the game, I only have the option to dig down one layer, not two additional… and from there is no way to progress… another dead save.

I've reported the issues in every way I can think of and I’m stuck with 8 saves, none of them are playable and now cannot start new ones and I’m told to wait for the next patch, it fixes the issues.

I’ve done the normal verify game files, try with and without the seemingly endless DLC… its just entirely unplayable now.

On the DLC front, the original had no DLC and it was great… Given how quickly DLC has been created and added to EG2, it really looks like its going the direction we built content for the main game, but lets partition it off into DLC and make a quick extra buck from the fans… or a half released game progressively fixed by DLC so you end up paying twice (or more) for a finished product…

I had high hopes for EG2, the nostalgia the comedic elements, just the feel of the game… all of that is gone now, I just have a bitter taste and a cold shiver when I think “Lets play EG2” so it's been uninstalled.

If you really want to grab EG2, wait till a complete edition comes around… In my opinion it's not worth it right now and at that point you’ll get all the DLC (or most of it) and hopefully a finished playable game.

I’m going to give this a go again in a year. (it may end up being a District 9 ‘three years’ event though).\t\t\t\t\t
