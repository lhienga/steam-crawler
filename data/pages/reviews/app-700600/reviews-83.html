\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is ok. I bought it when it released expecting more I guess. It gets sort of repetitive when you constantly get the foes constantly trying to stop you by sending their detectives. But as long as you keep a few steps ahead of them by capturing them in their cells you can keep interrogating them for points to use elsewhere. Keep up with the tourists traps to keep your lair running. Research new weapons for your minions, unlock your side kicks and keep it up to dominate the world. There were bugs that I noticed when I played which made the game frustrating at times, but the developers have been patching things up so hopefully they've taken care of most of the bugs I encountered, such as, delayed boss/sidekick/minion responses to intruders tot he lair, delayed world objectives showing up, and a few others. I recommend this game For the price of $19.99, it may be worth a look. This game definitely wasn't worth the $59.99 that I paid when it first released. Hope this review helps!\t\t\t\t\t
