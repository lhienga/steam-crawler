\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's not the Evil Genius you grew up with, but it's close. The mechanics are very similar and they've even found a way to make the research grind take even longer! They have improved the need/use of the Casino, it's now a more focal point for distraction/defense than the original ever was, so much improved there.

What should be a nice casual 8 hour game is dragged into a slothing multiple day event likely for the sake of making you feel like you're not blowing through the game to the finish line and not getting your moneys worth. This thanks to only being able to do singular tasks at a time despite having a base full of minions capable of completing multiple projects/tasks at once just because you have the mass of employees. There's replayability here, but why when it takes so friggin long to play!

Also introduced is a new "pay for this new character" system where they just keep asking for more money to give your boss a new skin and a few tweaks to their skills and room types. Again, it takes so long to play, why keep paying more money for a slight variation of the same game? I guess if you just REALLY like the game that much, this is a nice way of keeping the feeling fresh for you.

For me, this title is okay if on sale. If you haven't purchased it yet, go for the original as it's just as fun without all the added time and costs to play.\t\t\t\t\t
