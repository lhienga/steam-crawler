\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShocked at all the negativity surrounding this game. I remember playing the original game back in the day, and for all of it's charm it was still janky, broken, unbalanced, unfair, and a lot of the time simply not fun. Anyone who tries to tell you that the sequel is worse than the original has some serious nostalgia goggles on and needs to try actually playing the original again.

This is just the original game but far more polished, with much better graphics, and a much more satisfying gameplay experience that lets you actually enjoy creating your own evil lair and dominating the world. This is what the original evil genius wanted to be, and would've been if it had been released now and with a higher budget. This is fulfilling the original vision of a game limited by the technology of 2004. It is far from inferior, and the only way anyone new to the franchise should experience Evil Genius.

I sincerely thank Rebellion for not letting Evil Genius fade into obscurity when it had so much untapped potential left to explore.\t\t\t\t\t
