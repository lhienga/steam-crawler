\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of those insidious games that is designed to have a tutorial that is long and tedious, long enough for you to pass the steam refund timer. So be wary of that.

And because of the clear and obvious greedy behavior, and some obvious design decisions i refuse to recommend this title. Along with some other pointers ill point out below.

All of the next pointers comes from the perspective of someone who's a system designer, so take from that what you will.
- Gameplay design; a lot of things is obscure, and even doing the tutorial the best you possibly can, can EASILY lead you down bad roads in terms of what you place and where and how large, and half the time youll just struggle with "oh look this generator gives barely any power" and be frustrated with the size vs power output, and things of that nature in general, the dumb sizing/room requirements and HAVING to place things like beds against a wall, for no reason and a genuinely just a boring gameplay loop, at least from what ive experienced in the last couple of hours.

-UI Design; This is a bit of a mess, too many notifications that dont explain themselves, and just arbitrary clicking around, controlling your genius and various minions is quite obnoxious, whilst it looks nice, theres just much to be desired, like understanding -why- your research suddenly stops (if anyones confused by that, its because your scientists need to be actively working in their stations), whilst you'll probably figure it out, the fact it isnt readily apparent it very much just bad design.

- SFX/VFX/Writing design; Very stylistic and nice, the voicing is well done and the humor is quality - the music hits the notes of what this game likes to hit on, and it doesnt seem too repetitive, though i do think outside of the tutorial you'll end up being disappointed with the lack of actual interactions between your genius and your various henchmen. This i dont know too much about yet but i can easily imagine it becoming just a soulless grind after a while.

- Graphical evaluation; Again ill give heavy style points, its quality models, art direction is spot on, and gives great vibes. Theres some cool generative tech under the hood that is underappreciated here, like the generation of randomized wall segments and having them intersect, genuinely i just find it to be pleasing to look at, the only negative id say is some of the henchment designs, look too samey- its far too easy to get 2 people looking exactly the same kind of ruining the "i like this one in particular" vibe. But other than that, great.

Overall/TLDR; Its a decent game, but theres too many negatives to recommend it, seems greedy and abusive in terms of time/design/dlc's and part of what ive seen it seems like it could become repetitive. I recommend getting this on sale if you really feel into this particular vibe - if i was to give a % good vs bad id say 45% good, 55% bad, so take that however you will! I wouldnt refund it even going past the 2 hour mark - but its quite a mid game in general. Eh/10 from me\t\t\t\t\t
