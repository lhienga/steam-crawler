Minor gripes I have are some design decisions such as at a high level of heat it is difficult to get down because enemy agents will either attack on sight, resist all dissuasion attempts, or come so close together they immeadiatly notice the your hallways still full of bodies from the last incursion...which also causes your minions to lose morale... If you want a casual base management game, consider giving this a try.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy Overall Rating is 3 out of 5 stars!

Likes
1. This is great Genre, Along with other Dungeon Builder type games. Where you get to build traps etc, to fight the good guys.
2. The Content for the most part is quality with a few misses here and there.

Dislikes
1. Not enough Content, From Evil Genius's, to Henchmen, to Minions the game feels extremely limited. Evil Genius's are limited to 5 atm which i felt was an attempt for the game designers to stay true to the games origins rather than improve upon them, constantly I felt the Henchmen would be better Evil Genius's than what you get.

Likewise with the Henchmen themselves you have a massive conference table (This is the only object they interact with) for your Henchmen to sit at and chat with their boss, but out of 8 seats you only get 5 Henchmen this should be an oversight of some sort.

Minions are limited to a hard cap of 300, so if you reach that cap and then do research to upgrade it nothing happens, minions work in shifts so you need more for each job than it seems at first, even with 300 you will not expand throughout the whole available base because there is too much space available.

Combined together the game feels out sync with the message it sends, as a powerful Evil Genius why are there so many restrictions which are never explained. Why do you live on an island where you have to run cover operations to hide yourself when some tiny decrepit mountain village would work just as well without the weird cover operations which stretch your already thin manpower pool.

2. The game runs in slow motion unless you speed it up, so Slow Motion or Fast Forward the only two speed options.
