\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is fun if you want to play as a 80s style Spy Movie Villian and have a legion of minions do your bidding.

The Henchmen are full of character and are quite colourful and their is many different ways to play the game and you have a choice of Villians to choose from all 5 very unique from each other in personalities, playstyle and abilities.

Now the game does have some cons.
It lacks hotkeys and there is a lot of unnecessary micro for example the heat/cold mechanic is quite annoying and can burn you out and you need to individual click on objects and check auto-rebuild instead of being able to drag select multiple or do whole rooms.

You also can't stop access to minions wondering around certain places such as your guards just going for a stroll down a trap corridor preventing your traps going off if a agent is present.

The world operations can also be annoying as they build so much heat you rarely get to run any other schemes than reduce heat.

Late game can also be annoying as you can dedicate an entire floor to just your power generators and you will still need more if you want to build a large base which can be annoying as they keep breaking down and you have none stop notifications.

Sometimes this game can feel like a phone game.

I recommend you buy it if you enjoy base building and being a 80s spy villian otherwise I recommend you look towards another dungeon game.\t\t\t\t\t
