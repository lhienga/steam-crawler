\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPeople reviewing this game will cite 'mobile game mechanics' a lot. Well, having played both this and original, I feel compelled to say... This game has less 'waiting for sake of waiting' mechanics, unless that is just how you choose to play it. This game is upgrade over the original in most of ways (except, debatably, business model, which shapes up to be DLC spam). Developers are obviously receptive to feedback and adjusted mechanics for better gameplay a lot, and while I understand people's frustration with minimal-addition DLCs, those will not stop until all DLCs promised from season pass are released, and they WERE all promised ahead of time, and that should not be a surprise. What I am curious is to see where developers will take the game after the final DLC (new Genius/campaign) is released. I hope they won't just abandon it because game, despite business model, is really good and has room for both improvement and general broadening.

Despite all this, I do think this game is still great, and while this may be a skewed opinion due to original game's janky and half-broken state... It's 100% more playable and enjoyable. And I cannot for life of me people claiming that they brought mobile game mechanics into this game, while the original had way longer waiting times, insanely slow gameplay and simultaneously less actual game interactivity and more pointlessly tedious micromanagement. As much as I like first game, this one IS better and for more reasons than just graphics and technical improvements.

I do recommend this game, for at least a few campaigns on different difficulties. Its fun to both just chill out and build base of dreams; and to struggle against extremely strong agents as you try to use everything at your disposal against them. Game's just fun, and more fun than original in every way.\t\t\t\t\t

If you pick up this game, avoid any of the ANVIL territories and you will still enjoy the game. Its good; and feels like a proper scaling challenge. But the moment she appears, the rules of the game change for the worse.\t\t\t\t\t
