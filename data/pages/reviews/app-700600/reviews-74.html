\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game that looks great, and has some actually charming dialogue and flavor.

But everything else falls apart.

There is no 'risk' of falling behind or losing, you can never lose. But not in a casual way, in an almost mobile game try to keep them addicted way. Advancing your base is just "getting more money", you get more money to buy more stuff to get more money to buy more stuff, but you aren't actually "building" anything, you're just adding more of the same things. But also if you don't do this, nothing will happen.

A person having the game idle and a person micromanaging their heart out will have no difference in threats or chance of success, just how long it takes to get there... which is the other problem. The game's progress is timelocked. You know how mobile games will make you "wait 5 minutes/1 hour for this *whatever* to finish", that is what this does. That is this entire game.

Fans of strategy games/management sims will feel bored and will not be able to get more than 10-20 hours out of this game (which isn't enough to finish one narrative) once the novelty of the game wears off and gameplay has to drive enjoyment.\t\t\t\t\t
