\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe original title has always been - by far - one of my favorite games, despite its imperfections. I was really hoping for EG2 to live up to it - and to me it has. And then some!

PROS:
-The vibe and flavor are spot on
-The amount of visual and strategic detail is impressive
-The soundtrack is fantastic
-The tutorial is stellar
-EG2 really feels like the original EG's offspring, although it also improves it in many ways

CONS:
-The UI could use a little work, especially on the World Domination Screen
-Camera controls could be more flexible
-Having to buy numerous small DLC feels a bit gimmicky - I went with the season pass but I don't like to support that business model. I loooooooooove most Rebellion games but I wish they would change their selling strategies.

This franchise is really unique and, in my humble (and possibly nostagia-biased) opinion, EG2 is a must-have for base-building and strategy lovers.\t\t\t\t\t
