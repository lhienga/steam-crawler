\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI do recommend this game but only at a 80%+ discount.
if you loved the first game you will like this sequel, the nostalgia makes it worth it at a discount price. do note i got this games for 3,5 usd. for 5 bucks you will get your money out of it until you get frustrated after 10 hours. if you get it. please put up another review with your findings so devs might fix it. because it has great potential.

That said...

The game has a lot of unrefined but not that rough edges that are easily fixable. for example there is a minion manager where you set per minion individual tasks. this sounds great but at 300 ( i dont know if they raised the cap atm) minions this is not worth your time and highly innefficient.

let me just sum it up what i feel the game misses and if it would get these features it would be a great game to play:
- this is a biggest one... lack of selection tools. you have to pick each item specifically. no drag and select. no select type, no select all in a room function. to top it of there are missions where you need to turn down the power. go ahead when you have 100 generators. go do 3 clicks per generator. also this is really innefficent when rearranging stuff. you need to move each piece by hand or sell it and wait for it to be replaced. before you can rearrange other pieces due to overlapping orders
- the second but also very big. items that block each other leave invalid spaces. yeah this make sense. but if you 2 walls plumb to each other and put in the corners 2 items. the sqaure in the corner becomes inaccessible and therefore you cant order the construction/placing of said items. even tho it is a perfect spot for decor. you will see what i mean when you play.
- cant cancel certain orders; cancel interrogations is not possible.
- pathfinding issues. alot of minions wont go the facility they need to regen. they tend to wander through your base until they deplete stats or find something to do but somehow. but this feels to random.
- no actual blueprinting possibility. you cant use a pattern and reprint it. no.. you got to do it all again by hand.
- power balance... sigh. just assign a whole floor to nuclear power generators to power your base. some items are not balanced at all.
- poor item descriptions... spend time researching items that you already own but with better cushions and more space take up at 4 times the price of the smaller version... for real devs need to list what an item does.
- traps are fun but pointless. just put an checkpoint infront of the entrance and no one will get past it. sure you can do without but.. eh.
- poor and vague minion traits. i want to know what it wants and where it is going. i dont need to know it is a pet lover... make it make sense...
- lack of challenge yeah there are 3 choices here. i played on regular, i feel that harder just increases the waves. please add actual challenge.
- global operations... alot of the time you are at max gold cap and dont need to do global ops. but somehow you still generate heat i guess by intel generation but that is also unbalanced. yes there are upgrades to decrease heat but i feel intel generation should do like 10x less than what it currently does. the actual cash ops are fine tho but you dont need to do those often cuz cap.
- optional objectives are just pointless. these are just procedureally generated and you do these passively.
- lack of alot of hotkeys.
this is a pretty long list but there are other problems with this game i probably forgot or missed.
- better statistics...

still this game is fun for a couple of hours. but it gets boring and frustating after a 10 hours orso. it doesnt motivate you to keep going. unless you like the challenge of working with an unpolished hidden gem. this game should get more attention if the devs optimize all my points. and perhaps add some more arcade modes.

at the time of writing this game is at said discount and i really encourage you to try it. just dont expect more than it is.\t\t\t\t\t
