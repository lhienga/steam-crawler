\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis one is a tricky piece of work.

The launch of this game was rough, with a lot of bugs, design oversights and general 'incompetence' on display, while small piecemeal DLC were being pumped out in waves. This did not go over well with the playerbase, myself included, but it didn't manage to sully what the game really was at its core: a solid and very stylistically pleasing lair builder.

Now, some months later, the game is in a good spot. The developers have made a solid effort in patching and reworking many of the core gameplay systems, and while they still pump out DLC at an alarming pace but small package size, it doesn't give a complete 'nickle-and-dime' kind of vibe.

In confession, the game has many faults, but I love playing it and I don't regret my purchase. There are core elements that I would like to see changed or reworked, but ultimately it's a good time, and I can recommend it to anyone who's a fan of the original, or similar management-lite titles.\t\t\t\t\t
