\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile the game is fun it is plagued by a few game stopping glitches. I've been through several campaigns and in each there's a couple of separate missions where you need to meet with representatives in the villain's inner sanctum. Without fail, the game would glitch and not progress that quest despite the villain sitting in the chair for the quest progress. The representatives just walk around the room like nobody's in there and the quest won't progress. I encountered this about three times with two in the early game and one in the late game at a different quest. While the game is a lot of fun I can't recommend it until this bug is patched out due to how consistent it shows up for me.\t\t\t\t\t
