\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is a great refresher of the classical 1999. release and the later HD release which it trump in asthetics and features.

The good:
The population increase allows incredible huge battles even in 1v1 games. Turbo features which i have not use much as i prefer the classical gameplay can make for intense fast battles and more engaging stalemate periods. The new ai play smarter somewhat and multible ai options make it good for multible play styles. All of the new nations i have played so far are astonishingly balanced and fit right in.

The bad:
Elephants everywhere if you play full-tech like i personally do. Lag but that is to be expected though out of battle the game runs extremely well the only mayor issue for me being corpses instead of living units idk why. Games are a lot shorter which is not a true negative but i do miss old school games that went up to 30 hours and spanned weeks of play. Cloud storage for game saves might get frustrating but i do not fully understand if its a backup or a main way of saving. Map sizes feel a lot smaller or it might just be faster units. The standard ai is overly agressive and the surplus of relics fules the endless high tier unit spam. Defensive ai(CD) dose not wall up as good as it used to in the 1999. version which i do sorely miss.

I do love this game both me and my dad which are long time original players are well supprised and we both love our time ingame so far. I am yet to finish a campaign or play multiplayer but neither of us ever did do either.
