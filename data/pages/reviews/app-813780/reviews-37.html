\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile this is a fantastic remaster of one of my favorite games of all time I have a small issue with it.

It's called the "Definitive Edition" of the game, but it's technically not "Definitive" in that you will have to buy expansions, DLC, and cosmetics for it to be "complete" or "definitive". Game companies like to play loose and fast with words when they release new versions (or sometimes even new games with different versions) so this shouldn't come as a surprise.

You will have to consider that you might be paying more than the cost of a full game if you want everything that is now added to the remastered game. This was a slight annoyance but something I knew they would do when I saw the first announcements about the remastered "Definitive Edition". This makes the price of the game a bit misleading.

Still an amazing game with brand new features, and I'm happy to see more content being added to a 20 year old game, I just wish things could be a little clearer. Maybe they could have just called it the "remastered edition" and saved everyone from a bit of confusion.\t\t\t\t\t
