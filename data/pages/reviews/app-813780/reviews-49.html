Well done Microsoft, well done. People can hate on Microsoft all they want, but whoever is handling AOE2 knows exactly what they are doing, and more importantly, they respect AOE2 for the venerable, legendary game that it is, and recognizes the potential it has for being a little bit of an e-sport of it's own.

May AOE2 last forever.\t\t\t\t\t
