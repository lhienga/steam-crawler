It has layers of depth, and I can play the game five times, and it will never be precisely the same.
It requires strategy, speed, and a need to take a few chances (saves are needful)

Negatives: The interface can be a bit "clunky" at times. It is a game designed around mouse clicks.
There are times that the computer players seem to start with an unfair advantage, either in
resources or in personnel.

Conclusion: This is an old game that has stood the test of time. The graphics are decent, and the gameplay challenging enough to keep me coming back for more (once or twice every decade). Good job, Microsoft!\t\t\t\t\t
