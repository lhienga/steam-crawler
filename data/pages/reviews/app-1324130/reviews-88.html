Compared to Rimworld it is an obvious update on graphics , but a downgrade on gameplay depth.
Specifically comparing it with Rimworld (which you cannot avoid doing) , you will miss a lot of things , factions , caravans , attacking other settlements , events and quests , character customization ,families etc .

What this game offers :
You handle around 8 survivors on an relatively big map growing and building your base while alien swarms attack your settlement from time to time. For 35 euros , what this game offers is worth it.

I do hope in a future dlc they work on adding depth (hoping for other factions and human enemies), because modders can only add so much with gameplay options availiable atm.
