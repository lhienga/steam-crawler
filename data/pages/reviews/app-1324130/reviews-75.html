\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat survival simulator in a fun ~realistic sci-fi setting.

Games this reminds me of:
Stardew Valley-likes: for base building and farming
Various tower defense games: there are attacks you have to fend off
Subnautica: survival sim with a research tree and lots of crafting (but not first-person and not as story-driven)
The Sims: isometric view where you control a small group of people, set routines, and need to keep them happy and healthy to stay productive
(I haven't played Rimworld.)

This one has that "can't stop, just onnne more potato chip" quality that a lot of amazing games have - if you get into it you REALLY get into it. Enjoy!
