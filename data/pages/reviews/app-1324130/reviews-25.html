and whenever you seek help in the steam discussion, a whole bunch of ugly trolls jump on and start gas lighting and narcing.

======================
At the start I asked about a wiki and the trolls said I was dumb. Turns out, the game does not have the depth to warrant a wiki.

Once the format is learned is it a simple mater of slogging on through the achievements.

Where my PC was crashing every 10 minutes, i thought it was a bug of the game but it wasn't. There was an issue with the ducting on the pc's heat exchanger.

This one was ok. Needs more depth and game play hours to have a thumbs up.
