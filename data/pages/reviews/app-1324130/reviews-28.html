\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was at the top of my wishlist forever. Finally pulled the trigger and, my god, I wish I had left it there.

This game is boring. it is beyond boring if there is a word for that. Every level of this game is just a slow chore. And I play Anno in normal speed. I like slow builders. This one goes no where and take forever to get there.

Even the RTS combat lacks all forms of excitement. The NPCs just stand in place, shooting/swinging on their turn like its 1998.

And why is this a space game? Last map I played looked like Utah. No space suits. No zero G. Nothing Space unless you count the skin swapped, very earth like, creatures. I don't.

There is no game design beyond what has been stolen from other, more competent, developers. Bugs will attack your base like clockwork, their numbers inching up each time. Each area is dominated by a resource while lacking the others. This means your building will all be the same. You start with 4 people that burden the load and as your camp grows, well you better hope they don't die cause you may very well never see another person. Wooohoo....fun? A city builder that just makes you spread your labor paper thin. No, fun it is not.

TL:DR It's just boring. I played on 3X speed the whole time and that wasn't enough for an exciting pace. As I progressed I never felt accomplished, nor the desire to continue.
