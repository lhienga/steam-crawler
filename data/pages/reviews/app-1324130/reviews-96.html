It a nice game to play for a while, but AI sucks. Its flaws start to show realy fast. No priority sistem, to make something happen first. Low count of colonists basicaly means that you spend all time micromanaging them, changing priorities depending on season or resources that you need. Ranching sucks, cause animals dont know they can eat stuff gworing on the ground (grass, etc), like in rimworld, you have to feed them or they starve. That means you have to dedicate resources to constantly produce food items form them. And you already have only few people to manage.
Its quite easy to make enough food, to not to starve, but all that micromanaging just make the game tedious and not fun. The longer you play, the more tired you get from it.
