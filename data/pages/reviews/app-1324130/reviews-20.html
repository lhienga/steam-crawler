\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is incredible, and will probably remain one of my favorites for a long time. I'm finishing up my first playthrough and am already looking forward to several more, trying the different scenarios and upping the difficulty to see if I can manage it.

I'm finishing the survival scenario now, and have really been enjoying the fantastic graphics and animation. You really feel like these are real people you are caring for. My base is a mess, but that is because these survivors have never had to build a base to survive for years in (neither have I, but it is very easy to role-play the survivors a bit). As I near the end it is very satisfying to see how it evolved from a fire pit and a scrap shelter next to the crashed ship, to a fortified, comfortable base stocked full of food and building supplies, with robot helpers and a small army of robot soldiers to help defend it, along with the turrets.

I want to mention the graphics again - everything looks great, with day/night and seasonal cycles. The survivors, objects and wildlife all are well-designed and animate nicely. I love watching the base at dawn as the advanced solar panels all move to start tracking the sun. There are lots of little things like that which make it seem like a real place, with real people you are trying to keep alive.

Definitely my game of the year, and almost certainly a favorite for years to come!\t\t\t\t\t
