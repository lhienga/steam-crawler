\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst and foremost. This game delivers exactly what it promised. You have a survival building simulator with interesting survivors that have end-game goals or you can ignore them and just play nearly infinitely. It's pretty good, now for the "issues"

Outside of "Drafting" a unit, there is no way to tell them to stay inside a building or inside your base. Meaning if you have SOME combat-focused individuals and some production-based, sometimes your farmer will walk outside the gate into a swarm of bugs and simply... die. This is a much bigger issue if you have people working a long way from base when an attack happens or you get a sudden elemental effect because outside of drafting them which is easy to forget, they'll just wander into the enemies and get deep six'ed or become a .

There is only like 2 broad catagories of enemies in the game and the animals are extremely limited too. Most aren't worth taming from the ones that even ARE tameable and they don't feel very unique.

You can't select specifically what survivors eat. Lets say you have 1000 bug meat and 50 red meat, you can't tell them to use what you have an abundance of and save the rarer resource. This also applies to not wanting your surviors chewing on "raw" meat so you disable eating raw foods but then, how are you supposed to use up those 500 berries sitting in the freezer?

You can't move a specific resource out of a storage container. You can set a survivor to "Haul" a resource stack to the storage but say you're running out of space and those aforementioned berries that are not used in any recipe's need to go... tough tata's, you can't directly interract with them.

There is also only 3 or 4 scenario's and they all start on the same randomly generated world of desert or not desert...

It is incredibly hard to find new survivors. Completely luck-based and it takes so long you're already pretty much completely set up by that stage.

Would love a future ability to make a custom character with a trait buying system. Like you can take more positive traits if you also take some negative traits, be interested in 2 things in you take uninterested and incapable in another 2. Wishful thinking.


TLDR; This game is incredibly finite in it's playability and replayability. It's fairly easy to get to the end of the tech tree and look at your little survivor camp and say "Now What". Not worth the price tag IMO but as it's a fresh release i'll be watching future updates\t\t\t\t\t
