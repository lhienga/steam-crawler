\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of those games you'll either love or go not for me.

And if you do love it (like me) be prepared for a lot of "where did the time go?" moments. There is a bit of micro managing and waiting for something to happen, but over all there's enough game play for me to make those periods bearable. There's also a lot of resource juggling while keeping everyone from having a massive meltdown. Which when it does happen is often quite entertaining.
Like most games in this genre', there could be a few QoL additions to reduce the micro managing. Like being able to mass select bulldozing items, Or not having to bulldoze constructions instead of simply upgrading them to a better version. But I think this might be intentional, since much of the game play is managing a limited labour pool of 4 survivors and a massive amount of tasks to do. Still it makes for a lot of selecting/clicking...

But don't get me wrong in the end I do highly endorse the game if it's in your wheelhouse.\t\t\t\t\t
