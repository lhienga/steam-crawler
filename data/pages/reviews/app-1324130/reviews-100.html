\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish there was a "middle" button. This game is Rimworld-like, so if you've played Rimworld before, the learning curve won't be so steep.

PROS:
+++ Much better graphics than Rimworld - I do like those minimalist graphics but they get old after a while.
+++ Not so laggy end-game as Rimworld - at least I haven't experienced huge lag as in late-game RW. I'm not sure about other people's experience.
+++ Multi-story buildings - Recently the've added roof levels so you can now build a two-storied home. This is a huge addition, in my opinion.
++ Many things that are available in Rimworld are also available in this game - research tree, workbenches, meals, rotting food, temperature system, combat, electricity system, colonists skills and many more.
+ 3D: The fact that the game is 3D is pretty cool. I like that you can build higher or lower.

CONS:
---- No enemy survivors: no raiders, no other humans except for survivors that join your colony. That also means that there is no prison system. This is one of the main reasons this game is only a little above middle score. If there was prison system and raiders, it would be a certainly yes. (I do understand this is a subjective opinion but alas, every review should be subjective up to a point). The only raiders are hostile animals.
---- Not a complex health system: People get ill, people get wounded and so on. However, there is no "lung shot off, so your colonist breath harder so it moves slower and its consciousness drops" like in Rimworld. So the health system is not as complex as in Rimworld.

There are other minor things I don't like but nothing that's worth mentioning. Those 2 above are my main "dislikes" that are ALMOST a deal breaker.

CONCLUSION:

9 pluses and 8 minuses = 1 plus. I'd give this game 7.5/10.

It basically is to Rimworld what Bannerlord is to Warband: A prettier but simplified version.

UPDATE: Game got some nice changes lately. For example, now you can build on floors. There have also been androids introduced to the game. This means that the devs listen to the community and give them what they desire. I appreciate that.

What I recommend the devs to look into in the future:

- Prison system
- Improve health system

Yeah, that will make the game even closer to Rimworld but who cares? In fact, I believe most of the players in this community are Rimworld fans and would LOVE a 3D, pretty, multithreaded RW clone.\t\t\t\t\t
