There are some new fun things that this game does that are innovative such as the ability to discover new techs by observing the environment, but there are a lot of things that Rimworld had that are missing here - mostly raids that included gun wielding maniacs that I could capture and then convince to join my team.

I don't know if there is a way to get more team members but I used to get excited when I would recruit somebody in Rimworld and that is lacking here. The pets are also lacking a bit. I want to have something that I can gather fur or eggs from. The pets in this game seem more hassle than they are worth.

That said, if you love Rimworld then this game should be a treat for you! I love that your buildings can have multiple floors and the tech tree is really cool (even though it is slow to develop).

I would give this game a solid 8/10 whereas Rimworld is a 10/10. That said, I haven't explored enough yet to fully have a feel for what the game has to offer and I'm excited to see where the devs take us!\t\t\t\t\t
