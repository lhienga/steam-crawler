It took me a full 80 hours to finish all the research available and pretty much reach the peak of my settlement. About 6 hours into the game I added bunch of mods that added more dept and fun and I pretty much stuck to the first game I started and went to the end.
80 hours of fun for $24.49 is a decent deal as I've paid more for less.

Many reviews compare this game to RimsWorld but as a 3D version and while that is fair; I would compare it to Surviving Mars. And honestly while much respect to RimsWorld (which I have), it wasn't able to keep my attention as long as this game. I found it more enjoyable as it was at a reasonable pace as well.
