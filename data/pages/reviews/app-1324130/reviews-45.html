\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a copy of RimWorld except RimWorld has more depth in almost every regard and a solid modding community to top it off. After ~5-6 hours of playtime I was bored out of my mind and decided to quit because there wasn't anything interesting going on in the game.

I played on «Hard» difficulty. The three most notable things that ever happened to my camp were:
• a random lightning strike hit one of the colonists;
• a colonist randomly got sick with brain parasites;
• a designated fighter fainted due to being too tired while animals were attacking the camp.

The game lacks interesting events or enemies. It also lacks meaningful challenge, and I don't think raising the difficulty setting would solve it: enemies would have more HP and higher damage but fending off regular animal attacks is boring and tedious as it is.

Basically, this game doesn't have anything unique other games of the genre don't. This would be fine if the game took the best of everything that was done before and refined it. Yet it is not the case, this game has all the same flaws that many other colony sims have.

PS: if you want to try the game, buy the base version; don't buy the DLC until you progressed into lategame.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun but no where near the completeness of similar games like Rimworld or Oxygen Not Included. It has a strong visual foundation to beat Rimworld, just needs a lot more content, specifically enemy, resource, environment and mechanics variety. Also need better automation to prevent resource waste without tedious micromanagement. Oxygen Not Included does the automation and resource/mechanics variety extremely well.

The scenarios are different from each other, but you can't combine all the mechanics in one game. I feel like it was done on purpose to create an artificial sense of variety, by dragging the game out because you have a need to try another game to check out a new scenario mechanic. Very gimmicky.

Only recommending this to support the game to improve as above. Would love to see it become the new Rimworld, with Oxygen Not Included inspirations; and also stand on its own unique gameplay.

I'll give it a few years, if nothing happens it would be a disappointing waste of opportunity.

In saying that, I haven't been this engrossed in a game for a while. Had a very fun time and well worth the sales price. Full price is too much unless you want to support the game, or completely into this sort of genre and can't wait for a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
