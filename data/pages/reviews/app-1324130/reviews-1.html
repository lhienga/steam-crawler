\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMistakenly compared with Rimworld, the similarities are superficial only. The menus took on the fundamental design but the game play is not at all there, none of it. This is just as simple as simple can be

This is a Very Casual Heavy game and is far more comparable to Cozy games like Coral Island or Stardew Valley, but with some superficial combat, it's entirely there for flavor and will never actually present a challenge or threat or even have any meaning behind it.

All the colonists here are pre-generated characters. There aren't very many either. 30 or so total. There are no randomly created or player created characters. There are no randomly created maps. The "seed" only determines which map you start on and which colonists, but they will always be the same exact pool of colonists from one game to the next.

I wish I could say it played like a SciFi version of the first sims at least, but saddly the content here just too plain, simple, and basic to even feel much like a game at all... =(

Some really cool mods are available in the workshop once you get through the 40 hrs or so of the 3 built-in scenarios and one dlc scenario.
