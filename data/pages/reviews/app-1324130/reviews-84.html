\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my nearly-100-hours-review and I'm really missing a "on the fence" recommendation option.

The game is fine. It's taking a lot of inspiration from Rimworld, but not the crimes against humanity part, and reforging them into a more focused story. However, it has a lot of issues. In spite of all the issues I feel like I got my money's worth and that it's still got some life left in it.

* Resource hauling needs an overhaul. Priority haul order is missing and pawns being able to carry only one stack at a time makes it annoying. Moving settlement spot, mining not-so-far spots, and hunting as a result is to use a technical term "iffy"
* Glass windows really needs to be implemented, with thermal insulated windows as a breakthrough. As it is windows are actively detrimental.
* End game objective for regular scenario is a very tedious waiting game. You are increasingly unable to respond to issues and are just stuck waiting for things to happen.
* Ranching interface has none of the information you would expect about the animals. Info on reproduction status and growth amount/yields really should be there, but instead there's controls for allowing the animal to be petted.
* Clothing and mechanical equipment wear-and-tear rate tends to leave a colony stuck in perpetual maintainance mode.\t\t\t\t\t
