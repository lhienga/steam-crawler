\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlready selling DLC about 6 months after initial release? The game felt very early access in launch, I expected a lot of updates, tweaking, and refinement based on my intial time with it. Selling DLC so soon doesn't feel right.

It definitely feels like a 3D Rimworld though, so if that's what you are looking for it may be for you. The combat and game loop seemed a bit more boring to me compared to Rimworld though. I guess I wanted more personality and specific goals? After the 5th time the same little bugs attacked and I had to micromanage defense with bad controls, characters not doing what I ordered then dying, I lost interest.

The 3D aspect of it is a bit disingenuous too -- I'm watching the Steam trailers while writing this and you'll never see 99% of those angles or closeups in normal play. Many of the angles are not even possible with the camera controls, and the rest you would just never bother zooming in on except maybe once or twice when you first srart. But admittedly it is far better visually than Rimworld's basic icon/symbol/shape graphics.

I wish there was a neutral option for Steam reviews, but I'm leaning toward not recommending at this time. I really feel like it needed a lot of (non-DLC) updates the last time I tried it. I'll try it again at some point, with the updates and without the DLC and update this rating if warranted.\t\t\t\t\t
