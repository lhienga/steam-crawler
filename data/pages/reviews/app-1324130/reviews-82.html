\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like Rimworld and other similar games? This is a similar game! It's very Rimworld at release at the moment, lacking a lot of the complexity of current year Rimworld. But it's also much prettier than Rimworld. Stranded is a very competent entry in the genre of survival colony builder, but if you're expecting current year Rimworld or (god forbid) Dwarf Fortress levels of complexity and development you'll be disappointed. But if you want The Sims in Space, Stranded is right about that level of difficulty and complexity. Maybe slightly more complex than that, but not a whole lot.

Probably the best feature is that all the colonists are human made, with both obvious and hidden beneficial and detrimental qualities, and their backstories sort of have an impact on the game. All the other survival colony sims I've played have RNG colonists, which...are fine, in some ways better than Stranded's, but I think I prefer Stranded's intelligently designed colonists. I also admit that this may be a player preference thing and other people might hate it due to the monotony of always having the same pool of colonists (and no others) available.

I recommend it for a certain sort of person. I like to play this game while folding laundry because generally once you're past the initial (somewhat difficult) start, the game only requires setting some orders periodically and very little other player intervention.\t\t\t\t\t

You can't make your own character easily (you have to create your own mod to do it instead of having built in functionality for the game for it), the systems are very basic and there's no depth to the crafting system in any meaningful way. The mech's are awesome, love those they look absolutely sick. But it's missing its own identity honestly. It just feels like a 3D Rimworld lite. I'm concerned because this is the "released" version I bought.. Which means they're considering this feature complete, and honestly I disagree. Still, if you're wanting a less complex rimworld this may be right up your alley. It's very casual friendly, which is by no means a bad thing. It just wasn't what I was looking for.\t\t\t\t\t
