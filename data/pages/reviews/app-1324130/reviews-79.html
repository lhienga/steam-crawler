\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRimworld - Depth + 3D graphics = Stranded Alien Dawn

Its ok - 6/10 Not as great as I was hoping it would be honestly. Base building is very well designed and incredibly fun. Looks great as well however compared to similar colony sims like this it just doesnt have much depth. It plays exactly like Rimworld in every way, you can tell Rimworld was a huge inspiration for this game. You can also tell they had to soften the edges for that Teen rating. Unfortunately no effort was made to replace the brutal M rated aspects that made Rimworld so interesting.

Combat is exactly like Rimworld however there is no dismemberment and the organ damage has been dumbed down a ton. There is a status panel where you can see what limb or body part is hurt but the effect is almost always the same - a little movement and blood loss until they heal up. It doesnt go beyond that and this panel can almost be ignored completely. Your limbs will never actually be destroyed same as organs, your survivors just take damage until they die.

Unfortunately there is also huge lack of variety when it comes to enemies. There is basically just 1 enemy and its waves of bugs that comes in different intervals. Its like they tried to copy Rimworld here too but to get the T rating they cut all human enemies and didnt try to add any variety of their own just left it at waves of bugs who quickly get repetitive and boring super quick.\t\t\t\t\t
