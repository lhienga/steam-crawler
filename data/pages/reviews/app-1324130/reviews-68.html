\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFoundation vs. Banished vs. Frostpunk vs. Dawn of Man vs. Stranded: Alien Dawn
This game feels closer to Dawn of Man, as you won't build a lot of things, but you are managing your resources closer to Banished/Foundation. You can play forever, but have a specific win condition, similar to Frostpunk. Here you are much more zoomed into your few survivors and will micromanage them during combat. They can be micromanaged during calm periods, but a good schedule and task priority system will have you run this game 90% on 3x speed.
You aren't building "work buildings", but stations, and housing around, so your base feels smaller, but you have a much more intimate relationship with each detail you put in.
I have beat 100% achievements, including insane chaos difficulty and it requires your attention and careful planning. Even for experienced sim players, I recommend starting on easy and doing one scenario at a time with each difficulty progression from there.
This is welcoming for players to chill and play easy, but also for experienced players to work hard.
Interesting game, highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is hard to recommend this over Rimworld.

The graphics are meh, the ai pretty weak, managing happiness is no where near as good. There is no real benefit to skills aside from not failing and a finishing a bit faster. Is it a bad game at the moment? No. But it is not as good or even on the same level as the game it is emulating.

Some interesting things it does. Massive maps. Being an alien planet it makes you research the flora and fauna which is very cool. The research is interesting and it does storage way better than other games of the same style.

Game speed is slow even at the top speed. There is no quality system. Finding some of the plants or ore is very difficult. Combat is pretty weak. The pathing and hauling AI is pretty meh. Characters move pretty slow. Lightning strikes colonists too often. In my play through only one person ever got hit but they got hit like 6 times.

It is interesting and I hope modding can fix it up a bit.\t\t\t\t\t
