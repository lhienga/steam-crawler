\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros: Challenging with a lot of replayability. If you mess up and can catch the problem with your production early, you usually can recover without much effort - but sometimes you see the problem well after it happened and it's almost easier to start over. I guess this is true of a survival situation where you are learning as you are going. The graphics are enjoyable, the landscape shots at different times of day are really beautiful. I do wish the characters themselves had a bit more detail but overall good graphics. There is a lot of emergent gameplay and if you crank the difficulty it does get harder.

Cons: Despite the replayability aspect, the game can get stale if you aren't save scumming for the best outcomes or willing to try and burnout your colonists. I want to have more control over how the maps are generated and I definitely want some more colonists that are in the vanilla game.

Overall: Worth the price if you enjoy survival sims with a sci-fi twist. Almost feels like a better graphics version of Rimworld.\t\t\t\t\t
