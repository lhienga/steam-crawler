\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed playing this game and hated it at the same time. I understand that the game can improve over time so I updated my review to recommended as soon as I posted the negative review, they released the patch the next day. This would be a bit boring for some and would not be enjoyable for casuals. Insane mode is insane indeed.

The concept is good. Managing your survivors is great and they have a variety of characteristics. Able to build mechs and wreck havoc to those pesky insects is very satisfying when bombarding them with missiles, epic rain of fire. Circuitry and being able to program your defenses is cool and I liked it.

Now for the downsides.
BRAIN DEAD survivor AI. Leaving them alone during fights or tasks would be a great mistake. They won't do what they are told sometimes. Attacking enemies randomly rather than those closest. You can't cancel once they go down the ladder just because they are so stupid to stop and think. Survivor have collision while enemies even large ones doesn't and can pass through survivors blocking the door.

Selecting an object is also a pain once they are located on the same grid. There is this certain pixel somewhere of the object that you should click to select it only.

Building bases are easily targeted and destroyed. Once base is destroyed, all upper floors are instantly destroyed too. How about letting the walls being damaged first rather than the base which as it should be. Multiple enemies stacking in 1 grid without collision then attacks at the same time instantly 1 shot that building.

Lightning is so annoying because of the permanent lightning injury debuff on your survivor. Lightning is programmed to hit your survivor every thunderstorm event unless you have a lightning rod or indoors. If thunderstorm occurs early, then your done for. Temp lightning injury is also bugged sometimes doesn't cure which lasted for a number of years.

There is also a issue with calling for trader ships where no ships responds and call is cut-off. It would be nice if we get notified if this happened so that we can call again.

Survivors sometimes are not following their priority tasks. It would be nice if there is an option where we can directly select a workstation and assign the survivor here only.

Building floors on uneven terrain is a pain and restricting. I hope that we had the option to dig or flatten an area so that we can build on it. Would they even consider adding basements.

So yeah. The game needs more improvement at its current state. If you like a challenge and busy work, you can give this game a try.
