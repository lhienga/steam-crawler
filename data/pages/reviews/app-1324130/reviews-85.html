\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe AI of the survivors and their task scheduling could use some improvement. It can be frustrating to have to redo their schedule every time I need them to repeat a certain task. It would be helpful if there was an option to order survivors to craft a certain amount of items until they return to their regular schedule. This would be an easy improvement to implement.

The electronics in the game break too quickly, which can be a limiting factor for players who enjoy building megabases. While I understand that this is supposed to add a challenge to the game, it would be nice to have the option to slow down the rate of decay.

Manual farming for a game set in the future feels a bit outdated. It would be great if there were options for automated farming devices as a late-game option. This would add another layer of complexity to the game and make it more enjoyable for players who have progressed further.

The game could also benefit from more advanced automation options such as the addition of NOT and OR gates, as the current automation items are fairly simplistic.

Overall, the quality of life in the game could be improved as in current state its 1/10.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't really get why this is so highly rated or popular...

It's an OK game, it's really quite shallow, lacking depth and complexity, it's where Rimworld was when it was still at the very start of it's Beta phase. The only thing it has going for it over Rimworld, is being 3D and considering the huge gulf of features between this and just Rimworlds base game, It's really not worth getting just for the fact it's "3D".

It really needs Character customisation, some basic UI elements, like being able to see more of the map when zoomed out or a minimap type feature/world map button, notications for when characters get long term illnesses, I've recently had 2 characters in 2 different games have major long term health issues that I don't know how they developed or when It started, plus no way to fix it. More options to recruit people, playing for over 2 years with just 4 characters is so slow to get things done it just becomes boring.

Really should have been left in EA for another 6-12 months to flesh out but I feel like the dev's needed the cash to either continue developing the game or to start a new project, either way it's not a great way to release a game or treat your playerbase.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a fan of survival sims and city builders in general, but it's really the theme that convinced me to try this, and I was not disappointed.

I frequently found myself playing in 6 hour+ sessions, and grew somewhat attached to my settlers, which is highly unusual in most games of this genre. What there is here already is quite excellent, and I only have a few minor quibbles;
- Tech tree research times are very punishing in some instances.
- The building system is excellent, but I would love three things to expand on it: second storeys, more furnishing options (decor, paint, a pool table?... Modders?), and the ability to re-seed the ground with grass/the natural terrain.
- Survivors generally tend to dislike each other, which I suppose isn't wholly unrealistic but a 50/50 balance would be nice.

I look forward to the future of this game, and can only hope for more content patches or even DLC.

Also, Nova is the GOAT.\t\t\t\t\t
