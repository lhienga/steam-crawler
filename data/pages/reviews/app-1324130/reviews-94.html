\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish Steam had a "meh" category.

It's not bad, but it's far from great. It's not current Rimworld. It's closer to Rimworld during the early alpha days (I backed Rimworld on Kickstarter). It has an end-game. You can win it. But . . ..

The "colonist" AI actively fights you when you try to fight. It loves to spam massive hordes of enemies to zerg-rush your base. And your non-drafted colonists will actively "go for a leisurely walk" right through the horde.

At least, unlike AColony, I didn't die within one second of starting a map. But, being able to successfully win a game (evacuating a total of 6 survivors) on my second game . . ..

And let's talk about those survivors. Sure, they have pretty art for each one, but, they're all pre-made characters with pre-made baggage. And those are the only survivors you'll get to see. You can't make your own. And with the Denuvo Malware, you won't be adding your own, anytime soon.

This game isn't Rimworld. It wants to be, but it isn't. It's not a bad game. It's pretty, but coupled with Denuvo, I'd say go play Rimworld and avoid this until they remove the bloatware and it goes on sale.

It still needs an epic amount of work to approach Rimworld. It's not a $35USD game. It's a $20USD game. With Denuvo, it's a $5USD game.\t\t\t\t\t
