\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLike a lot of other people, I was looking for a Rimworld-like colony sim in 3D and this certainly fits that description. It's much more streamlined and focuses on smaller groups of survivors, at the cost of some depth that you would have experienced playing Rimworld. Multi-floor building is quite cool and it makes the 3D part seem like it actually matters.

I think there's still some room for improvements, for example I wish some resources were more granular (a sensor shouldn't cost the same number of CPUs as an automated turret imo) and that energy generation had more options (you are almost entirely dependent on renewables, fuel based sources don't really scale well in my experience).

Overall an excellent entry into the colony building genre, and the game continues to be updated.\t\t\t\t\t

In short, your colonists become less and less efficient over time (as well as more demanding in their needs/rest time). So each day you get less done as they waste more time, but the items you need to craft require greater volumes and variety of components, so you have more resources to discover/gather/farm/refine and less work hours to do it.

For me, the gameplay loop became unenjoyable and increasingly frustrating; fighting the games inefficient and overly complex systems to do basic things.\t\t\t\t\t
