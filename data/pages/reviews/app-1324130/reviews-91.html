\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo start out, I don't review games that often. This is basically a 3D version of Rimworld which I absolutely enjoy. I have hundreds of hours on Rimworld. This game right now as it is, is pretty good but It could be a lot better though.

My first thoughts on improvement would be little Hauler/Transporter robots, if we can research and build Giant battle mechs, then I don't feel like a little Roomba type Hauler droid would be that far fetched. Possibly even trained animals being to be que'd for pickup/transport.

2nd idea is that something for transportation to move around the map faster and to be able to carry more things, Perhaps a little go cart or even a wagon attached to a tamed animal, Hell i'd even settle for the mech being able to carry stuff... My people have to traverse for like 20 to 30 seconds or maybe like 1 to 3 hours in game just to go pick up 100 pieces of whatever. They soooo much rest and food and energy for something that could be simplified. Hell even rollerskates or something to quicken movement would be great. Maybe Jetpacks???

3rd though is being able to repair clothing, Unless you happen to get carbon armor breakthrough or are able to get a ton of synthetic thread for armor, this stuff breaks down soooo fast. While we're on the subject of clothing make it an option for them to change by themselves unless ordered not to! Their adults have a wardrobe and let them make heating/cooling decisions(unless ordered not to).

4th thing, Need more power generating options, if you don't get lucky with the Graphene Breakthrough or the Carbon wind tunnels then you're out of luck. A regular solar panel is only +20 the Graphene is +60! You'd have to have a whole ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ solar farm plus they only work during the day. so hopefully you got those batteries set up well enough.

5th thing, more options with animals. If I have a tamed vicious animal, allow me to attach it to a hunter that will follow them around and attack with them.

6th thing, add fishing! there's always these little ponds around or next to the giant ocean, there's already alien life everywhere, why is there no sea life?! Should be easy enough to be able to configure that out.

7th thing, easier allowance to building on water. If I want to build an ocean bungalow or a resort then let me. Granted if would cost a lot of resources then so be it.

That's all that comes to mind right now that would massively improve gameplay, in my opinion.


I'll end with saying the game is still very fun, I hope that he gets more love and attention. Could easily be the next Rimworld if they keep working on it.\t\t\t\t\t

Secondly, I'm a huge Rimworld player and have done many other survival type games. So let me explain a few things that need to be explained here.

First of all, many people are saying this is a 3D rimworld only easier. This is a good accurate point.

I'd like to hope that Devs will add harder stuff in the game, maybe alien humanoids that raid or harder insects? The issue right now is 1 human can easily kill off 6 or more insects alone and there's never real threat to losing your human pawn. In Rimworld you easily get wounded and are down for days sometimes or can be kidnapped or die.

IN this game, the threat of combat just isn't there. Sadly. What can happen is insects can tear down your base, destroy walls, and such. I have been playing 10 hours and have done combat many times and never is combat feeling like a threat. Even when its 4 humans vs 20 insects raiding.

Now with that said, this game has room for improvement and we will probably see it in the future. The game is very good and i haven't seen any bugs in game yet. Rimworld is much more dangerous and of a threat. This survival game seems way easier than Rimworld even on easy mode. But it is fun and just as good.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like this game. I'd say it's a simpler Rimworld in alot of ways.

It hits a lot of those Rimworld checkboxes. Rather than pointing out the laundry list of what I love about the game, I'll just say there is a LOT.

Some of things I didn't like were:
1. Zoning. In Rimworld you could Zone areas so animals or people wouldn't leave certain areas. For example: if there is a cold snap, I could confine them to being indoors and they would still do work in the base and stay warm.
2. Inventory Managment was pretty lacking. Rimworld does it right with stockpile priority, durability and quality threshholds.
3. A trashing mechanic where you can choose to trash items from stockpiles and storage.
4. The ability to drop items you craft on the ground and keep crafting so other workers could haul them.
5. Clothing priority and eating Priority (Rimworld had outfits for the seasons etc)

Maybe this will come with updates or mods and aren't really game breaking. Just some things I ran into that left me wanting to go play Rimworld so I could control the things I wanted to lol.
You have a smaller group in the game compared to Rimworld, so efficiency is key. It would be nice to create 1 square zones with higher priority than your main inventory storage so that workers would bring items to the 1 sqaure zone located next to a crafting table. This would reduce walk times for your workers and they could keep working at the crafting station.\t\t\t\t\t

They copied Rimworld systems but nothing is even remotely balanced correctly.

Nothing really happens; once you have a vague idea how to play you can just make a boat load of everything you need in one growing season and then just fast forward until the end. Every attack can be repulsed by the slightest bit of defense. Your frozen food appears to never have anything happen to it and the stack sizes are such that you don't need to make more ever again if you made a lot the first time.

Gear I just ignored beyond making sure they had some shoes, shirt, and pants.

Happiness apparently took care of itself just with a bedroom and a few instruments placed around.

Priority system doesn't exist on a per machine/station level so actually getting anyone to do what you want is seriously annoying. Luckily none of it mattered so I eventually stopped worrying about it.

The giant pretty world is bait. You can't even explore that far even if you found a need to, which I didn't.

The expeditions are just like pop up rewards, theres no gameplay involved.

Idk man, not for me. Maybe with mods some day.
