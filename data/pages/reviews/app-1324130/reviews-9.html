TL;DR: A more immersive 3D Rimworld.

=====OVERALL EXPERIENCE=====
I'm an idiot.
Poorly made.
Mediocre.
Excellent quality.
Masterpiece.

=====THIS IS A=====
Story-teller.
Time-killer.

=====REPLAYABILITY=====
Several rounds.
Fun for a while.
Until you get bored.
Full of surprise.
I can do this all day.

=====BGM=====
No music heard.
Like 100 nails scratching on the wall.
Repetitive and tolerable.
Highly immersive.
Top-band quality.
I need OST.

=====CONTROLS=====
Anti-human.
Requires advanced piano skills.
Complex but logical.
Incredibly smooth.
Your grandma can handle this.
No need for controls.

=====DIFFICULTY=====
Suitable for poor hand-eye coordination.
Requires some basic learning.
Haven't started after 20 hours.
Masochist's favorite.
The game plays YOU.

=====GRINDING=====
Nothing to grind at all.
Grind or not, your choice.
Grind is needed to play through. (Idle farming for resources)
You are going to work your butt off.
Meaningless & repetitive grinding through the whole game.

=====MULTIPLAYER=====
Zen: You travel alone.

Throw a party: 6 players and more.

=====MOST VALUED WHEN=====
Giveaway.
75% off.
50% off.
25% off.
Full price. Gabe's loss.

=====PROS=====
A 3D version of Rimworld with better graphics for more immersive gameplays.
Decent tutorial process.

=====CONS=====
Slow game pace.
Monotonous late-game content.
Easily to get repetitive (for now).

=====ANYTHING ELSE=====
Once you overcome the most challenging part of the game (which occurs at the very beginning of each round), you will find yourself spending a significant amount of time on idle farming for resources and waiting for technological research. However this is a common issue in many similar games.

The graphics are excellent with a high attention to details. Players of Rimworld can seamlessly transition to this game with realistic visuals that enhance immersion.

The game does not contain a random generator for survivors, so gameplay can become repetitive, as only a few among initial survivors have the strongest abilities and potentials. However, the game has recently introduced a random selection for survivors which significantly improves replayability.

With continuously updates and promising expectations, this game is worth diving into if it's your favorite type.\t\t\t\t\t
