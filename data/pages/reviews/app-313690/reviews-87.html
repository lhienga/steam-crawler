\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally great game!

I highly recommend this to any DC fans out there. Despite my age this game captivated me, and was the first Lego game to keep me coming back until I reached 100% completion.

Not only is this one of the most in-depth Batman games out there (no joke), it also includes pretty much every major (and minor) DC character you have always wanted to see in a game. From Krypto, Lobo, Brainiac, Arkillo, Man Bat, Atrocitus, and Kyle Rayner as the White Lantern, all the way to Daffy Duck as Green Lantern (Green Loontern), Condiment Man, The Grey Ghost, Kid Flash, Hush, Gorilla Grodd, Saint Walker, and Black Hand - E.V.E.R.Y.O.N.E. that could possibly be in a Batman game is in this game.

....Even the likes of Conan O Brian and the late and great Adam West have appearances....

If you, like me, love Batman and/or DC comics you owe yourself to play this game. Trust me, you will be more than pleasantly surprised!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy favourite of the Lego Games

While I did enjoy playing the other ones, this one had me going back for more even after I'd finished the story. I found myself wanting to find and complete everything there was. The story was fun and there are loads of side quests to complete after. There's a nice collection of characters to play as, even during the story.

The only thing that got on my nerves in this game was when on the Lantern Planets. The camera angles were quite fiddly and annoying. It was quite difficult to know where you were going at times. Countless times getting angry that my little Lego character wasn't going where I wanted it to.

I definitely recommend this game, especially to Lego fans.\t\t\t\t\t
