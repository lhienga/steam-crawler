The story is fun and the characters get interesting spins, but the navigation is clunky and annoying. It had a lot of potential that it just didn't own up to. There's no particular incentive to explore the planets or replay the levels, and while the open world capabilities are really neat, it feels like every attempt to go to another planet is out of the way and not worth the trip. Part of what makes similar games fun is that you actively approach the place where the quest will happen. Here, you're largely confined to the Watchtower.\t\t\t\t\t
