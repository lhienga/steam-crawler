The characters: The large selection of characters are not only focused on Batman but the entire DC universe, their voices, targeting icons, looks, combo moves all suit them greatly. Martian Manhunter's animations, The Flash's running, Alfred's mere stance, they all feel distinct and original.

The story: Brainiac is an amazing villain is well suited to face the League, finding ways to oppose him is fun and the levels against him are some of the best Lego has to offer.

Hub Worlds: The game doesn't focus on a single large hub but rather a smaller few. The Hall of Justice, The Hall of Doom, very fun to explore and do quests. The updated Batcave is much larger and well designed. The Watchtower? couldn't be better.

Cons:
The story: Yes, both a pro and a con but almost all cons to list come from a simple fact: the game focuses too much on the Lanterns and it now doesn't even feel like a Lego Batman game. The Lanternverse is vast with many great charaters but doesnt work well when they are all smashed into a single story like this as Batman's is (like in the other games)

Hub Worlds: Most of the hub worlds are the the home planets for the various Lantern Corps which are more of a small globe rather than an actual planet, these hubs have basicly no life, are mostly simple and plain and fairly boring as well. Morever, as they are fairly barren, most of the gold bricks are based on finishing races while small quests or puzzles are less common.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's more of the same as Lego Batman 2, but IMO not better, but often times worse.

The concept of suits (basically powers) from Batman 2 was kept, it was made weaker by giving almost all the suits to almost all the heroes, removing what made the power special. It doesn't help that the roster gets dramatically increased, with the same runtime, meaning any of the characters gets way less screen time.

They also added some mini game which didn't really bring much, except frustration.

The significant downside is definitely bugs, which we saw much MUCH more than in previous entries. Getting stuck in geometry is pretty common in this one.

Still, it's not a bad game: if you want more of the same having finished Batman 2, I'd say it's OK. If you haven't finished Batman 2, I'd recommend that one instead.\t\t\t\t\t
