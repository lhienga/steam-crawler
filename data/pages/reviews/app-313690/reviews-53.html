\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really liked the game but the justification the lack of recommendation is that I would instead say to get it on a console OR that playing with a controller is mandatory. If so, then this is a swell game.
This game is NOT for pc. The game is fun to play and the levels are amazing.. but oh goodness is it a console port if you ever wanted to feel one. My 2 problems I detail later on is 1. Keyboard controls and 2. No clear windowed fullscreen option.

(Edit): Have finished the whole main game 100%, wanted to revisit this. Leaving the stuff after this paragraph the same as it was before, but TLDR my thoughts are the same - this game is good BUT its not a good experience on the computer. I'm really enjoying the game, I have small gripes like I got bored by the time I finally unlocked the ability to purchase the minifigures I had already been unlocking in the shop menu, felt like it came too late. And also wish I learned not even to bother with freeplay until the very LAST mission is done. Because thats when you unlock brainiac - the single only character that can deal with the shrink ray so yeah dont bother 100% any level until the main game. Simple enough. My favorite custom hero to make is a hero with the powers of flash and green lantern together. Thats just for convenience. You get the run speed of flash, build speed of flash, and you can fly and shoot missiles through the power of green lantern. I wish there was an easier way to blow up the silver bomb items then my go-to of cyborg / batman. It very tedious to swap to the character, then have to bring up the side menu to select which suit for the hero you picked. If I had to rate this game: content wise its a 9/10, but for the sole reason of the PC platform, it gets a 5/10 from me. The annoyance of the alt tab black screen windows mess is the single thing that brings it so far down. If there was a workaround for a windowed fullscreen option that'd be heaven.

(Original review)

- The game doesnt appear to have a windowed fullscreen / editable fullscreen mode so everyime I want to alt tab its a 20 second process as all my monitors go black for a few moments.
- The keyboard controls are... well.. this is for a controller. Luckily I have a controller I used. Use a controller. If you try a keyboard, the mouse does nothing and instead alt tabs you if you do use your mouse, and the controls are wasd and then the main use button is h. freaking h. I cant say anymore because after 1 minute of that I dug out a controller. If i did not have one I would have stopped playing then.
- Freeplay? - I played other lego games more than a decade ago and I remember the staple of Lego starwars saga and how there was the main hub, I believe that is the case here BUT I'm like 5 levels in and am unable to visit it. I want to get there soon because I want to use the extras / cheats and buy stuff and also explore. But alas, I seem cut off from it. I'm now at a point where I can open a map and go back to prev levels in freeplay, but still not far in for that to make sense yet without the gadgets covered via characters.

Now the good parts, oh my gosh this game is good. The game. not the steam PC version. The robin tech hacking method is something I enjoyed and had never seen before, its like a tron world minigame. The space station level is mesmerizing, going around the outside of a space station in that circular method. That tripped me out. The multiplayer is solid, just like lego. You can weirdly have 1 player use the keyboard (which sucks) and another use the controller, or one probably use the other half of the keyboard I assume. It works how you expect a LEGO game to work. lovely. So for a grown up this game is annoying to play because of the technical things I mentioned, but prob is still a good kid game. I'm only that 10% in, will be completing so likely full edit later.

Reviewing a 2014 game in 2021 is silly, but that doesn't ever stop me! Play the game IF you can use a controller and IF you cant play it on a console. Don't buy or play if you hope this plays like a good PC game (you cant even use your mouse to select menu options, its all through arrow keys and the keyboard).\t\t\t\t\t
