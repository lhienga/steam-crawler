\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMeh... this game isn't nearly as good as other lego games. This game is incredibly lack luster in a lot of areas compared to the previous games. The story isn't as good. The open world design isn't as good. The level design isn't as good as last time.

A big problem lego has is dragging out their levels to make them long, and while long levels are nice, these ones can be boring at times. The big selling point of this game is the amount of characters. There are a lot of them this time around. Rather than just focusing on batman mostly, the game covers the entire DC universe of heroes. As a result there are a freak ton of characters, but they all take a while to unlock. Lego has created this thing where when you get the character, you now have to buy (with the in game money) the character after you do the mini game or side quest, which is honestly stupid because these characters can be super expensive, leading there to be no motive to use the characters you play to unlock. The games open world is no longer one big open world like gotham city from the last game or new york from marvel super heroes but rather planets from the DC comics. This game honestly should've been called lego justice league. While this is an actually good idea, but for some reason lego has opted to make this worlds super small, literally these worlds are spheres. As a result of this, these worlds aren't very fun for me to explore outside of some worlds that aren't fully spheres. I think it would've been cool to make SOME of these worlds spheres or different even, but it just makes everything not as fun to navigate because it feels super small and bundled up. Gotham and new york city were much more fun to explore. I think this game was focusing more on "hub worlds" than open worlds, which I think was a bad decision. The story is ok. Its not bad but its probably my least favorite out of the DC series.

I think the best thing this game does is all of the refreneces. This game has so many call backs and shows so much love to all of DC. Its great for beginners and people who don't know much outside of the usual batman and super man. The call backs are also really nice. The Adam west styled batman level is probably the best level in the game. While the characters aren't that fun to unlock, they are cool. And DLC based off the live action movies, and the extra characters they give you are so fun to play. Honestly I have to give these guys credit, this game is a love letter to DC fans, and those who are willing to explore the worlds will have a blast helping out the characters during the side quests.

The game isn't bad but your moneys better spent on another lego game. Unless you are all about those DC refreneces, this is the last one you get before you've run out of good lego game options.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy honest review on LEGO Batman Beyond Gotham.

I love this game so much y'all have no idea. It takes me back to when I was a kid and playing lego games. It is/was so fun. and they still are to this day. Here is my opinions.

PROS - The game and storyline are really interesting and have great characters to play with.

CONS - The game is glitchy, and I ran into a glitch so now I cannot play anymore. Read more below:

One of my two glitches is in the Batman space suit level. When Robin goes into the rover he kept getting stuck and was ready to quit. Everyday when I got home from school I kept trying for a few days and nothing helped. If you run into this problem. Just ALT + ESC when he gets stuck.

My second glitch and main concern is when I entered the cheat codes early in the game, like the codes to get Aquaman, Red Bricks, etc. Then I finished the story and whenever I tried finding a character to talk to around the map/planets to get a gold brick/character token, anything. I couldn't find any characters. So now if I want to play this i'm going to have to replay this whole story and redo the game. I'm not looking forward to that so I'm putting this game on hold for a while until i'm ready to play again.

Besides rare glitches and all, this game is definitely worth it's money and you should definitely buy this. Just please do your cheat codes last. Its not worth redoing the whole story.

My review, 12/28/2021. I will review a year later and see how it goes.
-charlie



Edit: I started playing this game again and im enjoying it alot. I redid the story and im currently at 73.2%. I have been grinding a lot and my goal is to finish the game by mid May. The glitch I have is gone and I love the game more then ever right now.


Please play this game.\t\t\t\t\t
