\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a lego game so it's fun. That much can be said. And they have a lot of fun easter eggs for both new and old fans of the series.
The story lags with the game pretty much giving you a straight path to follow early in the game that will lead you to the rest of the game with no hidden surprises. round up all the lanterns and help batman realize he needs friends. It's so boring it actually makes me want to cry.
And the story forces your characters onto the worlds of the different lanterns. Sounds kind of fun but the designs aren't really distinctive. They all come off as just "Alien world with a dominant color scheme" and it's not really fun. It made me miss the simpler yet more playful levels from the first lego batman game. Museum at night, Greenhouse, so on. Those were fun and had character. But the levels in this game are nothing special.
Over all, with full voice acting and a more fleshed out world, this game loses the simpler charm that comes from being a Lego game. And the roster at first seems fun but really turns out to be bloated. So many of the characters play exactly the same with only a change in color Scheme. It felt like they had no regard for how the characters would play and could be interesting, instead jamming it with as many hollow imitations of DC characters as possible.
Skip this one. It's really not worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
