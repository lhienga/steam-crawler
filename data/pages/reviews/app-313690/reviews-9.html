Don't get me wrong, Lego games are always usually hits and rarely misses with their simple formula and how they can re tell a story from a movie, or make a Original Story all together. However, Lego Batman 3 isn't it. The Main story is laughably short, they got completely rid of interesting stuff that was in Batman 2, no more Characters that you need to find and interact with and then challenge them to obtain them, you now get a character for a simple puzzle and beating up 20 dudes in a quest, no more real Open World that you can fly around for hours, in exchange you get a few mediocre Lantern Planets that only exist for you to leave once you finish them 100%. If you got this game through a Bundle I still recommend it, but if you are thinking of buying it standalone I would recommend the first or the second game to be a better choice.\t\t\t\t\t
