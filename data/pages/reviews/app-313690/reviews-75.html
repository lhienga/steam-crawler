\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely laggy and rife with glitches when it should be a fun romp. They’re immensely frustrating. There are level-breaking glitches, such as Flash not being able to turn the statue, and then you have to start the level or game again.
The game does have good stuff though; 2.5/5
Good:
Vast character selection
The story is nice LEGO fun and has a sweet message.
The game automatically changes the Batman/Robin/Cyborg suit upon pressing the right button next to a puzzle thing, rather than having to manually change your character again and again.
The Adam West level especially brought a big smile to my face.
The sheer ambition of the game is to be applauded. Clearly a lot of passion.
The combo attacks are pretty fun

Bad:
Unskippable cutscenes (even in free play mode?)
The hub is difficult to understand at first.
You have to play the whole game first in order to access different characters.
Too many races
A bizarre overuse of Conan O’Brien and Kevin Smith. It’s fine if he’s in the game, but I don’t see why he needs to be everywhere.
DLCs are necessary for trophies when they shouldn’t be.
The quests can only be played once (the Duck Dodgers quests are also fairly bland)

Would recommend to DC fans, but not really anyone else.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is enjoyable if you intend to simply waste time.
It doesn't contain many game breaking bugs, just the odd visual one. infuriatingly, it is poor as a collectathon because the achievements are locked behind a paywall for poor DLC that has limited content and isn't even worth the cheap prices for said content. The characters feel stale quick because they all have similar controls. The inclusion of the lantern worlds is cool, but it can be confusing to see the location of a mission and not have the quest giver appear because you haven't completed the quest branch up to that point. The controls can be annoying, Superman always wants to freeze things instead of hit them and that button should be heat vision when held down anyway. When playing a transformable character, changing into a different character becomes a hassle. Only one time did I experience a truly game-breaking bug, when trying to load the game the screen would go black. To fix this, you have to delete the pc configuration; this is annoying. Overall, it was a smooth monotonous experience.

7/10 - not great, not horrible, but better than some games
