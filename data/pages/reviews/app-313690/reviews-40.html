\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR - Not as good as the first or the second game, there are better games

No. Just... No.

I am a fan of LEGO games. Hell, I own every single one, DLC included. My favorites are, right now, LEGO Marvel Super Heroes, LEGO Batman 2 and LEGO Star Wars The Skywalker Saga. The former two are classics from my childhood, while I enjoyed 100%'ing TSS recently. So when I had gotten to playing LEGO Batman 3, my expectations were high.

But, after just 6 levels, I was disappointed. The first level was promising, pursuing Killer Croc. It was a nice tutorial (which I obviously didn't need, I am, after all, a LEGO gamer) and I was looking forward to more. But then... it went downhill pretty quickly. The 'Breaking BATS!' and 'Space suits you, Sir!' levels were alright, but the other three were basically the same- going around in a space station, something which I didn't like in LEGO Marvel Super Heroes as well.

Anyways, after level six (I believe), we are subjected to the most annoying creature TT Games could have created - Fear-influenced Cyborg. I hate it. No, I despise it. I was THIS close to just not completing the story solely because of him, but I pushed myself to it.

The story is a mess. I would not be surprised if they just adapted a fan-made comic book series, because, well, it's not good. If you basically DEFEAT THE VILLAIN HALFWAY THROUGH YOUR STORY AND THE OTHER HALF IS JUST 6 GLORIFIED FETCH QUESTS, WITH THE LAST ONE BEING alright actually, well, it was average, then you cannot expect me to praise it.

Also, why can I only go to freeplay AFTER the story? There was a point where you had to repair the portal to get to Earth or something, which could have at least opened up the Batcave for free roam, but no.

Lastly, I want to mention the crashes. My god. The game crashed on me about five times as of writing this review, which averages to about a crash per 3 hours. The game came out 8 years ago and it crashed on me more times than Cyberpunk 2077 did in about 30 hours.

So, would I recommend this game? No. But I will complete it. It is my obligation as a LEGO gamer to 100% every game, and so I must push myself to see that round number. And also, the levels are divided into thirds and you can choose which one to play. That actually played a big role in my decision.

Go play Batman 2, Marvel Super Heroes, LEGO Movie or any LEGO Star Wars.\t\t\t\t\t
