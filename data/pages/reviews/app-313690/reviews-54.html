The story levels are typically broken into 3 parts, and there's a small break in linearity during the story where you can complete missions 9 through 13 in any order you choose. Freeplay becomes a lot less of a hassle by giving you all the characters required to get everything after you finish all the story mode levels.

I've seen a lot of LEGO fans complain about the voice lines being worse than the mumbles, but in this game it's not terrible, since they aren't reading from a script based entirely around another part of media, like the rest of the LEGO batman series the game decides to tell it's own story with these characters, which is certainly refreshing.

My complaints about this game, like with most others, is the character swapping. Compared to LEGO batman 2, you no longer need a suit signal to switch suits, which is good, until you realize that means that all suits have to be accessible somehow. They tried to fix this problem by implementing a wheel to select up to 8 suits, but this wheel comes with a small but tedious cutscene of the hero switching suits and showing it off, which adds up over time. I believe they could've gotten away with this issue if the animation to switch suits was similar to pressing the quick cycle keys, which have their own issue of being very slow and painful to memorize what suits are where on the cycle.

Another complaint I don't see a lot talked about is the limits towards character creating. Unfortunately after the last game they decided to tone the customization down again, by both not allowing DLC characters to borrow from, and going back to the old system where your head and weapon is how powers are given. It's unusual given that in the DS/Vita version of LEGO batman 2, they managed to pull off the perfect balance, giving multiple "power packs" which included a variety of powers that weren't typically bundled together like in the mainline series heroes.

Oh, and don't bother buying the DLC until it's on sale, the quality of levels have decreased (they become single staged) and they'll really only last you an extra few hours at most.\t\t\t\t\t
