\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBatman 3 Beyond Gotham

These Lego games are actually not that bad. I got them to play with my son and he enjoys them a lot and I must say I enjoy them too. They are basic-ish there are puzzles to figure out when traveling through areas to fight the villain and every character has a special ability to assist with those puzzles. The boss fights are unique and interesting for the most part, rather than bosses being tough bullet sponges they are given a weakness that must be exploited to defeat them. Obviously making it easier for kids to do while working on their observational skills.

Original characters with a funny little Lego land twist leads to a fun experience for the kids and adults it you are quick enough to catch on to all the adult humor.

All in All a fun game and I recommend it to both kids and adults.\t\t\t\t\t
