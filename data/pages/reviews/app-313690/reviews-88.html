\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am a big fan of the LEGO games. However, I never could get into the LEGO Batman games. This one is different. This really should have been called "LEGO DC Heroes".

The game folows the tried and tested LEGO formula of playing the plot missions in story mode(play using the characters required by the plot) which unlocks Free Play mode. Free play mode alows you to play as any of the characters you have unlocked and switch between them at will. This allows you to solve puzzles and access areas you couldn't in story mode.

There are several hubs: Batcave, Watchtower, The Moon, Hall of Justice and about 7 latern worlds.

The red bricks are unlocked in the plot missions. Once found, you can buy them in the Trophy room to unlock an "Extra" (an in-game option to turn features on/off).

You can access all of the missions and hubs via the "View Map" option in the menu.

Conan O'Brian acts as your guide. While you can play as Kevin Smith and Adam West if you want. I especially enjoyed the surprise of Mr Mxyzptlk being voiced by Gilbert Gottfried LOL.

CONS (and I am being picky here):
* All of the Red Bricks require Plastic Man
* Half of the achievements require DLC
* The game sometimes starts in a window and requires you ALT+TAB to another progam and back to get full screen.\t\t\t\t\t
