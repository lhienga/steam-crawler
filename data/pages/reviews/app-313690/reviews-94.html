\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA LEGO Batman game with humor and satirical comedy just like the previous two but now with a much large cast and mythos from the comics. You can play as a acid-blood spitting cat or a man who shoots hotdog condiments. There even is Bat-Cow: a damn cow dressed as a bat Damien Wayne (Robin) saved in the New 52 Batman comics and decides to keep as a Bat Family pet. They've included both the essentials such as Deathstroke, Harley, the Justice League, and more, while also leaving space for the more obscure characters. Sadly, you can't play as Conan O'Brien, so don't get your hopes up.

If you love the LEGO games, you'll love this game, since it follows most of the same guideline features.

There is a lot of DLC as well, which is worth purchasing when on sale, since there is one new level for every pack except for the 'Heroines and Villanesses' & 'Rainbow Character' packs.

Getting everything to 100% is quite the challenge and takes time, but its a fun experience to get there.

8.5/10

I suggest purchasing during a sale for the DLC + Base Game incentive.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI remember playing LEGO Star Wars II on my PSP, and LEGO Batman 1 with my friends on my XBOX, taking turns to play whenever one died.
This game brought back such memories. Breaking stuff feels F***in' good when you're mad.

The Story is paced well, and it has mini puzzles and new mechanics.
After you complete the story , there is a bonus level with 1960s NANANANANANANANA..BATMAN,
Extra Levels in DLC packs are short but sure a lot of fun. Grab the Season Pass, it's worth the $$.

You may thing, after that, then what? Well the road to 100% begins, all the minikits, gold bricks and red bricks ! Replaying levels in FREE PLAY. Oh, one thing that amazes me is that I never felt bored even though I'm redo-ing all the missions. Lego managed to make the levels entertaining for free play, Stations that require Plastic Man and The Lantern Corps, all have different animations and ideas, making it very interesting, building a slide , a big shoe, transforming to a crane , a ufo, stuff like that.

Character-wise, every DC character you can think of is in there. Booster Gold? Check. Thunderer? Check. Hush? Check.

There are easter eggs and references all over the game, very very smart. Some you might pick up on, some you might not.

I love the song references as well, though most would not be able to spot them.
"Swamp Thing , I think I love you " - Wild Thing, The Troggs
"Catherine in Wheels" - Several Songs and the Name of a Band, Catherine Wheels
There are more but I will leave that.\t\t\t\t\t
