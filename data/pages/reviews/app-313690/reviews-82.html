\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot that this game Is Atrocious but this game has a few problems with it and it is the worst of the lego batman games

The good
-Great main storyline/voice acting for main storyline one of the strongest in any of the lego games
-Great nods to some of the old batman shows such as having the hitting captions, the old batman cape sneak on the original batman character, making fun of how they did the walking up a wall with a rope scene during the credits
-Other great details such as doing wonder womans/supermans themes when they fly
-Fun worlds to run around on like in mario galaxy type of situations
-Having many of your ability's readily available on the action wheel

The bad
-Inconsistent ability actuation/use - basically you can switch ability's with many characters such as from electric form to flying form, so if you go up to glass for example with batman with the flying suit on you can click the use ability button and it will automatically switch over to the sonar suit instead of having to bring up to the pick out your suit selection wheel and manually selecting the sonar suit however there are many instances where the character will do a animation of shrugging as if they didn't have a suit for the specific ability they would need most notable that I found had this problem was with robin so you would need to use the ability button 2 times once to do the shrugging animation the other to auto switch to the correct suit. there were times that characters would do a shrugging animation for the regular levers that anyone could pull so again you needed to try to pull it 2 times before they would do it
-slow switching Ability suits (it slows and zooms in to capture the change of every suit change)
-Overly long scanning time (it colors the entire screen either red or green) goes on for about 3-4 seconds each time
-Music or should I say lack of music, for example in the main games tron themed area's they have a great techno beat going but in the extra rooms when you are trying to 100% the game none of the tron rooms have music at all. but that is not all. In some of the lantern worlds where you can fly around the globe the music would randomly turn itself way way down and was super quiet, so to counteract that I turned my volume way up the problem with that is the talking/items sounds never got any quieter in fact sometimes it turned itself up (even when I had decided to just leave the game with default volume) so you would get blasted in your eardrums from people talking really loudly
-gas mechanics for flight/light were either useless or annoying
-Inconsistent hitting damages
-Inconsistent ability use times for example the difference between using batman's demolition suit and using cyborg's demo suit is night and day in how slow batman is at least 1/2 as slow to perform all of the same animations
- I used a controller and the right thumbstick controlled the pitch and direction when flying forward but when flying still it no longer controlled the pitch and only functioned as the camera movement so you could only control the pitch with the A and B buttons which was annoying (especially on flying missions and you just barely fly over an objective
-weak voice acting for the additional missions
- they overused a few tropes so it was kinda funny at first but it became really annoying from overuse such as the flash wanting everything and the devs bringing him up in scenes that he really didn't even need to be in to show that he still wanted everything
-navi syndrome........HEY LISTEN you have to blah blah.....HEY....HEY.....HEY....("ok gosh fine I'll do your little mission now cause your so annoying and I know you will shut up after its done") HEY... HEY!!!! (finishes mission)......silence*..........(from somewhere else on the map) HEY!!!! ("GAAAAAAAAHHHHHH")
-Bringing dumb pathing ai along small causeways (and them getting stuck on the ramp of every either ramp) was really annoying the devs choose one of the worst maps to put that rescue mission on

edit: played a bit more and found even more little problems
-there are some terminals that don't have an indicator for them
-on those terminals you cant be too close to them or your correct charactor/costume will get a I dont understand shrug
-some wrong placements of indicators with cheats showing where some things are
-more audio bugs
basically they only kinda polished the main chapters to decent playability then just dumped the rest of the game saying "well its there if you want it"

all in all it just has so many little things wrong with it that it just starts to drive you crazy so I don't recommend it\t\t\t\t\t
