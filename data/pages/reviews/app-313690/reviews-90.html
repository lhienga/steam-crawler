After half an hour in the tutorial level, I learned a few things.
1) The split-screen for the multiplayer was so jarring and discombobulating that my brother quit after 5-10 minutes.
2) There is very little explanation of what to do.
3) Even when I found things to do and moved forward, I had no idea what was going on.
And finally, the controls are such garbage, they make the whole thing that much more of a train wreck.
What really irks me is that I've heard such good things about this series, and that this game is supposed to have a rich, expansing cast and story. But if you can't stand playing it after half an hour, that's a major fail.
Difficult games can be fun -- I just spent the last week charging through Rogue Legacy, and that's a hard game. But it's fair: the controls are tight and WELL EXPLAINED.
This Batman game fails in these and so many other ways -- it is just a mess.\t\t\t\t\t
