\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone whose only played Lego Batman 1, this is a pretty decent sequel.

But personally for me this is not the best one, but had some pros and quite a lot of cons.

PROS:
- Character designs are a chefs kiss (my personal favourites were all the 1966 Batman mini-figures, Batman "The Brave and the Bold", Batman "First Appearance", most of "The Squad" (DLC) characters, Blue Beetle, Bane (Venom Powered) and Killer Croc.
- Larger roster of characters
- Simple yet decent story
- Had the typical kid friendly humour most lego games have

CONS
- Lantern Hub worlds.. even though the small planet idea was pretty innovative, sometimes the camera angles would shake or kinda glitch in certain areas
- I prefer the gibberish/mumble language that the old gen lego games had, I don't mind the regular voice acting but I guess thats just the nostalgia talking
- Nightwing only had his New 52 design (red/black), the classic blue/black suit would be a cool addition
- To get 100% you need the DLC packs, bad for someone whose on a budget
- DLC levels were short and kinda boring
- VR Tron style training is such an odd addition

Overall a great game for kids and a 7/10 for me.\t\t\t\t\t
