\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've played any of the Lego games from recent years you should know what to expect with Lego Batman 3, as it doesnt really deviate much from the usual Lego games formula.

Although this is a Batman game and Batman is the main star here, it is for all intents and purposes a Lego DC game. Pretty much every major DC hero and villain can be unlocked to play as in the game.

The main story is pretty fun and sees the Justice League and some of the villains pitted against Brainiac. The levels are pretty good and of course can be replayed in free play mode using all the character you unlock to try and gather the collectibles like gold and red bricks and minikits.

Theres also a hub section to explore between levels. The hub sections were a bit disappointing as its mainly some locations like the Batcave, Hall of Justice and Watchtower plus some scaled down Lantern planets and these werent as fun to explore as some of the hub areas in the other Lego games I've played.

Theres also DLC levels which are mostly based on some of the more famous DC movies of the last decade like the Dark Knight Trilogy and Man of Steel. They are fun levels although quite short in comparison to the base game levels.

Overall its safe to say if you've enjoyed any of the previous Lego Batman games or Lego games in general, then this one is a pretty safe bet.\t\t\t\t\t
