\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLego Batman 3 feels less like a Batman game and more of a DC game. The story was decent, the graphics looked great, and the DLC is extremely worth it. The issue that I think a lot of people can a agree with me however, is the open world aspect of the game. Basically, in instead of being able to explore the loved places in the DC universe, (like Gotham City or Metropolis) You get to explore the lantern planets. (like Nok and Qward) They all look great visually, they are very small and you never have to spend more than an hour on each planet. This game is alright for people who love Lego Batman, (myself included) but for completionist, this game will feel more like a chore. I rate Lego Batman 3 a 7/10.\t\t\t\t\t
