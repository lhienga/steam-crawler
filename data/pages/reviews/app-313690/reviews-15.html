\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have 100%, so here is my full thoughts. The character/mini-figure collection is one of the best in all Lego Games, with a lot of exclusive and very unique figures that are well designed and look good. For the most part the mission are fun, but some are kind of boring and not too fun to play, but overall the missions are good, not great. The Biggest common complaint though is the open world, and it is hard to critique. On one hand, there are multiple planets and locations which are unique to this game and are fun at first, but unfortunately there is no Gotham City, which is a big disappointment, and one each of the planets, excluding Oa, the camera kind of wraps around the planet so there is this curve which is annoying when flying on those planets. As well as, the planets are all pretty similar and don't really have anything interesting or cool about them. And both the Watchtower and the Bat-cave are good, but feel small and certain areas feel empty. Finally, the DLC in this game isn't as good as others, the figure selection provided with the DLC is great and there are some really cool characters, but the missions feel very short and have a very linear-straightforward design that isnt fun to replay. Overall, this game has some disappointing elements but there is some great levels, character selection, and it is fun, so I would give it like a 7/10. If you want to buy a Lego Batman game, I would recommend Lego DC Supervillians due to its (Arguably) best character selection, great open world/areas, and Good missions.\t\t\t\t\t
