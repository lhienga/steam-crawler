\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am so tired of this game, and I highly doubt I'll ever go back to it. The story was okay, but the hub worlds were terrible to travel through. I think the game expects you to go to the worlds during the story, but there's no incentive to do them, so they're stuck until the very end of your 100% journey. The quests and races are so, so repetitive, the game literally made me hate Daffy Duck.
The suits, while being slightly better than in 1 and 2, take forever to switch to and do the animation every single time. I think being able to skip the animation when using the wheel (I know you can use the triggers, but then you have to cycle through every single suit) would go a long way to make it more bearable.
I can tell they had a good idea with this game, but in execution it was just a pain to sit through and 100%.

Now onto a slightly different topic: DLC
I don't know which game this started with, but around the time of Lego Batman 2 they decided to have DLC characters. Then, around the time of this game's release they realized they could have DLC levels, too. The terrible part of this is that 30 of the 70 achivements (43%!!!) in this game are locked behind the various DLC. This is absolutely ridiculous, and I would have hated to pay full price for all this when it came out, just for the sake of 100%. Nowadays with a reduced price during sales it's a bit more bearable, but still a ridiculous practice. This is one reason I'm glad Skywalker Saga doesn't include DLC levels in its overpriced character packs, because they would probably include achievements behind that, too.

♥♥♥♥. I'm so glad I'm done with this game, I never want to play it again.
Oh, the fan service was cute. Honestly, I wouldn't be surprised if they stuffed it in because they knew their game was bad lol.
Anyways, bad game. Don't buy unless you're like me (mentally insane) and want to complete every Lego game.\t\t\t\t\t
