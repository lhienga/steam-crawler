Overall 6.5/10

I really like the concept and I am sure as they add more features and address the bugs (none of which i found to be game breaking) the score will improve. There were a few things that delighted me in my game play which were unexpected.
--Slapping the first pervy dude that walked into the ladies changing room
--Adopting a dog in the alley that I named Sneaks
--The blackjack mini game in the bar--that is my go to in Vegas so nice little find in game
--The Youtube URL to play actual videos on the TVs in the Gym

The Good:
--Interesting concept and I am not aware of any other game in this space
--Most of the major game play elements seem to be in place. From different staff to

The Bad:
--Too many duplicate NPC models, there can be dozens of people in the gym at any time and this town is full of twins/triplets all with different last names. Loses a bit of my feel of immersion
--The game play while interesting is not challenging, I would love to see a difficulty setting. I feel like I didn't actually have to work all that hard to open up the second side of the gym
--Not being able to fill the juice machine past 60%, it triggered my OCD every time.

The Ugly:
--I couldn't tell from a game play setting how happy my customers were. There was no way to tell without writing things down what my retention rate was, running a business is partially about analytics and this element is missing for a service industry entry in the simulator game space.
-- I was also confused my the different tiers of membership, I think it is based on the number of days they join. Not sure why someone would pay for higher tier memberships for the same services.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't understand all the positive reviews. After a few hours - at the latest when you have equipped the second floor - the game gets very boring. Cleaning the floor, filling up the vending machine and register new members. Boring as hell after a time. There is just no content.

The behaviour of the NPC members is ridiculous and annyoing. Dozens are walking through the rooms, walking through each other, walking through and over people who are doing a workout on the fitness mat. It's just a terrible mess and incredibly unrealistic.

Women are entering and leaving the gym wearing a bikini. People are swimming in the pool wearing their clothes. Peple come to the changing room, dress with their clothes and then move into the sauna or the massage room. It's so ridiculous.
There is a path where all the people walk to the second floor - in both directions at the same time - and all are walking through each other. Again, it looks ridiculous.

Staff people leave the gym at 8pm, while the last members leave at 10pm. So you have to clean up and repair the machines by your own - even you pay the staff. In the morning members arrive at 8am, but stuff only arrives at 9am.

The boxing club adds no value to the sim. You just accept or decline some bids, then a boxing animation take place for about a minute and then it's finished. But there is never a winner. So never win or loose some money.

Vis-a-vis of the gym are some outdoor fitness machines where you can do a kind of competition with some guys, i just could'nt figure out how it works.

Their is a Black Jack game in the bar which i did not play as i don't now how. But i'm wondering what's the sense of a Black Jack game in a Gym Simulator.

For customization of the gym you currently have two (yes, 2) floor tiles and 9 solid colors for the walls. And very few decoration objects, almost nothing.

So the game is very unpolished and gets boring very fast. Almost no content. Not worth the money at this state. Way too early for early access in my opinion. I think the devs are prototyping some features and now have the nerv to sell this as an early access title. But this seems to be very common nowadays.\t\t\t\t\t
