\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far, so good.

This early access game actually has quite a lot in it which is good because most EA games are bare bones, the equipment using animations are pretty good and you got three tiers of equipment to buy and changes you can make and you get more options are you progress in levels.

They recently added an employee that you can hire to run the bar, this makes things easier for players though maybe too easy as if you get staff to clean everything, run the bar and fix everything, all you got to do is enrol members, fill the bar and slap pervs.

You do run out of things to do after a few hours, more equipment is needed in the game to keep things interesting or boredom will hit by Level 9 or 10.

Bugs are being ironed out at a good pace, users are not waiting long at all to get problems sorted out.

Overall this game has had a good start, I just hope the developers don't drop the weights like so many do.\t\t\t\t\t
