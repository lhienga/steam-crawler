\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a lot of fun! I played the game with my friend over discord and we had to really communicate well in order to figure out what the other was looking at. There are moments in the game when you actually get to briefly peer into the other side and see what's going on with them but other than that, you're just listening to your partner's reactions.

The game itself is pretty straight forward and even when you get stuck, it's not that hard to work together (offering each other suggestions) and figure everything out. The environment you're in the game changes overtime and that was my favorite part of it all. You never got to be the other player but you got to experience their play through for a little while. The actual puzzles themselves were fun and inventive. You can also play this game more than once (because you can play as The Past or Present and you can choose different modes) I don't think you can play more than twice but it's still really enjoyable!!! I definitely recommend it to anyone who wants to play a quick co-op game with a friend!\t\t\t\t\t
