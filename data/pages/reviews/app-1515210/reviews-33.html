Having one person work in 3D and the other in 2D is so much fun and makes each players experience extremely unique. I liked also toward the end having that switch so even if you never end up playing the other players experience you still get to see how the 2D version of the game would play out.
I got to play the 3D player for most of the game, I ended up playing this with my boyfriend and we did well and also struggled which made for an average puzzle game experience.
I would recommend to any puzzle game lovers\t\t\t\t\t
