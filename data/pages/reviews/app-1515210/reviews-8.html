The puzzles are very simplistic, and there is a lot of partial repetition in them, i.e.: doing the same puzzle with 4 different shapes.
The story didn't really make an impact on me either.

There are no funny shapes to describe to your partner, no skill/movement based tasks, no funny voices to mimic.
it's not fun to do the actual solving of the tasks like in We Were Here.
On the other hand they also aren't very challenging if you have some experience with these.

I'd recommend it only if you are playing with inexperienced gamers who cant handle a first-person game.\t\t\t\t\t
