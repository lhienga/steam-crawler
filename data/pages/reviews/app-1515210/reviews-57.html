Puzzles have very clear directions and symbols that meant that we were more just talking instead of solving anything. Reminds me of the many puzzles present in the We Were Here series, but many of those puzzles had you translate the information in one way or another before the other player could interpret it, which meant the player was doing a lot more thinking.\t\t\t\t\t
