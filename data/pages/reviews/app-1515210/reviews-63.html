\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI Jumped into The Past Within by chance, looking for a not very expensive game to play with a close friend on our spare time. I'm glad I did, as it has been a very nice and fun experience.
This is a puzzle game in which one of the players is in the future, and another one in the past. Note that there is no internet connection needed for playing "together" as the progress of each of the player needs inputs from the other part of the game constantly, so it is more a game to be played "at the same time" than played "online". However, it is very recommended to use Discord or any other audio tool to be able to communicate with your partner live.
The puzzles are quite nice, not very complicated but fun and have a lot of different interactions. Think of it as a Escape Room game in which each player is in a different room but they need info from the other one to reach the goal.
It is not very long however: We finished in 2 evenings and I whish there could be more, but it was a fun time.\t\t\t\t\t
