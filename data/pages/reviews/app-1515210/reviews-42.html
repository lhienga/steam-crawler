\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Past Within is an asymmetrical co-op point & click puzzle game where you play with your partner in either the past and the future and solve puzzles to uncover Albert Vanderboom’s plan. Like every other Rusty Lake game, I recommend this game, especially since it is multiplayer. The story is fine like always. The artwork is quite improved compared to previous Rusty Lake games. The soundtrack is like the usual creepy but great. The puzzles are not very difficult, but still entertaining to solve. The main downside is the short content. The game could be finished in around 2 hours. 7/10 overall.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was an incredibly creative new re-imagining of the Rusty Lake formula for 2 players. It was difficult not to get totally absorbed into this virtual escape room! My wife and I had a blast describing our sides of the screen with enough precision to identify what clues were needed to advance the others'.

The puzzles were toned down a bit compared to the usual Rusty Lake, which suited the 2 player gameplay - Imagine it would be boring to wait too long on one teammate getting stuck.

Rare are games that inspire such intensive communication between players in a low stress environment. Playing the Past Within felt like playing an enjoyable social board game, but with all the narrative advantages and thrills of a digital medium. Couldn't recommend more for couples or groups of friends playing each side.

Absolute 10/10 for a 2 player narrative puzzle game.\t\t\t\t\t
