\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very well made 2 player coop puzzle game. You don't even need to connect to a network or the internet, you just load the game and communicate to a friend using voice comms like discord. You play your way through a narrative where each player is separated through time and space, one in the future and one in the past. You solve puzzles by relaying information to your partner which helps them progress their game, and they do the same back. It mixes a few different style together extremely well, 2D art drawn with a creepy aesthetic, and 3D scenes that allow you to view objects from different angles, both players switch between them which adds variety to the gameplay.

It's short and fairly easy, but it's also cheap, so it fills a niche for this kind of game, If you're a fan of 2 player coop puzzle games then this is a fun little experience. If you want some more substantial with much harder puzzles but the same kind of genre then consider playing the series of games called "We Were Here" the first in the series is free on steam and well worth trying.\t\t\t\t\t
