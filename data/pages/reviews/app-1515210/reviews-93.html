\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA nice little riddle-coop-game for two people. Would recommend if you're looking for something small to pass a nice evening with a partner.
A little creepy at times, but not too spooky for me - and I usually don't like the horror genre, so it's probably fairly mildly creepy :).
I liked the simple storyline and the general game mechanic. It's not too repetitive, but still recognizable and generally communicatable - which is very important in this game.
The first part of the game is very focused on exploration and finding clues, while the second part gets more routined and becomes more of an exchange of information between the players. That second part was also where I would see some smaller design flaws as there were some moments where I felt that I could do next to nothing while my partner was continuing on his side of the journey. It would be nice if there were more things to do (non riddle-related) that one can do to fill the time.
