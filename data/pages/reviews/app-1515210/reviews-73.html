\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my personal choice for the "Most Innovative Gameplay" award!

The concept itself is so intriguing, a co-op puzzle game that requires no internet connection (well except for your video call, but thats not the games resposibillity) and you can only solve puzzles by having the other give you the clues to solve it. What an amazing experience!

This game is significantly longer than most other Rusty Lake games, while it takes maybe 1 to 2 hours at most for the other games in the series, this one can take upwards of 5 - 6 hours if you try to do everything. and for the small asking price this is definitely worth all of the money! Great co-op puzzle horror game to play with a friend!\t\t\t\t\t
