\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGoing to go a little against the grain, only because there unfortunately isn't a neutral vote. The Past Within is almost a good game. There's a lot of cleverness. You don't need any network connection for this game, a simple discord call will work, but it's a really neat idea using your partner's paired info to unlock your game further and progress. The biggest problem is the puzzles. They're just not challenging or particularly clever. They also get quite repetitive once you, for instance, get the idea of putting in a code, they pointlessly make you repeat that 4-5x in one puzzle quest, let alone, coming back to the same puzzle again and again. In some ways, they're more tasks than puzzles. I feel an iteration on this game would be amazing if they thought through some of this stuff a little better, it would make for a fun and fantastic game. It just kind of missed the mark the way it is currently.\t\t\t\t\t
