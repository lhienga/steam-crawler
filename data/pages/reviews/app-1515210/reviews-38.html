\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great little co-op game that is pretty economically priced to boot. Definitely worth picking up if you have someone to play along with. Do note, co-op is necessary, it is not an option.

Pros:

1) An interesting, somewhat creepy story. Definitely unique and different.

2) Intuitive controls that are fun to explore. The game expects you to poke around and figure things out with the help of your teammate. You will absolutely have to work in tandem and I loved the experience.

3) Even though this is co-op, you aren't actually connected to each other, meaning that you can even play with someone across the world without having to bother about latency of the server. All you would need would be some communication channel to talk through.

Cons:

1) The game is quite short. I finished it in an hour and a half. You could get a second play-through in by swapping around the characters, but that would still be 3 hours at best. Of course, your time to complete may vary. Personally I do not see this as a significant con, especially since the game is so reasonably priced, but it is something to keep in mind.\t\t\t\t\t
