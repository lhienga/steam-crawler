I played this game with my Husband, who generally only likes to play games like Farming Simulator or Two Point Hospital. I'd read the puzzles were pretty tame, so I figured that'd be OK for him, since he's not huge on puzzle games.

I didn't expect him to love it so much! He seriously was smiling the entire time and I could tell he was really enjoying himself. The puzzles aren't difficult if you're a puzzle game player, but they were just challenging enough for him, and it had a few horror/scary elements - not enough to be considered a horror game, but just a perfect amount for someone who doesn't like to be scared.

If you're an avid puzzle gamer, this is a nice, casual break. If you struggle with puzzle games, this will be perfectly challenging for you. Great game!\t\t\t\t\t

With one in the Past and the other in the Future, communication is key to solving the puzzles and progressing through the story. The game's eerie atmosphere and unsettling motifs add to its charm, though it may be more appreciated by fans familiar with the Rusty Lake series. The asynchronous puzzle system is ingeniously designed, requiring players to relay information and coordinate their actions. While the game occasionally slows down when waiting for the other player, the overall experience is delightful and enjoyable. The Past Within offers a cozy and immersive co-op adventure that will appeal to escape room enthusiasts and Rusty Lake fans alike.
