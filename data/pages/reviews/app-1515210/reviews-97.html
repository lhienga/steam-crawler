\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like the Rusty Lake games in general, read no further, this is more of the best of the series, grab it as soon as you have someone to play it with. You will need to remember some info from other games to fill out the achievements fully, so if you don't want to need to do an extra runthrough, maybe brush up on your Rusty Lake trivia (or searching skills). If you don't know the Rusty Lake series, check out the free entries in the series, see if it is your cup of tea, then apply that decision to this game.

Either way, this is an unshared-screen co-op game where each of you receives different information, but that information needs to be applied in the other person's world. You don't need the games to be linked by data, but you do need the ability to talk (or type, but I recommend voice) to your partner. The replay value is two games (so you see both sides of it), plus maybe another couple of runs to finish out your achievements. Well worth it for six bucks, IMO.\t\t\t\t\t
