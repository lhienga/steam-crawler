\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat can I say? Rusty Lake has done it again but now with co-op play!

Very Important!
This is the last entry (as of Sept 23 2023) of the Rusty Lake story, so if you haven't played any previously, I would highly recommend starting out with Cube Escape Collection which has the first 9 games of the series. Continue from there. By release date is a good rhythm to follow, even though it may not be in chronological order.

There aren't really challenging "puzzles"to solve but rather the puzzle here is how your and your player 2's communication skills are. Both need tp be aware of the surroundings to be able to solve the instructions or solve the clues given. Using just your words to guide your player 2 that's the real challenge.

Story wise, it does follow the story from the Cube Collection and previous games so it does connect with the story line although I am unsure if it is canon or not. If it is, it does give you more insight about the whole picture and about certain characters.

If you are a fan I highly recommend you play this (as both sides) to get the full experience. Each side has a different perspective and "story". It also gives you a secondary option at the start so you don't get repeats of the puzzle formulas on your second run through.

If you can't find a player 2 there is a Discord channel at the beginning of the game to find someone who will play with you.

100% must play (same with all the previous games in the series)\t\t\t\t\t
