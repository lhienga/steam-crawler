\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Past Within is a short puzzle game split into two paths. The answers to the puzzles in your pathway can only be found in the other pathway, so you have to play with a partner and maintain good communication throughout.

I thought this was a really interesting concept and played it with my partner. We had a decent time - I'd say we enjoyed our time - but I don't think to the point where I'd enthusiastically recommend it to anybody else.

There's a spooky underlying plot which definitely holds the user's attention, but the puzzles are just a bit too simple. Essentially, Player 1 finds a lock which needs a number code. Player 2 finds a code somewhere on their screen and reads it out. Player 1 enters the code and you move on to the next part. Ultimately it felt like there were very few actual puzzles in the game - you are basically just reading to each other.\t\t\t\t\t
