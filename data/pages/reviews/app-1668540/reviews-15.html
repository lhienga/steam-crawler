\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI definitely do not recommend playing this game if you are new to the Legend of Heroes series, it would be liking skipping several seasons into a TV show. If you want to start from the beginning, start with Trails in the Sky. But you can start the series with either Trails From Zero, Trails of Cold Steel 1 or Trails of Cold Steel 3 and make your way from there. Each of those start separate story arcs that are all interconnected, but they can make for good starting points for those wanting to get into the series.

I have a little mixed feelings with this one, on one hand I loved it. A good continuation and closure to the Crossbell Independence storyline. But on the other, the post-game stuff left me feeling a little drained in the end. I was surprised how long it just kept going, and honestly the final little unlock thing after beating the post-game big boss left me feeling a little disappointed because it all ended up being just a visual novel-esque cutscene that felt like it dragged on for way too long.

It came out of nowhere too because all of the other memory unlock scenes were full cutscenes with even voiced parts, which made the final one all the more disappointing. Especially for how much work you have to do to get it.

But I still love the series and look forward to playing more in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
