\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSince this is the worst possible starting point in the series, I'll make this a quick one for those familiar with the franchise. All 9 games that precede it, in fact.

This game's one of the best in the entire series.

Each of the three routes are great. The mysteries are intriguing and all 3 protagonists get very satisfying conclusions to their character arcs. The new characters introduced are also incredibly likeable. (And be sure to read the in-universe book Three and Nine, accessible from the Backstory page on the main menu before getting into the game itself. Trust me, it'll pay off.)

Sidequests are kept to a minimum, with the game feeling a lot like Sky the 3rd in both progression and content. Whether this is a positive or a negative will depend on the person, but as for me, it's made the game feel a lot more focused and the story's pacing is excellent.

But for real, if you're a Trails fan, chances are Reverie will not disappoint. I personally consider it to be the 4th best game in the series (in other words it's amazing) and it made me excited for the series' future after Cold Steel 4 left a bad taste in my mouth. It's not without its hiccups (Lloyd Act 1 and how the postgame is structured come to mind), but it's overall a fantastic addition to the series, and I can't wait for Kuro's official announcement (even though the NISA store page is already up.)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've played /all the other Trails games/ in the Crossbell and Erebonia arcs, this is pure fanservice pron. If you haven't, don't bother.

All your characters from all the games come together for a sort of 'eh' new threat, but it's really just an excuse to close things down. If you've played Trails in the Sky 3rd you know what to expect, though this is much more expansive than that was. There are SO many side stories. Every NPC you can remember (and more) are here. There are even minigames like a 3d shooter with Arisa, Musette, Juna, Altina, etc. as magical girls. There is so much Pom Pom Party. And lots of optional side story narrative arcs if you were wondering what happened to certain characters.

So if you want to see all the Cold Steel, Zero, and Azure stuff tied up (with cameos from Sky peeps) you really can't miss this, right? You're already one of us. One of Us. ONE OF US.

Edit: Having now beat the game, everything I said earlier still is true. And just so you know, the game is not over at the 'ending' - when you beat the main meh threat, you have to beat the reverie corridor to get the real ending(s) and to set up for the next games in Calvard. And to see grown up Tita and Renne! Like I said, fanpron.\t\t\t\t\t
