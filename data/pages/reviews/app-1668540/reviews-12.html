\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGameplay is good but it's still all rehashed from all the previous Cold Steel games.

There are some annoying bugs and mistakes that still have not been solved like inumerous description text of items, quartz and crafts having wrong text of what they actually do.

Soundtrack is fine. Falcom sound team used to be better though. Many uninspired songs on crucial moments or just lack of stylistic variety like how most battle songs are pretty much all hard guitar j-rock every single time and variety is scarce.

The story however, is GARBAGE.
This game could literally not exist in the timeline and absolutely nothing would change in this world.
DIalogue is repetitive, tons of characters that have no personality and no time or space to develop. Lazy and unnecessary plot, asspulling, rule breaking of current and previous established rules of previous games and problems being solved CONSTANTLY with power of friendship alone.

Basically just lazy, uninspired and lame writing from begining to end. I'm seriously disappointed with the path this franchise is taking as it's going completely downhill ever since this Cold Steel arc started.

It's like they're sacrificing everything to make the most cliche anime for teenagers with TONS of fan service and harem elements instead of actual plot and world building like the older games were about.

I'm honestly just giving up being a fan of this franchise. Will check out Kuro no Kiseki when it comes out in the hopes that a new arc with new characters and gameplay will make things fresh.... Not putting my hopes up though, this saga just destroyed any excitement I had for Kiseki....\t\t\t\t\t

12 out of 10.

Legend of heroes series has always come packed with a lot of story, but this one? The story overwhelms the game play which usually I would be against it but, the story is good so all is forgiven even the fact that i fell asleep while watching some stories because its too long.
I started on Hard difficulty but it didn't last long until I switched to Normal. This is because in the beginning you'll be thrown into battle with default level and equipment and no option to grind levels. It is fair until there's some time-limited event that earns you point which seems unlikely to clear. But, in the later game, Normal difficulty became too easy after I could optimize the gears to suit my game play. The game comes with auto-battle function but really, with the new added system I could just one hit all mobs so I haven't had the chance to do any auto-battles.
