\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had been playing Motorsport Manager for a couple of years now, and having a blast with it. Once I got my new laptop, I figure I would give this game a try. For the most part, I am really enjoying this game. The graphics and sounds are really good. Since it is licensed by Formula 1, I don't have to download mods to get the real drivers, teams, tracks, and cars, like MM.

However, it is not without its cons. The graphics are really good except for crashes. The crash sequences remind me of games from the 1990s. All a crash shows is some small squares coming off the car as it hits another car or the wall. If the car has front wing damage, it will show the car with a damaged front wing. But for the most part, the car looks like it is in the same condition after hitting the wall as it does sitting in the garage.

Another thing I would like to see is to simulate the rest of a session. I would like to be able to start a practice session, or race, and then in the middle of the session simulate the rest. Also, I would like to be able to simulate a race.

I would also like to be able to save clips of the race, i.e. overtakes and crashes or have a highlights reel at the end of the race.

The last thing this game doesn't have that is included in MM is a listing of the Grid before the start of the race. It lists the qualifying rankings, but not a revised Grid after the Grid Penalties are applied.

Overall, I enjoy this game. On a scale of 1 to 10, I would give it a high 7 or an 8. Better more realistic crash sequences would definitely make me rate the game higher.\t\t\t\t\t
