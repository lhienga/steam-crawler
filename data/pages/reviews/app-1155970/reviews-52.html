\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis seems like a game I would like. It wasn't.

I just got tired of all the reading, and felt like I was thrown into a world with little real knowledge of just how powerful and experienced my character was, and what my purpose and goals were. The game would make mention of common fantasy staples, like goblins and griffons and the like, but they seem to mean slightly different things in this world. I see a group of creatures blocking my path and I can't tell if this is something I can read headlong into and slaughter or if I need to start sweating bullets and look for an exit strategy.

I liked everything in this game. Honestly. Just not together. Take the individual elements out, one at a time, and seat them in another game, and you've got gold. Together it felt lacking to me. Show me a location, but then let me hover over things to interact with them, so I know exactly what I'm doing. Give me an indication of my chances of success or failure, or at least a general confidence level, before I take actions.

This game has an audience, and those who will love it, will love it. Don't let my poor review dissuade you, but instead make this choice to purchase deliberately, after consideration of what others have said. Get a second opinion. This game didn't catch my interest, and all the reading kept putting me to sleep.\t\t\t\t\t
