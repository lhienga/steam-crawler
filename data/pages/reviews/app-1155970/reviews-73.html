\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally enjoyed playing through this game. It has some fantastic world building that really draws you in, and it has that can't-put-it-down quality that you get from a good book.

It plays mostly like a puzzle game in my opinion: the jist of each settlement is that "you turn up - you get told you need to earn the trust before they'll help with anything - you do odd jobs to earn trust to get info", with most of these jobs requiring you to carefully plan your traversal of the map in order to preserve your health/resources/time - hence the puzzle element. This can feel a little tired during a second play through, though, as you go back and forth again and again.

For anybody who enjoys a narrative-driven, thoughtful game, that is set in an engrossing game world: this is a must-play\t\t\t\t\t
