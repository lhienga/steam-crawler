\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCommits the classic adventure game blunder of requiring a B.S. in Moon Logic to figure out some quests
-Dolmen wants you to type 'trapdoor' instead of 'hidden door', 'fake door', 'hidden passage', 'secret passage', 'secret door', or even 'keyhole'.
-A spear is needed to make a quest less dangerous. You can recklessly fight a mob across the map, and if you "win" you get a spear (why would you do that? The game repeatedly drives home the point that unnecessary risks can be lethal and you don't know what you're going to get until you've already done it), or lie to someone to get it in time for the quest (you have no knowledge that you'd get a spear that way, and the game implies that your reputation is important, thereby discouraging lying)

Uses Pokemon "open world" design, where it looks open, but most of the paths except for the pre-determined one are either hard locked (come back later when the shiggus diggus leaves) or soft locked behind requirements so expensive that it makes zero sense for the player (doing a dangerous encounter exhausts you to the point you have to rest a few days before you can venture forth again, or you spend all of your money on something not worthwhile.)

The general time limit on the game is incredibly stupid. It encourages the player to "solve" the game instead of experiencing the open world naturally.

That being said, there are quite a few likable characters (nobody trusting you initially gets tedious), the world is decently fleshed out and presents a novel scenario, although if I squint really hard I can see this game being The Witcher fanfic (roadwardens are treated an awful lot like Geralt in Witcher 3). But once you see the man behind the curtain, typically if you die and savescum and play around with different choices, the game falls apart.

Overall, I wouldn't recommend to most people, especially not those on the fence about adventure-choice games

As an aside, what's up with all of these RPG games with glowing overwhelmingly positive reviews being a mixed bag of turds and truffles? I swear it's getting worse.\t\t\t\t\t
