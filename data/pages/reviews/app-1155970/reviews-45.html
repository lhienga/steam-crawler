\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMid

It's a sorry state of gaming that something this... mid can be so well loved.

The art style is cohesively bland and completely forgettable. There's zero animation as well, if that matters to you.

Some of the word puzzles are good and some go full bizzaro logic for no reason.

The worldbuilding is a decent mix of normal fantasy staples and new stuff, but almost all the new stuff is pretty forgettable.

The emerging story is... okay?

It's not very subtle about it's representation. I've played for about 4 hours and I've met at least 4 homosexual characters with more being expressly mentioned, am trying to find a lost trans person, and even played matchmaker for a woman who wanted more kids but was lesbian (??). Bear in mind that's out of roughly 30 named characters so far. It's like one in five people on the peninsula are some flavor of the rainbow. If that bothers you, stay away from this title.

Combat is not very mechanical, it's more about tactics and positioning. That said, I've scummed a few encounters and the internal logic is all over the place. Narrative-wise you're warned to avoid risk, but in some really tough-seeming encounters the best option is to sprint in screaming and swinging your axe like a madman. Other times it seems like an easy fight but you get dunked on unless you run away.

The religion system is weird and doesn't really make a ton of sense to me. There's all these factions, but they share a holy symbol, but they don't get along, but there aren't holy wars.... I have no clue what's going on with it honestly. It's even a tracked stat on your character sheet but I've yet to see it matter in a choice.

The economy takes a lot of management to come out on top. That said, if you use the fish cages and don't care about being dirty (it really doesn't seem to matter too much) and make sure you sleep somewhere with a free option, you can amass money without toooo much difficulty.

Idk fam. Maybe my expectations were too high. Just feels really mid. I'll try to update this when/if I finish it\t\t\t\t\t
