\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWOW, as soon as I bought this game I played nothing else. I never played older text based RPGs but once I finished this game I immediately was searching the web for anything even remotely similar.
The makers do a wonderful job of creating an engrossing atmosphere. I was thinking about the connections of different people and recalling the lore that characters had passed to me at my day job. I couldn't wait to get home and become one with the world.
I appreciate the options to put a time limit on the game which makes it a little more strategic and also the option to fully focus on the story. The options to choose different goals and also belief systems for your character are so cool as well and is fun to see what traits you gain from this later on.
Once I let this play settle I'm definitely starting a new game with a whole new character personality. This is one of the few games that makes me want to achievement hunt and explore every inch of it. I can't wait to see the developer do more games like this.\t\t\t\t\t
