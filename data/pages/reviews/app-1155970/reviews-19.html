Probably you'd remember what you learned over multiple character deaths / games.

Make tons more engineers, and not necessarily the LGBT kind either, more like the ones who can get their hands dirty. Humanity has to develop all the tech to get off this rock before we have the inevitable species-killing dice-roll events, and a bunch of defeatist ideology will NOT help us in that. /end rant

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably one of the best text adventures I've played, definitely up there with the Sabres series if you are familiar. Pondering change, legacy, ambition, your time spent in this life and does any of it matter. Do you want to be a hero, are you out to make money? Looking for a new home? Your characters class, story and goals are chosen through prompts and really draws you in to who your warden is as a person and how they might act, if you're big into roleplaying. If not, these are still important to ponder for your characters end goal in their journey, aside from the duties of a roadwarden.

Personally I think the 40 day time limit adds a lot to the game, but they added custom difficulty so you can turn that off if it's not your jam. Plus there's a console you can cheat with to your hearts content if you get stuck or otherwise need help to push through an obstacle.

If it's not clear, absolute 10/10 from me. Pick it up if you enjoy text based adventures.\t\t\t\t\t
