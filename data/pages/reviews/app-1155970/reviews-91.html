I think I probably should have played this game over several days, instead of two 6+ hours sitting. I was too much in a rush to finish this game. Take this game slow. It requires a lot of reading, because the game asks for open ended inputs. Very fun game.

11/28/22

This game has gotten such great reviews, and while playing this game I got a really good impression.
Ultimately though, it falls short in the same way divinity 2 falls short.

Its decision making seemed very nebulous and lacked any impact. I've only ever run away from creatures. never really had a chance to fight them.
I was hoping as a mage, It would open up more interesting choices, but all my spells did . . . nothing
Fighting anything is a bad idea. And the resting and eating mechanic felt extremely cumbersome. I just wanted to make interesting decisions, and the game didn't have as many options as I would like.

I have played, For the King, where you get a sense of how your decisions are going to impact the future. The charm of this game is lost with how nebulous it is. You travel to a town to be given more quests, only to be impeded by monsters in which you have to help more people to solve. I recommend playing this on easiest setting and just exploring.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're familiar with 1980s roleplaying gamebooks, in particular the Lone Wolf series, you'll know what to expect; this is basically an illustrated text adventure with strongly emphasised (if mechanically simple) RPG elements. Compared to the books of old, the digital format allows this game a higher degree of complexity and nuance in the outcomes of your decisions, particularly in relation to the various characters and factions you encounter, but it is still straightforward enough that plot progression usually feels as simple as "go to page 57", and dice rolls are very rare to the point of being negligible. All of that makes this game highly accessible, some obtuse moments aside, and the writing strikes a good balance between descriptive and dramatic. Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
