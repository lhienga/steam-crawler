\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely write reviews, but some games are just that good I had to.

Amazing Game, a must buy for anyone interested in a great story game/RPG/text-based adventure game/ or maybe a more relaxing game to wind down in the evenings.

I ended up loving this game so much I binged the entire thing in two days. I got immersed in the world like I never have before in an RPG, i cared about these people and the world and stuck to my roadwarden's morals and I didn't do the usual RPG game thing of rapidly changing my personality to get the best out of a situation. My Roadwarden dreamed of being a hero, and a hero they would become. I made them act heroically and protect the roads, and they never told lies or betrayed the trust of others and it felt great. I was even rewarded by this near the end of my playthrough where after enough Heroic acts I felt as if I could be called a Hero and got a permanent health increase.

The music and sound design is extremely well done, it has catchy light tunes that aren't too in your face but make for a very nice passive listening. Sometimes in certain areas more enviromental sounds will play like animals far off in the woods and it really plays into how untamed this area is compared to the others and that you should be more careful around here.

I really appreciated that you build the world in the game. Throughout the game people will ask your Roadwarden of where they come from and you will get various options to choose from, I remember there is a person that asks you of an old Inn in the city down south and you can say it's still there, or has been gentrified or it has taken a dive in quality and reputation and is one of the roughest parts in the city. This is great as you can choose the world you live in, is it cruel and the poor are downtrodden or things not so bad in the south. And at the end of the game when you return down south you will experience all these things you mentioned, it's a nice callback and makes the world feel very familiar to you,

Honestly, I can't even think of anything to nitpick about, there was a fly sound effect for like 5 minutes that was a bit annoying but it really is a perfect game and for that price you really can't beat it. I've gotten nearly double it's worth in playtime and I'm already debating going back in for a 2nd time.

Couldn't recommend more, PICK THIS GAME UP!!!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI booted up this game in normal mode, where it has a 40 day time limit, before getting an hour in and realizing I needed to play the game on the easy mode just so I could enthrall myself in this world without a time limit(My final count was 57 days). The writing for the world itself, its creatures, its towns and groups therein, and most of all on its characters is excellently done, if not a little overbearing at times.

I genuinely felt sucked into this world for the 10 hours that I spent with it. I was amazed at how realistically everything in this fantasy world melded together, and the ending I received felt both earned, but harsh. Everything from the tiny secrets of The Peninsula to the greater revelations at the end of quest lines felt fantastic.

I can't recommend this enough to people who love good writing and stories, especially those of you who love good character writing.



Also the music was pretty great.\t\t\t\t\t
