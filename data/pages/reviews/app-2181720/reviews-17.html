\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game as of 22 nov 2022, the day of the 0.1.0 weapon fusion release.

And overall i think this game has a great baseline since this still is a beta release. but its definitely lacking in content and has some balancing issues. Regardless however for the 4$ price point i would still recommend it for a few hours of fun.

there are 7 characters, but at the moment they all play fairly the same as only 2 of them have passive effects, the only difference between the rest is the starting weapon.

You can diversify your playstyle through talents and character specific glyphs, however the summons/minion talents currently lack synergy with any available items and the ignite talents are over tuned. as they currently let you afk through the game. this is mostly due to there being little healing available within the game and the ignite heal is really good since all attacks apply it and then it spreads on its own. which in turn overshadows any of the other talents available since they are pretty much always a worse choice.

The games current strong point is its aesthetics, visuals are amazing, music is great, game runs smooth overall (except for a screen filled with DOT then the game starts to lose frames).

Altough the visuals are very pleasing they currently are also quite cluttered later on during a run. the original VS was alot simpler and didnt really suffer from this. but in this game its faster paced and it expects you to utilize a dash to dodge enemies. so its important to be able to see them properly which gets tricky at times.

i would also like to recieve all my item upgrades before lvl ups and chests start giving me gold and gems. rather then getting 15k gold while still carrying lvl 2 items. chests near the end of the run also dont open properly its either delayed or you have to walk over them mutliple times to open.

And thats pretty much all there currently is, i mean its a VS style game so its nothing too complex.

one last note: currently the steam store screenshots are showing content that isnt (atleast to my knowledge) available in the game yet. the characters only have 1 glyph instead of the 3 shown in the screenshots and the second map with the dead trees isnt playable yet either. the in game difficulty slider goes up to 10, but only 4 are currently available.\t\t\t\t\t

Overall its already good and a steal for this price, I'll happily continue playing and eagerly waiting for new content as development unfolds.
8/10
