\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far very impressive. Great pixel-art graphics and a very nice gothic artstyle, if you like Castlevania, you will love it.
Gameplay is based on the survivors type, pretty fun, can hook you up for many hours. Only difference is you don't need a specific passive items to evolve a weapon, just level the weapon itself to 5. Soundtrack is neat too.


Edit:
Game keeps adding content, now there are weapon evolutions, but unlike the other games in this genre you combine 2 weapons, not a weapon and a support item. Some weapon can only be obtained from the secret shopkeeper, which spawns on random map-locations and sells weapons/potions depending on the number of slain enemys 1K for potions(permanent buffs to stats), 5K for the unobtainable weapons- Fire crown, Frost sword , etc. they also added a
Christmas event- it start to snow and new enemy types spawn, pretty neat.Lastly you can find
4 eggs hidden on any map, once you get them you permanently unlock a small pet ( it gives you a passive bonus and it follows you).

Can recommend the game even more! Still the best looking special effects/spell effects survivors game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've only played a bit of this, but I can see the potential here. The meta upgrades (those that are permanent between runs) are quite costly, the difficulty curve is VERY high (Barely killing 3-4 enemies an attack out of swarms by minute 8) and while if this were a finished game I'd drop it quickly, it's very clear this is in the barebones stage of development and with some time and effort it could be a great game. Visuals are stunning, and the beginning of the game feels very satisfying to play, but again by minute 8 it feels like you're barely doing anything. It would be cool if it were possible to beat the game barebones without any meta upgrades, even if it were extremely difficult. For those who like the bullet heaven genre (survivors-likes), keep an eye on this one. Might not be the best out there now, but it could be later.\t\t\t\t\t
