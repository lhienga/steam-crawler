Real Potential
This game is constantly being compared with Vampire Survivors and most of those comparisons are pretty accurate. However, I believe there are certain qualities Scarlet Tower has that will set it apart from other games in the genre if it continues to be developed with players, quality, and balance in mind.

For an early-access title, this game is pretty polished. I have yet to encounter any game-breaking errors/bugs. There are certain Quality of Life changes, game balance updates, and feature robustness that I think most players would happily welcome. However, for this stage of development, these are only small nuisances in my opinion.

Overall, wonderful game.
I've really enjoyed my time so far and hope this game continues to be developed in a positive direction.\t\t\t\t\t
