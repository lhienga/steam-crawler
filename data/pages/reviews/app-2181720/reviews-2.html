\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will recommend this game with a bit of a warning. This game is fun, and lots of things to unlock and explore, but...

You will have very little clue what is going on. You will die and not know what killed you and you will get no explanation.

Early game is fun while you check things out and think you will get used to the game play. The amount of reading you need to do for skills and mastery to try and make sense of how to build will deter some folks. You can blindly play for a while, but it will not get you far that way.

Late game becomes nothing but a scramble to try and pad yourself with HP and shields. If you do not focus this every run you will lose. If you focus it every run you will still lose a lot. You need a stupid amount of luck to get the right weapons to drop to get you the right mastery access. You have 3 rerolls and some runs they don't like to drop at all.

Banish doesn't work which makes it much harder. Many of the passives seem to be bugged, don't work, or maybe I just have no f-ing clue what is going on still. The mini-map will do its best to deceive you while you sprint to something you need and find out that it isn't actually there when it suddenly vanishes from your map.

It seems like it will come down to grinding resources for upgrading runes (and there is no health rune, so suck it if you think that will help with the HP problem), which is your only way to become stronger after you unlock all the basic things, which happens quite quickly.

I find myself less eager to keep doing runs knowing almost everything relies on luck, and the passive for a rebirth during a run has never been functional. If you like this style game you may enjoy it, but do not expect a game anywhere near finished.\t\t\t\t\t
