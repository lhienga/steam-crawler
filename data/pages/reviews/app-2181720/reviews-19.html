In its initial state the game was difficult to recommend. Problems with hitboxes and drops, general bugs etc.

But the past couple of updates have really this game into a much better state and after having had some more time to play around in it I can safely recommend this game.
While its not wildly different from other games in the genre, its got a very great aesthetic and a set of characters drawn from previous games from the Dev and I expect we'll get more with later updates. Really enjoy the high mobility aspect of this game, enjoy that enemies have a sort of heaviness and momentum to them that I haven't seen in other games which as a kind of novelty to training them around the map. While more will no doubt come, its pretty great as it is. And the dev is one of the most responsive Ive come across. Excited to see where it goes from here.\t\t\t\t\t
