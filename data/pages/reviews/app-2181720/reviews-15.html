\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy the horde survival type games and this one is great. I recently have conquered everything that Vampire Survivors has to offer to date and wanted to try something similar but different. I saw Never Nathaniel playing a bit on YouTube and decided to give it a try as another $5 game in this genre. I know it is early access so there is still much more content I am hoping to see but what we have so far is really good! I like how the mobs tend to behave as they are not so much just directly rushing at you and have a bit of a delayed response to your movement. This creates this kind of back and forth sling shot style movement which is a little different compared to other games. I like the overall appearance and theme with the Gothic styling with a touch of anime.

All the characters are distinct with their own personal weapon and unique abilities and passives which encourages varied play styles across the characters. The power up tree is the typical tiered buff system that is applied to all characters you play but the talent tree is quite nice. There are up to 5 tiers of defensive talents and up to 5 tiers of offensive talents with 3 selections in each tier that can each be brought to level 5. This, for me, is where the creativity of desired play style and variety of character nuances are combined in a way that makes this survival game stand out. There are quite a bit of ways you can put these talents together and also min/max to each particular character depending on what they do.

Another part I liked were the weapon evolutions and fusions, similar to those in Vampire Survivors except I don't think accessories are required to evolve weapons, however, combinations of max leveled weapons will fuse in the same way. This is still in it's infancy but has a lot of potential to be fun in a way that I am familiar with.

Final verdict of my first impression: Very fun! Definitely worth the $5 if you are curious and if you are on the fence about it maybe just keep checking up on the updates as content is added, don't give up on this game just yet!\t\t\t\t\t
