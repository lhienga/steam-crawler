\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want to play Chaos on Immortal Empires right now you pretty much have to choose Warriors of Chaos, since the monogod daemon factions are obviously not adjusted to fit (technology and mechanics don't fit IE map). If you have WoC from old games then they are quite fun, but the new WoC lords are the choice for IE monogod representation. While they receive different and sometimes weaker versions of the monogod daemon mechanics, most of the mechanics work far better combined with the vassal mechanic for WoC (playing with diplomacy from slaanesh/tzeentch, positive plague effect spreading for vassals).

While you don't receive completely new units, the lords have significant technology bonuses that help greatly differentiate their flavors of Chosen/Knights/Aspiring Champions/Exalted Heroes. In particular, Festus has a particularly monstrous set of bonuses for Aspiring Champions, Chosen of Nurgle, and Nurgle Exalted that really makes them feel like an unstoppable slow moving tide of inevitable death on the battlefield.\t\t\t\t\t
