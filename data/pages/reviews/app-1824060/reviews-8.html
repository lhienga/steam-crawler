\r\n\t\t\t\t\tComing alongside a free and much needed update to bring the Warriors of Chaos faction from the first game up to par with the fidelity and mechanics standards of Warhammer 3, the Champions of Chaos DLC introduces four new Legendary Lords - one for each Chaos God - representing the Warriors of Chaos. Alongside a good number of new units to utilize, and interesting faction mechanics for the Warriors of Chaos after their rework, I highly recommend it. You recruit armies of mortal followers, and upgrade them as they gain battle experience - eventually becoming elite Chosen of the Chaos Gods. You spread corruption and attempt to gain favor for your chosen Chaos God, gaining the ability to recruit various units from the Chaos Gods rosters - such as Plaguebearers or Bloodletters - over time. While playing your main goal is to plunge the world into chaos while taking over dark fortresses and vassalizing Norscan tribes and others along your path to use as minions.

All in all, I would say it's a very quality DLC. Compared to the usual Total War Warhammer Legendary Lord DLCs which only include 2 Lords, I would say that this one is definitely more substantial overall in terms of content and quality. If you're the kind of person I am and is willing to put hundreds of hours or more into these kinds of games, then this DLC is definitely one I would highly recommend before most of the others for all three games. The only ones I would suggest before would be perhaps the Taurox/Oxyotl Legendary Lord DLC, or one of the faction DLCs themselves (such as the Wood Elves or Tomb Kings, or even the Vampire Coast which are quite a lot of fun).

If on the other hand you aren't interested in playing much of this game, and it doesn't scratch the Grand Strategy RTS/TBS hybrid niche for you like it does for me, then maybe wait for this to eventually go on sale in a year or two for 50% off or more.\t\t\t\t\t
