\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSadly I have refunded this as I found this very uncomfortable to play on my neck and wrist. I got really bad cramps after a few songs and had to stop playing. The note bar on the UI is way to close the edge of the screen and due to how my monitor is positioned i found I was twisting my new awkwardly which caused quite a lot of pain and discomfort. I felt the mouse movement was also very taxing on my wrist.

This game is very fun and I love how unique it is. The developers really have a great sense of humour. The songs are great and do well to add to the games vibe of silliness. I wouldn't really call this a rhythm game but more of a musical instrument simulator with sheet notes. I could see this being very fun to play at parties. If this game had a custom trombone controller like it jokes about in the menu it would be epic.

Would have been nice to have an option to centre the note bar on screen and maybe have game pad controls using the analogue sticks.

Edit: Got this as part of the current Rhythm Humble Bundle so I gave it another chance. My points above still stand i find this very uncomfortable to play but in very short bursts it is a lot of silly fun. I still cant recommend paying full price for this but if you buy on sale you could get some short lived entertainment from this.\t\t\t\t\t
