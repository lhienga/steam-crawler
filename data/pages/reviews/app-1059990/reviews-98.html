\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA breath of fresh air in PC rhythm game, the core gameplay isn't new but it's fun and easy to pick for new players but not without a flaws which still can be improved/fixed with update.

1. The short chain note isn't perfect, why on earth I need to hold the button for a spit of seconds on it? Why don't just make it a single press like all the other rhythm game out there.
2. The line where people need to press the button/click is so small, I thought this game isn't about precision? Even Guitar Hero has a bigger line. It feels like this game has an identity crisis between fun or hard
3. The space between the sudden high up and low down note (or the opposite) is so damn small, like you want the people to react with a very precise hand muscle to get a good result. It become a nightmare when it mixed with chain note (with hold.)
4. The difficulty kinda wrong sometimes, there are songs with 2-4 difficulty but harder than 5+
5. The scoring system kinda confusing, in long songs I could get S even if I missed so many notes but in short songs I need to be more precise and stay in CHAMPS all the time. Here's another identity crisis.

But I'd still recommend this game to my friends.\t\t\t\t\t
