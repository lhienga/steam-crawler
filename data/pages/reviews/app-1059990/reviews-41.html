\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTrombone Champ is a rhythm game like Guitar Hero but with a trombone instead. It's as goofy and ridiculous as it sounds. Play through a selection of classical music, jazz, and other recognizable tunes from American and international music history. Toot your trombone by clicking to hit a note, and change the note by dragging your mouse up and down. As a former tromboner in my primary school days, I can confidently say that trombone skill simultaneously does and does not translate to this game, and it's great. There's a trading card collecting system and a whole bunch of hidden easter eggs to access the game's quote-unquote story mode. The modding scene for this game is also pretty decent, in the event that you get bored of playing the same twenty tunes over and over. I'd recommend Trombone Champ if you're looking for something just absolutely goofy to play, a totally un-serious rhythm game.\t\t\t\t\t
