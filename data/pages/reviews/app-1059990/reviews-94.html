I initially bought this game for the meme and didn't expect much from it (I don't even like rhythm games) but the game overall was a nice surprise. The graphics please the eye and the gameplay is enjoyable. Humour might not be for everyone, but you do not even need to worry about the story and focus on tooting.
I neither encountered any bugs, nor found any other flaws. Sure, there could be more levels and (official) mod support, but I'm sure the developers will add it eventually. Therefore, I'm keeping the game on my disc and waiting for more updates!\t\t\t\t\t
