The game's story along with all the achievements can be gotten in no more than 5 hours meaning a lot of your time with this game is going to come down to how much you enjoy playing the main set-list along with any custom songs you may want to implement. Still, of all the criticisms I could have towards the game lack of content isn't the worst. I would recommend this game on a sale (perhaps the Steam summer sale which is happening as I'm writing this). I do hope the devs go for a sequel and go all-out with improvements!
