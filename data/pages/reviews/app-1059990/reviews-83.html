\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've always been terrible at music games but this one just hits the spot for me.
I find be able to use my ears to get the timing and the pitch right really makes me enjoy music games more, and I feel like trombone champ really hits it on the head.
The humour is also massively on point. A bit of a pastiche of the Wii free style / music games / simulators that were just god awful and never really worked as music games. So it feels like an even more hilarious dig at this game genre considering trombone champ pulled it off way better.

The game is packed with weird easter eggs as well which are all different flavours of stupid. Get ready for the deep doot and toot lore of trombone champ and why baboons are an integral part of the story.

What takes this from a good game to a great game is the extent of the modding community. If you follow them you will have a ever constant stream of new songs and even memes to play on the platform of trombone champ. Additionally the love and care some of the modders put into their content is obvious. Some of them will use the models from the game to recreate famous music videos in the trombone champ style, which is hilarious. If you want to see a good example of this,
a-ha - Take On Me (Trombone Champ Chart)
or
Trombone Champ - Don't Stop Believin'
are great examples on youtube of trombone mods done in the best way possible. But there are so many people who have modded songs and memes into this game you are bound to find a collection of some custom tracks you like and finally get to toot along.

Easily my favourite game of the year.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright. This one's interesting.

Conceptually, as a rhythm game, it's very fun. It's mechanics are straightforward, and it doesn't need to explain itself too much for anyone to just pick up and play - it makes sense. That being said, the scoring system places priority on pitch accuracy over timing, and in fact, really mostly disregards timing so long as the pitch is close. I suppose this is why the breath mechanic is there (to force you to try and time your notes as opposed to just holding the mouse button down constantly), but I digress.

As for the rest of the game... this is quirky millennial humor at its absolute worst. This game has made me laugh a total of one time, and that is all. It wasn't hearty laughter, no, it was but a short chuckle. Do you realize how many times I have heard a fart sound effect in 3 hours? Absolutely unforgivable. Dan, I'm going to stunlock you and your wife.

Update: I S ranked every song and got every achievement in under 5 hours. This game is now permanently uninstalled.\t\t\t\t\t
