\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally Hilarious to some, and Really Annoying to others (who can't stand out of tune notes).
If you're a fan of Florence Foster Jenkins, then get this immediately. I am, and it had me laughing out loud!

This is a GAME CHANGER for rhythm games, because it's now a Rhythm & PITCH game. You are ACTUALLY playing a melodic instrument, instead of just taping a button in time to the music. The game lets you visually see if you are too high (sharp) or too low (flat) when playing a note, letting you 'bend' it in tune. It also simulates breathing (you can run out of air if you hold a note for too long), which encourages the player to create PHRASING within the music.

I am a professional trombonist. This is more like playing a SLIDE WHISTLE than it is a trombone (a trombone has only 7 slide positions, and each position has a series of notes (low to high) , whereas a slide whistle only has one note per position and has MANY positions). However I am pleasantly surprised that the game:
1) Incorporates trombone artist flash cards
2) Has feint guide lines in the game to show the whole-step and half-step differences that exist within an Major Scale (Ionian)
3) Similar to a trombone, the game relies on the player practicing and establishing muscle memory in order to master the instrument
4) inverts the vertical mouse to more accurately simulate how a slide works (the further you extend the slide- by moving the mouse away from you- the further the air has to travel in the trombone, which in direct parallel, lowers the pitch of the instrument)
5) The game takes into account Vibrato! A level of control over musical expression i have not seen in ANY other rhythm game. Those who can master it can charge up their multiplier VERY fast for Super High Scores.
6) The Musical literature includes mostly songs that EVERYONE has probably heard sometime in their life at least once. This lets the user focus more on being musical instead of trying to learn how the song goes.
7 The Glissando: bending the pitch from one note to another. Game changer.

I hope this game is the first of many, and that it is able to reach many more platforms, like mobile devices.
For future versions I hope to see more ways to ARTICULATE notes like SLURRING (not stopping the air between different notes while softly tonguing along the airstream). This could be implemented by holding a keyboard key (or right clicking) to start a slur, and pressing another key (or left clicking) to play notes within that slur. And perhaps more famous trombone literature like the Morceau Symphonique, Trombone Concerto/Concertino, or songs by performed by famous Jazz Trombonists like "I'm Getting Sentimental Over You", "Emily", and "Basin St. Blues".
