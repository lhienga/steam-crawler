This is a pretty difficult game to play well, since the controls are intentionally awkward. I managed to S-rank 20 songs before I sorta gave up because the remainder were just ridiculously difficult and in the time it would take me to practice and S-rank the rest, I could probably just learn the actual trombone. So yeah, its a goofy, difficult, fun, extremely unique game that I think everyone should play at least once.\t\t\t\t\t
