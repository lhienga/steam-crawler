\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had a lot of genuine fun with Trombone Champ!

This might not be surprising since it truly is the world's first trombone rhythm game but it has a lot of charm and silly moments. Picking it up and playing it is very easy but even if you are struggling with a more challenging song, you will be chuckling at the off-rhythm sounds your trombone makes or at the silly background images that appear during a song. There is a healthy amount of collectibles and secrets as well. While getting full completion might take you around 5-6 hours, the game doesn't outstay its welcome and it stays fresh throughout.

I was also surprised how well the game played on the Steam Deck - you simply tilted the Deck forward and backward to move the rhythm pin and pressed RT to play the trombone. It's surprisingly intuitive to play it this way and I even managed to get around 20 S ranks on the Deck. (To get the rest of the S ranks needed for the achievements, I used a mouse as clicking the mouse is faster than pressing RT where a song's rhythm needs lots of rapid presses.) Nevertheless, Trombone Champ is very lenient with its scoring (even allowing you to decrease the rhythm scrolling to 60%), so you shouldn't be concerned about the difficulty of the game.

Overall, the game is an easy recommendation at full price, especially for fans of rhythm games!\t\t\t\t\t
