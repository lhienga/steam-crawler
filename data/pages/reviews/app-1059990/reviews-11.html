Lore exists in this game. It is also very good lore! The story is fun, and easy to follow. The toot/turd system is also very well made, and I like that you can build a card with turds, so that if you are having bad luck on getting it there is no trouble.

I can't stress enough how fun this game is. There were no points where I didn't want to play, or had to grind a bunch. It is all amazing. At the time of writing this review I have 23 hours on the game, S - Tier on all but 2 songs, and an engoldenated version of every card.

However, I wish there were more songs. I have had so much fun with the existing ones that I would like others to try. But besides this, this is a perfect game!
