\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery challenging! Some songs are outright borderline impossible, but don't let that scare you away though.
It's the failures that makes this game so hilarious and not to mention the story about baboons which makes absolutely no sense.

After a while you will get better at hitting the notes and feel the satisfaction of getting some of those S-scores (highest score). And no, you don't have to play 100% perfectly or hit every single note to get the S-score. The game is to some extent forgiving if you should miss some or are not spot on the notes.

I highly recommend Trombone Champ, even if you're not really into rhythm games. It's a game that's not meant to be taken too seriously, but at the same time puts you at the edge of your seat.\t\t\t\t\t
