\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like grinding? Do you have a 30 minute lunch break and want to feel like you've accomplished something? A short downtime on the go with your Steam Deck? Do you have small breaks between other tasks and want to get a little dopamine from a finished task to keep you going?

Mega Man X Dive Offline hits all of those itches quite nicely. Grinding where upgrading literally anything makes you stronger, no matter how bad or good a character is. Like it? Get it, use it, it'll make you stronger overall. There's mods for the game too! Personally I use it on little breaks between work tasks, do a single stage to feel good and get myself back into the fray. Not every game can make you feel like you got something nice for 5 minutes of gameplay, but this game does.

Don't get bogged down too hard planning, just use stuff you like and upgrade everything you enjoy. Resources are bottomless with the grinding stages available.

9/10.

Why a 9? Because changing the graphics quality only affects menu transitions and interface items and not the game, and unless you're on steam deck, I hope you're ready to use mouse for interface items even on controller play.\t\t\t\t\t
